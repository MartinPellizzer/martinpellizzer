Cytisus supranubius

Cytisus supranubius, commonly known as the cloud alpine broom, is a shrub native to the high altitudes of the Pyrenees and the Alps.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are composed of numerous tiny leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, mountainous climates.

Cytisus supranubius is often found in alpine meadows and is a hardy species that can withstand harsh environmental conditions.
