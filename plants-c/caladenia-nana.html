Caladenia nana

Caladenia nana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, featuring a single, slender stem with a single, lance-shaped flower. The flower is usually pale green or yellow with a distinctive, hood-like labellum. It blooms during the spring and is found in various habitats, including heathlands and open forests.

This species is noted for its delicate appearance and is part of the Caladenia genus, which includes many other orchid species.
