Callista broadleyana

Callista broadleyana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms that are highly ornamental, often used in gardens and floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for various climates.

The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, adding a contrasting texture to the vibrant flowers.
