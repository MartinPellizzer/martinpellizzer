Coelogyne tricallosa

Coelogyne tricallosa, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with three prominent, fleshy, and elongated lobes. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red throat. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

Coelogyne tricallosa thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
