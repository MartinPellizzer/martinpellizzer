Croton illustris

Croton illustris, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Its leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture.

Croton illustris is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
