Centaurea kartschiana

Centaurea kartschiana, commonly known as the Kartschian cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, aiding in water retention.

Centaurea kartschiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
