Ceratostylis thailandica

Ceratostylis thailandica, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along streams. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered an important species for botanical research and conservation.
