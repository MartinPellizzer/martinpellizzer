Cystorchis appendiculata

Cystorchis appendiculata, commonly known as the appendiculate orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
