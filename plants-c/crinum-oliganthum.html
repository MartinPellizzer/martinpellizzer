Crinum oliganthum

Crinum oliganthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces bulbous underground stems, which are typical of the Amaryllis family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
