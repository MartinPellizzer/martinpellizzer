Chenopodium hortense

Chenopodium hortense, commonly known as garden goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. The plant features alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are a source of nutrition for both wildlife and humans.

Chenopodium hortense is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and resilience in various soil conditions.
