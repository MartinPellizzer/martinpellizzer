Crinum voyronii

Crinum voyronii, commonly known as the Voyron lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color and can grow up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum voyronii is also known for its strong fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
