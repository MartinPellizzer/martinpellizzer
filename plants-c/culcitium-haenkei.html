Culcitium haenkei

Culcitium haenkei, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it well adapted to harsh desert environments. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with multiple stems that can reach up to several feet in height.

Culcitium haenkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in hot, dry climates.
