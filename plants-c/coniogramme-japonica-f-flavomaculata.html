Coniogramme japonica f. flavomaculata

Coniogramme japonica f. flavomaculata, commonly known as the Japanese coniogramme, is a cultivar of the coniogramme plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, featuring bright yellow spots on dark green leaves. The plant is a member of the Araliaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It typically grows as an upright shrub with compound leaves that are glossy and textured.

This variety thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for shaded garden spaces.
