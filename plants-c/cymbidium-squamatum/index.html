Cymbidium Squamatum

Cymbidium squamatum is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its striking, long-lasting blooms that come in shades of white, pink, and red. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires careful attention to light and temperature. Its unique foliage and floral display make it a prized addition to both gardens and collections. Due to its limited natural habitat, conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival.
