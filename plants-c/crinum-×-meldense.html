Crinum × meldense

Crinum × meldense, commonly known as the hybrid Crinum lilies, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in various colors such as white, pink, and yellow. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves that are often broader at the base and taper towards the tip. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
