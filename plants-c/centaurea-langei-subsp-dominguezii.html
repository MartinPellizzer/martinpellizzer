Centaurea langei subsp. dominguezii

Centaurea langei subsp.

dominguezii, commonly known as the Dominguez's Blazing Star, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to red flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces tall, spindly stems.

It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.
