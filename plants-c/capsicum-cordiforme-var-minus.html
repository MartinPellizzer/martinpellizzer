Capsicum cordiforme var. minus

Capsicum cordiforme var. minus, commonly known as the small heart-shaped pepper.

This variant of the heart-shaped pepper is characterized by its compact size and distinctive heart-shaped leaves. It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is native to South America. The plant typically produces small, ornamental fruits that range in color from yellow to red.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value rather than for culinary use.
