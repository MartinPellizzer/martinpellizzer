Cuphea linifolia var. helicophora

Cuphea linifolia var. helicophora, commonly known as the spiral cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of tubular, red or pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and vibrant flower colors.
