Crepis hyoseroides

Crepis hyoseroides, commonly known as the slender mouse-ear chickweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Crepis hyoseroides is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
