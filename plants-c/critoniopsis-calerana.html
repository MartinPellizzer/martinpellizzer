Critoniopsis calerana

Critoniopsis calerana, commonly known as the Calerana cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its succulent structure. Its flowers are short-lived but attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological role.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable in some regions.
