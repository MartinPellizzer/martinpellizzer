Cullenia ceylanica

Cullenia ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon ironwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka and southern India.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hard, dense wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with stipules, and its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale yellow.

It produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds, and it plays an important ecological role in its native habitats.
