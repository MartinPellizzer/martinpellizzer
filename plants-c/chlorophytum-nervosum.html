Chlorophytum nervosum

Chlorophytum nervosum, commonly known as the striped spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, slender leaves that are often striped with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that hang in clusters from the ends of its stems. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum nervosum is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
