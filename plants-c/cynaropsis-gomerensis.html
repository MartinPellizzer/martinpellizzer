Cynaropsis gomerensis

Cynaropsis gomerensis, commonly known as the Gomer's globe thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky inflorescence composed of numerous small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Cynaropsis gomerensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
