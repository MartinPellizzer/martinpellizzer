Clematis spooneri

Clematis spooneri, commonly known as the spoonleaf clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the eastern United States.

It features heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its vigorous growth habit requires support structures, and it is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
