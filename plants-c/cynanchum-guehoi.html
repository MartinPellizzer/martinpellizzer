Cynanchum guehoi

Cynanchum guehoi, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a follicle containing several seeds, and it is adapted to grow in dry, rocky environments.

Cynanchum guehoi is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
