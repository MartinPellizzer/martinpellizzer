Cistus × bornetii

Cistus × bornetii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and clusters of pink to violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is part of the Cistaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in poor, well-drained soils. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
