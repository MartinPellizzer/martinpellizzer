Chrysanthemum maroccanum

Chrysanthemum maroccanum, commonly known as the Moroccan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with gray-green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
