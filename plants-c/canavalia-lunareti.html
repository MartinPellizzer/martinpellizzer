Canavalia lunareti

Canavalia lunareti, commonly known as moon bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces distinctive, moon-shaped seeds that are encased in a fibrous pod. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Canavalia lunareti is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
