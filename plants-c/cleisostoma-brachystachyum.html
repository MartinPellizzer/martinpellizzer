Cleisostoma brachystachyum

Cleisostoma brachystachyum, commonly known as the short-stalked spiral orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, spirally arranged leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose soil. It is known for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild.
