Cynanchum decaisneanum

Cynanchum decaisneanum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by follicles containing seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
