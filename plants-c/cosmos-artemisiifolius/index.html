Cosmos Artemisiifolius

Mexican cosmos, scientifically known as Cosmos artemisiifolius, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America. With its vibrant pink to red blooms and feathery foliage, it adds a dramatic touch to gardens and landscapes. This annual herb is prized for its ability to thrive in poor soil and full sun, making it a low-maintenance choice for gardeners. Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation have made it a popular choice in both ornamental and ecological settings. Whether grown for its aesthetic appeal or its role in supporting pollinators, Mexican cosmos is a versatile and captivating plant.
