Ceropegia ledgeri

Ceropegia ledgeri, commonly known as the "string of hearts," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
