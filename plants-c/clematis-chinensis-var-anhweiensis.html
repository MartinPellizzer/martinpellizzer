Clematis chinensis var. anhweiensis

Clematis chinensis var. anhweiensis, commonly known as the Anhui clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

The variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
