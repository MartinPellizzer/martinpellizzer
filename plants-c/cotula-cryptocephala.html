Cotula cryptocephala

Cotula cryptocephala, commonly known as the small-headed groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky outcrops.

Cotula cryptocephala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
