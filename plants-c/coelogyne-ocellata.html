Coelogyne ocellata

Coelogyne ocellata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spotted leaves and fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat.

Coelogyne ocellata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
