Campylosporus madagascariensis

Campylosporus madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar grasshopper plant, is a unique species native to Madagascar.

The plant belongs to the family Poaceae, which includes grasses, and is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions.
