Cassinia paniculata

Cassinia paniculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cassinia, is a shrub native to southern Australia.

It features small, yellow flower heads that are typically clustered in paniculate inflorescences. The leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in grasslands and open forests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens.
