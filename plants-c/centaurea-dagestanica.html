Centaurea dagestanica

Centaurea dagestanica, commonly known as the Dagestani star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright purple to red composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It has spiny leaves and a flowering period that occurs in summer.

Centaurea dagestanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
