Cassia wallichiana

Cassia wallichiana, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, oval-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia wallichiana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
