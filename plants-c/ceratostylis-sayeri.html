Ceratostylis sayeri

Ceratostylis sayeri, commonly known as the Sayer's spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, elongated spike of flowers. Its blossoms are usually white or pale green, with a distinctive, club-shaped lip.

Ceratostylis sayeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
