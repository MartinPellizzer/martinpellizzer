Carex duthiei

Carex duthiei, commonly known as Duthie's sedge, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in Guatemala and Mexico. This perennial plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its slender, grass-like leaves are arranged in a dense rosette, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Carex duthiei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its upright, textured foliage.
