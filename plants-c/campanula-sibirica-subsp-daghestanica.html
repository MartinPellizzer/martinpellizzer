Campanula sibirica subsp. daghestanica

Campanula sibirica subsp.

daghestanica, commonly known as the Daghestan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region. It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
