Crinum ornatum

Crinum ornatum, commonly known as the showy crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features tall, slender stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It is well adapted to warm, humid climates and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Crinum ornatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
