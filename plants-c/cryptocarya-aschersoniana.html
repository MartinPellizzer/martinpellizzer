Cryptocarya aschersoniana

Cryptocarya aschersoniana, commonly known as the New Zealand nutmeg.

It is a small evergreen tree native to New Zealand, characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, which is encased in a hard, woody shell. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
