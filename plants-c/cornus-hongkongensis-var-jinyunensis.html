Cornus hongkongensis var. jinyunensis

Cornus hongkongensis var. jinyunensis, commonly known as the Jinyun cornel.

It is a flowering plant native to China, specifically the Jinyun Mountains region. This variety is characterized by its distinctive yellowish-green to reddish-brown bark and showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic features.
