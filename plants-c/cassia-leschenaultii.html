Cassia leschenaultii

Cassia leschenaultii, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern and eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its feathery, compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain seeds surrounded by a silky, hairy covering. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.

Cassia leschenaultii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
