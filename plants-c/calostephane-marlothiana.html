Calostephane marlothiana

Calostephane marlothiana, commonly known as the Cape sugarbush, is a South African shrub belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic, evergreen leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
