Cynanchica biebersteinii

Cynanchica biebersteinii, commonly known as the Russian milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features slender, branching stems that often lie prostrate or ascend at the base. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three ovate to lanceolate leaflets.

Cynanchica biebersteinii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and deep root systems.
