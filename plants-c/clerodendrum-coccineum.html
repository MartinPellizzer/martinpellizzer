Clerodendrum coccineum

Clerodendrum coccineum, commonly known as the red-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red bracts that surround small, yellowish flowers, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Clerodendrum coccineum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
