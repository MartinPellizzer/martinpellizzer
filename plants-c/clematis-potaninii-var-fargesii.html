Clematis potaninii var. fargesii

Clematis potaninii var. fargesii, commonly known as the Farges' Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, typically arranged in a palmate pattern. The plant produces showy, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
