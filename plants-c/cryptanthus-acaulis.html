Cryptanthus acaulis

Cryptanthus acaulis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Bromeliaceae and is characterized by its short, compact stem and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of tubular or bell-shaped blooms in shades of red, pink, and orange, typically during the holiday season. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water in its native habitat.

Cryptanthus acaulis is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
