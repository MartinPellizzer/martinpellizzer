Crepidium runcinatum

Crepidium runcinatum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

It is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and relatively easy cultivation.
