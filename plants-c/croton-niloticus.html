Croton niloticus

Croton niloticus, commonly known as the Nile croton, is a tropical shrub native to Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Due to its striking foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in tropical landscapes.
