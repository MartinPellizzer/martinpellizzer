Cyphostemma Gallaense

Cyphostemma gallaense is a unique and lesser-known plant native to parts of Africa. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many toxic and ornamental species. This plant is characterized by its slender, woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh climates. Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often overlooked in botanical studies.
