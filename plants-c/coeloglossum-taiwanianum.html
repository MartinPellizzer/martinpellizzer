Coeloglossum taiwanianum

Coeloglossum taiwanianum, commonly known as the Taiwan orchid, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
