Citrosma eriocalyx

Citrosma eriocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved citrosma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round, and turn from green to yellow or orange when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
