Chrysanthemum roborowskii var. tsinghaicum

Chrysanthemum roborowskii var. tsinghaicum, commonly known as the Tsinghai chrysanthemum, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. This variety is known for its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its decorative value in gardens.
