Cynorkis graminea

Cynorkis graminea, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, erect stems that are often branched and bear narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like arrangement of small, greenish-white flowers.

Cynorkis graminea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
