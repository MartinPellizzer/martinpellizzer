Cyclonema spinescens

Cyclonema spinescens, commonly known as the spiny cyclonema, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Cyclonema spinescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
