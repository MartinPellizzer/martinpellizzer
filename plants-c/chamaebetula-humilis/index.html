Chamaebetula Humilis

Chamaebetula humilis is a rare and delicate plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests and mountainous terrains. Known for its small, clustered leaves and subtle flowers, this species is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. Due to habitat loss and climate change, its population has been declining, making it a vulnerable species. Protecting Chamaebetula humilis is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
