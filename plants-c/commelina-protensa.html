Commelina protensa

Commelina protensa, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae, which includes many grass-like plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, blue-violet flowers with three petals. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
