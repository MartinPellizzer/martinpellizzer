Carduus salinus

Carduus salinus, commonly known as the saltbush thistle, is a flowering plant native to arid and saline environments.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant has deeply lobed leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in harsh conditions. It produces yellow flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Carduus salinus is often used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic qualities.
