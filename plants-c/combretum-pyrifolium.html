Combretum pyrifolium

Combretum pyrifolium, commonly known as the flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also valued for its ecological role, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
