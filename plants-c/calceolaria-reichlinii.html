Calceolaria reichlinii

Calceolaria reichlinii, commonly known as the Reichlin's slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions with well-drained soil.
