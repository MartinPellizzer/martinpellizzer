Cissus stuhlmannii

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.
