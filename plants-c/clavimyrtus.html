Clavimyrtus

Clavimyrtus, commonly known as the flame tree, is a genus of flowering plants native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite and simple, with a slightly velvety texture.

Clavimyrtus species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are often planted in gardens and public spaces.
