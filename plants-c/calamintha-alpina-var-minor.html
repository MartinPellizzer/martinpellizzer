Calamintha alpina var. minor

Calamintha alpina var. minor, commonly known as alpine bluehead, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features small, fragrant, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, hairy leaves that are arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.

Calamintha alpina var. minor is valued for its ornamental appeal and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
