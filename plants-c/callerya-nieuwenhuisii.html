Callerya nieuwenhuisii

Callerya nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuis's callerya, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its woody stems, trifoliate leaves, and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
