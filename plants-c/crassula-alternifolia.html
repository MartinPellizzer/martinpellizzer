Crassula alternifolia

Crassula alternifolia, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Crassula ovata, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, storing water to help it survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring or summer. It is widely cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.

Crassula alternifolia thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
