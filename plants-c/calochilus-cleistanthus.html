Calochilus cleistanthus

Calochilus cleistanthus, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, violet-colored flowers with prominent veining. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in heathlands or near coastal areas. Its flowers are usually arranged in a spike and bloom during the cooler months.

Calochilus cleistanthus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
