Casearia balansae var. subglabra

Casearia balansae var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth casearia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive elliptical fruits that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
