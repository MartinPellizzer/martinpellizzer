Copernicia baileyana var. laciniosa

Copernicia baileyana var. laciniosa, commonly known as the lace fern copernicia, is a unique tropical plant belonging to the Arecaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are long, narrow, and often have a finely divided structure, resembling a lace pattern.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
