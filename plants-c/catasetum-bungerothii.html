Catasetum bungerothii

Catasetum bungerothii, commonly known as the Bungert's catasetum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are unique in structure, featuring a specialized lip that aids in pollination by specific insects.

Catasetum bungerothii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
