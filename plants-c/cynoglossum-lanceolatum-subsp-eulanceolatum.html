Cynoglossum lanceolatum subsp. eulanceolatum

Cynoglossum lanceolatum subsp.

eulanceolatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe. It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem.

The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color, blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
