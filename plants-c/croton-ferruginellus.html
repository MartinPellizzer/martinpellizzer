Croton ferruginellus

Croton ferruginellus, commonly known as the rusty croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often toxic species. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
