Convolvulus geniculatus

Convolvulus geniculatus, commonly known as the zigzag morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
