Cyperus brevifolius var. stellulatus

Cyperus brevifolius var. stellulatus, commonly known as the star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are often white or pale yellow. It typically grows in wetland or moist soil environments, making it a common sight in marshes and along water bodies.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for aquatic gardens and pond edges.
