Cotyledon procurva

Cotyledon procurva, commonly known as the fleshy cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant has a short, woody stem and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange. Its leaves are adapted to store water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Cotyledon procurva is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
