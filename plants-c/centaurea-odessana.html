Centaurea odessana

Centaurea odessana, commonly known as the Odessa knapweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Ukraine and the Crimea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as steppes and rocky soils. It produces bright yellow to orange flowers that are surrounded by pale blue bracts.

Centaurea odessana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
