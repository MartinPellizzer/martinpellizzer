Cyrtomium juglandifolium var. latifolium

Cyrtomium juglandifolium var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to eastern China.

It features large, leathery, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a compact, upright growth habit with a dense frond structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its foliage is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
