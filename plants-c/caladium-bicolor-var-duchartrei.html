Caladium bicolor var. duchartrei

Caladium bicolor var. duchartrei, commonly known as the "Duchartrei" caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Caladium bicolor var. duchartrei is typically propagated through tuberous roots, which can be planted in spring for optimal growth.
