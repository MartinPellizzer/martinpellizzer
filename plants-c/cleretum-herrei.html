Cleretum herrei

Cleretum herrei, commonly known as the Herre's clethra, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Clethraceae and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Cleretum herrei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
