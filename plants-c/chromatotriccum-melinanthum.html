Chromatotriccum melinanthum

Chromatotriccum melinanthum, commonly known as the golden starflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble small stars, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.
