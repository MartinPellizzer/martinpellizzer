Cabucala brachyantha

Cabucala brachyantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Cabucala brachyantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
