Clerodendrum floribundum var. latifolium

Clerodendrum floribundum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the warmer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
