Carex breviaristata

Carex breviaristata, commonly known as the short-bracted sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to North America and is typically found in wet or moist environments such as marshes and along stream banks. Carex breviaristata is an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
