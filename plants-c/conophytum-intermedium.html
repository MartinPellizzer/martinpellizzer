Conophytum intermedium

Conophytum intermedium, commonly known as the "Cape Rims" or "Rocky Rims," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. Conophytum intermedium is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
