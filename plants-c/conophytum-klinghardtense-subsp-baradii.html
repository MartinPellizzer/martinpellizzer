Conophytum klinghardtense subsp. baradii

Conophytum klinghardtense subsp.

baradii, commonly known as the Klein Karoo conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
