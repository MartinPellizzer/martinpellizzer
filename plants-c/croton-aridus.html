Croton aridus

Croton aridus, commonly known as the desert croton, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in dry environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by distinctive, spiky fruits. It is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal rainfall.

Croton aridus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
