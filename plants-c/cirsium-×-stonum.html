Cirsium × stonum

Cirsium × stonum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade. Cirsium × stonum is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its robust growth and adaptability.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice in gardens, though its prickly nature requires careful handling.
