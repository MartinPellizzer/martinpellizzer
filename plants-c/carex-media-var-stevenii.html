Carex media var. stevenii

Carex media var. stevenii, commonly known as Steven's sedge, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and produces spikes of small, pale green to brown flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually a dark green color.

Steven's sedge is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
