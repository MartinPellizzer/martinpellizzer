Cavendishia engleriana var. engleriana

Cavendishia engleriana var. engleriana, commonly known as the Engler's Cavendishia, is a member of the Cactaceae family native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stems that are typically green to blue-green in color. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and xeriscaping.
