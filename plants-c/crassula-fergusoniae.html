Crassula fergusoniae

Crassula fergusoniae, commonly known as the Frost Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
