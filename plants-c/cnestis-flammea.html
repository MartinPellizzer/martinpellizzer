Cnestis flammea

Cnestis flammea, commonly known as the flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its bark is often smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various wildlife.
