Columnea rubida

Columnea rubida, commonly known as the red columnea, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Columnea rubida thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
