Croton kaloae

Croton kaloae, commonly known as the Hawaiian croton, is a tropical plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall, characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and striking, colorful flowers. The leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped and exhibit a range of colors, including green, red, and yellow, depending on the variety. The flowers are small and clustered in spikes, often with yellow petals and red stamens.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
