Cousinia semenovii

Cousinia semenovii, commonly known as the Semenov's thistle, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

Cousinia semenovii is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
