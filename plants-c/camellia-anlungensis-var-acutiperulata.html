Camellia anlungensis var. acutiperulata

Camellia anlungensis var. acutiperulata, commonly known as the sharp-lobed camellia, is a species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with sharply pointed lobes. The plant produces striking, double flowers in shades of pink and red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both gardens and botanical collections.
