Coalisina angustifolia var. namaquensis

Coalisina angustifolia var. namaquensis, commonly known as theNamaquana coalisina, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky and arid environments. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xerophytic landscapes and rock gardens.
