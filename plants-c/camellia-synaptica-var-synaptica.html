Camellia synaptica var. synaptica

Camellia synaptica var. synaptica, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, camellia-like flowers that bloom in winter and early spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, while the flowers are usually pink or red with a white center.

Camellia synaptica var. synaptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
