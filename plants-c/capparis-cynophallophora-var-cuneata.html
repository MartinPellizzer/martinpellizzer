Capparis cynophallophora var. cuneata

Capparis cynophallophora var. cuneata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf caper, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and slightly serrated. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, tangy fruits. It is adapted to dry, rocky soils and can thrive in full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a resilient plant suitable for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
