Cryptocereus imitans

Cryptocereus imitans, commonly known as the imitation cactus or the moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a columnar cactus with a short, thick stem and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom at night, a characteristic shared by many species in the Cactaceae family. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and striking appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
