Cirsium canum

Cirsium canum, commonly known as hispid prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its spiny, hairy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Cirsium canum is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
