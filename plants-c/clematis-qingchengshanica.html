Clematis qingchengshanica

Clematis qingchengshanica, commonly known as the Qingchengshan Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy flowers. The plant produces fragrant, typically blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of palmately compound leaves, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
