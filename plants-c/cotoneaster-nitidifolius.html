Cotoneaster nitidifolius

Cotoneaster nitidifolius, commonly known as the glossyleaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, providing visual interest throughout the growing season. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cotoneaster nitidifolius is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to a variety of soil conditions.
