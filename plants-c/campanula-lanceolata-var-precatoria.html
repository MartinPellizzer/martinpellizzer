Campanula lanceolata var. precatoria

Campanula lanceolata var. precatoria, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lanceolate, often hairy, and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
