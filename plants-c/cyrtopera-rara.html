Cyrtopera rara

Cyrtopera rara, commonly known as the rare cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnately compound leaves. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a valuable but endangered species.

Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
