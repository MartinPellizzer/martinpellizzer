Coelogyne hastiloba

Coelogyne hastiloba, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Coelogyne hastiloba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
