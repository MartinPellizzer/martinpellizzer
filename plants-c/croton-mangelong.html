Croton mangelong

Croton mangelong, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along forest edges.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
