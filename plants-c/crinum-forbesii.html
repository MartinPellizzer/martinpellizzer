Crinum forbesii

Crinum forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically broad and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Crinum forbesii is also valued for its resilience and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
