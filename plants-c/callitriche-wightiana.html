Callitriche wightiana

Callitriche wightiana, commonly known as the Wight's water star, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale blue, and it has slender, branching stems with small, oval leaves.
