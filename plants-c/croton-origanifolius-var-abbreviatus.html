Croton origanifolius var. abbreviatus

Croton origanifolius var. abbreviatus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is popular as a houseplant and in tropical gardens.
