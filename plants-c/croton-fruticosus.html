Croton fruticosus

Croton fruticosus, commonly known as the creeping croton, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or subshrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive yellow-green coloration. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsules containing numerous seeds.

Croton fruticosus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh environments.
