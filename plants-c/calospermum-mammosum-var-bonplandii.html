Calospermum mammosum var. bonplandii

Calospermum mammosum var. bonplandii, commonly known as the bonplandii milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various insect species.
