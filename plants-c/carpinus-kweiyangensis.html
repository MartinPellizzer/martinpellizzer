Carpinus kweiyangensis

Carpinus kweiyangensis, commonly known as the Kweiyang hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, winged fruits that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
