Clusia ducu var. schlimiana

Clusia ducu var. schlimiana, commonly known as the Schlim's clusia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Clusia ducu var. schlimiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate urban environments.
