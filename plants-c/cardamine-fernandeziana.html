Cardamine fernandeziana

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with showy blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being finely toothed and slightly hairy.
