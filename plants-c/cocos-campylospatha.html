Cocos campylospatha

Cocos campylospatha, commonly known as the narrow-leaf coconut.

It is a species of palm native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and a tall, slender trunk. Its fruit, resembling a coconut, contains a fibrous husk and a hard inner shell.

This plant is adapted to grow in coastal and wetland environments, often forming dense stands in mangrove areas.
