Cymbidium linearisepalum

Cymbidium linearisepalum, commonly known as the linear-sheath orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and long, pendant-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. This orchid prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

It is valued for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
