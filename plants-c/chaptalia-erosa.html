Chaptalia erosa

Chaptalia erosa, commonly known as the hairy chaptalia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. Its small yellow flowers bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Chaptalia erosa is often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides, where it thrives in full sun.
