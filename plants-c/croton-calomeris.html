Croton calomeris

Croton calomeris, commonly known as the white croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that often display a mix of green, yellow, and white hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Croton calomeris is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
