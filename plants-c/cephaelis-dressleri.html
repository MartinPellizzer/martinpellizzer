Cephaelis dressleri

Cephaelis dressleri, commonly known as the golden flame creeper, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, preferring warm and humid conditions.

Cephaelis dressleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
