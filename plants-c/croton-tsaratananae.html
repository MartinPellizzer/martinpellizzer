Croton tsaratananae

Croton tsaratananae, commonly known as the Tsaratanana croton, is a unique species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can range from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the specific ecological conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
