Campanula pamphylica subsp. afyonica

Campanula pamphylica subsp.

afyonica, commonly known as the Afyon bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and resilience.
