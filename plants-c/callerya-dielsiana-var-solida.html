Callerya dielsiana var. solida

Callerya dielsiana var. solida, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
