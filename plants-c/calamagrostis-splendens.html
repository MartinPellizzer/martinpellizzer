Calamagrostis splendens

Calamagrostis splendens, commonly known as the shining grass or silver feather reed, is a tall, ornamental grass native to eastern North America.

It features slender, silver-green blades that turn coppery in the fall, creating a striking visual display. The plant produces feathery panicles of pale to golden flowers in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often used in wetland restoration projects.

Calamagrostis splendens is hardy and adaptable, making it a popular choice for water feature landscaping.
