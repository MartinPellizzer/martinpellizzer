Camellia × intermedia

Camellia × intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a hybrid shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, camellia-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and thrives in partial shade with well-drained, acidic soil. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding early color to gardens.

Camellia × intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in temperate climates.
