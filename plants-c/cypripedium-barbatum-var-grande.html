Cypripedium barbatum var. grande

Cypripedium barbatum var. grande, commonly known as the large beard lily, is a striking orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow lip and a dark red or purple upper lip. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its stems are usually unbranched and bear the flowers at the top.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with the right growing conditions.
