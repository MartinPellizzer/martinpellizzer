Calamus longibracteatus

Calamus longibracteatus, commonly known as the long-bract calamus, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is a perennial herb characterized by its tall, slender rhizome and large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces a spadix surrounded by a showy, petal-like spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for aquatic and bog gardens.
