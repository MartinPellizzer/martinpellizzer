Chlorophytum purpuratum

Chlorophytum purpuratum, commonly known as the Purple Vulture's Nest, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with a distinctive purple hue along the edges. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by pale blue or purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
