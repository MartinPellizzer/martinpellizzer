Cryptostylis papuana

Cryptostylis papuana, commonly known as the Papuan cryptostylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is an epiphytic plant native to the rainforests of New Guinea. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers are often short-lived but attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
