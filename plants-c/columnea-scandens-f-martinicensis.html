Columnea scandens f. martinicensis

Columnea scandens f. martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique flame flower, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the Caribbean.

It features vibrant red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has trailing stems that can grow up to several meters, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trained on trellises. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety pubescence.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.
