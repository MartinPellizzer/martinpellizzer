Crataegus babakhanloui

Crataegus babakhanloui, commonly known as Babakhanlou hawthorn, is a species of hawthorn native to Iran.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. The plant features thorny branches and produces white or pinkish flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries in the fall. Its leaves are generally oval to elliptical in shape, with serrated edges.

The plant is adapted to dry and rocky environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
