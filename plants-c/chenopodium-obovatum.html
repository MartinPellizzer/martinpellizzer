Chenopodium obovatum

Chenopodium obovatum, commonly known as smooth goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and has a sprawling growth habit. The plant features alternate, ovate to obovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces dry, three-angled fruits.

Chenopodium obovatum is often used in traditional medicine and is also a common weed in agricultural settings.
