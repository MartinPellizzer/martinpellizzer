Cotoneaster hicksii

Cotoneaster hicksii, commonly known as the Sierra Madre cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of bright red berries in the fall. Cotoneaster hicksii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in dry climates with minimal care.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and wildlife habitat in its native range.
