Callitriche verna var. fallax

Callitriche verna var. fallax, commonly known as the early spring water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, floating or submerged leaves that are usually oval or ovate in shape. Its flowers are tiny and clustered, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
