Caldasia brasiliensis

Caldasia brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian caldasia, is a herbaceous plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. Caldasia brasiliensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
