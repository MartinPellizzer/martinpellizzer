Camellia mutabilis

Camellia mutabilis, commonly known as the everblooming camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that can change color from white to pink to red as they bloom. The plant is evergreen, with dark green, leathery leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Camellia mutabilis is also valued for its fragrance and is often cultivated for both its beauty and its ability to provide winter interest in the garden.
