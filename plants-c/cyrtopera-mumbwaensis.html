Cyrtopera mumbwaensis

Cyrtopera mumbwaensis, commonly known as the Mumbwa Cyrtopera, is a unique plant species native to specific regions in Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often hairy foliage. The plant typically features small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and purple, adding to its ornamental appeal. Cyrtopera mumbwaensis thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to bright light conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
