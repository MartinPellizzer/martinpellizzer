Chlorophytum krookianum

Chlorophytum krookianum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with yellow or white stripes. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Chlorophytum krookianum is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
