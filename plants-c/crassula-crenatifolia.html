Crassula crenatifolia

Crassula crenatifolia, commonly known as the scalycap crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and attractive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula crenatifolia is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
