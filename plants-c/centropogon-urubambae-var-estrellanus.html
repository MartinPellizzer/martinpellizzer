Centropogon urubambae var. estrellanus

Centropogon urubambae var. estrellanus, commonly known as the Estrellan mountain grass, is a rare and endemic plant species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes, where it adapts to cool and arid conditions. Its flowering period occurs during the warmer months, producing delicate white or pale blue flowers.
