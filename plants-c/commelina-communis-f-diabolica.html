Commelina communis f. diabolica

Commelina communis f. diabolica, commonly known as the devil's lettuce, is a striking variant of the common Commelina.

It is characterized by its dark purple to almost black foliage, which contrasts sharply with its bright green stems and leaves. This plant belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
