Cotoneaster notabilis

Cotoneaster notabilis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Cotoneaster notabilis is relatively hardy and adaptable to various soil types, making it a versatile plant for landscaping.
