Costus sinningiiflorus

Costus sinningiiflorus, commonly known as the laceleaf costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Costaceae and is characterized by its ornamental, feather-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces striking, spiky inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for those seeking a tropical touch in their landscaping.
