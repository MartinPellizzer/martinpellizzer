Cnicus suffultus

Cnicus suffultus, commonly known as the sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy and gravelly soils. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in the spring. Its leaves are lobed and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cnicus suffultus is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to harsh, dry environments.
