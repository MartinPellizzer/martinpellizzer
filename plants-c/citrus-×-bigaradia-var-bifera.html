Citrus × bigaradia var. bifera

Citrus × bigaradia var. bifera, commonly known as the sour orange.

This hybrid citrus plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits that are typically green when mature. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in warm climates.

It is often cultivated for its fruit, which is used in cooking and traditional medicine.
