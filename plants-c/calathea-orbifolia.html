Calathea orbifolia

Calathea orbifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that display intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and rust-colored markings. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea orbifolia is known for its ability to purify air and thrive in humid environments.
