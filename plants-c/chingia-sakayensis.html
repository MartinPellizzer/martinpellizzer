Chingia sakayensis

Chingia sakayensis, commonly known as the Sakayen orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stem and delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the spring and early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish.
