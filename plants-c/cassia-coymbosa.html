Cassia coymbosa

Cassia coymbosa, commonly known as flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia coymbosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
