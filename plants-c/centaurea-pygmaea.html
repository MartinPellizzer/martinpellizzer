Centaurea pygmaea

Centaurea pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features bright blue or purple flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Centaurea pygmaea is often found in rocky or disturbed habitats, such as roadsides and dry fields.
