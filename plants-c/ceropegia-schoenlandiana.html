Ceropegia schoenlandiana

Ceropegia schoenlandiana, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
