Cynoglossum albanicum

Cynoglossum albanicum, commonly known as the Albanian tongue-shaped plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Balkan region.

It features basal leaves that are broad, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in late summer, which are arranged in a loose cluster at the end of each stem. Its flowering period makes it a valuable addition to gardens and wildflower habitats.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
