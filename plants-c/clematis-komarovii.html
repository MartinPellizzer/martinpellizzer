Clematis komarovii

Clematis komarovii, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its vigorous climbing habit makes it suitable for trellises or fences, while its ornamental value adds beauty to gardens.

Clematis komarovii is also valued for its adaptability and relatively low maintenance requirements.
