Centaurea soluntina

Centaurea soluntina, commonly known as the Solontine thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea soluntina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
