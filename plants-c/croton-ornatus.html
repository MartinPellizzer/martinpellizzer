Croton ornatus

Croton ornatus, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red bracts that surround its small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton ornatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
