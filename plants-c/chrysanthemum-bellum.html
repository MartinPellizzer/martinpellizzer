Chrysanthemum bellum

Chrysanthemum bellum, commonly known as the bell chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which typically range in color from white to yellow and sometimes pink. The plant features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It blooms from late summer into early autumn, producing clusters of small, tubular flowers.

Chrysanthemum bellum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
