Clematis tongluensis var. tongluensis

Clematis tongluensis var. tongluensis, commonly known as the Tonglu clematis, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are typically covered in fine, rusty-colored hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and hardiness.
