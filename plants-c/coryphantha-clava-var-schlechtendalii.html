Coryphantha clava var. schlechtendalii

Coryphantha clava var. schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Schlechtendalii coryphantha, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a short, thick stem and dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
