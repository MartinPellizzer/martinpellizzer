Centaurea debeauxii subsp. endressii

Centaurea debeauxii subsp.

endressii, commonly known as the Endress's knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow to orange flowers.

The species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is known for its composite flower heads. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.
