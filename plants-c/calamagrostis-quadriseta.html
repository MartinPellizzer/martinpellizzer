Calamagrostis quadriseta

Calamagrostis quadriseta, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, silver-blue blades that are finely textured and often have a frosted appearance. The plant produces feathery, silver-green panicles that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Calamagrostis quadriseta is often used in landscaping for its elegant, upright growth habit and ornamental value.
