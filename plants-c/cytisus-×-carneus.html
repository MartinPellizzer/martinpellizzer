Cytisus × carneus

Cytisus × carneus, commonly known as the hybrid Scotch broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the British Isles.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens but can be invasive in some regions.
