Callianthemum insigne var. miyabeanum

Callianthemum insigne var. miyabeanum, commonly known as the Miyabe's snowbell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Ranunculaceae and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 cm, with hairy stems and trifoliate leaves. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded woodland areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to rock gardens and shady borders.
