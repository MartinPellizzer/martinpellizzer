Citrus × aurantium var. umbilicata

Citrus × aurantium var. umbilicata, commonly known as the pomelo or Chinese grapefruit, is a citrus species characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruit.

The plant is a small evergreen shrub or tree with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically yellow or orange in color, with a sweet and tangy flavor. It is native to Southeast Asia and is widely cultivated for its fruit and essential oils.

The plant thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
