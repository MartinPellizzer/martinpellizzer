Cyrtochilum phylloglossum

Cyrtochilum phylloglossum, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a compact, low-growing habit. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

Cyrtochilum phylloglossum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
