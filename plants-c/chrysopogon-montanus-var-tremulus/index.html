Chrysopogon Montanus Var. Tremulus

Chrysopogon montanus var. tremulus is a rare and delicate grass species native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. It is known for its slender, drooping stems and fine, silvery-green foliage that shimmers in the sunlight. This variety thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountain meadows and rocky slopes. Its unique appearance makes it a prized subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Due to its limited habitat and environmental threats, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
