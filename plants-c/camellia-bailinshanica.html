Camellia bailinshanica

Camellia bailinshanica, commonly known as the Bailinshan camellia, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Camellia bailinshanica is valued for its horticultural beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
