Chlorophytum micranthum

Chlorophytum micranthum, commonly known as the miniature spider plant, is a compact, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a slight blue or silver tint. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soil.

Chlorophytum micranthum is a popular choice for rock gardens and containers due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
