Connarus verruculosus

Connarus verruculosus, commonly known as the warty connarus, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Anacardiaceae.

It is characterized by its woody, shrub-like structure and is native to regions in Africa and Southeast Asia. Connarus verruculosus is adapted to humid, tropical environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
