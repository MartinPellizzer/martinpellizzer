Chlorophytum mannii

Chlorophytum mannii, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale blue flowers. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Chlorophytum mannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
