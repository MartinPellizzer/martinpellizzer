Calathea undulata

Calathea undulata, commonly known as the pleated calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, wavy, and pleated leaves that display vibrant color patterns, often in shades of green, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Calathea undulata requires consistent moisture and high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.
