Carissa brownii var. ovata

Carissa brownii var. ovata, commonly known as the Natal plumbago, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, blooming throughout the year in some regions. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.
