Citrus × suhuiensis

Citrus × suhuiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan citrus or Sichuan mandarin.

It is a hybrid citrus species native to China, particularly found in the Sichuan province. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with leaves that are broad and ovate in shape. It produces small, round fruits that are typically orange in color, though they may vary in hue depending on the variety.

The fruit contains a high concentration of essential oils and is valued for both its aromatic properties and culinary uses.
