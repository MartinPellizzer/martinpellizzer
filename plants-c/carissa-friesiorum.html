Carissa friesiorum

Carissa friesiorum, commonly known as the yellow carissa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Apocynaceae and is characterized by its woody shrub structure with thick, spiny branches. Carissa friesiorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
