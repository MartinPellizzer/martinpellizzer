Chionanthus microstigma

Chionanthus microstigma, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually gland-dotted and have a slightly hairy texture. Its fruit is a small, capsule that splits open to release tiny, winged seeds.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens.
