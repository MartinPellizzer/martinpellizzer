Crossandra stenostachya

Crossandra stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed flame flower, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Crossandra stenostachya prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
