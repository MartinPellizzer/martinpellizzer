Cynometra bauhiniifolia var. meridiana

Cynometra bauhiniifolia var. meridiana, commonly known as the red sandalwood tree, is a member of the Rubiaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. This species is valued for its hard, aromatic wood, which has been historically used in traditional crafts and as a source of incense.
