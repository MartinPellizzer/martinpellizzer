Cyperus dubius var. coloratus

Cyperus dubius var. coloratus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, cylindrical leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves often display a striking purple or reddish hue, especially under certain light conditions.

This variation makes it a distinctive and visually appealing species in its native habitat.
