Cotoneaster meiophyllus

Cotoneaster meiophyllus, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a versatile plant for landscaping.
