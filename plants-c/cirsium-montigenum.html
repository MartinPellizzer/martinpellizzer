Cirsium montigenum

Cirsium montigenum, commonly known as the alpine thistle, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, purple to violet flowering heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are generally lance-shaped with serrated edges and covered in fine hairs.

Cirsium montigenum plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
