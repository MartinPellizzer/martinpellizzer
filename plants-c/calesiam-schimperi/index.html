Calesiam Schimperi

Calesiam schimperi is a rare and fascinating plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, known for its striking and often colorful foliage. This species is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in the early morning. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
