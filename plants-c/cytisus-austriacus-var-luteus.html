Cytisus austriacus var. luteus

Cytisus austriacus var. luteus, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, often thorned stems and small, trifoliate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
