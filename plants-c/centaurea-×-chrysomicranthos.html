Centaurea × chrysomicranthos

Centaurea × chrysomicranthos, commonly known as the golden centaury, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and spiny, bristly foliage. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed heads that are often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
