Crambe juncea var. glabrata

Crambe juncea var. glabrata, commonly known as smooth crambe, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cress plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, as well as yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, round seeds that are often used in industrial applications.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for studying plant morphology and adaptation.
