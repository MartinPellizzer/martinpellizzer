Ceratocephala testiculata

Ceratocephala testiculata, commonly known as the testicle cactus, is a unique species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, testicle-like fruits that are covered in soft, white spines. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces small, yellow flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting well to poor soil conditions.

The plant's morphology and reproductive structures make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
