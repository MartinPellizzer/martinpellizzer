Crotalaria cylindrica subsp. afrorientalis

Crotalaria cylindrica subsp.

afrorientalis, commonly known as the African sicklepod, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes.

The plant produces distinctive, elongated seed pods that turn brown as they mature. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
