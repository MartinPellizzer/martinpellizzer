Carthamus × vinyalsii

Carthamus × vinyalsii, commonly known as the hybrid sunflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a cross between Carthamus tinctorius (safflower) and Carthamus oxyacanthus (yellow gum plant). The plant is characterized by its tall, branching stems and vibrant yellow to orange ray flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it an attractive ornamental plant with potential for medicinal use.
