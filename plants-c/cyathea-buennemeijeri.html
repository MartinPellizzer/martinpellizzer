Cyathea buennemeijeri

Cyathea buennemeijeri, commonly known as the Buentenmeijer's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant exhibits a unique growth pattern, with the trunk developing a fibrous, woody structure over time. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its dramatic appearance in its natural habitat.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and plays a role in the ecological structure of its native forests.
