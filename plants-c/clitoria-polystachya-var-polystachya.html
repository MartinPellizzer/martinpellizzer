Clitoria polystachya var. polystachya

Clitoria polystachya var. polystachya, commonly known as the winged bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped purple flowers and compound leaves with finely serrated edges. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and nutritional purposes.

Its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil makes it a valuable crop in sustainable agriculture.
