Cyclospermum leptophyllum var. multisectum

Cyclospermum leptophyllum var. multisectum, commonly known as the multi-segmented cyclospermum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and it produces small, spherical fruits known as cypsela.
