Cirsium aomorense

Cirsium aomorense, commonly known as the Aomori thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forests and stream sides. It has a perennial growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Its botanical features make it an important species in its native ecosystem.
