Coelogyne micholitzii

Coelogyne micholitzii, commonly known as the Micholitz's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne micholitzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
