Cattleya × schunkiana

Cattleya × schunkiana, commonly known as the Schunk's orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically produces blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple, with a prominent lip that is often a different color. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often grown for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
