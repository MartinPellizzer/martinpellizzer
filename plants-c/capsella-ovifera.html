Capsella ovifera

Capsella ovifera, commonly known as the kidneyweed or false shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, featuring basal leaves that are kidney-shaped and hairy. Its flowers are small, yellow, and borne in racemes, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Capsella ovifera is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
