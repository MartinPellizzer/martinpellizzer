Crassula bartlettii

Crassula bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
