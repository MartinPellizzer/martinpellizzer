Callistemon buseanus

Callistemon buseanus, commonly known as the Busean bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are usually grey-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings.
