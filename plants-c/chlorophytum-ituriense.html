Chlorophytum ituriense

Chlorophytum ituriense, commonly known as the Ituri spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. This plant is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, grass-like leaves. It produces white or pale green flowers that are often hidden among the foliage.

Chlorophytum ituriense is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and is relatively easy to care for in cultivation.
