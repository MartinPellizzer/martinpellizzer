Codonopsis deltoidea

Codonopsis deltoidea, commonly known as the delta bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has hairy, lance-shaped leaves. Its tubular flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
