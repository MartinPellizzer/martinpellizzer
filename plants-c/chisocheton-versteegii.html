Chisocheton versteegii

Chisocheton versteegii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 15 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, simple leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing several seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests and secondary growth areas.
