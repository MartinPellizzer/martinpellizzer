Cryptocoryne aponogetifolia

Cryptocoryne aponogetifolia, commonly known as the dwarf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, broad leaves that often display a variegated pattern. This plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft, slightly acidic water. It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ability to help maintain water clarity.

Cryptocoryne aponogetifolia is also known for its resilience and ease of care, making it a favored choice among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
