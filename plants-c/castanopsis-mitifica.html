Castanopsis mitifica

Castanopsis mitifica, commonly known as the sacred chestnut, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and other chestnut species. Due to its ecological importance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
