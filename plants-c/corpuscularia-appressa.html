Corpuscularia appressa

Corpuscularia appressa, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, mound-like growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
