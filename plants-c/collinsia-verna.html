Collinsia verna

Collinsia verna, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a delicate annual plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Plantaginaceae family and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Collinsia verna plays an important role in early-season pollination by attracting bees and other pollinators.
