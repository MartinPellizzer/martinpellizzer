Citrus × aurantium var. colla

Citrus × aurantium var. colla, commonly known as the pomelo or sometimes the Chinese grapefruit, is a citrus plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruits that can weigh several kilograms. The plant produces fragrant white flowers and has evergreen leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its fruit is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The variety is valued for its high juice content and mild flavor compared to other citrus fruits.
