Chikusichloa

Chikusichloa, commonly known as blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and striking blue-gray foliage. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Chikusichloa is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
