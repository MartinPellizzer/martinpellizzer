Clidemia pubescens

Clidemia pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
