Centaurea ossethica

Centaurea ossethica, commonly known as the Ossethian star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its spiky, purple-blue flowers and bristly foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its inflorescence consists of a single, tall central flower surrounded by smaller, secondary flowers.

Centaurea ossethica is often found in steppe and mountainous areas, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
