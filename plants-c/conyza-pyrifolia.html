Conyza pyrifolia

Conyza pyrifolia, commonly known as the flame weed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Conyza pyrifolia is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
