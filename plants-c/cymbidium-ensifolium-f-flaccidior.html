Cymbidium ensifolium f. flaccidior

Cymbidium ensifolium f. flaccidior, commonly known as the flaccid leafed orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

This cultivar is characterized by its long, slender leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It is a hybrid form of Cymbidium ensifolium, known for its delicate appearance and relatively slow growth rate. The plant prefers cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a prized addition to orchid collections.
