Cyrtandra × trifida

Cyrtandra × trifida, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Cyrtandra pedata and Cyrtandra tetragona, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories in tropical and subtropical regions.

Cyrtandra × trifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
