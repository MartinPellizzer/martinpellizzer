Costus viridis

Costus viridis, commonly known as green costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, cylindrical rhizomes and narrow, sword-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and arranged in spikes, making it a striking ornamental plant.
