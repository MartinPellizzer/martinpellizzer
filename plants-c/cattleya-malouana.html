Cattleya malouana

Cattleya malouana, commonly known as the Malouan orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, featuring vibrant pink and white petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence is a raceme of fragrant blooms that bloom in late summer or early autumn.

Cattleya malouana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
