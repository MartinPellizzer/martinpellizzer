Chascanum pinnatifidum var. pinnatifidum

Chascanum pinnatifidum var. pinnatifidum, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has strap-like leaves that are narrow and pointed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage structure.
