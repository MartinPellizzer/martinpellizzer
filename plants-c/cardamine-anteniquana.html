Cardamine anteniquana

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes many aquatic and semi-aquatic plants. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately divided, and often have a waxy coating.
