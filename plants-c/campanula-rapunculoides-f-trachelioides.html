Campanula rapunculoides f. trachelioides

Campanula rapunculoides f. trachelioides, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to violet, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This cultivar is noted for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation in temperate climates.
