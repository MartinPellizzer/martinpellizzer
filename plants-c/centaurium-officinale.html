Centaurium officinale

Centaurium officinale, commonly known as lesser centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features slender stems that are often reddish in color and bear small, clustered flowers with five petals. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Centaurium officinale is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
