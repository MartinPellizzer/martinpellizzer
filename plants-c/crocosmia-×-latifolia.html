Crocosmia × latifolia

Crocosmia × latifolia, commonly known as the Montbretia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots that help it survive in various climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Crocosmia × latifolia is also known for its ability to naturalize and spread readily in suitable conditions.
