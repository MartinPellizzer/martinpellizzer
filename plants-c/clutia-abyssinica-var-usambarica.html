Clutia abyssinica var. usambarica

Clutia abyssinica var. usambarica, commonly known as Usambar cliffbush, is a shrub native to the Usambara Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in white or pale pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to humid, subtropical climates. Its leaves are typically leathery and have a slightly aromatic scent.

It is often found growing in coastal and mountainous regions, where it contributes to local biodiversity.
