Cistus × secallianus

Cistus × secallianus, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the heath family (Ericaceae) and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.

Cistus × secallianus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
