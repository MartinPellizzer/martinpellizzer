Chaetostoma cupressinum

Chaetostoma cupressinum, commonly known as the Mexican pineapple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, columnar stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces edible, pineapple-scented fruits that are often consumed fresh or used in culinary applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and fragrance make it a popular ornamental plant.
