Cotyledon papillosa

Cotyledon papillosa, commonly known as the velvet cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Cotyledon papillosa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
