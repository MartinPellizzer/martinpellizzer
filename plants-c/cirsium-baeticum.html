Cirsium baeticum

Cirsium baeticum, commonly known as the Iberian thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has deeply lobed, serrated leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands.

Cirsium baeticum plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing food and habitat for various insects and small animals.
