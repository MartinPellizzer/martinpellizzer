Chlorophytum pterocaulon

Chlorophytum pterocaulon, commonly known as the Cape ivy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. Chlorophytum pterocaulon is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a ground cover due to its vigorous growth and ornamental appeal.
