Cavendishia spectabilis

Cavendishia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cavendishia, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences that display a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant features rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a central cup, which can collect rainwater. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.

Cavendishia spectabilis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
