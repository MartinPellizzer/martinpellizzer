Cistus creticus var. eriocephalus

Cistus creticus var. eriocephalus, commonly known as the Cretan rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has woody stems and hairy leaves, adapted to thrive in dry, rocky environments. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
