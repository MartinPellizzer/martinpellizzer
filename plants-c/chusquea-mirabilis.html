Chusquea mirabilis

Chusquea mirabilis, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is often found in cloud forests or rocky slopes.

Chusquea mirabilis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique aesthetic appeal.
