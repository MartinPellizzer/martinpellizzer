Cucumis tuberosus

Cucumis tuberosus, commonly known as the wild cucumber or horned cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant produces long, spiny fruits that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The plant is often grown for its edible fruits, which have a mild, cucumber-like flavor.
