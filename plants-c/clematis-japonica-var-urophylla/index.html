Clematis Japonica Var. Urophylla

Clematis japonica var. urophylla is a captivating flowering vine native to eastern Asia. It is known for its striking, showy blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. This variety is prized for its delicate pink to white flowers and its ability to climb and cover trellises or fences. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a versatile addition to gardens. Its ornamental value and relatively easy care make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
