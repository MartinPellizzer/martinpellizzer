Caladium bicolor var. rubellum

Caladium bicolor var. rubellum, commonly known as the flame leaf caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant red, orange, and white patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

It is typically grown as an annual in regions with cold winters.
