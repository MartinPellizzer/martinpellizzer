Cotoneaster conspicuus var. nanus

Cotoneaster conspicuus var. nanus, commonly known as the dwarf cotoneaster, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and low maintenance requirements.
