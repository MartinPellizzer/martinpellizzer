Conophytum cupreatum

Conophytum cupreatum, commonly known as the red-flowered conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, leaf-like structures that store water. The plant produces small, vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
