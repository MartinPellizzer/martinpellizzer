Calea politii

Calea politii, commonly known as the velvetleaf calea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, velvety leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. Calea politii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its unique appearance and medicinal properties make it a subject of interest in both botanical and ethnobotanical studies.
