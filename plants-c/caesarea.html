Caesarea

Caesarea, commonly known as the coastal morning glory, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It is often found growing along dunes and rocky shores.

Caesarea is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
