Crassula setulosa var. rubra

Crassula setulosa var. rubra, commonly known as the Ruby Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically red or pink in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring or summer. It is well-suited for indoor growing and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for beginners.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a favored ornamental plant in gardens and collections.
