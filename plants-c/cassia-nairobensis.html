Cassia nairobensis

Cassia nairobensis, commonly known as the Nairobi cassia, is a shrub or small tree native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, flat pods containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Cassia nairobensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
