Crassula deltoidea

Crassula deltoidea, commonly known as the diamond plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula deltoidea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
