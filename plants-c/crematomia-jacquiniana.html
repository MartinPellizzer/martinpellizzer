Crematomia jacquiniana

Crematomia jacquiniana, commonly known as the Jacquin's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Cactaceae family, characterized by its spiny stems and clustered flowers. Crematomia jacquiniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
