Calophyllum augia

Calophyllum augia, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, yellowish wood. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
