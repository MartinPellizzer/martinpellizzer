Chlorophytum laxiflorum

Chlorophytum laxiflorum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with long, arching leaves that are typically green with a white or silver stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks, which bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a popular houseplant.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
