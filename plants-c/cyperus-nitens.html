Cyperus nitens

Cyperus nitens, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and ponds, and can thrive in both freshwater and brackish environments. It has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Cyperus nitens is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive, grass-like appearance and adaptability to various water conditions.
