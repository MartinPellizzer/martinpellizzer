Cruciata coronata var. glaberrima

Cruciata coronata var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth-flowered cruciata, is a variant of the cruciata species native to certain regions of South America.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and glabrous, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish, with a subtle fragrance.

This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.
