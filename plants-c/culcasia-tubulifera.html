Culcasia tubulifera

Culcasia tubulifera, commonly known as the arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep green veins and vibrant red or orange hues. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Culcasia tubulifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes.

Its tubular roots help in anchoring the plant and storing nutrients for growth.
