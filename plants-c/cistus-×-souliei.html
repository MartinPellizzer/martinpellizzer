Cistus × souliei

Cistus × souliei, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments. Its flowering stems are often covered in fine, white hairs, which contribute to its distinctive appearance.

Cistus × souliei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
