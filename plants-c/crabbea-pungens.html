Crabbea pungens

Crabbea pungens, commonly known as the Cape bell or red bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-shaped leaves and produces showy, bell-like bracts that enclose the actual flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is adapted to grow in well-drained soil.

Crabbea pungens is also known for its ability to thrive in full sun and requires minimal water once established.
