Cryptocarya sheikelmudiyana

Cryptocarya sheikelmudiyana, commonly known as the Sri Lankan cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the wet zone of Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green on top, and have a distinctive glossy texture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
