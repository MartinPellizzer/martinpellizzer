Chionanthus niloticus

Chionanthus niloticus, commonly known as the African snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its flowering display is often accompanied by small, bell-shaped bracts that add to its ornamental appeal.

Chionanthus niloticus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens and landscapes.
