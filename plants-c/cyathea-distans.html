Cyathea distans

Cyathea distans, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

Cyathea distans plays an important ecological role in tropical forests by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
