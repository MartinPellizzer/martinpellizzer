Cleophora lontaroides

Cleophora lontaroides, commonly known as the giant pandan, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in cooking and traditional medicine due to their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental and practical value.
