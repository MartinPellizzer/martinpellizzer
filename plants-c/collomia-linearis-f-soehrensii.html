Collomia linearis f. soehrensii

Collomia linearis f. soehrensii, commonly known as the Soehrens' linear larkspur, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
