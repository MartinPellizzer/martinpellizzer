Chrysanthemum Makinoi Var. Plenum

Chrysanthemum makinoi var. plenum is a striking ornamental plant known for its dense, rounded growth habit and vibrant floral displays. Native to Japan, this variety thrives in cool climates and is prized for its ability to produce an abundance of flowers throughout the growing season. Its compact form makes it ideal for container gardening and small garden spaces. The plant's foliage is lush and green, providing a striking contrast to its bright yellow blooms. With proper care, it can become a focal point in any garden setting.
