Carex yulungshanensis

Carex yulungshanensis, commonly known as the Yulungshan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yulungshan region, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typical of sedges. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in suitable habitats.

Carex yulungshanensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and potential medicinal properties.
