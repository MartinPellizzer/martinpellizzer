Cacalia nitens

Cacalia nitens, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Cacalia nitens is valued for its adaptability and nutritional content.
