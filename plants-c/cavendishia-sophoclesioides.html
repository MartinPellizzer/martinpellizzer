Cavendishia sophoclesioides

Cavendishia sophoclesioides, commonly known as the orchid vine, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, trailing stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value.
