Cassia fraseri

Cassia fraseri, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces flat, elliptical pods that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cassia fraseri thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
