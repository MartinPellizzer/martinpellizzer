Cynoglossum subalpinum

Cynoglossum subalpinum, commonly known as alpine tongue-shaped flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
