Croton microcalyx

Croton microcalyx, commonly known as the small-leaved croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by three-lobed, orange-red fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
