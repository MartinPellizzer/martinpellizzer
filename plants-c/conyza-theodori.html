Conyza theodori

Conyza theodori, commonly known as theodori's ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 100 centimeters, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and appear in clusters, often blooming from summer to fall.

Conyza theodori is adapted to disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
