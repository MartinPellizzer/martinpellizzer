Cassia rotundifolia

Cassia rotundifolia, commonly known as sicklepod, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its rounded leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Cassia rotundifolia has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
