Croton leptostachyus

Croton leptostachyus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its attractive appearance, it is popular in tropical gardens and as a houseplant in suitable environments.
