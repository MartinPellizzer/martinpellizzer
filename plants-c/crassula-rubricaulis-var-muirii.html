Crassula rubricaulis var. muirii

Crassula rubricaulis var. muirii, commonly known as the Muir's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically red or pink in color, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
