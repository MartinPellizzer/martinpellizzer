Coleus autranii

Coleus autranii, commonly known as the Madagascar coleus, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple.

Coleus autranii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
