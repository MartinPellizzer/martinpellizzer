Cynanchum viminale subsp. welwitschii

Cynanchum viminale subsp.

welwitschii, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas.

The plant features slender, climbing stems that can reach several meters in length. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in racemes, while the leaves are opposite and ovate.
