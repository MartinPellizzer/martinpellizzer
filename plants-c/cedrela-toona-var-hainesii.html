Cedrela toona var. hainesii

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and other valuable hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and simple, alternate leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody capsules containing flat, winged seeds.
