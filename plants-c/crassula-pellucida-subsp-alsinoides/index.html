Crassula Pellucida Subsp. Alsinoides

Crassula pellucida subsp. alsinoides is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rosette-forming structure and striking blue-green foliage. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its small size, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
