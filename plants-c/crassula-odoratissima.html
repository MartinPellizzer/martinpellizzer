Crassula odoratissima

Crassula odoratissima, commonly known as the sweet-scented crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
