Chaetaria canariensis

Chaetaria canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands heath, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.
