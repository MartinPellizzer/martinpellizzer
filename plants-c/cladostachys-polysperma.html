Cladostachys polysperma

Cladostachys polysperma, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of yellow flowers that resemble showers of golden beads, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, oval fruits that contain several seeds.

Cladostachys polysperma is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
