Croton nudifolius

Croton nudifolius, commonly known as the naked-leaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that can vary in shape and texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
