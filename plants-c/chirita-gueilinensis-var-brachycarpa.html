Chirita gueilinensis var. brachycarpa

Chirita gueilinensis var. brachycarpa, commonly known as the Guangdong ivy, is a perennial herb native to southern China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
