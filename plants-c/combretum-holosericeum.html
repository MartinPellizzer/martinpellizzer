Combretum holosericeum

Combretum holosericeum, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. Its bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or greyish hue.

Combretum holosericeum is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
