Ceratominthe

Ceratominthe, commonly known as the horned lettuce, is a leafy green plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves that often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Ceratominthe is native to Europe and is often grown as a decorative and edible plant.

Its leaves are typically harvested before the flowers appear for optimal flavor and texture.
