Commelina polyspatha

Commelina polyspatha, commonly known as the broad-leaved spiderwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, typical of the Commelina genus. The plant has heart-shaped or ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog gardens.
