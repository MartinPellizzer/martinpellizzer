Calanthe × yarlungzangboensis

Calanthe × yarlungzangboensis, commonly known as the Yarlung Zangbo Calanthe, is a hybrid orchid native to the Himalayan region of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of white to pale pink flowers with prominent yellow lip petals. It thrives in cool, moist environments at high altitudes, often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
