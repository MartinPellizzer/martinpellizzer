Cassia neesiana var. laxiracemosa

Cassia neesiana var. laxiracemosa, commonly known as the slender cassia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces yellow, fragrant flowers arranged in long, drooping racemes, which bloom during the dry season. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local medicine.
