Crassula watermeyeri

Crassula watermeyeri, commonly known as the Watermeyeri Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.

Crassula watermeyeri prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight for optimal growth.
