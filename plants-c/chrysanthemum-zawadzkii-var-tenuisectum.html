Chrysanthemum zawadzkii var. tenuisectum

Chrysanthemum zawadzkii var. tenuisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed chrysanthemum, is a cultivar native to China.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers. This variety is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring moderate water and regular pruning for optimal blooming.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both landscape design and floral arrangements.
