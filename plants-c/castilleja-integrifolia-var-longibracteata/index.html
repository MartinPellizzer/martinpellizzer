Castilleja Integrifolia Var. Longibracteata

Castilleja integrifolia var. longibracteata is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. This hemiparasitic plant thrives in dry, open habitats across western North America. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a fascinating subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts. The variety longibracteata is distinguished by its elongated bracts and dense inflorescences. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes.
