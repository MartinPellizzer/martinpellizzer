Clitoria velutina

Clitoria velutina, commonly known as the velvet bean or butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces vibrant purple or blue pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and is often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is cultivated for its seeds, which are rich in protein.

The flowers are also popular in ornamental gardening and natural dye production.
