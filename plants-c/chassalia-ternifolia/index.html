Chassalia Ternifolia

Chassalia ternifolia is a striking member of the Asteraceae family known for its vibrant, daisy-like blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils. Its delicate leaves and bright yellow flowers make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. The plant's unique structure and adaptability to harsh environments contribute to its ecological significance. With its elegant appearance and resilience, Chassalia ternifolia stands out as a remarkable addition to any garden or natural landscape.
