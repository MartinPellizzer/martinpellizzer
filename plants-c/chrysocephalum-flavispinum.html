Chrysocephalum flavispinum

Chrysocephalum flavispinum, commonly known as the yellow spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow to golden-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. It has a low, spreading habit with wiry stems and small, linear leaves.
