Ceropegia petrophila

Ceropegia petrophila, commonly known as the rock ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and distinctive, tubular flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are typically thick and fleshy, which helps it store water.

Ceropegia petrophila is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
