Cryptangium humile

Cryptangium humile, commonly known as the small cryptangium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its small, clustered white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight aromatic scent.
