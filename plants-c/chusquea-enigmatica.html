Chusquea enigmatica

Chusquea enigmatica, commonly known as the enigmatic bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and tall, slender culms that can reach up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Chusquea enigmatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
