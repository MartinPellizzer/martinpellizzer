Celosia argentea var. mumbaiana

Celosia argentea var. mumbaiana, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a striking ornamental plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a silvery-white powdery coating, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping and as a decorative plant in tropical regions.
