Cycas mindanaensis

Cycas mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao cycad, is a member of the Cycadaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is a gymnosperm with a distinctive appearance, featuring a stout, unbranched trunk and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants, and it has a long lifespan, often living over 100 years.

Cycas mindanaensis is adapted to tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.
