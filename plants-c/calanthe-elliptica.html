Calanthe elliptica

Calanthe elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic calanthe, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or white, with a distinctive lip petal. Calanthe elliptica thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
