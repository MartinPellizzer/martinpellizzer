Crocodilodes setiferum

Crocodilodes setiferum, commonly known as the woolly crocodilodes, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
