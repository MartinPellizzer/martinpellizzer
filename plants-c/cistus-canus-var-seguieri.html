Cistus canus var. seguieri

Cistus canus var. seguieri, commonly known as the Seguier's rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features silver-green foliage with glandular hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in spring. It is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic leaves and resilience to drought make it a popular choice in Mediterranean gardens.
