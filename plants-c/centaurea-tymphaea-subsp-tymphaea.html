Centaurea tymphaea subsp. tymphaea

Centaurea tymphaea subsp.

tymphaea, commonly known as the Thymphaea centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.
