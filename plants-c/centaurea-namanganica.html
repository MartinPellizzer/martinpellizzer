Centaurea namanganica

Centaurea namanganica, commonly known as Namangan thistle, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and resilient species in its native region.
