Columnea elongatifolia

Columnea elongatifolia, commonly known as the elongated flame flower, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and slightly hairy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique floral structure makes it an attractive choice for hanging baskets or trained on trellises.
