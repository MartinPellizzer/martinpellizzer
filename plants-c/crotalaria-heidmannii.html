Crotalaria heidmannii

Crotalaria heidmannii, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces bright yellow, spurred flowers typical of the Crotalaria genus. The plant has compound leaves with pairs of elliptical leaflets and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

The plant is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
