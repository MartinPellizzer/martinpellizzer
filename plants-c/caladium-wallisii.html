Caladium wallisii

Caladium wallisii, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial known for its striking, heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to the Caribbean and Central America. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. Its foliage comes in various shades of green, red, and bronze, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
