Croton sloanei

Croton sloanei, commonly known as the Sloane's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its vibrant foliage.
