Caiophora pterosperma

Caiophora pterosperma, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub with upright stems and broad, waxy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Caiophora pterosperma is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
