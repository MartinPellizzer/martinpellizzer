Cyrtopodium witeckii

Cyrtopodium witeckii, commonly known as the Witeck's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Cyrtopodium witeckii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
