Cactus variamamma

Cactus variamamma, commonly known as the variable cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of North America.

It features a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. The plant produces spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss through transpiration.

Cactus variamamma is often used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and unique appearance.
