Chrysanthemum brachyglossum

Chrysanthemum brachyglossum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, white, and orange. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Chrysanthemum brachyglossum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
