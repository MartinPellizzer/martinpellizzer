Coleus megadontus

Coleus megadontus, commonly known as the giant-lobed coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves that often display vibrant colors such as red, purple, and orange. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or ovate with serrated edges, and they can grow quite large, sometimes reaching up to 15 cm in length. This plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and prefers well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.
