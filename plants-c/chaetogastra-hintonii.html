Chaetogastra hintonii

Chaetogastra hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's thorn.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Jamaica and Cuba. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
