Cotula minima

Cotula minima, commonly known as dwarf daisy or little daisy, is a small flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other flowering plants. Cotula minima typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters, with small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is cultivated as a garden plant for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
