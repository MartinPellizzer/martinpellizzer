Clitoria pubescens var. amphigona

Clitoria pubescens var. amphigona, commonly known as the purple wisteria, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, twining stems and heart-shaped, velvety green leaves. The plant produces striking purple or violet pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, pod-like fruits that split open when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
