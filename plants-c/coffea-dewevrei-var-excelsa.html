Coffea dewevrei var. excelsa

Coffea dewevrei var. excelsa, commonly known as the African coffee tree, is a species native to the highland regions of Ethiopia and neighboring countries.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with glossy dark green leaves and white flowers. The plant produces red berries containing two seeds, which are used to make coffee. It thrives in tropical climates with high rainfall and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique flavor profile, it is considered a rare and valuable variety in the coffee industry.
