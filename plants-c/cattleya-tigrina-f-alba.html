Cattleya tigrina f. alba

Cattleya tigrina f. alba, commonly known as the White Tiger Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid variety characterized by its pure white petals and vibrant yellow lip.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its flowers typically bloom in late summer or early fall, producing a dramatic display that resembles a tiger's stripe pattern, albeit in white and yellow. The plant is known for its strong fragrance and is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding species for advanced growers.
