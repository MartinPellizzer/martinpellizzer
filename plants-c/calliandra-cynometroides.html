Calliandra cynometroides

Calliandra cynometroides, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery, pink to red inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

Additionally, it is sometimes used in erosion control due to its extensive root system.
