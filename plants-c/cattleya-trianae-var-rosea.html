Cattleya trianae var. rosea

Cattleya trianae var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, rose-like blooms.

It features large, fragrant flowers with a pinkish-red hue and white accents, often resembling roses in appearance. Native to the Andes Mountains, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

The variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
