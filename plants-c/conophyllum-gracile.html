Conophyllum gracile

Conophyllum gracile, commonly known as the slender conophyllum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow in color. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often arranged in opposite pairs along the stems.

Conophyllum gracile is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.
