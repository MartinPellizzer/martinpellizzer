Caryopteris

Caryopteris, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Lamiaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is characterized by its aromatic, often fragrant leaves and showy, blue or purple flowers. Caryopteris species are valued in gardens for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun with well-drained soil.
