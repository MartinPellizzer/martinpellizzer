Crambe strigosa var. glabrata

Crambe strigosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth crambe, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are typically smooth and alternate, and the plant can grow up to 1 meter in height.

It is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.
