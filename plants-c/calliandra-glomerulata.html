Calliandra glomerulata

Calliandra glomerulata, commonly known as the clustered calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflorescences, which are dense clusters of bright red or orange flowers. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental and agroforestry purposes.

Additionally, it is sometimes used in traditional medicine due to its potential pharmacological properties.
