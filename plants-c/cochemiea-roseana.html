Cochemiea roseana

Cochemiea roseana, commonly known as the pink hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a low-growing, spineless cactus with a compact, globular shape. The plant features pale green to pinkish stems that are covered in small, white tubercles. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Cochemiea roseana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
