Croton gossweileri

Croton gossweileri, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in coastal areas or near riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant in suitable climates.
