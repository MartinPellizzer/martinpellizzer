Coelogyne Dempoensis

Coelogyne dempoensis is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. This species is known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its beauty and scarcity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts worldwide. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and over-collection.
