Cymbidium linearisepalum f. atrovirens

Cymbidium linearisepalum f. atrovirens, commonly known as the dark green sword orchid, is a hybrid orchid species characterized by its elongated, sword-like leaves.

It produces racemes of fragrant, pendulous flowers with a striking combination of dark green and yellow hues. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright indirect light. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and moderate watering.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
