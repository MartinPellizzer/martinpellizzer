Cleisostoma inflatum

Cleisostoma inflatum, commonly known as the inflated hooded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, inflated, hood-like petals that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cleisostoma inflatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
