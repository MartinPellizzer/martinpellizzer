Crassula revolvens

Crassula revolvens, commonly known as the rolling crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is a compact, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Crassula revolvens is often grown as a decorative houseplant or in rock gardens due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
