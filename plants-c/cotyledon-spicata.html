Cotyledon spicata

Cotyledon spicata, commonly known as the spike cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges. The plant produces tall, branched flowering stems with small, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with full sun exposure.

Cotyledon spicata is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
