Ctenomeria kraussiana

Ctenomeria kraussiana, commonly known as the Cape mountain heath, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features small, leathery leaves and produces clusters of white or pink flowers during the spring and summer seasons. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
