Centranthus calcitrapae var. donatii

Centranthus calcitrapae var. donatii, commonly known as the Donatii's red campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-gray hue, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained, sunny conditions.
