Crassula crenulata

Crassula crenulata, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are oval-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula crenulata is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive appearance.
