Crinum coccineum

Crinum coccineum, commonly known as the red-flowered crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped red flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has strap-like, evergreen leaves that form a dense rosette around the bulb. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum coccineum is also known for its strong, fragrant scent, which enhances its ornamental appeal.
