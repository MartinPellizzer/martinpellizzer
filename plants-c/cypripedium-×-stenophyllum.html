Cypripedium × stenophyllum

Cypripedium × stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers with a pouch-like lip and a single, upright spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or wooded slopes. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, and it produces a single flower per stem, usually in late spring or early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral appearance.
