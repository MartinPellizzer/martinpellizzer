Cenocline albida

Cenocline albida, commonly known as the white cenocline, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
