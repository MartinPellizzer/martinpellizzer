Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. sibiricum

Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. sibiricum, commonly known as Siberian golden moss, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the colder regions of Asia.

It features dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat.
