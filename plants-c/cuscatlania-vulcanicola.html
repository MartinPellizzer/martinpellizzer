Cuscatlania vulcanicola

Cuscatlania vulcanicola, commonly known as the fire-ash fern, is a rare plant species adapted to thrive in volcanic soils.

It possesses a unique root system that allows it to absorb minerals from nutrient-poor, ash-rich environments. The plant exhibits a compact, low-growing structure with dark green, leathery leaves that help retain moisture. Its reproductive structures are specialized for wind pollination, typical of many ferns found in harsh climates.

Cuscatlania vulcanicola is a symbol of resilience, often found in areas with recent volcanic activity.
