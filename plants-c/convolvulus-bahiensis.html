Convolvulus bahiensis

Convolvulus bahiensis, commonly known as Bahia bindweed, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through runners make it an invasive species in some areas.
