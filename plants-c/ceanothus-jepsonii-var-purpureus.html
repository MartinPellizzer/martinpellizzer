Ceanothus jepsonii var. purpureus

Ceanothus jepsonii var. purpureus, commonly known as the purple ceanothus, is a flowering shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.

Its unique coloration and adaptability make it a distinctive species within the Ceanothus genus.
