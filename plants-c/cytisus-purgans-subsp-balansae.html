Cytisus purgans subsp. balansae

Cytisus purgans subsp.

balansae, commonly known as the Balansae broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands. It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native habitat.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens in similar climates.
