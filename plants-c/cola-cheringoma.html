Cola cheringoma

Cola cheringoma, commonly known as the African cola tree, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces berries that contain caffeine and other alkaloids, making it economically significant. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their stimulant properties.
