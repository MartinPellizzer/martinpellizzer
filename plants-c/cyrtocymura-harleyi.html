Cyrtocymura harleyi

Cyrtocymura harleyi, commonly known as the Harley's cycad, is a species of cycad native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feathery, compound leaves. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
