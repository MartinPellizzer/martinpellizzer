Commelina elegans var. hirsuta

Commelina elegans var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, which gives it its varietal name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
