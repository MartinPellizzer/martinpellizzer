Croton lanatus var. lanatus

Croton lanatus var. lanatus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a distinct yellow or orange midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is cultivated in some regions for both aesthetic and practical purposes.
