Cyclobalanus encleisacarpa

Cyclobalanus encleisacarpa, commonly known as the island pandanus, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the family Pandanaceae and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves that form a rosette at the base.
