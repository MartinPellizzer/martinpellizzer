Camellia sasanqua var. fujikoana

Camellia sasanqua var. fujikoana, commonly known as Fujikoana camellia, is a compact evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features broad, glossy dark green leaves and produces showy, semi-double pink flowers in late autumn to early winter. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

This variety is also noted for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of climates.
