Citrus × acida

Citrus × acida, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a hybrid citrus species native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its thick, peels and high acidity. The plant produces small, round fruits with a yellowish-orange color and a very tart flavor. It is often used as a rootstock in commercial citrus cultivation due to its hardiness and disease resistance.

The leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a slightly serrated edge.
