Cenolophon mollissimum

Cenolophon mollissimum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Cenolophon mollissimum is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
