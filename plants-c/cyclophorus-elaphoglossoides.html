Cyclophorus elaphoglossoides

Cyclophorus elaphoglossoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a fern species native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.
