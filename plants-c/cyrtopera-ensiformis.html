Cyrtopera ensiformis

Cyrtopera ensiformis, commonly known as the slender leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks. Cyrtopera ensiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
