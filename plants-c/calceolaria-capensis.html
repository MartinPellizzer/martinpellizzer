Calceolaria capensis

Calceolaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from purple to white, are adapted to attract specific pollinators.

Calceolaria capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
