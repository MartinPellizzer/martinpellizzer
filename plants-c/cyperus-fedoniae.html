Cyperus fedoniae

Cyperus fedoniae, commonly known as the Fedonia flatsedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically brown or reddish in color. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in parts of Africa and Asia.

Cyperus fedoniae thrives in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in swampy areas and along waterways.
