Chorispora iberica var. torulosa

Chorispora iberica var. torulosa, commonly known as the Iberian chorispora, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as steppes and coastal areas.
