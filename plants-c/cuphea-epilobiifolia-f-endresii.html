Cuphea epilobiifolia f. endresii

Cuphea epilobiifolia f. endresii, commonly known as the Endres' cuphea, is a compact, flowering shrub native to Mexico.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in dense clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
