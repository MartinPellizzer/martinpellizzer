Clematis koreana f. biternata

Clematis koreana f. biternata, commonly known as the Korean Clematis, is a compact shrub native to Korea.

It features attractive, palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, blue-violet flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and hardiness.
