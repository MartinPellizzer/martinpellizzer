Castalis spectabilis

Castalis spectabilis, commonly known as the showy castilleia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Castalis spectabilis is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and slopes.
