Conchograecum stolzii

Conchograecum stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii orchid, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
