Columnea scandens var. australis

Columnea scandens var. australis, commonly known as the Australian climbing columnea, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It features vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with slightly serrated edges, and it produces small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative climbing plant.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in ecological pollination systems.
