Crotalaria balansae

Crotalaria balansae, commonly known as the Balansae cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural use.

However, it can also be invasive in certain environments.
