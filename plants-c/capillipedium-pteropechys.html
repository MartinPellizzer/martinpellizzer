Capillipedium pteropechys

Capillipedium pteropechys, commonly known as the winged feather orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often near streams or in forest understories. Its flowers are small but numerous, arranged in loose clusters, and are usually a combination of white, green, and pale yellow.

Capillipedium pteropechys is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique appearance.
