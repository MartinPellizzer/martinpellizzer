Calamosagus harinifolius

Calamosagus harinifolius, commonly known as the silky feather grass, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, feathery leaves that give it a soft, delicate appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often white or pale green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in swampy or wetland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
