Cnicus medius

Cnicus medius, commonly known as the common centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers with five petals and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and waste areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stem is erect and branched.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
