Coelogyne incrassata var. valida

Coelogyne incrassata var. valida, commonly known as the thick-lobed orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with red or brown markings. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience in cultivation.
