Castilleja densiflora var. gracilis

Castilleja densiflora var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender leaf Indian paintbrush, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its bright red to orange floral bracts that resemble paintbrushes. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
