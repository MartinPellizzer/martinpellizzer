Cyclamen neapolitanum

Cyclamen neapolitanum, commonly known as the Naples cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a wavy margin and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant is characterized by its tuberous root system, which allows it to store nutrients and water. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Cyclamen neapolitanum is well-suited for indoor growing and can bloom throughout the winter months.
