Cycnoches pentadactylon

Cycnoches pentadactylon, commonly known as the white cycnoches, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant native to the wet tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features a short rhizome with several fleshy, clustered leaves. Its flowers are small, white, and fragrant, typically arranged in a dense inflorescence.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
