Canthium Macrocalyx

Canthium macrocalyx is a striking tropical plant known for its large, showy flowers and unique foliage. Native to regions across Africa and Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. Its broad, waxy leaves and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant's ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies enhances its ecological value. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure optimal growth and flowering.
