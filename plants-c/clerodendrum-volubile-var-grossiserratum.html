Clerodendrum volubile var. grossiserratum

Clerodendrum volubile var. grossiserratum, commonly known as the giant hairy clerodendrum, is a vigorous climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges, which are a distinguishing botanical characteristic. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers, followed by distinctive blue or purple berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover in suitable environments.
