Canavalia dictyota

Canavalia dictyota, commonly known as the sea beans or green bean vine, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are rich in protein and nutrients. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Canavalia dictyota thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils along shorelines.
