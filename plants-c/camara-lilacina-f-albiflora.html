Camara lilacina f. albiflora

Camara lilacina f. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered nightshade, is a variant of the common nightshade plant.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast with the typical purple or violet hues of the species. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically toxic to humans and animals.

This cultivar is often grown for its ornamental value in gardens due to its unique floral display.
