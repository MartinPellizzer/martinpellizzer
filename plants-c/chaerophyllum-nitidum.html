Chaerophyllum nitidum

Chaerophyllum nitidum, commonly known as the smooth hawksbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.

The plant's botanical features make it a valuable component of diverse ecosystems and a subject of interest in botanical studies.
