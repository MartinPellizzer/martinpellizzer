Carex peichuniana

Carex peichuniana, commonly known as the Peichun sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often wind-pollinated.
