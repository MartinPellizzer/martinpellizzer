Callitriche mandonis

Callitriche mandonis, commonly known as the water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Callitrichaceae.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Callitriche mandonis is an important part of aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and aquatic life.
