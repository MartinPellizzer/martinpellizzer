Camellia albosericea

Camellia albosericea, commonly known as the white silky camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with silky, pale yellow stamens. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Camellia albosericea is a member of the Theaceae family and is closely related to tea plants.
