Chlorophytum nidulans

Chlorophytum nidulans, commonly known as the bird's nest plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a dense, nest-like structure around the base of the plant. The leaves are typically narrow, grass-like, and have a bluish-green hue with white midribs. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor conditions.
