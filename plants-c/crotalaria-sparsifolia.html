Crotalaria sparsifolia

Crotalaria sparsifolia, commonly known as the sparsely leafy crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit, palmate leaves, and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces distinctive seed pods that split open when mature, releasing tiny seeds.
