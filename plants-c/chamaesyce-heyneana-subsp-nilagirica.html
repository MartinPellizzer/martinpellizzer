Chamaesyce heyneana subsp. nilagirica

Chamaesyce heyneana subsp.

nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India. It is a low-growing perennial herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to the humid, tropical climate of its native habitat.
