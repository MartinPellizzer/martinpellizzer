Chlorophytum bakeri

Chlorophytum bakeri, commonly known as Baker's spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by clusters of red berries. It is well adapted to grow in a variety of soil types and requires moderate watering.

Chlorophytum bakeri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
