Caryopteris paniculata

Caryopteris paniculata, commonly known as blue mist shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, blue-lavender flowers in late summer. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are gray-green in color and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Caryopteris paniculata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
