Chromatotriccum fruticicola

Chromatotriccum fruticicola, commonly known as the red-stemmed moss, is a small, low-growing moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its dark red stems and dense, cushion-like growth habit. The plant has a complex life cycle involving both gametophyte and sporophyte stages. Its leaves are typically narrow and closely spaced, with a distinct midrib.

Chromatotriccum fruticicola plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for microorganisms in its native ecosystems.
