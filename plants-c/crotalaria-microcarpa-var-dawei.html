Crotalaria microcarpa var. dawei

Crotalaria microcarpa var. dawei, commonly known as the dwarf crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically surrounded by leaf-like bracts. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

This variety is noted for its compact size and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
