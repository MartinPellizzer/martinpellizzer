Croton thouarsianus var. robustior

Croton thouarsianus var. robustior, commonly known as the robust croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It features large, palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often displaying a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Due to its striking appearance, it is popular in horticulture but requires well-drained soil and protection from frost.
