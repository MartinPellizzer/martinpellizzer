Clematis orientalis subsp. oweniae

Clematis orientalis subsp.

oweniae, commonly known as the Chinese vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China. It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to climb trellises or walls.
