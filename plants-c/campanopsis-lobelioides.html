Campanopsis lobelioides

Campanopsis lobelioides, commonly known as the bellflower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Campanopsis lobelioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its delicate floral display.
