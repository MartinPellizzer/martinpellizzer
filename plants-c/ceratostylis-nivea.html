Ceratostylis nivea

Ceratostylis nivea, commonly known as the white-flowered helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy white flower that has a distinctive helmet-shaped lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are linear and arranged spirally around the stem.

The orchid is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
