Carex stellulata var. radiata

Carex stellulata var. radiata, commonly known as the star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers in early to mid-summer. It typically grows in moist to wet soils in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wetland habitats.
