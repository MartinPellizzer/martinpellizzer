Croton widgrenianus

Croton widgrenianus, commonly known as the Widgren's croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that vary in color from green to reddish-brown, depending on the season. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its unique foliage.
