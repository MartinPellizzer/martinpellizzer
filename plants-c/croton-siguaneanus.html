Croton siguaneanus

Croton siguaneanus, commonly known as the Siguana croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features alternate, simple leaves that vary in color from green to red and yellow, depending on the species. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
