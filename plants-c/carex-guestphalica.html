Carex guestphalica

Carex guestphalica, commonly known as the mountain sedge.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and grass-green in color. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually brown or reddish in hue.

Carex guestphalica is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or alpine environments.
