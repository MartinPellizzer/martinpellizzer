Cordia costaricensis

Cordia costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican cordia, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Cordia costaricensis is often found in moist forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
