Cichorium polystachyum

Cichorium polystachyum, commonly known as the Ethiopian lettuce, is a leafy vegetable native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically dark green and slightly bitter in taste. The plant thrives in cool climates and is often cultivated in high-altitude regions. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes other familiar plants like daisies and lettuce.

Its leaves are used both as a vegetable and for medicinal purposes in traditional practices.
