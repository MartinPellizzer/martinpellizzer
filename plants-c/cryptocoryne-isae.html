Cryptocoryne isae

Cryptocoryne isae, commonly known as the Isae cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments with moderate to high light levels. Its rhizomes form dense mats, which help stabilize substrates in aquatic habitats.

Cryptocoryne isae is often cultivated in aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
