Cyathea amaiambitensis

Cyathea amaiambitensis, commonly known as the Amaiambitensis tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in rainforests. Its reproductive structures, including spore-bearing cones, are located at the base of the fronds.

Cyathea amaiambitensis plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
