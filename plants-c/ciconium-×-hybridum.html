Ciconium × hybridum

Ciconium × hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid ciconium, is a rare ornamental plant resulting from the crossbreeding of two species within the Ciconium genus.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and often colorful inflorescences that resemble plumes, which are typical of the genus. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments, making it suitable for woodland gardens or shaded rockeries. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy.

Due to its rarity and specific cultivation requirements, Ciconium × hybridum is a prized specimen among horticultural enthusiasts.
