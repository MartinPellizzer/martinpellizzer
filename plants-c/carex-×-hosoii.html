Carex × hosoii

Carex × hosoii, commonly known as the Hosoii sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and dense clusters of small, spike-like flowers. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and wetlands. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization.

The plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate wet conditions.
