Coelogyne tiomanensis

Coelogyne tiomanensis, commonly known as the Tioman orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescence and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within montane forests. Its flowers are usually pink or white with a yellow lip, and it blooms during the rainy season.

Coelogyne tiomanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
