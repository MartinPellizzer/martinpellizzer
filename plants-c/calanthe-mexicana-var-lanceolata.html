Calanthe mexicana var. lanceolata

Calanthe mexicana var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lance-leaf calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with purple and white petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests or rocky slopes. Its botanical features include a sympodial growth habit and a rhizomatous root system.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
