Chlorophytum subrugosum

Chlorophytum subrugosum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a perennial evergreen plant characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with green and white patterns. The plant is renowned for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal indoor plant. Its leaves are fibrous and can store water, which contributes to its resilience in arid environments.

Additionally, it is known for its air-purifying properties, improving indoor air quality.
