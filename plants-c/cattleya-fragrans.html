Cattleya fragrans

Cattleya fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant produces a strong, sweet fragrance, especially during the evening. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, warm environments.

Cattleya fragrans is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique scent.
