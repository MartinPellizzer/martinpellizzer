Crotalaria loandae var. annua

Crotalaria loandae var. annua, commonly known as the annual crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces yellowish or reddish flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and agricultural fields.

The species is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a potential candidate for soil enrichment in certain ecological contexts.
