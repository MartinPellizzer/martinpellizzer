Croton betaceus

Croton betaceus, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture, while the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers.
