Croton lucidus var. pubiger

Croton lucidus var. pubiger, commonly known as the glossy croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright green to shades of red and orange. The plant features small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are borne in clusters. Its leaves are typically glossy and have a velvety texture due to the presence of fine pubescence.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
