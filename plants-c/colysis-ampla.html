Colysis ampla

Colysis ampla, commonly known as the broadleaf colysis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
