Cytisus nigricans reflexus

Cytisus nigricans reflexus, commonly known as the blackthorn broom, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and small, dark green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are clustered in racemes. Its fruits are small, leathery pods that contain several seeds.

The species is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.
