Cynoctonum wallichii

Cynoctonum wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan fig tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Moraceae family.

It is native to the Himalayas and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fig-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ecological and historical significance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes as well.
