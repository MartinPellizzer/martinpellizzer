Clematis gouriana var. finetii

Clematis gouriana var. finetii, commonly known as the silky clematis, is a climbing vine native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces showy, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
