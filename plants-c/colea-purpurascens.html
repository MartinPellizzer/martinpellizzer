Colea purpurascens

Colea purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed colea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive purple stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Colea purpurascens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
