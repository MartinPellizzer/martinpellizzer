Clerodendrum bipindense

Clerodendrum bipindense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
