Cyrtochilum Kopfianum

Cyrtochilum kopfianum is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. This species is known for its vibrant, intricate flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered by conservationists. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of both admiration and concern.
