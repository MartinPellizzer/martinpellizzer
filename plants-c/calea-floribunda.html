Calea floribunda

Calea floribunda, commonly known as the "flowering calea," is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are often velvety and have a distinctive, waxy texture.
