Cotula Mariae

Cotula mariae is a small, delicate flowering plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. This species is known for its tiny, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky habitats. Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, Cotula mariae is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
