Chloracantha spinosa var. jaliscensis

Chloracantha spinosa var. jaliscensis, commonly known as the Jalisco spiny acanthus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

This variety is often found in rocky or limestone soils in the state of Jalisco.
