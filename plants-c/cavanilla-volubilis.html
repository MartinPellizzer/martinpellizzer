Cavanilla volubilis

Cavanilla volubilis, commonly known as the climbing evergreen vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cavanilla volubilis is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
