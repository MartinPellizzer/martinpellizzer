Cochleanthes lobata

Cochleanthes lobata, commonly known as the lobate orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, often fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically features broad, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, intricate blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cochleanthes lobata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
