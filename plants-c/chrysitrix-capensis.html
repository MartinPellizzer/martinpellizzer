Chrysitrix capensis

Chrysitrix capensis, commonly known as the Cape chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
