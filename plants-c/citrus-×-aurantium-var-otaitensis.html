Citrus × aurantium var. otaitensis

Citrus × aurantium var. otaitensis, commonly known as the Tahitian lime, is a citrus hybrid native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It is characterized by its small, round, green fruits with a thin, smooth rind. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is valued for its high acidity and is commonly used in culinary applications and traditional medicine.
