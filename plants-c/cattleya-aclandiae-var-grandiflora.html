Cattleya aclandiae var. grandiflora

Cattleya aclandiae var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Grandiflora Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, large flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features thick, leathery leaves and produces showy, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, white, and red. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

This variety is noted for its robust growth and ability to rebloom regularly under proper care.
