Croton pampangensis

Croton pampangensis, commonly known as the Pampanga croton, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in complex inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and can vary in shape, sometimes with lobed or serrated edges.

Croton pampangensis is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
