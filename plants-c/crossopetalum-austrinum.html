Crossopetalum austrinum

Crossopetalum austrinum, commonly known as the Australian crossopetalum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and leathery, with a glossy surface.
