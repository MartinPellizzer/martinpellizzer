Caragana jubata f. seczuanica

Caragana jubata f. seczuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan shrub pea.

It is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of western China. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by elongated, bean-like pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is well adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it a hardy plant suitable for dry landscapes.
