Cattleya forbesii f. viridipetala

Cattleya forbesii f. viridipetala, commonly known as the Emerald Petal Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its unique foliage and floral characteristics.

This cultivar is notable for its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are a vibrant green, contrasting with its striking orchid flowers. The blooms are typically large, with a mix of white and green hues, and often feature a subtle fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
