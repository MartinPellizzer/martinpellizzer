Cotoneaster cardinalis

Cotoneaster cardinalis, commonly known as the cardinal cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, red-orange berries that are an important food source for birds and wildlife. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red in the autumn. It produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Cotoneaster cardinalis is valued for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
