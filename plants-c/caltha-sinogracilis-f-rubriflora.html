Caltha sinogracilis f. rubriflora

Caltha sinogracilis f. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered silky buttercup, is a variant of the silky buttercup native to eastern Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, bright red flowers. The flowers typically bloom in early spring, adding a striking visual contrast to the green foliage. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and wetland habitats.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
