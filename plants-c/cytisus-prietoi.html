Cytisus prietoi

Cytisus prietoi, commonly known as the Canary Island broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure. The plant features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Cytisus prietoi is adapted to the arid and rocky conditions of its native habitat.
