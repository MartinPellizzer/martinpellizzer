Cyperus pilosus var. purpurascens

Cyperus pilosus var. purpurascens, commonly known as purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes, swamps, and along water edges. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive purple hue on the undersides.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that produce small, three-lobed flowers, contributing to its ornamental value in aquatic gardens.
