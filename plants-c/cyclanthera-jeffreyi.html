Cyclanthera jeffreyi

Cyclanthera jeffreyi, commonly known as the Jeffrey's bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces elongated, warty fruits that are typically green and have a bitter taste. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.
