Caragana sinica var. megalantha

Caragana sinica var. megalantha, commonly known as the large-flowered shrub willow, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-branched stems and small, needle-like leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
