Centaurea congesta

Centaurea congesta, commonly known as the clustered knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, pinkish to reddish flowers in dense clusters. Its flowers are surrounded by bracts that give it a distinctive appearance.

Centaurea congesta is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
