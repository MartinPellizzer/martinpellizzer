Calanthe rubicallosa

Calanthe rubicallosa, commonly known as the red-veined calanthe, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces striking, showy flowers with red veins on white petals. The plant typically blooms in spring, with a fragrant, orchid-like flower structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Calanthe rubicallosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
