Cerasus Vulgaris F. Semiplena

Cerasus vulgaris f. semiplena is a unique cultivar of the common cherry tree known for its distinctive double flowers. This ornamental plant is prized for its showy, layered blossoms that add visual interest to gardens in spring. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. The plant is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal color. Its semi-double flowers make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts alike.
