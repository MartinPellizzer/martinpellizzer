Cyrtochilum minax

Cyrtochilum minax, commonly known as the miniature slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant features a short, erect stem with a single or few leaves and produces delicate, pale yellow to greenish flowers. Cyrtochilum minax thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ecological significance.
