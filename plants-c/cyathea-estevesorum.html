Cyathea estevesorum

Cyathea estevesorum, commonly known as the Estevoso's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizome is robust, supporting its height and enabling it to thrive in nutrient-rich soil.

Cyathea estevesorum plays a role in providing habitat for various organisms and contributes to the ecological diversity of its native ecosystems.
