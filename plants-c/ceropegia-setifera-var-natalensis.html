Ceropegia setifera var. natalensis

Ceropegia setifera var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces a milky sap, which is a common feature among species in the Asclepiadaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
