Cyananthus pilifolius f. leiocalyx

Cyananthus pilifolius f. leiocalyx, commonly known as the white-flowered cyananthus, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are densely arranged along the stem. The plant produces clusters of white or pale blue flowers, which are typically bell-shaped and arranged in inflorescences. This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate floral display.
