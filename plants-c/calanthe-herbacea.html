Calanthe herbacea

Calanthe herbacea, commonly known as the slender calanthe, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe herbacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
