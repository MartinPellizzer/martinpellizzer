Cavendishia spicata

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spikelike inflorescence that bears numerous small, colorful flowers. Cavendishia spicata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
