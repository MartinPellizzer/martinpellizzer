Cleisostoma fuscum

Cleisostoma fuscum, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to brownish flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, blooming during the warmer months.

Cleisostoma fuscum is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
