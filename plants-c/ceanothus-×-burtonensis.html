Ceanothus × burtonensis

Ceanothus × burtonensis, commonly known as the California lilac, is a hybrid shrub native to California.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in Mediterranean climates.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
