Costa aromatica

Costa aromatica, commonly known as the aromatic costea, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a fragrant aroma. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its aromatic properties make it useful in traditional herbal medicine.

Costa aromatica thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.
