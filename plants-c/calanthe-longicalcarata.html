Calanthe longicalcarata

Calanthe longicalcarata, commonly known as the long-spurred calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated spurs that can reach several centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

Calanthe longicalcarata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
