Ceropegia seticorona var. dilatiloba

Ceropegia seticorona var. dilatiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thin, fleshy stems that often trail or climb, and its leaves are typically narrow and slightly fleshy. The flowers are small, tubular, and often have a distinctive hood-like structure, typical of the Asclepiadaceae family. It prefers well-drained soil and bright, indirect light, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
