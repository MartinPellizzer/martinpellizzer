Cassia trichoneura

Cassia trichoneura, commonly known as the flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of multiple small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia trichoneura is also valued for its ability to provide habitat and food for native wildlife.
