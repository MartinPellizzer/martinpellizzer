Collomia linearis var. subulata

Collomia linearis var. subulata, commonly known as the slender phlox, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and clusters of pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in mountainous regions. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and is adapted to arid environments.
