Crotalaria edmundi-bakeri

Crotalaria edmundi-bakeri, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it an important plant in agricultural systems.
