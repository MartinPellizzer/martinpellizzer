Clematis mandshurica f. pubescens

Clematis mandshurica f. pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent mandshurian clematis, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its woody vines and showy, typically blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features pubescent (hairy) stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various growing conditions.
