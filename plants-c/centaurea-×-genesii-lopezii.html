Centaurea × genesii-lopezii

Centaurea × genesii-lopezii, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads, which are composed of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with lanceolate, hairy leaves that are often glandular. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
