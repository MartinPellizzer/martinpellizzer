Coelogyne elata

Coelogyne elata, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of white or pale yellow blooms with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Coelogyne elata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
