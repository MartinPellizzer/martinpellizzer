Coleus splendens

Coleus splendens, commonly known as the flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, pink, and purple. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and basil. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance.

Coleus splendens thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil and bright indirect light.
