Cuphea ornithoides

Cuphea ornithoides, commonly known as the flame bean, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that resemble small beans, which bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant colors and compact growth habit.

Cuphea ornithoides is also valued for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators.
