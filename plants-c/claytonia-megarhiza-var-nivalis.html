Claytonia megarhiza var. nivalis

Claytonia megarhiza var. nivalis, commonly known as the snowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high elevations of the western United States.

It belongs to the Portulacaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-like growth habit and succulent, fleshy leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous regions.
