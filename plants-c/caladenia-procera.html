Caladenia procera

Caladenia procera, commonly known as the tall spider-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, supporting a single, showy orchid flower. The flower has a distinctive spider-like appearance, with a narrow, erect labellum and intricate petal structures. It typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant pink or purple hues that attract specific pollinators.

This plant is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, and it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
