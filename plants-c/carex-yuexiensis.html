Carex yuexiensis

Carex yuexiensis, commonly known as the Yuexi sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes various grass-like plants known for their ecological importance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands.

Carex yuexiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in maintaining soil stability in its natural habitat.
