Coeloglossum viride var. interjecta

Coeloglossum viride var. interjecta, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a variant of the hawksbeard genus native to Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common sight in meadows and disturbed areas.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex inflorescence and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
