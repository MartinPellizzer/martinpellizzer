Ctenodon genistoides var. latifoliolus

Ctenodon genistoides var. latifoliolus, commonly known as the broad-leafed broom, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters. The leaves are compound, with several leaflets that are broad and oval in shape. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in garden landscaping.
