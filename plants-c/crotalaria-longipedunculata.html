Crotalaria longipedunculata

Crotalaria longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-pedunculate crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and pairs of opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers arranged in dense, terminal clusters, which are often surrounded by leaf-like bracts. Its seed pods are cylindrical and dehisce to release small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Crotalaria longipedunculata is native to tropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
