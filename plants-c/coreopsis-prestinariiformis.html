Coreopsis prestinariiformis

Coreopsis prestinariiformis, commonly known as the slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and riverbanks. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native gardens.
