Camphora rougieri

Camphora rougieri, commonly known as the Mexican camphor tree, is a species of evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, thick bark and glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by dark purple berries. Camphora rougieri is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is commonly found in forested areas.
