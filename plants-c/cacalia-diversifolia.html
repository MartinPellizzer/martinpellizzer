Cacalia diversifolia

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and vary in size, contributing to its common name.
