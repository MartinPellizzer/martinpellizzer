Chytroma idatimon

Chytroma idatimon, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. Chytroma idatimon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
