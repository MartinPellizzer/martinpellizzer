Clerodendrum umbratile

Clerodendrum umbratile, commonly known as the dark clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Clerodendrum umbratile is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
