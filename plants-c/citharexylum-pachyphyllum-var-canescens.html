Citharexylum pachyphyllum var. canescens

Citharexylum pachyphyllum var. canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf citharexylum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, usually white or pale yellow, which bloom in spring.

This plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
