Calochortus greenei

Calochortus greenei, commonly known as the Greene's mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes various species of mariposa lilies. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It produces showy, bowl-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or purple throat.

Calochortus greenei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
