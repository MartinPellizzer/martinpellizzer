Calamagrostis fauriei

Calamagrostis fauriei, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and soft, silvery-green foliage that adds a delicate texture to gardens. The plant produces feathery plumes in late summer, which give it a graceful appearance. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Calamagrostis fauriei is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
