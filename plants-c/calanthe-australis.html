Calanthe australis

Calanthe australis, commonly known as the southern calanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Calanthe australis thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
