Cryptomeria japonica var. fortunei

Cryptomeria japonica var. fortunei, commonly known as the Japanese cedar or Japanese red cedar, is a variant of the Japanese cedar native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its conical shape, dark green foliage, and reddish-brown bark. The plant is a member of the cypress family (Cupressaceae) and is valued for its ornamental qualities and durable wood. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

This variety is also noted for its ability to adapt to various climates, though it prefers temperate conditions.
