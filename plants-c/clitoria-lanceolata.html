Clitoria lanceolata

Clitoria lanceolata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, though it is cultivated in various warm climates. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain edible seeds and a starchy bean-like pulp.

Its rapid growth and nitrogen-fixing ability make it valuable for both agricultural and ornamental purposes.
