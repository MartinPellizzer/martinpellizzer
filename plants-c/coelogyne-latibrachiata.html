Coelogyne latibrachiata

Coelogyne latibrachiata, commonly known as the broad-armed coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces showy, often pink or white flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne latibrachiata is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
