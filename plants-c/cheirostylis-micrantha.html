Cheirostylis micrantha

Cheirostylis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered cheirostylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short rhizome and linear, glabrous leaves. The plant produces small, pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, spurred labellum. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
