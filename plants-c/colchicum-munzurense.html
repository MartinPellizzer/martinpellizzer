Colchicum munzurense

Colchicum munzurense, commonly known as the Munzur colchic, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in rocky and mountainous habitats, often growing in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
