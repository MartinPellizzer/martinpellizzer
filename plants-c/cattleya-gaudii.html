Cattleya gaudii

Cattleya gaudii, commonly known as the Gaudii Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya gaudii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
