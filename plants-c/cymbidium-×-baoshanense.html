Cymbidium × baoshanense

Cymbidium × baoshanense, commonly known as the Baoshan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires well-drained soil.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
