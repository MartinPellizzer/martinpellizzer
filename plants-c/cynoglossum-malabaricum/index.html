Cynoglossum Malabaricum

Blueweed, scientifically known as Cynoglossum malabaricum, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Asia. With its vibrant blue flowers and distinctive heart-shaped leaves, it stands out in any garden or natural setting. This herbaceous perennial is often found in moist, shaded areas and thrives in temperate climates. Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Blueweed remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticulture.
