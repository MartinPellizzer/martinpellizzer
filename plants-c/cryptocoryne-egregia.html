Cryptocoryne egregia

Cryptocoryne egregia, commonly known as the black velvet cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne egregia is also known for its ability to tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a versatile choice for planted tanks.
