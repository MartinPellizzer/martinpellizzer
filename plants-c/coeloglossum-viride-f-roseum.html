Coeloglossum viride f. roseum

Coeloglossum viride f. roseum, commonly known as the pink-stemmed green orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring bright green leaves with a pinkish or reddish hue on the underside. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of green and white, often with a subtle pink tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
