Cyanea solanacea

Cyanea solanacea, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which bloom in the morning and close in the afternoon. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

Cyanea solanacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and unique blooming pattern.
