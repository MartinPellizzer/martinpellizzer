Cyrtopodium broadwayi

Cyrtopodium broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and cloud forests. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its roots are fibrous and help it anchor in the moist, organic-rich soil.

Cyrtopodium broadwayi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
