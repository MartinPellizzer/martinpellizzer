Cucumis flexuosus var. rectoviridis

Cucumis flexuosus var. rectoviridis, commonly known as the straight-green cucumber, is a variant of the flexible cucumber species.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The leaves are lobed and covered in fine pubescence, while the fruits are typically elongated and have a smooth, green skin. This variety is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates.

It is valued for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary applications.
