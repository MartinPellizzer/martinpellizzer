Cacalia sempervirens

Cacalia sempervirens, commonly known as evergreen cacalia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure. The plant features dark green, slightly hairy leaves and produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Cacalia sempervirens is relatively drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.
