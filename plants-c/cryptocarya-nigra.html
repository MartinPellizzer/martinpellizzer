Cryptocarya nigra

Cryptocarya nigra, commonly known as black cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its bark is dark and often covered in a thick, leathery layer, which contributes to its common name.

Cryptocarya nigra is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
