Centaurea hladnickiana

Centaurea hladnickiana, commonly known as the Hladnička centaurea, is a flowering plant species native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves with a rough texture, and it produces small, fluffy seed heads that aid in wind dispersal. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in alpine or subalpine regions.

Centaurea hladnickiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
