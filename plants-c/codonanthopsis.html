Codonanthopsis

Codonanthopsis, commonly known as the fairy lantern.

It is a member of the Asphodelaceae family, native to South Africa. The plant is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. It produces small, fleshy leaves that are often clustered at the base.

Codonanthopsis is typically grown as a succulent, thriving in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.
