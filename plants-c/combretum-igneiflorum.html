Combretum igneiflorum

Combretum igneiflorum, commonly known as flame flower, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in clusters along the branches. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as birds and bees.
