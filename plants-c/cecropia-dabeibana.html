Cecropia dabeibana

Cecropia dabeibana, commonly known as the Chinese cecropia, is a fast-growing tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cecropiaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its hollow, smooth-stemmed trunk is a distinctive feature, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers.

The species plays an important ecological role in supporting various insect and bird populations.
