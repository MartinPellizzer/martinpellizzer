Crepidiastrum chelidoniifolium

Crepidiastrum chelidoniifolium, commonly known as the yellow celandine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has distinctive, heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its medicinal properties have been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
