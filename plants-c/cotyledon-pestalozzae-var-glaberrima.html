Cotyledon pestalozzae var. glaberrima

Cotyledon pestalozzae var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The leaves are often adorned with subtle markings or a slight bluish tint. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months.

This plant is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal watering to thrive.
