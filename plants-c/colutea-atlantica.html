Colutea atlantica

Colutea atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic broom.

It is a flowering plant native to the western Mediterranean region, particularly the Iberian Peninsula and North Africa. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces bright yellow flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Colutea atlantica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
