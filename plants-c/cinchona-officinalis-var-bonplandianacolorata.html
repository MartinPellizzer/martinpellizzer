Cinchona officinalis var. bonplandianacolorata

Cinchona officinalis var. bonplandianacolorata, commonly known as the Crimson Queen cinchona, is a species of the Rubiaceae family native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red and pink bracts that surround its small, white flowers, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
