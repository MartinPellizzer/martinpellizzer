Colysis × shintenensis

Colysis × shintenensis, commonly known as the hybrid Japanese maple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Japan.

It is a cross between *Acer colysis* and *Acer shintenense*, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its ornamental foliage, which displays vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues during the autumn season. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability, it is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes.
