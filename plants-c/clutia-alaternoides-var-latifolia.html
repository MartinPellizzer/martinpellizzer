Clutia alaternoides var. latifolia

Clutia alaternoides var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf clutia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2-5 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
