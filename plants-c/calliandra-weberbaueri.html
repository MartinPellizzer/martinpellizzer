Calliandra weberbaueri

Calliandra weberbaueri, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences. The plant features bipinnate leaves and produces clusters of pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and unique foliage.

Calliandra weberbaueri is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
