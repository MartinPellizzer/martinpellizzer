Cineraria webberiana

Cineraria webberiana, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California and Nevada.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves and striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine environments. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
