Cyperus schinzii

Cyperus schinzii, commonly known as Schinz's flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often forming dense colonies. Its slender, grass-like stems are usually green or reddish, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Cyperus schinzii is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
