Caladium mafaffa

Caladium mafaffa, commonly known as the African shield leaf, is a striking tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like philodendrons and calla lilies. Caladium mafaffa thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant displays.
