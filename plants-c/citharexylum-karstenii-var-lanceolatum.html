Citharexylum karstenii var. lanceolatum

Citharexylum karstenii var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and adaptability to various garden settings.
