Cistus sennenianus

Cistus sennenianus, commonly known as the Sennean rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is adapted to rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves and flowers, are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

Cistus sennenianus plays a role in stabilizing soils and supporting local biodiversity in its native habitat.
