Cyclophyllum brevipes

Cyclophyllum brevipes, commonly known as the short-footed cyclophyllum, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features broad, rounded leaves with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Cyclophyllum brevipes is native to specific regions in southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils.

Its growth habit is low and sprawling, making it well adapted to its ecological niche.
