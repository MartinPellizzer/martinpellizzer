Calathea lasiostachya

Calathea lasiostachya, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of green, silver, and purple. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its foliage is typically dark green with contrasting veins and margins.
