Callisia repens var. ciliata

Callisia repens var. ciliata, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-blue hue. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
