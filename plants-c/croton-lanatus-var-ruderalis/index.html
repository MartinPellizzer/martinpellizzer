Croton Lanatus Var. Ruderalis

Croton lanatus var. ruderalis is a lesser-known but highly adaptable variant of the Croton plant family. It thrives in disturbed environments, often found growing along roadsides and in urban areas. This plant is notable for its striking foliage, featuring vibrant colors and unique leaf patterns. Despite its beauty, it is sometimes considered a weed due to its aggressive growth habits. Its resilience and ecological significance make it an intriguing subject for botanical study.
