Cynanchum apocynellum

Cynanchum apocynellum, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cynanchum apocynellum is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
