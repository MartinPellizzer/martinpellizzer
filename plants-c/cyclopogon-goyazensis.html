Cyclopogon goyazensis

Cyclopogon goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis cyclopogon, is a rare bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet inflorescence and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in rocky or forested areas. It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
