Cypripedium × yvonnae

Cypripedium × yvonnae, commonly known as the Yvonne's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of China and is often found in moist, shaded forest habitats. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces a single flower per stem, which blooms in late spring to early summer.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
