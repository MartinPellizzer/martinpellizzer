Coelogyne septemnervia

Coelogyne septemnervia, commonly known as the seven-veined orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, using its aerial roots to absorb moisture and nutrients. Its leaves are usually strap-shaped and can be quite large, contributing to its ornamental value.

Coelogyne septemnervia is prized in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
