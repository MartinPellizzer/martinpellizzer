Cissampelos calcarifera

Cissampelos calcarifera, commonly known as the rock fig or stone fig, is a climbing plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. Its roots are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
