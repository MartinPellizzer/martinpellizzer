Crotalaria quangensis var. malangensis

Crotalaria quangensis var. malangensis, commonly known as the Malang false flax, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, well-drained soils. It features compound leaves and produces distinctive yellow or orange flowers.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and research settings.
