Clerodendrum chinense var. plenum

Clerodendrum chinense var. plenum, commonly known as the full-flowered Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from red to orange, often appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
