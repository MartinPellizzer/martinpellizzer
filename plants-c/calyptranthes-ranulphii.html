Calyptranthes ranulphii

Calyptranthes ranulphii, commonly known as the ranulphii calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a glossy texture. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, typical of the Myrtaceae family.
