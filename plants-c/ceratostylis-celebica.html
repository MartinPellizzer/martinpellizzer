Ceratostylis celebica

Ceratostylis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes torch orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Indonesia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with dense, fleshy roots that help it survive in its tropical environment. The plant produces striking, red-orange flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur that is characteristic of the genus Ceratostylis. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
