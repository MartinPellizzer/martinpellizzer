Chamaecrista orenocensis

Chamaecrista orenocensis, commonly known as the Orenoco burr legume, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Venezuela and Colombia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, tropical regions. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seed pods are woody and often contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Chamaecrista orenocensis is part of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has adapted to arid environments with its deep root system.
