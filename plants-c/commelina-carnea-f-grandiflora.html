Commelina carnea f. grandiflora

Commelina carnea f. grandiflora, commonly known as the pink arum or pink spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, tropical-looking plants.
