Calantica Jalbertii

Calantica jalbertii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. It is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red and orange. This species belongs to the nightshade family and is often found in rocky, well-drained soils. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is considered an endangered plant. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
