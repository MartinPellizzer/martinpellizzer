Coelogyne fimbriata var. primulina

Coelogyne fimbriata var. primulina, commonly known as the Primrose Orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with intricate, fringed petals. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It blooms during the winter months and requires bright, indirect light and moderate humidity to thrive.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
