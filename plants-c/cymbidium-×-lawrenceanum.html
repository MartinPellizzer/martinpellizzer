Cymbidium × lawrenceanum

Cymbidium × lawrenceanum, commonly known as the lawrence orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and large, showy flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with multiple blooms, often in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
