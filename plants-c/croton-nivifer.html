Croton nivifer

Croton nivifer, commonly known as the snow croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often adorned with white or cream-colored veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
