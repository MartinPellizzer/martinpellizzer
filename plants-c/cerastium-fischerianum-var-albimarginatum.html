Cerastium fischerianum var. albimarginatum

Cerastium fischerianum var. albimarginatum, commonly known as the white-margin sandwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe.

It features small, white or pale pink flowers with distinctive white margins, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has slender, branched stems covered in fine hairs and produces tiny, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and rocky slopes.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
