Chariessa smythii

Chariessa smythii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a glossy appearance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its showy floral display.
