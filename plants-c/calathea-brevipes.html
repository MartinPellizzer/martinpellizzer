Calathea brevipes

Calathea brevipes, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns and deep green tones. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often featuring contrasting colors that enhance its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
