Cimicifuga mairei

Cimicifuga mairei, commonly known as Chinese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges, and its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Cimicifuga mairei thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
