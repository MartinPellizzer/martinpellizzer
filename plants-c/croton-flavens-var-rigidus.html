Croton flavens var. rigidus

Croton flavens var. rigidus, commonly known as the rigid croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
