Covellia macrophylla

Covellia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf covellia, is a flowering plant native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Covellia macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
