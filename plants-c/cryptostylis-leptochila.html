Cryptostylis leptochila

Cryptostylis leptochila, commonly known as the slender-stemmed star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of northeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy stems and small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale green. The plant produces its flowers in late winter to early spring, often in clusters at the base of the stems. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cryptostylis leptochila is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate and ephemeral blooms.
