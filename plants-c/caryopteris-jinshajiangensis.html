Caryopteris jinshajiangensis

Caryopteris jinshajiangensis, commonly known as the Jinshajiang silverleaf sage, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of blue-lilac flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
