Coelogyne brevilamellata

Coelogyne brevilamellata, commonly known as the narrow-lamella coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne brevilamellata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
