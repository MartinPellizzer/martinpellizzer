Cypripedium gomezianum

Cypripedium gomezianum, commonly known as the Gomez's lady's slipper, is a rare orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like flower that is typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a single, upright stem that supports a single or occasionally two flowers. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Cypripedium gomezianum is adapted to cold, humid environments and thrives in shaded, moist habitats.
