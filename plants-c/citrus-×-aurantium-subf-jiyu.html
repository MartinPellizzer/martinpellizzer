Citrus × aurantium subf. jiyu

Citrus × aurantium subf.

jiyu, commonly known as the Chinese pomelo, is a citrus hybrid with origins in Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruit that typically contains a high amount of juice and a mild, sweet flavor.

The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is widely used in both culinary and medicinal applications due to its nutritional and therapeutic properties.
