Carex nova var. erxlebenii

Carex nova var. erxlebenii, commonly known as the Erxleben's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, rushes, and grasses. This plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are usually dark green in color.
