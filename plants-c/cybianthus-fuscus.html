Cybianthus fuscus

Cybianthus fuscus, commonly known as the blackbrush.

It is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Cybianthus fuscus plays a role in stabilizing desert soils and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
