Crassula schmidtii f. alba

Crassula schmidtii f. alba, commonly known as the White Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its striking white to pale green foliage. The leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps the plant store water efficiently. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
