Crotalaria ferruginea var. chiayiana

Crotalaria ferruginea var. chiayiana, commonly known as the Chiayi crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers with prominent spurred petals. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in open woodlands and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
