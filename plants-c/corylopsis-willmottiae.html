Corylopsis willmottiae

Corylopsis willmottiae, commonly known as the Chinese flame-tipped cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that turn red in the fall. It is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and full sun.

Corylopsis willmottiae is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of garden conditions.
