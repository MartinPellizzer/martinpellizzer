Centaurea urgellensis

Centaurea urgellensis, commonly known as the Urgellensis thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in areas with mild climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and often ornamental plant in its native habitat.
