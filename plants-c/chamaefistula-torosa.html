Chamaefistula torosa

Chamaefistula torosa, commonly known as the Turkish smoke bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Turkey and the eastern Mediterranean.

It features striking, feathery panicles of pale pink to white flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability to various climates contribute to its horticultural value.
