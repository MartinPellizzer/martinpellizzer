Calandrinia minutissima

Calandrinia minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small, annual herb that produces vibrant pink or red flowers. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves and stems, which help it retain water in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its compact size and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance plant for gardeners.
