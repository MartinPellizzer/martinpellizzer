Croton wellensii

Croton wellensii, commonly known as the wellensii croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, palmate leaves with vibrant green or reddish hues, depending on the season. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and hairy foliage, which contribute to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
