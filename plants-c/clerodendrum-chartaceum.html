Clerodendrum chartaceum

Clerodendrum chartaceum, commonly known as the paper clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, papery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the warm seasons. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
