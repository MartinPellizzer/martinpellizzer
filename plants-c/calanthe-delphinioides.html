Calanthe delphinioides

Calanthe delphinioides, commonly known as the Delphinioid Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically features long, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Calanthe delphinioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
