Carduus spinigerus proles australis

Carduus spinigerus proles australis, commonly known as the Australian prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions. It has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh environmental conditions.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
