Campanula gracilis var. stricta

Campanula gracilis var. stricta, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and delicate floral display.
