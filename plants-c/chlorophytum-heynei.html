Chlorophytum heynei

Chlorophytum heynei, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy succulent native to tropical Africa.

It features long, upright leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, which help in photosynthesis. The plant is known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for beginners.

Its botanical features include fibrous roots and the ability to store water in its leaves, aiding in survival in arid environments.
