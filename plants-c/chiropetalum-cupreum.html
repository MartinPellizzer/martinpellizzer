Chiropetalum cupreum

Chiropetalum cupreum, commonly known as the copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, copper-colored leaves that change hue with seasonal variations. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its aesthetic appeal.
