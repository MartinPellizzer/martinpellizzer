Calanthe muelleri

Calanthe muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's calanthe, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces a raceme of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate markings, enhancing its ornamental value.

Calanthe muelleri is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and relatively easy care requirements.
