Cephalanthera Ensifolia Var. Maravignae

Cephalanthera ensifolia var. maravignae is a rare and striking orchid native to the montane forests of Madagascar. This variant is distinguished by its slender, sword-shaped leaves and delicate, pale greenish-white flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested habitats. Due to its limited distribution and habitat vulnerability, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique species from further decline.
