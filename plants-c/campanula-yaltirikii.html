Campanula yaltirikii

Campanula yaltirikii, commonly known as the Yaltirikii bellflower, is a perennial flowering plant native to Turkey.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

Campanula yaltirikii is relatively low maintenance and attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
