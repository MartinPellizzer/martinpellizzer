Crinum erubescens var. rubrilimbum

Crinum erubescens var. rubrilimbum, commonly known as the red-limbed crinum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a reddish tinge. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purplish hue along the edges. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant colors and long-lasting blooms.
