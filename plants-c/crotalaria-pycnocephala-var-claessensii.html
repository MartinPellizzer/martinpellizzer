Crotalaria pycnocephala var. claessensii

Crotalaria pycnocephala var. claessensii, commonly known as the dense-headed crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, compact flower clusters. The plant typically features palmate leaves with three to five leaflets and produces bright yellow or orange flowers.
