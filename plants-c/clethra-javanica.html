Clethra javanica

Clethra javanica, commonly known as the Javan clethra, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Clethra javanica is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
