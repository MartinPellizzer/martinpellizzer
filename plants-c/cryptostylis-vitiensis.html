Cryptostylis vitiensis

Cryptostylis vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a short, thick rhizome and produces a single, erect stem with several leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in a spiral and have a unique, intricate structure.

Cryptostylis vitiensis is a rare and specialized orchid that thrives in specific, often moist and shaded habitats.
