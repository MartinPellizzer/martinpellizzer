Centaurea vandasii

Centaurea vandasii, commonly known as the Vandasii cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea vandasii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
