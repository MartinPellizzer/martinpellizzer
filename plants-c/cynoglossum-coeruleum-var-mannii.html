Cynoglossum coeruleum var. mannii

Cynoglossum coeruleum var. mannii, commonly known as blue mouse ear, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a basal rosette of velvety, rounded leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice for gardeners.
