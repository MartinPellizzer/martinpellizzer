Croton polypleurus

Croton polypleurus, commonly known as the red-stemmed croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often variegated with yellow and green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
