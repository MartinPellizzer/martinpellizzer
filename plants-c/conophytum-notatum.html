Conophytum notatum

Conophytum notatum, commonly known as the "Cape Karoo stone plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water. Conophytum notatum is often used in rock gardens and is valued for its unique appearance and resilience.
