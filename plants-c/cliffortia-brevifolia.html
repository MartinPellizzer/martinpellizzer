Cliffortia brevifolia

Cliffortia brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cliffortia brevifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
