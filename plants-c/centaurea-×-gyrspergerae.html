Centaurea × gyrspergerae

Centaurea × gyrspergerae, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea jacea (brown cock's-comb), exhibiting a mix of characteristics from both parents. The plant typically features vibrant blue to purple flowers with a distinctive central disk, and it has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in gardens and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for floral arrangements and garden cultivation.
