Celosia spicata var. thouarsii

Celosia spicata var. thouarsii, commonly known as the flame daisy, is a striking ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky inflorescences that resemble flames, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to both tropical and temperate gardens.
