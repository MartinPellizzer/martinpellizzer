Crotalaria androyensis

Crotalaria androyensis, commonly known as the Madagascar crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is typically shrub-like, with erect stems and can grow up to several meters in height.

It has a deep root system that helps it thrive in nutrient-poor soils, making it an important component of its native ecosystem.
