Cereus speciosissimus var. sarniensis

Cereus speciosissimus var. sarniensis, commonly known as the Sarnian torch cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a columnar cactus characterized by its spiny stems and ability to thrive in extreme desert conditions. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in low-rainfall environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
