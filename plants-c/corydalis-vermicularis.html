Corydalis vermicularis

Corydalis vermicularis, commonly known as the creeping lousewort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fumariaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and is native to regions of Europe and Asia. Corydalis vermicularis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.
