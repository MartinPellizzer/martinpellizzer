Cola flavovelutina

Cola flavovelutina, commonly known as the yellow velvet cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Sterculiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Nigeria and Cameroon. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are covered in a dense, yellowish pubescence. Its flowers are clustered in panicles and are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of caffeine.
