Chorizema ilicifolium

Chorizema ilicifolium, commonly known as the spiny broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in pairs along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Chorizema ilicifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
