Croton korthalsii

Croton korthalsii, commonly known as the Congo croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, with leaves often displaying shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant in warm climates.
