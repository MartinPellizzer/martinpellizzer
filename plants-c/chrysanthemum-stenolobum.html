Chrysanthemum stenolobum

Chrysanthemum stenolobum, commonly known as the narrow-lobed chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for horticultural displays.
