Crossandra elatior

Crossandra elatior, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a yellow throat. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.

Crossandra elatior prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
