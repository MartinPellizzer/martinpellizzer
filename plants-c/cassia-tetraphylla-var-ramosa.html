Cassia tetraphylla var. ramosa

Cassia tetraphylla var. ramosa, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for landscaping and garden decoration.
