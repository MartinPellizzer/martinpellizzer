Cincinalis setigera

Cincinalis setigera, commonly known as the slender cypress, is a member of the Cupressaceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and distinct, clustered cones. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
