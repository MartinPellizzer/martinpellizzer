Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. desfontainesii

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. desfontainesii, commonly known as the Desfontaines cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is particularly noted for its compact growth habit and winter interest.
