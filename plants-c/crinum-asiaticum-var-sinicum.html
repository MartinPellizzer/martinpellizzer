Crinum asiaticum var. sinicum

Crinum asiaticum var. sinicum, commonly known as the Chinese spider lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a red throat. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are broad and sword-shaped, contributing to its dramatic appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
