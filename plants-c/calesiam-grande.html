Calesiam grande

Calesiam grande, commonly known as the giant calesiam, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to two meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in moist, shady environments make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, due to its invasive nature, it is often considered a weed in some regions.
