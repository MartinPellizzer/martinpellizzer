Cotoneaster russanovii

Cotoneaster russanovii, commonly known as the Russian cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and features dense, sparsely branched stems. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and turn yellow in autumn.

Cotoneaster russanovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
