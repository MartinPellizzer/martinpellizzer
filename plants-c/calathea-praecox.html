Calathea praecox

Calathea praecox, commonly known as the early prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and display a range of colors, including green, silver, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea praecox is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
