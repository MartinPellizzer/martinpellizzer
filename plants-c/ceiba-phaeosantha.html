Ceiba phaeosantha

Ceiba phaeosantha, commonly known as the pink ceiba, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and large, showy pink flowers that bloom during the dry season. The tree has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets and produces woody fruits that contain seeds. It plays an important role in the ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable plant in tropical botany.
