Centaurea paniculata proles polycephala

Centaurea paniculata proles polycephala, commonly known as the clustered blueweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
