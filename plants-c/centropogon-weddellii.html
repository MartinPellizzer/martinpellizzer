Centropogon weddellii

Centropogon weddellii, commonly known as the Weddell's grass.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil, and belongs to the Poaceae family. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically with a bluish-green hue.

Centropogon weddellii is often found in grasslands and open habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
