Citrus × amara

Citrus × amara, commonly known as the hybrid citron.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between citron (Citrus medica) and pomelo (Citrus maxima). This plant is characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruits that are typically green or yellow when mature. The leaves are glossy and ovate, with a strong citrus scent.

It is primarily cultivated for its fruit, which is used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
