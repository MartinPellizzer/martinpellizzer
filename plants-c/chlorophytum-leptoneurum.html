Chlorophytum leptoneurum

Chlorophytum leptoneurum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum leptoneurum is often used as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
