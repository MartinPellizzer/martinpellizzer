Coccinia stefaninii

Coccinia stefaninii, commonly known as the Stefanini's cucumber, is a tropical vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. Its fruit is a small, round, orange-colored berry that matures in late summer.

Coccinia stefaninii is native to parts of Africa and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
