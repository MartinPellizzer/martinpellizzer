Calanthe graciliflora f. jiangxiensis

Calanthe graciliflora f. jiangxiensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi slender-flowered calanthe, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and small, pale green to white flowers with prominent purple or brown markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
