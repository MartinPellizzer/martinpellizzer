Cynoglossum hedgei

Cynoglossum hedgei, commonly known as the purple heart or heartleaf bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of violet-blue flowers with five petals, typical of the Boraginaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and drought tolerance.
