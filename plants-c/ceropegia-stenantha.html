Ceropegia stenantha

Ceropegia stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, pale yellow to greenish flowers that are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia stenantha is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
