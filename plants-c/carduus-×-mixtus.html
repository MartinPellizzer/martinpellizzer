Carduus × mixtus

Carduus × mixtus, commonly known as the common hawkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically has dark green, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and a rough texture. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to various regions around the world.

Carduus × mixtus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including roadsides and meadows.
