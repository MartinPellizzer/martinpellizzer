Cirsium scariosum var. robustum

Cirsium scariosum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, lobed leaves covered in a whitish, waxy coating. Its flowers are purple to pink, forming dense, spherical heads that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
