Conandron minor

Conandron minor, commonly known as the dwarf conandron, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in open, sunny areas.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
