Cotyledon sturmiana

Cotyledon sturmiana, commonly known as the Sturm's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which help it store water and protect against intense sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Cotyledon sturmiana is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
