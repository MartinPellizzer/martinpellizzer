Centaurea tenuifolia

Centaurea tenuifolia, commonly known as slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

Centaurea tenuifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
