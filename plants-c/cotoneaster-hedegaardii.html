Cotoneaster hedegaardii

Cotoneaster hedegaardii, commonly known as the Hedegaard's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers in spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its seasonal color changes and persistent fruiting.
