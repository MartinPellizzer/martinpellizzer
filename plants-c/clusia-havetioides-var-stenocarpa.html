Clusia havetioides var. stenocarpa

Clusia havetioides var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often succulent and can store water, adapting it to dry environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels, followed by elliptical, fleshy fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in various conditions.

The varietal form stenocarpa refers to its narrow, elongated fruits, distinguishing it from other varieties of Clusia.
