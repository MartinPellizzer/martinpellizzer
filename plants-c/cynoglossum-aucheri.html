Cynoglossum aucheri

Cynoglossum aucheri, commonly known as heart's-ease, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and parts of Europe.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial that produces striking blue or violet flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum aucheri is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
