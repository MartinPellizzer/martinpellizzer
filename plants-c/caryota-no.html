Caryota no

Caryota no, commonly known as the horse's tail palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and long, pinnately compound leaves that resemble a horse's tail. The plant belongs to the Arecaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Caryota no is adapted to humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
