Cestrum cyaneum

Cestrum cyaneum, commonly known as the purple cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its flowers are usually violet or purple in color, and they bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
