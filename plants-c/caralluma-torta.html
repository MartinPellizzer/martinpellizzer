Caralluma torta

Caralluma torta, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems that are often segmented and adorned with vibrant, colorful flowers during the holiday season. The plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is drought-tolerant and prefers cooler temperatures, making it an ideal houseplant for indoor environments.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
