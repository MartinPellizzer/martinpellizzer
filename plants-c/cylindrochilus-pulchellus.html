Cylindrochilus pulchellus

Cylindrochilus pulchellus, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and has a complex root system adapted for nutrient absorption in its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid's botanical features make it a popular subject for both horticultural cultivation and botanical study.
