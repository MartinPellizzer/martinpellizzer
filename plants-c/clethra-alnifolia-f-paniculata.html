Clethra alnifolia f. paniculata

Clethra alnifolia f. paniculata, commonly known as the panicle clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in panicle-like clusters during late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate to elliptic leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
