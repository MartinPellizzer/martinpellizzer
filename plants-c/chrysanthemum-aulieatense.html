Chrysanthemum aulieatense

Chrysanthemum aulieatense, commonly known as the Aulieatense chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
