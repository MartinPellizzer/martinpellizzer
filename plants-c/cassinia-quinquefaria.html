Cassinia quinquefaria

Cassinia quinquefaria, commonly known as the fiveflower cassinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow flower heads composed of many tiny florets, often surrounded by pale green bracts. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.

Cassinia quinquefaria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in gardens for its bright, long-lasting blooms.
