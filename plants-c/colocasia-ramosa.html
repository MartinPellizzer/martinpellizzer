Colocasia ramosa

Colocasia ramosa, commonly known as the arrowleaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a smooth texture and a prominent midrib.

Colocasia ramosa is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
