Cleistanthus major

Cleistanthus major, commonly known as the giant cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy green surface. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
