Ctenopteris tenuisecta

Ctenopteris tenuisecta, commonly known as the slender fern or narrow-leaf cliff fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, pinnately divided fronds. The fronds are typically dark green and have a smooth, glossy appearance. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in dense forests.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a distinctive and ornamental species in horticulture.
