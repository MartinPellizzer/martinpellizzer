Croton regelianus

Croton regelianus, commonly known as the Regelian croton, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds to bright yellows and greens. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often showy species. Its leaves are typically large and have a rough, textured surface, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Croton regelianus thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
