Cattleya warscewiczii f. rochellensis

Cattleya warscewiczii f. rochellensis, commonly known as the Rochell's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and fragrance.
