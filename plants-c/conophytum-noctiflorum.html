Conophytum noctiflorum

Conophytum noctiflorum, commonly known as the night-flowering conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that open at night, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and nocturnal blooming habit, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and collections of succulents.
