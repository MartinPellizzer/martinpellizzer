Chaerophyllum hirsutum var. cicutaria

Chaerophyllum hirsutum var. cicutaria, commonly known as the hairy cow parsley, is a flowering plant in the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers.
