Chionachne koenigii

Chionachne koenigii, commonly known as Indian borage, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly rough texture. Its flowers are blue or violet in color, with five petals and are arranged in clusters.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for gardens and landscapes.
