Cliffortia × subdura

Cliffortia × subdura, commonly known as the hybrid sweetpea or rock sweetpea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cliffortia paniculata and Cliffortia tenuifolia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found growing on rocky slopes and cliffs.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
