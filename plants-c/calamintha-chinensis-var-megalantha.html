Calamintha chinensis var. megalantha

Calamintha chinensis var. megalantha, commonly known as Chinese calamint, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a fragrant aroma. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically violet or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
