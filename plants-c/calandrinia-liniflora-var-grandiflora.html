Calandrinia liniflora var. grandiflora

Calandrinia liniflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, hairy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers. Its blooms are typically borne on erect stems and can last for several weeks during the summer months.

It is also known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions, which makes it a low-maintenance ornamental plant.
