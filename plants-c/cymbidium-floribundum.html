Cymbidium floribundum

Cymbidium floribundum, commonly known as the Chinese everlasting orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with a rhizomatous growth habit, producing multiple shoots from the base. The plant features linear to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Cymbidium floribundum produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous fragrant, semi-closed flowers in shades of white, pink, and red.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its long-lasting blooms.
