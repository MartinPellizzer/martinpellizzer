Canna rufa

Canna rufa, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, vibrantly colored leaves that range from green to red, and its showy, spadix-like flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is a member of the Cannaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Canna rufa is also known for its ability to add a dramatic focal point to water gardens or pond edges.
