Ceropegia leptotes

Ceropegia leptotes, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a climbing or trailing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it an attractive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia leptotes is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ornamental appeal.
