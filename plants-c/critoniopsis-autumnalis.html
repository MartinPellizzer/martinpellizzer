Critoniopsis autumnalis

Critoniopsis autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features upright, hairy stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant's leaves are lanceolate, with a rough texture and a strong aromatic scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or alpine environments.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
