Cuphea origanifolia var. minor

Cuphea origanifolia var. minor, commonly known as the silverleaf cuphea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features striking silver-white foliage with a velvety texture, complemented by showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a bushy growth habit and is often used in ornamental landscaping for its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
