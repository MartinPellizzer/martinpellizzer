Cynoglossum macrostylum

Cynoglossum macrostylum, commonly known as broadleaf bugloss, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and striking blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. The plant is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed sites.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
