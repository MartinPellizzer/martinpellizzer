Cirsium norikurense var. kisoense

Cirsium norikurense var. kisoense, commonly known as the Kiso thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces purple to pinkish flower heads composed of numerous small florets. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and streamside habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
