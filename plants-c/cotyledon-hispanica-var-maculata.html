Cotyledon hispanica var. maculata

Cotyledon hispanica var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinctive red or purple markings. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
