Crotalaria glabripedicellata

Crotalaria glabripedicellata, commonly known as the African spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and palmately lobed leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a unique spurred structure at the base of the petals.

Crotalaria glabripedicellata is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.
