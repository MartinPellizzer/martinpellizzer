Cypripedium × fitchianum

Cypripedium × fitchianum, commonly known as the Fitchian lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has a rhizomatous root system. Its flowers are usually pink or white with a yellow or orange lip, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
