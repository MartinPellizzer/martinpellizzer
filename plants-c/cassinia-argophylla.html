Cassinia argophylla

Cassinia argophylla, commonly known as the silver-leaf cassinia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, yellowish flower heads. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as woodlands and grasslands.

Cassinia argophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
