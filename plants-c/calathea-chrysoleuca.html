Calathea chrysoleuca

Calathea chrysoleuca, commonly known as the Golden Cane plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and yellow. Its foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting colors, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
