Calligonum kuerlense

Calligonum kuerlense, commonly known as the Kuerlense saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is well adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable species for ecological restoration.

Calligonum kuerlense plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
