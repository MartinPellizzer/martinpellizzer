Calanthe graciliscapa

Calanthe graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-leaf calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped leaves and its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Calanthe graciliscapa is often found in forest understories, where it benefits from the dappled sunlight and cool temperatures.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
