Callitris robusta var. verrucosa

Callitris robusta var. verrucosa, commonly known as the rough bark cypress, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its rough, fissured bark that provides a distinctive appearance. The plant has linear, needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in spirals along the stems. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain the plant's seeds.

This species is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland arid regions.
