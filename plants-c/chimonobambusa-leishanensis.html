Chimonobambusa leishanensis

Chimonobambusa leishanensis, commonly known as the Leishan bamboo, is a species of grass family plant native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. This bamboo is typically found in mountainous regions with moist, well-drained soils. It grows in dense stands and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The plant has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
