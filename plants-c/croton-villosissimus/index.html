Croton Villosissimus

Croton villosissimus is a strikingly unique plant known for its vibrant, often variegated foliage. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm and humid environments. Its hairy leaves give it a distinctive appearance, making it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. This species is also valued for its ability to adapt to various indoor conditions. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and light to flourish.
