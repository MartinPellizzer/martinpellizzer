Croton pyrifolius

Croton pyrifolius, commonly known as the fireleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that often display a vibrant yellow or red coloration, especially during the dry season. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
