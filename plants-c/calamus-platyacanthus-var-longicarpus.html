Calamus platyacanthus var. longicarpus

Calamus platyacanthus var. longicarpus, commonly known as the long-fruited flat-spined calamus, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical fruits that are typically clustered at the base of the plant. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive species within the genus Calamus.
