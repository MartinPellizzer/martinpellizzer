Caesalpinia culebrae

Caesalpinia culebrae, commonly known as the Culebrae candlewood, is a shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm regions.
