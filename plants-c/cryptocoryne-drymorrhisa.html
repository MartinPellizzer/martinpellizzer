Cryptocoryne drymorrhisa

Cryptocoryne drymorrhisa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically dark green in color. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests. Cryptocoryne drymorrhisa is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Its rhizomes form dense mats, which help in stabilizing the substrate and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
