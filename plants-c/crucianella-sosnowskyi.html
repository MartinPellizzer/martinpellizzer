Crucianella sosnowskyi

Crucianella sosnowskyi, commonly known as the Sosnowsky's crucianella, is a rare and endemic species native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant features small, elliptical leaves and produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers. It is characterized by its unique morphology and is of particular interest to botanists due to its limited distribution.

Due to its rarity, it is considered a conservation concern and is protected in its native environment.
