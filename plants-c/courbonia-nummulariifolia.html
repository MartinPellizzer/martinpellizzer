Courbonia nummulariifolia

Courbonia nummulariifolia, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is well adapted to arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
