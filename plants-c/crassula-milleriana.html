Crassula milleriana

Crassula milleriana, commonly known as the Miller's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

Crassula milleriana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
