Crataegus dzhairensis

Crataegus dzhairensis, commonly known as the Dzhairan hawthorn, is a species of shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny branches and small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red or orange berries that are typically edible and serve as a food source for various wildlife.

Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptic, with serrated edges, and the plant is adapted to thrive in dry, rocky soils.
