Cyathea ornata

Cyathea ornata, commonly known as the ornate tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which are composed of numerous pinnae.

Cyathea ornata is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
