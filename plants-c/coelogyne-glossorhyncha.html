Coelogyne glossorhyncha

Coelogyne glossorhyncha, commonly known as the golden throat orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, elongated leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive golden throat. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Coelogyne glossorhyncha is known for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
