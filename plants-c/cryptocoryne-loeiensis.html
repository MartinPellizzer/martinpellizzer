Cryptocoryne loeiensis

Cryptocoryne loeiensis, commonly known as the Loei cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. Cryptocoryne loeiensis is popular among aquarium enthusiasts due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.

Its ability to tolerate a wide range of water conditions makes it a versatile addition to both natural and artificial aquatic ecosystems.
