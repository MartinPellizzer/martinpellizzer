Caladium striatipes

Caladium striatipes, commonly known as the streaked arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive red and white streaks. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium striatipes thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage.

Its leaves can grow up to 12 inches in diameter, making it a popular choice for garden displays.
