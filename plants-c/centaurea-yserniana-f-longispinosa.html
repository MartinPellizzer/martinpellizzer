Centaurea yserniana f. longispinosa

Centaurea yserniana f. longispinosa, commonly known as the long-spined centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in dense, spiny hairs, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, composite-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine, soft hairs.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
