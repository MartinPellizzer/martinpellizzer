Cyphostemma junceum subsp. junceum

Cyphostemma junceum subsp.

junceum, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa. It features a woody stem with a milky sap that can be toxic if ingested.

The plant has small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces yellow flowers followed by small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

This species is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
