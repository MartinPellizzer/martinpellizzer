Cyrtochilum kraenzlinianum

Cyrtochilum kraenzlinianum, commonly known as the Kraenzlin's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper, a feature common in many orchid species. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to thrive. Its flowers are usually small, with a range of colors including white, yellow, and green, and bloom during the cooler months.

Cyrtochilum kraenzlinianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
