Cristaria integerrima var. integerrima

Cristaria integerrima var. integerrima, commonly known as the red-flowered bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of showy, tubular red or pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and long blooming period.
