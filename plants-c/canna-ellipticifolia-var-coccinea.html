Canna ellipticifolia var. coccinea

Canna ellipticifolia var. coccinea, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces showy, red-orange flower spikes that emerge from the leaf axils, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires moist, well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
