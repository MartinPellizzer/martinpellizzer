Coumarouna alata

Coumarouna alata, commonly known as the winged senna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with distinctive wing-like structures on the leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by elongated pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
