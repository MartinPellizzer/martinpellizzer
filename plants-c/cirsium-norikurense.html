Cirsium norikurense

Cirsium norikurense, commonly known as the Norikure thistle, is a perennial plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, and it is often found in regions with temperate climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and somewhat obscure species within its native range.
