Clerodendrum tricholobum

Clerodendrum tricholobum, commonly known as the white-flowered cebu ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with prominent veins.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
