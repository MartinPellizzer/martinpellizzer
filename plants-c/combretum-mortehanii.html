Combretum mortehanii

Combretum mortehanii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in late summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The bark is smooth and may exude a reddish sap when damaged.
