Ceropegia campanulata var. campanulata

Ceropegia campanulata var. campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple. The plant features fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
