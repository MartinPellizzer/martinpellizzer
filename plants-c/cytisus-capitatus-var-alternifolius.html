Cytisus capitatus var. alternifolius

Cytisus capitatus var. alternifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and alternate, narrow, elliptical leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in suitable climates.
