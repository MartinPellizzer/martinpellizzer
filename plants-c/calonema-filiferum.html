Calonema filiferum

Calonema filiferum, commonly known as the thread-leaf calonema, is a small, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, linear leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom in spring. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Calonema filiferum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant appearance and ease of care.
