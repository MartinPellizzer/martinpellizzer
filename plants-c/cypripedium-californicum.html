Cypripedium californicum

Cypripedium californicum, commonly known as the California lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its distinctive slipper-shaped flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and meadows. Its flowers are usually pink or white with a prominent pouch-like structure.

Cypripedium californicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also of interest in botanical research due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
