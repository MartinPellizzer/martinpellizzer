Cassia martiana

Cassia martiana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that resemble small showers, hence its common name. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia martiana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
