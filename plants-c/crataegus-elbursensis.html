Crataegus elbursensis

Crataegus elbursensis, commonly known as the Elburz hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Iran, particularly the Elburz Mountains. The plant is characterized by its thorny branches, small white or pinkish flowers, and red or orange berries that appear in late summer. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

The Elburz hawthorn plays a role in local ecosystems and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
