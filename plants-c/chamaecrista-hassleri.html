Chamaecrista hassleri

Chamaecrista hassleri, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, making it visually striking. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and forest edges.

Its botanical features include hairy stems and trifoliate leaves, which are typical of many legumes.
