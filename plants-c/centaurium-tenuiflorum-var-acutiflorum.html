Centaurium tenuiflorum var. acutiflorum

Centaurium tenuiflorum var. acutiflorum, commonly known as slenderflower centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, ascending stems with opposite, linear to lanceolate leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
