Chamaecrista aldabrensis

Chamaecrista aldabrensis, commonly known as the Aldabra burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the Socotra Archipelago.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny pods and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to extreme conditions.

Its botanical features make it a unique and notable species within the Fabaceae family.
