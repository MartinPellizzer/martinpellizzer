Croton thomsonii

Croton thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a variety of hues including red, yellow, and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
