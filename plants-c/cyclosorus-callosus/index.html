Cyclosorus Callosus

Cyclosorus callosus is a unique and lesser-known aquatic plant that thrives in freshwater environments. It is characterized by its distinctive, warty appearance and is often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. This species is part of the Marsileaceae family, which includes water clovers and other similar plants. Despite its beauty, it is rarely cultivated due to its specific habitat requirements. Its presence indicates a healthy ecosystem, making it an important indicator species for aquatic environments.
