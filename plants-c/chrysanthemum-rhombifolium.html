Chrysanthemum rhombifolium

Chrysanthemum rhombifolium, commonly known as the rhombic leaf chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its rhombic-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow to orange flowers. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The flowers bloom in the fall, adding color to the garden during the cooler months.
