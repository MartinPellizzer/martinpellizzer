Carmichaelia curta var. glabra

Carmichaelia curta var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Carmichaelia, is a perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are usually smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. It produces yellow flowers in spring, which are clustered in racemes, and it is often found in coastal and subalpine habitats.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
