Cistus Salviifolius Proles Fruticans

Cistus salviifolius proles fruticans is a unique plant species known for its striking appearance and ecological significance. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant features dark green leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring. Its ability to produce fruit even on its stems makes it a distinctive member of the Cistus genus. This plant plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining biodiversity in its native habitat.
