Canavalia rusiosperma

Canavalia rusiosperma, commonly known as the yellow burr bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, spiny pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
