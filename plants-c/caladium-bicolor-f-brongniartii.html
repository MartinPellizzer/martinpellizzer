Caladium bicolor f. brongniartii

Caladium bicolor f. brongniartii, commonly known as the 'Brongniartii' caladium, is a cultivar prized for its striking, variegated foliage.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is native to South America, specifically Brazil. The leaves are typically heart-shaped with intricate patterns of white, cream, and green, creating a dramatic visual effect. This plant thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often used as an ornamental plant in gardens and indoor spaces due to its vibrant appearance.
