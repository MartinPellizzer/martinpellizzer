Caucalis mossamedensis

Caucalis mossamedensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean caucalis, is a small, mat-forming moss that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit and dark green, wiry stems. The leaves are narrow and often recurved, with a smooth, glossy surface. This species is typically found in rocky crevices and along the edges of streams in the Mediterranean region.

It plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and maintaining moisture in its habitat.
