Chisocheton sandoricocarpus

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines, particularly found in the islands of Mindanao and Samar. Its flowers are small and clustered, with yellowish-green petals, and it produces a spherical, woody fruit that contains several seeds.
