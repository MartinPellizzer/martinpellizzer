Cyclophorus nummulariifolius

Cyclophorus nummulariifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf cyclophorus, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features small, silver-gray leaves that are round or kidney-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tiny, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique foliage and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated for ornamental purposes.
