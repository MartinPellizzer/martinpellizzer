Chaetocalyx magniflorus

Chaetocalyx magniflorus, commonly known as the magnificent flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The species is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in landscaping settings.
