Centipeda racemosa var. lanata

Centipeda racemosa var. lanata, commonly known as the hairy centipeda, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in botany and ecological research.
