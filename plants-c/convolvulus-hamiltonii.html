Convolvulus hamiltonii

Convolvulus hamiltonii, commonly known as the Hamilton's morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a trailing or climbing herb that produces vibrant blue or violet flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its heliotropic behavior, where the flowers follow the sun. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Convolvulus hamiltonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
