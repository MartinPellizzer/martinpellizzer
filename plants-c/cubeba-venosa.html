Cubeba venosa

Cubeba venosa, commonly known as cubeb, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, dark purple berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its flowers are small and yellow, often clustered in inflorescences.

Cubeb is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
