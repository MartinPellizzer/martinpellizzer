Centaurea arachnoidea subsp. arachnoidea

Centaurea arachnoidea subsp.

arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its striking, spiky inflorescence and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

Native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, this species thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

Its common name derives from the spider-like appearance of its flower heads.
