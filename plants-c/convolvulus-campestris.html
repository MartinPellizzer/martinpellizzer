Convolvulus campestris

Convolvulus campestris, commonly known as field bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale purple or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its flowers are typically five-petaled and have a tubular structure at the base.

Field bindweed is known for its aggressive growth and ability to spread through extensive root systems.
