Cotyledon subulifolia

Cotyledon subulifolia, commonly known as the needleleaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and pointed, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its ability to store water in its leaves. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months.

Cotyledon subulifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
