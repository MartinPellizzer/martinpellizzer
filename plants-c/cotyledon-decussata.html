Cotyledon decussata

Cotyledon decussata, commonly known as the wrinkled cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern and display a variety of colors, including green, red, and purple. It produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, and it is relatively easy to care for in a sunny, well-draining environment.

This plant is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
