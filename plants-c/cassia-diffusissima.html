Cassia diffusissima

Cassia diffusissima, commonly known as the Chinese cassia, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Cassia diffusissima thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
