Clematis tenuimarginata

Clematis tenuimarginata, commonly known as the narrow-margin clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features slender, climbing stems with small, trifoliate leaves that have a distinctive narrow margin. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale blue, typically blooming in late spring. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden trellises or supports.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its graceful growth habit and seasonal floral display.
