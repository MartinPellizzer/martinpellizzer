Convolvulus pulcher

Convolvulus pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the bindweed family, Convolvulaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
