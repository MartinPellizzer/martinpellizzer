Commelina guyanensis

Commelina guyanensis, commonly known as the Guyana arum, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many species of flowering plants. The plant features striking, arrow-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple sheath at the base. Its flowers are small and clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a spathe that is typically white or pale green.
