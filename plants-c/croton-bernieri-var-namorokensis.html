Croton bernieri var. namorokensis

Croton bernieri var. namorokensis, commonly known as the Namorokensis croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
