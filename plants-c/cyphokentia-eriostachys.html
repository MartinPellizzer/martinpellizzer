Cyphokentia eriostachys

Cyphokentia eriostachys, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a unique member of the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its unbranched trunk and feathery, pinnately compound leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish male cones and large, spiky female cones, which are essential for its reproduction. Native to Australia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.
