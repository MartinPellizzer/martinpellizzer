Cotoneaster hsingshangensis

Cotoneaster hsingshangensis, commonly known as the Hsingshang cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is typically found in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
