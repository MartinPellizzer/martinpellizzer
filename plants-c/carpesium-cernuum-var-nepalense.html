Carpesium cernuum var. nepalense

Carpesium cernuum var. nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal goatweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal and northern India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
