Campsis chinensis f. thunbergii

Campsis chinensis f. thunbergii, commonly known as the Chinese trumpetflower or Thunberg's flame bush, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters.

The fruit is a follicle containing several winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
