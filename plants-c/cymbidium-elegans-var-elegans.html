Cymbidium elegans var. elegans

Cymbidium elegans var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to China and is prized for its striking, long-lasting blooms.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in temperate climates. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, elongated shape and a pleasant fragrance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

This variety is also noted for its ease of care and ability to bloom reliably under proper conditions.
