Crotalaria grandiflora

Crotalaria grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cowpea, is a tropical herbaceous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with distinctive black veins, which bloom in clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several elliptical seeds.

Crotalaria grandiflora is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
