Cyrtopodium polyphyllum

Cyrtopodium polyphyllum, commonly known as the many-leaved cattleya, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and numerous, often clustered, leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white with a yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cyrtopodium polyphyllum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
