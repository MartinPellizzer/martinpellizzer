Corynephorus laxus

Corynephorus laxus, commonly known as the slender grass or narrowleaf grass, is a species of grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. Corynephorus laxus produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is a hardy plant that is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
