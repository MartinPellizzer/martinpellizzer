Cymbopetalum oppositiflorum

Cymbopetalum oppositiflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple to violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbopetalum oppositiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticulture.
