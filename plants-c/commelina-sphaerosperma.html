Commelina sphaerosperma

Commelina sphaerosperma, commonly known as the globe spheroberry, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes a variety of flowering plants often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
