Chlorophytum chloranthum

Chlorophytum chloranthum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually green with distinctive yellow or white markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are succulent, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Chlorophytum chloranthum is also noted for its air-purifying properties, as it can remove toxins from the air.
