Consolea falcata

Consolea falcata, commonly known as the sword cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 10 feet in height. The plant has sharp, spiny leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Consolea falcata is often cultivated for its striking appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
