Cleisostoma menghaiense

Cleisostoma menghaiense, commonly known as the Menghai orchid, is a species of orchid native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil.
