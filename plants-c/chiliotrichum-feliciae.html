Chiliotrichum feliciae

Chiliotrichum feliciae, commonly known as the fairy duster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its fluffy, white to pale yellow flower heads that resemble miniature dusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Chiliotrichum feliciae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
