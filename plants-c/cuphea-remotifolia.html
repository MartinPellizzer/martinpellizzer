Cuphea remotifolia

Cuphea remotifolia, commonly known as the remoteleaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. Cuphea remotifolia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, thriving in warm, tropical climates.
