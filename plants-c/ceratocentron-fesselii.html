Ceratocentron fesselii

Ceratocentron fesselii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a perennial herb native to tropical Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils. It is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in warm climates.
