Cymbidium sundaicum

Cymbidium sundaicum, commonly known as the Sunda Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms in winter and early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Cymbidium sundaicum is also valued for its fragrance and is often used in traditional floral arrangements.
