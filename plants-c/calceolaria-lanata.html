Calceolaria lanata

Calceolaria lanata, commonly known as the woolly slipper flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking, slipper-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Calceolaria lanata is valued for its unique morphology and vibrant floral display.
