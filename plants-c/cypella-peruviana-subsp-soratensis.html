Cypella peruviana subsp. soratensis

Cypella peruviana subsp.

soratensis, commonly known as the Peruvian cypella, is a perennial herb native to the high Andes of Peru. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes caraway and parsley, and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
