Clerodendrum formosanum

Clerodendrum formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that contain seeds.

Clerodendrum formosanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
