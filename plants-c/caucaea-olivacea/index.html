Caucaea Olivacea

Caucaea olivacea is a rare and unique plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia. It is known for its striking olive-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires specific humidity levels to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the focus of conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of scientific and botanical interest.
