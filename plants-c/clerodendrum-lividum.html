Clerodendrum lividum

Clerodendrum lividum, commonly known as the purple-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly rough texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The genus Clerodendrum is part of the Rubiaceae family, known for its diverse and often showy flowers.
