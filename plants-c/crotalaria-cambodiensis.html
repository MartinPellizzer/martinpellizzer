Crotalaria cambodiensis

Crotalaria cambodiensis, commonly known as Cambodian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves and distinctive yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is commonly found in disturbed soils.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become an invasive species in certain regions.
