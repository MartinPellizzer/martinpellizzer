Campanula delicatula

Campanula delicatula, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, violet, or white. The leaves are typically narrow and lanceolate, arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
