Cleisostoma macphersonii

Cleisostoma macphersonii, commonly known as the Macpherson's cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
