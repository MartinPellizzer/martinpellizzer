Crassula aurosensis

Crassula aurosensis, commonly known as the "Snowy Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula aurosensis is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and attractive appearance.
