Cattleya × elegans var. gigantea

Cattleya × elegans var. gigantea, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, featuring vibrant colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with long, arching leaves that support its heavy floral display. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires a warm, humid environment with good air circulation.

The flowers typically bloom once a year, making it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
