Celmisia ramulosa

Celmisia ramulosa, commonly known as the slender celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow blooms.

Celmisia ramulosa is often found in the South Island of New Zealand and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
