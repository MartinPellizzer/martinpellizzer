Cotyledon chlorantha

Cotyledon chlorantha, commonly known as the blue cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
