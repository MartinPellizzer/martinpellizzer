Chrysanthemum naktongense var. dissectum

Chrysanthemum naktongense var. dissectum, commonly known as the Naktong dissectum chrysanthemum, is a decorative plant native to Korea.

It is characterized by its finely cut, feathery leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a potted plant due to its compact growth habit and seasonal blooms.

This variety is also valued for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
