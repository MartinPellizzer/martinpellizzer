Cyperus iria var. paniciformis

Cyperus iria var. paniciformis, commonly known as the panicled flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flower heads. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in wetlands and along water bodies. Its flowers are usually pale green to brown, and it produces small, three-sided berries.

This variant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
