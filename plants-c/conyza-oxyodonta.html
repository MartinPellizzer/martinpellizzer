Conyza oxyodonta

Conyza oxyodonta, commonly known as the common fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.

Conyza oxyodonta is also noted for its potential as a weed in agricultural areas.
