Calanthe formosana

Calanthe formosana, commonly known as the Formosan calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are relatively short, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Calanthe formosana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
