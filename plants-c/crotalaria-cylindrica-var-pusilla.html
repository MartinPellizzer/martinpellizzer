Crotalaria cylindrica var. pusilla

Crotalaria cylindrica var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf cylindrical cowpea, is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods that mature to a brownish hue. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is often grown as a forage crop or for ornamental purposes due to its compact size and attractive blooms.
