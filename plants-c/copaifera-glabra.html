Copaifera glabra

Copaifera glabra, commonly known as the smooth copaiba, is a species of tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant is notable for its aromatic resin, which has been used traditionally for its medicinal properties. Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
