Ceropegia deflersiana

Ceropegia deflersiana, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used as a decorative hanging plant or in rock gardens.
