Crepidiastrum denticulatum var. cornutum

Crepidiastrum denticulatum var. cornutum, commonly known as the horned morning glory, is a tropical vine characterized by its distinctive, horn-like bracts.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant is native to regions of Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The vine is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, climbing plants.
