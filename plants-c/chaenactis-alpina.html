Chaenactis alpina

Chaenactis alpina, commonly known as the alpine ragwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

Chaenactis alpina is adapted to cold climates and can tolerate harsh environmental conditions typical of high-altitude regions.
