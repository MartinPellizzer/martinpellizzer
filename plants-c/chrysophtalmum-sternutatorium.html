Chrysophtalmum sternutatorium

Chrysophtalmum sternutatorium, commonly known as the sneezeweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features upright, hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers bloom in clusters, producing a striking visual display during the growing season.

It is often found in open grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun.
