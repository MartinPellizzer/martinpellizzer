Centaurea sylvatica var. catalaunica

Centaurea sylvatica var. catalaunica, commonly known as the Catalan thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive central disk, typically blooming from June to August. The plant has spiny, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in Mediterranean flora.
