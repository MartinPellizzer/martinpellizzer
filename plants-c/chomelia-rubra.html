Chomelia rubra

Chomelia rubra, commonly known as the red chomelia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers, which are often clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

Chomelia rubra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
