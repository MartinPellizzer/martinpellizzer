Cotyledon obermeyeriana

Cotyledon obermeyeriana, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Coral Cactus," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often red or purple, with a waxy texture that helps it store water. The plant produces vibrant orange or yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
