Croton chunianus

Croton chunianus, commonly known as the Chinese croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, including regions of China, India, and parts of Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its showy, colorful leaves, which can range from yellow to red and orange, often with striking patterns. It produces small, greenish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

Croton chunianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
