Croton seccoi

Croton seccoi, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton seccoi typically grows in moist, shaded environments and can reach heights of up to 3 meters.
