Chamaemelum pinnatifidum

Chamaemelum pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tansy, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and its yellow flowers are typically arranged in clusters. Chamaemelum pinnatifidum is known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
