Cirsium erisithales var. hybridum

Cirsium erisithales var. hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads that bloom in late summer.
