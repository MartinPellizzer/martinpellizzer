Chrysosplenium barbeyi

Chrysosplenium barbeyi, commonly known as the barbey's golden spleenwort, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a small, evergreen perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy layer of trichomes. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Chrysosplenium barbeyi is valued for its ability to thrive in challenging conditions and its aesthetic value in garden settings.
