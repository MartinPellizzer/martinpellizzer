Cistus libanotis var. pruinosus

Cistus libanotis var. pruinosus, commonly known as the Prunose Rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with a distinctive silver-gray or whitish pubescence. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink to violet, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
