Cuphea mimuloides f. gratioloides

Cuphea mimuloides f. gratioloides, commonly known as the pink flamebush, is a strikingly ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range from pink to red.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is native to Mexico, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of showy bracts that enhance its visual appeal.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant due to its compact growth habit and drought tolerance.
