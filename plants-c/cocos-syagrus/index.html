Cocos Syagrus

Cocos syagrus is a striking palm native to northeastern Brazil. Known for its vibrant green fronds and tall, slender trunk, it is a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with plenty of sunlight. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite in gardens and landscapes. This plant also has cultural and ecological significance in its native region.
