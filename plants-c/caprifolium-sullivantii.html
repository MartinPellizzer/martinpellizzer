Caprifolium sullivantii

Caprifolium sullivantii, commonly known as Sullivant's honeysuckle, is a rare flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring.
