Centrosema heteroneurum

Centrosema heteroneurum, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a legume, belonging to the Fabaceae family, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a deep taproot system, allowing it to thrive in poor soils and drought conditions. It is often found in open habitats such as prairies and disturbed areas.

Its seeds are adapted for dispersal by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
