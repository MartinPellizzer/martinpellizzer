Chrysophyllum leptocarpum

Chrysophyllum leptocarpum, commonly known as the dwarf star apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in home gardens.
