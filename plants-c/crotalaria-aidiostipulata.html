Crotalaria aidiostipulata

Crotalaria aidiostipulata, commonly known as the slender cowpea or small-leaved velvetbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive yellow flowers with a spurred structure. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.

Crotalaria aidiostipulata is also noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
