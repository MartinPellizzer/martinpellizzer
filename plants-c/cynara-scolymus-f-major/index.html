Cynara Scolymus F. Major

Cynara scolymus f. major is a unique variant of the artichoke plant known for its large, edible leaves and tender hearts. This cultivar is prized in culinary circles for its robust flavor and versatility in cooking. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. The plant's striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both ornamental and edible gardens. Its cultivation offers a rewarding blend of beauty, nutrition, and gastronomic potential.
