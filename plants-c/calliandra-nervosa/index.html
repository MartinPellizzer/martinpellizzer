Calliandra Nervosa

Calliandra tree, scientifically known as Calliandra nervosa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is recognized for its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that bloom in shades of pink and red, adding visual appeal to landscapes. The tree is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its showy flowers and attractive foliage. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Despite its beauty, the Calliandra tree also holds ecological significance, providing nectar for pollinators and supporting local biodiversity.
