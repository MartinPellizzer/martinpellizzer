Cryptanthus bromelioides

Cryptanthus bromelioides, commonly known as the "Cushion Plant," is a species of flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth habit. The plant features vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often in spring or early summer. Its leaves are thick and waxy, forming a dense, water-holding structure at the center.

Cryptanthus bromelioides is well-suited for indoor cultivation and thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate humidity.
