Calamagrostis Zenkeri

Calamagrostis zenkeri is a striking ornamental grass native to southern Africa. It features tall, slender culms that sway gracefully in the wind. Its leaves are narrow and silver-blue, creating a striking visual contrast. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favorite among landscape designers.
