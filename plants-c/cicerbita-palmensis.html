Cicerbita palmensis

Cicerbita palmensis, commonly known as the Palmese bittercress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. It features small, cruciform flowers that are usually white or pale yellow.

Its leaves are pinnately divided, and the plant produces small, elongated pods containing several seeds.
