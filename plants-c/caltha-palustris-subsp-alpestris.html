Caltha palustris subsp. alpestris

Caltha palustris subsp.

alpestris, commonly known as the alpine goldilocks, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to alpine regions of Europe. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous areas. It has slender, upright stems with trifoliate leaves that are deeply lobed.
