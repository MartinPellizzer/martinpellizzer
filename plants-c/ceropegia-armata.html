Ceropegia armata

Ceropegia armata, commonly known as the snake plant or mother of thousands, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, elongated leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and sunlight.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
