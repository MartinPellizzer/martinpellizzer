Canna eximia

Canna eximia, commonly known as the giant cane plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length, and its showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a member of the Cannaceae family and thrives in moist, sunny environments. It is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and dramatic floral display.

Canna eximia is relatively low maintenance and can be grown in containers or in garden beds with well-draining soil.
