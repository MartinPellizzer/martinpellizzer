Crambe juncea

Crambe juncea, commonly known as yellow mustard, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It features alternate, pinnately compound leaves with serrated edges, and its flowers are yellow, arranged in racemes. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is cultivated for its oilseed, which is used in the production of biodiesel and industrial applications.

Crambe juncea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a beneficial cover crop.
