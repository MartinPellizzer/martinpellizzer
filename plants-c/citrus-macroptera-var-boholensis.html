Citrus macroptera var. boholensis

Citrus macroptera var. boholensis, commonly known as Bohol citrus or Bohol lemon, is a rare citrus species native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits with a thick, aromatic rind. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is used in traditional medicine and local cuisine, valued for its unique flavor and medicinal properties.

This variety is notable for its limited distribution and is considered an important part of the region's biodiversity.
