Cedrela tubiflora var. lagenaria

Cedrela tubiflora var. lagenaria, commonly known as the African teak, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its straight, heavy, and durable wood, which is highly valued for furniture and construction. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It produces small, pale yellow flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its role in local ecosystems.
