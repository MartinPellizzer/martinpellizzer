Carica × tunariensis

Carica × tunariensis, commonly known as the yellow sapote, is a hybrid plant in the genus Carica, native to Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that produces yellow or orange fruits with a smooth, leathery skin. The fruit has a sweet, creamy pulp that is rich in nutrients, including vitamins A and C. It thrives in tropical climates with well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its edible fruit.
