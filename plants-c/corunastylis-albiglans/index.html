Corunastylis Albiglans

Corunastylis albiglans is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia. It is known for its striking white petals and unique floral structure that sets it apart from other members of the orchid family. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and is the subject of conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and preservation.
