Camellia parvipetala

Camellia parvipetala, commonly known as the small-petaled camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and provide a striking contrast to its blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia parvipetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its winter flowering.
