Campomanesia xanthocarpa var. xanthocarpa

Campomanesia xanthocarpa var. xanthocarpa, commonly known as the yellow mangabey apple, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves with a reddish underside. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are typically harvested when fully ripe. The fruit has a sweet, tangy flavor and is often consumed fresh or used in local cuisines.

It also has potential medicinal uses due to its high content of bioactive compounds.
