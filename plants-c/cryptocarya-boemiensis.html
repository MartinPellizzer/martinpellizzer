Cryptocarya boemiensis

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically glossy and elliptical in shape. Cryptocarya boemiensis is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
