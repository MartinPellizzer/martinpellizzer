Clethra nanchuanensis

Clethra nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing an attractive appearance throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Clethra nanchuanensis is valued for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
