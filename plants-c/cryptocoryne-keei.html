Cryptocoryne keei

Cryptocoryne keei, commonly known as the Java crypts, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with a yellowish or brownish midrib. This plant is popular in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions. It prefers moderate to high light levels and can grow in both soft and hard water.

Cryptocoryne keei is also noted for its ability to tolerate a wide range of temperatures and is relatively easy to care for.
