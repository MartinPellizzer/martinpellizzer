Chlorophytum angustissimum

Chlorophytum angustissimum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spider plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear small, linear leaves arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to propagate through runners and offsets contributes to its resilience and ease of cultivation.
