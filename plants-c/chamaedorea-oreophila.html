Chamaedorea oreophila

Chamaedorea oreophila, commonly known as the mountain button fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Central America.

It features delicate, arching fronds that are typically light green with a slightly hairy texture. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining, moist soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Chamaedorea oreophila is valued for its ability to purify indoor air and enhance the aesthetic of indoor spaces.
