Centaurea × lebeliana

Centaurea × lebeliana, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, conical inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bracteate flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Centaurea × lebeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
