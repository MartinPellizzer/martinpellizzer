Catasetum pileatum var. regale

Catasetum pileatum var. regale, commonly known as the royal catasetum, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces unique, elongated pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in variable environmental conditions. Its flowers are also notable for their intricate structure, featuring specialized pollination mechanisms.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
