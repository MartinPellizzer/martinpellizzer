Combretum populifolium

Combretum populifolium, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy white to pink flowers that appear in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often pubescent underneath. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its ability to withstand drought and its use in traditional medicine.
