Cirsium matsumurae

Cirsium matsumurae, commonly known as Matsumura's thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forests and stream sides. It has a short stem with basal leaves that are usually lobed or toothed.

Cirsium matsumurae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
