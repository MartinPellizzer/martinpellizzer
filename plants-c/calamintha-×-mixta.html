Calamintha × mixta

Calamintha × mixta, commonly known as hybrid calamint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its aromatic, slightly fuzzy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Calamintha × mixta is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and mild mint-like fragrance.
