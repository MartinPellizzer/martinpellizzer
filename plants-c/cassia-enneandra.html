Cassia enneandra

Cassia enneandra, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, slender pods that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia enneandra is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
