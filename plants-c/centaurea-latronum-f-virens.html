Centaurea latronum f. virens

Centaurea latronum f. virens, commonly known as the green-centred centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and bright yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is noted for its vibrant coloration and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
