Callitriche hegelmaieriana

Callitriche hegelmaieriana, commonly known as the Hegelmaier's water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Callitrichaceae.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wet meadows. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often floating or submerged, with small, opposite leaves that are usually less than 1 centimeter in length. Its flowers are tiny, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late summer.

This species is native to parts of Europe and is often found in still or slow-moving water bodies.
