Centaurea koeieana

Centaurea koeieana, commonly known as the Koeie's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with the stems often branching near the base.

Centaurea koeieana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
