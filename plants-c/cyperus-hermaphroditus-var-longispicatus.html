Cyperus hermaphroditus var. longispicatus

Cyperus hermaphroditus var. longispicatus, commonly known as the broadleaf paper plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically broad and flat, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that are often reddish or purplish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to various aquatic environments.
