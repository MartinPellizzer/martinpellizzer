Chrysosplenium lanuginosum var. lanuginosum

Chrysosplenium lanuginosum var. lanuginosum, commonly known as the downy chrysosplenium, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, downy hairs. Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental plant in its native habitats.
