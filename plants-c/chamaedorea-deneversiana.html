Chamaedorea deneversiana

Chamaedorea deneversiana, commonly known as the Deneversiana palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Costa Rica and Panama. This plant is characterized by its slender, upright trunk and pinnate, feather-like leaves that give it a graceful appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in understory layers of tropical forests.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries, contributing to its ecological significance in its native habitat.
