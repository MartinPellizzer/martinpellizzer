Croton undulatus

Croton undulatus, commonly known as the wavy croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage, which can range from green to yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Croton undulatus is popular as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaves and adaptability to indoor conditions.
