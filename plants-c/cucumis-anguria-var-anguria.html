Cucumis anguria var. anguria

Cucumis anguria var. anguria, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a vine species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces distinctive, spiky, elongated fruits that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and prefers well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits, which have a mild, cucumber-like flavor.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
