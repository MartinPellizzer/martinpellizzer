Crassula barbata subsp. broomii

Crassula barbata subsp.

broomii, commonly known as Broomii Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
