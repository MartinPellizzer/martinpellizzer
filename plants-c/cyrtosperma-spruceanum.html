Cyrtosperma spruceanum

Cyrtosperma spruceanum, commonly known as the Malayan arrowroot, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves and a tuberous root system. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tubers of Cyrtosperma spruceanum are used as a starchy food source in some local communities.
