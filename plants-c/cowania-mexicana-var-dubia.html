Cowania mexicana var. dubia

Cowania mexicana var. dubia, commonly known as the Mexican cowania, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with grey-green, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
