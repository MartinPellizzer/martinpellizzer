Cooperia smallii

Cooperia smallii, commonly known as the smallii grass, is a species of flowering plant in the Poaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial grass that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant is native to South Africa, where it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. Its narrow, grass-like leaves are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Cooperia smallii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
