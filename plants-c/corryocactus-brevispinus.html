Corryocactus brevispinus

Corryocactus brevispinus, commonly known as the short-spined cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a small, columnar cactus with a compact growth habit and smooth, green stems. The plant features short, cylindrical spines that are typically brown or yellowish in color. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Corryocactus brevispinus is adapted to thrive in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.
