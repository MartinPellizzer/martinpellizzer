Conophytum marginatum var. marginatum

Conophytum marginatum var. marginatum, commonly known as the "Margined Lithops," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features two fleshy, paired leaves that are typically green with distinct marginal markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the cooler months, usually in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
