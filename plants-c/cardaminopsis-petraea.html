Cardaminopsis petraea

Cardaminopsis petraea, commonly known as the alpine buttercup, is a perennial herb native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae and is characterized by its small, yellow, cup-shaped flowers. Cardaminopsis petraea is a low-growing plant with a compact habit, often forming dense mats in its natural habitat.
