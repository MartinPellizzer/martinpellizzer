Calanthe versicolor

Calanthe versicolor, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe versicolor is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
