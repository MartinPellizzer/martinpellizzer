Critoniopsis woytkowskii

Critoniopsis woytkowskii, commonly known as the Woytkowski's orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
