Cyperus Stenophyllus Var. Ornans

Ornamental flatsedge, scientifically known as Cyperus stenophyllus var. ornans, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is prized for its elegant, grass-like foliage that adds a unique texture to water gardens and ponds. This variant of flatsedge is particularly popular among horticulturists for its ability to thrive in wet environments without requiring excessive maintenance. Its slender, dark green leaves create a lush visual effect, making it a versatile choice for landscaping. Whether used in ponds, bog gardens, or as a decorative filler, ornamental flatsedge offers both beauty and ecological benefits.
