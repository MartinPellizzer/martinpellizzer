Calandrinia porifera

Calandrinia porifera, commonly known as the porous portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for arid environments. Calandrinia porifera is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and striking floral display.
