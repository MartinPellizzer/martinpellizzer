Cryptolepis elegans var. ciliata

Cryptolepis elegans var. ciliata, commonly known as the silky cryptolepis, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with soft, silky hairs along the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
