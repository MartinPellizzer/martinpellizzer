Clinopodium banaoense

Clinopodium banaoense, commonly known as Banao oregano, is a perennial herb native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of montane forests. Its leaves are aromatic and have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.

Clinopodium banaoense is valued for its potential in herbal medicine and as a source of essential oils.
