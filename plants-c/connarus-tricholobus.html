Connarus tricholobus

Connarus tricholobus, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its dense, dark-colored wood, which is highly valued for musical instruments.
