Campanula rotundifolia var. lubiciana

Campanula rotundifolia var. lubiciana, commonly known as the round-leaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has rounded, succulent leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or wet meadows.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ease of care.
