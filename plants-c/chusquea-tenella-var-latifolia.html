Chusquea tenella var. latifolia

Chusquea tenella var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf chusquea, is a species of bamboo native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 5 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a dense, grass-like foliage. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
