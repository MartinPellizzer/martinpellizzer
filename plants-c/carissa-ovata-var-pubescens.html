Carissa ovata var. pubescens

Carissa ovata var. pubescens, commonly known as the rough carissa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its spiny, woody stems and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces yellow, berry-like fruits that are edible but have a bitter taste. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
