Cynodon polevansii

Cynodon polevansii, commonly known as the Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are clustered along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open habitats.

Cynodon polevansii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and erosion control.
