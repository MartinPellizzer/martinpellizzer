Cattleya gigas var. sanderiana

Cattleya gigas var. sanderiana, commonly known as the Sander's giant orchid, is a hybrid orchid renowned for its large, showy flowers.

It features thick, fleshy pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green. The flowers are usually pale yellow with deep orange-red markings and can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. This variety is prized for its vibrant color and dramatic appearance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.
