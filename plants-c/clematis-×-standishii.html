Clematis × standishii

Clematis × standishii, commonly known as the Blue Sweets, is a hybrid vine native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to climb using its twining stems. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically deep green and slightly hairy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy blooms.

Clematis × standishii is well-suited for trellises or as a climbing plant in gardens due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
