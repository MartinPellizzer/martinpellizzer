Cymbidium javanicum var. aspidistrifolium

Cymbidium javanicum var. aspidistrifolium, commonly known as the Java orchid or aspidistrifolium orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
