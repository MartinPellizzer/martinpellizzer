Cycladenia humilis var. humilis

Cycladenia humilis var. humilis, commonly known as the small blue phlox, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features showy, clustered blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and typically reaches a height of only a few inches. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Cycladenia humilis var. humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
