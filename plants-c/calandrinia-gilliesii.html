Calandrinia gilliesii

Calandrinia gilliesii, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often appearing in clusters. The plant has hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy areas.

Calandrinia gilliesii is also known for its ability to bloom throughout the year in suitable conditions.
