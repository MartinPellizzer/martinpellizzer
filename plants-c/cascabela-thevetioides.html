Cascabela thevetioides

Cascabela thevetioides, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical plant native to Africa and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, star-shaped fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental and fruit-bearing plant in subtropical and tropical regions.
