Chirita orbicularis

Chirita orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf gesneria, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically orbicular in shape, giving it its common name. It produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom in the spring.

Chirita orbicularis is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
