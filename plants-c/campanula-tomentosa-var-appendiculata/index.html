Campanula Tomentosa Var. Appendiculata

Campanula tomentosa var. appendiculata is a unique and charming member of the bellflower family known for its fluffy, grayish foliage and delicate blue-violet blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders in horticulture. The variety appendiculata is particularly noted for its trailing habit and ability to form dense mats, making it both ornamental and easy to maintain. With its subtle beauty and adaptability, it remains a favorite among gardeners seeking a touch of wild elegance.
