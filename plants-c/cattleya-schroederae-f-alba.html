Cattleya schroederae f. alba

Cattleya schroederae f. alba, commonly known as the White Schroeder's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking white blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces large, fragrant flowers with a white coloration. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and ease of cultivation.

This variety is often grown in shaded environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.
