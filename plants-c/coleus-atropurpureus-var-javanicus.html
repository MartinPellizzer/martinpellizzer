Coleus atropurpureus var. javanicus

Coleus atropurpureus var. javanicus, commonly known as the Java coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of purple, red, and green. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

This ornamental plant is popular for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping and container gardening.
