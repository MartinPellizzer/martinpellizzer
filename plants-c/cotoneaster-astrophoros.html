Cotoneaster astrophoros

Cotoneaster astrophoros, commonly known as the star-bearing cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, dark green leaves that turn red or orange in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Cotoneaster astrophoros is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats, providing food for birds and other small animals.
