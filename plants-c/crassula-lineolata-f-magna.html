Crassula lineolata f. magna

Crassula lineolata f. magna, commonly known as the Giant Crassula or Broadleaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental leaves make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
