Chasmone tenuis

Chasmone tenuis, commonly known as the slender chasmone, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
