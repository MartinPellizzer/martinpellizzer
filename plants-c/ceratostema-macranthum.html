Ceratostema macranthum

Ceratostema macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered ceratostema, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of showy, white to pink flowers in late spring to early summer. Its botanical characteristics include a woody stem and small, oval-shaped fruits that mature into red berries.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
