Caladenia saggicola

Caladenia saggicola, commonly known as the Saggicola Spider-orchid, is an exquisite flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with slender, erect stems and delicate, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is adapted to the arid and semi-arid environments of its native region. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in full sun and has a short flowering period, usually during the cooler months.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in some areas.
