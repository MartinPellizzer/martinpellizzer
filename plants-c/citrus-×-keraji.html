Citrus × keraji

Citrus × keraji, commonly known as the kaffir lime.

This hybrid citrus plant is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive, bumpy, green fruit with a strong aromatic zest. The leaves are dark green and slightly serrated, while the flowers are fragrant and white. The fruit is often used in cooking for its intense flavor and is also valued in traditional medicine.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and plenty of sunlight.
