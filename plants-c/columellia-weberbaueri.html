Columellia weberbaueri

Columellia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's columnar cactus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cactaceae.

It is native to the arid regions of South America, particularly in Peru and Bolivia. This plant is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The stem is ribbed and covered with spines, and it produces yellow flowers during the dry season.

Columellia weberbaueri is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water availability.
