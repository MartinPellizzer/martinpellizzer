Costus foliaceus

Costus foliaceus, commonly known as the leaf costus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care contribute to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
