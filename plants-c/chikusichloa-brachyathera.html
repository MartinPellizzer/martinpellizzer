Chikusichloa brachyathera

Chikusichloa brachyathera, commonly known as the African daisy or chikusichloa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with a central disk surrounded by ray florets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
