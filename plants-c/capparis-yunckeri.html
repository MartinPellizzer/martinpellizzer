Capparis yunckeri

Capparis yunckeri, commonly known as the Yungkerran caper, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Capparidaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Capparis yunckeri is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
