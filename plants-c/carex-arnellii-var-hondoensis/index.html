Carex Arnellii Var. Hondoensis

Carex arnellii var. hondoensis is a striking sedge native to the wetlands of Japan. This variant is known for its dense, grass-like foliage that forms lush, green clumps. It thrives in moist to wet conditions, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes. The plant's ability to tolerate partial shade adds to its versatility in garden design. With its elegant appearance and ecological benefits, it is a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated environments.
