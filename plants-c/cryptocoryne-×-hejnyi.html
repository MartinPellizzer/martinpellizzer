Cryptocoryne × hejnyi

Cryptocoryne × hejnyi, commonly known as the hybrid cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid between Cryptocoryne balansae and Cryptocoryne wendtii, resulting in a plant with unique characteristics. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often with a dark green to bluish-green coloration. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is relatively hardy, making it a favored choice for aquariums.

Its ability to tolerate a range of water parameters contributes to its popularity among aquarists.
