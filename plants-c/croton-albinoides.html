Croton albinoides

Croton albinoides, commonly known as the white croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton albinoides typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
