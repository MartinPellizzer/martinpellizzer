Conioselinum chinense

Conioselinum chinense, commonly known as Chinese fennel, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes carrots, parsley, and dill. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in umbels. Its leaves are finely divided and aromatic, often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

The seeds are used as a spice and have a licorice-like flavor.
