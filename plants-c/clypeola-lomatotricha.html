Clypeola lomatotricha

Clypeola lomatotricha, commonly known as the woolly clypeola, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that helps it retain moisture in harsh conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters and attracting various pollinators.

Clypeola lomatotricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
