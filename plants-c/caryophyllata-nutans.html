Caryophyllata nutans

Caryophyllata nutans, commonly known as the nodding carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink or white flowers that droop downward, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun or partial shade. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, where it thrives in temperate climates.
