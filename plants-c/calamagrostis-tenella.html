Calamagrostis tenella

Calamagrostis tenella, commonly known as silky windgrass, is a perennial grass native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, arching stems that produce fluffy, silver-green panicles, which are its distinctive flowering structures. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate, feathery appearance and its ability to form dense, attractive clumps.

Calamagrostis tenella is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
