Corallodendron abyssinicum

Corallodendron abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian coral tree, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences, which consist of dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant in suitable regions.
