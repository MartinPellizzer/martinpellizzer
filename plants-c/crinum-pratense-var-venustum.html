Crinum pratense var. venustum

Crinum pratense var. venustum, commonly known as the showy crinum, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces large, showy white to pinkish flowers with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

The flowers bloom in late summer to early fall, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
