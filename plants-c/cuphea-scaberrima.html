Cuphea scaberrima

Cuphea scaberrima, commonly known as the rough cuphea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. Cuphea scaberrima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
