Cyrtandra × malacophylla

Cyrtandra × malacophylla, commonly known as the hybrid beauty leaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra and another closely related species, typically Cyrtandra malacophylla. The plant is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a range of colors, including deep green, purple, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
