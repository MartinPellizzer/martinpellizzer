Cineraria incana

Cineraria incana, commonly known as the woolly cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, silver-green leaves and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
