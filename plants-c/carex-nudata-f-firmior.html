Carex nudata f. firmior

Carex nudata f. firmior, commonly known as the firm-flowered bog sedge, is a variant of the bog sedge species native to wetland habitats in North America.

It is a perennial herb with grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and somewhat stiff. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in moist to wet soils, often found in bogs, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

Its foliage and growth habit make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
