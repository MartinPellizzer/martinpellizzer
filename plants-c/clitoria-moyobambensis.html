Clitoria moyobambensis

Clitoria moyobambensis, commonly known as the Moyobamba butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its legume fruits and often nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces vibrant purple or blue flowers that resemble butterflies, hence its common name. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and slightly hairy.
