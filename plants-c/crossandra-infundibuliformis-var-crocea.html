Crossandra infundibuliformis var. crocea

Crossandra infundibuliformis var. crocea, commonly known as the golden crossandra, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers, which are tubular and borne in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Crossandra infundibuliformis var. crocea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
