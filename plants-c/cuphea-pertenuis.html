Cuphea pertenuis

Cuphea pertenuis, commonly known as the slender cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance blooms.

Cuphea pertenuis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
