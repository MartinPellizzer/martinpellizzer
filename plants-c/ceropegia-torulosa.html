Ceropegia torulosa

Ceropegia torulosa, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which are pollinated by specific insects. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia torulosa is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
