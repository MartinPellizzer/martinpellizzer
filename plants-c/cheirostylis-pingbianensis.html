Cheirostylis pingbianensis

Cheirostylis pingbianensis, commonly known as the Pingbian orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinct coloration pattern. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
