Cissus duboisii

Cissus duboisii, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive reddish hue. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
