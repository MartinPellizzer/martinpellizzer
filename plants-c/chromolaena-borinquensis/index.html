Chromolaena Borinquensis

Chromolaena borinquensis is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Puerto Rico. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple blooms that attract a variety of pollinators. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Its unique appearance and ecological role make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions, posing challenges to local ecosystems.
