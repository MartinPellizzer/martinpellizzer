Centaurea arenaria subsp. borysthenica

Centaurea arenaria subsp.

borysthenica, commonly known as the Borysthenic sand-centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the steppes of Eastern Europe and Western Asia. It typically grows in dry, sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to harsh, arid environments.

The plant features erect, branching stems with narrow, linear leaves and produces bright yellow flowers with purple or brown centers. Its small, bracts and involucres are characteristic of the genus Centaurea.

It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed sites.
