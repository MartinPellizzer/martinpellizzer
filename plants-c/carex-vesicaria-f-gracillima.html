Carex vesicaria f. gracillima

Carex vesicaria f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender bladder sedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along streams. It produces tiny, greenish flowers that are wind-pollinated.

This variety is noted for its delicate appearance and is often used in ornamental wetland gardens.
