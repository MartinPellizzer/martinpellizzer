Cleomella medicaginea

Cleomella medicaginea, commonly known as the medicinal cleome, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cleomaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant has hairy leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its potential medicinal properties.

Cleomella medicaginea is also noted for its ornamental value due to its attractive flower clusters and robust growth habit.
