Cirsium afrum

Cirsium afrum, commonly known as the African thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. It has a robust root system that allows it to survive in arid conditions.

Cirsium afrum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
