Calathea juncea

Calathea juncea, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns in shades of green, silver, and pink. The plant is known for its unique leaf movement, where the leaves fold upwards at night and unfold in the morning. Calathea juncea prefers bright, indirect light and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and a fascinating subject for study in plant physiology.
