Chlorophytum floribundum

Chlorophytum floribundum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is a perennial evergreen herb that features long, arching leaves with a distinctive white stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright orange berries. It is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a popular houseplant.

Its air-purifying qualities also contribute to its popularity among indoor gardeners.
