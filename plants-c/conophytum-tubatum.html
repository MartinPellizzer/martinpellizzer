Conophytum tubatum

Conophytum tubatum, commonly known as the tuberous conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. Its unique tuberous root system helps it endure long periods of drought.

Conophytum tubatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and striking appearance.
