Cliffortia ilicifolia var. cordifolia

Cliffortia ilicifolia var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves that are heart-shaped, giving it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to its arid habitat. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found growing on slopes or cliffs.

This species is well adapted to dry conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.
