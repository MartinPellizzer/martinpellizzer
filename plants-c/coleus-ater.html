Coleus ater

Coleus ater, commonly known as black coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its dark purple to almost black foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a velvety texture. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or violet in color.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking leaf color and ease of care.
