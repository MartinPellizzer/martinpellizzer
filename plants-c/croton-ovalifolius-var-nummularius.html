Croton ovalifolius var. nummularius

Croton ovalifolius var. nummularius, commonly known as the Nummularius croton, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, round or oval-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.
