Crassula namaquensis var. lutea

Crassula namaquensis var. lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a yellowish tinge, especially under sunny conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
