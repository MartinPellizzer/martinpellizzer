Cymbidium suavissimum

Cymbidium suavissimum, commonly known as the sweet orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blossoms. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cymbidium suavissimum is a hardy orchid that can thrive in both indoor and outdoor settings with proper care.
