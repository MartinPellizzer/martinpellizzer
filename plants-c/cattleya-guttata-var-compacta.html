Cattleya guttata var. compacta

Cattleya guttata var. compacta, commonly known as the compact cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often bicolor flowers with a distinctive red or orange hue. The plant typically produces showy, fragrant blooms in late summer or early fall. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
