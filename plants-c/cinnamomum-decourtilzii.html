Cinnamomum decourtilzii

Cinnamomum decourtilzii, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum decourtilzii, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional Chinese medicine and for their aromatic properties.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.
