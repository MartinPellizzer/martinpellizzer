Cotula plumosa

Cotula plumosa, commonly known as the featherleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a fluffy appearance, and it produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. The plant typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas. Cotula plumosa is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
