Cyathea grandifolia var. bullata

Cyathea grandifolia var. bullata, commonly known as the Bullate tree fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The fronds are typically pinnately divided and have a distinctive, leathery texture. This variant is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

It plays an important ecological role in tropical forests by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
