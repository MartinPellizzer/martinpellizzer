Citrus × limonum var. laurae

Citrus × limonum var. laurae, commonly known as the Laura lemon, is a citrus hybrid characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits.

The plant is a vigorous shrub with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. The fruit is prized for its high vitamin C content and distinct, aromatic flavor.

This variety is often cultivated for both ornamental purposes and as a source of culinary and medicinal uses.
