Cypripedium canadense

Cypripedium canadense, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, large, slipper-shaped flower that is typically pink or white with a prominent yellow or orange interior. The plant has a single, erect stem that bears a single flower, and its leaves are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forested areas, particularly in the understory.

Cypripedium canadense is an important species in the orchid family, known for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
