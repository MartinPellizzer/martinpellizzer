Cirsium × jouffroyi

Cirsium × jouffroyi, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in disturbed habitats. Cirsium × jouffroyi is known for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy blooms.
