Cyclosorus oligophyllus var. lutescens

Cyclosorus oligophyllus var. lutescens, commonly known as the golden thread moss, is a variant of the cyclosorus genus within the family Pterobryaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves that are typically golden to yellowish in color. This moss thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found on rocks or tree trunks in temperate regions. Its structure consists of closely appressed, acrocarpous shoots with a distinct central axis.

The plant's unique coloration and morphology make it a notable species in ecological and botanical studies.
