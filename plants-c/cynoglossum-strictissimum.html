Cynoglossum strictissimum

Cynoglossum strictissimum, commonly known as narrow-leaved bugloss, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and can be propagated through seeds or division.
