Coleus malinvaldii

Coleus malinvaldii, commonly known as the Malinvald's coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant features opposite leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, red, and yellow. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color.

Coleus malinvaldii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking leaf patterns and adaptability to various growing conditions.
