Crassula × christyi

Crassula × christyi, commonly known as the "Lamb's Ear" or "Fleshy Crassula," is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Crassula × christyi is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
