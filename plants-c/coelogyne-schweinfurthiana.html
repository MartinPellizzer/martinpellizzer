Coelogyne schweinfurthiana

Coelogyne schweinfurthiana, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Coelogyne schweinfurthiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
