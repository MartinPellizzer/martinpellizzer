Clusia pseudohavetia

Clusia pseudohavetia, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or the glorybower, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is evergreen, meaning it retains its foliage throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens.

Clusia pseudohavetia is also known for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
