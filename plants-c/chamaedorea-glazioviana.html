Chamaedorea glazioviana

Chamaedorea glazioviana, commonly known as the Glaziov's bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clustered, feather-like leaves. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.

Chamaedorea glazioviana prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
