Callicarpa mollis f. ramosissima

Callicarpa mollis f. ramosissima, commonly known as the fragrant beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, vibrant purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its showy fruits attract birds and add visual interest to landscapes throughout the growing season.
