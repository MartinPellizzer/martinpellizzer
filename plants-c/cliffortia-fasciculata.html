Cliffortia fasciculata

Cliffortia fasciculata, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant produces clusters of small, white, woolly flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense, compact appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in harsh, sunny conditions.
