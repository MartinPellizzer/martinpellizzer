Centaurea masjedsoleymanensis

Centaurea masjedsoleymanensis, commonly known as the Masjed Soleiman centaurea, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its striking purple-blue flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its limited distribution and unique features, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
