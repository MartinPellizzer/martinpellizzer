Chenopodium quinoa var. lutescens

Chenopodium quinoa var. lutescens, commonly known as yellow quinoa, is a variety of the Andean plant quinoa, native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow seeds, which are rich in protein, fiber, and essential amino acids. The plant thrives in cool climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are edible and often used in traditional Andean cuisine.

This variety is valued for its nutritional profile and adaptability to diverse environmental conditions.
