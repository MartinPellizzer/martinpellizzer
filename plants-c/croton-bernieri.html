Croton bernieri

Croton bernieri, commonly known as the red croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
