Coprosma microphylla

Coprosma microphylla, commonly known as silver coprosma, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, oval-shaped leaves that give it a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by bright red or orange berries. It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

Coprosma microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
