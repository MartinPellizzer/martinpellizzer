Cyclophorus subfissus

Cyclophorus subfissus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cyclophorus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or coastal areas, where it adapts well to harsh environmental conditions.

The plant's structure and growth habits make it an important component of its native ecosystems.
