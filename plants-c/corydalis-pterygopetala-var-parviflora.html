Corydalis pterygopetala var. parviflora

Corydalis pterygopetala var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered winged corydalis, is a member of the Fumariaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a distinctive waxy coating.
