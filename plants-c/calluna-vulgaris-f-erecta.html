Calluna vulgaris f. erecta

Calluna vulgaris f. erecta, commonly known as the upright heather, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to western Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, upright habit, giving it a neat appearance. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in challenging garden conditions.
