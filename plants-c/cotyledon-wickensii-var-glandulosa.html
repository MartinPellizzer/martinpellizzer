Cotyledon wickensii var. glandulosa

Cotyledon wickensii var. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, glandular layer, giving it a unique texture. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
