Chimonobambusa marmorea f. gimmei

Chimonobambusa marmorea f. gimmei, commonly known as the Pink Bamboo, is a unique and visually striking plant native to China.

It is a clump-forming bamboo characterized by its striking pink to red-colored culms, which become more vibrant as the plant matures. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface that contrasts beautifully with the colorful stems. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile choice for various landscaping applications.
