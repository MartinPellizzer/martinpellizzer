Cynoctonum petiolatum

Cynoctonum petiolatum, commonly known as the yellow bell or yellow star, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a humid, warm climate to flourish.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant color and unique appearance.
