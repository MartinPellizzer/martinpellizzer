Coelogyne graciliscapa

Coelogyne graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks in the undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, and it produces showy, pendulous blooms during the dry season.

Coelogyne graciliscapa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
