Crossandra madagascariensis

Crossandra madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar flame flower, is a striking herbaceous plant native to Madagascar.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting yellow or white throat. The plant typically grows in tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its foliage is usually dark green and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Crossandra madagascariensis is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and floral arrangements.
