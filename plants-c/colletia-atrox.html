Colletia atrox

Colletia atrox, commonly known as the deadly nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other toxic plants like belladonna and tobacco. The plant produces striking purple or red flowers and has a distinctive, spiny stem. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, with a rough texture.
