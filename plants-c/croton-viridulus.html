Croton viridulus

Croton viridulus, commonly known as green croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a smooth surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant contains toxic compounds, making it potentially harmful to both humans and animals.
