Catasetum schmidtianum

Catasetum schmidtianum, commonly known as the Schmidt's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored flowers, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its flowers are specialized for pollination by bees, featuring a distinctive pouch-like structure.

Catasetum schmidtianum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
