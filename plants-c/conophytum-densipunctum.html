Conophytum densipunctum

Conophytum densipunctum, commonly known as the dense-punctured conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that are densely covered with small, white tubercles. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
