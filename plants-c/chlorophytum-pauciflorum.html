Chlorophytum pauciflorum

Chlorophytum pauciflorum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, low-maintenance plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, upright leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and its capacity to purify indoor air. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers on slender stalks, which are not the primary ornamental feature.

Chlorophytum pauciflorum is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
