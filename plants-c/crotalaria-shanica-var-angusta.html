Crotalaria shanica var. angusta

Crotalaria shanica var. angusta, commonly known as narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. It has a hairy stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
