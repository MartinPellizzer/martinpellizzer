Centaurea pseudosphaerocephala

Centaurea pseudosphaerocephala, commonly known as the narrowhead centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny, open habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and blue to purple in color.

It is often found in Mediterranean regions and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
