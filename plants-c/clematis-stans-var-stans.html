Clematis stans var. stans

Clematis stans var. stans, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, woody berries that are edible but not particularly flavorful.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.
