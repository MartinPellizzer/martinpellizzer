Chamaecrista xanthadena

Chamaecrista xanthadena, commonly known as yellow burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and produces pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and used in traditional medicine in some regions.
