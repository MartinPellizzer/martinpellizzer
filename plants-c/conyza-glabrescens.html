Conyza glabrescens

Conyza glabrescens, commonly known as whiteleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to North America, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant has small, alternate leaves that are often covered with fine hairs.

It is commonly found in fields, roadsides, and other sunny, dry environments.
