Cosmos palmeri var. odontophyllus

Cosmos palmeri var. odontophyllus, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has narrow, hairy leaves and produces seed pods that split open to release its seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
