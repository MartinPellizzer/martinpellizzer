Cytisus absinthioides var. austriacoides

Cytisus absinthioides var. austriacoides, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to the Alps.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant flowers and compact growth habit.
