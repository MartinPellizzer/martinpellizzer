Cyrtanthus flammosus

Cyrtanthus flammosus, commonly known as the flame lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, orange-red flowers that resemble flames, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces underground bulbs that store nutrients. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Cyrtanthus flammosus is also valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in various climates.
