Comarostaphylis mucronata var. brevifolia

Comarostaphylis mucronata var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
