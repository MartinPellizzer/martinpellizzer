Chamaemeles mexicana

Chamaemeles mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
