Cynanchica daphneola

Cynanchica daphneola, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its ability to fix nitrogen, it plays a role in soil enrichment and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
