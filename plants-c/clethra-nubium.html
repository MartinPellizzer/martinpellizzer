Clethra nubium

Clethra nubium, commonly known as cloudclethra, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories. Clethra nubium is valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity and its potential for use in horticultural settings.
