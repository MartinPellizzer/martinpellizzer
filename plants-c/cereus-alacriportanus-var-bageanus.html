Cereus alacriportanus var. bageanus

Cereus alacriportanus var. bageanus, commonly known as the Bagean Cereus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, white, night-blooming flowers that are highly fragrant. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
