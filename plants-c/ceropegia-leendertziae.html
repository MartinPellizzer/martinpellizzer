Ceropegia leendertziae

Ceropegia leendertziae, commonly known as the Cape Verde ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow in color. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia leendertziae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
