Croton triangularis

Croton triangularis, commonly known as the triangular croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically triangular in shape, giving it its common name.

Croton triangularis thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
