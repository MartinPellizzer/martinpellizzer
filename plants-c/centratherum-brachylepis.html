Centratherum brachylepis

Centratherum brachylepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centatherum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and rocky soils, preferring full sun to partial shade. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
