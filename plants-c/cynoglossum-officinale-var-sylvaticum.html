Cynoglossum officinale var. sylvaticum

Cynoglossum officinale var. sylvaticum, commonly known as wild forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, typically on slender, branching stems. The leaves are heart-shaped, with a rough texture and a slightly woolly surface. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges, meadows, and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
