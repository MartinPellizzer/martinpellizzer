Carpinus tsiangiana

Carpinus tsiangiana, commonly known as the Chinese hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and oval-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring, followed by small, winged fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
