Ceropegia stenantha var. parviflora

Ceropegia stenantha var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered milkweed, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with tuberous roots and fleshy, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for indoor gardens and hanging baskets.
