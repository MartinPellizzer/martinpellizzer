Callipeltis microstegia

Callipeltis microstegia, commonly known as the dwarf rock orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It typically grows in rocky or well-drained soil, often in shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, with a distinctive hooded structure.

It is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
