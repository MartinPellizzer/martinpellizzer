Combretum sonderi

Combretum sonderi, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
