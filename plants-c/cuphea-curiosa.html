Cuphea curiosa

Cuphea curiosa, commonly known as the curious cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that resemble small, colorful cups. The plant produces vibrant red, orange, or yellow tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. Its foliage is often green with a slight pubescent texture, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Cuphea curiosa is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
