Coronilla hyrcana

Coronilla hyrcana, commonly known as the Hyrcanian crownvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Coronilla hyrcana is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and land reclamation projects.
