Curculigo strobiliformis

Curculigo strobiliformis, commonly known as the mountain curculigo, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Hyperepidae and is characterized by its compact, herbaceous growth habit. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
