Cotyledon barbeyi var. soutpansbergensis

Cotyledon barbeyi var. soutpansbergensis, commonly known as the Soutpansberg cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to arid landscapes.
