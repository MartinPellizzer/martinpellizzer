Cirsium chokaiense

Cirsium chokaiense, commonly known as Chokai thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive leaf structures. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers, typical of the Cirsium genus. It thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
