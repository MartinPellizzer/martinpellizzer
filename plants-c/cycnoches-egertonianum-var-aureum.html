Cycnoches egertonianum var. aureum

Cycnoches egertonianum var. aureum, commonly known as the golden cycnoches, is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest understories, where it thrives in the humid and cool conditions. Its flowers are highly fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
