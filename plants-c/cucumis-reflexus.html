Cucumis reflexus

Cucumis reflexus, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, green, spiny fruits that turn yellow when mature. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

It is native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Middle East, often found in dry, disturbed habitats.
