Croton russellii

Croton russellii, commonly known as Russell's croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is characterized by its diverse species with often striking foliage. The plant is noted for its vibrant, colorful leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, or orange, depending on the variety. Croton russellii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
