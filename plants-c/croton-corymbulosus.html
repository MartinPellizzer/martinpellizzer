Croton corymbulosus

Croton corymbulosus, commonly known as the flame tree or the Christmas tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically green with a reddish or yellowish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by distinctive orange or red fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton corymbulosus is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its use in traditional medicine.
