Caladium bicolor var. leopoldii

Caladium bicolor var. leopoldii, commonly known as the Leopard Leaf Caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with bold patterns of red, white, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often display a variegated appearance that varies with light exposure. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant, decorative leaves.
