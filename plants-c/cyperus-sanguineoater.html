Cyperus sanguineoater

Cyperus sanguineoater, commonly known as the red-flowered flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along water bodies. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of reddish-brown spikelets, which are characteristic of the genus Cyperus.

Cyperus sanguineoater is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various water conditions.
