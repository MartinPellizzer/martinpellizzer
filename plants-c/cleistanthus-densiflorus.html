Cleistanthus densiflorus

Cleistanthus densiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower cleistanthus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has a slender, straight trunk and can grow up to 20 meters tall in its natural habitat. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
