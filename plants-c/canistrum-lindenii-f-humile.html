Canistrum lindenii f. humile

Canistrum lindenii f. humile, commonly known as the dwarf fig tree, is a compact shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are similar in appearance to figs, though they are not true figs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of cultivation, it is often grown as a houseplant or in garden settings.
