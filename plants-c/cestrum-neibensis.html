Cestrum neibensis

Cestrum neibensis, commonly known as the Neib's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, which bloom at night. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
