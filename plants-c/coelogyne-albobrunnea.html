Coelogyne albobrunnea

Coelogyne albobrunnea, commonly known as the white-banded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences that bear clusters of fragrant, white and greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Coelogyne albobrunnea is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral structure.
