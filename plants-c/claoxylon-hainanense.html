Claoxylon hainanense

Claoxylon hainanense, commonly known as Hainan white wood, is a tropical tree native to the island of Hainan in China.

It belongs to the family Claoxylaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody fruits containing ellipsoidal seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
