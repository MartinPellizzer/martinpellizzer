Cissus marionae

Cissus marionae, commonly known as Marion's devil's ivy, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus marionae is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
