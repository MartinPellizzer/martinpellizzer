Cotyledon Desmetiana

Desmet's cotyledon, scientifically known as Cotyledon desmetiana, is a rare and striking succulent native to the arid regions of Namibia. This plant is prized for its unique, fleshy leaves that often display vibrant colors and intricate patterns. It belongs to the family Aizoaceae, commonly known as the ice plant family, which is characterized by its adaptability to harsh environments. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and rarity make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
