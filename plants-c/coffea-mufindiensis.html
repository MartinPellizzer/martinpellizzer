Coffea mufindiensis

Coffea mufindiensis, commonly known as the Mufindi coffee.

It is a wild coffee species native to the Eastern Arc Mountains of Tanzania. This plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and produces red, fleshy berries containing two seeds. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and conservation status, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.
