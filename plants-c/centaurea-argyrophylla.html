Centaurea argyrophylla

Centaurea argyrophylla, commonly known as silverleaf centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and bright blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species that produces small, spiky seed heads after flowering.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
