Costus zingiberoides

Costus zingiberoides, commonly known as the ginger costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Botanically, it is characterized by its aromatic foliage and unique floral structure.
