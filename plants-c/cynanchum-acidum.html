Cynanchum acidum

Cynanchum acidum, commonly known as Chinese snake root, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a milky sap that contains various bioactive compounds. It is often found in moist, rocky soils in forested areas.

Due to its medicinal properties, it is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
