Carex laxiflora var. styloflexa

Carex laxiflora var. styloflexa, commonly known as the slender-flowered sedge, is a variant of the Carex genus native to North America.

It is a perennial herb with grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist to wet habitats such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
