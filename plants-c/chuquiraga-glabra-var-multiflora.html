Chuquiraga glabra var. multiflora

Chuquiraga glabra var. multiflora, commonly known as the smooth chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are usually smooth and lance-shaped, with a somewhat hairy texture.

It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in shrublands and coastal areas.
