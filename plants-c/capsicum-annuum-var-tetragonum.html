Capsicum annuum var. tetragonum

Capsicum annuum var. tetragonum, commonly known as the square bell pepper, is a unique variety of chili pepper characterized by its distinctive square or rectangular shape.

This variety belongs to the Solanaceae family and is native to the Americas. It typically produces small, firm fruits with a sweet to mildly spicy flavor, depending on the cultivar. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value, combined with its culinary uses, makes it a popular choice for both gardens and kitchens.
