Cenchrus prieurii

Cenchrus prieurii, commonly known as the Prieur's grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance and resilience.

Its fibrous root system helps stabilize soil, making it useful in erosion control and rehabilitation projects.
