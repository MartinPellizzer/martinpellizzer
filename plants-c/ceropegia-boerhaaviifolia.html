Ceropegia boerhaaviifolia

Ceropegia boerhaaviifolia, commonly known as the Boerhaaviifolia Crown of Thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tubular stems that are often branching and covered in small, pointed leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pale pink or white, with a unique, spurred shape. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
