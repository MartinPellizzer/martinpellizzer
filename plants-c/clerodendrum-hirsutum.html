Clerodendrum hirsutum

Clerodendrum hirsutum, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, giving it its common name. Its showy, clustered flowers are typically red or orange, with a distinctive tubular shape.

It is commonly cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
