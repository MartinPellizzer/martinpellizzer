Conophytum helenae

Conophytum helenae, commonly known as the Helen's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, compact leaves that form a basal rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Conophytum helenae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
