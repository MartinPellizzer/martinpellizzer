Ceratostylis macra

Ceratostylis macra, commonly known as the large-lobed marsh orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pale green to yellowish flowers with elongated, spurred petals. It typically grows in wet, shaded environments such as marshes and swamps.

Ceratostylis macra is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
