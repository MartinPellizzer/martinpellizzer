Curio ficoides

Curio ficoides, commonly known as the African violet or the crown of thorns, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Curio ficoides thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
