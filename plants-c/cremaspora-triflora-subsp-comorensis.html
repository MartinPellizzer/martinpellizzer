Cremaspora triflora subsp. comorensis

Cremaspora triflora subsp.

comorensis, commonly known as the Comorian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Comoros archipelago. It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed soils.

The subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
