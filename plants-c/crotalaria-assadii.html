Crotalaria assadii

Crotalaria assadii, commonly known as the Assad's cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants that often form symbiotic relationships with nitrogen-fixing bacteria. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers with prominent spurred petals, and its leaves are typically trifoliate. Crotalaria assadii is characterized by its small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be an invasive species in certain areas.
