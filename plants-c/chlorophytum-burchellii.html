Chlorophytum burchellii

Chlorophytum burchellii, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically variegated with yellow and green stripes. The plant is well-known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its air-purifying qualities make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Chlorophytum burchellii is also capable of producing small, white, star-shaped flowers on tall stalks during the growing season.
