Craterostigma plantagineum

Craterostigma plantagineum, commonly known as the desert star or sand star, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through its succulent leaves and deep root system. It is often found growing in rocky soils and along dry streambeds.
