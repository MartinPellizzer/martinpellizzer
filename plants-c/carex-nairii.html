Carex nairii

Carex nairii, commonly known as the Nair's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and produces small, clustered spikelets of flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for wetland restoration and ecological studies.
