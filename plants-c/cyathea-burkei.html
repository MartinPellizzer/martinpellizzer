Cyathea burkei

Cyathea burkei, commonly known as the Burke's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea burkei is a slow-growing species that thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
