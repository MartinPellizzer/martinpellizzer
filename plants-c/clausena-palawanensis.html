Clausena palawanensis

Clausena palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan Clausena, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The fruit is a drupe, typically yellow or orange in color, and is edible, though its taste can vary.

This plant is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
