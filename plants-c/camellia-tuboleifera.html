Camellia tuboleifera

Camellia tuboleifera, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in winter or early spring. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and produces elliptical, red or orange-red fruits. Camellia tuboleifera is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for temperate climates.
