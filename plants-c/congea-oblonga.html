Congea oblonga

Congea oblonga, commonly known as the Indian snakeroot, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, white to pale yellow, and clustered in terminal spikes.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
