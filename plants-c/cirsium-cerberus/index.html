Cirsium Cerberus

Cerberus thistle, scientifically known as Cirsium cerberus, is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny leaves and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late summer. This species thrives in rocky and dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Despite its tough appearance, the Cerberus thistle plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and pollinators. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
