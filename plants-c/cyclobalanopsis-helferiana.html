Cyclobalanopsis helferiana

Cyclobalanopsis helferiana, commonly known as the Helfer's chinquapin, is a member of the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China and northern Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and paler beneath. Its flowers are dioecious, with separate male and female plants, and it produces edible, chestnut-like nuts enclosed in a spiny cupule.

The species is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also of interest for its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
