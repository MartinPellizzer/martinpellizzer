Cirsium × okamotoi f. albiflorum

Cirsium × okamotoi f. albiflorum, commonly known as the whiteflower thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its large, spiny leaves and showy, white to pale yellow flowers. This form is a naturally occurring variant of Cirsium × okamotoi, known for its distinctive floral coloration. The plant is native to Japan and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
