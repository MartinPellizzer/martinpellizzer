Cattleya lueddemanniana var. alba

Cattleya lueddemanniana var. alba, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, fragrant white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically has dark green, strap-like leaves and produces blooms in shades of white to pale pink. It is a popular choice for both indoor and garden cultivation due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often used in orchid shows and as a cut flower due to its elegance and long-lasting blooms.
