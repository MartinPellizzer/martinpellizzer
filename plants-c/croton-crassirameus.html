Croton crassirameus

Croton crassirameus, commonly known as the thick-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features large, thick, and often glossy leaves that can be green, red, or variegated in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental foliage.

Due to its striking leaves, it is a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plants.
