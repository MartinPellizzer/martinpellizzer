Conopodium smyrnioides

Conopodium smyrnioides, commonly known as the smyrnioides cress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbage. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, green, ovate leaves.
