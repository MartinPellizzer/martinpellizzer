Cryptocoryne crispatula var. balansae

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often curled or twisted, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded, nutrient-rich substrates. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, allowing it to form dense underwater colonies.

It is popular in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
