Cardamine praecox

Cardamine praecox, commonly known as early watercress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland areas. It has a short growing season and is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

Its botanical features make it a valuable indicator species for wetland habitats.
