Centropogon preslii

Centropogon preslii, commonly known as the Preslii grass.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family, native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant typically grows as a perennial grass with slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that bear small, two-lobed flowers.

Centropogon preslii is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional practices.
