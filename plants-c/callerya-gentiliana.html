Callerya gentiliana

Callerya gentiliana, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange inflorescences that appear in spring, making it a visually striking plant. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
