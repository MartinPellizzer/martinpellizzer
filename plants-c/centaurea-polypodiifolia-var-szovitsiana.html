Centaurea polypodiifolia var. szovitsiana

Centaurea polypodiifolia var. szovitsiana, commonly known as the Szovits' centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Basin.

It features slender, upright stems that bear narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in composite-like inflorescences, typically blooming from June to August. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
