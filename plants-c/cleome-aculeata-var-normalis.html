Cleome aculeata var. normalis

Cleome aculeata var. normalis, commonly known as the prickly cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant features spiny stems and leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Cleome aculeata var. normalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
