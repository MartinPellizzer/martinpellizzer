Cousinia kilouyensis

Cousinia kilouyensis, commonly known as the Turkestan hawk's beard, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It has basal leaves that are linear to lanceolate in shape.

Cousinia kilouyensis is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
