Clematis siamensis var. monantha

Clematis siamensis var. monantha, commonly known as the Siam Clematis, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to climb trellises and provide seasonal floral displays.
