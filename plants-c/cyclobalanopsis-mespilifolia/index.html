Cyclobalanopsis Mespilifolia

Cyclobalanopsis mespilifolia is a rare and ancient tree species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts. This deciduous tree is known for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native habitat. Its fruit, resembling a small apple, is an important food source for various wildlife species. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is now classified as endangered, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
