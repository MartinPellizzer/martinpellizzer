Crinum amoenum

Crinum amoenum, commonly known as the fragrant crinum, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllis family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant features large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces bulbous roots that store nutrients.

Crinum amoenum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
