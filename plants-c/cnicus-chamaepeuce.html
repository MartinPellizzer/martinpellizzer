Cnicus chamaepeuce

Cnicus chamaepeuce, commonly known as sand thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a low, spreading growth habit with hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Sand thistle is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
