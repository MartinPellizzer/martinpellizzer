Clypeola altaica

Clypeola altaica, commonly known as the Altai shield-leaf, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Its small white or pale yellow flowers appear in umbrella-like clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Clypeola altaica is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
