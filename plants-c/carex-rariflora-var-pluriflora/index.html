Carex Rariflora Var. Pluriflora

Carex rariflora var. pluriflora is a striking sedge species known for its unique floral structure and ecological significance. This variety is native to specific regions where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its delicate flowers and grass-like leaves make it a distinctive addition to wetland habitats. It plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining ecosystem balance. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles.
