Cabanisia caracasana

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Cabanisia caracasana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
