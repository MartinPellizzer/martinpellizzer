Crataegus praestans

Crataegus praestans, commonly known as the showy hawthorn, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by red berries in the fall. The plant has small, oval leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and full sun.

Crataegus praestans is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
