Cyrtochilum ramulosum

Cyrtochilum ramulosum, commonly known as the ramulate orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white or pale yellow blooms that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Cyrtochilum ramulosum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
