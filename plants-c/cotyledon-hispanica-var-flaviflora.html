Cotyledon hispanica var. flaviflora

Cotyledon hispanica var. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces showy yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
