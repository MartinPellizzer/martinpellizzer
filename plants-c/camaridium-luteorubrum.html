Camaridium luteorubrum

Camaridium luteorubrum, commonly known as the yellow-red camaridium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers, which bloom in late spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Camaridium luteorubrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
