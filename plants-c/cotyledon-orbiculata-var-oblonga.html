Cotyledon orbiculata var. oblonga

Cotyledon orbiculata var. oblonga, commonly known as the elephant's ear cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique leaf shape and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
