Croton corrientesianus

Croton corrientesianus, commonly known as the Corrientes croton, is a shrub native to South America, particularly found in regions of Colombia and Ecuador.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly colored plants. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed segments, and its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
