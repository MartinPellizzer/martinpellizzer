Codiaeum stellingianum

Codiaeum stellingianum, commonly known as the flame leaf or stellingian croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

The leaves are typically opposite and can grow up to 30 cm in length, making it a striking addition to gardens and indoor spaces.
