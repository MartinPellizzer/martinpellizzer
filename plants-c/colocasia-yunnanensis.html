Colocasia yunnanensis

Colocasia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 50 cm in diameter. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which is known for its distinctive inflorescence structure called a spadix. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, shady environments.
