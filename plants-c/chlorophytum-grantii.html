Chlorophytum grantii

Chlorophytum grantii, commonly known as the Tiger Tooth Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that resemble tiger teeth. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
