Culcasia obliquifolia

Culcasia obliquifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that often display striking variegation in shades of green, red, and white. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its leaves are typically broad and arrow-shaped, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Culcasia obliquifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
