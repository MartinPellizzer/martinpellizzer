Camellia columna

Camellia columna, commonly known as the columnar camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers in the spring, which are borne on long, pendulous stems. Camellia columna thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for horticultural gardens.
