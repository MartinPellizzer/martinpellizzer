Cynanchum somaliense

Cynanchum somaliense, commonly known as Somali milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia and Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, often covered in fine hairs.

The species is adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient plant in its native habitat.
