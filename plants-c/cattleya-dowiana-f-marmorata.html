Cattleya dowiana f. marmorata

Cattleya dowiana f. marmorata, commonly known as the marbled cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, mottled foliage and vibrant orchid flowers.

This cultivar is a cross between Cattleya dowiana and Cattleya marmorata, resulting in unique patterns on its leaves and flowers. It typically produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink, white, and orange, with a distinctive marbled effect. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments.

It requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light to flourish.
