Cyphostemma ruacanense

Cyphostemma ruacanense, commonly known as the Ruacana moonvine, is a climbing plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and unique morphology, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
