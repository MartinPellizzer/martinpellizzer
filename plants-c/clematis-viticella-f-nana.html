Clematis viticella f. nana

Clematis viticella f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf clematis, is a compact, deciduous vine native to Europe.

It features small, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, wiry stems that can be trained on supports or allowed to trail. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the blooms.

It is well-suited for gardens due to its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
