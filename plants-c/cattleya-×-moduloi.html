Cattleya × moduloi

Cattleya × moduloi, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a species resulting from the cross between Cattleya × labiata and Cattleya × leopoldii.

It is a compact, sympodial orchid with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often featuring a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This hybrid is prized for its beauty and is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
