Cestichis latibasis

Cestichis latibasis, commonly known as the broad-leaved cestichis, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads, which are typical of the daisy family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
