Cynanchum peraffine

Cynanchum peraffine, commonly known as the silky milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive corona structure.

The plant produces pods that contain numerous small, white seeds with silky filaments, aiding in wind dispersal.
