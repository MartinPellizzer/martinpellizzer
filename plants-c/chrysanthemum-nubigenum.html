Chrysanthemum nubigenum

Chrysanthemum nubigenum, commonly known as the cloud chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring cooler climates. Its foliage is usually dark green with a slightly hairy texture.

Chrysanthemum nubigenum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
