Celastrus robustus

Celastrus robustus, commonly known as the Indian sativa or the yellow star.

It is a deciduous climbing shrub native to the Indian subcontinent and Southeast Asia. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in umbels, blooming in the summer months.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, rounded seeds.
