Centaurea pratensis

Centaurea pratensis, commonly known as meadow centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are often hairy.

Centaurea pratensis is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
