Campanula sphaerothrix var. oxya

Campanula sphaerothrix var. oxya, commonly known as the spherical bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique spherical or rounded flower heads, which consist of numerous small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage is usually compact and grass-like, with dark green, narrow leaves.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its distinctive floral display.
