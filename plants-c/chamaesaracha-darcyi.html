Chamaesaracha darcyi

Chamaesaracha darcyi, commonly known as the Darcy's chamaesaracha, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, needle-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and resilience.
