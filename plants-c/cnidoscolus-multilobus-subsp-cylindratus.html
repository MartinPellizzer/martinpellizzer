Cnidoscolus multilobus subsp. cylindratus

Cnidoscolus multilobus subsp.

cylindratus, commonly known as the spiny leaf giant soursop, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with deep lobes and spiny margins.

The plant produces large, green, spiny fruits that are typically harvested when mature. The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruits.
