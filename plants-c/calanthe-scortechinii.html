Calanthe scortechinii

Calanthe scortechinii, commonly known as the Sichuan calanthe, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy texture.

Calanthe scortechinii thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
