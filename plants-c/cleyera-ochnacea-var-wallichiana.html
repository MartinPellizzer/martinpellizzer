Cleyera ochnacea var. wallichiana

Cleyera ochnacea var. wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan cleyera, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows at high altitudes in temperate regions. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red berries. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a leathery texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to shaded environments.
