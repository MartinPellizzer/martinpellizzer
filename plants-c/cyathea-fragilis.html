Cyathea fragilis

Cyathea fragilis, commonly known as the fragile tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that give it a lush, tropical appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cyathea fragilis plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and shelter for various small animals and insects.
