Campanula Pamphylica

Campanula pamphylica is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. It belongs to the bellflower family, known for its distinctive bell-shaped blooms. This species thrives in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its vivid blue or purple flowers attract pollinators and add beauty to its natural habitat. Due to its limited range and fragility, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
