Crotalaria ternata

Crotalaria ternata, commonly known as smallflower crotalaria, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and compound leaves. The plant features yellow flowers with two upper petals and two lower petals, typical of the genus Crotalaria. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Its seeds are enclosed in a spiny capsule, which aids in seed dispersal.
