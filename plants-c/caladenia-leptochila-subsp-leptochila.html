Caladenia leptochila subsp. leptochila

Caladenia leptochila subsp.

leptochila, commonly known as the slender-stemmed spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and grassy woodlands.

The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, narrow, greenish-brown flower with a distinctive, intricate pattern on its dorsal sepal and petals. It blooms during the autumn season, usually between August and October.

This sub-species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
