Centaurea cineraria

Centaurea cineraria, commonly known as the cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is also known for its medicinal properties, with historical use in treating respiratory ailments.

Centaurea cineraria is a hardy perennial that prefers full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.
