Cyclolobium blanchetianum

Cyclolobium blanchetianum, commonly known as the golden wisteria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces long, pendulous inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cyclolobium blanchetianum is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
