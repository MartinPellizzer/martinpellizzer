Cleidion velutinum

Cleidion velutinum, commonly known as the velvet tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical leaves that are leathery and velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Cleidion velutinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
