Cristaria cordatorotundifolia

Cristaria cordatorotundifolia, commonly known as the heart-shaped leaf plant, is a species characterized by its unique foliage.

The leaves are cordate, or heart-shaped, with a rounded base and a slightly notched tip. This plant typically has a compact growth habit and thrives in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are often a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

While it is not widely cultivated, it is appreciated for its ornamental value in specialized gardens.
