Calyptranthes smithii

Calyptranthes smithii, commonly known as the Smith's calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America, particularly Panama and Costa Rica. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for tropical gardens and botanical collections.
