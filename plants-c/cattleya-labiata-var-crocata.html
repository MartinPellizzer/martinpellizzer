Cattleya labiata var. crocata

Cattleya labiata var. crocata, commonly known as the Crocus Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, colorful blooms.

It features showy, pendulous flowers with a distinctive crocus-like appearance, often in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid environments. It has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
