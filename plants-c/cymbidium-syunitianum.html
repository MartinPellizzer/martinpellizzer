Cymbidium syunitianum

Cymbidium syunitianum, commonly known as the Syunitian orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems that bear numerous lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium syunitianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
