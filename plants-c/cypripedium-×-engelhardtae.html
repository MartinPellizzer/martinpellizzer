Cypripedium × engelhardtae

Cypripedium × engelhardtae, commonly known as the Engelhardts' lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically pink or white in color. The plant features a thick, fleshy rhizome and grows in moist, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
