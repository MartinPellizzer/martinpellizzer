Crotalaria retzii

Crotalaria retzii, commonly known as yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often grown as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value for livestock.

Additionally, it has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
