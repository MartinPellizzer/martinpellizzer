Coelorachis forsteriana

Coelorachis forsteriana, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions and well-drained soil.

Coelorachis forsteriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
