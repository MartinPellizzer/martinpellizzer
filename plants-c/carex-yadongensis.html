Carex yadongensis

Carex yadongensis, commonly known as the Yadong sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers, typical of the sedge genus. It is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, humid climates.

Carex yadongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
