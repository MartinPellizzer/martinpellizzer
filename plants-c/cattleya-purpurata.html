Cattleya purpurata

Cattleya purpurata, commonly known as the Purple Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya purpurata is popular among orchid growers for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
