Craterispermum microdon

Craterispermum microdon, commonly known as the small-seeded crater plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. Its fruit is a small, dry capsule containing several tiny seeds.

The species is characterized by its compact growth form and adaptive features suited to arid environments.
