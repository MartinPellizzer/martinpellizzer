Crassula acutifolia var. typica

Crassula acutifolia var. typica, commonly known as the Campfire Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues along the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
