Clematis subumbellata

Clematis subumbellata, commonly known as the pink clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis subumbellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
