Calamus nambariensis var. furfuraceus

Calamus nambariensis var. furfuraceus, commonly known as the Nambari reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the wet tropical regions of Australia, particularly in Queensland. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in humid environments.
