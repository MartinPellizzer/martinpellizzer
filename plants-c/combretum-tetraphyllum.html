Combretum tetraphyllum

Combretum tetraphyllum, commonly known as the four-leaf combretum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and often have a reddish or yellowish color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.
