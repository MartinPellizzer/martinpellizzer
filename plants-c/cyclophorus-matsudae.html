Cyclophorus matsudae

It is characterized by its distinctive, spirally arranged leaves that form a dense, compact rosette. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its growth habit and morphology are adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.
