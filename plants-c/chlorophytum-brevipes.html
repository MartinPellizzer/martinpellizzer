Chlorophytum brevipes

Chlorophytum brevipes, commonly known as the compact spider plant, is a compact, rosette-forming succulent native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically light green with a slightly bluish tint. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks during the summer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal watering.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
