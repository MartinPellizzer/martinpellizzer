Cyathea janseniana

Cyathea janseniana, commonly known as the Jansen's tree fern, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a sturdy, unbranched trunk that is covered with a fibrous, brownish layer of old leaf bases. Cyathea janseniana typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests.

It plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and contributing to the forest's biodiversity.
