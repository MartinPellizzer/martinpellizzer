Cattleya × sidneana

Cattleya × sidneana, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically has a pseudobulb structure and produces multiple inflorescences with fragrant blooms. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native tropical environment.

Cattleya × sidneana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
