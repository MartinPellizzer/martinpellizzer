Cymbidium floribundum f. virescens

Cymbidium floribundum f. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a hybrid cultivar of the Cymbidium genus.

It is characterized by its vibrant greenish-white flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. This orchid is native to China and is prized for its elegant appearance and fragrance. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a potted plant.
