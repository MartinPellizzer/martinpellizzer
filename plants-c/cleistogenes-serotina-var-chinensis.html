Cleistogenes serotina var. chinensis

Cleistogenes serotina var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese false brome, is a perennial grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional herbal medicine.
