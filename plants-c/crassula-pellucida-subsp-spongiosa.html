Crassula pellucida subsp. spongiosa

Crassula pellucida subsp.

spongiosa, commonly known as the Sponge Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
