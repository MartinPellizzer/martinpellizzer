Camellia venusta

Camellia venusta, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the pink camellia, is a species native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. Camellia venusta thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding color to the landscape during the colder months.
