Cerastium tatrense

Cerastium tatrense, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers in early summer.
