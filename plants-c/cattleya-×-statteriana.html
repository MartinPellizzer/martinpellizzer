Cattleya × statteriana

Cattleya × statteriana, commonly known as the Statteriana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid celebrated for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It originates from a cross between Cattleya bowringiana and Cattleya mossiae, resulting in a plant with strong stems and large, showy blooms. The flowers typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues, with a distinctive lip that is often a contrasting color. This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
