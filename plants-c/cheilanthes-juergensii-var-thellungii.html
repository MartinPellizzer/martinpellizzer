Cheilanthes juergensii var. thellungii

Cheilanthes juergensii var. thellungii, commonly known as the Thellung's rockcap, is a terrestrial fern native to the arid regions of western North America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit. This variety is particularly noted for its ability to thrive in rocky and crevice habitats, making it a common sight in desert landscapes.
