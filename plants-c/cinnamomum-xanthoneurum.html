Cinnamomum xanthoneurum

Cinnamomum xanthoneurum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered cinnamon or Cinnamomum xanthoneurum, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces dark brown, aromatic seeds enclosed in a fleshy, red or orange aril. It is often cultivated for its essential oils and medicinal properties.

The tree prefers warm, humid climates and is typically found in tropical rainforests.
