Cestrum johnniegentryanum

Cestrum johnniegentryanum, commonly known as the Johnny Gentry cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
