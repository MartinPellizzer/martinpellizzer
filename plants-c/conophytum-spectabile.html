Conophytum spectabile

Conophytum spectabile, commonly known as the spectabile rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.

Conophytum spectabile thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
