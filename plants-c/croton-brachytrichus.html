Croton brachytrichus

Croton brachytrichus, commonly known as the short-hair croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton brachytrichus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
