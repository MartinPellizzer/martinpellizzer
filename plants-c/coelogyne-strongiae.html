Coelogyne strongiae

Coelogyne strongiae, commonly known as the strong's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its natural habitat. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple lip. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
