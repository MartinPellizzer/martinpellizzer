Chrysophyllum anomalum

Chrysophyllum anomalum, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe, containing a single seed.
