Coelogyne concinna

Coelogyne concinna, commonly known as the pink lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a dense, rhizomatous growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant inflorescences with pink to white flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Coelogyne concinna is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
