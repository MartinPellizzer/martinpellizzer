Casselia viliosula

Casselia viliosula, commonly known as the silky cassia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its soft, silky hairs that cover its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

Casselia viliosula is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
