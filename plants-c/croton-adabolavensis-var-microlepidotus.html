Croton adabolavensis var. microlepidotus

Croton adabolavensis var. microlepidotus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It features smooth, dark green leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its ornamental value lies primarily in its foliage, which displays a rich coloration and unique leaf patterns.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
