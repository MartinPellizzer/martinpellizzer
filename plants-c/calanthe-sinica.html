Calanthe sinica

Calanthe sinica, commonly known as the Chinese calanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features erect, hairy stems with broad, ovate leaves that are usually dark green in color. Its flowers are showy, with a white to pale pink coloration and a distinctive, spurred lip.

Calanthe sinica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, shaded environments.
