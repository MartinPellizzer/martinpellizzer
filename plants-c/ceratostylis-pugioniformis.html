Ceratostylis pugioniformis

Ceratostylis pugioniformis, commonly known as the sword-leafed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, sword-shaped leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence bears pale green to white flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
