Chlorophytum ramiferum

Chlorophytum ramiferum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often have a white stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, grass-like seed capsules. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum ramiferum is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
