Cadetia maliliensis

Cadetia maliliensis, commonly known as the Malili mountain cress, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its small, white or yellow flowers and pinnately divided leaves. It typically grows in rocky or mountainous terrain, preferring cool and moist conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
