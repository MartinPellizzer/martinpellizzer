Crinum defixum

Crinum defixum, commonly known as the black crinum, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It produces large, strap-like leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Crinum defixum is also known for its strong fragrance, which can be both pleasant and overwhelming.
