Cymbidium canaliculatum var. marginatum

Cymbidium canaliculatum var. marginatum, commonly known as the variegated cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The flowers are typically fragrant and come in shades of white, pink, and yellow, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

It prefers cool, humid conditions and is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
