Cimicifuga yunnanensis

Cimicifuga yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan bugbane, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of white to pale yellow flowers with prominent sepals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are palmately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Cimicifuga yunnanensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
