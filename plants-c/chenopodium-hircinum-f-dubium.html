Chenopodium hircinum f. dubium

Chenopodium hircinum f. dubium, commonly known as the slender goosefoot, is a variant of the common goosefoot.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with a rough, textured surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically arranged in clusters.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.
