Clerodendrum longiflorum

Clerodendrum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.
