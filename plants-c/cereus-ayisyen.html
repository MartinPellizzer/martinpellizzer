Cereus ayisyen

Cereus ayisyen, commonly known as the Haitian Christmas cactus, is a species native to the Caribbean.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Cactaceae family, characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant blooms. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically red, orange, or yellow, often blooming during the holiday season. It thrives in warm, sunny environments with well-draining soil and requires minimal watering.

Its unique appearance and seasonal flowering make it a popular ornamental plant.
