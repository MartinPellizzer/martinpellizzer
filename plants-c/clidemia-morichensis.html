Clidemia morichensis

Clidemia morichensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian mistflower, is a perennial shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, clustered white or pale green flowers that bloom throughout the year.

Clidemia morichensis is an important component of Hawaii's native flora and plays a role in supporting local ecosystems.
