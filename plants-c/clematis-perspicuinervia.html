Clematis perspicuinervia

Clematis perspicuinervia, commonly known as the showy clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with finely divided, feathery foliage that provides a delicate, lace-like appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its stems are covered in fine hairs, and it has a complex vascular system that supports its rapid growth.

Clematis perspicuinervia thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
