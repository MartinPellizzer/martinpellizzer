Campanula hercegovina f. ovalifolia

Campanula hercegovina f. ovalifolia, commonly known as the Hercegovina bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to the Balkan region.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value in gardens, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
