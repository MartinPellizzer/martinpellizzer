Centaurium spicatum subsp. japonicum

Centaurium spicatum subsp.

japonicum, commonly known as Japanese centaury, is a flowering plant native to East Asia. It is a low-growing herb with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers, which are typically tubular in shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas and stream sides.
