Cissus bignonioides

Cissus bignonioides, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-white venation on its heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often used in ornamental horticulture.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb make it a popular choice for vertical gardening.
