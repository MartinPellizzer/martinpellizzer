Cyclosorus Sumatranus

Cyclosorus sumatranus is a unique fern species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its distinctive fronds that form a circular or spiral pattern, giving it its scientific name. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its delicate appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the wider botanical community.
