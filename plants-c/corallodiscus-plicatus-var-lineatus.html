Corallodiscus plicatus var. lineatus

Corallodiscus plicatus var. lineatus, commonly known as the zigzag coralplant, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzag-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
