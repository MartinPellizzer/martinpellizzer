Cineraria oxyodonta

Cineraria oxyodonta, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
