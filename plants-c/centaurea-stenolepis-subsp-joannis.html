Centaurea stenolepis subsp. joannis

Centaurea stenolepis subsp.

joannis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrow, linear leaves and compact inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
