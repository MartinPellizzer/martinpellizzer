Ceodes brunoniana

Ceodes brunoniana, commonly known as the New Zealand broom.

It is a flowering plant native to New Zealand, belonging to the Fabaceae family. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is often found in coastal and inland scrublands.
