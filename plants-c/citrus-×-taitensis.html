Citrus × taitensis

Citrus × taitensis, commonly known as the Tahitian lime, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from Tahiti.

It is a small evergreen tree with dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically round or oval, with a thick, yellow rind and juicy, acidic green pulp. It is valued for its high vitamin C content and is often used in cooking and beverages.

The plant thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
