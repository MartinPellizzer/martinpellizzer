Carduus × haussknechtii

Carduus × haussknechtii, commonly known as the German thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands and along roadsides. It has a taproot system and is perennial, with a lifespan of several years.

Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
