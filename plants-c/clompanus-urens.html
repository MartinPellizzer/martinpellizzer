Clompanus urens

Clompanus urens, commonly known as the stinkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found growing near water sources such as streams and wetlands.

Clompanus urens is known for its strong, unpleasant odor, which is released when the plant is disturbed.
