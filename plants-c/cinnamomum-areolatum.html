Cinnamomum areolatum

Cinnamomum areolatum, commonly known as the Malayan cinnamon or areolatum cinnamon, is a species of tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark and leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its bark is used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.

The leaves are also utilized in local cuisines and herbal remedies.
