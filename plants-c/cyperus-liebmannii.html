Cyperus liebmannii

Cyperus liebmannii, commonly known as the spike rush, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has cylindrical, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or brown in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in wetland habitats.

Cyperus liebmannii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
