Chilita capensis

Chilita capensis, commonly known as the Cape chilti, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal and inland regions. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Chilita capensis is often found in open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
