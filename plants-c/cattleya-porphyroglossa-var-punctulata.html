Cattleya porphyroglossa var. punctulata

Cattleya porphyroglossa var. punctulata, commonly known as the spotted cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive pink or purple coloration and prominent dark spots on the petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
