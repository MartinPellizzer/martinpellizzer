Clematis hupehensis

Clematis hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant has a climbing habit and can reach lengths of up to 20 feet, making it suitable for trellises or arbors. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

The plant prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil for optimal growth.
