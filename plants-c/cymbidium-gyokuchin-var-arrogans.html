Cymbidium gyokuchin var. arrogans

Cymbidium gyokuchin var. arrogans, commonly known as the Japanese orchid or the noble orchid, is a species native to Japan and parts of Korea.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, featuring a tall, upright stem that bears multiple leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinct yellow lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
