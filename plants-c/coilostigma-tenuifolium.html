Coilostigma tenuifolium

Coilostigma tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved coilostigma, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features slender, ascending stems that often branch near the base. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in terminal clusters, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous tiny seeds.

It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
