Carpodiptera cubensis subsp. ophiticola

Carpodiptera cubensis subsp.

ophiticola, commonly known as the Cuban orchid bee, is a species of bee native to Cuba. It belongs to the family Apidae and is characterized by its distinctive black body with metallic green or blue reflections.

The plant it interacts with, specifically the orchid *Dendrophylax lindenii*, is a rare epiphytic orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments. This bee plays a crucial role in pollinating the orchid, contributing to the plant's reproductive success.

Its behavior and ecological interactions highlight the intricate relationships within tropical ecosystems.
