Commelina ovato-oblonga

Commelina ovato-oblonga, commonly known as the broad-ovate commelina, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features broad, ovate-oblong leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
