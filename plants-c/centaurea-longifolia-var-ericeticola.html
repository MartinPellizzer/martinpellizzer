Centaurea longifolia var. ericeticola

Centaurea longifolia var. ericeticola, commonly known as the heath centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils, often found in heathlands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, blue to violet flowers in summer. Its flowers are surrounded by white to pale blue bracts, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
