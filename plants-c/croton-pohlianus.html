Croton pohlianus

Croton pohlianus, commonly known as the Pohli's croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor plant collections.
