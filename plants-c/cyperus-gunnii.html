Cyperus gunnii

Cyperus gunnii, commonly known as the silver flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the sedge family, Cyperaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in swampy areas or along waterways.

Cyperus gunnii is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
