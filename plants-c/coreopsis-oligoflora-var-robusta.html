Coreopsis oligoflora var. robusta

Coreopsis oligoflora var. robusta, commonly known as the robust coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet, featuring upright stems and daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture, and it blooms from late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and pollinator habitats.
