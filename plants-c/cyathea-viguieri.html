Cyathea viguieri

Cyathea viguieri, commonly known as the Viguier's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in montane forests. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth.

Cyathea viguieri is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
