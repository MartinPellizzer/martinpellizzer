Cotyledon fulgens

Cotyledon fulgens, commonly known as the "Firecotyledon," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the warmer months.

Cotyledon fulgens is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
