Calanthe × sibuyanense

Calanthe × sibuyanense, commonly known as the Sibuyan calanthe, is a hybrid orchid native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in montane forest habitats, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of flowers, often with a unique coloration pattern.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
