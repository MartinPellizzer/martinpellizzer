Chlorophytum sphacelatum subsp. milanjianum

Chlorophytum sphacelatum subsp.

milanjianum, commonly known as the Milanjianum spider plant, is a species native to South Africa. It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically broad and strap-like.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in umbels, which are followed by clusters of bright red berries. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soil.

This subspecies is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
