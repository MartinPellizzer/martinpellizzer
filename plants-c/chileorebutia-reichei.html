Chileorebutia reichei

Chileorebutia reichei, commonly known as the Rainbow Cactus, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and its ability to produce vibrant, colorful flowers. The plant features thick, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Its flowers, which range in color from yellow to orange and red, bloom during the warmer months.

Chileorebutia reichei is well-suited for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.
