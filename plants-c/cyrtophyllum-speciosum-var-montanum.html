Cyrtophyllum speciosum var. montanum

Cyrtophyllum speciosum var. montanum, commonly known as the mountain cyrtophyllum, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that are often dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found at high elevations.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
