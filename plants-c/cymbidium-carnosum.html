Cymbidium carnosum

Cymbidium carnosum, commonly known as the woolly orchid, is a species native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a rosette of fleshy, woolly leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. Its blooms typically appear in spring and summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Cymbidium carnosum is valued for its drought tolerance and unique appearance, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
