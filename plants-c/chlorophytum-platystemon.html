Chlorophytum platystemon

Chlorophytum platystemon, commonly known as the snake plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a distinctive white or yellowish stripe. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, which helps it store water and survive in arid environments.

Chlorophytum platystemon is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
