Cyperus triceps var. angustifolius

Cyperus triceps var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland environments.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

Its botanical structure makes it an important component of aquatic ecosystems.
