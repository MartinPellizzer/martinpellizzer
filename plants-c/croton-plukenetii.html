Croton plukenetii

Croton plukenetii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often brightly colored leaves that can range from green to red or orange. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Croton plukenetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
