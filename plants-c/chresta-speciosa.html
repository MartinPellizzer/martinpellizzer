Chresta speciosa

Chresta speciosa, commonly known as the showy chresta, is a striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features palmate leaves with a glossy surface, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is often cultivated for both its aesthetic value and its role in supporting local pollinators.
