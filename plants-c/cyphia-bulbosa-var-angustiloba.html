Cyphia bulbosa var. angustiloba

Cyphia bulbosa var. angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Cyphia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bulbous stem and narrow, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
