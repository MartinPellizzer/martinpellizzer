Chamaecrista cytisoides var. unijuga

Chamaecrista cytisoides var. unijuga, commonly known as the narrowleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of many leguminous species. It thrives in sandy or loamy soils and is often found in open woodlands and along roadsides.
