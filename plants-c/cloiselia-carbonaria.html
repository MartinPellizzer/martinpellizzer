Cloiselia carbonaria

Cloiselia carbonaria, commonly known as the carbonaria lily, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a basal rosette.

Cloiselia carbonaria thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
