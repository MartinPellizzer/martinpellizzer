Cyrtopodium lissochiloides

Cyrtopodium lissochiloides, commonly known as the smooth-lipped orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome and multiple pseudobulbs, each bearing a single leaf. The plant produces pendant, showy flowers with a distinctive lip that is smooth and often marked with contrasting patterns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both greenhouse and outdoor cultivation.
