Cryptocarya percoriacea

Cryptocarya percoriacea, commonly known as the broad-leafed cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, dark brown drupe that contains a single seed.
