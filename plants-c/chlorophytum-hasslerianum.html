Chlorophytum hasslerianum

Chlorophytum hasslerianum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green stripes, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
