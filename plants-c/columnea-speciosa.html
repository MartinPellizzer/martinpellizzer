Columnea speciosa

Columnea speciosa, commonly known as the showy flame flower, is a tropical perennial shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Columnea speciosa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
