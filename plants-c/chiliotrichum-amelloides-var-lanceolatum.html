Chiliotrichum amelloides var. lanceolatum

Chiliotrichum amelloides var. lanceolatum, commonly known as the lanceolate tawny veldt, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in its natural habitat.
