Casearia reflexa

Casearia reflexa, commonly known as the coral tree or red coral tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Casearia reflexa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
