Costus osae

Costus osae, commonly known as the red ginger or red costus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and related plants. The plant features tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. Its most striking characteristic is the clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom at the top of the stems.
