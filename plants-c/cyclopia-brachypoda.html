Cyclopia brachypoda

Cyclopia brachypoda, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky, coastal areas. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cyclopia brachypoda is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
