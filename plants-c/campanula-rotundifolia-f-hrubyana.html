Campanula rotundifolia f. hrubyana

Campanula rotundifolia f. hrubyana, commonly known as the Hrubyana bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are rounded and often hairy, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in gardens and landscape designs.
