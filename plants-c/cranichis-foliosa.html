Cranichis foliosa

Cranichis foliosa, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette. The plant produces small, delicate orchid flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
