Clematis odorata azurea

Clematis odorata azurea, commonly known as the Azure Clematis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features climbing stems with palmate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its showy blooms and pleasant fragrance.
