Carex aperta f. hydroessa

Carex aperta f. hydroessa, commonly known as the water sedge.

This variant of Carex aperta is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland habitats. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain both male and female flowers.

It is often found in marshy areas and is valued for its ability to thrive in saturated soils.
