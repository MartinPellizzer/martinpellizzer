Coelogyne minimiflora

Coelogyne minimiflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid or small-flowered coelogyne, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms that are clustered in a raceme. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne minimiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
