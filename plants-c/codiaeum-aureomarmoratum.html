Codiaeum aureomarmoratum

Codiaeum aureomarmoratum, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of yellow, green, and white in intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its eye-catching appearance.

However, it requires bright indirect light and well-draining soil to maintain its health and vibrant coloration.
