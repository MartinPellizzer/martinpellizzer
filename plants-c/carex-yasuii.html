Carex yasuii

Carex yasuii, commonly known as Yasui's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, rushes, and similar plants. This species is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Carex yasuii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
