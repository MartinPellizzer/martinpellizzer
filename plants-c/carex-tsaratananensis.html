Carex tsaratananensis

Carex tsaratananensis, commonly known as the Tsaratanana sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or swampy areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny flowers.
