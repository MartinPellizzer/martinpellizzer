Critesion marinum subsp. gussoneanum

Critesion marinum subsp.

gussoneanum, commonly known as the Mediterranean thyme, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and the Mediterranean region. It features aromatic, narrow leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas. Its subspecies distinction is based on subtle differences in leaf morphology and habitat preferences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
