Cistus medius

Cistus medius, commonly known as rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus medius is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
