Catevala tessellata

Catevala tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its intricate, mosaic-like patterns on its leaves and petals, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, thriving in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Catevala tessellata is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
