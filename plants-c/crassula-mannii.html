Crassula mannii

Crassula mannii, commonly known as the rock rose or silver bush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula mannii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
