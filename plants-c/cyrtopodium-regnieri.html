Cyrtopodium regnieri

Cyrtopodium regnieri, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces slender, hairy stems that bear several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Cyrtopodium regnieri thrives in shady, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
