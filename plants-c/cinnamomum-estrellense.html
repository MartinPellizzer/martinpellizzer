Cinnamomum estrellense

Cinnamomum estrellense, commonly known as Estrellense cinnamon, is a species of tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits containing dark brown seeds. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a leathery texture and a distinctive cinnamon-like scent.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
