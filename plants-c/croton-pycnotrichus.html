Croton pycnotrichus

Croton pycnotrichus, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton pycnotrichus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
