Corybas dilatatus

Corybas dilatatus, commonly known as the broad-leaved hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, hood-like flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant has a short, stocky stem with a single, broad ovate leaf. Its flowers are usually borne in a loose cluster and are pollinated by flies.

Corybas dilatatus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
