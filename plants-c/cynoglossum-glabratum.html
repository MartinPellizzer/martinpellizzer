Cynoglossum glabratum

Cynoglossum glabratum, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

The plant is typically found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family, which is known for its hairy stems and showy flowers.
