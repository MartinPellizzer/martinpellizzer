Calamagrostis avenacea

Calamagrostis avenacea, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a tall perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray leaves and feathery plumes that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for prairie restoration and ornamental gardens. It is drought-tolerant and often used in landscape design for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.

The plant plays a role in supporting local wildlife, including birds and insects, by providing food and habitat.
