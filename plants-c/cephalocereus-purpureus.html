Cephalocereus purpureus

Cephalocereus purpureus, commonly known as the Purple Cactus or the Purple Moon Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are typically green with a purple tint. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing at the top of the stem. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
