Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. suavis

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. suavis, commonly known as the sweet cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a smooth texture and a dark green color.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
