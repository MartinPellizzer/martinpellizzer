Cattleya dowiana var. aurea-marmorata

Cattleya dowiana var. aurea-marmorata, commonly known as the Golden-Marbled Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, showy flowers with a mix of yellow and marbled patterns, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces fragrant blooms that typically appear during the warmer months of the year.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation under proper care.
