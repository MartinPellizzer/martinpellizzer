Cotoneaster nitidus var. duthieanus

Cotoneaster nitidus var. duthieanus, commonly known as the Duthie's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is often used in landscaping due to its compact growth habit and seasonal interest.
