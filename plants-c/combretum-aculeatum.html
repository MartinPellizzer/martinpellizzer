Combretum aculeatum

Combretum aculeatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flower clusters that appear during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It is often used in traditional medicine and is also valued for its ornamental appeal in landscaping.
