Coleus caldericola

Coleus caldericola, commonly known as the Caldeira coleus, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves displaying shades of red, orange, and purple. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other herbs and ornamental plants. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
