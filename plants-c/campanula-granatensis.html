Campanula granatensis

Campanula granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Campanula granatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
