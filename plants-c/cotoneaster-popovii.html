Cotoneaster popovii

Cotoneaster popovii, commonly known as the Popov's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Central Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Cotoneaster popovii is adaptable to various soil types and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.
