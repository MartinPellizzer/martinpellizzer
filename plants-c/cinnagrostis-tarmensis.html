Cinnagrostis tarmensis

Cinnagrostis tarmensis, commonly known as the Tarmian grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue, and it thrives in well-drained, rocky soils.

Cinnagrostis tarmensis is often found in alpine and subalpine environments, where it contributes to the ecological diversity of these high-altitude habitats.
