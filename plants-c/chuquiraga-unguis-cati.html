Chuquiraga unguis-cati

Chuquiraga unguis-cati, commonly known as the cat's claw.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina, Chile, and Peru. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture.

Chuquiraga unguis-cati is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
