Caesalpinia volkensii

Caesalpinia volkensii, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Caesalpinia volkensii is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
