Cordyline mauritiana

Cordyline mauritiana, commonly known as the red-veined dracaena, is a striking tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent red veins. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its dramatic appearance and air-purifying qualities.

Cordyline mauritiana is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
