Cynanchum stepposum

Cynanchum stepposum, commonly known as steppe milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers clustered in umbels, which bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Cynanchum stepposum is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
