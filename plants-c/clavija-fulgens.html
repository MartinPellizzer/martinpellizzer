Clavija fulgens

Clavija fulgens, commonly known as the golden clavija, is a striking plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their medicinal properties.
