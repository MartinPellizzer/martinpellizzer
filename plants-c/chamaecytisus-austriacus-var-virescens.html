Chamaecytisus austriacus var. virescens

Chamaecytisus austriacus var. virescens, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to the Alps.

It features bright green, leathery leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers in early summer, which are attractive to pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice in horticulture.
