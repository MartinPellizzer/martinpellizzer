Cymbidium ustulatum

Cymbidium ustulatum, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that often display a striking red or orange coloration. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, producing clusters of fragrant, strap-shaped petals. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cymbidium ustulatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
