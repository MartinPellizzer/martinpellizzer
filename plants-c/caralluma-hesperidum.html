Caralluma hesperidum

Caralluma hesperidum, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems that bear clusters of vibrant flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems and leaves. It produces flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the winter months.

Its unique appearance and blooming habit make it a popular ornamental plant.
