Campanula glomerata var. glabra

Campanula glomerata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its clustered, bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils in full sun to partial shade. It is native to southern Europe and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This perennial species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
