Cerberiopsis obtusifolia

Cerberiopsis obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved cerberiopsis, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is typically found in coastal or lowland areas.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
