Callianthemum imbricatum

Callianthemum imbricatum, commonly known as the mountain daffodil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Callianthemum imbricatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
