Coelogyne veronicae

Coelogyne veronicae, commonly known as the Veronica orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, China, and Indonesia. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It is epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks, and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
