Cyclamen balearicum

Cyclamen balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which are typical of the Cyclamen genus. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Cyclamen balearicum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
