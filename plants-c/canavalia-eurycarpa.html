Canavalia eurycarpa

Canavalia eurycarpa, commonly known as the broad-seeded cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with stipules. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruit is a flat, dehiscent pod containing numerous seeds.

Canavalia eurycarpa is valued for its nutritional content and is used in both traditional and modern agricultural practices.
