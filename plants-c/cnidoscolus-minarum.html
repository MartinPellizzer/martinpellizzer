Cnidoscolus minarum

Cnidoscolus minarum, commonly known as the Mexican huito, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are small and yellow. It produces edible fruits that resemble small, green, round balls.
