Comanthosphace nepalensis

Comanthosphace nepalensis, commonly known as the Himalayan liverwort, is a small, leafy liverwort belonging to the family Comastomataceae.

It is characterized by its closely packed, green, and succulent leaves that form a dense mat. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
