Crassula commutata

Crassula commutata, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula commutata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
