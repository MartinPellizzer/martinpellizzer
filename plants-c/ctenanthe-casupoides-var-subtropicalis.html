Ctenanthe casupoides var. subtropicalis

Ctenanthe casupoides var. subtropicalis, commonly known as the subtropical flameleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, waxy leaves with a striking red or orange hue, often with a contrasting green or yellow edge. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It is relatively low-maintenance but benefits from regular pruning to encourage bushier growth.
