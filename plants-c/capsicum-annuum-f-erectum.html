Capsicum annuum f. erectum

Capsicum annuum f. erectum, commonly known as the upright bell pepper.

This variety is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit, which makes it ideal for small spaces and container gardening. It produces large, bell-shaped fruits that are typically orange, yellow, or red when mature. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves and thick, fleshy stems.

Botanically, it is a cultivar of the common chili pepper species, known for its high yield and adaptability to various growing conditions.
