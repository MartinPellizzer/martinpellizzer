Cardamine changbaiana

Cardamine changbaiana, commonly known as the Changba watercress, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features small, delicate leaves that are often submerged or floating in cool, clear streams. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round pods containing several seeds.

This species is valued for its edible leaves and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.
