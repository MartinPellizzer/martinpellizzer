Chrysopsis villosa var. camphorata

Chrysopsis villosa var. camphorata, commonly known as the camphoraceous chrysopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant features a sprawling growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped foliage. Its flowers are typically yellow, with ray florets that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
