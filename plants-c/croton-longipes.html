Croton longipes

Croton longipes, commonly known as the longipes croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Croton longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for tropical landscapes.
