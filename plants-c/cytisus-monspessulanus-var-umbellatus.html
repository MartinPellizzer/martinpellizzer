Cytisus monspessulanus var. umbellatus

Cytisus monspessulanus var. umbellatus, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe.

It features bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and long-lasting blooms.
