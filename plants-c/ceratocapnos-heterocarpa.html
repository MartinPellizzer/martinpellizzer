Ceratocapnos heterocarpa

Ceratocapnos heterocarpa, commonly known as the horned poppy, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The seeds are enclosed in a capsule that splits open to release the tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
