Cinnamomum henricii

Cinnamomum henricii, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Kuanhong, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark and leaves. The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall, with smooth, reddish-brown bark that exudes a strong cinnamon scent. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a distinctively fragrant aroma.

The plant is valued for its medicinal and culinary uses, particularly in traditional Chinese medicine.
