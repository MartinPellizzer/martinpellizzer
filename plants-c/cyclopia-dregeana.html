Cyclopia dregeana

Cyclopia dregeana, commonly known as the mountain tea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, and its leaves are often used in traditional medicine. Cyclopia dregeana is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal teas.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
