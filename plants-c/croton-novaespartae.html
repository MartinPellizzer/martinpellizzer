Croton novaespartae

Croton novaespartae, commonly known as the New Spartans croton, is a flowering plant native to the island of Sardinia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture despite its limited natural range.
