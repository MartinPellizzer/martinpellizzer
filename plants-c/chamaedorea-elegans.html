Chamaedorea elegans

Chamaedorea elegans, commonly known as the parlor palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, feather-like leaves that grow in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and elegant appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is highly adaptable and suitable for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements.

Chamaedorea elegans is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic of interior spaces.
