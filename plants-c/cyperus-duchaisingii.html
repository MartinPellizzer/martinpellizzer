Cyperus duchaisingii

Cyperus duchaisingii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, often forming dense stands in shallow water or moist soil. Its slender, grass-like stems are usually unbranched, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Cyperus duchaisingii is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for aquatic life.
