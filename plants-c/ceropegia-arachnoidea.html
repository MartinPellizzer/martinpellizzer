Ceropegia arachnoidea

Ceropegia arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider plant or string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are typically hidden within leaf-like bracts. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia arachnoidea is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
