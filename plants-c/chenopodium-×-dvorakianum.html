Chenopodium × dvorakianum

Chenopodium × dvorakianum, commonly known as Dvorak's goosefoot, is a hybrid plant that belongs to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically alternate and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its growth habit is prostrate to upright, depending on environmental conditions.

Chenopodium × dvorakianum is known for its adaptability and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
