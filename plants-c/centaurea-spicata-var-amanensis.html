Centaurea spicata var. amanensis

Centaurea spicata var. amanensis, commonly known as the Amanescent thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, spiny stems that rise from a basal rosette of leaves. The plant produces upright, branched inflorescences with purple to pink flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
