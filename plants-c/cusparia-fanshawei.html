Cusparia fanshawei

Cusparia fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its bark is often smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Due to its vibrant flowers and adaptability, it is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal.
