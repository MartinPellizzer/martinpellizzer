Cattleya × elegans var. tautziana

Cattleya × elegans var. tautziana, commonly known as the Tautziana orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features upright, pseudobulbous stems with broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically large, with a distinctive coloration of white and pink, and a fragrant scent.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
