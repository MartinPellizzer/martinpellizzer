Cytisus ratisbonensis f. pedunculosus

Cytisus ratisbonensis f. pedunculosus, commonly known as the Ratisbon broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate, fern-like foliage and bright floral display.
