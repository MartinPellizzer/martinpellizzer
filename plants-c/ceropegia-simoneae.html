Ceropegia simoneae

Ceropegia simoneae, commonly known as the Simone's African Violet, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its unique floral structure is adapted for specific pollinators, making it an interesting subject for botanical study.
