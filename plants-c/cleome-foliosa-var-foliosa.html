Cleome foliosa var. foliosa

Cleome foliosa var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, yellowish-white flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage.

Cleome foliosa var. foliosa is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
