Caladium sieboldii

Caladium sieboldii, commonly known as the Siebold's arrowhead, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium sieboldii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, especially in shaded garden beds.
