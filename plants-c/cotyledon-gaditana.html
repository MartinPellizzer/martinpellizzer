Cotyledon gaditana

Cotyledon gaditana, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it both ornamental and drought-tolerant.
