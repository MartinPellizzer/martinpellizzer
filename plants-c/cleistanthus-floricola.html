Cleistanthus floricola

Cleistanthus floricola, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a visually stunning display. The plant has smooth, pale green bark and elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are not only showy but also attract various pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
