Caladenia rigens

Caladenia rigens, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to south-western Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained sandy soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single or two flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its flowers have a distinctive, elongated labellum that resembles a spider, giving it its common name.

Caladenia rigens is a protected species due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss.
