Cathormion moniliforme

Cathormion moniliforme, commonly known as the bead-leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, bead-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a desirable addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
