Columnea perpulchra

Columnea perpulchra, commonly known as the red-tipped plectranthus, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. Columnea perpulchra is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental foliage and vibrant blooms.
