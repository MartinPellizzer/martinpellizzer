Callisia gentlei var. elegans

Callisia gentlei var. elegans, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers, which are followed by distinctive, velvety, silver-green leaves. The plant is well-suited for hanging baskets or as a trailing plant in shaded areas due to its delicate appearance. It thrives in indirect light and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its foliage and flowering make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
