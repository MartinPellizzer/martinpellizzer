Cistus populifolius var. psilocalyx

Cistus populifolius var. psilocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are narrow and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
