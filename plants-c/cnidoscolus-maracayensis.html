Cnidoscolus maracayensis

Cnidoscolus maracayensis, commonly known as the arrowleaf batocera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers that are followed by spiny, spherical fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
