Clematis indica

Clematis indica, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is a deciduous vine that produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and blue. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Clematis indica thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for climbing structures or trellises.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.
