Croton rex

Croton rex, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are often variegated with contrasting patterns, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden areas.
