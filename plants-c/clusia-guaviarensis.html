Clusia guaviarensis

Clusia guaviarensis, commonly known as the Guaviare clusia, is a tropical shrub native to the Colombian region of Guaviare.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family, which includes plants known for their showy flowers and often ornamental value. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its native habitat. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Clusia guaviarensis is adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
