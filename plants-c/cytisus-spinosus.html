Cytisus spinosus

Cytisus spinosus, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with numerous leaflets, and it produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
