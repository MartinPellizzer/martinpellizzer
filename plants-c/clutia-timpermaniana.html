Clutia timpermaniana

Clutia timpermaniana, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Clutia timpermaniana is also valued for its role in supporting pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
