Coelogyne wichersii

Coelogyne wichersii, commonly known as the White Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Coelogyne wichersii is prized for its elegant appearance and is commonly cultivated for ornamental purposes.
