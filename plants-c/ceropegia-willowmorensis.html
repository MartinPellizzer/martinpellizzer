Ceropegia willowmorensis

Ceropegia willowmorensis, commonly known as the Willowmore vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, fleshy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental flowers and ability to climb or trail.

Ceropegia willowmorensis is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
