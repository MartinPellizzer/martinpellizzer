Conyza Nitida

Conyza nitida is a small, annual flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East. It is commonly found in dry, disturbed habitats such as fields and roadsides. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Despite its modest size, Conyza nitida plays a role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems.
