Cestrum porphyreum

Cestrum porphyreum, commonly known as the purple cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
