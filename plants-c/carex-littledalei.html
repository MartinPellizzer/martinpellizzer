Carex littledalei

Carex littledalei, commonly known as the Littledale sedge, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. This species typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silver-white stripe running down the center.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color.
