Claoxylon columnare

Claoxylon columnare, commonly known as the straight-stemmed tree or columnar tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender, and columnar growth habit, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has smooth, dark-brown to grey bark and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Claoxylon columnare is often cultivated for its unique form and is used in horticulture for landscaping and as a specimen plant.
