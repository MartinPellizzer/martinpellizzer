Centaurea scabiosa

Centaurea scabiosa, commonly known as the Scabious, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its bright blue or purple composite flowers, which are arranged in a dense, spherical shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in meadows, grasslands, and disturbed soils. Centaurea scabiosa is known for its medicinal properties, with historical use in treating skin conditions and as a vulnerary.

It is also a valuable plant for pollinators, attracting bees and butterflies.
