Cymbalariella mollis

Cymbalariella mollis, commonly known as the soft cymbalariella, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with several narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and pale greenish-yellow, often appearing in clusters.

Due to its delicate nature and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species within the orchid family.
