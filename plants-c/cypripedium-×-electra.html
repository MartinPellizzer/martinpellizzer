Cypripedium × electra

Cypripedium × electra, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Cypripedium calceolus and Cypripedium pubescens, resulting in a plant with distinctive, showy flowers. The plant features a tall, slender stem with a single, pouch-like lip that is often a deep purple or reddish color. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
