Ceratostylis sphaerocephala

Ceratostylis sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globular spike orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular inflorescence. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in a rounded spike. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.

The species is notable for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
