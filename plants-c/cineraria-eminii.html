Cineraria eminii

Cineraria eminii, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Sierra Nevada mountain range in Spain.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist conditions. It has narrow, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Cineraria eminii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, colorful blooms.
