Catevala Viscosa

Catevala viscosa is a unique plant known for its sticky, resinous properties. Found primarily in arid and semi-arid regions, it has adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its thick, waxy leaves help retain moisture in dry climates. The plant's sticky secretions have been used by indigenous communities for centuries. This article explores its ecological role, traditional uses, and scientific significance.
