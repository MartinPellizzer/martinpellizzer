Chrysanthemum oreastrum

Chrysanthemum oreastrum, commonly known as the golden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
