Costularia robusta

Costularia robusta, commonly known as the roughleaf woodruff, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant has dark green, rough-textured leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Costularia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
