Carruthersia scandens

Carruthersia scandens, commonly known as the climbing lily, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
