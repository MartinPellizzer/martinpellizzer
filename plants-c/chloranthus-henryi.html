Chloranthus henryi

Chloranthus henryi, commonly known as the Chinese angel's trumpet, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Chloranthaceae and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has simple, opposite leaves. It produces small, oval fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native habitat.
