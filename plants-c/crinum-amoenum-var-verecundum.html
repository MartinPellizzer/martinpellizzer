Crinum amoenum var. verecundum

Crinum amoenum var. verecundum, commonly known as the shy lily or the white crinum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped blooms that are often borne on long, slender stems. It is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

The variety verecundum is distinguished by its more subdued flower color compared to other Crinum species.
