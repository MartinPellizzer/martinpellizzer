Cissampelos umbellata

Cissampelos umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella leaf or yellow cissampelos, is a climbing perennial vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves that are often bright green with a velvety texture. The plant produces yellow, star-shaped flowers that are clustered in umbels, giving it its common name. Its fruits are fleshy, berry-like structures that turn red when mature.

Cissampelos umbellata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
