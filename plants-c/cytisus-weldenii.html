Cytisus weldenii

Cytisus weldenii, commonly known as the Welden's broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod fruits and compound leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, adapting well to the island's climate.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
