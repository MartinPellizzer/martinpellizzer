Capsicum annuum var. ovoideum

Capsicum annuum var. ovoideum, commonly known as the eggplant pepper, is a variety of chili pepper characterized by its elongated, oval-shaped fruits.

The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with hairy stems and alternate, ovate leaves. Its flowers are yellow and bilaterally symmetrical, typical of the Solanaceae family. The fruit develops from a single ovary and matures from green to a deep red or orange hue.

This variety is valued for its mild to medium heat level and is often used in culinary applications.
