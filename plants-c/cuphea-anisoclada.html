Cuphea anisoclada

Cuphea anisoclada, commonly known as the zigzag hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cuphea anisoclada is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
