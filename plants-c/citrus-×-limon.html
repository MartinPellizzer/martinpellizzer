Citrus × limon

Citrus × limon, commonly known as the lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus fruit that originated from the crossbreeding of the citron and the pomelo. The plant is characterized by its evergreen shrub or small tree with glossy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are fragrant and typically white, leading to the formation of yellow, oval-shaped fruits.

The fruit's peel contains essential oils and is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
