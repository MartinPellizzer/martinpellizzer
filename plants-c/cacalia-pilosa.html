Cacalia pilosa

Cacalia pilosa, commonly known as the hairy cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Cacalia pilosa is often found in dry, open habitats like grasslands and rocky slopes.
