Chamarea gracillima

Chamarea gracillima, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a slightly fuzzy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Chamarea gracillima is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate, long-lasting blooms.
