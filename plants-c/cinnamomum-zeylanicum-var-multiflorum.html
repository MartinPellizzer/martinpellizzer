Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. multiflorum

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. multiflorum, commonly known as cinnamon tree with multiple flowers, is a variant of the cinnamon tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that give way to dark blue berries. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its essential oils and medicinal properties.

This variety is closely related to the commercial cinnamon species used in culinary applications.
