Caulinia nigricans

Caulinia nigricans, commonly known as the black nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants such as tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are toxic to humans and animals. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, with a distinctive dark green color.

Caulinia nigricans is often found in disturbed habitats and can be invasive in certain areas.
