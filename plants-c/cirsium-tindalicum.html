Cirsium tindalicum

Cirsium tindalicum, commonly known as the Tindal thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the British Isles.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features a rosette of spiny, grey-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are purple to pink, forming a tall, spiky inflorescence that blooms from late summer to autumn.

Cirsium tindalicum is known for its robust structure and is often found in coastal and upland regions.
