Callicarpa Cana Var. Sumatrana

Callicarpa cana var. sumatrana is a unique species of flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, commonly known as the mint family. This variant is distinguished by its vibrant red berries and aromatic foliage. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is gaining attention among horticulturists and botanists.
