Crambe orientalis var. glaberrima

Crambe orientalis var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth crambe, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered with smooth, hairless leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its root system is notable for containing high levels of erucic acid, making it valuable in industrial applications.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.
