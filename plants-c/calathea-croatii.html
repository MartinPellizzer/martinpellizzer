Calathea croatii

Calathea croatii, commonly known as the Croatian calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.
