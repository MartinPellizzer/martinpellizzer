Cypripedium parviflorum var. exiliens

Cypripedium parviflorum var. exiliens, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense clusters of small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
