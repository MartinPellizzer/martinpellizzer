Cyperus Bifax

Cyperus bifax is a unique member of the sedge family known for its distinctive appearance and adaptability. This plant is often found in wetland environments, thriving in moist to aquatic conditions. Its slender stems and small, clustered flowers make it a subtle yet resilient species. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem. Understanding Cyperus bifax helps highlight the diversity and importance of wetland flora.
