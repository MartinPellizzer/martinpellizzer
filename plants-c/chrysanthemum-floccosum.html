Chrysanthemum floccosum

Chrysanthemum floccosum, commonly known as the woolly chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its soft, fluffy flowers that resemble clouds, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in woodland areas and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are hairy and grayish-green, providing a striking contrast to its bright yellow to white blossoms.

Chrysanthemum floccosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
