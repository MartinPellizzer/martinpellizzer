Crateva adansonii subsp. odora

Crateva adansonii subsp.

odora, commonly known as the sweet-scented horned cucumber, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, heart-shaped leaves and yellowish-orange flowers.

The fruit is a spiny, elongated capsule that turns red when mature. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its fragrant, edible fruits.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic and culinary uses.
