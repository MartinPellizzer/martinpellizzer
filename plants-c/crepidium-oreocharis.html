Crepidium oreocharis

Crepidium oreocharis, commonly known as the black orchid, is a unique orchid species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black-colored flowers, which are highly unusual in the orchid family. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to bloom.

Due to its rarity and striking appearance, it is highly sought after by botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
