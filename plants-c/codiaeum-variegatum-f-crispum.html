Codiaeum variegatum f. crispum

Codiaeum variegatum f. crispum, commonly known as the croton.

This plant is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage that displays shades of green, yellow, and red. The leaves are typically crinkled or wavy, giving the plant its "crispum" designation. It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Asia.

The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
