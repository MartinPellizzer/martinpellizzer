Centaurea calcitrapa var. autumnalis

Centaurea calcitrapa var. autumnalis, commonly known as autumn centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, bract-like leaves around the flower heads.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.
