Callitris preissii var. murrayensis

Callitris preissii var. murrayensis, commonly known as the Murray Cypress, is a variant of the Australian cypress species native to the arid regions of New South Wales and Queensland.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree with a distinctive, narrow, conical shape and dense, scale-like foliage. The leaves are typically dark green and arranged in crossed pairs along the branches. It produces small, cone-like structures that contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This plant is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and can thrive in harsh, semi-arid environments.
