Convolvulus rectangularis

Convolvulus rectangularis, commonly known as the squareleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive square-shaped stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its rapid growth and trailing habit make it suitable for container planting or as a ground cover.
