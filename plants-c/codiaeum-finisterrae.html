Codiaeum finisterrae

Codiaeum finisterrae, commonly known as the "Tahitian flame" or "Rubiaceae," is a striking tropical plant native to the island of Tahiti.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red to yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes over 12,000 species of flowering plants. Codiaeum finisterrae thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
