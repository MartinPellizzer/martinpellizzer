Cistus lanuginosus

Cistus lanuginosus, commonly known as the woolly rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and clusters of pink or pale pink flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cistus lanuginosus is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
