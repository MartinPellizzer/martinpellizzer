Clitandropsis grandiflora

Clitandropsis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered clitandropsis, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, spurred shape. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
