Chaptalia undulata

Chaptalia undulata, commonly known as the zigzag phlox, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the tribe Phlox, which includes many species of flowering plants known for their showy blooms. The plant is characterized by its zigzagging stems and clusters of small, pink or purple flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Chaptalia undulata is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
