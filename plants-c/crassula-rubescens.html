Crassula rubescens

Crassula rubescens, commonly known as the red crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, upright stems and small, rounded leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Crassula rubescens is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.
