Cynorkis filiformis

Cynorkis filiformis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, thin stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
