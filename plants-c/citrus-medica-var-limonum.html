Citrus medica var. limonum

Citrus medica var. limonum, commonly known as the lemon.

It is a citrus fruit characterized by its yellow, acidic pulp and thin, fragrant peel. The plant is a small evergreen shrub with glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is widely cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses.
