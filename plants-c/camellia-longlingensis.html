Camellia longlingensis

Camellia longlingensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species native to China, particularly found in Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers in the spring, which are often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Camellia longlingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for both its flowers and its seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
