Crassula rubricaulis

Crassula rubricaulis, commonly known as the Red-veined Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with distinct red veins, giving it its characteristic appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
