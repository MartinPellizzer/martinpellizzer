Clusia loranthacea f. heliophoba

Clusia loranthacea f. heliophoba, commonly known as the sun-loving Clusia, is a tropical shrub characterized by its evergreen, thick, waxy leaves that reflect sunlight.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Central and South America. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit with multiple stems, often forming dense clusters. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow, and are clustered in inflorescences.

This variety is noted for its ability to thrive in full sun conditions, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
