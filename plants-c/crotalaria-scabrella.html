Crotalaria scabrella

Crotalaria scabrella, commonly known as the scarlet burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by animals. Crotalaria scabrella is part of the Fabaceae family, known for its nitrogen-fixing abilities.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
