Chelonopsis pseudobracteata var. rubra

Chelonopsis pseudobracteata var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered chelonopsis, is a unique member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers, which contrast with its deep green, lance-shaped leaves. Native to tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It produces small, clustered inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in specialty gardens and greenhouses.
