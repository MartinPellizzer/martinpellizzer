Cyperus conglomeratus

Cyperus conglomeratus, commonly known as the clustered flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with triangular stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Cyperus conglomeratus is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in wetlands.
