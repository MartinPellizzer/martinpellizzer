Croton stellatorotatus

Croton stellatorotatus, commonly known as the star-shaped croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that resemble a star, hence its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange to red berries. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant coloration.

Croton stellatorotatus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
