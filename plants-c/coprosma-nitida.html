Coprosma nitida

Coprosma nitida, commonly known as silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine silvery or whitish bloom, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers in spring, which are followed by bright red berries in late summer. It is typically found in coastal and lowland areas, growing in a variety of soil types.

Coprosma nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
