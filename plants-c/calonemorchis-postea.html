Calonemorchis postea

Calonemorchis postea, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate flowers that are typically white or pale green, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calonemorchis postea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
