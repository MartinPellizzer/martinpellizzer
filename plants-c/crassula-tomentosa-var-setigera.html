Crassula tomentosa var. setigera

Crassula tomentosa var. setigera, commonly known as the Wooly Crassula or Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-gray leaves covered in a dense layer of white, woolly hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
