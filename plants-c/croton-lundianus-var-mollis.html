Croton lundianus var. mollis

Croton lundianus var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a striking shrub native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a rich green color. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange or red berries.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique texture and vibrant seasonal color changes.
