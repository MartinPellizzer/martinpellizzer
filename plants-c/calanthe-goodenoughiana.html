Calanthe goodenoughiana

Calanthe goodenoughiana, commonly known as the Goodenough's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces small, clustered flowers on a sturdy scape, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Calanthe goodenoughiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
