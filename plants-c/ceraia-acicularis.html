Ceraia acicularis

Ceraia acicularis, commonly known as the slender sea snail, is a species of sea snail in the family Littorinidae.

It is a small gastropod mollusk characterized by its elongated, conical shell that is typically pale in color with a smooth surface. The shell provides protection against predation and environmental stressors, making it an important feature of its survival strategy.
