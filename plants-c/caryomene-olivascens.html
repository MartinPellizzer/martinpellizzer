Caryomene olivascens

Caryomene olivascens, commonly known as the olive-scented caryomene, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers are typically yellow to orange in color. Caryomene olivascens is known for its aromatic properties, particularly the subtle olive scent emitted from its foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and shrublands.
