Cyclamen vernum f. alpinum

Cyclamen vernum f. alpinum, commonly known as the alpine cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically bright green with a silvery underside. The plant produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, slightly acidic soils.

Cyclamen vernum f. alpinum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in rock gardens and shaded woodland areas.
