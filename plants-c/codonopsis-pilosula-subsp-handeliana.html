Codonopsis pilosula subsp. handeliana

Codonopsis pilosula subsp.

handeliana, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng or "dang shen," is a perennial herb native to China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy roots that resemble those of true ginseng.

The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and partial shade. Its leaves are lanceolate and slightly hairy, while its flowers are bell-shaped and usually pale yellow or greenish-white.

This subspecies is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits and is often cultivated for its medicinal roots.
