Codiaeum macgregorii

Codiaeum macgregorii, commonly known as the flame maple, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by red or orange berries.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant, eye-catching leaves.
