Cleistanthus euphlebius

Cleistanthus euphlebius, commonly known as the white-flowered cleistanthus, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers.
