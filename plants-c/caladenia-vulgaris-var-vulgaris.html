Caladenia vulgaris var. vulgaris

Caladenia vulgaris var. vulgaris, commonly known as the common spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands, often in sandy or loamy soils. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, strikingly colored flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pink or mauve, with a long, narrow spur that houses the nectar.

This species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique and attractive blooms.
