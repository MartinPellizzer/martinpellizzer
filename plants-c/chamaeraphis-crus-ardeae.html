Chamaeraphis crus-ardeae

Chamaeraphis crus-ardeae, commonly known as the red sandreed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces small, clustered greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. Chamaeraphis crus-ardeae is well adapted to coastal and arid conditions, making it a hardy ornamental and ecological plant.
