Cnemidostachys tragioides

Cnemidostachys tragioides, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its seed pods are elongated and contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its resilience to drought conditions.
