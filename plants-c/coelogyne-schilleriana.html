Coelogyne schilleriana

Coelogyne schilleriana, commonly known as the Schiller's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Coelogyne schilleriana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
