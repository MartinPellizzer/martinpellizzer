Coelogyne yuccifolia

Coelogyne yuccifolia, commonly known as the Yucca orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that resemble those of the yucca plant, giving it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Coelogyne yuccifolia is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
