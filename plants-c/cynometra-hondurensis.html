Cynometra hondurensis

Cynometra hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduran ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its hard, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has compound leaves with alternate arrangement and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

Cynometra hondurensis plays an important role in the ecological systems of its native habitat.
