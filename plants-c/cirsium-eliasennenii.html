Cirsium eliasennenii

Cirsium eliasennenii, commonly known as Eliasen's thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
