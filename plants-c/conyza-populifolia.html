Conyza populifolia

Conyza populifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture, and it produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Conyza populifolia is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in some areas.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in agricultural fields.
