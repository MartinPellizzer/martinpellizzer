Cattleya × wyattiana

Cattleya × wyattiana, commonly known as the Wyatt's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant typically produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Cattleya × wyattiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
