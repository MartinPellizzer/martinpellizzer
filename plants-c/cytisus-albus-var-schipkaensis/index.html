Cytisus Albus Var. Schipkaensis

Cytisus albus var. schipkaensis is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of Eastern Europe. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, known for its leguminous characteristics and nitrogen-fixing abilities. This variant is distinguished by its pale yellow flowers and slender, branching structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade, making it a unique addition to specialized gardens. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
