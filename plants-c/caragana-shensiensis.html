Caragana shensiensis

Caragana shensiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan shrub vetch, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

Its seeds are also utilized in traditional Chinese medicine.
