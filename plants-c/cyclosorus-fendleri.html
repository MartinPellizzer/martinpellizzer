Cyclosorus fendleri

Cyclosorus fendleri, commonly known as the Mexican water clover, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Mexico.

It features delicate, floating leaves that are typically oval or lance-shaped with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Cyclosorus fendleri is often used in water gardens and as a decorative element in ponds.
