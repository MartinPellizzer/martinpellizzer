Cyathea mindanensis

Cyathea mindanensis, commonly known as the Mindanao tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and the plant is known for its slow growth rate.

Cyathea mindanensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
