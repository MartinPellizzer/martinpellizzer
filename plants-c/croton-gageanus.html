Croton gageanus

Croton gageanus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the spring. Croton gageanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
