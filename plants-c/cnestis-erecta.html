Cnestis erecta

Cnestis erecta, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and milky sap. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
