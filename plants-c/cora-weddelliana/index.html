Cora Weddelliana

Cora weddelliana is a rare and striking epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of South America. It is known for its vibrant, colorful flowers that bloom in a unique spiral pattern. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience in the wild. Collectors and botanists alike are drawn to its beauty and ecological significance.
