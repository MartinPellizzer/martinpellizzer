Cryptolepis microphylla

Cryptolepis microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved cryptolepis, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually opposite or alternate in arrangement. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, round fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
