Crotalaria modesta

Crotalaria modesta, commonly known as the small crownflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant features erect, hairy stems and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Crotalaria modesta is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
