Campanula rotundifolia var. ovata

Campanula rotundifolia var. ovata, commonly known as the round-leaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its rounded, broad leaves and bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland environments and thrives in partial to full shade. It is a perennial herb that produces showy blooms in the summer months.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental and ecologically beneficial plants.
