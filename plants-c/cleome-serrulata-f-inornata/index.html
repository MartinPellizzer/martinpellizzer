Cleome Serrulata F. Inornata

Serrated cleome, scientifically known as Cleome serrulata f. inornata, is a strikingly unique plant native to the southeastern United States. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value, featuring vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the summer. Its foliage is characterized by serrated edges, giving it a distinctive and rugged appearance. While it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, it requires minimal maintenance once established. This plant is an excellent choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience in their landscapes.
