Clerodendrum phyllomega

Clerodendrum phyllomega, commonly known as the golden leaf clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a metallic sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clerodendrum phyllomega is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
