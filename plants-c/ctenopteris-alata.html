Ctenopteris alata

Ctenopteris alata, commonly known as the sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and have a smooth, leathery texture. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to form dense mats or colonies.

Ctenopteris alata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens.
