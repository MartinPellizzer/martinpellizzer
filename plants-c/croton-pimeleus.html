Croton pimeleus

Croton pimeleus, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or orange. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red fruits. Croton pimeleus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.

It prefers warm, humid climates and well-draining soil.
