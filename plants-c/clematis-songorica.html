Clematis songorica

Clematis songorica, commonly known as the Java clematis, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features attractive, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and blue, depending on the variety. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Clematis songorica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
