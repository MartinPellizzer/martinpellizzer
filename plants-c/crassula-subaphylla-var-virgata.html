Crassula subaphylla var. virgata

Crassula subaphylla var. virgata, commonly known as the silver moon crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped leaves that are pale blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
