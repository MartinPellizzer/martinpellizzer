Ctenanthe burle-marxii var. obscura

Ctenanthe burle-marxii var. obscura, commonly known as the obscura crocodile plant, is a striking tropical foliage plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique, vibrant, and often variegated leaves. The plant features dark green, waxy leaves with a textured surface that resembles the scales of a crocodile, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular as a houseplant and is often used in interior landscaping.
