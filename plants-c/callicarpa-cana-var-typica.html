Callicarpa cana var. typica

Callicarpa cana var. typica, commonly known as the red-berried beauty, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its small, oval leaves that turn vibrant red in the fall. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits that are edible. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its colorful foliage and attractive fruits.
