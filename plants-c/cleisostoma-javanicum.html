Cleisostoma javanicum

Cleisostoma javanicum, commonly known as the Java cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Cleisostoma javanicum thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
