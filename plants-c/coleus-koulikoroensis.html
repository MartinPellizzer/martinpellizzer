Coleus koulikoroensis

Coleus koulikoroensis, commonly known as the Koulikoro coleus, is a flowering plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are often brightly colored with patterns of red, purple, and green, making it a popular ornamental plant. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soil.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or purple, adding to its visual appeal.
