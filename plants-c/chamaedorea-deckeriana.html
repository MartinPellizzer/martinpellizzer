Chamaedorea deckeriana

Chamaedorea deckeriana, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a compact, upright palm native to the tropical regions of Mexico and Central America.

It features a slender trunk and feather-like, dark green leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its relatively low maintenance requirements. It prefers well-draining soil and consistent moisture without overwatering.

The plant's slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for smaller spaces and container gardening.
