Campium feeianum

Campium feeianum, commonly known as the Feeian campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

Campium feeianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rocky landscapes.
