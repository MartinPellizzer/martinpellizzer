Chlorophytum maculatum

Chlorophytum maculatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by delicate, grass-like berries. It is a hardy and easy-to-care-for plant that thrives in well-draining soil and moderate light.

Chlorophytum maculatum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air and is often used as a houseplant.
