Cnicus ciliatus

Cnicus ciliatus, commonly known as the common sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to June. The plant has slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy leaves that are often described as silky or velvety. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed sites.

Cnicus ciliatus is a hardy species that is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions.
