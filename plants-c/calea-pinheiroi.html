Calea pinheiroi

Calea pinheiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian candle plant, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive, candle-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Calea pinheiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
