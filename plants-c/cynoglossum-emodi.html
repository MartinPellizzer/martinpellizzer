Cynoglossum emodi

Cynoglossum emodi, commonly known as the Himalayan bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Cynoglossum emodi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
