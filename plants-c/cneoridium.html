Cneoridium

Cneoridium, commonly known as the rock rose or rock spurge, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cneoraceae.

These plants are typically found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing their adaptability to harsh conditions. They are characterized by their small, often clustered flowers that can range in color from white to yellow. The leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a rough or hairy texture.

Cneoridium species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
