Coelogyne pallida

Coelogyne pallida, commonly known as the white lady orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which are often adorned with green or brown markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting the growth of its leaves and flowers.

Coelogyne pallida is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for both horticultural and conservation purposes.
