Coelogyne thailandica

Coelogyne thailandica, commonly known as the Thai orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical rainforest environments. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique morphology and vibrant blooms make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
