Conocephalus amoenus

Conocephalus amoenus, commonly known as the elegant conocephalus, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, elongated inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
