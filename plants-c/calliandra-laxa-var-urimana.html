Calliandra laxa var. urimana

Calliandra laxa var. urimana, commonly known as the Urimana calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences that produce vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its showy blooms.
