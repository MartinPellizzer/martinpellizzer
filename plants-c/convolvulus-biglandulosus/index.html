Convolvulus Biglandulosus

Convolvulus biglandulosus is a flowering plant known for its distinctive spiral-shaped leaves and vibrant purple blossoms. Native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. This species is often found growing along coastal cliffs and in dry, sandy soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and drought-tolerant landscaping. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the broader horticultural community.
