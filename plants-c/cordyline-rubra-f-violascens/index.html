Cordyline Rubra F. Violascens

Cordyline rubra f. violascens is a striking variant of the cordyline plant known for its vibrant purple foliage. This plant is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value. It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that display a rich, deep violet hue. The plant is relatively low maintenance, making it ideal for novice gardeners. Its unique coloration adds a dramatic touch to any garden or living space.
