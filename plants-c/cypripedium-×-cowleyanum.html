Cypripedium × cowleyanum

Cypripedium × cowleyanum, commonly known as the Cowley's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch and a vibrant pink or white coloration. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and requires well-drained, acidic soils. It has a pseudobulb structure and produces both showy and sterile flowers.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical conservation efforts.
