Citharexylum spinosum f. villosum

Citharexylum spinosum f. villosum, commonly known as the silky citharexylum, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, and hairy foliage, which gives it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a slightly serrated margin.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.
