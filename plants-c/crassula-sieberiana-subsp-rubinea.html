Crassula sieberiana subsp. rubinea

Crassula sieberiana subsp.

rubinea, commonly known as the red crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a reddish tint, especially under bright light.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
