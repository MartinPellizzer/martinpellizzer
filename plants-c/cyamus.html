Cyamus

Cyamus, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pinnately divided leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in saline environments. Its seeds are often used in culinary applications, adding a sharp, peppery flavor to dishes.

Cyamus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
