Costus discolor

Costus discolor, commonly known as the red ginger or red costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features tall, sword-shaped leaves and produces striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its potential medicinal applications.
