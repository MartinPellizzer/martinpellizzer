Crotalaria piedboeufii

Crotalaria piedboeufii, commonly known as the pied boeuf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers. Its seed pods are typically slender and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Crotalaria piedboeufii is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.
