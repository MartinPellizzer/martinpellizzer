Cordia gerascanthus f. micrantha

Cordia gerascanthus f. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered cordia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its compact shrub form. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
