Chorizanthe nivea

Chorizanthe nivea, commonly known as white sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.
