Croton grangerioides

Croton grangerioides, commonly known as the Granger's croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a smooth or slightly textured surface. This plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
