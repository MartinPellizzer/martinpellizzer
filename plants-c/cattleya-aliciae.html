Cattleya aliciae

Cattleya aliciae, commonly known as the Alice's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces pendulous blooms with a distinctive, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya aliciae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
