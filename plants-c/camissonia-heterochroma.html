Camissonia heterochroma

Camissonia heterochroma, commonly known as the heterochromatic camissonia, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the Camissoniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white to pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are generally narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, inconspicuous fruits.
