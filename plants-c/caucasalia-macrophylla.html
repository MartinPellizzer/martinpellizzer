Caucasalia macrophylla

Caucasalia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved Caucasalia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
