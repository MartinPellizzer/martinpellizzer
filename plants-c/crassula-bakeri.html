Crassula bakeri

Crassula bakeri, commonly known as the "Baker's Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple tint under stress. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula bakeri is often grown as a houseplant or in rock gardens due to its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
