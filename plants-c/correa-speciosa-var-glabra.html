Correa speciosa var. glabra

Correa speciosa var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth correa, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its lance-shaped, smooth leaves that are typically dark green. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
