Coleus eureka

Coleus eureka, commonly known as the "Eureka Coleus," is a vibrant tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is prized for its striking, colorful foliage that displays shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture, adding a tactile element to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its eye-catching appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
