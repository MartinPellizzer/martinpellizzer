Crassula aitonii

Crassula aitonii, commonly known as the "Lucky Jade," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula aitonii is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
