Chamaefilix Alata

Chamaefilix alata is a flowering plant known for its striking appearance and hardy nature. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. This species is also called the alpine everlasting due to its ability to bloom in harsh conditions. Its delicate flowers and evergreen foliage make it a popular choice for gardens. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known gem in the world of horticulture.
