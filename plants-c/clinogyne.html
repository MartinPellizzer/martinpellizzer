Clinogyne

Clinogyne, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species native to South Africa, particularly in the mountainous regions. The plants are characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Clinogyne species typically have narrow, grass-like leaves and are often found in rocky or sandy soils.

They are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens.
