Cattleya neokautskyi

Cattleya neokautskyi, commonly known as the Neokautsky's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, with a distinctive red or purple lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its tropical environment. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

This species is noted for its fragrant blooms and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
