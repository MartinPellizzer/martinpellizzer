Cattleya crispa var. buchananiana

Cattleya crispa var. buchananiana, commonly known as the Buchanan's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescences and showy, fragrant flowers that typically display a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
