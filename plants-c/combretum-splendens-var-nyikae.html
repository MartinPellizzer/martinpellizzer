Combretum splendens var. nyikae

Combretum splendens var. nyikae, commonly known as the red-flowered flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.
