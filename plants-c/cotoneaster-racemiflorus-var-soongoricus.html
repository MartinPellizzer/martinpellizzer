Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. soongoricus

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. soongoricus, commonly known as the Korean cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It features small, glossy green leaves that turn reddish in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and as a hedge due to its compact growth habit.
