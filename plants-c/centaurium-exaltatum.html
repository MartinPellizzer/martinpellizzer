Centaurium exaltatum

Centaurium exaltatum, commonly known as the tall centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the Gentianaceae family and typically grows in moist meadows, woodlands, and stream banks. The plant features slender stems that can reach up to 60 cm in height, adorned with lance-shaped, dark green leaves. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually pink or red, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem.

Centaurium exaltatum blooms from June to September and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
