Caladenia strigosa

Caladenia strigosa, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathland and forested areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single flower that is usually pale green to yellowish in color. Its intricate floral structure includes a labellum that resembles a spider, giving it its common name.

Caladenia strigosa is a perennial herb that blooms during the spring and early summer months.
