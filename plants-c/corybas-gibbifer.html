Corybas gibbifer

Corybas gibbifer, commonly known as the hairy helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flower that is often adorned with hairy structures. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forests and rock crevices. Its flowers are usually green or brownish in color, with a short, narrow spur.

Corybas gibbifer is part of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
