Cleisostoma piliferum

Cleisostoma piliferum, commonly known as the hairy cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, hairy structures. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, spurred petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic substrates.

Cleisostoma piliferum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
