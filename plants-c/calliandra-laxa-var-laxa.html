Calliandra laxa var. laxa

Calliandra laxa var. laxa, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and showy, pink to red flower clusters that bloom in spring and summer. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it suitable for use in xeriscaping and as a hedge or screening plant.
