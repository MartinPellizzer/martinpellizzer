Clausena brevistyla var. halmaheirae

Clausena brevistyla var. halmaheirae, commonly known as the Halmaheira citrusleaf, is a variant of the citrusleaf tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and have a distinctive citrus scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange to red fruits that are edible but not widely cultivated. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for horticulturists and botanists.
