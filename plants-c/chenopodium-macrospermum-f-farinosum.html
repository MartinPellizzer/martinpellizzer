Chenopodium macrospermum f. farinosum

Chenopodium macrospermum f. farinosum, commonly known as sandmat, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces numerous small, round seeds that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is commonly found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Its structure and adaptations make it well-suited for survival in harsh, dry environments.
