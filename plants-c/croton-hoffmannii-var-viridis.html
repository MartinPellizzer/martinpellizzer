Croton hoffmannii var. viridis

Croton hoffmannii var. viridis, commonly known as the green croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features vibrant, often variegated leaves with a broad, lance-shaped form that adds visual interest to gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
