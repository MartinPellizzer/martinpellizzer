Citharexylum ambiguum

Citharexylum ambiguum, commonly known as the fragrant cithara, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a pleasant, aromatic scent. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Citharexylum ambiguum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
