Calceolaria spathulata

Calceolaria spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and ovate, with a slightly hairy texture.
