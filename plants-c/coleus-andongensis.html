Coleus andongensis

Coleus andongensis, commonly known as the Andong coleus, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South Korea.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant features square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It is often cultivated for its attractive foliage, which displays vibrant patterns of red, orange, and green.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
