Cnidoscolus Tubulosus

Cnidoscolus tubulosus is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, palmate leaves and unique, spiky fruits. Native to Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant's foliage displays a rich spectrum of colors, ranging from deep greens to fiery reds and purples. Its fruit, which resembles a small, hollow tube, contains edible seeds that are sometimes used in local cuisines. Despite its beauty, the plant can be toxic if ingested, making it both fascinating and cautionary.
