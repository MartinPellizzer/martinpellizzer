Cattleya mossiae var. kermesiana-marginata

Cattleya mossiae var. kermesiana-marginata, commonly known as the Kermesiana-marginata Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, fragrant flowers.

It features large, velvety petals with a distinctive red edge and a contrasting white or cream center. This variety is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees and absorbing moisture from the air.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
