Commianthus pilosus

Commianthus pilosus, commonly known as the silky mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Commianthus pilosus is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
