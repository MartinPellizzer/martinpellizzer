Cymbidium trichocarpon

Cymbidium trichocarpon, commonly known as the Chinese orchid or the golden orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with long, sword-shaped leaves that are often dark green in color. The plant produces tall spikes of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high light conditions.

Cymbidium trichocarpon is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in horticulture.
