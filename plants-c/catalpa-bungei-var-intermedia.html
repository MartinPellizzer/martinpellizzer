Catalpa bungei var. intermedia

Catalpa bungei var. intermedia, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces long, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release its cotton-like seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various garden settings.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
