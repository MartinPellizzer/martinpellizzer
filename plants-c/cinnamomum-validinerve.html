Cinnamomum validinerve

Cinnamomum validinerve, commonly known as the slender-veined cinnamon.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant belongs to the Lauraceae family and is valued for its essential oils and medicinal properties. Its leaves are narrow with distinct, parallel veins, and the bark is often used in traditional medicine.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
