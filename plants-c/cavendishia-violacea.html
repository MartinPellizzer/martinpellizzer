Cavendishia violacea

Cavendishia violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Cavendishia violacea thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.
