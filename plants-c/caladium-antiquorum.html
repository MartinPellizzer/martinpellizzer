Caladium antiquorum

Caladium antiquorum, commonly known as the old world arrowhead, is a tropical perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant and unique appearance.

Caladium antiquorum is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
