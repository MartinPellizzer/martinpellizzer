Conophytum pearsonii var. minor

Conophytum pearsonii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a rosette shape. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
