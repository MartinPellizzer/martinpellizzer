Correa × rubra var. virens

Correa × rubra var. virens, commonly known as the green-flowered correa, is a hybrid shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its delicate, bell-shaped flowers. The flowers are usually a soft greenish-white color, blooming in spring and attracting native pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native gardens and landscaping.
