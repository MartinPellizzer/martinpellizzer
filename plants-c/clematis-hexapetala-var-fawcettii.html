Clematis hexapetala var. fawcettii

Clematis hexapetala var. fawcettii, commonly known as the Fawcettii Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers with five to six petals, typically in shades of white to pale blue. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to climb trellises or walls.
