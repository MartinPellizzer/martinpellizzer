Clivia × cyrtanthiflora

Clivia × cyrtanthiflora, commonly known as the hybrid clivia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Clivia nobilis and Cyrtanthus.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and can grow up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Clivia × cyrtanthiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
