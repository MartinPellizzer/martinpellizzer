Codiaeum affine

Codiaeum affine, commonly known as the flame leaf or red leaf plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically large, palmately lobed, and display a range of vibrant colors including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.
