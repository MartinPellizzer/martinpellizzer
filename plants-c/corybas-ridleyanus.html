Corybas ridleyanus

Corybas ridleyanus, commonly known as the Ridley's helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically dark red or purple in color. The plant produces a single, showy bloom that is often adorned with intricate patterns and a prominent, spathelike structure. Corybas ridleyanus thrives in shaded, moist environments, often growing on rocky substrates or in dense undergrowth.

Its botanical significance lies in its distinctive morphology and its role in the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
