Carduus oleraceus

Carduus oleraceus, commonly known as common sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the daisy family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa but has been naturalized in many parts of the world. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
