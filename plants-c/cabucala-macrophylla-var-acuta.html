Cabucala macrophylla var. acuta

Cabucala macrophylla var. acuta, commonly known as the sharp-leafed cabucala, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, elongated leaves with a pointed tip.

The leaves are typically dark green and have a smooth, waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss. It is native to the Philippines and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked due to their modest size.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to tropical gardens and botanical collections.
