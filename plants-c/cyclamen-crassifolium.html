Cyclamen crassifolium

Cyclamen crassifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. Cyclamen crassifolium prefers cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
