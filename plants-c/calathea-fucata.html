Calathea fucata

Calathea fucata, commonly known as the Black Velvet plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves with striking silver veins, which become more pronounced in low light conditions. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It prefers indirect sunlight and consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea fucata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for beginners and experienced plant enthusiasts alike.
