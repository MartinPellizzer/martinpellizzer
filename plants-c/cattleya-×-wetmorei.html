Cattleya × wetmorei

Cattleya × wetmorei, commonly known as the Wetmore cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with dark red or purple markings. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with several blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

Cattleya × wetmorei thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
