Coussarea longiflora var. longiflora

Coussarea longiflora var. longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered coussarea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has a woody stem with smooth, pale bark and produces small, elliptical leaves that are oppositely arranged. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The flowers attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
