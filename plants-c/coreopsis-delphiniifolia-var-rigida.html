Coreopsis delphiniifolia var. rigida

Coreopsis delphiniifolia var. rigida, commonly known as rigid coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy perennial suitable for gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
