Calathea gentryi

Calathea gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, intricate leaf patterns. The leaves are typically a deep green with contrasting white or silver veins, which are more pronounced in low light conditions. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture without being waterlogged.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
