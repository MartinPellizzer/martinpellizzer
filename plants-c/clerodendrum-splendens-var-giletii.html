Clerodendrum splendens var. giletii

Clerodendrum splendens var. giletii, commonly known as the Giletii Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that range in color from red to purple, often mistaken for flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
