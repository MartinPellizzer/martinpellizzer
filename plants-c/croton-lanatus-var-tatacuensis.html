Croton lanatus var. tatacuensis

Croton lanatus var. tatacuensis, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, or orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by orange or red capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
