Curcuma ceratotheca

Curcuma ceratotheca, commonly known as the horned turmeric, is a member of the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like India, Indonesia, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its ornamental, fleshy, and often brightly colored rhizomes. The plant is valued both for its aesthetic appeal and for its potential medicinal properties.
