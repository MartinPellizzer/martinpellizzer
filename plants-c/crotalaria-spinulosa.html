Crotalaria spinulosa

Crotalaria spinulosa, commonly known as the prickly crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, which splits open when mature to release the seeds.

Crotalaria spinulosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
