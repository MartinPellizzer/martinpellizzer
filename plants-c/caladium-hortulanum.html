Caladium hortulanum

Caladium hortulanum, commonly known as the angel's wing, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that comes in shades of white, pink, red, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have intricate patterns, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Caladium hortulanum is typically grown as an annual in temperate regions but can be perennial in tropical areas.
