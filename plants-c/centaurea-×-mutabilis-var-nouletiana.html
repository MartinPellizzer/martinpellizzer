Centaurea × mutabilis var. nouletiana

Centaurea × mutabilis var. nouletiana, commonly known as the Variable Blazing Star, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep red, depending on the stage of bloom. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
