Calceolaria parviflora f. glabra

Calceolaria parviflora f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth calceolaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Calceolariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, but the 'glabra' form is distinguished by its smooth, hairless texture. Native to South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
