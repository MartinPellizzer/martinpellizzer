Coelogyne fuscescens var. integrilabia

Coelogyne fuscescens var. integrilabia, commonly known as the dark-labellum coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a dark red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, thriving in humid, shaded environments. It blooms during the winter months, adding a unique ornamental value to gardens and collections.

Its botanical significance lies in its distinct morphology and ecological adaptations.
