Cicca

Cicca, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has a woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The cicca thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
