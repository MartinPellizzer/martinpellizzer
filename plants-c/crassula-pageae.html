Crassula pageae

Crassula pageae, commonly known as the Page's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Crassula pageae is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its compact size and ease of care.
