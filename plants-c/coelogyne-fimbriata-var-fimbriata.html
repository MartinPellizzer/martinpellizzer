Coelogyne fimbriata var. fimbriata

Coelogyne fimbriata var. fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with long, fleshy leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique fringed appearance. The flowers are typically pink to red, with a white or yellow lip, and bloom during the dry season. This species prefers warm, humid environments and is often grown as a houseplant or in greenhouses.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
