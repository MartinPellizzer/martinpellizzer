Cyperus ostenii

Cyperus ostenii, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedges, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically brown or reddish in color. Cyperus ostenii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along water bodies.

It is a perennial herb that plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
