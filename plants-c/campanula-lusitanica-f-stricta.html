Campanula lusitanica f. stricta

Campanula lusitanica f. stricta, commonly known as the compact bellflower, is a compact, low-growing perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It features upright, branching stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This cultivar is prized for its compact growth habit and ornamental value in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens or borders.
