Campanula cochleariifolia f. venusta

Campanula cochleariifolia f. venusta, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a cultivar of the bellflower species native to Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features upright, hairy stems and heart-shaped, serrated leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is often grown in gardens for its attractive appearance and ease of care.
