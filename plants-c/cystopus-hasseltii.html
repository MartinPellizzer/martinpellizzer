Cystopus hasseltii

Cystopus hasseltii, commonly known as the mountain bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. Cystopus hasseltii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
