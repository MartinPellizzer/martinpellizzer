Cotyledon rosea

Cotyledon rosea, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or pink markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently.

Cotyledon rosea is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
