Croton klotzschii f. divaricatus

Croton klotzschii f. divaricatus, commonly known as the zigzag croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often zigzagging stems and vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and may display shades of green, red, and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
