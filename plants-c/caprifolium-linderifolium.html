Caprifolium linderifolium

Caprifolium linderifolium, commonly known as the Lindera leaf honeysuckle, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in late spring, followed by red berries that attract wildlife. It thrives in partial shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
