Crinum luteolum

Crinum luteolum, commonly known as the yellow crinum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves and produces a strong, spicy scent. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Crinum luteolum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively drought-tolerant.
