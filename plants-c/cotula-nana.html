Cotula nana

Cotula nana, commonly known as dwarf daisy or little daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of numerous tiny florets. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters, with bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Cotula nana has narrow, hairy leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is widely cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its compact size and cheerful appearance.
