Centaurea deusta var. nobilis

Centaurea deusta var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. Its flowers are tubular and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
