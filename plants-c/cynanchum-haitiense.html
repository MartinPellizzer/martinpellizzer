Cynanchum haitiense

Cynanchum haitiense, commonly known as Haitian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Haiti.

It is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its latex contains milky sap, which is characteristic of the genus Cynanchum.

It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to tropical climates.
