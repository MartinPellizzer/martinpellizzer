Croton intercedens

Croton intercedens, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from yellow to red and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton intercedens is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
