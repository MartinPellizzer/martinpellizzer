Chrysopogon crevostii

Chrysopogon crevostii, commonly known as the crevost grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-gray hue due to dense pubescence.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
