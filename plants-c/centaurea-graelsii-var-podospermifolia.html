Centaurea graelsii var. podospermifolia

Centaurea graelsii var. podospermifolia, commonly known as the Podosperma Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
