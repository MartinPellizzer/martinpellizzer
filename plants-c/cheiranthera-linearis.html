Cheiranthera linearis

Cheiranthera linearis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. It has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a new shoot from the base.

The plant's unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a sought-after species in horticulture.
