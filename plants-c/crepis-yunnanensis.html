Crepis yunnanensis

Crepis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant species native to China.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high elevations. It produces small, yellow to brown flowers that are arranged in a single head.

Crepis yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
