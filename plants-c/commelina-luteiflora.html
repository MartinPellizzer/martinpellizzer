Commelina luteiflora

Commelina luteiflora, commonly known as the yellow-flowered spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with three petals and three sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and often have a waxy surface.
