Cirsium japonicum var. takaoense

Cirsium japonicum var. takaoense, commonly known as the Takao thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cool, moist environments. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, rocky habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
