Cheirostylis tortilacinia var. wutaiensis

Cheirostylis tortilacinia var. wutaiensis, commonly known as the Wutai orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure and compact growth habit. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, intricate flowers with a distinct coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.
