Croton humilis var. origanifolius

Croton humilis var. origanifolius, commonly known as the fragrant croton, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves with a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed. The plant produces clusters of yellow-orange flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden use in suitable environments.
