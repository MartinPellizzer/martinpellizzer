Croton alpinus

Croton alpinus, commonly known as the Alpine croton, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, alpine climates.

Croton alpinus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and hardy nature.
