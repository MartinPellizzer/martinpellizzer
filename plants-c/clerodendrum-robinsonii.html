Clerodendrum robinsonii

Clerodendrum robinsonii, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Clerodendrum robinsonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
