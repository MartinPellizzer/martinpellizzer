Ctenanthe luschnathiana

Ctenanthe luschnathiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, red, and orange. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. Its foliage is typically broad and waxy, with a velvety texture that gives it its common name.

Ctenanthe luschnathiana prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist soil to thrive.
