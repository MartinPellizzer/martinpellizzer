Coelogyne trusmadiensis

Coelogyne trusmadiensis, commonly known as the Trus Madi orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a unique growth habit, characterized by its erect, leafy stems and clustered pseudobulbs. The plant produces strikingly fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with red or purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a conservation concern.
