Carduus × cadevallii

Carduus × cadevallii, commonly known as the Cadevallii thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads that resemble those of other thistle species. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in dry, rocky soils and full sun conditions. The plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some regions despite its potential to become invasive.
