Cydonia japonica var. atrosanguineaplena

Cydonia japonica var. atrosanguineaplena, commonly known as the Japanese quince with dark red flowers, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy, double pink to dark red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish fruits that are typically harvested in late summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping and traditional herbal uses.
