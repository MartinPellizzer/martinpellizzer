Centipeda cunninghamii

Centipeda cunninghamii, commonly known as the narrow-leaf centipede grass, is a perennial herb native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in dry conditions, making it a resilient plant in arid environments.
