Croton bredemeyeri

Croton bredemeyeri, commonly known as the Bredemeyeri croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Croton bredemeyeri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
