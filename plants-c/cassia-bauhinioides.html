Cassia bauhinioides

Cassia bauhinioides, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and long panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia bauhinioides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
