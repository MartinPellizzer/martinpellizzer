Clerodendrum hildebrandtii var. hildebrandtii

Clerodendrum hildebrandtii var. hildebrandtii, commonly known as the Hildebrandt's Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It is a woody shrub or small tree, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional medicine.
