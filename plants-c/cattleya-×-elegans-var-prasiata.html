Cattleya × elegans var. prasiata

Cattleya × elegans var. prasiata, commonly known as the Emerald Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.

It features large, waxy petals that range in color from green to yellow, often with intricate patterns. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

The variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
