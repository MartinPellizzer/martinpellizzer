Cyperus purpureoviridis

Cyperus purpureoviridis, commonly known as the purple and yellow flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale green to purple flowers. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens and landscapes.
