Cereus speciosissimus var. bollwillerianus

Cereus speciosissimus var. bollwillerianus, commonly known as the Bollwiller candle, is a cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and clusters of white, night-blooming flowers. The plant is a succulent, adapted to arid environments with thick, fleshy stems that store water. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
