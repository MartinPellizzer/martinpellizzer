Cattleya mossiae f. reineckeana

Cattleya mossiae f. reineckeana, commonly known as the Reineckeana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features tall, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green in color. Its flowers are typically pink to purple with a yellow lip, and they bloom once a year.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
