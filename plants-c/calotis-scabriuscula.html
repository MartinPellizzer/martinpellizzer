Calotis scabriuscula

Calotis scabriuscula, commonly known as the rough calotis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub with hairy, woody stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Calotis scabriuscula is often found in coastal areas and is a component of the local flora in sandy habitats.
