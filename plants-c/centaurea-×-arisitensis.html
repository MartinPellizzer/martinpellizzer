Centaurea × arisitensis

Centaurea × arisitensis, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are arranged in dense, conical clusters, and it produces small, achene-like seeds.

Centaurea × arisitensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
