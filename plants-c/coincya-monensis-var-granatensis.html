Coincya monensis var. granatensis

Coincya monensis var. granatensis, commonly known as the Granatense Water Sprite, is a delicate aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface, often giving it a lacy appearance. The plant is typically found in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats, such as ponds and marshes. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually submerged, contributing to its role in aquatic ecosystems.

This variety is valued in aquariums for its ornamental beauty and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
