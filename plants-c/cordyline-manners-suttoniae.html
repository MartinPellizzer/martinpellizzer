Cordyline manners-suttoniae

Cordyline manners-suttoniae, commonly known as the Manners-Sutton cordyline, is a striking evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender stems with large, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays due to its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
