Chlorophytum minutiflorum

Chlorophytum minutiflorum, commonly known as the little flowered spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its adaptability and low maintenance make it an excellent plant for both novice and experienced gardeners.
