Chomelia ulugurensis

Chomelia ulugurensis, commonly known as the Uluguru flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Uluguru Mountains of Tanzania.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
