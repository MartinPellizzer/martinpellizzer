Citharexylum brachyanthum var. glabrum

Citharexylum brachyanthum var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and are arranged in clusters along the stems.

It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
