Calyptranthes variabilis var. oblongata

Calyptranthes variabilis var. oblongata, commonly known as the variable calyptranthes, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elliptical to oblong leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on the upper surface. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in inflorescences, often emitting a faint fragrance.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
