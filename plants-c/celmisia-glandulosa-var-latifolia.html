Celmisia glandulosa var. latifolia

Celmisia glandulosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rock crevices. Its glandular leaves and unique floral structure contribute to its ecological significance in its native habitat.

It is often found in the South Island of New Zealand, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions.
