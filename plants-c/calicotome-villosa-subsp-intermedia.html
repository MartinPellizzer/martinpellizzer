Calicotome villosa subsp. intermedia

Calicotome villosa subsp.

intermedia, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its slender, often branching stems covered in fine, silky hairs.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Its leaves are typically small, alternate, and covered in a similar silky pubescence.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and along coastal regions.
