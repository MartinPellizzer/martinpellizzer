Clematis japonica f. cremea

Clematis japonica f. cremea, commonly known as the Cream Clematis, is a compact, deciduous vine native to Japan.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale yellow, often with a slight pinkish hue. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is typically heart-shaped and provides a lush backdrop to the blooms.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is well-suited for trellises or as a garden accent.
