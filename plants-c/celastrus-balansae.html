Celastrus balansae

Celastrus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae staff-vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its twining stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticultural collections.
