Cycnoches egertonianum var. viride

Cycnoches egertonianum var. viride, commonly known as the green cycnoches, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and strikingly colored flowers that range from green to yellowish-green. The plant is native to specific regions in Africa, particularly in Tanzania and Kenya, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its unique coloration and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
