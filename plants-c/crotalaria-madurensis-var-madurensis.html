Crotalaria madurensis var. madurensis

Crotalaria madurensis var. madurensis, commonly known as the Madura cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1-2 meters tall, featuring palmately lobed leaves and vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive spurred structure. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds, which are often used as a legume crop.

It is adapted to thrive in warm, humid climates and is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
