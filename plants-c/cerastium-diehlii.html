Cerastium diehlii

Cerastium diehlii, commonly known as the Sierra sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

Cerastium diehlii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptations to high-altitude conditions.
