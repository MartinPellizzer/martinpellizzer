Calluna vulgaris f. rhodostachys

Calluna vulgaris f. rhodostachys, commonly known as the red-stemmed heather, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its foliage consists of needle-like leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy cuticle.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant fall color.
