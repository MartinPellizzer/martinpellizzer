Crotalaria deflersii

Crotalaria deflersii, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in racemes, often with a distinctive crown-like appearance. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces oblong seed pods that split open when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Crotalaria deflersii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
