Citharexylum broadwayi

Citharexylum broadwayi, commonly known as the broadway hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often velvety in texture. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
