Coelogyne decipiens

Coelogyne decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive coelogyne, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has long, narrow pseudobulbs and linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to grow in shaded, humid environments.

Coelogyne decipiens is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
