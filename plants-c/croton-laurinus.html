Croton laurinus

Croton laurinus, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed and have a waxy texture.

Croton laurinus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
