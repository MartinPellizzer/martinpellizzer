Castalia odorata var. minor

Castalia odorata var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf castalia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features aromatic, dark green leaves that are glossy and lance-shaped, with a pleasant fragrance when crushed. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well-suited for container gardening due to its manageable size and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor spaces in warm climates.
