Chloranthus fortunei var. holostegius

Chloranthus fortunei var. holostegius, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Chloranthaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lantern-shaped inflorescences that are often brightly colored, typically red or orange. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
