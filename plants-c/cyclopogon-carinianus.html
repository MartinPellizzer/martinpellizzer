Cyclopogon carinianus

Cyclopogon carinianus, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag pattern in its leaf blades, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Cyclopogon carinianus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
