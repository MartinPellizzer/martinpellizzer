Chamaecrista complexa

Chamaecrista complexa, commonly known as the small leaf ticktreed.

It is a shrub native to the southeastern United States, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties. It has a creeping growth habit and produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds.
