Cheirodendron platyphyllum

Cheirodendron platyphyllum, commonly known as the flat-leaf cheirodendron, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that are typically arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
