Crassula stewartiae

Crassula stewartiae, commonly known as Stewart's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

Crassula stewartiae is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
