Cacalia stellaris

Cacalia stellaris, commonly known as starry cacalia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Cacalia stellaris thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as meadows and rocky slopes.
