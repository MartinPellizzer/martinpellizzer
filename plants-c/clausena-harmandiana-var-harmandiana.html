Clausena harmandiana var. harmandiana

Clausena harmandiana var. harmandiana, commonly known as Harmand's klotsch, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often with yellowish-white petals.

The fruit is a fleshy drupe, which can be yellow or orange when ripe, and contains a single seed.
