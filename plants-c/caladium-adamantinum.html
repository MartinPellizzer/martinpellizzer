Caladium adamantinum

Caladium adamantinum, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with bright red veins against a deep green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium adamantinum thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its foliage is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant, colorful appearance.
