Coreopsis lucida

Coreopsis lucida, commonly known as the shining coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with a dark central disk, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Coreopsis lucida thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance, ornamental plants.
