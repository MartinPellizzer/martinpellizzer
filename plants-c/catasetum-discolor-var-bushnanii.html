Catasetum discolor var. bushnanii

Catasetum discolor var. bushnanii, commonly known as the Bushnan's Orchid, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Central and South America.

This variant is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a specialized lip that facilitates pollination by specific wasps. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with a contrasting coloration, often displaying a mix of white, yellow, and green tones. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Catasetum discolor var. bushnanii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its exotic beauty.
