Cola seretii

Cola seretii, commonly known as the African cola tree, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces ellipsoidal fruits that contain hard, brown seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used traditionally in various cultural and medicinal practices.

Cola seretii is also valued for its role in local ecosystems and as a source of natural compounds.
