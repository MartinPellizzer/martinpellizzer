Carpotroche crispidentata

Carpotroche crispidentata, commonly known as the African sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or inland regions with well-drained soil.
