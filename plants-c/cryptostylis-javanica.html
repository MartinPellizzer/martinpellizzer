Cryptostylis javanica

Cryptostylis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes, often with a faintly fragrant scent. Cryptostylis javanica thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its unique floral structure and ecological significance.
