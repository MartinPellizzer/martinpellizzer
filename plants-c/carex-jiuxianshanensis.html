Carex jiuxianshanensis

Carex jiuxianshanensis, commonly known as the Jiuxianshan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Jiuxianshan area of Hubei Province. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream sides. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are characteristic of the sedge family.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms in its native ecosystem.
