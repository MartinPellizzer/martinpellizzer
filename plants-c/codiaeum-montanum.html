Codiaeum montanum

Codiaeum montanum, commonly known as the mountain croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its striking foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Codiaeum montanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.
