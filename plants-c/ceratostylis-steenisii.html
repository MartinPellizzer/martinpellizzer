Ceratostylis steenisii

Ceratostylis steenisii, commonly known as the Stene's spike orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short, erect stem and a single, narrow, linear leaf. The plant produces a spike of small, greenish-white flowers with prominent, spurred petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
