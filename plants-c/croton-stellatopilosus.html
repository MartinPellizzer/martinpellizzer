Croton stellatopilosus

Croton stellatopilosus, commonly known as the starry croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with a distinctive star-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

Due to its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties, it is a subject of botanical and pharmacological study.
