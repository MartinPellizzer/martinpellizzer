Cochlearia Yunnanensis

Cochlearia yunnanensis is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, known for its distinctive cruciform flowers. This species is characterized by its delicate, curling leaves and small, pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its striking appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
