Cirsium ashinokuraense

Cirsium ashinokuraense, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the daisy family.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It has a robust root system that helps it thrive in wet conditions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
