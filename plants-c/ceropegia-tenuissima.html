Ceropegia tenuissima

Ceropegia tenuissima, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia tenuissima is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
