Coffea lulandoensis

Coffea lulandoensis, commonly known as the Lulando coffee.

It is a rare and lesser-known species of coffee native to the highlands of Tanzania. This plant is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and produces red, fleshy berries containing two seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and low yields, it is not widely cultivated or commercially available.
