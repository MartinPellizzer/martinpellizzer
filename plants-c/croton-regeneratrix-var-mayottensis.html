Croton regeneratrix var. mayottensis

Croton regeneratrix var. mayottensis, commonly known as the Mayotte croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Comoros Islands.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and striking, colorful inflorescences that range from yellow to red. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
