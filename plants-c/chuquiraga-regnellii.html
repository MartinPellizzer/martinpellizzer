Chuquiraga regnellii

Chuquiraga regnellii, commonly known as the Andean bur plant, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and spiny, spherical fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are alternate and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Chuquiraga regnellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine by local communities.
