Ceratocephalus karwinskianus

Ceratocephalus karwinskianus, commonly known as the Karwin's burr bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, spherical seed heads. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by the development of its unique, woody, thorn-covered fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy soils.
