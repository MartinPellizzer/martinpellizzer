Calamagrostis producta

Calamagrostis producta, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, silver-green plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream edges. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that becomes more silvery as the plant matures.

Calamagrostis producta is valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
