Cyathea nilgirensis var. lobata

Cyathea nilgirensis var. lobata, commonly known as the Nilgiri tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The fronds are large, pinnately compound, and often have a lobed or wavy appearance, giving the varietal name *lobata*. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

This plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
