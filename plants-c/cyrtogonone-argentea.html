Cyrtogonone argentea

Cyrtogonone argentea, commonly known as the silverleaf cyrtogonone, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
