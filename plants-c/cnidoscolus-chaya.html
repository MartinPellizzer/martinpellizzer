Cnidoscolus chaya

Cnidoscolus chaya, commonly known as chaya or tree spinach, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are edible. The plant produces small green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are rich in nutrients, including vitamins A and C, calcium, and iron, making it a valuable food source.

However, it contains toxic compounds that require proper preparation before consumption.
