Clowesia glaucoglossa

Clowesia glaucoglossa, commonly known as the silver-leaved daisy, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features basal leaves that are silver-green in color, covered in fine hairs, and stems that are often hairy as well. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a daisy-like pattern, with a yellow central disk. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Clowesia glaucoglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
