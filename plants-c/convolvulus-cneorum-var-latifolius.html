Convolvulus cneorum var. latifolius

Convolvulus cneorum var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped, pale yellow to orange flowers. Native to southern Europe, this species thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

It is often considered a weed in agricultural and garden settings due to its invasive tendencies.
