Cephalaria uralensis var. multifida

Cephalaria uralensis var. multifida, commonly known as the Ural cephalaria, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaf structures. The plant features deeply lobed, fern-like leaves that are often subdivided into multiple parts, giving it a delicate, lace-like appearance. Its flowering period typically occurs in late summer, producing clusters of small, yellowish or pale green flowers.

This species is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
