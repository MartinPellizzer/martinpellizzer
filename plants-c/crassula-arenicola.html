Crassula arenicola

Crassula arenicola, commonly known as the sand crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and full sunlight, making it well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer months.

Due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
