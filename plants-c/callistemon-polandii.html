Callistemon polandii

Callistemon polandii, commonly known as the Pollock's bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Callistemon polandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
