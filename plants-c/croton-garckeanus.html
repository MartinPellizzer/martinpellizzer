Croton garckeanus

Croton garckeanus, commonly known as the red-veined croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with deep green leaves featuring prominent red veins. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant appearance.

Croton garckeanus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
