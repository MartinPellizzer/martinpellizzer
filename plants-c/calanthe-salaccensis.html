Calanthe salaccensis

Calanthe salaccensis, commonly known as the Salakense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value.

Calanthe salaccensis is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
