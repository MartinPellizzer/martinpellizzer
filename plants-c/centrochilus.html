Centrochilus

Centrochilus, commonly known as the "star orchid," is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

They are characterized by their intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. Centrochilus species typically grow as epiphytes or lithophytes, attaching themselves to trees or rocks in their natural habitats.
