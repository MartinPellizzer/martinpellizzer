Camellia longgangensis

Camellia longgangensis, commonly known as the Longgang camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in winter or early spring. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.

The camellia is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a versatile addition to horticultural landscapes.
