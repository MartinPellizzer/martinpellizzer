Coelogyne angustipetala

Coelogyne angustipetala, commonly known as the narrow-petalled coelogyne, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink with contrasting yellow or orange markings. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, which stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
