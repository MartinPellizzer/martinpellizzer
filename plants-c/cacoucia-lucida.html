Cacoucia lucida

Cacoucia lucida, commonly known as the shining cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, similar to other members of the legume family. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.

Cacoucia lucida is often cultivated for its nutritional value and is used in various traditional cuisines.
