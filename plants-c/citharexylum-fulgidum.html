Citharexylum fulgidum

Citharexylum fulgidum, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery underside, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.

The tree is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
