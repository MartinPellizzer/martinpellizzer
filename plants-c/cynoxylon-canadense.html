Cynoxylon canadense

Cynoxylon canadense, commonly known as the Canadian ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and woody stems that are highly resistant to decay. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are leathery and oval-shaped, providing a textured appearance.

Cynoxylon canadense is often found in moist, acidic soils along stream banks and in forested areas.
