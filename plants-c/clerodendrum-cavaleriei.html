Clerodendrum cavaleriei

Clerodendrum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and emits a mild, aromatic scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.
