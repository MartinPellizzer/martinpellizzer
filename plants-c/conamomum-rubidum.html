Conamomum rubidum

Conamomum rubidum, commonly known as the crimson spice tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and striking red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are rich in essential oils and have a spicy, aromatic flavor. Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Conamomum rubidum thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
