Coryphopteris viscosa var. viscosa

Coryphopteris viscosa var. viscosa, commonly known as the viscid hederaceae, is a fern species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Hederaceae and is characterized by its leathery, viscid leaves that are often covered in a sticky substance.
