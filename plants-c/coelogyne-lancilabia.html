Coelogyne lancilabia

Coelogyne lancilabia, commonly known as the long-labia orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender petals and a distinctive labellum that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually fragrant and bloom in shades of yellow and orange, adding to its ornamental value.

Coelogyne lancilabia is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
