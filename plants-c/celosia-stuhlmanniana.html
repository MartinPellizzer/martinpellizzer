Celosia stuhlmanniana

Celosia stuhlmanniana, commonly known as the flame flower or crown of thorns, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for gardens and containers.

Celosia stuhlmanniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a seasonal or perennial plant depending on the climate.
