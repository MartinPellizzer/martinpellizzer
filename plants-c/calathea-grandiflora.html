Calathea grandiflora

Calathea grandiflora, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns of white and silver. The plant produces small, pale pink flowers, though it is primarily cultivated for its foliage rather than its blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea grandiflora is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
