Columnea florida

Columnea florida, commonly known as the flame flower, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has opposite, ovate, and glossy dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to flourish.

Columnea florida is often cultivated as a hanging plant due to its trailing growth habit.
