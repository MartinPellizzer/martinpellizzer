Cleistesiopsis divaricata f. leucantha

Cleistesiopsis divaricata f. leucantha, commonly known as the white-flowered cleistesiopsis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
