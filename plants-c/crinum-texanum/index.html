Crinum Texanum

Texas crinum, scientifically known as Crinum texanum, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the southern United States, particularly Texas. This perennial bulbous plant is celebrated for its showy, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well adapted to the hot, dry climate of its natural habitat. The Texas crinum is also known for its robust growth and ability to withstand drought conditions. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in the southern regions.
