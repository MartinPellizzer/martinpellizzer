Cryptandra orbicularis

Cryptandra orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaved cryptandra, is a small shrub native to southwestern Australia.

It is characterized by its orbicular, or nearly round, leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color, which bloom in spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Cryptandra orbicularis is often found in heathlands and is an important component of the local flora.
