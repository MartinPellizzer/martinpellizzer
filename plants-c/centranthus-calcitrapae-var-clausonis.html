Centranthus calcitrapae var. clausonis

Centranthus calcitrapae var. clausonis, commonly known as the Clauson's red centranthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright red, tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in rocky or limestone soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor, well-drained conditions.
