Cineraria hybrida

Cineraria hybrida, commonly known as the blueweed or rock carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soil. Cineraria hybrida has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy leaves that help it retain moisture.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
