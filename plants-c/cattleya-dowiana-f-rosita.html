Cattleya dowiana f. rosita

Cattleya dowiana f. rosita, commonly known as the Rosita Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in high-altitude, humid environments. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with showy, often bicolor blooms that range from white to pink and orange. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
