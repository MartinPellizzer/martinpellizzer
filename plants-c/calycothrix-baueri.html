Calycothrix baueri

Calycothrix baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's rock cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white or yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Calycothrix baueri is native to arid and semi-arid regions, where it thrives in rocky or gravelly soils.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping.
