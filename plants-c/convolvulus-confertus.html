Convolvulus confertus

Convolvulus confertus, commonly known as the dense convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a trailing or ascending annual herb that produces small, funnel-shaped flowers in shades of purple, pink, or white. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Its rapid growth makes it a common weed in agricultural areas.
