Cypripedium × goweri

Cypripedium × goweri, commonly known as the Gower's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch and a prominent spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodland habitats and has lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a contrasting yellow or orange lip.

Cypripedium × goweri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
