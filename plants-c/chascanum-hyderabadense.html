Chascanum hyderabadense

Chascanum hyderabadense, commonly known as the Hyderabad chascanum, is a flowering plant native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, herbaceous stems.

Chascanum hyderabadense is often found in regions with moderate to high sunlight exposure.
