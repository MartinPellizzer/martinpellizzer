Capsella bursa-pastoris subsp. gracilis

Capsella bursa-pastoris subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as slender shepherd's purse, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae. It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas.

The plant features small, triangular seed pods that resemble a purse, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in racemes, and it has alternate, pinnately lobed leaves.

The subspecies gracilis is distinguished by its more slender and delicate appearance compared to the typical form of C. bursa-pastoris.
