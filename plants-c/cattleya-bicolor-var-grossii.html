Cattleya bicolor var. grossii

Cattleya bicolor var. grossii, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that help in photosynthesis.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance.
