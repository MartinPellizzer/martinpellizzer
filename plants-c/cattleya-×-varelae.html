Cattleya × varelae

Cattleya × varelae, commonly known as the Varela's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant produces pendulous blooms on tall, upright stems, often with a sweet, spicy fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
