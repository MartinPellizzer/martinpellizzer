Consolea nashii

Consolea nashii, commonly known as the Nash's cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its tall, columnar stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces large, showy yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Consolea nashii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
