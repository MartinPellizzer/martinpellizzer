Chenopodium × brunense

Chenopodium × brunense, commonly known as hybrid goosefoot, is a perennial plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically in the axils of the leaves. Its seeds are small and numerous, often dispersed by wind.

Chenopodium × brunense is typically found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
