Clematis gouriana subsp. lishanensis

Clematis gouriana subsp.

lishanensis, commonly known as the Lishan clematis, is a perennial vine native to China. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, typically blue or purple flowers.

The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fluffy fruits that disperse its seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
