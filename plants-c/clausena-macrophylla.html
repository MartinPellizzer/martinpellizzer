Clausena macrophylla

Clausena macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leafed Clausena, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elliptical, red or orange fruits containing several seeds. Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and potential medicinal uses.

The tree thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
