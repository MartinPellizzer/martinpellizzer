Chamaecrista tenuicaulis

Chamaecrista tenuicaulis, commonly known as slender crown vetch, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in open woodlands and disturbed soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, pinnate leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in small, pod-like fruits that split open when mature.

Chamaecrista tenuicaulis is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
