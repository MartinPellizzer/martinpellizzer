Centaurea hyalolepis var. brevispina

Centaurea hyalolepis var. brevispina, commonly known as the narrow-spined centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered with fine, bristly hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.
