Cheirostylis cristata

Cheirostylis cristata, commonly known as the crested cheirostylis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive crest-like structure on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cheirostylis cristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
