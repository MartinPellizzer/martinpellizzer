Crybe rosea

Crybe rosea, commonly known as the pink-flowered crybe, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking pink or rose-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Crybe rosea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
