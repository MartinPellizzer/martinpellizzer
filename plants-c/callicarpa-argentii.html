Callicarpa argentii

Callicarpa argentii, commonly known as silver leaf beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that appear in late spring. The plant produces vibrant purple berries in the fall, which are a food source for birds and other wildlife. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Callicarpa argentii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy maintenance in gardens.
