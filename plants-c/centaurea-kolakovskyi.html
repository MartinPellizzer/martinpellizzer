Centaurea kolakovskyi

Centaurea kolakovskyi, commonly known as the Kolakovsky's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Centaurea kolakovskyi is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
