Cathcartia chelidoniifolia

Cathcartia chelidoniifolia, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its bell-shaped, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
