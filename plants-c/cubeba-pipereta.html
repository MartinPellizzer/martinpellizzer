Cubeba pipereta

Cubeba pipereta, commonly known as cubeb, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and aromatic, woody stems. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that contain the essential oil responsible for its distinctive spicy flavor. Cubeb is often cultivated for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and pharmacology.
