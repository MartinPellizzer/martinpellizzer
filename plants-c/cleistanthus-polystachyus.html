Cleistanthus polystachyus

Cleistanthus polystachyus, commonly known as the Indian flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cleistanthus polystachyus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
