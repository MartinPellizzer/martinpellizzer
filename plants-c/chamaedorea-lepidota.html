Chamaedorea lepidota

Chamaedorea lepidota, commonly known as the silver leaf palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features a slender, unbranched stem with a crown of feathery, glossy green leaves that have a distinctive silvery underside. The plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its minimal water requirements. Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in both residential and commercial spaces.

Chamaedorea lepidota is also valued for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
