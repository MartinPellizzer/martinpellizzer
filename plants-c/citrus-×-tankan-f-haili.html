Citrus × tankan f. haili

Citrus × tankan f. haili, commonly known as the Hainan lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus plant native to China, specifically the Hainan Island. The plant is characterized by its small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are typically harvested when fully ripe. The leaves are dark green and glossy, and the tree has a compact growth habit.

It is valued for its aromatic fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
