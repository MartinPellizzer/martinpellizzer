Costus loangensis

Costus loangensis, commonly known as the Longan Costus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes ginger and turmeric. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive spurred shape.
