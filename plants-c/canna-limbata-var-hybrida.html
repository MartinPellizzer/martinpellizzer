Canna limbata var. hybrida

Canna limbata var. hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid cannonball plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. The plant produces vibrant, colorful inflorescences that range from red to orange to yellow, often resembling cannonballs in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Canna limbata var. hybrida is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add a tropical feel to landscapes.
