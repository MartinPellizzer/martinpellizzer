Cypripedium × ledouxiae

Cypripedium × ledouxiae, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a rare and elegant flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pouch-shaped lip, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded forest environments, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its flowers, which can be pink, white, or pale yellow, are highly prized for their beauty and rarity in horticultural circles.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is often protected in conservation programs.
