Cynanchum montevidense

Cynanchum montevidense, commonly known as the Montevidense milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows in grasslands and shrublands, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a hairy surface, and it produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
