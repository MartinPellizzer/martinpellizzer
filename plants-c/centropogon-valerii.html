Centropogon valerii

Centropogon valerii, commonly known as the Valerian centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. Centropogon valerii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
