Croton carrii

Croton carrii, commonly known as the Carrii croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red, depending on the season and environmental conditions. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits.

Croton carrii is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
