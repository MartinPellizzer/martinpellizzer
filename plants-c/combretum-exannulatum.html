Combretum exannulatum

Combretum exannulatum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Combretum exannulatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
