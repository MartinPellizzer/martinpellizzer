Cytisus Canariensis Var. Ramosissimus

Cytisus canariensis var. ramosissimus is a strikingly ornamental shrub native to the Canary Islands. Known for its dense, sprawling growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers, it adds a bold splash of color to gardens and landscapes. This variant is particularly noted for its highly branched structure, which gives it a lush, bushy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates. Despite its beauty, it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive in non-native environments.
