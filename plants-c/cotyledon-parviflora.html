Cotyledon parviflora

Cotyledon parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, pink or purple flowers during the summer months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and compact size.

Cotyledon parviflora prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure for optimal growth.
