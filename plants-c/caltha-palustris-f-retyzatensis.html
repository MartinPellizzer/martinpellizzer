Caltha palustris f. retyzatensis

Caltha palustris f. retyzatensis, commonly known as the Retzyatenskaya marsh marigold, is a botanical variant of the marsh marigold.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions in Eastern Europe. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, typically appearing in clusters.

It thrives in moist, boggy environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
