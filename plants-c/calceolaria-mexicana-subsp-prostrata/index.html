Calceolaria Mexicana Subsp. Prostrata

Calceolaria mexicana subsp. prostrata is a unique and lesser-known member of the Calceolariaceae family. Native to the arid regions of Mexico, this plant thrives in harsh, rocky environments where few others can survive. Its prostrate growth habit allows it to hug the ground, reducing water loss and protecting it from strong winds. The plant produces striking, pouch-like flowers that come in a range of vibrant colors, making it a standout in its ecosystem. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in botanical studies.
