Cheilanthes madagascariensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its small, leathery, evergreen leaves. The plant typically grows on rocky outcrops and in dry, shaded environments. Its fronds are usually pinnately divided, with a dense arrangement of tiny, scale-like structures on the underside.
