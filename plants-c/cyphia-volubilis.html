Cyphia volubilis

Cyphia volubilis, commonly known as the climbing cyphia, is a vine-like plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. Its flowers give way to spherical, red berries that are edible and attract various wildlife.

Cyphia volubilis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
