Coelogyne angustiloba

Coelogyne angustiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments within forest understories. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Coelogyne angustiloba is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in its native habitats.
