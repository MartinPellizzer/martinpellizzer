Ceropegia cummingii

Ceropegia cummingii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia cummingii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
