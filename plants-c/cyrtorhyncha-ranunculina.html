Cyrtorhyncha ranunculina

Cyrtorhyncha ranunculina, commonly known as the slender buttercup, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often yellow or white flowers. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. It produces small, aggregate fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cyrtorhyncha ranunculina thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
