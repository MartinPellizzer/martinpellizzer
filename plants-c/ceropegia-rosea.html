Ceropegia rosea

Ceropegia rosea, commonly known as the angel's wings, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia rosea is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
