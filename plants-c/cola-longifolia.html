Cola longifolia

Cola longifolia, commonly known as African cola or wild cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Sterculiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cola longifolia is related to the cola genus, which includes species used for their stimulant-containing seeds.
