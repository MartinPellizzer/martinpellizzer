Crotalaria natalitia var. rutshuruensis

Crotalaria natalitia var. rutshuruensis, commonly known as the Rutshuru crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate arrangements, and its flowers are typically yellow with a distinctive spurred petal. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and potential ecological importance in soil enrichment.
