Cerastium stellatum

Cerastium stellatum, commonly known as starwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, which includes other flowering plants such as carnations and pinks. The plant features small, white or pinkish star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and the stems are often hairy.
