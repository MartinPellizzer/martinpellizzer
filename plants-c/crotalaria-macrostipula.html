Crotalaria macrostipula

Crotalaria macrostipula, commonly known as the large-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Its seeds are encased in a hard, woody pod, and the plant can grow as an annual or perennial depending on the climate.

Crotalaria macrostipula is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value but can also be invasive in certain environments.
