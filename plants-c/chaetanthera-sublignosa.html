Chaetanthera sublignosa

Chaetanthera sublignosa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in the summer. It produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature.
