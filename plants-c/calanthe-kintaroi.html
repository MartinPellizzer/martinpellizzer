Calanthe kintaroi

Calanthe kintaroi, commonly known as the Kintaroi orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Calanthe kintaroi thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
