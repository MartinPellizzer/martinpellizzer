Cerastium brachypetalum var. eglandulosum

Cerastium brachypetalum var. eglandulosum, commonly known as the glandular mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.
