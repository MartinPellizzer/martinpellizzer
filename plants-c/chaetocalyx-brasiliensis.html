Chaetocalyx brasiliensis

Chaetocalyx brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid or the red orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a distinctive red coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and succulent, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects.
