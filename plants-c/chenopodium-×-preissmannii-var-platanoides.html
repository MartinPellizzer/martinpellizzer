Chenopodium × preissmannii var. platanoides

Chenopodium × preissmannii var. platanoides, commonly known as the giant goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

This variant is notable for its robust growth and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
