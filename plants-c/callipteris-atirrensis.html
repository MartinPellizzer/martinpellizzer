Callipteris atirrensis

Callipteris atirrensis, commonly known as the Florida silver feather palm, is a striking tropical plant native to the southeastern United States.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that is covered in a fibrous, silver-gray sheath. The plant produces feather-like, compound leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern around the trunk. Its foliage is evergreen, providing a lush, ornamental appearance throughout the year.

Callipteris atirrensis is well adapted to coastal and wetland environments, making it a resilient and visually appealing species for landscaping.
