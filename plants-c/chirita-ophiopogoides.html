Chirita ophiopogoides

Chirita ophiopogoides, commonly known as the silverleaf begonia, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, silver-tomentose leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.
