Cinnamomum mohanense

Cinnamomum mohanense, commonly known as Mohan cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to the Eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
