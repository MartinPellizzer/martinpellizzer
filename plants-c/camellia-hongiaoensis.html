Camellia hongiaoensis

Camellia hongiaoensis, commonly known as Hongiao camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in late autumn to early winter. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a prominent central stamen, and are highly valued for ornamental purposes.

Camellia hongiaoensis is often cultivated in gardens and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
