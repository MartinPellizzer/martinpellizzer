Cordia glabrata var. genuina

Cordia glabrata var. genuina, commonly known as the smooth cordia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features simple, opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by fleshy, red or orange berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and colorful fruits.
