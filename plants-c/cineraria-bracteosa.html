Cineraria bracteosa

Cineraria bracteosa, commonly known as the silverleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in dense, fine hairs. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in Mediterranean climates.
