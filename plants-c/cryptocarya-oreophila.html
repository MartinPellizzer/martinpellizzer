Cryptocarya oreophila

Cryptocarya oreophila, commonly known as the mountain cryptocarya, is a flowering plant species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions.
