Callopsis volkensii

Callopsis volkensii, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a deciduous shrub native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for various bird species. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a dense, compact growth habit.
