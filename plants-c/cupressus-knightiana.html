Cupressus knightiana

Cupressus knightiana, commonly known as the Sierra madre cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, columnar form and scale-like leaves that are dark green and densely packed. The tree produces small, spherical cones that mature to a reddish-brown color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions with a temperate climate.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
