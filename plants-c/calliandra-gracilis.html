Calliandra gracilis

Calliandra gracilis, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its feathery, pink to red bracts that surround small, yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves composed of numerous small, elliptical leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Calliandra gracilis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, enhancing soil fertility.
