Carduus pteracanthus

Carduus pteracanthus, commonly known as the common thistle.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Carduus pteracanthus is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.
