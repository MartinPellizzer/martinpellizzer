Codiaeum hanburyanum

Codiaeum hanburyanum, commonly known as the Hanbury croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, with a velvety texture that adds to its visual appeal.
