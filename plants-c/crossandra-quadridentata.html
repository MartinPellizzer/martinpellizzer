Crossandra quadridentata

Crossandra quadridentata, commonly known as the four-toothed crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and southern Asia.

Crossandra quadridentata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
