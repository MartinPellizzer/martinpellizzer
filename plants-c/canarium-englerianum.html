Canarium englerianum

Canarium englerianum, commonly known as the Engler's canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its latex is used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural rubber.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
