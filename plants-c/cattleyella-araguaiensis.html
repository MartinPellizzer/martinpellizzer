Cattleyella araguaiensis

Cattleyella araguaiensis, commonly known as the Araguaiense orchid, is a rare and exquisite flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its botanical features include strap-like leaves and a pseudobulb structure that helps store water and nutrients.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly valued in horticultural circles.
