Cliffortia flabellifolia

Cliffortia flabellifolia, commonly known as the flabellifoliate starling's-tail, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cliffortia flabellifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
