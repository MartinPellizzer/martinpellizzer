Croton opponens

Croton opponens, commonly known as the warty croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, warty, and often colorful leaves that can range from green to red or purple. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Croton opponens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
