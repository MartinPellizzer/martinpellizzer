Chloranthus Fortunei Var. Koreanus

Chloranthus fortunei var. koreanus is a rare and distinctive flowering plant native to parts of East Asia. It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is known for its unique floral structure and medicinal properties. This variant of Chloranthus fortunei is particularly found in Korea and neighboring regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and emit a subtle fragrance, making it a prized specimen among botanists. Due to habitat loss, it is increasingly threatened, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
