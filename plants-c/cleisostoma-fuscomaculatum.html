Cleisostoma fuscomaculatum

Cleisostoma fuscomaculatum, commonly known as the spotted cleisostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with distinctive dark spots. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral morphology.

Cleisostoma fuscomaculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
