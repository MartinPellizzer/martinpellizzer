Cryptocoryne ovata

Cryptocoryne ovata, commonly known as the broadleaf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 10 inches in length. This plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water with moderate to high light conditions. It is often cultivated in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

Cryptocoryne ovata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquarists.
