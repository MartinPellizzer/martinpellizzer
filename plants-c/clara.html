Clara

Clara, commonly known as the Clara plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Clara is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
