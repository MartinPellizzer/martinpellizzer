Croton quisumbingianus

Croton quisumbingianus, commonly known as the Quezon croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows in lowland forests and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
