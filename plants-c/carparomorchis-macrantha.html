Carparomorchis macrantha

Carparomorchis macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a prominent lip and vibrant colors. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with rhizomes that allow it to form clumps over time. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, providing a lush backdrop to its ornamental flowers.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
