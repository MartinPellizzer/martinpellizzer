Calanthe ramosa

Calanthe ramosa, commonly known as the branched calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with prominent lip structures. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe ramosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for its elegant floral display.
