Collabium Aneityumense

Collabium aneityumense is a rare and intriguing plant native to the remote highlands of Aneityum. Known for its striking bioluminescent properties, it emits a soft blue glow during the night. This unique characteristic has made it a subject of scientific curiosity and local folklore. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires specific soil conditions to flourish. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and is seldom found in the wild.
