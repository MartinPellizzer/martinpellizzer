Citrus × sechen subsp. sjanchen

Citrus × sechen subsp.

sjanchen, commonly known as the Yunnan citrus or Yunnan kumquat, is a hybrid citrus species native to China. It is characterized by its small, orange-colored fruits that are typically harvested when they are still green and unripe.

The plant produces fragrant, white flowers and has glossy, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruits.

The subspecies is notable for its adaptability to various soil types and its resistance to certain citrus diseases.
