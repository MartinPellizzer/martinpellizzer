Cytisus austriacus var. kissii

Cytisus austriacus var. kissii, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a flowering shrub native to Central Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
