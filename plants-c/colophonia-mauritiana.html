Colophonia mauritiana

Colophonia mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius colophony plant, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic resinous secretions. The plant produces a sticky, gum-like substance called colophony, which is used in various industrial applications.
