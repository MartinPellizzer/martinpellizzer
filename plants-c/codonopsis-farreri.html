Codonopsis farreri

Codonopsis farreri, commonly known as the Chinese ginseng or "Dang Shen," is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its erect stems and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and partial shade. Its root system is highly valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

The leaves are lanceolate and the flowers are usually pink or white, blooming in summer.
