Cardiospermum corindum var. leptocarpum

Cardiospermum corindum var. leptocarpum, commonly known as the slender fruit heartseed, is a member of the葫芦科 (Cucurbitaceae) family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that mature to a red or orange color. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a distinctive seed structure.

This variant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
