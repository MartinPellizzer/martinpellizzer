Carduus tenuifolius

Carduus tenuifolius, commonly known as slenderleaf thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia, and it thrives in dry, open habitats such as fields and roadsides. The plant is characterized by its upright, spiny stems and narrow, gray-green leaves. Its composite flowers, which appear in late summer, consist of both ray and disk florets.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and is also a host plant for certain insect species.
