Calliandra mucugeana

Calliandra mucugeana, commonly known as the Brazilian peacock bean, is a leguminous shrub native to South America.

It features compound leaves with bright red or orange flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
