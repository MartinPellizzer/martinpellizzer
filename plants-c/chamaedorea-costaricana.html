Chamaedorea costaricana

Chamaedorea costaricana, commonly known as the Costa Rican bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of elongated, bright green leaves. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It has a compact growth habit and can reach heights of up to 6 feet in its natural habitat.

Its foliage provides a lush, tropical appearance, enhancing both indoor and outdoor spaces.
