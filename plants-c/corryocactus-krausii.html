Corryocactus krausii

Corryocactus krausii, commonly known as the Kraus's corryocactus, is a rare and unique cactus native to a very limited geographic area in South America.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems. Due to its restricted habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered a threatened species.
