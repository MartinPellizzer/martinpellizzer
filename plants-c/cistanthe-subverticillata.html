Cistanthe subverticillata

Cistanthe subverticillata, commonly known as the desert dandelion, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods that release seeds when disturbed.
