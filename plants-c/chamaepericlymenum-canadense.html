Chamaepericlymenum canadense

Chamaepericlymenum canadense, commonly known as Canadian white avens, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically features a mat-forming growth habit with hairy stems and small, rounded leaves. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its leaves and flowers are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported healing properties.
