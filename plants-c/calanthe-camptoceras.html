Calanthe camptoceras

Calanthe camptoceras, commonly known as the narrow-leaf calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Calanthe camptoceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
