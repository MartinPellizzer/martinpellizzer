Crotalaria diminuta

Crotalaria diminuta, commonly known as the dwarf crown flower, is a small annual herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow and purple flowers. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
