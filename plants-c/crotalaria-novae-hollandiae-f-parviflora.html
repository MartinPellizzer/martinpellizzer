Crotalaria novae-hollandiae f. parviflora

Crotalaria novae-hollandiae f. parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf yellow burr.

It is a flowering plant native to Australia, characterized by its small, yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and possesses nitrogen-fixing abilities through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria. Its small, pod-like fruits contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
