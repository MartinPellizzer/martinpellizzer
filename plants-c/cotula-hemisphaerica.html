Cotula hemisphaerica

Cotula hemisphaerica, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers and rosette-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun. It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its compact size and bright floral display.

Cotula hemisphaerica is also known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping.
