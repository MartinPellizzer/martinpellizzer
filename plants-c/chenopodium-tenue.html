Chenopodium tenue

Chenopodium tenue, commonly known as slender goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to arid and semi-arid regions.

It has succulent, prostrate or upright stems that often branch near the base. The leaves are usually alternate, lanceolate to ovate, and covered with fine, stellate hairs. The small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense, axillary inflorescences.

This plant is adapted to dry environments and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
