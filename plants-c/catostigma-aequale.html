Catostigma aequale

Catostigma aequale, commonly known as the small-flowered catostigma, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically borne in clusters, often in shades of pink or purple.
