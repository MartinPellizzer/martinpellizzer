Clematis tosaensis

Clematis tosaensis, commonly known as the Tosa clematis, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It is a deciduous vine that belongs to the Ranunculaceae family. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three heart-shaped leaflets, and it produces distinctive, elongated fruit capsules.

Clematis tosaensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
