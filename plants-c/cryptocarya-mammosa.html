Cryptocarya mammosa

Cryptocarya mammosa, commonly known as the broad-leafed cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
