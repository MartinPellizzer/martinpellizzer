Croton pierrei

Croton pierrei, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that often display vibrant colors ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
