Cistus piloselloides

Cistus piloselloides, commonly known as the silky rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to arid environments. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a silky appearance.

The plant is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
