Cheiranthus tenuifolius

Cheiranthus tenuifolius, commonly known as narrow-leafed wallflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits.
