Combretum latifolium var. ellipticum

Combretum latifolium var. ellipticum, commonly known as the broad-leaved flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
