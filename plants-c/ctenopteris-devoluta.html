Ctenopteris devoluta

Ctenopteris devoluta, commonly known as the curled fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that curl inward, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with a soft, velvety texture.

Ctenopteris devoluta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
