Clinostigma macrostachyum

Clinostigma macrostachyum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets, and its bark is typically smooth and reddish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clinostigma macrostachyum is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
