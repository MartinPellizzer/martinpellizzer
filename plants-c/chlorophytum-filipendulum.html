Chlorophytum filipendulum

Chlorophytum filipendulum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Chlorophytum filipendulum is also known for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular houseplant.
