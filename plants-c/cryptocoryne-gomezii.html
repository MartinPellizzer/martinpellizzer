Cryptocoryne gomezii

Cryptocoryne gomezii, commonly known as the Gomez's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, elongated leaves that often display a metallic sheen. The plant is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. It is popular among aquarium enthusiasts due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.

Cryptocoryne gomezii is also noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its tolerance to a range of water parameters.
