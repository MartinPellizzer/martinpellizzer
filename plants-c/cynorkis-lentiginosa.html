Cynorkis lentiginosa

Cynorkis lentiginosa, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive spotted pattern. The plant typically blooms in late summer, with violet-blue flowers that have a yellowish-green throat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cynorkis lentiginosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
