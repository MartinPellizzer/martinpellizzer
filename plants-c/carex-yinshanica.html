Carex yinshanica

Carex yinshanica, commonly known as Yinshan sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered flowers in the summer.

Carex yinshanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland habitats.
