Cystorchis aphylla

Cystorchis aphylla, commonly known as the leafless orchid, is a unique species within the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, with photosynthesis occurring through its specialized green stems. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale in color.

Its botanical features make it a rare and fascinating subject for botanical study.
