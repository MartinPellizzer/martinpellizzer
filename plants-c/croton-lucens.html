Croton lucens

Croton lucens, commonly known as the shining croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including yellow, orange, and red. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton lucens typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its leaves are palmately lobed, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits.
