Centaurea grisebachii subsp. confusa

Centaurea grisebachii subsp.

confusa, commonly known as the grey-centred knapweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive greyish-blue flowers that bloom in summer.
