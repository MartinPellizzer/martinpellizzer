Combretum excelsum

Combretum excelsum, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tall, deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters of red and yellow flowers. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
