Cyclamen aleppicum subsp. puniceum

Cyclamen aleppicum subsp.

puniceum, commonly known as the Aleppo cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink to red.

The flowers are typically borne on slender stems and bloom in early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
