Celmisia cordatifolia var. cordatifolia

Celmisia cordatifolia var. cordatifolia, commonly known as the heart-leaf celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves, which are often a deep green and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, daisy-like white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in forested areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
