Croton fragrantulus

Croton fragrantulus, commonly known as the fragrant croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton fragrantulus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf patterns and ability to add visual interest to indoor or outdoor spaces.
