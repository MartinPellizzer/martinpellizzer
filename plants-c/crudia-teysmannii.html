Crudia teysmannii

Crudia teysmannii, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food for both humans and wildlife.
