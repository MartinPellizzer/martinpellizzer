Cardaminum nasturtium

Cardaminum nasturtium, commonly known as the Indian cress, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are lobed and often covered in fine hairs. It is known for its peppery, edible leaves and seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
