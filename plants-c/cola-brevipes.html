Cola brevipes

Cola brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed cola, is a species of plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to western Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. It produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that develop into the characteristic cola nuts.

These nuts are used in the production of caffeine-containing beverages and have economic significance in the region.
