Cyminosma ankenda

Cyminosma ankenda, commonly known as the Ankenda thyme.

It is a small perennial shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense spikes, and typically range in color from white to pale pink.

Cyminosma ankenda is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
