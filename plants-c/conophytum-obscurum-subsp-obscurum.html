Conophytum obscurum subsp. obscurum

Conophytum obscurum subsp.

obscurum, commonly known as the dark conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge.
