Corallodendron coralloides

Corallodendron coralloides, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, brightly colored flowers clustered together. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The flowers, which bloom in spring, are typically pink or red, adding ornamental value to the plant.
