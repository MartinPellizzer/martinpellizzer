Cerastium banaticum f. nikolicii

Cerastium banaticum f. nikolicii, commonly known as the Nikolicii chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Southeast Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its prostrate habit and small, white flowers. The foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving the plant a soft, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and rocky slopes.

This horticultural form is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in naturalized gardens.
