Cattleya lawrenceana var. rosea-superba

Cattleya lawrenceana var. rosea-superba, commonly known as the rose-superba cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically display a striking combination of pink and white hues. The plant features broad, leathery leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.
