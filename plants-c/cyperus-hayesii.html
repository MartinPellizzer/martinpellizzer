Cyperus hayesii

Cyperus hayesii, commonly known as the Hayes' flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly found in wetland and marshy habitats. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in moist to aquatic environments and is often found in association with other wetland vegetation.

Cyperus hayesii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
