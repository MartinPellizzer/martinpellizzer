Cephalaria flava subsp. askiensis

Cephalaria flava subsp.

askiensis, commonly known as the yellow cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.
