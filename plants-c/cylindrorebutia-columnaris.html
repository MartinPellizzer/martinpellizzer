Cylindrorebutia columnaris

Cylindrorebutia columnaris, commonly known as the columnar cactus, is a species native to the deserts of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual element to its arid habitat. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and succulent collections.
