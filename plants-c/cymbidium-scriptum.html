Cymbidium scriptum

Cymbidium scriptum, commonly known as the script patterned orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features tall, upright pseudobulbs with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are striking, with a unique pattern of dark markings resembling script, giving it its common name.

Cymbidium scriptum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
