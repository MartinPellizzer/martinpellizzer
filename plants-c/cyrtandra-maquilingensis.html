Cyrtandra maquilingensis

Cyrtandra maquilingensis, commonly known as the Maquiling sand orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Luzon. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers with vibrant colors. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
