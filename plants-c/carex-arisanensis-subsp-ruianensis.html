Carex arisanensis subsp. ruianensis

Carex arisanensis subsp.

ruianensis, commonly known as the Ruian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern China. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
