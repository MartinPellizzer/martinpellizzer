Crassula ramuliflora var. bolusii

Crassula ramuliflora var. bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
