Cirsium hupehense

Cirsium hupehense, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and stream banks. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in various soil types.

Cirsium hupehense is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
