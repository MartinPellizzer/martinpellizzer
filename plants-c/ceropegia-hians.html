Ceropegia hians

Ceropegia hians, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its trailing vines and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia hians is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
