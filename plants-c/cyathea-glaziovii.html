Cyathea glaziovii

Cyathea glaziovii, commonly known as Glaziov's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, meaning they have two levels of division, giving the plant a lush, feathery appearance.

Cyathea glaziovii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
