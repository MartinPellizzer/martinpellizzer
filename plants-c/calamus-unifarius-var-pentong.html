Calamus unifarius var. pentong

Calamus unifarius var. pentong, commonly known as the pentong reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height. Its leaves are long and narrow, often used in traditional crafts and as a natural material for various purposes.

The inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.
