Cynorkis flabellifera

Cynorkis flabellifera, commonly known as the fan-leaved orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Cynorkis flabellifera thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
