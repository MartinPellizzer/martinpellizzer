Ceropegia sphenanantha

Ceropegia sphenanantha, commonly known as the star-shaped ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, star-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
