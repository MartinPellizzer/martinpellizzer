Chillania

Chillania, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania and Kenya.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-draining soil. Chillania is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and ease of care.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities in both gardens and indoor settings.
