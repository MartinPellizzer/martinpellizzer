Codiaeum variegatum f. cornutum

Codiaeum variegatum f. cornutum, commonly known as the flame codiaeum, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The foliage is often adorned with intricate patterns and variegation, making it highly sought after for indoor and outdoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for those looking to add visual interest to their spaces.
