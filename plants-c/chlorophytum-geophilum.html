Chlorophytum geophilum

Chlorophytum geophilum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually green with white or yellow variegation. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Chlorophytum geophilum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to improved indoor air quality.
