Cassia didymobotrya

Cassia didymobotrya, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its vibrant floral display.
