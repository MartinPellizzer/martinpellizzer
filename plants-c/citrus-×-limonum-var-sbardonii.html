Citrus × limonum var. sbardonii

Citrus × limonum var. sbardonii, commonly known as the Sicilian lemon.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its small, yellow-orange fruits with a thick, aromatic rind. The leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, typical of the citrus family. It thrives in warm, subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is valued for its high citric acid content and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications.
