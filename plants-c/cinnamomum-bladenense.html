Cinnamomum bladenense

Cinnamomum bladenense, commonly known as Bladen's cinnamon, is a species of evergreen tree native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in lowland rainforests and thrives in humid, tropical climates. Its leaves are elliptical with a glossy surface, and the bark is reddish-brown and fragrant.

The tree is valued for its essential oils, which are used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
