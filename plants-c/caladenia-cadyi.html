Caladenia cadyi

Caladenia cadyi, commonly known as the Cady's Spider Orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance. The plant typically features a single, erect stem with a single flower that has a distinctive, intricate pattern on its dorsal sepal and petal. Caladenia cadyi thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heaths and woodlands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and vulnerable species.
