Cynoglossum drummondii

Cynoglossum drummondii, commonly known as Drummond's forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet petals and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cynoglossum drummondii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
