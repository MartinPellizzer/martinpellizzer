Calamus nambariensis subsp. nambariensis

Calamus nambariensis subsp.

nambariensis, commonly known as the Nambari calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae. It is native to the tropical regions of Australia, particularly Queensland.

The plant is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and large, arrow-shaped leaves. It produces inflorescences that consist of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

This subspecies is often found in wetland and swampy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
