Calea ramosissima

Calea ramosissima, commonly known as the sacred candle plant, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, candle-like inflorescence. The plant features hairy, branched stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Calea ramosissima is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.
