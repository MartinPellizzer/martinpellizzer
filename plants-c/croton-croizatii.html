Croton croizatii

Croton croizatii, commonly known as the Zulu croton, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Croton croizatii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
