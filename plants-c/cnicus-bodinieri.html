Cnicus bodinieri

Cnicus bodinieri, commonly known as the Bodinier's thistle, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. Its flowers bloom in the summer, producing a showy display that attracts pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
