Cogswellia foeniculacea

Cogswellia foeniculacea, commonly known as the yellow sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

Its leaves are usually covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss in its dry environment.

Cogswellia foeniculacea is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
