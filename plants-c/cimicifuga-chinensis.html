Cimicifuga chinensis

Cimicifuga chinensis, commonly known as Chinese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are compound, with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, greenish flowers in clusters.

Cimicifuga chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
