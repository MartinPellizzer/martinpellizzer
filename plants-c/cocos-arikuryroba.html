Cocos arikuryroba

Cocos arikuryroba, commonly known as the Brazilian coconut, is a rare and unique species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small, palm-like tree that produces a distinctive fruit with a thick, fibrous husk and a sweet, creamy pulp.
