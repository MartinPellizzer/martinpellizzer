Chamaecrista nictitans subsp. disadena

Chamaecrista nictitans subsp.

disadena, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
