Curio × kleiniiformis

Curio × kleiniiformis, commonly known as the Natal hibiscus, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species, Hibiscus rosa-sinensis and Hibiscus tiliaceus.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its showy, vibrant flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Curio × kleiniiformis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to bloom throughout the year.
