Ctenodon martii

Ctenodon martii, commonly known as the Mexican hairless dog, is a unique plant species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hairless, and often wrinkled appearance. The plant has a short, erect stem with small, clustered leaves that are usually green in color. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Ctenodon martii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine by indigenous communities.
