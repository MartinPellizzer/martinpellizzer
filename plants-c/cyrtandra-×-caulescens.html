Cyrtandra × caulescens

Cyrtandra × caulescens, commonly known as the hybrid cyrtandra, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant is a hybrid between Cyrtandra pedata and Cyrtandra frondosa, resulting in unique morphological traits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
