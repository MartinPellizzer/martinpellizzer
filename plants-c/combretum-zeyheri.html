Combretum zeyheri

Combretum zeyheri, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape and have a slightly pubescent surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

The tree is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
