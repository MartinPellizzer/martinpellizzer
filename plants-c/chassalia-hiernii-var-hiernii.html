Chassalia hiernii var. hiernii

Chassalia hiernii var. hiernii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Chassalia hiernii var. hiernii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
