Cirsium Taliense

Cirsium taliense is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the thistle family, known for its spiny leaves and showy purple flowers. This species is often found in rocky and well-drained soils at higher elevations. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important subject for botanical study.
