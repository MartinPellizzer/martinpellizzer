Centrosema herteri

Centrosema herteri, commonly known as the yellow cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its twining habit and is often found in disturbed habitats. It has a deep root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Its seeds are edible and used in various culinary traditions.
