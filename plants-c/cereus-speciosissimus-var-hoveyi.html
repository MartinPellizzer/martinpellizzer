Cereus speciosissimus var. hoveyi

Cereus speciosissimus var. hoveyi, commonly known as the Hove's night bloomer, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces large, white flowers that open at night, emitting a sweet fragrance. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its striking appearance and unique blooming habit, it is a popular choice for collectors and desert gardeners.
