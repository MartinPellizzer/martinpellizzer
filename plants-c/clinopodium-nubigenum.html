Clinopodium nubigenum

Clinopodium nubigenum, commonly known as cloud clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or purple flowers. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, globular inflorescences, and it blooms during the late spring to early summer.

Clinopodium nubigenum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
