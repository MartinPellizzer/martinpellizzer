Clitoria flexuosa var. flexuosa

Clitoria flexuosa var. flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features delicate, butterfly-like purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for gardens and traditional herbal uses.
