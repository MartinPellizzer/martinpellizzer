Clutia abyssinica var. calvescens

Clutia abyssinica var. calvescens, commonly known as the African clutia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
