Campylocentrum mattogrossense

Campylocentrum mattogrossense, commonly known as the Brazilian star orchid, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest of Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, yellowish-white blooms during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
