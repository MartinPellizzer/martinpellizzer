Crotalaria phillipsiae

Crotalaria phillipsiae, commonly known as the Australian burr medic, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that produces distinctive yellow flowers with prominent black markings. The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and is characterized by its unique seed pods that are covered in hooked spines. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in arid and semi-arid regions.

Crotalaria phillipsiae is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
