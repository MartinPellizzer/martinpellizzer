Centranthus × anselmii

Centranthus × anselmii, commonly known as the hybrid firecracker plant, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
