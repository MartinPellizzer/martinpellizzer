Cleistogenes Hancei F. Jeholensis

Cleistogenes hancei f. jeholensis is a rare and unique grass species native to northern China. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive floral structure. This subspecies is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Despite its small size, it plays an important role in its ecosystem.
