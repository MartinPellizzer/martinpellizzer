Caladenia pendens subsp. talbotii

Caladenia pendens subsp.

talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's Spider Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to south-western Australia. It features a slender, erect stem with a single, narrow, greenish-white flower that has a distinctive, elongated, spider-like appearance.

The plant produces a single, narrow leaf that is often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It blooms during the autumn season and is known for its unique morphology and specific habitat requirements.

This subspecies is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution.
