Cirsium × rigens subsp. acauliforme

Cirsium × rigens subsp.

acauliforme, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, spiny leaves.

The plant produces purple to pink flowers in late summer, which are clustered in a cylindrical shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
