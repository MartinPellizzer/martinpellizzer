Coronidium telfordii

Coronidium telfordii, commonly known as the Telford's crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, hairy stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a dense, crown-like cluster at the top of the stem.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate appearance.
