Cordia jamaicensis

Cordia jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican cordia, is a tropical tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the genus Cordia, which includes over 200 species of flowering plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds.

Cordia jamaicensis is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils.
