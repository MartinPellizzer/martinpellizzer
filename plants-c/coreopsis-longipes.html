Coreopsis longipes

Coreopsis longipes, commonly known as the longpod coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with narrow, elongated petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Coreopsis longipes thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for native gardens.
