Cineraria glabrata

Cineraria glabrata, commonly known as the smooth cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to violet flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often used in ornamental gardening.

Cineraria glabrata is valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
