Cotyledon marginatum

Cotyledon marginatum, commonly known as the Margined Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves with striking white or yellow margins that contrast with its green or reddish center. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Cotyledon marginatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns.
