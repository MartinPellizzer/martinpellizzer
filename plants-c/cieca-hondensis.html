Cieca hondensis

Cieca hondensis, commonly known as the Honduran ghost plant, is a rare and enigmatic species native to the tropical rainforests of Honduras.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent leaves that appear almost ethereal in appearance. The plant exhibits a specialized adaptation to its humid, shaded environment, with a shallow root system that allows it to absorb moisture efficiently. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically blooming during the rainy season.

Despite its rarity, Cieca hondensis plays a vital role in the ecological balance of its native habitat.
