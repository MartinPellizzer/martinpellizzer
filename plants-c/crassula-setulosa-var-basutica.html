Crassula setulosa var. basutica

Crassula setulosa var. basutica, commonly known as the Basutland crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
