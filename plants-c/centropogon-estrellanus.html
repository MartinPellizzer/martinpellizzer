Centropogon estrellanus

Centropogon estrellanus, commonly known as the Estrellan grass.

It is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly found in Cuba and the Isle of Youth. This species belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with numerous tiny flowers, typical of many grass species.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or wetland habitats.
