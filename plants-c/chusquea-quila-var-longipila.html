Chusquea quila var. longipila

Chusquea quila var. longipila, commonly known as the long-bracted dwarf bamboo, is a shrub-like plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit. The plant features slender, often glabrous culms that are typically less than 1 meter in height. Its leaves are narrow, linear, and often have a bluish-green hue, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
