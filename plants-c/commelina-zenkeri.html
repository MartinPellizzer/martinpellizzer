Commelina zenkeri

Commelina zenkeri, commonly known as the zigzag commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers with white markings. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, shady environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a zigzag pattern along the edges, giving it its common name.

Commelina zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowers.
