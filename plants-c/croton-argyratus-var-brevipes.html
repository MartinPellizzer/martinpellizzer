Croton argyratus var. brevipes

Croton argyratus var. brevipes, commonly known as the silver croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, velvety foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad, ovate, and covered in a dense layer of silver-white hairs, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in indoor and outdoor gardens due to its unique foliage.
