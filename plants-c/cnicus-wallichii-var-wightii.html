Cnicus wallichii var. wightii

Cnicus wallichii var. wightii, commonly known as the Wight's sow thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas and parts of southern Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a single terminal head and bloom in the summer months.

It is often found in open grasslands and among shrubs at high elevations.
