Croton laevigatus

Croton laevigatus, commonly known as the smooth croton, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Croton.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of yellow, red, and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in its native arid environments.

Croton laevigatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
