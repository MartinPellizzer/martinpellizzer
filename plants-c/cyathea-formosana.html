Cyathea formosana

Cyathea formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in its natural habitat. Its fronds are typically dark green and can have a leathery texture.

Cyathea formosana is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
