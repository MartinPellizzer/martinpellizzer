Cymbidium purpureisepalum

Cymbidium purpureisepalum, commonly known as the purple-sepal orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that bear clusters of striking, fragrant flowers. The petals and sepals are often a deep purple or violet, with a white or cream-colored lip. This orchid prefers cool, humid environments and is often grown in shaded areas.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
