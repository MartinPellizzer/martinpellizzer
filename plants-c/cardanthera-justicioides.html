Cardanthera justicioides

Cardanthera justicioides, commonly known as the scarlet cardinal flower, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its striking red-orange tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and wetlands.

The flowers attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees, making it a valuable addition to native gardens.
