Coelogyne longicaulis

Coelogyne longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with long, slender stems that can reach several meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive green throat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, arranged along the stem.

Coelogyne longicaulis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its fragrant blooms.
