Caradesia

Caradesia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Caradesia is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
