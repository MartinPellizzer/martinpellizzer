Conophytum anomalum

Conophytum anomalum, commonly known as the "Lizard's Tail" or "Cape Racer," is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, triangular leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
