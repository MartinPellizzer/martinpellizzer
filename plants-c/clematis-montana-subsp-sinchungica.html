Clematis montana subsp. sinchungica

Clematis montana subsp.

sinchungica, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia. It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to violet.

The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. It is known for its vigorous growth and is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value.

The subspecies is characterized by its distinct floral structure and adaptability to various climatic conditions.
