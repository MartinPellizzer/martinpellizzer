Clematis × francofurtensis f. paviflora

Clematis × francofurtensis f. paviflora, commonly known as the Paviflora Clematis, is a hybrid plant that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a deciduous vine with vigorous growth, featuring compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive flat, open structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to climb and cover trellises or fences effectively.
