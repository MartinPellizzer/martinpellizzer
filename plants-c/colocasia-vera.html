Colocasia vera

Colocasia vera, commonly known as the African elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture and are often variegated with white or cream patterns. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a houseplant or in gardens with the right conditions.
