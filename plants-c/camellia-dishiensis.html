Camellia dishiensis

Camellia dishiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan province.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can be pink, red, or white. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and dark green, while its flowers bloom in winter and early spring, making it a popular ornamental plant.
