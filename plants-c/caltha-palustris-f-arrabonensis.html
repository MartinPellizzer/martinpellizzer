Caltha palustris f. arrabonensis

Caltha palustris f. arrabonensis, commonly known as the Arrabonensis water buttercup, is a botanical variant of the water buttercup.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in Europe. The plant features striking yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in moist soil conditions.
