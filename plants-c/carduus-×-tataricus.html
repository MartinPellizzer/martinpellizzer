Carduus × tataricus

Carduus × tataricus, commonly known as the Russian thistle or tansy.

It is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or yellow flowers. The plant is native to Eurasia and is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in various environmental conditions.

However, it is considered an invasive species in many regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.
