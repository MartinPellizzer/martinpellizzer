Cnicus altissimus var. discolor

Cnicus altissimus var. discolor, commonly known as the purple sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are usually covered in a whitish or purple hue, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
