Cinnamomum alexii

Cinnamomum alexii, commonly known as Chinese cinnamon or Kuanhong, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark purple berries. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a glossy surface and a strong, spicy scent.

The bark is used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.
