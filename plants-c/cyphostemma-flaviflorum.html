Cyphostemma flaviflorum

Cyphostemma flaviflorum, commonly known as the yellow-flowered cyphostemma, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems that can grow up to several meters in length, often twining around supports. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy texture.

Cyphostemma flaviflorum is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
