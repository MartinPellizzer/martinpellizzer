Calamagrostis lanceolata var. virescens

Calamagrostis lanceolata var. virescens, commonly known as the green fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching stems that produce dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant is characterized by its vibrant green foliage, which remains lush throughout the growing season. It typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
