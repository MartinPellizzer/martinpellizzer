Cotoneaster lancasteri

Cotoneaster lancasteri, commonly known as the Lancaster cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, sparsely branched growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Cotoneaster lancasteri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
