Centaurea sosnowskyi

Centaurea sosnowskyi, commonly known as Sosnowsky's centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. It is a perennial species, often found in high-altitude habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant plant.
