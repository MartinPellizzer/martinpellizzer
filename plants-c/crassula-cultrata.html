Crassula cultrata

Crassula cultrata, commonly known as the Cut-leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula cultrata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
