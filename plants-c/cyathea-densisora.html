Cyathea densisora

Cyathea densisora, commonly known as the dense-veined tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall stature, while its fronds contribute to its ornamental value.

Cyathea densisora plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various forest organisms.
