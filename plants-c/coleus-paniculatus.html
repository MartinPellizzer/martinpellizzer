Coleus paniculatus

Coleus paniculatus, commonly known as the flame flower or red hot poker, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a perennial shrub that produces tall, branched inflorescences of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often variegated, adding visual interest to gardens and containers.

Coleus paniculatus is popular for its vibrant blooms and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
