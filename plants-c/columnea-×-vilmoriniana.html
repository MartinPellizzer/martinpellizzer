Columnea × vilmoriniana

Columnea × vilmoriniana, commonly known as the hybrid flame flower, is a tropical plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces showy bracts that enhance its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Columnea × vilmoriniana is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings due to its sensitivity to direct sunlight.
