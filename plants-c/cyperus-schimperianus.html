Cyperus schimperianus

Cyperus schimperianus, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in wetland or marshy environments, often forming dense vegetation in shallow water or moist soil.

Cyperus schimperianus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various aquatic conditions.
