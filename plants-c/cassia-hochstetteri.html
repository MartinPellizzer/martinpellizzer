Cassia hochstetteri

Cassia hochstetteri, commonly known as the New Zealand glory flower, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, papery flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Cassia hochstetteri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping.
