Cyclopogon rotundifolius

Cyclopogon rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf cyclopogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with tiny, white to pale blue flowers.

Cyclopogon rotundifolius is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
