Cryptostylis erythroglossa

Cryptostylis erythroglossa, commonly known as the red-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or red with a distinctive yellow lip, and it blooms during the cooler months.

Cryptostylis erythroglossa is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
