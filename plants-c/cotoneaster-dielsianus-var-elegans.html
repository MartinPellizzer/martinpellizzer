Cotoneaster dielsianus var. elegans

Cotoneaster dielsianus var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
