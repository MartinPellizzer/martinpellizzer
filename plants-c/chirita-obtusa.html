Chirita obtusa

Chirita obtusa, commonly known as the broadleaf chirita, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. Chirita obtusa is often grown as a houseplant or in greenhouse settings due to its preference for warm, humid conditions.
