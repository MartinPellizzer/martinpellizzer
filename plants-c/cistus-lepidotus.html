Cistus lepidotus

Cistus lepidotus, commonly known as the rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has silver-gray foliage and is characterized by its aromatic, resinous scent. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus lepidotus is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
