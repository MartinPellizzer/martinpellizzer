Croton sclerocalyx f. luxurians

Croton sclerocalyx f. luxurians, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
