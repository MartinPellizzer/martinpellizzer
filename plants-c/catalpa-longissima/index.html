Catalpa Longissima

The catalpa longissima is a striking deciduous tree native to eastern asia known for its large heart-shaped leaves and showy trumpet-shaped flowers. it thrives in a variety of soil types and is often planted for its ornamental value and ability to provide shade. the tree is also valued for its hardiness and ability to withstand urban environments. its seeds are encased in long, slender pods that split open to release its seeds. catalpa longissima is a versatile and visually appealing plant that adds both beauty and ecological benefits to landscapes.
