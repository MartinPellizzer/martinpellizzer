Cattleya bicolor var. olocheilos

Cattleya bicolor var. olocheilos, commonly known as the Olocheilos Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking bicolor flowers, typically featuring a combination of white and purple hues. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation under proper care conditions.
