Cotyledon mucronata

Cotyledon mucronata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, purple, or white during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Cotyledon mucronata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
