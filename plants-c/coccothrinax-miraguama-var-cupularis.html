Coccothrinax miraguama var. cupularis

Coccothrinax miraguama var. cupularis, commonly known as the cupular palm, is a flowering plant species native to the Caribbean.

It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to saline conditions, making it a hardy species in tropical climates.
