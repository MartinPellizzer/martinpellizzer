Crinitina fominii

Crinitina fominii, commonly known as the Fomin's horned lichen, is a unique species belonging to the family Cladoniaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, branched, and often horn-like thallus structure, which gives it its common name. The plant exhibits a symbiotic relationship with algae, which contributes to its greenish or yellowish coloration. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant lichen species.
