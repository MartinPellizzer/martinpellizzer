Coelogyne complanata

Coelogyne complanata, commonly known as the flat-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth. The plant produces a single, narrow leaf and has a distinctive inflorescence with fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Coelogyne complanata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
