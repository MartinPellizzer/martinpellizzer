Cirsium yunnanense

Cirsium yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan thistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, showy purple flowers. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with stiff hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer.

Cirsium yunnanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
