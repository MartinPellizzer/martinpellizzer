Chamaedrys nissoliana

Chamaedrys nissoliana, commonly known as the Nissolian rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its compact form and dense foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round interest in the garden.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
