Chionanthus cotinifolius

Chionanthus cotinifolius, commonly known as the sweetgum or Chinese sweetgum, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers in panicles during the spring, which are followed by spherical, spiky fruit clusters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens and parks.

Its rapid growth and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in temperate regions.
