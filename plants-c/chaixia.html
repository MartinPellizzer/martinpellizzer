Chaixia

Chaixia, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants in the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to East Asia, particularly China, and includes several species that are often cultivated for their ornamental value. The plants typically have showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. They are perennial herbs that thrive in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Chaixia species are valued in traditional medicine and horticulture for their beauty and versatility.
