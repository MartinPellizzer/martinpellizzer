Cynanchum chirindense

Cynanchum chirindense, commonly known as African milkweed, is a member of the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its milky sap, which contains latex, is a notable botanical feature.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
