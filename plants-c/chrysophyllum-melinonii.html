Chrysophyllum melinonii

Chrysophyllum melinonii, commonly known as the African cherry or melinonii cherry, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy white to pale yellow flowers. The fruit is a small, smooth, yellow or orange berry that contains a single seed.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions for its wood and fruit.
