Caiophora nivalis

Caiophora nivalis, commonly known as the snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cold, mountainous environments. Its leaves are small, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Caiophora nivalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in alpine gardens.
