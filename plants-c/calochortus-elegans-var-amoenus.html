Calochortus elegans var. amoenus

Calochortus elegans var. amoenus, commonly known as the pink fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Liliaceae family native to California.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink to purple flowers that resemble small lanterns, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features grass-like basal leaves and a short, erect stem that supports its showy inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and open woodlands.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
