Coreosma florida

Coreosma florida, commonly known as the Florida coreopsis, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Coreosma florida is relatively low maintenance and suitable for USDA hardiness zones 6 through 9.
