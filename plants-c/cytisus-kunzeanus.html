Cytisus kunzeanus

Cytisus kunzeanus, commonly known as the yellow broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Cytisus kunzeanus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
