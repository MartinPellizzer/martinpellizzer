Comparettia Embreei

Comparettia embreei is a rare and enigmatic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This delicate plant is known for its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires precise conditions to survive. Due to habitat loss, it is now critically endangered and protected by conservation efforts. Its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of great interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
