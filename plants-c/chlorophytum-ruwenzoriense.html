Chlorophytum ruwenzoriense

Chlorophytum ruwenzoriense, commonly known as the Ruwenzori spider plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white and green stripes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
