Clavimyrtus firma

Clavimyrtus firma, commonly known as the Crimson Flame Myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom throughout the spring and summer. The plant belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes other well-known species such as eucalyptus and tea tree. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Clavimyrtus firma thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal maintenance once established.
