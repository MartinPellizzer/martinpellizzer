Carduus × albescens

Carduus × albescens, commonly known as the white thistle.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and showy white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions of Europe and western Asia. Its morphology includes a robust stem and a basal rosette of leaves.

The flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
