Centaurea axillaris var. fuscomarginata

Centaurea axillaris var. fuscomarginata, commonly known as the dark-margin centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking blue to purple flowers with dark red margins. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
