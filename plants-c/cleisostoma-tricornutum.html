Cleisostoma tricornutum

Cleisostoma tricornutum, commonly known as the three-horned orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with three prominent lobes on the lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Cleisostoma tricornutum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
