Cestrum floribundum

Cestrum floribundum, commonly known as the tropical nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
