Crinum gouwsii

Crinum gouwsii, commonly known as the Gouwsii Crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a red or purple stripe down the center. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are often striped or mottled with green and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warmer climates.

Crinum gouwsii is also known for its strong fragrance, which can be both pleasant and overpowering.
