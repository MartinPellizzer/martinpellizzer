Cousinia khansarica

Cousinia khansarica, commonly known as the Khansar cushion plant, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing cushion-like structure. The plant features small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in alpine and steppe environments.

Cousinia khansarica is adapted to withstand harsh climatic conditions, including high UV exposure and extreme temperature fluctuations.
