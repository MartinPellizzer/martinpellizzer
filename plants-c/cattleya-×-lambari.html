Cattleya × lambari

Cattleya × lambari, commonly known as the Lambari Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink, with a distinct yellow lip. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its humid, tropical environment. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its blooming period usually occurs in late winter to early spring, adding a striking visual element to gardens and greenhouses.
