Calycera viridiflora f. sinuata

Calycera viridiflora f. sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous green-flowered calycera, is a rare and distinctive plant species characterized by its unique foliage and floral structure.

It features elongated, sinuously curled leaves that are typically a vibrant green, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are usually greenish in color, which stand out against the foliage. It is native to specific regions with humid climates and thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized collections.
