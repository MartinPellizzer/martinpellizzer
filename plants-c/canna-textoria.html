Canna textoria

Canna textoria, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful leaf blades that can reach up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces showy, spadix-like inflorescences surrounded by brightly colored bracts, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Canna textoria is a perennial herb with a robust rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread aggressively in suitable conditions.
