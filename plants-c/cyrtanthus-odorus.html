Cyrtanthus odorus

Cyrtanthus odorus, commonly known as the sweet-scented gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. Cyrtanthus odorus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its pleasant, sweet fragrance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
