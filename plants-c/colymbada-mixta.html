Colymbada mixta

Colymbada mixta, commonly known as the hybrid water lily, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, supported by long, fibrous petioles. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive yellow center. Colymbada mixta thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats, often found in ponds and lakes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental water gardens.
