Cosmocalyx spectabilis

Cosmocalyx spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often hairy to the touch.

Cosmocalyx spectabilis is typically cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
