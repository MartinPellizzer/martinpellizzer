Cotyledon orbiculata f. dinteri

Cotyledon orbiculata f. dinteri, commonly known as the Dinter's African Sunset, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The leaves are typically a vibrant red or orange, especially when exposed to direct sunlight. This variety is a compact shrub with a rounded shape, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
