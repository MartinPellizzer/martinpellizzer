Cucurbita pepo var. serinina

Cucurbita pepo var. serinina, commonly known as the Turk's turban, is a variant of the common gourd.

It is characterized by its large, round, and often ribbed fruits that can range in color from pale green to yellow or orange. The plant is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family and is native to North America. It typically grows as a trailing vine with palmate leaves and produces yellow flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its distinctive appearance.
