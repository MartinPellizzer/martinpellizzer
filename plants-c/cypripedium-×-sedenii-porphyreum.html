Cypripedium × sedenii porphyreum

Cypripedium × sedenii porphyreum, commonly known as the Porphyreum lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid prized for its ornamental value.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically ranging in color from pink to purple. This plant is native to regions in China and is often cultivated for its beauty and rarity. It thrives in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

The species is also notable for its complex hybrid origin, combining traits from several cypripedium species.
