Cnicus radians

Cnicus radians, commonly known as goatweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing annual plant that produces yellow flowers and has hairy, lobed leaves. The plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found along roadsides and in dry fields. It has a taproot system and is known for its ability to spread through seeds dispersed by wind.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
