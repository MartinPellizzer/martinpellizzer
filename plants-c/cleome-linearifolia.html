Cleome linearifolia

Cleome linearifolia, commonly known as the slender cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and simple, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Cleome linearifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and ease of cultivation.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in warmer climates.
