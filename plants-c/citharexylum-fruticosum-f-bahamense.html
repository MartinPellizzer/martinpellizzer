Citharexylum fruticosum f. bahamense

Citharexylum fruticosum f. bahamense, commonly known as the Bahamian citharexylum, is a shrub native to the Bahamas and parts of the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and narrow, elliptical leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

However, it is not widely cultivated beyond its native range.
