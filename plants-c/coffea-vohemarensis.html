Coffea vohemarensis

Coffea vohemarensis, commonly known as the Vohemarenensis coffee.

This rare species is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and red flowers. It produces small, round berries that contain two seeds used for coffee production. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and genetic uniqueness, it is of significant interest to botanists and coffee researchers.
