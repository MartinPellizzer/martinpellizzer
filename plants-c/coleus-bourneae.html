Coleus bourneae

Coleus bourneae, commonly known as the flame angel, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is closely related to other coleus species. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
