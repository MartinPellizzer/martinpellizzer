Caladium duchartrei

Caladium duchartrei, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting patterns. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and prefers well-drained, fertile soil. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by red berries.

Caladium duchartrei is popular as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and is often used in containers or as part of a shaded garden display.
