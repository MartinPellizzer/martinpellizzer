Cosmos steyermarkii

Cosmos steyermarkii, commonly known as the Steyermark's cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features narrow, gray-green leaves. Cosmos steyermarkii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
