Caladenia splendens

Caladenia splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Spider-orchid, is an endemic species of Australia, primarily found in the eastern regions.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a single, slender stem that bears a single, strikingly colored flower. The flower has a distinctive spider-like appearance, with a narrow, elongated labellum and a narrow, curved dorsal sepal. It typically blooms in spring, producing vibrant pink or purple flowers with intricate patterns.

The plant prefers well-drained soils and is often found in open forests or grasslands.
