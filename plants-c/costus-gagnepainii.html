Costus gagnepainii

Costus gagnepainii, commonly known as the Gagnepain's costus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Costaceae.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, spadix-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Costus gagnepainii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
