Capnoides cava

Capnoides cava, commonly known as the slender capnoid.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Capnoides cava produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.
