Crassula sparsa

Crassula sparsa, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
