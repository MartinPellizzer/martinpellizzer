Crassula exilis subsp. picturata

Crassula exilis subsp.

picturata, commonly known as the "Picturata Crassula," is a compact, succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features small, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in sunlight.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped white flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance.

Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
