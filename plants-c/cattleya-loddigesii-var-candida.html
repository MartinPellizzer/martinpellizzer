Cattleya loddigesii var. candida

Cattleya loddigesii var. candida, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, large white flowers with a subtle pinkish tinge.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It features broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant blooms that typically appear in summer. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.

Its hybrid origin contributes to its unique appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
