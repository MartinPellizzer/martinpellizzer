Cymbopogon xichangensis

Cymbopogon xichangensis, commonly known as the Xichang citron grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often overlooked due to its subtle appearance. It is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

This species is also noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its resilience in different climatic conditions.
