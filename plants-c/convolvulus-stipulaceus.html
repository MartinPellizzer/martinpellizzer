Convolvulus stipulaceus

Convolvulus stipulaceus, commonly known as the slender bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, pale yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has slender, twining stems that often climb or trail along the ground. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a distinctive stipular structure.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.
