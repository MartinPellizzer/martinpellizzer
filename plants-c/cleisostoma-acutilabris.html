Cleisostoma acutilabris

Cleisostoma acutilabris, commonly known as the sharp-beaked cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant features a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are known for their intricate, spurred petals.

Cleisostoma acutilabris thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
