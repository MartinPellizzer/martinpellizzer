Castilla elastica

Castilla elastica, commonly known as the rubber vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its vigorous growth and ability to produce latex. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

However, it can be invasive in some regions due to its rapid spread and adaptability.
