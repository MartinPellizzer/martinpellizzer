Crassula parvifolia

Crassula parvifolia, commonly known as the Little Leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
