Croton decalvatus

Croton decalvatus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that can range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton decalvatus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors.
