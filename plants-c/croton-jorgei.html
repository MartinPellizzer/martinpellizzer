Croton jorgei

Croton jorgei, commonly known as the Jorge's croton, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically broad and have a velvety texture, often displaying shades of green, red, and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
