Coelococcus

Coelococcus, commonly known as the orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their showy, often fragrant flowers and specialized structures such as the labellum and column. They have pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, and their roots are adapted for absorbing moisture from the air.
