Canistrum seidelianum

Canistrum seidelianum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub-like plant that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats. The plant features fleshy, succulent stems that are covered in a dense, velvety coating of fine hairs. Its leaves are usually small, opposite, and often reduced to scales, while its flowers are showy, typically yellow or orange in color.

Canistrum seidelianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
