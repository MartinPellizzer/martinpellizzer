Camellia huana

Camellia huana, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy and dark green. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, thriving in temperate climates.

Camellia huana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
