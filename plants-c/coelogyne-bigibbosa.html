Coelogyne bigibbosa

Coelogyne bigibbosa, commonly known as the two-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters of two. The plant features long, narrow pseudobulbs and linear, evergreen leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink with a yellow lip, and they emit a sweet fragrance.

Coelogyne bigibbosa is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
