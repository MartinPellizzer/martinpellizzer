Cyathea binuangensis

Cyathea binuangensis, commonly known as the Binuang tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in lowland and montane rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall stem, while its fronds contribute to the forest canopy.

Cyathea binuangensis plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various forest organisms.
