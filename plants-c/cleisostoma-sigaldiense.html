Cleisostoma sigaldiense

Cleisostoma sigaldiense, commonly known as the Sigalde's cleistostoma, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, white to pale yellow blooms with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
