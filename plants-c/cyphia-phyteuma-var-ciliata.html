Cyphia phyteuma var. ciliata

Cyphia phyteuma var. ciliata, commonly known as the Ciliate Cyphia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish-brown seeds in its composite flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
