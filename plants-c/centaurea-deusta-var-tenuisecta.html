Centaurea deusta var. tenuisecta

Centaurea deusta var. tenuisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are narrow and linear, often with a hairy texture, and the plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.

This variety is relatively low maintenance and thrives in full sun conditions.
