Chenopodium nudiflorum

Chenopodium nudiflorum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, spine-tipped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Chenopodium nudiflorum is also known for its edible young leaves, which are used in some traditional cuisines.
