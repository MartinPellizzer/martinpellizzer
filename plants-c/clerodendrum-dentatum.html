Clerodendrum dentatum

Clerodendrum dentatum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often brightly colored, typically red or orange. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in umbels. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Clerodendrum dentatum is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and unique floral display in gardens and as a houseplant.
