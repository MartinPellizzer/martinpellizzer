Croton boissieri

Croton boissieri, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly colored plants. The plant is characterized by its alternate, simple leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces capsule fruits containing seeds.

Croton boissieri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage and unique appearance.
