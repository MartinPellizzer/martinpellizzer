Calymella circinnata

Calymella circinnata, commonly known as the spiral leaf vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in tropical plants.
