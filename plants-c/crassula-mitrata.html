Crassula mitrata

Crassula mitrata, commonly known as the helmeted crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant features dense clusters of fleshy, ovate leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the cooler months.

Crassula mitrata is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
