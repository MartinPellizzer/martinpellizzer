Cistus × akamantis var. polyanthus

Cistus × akamantis var. polyanthus, commonly known as the Polyanthus rockrose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with a grayish-green hue and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is well adapted to poor, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its fragrant blooms attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, enhancing its ecological value.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it a popular choice in horticulture.
