Cryptomeria lobbii

Cryptomeria lobbii, commonly known as the African red cedar, is a coniferous tree native to the mountains of South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark-green foliage. The tree produces small, cone-like structures that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and aromatic properties.

Cryptomeria lobbii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
