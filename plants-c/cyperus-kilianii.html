Cyperus kilianii

Cyperus kilianii, commonly known as the kilian's flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family, Cyperaceae, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along waterways, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions. Its flowering inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often clustered at the base of the plant.

Cyperus kilianii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.
