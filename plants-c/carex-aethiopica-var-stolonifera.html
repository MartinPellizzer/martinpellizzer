Carex aethiopica var. stolonifera

Carex aethiopica var. stolonifera, commonly known as the African sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa.

It features dense clusters of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, which are usually borne on slender stems. It thrives in moist to wet soil conditions and is often found in marshy areas or along stream banks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
