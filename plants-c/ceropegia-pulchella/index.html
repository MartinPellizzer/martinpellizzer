Ceropegia Pulchella

Ceropegia pulchella is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, tubular flowers, it captivates botanists and gardeners alike. This species belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes many milkweed-like plants. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience in arid environments. Often grown as a houseplant, it requires bright light and well-draining soil to thrive.
