Chlorophytum dalzielii

Chlorophytum dalzielii, commonly known as the African lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its strap-like, grass-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. The plant produces tall, erect flower stalks bearing small, white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Chlorophytum dalzielii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
