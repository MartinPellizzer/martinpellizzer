Clematis × crippsii

Clematis × crippsii, commonly known as the Crippsii Clematis, is a hybrid plant that combines the characteristics of several Clematis species.

It is a vigorous climber with long, arching stems that can reach heights of up to 15 feet. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is dark green and provides a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
