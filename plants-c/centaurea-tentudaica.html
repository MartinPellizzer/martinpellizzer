Centaurea tentudaica

Centaurea tentudaica, commonly known as the Turkish knapweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia, including Turkey. The plant features erect, sparsely branched stems with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, arranged in composite heads with both ray and disk florets.

Centaurea tentudaica is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
