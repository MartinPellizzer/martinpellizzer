Citrus × nobilis var. koozi

Citrus × nobilis var. koozi, commonly known as koozi lime, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the intersection of pomelo and mandarin.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruit with a thick, green rind and a high concentration of essential oils. The leaves are typically broad and dark green, with a slightly rough texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is valued for its aromatic zest and juice, often used in culinary and aromatic applications.
