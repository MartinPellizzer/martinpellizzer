Cleyera japonica tricolor

Cleyera japonica tricolor, commonly known as the three-color cleyera, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which display three distinct colors—green, white, and red—on the same leaf blade. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage makes it a standout plant in both landscape design and botanical collections.
