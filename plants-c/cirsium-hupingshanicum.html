Cirsium hupingshanicum

Cirsium hupingshanicum, commonly known as the Hupingshan thistle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It has a rosette growth habit and produces achenes as its seeds.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
