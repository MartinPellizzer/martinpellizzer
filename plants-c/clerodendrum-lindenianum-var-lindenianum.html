Clerodendrum lindenianum var. lindenianum

Clerodendrum lindenianum var. lindenianum, commonly known as the purple glorybower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a red calyx. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
