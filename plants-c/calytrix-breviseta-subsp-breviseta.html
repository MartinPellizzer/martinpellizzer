Calytrix breviseta subsp. breviseta

Calytrix breviseta subsp.

breviseta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calytrix, is a small evergreen shrub native to Western Australia. It features dense, compact growth with lance-shaped, narrow leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal conditions.
