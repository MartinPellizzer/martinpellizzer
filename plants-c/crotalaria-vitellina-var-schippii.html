Crotalaria vitellina var. schippii

Crotalaria vitellina var. schippii, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and palmate leaves, which are typically trifoliate. The plant is native to regions of South America and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a short stem with upright growth and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its bright yellow blooms.
