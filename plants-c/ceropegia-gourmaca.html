Ceropegia gourmaca

Ceropegia gourmaca, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped flowers that resemble tiny stars. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia gourmaca is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
