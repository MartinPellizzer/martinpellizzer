Cryptocoryne walkeri var. legroi

Cryptocoryne walkeri var. legroi, commonly known as the Java Cryptocoryne, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 15 cm in length, often with a waxy texture and a slightly serrated edge. This variety is prized for its ability to thrive in a wide range of water conditions, making it a popular choice for planted aquariums. Its rhizome-based growth habit allows it to spread easily, forming dense underwater foliage.

The plant is also noted for its resilience and minimal care requirements, contributing to its popularity among aquarists.
