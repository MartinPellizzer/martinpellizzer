Claytonia rubra

Claytonia rubra, commonly known as the red spring beauty, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features delicate, showy flowers that are typically pink to red in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has rosettes of basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers are pollinated by insects, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
