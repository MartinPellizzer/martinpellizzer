Clausena grandifolia

Clausena grandifolia, commonly known as the large-fruited Clausena, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by oval, yellowish fruits containing a single seed.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
