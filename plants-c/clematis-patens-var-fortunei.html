Clematis patens var. fortunei

Clematis patens var. fortunei, commonly known as Fortune's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive, showy structure. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
