Chenopodium integrifolium

Chenopodium integrifolium, commonly known as smoothleaf goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, entire leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in fields, and is often found in temperate climates. Its seeds are small and wind-dispersed, contributing to its widespread distribution.

Chenopodium integrifolium is also known for its edible leaves, which have been used in traditional cuisines.
