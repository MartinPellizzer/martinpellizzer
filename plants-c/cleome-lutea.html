Cleome lutea

Cleome lutea, commonly known as yellow cleome, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and branched stems that are often hairy. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in small, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Cleome lutea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and ease of growth.
