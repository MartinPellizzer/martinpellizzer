Carduus diffusus

Carduus diffusus, commonly known as the common thistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe, it has been introduced to many other regions and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has a robust root system and can grow up to 1.5 meters tall.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its anti-inflammatory effects.
