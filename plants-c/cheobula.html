Cheobula

Cheobula, commonly known as the Korean bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or stream sides. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
