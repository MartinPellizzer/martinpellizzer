Codonopsis forrestii

Codonopsis forrestii, commonly known as the Yunnan Codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its tubular, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and has a long, fibrous root system. Its leaves are opposite and lanceolate, with a rough texture.
