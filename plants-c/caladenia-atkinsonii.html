Caladenia atkinsonii

Caladenia atkinsonii, commonly known as the Atkinson's spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with a single, elongated flower. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of a spider.

Caladenia atkinsonii is a protected species due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss.
