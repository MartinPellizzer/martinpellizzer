Cryptocalyx

Cryptocalyx, commonly known as the lantern plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its unique, lantern-like floral structures that enclose the reproductive parts. The genus includes several species, most of which are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and Asia. Cryptocalyx plants typically have simple, alternate leaves and produce small, showy flowers that are often brightly colored.

These plants are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their distinctive and attractive blooms.
