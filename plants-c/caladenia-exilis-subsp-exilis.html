Caladenia exilis subsp. exilis

Caladenia exilis subsp.

exilis, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to Western Australia. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open woodlands and heathlands.

The plant features a single, slender stem with a single, narrow, greenish-white flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. It blooms during the autumn season, producing a delicate, fragrant flower with a specialized labellum.

This subspecies is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and fragmentation.
