Conyza hieraciifolia

Conyza hieraciifolia, commonly known as the common ragwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers arranged in composite heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in various habitats such as roadsides, fields, and waste areas. It has alternate, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Conyza hieraciifolia is known for its ability to thrive in a wide range of environmental conditions.
