Corysanthes fimbriata

Corysanthes fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed corysanthes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Corysanthes fimbriata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and unique floral structure.
