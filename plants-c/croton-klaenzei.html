Croton klaenzei

Croton klaenzei, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety layer of fine hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
