Commelina eckloniana subsp. critica

Commelina eckloniana subsp.

critica, commonly known as the critical commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
