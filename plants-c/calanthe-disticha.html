Calanthe disticha

Calanthe disticha, commonly known as the zigzag calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizomes are thick and fleshy, allowing it to store water and nutrients efficiently.

Calanthe disticha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique appearance.
