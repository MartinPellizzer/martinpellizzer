Calathea misantlensis

Calathea misantlensis, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns of silver and cream. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its ornamental foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Calathea misantlensis is known for its ability to thrive in humid environments, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
