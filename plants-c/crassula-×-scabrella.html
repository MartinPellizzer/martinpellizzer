Crassula × scabrella

Crassula × scabrella, commonly known as the Christmas Jade, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
