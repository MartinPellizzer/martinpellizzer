Cicercula cicera var. amoena

Cicercula cicera var. amoena, commonly known as the Amoena vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces elongated, slender pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or cultivated areas.

Due to its ability to fix nitrogen, it is sometimes used in agricultural settings to improve soil fertility.
