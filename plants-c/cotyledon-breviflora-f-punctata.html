Cotyledon breviflora f. punctata

Cotyledon breviflora f. punctata, commonly known as the speckled cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The leaves are typically green with distinctive white or yellow spots, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
