Cryptanthus suaveolens

Cryptanthus suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented ground orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fragrant, often pink or red blossoms that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Cryptanthus suaveolens thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its unique appearance and pleasant aroma make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
