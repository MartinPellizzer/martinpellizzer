Croton malambo

Croton malambo, commonly known as the Malambo croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that exhibit a wide range of shades, including green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
