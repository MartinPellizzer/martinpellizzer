Cyclamen europaeum var. peakianum

Cyclamen europaeum var. peakianum, commonly known as the Peak cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and striking, spurred flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland and alpine habitats. Its tuberous roots allow it to survive harsh winters, and it blooms from late autumn to early spring.

Cyclamen europaeum var. peakianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
