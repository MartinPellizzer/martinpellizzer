Calamosagus polystachys

Calamosagus polystachys, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, arching stems that bear clusters of fine, feathery inflorescences, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue that adds visual interest to gardens.

Calamosagus polystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its graceful, wind-swaying form.
