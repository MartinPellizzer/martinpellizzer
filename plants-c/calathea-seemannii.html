Calathea seemannii

Calathea seemannii, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that often change color in response to light and temperature. Calathea seemannii is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
