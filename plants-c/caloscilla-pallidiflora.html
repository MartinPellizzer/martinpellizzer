Caloscilla pallidiflora

Caloscilla pallidiflora, commonly known as the pale-flowered caloscilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and medicinal properties. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.
