Capsella poimenobalantion

Capsella poimenobalantion, commonly known as the mountain flax.

It is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Himalayas. The plant features slender stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Capsella poimenobalantion is adapted to cold climates and is often found growing in rocky or alpine soils.
