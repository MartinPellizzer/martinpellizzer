Coleosanthus populifolius

Coleosanthus populifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, or along stream banks. The plant features grass-like leaves that are broad and flat, with a fibrous root system. Its flowering stems produce small, clustered inflorescences, and it is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The species is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity in wetland ecosystems.
