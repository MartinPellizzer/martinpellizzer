Crassula densifolia

Crassula densifolia, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Silver Jade, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically oval or rounded, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring season. It is drought-tolerant and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
