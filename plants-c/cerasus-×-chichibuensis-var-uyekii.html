Cerasus × chichibuensis var. uyekii

Cerasus × chichibuensis var. uyekii, commonly known as the Uyekii cherry, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, intensely red flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blossoms.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
