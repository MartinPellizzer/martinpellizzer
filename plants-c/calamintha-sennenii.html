Calamintha sennenii

Calamintha sennenii, commonly known as mountain thyme, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a perennial herb with small, aromatic leaves and pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like mint and sage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often growing in rocky or alpine environments.

Its fragrance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and medicinal use.
