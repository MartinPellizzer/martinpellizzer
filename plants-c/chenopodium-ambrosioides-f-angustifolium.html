Chenopodium ambrosioides f. angustifolium

Chenopodium ambrosioides f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved wormseed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, angular stems and small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it is commonly found in disturbed soils and dry habitats. It is native to North America and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its seeds are also known to release a strong, aromatic compound that can repel certain insects.
