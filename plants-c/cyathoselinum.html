Cyathoselinum

Cyathoselinum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-green foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant produces berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is native to regions in southern Africa and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
