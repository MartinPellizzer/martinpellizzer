Croton campenonii

Croton campenonii, commonly known as the mountain croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
