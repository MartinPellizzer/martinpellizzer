Cleome bechuanensis

Cleome bechuanensis, commonly known as the Bechuanaland cleome, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Cleome bechuanensis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
