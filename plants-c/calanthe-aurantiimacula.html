Calanthe aurantiimacula

Calanthe aurantiimacula, commonly known as the orange-stained calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly colored flowers, which feature a bright orange lip with dark red markings. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe aurantiimacula is valued in horticulture for its unique and vibrant floral display.
