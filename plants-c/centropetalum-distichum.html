Centropetalum distichum

Centropetalum distichum, commonly known as the two-ranked centropetalum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive two-ranked arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
