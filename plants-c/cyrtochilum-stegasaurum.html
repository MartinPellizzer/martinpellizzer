Cyrtochilum stegasaurum

Cyrtochilum stegasaurum, commonly known as the stegasaurum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Cyrtochilum stegasaurum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
