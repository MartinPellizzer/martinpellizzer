Cypripedium × crossianum

Cypripedium × crossianum, commonly known as the Cross's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist woodland habitats, preferring rich, well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pink or white with a yellow or orange lip, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
