Cotyledon laxa var. setchellii

Cotyledon laxa var. setchellii, commonly known as the Setchell's African Sunset, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or pink margins. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
