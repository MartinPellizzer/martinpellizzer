Chilita blossfeldiana

Chilita blossfeldiana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant flowers. The plant produces showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers in shades of red, pink, orange, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Chilita blossfeldiana is popular as a houseplant due to its long blooming period and ease of care.
