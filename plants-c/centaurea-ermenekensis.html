Centaurea ermenekensis

Centaurea ermenekensis, commonly known as the Ermenek thistle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its structure includes a basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that rises above the foliage.

Centaurea ermenekensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
