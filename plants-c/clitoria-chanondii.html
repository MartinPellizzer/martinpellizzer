Clitoria chanondii

Clitoria chanondii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its pendulous, showy clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers emit a sweet, pleasant fragrance.

Clitoria chanondii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
