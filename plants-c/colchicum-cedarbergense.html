Colchicum cedarbergense

Colchicum cedarbergense, commonly known as the Cedarberg colchic, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces large, waxy, purple or pink flowers that are typically borne on long, slender stems. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy coating.

This species is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
