Caroxylon namibicum

Caroxylon namibicum, commonly known as the Namib Desert date palm, is a unique plant adapted to the extreme conditions of the Namib Desert.

It is a small, shrub-like plant with a dense crown of leaves that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are pollinated by wind, leading to the development of hard, woody fruits. Its root system is highly specialized, allowing it to access deep groundwater.

Caroxylon namibicum plays an important ecological role in the desert ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various desert organisms.
