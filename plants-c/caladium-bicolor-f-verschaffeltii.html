Caladium bicolor f. verschaffeltii

Caladium bicolor f. verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Verschaffeltii caladium, is a striking cultivar of the caladium plant characterized by its vibrant, mottled foliage.

This variety is prized for its unique leaf patterns, which often feature a mix of red, white, and green hues. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is typically grown as an ornamental, adding color and texture to gardens and indoor spaces.

Its foliage remains decorative throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
