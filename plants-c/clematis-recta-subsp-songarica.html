Clematis recta subsp. songarica

Clematis recta subsp.

songarica, commonly known as the Songarica Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to Central Asia. It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, oval berries that ripen to a dark blue or black color.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
