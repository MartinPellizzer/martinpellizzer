Capnorchis eximia

Capnorchis eximia, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of white, yellow, and green hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Capnorchis eximia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.

Its growth habit includes a sympodial structure with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.
