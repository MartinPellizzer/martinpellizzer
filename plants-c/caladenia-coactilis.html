Caladenia coactilis

Caladenia coactilis, commonly known as the red-streaked spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or loamy soils in heathlands and open forests. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf and a single, strikingly colored flower. Its flowers are usually pink to red with distinctive dark streaks, and they bloom during the autumn season.

The species is known for its unique morphology and is often found in specific microhabitats within its range.
