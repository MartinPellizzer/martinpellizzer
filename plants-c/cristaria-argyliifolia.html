Cristaria argyliifolia

Cristaria argyliifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf crista, is a unique plant characterized by its striking silver-green foliage.

Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape, with a waxy coating that gives them a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, adding a delicate touch to its overall appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance.
