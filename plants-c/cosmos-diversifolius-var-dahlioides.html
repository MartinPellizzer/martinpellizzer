Cosmos diversifolius var. dahlioides

Cosmos diversifolius var. dahlioides, commonly known as the Dahlioides cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink to purple flowers that bloom from summer to fall. The plant features feathery, bipinnate leaves that are finely divided, giving it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
