Cardamine oligosperma var. lucens

Cardamine oligosperma var. lucens, commonly known as the shining watercress, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland floors. Its leaves are often submerged or partially submerged in water, aiding in its aquatic or semi-aquatic habitat.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich soils.
