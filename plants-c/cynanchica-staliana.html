Cynanchica staliana

Cynanchica staliana, commonly known as Staliana milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are legumes that contain several seeds, and it is adapted to grow in dry, rocky habitats.

Cynanchica staliana is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
