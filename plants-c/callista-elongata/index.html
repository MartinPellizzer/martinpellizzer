Callista Elongata

Callista elongata is a rare and striking plant known for its unique elongated leaves and vibrant floral displays. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its delicate flowers often bloom in shades of pink and white, adding a touch of elegance to its natural habitat. Despite its beauty, Callista elongata remains understudied due to its limited distribution and elusive nature. Conservation efforts are increasingly important to protect this endangered plant from habitat loss and over-collection.
