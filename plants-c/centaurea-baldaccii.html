Centaurea baldaccii

Centaurea baldaccii, commonly known as the Baldaccii knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Centaurea baldaccii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
