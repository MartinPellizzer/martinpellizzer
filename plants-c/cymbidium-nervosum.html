Cymbidium nervosum

Cymbidium nervosum, commonly known as the veined orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive veined pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium nervosum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
