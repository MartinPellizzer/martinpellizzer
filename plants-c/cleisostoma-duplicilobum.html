Cleisostoma duplicilobum

Cleisostoma duplicilobum, commonly known as the double-lobed hooded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, showy, hooded flower that is typically white or pale yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in two rows along the stem, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

The flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.
