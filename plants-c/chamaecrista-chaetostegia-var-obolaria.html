Chamaecrista chaetostegia var. obolaria

Chamaecrista chaetostegia var. obolaria, commonly known as the African burr legume, is a flowering plant native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, spiny pods that enclose its seeds. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

This variety is noted for its hardy nature and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
