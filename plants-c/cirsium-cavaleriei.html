Cirsium cavaleriei

Cirsium cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its foliage is covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cirsium cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
