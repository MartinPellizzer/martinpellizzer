Clerodendrum tomentosum var. mollissimum

Clerodendrum tomentosum var. mollissimum, commonly known as the velvet clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub characterized by its dense, velvety foliage.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a soft, hairy texture that gives the plant its common name. It produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, warm climates.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
