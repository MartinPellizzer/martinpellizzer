Cattleya nobilior f. hugueneyi

Cattleya nobilior f. hugueneyi, commonly known as the Hugueney's noble cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, warm climates. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves and produces large, showy blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
