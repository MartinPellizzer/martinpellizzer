Cathedra rupestris

Cathedra rupestris, commonly known as the rock cathedra, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces striking blue or purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
