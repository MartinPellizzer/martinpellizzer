Cucurbita moschata var. meloniformis

Cucurbita moschata var. meloniformis, commonly known as the Turk's Turban squash, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, and often ribbed fruit that resembles a turban in shape. The plant produces large, yellow to orange-colored fruits with a thick, edible rind and sweet, nutty flesh. It is typically cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for culinary use.

This variety is well-suited to warm climates and requires a long growing season to mature.
