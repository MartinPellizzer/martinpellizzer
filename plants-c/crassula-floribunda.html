Crassula floribunda

Crassula floribunda, commonly known as the Christmas Chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, cylindrical leaves that form a rosette structure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
