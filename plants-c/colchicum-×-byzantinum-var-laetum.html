Colchicum × byzantinum var. laetum

Colchicum × byzantinum var. laetum, commonly known as the Byzantine colchicum, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Colchicaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer or early autumn. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique flowering display.
