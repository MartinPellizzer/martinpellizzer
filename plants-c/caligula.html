Caligula

Caligula, commonly known as *Calyptera calyptra*, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant features erect stems and lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are typically clustered in whorls along the stem, creating an ornamental display.

Caligula is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
