Croton umbellatus

Croton umbellatus, commonly known as the umbrella croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, umbrella-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically thick, leathery, and often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. The plant is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
