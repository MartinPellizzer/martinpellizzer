Calcearia Nana

Baby's breath, scientifically known as Calcearia nana, is a small, delicate flowering plant native to South Africa. It is prized for its clusters of tiny, white flowers that resemble clouds, giving it its common name. Despite its modest size, it has become a popular choice in floral arrangements and garden designs. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it easy to care for. Its beauty and simplicity have made it a favorite among gardeners and florists alike.
