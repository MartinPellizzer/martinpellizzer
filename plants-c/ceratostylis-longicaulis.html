Ceratostylis longicaulis

Ceratostylis longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, grass-like rhizome and produces striking, pale green to yellowish flowers with a distinctive horn-shaped spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are often clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the wet season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
