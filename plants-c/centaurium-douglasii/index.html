Centaurium Douglasii

Douglas's centaury, scientifically known as Centaurium douglasii, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forest understories. This species is characterized by its slender stems, small blue or violet flowers, and its ability to bloom in early spring. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered endangered in many regions. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ecologically important plant.
