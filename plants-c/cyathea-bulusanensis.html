Cyathea bulusanensis

Cyathea bulusanensis, commonly known as the Bulusan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant is adapted to humid, tropical climates and thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cyathea bulusanensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
