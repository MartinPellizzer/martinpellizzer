Clematis wuxiensis

Clematis wuxiensis, commonly known as the Wuxi clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, with a distinctive spurred structure. The plant produces small, spherical berries that mature to a dark blue color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Clematis wuxiensis is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
