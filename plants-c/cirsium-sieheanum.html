Cirsium sieheanum

Cirsium sieheanum, commonly known as Siehe's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. It produces achenes with pappus structures that aid in wind dispersal.

Cirsium sieheanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
