Calycera crassifolia var. crassifolia

Calycera crassifolia var. crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved calycera, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically broad and thick, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, blooming during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it an interesting addition to succulent gardens.
