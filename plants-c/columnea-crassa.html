Columnea crassa

Columnea crassa, commonly known as the thick-stemmed flame flower, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value.
