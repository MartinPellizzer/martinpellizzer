Crepidomanes intricatum

Crepidomanes intricatum, commonly known as the intricate sandwort, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or gravelly soils, often found in dune environments. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve water in arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal dunes.
