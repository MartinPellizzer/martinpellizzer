Cinnamomum austrosinense

Cinnamomum austrosinense, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Guangxi cinnamon, is a species of flowering plant in the樟科 (Lauraceae) family.

It is native to southern China, particularly Guangxi and Guangdong provinces, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces dark brown to black berries, which contain small, hard seeds. It is often cultivated for its aromatic oils and medicinal properties.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is valued for its timber and essential oils.
