Cirsium tenuifolium

Cirsium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a rough texture. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Cirsium tenuifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators.
