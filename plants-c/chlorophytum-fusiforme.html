Chlorophytum fusiforme

Chlorophytum fusiforme, commonly known as the snake plant, is a tropical plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a white stripe running down the center. This plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for indoor environments. The leaves are succulent, allowing the plant to store water efficiently.

Chlorophytum fusiforme is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can help remove toxins from the air.
