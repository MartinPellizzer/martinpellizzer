Cotyledon scheeri

Cotyledon scheeri, commonly known as the "Scheeri's cotyledon," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
