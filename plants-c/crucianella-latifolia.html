Crucianella latifolia

Crucianella latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf crucianella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
