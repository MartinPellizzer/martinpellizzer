Cnopos

Cnopos, commonly known as the common tamarisk, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its woody stems, small scale-like leaves, and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant is often found in coastal areas and saline soils due to its tolerance for harsh environmental conditions. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize new areas.

Cnopos plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing soil erosion.
