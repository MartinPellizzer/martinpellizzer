Citrus Macroptera Var. Southwickii

Citrus macroptera var. southwickii is a rare and intriguing citrus species native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This variant is known for its unique foliage and fragrant, small fruit that differs from the more common citrus varieties. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Due to its limited distribution and specific growing conditions, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and botanists. Its beauty and distinct characteristics make it a valuable subject for both research and horticultural interest.
