Coprosmanthus lasioneuron

Coprosmanthus lasioneuron, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped orange-red berries that persist through winter, adding visual interest to the landscape. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Coprosmanthus lasioneuron is often used in ornamental landscaping for its vibrant seasonal color and low maintenance requirements.
