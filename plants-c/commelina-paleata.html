Commelina paleata

Commelina paleata, commonly known as the white arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and small, white or pale blue flowers. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy areas and is often found in disturbed soils. It has rhizomatous growth, allowing it to spread vegetatively.

Commelina paleata is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
