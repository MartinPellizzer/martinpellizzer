Cotyledon rusbyi

Cotyledon rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's window leaf, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, with a waxy surface that helps it retain moisture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are known for their striking patterns and can be used as cuttings for propagation.

Cotyledon rusbyi is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
