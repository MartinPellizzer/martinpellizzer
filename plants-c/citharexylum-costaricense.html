Citharexylum costaricense

Citharexylum costaricense, commonly known as the Costa Rican citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features opposite, simple, and often aromatic leaves, which are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a fragrant scent.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
