Chlorophora humbertii

Chlorophora humbertii, commonly known as the Humbert's sterculia, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is noted for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
