Cyathea tatei

Cyathea tatei, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant is typically found in wet, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Cyathea tatei can grow up to 10 meters in height and has a slow growth rate, making it a long-lived species.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an important component of New Zealand's native flora.
