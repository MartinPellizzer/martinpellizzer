Canarium mansfeldianum

Canarium mansfeldianum, commonly known as the African nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing aromatic seeds. The plant is also noted for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
