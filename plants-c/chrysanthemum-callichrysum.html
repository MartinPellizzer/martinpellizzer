Chrysanthemum callichrysum

Chrysanthemum callichrysum, commonly known as the golden marguerite, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are typically daisy-like in appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.

Chrysanthemum callichrysum is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
