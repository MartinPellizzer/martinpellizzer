Cleisostoma verrucosum

Cleisostoma verrucosum, commonly known as the疣茎蝴蝶兰, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red streaks. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Cleisostoma verrucosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a suitable growing environment.
