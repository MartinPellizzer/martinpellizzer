Cytisus austriacus var. heuffelii

Cytisus austriacus var. heuffelii, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to Central Europe.

It features slender, wiry stems that often grow in dense, upright clusters. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early summer, which are arranged in racemes along the stems. Its leaves are small, oval, and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
