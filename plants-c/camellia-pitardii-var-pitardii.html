Camellia pitardii var. pitardii

Camellia pitardii var. pitardii, commonly known as the Pitard's camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and has a compact growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding color to the landscape during the colder months.
