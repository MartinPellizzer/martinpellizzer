Cyrtomium acutidens

Cyrtomium acutidens, commonly known as the narrow-leaf holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small garden spaces or as a container plant. It thrives in shady, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Cyrtomium acutidens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
