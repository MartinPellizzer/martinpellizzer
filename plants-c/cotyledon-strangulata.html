Cotyledon strangulata

Cotyledon strangulata, commonly known as the hanging cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by unique, spiky seed pods. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Cotyledon strangulata is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its striking appearance and ease of care.
