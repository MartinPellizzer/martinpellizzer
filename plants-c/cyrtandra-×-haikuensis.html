Cyrtandra × haikuensis

Cyrtandra × haikuensis, commonly known as the Haiku hybrid, is a hybrid plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Hawaii and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Cyrtandra × haikuensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
