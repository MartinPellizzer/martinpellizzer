Cynoglossum valentinum

Cynoglossum valentinum, commonly known as Valentine's Wort, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers moist, well-drained soil. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
