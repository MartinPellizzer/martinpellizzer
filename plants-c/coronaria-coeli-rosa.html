Coronaria coeli-rosa

Coronaria coeli-rosa, commonly known as the celestial rose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are typically erect and branching.

The botanical name reflects its ethereal appearance, which has inspired its poetic common name.
