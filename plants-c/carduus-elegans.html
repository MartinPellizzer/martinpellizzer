Carduus elegans

Carduus elegans, commonly known as the elegant thistle, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink composite flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs and have a distinctive, often lobed shape.

The flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, typical of the Asteraceae family.
