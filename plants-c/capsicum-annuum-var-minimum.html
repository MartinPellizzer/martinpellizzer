Capsicum annuum var. minimum

Capsicum annuum var. minimum, commonly known as the dwarf chili pepper, is a compact variety of the common chili plant.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits that typically range in color from red to orange. This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its mild to medium heat level. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its size, it is ideal for container gardening and small spaces.
