Cirsium × soroksarense

Cirsium × soroksarense, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that resemble those of other thistle species. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to regions in Europe and Asia. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and waste grounds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
