Croton linearis

Croton linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often bright green with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range from yellow to red, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton linearis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and vibrant floral display.
