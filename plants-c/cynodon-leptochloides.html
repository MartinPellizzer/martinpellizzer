Cynodon leptochloides

Cynodon leptochloides, commonly known as slender grass or coastal grass, is a perennial grass species native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted foliage and ability to thrive in sandy, saline soils. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in dunes and along the coastline. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is well adapted to coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and wind exposure.
