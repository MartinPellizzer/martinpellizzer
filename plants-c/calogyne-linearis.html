Calogyne linearis

Calogyne linearis, commonly known as the slender spider plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that resemble those of a spider. The plant produces delicate, white or pale green flowers that are clustered in panicles, adding an elegant touch to its appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Calogyne linearis is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
