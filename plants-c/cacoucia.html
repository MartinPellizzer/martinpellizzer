Cacoucia

Cacoucia, commonly known as cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to Africa.

It produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and nutrients. The plant thrives in warm climates and is often grown as a staple crop in many tropical and subtropical regions. Its deep root system helps improve soil fertility by fixing nitrogen.

Cacoucia is also valued for its drought tolerance and adaptability to various soil types.
