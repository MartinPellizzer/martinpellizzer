Ctenitis attenuata

Ctenitis attenuata, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds that give it a distinctive appearance.
