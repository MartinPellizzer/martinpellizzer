Campanula firma

Campanula firma, commonly known as the hardy bellflower, is a perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, providing a textured appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Campanula firma is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
