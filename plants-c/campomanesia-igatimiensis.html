Campomanesia igatimiensis

Campomanesia igatimiensis, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its simple, elliptic leaves with a smooth surface. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are followed by edible, yellow or orange fruits resembling small apples. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.
