Cyathula prostrata var. prostrata

Cyathula prostrata var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate spurge, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in cup-like cyathia, which are characteristic of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and typically have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
