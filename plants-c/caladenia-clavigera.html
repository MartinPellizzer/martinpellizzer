Caladenia clavigera

Caladenia clavigera, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained sandy soils. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, showy orchid flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its pale green to yellowish petals and sepals are adorned with intricate markings that enhance its visual appeal.

Caladenia clavigera is a protected species, valued for its ecological significance and ornamental beauty.
