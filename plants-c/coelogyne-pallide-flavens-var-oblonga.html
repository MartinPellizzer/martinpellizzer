Coelogyne pallide-flavens var. oblonga

Coelogyne pallide-flavens var. oblonga, commonly known as the pale yellow orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescences bearing fragrant, pale yellow flowers with a subtle fragrance. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
