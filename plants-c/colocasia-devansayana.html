Colocasia devansayana

Colocasia devansayana, commonly known as the Devansaya arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as swamps and forest understories. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a smooth or slightly rough texture.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
