Ctenitopsis membranifolia

Ctenitopsis membranifolia, commonly known as the membranous leaf ctenitopsis, is a unique plant belonging to the family Ctenitopsidaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, thin, and translucent leaves that give it a distinct appearance. The plant exhibits a creeping growth habit, often forming dense mats on the forest floor. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy surface, which helps in reducing water loss.

Ctenitopsis membranifolia is often found in moist, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests.
