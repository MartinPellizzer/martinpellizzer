Canavalia gladiata

Canavalia gladiata, commonly known as the sword bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sunlight for optimal growth.

Canavalia gladiata is often cultivated for its edible seeds and is also used in traditional medicine.
