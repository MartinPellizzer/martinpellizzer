Centaurea × kuemmerlei

Centaurea × kuemmerlei, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea solstitialis (yellow starthistle). The plant typically grows to a height of 30–100 cm, featuring blue or purple ray florets and yellow disk flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical characteristics make it an attractive ornamental plant, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
