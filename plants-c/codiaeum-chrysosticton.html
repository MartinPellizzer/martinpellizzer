Codiaeum chrysosticton

Codiaeum chrysosticton, commonly known as the golden flame or flame leaf plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in landscaping and indoor gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
