Callipteris ceratolepis

Callipteris ceratolepis, commonly known as the Hawaiian snowflake.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance and evergreen foliage. This plant is native to the Hawaiian Islands and thrives in coastal and upland areas. Its narrow, needle-like leaves are arranged in dense clusters, giving it a distinctive, compact appearance.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden among its foliage.
