Cucumis intermedius

Cucumis intermedius, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a species of flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and the Middle East, and it thrives in warm, arid environments. The botanical features of Cucumis intermedius make it an interesting subject for ecological and agricultural study.
