Centaurea fistulosa

Centaurea fistulosa, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright blue to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea fistulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
