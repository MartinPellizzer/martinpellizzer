Ceropegia dolichophylla var. brachyloba

Ceropegia dolichophylla var. brachyloba, commonly known as the short-leafed ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, trailing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
