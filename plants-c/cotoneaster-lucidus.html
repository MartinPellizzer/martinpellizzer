Cotoneaster lucidus

Cotoneaster lucidus, commonly known as the glossy cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright red berries that appear in the fall. The plant has elliptical, glossy dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cotoneaster lucidus is also valued for its ability to provide winter interest and support for wildlife.
