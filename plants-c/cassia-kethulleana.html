Cassia kethulleana

Cassia kethulleana, commonly known as the Malayan Cassia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its pods contain small, hard seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Cassia kethulleana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
