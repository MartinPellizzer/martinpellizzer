Ceropegia odorata

Ceropegia odorata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia odorata is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
