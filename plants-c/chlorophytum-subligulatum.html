Chlorophytum subligulatum

Chlorophytum subligulatum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often marked with white or silver stripes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal houseplant. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, which is a characteristic of many succulents.

Chlorophytum subligulatum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, contributing to indoor air quality.
