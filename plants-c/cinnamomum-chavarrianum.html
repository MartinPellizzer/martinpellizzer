Cinnamomum chavarrianum

Cinnamomum chavarrianum, commonly known as the Ceylon cinnamon or true cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is harvested and dried to produce the spice cinnamon. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elliptical berries containing a single seed.

This species is valued not only for its culinary use but also for its medicinal properties.
