Cordyline terminalis var. manners-suttoniae

Cordyline terminalis var. manners-suttoniae, commonly known as the Manners-Sutton Cordyline, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant is evergreen, with leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, giving it a dramatic visual presence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
