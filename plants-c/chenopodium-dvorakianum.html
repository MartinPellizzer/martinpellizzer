Chenopodium dvorakianum

Chenopodium dvorakianum, commonly known as Dvorak's goosefoot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically alternate and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish flowers arranged in dense clusters. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia, including parts of Kazakhstan and Uzbekistan.

Chenopodium dvorakianum is often found growing in dry soils and is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
