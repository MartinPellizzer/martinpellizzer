Clausena moningerae

Clausena moningerae, commonly known as the red-fruited banyan, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and distinctive red, fleshy fruits. The fruit is edible and contains a single seed, making it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
