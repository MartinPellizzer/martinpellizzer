Callicarpa Acuminata Var. Pringlei

Callicarpa acuminata var. pringlei is a unique and lesser-known species of the honeysuckle family. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its most striking feature is its vibrant red berries, which appear in clusters during the late summer and early fall. The plant is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its ecological importance. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in horticultural circles.
