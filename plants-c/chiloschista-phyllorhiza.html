Chiloschista phyllorhiza

Chiloschista phyllorhiza, commonly known as the broad-leafed chiloschista, is a rare and unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, flat leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces striking, spurred orchid flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
