Croton soratensis var. pycnanthus

Croton soratensis var. pycnanthus, commonly known as the Shiny Croton, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
