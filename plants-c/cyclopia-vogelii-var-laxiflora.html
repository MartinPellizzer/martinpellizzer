Cyclopia vogelii var. laxiflora

Cyclopia vogelii var. laxiflora, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, tubular yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
