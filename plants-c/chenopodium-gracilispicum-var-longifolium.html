Chenopodium gracilispicum var. longifolium

Chenopodium gracilispicum var. longifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America and is often found in disturbed soils.

This variety is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
