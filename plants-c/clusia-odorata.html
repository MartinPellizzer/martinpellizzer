Clusia odorata

Clusia odorata, commonly known as the sweetshrub, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, orange-red fruits that contain several seeds. Clusia odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
