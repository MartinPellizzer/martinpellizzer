Correa × densa

Correa × densa, commonly known as the dense correa, is a hybrid shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features narrow, aromatic leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, depending on the variety. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Correa × densa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its hardy and drought-resistant characteristics.
