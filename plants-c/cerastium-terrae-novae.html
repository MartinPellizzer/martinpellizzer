Cerastium terrae-novae

Cerastium terrae-novae, commonly known as the Terra Nova chickweed, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the coastal regions of Newfoundland and Labrador.

It features small, white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early summer. The plant has hairy, succulent stems and leaves that are opposite and often lobed. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and along rocky shores.

Cerastium terrae-novae is adapted to withstand salty air and strong winds typical of its coastal habitat.
