Clitoria ternatea var. major

Clitoria ternatea var. major, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant produces edible pods containing seeds and also has trifoliate leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown as a vegetable or for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
