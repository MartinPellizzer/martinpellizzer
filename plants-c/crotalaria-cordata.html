Crotalaria cordata

Crotalaria cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically oval and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warm season.
