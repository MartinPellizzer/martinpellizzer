Corymborkis angusta

Corymborkis angusta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its flowering period occurs in late spring to early summer, and it is often found in association with other orchid species in its native habitat.
