Cheirostemon californicus

Cheirostemon californicus, commonly known as the California evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that open in the evening and close by morning. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
