Cycnium meyeri-johannis

Cycnium meyeri-johannis, commonly known as the Meyer's gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, characterized by its rapid growth and sprawling vines.

It produces large, lobed leaves and distinctive, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to Central America and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.
