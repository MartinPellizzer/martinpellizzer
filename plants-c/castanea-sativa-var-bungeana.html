Castanea sativa var. bungeana

Castanea sativa var. bungeana, commonly known as the Chinese sweet chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family and is characterized by its large, spiny husks that enclose edible nuts. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge. It produces male and female flowers on the same tree, with the female flowers being more prominent.

This variety is valued for its hardy nature and the nutritional content of its nuts.
