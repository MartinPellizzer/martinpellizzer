Cymbidium motuoense

Cymbidium motuoense, commonly known as the Motuo orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green, with prominent purple or red markings.

Cymbidium motuoense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings due to its specific environmental requirements.
