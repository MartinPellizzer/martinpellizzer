Croton baldauffii

Croton baldauffii, commonly known as the Baldauff's croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, often displaying vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton baldauffii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and eye-catching appearance.
