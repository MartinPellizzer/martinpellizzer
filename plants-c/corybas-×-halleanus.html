Corybas × halleanus

Corybas × halleanus, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, hooded flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling a Christmas ornament, hence its common name. The plant is a terrestrial orchid with a short, stocky stem and broad, elliptical leaves. It typically blooms in late summer to early autumn, producing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers.

Corybas × halleanus is well adapted to the temperate climate of New Zealand and thrives in well-drained soils.
