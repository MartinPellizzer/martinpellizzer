Cyathea macgregorii

Cyathea macgregorii, commonly known as the MacGregor's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in rainforests. Its fronds can grow up to 3 meters in length, adding to its striking visual presence.

Cyathea macgregorii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
