Caballeria pellucida

Caballeria pellucida, commonly known as the glassy leaf or glassy plant, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its translucent, waxy leaves that give it a glass-like appearance, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
