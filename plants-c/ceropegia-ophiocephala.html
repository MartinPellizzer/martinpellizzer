Ceropegia ophiocephala

Ceropegia ophiocephala, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow, which are pollinated by specific insects. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular houseplant.
