Cymbidium serratum

Cymbidium serratum, commonly known as the serrated orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Cymbidium serratum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
