Crassula muscosa var. muscosa

Crassula muscosa var. muscosa, commonly known as the stone plant or fairy fountain, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that form a compact, low-growing rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
