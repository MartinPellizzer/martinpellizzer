Cassia buchananii

Cassia buchananii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It produces elongated, flat pods that contain several seeds.

Cassia buchananii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
