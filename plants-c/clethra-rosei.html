Clethra rosei

Clethra rosei, commonly known as the roseleaf clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features attractive, fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a glossy green surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Clethra rosei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
