Caladium schomburgkii f. argyroneurum

Caladium schomburgkii f. argyroneurum, commonly known as the silver-edged caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a hybrid form of Caladium schomburgkii, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive silver veins. The foliage displays a vibrant mix of green, white, and cream, creating a visually appealing contrast. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
