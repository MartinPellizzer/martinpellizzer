Clethra pyrogena

Clethra pyrogena, commonly known as the flameleaf clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It features attractive, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that turn reddish in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Clethra pyrogena is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
