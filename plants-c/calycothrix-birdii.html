Calycothrix birdii

Calycothrix birdii, commonly known as the Bird's calyx.

It is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America. The plant features small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.
