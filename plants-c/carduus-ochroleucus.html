Carduus ochroleucus

Carduus ochroleucus, commonly known as the yellow hawkweed, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and is native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are typically hairy and have a bluish-green hue. It produces a tall flowering stem with numerous small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Carduus ochroleucus is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
