Clusia aristeguietae

Clusia aristeguietae, commonly known as the Caribbean beauty, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clusia aristeguietae is also valued for its ability to tolerate shade and poor soil conditions.
