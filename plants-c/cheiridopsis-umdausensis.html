Cheiridopsis umdausensis

Cheiridopsis umdausensis, commonly known as the Umdaus cactus, is a small, flowering succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and spiny stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves and stems.
