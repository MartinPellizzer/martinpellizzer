Calochilus platychilus

Calochilus platychilus, commonly known as the broad-lipped star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky areas, and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often form a rosette, while the flowers bloom in late spring to early summer.
