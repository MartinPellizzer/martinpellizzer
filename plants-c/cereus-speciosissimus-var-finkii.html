Cereus speciosissimus var. finkii

Cereus speciosissimus var. finkii, commonly known as the Fink's Christmas Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented, branching stems that produce vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and ability to bloom during the holiday season.

Its unique morphology and seasonal blooming make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts.
