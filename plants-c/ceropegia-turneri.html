Ceropegia turneri

Ceropegia turneri, commonly known as the "string of hearts" or "heartleaf," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often clustered along its trailing stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia turneri is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its unique foliage and ease of care.
