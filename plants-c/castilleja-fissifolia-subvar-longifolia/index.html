Castilleja Fissifolia Subvar. Longifolia

Castilleja fissifolia subvar. longifolia is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. This subspecies thrives in the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike. The plant is often found growing in association with other flowering plants in open habitats. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and environmental changes.
