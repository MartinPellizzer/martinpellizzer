Cistus × ledon

Cistus × ledon, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often releasing a distinctive scent when crushed.

Cistus × ledon is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
