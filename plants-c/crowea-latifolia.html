Crowea latifolia

Crowea latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf crowea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Crowea latifolia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
