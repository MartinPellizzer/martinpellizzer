Cyclodium meniscioides

Cyclodium meniscioides, commonly known as the desert daisy, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, succulent growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are fleshy and adapted to store water, making it well-suited for drought conditions.

Cyclodium meniscioides is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping.
