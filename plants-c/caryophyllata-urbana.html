Caryophyllata urbana

Caryophyllata urbana, commonly known as the urban carnation, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, colorful flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for urban environments such as parks and gardens. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Caryophyllata urbana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand urban pollution and compacted soils.
