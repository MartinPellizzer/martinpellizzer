Colpodium stevenii

Colpodium stevenii, commonly known as the Steven's colpodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Colpodium stevenii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
