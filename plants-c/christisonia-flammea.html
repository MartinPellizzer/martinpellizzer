Christisonia flammea

Christisonia flammea, commonly known as the flame bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Christisonia flammea is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
