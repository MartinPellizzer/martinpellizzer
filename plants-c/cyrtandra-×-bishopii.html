Cyrtandra × bishopii

Cyrtandra × bishopii, commonly known as Bishop's Cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant native to Hawaii.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
