Crassula cylindrica

Crassula cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in response to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula cylindrica is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
