Calathea gouletii

Calathea gouletii, commonly known as the Goulet's Calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, patterned leaves that often display shades of green, silver, and pink, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped or oval, with intricate veining that enhances its aesthetic appeal.

Calathea gouletii thrives in humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.
