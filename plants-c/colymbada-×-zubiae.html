Colymbada × zubiae

Colymbada × zubiae, commonly known as the hybrid water lily, is a natural hybrid resulting from the cross between *Colymboda cataractarum* and *Nymphaea zubii*.

It is characterized by its large, showy, floating leaves and vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a complex arrangement of vascular tissues.

The hybrid is notable for its adaptability and ornamental value in aquatic gardens.
