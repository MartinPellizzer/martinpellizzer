Centaurea savranica

Centaurea savranica, commonly known as the Balkan star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other thistle and daisy species.

Centaurea savranica is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
