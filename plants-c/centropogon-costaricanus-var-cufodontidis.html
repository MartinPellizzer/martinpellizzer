Centropogon costaricanus var. cufodontidis

Centropogon costaricanus var. cufodontidis, commonly known as the Costa Rican centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking tubular flowers that often display vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy texture.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
