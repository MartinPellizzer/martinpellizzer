Chrysophyllum glabrum

Chrysophyllum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth chrysophyllum, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers.
