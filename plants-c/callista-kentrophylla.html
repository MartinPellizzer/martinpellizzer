Callista kentrophylla

Callista kentrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, often blooming in spring and summer. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it prefers well-drained soils in full sun.

Callista kentrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
