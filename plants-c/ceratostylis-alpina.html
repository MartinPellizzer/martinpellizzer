Ceratostylis alpina

Ceratostylis alpina, commonly known as the alpine horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the alpine regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or mossy habitats at high elevations. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and it produces a single, elongated inflorescence with several flowers.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
