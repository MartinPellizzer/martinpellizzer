Cissus vinosa

Cissus vinosa, commonly known as the rock fig or African ice plant, is a climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often covered in tiny, translucent glands. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits that are rich in nutrients. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing on rocky surfaces or in shaded areas.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
