Chrysophyllum gatopense

Chrysophyllum gatopense, commonly known as the African satinleaf, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
