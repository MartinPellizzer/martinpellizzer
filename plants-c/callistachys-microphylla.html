Callistachys microphylla

Callistachys microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and bright red or orange bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. It has a compact growth habit and is well-suited for small garden spaces.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
