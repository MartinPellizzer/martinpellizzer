Cinnamomum officinarum

Cinnamomum officinarum, commonly known as the cinnamon tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is the source of the spice cinnamon. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by clustered, dark blue berries.

The tree thrives in humid climates and is often cultivated for its valuable aromatic compounds.
