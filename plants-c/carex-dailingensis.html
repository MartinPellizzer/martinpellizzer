Carex dailingensis

Carex dailingensis, commonly known as the Dailing sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Carex dailingensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
