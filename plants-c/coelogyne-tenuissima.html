Coelogyne tenuissima

Coelogyne tenuissima, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright pseudobulbs and linear, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne tenuissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
