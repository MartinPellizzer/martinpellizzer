Cypripedium lichiangense subsp. lentiginosum

Cypripedium lichiangense subsp.

lentiginosum, commonly known as the spotted lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive pinkish-brown lip that features dark, velvety spots.

The plant typically grows in cool, shaded habitats with moist, well-drained soil, often in forest understories. It has a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and several pseudobulbs that support its flowering cycle.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and over-collection.
