Callista walkeria

Callista walkeria, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant blooms and airy foliage.
