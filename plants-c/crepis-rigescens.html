Crepis rigescens

Crepis rigescens, commonly known as the rigid thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and dense, woolly leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in a head. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grassy areas.

Crepis rigescens is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
