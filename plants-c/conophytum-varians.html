Conophytum varians

Conophytum varians, commonly known as the "stone plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens.

Conophytum varians is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
