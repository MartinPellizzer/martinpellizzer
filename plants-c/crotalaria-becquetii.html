Crotalaria becquetii

Crotalaria becquetii, commonly known as the African spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate and trifoliate, with small, rounded leaflets.
