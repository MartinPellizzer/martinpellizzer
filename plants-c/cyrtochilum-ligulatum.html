Cyrtochilum ligulatum

Cyrtochilum ligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cyrtochilum ligulatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
