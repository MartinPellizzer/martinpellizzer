Crinum woolliamsii

Crinum woolliamsii, commonly known as the Woolliam's crinum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Crinum woolliamsii is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
