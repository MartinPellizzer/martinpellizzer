Cliffortia phylicoides

Cliffortia phylicoides, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, coastal areas and is well adapted to coastal climates. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Cliffortia phylicoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
