Calamus yentuensis

Calamus yentuensis, commonly known as the Yentuensis arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, arrowhead-shaped leaves. It produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in spadices, a common feature in the Araceae family.

Calamus yentuensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
