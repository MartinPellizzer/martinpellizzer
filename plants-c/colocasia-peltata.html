Colocasia peltata

Colocasia peltata, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple to white spadices, which are the actual flowers surrounded by a showy leaf-like bract. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also known for its edible tubers, which are rich in starch and used in various cuisines.
