Cajanus reticulatus var. grandifolius

Cajanus reticulatus var. grandifolius, commonly known as the large-leaved cluster bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, edible pods that contain small, round seeds used in various cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its seeds and ornamental value.

Cajanus reticulatus var. grandifolius is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil.
