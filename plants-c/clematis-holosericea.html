Clematis holosericea

Clematis holosericea, commonly known as the silky clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its long, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Its leaves are compound, with three to five leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Clematis holosericea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
