Cattleya warneri var. amoena

Cattleya warneri var. amoena, commonly known as the Amoena Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white, with a distinctive, velvety texture. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape, and it produces showy, pendulous inflorescences.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
