Chamaesium jiulongense

Chamaesium jiulongense, commonly known as the Jiulongshan bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are hairy and upright.

Chamaesium jiulongense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
