Carex myosurus var. ratongensis

Carex myosurus var. ratongensis, commonly known as the Ratongensis sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like leaves and dense, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, wind-pollinated flowers. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, this plant typically thrives in moist, well-drained soils in shaded or semi-shaded environments. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense clumps, making it a useful plant for erosion control and habitat restoration.
