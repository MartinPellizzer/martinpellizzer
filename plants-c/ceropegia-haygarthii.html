Ceropegia haygarthii

Ceropegia haygarthii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a trailing succulent native to South Africa.

It features delicate, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, adding visual interest to its appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish-white, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
