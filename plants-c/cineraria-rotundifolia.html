Cineraria rotundifolia

Cineraria rotundifolia, commonly known as the roundleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, woolly leaves and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance.

Cineraria rotundifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
