Cyclamen repandum var. baborense

Cyclamen repandum var. baborense, commonly known as the Baborenese cyclamen, is a striking perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bowl-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, slightly acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
