Cistus × bornetianus

Cistus × bornetianus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, white, or yellow, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Its horticultural value is enhanced by its ornamental flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
