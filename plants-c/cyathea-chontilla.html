Cyathea chontilla

Cyathea chontilla, commonly known as the Chontilla tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

Cyathea chontilla plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and shelter for various organisms.
