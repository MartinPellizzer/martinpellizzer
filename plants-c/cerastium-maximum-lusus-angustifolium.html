Cerastium maximum lusus angustifolium

Cerastium maximum lusus angustifolium, commonly known as narrowleaf mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae, characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers.
