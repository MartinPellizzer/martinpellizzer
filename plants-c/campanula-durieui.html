Campanula durieui

Campanula durieui, commonly known as the Durieu's bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, characterized by its bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its flowers, which range in color from blue to purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Campanula durieui is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
