Coelogyne trilobulata

Coelogyne trilobulata, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with three lobes, typically in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne trilobulata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
