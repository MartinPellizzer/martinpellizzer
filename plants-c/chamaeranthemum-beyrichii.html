Chamaeranthemum beyrichii

Chamaeranthemum beyrichii, commonly known as the Cape marigold, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.
