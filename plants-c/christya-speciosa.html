Christya speciosa

Christya speciosa, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, woodland areas and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of hairs.
