Catonia lantanoides

Catonia lantanoides, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and produces small, yellow flowers clustered in umbels. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
