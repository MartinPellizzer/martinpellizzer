Cordia lenis

Cordia lenis, commonly known as the white cordia, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and opposite, ovate leaves. Cordia lenis is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
