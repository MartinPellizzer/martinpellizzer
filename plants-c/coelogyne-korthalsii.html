Coelogyne korthalsii

Coelogyne korthalsii, commonly known as the Korthals' orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a rhizomatous growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive red or purple lip.

Coelogyne korthalsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
