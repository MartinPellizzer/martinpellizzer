Callistemon subulatus

Callistemon subulatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical, bright red flower spikes that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

The botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and native landscapes.
