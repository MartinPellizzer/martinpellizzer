Crotalaria diversifolia var. unifoliata

Crotalaria diversifolia var. unifoliata, commonly known as the single-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate or single leaflets, depending on the variety. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which enriches the soil. It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and savannas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
