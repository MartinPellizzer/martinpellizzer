Cinnamomum baileyanum

Cinnamomum baileyanum, commonly known as the Yunnan cinnamon or Baileya cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces dark brown, aromatic bark that is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
