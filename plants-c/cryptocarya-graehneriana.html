Cryptocarya graehneriana

Cryptocarya graehneriana, commonly known as the Hairy Cryptocarya, is a flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves with a leathery texture and dense, hairy covering. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by spherical berries.

Cryptocarya graehneriana is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
