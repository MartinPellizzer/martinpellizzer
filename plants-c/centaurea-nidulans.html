Centaurea nidulans

Centaurea nidulans, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Centaurea nidulans is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and healing properties.
