Curtia Obtusifolia

Curtia obtusifolia is a unique plant species native to the tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the family Malvaceae, which includes hibiscus and cotton. This plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and striking pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.
