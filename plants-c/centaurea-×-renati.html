Centaurea × renati

Centaurea × renati, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It produces both ray and disk florets, with the former being more showy and the latter contributing to seed production.

Centaurea × renati is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its vibrant color and ease of care.
