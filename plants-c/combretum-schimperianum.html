Combretum schimperianum

Combretum schimperianum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
