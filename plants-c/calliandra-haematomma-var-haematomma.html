Calliandra haematomma var. haematomma

Calliandra haematomma var. haematomma, commonly known as the red calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, red-pink inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small, oval leaflets and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.

The species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
