Colocasia esculenta var. fontanesii

Colocasia esculenta var. fontanesii, commonly known as the African elephant ear, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter.

The leaves are typically a deep green with a velvety texture and often have a slight purple tinge on the underside. This variety is native to Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, pale yellow flowers in the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
