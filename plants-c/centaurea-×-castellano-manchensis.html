Centaurea × castellano-manchensis

Centaurea × castellano-manchensis, commonly known as the Castellano-Manchensk centaurea, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads, which are typically surrounded by pale yellow ray florets. The plant is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is often found in rocky or disturbed soils. It exhibits a compact growth habit with upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
