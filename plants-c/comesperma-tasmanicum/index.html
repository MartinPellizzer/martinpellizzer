Comesperma Tasmanicum

Comesperma tasmanicum is a rare and unique plant native to the coastal regions of Tasmania. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found growing in sheltered areas along the shoreline. This species is known for its delicate, waxy leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Due to its limited habitat and threats from environmental changes, it is considered vulnerable. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
