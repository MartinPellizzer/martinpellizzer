Conophytum fraternum var. leptanthum

Conophytum fraternum var. leptanthum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
