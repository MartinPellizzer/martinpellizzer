Caladium argyrites

Caladium argyrites, commonly known as the silver leaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are predominantly white with deep green veins. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant, decorative appearance.

Caladium argyrites is typically grown as an annual in regions with cold winters.
