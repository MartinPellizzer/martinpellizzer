Calathea argyrophylla

Calathea argyrophylla, commonly known as the silver stripe calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green stripes. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy texture, which helps in reducing water loss. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
