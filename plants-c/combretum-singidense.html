Combretum singidense

Combretum singidense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often ovate or elliptic in shape. It is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.
