Cynorkis lilacina var. tereticalcar

Cynorkis lilacina var. tereticalcar, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant lilac-pink petals and a unique, fragrant scent that attracts pollinators. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy flowers.

This variety is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
