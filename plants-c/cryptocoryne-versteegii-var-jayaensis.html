Cryptocoryne versteegii var. jayaensis

Cryptocoryne versteegii var. jayaensis, commonly known as the Jayaensis Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, grass-like leaves that are typically dark green with a subtle veining pattern. This variety thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams, where it can form dense underwater vegetation. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize large areas efficiently.

The plant is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
