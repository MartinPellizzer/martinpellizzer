Cyperus atrobrunneus

Cyperus atrobrunneus, commonly known as black-brown flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wet or moist habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. Its leaves are grass-like, slender, and often have a dark brown or purple hue, giving it its common name.

The inflorescence consists of spikelets that are usually clustered in dense, cylindrical arrangements.
