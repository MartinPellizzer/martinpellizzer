Cytisus austriacus var. maezius

Cytisus austriacus var. maezius, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to the Alps of Central Europe.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has compound leaves made up of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
