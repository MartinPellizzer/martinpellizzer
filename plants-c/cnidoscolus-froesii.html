Cnidoscolus froesii

Cnidoscolus froesii, commonly known as the Brazilian arrowroot or arrowhead plant, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.
