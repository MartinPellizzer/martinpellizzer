Catasetum secundum

Catasetum secundum, commonly known as the secondary orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a specialized column that plays a role in pollination. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Catasetum secundum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
