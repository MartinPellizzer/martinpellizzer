Chenopodium phillipsianum

Chenopodium phillipsianum, commonly known as New Zealand spinach, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

Chenopodium phillipsianum is well adapted to temperate climates and is often cultivated in home gardens for its nutritional and culinary benefits.
