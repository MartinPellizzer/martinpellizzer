Cephalaria chaldoranensis

Cephalaria chaldoranensis, commonly known as the Chaldoran cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, providing a distinctive appearance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
