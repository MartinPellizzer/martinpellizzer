Carduus × stiriacus

Carduus × stiriacus, commonly known as the common sowthistle.

This plant is a hybrid species belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, followed by small, spiky fruits.

It is considered a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
