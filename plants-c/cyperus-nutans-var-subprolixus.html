Cyperus nutans var. subprolixus

Cyperus nutans var. subprolixus, commonly known as the spreading flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with narrow, linear leaves that are often slightly hairy. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, inconspicuous flowers.

It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and pond edges.
