Cytisus barbarus

Cytisus barbarus, commonly known as the Scottish broom, is a shrub native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has slender, thorned stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in heathland and coastal areas.

Cytisus barbarus is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
