Cynanchum insulanum var. insulanum

Cynanchum insulanum var. insulanum, commonly known as the island milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is known for its milky sap, which is a common feature among plants in the Asclepiadaceae family.
