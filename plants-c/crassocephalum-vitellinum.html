Crassocephalum vitellinum

Crassocephalum vitellinum, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in dense, conical inflorescences. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and can grow up to 1.5 meters tall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes in warmer regions.
