Cryphiospermum repens

Cryphiospermum repens, commonly known as the silverleaf creeper, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

Its adaptability and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
