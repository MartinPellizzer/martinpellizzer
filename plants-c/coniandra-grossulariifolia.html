Coniandra grossulariifolia

Coniandra grossulariifolia, commonly known as the African rose or star flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features showy, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or red in color, blooming in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
