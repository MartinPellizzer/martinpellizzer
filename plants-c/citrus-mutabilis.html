Citrus mutabilis

Citrus mutabilis, commonly known as the Mexican lime or mock orange, is a small evergreen tree native to Mexico and Central America.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink to red, depending on the stage of bloom. The fruit is typically a small, green, sour citrus fruit with a high acidity level. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and aromatic foliage.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.
