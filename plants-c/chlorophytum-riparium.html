Chlorophytum riparium

Chlorophytum riparium, commonly known as the water spider plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, grass-like leaves that often trail from the pot, giving it a cascading appearance. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers on slender spikes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Chlorophytum riparium is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage.
