Chlorophytum africanum var. silvaticum

Chlorophytum africanum var. silvaticum, commonly known as the African snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant is characterized by its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering. It produces small, white flowers on tall stalks during the warmer months.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and ease of care.
