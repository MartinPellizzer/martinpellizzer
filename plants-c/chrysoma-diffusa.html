Chrysoma diffusa

Chrysoma diffusa, commonly known as the yellow starbursh, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, often covered in a fine layer of white hair.

Chrysoma diffusa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
