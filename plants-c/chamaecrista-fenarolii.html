Chamaecrista fenarolii

Chamaecrista fenarolii, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in sandy, well-drained soils. It has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
