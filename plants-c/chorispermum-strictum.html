Chorispermum strictum

Chorispermum strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf chorisperm, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a small annual herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through mechanisms such as deep root systems and reduced leaf surface area.

Chorispermum strictum plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.
