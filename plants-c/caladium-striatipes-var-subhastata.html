Caladium striatipes var. subhastata

Caladium striatipes var. subhastata, commonly known as the striped arrowleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive red and white striped patterns. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant coloration.

It is typically grown as an annual in regions with colder winters.
