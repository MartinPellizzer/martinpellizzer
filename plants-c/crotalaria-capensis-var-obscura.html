Crotalaria capensis var. obscura

Crotalaria capensis var. obscura, commonly known as the dark capensis crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with erect, branched stems and palmate leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. It is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in full sun.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
