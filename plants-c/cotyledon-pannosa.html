Cotyledon pannosa

Cotyledon pannosa, commonly known as the wrinkled cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The leaves are typically grayish-green and have a distinctive wrinkled texture, which gives the plant its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
