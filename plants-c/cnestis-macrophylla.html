Cnestis macrophylla

Cnestis macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf cnestis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Asteraceae family.
