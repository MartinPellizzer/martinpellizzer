Cattleya mossiae f. wageneri

Cattleya mossiae f. wageneri, commonly known as the Wagener's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.

This cultivar is characterized by its large, showy blooms that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it grows on trees or rocks.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for both its beauty and ease of cultivation.
