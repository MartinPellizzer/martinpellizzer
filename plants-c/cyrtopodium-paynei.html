Cyrtopodium paynei

Cyrtopodium paynei, commonly known as Payne's coryphostylis, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant produces showy, greenish-yellow blooms with a distinctive, twisted lip that attracts pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth.

Cyrtopodium paynei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
