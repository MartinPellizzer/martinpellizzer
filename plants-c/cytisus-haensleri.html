Cytisus haensleri

Cytisus haensleri, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, often spiny stems and small, leathery leaves that are arranged in pairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Cytisus haensleri is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
