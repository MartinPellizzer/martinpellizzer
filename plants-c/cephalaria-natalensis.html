Cephalaria natalensis

Cephalaria natalensis, commonly known as Natal's cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It features a tall, slender stem with narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

Its showy yellow flowers bloom in the summer months, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
