Ceropegia Laevis

String of hearts, scientifically known as Ceropegia laevis, is a popular trailing plant admired for its heart-shaped leaves that dangle gracefully from its stems. Native to southern Africa, this succulent thrives in warm, bright environments with well-draining soil. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite for hanging baskets and shelves, adding a touch of greenery to any space. Easy to care for, it requires minimal watering and indirect sunlight to flourish. With its charming look and low maintenance, the String of hearts is a beloved choice for both novice and experienced plant lovers.
