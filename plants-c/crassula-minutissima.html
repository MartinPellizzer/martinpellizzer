Crassula minutissima

Crassula minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf crassula or miniature jade.

It is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its tiny, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its compact leaves.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
