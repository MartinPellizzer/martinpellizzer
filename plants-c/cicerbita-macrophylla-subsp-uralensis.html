Cicerbita macrophylla subsp. uralensis

Cicerbita macrophylla subsp.

uralensis, commonly known as the Ural giant bittercress, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Asia and Europe. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants.

Its leaves and flowers are edible, though they possess a sharp, peppery flavor.
