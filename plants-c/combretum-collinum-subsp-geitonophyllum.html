Combretum collinum subsp. geitonophyllum

Combretum collinum subsp.

geitonophyllum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters, often in late summer to early autumn.

The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are usually opposite in arrangement. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique floral display and adaptability to various climates contribute to its ecological and horticultural significance.
