Crassula longipes

Crassula longipes, commonly known as the Woolly Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, woolly leaves that are gray-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula longipes is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
