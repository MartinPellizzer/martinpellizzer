Columnea hirsuta f. hansenii

Columnea hirsuta f. hansenii, commonly known as the Hansen's hairy columnea, is a compact evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive textured appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange with yellow throats, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its vibrant foliage and flowering potential.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown as a container plant or in shaded garden settings.
