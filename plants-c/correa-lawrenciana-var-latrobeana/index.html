Correa Lawrenciana Var. Latrobeana

Correa lawrenciana var. latrobeana is a striking evergreen shrub native to south-eastern australia. it is known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range from pink to red. this variety is particularly prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. it thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile plant for various climates. its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
