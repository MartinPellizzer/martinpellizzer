Clerodendrum ringoetii

Clerodendrum ringoetii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are opposite and often have a slightly hairy texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
