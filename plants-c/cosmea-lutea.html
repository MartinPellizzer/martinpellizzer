Cosmea lutea

Cosmea lutea, commonly known as the yellow cosmea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its foliage is often lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cosmea lutea is popular in gardens for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
