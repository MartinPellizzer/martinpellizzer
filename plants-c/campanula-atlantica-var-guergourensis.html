Campanula atlantica var. guergourensis

Campanula atlantica var. guergourensis, commonly known as the Guergour Mountains bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Morocco.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in alpine meadows.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
