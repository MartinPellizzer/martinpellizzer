Catasetum deusvandoi

Catasetum deusvandoi, commonly known as the Deusvando's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are notable for their intricate structure, featuring specialized pollination mechanisms.

Catasetum deusvandoi is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique botanical features.
