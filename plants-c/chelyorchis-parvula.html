Chelyorchis parvula

Chelyorchis parvula, commonly known as the small marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet meadows, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. The plant features a slender, hairy stem that supports a few narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, often with a distinctive hood-like structure.

Chelyorchis parvula is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in moist, temperate habitats.
