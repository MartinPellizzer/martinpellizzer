Cowania mexicana var. stansburiana

Cowania mexicana var. stansburiana, commonly known as the Stansburian cowania, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to thrive in dry, well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by succulent enthusiasts.
