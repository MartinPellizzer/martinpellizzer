Clerodendrum capitatum

Clerodendrum capitatum, commonly known as the Cape jasmine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Clerodendrum capitatum thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
