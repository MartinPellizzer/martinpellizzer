Croton medusae

Croton medusae, commonly known as the Medusa croton, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that resemble the flowing hair of the mythological figure Medusa. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Croton medusae is a deciduous shrub that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
