Carduus ibicensis

Carduus ibicensis, commonly known as the Iberian thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and grasslands. It has a short lifespan and is an annual or biennial species.

The plant's structure and adaptations make it well-suited to its arid environment.
