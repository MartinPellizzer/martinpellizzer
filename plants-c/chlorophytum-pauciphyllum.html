Chlorophytum pauciphyllum

Chlorophytum pauciphyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features long, slender leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers bright, indirect light.

Due to its low maintenance and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
