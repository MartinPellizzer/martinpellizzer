Calanthe discolor var. tyoh-harae

Calanthe discolor var. tyoh-harae, commonly known as the Tyoh-hara calanthe, is a rare orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking white and green variegated leaves and pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid forest environments, preferring well-drained, acidic soil. It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, which helps it store water and nutrients.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
