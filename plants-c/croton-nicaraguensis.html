Croton nicaraguensis

Croton nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly colored plants. Croton nicaraguensis is valued in horticulture for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
