Cousinia tafreshensis

Cousinia tafreshensis, commonly known as the Tafresh thistle, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

Cousinia tafreshensis plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitat and may have potential uses in ecological restoration projects.
