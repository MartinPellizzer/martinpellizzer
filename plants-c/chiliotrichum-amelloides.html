Chiliotrichum amelloides

Chiliotrichum amelloides, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of silvery, woolly leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to native plant gardens.
