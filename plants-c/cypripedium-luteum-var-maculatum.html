Cypripedium luteum var. maculatum

Cypripedium luteum var. maculatum, commonly known as the spotted yellow lady's slipper, is a striking orchid native to parts of Asia.

It features a large, showy yellow pouch-like flower with distinctive red and purple markings. The plant has a pseudobulb and produces a single, intricate flower per stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate forests.

Its unique morphology and vibrant colors make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
