Croton bispinosus

Croton bispinosus, commonly known as the spiny croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny leaves that are typically opposite or alternate in arrangement. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange or red berries. Its foliage is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Croton bispinosus thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
