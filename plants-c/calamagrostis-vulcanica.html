Calamagrostis vulcanica

Calamagrostis vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic reed, is a tall grass species native to volcanic regions.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and tall, upright growth habit. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or volcanic landscapes. Its feathery plumes add ornamental value, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Calamagrostis vulcanica is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in challenging environments.
