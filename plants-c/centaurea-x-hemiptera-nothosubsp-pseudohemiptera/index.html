Centaurea × Hemiptera Nothosubsp. Pseudohemiptera

The centaurea × hemiptera nothosubsp. pseudohemiptera is a hybrid plant species that showcases unique botanical characteristics. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its striking blue to purple flower heads. This subspecies is often found in rocky and arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its name reflects its hybrid origin, combining traits from its parent species. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it has become a subject of interest in botanical studies.
