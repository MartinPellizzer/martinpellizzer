Chrysolaena Propinqua

Chrysolaena propinqua is a rare and delicate plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its striking yellow and green foliage that catches the eye in any garden. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments with plenty of indirect sunlight. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among plant enthusiasts and collectors. Despite its beauty, it is quite sensitive to environmental changes, requiring careful cultivation.
