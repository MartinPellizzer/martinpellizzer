Cordyline cheesemanii

Cordyline cheesemanii, commonly known as the Kauri cabbage, is a distinctive New Zealand plant with striking, sword-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red berries, which are an important food source for native birds.
