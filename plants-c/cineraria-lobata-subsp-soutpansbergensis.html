Cineraria lobata subsp. soutpansbergensis

Cineraria lobata subsp.

soutpansbergensis, commonly known as the Soutpansberg cineraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions. It is often found in mountainous regions, particularly in the Soutpansberg area of South Africa.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
