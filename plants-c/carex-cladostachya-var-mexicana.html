Carex cladostachya var. mexicana

Carex cladostachya var. mexicana, commonly known as Mexican sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known for their distinctive inflorescences. The flowering spikes are usually pale green to brown, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
