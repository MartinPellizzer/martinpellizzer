Croton lundianus

Croton lundianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often in shades of yellow or green.

Croton lundianus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
