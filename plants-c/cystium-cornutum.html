Cystium cornutum

Cystium cornutum, commonly known as the horned rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Cystium cornutum thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive blooms.
