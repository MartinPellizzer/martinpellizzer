Clusiophyllea pervilleana

Clusiophyllea pervilleana, commonly known as the Pervillean flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its environment. The plant belongs to the Clusiaceae family, which is known for its showy and often brightly colored flowers. Its leaves are typically large, glossy, and have a deep green hue.

Clusiophyllea pervilleana is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
