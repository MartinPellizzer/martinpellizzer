Cyrtochilum kopfianum

Cyrtochilum kopfianum, commonly known as the Kopfianum orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cyrtochilum kopfianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
