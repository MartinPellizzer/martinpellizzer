Cirsium fangii

Cirsium fangii, commonly known as the Fang's thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Cirsium fangii is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
