Cynanchum cathayense

Cynanchum cathayense, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
