Centaurea × piifontiana

Centaurea × piifontiana, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are composed of ray florets and tubular disc florets, typical of the daisy family.

Centaurea × piifontiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
