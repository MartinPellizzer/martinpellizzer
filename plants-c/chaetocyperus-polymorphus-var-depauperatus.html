Chaetocyperus polymorphus var. depauperatus

Chaetocyperus polymorphus var. depauperatus, commonly known as the sparse chaetocyperus, is a grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and habitat restoration.
