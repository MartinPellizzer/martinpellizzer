Chrysophyllum sericeum

Chrysophyllum sericeum, commonly known as the silverleaf cherimoya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also cultivated for its nutritious and flavorful fruit.
