Corethrodendron lignosum var. laeve

Corethrodendron lignosum var. laeve, commonly known as the smooth-leaved corethrodendron, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and broad, smooth, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable environments.
