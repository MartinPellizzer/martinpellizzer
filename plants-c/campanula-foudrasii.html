Campanula foudrasii

Campanula foudrasii, commonly known as the Foudras' bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
