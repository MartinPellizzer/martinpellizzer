Croton dissimilis

Croton dissimilis, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that often display a vibrant mix of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Croton dissimilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
