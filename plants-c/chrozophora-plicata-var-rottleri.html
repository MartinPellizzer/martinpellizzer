Chrozophora plicata var. rottleri

Chrozophora plicata var. rottleri, commonly known as the narrowleaf false nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, opposite leaves that are typically narrow and pinnately lobed. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits.
