Centaurea atrata

Centaurea atrata, commonly known as the dark centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, dark violet to blackish-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

Centaurea atrata is often found in areas with poor soil, demonstrating its adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
