Croton vergarenae

Croton vergarenae, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from deep red to orange and yellow hues. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton vergarenae typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening, though it requires well-drained soil and partial shade to thrive.
