Chrysitrix capensis var. subteres

Chrysitrix capensis var. subteres, commonly known as the Cape chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange-yellow ray florets and dark brown disc florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. It has a compact, bushy growth habit with linear to oblanceolate leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
