Carex jinfoshanensis

Carex jinfoshanensis, commonly known as the Jinfoshan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Jinfoshan area of Sichuan Province. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous regions and forest understories. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in maintaining soil stability in its native habitat.
