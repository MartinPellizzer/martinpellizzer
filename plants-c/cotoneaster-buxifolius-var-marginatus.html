Cotoneaster buxifolius var. marginatus

Cotoneaster buxifolius var. marginatus, commonly known as the margined cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Himalayas.

It features small, oval leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside, with a distinctive reddish margin. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in autumn. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

The variety is characterized by its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
