Cryptocarya ferrea var. oblongifolia

Cryptocarya ferrea var. oblongifolia, commonly known as Brazilian pepper tree or *Schinus terebinthifolius*, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub with glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It is native to South America but has been introduced to other regions, including the southeastern United States.

The leaves and fruits contain toxic compounds, making it a potential invasive species in some areas.
