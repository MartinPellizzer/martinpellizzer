Crotalaria leptocarpa

Crotalaria leptocarpa, commonly known as the slender cowpea or smallleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces seed pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some regions for forage or traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
