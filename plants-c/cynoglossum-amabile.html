Cynoglossum amabile

Cynoglossum amabile, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, typically from July to September. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Cynoglossum amabile is also known for its medicinal properties, having been used historically in herbal remedies.
