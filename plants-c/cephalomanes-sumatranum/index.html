Cephalomanes Sumatranum

Cephalomanes sumatranum is a unique species of sea anemone found in the shallow waters of the Indo-Pacific region. This organism belongs to the family Zoanthidae and is known for its vibrant colors and delicate structure. It thrives in coral reefs and rocky substrates, forming colonies that contribute to the biodiversity of marine ecosystems. Despite its beauty, it plays a crucial role in the food chain, serving as both predator and prey. Its study offers insights into marine biology and the delicate balance of oceanic life.
