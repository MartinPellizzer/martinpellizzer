Coreopsis debilis

Coreopsis debilis, commonly known as the slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 6 to 12 inches. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with ray florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its delicate appearance and easy maintenance make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
