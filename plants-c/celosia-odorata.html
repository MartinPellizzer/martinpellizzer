Celosia odorata

Celosia odorata, commonly known as the flame flower or crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, plume-like inflorescences that can range in color from red to orange and pink. The plant is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, sunny climates with well-drained soil. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, and the plant is often used in ornamental gardening.

Celosia odorata is also valued for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
