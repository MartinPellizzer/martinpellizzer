Cabomba aubletii

Cabomba aubletii, commonly known as the water crown or water star.

It is a semi-aquatic plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features delicate, floating leaves that form a rosette pattern on the water's surface. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often resembling a crown.

Cabomba aubletii is often cultivated in ponds and water gardens for its ornamental value and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
