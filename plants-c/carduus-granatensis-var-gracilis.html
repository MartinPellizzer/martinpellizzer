Carduus granatensis var. gracilis

Carduus granatensis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender Spanish thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its slender, sparsely haired stems and pale green, lobed leaves. The plant produces purple to pink flowers in a compact, spherical inflorescence, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
