Ceratostylis vagans

Ceratostylis vagans, commonly known as the wandering horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its striking, yellow-green flowers with prominent, horn-like structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, and it is known for its ephemeral blooming period.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
