Ceropegia nilotica

Ceropegia nilotica, commonly known as the silver leaf or string of hearts plant, is a succulent perennial native to Africa.

It features trailing stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or violet in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Ceropegia nilotica is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
