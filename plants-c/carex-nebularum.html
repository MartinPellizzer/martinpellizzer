Carex nebularum

Carex nebularum, commonly known as the cloud sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. It produces small, spadix-like inflorescences that are usually green or reddish in color.

Carex nebularum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
