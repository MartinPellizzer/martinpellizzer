Campanula billardierei var. major

Campanula billardierei var. major, commonly known as the large bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Australia.

It features striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
