Croton billbergianus subsp. pyramidalis

Croton billbergianus subsp.

pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa. It is characterized by its dense, pyramidal growth habit and striking, colorful foliage that ranges from green to red and purple.

The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and often have a waxy texture. This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
