Cleisostoma ancoriferum

Cleisostoma ancoriferum, commonly known as the anchored cleisostoma, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has dark green, strap-like leaves and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.

Cleisostoma ancoriferum requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
