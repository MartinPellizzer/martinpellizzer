Cotula renwickii

Cotula renwickii, commonly known as the Renwick's everlasting, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces numerous yellow to orange flowers that are often arranged in a flat-topped inflorescence. Cotula renwickii is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

It is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its delicate appearance and long-lasting blooms.
