Cytisus junceus

Cytisus junceus, commonly known as narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are narrow and leathery, with a distinctive yellowish-green color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
