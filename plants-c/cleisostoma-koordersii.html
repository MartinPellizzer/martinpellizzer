Cleisostoma koordersii

Cleisostoma koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral structures. Its leaves are usually elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine, appressed hairs.

The orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories where it can receive indirect light.
