Cirsium × viridipurpureum

Cirsium × viridipurpureum, commonly known as the purpleleaf thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, which features a mix of green and purple tones on its leaves. The plant produces showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to the western United States and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens despite its prickly nature.
