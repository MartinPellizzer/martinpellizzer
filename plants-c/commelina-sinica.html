Commelina sinica

Commelina sinica, commonly known as the Chinese spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy surface.

Commelina sinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
