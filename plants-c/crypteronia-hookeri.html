Crypteronia hookeri

Crypteronia hookeri, commonly known as the African star orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue with contrasting purple or violet markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the base.

Crypteronia hookeri thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, forested areas.
