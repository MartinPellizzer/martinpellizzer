Centaurea reuteriana var. reuteriana

Centaurea reuteriana var. reuteriana, commonly known as the Reuter's centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and bright blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a rough, hairy texture.

The flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
