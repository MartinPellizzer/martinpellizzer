Croton lanatus var. lorentzii

Croton lanatus var. lorentzii, commonly known as the Lorentz's croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves ranging from bright yellow to deep red. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
