Cryophytum lineare

Cryophytum lineare, commonly known as the linear iceplant, is a unique succulent native to the cold regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its elongated, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its cold-hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping in temperate climates.
