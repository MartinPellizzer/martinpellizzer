Cyclamen repandum var. peloponnesiacum

Cyclamen repandum var. peloponnesiacum, commonly known as the Peloponnesian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, velvety foliage with a deep green color and produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. It prefers cool, moist conditions and well-draining soil, often thriving in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
