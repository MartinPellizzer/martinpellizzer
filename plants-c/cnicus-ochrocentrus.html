Cnicus ochrocentrus

Cnicus ochrocentrus, commonly known as the yellow centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in umbels, blooming from late spring to early summer.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally for its purported health benefits.
