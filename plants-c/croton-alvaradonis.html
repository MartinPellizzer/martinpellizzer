Croton alvaradonis

Croton alvaradonis, commonly known as the Alvarado croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, with a waxy surface that reflects light.
