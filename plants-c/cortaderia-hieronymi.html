Cortaderia hieronymi

Cortaderia hieronymi, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, arching blades that are covered in silvery-white hairs, giving it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces plume-like seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.

Its upright growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
