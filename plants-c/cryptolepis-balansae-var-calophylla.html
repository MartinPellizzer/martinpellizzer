Cryptolepis balansae var. calophylla

Cryptolepis balansae var. calophylla, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, and it is often found in moist, wooded areas.

Its roots and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
