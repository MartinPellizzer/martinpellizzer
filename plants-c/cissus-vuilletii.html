Cissus vuilletii

Cissus vuilletii, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found growing on rocky surfaces or in shaded areas.
