Cortaderia hapalotricha

Cortaderia hapalotricha, commonly known as the narrow leaf fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, arching leaves that form a dense, fountain-like shape. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, spikelet-like flowers. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its foliage provides year-round visual interest, while its flowering display adds color in the spring.
