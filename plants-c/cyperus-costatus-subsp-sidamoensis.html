Cyperus costatus subsp. sidamoensis

Cyperus costatus subsp.

sidamoensis, commonly known as the Ethiopian flatsedge, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia. It typically grows in wetland habitats such as marshes and seasonally flooded areas.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are characteristic of the Cyperaceae family. It is often found in regions with high humidity and abundant water sources.

Its botanical structure supports its adaptation to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments.
