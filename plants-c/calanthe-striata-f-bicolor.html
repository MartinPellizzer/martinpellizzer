Calanthe striata f. bicolor

Calanthe striata f. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor calanthe, is a hybrid orchid species that exhibits striking color variation.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy sheen. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, creating a vibrant display. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique coloration and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for both ornamental gardening and botanical study.
