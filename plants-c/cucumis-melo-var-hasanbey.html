Cucumis melo var. hasanbey

Cucumis melo var. hasanbey, commonly known as the Hasanbey melon, is a variety of muskmelon native to Turkey.

It is characterized by its elongated, striped fruit with a thick, aromatic rind and sweet, juicy flesh. The plant is a vine that produces yellow flowers and thrives in warm, sunny climates. It requires well-drained soil and regular watering for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its flavor and is often cultivated for both local consumption and commercial trade.
