Cyanthillium cinereum var. parviflorum

Cyanthillium cinereum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered blueweed, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, hairy stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in grassland habitats.

This variety is noted for its compact growth and ornamental value in gardens.
