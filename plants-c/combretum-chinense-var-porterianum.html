Combretum chinense var. porterianum

Combretum chinense var. porterianum, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, woody capsules that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
