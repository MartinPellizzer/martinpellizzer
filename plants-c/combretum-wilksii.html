Combretum wilksii

Combretum wilksii, commonly known as the red-flowered combretum, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
