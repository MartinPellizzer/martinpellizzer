Chrysophyllum cerasiferum

Chrysophyllum cerasiferum, commonly known as the Natal plum or sweet-scented cherry, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

Chrysophyllum cerasiferum is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
