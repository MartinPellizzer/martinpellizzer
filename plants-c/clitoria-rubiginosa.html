Clitoria rubiginosa

Clitoria rubiginosa, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features striking violet to purple flowers with two pairs of wing-like petals, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain protein-rich seeds, making it a valuable crop in some regions. Its leaves are trifoliate and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Clitoria rubiginosa thrives in warm, humid climates and is cultivated for both its ornamental value and nutritional content.
