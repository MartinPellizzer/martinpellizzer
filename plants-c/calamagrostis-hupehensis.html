Calamagrostis hupehensis

Calamagrostis hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along stream banks.

Calamagrostis hupehensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in wetland restoration projects.
