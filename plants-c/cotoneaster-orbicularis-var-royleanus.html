Cotoneaster orbicularis var. royleanus

Cotoneaster orbicularis var. royleanus, commonly known as the Himalayan cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features small, orbicular-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster orbicularis var. royleanus is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in cooler climates.
