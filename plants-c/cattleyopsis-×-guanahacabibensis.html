Cattleyopsis × guanahacabibensis

Cattleyopsis × guanahacabibensis, commonly known as the Guanahacabibensis cattleyopsis, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Cattleya and Brassia.

It is native to the humid forests of Central and South America, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces large, showy flowers with vibrant colors, typically in shades of pink, red, and white. Its pseudobulbs store water, allowing it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking floral display.
