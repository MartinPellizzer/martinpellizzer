Cattleyopsis cubensis

Cattleyopsis cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Cuba.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet scent.

Cattleyopsis cubensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
