Cerastium Arvense Var. Strictum

Cerastium arvense var. strictum is a low-growing perennial plant commonly found in disturbed soils and roadside areas. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is known for its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. Its ability to thrive in harsh conditions makes it a resilient species in various environments. This plant is often overlooked but plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
