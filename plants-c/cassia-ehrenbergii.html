Cassia ehrenbergii

Cassia ehrenbergii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cassia ehrenbergii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
