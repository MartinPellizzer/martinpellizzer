Coleus personatus

Coleus personatus, commonly known as the silver leaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green tones. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of indoor settings.
