Coreopsis linifolia

Coreopsis linifolia, commonly known as threadleaf coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, thread-like leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and prairies. It is a perennial herb that produces seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Coreopsis linifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
