Cytisus austriacus var. noeanus

Cytisus austriacus var. noeanus, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to the Alps and surrounding regions of Central Europe.

It features slender, often thorned stems that bear small, bright yellow flowers in early summer. The leaves are pinnate, consisting of several small, oval leaflets. This variety is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sunny habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
