Carex × thermalis

Carex × thermalis, commonly known as the thermal sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are often brown or reddish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Carex × thermalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
