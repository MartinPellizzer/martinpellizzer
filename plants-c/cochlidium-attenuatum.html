Cochlidium attenuatum

Cochlidium attenuatum, commonly known as the slender spiral snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Aegidionidae and is characterized by its elongated, spiral shell. The shell is typically pale in color with a smooth surface and a distinct spiral pattern. This species is native to parts of Europe and is often found in moist, forested environments.

It plays a role in decomposing organic matter and contributes to soil fertility through its feeding habits.
