Coelocaryon sphaerocarpum

Coelocaryon sphaerocarpum, commonly known as the globe sphaerocarp, is a rare and unique aquatic plant belonging to the family Sphaerocarpaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spherical fruiting bodies that resemble small, floating globes. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and lakes. Its morphology includes a slender, branched stem with small, scale-like leaves.
