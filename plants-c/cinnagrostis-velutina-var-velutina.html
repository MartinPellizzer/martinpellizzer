Cinnagrostis velutina var. velutina

Cinnagrostis velutina var. velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Velvetleaf is known for its rapid growth and is considered a weed in many regions.
