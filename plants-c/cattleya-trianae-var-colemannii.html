Cattleya trianae var. colemannii

Cattleya trianae var. colemannii, commonly known as the Colemann's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with red or purple markings. The plant has pseudobulbs and produces multiple inflorescences with numerous blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting flowers.
