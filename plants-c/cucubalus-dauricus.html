Cucubalus dauricus

Cucubalus dauricus, commonly known as Daurian sandthorn, is a shrub native to the cold regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically covered in a sticky substance. It is adapted to harsh environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in its native ecosystem.
