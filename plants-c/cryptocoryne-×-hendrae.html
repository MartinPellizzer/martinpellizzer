Cryptocoryne × hendrae

Cryptocoryne × hendrae, commonly known as the Java cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, bushy foliage. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often used in planted aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

Cryptocoryne × hendrae is also valued for its ability to help maintain water clarity by absorbing nutrients.
