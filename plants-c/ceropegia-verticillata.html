Ceropegia verticillata

Ceropegia verticillata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia verticillata is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
