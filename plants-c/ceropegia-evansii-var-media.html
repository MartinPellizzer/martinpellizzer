Ceropegia evansii var. media

Ceropegia evansii var. media, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
