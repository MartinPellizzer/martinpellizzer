Cassia foliosa

Cassia foliosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, winged seeds. Cassia foliosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types make it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
