Croton phebalioides var. acuminatus

Croton phebalioides var. acuminatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves with a pointed tip.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features showy, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange, surrounded by bright red bracts.
