Crucifera sophia

Crucifera sophia, commonly known as the celestial mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several seeds. Native to temperate regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
