Cymbachne ciliata var. perligulata

Cymbachne ciliata var. perligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cymbachne, is a grass species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves with finely serrated margins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along streambanks. Its inflorescence consists of a single, spike-like cluster of small, two-lipped flowers.

The species is notable for its delicate appearance and ecological role in maintaining soil stability in its native habitats.
