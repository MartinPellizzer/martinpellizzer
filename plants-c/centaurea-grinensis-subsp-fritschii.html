Centaurea grinensis subsp. fritschii

Centaurea grinensis subsp.

fritschii, commonly known as the Fritsch's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to purple flowers and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and slopes. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces a single, upright stem with several flower heads.
