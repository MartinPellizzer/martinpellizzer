Croton lundellii

Croton lundellii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage. The plant features alternate, simple leaves that can be elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton lundellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf colors.
