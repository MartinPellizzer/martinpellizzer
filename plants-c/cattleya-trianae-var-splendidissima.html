Cattleya trianae var. splendidissima

Cattleya trianae var. splendidissima, commonly known as the Splendid Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with bright orange petals and a contrasting yellow lip, making it a striking ornamental plant. Native to the Andean regions of South America, this variety thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts and collectors.
