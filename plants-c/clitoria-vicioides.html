Clitoria vicioides

Clitoria vicioides, commonly known as the butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant blue or purple butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant is a climbing vine that produces pods containing seeds, and its leaves are trifoliate, meaning they have three leaflets.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes or as a source of natural dye.
