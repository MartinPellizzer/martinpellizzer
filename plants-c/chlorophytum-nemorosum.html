Chlorophytum nemorosum

Chlorophytum nemorosum, commonly known as the forest spider plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features strap-like, bright green leaves that are often marked with white or silver veins, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that are followed by delicate, feathery seed heads. It is adapted to grow in shaded, moist environments, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying properties make it both ornamental and beneficial in indoor settings.
