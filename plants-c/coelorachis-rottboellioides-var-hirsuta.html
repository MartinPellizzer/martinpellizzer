Coelorachis rottboellioides var. hirsuta

Coelorachis rottboellioides var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy coelorachis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a soft, hairy texture.

This species thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
