Cymbidium sagamiense

Cymbidium sagamiense, commonly known as the Sagami orchid, is a species native to Japan.

It is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces racemes of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with dark red or purple markings. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Cymbidium sagamiense is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
