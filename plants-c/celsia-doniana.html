Celsia doniana

Celsia doniana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb, often reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its leaves are thick and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Celsia doniana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
