Carex Nanella Var. Callitrichos

Carex nanella var. callitrichos is a rare and delicate sedge native to specific regions of eastern europe. this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. its small, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit make it easily overlooked. despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in maintaining ecological balance. conservation efforts are crucial to protect this endangered species from habitat loss and climate change.
