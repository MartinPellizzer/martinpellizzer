Cavendishia latifolia

Cavendishia latifolia, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter. The plant produces vibrant pink or red blooms with a distinctive, intricate pattern of lines and spots. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
