Cladostachys frutescens

Cladostachys frutescens, commonly known as the red-flowered ironwood, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woody structure and small, clustered flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round foliage.

Cladostachys frutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
