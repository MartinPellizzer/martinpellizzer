Crotalaria nana var. nana

Crotalaria nana var. nana, commonly known as dwarf rattlebox, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features small, yellow flowers clustered in racemes, which are surrounded by distinctive, inflated seed pods. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small leaflets that help in photosynthesis.

Crotalaria nana var. nana is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
