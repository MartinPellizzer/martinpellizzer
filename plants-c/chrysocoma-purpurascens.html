Chrysocoma purpurascens

Chrysocoma purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leaved chrysocoma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its green stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
