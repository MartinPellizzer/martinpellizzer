Crinum baumii

Crinum baumii, commonly known as the African lily, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant is part of the Amaryllidaceae family, which includes other ornamental and medicinal plants. Crinum baumii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its bulbs are toxic, so care should be taken when handling the plant.
