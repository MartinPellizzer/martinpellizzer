Centaurea panormitana subsp. seguenzae

Centaurea panormitana subsp.

seguenzae, commonly known as the Seguenza's Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a perennial herb characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces bright blue or purple flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in parts of its native range.
