Caleana major

Caleana major, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a striking Australian plant characterized by its unique, spider-like floral structure.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to the eastern regions of Australia. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, showy flower that resembles a spider in appearance. Its flowers are typically pink or red, with a long, narrow spur that houses the nectar.

Caleana major thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests or along stream banks.
