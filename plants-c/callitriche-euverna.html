Callitriche euverna

Callitriche euverna, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Callitrichaceae.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wetlands. The plant has small, floating or submerged leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. Its flowers are usually tiny, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Callitriche euverna plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
