Crassula albiflora

Crassula albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped white flowers during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula albiflora is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact growth and ornamental appeal.
