Cliffortia hirta

Cliffortia hirta, commonly known as the woolly cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features dense, woolly foliage that gives it its common name, and it produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to thrive in coastal climates with moderate rainfall.

Cliffortia hirta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
