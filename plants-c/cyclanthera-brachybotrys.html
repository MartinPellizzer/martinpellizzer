Cyclanthera brachybotrys

Cyclanthera brachybotrys, commonly known as the short-fruited bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces small, oval fruits with a bitter taste, often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are hairy and trailing.
