Crassula exilis subsp. exilis

Crassula exilis subsp.

exilis, commonly known as the Littleleaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering, making it ideal for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its compact size and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
