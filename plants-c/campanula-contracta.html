Campanula contracta

Campanula contracta, commonly known as the narrowleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows and woodland edges. The plant features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are bell-shaped, blue to violet in color, and bloom in late summer.

Campanula contracta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
