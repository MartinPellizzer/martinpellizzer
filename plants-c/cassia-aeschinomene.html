Cassia aeschinomene

Cassia aeschinomene, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several ovate to lance-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract a variety of pollinators such as bees and birds.

Cassia aeschinomene is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and can thrive in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.
