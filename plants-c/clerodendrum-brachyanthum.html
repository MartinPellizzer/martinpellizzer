Clerodendrum brachyanthum

Clerodendrum brachyanthum, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
