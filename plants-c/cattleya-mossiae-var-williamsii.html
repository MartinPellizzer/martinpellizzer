Cattleya mossiae var. williamsii

Cattleya mossiae var. williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and broad, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and long-lasting flowers.
