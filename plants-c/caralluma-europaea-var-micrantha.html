Caralluma europaea var. micrantha

Caralluma europaea var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered nightshade, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or dry soils, often in Mediterranean climates. The plant features succulent stems and leaves, adapted to arid conditions. Its small, star-shaped flowers are usually white or pale purple, blooming in late spring.

It is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
