Cattleya × suavior

Cattleya × suavior, commonly known as the Suavior orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines traits from various Cattleya species.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, warm environments. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with intricate patterns and ruffled edges. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive in less-than-ideal conditions.

The Suavior orchid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
