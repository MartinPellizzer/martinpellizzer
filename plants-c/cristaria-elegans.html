Cristaria elegans

Cristaria elegans, commonly known as the elegant starflower, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit, often reaching heights of only a few inches, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Cristaria elegans is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
