Ceropegia chortophylla

Ceropegia chortophylla, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for collectors and gardeners.
