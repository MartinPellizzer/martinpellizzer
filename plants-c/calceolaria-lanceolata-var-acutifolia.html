Calceolaria lanceolata var. acutifolia

Calceolaria lanceolata var. acutifolia, commonly known as the acute-leaved slipper orchid, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features striking, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically orange or yellow with a distinctive hooded lip. The plant has lanceolate, acute leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
