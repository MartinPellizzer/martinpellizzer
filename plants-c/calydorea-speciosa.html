Calydorea speciosa

Calydorea speciosa, commonly known as the showy calydorea, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, silky texture.

Calydorea speciosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
