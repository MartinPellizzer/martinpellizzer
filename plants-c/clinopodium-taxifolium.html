Clinopodium taxifolium

Clinopodium taxifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved betony, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in whorled clusters, and bloom from June to August.

Clinopodium taxifolium is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky habitats.
