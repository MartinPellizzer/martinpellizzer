Crassula sericea

Crassula sericea, commonly known as the silver crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Crassula sericea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
