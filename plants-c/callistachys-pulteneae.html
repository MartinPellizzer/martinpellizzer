Callistachys pulteneae

Callistachys pulteneae, commonly known as the pink-flowered hakea, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and distinctive pink or purple flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Callistachys pulteneae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
