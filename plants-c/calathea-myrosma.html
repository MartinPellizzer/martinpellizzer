Calathea myrosma

Calathea myrosma, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. The leaves are typically oval to lance-shaped and exhibit a unique ability to fold shut at night, a trait known as nyctinasty. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
