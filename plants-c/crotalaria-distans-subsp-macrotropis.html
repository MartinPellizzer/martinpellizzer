Crotalaria distans subsp. macrotropis

Crotalaria distans subsp.

macrotropis, commonly known as the broad-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmately lobed leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its seeds are enclosed in a pod and dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in arid and semi-arid environments.
