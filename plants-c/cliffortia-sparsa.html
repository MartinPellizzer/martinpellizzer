Cliffortia sparsa

Cliffortia sparsa, commonly known as the sparse cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is a perennial shrub that thrives in full sun and is well adapted to arid conditions.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
