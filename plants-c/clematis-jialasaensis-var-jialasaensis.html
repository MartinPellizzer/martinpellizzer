Clematis jialasaensis var. jialasaensis

Clematis jialasaensis var. jialasaensis, commonly known as the Jialasa clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, typically white or pale blue flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces follicles as its fruit type.

It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
