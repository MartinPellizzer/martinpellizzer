Croton mentiens

Croton mentiens, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright red to deep green and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
