Cnestis tomentosa

Cnestis tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy cnestis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of about 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, round seeds.

The species is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to humid tropical climates.
