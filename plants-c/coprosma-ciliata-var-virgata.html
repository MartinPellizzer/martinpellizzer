Coprosma ciliata var. virgata

Coprosma ciliata var. virgata, commonly known as the silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers, which are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
