Cerbera musculiformis

Cerbera musculiformis, commonly known as the muscle tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a unique, muscular appearance due to its thick, fleshy stems and broad, dark green leaves. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
