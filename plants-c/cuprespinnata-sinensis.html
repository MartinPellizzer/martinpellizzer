Cuprespinnata sinensis

Cuprespinnata sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight.

Cuprespinnata sinensis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
