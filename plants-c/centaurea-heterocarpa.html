Centaurea heterocarpa

Centaurea heterocarpa, commonly known as the heterocarp centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has slender, branching stems. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

The fruit is a small, compressed achene, which is adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.
