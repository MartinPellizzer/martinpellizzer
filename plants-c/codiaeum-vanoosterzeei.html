Codiaeum vanoosterzeei

Codiaeum vanoosterzeei, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red, orange, and yellow to green, often displaying striking patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Codiaeum vanoosterzeei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
