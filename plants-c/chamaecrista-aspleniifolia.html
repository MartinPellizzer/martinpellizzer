Chamaecrista aspleniifolia

Chamaecrista aspleniifolia, commonly known as the fernleaf burr legume, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are palmately compound, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
