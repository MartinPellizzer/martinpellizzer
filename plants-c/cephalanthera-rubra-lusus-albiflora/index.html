Cephalanthera Rubra Lusus Albiflora

Red helleborine, scientifically known as Cephalanthera rubra lusus albiflora, is a rare and elegant orchid native to certain regions of Europe. This plant is characterized by its striking red flowers and distinctive white-veined leaves, making it a standout in its natural habitat. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forest floors, where it relies on specific fungal associations for nourishment. Due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically significant plant.
