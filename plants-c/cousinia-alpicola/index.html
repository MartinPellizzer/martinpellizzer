Cousinia Alpicola

Cousinia alpicola is a hardy plant native to high altitude regions of the Alps and surrounding areas. It thrives in cold, rocky environments where few other plants can survive. This species is known for its compact growth and dense foliage that helps it withstand harsh weather conditions. Its flowers, typically pink or purple, bloom in early summer and attract pollinators. Cousinia alpicola plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems in its mountainous habitat.
