Cherleria verna var. juressi

Cherleria verna var. juressi, commonly known as the spring cherleria, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with five petals and a showy appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.
