Clerodendrum adenocalyx

Clerodendrum adenocalyx, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, lantern-shaped red bracts that enclose small, pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and square stems. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture, while the stems are woody and can grow up to several meters in height.
