Cimicifuga japonica var. peltata

Cimicifuga japonica var. peltata, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features tall, slender stems that bear large, white, lacecap flowers in late summer. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate lobes, giving it a distinctive, fern-like appearance. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
