Cymbidium giganteum var. discolor

Cymbidium giganteum var. discolor, commonly known as the Giant Orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in diameter, with a distinctive coloration pattern of white and pink petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright but indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its long-lasting blooms and elegant appearance make it a prized addition to botanical gardens and collections.
