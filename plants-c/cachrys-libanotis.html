Cachrys libanotis

Cachrys libanotis, commonly known as the Lebanon cedar, is a coniferous tree native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The tree has scale-like leaves that are tightly arranged along the branches. It produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in ancient architecture.
