Connarus negrensis

Connarus negrensis, commonly known as the Negrense Connarus, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Connaraceae, which includes shrubs and small trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.
