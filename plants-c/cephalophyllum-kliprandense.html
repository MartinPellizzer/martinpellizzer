Cephalophyllum kliprandense

Cephalophyllum kliprandense, commonly known as the Kliprand candelabra, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its branching, cylindrical stems that resemble a candlestick, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
