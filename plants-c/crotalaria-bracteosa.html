Crotalaria bracteosa

Crotalaria bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteosa crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow or orange flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Crotalaria bracteosa is also noted for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
