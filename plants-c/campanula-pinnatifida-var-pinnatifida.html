Campanula pinnatifida var. pinnatifida

Campanula pinnatifida var. pinnatifida, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem, giving it a delicate, airy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
