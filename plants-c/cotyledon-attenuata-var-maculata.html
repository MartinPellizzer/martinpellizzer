Cotyledon attenuata var. maculata

Cotyledon attenuata var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
