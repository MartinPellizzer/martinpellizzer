Calanthe mannii

Calanthe mannii, commonly known as the Malayan calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has erect, leafy stems and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Calanthe mannii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is found in shaded, moist forest environments.
