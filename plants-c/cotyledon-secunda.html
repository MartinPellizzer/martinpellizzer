Cotyledon secunda

Cotyledon secunda, commonly known as the "Cape Sunset" or "Cotyledon sunset," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to bright sunlight. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently.

Cotyledon secunda is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
