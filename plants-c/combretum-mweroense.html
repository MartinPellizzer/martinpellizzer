Combretum mweroense

Combretum mweroense, commonly known as the Wero bush.

It is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa, particularly found in Tanzania and southern Kenya. The plant belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in moist, wooded areas and is adapted to tropical climates.

The bark and leaves have been used traditionally in local medicine for their potential health benefits.
