Chamaesyce orbiculata

Chamaesyce orbiculata, commonly known as the roundleaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features small, orbicular (rounded) leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in clusters, blooming in early spring.

It is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal dunes or open woodlands.
