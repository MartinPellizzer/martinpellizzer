Coprosma glabrata

Coprosma glabrata, commonly known as smooth coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and dark green, oval-shaped leaves. Coprosma glabrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting native wildlife.
