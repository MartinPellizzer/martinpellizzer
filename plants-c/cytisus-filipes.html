Cytisus filipes

Cytisus filipes, commonly known as the slender broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or coastal gardens.

Cytisus filipes is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
