Cymbidium teretipetiolatum

Cymbidium teretipetiolatum, commonly known as the slender petiole cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with numerous fragrant, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
