Clerodendrum phlomidis var. rubrum

Clerodendrum phlomidis var. rubrum, commonly known as the red flame clerodendrum, is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small, yellowish flowers, creating a visually dramatic display. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and often hairy foliage. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
