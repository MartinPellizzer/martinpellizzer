Coleus homblei

Coleus homblei, commonly known as the Hombli's coleus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
