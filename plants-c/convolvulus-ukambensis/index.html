Convolvulus Ukambensis

Convolvulus ukambensis is a striking flowering plant native to eastern Africa. It is known for its vibrant pink to purple blooms and heart-shaped leaves. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its climbing habit allows it to form dense foliage, often used as a decorative ground cover. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not managed properly.
