Crinum ugentii

Crinum ugentii, commonly known as the Ugenti crinum, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that grow from a bulbous stem, typical of many amaryllid species. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum ugentii is also noted for its strong fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
