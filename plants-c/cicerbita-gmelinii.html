Cicerbita gmelinii

Cicerbita gmelinii, commonly known as the European bittercress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branching stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its fruits are dry pods that contain several tiny, rounded seeds.

Cicerbita gmelinii is typically found in moist soils along riverbanks, meadows, and disturbed areas.
