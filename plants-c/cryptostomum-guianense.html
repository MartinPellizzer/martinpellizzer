Cryptostomum guianense

Cryptostomum guianense, commonly known as the Guianese cryptostomum, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to South America, particularly found in Guyana and parts of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
