Cyclamen calcareum

Cyclamen calcareum, commonly known as the limestone cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers cool, moist conditions, often growing in rocky or limestone-rich environments. Its blooming period typically occurs in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Cyclamen calcareum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in suitable conditions.
