Centaurea polyacantha f. pallida

Centaurea polyacantha f. pallida, commonly known as the pale centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense heads, with pale blue to white petals and prominent yellow centers.

The pale centaury is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
