Calceolaria Biflora Var. Obtusifolia

Calceolaria biflora var. obtusifolia is a unique and striking plant known for its intricate, hood-like flowers. Native to the Andes mountains, this variant thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist climates. Its foliage is characterized by broad, velvety leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. The plant is popular among horticulturists for its ability to bloom in a variety of conditions when cared for properly. With its distinctive appearance and adaptability, it stands out as a fascinating addition to any botanical collection.
