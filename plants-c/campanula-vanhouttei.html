Campanula vanhouttei

Campanula vanhouttei, commonly known as Vanhoutte's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
