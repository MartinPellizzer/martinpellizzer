Convolvulus ser

Convolvulus ser, commonly known as the common bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its twining stems that often climb or trail along the ground. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

Convolvulus ser is known for its extensive root system, which allows it to persist and spread aggressively in various habitats.
