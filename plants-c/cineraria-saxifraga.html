Cineraria saxifraga

Cineraria saxifraga, commonly known as the rock cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of blue or violet flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically gray-green with a fuzzy texture, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

Cineraria saxifraga is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
