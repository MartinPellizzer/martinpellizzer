Cotoneaster glaucophyllus var. meiophyllus

Cotoneaster glaucophyllus var. meiophyllus, commonly known as the silver-leaf cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically a silvery-green color on the upper surface and a lighter green underneath. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

This variety is valued for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
