Calanthe truncicola

Calanthe truncicola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, showy blooms. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe truncicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
