Cassia saxatilis

Cassia saxatilis, commonly known as rock cassia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in rocky and well-drained soils, often found in arid or semi-arid regions. It is drought-tolerant and can withstand harsh environmental conditions.

Cassia saxatilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
