Cattleya labiata var. rochellensis

Cattleya labiata var. rochellensis, commonly known as the Rochellensis Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

This variety is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its flowers typically feature a combination of pink, white, and purple hues, with a distinctive lip that adds to its ornamental appeal. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, producing new shoots from the base of the old ones.

It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to flourish.
