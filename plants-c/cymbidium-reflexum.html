Cymbidium reflexum

Cymbidium reflexum, commonly known as the reflexed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. Its inflorescence is often curved, giving it its common name.

Cymbidium reflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
