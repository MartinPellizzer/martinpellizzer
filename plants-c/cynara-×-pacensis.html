Cynara × pacensis

Cynara × pacensis, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky blue or purple flower heads that resemble a globe, giving it its common name. The plant features silver-gray, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
