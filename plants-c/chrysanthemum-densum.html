Chrysanthemum densum

Chrysanthemum densum, commonly known as the dense chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China and Korea.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in the fall, producing a range of colors including white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture and appearance.
