Chrysosplenium nagasei var. nagasei

Chrysosplenium nagasei var. nagasei, commonly known as the Nagase's golden spleenwort, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers. Its leaves are usually covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs, giving them a velvety appearance.

This variety is native to Japan and is noted for its ornamental value in horticulture.
