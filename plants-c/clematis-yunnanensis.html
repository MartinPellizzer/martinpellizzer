Clematis yunnanensis

Clematis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, often with a white or pink throat. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires a support structure for its vigorous growth.

Clematis yunnanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
