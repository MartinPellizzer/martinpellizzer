Campanula hostii

Campanula hostii, commonly known as the Hosta bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Europe.

It features striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Campanula hostii is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
