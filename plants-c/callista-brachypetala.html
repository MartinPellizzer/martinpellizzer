Callista brachypetala

Callista brachypetala, commonly known as the broad-leaved callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Callista brachypetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
