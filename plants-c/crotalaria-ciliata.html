Crotalaria ciliata

Crotalaria ciliata, commonly known as the velvetleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive velvety leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings. It typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter.

Crotalaria ciliata is also noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
