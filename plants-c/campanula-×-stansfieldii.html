Campanula × stansfieldii

Campanula × stansfieldii, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
