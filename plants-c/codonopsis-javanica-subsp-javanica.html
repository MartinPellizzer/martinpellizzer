Codonopsis javanica subsp. javanica

Codonopsis javanica subsp.

javanica, commonly known as the Javan codonopsis, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of bell-shaped flowers.
