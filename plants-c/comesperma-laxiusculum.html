Comesperma laxiusculum

Comesperma laxiusculum, commonly known as the slender comesperma, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Australia.

It features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
