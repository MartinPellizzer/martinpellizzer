Commelina hasskarlii

Commelina hasskarlii, commonly known as the Indian spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Commelina genus within the family Commelinaceae. The plant features striking blue or violet flowers with three petals and a distinctive three-lobed bract. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its foliage is broad and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
