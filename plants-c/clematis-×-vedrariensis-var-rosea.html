Clematis × vedrariensis var. rosea

Clematis × vedrariensis var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered Clematis, is a hybrid vine prized for its showy, fragrant flowers.

It features large, semi-double blooms in shades of pink and white, often with a soft, rose-like appearance. The plant has compound, trifoliate leaves that are typically lobed and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and vigorous climbing habit.
