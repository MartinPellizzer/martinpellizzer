Cestrum parqui f. heterophyllum

Cestrum parqui f. heterophyllum, commonly known as the purple cestrum, is a compact shrub native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
