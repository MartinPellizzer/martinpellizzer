Ceropegia hians var. striata

Ceropegia hians var. striata, commonly known as the zigzag string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often marked with white or pale green stripes. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
