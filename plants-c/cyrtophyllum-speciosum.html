Cyrtophyllum speciosum

Cyrtophyllum speciosum, commonly known as the beauty leaf, is a striking evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a waxy texture that enhances their visual appeal. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by ornate, papery bracts, which contribute to its ornamental value.

This plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
