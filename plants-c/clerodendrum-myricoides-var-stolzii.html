Clerodendrum myricoides var. stolzii

Clerodendrum myricoides var. stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's flame-of-the-forest, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
