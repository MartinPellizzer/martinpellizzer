Centaurea × aschersoniana

Centaurea × aschersoniana, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, bright red flowers with a distinctive yellow center, resembling a gladiolus. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces seed heads that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its cultivation in various climates.
