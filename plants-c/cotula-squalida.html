Cotula squalida

Cotula squalida, commonly known as the yellow daisy or small daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, bright yellow flowers surrounded by pale green leaves. Cotula squalida is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
