Coryanthes speciosa var. eximia

Coryanthes speciosa var. eximia, commonly known as the giant boat orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its large, boat-shaped flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant has a unique inflorescence with a single, showy flower that is often adorned with intricate, colorful patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
