Croton gossypiifolius var. heterophyllus

Croton gossypiifolius var. heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from elliptical to lobed or even palmate. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are often glossy and can vary in color from green to reddish hues.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
