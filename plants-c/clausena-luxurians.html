Clausena luxurians

Clausena luxurians, commonly known as the wild mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces edible, reddish-purple fruits that are rich in antioxidants and have a tart, sweet flavor. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
