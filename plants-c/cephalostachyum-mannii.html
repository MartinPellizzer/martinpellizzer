Cephalostachyum mannii

Cephalostachyum mannii, commonly known as the African bamboo, is a member of the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 5 meters in height. Cephalostachyum mannii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
