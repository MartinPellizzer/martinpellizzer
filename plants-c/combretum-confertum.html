Combretum confertum

Combretum confertum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and its striking, fiery red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a leathery texture, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
