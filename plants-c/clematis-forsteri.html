Clematis forsteri

Clematis forsteri, commonly known as the New Zealand clematis, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a deciduous vine that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are usually white or pale blue with five petals. It produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain several seeds.

Clematis forsteri thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
