Croton fuscescens

Croton fuscescens, commonly known as the black croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and striking orange to red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
