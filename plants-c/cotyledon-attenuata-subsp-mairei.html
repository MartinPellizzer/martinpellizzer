Cotyledon attenuata subsp. mairei

Cotyledon attenuata subsp.

mairei, commonly known as the Maire's African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture.

The leaves often display striking patterns of red, purple, or pink, which are more pronounced in direct sunlight. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months.

This species is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
