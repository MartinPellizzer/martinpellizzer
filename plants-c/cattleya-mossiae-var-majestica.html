Cattleya mossiae var. majestica

Cattleya mossiae var. majestica, commonly known as the Majestic Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces showy blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and garden displays due to its ornamental value.

This variety is known for its strong fragrance and is often used in floral arrangements.
