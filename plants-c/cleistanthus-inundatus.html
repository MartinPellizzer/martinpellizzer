Cleistanthus inundatus

Cleistanthus inundatus, commonly known as the water cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows near water bodies, showcasing its aquatic adaptation. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly glossy surface.

Cleistanthus inundatus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
