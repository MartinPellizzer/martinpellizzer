Cleisostoma flexum

Cleisostoma flexum, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a sweet, spicy aroma. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Cleisostoma flexum is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and unique fragrance.
