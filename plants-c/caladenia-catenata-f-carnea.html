Caladenia catenata f. carnea

Caladenia catenata f. carnea, commonly known as the Carnation Spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a pinkish or reddish hue, which is particularly striking. It typically grows in sandy soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

This orchid is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and beautiful blooms.
