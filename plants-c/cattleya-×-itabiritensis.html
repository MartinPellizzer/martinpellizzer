Cattleya × itabiritensis

Cattleya × itabiritensis, commonly known as the Itabira Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
