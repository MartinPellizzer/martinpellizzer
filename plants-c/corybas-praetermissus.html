Corybas praetermissus

Corybas praetermissus, commonly known as the slender helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive, elongated pouch at the base.

Corybas praetermissus is known for its limited distribution and is often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
