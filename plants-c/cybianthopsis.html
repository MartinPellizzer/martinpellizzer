Cybianthopsis

Cybianthopsis, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. They are characterized by their compact, often epiphytic growth habit and their striking, star-shaped flowers. Cybianthopsis species typically have showy, brightly colored petals and sepals that are arranged in a radial pattern.

The genus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique and beautiful blooms.
