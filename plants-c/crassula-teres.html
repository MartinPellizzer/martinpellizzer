Crassula teres

Crassula teres, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded leaves that are fleshy and often a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula teres is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
