Cymbidium finlaysonianum

Cymbidium finlaysonianum, commonly known as the Finlayson's orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features tall, branched inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
