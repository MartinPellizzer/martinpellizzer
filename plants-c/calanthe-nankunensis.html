Calanthe nankunensis

Calanthe nankunensis, commonly known as the Nankun orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically has linear to lanceolate leaves and blooms in late spring or early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
