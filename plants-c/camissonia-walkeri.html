Camissonia walkeri

Camissonia walkeri, commonly known as the Walker's camissonia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Camissoniaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils in open habitats such as grasslands and slopes. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, while its flowers are small and often white or pink in color.
