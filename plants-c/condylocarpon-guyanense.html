Condylocarpon guyanense

It is characterized by its foliose thallus, which is typically yellowish to orange in color. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship between a photosynthetic partner, usually a green alga, and a fungal partner from the genus Condylocarpon. It is often found growing on rocky surfaces in humid tropical environments.
