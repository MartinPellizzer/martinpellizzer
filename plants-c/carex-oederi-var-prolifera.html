Carex oederi var. prolifera

Carex oederi var. prolifera, commonly known as the Oeder's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This variety is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically brown or reddish in color.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
