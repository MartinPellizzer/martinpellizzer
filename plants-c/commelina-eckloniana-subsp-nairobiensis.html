Commelina eckloniana subsp. nairobiensis

Commelina eckloniana subsp.

nairobiensis, commonly known as the Nairobi Commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to East Africa. It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
