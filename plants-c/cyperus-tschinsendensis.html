Cyperus tschinsendensis

Cyperus tschinsendensis, commonly known as the Tschinsendens Cyperus, is a species of sedge native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, preferring moist soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
