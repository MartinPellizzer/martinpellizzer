Croton poomae

Croton poomae, commonly known as the Poomae croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
