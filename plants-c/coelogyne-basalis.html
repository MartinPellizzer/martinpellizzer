Coelogyne basalis

Coelogyne basalis, commonly known as the black orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, elongated pseudobulbs and leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne basalis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
