Coniogramme japonica subsp. gracilis

Coniogramme japonica subsp.

gracilis, commonly known as the slender Japanese coniogramme, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia. It features delicate, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or greenish flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant foliage rather than its flowers.
