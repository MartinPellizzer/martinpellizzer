Chrysophyllum africanum var. aubrevillei

Chrysophyllum africanum var. aubrevillei, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits with a yellow or orange skin and a sweet, starchy pulp.
