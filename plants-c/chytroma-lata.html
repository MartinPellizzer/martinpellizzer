Chytroma lata

Chytroma lata, commonly known as the broadleaf chytroma, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant is notable for its complex vascular system, which allows for efficient nutrient transport. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Chytroma lata is primarily found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, well-drained soils.
