Cineraria incisa

Cineraria incisa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes. Its foliage is often used in horticultural displays due to its ornamental appearance.

Cineraria incisa is a perennial species that produces flowers throughout the growing season.
