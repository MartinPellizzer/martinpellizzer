Calceolaria foliosa

Calceolaria foliosa, commonly known as the leafy slipper orchid, is a striking member of the Calceolaria genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored, typically in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.

Calceolaria foliosa is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a distinctive and visually appealing flowering plant.
