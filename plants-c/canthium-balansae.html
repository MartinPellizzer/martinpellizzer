Canthium balansae

Canthium balansae, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes many species with milky sap. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Canthium balansae is adapted to well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and savannas.
