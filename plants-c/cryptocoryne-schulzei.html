Cryptocoryne schulzei

Cryptocoryne schulzei, commonly known as the red cryptocoryne, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange-brown leaves that are often speckled with dark spots. This plant prefers moderate to high water temperatures and is often used in planted aquariums for its aesthetic appeal. It has a rhizomatic growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense underwater foliage.

Cryptocoryne schulzei is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
