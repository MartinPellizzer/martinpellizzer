Cheiridopsis delphinoides

Cheiridopsis delphinoides, commonly known as the blue ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, mat-forming growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of blue and purple during the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
