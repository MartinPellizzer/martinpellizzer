Coleostephus clausonis

Coleostephus clausonis, commonly known as the white gentian, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which are typically tubular in shape. The plant grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
