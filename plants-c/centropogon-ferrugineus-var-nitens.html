Centropogon ferrugineus var. nitens

Centropogon ferrugineus var. nitens, commonly known as the rusty false dawnflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
