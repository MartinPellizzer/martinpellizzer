Cistus rotundifolius

Cistus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaved rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with rounded, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, pink or purple flowers in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rocky or coastal environments.

Cistus rotundifolius is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in herbal medicine.
