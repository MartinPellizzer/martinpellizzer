Callerya sumatrana

Callerya sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran callerya, is a flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. Callerya sumatrana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
