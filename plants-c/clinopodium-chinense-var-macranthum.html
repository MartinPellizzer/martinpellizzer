Clinopodium chinense var. macranthum

Clinopodium chinense var. macranthum, commonly known as Chinese oregano or large-flowered wood betony, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features aromatic leaves with a mint-like scent and produces showy, violet-blue flowers in whorled clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its foliage is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.

The species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
