Calanthe textorii

Calanthe textorii, commonly known as the Chinese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Calanthe textorii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
