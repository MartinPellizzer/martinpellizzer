Cayaponia multiglandulosa

Cayaponia multiglandulosa, commonly known as the Mexican cayaponia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
