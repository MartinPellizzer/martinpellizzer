Cremanium laurinum

Cremanium laurinum, commonly known as the silverleaf sterculia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
