Coryphantha sneedii var. albicolumnaria

Coryphantha sneedii var. albicolumnaria, commonly known as the white-columned hedgehog cactus, is a unique species native to the Chihuahuan Desert in Mexico.

It is characterized by its spiny, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a layer of white, waxy hairs, giving it a distinct appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a splash of color to its arid habitat. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry conditions with minimal water requirements.
