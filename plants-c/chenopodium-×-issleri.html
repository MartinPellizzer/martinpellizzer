Chenopodium × issleri

Chenopodium × issleri, commonly known as the hybrid goosefoot, is a member of the Amaranthaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, known as bloom. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
