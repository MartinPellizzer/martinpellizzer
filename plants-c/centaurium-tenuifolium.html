Centaurium tenuifolium

Centaurium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, erect stems and small, bright blue or purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
