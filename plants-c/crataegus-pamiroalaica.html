Crataegus pamiroalaica

Crataegus pamiroalaica, commonly known as the Pamir hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, particularly the Pamir Mountains. This shrub or small tree typically grows at high altitudes, often in rocky or alpine environments. It produces small, white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the autumn.

The plant is adapted to cold climates and is characterized by its thorny branches and dense foliage.
