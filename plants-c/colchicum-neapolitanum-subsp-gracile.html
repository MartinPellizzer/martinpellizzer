Colchicum neapolitanum subsp. gracile

Colchicum neapolitanum subsp.

gracile, commonly known as the slender Naples colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color, blooming in late summer.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive, helmet-shaped cymes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
