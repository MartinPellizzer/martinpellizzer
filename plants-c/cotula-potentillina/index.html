Cotula Potentillina

Cotula potentillina is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its delicate, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for gardens. Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular ornamental plant. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more showy flowers.
