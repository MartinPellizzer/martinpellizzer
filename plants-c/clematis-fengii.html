Clematis fengii

Clematis fengii, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits that mature to a dark blue or black color. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis fengii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
