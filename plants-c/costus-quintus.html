Costus quintus

Costus quintus, commonly known as the zigzag ginger, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant is characterized by its tall, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in spike-like clusters.
