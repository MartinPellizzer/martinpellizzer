Carduus nutans var. coloratus

Carduus nutans var. coloratus, commonly known as the showy carducum, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and large, composite flower heads that range from yellow to reddish hues. The plant produces numerous small, bright yellow flowers arranged in a spherical shape, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking visual appeal.
