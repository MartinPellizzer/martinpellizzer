Clerodendrum pulchrum

Clerodendrum pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences. The flowers are typically pink or red, with a tubular corolla that attracts pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also noted for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
