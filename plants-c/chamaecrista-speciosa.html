Chamaecrista speciosa

Chamaecrista speciosa, commonly known as the showy crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and sandy habitats.
