Clematis songorica var. integrifolia

Clematis songorica var. integrifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf clematis, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with entire margins, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscaping.
