Cistus × campsii

Cistus × campsii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and coastal landscapes. Its leaves are typically leathery and aromatic, and it produces small, woody capsules as fruit.

Cistus × campsii is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to dry, rocky environments.
