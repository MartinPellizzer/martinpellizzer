Cremocarpon bernieri

Cremocarpon bernieri, commonly known as the Corsican lichen, is a foliose lichen belonging to the family Parmeliaceae.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to greenish thallus with a distinctive, wrinkled surface. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with a green alga, which provides photosynthetic capabilities. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found on bark and soil in temperate regions.

Its chemical composition includes various secondary metabolites that contribute to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
