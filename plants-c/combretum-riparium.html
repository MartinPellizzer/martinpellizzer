Combretum riparium

Combretum riparium, commonly known as watercombretum, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, sandy soils near water bodies such as rivers and lakes.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to wetland environments.
