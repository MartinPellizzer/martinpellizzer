Calamus distichus var. shangsiensis

Calamus distichus var. shangsiensis, commonly known as the Shangsiensis arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arales.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Malaysia. This variety is noted for its adaptability to wet, tropical environments and its role in local ecosystems.
