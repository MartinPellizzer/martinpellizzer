Cavendishia dendrophila

Cavendishia dendrophila, commonly known as the tree orchid, is a unique species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a member of the orchid family and is characterized by its tall, tree-like growth habit, often reaching heights of several meters. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns and a pleasant fragrance. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green canopy.

Cavendishia dendrophila thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish.
