Carex × tenebricans

Carex × tenebricans, commonly known as the shadow sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or wetlands.

Carex × tenebricans is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in natural habitats.
