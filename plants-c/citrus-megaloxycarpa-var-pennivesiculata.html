Citrus megaloxycarpa var. pennivesiculata

Citrus megaloxycarpa var. pennivesiculata, commonly known as the giant citron.

This variant is characterized by its large, thick-skinned fruits that can weigh several kilograms. The leaves are broad and dark green, with a slightly serrated margin. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
