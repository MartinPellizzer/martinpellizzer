Cigdemia cilicica

Cigdemia cilicica, commonly known as the Cilician rose, is a shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and pink to white flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often growing on slopes and cliffs.

Cigdemia cilicica is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
