Coeloglossum viride f. lancifolium

Coeloglossum viride f. lancifolium, commonly known as the green orchid or green spike orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and long, narrow leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and subtle floral display.
