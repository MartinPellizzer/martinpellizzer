Cyrtochilum Quinqueseriale

Cyrtochilum quinqueseriale is a rare and delicate orchid native to the high altitudes of the Andes. This plant is known for its striking violet-blue flowers and compact growth habit. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
