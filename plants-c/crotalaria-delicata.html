Crotalaria delicata

Crotalaria delicata, commonly known as the slender crown flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and small, palmately lobed leaves. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several elliptical seeds.

Crotalaria delicata is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
