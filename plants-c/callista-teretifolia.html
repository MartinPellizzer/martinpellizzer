Callista teretifolia

Callista teretifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive central disc surrounded by ray florets.

Callista teretifolia is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
