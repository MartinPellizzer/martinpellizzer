Crepidium propinquum

Crepidium propinquum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of the northern hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in a raceme, often with a slight fragrance.

Crepidium propinquum is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
