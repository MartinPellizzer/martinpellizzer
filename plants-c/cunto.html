Cunto

Cunto, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are attractive but toxic to humans and animals. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, often blooming in the spring.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
