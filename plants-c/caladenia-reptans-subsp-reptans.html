Caladenia reptans subsp. reptans

Caladenia reptans subsp.

reptans, commonly known as the snake's head orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia. It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

The plant produces a single, slender stem with a single, erect flower that is usually pink or purple in color. Its flower has a distinctive, elongated labellum that resembles a snake's head, giving it its common name.

It is a perennial species, with a short flowering period during the spring.
