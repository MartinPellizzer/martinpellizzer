Cooperia drummondiana var. chlorosolen

Cooperia drummondiana var. chlorosolen, commonly known as the golden cooperia, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit and distinctive golden-green foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland margins. Its leaves are narrow and slightly rolled, with a dense, fibrous structure.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native garden landscapes.
