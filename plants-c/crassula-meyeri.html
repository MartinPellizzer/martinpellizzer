Crassula meyeri

Crassula meyeri, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Money Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted for storing water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect sunlight.

Its compact growth habit makes it an ideal choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays.
