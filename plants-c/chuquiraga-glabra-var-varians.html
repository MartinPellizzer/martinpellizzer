Chuquiraga glabra var. varians

Chuquiraga glabra var. varians, commonly known as the smooth chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often in rocky or sandy soils. It has a low, spreading growth habit with hairy stems and narrow, linear leaves.

Chuquiraga glabra var. varians is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
