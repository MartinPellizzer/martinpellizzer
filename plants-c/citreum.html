Citreum

Citreum, commonly known as citronella, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant is widely cultivated for its essential oils, which are extracted from the leaves and stems. Citreum thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often used in gardens and as a natural pest repellent due to its strong aromatic properties.
