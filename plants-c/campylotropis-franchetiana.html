Campylotropis franchetiana

Campylotropis franchetiana, commonly known as the Chinese peavine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds.
