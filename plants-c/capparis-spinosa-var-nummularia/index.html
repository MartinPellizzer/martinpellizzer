Capparis Spinosa Var. Nummularia

Capparis spinosa var. nummularia is a unique variant of the common caper plant known for its distinctive leaf shape and ornamental value. This plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, making it a hardy choice for drought-tolerant gardens. Its small, rounded leaves give it a compact appearance, while its flowers add a splash of color during the growing season. Often used in Mediterranean landscapes, it is valued for both its beauty and its ability to withstand harsh conditions. Despite its tough exterior, this plant offers a delicate charm that makes it a favorite among gardeners.
