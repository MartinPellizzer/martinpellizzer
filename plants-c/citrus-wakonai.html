Citrus wakonai

Citrus wakonai, commonly known as the Japanese bitter orange, is a small evergreen tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small, round, and has a thick, leathery rind with a bitter taste. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
