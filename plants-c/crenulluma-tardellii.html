Crenulluma tardellii

Crenulluma tardellii, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes in South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

Crenulluma tardellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialized gardens.
