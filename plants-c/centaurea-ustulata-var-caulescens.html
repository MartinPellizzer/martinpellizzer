Centaurea ustulata var. caulescens

Centaurea ustulata var. caulescens, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, spiny seed pods.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
