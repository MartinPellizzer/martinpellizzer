Crotalaria stipularia f. oblongifolia

Crotalaria stipularia f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and distinctive yellow flowers with a prominent keel. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides. It is an annual or biennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds by wind or animal contact.
