Chamaerops elegans

Chamaerops elegans, commonly known as the slender palm or desert date, is a species of palm native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree with a slender trunk and a crown of pinnate leaves. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, round fruits that are often consumed by wildlife.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh climates.
