Clematis stanleyi var. tomentosa

Clematis stanleyi var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale blue, often with a slight pinkish tinge. The plant has a dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb trellises or walls.
