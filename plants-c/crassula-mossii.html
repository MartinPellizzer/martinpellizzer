Crassula mossii

Crassula mossii, commonly known as the Moss's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula mossii is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements.
