Cleome cleomoides

Cleome cleomoides, commonly known as the spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like inflorescences that consist of numerous small, white or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb with branching stems and palmate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in gardens and wild areas.
