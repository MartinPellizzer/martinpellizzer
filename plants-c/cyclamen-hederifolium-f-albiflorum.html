Cyclamen hederifolium f. albiflorum

Cyclamen hederifolium f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered ivy-leaved cyclamen, is a horticultural form of the ivy-leaved cyclamen.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves that resemble those of ivy. The plant produces clusters of pale white flowers with a distinctive red or purple stripe along the petals. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown in gardens and as a houseplant.
