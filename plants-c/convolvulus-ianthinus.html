Convolvulus ianthinus

Convolvulus ianthinus, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it suitable for various landscaping applications.
