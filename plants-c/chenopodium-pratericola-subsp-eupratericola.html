Chenopodium pratericola subsp. eupratericola

Chenopodium pratericola subsp.

eupratericola, commonly known as the Prater goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae. It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically alternate and ovate to lanceolate in shape.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are often surrounded by bracts. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

This subspecies is notable for its ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient and adaptable plant.
