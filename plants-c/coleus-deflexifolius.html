Coleus deflexifolius

Coleus deflexifolius, commonly known as the drooping coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that often curl downward, giving it its common name. It produces small, tubular flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
