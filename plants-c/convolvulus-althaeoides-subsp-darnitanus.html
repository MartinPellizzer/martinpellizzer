Convolvulus althaeoides subsp. darnitanus

Convolvulus althaeoides subsp.

darnitanus, commonly known as the Darnitan convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils.

The plant features slender stems and heart-shaped leaves, with small, funnel-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
