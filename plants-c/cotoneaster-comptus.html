Cotoneaster comptus

Cotoneaster comptus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that turn red in the fall, adding ornamental value to the landscape. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Cotoneaster comptus is also valued for its hardiness and ability to tolerate urban conditions.
