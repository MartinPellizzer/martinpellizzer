Corispermum elongatum var. intermedium

Corispermum elongatum var. intermedium, commonly known as the intermediate glassworts, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, salt marshes, and other saline habitats. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, hard seeds.
