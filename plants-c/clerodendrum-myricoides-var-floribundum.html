Clerodendrum myricoides var. floribundum

Clerodendrum myricoides var. floribundum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking, lantern-like red bracts that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are followed by the showy bracts. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting inflorescences.
