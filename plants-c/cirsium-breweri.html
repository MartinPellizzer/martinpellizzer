Cirsium breweri

Cirsium breweri, commonly known as the Breweri thistle, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and large, showy purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as slopes and meadows. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Cirsium breweri is noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for various insects.
