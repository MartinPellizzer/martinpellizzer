Clematis cirrhosa var. atava

Clematis cirrhosa var. atava, commonly known as the Atava Clematis, is a climbing vine native to parts of Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, twining stems and heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
