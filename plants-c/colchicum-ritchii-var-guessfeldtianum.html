Colchicum ritchii var. guessfeldtianum

Colchicum ritchii var. guessfeldtianum, commonly known as the Red Mountain Colchicum, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It produces striking, showy flowers that are typically red or reddish-purple in color, with a distinctive spurred shape. The plant features a large, tunicated bulb that stores nutrients for the growing season. Its foliage consists of broad, grass-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

This species is adapted to cold, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.
