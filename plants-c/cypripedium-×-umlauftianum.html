Cypripedium × umlauftianum

Cypripedium × umlauftianum, commonly known as the Umlauft lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Cypripedium genus.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive pouch-shaped lip, typical of the orchid family. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, including China and Korea. It typically grows in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and mountain slopes.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
