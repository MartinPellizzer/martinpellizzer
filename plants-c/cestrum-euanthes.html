Cestrum euanthes

Cestrum euanthes, commonly known as the night blooming cereus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant white flowers that open at night and close by morning. The plant belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. Its stems are often woody and can grow as shrubs or small trees.

Cestrum euanthes is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
