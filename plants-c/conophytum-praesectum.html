Conophytum praesectum

Conophytum praesectum, commonly known as the "Bottle Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its unique, fleshy, bottle-shaped leaves that store water. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Conophytum praesectum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience.
