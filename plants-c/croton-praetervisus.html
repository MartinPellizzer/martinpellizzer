Croton praetervisus

Croton praetervisus, commonly known as the false croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and diverse leaf structures. The plant features alternate, palmate leaves with a vibrant green color and prominent veins. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
