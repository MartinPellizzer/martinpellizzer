Cynoglossum pictum var. umbrosum

Cynoglossum pictum var. umbrosum, commonly known as the shadow lungwort, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features basal leaves that are rounded with a wavy margin and covered in fine, hairy textures. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming from late summer to early autumn. It thrives in shady, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its scientific name reflects its distinctive leaf patterns and its association with shaded habitats.
