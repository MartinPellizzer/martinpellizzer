Croton chamaepitys

Croton chamaepitys, commonly known as the lowland croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain seeds.

Croton chamaepitys is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage.
