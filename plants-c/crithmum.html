Crithmum

Crithmum, commonly known as sea fennel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in coastal areas with sandy or rocky soils. The plant features aromatic, fern-like leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Crithmum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
