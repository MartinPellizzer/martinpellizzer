Crassula alba var. parvisepala

Crassula alba var. parvisepala, commonly known as the Snowflake Crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are pale green with a slight bluish tint, adapted to store water. It thrives in well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
