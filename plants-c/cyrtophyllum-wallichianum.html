Cyrtophyllum wallichianum

Cyrtophyllum wallichianum, commonly known as the Himalayan snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows at high altitudes, often in cool, moist environments. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers that are often fragrant. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a leathery texture.

Cyrtophyllum wallichianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
