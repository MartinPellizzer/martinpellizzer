Cotoneaster antoninae

Cotoneaster antoninae, commonly known as the Philippine cotoneaster, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces red, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for local wildlife. Cotoneaster antoninae typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
