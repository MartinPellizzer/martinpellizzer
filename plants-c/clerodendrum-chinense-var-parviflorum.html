Clerodendrum chinense var. parviflorum

Clerodendrum chinense var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered Chinese clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in summer. Its ornamental value is due to its attractive foliage and showy inflorescences.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for.
