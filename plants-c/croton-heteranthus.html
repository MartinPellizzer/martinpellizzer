Croton heteranthus

Croton heteranthus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are typically alternate and ovate, with a smooth or slightly serrated edge.

Croton heteranthus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
