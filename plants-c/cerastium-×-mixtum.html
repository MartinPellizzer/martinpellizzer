Cerastium × mixtum

Cerastium × mixtum, commonly known as the hybrid snow in the snow.

It is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically appearing in early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its frost-resistant nature.

Cerastium × mixtum is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
