Chihuahuana

Chihuahuana, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and red. Chihuahuana thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
