Cymbidium codonanthum

Cymbidium codonanthum, commonly known as the Codonanthe orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with linear, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its delicate appearance.
