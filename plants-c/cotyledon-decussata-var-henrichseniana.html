Cotyledon decussata var. henrichseniana

Cotyledon decussata var. henrichseniana, commonly known as the Henrichsen's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which are characteristic of the Cotyledon genus. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements.
