Cranichis stachyodes

Cranichis stachyodes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or wetlands. Its flowering stems produce clusters of white or pale yellow blossoms that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Cranichis stachyodes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
