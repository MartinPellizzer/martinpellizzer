Cylichnanthus vaginatus

Cylichnanthus vaginatus, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Cylichnanthus vaginatus is notable for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative reproduction.
