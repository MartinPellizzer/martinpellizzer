Campanula linifolia var. ruscinonensis

Campanula linifolia var. ruscinonensis, commonly known as the Ruscinonensis bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Carpathian Mountains.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
