Cerastites hybridus

Cerastites hybridus, commonly known as the hybrid sandthorn, is a shrub native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually small and succulent, adapted to conserve water.

Cerastites hybridus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
