Cordia Grandifolia

Codia grandifolia is a striking tropical plant known for its large, showy flowers and vibrant foliage. Native to parts of Central and South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with plenty of sunlight. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that can reach several feet in length, making it a dramatic addition to any garden. Its blossoms, which range from yellow to red, attract a variety of pollinators including bees and birds. Despite its ornamental appeal, Cordia grandifolia is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
