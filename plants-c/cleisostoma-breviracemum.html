Cleisostoma breviracemum

Cleisostoma breviracemum, commonly known as the short-stemmed hooded orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, sympodial growth habit with short, fleshy stems and numerous small, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive hood-like structure covering the reproductive organs. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular specimen among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
