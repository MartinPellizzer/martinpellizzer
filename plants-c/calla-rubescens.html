Calla rubescens

Calla rubescens, commonly known as the red calla lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves and a showy, white spathe with a deep red or orange spadix, creating a dramatic floral display. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas. Its blooms typically appear in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens or indoor spaces.

Calla rubescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements.
