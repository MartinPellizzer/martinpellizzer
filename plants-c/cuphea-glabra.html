Cuphea glabra

Cuphea glabra, commonly known as the smooth cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. Cuphea glabra is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds.
