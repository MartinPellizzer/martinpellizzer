Cirsium × khekii

Cirsium × khekii, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to China, this plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands. Its prickly foliage serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
