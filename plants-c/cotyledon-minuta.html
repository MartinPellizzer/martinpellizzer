Cotyledon minuta

Cotyledon minuta, commonly known as the miniature cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Cotyledon minuta is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and attractive foliage.
