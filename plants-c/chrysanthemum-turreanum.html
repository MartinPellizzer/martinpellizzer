Chrysanthemum turreanum

Chrysanthemum turreanum, commonly known as the mountain chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
