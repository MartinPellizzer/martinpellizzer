Cirsium rigidum

Cirsium rigidum, commonly known as rigid thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with rough, hairy surfaces and prominent spines.

Cirsium rigidum is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
