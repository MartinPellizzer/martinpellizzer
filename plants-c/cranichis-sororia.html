Cranichis sororia

Cranichis sororia, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and its long, spiraled inflorescence of small, pale pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces seeds in capsules.

Cranichis sororia is a perennial herb that blooms from late spring to early summer.
