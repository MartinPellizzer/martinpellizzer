Coelogyne williamsii

Coelogyne williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne williamsii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
