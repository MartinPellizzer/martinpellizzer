Camellia longissima

Camellia longissima, commonly known as the long-stemmed camellia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its blooms. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Camellia longissima is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
