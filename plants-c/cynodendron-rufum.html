Cynodendron rufum

Cynodendron rufum, commonly known as the red-leafed cynodendron, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes species known for their medicinal properties. Cynodendron rufum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in some horticultural settings.
