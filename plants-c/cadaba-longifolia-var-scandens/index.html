Cadaba Longifolia Var. Scandens

Cadaba longifolia var. scandens is a unique and lesser-known plant species that thrives in specific tropical environments. This climbing variant of the Cadaba longifolia is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers and trailing stems that make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Native to regions in Southeast Asia, it requires well-drained soil and partial shade to flourish. Its ability to climb and spread makes it an excellent addition to hanging baskets or trellises. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in mainstream horticulture, making it a hidden gem for plant enthusiasts.
