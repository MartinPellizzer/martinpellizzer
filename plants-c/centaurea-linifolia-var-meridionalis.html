Centaurea linifolia var. meridionalis

Centaurea linifolia var. meridionalis, commonly known as the southern linifolia centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces striking blue to purple flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
