Crepis jacquinii

Crepis jacquinii, commonly known as the Jacob's grass or Jacob's thistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Crepis jacquinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
