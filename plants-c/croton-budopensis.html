Croton budopensis

Croton budopensis, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from red to yellow and orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by woody, spiky fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton budopensis thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
