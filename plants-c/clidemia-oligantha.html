Clidemia oligantha

Clidemia oligantha, commonly known as silverleaf sage, is a perennial shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Clidemia oligantha is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
