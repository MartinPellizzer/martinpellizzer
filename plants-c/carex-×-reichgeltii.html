Carex × reichgeltii

Carex × reichgeltii, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, grass-green leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or brown. This plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. It is valued in landscaping for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate waterlogged conditions.

Carex × reichgeltii is also known for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
