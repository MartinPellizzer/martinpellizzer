Citrus zeylanica

Citrus zeylanica, commonly known as the Java lemon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant produces small, yellow, sour fruits that are used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are dark green and have a rough texture, while its flowers are fragrant and white.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is often cultivated for its fruit and ornamental appeal.
