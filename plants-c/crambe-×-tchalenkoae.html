Crambe × tchalenkoae

Crambe × tchalenkoae, commonly known as the hybrid crambe, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a biennial or perennial plant native to South America, particularly Argentina. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a distinctive, branching structure. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed, and covered in a fine layer of pubescence.

Crambe × tchalenkoae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
