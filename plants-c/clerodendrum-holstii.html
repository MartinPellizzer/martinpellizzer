Clerodendrum holstii

Clerodendrum holstii, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual appeal to gardens. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clerodendrum holstii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be used in landscaping or as a container plant.
