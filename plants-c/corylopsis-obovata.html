Corylopsis obovata

Corylopsis obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed flame twisted, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features narrow, obovate leaves that are typically dark green and often have a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are characteristic of the Corylopsis genus. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and early blooming period.
