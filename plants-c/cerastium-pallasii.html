Cerastium pallasii

Cerastium pallasii, commonly known as the snow-in-summer, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

Cerastium pallasii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
