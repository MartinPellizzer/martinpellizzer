Crinum belleymei

Crinum belleymei, commonly known as the Yellow Crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, arranged in umbels. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often broad and slightly wavy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several months.
