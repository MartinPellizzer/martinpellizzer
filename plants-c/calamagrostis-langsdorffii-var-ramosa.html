Calamagrostis langsdorffii var. ramosa

Calamagrostis langsdorffii var. ramosa, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-green plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along stream banks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, feathery texture that adds visual interest to gardens.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
