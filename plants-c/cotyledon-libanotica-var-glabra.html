Cotyledon libanotica var. glabra

Cotyledon libanotica var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, and it produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant.

It thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
