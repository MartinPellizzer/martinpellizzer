Coelogyne plocoglottoides

Coelogyne plocoglottoides, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with red or purple markings, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Coelogyne plocoglottoides is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
