Cattleya × sororia

Cattleya × sororia, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a hybrid between Cattleya mossiae and Cattleya labiata, known for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically has broad, strap-like leaves and produces large, pendulous blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cattleya × sororia is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
