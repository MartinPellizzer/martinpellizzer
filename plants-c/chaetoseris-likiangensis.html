Chaetoseris likiangensis

Chaetoseris likiangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan composite, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. It typically grows in alpine or subalpine meadows at high elevations.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
