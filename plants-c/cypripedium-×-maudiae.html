Cypripedium × maudiae

Cypripedium × maudiae, commonly known as the Maudiae lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade.

Cypripedium × maudiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
