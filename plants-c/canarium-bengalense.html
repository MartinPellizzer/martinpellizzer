Canarium bengalense

Canarium bengalense, commonly known as the Indian candlenut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lance-shaped leaves. Canarium bengalense thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
