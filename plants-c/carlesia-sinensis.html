Carlesia sinensis

Carlesia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Carlesia sinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
