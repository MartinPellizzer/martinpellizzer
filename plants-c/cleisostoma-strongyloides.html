Cleisostoma strongyloides

Cleisostoma strongyloides, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with numerous fragrant blooms, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are usually ovate and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Cleisostoma strongyloides requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
