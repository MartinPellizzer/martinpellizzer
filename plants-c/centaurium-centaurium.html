Centaurium centaurium

Centaurium centaurium, commonly known as lesser centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe, western Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, bright blue or purple flowers. The plant features opposite, narrow leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

Centaurium centaurium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
