Crepidaria

Crepidaria, commonly known as the "velvet leaf" or "cushion plant," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dense clusters of small, often hairy leaves. They produce small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a subtle floral display to their foliage.
