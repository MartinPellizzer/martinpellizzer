Crassula broomii

Crassula broomii, commonly known as the Broomii Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
