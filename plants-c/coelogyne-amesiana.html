Coelogyne amesiana

Coelogyne amesiana, commonly known as the Ames's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces strikingly fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne amesiana is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
