Consolea × acaulis

Consolea × acaulis, commonly known as the stemless nightshade, is a hybrid cactus species native to South America.

It is characterized by its lack of a distinct stem, with its leaves and flowers emerging directly from the root system. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and xeriscapes.
