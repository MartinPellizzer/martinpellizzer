Cimicifuga macrophylla

Cimicifuga macrophylla, commonly known as giant bugbane, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, pinnately compound leaves that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense colonies in moist, woodland habitats.

Cimicifuga macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and its historical use in treating various ailments.
