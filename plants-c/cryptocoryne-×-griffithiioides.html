Cryptocoryne × griffithiioides

Cryptocoryne × griffithiioides, commonly known as the Griffith's cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, dark green leaves that are often veined with a lighter color, giving it a striking appearance. The plant is popular in aquariums due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a range of water conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Cryptocoryne × griffithiioides is known for its adaptability and ease of care, making it a favored choice among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
