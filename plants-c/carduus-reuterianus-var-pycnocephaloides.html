Carduus reuterianus var. pycnocephaloides

Carduus reuterianus var. pycnocephaloides, commonly known as the dense-headed thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a robust, upright stem covered in coarse, spiny hairs. The plant produces large, spherical flower heads composed of numerous small, yellow florets surrounded by bracts. Its leaves are deeply lobed, with a rough, hairy texture and a bluish-green color.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
