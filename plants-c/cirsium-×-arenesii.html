Cirsium × arenesii

Cirsium × arenesii, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a natural hybrid between Cirsium arvense (creeping thistle) and Cirsium vulgare (common thistle). The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring spiny, lance-shaped leaves and purple to pink flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in roadsides, fields, and wastelands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a resilient and adaptable species within its native habitat.
