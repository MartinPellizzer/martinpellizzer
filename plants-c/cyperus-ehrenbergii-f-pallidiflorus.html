Cyperus ehrenbergii f. pallidiflorus

Cyperus ehrenbergii f. pallidiflorus, commonly known as the pale-flowered flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.

This ornamental plant is valued for its delicate foliage and is often used in water gardens and bog landscapes.
