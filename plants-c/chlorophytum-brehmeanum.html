Chlorophytum brehmeanum

Chlorophytum brehmeanum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white stripes running along their length. The plant produces small, white flowers that are followed by clusters of white or greenish-white berries. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Chlorophytum brehmeanum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air and is often used as a houseplant.
