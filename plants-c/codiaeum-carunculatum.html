Codiaeum carunculatum

Codiaeum carunculatum, commonly known as the flame plant or croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The leaves are typically large, glossy, and have a unique, often irregular shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This plant is popular as a ornamental species due to its striking appearance and adaptability to indoor conditions.
