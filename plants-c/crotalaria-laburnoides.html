Crotalaria laburnoides

Crotalaria laburnoides, commonly known as the golden chain flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking yellow flowers arranged in cascading clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has compound leaves with numerous leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, which splits open to release the seeds when mature.
