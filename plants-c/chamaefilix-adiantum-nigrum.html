Chamaefilix adiantum-nigrum

Chamaefilix adiantum-nigrum, commonly known as black fern, is a species of flowering plant in the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive yet understated member of the Polygonaceae family.
