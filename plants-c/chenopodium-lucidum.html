Chenopodium lucidum

Chenopodium lucidum, commonly known as smooth goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and thrives in full sun. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy, giving it its common name. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences.

Chenopodium lucidum is often used in traditional medicine and is also a food source for various wildlife species.
