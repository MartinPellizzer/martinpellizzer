Carex ornithopoda var. elongata

Carex ornithopoda var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated sedge, is a perennial plant native to parts of Asia and North America.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and related plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets of flowers. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for various invertebrates and birds.
