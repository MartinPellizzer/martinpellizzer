Corethrogyne filaginifolia var. californica

Corethrogyne filaginifolia var. californica, commonly known as the California corethrogyne, is a rare and endemic plant native to the coastal regions of California.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and requires full sun exposure.
