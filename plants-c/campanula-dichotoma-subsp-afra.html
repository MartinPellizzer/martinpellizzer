Campanula dichotoma subsp. afra

Campanula dichotoma subsp.

afra, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces.

The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
