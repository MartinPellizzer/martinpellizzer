Chrysanthemum kennedyi

Chrysanthemum kennedyi, commonly known as Kennedy's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pink petals. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Chrysanthemum kennedyi is also notable for its adaptability to arid environments.
