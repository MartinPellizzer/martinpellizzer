Celosia trigyna var. adoensis

Celosia trigyna var. adoensis, commonly known as the flame flower or coxcomb, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both container gardening and in-ground planting.

Its long blooming period and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.
