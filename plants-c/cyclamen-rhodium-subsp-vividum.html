Cyclamen rhodium subsp. vividum

Cyclamen rhodium subsp.

vividum, commonly known as the vivid cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage and showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers well-drained conditions. Its tuberous root system allows it to survive in arid environments by storing water and nutrients.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
