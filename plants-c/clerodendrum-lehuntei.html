Clerodendrum lehuntei

Clerodendrum lehuntei, commonly known as the Hong Kong hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

Clerodendrum lehuntei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
