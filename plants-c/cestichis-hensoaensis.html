Cestichis hensoaensis

Cestichis hensoaensis, commonly known as the Hensona sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its unique adaptations, it is considered an important species for ecological studies in arid ecosystems.
