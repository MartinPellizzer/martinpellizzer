Cymbidium vestitum

Cymbidium vestitum, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces upright, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with dark markings, blooming in winter or early spring.

It is appreciated for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
