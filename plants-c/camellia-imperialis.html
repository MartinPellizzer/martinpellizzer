Camellia imperialis

Camellia imperialis, commonly known as the imperial camellia, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. Camellia imperialis thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

It is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
