Caladenia vulgata

Caladenia vulgata, commonly known as the common spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in heathland and woodland habitats, often in well-drained soils. Caladenia vulgata is a perennial herb that produces its flowers during the autumn season.
