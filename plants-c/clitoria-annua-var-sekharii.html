Clitoria annua var. sekharii

Clitoria annua var. sekharii, commonly known as the sacred bean or Indian bean, is a flowering plant native to India and Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its delicate, butterfly-like blue or violet flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its leaves are trifoliate, and it has a shallow root system that helps prevent soil erosion.
