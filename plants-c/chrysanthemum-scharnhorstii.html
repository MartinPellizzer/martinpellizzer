Chrysanthemum scharnhorstii

Chrysanthemum scharnhorstii, commonly known as the Scharnhorst chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its daisy-like flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters, with yellow to orange flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes in horticulture.
