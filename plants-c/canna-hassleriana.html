Canna hassleriana

Canna hassleriana, commonly known as the red bamboo canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features tall, bamboo-like stems that can reach heights of up to 6 feet, supporting broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences with bright red bracts that resemble petals, adding dramatic visual impact to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Canna hassleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add a tropical flair to landscapes.
