Callistemon phoeniceus

Callistemon phoeniceus, commonly known as the red bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in winter and spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, grayish-white bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique appearance and ability to attract birds make it a valuable addition to native landscapes.
