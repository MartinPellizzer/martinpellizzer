Cyclamen numidicum

Cyclamen numidicum, commonly known as the Algerian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Cyclamen numidicum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
