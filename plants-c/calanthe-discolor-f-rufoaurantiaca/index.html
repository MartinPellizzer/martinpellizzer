Calanthe Discolor F. Rufoaurantiaca

Calanthe discolor f. rufoaurantiaca is a rare and striking orchid known for its unique coloration and elegant blooms. This plant belongs to the orchid family, which includes over 25,000 species, many of which are prized for their beauty and rarity. The variety rufoaurantiaca is particularly notable for its red and orange hues, which set it apart from the typical green and white of its parent species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
