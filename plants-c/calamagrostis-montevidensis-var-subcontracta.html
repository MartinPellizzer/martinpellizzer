Calamagrostis montevidensis var. subcontracta

Calamagrostis montevidensis var. subcontracta, commonly known as the Montevidense Panicgrass, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
