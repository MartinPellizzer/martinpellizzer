Centaurea Pratensis Subsp. Nigrescens

Centaurea pratensis subsp. nigrescens is a lesser-known member of the sunflower family native to central and eastern Europe. This perennial herb thrives in open grasslands and disturbed soils, often found in meadows and along roadsides. Its dark purple flowers and distinctive foliage make it a subtle yet striking addition to wildflower landscapes. Despite its beauty, it is frequently overlooked in favor of more showy wildflowers. Understanding this plant highlights the importance of preserving biodiversity in native habitats.
