Coryanthes boyi

Coryanthes boyi, commonly known as the Boyi's hooded orchid, is a rare and striking tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, intricate flowers that resemble a hood, with a distinctive spur at the base. The plant's inflorescence consists of several showy, pendulous blooms that are often brightly colored, attracting specific pollinators. Its habitat is typically shaded and humid, requiring specific environmental conditions to thrive.

Due to its rarity and unique morphology, it is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
