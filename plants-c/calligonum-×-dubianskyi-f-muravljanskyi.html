Calligonum × dubianskyi f. muravljanskyi

Calligonum × dubianskyi f. muravljanskyi, commonly known as the hybrid tamarisk, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Tamaricaceae and is characterized by its dense, woody growth and small, scale-like leaves. The plant is adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a hardy species in harsh environments. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

This hybrid is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to stabilize soils in degraded areas.
