Croton Texensis

Croton texensis, also known as the Texas croton, is a rare and striking plant native to the southern United States. It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the southeastern regions of Texas. Due to its unique appearance and limited natural habitat, it is considered an endangered species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and rare plant for future generations.
