Campylospermum dependens var. integrifolium

Campylospermum dependens var. integrifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved campylospermum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are entire, giving it a clean, unbroken appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Goodeniaceae family.
