Cissus fuliginea

Cissus fuliginea, commonly known as the black devil or velvet leaf, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round, green fruits. Cissus fuliginea thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
