Coula utilis

Coula utilis, commonly known as African cherry or *Prunus africana*, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, cherry-like fruits that contain a single seed. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of forests and is valued for its medicinal properties.

The tree is also important for soil conservation and provides habitat for various wildlife species.
