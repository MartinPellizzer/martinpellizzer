Campanula bosniaca

Campanula bosniaca, commonly known as the Bosnian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Balkan region.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Campanula bosniaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
