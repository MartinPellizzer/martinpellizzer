Carex Dolichostachya F. Multifolia

Carex dolichostachya f. multifolia is a unique variant of the common sedge plant known for its distinctive foliage. This ornamental grass-like plant features multiple narrow leaves that give it a lush, feathery appearance. It thrives in moist to wet environments, making it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes. Its ability to tolerate wet conditions while adding visual interest makes it a valuable addition to ecological and decorative settings. This plant is not only aesthetically pleasing but also supports local wildlife and biodiversity.
