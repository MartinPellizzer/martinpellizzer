Ciconium leptopetalum

Ciconium leptopetalum, commonly known as the slender-petaled ciconium, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated petals that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, and they bloom in clusters during the wet season.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a waxy surface, which helps the plant retain moisture in humid environments.
