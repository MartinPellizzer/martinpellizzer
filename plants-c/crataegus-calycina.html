Crataegus calycina

Crataegus calycina, commonly known as the California hawthorn, is a species of shrub or small tree native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its thorny branches and clusters of white or pink flowers. Its botanical features make it a valuable component of native plant communities in its range.
