Centaurium sebaeoides

Centaurium sebaeoides, commonly known as the African sandwort, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, delicate flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, white to pale pink flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Centaurium sebaeoides is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its resilience to extreme conditions.
