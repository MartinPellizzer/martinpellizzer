Carissophyllum longiflorum

Carissophyllum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered carissa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
