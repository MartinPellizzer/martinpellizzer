Cyrtanthera umbrosa

Cyrtanthera umbrosa, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, with a dark green hue that complements its vibrant blooms.

Cyrtanthera umbrosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
