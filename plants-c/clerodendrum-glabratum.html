Clerodendrum glabratum

Clerodendrum glabratum, commonly known as the smooth clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in the summer months. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and showy inflorescences, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
