Cytisus purpureus f. atropurpureus

Cytisus purpureus f. atropurpureus, commonly known as the dark purple broom, is a flowering shrub characterized by its striking, deep purple flowers.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves and produces elongated pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant in gardens.

Its vibrant color and compact growth make it a popular choice for landscaping and ecological restoration projects.
