Convolvulus tiliifolius

Convolvulus tiliifolius, commonly known as the leafy bindweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in clusters. It is native to regions of Europe and Asia and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types.

Due to its invasive nature in some areas, it can pose a challenge in agricultural and natural settings.
