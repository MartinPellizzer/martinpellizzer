Cynanchum Chiloense

Chiloese milkweed, scientifically known as Cynanchum chiloense, is a striking member of the Asclepiadaceae family native to South America. This plant is recognized for its unique appearance, featuring dark green leaves and striking purple to pink flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas of its natural habitat. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds that can be harmful to animals and humans. Its ecological role is significant, supporting specific pollinators and contributing to biodiversity in its region.
