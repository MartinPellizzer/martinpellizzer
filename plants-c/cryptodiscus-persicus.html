Cryptodiscus persicus

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, commonly known as the buckwheat family. The plant is characterized by its upright, branched stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
