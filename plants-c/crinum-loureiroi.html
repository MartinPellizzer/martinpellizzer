Crinum loureiroi

Crinum loureiroi, commonly known as the purple crinum, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking purple flowers and broad, strap-like leaves. The plant produces large, showy inflorescences that can reach several feet in height, making it a dramatic addition to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Crinum loureiroi is also known for its strong fragrance, which can be both attractive and overpowering.
