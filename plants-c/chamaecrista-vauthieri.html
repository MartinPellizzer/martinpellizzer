Chamaecrista vauthieri

Chamaecrista vauthieri, commonly known as the Vauthier's burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, spiny pods that contain several seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as savannas and forest edges.

Its ecological role includes providing food for various wildlife species and contributing to soil stabilization.
