Clompanus ensifolia

Clompanus ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf clompanus, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Clompanus ensifolia thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
