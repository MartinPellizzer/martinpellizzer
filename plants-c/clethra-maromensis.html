Clethra maromensis

Clethra maromensis, commonly known as the Maroma clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has opposite, simple, and elliptic leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Clethra maromensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for pollinators.
