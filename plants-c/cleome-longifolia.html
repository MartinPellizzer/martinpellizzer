Cleome longifolia

Cleome longifolia, commonly known as the tall cleome, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Cleome longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
