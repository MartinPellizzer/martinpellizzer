Cyphomandra holtonii

Cyphomandra holtonii, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by bright orange, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are ovate with a slightly hairy texture, and the plant is often found in moist, tropical environments.

Despite its ornamental appeal, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
