Cotula pruinosa

Cotula pruinosa, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a small flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves and small yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

Cotula pruinosa is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
