Cistus × pratii

Cistus × pratii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Cistus × pratii is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and coastal areas.
