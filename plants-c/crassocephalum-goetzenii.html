Crassocephalum goetzenii

Crassocephalum goetzenii, commonly known as the African blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open grasslands or disturbed soils.

Crassocephalum goetzenii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for landscaping purposes.
