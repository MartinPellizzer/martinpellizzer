Camellia pitardii var. yunnanica

Camellia pitardii var. yunnanica, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a reddish tinge. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a specimen plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
