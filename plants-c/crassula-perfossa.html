Crassula perfossa

Crassula perfossa, commonly known as the rock rose or silver rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Crassula perfossa is prized for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
