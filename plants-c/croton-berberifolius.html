Croton berberifolius

Croton berberifolius, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the genus Croton. Its leaves are often variegated with shades of yellow, green, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
