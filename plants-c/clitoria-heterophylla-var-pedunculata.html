Clitoria heterophylla var. pedunculata

Clitoria heterophylla var. pedunculata, commonly known as the slender butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped blue or violet flowers. The plant has alternate, trifoliate leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant flowers and is also used in traditional medicine.

The species is adapted to tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils.
