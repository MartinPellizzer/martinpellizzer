Coeloglossum viride f. grandiflorum

Coeloglossum viride f. grandiflorum, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in a single, terminal head, which is typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
