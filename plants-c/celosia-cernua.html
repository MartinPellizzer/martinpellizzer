Celosia cernua

Celosia cernua, commonly known as the flame flower or hedge fire, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like bracts that resemble flames. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often hidden within the colorful bracts. Celosia cernua is typically grown as an ornamental plant for its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.

It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
