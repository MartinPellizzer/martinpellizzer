Coreopsis saxicola var. duncanii

Coreopsis saxicola var. duncanii, commonly known as Duncan's rock coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, daisy-like blooms.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert landscapes.
