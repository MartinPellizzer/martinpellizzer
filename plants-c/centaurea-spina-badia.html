Centaurea spina-badia

Centaurea spina-badia, commonly known as the spiny blightweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and bright yellow to orange flower heads. The plant produces small, bract-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in disturbed soils.

Centaurea spina-badia is known for its hardy nature and ability to survive in arid conditions.
