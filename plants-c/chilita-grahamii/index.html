Chilita Grahamii

Chilita grahamii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes. It is known for its striking violet-blue blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and faces threats from climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
