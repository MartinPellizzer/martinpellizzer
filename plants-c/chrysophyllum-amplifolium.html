Chrysophyllum amplifolium

Chrysophyllum amplifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf chrysophyllum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green.
