Coprosma tahitensis

Coprosma tahitensis, commonly known as Tahitian coprosma, is a shrub native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and arranged oppositely on the stems. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, red berries.
