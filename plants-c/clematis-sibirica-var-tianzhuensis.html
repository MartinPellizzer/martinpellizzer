Clematis sibirica var. tianzhuensis

Clematis sibirica var. tianzhuensis, commonly known as the Tianzhu Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the mountainous regions of northern China.

It features slender, climbing stems with palmate leaves that are typically 5-7 lobed. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to violet, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
