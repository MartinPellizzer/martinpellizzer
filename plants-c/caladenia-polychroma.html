Caladenia polychroma

Caladenia polychroma, commonly known as the Rainbow Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multi-colored flowers that range from red, orange, and yellow to green and blue, creating a visually stunning display. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are pollinated by thynnid wasps, which are attracted to the orchid's unique color patterns and scent.

Caladenia polychroma is a rare and protected species, highlighting its ecological significance and the need for conservation efforts.
