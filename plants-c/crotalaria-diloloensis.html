Crotalaria diloloensis

Crotalaria diloloensis, commonly known as the Lolo's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and palmate leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with distinctive spurred petals.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in arid conditions.
