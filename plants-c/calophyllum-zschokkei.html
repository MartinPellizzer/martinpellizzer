Calophyllum zschokkei

Calophyllum zschokkei, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree's fruit is a thick-skinned, segmented berry that contains sweet, white, fleshy arils. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
