Cyathea australis var. pallida

Cyathea australis var. pallida, commonly known as the pale tree fern, is a species native to New Zealand.

It is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forests or along stream banks. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and the plant is known for its slow growth rate.

The pale variegation on its fronds gives it a distinctive appearance, making it a popular ornamental plant.
