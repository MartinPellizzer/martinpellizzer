Chamaedorea resinifera

Chamaedorea resinifera, commonly known as the resinous bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clustered, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
