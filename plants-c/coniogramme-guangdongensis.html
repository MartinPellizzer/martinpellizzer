Coniogramme guangdongensis

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that are often adorned with finely divided pinnae. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
