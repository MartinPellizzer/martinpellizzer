Cynoglossum linifolium var. coerulescens

Cynoglossum linifolium var. coerulescens, commonly known as blue hare's foot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, blooming in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
