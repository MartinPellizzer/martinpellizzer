Cissus longipedunculata

Cissus longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed grape ivy, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by berries containing tiny seeds. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its vigorous growth makes it a popular choice for vertical gardening and indoor decoration.
