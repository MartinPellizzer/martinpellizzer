Calytrix plumulosa

Calytrix plumulosa, commonly known as the silky calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its soft, fluffy white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and sandy habitats.

Calytrix plumulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
