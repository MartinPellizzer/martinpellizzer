Cryptocoryne spiralis var. caudigera

Cryptocoryne spiralis var. caudigera, commonly known as the Caudate Cryptocoryne, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, flowing leaves that can reach lengths of up to 30 cm, often with a wavy or undulating edge. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft to moderately hard water with a neutral to slightly acidic pH. It is a popular choice for planted aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.

Its unique leaf morphology makes it a standout feature in both natural and artificial aquatic environments.
