Coreopsis pubescens var. robusta

Coreopsis pubescens var. robusta, commonly known as the robust coreopsis, is a perennial flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It prefers well-drained soil and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
