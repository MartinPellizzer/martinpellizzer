Commelina somalensis

Commelina somalensis, commonly known as the Somali dayflower, is a flowering plant native to Somalia and parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many grass-like plants and ornamental species. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing in dense clusters near the ground.
