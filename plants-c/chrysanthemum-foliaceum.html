Chrysanthemum foliaceum

Chrysanthemum foliaceum, commonly known as the leaf chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a distinctive, almost lacey appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, typically blooming in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Due to its delicate foliage and vibrant blooms, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
