Canna spectabilis

Canna spectabilis, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy, arrowhead-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, spadix-like inflorescences in shades of red, orange, or yellow, surrounded by colorful bracts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Canna spectabilis is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 6 feet, making it a bold statement in landscape design.
