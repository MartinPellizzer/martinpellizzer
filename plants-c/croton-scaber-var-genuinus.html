Croton scaber var. genuinus

Croton scaber var. genuinus, commonly known as the rough croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves and vibrant, often multicolored foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
