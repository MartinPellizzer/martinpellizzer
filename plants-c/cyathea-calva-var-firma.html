Cyathea calva var. firma

Cyathea calva var. firma, commonly known as the firm-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a slow growth rate and thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in tropical gardens and as a decorative plant in greenhouses.
