Cirsium makinoi

Cirsium makinoi, commonly known as Makino's thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forests and stream banks. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Cirsium makinoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
