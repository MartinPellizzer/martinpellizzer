Cissus dysocarpa var. gymnocarpa

Cissus dysocarpa var. gymnocarpa, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed grape ivy, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine, short hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into spherical, fleshy fruits. Its stems are typically smooth and can trail or climb, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
