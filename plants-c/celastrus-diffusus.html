Celastrus diffusus

Celastrus diffusus, commonly known as the spreading staffvetch, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in moist, wooded areas. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several small, elliptical seeds.
