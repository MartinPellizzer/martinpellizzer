Cedrela tubiflora subsp. bertoniensis

Cedrela tubiflora subsp.

bertoniensis, commonly known as the African mahogany, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa. It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves.

The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by elongated, woody fruits containing flat, winged seeds. It is valued for its durable, aromatic wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
