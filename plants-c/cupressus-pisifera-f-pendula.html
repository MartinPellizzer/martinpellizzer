Cupressus pisifera f. pendula

Cupressus pisifera f. pendula, commonly known as the weeping Chinese fir, is a unique cultivar of the Chinese fir tree.

It is characterized by its drooping branches that create a cascading effect, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has scale-like leaves that are dark green and densely packed, providing a lush, textured foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental landscaping.

This cultivar is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in gardens and parks for its visual impact.
