Chrysanthemum speciosum

Chrysanthemum speciosum, commonly known as the showy chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
