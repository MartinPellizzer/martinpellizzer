Cyathea novae-caledoniae

Cyathea novae-caledoniae, commonly known as the New Caledonia tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the island of New Caledonia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a fibrous sheath. The plant has large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests.

The species is of significant ecological importance in its native habitat, contributing to forest biodiversity and soil stability.
