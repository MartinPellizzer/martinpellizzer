Croton melanostictus

Croton melanostictus, commonly known as the black-streaked croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red, with dark streaks running through the foliage. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton melanostictus is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive indoors with proper care.
