Convolvulus trichosanthes var. patens

Convolvulus trichosanthes var. patens, commonly known as the Patens Cucumber.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by elongated, edible fruits that resemble cucumbers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
