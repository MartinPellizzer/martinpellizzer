Crepis meletonis

Crepis meletonis, commonly known as the meletonis mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its small, hairy leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces numerous small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
