Citrus × succosa

Citrus × succosa, commonly known as the kaffir lime.

It is a hybrid citrus plant that exhibits traits of both the kumquat and the mandarin orange. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic leaves and small, green, oval-shaped fruits. Its flowers are typically white and fragrant, attracting pollinators such as bees.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications for its distinctive flavor and essential oils.
