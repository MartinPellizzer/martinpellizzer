Chiliotrichum amelloides var. diffusum

Chiliotrichum amelloides var. diffusum, commonly known as the spreading starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white to pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens.

It is relatively low maintenance and resistant to pests and diseases.
