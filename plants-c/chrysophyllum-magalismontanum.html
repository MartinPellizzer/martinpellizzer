Chrysophyllum magalismontanum

Chrysophyllum magalismontanum, commonly known as the Magalismontanum cherry, is a species of tree native to the tropical rainforests of western Africa.

Due to its ornamental value and potential economic importance, it is of interest for conservation and horticultural research.
