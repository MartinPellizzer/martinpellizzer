Chiloschista bhutanensis

Chiloschista bhutanensis, commonly known as the Bhutan rock orchid, is a rare and endemic species found primarily in the mountainous regions of Bhutan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral morphology. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates at high altitudes, showcasing its adaptation to harsh environmental conditions. Its flowers are usually small, with a distinctive coloration pattern that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Chiloschista bhutanensis is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
