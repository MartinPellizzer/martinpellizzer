Corylopsis trabeculosa

Corylopsis trabeculosa, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Corylopsis trabeculosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
