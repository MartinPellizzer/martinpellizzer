Corokia × budcotonea

Corokia × budcotonea, commonly known as the hybrid kōwhai, is a deciduous shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is notable for its ornamental value, with showy, fragrant blossoms that attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.
