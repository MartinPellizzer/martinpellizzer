Cladrastis sinensis

Cladrastis sinensis, commonly known as Chinese trumpet tree, is a deciduous flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, pendulous, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure.

Cladrastis sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and graceful appearance.
