Coelogyne blumea

Coelogyne blumea, commonly known as the Blume's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features a rhizomatous root system and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Coelogyne blumea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
