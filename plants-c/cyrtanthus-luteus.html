Cyrtanthus luteus

Cyrtanthus luteus, commonly known as the yellow rain lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bulbs that store nutrients for future growth. Cyrtanthus luteus thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
