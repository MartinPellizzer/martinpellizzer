Castilleja Exserta Var. Venusta

Castilleja exserta var. venusta is a striking member of the Orobanchaceae family known for its vivid red and orange flowers. This hemiparasitic plant thrives in open, sunny habitats across the western United States. It forms intricate relationships with neighboring plants by drawing nutrients through its root system. Its unique appearance makes it a popular subject for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a rare and often overlooked species in its native range.
