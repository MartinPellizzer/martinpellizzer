Cremanthodium hookeri f. angustiligulatum

Cremanthodium hookeri f. angustiligulatum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped cremanthodium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers with yellow ray florets and dark brown disk florets. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine regions, preferring rocky and well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This subspecies is native to the mountainous areas of central Asia, including parts of the Tibetan Plateau and the Himalayas.
