Caleana Dixonii

Caleana dixonii is a striking and rare orchid native to the arid regions of central Australia. Known for its unique and colorful floral structure, it resembles a bee in both shape and color. This plant is part of the orchid family, which includes over 25,000 species worldwide. Due to its limited habitat and environmental threats, it is considered vulnerable. Conservation efforts are crucial to ensure its survival in the wild.
