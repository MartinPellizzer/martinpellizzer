Coelogyne duplicibrachia

Coelogyne duplicibrachia, commonly known as the broad-armed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow pseudobulbs and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and blooms during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne duplicibrachia is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
