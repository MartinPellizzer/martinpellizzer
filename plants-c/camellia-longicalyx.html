Camellia longicalyx

Camellia longicalyx, commonly known as the longleaf camellia, is a species native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring glossy dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces striking, camellia-like flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow center. These flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Camellia longicalyx is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated for its beautiful foliage and floral display.
