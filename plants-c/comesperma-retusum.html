Comesperma retusum

Comesperma retusum, commonly known as the rough comesperma, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.
