Cytisus Capitatus F. Elongatus

Cytisus capitatus f. elongatus is a rare and striking variant of the common broom plant. Known for its elongated leaves and vibrant yellow flowers, this plant adds a unique touch to any garden or natural setting. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for coastal or rocky landscapes. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. This article explores its characteristics, cultivation tips, and significance in the plant world.
