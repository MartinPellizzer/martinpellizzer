Cyathea bellisquamata

Cyathea bellisquamata, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a fibrous, dark brown rind. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in New Zealand's flora.
