Camellia rusticana f. plena

Camellia rusticana f. plena, commonly known as the double-flowered camellia, is a striking ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with a double petal structure that gives it a lush, full appearance. The plant is evergreen, featuring glossy, dark green foliage that provides an attractive backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Camellia rusticana f. plena is also valued for its winter blooming habit, adding color to gardens during the colder months.
