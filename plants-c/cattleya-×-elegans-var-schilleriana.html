Cattleya × elegans var. schilleriana

Cattleya × elegans var. schilleriana, commonly known as the Schiller's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often bicolor blooms that range from white to pink and orange. The plant typically produces 4–6 flowers per spike, with petals that are broad and fleshy. It is a tropical orchid that thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
