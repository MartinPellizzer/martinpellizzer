Crotalaria laxiflora var. acuta

Crotalaria laxiflora var. acuta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow with prominent purple veins, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
