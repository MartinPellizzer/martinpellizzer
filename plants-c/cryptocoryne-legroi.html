Cryptocoryne legroi

Cryptocoryne legroi, commonly known as the Java cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, elongated leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water with moderate lighting and is often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense underwater mats.

Cryptocoryne legroi is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
