Calathea prasina

Calathea prasina, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that change color in response to light conditions. The leaves often display a combination of green, silver, and pink hues, creating a visually appealing effect. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
