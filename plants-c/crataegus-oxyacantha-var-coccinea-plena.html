Crataegus oxyacantha var. coccinea-plena

Crataegus oxyacantha var. coccinea-plena, commonly known as the crimson-flowered hawthorn, is a cultivar of the hawthorn plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, double flowers that are a deep crimson red, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often used in gardens for its aesthetic value.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in temperate climates.
