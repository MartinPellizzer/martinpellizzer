Cynorkis obcordata

Cynorkis obcordata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive, narrow ovary.

Cynorkis obcordata is often found in association with other orchid species in its native environment.
