Caesia dregeana

Caesia dregeana, commonly known as the dwarf lily or dwarf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that resemble miniature orchids. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained conditions. Its leaves are grass-like, and it produces showy, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Caesia dregeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and collections.
