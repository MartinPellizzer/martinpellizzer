Croton swynnertonii

Croton swynnertonii, commonly known as the Swynnerton's croton, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.
