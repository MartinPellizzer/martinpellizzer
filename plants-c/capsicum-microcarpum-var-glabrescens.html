Capsicum microcarpum var. glabrescens

Capsicum microcarpum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth dwarf pepper, is a compact variety of chili pepper native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits that typically range in color from green to red when mature. The plant has a bushy growth habit and produces small, clustered flowers. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a slightly pubescent surface.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild to moderate heat level.
