Cassia lorentzii

Cassia lorentzii, commonly known as the flame tree or torch tree, is a tropical deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and greyish, while its wood is valued for its durability and workability.

Cassia lorentzii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
