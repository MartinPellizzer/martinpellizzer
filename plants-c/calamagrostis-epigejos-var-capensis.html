Calamagrostis epigejos var. capensis

Calamagrostis epigejos var. capensis, commonly known as Cape reed, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It features slender, arching blades that are typically blue-green in color, with a fine texture. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are usually pink or purple in hue. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
