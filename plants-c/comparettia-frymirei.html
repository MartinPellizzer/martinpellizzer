Comparettia frymirei

Comparettia frymirei, commonly known as the Frymire's comparettia, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features a pseudobulb structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting it to its natural habitat. Its inflorescence typically consists of multiple flowers arranged in a raceme, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.

Comparettia frymirei thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
