Caladenia audasii

Caladenia audasii, commonly known as the bold spider-orchid, is an Australian native orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears several showy, hooded flowers. Caladenia audasii is found in various habitats across southeastern Australia, including heathlands and woodland areas.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often associated with sandy or gravelly substrates.
