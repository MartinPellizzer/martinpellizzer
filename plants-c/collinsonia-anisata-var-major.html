Collinsonia anisata var. major

Collinsonia anisata var. major, commonly known as the showy collinsonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red throat. The plant typically grows in moist, rich soils along stream banks and in woodland areas. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
