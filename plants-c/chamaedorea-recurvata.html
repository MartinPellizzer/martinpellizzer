Chamaedorea recurvata

Chamaedorea recurvata, commonly known as the zigzag palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, arching fronds with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the leaflets. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet and has a compact, bushy appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Chamaedorea recurvata is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant foliage and air-purifying qualities.
