Cryptocarya trinervia

Cryptocarya trinervia, commonly known as the three-veined cryptocarya, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves with three distinct veins, giving it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.
