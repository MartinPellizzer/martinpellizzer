Citrus balotina

Citrus balotina, commonly known as the Java lemon.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant produces small, yellow, citrus fruits that are similar in appearance to lemons but have a more acidic and aromatic flavor. Its leaves are glossy and oval-shaped, while its flowers are white and fragrant.

Citrus balotina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and for the use of its fruit in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
