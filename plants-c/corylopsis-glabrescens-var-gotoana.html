Corylopsis glabrescens var. gotoana

Corylopsis glabrescens var. gotoana, commonly known as the Goto's early yellow-flowering corylopsis, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This variety is valued for its early blooming habit and ornamental appeal in garden settings.
