Cyperus chermezonianus

Cyperus chermezonianus, commonly known as the flame sedge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or brown, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Cyperus chermezonianus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
