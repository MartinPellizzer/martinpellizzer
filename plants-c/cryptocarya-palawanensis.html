Cryptocarya palawanensis

Cryptocarya palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves, which are elliptical and glossy, often with a waxy texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark purple berries.

Cryptocarya palawanensis is typically found in lowland to montane rainforests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
