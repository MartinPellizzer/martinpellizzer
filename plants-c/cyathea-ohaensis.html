Cyathea ohaensis

Cyathea ohaensis, commonly known as the Ohaena tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting its tall structure.

Cyathea ohaensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
