Crotalaria tenuirama

Crotalaria tenuirama, commonly known as slender cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
