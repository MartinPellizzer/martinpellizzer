Camellia multiplex

Camellia multiplex, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers in late winter to early spring. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms. Camellia multiplex prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for temperate climates.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, enhancing its adaptability in different environments.
