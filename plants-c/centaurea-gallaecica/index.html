Centaurea Gallaecica

Centaurea gallaecica is a rare and striking wildflower native to the coastal regions of northwest Spain. With its vibrant blue petals and distinctive yellow center, it stands out among the rugged landscapes where it thrives. This plant is often found in sandy or gravelly soils along cliffs and dunes, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its unique appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Protecting Centaurea gallaecica is crucial for preserving the biodiversity of its fragile ecosystem.
