Chenopodium ambrosioides var. microphyllum

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. microphyllum, commonly known as jointweed, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, alternate leaves and clusters of greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions of North America. It has a prostrate or ascending growth habit and produces small, dry fruits.

This variety is often confused with other Chenopodium species due to its similar appearance.
