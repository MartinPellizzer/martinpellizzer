Cyminosma pedunculata

Cyminosma pedunculata, commonly known as the clustered corymbosa, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
