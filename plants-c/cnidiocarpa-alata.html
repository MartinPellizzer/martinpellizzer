Cnidiocarpa alata

Cnidiocarpa alata, commonly known as the winged cactus or desert cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, cylindrical stems that are covered in spiny, wing-like structures. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in xerophytic plant adaptations.
