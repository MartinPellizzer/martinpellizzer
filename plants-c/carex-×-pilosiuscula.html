Carex × pilosiuscula

Carex × pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silky sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and produces spike-like flower clusters. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and wetlands. It has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered with fine, silky hairs.

Carex × pilosiuscula is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
