Cyclobalanopsis × takaoyamensis

Cyclobalanopsis × takaoyamensis, commonly known as the Takaoyama cyclobalanopsis, is a hybrid evergreen tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks and beeches, and is characterized by its hard, durable wood. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils and temperate climates. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy green upper surface with a pale underside.

The plant produces small, cup-shaped acorns that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
