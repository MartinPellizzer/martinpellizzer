Coreopsis tripartita

Coreopsis tripartita, commonly known as the three-part coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with three distinct lobes, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a hairy texture, and it prefers well-drained soil.

Coreopsis tripartita is often found in prairies, open woodlands, and along roadsides.
