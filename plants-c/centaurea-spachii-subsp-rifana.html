Centaurea spachii subsp. rifana

Centaurea spachii subsp.

rifana, commonly known as the Rif centaury, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of North Africa. It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh, dry climates.

The plant features erect, branched stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are bright blue to violet, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
