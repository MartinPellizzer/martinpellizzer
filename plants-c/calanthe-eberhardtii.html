Calanthe eberhardtii

Calanthe eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces a raceme of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent sepals and petals. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Calanthe eberhardtii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
