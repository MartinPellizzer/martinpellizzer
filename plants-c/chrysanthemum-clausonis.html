Chrysanthemum clausonis

Chrysanthemum clausonis, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Chrysanthemum clausonis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional Chinese horticulture.
