Calesiam aspleniifolium

Calesiam aspleniifolium, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to New Zealand.

It features delicate, lacy fronds that are typically silver-white in color, with a distinctive appearance that resembles a fern with a metallic sheen. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its fronds are bi-directionally compound, with pinnae that are elongated and finely divided.

Calesiam aspleniifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique visual characteristics.
