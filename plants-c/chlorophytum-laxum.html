Chlorophytum laxum

Chlorophytum laxum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes, and it produces small, white flowers in spring. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It also spreads easily through runners, which can be used to propagate new plants.

Its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal make it a favorite among both novice and experienced gardeners.
