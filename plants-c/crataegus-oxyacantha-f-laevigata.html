Crataegus oxyacantha f. laevigata

Crataegus oxyacantha f. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth hawthorn, is a cultivar of the hawthorn plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces red berries in the fall, which are a food source for birds and other wildlife. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

This variety is well-suited for gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
