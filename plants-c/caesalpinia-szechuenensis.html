Caesalpinia szechuenensis

Caesalpinia szechuenensis, commonly known as the Sichuan goldenbird.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Its flowers are typically clustered in terminal panicles and bloom in the spring.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
