Centaurea bombycina subsp. funkii

Centaurea bombycina subsp.

funkii, commonly known as the silky centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It features slender, erect stems and produces striking blue to purple flower heads composed of numerous small florets.

The plant has hairy leaves and is characterized by its showy, fluffy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
