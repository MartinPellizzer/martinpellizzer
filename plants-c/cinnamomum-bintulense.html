Cinnamomum bintulense

Cinnamomum bintulense, commonly known as Bintulens cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by elliptical, dark blue-black fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and its potential in the spice and fragrance industries.
