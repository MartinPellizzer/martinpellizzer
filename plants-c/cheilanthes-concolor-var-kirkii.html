Cheilanthes concolor var. kirkii

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, drought-tolerant growth habit. The plant has dark green, leathery fronds with a distinct midrib and serrated margins. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found on limestone or sandstone outcrops.
