Cyrtandra bicolor

Cyrtandra bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored heliconia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Heliconiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, brightly colored bracts that resemble petals.
