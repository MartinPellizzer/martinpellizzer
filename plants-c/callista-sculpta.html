Callista sculpta

Callista sculpta, commonly known as the sculpted callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricately patterned leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
