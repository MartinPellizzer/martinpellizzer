Coreopsis neaei

Coreopsis neaei, commonly known as the Florida coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wetlands and disturbed soils. The plant features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, oval seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Coreopsis neaei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
