Cleretum cuneifolium

Cleretum cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cleretum, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
