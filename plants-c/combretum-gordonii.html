Combretum gordonii

Combretum gordonii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often pubescent, and it produces small, woody capsules as fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The species is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
