Crotalaria thomsonii

Crotalaria thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is a herbaceous annual that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often found in disturbed soils and arid environments.

Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
