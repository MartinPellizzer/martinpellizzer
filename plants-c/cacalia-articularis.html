Cacalia articularis

Cacalia articularis, commonly known as the jointweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Cacalia articularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
