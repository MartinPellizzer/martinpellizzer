Cusparia febrifuga

Cusparia febrifuga, commonly known as the feverfew plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

Cusparia febrifuga is valued in herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antipyretic effects.
