Clusia gentlei

Clusia gentlei, commonly known as the glossy abutilon, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil.

Clusia gentlei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
