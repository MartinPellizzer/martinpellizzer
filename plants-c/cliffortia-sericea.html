Cliffortia sericea

Cliffortia sericea, commonly known as the silverweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky and coastal environments. The plant features silvery-white hairs on its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in spring.

Cliffortia sericea is well adapted to harsh conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
