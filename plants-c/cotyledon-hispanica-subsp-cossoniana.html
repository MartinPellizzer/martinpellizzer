Cotyledon hispanica subsp. cossoniana

Cotyledon hispanica subsp.

cossoniana, commonly known as the Cosson's windowleaf, is a succulent plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and purple.

The leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps the plant store water and protect it from harsh environmental conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of care.
