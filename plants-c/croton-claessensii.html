Croton claessensii

Croton claessensii, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal croton, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features large, palmate leaves with a deep green upper surface and a lighter green underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
