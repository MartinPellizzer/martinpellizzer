Crepinella gracillima

Crepinella gracillima, commonly known as the slender crepe flower, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces delicate, papery flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It is well adapted to dry conditions and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
