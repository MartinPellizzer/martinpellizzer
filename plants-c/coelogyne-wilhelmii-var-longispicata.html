Coelogyne wilhelmii var. longispicata

Coelogyne wilhelmii var. longispicata, commonly known as the long-spurred coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, evergreen leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with a distinctive, elongated spur.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
