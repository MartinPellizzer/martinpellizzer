Cenchrus Arnhemicus

Cenchrus arnhemicus is a resilient grass species native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia. It thrives in harsh environments with low rainfall and poor soil conditions. This plant is known for its ability to survive extreme temperatures and drought. Its dense growth pattern makes it an important component of local ecosystems. Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in rehabilitation of degraded lands.
