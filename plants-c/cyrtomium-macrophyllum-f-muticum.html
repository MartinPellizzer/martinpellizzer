Cyrtomium macrophyllum f. muticum

Cyrtomium macrophyllum f. muticum, commonly known as the giant fern or large-leaf holly fern, is a variant of the Cyrtomium macrophyllum species.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, dark green fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant features a compact, upright growth habit with a dense rosette of sterile fronds surrounding the fertile spore-bearing structures. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions with high humidity.

Botanically, it is noted for its distinctive morphology and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal.
