Columnea kalbreyeri

Columnea kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's flame bell, is a striking tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with contrasting yellow or white markings. The plant produces showy bracts that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. It thrives in humid, well-drained conditions and prefers partial to full shade.

Columnea kalbreyeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
