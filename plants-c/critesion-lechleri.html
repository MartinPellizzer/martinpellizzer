Critesion lechleri

Critesion lechleri, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its thick, dark green leaves and a smooth, reddish-bark trunk. The plant is most famous for its latex sap, which is harvested for producing natural rubber. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory properties.

The rubber tree thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
