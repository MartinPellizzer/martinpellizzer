Clematis jeypurensis

Clematis jeypurensis, commonly known as the Himalayan clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the Himalayas.

It features slender, climbing stems with palmately lobed leaves that are typically green and hairy. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
