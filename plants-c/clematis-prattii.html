Clematis prattii

Clematis prattii, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with serrated edges.

Clematis prattii thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
