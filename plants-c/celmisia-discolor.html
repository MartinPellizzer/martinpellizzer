Celmisia discolor

Celmisia discolor, commonly known as the silverweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to dense pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Celmisia discolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
