Chassalia discolor subsp. taitensis

Chassalia discolor subsp.

The plant typically grows in coastal or damp habitats, often in rocky or sandy soils. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, clustered blooms during the spring and summer months.
