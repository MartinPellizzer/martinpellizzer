Cattleya mendelii var. grandiflora

Cattleya mendelii var. grandiflora, commonly known as the Grandiflora Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It originates from the Andes mountains of South America, where it thrives in humid, high-altitude environments. The plant features tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves that support its robust growth. Its flowers are typically fragrant, with vibrant colors ranging from white to pink and yellow.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
