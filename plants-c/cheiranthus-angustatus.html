Cheiranthus angustatus

Cheiranthus angustatus, commonly known as the narrowleaf honesty, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features slender, upright stems and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
