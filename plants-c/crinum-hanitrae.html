Crinum hanitrae

Crinum hanitrae, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to Madagascar.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Crinum hanitrae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
