Cotoneaster shannanensis

Cotoneaster shannanensis, commonly known as the Shannan cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces red berries that are an important food source for birds and small mammals. It typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soil.

Cotoneaster shannanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
