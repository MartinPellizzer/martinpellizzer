Calochortus nuttallii var. australis

Calochortus nuttallii var. australis, commonly known as the Sierra mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall stem that supports its vibrant blooms.
