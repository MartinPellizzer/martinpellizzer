Cotula stricta

Cotula stricta, commonly known as narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Cotula stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
