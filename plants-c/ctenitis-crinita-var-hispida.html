Ctenitis crinita var. hispida

Ctenitis crinita var. hispida, commonly known as the hairy ctenitis, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Davalliaceae and is characterized by its distinctive fronds with a hairy texture on the underside. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are pinnate, with closely spaced pinnae that are often recurved.
