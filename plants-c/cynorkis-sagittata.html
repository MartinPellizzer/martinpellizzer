Cynorkis sagittata

Cynorkis sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features arrow-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cynorkis sagittata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid environments.
