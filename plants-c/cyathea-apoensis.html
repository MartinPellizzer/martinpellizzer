Cyathea apoensis

Cyathea apoensis, commonly known as the Philippine tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland and montane rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which can reach several meters in length.

Cyathea apoensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
