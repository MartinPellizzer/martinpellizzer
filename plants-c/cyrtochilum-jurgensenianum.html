Cyrtochilum jurgensenianum

Cyrtochilum jurgensenianum, commonly known as the Jurgensen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and a single, narrow leaf. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically have a yellow or greenish hue with intricate markings.

Cyrtochilum jurgensenianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
