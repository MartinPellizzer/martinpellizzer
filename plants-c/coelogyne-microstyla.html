Coelogyne microstyla

Coelogyne microstyla, commonly known as the slender-lipped orchid, is a species native to the forests of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often have a pink or white coloration.

Coelogyne microstyla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
