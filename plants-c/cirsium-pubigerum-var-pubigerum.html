Cirsium pubigerum var. pubigerum

Cirsium pubigerum var. pubigerum, commonly known as the hairy thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves covered in fine, soft hairs, which give it its common name. The plant produces purple to pinkish flowers in dense, spherical clusters, typically blooming from late summer to early autumn. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides, meadows, and waste areas.

Cirsium pubigerum var. pubigerum is often found in temperate climates and is known for its hardy, resilient nature.
