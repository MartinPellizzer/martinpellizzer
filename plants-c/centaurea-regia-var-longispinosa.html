Centaurea regia var. longispinosa

Centaurea regia var. longispinosa, commonly known as the long-spined centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant features erect, spiny stems and produces vibrant blue or purple composite flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual impact.
