Cuphea linifolia var. hervita

Cuphea linifolia var. hervita, commonly known as the silverleaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of tubular, pink to red flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
