Chiloglossa glabra

Chiloglossa glabra, commonly known as the smooth-tongued plant, is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

It is native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
