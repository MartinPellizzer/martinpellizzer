Canavali

Canavali, commonly known as the Christmas flower, is a tropical shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often blooming during the holiday season. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in festive decorations. Canavali thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is typically dark green and glossy, adding to its visual appeal.
