Croton semunculus

Croton semunculus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a small shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features showy, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from yellow to red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The species is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.
