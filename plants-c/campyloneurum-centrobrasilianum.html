Campyloneurum centrobrasilianum

Campyloneurum centrobrasilianum, commonly known as the Brazilian campyloneurum, is a member of the Polypodiaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a terrestrial fern characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically light green in color.
