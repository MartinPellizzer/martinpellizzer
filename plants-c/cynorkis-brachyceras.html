Cynorkis brachyceras

Cynorkis brachyceras, commonly known as the narrow-capsuled rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with few leaves, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in late spring or early summer. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several flowers, each with three sepals and three petals.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in specialized microhabitats.
