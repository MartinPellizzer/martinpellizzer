Cirsium × oenipontanum

Cirsium × oenipontanum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust growth and hardy nature.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens despite its prickly foliage.
