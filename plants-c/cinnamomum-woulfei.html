Cinnamomum woulfei

Cinnamomum woulfei, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum woulfei, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a spice.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
