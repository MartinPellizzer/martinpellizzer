Cinnamomum solomonense

Cinnamomum solomonense, commonly known as the Solomon Islands cinnamon or wild cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Solomon Islands.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its essential oils and medicinal properties.
