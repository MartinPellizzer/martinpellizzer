Crotalaria breviflora var. riedelii

Crotalaria breviflora var. riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's short-flowered crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces clusters of yellow, spurred flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
