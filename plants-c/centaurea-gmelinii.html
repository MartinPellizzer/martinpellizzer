Centaurea gmelinii

Centaurea gmelinii, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems and small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Centaurea gmelinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
