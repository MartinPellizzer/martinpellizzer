Celosia latifolia

Celosia latifolia, commonly known as the plume grass or crown of thorns, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, branching stems and vibrant, feathery bracts that resemble plumes or fireworks. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often surrounded by colorful, papery bracts. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Celosia latifolia is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators.
