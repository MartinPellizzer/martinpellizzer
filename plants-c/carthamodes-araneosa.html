Carthamodes araneosa

Carthamodes araneosa, commonly known as the spider plant or spider flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, spider-like inflorescences that consist of numerous small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Carthamodes araneosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in gardens.
