Cyathea aristata

Cyathea aristata, commonly known as the noble tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a distinctive, elegant appearance.

Cyathea aristata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic value and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
