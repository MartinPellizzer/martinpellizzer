Cleistanthus myrianthus

Cleistanthus myrianthus, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, creating a vibrant display during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy inflorescences.

The tree is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
