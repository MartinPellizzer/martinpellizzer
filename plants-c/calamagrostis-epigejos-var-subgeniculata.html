Calamagrostis epigejos var. subgeniculata

Calamagrostis epigejos var. subgeniculata, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.
