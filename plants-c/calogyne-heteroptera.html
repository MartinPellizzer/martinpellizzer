Calogyne heteroptera

Calogyne heteroptera, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Calogyne heteroptera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts for its distinctive foliage and delicate blooms.
