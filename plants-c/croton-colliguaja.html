Croton colliguaja

Croton colliguaja, commonly known as the flame tree or red croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, papery flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has palmately lobed leaves with a distinctive greenish-white underside. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance.

Croton colliguaja is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
