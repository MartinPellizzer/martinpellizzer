Cotula tanacetifolia

Cotula tanacetifolia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas or along roadsides.

Cotula tanacetifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
