Cymbidium canaliculatum f. viridiflorum

Cymbidium canaliculatum f. viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered cymbidium, is a rare and ornamental orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often glossy. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers that have a unique coloration. It is a cool-growing orchid that thrives in high-altitude environments with good air circulation.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
