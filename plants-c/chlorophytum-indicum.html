Chlorophytum indicum

Chlorophytum indicum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, grass-like inflorescences that bear white or pale green flowers. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Chlorophytum indicum is also known for its ability to purify indoor air, making it a valuable addition to homes and offices.
