Chondrilla denticulata

Chondrilla denticulata, commonly known as the toothed goat's beard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its finely toothed leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and scrublands. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that attract various pollinators.

Chondrilla denticulata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
