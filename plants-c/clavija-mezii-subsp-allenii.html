Clavija mezii subsp. allenii

Clavija mezii subsp.

allenii, commonly known as the Allen's clavija, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers.

The plant is adapted to survive in dry, rocky soils and can tolerate high temperatures. It is often found in desert scrub and chaparral habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in semi-arid environments.
