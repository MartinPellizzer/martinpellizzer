Cynoctonum meyeri

Cynoctonum meyeri, commonly known as the Meyer's nightshade, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cynoctonum meyeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and vibrant floral display.
