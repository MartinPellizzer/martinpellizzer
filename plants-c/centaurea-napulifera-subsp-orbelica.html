Centaurea napulifera subsp. orbelica

Centaurea napulifera subsp.

orbelica, commonly known as the Alboran Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula and northwestern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bracteate flower heads. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
