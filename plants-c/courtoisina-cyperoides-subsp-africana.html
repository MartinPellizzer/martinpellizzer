Courtoisina cyperoides subsp. africana

Courtoisina cyperoides subsp.

africana, commonly known as African flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to sub-Saharan Africa. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants.

The plant typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as along riverbanks and in swampy areas. It has slender, grass-like stems with small, triangular leaves and produces spikelets that are arranged in clusters.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic environments.
