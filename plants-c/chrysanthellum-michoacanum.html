Chrysanthellum michoacanum

Chrysanthellum michoacanum, commonly known as the Michoacan chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers range in color from white to yellow.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
