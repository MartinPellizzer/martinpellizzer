Crotalaria oreadum

Crotalaria oreadum, commonly known as the mountain crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides.

Crotalaria oreadum is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
