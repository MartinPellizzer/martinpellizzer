Cola nana

Cola nana, commonly known as the dwarf cola or small cola, is a species of flowering plant in the family Sterculiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the western and central parts. The plant is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of less than one meter. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that develop into hard, capsule-like fruits containing stimulant-rich seeds.
