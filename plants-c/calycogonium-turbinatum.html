Calycogonium turbinatum

Calycogonium turbinatum, commonly known as the turban snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail belonging to the family Achatinellidae.

It is characterized by its large, spiral-shaped shell that is typically tan to brown in color with a smooth, glossy surface. The shell is elongated and conical, with a distinct whorl separation and a thick, robust lip. Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it is considered endangered and is protected under conservation efforts.
