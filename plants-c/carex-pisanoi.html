Carex pisanoi

Carex pisanoi, commonly known as the Pisano sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region, particularly found in southern Europe and parts of North Africa. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or shrubland habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers.
