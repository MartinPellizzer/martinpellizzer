Corysanthes fimbriata var. diemenica

Corysanthes fimbriata var. diemenica, commonly known as the Diocese candlewood, is a unique flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, candle-like inflorescences. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in terminal spikes.

This variety is rare and is typically found in specific habitats within Tasmania's coastal regions.
