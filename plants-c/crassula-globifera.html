Crassula globifera

Crassula globifera, commonly known as the "Lemon Lime Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a pale yellow or pinkish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Crassula globifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
