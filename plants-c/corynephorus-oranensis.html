Corynephorus oranensis

Corynephorus oranensis, commonly known as the Oran grass, is a species of grass native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial plant characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Corynephorus oranensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in some horticultural settings.
