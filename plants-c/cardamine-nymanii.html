Cardamine nymanii

Cardamine nymanii, commonly known as the narrowleaf water crowfoot, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, trifoliate leaves that are typically submerged or floating on the surface of freshwater habitats. Its presence is an indicator of good water quality and healthy aquatic ecosystems.
