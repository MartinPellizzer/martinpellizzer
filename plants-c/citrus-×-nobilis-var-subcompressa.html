Citrus × nobilis var. subcompressa

Citrus × nobilis var. subcompressa, commonly known as the flat-seeded lemon, is a botanical variant of the lemon plant.

It is characterized by its flattened, compressed seeds and a slightly smaller fruit size compared to the standard lemon. The plant produces yellow-orange fruits with a high acidity level and a distinct aromatic profile. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and is used in both food preparation and traditional herbal remedies.
