Centaurea liliniea

Centaurea liliniea, commonly known as the slender cornflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its blue to violet petals and yellow center. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.
