Clematis goudotiana

Clematis goudotiana, commonly known as the white clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to southern China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its most striking characteristic is its fragrant, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
