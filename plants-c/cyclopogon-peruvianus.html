Cyclopogon peruvianus

Cyclopogon peruvianus, commonly known as the Peruvian cyclopogon, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Cyclopogon peruvianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
