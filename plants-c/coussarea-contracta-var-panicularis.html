Coussarea contracta var. panicularis

Coussarea contracta var. panicularis, commonly known as the panicled coussarea, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and striking panicle inflorescences that bloom in clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a ornamental species in shaded garden settings. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for humid tropical and subtropical climates.

Its unique floral display and foliage make it a desirable plant for botanical gardens and specialized collections.
