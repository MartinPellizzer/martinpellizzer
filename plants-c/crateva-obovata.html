Crateva obovata

Crateva obovata, commonly known as the Indian Beggarticks, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Crateva obovata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
