Cyrtochilum distans

Cyrtochilum distans, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a basal rosette.

Cyrtochilum distans thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
