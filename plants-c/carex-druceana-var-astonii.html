Carex druceana var. astonii

Carex druceana var. astonii, commonly known as the Aston's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-rich soils, often in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The inflorescence consists of a cylindrical spike of small, three-lobed flowers, which are usually greenish or brownish in color.
