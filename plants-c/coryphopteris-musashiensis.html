Coryphopteris musashiensis

Coryphopteris musashiensis, commonly known as the Japanese tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Japan.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions. Its fronds are dark green and often have a leathery texture.

Coryphopteris musashiensis plays a role in forest ecosystems by providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
