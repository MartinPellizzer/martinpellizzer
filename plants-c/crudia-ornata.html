Crudia ornata

Crudia ornata, commonly known as the golden thorn, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, thorny stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible when ripe. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species in local ecosystems and traditional medicine.
