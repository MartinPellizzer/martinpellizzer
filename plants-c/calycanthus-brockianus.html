Calycanthus brockianus

Calycanthus brockianus, commonly known as the Brock's sweetshrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its aromatic foliage and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant collections.
