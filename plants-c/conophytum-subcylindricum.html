Conophytum subcylindricum

Conophytum subcylindricum, commonly known as the cylindrical conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, cylindrical stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal watering.

Its unique shape and colorful blooms make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
