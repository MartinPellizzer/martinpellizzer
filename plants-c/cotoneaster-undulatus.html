Cotoneaster undulatus

Cotoneaster undulatus, commonly known as the wavy cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are often wavy-margined, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is commonly used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Cotoneaster undulatus is adaptable to various soil types and thrives in full sun to partial shade.
