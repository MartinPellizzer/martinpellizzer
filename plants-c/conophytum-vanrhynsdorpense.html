Conophytum vanrhynsdorpense

Conophytum vanrhynsdorpense, commonly known as the Vanrhynsdorp ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, triangular leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Conophytum vanrhynsdorpense is also known for its ability to form dense clusters, which provide habitat for small desert creatures.
