Campanula napuligera f. stenophylloides

Campanula napuligera f. stenophylloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a compact perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
