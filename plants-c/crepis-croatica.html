Crepis croatica

Crepis croatica, commonly known as the Croatian hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
