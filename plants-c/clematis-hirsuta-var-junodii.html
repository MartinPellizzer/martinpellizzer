Clematis hirsuta var. junodii

Clematis hirsuta var. junodii, commonly known as the Junod's hirsute clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features large, showy flowers that range from white to pale blue, often with a faint purple tinge. The plant has distinctive, hairy stems and heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to climb trellises or fences.
