Craibiodendron yunnanense

Craibiodendron yunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan rock orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on rocky substrates, showcasing its adaptability to harsh, high-altitude environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a complex structure typical of orchids.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
