Coelogyne quadricarinata

Coelogyne quadricarinata, commonly known as the Four-ribbed Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescence that bears numerous fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive four-ribbed structure on the petals. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, loose substrates for optimal growth. Its flowers bloom during the winter months, adding a dramatic display to tropical gardens and greenhouses.

Coelogyne quadricarinata is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
