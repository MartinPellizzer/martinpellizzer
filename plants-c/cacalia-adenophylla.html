Cacalia adenophylla

Cacalia adenophylla, commonly known as the woolly cacalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features hairy, glandular leaves that give it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in shades of pink or white.

It is often found in open grasslands and along coastal areas.
