Calochilus stramenicola

Calochilus stramenicola, commonly known as the coastal orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to coastal regions of southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along the edges of beaches and dunes. The plant features a slender, erect stem with a few broad, ovate leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellowish with a distinctive red or orange lip.

Calochilus stramenicola is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in its habitat.
