Claytonia caroliniana

Claytonia caroliniana, commonly known as the spring beauty, is a low-growing herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features small, delicate flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has a rosette of basal leaves that are usually hairy and slightly lobed. It thrives in moist, shady woodland habitats and is often found in rich, loamy soils.

Claytonia caroliniana is a popular choice for native gardens due to its aesthetic appeal and ecological value.
