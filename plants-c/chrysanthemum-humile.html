Chrysanthemum humile

Chrysanthemum humile, commonly known as the dwarf chrysanthemum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Asia.

It features daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale yellow petals, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has finely divided, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its small stature and vibrant blooms make it a valuable addition to ornamental horticulture.
