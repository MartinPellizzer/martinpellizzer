Coreopsis californica subsp. newberryi

Coreopsis californica subsp.

newberryi, commonly known as Newberry's coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to California. It is a low-growing perennial with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and is adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and attractive addition to native gardens.
