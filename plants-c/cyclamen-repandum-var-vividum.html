Cyclamen repandum var. vividum

Cyclamen repandum var. vividum, commonly known as the vivid cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from pink to red and white, often with a distinctive, spurred petal. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety foliage that adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers.

Cyclamen repandum var. vividum is known for its winter blooming habit, making it a popular choice for seasonal displays.
