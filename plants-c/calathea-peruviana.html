Calathea peruviana

Calathea peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and variegation. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, bright indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Calathea peruviana is popular as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
