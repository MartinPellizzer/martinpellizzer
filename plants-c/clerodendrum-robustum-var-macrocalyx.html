Clerodendrum robustum var. macrocalyx

Clerodendrum robustum var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. It produces small, clustered fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability.
