Cymbidium acutum

Cymbidium acutum, commonly known as the acute cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, linear flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often growing in a fan shape.

Cymbidium acutum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
