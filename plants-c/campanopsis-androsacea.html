Campanopsis androsacea

Campanopsis androsacea, commonly known as the Androsace bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, violet or blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
