Calamintha stenostoma

Calamintha stenostoma, commonly known as narrow-leaved calamint, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features slender, aromatic leaves with a serrated margin and produces small, tubular blue or violet flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Calamintha stenostoma is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its calming and digestive properties.
