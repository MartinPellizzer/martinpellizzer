Cynorkis angustipetala var. tananarivensis

Cynorkis angustipetala var. tananarivensis, commonly known as the Tananarivensis Cynorkis, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall compact appearance.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
