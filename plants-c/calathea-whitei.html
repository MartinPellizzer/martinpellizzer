Calathea whitei

Calathea whitei, commonly known as the White Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricately patterned leaves that display a combination of white, green, and silver hues. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil. Its foliage is often described as having a metallic sheen, which enhances its ornamental appeal.

Calathea whitei is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
