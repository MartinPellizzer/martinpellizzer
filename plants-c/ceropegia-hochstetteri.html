Ceropegia hochstetteri

Ceropegia hochstetteri, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia hochstetteri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
