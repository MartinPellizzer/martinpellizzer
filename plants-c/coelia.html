Coelia

Coelia, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are alternate and often covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Coelia is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
