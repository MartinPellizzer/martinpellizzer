Chenopodium vachellii

Chenopodium vachellii, commonly known as prairie sandgrass, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with small, greenish-white flowers. Chenopodium vachellii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and thrive in harsh environments.
