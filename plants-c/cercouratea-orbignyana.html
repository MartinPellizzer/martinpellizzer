Cercouratea orbignyana

Cercouratea orbignyana, commonly known as the Brazilian rosewood, is a tropical tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its dark, dense wood that is highly valued in musical instrument making. Due to overharvesting, it is now considered an endangered species.
