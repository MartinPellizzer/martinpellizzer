Carduus nigrescens subsp. nigrescens

Carduus nigrescens subsp.

nigrescens, commonly known as the blackthorn sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and heart-shaped, hairy leaves.

The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, dark purple to black seeds. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

It has been used traditionally in herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.
