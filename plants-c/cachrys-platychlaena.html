Cachrys platychlaena

Cachrys platychlaena, commonly known as the broad-leafed heath, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with thick, leathery leaves that are typically broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cachrys platychlaena is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
