Capnorea californica

Capnorea californica, commonly known as the California poppy, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features showy, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and grows in a low, spreading habit. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, open habitats.

Capnorea californica is also the state flower of California and is known for its medicinal and ornamental value.
