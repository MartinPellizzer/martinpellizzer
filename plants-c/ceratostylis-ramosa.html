Ceratostylis ramosa

Ceratostylis ramosa, commonly known as the branched spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with numerous short, branching inflorescences. The flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive, intricate structure with a spurred labellum. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often in rainforest understoreys.

The plant is notable for its unique floral morphology and is a subject of interest in botanical studies.
