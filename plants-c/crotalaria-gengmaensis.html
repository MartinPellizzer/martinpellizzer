Crotalaria gengmaensis

Crotalaria gengmaensis, commonly known as the Gengma stinkweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves with a pinnate structure. It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria gengmaensis is known for its strong, often unpleasant odor, which is a common trait among species in the Crotalaria genus.
