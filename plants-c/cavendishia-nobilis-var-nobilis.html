Cavendishia nobilis var. nobilis

Cavendishia nobilis var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble cavendishia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length. The plant produces striking, showy flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
