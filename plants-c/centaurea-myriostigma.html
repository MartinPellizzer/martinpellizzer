Centaurea myriostigma

Centaurea myriostigma, commonly known as the common centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and features narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed soils.

Centaurea myriostigma is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
