Carex × pseudonutans

Carex × pseudonutans, commonly known as the hybrid sedges, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and produces spikes of small, usually green or brown flowers. This plant is often found in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The plant's botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
