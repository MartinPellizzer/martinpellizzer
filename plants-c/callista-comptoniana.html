Callista comptoniana

Callista comptoniana, commonly known as the Compton's callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces yellow-orange blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Callista comptoniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
