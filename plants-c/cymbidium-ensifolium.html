Cymbidium ensifolium

Cymbidium ensifolium, commonly known as the sword-leaf orchid, is a species native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a perennial herb with tall, erect stems that bear lance-shaped leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium ensifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
