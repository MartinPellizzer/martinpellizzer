Ceratostema rigidum

Ceratostema rigidum, commonly known as the rigid horned grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, stiff stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an interesting addition to xeriscapes.
