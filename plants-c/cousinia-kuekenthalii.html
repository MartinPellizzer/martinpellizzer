Cousinia kuekenthalii

Cousinia kuekenthalii, commonly known as the Kuekenthal's cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to harsh, dry environments. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Cousinia kuekenthalii is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal.
