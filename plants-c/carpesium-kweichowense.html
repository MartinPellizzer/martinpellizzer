Carpesium kweichowense

Carpesium kweichowense, commonly known as the Yunnan cowplant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers with prominent central disk. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
