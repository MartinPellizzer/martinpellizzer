Campyloneurum pichinchae

It is native to the Andean regions of Ecuador and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often on rocky or forested slopes. Campyloneurum pichinchae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
