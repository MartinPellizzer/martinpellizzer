Ctenodon carichanicus

Ctenodon carichanicus, commonly known as the Caribbean firecracker, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline environments. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, which helps it retain water in arid conditions.

Ctenodon carichanicus is often found in sandy soils and is considered an important species for local biodiversity.
