Calliandra yunckeri

Calliandra yunckeri, commonly known as the yellow calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features delicate, feather-like leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies adds ecological value to gardens.
