Chrysanthemum palmatum

Chrysanthemum palmatum, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.

Its foliage is palmately lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance that adds visual interest to the garden.
