Carex indica var. milnei

Carex indica var. milnei, commonly known as the Milne's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes grass-like plants known as sedges. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a dark green color and a rough texture.

The flowers are small and clustered in spikelets, which are characteristic of the sedge family.
