Commelina prostrata

Commelina prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shady environments. The plant features small, arrow-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. It has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread easily through vegetative reproduction.

Commelina prostrata is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and forest floors where it thrives in high humidity.
