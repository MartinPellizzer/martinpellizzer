Croton ypanemensis

Croton ypanemensis, commonly known as the Ypanemense croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in Brazil.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including red, yellow, and green. Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
