Cryptostylis apiculata

Cryptostylis apiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sun orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to eastern Australia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understoreys. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale yellow to greenish-yellow, with a distinctive, elongated spur.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and ecological significance.
