Colpodium variegatum var. leianthum

Colpodium variegatum var. leianthum, commonly known as the variegated colpodium, is a variant of the colpodium plant characterized by its distinctive leaf patterns.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The leaves are typically lance-shaped with a bluish-green color and white or yellow variegation, which gives it its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
