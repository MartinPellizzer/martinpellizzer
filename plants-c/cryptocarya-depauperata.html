Cryptocarya depauperata

Cryptocarya depauperata, commonly known as the slender cryptocarya, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, berry-like fruits.

Cryptocarya depauperata is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
