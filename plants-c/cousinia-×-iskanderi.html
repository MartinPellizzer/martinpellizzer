Cousinia × iskanderi

Cousinia × iskanderi, commonly known as the hybrid snowdrop, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit with dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features silver-gray foliage with a woolly texture, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Cousinia × iskanderi is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
