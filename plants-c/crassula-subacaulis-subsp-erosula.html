Crassula subacaulis subsp. erosula

Crassula subacaulis subsp.

erosula, commonly known as the rock rose or red crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, upright growth with dense, fleshy leaves that are typically a reddish or pinkish hue.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red, usually during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
