Cola attiensis var. bodardii

Cola attiensis var. bodardii, commonly known as Bodard's cola, is a species native to western Africa.

It is a member of the Sterculiaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and showy, red or orange flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, tropical lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

The species is also occasionally cultivated for its potential medicinal properties.
