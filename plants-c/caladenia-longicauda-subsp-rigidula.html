Caladenia longicauda subsp. rigidula

Caladenia longicauda subsp.

rigidula, commonly known as the rigid tail spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Australia. It features a slender, erect stem with a single or occasionally two flowers that are typically pale green to yellowish in color.

The flowers have a distinctive, narrow spur and are often found in open, sandy habitats. The plant produces a single, linear leaf that is often covered in fine hairs.

It blooms during late winter to early spring, contributing to the region's floral diversity.
