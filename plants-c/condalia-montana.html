Condalia montana

Condalia montana, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

Condalia montana is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
