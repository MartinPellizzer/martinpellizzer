Crepidomanes × amabile

Crepidomanes × amabile, commonly known as the hybrid sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Crepidomanes acutifolium and Crepidomanes lanceolatum. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodland or grassy areas.

Its long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for dried flower arrangements.
