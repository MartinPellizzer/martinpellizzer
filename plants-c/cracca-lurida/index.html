Cracca Lurida

Cracca lurida is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure, it thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures. This species is often found in rocky soils and alpine meadows where it adapts to harsh climatic conditions. Due to its limited habitat and slow growth rate, Cracca lurida is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
