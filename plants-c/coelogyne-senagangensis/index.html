Coelogyne Senagangensis

Coelogyne senagangensis is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the mountainous regions of Thailand and Malaysia. This species is known for its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Due to its beauty and scarcity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts worldwide. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and over-collection.
