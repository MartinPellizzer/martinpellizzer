Chytraculia poeppigiana

Chytraculia poeppigiana, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in bright yellow spines, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and collections.
