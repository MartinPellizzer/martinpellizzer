Cortaderia vaginata

Cortaderia vaginata, commonly known as the silver grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, slender blades that are covered in a silvery-white bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that add movement and texture to gardens. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for perennial borders and landscapes.

Its drought tolerance and minimal maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among gardeners.
