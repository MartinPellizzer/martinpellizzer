Cattleya purpurata var. nelisii

Cattleya purpurata var. nelisii, commonly known as the Nelisii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It features large, showy flowers with vibrant purple and white petals, often adorned with intricate markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in the tropical climate.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is popular among orchid enthusiasts.
