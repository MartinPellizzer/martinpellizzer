Ceratostylis latipetala

Ceratostylis latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, often branched stem with several leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. The plant produces a single or few racemes of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with long, slender sepals and petals. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the acidic soils of its natural habitat.

This orchid is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
