Cuphea carajasensis

Cuphea carajasensis, commonly known as the red flame cuphea, is a striking flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround its small, tubular flowers. The plant produces showy inflorescences that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long blooming period.
