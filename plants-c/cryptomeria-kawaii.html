Cryptomeria kawaii

Cryptomeria kawaii, commonly known as the Japanese red cedar, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It belongs to the cypress family (Cupressaceae) and is characterized by its slender, conical shape and reddish-brown bark. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain winged seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its foliage consists of scale-like leaves that are typically reddish in color, giving the tree its distinctive appearance.

Cryptomeria kawaii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
