Cercidiphyllum japonicum f. pendulum

Cercidiphyllum japonicum f. pendulum, commonly known as the weeping Japanese maple, is a unique cultivar of the Japanese maple tree.

It is characterized by its cascading, drooping branches that create a dramatic, flowing appearance. The plant features delicate, palmate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. It thrives in partial to full shade and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This ornamental tree is prized for its aesthetic appeal and is often used in landscaping for its visual interest throughout the growing season.
