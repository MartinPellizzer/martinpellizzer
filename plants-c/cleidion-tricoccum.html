Cleidion tricoccum

Cleidion tricoccum, commonly known as the sweetgum or American sweetgum, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and spherical, spiky fruit capsules.
