Croton integrilobus

Croton integrilobus, commonly known as the smooth-leafed croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that are typically smooth on both surfaces. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
