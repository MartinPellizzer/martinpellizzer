Cynanchum sibiricum var. latifolium

Cynanchum sibiricum var. latifolium, commonly known as Siberian milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a distinctive waxy coating.

This species is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in steppe and forest-steppe habitats.
