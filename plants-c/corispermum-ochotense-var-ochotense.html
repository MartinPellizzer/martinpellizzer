Corispermum ochotense var. ochotense

Corispermum ochotense var. ochotense, commonly known as the Siberian sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the cold climates of Eastern Siberia.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are succulent and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in arid environments.
