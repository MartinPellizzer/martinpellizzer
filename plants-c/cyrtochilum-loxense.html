Cyrtochilum loxense

Cyrtochilum loxense, commonly known as the Loxense Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil, and it has a short, stocky stem.

Cyrtochilum loxense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
