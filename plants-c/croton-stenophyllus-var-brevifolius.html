Croton stenophyllus var. brevifolius

Croton stenophyllus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers followed by distinctive, spiky, red or orange fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
