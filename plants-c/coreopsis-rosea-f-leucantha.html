Coreopsis rosea f. leucantha

Coreopsis rosea f. leucantha, commonly known as the white coreopsis, is a hybrid form of the common coreopsis.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which contrast with the typical orange and red hues of the species. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. It produces feathery, lance-shaped leaves and has a compact growth habit.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation in gardens.
