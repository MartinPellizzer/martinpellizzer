Crinum moorei var. variegatum

Crinum moorei var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves with distinctive white or yellow variegation. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
