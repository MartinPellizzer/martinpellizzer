Centaurea macrorrhiza var. toletana

Centaurea macrorrhiza var. toletana, commonly known as the Toletan plume thistle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features tall, sparsely branched stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces striking, feathery purple to pink flowers arranged in dense, conical clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped, with rough, hairy surfaces and distinct lobes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.
