Cistus ladanifer f. guttatus

Cistus ladanifer f. guttatus, commonly known as the glandular rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that secrete a sticky, resinous substance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
