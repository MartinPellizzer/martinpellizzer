Calanthe maxii

Calanthe maxii, commonly known as the Maxii Calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and its clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid air.

Calanthe maxii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
