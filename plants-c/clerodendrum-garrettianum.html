Clerodendrum garrettianum

Clerodendrum garrettianum, commonly known as the pink flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Clerodendrum garrettianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
