Chlorophytum glaucoides

Chlorophytum glaucoides, commonly known as the Variegated Spider Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in spring or early summer. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.

Its ability to propagate easily through runners makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
