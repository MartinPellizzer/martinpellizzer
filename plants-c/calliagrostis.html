Calliagrostis

Calliagrostis, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a genus of grasses native to North America.

These plants are characterized by their tall, slender stems and silver-gray foliage, which gives them their common name. They are typically found in dry, open habitats such as prairies and woodlands. Calliagrostis species are often used in landscaping for their ornamental value and drought tolerance.

The genus includes several species, with Calliagrostis arida and Calliagrostis tabernaemontana being among the most well-known.
