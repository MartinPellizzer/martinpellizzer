Cucurbita coccinea

Cucurbita coccinea, commonly known as the red-orange bell pepper, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful fruits that range from orange to red. The plant produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom before the fruits develop. Its fruits are edible and commonly used in cooking, while the plant also has ornamental value due to its striking appearance.

Cucurbita coccinea thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
