Clerodendrum dusenii

Clerodendrum dusenii, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular, red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Clerodendrum dusenii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
