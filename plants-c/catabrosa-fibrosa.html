Catabrosa fibrosa

Catabrosa fibrosa, commonly known as the fibrous catabrosa, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered with fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Catabrosa fibrosa is known for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
