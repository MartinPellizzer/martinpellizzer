Cubeba celebica

Cubeba celebica, commonly known as cubeb, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark reddish-brown berries. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a spice, valued for its strong, pungent aroma.
