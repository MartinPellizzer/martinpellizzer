Cotoneaster tytthocarpus

Cotoneaster tytthocarpus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features dense, compact growth with small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers in the spring, followed by red or orange berries in the autumn. Cotoneaster tytthocarpus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its seasonal color changes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to a variety of garden environments.
