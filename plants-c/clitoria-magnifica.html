Clitoria magnifica

Clitoria magnifica, commonly known as the sacred bean or butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the plant thrives in warm, humid climates.

Clitoria magnifica is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
