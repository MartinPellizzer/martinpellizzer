Caladenia filamentosa var. pallens

Caladenia filamentosa var. pallens, commonly known as the pale spider orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to southern Australia.

This variant is characterized by its slender, pale pink or cream-colored flowers that resemble spiders, with long, filamentous sepals and petals. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats, often in heathlands or along coastal areas. The plant produces a single upright stem with a few flowers, and its blooms are short-lived but highly ornamental.

It is a rare and endangered species, requiring specific environmental conditions for successful growth and reproduction.
