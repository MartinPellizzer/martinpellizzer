Cryptocoryne walkeri var. lutea

Cryptocoryne walkeri var. lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to golden-green leaves, which provide a striking contrast in aquarium settings. This variety thrives in low to moderate light conditions and prefers soft to moderately hard water with a slightly acidic to neutral pH. It is a slow-growing plant that benefits from regular pruning to maintain its shape and encourage new growth.

Cryptocoryne walkeri var. lutea is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care, making it a favorite among aquarists.
