Camellia kirinoi

Camellia kirinoi, commonly known as the Japanese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant blooms.

Camellia kirinoi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
