Centella linifolia var. linifolia

Centella linifolia var. linifolia, commonly known as Asiatic pennywort, is a small, creeping herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features rounded, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its leaves are widely used in traditional medicine for their purported skin-healing properties.
