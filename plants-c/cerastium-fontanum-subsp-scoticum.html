Cerastium fontanum subsp. scoticum

Cerastium fontanum subsp.

scoticum, commonly known as Scottish mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the British Isles. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit.
