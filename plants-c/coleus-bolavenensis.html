Coleus bolavenensis

Coleus bolavenensis, commonly known as the Bolaven coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant for its striking leaves.

Coleus bolavenensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts.
