Celmisia laricifolia

Celmisia laricifolia, commonly known as the silver tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of white and yellow. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.
