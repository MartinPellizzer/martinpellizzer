Clematis mandshurica var. lathyrifolia

Clematis mandshurica var. lathyrifolia, commonly known as the Siberian Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five ovate leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, appearing in late summer. The plant produces small, cylindrical fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
