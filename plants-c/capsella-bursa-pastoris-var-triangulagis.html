Capsella bursa-pastoris var. triangulagis

Capsella bursa-pastoris var. triangulagis, commonly known as the triangular weed or tri-lobed chickweed, is a variant of the common chickweed.

It is a low-growing annual herb with trifoliate leaves that are distinctly triangular in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its seed pods are hairy and triangular, giving it a unique appearance.

This variant is often found in disturbed soils and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
