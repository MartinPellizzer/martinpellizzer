Calamagrostis × kotulae

Calamagrostis × kotulae, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a hybrid grass species native to the northeastern United States.

It is characterized by its graceful, arching silver-green foliage and feathery plumes that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in riparian areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for wetland restoration and water garden landscapes.
