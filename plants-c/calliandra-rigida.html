Calliandra rigida

Calliandra rigida, commonly known as the rigid calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features compound leaves with numerous small, elliptical leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom throughout the dry season. It is adapted to drought conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
