Chrysanthemum argyranthemoides

Chrysanthemum argyranthemoides, commonly known as silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its ornamental value comes from both its foliage and its long-lasting blooms.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
