Croton pomaderris

Croton pomaderris, commonly known as the silky croton, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by woody capsules containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its unique foliage, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
