Clarisia mollis

Clarisia mollis, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its attractive, waxy, silver-green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is evergreen and thrives in humid, tropical climates. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.

Clarisia mollis is also known for its ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.
