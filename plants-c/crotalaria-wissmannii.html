Crotalaria wissmannii

Crotalaria wissmannii, commonly known as the wissmann's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmate leaves. The plant produces bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
