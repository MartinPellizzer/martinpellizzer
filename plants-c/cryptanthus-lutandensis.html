Cryptanthus lutandensis

Cryptanthus lutandensis, commonly known as the Lutandensis carpet plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Haemodoraceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its dense, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and compact growth habit.

Cryptanthus lutandensis thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
