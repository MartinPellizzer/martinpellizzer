Chlorophytum chinense

Chlorophytum chinense, commonly known as the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often have white stripes, and it produces small, grass-like flowers in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and is highly tolerant of different soil types. It is also popular for its air-purifying qualities and ease of care, making it a favored houseplant.

Its ability to produce plantlets along its stems adds to its ornamental value and propagation potential.
