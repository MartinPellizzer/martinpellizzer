Cyclobalanopsis glauca var. linearifolia

Cyclobalanopsis glauca var. linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed evergreen oak, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The leaves are elongated and narrow, with a smooth, glossy surface and a bluish-green color. It produces small, brownish flowers followed by hard, woody fruits containing one or two seeds.

This species is valued for its durable timber and is often found in mixed forests.
