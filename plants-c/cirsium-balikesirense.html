Cirsium balikesirense

Cirsium balikesirense, commonly known as the Balikesir thistle, is a perennial herb native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
