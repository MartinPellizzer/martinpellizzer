Cyperus cleaveri

Cyperus cleaveri, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and other moist environments, often forming dense stands. Its slender, grass-like stems are usually less than 1 meter tall, with narrow, linear leaves.

Cyperus cleaveri produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the sedge family.
