Condalia brandegeei

Condalia brandegeei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces woody, spiny fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal display make it a notable species in its native habitat.
