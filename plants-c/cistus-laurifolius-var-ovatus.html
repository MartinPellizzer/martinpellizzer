Cistus laurifolius var. ovatus

Cistus laurifolius var. ovatus, commonly known as the broad-leaved rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with oval-shaped leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its aromatic leaves and flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and traditional herbal uses.
