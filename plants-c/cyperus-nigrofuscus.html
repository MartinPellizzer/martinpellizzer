Cyperus nigrofuscus

Cyperus nigrofuscus, commonly known as blackish flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands. Its morphology features narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences.

Cyperus nigrofuscus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both freshwater and brackish environments.
