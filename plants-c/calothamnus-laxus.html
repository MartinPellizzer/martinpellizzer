Calothamnus laxus

Calothamnus laxus, commonly known as the slender sickle-hillary, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and distinctive, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions. It is a shrub that can reach up to 1 meter in height, with small, clustered inflorescences.

Calothamnus laxus is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
