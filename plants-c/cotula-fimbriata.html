Cotula fimbriata

Cotula fimbriata, commonly known as the silverleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with silver-gray foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Cotula fimbriata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
