Ceropegia illegitima

Ceropegia illegitima, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a trailing vine that produces delicate, tubular flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
