Chlorophytum graminifolium

Chlorophytum graminifolium, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a distinctive yellow or white stripe running down the center. This plant is noted for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening. The leaves are succulent, allowing the plant to store water efficiently, which contributes to its resilience.

Additionally, it is known for its air-purifying qualities, helping to improve indoor air quality.
