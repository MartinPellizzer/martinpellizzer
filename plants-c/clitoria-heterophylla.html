Clitoria heterophylla

Clitoria heterophylla, commonly known as the variable blue pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy, butterfly-like blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, lens-shaped pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Clitoria heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
