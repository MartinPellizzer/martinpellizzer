Centaurea pallidior subsp. vatevii

Centaurea pallidior subsp.

vatevii, commonly known as the pale centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, yellow to orange composite flowers.

The subspecies is native to southern Europe and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
