Crassocephalum miniatum

Crassocephalum miniatum, commonly known as the miniature-headed sunflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive inflorescence structure. The plant features small, yellow-orange flowers arranged in a dense, rounded head, giving it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and vibrant blooms.
