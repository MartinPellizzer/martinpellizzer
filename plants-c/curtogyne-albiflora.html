Curtogyne albiflora

Curtogyne albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments.

Curtogyne albiflora is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
