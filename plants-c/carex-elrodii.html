Carex elrodii

Carex elrodii, commonly known as Elrod's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in swampy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often have a bluish-green hue.

Elrod's sedge produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikes, which are typically pollinated by wind.
