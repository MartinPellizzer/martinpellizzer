Cattleya mendelii

Cattleya mendelii, commonly known as the Mendel's cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features long, arching pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, arranged in a fan shape, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Cattleya mendelii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
