Campanula lusitanica f. tenuis

Campanula lusitanica f. tenuis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a compact, flowering perennial native to southern Europe.

It features delicate, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
