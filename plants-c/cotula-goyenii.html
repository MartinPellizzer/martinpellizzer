Cotula goyenii

Cotula goyenii, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow, daisy-like flowers. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cotula goyenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
