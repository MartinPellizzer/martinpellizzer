Crepis meifenggensis

Crepis meifenggensis, commonly known as the Meifenggengensis mouse-ear, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its basal rosette of narrow, hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically grouped in clusters. It thrives in moist, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.
