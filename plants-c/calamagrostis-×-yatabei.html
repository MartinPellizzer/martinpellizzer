Calamagrostis × yatabei

Calamagrostis × yatabei, commonly known as the Japanese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes that turn silver-white in the fall. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that are often used in ornamental landscaping. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Calamagrostis × yatabei is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden conditions.
