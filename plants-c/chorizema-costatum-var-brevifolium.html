Chorizema costatum var. brevifolium

Chorizema costatum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chorizema, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in its natural habitat.
