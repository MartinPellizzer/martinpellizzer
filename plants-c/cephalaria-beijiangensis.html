Cephalaria beijiangensis

Cephalaria beijiangensis, commonly known as the Beijiang cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowering period occurs in the summer months, producing yellow to orange ray florets.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
