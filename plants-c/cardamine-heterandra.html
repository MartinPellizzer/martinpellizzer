Cardamine heterandra

Cardamine heterandra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved water shield, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions. Its leaves are alternate, with a distinctive basal rosette, and it produces small, white or greenish flowers that bloom in early spring.
