Cabralea estrellensis

Cabralea estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellan candelilla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its seeds are enclosed in a hairy pod, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
