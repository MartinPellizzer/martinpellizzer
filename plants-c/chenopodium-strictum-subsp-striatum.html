Chenopodium strictum subsp. striatum

Chenopodium strictum subsp.

striatum, commonly known as the slender goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America. It typically grows in disturbed soils such as roadsides, fields, and waste areas.

The plant has alternate, lanceolate leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense, compact inflorescences.

The seeds are tiny, dry, and often dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
