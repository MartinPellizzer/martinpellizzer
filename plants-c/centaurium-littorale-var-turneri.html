Centaurium littorale var. turneri

Centaurium littorale var. turneri, commonly known as the coastal centaury, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of North America.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright red to orange flowers, which are typically tubular in shape. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils along the shorelines and is adapted to salt spray and wind exposure.

This species is often found in dune ecosystems and is an important part of coastal vegetation.
