Capsella bursa-pastoris cuneolata

Capsella bursa-pastoris cuneolata, commonly known as the common chickweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by heart-shaped, spiny seed pods. The plant has prostrate or upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly toothed margin.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.
