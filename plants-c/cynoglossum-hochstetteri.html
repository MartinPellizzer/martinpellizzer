Cynoglossum hochstetteri

Cynoglossum hochstetteri, commonly known as the Hochstetter's bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features attractive blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and easy care requirements.
