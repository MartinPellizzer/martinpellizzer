Cotoneaster gracilis var. gracilis

Cotoneaster gracilis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
