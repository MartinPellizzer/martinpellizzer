Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. intermedia

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris var. intermedia, commonly known as the Variegated Umbilicus Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with striking white or yellow variegation. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the spring or summer.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
