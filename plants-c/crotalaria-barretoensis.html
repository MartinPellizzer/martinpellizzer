Crotalaria barretoensis

Crotalaria barretoensis, commonly known as the Barreto's cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and research settings.
