Carduus × alleizettei

Carduus × alleizettei, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant that belongs to the Asteraceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Carduus nutans and Carduus acanthoides, resulting in a plant with unique morphological characteristics. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in various regions across Europe. It features spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
