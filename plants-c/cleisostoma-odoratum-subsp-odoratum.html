Cleisostoma odoratum subsp. odoratum

Cleisostoma odoratum subsp.

odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its pendant, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive greenish throat.

The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is prized for its delicate fragrance and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
