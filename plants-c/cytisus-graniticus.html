Cytisus graniticus

Cytisus graniticus, commonly known as the granite broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and slender, often spiny stems. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are small, leathery, and typically arranged in pairs.

Cytisus graniticus is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
