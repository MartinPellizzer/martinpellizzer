Coelogyne distans

Coelogyne distans, commonly known as the orchid of the forest, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are often used in traditional floral arrangements due to their elegance and longevity.

Coelogyne distans is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
