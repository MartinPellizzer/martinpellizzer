Celosia trigyna var. subdensiflora

Celosia trigyna var. subdensiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered cockscomb, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that can be red, orange, or yellow, depending on the variety. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for containers or garden borders. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering during the growing season.

Its unique appearance and ease of cultivation make it a popular choice for both gardeners and florists.
