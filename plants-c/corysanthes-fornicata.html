Corysanthes fornicata

Corysanthes fornicata, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like inflorescences that display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is known for its intricate floral structure, which includes specialized petals and sepals. Corysanthes fornicata thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is often found in coastal regions.

Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a notable species in Australian botany.
