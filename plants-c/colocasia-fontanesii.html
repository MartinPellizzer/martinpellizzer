Colocasia fontanesii

Colocasia fontanesii, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a tropical perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
