Commelina chamissonis

Commelina chamissonis, commonly known as the blue commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Commelina chamissonis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
