Centaurea haynaldii subsp. julica

Centaurea haynaldii subsp.

julica, commonly known as the Julica thistle, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, such as slopes and grasslands.

The plant features upright stems with spiny leaves and produces showy purple to pink flowers in summer. Its bracts and involucres are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soils.
