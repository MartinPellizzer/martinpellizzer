Cliffortia alnifolia

Cliffortia alnifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf carnation, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to arid environments. It is a member of the carnation family (Caryophyllaceae) and is often found in mountainous regions.
