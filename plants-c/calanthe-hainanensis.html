Calanthe hainanensis

Calanthe hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the island of Hainan in China.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing several flowers with showy, often white or pale pink petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Calanthe hainanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
