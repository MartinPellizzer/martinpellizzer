Chlorophytum holotrichum

Chlorophytum holotrichum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that are followed by green, three-lobed berries. It is a hardy plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum holotrichum is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and ability to propagate through offsets.
