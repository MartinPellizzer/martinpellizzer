Cornulaca amblyacantha

Cornulaca amblyacantha, commonly known as desert caryopsis, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa, the Middle East, and parts of Asia.

Cornulaca amblyacantha is also valued for its drought tolerance and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
