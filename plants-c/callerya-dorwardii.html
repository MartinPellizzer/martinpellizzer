Callerya dorwardii

Callerya dorwardii, commonly known as the Dorward's callerya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, often fragrant flowers. Callerya dorwardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
