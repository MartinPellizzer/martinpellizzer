Conophytum stipitatum

Conophytum stipitatum, commonly known as the "Cape Karoo stone plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance with fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic gardens.

Its unique morphology and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
