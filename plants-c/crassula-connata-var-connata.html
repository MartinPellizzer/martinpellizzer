Crassula connata var. connata

Crassula connata var. connata, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy foliage that forms a compact, shrub-like structure. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy texture that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
