Celmisia spectabilis var. magnifica

Celmisia spectabilis var. magnifica, commonly known as the showy celmisia, is a striking perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, pale yellow to golden-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its flowers are typically arranged in dense, terminal inflorescences, adding a dramatic visual impact to the landscape.

Celmisia spectabilis var. magnifica thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested or rocky habitats.
