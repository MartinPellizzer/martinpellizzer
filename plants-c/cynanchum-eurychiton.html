Cynanchum eurychiton

Cynanchum eurychiton, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its erect stem and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by follicles containing seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Its roots contain alkaloids and are valued for their medicinal properties.
