Chrysopteris lanosa

Chrysopteris lanosa, commonly known as the woolly chrysopteris, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Chrysopteris lanosa thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
