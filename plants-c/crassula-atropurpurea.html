Crassula atropurpurea

Crassula atropurpurea, commonly known as the Forest Giant or Red Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically reddish-purple in color, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
