Cotoneaster acutifolius var. laetevirens

Cotoneaster acutifolius var. laetevirens, commonly known as the golden cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant green in spring, turning yellow in autumn. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
