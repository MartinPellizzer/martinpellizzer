Celosia whitei

Celosia whitei, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking ornamental plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant is typically upright, with narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Celosia whitei is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
