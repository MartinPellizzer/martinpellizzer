Cryptostylis arachnites var. taiwaniana

Cryptostylis arachnites var. taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan cryptostylis, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. The plant produces erect, slender stems with few leaves and blooms during the spring and early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions with high humidity.

This variety is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
