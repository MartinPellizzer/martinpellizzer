Chaetoseris bonatii

Chaetoseris bonatii, commonly known as the Bonatti's hairy aster, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale blue petals. Its leaves are typically hairy and grayish-green, adapted to reduce water loss.

It is often found in open habitats such as coastal areas and limestone formations.
