Cynanchum moratii

Cynanchum moratii, commonly known as Morat's milkweed, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release silky, wind-dispersed seeds.

Cynanchum moratii is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
