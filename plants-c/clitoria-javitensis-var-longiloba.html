Clitoria javitensis var. longiloba

Clitoria javitensis var. longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, butterfly-like blue or violet flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species in both horticulture and ecological studies.
