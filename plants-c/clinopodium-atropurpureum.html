Clinopodium atropurpureum

Clinopodium atropurpureum, commonly known as purple moss campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine environments.

Clinopodium atropurpureum is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
