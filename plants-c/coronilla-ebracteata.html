Coronilla ebracteata

Coronilla ebracteata, commonly known as the slender vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features slender, erect stems with small, trifoliate leaves. Its flowers are pea-like, ranging in color from white to purple, and are borne in clusters.

Coronilla ebracteata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
