Celosia leptostachya var. robusta

Celosia leptostachya var. robusta, commonly known as the flame flower or cookea, is a striking ornamental plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, feather-like bracts that range in color from red to orange and yellow, creating a fiery display. The plant is a herbaceous annual, typically growing up to 1 meter in height, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its unique appearance and long-lasting blooms make it a valuable addition to both cultivated and natural landscapes.
