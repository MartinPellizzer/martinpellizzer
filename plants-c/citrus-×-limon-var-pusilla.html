Citrus × limon var. pusilla

Citrus × limon var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf lemon.

This variety is a small citrus plant characterized by its compact growth habit and small, round leaves. It produces small, yellow fruits that are typically less than an inch in diameter. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and fragrant foliage.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and regular watering.
