Centaurea linifolia var. caballeroi

Centaurea linifolia var. caballeroi, commonly known as the silver centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
