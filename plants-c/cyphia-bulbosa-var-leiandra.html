Cyphia bulbosa var. leiandra

Cyphia bulbosa var. leiandra, commonly known as the Lemon Tree or Lemon Bush, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its aromatic, lemon-scented leaves and clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
