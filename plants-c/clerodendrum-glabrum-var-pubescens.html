Clerodendrum glabrum var. pubescens

Clerodendrum glabrum var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "pubescens.". The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
