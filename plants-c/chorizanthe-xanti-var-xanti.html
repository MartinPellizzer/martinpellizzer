Chorizanthe xanti var. xanti

Chorizanthe xanti var. xanti, commonly known as desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, dry fruits known as schizocarps, which split into two one-seeded parts upon maturity. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
