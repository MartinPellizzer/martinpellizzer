Conophytum simile

Conophytum simile, commonly known as the "stone plant" or "cushion plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that form a dense, rounded rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
