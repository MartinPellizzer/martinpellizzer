Cnicus leducii

Cnicus leducii, commonly known as the sand rocket, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial plant that typically reaches a height of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a rough, sandpaper-like texture, and the stems are often covered in fine hairs.

Cnicus leducii is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in disturbed or coastal areas.
