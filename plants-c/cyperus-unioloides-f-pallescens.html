Cyperus unioloides f. pallescens

Cyperus unioloides f. pallescens, commonly known as the pale Cyperus, is a variant of the Cyperus unioloides species.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, thriving in environments with consistent moisture.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically important plant in wetland ecosystems.
