Clidemia coccinea

Clidemia coccinea, commonly known as red-leaved false hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

Clidemia coccinea is also known for its ability to spread rapidly, making it an invasive species in some regions.
