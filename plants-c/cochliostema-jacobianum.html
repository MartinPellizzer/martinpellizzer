Cochliostema jacobianum

Cochliostema jacobianum, commonly known as the Jacobian's cochliostema, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Cochliostema jacobianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
