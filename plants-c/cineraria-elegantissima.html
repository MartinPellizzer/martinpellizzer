Cineraria elegantissima

Cineraria elegantissima, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery, woolly leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Cineraria elegantissima is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for xeriscaping in Mediterranean climates.
