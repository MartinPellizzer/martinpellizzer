Candollea exasperata

Candollea exasperata, commonly known as the spiny fern, is a unique member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, pinnately divided fronds that are often covered in small, spiny structures. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of its leaves.

The plant's distinctive appearance and adaptability make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
