Colocasia gaoligongensis

Colocasia gaoligongensis, commonly known as the Gaoligong mountain taro, is a rare species native to the Yunnan province in China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 50 cm in diameter, often with a dark green to almost black coloration. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in mountainous regions with high humidity. It belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other familiar plants like taro and caladium.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
