Cissampelos violifolia

Cissampelos violifolia, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, palmate leaves with a distinctive blue-green coloration. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the early morning, giving it its common name. It is often found in moist, shady habitats such as forests and stream banks.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
