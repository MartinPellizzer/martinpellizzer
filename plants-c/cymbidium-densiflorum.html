Cymbidium densiflorum

Cymbidium densiflorum, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically produces large, lance-shaped leaves and blooms in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium densiflorum is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and cultural significance in traditional Chinese gardens.
