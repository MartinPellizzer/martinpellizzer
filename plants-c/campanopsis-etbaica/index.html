Campanopsis Etbaica

Campanopsis etbaica is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Ethiopia. Known for its bell-shaped, violet-blue flowers, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike with its unique appearance. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous areas with rich soil. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for conservation efforts and botanical research.
