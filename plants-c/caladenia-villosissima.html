Caladenia villosissima

Caladenia villosissima, commonly known as the Hairy Spider-Orchid, is a striking orchid native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and its unique, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in heathland and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Caladenia villosissima is a protected species due to its limited distribution and sensitivity to environmental changes.
