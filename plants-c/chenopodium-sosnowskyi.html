Chenopodium sosnowskyi

Chenopodium sosnowskyi, commonly known as Sosnowsky's goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, arid environments. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a rough, sandpaper-like texture. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and are wind-pollinated.

The plant is known for its high salt tolerance, making it adaptable to saline soils.
