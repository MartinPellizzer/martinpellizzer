Croton ophiticola

Croton ophiticola, commonly known as the black croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo and Uganda. The plant's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in suitable climates.
