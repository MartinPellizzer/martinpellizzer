Cortaderia araucana var. skottsbergii

Cortaderia araucana var. skottsbergii, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, arching plumes that turn a silvery-white color as they mature, creating a dramatic visual effect in gardens. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are dark green with a slight metallic sheen, adding texture and contrast. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a versatile addition to both residential and public gardens.
