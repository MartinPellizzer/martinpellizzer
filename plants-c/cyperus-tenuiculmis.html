Cyperus tenuiculmis

Cyperus tenuiculmis, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swales, and along the edges of water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often inconspicuous. It is native to parts of Africa and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening due to its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils.

Cyperus tenuiculmis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for aquatic insects and small vertebrates.
