Cyrtochilum rodrigoi

Cyrtochilum rodrigoi, commonly known as the Rodrigues orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to the island of Rodrigues in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, showy blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. Cyrtochilum rodrigoi thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the unique microclimate of its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
