Camellia lutchuensis var. lutchuensis

Camellia lutchuensis var. lutchuensis, commonly known as the Lushan camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces striking red or pink flowers during winter. The plant is a member of the Theaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade for optimal growth.

This variety is also notable for its historical significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
