Clerodendrum excavatum var. rotundatum

Clerodendrum excavatum var. rotundatum, commonly known as the Round-leaved Flame Bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright red to orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The leaves are broadly ovate with a rounded base and are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
