Cotoneaster nefedovii

Cotoneaster nefedovii, commonly known as the Nefedovii cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, which are followed by bright red berries in the fall. These berries are an important food source for birds and other wildlife.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
