Callista boxaleii

Callista boxaleii, commonly known as the boxaleii daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Callista boxaleii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
