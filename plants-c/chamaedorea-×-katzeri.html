Chamaedorea × katzeri

Chamaedorea × katzeri, commonly known as the Katzer's bamboo palm, is a hybrid evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features gracefully arching fronds that give it a lush, tropical appearance, making it a popular choice for indoor spaces. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 feet, with a compact and upright form. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care make it a favored ornamental plant in both residential and commercial settings.
