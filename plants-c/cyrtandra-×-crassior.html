Cyrtandra × crassior

Cyrtandra × crassior, commonly known as the broad-leaved cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. Native to tropical regions of the Pacific, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.

Its growth habits make it a popular choice for greenhouse and indoor gardening.
