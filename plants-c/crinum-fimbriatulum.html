Crinum fimbriatulum

Crinum fimbriatulum, commonly known as the fringed crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, often with distinctive fringed petals. The plant produces a tall, upright stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Crinum fimbriatulum is a popular ornamental plant due to its dramatic floral display and resilience in various climates.
