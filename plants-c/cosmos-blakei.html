Cosmos blakei

Cosmos blakei, commonly known as Blake's cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features feathery, finely divided leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Cosmos blakei is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
