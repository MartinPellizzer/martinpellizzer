Coelogyne stenobulbum

Coelogyne stenobulbum, commonly known as the narrow-bulb coelogyne, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a short, cylindrical pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf. The plant produces a raceme of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with white markings. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
