Caladium bicolor var. stangeanum

Caladium bicolor var. stangeanum, commonly known as the Stangeanum caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with patterns of red, pink, and white on a green background. The leaves are heart-shaped and can grow up to 12 inches in diameter, making it a popular ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

This variety is often used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its vibrant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
