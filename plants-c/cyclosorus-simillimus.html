Cyclosorus simillimus

Cyclosorus simillimus, commonly known as the similar fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, creeping rhizomes and small, lanceolate leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. The plant thrives in shallow, still freshwater environments such as ponds and wetlands. Its fronds are usually unrolled and have a soft, feathery appearance.

Cyclosorus simillimus is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal in aquatic gardens.
