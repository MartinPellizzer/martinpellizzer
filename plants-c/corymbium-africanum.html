Corymbium africanum

Corymbium africanum, commonly known as the African sweet pea, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, twining stems that support its clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately compound, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Corymbium africanum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
