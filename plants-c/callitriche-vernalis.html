Callitriche vernalis

Callitriche vernalis, commonly known as spring starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic flowering plant that thrives in wetland environments.

It typically grows in shallow water or damp soil, often near the edges of ponds, streams, and ditches. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and ribbon-like, arranged in whorls around the stems.

Callitriche vernalis is an important component of wetland ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
