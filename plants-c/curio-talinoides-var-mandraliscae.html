Curio talinoides var. mandraliscae

Curio talinoides var. mandraliscae, commonly known as the Mandralisca Aloe, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of thick, fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often marked with red or purple streaks. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative indoor plant.
