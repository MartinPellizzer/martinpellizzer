Cymbidium pubescens var. celebicum

Cymbidium pubescens var. celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and the production of long, narrow pseudobulbs. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine, velvety pubescence.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
