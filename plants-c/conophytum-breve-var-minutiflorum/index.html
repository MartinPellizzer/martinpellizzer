Conophytum Breve Var. Minutiflorum

Conophytum breve var. minutiflorum is a striking member of the Aizoaceae family native to southern Africa. This compact succulent is known for its unique, rounded leaves and delicate, star-shaped flowers. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to flourish. Its small size and vibrant appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. This variety is particularly prized for its resilience and ornamental beauty.
