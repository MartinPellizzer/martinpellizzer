Calanthe habbemensis

Calanthe habbemensis, commonly known as the Habbemense Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and strikingly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, feature a combination of white and green petals with a distinctive lip.

Calanthe habbemensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
