Cyclopia

Cyclopia, commonly known as the "pearl of the veld," is a genus of flowering plants native to South Africa.

It belongs to the daisy family, Asteraceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, small, yellowish-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Cyclopia species are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

They are also popular in ornamental gardening due to their compact size and vibrant blooms.
