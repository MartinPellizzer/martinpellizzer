Chrysosplenium tenellum

Chrysosplenium tenellum, commonly known as the slender chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically features small, bright green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
