Calathea yawankama

Calathea yawankama, commonly known as the Yawan Kama calathea, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in its humid environment. It thrives in low light conditions and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care.
