Chamaecrista rufa

Chamaecrista rufa, commonly known as scarlet burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. Its leaves are bipinnately divided, giving it a feathery appearance.

Chamaecrista rufa is often found in open habitats such as roadsides, fields, and forest edges.
