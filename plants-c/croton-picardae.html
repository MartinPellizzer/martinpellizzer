Croton picardae

Croton picardae, commonly known as the Picard's croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves that can range from yellow to red and green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton picardae thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.
