Columnea darienensis

Columnea darienensis, commonly known as the Darien flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Columnea darienensis thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
