Chelonespermum unguiculatum

Chelonespermum unguiculatum, commonly known as the slender false heather, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces capsule fruits containing several seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
