Chenopodium stellatum

Chenopodium stellatum, commonly known as star goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate or upright growth habit. The plant has alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in dense, star-like inflorescences, giving it its common name.

Chenopodium stellatum is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
