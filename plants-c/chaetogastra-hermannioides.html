Chaetogastra hermannioides

Chaetogastra hermannioides, commonly known as the Hermann's thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features spiny, hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive lobed shape. Its flowers are yellow and appear in dense clusters, blooming from late spring to early autumn.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
