Coronaria × media

Coronaria × media, commonly known as the hybrid carnation, is a flowering plant in the genus *Coronaria* within the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, bicolor flowers that typically range from pink to red. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It is native to the Mediterranean region and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a variety of color and form variations, making it a popular choice for floral arrangements and landscaping.
