Coffea mexicana

Coffea mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican coffee plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and Central America, growing in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, red berries that contain the seeds used for coffee production. It typically thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.
