Chlorophytum paucinervatum

Chlorophytum paucinervatum, commonly known as the narrow-veined spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, and thrives in well-draining soil. The plant features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with a silvery underside. It produces small, white flowers in clusters, which are followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads.

This species is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
