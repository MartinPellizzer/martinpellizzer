Crinum Jagus

The Crinum jagus is a striking bulbous plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its large, showy flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens. Its unique appearance and resilience have made it a subject of both botanical study and horticultural interest. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds, requiring caution when handling.
