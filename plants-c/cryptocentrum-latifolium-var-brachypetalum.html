Cryptocentrum latifolium var. brachypetalum

Cryptocentrum latifolium var. brachypetalum, commonly known as the broad-leaved cryptocentrum, is a unique variant of the Cryptocentrum genus.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with a distinctive purple hue, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological study.
