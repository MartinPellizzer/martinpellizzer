Cicer arietinum var. azureocoloratum

Cicer arietinum var. azureocoloratum, commonly known as the Azure Blue Pea, is a variant of the common chickpea characterized by its striking blue or purple flower color.

This plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. The flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive azure hue that sets it apart from standard chickpeas. It is primarily cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color, though it can also be used in minor culinary applications.

The plant exhibits a bushy growth habit and thrives in well-drained soil with full sunlight.
