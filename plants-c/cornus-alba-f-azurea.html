Cornus alba f. azurea

Cornus alba f. azurea, commonly known as the Azure Cornel.

It is a compact, deciduous shrub that features striking blue-gray foliage, which becomes more pronounced in the fall. The plant produces small, white flowers in early spring, followed by red berries that attract wildlife. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and adaptability to a variety of garden settings.
