Cerastium madrense

Cerastium madrense, commonly known as the Madrean sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features include small, lobed leaves and a compact growth habit, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.
