Clusia inesiana

Clusia inesiana, commonly known as the evergreen shrub or false arum, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color. Clusia inesiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
