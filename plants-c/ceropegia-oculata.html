Ceropegia oculata

Ceropegia oculata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
