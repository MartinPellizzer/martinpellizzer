Calceolaria lanceolata f. polyrrhiza

Calceolaria lanceolata f. polyrrhiza, commonly known as the lanceleaf slipper orchid, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Calceolariaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant features lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
