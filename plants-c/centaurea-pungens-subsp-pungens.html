Centaurea pungens subsp. pungens

Centaurea pungens subsp.

pungens, commonly known as the spiked centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, bristly foliage.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in habitats such as gravelly slopes and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental addition to gardens and natural landscapes.
