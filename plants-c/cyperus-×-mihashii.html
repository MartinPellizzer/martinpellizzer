Cyperus × mihashii

Cyperus × mihashii, commonly known as the Japanese flatsedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flowering spikes. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soil conditions. It is native to parts of Asia, including Japan and Korea, and is often found in riparian zones.

Due to its adaptability, it can sometimes become invasive in non-native regions.
