Clutia mollis

Clutia mollis, commonly known as the silky clutia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape and are covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
