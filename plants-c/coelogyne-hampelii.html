Coelogyne hampelii

Coelogyne hampelii, commonly known as the golden spike orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne hampelii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
