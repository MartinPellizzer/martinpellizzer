Cryptanthus zonatus var. fuscus

Cryptanthus zonatus var. fuscus, commonly known as the dark zonatus carnation.

This plant is a compact, low-growing species native to South America, specifically Brazil. It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a dark purple or blackish hue. The foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep green.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
