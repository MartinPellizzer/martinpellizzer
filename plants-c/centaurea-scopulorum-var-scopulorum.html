Centaurea scopulorum var. scopulorum

Centaurea scopulorum var. scopulorum, commonly known as the rock centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, thriving in well-drained environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are erect and branching.

It is a perennial species that produces small, showy flowers throughout the summer months.
