Calamagrostis mannii

Calamagrostis mannii, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, silvery-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It produces panicle-like inflorescences that are often adorned with pale or silver-colored florets.

Calamagrostis mannii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
