Cynorkis imbellis

Cynorkis imbellis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, pale green or white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is a perennial herb that produces showy, fragrant blooms during the summer months.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
