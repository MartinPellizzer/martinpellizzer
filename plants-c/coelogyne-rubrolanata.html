Coelogyne rubrolanata

Coelogyne rubrolanata, commonly known as the red-lanated orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers with a distinctive red and white coloration, often with a yellow throat. It typically grows as an epiphyte on trees, thriving in humid, tropical environments.

Coelogyne rubrolanata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
