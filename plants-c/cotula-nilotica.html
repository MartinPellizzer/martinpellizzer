Cotula nilotica

Cotula nilotica, commonly known as African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Cotula nilotica has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small blooms throughout the growing season.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
