Capsella bursa-pastoris bottnica

Capsella bursa-pastoris bottnica, commonly known as common chickweed, is a low-growing annual herb belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It features small, white or pink flowers and hairy, alternate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in gardens, fields, and along pathways.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and widespread weed across temperate regions.
