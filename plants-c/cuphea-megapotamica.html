Cuphea megapotamica

Cuphea megapotamica, commonly known as the flamebeard plant, is a member of the Lythraceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking red, tubular flowers. Cuphea megapotamica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
