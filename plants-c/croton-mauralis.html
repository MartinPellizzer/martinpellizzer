Croton mauralis

Croton mauralis, commonly known as the Malabar croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Indian subcontinent.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that can range from yellow to red, depending on the variety. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules containing seeds.

Croton mauralis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant due to its striking foliage.
