Clematis henryi subsp. morii

Clematis henryi subsp.

morii, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China. It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

It is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its vigorous growth and showy blooms.
