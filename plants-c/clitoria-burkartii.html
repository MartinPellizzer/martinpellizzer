Clitoria burkartii

Clitoria burkartii, commonly known as the Burkart's winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It features delicate, butterfly-like blue or purple flowers that are attractive to pollinators. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its potential in sustainable agriculture due to its nitrogen-fixing properties.
