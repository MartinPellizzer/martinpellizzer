Chimarrhis goudotii

Chimarrhis goudotii, commonly known as the Goudot's flame tree, is a striking tropical shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Chimarrhis goudotii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
