Cirsium integriusculum

Cirsium integriusculum, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lance-shaped leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are usually covered with fine hairs, and the flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Cirsium integriusculum is often found in the western United States and parts of Canada.
