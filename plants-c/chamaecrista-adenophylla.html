Chamaecrista adenophylla

Chamaecrista adenophylla, commonly known as the silky crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
