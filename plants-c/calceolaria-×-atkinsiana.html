Calceolaria × atkinsiana

Calceolaria × atkinsiana, commonly known as the hybrid slipper orchid, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between species of the genus Calceolaria.

It is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that come in a variety of vibrant colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant is native to South America and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and showy blooms.

Calceolaria × atkinsiana prefers partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, easy-to-care-for plants.
