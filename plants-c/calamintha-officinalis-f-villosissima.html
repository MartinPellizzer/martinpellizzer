Calamintha officinalis f. villosissima

Calamintha officinalis f. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy calamintha, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, fragrant blue or purple flowers. Native to southern Europe, this plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and sunny locations. The species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and ornamental planting.
