Clematis × durandii

Clematis × durandii, commonly known as the durandii clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and striking, large, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × durandii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
