Carduus nelsonii

Carduus nelsonii, commonly known as Nelson's thistle, is a perennial plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions.

Nelson's thistle is often found in the western United States and parts of Canada.
