Chomelia neurocarpa

Chomelia neurocarpa, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with long, slender leaflets, and its bark is smooth and often reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and furniture.
