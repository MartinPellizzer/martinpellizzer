Clethra

It consists of about 15 species, most of which are shrubs native to eastern North America and parts of Asia. The plants are characterized by their showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer, often in clusters of white or pink. They are valued in gardens for their ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in moist, acidic soils.
