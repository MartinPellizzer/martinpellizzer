Caladenia macrostylis

Caladenia macrostylis, commonly known as the Large-styled Spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender petals and a distinctive, elongated column. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a prominent, showy labellum that attracts pollinators.

Caladenia macrostylis is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in specific, localized areas.
