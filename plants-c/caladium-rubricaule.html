Caladium rubricaule

Caladium rubricaule, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a creamy white background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium rubricaule thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant foliage.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its colorful leaves, which add visual interest to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
