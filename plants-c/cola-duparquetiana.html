Cola duparquetiana

Cola duparquetiana, commonly known as the African cola tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Sterculiaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central and West Africa. The plant is characterized by its large, leathery leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a hard, woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is notable for its role in traditional medicine and as a source of the stimulant caffeine.
