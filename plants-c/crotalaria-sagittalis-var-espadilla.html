Crotalaria sagittalis var. espadilla

Crotalaria sagittalis var. espadilla, commonly known as the arrowhead crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking floral display.
