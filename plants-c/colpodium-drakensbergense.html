Colpodium drakensbergense

Colpodium drakensbergense, commonly known as the Drakensberg colpodium, is a rare and endemic plant species found in the Drakensberg mountain range of South Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous plant with delicate, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, greenish flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically along stream banks and in rocky crevices. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it an important subject for botanical research and conservation efforts.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered vulnerable and requires protection to ensure its survival.
