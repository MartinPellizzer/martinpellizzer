Conyza humifusa

Conyza humifusa, commonly known as the prostrate conyza, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows close to the ground, forming a dense mat of foliage. Its small, white to yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Conyza humifusa is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
