Cerastium Brachypetalum Var. Strigosum

Cerastium brachypetalum var. strigosum is a lesser-known member of the Caryophyllaceae family. It is a low-growing perennial plant native to specific regions in Europe. The species is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in alpine environments. Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
