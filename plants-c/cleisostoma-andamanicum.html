Cleisostoma andamanicum

Cleisostoma andamanicum, commonly known as the Andaman star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Andaman Islands.

It features a slender, wiry stem and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
