Caladium smitzii

Caladium smitzii, commonly known as the Smitz's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored with shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium smitzii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its striking foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant displays.
