Cyrtochilum dodianum

Cyrtochilum dodianum, commonly known as the Dodian's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Cyrtochilum dodianum thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
