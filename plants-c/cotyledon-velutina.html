Cotyledon velutina

Cotyledon velutina, commonly known as velvet cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a soft, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The leaves are typically green or reddish in color and can change hue in response to light exposure. This plant is known for its ability to store water in its leaves, making it drought-tolerant.

It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding ornamental value to gardens.
