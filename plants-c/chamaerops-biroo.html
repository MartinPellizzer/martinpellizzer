Chamaerops biroo

Chamaerops biroo, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a species of palm native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, fan-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. Its leaves are often used in traditional crafts and as thatching material.

Chamaerops biroo is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is cultivated in gardens and parks.
