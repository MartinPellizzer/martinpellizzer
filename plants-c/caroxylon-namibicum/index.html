Caroxylon Namibicum

Caroxylon namibicum is a rare and fascinating plant native to the Namib Desert. It is known for its unique adaptations to survive in one of the harshest environments on Earth. This succulent has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water and nutrients. Its leaves are modified into spines to reduce water loss and deter herbivores. Despite its tough conditions, Caroxylon namibicum plays an important role in the desert ecosystem.
