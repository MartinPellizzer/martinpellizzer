Crotalaria pearsonii

Crotalaria pearsonii, commonly known as Pearson's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange flowers. The plant has palmate leaves with three to five leaflets, and its seed pods are typically cylindrical and dehiscent.
