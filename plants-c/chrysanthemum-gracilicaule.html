Chrysanthemum gracilicaule

Chrysanthemum gracilicaule, commonly known as slender chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, white, and orange, often blooming in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and medicinal horticulture.
