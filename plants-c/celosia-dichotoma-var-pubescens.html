Celosia dichotoma var. pubescens

Celosia dichotoma var. pubescens, commonly known as the flame flower or croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, fuzzy hairs, giving it a textured appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
