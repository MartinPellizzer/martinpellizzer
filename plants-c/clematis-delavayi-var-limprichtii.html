Clematis delavayi var. limprichtii

Clematis delavayi var. limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, often with a serrated margin. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically appearing in late spring. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
