Chenopodium standleyanum

Chenopodium standleyanum, commonly known as the slender goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

Chenopodium standleyanum is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in warm, humid conditions.
