Chondroscaphe dabeibaensis

Chondroscaphe dabeibaensis, commonly known as the Dabeiba rockcress, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically narrow and hairy, adapted to thrive in rocky and arid environments.
