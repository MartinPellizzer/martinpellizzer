Camellia semiserrata var. magnocarpa

Camellia semiserrata var. magnocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is notable for its large, fleshy red fruits that contain seeds surrounded by a fleshy, orange aril. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This species is also valued for its historical significance in traditional Chinese culture.
