Cephalaria qeydarensis

Cephalaria qeydarensis, commonly known as the Qeydarensis cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and feathery leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky and limestone soils, thriving in temperate and alpine environments. Its flowers bloom in the summer months, producing yellow to orange composite inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
