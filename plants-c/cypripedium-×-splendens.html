Cypripedium × splendens

Cypripedium × splendens, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper, which is a key feature in its pollination strategy. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments and thrives in cool, humid climates. Its flowers are usually pink or white with a deep red or orange base, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Cypripedium × splendens is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
