Cynoglossum officinale subsp. rotatum

Cynoglossum officinale subsp.

rotatum, commonly known as narrow-leaved forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This subspecies is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and for its historical use in traditional medicine.
