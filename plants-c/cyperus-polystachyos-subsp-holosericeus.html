Cyperus polystachyos subsp. holosericeus

Cyperus polystachyos subsp.

holosericeus, commonly known as the silky flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants.

The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clustered, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are typically small and inconspicuous, with a three-lobed perianth.

This subspecies is often found in wetland habitats, including marshes and along the edges of water bodies.
