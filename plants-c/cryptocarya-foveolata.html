Cryptocarya foveolata

Cryptocarya foveolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved wax tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with a waxy coating. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by spherical, dark blue to black berries.
