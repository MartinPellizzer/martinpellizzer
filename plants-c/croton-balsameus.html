Croton balsameus

Croton balsameus, commonly known as the balsam croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that can range from green to red and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits.

Croton balsameus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf coloration.
