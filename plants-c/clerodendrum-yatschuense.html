Clerodendrum yatschuense

Clerodendrum yatschuense, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
