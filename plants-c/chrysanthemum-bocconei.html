Chrysanthemum bocconei

Chrysanthemum bocconei, commonly known as the Boccone's chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its dense, bushy growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in the autumn, producing a variety of flower colors including yellow, white, and red. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
