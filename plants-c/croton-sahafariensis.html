Croton sahafariensis

Croton sahafariensis, commonly known as the Saharan croton, is a shrub native to arid regions of Africa.

It features thick, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain various bioactive compounds, making it of interest in traditional medicine.

Croton sahafariensis is adapted to survive in extreme conditions with minimal water availability.
