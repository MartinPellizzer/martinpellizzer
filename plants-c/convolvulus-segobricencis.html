Convolvulus segobricencis

Convolvulus segobricencis, commonly known as the Segobricencis morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other climbing and trailing plants.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
