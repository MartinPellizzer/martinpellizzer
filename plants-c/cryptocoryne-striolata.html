Cryptocoryne striolata

Cryptocoryne striolata, commonly known as the zigzag cryptocoryne, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, ribbon-like leaves that have a distinctive zigzag pattern along their midrib. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often used in freshwater aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care. Its rhizome system allows it to spread slowly across the substrate, making it suitable for background planting.

Cryptocoryne striolata is also valued for its ability to help maintain water quality by absorbing nitrates and other nutrients.
