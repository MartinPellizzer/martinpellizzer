Clerodendrum porphyrocalyx var. reflexum

Clerodendrum porphyrocalyx var. reflexum, commonly known as the purple flame clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking purple and red inflorescences, which are clustered in panicles and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture with a deep green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into woody capsules containing numerous seeds.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
