Croton hemiargyreus

Croton hemiargyreus, commonly known as the silverleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features a mix of green, white, and silver hues. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or orange in color.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
