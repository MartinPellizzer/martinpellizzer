Callerya dielsiana var. heterocarpa

Callerya dielsiana var. heterocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in dense clusters during the spring. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. Its fruit is a distinctive, elongated pod that splits open to release seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
