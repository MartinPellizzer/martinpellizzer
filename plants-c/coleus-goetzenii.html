Coleus goetzenii

Coleus goetzenii, commonly known as the blue coleus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its striking blue or purple foliage, which is often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in spikes at the ends of the stems.

Coleus goetzenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
