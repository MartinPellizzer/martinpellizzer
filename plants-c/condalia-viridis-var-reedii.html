Condalia viridis var. reedii

Condalia viridis var. reedii, commonly known as the green condalia or Reed's condalia, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green, with a waxy surface to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
