Crepis tuberculata

Crepis tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
