Caladenia fragrantissima

Caladenia fragrantissima, commonly known as the fragrant spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to south-western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with a single, elongated flower that has a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to cream in color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Caladenia fragrantissima is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in conservation reserves.
