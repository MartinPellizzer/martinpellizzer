Commelina modesta

Commelina modesta, commonly known as the modest spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Commelina genus within the family Commelinaceae. The plant features slender, erect stems with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, with three blue petals and three white sepals, and are typically arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Commelina modesta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
