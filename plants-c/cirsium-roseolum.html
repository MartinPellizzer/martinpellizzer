Cirsium roseolum

Cirsium roseolum, commonly known as the pink thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Cirsium roseolum typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a notable species in its native habitat.
