Camellia chekiangoleosa f. tanglii

Camellia chekiangoleosa f. tanglii, commonly known as the Tanglii camellia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It is a cultivar of the camellia species, characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
