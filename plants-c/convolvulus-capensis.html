Convolvulus capensis

Convolvulus capensis, commonly known as the Cape convolvulus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red throat. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and rocky habitats.
