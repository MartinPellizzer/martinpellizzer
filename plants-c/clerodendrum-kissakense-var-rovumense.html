Clerodendrum kissakense var. rovumense

Clerodendrum kissakense var. rovumense, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange bracts that resemble flames, which are actually modified leaves rather than flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and vibrant foliage make it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
