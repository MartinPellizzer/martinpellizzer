Canna bangii

Canna bangii, commonly known as the red canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, often with a reddish or purple tinge. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers with three narrow petals and three broad, leaf-like bracts. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Canna bangii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
