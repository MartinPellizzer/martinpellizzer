Cymbidium wadae

Cymbidium wadae, commonly known as the Wadae orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescences. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium wadae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
