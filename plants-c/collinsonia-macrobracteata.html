Collinsonia macrobracteata

Collinsonia macrobracteata, commonly known as the showy collinsonia, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of numerous tubular flowers clustered in a dense, spire-like arrangement. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is a sought-after plant in native plant gardens.
