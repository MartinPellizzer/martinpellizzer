Curculigo pilosa subsp. pilosa

Curculigo pilosa subsp.

pilosa, commonly known as the hairy arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the family Piperaceae and is characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers.
