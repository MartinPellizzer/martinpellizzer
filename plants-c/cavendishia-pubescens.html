Cavendishia pubescens

Cavendishia pubescens, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a striking plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often blooming during the cooler months. Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

However, it requires specific care, including high humidity and indirect light, to thrive.
