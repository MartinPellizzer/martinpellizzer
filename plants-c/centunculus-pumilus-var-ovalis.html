Centunculus pumilus var. ovalis

Centunculus pumilus var. ovalis, commonly known as the oval mouse-ear cress, is a small, herbaceous annual plant native to Europe.

It features tiny, oval-shaped leaves that are densely packed, giving it a compact appearance. The flowers are small, yellow, and borne in clusters, typically blooming in early spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and short life cycle, making it a common sight in disturbed or cultivated soils.
