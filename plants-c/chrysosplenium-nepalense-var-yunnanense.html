Chrysosplenium nepalense var. yunnanense

Chrysosplenium nepalense var. yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan chrysosplenium, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southwestern China.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces clusters of tiny, white or pale yellow flowers in spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturalists alike.
