Campanula linearis

Campanula linearis, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and linear, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
