Cotula pedunculata

Cotula pedunculata, commonly known as the common groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant features small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Cotula pedunculata is often found in arid or semi-arid regions and is adapted to thrive in poor soils.
