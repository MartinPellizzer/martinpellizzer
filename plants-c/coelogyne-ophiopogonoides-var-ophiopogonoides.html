Coelogyne ophiopogonoides var. ophiopogonoides

Coelogyne ophiopogonoides var. ophiopogonoides, commonly known as the Ophiopogon-like orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and fibrous root system. The plant produces upright, lanceolate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It blooms with fragrant, yellowish-green to orange-red flowers arranged in a raceme.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded, moist environments.
