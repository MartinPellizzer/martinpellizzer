Croton torreyanus

Croton torreyanus, commonly known as Torrey's croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or yellow, depending on the season and environmental conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
