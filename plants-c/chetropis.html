Chetropis

Chetropis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Bignoniaceae and is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces woody capsules as fruit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
