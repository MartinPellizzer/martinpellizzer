Centaurea × mrazeci

Centaurea × mrazeci, commonly known as the Mrazec's centaury, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets arranged in a composite structure. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with upright, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to regions of Central and Eastern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and public landscapes.
