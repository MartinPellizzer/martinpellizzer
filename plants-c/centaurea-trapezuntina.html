Centaurea trapezuntina

Centaurea trapezuntina, commonly known as the Trapezuntian cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

Centaurea trapezuntina is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
