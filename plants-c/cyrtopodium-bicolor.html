Cyrtopodium bicolor

Cyrtopodium bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Cyrtopodium bicolor is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate for experienced orchid growers.
