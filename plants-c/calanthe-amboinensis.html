Calanthe amboinensis

Calanthe amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Calanthe amboinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
