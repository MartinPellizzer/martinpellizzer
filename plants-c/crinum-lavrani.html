Crinum lavrani

Crinum lavrani, commonly known as the Cape Town lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a distinctive yellow or orange spot at the base. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically evergreen, and it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Crinum lavrani is known for its strong fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
