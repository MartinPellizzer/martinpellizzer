Crassula ausensis subsp. ausensis

Crassula ausensis subsp.

ausensis, commonly known as the Cape Town native or simply Crassula ausensis, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green to reddish in color, depending on light exposure.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in harsh, dry conditions.
