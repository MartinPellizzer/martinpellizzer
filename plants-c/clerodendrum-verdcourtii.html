Clerodendrum verdcourtii

Clerodendrum verdcourtii, commonly known as the African hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink, red, or white. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant is characterized by its branched inflorescences and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
