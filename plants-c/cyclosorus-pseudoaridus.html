It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and is typically found in arid or semi-arid environments. It thrives in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought.

Cyclosorus pseudoaridus plays a minor but ecologically significant role in stabilizing soil in its harsh habitat.
