Clerodendrum lobbii

Clerodendrum lobbii, commonly known as the African balsam, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that resemble petals, which are often used to attract pollinators. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Clerodendrum lobbii is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
