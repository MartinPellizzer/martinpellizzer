Cotula montana

Cotula montana, commonly known as mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in spring and early summer. Cotula montana thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
