Cytisogenista

Cytisogenista, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its feathery, compound leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its seeds are encased in pods that split open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for ecological restoration projects.
