Cyclopia glabra

Cyclopia glabra, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy leaves that are often glandular. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Cyclopia glabra is also noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
