Croton hendersonii

Croton hendersonii, commonly known as Henderson's croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which are followed by capsule fruits.

Croton hendersonii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
