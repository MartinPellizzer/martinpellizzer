Calathiana fenetii

Calathiana fenetii, commonly known as the Fenet's calathia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
