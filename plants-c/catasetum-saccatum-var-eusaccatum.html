Catasetum saccatum var. eusaccatum

Catasetum saccatum var. eusaccatum, commonly known as the Elegant Catasetum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers that display a range of colors, including yellow, orange, and red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of showy, spurred flowers, which are unique to the Catasetum genus.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively straightforward care requirements.
