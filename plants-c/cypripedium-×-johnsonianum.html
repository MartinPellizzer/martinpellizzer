Cypripedium × johnsonianum

Cypripedium × johnsonianum, commonly known as the Johnstone's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces a single, elongated leaf that is often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is typically found in shaded forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
