Clematis subtriternata var. tenuifolia

Clematis subtriternata var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved clematis, is a climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features delicate, trifoliate leaves that are narrow and finely textured, contributing to its ornamental appeal. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its graceful habit and is often used in garden settings for its visual interest and ability to climb supports.
