Caladenia fitzgeraldii

Caladenia fitzgeraldii, commonly known as Fitzgerald's spider orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, intricately patterned orchid flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its blossoms are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a prominent, hooded labellum.

Caladenia fitzgeraldii is a rare and protected species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
