Calamagrostis eminens var. discreta

Calamagrostis eminens var. discreta, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-green plumes that add ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive silvery-blue hue that enhances its visual appeal.

It is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
