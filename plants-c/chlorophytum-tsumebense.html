Chlorophytum tsumebense

Chlorophytum tsumebense, commonly known as the Tsumeb snake plant, is a succulent plant native to Namibia.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with a yellowish or white midrib. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks during the growing season. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-drained soil, making it a low-maintenance houseplant.

Its ability to purify indoor air makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
