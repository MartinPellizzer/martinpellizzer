Cynanchum dorrii

Cynanchum dorrii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or the yellow milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its latex is milky and contains toxic compounds, which serve as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

Cynanchum dorrii is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
