Citrosma estrellensis

Citrosma estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellan citrus tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes other citrus and aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit, though not widely cultivated, has a unique aroma and is used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Citrosma estrellensis make it an interesting subject for ecological and phytochemical studies.
