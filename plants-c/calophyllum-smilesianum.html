Calophyllum smilesianum

Calophyllum smilesianum, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its fruit is a round, green to yellowish drupe, containing a single seed.
