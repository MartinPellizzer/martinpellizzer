Clerodendrum myricoides subsp. austromonticola

Clerodendrum myricoides subsp.

austromonticola, commonly known as the Australian mountain clerodendrum, is a shrub native to Australia. It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often gland-dotted and have a rough texture.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

This species is adapted to grow in moist, well-drained soils in temperate to subtropical regions.
