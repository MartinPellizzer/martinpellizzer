Cymbidium ensifolium var. susin

Cymbidium ensifolium var. susin, commonly known as the Chinese orchid or the sword-leafed orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
