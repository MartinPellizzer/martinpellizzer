Clinopodium megalanthum var. speciosum

Clinopodium megalanthum var. speciosum, commonly known as the showy mountain savory, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, tubular flowers in shades of purple or blue. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or alpine gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and clusters of colorful blooms.
