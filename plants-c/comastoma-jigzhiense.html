Comastoma jigzhiense

Comastoma jigzhiense, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is native to regions of China. It features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly found in its natural habitat.
