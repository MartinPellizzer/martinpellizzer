Cirsium venustum

Cirsium venustum, commonly known as the purple thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and showy purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. Its flowers are visited by a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Cirsium venustum is known for its robust structure and ability to thrive in challenging environments.
