Croton schimperianus var. acutissimus

Croton schimperianus var. acutissimus, commonly known as the sharp-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that display a variety of shades, including green, red, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped with a pointed tip, contributing to its common name.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
