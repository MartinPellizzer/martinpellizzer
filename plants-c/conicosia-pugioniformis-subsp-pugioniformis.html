Conicosia pugioniformis subsp. pugioniformis

Conicosia pugioniformis subsp.

pugioniformis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf conicosia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a perennial shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, tubular flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, and it produces small, dark blue berries.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
