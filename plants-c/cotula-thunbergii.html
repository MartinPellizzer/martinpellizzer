Cotula thunbergii

Cotula thunbergii, commonly known as silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing herb with succulent, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Cotula thunbergii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
