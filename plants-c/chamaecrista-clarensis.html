Chamaecrista clarensis

Chamaecrista clarensis, commonly known as the slender ticktree, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces legume pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or disturbed areas.

Chamaecrista clarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
