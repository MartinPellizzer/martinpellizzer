Ceropegia guttata subsp. reticulata

Ceropegia guttata subsp.

reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined arrowroot, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves with a distinctive net-like venation pattern.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often clustered in inflorescences. It is adapted to arid environments and can store water in its stems and leaves.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
