Carex Brachystachys Var. Refracta

Carex brachystachys var. refracta is a unique variant of the sedge plant known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This plant is often found in wetland environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its narrow, grass-like leaves and compact growth habit make it easily recognizable among other sedges. The variety refracta stands out due to its unique leaf structure and subtle color variations. It plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining wetland ecosystems.
