Coelogyne gravenhorstii

Coelogyne gravenhorstii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous fragrant, golden-yellow flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It is prized for its showy blooms and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Coelogyne gravenhorstii is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
