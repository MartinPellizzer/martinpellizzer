Cryptocarya alleniana

Cryptocarya alleniana, commonly known as the Allen's cryptocarya, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the wet tropical forests of Queensland, Australia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy berries that are an important food source for native wildlife.

The tree is often found in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
