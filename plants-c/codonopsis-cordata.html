Codonopsis cordata

Codonopsis cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments in mountainous regions. Its flowering period occurs in the spring and early summer, and it is often found in forest understories.

Codonopsis cordata is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
