Cypripedium × sanderianum

Cypripedium × sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive pouch-like lip, which is a key feature of the lady's slipper orchid genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments and has lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white with a deep red or orange pouch.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and conservation programs.
