Chytroma marawynensis

Chytroma marawynensis, commonly known as the Moonlight Orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, slender stems and clusters of small, pale lavender flowers that emit a faint, sweet fragrance at night. The plant's leaves are broad and glossy, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture in its humid environment. It thrives in shaded, well-drained soil and requires consistent humidity and indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and limited habitat, Chytroma marawynensis is considered a valuable species in botanical research and conservation efforts.
