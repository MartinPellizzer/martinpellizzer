Cnicus foliosus

Cnicus foliosus, commonly known as the leafy cinquefoil, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, typically found in meadows, grasslands, and rocky areas. The plant features basal leaves that are palmately lobed, with a hairy surface, and produces yellow flowers in early summer. Its root system is fibrous, and it is often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Cnicus foliosus is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in various soil conditions.
