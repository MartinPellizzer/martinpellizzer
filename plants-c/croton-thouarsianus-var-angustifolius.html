Croton thouarsianus var. angustifolius

Croton thouarsianus var. angustifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved croton, is a shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, spiky, orange-red fruits. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its unique foliage and colorful fruits make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
