Cephalostigma fockeanum

Cephalostigma fockeanum, commonly known as the Fockean rock orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with erect, linear leaves. Its flowers are pale yellow to greenish-yellow, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils.
