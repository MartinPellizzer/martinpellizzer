Conyza vagans

Conyza vagans, commonly known as the common ironweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Conyza vagans is native to North America and is often found in fields, roadsides, and other sunny areas.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
