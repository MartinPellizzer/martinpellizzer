Convolvulus althaeoides var. argyraeus

Convolvulus althaeoides var. argyraeus, commonly known as silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
