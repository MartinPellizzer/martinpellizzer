Centaurea brevifimbriata

Centaurea brevifimbriata, commonly known as the narrow-fringed centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial with upright stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers with distinctive, narrow fringed petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
