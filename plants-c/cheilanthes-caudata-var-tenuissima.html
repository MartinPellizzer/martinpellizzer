Cheilanthes caudata var. tenuissima

Cheilanthes caudata var. tenuissima, commonly known as the slender tailed rock fern, is a rare and delicate fern species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are adapted for wind pollination, and it has a slow growth rate, making it a challenging species to cultivate.
