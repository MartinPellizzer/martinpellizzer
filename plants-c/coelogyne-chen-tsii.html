Coelogyne chen-tsii

Coelogyne chen-tsii, commonly known as the Chen Tsii orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red and green markings. The plant features a short, rhizomatous stem and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-like, dark green, and often have a waxy texture.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
