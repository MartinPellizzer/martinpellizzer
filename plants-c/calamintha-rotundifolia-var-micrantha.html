Calamintha rotundifolia var. micrantha

Calamintha rotundifolia var. micrantha, commonly known as round-leaved germander, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, rounded leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valued plant in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
