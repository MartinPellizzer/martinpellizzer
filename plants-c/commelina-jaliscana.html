Commelina jaliscana

Commelina jaliscana, commonly known as the Jalisco spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Commelina jaliscana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
