Cnestis iomalla

Cnestis iomalla, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Cnestis iomalla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
