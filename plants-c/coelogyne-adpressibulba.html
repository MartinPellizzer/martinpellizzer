Coelogyne adpressibulba

Coelogyne adpressibulba, commonly known as the flat-lipped coelogyne, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a unique flat lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of yellow and orange, are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Coelogyne adpressibulba requires specific care, including bright indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
