Cotoneaster rugosus var. pritzellii

Cotoneaster rugosus var. pritzellii, commonly known as the Pritzellii cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically red-tinged when young, turning dark green as they mature. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a popular choice for landscaping.
