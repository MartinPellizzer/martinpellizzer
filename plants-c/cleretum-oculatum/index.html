Cleretum Oculatum

Cleretum oculatum is a rare and striking plant known for its unique, eye-like markings. It thrives in specific, often humid environments, making it a sought-after species among botanists and collectors. The plant's distinctive appearance has led to its common name, which reflects its visually captivating foliage. Despite its beauty, Cleretum oculatum is challenging to cultivate due to its particular care requirements. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and conservation status of this fascinating plant.
