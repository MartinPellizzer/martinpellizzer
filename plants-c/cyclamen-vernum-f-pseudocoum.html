Cyclamen vernum f. pseudocoum

Cyclamen vernum f. pseudocoum, commonly known as the spring cyclamen, is a hybrid form of the European cyclamen.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a natural hybrid and its contribution to the diversity of the Cyclamen genus.
