Cornutia latifolia

Cornutia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cornutia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salt spray and coastal winds.

Cornutia latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
