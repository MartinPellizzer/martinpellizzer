Caladium bicolor var. bohemicum

Caladium bicolor var. bohemicum, commonly known as the Bohemian caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including shades of white, pink, and red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-draining soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.

Its foliage is typically used in landscape design to add visual interest to gardens and containers.
