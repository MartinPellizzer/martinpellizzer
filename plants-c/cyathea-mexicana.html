Cyathea mexicana

Cyathea mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea mexicana thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its spores are produced on the undersides of its fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
