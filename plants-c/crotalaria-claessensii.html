Crotalaria claessensii

Crotalaria claessensii, commonly known as the African spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and vibrant yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats. It has a bushy growth habit with compound leaves and produces seed pods that burst open to disperse seeds.

Crotalaria claessensii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking blooms.
