Cleisostoma orbiculare

Cleisostoma orbiculare, commonly known as the orbiculate cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with intricate markings. The plant has a slender, erect stem that bears several clustered blooms. Its leaves are ovate and slightly fleshy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Cleisostoma orbiculare thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
