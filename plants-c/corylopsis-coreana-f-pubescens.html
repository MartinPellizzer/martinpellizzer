Corylopsis coreana f. pubescens

Corylopsis coreana f. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy coreopsis, is a deciduous shrub native to Korea.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from February to March. The plant has slender, arching branches covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
