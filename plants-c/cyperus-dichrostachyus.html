Cyperus dichrostachyus

Cyperus dichrostachyus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are typically green or purplish in color, and it often grows in wet or moist environments.

Cyperus dichrostachyus is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
