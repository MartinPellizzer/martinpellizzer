Cattleya × elegans var. warneri

Cattleya × elegans var. warneri, commonly known as the Warner's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with a rich mix of colors, often including shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. It has lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety to the touch.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
