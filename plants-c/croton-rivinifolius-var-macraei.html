Croton rivinifolius var. macraei

Croton rivinifolius var. macraei, commonly known as the Macrae's croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful foliage, which can range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
