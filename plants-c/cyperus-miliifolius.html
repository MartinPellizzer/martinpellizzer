Cyperus miliifolius

Cyperus miliifolius, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically pale green to brown in color. It thrives in wetland environments, including marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus miliifolius is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
