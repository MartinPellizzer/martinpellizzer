Chrysopogon Nutans Var. Linnaeanus

Chrysopogon nutans var. linnaeanus is a lesser-known grass species native to parts of Asia and Africa. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in grasslands and open woodlands. This plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. While not widely cultivated, it plays a role in local ecosystems and traditional practices. Its study offers insights into grassland biodiversity and ecological adaptation.
