Cardopatium billardierei

Cardopatium billardierei, commonly known as the golden wattle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which are clustered in spherical inflorescences. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in coastal and inland regions.

Cardopatium billardierei is a hardy species that is popular in gardens for its ornamental value.
