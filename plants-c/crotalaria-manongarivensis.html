Crotalaria manongarivensis

Crotalaria manongarivensis, commonly known as the Manongarivensis Crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
