Caesalpinia berteroi

Caesalpinia berteroi, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Caesalpinia berteroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
