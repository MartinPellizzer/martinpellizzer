Croton ovalifolius var. genuinus

Croton ovalifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It features elliptical, waxy leaves that are often dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange or red fruits containing three seeds. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
