Colletia hystrix

Colletia hystrix, commonly known as the spiny candelabra cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its branched, spiny stems that form a shrub-like structure. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a notable species in its native habitat.
