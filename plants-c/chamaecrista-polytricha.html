Chamaecrista polytricha

Chamaecrista polytricha, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a perennial legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
