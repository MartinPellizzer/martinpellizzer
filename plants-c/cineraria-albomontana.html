Cineraria albomontana

Cineraria albomontana, commonly known as the white mountain cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking white, woolly leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners in mild climates.
