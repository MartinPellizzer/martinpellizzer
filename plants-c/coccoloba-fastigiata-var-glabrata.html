Coccoloba fastigiata var. glabrata

Coccoloba fastigiata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth coccoloba, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its palmately lobed leaves, which are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature, attracting various bird species.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along stream banks.
