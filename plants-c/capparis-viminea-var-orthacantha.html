Capparis viminea var. orthacantha

Capparis viminea var. orthacantha, commonly known as the slender coral tree, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when mature.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
