Carex trifida var. franchetii

Carex trifida var. franchetii, commonly known as the Franchet's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
