Centaurea oscensis

Centaurea oscensis, commonly known as the Alpina Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its bright yellow flowers, surrounded by blue or purple bracts, bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Centaurea oscensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
