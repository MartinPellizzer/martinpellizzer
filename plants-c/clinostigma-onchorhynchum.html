Clinostigma onchorhynchum

Clinostigma onchorhynchum, commonly known as the bluebell lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette at the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking floral display and fragrant blooms.
