Cactus setispinus

Cactus setispinus, commonly known as the spiny cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its rugged appearance. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.

Its structure helps reduce water loss and provides protection against herbivores.
