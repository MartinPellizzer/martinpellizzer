Columnea dichroa

Columnea dichroa, commonly known as the two-colored columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange with a contrasting throat. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.
