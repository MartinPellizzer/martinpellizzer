Campanula Lusitanica F. Bracteosa

Campanula lusitanica f. bracteosa is a rare and striking plant native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, known for its bell-shaped flowers. This particular cultivar is distinguished by its unique bracteate leaves and vibrant blue-violet blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to gardens and botanical collections.
