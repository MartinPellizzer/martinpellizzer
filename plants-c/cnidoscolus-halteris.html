Cnidoscolus halteris

Cnidoscolus halteris, commonly known as the arrow leaf or arrowhead plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically large and have a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that are followed by edible, spiny fruits containing seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid tropical environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
