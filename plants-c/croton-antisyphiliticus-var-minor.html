Croton antisyphiliticus var. minor

Croton antisyphiliticus var. minor, commonly known as the lesser croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that display a variety of hues, including red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with caution.
