Cyrtanthus elatus

Cyrtanthus elatus, commonly known as the star lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces a tall, slender stem with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Cyrtanthus elatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
