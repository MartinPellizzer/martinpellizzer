Coelogyne venusta

Coelogyne venusta, commonly known as the orchid with the golden beard, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow with red or orange markings. The plant produces a pseudobulb and has long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, hairy coating. Coelogyne venusta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
