Chamaedorea conocarpa

Chamaedorea conocarpa, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen palm, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stem and pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its compact size and elegant appearance.

Chamaedorea conocarpa is also valued for its ability to purify air and enhance indoor environments.
