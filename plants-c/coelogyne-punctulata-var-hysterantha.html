Coelogyne punctulata var. hysterantha

Coelogyne punctulata var. hysterantha, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white with yellow or green markings. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
