Corryocactus tarijensis

Corryocactus tarijensis, commonly known as the Tarijensis coryocactus, is a flowering plant native to Bolivia.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring and summer. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by cactus enthusiasts.
