Cheirostylis liukiuensis var. nantouensis

Cheirostylis liukiuensis var. nantouensis, commonly known as the Nantou orchid, is a rare orchid species native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountainous forests. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette, while its inflorescence bears several small, showy blooms.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
