Canarium sikkimense

Canarium sikkimense, commonly known as the Sikkim canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, fleshy berries that contain a single seed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mixed forests at elevations between 1,000 and 2,500 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
