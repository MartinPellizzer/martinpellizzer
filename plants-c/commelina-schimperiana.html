Commelina schimperiana

Commelina schimperiana, commonly known as the Schimper's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinales order and the Commelinaceae family, which includes many species with strikingly colored flowers. Commelina schimperiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological roles in certain habitats.
