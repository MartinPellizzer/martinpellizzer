Cirsium subinerme

Cirsium subinerme, commonly known as the slender thistle, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in dry, rocky soils and open habitats. The plant's structure includes a central stem with alternate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
