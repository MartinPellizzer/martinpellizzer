Christopteris sagitta

Christopteris sagitta, commonly known as the arrowhead fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped fronds that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining, moist soil. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by spore-bearing capsules.

Christopteris sagitta is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
