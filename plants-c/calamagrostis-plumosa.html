Calamagrostis plumosa

Calamagrostis plumosa, commonly known as the silky grass or fountain grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It features slender, upright stems that produce feathery, silver-green plumes during the summer months. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Calamagrostis plumosa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract birds and butterflies.
