Collomia Larsenii

Larsen's collomia, scientifically known as Collomia larsenii, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Great Basin in North America. This species is distinguished by its striking pink or purple flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, arid environments. Found primarily in rocky soils and open slopes, it is a symbol of resilience in extreme conditions. Its limited distribution and fragile habitat make it a priority for conservation efforts. As a unique member of the pink mallow family, it offers valuable insights into ecological adaptation and biodiversity.
