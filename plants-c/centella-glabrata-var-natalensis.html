Centella glabrata var. natalensis

Centella glabrata var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal centella, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky outcrops. The plant features a rosette of smooth, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in umbels.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
