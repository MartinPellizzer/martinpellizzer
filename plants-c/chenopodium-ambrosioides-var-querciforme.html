Chenopodium ambrosioides var. querciforme

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. querciforme, commonly known as broadleaf goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual with alternate, broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are enclosed in small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

This species is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
