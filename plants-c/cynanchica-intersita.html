Cynanchica intersita

Cynanchica intersita, commonly known as the Chinese devil's backbone, is a climbing plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers with a unique corona structure. It is often found in rocky or forested areas and is known for its medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
