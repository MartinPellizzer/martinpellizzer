Cyclamen hederifolium subsp. romanum

Cyclamen hederifolium subsp.

romanum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive mottled pattern and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers in early spring.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its foliage remains evergreen throughout the growing season, adding visual interest even when not in bloom.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
