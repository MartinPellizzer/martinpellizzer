Coalisina polyanthera

Coalisina polyanthera, commonly known as the clustered starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white and yellow. The plant has thin, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
