Commicarpus chinensis subsp. natalensis

Commicarpus chinensis subsp.

natalensis, commonly known as Natal commicarpus, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in open woodland or rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
