Centaurea paniculata subsp. esterellensis

Centaurea paniculata subsp.

esterellensis, commonly known as the Esterellense knapweed, is a perennial herb native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces dense, branched inflorescences of pink to purple flowers.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native range.
