Carex caryophyllea f. monostachya

Carex caryophyllea f. monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sedges, is a variant of the Carex caryophyllea species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It typically grows in moist or wet environments, such as marshes or along stream banks.
