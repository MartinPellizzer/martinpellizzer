Citharexylum rosei var. pilosum

Citharexylum rosei var. pilosum, commonly known as the silky cithara, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
