Cosmos landii

Cosmos landii, commonly known as the Mexican glory of the woods, is a herbaceous perennial native to Mexico.

It features showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a prominent yellow center. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Cosmos landii is often grown for its attractive blooms and is relatively easy to care for in gardens.
