Cotyledon curviflora

Cotyledon curviflora, commonly known as the curling cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that often curl or twist, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
