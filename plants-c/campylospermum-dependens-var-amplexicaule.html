Campylospermum dependens var. amplexicaule

Campylospermum dependens var. amplexicaule, commonly known as the clinging campylospermum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that often wrap around the stems, giving it a unique, clustered appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
