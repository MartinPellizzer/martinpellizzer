Cleome rubiginosa

Cleome rubiginosa, commonly known as redstem cleome, is an annual flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Cleome rubiginosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
