Calycanthus nanus

Calycanthus nanus, commonly known as the dwarf sweetshrub, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features fragrant, showy flowers that are typically red or maroon in color, with a unique, spicy aroma. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial to full shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Calycanthus nanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
