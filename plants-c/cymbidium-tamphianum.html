Cymbidium tamphianum

Cymbidium tamphianum, commonly known as the Phuket orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter. The plant produces inflorescences with multiple flowers, each having a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium tamphianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
