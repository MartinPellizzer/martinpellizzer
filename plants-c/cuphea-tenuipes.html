Cuphea tenuipes

Cuphea tenuipes, commonly known as the slender cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, branching stems and vibrant red or orange tubular flowers. Cuphea tenuipes thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking flowers and compact growth habit.
