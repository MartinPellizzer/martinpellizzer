Cryptocarya perareolata

Cryptocarya perareolata, commonly known as the Perareolata CRYPTOCARYA, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
