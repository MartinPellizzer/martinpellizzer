Convolvulus unilateralis

Convolvulus unilateralis, commonly known as the one-sided morning glory, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, spiral-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its climbing habit is facilitated by its twining stems, which wrap around supports.

Botanically, it is noted for its unique leaf arrangement and rapid growth rate.
