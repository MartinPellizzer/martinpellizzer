Calanthe chlorantha

Calanthe chlorantha, commonly known as the green calanthe, is a species of orchid native to the forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its strikingly green, velvety leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, typically blooming in spring. Calanthe chlorantha prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests.

Its unique foliage and delicate flowers make it a popular choice for shade gardens and greenhouse cultivation.
