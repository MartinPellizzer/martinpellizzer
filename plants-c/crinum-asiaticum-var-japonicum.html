Crinum asiaticum var. japonicum

Crinum asiaticum var. japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese crinum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces striking, fragrant white or pink flowers with a strong scent. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in wetlands and along riverbanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
