Calochilus ammobius

Calochilus ammobius, commonly known as the sandstone orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, often in areas with low rainfall. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. Its flowers have a distinctive, spurred shape typical of the Orchidaceae family.

Calochilus ammobius is adapted to thrive in harsh, arid environments, making it a resilient and hardy species.
