Crassula gentianoides

Crassula gentianoides, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula gentianoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
