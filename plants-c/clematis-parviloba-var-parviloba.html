Clematis parviloba var. parviloba

Clematis parviloba var. parviloba, commonly known as the small-flowered clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the western United States.

It features delicate, small white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, wiry stems and trifoliate leaves with three heart-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden landscapes.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
