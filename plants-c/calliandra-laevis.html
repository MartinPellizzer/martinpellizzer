Calliandra laevis

Calliandra laevis, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardens and dry landscapes. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Calliandra laevis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, enhancing soil fertility.
