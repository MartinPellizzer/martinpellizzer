Celosia globosa var. porphyrostachya

Celosia globosa var. porphyrostachya, commonly known as the red flame celosia, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spiky inflorescences that range in color from deep red to orange and pink. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by colorful bracts, which are the primary showy feature. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant colors make it an excellent addition to floral arrangements and landscape designs.
