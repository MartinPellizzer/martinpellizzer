Calamagrostis Epigejos Var. Flavescens

Calamagrostis epigejos var. flavescens is a striking ornamental grass known for its vibrant yellow plumes that stand out in any landscape. Native to eastern North America, this plant thrives in moist to wet soils and full sun to partial shade. Its feathery appearance adds texture and movement to gardens and natural settings. It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements. This variety is particularly valued for its ability to attract wildlife and enhance biodiversity in ecological gardens.
