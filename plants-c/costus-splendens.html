Costus splendens

Costus splendens, commonly known as the splendour costus, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which also includes ginger and turmeric, and is characterized by its ornamental, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall spikes of vibrant orange flowers that bloom in summer, adding a dramatic visual element to gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Costus splendens is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and can be grown in containers or as a landscape plant in suitable climates.
