Calytrix Muricata

Calytrix muricata is a unique and striking plant native to Australia. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its vibrant red flowers. The plant features dense, leathery leaves that give it a rugged appearance. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils. Calytrix muricata is both ornamental and ecologically significant in its habitat.
