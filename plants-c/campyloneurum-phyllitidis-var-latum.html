Campyloneurum phyllitidis var. latum

Campyloneurum phyllitidis var. latum, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock fern, is a variant of the rock fern genus Campyloneurum.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are typically leathery and dark green. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found growing on rocky substrates or in shaded, moist environments. Its fronds are usually unifacial, with a distinct midrib and a dense arrangement of small, rounded sori on the underside.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in shaded garden settings.
