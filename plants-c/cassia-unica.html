Cassia unica

Cassia unica, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Cassia unica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
