Calopsis burchellii

Calopsis burchellii, commonly known as the yellow oxalis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Oxalidaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive patterns. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.
