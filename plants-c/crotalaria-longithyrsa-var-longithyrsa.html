Crotalaria longithyrsa var. longithyrsa

Crotalaria longithyrsa var. longithyrsa, commonly known as the long-thyrsed crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of yellow flowers with distinctive spurred petals. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its fruit is a flat, pod-like structure that splits open to release seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
