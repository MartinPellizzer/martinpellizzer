Cleistocactus vallegrandensis

Cleistocactus vallegrandensis, commonly known as the Vallegrandensis Cactus, is a species of cactus native to northern Chile.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and smooth, pale green to blue-gray skin. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom at night, a trait typical of many nocturnal flowering plants. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic conditions.

Its unique appearance and adaptations make it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
