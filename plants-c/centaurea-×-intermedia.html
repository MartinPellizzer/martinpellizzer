Centaurea × intermedia

Centaurea × intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate centaury, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It has lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, achene fruits.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
