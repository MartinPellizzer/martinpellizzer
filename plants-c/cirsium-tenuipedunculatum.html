Cirsium tenuipedunculatum

Cirsium tenuipedunculatum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a compact growth habit with a short stem and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, stiff hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
