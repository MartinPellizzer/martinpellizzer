Crotalaria divaricata

Crotalaria divaricata, commonly known as the variable crownflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant characterized by its erect or spreading growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds. Crotalaria divaricata is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It is also known for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
