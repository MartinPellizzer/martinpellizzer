Crotalaria lachnosema

Crotalaria lachnosema, commonly known as the woolly crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, yellow, spurred flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are pinnately compound, with oval to lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is considered an invasive species in some areas.
