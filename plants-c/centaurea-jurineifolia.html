Centaurea jurineifolia

Centaurea jurineifolia, commonly known as Jurine's centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, while the stems are often branching and upright.

Centaurea jurineifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
