Calophyllum streimannii

Calophyllum streimannii, commonly known as the Streimann's calophyllum, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Calophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces woody fruits that contain a single large seed, which is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance in its native habitat.
