Calathea lasiostachya var. minor

Calathea lasiostachya var. minor, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green veins and pale yellow or white margins. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

This variety is popular among indoor gardeners due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
