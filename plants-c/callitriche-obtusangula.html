Callitriche obtusangula

Callitriche obtusangula, commonly known as broad-leaved water starwort, is a small aquatic plant found in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It has slender, branching stems that often lie close to the water surface. The plant features rounded, broad leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. Its tiny, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the summer months.

Callitriche obtusangula plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
