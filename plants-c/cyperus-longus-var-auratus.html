Cyperus longus var. auratus

Cyperus longus var. auratus, commonly known as the golden flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright yellow to golden in color, giving it its striking appearance. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale to yellowish flowers, which are often hidden among the foliage. It thrives in wet or moist soil conditions and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wetland restoration projects.
