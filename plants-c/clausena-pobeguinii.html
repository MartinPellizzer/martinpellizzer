Clausena pobeguinii

Clausena pobeguinii, commonly known as the Malayan sour orange, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red fruits that are tart and acidic.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in tropical horticulture and traditional medicine.
