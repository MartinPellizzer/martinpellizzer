Clematis × symesiana

Clematis × symesiana, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Clematis × symesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
