Chrysosplenium kamtschaticum var. aomorense

Chrysosplenium kamtschaticum var. aomorense, commonly known as the Aomori chrysosplenium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bright green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters, and bloom in early summer.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors or along stream banks.
