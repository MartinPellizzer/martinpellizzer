Cryptocarya pullenii

Cryptocarya pullenii, commonly known as the New Zealand honeysuckle, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. It produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

Cryptocarya pullenii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrance.
