Cirsium formosanum

Cirsium formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan thistle, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a rosette of basal leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and its flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Cirsium formosanum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
