Cymbidium eburneum var. dayanum

Cymbidium eburneum var. dayanum, commonly known as the Dayanum orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with erect, often branching stems that bear numerous leaves. The plant produces inflorescences of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its elegant, long-lasting blooms.
