Croton hauthalii

Croton hauthalii, commonly known as the Hauthal's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are often lobed and have a rough texture, making it a distinctive species within the Croton genus.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
