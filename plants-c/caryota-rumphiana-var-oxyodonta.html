Caryota rumphiana var. oxyodonta

Caryota rumphiana var. oxyodonta, commonly known as the slender palm or dwarf fishtail palm, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, feather-like leaves that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a smooth, waxy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens.
