Croton volutus

Croton volutus, commonly known as the spiral croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spirally arranged leaves that give it a unique and ornamental appearance. The leaves are typically bright green with a waxy texture and often have a slightly serrated edge. It produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant.
