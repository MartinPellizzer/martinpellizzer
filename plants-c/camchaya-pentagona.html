Camchaya pentagona

Camchaya pentagona, commonly known as the pentagon leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its distinctive pentagonal-shaped leaves.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm and humid climates with well-draining soil. The plant's foliage is often a deep green, and it may exhibit variegation in certain cultivars. It is typically evergreen, showcasing a compact growth habit that makes it suitable for indoor cultivation.

While not widely studied, its ornamental value has led to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
