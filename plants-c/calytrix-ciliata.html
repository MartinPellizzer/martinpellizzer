Calytrix ciliata

Calytrix ciliata, commonly known as the silky calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves with a hairy texture, and it typically grows in coastal areas or sandy soils.
