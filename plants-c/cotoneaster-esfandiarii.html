Cotoneaster esfandiarii

Cotoneaster esfandiarii, commonly known as the Persian cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Iran.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries in the fall, which are an important food source for birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cotoneaster esfandiarii is relatively low-maintenance and suitable for rock gardens or as a hedge.
