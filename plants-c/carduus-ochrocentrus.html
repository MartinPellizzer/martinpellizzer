Carduus ochrocentrus

Carduus ochrocentrus, commonly known as the yellow centaury.

This plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. The leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are arranged in a composite head.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.
