Crotalaria stenothyrsa

Crotalaria stenothyrsa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces distinctive yellow flowers with prominent, spurred petals. Its seed pods are typically cylindrical and contain several elliptical seeds.

Crotalaria stenothyrsa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a forage crop.
