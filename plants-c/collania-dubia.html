Collania dubia

Collania dubia, commonly known as the rare desert orchid, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white, typically during the cooler months. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperature fluctuations.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Collania dubia is considered a vulnerable species.
