Calceolaria diversifolia

Calceolaria diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its unique, pouch-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and lobed, with a velvety texture.

Calceolaria diversifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, colorful blooms.
