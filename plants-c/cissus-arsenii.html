Cissus arsenii

Cissus arsenii, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Cissus arsenii is also valued for its ability to purify air and its ornamental appeal.
