Colocasia

Colocasia, commonly known as elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy surface.

Colocasia prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
