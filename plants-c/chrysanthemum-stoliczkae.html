Chrysanthemum stoliczkae

Chrysanthemum stoliczkae, commonly known as the Stoliczka's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and displays a variety of flower colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
