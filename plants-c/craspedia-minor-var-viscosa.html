Craspedia minor var. viscosa

Craspedia minor var. viscosa, commonly known as the sticky craspedia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with dense, mat-forming foliage. The leaves are typically covered in a fine, sticky resin, which gives the plant its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in spring.

This plant thrives in dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal and inland regions of New Zealand.
