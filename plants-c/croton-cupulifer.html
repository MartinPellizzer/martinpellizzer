Croton cupulifer

Croton cupulifer, commonly known as the cupulifer croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves and vibrant, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in its native habitats.
