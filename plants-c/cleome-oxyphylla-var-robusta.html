Cleome oxyphylla var. robusta

Cleome oxyphylla var. robusta, commonly known as the robust cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of showy, pink to red flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Cleome oxyphylla var. robusta is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
