Codiaeum variegatum var. cavernicola

Codiaeum variegatum var. cavernicola, commonly known as the Marble Queen Croton, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored foliage featuring shades of yellow, green, red, and orange in intricate patterns. The leaves are typically thick and leathery, with a glossy texture that enhances its visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ability to add bold color and texture to indoor spaces.
