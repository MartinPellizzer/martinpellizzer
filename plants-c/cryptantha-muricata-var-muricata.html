Cryptantha muricata var. muricata

Cryptantha muricata var. muricata, commonly known as the rough cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple.
