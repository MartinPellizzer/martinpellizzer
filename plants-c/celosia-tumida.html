Celosia tumida

Celosia tumida, commonly known as the flame flower or crested grass, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Celosia tumida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
