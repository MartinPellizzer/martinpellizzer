Chimonobambusa multigemmia

Chimonobambusa multigemmia, commonly known as the Chinese flowering bamboo, is a species of grass that belongs to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, clumping growth habit and its ability to produce small, clustered flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.

Chimonobambusa multigemmia is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
