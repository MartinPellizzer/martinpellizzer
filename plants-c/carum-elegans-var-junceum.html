Carum elegans var. junceum

Carum elegans var. junceum, commonly known as slender caraway, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often resembling those of a true grass. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers in clustered umbels, which bloom in early summer. Its seeds are aromatic and have been used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

Carum elegans var. junceum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable addition to rock gardens or dry landscapes.
