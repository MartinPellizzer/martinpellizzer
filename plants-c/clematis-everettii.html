Clematis everettii

Clematis everettii, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, sweet fragrance. The plant produces small, round berries that turn red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and support for its climbing habit.

Clematis everettii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability.
