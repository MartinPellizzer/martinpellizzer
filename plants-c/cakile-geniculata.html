Cakile geniculata

Cakile geniculata, commonly known as the bent-leaf sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its low-growing, sprawling habit and small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are typically lobed and often appear to be bent or twisted, giving it its common name.
