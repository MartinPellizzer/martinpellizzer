Citrus × aurantium subf. iyo

Citrus × aurantium subf.

iyo, commonly known as the yuzu, is a small evergreen shrub native to East Asia. It produces small, round, yellow fruits with a thin, aromatic peel.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its high vitamin C content and distinctive flavor.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
