Chamaecrista cytisoides var. cytisoides

Chamaecrista cytisoides var. cytisoides, commonly known as the silky senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is often found in open habitats such as fields and disturbed soils, where it thrives in full sun. Its small, pod-like fruits contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
