Calceolaria mexicana subsp. prostrata

Calceolaria mexicana subsp.

prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate Mexican slipper orchid, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Mexico. It features succulent, prostrate stems that hug the ground, often forming dense mats.

The plant produces showy, slipper-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom from spring to summer. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid habitats.
