Castalia Jamesoniana

Castalia jamesoniana is a rare and striking plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia. Known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers, it captivates botanists and nature lovers alike. This species belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical marvel.
