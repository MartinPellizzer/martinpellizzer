Crepidium balabacense

Crepidium balabacense, commonly known as the Balabac orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, intricate flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
