Crepidium latipetalum

Crepidium latipetalum, commonly known as the broad-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are showy, with elongated petals and a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
