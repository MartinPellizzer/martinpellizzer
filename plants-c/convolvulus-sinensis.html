Convolvulus sinensis

Convolvulus sinensis, commonly known as Chinese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trumpet-shaped, vibrant purple or pink flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces spiral-shaped seed pods that contain small, dark seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Convolvulus sinensis thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
