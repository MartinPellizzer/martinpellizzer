Coelogyne elegans

Coelogyne elegans, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its long, narrow pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with a green throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne elegans is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
