Canna fintelmannii

Canna fintelmannii, commonly known as the purple leaf canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It features large, broad leaves that are typically a deep green with a distinctive purple underside. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. Canna fintelmannii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be propagated through division or rhizomes.
