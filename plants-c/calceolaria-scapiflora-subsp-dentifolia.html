Calceolaria scapiflora subsp. dentifolia

Calceolaria scapiflora subsp.

dentifolia, commonly known as the toothed calceolaria, is a member of the Campanulaceae family. It is native to the Andes Mountains of South America, where it thrives in high-altitude environments.
