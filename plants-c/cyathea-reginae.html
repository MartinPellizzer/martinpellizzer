Cyathea reginae

Cyathea reginae, commonly known as the royal tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its fronds are typically a deep green, with a distinctive, glossy appearance.

Cyathea reginae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to moist, shaded environments.
