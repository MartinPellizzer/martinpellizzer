Cytisus Ratisbonensis Var. Glaber

Cytisus ratisbonensis var. glaber is a unique variant of the broom plant known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. Native to southern Europe, this shrub thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its slender stems and clusters of bright yellow flowers make it a striking addition to gardens and natural landscapes. While it shares similarities with other cytisus species, its smooth, glabrous leaves set it apart. This plant is not only aesthetically pleasing but also has historical and ecological significance in its native habitat.
