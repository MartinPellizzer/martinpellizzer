Convolvulus denudatus

Convolvulus denudatus, commonly known as the naked morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that produces small, pale yellow or white flowers. The plant has slender, winding stems that climb or trail along the ground. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The flowers bloom in the early morning and close by midday, giving it its common name.
