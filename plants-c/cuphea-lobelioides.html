Cuphea lobelioides

Cuphea lobelioides, commonly known as the red flame cuphea, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, tubular red flowers resembling flames. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cuphea lobelioides is known for its ability to attract hummingbirds and other pollinators with its nectar-rich blooms.
