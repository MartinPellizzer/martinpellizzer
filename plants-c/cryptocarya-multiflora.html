Cryptocarya multiflora

Cryptocarya multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered cryptocarya, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Cryptocarya multiflora is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
