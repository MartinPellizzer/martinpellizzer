Crataegus × rubrocarnea

Crataegus × rubrocarnea, commonly known as the red-flowered hawthorn, is a hybrid species within the genus Crataegus.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it produces small, red berries in the fall.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
