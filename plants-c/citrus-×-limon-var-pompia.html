Citrus × limon var. pompia

Citrus × limon var. pompia, commonly known as the Pompia lemon, is a citrus hybrid originating from Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, yellow peel and a high oil content. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are glossy and ovate, with a slightly serrated edge.

The fruit is prized for its aromatic zest and is often used in culinary and fragrance applications.
