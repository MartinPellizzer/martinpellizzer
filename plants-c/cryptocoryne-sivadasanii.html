Cryptocoryne sivadasanii

Cryptocoryne sivadasanii, commonly known as the Sivadasanii cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of India.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green to bluish-green in color. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is often found in slow-moving or still waters. It is popular among aquarium enthusiasts due to its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal.

The plant plays a role in maintaining water quality by absorbing nutrients and providing shelter for aquatic life.
