Caladenia floribunda

Caladenia floribunda, commonly known as the Western Lily-livered Orchid, is an endemic species native to Western Australia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a slender, erect stem and a single, showy flower that typically blooms in spring. The plant features a fleshy, tuberous root system adapted to its arid environment. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker and more prominent.

Caladenia floribunda is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and is protected under conservation efforts.
