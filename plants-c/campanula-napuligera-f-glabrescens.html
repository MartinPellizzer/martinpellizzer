Campanula napuligera f. glabrescens

Campanula napuligera f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a rare and elegant plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and bell-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or violet. Native to specific regions in Europe, this variety thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. Its compact growth habit makes it a desirable ornamental plant for gardens and botanical collections.

Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
