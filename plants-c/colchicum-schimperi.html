Colchicum schimperi

Colchicum schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian autumn crocus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It features striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in the autumn, typically from September to November. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to grow in cool, moist environments at high altitudes.

Colchicum schimperi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
