Capparis henryi

Capparis henryi, commonly known as the Chinese sour orange, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its woody stems and pinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

The fruit is edible but has a tart, acidic flavor.
