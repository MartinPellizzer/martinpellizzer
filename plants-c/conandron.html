Conandron

Conandron, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has a broad, spreading canopy with pinnately compound leaves. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish hue, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

Conandron is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in sunny, warm climates.
