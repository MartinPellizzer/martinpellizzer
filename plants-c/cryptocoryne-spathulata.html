Cryptocoryne spathulata

Cryptocoryne spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leaf cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant thrives in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments with soft, nutrient-rich substrates. It is often found in ponds and rivers, where it forms dense underwater vegetation.

Cryptocoryne spathulata is popular in aquariums due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
