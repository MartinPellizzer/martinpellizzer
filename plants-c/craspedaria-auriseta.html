Craspedaria auriseta

Craspedaria auriseta, commonly known as the golden craspedaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, spherical clusters. Craspedaria auriseta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant color and compact growth habit.
