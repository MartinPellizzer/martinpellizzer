Calyptranthes fluviatilis

Calyptranthes fluviatilis, commonly known as the river calyptranthes, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as riverbanks and forest understories. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. Its small, white flowers are borne in clusters and emit a mild fragrance.

The fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that splits open to release seeds.
