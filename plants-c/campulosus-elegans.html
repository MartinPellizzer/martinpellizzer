Campulosus elegans

Campulosus elegans, commonly known as the elegant campion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Its leaves are linear and often have a slight bluish tint, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Campulosus elegans is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
