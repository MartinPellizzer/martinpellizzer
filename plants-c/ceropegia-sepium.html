Ceropegia sepium

Ceropegia sepium, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia sepium is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
