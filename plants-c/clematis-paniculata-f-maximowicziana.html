Clematis paniculata f. maximowicziana

Clematis paniculata f. maximowicziana, commonly known as the Maximowicziana Clematis, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to the Far East.

It features slender, arching stems that produce clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers in early summer. The foliage is typically dark green and finely cut, adding ornamental value throughout the growing season. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its attractive flowers and hardy nature, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
