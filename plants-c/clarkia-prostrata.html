Clarkia prostrata

Clarkia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate clarkia, is a low-growing annual plant native to western North America.

It features delicate, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has prostrate or decumbent stems that spread along the ground, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a soft, hairy texture.

Clarkia prostrata is often found in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
