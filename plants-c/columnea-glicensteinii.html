Columnea glicensteinii

Columnea glicensteinii, commonly known as the Glicenstein's flame.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. This evergreen shrub is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
