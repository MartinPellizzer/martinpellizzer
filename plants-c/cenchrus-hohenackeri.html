Cenchrus hohenackeri

Cenchrus hohenackeri, commonly known as the Hohenacker's lovegrass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny florets. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species suitable for dryland agriculture.

Cenchrus hohenackeri is often used for forage and soil stabilization in degraded landscapes.
