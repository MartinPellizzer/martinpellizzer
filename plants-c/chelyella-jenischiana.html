Chelyella jenischiana

Chelyella jenischiana, commonly known as the golden star cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family and is native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a thick, fleshy stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help reduce water loss and provide protection from herbivores.

Chelyella jenischiana is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
