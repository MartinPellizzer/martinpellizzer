Cuphea origanifolia var. sublanata

Cuphea origanifolia var. sublanata, commonly known as the woolly red hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its hairy, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, tubular red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
