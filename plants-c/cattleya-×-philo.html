Cattleya × philo

Cattleya × philo, commonly known as the Phaleno Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Cattleya and Phalaenopsis species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in low-light conditions. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve moisture.

Cattleya × philo is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
