Citrus × bigaradia

Citrus × bigaradia, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the cross between the mandarin and the pomelo. The tree is evergreen, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically small, round, and has a thick, leathery rind.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in perfumery and as a source of essential oils.
