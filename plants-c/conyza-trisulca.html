Conyza trisulca

Conyza trisulca, commonly known as the three-rayed weed, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Conyza trisulca is often considered a weed but has some medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
