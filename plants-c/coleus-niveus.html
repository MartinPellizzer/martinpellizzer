Coleus niveus

Coleus niveus, commonly known as white coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
