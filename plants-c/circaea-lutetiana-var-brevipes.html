Circaea lutetiana var. brevipes

Circaea lutetiana var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed wood sorrel, is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale green coloration. The plant produces clusters of delicate, yellowish-white flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and habitat restoration.
