Cattleya wallisii var. ornata

Cattleya wallisii var. ornata, commonly known as the Wallisii Orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to white, often with a yellow or green throat. The plant features long, arching stems and broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and slightly velvety in texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is highly prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
