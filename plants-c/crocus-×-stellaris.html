Crocus × stellaris

Crocus × stellaris, commonly known as the Persian shield, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features striking, shield-shaped leaves that are a deep green with a silvery underside, and its flowers are typically purple or violet with a distinctive, star-like appearance. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. It blooms in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to shaded landscapes.

Crocus × stellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays for its unique foliage and flowers.
