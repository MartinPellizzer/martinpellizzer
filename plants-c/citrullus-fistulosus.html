Citrullus fistulosus

Citrullus fistulosus, commonly known as the horned melon, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and watermelons. The plant produces large, spiky, green fruits that turn yellow when mature. The fruit's interior contains a sweet, edible pulp with black, seed-like structures.
