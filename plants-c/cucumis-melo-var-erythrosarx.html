Cucumis melo var. erythrosarx

Cucumis melo var. erythrosarx, commonly known as the red-fruit melon, is a variant of the cantaloupe belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its thick, netted rind that ranges from pale yellow to deep red, and its sweet, aromatic, orange-colored flesh. The plant is a perennial vine that produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its unique appearance and flavor, making it a popular choice in both ornamental and culinary contexts.
