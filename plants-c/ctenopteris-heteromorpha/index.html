Ctenopteris Heteromorpha

Ctenopteris fern, scientifically known as Ctenopteris heteromorpha, is a strikingly beautiful and versatile plant native to Southeast Asia. This species is prized for its lush, feathery fronds that add a tropical aesthetic to both indoor and outdoor spaces. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts alike. The fern thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish. With its unique appearance and ease of care, Ctenopteris heteromorpha remains a favorite in modern botanical collections.
