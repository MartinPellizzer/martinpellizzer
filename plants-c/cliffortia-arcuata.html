Cliffortia arcuata

Cliffortia arcuata, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers during the winter months. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found growing on cliffs or slopes.

Its evergreen leaves are narrow and leathery, providing a dense cover that helps it withstand harsh coastal conditions.
