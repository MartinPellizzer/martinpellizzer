Caladenia uliginosa subsp. candicans

Caladenia uliginosa subsp.

candicans, commonly known as the Showy Greenhood, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia. It typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors and heathlands.

The plant produces a single, lance-shaped leaf and a single, showy greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive hood-like structure. Its flowers are pollinated by insects, particularly bees, and it blooms during the spring season.

The species is part of the Caladenia genus, which is known for its intricate and often colorful floral morphology.
