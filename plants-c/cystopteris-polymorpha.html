Cystopteris polymorpha

Cystopteris polymorpha, commonly known as the variable water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like leaves that are typically divided into small, finger-like segments called pinnae. Cystopteris polymorpha is often found in temperate regions and is known for its ability to tolerate a variety of soil conditions.
