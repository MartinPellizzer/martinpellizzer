Cratoxylum thorelii

Cratoxylum thorelii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Additionally, it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
