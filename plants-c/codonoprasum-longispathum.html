Codonoprasum longispathum

Codonoprasum longispathum, commonly known as the long-spurred codonopsis, is a flowering plant belonging to the familyApiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and compound leaves with finely divided lobes. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its fruit is a dry, two-valved capsule containing several seeds.

It is often found in moist, temperate habitats and is valued for its aromatic properties in traditional herbal medicine.
