Ceropegia leroyi

Ceropegia leroyi, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that are covered in a layer of fine, silvery hair, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
