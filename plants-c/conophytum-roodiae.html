Conophytum roodiae

Conophytum roodiae, commonly known as the Rosy Coneplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its unique, cone-shaped leaves that store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
