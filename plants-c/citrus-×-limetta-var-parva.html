Citrus × limetta var. parva

Citrus × limetta var. parva, commonly known as the dwarf lemon.

It is a compact variety of lemon that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, round fruits with a thin, yellow skin. The fruit contains a high concentration of citric acid and is often used in culinary applications.

It is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in warm, sunny climates.
