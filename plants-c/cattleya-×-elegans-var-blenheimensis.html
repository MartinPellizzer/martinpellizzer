Cattleya × elegans var. blenheimensis

Cattleya × elegans var. blenheimensis, commonly known as the Blenheim Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, white, and yellow. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in segments with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
