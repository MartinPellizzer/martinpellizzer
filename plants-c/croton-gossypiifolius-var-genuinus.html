Croton gossypiifolius var. genuinus

Croton gossypiifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the Indian croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant features alternate, palmately lobed leaves that can range in color from green to red, orange, or yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules containing seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf colors and adaptability to various growing conditions.
