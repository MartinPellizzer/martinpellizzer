Conyza fistulosa

Conyza fistulosa, commonly known as the slender weed or false dandelion, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in open fields, along roadsides, and in agricultural areas. The plant has slender, hairy stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually alternate, lance-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Conyza fistulosa is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
