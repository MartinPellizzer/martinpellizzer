Cleistanthus contractus

Cleistanthus contractus, commonly known as the narrowleaf hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are showy, with five petals and a yellow center, blooming in clusters during the warmer months.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
