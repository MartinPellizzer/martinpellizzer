Cleistocactus baumannii subsp. horstii

Cleistocactus baumannii subsp.

horstii, commonly known as the Horstii cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and thick, ribbed stems that store water.

The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
