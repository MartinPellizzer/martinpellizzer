Canthium shabanii

Canthium shabanii, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its spiny stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry woodland and savanna habitats.

Canthium shabanii is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
