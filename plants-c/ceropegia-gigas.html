Ceropegia gigas

Ceropegia gigas, commonly known as the giant African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and large, velvety leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia gigas is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
