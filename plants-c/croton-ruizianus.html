Croton ruizianus

Croton ruizianus, commonly known as the flame tree or red croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often bright green with red or orange veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkins, followed by woody, ornamental seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage.

Croton ruizianus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
