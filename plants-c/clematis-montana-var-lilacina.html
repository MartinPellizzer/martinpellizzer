Clematis montana var. lilacina

Clematis montana var. lilacina, commonly known as the purple clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the mountainous regions of China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically large, showy, and range in color from white to deep purple. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a sunny to partially shaded location for optimal growth.
