Chrysanthemum hwangshanense

Chrysanthemum hwangshanense, commonly known as the Hwangshan chrysanthemum, is a perennial flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. Its foliage is usually gray-green, and it produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in the autumn season.

This species is valued both for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses in Chinese herbal medicine.
