Cyperus leptostachyus

Cyperus leptostachyus, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly referred to as the sedge family. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Cyperus leptostachyus is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various aquatic organisms.
