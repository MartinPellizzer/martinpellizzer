Cyperus hilairenus

Cyperus hilairenus, commonly known as the Hilaire's flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. Its slender, triangular stems are often clustered, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.

Cyperus hilairenus is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
