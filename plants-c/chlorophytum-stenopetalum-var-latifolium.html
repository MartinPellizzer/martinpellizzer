Chlorophytum stenopetalum var. latifolium

Chlorophytum stenopetalum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider plant, is a variant of the spider plant species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves that are broader than the typical variety, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that emerge from the leaf axils, adding ornamental value. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and indirect sunlight.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its resilience and ability to propagate through offsets.
