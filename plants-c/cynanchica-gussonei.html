Cynanchica gussonei

Cynanchica gussonei, commonly known as the Gussone's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is characterized by its milky sap and showy flowers. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive corona surrounding the central ovary.

Cynanchica gussonei is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
