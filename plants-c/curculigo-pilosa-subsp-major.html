Curculigo pilosa subsp. major

Curculigo pilosa subsp.

major, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
