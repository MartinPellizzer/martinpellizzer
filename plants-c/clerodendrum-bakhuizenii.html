Clerodendrum bakhuizenii

Clerodendrum bakhuizenii, commonly known as the red-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is known for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses.

Clerodendrum bakhuizenii thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
