Cymbocarpa urbani

Cymbocarpa urbani, commonly known as the urban cymbocarpa, is a small shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a deep red color.

Cymbocarpa urbani is adapted to well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for rocky or arid environments.
