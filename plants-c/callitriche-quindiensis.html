Callitriche quindiensis

Callitriche quindiensis, commonly known as the Theni water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, star-shaped leaves that are often floating or partially submerged. Its flowers are tiny and clustered, usually appearing in greenish-white or yellowish hues.

Callitriche quindiensis plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
