Cenekia rapunculoides var. reflexa

Cenekia rapunculoides var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed cenekia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often reflexed at the base. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months. Its root system is fibrous and adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in alpine gardens.
