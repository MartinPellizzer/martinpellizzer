Coprosma elliptica

Coprosma elliptica, commonly known as the oval coprosma, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage with elliptical leaves that are usually a deep green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, which appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a range of soil conditions.

Coprosma elliptica is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its resilience and ability to support local wildlife.
