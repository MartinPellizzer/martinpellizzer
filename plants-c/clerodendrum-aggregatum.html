Clerodendrum aggregatum

Clerodendrum aggregatum, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, oval fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly rough texture.
