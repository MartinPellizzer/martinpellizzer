Cajanus luteus

Cajanus luteus, commonly known as the yellow gram or cowpea, is an annual legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing small, yellowish seeds that are rich in protein. Cajanus luteus is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

It is widely cultivated for both its seeds and as a forage crop.
