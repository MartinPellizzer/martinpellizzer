Cornutia odorata var. calvescens

Cornutia odorata var. calvescens, commonly known as the sweet-scented cornutia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its fragrance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
