Carex × knieskernii

Carex × knieskernii, commonly known as the knieskern's sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and stream banks. It is often found in temperate regions of North America and parts of Europe.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for wetland restoration and ecological studies.
