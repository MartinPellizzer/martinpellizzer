Cheirostylis malipoensis

Cheirostylis malipoensis, commonly known as the Malipo orchid, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. Its leaves are usually elongated and lance-shaped, while its inflorescence bears small, clustered blooms.
