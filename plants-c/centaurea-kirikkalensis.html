Centaurea kirikkalensis

Centaurea kirikkalensis, commonly known as the Kirikkale thistle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its structure includes a central stem with alternate leaves and composite flowers that attract pollinators.

Centaurea kirikkalensis plays a role in local ecosystems and is of interest to botanists studying plant adaptation.
