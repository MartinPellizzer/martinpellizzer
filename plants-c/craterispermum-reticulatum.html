Craterispermum reticulatum

Craterispermum reticulatum, commonly known as the net-veined sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, succulent stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in dry, sandy soils. Its leaves are usually small and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
