Crinum delagoense

Crinum delagoense, commonly known as the Delagoense Glory-of-the-Snow, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, straight stems with large, showy white or pale blue flowers that resemble snowflakes, blooming in summer. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are typically green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is often cultivated for its beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
