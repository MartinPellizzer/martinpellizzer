Coleus Amicorum

Coleus amicorum is a strikingly ornamental plant known for its vivid, colorful leaves. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in warm, humid environments with plenty of sunlight. Its leaves come in a wide range of hues including red, purple, and green, making it a popular choice for gardeners and plant enthusiasts. Easy to care for, it requires well-draining soil and regular watering. This plant not only adds visual appeal to any space but also brings a touch of nature's beauty indoors.
