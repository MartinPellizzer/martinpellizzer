Cryptanthus giganteus

Cryptanthus giganteus, commonly known as the giant carpet plant, is a striking member of the Amaranthaceae family native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing clusters of vibrant red, orange, or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically green with a reddish hue, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
