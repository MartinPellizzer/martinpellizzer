Commicarpus fruticosus

Commicarpus fruticosus, commonly known as the African sandmat, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, sprawling shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
