Crotalaria dumetorum

Crotalaria dumetorum, commonly known as devil's flower or yellow top, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant contains toxic alkaloids, making it potentially harmful to livestock and humans. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Its rapid growth and ability to colonize open areas make it both a weed and a plant of ecological interest.
