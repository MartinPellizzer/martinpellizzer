Cryptocoryne siamensis var. ewansii

Cryptocoryne siamensis var. ewansii, commonly known as the Ewans' Siamese cryptocoryne, is a rare aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often have a subtle metallic sheen. This variety is typically found in slow-moving freshwater environments with soft, acidic substrates. The plant is known for its moderate growth rate and adaptability to a range of aquarium conditions.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a sought-after addition to specialized aquatic collections.
