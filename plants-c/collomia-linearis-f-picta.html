Collomia linearis f. picta

Collomia linearis f. picta, commonly known as the painted phlox, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features linear, narrow leaves and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats. Its distinctive floral display makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.
