Ctenitis Villosula

Ctenitis villosula is a unique fern species known for its delicate, hairy fronds and distinctive appearance. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments where it can flourish. Its name, derived from Greek roots, reflects its peculiar structure and texture. This plant is often overlooked due to its modest size and subtle features. Despite its quiet presence, Ctenitis villosula holds significant value in both ecological and botanical studies.
