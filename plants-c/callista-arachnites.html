Callista arachnites

Callista arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals that resemble the legs of a spider. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and thrives in coastal regions. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, with a distinctive, elongated lip that aids in pollination by specific insect species.

Callista arachnites is a perennial herb that blooms during the late winter to early spring season.
