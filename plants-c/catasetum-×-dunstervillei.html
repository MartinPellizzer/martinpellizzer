Catasetum × dunstervillei

Catasetum × dunstervillei, commonly known as the orchid hybrid, is a tropical orchid species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, colorful blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Catasetum × dunstervillei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
