Collinsia torreyi var. brevicarinata

Collinsia torreyi var. brevicarinata, commonly known as the dwarf tasselflower, is a perennial herb native to California.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its delicate appearance and suitability in rock gardens and native plant landscapes.
