Ceiba trischistandra

Ceiba trischistandra, commonly known as the yellow ceiba, is a large, fast-growing tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its massive, buttressed roots and a distinctive crown of spreading branches. The tree produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, woody fruits containing numerous seeds. Its bark is thick and corky, providing it with some resistance to drought and pests.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various species of birds and insects.
