Cryptantha Tumulosa

Cryptantha tumulosa is a small, flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It thrives in harsh environments, often found in rocky soils and desert landscapes. This species is known for its delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators. Its resilience and adaptability make it a fascinating subject for ecological study.
