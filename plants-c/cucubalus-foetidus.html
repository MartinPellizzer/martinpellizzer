Cucubalus foetidus

Cucubalus foetidus, commonly known as the stinking cucumber, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, foul-smelling flowers that are often described as having a strong, unpleasant odor. The plant produces yellowish-green fruits that are typically small and oblong in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.
