Cattleya × silvana

Cattleya × silvana, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cattleya labiata and Cattleya mossiae.

It is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers with vibrant colors, often in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
