Coelogyne wilhelmii

Coelogyne wilhelmii, commonly known as the Wilhelm's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media. It blooms once a year, typically in spring or early summer.

Coelogyne wilhelmii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
