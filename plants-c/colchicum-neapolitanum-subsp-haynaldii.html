Colchicum neapolitanum subsp. haynaldii

Colchicum neapolitanum subsp.

haynaldii, commonly known as the Neapolitan colchicum, is a member of the Colchicaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking violet to purple flowers with prominent yellow stamens.

Native to southern Europe, this species is typically found in rocky or limestone soils in woodland areas. The plant features basal leaves that are lanceolate and often persist through the winter.

It is known for its ornamental value and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
