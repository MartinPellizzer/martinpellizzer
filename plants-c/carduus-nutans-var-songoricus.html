Carduus nutans var. songoricus

Carduus nutans var. songoricus, commonly known as the Songarica thistle, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny stems and large, silvery-green leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and resilient species within the Asteraceae family.
