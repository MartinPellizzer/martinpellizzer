Clavija ornata var. subintegra

Clavija ornata var. subintegra, commonly known as the broad-leafed clavija, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. It is often found in coastal regions and can tolerate moderate salt exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
