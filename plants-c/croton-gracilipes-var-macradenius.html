Croton gracilipes var. macradenius

Croton gracilipes var. macradenius, commonly known as the slender croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins and a striking, branched inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
