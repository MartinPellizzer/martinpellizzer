Cistus × nigricans

Cistus × nigricans, commonly known as the dark scented rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is adapted to rocky and coastal environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cistus × nigricans is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional herbal medicine.
