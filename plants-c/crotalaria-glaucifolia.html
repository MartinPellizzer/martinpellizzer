Crotalaria glaucifolia

Crotalaria glaucifolia, commonly known as the blue morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open fields or along roadsides.

Crotalaria glaucifolia is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
