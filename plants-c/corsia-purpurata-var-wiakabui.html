Corsia purpurata var. wiakabui

Corsia purpurata var. wiakabui, commonly known as the Waiakabui corsia, is a striking plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and red flowers, which bloom in dense clusters. The plant has narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

Corsia purpurata var. wiakabui is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
