Chimaphila mexicana

Chimaphila mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican flameleaf, is a perennial evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico.

Chimaphila mexicana thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, forested environments.
