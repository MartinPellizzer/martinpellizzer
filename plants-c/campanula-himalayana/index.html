Campanula Himalayana

Campanula himalayana is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family known for its bell-shaped flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its vibrant blue or purple blooms make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits. This species plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and preserving biodiversity.
