Cotyledon cespitosa

Cotyledon cespitosa, commonly known as the compact cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with red or purple hues. The plant has a compact growth habit, forming a low, bushy rosette. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

Cotyledon cespitosa is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
