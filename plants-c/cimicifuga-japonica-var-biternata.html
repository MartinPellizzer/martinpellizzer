Cimicifuga japonica var. biternata

Cimicifuga japonica var. biternata, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features tall, upright stems with large, compound leaves that are pinnately arranged. The plant produces showy white or pale yellow flowers in panicles during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and traditional use in herbal medicine.
