Camphora decandollei

Camphora decandollei, commonly known as the African camphor tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, aromatic bark that exudes a camphor-like scent when broken. The tree has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and dark green in color. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional medicine.
