Crocodeilanthe Verbiformis

Crocodeilanthe verbiformis is a rare and enigmatic fern native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. Its unique, snake-like fronds have earned it the nickname "the crocodile fern." This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific conditions to flourish. Due to habitat loss, it is now critically endangered and listed on the IUCN Red List. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this fascinating species.
