Clematis fargesii var. souliei

Clematis fargesii var. souliei, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features striking, large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, depending on the variety. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, white hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
