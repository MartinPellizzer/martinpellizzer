Clivia caulescens

Clivia caulescens, commonly known as the stalked clivia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, slender stem and dark green, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright orange or red tubular flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Clivia caulescens is a popular ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
