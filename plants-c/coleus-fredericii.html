Coleus fredericii

Coleus fredericii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves that come in a variety of colors, including green, red, and purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Coleus fredericii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
