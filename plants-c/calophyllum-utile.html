Calophyllum utile

Calophyllum utile, commonly known as the beauty leaf or yellow cedar, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, aromatic bark and large, elliptical leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces yellowish flowers followed by woody, egg-shaped fruits containing a single seed. Its leaves are widely used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory and antiseptic properties.

The tree is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
