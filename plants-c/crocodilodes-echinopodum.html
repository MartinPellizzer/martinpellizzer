Crocodilodes echinopodum

Crocodilodes echinopodum, commonly known as the spiny bladderpod, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. Its fruit is a dry pod that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
