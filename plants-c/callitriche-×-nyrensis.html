Callitriche × nyrensis

Callitriche × nyrensis, commonly known as the water starwort hybrid, is a semi-aquatic plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Callitriche heterophylla and Callitriche palustris, exhibiting traits from both parent species. The plant typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. It has small, floating or submerged leaves that are often opposite in arrangement, and it produces tiny, greenish flowers.

Callitriche × nyrensis is notable for its adaptability to varying water conditions and its role in aquatic ecosystems.
