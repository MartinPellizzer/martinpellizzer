Cytisus × praecox luteus

Cytisus × praecox luteus, commonly known as the yellow flowering broom, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers, which bloom in early spring, and its slender, wiry stems covered in small, scale-like leaves. The plant is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as coastal areas and rocky soils. It has a woody structure and produces pods that contain several seeds.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
