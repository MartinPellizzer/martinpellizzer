Chlora citrina var. ternata

Chlora citrina var. ternata, commonly known as the golden citrina ternata, is a variant of the Chlora citrina species.

It is characterized by its striking yellow foliage, which is more intense than that of the standard variety. The plant typically has a compact growth habit with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
