Codonopsis tangshen

Codonopsis tangshen, commonly known as Chinese ginseng, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy roots that are highly valued in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

The flowers are bell-shaped and usually white or pale blue, blooming in late summer.
