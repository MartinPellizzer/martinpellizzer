Cryptocarya peumo

Cryptocarya peumo, commonly known as the peumo or peumo tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Chile and Peru, and is often found in temperate rainforests. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by dark blue berries.

Cryptocarya peumo is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
