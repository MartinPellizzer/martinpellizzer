Colchicum guessfeldtianum

Colchicum guessfeldtianum, commonly known as the Cape colchicum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a short, stout stem that supports its large, showy inflorescence. Its leaves are basal, lance-shaped, and often with a waxy coating.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
