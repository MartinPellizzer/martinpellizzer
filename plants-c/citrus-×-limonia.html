Citrus × limonia

Citrus × limonia, commonly known as the citron.

It is a hybrid citrus plant that originated from the cross between the pomelo and the mandarin. The plant is characterized by its thick, rough, and often fragrant peel, which contains a high amount of essential oils. Its fruit is typically large, with a yellow or greenish-yellow color and a thick, segmented pulp.

Citrus × limonia is widely cultivated for both its fruit and its aromatic properties used in perfumes and traditional medicine.
