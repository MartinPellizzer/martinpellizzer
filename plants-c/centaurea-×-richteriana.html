Centaurea × richteriana

Centaurea × richteriana, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which consist of both ray and disk florets, blooming from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, aiding in water retention.

Centaurea × richteriana is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
