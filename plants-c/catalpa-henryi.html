Catalpa henryi

Catalpa henryi, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in the summer. The tree produces long, bean-like pods that contain numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 20 meters in height.

Catalpa henryi is adapted to a variety of soil types and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
