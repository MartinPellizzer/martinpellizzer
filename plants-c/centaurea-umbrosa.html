Centaurea umbrosa

Centaurea umbrosa, commonly known as the shadow fleabane, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its dark green, hairy leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils.

Centaurea umbrosa is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
