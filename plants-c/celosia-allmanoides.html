Celosia allmanoides

Celosia allmanoides, commonly known as the flame flower or red dwarf celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are usually bright red or orange, and they bloom in summer.

Celosia allmanoides is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
