Cattleya crispa

Cattleya crispa, commonly known as the Crispa Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces fragrant, butterfly-shaped blooms that are highly prized in horticulture for their beauty and longevity. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cattleya crispa is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
