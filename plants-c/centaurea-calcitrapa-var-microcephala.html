Centaurea calcitrapa var. microcephala

Centaurea calcitrapa var. microcephala, commonly known as the dwarf centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features wiry, branched stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, spiky seed heads. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry habitats.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
