Chresta subverticillata

Chresta subverticillata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that twist and turn, creating a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive form and adaptability, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
