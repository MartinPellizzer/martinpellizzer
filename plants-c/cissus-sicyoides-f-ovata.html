Cissus sicyoides f. ovata

Cissus sicyoides f. ovata, commonly known as the rounded cutleaf goosefoot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are ovate in shape, often with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, sour fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its adaptability and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for both landscaping and indoor cultivation.
