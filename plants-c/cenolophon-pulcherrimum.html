Cenolophon pulcherrimum

Cenolophon pulcherrimum, commonly known as the golden star ginger, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a prominent yellow midrib, creating a dramatic visual effect. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
