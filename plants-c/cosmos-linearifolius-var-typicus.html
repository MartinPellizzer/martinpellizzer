Cosmos linearifolius var. typicus

Cosmos linearifolius var. typicus, commonly known as the slender cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom throughout the summer. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and as a cut flower.
