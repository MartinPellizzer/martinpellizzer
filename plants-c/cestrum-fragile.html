Cestrum fragile

Cestrum fragile, commonly known as the delicate trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Cestrum fragile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
