Caladenia graniticola

Caladenia graniticola, commonly known as the granite rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white in color, with a distinctive lip that is often darker.

Caladenia graniticola is a protected species due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements.
