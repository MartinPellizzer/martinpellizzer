Chionanthus heterophylus

Chionanthus heterophylus, commonly known as the variable fringeflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale yellow flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant exhibits variable leaf morphology, with some leaves being simple and others compound, hence its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
