Cryptandra polyclada subsp. polyclada

Cryptandra polyclada subsp.

polyclada, commonly known as the yellow cryptandra, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It is characterized by its yellow or orange flowers that appear in clusters, typically during spring.

The plant has small, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is part of the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalypts and other aromatic plants.
