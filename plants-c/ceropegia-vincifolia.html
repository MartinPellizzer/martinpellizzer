Ceropegia vincifolia

Ceropegia vincifolia, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features cascading stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are adapted for pollination by specific insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
