Chamaephoenix sargentii

Chamaephoenix sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's fan palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the island of Socotra, Yemen, and is characterized by its slender, unbranched trunk and fan-shaped leaves. The plant has a unique morphology with a dense crown of pinnate leaves that form a compact, umbrella-like structure. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils.
