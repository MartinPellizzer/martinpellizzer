Cinnamosma madagascariensis

Cinnamosma madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar cinnamon tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented leaves and bark. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to their aromatic properties.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
