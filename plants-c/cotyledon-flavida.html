Cotyledon flavida

Cotyledon flavida, commonly known as the yellow cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with yellow or orange markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
