Caladenia serotina

Caladenia serotina, commonly known as the late-flowering spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and a single, showy pink or purple flower that resembles a spider. Caladenia serotina is a terrestrial orchid that grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in heathland or open forests.
