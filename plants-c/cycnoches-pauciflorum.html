Cycnoches pauciflorum

Cycnoches pauciflorum, commonly known as the few-flowered cycnoches, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features a slender rhizome with a few short, erect stems bearing small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact appearance.

The species is notable for its limited floral display, which is characteristic of its scientific name, "pauciflorum.".
