Cephalocereus senilis f. fasciatus

Cephalocereus senilis f. fasciatus, commonly known as the Zebra Cactus, is a unique variant of the Senile Cactus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, banded appearance with alternating light and dark green stripes along the stem. This plant is a succulent, adapted to arid environments, with thick, fleshy stems that store water. The stems are typically short and cylindrical, and the plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months.

It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sunlight.
