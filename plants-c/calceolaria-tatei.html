Calceolaria tatei

Calceolaria tatei, commonly known as the Japanese slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its distinctive slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Calceolaria tatei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its unique and colorful blooms.
