Condalia fasciculata

Condalia fasciculata, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright red flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
