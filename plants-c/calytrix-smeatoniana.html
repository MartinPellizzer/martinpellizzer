Calytrix smeatoniana

Calytrix smeatoniana, commonly known as the red-flowered waratah, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.
