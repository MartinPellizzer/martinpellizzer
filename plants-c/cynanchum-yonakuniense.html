Cynanchum yonakuniense

Cynanchum yonakuniense, commonly known as Yonakuni bellflower, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers range in color from white to pink.

Cynanchum yonakuniense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
