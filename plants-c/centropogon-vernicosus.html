Centropogon vernicosus

Centropogon vernicosus, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or disturbed soils.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds make it an invasive species in some areas.
