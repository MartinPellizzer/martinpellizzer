Conophytum minutiflorum

Conophytum minutiflorum, commonly known as the miniature flower conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, paired leaves that form a compact rosette, which helps it store water in arid environments. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
