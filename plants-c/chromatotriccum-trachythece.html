Chromatotriccum trachythece

Chromatotriccum trachythece, commonly known as the rough-leaved lichen, is a member of the family Parmeliaceae.

It is a foliose lichen characterized by its loosely branched, lobed thallus that is typically grayish to brown in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is commonly found on tree bark and rocks in temperate regions.
