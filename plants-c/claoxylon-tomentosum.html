Claoxylon tomentosum

Claoxylon tomentosum, commonly known as the silky tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery down. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and potential medicinal properties.

The silky tree is also noted for its ability to thrive in tropical climates with high humidity.
