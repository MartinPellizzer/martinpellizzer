Corybas echinulus

Corybas echinulus, commonly known as the spiny corymb, is a species of orchid native to New Zealand.

It is a small, terrestrial plant with a single, erect stem that bears a single, distinctive flower. The flower is typically greenish-yellow with a prominent, spiny spur that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or rocky areas.

The plant is noted for its unique morphology and is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
