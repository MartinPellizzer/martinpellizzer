Cyperus concolor

Cyperus concolor, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or brown in color. Cyperus concolor is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping or as a water filtration plant.
