Ceropegia Plicatiloba

Ceropegia plicatiloba is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. Known for its cascading stems and vibrant, tubular flowers, it adds a touch of exotic beauty to any garden. This species thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation. Its intricate foliage and delicate blooms make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its ornamental appeal, it is also valued for its ecological role in supporting specific pollinators.
