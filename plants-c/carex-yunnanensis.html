Carex yunnanensis

Carex yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province, and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and wetlands. The plant has grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes. It is often found in association with other moisture-loving plants in its native habitat.

Carex yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland ecosystems.
