Cynanchum tamifolium var. minor

Cynanchum tamifolium var. minor, commonly known as lesser milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
