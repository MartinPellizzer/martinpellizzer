Combretum purpureum f. velutinum

Combretum purpureum f. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet flame bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers during the summer months, which attract various pollinators. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes and ecological restoration projects.
