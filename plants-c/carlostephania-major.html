Carlostephania major

Carlostephania major, commonly known as the giant rock cress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant features basal rosettes of leaves and produces yellow flowers in late spring. Its seeds are small and are dispersed by wind, allowing it to colonize new areas.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often found in regions with poor soil and limited water availability.
