Crassula ericoides subsp. tortuosa

Crassula ericoides subsp.

tortuosa, commonly known as the twisted crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
