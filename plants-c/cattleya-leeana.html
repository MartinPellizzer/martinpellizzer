Cattleya leeana

Cattleya leeana, commonly known as the "Ceylon orchid," is a tropical orchid native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are strap-shaped and often have a waxy coating.

Cattleya leeana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses due to its specific humidity and light requirements.
