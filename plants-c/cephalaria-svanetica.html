Cephalaria svanetica

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy stems and yellow to orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial herb that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.
