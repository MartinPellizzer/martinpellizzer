Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. arenarium

Chlorophytum sphacelatum var. arenarium, commonly known as the sand-leafed spider plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color, covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a potted plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
