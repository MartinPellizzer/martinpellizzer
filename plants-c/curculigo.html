Curculigo

Curculigo, commonly known as the "screw vine," is a genus of flowering plants in the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its unique spiral-shaped flowers and rhizomatous growth habit. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Curculigo species are known for their medicinal properties, particularly in traditional Asian medicine.
