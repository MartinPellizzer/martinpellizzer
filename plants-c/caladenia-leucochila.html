Caladenia leucochila

Caladenia leucochila, commonly known as the White-veined Spider-orchid, is an endemic orchid species found in Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and a single, pale green to white flower with distinctive veining. The plant typically grows in sandy soils within heathlands and woodland areas. It produces a single, fragrant flower that is often pale green with white markings.

Caladenia leucochila is a protected species due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity.
