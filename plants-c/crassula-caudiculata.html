Crassula caudiculata

Crassula caudiculata, commonly known as the Stemless Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula caudiculata is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
