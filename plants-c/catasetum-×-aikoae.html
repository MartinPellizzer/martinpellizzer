Catasetum × aikoae

Catasetum × aikoae, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant produces specialized structures called "catasetum" that are adapted for pollination by specific insect species, particularly bees. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking floral display.
