Cyperus hangianus

Cyperus hangianus, commonly known as the Hangi flatsedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its triangular stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wet or marshy environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.
