Cattleya dowiana var. rosita

Cattleya dowiana var. rosita, commonly known as the Rosita Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.

It features large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
