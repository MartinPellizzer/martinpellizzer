Cogswellia serpentina

Cogswellia serpentina, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, sword-shaped leaves that are usually green with white or silver markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water. It is also noted for its air-purifying properties, making it a popular houseplant.

Cogswellia serpentina is a hardy and low-maintenance plant suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
