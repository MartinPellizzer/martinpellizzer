Cattleya × edmarxii

Cattleya × edmarxii, commonly known as the Edmarxii orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other structures without being parasitic. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cattleya × edmarxii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
