Cotoneaster pekinensis

Cotoneaster pekinensis, commonly known as the Peking cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in the spring, which are followed by red berries in the fall. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

Cotoneaster pekinensis prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
