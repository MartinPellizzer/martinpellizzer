Coelogyne speciosa var. major

Coelogyne speciosa var. major, commonly known as the showy coelogyne, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, with petals displaying a vibrant mix of white, yellow, and green hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a dense layer of white, hairy tissue.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
