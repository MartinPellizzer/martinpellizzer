Croton krabas

Croton krabas, commonly known as the Krabas croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by unique, spiky seed capsules. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Croton krabas thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
