Camellia sophiae

Camellia sophiae, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. Camellia sophiae thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
