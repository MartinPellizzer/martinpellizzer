Crinum rueppelianum

Crinum rueppelianum, commonly known as the African lily or swamp lily, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Crinum rueppelianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
