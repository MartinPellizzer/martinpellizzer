Cremastosperma juruense

Cremastosperma juruense, commonly known as the Juruena burrbean, is a climbing legume native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in secondary forests.

Due to its unique morphology and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
