Cenolophon rubrum

Cenolophon rubrum, commonly known as the red flame orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red petals and sepals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both botanical gardens and private collections.
