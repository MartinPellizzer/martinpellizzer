Coleus schimperi

Coleus schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian coleus, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy bracts that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are often mistaken for flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a glossy texture. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
