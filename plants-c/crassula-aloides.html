Crassula aloides

Crassula aloides, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula aloides is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
