Crotalaria misella

Crotalaria misella, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Crotalaria misella is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sandy environments.
