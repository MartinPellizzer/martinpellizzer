Carex macrosolen

Carex macrosolen, commonly known as the giant sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, rushes, and other grass-like plants. Carex macrosolen is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.
