Chlorophytum kyimbilense

Chlorophytum kyimbilense, commonly known as the African Spear Lily, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers in clustered spikes during the warmer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.

Chlorophytum kyimbilense is also valued for its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
