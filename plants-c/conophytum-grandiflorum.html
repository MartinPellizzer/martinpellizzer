Conophytum grandiflorum

Conophytum grandiflorum, commonly known as the giant conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and requires minimal water to thrive.
