Croton grandivelum var. algernonii

Croton grandivelum var. algernonii, commonly known as the Algernon's croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its striking, colorful foliage.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to parts of Southeast Asia. The leaves are typically large, with a velvety texture and a variety of hues, including shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
