Croton steenkampianus

Croton steenkampianus, commonly known as the Steenkamp's croton, is a striking shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking flora. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
