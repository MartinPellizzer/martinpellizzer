Cnicus subcoriaceus

Cnicus subcoriaceus, commonly known as the rough sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has alternate, rough-hairy leaves that are typically lobed or toothed. Its yellow flowers are arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Cnicus subcoriaceus is an annual or biennial plant that can grow up to 60 cm tall.
