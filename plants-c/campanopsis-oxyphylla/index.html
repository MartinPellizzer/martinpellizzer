Campanopsis Oxyphylla

Campanopsis oxyphylla, also known as the golden bell vine, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This evergreen vine is prized for its pendant-like, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often displaying a vibrant golden-yellow hue. Its unique appearance and fragrant blossoms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design. The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of climates. With its elegant form and seasonal display, Campanopsis oxyphylla adds both beauty and interest to any garden setting.
