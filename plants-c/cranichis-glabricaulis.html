Cranichis glabricaulis

Cranichis glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, unbranched stem that supports a single or few flowers. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, contributing to its overall herbaceous appearance.

The flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination by insects.
