Croton rubiginosus

Croton rubiginosus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from green to red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

Croton rubiginosus thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
