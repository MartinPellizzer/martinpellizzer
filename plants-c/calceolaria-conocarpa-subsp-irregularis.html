Calceolaria conocarpa subsp. irregularis

Calceolaria conocarpa subsp.

irregularis, commonly known as the irregular calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America. It is characterized by its distinctive calyx-shaped flowers, which are typically bright yellow or orange.

The plant produces small, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.
