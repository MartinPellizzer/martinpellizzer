Combretum kwangsiense

Combretum kwangsiense, commonly known as the Guangxi Combretum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pink flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
