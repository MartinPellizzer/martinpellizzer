Cranichis tenuiflora

Cranichis tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high altitudes of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a single, slender stem that bears a single flower. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a delicate, pale yellow or greenish-white flower with a distinctive lip. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
