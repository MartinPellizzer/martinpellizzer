Camellia lungyaiensis

Camellia lungyaiensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, and the flowers bloom in late winter to early spring.

Camellia lungyaiensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
