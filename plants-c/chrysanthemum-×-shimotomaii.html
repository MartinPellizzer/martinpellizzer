Chrysanthemum × shimotomaii

Chrysanthemum × shimotomaii, commonly known as the Shimotomaii chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant typically blooms in the autumn season, making it a popular choice for fall gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, and it is relatively low maintenance once established.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in floral arrangements and garden landscapes.
