Chamaerops palmetto

Chamaerops palmetto, commonly known as the African fan palm, is a hardy, evergreen plant native to southern Africa.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous, leafless sheath. The plant produces a large, fan-shaped crown of leaves that are typically bright green and have a distinctive, palmate appearance. Chamaerops palmetto is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient choice for landscaping in dry regions.

It also produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible, fleshy fruits.
