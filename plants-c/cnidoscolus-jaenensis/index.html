Cnidoscolus Jaenensis

This plant is notable for its distinctive, palmate leaves and the presence of urticating hairs that can cause skin irritation. While it may appear unassuming, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitats. Its study offers insights into plant adaptations and potential medicinal uses.
