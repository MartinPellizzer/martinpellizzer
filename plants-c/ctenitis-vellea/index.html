Ctenitis Vellea

Ctenitis vellea is a rare and fascinating fern species known for its unique leaf structure and delicate appearance. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This plant is often overlooked due to its subtle beauty and limited distribution. Its fronds exhibit a distinct pattern of small, claw-like structures along the margins. Despite its modest size, Ctenitis vellea holds significant ecological value in its native habitats.
