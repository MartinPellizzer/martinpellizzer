Camellia xanthochroma

Camellia xanthochroma, commonly known as the yellow camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is an evergreen shrub that can grow up to 6 feet tall, featuring glossy dark green leaves. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Camellia xanthochroma is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to provide winter color in gardens.
