Cyrtochilum stellatum

Cyrtochilum stellatum, commonly known as the star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers arranged in a dense spike, often with a distinctive star-like appearance. Cyrtochilum stellatum thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
