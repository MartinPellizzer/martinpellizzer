Conophytum ornatum

Conophytum ornatum, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Cape Flax," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, cushion-like growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. Its leaves are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

Conophytum ornatum is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
