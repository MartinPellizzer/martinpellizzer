Callerya songgangensis

Callerya songgangensis, commonly known as the Guangdong callerya, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces striking raceme inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is less commonly found in cultivation outside its native range.
