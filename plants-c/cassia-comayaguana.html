Cassia comayaguana

Cassia comayaguana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow inflorescences that resemble cascading showers of flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia comayaguana is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
