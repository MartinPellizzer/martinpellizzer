Chirita tribracteata var. zhuana

Chirita tribracteata var. zhuana, commonly known as the Zhuana ivy, is a variant of the evergreen vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, tubular white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for hanging baskets and vertical gardens.
