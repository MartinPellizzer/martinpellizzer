Calamagrostis balansae

Calamagrostis balansae, commonly known as the Balansae reed, is a perennial grass native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, stream sides, and damp meadows. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, panicle-shaped flower heads in the summer. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wetland areas.

Calamagrostis balansae is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
