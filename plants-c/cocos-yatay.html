Cocos yatay

Cocos yatay, commonly known as the Yatay palm, is a species of palm native to South America, particularly Argentina and Uruguay.

It is a medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with a slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
