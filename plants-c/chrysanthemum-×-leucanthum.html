Chrysanthemum × leucanthum

Chrysanthemum × leucanthum, commonly known as the garden chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including white, yellow, and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–90 cm and features feathery, gray-green leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Chrysanthemum × leucanthum is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
