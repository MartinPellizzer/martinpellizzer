Cynosciadium pinnatum var. pumilum

Cynosciadium pinnatum var. pumilum, commonly known as the small-leaf cowplant, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves with a pinnate structure, and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its botanical characteristics include a fibrous root system and a tendency to form dense colonies.

It is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and ecological value to local wildlife.
