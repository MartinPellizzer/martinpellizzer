Cineraria polyglossa

Cineraria polyglossa, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery, woolly leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
