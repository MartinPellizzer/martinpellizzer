Camellia granthamiana

Camellia granthamiana, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting eye. The plant is evergreen, featuring glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking backdrop to its blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Camellia granthamiana is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture.
