Carpinus betulus f. columnaris

Carpinus betulus f. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar hornbeam, is a unique form of the European hornbeam.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit, making it an excellent choice for space-constrained gardens. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the autumn, adding seasonal interest. Its bark is smooth and greyish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

This cultivar is highly resistant to urban conditions, making it a popular ornamental tree in landscaping.
