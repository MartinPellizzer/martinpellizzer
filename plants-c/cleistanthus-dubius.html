Cleistanthus dubius

Cleistanthus dubius, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small evergreen tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, reddish-brown fruits that contain a single seed. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.

Cleistanthus dubius thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry woodlands and savannas.
