Cheilanthes bureaui

Cheilanthes bureaui, commonly known as the Burreau's rockcap, is a perennial fern belonging to the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth habit and is typically found growing on rocky outcrops or in dry, shaded environments. Cheilanthes bureaui is well adapted to arid conditions, making it a resilient and drought-tolerant species.
