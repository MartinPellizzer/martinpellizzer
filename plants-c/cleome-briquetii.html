Cleome briquetii

Cleome briquetii, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome briquetii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a notable species within its native habitat.
