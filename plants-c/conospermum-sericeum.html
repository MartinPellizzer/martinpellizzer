Conospermum sericeum

Conospermum sericeum, commonly known as the silky conospermum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Conospermum sericeum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
