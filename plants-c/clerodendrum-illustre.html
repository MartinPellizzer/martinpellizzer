Clerodendrum illustre

Clerodendrum illustre, commonly known as the red lantern plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-shaped red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and serrated edges. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Clerodendrum illustre is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
