Congea

Congea, commonly known as the "Bitterleaf" or "Congea paniculata," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias, and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves. Congea is also valued in some cultures for its ornamental appeal and as a source of natural dyes.
