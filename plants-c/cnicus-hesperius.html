Cnicus hesperius

Cnicus hesperius, commonly known as the western prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It features a hairy stem and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Its leaves are often covered in fine, stiff hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments.
