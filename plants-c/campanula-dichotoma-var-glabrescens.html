Campanula dichotoma var. glabrescens

Campanula dichotoma var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, though the "glabrescens" variety has a smoother texture compared to the typical form. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively low maintenance.
