Cotoneaster acuminatus var. symonsii

Cotoneaster acuminatus var. symonsii, commonly known as the Symons' cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its seasonal interest.
