Citrus medica var. tuberosa

Citrus medica var. tuberosa, commonly known as the Chinese citron, is a citrus species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, aromatic rind and a small, seed-filled fruit. The plant produces a unique, fragrant fruit that is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are broadly ovate with a slightly waxy texture, and the plant thrives in warm, humid climates.

The fruit is also valued for its essential oils and historical significance in herbal remedies.
