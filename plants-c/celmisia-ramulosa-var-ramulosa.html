Celmisia ramulosa var. ramulosa

Celmisia ramulosa var. ramulosa, commonly known as the slender celmisia, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
