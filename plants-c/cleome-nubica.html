Cleome nubica

Cleome nubica, commonly known as the cloudcleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome nubica thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it a valuable addition to diverse ecosystems.
