Cyathea salletii

Cyathea salletii, commonly known as the Sallet's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of feathery leaves that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea salletii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its rhizome system allows it to anchor firmly in moist, nutrient-rich soil.
