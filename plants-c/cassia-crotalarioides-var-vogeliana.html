Cassia crotalarioides var. vogeliana

Cassia crotalarioides var. vogeliana, commonly known as the Vogel's cassia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its bark is smooth and often has a reddish or brownish hue, while its leaves are pinnately arranged with oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
