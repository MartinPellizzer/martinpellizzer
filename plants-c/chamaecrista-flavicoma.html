Chamaecrista flavicoma

Chamaecrista flavicoma, commonly known as yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual or perennial plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oblong leaflets and produces bright yellow to orange flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, and it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The plant has medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
