Cordyla discolor

Cordyla discolor, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to its often-dry environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Cordyla discolor is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
