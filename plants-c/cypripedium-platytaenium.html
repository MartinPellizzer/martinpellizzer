Cypripedium platytaenium

Cypripedium platytaenium, commonly known as the broad-leaf lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble a slipper, with a distinctive pouch-like structure at the base. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories where it can receive adequate light without being exposed to direct sunlight. Its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its cool, humid habitat.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
