Crucianella monspeliaca

Crucianella monspeliaca, commonly known as the Mediterranean sand verbena, is a small, flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Verbenaceae family and typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer, and its leaves are narrow and often hairy. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal areas or on dunes.
