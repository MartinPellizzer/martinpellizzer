Calamagrostis Arundinacea Var. Latifolia

Calamagrostis arundinacea var. latifolia is a striking ornamental grass known for its feathery plumes and graceful appearance. Native to the wetlands of eastern North America, it thrives in moist to wet soils and full sun. Its wide, bluish-green leaves and dramatic fall color make it a popular choice for gardeners. This variety is often used in naturalistic landscapes to add texture and movement. With its ability to attract wildlife and withstand harsh weather, it's a versatile and hardy plant.
