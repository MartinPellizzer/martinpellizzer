Chrysanthemum latifolium

Chrysanthemum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy yellow to orange flowers. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
