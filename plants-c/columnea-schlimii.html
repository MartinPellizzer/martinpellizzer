Columnea schlimii

Columnea schlimii, commonly known as the scarlet miter, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a distinctive red calyx that resembles a miter, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouses.

Columnea schlimii is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering conditions.
