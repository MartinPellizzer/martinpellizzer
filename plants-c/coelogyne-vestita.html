Coelogyne vestita

Coelogyne vestita, commonly known as the adorned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features a thick, fleshy rhizome and strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense, woolly coating. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with red or purple markings, and they are borne on long, slender stalks.

Coelogyne vestita is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
