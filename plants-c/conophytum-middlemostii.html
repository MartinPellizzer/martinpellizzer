Conophytum middlemostii

Conophytum middlemostii, commonly known as the Cape Town stone plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rock-like appearance. The plant features fleshy, triangular leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise rugged form.

Conophytum middlemostii is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
