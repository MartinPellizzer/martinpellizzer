Codiaeum evansianum

Codiaeum evansianum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that come in shades of red, orange, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Codiaeum evansianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
