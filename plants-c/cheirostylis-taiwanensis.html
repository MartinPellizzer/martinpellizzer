Cheirostylis taiwanensis

Cheirostylis taiwanensis, commonly known as the Taiwan cheirostylis, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulbous structure, typical of many orchid genera. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in two rows along the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
