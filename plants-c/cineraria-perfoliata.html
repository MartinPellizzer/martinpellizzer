Cineraria perfoliata

Cineraria perfoliata, commonly known as the perfoliate cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Cineraria perfoliata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural displays.
