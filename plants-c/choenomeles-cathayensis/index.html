Choenomeles Cathayensis

Choenomeles cathayensis is a rare and striking member of the Rosaceae family known for its ornamental value and unique characteristics. Native to eastern China, this plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments. It features showy white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by vibrant red or orange berries in the fall. Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. Despite its charm, it remains a lesser-known species among horticultural enthusiasts.
