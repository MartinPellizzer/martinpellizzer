Chenopodium insulare

Chenopodium insulare, commonly known as the island goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in coastal or sandy soils. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a bluish-green color. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom during the dry season.

Chenopodium insulare is often found in disturbed areas and has adapted to thrive in arid environments.
