Cypripedium × denisianum

Cypripedium × denisianum, commonly known as the Denisian lily, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a white or pale pink color with a deep red or purple lip. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces long, slender leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in cool, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
