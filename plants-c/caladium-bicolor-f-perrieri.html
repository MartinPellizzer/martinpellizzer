Caladium bicolor f. perrieri

Caladium bicolor f. perrieri, commonly known as the Perrieri variety, is a cultivar of the caladium plant known for its striking, colorful foliage.

This hybrid is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is a tropical plant that thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. The leaves are often used as ornamental plants in gardens and as decorative elements in indoor spaces.

Unlike the typical caladium, this variety is more compact and suitable for container gardening.
