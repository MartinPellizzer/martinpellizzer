Cyrtochilum gyriferum

Cyrtochilum gyriferum, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cyrtochilum gyriferum is a rare and delicate species, making it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
