Casearia astyla

Casearia astyla, commonly known as the white casearia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy appearance.

Casearia astyla is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
