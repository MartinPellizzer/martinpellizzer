Carex turbinata var. rigens

Carex turbinata var. rigens, commonly known as the rigid turban sedge, is a perennial plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its stiff, upright foliage and compact, cylindrical inflorescences that bear small, three-angled spikelets. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments.

This variety is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive, textured appearance and low maintenance requirements.
