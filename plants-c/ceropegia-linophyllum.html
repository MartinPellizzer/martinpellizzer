Ceropegia linophyllum

Ceropegia linophyllum, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery or white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
