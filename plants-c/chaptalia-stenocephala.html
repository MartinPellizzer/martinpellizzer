Chaptalia stenocephala

Chaptalia stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed chaptalia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
