Crassula × pulverulenta

Crassula × pulverulenta, commonly known as the "Snowplant," is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its ability to form a cushion-like structure. The plant features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, often with a powdery white coating. It produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months.

Crassula × pulverulenta is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it an ideal choice for xeriscaping.
