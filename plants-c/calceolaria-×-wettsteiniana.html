Calceolaria × wettsteiniana

Calceolaria × wettsteiniana, commonly known as the hybrid slipper orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Calceolaria microphylla and Calceolaria oppositifolia.

It is native to the Andes Mountains of South America, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that resemble small slippers, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It is a perennial herbaceous plant with hairy, lance-shaped leaves.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique floral display.
