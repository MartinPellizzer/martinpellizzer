Cinnamomum burmanni var. chinense

Cinnamomum burmanni var. chinense, commonly known as Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum burmanni var. chinense, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its aromatic, dark brown bark that exudes a cinnamon-like scent. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by oval, dark blue berries. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface with a strong, spicy aroma.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
