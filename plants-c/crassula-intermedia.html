Crassula intermedia

Crassula intermedia, commonly known as the Little Leaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, dense rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Crassula intermedia is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
