Chrysolaena herbacea

Chrysolaena herbacea, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in inflorescences and bloom throughout the growing season.

Chrysolaena herbacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
