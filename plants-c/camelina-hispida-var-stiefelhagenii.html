Camelina hispida var. stiefelhagenii

Camelina hispida var. stiefelhagenii, commonly known as the Stiefelhagen's fennel, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its branched stems and small, yellow flowers. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture. It produces small, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds.

This plant is native to Central Asia and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
