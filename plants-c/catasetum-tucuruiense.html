Catasetum tucuruiense

Catasetum tucuruiense, commonly known as the Tucurui orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of northern Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often yellow or orange with intricate patterns, and they emit a mild fragrance.

Catasetum tucuruiense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
