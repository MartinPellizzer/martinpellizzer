Calochilus praealtus

Calochilus praealtus, commonly known as the tall spider orchid, is an orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and striking, spider-like flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant produces its showy blooms during the spring and summer seasons, often in shaded or moist environments. Calochilus praealtus is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in heathlands and forest margins.
