Calliandra dysantha var. opulenta

Calliandra dysantha var. opulenta, commonly known as the showy calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its bright red, pink, or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has slender, branching stems and pinnately arranged leaves with small, pointed leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
