Chionanthus caymanensis

Chionanthus caymanensis, commonly known as the Cayman mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Cayman Islands.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and limestone habitats. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating.

The Cayman mistflower is adapted to the tropical climate and is an important part of the local flora.
