Centaurea nervosa

Centaurea nervosa, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Centaurea nervosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
