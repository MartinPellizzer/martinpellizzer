Calligonum × dubianskyi f. bubyrii

Calligonum × dubianskyi f. bubyrii, commonly known as the hybrid saltbush, is a shrub native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Calligonum dubianskyi and Calligonum bubyrii, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant is adapted to saline and alkaline soils, making it a valuable species for reclaiming degraded lands. It has a compact growth habit with dense foliage, and its leaves are typically small and scale-like.

This species is also noted for its resilience to drought and extreme temperatures, contributing to its ecological importance in harsh environments.
