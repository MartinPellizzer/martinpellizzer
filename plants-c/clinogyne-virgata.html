Clinogyne virgata

Clinogyne virgata, commonly known as the slender sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered and produce small, inconspicuous flowers in early spring.
