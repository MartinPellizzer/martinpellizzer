Crotalaria maypurensis var. depauperata

Crotalaria maypurensis var. depauperata, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, yellow-orange flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of the genus Crotalaria. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role in nitrogen fixation, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
