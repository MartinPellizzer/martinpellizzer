Caesalpinia jayabo var. microphylla

Caesalpinia jayabo var. microphylla, commonly known as the dwarf jayabo or small-leaf jayabo, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, elliptical leaflets and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and unique foliage structure.
