Cleisostoma beckleri

Cleisostoma beckleri, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant features a slender, branching stem with numerous small, ovate leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale green, with a unique structure that resembles a closed lid, giving it its genus name.

Cleisostoma beckleri thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
