Coelogyne huettneriana var. lactea

Coelogyne huettneriana var. lactea, commonly known as the white-laced coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often white or cream-colored flowers with intricate, lace-like patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both greenhouse and garden settings.
