Ciconium lateritium

Ciconium lateritium, commonly known as the rock fern, is a unique plant characterized by its intricate fronds and specialized growth habits.

It thrives in rocky, arid environments, often found in crevices where it can anchor itself securely. The plant's leaves are typically divided into narrow, feather-like segments, which help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. Its reproductive structures are concealed within protective sheaths, a feature that aids in survival in extreme conditions.

Ciconium lateritium plays a vital role in stabilizing soil in rocky terrains and supporting local ecosystems.
