Callicysthus volubilis

Callicysthus volubilis, commonly known as the creeping beauty, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and has a fibrous root system that allows it to cling to surfaces. Its stems are covered in fine hairs, which aid in moisture retention.

Callicysthus volubilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to cover trellises or walls.
