Coelogyne quinquelamellata

Coelogyne quinquelamellata, commonly known as the five-lobed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with long, strap-like leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its inflorescence consists of multiple racemes bearing showy, spurred flowers.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
