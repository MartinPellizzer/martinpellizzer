Conophytum albifissum

Conophytum albifissum, commonly known as the white-streaked conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. Conophytum albifissum is prized for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
