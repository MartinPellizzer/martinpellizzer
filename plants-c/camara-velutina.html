Camara velutina

Camara velutina, commonly known as velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous small, hairy seeds.

Camara velutina is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain regions.
