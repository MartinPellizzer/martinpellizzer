Croton myriaster

Croton myriaster, commonly known as the many-leafed croton, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that can display a variety of hues, including red, yellow, and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton myriaster typically grows as an understory plant in tropical forests, thriving in humid and shaded environments.
