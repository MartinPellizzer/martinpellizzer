Cineraria discolor

Cineraria discolor, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

Cineraria discolor is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and flowers.
