Collinsonia canadensis var. ovata

Collinsonia canadensis var. ovata, commonly known as Canada collinsonia, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.

The species is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
