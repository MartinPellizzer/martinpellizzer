Citharexylum fruticosum

Citharexylum fruticosum, commonly known as the fragrant honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow and bloom in the spring.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent.
