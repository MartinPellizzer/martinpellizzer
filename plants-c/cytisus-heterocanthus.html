Cytisus heterocanthus

Cytisus heterocanthus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and often slightly hairy. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cytisus heterocanthus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soils in certain environments.
