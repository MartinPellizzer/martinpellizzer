Centropogon yarumalensis var. puniceus

Centropogon yarumalensis var. puniceus, commonly known as the Yarumal redflower, is a striking tropical plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or pink flowers, which bloom in dense clusters along the stems. The plant belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. Its foliage is typically lance-shaped and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful inflorescences.
