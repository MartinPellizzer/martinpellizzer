Ceratostylis pristina

Ceratostylis pristina, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its short, creeping rhizomes. The plant produces slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically arranged in a raceme, with three sepals and three petals.

This orchid is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
