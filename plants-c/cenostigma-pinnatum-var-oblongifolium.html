Cenostigma pinnatum var. oblongifolium

It is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
