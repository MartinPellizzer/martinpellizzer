Caladium macrotites

Caladium macrotites, commonly known as the giant leaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length. The foliage is typically a vibrant mix of red, pink, and white colors, making it a striking ornamental plant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
