Conyza trinervis

Conyza trinervis, commonly known as the three-veined blumea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and hairy leaves. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
