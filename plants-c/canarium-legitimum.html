Canarium legitimum

Canarium legitimum, commonly known as the yellow mangostin, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The tree produces a fruit with a thick, reddish-purple rind and a segmented, white, juicy pulp. The fruit is prized for its aromatic qualities and is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
