Calligonum comosum var. turkestanicum

Calligonum comosum var. turkestanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan spurge, is a perennial shrub native to Central Asia.

It features gray-green, woolly leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers in the summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is often found in steppe and desert regions, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion.

Its resilience makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects in harsh climates.
