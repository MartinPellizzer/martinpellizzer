Cactus stellaris

Cactus stellaris, commonly known as the star cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, globular stem covered in short, spiny bristles. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for desert gardens and indoor collections.
