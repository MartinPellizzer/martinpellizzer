Carphephorus Tomentosus

Carphephorus tomentosus is a unique grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is known for its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and marshy areas. Its fibrous roots help in soil stabilization, making it ecologically significant. Despite its modest size, Carphephorus tomentosus plays an important role in local ecosystems.
