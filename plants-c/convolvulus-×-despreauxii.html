Convolvulus × despreauxii

Convolvulus × despreauxii, commonly known as the hybrid morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a variety of morphological traits that can vary among individual plants.
