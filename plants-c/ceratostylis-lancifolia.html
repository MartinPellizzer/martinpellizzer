Ceratostylis lancifolia

Ceratostylis lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spathephyllum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, pendant-like flowers in shades of yellow and green. Its inflorescence consists of a single spike bearing several small, spurred flowers.

Ceratostylis lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
