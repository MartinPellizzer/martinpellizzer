Citrus micrantha var. balincolong

Citrus micrantha var. balincolong, commonly known as Balincolong kaffir lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and fragrant, small, yellow-orange fruits. The plant produces small, round citrus fruits with a thick, aromatic rind and a segmented, juicy pulp. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its culinary and ornamental value.

The leaves and fruit are used in traditional medicine and cooking, particularly in Southeast Asian cuisines.
