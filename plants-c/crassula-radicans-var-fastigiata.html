Crassula radicans var. fastigiata

Crassula radicans var. fastigiata, commonly known as the compact crassula or upright crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, upright growth with small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green color. The plant produces clusters of tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
