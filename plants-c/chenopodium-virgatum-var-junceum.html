Chenopodium virgatum var. junceum

Chenopodium virgatum var. junceum, commonly known as slender goosefoot, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the western United States.

It typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and the stems are cylindrical and somewhat succulent.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
