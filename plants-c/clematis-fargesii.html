Clematis fargesii

Clematis fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese vine or Farges' Clematis, is a deciduous climbing shrub native to China.

It features compound, palmately arranged leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in late spring to early summer, with blue to purple petals and a distinctive yellow center. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis fargesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
