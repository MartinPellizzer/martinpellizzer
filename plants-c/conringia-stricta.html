Conringia stricta

Conringia stricta, commonly known as yellow rocket, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features a straight, branching stem that is usually hairy and can grow up to 1 meter in height. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in the shape of a cross, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Conringia stricta is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.
