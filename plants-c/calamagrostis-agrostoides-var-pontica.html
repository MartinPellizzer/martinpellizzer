Calamagrostis agrostoides var. pontica

Calamagrostis agrostoides var. pontica, commonly known as the Pontic reed, is a tall, grass-like plant native to the Black Sea region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often described as having a metallic sheen. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray panicles of seeds during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near wetlands or along riverbanks.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in wetland environments.
