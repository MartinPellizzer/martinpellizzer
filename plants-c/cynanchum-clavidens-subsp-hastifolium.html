Cynanchum clavidens subsp. hastifolium

Cynanchum clavidens subsp.

hastifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are follicles containing toxic seeds.
