Cotyledon adscendens

Cotyledon adscendens, commonly known as the upright cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are usually green with red or purple margins. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cotyledon adscendens is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
