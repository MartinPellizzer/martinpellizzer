Cyclamen somalense

Cyclamen somalense, commonly known as the Somali cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves and delicate, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions. Its tuberous roots allow it to store water and nutrients, enabling survival in arid conditions.

Cyclamen somalense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with proper care.
