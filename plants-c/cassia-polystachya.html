Cassia polystachya

Cassia polystachya, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods that split open when mature.

Cassia polystachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
