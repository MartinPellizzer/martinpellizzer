Cyanotris

Cyanotris, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet-colored flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as tropical forests. Cyanotris species are native to various regions in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and botanical study.
