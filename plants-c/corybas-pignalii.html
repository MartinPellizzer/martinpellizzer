Corybas pignalii

Corybas pignalii, commonly known as the Pignal's hooded orchid, is a unique species of orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking, hood-like flower that resembles a helmet, which is a distinctive feature among orchids. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, shaded conditions. Its flowers are usually a deep purple or violet color, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insects.

Corybas pignalii is a rare and protected species, highlighting its ecological and botanical significance.
