Crotalaria microcarpa f. subuniflora

Crotalaria microcarpa f. subuniflora, commonly known as the narrow-fruit cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and narrow, elongated pods. The plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. It has trifoliate leaves with three oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
