Callisemaea sericea

Callisemaea sericea, commonly known as silverleaf phlox, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy clusters of pink to lavender flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact growth habit with numerous stems that produce numerous small, fragrant blooms.

Callisemaea sericea is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
