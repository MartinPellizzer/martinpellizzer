Cyclamen kusnetzovii

Cyclamen kusnetzovii, commonly known as the Kusnetzov's cyclamen, is a species native to the Caucasus region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy coating and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Cyclamen kusnetzovii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
