Crinum cordofanum

Crinum cordofanum, commonly known as the African lily or broadleaf crinum, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant features strap-shaped, broad leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Crinum cordofanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
