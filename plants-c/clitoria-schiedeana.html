Clitoria schiedeana

Clitoria schiedeana, commonly known as the blue pea or butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine and as a natural dye. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
