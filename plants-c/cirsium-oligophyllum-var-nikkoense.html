Cirsium oligophyllum var. nikkoense

Cirsium oligophyllum var. nikkoense, commonly known as Nikko thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It features tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, often with a reddish tint. The plant produces purple to pink flowers in spherical clusters, typically blooming in late summer. Its leaves are deeply lobed and covered in fine, prickly hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
