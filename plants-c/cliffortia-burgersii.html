Cliffortia burgersii

Cliffortia burgersii, commonly known as the Burgers' cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color, blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, particularly in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
