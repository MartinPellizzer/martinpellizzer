Cliffordiochloa parvispicula

Cliffordiochloa parvispicula, commonly known as the small-spiked grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often found in mountainous regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of local ecosystems and potentially valuable for ecological studies.
