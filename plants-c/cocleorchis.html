Cocleorchis

Cocleorchis, commonly known as the orchid with a unique spiral-shaped flower.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family, characterized by its intricate and often showy blooms. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers exhibit a distinctive spiral pattern, which is a rare feature among orchids.

The botanical structure includes a sympodial growth habit and specialized roots adapted for nutrient absorption.
