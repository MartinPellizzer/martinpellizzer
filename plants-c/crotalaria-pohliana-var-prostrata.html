Crotalaria pohliana var. prostrata

Crotalaria pohliana var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate crotalaria, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its prostrate or creeping growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow to orange flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often have a hairy surface.
