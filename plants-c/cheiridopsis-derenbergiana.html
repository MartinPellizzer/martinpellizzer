Cheiridopsis derenbergiana

Cheiridopsis derenbergiana, commonly known as the Doringkop or "Ringhead" ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and vibrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
