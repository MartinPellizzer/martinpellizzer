Caryocar butyrosum

Caryocar butyrosum, commonly known as the butter tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters in height. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by large, woody fruits that contain a single, oily seed. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a leathery texture.

The tree is valued for its oil-rich seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of biodiesel.
