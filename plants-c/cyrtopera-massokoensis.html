Cyrtopera massokoensis

Cyrtopera massokoensis, commonly known as the Massoko orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories or rocky areas. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, are often pale green to white with a subtle fragrance.

Cyrtopera massokoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
