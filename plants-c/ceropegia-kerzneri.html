Ceropegia kerzneri

Ceropegia kerzneri, commonly known as the Madagascar star vine, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It features thin, wiry stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical environments. It is often grown as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.

Ceropegia kerzneri prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
