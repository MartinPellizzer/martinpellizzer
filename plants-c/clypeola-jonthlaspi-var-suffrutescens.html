Clypeola jonthlaspi var. suffrutescens

Clypeola jonthlaspi var. suffrutescens, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or arctic environments. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and small, erect flowering stems with clustered flowers. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, with four petals arranged in a cross shape, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

The plant is adapted to harsh conditions and is often found in regions with cold climates.
