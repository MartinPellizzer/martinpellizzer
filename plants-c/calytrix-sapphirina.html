Calytrix sapphirina

Calytrix sapphirina, commonly known as the Sapphire Beauty, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, featuring small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.
