Cotula intermedia

Cotula intermedia, commonly known as the common groundsel, is a low-growing annual herb native to southern Africa.

It has small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Cotula intermedia is adapted to arid conditions and can tolerate poor soil quality.
