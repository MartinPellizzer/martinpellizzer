Chamaecrista onusta

Chamaecrista onusta, commonly known as the yellow cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Chamaecrista onusta is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties, which can benefit soil health in agricultural settings.
