Chenopodium minuatum

Chenopodium minuatum, commonly known as slender goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its small, triangular leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of North America, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

Chenopodium minuatum is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
