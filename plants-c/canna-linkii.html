Canna linkii

Canna linkii, commonly known as the giant canna, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, vibrant leaves that can grow up to 2 feet in length, and its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a landscape plant.

Canna linkii is also noted for its ability to tolerate a wide range of environmental conditions, making it a versatile choice for many garden settings.
