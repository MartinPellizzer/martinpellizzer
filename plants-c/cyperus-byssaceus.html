Cyperus byssaceus

Cyperus byssaceus, commonly known as swamp spikerush, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and swamps. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It is characterized by its ability to thrive in saturated soils and its role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems.

Cyperus byssaceus is often used in wetland restoration projects due to its ecological importance.
