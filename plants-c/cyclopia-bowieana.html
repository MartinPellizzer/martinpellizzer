Cyclopia bowieana

Cyclopia bowieana, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in coastal and mountainous regions, where it adapts well to arid conditions.

Cyclopia bowieana is also noted for its medicinal properties, used traditionally for its anti-inflammatory and digestive benefits.
