Chrysanthemum gloriosum

Chrysanthemum gloriosum, commonly known as the Glory chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with serrated, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscaping.

Its blooming season usually spans from late summer to early autumn.
