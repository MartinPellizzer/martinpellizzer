Cirsium clokeyi

Cirsium clokeyi, commonly known as the Clokey's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of western North America, particularly in California and Nevada. The plant is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, showy purple to pink flowers that bloom in the summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cirsium clokeyi is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
