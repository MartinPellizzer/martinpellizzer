Centaurea Jordaniana Subsp. Balbisiana

Centaurea jordaniana subsp. balbisiana is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East. It is known for its vibrant blue to purple flowers and its ability to thrive in harsh, dry environments. This subspecies is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
