Coelogyne Angustiloba

Coelogyne angustiloba is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its elegant, narrow leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers, this species is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture levels. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers. This plant exemplifies the beauty and diversity found within the orchid family.
