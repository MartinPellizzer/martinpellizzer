Citrosma ovalis

Citrosma ovalis, commonly known as the oval citrus tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-colored fruits that are similar in appearance to citrus fruits. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study and potential medicinal use.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
