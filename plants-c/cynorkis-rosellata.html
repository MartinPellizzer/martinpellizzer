Cynorkis rosellata

Cynorkis rosellata, commonly known as the Rosella orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.
