Coelogyne tenuifolia

Coelogyne tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a silvery underside. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne tenuifolia is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
