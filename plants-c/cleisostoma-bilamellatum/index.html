Cleisostoma Bilamellatum

Cleisostoma bilamellatum is a rare and striking orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, fragrant flowers and unique floral structure, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its two-lipped petals and intricate sepals make it a standout in the orchid family. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the global horticultural community.
