Colliguaja integerrima

Colliguaja integerrima, commonly known as the mountain nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in high-altitude environments. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
