Cynanchum undatum

Cynanchum undatum, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes milkweeds, and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its milky sap contains toxic compounds.

Cynanchum undatum is often found in open habitats such as prairies, woodlands, and disturbed areas, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
