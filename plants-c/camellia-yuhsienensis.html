Camellia yuhsienensis

Camellia yuhsienensis, commonly known as the Yuhsien camellia, is a species native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces striking, large, double-flowered blooms in shades of pink and white during late winter to early spring. The plant features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Camellia yuhsienensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
