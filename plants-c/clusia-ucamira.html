Clusia ucamira

Clusia ucamira, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.

Clusia ucamira is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its resistance to pests and diseases.
