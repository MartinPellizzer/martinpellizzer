Cyperus xanthocomus

Cyperus xanthocomus, commonly known as the yellow flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically yellow or brown in color. It thrives in wet or moist soils, often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Cyperus xanthocomus is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and ability to tolerate wet conditions.
