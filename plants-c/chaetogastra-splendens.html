Chaetogastra splendens

Chaetogastra splendens, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking golden-yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern, which bloom during the wet season. The plant has long, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
