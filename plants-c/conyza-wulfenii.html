Conyza wulfenii

Conyza wulfenii, commonly known as white cudweed, is an annual flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, while the flowers are white with yellow centers.

Conyza wulfenii is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
