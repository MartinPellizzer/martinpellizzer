Cattleya × resplendens

Cattleya × resplendens, commonly known as the Royal Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip. The plant is an epiphyte, typically growing on trees in humid tropical environments. It produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in nutrient-poor conditions.

Cattleya × resplendens is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice in horticulture and floral arrangements.
