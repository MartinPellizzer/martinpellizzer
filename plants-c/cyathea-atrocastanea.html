Cyathea atrocastanea

Cyathea atrocastanea, commonly known as the black-stemmed tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a dark, leathery rind. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically in tropical rainforests.

Cyathea atrocastanea plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various species.
