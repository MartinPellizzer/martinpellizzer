Cyperus bulbosus f. depauperatus

Cyperus bulbosus f. depauperatus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a compact, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded soils, often in marshy areas or along waterways. Its botanical structure is characterized by a short rhizome and a basal rosette of leaves.

It is valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in moist conditions and its ornamental appeal in water gardens.
