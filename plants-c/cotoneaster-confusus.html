Cotoneaster confusus

Cotoneaster confusus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rosaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are attractive to birds. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy appearance.

Cotoneaster confusus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
