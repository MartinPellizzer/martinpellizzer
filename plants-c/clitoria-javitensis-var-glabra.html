Clitoria javitensis var. glabra

Clitoria javitensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers, which are typically violet or purple in color. The plant has compound leaves with three leaflets and produces elongated pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food.

The smooth, glabrous texture of its stems and leaves is a distinguishing feature of this variety.
