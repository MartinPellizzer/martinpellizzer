Clutia robusta var. polyphylla

Clutia robusta var. polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved clutia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical regions.
