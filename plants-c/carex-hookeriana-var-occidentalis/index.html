Carex Hookeriana Var. Occidentalis

Carex hookeriana var. occidentalis is a striking sedge native to the western united states. it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and riparian zones. this plant is known for its dense, grass-like foliage and its ability to stabilize soil in sensitive ecosystems. it plays an important role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining wetland habitats. its unique appearance and ecological value make it a noteworthy subject for both botanists and nature enthusiasts.
