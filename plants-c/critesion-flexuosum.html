Critesion flexuosum

Critesion flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible cress, is a herbaceous plant native to wetland environments in North America.

It features slender, ascending stems that often curl or twist, giving it its common name. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and have a distinct, peppery aroma. Its small, white or greenish flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Critesion flexuosum is often found in moist soils near streams, ponds, and marshes.
