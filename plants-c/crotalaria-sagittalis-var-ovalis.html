Crotalaria sagittalis var. ovalis

Crotalaria sagittalis var. ovalis, commonly known as the arrowhead crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family, and is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a common weed in agricultural and disturbed areas.

Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
