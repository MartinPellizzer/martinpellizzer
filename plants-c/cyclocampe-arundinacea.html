Cyclocampe arundinacea

Cyclocampe arundinacea, commonly known as the bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly China and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and clusters of small, fragrant orchid flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cyclocampe arundinacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
