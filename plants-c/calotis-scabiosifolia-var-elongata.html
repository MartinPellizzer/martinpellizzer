Calotis scabiosifolia var. elongata

Calotis scabiosifolia var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated calotis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in dry, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value and resilience, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
