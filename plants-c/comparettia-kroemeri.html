Comparettia kroemeri

Comparettia kroemeri, commonly known as the Kroemeri's ghost orchid, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It possesses long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces striking, white to pale pink flowers with intricate patterns resembling ghostly shapes, hence its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat needs, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
