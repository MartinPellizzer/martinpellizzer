Camellia japonica var. lysantha

Camellia japonica var. lysantha, commonly known as the Japanese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a contrasting veining. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia japonica var. lysantha is also valued for its ability to bloom in winter, adding color to gardens during the colder months.
