Commelinidium nervosum

Commelinidium nervosum, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae, which includes grass-like plants and is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white or pale blue flowers with three sepals and three petals. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
