Cleistocactus guentheri

Cleistocactus guentheri, commonly known as the ghost cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that open only at night, a trait known as nocturnal flowering. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and adapting to its harsh desert environment.

Cleistocactus guentheri is often cultivated for its unique appearance and resilience in dry conditions.
