Columnea hirsuta f. wullschaegelii

Columnea hirsuta f. wullschaegelii, commonly known as the Wullschaegel's hairy columnea, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant blooms and unique foliage texture.

It is often cultivated as a container plant or in shaded garden beds.
