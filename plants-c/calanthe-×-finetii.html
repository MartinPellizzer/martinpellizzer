Calanthe × finetii

Calanthe × finetii, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a naturally occurring hybrid between Calanthe triplicata and Calanthe formosana.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Taiwan, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
