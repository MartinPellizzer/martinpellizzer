Cnestis polyphylla var. bullata

Cnestis polyphylla var. bullata, commonly known as the bullate cnestis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, warty, or bullate texture on its leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shrubland and woodland habitats, often in well-drained soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
