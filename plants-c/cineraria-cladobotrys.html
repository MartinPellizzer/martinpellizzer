Cineraria cladobotrys

Cineraria cladobotrys, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and salty air.
