Citrus × limonum var. balotina

Citrus × limonum var. balotina, commonly known as the Balotina lemon, is a variety of lemon characterized by its small, round fruits.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is cultivated for its aromatic zest and juice. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces fragrant white flowers. The fruit has a high concentration of citric acid and essential oils, contributing to its distinct flavor.

It is often used in culinary applications and traditional medicine due to its nutritional and therapeutic properties.
