Calliandra kunthii

Calliandra kunthii, commonly known as the Mexican beauty or pink shower tree, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences that resemble small showers of pink or red flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
