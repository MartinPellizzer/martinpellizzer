Coelogyne Lamellulifera

Coelogyne lamellulifera is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms and unique leaf structure, this plant is a favorite among orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation to flourish. Its intricate flowers often display shades of pink and white, making it a prized addition to any collection. Despite its beauty, Coelogyne lamellulifera remains relatively unknown to the general public.
