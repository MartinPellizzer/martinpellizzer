Chrysanthemum praealtum

Chrysanthemum praealtum, commonly known as the tall chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to yellow and orange. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with finely divided, fern-like leaves. Its flowering period occurs in the autumn, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
