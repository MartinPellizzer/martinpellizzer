Cassia multijuga

Cassia multijuga, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Cassia multijuga is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
