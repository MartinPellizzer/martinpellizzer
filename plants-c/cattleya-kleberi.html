Cattleya kleberi

Cattleya kleberi, commonly known as the Kleber's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with bright yellow petals and a deep red lip. The plant typically blooms once a year, often in the spring or early summer. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements have made it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.
