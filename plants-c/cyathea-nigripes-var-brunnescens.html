Cyathea nigripes var. brunnescens

Cyathea nigripes var. brunnescens, commonly known as the brown-stemmed tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a thick, brownish layer of old leaf bases. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length, giving it a dramatic appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique morphology and ecological role make it an important component of its native ecosystem.
