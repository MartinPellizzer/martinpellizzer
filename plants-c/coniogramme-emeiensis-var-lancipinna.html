Coniogramme emeiensis var. lancipinna

Coniogramme emeiensis var. lancipinna, commonly known as the Emei Mountain coniogramme, is a rare fern species native to China.

It features delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are typically dark green and covered in fine hairs. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions with cool, humid climates. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitat.
