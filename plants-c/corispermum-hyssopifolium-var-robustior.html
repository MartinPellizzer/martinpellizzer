Corispermum hyssopifolium var. robustior

Corispermum hyssopifolium var. robustior, commonly known as the robust hedge mustard, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, branching stems covered in small, glandular hairs. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly serrated margin. Its flowers are small, yellowish, and clustered in dense, terminal spikes.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
