Crinum chinense

Crinum chinense, commonly known as the Chinese crinum, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are arranged in a fan shape at the base. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies.

Crinum chinense is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in some cultures.
