Croton decipiens

Croton decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves that can range from green to red, yellow, or even purple. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine, though its toxicity requires caution.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
