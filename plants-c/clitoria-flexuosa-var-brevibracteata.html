Clitoria flexuosa var. brevibracteata

Clitoria flexuosa var. brevibracteata, commonly known as the dwarf winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, trailing stems with heart-shaped, bright green leaves. The plant produces clusters of violet or purple pea-like flowers, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. Its small, bracteate pods contain edible seeds that are used in traditional cuisines.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and nutritional value.
