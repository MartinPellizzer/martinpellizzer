Cystea

Cystea, commonly known as the Christmas tree fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that allows it to anchor itself in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its dramatic appearance.

Cystea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique and evergreen foliage.
