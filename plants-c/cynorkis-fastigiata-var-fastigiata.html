Cynorkis fastigiata var. fastigiata

Cynorkis fastigiata var. fastigiata, commonly known as the upright cynorkis, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, columnar growth with narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a compact, upright fashion. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a specimen plant in landscaped settings.
