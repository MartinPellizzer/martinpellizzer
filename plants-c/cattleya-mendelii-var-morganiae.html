Cattleya mendelii var. morganiae

Cattleya mendelii var. morganiae, commonly known as the Morgan's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking floral display.

It features large, fragrant blossoms that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, often in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
