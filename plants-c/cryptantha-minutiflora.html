Cryptantha minutiflora

Cryptantha minutiflora, commonly known as the small-flowered cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

Cryptantha minutiflora plays a role in soil stabilization and provides nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
