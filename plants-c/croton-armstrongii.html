Croton armstrongii

Croton armstrongii, commonly known as Armstrong's croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Its leaves are typically alternate and palmately lobed, adding to its ornamental appeal.
