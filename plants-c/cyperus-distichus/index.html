Cyperus Distichus

Cyperus distichus is a unique aquatic plant known for its striking, grass-like appearance. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshes and shallow waters. This plant has narrow, linear leaves that grow in pairs, giving it a distinctive look. It plays an important role in maintaining ecological balance by providing habitat for small aquatic life. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both natural and artificial water features.
