Centaurea lasiorhiza

Centaurea lasiorhiza, commonly known as the silky centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Centaurea lasiorhiza is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
