Caladenia ensigera

Caladenia ensigera, commonly known as the pink spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance with long, slender sepals and petals. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats such as heathlands and woodlands. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive, elongated labellum that resembles a spider's leg.

Caladenia ensigera is a perennial herb that blooms during the autumn season.
