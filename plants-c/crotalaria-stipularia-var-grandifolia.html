Crotalaria stipularia var. grandifolia

Crotalaria stipularia var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with prominent spurred petals. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
