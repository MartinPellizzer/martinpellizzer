Chysis × sedenii

Chysis × sedenii, commonly known as the hybrid Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are usually red or orange in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers in the spring, followed by elliptical, dark purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and seasonal color changes make it a visually appealing addition to gardens and landscapes.
