Centaurea paucijuga

Centaurea paucijuga, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, bracteate flower clusters.

Centaurea paucijuga is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
