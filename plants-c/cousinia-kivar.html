Cousinia kivar

Cousinia kivar. commonly known as the Kashmir Himalayan fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and hairy, helping to reduce water loss in dry climates.

Cousinia kivar produces small, yellow to brown flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
