Clematis fruticosa var. viridis

Clematis fruticosa var. viridis, commonly known as the green Clematis, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features compound leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white to pale blue, usually blooming in late spring. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden conditions.
