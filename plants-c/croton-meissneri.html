Croton meissneri

Croton meissneri, commonly known as the Meissner's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, colorful, and often strikingly shaped leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or even purple. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its foliage is highly variable, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Croton meissneri prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, thriving in warm, humid climates.
