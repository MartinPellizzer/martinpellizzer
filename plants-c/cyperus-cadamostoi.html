Cyperus cadamostoi

Cyperus cadamostoi, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedgeland, is a perennial grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically brown or purple in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands.

Cyperus cadamostoi plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
