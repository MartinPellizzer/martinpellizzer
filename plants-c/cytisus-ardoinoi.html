Cytisus ardoinoi

Cytisus ardoinoi, commonly known as the Madagascar broom, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to the island's unique climate. Its leaves are small, compound, and often hairy, while its pods contain several seeds.
