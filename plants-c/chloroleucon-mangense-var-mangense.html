Chloroleucon mangense var. mangense

Chloroleucon mangense var. mangense, commonly known as the mangrove chloroleucon, is a halophytic plant adapted to saline environments.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its succulent leaves and flowering heads. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, including mangrove swamps and tidal flats, where it tolerates high salt concentrations. Its root system helps stabilize sediments and prevent erosion in these dynamic ecosystems.
