Cleome bangiana

Cleome bangiana, commonly known as the tropical spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescences that consist of small, pale yellow to white flowers clustered around a central stem. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with alternate, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Cleome bangiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
