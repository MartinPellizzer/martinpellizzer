Campanula afra

Campanula afra, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Campanula afra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
