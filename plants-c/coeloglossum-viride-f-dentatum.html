Coeloglossum viride f. dentatum

Coeloglossum viride f. dentatum, commonly known as the serrated green hawksbeard, is a variant of the green hawksbeard plant.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features finely serrated leaves and produces bright yellow composite flowers. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
