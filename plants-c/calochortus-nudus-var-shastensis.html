Calochortus nudus var. shastensis

Calochortus nudus var. shastensis, commonly known as the Shasta mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their showy blooms. This particular variety is characterized by its pale pink to white petals and a distinctive, nodding flower structure. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in meadows and along streambanks.
