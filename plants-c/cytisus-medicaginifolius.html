Cytisus medicaginifolius

Cytisus medicaginifolius, commonly known as the medicinal broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and slightly hairy.

Cytisus medicaginifolius is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
