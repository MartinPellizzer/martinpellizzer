Cleistanthus stipitatus f. buxifolia

Cleistanthus stipitatus f. buxifolia, commonly known as the glossy boxleaf cleistanthus, is a variant of the cleistanthus genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage with a glossy, almost waxy texture. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a prominent midrib and a slightly serrated margin. This plant is often cultivated as a decorative shrub due to its compact growth habit and attractive appearance.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden settings.
