Crassula abyssinica var. mannii

Crassula abyssinica var. mannii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces dense clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
