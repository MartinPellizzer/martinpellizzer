Critoniopsis persetosa

Critoniopsis persetosa, commonly known as the hairy leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy structures, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pale pink.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
