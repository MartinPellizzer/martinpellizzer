Caryopteris forrestii

Caryopteris forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese silver bush, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of blue-lilac flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long flowering period.
