Croton mayanus

Croton mayanus, commonly known as the red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that range from bright red to deep orange and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton mayanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant and long-lasting leaf colors.
