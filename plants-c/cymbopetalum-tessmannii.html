Cymbopetalum tessmannii

Cymbopetalum tessmannii, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange, resembling small flames. The plant features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cymbopetalum tessmannii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to add color to gardens.
