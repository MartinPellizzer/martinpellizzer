Chlorophytum pilosicarinatum

Chlorophytum pilosicarinatum, commonly known as the curly spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often curl at the ends. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its ability to produce offsets makes it an easy plant to propagate and care for.
