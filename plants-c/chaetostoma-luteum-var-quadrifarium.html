Chaetostoma luteum var. quadrifarium

Chaetostoma luteum var. quadrifarium, commonly known as the four-leafed yellow chaetostoma, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive four-lobed leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking colorful, exotic plants.
