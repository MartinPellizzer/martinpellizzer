Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. chinense

Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. chinense, commonly known as the Chinese chrysosplenium, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The leaves are alternate, ovate to elliptic, and often have a bluish-green hue with a waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous white or pale yellow flowers in early spring.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices.
