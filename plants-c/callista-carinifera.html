Callista carinifera

Callista carinifera, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
