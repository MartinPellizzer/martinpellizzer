Chromolaena diaphanophlebia

Chromolaena diaphanophlebia, commonly known as the whiteleaf ironwood, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated, and often hairy fruits. Chromolaena diaphanophlebia is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making.
