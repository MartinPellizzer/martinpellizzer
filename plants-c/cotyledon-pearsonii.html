Cotyledon pearsonii

Cotyledon pearsonii, commonly known as the Pearson's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
