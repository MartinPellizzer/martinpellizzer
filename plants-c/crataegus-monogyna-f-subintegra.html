Crataegus monogyna f. subintegra

Crataegus monogyna f. subintegra, commonly known as the European hawthorn, is a cultivar of the common hawthorn.

It is characterized by its rounded, compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features small, white flowers that appear in early spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are typically oval with a serrated margin, and the bark is smooth and reddish-brown.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and hedgerows.
