Clomium

Clomium, commonly known as the false dandelion, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It consists of several species native to various regions of the Americas. The plants are typically herbaceous, with basal leaves and yellow composite flowers. They often grow in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Clomium species are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
