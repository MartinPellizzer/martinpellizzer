Crotalaria trigonelloides

Crotalaria trigonelloides, commonly known as the three-lobed crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, South America, and Asia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are palmately arranged with three leaflets. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, with a distinctive spurred structure at the base of the petals.
