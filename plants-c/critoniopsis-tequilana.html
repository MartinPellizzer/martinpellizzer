Critoniopsis tequilana

Critoniopsis tequilana, commonly known as the Tequila orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
