Clerodendrum trichotomum var. esculentum

Clerodendrum trichotomum var. esculentum, commonly known as the edible spider flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescences and heart-shaped, glossy leaves. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers. Its edible parts, including the young shoots and leaves, are used in local cuisines.

However, it is important to note that while some parts are edible, the plant can be toxic if not prepared properly.
