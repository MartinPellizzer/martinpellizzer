Ceropegia monteiroae

Ceropegia monteiroae, commonly known as the Monteiro's African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale purple or white, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sought-after species among collectors.
