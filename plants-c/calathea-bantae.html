Calathea bantae

Calathea bantae, commonly known as the Bantai Calathea, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and variegation. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, often featuring a waxy texture. This plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its botanical features make it a visually appealing addition to any plant collection.
