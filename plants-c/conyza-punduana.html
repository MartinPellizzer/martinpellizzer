Conyza punduana

Conyza punduana, commonly known as the African dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its bright yellow flowers are followed by fluffy seeds that are easily dispersed by the wind.

Conyza punduana is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
