Cirrhaea dependens var. tigrina

Cirrhaea dependens var. tigrina, commonly known as the tiger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a few leaves and produces striking, showy flowers with dark purple or brown markings resembling tiger stripes. The flowers are typically arranged in a loose cluster and bloom during the rainy season. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it a notable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
