Cyclopia vogelii

Cyclopia vogelii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in a circular pattern. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.

Cyclopia vogelii is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
