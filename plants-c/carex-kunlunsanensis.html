Carex kunlunsanensis

Carex kunlunsanensis, commonly known as the Kunlun sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and stream banks. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or brown, and it is often found in high-altitude regions.

Carex kunlunsanensis is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various organisms.
