Cynodon linearis

Cynodon linearis, commonly known as the common bermudagrass, is a perennial grass native to warm climates.

It features narrow, linear leaves that are typically 2 to 4 inches long and have a bluish-green color. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It is highly drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for lawns and pastures.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly and form dense turf.
