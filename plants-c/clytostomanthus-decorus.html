Clytostomanthus decorus

Clytostomanthus decorus, commonly known as the ornate beauty tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, lantern-like shape. The plant has compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Clytostomanthus decorus is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
