Cotyledon reticulata

Cotyledon reticulata, commonly known as the wrinkled cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a fuzzy, waxy coating. The leaves are typically green with a distinctive network of veins, giving it a reticulated appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
