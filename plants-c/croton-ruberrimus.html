Croton ruberrimus

Croton ruberrimus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright red to orange and yellow. The leaves are often deeply lobed and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
