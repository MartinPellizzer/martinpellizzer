Chenopodium hybridum subsp. gigantospermum

Chenopodium hybridum subsp.

gigantospermum, commonly known as giant goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae. It is characterized by its large, fleshy leaves that are typically green to reddish in color.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or yellowish in hue. It is native to the western United States and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments.

The subspecies is notable for its robust growth and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
