Crossandra pubescens

Crossandra pubescens, commonly known as the hairy crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant orange to red tubular flowers. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Crossandra pubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a potted plant.
