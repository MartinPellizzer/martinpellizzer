Campanula numidica

Campanula numidica, commonly known as the Numidian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North Africa.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and slightly serrated. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula numidica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
