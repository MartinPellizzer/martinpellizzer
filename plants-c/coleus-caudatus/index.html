Coleus Caudatus

Coleus caudatus is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative displays. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care. This article explores the characteristics, care requirements, and cultural significance of Coleus caudatus.
