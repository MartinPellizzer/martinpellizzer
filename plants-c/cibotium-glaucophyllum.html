Cibotium glaucophyllum, commonly known as the glaucous tree fern, is a striking member of the Dicksoniaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of the Philippines and Indonesia. Cibotium glaucophyllum is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
