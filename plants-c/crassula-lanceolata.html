Crassula lanceolata

Crassula lanceolata, commonly known as the football plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
