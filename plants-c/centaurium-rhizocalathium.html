Centaurium rhizocalathium

Centaurium rhizocalathium, commonly known as the purple centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It features slender, branching stems that are typically covered in fine, hairy hairs. The plant produces small, bright purple flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered with a waxy coating.

Centaurium rhizocalathium is known for its medicinal properties and is often found in meadows and rocky habitats.
