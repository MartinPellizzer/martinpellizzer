Chironia nummulariifolia

Chironia nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf chironia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
