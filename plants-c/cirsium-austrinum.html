Cirsium austrinum

Cirsium austrinum, commonly known as the Australian thistle, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant has deeply lobed, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Cirsium austrinum is known for its ability to spread through both seeds and its extensive root system.
