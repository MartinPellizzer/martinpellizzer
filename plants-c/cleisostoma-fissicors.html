Cleisostoma fissicors

Cleisostoma fissicors, commonly known as the slit-veined orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to white in color. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a prominent midrib.
