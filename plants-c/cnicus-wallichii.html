Cnicus wallichii

Cnicus wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South Asia.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, characterized by its hairy stems and lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are edible and have been used in traditional cuisine and herbal medicine.

Cnicus wallichii thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.
