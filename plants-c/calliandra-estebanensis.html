Calliandra estebanensis

Calliandra estebanensis, commonly known as the yellow calliandra, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant has compound leaves with small, pointed leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.

Calliandra estebanensis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding plants.
