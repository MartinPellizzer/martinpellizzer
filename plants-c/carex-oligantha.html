Carex oligantha

Carex oligantha, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedges, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. Carex oligantha plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various organisms.
