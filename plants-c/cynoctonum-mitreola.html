Cynoctonum mitreola

Cynoctonum mitreola, commonly known as the Mitreola nightshade, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale blue, and are clustered in umbels.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that turns red when ripe, containing several seeds.
