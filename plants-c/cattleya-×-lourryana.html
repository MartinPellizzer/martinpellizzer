Cattleya × lourryana

Cattleya × lourryana, commonly known as the Lourryana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant and showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces large, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
