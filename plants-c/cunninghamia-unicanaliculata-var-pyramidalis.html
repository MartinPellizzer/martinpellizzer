Cunninghamia unicanaliculata var. pyramidalis

Cunninghamia unicanaliculata var. pyramidalis, commonly known as the pyramidal Chinese fir, is a variant of the Chinese fir species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its conical or pyramidal shape, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically arranged in a dense, compact manner. It is a slow-growing evergreen conifer that thrives in well-drained soils and temperate climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural settings.
