Cannabis indica var. afghanica

Cannabis indica var. afghanica, commonly known as Afghan hashish plant, is a variety native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan.

It is characterized by its dense, resinous buds and compact growth habit. The plant typically features broad, serrated leaves and can thrive in cooler climates. It is known for its high concentration of cannabinoids, particularly THC.

This variety is often cultivated for its psychoactive properties and traditional medicinal uses.
