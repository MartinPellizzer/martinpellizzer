Cremastra appendiculata var. appendiculata

Cremastra appendiculata var. appendiculata, commonly known as the showy lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, pouch-like flower that is typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a unique morphology, with a single leaf and a tall, erect stem that bears the showy floral structure. It thrives in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and swamps.
