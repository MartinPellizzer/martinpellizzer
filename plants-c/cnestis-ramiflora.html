Cnestis ramiflora

Cnestis ramiflora, commonly known as the branching flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2-5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its showy, yellow-orange flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the dry season.

Cnestis ramiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
