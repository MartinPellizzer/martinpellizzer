Cedronella mexicana var. breviflora

Cedronella mexicana var. breviflora, commonly known as Mexican cedronella, is a perennial herb native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences. The plant features slender, hairy stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a distinct, spicy fragrance.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and traditional medicinal uses.
