Cordylanthus diffusus

Cordylanthus diffusus, commonly known as the diffuse globemallow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Cordylanthus diffusus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
