Cyperus ornans

Cyperus ornans, commonly known as the golden flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae) and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically borne on slender, erect stems. Cyperus ornans thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along water bodies.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and water garden settings.
