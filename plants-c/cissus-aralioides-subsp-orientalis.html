Cissus aralioides subsp. orientalis

Cissus aralioides subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental snow vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia. It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are often covered in small, white, waxy bumps, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, round, green berries. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-draining soil.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
