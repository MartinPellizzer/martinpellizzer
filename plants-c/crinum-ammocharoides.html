Crinum ammocharoides

Crinum ammocharoides, commonly known as the Cape Lily, is a striking plant native to southern Africa.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features strap-like, aromatic leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

Crinum ammocharoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and has cultural significance in its native regions.
