Crepidium holttumianum

Crepidium holttumianum, commonly known as the Holttum's orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and green. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture from the air, typical of its epiphytic nature.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests.
