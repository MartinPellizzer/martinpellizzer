Capsella bursa-pastoris f. gracilis

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. gracilis, commonly known as the slender weed or small heartweed, is a variant of the common chickweed.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender, branched stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a distinctive five-lobed calyx. This form exhibits reduced stature and more delicate foliage compared to the typical species.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.
