Chiliadenus saxatilis

Chiliadenus saxatilis, commonly known as the rock chiliadenus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
