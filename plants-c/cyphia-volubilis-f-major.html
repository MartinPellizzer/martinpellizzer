Cyphia volubilis f. major

Cyphia volubilis f. major, commonly known as the giant cyphia, is a vigorous climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in clusters. Its stems are covered in fine, velvety hairs, and it thrives in humid, shady environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and floral display.
