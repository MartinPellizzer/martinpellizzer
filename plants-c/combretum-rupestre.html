Combretum rupestre

Combretum rupestre, commonly known as the rock combretum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Combretum rupestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
