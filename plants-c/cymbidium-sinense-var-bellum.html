Cymbidium sinense var. bellum

Cymbidium sinense var. bellum, commonly known as the Bellum Cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms in winter or early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are long, narrow, and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The orchid is relatively hardy and can thrive in a variety of growing conditions, provided it receives adequate light and proper drainage.
