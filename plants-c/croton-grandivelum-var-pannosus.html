Croton grandivelum var. pannosus

Croton grandivelum var. pannosus, commonly known as the velvety croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by clusters of orange to red berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical gardens.
