Cattleya dupontii

Cattleya dupontii, commonly known as the Dupont's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cattleya dupontii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
