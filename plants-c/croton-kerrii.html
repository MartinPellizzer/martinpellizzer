Croton kerrii

Croton kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that can display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Croton kerrii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor plant collections.
