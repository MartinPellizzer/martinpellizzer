Clusia erectistigma

Clusia erectistigma, commonly known as the wax plant, is a member of the Clusiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of the Americas. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often green or variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in terminal panicles.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
