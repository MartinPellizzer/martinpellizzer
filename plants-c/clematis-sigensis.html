Clematis sigensis

Clematis sigensis, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to East Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Clematis sigensis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners.
