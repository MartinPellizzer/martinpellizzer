Calpidosicyos

Calpidosicyos, commonly known as the desert bottle tree, is a unique succulent native to arid regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its thick, water-storing stems that resemble bottles. The plant features spiny, fleshy leaves and produces yellow flowers during the rainy season. Its ability to store water makes it highly adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.

Calpidosicyos is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
