Changiostyrax

Changiostyrax, commonly known as the Chinese sandalwood, is a member of the genus *Styrax* in the family *Styracaceae*.

It is characterized by its fragrant, resinous wood, which is highly valued for its aromatic properties. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

Changiostyrax is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal uses.
