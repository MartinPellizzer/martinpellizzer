Centaurea sicula subsp. kroumirensis

Centaurea sicula subsp.

kroumirensis, commonly known as the Kroumire centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf morphology and flower structure compared to the typical Centaurea sicula. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in coastal areas and limestone soils.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
