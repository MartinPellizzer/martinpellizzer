Cyclamen ficariifolium

Cyclamen ficariifolium, commonly known as the Sicilian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of delicate, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Cyclamen ficariifolium is notable for its ability to survive in rocky or calcareous soils.
