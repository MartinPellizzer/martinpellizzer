Costularia pantopoda subsp. pantopoda

Costularia pantopoda subsp.

pantopoda, commonly known as the broad-leaved costularia, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and along stream banks.

The plant features lanceolate leaves with a rough, hairy texture on the upper surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or purple in color.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
