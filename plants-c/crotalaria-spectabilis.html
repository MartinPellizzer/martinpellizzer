Crotalaria spectabilis

Crotalaria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cowpea or yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in some areas.
