Cymbidium eburneum var. dayi

Cymbidium eburneum var. dayi, commonly known as the Dayi orchid, is a species native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
