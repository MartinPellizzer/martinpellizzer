Cleisostoma hincksianum

Cleisostoma hincksianum, commonly known as the Hinch's cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, loose soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are often hidden within the foliage, making it a unique and attractive addition to a tropical garden.

Cleisostoma hincksianum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
