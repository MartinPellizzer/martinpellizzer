Cambajuva ulei

Cambajuva ulei, commonly known as the oil bean tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, compound leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The tree produces pods containing seeds that are rich in oil, which is extracted for various industrial uses. Its botanical name is *Pterocarpus* species, and it belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant is also valued for its durable wood and traditional medicinal properties.
