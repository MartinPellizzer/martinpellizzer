Cattleya harrisonii

Cattleya harrisonii, commonly known as the Harrison's Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that support its growth. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

This orchid is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
