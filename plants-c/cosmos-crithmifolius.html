Cosmos crithmifolius

Cosmos crithmifolius, commonly known as sea cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, yellow flowers and feathery, silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are often used in herbal medicine for their purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

Cosmos crithmifolius is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
