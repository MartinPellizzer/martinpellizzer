Cynanchum cheiranthoides

Cynanchum cheiranthoides, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a cylindrical capsule that splits open to release small, wind-dispersed seeds.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
