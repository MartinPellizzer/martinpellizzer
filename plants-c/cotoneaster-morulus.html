Cotoneaster morulus

Cotoneaster morulus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, bright red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. Its leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Cotoneaster morulus is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
