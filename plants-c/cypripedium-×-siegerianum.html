Cypripedium × siegerianum

Cypripedium × siegerianum, commonly known as the Sieger's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, slipper-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on the forest floor or in rocky crevices. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single, large flower per stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique and striking blooms.
