Cattleya mossiae var. aurea

Cattleya mossiae var. aurea, commonly known as the Golden Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant yellow flowers.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy blooms in shades of yellow and gold. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
