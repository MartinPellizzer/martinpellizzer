Citrosma subinodora

Citrosma subinodora, commonly known as the Malabar citron.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly rough texture. Its fragrant flowers are yellowish-green and are followed by small, round fruits that are used in traditional medicine.

The botanical features of Citrosma subinodora make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
