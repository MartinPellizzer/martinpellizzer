Crassula debilis

Crassula debilis, commonly known as the baby crassula or miniature crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the summer months. It is a compact, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Crassula debilis is often used in rock gardens or as a potted plant due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
