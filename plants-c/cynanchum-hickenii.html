Cynanchum hickenii

Cynanchum hickenii, commonly known as Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are follicles containing toxic seeds. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.

However, due to its toxic compounds, it should be handled with care.
