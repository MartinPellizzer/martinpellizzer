Clinogyne ramosissima

Clinogyne ramosissima, commonly known as the silverleaf daphne, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a woody structure with many branches, giving it a bushy appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage and subtle floral display.
