Cynanchica nitida

Cynanchica nitida, commonly known as the slender milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the arid and semi-arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is adapted to survive in harsh environments with limited water availability, showcasing deep root systems and efficient water conservation mechanisms.

Cynanchica nitida is often found in dry steppes and rocky areas, contributing to the ecological diversity of its native habitats.
