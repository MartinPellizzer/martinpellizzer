Crotalaria mildbraedii

Crotalaria mildbraedii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing several seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils, making it an adaptable species in various ecological settings.
