Caladenia stellata

Caladenia stellata, commonly known as the star lily, is a striking orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in heathlands and open forests. Its floral structure includes a single, erect, showy labellum that distinguishes it from other orchid species.

Caladenia stellata is a perennial herb with a short, underground rhizome and is often found in coastal regions of southern Australia.
