Cleisostoma fordii

Cleisostoma fordii, commonly known as the Ford's cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration. The plant typically blooms in the summer months, and its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with purple markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cleisostoma fordii is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
