Cyclopia subternata var. laxiflora

Cyclopia subternata var. laxiflora, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has small, narrow leaves and produces flowers in clusters.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh coastal environments.
