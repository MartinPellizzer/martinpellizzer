Crinum ornatum var. careyanum

Crinum ornatum var. careyanum, commonly known as the Careyanum Crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that form a dense rosette around the base. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
