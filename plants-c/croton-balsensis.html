Croton balsensis

Croton balsensis, commonly known as the balsam croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique foliage, it is a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
