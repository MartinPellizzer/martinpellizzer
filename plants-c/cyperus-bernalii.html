Cyperus bernalii

Cyperus bernalii, commonly known as the Bernal's flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and riparian zones. Its slender, grass-like stems are often clustered, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Cyperus bernalii is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for various wildlife species.
