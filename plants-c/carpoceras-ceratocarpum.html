Carpoceras ceratocarpum

Carpoceras ceratocarpum, commonly known as the horned burr, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, and often prickly fruiting bodies that resemble horns or burs.
