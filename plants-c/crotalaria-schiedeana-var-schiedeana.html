Crotalaria schiedeana var. schiedeana

Crotalaria schiedeana var. schiedeana, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a distinctive rattle-like structure at the base. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces pods that contain several seeds.

The species is also noted for its role in ecological systems, as it can fix nitrogen in the soil, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
