Clematis cadmia var. leptomera

Clematis cadmia var. leptomera, commonly known as the slender clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern North America.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with five petals. The plant produces small, round, greenish-yellow fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy cultivation in gardens.
