Cattleya forbesii f. flammea

Cattleya forbesii f. flammea, commonly known as the Flame Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, pendulous blooms with a striking combination of orange and red hues, often accented with yellow and purple. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. It has thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, aiding its survival in dry conditions.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ease of cultivation and dramatic floral display.
