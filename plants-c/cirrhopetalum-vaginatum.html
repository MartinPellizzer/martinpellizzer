Cirrhopetalum vaginatum

Cirrhopetalum vaginatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure makes it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
