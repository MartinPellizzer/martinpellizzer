Croton lagunensis

Croton lagunensis, commonly known as the Hawaiian croton, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton lagunensis is adapted to the tropical climate and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
