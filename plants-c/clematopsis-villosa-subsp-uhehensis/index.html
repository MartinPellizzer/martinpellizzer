Clematopsis Villosa Subsp. Uhehensis

Clematopsis villosa subsp. uhehensis is a lesser-known member of the grape family native to the highlands of eastern Africa. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing in shaded forest understories. Its distinctive foliage and unique floral structure make it an intriguing subject for botanical study. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Understanding this subspecies can offer insights into the biodiversity of its native habitats.
