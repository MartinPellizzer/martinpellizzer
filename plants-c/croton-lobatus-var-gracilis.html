Croton lobatus var. gracilis

Croton lobatus var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, often variegated foliage.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with prominent yellow or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in garden settings.
