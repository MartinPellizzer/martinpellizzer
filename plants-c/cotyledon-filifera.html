Cotyledon filifera

Cotyledon filifera, commonly known as the thread-leaf cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon filifera is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
