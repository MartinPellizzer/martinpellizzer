Crudia tenuipes

Crudia tenuipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed Crudia, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the daisy family.

It is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
