Cryptosepalum katangense

Cryptosepalum katangense, commonly known as the Katanga cryptosepalum, is a rare and unique member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo, where it thrives in humid, tropical forest environments. The plant is characterized by its distinctive floral structure, featuring a short, narrow sepal that is fused with the petals, giving it a compact appearance. Its flowers are typically small, with intricate patterns and vibrant colors that attract specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
