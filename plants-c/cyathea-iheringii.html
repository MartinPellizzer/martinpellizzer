Cyathea iheringii

Cyathea iheringii, commonly known as the Ihering's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Cyathea iheringii plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing shelter and food for various organisms.
