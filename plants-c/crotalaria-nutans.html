Crotalaria nutans

Crotalaria nutans, commonly known as the drooping cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and distinctive yellow flowers that hang downward. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Crotalaria nutans is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soils.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop in some areas.
