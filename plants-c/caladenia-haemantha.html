Caladenia haemantha

Caladenia haemantha, commonly known as the red-tongued spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant features a single, slender stem with a single, erect flower that has a distinctive red lip and white petals. Caladenia haemantha typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.

It is a perennial herb that blooms during the spring and summer seasons.
