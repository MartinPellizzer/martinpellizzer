Cervantesia colombiana

Cervantesia colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian Cervantesia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of Colombia.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae, which includes cacti and related succulent plants. The plant is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of small, colorful flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in high-altitude environments with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
