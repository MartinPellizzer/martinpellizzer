Centaurea satarataensis

Centaurea satarataensis, commonly known as the Satarata thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and cylindrical stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments. Its blue to purple flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
