Carduus Pycnocephalus Subsp. Tenuiflorus

Carduus pycnocephalus subsp. tenuiflorus is a lesser-known member of the thistle family known for its distinctive appearance and hardy nature. This subspecies thrives in dry, rocky environments and is commonly found in mountainous regions across Europe and parts of Asia. Its narrow, delicate flowers stand out against its spiny foliage, making it both a unique and resilient plant. Despite its tough exterior, it plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and pollinators. Understanding this subspecies helps highlight the biodiversity within the Carduus genus.
