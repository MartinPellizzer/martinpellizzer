Cynoglossum integerrimum

Cynoglossum integerrimum, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum integerrimum is often found in meadows, roadsides, and disturbed areas.
