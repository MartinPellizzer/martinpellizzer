Citrus × bigaradia var. itan

Citrus × bigaradia var. itan, commonly known as the Itan lime, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from Brazil.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, aromatic rind and a juicy, acidic pulp. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It thrives in warm, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications and for its essential oils due to its strong aroma and flavor.
