Croton muriculatus

Croton muriculatus, commonly known as the spiny croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves that are often covered in fine, raised dots. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking exotic foliage.
