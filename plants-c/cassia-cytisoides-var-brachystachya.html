Cassia cytisoides var. brachystachya

Cassia cytisoides var. brachystachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its seeds are enclosed in slender pods and are often dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
