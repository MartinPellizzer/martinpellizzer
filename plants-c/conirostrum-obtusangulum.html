Conirostrum obtusangulum

Conirostrum obtusangulum, commonly known as the broad-angled conirostrum, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

Its leaves are broad and angled, giving the plant a striking visual appearance that sets it apart from other members of the Conirostrum genus. The plant exhibits a low-growing habit, often forming dense mats on rocky or arid soils. It is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Botanically, it is notable for its specialized floral structures that facilitate pollination by specific insect species.
