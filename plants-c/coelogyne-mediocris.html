Coelogyne mediocris

Coelogyne mediocris, commonly known as the common coelogyne, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect pseudobulbs and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, typically during the rainy season. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne mediocris is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
