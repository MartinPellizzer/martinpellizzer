Cneorum trimerum

Cneorum trimerum, commonly known as sweet cneorum, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Cneorum trimerum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and borders.
