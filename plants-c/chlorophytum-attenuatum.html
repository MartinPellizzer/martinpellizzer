Chlorophytum attenuatum

Chlorophytum attenuatum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often curl at the ends, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by green, rope-like structures that bear numerous tiny white flowers. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in a variety of light conditions.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it easy to grow and share.
