Chytroma tessmannii

Chytroma tessmannii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely branched stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments through deep root systems and reduced leaf surfaces. Chytroma tessmannii plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing food for local wildlife.
