Cleisostoma bicrure

Cleisostoma bicrure, commonly known as the two-lobed hooded orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, hood-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically greenish-yellow with a contrasting lip. Cleisostoma bicrure thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for enthusiasts of rare and exotic orchids.
