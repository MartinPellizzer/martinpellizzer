Croton moluccanus

Croton moluccanus, commonly known as the Moluccan croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton moluccanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
