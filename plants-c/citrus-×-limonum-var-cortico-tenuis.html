Citrus × limonum var. cortico-tenuis

Citrus × limonum var. cortico-tenuis, commonly known as the thin-skinned lemon.

This variety is characterized by its delicate, thin rind that is less fibrous compared to other citrus fruits. The fruit typically has a high juice content and a mild, sweet aroma. It is often used in culinary applications and for its essential oils.

The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
