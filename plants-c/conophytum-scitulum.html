Conophytum scitulum

Conophytum scitulum, commonly known as the "Lizard's Tail" or "Cape Flax," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Conophytum scitulum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental appeal.
