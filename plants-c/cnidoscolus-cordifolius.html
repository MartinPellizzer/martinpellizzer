Cnidoscolus cordifolius

Cnidoscolus cordifolius, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically deep green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spiny, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid tropical environments.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular ornamental plant in warmer climates.
