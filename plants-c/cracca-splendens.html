Cracca splendens

Cracca splendens, commonly known as the splendour cracca, is a striking member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Cracca splendens thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
