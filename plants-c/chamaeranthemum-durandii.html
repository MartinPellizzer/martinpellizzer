Chamaeranthemum durandii

Chamaeranthemum durandii, commonly known as the Durand's rock rose, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

Chamaeranthemum durandii is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
