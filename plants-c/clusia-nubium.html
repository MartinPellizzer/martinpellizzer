Clusia nubium

Clusia nubium, commonly known as the cloud cluster or cloud glory, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow patterns. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Clusia nubium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.
