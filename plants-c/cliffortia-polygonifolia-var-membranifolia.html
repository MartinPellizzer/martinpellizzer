Cliffortia polygonifolia var. membranifolia

Cliffortia polygonifolia var. membranifolia, commonly known as the Cape rose or mountain rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, pink or red flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
