Coreopsis mollicula

Coreopsis mollicula, commonly known as the softhead coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving the plant a distinctive, delicate texture.

Coreopsis mollicula is well-suited for garden use due to its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
