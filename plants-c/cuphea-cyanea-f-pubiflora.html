Cuphea cyanea f. pubiflora

Cuphea cyanea f. pubiflora, commonly known as the fuzzy cuphea, is a striking ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to purple, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for borders, containers, and garden beds.
