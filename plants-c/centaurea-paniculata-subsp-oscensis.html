Centaurea paniculata subsp. oscensis

Centaurea paniculata subsp.

oscensis, commonly known as the Alboran Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula and northwestern Africa. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
