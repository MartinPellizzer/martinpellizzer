Chamaemelum glabrum

Chamaemelum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowers are yellow and appear in clusters, and the plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits.

Chamaemelum glabrum is often found in dry, sunny areas and is valued for its aromatic properties.
