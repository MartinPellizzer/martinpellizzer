Chrysanthemum Fisherae

Chrysanthemum fisherae is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its striking yellow and white blooms that appear in late summer. This species is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and medicinal properties. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, it is now critically endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and restore its natural habitat.
