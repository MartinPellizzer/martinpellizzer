Cerbera nitida

Cerbera nitida, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

The plant is also notable for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
