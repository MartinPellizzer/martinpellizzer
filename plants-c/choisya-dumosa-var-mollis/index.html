Choisya Dumosa Var. Mollis

Choisya mollis, scientifically known as Choisya dumosa var. mollis, is a versatile and attractive evergreen shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. This plant is prized for its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the spring and summer. Its dense, glossy foliage provides year-round visual interest, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Choisya mollis thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, adapting well to a variety of growing conditions. Whether used as a foundation plant, hedge, or specimen, it offers both ornamental value and ecological benefits.
