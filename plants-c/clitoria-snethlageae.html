Clitoria snethlageae

Clitoria snethlageae, commonly known as the "Blue Pea" or "Coral Bean," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple pea-like flowers, which are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants. Its climbing habit and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
