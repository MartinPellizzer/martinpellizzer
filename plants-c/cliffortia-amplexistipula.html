Cliffortia amplexistipula

Cliffortia amplexistipula, commonly known as the Cape snowball, is a South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky, coastal areas and fynbos vegetation. The plant features delicate, white, fluffy flowers that resemble small balls, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is adapted to withstand coastal conditions, including salt spray and strong winds.
