Corydalis tongolensis

Corydalis tongolensis, commonly known as the Tongol fumariol, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Fumariaceae and is characterized by its slender, herbaceous stems and clusters of small, delicate flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spurred flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Corydalis tongolensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
