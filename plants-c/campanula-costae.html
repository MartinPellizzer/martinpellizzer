Campanula costae

Campanula costae, commonly known as the Costate Bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to purple. The leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive wavy margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and ease of care.
