Cassia brongniartii

Cassia brongniartii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its long, drooping panicles of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. The plant has pinnately compound leaves, with each leaflet being oval-shaped and slightly serrated. Cassia brongniartii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
