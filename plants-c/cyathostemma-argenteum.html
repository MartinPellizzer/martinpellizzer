Cyathostemma argenteum

Cyathostemma argenteum, commonly known as the silverleaf star.

It is a succulent plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it store water, making it well adapted to arid environments. It produces small, round fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Cyathostemma argenteum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
