Cryophytum galpinii

Cryophytum galpinii, commonly known as the Galpin's cryophyte, is a rare and unique plant species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent leaves and ability to survive in extreme environmental conditions. This plant is adapted to withstand freezing temperatures, which is why it is classified as a cryophyte. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and specialized structures that help in water retention.
