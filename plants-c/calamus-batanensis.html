Calamus batanensis

Calamus batanensis, commonly known as the Bataan calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, particularly found in the Bataan region. The plant is a tall, slender palm with long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fibrous fruits.

Calamus batanensis is valued for its ornamental appearance and has cultural significance in local communities.
