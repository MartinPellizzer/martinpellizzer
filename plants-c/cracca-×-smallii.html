Cracca × smallii

Cracca × smallii, commonly known as the hybrid yellow gentian, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Gentianaceae and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are typically tubular with a prominent corolla. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Cracca × smallii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or alpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
