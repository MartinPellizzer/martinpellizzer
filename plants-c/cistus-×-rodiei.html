Cistus × rodiei

Cistus × rodiei, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Cistus × rodiei is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in perfumery and traditional medicine.
