Clitoria sagotii var. canaliculata

Clitoria sagotii var. canaliculata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped blue or purple flowers. The plant has compound leaves with stipules and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
