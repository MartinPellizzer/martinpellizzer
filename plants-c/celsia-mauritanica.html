Celsia mauritanica

Celsia mauritanica, commonly known as the Mauritania celsia, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of North Africa.

Celsia mauritanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in regions with similar climatic conditions.
