Crataegus monostyla

Crataegus monostyla, commonly known as the singlestyle hawthorn, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its small, white flowers and red berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

The fruit, which is a pome, is edible but often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
