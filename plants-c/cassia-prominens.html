Cassia prominens

Cassia prominens, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and long, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and its leaves are compound, with numerous small leaflets. Cassia prominens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy blooms and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

It also has historical uses in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dyes.
