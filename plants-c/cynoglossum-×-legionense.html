Cynoglossum × legionense

Cynoglossum × legionense, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often a deep purple or blue-gray in color. It produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue, which bloom in late summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and unique floral display.
