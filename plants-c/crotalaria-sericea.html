Crotalaria sericea

Crotalaria sericea, commonly known as silky cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is a legume, belonging to the family Fabaceae, and is characterized by its finely hairy stems and leaves. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that are often clustered in racemes, and it bears pods containing kidney-shaped seeds. Crotalaria sericea is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant in agricultural systems.
