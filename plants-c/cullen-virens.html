Cullen virens

Cullen virens, commonly known as the green cullen, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright green foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is often found in coastal areas and can thrive in well-drained environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
