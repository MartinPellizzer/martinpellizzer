Cryptophragmium leptostachyum

Cryptophragmium leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stalked cryptophragmium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, leafy stems and small, greenish-white flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments within forest understories. The plant's unique botanical features include its clustered inflorescence and specialized root system adapted for nutrient absorption.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
