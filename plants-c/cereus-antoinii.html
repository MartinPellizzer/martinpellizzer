Cereus antoinii

Cereus antoinii, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a flowering cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and clusters of white, star-shaped flowers that bloom during the holiday season. The plant has a succulent structure, allowing it to store water in its thick, fleshy stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a striking addition to any collection.
