Cyrtandra × umbraculiflora

Cyrtandra × umbraculiflora, commonly known as the shaded-leaf cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that often have a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
