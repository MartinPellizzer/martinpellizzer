Cassia capensis var. keiensis

Cassia capensis var. keiensis, commonly known as the Keiensis flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow, fluffy inflorescences that appear in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is often planted in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
