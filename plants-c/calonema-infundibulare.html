Calonema infundibulare

Calonema infundibulare, commonly known as the shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The plant features a short, creeping rhizome with dense, overlapping leaves that form a rosette-like structure. Its fronds are typically lance-shaped and have a leathery texture.
