Callisia hexandra var. mandonii

Callisia hexandra var. mandonii, commonly known as the Mandon's spider plant, is a varietal form of the spider plant native to Mexico.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its trailing growth habit and long, thin leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiky seed capsules. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive foliage.
