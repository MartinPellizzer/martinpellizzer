Centaurea heleniifolia

Centaurea heleniifolia, commonly known as the Greek star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, erect stems and bright blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Centaurea heleniifolia is known for its ornamental value and has been introduced to other parts of the world as a garden plant.
