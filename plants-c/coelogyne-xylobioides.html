Coelogyne xylobioides

Coelogyne xylobioides, commonly known as the False Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

Coelogyne xylobioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy blooms.
