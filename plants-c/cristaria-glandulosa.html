Cristaria glandulosa

Cristaria glandulosa, commonly known as the sticky bladderwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, bladder-like structures that trap small aquatic organisms, aiding in its nutrient acquisition. The plant is known for its unique adaptations that allow it to thrive in nutrient-poor environments.
