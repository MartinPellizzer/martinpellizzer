Camellia cavaleriana

Camellia cavaleriana, commonly known as the Cavaleriana camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pink and white, often with a distinctive yellow or green throat. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Camellia cavaleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
