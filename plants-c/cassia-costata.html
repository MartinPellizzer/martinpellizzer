Cassia costata

Cassia costata, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly serrated edge. Cassia costata is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice in tropical and subtropical gardens.
