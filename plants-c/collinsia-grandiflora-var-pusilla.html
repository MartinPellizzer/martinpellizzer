Collinsia grandiflora var. pusilla

Collinsia grandiflora var. pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf fairy bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to California.

It features small, delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant colors and unique, bell-shaped blooms.
