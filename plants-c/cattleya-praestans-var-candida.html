Cattleya praestans var. candida

Cattleya praestans var. candida, commonly known as the White Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid celebrated for its striking white flowers and elegant appearance.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, showy blooms with a delicate, waxy texture. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.

This variety is often used in hybridization to create new orchid cultivars with unique color variations.
