Commelinidium mayumbense

Commelinidium mayumbense, commonly known as the Mayumbense grass, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Cameroon and Nigeria. This plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It features slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in spikelets, typical of the grass family.
