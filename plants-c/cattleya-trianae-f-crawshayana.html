Cattleya trianae f. crawshayana

Cattleya trianae f. crawshayana, commonly known as the Crawshay's Cattleya, is a rare orchid species native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it visually striking. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled greenhouse settings.
