Chamaedorea polyclada

Chamaedorea polyclada, commonly known as the Variegated Bamboo Palm, is a member of the Arecaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a slow-growing, evergreen plant that typically reaches a height of 3 to 5 feet. The plant features slender, arching fronds with a distinctive variegated pattern of light and dark green. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Chamaedorea polyclada is popular as a houseplant due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying qualities.
