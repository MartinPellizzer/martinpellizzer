Cotula maderaspatana

Cotula maderaspatana, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cotula maderaspatana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
