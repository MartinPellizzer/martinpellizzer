Cybelion tenerum

Cybelion tenerum, commonly known as the slender cybelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its reproductive strategy involves wind pollination, which is common among plants in its ecological niche.

Cybelion tenerum is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, where it contributes to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
