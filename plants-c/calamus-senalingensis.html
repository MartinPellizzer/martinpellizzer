Calamus senalingensis

Calamus senalingensis, commonly known as the Sunda calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Calamus senalingensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
