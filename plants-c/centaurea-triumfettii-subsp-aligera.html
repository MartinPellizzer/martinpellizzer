Centaurea triumfettii subsp. aligera

Centaurea triumfettii subsp.

aligera, commonly known as the alpine centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer.
