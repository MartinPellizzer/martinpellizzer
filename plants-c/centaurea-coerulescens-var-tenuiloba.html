Centaurea coerulescens var. tenuiloba

Centaurea coerulescens var. tenuiloba, commonly known as the slender blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It has narrow, linear leaves and is known for its compact growth habit.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
