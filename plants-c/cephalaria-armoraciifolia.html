Cephalaria armoraciifolia

Cephalaria armoraciifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cephalaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, often with a reddish hue. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive, finely toothed margin.

Cephalaria armoraciifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
