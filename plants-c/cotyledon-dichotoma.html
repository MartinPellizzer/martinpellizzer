Cotyledon dichotoma

Cotyledon dichotoma, commonly known as the "Double Leaf" or "Coral Cotyledon," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple edges, and it produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is characterized by its unique branching pattern, with each stem splitting into two at the base, giving it a dichotomous appearance.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
