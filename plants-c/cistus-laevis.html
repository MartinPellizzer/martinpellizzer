Cistus laevis

Cistus laevis, commonly known as the smooth rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, grey-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rocky or coastal environments.

Cistus laevis is also known for its ability to produce a resinous sap, which has been used historically in various applications.
