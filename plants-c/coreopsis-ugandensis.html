Coreopsis ugandensis

Coreopsis ugandensis, commonly known as the Uganda coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. Coreopsis ugandensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
