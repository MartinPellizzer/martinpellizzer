Chrysophyllum manaosense

Chrysophyllum manaosense, commonly known as the Amazonian star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces small, green fruits that mature to a yellowish color, containing a single large seed surrounded by a sweet, white pulp. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and has potential economic importance due to its edible fruit.

However, it remains relatively understudied compared to other tropical species.
