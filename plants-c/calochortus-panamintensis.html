Calochortus panamintensis

Calochortus panamintensis, commonly known as the Panamint mariposa lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the deserts of California and Nevada.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces a single, upright stem with a cluster of petals at the top.

Calochortus panamintensis is often found in areas with limited water availability, showcasing its resilience to harsh desert conditions.
