Cirsium Tuberosum Subsp. Filipendulum

Cirsium tuberosum subsp. filipendulum is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. It is known for its tall, spiny stems and clusters of purple-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. This subspecies is often found in rocky habitats and is adapted to harsh, alpine conditions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular subject for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader plant world.
