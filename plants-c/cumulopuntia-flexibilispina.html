Cumulopuntia flexibilispina

Cumulopuntia flexibilispina, commonly known as the flexible spiny cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, spiny areoles, which provide protection from herbivores. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green, spiky structure. Its adaptations include succulent stems that store water and reduce transpiration, making it well-suited for harsh desert environments.

Overall, Cumulopuntia flexibilispina exemplifies the resilience and specialized features of desert flora.
