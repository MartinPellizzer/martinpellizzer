Cotoneaster integerrimus var. nummularius

Cotoneaster integerrimus var. nummularius, commonly known as the silver cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green on top and silvery white underneath. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in the spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is also valued for its use in wildlife habitats and as a hedge plant.
