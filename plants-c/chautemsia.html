Chautemsia

Chautemsia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. Chautemsia is well adapted to drought conditions, making it a resilient species in semi-desert ecosystems.
