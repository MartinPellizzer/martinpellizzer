Cavendishia quereme

Cavendishia quereme, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural collections.
