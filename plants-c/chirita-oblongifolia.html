Chirita oblongifolia

Chirita oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leafed gesneriad, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant features heart-shaped or oblong leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
