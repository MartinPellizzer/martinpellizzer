Calycotome

Calycotome, commonly known as the Spanish broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are opposite in arrangement. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to thrive in poor, dry soils.

Calycotome is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil on slopes.
