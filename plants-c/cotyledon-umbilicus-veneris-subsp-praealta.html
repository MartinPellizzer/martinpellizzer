Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris subsp. praealta

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris subsp.

praealta, commonly known as the "lady's mantle," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
