Chusua kunikikoana

Chusua kunikikoana, commonly known as the African fireweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and vibrant red or orange flowers. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and along roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Chusua kunikikoana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
