Cyperus dubius var. polyactis

Cyperus dubius var. polyactis, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and pond edges. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are often clustered at the tips of the stems.

This variety is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, disturbed soils.
