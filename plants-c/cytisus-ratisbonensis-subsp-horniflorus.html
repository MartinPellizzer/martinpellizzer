Cytisus ratisbonensis subsp. horniflorus

Cytisus ratisbonensis subsp.

horniflorus, commonly known as the horned flame flower, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its bright yellow, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense racemes.

The plant has slender, woody stems with thorn-like projections, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are small, pinnate, and often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
