Clerodendrum javanicum

Clerodendrum javanicum, commonly known as the Java glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often appearing in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a strong, somewhat aromatic scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum javanicum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
