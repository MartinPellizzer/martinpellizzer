Cnestis agelaeoides

Cnestis agelaeoides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Cnestis agelaeoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
