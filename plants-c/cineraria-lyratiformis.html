Cineraria lyratiformis

Cineraria lyratiformis, commonly known as the silver cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its silvery-blue foliage and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is a hardy perennial that is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
