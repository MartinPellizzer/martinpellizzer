Cattleya acklandiae

Cattleya acklandiae, commonly known as the Ackland's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

Cattleya acklandiae thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
