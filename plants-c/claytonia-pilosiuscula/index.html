Claytonia Pilosiuscula

Claytonia pilosiuscula, commonly known as the hairy purple sandwort, is a small yet striking plant native to the western united states. it thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in alpine and coastal regions. its delicate flowers, which bloom in shades of purple and white, add a touch of elegance to its rugged habitat. the plant's hairy stems and leaves help it retain moisture in harsh environments. despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems and is a favorite among wildflower enthusiasts.
