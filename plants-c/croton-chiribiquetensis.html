Croton chiribiquetensis

Croton chiribiquetensis, commonly known as the Chiribiquet croton, is a shrub native to the Chiribiquet region of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, typically in well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
