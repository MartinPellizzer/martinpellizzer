Callitriche hedbergiorum

Callitriche hedbergiorum, commonly known as the Hedberg's water star, is a small aquatic plant native to New Zealand.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has tiny, star-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the water's surface. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in early spring.
