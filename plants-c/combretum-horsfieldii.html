Combretum horsfieldii

Combretum horsfieldii, commonly known as the red combretum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and clusters of small, red or orange flowers. It produces woody capsules as fruit, which contain several seeds.

Combretum horsfieldii is often found in savannas and woodland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.
