Crassocephalum diversifolium

Crassocephalum diversifolium, commonly known as the African sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant is an annual herb that thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional medicine and ornamental gardening.
