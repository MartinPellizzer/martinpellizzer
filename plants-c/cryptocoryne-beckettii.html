Cryptocoryne beckettii

Cryptocoryne beckettii, commonly known as the Beckett's cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves that are typically lance-shaped and often have a slight waxy coating. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded, nutrient-rich substrates. Its rhizomes form dense clusters, making it an effective plant for stabilizing substrates in aquariums and ponds.

Cryptocoryne beckettii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in aquatic setups.
