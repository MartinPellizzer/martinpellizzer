Calytrix baueri

Calytrix baueri, commonly known as the red-flowered everlasting, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, showy pink to red flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Calytrix baueri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and floral arrangements.
