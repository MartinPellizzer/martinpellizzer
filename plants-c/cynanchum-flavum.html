Cynanchum flavum

Cynanchum flavum, commonly known as yellow milkvetch, is a perennial flowering plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant produces small, legume-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of native plant communities and a subject of ecological study.
