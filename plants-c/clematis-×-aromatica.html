Clematis × aromatica

Clematis × aromatica, commonly known as the sweet autumn clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink to purple. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Clematis × aromatica is known for its vigorous growth and ability to climb trellises or other structures.
