Cotyledon eurychlamys

Cotyledon eurychlamys, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, often blooming in spring. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.

Cotyledon eurychlamys thrives in well-draining soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
