Calluna vulgaris f. grandiflora

Calluna vulgaris f. grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora heather, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the British Isles.

It features dense, dark green foliage and produces large, showy pink or white flowers in late summer. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade. It is widely cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Due to its cold hardiness, it is suitable for coastal and maritime climates.
