Campanula scheuchzeri subsp. valdensis

Campanula scheuchzeri subsp.

valdensis, commonly known as the Valdensian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe. It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This subspecies is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
