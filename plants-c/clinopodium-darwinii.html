Clinopodium darwinii

Clinopodium darwinii, commonly known as Darwin's hedge nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, and it produces small, hairy fruits.

Clinopodium darwinii is often found in temperate regions of the Americas.
