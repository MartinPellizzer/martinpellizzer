Camellia chrysantha f. longistyla

Camellia chrysantha f. longistyla, commonly known as the longistyla camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a striking addition to gardens. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, preferring a humid, temperate climate.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in horticulture and botanical collections.
