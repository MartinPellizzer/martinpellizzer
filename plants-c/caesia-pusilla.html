Caesia pusilla

Caesia pusilla, commonly known as the small caesia, is a delicate herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.
