Chamaesyce montana

Chamaesyce montana, commonly known as mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of foliage. Its flowers are tiny, yellowish-green, and appear in clusters, blooming in late spring to early summer.
