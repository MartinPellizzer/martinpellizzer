Croton matourensis var. genuinus

Croton matourensis var. genuinus, commonly known as the Matourensis Croton, is a tropical plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically large, with a broad ovate shape and often display vibrant shades of red, yellow, and green.
