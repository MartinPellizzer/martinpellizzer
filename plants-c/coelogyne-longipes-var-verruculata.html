Coelogyne longipes var. verruculata

Coelogyne longipes var. verruculata, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and hairy, velvety stems, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive yellowish-green color and red-brown markings. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
