Ceropegia frerei

Ceropegia frerei, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, thin stems that produce heart-shaped leaves, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, attracting specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
