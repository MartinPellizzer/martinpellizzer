Cheilanthes afra

Cheilanthes afra, commonly known as the African rock fern, is a species of fern native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen fern that typically grows on rocky outcrops and in dry, shaded environments. The plant has a creeping rhizome with dense clusters of small, lance-shaped leaves. Its fronds are usually dark green and remain compact throughout the year.

Cheilanthes afra is well adapted to arid conditions and is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature.
