Clethra michoacana

Clethra michoacana, commonly known as Mexican sweet pepper, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant typically features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are white and clustered in umbels, producing a subtle fragrance.

Clethra michoacana is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
