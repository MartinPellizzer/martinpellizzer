Cristaria pinnatifida

Cristaria pinnatifida, commonly known as the featherleaf clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Cristaria pinnatifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological systems.
