Clematis pseudootophora var. integra

Clematis pseudootophora var. integra, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, recurved form. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow berries that mature to a dark purple or black color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

Its botanical characteristics make it both visually appealing and ecologically valuable in native landscapes.
