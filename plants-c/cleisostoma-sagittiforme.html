Cleisostoma sagittiforme

Cleisostoma sagittiforme, commonly known as the arrow-shaped cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique arrow-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers are often pale yellow or white, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Cleisostoma sagittiforme is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
