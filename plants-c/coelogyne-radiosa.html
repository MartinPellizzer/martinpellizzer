Coelogyne radiosa

Coelogyne radiosa, commonly known as the radiant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a range of colors, including white, pink, and red. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne radiosa is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
