Calesiam rubra

Calesiam rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered caleia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals that contrast beautifully with its dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. Its flowers are borne on upright stems and bloom during the warmer months.

Calesiam rubra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
