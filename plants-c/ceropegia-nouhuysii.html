Ceropegia nouhuysii

Ceropegia nouhuysii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
