Cymbopogon versicolor

Cymbopogon versicolor, commonly known as the variable grass or rainbow grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and its ability to produce vibrant, colorful inflorescences. Cymbopogon versicolor thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and horticultural displays.
