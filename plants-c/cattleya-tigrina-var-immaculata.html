Cattleya tigrina var. immaculata

Cattleya tigrina var. immaculata, commonly known as the immaculate tiger orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, intricate floral patterns.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features large, showy flowers with a distinctive tiger-striped pattern on its petals and sepals. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy cultivation requirements.
