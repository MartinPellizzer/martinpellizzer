Citharexylum obtusifolium

Citharexylum obtusifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf citharexylum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, often with a slight reddish tint, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a valued addition to both indoor and outdoor landscapes.
