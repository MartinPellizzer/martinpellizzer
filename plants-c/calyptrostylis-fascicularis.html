Calyptrostylis fascicularis

Calyptrostylis fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered spike orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, clustered growth habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers with prominent red or purple markings. The plant has a short, stocky rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforest or heathland areas.

Its unique floral structure and specific habitat requirements make it a distinctive species within the Orchidaceae family.
