Calathea picturata

Calathea picturata, commonly known as the Painted Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a combination of green, white, and pink hues. The leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with intricate patterns that change subtly depending on light conditions. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
