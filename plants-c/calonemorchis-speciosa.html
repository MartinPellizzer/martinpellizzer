Calonemorchis speciosa

Calonemorchis speciosa, commonly known as the showy calonemorchis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers that come in shades of pink and white. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
