Calliandra iselyi

Calliandra iselyi, commonly known as the yellow calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters, which bloom throughout the growing season. Calliandra iselyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
