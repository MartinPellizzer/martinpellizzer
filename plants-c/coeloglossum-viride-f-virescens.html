Coeloglossum viride f. virescens

Coeloglossum viride f. virescens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often tinged with yellow, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its distinctive coloration and its role in local ecosystems.
