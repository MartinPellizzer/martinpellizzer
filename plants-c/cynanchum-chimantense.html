Cynanchum chimantense

Cynanchum chimantense, commonly known as Chimantense Milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Ecuador and Peru.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is characterized by its milky sap and showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive structure that attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Cynanchum chimantense is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
