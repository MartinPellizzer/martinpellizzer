Conophytum sitzlerianum

Conophytum sitzlerianum, commonly known as the Sitzler's conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a fleshy, triangular stem that often remains close to the ground, with small, rounded leaves that store water. The plant produces clusters of yellow, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
