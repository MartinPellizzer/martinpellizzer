Chimonobambusa jaunsarensis

Chimonobambusa jaunsarensis, commonly known as the Jaunsar bamboo, is a species of grass bamboo native to the Himalayan regions of India.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated culms that are typically green to yellow-green in color. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are long and narrow, with a smooth texture and a slightly pointed tip.

Chimonobambusa jaunsarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional construction and crafts in its native region.
