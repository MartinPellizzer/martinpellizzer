Chamaebuxus Alpestris Var. Grandiflora

Alpine buxus, scientifically known as Chamaebuxus alpestris var. grandiflora, is a hardy evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It thrives in cool, moist climates and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky outcrops. This plant is prized for its compact growth habit and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions. Its dense foliage provides excellent ground cover and supports local wildlife. With its ornamental value and resilience, Alpine buxus is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add a touch of nature to their landscapes.
