Croton broomfieldii

Croton broomfieldii, commonly known as the Broomfield's croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from deep green to vibrant red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its unique leaf patterns.
