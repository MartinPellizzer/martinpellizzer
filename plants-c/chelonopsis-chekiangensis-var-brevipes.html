Chelonopsis chekiangensis var. brevipes

Chelonopsis chekiangensis var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers are pale blue to violet in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
