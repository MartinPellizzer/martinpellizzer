Caladenia filamentosa

Caladenia filamentosa, commonly known as the filamentous spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in open, sunny habitats. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by insects.

Caladenia filamentosa is a rare and protected species, often found in specific regions of New South Wales and Victoria.
