Conyza vatkeana

Conyza vatkeana, commonly known as the yellow starthistle, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has a bushy growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are typically hairy and have a serrated edge.

Conyza vatkeana is considered an invasive species in many regions outside its native range.
