Ceratostylis minutiflora

Ceratostylis minutiflora, commonly known as the tiny-flowered horned-stylis, is a small orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that are typically less than 1 cm in diameter. The plant grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories or along stream banks. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for anchoring in damp soil.

The species is noted for its intricate floral structure and is a subject of interest in orchid taxonomy.
