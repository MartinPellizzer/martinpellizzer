Camellia handelii

Camellia handelii, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Camellia handelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often grown in temperate climates.
