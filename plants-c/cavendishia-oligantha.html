Cavendishia oligantha

Cavendishia oligantha, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a delicate, fragrant scent.

Cavendishia oligantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
