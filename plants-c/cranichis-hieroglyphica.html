Cranichis hieroglyphica

Cranichis hieroglyphica, commonly known as the Egyptian orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Nile Valley.

It features intricate, showy flowers with a unique, hieroglyph-like pattern on its petals, giving it its common name. The plant has a delicate, grass-like foliage and produces small, clustered blooms that are often pale green or white. It thrives in moist, sandy soils and is typically found in shaded, humid environments.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
