Calamagrostis mulleri

Calamagrostis mulleri, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist to wet soil conditions and is often found in marshy areas or along stream banks.

Calamagrostis mulleri is valued in horticulture for its graceful form and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
