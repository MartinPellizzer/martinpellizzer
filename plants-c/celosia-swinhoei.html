Celosia swinhoei

Celosia swinhoei, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a striking annual plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens and floral arrangements. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Celosia swinhoei thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.
