Crotalaria obscura var. chrysotricha

Crotalaria obscura var. chrysotricha, commonly known as the golden crown bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive crown-like structure, which gives it its common name. Its foliage is typically green with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils.

This species is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and unique appearance.
