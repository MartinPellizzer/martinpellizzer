Caladium eckhartii

Caladium eckhartii, commonly known as the Easter Caladium, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant patterns of red, white, and green. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Caladium eckhartii prefers well-draining soil and partial to full shade, making it a versatile choice for shaded garden spaces.
