Calamagrostis alpina var. aristata

Calamagrostis alpina var. aristata, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and feathery plumes that add ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and prefers well-drained soils in full sun. Its foliage is often described as having a metallic sheen, which enhances its visual appeal in landscaping.

It is drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant in perennial beds.
