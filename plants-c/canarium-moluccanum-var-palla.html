Canarium moluccanum var. palla

Canarium moluccanum var. palla, commonly known as the Moluccan canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing aromatic seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its timber and resin.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
