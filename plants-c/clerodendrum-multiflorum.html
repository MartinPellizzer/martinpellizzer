Clerodendrum multiflorum

Clerodendrum multiflorum, commonly known as the multibract clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, often in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Clerodendrum multiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
