Cinchona scrobiculata

Cinchona scrobiculata, commonly known as the rough bark cinchona, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers and red berries, which are typical of the genus Cinchona. This species plays an important role in both ecological and historical contexts.
