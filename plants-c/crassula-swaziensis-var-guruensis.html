Crassula swaziensis var. guruensis

Crassula swaziensis var. guruensis, commonly known as the Guru Crassula, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green with a powdery white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its compact form.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
