Centaurea × splendida

Centaurea × splendida, commonly known as the hybrid gladiolus, is a perennial flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, sparsely leafy stems and striking, showy inflorescences that resemble a gladiolus. The flowers typically feature a central yellow disk surrounded by vibrant red or orange ray florets. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
