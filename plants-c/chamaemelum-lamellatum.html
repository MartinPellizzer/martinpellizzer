Chamaemelum lamellatum

Chamaemelum lamellatum, commonly known as the slender fennel, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Chamaemelum lamellatum is also valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
