Crepis taurinensis

Crepis taurinensis, commonly known as the Taurine hawk's-beard, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It produces small, wind-dispersed seeds that aid in its propagation.
