Clusia martiana

Clusia martiana, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered together. Clusia martiana is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

It is also valued for its air-purifying properties and low maintenance requirements.
