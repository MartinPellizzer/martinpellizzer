Coula edulis

Coula edulis, commonly known as African mango or butter fruit, is a tropical shrub native to West Africa.

It produces large, green, fleshy fruits that are rich in nutrients and have a creamy texture. The fruit's pulp is often consumed fresh or used in traditional foods. The plant is valued for its edible fruits and has been studied for potential health benefits.

Its botanical features include simple leaves and small, yellowish flowers that attract pollinators.
