Combretum pavonii

Combretum pavonii, commonly known as the red-flowered combretum, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Combretum pavonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
