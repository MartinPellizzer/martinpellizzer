Castanopsis Sessilifolia

Castanopsis sessilifolia is a unique evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its dense foliage and distinctive cone-like structures that resemble small pine cones. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and reforestation projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
