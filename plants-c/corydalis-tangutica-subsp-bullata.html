Corydalis tangutica subsp. bullata

Corydalis tangutica subsp.

bullata, commonly known as the Tibetan fritillary, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of Tibet and western China. It features slender, branched stems that grow up to 30 cm in height, adorned with lanceolate, hairy leaves.
