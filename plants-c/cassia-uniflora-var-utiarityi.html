Cassia uniflora var. utiarityi

Cassia uniflora var. utiarityi, commonly known as the slender cassia, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in the spring. Its seeds are encased in slender, elongated pods that split open when mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
