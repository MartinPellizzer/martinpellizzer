Cineraria caucasica

Cineraria caucasica, commonly known as Caucasian cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Cineraria caucasica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
