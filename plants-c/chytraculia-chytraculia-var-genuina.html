Chytraculia chytraculia var. genuina

Chytraculia chytraculia var. genuina, commonly known as the rare mountain orchid, is a unique species native to specific alpine regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, intricate flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often found in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its subtle appearance.

Due to its limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the importance of conservation efforts.
