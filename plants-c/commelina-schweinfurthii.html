Commelina schweinfurthii

Commelina schweinfurthii, commonly known as the swamp morning glory, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers with a distinctive white spot. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found near water bodies. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the early morning and closing by midday.

Commelina schweinfurthii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
