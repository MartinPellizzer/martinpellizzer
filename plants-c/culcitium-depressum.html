Culcitium depressum

Culcitium depressum, commonly known as the flat culcitium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and features small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Culcitium depressum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
