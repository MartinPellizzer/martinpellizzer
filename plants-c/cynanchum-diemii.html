Cynanchum diemii

Cynanchum diemii, commonly known as Chinese milkweed or Chinese dogbane, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its latex sap is milky and mildly toxic. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.

Cynanchum diemii has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
