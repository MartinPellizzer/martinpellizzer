Chamaecrista planaltoana

Chamaecrista planaltoana, commonly known as the Brazilian burr legume, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces leguminous pods that contain small, hard seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry or disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
