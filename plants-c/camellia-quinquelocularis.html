Camellia quinquelocularis

Camellia quinquelocularis, commonly known as the five-chambered camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red and bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and cold hardiness.
