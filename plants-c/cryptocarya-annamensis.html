Cryptocarya annamensis

Cryptocarya annamensis, commonly known as the Annam cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Laos, where it grows in tropical and subtropical regions. Cryptocarya annamensis is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
