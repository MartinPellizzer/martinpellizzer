Cymbopetalum alkekengi

Cymbopetalum alkekengi, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a striking member of the Solanaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its vibrant, papery orange to red calyces that resemble small lanterns. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or white, followed by the showy, inflated fruit. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique and colorful fruiting structures.
