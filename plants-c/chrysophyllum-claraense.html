Chrysophyllum claraense

Chrysophyllum claraense, commonly known as the Clara's star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit, which resembles a small apple, is edible and has a sweet, slightly tart flavor. It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
