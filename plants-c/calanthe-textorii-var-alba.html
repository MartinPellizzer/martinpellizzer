Calanthe textorii var. alba

Calanthe textorii var. alba, commonly known as the White Calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are borne on tall, slender stalks and are known for their delicate appearance.

Calanthe textorii var. alba is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
