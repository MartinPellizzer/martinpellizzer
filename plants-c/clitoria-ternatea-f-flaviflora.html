Clitoria ternatea f. flaviflora

Clitoria ternatea f. flaviflora, commonly known as the yellow butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant blue or yellow flowers. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and has a climbing habit, often used in ornamental gardening. Its flowers are notable for their ability to change color when exposed to acid, a trait that makes it popular in food and dye applications.

The plant also has medicinal uses in traditional medicine systems.
