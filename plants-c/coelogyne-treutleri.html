Coelogyne treutleri

Coelogyne treutleri, commonly known as the Treutler's orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It blooms once a year, typically in spring or early summer.

Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for both collectors and gardeners.
