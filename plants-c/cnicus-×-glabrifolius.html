Cnicus × glabrifolius

Cnicus × glabrifolius, commonly known as the smoothleaf sowthistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth or slightly hairy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, which are typical of the daisy family. It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits variability in its morphological features.
