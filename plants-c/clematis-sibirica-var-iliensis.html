Clematis sibirica var. iliensis

Clematis sibirica var. iliensis, commonly known as the Ili Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires support for its climbing growth habit.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
