Ceratostema smilacifolium

Ceratostema smilacifolium, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
