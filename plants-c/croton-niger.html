Croton niger

Croton niger, commonly known as black croton, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves and striking, colorful bracts that range from red to orange. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
