Cyrtochilum nigratum

Cyrtochilum nigratum, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Cyrtochilum nigratum has small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
