Cirsium mollissimum

Cirsium mollissimum, commonly known as the soft thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and covered in a fine layer of soft, white hairs.

The flowers are usually arranged in a single head, with ray florets that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
