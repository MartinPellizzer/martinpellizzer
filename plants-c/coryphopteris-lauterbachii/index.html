Coryphopteris Lauterbachii

Coryphopteris lauterbachii is a rare and striking fern native to the mountainous regions of Central America. With its distinctive, broad, and often lobed fronds, it stands out among other fern species. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests and misty highlands. Its unique appearance makes it a prized specimen for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly understood and is threatened by habitat loss and environmental changes.
