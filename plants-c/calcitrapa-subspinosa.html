Calcitrapa subspinosa

Calcitrapa subspinosa, commonly known as the spiny calcitrapa, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. Its scientific name reflects its calcareous habitat and spiny characteristics.
