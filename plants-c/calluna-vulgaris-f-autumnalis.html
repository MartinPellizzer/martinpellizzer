Calluna vulgaris f. autumnalis

Calluna vulgaris f. autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn heather, is a compact shrub native to the northern hemisphere.

It is a cultivar of the common heather, characterized by its vibrant autumn foliage that displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its small, bell-shaped flowers appear in late summer, adding a touch of color before the leaves change.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in garden settings with minimal maintenance.
