Cyperus lanceolatus var. subunioloides

Cyperus lanceolatus var. subunioloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae) and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in wet or seasonally flooded soils, often found in marshy areas or along waterways.

Its botanical structure makes it adaptable to a variety of wetland environments.
