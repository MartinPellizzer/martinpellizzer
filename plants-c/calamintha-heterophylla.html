Calamintha heterophylla

Calamintha heterophylla, commonly known as broad-leaved calamintha, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features opposite, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a strong aromatic scent. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers in terminal clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Calamintha heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
