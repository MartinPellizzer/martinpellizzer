Centradenia floribunda subsp. floribunda

Centradenia floribunda subsp.

floribunda, commonly known as the yellow-flowered centradenia, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
