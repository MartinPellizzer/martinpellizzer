Calanthe hoshii

Calanthe hoshii, commonly known as the Hoshii calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calanthe hoshii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
