Canna gigantea

Canna gigantea, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves and brightly colored flowers, which can range from red to orange to yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet, with broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Canna gigantea is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes due to its dramatic appearance.
