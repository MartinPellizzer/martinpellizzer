Citrus × limonum var. fructu-elongato

Citrus × limonum var. fructu-elongato, commonly known as the elongated fruit lemon.

This variant is characterized by its elongated, oblong-shaped fruits, which differ from the typical round shape of common lemons. The plant exhibits the typical citrus morphology, with glossy dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. It produces fruit that is generally smaller than standard lemons but has a similar aromatic profile.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique fruit shape and potential use in specialty culinary or ornamental applications.
