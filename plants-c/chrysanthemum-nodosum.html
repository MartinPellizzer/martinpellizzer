Chrysanthemum nodosum

Chrysanthemum nodosum, commonly known as the nodding chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow to orange petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and has a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are dark green, lobed, and often covered in fine hairs.

Chrysanthemum nodosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
