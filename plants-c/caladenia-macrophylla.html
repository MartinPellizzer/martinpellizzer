Caladenia macrophylla

Caladenia macrophylla, commonly known as the Large-leafed Spider-orchid, is a species of orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has a single, erect stem that bears several flowers, each with a narrow, spurred labellum. Its leaves are broad and strap-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

Caladenia macrophylla is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in sandy or loamy soils in woodland habitats.
