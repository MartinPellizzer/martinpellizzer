Chamaecrista desvauxii var. pirebebuiensis

Chamaecrista desvauxii var. pirebebuiensis, commonly known as the Pirebebi shrub, is a perennial legume native to the rainforests of western Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange floral clusters. The plant produces pods that contain seeds used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and local herbal practices.
