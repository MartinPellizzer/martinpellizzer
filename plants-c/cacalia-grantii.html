Cacalia grantii

Cacalia grantii, commonly known as the pink sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It is characterized by its delicate, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
