Cheirostylis latipetala

Cheirostylis latipetala, commonly known as the broad-petalled cheirostylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant features striking, fragrant flowers with broad, lance-shaped petals and a prominent lip. It typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial plant in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
