Cyrtochilum diodon

Cyrtochilum diodon, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish-white, often with a slight purple or pink tinge. Cyrtochilum diodon thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
