Clausena suffruticosa var. paucijuga

Clausena suffruticosa var. paucijuga, commonly known as the narrow-leafed Clausena, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy berries that turn red when mature. Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various soil types and climates.

It is occasionally cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
