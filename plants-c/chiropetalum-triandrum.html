Chiropetalum triandrum

Chiropetalum triandrum, commonly known as the three-lobed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Chiropetalum triandrum is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
