Carex jiaodongensis

Carex jiaodongensis, commonly known as the Jiaodong sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its triangular stems and narrow leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested or marshy areas. It produces small, greenish flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the sedge family.

Carex jiaodongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
