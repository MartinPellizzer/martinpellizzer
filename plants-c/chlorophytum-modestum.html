Chlorophytum modestum

Chlorophytum modestum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems, followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads. It is well-suited for indoor growing and thrives in bright, indirect light.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners.
