Cynanchica staliana subsp. staliana

Cynanchica staliana subsp.

staliana, commonly known as the Staliana milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing a robust root system adapted to dry conditions.

The plant features compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in racemes. Its fruits are follicles that release seeds with tufts of hair, aiding in wind dispersal.

This subspecies is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
