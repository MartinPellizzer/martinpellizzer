Combretum menyhartii

Combretum menyhartii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has small, leathery leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, round fruits that split open to release seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Combretum menyhartii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
