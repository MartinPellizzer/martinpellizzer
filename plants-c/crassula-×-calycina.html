Crassula × calycina

Crassula × calycina, commonly known as the Christmas Crassula, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
