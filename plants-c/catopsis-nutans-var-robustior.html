Catopsis nutans var. robustior

Catopsis nutans var. robustior, commonly known as the robust catopsis, is a flowering plant native to the humid forests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a water-holding cup. The plant produces small, colorful flowers that emerge from the center of the rosette, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its striking appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
