Croton stockeri

Croton stockeri, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to eastern Africa.

It features large, palmate leaves with a distinctive lobed shape and a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

Croton stockeri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
