Citrus × nobilis var. melanocarpa

Citrus × nobilis var. melanocarpa, commonly known as the black citrus or black lemon, is a hybrid citrus plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black fruit skin, which contrasts with the yellow-orange color of typical citrus fruits. The plant produces small, tart fruits with a high acidity level and a complex flavor profile. It is often grown for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and unique fragrance.

The leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, with a slightly rough texture.
