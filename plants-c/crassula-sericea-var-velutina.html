Crassula sericea var. velutina

Crassula sericea var. velutina, commonly known as the velvety crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bluish-green leaves covered in a fine, velvety texture, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
