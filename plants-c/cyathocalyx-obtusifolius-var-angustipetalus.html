Cyathocalyx obtusifolius var. angustipetalus

Cyathocalyx obtusifolius var. angustipetalus, commonly known as the narrow-petaled obtusifolius cyathocalyx, is a variant of the genus Cyathocalyx, which belongs to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a rounded apex and a smooth, waxy surface. The flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, which are cup-like structures that enclose the reproductive organs. This variant is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in tropical and subtropical regions.
