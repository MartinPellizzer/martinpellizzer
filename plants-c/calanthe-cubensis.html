Calanthe cubensis

Calanthe cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Cuba.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short rhizome and a rosette of lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, erect stem bearing several showy, fragrant flowers with white petals and a yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe cubensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively rare in cultivation.
