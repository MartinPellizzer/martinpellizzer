Coussarea neei

Coussarea neei, commonly known as the red-flowered coussarea, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are opposite in arrangement, and its stems are often woody and branched. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The botanical features of Coussarea neei make it a visually appealing and ecologically significant plant in its native habitat.
