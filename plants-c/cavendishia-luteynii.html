Cavendishia luteynii

Cavendishia luteynii, commonly known as the yellow-stemmed orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow stems and intricate floral structures. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. Cavendishia luteynii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species in its natural range.
