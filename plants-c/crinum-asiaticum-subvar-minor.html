Crinum asiaticum subvar. minor

Crinum asiaticum subvar. minor, commonly known as the dwarf Asian lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
