Ceropegia volubilis

Ceropegia volubilis, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia volubilis is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
