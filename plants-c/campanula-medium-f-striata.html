Campanula medium f. striata

Campanula medium f. striata, commonly known as the striped bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet with distinctive white or pale longitudinal stripes. The plant has lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
