Cyclaminus persica

Cyclaminus persica, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bowl-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.

Cyclaminus persica is known for its ability to bloom throughout the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
