Chrysanthemum coronarium var. spatiosum

Chrysanthemum coronarium var. spatiosum, commonly known as the broadleaf chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow or orange flowers. The plant has alternate, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its blooming period typically occurs in the summer months, adding vibrant color to landscapes.
