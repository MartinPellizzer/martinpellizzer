Cissus phyllomicron

Cissus phyllomicron, commonly known as the miniature leaf cissus, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, pale green leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by tiny, round fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus phyllomicron is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
