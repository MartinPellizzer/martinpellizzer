Crassula curta

Crassula curta, commonly known as the dwarf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing form and fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring or summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Crassula curta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
