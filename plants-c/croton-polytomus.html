Croton polytomus

Croton polytomus, commonly known as the variegated croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are often lobed and have a textured surface, adding to its ornamental value.

Croton polytomus is popular as a houseplant due to its vibrant foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
