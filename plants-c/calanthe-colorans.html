Calanthe colorans

Calanthe colorans, commonly known as the colorful calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to red, often with intricate patterns. The plant has broad, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette formation, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms. Calanthe colorans thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and its role in the diverse orchid flora of its native habitats.
