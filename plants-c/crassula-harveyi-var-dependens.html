Crassula harveyi var. dependens

Crassula harveyi var. dependens, commonly known as the Forest Crassula, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container gardening.
