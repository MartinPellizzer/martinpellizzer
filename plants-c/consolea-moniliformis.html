Consolea moniliformis

Consolea moniliformis, commonly known as the chain cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, cylindrical stems that form a chain-like structure, which gives it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the warmer months. It is a slow-growing plant that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its striking appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in arid and semi-arid environments.
