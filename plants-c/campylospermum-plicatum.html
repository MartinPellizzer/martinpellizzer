Campylospermum plicatum

Campylospermum plicatum, commonly known as the pleated campylospermum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive pleated or folded appearance, which gives it its scientific name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Campylospermum plicatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its unique foliage structure.
