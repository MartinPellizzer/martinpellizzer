Ctenitopsis sagenioides

Ctenitopsis sagenioides, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

Ctenitopsis sagenioides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
