Conophytum parvulum

Conophytum parvulum, commonly known as the dwarf conophytum, is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are typically green with a subtle blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Conophytum parvulum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
