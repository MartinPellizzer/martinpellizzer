Conophytum saxetanum var. loeschianum

Conophytum saxetanum var. loeschianum, commonly known as the saxetanum loeschianum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
