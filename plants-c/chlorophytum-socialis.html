Chlorophytum socialis

Chlorophytum socialis, commonly known as the social spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often form a rosette shape. The plant produces small, white, bell-shaped flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
