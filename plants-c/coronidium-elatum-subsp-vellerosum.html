Coronidium elatum subsp. vellerosum

Coronidium elatum subsp.

vellerosum, commonly known as the velvet crownbeard, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America. It features upright stems covered in soft, velvety hairs, giving it its common name.

The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
