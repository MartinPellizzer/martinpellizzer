Cyperus tenuicomus

Cyperus tenuicomus, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green or reddish in color. It thrives in moist to wet soil conditions and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Cyperus tenuicomus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
