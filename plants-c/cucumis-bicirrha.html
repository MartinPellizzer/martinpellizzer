Cucumis bicirrha

Cucumis bicirrha, commonly known as the wild cucumber, is a species native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous vine that produces small, spiny fruits resembling cucumbers. The plant has hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are yellow and typically appear in clusters.

Cucumis bicirrha is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.
