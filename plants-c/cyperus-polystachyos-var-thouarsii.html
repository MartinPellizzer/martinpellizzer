Cyperus polystachyos var. thouarsii

Cyperus polystachyos var. thouarsii, commonly known as the narrowleaf paperplant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the sedge family (Cyperaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and dense, cylindrical clusters of small, pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands, making it a common component of wetland vegetation.
