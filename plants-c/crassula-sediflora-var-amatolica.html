Crassula sediflora var. amatolica

Crassula sediflora var. amatolica, commonly known as the Amatola crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a powdery blue or grey coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
