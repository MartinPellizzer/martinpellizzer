Chionanthus macrobotrys

Chionanthus macrobotrys, commonly known as the Chinese fringe flower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and are popular in ornamental horticulture.
