Cotula oederi

Cotula oederi, commonly known as the yellow daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often surrounded by white or pale green ray florets. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Cotula oederi is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is also cultivated as a cut flower.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers.
