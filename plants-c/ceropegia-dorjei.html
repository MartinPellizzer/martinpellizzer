Ceropegia dorjei

Ceropegia dorjei, commonly known as the Dorjei's string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing vines and tubular flowers. The plant features succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia dorjei is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
