Crepis deloynei

Crepis deloynei, commonly known as Deloyne's mouse-ear, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It features small, spoon-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer.

Crepis deloynei is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along rocky outcrops.
