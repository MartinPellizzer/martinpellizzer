Caladium lowii

Caladium lowii, commonly known as the "Coral Bells" or "Coral Leaves," is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 feet. Caladium lowii thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
