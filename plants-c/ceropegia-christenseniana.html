Ceropegia christenseniana

Ceropegia christenseniana, commonly known as the Christmas star or star ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent vine characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in winter, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its leaves are fleshy and often have a waxy texture, adapted to store water in arid conditions.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
