Chamaecrista rotundifolia var. rotundifolia

Chamaecrista rotundifolia var. rotundifolia, commonly known as roundleaf burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive round or oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of yellow to orange flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

Its small, pod-like fruits contain seeds that are often dispersed by wind or animals.
