Cochliopetalum odoratum

Cochliopetalum odoratum, commonly known as the fragrant hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, red, and white. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, elliptical seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The aromatic fragrance of its flowers makes it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
