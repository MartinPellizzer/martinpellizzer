Capnorchis spectabilis

Capnorchis spectabilis, commonly known as the showy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers with prominent yellow markings, which are highly attractive to pollinators. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it thrives in moist, well-drained soil. Its flowers bloom during the summer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Capnorchis spectabilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
