Cytisus monspessulanus var. burnatii

Cytisus monspessulanus var. burnatii, commonly known as the Burnatii broom, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, wiry stems and small, leathery leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

This variety is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.
