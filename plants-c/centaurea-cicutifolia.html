Centaurea cicutifolia

Centaurea cicutifolia, commonly known as the sickle-centaurea, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and spiny, bristly foliage. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a compact growth habit with narrow, linear leaves.

Centaurea cicutifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
