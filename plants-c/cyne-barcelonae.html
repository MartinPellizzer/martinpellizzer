Cyne barcelonae

Cyne barcelonae, commonly known as the Barcelona bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are erect and branched.

Cyne barcelonae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
