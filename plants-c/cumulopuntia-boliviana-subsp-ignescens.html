Cumulopuntia boliviana subsp. ignescens

Cumulopuntia boliviana subsp.

ignescens, commonly known as the fiery cactus, is a species of cactus native to the Andes of Bolivia. It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can reach up to 3 meters in height.

The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by cylindrical fruits. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a dense covering of fine hairs.

This subshrub is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and full sun exposure.
