Cypripedium × nitidissimum

Cypripedium × nitidissimum, commonly known as the white lady's tresses, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and dense clusters of white, fragrant flowers that resemble a tresses of hair. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Cypripedium × nitidissimum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
