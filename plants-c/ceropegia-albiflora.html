Ceropegia albiflora

Ceropegia albiflora, commonly known as the silver leaf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, trailing stems with opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery coating. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
