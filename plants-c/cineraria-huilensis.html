Cineraria huilensis

Cineraria huilensis, commonly known as the Huiltepec cineraria, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers and fuzzy, silver-gray leaves. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in high-altitude regions.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
