Croton virens

Croton virens, commonly known as the Emerald Beauty, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
