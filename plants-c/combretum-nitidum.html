Combretum nitidum

Combretum nitidum, commonly known as the glossy flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, shiny bark and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate drought conditions further contribute to its adaptability in various environments.
