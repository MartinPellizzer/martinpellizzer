Coelogyne complectens

Coelogyne complectens, commonly known as the complex coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a mix of white and yellow hues. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its leaves are strap-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Coelogyne complectens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
