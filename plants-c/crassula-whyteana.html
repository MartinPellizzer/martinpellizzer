Crassula whyteana

Crassula whyteana, commonly known as the Whyte's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula whyteana is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
