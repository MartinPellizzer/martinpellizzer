Cotoneaster rubens var. rubens

Cotoneaster rubens var. rubens, commonly known as the red cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes make it a visually appealing addition to landscapes.
