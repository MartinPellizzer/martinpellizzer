Capraea grandifolia

Capraea grandifolia, commonly known as the large-leafed rock cress, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

Its leaves are lobed and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, while its small white or yellow flowers appear in clusters.
