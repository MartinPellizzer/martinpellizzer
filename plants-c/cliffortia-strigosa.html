Cliffortia strigosa

Cliffortia strigosa, commonly known as the silver leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small, perennial shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of white and pink. It is well adapted to arid conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Cliffortia strigosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
