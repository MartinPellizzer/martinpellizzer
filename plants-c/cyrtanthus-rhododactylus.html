Cyrtanthus rhododactylus

Cyrtanthus rhododactylus, commonly known as the rock lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

Cyrtanthus rhododactylus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom reliably in suitable climates.
