Chlorophytum glaucidulum

Chlorophytum glaucidulum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features a rosette of fleshy, silver-blue leaves that are covered in a powdery waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces tall, branched stems with small white flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
