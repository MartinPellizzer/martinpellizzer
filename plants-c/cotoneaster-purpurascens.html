Cotoneaster purpurascens

Cotoneaster purpurascens, commonly known as the purpleleaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that turn a striking reddish-purple color in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white, star-shaped flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Cotoneaster purpurascens is relatively low maintenance and adaptable to various soil types.
