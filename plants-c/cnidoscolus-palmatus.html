Cnidoscolus palmatus

Cnidoscolus palmatus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by edible, spiny fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their anti-inflammatory properties.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and riverbanks.
