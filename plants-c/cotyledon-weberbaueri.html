Cotyledon weberbaueri

Cotyledon weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon weberbaueri is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
