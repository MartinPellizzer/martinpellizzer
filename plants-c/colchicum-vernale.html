Colchicum vernale

Colchicum vernale, commonly known as the spring colchicum, is a perennial plant belonging to the Colchicaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant features basal leaves that are often lance-shaped and covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in woodland and meadow habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

The flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a unique, helmet-like shape that distinguishes it from other similar species.
