Crinum capense

Crinum capense, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that grow from a central bulb, and it thrives in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various garden conditions.

Crinum capense is also known for its resilience and ability to withstand drought.
