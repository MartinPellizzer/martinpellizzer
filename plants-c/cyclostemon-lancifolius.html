Cyclostemon lancifolius

Cyclostemon lancifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cyclostemon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
