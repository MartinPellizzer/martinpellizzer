Cotyledon insignis

Cotyledon insignis, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are often marked with distinctive patterns and can be used for propagation through leaf cuttings.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
