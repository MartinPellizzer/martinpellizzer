Chrysopogon Pauciflorus

Chrysopogon pauciflorus is a rare grass species native to the arid regions of southern India. It is characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered inflorescences. This plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often found in degraded landscapes. Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts. Its unique morphology makes it an important subject for ecological and botanical study.
