Cyclanthera naudiniana var. tenuifolia

Cyclanthera naudiniana var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, pale green fruits that are typically bitter in taste. Its botanical characteristics include a robust root system and tendrils that aid in climbing.

This variety is often cultivated for its edible fruits, though it requires careful handling due to its bitterness.
