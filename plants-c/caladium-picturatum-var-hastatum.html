Caladium picturatum var. hastatum

Caladium picturatum var. hastatum, commonly known as the painted leaf caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful, heart-shaped leaves that display intricate patterns of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is often used in gardens and as a houseplant for its vibrant, eye-catching leaves.
