Centaurea dhofarica

Centaurea dhofarica, commonly known as Dhofar star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and vibrant purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture, adapted to the arid climate of its native habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Centaurea dhofarica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
