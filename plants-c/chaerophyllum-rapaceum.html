Chaerophyllum rapaceum

Chaerophyllum rapaceum, commonly known as the common ragwort.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae, characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves and yellowish-green flowers. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom from late spring to early autumn.
