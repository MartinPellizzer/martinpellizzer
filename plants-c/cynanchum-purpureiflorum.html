Cynanchum purpureiflorum

Cynanchum purpureiflorum, commonly known as purpleflower milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its showy, purple flowers. The plant has a climbing habit and features hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped leaflet arrangement. It produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, oval seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
