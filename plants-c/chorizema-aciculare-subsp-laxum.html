Chorizema aciculare subsp. laxum

Chorizema aciculare subsp.

laxum, commonly known as the slender thimble cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features a cylindrical stem with a smooth, green surface and produces small, white flowers during the summer months.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss and deter herbivores.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
