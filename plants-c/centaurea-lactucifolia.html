Centaurea lactucifolia

Centaurea lactucifolia, commonly known as lettuceleaf star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a taproot system and thrives in dry, sunny environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its foliage resembles that of lettuce, hence its common name.
