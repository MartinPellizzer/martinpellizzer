Chamaecrista ixodes

Chamaecrista ixodes, commonly known as the hairy burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Chamaecrista ixodes is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
