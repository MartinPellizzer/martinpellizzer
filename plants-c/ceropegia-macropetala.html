Ceropegia macropetala

Ceropegia macropetala, commonly known as the giant ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has fleshy, succulent stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia macropetala is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
