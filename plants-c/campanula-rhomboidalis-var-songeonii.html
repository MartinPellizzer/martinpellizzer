Campanula rhomboidalis var. songeonii

Campanula rhomboidalis var. songeonii, commonly known as the Songeonii bellflower, is a compact, flowering perennial native to Korea.

It features upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate flowers and compact growth habit.
