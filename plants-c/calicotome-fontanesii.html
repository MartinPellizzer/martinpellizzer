Calicotome fontanesii

Calicotome fontanesii, commonly known as the African sand rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, rose-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or rocky habitats.

Calicotome fontanesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
