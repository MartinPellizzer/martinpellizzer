Cyclosorus javanicus

Cyclosorus javanicus, commonly known as the Java cyclosorus, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has slender, branched rhizomes that form dense mats on the water surface. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with small, ovate fronds that float on the water.

Cyclosorus javanicus is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in water gardens.
