Coffea abeokutae var. longicarpa

Coffea abeokutae var. longicarpa, commonly known as the long-sepaled coffee.

It is a wild species native to western Africa, particularly Nigeria. The plant is characterized by its elongated, slender berries and distinctive floral structure. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a pointed tip.

This variety is of interest to botanists and coffee researchers due to its unique genetic traits and potential for cultivation.
