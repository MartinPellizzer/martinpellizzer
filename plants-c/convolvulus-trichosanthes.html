Convolvulus trichosanthes

Convolvulus trichosanthes, commonly known as the Chinese snakegourd, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, ribbed fruits. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its edible fruits and ornamental value.
