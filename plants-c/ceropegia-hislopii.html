Ceropegia hislopii

Ceropegia hislopii, commonly known as the African violet or the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous roots and produces clusters of small, pale greenish-white flowers. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia hislopii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
