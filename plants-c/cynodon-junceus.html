Cynodon junceus

Cynodon junceus, commonly known as silvergrass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray blades that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and wiry, with a soft texture that distinguishes it from other grasses.

Cynodon junceus is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
