Cartiera cordata

Cartiera cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cartiera cordata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
