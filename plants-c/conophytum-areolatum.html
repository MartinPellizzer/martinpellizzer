Conophytum areolatum

Conophytum areolatum, commonly known as the "Karoo star" or "dwarf ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Conophytum areolatum is well adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often growing in crevices or on rock surfaces.
