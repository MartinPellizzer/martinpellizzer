Chlorophytum longebracteatum

Chlorophytum longebracteatum, commonly known as the long bracted spider plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bright green coloration. The plant produces long, thin flowering stems that bear small, white or pale green flowers. It is a hardy and drought-tolerant plant, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage and the delicate appearance of its flowering stems.
