Calicotome infesta var. ponsgueraui

Calicotome infesta var. ponsgueraui, commonly known as the Ponsgueraui broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in coastal and rocky environments, often found in well-drained soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in landscaping for erosion control.
