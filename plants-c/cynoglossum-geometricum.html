Cynoglossum geometricum

Cynoglossum geometricum, commonly known as geometric bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum geometricum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
