Catapodium marinum var. balearicum

Catapodium marinum var. balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic catapodium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and often covered with a fine layer of hair.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in saline environments.
