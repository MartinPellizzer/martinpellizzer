Carex coriophora subsp. langtaodianensis

Carex coriophora subsp.

langtaodianensis, commonly known as the Langtaodian sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streams.
