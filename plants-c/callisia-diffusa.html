Callisia diffusa

Callisia diffusa, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It features striking, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are typically borne on slender stalks. It is well adapted to humid environments and thrives in partial to full shade.

Callisia diffusa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
