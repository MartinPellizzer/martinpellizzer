Cineraria americana

Cineraria americana, commonly known as the American cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are often hairy and have a silvery or grayish appearance.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
