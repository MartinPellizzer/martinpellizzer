Cirsium × huteri

Cirsium × huteri, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Cirsium vulgare and Cirsium palustre, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. It has a rosette of basal leaves and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
