Cynocrambe formosana

Cynocrambe formosana, commonly known as the Formosa cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cynocrambe formosana thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in open woodlands and grassy areas.
