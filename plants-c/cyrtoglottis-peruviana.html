Cyrtoglottis peruviana

Cyrtoglottis peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian arrowroot or arrowleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length. The plant produces striking, spatulate-shaped flowers that are typically green with a yellow or white spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its starchy, edible tubers.

The botanical features of Cyrtoglottis peruviana make it a unique and ornamental addition to tropical gardens.
