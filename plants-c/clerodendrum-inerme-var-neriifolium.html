Clerodendrum inerme var. neriifolium

Clerodendrum inerme var. neriifolium, commonly known as the Black Beauty Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast strikingly with its green foliage. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and dramatic floral display.
