Cephalocarpus confertus

Cephalocarpus confertus, commonly known as the dense-headed grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.
