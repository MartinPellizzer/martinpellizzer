Calanthe ensifolia

Calanthe ensifolia, commonly known as the sword-leaf calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, strap-like leaves that are long and narrow, giving it its common name. The plant produces striking flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe ensifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
