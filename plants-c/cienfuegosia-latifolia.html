Cienfuegosia latifolia

Cienfuegosia latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf cienfuegosia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as along riverbanks and in dense forests. Its flowering period occurs during the late spring and early summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
