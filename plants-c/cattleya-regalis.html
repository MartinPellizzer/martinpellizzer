Cattleya regalis

Cattleya regalis, commonly known as the royal cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and sometimes yellow. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are thick and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Cattleya regalis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
