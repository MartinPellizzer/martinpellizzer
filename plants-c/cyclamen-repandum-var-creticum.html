Cyclamen repandum var. creticum

Cyclamen repandum var. creticum, commonly known as the Cretan cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Crete.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers during late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
