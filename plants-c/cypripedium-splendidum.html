Cypripedium splendidum

Cypripedium splendidum, commonly known as the splendid lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically pink or white in color. The plant has a pseudobulb and produces a single, erect stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
