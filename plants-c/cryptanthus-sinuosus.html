Cryptanthus sinuosus

Cryptanthus sinuosus, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is known for its striking, zigzagging leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the tips of the stems. It is a compact, evergreen shrub that thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cryptanthus sinuosus is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
