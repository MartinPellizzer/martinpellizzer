Cyathea barringtonii

Cyathea barringtonii, commonly known as the Barrington's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. Its fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, leathery texture.

Cyathea barringtonii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various native species.
