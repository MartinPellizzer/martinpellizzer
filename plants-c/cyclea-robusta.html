Cyclea robusta

Cyclea robusta, commonly known as the Indian arrowroot or arrowhead plant, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Convolvulaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and climbing habit. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are tuberous and have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.

Cyclea robusta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
