Chadsia flammea

Chadsia flammea, commonly known as the flame chadsia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its desert environment. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to dry conditions.

Chadsia flammea is often used in ornamental gardening for its eye-catching floral display.
