Citrus × tankan f. koshotankan

Citrus × tankan f. koshotankan, commonly known as the Koshotankan mandarin.

This hybrid citrus plant is a cross between the Ponkan and the Kishu mandarin, resulting in a unique combination of traits. It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thin, smooth peel and a sweet, aromatic flavor. The plant thrives in warm, subtropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value, along with its edible fruit, makes it a popular choice for both cultivation and ornamental purposes.
