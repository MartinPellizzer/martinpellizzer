Cryptanthus acaulis var. purpureus

Cryptanthus acaulis var. purpureus, commonly known as the purple-flowered carpet plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming succulent native to Brazil.

It features dense clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a reddish or purple hue, especially under bright light. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a houseplant.

Its compact size and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
