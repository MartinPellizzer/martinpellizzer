Coelogyne amplissima var. schweinfurthiana

Coelogyne amplissima var. schweinfurthiana, commonly known as the giant coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, often displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid environments.

The orchid blooms once every few years, making it a rare and prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
