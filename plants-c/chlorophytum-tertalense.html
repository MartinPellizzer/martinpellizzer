Chlorophytum tertalense

Chlorophytum tertalense, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Tiger's Head plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves that are often marked with white or yellow stripes. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Chlorophytum tertalense thrives in well-drained soil and prefers bright, indirect light.
