Clitoria lasciva

Clitoria lasciva, commonly known as the "Lascivious Crown," is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
