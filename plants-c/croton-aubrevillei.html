Croton aubrevillei

Croton aubrevillei, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in both indoor and outdoor settings.
