Clusia palmicida

Clusia palmicida, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color. Clusia palmicida is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to different environments.
