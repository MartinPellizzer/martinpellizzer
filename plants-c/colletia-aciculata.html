Colletia aciculata

Colletia aciculata, commonly known as the needle cactus, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems covered in sharp, spiny bristles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Colletia aciculata is often used in horticulture for its unique appearance and resilience.
