Calamagrostis epigejos var. parviflora

Calamagrostis epigejos var. parviflora, commonly known as the slender flatgrass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Asia and Europe.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower spikes that bloom in late summer. The plant is characterized by its delicate, airy appearance and is often used in ornamental landscaping. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Calamagrostis epigejos var. parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
