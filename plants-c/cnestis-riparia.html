Cnestis riparia

Cnestis riparia, commonly known as the riverbank morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows along riverbanks and moist soils. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It blooms throughout the rainy season and is often found in wetland habitats.

Cnestis riparia is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing riverbanks.
