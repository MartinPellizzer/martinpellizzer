Centema biflora

Centema biflora, commonly known as the two-flowered centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers with five petals, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
