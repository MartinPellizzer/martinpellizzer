Campnosperma panamense

Campnosperma panamense, commonly known as the Panamanian flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces legume pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
