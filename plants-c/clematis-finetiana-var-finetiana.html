Clematis finetiana var. finetiana

Clematis finetiana var. finetiana, commonly known as the slender clematis, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, woody stems that can grow up to several meters in length, supported by tendrils that help it climb. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in the spring and early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
