Cymbidium goeringii var. formosanum

Cymbidium goeringii var. formosanum, commonly known as the Formosan orchid, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth. The plant produces tall, slender flower spikes bearing multiple fragrant, lanceolate flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
