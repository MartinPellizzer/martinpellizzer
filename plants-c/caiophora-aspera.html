Caiophora aspera

Caiophora aspera, commonly known as the rough caiophora, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub-like plant native to certain regions of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its rough, often hairy foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
