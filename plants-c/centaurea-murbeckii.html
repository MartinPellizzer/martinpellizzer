Centaurea murbeckii

Centaurea murbeckii, commonly known as the Murbeck's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and serrated, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments.
