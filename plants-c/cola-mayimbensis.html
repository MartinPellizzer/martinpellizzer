Cola mayimbensis

Cola mayimbensis, commonly known as the Mayimbe cola, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the rainforests of Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into oval, woody pods containing glossy, brown seeds. Cola mayimbensis is notable for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.

Its ecological role includes providing habitat and food for various wildlife species in its native environment.
