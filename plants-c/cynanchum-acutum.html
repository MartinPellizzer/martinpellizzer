Cynanchum acutum

Cynanchum acutum, commonly known as the sharpleaf milkweed, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lanceolate leaves. Cynanchum acutum is also known for its latex-like sap, which can be toxic to some animals.
