Cliffortia ruscifolia var. purpurea

Cliffortia ruscifolia var. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Carpet Bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, low-growing habit and small, bright purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces yellowish-green, fuzzy bracts that surround the flower clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural and ecological landscaping.
