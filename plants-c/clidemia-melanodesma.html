Clidemia melanodesma

Clidemia melanodesma, commonly known as velvetleaf or blackstem shrub, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. Clidemia melanodesma is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and along stream banks.
