Centaurea pulcherrima var. freynii

Centaurea pulcherrima var. freynii, commonly known as the Freyn's gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences that resemble gladioli. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are upright and sturdy.
