Clerodendrum hirsutum var. ciliatum

Clerodendrum hirsutum var. ciliatum, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, often reddish stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
