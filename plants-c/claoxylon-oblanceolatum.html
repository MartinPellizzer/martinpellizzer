Claoxylon oblanceolatum

Claoxylon oblanceolatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its hard, heavy wood is highly valued for its durability and is used in construction and furniture-making.

The tree also plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
