Cypripedium ferrugineum

Cypripedium ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and dense clusters of pale yellow to brownish-red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its leaves are grass-like and arranged in a rosette at the base of the stem.

Cypripedium ferrugineum is a member of the orchid family and is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
