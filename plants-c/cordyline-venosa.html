Cordyline venosa

Cordyline venosa, commonly known as the red-veined dracaena, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green with prominent red veins. The plant is evergreen, thriving in warm, humid climates and requiring well-draining soil. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.

Cordyline venosa is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for both landscaping and as a houseplant.
