Chloris prieurii

Chloris prieurii, commonly known as the Prieur's grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it grows in rocky and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.
