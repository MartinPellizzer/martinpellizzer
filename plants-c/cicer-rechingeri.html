Cicer rechingeri

Cicer rechingeri, commonly known as the Rechinger's vetch, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are typically dispersed by wind. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some areas.
