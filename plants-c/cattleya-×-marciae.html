Cattleya × marciae

Cattleya × marciae, commonly known as the Marcei Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and yellow, with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences, and its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are often arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Cattleya × marciae is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
