Camellia pukhangensis

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces red or orange-red flowers with a distinctive, waxy texture, making it a striking ornamental species.
