Cattleya gardneriana

Cattleya gardneriana, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from pink to purple, with a distinctive pouch-like lip. The plant produces long, arching stems with broad, leathery leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cattleya gardneriana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
