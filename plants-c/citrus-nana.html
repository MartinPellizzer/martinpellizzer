Citrus nana

Citrus nana, commonly known as dwarf lemon or lemon tree miniature, is a compact citrus species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically less than an inch in diameter, with a thin, yellow rind and juicy, acidic pulp. Citrus nana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for container gardening and small spaces.
