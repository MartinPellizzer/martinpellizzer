Cheilosoria javensis

Cheilosoria javensis, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and delicate, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Cheilosoria javensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
