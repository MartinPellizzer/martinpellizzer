Costus afer var. maculatus

Costus afer var. maculatus, commonly known as the spotted costus, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that often have a variegated or spotted appearance. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both garden beds and container planting.

Its unique foliage and vibrant floral display make it a popular choice for tropical landscapes.
