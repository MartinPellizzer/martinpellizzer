Crossandra longispica

Crossandra longispica, commonly known as the long-spined crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. Crossandra longispica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
