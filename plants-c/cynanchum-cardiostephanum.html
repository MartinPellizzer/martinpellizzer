Cynanchum cardiostephanum

Cynanchum cardiostephanum, commonly known as the heart-step flower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels, which bloom in late summer. Its roots are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
