Cryptocoryne crispatula var. sinensis

Cryptocoryne crispatula var. sinensis, commonly known as the Sinensis Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often exhibit a wavy or crinkled edge. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for planted aquariums. Its rhizome grows horizontally, anchoring the plant in the substrate and supporting the growth of its foliage.

It is relatively hardy and can tolerate a range of water parameters, making it a popular choice among aquarium hobbyists.
