Costarica

Costarica, commonly known as the Costa Rican pine, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its wood is highly valued for its strength and durability.

Costarica plays an important role in the ecosystems of Costa Rica, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
