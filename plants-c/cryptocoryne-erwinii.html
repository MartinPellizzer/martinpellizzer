Cryptocoryne erwinii

Cryptocoryne erwinii, commonly known as the Erwin's cryptogram, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette formation. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is popular among aquarium enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal.

Cryptocoryne erwinii is also known for its resilience and ability to adapt to a variety of water parameters.
