Camellia cuspidata var. chekiangensis

Camellia cuspidata var. chekiangensis, commonly known as the Chekiang camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or yellow base. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained acidic soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
