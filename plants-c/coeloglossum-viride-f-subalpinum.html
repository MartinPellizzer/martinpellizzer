Coeloglossum viride f. subalpinum

Coeloglossum viride f. subalpinum, commonly known as the alpine green hawksbeard, is a rare and specialized plant adapted to high-altitude environments.

This subspecies thrives in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it tolerates cold temperatures and strong winds.
