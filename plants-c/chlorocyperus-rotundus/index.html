Chlorocyperus Rotundus

Chlorocyperus rotundus is a common weed found in various habitats around the world. It is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and adapt to different environmental conditions. This plant has a distinctive appearance with round, spongy leaves and small, grass-like flowers. It is often considered a pest in agricultural fields due to its rapid growth and competitive nature. Despite its invasive tendencies, it plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small organisms.
