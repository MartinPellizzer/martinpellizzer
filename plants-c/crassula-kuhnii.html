Crassula kuhnii

Crassula kuhnii, commonly known as the "Little Carpet" or "Baby Jade," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and requiring minimal watering.

Crassula kuhnii is popular as a low-maintenance houseplant due to its compact size and aesthetic appeal.
