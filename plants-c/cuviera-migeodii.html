Cuviera migeodii

Cuviera migeodii, commonly known as the Mexican feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-green leaves and feathery plumes that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its ornamental value comes from its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.

Cuviera migeodii is often used in native plant gardens for its low maintenance and ecological benefits.
