Crepidium crassilabris

Crepidium crassilabris, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Crepidium crassilabris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
