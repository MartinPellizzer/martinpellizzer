Campylotropis polyantha var. polyantha

Campylotropis polyantha var. polyantha, commonly known as the Chinese peacock bean, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with serrated edges and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several bean-like seeds, which are edible when young. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
