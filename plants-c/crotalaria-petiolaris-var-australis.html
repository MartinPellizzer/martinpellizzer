Crotalaria petiolaris var. australis

Crotalaria petiolaris var. australis, commonly known as the Australian crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or loamy soils and thrives in full sun. It has a short life cycle and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and grasslands.

Its seeds are adapted for dispersal by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
