Centaurea argentea var. macrothysana

Centaurea argentea var. macrothysana, commonly known as silver centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has a compact growth habit with upright stems and is often found in disturbed or open areas.

Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, contributing to its ecological significance.
