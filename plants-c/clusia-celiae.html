Clusia celiae

Clusia celiae, commonly known as the "Evergreen Trumpet Tree," is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, and its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas. Clusia celiae is also noted for its rapid growth rate and adaptability to various soil types, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Its botanical features make it both visually striking and ecologically valuable in its native habitats.
