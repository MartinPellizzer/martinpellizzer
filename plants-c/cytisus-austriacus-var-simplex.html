Cytisus austriacus var. simplex

Cytisus austriacus var. simplex, commonly known as the Austrian broom, is a shrub native to Central Europe.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a striking visual element to its habitat. The plant has slender, woody stems and trifoliate leaves, which are compound and consist of three leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

This variety is less branched than the typical form, giving it a more open and elegant appearance.
