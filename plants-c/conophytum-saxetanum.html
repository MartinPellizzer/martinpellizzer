Conophytum saxetanum

Conophytum saxetanum, commonly known as the "Saxetan stone plant," is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help store water and reduce transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
