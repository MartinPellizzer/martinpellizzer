Cyclamen pseudibericum f. roseum

Cyclamen pseudibericum f. roseum, commonly known as the pink cyclamen, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant pink to rose-colored flowers.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is native to the Iberian Peninsula, where it thrives in woodland and shaded areas. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety foliage that contrasts beautifully with its showy blooms. It is typically grown as a tuberous perennial, requiring well-drained soil and cool, moist conditions to flourish.

Cyclamen pseudibericum f. roseum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
