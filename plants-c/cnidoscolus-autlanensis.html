Cnidoscolus autlanensis

Cnidoscolus autlanensis, commonly known as the Mexican arrowroot or spurgeleaf, is a shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and research settings.
