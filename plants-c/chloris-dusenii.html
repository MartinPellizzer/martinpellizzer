Chloris dusenii

Chloris dusenii, commonly known as the Sierra grass, is a perennial grass species native to California and parts of Nevada.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

Chloris dusenii is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in areas with well-drained soils.
