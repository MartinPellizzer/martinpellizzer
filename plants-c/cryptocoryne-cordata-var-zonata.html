Cryptocoryne cordata var. zonata

Cryptocoryne cordata var. zonata, commonly known as the Zonate Cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically green with a distinctive zonation pattern, often featuring a lighter or yellowish stripe along the midrib. The plant is well adapted to low-light conditions and can thrive in a variety of water conditions, making it popular among aquarium enthusiasts. It is a hardy and easy-to-care-for plant that grows from a rhizome and can form dense stands in suitable environments.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant appearance and ability to enhance the aesthetic of planted tanks.
