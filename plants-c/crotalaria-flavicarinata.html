Crotalaria flavicarinata

Crotalaria flavicarinata, commonly known as the yellow flame bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers with contrasting red veins. The plant has compound leaves with pairs of oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in sunny, open habitats.

Crotalaria flavicarinata is also noted for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
