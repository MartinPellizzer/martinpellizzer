Chamaecrista arborescens

Chamaecrista arborescens, commonly known as the tree clover, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree with compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain small, kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential agricultural use.
