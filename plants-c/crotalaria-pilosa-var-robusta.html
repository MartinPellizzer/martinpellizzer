Crotalaria pilosa var. robusta

Crotalaria pilosa var. robusta, commonly known as the robust crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are typically green and slightly pubescent. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with prominent violet or purple markings, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.

This species is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
