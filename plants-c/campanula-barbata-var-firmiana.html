Campanula barbata var. firmiana

Campanula barbata var. firmiana, commonly known as the firmiana bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats and prefers well-drained soils. It has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
