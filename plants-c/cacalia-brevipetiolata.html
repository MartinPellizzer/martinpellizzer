Cacalia brevipetiolata

Cacalia brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-petioled bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are small, oval, and have a hairy texture, while the stems are often reddish in hue.

Cacalia brevipetiolata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
