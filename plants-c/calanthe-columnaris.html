Calanthe columnaris

Calanthe columnaris, commonly known as the slender calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Calanthe columnaris is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
