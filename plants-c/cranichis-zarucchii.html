Cranichis zarucchii

Cranichis zarucchii, commonly known as the Zarucchii orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found growing in rocky or forested areas. Its botanical features include a slender stem and narrow, strap-like leaves.
