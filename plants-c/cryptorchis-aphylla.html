Cryptorchis aphylla

Cryptorchis aphylla, commonly known as the leafless orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its lack of leaves, which is an unusual trait among orchids. The plant produces a single, erect stem that bears a single, showy flower with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, typically in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its specialized morphology and habitat requirements make it a rare and fascinating subject for botanical study.
