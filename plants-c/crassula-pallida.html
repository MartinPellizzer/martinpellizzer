Crassula pallida

Crassula pallida, commonly known as the pale crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, blue-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its compact size and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
