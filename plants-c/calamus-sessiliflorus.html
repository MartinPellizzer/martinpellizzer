Calamus sessiliflorus

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender rhizomes and narrow, linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikes of greenish-white flowers.
