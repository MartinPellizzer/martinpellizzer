Cryptocoryne × purpurea nothovar. purpurea

Cryptocoryne × purpurea nothovar. purpurea, commonly known as the purple cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking violet to purple leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in soft, slightly acidic water with moderate to high light conditions. It is popular in aquariums for its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its growth is typically slow to moderate, making it suitable for both beginner and experienced aquarists.
