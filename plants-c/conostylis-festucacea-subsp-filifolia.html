Conostylis festucacea subsp. filifolia

Conostylis festucacea subsp.

filifolia, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to eastern Australia. It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, and it produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native gardens. Conostylis festucacea subsp.

filifolia plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
