Cardamine faeroensis

Cardamine faeroensis, commonly known as the Faeroese watercress, is a flowering plant native to the Faeroe Islands.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats, often near streams or in shaded areas. Its leaves are fern-like and can be harvested for culinary use, similar to other watercress varieties.

The flowers are small and white, arranged in clusters, and contribute to its ornamental appeal.
