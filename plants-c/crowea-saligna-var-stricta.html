Crowea saligna var. stricta

Crowea saligna var. stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf crowea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellow-orange flowers during the summer months. Its botanical characteristics include woody stems and small, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface.

It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.
