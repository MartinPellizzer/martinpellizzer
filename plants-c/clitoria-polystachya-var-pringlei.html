Clitoria polystachya var. pringlei

Clitoria polystachya var. pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's winged bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers, which are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces clusters of small, edible beans that are used in local cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and nutritional benefits.

Its leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health properties.
