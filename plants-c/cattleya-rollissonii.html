Cattleya rollissonii

Cattleya rollissonii, commonly known as the Red Cattleya, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a deep red color with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in seasonal climates. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

This species is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and fragrance.
