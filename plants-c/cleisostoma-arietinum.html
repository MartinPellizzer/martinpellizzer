Cleisostoma arietinum

Cleisostoma arietinum, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and a unique, intricate floral structure that attracts specific pollinators. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a striking contrast between the deep red petals and the green sepals.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated for horticultural purposes in various regions.
