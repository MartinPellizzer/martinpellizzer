Coleus mannii

Coleus mannii, commonly known as the African shield plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, shield-shaped leaves that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, adding to its ornamental value.

Coleus mannii is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens for its vibrant foliage and easy care requirements.
