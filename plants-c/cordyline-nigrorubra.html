Cordyline nigrorubra

Cordyline nigrorubra, commonly known as the red-black cordyline, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that range in color from deep red to dark purple. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
