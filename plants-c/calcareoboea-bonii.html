Calcareoboea bonii

Calcareoboea bonii, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant thrives in calcareous soils, which are rich in limestone, giving it its specific name. It has a compact growth habit with a short stem and broad, fleshy leaves.

Calcareoboea bonii is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
