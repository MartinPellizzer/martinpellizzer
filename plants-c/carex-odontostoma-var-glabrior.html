Carex odontostoma var. glabrior

Carex odontostoma var. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved carex, is a variant of the broader Carex odontostoma species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are smooth and less hairy compared to the typical variety. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its reproductive structures include spike-like inflorescences that produce small, wind-pollinated flowers.
