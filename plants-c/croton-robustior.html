Croton robustior

Croton robustior, commonly known as the roughleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in small, raised bumps, giving them a textured appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Croton robustior is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
