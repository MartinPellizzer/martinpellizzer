Centaurea dufourii subsp. rifana

Centaurea dufourii subsp.

rifana, commonly known as Rif centaury, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in dry and sunny environments.

The plant features slender, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
