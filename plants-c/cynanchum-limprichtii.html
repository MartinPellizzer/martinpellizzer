Cynanchum limprichtii

Cynanchum limprichtii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruit is a follicle containing several seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
