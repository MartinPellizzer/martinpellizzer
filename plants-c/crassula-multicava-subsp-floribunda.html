Crassula multicava subsp. floribunda

Crassula multicava subsp.

floribunda, commonly known as the "Lucky Plant" or "Pineapple Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa. It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a reddish tint under stress or in full sun.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for both decorative and low-maintenance gardening.
