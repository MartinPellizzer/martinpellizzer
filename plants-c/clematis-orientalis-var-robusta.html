Clematis orientalis var. robusta

Clematis orientalis var. robusta, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
