Centronia insignis

Centronia insignis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Centronia insignis is widely cultivated as a decorative plant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

It is also known for its ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper light and humidity.
