Clematis sophia var. plena

Clematis sophia var. plena, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its robust growth and showy, double flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis sophia var. plena is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
