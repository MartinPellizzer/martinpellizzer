Cuphea fulgida

Cuphea fulgida, commonly known as the flamebeard plant, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

Its foliage is dark green and glossy, adding a rich contrast to its vibrant floral display.

Cuphea fulgida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower due to its long-lasting blooms.
