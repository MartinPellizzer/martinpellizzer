Carduus × estivali subsp. estivali

Carduus × estivali subsp.

estivali, commonly known as the summer thistle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pink flower heads, which consist of numerous small florets.

The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is native to southern Europe. It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and other open habitats.

Its botanical features make it both a weed and a potential ornamental plant.
