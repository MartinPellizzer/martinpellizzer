Chenopodium ambrosioides var. pubescens

Chenopodium ambrosioides var. pubescens, commonly known as gooseweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, succulent stems and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

The species is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in various herbal remedies.
