Caustis dioica var. eciliata

Caustis dioica var. eciliata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf caustis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and woodland habitats. The plant features linear, often hairy leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its small, yellowish-white flowers are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal blooms.
