Cyclamen rhodium

Cyclamen rhodium, commonly known as the silver cyclamen, is a striking plant with unique botanical features.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is native to South Africa. The plant is characterized by its velvety, silver-green leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. Its foliage is often covered in a fine, waxy coating that gives it a distinctive appearance.

Cyclamen rhodium thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
