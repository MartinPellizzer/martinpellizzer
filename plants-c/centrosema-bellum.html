Centrosema bellum

Centrosema bellum, commonly known as the smallflower cowpea, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen and stabilize soil.

Its seeds are edible and have been traditionally used in various culinary applications.
