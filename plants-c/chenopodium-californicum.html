Chenopodium californicum

Chenopodium californicum, commonly known as California goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in dry, disturbed soils. The plant features alternate, ovate to rhombic leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in dense, terminal inflorescences.

Chenopodium californicum is often found in grasslands, along roadsides, and in open woodland areas.
