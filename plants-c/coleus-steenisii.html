Coleus steenisii

Coleus steenisii, commonly known as the red-stemmed coleus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant foliage and ornamental value. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from red to purple and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in whorled clusters.

Coleus steenisii is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its striking appearance and ease of care.
