Cissus sulfurosa

Cissus sulfurosa, commonly known as the sulfur vine, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by bright red berries, which are a notable feature. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
