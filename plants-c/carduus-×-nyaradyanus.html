Carduus × nyaradyanus

Carduus × nyaradyanus, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its robust, spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads that resemble those of other thistle species. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides. Its flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
