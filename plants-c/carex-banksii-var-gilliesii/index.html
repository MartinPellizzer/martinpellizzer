Carex Banksii Var. Gilliesii

Gillies' sedge, scientifically known as Carex banksii var. gilliesii, is a unique and lesser-known member of the sedge family. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of New Zealand, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. Its distinctive appearance includes narrow, grass-like leaves and a subtle, elegant structure that sets it apart from other sedges. Despite its beauty, Gillies' sedge remains relatively obscure in both botanical and horticultural circles. This article explores its habitat, characteristics, and significance in the natural world.
