Caladium hastatum

Caladium hastatum, commonly known as the "Angel's Wings," is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting veins. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium hastatum is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant.
