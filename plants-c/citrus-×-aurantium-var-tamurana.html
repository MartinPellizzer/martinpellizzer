Citrus × aurantium var. tamurana

Citrus × aurantium var. tamurana, commonly known as the Yuzu, is a small evergreen shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, oval-shaped fruits that are typically harvested when they are still green. The leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated margin. The plant is valued for its essential oils, which are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.

It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
