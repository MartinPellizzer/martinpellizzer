Clethra costaricensis

Clethra costaricensis, commonly known as the Guanacaste clethra, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Clethraceae and is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Clethra costaricensis is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
