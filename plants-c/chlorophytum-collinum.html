Chlorophytum collinum

Chlorophytum collinum, commonly known as the snake plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal water, making it an ideal indoor plant. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in arid environments.

Chlorophytum collinum is also noted for its air-purifying qualities, as it can absorb toxins from the air.
