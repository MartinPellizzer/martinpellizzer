Cephaelis amoena

Cephaelis amoena, commonly known as the pink fairy fanflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that produces clusters of delicate, pink to purple flowers. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cephaelis amoena is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
