Cousinia lucida var. arachnoidea

Cousinia lucida var. arachnoidea, commonly known as the silky cushion flower, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, woolly appearance.

This variety is well adapted to harsh, arid environments and is often found in rocky or alpine habitats.
