Citharexylum scandens

Citharexylum scandens, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.

Citharexylum scandens is also valued for its ability to grow in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to partial shade.
