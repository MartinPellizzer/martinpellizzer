Crossandra warneckei

Crossandra warneckei, commonly known as the Warneckei flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Crossandra warneckei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
