Chamaedorea stenocarpa

Chamaedorea stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Mexico and Guatemala. Chamaedorea stenocarpa is popular as a houseplant due to its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance requirements.
