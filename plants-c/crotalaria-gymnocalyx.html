Crotalaria gymnocalyx

Crotalaria gymnocalyx, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to autumn. Its seeds are encased in a hard, ridged pod that splits open when mature.
