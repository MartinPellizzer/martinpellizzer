Chlorophytum africanum var. africanum

Chlorophytum africanum var. africanum, commonly known as the African spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often curl at the tips. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that appear in clusters on tall, slender stalks. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to purify air and tolerate low light conditions further enhances its appeal as a versatile houseplant.
