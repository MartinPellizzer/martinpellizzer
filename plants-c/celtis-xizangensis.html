Celtis xizangensis

Celtis xizangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hackberry, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Cannabaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The tree produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in clusters, followed by spherical, green fruits that turn reddish-brown when mature.

It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its hardiness and ornamental qualities.
