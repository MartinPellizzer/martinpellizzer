Cyclamen hiemale

Cyclamen hiemale, commonly known as the winter cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Mediterranean.

It produces striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. Cyclamen hiemale is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its elegant blooms and foliage.

It is typically grown in pots or as a houseplant due to its sensitivity to heat and humidity.
