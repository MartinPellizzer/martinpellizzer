Ceropegia Volkartii

Ceropegia volkartii is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. Known for its long, trailing stems and vibrant, tubular flowers, it has captivated plant lovers worldwide. This species is often referred to as the "string of hearts" due to its heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. Its beauty and ease of care make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
