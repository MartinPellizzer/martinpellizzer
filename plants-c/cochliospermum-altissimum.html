Cochliospermum altissimum

Cochliospermum altissimum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with numerous small leaflets and produces woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

Its botanical features make it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
