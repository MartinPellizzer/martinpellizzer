Caleacte rotundifolia

Caleacte rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf caleacte, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
