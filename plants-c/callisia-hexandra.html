Callisia hexandra

Callisia hexandra, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescence that consists of numerous small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces showy, elongated clusters of blooms that hang from the stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal.

Callisia hexandra is also valued for its ability to purify the air and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
