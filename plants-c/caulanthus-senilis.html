Caulanthus senilis

Caulanthus senilis, commonly known as the silverleaf silversword, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and erect stems that bear clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in arid environments, often found in rocky soils and coastal areas. It is part of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with similar structural features.
