Convolvulus leiocalycinus var. leiocalycinus

Convolvulus leiocalycinus var. leiocalycinus, commonly known as the slender morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its rapid growth and attractive flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
