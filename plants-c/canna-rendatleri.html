Canna rendatleri

Canna rendatleri, commonly known as the Red River canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, lance-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish tint. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Canna rendatleri is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and floral display in tropical and subtropical gardens.
