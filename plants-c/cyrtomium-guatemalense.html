Cyrtomium guatemalense

Cyrtomium guatemalense, commonly known as the Guatemalan holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Blechnaceae and is characterized by its leathery, dark green fronds that are often adorned with spore-producing sori on the underside. The foliage is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
