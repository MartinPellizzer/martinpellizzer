Caryopteris wallichiana

Caryopteris wallichiana, commonly known as blue mist shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and clusters of blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant. Its compact growth habit and ornamental value make it a popular choice in horticulture.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine, highlighting its dual botanical and practical significance.
