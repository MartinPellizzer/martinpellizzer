Cotoneaster kerstanii

Cotoneaster kerstanii, commonly known as the Kerstan's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters, featuring dense, often thorny branches. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are oval-shaped, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated margin.

Cotoneaster kerstanii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
