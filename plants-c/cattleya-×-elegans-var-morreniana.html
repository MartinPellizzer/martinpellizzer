Cattleya × elegans var. morreniana

Cattleya × elegans var. morreniana, commonly known as the Morren's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, fragrant flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features strikingly colored blooms in shades of pink, white, and yellow, often with a contrasting lip. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal, branching pattern with pseudobulbs.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
