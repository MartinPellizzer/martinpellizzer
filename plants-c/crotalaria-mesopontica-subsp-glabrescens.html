Crotalaria mesopontica subsp. glabrescens

Crotalaria mesopontica subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and the Middle East. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and often nitrogen-fixing capabilities.

The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a distinctive spurred structure at the base of the petals.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smooth, glabrous leaves and reduced pubescence compared to other members of the species.
