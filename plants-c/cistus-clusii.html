Cistus clusii

Cistus clusii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. It is often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions.

Cistus clusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
