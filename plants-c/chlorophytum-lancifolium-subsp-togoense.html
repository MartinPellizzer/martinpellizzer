Chlorophytum lancifolium subsp. togoense

Chlorophytum lancifolium subsp.

togoense, commonly known as the Togo spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family. It is native to western Africa, particularly Togo and neighboring countries.

The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a slight blue or grayish tint. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often hidden beneath the leaves.

This species is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
