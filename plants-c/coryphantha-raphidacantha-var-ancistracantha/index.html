Coryphantha Raphidacantha Var. Ancistracantha

Coryphantha raphidacantha var. ancistracantha is a rare and striking cactus native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States. Known for its spiny stems and vibrant yellow flowers, this plant is a favorite among desert horticulturists. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping. Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species in the world of succulent enthusiasts.
