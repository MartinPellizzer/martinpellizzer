Castanopsis nhatrangensis

Castanopsis nhatrangensis, commonly known as Nhatrang stone oak, is a species of evergreen tree native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts. The plant is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves and produces small, cup-shaped acorns. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.
