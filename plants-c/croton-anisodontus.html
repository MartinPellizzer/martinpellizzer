Croton anisodontus

Croton anisodontus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The plant has alternate, simple leaves that can be elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton anisodontus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf colors.
