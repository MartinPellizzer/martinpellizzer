Crotalaria Smithiana

Crotalaria smithiana is a unique and striking plant native to the arid regions of South Africa. It is known for its vibrant, flame-like flowers that bloom in shades of orange and yellow. This species belongs to the legume family and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
