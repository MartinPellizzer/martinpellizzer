Cleistes caloptera

Cleistes caloptera, commonly known as the white fairy lantern, is a rare and striking orchid native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial with a distinctive, bell-shaped flower that is typically white or pale green. The plant has a short, fleshy stem and produces a single, intricate bloom that resembles a lantern. Its flowers open at night, attracting specific nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
