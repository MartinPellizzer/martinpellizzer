Cineraria diversifolia

Cineraria diversifolia, commonly known as the blue cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant features hairy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine, white trichomes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cineraria diversifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
