Castela salubris var. endlichiana

Castela salubris var. endlichiana, commonly known as the Endlich's castela, is a perennial shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often blooming during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening or as a decorative plant in shaded areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
