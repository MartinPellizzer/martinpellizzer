Cyclosorus Boninensis

Cyclosorus boninensis is a rare and intriguing species of aquatic fern native to the Bonin Islands. This plant belongs to the family Water Ferns and is known for its delicate, spiral-shaped fronds. It thrives in shallow, freshwater habitats and is often found in rocky or muddy areas. Due to its limited distribution and fragile nature, it is considered a vulnerable species. Studying Cyclosorus boninensis provides valuable insights into the biodiversity of island ecosystems.
