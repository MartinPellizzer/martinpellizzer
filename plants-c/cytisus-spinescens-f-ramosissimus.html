Cytisus spinescens f. ramosissimus

Cytisus spinescens f. ramosissimus, commonly known as the ramose broom, is a shrub characterized by its dense, branching structure.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
