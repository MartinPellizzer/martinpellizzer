Centaurea zuvandica subsp. jelenevskyi

Centaurea zuvandica subsp.

jelenevskyi, commonly known as the Jelenevsky's cornflower, is a rare and endemic species native to the Caucasus region. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats.
