Castilleja purpurea f. corallina

Castilleja purpurea f. corallina, commonly known as the coral bract castilleja, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its striking, coral-pink bracts that resemble small flowers.
