Citrosma oligocarpa

Citrosma oligocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit citrosma, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in lowland rainforests. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is a globose capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
