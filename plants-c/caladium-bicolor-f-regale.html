Caladium bicolor f. regale

Caladium bicolor f. regale, commonly known as the Regal Caladium, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with vibrant, colorful patterns.

The foliage displays a wide range of hues, including red, pink, white, and green, creating a dramatic visual effect. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. This cultivar is often grown as an ornamental plant for its showy leaves rather than its small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is a popular choice for shaded garden beds and indoor spaces where it can add a splash of color.
