Caesia eckloniana

Caesia eckloniana, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. Caesia eckloniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
