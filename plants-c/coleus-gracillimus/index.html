Coleus Gracillimus

Coleus gracillimus is a strikingly beautiful plant known for its vibrant, colorful leaves. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments. Its foliage displays a wide range of hues, from deep reds to bright purples and soft pinks. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. It makes an excellent addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens.
