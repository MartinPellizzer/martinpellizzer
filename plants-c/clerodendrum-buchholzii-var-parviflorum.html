Clerodendrum buchholzii var. parviflorum

Clerodendrum buchholzii var. parviflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered clerodendrum, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming during the warmer months. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release tiny seeds when mature.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and floral display.
