Clerodendrum capitatum var. conglobatum

Clerodendrum capitatum var. conglobatum, commonly known as the globular clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a subtle fragrance.
