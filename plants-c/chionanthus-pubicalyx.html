Chionanthus pubicalyx

Chionanthus pubicalyx, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, white to pale yellow flowers that resemble lanterns, which bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically pubescent, especially on the underside. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Chionanthus pubicalyx is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
