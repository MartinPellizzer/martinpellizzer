Casearia nigrescens var. lucida

Casearia nigrescens var. lucida, commonly known as the shining casearia, is a species of flowering plant in the Salicaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded forest understories.
