Catasetum taguariense

Catasetum taguariense, commonly known as the Guianan bee orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bee-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange with purple markings. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which is pollinated by specific bee species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Catasetum taguariense is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
