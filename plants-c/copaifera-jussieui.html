Copaifera jussieui

Copaifera jussieui, commonly known as Brazilian copaiba, is a species of tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, simple leaves and produces yellowish to reddish resinous sap. The plant belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is known for its aromatic properties. Its bark and resin have been used traditionally in medicine for their anti-inflammatory and antimicrobial effects.

The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and thrives in humid, tropical environments.
