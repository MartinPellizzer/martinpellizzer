Cypripedium × gibezianum

Cypripedium × gibezianum, commonly known as the giant white lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a white pouch-like lip and greenish-yellow petals. The plant typically grows in cool, moist forest habitats and thrives in partial shade. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single flower per stem.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
