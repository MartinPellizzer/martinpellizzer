Cynodon aristulatus

Cynodon aristulatus, commonly known as the silvergrass or silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-colored foliage and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in summer. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.

Cynodon aristulatus is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.
