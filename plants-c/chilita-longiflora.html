Chilita longiflora

Chilita longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered chilita, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that range in color from pink to red. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and specialized horticultural collections.
