Cassia desvauxii var. mollissima

Cassia desvauxii var. mollissima, commonly known as the silky cassia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its soft, silky foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, and the plant can grow as a shrub or small tree.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
