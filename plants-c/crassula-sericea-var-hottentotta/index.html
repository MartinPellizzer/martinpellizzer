Crassula Sericea Var. Hottentotta

Crassula sericea var. hottentotta is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rounded shape and fuzzy, silver-green leaves that catch the light. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its hardy nature, it still requires proper care to flourish.
