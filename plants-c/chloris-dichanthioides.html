Chloris dichanthioides

Chloris dichanthioides, commonly known as the narrow-leafed grass or slender grass, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

This grass is also valued for its use in erosion control and as a forage plant for livestock.
