Castanopsis purpurella

Castanopsis purpurella, commonly known as the purple-barked tanoak, is a species of evergreen tree native to the temperate rainforests of southern Japan.

Castanopsis purpurella plays a significant ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to forest structure and biodiversity.
