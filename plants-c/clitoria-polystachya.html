Clitoria polystachya

Clitoria polystachya, commonly known as the winged bean or evergreen bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-blue flowers with wing-like petals that resemble the shape of a butterfly. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds and a starchy, tuberous root. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval and slightly hairy.

Clitoria polystachya is valued for its nutritional content and is used in traditional cuisines and as a medicinal plant.
