Cotoneaster bis-ramianus

Cotoneaster bis-ramianus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soil in full sun to partial shade.

Cotoneaster bis-ramianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
