Cynorkis lindleyana

Cynorkis lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or red hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Cynorkis lindleyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
