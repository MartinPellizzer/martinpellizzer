Centaurea recta

Centaurea recta, commonly known as the upright centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces bright blue or purple flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea recta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
