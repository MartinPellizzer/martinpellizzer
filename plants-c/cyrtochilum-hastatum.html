Cyrtochilum hastatum

Cyrtochilum hastatum, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Cyrtochilum hastatum is noted for its unique floral structure, with petals that are often curved and adorned with intricate patterns.
