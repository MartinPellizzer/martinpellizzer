Citharexylum kunthianum

Citharexylum kunthianum, commonly known as the silverwood tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense, silvery-green foliage and fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a leathery texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
