Combretum accedens

Combretum accedens, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, and is often used in landscaping for its vibrant floral display.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
