Cucumis sativus var. praecox

Cucumis sativus var. praecox, commonly known as early cucumber, is a variety of cucumber characterized by its rapid growth and early maturation cycle.

It typically produces small to medium-sized fruits with a smooth, green skin and a mild, refreshing flavor. This variety thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It is often cultivated in greenhouse or indoor settings to extend the growing season.

Due to its quick harvest time, it is popular among home gardeners and commercial growers seeking early yields.
