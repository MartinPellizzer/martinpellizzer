Centaurea soskae subsp. soskae

Centaurea soskae subsp.

soskae, commonly known as the Soska's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers.

The species is typically found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. It has linear leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic plant in its native region.
