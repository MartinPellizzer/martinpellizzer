Calliandra toroana

Calliandra toroana, commonly known as the Toranana acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has bipinnate leaves, with each leaflet being narrow and elongated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Calliandra toroana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
