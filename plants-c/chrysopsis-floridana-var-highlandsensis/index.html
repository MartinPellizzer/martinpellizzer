Chrysopsis Floridana Var. Highlandsensis

Chrysopsis floridana var. highlandsensis is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the asteraceae family and is known for its vibrant yellow blooms that appear in late spring. this variety is particularly found in the highlands of florida and is often seen growing in moist, shaded environments. due to its limited range and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a valuable subject for botanical study and preservation.
