Colchicum × veratrifolium

Colchicum × veratrifolium, commonly known as the hybrid meadow saffron, is a perennial bulbous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often violet or purple, nodding flowers. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring partial shade. Its leaves are basal, strap-like, and often persist through the winter.

The hybrid is notable for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
