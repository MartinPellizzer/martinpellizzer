Crassula pentandra var. denticulata

Crassula pentandra var. denticulata, commonly known as the Denticulate Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ease of care and attractive appearance.
