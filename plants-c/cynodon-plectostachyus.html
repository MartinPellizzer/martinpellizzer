Cynodon plectostachyus

Cynodon plectostachyus, commonly known as the silvergrass or silver bluestem, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems with a bluish-gray sheen, giving it its common name. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family.

Cynodon plectostachyus is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
