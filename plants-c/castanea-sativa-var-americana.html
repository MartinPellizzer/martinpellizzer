Castanea sativa var. americana

Castanea sativa var. americana, commonly known as the American chestnut.

This variant is a subspecies of the European chestnut, adapted to the North American climate. It is characterized by its large, sweet, and edible nuts enclosed in spiny husks. The plant is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 30 meters tall.

Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with serrated edges, and it produces male and female flowers on the same tree.
