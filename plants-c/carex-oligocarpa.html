Carex oligocarpa

Carex oligocarpa, commonly known as the slender spikerush, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescence that bears small, spike-like clusters of flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often have a waxy coating.

Carex oligocarpa plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
