Cynoglossum foliosum

Cynoglossum foliosum, commonly known as the broadleaf forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features striking blue to purple flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along streambanks.

Cynoglossum foliosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in herbal medicine.
