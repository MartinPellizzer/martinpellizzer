Crassula corallina

Crassula corallina, commonly known as the coral crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense, compact rosette. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
