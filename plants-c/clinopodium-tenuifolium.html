Clinopodium tenuifolium

Clinopodium tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, tubular flowers are usually blue to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
