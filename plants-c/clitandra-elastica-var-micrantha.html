Clitandra elastica var. micrantha

Clitandra elastica var. micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered white wood.

It is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its smooth, light grey bark and elliptical leaves with a slightly wavy margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative tree in tropical landscapes.
