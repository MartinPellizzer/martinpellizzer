Coelogyne rubra

Coelogyne rubra, commonly known as the red lady's tresses, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and yellow flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has erect, often branched stems and produces dense clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Coelogyne rubra is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil.
