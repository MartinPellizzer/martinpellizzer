Calanthe jusnerii

Calanthe jusnerii, commonly known as the Jusner's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces upright spikes of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and early summer. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

Calanthe jusnerii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
