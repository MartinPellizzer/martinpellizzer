Cyperus tetrastachyos

Cyperus tetrastachyos, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, although it is often classified in the Cyperaceae family due to its sedge-like appearance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. It typically grows in wet or marshy habitats, such as swamps and roadside ditches.

Cyperus tetrastachyos is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
