Cyathea praeceps

Cyathea praeceps, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its growth in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dimorphic, with sterile and fertile fronds differing in shape and structure.

Cyathea praeceps plays an important ecological role in tropical forests, contributing to biodiversity and soil stability.
