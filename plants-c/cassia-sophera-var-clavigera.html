Cassia sophera var. clavigera

Cassia sophera var. clavigera, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, pod-like fruits containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
