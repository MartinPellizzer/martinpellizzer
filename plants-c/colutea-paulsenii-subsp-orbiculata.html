Colutea paulsenii subsp. orbiculata

Colutea paulsenii subsp.

orbiculata, commonly known as the broadleaf cowpea or mountain cowpea, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It features compound leaves with rounded leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to temperate to subtropical climates.

This species is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
