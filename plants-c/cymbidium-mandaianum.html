Cymbidium mandaianum

Cymbidium mandaianum, commonly known as the Mandaian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its inflorescence can reach several feet in height. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
