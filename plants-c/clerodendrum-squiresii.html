Clerodendrum squiresii

Clerodendrum squiresii, commonly known as the red-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly rough texture and a fragrant scent. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum squiresii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
