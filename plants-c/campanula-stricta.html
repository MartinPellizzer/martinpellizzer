Campanula stricta

Campanula stricta, commonly known as the upright bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically in shades of blue or violet. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula stricta is known for its long blooming period, often from late spring through early autumn.
