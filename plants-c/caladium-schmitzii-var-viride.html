Caladium schmitzii var. viride

Caladium schmitzii var. viride, commonly known as the Greenheart caladium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a metallic sheen. The plant prefers warm, humid environments and thrives in partial to full shade. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden beds due to its striking appearance.
