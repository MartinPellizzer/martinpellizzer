Cnestis iomalla var. grandifoliolata

Cnestis iomalla var. grandifoliolata, commonly known as the large-leaved cnestis, is a variant of the cnestis plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its foliage is thick and succulent, allowing it to retain moisture in arid conditions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
