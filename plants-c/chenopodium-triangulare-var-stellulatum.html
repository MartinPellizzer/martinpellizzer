Chenopodium triangulare var. stellulatum

Chenopodium triangulare var. stellulatum, commonly known as the starleaf goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its triangular, often lobed leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, which are typically borne on slender stalks. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

This species is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
