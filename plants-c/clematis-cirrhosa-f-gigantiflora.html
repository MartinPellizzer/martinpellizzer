Clematis cirrhosa f. gigantiflora

Clematis cirrhosa f. gigantiflora, commonly known as the giant-flowered clematis, is a cultivar of the clematis plant known for its large, showy blooms.

It features vigorous growth and can reach heights of up to 10 feet, making it a striking addition to gardens. The flowers are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive, fragrant scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its dramatic floral display.
