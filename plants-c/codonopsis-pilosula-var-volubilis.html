Codonopsis pilosula var. volubilis

Codonopsis pilosula var. volubilis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower or threadleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its leaves are lanceolate and hairy, while its flowers range in color from white to pale blue.
