Cynoglossum coeruleum var. hedbergiorum

Cynoglossum coeruleum var. hedbergiorum, commonly known as the Blue Mouse Eardrop, is a perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
