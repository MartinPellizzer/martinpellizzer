Cytisus kerneri

Cytisus kerneri, commonly known as the Canary Island broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

Cytisus kerneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
