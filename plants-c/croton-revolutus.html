Croton revolutus

Croton revolutus, commonly known as the curled leaf croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique, waxy leaves with a distinctive curling pattern.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that give way to orange or red berries, adding visual interest in the fall. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring shades of green, yellow, and red, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Croton revolutus is relatively easy to care for, making it a favored choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
