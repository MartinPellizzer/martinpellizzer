Colocasia euchlora

Colocasia euchlora, commonly known as the swamp taro, is a perennial herb native to wetland areas of the southeastern United States.

It features broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in spadices, typical of the Araceae family. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

Colocasia euchlora thrives in moist, swampy environments and is adapted to tolerate waterlogged soils.
