Conyza tenera

Conyza tenera, commonly known as common ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, white hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a rough texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Conyza tenera is noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
