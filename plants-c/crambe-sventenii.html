Crambe sventenii

Crambe sventenii, commonly known as the Hungarian crambe, is a flowering plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, pinnately lobed, and covered in fine hairs.

Crambe sventenii is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.
