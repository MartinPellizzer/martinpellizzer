Cornutia latifolia f. alba

Cornutia latifolia f. alba, commonly known as the white form of the broad-leaved cornutia, is a rare and distinctive plant species native to specific regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and striking white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its white inflorescence contrasts sharply with the green foliage, making it visually striking.

Botanically, it is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to horticulturists and conservationists alike.
