Carex appendiculata var. sacculiformis

Carex appendiculata var. sacculiformis, commonly known as the sacculiform sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.
