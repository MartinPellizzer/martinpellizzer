Coelogyne linearifolia

Coelogyne linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, linear leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne linearifolia is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouse environments.
