Ceropegia denticulata var. denticulata

Ceropegia denticulata var. denticulata, commonly known as the zigzag vine, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive, zigzagging stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
