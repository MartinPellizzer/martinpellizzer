Clivia miniata

Clivia miniata, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a perennial evergreen herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil.

Clivia miniata is relatively low maintenance and suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
