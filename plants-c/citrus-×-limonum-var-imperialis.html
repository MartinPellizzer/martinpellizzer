Citrus × limonum var. imperialis

Citrus × limonum var. imperialis, commonly known as the Imperial lemon, is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its large, elongated fruit.

The fruit is typically yellow when ripe, with a thick, aromatic peel and a high juice content. It is native to the Mediterranean region and is cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces fragrant white flowers.

It is relatively cold-hardy compared to other citrus varieties, making it suitable for cooler climates.
