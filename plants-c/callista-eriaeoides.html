Callista eriaeoides

Callista eriaeoides, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bract-like structures that enclose its flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooming make it a notable ornamental plant.
