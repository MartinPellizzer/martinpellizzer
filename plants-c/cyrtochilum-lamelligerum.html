Cyrtochilum lamelligerum

Cyrtochilum lamelligerum, commonly known as the leafy orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and is typically found in shaded, humid environments. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually arranged in a loose inflorescence, with a distinctive yellow and green coloration.

Cyrtochilum lamelligerum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation under proper conditions.
