Cortaderia bifida

Cortaderia bifida, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features long, narrow, silver-blue leaves that are often described as resembling feathers, giving it a unique visual appeal. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that add a dramatic effect in both garden and landscape settings. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for contemporary garden designs.
