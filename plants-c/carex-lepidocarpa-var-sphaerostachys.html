Carex lepidocarpa var. sphaerostachys

Carex lepidocarpa var. sphaerostachys, commonly known as the globular sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact, rounded inflorescences. This variety is native to parts of North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
