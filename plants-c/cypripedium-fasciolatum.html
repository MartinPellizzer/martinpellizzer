Cypripedium fasciolatum

Cypripedium fasciolatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch that is typically pale pink to white in color. The plant features a single leaf that is broad and ovate, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is highly sought after by botanists and conservationists alike.
