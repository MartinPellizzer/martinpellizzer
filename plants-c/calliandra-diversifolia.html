Calliandra diversifolia

Calliandra diversifolia, commonly known as the showy calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, pea-like flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has compound leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to attract pollinators make it both ecologically and aesthetically valuable.
