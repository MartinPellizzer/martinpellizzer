Curcuma soloensis

Curcuma soloensis, commonly known as the Solo ginger or Solo turmeric, is a rhizomatous herbaceous plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes other well-known spices like ginger and turmeric. The plant produces large, broad leaves and has a distinctive yellow-orange rhizome that is often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its botanical features include a robust root system and showy inflorescences that can be brightly colored.

Curcuma soloensis is valued for its aromatic properties and potential health benefits.
