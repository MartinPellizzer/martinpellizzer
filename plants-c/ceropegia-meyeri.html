Ceropegia meyeri

Ceropegia meyeri, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia meyeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
