Cymbidium giganteum var. hookerianum

Cymbidium giganteum var. hookerianum, commonly known as the Hooker's giant orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, pendulous flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length, displaying vibrant colors ranging from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique appearance and fragrant blooms make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
