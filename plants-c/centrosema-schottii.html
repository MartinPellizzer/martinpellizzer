Centrosema schottii

Centrosema schottii, commonly known as the yellow centrosema, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its ornamental value and ability to improve soil fertility make it a useful plant in ecological restoration projects.
