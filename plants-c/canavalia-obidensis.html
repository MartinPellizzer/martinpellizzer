Canavalia obidensis

Canavalia obidensis, commonly known as the Obidos bean or yellow cluster bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that give way to pods containing edible seeds. It is often cultivated for its nutritional seeds, which are rich in protein and can be used in various culinary preparations.

Additionally, like other legumes, it has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
