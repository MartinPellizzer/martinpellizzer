Cyclea sutchuenensis

Cyclea sutchuenensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are tubular with five lobes.

Cyclea sutchuenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
