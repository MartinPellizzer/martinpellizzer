Chromolaena costatipes

Chromolaena costatipes, commonly known as the creeping chromolaena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Chromolaena costatipes is also known for its potential invasive tendencies in certain regions.
