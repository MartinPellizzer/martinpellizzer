Cynoglossum Yemenense

Cynoglossum yemenense is a rare and intriguing plant native to the highlands of Yemen. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is known for its striking blue to violet flowers. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
