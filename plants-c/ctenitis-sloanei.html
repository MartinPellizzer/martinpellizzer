Ctenitis sloanei

Ctenitis sloanei, commonly known as the Sloane's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Caribbean.

The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system supports the development of its tall stem, while its fronds are used for photosynthesis.
