Cymbidium canaliculatum

Cymbidium canaliculatum, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces tall spikes of fragrant, showy flowers. Its blooms are usually pink or white with a distinctive, narrow lip.

Cymbidium canaliculatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
