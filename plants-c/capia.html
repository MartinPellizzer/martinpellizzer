Capia

Capia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety foliage and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, with leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture. Capia thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
