Crotalaria nyikensis

Crotalaria nyikensis, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with a distinctive crown-like structure, which bloom in the morning. Its seeds are encased in a hard, spiny capsule, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.

Crotalaria nyikensis is also noted for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it valuable in soil enrichment.
