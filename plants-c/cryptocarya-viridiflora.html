Cryptocarya viridiflora

Cryptocarya viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed, contributing to its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
