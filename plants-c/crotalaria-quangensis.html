Crotalaria quangensis

Crotalaria quangensis, commonly known as the Quang Province crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open woodland or grassland habitats. It has small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
