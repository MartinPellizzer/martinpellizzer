Capparis artensis var. artensis

Capparis artensis var. artensis, commonly known as the Artensian Caper, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its thorny stems and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible, tart berries that are used in traditional cuisines. Its leaves are usually ovate and have a slightly succulent texture.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
