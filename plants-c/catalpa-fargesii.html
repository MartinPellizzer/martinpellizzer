Catalpa fargesii

Catalpa fargesii, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces long, cylindrical seed pods that split open to release winged seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Catalpa fargesii is also valued for its role in supporting local wildlife, particularly insects and birds.
