Capparis flexuosa f. hastata

Capparis flexuosa f. hastata, commonly known as the broad-leaved caper, is a variant of the caper plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round, greenish-yellow fruits that are typically edible. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This ornamental plant is valued for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators.
