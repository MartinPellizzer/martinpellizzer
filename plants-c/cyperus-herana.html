Cyperus herana

Cyperus herana, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Cyperus herana is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively and colonize wetland areas.

Its botanical features make it an important component of aquatic and riparian ecosystems.
