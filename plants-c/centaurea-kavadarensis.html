Centaurea kavadarensis

Centaurea kavadarensis, commonly known as the Kavadarensis thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust growth habits. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in rocky or dry soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
