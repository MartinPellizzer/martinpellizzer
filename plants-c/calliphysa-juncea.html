Calliphysa juncea

Calliphysa juncea, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, fragrant orchid flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the soil.

Calliphysa juncea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
