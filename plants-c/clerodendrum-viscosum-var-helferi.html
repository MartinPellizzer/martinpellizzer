Clerodendrum viscosum var. helferi

Clerodendrum viscosum var. helferi, commonly known as sticky clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, sticky leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a glandular, viscous substance.

The flowers bloom in clusters and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
