Calliandra macrocalyx

Calliandra macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, pink to red flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Calliandra macrocalyx is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
