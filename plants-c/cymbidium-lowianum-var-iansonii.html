Cymbidium lowianum var. iansonii

Cymbidium lowianum var. iansonii, commonly known as the Ianson's orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, sword-shaped leaves and tall inflorescences bearing numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The blooms typically range in color from white to pale yellow, with a distinctive red or purple lip. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

It prefers cool, humid conditions and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
