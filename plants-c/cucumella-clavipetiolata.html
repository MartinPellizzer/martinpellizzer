Cucumella clavipetiolata

Cucumella clavipetiolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved cucumber, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, green fruits that are typically harvested when immature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruits.

Botanically, it is notable for its unique petiole structure and distinct floral morphology.
