Connarus euphlebius

Connarus euphlebius, commonly known as the marsh holly.

It is a flowering plant native to wetland and marsh habitats in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a waxy texture, adapted to retain moisture.
