Chionanthus sabahensis

Chionanthus sabahensis, commonly known as the Sabah snowbell, is a flowering plant native to the Bornean rainforests.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in spring. The plant typically grows as a small shrub or tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy green surface.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
