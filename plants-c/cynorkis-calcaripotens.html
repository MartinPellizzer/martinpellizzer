Cynorkis calcaripotens

Cynorkis calcaripotens, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, spurred flowers that are typically pale green to yellow in color. The plant thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Cynorkis calcaripotens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
