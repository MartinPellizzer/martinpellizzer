Cirsium uzenense

Cirsium uzenense, commonly known as the Uzen thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lance-shaped leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its flowers are composite, consisting of both ray and disk florets, and it produces achenes as its seeds.

Cirsium uzenense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
