Cirsium creticum

Cirsium creticum, commonly known as the Cretan thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky and dry soils, often found in coastal and mountainous areas. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to pink flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs and have a waxy coating, helping to reduce water loss.

Cirsium creticum is part of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.
