Crassula impressa

Crassula impressa, commonly known as the "Lucky Bay" or "Pineapple Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often ribbed and have a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Crassula impressa is popular as a low-maintenance indoor plant due to its ability to store water in its leaves.
