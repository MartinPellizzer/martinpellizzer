Codiaeum membranaceum

Codiaeum membranaceum, commonly known as flame vine, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
