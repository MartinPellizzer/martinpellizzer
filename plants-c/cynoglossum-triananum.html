Cynoglossum triananum

Cynoglossum triananum, commonly known as the three-lobed bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with three distinct lobes, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum triananum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
