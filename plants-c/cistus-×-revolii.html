Cistus × revolii

Cistus × revolii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant thrives in well-drained, alkaline soils and is adapted to rocky and arid environments. It produces small, woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cistus × revolii is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
