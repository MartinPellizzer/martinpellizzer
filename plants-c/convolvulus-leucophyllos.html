Convolvulus leucophyllos

Convolvulus leucophyllos, commonly known as white-flowered morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden use.
