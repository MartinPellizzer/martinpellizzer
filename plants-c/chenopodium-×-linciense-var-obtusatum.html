Chenopodium × linciense var. obtusatum

Chenopodium × linciense var. obtusatum, commonly known as bluntleaf goosefoot, is a hybrid species within the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white to grayish powder. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its seeds are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in disturbed soils and is commonly found in arid or semi-arid regions.

This variety is valued for its nutritional content and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
