Caiophora lechleri

Caiophora lechleri, commonly known as the caucho tree or rubber tree, is a species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, oblong leaves and can grow up to 30 meters tall. The tree produces a latex sap that is harvested for its rubber content. Its bark and latex have been used traditionally for medicinal purposes.

The plant plays a significant ecological role in its native habitat.
