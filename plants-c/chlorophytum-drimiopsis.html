Chlorophytum drimiopsis

Chlorophytum drimiopsis, commonly known as the Drimiopsis spider plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often display a variegated pattern of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by clusters of bright green berries. It is a low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
