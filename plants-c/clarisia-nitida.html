Clarisia nitida

Clarisia nitida, commonly known as the evergreen hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which bloom throughout the year. Clarisia nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
