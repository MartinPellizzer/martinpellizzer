Centaurea rouyi var. macrocephala

Centaurea rouyi var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and has spiny, hairy stems. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, while the flower heads consist of both ray and disk florets.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
