Coleonema album

Coleonema album, commonly known as white chandelier plant, is a flowering shrub native to South Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a cascading effect. The plant has succulent, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a versatile addition to gardens and landscapes.
