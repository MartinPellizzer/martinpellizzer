Chamaedorea ernesti-augusti

Chamaedorea ernesti-augusti, commonly known as the Parlor Palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, upright stems with feather-like, dark green leaves that give it a graceful appearance. This plant is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low light requirements and minimal water needs. It thrives in humid environments and benefits from regular misting to maintain its lush foliage.

The Parlor Palm is also valued for its ability to improve air quality by filtering toxins.
