Centaurea × dobrogensis

Centaurea × dobrogensis, commonly known as the Dobrogian centaury, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the Balkans.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves, and it produces small, bract-like leaves around the flower cluster.

Centaurea × dobrogensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
