Clompanus purpurascens

Clompanus purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-flowered clompanus, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple petals and compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
