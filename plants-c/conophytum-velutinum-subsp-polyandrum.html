Conophytum velutinum subsp. polyandrum

Conophytum velutinum subsp.

polyandrum, commonly known as the velvet conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is a low-growing species, typically forming dense clusters of foliage.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
