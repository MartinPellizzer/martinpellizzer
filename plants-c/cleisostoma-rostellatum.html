Cleisostoma rostellatum

Cleisostoma rostellatum, commonly known as the rostellate hooded orchid, is a species of epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its unique, hooded flower structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cleisostoma rostellatum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
