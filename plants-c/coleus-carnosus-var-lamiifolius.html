Coleus carnosus var. lamiifolius

Coleus carnosus var. lamiifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with leaves that can range from deep red to orange and purple. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its showy, ornamental appearance. It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking visual appeal.
