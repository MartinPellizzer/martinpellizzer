Claoxylon parvicoccum

Claoxylon parvicoccum, commonly known as the small-seeded claoxylon, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small, shrub-like plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.
