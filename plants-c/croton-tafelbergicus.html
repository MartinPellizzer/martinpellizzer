Croton tafelbergicus

Croton tafelbergicus, commonly known as the Tafelberg croton, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
