Cyrtorchis Praetermissa Subsp. Zuluensis

Cyrtorchis praetermissa subsp. zuluensis is a rare and fascinating orchid native to southern Africa. This subspecies is known for its striking floral morphology and unique ecological adaptations. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied and often overlooked by botanists. Its habitat is limited to specific microclimates, making it highly vulnerable to environmental changes. Protecting this species is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native region.
