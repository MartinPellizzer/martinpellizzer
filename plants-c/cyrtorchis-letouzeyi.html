Cyrtorchis letouzeyi

Cyrtorchis letouzeyi, commonly known as the Letouzey's orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow leaves that are arranged in a compact rosette, allowing it to thrive in its alpine habitat. Cyrtorchis letouzeyi is adapted to cold climates and requires well-drained, rocky soils to flourish.
