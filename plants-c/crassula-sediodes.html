Crassula sediodes

Crassula sediodes, commonly known as the stonecrop crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula sediodes is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact growth habit.
