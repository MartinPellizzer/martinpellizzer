Crassula cephalophora var. thunbergii

Crassula cephalophora var. thunbergii, commonly known as the Thunberg's crassula, is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
