Calathea modesta

Calathea modesta, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns of deep green and silver. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its foliage changes color subtly with the seasons, often shifting to a reddish hue in response to light conditions.

Calathea modesta is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
