Caladenia gemmata f. lutea

Caladenia gemmata f. lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Western Australia.

It features a slender, hairy stem with a single, showy flower that has a pale yellow to orange hue. The flower has a distinctive, elongated labellum that resembles a spider, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as heaths and woodlands.

This rare and delicate orchid is listed as vulnerable due to habitat loss and environmental changes.
