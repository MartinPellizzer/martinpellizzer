Cyclopia aurescens

Cyclopia aurescens, commonly known as the golden cyclopia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It has a compact, shrub-like form with narrow, linear leaves.

Cyclopia aurescens is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine.
