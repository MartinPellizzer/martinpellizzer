Croton icche

Croton icche, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton icche typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its leaves are usually alternate and have a rough, waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens and indoor settings.
