Chiloglossa

Chiloglossa, commonly known as the golden crownbeard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its yellow-orange ray florets and dark brown disk florets. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, oval-shaped fruits called cypsela, which are dispersed by wind.

Chiloglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
