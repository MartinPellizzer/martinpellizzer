Croton holtonii

Croton holtonii, commonly known as the Holton's croton, is a shrub native to the Caribbean islands, particularly Puerto Rico.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes.
