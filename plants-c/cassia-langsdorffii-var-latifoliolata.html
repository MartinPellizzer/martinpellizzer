Cassia langsdorffii var. latifoliolata

Cassia langsdorffii var. latifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved cassia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets and yellow, fluffy inflorescences that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to both landscape design and ecological habitats.
