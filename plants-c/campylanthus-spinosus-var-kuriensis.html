Campylanthus spinosus var. kuriensis

Campylanthus spinosus var. kuriensis, commonly known as the Kuriensis spiny campanula, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its spiny stems and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pale blue.

This variety is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
