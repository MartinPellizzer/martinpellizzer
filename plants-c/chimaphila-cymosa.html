Chimaphila cymosa

Chimaphila cymosa, commonly known as the whorled pipsissewa, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the northeastern United States and eastern Canada.

Its small, bell-shaped flowers are typically pink or red, appearing in late spring to early summer.

Chimaphila cymosa thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded forest environments.
