Carex longshengensis

Carex longshengensis, commonly known as the Longsheng sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and compact inflorescences. It plays a role in local ecosystems by contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for small organisms.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
