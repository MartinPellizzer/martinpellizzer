Calophyllum venulosum var. venulosum

Calophyllum venulosum var. venulosum, commonly known as the veined calophyllum, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale green to reddish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
