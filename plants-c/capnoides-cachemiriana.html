Capnoides cachemiriana

Capnoides cachemiriana, commonly known as the Kashmir lily, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Liliaceae and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky soils and thrives in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stem is often leafless.

Capnoides cachemiriana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
