Cypripedium × janus

Cypripedium × janus, commonly known as the Janus lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its striking, showy flowers that resemble a slipper. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in temperate regions of Asia. Its flowers display a distinctive coloration, with a white or pale pink pouch and contrasting sepals and petals.

Cypripedium × janus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
