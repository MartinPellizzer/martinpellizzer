Coleus galeatus var. borneensis

Coleus galeatus var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean coleus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of red, purple, and green hues. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its visual appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
