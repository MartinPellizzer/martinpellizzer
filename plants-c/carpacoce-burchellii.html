Carpacoce burchellii

Carpacoce burchellii, commonly known as the Burchell's carpetweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, inconspicuous white flowers and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive seed structures.
