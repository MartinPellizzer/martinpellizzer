Crassula microcarpa

Crassula microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf crassula or baby tears plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly bluish tinge. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring season. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula microcarpa is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and ease of care.
