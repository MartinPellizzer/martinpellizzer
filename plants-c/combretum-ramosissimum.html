Combretum ramosissimum

Combretum ramosissimum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
