Crassula brachypetala

Crassula brachypetala, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight blue or purple tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings.
