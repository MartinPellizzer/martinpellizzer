Clinopodium asiaticum

Clinopodium asiaticum, commonly known as Asiatic savory, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant has small, opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pink or purple and are arranged in spikes.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
