Cryptandra vexillifera

Cryptandra vexillifera, commonly known as the yellow wattle, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, woody pods that contain seeds. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Cryptandra vexillifera is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes many legumes.
