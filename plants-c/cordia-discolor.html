Cordia discolor

Cordia discolor, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, featuring a smooth, greyish bark. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, brown seeds.
