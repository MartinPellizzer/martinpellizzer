Campanula hortelensis

Campanula hortelensis, commonly known as the garden bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features upright, slender stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
