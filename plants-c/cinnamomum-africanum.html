Cinnamomum africanum

Cinnamomum africanum, commonly known as African cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the laurel family (Lauraceae) and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and fragrant bark. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by oval, dark blue berries. Its bark and leaves are used in traditional medicine and for their aromatic properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in its native habitats.
