Caladenia cretacea

Caladenia cretacea, commonly known as the Cretaceous Spider-orchid, is an orchid species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance with a single, slender flower that typically blooms in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to cream in color, with a distinctive, elongated labellum.

Caladenia cretacea is a terrestrial orchid that prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.
