Canavalia gladiata var. alba

Canavalia gladiata var. alba, commonly known as the white urd bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain several edible seeds. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

Its botanical features make it a valuable crop in both traditional and modern agricultural systems.
