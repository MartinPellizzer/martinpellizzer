Capnorchis pusilla

Capnorchis pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and forest floors. The plant features a slender, leafless stem with a few narrow leaves at the base. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a faint scent.

Capnorchis pusilla is known for its delicate appearance and is often difficult to spot in its natural habitat.
