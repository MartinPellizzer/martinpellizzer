Caralluma Retrospiciens Var. Acutangula

Coral spine, scientifically known as Caralluma retrospiciens var. acutangula, is a striking desert plant native to the arid regions of India and Pakistan. Its unique appearance, characterized by spiky, fleshy stems and vibrant green coloration, makes it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts. Despite its intimidating look, the plant is non-toxic and thrives in minimal water conditions, making it ideal for xeriscaping. It is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in Ayurvedic practices. This resilient species exemplifies the beauty and adaptability of desert flora.
