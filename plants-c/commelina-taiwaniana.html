Commelina taiwaniana

Commelina taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped with a waxy surface, and it produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and flies.
