Ceropegia albisepta var. viridis

Ceropegia albisepta var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Trumpet Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots and succulent, fleshy stems. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.
