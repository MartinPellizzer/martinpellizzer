Chirita sinensis

Chirita sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese violet or Sinensis violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb with heart-shaped leaves and a compact, bushy habit. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to produce numerous small, showy blooms throughout the growing season.
