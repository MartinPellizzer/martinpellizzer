Clerodendrum silvanum

Clerodendrum silvanum, commonly known as the silky clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance. It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

Clerodendrum silvanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
