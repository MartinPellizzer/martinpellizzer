Coelogyne cornuta

Coelogyne cornuta, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. Coelogyne cornuta thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
