Crassula ramuliflora var. flanaganii

Crassula ramuliflora var. flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
