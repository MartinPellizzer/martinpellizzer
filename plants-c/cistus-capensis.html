Cistus capensis

Cistus capensis, commonly known as the Cape heath or rock rose, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant has grey-green, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to arid and rocky environments, thriving in full sun.

Cistus capensis is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
