Caesalpinia sumatrana

Caesalpinia sumatrana, commonly known as the flame tree or golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red flowers that bloom in long, pendulous clusters. The plant has bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small leaflets, and its woody stems are often covered in thorns. Caesalpinia sumatrana thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
