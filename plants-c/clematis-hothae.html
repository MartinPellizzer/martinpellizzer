Clematis hothae

Clematis hothae, commonly known as the Hothae Clematis, is a climbing vine native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and lavender, often with a distinctive veined pattern. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, hairy hairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis hothae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
