Costus cylindricus var. anachiri

Costus cylindricus var. anachiri, commonly known as the narrow-leaved costus, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.

The species is also used in traditional medicine in some regions for its purported health benefits.
