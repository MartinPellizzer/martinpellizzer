Clarkia speciosa subsp. polyantha

Clarkia speciosa subsp.

polyantha, commonly known as the showy clarkia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsules as its fruiting structure.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
