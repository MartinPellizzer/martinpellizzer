Cephalaria dagestanica

Cephalaria dagestanica, commonly known as the Dagestan cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and mountainous areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink to purple, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
