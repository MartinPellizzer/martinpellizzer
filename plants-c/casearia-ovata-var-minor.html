Casearia ovata var. minor

Casearia ovata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser casearia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
