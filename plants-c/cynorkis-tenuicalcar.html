Cynorkis tenuicalcar

Cynorkis tenuicalcar, commonly known as the narrow-spurred orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with distinctive, narrow spurs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
