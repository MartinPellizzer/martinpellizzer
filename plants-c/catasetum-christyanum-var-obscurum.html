Catasetum christyanum var. obscurum

Catasetum christyanum var. obscurum, commonly known as the dark catasetum, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
