Cybianthus froelichii

Cybianthus froelichii, commonly known as the Froelich's cybianthus, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
