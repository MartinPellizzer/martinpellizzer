Cymbidium lowianum var. superbissimum

Cymbidium lowianum var. superbissimum, commonly known as the superbissimum orchid, is a species native to China.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, dark pink to purple blooms during the winter months. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
