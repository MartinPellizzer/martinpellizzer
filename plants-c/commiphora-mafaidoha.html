Commiphora mafaidoha

Commiphora mafaidoha, commonly known as the Socotra cypress, is a unique plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is a coniferous tree that exhibits a distinctive umbrella-like shape, with long, drooping branches. The plant produces small, scale-like leaves and has a slow growth rate, making it a rare and ancient species. It is adapted to the arid and rocky environment of its native habitat.

The Socotra cypress is also notable for its historical and cultural significance in the region.
