Crinum stuhlmannii subsp. stuhlmannii

Crinum stuhlmannii subsp.

stuhlmannii, commonly known as the Stuhlmann's crinum, is a bulbous plant native to southern Africa. It produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, spicy fragrance.

The plant features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are often broad and pointed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
