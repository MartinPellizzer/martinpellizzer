Citrus medica f. aurantiifolium

Citrus medica f. aurantiifolium, commonly known as the citron lemon or golden leaf citron, is a unique variant of the citron tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive golden-yellow leaves, which give it its common name. The fruit is typically smaller than the standard citron and has a more acidic flavor. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage.

This variety is native to Southeast Asia and is closely related to other citrus species.
