Clitoria javitensis var. grandifolia

Clitoria javitensis var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered winged bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue flowers with distinctive wing-like petals. The plant produces bean-like pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and as a cover crop.
