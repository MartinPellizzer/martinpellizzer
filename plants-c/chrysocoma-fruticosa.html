Chrysocoma fruticosa

Chrysocoma fruticosa, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, spherical berries. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in woodland and scrub habitats.

Chrysocoma fruticosa is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of food for wildlife.
