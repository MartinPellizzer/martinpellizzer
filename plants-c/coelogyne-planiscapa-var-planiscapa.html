Coelogyne planiscapa var. planiscapa

Coelogyne planiscapa var. planiscapa, commonly known as the flat-leafed coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
