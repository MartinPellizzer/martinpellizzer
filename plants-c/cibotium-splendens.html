Cibotium splendens

Cibotium splendens, commonly known as the golden tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive, hollow stem that resembles a tree trunk, and its fronds are typically bright green with a glossy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cibotium splendens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
