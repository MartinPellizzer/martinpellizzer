Cineraria dregeana

Cineraria dregeana, commonly known as the silver leaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage. The plant produces showy blue to violet flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
