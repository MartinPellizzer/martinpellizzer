Castanea Japonica Var. Stricta

Castanea japonica var. stricta is a unique variant of the Japanese chestnut known for its compact growth habit. Native to Japan, this plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its leaves are dark green and oval-shaped, providing a lush appearance throughout the growing season. The plant produces small, edible nuts that are prized for their flavor and nutritional value. Despite its modest size, it holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitat.
