Croton bridgesii

Croton bridgesii, commonly known as the Bridge's croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright yellow to deep red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
