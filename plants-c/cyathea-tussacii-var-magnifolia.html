Cyathea tussacii var. magnifolia

Cyathea tussacii var. magnifolia, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds that can reach impressive lengths. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil. Its fronds are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.

This variety is notable for its rapid growth and ability to form dense foliage, making it a striking addition to tropical landscapes.
