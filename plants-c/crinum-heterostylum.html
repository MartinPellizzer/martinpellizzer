Crinum heterostylum

Crinum heterostylum, commonly known as the broad-leaved crinum, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features large, strap-like leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels, and emit a strong, often unpleasant odor.

Crinum heterostylum is known for its showy blooms and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
