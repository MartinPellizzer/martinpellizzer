Centaurea paniculata var. eupaniculata

Centaurea paniculata var. eupaniculata, commonly known as the European panicled centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind. Native to southern Europe and western Asia, it thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
