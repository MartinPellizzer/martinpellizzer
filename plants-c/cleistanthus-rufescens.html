Cleistanthus rufescens

Cleistanthus rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed cleistanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in dense inflorescences, and are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
