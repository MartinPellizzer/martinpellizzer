Citrosma mutisii

Citrosma mutisii, commonly known as the Malayan citron tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically followed by elliptical, orange-colored fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their aromatic properties.

The tree is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its potential medicinal uses.
