Cereus ackermannii

Cereus ackermannii, commonly known as the Christmas Star, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces striking white flowers that bloom at night, typically during the holiday season. Its stems are covered in spines, and it is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming pattern.
