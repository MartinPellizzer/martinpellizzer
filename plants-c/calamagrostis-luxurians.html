Calamagrostis luxurians

Calamagrostis luxurians, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a tall, ornamental grass native to eastern North America.

It features slender, arching blades that are silver-white on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads in the fall, which add visual interest to the landscape. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Calamagrostis luxurians is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for wildlife.
