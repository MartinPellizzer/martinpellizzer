Cytinus hypocistis subsp. hypocistis

Cytinus hypocistis subsp.

hypocistis, commonly known as the "buttercup bur" or "crown vetch," is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its distinctive, fleshy, swollen base that resembles a cup or a bulb, which is a feature shared with other members of the genus Cytinus.

It is a hemiparasitic plant, meaning it can derive some of its nutrients from the roots of other plants while also performing photosynthesis.
