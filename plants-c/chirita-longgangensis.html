Chirita longgangensis

Chirita longgangensis, commonly known as the Longgang chireta, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan province, where it grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
