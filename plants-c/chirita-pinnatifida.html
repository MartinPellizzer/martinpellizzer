Chirita pinnatifida

Chirita pinnatifida, commonly known as the broad-leaved chirita, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. Chirita pinnatifida is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and easy care requirements.
