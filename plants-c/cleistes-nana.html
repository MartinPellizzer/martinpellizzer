Cleistes nana

Cleistes nana, commonly known as the dwarf orchid or small cleistes, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and wetlands. Cleistes nana has a short, creeping stem with few leaves and produces its flowers in late spring to early summer.

Its botanical features make it a unique and relatively rare species within the orchid family.
