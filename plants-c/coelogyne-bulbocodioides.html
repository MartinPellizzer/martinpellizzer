Coelogyne bulbocodioides

Coelogyne bulbocodioides, commonly known as the bulbocodioides orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in seasonal environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded conditions and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
