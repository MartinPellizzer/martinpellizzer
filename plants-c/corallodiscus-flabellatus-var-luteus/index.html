Corallodiscus Flabellatus Var. Luteus

Corallodiscus flabellatus var. luteus is a rare and striking species of diatom known for its intricate silica shell patterns. This variant exhibits a unique yellowish hue, distinguishing it from other members of the Corallodiscus genus. Found primarily in freshwater environments, it thrives in nutrient-rich, shallow waters with ample sunlight. Its delicate structure makes it a favorite among microscopists and aquatic researchers. Despite its beauty, it remains under-studied, highlighting the need for further exploration of its ecological role.
