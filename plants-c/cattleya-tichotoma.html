Cattleya tichotoma

Cattleya tichotoma, commonly known as the Tichotoma orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya tichotoma is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
