Caletia orientalis var. orbicularis

Caletia orientalis var. orbicularis, commonly known as the oriental caletia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of East Asia.

It features broad, orbicular-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are arranged in a racemose inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its distinctive foliage and delicate floral display.
