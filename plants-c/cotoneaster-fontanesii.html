Cotoneaster fontanesii

Cotoneaster fontanesii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of white, five-petaled flowers in the spring, followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster fontanesii is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
