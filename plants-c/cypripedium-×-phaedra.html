Cypripedium × phaedra

Cypripedium × phaedra, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper, is a striking orchid with a complex botanical structure.

It features a large, showy pouch-shaped lip that is often brightly colored, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has a single upright stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. It produces a cluster of small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered at the top of the stem.

This hybrid is native to eastern Asia and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
