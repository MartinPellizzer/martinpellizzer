Centaurea lucescens

Centaurea lucescens, commonly known as the blushing centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Centaurea lucescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
