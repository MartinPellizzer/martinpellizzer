Crotalaria tenuirostrata

Crotalaria tenuirostrata, commonly known as narrow-beaked crotalaria, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several elliptical seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some traditional medicinal practices.
