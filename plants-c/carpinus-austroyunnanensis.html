Carpinus austroyunnanensis

Carpinus austroyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, spiky fruits that are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions.

Its wood is dense and has been historically used for construction and tool-making.
