Cavendishia davidsei

Cavendishia davidsei, commonly known as the David's Cavendish, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves that form a central cup. The plant produces striking inflorescences with vibrant, showy flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.
