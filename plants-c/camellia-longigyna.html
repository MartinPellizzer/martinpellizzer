Camellia longigyna

Camellia longigyna, commonly known as the long-styled camellia, is a species native to southern China and Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous evergreen shrub characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Camellia longigyna typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural collections.
