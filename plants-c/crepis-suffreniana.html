Crepis suffreniana

Crepis suffreniana, commonly known as the Suffren's hawkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its basal rosette of leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in regions with a Mediterranean climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
