Cyclamen trochopteranthum

Cyclamen trochopteranthum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Iran and Turkey.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant, bowl-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habits.

Cyclamen trochopteranthum is a perennial herb that blooms in winter and early spring.
