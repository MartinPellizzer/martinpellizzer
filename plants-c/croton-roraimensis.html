Croton roraimensis

Croton roraimensis, commonly known as the Roraima croton, is a striking tropical plant native to the dense rainforests of South America, particularly in the region of Roraima.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking members. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Despite its ornamental appeal, it is important to note that some parts of the plant may contain toxic compounds.
