Castanopsis castanicarpa

Castanopsis castanicarpa, commonly known as the Chinese stone oak, is a deciduous evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, chestnuts, and beeches. The tree is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves and produces small, woody acorns that mature over several years. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing a rugged appearance.
