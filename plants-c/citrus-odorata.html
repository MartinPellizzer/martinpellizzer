Citrus odorata

Citrus odorata, commonly known as the citron or sweet lime, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pale yellow fruit, which has a high content of citric acid and essential oils. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers. Its fruit is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its aromatic properties.

Citrus odorata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
