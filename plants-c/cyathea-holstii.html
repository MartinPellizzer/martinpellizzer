Cyathea holstii

Cyathea holstii, commonly known as the Holst's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The fronds are large, pinnately compound, and typically have a dark green color with a glossy appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
