Celsia suworowiana var. papillosum

Celsia suworowiana var. papillosum, commonly known as the papillate celsia, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spiny stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant exhibits a peculiar feature of having papillae—small, nipple-like projections—on its stems, which are believed to aid in water retention.
