Cranzia willdenowii

Cranzia willdenowii, commonly known as the willdenow's cranza, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a hairy texture.
