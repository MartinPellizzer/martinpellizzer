Centaurea subtilis

Centaurea subtilis, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and typically blue or purple, arranged in dense, conical clusters.

Centaurea subtilis is known for its hardy nature and is often used in ornamental gardening.
