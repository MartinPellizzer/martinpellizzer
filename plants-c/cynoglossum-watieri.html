Cynoglossum watieri

Cynoglossum watieri, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to white, often with a distinctive blue or violet hue. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Cynoglossum watieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
