Coronidium

Coronidium, commonly known as the crown flower, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically feature a central disk surrounded by ray florets. These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant blooms.
