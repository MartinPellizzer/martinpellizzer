Coniogramme javanica var. macrophylla

Coniogramme javanica var. macrophylla, commonly known as the Javanese fern or large-leaf coniogramme, is a striking evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, arching fronds that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length, with a feathery appearance. The plant produces bright yellow spore-bearing cones, which are distinctive and add to its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological role in its native habitat.
