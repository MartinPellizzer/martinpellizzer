Cyphia elata var. stenophylla

Cyphia elata var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf cyphia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
