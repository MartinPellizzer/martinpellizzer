Cucurbita pyriformis

Cucurbita pyriformis, commonly known as the horned melon, is a tropical vine native to southern Africa.

It produces large, distinctive fruits with a thick, spiky rind that resembles a horn. The fruit's interior contains a sweet, juicy pulp with edible seeds. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires a long growing season.

It is cultivated for its unique appearance and flavorful, aromatic fruit.
