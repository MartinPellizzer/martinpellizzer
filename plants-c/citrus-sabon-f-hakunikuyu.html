Citrus sabon f. hakunikuyu

Citrus sabon f. hakunikuyu, commonly known as the Hakunikuyu citrus, is a unique hybrid citrus plant native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, round fruits with a thick, waxy peel that is often used in traditional Japanese cuisine. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with fragrant, glossy leaves and clusters of small white flowers. Its fruit is prized for its aromatic zest and is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The botanical features of this plant make it a valuable species in horticultural and agricultural research.
