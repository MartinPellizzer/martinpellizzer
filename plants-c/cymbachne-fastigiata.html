Cymbachne fastigiata

Cymbachne fastigiata, commonly known as the upright bamboo, is a grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cymbachne fastigiata is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover.
