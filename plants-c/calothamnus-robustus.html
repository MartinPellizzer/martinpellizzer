Calothamnus robustus

Calothamnus robustus, commonly known as the robust waratah, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils, often found in heathlands and open forests. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant is known for its hardy, drought-resistant nature.

Calothamnus robustus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
