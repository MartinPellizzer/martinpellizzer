Ceratostema graebnerianum

Ceratostema graebnerianum, commonly known as the silver bell vine, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Ceratostema graebnerianum is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
