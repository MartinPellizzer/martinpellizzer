Coelogyne graminoides

Coelogyne graminoides, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with long, grass-like leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers during the rainy season. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest floors or rocky areas.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
