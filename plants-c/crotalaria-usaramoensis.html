Crotalaria usaramoensis

Crotalaria usaramoensis, commonly known as the Usaramo cowpea, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, often growing in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, and the plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
