Citrosma ruficeps

Citrosma ruficeps, commonly known as the red-headed citrosma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a reddish underside, giving it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by orange-colored berries that contain aromatic seeds.

Citrosma ruficeps is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
