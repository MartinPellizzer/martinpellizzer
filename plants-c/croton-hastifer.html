Croton hastifer

Croton hastifer, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in spikes, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

Croton hastifer requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
