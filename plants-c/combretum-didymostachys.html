Combretum didymostachys

Combretum didymostachys, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames, hence its common name. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
