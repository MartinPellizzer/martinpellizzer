Cymbidium diurnum

Cymbidium diurnum, commonly known as the sun orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in bright yellow or orange hues. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a fan shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cymbidium diurnum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
