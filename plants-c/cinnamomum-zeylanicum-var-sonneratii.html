Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. sonneratii

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. sonneratii, commonly known as Ceylon cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is used to produce cinnamon spice. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval berries. The bark is harvested from the inner layers of the trunk, and it contains essential oils that give it its distinctive scent and flavor.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental qualities and its commercial use in the spice trade.
