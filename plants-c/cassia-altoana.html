Cassia altoana

Cassia altoana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, drooping clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy surface. Its bark is smooth and light brown, often marked with irregular fissures.

Cassia altoana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
