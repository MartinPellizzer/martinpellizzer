Centaurea daralagoezica

Centaurea daralagoezica, commonly known as the Daralagoezica cornflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
