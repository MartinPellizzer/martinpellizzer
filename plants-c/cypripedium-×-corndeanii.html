Cypripedium × corndeanii

Cypripedium × corndeanii, commonly known as the Corndean lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soils. Its botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system and a single upright stem bearing a single flower.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
