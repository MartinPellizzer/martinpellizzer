Clematis mechowiana

Clematis mechowiana, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis mechowiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
