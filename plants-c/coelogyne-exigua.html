Coelogyne exigua

Coelogyne exigua, commonly known as the slender coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne exigua is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
