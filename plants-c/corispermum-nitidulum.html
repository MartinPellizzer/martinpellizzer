Corispermum nitidulum

Corispermum nitidulum, commonly known as the smooth bladderwrack, is a small annual herbaceous plant belonging to the family Chenopodiaceae.

It is characterized by its prostrate growth habit and small, fleshy, bladder-like leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. Its ability to thrive in harsh, saline conditions makes it an important component of these unique ecosystems.
