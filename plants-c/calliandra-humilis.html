Calliandra humilis

Calliandra humilis, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences that produce vibrant pink or red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Calliandra humilis is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
