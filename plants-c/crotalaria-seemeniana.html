Crotalaria seemeniana

Crotalaria seemeniana, commonly known as the African velvetbean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry and semi-arid environments.

Crotalaria seemeniana is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.
