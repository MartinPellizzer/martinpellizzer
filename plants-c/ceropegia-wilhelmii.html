Ceropegia wilhelmii

Ceropegia wilhelmii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to bright light. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
