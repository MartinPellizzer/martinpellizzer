Cattleya mossiae var. mcmorlandii

Cattleya mossiae var. mcmorlandii, commonly known as the McMorland's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and pendulous inflorescences that bear multiple showy flowers. The petals and sepals are typically a combination of pink and white, with a deep pink lip that adds to its ornamental appeal. This variety thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
