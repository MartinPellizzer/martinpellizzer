Clematis forrestii

Clematis forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with a distinctive, showy structure. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis forrestii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
