Cirsium Transsilvanicum

Cirsium transsilvanicum is a striking perennial plant native to parts of Eastern Europe and Asia. Known for its tall, spiky stems and vibrant purple flowers, it adds a dramatic touch to any landscape. This thistle species is often found in meadows and grasslands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts. Despite its beauty, it can be challenging to grow due to its specific environmental needs.
