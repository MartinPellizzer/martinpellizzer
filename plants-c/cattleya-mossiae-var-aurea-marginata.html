Cattleya mossiae var. aurea-marginata

Cattleya mossiae var. aurea-marginata, commonly known as the Aurea-Marginata Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers with distinctive white margins.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features dense, dark green foliage and produces fragrant blooms that typically appear in late spring or early summer. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
