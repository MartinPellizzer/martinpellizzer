Cattleya superba var. ashworthii

Cattleya superba var. ashworthii, commonly known as the Ashworth's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with red or purple markings. The plant features strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
