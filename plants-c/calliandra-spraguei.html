Calliandra spraguei

Calliandra spraguei, commonly known as the red calliandra, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of the Caribbean.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy inflorescences composed of bright red or pink flowers. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various soil types. It also has nitrogen-fixing capabilities, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Calliandra spraguei thrives in full sun and is often used in landscaping and erosion control.
