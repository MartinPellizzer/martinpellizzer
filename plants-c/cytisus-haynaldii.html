Cytisus haynaldii

Cytisus haynaldii, commonly known as the Haynald's broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils, showing a preference for well-drained environments. Its yellow flowers bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its natural habitat.

Cytisus haynaldii is notable for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh, arid conditions.
