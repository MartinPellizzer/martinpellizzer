Cotoneaster microphyllus var. conspicuus

Cotoneaster microphyllus var. conspicuus, commonly known as the showy cotoneaster, is a shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It blooms in late spring with small, white to pink flowers.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in various climates.
