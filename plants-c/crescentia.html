Crescentia

Crescentia, commonly known as the horned melon or moonfruit, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Bombacaceae.

It is characterized by its large, warty, green to purple fruit with a thick, spiky rind. The plant is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it produces showy, yellow flowers that bloom in the evening.

The fruit is edible when ripe, with a sweet, musky flavor and a gelatinous interior.
