Centaurea yaltirikii subsp. yaltirikii

Centaurea yaltirikii subsp.

yaltirikii, commonly known as the Yaltirikii knapweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey. It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The plant features slender, upright stems and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, with a slight hairy texture.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
