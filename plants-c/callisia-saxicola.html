Callisia saxicola

Callisia saxicola, commonly known as the rock ctenanthe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, succulent leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
