Cymbidium wallichii

Cymbidium wallichii, commonly known as the Himalayan orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, which make it a popular ornamental plant. It prefers cool, humid environments and is often grown in shaded areas with well-draining soil.

Cymbidium wallichii is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of garden settings.
