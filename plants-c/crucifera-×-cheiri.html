Crucifera × cheiri

Crucifera × cheiri, commonly known as the yellow rocket, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves, which are typical of many cruciferous species. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in open fields.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it can become invasive in certain regions.
