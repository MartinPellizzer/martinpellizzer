Colchicum bifolium

Colchicum bifolium, commonly known as the two-leaved colchicum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has two large, waxy leaves that are typically broader than they are long. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or woodland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
