Cyrtanthus suaveolens

Cyrtanthus suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented bell-shaped lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The flowers emit a sweet, fragrant aroma, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Cyrtanthus suaveolens is also valued for its ornamental beauty and resilience in mild climates.
