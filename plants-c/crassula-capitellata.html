Crassula capitellata

Crassula capitellata, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, upright growth habit with dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
