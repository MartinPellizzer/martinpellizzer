Cyclamen rarinivum

Cyclamen rarinivum, commonly known as the rare Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It features heart-shaped, velvety foliage with a distinctive silver-green hue, and its flowers are typically pink to purple, with a spurred petal at the base. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained, loamy soil. It is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded garden areas.

Cyclamen rarinivum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
