Cynorkis flexuosa

Cynorkis flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Cynorkis flexuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
