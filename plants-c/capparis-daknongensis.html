Capparis daknongensis

Capparis daknongensis, commonly known as the Daknong glory flower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Capparidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture, and the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Capparis daknongensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
