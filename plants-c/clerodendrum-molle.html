Clerodendrum molle

Clerodendrum molle, commonly known as the soft clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
