Cyperus elapsus

Cyperus elapsus, commonly known as umbrella sedge, is a flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brown flowers. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in wetland habitats.
