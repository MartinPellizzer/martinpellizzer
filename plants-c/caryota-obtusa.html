Caryota obtusa

Caryota obtusa, commonly known as the broad-leafed palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in diameter. Its trunk is short and stout, often covered with a fibrous sheath.
