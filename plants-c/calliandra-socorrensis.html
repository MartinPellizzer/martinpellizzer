Calliandra socorrensis

Calliandra socorrensis, commonly known as Socorro coral tree, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange inflorescences. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its attractive appearance, it is also used in ecological restoration projects.
