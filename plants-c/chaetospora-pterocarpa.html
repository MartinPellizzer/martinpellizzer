Chaetospora pterocarpa

Chaetospora pterocarpa, commonly known as the hairy pod tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its woody structure and compound leaves with small, hairy stipules. The plant produces distinctive pod fruits that are often covered in fine, bristly hairs. It is native to parts of Africa and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.
