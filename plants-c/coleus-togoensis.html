Coleus togoensis

Coleus togoensis, commonly known as Togo coleus, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to West Africa.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, displaying shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
