Crotalaria lukafuensis

Crotalaria lukafuensis, commonly known as the Lukafu crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a bushy growth habit and produces seed pods that contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Crotalaria lukafuensis is notable for its potential medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
