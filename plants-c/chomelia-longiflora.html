Chomelia longiflora

Chomelia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered chomelia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Chomelia longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
