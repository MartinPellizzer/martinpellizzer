Carpinus Chinensis

Carpinus chinensis is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia. It is known for its attractive copper-colored bark and heart-shaped leaves. This species thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas. Its fruit provides food for wildlife, making it an important part of local ecosystems. The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and timber.
