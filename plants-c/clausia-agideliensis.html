Clausia agideliensis

Clausia agideliensis, commonly known as the Agideliensis Clausia, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern South America.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to deep orange. The plant has adapted to its harsh environment with thick, waxy leaves that reduce water loss. Its root system is extensive, allowing it to access deep underground water sources.
