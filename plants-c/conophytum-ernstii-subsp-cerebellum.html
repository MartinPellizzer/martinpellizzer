Conophytum ernstii subsp. cerebellum

Conophytum ernstii subsp.

cerebellum, commonly known as the brain-shaped rock plant, is a succulent native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, flattened, and often lobed leaves that resemble a brain.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.
