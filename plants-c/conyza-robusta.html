Conyza robusta

Conyza robusta, commonly known as roughleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in open areas such as fields, roadsides, and disturbed soils. The plant features hairy, alternate leaves that are often grayish-green in color. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.

Conyza robusta is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
