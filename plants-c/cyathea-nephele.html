Cyathea nephele

Cyathea nephele, commonly known as the cloud fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately divided fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach several meters in height, giving it a lush, tropical appearance. Cyathea nephele thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental value make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
