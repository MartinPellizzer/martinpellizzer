Coreopsis arizonica

Coreopsis arizonica, commonly known as Arizona coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces bright yellow to orange flowers in the summer months. The plant has hairy, narrow leaves and is adapted to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
