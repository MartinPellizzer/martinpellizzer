Chamaenerion angustifolium var. macranthum

Chamaenerion angustifolium var. macranthum, commonly known as the tall fireweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It features upright, branching stems that can grow up to 2 meters tall, covered with glandular hairs. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine, sticky hairs. Its showy, pink to red flowers appear in clusters at the ends of the stems, typically blooming in late summer.

This plant thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in tundra, wetlands, and along roadsides.
