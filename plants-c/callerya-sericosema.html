Callerya sericosema

Callerya sericosema, commonly known as the silky callerya, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its delicate, silky-textured leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The seeds are enclosed in pods and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural distribution in tropical regions.
