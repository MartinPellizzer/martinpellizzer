Cyrtorchis belloneorum

Cyrtorchis belloneorum, commonly known as the Bellone's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru. This species is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that are typically borne on slender stems. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its unique morphology and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
