Ceropegia mixta

Ceropegia mixta, commonly known as the String of Hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
