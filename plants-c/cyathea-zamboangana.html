Cyathea zamboangana

Cyathea zamboangana, commonly known as the Zamboanga tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its tall, woody trunk, which can reach several meters in height.

The fronds are bi-directionally arranged, contributing to its distinctive appearance and ecological role in its native habitat.
