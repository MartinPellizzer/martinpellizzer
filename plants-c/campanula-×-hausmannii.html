Campanula × hausmannii

Campanula × hausmannii, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and features upright, hairy stems. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered with fine hairs.

Native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and partial shade.
