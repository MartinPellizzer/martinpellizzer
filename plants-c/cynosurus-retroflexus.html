Cynosurus retroflexus

Cynosurus retroflexus, commonly known as the bent grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often slightly curled at the edges. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small brown flowers.

Cynosurus retroflexus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
