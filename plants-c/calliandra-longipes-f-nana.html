Calliandra longipes f. nana

Calliandra longipes f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf calliandra, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, upright growth habit and profuse display of bright red, feather-like flowers. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Due to its compact size and vibrant blooms, it is a popular choice for container gardening and small garden spaces.
