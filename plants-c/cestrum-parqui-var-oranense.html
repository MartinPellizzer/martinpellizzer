Cestrum parqui var. oranense

Cestrum parqui var. oranense, commonly known as the Orange Cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a glossy texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aromatic blooms.
