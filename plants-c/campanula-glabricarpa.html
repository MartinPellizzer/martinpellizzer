Campanula glabricarpa

Campanula glabricarpa, commonly known as the smoothstem bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant features smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula glabricarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rocky landscapes.
