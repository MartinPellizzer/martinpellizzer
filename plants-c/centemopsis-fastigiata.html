Centemopsis fastigiata

Centemopsis fastigiata, commonly known as the slender bamboo or narrow-leaved bamboo, is a perennial grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, elongated leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its elegant foliage and ability to form dense clumps.

Centemopsis fastigiata is also valued for its resilience and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
