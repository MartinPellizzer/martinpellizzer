Cyathea incognita

Cyathea incognita, commonly known as the unknown tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant exhibits a distinct rhizome system that supports its growth and helps in nutrient absorption. Cyathea incognita is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and along riverbanks.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental value make it a sought-after plant in horticulture.
