Calandrinia fuegiana

Calandrinia fuegiana, commonly known as the Fuegian portulaca, is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically the southern regions of Chile and Argentina.

It belongs to the family Portulacaceae and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
