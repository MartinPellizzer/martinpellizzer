Conophytum rubrolineatum

Conophytum rubrolineatum, commonly known as the red-line ice plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often covered in a fine, white coating. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the winter months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
