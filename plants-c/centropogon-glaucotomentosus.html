Centropogon glaucotomentosus

Centropogon glaucotomentosus, commonly known as the silky centropogon, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, grayish-blue leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The silky centropogon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
