Cymbidium goeringii f. viridiflorum

Cymbidium goeringii f. viridiflorum, commonly known as the Greenflower Cymbidium, is a unique orchid hybrid originating from Japan.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces striking, upright inflorescences with long, slender flower spikes. Its flowers are notable for their vivid greenish-white color, which sets it apart from other Cymbidium species.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a potted plant.
