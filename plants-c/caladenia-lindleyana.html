Caladenia lindleyana

Caladenia lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open woodlands and heathlands. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange-red, with a distinctive hooded labellum.

Caladenia lindleyana is a protected species due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss.
