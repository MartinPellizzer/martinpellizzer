Croton reitzii

Croton reitzii, commonly known as the Zulu croton, is a tropical shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which features a mix of green, yellow, red, and orange hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are typically palmate, with multiple lobes, and can vary in shape and size.

Croton reitzii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
