Cotoneaster chungtiensis

Cotoneaster chungtiensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in white or pink hues, followed by bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. Cotoneaster chungtiensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal color changes.

It thrives in well-drained soil and is adaptable to a variety of light conditions.
