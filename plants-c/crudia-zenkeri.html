Crudia zenkeri

Crudia zenkeri, commonly known as the Zambesi thornveld, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
