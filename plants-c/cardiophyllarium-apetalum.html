Cardiophyllarium apetalum

Cardiophyllarium apetalum, commonly known as the heart-leaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Apetalaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a compact, shrub-like structure. Its leaves are thick and leathery, with a smooth surface and a deep green color.

While it lacks showy flowers, it is valued for its distinctive foliage and ease of care.
