Callista gratiosissima

Callista gratiosissima, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
