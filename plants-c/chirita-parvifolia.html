Chirita parvifolia

Chirita parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved African violet, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical Africa.

It features rosettes of velvety, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and small, showy flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is widely cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Chirita parvifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
