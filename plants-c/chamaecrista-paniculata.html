Chamaecrista paniculata

Chamaecrista paniculata, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in panicles, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and disturbed sites.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
