Cyperus miliifolius var. saturatus

Cyperus miliifolius var. saturatus, commonly known as the purple flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, making it a common sight in wetlands and along water bodies.

Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in gardens and water features.
