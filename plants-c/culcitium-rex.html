Culcitium rex

Culcitium rex, commonly known as the royal culcitium, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it requires specific care, including high humidity and well-draining soil, to thrive.
