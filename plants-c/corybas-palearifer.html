Corybas palearifer

Corybas palearifer, commonly known as the pale-leaf hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the genus Corybas, which includes a variety of small, often terrestrial orchids with distinctive hood-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent, hood-like structure that protects the reproductive organs.
