Cerasus bungei

Cerasus bungei, commonly known as the Bunge cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, multi-branched structure and produces small, fragrant white flowers in early spring. The plant's leaves are ovate with serrated edges and turn yellow in autumn. Its fruit is a small, yellowish-red cherry that ripens in late summer.

Cerasus bungei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
