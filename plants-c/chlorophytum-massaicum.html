Chlorophytum massaicum

Chlorophytum massaicum, commonly known as the African spear plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are typically green with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by blue or purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its adaptability and ornamental appearance make it a favored plant for both ornamental and medicinal uses.
