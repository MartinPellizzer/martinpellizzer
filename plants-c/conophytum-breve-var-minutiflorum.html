Conophytum breve var. minutiflorum

Conophytum breve var. minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf flowered conophytum, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
