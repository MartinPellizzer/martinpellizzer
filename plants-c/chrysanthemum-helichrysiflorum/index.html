Chrysanthemum Helichrysiflorum

Chrysanthemum helichrysiflorum is a rare and beautiful flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other popular garden plants. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
