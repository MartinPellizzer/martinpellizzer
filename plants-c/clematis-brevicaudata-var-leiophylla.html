Clematis brevicaudata var. leiophylla

Clematis brevicaudata var. leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth-leafed clematis, is a climbing vine native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves with smooth, lance-shaped leaflets. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of blue to purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
