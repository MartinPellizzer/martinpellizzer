Camellia tachangensis

Camellia tachangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with a humid climate and well-drained soil. Its leaves are dark green, leathery, and elliptical in shape.

Camellia tachangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also studied for its potential in botanical research.
