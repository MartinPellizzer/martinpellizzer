Cattleya intermedia var. amethystina

Cattleya intermedia var. amethystina, commonly known as the Amethystine Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical and covered in a dense, velvety leaf sheath.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
