Calophyllum miquelii

Calophyllum miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, fiery red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, spherical fruits that contain a single seed. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy flowers and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.

Calophyllum miquelii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
