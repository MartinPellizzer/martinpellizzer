Caladium holtonianum

Caladium holtonianum, commonly known as the Holton's arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, pink, and white, often with contrasting veins. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Caladium holtonianum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
