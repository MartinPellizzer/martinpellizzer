Colchicum troodi

Colchicum troodi, commonly known as the Turk's Cap lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It produces striking, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a purple or red base. The plant features fibrous roots and a bulbous corm, which stores nutrients for the growing season. Its foliage consists of strap-like leaves that emerge from the base of the plant.

Colchicum troodi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
