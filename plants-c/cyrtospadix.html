Cyrtospadix

Cyrtospadix, commonly known as the spathaceous spadix, is a unique plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive inflorescence, which consists of a spadix surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe. The spadix is often densely packed with tiny flowers, some of which are male and others female, arranged in a specific pattern. This plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shady environments.

Its botanical structure makes it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
