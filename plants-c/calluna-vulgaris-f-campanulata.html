Calluna vulgaris f. campanulata

Calluna vulgaris f. campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped heather, is a variant of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are typically evergreen.

This cultivar is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in acidic, well-drained soils.
