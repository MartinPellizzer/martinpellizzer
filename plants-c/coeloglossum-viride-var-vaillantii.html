Coeloglossum viride var. vaillantii

Coeloglossum viride var. vaillantii, commonly known as the green hawksbeard, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its striking foliage and long-lasting blooms.
