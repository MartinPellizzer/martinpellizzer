Chenopodium superalbum f. kuehnii

Chenopodium superalbum f. kuehnii, commonly known as the silverleaf goosefoot, is a variant of the species Chenopodium superalbum.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence covering the leaves. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its small, greenish-white flowers are arranged in clusters, and it is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
