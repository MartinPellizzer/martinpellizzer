Cyrtotropis

Cyrtotropis, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly in countries like India, China, and Southeast Asia. Cyrtotropis species are characterized by their small, delicate flowers that often have a star-like appearance, hence their common name. They typically grow in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous areas.

The genus includes around 40 species, many of which are prized for their ornamental value in horticulture.
