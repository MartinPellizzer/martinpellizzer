Chamaecytisus calcareus

Chamaecytisus calcareus, commonly known as the limestone broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and dense, thorny growth habit. The plant thrives in calcareous soils, which is reflected in its scientific name. It has small, leathery leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

This species is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
