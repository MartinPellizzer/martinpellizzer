Chlorophytum orchidastrum

Chlorophytum orchidastrum, commonly known as the orchid-tongue plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its striking, orchid-like inflorescence. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in summer.

This succulent is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
