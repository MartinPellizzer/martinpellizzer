Celtis yunnanensis

Celtis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan hackberry, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Ulmaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by spherical, pale yellow fruits. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.

Celtis yunnanensis is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health properties.
