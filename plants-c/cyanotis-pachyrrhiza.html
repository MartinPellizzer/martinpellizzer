Cyanotis pachyrrhiza

Cyanotis pachyrrhiza, commonly known as the thick-stemmed bluebell, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. Cyanotis pachyrrhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
