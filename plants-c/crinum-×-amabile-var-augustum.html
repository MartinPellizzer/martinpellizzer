Crinum × amabile var. augustum

Crinum × amabile var. augustum, commonly known as the Cape jasmine or African lily, is a hybrid bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces large, strap-like leaves that are typically broad and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several months.
