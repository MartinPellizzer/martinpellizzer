Cryptocarya yunnanensis

Cryptocarya yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cryptocarya, is a deciduous evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.
