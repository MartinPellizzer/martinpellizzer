Cyperus melanostachyus var. lorentzianus

Cyperus melanostachyus var. lorentzianus, commonly known as the Lorentzian flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale to brownish flowers. It thrives in wet or moist environments, often found in marshes, swamps, and along waterways.

Its botanical structure is adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic habitats, with rhizomes that help in spreading and stabilizing soil.
