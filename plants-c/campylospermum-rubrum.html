Campylospermum rubrum

Campylospermum rubrum, commonly known as the red campylspermum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, red or pink flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
