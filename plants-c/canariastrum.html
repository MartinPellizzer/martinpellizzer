Canariastrum

Canariastrum, commonly known as the prickly pear cactus, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cactaceae.

It is characterized by its spine-covered stems and clusters of vibrant, showy flowers. These plants are native to the Americas, particularly in arid and semi-arid regions. Canariastrum species are often used for ornamental purposes due to their unique appearance and drought tolerance.
