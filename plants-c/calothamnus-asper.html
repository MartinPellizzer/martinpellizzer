Calothamnus asper

Calothamnus asper, commonly known as the rough calothamnus, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a rough texture. Its flowers are typically bright red or orange, arranged in dense, cylindrical clusters.
