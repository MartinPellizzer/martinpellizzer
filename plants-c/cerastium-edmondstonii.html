Cerastium edmondstonii

Cerastium edmondstonii, commonly known as Edmondston's mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring.
