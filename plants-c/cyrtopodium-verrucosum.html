Cyrtopodium verrucosum

Cyrtopodium verrucosum, commonly known as the疣茎万年青, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant features thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in drier conditions. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-shaped, providing a striking contrast to the delicate flowers.

Cyrtopodium verrucosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
