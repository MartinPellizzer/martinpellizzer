Cnestidium guianense

Cnestidium guianense, commonly known as the Guianese orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly found in Guyana, Suriname, and French Guiana.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant has a slender, upright stem with several elongated, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and white, often arranged in a loose cluster, and are characterized by their delicate, intricate structure.

Cnestidium guianense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
