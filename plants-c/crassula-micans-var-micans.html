Crassula micans var. micans

Crassula micans var. micans, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.

The species is also valued for its ability to store water in its leaves, allowing it to survive in harsh conditions.
