Crassula capensis var. capensis

Crassula capensis var. capensis, commonly known as the Fat Plant or Jade Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating, adapted for water storage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
