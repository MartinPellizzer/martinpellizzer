Coleus reveesii

Coleus reveesii, commonly known as the Blue Mound coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its striking blue and purple foliage, which adds visual interest to gardens. It typically grows as a low-growing shrub with square stems and small, tubular flowers.

Coleus reveesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in partial shade with well-drained soil.
