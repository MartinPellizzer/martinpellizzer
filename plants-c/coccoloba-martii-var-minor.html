Coccoloba martii var. minor

Coccoloba martii var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf coccoloba, is a small shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

Due to its ornamental appeal and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
