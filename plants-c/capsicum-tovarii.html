Capsicum tovarii

Capsicum tovarii, commonly known as the Tovar's pepper, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Mexico, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, round fruits that range in color from yellow to red, depending on maturity. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a rough texture and dark green color.

Capsicum tovarii is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild flavor, making it suitable for both culinary and garden use.
