Coronanthera deltoidifolia

Coronanthera deltoidifolia, commonly known as the diamond-leaved orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, diamond-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color.

This species is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and compact growth habit.
