Centaurea ultreiae var. bazarensis

Centaurea ultreiae var. bazarensis, commonly known as the Bazarenian Blazing Star, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a cluster of small flowers. The plant features striking blue to purple ray florets surrounded by yellow disk florets, giving it a distinctive appearance. It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
