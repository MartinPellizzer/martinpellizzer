Chenopodium dacoticum

Chenopodium dacoticum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and small, greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas. Its seeds are often dispersed by wind, and the plant is sometimes considered a weed.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
