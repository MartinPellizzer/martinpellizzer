Chrysanthemum cilicium

Chrysanthemum cilicium, commonly known as the Cilician chrysanthemum, is a species native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
