Cymbidium lowianum var. changningense

Cymbidium lowianum var. changningense, commonly known as the Changning orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with several large, lance-shaped petals and a distinctive, recurved lip. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance in traditional Chinese gardens.
