Centaurea attica var. pateraea

Centaurea attica var. pateraea, commonly known as the Attic centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in rocky soils and dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments.

Centaurea attica var. pateraea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
