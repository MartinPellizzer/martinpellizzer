Cattleya loddigesii var. maculata

Cattleya loddigesii var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking, fragrant flowers.

It features large, velvety petals and a prominent, spidery lip that adds to its ornamental appeal. Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.

Its unique spotted pattern on the petals makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
