Caladenia wanosa

Caladenia wanosa, commonly known as the Red-tongued Spider-orchid, is an endemic species of the Australian flora.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive red and yellow floral structure. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are pollinated by specific insect species, contributing to its ecological significance.

Caladenia wanosa is a protected species due to habitat loss and its limited distribution.
