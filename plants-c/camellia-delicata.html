Camellia delicata

Camellia delicata, commonly known as the delicate camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Camellia delicata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for temperate climates.
