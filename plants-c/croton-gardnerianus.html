Croton gardnerianus

Croton gardnerianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that can vary in shape and hue depending on the variety. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and riverine forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses worldwide.
