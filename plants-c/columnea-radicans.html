Columnea radicans

Columnea radicans, commonly known as the trailing columnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, tubular flowers. Columnea radicans is often cultivated as a houseplant or in hanging baskets due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
