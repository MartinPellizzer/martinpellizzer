Columnea scandens f. montserratensis

Columnea scandens f. montserratensis, commonly known as the Montserrat flame flower, is a vigorous climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that are highly attractive to hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are opposite in arrangement along its stems. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is often cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
