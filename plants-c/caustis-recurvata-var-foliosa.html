Caustis recurvata var. foliosa

Caustis recurvata var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy caustis, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are often clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is adapted to grow in sandy or rocky soils and prefers full sun.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and compact growth habit.
