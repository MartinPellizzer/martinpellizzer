Caltha nirbisia

Caltha nirbisia, commonly known as the narrowleaf marsh marigold, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to wetland habitats in northern regions.

It features showy, yellow flowers with prominent yellow stamens that bloom in early spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often submerged or floating in shallow water. It thrives in moist to aquatic environments and is often found in marshes, stream banks, and pond edges.

Caltha nirbisia plays an important role in supporting pollinators and maintaining wetland biodiversity.
