Cystopteris fragilis var. diaphana

Cystopteris fragilis var. diaphana, commonly known as the fragrant water fern, is a delicate fern species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, delicate fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are usually bright green and may develop a slight bluish tint under certain conditions.

The species is noted for its fragrant scent when crushed, which is a distinguishing feature.
