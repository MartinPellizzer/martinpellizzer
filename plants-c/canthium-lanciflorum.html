Canthium lanciflorum

Canthium lanciflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. Its stems are often covered in a milky sap, which is characteristic of the spurge family.

Canthium lanciflorum is typically found in wetland or marshy habitats, where it thrives in moist, sunny conditions.
