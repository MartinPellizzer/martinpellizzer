Centaurea praetoria

Centaurea praetoria, commonly known as the Pretoria star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow or orange flowers, which are typically surrounded by a ring of bracts. The plant grows in a variety of habitats, including grasslands and rocky soils, and is often found in open areas with full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Centaurea praetoria is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
