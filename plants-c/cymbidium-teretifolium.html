Cymbidium teretifolium

Cymbidium teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a prominent white lip, and they bloom in late winter to early spring.

Cymbidium teretifolium is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
