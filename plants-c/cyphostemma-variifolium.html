Cyphostemma variifolium

Cyphostemma variifolium, commonly known as the variable leaf cyphostemma, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often lobed leaves that vary in shape and size across different parts of the plant. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Cyphostemma variifolium is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
