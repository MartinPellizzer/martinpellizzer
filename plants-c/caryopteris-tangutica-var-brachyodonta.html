Caryopteris tangutica var. brachyodonta

Caryopteris tangutica var. brachyodonta, commonly known as the Chinese bluemouth, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer. It is well adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and hardy nature in garden settings.
