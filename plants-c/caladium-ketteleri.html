Caladium ketteleri

Caladium ketteleri, commonly known as the Ketteler's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped with a pointed tip, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium ketteleri prefers warm, humid conditions and well-draining soil to thrive.
