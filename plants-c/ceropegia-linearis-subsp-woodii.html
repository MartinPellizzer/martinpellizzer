Ceropegia linearis subsp. woodii

Ceropegia linearis subsp.

woodii, commonly known as the Wood's string fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features trailing stems with small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color.

The plant produces unique, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple, attracting specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
