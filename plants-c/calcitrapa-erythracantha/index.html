Calcitrapa Erythracantha

Redstem grass, scientifically known as Calcitrapa erythracantha, is a hardy perennial plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States. It is characterized by its vibrant red stems and small, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in harsh, dry environments. This plant plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife in its native habitat. Despite its resilience, Redstem grass is often overlooked for its ecological value and aesthetic appeal. Its unique features make it a fascinating subject for botanists and land managers alike.
