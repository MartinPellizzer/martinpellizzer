Conophytum saxetanum f. hallianum

Conophytum saxetanum f. hallianum, commonly known as the Hallian's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
