Croton vernicosus

Croton vernicosus, commonly known as the red croton, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens.
