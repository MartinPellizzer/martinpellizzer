Cyperus Aureus Var. Aurantiacus

Cyperus aureus var. aurantiacus is a striking plant known for its vibrant orange flowers and grass-like foliage. It belongs to the sedge family and is often found in wetland environments. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist soils. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for gardeners and landscapers. Despite its beauty, it requires careful maintenance to prevent overgrowth and maintain its aesthetic appeal.
