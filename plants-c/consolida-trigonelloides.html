Consolida trigonelloides

Consolida trigonelloides, commonly known as the yellow rocket or lesser celandine, is a member of the Fabaceae family.

It is a biennial or short-lived perennial plant that produces bright yellow flowers in early spring. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or along stream banks.

Its seed pods are cylindrical and split open to release small, kidney-shaped seeds.
