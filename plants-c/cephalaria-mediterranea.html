Cephalaria mediterranea

Cephalaria mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features upright stems and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is often found in open habitats such as slopes and wastelands.
