Chamaerops kwanwortsik

Chamaerops kwanwortsik, commonly known as the Chinese fan palm, is a species of palm native to southern China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, ornamental palm with a slender trunk and a crown of feathery, fan-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and landscapes. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by black, berry-like fruits.

The leaves are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a source of fiber.
