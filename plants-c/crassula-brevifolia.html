Crassula brevifolia

Crassula brevifolia, commonly known as the Fat Plant or Dwarf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Crassula brevifolia is popular as a houseplant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
