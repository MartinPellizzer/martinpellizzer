Callistemon sabrina

Callistemon sabrina, commonly known as the pink bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical flower spikes that are typically pink or red in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are clustered in dense, brush-like formations. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.

Callistemon sabrina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract birds and pollinators.
