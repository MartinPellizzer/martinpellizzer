Carex retrorsa var. hartii

Carex retrorsa var. hartii, commonly known as Hart's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its grass-like leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale yellow to greenish flowers in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Hart's sedge is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for various wildlife species.
