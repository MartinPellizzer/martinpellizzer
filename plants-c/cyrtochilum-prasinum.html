Cyrtochilum prasinum

Cyrtochilum prasinum, commonly known as the green orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America.

This epiphytic plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically growing on trees or rocks. It has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its unique root system, which helps it absorb moisture and nutrients from its surroundings.

Cyrtochilum prasinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
