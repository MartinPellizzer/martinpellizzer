Crataegus nigra f. prodanii

Crataegus nigra f. prodanii, commonly known as the blackthorn form prodanii, is a botanical variant of the blackthorn species.

It is a shrub characterized by its dark, thorny branches and small, oval leaves. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers in early spring, followed by small, dark blue berries in the fall. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in woodland edges and hedgerows.

The foliage and fruit are important food sources for various birds and insects.
