Crocodilodes umbellata

Crocodilodes umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive fronds that resemble an umbrella.

The plant features a short, creeping rhizome from which the fronds emerge in a clustered arrangement. This unique adaptation makes it a fascinating subject in botanical studies.
