Cymbidium viridiflorum

Cymbidium viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces tall, branched inflorescences with numerous showy, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are strap-like and long, providing a lush backdrop to its floral display.

Cymbidium viridiflorum is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and adaptability to cultivation.
