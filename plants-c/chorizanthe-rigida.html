Chorizanthe rigida

Chorizanthe rigida, commonly known as rigid jointweed, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Chorizanthe rigida is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a resilient plant in desert ecosystems.
