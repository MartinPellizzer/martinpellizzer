Chaetaria royleana

Chaetaria royleana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose or Himalayan rose, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows in rocky and alpine environments. The plant produces showy pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Chaetaria royleana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
