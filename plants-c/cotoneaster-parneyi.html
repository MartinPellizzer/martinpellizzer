Cotoneaster parneyi

Cotoneaster parneyi, commonly known as the Parney's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

Cotoneaster parneyi is also valued for its adaptability and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
