Calyptrocarya brevicaulis

Calyptrocarya brevicaulis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved calyptrocarya, is a rare and unique plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its seeds are enclosed in a hard, woody capsule, which splits open to release the seeds when mature.
