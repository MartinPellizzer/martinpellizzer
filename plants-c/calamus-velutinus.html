Calamus velutinus

Calamus velutinus, commonly known as the velvet reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. Calamus velutinus produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional uses.
