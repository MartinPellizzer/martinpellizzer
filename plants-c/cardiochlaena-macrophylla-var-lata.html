Cardiochlaena macrophylla var. lata

Cardiochlaena macrophylla var. lata, commonly known as the broad-leafed heartleaf, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically broad and glossy, with a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shade gardens and woodland landscapes.
