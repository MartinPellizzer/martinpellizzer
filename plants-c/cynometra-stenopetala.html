Cynometra stenopetala

Cynometra stenopetala, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
