Canna lancifolia

Canna lancifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf canna, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces vibrant orange or red flowers that bloom in summer, adding a bold visual impact to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Canna lancifolia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
