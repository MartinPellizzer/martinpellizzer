Cattleya Duveenii

Cattleya duveenii is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of Costa Rica. Known for its large, vibrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This species is highly prized for its beauty and is often sought after in the horticultural trade. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its cultivation requires specific care, making it a rewarding yet demanding plant for growers.
