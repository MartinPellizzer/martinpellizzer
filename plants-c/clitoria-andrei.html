Clitoria andrei

Clitoria andrei, commonly known as the purple orchid bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers, which are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Clitoria andrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and serrated.
