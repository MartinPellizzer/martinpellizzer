Crepis sodiroi

Crepis sodiroi, commonly known as the Sierra crepis, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky soils, thriving in cool and temperate climates. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and clustered in a head.
