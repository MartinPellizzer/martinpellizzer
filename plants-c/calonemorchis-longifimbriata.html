Calonemorchis longifimbriata

Calonemorchis longifimbriata, commonly known as the long-fringed calonemorchis, is a unique species within the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers that display a complex arrangement of petals and sepals. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as tropical forests, where it thrives in the understory. Its leaves are usually elongated and possess a waxy texture, aiding in water retention.
