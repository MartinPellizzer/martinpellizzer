Crepidium umbonatum

Crepidium umbonatum, commonly known as the cushion orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant has a compact growth habit, with a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that store water. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive hooded structure on the dorsal sepal.

It is found in various regions of South America, particularly in the Andes.
