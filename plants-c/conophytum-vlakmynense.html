Conophytum vlakmynense

Conophytum vlakmynense, commonly known as the Vlok's rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the cooler months. It typically grows in rocky habitats and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a distinctive addition to succulent collections.
