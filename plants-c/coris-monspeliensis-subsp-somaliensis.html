Coris monspeliensis subsp. somaliensis

Coris monspeliensis subsp.

somaliensis, commonly known as the Somali coris, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia and Ethiopia. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, succulent stems.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It is adapted to withstand drought conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.

Its botanical structure supports its survival in harsh, semi-desert environments.
