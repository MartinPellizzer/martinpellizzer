Cynorkis ridleyi

Cynorkis ridleyi, commonly known as the Malabar orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
