Cneoridium Dumosum

Cneoridium dumosum is a hardy shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of central and southern Asia. It is known for its dense, bushy growth and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions. This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits. Its resilience makes it an important species for ecological restoration projects. Despite its usefulness, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
