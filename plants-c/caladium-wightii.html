Caladium wightii

Caladium wightii, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial belonging to the Araceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, brightly colored leaves that come in shades of green, red, and white. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its foliage. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shady environments with well-draining soil.

Caladium wightii is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and ease of care.
