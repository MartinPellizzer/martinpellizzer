Chamaecrista polytricha var. pauciflora

Chamaecrista polytricha var. pauciflora, commonly known as the slender crown flower, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features slender, erect stems covered in soft, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, yellow pea-like flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small, oval leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
