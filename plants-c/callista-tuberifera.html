Callista tuberifera

Callista tuberifera, commonly known as the tuberous callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces tuberous roots that serve as storage organs, enabling it to survive in dry conditions. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, while its flowers range in color from white to yellow.

Callista tuberifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
