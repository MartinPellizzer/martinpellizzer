Cryptocoryne tambraparaniana

Cryptocoryne tambraparaniana, commonly known as the Bornean cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of Borneo.

It is characterized by its dense, bushy growth and dark green, elongated leaves that often have a waxy texture. This plant thrives in low-light conditions and is well-suited for aquariums, where it can form a lush underwater carpet. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, anchoring the plant in the substrate and facilitating nutrient absorption.

Cryptocoryne tambraparaniana is also valued for its ability to improve water quality by absorbing excess nutrients.
