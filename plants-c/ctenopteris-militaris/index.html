Ctenopteris Militaris

Militaris brake, scientifically known as Ctenopteris militaris, is a unique fern species native to Southeast Asia. This plant is characterized by its distinctive fronds that display a striking contrast between light and dark green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests. The name "militaris" refers to the plant's ability to withstand harsh conditions, much like a soldier. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is increasingly sought after in horticulture and conservation efforts.
