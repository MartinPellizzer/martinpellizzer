Cochliostema jacobianum var. variegatum

Cochliostema jacobianum var. variegatum, commonly known as the variegated Jacobian cochliostema, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking variegated foliage with white and green patterns. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
