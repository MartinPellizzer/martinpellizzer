Crocanthemum propinquum

Crocanthemum propinquum, commonly known as the common crocanthemum, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Crocanthemum propinquum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
