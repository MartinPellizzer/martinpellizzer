Cymbidium roseum

Cymbidium roseum, commonly known as the rose orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically blooms in winter or early spring, making it a popular choice for holiday decorations. Its leaves are long and strap-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base of the plant.

Cymbidium roseum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
