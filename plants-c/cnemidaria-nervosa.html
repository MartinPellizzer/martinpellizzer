Cnemidaria nervosa

Cnemidaria nervosa, commonly known as the slender nettle, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Urticaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems covered in fine hairs and its alternate, ovate leaves with prominent veins. Cnemidaria nervosa is known for its stinging hairs, which can cause skin irritation upon contact.
