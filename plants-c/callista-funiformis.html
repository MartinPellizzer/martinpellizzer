Callista funiformis

Callista funiformis, commonly known as the spiral rush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in wetland environments and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of water bodies.

Callista funiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with moist soil conditions.
