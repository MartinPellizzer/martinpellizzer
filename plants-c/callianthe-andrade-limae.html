Callianthe andrade-limae

Callianthe andrade-limae, commonly known as the Andrade-Limae orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a complex arrangement of sepals and petals.

Callianthe andrade-limae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated by horticulturists for its aesthetic appeal.
