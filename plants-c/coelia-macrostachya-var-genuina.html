Coelia macrostachya var. genuina

Coelia macrostachya var. genuina, commonly known as the giant African violet, is a striking ornamental plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves and showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its foliage and flowers make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
