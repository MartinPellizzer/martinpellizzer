Crinum harmsii

Crinum harmsii, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its robust, fleshy bulbs that store water and nutrients. Crinum harmsii thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which can last several weeks.
