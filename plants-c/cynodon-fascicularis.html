Cynodon fascicularis

Cynodon fascicularis, commonly known as Bermuda grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and its ability to thrive in a wide range of soil types. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is highly drought-resistant and often used for lawns, sports fields, and erosion control.

Cynodon fascicularis is also valued for its rapid growth and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
