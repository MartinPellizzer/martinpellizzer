Cryptocoryne bullosa

Cryptocoryne bullosa, commonly known as the balloon cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, bulbous leaves that resemble floating blisters. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and rivers. Its leaves are typically bright green and can grow up to 15 centimeters in diameter.

Cryptocoryne bullosa is popular in aquariums due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
