Coleus scebeli

Coleus scebeli, commonly known as the silver leaf coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, featuring shades of silver, white, and green. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

Coleus scebeli is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
