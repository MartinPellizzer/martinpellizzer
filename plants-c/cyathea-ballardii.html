Cyathea ballardii

Cyathea ballardii, commonly known as the Ballard's tree fern, is a tropical fern native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its tall, trunk-like stem covered in a fibrous sheath, which gives it a tree-like appearance. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. Cyathea ballardii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique structure and growth habit make it an important species in tropical ecosystems.
