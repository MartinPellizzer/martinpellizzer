Crassula scheppigiana

Crassula scheppigiana, commonly known as the Scheppig's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, bluish-green leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
