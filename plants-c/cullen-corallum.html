Cullen corallum

Cullen corallum, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with woody stems and leathery, elliptical leaves. It blooms in spring and summer, producing bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
