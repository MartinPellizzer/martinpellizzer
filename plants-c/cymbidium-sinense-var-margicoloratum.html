Cymbidium sinense var. margicoloratum

Cymbidium sinense var. margicoloratum, commonly known as the Margicolored Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to China.

It features tall, gracefully arching inflorescences that bear clusters of large, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The flowers typically display a combination of white, pink, and purple hues, with a distinctive marginal stripe that gives it its name. This orchid thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty and fragrance.
