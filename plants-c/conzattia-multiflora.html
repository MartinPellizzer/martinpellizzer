Conzattia multiflora

Conzattia multiflora, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Conzattia multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
