Coelogyne rigida

Coelogyne rigida, commonly known as the rigid orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a distinctive, rigid appearance due to its thick, fleshy leaves and sturdy pseudobulbs. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne rigida is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
