Calathea cardiophylla

Calathea cardiophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of silver and green. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its foliage is known for its velvety texture, which adds to its ornamental appeal.

Calathea cardiophylla is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
