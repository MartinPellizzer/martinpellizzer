Conyza odora

Conyza odora, commonly known as sweet everlasting, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides, thriving in a variety of environmental conditions. Its leaves are hairy and aromatic, releasing a sweet scent when crushed.

Conyza odora is also known for its ability to reseed itself readily, making it a common weed in many regions.
