Cotula longipes

Cotula longipes, commonly known as the longipes daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant is characterized by its small, white to yellow flowers arranged in clusters, with narrow, hairy leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and rocky areas.

Cotula longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
