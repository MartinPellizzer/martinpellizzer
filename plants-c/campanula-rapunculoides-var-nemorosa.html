Campanula rapunculoides var. nemorosa

Campanula rapunculoides var. nemorosa, commonly known as the wood campanula, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in woodland areas and prefers partial shade with moist, well-drained soil.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to attract pollinators.
