Callitris cupressoides

Callitris cupressoides, commonly known as the white cypress-pine, is a species of coniferous tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and its distinctive, aromatic wood. The plant produces cones that contain seeds, and it is often found in dry, sandy soils. It has a long lifespan and can grow in various climatic conditions, making it a resilient species.

Its botanical features make it an important component of many Australian ecosystems.
