Caladium belleymei

Caladium belleymei, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins contrasting against a bright green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is typically grown as an ornamental foliage plant. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Caladium belleymei is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor spaces.
