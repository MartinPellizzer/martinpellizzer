Clematis nannophylla var. foliosa

Clematis nannophylla var. foliosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved clematis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, delicate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological landscapes.
