Cyclamen oedirrhizum

Cyclamen oedirrhizum, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Persia and Afghanistan.

It features heart-shaped, hairy leaves and produces vibrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its tuberous roots store nutrients, allowing it to survive in harsh conditions.

Cyclamen oedirrhizum is well-suited for shady garden areas and indoor cultivation.
