Cyphostemma niveum

Cyphostemma niveum, commonly known as the white cyphostemma, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, often pale green to white stems and its alternate, simple, ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Cyphostemma niveum is also known for its use in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance.
