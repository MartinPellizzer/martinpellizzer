Cattleya isopetala

Cattleya isopetala, commonly known as the "Island Lady," is a tropical orchid native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal climates.

Cattleya isopetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
