Cucumis flexuosus var. curvatoaurantiacus

Cucumis flexuosus var. curvatoaurantiacus, commonly known as the wavy gourd or horned cucumber, is a unique variant of the genus Cucumis.

It is characterized by its long, serpentine fruits that curve and twist, often resembling a horn or a spiral. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has a distinct, aromatic scent. Its leaves are broad and lobed, with a hairy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unusual appearance and is also used in some traditional cuisines.
