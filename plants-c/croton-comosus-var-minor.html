Croton comosus var. minor

Croton comosus var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with a distinctive yellow-green coloration and prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in garden settings.
