Caladium longipodum

Caladium longipodum, commonly known as the long-podded arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that can be brightly colored in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant appearance.

Caladium longipodum prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
