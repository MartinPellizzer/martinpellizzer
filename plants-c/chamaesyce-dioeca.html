Chamaesyce dioeca

Chamaesyce dioeca, commonly known as the yellow sandwort, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually alternate and linear, with a rough, textured surface.

Chamaesyce dioeca is often found in dry, open habitats such as dunes, roadsides, and disturbed areas.
