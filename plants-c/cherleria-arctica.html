Cherleria arctica

Cherleria arctica, commonly known as Arctic willow, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing shrub that thrives in harsh, cold environments with limited sunlight. The plant has small, narrow leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It is adapted to permafrost conditions and is often found in tundra ecosystems.

Cherleria arctica plays a role in providing food for local wildlife and contributes to the ecological balance of its habitat.
