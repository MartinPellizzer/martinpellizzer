Crinum × superbum

Crinum × superbum, commonly known as the giant lily or grandiflorum lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. Its leaves are broad, strap-like, and often have a waxy texture.

The botanical features of Crinum × superbum make it a striking ornamental plant, valued for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
