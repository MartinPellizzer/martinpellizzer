Chenopodium quinoa var. rubescens

Chenopodium quinoa var. rubescens, commonly known as red quinoa, is a variety of the Andean plant quinoa.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish hue, which develops as the seeds mature. This variety is cultivated for its nutrient-rich seeds, which are high in protein and essential amino acids. The plant thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its botanical features include feather-like leaves and small, green flowers that yield the edible seeds.
