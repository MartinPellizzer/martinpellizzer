Chenopodium oahuense subsp. ilioensis

ilioensis, commonly known as Oahu spinach, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, edible leaves that are often used in traditional Hawaiian cuisine.
