Cotoneaster zangezuricus

Cotoneaster zangezuricus, commonly known as the Zangezur cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It typically grows in mountainous areas with rocky soils, preferring temperate climates. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are pink or white, appearing in clusters, followed by red or orange berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and seasonal color display.
