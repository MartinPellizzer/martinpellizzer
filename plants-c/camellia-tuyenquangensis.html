Camellia tuyenquangensis

Camellia tuyenquangensis, commonly known as Tuyen Quang camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the Theaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, camellia-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in winter, producing pink to red flowers that are highly ornamental. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
