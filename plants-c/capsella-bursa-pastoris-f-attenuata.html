Capsella bursa-pastoris f. attenuata

Capsella bursa-pastoris f. attenuata, commonly known as the slender shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae, characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is found in temperate regions. It has a low-growing habit with lobed leaves and small white flowers.

This form is distinguished by its more elongated and attenuated seed pods compared to the typical variety.
