Calathea utilis

Calathea utilis, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and silver. The plant is known for its unique leaf movement, where the leaves close at night and open in the morning, resembling prayer. Calathea utilis thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
