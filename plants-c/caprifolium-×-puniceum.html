Caprifolium × puniceum

Caprifolium × puniceum, commonly known as the scarlet honeysuckle, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between *Lonicera periclymenum* and *Lonicera tatarica*.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a deciduous shrub, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
