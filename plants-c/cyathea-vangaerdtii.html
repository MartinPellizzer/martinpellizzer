Cyathea vangaerdtii

Cyathea vangaerdtii, commonly known as the Vangaerdt's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, and the plant can grow to a height of several meters.

Cyathea vangaerdtii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
