Chrysophyllum sundaicum

Chrysophyllum sundaicum, commonly known as the Sunda cherry, is a small evergreen tree native to the Indonesian archipelago.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. The tree is also appreciated for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
