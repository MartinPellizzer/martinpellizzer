Chlorophytum gramineum var. typicum

Chlorophytum gramineum var. typicum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically bright green with white stripes, and it produces small, white, star-shaped flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It also has the ability to propagate through offsets, which are small plantlets that grow from the base of the mother plant.

This versatility and aesthetic appeal contribute to its widespread cultivation.
