Crassula corpusculariopsis

Crassula corpusculariopsis, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish or reddish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive choice for rock gardens and container planting.
