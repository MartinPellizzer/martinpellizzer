Cheiranthus × cheiri var. hortensis

Cheiranthus × cheiri var. hortensis, commonly known as the garden wallflower, is a hybrid plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically produces yellow, orange, or red petals, often with a distinctive dark center. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
