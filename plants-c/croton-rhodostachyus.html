Croton rhodostachyus

Croton rhodostachyus, commonly known as the red-stalked croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its stems are thick and often have a reddish hue, which contributes to its ornamental appeal.

Croton rhodostachyus is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
