Commelina kurzii

Commelina kurzii, commonly known as the Kurz's spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Commelina genus within the family Commelinaceae. The plant features striking blue or violet flowers with white markings, which are clustered in umbel-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped and have a waxy surface.

Commelina kurzii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
