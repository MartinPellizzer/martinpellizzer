Cirsium vallis-demonii

Cirsium vallis-demonii, commonly known as the demon's valley thistle, is a perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its height can range from 1 to 3 meters, making it a striking presence in its natural habitat.

The species is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.
