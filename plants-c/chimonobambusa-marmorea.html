Chimonobambusa marmorea

Chimonobambusa marmorea, commonly known as the pink bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Bambusoideae subfamily.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. The plant is characterized by its striking pinkish-purple culms, which are often adorned with a waxy coating. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a pale green color.

This ornamental bamboo is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic value.
