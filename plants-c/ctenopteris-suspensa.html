Ctenopteris suspensa

Ctenopteris suspensa, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, pinnate fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with a creeping underground stem that produces both roots and new shoots. Its fronds are typically bioluminescent, a unique feature that helps in attracting pollinators.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
