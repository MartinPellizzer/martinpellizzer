Campanula rotundifolia var. reflexa

Campanula rotundifolia var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features distinctive bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that hang downward, giving it a unique appearance. The leaves are rounded and often have a slightly hairy texture, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
