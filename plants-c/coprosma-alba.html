Coprosma alba

Coprosma alba, commonly known as silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically a silvery-gray color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers that bloom in spring. These flowers are followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds.

Coprosma alba is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
