Clerodendrum philippinense

Clerodendrum philippinense, commonly known as the Philippine flame bush, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange tubular flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Clerodendrum philippinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
