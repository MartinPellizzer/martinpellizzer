Chamaecrista serpens var. grandiflora

Chamaecrista serpens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, often spiny pods. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
