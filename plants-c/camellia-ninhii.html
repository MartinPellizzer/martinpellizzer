Camellia ninhii

Camellia ninhii, commonly known as the Ninhui camellia, is a species native to China.

It is a shrub that produces striking, double flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. Camellia ninhii thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also appreciated for its cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
