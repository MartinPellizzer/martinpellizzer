Croton isomonensis

Croton isomonensis, commonly known as the velvet croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that range in color from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow. Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
