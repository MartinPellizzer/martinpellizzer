Coelogyne assamica

Coelogyne assamica, commonly known as the Assam orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, often in clusters of 5 to 10 flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne assamica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in both home and greenhouse settings.
