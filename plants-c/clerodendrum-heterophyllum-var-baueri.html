Clerodendrum heterophyllum var. baueri

Clerodendrum heterophyllum var. baueri, commonly known as the Bauer's flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often attracting pollinators like birds and insects. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and emits a mild fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it suitable for both garden landscapes and container planting.
