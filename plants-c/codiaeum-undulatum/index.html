Codiaeum Undulatum

Codiaeum undulatum, also known as the croton plant, is a vibrant and striking member of the Euphorbiaceae family. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it is celebrated for its colorful, waxy leaves that come in a wide range of hues. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners due to its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light and its low maintenance requirements. Its unique foliage makes it a standout addition to any living space or garden. With proper care, Codiaeum undulatum can flourish and bring a burst of color to any environment.
