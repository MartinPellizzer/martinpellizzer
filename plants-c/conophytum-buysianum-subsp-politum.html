Conophytum buysianum subsp. politum

Conophytum buysianum subsp.

politum, commonly known as the "Polished Conophytum," is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, stone-like leaves that store water, adapting to arid environments.

The leaves are typically smooth and polished, giving the plant its common name. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.
