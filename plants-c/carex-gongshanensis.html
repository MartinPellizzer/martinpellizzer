Carex gongshanensis

Carex gongshanensis, commonly known as Gongshan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream sides. The plant has grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
