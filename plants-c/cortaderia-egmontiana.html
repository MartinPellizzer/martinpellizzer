Cortaderia egmontiana

Cortaderia egmontiana, commonly known as the Egmont grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, arching blades that can grow up to 6 feet in height, with a feathery, silver-blue appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for temperate climates. Its foliage is evergreen, providing visual interest throughout the year.

It is often used in landscaping for its dramatic presence and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
