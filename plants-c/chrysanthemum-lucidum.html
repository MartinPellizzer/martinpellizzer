Chrysanthemum lucidum

Chrysanthemum lucidum, commonly known as the golden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers, which bloom in the fall. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Chrysanthemum lucidum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
