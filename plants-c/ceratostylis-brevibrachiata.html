Ceratostylis brevibrachiata

Ceratostylis brevibrachiata, commonly known as the short-braced sword orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky crevices. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and have a distinctive spurred labellum.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
