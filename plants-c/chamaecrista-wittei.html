Chamaecrista wittei

Chamaecrista wittei, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features palmately divided leaves and produces yellow to orange flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Chamaecrista wittei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
