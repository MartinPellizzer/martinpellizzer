Croton junceus

Croton junceus, commonly known as the white croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are typically green with yellow or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton junceus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.
