Cardamine pratensis var. nemorosa

Cardamine pratensis var. nemorosa, commonly known as water shield, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy habitats.

It features a rosette of basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a raceme, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are often submerged or floating, adapted to aquatic environments.

It plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and amphibians.
