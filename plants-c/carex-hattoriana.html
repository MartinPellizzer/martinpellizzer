Carex hattoriana

Carex hattoriana, commonly known as the Hattori sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and cylindrical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a bluish-green hue, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in early summer.

Carex hattoriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
