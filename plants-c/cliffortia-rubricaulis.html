Cliffortia rubricaulis

Cliffortia rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed carpet flower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with dense clusters of small, bright red flowers. The plant features hairy, reddish stems that give it its common name, and its leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Cliffortia rubricaulis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
