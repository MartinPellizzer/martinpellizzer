Commelina simsonii

Commelina simsonii, commonly known as the Simpson's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Commelina genus within the family Commelinaceae. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often accompanied by white bracts. It thrives in moist, tropical environments and is often found in shaded, wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
