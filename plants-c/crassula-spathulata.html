Crassula spathulata

Crassula spathulata, commonly known as the spoon-leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, spoon-shaped leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full to partial sunlight.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is often used as a decorative indoor or outdoor plant.
