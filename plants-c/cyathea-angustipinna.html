Cyathea angustipinna

Cyathea angustipinna, commonly known as the narrow-pinnate tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate fronds that are often used in horticulture for their ornamental value. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The fronds are typically evergreen, providing a lush appearance throughout the year.
