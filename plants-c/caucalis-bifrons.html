Caucalis bifrons

Caucalis bifrons, commonly known as the two-flowered caucalis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has a low-growing habit with slender, branching stems and small, elongated leaves. It is native to regions of Europe and parts of Asia, often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Caucalis bifrons is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
