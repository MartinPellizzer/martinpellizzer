Celsia freynii

Celsia freynii, commonly known as the Freyn's celsia, is a member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Peru and Ecuador. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.
