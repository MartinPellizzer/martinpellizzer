Camellia chekiangoleosa

Camellia chekiangoleosa, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to deep red. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its blooms. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.

Camellia chekiangoleosa is also valued for its historical and cultural significance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
