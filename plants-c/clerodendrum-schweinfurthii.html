Clerodendrum schweinfurthii

Clerodendrum schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of bright red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are yellow when mature. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
