Citrosma riparia

Citrosma riparia, commonly known as the Malayan citron tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are small, round, and resemble citrus fruits, though they are not typically consumed by humans.
