Carex alba var. ussuriensis

Carex alba var. ussuriensis, commonly known as the Ussuri sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern regions of Russia and parts of northeastern China.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes grass-like plants commonly found in wet or moist environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale green flowers in clusters. It typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds or streams.
