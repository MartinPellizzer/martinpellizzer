Crambe viraeana

Crambe viraeana, commonly known as the purple crambe, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, violet-colored flowers. Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and covered in a fine layer of white hair.
