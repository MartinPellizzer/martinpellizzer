Cardiophyllum

Cardiophyllum, commonly known as the heartleaf or heart plant, is a unique species characterized by its heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often velvety in texture. Cardiophyllum is typically evergreen and can grow as either a shrub or a climbing vine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
