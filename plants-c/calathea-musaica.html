Calathea musaica

Calathea musaica, commonly known as the Musaica Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a combination of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening enthusiasts.
