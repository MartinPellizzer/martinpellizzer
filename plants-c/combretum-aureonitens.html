Combretum aureonitens

Combretum aureonitens, commonly known as golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant appearance during the flowering season. The leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant is adapted to dry conditions and can thrive in well-drained soils.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy blooms and drought tolerance.
