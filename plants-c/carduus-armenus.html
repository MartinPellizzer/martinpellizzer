Carduus armenus

Carduus armenus, commonly known as Armenian thistle.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and Central Asia, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh conditions.

Its foliage and flowers are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
