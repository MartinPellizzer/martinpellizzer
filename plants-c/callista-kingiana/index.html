Callista Kingiana

Callista kingiana is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of New Guinea. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including pink, purple, and white. This species is highly sought after by botanists and gardeners alike due to its unique appearance and limited distribution. Despite its beauty, it remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat. Its conservation status highlights the need for greater protection and study of this rare botanical treasure.
