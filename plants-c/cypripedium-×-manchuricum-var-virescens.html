Cypripedium × manchuricum var. virescens

Cypripedium × manchuricum var. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-yellow petals and a distinctive pouch-shaped lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments and thrives in cool climates. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
