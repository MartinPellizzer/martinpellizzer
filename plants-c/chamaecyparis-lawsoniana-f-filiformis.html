Chamaecyparis lawsoniana f. filiformis

Chamaecyparis lawsoniana f. filiformis, commonly known as the Lawson cypress filiformis, is a unique variant of the Lawson cypress.

It is characterized by its narrow, thread-like foliage that gives it a distinctive, feathery appearance. This form is a slow-growing evergreen with a conical shape, often used in landscaping for its ornamental value. The plant has scale-like leaves that are closely arranged along the branches.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for smaller garden spaces.
