Canarium thollonicum

Canarium thollonicum, commonly known as the thollon tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces a unique type of fruit known as a syncarp, which consists of multiple fused berries. Its bark and resin have been used in traditional medicine and for making incense.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in forest ecosystems.
