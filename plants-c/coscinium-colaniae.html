Coscinium colaniae

Coscinium colaniae, commonly known as the African moonseed, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional African medicine for various ailments.
