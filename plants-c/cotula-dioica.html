Cotula dioica

Cotula dioica, commonly known as the common white daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, white, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers. The plant has alternate, hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Cotula dioica is often found in open, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is used in traditional medicine and is also cultivated as an ornamental plant.
