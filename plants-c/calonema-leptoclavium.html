Calonema leptoclavium

Calonema leptoclavium, commonly known as the slender-leaf calonema, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. This evergreen shrub typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually come in shades of pink or red, blooming during the spring and summer months.

The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
