Cissus marcanii

Cissus marcanii, commonly known as the African stonecrop, is a species of flowering plant in the family Vitaceae.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. Cissus marcanii is also valued for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
