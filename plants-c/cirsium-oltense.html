Cirsium oltense

Cirsium oltense, commonly known as the Oltense thistle, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in harsh environments.

The flowers are usually visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
