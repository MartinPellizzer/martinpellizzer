Cyathea parianensis

Cyathea parianensis, commonly known as the Pariangan tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and mountainous regions. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea parianensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing habitat and shelter for various organisms.
