Callitriche transvolgensis

Callitriche transvolgensis, commonly known as the Transvolg water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater habitats such as ponds, streams, and wetlands. The plant has slender, branching stems that are often floating or submerged, with small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various invertebrates and small fish.
