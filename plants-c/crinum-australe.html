Crinum australe

Crinum australe, commonly known as the Australian lily or swamp lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pink, with a distinctive red or purple stripe down the center. The plant has strap-like leaves that are often broad and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in moist, swampy environments and is often found near water bodies.

Crinum australe is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate moisture conditions.
