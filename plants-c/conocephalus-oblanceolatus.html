Conocephalus oblanceolatus

Conocephalus oblanceolatus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed conocephalus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae, commonly referred to as sedges.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flower heads. The plant produces pale to brownish flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated.
