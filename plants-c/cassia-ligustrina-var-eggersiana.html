Cassia ligustrina var. eggersiana

Cassia ligustrina var. eggersiana, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in late spring to early summer. The plant produces long, flat pods that contain several seeds, and its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple small leaflets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
