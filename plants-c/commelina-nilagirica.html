Commelina nilagirica

Commelina nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri commelina, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South Asia.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes a variety of grass-like and herbaceous plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, blue or violet flowers with three petals.
