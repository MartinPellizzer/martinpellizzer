Carduus cebennensis

Carduus cebennensis, commonly known as the Cebenna thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as meadows and grasslands. It has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
