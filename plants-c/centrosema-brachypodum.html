Centrosema brachypodum

Centrosema brachypodum, commonly known as smallleaf crownvetch, is a perennial legume native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for restoration projects.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it a valuable plant in sustainable landscaping and agricultural systems.
