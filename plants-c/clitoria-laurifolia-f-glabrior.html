Clitoria laurifolia f. glabrior

Clitoria laurifolia f. glabrior, commonly known as the smooth-leaved butterfly pea, is a variant of the butterfly pea plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and vibrant violet-blue flowers that resemble butterflies. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its flowers are rich in flavonoids, making it useful in traditional medicine and as a natural dye.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.
