Chlorophytum abyssinicum

Chlorophytum abyssinicum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and clusters of violet or white flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.

Its botanical features make it a favorite among indoor gardeners.
