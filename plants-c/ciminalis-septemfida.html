Ciminalis septemfida

Ciminalis septemfida, commonly known as the seven-lobed ciminalis, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive seven-lobed leaves.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its leaves are usually serrated and have a slightly hairy texture.

Ciminalis septemfida is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
