Cyathea sternbergii

Cyathea sternbergii, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and long lifespan. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The fronds are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in horticulture.
