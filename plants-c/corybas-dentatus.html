Corybas dentatus

Corybas dentatus, commonly known as the toothed helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that is typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which is often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive beak-like structure. Corybas dentatus thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
