Carex autumnalis

Carex autumnalis, commonly known as autumn sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. This plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as wetlands, stream banks, and marshes. Its leaves are grass-like, narrow, and often have a bluish-green hue.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
