Chamaedorea oerstedii

Chamaedorea oerstedii, commonly known as the Oersted's bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feather-like, compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by dark blue, spherical fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant.

Its ability to tolerate low light and moderate watering makes it a popular choice for home and office settings.
