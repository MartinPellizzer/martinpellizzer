Centaurea parlatoris subsp. virescens

Centaurea parlatoris subsp.

virescens, commonly known as the greenhead centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers.

The species features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seed heads that are typically bristly and spiky. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
