Cavendishia skutchii

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its rosette of grass-like leaves that form a central cup. The plant produces striking inflorescences with showy, colorful flowers that attract pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.
