Cirsium ibericum

Cirsium ibericum, commonly known as the Iberian thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple or pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in arid conditions.

Cirsium ibericum is also known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
