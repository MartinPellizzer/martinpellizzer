Ctenodon tumbezensis

Ctenodon tumbezensis, commonly known as the Tumbes ctenodon, is a species of flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing features that help it conserve water. It belongs to the family Cactaceae, which includes many desert plants.

Ctenodon tumbezensis is relatively rare and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
