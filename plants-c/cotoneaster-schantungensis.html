Cotoneaster schantungensis

Cotoneaster schantungensis, commonly known as the Schantung cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, sprawling growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during late spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly serrated edge.

Cotoneaster schantungensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
