Calathea rhizanthoides

Calathea rhizanthoides, commonly known as the pink prayer plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with pink and green patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically hidden beneath its leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Calathea rhizanthoides is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
