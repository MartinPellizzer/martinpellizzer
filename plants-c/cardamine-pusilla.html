Cardamine pusilla

Cardamine pusilla, commonly known as mouse-ear chickweed, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Cardamine pusilla has a short stem and is often found in clusters.
