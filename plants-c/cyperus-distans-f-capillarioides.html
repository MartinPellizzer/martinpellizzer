Cyperus distans f. capillarioides

Cyperus distans f. capillarioides, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland or marshy environments. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of small, three-sided spikelets.

This variety is characterized by its elongated, thread-like leaves, which distinguish it from other Cyperus species.
