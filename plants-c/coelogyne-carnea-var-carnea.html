Coelogyne carnea var. carnea

Coelogyne carnea var. carnea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces striking pink to red flowers with yellow lips. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and is known for its fragrant blooms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
