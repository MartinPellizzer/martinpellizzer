Cymbidium erythraeum var. erythraeum

Cymbidium erythraeum var. erythraeum, commonly known as the Red Sea orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with tall, erect stems that bear lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting dark veins. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
