Cryptocarya ilocana

Cryptocarya ilocana, commonly known as the Ilocano cryptocarya, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in lowland to montane forests, thriving in humid and shaded environments.

The fruit is a dark blue berry that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
