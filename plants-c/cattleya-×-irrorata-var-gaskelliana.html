Cattleya × irrorata var. gaskelliana

Cattleya × irrorata var. gaskelliana, commonly known as the Gaskell's Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or purple hue with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences, and its leaves are broad and leathery, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments. It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
