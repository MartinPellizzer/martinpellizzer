Crassula mesembryanthoides

Crassula mesembryanthoides, commonly known as the Ice Plant or Frost Plant, is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red, typically during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula mesembryanthoides is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
