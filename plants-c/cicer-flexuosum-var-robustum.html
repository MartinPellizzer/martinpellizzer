Cicer flexuosum var. robustum

Cicer flexuosum var. robustum, commonly known as the robust wild vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
