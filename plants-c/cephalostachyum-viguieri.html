Cephalostachyum viguieri

Cephalostachyum viguieri, commonly known as the golden bamboo or golden leaved bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its striking golden-yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its green stems. It produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, adding to its ornamental value.

Cephalostachyum viguieri is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a decorative plant.
