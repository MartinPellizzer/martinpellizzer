Calanthe yaoshanensis

Calanthe yaoshanensis, commonly known as the Yaoshan calanthe, is a rare orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, where it thrives in the undergrowth.
