Crassula portulacastrum

Crassula portulacastrum, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for dry environments.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
