Cenostigma myabense var. myabense

Cenostigma myabense var. myabense, commonly known as the Myabense cenostigma, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are often fragrant. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
