Cyanea knudsenii var. glabra

Cyanea knudsenii var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth Cyanea, is a rare Hawaiian plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which are typically dark green and lance-shaped. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It is endemic to the island of Hawaii and thrives in moist, shaded environments.
