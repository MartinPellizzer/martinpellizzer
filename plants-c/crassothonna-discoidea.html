Crassothonna discoidea

Crassothonna discoidea, commonly known as the spiral orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is often found growing in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
