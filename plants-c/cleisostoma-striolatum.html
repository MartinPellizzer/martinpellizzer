Cleisostoma striolatum

Cleisostoma striolatum, commonly known as the streaked cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in summer. The plant has pendulous inflorescences with small, greenish-white to yellowish flowers that have a distinctive striped pattern on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cleisostoma striolatum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral structure.
