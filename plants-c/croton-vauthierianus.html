Croton vauthierianus

Croton vauthierianus, commonly known as the Vauthier's croton, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often multicolored leaves that display a range of shades from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a rough, sometimes hairy texture.

Croton vauthierianus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
