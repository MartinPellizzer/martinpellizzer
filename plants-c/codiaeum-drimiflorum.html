Codiaeum drimiflorum

Codiaeum drimiflorum, commonly known as the velvetleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in spikes. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Codiaeum drimiflorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.
