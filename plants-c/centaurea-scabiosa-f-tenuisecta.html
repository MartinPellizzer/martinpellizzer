Centaurea scabiosa f. tenuisecta

Centaurea scabiosa f. tenuisecta, commonly known as the slenderleaf knapweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, pale pink to white flowers arranged in dense, fluffy clusters. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky soils. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Centaurea genus.
