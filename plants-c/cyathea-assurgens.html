Cyathea assurgens

Cyathea assurgens, commonly known as the silver tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant has a distinctive silver-gray sheath covering its trunk, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Cyathea assurgens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
