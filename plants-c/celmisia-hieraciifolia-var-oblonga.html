Celmisia hieraciifolia var. oblonga

Celmisia hieraciifolia var. oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaved celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
