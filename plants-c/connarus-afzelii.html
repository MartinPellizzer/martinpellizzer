Connarus afzelii

Connarus afzelii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a tropical tree native to Central and Southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability and strength. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
