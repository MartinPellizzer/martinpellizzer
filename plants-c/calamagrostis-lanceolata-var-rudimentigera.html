Calamagrostis lanceolata var. rudimentigera

Calamagrostis lanceolata var. rudimentigera, commonly known as the slender false panicgrass, is a grass species native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and wet meadows.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
