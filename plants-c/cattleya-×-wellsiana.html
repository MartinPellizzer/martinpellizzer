Cattleya × wellsiana

Cattleya × wellsiana, commonly known as the Wellsiana orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a vibrant pink or purple coloration with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid is popular among growers for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
