Coppensia williamsii

Coppensia williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' heath, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring and summer. Coppensia williamsii thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
