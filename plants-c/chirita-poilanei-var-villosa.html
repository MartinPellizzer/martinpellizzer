Chirita poilanei var. villosa

Chirita poilanei var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy chirita, is a species of flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Vietnam and Cambodia. The plant is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "villosa.". It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red in color.
