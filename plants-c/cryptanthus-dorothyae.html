Cryptanthus dorothyae

Cryptanthus dorothyae, commonly known as the Dorothy's carpet.

It is a low-growing, evergreen perennial native to Brazil, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the summer months. Its foliage consists of broad, glossy leaves that are often variegated with white or silver markings.

Cryptanthus dorothyae is well-suited for rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
