Caryophyllus jambos

Caryophyllus jambos, commonly known as the Java carnation, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
