Chabertia istriaca

Chabertia istriaca, commonly known as the Istrian chabertia, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and along riverbanks. The plant has slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are pale blue to violet, arranged in dense spikes, and bloom from June to August.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
