Chasmatophyllum braunsii

Chasmatophyllum braunsii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other composite flowers. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular florets, typical of the family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
