Centaurea coerulescens var. aristata

Centaurea coerulescens var. aristata, commonly known as the blueweed or blue centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces bracteate involucres that enclose the small, tubular florets. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in dry grasslands and rocky soils.

Centaurea coerulescens var. aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
