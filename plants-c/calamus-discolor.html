Calamus discolor

Calamus discolor, commonly known as the yellow flag, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking yellow-green spadices, which are its inflorescences, often surrounded by a colorful spathe. Native to wetlands and marshy areas, it thrives in moist, sunny environments.

Calamus discolor is often used in traditional medicine and is also a popular ornamental plant in aquatic gardens.
