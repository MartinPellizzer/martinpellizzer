Chenopodium × leptophylliforme

Chenopodium × leptophylliforme, commonly known as the slenderleaf goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, and it is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its succulent stems and leaves make it somewhat drought-tolerant, though it prefers moist environments.

This species is sometimes cultivated as a leaf vegetable in certain regions due to its nutritional value.
