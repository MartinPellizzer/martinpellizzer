Coelogyne angustichila

Coelogyne angustichila, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with narrow, linear leaves and produces striking, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with red or purple markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth. It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.

Coelogyne angustichila is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
