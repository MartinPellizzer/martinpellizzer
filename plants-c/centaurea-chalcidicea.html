Centaurea chalcidicea

Centaurea chalcidicea, commonly known as the Chalcidice centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple-blue flowers and spiny leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. Its flowers bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
