Croton casarettoanus

Croton casarettoanus, commonly known as the Casaretto's croton, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often striking foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their aesthetic appeal.
