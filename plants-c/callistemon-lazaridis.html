Callistemon lazaridis

Callistemon lazaridis, commonly known as the Lazaridis bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant once established.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract nectar-feeding birds and insects.
