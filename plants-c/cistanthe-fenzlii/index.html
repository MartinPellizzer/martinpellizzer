Cistanthe Fenzlii

Cistanthe fenzlii is a rare and resilient plant native to the arid regions of South America. It thrives in harsh, dry environments where few other species can survive. This succulent has adapted to store water in its thick, fleshy leaves. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Understanding its biology is key to preserving biodiversity in threatened ecosystems.
