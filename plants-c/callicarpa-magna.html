Callicarpa magna

Callicarpa magna, commonly known as the Chinese beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of vibrant purple berries that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

The berries are not only visually striking but also attract birds and other wildlife.
