Coelogyne radioferens

Coelogyne radioferens, commonly known as the Radiant Coelogyne, is a striking orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of yellow and orange. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Coelogyne radioferens thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.
