Coelogyne echinolabium

Coelogyne echinolabium, commonly known as the spiky labellum orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with long, strap-like leaves and a distinctive, spiky labellum that gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne echinolabium is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
