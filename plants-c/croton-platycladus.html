Croton platycladus

Croton platycladus, commonly known as the Chinese croton, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually green with a smooth surface. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers in panicles, followed by distinctive three-lobed, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it is often used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
