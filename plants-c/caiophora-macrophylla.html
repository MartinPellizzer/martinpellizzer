Caiophora macrophylla

Caiophora macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf caiophora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
