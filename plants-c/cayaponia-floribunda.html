Cayaponia floribunda

Cayaponia floribunda, commonly known as the red-flowered cayaponia, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a mild, aromatic scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Cayaponia floribunda is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
