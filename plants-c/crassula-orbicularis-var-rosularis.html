Crassula orbicularis var. rosularis

Crassula orbicularis var. rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Crassula, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing form with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings.
