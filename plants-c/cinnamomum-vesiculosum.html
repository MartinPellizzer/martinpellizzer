Cinnamomum vesiculosum

Cinnamomum vesiculosum, commonly known as the bubble cinnamon or bubble laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant bark and leaves that emit a strong cinnamon scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, fleshy berries. Its bark is often used in traditional medicine and as a spice, similar to cinnamon.

The tree thrives in humid, tropical climates and is valued for both its ornamental and practical uses.
