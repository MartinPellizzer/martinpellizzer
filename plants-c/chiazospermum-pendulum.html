Chiazospermum pendulum

Chiazospermum pendulum, commonly known as the hanging chia seed plant, is a unique species native to arid regions.

It features elongated, pendulous stems that bear clusters of small, capsule-like fruits. The plant produces seeds that are rich in omega-3 fatty acids, similar to traditional chia seeds. Its structure allows it to thrive in low-water environments by reducing surface area exposed to the sun.

The botanical characteristics of Chiazospermum pendulum make it an interesting subject for both ecological and agricultural studies.
