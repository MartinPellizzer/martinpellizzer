Cerastium syvaschicum

Cerastium syvaschicum, commonly known as the alpine mouse-ear chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Cerastium syvaschicum is well adapted to cold climates and can withstand harsh environmental conditions.
