Cleisostoma tonkinense

Cleisostoma tonkinense, commonly known as the Tonkin cleistostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its flowers are borne on short lateral shoots and have a unique structure with a closed lip.

Cleisostoma tonkinense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in a tropical greenhouse setting.
