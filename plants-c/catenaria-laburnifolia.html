Catenaria laburnifolia

Catenaria laburnifolia, commonly known as the golden chain tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that resemble small chains, hence its common name. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in Mediterranean climates.

Catenaria laburnifolia is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ability to attract pollinators.
