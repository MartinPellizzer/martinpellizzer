Clematis × guascoi

Clematis × guascoi, commonly known as the Guasco hybrid clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × guascoi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
