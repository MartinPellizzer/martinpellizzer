Cyclamen mindleri

Cyclamen mindleri, commonly known as the Persian cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a distinctive waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Cyclamen mindleri is known for its ornate appearance and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled indoor environment.
