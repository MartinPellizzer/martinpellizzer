Cytisus × dallimorei

Cytisus × dallimorei, commonly known as the hybrid Scotch broom, is a deciduous shrub belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to the British Isles and is often found in coastal areas and sandy soils. It has been widely cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions due to its rapid growth and seed dispersal.
