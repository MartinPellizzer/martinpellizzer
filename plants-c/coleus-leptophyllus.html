Coleus leptophyllus

Coleus leptophyllus, commonly known as the slender-leaf coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and typically grows as an herbaceous perennial. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance.

It prefers warm, humid climates and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
