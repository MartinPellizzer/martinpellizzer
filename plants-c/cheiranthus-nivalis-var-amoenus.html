Cheiranthus nivalis var. amoenus

Cheiranthus nivalis var. amoenus, commonly known as the snowdrop beauty, is a herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, four-petaled flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers, which bloom in early spring, are usually white or pale yellow, adding a delicate touch to the mountainous landscape.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and sensitive species.
