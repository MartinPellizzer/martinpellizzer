Cyclaminum vernum

Cyclaminum vernum, commonly known as the spring cyclamen, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and vibrant, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens. Its flowers typically appear in early spring, adding a touch of color to the landscape during the transition from winter to summer.

Cyclaminum vernum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
