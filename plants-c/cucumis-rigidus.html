Cucumis rigidus

Cucumis rigidus, commonly known as the prickly cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of western Asia. The plant produces small, spiny fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are palmately lobed and covered with fine hairs.

The species is notable for its unique morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
