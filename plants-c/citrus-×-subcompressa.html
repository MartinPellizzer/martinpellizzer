Citrus × subcompressa

Citrus × subcompressa, commonly known as the dwarf lemon.

It is a hybrid citrus plant that exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces. The plant produces small, yellow citrus fruits that are often used for culinary purposes. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, with a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and regular watering.
