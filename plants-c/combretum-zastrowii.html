Combretum zastrowii

Combretum zastrowii, commonly known as the Zastrow's flame tree, is a small to medium-sized tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, making it a visually appealing species. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or semi-arid regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
