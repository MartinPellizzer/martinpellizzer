Cyrtandra × subaequalis

Cyrtandra × subaequalis, commonly known as the hybrid beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is a hybrid between Cyrtandra subaequalis and Cyrtandra hawaiiensis. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that produce small, violet-blue berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in native forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
