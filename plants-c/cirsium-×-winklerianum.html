Cirsium × winklerianum

Cirsium × winklerianum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and coastal areas. It produces small, wind-pollinated flowers that develop into bristly seed heads.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
