Cliffortia intermedia

Cliffortia intermedia, commonly known as the mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, fleshy leaves and produces clusters of delicate, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Cliffortia intermedia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
