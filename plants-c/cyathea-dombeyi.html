Cyathea dombeyi

Cyathea dombeyi, commonly known as the Dombey's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for many decades, making it a long-lived species. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which can reach several meters in length.

Cyathea dombeyi plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
