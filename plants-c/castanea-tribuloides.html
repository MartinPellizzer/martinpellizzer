Castanea tribuloides

Castanea tribuloides, commonly known as the Chinese chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and hickories. The tree produces small, spiny fruits called nuts, which are encased in a prickly husk. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated margin.
