Callistemon acerifolius

Callistemon acerifolius, commonly known as the bottlebrush tree, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia and is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in winter and spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The bark is smooth and often has a reddish or greyish hue, which adds to its ornamental value.
