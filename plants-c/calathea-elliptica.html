Calathea elliptica

Calathea elliptica, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical perennial native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring contrasting colors that make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Calathea elliptica is known for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of any space.
