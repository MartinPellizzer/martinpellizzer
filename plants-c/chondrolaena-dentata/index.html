Chondrolaena Dentata

Chondrolaena dentata is a unique flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia. It is known for its striking, palmate leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. This species thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its name reflects its historical use in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the world of horticulture.
