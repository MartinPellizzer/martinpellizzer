Cojoba valerioi

Cojoba valerioi, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to the island of Madagascar.

It features a rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized collections.
