Combretum comosum var. dolichopetalum

Combretum comosum var. dolichopetalum, commonly known as the long-petaled flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange inflorescences that appear in clusters, resembling flames. The leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough, hairy texture. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
