Cakile

Cakile, commonly known as sea rocket, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often found growing along coastal areas and in sandy soils, thriving in saline environments. Its seeds are small and contain compounds that have been studied for potential medicinal properties.

Cakile is also a food source for various insects and birds.
