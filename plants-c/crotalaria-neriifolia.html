Crotalaria neriifolia

Crotalaria neriifolia, commonly known as the blackleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine.

Crotalaria neriifolia is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
