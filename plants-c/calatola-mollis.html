Calatola mollis

Calatola mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and has opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, usually in shades of white or pale green.

Calatola mollis is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings due to its adaptability to various soil types.
