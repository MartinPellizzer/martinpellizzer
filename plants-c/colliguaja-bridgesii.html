Colliguaja bridgesii

Colliguaja bridgesii, commonly known as the Bornean star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red markings. The plant grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
