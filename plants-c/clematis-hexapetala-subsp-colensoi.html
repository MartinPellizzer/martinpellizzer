Clematis hexapetala subsp. colensoi

Clematis hexapetala subsp.

colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's Clematis, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its delicate, five-petaled white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant has compound leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces long, slender stems that climb or trail. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
