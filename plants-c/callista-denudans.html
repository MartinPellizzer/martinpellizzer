Callista denudans

Callista denudans, commonly known as the naked callista, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
