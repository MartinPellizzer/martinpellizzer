Chlorophytum papilliferum

Chlorophytum papilliferum, commonly known as the African spear lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow tubercles, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces tall, slender flower spikes that bear small, greenish-white flowers. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
