Coelogyne charisae

Coelogyne charisae, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, fragrant, yellow flowers with a distinctive red lip, blooming on pendulous inflorescences. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves and thrives in bright, indirect light with high humidity. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Coelogyne charisae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
