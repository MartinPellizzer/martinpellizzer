Caragana franchetiana var. gyirongensis

Caragana franchetiana var. gyirongensis, commonly known as the Gyirong caragana, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky or sandy soils. It is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.

The species is also noted for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
