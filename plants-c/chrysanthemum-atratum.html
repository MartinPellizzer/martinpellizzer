Chrysanthemum atratum

Chrysanthemum atratum, commonly known as the dark chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which contrast with its green foliage. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. It prefers partial shade to full sun and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are used in traditional Chinese medicine and are also popular in floral arrangements.
