Caladenia phaeoclavia

Caladenia phaeoclavia, commonly known as the dark-veined spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features a single, slender stem that bears a single, strikingly colored flower with a distinctive dark veining. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of a spider.

This species is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and elegant blooms.
