Cliffortia cuneata var. cylindrica

Cliffortia cuneata var. cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical rock orchid, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and fleshy, cylindrical leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink or white, typically during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
