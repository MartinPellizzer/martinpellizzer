Cattleya mossiae var. purpurata

Cattleya mossiae var. purpurata, commonly known as the Purple Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, velvety purple flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features upright pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves, typical of the Cattleya genus. Its flowers are fragrant and can bloom multiple times a year under optimal care.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy cultivation requirements.
