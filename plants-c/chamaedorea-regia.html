Chamaedorea regia

Chamaedorea regia, commonly known as the royal palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. This plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Chamaedorea regia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and air-purifying properties.
