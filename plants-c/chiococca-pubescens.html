Chiococca pubescens

Chiococca pubescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves covered in fine, soft hairs. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is cultivated in some regions for horticultural purposes.
