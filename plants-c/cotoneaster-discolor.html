Cotoneaster discolor

Cotoneaster discolor, commonly known as the variable cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that change color throughout the seasons, ranging from dark green to red or purple. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by orange-red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster discolor is also valued for its ability to thrive in a range of light conditions.
