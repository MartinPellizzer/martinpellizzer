Chlorophytum goetzei

Chlorophytum goetzei, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its long, grass-like leaves with striking white and green stripes. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers on spikes during the spring and summer months. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum goetzei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
