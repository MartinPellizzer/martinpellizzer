Crepis Triasii

Crepis triasii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It thrives in high-altitude environments where the climate is cold and the soil is rocky. This species is known for its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this unique and fragile species from extinction.
