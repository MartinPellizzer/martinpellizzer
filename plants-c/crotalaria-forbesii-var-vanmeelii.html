Crotalaria forbesii var. vanmeelii

Crotalaria forbesii var. vanmeelii, commonly known as the Vanmeel's crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The seeds are encased in hard pods, which contribute to its ecological role in soil stabilization and nutrient cycling.
