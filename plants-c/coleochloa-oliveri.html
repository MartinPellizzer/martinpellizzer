Coleochloa oliveri

Coleochloa oliveri, commonly known as Oliver's grass, is a perennial grass species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit with slender, erect stems. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green and have a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets.

Coleochloa oliveri is often found in open, sandy or rocky habitats and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.
