Crepidium caulescens

Crepidium caulescens, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stem and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually white or pale green, with three sepals and three petals, and it produces a single, slender spur.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
