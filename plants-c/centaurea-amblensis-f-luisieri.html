Centaurea amblensis f. luisieri

Centaurea amblensis f. luisieri, commonly known as the Luisieri's Bluestem, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, blue to purple flower heads that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is native to the Mediterranean region. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in open habitats such as coastal dunes and rocky slopes.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
