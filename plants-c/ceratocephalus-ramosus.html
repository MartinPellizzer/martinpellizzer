Ceratocephalus ramosus

Ceratocephalus ramosus, commonly known as the branched burrweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny, globe-shaped fruits that contain seeds covered in a dense, prickly coating.
