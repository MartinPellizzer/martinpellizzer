Croton villosus var. cinerascens

Croton villosus var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, often grayish stems and leaves that are typically alternate and ovate. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or savanna habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its unique foliage and resilience.
