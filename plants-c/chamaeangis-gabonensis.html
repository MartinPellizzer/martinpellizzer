Chamaeangis gabonensis

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, blending in with its foliage. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

Due to habitat loss and limited study, Chamaeangis gabonensis remains a poorly understood species in botanical science.
