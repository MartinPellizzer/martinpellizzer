Caragana polyacantha

Caragana polyacantha, commonly known as the many-branched shrub or Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces yellow flowers in early summer. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it valuable for soil enrichment. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its hardy nature makes it suitable for use as a windbreak or in reclamation projects.
