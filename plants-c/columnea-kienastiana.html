Columnea kienastiana

Columnea kienastiana, commonly known as the Kienast's flame.

It is a tropical shrub native to Central America, characterized by its vibrant red tubular flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Columnea kienastiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract hummingbirds.
