Centaurea scabiosa var. menteyerica

Centaurea scabiosa var. menteyerica, commonly known as the Mentz's Scotch thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features spiny, lobed leaves and produces striking purple to pink composite flower heads. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Its botanical structure includes a central stem with alternate leaves and a composite inflorescence composed of both ray and disk flowers.
