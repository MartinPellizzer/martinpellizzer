Coincya monensis var. johnstonii

Coincya monensis var. johnstonii, commonly known as the Johnston's water starwort, is a rare aquatic plant native to freshwater environments in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves that float on the water surface, with a distinct central vein and wavy margins. The plant typically grows in still or slow-moving waters, often in shallow, nutrient-rich habitats. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters near the water's surface.
