Croton arboreus

Croton arboreus, commonly known as the tree croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Croton arboreus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
