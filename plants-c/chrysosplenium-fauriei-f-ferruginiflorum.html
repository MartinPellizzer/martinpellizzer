Chrysosplenium fauriei f. ferruginiflorum

Chrysosplenium fauriei f. ferruginiflorum, commonly known as the rustyflower chrysosplenium, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive rusty or reddish hue on the petals.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions of eastern Asia.
