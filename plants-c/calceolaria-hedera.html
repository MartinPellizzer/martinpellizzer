Calceolaria hedera

Calceolaria hedera, commonly known as the ivy calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves that resemble those of ivy. The plant produces striking, hooded flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which are unique in structure. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Calceolaria hedera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
