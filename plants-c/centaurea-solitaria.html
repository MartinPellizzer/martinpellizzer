Centaurea solitaria

Centaurea solitaria, commonly known as the solitary centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking blue to purple flower heads that resemble small daisies. Its flowers are composed of numerous ray florets surrounding a central cluster of tubular florets.

Centaurea solitaria is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
