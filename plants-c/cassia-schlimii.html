Cassia schlimii

Cassia schlimii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous flat, winged seeds. Cassia schlimii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
