Cymbidium lowianum var. concolor

Cymbidium lowianum var. concolor, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen orchid, is a species native to China and is prized for its elegant, fragrant flowers.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in a variety of conditions. The plant produces racemes of long, narrow petals that are typically white with green or yellow markings. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a favored ornamental plant in both traditional and modern gardens.
