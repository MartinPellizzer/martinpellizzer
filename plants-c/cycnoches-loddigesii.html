Cycnoches loddigesii

Cycnoches loddigesii, commonly known as the white orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cycnoches loddigesii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
