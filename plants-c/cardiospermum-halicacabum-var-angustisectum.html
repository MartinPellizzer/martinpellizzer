Cardiospermum halicacabum var. angustisectum

Cardiospermum halicacabum var. angustisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf velvet bean, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Leguminosae (Fabaceae), characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with narrow, elongated leaflets and produces showy, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a distinctive, inflated pod containing kidney-shaped seeds covered in a soft, hairy coating.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage and floral display.
