Coleus graniticola

Coleus graniticola, commonly known as the granite coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is valued for its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Coleus graniticola is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor settings.
