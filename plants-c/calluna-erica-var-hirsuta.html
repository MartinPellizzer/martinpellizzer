Calluna erica var. hirsuta

Calluna erica var. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy heather, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the British Isles.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late summer. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are covered in fine, hairy pubescence, giving it a distinctive texture. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and moorland environments.

This hardy species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in challenging landscapes.
