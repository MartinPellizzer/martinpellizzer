Cucurbita mixta

Cucurbita mixta, commonly known as the Mexican gourd, is a species of squash native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like pumpkins and cucumbers. The plant produces large, elongated fruits that can vary in color from green to orange or red. Its vines are trailing and can grow up to several meters in length.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional cuisines.
