Conophytum subtenue

Conophytum subtenue, commonly known as the narrow-leaved conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Conophytum subtenue is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
