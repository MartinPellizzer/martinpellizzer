Crenularia orbiculata

Crenularia orbiculata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

Crenularia orbiculata is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
