Cathedra rubricaulis

Cathedra rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed cathedra, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red-tinged stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Due to its striking appearance and limited distribution, Cathedra rubricaulis is a sought-after species among succulent enthusiasts.
