Croton denudatus

Croton denudatus, commonly known as the naked croton, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that can vary in shape and texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton denudatus is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
