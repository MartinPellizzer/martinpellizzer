Cymbopogon Prostratus

Cymbopogon prostratus is a unique grass species known for its creeping growth habit and aromatic properties. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in warm, moist environments with well-drained soil. This plant is often used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits. Its fine leaves and dense growth make it suitable for erosion control and ornamental purposes. Despite its benefits, it remains relatively under-studied compared to other members of the Cymbopogon genus.
