Catapodium rigidum f. robustum

Catapodium rigidum f. robustum, commonly known as the robust catapodium, is a perennial grass species native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, sturdy stems that are typically covered in rough, hairy texture. The plant produces dense clusters of small, spike-like inflorescences that are characteristic of the Poaceae family. Its leaves are narrow and rigid, contributing to its robust appearance.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
