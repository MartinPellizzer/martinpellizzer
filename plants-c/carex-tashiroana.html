Carex tashiroana

Carex tashiroana, commonly known as Tashiro's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Japan and is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Carex tashiroana is an important component of wetland ecosystems, contributing to soil stabilization and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
