Cybianthus tayoensis

Cybianthus tayoensis, commonly known as the Tayan Island lily, is a rare flowering plant native to the Tayan Islands in the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking white and pink petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in coastal areas with sandy soil, thriving in humid and saline conditions. Its flowers bloom during the dry season, adding a unique visual element to the local flora.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
