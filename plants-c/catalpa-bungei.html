Catalpa bungei

Catalpa bungei, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The tree produces long, bean-like pods that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, sticky aril. Catalpa bungei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
