Cryptomeria Mairei

Maire's cedar, scientifically known as Cryptomeria mairei, is a rare and ancient conifer native to the mountainous regions of southern China. This unique tree is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and its distinctive, scale-like leaves that give it a striking appearance. It is often referred to as the "Chinese cedar" due to its historical and cultural significance in the region. Despite its beauty and ecological importance, Maire's cedar is critically endangered due to habitat loss and overharvesting. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this remarkable species for future generations.
