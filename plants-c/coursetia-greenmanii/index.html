Coursetia Greenmanii

Coursetia greenmanii is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the remote regions of southern China. It thrives in harsh, mountainous environments where few other species can survive. This plant is characterized by its delicate, narrow leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. Due to its limited habitat and vulnerability to environmental changes, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and study this unique species.
