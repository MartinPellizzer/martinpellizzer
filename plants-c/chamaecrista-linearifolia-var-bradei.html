Chamaecrista linearifolia var. bradei

Chamaecrista linearifolia var. bradei, commonly known as the Brade's linearleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
