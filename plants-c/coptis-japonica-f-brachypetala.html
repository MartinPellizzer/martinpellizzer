Coptis japonica f. brachypetala

Coptis japonica f. brachypetala, commonly known as the short-petaled Japanese goldthread, is a variant of the Japanese goldthread plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia, particularly Japan and Korea. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early spring. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

The f. brachypetala form is distinguished by its shorter petals compared to the typical variety.
