Cyathea hornei

Cyathea hornei, commonly known as the Horne's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, frond-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its spores are produced on the underside of the fronds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.

Cyathea hornei plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various organisms.
