Cadaba longifolia var. frutescens

Cadaba longifolia var. frutescens, commonly known as the dwarf African rose, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for rock gardens and containers.
