Cypripedium insigne var. sanderianum

Cypripedium insigne var. sanderianum, commonly known as the Sander's lady's slipper, is a rare and striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically a deep pink or red color. The plant has a tall, erect stem with few leaves and produces a single, fragrant flower per stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
