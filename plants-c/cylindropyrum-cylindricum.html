Cylindropyrum cylindricum

Cylindropyrum cylindricum, commonly known as the slender lily, is a species of flowering plant in the family Liliaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in cool, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
