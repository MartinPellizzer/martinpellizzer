Cortaderia archboldii

Cortaderia archboldii, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to South America.

It features tall, arching clumps of silver-white, feathery plumes that stand out in gardens. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are dark green and often have a slight bluish tint. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Its ornamental value is heightened by its ability to provide winter interest with its persistent, silvery foliage.
