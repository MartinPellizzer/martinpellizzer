Catalpa bignonioides var. koehnei

Catalpa bignonioides var. koehnei, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces long, bean-like pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a suitable choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
