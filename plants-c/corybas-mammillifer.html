Corybas mammillifer

Corybas mammillifer, commonly known as the hairy spurred orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a slender, hairy stem that supports a single, showy flower with a distinctive spurred lip. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine, hairy structures.

The flower's unique structure and coloration make it an intriguing subject for botanical study and conservation efforts.
