Chionothrix somalensis

Chionothrix somalensis, commonly known as the Somalian snowgrass, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Somalia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that helps retain moisture in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, white or pale blue flowers that bloom during the rainy season. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to high temperatures and low precipitation.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
