Ceratostema bracteolatum

Ceratostema bracteolatum, commonly known as the silverleaf ceratostema, is a perennial shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Sarcobathraceae and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny capsules that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.
