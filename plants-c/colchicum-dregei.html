Colchicum dregei

Colchicum dregei, commonly known as the Cape colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, typically blooming in late autumn. The plant has basal leaves that are broad and hairy, and it produces a tall, slender stem. Its bulbs are toxic and contain colchicine, a compound used in medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
