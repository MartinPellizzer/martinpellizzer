Celmisia × boweana

Celmisia × boweana, commonly known as the Boweana celmisia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow or orange composite flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to semi-shaded environments.

Celmisia × boweana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
