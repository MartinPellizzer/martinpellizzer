Calochortus weedii var. intermedius

Calochortus weedii var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate mariposa lily, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes a variety of bulbous plants known for their showy, often showy flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.
