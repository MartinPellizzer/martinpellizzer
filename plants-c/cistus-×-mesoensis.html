Cistus × mesoensis

Cistus × mesoensis, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which is part of the family Cistaceae. The plant is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

Cistus × mesoensis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
