Cytisus dalmaticus

Cytisus dalmaticus, commonly known as the Dalmatian broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, often spiny stems and small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or dry landscapes.

Cytisus dalmaticus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in marginal environments.
