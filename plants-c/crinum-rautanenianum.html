Crinum rautanenianum

Crinum rautanenianum, commonly known as the Rautanen's crinum, is a bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in umbels. The plant has strap-shaped, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
