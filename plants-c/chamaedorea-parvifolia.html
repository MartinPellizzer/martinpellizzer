Chamaedorea parvifolia

Chamaedorea parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf bamboo palm, is a compact, evergreen plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It features gracefully arching, slender leaves that are typically a deep green, giving it a lush, tropical appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by pale green, berry-like fruits. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its compact size and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
