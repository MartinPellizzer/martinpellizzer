Cryptocarya natalensis

Cryptocarya natalensis, commonly known as Natal's cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to South Africa, specifically the eastern regions, and is typically found in moist, wooded areas. The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
