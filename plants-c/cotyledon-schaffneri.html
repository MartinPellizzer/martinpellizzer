Cotyledon schaffneri

Cotyledon schaffneri, commonly known as the Schaffner's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which help it store water and protect against intense sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
