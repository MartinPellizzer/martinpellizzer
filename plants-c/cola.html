Cola

Cola, commonly known as the cola nut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is closely related to the coffee plant. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves and clusters of bright red berries containing the seeds used in the production of cola beverages. The seeds, known as cola nuts, contain caffeine and other alkaloids.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in various tropical regions around the world.
