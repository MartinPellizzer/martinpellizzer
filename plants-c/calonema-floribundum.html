Calonema floribundum

Calonema floribundum, commonly known as the flowering calonema, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial shrub native to South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the summer months.

Calonema floribundum is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
