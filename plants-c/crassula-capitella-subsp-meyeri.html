Crassula capitella subsp. meyeri

Crassula capitella subsp.

meyeri, commonly known as the "Lemon Lime Jade," is a succulent native to South Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically light green to yellowish in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
