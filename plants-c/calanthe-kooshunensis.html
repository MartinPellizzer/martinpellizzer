Calanthe kooshunensis

Calanthe kooshunensis, commonly known as the Kooshung orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calanthe kooshunensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
