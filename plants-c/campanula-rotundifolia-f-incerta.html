Campanula rotundifolia f. incerta

Campanula rotundifolia f. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain roundleaf bellflower, is a holarctic perennial herb characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes. The leaves are rounded and often hairy, while the flowers are usually blue or violet in color. This cultivar is noted for its variability in form and color, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
