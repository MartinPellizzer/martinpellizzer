Cheilanthes tricholepifera

Cheilanthes tricholepifera, commonly known as the hairy shield fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rhizome and its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically short and broad, with a dense covering of fine, hairy scales on the underside. This plant is often found growing on rocky outcrops or in shaded, moist environments.

It is well adapted to arid conditions, making it a resilient and hardy species.
