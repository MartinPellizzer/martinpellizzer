Chlorophytum decoratum

Chlorophytum decoratum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial herb with long, arching leaves that are typically dark green with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a favored houseplant.

Additionally, it is capable of producing offsets, which can be easily propagated to create new plants.
