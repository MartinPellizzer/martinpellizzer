Cheiridopsis Minor

Cheiridopsis minor is a small, succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its compact, rounded shape and vibrant blue-green foliage. This species thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens. Its delicate flowers bloom in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Due to its hardiness and low maintenance, it is a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
