Conostegia fulvostellata

Conostegia fulvostellata, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features showy, yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the petals. Its leaves are usually opposite, simple, and have a rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
