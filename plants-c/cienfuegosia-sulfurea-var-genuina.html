Cienfuegosia sulfurea var. genuina

Cienfuegosia sulfurea var. genuina, commonly known as the golden cienfuegosia, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters along its stems. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
