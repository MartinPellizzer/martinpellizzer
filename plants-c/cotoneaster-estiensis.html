Cotoneaster estiensis

Cotoneaster estiensis, commonly known as the Estian cotoneaster, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers in spring, followed by red, fleshy berries that attract birds. It has small, oval leaves and a compact growth habit.

Cotoneaster estiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
