Crassula inandensis

Crassula inandensis, commonly known as the Cape snowflake, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped white or pink flowers during the winter months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula inandensis is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its ornamental appeal and ease of care.
