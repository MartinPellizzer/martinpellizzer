Cicerbita Macrophylla Subsp. Uralensis

Cicerbita macrophylla subsp. uralensis is a lesser-known member of the Cucurbitaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this plant thrives in cool, moist environments. It is distinguished by its large, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. Despite its ornamental potential, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Its unique features make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and cultivation.
