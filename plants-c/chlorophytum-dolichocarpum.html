Chlorophytum dolichocarpum

Chlorophytum dolichocarpum, commonly known as the long-seeded spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically narrow and grass-like. The plant produces long, slender flower stalks that bear small, white, tubular flowers. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.

The plant's ability to propagate through offsets makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
