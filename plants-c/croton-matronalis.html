Croton matronalis

Croton matronalis, commonly known as the Matron's Croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that range from deep red to yellow and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by orange to red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Croton matronalis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
