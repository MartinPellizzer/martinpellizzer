Cerastium decalvans f. orbelicum

Cerastium decalvans f. orbelicum, commonly known as the Orbelicum snowdrop, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves fully emerge. Its foliage consists of opposite, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

This frost-tolerant species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments.
