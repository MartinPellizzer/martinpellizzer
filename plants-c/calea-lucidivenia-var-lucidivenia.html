Calea lucidivenia var. lucidivenia

Calea lucidivenia var. lucidivenia, commonly known as the shining candleplant, is a striking tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, candle-like inflorescences that are often covered in a waxy, reflective coating. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers typically bloom in shades of white and yellow, adding a dramatic visual element to any landscape.
