Cyclopogon soniae-juaniorum

Cyclopogon soniae-juaniorum, commonly known as the Juaniorum cyclopogon, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are notable for their intricate morphology, which includes a distinctive lip that plays a role in pollination.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
