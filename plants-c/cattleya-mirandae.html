Cattleya mirandae

Cattleya mirandae, commonly known as the Miranda Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cattleya mirandae is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
