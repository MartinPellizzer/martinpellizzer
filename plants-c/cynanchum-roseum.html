Cynanchum roseum

Cynanchum roseum, commonly known as the pink milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Cynanchum roseum is known for its milky sap, which is characteristic of the Asclepiadaceae family.
