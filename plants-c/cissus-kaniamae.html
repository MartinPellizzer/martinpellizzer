Cissus kaniamae

Cissus kaniamae, commonly known as the Kaniama cissus, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and distinctive leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are often covered in tendrils, which help it climb and attach to surfaces.
