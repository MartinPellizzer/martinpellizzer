Carex × mastnyana

Carex × mastnyana, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Carex acutiformis and Carex russowii, exhibiting a combination of traits from both parent species. The plant typically forms dense clumps of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, usually green or brownish flowers. It thrives in moist to wet environments, such as marshes, pond edges, and stream banks.

Its ecological role includes stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
