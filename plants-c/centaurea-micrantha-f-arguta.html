Centaurea micrantha f. arguta

Centaurea micrantha f. arguta, commonly known as the argute centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers arranged in dense, compact clusters called capitula. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
