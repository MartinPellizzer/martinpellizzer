Calophyllum spectabile var. miquelii

Calophyllum spectabile var. miquelii, commonly known as the Miquel's flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with alternate arrangement and produces woody, elongated fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
