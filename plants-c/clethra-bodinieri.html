Clethra bodinieri

Clethra bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese sweet pepper tree, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a tart, slightly sweet flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Clethra bodinieri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its edible fruits.
