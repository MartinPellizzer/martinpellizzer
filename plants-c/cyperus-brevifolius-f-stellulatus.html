Cyperus brevifolius f. stellulatus

Cyperus brevifolius f. stellulatus, commonly known as the starleaf flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, such as swamps and seasonally flooded areas. Its leaves are often adorned with small, star-shaped markings, giving it its common name.

This ornamental plant is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in moist conditions.
