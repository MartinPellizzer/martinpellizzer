Coutareopsis coutaportloides

Coutareopsis coutaportloides, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a rare and striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers that resemble orchids. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of broad, glossy leaves that help it thrive in the humid, shaded environments of the forest canopy.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
