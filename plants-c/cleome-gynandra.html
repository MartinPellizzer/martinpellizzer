Cleome gynandra

Cleome gynandra, commonly known as spider plant or leafy vegetable, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are edible and often used in traditional cuisines across sub-Saharan Africa.

Cleome gynandra thrives in warm, tropical climates and is relatively easy to cultivate.
