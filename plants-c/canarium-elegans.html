Canarium elegans

Canarium elegans, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white, flame-like inflorescences that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Canarium elegans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a shade tree.
