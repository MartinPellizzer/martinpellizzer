Cinnagrostis mulleri

Cinnagrostis mulleri, commonly known as the Chinese ironweed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and dense clusters of small, pinkish to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cinnagrostis mulleri is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and hardy nature.
