Claytonia nevadensis

Claytonia nevadensis, commonly known as Sierra sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are usually fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve moisture in arid environments.

Claytonia nevadensis is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
