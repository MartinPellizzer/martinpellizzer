Centaurea litigiosa

Centaurea litigiosa, commonly known as the fighting centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and scrublands. Centaurea litigiosa is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its common name reflects the historical use of the plant in traditional medicine.
