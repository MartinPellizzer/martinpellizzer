Cyananthus inflatus

Cyananthus inflatus, commonly known as the inflated bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple.
