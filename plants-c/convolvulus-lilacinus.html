Convolvulus lilacinus

Convolvulus lilacinus, commonly known as sweet potato vine, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that range from green to purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or lilac in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Convolvulus lilacinus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
