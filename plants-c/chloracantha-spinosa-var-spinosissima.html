Chloracantha spinosa var. spinosissima

Chloracantha spinosa var. spinosissima, commonly known as the spiny chloracantha, is a variant of the chloracantha plant characterized by its more pronounced spines.

It is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions, typically found in southern Africa. The plant features small, green, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, round fruits.

This variant is adapted to survive in harsh conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
