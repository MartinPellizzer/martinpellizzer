Cliffordia spectabilis

Cliffordia spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cliffordia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are usually narrow and evergreen, providing a contrast to the vibrant floral displays.

Cliffordia spectabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
