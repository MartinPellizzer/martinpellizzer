Croton Draco Var. Genuinus

Croton draco var. genuinus is a rare and striking plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. This variety is prized by plant enthusiasts for its unique leaf patterns and ornamental value. Its care requires attention to soil moisture and nutrient balance to ensure healthy growth. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
