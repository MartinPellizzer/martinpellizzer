Clerodendrum reflexum

Clerodendrum reflexum, commonly known as the hanging clerodendrum, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are opposite and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Clerodendrum reflexum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
