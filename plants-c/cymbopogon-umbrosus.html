Cymbopogon umbrosus

Cymbopogon umbrosus, commonly known as the dark grass or umbrosus lemon grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often hidden among the foliage. It is known for its aromatic properties, with the leaves containing essential oils that have been used in traditional medicine.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often cultivated as a ornamental or for its aromatic qualities in various regions.
