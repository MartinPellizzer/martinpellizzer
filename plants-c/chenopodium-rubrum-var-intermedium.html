Chenopodium rubrum var. intermedium

Chenopodium rubrum var. intermedium, commonly known as red goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in dry, disturbed soils. The plant features alternate, ovate to triangular leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture. Its small, greenish flowers are arranged in clusters and are wind-pollinated.

It is often found in arid and semi-arid environments, such as deserts and along roadsides.
