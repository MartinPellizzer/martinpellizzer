Correa reflexa var. insularis

Correa reflexa var. insularis, commonly known as the Island Correa, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the island of Tasmania.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of red and pink, which bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
