Centaurea seridis var. maritima

Centaurea seridis var. maritima, commonly known as the maritime centaurea, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It is well adapted to saline and windy environments, making it a hardy species for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
