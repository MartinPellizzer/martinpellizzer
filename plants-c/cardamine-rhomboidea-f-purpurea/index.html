Cardamine Rhomboidea F. Purpurea

Cardamine rhomboidea f. purpurea is a rare and delicate plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is known for its distinctive purple foliage and small, clustered white flowers. This ferny plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after species among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and poorly documented in many regions.
