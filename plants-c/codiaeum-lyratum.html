Codiaeum lyratum

Codiaeum lyratum, commonly known as the "Rainbow Plant" or "Variegated Flame," is a striking tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid environments. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy capsules containing seeds.

Codiaeum lyratum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its eye-catching foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
