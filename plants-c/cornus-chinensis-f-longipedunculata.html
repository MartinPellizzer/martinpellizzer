Cornus chinensis f. longipedunculata

Cornus chinensis f. longipedunculata, commonly known as the Chinese dogwood with long peduncles, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Cornaceae and is characterized by its showy white bracts that surround small yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it produces red berries in the fall. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique floral display and adaptability contribute to its horticultural value.
