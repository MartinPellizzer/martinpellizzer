Caladium albopunctatissimum

Caladium albopunctatissimum, commonly known as the white-spotted arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with prominent white spots. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant, colorful patterns.

Caladium albopunctatissimum is a perennial that can be propagated through rhizome division or seeds.
