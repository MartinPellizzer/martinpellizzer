Camellia changningensis

Camellia changningensis, commonly known as the Changning camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a yellow or orange center. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers partial shade to full sun.

Camellia changningensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
