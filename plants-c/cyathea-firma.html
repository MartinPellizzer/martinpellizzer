Cyathea firma

Cyathea firma, commonly known as the firm tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical environments. Its rhizome system supports its growth, allowing it to form dense clusters in forest understories.

Cyathea firma plays an important ecological role in providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
