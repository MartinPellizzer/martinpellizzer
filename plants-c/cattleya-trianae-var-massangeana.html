Cattleya trianae var. massangeana

Cattleya trianae var. massangeana, commonly known as the Massangeana orchid, is a hybrid orchid prized for its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America, particularly Colombia. The plant produces pendulous blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
