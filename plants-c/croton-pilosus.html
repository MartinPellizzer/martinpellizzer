Croton pilosus

Croton pilosus, commonly known as the woolly croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.

Croton pilosus is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
