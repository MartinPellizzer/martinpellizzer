Cassia negrensis

Cassia negrensis, commonly known as the Negros cassia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain numerous seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in open woodlands and along roadsides.

Cassia negrensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
