Cladothamnus pyroliflorus

Cladothamnus pyroliflorus, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in coastal or inland regions. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.
