Calathea acuminata

Calathea acuminata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and color variations. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. Its foliage typically features a combination of green, silver, and pink hues, creating a visually appealing display.

Calathea acuminata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil for optimal growth.
