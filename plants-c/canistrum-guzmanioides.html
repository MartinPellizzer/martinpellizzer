Canistrum guzmanioides

Canistrum guzmanioides, commonly known as the Guzmanioides gooseberry, is a climbing shrub native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. Canistrum guzmanioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
