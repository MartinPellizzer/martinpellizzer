Ceropegia zeyheri

Ceropegia zeyheri, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia zeyheri is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
