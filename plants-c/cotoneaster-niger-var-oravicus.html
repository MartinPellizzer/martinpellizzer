Cotoneaster niger var. oravicus

Cotoneaster niger var. oravicus, commonly known as the Black Cotoneaster, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of central and southern Europe.

It features small, dark purple to black berries that are an attractive feature in the winter months. The plant has glossy, oval-shaped leaves that remain green throughout the year, providing visual interest in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in temperate climates.
