Cotoneaster multiflorus var. typicus

Cotoneaster multiflorus var. typicus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features small, white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and thrives in well-drained soil.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
