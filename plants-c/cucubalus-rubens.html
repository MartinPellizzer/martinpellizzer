Cucubalus rubens

Cucubalus rubens, commonly known as the red bignay, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by edible red berries. The berries are rich in antioxidants and are often used in local cuisines.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit.
