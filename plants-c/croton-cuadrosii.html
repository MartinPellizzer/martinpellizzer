Croton cuadrosii

Croton cuadrosii, commonly known as the Cuadrosii Croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The leaves are typically large, ovate, and have a velvety texture, adding to its visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is relatively low maintenance, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in warm climates.
