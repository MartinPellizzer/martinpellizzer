Caladium stevensonii

Caladium stevensonii, commonly known as the "Crimson Knight," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant belongs to the Araceae family and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces. Its foliage is the primary feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Caladium stevensonii thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
