Crassula hedbergii

Crassula hedbergii, commonly known as the "Laceleaf Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rosette-shaped foliage with fleshy, blue-green leaves that have a distinctive crinkled texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
