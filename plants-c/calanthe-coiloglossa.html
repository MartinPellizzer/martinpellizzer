Calanthe coiloglossa

Calanthe coiloglossa, commonly known as the spiral lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped lip that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a distinctive, elongated lip.

Calanthe coiloglossa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in tropical gardens for its unique floral structure.
