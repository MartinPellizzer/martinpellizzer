Calycostemma

Calycostemma, commonly known as the "California goldenbush," is a member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbel-like flower clusters.

Calycostemma is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
