Columbaria crenata

Columbaria crenata, commonly known as the crested or curled leaf plant, is a unique species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wavy, and often curled leaves that give it a visually striking appearance. Native to certain regions of South America, it thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow or white, typically blooming in the spring.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
