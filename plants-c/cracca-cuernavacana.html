Cracca cuernavacana

Cracca cuernavacana, commonly known as the Cuernavaca cracca, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow to orange ray florets and yellow disk florets, making it visually striking. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands.

Cracca cuernavacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
