Chenopodium × pseudopunctulatum

Chenopodium × pseudopunctulatum, commonly known as tumbleweed, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its sprawling growth habit and production of small, greenish flowers. The plant is native to North America and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its rapid spread across disturbed soils.

The plant's fibrous roots and succulent stems make it well adapted to harsh, dry conditions.
