Codiaeum hillianum

Codiaeum hillianum, commonly known as the flame leaf or hillianum croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. It typically has a shrub-like growth habit with smooth, glossy leaves.

Codiaeum hillianum is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
