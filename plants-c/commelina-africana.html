Commelina africana

Commelina africana, commonly known as African spiderwort, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Commelina africana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
