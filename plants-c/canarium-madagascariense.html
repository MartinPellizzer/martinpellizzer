Canarium madagascariense

Canarium madagascariense, commonly known as the Madagascar nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. It is valued for its edible seeds, which are rich in oil and used in traditional cuisine.
