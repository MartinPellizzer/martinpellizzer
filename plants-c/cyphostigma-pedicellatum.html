Cyphostigma pedicellatum

Cyphostigma pedicellatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or spider plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are dark green with a soft texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in umbels. Its roots are fibrous and can spread extensively, making it a resilient species.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
