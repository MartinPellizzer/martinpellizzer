Coelogyne latifolia var. latifolia

Coelogyne latifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, and often fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, providing a striking visual contrast to its colorful blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for both horticultural and commercial purposes.
