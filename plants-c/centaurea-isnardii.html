Centaurea isnardii

Centaurea isnardii, commonly known as the Iskenderun centaury, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features slender stems and striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a slight hairy texture.

Centaurea isnardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
