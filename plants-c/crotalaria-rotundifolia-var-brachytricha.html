Crotalaria rotundifolia var. brachytricha

Crotalaria rotundifolia var. brachytricha, commonly known as the broad-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, broad leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. It has a short stem with upright growth habit and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
