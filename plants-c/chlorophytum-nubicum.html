Chlorophytum nubicum

Chlorophytum nubicum, commonly known as the African Spear Lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its strap-like, grass-like leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. It is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Chlorophytum nubicum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
