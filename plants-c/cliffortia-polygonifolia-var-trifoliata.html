Cliffortia polygonifolia var. trifoliata

Cliffortia polygonifolia var. trifoliata, commonly known as the three-leafed cliffortia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in spring. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
