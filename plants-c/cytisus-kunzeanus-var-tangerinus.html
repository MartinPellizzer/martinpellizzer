Cytisus kunzeanus var. tangerinus

Cytisus kunzeanus var. tangerinus, commonly known as the Tangerine broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to its habitat. The plant has slender, woody stems and small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
