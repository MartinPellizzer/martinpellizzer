Carumbium pallidum

Carumbium pallidum, commonly known as the pale carumbium, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale green leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
