Camellia pitardii var. panxiensis

Camellia pitardii var. panxiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to western China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow or white center. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in cool, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and botanical collections.

The species is also valued for its potential in horticultural research due to its unique floral traits and genetic diversity.
