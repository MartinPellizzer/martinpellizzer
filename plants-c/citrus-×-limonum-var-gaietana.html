Citrus × limonum var. gaietana

Citrus × limonum var. gaietana, commonly known as the sweet lemon or gaietana lemon, is a variety of lemon characterized by its high sugar content and mild, sweet flavor.

It is a hybrid of Citrus limon and other citrus species, exhibiting a unique combination of traits from its parent plants. The fruit is typically small to medium in size, with a thick, yellowish-orange peel that is rich in essential oils. The leaves are glossy and dark green, often with a slightly serrated edge, typical of many citrus species.

This variety is valued for its use in both culinary and ornamental contexts due to its aesthetic appeal and flavorful fruit.
