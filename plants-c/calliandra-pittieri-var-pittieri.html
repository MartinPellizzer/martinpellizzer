Calliandra pittieri var. pittieri

Calliandra pittieri var. pittieri, commonly known as the red calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery, red or pink inflorescences that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has compound leaves composed of small, oval-shaped leaflets arranged in pairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its nectar-rich flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
