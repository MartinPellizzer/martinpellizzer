Clitoria dendrina

Clitoria dendrina, commonly known as the tree bean or velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with heart-shaped leaflets and produces vibrant purple or violet pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its woody stem and can grow up to several meters in height. Its seeds are encased in a fibrous pod and are used in traditional medicine and as a source of protein.

Clitoria dendrina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in agroforestry systems.
