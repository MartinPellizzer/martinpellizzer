Cotoneaster schansiensis

Cotoneaster schansiensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are typically eaten by birds, aiding in seed dispersal. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

Cotoneaster schansiensis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
