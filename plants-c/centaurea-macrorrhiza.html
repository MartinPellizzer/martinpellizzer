Centaurea macrorrhiza

Centaurea macrorrhiza, commonly known as the large root centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright blue or purple flowers in clusters. Its root system is thick and fleshy, which contributes to its common name.

Centaurea macrorrhiza is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.
