Centaurea isiliae

Centaurea isiliae, commonly known as the Isilis knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and bright yellow to orange-red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically reaches a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Centaurea isiliae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
