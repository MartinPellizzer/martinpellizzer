Cynanchum rockii

Cynanchum rockii, commonly known as Chinese milkweed or rock milkweed, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it is known for its milky sap, which is a common feature in the genus Cynanchum. Cynanchum rockii thrives in rocky and dry environments, often found in mountainous regions.

It has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
