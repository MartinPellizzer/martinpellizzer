Casearia fissistipula

Casearia fissistipula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved casearia, is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
