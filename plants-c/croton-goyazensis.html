Croton goyazensis

Croton goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis Croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with leaves that display a range of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are often lobed or deeply divided, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Croton goyazensis is valued in horticulture for its unique and eye-catching appearance.
