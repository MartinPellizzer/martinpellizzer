Cyrilla oldenlandoides

Cyrilla oldenlandoides, commonly known as the Florida holly, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in wetland habitats such as swamps and marshes. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a dark blue berry that contains several seeds.
