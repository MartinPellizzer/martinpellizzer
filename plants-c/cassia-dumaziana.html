Cassia dumaziana

Cassia dumaziana, commonly known as the African cassia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Cassia dumaziana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
