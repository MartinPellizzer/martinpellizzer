Croton tsiampiensis var. microphyllus

Croton tsiampiensis var. microphyllus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a glossy texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive red or orange berries. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
