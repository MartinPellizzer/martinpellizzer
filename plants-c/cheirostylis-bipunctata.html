Cheirostylis bipunctata

Cheirostylis bipunctata, commonly known as the two-spotted orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with distinctive dark markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cheirostylis bipunctata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
