Cymbidium tracyanum f. tracyanum

Cymbidium tracyanum f. tracyanum, commonly known as the Tracyanum orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are evergreen and provide a lush backdrop for its vibrant blooms.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
