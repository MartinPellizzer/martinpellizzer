Chamaecrista urophyllidia

Chamaecrista urophyllidia, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.
