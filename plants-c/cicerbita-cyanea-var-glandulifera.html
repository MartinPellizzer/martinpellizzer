Cicerbita cyanea var. glandulifera

Cicerbita cyanea var. glandulifera, commonly known as the spotted cowslip, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive purple spot at the base. The plant typically grows in moist meadows and woodland edges, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are alternate, lobed, and often have glandular dots, contributing to its scientific name.

The species is known for its ornamental value and has been cultivated for its attractive blooms.
