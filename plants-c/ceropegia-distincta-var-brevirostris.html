Ceropegia distincta var. brevirostris

Ceropegia distincta var. brevirostris, commonly known as the narrow-beaked ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, trailing stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, tubular flowers with a distinctive, hooded structure, typically in shades of pink or purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
