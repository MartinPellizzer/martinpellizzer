Clusia sipapoana

Clusia sipapoana, commonly known as the tropical evergreen shrub, is a member of the Clusiaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often covered in a layer of fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color.

Clusia sipapoana is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates and is often used in ornamental gardening.
