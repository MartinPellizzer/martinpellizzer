Calamagrostis bungeana

Calamagrostis bungeana, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes and stream banks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Calamagrostis bungeana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
