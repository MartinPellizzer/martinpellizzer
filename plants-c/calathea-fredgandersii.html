Calathea fredgandersii

Calathea fredgandersii, commonly known as the Fredganders' Calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, variegated foliage featuring deep green, silver, and pink patterns that change with light exposure. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, with a velvety texture that enhances its visual appeal.

Calathea fredgandersii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil to flourish.
