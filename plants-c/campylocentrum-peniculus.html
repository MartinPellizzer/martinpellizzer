Campylocentrum peniculus

Campylocentrum peniculus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved piper.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. It belongs to the family Piperaceae, which includes other species known for their aromatic properties.

Campylocentrum peniculus is often found growing in shaded, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
