Campanula rotundifolia var. euxina

Campanula rotundifolia var. euxina, commonly known as the pink bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features rounded, broad leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
