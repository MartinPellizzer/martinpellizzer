Croton scoriarum

Croton scoriarum, commonly known as the scarlet croton, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Croton scoriarum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
