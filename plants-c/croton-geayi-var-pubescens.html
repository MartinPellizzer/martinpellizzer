Croton geayi var. pubescens

Croton geayi var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy croton, is a tropical plant characterized by its distinctive, often brightly colored leaves.

The leaves are typically large, with a velvety texture due to the presence of fine, short hairs. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor cultivation.
