Campaccia venusta

Campaccia venusta, commonly known as the beautiful campaccia, is a striking flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. Campaccia venusta is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
