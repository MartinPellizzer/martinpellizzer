Crinum verdoorniae

Crinum verdoorniae, commonly known as the Verdoorn's Crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Crinum verdoorniae is known for its resilience and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
