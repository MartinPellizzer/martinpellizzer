Coleosanthus venulosus

Coleosanthus venulosus, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

The species is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
