Croton persimilis

Croton persimilis, commonly known as the silky croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage with a mix of green, yellow, and red tones. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
