Clutia tomentosa var. genuina

Clutia tomentosa var. genuina, commonly known as the woolly clutia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, white, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in humid climates.
