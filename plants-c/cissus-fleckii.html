Cissus fleckii

Cissus fleckii, commonly known as the African rock fig, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, fig-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for ornamental purposes.
