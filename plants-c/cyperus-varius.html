Cyperus varius

Cyperus varius, commonly known as the variable flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along riverbanks. Its appearance includes slender, grass-like stems with narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers.

Cyperus varius is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its attractive, upright growth habit.
