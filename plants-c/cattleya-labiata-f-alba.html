Cattleya labiata f. alba

Cattleya labiata f. alba, commonly known as the white-lipped cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking white petals and vibrant yellow lip.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. It features a sympodial growth habit, producing new pseudobulbs along the stem, which support its evergreen foliage. The flowers typically bloom in summer, showcasing a unique combination of color and form that makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and elegant appearance.
