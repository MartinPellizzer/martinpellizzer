Camaridium ctenostachys

Camaridium ctenostachys, commonly known as the silky grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silky hairs. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in panicles, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.
