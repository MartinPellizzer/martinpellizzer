Croton tristis

Croton tristis, commonly known as the black croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its milky sap and diverse leaf shapes. The plant features striking, palmately lobed leaves that vary in color from green to reddish-brown. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are often surrounded by specialized bracts.

Croton tristis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant foliage.
