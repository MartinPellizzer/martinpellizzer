Ctenanthe kummeriana

Ctenanthe kummeriana, commonly known as the velvetleaf caladium, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and philodendrons. Its foliage comes in a variety of colors, including deep red, green, and variegated patterns, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Ctenanthe kummeriana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
