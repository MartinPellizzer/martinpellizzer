Cynorkis fastigiata

Cynorkis fastigiata, commonly known as the upright orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, violet-colored blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cynorkis fastigiata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
