Cattleya intermedia var. superba

Cattleya intermedia var. superba, commonly known as the Superba Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant features tall, upright stems with large, showy flowers that can range in color from white to pink and purple. Its flowers typically bloom in summer and are known for their intricate, complex structure.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
