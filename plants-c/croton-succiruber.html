Croton succiruber

Croton succiruber, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and striking, colorful flowers that range from red to orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often striking members. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to high light exposure.

Croton succiruber is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and vibrant blooms.
