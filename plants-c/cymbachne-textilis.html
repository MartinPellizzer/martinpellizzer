Cymbachne textilis

Cymbachne textilis, commonly known as the textile grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. Its fibrous stems have been historically used in textile production, giving it its common name.

Cymbachne textilis plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various small organisms.
