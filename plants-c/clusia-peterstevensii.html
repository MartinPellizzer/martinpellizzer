Clusia peterstevensii

Clusia peterstevensii, commonly known as the Peter Stevens clusia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Clusiaceae.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly the Bahamas and the Greater Antilles. This evergreen shrub or small tree is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by orange or red berries.

Clusia peterstevensii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
