Chionanthus zeylanicus var. rostratus

Chionanthus zeylanicus var. rostratus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chionanthus, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Asia.

It features slender, arching branches and small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in temperate climates.
