Cicendia tenella

Cicendia tenella, commonly known as the slender zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils. The plant features slender, zigzagging stems that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, yellow flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Cicendia tenella is known for its unique growth habit and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
