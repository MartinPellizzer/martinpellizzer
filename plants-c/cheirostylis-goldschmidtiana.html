Cheirostylis goldschmidtiana

Cheirostylis goldschmidtiana, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with golden-yellow petals and a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the surrounding soil.

The flowering period usually occurs during the warmer months, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.
