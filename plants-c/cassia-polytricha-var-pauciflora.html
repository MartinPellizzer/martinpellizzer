Cassia polytricha var. pauciflora

Cassia polytricha var. pauciflora, commonly known as the slender cassia, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces yellow flowers in dense clusters, which are typical of the legume family. Its pods are slender and contain several seeds, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cassia polytricha var. pauciflora is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
