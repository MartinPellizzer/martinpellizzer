Clematis viticella var. cordifolia

Clematis viticella var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Europe.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple. The plant is characterized by its strong stems and ability to climb by means of small tendrils. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
