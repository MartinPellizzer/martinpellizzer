Calathea sphaerocephala

Calathea sphaerocephala, commonly known as the globe-headed calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns and a distinctive rounded leaf shape. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that are not particularly showy but add to its ornamental value. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage makes it a sought-after plant for collectors and enthusiasts of tropical flora.
