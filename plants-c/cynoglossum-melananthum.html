Cynoglossum melananthum

Cynoglossum melananthum, commonly known as black nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often glandular and emit a mild scent. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

While not edible, it plays a role in supporting pollinators and can be a weed in agricultural settings.
