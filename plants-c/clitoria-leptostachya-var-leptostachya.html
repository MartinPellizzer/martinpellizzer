Clitoria leptostachya var. leptostachya

Clitoria leptostachya var. leptostachya, commonly known as the small-flowered winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves and distinctive butterfly-like purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its rapid growth habit makes it useful for erosion control and as a cover crop in agricultural settings.
