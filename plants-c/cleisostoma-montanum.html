Cleisostoma montanum

Cleisostoma montanum, commonly known as the mountain cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth surface and a waxy texture.

Cleisostoma montanum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
