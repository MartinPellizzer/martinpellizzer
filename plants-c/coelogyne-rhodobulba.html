Coelogyne rhodobulba

Coelogyne rhodobulba, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in forested areas with high humidity and partial shade. The plant features a pseudobulb with long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers. Its flowers are showy and fragrant, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

The plant requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to thrive.
