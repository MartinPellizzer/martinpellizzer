Cynanchum trilobulatum

Cynanchum trilobulatum, commonly known as the three-lobed milkweed, is a perennial herb native to parts of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped with three distinct lobes, and it produces small, greenish-white flowers.

Cynanchum trilobulatum is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
