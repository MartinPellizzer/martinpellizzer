Conyza erythrolaena

Conyza erythrolaena, commonly known as the redseed fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in composite inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural areas.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive potential in some regions.
