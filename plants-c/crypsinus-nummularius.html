Crypsinus nummularius

Crypsinus nummularius, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value lies in its striking foliage rather than its flowers.
