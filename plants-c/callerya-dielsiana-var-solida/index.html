Callerya Dielsiana Var. Solida

Callerya dielsiana var. solida is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in shades of white and pale pink. This variant stands out for its compact growth habit and unique leaf structure. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Its cultivation is challenging due to specific environmental requirements.
