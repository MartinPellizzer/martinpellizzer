Cnicus americanus

Cnicus americanus, commonly known as American sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, featuring a hairy stem and lobed leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom from June to September. It is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, allowing it to spread easily across various habitats.
