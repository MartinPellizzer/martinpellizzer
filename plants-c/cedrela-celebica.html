Cedrela celebica

Cedrela celebica, commonly known as Java cedar, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae and is characterized by its straight, durable wood that is highly valued for furniture and construction. The plant has compound leaves with alternate arrangement, and its flowers are small and clustered in panicles. Cedrela celebica plays an important role in the ecosystems of its native habitats, providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
