Cyperus filiculmis var. macilentus

Cyperus filiculmis var. macilentus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically appear in late summer. The plant is native to wetland habitats and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in marshes, swales, and other shallow water environments.

Its morphology makes it adaptable to a variety of wetland ecosystems, contributing to its ecological significance.
