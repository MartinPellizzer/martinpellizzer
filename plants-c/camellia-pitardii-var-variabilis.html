Camellia pitardii var. variabilis

Camellia pitardii var. variabilis, commonly known as the variable camellia, is a species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in horticultural settings.
