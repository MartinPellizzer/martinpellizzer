Cymbidium recurvatum

Cymbidium recurvatum, commonly known as the recurved orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces pendulous flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium recurvatum is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
