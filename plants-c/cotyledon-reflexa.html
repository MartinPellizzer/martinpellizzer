Cotyledon reflexa

Cotyledon reflexa, commonly known as the fleshy cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often ribbed and covered in a powdery coating. The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Cotyledon reflexa is prized for its ornamental value and easy care, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
