Crotalaria kuiririensis

Crotalaria kuiririensis, commonly known as the Kuiriri crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive pod-like fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in warm, sunny environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, with a unique spurred structure that aids in pollination.
