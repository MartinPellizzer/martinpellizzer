Cryptandra tubulosa

Cryptandra tubulosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers are often pale green to yellowish in color and are visited by various pollinators.

Cryptandra tubulosa is an important component of local ecosystems and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
