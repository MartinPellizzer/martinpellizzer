Cymbalaria hepaticifolia

Cymbalaria hepaticifolia, commonly known as the liverleaf.

It is a flowering plant species native to parts of Europe and Asia, characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found growing along stream banks or in woodland areas.

Cymbalaria hepaticifolia is a perennial herb that spreads through both seeds and rhizomes.
