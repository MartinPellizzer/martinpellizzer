Clitoria plumosa

Clitoria plumosa, commonly known as the featherwing bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of violet or pink pea-shaped flowers. The plant is a climbing legume that produces small, hairy pods containing several seeds. Its flowers are pollinated primarily by bees and other insects, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Clitoria plumosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
