Chamitea reticulata

Chamitea reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves that are often used in herbal remedies. Chamitea reticulata thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and useful in traditional medicine.
