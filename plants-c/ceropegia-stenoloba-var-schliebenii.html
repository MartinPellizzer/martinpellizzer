Ceropegia stenoloba var. schliebenii

Ceropegia stenoloba var. schliebenii, commonly known as the Schlieben's African violet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
