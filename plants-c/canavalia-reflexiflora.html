Canavalia reflexiflora

Canavalia reflexiflora, commonly known as the reflexed cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pod fruits that contain edible seeds, similar to other members of the cowpea genus. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in agricultural systems for its nitrogen-fixing abilities.

Canavalia reflexiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
