Crassula scabra

Crassula scabra, commonly known as the rough crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, gritty texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula scabra is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
