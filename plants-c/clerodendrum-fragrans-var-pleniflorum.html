Clerodendrum fragrans var. pleniflorum

Clerodendrum fragrans var. pleniflorum, commonly known as the fragrant clerodendrum, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a glossy surface and a mild aromatic scent.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, humid climates.
