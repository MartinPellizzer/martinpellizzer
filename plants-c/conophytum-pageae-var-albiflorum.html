Conophytum pageae var. albiflorum

Conophytum pageae var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. This variety is prized for its unique appearance and adaptability to dry conditions, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
