Centaurea klokovii

Centaurea klokovii, commonly known as Klokov's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads.
