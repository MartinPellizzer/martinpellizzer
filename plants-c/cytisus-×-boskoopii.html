Cytisus × boskoopii

Cytisus × boskoopii, commonly known as the Boskoopii broom, is a hybrid shrub belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit and produces clusters of bright yellow flowers in early summer. The plant features small, trifoliate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to the Netherlands, it is cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Its flowering period makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, early-season blooms.
