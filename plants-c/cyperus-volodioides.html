Cyperus volodioides

Cyperus volodioides, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant typically grows from 30 to 100 centimeters in height, and its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread vegetatively.

It plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
