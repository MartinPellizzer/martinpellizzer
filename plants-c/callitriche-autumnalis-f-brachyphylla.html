Callitriche autumnalis f. brachyphylla

Callitriche autumnalis f. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-leaved waterstar, is a variant of the waterstarwort plant.

It is a small, aquatic or semi-aquatic plant characterized by its compact growth habit and short, rounded leaves. The leaves are typically arranged in whorls around the stem and are often submerged in water. This plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and ditches.

It plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for small invertebrates and contributing to water filtration.
