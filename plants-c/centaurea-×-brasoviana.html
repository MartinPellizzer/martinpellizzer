Centaurea × brasoviana

Centaurea × brasoviana, commonly known as the Brasov hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers and bristly, spiny leaves. Native to the Carpathian Mountains, it thrives in rocky and calcareous soils. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and blooms from June to August.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
