Coffea pulchella

Coffea pulchella, commonly known as the Ethiopian coffee tree, is a species native to Ethiopia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by red, fleshy berries containing the coffee seeds. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
