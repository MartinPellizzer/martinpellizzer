Crassula umbella

Crassula umbella, commonly known as the Umbrella Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact, rounded shape. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula umbella is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
