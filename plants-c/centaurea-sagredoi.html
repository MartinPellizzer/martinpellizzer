Centaurea sagredoi

Centaurea sagredoi, commonly known as the sacred centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically blue to purple in color, with a central yellow disk.

Centaurea sagredoi is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental and traditional medicinal uses.
