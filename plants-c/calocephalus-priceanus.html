Calocephalus priceanus

Calocephalus priceanus, commonly known as the Cape rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South Africa, characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is well adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
