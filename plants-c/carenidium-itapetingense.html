Carenidium itapetingense

Carenidium itapetingense, commonly known as the Itapetinga fern, is a rare and delicate member of the Polypodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its intricate, feathery fronds that are typically light green with a subtle sheen. Native to the humid tropical regions of Brazil, this plant thrives in shaded, moist environments with high humidity. Its rhizome system is fibrous and helps anchor it to rocky substrates or tree bark.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
