Cymbidium sigmoideum

Cymbidium sigmoideum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, dark pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or terrestrial species, often found in shaded forest environments. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a rosette, contributing to its unique appearance.

Cymbidium sigmoideum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various growing conditions.
