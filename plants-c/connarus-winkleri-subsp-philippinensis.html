Connarus winkleri subsp. philippinensis

Connarus winkleri subsp.

philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine connarus, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines. It belongs to the family Connaraceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically orange to red in color, which are an important food source for local wildlife.
