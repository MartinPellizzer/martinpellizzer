Calliandra bella var. trianae

Calliandra bella var. trianae, commonly known as the Triantha calliandra, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It features showy, bright red or orange inflorescences that are composed of numerous small, petal-like bracts. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
