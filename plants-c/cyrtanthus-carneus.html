Cyrtanthus carneus

Cyrtanthus carneus, commonly known as the red-flowered bell-shaped orchid, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color, with a distinctive bell-like shape. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces its flowers in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Cyrtanthus carneus is known for its long-lasting blooms and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
