Cirsium Anglicum Subsp. Filipendulum

Cirsium anglicum subsp. filipendulum is a striking plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. Known for its tall, spiky stems and large, showy purple flowers, it adds dramatic flair to any garden or natural setting. This subspecies is often mistaken for its more common relative, Cirsium vulgare, due to similar appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for many climates. Its unique structure and vibrant blooms make it a popular subject for botanists and garden enthusiasts alike.
