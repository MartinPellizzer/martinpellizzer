Capelio caledonica

Capelio caledonica, commonly known as the Scottish heath.

This plant is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the Scottish Highlands. It features small, needle-like leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers in early summer. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in heathland and moorland environments.

It plays an important role in supporting local wildlife, including insects and birds.
