Caldesia reniformis

Caldesia reniformis, commonly known as the kidneyleaf caldesia, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and wetlands. Its flowers are small and clustered in an inflorescence called a spadix, which is enclosed by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Caldesia reniformis is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
