Cymbilabia shanica

Cymbilabia shanica, commonly known as the Shanica orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of China, Vietnam, and Myanmar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Cymbilabia shanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant appearance and unique floral structure.
