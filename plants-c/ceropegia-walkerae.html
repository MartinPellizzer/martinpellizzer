Ceropegia walkerae

Ceropegia walkerae, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which are pollinated by specific moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
