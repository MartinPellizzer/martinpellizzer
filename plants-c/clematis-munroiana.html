Clematis munroiana

Clematis munroiana, commonly known as the Munro's Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with five to seven petals. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil and support for its climbing habit.

Clematis munroiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
