Centrostachys Abyssinica

Centrostachys abyssinica is a rare and striking plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia. It is known for its unique, star-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of red and orange. This plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine environments. Its unusual appearance has made it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, Centrostachys abyssinica remains poorly understood and is at risk due to habitat loss.
