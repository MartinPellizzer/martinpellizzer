Chlorophytum semlikiense

Chlorophytum semlikiense, commonly known as the Semliki spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to East Africa.

It features long, slender leaves that are typically green with white or silver markings, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. It is well adapted to grow in shaded to semi-shaded environments and requires moderate watering.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
