Crocosmia tenuiflora

Crocosmia tenuiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved gladiolus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces striking, sword-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
