Coleus darfurensis

Coleus darfurensis, commonly known as the Somali coleus, is a herbaceous plant native to the Horn of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Coleus darfurensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable conditions.
