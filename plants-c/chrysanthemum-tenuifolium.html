Chrysanthemum tenuifolium

Chrysanthemum tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chrysanthemum, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, linear leaves and produces daisy-like yellow or white flowers. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

The plant is known for its resilience and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
