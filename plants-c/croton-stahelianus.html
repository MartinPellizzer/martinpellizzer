Croton stahelianus

Croton stahelianus, commonly known as the Stahleman's croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of green, yellow, red, and orange hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions for its vibrant foliage.
