Cnidoscolus tenuilobus

Cnidoscolus tenuilobus, commonly known as the arrow leaf or arrowhead plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves, which are typically green and have a rough, textured surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by spiny, spherical fruits containing edible seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.

The plant is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine.
