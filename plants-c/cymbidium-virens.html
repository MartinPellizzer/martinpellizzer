Cymbidium virens

Cymbidium virens, commonly known as the green orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that produces tall, upright spikes of fragrant, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium virens is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in various Asian countries.
