Calamagrostis arundinacea var. ligulata

Calamagrostis arundinacea var. ligulata, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces feathery, blue-green flower plumes in late summer, adding visual interest to gardens. It prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, thriving in a variety of climates.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense clumps make it a low-maintenance choice for landscaping.
