Chamaecrista mwangokae

Chamaecrista mwangokae, commonly known as the Mwangokae burr legume, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

The plant typically grows in open woodland and grassland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its natural environment.
