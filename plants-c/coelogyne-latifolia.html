Coelogyne latifolia

Coelogyne latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with broad, fleshy leaves that store water, aiding in its survival in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Coelogyne latifolia is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
