Cistus helianthemum var. nummularius

Cistus helianthemum var. nummularius, commonly known as the silver cushion rockrose, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, rounded clusters of small, silver-gray, scale-like leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring to early summer, which are borne on short, hairy stems. It is well adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.

Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
