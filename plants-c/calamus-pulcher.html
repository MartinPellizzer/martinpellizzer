Calamus pulcher

Calamus pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful calamus, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a striking inflorescence consisting of a spadix surrounded by a spathe. Native to tropical regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
