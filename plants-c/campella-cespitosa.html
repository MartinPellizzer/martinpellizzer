Campella cespitosa

Campella cespitosa, commonly known as the tufted campella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Campella cespitosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
