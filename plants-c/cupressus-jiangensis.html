Cupressus jiangensis

Cupressus jiangensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi cypress, is a species of coniferous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Cupressaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and scale-like leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates. It produces small, spherical cones and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
