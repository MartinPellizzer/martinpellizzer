Cynoglossum album

Cynoglossum album, commonly known as blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Cynoglossum album is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
