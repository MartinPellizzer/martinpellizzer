Cattleya forbesii var. viridiflora

Cattleya forbesii var. viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking greenish-white petals and a distinctive yellow lip, which set it apart from other Cattleya varieties. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Due to its unique coloration and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
