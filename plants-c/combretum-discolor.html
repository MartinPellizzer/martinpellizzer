Combretum discolor

Combretum discolor, commonly known as the variable flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, papery, orange to red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. Its bark is smooth and varies in color from gray to reddish-brown.

Combretum discolor is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.
