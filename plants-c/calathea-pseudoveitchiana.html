Calathea pseudoveitchiana

Calathea pseudoveitchiana, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a tropical ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, mottled leaves with contrasting green and silver patterns, which are highly ornamental. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its aesthetic appeal.
