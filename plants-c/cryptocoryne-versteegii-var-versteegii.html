Cryptocoryne versteegii var. versteegii

Cryptocoryne versteegii var. versteegii, commonly known as the Java cryptocoryne, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features dark green, lance-shaped leaves that are often marked with subtle yellow or brown veins. This variety is highly adaptable, thriving in a range of water conditions and light levels. It is popular in aquariums for its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.

The plant is also known for its ability to tolerate low light and nutrient-poor substrates.
