Cleisostoma bicorne

Cleisostoma bicorne, commonly known as the horned cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, horn-like sepals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a unique coloration that ranges from white to pale yellow.

Cleisostoma bicorne is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its exotic appearance.
