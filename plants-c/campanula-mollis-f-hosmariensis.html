Campanula mollis f. hosmariensis

Campanula mollis f. hosmariensis, commonly known as the Hosmariensis bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features small, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, upright stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
