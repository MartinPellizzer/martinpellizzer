Craspedia uniflora var. viscosa

Craspedia uniflora var. viscosa, commonly known as the sticky craspedia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with a distinctive red or orange center.
