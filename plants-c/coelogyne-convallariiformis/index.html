Coelogyne Convallariiformis

Coelogyne convallariiformis is a striking orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, this plant captivates both botanists and horticulturists alike. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture levels. The plant's unique appearance and rarity make it a prized addition to many orchid collections. Its name, derived from the Greek words for "hollow" and "flower," hints at its intricate floral structure.
