Carex × Haematolepis Var. Vacillans

Carex × haematolepis var. vacillans is a fascinating sedge species known for its striking red-tinted foliage. This variant exhibits a unique coloration that sets it apart from other members of the Carex genus. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands and forest floors. The plant's ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance plants. Its ecological importance also makes it a valuable addition to native plant restoration projects.
