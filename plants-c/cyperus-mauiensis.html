Cyperus mauiensis

Cyperus mauiensis, commonly known as Maui flatsedge, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of freshwater bodies. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Cyperus mauiensis is adapted to the humid, tropical climate of Hawaii and plays a role in the local ecosystem.
