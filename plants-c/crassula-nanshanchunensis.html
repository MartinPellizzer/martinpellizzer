Crassula nanshanchunensis

Crassula nanshanchunensis, commonly known as the Nanshan Crassula, is a succulent plant native to China.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
