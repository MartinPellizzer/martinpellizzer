Carpesium humile

Carpesium humile, commonly known as the dwarf thistle, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. Carpesium humile is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
