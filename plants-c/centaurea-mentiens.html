Centaurea mentiens

Centaurea mentiens, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically growing in dry, rocky soils. The plant features spiny leaves and produces purple to reddish flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in meadows, wastelands, and along roadsides.

Centaurea mentiens is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
