Chirita spectabilis

Chirita spectabilis, commonly known as the showy chirita, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to white. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts worldwide.
