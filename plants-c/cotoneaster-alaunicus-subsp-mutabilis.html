Cotoneaster alaunicus subsp. mutabilis

Cotoneaster alaunicus subsp.

mutabilis, commonly known as the variable cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to the Caucasus region. It features small, elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside.

The plant produces clusters of white to pink flowers in spring, followed by orange to red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

However, it can be invasive in some regions outside its native range.
