Chusquea discolor

Chusquea discolor, commonly known as the silver bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright culms that are often covered in a whitish, powdery bloom. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in panicles, and it thrives in cool, moist environments.
