Coelogyne pachyglossa

Coelogyne pachyglossa, commonly known as the broad-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring a short, thick rhizome and a cluster of fleshy, strap-like leaves. The plant produces large, showy flowers with broad, lip-like petals that are often brightly colored. Its flowers typically bloom in summer and are characterized by their intricate patterns and fragrance.

Coelogyne pachyglossa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
