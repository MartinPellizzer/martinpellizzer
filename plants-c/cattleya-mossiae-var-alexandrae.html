Cattleya mossiae var. alexandrae

Cattleya mossiae var. alexandrae, commonly known as the Alexandra Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits striking floral characteristics.

It features large, showy blooms with a vibrant mix of pink and white petals, often accented with deep red markings. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in its natural habitat.

This variety is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
