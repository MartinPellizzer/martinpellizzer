Cyrtandra × ustulata

Cyrtandra × ustulata, commonly known as the hybrid blue ginger, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. Cyrtandra × ustulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a container plant.
