Calceolaria × pardanthera

Calceolaria × pardanthera, commonly known as the hybrid slipper orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Calceolariaceae.

It is a hybrid between Calceolaria lupulina and Calceolaria paradoxa, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms.

Its botanical interest lies in its hybrid origin and the diversity of its floral morphology.
