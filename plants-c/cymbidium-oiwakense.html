Cymbidium oiwakense

Cymbidium oiwakense, commonly known as the Oiwakense orchid, is a species native to Japan.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white or pale pink blooms. It prefers cool, humid environments and well-draining soil.

Cymbidium oiwakense is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
