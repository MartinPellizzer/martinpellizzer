Cotoneaster hypocarpus

Cotoneaster hypocarpus, commonly known as the red cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Cotoneaster hypocarpus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden conditions.
