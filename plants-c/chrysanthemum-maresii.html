Chrysanthemum maresii

Chrysanthemum maresii, commonly known as the Chinese chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, daisy-like flowers that come in a variety of colors including yellow, orange, and red. The plant is typically perennial, thriving in temperate climates and well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of dark green, lobed leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem.

Chrysanthemum maresii is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes and has cultural significance in traditional Chinese medicine.
