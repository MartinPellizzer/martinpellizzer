Coreopsis vulgaris

Coreopsis vulgaris, commonly known as the common coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often branching.

Coreopsis vulgaris is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and full sun conditions.
