Callitris pancheri

Callitris pancheri, commonly known as the Eucalyptus-like tree, is a member of the Cupressaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the arid regions of Western Australia. The plant features scale-like leaves that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its cones are woody and contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Callitris pancheri is adapted to survive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland dune environments.
