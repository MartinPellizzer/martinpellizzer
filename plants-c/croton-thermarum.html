Croton thermarum

Croton thermarum, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by woody, spiny capsules containing seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Croton thermarum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.
