Crocosmia × crocosmiiflora

Crocosmia × crocosmiiflora, commonly known as the African flame or torch lily, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Crocosmia in the family Xanthorrhoeaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to South Africa but has been widely cultivated in temperate regions for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and borders.

The flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their bold color and long-lasting blooms.
