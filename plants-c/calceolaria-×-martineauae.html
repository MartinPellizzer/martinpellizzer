Calceolaria × martineauae

Calceolaria × martineauae, commonly known as the hybrid slipper orchid, is a member of the Calceolariaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Calceolaria microphylla and Calceolaria violacea. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. It is native to South America, particularly Peru and Bolivia, and thrives in temperate climates.

The leaves are typically ovate and hairy, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
