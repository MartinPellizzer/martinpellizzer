Chomelia fuscoflava

Chomelia fuscoflava, commonly known as the dark-flowered chomelia, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Chomelia fuscoflava is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
