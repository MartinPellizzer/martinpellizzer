Convolvulus rubescens

Convolvulus rubescens, commonly known as the redstem morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, hairy fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail make it adaptable to various garden settings.
