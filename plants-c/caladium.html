Caladium

Caladium, commonly known as the "coleus" or "angel's trumpets," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is prized for its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, pink, white, and green. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or arrow-shaped and are the main ornamental feature of the plant. Caladiums thrive in warm, humid climates and prefer well-draining soil.

They are often grown as houseplants or in gardens where they can add a striking visual impact.
