Cimicifuga japonica var. macrophylla

Cimicifuga japonica var. macrophylla, commonly known as Japanese bugbane, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features tall, unbranched stems that bear large, white, lacecap flowers in late summer. The plant has compound, pinnately arranged leaves that are broad and serrated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Botanically, it belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
