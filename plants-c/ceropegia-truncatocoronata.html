Ceropegia truncatocoronata

Ceropegia truncatocoronata, commonly known as the dwarf string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and hanging baskets.
