Crotalaria kolbergii

Crotalaria kolbergii, commonly known as the yellow crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
