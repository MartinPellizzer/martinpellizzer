Camelina yunnanensis

Camelina yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan camelina, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Camelina yunnanensis is notable for its potential as a source of oilseed and its ecological importance in its native habitat.
