Crotalaria hossei

Crotalaria hossei, commonly known as the Cape York crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal or inland regions. It has been used traditionally by Indigenous communities for various purposes, including medicinal applications.

Crotalaria hossei is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
