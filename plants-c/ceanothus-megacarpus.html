Ceanothus megacarpus

Ceanothus megacarpus, commonly known as the coast ceanothus, is a shrub native to coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It features aromatic, blue-green leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers. The plant is adapted to coastal climates with mild, wet winters and dry summers. It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Its ecological significance makes it a valuable component of coastal ecosystems.
