Columnea ringens

Columnea ringens, commonly known as the coral vine, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Columnea ringens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
