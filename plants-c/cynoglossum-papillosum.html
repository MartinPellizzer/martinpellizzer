Cynoglossum papillosum

Cynoglossum papillosum, commonly known as the velvet leaf or purple heart, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from purple to blue, often blooming in late summer. It is typically found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, thriving in full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum papillosum is not only ornamental but also has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
