Centaurea rarauensis

Centaurea rarauensis, commonly known as the Rarau thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Romania.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its height can range from 30 to 100 centimeters, with dense foliage that helps it retain moisture.

Centaurea rarauensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
