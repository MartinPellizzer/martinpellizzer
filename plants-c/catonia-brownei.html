Catonia brownei

Catonia brownei, commonly known as the Brown's catonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flower heads that attract pollinators. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Catonia brownei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
