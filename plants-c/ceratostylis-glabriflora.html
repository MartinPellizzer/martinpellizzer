Ceratostylis glabriflora

Ceratostylis glabriflora, commonly known as the smooth-flowered horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, horn-shaped floral structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, elongated spur.

Ceratostylis glabriflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
