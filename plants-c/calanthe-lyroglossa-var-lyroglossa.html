Calanthe lyroglossa var. lyroglossa

Calanthe lyroglossa var. lyroglossa, commonly known as the lyre-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive lyre-shaped lip, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
