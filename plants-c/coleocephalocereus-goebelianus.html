Coleocephalocereus goebelianus

Coleocephalocereus goebelianus, commonly known as the Moon Cactus, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive red to purple spines and a unique, flattened stem called a globose body. The plant is often cultivated as a decorative specimen due to its striking appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Despite its beauty, it is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for beginners in cactus cultivation.
