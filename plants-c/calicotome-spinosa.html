Calicotome spinosa

Calicotome spinosa, commonly known as the spiny calicotome, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its thorny stems and small, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by small, woody fruits. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Calicotome spinosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping in arid regions.
