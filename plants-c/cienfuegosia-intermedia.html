Cienfuegosia intermedia

Cienfuegosia intermedia, commonly known as the Cienfuegosia shrub, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal and tropical gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
