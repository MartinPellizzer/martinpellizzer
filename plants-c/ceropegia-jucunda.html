Ceropegia jucunda

Ceropegia jucunda, commonly known as the silver string plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and small, waxy leaves. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia jucunda is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
