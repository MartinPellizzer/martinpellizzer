Cyperus albomarginatus var. pachyanthemos

Cyperus albomarginatus var. pachyanthemos, commonly known as the white-margin sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a distinct white or pale margin. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are often reddish or brownish in color. It thrives in wet or moist environments, such as marshes, swamps, and along water bodies.

Its botanical characteristics make it adaptable to various aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
