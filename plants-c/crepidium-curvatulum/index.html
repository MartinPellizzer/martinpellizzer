Crepidium Curvatulum

Crepidium curvatulum is a fascinating orchid native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique, curved flower spikes and delicate, fragrant blooms. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in forest understories. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and underappreciated species in the plant world.
