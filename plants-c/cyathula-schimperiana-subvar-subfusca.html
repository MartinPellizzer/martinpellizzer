Cyathula schimperiana subvar. subfusca

Cyathula schimperiana subvar. subfusca, commonly known as the darkish cyathula, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a distinctive cyathium inflorescence, which is a cup-like structure that houses its reduced flowers. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically yellowish-green, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in subtropical gardens.
