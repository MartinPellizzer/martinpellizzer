Cyclamen miliarakesii

Cyclamen miliarakesii, commonly known as the Cyprus cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Cyprus.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves with a deep green color and striking, bowl-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adding a touch of color to rocky or woodland habitats. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens and woodland borders.

Cyclamen miliarakesii is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate appearance.
