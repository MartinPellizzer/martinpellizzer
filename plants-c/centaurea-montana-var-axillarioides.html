Centaurea montana var. axillarioides

Centaurea montana var. axillarioides, commonly known as the Alpine blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is a perennial species that produces a compact, bushy growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine plant collections.
