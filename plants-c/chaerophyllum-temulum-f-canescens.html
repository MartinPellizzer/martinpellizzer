Chaerophyllum temulum f. canescens

Chaerophyllum temulum f. canescens, commonly known as the hairy cow parsley, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery leaves and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in umbrella-like clusters.
