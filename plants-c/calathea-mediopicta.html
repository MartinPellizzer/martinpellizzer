Calathea mediopicta

Calathea mediopicta, commonly known as the mid-picture calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with a unique pattern of green and silver markings. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.

Due to its aesthetic value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
