Crotalaria brevidens var. dorumaensis

Crotalaria brevidens var. dorumaensis, commonly known as the Dorumaensis Crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and savannas.
