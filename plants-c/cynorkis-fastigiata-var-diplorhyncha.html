Cynorkis fastigiata var. diplorhyncha

Cynorkis fastigiata var. diplorhyncha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a unique plant with striking botanical features.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers that are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Its morphology and floral structure make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and botanical research.
