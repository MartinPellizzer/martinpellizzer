Canangium odoratum var. fruticosum

Canangium odoratum var. fruticosum, commonly known as the sacred lotus or water hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough, textured surface.

The flowers are known for their aromatic fragrance, which is released during the day.
