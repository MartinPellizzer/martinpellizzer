Coreosma multiflora

Coreosma multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered coreopsis, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and it produces clusters of bright yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Its botanical name reflects its characteristic of bearing multiple flowers along the stems.
