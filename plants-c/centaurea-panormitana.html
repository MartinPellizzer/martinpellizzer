Centaurea panormitana

Centaurea panormitana, commonly known as the Pantelleria centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the island of Pantelleria in the Mediterranean.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its bright blue to purple flowers are surrounded by pale yellow ray florets, making it visually striking.

Centaurea panormitana is also noted for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
