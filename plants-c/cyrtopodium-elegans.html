Cyrtopodium elegans

Cyrtopodium elegans, commonly known as the elegant hooded orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, hood-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cyrtopodium elegans is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
