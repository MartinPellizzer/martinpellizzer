Citharexylum ovatum

Citharexylum ovatum, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, silver-green foliage and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of conditions.

The leaves are also known to attract cats, making it a popular plant for pet owners.
