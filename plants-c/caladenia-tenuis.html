Caladenia tenuis

Caladenia tenuis, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stem and delicate, spider-like flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a distinctive, elongated labellum that aids in pollination.

Caladenia tenuis is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in specific, threatened ecosystems.
