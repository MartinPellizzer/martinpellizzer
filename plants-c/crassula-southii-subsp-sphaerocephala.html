Crassula southii subsp. sphaerocephala

Crassula southii subsp.

sphaerocephala, commonly known as the Cape Town crassula, is a compact, succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
