Cynorkis baronii

Cynorkis baronii, commonly known as the Baron's orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features a short, stout stem with a few leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by horticulturists.
