Calliandra Metrosideriflora

Calliandra metrosideriflora is a striking flowering plant native to South America, known for its vibrant pink and red blooms. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. This species belongs to the legume family, making it a valuable addition to ecological restoration projects. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. With proper care, Calliandra metrosideriflora can flourish and add color to any landscape.
