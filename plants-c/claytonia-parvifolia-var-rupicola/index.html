Claytonia Parvifolia Var. Rupicola

Claytonia parvifolia var. rupicola is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western united states. it thrives in rocky and alpine environments where it forms small, cushion-like growths. its white or pink flowers bloom in early spring, adding a subtle beauty to its harsh habitat. the plant is known for its hardy nature and ability to survive in nutrient-poor soils. it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists due to its limited range and ecological significance.
