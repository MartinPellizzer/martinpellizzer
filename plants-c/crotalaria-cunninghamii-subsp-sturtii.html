Crotalaria cunninghamii subsp. sturtii

Crotalaria cunninghamii subsp.

sturtii, commonly known as Sturt's crotalaria, is a perennial shrub native to Australia. It is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and palmate leaves, which are deeply lobed.

The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it thrives in dry, sandy soils. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, particularly in the inland parts of South Australia and Western Australia.

This species is notable for its ornamental value and its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
