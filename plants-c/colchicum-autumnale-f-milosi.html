Colchicum autumnale f. milosi

Colchicum autumnale f. milosi, commonly known as the Milos hybrid autumn crocus, is a botanical variant of the autumn crocus.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant features showy, spurred petals and a distinctive, helmet-shaped structure at the base of the flower. It thrives in woodland and grassland habitats, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
