Cenesmon lineare

Cenesmon lineare, commonly known as the linear cenesmon, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying shades of white or pale yellow.

Cenesmon lineare is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
