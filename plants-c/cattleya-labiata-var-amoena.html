Cattleya labiata var. amoena

Cattleya labiata var. amoena, commonly known as the Amoena Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It features large, showy blooms with a distinctive coloration pattern, typically in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on trees or other plants without being parasitic. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
