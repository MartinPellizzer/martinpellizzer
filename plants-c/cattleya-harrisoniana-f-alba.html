Cattleya harrisoniana f. alba

Cattleya harrisoniana f. alba, commonly known as the White Harrison Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant white flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces large, fragrant blooms with a subtle scent. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
