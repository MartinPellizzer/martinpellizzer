Carex aristata var. laeviconica

Carex aristata var. laeviconica, commonly known as the smooth awn sedge, is a perennial plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist to wet environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to form dense colonies, contributing to soil stabilization and habitat for various wildlife species.
