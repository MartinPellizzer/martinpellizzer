Calanthe fimbriatomarginata

Calanthe fimbriatomarginata, commonly known as the fringed-margin calanthe, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces strikingly beautiful, lanceolate leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green to white, with a distinctive fringed margin on the petals.

It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
