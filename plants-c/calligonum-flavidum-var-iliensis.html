Calligonum flavidum var. iliensis

Calligonum flavidum var. iliensis, commonly known as the Ili Valley spurge, is a shrub native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its yellowish-green foliage and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora in its native habitat.
