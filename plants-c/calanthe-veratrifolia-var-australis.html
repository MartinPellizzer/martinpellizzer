Calanthe veratrifolia var. australis

Calanthe veratrifolia var. australis, commonly known as the Australian lady's tresses, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences of small, white to pale pink flowers. The leaves are typically broad and strap-shaped, arranged in a rosette formation around the plant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in rocky or forested areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
