Centaurea Sphaerocephala Var. Oligocentra

Bluehead centaury, scientifically known as Centaurea sphaerocephala var. oligocentra, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. This variant of centaury is characterized by its compact, spherical blue flower heads that stand out against its silver-green foliage. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, making it a resilient addition to xeriscaped gardens. Its unique appearance and adaptability have made it a popular choice among botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species within the Centaurea genus.
