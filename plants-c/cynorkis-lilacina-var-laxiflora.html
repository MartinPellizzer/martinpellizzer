Cynorkis lilacina var. laxiflora

Cynorkis lilacina var. laxiflora, commonly known as the laxiflora orchid, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to regions in Southeast Asia. The plant features slender, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of pale purple or lilac-colored blooms. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
