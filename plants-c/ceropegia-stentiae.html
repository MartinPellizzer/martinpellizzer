Ceropegia stentiae

Ceropegia stentiae, commonly known as the Stentia's trumpet vine, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, tuberous stems and produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellow. The plant has a creeping or trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability to arid conditions make it a notable species within the Ceropegia genus.
