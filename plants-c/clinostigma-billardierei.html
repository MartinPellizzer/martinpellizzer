Clinostigma billardierei

Clinostigma billardierei, commonly known as the pink fairy fan, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils of coastal regions. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers arranged in a fan-like pattern, which bloom during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silvery bloom.

Clinostigma billardierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
