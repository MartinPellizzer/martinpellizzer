Conophytum ectypum

Conophytum ectypum, commonly known as the "Cape Karoo beauty," is a small, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, water-storing leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the summer months. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Conophytum ectypum is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
