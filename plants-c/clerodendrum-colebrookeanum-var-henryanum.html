Clerodendrum colebrookeanum var. henryanum

Clerodendrum colebrookeanum var. henryanum, commonly known as the purple flame or red flame tree, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from red to purple, often with a yellow or white throat. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
