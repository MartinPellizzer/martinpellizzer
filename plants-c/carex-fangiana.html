Carex fangiana

Carex fangiana, commonly known as the Fang's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, India, and Southeast Asia. Carex fangiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and soil conditions.
