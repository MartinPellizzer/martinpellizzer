Calamus wrightmyoensis

Calamus wrightmyoensis, commonly known as Wright's slender reed, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Myanmar and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated rhizomes and narrow, grass-like leaves.
