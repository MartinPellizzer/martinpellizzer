Cymbidium aspidistrifolium

Cymbidium aspidistrifolium, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen orchid, is a species native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish-brown lip. It thrives in shady, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cymbidium aspidistrifolium is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
