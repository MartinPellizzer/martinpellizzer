Crinum broussonetianum var. yuccioides

Crinum broussonetianum var. yuccioides, commonly known as the Yucca-like Crinum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are blue-green in color and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical characteristics make it a striking addition to both tropical and temperate landscapes.
