Cartrema matsumurana

Cartrema matsumurana, commonly known as the Japanese spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The flowers bloom in the summer and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
