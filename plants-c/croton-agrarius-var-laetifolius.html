Croton agrarius var. laetifolius

Croton agrarius var. laetifolius, commonly known as the Happy Leaf Croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage, which often displays shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
