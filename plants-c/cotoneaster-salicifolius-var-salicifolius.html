Cotoneaster salicifolius var. salicifolius

Cotoneaster salicifolius var. salicifolius, commonly known as the willow-leaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Europe.

It features small, oval leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, resembling willow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and adaptability.
