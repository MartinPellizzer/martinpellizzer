Cyclanthera dressleri

Cyclanthera dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's cucumber, is a climbing vine native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It produces small, pale green, edible fruits that resemble cucumbers in appearance. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cyclanthera dressleri is valued for its unique flavor and is sometimes cultivated for culinary use.
