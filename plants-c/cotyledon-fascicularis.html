Cotyledon fascicularis

Cotyledon fascicularis, commonly known as the compact cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings, which help it store water and perform photosynthesis efficiently. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and striking foliage.

Cotyledon fascicularis prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
