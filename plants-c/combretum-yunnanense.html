Combretum yunnanense

Combretum yunnanense, commonly known as the Yunnan flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
