Casearia commutata

Casearia commutata, commonly known as the silky casearia, is a species of flowering plant in the Salicaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, Colombia, and Peru. Casearia commutata is often found in humid, lowland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
