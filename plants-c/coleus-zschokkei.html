Coleus zschokkei

Coleus zschokkei, commonly known as the African violet, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features upright, branching stems and heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Coleus zschokkei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
