Cynometra ramiflora subsp. bijuga

Cynometra ramiflora subsp.

bijuga, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to regions of Africa. It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant produces small, round fruits that contain hard, woody seeds. It is valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.

The species is also important in traditional medicine and ecological systems.
