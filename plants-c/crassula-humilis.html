Crassula humilis

Crassula humilis, commonly known as the dwarf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula humilis is often grown as a decorative houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
