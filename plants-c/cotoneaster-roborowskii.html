Cotoneaster roborowskii

Cotoneaster roborowskii, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China and Korea.

It features small, orange-red berries that are an important food source for birds and other wildlife. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall, contributing to its seasonal visual appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

Cotoneaster roborowskii is also valued for its ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
