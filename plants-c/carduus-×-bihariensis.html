Carduus × bihariensis

Carduus × bihariensis, commonly known as the Himalayan thistle.

It is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. Native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas, it thrives in cold, arid environments. The plant has a rosette growth form, with basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Its medicinal properties have been explored in traditional medicine for their potential anti-inflammatory and antiseptic effects.
