Castilleja affinis subsp. neglecta

Castilleja affinis subsp.

neglecta, commonly known as the slender stickseed, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
