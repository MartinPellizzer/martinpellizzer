Cassia revoluta

Cassia revoluta, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with multiple pairs of ovate to lanceolate leaflets. The plant produces long, pendulous clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Cassia revoluta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its showy inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun for optimal growth.
