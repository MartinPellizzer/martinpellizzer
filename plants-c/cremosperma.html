Cremosperma

Cremosperma, commonly known as the silky bean, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

These plants are characterized by their slender, often climbing stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The flowers are typically small and clustered in racemes, with a distinctive pink or purple coloration. The fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Cremosperma species are native to various regions in Africa and are adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
