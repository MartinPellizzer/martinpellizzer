Conopholis Americana

Conopholis americana is a unique flowering plant native to the eastern united states. it is known for its distinctive appearance and specialized ecological role. this plant lacks chlorophyll and relies on mycorrhizal fungi for nutrients. it grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and forests. its presence indicates a healthy ecosystem and contributes to biodiversity.
