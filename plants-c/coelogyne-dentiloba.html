Coelogyne dentiloba

Coelogyne dentiloba, commonly known as the toothed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with narrow, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant blooms in clusters, with each flower having a distinctive, tooth-like appearance on its lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne dentiloba is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate, long-lasting blooms.
