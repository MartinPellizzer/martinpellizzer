Cryphiacanthus barbadensis

Cryphiacanthus barbadensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.

The flowers typically bloom in spring and summer, adding a touch of color to indoor spaces.
