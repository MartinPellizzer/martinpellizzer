Cotyledon breviflora f. purpureoapiculata

Cotyledon breviflora f. purpureoapiculata, commonly known as the Purple-tipped Cotyledon, is a compact, succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a distinctive purple or reddish hue at the tips. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ability to store water, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens or indoor collections.
