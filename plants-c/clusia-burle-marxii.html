Clusia burle-marxii

Clusia burle-marxii, commonly known as the Burle Marx Clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions.

Clusia burle-marxii is also known for its resilience to drought and pollution, making it a popular choice for urban landscaping.
