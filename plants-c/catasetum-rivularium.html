Catasetum rivularium

Catasetum rivularium, commonly known as the river catasetum, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows in wet, shaded environments near streams and rivers. The plant features long, arching stems with clustered, showy flowers that are often yellow or orange in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the moist soil and water.

The orchid is noted for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with high rainfall.
