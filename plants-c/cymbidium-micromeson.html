Cymbidium micromeson

Cymbidium micromeson, commonly known as the miniature cymbidium, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact size and elegant, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
