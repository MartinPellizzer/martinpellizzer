Cnicus galactites

Cnicus galactites, commonly known as the yellow centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are hairy and alternate, while its yellow flowers bloom in summer.

It is often used in traditional medicine and as a ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
