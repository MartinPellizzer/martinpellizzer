Cymbidium inconspicuum

Cymbidium inconspicuum, commonly known as the inconspicuous orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, pale green to white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Cymbidium inconspicuum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
