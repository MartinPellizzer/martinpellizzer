Crotalaria kurtii

Crotalaria kurtii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used as a protein source in some regions. Crotalaria kurtii is also known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids and should be handled with care.
