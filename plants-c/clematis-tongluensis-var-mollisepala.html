Clematis tongluensis var. mollisepala

Clematis tongluensis var. mollisepala, commonly known as the soft-petaled clematis, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and showy, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The foliage is typically heart-shaped and has a smooth texture. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various garden conditions.
