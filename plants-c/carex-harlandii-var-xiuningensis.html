Carex harlandii var. xiuningensis

Carex harlandii var. xiuningensis, commonly known as the Xiuning sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and dense, cylindrical inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

The Xiuning sedge is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wet habitats.
