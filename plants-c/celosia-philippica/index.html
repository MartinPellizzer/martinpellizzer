Celosia Philippica

Celosia philippica is a vibrant flowering plant native to the Philippines known for its striking, spiky blooms. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. With proper care, Celosia philippica can bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
