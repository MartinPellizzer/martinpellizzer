Calea arachnoidea

Calea arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider plant or spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Calea arachnoidea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
