Chorizanthe thurberi

Chorizanthe thurberi, commonly known as Thurber's choris, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers.
