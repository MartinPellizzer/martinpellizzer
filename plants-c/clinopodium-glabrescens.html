Clinopodium glabrescens

Clinopodium glabrescens, commonly known as slender wood betony, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in late summer.

Clinopodium glabrescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in herbal medicine.
