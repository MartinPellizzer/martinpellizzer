Combretum kottoense

Combretum kottoense, commonly known as the Kottoense flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. It is typically found in moist, well-drained soils in savanna or woodland habitats.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
