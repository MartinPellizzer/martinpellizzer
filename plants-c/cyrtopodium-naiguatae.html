Cyrtopodium naiguatae

Cyrtopodium naiguatae, commonly known as the Naiguata orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the warmer months. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrate.

Cyrtopodium naiguatae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to light and humidity levels for optimal growth.
