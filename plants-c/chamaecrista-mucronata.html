Chamaecrista mucronata

Chamaecrista mucronata, commonly known as the sicklepod or smallleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by bean-like pods containing seeds. Its leaves are bipinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Chamaecrista mucronata is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
