Croton picturatum

Croton picturatum, commonly known as the picture croton, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow, creating a visually captivating appearance. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking foliage. Its leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape and are often adorned with intricate patterns.

Croton picturatum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
