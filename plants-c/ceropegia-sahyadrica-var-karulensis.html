Ceropegia sahyadrica var. karulensis

Ceropegia sahyadrica var. karulensis, commonly known as the Karulensis milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
