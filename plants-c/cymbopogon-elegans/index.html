Cymbopogon Elegans

Elegant cymbopogon, scientifically known as Cymbopogon elegans, is a striking grass species native to the wet tropical regions of Africa and Asia. With its slender, upright stems and finely cut leaves, it adds a refined touch to both natural and cultivated landscapes. This plant is often mistaken for lemongrass due to its similar appearance and aromatic qualities. Its fragrant oils have been used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy for centuries. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more widely cultivated grasses.
