Canna × orchioides

Canna × orchioides, commonly known as the orchid cannas, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Canna indica and Canna edulis.

It is characterized by its large, showy inflorescences that resemble orchids, featuring vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 feet, with broad, arrow-shaped leaves that are a striking green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Canna × orchioides is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and ability to add a tropical flair to landscapes.
