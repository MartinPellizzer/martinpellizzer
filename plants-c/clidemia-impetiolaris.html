Clidemia impetiolaris

Clidemia impetiolaris, commonly known as the Hawaiian mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions outside Hawaii, where it can outcompete native flora.
