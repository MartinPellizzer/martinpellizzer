Christia Obcordata

Christia obcordata is a flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the legume family and is known for its delicate pink to purple flowers. this annual plant thrives in moist, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands. its small obcordate leaves and showy blooms make it a unique addition to native plant gardens. due to habitat loss, it is considered a threatened species in some regions.
