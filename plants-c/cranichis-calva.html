Cranichis calva

Cranichis calva, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stem and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with each stem producing a single flower at the apex. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem.

Cranichis calva is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in well-drained environments.
