Cucurbita melopepo var. pyriformis

Cucurbita melopepo var. pyriformis, commonly known as the horned melon, is a tropical vine native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, orange to red fruit with a thick, leathery rind. The fruit contains a sweet, juicy, seed-filled pulp that is often used in local cuisines. The plant produces large, palmate leaves and yellowish-orange flowers.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
