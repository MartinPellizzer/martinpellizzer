Cystopteris squamata

Cystopteris squamata, commonly known as the silver maidenhair fern, is a species of fern characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas. The plant features small, scale-like structures on its fronds, which give it its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
