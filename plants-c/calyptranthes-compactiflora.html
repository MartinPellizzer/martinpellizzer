Calyptranthes compactiflora

Calyptranthes compactiflora, commonly known as the compact-flowered calyptranthes, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, rounded foliage with small, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is often used in rock gardens or as a container plant.
