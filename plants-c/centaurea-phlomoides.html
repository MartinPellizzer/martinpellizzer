Centaurea phlomoides

Centaurea phlomoides, commonly known as the narrowleaf heliotrope, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and clusters of small, pink to purple flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and rocky areas.

Centaurea phlomoides is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early autumn.
