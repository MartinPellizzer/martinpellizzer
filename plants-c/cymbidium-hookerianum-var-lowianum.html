Cymbidium hookerianum var. lowianum

Cymbidium hookerianum var. lowianum, commonly known as the Low's orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often reaching lengths of up to 30 centimeters.

This variety is prized for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
