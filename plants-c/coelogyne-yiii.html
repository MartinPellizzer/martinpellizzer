Coelogyne yiii

Coelogyne yiii, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, golden-yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered with a thick, leathery epidermis.

Coelogyne yiii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for horticultural purposes.
