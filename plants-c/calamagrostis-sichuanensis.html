Calamagrostis sichuanensis

Calamagrostis sichuanensis, commonly known as Sichuan reed, is a perennial grass native to China.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, spike-like flowers. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.

Calamagrostis sichuanensis is also known for its ecological importance in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
