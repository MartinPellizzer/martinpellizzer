Calliandra fasciculata var. fasciculata

Calliandra fasciculata var. fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered calliandra, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, bright yellow flowers. The plant has compound leaves with multiple pairs of elliptic leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological biodiversity.
