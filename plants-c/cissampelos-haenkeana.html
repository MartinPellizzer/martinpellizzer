Cissampelos haenkeana

Cissampelos haenkeana, commonly known as the African moon vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is characterized by its unique, serpentine stems that can twine around supports. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
