Cassia langsdorffii

Cassia langsdorffii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces elongated, flat pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia langsdorffii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
