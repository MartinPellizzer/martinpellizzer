Cordia guanacastensis

Cordia guanacastensis, commonly known as the Guanacaste tree, is a large evergreen tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is also used in traditional medicine and for its durable wood.
