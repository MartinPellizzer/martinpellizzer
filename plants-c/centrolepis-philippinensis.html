Centrolepis philippinensis

Centrolepis philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine grass.

It is a flowering plant native to the Philippines, characterized by its tufted, grass-like appearance. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Centrolepis philippinensis is valued for its ornamental potential and ecological role in native ecosystems.
