Collinsonia tuberosa

Collinsonia tuberosa, commonly known as the pinkroot or Indian pink, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, pink to red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a distinctive tuberous root system, which is its namesake and a key feature for propagation. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
