Campanopsis stellarioides var. integrifolia

Campanopsis stellarioides var. integrifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern China.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in racemes. The leaves are broad and entire, giving the plant its varietal name "integrifolia.". It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural habitats.
