Coelogyne philippinensis

Coelogyne philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Philippines.

It features a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, supporting its growth in tropical climates. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with intricate patterns. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, adapted to capture sunlight efficiently.

Coelogyne philippinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
