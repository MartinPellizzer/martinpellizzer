Clusia leprantha

Clusia leprantha, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Clusia leprantha is also valued for its ability to tolerate shade and poor soil conditions.
