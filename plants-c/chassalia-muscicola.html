Chassalia muscicola

Chassalia muscicola, commonly known as the musk orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has a slender, erect stem that bears several showy, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive musk-like fragrance. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from the soil.

Chassalia muscicola is often found in association with other orchid species in its native habitat.
