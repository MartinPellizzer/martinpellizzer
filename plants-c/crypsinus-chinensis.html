Crypsinus chinensis

Crypsinus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden beneath a leaf-like structure called a spathe. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and its air-purifying qualities.

Crypsinus chinensis is also valued for its resilience and ease of care, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.
