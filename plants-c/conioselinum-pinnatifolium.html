Conioselinum pinnatifolium

Conioselinum pinnatifolium, commonly known as the slender parsley.

It is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. The plant features finely divided, pinnately arranged leaves that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in umbels, typical of the Apiaceae family.

Conioselinum pinnatifolium is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and mild scent.
