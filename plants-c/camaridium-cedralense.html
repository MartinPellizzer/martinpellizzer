Camaridium cedralense

Camaridium cedralense, commonly known as the Cedralense Camaridium, is a rare flowering plant native to the subtropical regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within dense forests. Its flowering period occurs during the late spring and early summer months.
