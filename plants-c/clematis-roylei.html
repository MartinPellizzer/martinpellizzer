Clematis roylei

Clematis roylei, commonly known as the Kashmir clematis, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in late spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Clematis roylei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
