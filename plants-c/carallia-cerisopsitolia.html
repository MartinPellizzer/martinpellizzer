Carallia cerisopsitolia

Carallia cerisopsitolia, commonly known as the sweetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Phyllanthaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often glossy and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange to red fruits containing several seeds.
