Conophytum truncatellum

Conophytum truncatellum, commonly known as the "Truncate Crambe," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Conophytum truncatellum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
