Coelogyne padangensis

Coelogyne padangensis, commonly known as the Padang orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in two rows along the stem. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Coelogyne padangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
