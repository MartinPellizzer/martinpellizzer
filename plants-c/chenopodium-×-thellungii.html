Chenopodium × thellungii

Chenopodium × thellungii, commonly known as hybrid goosefoot, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Chenopodium album and Chenopodium berlandieri.

It is an annual herb characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating called bloom. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its stems are erect and can grow up to 60 cm in height, and it is often found in disturbed soils such as roadsides and fields.

Chenopodium × thellungii is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
