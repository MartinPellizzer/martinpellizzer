Cymbopogon distans

Cymbopogon distans, commonly known as the narrowleaf lemon grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, often found in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are aromatic and emit a citrus scent when crushed. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to brown flowers.

Cymbopogon distans is valued for its essential oils, which are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
