Callista

Callista, commonly known as the star anise plant, is a member of the Schisandraceae family.

It is native to regions of China, Vietnam, and Indonesia, where it thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which is used in both culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Callista's botanical features make it a valuable plant in both horticulture and herbal practices.
