Cistus formosissimus

Cistus formosissimus, commonly known as the beautiful rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. The plant produces vibrant pink or purple flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Cistus formosissimus is also valued for its role in supporting local biodiversity and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
