Chrysanthemum cinerarium

Chrysanthemum cinerarium, commonly known as the cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow or white flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its distinctive appearance and ability to thrive in coastal climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Chrysanthemum cinerarium is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
