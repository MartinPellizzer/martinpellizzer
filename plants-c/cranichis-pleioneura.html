Cranichis pleioneura

Cranichis pleioneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, pale green or white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
