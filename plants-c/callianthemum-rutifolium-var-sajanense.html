Callianthemum rutifolium var. sajanense

Callianthemum rutifolium var. sajanense, commonly known as the Sajanense rock jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features a compact rosette of hairy, spoon-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardiness in alpine garden settings.
