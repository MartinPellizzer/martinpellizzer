Cleistocactus colubrinus var. grossei

Cleistocactus colubrinus var. grossei, commonly known as the Grosse's columnar cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne in clusters at the top of the stems. Its stems are ribbed and covered in short, white spines, providing a distinctive appearance.

This cactus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
