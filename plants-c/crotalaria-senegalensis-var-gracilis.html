Crotalaria senegalensis var. gracilis

Crotalaria senegalensis var. gracilis, commonly known as slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in semi-arid conditions, making it an adaptable species in various ecosystems.

Its botanical features make it both ecologically significant and potentially useful in agricultural or medicinal contexts.
