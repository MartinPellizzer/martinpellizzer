Calappa eriospatha

Calappa eriospatha, commonly known as the woolly calappa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are covered in a dense layer of fine, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Calappa eriospatha is often found in coastal or mountainous regions where it thrives in well-drained conditions.
