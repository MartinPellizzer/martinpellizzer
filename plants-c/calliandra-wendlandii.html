Calliandra wendlandii

Calliandra wendlandii, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a striking visual appeal. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Calliandra wendlandii is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.
