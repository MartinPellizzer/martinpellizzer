Cotyledon attenuata var. purpurea

Cotyledon attenuata var. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Leaf Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a distinctive purple or reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to maintain its vibrant coloration.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
