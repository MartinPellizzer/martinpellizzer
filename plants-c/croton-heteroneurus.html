Croton heteroneurus

Croton heteroneurus, commonly known as the zigzag croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging stems and vibrant, colorful leaves that range from green to red and orange. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often striking foliage. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, textured surface.

Croton heteroneurus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
