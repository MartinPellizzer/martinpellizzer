Calathea anderssonii

Calathea anderssonii, commonly known as the Midnight Beauty Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate leaf patterns that display a combination of deep green, silver, and purple hues. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that enhances their visual appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
