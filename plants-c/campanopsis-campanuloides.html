Campanopsis campanuloides

Campanopsis campanuloides, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical with a slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
