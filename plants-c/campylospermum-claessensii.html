Campylospermum claessensii

Campylospermum claessensii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and showy flowers make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
