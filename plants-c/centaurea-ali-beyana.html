Centaurea ali-beyana

Centaurea ali-beyana, commonly known as the Ali-Bey's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Centaurea ali-beyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
