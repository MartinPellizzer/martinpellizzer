Cymbopogon munroi

Cymbopogon munroi, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and tall, slender inflorescences. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its aromatic leaves are sometimes used in aromatherapy and as a natural insect repellent.
