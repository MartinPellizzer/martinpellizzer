Cymbalaria globosa

Cymbalaria globosa, commonly known as the globular figwart, is a flowering plant in the family Scrophulariaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its fleshy, globular leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-purple flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in ecological studies and horticulture.
