Crassula longistyla

Crassula longistyla, commonly known as the Long-stemmed Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the summer months. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water, making it drought-tolerant.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
