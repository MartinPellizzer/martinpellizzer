Colona grandiflora

Colona grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora colona, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Colona grandiflora is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display.
