Carpesium nepalense var. nepalense

Carpesium nepalense var. nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine environments, thriving at high elevations. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small insects.
