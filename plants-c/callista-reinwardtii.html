Callista reinwardtii

Callista reinwardtii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and clusters of yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
