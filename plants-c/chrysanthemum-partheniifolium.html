Chrysanthemum partheniifolium

Chrysanthemum partheniifolium, commonly known as the New Zealand chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features upright stems covered in finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces abundant yellow to orange flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
