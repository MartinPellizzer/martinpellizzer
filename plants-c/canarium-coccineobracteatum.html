Canarium coccineobracteatum

Canarium coccineobracteatum, commonly known as the red-bract canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by round, fleshy fruits that are orange-red in color. Its fruit is edible and has a sweet, acidic flavor, making it valuable in local cuisines.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its timber.
