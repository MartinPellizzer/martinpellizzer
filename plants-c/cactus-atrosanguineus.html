Cactus atrosanguineus

Cactus atrosanguineus, commonly known as the blood red cactus, is a member of the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the arid regions of Mexico and is characterized by its distinctive red or pink spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It has a cylindrical stem that stores water, allowing it to survive in dry environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
