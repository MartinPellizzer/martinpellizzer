Crassula verticillaris

Crassula verticillaris, commonly known as the Christmas Chandelier, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves arranged in whorls around the stems, giving it a unique, chandelier-like appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
