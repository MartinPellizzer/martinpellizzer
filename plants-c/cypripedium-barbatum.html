Cypripedium barbatum

Cypripedium barbatum, commonly known as the beard tassel orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that feature a single, upright lip with a prominent, hairy callus. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single stem that bears the floral display.

Cypripedium barbatum is a member of the orchid family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
