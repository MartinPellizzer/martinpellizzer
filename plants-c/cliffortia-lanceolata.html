Cliffortia lanceolata

Cliffortia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It is well adapted to arid environments and can survive with minimal water.

Its unique morphology and resilience make it a notable species in its native habitat.
