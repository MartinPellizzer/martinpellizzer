Clerodendrum fragrans var. multiplex

Clerodendrum fragrans var. multiplex, commonly known as the fragrant cleerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
