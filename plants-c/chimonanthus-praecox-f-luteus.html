Chimonanthus praecox f. luteus

Chimonanthus praecox f. luteus, commonly known as the yellow fragrant chimonanthus, is a cultivar of the winter jasmine plant.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, showy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant is a deciduous shrub with opposite, simple, and ovate leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value lies in its early blooming habit and vibrant floral display.
