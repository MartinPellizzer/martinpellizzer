Callista × vexabilis

Callista × vexabilis, commonly known as the hybrid callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early autumn.
