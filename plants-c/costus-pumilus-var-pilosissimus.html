Costus pumilus var. pilosissimus

Costus pumilus var. pilosissimus, commonly known as the silky costus, is a compact herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are densely covered with fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or container cultivation.

Its ornamental value lies in its unique foliage and delicate floral display.
