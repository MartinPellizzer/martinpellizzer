Chlorophytum bulbinifolium

Chlorophytum bulbinifolium, commonly known as the bulbiferous spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves that are typically gray-green with a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white flowers, followed by spherical, green seed pods. It is a hardy, low-maintenance plant that thrives in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
