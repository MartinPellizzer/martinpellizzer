Coelidium perplexum

It is characterized by its small, bladder-like structures that trap tiny aquatic organisms, aiding in its nutrient acquisition. Coelidium perplexum is notable for its ability to photosynthesize and supplement its nutrition through both aquatic and carnivorous means.
