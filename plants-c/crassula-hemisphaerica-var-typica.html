Crassula hemisphaerica var. typica

Crassula hemisphaerica var. typica, commonly known as the Roundleaf Crassula, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded shape and fleshy, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
