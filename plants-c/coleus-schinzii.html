Coleus schinzii

Coleus schinzii, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its upright growth habit and strikingly colored foliage. The leaves are typically velvety and come in shades of green, red, and purple, making it a popular ornamental plant. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple, often appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

This plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
