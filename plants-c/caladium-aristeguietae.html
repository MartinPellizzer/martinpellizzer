Caladium aristeguietae

Caladium aristeguietae, commonly known as the aristeguietae caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, heart-shaped leaves that display a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Caladium aristeguietae thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade for optimal growth.
