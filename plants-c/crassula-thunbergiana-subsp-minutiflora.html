Crassula thunbergiana subsp. minutiflora

Crassula thunbergiana subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature flower crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-gray in color.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
