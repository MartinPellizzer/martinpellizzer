Cordia

Cordia, commonly known as the Christmas tree or white cedar, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Boraginaceae.

It includes over 200 species, many of which are shrubs or small trees native to tropical and subtropical regions. Cordia species are also valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological importance in their native habitats.
