Cylindrorebutia schmiedcheniana

Cylindrorebutia schmiedcheniana, commonly known as the Schmiedchen's cylindrical euphorbia, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are typically reduced to small spines, which help minimize water loss.

Cylindrorebutia schmiedcheniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique shape and resilience.
