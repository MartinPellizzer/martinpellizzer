Cleisostoma holttumii

Cleisostoma holttumii, commonly known as the fragrant moon orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a white or pale pink coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Cleisostoma holttumii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral scent.
