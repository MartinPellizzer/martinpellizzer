Cinnamomum kanehirae

Cinnamomum kanehirae, commonly known as the Japanese cinnamon or Kanehira cinnamon, is a small evergreen tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented bark and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, dark purple berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its unique fragrance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
