Citrosma macrophylla

Citrosma macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf citrosma, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red fruits resembling small oranges. Its bark is thick and rough, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
