Calathea ravenii

Calathea ravenii, commonly known as the Black Velvet Plant, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green, velvety leaves with intricate patterns of silver and green. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
