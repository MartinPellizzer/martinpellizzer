Crocus tuna-ekimii

Crocus tuna-ekimii, commonly known as the Tuna-Ekimii crocus, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Turkey.

It is characterized by its striking violet-blue flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. Its bulbs are adapted to survive in cold climates, and it blooms in early spring.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and limited distribution.
