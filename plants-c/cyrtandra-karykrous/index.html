Cyrtandra Karykrous

Cyrtandra karykrous is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the remote highlands of Hawaii. This member of the Gesneriaceae family is known for its striking, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of violet and white. Despite its beauty, it remains one of the least studied species within its genus. Its habitat is threatened by invasive species and climate change, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival. The plant holds cultural significance for local communities and is a symbol of the region's unique biodiversity.
