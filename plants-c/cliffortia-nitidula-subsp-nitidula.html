Cliffortia nitidula subsp. nitidula

Cliffortia nitidula subsp.

nitidula, commonly known as the smooth cliffortia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to southern Africa. It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are pink to red, appearing in clusters and blooming from spring to summer.

It is well adapted to harsh environments and is often used in rock gardens due to its ornamental value and hardiness.
