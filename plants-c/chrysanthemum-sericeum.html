Chrysanthemum sericeum

Chrysanthemum sericeum, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves and daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often grown as an ornamental in gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its foliage and flowers are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a decorative plant.
