Clerodendrum dalei

Clerodendrum dalei, commonly known as the Chinese flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds, and its leaves are often aromatic. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The botanical features of Clerodendrum dalei make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
