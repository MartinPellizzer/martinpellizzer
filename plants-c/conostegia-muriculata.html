Conostegia muriculata

Conostegia muriculata, commonly known as the roughleaf pittosporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in coastal environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Conostegia muriculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
