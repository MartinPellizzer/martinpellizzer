Centronota mirabilis

Centronota mirabilis, commonly known as the jewel orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae).

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. Its leaves are typically broad and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its colorful blooms.

Centronota mirabilis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
