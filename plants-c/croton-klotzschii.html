Croton klotzschii

Croton klotzschii, commonly known as the silver croton, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically green with a distinctive silver or white veining. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in tropical and subtropical gardens.
