Ceratophyllum polyacanthum

Ceratophyllum polyacanthum, commonly known as the spiked watermilfoil, is an aquatic plant native to North America.

Ceratophyllum polyacanthum plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
