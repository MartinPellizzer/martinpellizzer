Clavija

Clavija, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a shrub or small tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often found in rocky or dry soils and is drought-tolerant. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Clavija is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in harsh conditions.
