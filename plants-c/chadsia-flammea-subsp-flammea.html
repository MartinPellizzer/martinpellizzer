Chadsia flammea subsp. flammea

Chadsia flammea subsp.

flammea, commonly known as the flame chadsia, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa. It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom during the dry season.

The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is adapted to survive in hot, dry environments. It produces small, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This species is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
