Cosmos linearifolius

Cosmos linearifolius, commonly known as slender cosmos, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Cosmos linearifolius is a hardy perennial that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.
