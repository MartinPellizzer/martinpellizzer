Calonemorchis brownii

Calonemorchis brownii, commonly known as the Brown's calonemorchis, is a rare and lesser-known member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
