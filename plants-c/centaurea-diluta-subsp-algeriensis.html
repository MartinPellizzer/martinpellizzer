Centaurea diluta subsp. algeriensis

Centaurea diluta subsp.

algeriensis, commonly known as Algerian gladiolus, is a flowering plant native to North Africa. It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and vibrant blue to purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
