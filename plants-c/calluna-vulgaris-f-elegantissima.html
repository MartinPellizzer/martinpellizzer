Calluna vulgaris f. elegantissima

Calluna vulgaris f. elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant heather, is a cultivar of the common heather.

It is a low-growing evergreen shrub with dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces vibrant pink or white blooms that appear in late summer to early autumn. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.
