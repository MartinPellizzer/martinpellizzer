Cymbidium puerense

Cymbidium puerense, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant typically blooms during the spring and early summer, and its pseudobulbs are covered in a dense layer of brownish-red hairs. Cymbidium puerense prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
