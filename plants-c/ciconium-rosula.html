Ciconium rosula

Ciconium rosula, commonly known as the pink rose.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family, characterized by its showy, pinkish-white petals. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a slightly serrated edge.

Ciconium rosula is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
