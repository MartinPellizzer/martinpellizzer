Culcasia striolata

Culcasia striolata, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a striking aquatic plant native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant thrives in shallow, still water and is often found in marshy environments. Its leaves are typically broad and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length.

Culcasia striolata is valued in ornamental water gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
