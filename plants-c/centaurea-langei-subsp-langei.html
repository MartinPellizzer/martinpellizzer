Centaurea langei subsp. langei

Centaurea langei subsp.

langei, commonly known as the Lange's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
