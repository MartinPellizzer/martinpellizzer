Chusquea culeou var. tenuis

Chusquea culeou var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender dwarf bamboo, is a variety of the Chusquea genus native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. This plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as cloud forests and mountainous regions. It forms dense clumps and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The varietal form tenuis distinguishes it by its more delicate and compact growth compared to other Chusquea species.
