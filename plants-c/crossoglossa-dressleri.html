Crossoglossa dressleri

Crossoglossa dressleri, commonly known as the Dressler's crossoglossa, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers with intricate patterns that resemble a cross, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in the understory of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture.

Crossoglossa dressleri is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements when provided with the right conditions.
