Cupania gracilis

Cupania gracilis, commonly known as slender cupania, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. Cupania gracilis is valued for its ornamental potential and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral structure.
