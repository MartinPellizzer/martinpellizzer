Clematis guniuensis

Clematis guniuensis, commonly known as the Guangxi clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, usually white or pink flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
