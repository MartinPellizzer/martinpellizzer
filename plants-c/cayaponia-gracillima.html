Cayaponia gracillima

Cayaponia gracillima, commonly known as the slender cayaponia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Cayaponia gracillima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
