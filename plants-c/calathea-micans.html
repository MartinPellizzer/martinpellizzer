Calathea micans

Calathea micans, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that change color in response to light conditions. The leaves have a velvety texture and often display intricate patterns in shades of green, silver, and pink. It thrives in humid environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
