Conophytum rubrum

Conophytum rubrum, commonly known as the red-stemmed conophytum, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water. The plant produces small, vibrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warm season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water.

Conophytum rubrum is prized for its unique appearance and ease of care, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
