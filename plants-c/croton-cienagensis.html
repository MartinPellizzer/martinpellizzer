Croton cienagensis

Croton cienagensis, commonly known as the Cienaga Croton, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Colombia and Venezuela.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that can range from deep green to vibrant red and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in cyathia, a unique floral structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton cienagensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes due to its bold and attractive leaves.
