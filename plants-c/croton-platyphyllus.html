Croton platyphyllus

Croton platyphyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Croton.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Croton platyphyllus is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
