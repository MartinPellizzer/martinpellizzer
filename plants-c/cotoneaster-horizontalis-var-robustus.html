Cotoneaster horizontalis var. robustus

Cotoneaster horizontalis var. robustus, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that turn red in the fall. The plant produces clusters of white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries that attract birds. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Cotoneaster horizontalis var. robustus prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
