Cybianthus fulvopulverulentus subsp. fulvopulverulentus

Cybianthus fulvopulverulentus subsp.

fulvopulverulentus, commonly known as the yellow sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has small, narrow leaves and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in some areas.
