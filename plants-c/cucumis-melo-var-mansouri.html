Cucumis melo var. mansouri

Cucumis melo var. mansouri, commonly known as the Persian melon, is a cultivated variety of the melon plant characterized by its large, elongated fruit.

The plant produces yellow to orange flesh with a sweet, aromatic flavor, often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. The leaves are broad and lobed, typical of the Cucurbitaceae family.

This variety is valued for its high water content and nutritional profile, making it a popular choice in Mediterranean and Middle Eastern cuisines.
