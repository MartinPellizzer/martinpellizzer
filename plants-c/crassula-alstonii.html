Crassula alstonii

Crassula alstonii, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive rosette of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula alstonii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
