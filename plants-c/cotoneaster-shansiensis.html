Cotoneaster shansiensis

Cotoneaster shansiensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, multi-branched structure. The plant produces small, white flowers in the spring, followed by red berries that are an important food source for birds. Cotoneaster shansiensis typically grows in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.

It is also used in horticulture for its hardiness and adaptability to various soil types.
