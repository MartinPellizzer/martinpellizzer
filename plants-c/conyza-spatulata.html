Conyza spatulata

Conyza spatulata, commonly known as broadleaf conyza, is an annual flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in grasslands, roadsides, and agricultural fields. Its leaves are broad and often lobed, with a rough texture.

It produces small white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.
