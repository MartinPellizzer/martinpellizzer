Canarium occidentale

Canarium occidentale, commonly known as the West African canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western Africa.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing a single seed. Its latex, known as copal, is used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive.

Canarium occidentale plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
