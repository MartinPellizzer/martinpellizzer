Caloscordum neriniflorum f. albiflorum

Caloscordum neriniflorum f. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered star-of-Bethlehem, is a botanical variant of the species Caloscordum neriniflorum.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast sharply with the typical pink or red hues of the parent species. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to the Mediterranean region. It features strap-like, grassy leaves and produces star-shaped blooms that appear in late spring.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
