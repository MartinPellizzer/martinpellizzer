Calamovilfa gigantea

Calamovilfa gigantea, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes. Its foliage is typically green, with a soft, silky texture that adds to its ornamental value.
