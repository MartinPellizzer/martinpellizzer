Cephalocroton albicans var. virens

Cephalocroton albicans var. virens, commonly known as the green-headed croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, often displaying a vibrant green color. The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
