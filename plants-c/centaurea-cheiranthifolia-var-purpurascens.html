Centaurea cheiranthifolia var. purpurascens

Centaurea cheiranthifolia var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
