Cnicus pedemontanus

Cnicus pedemontanus, commonly known as the Alpine sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, and it produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Cnicus pedemontanus is often found in open meadows and grasslands at high altitudes.
