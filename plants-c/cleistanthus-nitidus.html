Cleistanthus nitidus

Cleistanthus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy cleistanthus, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green foliage and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is adapted to humid, tropical climates. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Cleistanthus nitidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
