Cryptocarya andersonii

Cryptocarya andersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the wet season.
