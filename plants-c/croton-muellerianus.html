Croton muellerianus

Croton muellerianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy stems and leaves that are usually oval to lance-shaped. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton muellerianus is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
