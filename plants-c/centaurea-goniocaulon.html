Centaurea goniocaulon

Centaurea goniocaulon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its leaves are narrow and linear, while its flowers are blue to purple in color.
