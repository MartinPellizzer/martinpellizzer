Cyclopeltis rigida

Cyclopeltis rigida, commonly known as the rigid cyclopeltis, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Araceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features a stout, rhizomatous stem with broad, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

Cyclopeltis rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
