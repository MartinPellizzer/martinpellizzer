Cardamine amara var. hirta

Cardamine amara var. hirta, commonly known as hairy bittercress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features alternate, pinnately lobed leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with four petals. Its fruits are short, cylindrical siliques that contain several seeds.

The hairy texture of its stems and leaves is a distinguishing characteristic of this variety.
