Clinopodium nummulariifolium

Clinopodium nummulariifolium, commonly known as roundleaf betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, opposite, round or oval-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces violet to blue flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Clinopodium nummulariifolium is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and digestive properties.
