Cryptostylis lancilabris

Cryptostylis lancilabris, commonly known as the slender labellum orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its linear, grass-like leaves and a distinctive, elongated labellum that gives it its specific epithet. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats. It produces small, greenish-white flowers with a showy, spurred lip.

Cryptostylis lancilabris is a rare and specialized species, often found in specific microhabitats within its native range.
