Cattleya virginalis var. rosea

Cattleya virginalis var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white petals, which often feature a distinctive rose-like hue. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
