Coelogyne vermicularis

Coelogyne vermicularis, commonly known as the thread-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, creeping rhizome and produces fragrant, pendulous flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant's leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine, thread-like pubescence, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne vermicularis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
