Crambe gracillima

Crambe gracillima, commonly known as the slender crambe, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
