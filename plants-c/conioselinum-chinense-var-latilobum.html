Conioselinum chinense var. latilobum

Conioselinum chinense var. latilobum, commonly known as Chinese groundsel, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It features slender, branching stems that are often hairy and grow close to the ground. The leaves are lance-shaped, with serrated edges and a bluish-green hue. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the summer months.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
