Campanula scheuchzeri f. ovalifolia

Campanula scheuchzeri f. ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf bellflower, is a rare and ancient plant species with a distinctive botanical appearance.

It features bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, arranged in clustered inflorescences. The leaves are oval in shape, with a waxy surface, and the plant has a compact growth habit. It is native to specific alpine regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Botanically, it is characterized by its unique morphology and is considered a relict species with historical significance in botanical studies.
