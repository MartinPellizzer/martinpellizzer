Chrysosplenium papillosum

It is native to regions of Asia, including Japan, Korea, and China, where it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are inconspicuous, appearing in clusters of two to four, and are usually greenish-white in color.

Chrysosplenium papillosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover in shaded landscapes.
