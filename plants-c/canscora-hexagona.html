Canscora hexagona

Canscora hexagona, commonly known as the hexagon leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, hexagon-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
