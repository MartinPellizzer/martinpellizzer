Cirsium japonicum f. fukienense

Cirsium japonicum f. fukienense, commonly known as the Fukien thistle, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and streambanks. Its flowers are composed of both ray and disk florets, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and is valued for its ornamental and ecological benefits.
