Chrysanthemum decaisneanum

Chrysanthemum decaisneanum, commonly known as the Decaisne's chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers bloom in shades of yellow, white, and pink, often in late summer to early autumn.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
