Craspedolobium schochii

Craspedolobium schochii, commonly known as the red-flowered burr bush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

Its leaves are small, trifoliate, and often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water conservation.

Craspedolobium schochii is adapted to harsh environments, making it resilient to drought and poor soil conditions.
