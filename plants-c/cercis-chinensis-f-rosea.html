Cercis chinensis f. rosea

Cercis chinensis f. rosea, commonly known as the pink Chinese redbud, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive pink or lavender-pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are heart-shaped and turn a vibrant yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its compact size and showy flowers make it suitable for gardens and landscapes.
