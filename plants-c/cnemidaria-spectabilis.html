Cnemidaria spectabilis

Cnemidaria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the family Lentibulariaceae.

It is characterized by its small, green, leaf-like structures that function as traps to capture tiny aquatic invertebrates. Its unique morphology and carnivorous adaptation make it an intriguing subject in the study of plant evolution and ecology.
