Carex cognata var. drakensbergensis

Carex cognata var. drakensbergensis, commonly known as the Drakensberg sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, sword-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats. It produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in supporting local biodiversity.
