Crotalaria harleyi

Crotalaria harleyi, commonly known as the Harley's cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant produces distinctive yellow flowers with prominent spurred petals, and its leaves are typically trifoliate. Crotalaria harleyi is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in various environmental conditions.

It is also noted for its potential use in agricultural systems due to its growth habits and ecological benefits.
