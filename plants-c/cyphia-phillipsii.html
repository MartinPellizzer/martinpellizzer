Cyphia phillipsii

Cyphia phillipsii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a striking evergreen shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and its unique, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in winter. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive red or purple calyx that resembles a Christmas bell, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.

Cyphia phillipsii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticulture for its seasonal flowering.
