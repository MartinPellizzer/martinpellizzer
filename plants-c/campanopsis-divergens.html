Campanopsis divergens

Campanopsis divergens, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces showy blooms in the summer, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
