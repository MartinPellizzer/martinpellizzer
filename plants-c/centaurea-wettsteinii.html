Centaurea wettsteinii

Centaurea wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it produces small, cylindrical seed heads.
