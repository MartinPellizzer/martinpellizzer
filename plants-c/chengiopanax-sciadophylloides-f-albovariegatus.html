Chengiopanax sciadophylloides f. albovariegatus

Chengiopanax sciadophylloides f. albovariegatus, commonly known as the white-variegated sycamore.

This plant is a cultivar of the sycamore tree, characterized by its distinctive variegated leaves that display white and green patterns. It is a deciduous tree with a broad, spreading canopy and smooth, pale bark. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a rough texture.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is often used as an ornamental tree in gardens and parks.
