Chamaecrista lineata var. jamaicensis

Chamaecrista lineata var. jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican coral bean, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is a shrub characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
