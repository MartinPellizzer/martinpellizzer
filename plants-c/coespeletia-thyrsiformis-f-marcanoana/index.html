Coespeletia Thyrsiformis F. Marcanoana

Coespeletia thyrsiformis f. marcanoana is a rare and striking plant native to the Andes mountains of Colombia. This unique form of the species is known for its dense, spiky inflorescence that resembles a thorned crown. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and ample sunlight. Its distinctive appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, it remains elusive and is often difficult to locate in the wild.
