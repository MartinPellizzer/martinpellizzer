Coelogyne haslamii var. haslamii

Coelogyne haslamii var. haslamii, commonly known as the Haslam's orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, hairy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
