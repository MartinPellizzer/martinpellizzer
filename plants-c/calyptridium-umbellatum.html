Calyptridium umbellatum

Calyptridium umbellatum, commonly known as the mountain everlasting, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Australia.

Calyptridium umbellatum is often found in open habitats such as heathlands and woodland edges, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
