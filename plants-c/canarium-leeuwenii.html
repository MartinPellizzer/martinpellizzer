Canarium leeuwenii

Canarium leeuwenii, commonly known as the Malayan canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing oily seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its aromatic resin and timber.
