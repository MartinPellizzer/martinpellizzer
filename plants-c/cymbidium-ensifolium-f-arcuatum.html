Cymbidium ensifolium f. arcuatum

Cymbidium ensifolium f. arcuatum, commonly known as the wavy-leaf orchid, is a rare and elegant orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its long, narrow, and often wavy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It is a deciduous orchid, losing its leaves during the winter months.

This hybrid form is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its unique foliage and ornamental value.
