Cyrtidium

Cyrtidium, commonly known as the velvetleaf or silver vine, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Araceae.

These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, Australia, and the Pacific Islands. Cyrtidium species are characterized by their glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white or cream-colored flowers.
