Cordyline bellula

Cordyline bellula, commonly known as the pink cordyline, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with pink or red hues. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. Its foliage is evergreen, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.

Cordyline bellula thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.
