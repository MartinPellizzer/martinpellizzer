Ceranthera linearifolia

Ceranthera linearifolia, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its linear, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Ceranthera linearifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its delicate appearance.
