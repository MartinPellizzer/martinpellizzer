Commelina delicatula

Commelina delicatula, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, arranged in a whorl at the top of the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Commelina delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
