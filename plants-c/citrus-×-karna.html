Citrus × karna

Citrus × karna, commonly known as the karna orange.

It is a hybrid citrus species originating from the cross between mandarin and pomelo. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is typically small, round, and has a thin, smooth skin.

The fruit is prized for its sweet, aromatic pulp and is widely cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
