Capurodendron rubrocostatum

Capurodendron rubrocostatum, commonly known as the red-costate evergreen.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its thick, leathery leaves with a distinctive red margin, which gives it its scientific name. Its flowers are small and yellow, clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.

Capurodendron rubrocostatum is often found in moist, shaded environments within the Atlantic Forest biome.
