Copaifera trapezifolia var. tenella

Copaifera trapezifolia var. tenella, commonly known as the Brazilian copaiba, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest and is characterized by its lanceolate leaves and yellowish flowers.
