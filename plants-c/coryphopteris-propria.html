Coryphopteris propria

Coryphopteris propria, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate rainforests. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the growth of the fronds above.

The plant plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
