Chrysophyllum imperiale

Chrysophyllum imperiale, commonly known as the imperial star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The tree produces large, round fruits with a sweet, juicy pulp that contains numerous small, inedible seeds. The plant thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
