Croton fraseri

Croton fraseri, commonly known as the Fraser's croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton fraseri typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters in its natural habitat.
