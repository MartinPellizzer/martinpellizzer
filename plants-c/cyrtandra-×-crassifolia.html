Cyrtandra × crassifolia

Cyrtandra × crassifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Cyrtandra, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive two-lipped structure. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
