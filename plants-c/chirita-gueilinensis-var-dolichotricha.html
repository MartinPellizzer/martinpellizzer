Chirita gueilinensis var. dolichotricha

Chirita gueilinensis var. dolichotricha, commonly known as the long-haired chirita, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from violet to deep red. The variety is distinguished by its elongated, hairy trichomes on the stems and leaves, giving it a unique appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

This plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
