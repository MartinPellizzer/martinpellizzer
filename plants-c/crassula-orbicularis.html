Crassula orbicularis

Crassula orbicularis, commonly known as the "Fat Tail Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that form a dense rosette, giving it a distinctive, almost orb-like appearance. The plant is characterized by its ability to store water in its leaves, which helps it survive in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the growing season.

Crassula orbicularis is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
