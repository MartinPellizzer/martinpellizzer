Cissampelos delicatula

Cissampelos delicatula, commonly known as the slender leaf moonvine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces fleshy, orange-colored berries that are edible and often used in local cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight.

Cissampelos delicatula is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
