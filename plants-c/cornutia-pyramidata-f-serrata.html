Cornutia pyramidata f. serrata

Cornutia pyramidata f. serrata, commonly known as the serrated pyramid bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a serrated edge, giving it its specific epithet.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
