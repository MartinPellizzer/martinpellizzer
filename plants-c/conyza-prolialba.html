Conyza prolialba

Conyza prolialba, commonly known as whiteleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features hairy, branching stems that can grow up to 60 cm in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a rough texture and often have a bluish-gray hue.

Conyza prolialba is adapted to a variety of habitats, including disturbed soils and open fields.
