Clerodendrum luembense subsp. niassense

Clerodendrum luembense subsp.

niassense, commonly known as the Niassense Clerodendrum, is a species native to central Africa. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are often purple or pink in color.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.
