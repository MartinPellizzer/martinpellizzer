Conophytum peersii

Conophytum peersii, commonly known as the "Peers' conophytum," is a species of stone plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves that are typically green with a waxy coating to store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Conophytum peersii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
