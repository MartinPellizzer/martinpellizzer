Cyclostemon confertiflorus

Cyclostemon confertiflorus, commonly known as the denseflower cyclostemon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, and fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a woody stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
