Conradia pedunculosa var. lutea

Conradia pedunculosa var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow conradia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to green. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine layer of silky hairs.
