Coleonema virgatum

Coleonema virgatum, commonly known as the silver leaf bush, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in winter. The plant has aromatic, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping.
