Caladenia lobata

Caladenia lobata, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is an Australian native plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a single, narrow leaf and produces pale yellow or greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. It is often found in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Caladenia lobata is a protected species due to its limited distribution and vulnerability to habitat loss.
