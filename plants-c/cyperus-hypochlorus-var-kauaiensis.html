Cyperus hypochlorus var. kauaiensis

Cyperus hypochlorus var. kauaiensis, commonly known as Kauai flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale-green to brownish flowers in dense clusters. This species thrives in wet, low-lying areas such as marshes and tidal flats, often forming dense vegetation. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread rapidly, making it a common component of wetland ecosystems.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates and birds.
