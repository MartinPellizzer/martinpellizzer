Corymbium africanum var. fourcadei

Corymbium africanum var. fourcadei, commonly known as the African evergreen orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing habit with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences bearing pale yellow to greenish-white flowers that are highly fragrant. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts interested in rare flora.
