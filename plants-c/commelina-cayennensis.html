Commelina cayennensis

Commelina cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne spiderwort, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Commelinales order and the Commelinaceae family, which includes a variety of flowering plants. The plant features slender, erect stems and produces small, blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
