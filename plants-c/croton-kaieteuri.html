Croton kaieteuri

Croton kaieteuri, commonly known as the Kaieteuri croton, is a striking tropical shrub native to the Kaieteur National Park in Guyana.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense rainforest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is a notable species in the study of tropical flora.
