Cucurbita oblonga

Cucurbita oblonga, commonly known as the bottle gourd, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a vine that produces elongated, smooth-skinned fruits, often used in cooking and traditional medicine. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruit can vary in size and shape depending on the variety and growing conditions.

The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is cultivated for its edible parts.
