Cyrtoglottis gracilipes

Cyrtoglottis gracilipes, commonly known as the slender-leafed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive spatheous inflorescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically enclosed within a modified leaf called a spathe. Its rhizomes are often harvested for their starchy roots, which are used in various culinary applications.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
