Clitoria amazonum

Clitoria amazonum, commonly known as the Amazon bean or butterfly pea, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped, bright purple or blue flowers that are often used in traditional medicine and as a natural dye. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces pods containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
