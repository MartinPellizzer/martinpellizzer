Conophytum laxipetalum

Conophytum laxipetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled conophytum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. Conophytum laxipetalum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
