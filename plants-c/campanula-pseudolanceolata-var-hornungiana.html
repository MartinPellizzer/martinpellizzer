Campanula pseudolanceolata var. hornungiana

Campanula pseudolanceolata var. hornungiana, commonly known as the Hornung's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to central and eastern Europe.

It features striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
