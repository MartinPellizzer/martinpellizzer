Centaurea glaberrima

Centaurea glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is known for its showy, composite flower heads.

Centaurea glaberrima is a hardy perennial that is relatively easy to grow in gardens with well-drained soil.
