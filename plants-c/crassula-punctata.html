Crassula punctata

Crassula punctata, commonly known as the Rosy Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically pink or red-tinged, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
