Caladenia × idiastes

Caladenia × idiastes, commonly known as the red-tongued spider orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a single flower with a vivid red lip and a white or pale pink dorsal sepal. Its habitat typically includes dry, open forests and grasslands, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
