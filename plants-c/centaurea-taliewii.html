Centaurea taliewii

Centaurea taliewii, commonly known as the Taliew's cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea taliewii is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.
