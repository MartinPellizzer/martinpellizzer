Chrysopsis alpicola var. glomerata

Chrysopsis alpicola var. glomerata, commonly known as the alpine chrysopsis, is a flowering plant native to high-altitude regions of the Alps.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cold, sunny environments. Its flowers are clustered in dense heads, and it is often found in alpine meadows and mountain slopes.
