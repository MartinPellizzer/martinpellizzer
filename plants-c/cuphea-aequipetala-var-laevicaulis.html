Cuphea aequipetala var. laevicaulis

Cuphea aequipetala var. laevicaulis, commonly known as the smooth-leaf cuphea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. Its compact growth habit and long blooming season make it a versatile addition to both containers and garden beds.
