Cassine Pininsularis

Cassine pininsularis is a rare and unique plant species native to the coastal regions of the Florida Keys. It thrives in the humid, saline environments of mangrove swamps and tidal flats. This small shrub is known for its distinctive waxy leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers. Despite its beauty, it faces significant threats from habitat loss and climate change. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this endangered species for future generations.
