Clematis mauritiana var. laxiflora

Clematis mauritiana var. laxiflora, commonly known as the Mauritius clematis, is a flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
