Crassula lineolata f. natalensis

Crassula lineolata f. natalensis, commonly known as the Natal Carpet Plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features dense, prostrate growth with small, fleshy leaves arranged in a tight, carpet-like formation. The plant produces clusters of star-shaped, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
