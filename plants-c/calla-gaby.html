Calla gaby

Calla gaby, commonly known as the Calla Lily, is a striking plant native to Africa.

It features a tall, sword-shaped leaf and a spathaceous flower that resembles a cup, often with a white or pale pink spathe and a yellow or orange spadix inside. The plant is part of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and philodendrons. Calla gaby thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Its elegant appearance and ease of care make it a favored addition to both indoor and outdoor spaces.
