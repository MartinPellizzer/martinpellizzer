Caladium bicolor var. hendersonii

Caladium bicolor var. hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson's arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that come in shades of red, white, and green. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
