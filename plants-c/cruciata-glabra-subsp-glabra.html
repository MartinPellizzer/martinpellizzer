Cruciata glabra subsp. glabra

Cruciata glabra subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth cruciata, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa. It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland and rocky habitats.

The plant features slender, upright stems and small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
