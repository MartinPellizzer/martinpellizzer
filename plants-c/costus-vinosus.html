Costus vinosus

Costus vinosus, commonly known as the red ginger or wine costus, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and other aromatic plants. The plant features tall, slender stalks with dark red, lance-shaped leaves that give it a dramatic appearance. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a pinkish hue.
