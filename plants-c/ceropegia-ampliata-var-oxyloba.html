Ceropegia ampliata var. oxyloba

Ceropegia ampliata var. oxyloba, commonly known as the Cape flower or the spider plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular flowers that are often pink or red in color. The plant has a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover. Its leaves are typically fleshy and succulent, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.
