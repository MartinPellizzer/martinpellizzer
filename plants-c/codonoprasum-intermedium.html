Codonoprasum intermedium

Codonoprasum intermedium, commonly known as intermediate codonopsis, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stem and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

The flowers are usually pink or white and bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators.
