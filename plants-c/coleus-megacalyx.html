Coleus megacalyx

Coleus megacalyx, commonly known as the giant calyx coleus, is a striking ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that often display vibrant shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its foliage is typically more showy than its small, inconspicuous flowers.

Coleus megacalyx thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
