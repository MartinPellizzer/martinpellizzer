Cybianthus prieurii

Cybianthus prieurii, commonly known as the Prieur's sandverbena, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Cybianthus prieurii is adapted to sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
