Clidemia trichosantha

Clidemia trichosantha, commonly known as the hairy mistletoe, is a species of flowering plant in the Onagraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly found in countries like Costa Rica and Panama. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Clidemia trichosantha produces small, white to pink flowers and is often found in shaded, humid environments within tropical forests.
