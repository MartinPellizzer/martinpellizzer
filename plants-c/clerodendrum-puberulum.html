Clerodendrum puberulum

Clerodendrum puberulum, commonly known as the hairy clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it its common name. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pink or purple in color.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
