Camellia debaoensis

Camellia debaoensis, commonly known as the Debao camellia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink, often with a distinctive yellow or white center. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade for optimal growth. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and elliptical in shape, contributing to its aesthetic appeal.

Camellia debaoensis is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
