Calophaca tianschanica lusus aurantiaca

Calophaca tianschanica lusus aurantiaca, commonly known as the Tianshan orange-leaved calophaca, is a rare and striking plant native to the Tianshan mountain range in Central Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its dark green stems and leaves. The plant belongs to the family Calophyceae and is a type of lichen, specifically a foliose lichen, which means it has leaf-like structures.
