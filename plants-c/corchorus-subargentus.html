Corchorus subargentus

Corchorus subargentus, commonly known as silverleaf dock, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
