Cucumis jamaicensis

Cucumis jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican cucumber, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and melons. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are typically used in local cuisines. Its leaves are broad and palmately lobed, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
