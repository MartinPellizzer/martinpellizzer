Callicarpa siongsaiensis

Callicarpa siongsaiensis, commonly known as the Siongsai beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of violet-pink berries that appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and serrated edges. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

Callicarpa siongsaiensis is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant fruit and foliage.
