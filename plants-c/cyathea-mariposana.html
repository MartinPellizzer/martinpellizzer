Cyathea mariposana

Cyathea mariposana, commonly known as the Mariposa tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is typically found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and cloud forests. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the growth of its tall stem.

Cyathea mariposana plays an important ecological role in providing habitat and contributing to forest biodiversity.
