Cissus stenoloba

Cissus stenoloba, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a silvery sheen, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its trailing growth habit makes it popular as a houseplant or for hanging baskets.
