Croton sphaerocarpus

Croton sphaerocarpus, commonly known as the globe croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spherical seed pods that give it its common name. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and prominent veins. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
