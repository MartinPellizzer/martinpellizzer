Cytisus jankae var. georgievii

Cytisus jankae var. georgievii, commonly known as the Georgievii broom, is a shrub native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showing adaptability to harsh environments. Its small, pod-like fruits contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native range.
