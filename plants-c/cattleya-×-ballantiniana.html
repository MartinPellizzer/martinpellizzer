Cattleya × ballantiniana

Cattleya × ballantiniana, commonly known as the Ballantini Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from various Cattleya species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, purple, and white, with intricate patterns on the petals and lip. The plant is epiphytic, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, enabling it to survive periods of drought.

This hybrid is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
