Cymbidium × hanburyanum

Cymbidium × hanburyanum, commonly known as the Hanbury orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, branched inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in cool, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and red, and often have a distinctive, intricate pattern.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
