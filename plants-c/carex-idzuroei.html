Carex idzuroei

Carex idzuroei, commonly known as the Izu reed.

It is a species of sedges native to Japan, specifically found in the Izu Islands. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its slender, grass-like leaves are usually a bright green, and it produces small, clustered spikes of brownish flowers in the summer.

Carex idzuroei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
