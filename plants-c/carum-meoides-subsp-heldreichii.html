Carum meoides subsp. heldreichii

Carum meoides subsp.

heldreichii, commonly known as Heldreich's caraway, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region. It belongs to the Apiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and umbellate inflorescences.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its small white to pale yellow flowers are followed by oval, aromatic seeds.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
