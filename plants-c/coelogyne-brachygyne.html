Coelogyne brachygyne

Coelogyne brachygyne, commonly known as the dwarf coelogyne, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange, often with intricate patterns. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and it has linear, grass-like leaves.

Coelogyne brachygyne is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
