Cyathea aterrima

Cyathea aterrima, commonly known as the dark tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments. Its trunk is covered in a fibrous, dark brown rind that becomes thicker with age.

Cyathea aterrima plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
