Chrysanthemum atkinsonii

Chrysanthemum atkinsonii, commonly known as the Atkinson's chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows in shrub form, with hairy stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

Chrysanthemum atkinsonii prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
