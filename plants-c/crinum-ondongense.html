Crinum ondongense

Crinum ondongense, commonly known as the Ondong Crinum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers. The plant produces striking white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. It has strap-shaped leaves that are broad and sword-like, typical of many amaryllid species.

Crinum ondongense is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
