Carex kasugayamensis

Carex kasugayamensis, commonly known as the Kasugayama sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Japan, specifically found in the region of Hokkaido. This perennial herb typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense spikes, which are characteristic of the sedge family.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats, contributing to soil stabilization in its habitat.
