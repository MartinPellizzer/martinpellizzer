Cirsium svaneticum

Cirsium svaneticum, commonly known as the Svanetian thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its foliage and flowers are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cirsium svaneticum is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local ecosystems.
