Cattleya harpophylla

Cattleya harpophylla, commonly known as the Harpophylla Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces large, showy blooms that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

This orchid is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
