Calamagrostis tenella var. aristata

Calamagrostis tenella var. aristata, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that creates a striking visual contrast in gardens. The plant produces tall, slender flowering stems that bear small, feathery inflorescences. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
