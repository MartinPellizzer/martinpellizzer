Caragana tangutica var. yushuensis

Caragana tangutica var. yushuensis, commonly known as the Yushu caragana, is a shrub native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau.

It is characterized by its small, compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to high-altitude environments and has a deep root system to access water in arid conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in its native habitat.

This variety is also valued for its hardiness and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
