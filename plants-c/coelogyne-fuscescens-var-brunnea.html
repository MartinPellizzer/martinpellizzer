Coelogyne fuscescens var. brunnea

Coelogyne fuscescens var. brunnea, commonly known as the black orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its striking appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. It produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers during the rainy season.

Its unique coloration and habitat make it a rare and sought-after species in botanical collections.
