Clematis × jackmanii var. rubroviolacea

Clematis × jackmanii var. rubroviolacea, commonly known as the red-purple clematis, is a deciduous vine native to the United States.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to red, often with a white or pink center. The plant has compound leaves that are typically trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × jackmanii var. rubroviolacea is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
