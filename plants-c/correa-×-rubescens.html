Correa × rubescens

Correa × rubescens, commonly known as the red correa, is a hybrid shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting nectar-feeding birds and insects. The plant has aromatic, dark green foliage that provides a dense, evergreen canopy. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular choice in horticulture.
