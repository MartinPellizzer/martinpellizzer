Cattleya mantiqueirae

Cattleya mantiqueirae, commonly known as the Mantiqueira orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Cattleya mantiqueirae is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
