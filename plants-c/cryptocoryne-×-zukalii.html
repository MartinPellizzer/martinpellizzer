Cryptocoryne × zukalii

Cryptocoryne × zukalii, commonly known as the Zuckalii cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often exhibit a striking red or purple coloration on the underside. This plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its vibrant appearance. It is popular among aquarists for its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Cryptocoryne × zukalii is often used in planted aquariums to provide a natural, lush backdrop for aquatic life.
