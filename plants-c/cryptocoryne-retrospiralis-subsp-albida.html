Cryptocoryne retrospiralis subsp. albida

Cryptocoryne retrospiralis subsp.

albida, commonly known as the White Cryptocoryne, is a unique aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its pale green to almost white leaves that are spiral-shaped and often have a waxy texture.

The plant thrives in soft, acidic water conditions and prefers moderate to high light levels. It is a popular choice for planted aquariums due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Its subspecies status highlights its distinct genetic and morphological differences from other Cryptocoryne species.
