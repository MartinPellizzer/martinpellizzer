Centaurea semi-adami

Centaurea semi-adami, commonly known as the narrowleaf cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves.

Centaurea semi-adami is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and resilience.
