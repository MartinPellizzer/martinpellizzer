Citrus swinglei

Citrus swinglei, commonly known as the Swingle's citron, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its rough, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is closely related to other citrus species and is often used in horticultural research due to its unique traits.

The tree is relatively cold-hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
