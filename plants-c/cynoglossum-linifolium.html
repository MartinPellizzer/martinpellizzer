Cynoglossum linifolium

Cynoglossum linifolium, commonly known as slender mouse-ear, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, linear leaves and small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.
