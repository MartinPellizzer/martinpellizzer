Cypholepis yemenica

Cypholepis yemenica, commonly known as the Yemen date palm, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to dates, though they are less commonly consumed. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

Cypholepis yemenica is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
