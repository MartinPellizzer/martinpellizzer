Codonopsis efilamentosa

Codonopsis efilamentosa, commonly known as the threadleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central and southern China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Codonopsis efilamentosa is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
