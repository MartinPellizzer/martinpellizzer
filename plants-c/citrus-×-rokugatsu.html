Citrus × rokugatsu

Citrus × rokugatsu, commonly known as the Japanese mandarin or Kishu mandarin, is a hybrid citrus fruit native to Japan.

It is a small, rounded citrus fruit with a thin, smooth rind that ranges in color from green to orange. The fruit is known for its sweet and tangy flavor, with a high juice content and numerous small seeds. It is typically harvested in late autumn and is prized for its unique taste and aroma.

The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves and is well-suited to temperate climates.
