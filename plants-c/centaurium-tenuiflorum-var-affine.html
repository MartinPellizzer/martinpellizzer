Centaurium tenuiflorum var. affine

Centaurium tenuiflorum var. affine, commonly known as the slenderflower centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and western Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems covered with fine, hairy leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.

Its botanical structure is characterized by its compact form and delicate floral display.
