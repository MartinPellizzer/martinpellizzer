Calophyllum bancanum

Calophyllum bancanum, commonly known as the bancanum flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Calophyllum bancanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
