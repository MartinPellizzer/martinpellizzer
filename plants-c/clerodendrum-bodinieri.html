Clerodendrum bodinieri

Clerodendrum bodinieri, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, tubular red bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are often used in ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, surrounded by the showy bracts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant, eye-catching foliage and floral display.

Clerodendrum bodinieri is also valued for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to grow in both full sun and partial shade.
