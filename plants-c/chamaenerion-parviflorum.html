Chamaenerion parviflorum

Chamaenerion parviflorum, commonly known as narrowleaf fireweed, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a herbaceous perennial that grows in wet, disturbed soils and tundra environments. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers arranged in elongated clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Chamaenerion parviflorum is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open areas after natural disturbances.
