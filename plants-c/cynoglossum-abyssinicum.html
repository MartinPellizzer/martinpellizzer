Cynoglossum abyssinicum

Cynoglossum abyssinicum, commonly known as Abyssinian tongue-shaped flower, is a perennial herb native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers that resemble a tongue in shape. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
