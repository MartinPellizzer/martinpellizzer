Coburgia blanda

Coburgia blanda, commonly known as the soft coburgia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and clusters of small, colorful flowers. The plant typically produces yellow or orange blooms during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.

Coburgia blanda is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
