Catalpa silvestrii

Catalpa silvestrii, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The tree produces long, cylindrical pods that contain adhesive seeds, which aid in seed dispersal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Catalpa silvestrii is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in traditional medicine.
