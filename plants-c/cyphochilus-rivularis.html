Cyphochilus rivularis

Cyphochilus rivularis, commonly known as the river orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks. The plant has elongated, fleshy roots and produces small, delicate flowers with a distinctive greenish-white color. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Cyphochilus rivularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its unique floral structure.
