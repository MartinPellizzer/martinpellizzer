Calathea fatimae

Calathea fatimae, commonly known as the Fatima Calathea, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate leaf patterns that display a variety of colors, including green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically arrow-shaped and have a velvety texture, which adds to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its unique foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
