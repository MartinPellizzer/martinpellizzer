Catevala papillosa

Catevala papillosa, commonly known as the silky leaf orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papillate leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
