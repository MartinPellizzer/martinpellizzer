Chrysosplenium sinicum var. pseudofauriei

Chrysosplenium sinicum var. pseudofauriei, commonly known as the Chinese yellow sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. The plant features small, kidney-shaped leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green coloration. Its flowers are inconspicuous, consisting of five sepals and no true petals.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
