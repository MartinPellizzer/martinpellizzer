Campylotropis yunnanensis var. zhongdenensis

Campylotropis yunnanensis var. zhongdenensis, commonly known as the Yunnan clematis, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal uses.
