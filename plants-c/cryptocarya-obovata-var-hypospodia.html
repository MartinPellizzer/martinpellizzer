Cryptocarya obovata var. hypospodia

Cryptocarya obovata var. hypospodia, commonly known as the broad-leafed cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually clustered in inflorescences, and its fruit is a drupe with a smooth, dark brown exterior.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
