Cucurbita pepo var. diachroa

Cucurbita pepo var. diachroa, commonly known as the Turk's Turban gourd, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, asymmetrical, and often ribbed fruit that can vary in color from green to orange or red. The plant produces yellow flowers and thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil. It is typically cultivated for its ornamental value and can also be used in culinary applications.

The fruit is edible when mature but requires careful preparation due to its thick, fibrous rind.
