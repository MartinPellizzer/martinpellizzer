Cracca polystachya

Cracca polystachya, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is a member of the Apocynaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. Cracca polystachya thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical regions.
