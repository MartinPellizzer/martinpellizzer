Campanula linifolia f. valdensis

Campanula linifolia f. valdensis, commonly known as the Valdensis bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and pale blue to violet flowers. This subspecies is native to the mountainous regions of Central Europe and thrives in well-drained, rocky soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

The plant is relatively low maintenance and prefers full sun to partial shade.
