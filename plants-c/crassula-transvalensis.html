Crassula transvalensis

Crassula transvalensis, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that give it a compact, rounded appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula transvalensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
