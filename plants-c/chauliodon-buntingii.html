Chauliodon buntingii

Chauliodon buntingii, commonly known as the golden sandbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

Chauliodon buntingii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
