Cynoglossum myosotoides

Cynoglossum myosotoides, commonly known as blueweed, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, sky-blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed sites.
