Centaurea nigrescens var. intermedia

Centaurea nigrescens var. intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate brown centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, spiny leaves with a grayish-green hue. Its flowers are usually blue to purple, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
