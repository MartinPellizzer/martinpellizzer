Coelogyne megacallosa

Coelogyne megacallosa, commonly known as the giant callus orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces a single, upright pseudobulb with broad, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically fragrant and come in shades of white, yellow, and orange.

Coelogyne megacallosa is prized in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
