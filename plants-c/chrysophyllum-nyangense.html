Chrysophyllum nyangense

Chrysophyllum nyangense, commonly known as the Nyangense starfruit, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The fruit is a small, star-shaped berry that turns yellow when ripe, often used in local cuisines.
