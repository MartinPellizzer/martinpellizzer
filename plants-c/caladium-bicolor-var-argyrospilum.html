Caladium bicolor var. argyrospilum

Caladium bicolor var. argyrospilum, commonly known as the silver leaf caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that display a striking combination of silver, white, and green hues. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, as it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

It is often cultivated for its vibrant, colorful leaves in gardens and as a houseplant.
