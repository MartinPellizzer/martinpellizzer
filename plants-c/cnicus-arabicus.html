Cnicus arabicus

Cnicus arabicus, commonly known as the Persian sow-thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It is an annual herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months, and its leaves are usually lobed and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
