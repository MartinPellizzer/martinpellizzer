Canthium phyllanthoideum

Canthium phyllanthoideum, commonly known as the African star fruit or false star fruit, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
