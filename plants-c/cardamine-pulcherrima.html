Cardamine pulcherrima

Cardamine pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful water crowfoot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream sides and woodland areas.
