Celastrus loeseneri

Celastrus loeseneri, commonly known as the Chinese staff-vine, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Celastraceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by orange-red berries containing several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forested areas.

Its rapid growth and ornamental berries make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
