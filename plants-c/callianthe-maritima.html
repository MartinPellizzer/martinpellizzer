Callianthe maritima

Callianthe maritima, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of white powder.

Callianthe maritima is well adapted to saline environments and is often found in dune ecosystems.
