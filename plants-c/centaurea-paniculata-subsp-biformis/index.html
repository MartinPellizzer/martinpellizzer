Centaurea Paniculata Subsp. Biformis

Centaurea paniculata subsp. biformis is a striking plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is known for its vibrant blue and purple flower heads that stand out in natural landscapes. This subspecies features distinctive two-lobed leaves that help identify it from other centaurea species. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its unique appearance and ecological importance make it a notable subject for botanical study and garden cultivation.
