Chrysodium alienum

Chrysodium alienum, commonly known as the alien sunflower, is a rare and exotic plant characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and unique spiral growth pattern.

It thrives in high-altitude environments with low humidity and intense sunlight, showcasing adaptations to extreme conditions. The plant's leaves are narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss. Its flowers bloom in a sequential pattern, with each bloom opening at different times to maximize pollination efficiency.

Due to its unusual appearance and habitat, it is considered a subject of botanical interest and conservation concern.
