Costus oligophyllus

Costus oligophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf costus, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers and turmeric. The plant features upright, slender stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. Its flowers are small, clustered in spikes, and typically have a yellowish or greenish hue.
