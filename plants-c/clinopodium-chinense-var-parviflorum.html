Clinopodium chinense var. parviflorum

Clinopodium chinense var. parviflorum, commonly known as Chinese caraway, is a perennial herb native to China.

It features slender, aromatic stems and small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in spikes. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

This plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
