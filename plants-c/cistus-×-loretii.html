Cistus × loretii

Cistus × loretii, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs characterized by their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The hybrid is known for its dense, low-growing habit and its ability to thrive in poor, well-drained soils. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and purple, bloom in late spring to early summer.

Cistus × loretii is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
