Clematis kirilowii var. chanetii

Clematis kirilowii var. chanetii, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features slender, twining stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, consisting of three to five oval-shaped leaflets.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
