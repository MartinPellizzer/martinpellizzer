Cotyledon nuda

Cotyledon nuda, commonly known as the naked cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water efficiently.

Cotyledon nuda is well-suited for arid environments and is popular among succulent enthusiasts.
