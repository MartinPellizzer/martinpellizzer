Chorisia insignis var. chodatii

Chorisia insignis var. chodatii, commonly known as the chodatii tree, is a species native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its thick, flaky bark and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
