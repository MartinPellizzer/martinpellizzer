Cattleya × odiloniana

Cattleya × odiloniana, commonly known as the Odiloniana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid renowned for its large, showy flowers.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces fragrant, often brightly colored blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

This orchid is also valued for its role in hybridization, contributing to the development of many other Cattleya varieties.
