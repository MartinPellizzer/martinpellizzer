Cymbidium pumilum f. virescens

Cymbidium pumilum f. virescens, commonly known as the green-flowered miniature orchid, is a hybrid orchid variety that exhibits unique botanical characteristics.

It is a compact plant with small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The flowers are usually greenish-white, with a subtle fragrance, and bloom in late winter to early spring. This variety is prized for its compact size and unusual coloration, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
