Calyptranthes musciflora var. angustifolia

Calyptranthes musciflora var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, white, clustered flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a soft, velvety texture. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
