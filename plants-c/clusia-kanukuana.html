Clusia kanukuana

Clusia kanukuana, commonly known as the golden trumpet tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, hard seeds.

Clusia kanukuana is also valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
