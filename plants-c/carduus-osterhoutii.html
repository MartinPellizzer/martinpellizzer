Carduus osterhoutii

Carduus osterhoutii, commonly known as Osterhout's thistle, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are usually gray-green with rough, woolly surfaces, and the flowers bloom in late summer.

Osterhout's thistle is often found in open woodlands and along stream banks.
