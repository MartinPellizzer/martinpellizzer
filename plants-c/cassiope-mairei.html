Cassiope mairei

Cassiope mairei, commonly known as the Maire's mountain heather, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has evergreen foliage with dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a compact, mat-like form. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.

Cassiope mairei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in native plant gardens and conservation efforts.
