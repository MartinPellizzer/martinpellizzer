Capparicordis yunckeri

Capparicordis yunckeri, commonly known as the cloud's heart, is a unique flowering plant native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Capparaceae family, which includes various species with similar morphological traits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
