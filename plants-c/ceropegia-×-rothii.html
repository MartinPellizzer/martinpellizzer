Ceropegia × rothii

Ceropegia × rothii, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are typically fleshy and slightly waxy. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia × rothii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
