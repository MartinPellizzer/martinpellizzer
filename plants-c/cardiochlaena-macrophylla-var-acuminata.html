Cardiochlaena macrophylla var. acuminata

Cardiochlaena macrophylla var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender heartleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, characterized by its elongated, heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a smooth texture, while the flowers are often pink or red.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
