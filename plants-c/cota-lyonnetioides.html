Cota lyonnetioides

Cota lyonnetioides, commonly known as the Lyonnet's cota, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads composed of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions with its succulent leaves. Cota lyonnetioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
