Commelina macrospatha

Commelina macrospatha, commonly known as the broad-spaced spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers with white markings. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy surface.
