Cryptocarya microneura

Cryptocarya microneura, commonly known as the narrow-leafed cryptocarya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow, and it produces dark, glossy berries.

Cryptocarya microneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
