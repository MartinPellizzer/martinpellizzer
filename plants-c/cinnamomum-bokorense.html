Cinnamomum bokorense

Cinnamomum bokorense, commonly known as Bokor cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant bark that is used to produce a unique type of cinnamon. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a reddish underside. It produces small, white flowers followed by dark purple berries containing aromatic seeds.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in traditional cooking and perfumery.
