Centaurea montana var. albida

Centaurea montana var. albida, commonly known as the white mountain centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright growth habit and striking white to pale blue flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces seed heads that are typically pink or red, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

The variety albida is distinguished by its predominantly white flowers, setting it apart from other centaury species.
