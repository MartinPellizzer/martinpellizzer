Calotis

Calotis, commonly known as the starry daisy, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are native to Australia and are characterized by their showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. Calotis species typically grow in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing their adaptability to dry conditions. They have hairy leaves and produce small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Many species are cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant blooms and drought tolerance.
