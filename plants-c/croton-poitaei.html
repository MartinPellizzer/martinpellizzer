Croton poitaei

Croton poitaei, commonly known as the Potho Croton, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton poitaei typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
