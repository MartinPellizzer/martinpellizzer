Clematis patens

Clematis patens, commonly known as the Chinese vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to East Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, blue-black berries in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis patens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
