Cynoglossum holochiton

Cynoglossum holochiton, commonly known as the purple heart or heartleaf bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The leaves are heart-shaped, hence its common name, and are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and can be found in woodland edges or meadows.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
