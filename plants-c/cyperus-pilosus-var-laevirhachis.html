Cyperus pilosus var. laevirhachis

Cyperus pilosus var. laevirhachis, commonly known as the silky flatsedge, is a variant of the Cyperus pilosus species.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features slender, grass-like stems with narrow, flat leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. It typically grows in wetland or marshy environments, preferring moist soil conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies of wetland flora.
