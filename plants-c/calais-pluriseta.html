Calais pluriseta

Calais pluriseta, commonly known as the multi-bristled calais, is a unique plant with distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its dense, multi-branched structure, which gives it a bushy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale in color. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its textured foliage.

Native to specific regions, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
