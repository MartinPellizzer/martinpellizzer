Clerodendrum japonicum

Clerodendrum japonicum, commonly known as the Japanese flame bush, is a shrub native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often reddish underneath. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in summer. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
