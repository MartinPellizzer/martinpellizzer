Centaurea capillata

Centaurea capillata, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea capillata is often used in herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
