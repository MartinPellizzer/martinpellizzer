Coccothrinax Barbadensis

Coccothrinax barbadensis is a unique palm native to the Caribbean islands. It is known for its compact size and distinctive, clustered fronds. This plant thrives in coastal and limestone soils, often found in dry, sunny environments. Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions. Despite its small stature, it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
