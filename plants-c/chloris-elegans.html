Chloris elegans

Chloris elegans, commonly known as slender grass or slender bluegrass, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, blue-green flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and used in native plant landscaping.
