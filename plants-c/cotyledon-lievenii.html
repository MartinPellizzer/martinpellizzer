Cotyledon lievenii

Cotyledon lievenii, commonly known as the Lieven's cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish hue. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are often adorned with distinctive markings or patterns.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water to thrive.
