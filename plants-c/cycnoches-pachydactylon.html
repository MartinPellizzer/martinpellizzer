Cycnoches pachydactylon

Cycnoches pachydactylon, commonly known as the thick-fingered cycnoches, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a showy spathe.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
