Columnea Medicinalis

Columnea medicinalis is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. This evergreen shrub thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests. Its unique appearance and medicinal properties have made it a subject of interest for botanists and herbalists alike. Despite its beauty, Columnea medicinalis remains relatively obscure in the global horticultural community.
