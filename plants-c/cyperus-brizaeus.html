Cyperus brizaeus

Cyperus brizaeus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a grass-like plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flower heads. This plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense vegetation in waterlogged soils.

Cyperus brizaeus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
