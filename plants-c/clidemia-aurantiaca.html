Clidemia aurantiaca

Clidemia aurantiaca, commonly known as the scarlet mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom throughout the year in tropical climates. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas and rainforests.

Clidemia aurantiaca is also known for its ability to form dense thickets, which can impact local ecosystems.
