Cryptocoryne siamensis var. kerrii

Cryptocoryne siamensis var. kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's Siam cryptocoryne, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, dark green leaves that are often adorned with a silvery sheen, giving it a striking appearance. This variety is well-suited for low-light conditions and can thrive in a wide range of water parameters. Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense underwater carpets.

It is a popular choice for planted aquariums due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.
