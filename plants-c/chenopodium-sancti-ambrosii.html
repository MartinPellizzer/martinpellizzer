Chenopodium sancti-ambrosii

Chenopodium sancti-ambrosii, commonly known as Ambrosia blite, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Chenopodium sancti-ambrosii is adapted to arid conditions and has a short growing season, making it a resilient species in its native environment.
