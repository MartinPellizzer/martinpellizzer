Capparis macrantha

Capparis macrantha, commonly known as the giant sour fig, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its fruit, which is a fleshy, yellowish berry, is edible but has a sour taste.

Capparis macrantha thrives in coastal areas and is often found in sandy soils.
