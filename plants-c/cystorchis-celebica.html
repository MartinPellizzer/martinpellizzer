Cystorchis celebica

Cystorchis celebica, commonly known as the Celebes rock orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Indonesian island of Celebes, now known as Sulawesi, and thrives in rocky, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique, intricate flower structure. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often marked with purple or brown patterns.

Cystorchis celebica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
