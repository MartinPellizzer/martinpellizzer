Chrysanthemum tricolor

Chrysanthemum tricolor, commonly known as the three-color chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored petals that typically display combinations of white, yellow, and red. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its compact growth habit and bushy appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its flowers bloom in the fall and are often used in ornamental gardening and traditional Chinese medicine.
