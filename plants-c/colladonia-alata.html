Colladonia alata

Colladonia alata, commonly known as the winged fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, pinnately compound leaves that have a wing-like appearance on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spore-bearing capsules. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
