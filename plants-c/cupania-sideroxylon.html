Cupania sideroxylon

Cupania sideroxylon, commonly known as ironwood or black ironwood, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized tree native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. Cupania sideroxylon also produces small, inconspicuous flowers and fruits that are adapted for animal dispersal.
