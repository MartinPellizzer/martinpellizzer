Caladium bicolor f. neumannii

Caladium bicolor f. neumannii, commonly known as the Neumann's flameleaf, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage.

This cultivar is prized for its heart-shaped leaves that display a range of red, orange, and yellow hues, often with intricate patterns. It is a member of the Araceae family and thrives in warm, humid climates with bright, indirect light. The plant is typically propagated through tuberous roots and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden displays and container planting.
