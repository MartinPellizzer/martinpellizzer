Cistus umbellatus subsp. libani

Cistus umbellatus subsp.

libani, commonly known as Lebanon rockrose, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and clusters of pink to pale purple flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
