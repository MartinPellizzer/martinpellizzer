Cirsium degenii

Cirsium degenii, commonly known as Degen's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and robust, spiny leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with deeply lobed, often hairy foliage. Its flowers are usually purple or pink, arranged in dense clusters at the top of the stem.

Cirsium degenii is adapted to harsh environments, featuring a deep root system and a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.
