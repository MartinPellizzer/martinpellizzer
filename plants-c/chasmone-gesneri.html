Chasmone gesneri

Chasmone gesneri, commonly known as the Gesner's chasmone, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental and tropical plants. The plant is characterized by its trailing stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
