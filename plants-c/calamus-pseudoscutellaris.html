Calamus pseudoscutellaris

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is a tall, slender palm with long, pinnate leaves that are often used in traditional crafts. Calamus pseudoscutellaris is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
