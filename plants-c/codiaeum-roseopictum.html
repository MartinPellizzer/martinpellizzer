Codiaeum roseopictum

Codiaeum roseopictum, commonly known as the flame leaf or flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and textured foliage, which often features shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a waxy, glossy appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant colors.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to add visual interest to indoor and outdoor spaces.
