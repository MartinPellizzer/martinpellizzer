Casearia polyantha

Casearia polyantha, commonly known as the pink casearia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds, often dispersed by wind or animals.
