Clematis zanzibarensis

Clematis zanzibarensis, commonly known as the Zanzibar Clematis, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa.

It is a climbing vine that produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and is typically found in moist, tropical environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.

The flowers bloom throughout the year, making it a continuous source of visual interest.
