Cyperus miliifolius var. rotheryi

Cyperus miliifolius var. rotheryi, commonly known as the Rothery's flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often arranged in a fan shape at the base. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are densely packed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is known for its ability to thrive in moist soils and is sometimes used in ornamental horticulture.
