Colletia Spinosa Var. Valdiviana

Colletia spinosa var. valdiviana is a unique and hardy plant native to the coastal regions of southern Chile. It is known for its distinctive spiny stems and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. This variety is a variant of the broader Colletia spinosa species, which is adapted to arid and coastal environments. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and can tolerate salt spray, making it suitable for coastal gardens. Its resilience and ornamental features make it a popular choice for landscaping in maritime climates.
