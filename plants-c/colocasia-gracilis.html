Colocasia gracilis

Colocasia gracilis, commonly known as the slender arrowleaf taro, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, white, spadix-like flowers enclosed within a modified leaf called a spathe. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its edible corms.

The botanical characteristics of Colocasia gracilis make it a popular choice for ornamental and agricultural purposes.
