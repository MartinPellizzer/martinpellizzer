Clematis lishanensis

Clematis lishanensis, commonly known as the Lishan Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and showy, typically blue or purple flowers. The plant produces small, green, oval-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
