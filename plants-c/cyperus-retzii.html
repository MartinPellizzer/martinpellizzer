Cyperus retzii

Cyperus retzii, commonly known as spikerush, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and tall, spiky inflorescences. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, making it a common species in wetlands and along water bodies. Cyperus retzii produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense spikes, and it is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily, contributing to its success in various aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
