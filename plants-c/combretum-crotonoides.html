Combretum crotonoides

Combretum crotonoides, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red capsules that contain seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
