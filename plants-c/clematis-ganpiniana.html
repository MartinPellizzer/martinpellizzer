Clematis ganpiniana

Clematis ganpiniana, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue to purple. The plant thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation. Its vigorous growth habit requires support structures like trellises or fences.

Clematis ganpiniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscaping for its cascading blooms.
