Clinopodium chinense subsp. glabrescens

Clinopodium chinense subsp.

glabrescens, commonly known as Chinese wood betony, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with a fine, velvety texture.

It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or blue, arranged in whorled clusters.
