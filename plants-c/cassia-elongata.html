Cassia elongata

Cassia elongata, commonly known as the long pod senna, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive elongated shape and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

Cassia elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
