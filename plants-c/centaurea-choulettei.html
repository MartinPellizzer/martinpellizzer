Centaurea choulettei

Centaurea choulettei, commonly known as the French star-thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its spiny, purple-blue flowers and is part of the Asteraceae family. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in Mediterranean climates. It has been introduced to other regions, including parts of North America, where it can become invasive.

Its botanical features include narrow, lance-shaped leaves and a tall, branching stem.
