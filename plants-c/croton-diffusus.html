Croton diffusus

Croton diffusus, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that vary in color from green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
