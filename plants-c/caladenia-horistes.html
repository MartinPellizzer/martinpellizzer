Caladenia horistes

Caladenia horistes, commonly known as the Narrow-leafed Spider-Orchid, is a striking orchid native to Western Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, elongated flower that has a distinctive spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, narrow leaf and has a unique structure with a spurred labellum. It thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open habitats such as heathlands.

Caladenia horistes is a protected species due to its limited distribution and ecological significance.
