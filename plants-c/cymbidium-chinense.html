Cymbidium chinense

Cymbidium chinense, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species native to eastern Asia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with tall, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive green or yellow lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cymbidium chinense is widely cultivated for its ornamental value and cultural significance in traditional Chinese gardens.
