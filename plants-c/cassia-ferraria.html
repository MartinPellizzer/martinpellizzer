Cassia ferraria

Cassia ferraria, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers that appear in clusters, creating a dramatic visual impact. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple small leaflets, and its bark is smooth and greyish.

Cassia ferraria thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
