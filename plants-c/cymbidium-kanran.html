Cymbidium kanran

Cymbidium kanran, commonly known as the Japanese orchid, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments and prefers well-drained, acidic soil. It is a perennial herbaceous plant that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Cymbidium kanran is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
