Coelorachis corrugata

Coelorachis corrugata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a tropical epiphytic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive zigzag-shaped stems and clustered, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often have a waxy coating to reduce water loss.

Coelorachis corrugata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
