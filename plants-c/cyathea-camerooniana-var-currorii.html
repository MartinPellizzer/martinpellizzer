Cyathea camerooniana var. currorii

Cyathea camerooniana var. currorii, commonly known as the Cameroon tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the rainforests of Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant exhibits a distinctive trunk that is often covered with remnants of old leaf bases, giving it a rugged appearance. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length, contributing to its ornamental value.

This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
