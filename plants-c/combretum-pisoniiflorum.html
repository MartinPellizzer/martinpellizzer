Combretum pisoniiflorum

Combretum pisoniiflorum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is well adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savannah and woodland habitats.
