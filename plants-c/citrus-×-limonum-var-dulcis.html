Citrus × limonum var. dulcis

Citrus × limonum var. dulcis, commonly known as the sweet lemon.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its thick, aromatic peel and juicy, sweet-tart fruit. It is native to Southeast Asia and has been widely cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses. The leaves are glossy and dark green, while the flowers are white and fragrant.

The fruit typically matures in late summer and is rich in vitamin C and essential oils.
