Cnicus oligophyllus

Cnicus oligophyllus, commonly known as the lesser centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial with hairy stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open habitats such as roadsides and meadows.

Cnicus oligophyllus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
