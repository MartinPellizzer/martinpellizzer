Coelogyne weixiensis

Coelogyne weixiensis, commonly known as the Weixi coelogyne, is an epiphytic orchid native to China.

It features striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow with red or orange markings, making it a visually appealing species.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
