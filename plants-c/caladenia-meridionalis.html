Caladenia meridionalis

Caladenia meridionalis, commonly known as the Southern Spider Orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in sandy soils and is found in regions with a Mediterranean climate. Its flowers are usually green or brown with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of spiders.

Caladenia meridionalis is a protected species due to habitat loss and is often found in conservation reserves.
