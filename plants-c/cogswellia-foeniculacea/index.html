Cogswellia Foeniculacea

Cape honeysuckle, scientifically known as Cogswellia foeniculacea, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South Africa. This unique species is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in arid climates. Despite its ornamental appeal, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to flourish. Its ability to attract pollinators adds to its ecological value in the right environment.
