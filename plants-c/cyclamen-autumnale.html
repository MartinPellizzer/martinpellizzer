Cyclamen autumnale

Cyclamen autumnale, commonly known as the autumn cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and vibrant, bowl-shaped flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant prefers cool, moist environments and thrives in partial shade. Its flowers come in a range of colors, including pink, red, and white, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Cyclamen autumnale is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique foliage and seasonal bloom.
