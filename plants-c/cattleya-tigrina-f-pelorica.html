Cattleya tigrina f. pelorica

Cattleya tigrina f. pelorica, commonly known as the tigridian cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers with intricate, tiger-like markings, which give it its common name. The plant produces large, showy blooms that typically appear in late summer or early autumn. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This cultivar is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
