Croton tawaoensis

Croton tawaoensis, commonly known as the Tawao croton, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from bright yellow to deep red. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Croton tawaoensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique and vibrant leaf patterns.
