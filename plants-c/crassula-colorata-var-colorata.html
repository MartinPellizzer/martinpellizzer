Crassula colorata var. colorata

Crassula colorata var. colorata, commonly known as the "Laceleaf Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially in response to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
