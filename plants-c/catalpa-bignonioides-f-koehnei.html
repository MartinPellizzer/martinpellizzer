Catalpa bignonioides f. koehnei

Catalpa bignonioides f. koehnei, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a cultivar of the catalpa tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows as a deciduous tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white with a slight greenish tinge, and it produces long, bean-like pods containing seeds.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often planted in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
