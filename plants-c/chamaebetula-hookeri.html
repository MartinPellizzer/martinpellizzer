Chamaebetula hookeri

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces tiny, yellowish catkins in the spring, which are typical of the birch family.
