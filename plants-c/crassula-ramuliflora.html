Crassula ramuliflora

Crassula ramuliflora, commonly known as the rock rose or rock fig, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Crassula ramuliflora is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
