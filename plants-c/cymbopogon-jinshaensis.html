Cymbopogon jinshaensis

Cymbopogon jinshaensis, commonly known as the Jinsha grass, is a species of grass native to China, specifically found in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes numerous grass species known for their ecological and economic importance. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, elongated leaves, typical of many grasses. It thrives in moist, temperate environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species, highlighting its botanical significance in regional flora.
