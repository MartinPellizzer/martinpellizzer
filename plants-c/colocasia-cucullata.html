Colocasia cucullata

Colocasia cucullata, commonly known as the giant elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in humid climates.

The plant is also known for its ability to spread rapidly through rhizomes, making it both a popular garden plant and a potential weed.
