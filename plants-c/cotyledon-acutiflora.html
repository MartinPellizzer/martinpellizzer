Cotyledon acutiflora

Cotyledon acutiflora, commonly known as the sharp-flowered cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with red or purple markings. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, or orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
