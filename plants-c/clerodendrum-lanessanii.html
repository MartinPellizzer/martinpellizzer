Clerodendrum lanessanii

Clerodendrum lanessanii, commonly known as the purple flame or velvet tangerine, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant has elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Clerodendrum lanessanii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
