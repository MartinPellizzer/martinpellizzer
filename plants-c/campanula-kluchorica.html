Campanula kluchorica

Campanula kluchorica, commonly known as the Kluchorica bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Campanula kluchorica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
