Cymbidium goeringii

Cymbidium goeringii, commonly known as the Goering's orchid, is a species of orchid native to China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems and large, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a contrasting lip. It blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for winter floral arrangements.

Cymbidium goeringii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
