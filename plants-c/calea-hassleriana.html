Calea hassleriana

Calea hassleriana, commonly known as the "flower of the night," is a striking tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night and close in the morning. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its unique floral structure, which resembles a cluster of small flowers. Its leaves are broad and slightly hairy, often with a waxy texture.

Calea hassleriana is also noted for its medicinal properties, used in traditional remedies for various ailments.
