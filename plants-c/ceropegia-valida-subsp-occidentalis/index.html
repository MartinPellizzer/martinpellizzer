Ceropegia Valida Subsp. Occidentalis

Ceropegia valida subsp. occidentalis is a unique and striking plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its intricate, tubular flowers that attract specific pollinators. This species thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to harsh conditions. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its resilient nature, making it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure plant in the broader horticultural world.
