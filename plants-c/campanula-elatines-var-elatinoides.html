Campanula elatines var. elatinoides

Campanula elatines var. elatinoides, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, wetlands, and along stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
