Crassula merxmuelleri

Crassula merxmuelleri, commonly known as the Blue Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with a striking blue or purple hue, especially in cooler conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
