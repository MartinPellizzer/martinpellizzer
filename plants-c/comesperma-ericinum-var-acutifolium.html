Comesperma ericinum var. acutifolium

Comesperma ericinum var. acutifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved comesperma, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features dense, evergreen foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in spikes during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and rocky areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and ornamental gardening.
