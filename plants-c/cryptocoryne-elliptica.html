Cryptocoryne elliptica

Cryptocoryne elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and have a smooth, waxy surface. This plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions and is often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. Cryptocoryne elliptica is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

It is also known for its resilience and adaptability to various water conditions.
