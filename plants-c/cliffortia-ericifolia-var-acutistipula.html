Cliffortia ericifolia var. acutistipula

Cliffortia ericifolia var. acutistipula, commonly known as the narrow-leaved silverflax, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that thrives in full sun and is popular in rock gardens.
