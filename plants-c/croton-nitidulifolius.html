Croton nitidulifolius

Croton nitidulifolius, commonly known as the glossy leaf croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. Its growth habit is shrub-like, with a dense branching structure that makes it suitable for ornamental use.

The species is valued in horticulture for its striking foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
