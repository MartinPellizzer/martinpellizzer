Cyrtorchis aberrans

Cyrtorchis aberrans, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. Its flowers are usually arranged in a dense spike and are pollinated by insects.

Cyrtorchis aberrans is relatively rare and is considered a vulnerable species due to habitat loss and limited distribution.
