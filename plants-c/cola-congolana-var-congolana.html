Cola congolana var. congolana

Cola congolana var. congolana, commonly known as African cola, is a species of the cola genus native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. Its fruits are capsule-like, containing seeds that are used in traditional medicine and as a stimulant.

The plant is valued for its caffeine content and has been used in various cultural and medicinal practices.
