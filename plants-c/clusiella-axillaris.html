Clusiella axillaris

Clusiella axillaris, commonly known as the axillary clusiella, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae, which includes various species with ornamental and medicinal value. The plant is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy appearance.

Clusiella axillaris is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
