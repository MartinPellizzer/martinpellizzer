Cyathea bryophila

Cyathea bryophila, commonly known as the dwarf tree fern, is a species of tree fern belonging to the family Cyatheaceae.

It is characterized by its short, sturdy trunk and a dense, feathery frond structure. Native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a unique rhizome system that supports its growth and helps in nutrient absorption.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.
