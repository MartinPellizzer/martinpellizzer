Carex ekmanii var. hottensis

Carex ekmanii var. hottensis, commonly known as the Hottentot sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale to brownish flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water bodies.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
