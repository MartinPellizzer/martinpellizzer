Chusquea maurofernandeziana

Chusquea maurofernandeziana, commonly known as the Maurofernandezian bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Chusquea genus, which is part of the Poaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in Ecuador and Peru, where it thrives in humid, temperate climates.
