Croton lundianus var. similis

Croton lundianus var. similis, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves that vary in shape and hue. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark and leaves contain compounds that have been traditionally used in local medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in its native habitat.
