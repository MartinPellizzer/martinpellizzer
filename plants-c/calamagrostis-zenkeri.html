Calamagrostis zenkeri

Calamagrostis zenkeri, commonly known as the Zenker's reed, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and stream banks. The plant features tall, slender culms with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowering stems bear panicles of small, spike-like flowers that are usually pale green to golden in color.

Calamagrostis zenkeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland habitats.
