Calyptranthes hondurensis

Calyptranthes hondurensis, commonly known as the Honduras calyptranthes, is a member of the Myrtaceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Central America, particularly Honduras. The plant features simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy texture. Its flowers are small, white or pale pink, and arranged in clusters, while the fruit is a small, woody capsule.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.
