Cleistanthus pyrrhocarpus

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy green upper surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by distinctive red, fleshy fruits that are a key feature of the species.
