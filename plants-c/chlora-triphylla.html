Chlora triphylla

Chlora triphylla, commonly known as the three-leafed green, is a unique plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's foliage is rich in chlorophyll, contributing to its vibrant green color. It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal.

However, it is important to note that it is not commonly found in commercial horticulture.
