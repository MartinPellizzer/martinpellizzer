Clematis savatieri

Clematis savatieri, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, with a distinctive, almost spider-like appearance. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces small, greenish-white flowers in early summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis savatieri is often used in garden landscapes for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
