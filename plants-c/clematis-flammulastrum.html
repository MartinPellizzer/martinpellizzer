Clematis flammulastrum

Clematis flammulastrum, commonly known as the flameleaf clematis, is a deciduous vine native to parts of Asia.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis flammulastrum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
