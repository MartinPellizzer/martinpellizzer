Convolvulus tedmoorei

Convolvulus tedmoorei, commonly known as the Tedmoore's bindweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, characterized by its twining stems and showy flowers. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Convolvulus tedmoorei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
