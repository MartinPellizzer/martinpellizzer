Cyphomandra luteoalba

Cyphomandra luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow banana or yellow eggplant, is a member of the Solanaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant produces elongated, yellow fruits that resemble bananas, with a slightly acidic and sweet flavor. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while the stems are covered in fine pubescence.
