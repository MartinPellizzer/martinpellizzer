Chrysobalanus orbicularis

Chrysobalanus orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf chrysobalanus, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, orbicular-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
