Combretum lucidum

Combretum lucidum, commonly known as the shining flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and its striking, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, waxy surface that gives the plant its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in savannas and woodland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
