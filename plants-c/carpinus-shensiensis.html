Carpinus shensiensis

Carpinus shensiensis, commonly known as the Szechuan hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Betulaceae family and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The tree produces small, winged seeds that are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
