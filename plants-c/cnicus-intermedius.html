Cnicus intermedius

Cnicus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate sowthistle, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features lobed, hairy leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in a composite head. Its botanical characteristics include a taproot system and a preference for disturbed soils.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been introduced to other regions.
