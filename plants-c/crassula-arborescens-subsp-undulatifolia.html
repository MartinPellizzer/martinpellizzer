Crassula arborescens subsp. undulatifolia

Crassula arborescens subsp.

undulatifolia, commonly known as the undulating crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features a compact, shrub-like growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that have a wavy or undulating edge.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
