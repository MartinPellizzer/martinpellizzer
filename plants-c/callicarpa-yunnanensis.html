Callicarpa yunnanensis

Callicarpa yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its striking clusters of bright red berries that appear in the fall. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, white, tubular flowers in the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

The berries are not only ornamental but also attract birds and are used in traditional medicine.
