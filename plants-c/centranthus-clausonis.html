Centranthus clausonis

Centranthus clausonis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved red campion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers in dense clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it prefers well-drained soils in full sun.

Centranthus clausonis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
