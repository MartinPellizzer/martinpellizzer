Casearia mannii

Casearia mannii, commonly known as the African snowberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and often elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, followed by berries that mature to a bright red color. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some African cultures.
