Chlorophytum alpinum

Chlorophytum alpinum, commonly known as the alpine spider plant, is a hardy, evergreen perennial native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant is well adapted to cool, moist environments and thrives in partial to full shade. It produces small, white flowers in the summer, followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads.

Chlorophytum alpinum is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
