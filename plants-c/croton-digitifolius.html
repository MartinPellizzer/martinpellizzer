Croton digitifolius

Croton digitifolius, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
