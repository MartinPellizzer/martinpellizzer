Carphobolus asterotrichius

Carphobolus asterotrichius, commonly known as the star-hair grass, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of grasses. Its leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its common name.
