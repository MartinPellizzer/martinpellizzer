Calamagrostis Tenuifolia

Slender feathergrass, scientifically known as Calamagrostis tenuifolia, is a graceful perennial grass native to the wetlands and marshes of North America. It is characterized by its fine, slender leaves and delicate, feathery plumes that sway gracefully in the breeze. This plant thrives in moist environments and is often found along the edges of ponds, streams, and wet meadows. Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and wetland restoration projects. With its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife, Slender feathergrass plays an important ecological role in its habitat.
