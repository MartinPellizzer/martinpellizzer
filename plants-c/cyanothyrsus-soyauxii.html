Cyanothyrsus soyauxii

Cyanothyrsus soyauxii, commonly known as the African snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates. Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and winter flowering.

Cyanothyrsus soyauxii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
