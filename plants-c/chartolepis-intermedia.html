Chartolepis intermedia

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and dark green coloration. Its flowers are typically tubular with vibrant colors, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow.
