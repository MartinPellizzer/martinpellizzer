Cliffortia curvifolia

Cliffortia curvifolia, commonly known as the bent-leaved cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Cliffortia curvifolia is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
