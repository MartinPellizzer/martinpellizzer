Cymbidium simulans

Cymbidium simulans, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb with erect, grass-like foliage and produces tall, branched inflorescences. The flowers are typically fragrant, with a complex structure featuring three outer sepals and three inner petals. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.

This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
