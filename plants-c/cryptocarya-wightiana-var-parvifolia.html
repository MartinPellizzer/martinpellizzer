Cryptocarya wightiana var. parvifolia

Cryptocarya wightiana var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved cryptocarya, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, glossy leaves and fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests.
