Crepidium Warapussae

Crepidium warapussae is a rare orchid species discovered in the remote rainforests of Borneo. This delicate plant is known for its unique, intricate flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is highly sensitive to environmental changes. Due to habitat loss, it is now critically endangered and requires urgent conservation efforts. Its discovery has sparked interest in botanical research and biodiversity preservation.
