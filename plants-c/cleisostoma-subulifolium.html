Cleisostoma subulifolium

Cleisostoma subulifolium, commonly known as the needle-leaf cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, needle-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cleisostoma subulifolium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
