Coccothrinax barbadensis

Coccothrinax barbadensis, commonly known as the royal palm, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the islands of the Lesser Antilles. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of long, feathery leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark blue fruits containing a single seed.
