Clerodendrum assurgens

Clerodendrum assurgens, commonly known as the climbing clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that contain seeds, and its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
