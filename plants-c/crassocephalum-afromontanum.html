Crassocephalum afromontanum

Crassocephalum afromontanum, commonly known as the African blue daisy, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple composite flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
