Cattleya alexandrae var. rosea

Cattleya alexandrae var. rosea, commonly known as the Rose Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, ruffled blooms.

It features large, showy flowers with a pinkish-red hue and a distinctive, velvety texture. This variety is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

The plant is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
