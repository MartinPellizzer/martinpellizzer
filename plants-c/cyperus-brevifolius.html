Cyperus brevifolius

Cyperus brevifolius, commonly known as the narrowleaf flatsedge, is a member of the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its grass-like appearance, with slender, triangular stems and narrow leaves. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it often grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically green or brown in color.

Cyperus brevifolius is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various water conditions.
