Cyathea francii

Cyathea francii, commonly known as the French tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to anchor itself in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically evergreen and can reach lengths of up to several meters.

Cyathea francii is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
