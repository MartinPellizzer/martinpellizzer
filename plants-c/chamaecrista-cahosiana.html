Chamaecrista cahosiana

Chamaecrista cahosiana, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or disturbed habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural areas.
