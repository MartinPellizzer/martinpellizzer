Cistus × cyprius var. major

Cistus × cyprius var. major, commonly known as the Cyprus rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic evergreen leaves and clusters of pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

The species is also valued for its role in traditional herbal medicine and its ability to support local pollinators.
