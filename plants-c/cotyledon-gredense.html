Cotyledon gredense

Cotyledon gredense, commonly known as the Gredense Hooded Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
