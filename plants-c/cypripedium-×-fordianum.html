Cypripedium × fordianum

Cypripedium × fordianum, commonly known as the Fordian lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is a key feature of the Cypripedium genus. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on rocky or wooded slopes. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces a single, fragrant flower per stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticulture for its unique and elegant appearance.
