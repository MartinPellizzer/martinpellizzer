Crotalaria madurensis var. kurnoolica

Crotalaria madurensis var. kurnoolica, commonly known as Kurnool crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is typically found in moist, sandy soils and is used in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory properties.

The species is also noted for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
