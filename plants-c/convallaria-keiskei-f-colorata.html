Convallaria keiskei f. colorata

Convallaria keiskei f. colorata, commonly known as the pink star-of-Bethlehem, is a rare and striking variant of the star-of-Bethlehem plant.

It belongs to the Convallariaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive pink or lilac-colored flowers, which contrast with the typical white blooms of the species. The plant is native to Japan and is often found in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green, providing a striking backdrop to its vibrant flowers.
