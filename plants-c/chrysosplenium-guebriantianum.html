Chrysosplenium guebriantianum

Chrysosplenium guebriantianum, commonly known as the Guebriant's golden spleenwort, is a small, evergreen perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. Chrysosplenium guebriantianum thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found growing on rocky slopes or in shaded forest understories.
