Centaurea jacea subsp. nigrescens

Centaurea jacea subsp.

nigrescens, commonly known as the dark-flowered centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia. It is a perennial herb with erect, branching stems that often grow up to 60 cm in height.

The plant produces showy, spiky inflorescences composed of blue to purple ray flowers and yellow disk flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped, hairy, and often have a reddish or purplish tinge.

It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.
