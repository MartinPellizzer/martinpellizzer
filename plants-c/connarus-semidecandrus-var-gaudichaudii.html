Connarus semidecandrus var. gaudichaudii

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which consist of multiple leaflets arranged in a distinctive pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
