Coelogyne pyrranthela

Coelogyne pyrranthela, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne pyrranthela is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
