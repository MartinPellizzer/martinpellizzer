Clethra wuyishanica

Clethra wuyishanica, commonly known as the Wuyi clethra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Clethraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Wuyi Mountains region. The plant is characterized by its clusters of white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Clethra wuyishanica typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
