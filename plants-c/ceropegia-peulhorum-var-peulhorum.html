Ceropegia peulhorum var. peulhorum

Ceropegia peulhorum var. peulhorum, commonly known as the Cape Verde ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to the Cape Verde Islands.

It is a succulent perennial with fleshy, cylindrical stems and leaves that are typically opposite and lanceolate. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are often pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
