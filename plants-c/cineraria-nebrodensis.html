Cineraria nebrodensis

Cineraria nebrodensis, commonly known as the Sicilian cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the volcanic regions of Sicily.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cineraria nebrodensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
