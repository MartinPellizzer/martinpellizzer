Cyathea heteromorpha

Cyathea heteromorpha, commonly known as the variable tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow up to several meters in length.

Cyathea heteromorpha plays an important ecological role in forest ecosystems, providing habitat and contributing to soil stability.
