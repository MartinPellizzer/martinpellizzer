Chaetachme microcarpa

Chaetachme microcarpa, commonly known as the narrowleaf lily.

It is a perennial herb native to the western United States and parts of Canada. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Liliaceae family.
