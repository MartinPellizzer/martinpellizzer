Centaurea × jonescui

Centaurea × jonescui, commonly known as the Jones' cornflower, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowering heads and grass-like basal leaves. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and produces showy composite flowers that attract pollinators.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
