Consolida pubescens var. loscosii

Consolida pubescens var. loscosii, commonly known as the Loscosii poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to deep red. The plant produces slender, erect stems with hairy foliage, which gives it its scientific name "pubescens.". It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

The seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its natural spread in suitable habitats.
