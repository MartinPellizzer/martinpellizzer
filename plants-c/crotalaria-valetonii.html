Crotalaria valetonii

Crotalaria valetonii, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are encased in a hard, spiny pod, and the plant is often grown for its ornamental value and as a source of protein-rich seeds.

Crotalaria valetonii is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
