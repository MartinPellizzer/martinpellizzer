Croton myriaster var. austromadecassus

Croton myriaster var. austromadecassus, commonly known as the Madagascan croton, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The plant features palmate leaves with multiple lobes, and its flowers are typically small and clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical climates and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
