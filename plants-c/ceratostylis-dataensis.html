Ceratostylis dataensis

Ceratostylis dataensis, commonly known as the Data Mountain orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the orchid family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near streams or in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Ceratostylis dataensis is noted for its delicate appearance and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts due to its unique habitat and limited distribution.
