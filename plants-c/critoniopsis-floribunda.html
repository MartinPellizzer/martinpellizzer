Critoniopsis floribunda

Critoniopsis floribunda, commonly known as the flowering critoniopsis, is a perennial herb native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
