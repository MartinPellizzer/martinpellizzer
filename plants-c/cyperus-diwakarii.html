Cyperus diwakarii

Cyperus diwakarii, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are usually green or brown in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in suitable habitats.

Cyperus diwakarii is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity.
