Cryptandra largiflora

Cryptandra largiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered cryptandra, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Western Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to low rainfall conditions. Its flowers are typically visited by nectar-feeding insects and birds, contributing to its ecological significance.

Cryptandra largiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
