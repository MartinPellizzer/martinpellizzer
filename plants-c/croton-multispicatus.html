Croton multispicatus

Croton multispicatus, commonly known as the multispiculate croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often display a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Croton multispicatus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
