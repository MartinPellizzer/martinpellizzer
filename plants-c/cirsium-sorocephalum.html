Cirsium sorocephalum

Cirsium sorocephalum, commonly known as the purple stem thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands. Its robust root system allows it to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.

The species is also known for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
