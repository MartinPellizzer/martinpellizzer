Cineraria pratensis

Cineraria pratensis, commonly known as the meadow cineraria, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Campanulaceae and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring moist, well-drained soils. It has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine down.
