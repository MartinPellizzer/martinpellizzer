Cercocarpus betulifolius var. minor

Cercocarpus betulifolius var. minor, commonly known as the California buckthorn, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically green on top and whitish underneath. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for various wildlife species. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in chaparral and woodland habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and ecologically significant plant in its native range.
