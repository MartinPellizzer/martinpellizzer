Cryptanthus viridovinosus

Cryptanthus viridovinosus, commonly known as the green wine bellflower, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Haemodoraceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a greenish tinge. Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
