Casuarina deplancheana var. debibilis

Casuarina deplancheana var. debibilis, commonly known as the slender casuarina, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive feathery foliage, composed of tiny scale-like leaves arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, cone-like fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in its native habitat.
