Centaurea diffusa var. ammophila

Centaurea diffusa var. ammophila, commonly known as the sand centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that thrives in sandy or coastal environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow or orange flowers with purple markings. It is often found in dunes and other sandy soils where it helps stabilize the substrate.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it an important species in coastal ecosystems.
