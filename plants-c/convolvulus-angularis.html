Convolvulus angularis

Convolvulus angularis, commonly known as the horned morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive horn-like seed capsules and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Convolvulus angularis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
