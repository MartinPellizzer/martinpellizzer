Campanula linifolia f. langsdorffiana

Campanula linifolia f. langsdorffiana, commonly known as the langsdorffian bluebell, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a silvery-white underside. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hardy perennial.
