Chamaecrista enneryana

Chamaecrista enneryana, commonly known as the yellow burr legume, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
