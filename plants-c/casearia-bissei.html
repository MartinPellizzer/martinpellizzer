Casearia bissei

Casearia bissei, commonly known as the Brazilian beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking clusters of violet-blue berries. The berries, which are edible when ripe, are a key feature of the plant and attract various bird species.
