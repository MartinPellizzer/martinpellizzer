Chrysophyllum metallicum

Chrysophyllum metallicum, commonly known as the metallic waxtree, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that have a waxy texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, citrus-like fruits. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its relatively low maintenance requirements.
