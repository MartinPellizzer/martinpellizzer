Claudia

Claudia, commonly known as the Claudia orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its elegant, slender stems and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Native to tropical regions of South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil. Claudia orchids require indirect sunlight and regular watering to maintain their health and promote blooming.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a potted plant.
