Conospermum eatoniae

Conospermum eatoniae, commonly known as the Kangaroo Island flame bush, is a shrub native to the south-eastern coast of Australia.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, leathery leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. It is well adapted to sandy soils and can tolerate drought conditions.

This species is of interest for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
