Clerodendrum ternifolium var. serratifolium

Clerodendrum ternifolium var. serratifolium, commonly known as the serrated-leaved clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are serrated along the edges, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
