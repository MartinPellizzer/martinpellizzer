Croton Verbascoides

Mexican croton, scientifically known as Croton verbascoides, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico. With its vibrant, paddle-shaped leaves that display a range of colors from deep green to fiery red, it stands out in any garden or indoor setting. This shrub-like plant is valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of conditions, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Its unique foliage makes it a standout addition to landscapes, though it requires careful handling due to its toxic sap. Despite its beauty, it is important to exercise caution when handling or growing this plant.
