Cyrtopodium ecristatum

Cyrtopodium ecristatum, commonly known as the naked orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It features a slender, upright stem with several lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a white or pale pink lip and yellow petals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyrtopodium ecristatum is valued for its elegant appearance and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental qualities.
