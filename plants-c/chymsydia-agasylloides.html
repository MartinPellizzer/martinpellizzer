Chymsydia agasylloides

Chymsydia agasylloides, commonly known as the silver leaf orchid, is a unique epiphytic plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-gray leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and surrounding substrates.
