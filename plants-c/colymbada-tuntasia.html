Colymbada tuntasia

Colymbada tuntasia, commonly known as the Tuntasia water lily, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that provide shade and habitat for aquatic life. The plant produces white, fragrant flowers that bloom in the early morning. Its roots are submerged, anchoring it in shallow water bodies.

Colymbada tuntasia plays a vital role in maintaining water quality and supporting biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
