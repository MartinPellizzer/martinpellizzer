Crucianella latifolia var. monspeliaca

Crucianella latifolia var. monspeliaca, commonly known as the broadleaf crucianella, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or Mediterranean climates.

The fruit is a capsule that releases tiny seeds, contributing to its dispersal in suitable habitats.
