Croton wittianus

Croton wittianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate or elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, and it produces woody, capsule-like fruits.

Croton wittianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some regions.
