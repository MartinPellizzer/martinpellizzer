Cheiridopsis velutina

Cheiridopsis velutina, commonly known as the velvet ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

Cheiridopsis velutina is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
