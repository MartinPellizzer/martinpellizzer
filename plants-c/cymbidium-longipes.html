Cymbidium longipes

Cymbidium longipes, commonly known as the longiped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium longipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
