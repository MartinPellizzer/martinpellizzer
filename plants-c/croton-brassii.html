Croton brassii

Croton brassii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that are typically green with prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. Croton brassii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
