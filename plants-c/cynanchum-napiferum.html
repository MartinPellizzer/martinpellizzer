Cynanchum napiferum

Cynanchum napiferum, commonly known as Chinese milkweed or Chinese dogwood, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist woodlands and stream banks. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a follicle that splits open to release seeds with silky hairs.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
