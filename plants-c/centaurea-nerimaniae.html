Centaurea nerimaniae

Centaurea nerimaniae, commonly known as the Nerimania thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and bright yellow to orange flower heads. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in some regions.
