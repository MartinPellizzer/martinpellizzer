Cymbidium undulatum

Cymbidium undulatum, commonly known as the wavy-leaf orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant with tall, upright stems that bear lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a wavy or undulating pattern on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium undulatum is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
