Cytisus cassius

Cytisus cassius, commonly known as the golden broom, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, woody stems and small, compound leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

Cytisus cassius is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
