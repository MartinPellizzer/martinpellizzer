Carum loloense

Carum loloense, commonly known as Lolo mountain caraway, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant features slender, branched stems and small white or greenish flowers arranged in umbels. Its leaves are finely divided and have a sharp, peppery aroma.

Carum loloense is often used in traditional medicine for its purported digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.
