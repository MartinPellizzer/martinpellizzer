Chamaedorea vanninii

Chamaedorea vanninii, commonly known as the Vannini's bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, upright stems that produce feathery, pinnate leaves, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining, moist soil. It is relatively low-maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its compact size and air-purifying qualities add both aesthetic and functional value to any space.
