Citrus × aurantium var. torulosa

Citrus × aurantium var. torulosa, commonly known as the sour orange.

It is a citrus hybrid native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits with a thick, yellowish-orange peel. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with thorny branches and dark green, glossy leaves. Its flowers are fragrant and white, and the fruit is typically used in culinary and medicinal applications.

The varietal name "torulosa" refers to its distinctive, warty or bumpy fruit surface.
