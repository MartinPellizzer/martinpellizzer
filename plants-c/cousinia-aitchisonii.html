Cousinia aitchisonii

Cousinia aitchisonii, commonly known as Aitchison's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Pakistan and northern India.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand harsh climatic conditions.

Cousinia aitchisonii is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
