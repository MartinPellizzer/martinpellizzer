Cheirostylis javanica

Cheirostylis javanica, commonly known as the Javanese orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
