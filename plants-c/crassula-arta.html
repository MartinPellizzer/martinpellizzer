Crassula arta

Crassula arta, commonly known as the Art's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping.

Crassula arta is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
