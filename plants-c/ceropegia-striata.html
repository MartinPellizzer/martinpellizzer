Ceropegia striata

Ceropegia striata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires moderate watering to prevent root rot.

Ceropegia striata is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
