Cattleya spectabilis

Cattleya spectabilis, commonly known as the showy cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and sword-shaped, often arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Cattleya spectabilis is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
