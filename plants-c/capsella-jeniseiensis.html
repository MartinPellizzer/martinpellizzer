Capsella jeniseiensis

Capsella jeniseiensis, commonly known as the Jenisei weed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and heart-shaped leaves. Native to the temperate regions of Asia, particularly Siberia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant produces slender, elongated pods that contain several small, round seeds.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural fields and along roadsides.
