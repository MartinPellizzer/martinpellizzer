Crotalaria kasikiensis

Crotalaria kasikiensis, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to seasonal rainfall patterns.

Crotalaria kasikiensis is also known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial in agricultural systems.
