Caladium andreanum

Caladium andreanum, commonly known as the Orange-leaf Caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which features vibrant shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically heart-shaped or arrowhead-shaped, with a waxy texture and prominent veins. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and indoor settings.
