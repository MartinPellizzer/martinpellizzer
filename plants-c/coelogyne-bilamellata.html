Coelogyne bilamellata

Coelogyne bilamellata, commonly known as the two-lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring or early summer. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

Coelogyne bilamellata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
