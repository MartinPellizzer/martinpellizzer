Ceropegia humilis

Ceropegia humilis, commonly known as the string of hearts plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features trailing stems with heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
