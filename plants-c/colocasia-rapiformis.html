Colocasia rapiformis

Colocasia rapiformis, commonly known as the arrow leaf elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces striking purple or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full shade.

Colocasia rapiformis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative houseplant.
