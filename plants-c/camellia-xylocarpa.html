Camellia xylocarpa

Camellia xylocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to pale pink. The plant produces woody capsules that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and prefers partial shade.

Camellia xylocarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
