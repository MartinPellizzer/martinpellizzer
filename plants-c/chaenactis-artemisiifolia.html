Chaenactis artemisiifolia

Chaenactis artemisiifolia, commonly known as the goatweed or yarrowleaf mouseear, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and ray flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has small, hairy leaves and produces yellow to reddish flowers that bloom in the summer.

It is often considered a weed but plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small insects.
