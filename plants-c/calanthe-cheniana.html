Calanthe cheniana

Calanthe cheniana, commonly known as the Chen's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer or early autumn, producing pink to purple blooms with a distinctive hooded lip. Calanthe cheniana is often found in shaded, humid environments such as forest understories.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
