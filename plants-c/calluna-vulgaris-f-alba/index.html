Calluna Vulgaris F. Alba

Calluna vulgaris f. alba, commonly known as the white heather, is a striking variant of the common heather plant. This low-growing evergreen shrub is native to the cool, acidic soils of northern Europe. Its delicate white flowers bloom in late summer, adding a soft and ethereal beauty to the landscape. Often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its unique coloration makes it a popular choice for both landscaping and floral arrangements.
