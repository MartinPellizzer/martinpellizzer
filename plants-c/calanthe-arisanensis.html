Calanthe arisanensis

Calanthe arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually arranged in a fan shape, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Calanthe arisanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
