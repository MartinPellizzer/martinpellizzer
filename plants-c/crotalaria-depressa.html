Crotalaria depressa

Crotalaria depressa, commonly known as the prostrate cowpea or yellow burr medic, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture and produces yellow flowers in clusters. Its seed pods are cylindrical and contain several oblong seeds.

Crotalaria depressa is often used in agricultural settings for soil stabilization and as a forage crop.
