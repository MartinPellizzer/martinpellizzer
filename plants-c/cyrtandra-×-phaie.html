Cyrtandra × phaie

Cyrtandra × phaie, commonly known as the hybrid beautyberry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Cyrtandra and Phaeanthus species, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parents. The plant is native to tropical regions of the Americas, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It produces clusters of small, showy flowers that transition into bright red berries, which are a key feature of its ornamental value.

Its foliage is typically glossy and evergreen, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
