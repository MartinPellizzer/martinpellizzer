Coreopsis nudata

Coreopsis nudata, commonly known as the slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with prominent central disks. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its blooming period extends from late spring to early fall, adding vibrant color to the landscape.
