Cassinia thinicola

Cassinia thinicola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cassinia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellow-orange flower clusters. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Cassinia thinicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
