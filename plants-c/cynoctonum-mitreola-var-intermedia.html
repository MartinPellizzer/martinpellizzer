Cynoctonum mitreola var. intermedia

Cynoctonum mitreola var. intermedia, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, orange-red berries and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils. It has a rapid growth rate and can become invasive in certain regions.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
