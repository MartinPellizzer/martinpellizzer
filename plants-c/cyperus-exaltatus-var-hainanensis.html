Cyperus exaltatus var. hainanensis

Cyperus exaltatus var. hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with triangular stems. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist to aquatic conditions. Its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of spikelets, which are often reddish in color.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils.
