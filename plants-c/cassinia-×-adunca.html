Cassinia × adunca

Cassinia × adunca, commonly known as the silver bush, is a hybrid shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Cassinia × adunca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in both garden landscaping and traditional medicine.
