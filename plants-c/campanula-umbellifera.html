Campanula umbellifera

Campanula umbellifera, commonly known as the bellflower of the umbel, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and rocky areas. The plant features showy blue or violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Campanula umbellifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
