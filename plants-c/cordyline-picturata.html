Cordyline picturata

Cordyline picturata, commonly known as the picture cordyline, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of green, red, and purple. The plant is characterized by its striking variegation, which adds visual interest to gardens and landscapes. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Cordyline picturata is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add a tropical feel to any setting.
