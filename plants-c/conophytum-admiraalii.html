Conophytum admiraalii

Conophytum admiraalii, commonly known as the admiraal's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are often ribbed and covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. Its unique appearance and ability to store water make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
