Centauropsis laurifolia

Centauropsis laurifolia, commonly known as the golden mistflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Centauropsis laurifolia is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
