Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. commersonii

Cinnamomum zeylanicum var. commersonii, commonly known as Ceylon cinnamon, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark, which is harvested to produce the spice cinnamon. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its small, yellowish flowers are followed by dark blue berries containing winged seeds.

This variety is valued for its essential oils and is often cultivated for both ornamental and commercial purposes.
