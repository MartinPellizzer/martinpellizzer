Carex stellulata f. excelsior

Carex stellulata f. excelsior, commonly known as the alpine sedge, is a variant of the slender sedge native to high-altitude environments.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold, harsh climates.

Due to its specialized habitat, it is often found in alpine meadows and mountainous regions.
