Callista Kuhlii

Callista kuhlii, scientifically known as Callista kuhlii, is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of Borneo. This unique species belongs to the family Araceae, which includes many aquatic and semi-aquatic plants. Its most notable feature is its vibrant, colorful inflorescence that resembles a delicate lantern. Callista kuhlii is often found in shaded, humid environments near water sources. Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is highly valued by botanists and conservationists alike.
