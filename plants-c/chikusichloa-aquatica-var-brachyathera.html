Chikusichloa aquatica var. brachyathera

Chikusichloa aquatica var. brachyathera, commonly known as water swordgrass, is a perennial aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant thrives in shallow water, often found in ponds, marshes, and slow-moving streams. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily along the water's edge.

It plays an important role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems by preventing erosion and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
