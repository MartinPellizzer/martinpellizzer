Chlorophytum turritum

Chlorophytum turritum, commonly known as the turban-shaped spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features strap-like, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green with a white midrib, forming a dense rosette. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by pale blue or white berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ability to propagate through offsets makes it easy to cultivate and maintain.
