Chusquea quila var. laxiflora

Chusquea quila var. laxiflora, commonly known as the Andean bamboo, is a species of grass-like plant native to the Andes region of South America.

The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.
