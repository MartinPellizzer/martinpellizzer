Cnidoscolus basiacanthus

Cnidoscolus basiacanthus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its roots contain compounds that have been used traditionally for their medicinal properties.

However, it is important to note that the plant is toxic and should not be ingested.
