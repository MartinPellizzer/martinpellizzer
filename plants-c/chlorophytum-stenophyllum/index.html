Chlorophytum Stenophyllum

Spider plant, scientifically known as Chlorophytum stenophyllum, is a popular houseplant cherished for its easy care and aesthetic appeal. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in a variety of indoor environments with minimal maintenance. Its long, arching leaves and small white flowers make it a favorite among plant enthusiasts. This plant is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it both functional and decorative. With its resilience and adaptability, the spider plant is an ideal choice for both beginners and experienced gardeners.
