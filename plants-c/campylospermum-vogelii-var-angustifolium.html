Campylospermum vogelii var. angustifolium

Campylospermum vogelii var. angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leafed campylospermum, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal and sandy environments.
