Chaetogastra cogniauxiana

Chaetogastra cogniauxiana, commonly known as the African orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and grasslands. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of a dense cluster of colorful blooms.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific care to thrive.
