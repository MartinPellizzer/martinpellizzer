Coryanthes bruchmuelleri

Coryanthes bruchmuelleri, commonly known as the Bruchmueller's hooded orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, intricate flowers that resemble a hooded structure, with a distinctive spur at the base. The plant produces vibrant, colorful blooms that can range from deep red to orange, often with contrasting patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat specificity, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
