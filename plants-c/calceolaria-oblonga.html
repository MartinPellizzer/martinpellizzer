Calceolaria oblonga

Calceolaria oblonga, commonly known as the oblong slipper orchid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments, often in mountainous regions. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Calceolaria oblonga is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, colorful blooms.
