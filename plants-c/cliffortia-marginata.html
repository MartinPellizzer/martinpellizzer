Cliffortia marginata

Cliffortia marginata, commonly known as the silver-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky, coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves and produces clusters of small, white flowers with yellow centers. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water once established.

Cliffortia marginata is often used in coastal gardens for its hardiness and ornamental value.
