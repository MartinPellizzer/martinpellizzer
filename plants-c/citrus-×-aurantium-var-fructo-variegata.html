Citrus × aurantium var. fructo-variegata

Citrus × aurantium var. fructo-variegata, commonly known as the variegated citron.

This variant is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its unique variegated foliage and fruit. The leaves exhibit a mix of green and white or yellow patterns, making it visually distinct. The fruit is typically smaller than standard citrons and has a thick, aromatic rind.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and has limited use in culinary applications due to its flavor profile.
