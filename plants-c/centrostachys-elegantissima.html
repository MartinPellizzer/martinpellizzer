Centrostachys elegantissima

Centrostachys elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant stachys.

It is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States, belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae. The plant features opposite, hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces spikes of purple to pink flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners interested in native plants.
