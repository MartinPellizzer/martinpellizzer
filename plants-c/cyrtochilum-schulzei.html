Cyrtochilum schulzei

Cyrtochilum schulzei, commonly known as the Schulze's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, typically featuring a short rhizome and a few erect, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cyrtochilum schulzei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
