Crotalaria grata

Crotalaria grata, commonly known as the pretty spurred crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its distinctive spurred petals and clusters of yellow or orange flowers. It typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and trifoliate leaves.

Crotalaria grata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
