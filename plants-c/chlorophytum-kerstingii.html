Chlorophytum kerstingii

Chlorophytum kerstingii, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail Plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, sword-shaped leaves that are often striped with white or yellow markings, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in a loose inflorescence, which are followed by spherical, green berries. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
