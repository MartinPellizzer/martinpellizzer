Corybas pruinosus

Corybas pruinosus, commonly known as the glossy hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, hood-like flower that is typically greenish-brown with a prominent central spur. The plant produces a single, showy flower that blooms from late autumn to early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a waxy, glossy coating.

Corybas pruinosus thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understoreys.
