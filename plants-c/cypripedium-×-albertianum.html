Cypripedium × albertianum

Cypripedium × albertianum, commonly known as the Alberta lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, typically pink or white in color. The plant is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. It has fleshy, dark green leaves and produces a single flower per stem.

Due to its ornamental value and limited natural range, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
