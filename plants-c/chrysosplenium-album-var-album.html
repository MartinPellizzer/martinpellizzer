Chrysosplenium album var. album

Chrysosplenium album var. album, commonly known as white chrysosplenium, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its silver-white to pale green foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in woodland areas.

Its ornamental value lies in its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
