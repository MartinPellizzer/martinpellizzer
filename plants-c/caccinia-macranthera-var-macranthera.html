Caccinia macranthera var. macranthera

Caccinia macranthera var. macranthera, commonly known as the large-flowered caccinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and aromatic.
