Cynoglossum argenteum

Cynoglossum argenteum, commonly known as silverleaf forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features distinctive silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Cynoglossum argenteum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and easy maintenance.
