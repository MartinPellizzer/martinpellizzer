Chamaecrista kirkbridei

Chamaecrista kirkbridei, commonly known as Kirkbride's burr legume, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces distinctive, compound leaves and yellow or orange floral clusters. Its fruits are pod-like structures that contain several seeds, and it is adapted to arid environments.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
