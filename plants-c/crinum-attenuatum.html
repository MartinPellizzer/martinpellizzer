Crinum attenuatum

Crinum attenuatum, commonly known as the slender crinum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, narrow leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. Crinum attenuatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
