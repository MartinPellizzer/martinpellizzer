Coprosma stephanocarpa

Coprosma stephanocarpa, commonly known as the silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a silvery-gray color, providing a striking visual contrast. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers followed by red berries that are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers moist, well-drained conditions.

Coprosma stephanocarpa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
