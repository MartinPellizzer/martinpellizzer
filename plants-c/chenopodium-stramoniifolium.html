Chenopodium stramoniifolium

Chenopodium stramoniifolium, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, and has a sprawling growth habit. The plant features alternate, ovate to triangular leaves with serrated margins and a rough texture. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in dense, terminal spikes, and it produces oblong, three-angled seeds.

It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions and is adapted to thrive in poor soil conditions.
