Cyperus orthostachyus var. dahuricus

Cyperus orthostachyus var. dahuricus, commonly known as the Siberian flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Asia.

It typically grows in wet or moist soils, often found in marshes, wetlands, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in water gardens or as a ground cover.

The species is also known for its adaptability to various water conditions and its ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
