Conospermum scorzonerifolium

Conospermum scorzonerifolium, commonly known as the rock heath or coastal rock heath, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woody growth and small, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Conospermum scorzonerifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
