Crepidomanes thysanostomum var. blepharistomum

Crepidomanes thysanostomum var. blepharistomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, fleshy leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas or rocky habitats. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and it is adapted to withstand drought and salt spray.

The variety blepharistomum is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form.
