Cassinia scabrida

Cassinia scabrida, commonly known as rough cassinia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. Its leaves are rough and hairy, giving it a distinctive texture.
