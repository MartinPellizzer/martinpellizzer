Cineraria candida

Cineraria candida, commonly known as the white cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, dandelion-like flowers. Cineraria candida is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, biennial blooms.
