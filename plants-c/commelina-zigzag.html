Commelina zigzag

Commelina zigzag, commonly known as zigzag commelina, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the midrib, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water gardens and bog landscapes.
