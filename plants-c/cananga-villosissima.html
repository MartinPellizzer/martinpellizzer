Cananga villosissima

Cananga villosissima, commonly known as the torch tree or Chinese hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a sweet, fragrant scent. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its bark is smooth and grayish in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing as a shrub or small tree.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
