Cyrtanthus spiralis

Cyrtanthus spiralis, commonly known as the spiral bellflower, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features elegant, spiral-shaped leaves that contribute to its unique appearance. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a favored plant among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts.
