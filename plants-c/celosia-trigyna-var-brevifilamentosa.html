Celosia trigyna var. brevifilamentosa

Celosia trigyna var. brevifilamentosa, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a compact, ornamental grass-like plant native to tropical regions.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, plume-like inflorescences in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

It is relatively drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
