Chromolaena pseudinsignis

Chromolaena pseudinsignis, commonly known as the false chromolaena, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.
