Centaurea sublanata var. litochorensis

Centaurea sublanata var. litochorensis, commonly known as the Litochorensis Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Greece.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, silver-gray leaves covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat. It produces striking blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
