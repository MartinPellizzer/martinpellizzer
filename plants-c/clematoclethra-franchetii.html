Clematoclethra franchetii

Clematoclethra franchetii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese holly, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are an important food source for birds. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
