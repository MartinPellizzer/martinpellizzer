Cassia thunbergiana

Cassia thunbergiana, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain flat, dark brown seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia thunbergiana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
