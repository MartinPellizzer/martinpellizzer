Centaurea tenuiflora var. fastigiata

Centaurea tenuiflora var. fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems and small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
