Chytraculia nigricans

Chytraculia nigricans, commonly known as the black velvet fern, is a unique plant characterized by its dark, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Lepidocarpaceae and is native to tropical regions of South America. The plant exhibits a distinctive morphology with dense, overlapping scales that give it a soft, almost fuzzy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
