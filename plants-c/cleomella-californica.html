Cleomella californica

Cleomella californica, commonly known as the California cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet. The plant produces showy, yellow or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of alternate, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture.

Cleomella californica is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
