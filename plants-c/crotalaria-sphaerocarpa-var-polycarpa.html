Crotalaria sphaerocarpa var. polycarpa

Crotalaria sphaerocarpa var. polycarpa, commonly known as the globe crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its spherical seed pods. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy, alternate leaves and yellow flowers. Its seeds are encased in thick, woody pods that split open to release the seeds.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
