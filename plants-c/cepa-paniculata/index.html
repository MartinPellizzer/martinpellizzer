Cepa Paniculata

Cepa paniculata is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family known for its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts. The plant's foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that add a lush texture to any landscape. With proper care, Cepa paniculata can bloom spectacularly, making it a standout addition to any collection.
