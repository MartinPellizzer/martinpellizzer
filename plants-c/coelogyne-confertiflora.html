Coelogyne confertiflora

Coelogyne confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia, Indonesia, and the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and its ability to produce multiple inflorescences. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and is known for its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings, making it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.

Coelogyne confertiflora is often grown in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
