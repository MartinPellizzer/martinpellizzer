Cyanothamnus inflexus subsp. montiazureus

Cyanothamnus inflexus subsp.

montiazureus, commonly known as the mountain bluebush, is a low-growing shrub native to southern Australia. It features dense clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, silver-blue leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to attract native pollinators.
