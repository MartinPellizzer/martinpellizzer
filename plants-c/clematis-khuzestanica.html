Clematis khuzestanica

Clematis khuzestanica, commonly known as the Khuzestan clematis, is a flowering plant native to Iran.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, usually white or pink flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and has a woody vine structure. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant for gardens and botanical collections.
