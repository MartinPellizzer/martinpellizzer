Crassula alsinoides

Crassula alsinoides, commonly known as the "Laceleaf Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, densely packed foliage with a distinctive crinkled, almost lace-like appearance. The leaves are typically blue-gray in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the summer months.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or as a potted specimen due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.
