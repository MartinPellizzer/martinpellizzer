Croton syringifolius

Croton syringifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by distinctive, spiny capsules that contain the seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
