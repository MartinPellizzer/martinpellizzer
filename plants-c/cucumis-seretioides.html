Cucumis seretioides

Cucumis seretioides, commonly known as the African horned cucumber, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and melons. The plant produces distinctive, spiny fruits that resemble horns, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it thrives in warm, tropical climates.

The fruit is edible and has a mild, slightly bitter flavor, often used in local cuisines.
