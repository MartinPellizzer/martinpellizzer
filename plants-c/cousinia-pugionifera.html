Cousinia pugionifera

Cousinia pugionifera, commonly known as the swordleaf fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow to pale green flowers. The plant thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in dry, open habitats. It is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions, including high temperatures and low precipitation.

Cousinia pugionifera is typically found in mountainous and desert environments, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
