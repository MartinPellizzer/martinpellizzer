Cassinia berberifolia

Cassinia berberifolia, commonly known as the yellow corymbia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its clusters of yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

Cassinia berberifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
