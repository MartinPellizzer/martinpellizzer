Corunastylis clivicola

Corunastylis clivicola, commonly known as the cliff rock-daisy, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils along stream banks and cliffs. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and arranged in a loose cluster at the top of the stem.

It is adapted to thrive in sunny, dry environments and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
