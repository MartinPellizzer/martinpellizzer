Caladium caracasanum

Caladium caracasanum, commonly known as the red-veined caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with deep red veins against a pale green background. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like peace lilies and calla lilies. Caladium caracasanum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant foliage.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for its ornamental value in gardens and indoor spaces.
