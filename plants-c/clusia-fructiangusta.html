Clusia fructiangusta

Clusia fructiangusta, commonly known as the fruit-bearing clusia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a evergreen shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange to red berries.

Clusia fructiangusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in humid, tropical conditions.
