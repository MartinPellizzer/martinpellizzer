Cineraria heldreichii

Cineraria heldreichii, commonly known as the Balkan cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features dark green, velvety leaves and produces showy blue or violet flowers. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.

Cineraria heldreichii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
