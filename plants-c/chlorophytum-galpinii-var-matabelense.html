Chlorophytum galpinii var. matabelense

Chlorophytum galpinii var. matabelense, commonly known as the Matabelense Spider Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with white or yellow variegation, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers on slender stalks, which are followed by delicate, grass-like seed heads. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its unique foliage and ease of care make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
