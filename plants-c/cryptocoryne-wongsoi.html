Cryptocoryne wongsoi

Cryptocoryne wongsoi, commonly known as the Wongsoi cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often displaying a striking green to bluish-green coloration. This plant is typically found in slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and lakes. It is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.

Cryptocoryne wongsoi is also known for its moderate growth rate and relatively low maintenance requirements.
