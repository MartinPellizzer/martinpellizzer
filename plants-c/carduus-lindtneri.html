Carduus lindtneri

Carduus lindtneri, commonly known as Lindtner's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the dandelion family, Asteraceae.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically found in dry, rocky habitats. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flowers that bloom in late summer. It grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has a robust root system.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
