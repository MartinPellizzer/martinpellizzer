Combretum kerengense

Combretum kerengense, commonly known as the kereng tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its rough, often fissured bark and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as riverbanks and forest edges.
