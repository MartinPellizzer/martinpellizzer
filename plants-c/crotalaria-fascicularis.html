Crotalaria fascicularis

Crotalaria fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. Crotalaria fascicularis is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in soil enrichment.
