Cypripedium × peetersianum

Cypripedium × peetersianum, commonly known as the Peeters' lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the mountainous regions of China and Vietnam.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a large, slipper-shaped pouch and a dark, velvety lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, often on rocky or humus-rich soil. Its flowers range in color from pale yellow to deep orange, and it blooms in late spring to early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
