Cymbopogon arundinaceus

Cymbopogon arundinaceus, commonly known as giant bamboo grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. It is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.

Its rapid growth rate makes it a useful plant for erosion control and ornamental purposes.
