Crassula subbifera

Crassula subbifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula subbifera is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
