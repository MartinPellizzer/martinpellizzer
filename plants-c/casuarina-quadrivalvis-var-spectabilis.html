Casuarina quadrivalvis var. spectabilis

Casuarina quadrivalvis var. spectabilis, commonly known as the spectacle casuarina, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its unique, needle-like foliage that forms dense, cylindrical clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, woody cones that mature to a reddish-brown color, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This species is valued for its hardiness and ability to stabilize soils in arid and coastal regions.
