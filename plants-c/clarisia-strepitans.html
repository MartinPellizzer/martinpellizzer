Clarisia strepitans

Clarisia strepitans, commonly known as the rainbow vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, yellow, and even pink. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bitter taste. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Clarisia strepitans thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its climbing growth habit.
