Calathea gigas

Calathea gigas, commonly known as the giant calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 feet in length, often with intricate patterns and vibrant coloration. The leaves are typically a deep green with contrasting white or silver veins, creating a visually appealing effect. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
