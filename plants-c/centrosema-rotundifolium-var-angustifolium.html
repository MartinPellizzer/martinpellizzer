Centrosema rotundifolium var. angustifolium

Centrosema rotundifolium var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, ovate leaflets and produces small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant is characterized by its robust growth and ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in agricultural settings. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
