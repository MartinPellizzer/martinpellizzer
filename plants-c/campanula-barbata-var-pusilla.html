Campanula barbata var. pusilla

Campanula barbata var. pusilla, commonly known as the small bellflower, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Europe.

It features delicate, bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has hairy, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
