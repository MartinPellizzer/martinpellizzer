Cosmos diversifolius var. atrosanguineus

Cosmos diversifolius var. atrosanguineus, commonly known as the dark red cosmos, is a striking perennial plant native to the southern United States.

It features showy, semi-double pink to reddish-pink flowers that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has feathery, fern-like leaves that are finely divided and give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and its ability to attract pollinators.
