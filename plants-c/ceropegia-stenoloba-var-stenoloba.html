Ceropegia stenoloba var. stenoloba

Ceropegia stenoloba var. stenoloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often white or pale pink, with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers bright, indirect light.

This species is popular among succulent enthusiasts due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
