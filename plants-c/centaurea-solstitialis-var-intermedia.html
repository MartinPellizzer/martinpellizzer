Centaurea solstitialis var. intermedia

Centaurea solstitialis var. intermedia, commonly known as the California poppy, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in open fields and along roadsides. It is an annual herb that produces numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Its botanical features make it a common weed in agricultural and natural areas.
