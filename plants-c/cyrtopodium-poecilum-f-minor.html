Cyrtopodium poecilum f. minor

Cyrtopodium poecilum f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf cyrtopodium, is a compact orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of flowers, which bloom during the warmer months.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and ease of care.
