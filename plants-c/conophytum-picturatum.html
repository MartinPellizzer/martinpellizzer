Conophytum picturatum

Conophytum picturatum, commonly known as the "Picture Leaf Plant," is a succulent native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that store water. The leaves are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red, and feature intricate patterns that resemble drawings or pictures. It produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer.

This plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
