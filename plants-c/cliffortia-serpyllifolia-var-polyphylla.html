Cliffortia serpyllifolia var. polyphylla

Cliffortia serpyllifolia var. polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved sandthrift, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit and dense clusters of small, white flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it is adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various small organisms.
