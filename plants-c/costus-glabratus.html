Costus glabratus

Costus glabratus, commonly known as the smooth costus, is a tropical flowering plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Costus glabratus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
