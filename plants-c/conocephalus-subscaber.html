Conocephalus subscaber

Conocephalus subscaber, commonly known as the rough conocephalus, is a species of grass native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture, contributing to its common name.

Conocephalus subscaber is generally low-maintenance and can thrive in a variety of soil types with adequate sunlight.
