Cheirostylis pusilla var. simplex

Cheirostylis pusilla var. simplex, commonly known as the small-leafed orchid, is a miniature orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its compact size and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and rare species in the orchid family.
