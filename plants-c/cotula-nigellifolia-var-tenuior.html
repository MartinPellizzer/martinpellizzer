Cotula nigellifolia var. tenuior

Cotula nigellifolia var. tenuior, commonly known as narrowleaf groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It is often found in coastal regions and is adapted to arid or semi-arid conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
