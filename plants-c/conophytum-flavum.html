Conophytum flavum

Conophytum flavum, commonly known as the yellow conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, compact leaves that store water. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
