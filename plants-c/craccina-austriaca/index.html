Craccina Austriaca

Craccina austriaca is a small, annual flowering plant native to central and eastern Europe. It typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is often overlooked due to its modest appearance but plays a role in local ecosystems. Despite its simplicity, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
