Cryptocoryne wendtii var. jahnelii

Cryptocoryne wendtii var. jahnelii, commonly known as the Java crypts, is a popular aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slight metallic sheen. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is well-suited for aquariums with soft to moderately hard water. It is known for its ease of care and ability to grow rapidly, making it a favorite among aquarists.

The varietal form jahnelii is distinguished by its slightly broader leaves and a more compact growth habit compared to the standard Cryptocoryne wendtii.
