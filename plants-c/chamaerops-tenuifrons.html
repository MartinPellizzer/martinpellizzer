Chamaerops tenuifrons

Chamaerops tenuifrons, commonly known as the slender fan palm, is a species of palm native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its narrow, fan-shaped leaves that are composed of numerous closely spaced leaflets. The plant typically grows as a solitary trunk with a short stem, and its foliage is often dense and lush. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to the mild, temperate climate of its native region.

The slender fan palm is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
