Cucumis melo subvar. agrestis

Cucumis melo subvar. agrestis, commonly known as the wild melon or wild cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a perennial vine that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is generally small, with a spiny exterior and a sweet, aromatic flesh.

This subspecies is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of edible seeds.
