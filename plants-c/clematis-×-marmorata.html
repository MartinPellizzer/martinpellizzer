Clematis × marmorata

Clematis × marmorata, commonly known as the marble clematis, is a hybrid shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from white to pink and purple, often with a marbled pattern. The plant produces trifoliate leaves with a serrated margin and has a climbing growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Clematis × marmorata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
