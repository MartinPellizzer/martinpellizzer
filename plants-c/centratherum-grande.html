Centratherum grande

Centratherum grande, commonly known as the large centipede grass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the Poaceae family. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color and a rough texture.

Centratherum grande is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
