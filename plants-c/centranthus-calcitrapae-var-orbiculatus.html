Centranthus calcitrapae var. orbiculatus

Centranthus calcitrapae var. orbiculatus, commonly known as the orbiculate centranthus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with dense, rounded foliage. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters at the ends of the branches. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical features make it an attractive choice for gardens seeking drought-resistant and low-maintenance flora.
