Croton lindheimerianus var. lindheimerianus

Croton lindheimerianus var. lindheimerianus, commonly known as Lindheimer's croton, is a tropical plant native to the southern United States.

It features striking, colorful foliage with leaves that can range from green to red, orange, or yellow, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor spaces.
