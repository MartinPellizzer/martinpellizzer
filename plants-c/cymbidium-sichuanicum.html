Cymbidium sichuanicum

Cymbidium sichuanicum, commonly known as the Sichuan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China, particularly the Sichuan province.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, leafy stems that bear clusters of fragrant, lance-shaped flowers. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, producing showy, often pink or white flowers with a prominent lip. It prefers cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas at moderate elevations.

Cymbidium sichuanicum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
