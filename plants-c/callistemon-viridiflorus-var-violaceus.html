Callistemon viridiflorus var. violaceus

Callistemon viridiflorus var. violaceus, commonly known as the purple bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, crimson to purple flowers that resemble bottlebrushes, which bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, needle-like leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract birds and bees due to its nectar-rich flowers.
