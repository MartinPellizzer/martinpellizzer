Crudia cynometroides

Crudia cynometroides, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by small, round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The silverleaf tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
