Coreopsis abyssinica

Coreopsis abyssinica, commonly known as Abyssinian coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Coreopsis abyssinica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
