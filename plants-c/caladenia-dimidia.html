Caladenia dimidia

Caladenia dimidia, commonly known as the pink spider-orchid, is a terrestrial orchid species native to southeastern Australia.

It features a single, slender leaf and produces a spike of up to 15 pale pink to cream-colored flowers. The flowers have a distinctive, narrow, spider-like appearance with a hooded labellum. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils in open woodlands and heathlands.

Caladenia dimidia is known for its delicate beauty and is often found in coastal regions of New South Wales and Victoria.
