Cnestis corniculata

Cnestis corniculata, commonly known as the horned morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped calyx that gives it its common name. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Cnestis corniculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
