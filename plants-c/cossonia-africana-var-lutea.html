Cossonia africana var. lutea

Cossonia africana var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow cossonia, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, covered in dense, narrow leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green color. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange inflorescences composed of numerous small, tubular flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in rocky or arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and seasonal floral display.
