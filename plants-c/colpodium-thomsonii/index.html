Colpodium Thomsonii

Colpodium thomsonii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It thrives in high-altitude environments where few other species can survive. Its small, bell-shaped flowers bloom in late spring, adding a subtle beauty to the rugged landscape. The plant is known for its unique adaptations to cold and arid conditions. Despite its resilience, it remains a threatened species due to habitat loss and climate change.
