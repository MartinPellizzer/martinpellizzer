Centaurea balcanica

Centaurea balcanica, commonly known as the Balkan centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It produces bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Centaurea balcanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
