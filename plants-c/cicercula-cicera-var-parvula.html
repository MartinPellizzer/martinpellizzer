Cicercula cicera var. parvula

Cicercula cicera var. parvula, commonly known as the dwarf vetch, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the legume family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces tiny, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its compact size and adaptability, it is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
