Colymbada macedonica

Colymbada macedonica, commonly known as the Macedonian watercress, is a rare aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of northern Greece.

The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
