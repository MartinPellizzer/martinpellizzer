Cuphea curiosa var. oresbia

Cuphea curiosa var. oresbia, commonly known as the Red Hot Poker Plant, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, tubular red bracts that resemble poker chips, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding contrast to its vibrant foliage. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements make it a favorite among gardeners seeking visual interest.
