Colobachne brachystachyus

Colobachne brachystachyus, commonly known as narrow-stemmed grass, is a species of grass native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and often have a rough texture.

Botanically, it is classified within the Poaceae family, which includes many economically important grasses.
