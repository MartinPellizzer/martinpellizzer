Crassula retrorsa

Crassula retrorsa, commonly known as the "Good Luck Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are often bluish-green in color, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
