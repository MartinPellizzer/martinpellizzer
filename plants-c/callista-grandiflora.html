Callista grandiflora

Callista grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant features upright, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Callista grandiflora is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
