Cyrtandra × carinata

Cyrtandra × carinata, commonly known as the ridged Cyrtandra, is a hybrid species within the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive ridged or grooved stems, which give it its common name. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color.

Cyrtandra × carinata is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments.
