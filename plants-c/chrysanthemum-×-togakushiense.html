Chrysanthemum × togakushiense

Chrysanthemum × togakushiense, commonly known as the Togakushi chrysanthemum, is a hybrid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and red. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Due to its horticultural significance, it is also valued for its use in traditional Japanese floral arrangements.
