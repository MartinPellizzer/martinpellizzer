Calathea polystachya

Calathea polystachya, commonly known as the flame calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage with intricate patterns of green, red, and yellow. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often adorned with contrasting veins that create a visually appealing effect. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid environments.
