Cistus chinamadensis subsp. chinamadensis

Cistus chinamadensis subsp.

chinamadensis, commonly known as the Chinese rock rose, is a shrub native to China. It is characterized by its woody stems and aromatic, hairy leaves.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
