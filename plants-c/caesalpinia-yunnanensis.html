Caesalpinia yunnanensis

Caesalpinia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cockspur, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive, bright red or orange floral spikes. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers and unique foliage.
