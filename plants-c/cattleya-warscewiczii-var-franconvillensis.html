Cattleya warscewiczii var. franconvillensis

Cattleya warscewiczii var. franconvillensis, commonly known as the Franconvillensis Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, violet-blue flowers with a contrasting white lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical forests. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and floral fragrance.
