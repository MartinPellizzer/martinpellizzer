Claytonia nitida

Claytonia nitida, commonly known as the slender springbeauty, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of only a few inches. The plant features hairy, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to cool, temperate climates.
