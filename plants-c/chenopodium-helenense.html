Chenopodium helenense

Chenopodium helenense, commonly known as Helen's goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in dry, rocky soils. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with a rough, sandpapery texture. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often found in disturbed areas and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.
