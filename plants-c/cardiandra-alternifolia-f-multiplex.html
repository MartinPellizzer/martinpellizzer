Cardiandra alternifolia f. multiplex

Cardiandra alternifolia f. multiplex, commonly known as the variegated heartleaf.

This plant is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that exhibit a variegated pattern of green and white. It is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions, often cultivated for its ornamental value. The leaves are alternate, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
