Coprosmanthus pseudochinus

Coprosmanthus pseudochinus, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a shrub native to eastern Asia.

It features fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy flowers. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Coprosmanthus pseudochinus is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
