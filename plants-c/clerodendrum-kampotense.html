Clerodendrum kampotense

Clerodendrum kampotense, commonly known as the Kampot flame tree, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and can grow as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.

The flowers are not only visually appealing but also attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
