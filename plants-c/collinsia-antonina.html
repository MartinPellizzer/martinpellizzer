Collinsia antonina

Collinsia antonina, commonly known as the scarlet fairy bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a striking red throat. The plant typically grows to a height of 6 to 12 inches, with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Collinsia antonina is noted for its early spring blooming period and its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
