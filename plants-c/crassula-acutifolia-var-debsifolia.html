Crassula acutifolia var. debsifolia

Crassula acutifolia var. debsifolia, commonly known as the Debsifolia Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance and ability to thrive in arid conditions make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
