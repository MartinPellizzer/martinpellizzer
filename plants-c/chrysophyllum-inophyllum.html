Chrysophyllum inophyllum

Chrysophyllum inophyllum, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in traditional medicine.
