Cymbidium aestivum

Cymbidium aestivum, commonly known as the summer orchid, is a species of orchid native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in the summer months. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Cymbidium aestivum is a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and indoor cultivation due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
