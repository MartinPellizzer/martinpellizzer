Croton pleytei

Croton pleytei, commonly known as the Pleyte's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that often display a mix of green, red, and yellow hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
