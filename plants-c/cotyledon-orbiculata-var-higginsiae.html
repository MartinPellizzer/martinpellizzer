Cotyledon orbiculata var. higginsiae

Cotyledon orbiculata var. higginsiae, commonly known as the Higgins' Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins, especially when exposed to sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among succulent enthusiasts for its compact growth and ornamental appeal.
