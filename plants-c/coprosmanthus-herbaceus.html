Coprosmanthus herbaceus

Coprosmanthus herbaceus, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in late summer. These flowers are followed by bright red, lantern-shaped fruits that persist into winter.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
