Cyperus acutus

Cyperus acutus, commonly known as spiny flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often surrounded by bracts. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

Cyperus acutus is often found in marshy habitats and can thrive in both freshwater and brackish environments.
