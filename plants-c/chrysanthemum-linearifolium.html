Chrysanthemum linearifolium

Chrysanthemum linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. This species belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
