Clethra spicigera

Clethra spicigera, commonly known as the slender clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the plant family Clethraceae and is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Clethra spicigera is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
