Combretum puberum

Combretum puberum, commonly known as the velvetwood or African mahogany, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a pubescent underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.
