Clidemia foliosa

Clidemia foliosa, commonly known as Hawaiian false mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

Clidemia foliosa is also considered an invasive species in some regions outside Hawaii due to its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.
