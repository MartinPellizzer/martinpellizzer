Cyrtochilum quinqueseriale

Cyrtochilum quinqueseriale, commonly known as the five-lobed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Cyrtochilum quinqueseriale thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
