Cryptostylis subulata

Cryptostylis subulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, delicate flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. It produces pale green or yellowish flowers with a distinctive, intricate structure.

Cryptostylis subulata is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique appearance.
