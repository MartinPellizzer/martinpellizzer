Chenopodium capillare

Chenopodium capillare, commonly known as slender goosefoot, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist soils along stream banks and wet meadows. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a bluish-green hue. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces tiny, triangular seeds.

It is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a weed in agricultural settings.
