Camerunia bouquetii

Camerunia bouquetii, commonly known as the Cameroon flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds. Camerunia bouquetii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in forest edges and riverine areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical gardens.
