Carduus defloratus var. zetterstedtianus

Carduus defloratus var. zetterstedtianus, commonly known as the Zetterstedt's thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It has a robust root system and can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
