Clinogyne azurea

Clinogyne azurea, commonly known as the blue-eyed daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Clinogyne azurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color.
