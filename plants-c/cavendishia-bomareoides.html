Cavendishia bomareoides

Cavendishia bomareoides, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its segmented, often branching stems that bear clusters of bright flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant blooms in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. Its leaves are usually modified into spines, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with well-draining soil.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
