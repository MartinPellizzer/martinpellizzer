Cistus × ingwersenii

Cistus × ingwersenii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of evergreen shrubs characterized by their aromatic foliage and showy flowers. The plant produces tubular, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.

Cistus × ingwersenii is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
