Cupressus gansuensis

Cupressus gansuensis, commonly known as the Gansu cypress, is a coniferous tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Cupressaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and scale-like leaves. The tree typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to cold and dry climates. It produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
