Capsicum ceratocalyx

Capsicum ceratocalyx, commonly known as the horned pepper, is a species native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous plant with a compact growth habit and hairy stems. The leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with serrated margins, and the flowers are typically yellow or green. The fruit is a small, round or oval capsule that turns red when mature.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
