Cattleya colnagoi

Cattleya colnagoi, commonly known as the Colnago's Cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive white lip. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It blooms once a year, usually in the summer months.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
