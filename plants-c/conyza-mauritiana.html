Conyza mauritiana

Conyza mauritiana, commonly known as goatweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides. Its small, white to yellow flowers are arranged in clusters, and it produces numerous lightweight seeds that are easily dispersed by wind.

Conyza mauritiana is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
