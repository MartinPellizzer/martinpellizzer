Carex egglestonii

Carex egglestonii, commonly known as the Eggleston sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, grasses, and rushes. This plant typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of greenish-yellow flowers.
