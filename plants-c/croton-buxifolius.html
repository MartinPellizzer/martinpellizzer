Croton buxifolius

Croton buxifolius, commonly known as the boxleaf croton, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are oval and resemble those of boxwood, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.

Croton buxifolius prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas or indoor cultivation.
