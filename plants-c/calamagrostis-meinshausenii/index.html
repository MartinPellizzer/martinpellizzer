Calamagrostis Meinshausenii

Calamagrostis meinshausenii is a striking ornamental grass known for its unique silver-blue foliage and feathery plumes. Native to central and eastern europe, this plant thrives in moist to wet soils and partial shade. its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice for garden borders and water features. it is also valued for its ability to attract birds and beneficial insects. with its hardy nature and low maintenance requirements, calamagrostis meinshausenii is a versatile addition to any landscape.
