Combretum fischeri

Combretum fischeri, commonly known as Fischer's flame bush, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lythraceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
