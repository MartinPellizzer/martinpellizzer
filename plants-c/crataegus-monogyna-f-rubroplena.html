Crataegus monogyna f. rubroplena

Crataegus monogyna f. rubroplena, commonly known as the red-flowered hawthorn, is a compact shrub native to Europe.

It is characterized by its showy, double pink to red flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant features thorny branches and elliptical leaves with serrated edges. Its fruit is a small, red pome that matures in late summer.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
