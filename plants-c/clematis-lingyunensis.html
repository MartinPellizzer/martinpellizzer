Clematis lingyunensis

Clematis lingyunensis, commonly known as the Lingyun Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue blossoms that appear in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are heart-shaped and serrated.

Clematis lingyunensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
