Catasetum caatingense f. flavovirens

Catasetum caatingense f. flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green Catasetum, is a rare orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically yellow-green in color, with intricate patterns and a distinct lip that aids in pollination.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to flourish.
