Calamagrostis leiophylla

Calamagrostis leiophylla, commonly known as the smooth feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-green blades and fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils, often found in wetlands, marshes, and along streambanks. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping in water features and bog gardens.

Calamagrostis leiophylla plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
