Cynoglossum longiflorum

Cynoglossum longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered mouse-ear, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features hairy, spoon-shaped leaves and produces clusters of violet or blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
