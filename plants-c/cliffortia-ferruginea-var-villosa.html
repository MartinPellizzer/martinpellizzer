Cliffortia ferruginea var. villosa

Cliffortia ferruginea var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often appearing in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its varietal name "villosa.". It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
