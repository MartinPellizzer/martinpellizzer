Cerasus yunnanensis

Cerasus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan cherry, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in early spring, followed by edible red berries. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated edge.

Cerasus yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
