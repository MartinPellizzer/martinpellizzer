Calanthe ventilabrum

Calanthe ventilabrum, commonly known as the ventilated calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a unique, ventilated lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe ventilabrum is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is often cultivated for its decorative flowers.
