Cryptanthus odoratissimus

Cryptanthus odoratissimus, commonly known as the sweet-scented ground orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features dense, rosette-like clusters of fleshy, grass-like leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value and unique scent, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare plants.
