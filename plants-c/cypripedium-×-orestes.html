Cypripedium × orestes

Cypripedium × orestes, commonly known as the showy lady's tresses, is a hybrid orchid native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, gracefully arching inflorescence bearing numerous fragrant, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a dense rosette at the base.

Cypripedium × orestes is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
