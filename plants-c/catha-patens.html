Catha patens

Catha patens, commonly known as the African spurge, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant has smooth, often hairy stems and alternate, ovate to lance-shaped leaves. It is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

Catha patens is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional practices.
