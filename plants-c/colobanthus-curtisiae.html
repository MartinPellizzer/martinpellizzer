Colobanthus curtisiae

Colobanthus curtisiae, commonly known as the Antarctic hairseeds, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of the Southern Hemisphere.

It is a small, mat-forming plant that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky soils. The plant produces small, white flowers and has a unique ability to survive in harsh, low-nutrient environments. Its leaves are hairy and succulent, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.
