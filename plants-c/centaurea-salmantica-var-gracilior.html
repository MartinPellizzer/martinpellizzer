Centaurea salmantica var. gracilior

Centaurea salmantica var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender Spanish thistle, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and blue to purple flower heads that appear in summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces distinctive bracts beneath its flowers. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
