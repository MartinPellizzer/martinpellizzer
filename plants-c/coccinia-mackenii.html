Coccinia mackenii

Coccinia mackenii, commonly known as the African butterfruit, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and melons, and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow-orange flowers. Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agroforestry systems.
