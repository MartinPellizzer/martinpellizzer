Cotula alba

Cotula alba, commonly known as the white daisy or African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, gray-green leaves and is often found in open, sunny habitats. Cotula alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.

It is adapted to arid conditions and can thrive in well-drained soils.
