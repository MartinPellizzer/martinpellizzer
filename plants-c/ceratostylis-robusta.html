Ceratostylis robusta

Ceratostylis robusta, commonly known as the robust lady's-tongue, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Ceratostylis robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
