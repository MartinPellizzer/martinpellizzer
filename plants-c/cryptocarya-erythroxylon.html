Cryptocarya erythroxylon

Cryptocarya erythroxylon, commonly known as the redwood cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and is often found in lowland rainforests.
