Ceropegia fimbriata subsp. geniculata

Ceropegia fimbriata subsp.

geniculata, commonly known as the African violet or velvet leaf, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, cordate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
