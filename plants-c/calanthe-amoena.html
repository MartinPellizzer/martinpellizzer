Calanthe amoena

Calanthe amoena, commonly known as the elegant calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rhizome and its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Calanthe amoena is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated for its beauty in horticultural settings.
