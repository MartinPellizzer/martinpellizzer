Cotoneaster marquandii

Cotoneaster marquandii, commonly known as the Marquand's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster marquandii is often used in gardens for its hardiness and seasonal interest.
