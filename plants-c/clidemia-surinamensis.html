Clidemia surinamensis

Clidemia surinamensis, commonly known as Surinam nettle, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.
