Campanopsis ramulosa

Campanopsis ramulosa, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, pale blue to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has hairy stems and leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Campanopsis ramulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate, fragrant blooms.
