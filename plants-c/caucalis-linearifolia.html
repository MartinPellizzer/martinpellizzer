Caucalis linearifolia

Caucalis linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leafed bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Caucalis linearifolia is often used in rock gardens and as a companion plant due to its ornamental value and hardy nature.
