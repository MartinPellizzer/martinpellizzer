Chenopodium acuminatum f. ovatum

Chenopodium acuminatum f. ovatum, commonly known as the broadleaf goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to the western United States and thrives in disturbed soils and arid environments.

This variety is often used as a weed in agricultural settings due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
