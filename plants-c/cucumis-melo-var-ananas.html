Cucumis melo var. ananas

Cucumis melo var. ananas, commonly known as the pineapple melon, is a variety of melon that features a distinctive, spiky, pineapple-like exterior.

Its skin is typically a pale yellow to greenish hue, with a rough, textured surface. The fruit has a sweet, aromatic flavor with a subtle tang, reminiscent of both melon and pineapple. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its unique appearance and flavor, making it a popular choice in both home gardens and commercial agriculture.
