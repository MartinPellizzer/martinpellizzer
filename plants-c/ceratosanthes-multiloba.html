Ceratosanthes multiloba

Ceratosanthes multiloba, commonly known as the purpleheart tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has a woody stem and produces heart-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
