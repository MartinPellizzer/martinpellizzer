Clematis zeylanica

Clematis zeylanica, commonly known as the Sri Lankan clematis, is a climbing shrub native to South and Southeast Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet, often with a distinctive red or purple center. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing along forest edges or in moist, shaded areas.

Clematis zeylanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beautiful blooms.
