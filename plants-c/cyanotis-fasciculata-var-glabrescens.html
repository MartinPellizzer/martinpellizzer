Cyanotis fasciculata var. glabrescens

Cyanotis fasciculata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth bluebell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and clusters of bell-shaped blue or violet flowers. The varietal name "glabrescens" refers to its smooth, hairless appearance compared to other varieties.
