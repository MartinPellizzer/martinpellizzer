Chlorophytum pleiophyllum

Chlorophytum pleiophyllum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that often form a rosette shape, and it produces small, white or greenish flowers on slender stalks. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its air-purifying qualities and ease of care have contributed to its widespread cultivation and appreciation.
