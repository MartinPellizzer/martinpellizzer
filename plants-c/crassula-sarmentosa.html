Crassula sarmentosa

Crassula sarmentosa, commonly known as the stringy crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
