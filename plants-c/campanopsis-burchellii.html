Campanopsis burchellii

Campanopsis burchellii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has a fibrous root system and grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
