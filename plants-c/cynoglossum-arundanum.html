Cynoglossum arundanum

Cynoglossum arundanum, commonly known as the heartworts, is a flowering plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue or violet flowers in summer. Its common name refers to the heart-shaped leaves, which are a distinctive feature of the species.
