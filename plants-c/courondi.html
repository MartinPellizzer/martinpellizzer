Courondi

Courondi, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has feathery, opposite leaves and produces small, winged fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Courondi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
