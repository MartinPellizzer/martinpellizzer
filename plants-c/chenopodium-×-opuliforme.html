Chenopodium × opuliforme

Chenopodium × opuliforme, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine, prickly hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is native to North America and thrives in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields.

Chenopodium × opuliforme is an annual herb that is often considered a weed due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
