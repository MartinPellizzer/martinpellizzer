Campanula luzhijiangensis

Campanula luzhijiangensis, commonly known as the Luzhijiang bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, while its stems are upright and branching.

Campanula luzhijiangensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
