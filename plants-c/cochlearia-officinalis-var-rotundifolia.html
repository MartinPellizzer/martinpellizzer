Cochlearia officinalis var. rotundifolia

Cochlearia officinalis var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaved scurvygrass, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant is characterized by its rosette of rounded, fleshy leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, coastal environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
