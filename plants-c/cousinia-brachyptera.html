Cousinia brachyptera

Cousinia brachyptera, commonly known as the short-winged cudweed, is a flowering plant native to the arid and semi-arid regions of central Asia.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of gray-green, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers in early spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cousinia brachyptera is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
