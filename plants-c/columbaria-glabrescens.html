Columbaria glabrescens

Columbaria glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth columbaria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant features alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months.

The species is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in sandy or loamy soils.
