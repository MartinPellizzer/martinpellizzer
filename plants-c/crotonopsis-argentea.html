Crotonopsis argentea

Crotonopsis argentea, commonly known as silver croton.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silvery hair. It produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

Crotonopsis argentea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
