Clitoria macrophylla var. sericea

Clitoria macrophylla var. sericea, commonly known as the velvet bean or silk-flowered bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers, which are often butterfly-shaped and arranged in clusters. The leaves are large, heart-shaped, and covered with a fine, silky hair that gives the plant its common name. It is a legume, meaning it belongs to the Fabaceae family, and produces pods containing edible seeds.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
