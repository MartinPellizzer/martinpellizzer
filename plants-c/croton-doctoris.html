Croton doctoris

Croton doctoris, commonly known as the doctor's croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by unique, spiky fruit structures. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Croton doctoris thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
