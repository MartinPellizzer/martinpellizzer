Calathea lindenii

Calathea lindenii, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its uniquely patterned, waxy leaves that display a combination of deep green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves are often described as having a velvety texture, which enhances its visual appeal.

Calathea lindenii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.
