Chaetostoma castratum

Chaetostoma castratum, commonly known as the red-tailed cichlid, is a species of freshwater fish native to Lake Malawi in East Africa.

It belongs to the family Cichlidae and is characterized by its striking coloration, featuring a deep red tail and a vibrant blue body. The plant-like appearance of its fins and body gives it a distinctive, almost floral aesthetic. It is a popular ornamental fish in aquariums due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.

This species is known for its territorial behavior and is often found in rocky habitats within its native environment.
