Cosmiza multifida

Cosmiza multifida, commonly known as the featherleaf cosmos, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant features feathery, finely divided leaves that give it its common name, and it produces vibrant purple or pink blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Cosmiza multifida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
