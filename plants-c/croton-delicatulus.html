Croton delicatulus

Croton delicatulus, commonly known as the delicate croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage, which often displays shades of green, yellow, and red. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its unique floral structures and milky sap. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
