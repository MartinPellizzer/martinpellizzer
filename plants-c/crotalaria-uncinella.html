Crotalaria uncinella

Crotalaria uncinella, commonly known as the hooked crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Crotalaria uncinella is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats, including fields and roadsides.
