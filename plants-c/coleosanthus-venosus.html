Coleosanthus venosus

Coleosanthus venosus, commonly known as the veined spurge.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant features a succulent, branched stem with a distinctive network of veins on its surface. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
