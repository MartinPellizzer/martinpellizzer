Cerasus Serrulata F. Shidaresakura

The Cerasus serrulata f. shidaresakura is a rare and exquisite ornamental cherry tree known for its delicate, cascading pink blossoms. Native to Japan, this variety is prized for its unique floral display that resembles a waterfall of petals. Its foliage provides a striking contrast to the blooms, offering a lush green backdrop during the growing season. The tree is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its beauty and seasonal charm. Despite its popularity, it remains a relatively uncommon sight in many parts of the world.
