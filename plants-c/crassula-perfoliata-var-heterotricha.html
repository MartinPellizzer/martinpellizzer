Crassula perfoliata var. heterotricha

Crassula perfoliata var. heterotricha, commonly known as the "Lace Leaf Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, crinkled, and often variegated foliage, which can range from green to yellow and pink hues. The plant forms a dense, compact rosette with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the warmer months.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
