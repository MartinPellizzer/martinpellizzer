Cryptandra nutans var. micrantha

Cryptandra nutans var. micrantha, commonly known as the slender cryptandra, is a shrub native to Western Australia.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is adapted to sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland heathland environments.
