Cirsium × sextenum

Cirsium × sextenum, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its robust growth habit. The plant typically reaches heights of 1 to 3 meters, with a woody stem that becomes more pronounced with age. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs and have a serrated edge, while the flowers are arranged in a dense, spherical cluster.

Cirsium × sextenum is native to Europe and is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.
