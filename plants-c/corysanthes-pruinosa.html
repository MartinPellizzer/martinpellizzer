Corysanthes pruinosa

Corysanthes pruinosa, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal heathlands and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance and habitat make it a distinctive species within the genus Corysanthes.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
