Chenopodium incisum f. pumilum

Chenopodium incisum f. pumilum, commonly known as dwarf lamb's quarters, is a herbaceous annual plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its small, smooth, oval leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in length. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along roadsides.
