Cryptocoryne × zukalii nothovar. sumateraensis

Cryptocoryne × zukalii nothovar. sumateraensis, commonly known as the Sumatran cryptocoryne, is a hybrid aquatic plant native to the freshwater habitats of Sumatra.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and requires stable water parameters to maintain its health. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize substrates effectively in aquariums.

This variety is prized for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability in planted tank environments.
