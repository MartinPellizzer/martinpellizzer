Combretum coursianum

Combretum coursianum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that turn yellow before falling off. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
