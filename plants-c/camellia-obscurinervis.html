Camellia obscurinervis

Camellia obscurinervis, commonly known as the dark-veined camellia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent, dark veins that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, camellia-like flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Camellia obscurinervis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
