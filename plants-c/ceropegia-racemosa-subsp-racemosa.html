Ceropegia racemosa subsp. racemosa

Ceropegia racemosa subsp.

racemosa, commonly known as the clustered milkweed or racemose ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a succulent perennial that produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple.

The plant has fleshy, trailing stems and leaves that are typically opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a warm, dry climate.
