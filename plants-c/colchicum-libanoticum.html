Colchicum libanoticum

Colchicum libanoticum, commonly known as the Lebanon colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features striking, nodding purple flowers that bloom in late autumn. The plant has a short, stout stem that supports a single, showy inflorescence. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and often withering before flowering.

Colchicum libanoticum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique blooming habit.
