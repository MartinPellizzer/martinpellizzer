Cryptocarya velloziana

Cryptocarya velloziana, commonly known as the Velloziana tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental and ecological importance in its native habitat.
