Convolvulus lanuginosus var. argenteus

Convolvulus lanuginosus var. argenteus, commonly known as silver hairbind, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
