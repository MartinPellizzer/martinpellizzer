Calyptranthes ishoaquinicca

Calyptranthes ishoaquinicca, commonly known as the Ecuadorian calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of Ecuador and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside.
