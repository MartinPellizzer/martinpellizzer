Crassula thorncroftii

Crassula thorncroftii, commonly known as the Thorncroft Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded growth with fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil, making it ideal for rock gardens or indoor cultivation.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
