Cytisus Hirsutus Var. Glaber

Cytisus hirsutus var. glaber is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and delicate foliage, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. This variant of the common broom is distinguished by its smoother leaves and more compact growth habit. It is often found in rocky and limestone areas where it plays a vital role in local ecosystems. Due to its beauty and ecological importance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
