Cochlidiosperma sibthorpioides

Cochlidiosperma sibthorpioides, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. Its latex sap is milky and can be toxic, which contributes to its common name.
