Croton klotzschii var. microphyllus

Croton klotzschii var. microphyllus, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of yellow-orange flowers, which are followed by small, spherical fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
