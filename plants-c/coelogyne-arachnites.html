Coelogyne arachnites

Coelogyne arachnites, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender petals and a distinctive, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers, which bloom in shades of white, yellow, and green, are highly prized for their ornamental value.

Coelogyne arachnites requires specific care, including bright indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
