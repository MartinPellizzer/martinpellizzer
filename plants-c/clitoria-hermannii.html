Clitoria hermannii

Clitoria hermannii, commonly known as the Hermann's pea or the butterfly pea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a climbing legume with slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces vibrant blue or purple pea-like flowers that are highly ornamental. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown for its aesthetic value in gardens.

Clitoria hermannii is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
