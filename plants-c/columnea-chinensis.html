Columnea chinensis

Columnea chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame vine, is a striking evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces showy, pendulous inflorescences that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Columnea chinensis thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
