Clematis simsii var. sargentii

Clematis simsii var. sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive violet or purple hue. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.

This variety is valued for its beauty and adaptability in temperate climates.
