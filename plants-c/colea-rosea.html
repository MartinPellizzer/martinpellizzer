Colea rosea

Colea rosea, commonly known as the rose geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pink or red flowers. The plant has rounded, dark green leaves and is often grown for its ornamental value and aromatic properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.

Colea rosea is also valued for its use in perfumery and as a natural remedy in traditional medicine.
