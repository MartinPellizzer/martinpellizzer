Crassula hirtipes

Crassula hirtipes, commonly known as the woolly crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are typically green with a bluish tinge and are covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
