Calamagrostis Aniselytron

Calamagrostis aniselytron is a striking ornamental grass known for its feathery plumes and vibrant autumn color. Native to eastern North America, it thrives in wet or moist environments like marshes and stream sides. This plant is valued for its ability to add texture and movement to garden landscapes. Its tall, slender stems create a dramatic visual impact in both summer and fall. With minimal maintenance, it is a popular choice for water gardeners and landscape designers.
