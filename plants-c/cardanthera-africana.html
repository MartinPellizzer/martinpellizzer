Cardanthera africana

Cardanthera africana, commonly known as the African star flower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and narrow, linear leaves. Its most striking feature is the showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange.

Cardanthera africana is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
