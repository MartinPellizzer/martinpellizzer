Cereus candicans var. robustior

Cereus candicans var. robustior, commonly known as the robust night bloomer, is a cacti species native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stem and spiny, ribbed structure. The plant produces large, showy white flowers that open at night, emitting a sweet fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its striking appearance and nocturnal blooming habit, it is a popular choice for ornamental cultivation.
