Calochortus Weedii Var. Obispoensis

Calochortus weedii var. obispoensis is a rare and striking member of the lily family found primarily in the coastal regions of California. This delicate flower features showy, cup-shaped blooms that range in color from pale pink to deep red, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to the harsh, sunny conditions of its native habitat. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and is protected in many areas. Its unique beauty and ecological importance make it a valuable subject for conservation efforts and botanical study.
