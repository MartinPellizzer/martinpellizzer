Calliandra coccinea var. coccinea

Calliandra coccinea var. coccinea, commonly known as the red calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant floral display.

Calliandra coccinea var. coccinea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
