Canavalia nitida

Canavalia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing legume characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in the morning. Canavalia nitida is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun.

It is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.
