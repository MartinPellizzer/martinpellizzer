Caladium guttiferum

Caladium guttiferum, commonly known as the spotted arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display vibrant red, orange, and yellow patterns. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Caladium guttiferum is known for its ability to add color and texture to shaded landscapes.
