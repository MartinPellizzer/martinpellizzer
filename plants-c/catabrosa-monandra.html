Catabrosa monandra

Catabrosa monandra, commonly known as the sweetpea flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, pea-like flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous annual, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are trifoliate, and the flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their beauty and scent.

Catabrosa monandra thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.
