Cyperus karthikeyanii

Cyperus karthikeyanii, commonly known as the Karthikeyan's flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly India, where it thrives in wetland and marshy environments. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds and streams.

Cyperus karthikeyanii plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
