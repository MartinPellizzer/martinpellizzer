Carduus lecontei

Carduus lecontei, commonly known as Le Conte's thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the western United States and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pinkish flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with a rough, hairy texture, and the flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Carduus lecontei is often found in regions with well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
