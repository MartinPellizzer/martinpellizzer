Crotalaria willdenowiana subsp. glabrifoliolata

Crotalaria willdenowiana subsp.

glabrifoliolata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow, spurred flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically ovate to lanceolate, with a smooth texture and reduced pubescence. This subspecies is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats, thriving in tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
