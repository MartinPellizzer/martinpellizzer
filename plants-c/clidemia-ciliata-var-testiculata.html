Clidemia ciliata var. testiculata

Clidemia ciliata var. testiculata, commonly known as the hairy mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and typically grows on the branches of host trees such as mangroves and other tropical hardwoods. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Due to its parasitic nature, it can significantly impact the health of its host plants if left unchecked.
