Cotoneaster huahongdongensis

Cotoneaster huahongdongensis, commonly known as the Hua Hong Dong cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically the Hua Hong Dong region, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and persistent red berries. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Cotoneaster huahongdongensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its seasonal display of flowers and fruits.
