Clematis staintonii

Clematis staintonii, commonly known as the Stainton's clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
