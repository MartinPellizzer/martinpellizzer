Collinsonia ovalis

Collinsonia ovalis, commonly known as the narrowleaf bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Collinsonia ovalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
