Codiaeum picturatum

Codiaeum picturatum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a vibrant tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, yellow, and green, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
