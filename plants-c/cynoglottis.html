Cynoglottis

Cynoglottis, commonly known as cow's milk weed, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, often hairy leaves. The flowers are typically yellow or brown and are arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family. Cynoglottis species are native to various regions in Africa, Asia, and Australia.

They are often found in dry, open habitats and can be invasive in some areas.
