Carex gifuensis f. argyrostachys

Carex gifuensis f. argyrostachys, commonly known as the silver-stem sedge, is a varietal form of the Japanese sedge.

It is characterized by its silvery-white stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. This plant is typically found in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is native to Japan.

The plant's ornamental value lies in its distinctive coloration and graceful appearance.
