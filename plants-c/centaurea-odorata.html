Centaurea odorata

Centaurea odorata, commonly known as sweet centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, bristly leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea odorata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a long history of use in traditional medicine.
