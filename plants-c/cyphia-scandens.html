Cyphia scandens

Cyphia scandens, commonly known as the climbing cyphia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a vine-like shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy habitats. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Cyphia scandens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
