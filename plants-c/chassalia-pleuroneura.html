Chassalia pleuroneura

Chassalia pleuroneura, commonly known as the narrowleaf chassalia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. Its flowers are small and yellow, forming dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

Chassalia pleuroneura is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is adapted to the semi-arid conditions of its native habitat.
