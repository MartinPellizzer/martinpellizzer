Calophyllum undulatum

Calophyllum undulatum, commonly known as the zigzag tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, undulating leaves that give it its common name. The tree produces yellowish-orange flowers and red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their potential medicinal properties.

The plant is also valued for its wood, which is durable and used in construction and furniture-making.
