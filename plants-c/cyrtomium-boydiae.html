Cyrtomium boydiae

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, dense fronds that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. Cyrtomium boydiae is valued for its ornamental appearance and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
