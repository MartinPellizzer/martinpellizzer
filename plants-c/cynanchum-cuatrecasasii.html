Cynanchum cuatrecasasii

Cynanchum cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas' milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which is known for its milkweed species, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by follicles containing toxic seeds.

It is adapted to grow in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
