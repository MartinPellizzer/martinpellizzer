Christensenia aesculifolia

Christensenia aesculifolia, commonly known as the silver fern, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, silvery-white, fern-like foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are compound, with a unique, feathery structure that helps it thrive in moist, temperate environments.

Christensenia aesculifolia is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance.
