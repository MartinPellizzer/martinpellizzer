Coelogyne bigibbosa var. bigibbosa

Coelogyne bigibbosa var. bigibbosa, commonly known as the giant coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 cm in diameter. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its inflorescence consists of multiple racemes, each bearing several fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers.

The leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its arid environment.
