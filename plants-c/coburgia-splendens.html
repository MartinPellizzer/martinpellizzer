Coburgia splendens

Coburgia splendens, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the genus Coburgia, which is part of the family Cactaceae. This species is characterized by its segmented, leaf-like stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in winter. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom reliably under proper care.
