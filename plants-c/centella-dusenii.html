Centella dusenii

Centella dusenii, commonly known as the Himalayan pennywort, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, round leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional medicine.
