Casearia completa

Casearia completa, commonly known as the Brazilian beautyberry, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Salicaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and ovate leaves. The plant produces small, clustered, white flowers that are followed by bright blue, fleshy berries. These berries are an important food source for various birds and mammals.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
