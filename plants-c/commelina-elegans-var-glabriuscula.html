Commelina elegans var. glabriuscula

Commelina elegans var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the velvetleaf morning glory, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive blue-violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features a short, thick rhizome and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. Its foliage is often covered in fine, short hairs, giving it a velvety texture.
