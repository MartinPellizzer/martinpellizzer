Campyloneurum fallax

Campyloneurum fallax, commonly known as the false button fern, is a species of epiphytic fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, rosette-like growth habit. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, humus-rich soil to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
