Chrysosplenium nudicaule var. intermedium

Chrysosplenium nudicaule var. intermedium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Asia.

It features small, rounded leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters in diameter, with a dense covering of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of delicate, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or rocky crevices.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and garden cultivation in suitable climates.
