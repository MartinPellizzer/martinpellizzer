Calceolaria prostrata

Calceolaria prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate slipper orchid, is a low-growing perennial plant native to South America.

It features delicate, tubular flowers that resemble small slippers, typically in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest floors.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
