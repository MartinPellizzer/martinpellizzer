Clypeola eriophora

Clypeola eriophora, commonly known as the woolly clypeola, is a small, perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland or shrubland habitats. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Clypeola eriophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
