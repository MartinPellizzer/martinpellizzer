Calytrix purpurea

Calytrix purpurea, commonly known as the purple calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Calytrix purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
