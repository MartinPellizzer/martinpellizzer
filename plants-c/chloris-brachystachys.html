Chloris brachystachys

Chloris brachystachys, commonly known as slender wheatgrass, is a small annual grass native to Australia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of tiny, spikelet-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas. It is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in restoration ecology.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in arid environments.
