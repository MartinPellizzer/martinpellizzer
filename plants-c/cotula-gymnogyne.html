Cotula gymnogyne

Cotula gymnogyne, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and yellow. The leaves are typically narrow and hairy, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Cotula gymnogyne is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
