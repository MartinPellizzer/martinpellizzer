Chloris verticillata var. aristulata

Chloris verticillata var. aristulata, commonly known as the silver grass or silverhair, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silvery hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a pale blue-green coloration. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in late winter to early spring.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening for its ornate foliage and drought tolerance.
