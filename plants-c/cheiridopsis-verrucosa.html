Cheiridopsis verrucosa

Cheiridopsis verrucosa, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features dense, hairy foliage that helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
