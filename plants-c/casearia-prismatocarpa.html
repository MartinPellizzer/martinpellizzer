Casearia prismatocarpa

Casearia prismatocarpa, commonly known as the purple casearia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange to red berries. Its bark is smooth and may have a reddish hue, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Casearia prismatocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
