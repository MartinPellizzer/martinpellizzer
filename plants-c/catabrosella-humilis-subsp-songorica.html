Catabrosella humilis subsp. songorica

Catabrosella humilis subsp.

songorica, commonly known as the Songorica sandmat, is a low-growing annual plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its prostrate habit and hairy, lance-shaped leaves.
