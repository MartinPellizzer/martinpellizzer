Clematis × triternata f. rubromarginata

Clematis × triternata f. rubromarginata, commonly known as the red-margin Clematis, is a compact, vigorous climbing plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, with distinctive red margins on the petals. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are typically trifoliate, giving it a unique foliage appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
