Chaetocyperus trichodes

Chaetocyperus trichodes, commonly known as the hairy flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowering structures. It typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats such as marshes and wetlands.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
