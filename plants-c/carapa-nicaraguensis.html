Carapa nicaraguensis

Carapa nicaraguensis, commonly known as the Nicaraguan mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Meliaceae family, which includes other valuable hardwood species. The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and has a straight trunk with a smooth, greyish-brown bark. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a leathery texture.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by woody capsules containing elliptical seeds.
