Cortaderia boliviensis

Cortaderia boliviensis, commonly known as the Bolivian feather reed, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces inflorescences that are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant, flowing form. It thrives in moist environments and is often found near water bodies.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
