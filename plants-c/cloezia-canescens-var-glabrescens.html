Cloezia canescens var. glabrescens

Cloezia canescens var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth cloezia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, short hairs that become smoother as the plant matures. The leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and have a slightly hairy surface. The flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in loose inflorescences.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.
