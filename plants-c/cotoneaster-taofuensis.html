Cotoneaster taofuensis

Cotoneaster taofuensis, commonly known as the Taofu cotoneaster, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rosaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Sichuan province, where it grows in mountainous regions. The plant is characterized by its small, red berries that are typically consumed by birds, aiding in seed dispersal. It has small, ovate leaves and produces clusters of white flowers in the spring.

Cotoneaster taofuensis is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
