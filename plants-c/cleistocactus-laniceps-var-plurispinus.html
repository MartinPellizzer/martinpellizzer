Cleistocactus laniceps var. plurispinus

Cleistocactus laniceps var. plurispinus, commonly known as the spiny cholla, is a species of cactus native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are densely covered with spines, which can be either straight or curved. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It is a succulent plant, capable of storing water in its stems to survive in arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
