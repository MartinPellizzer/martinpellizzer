Corytoplectus speciosus var. speciosus

Corytoplectus speciosus var. speciosus, commonly known as the colorful sea cucumber, is a species of holothurian found in marine environments.

It belongs to the family Holothuriidae and is characterized by its vibrant, often iridescent skin. The plant exhibits a unique morphology with a soft, cylindrical body and a network of tube feet for movement and feeding. It thrives in tropical and subtropical waters, often inhabiting coral reefs and rocky substrates.

Its ecological role includes contributing to nutrient cycling and serving as prey for various marine organisms.
