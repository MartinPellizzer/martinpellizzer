Chamaecrista Caribaea Var. Inaguensis

Chamaecrista caribaea var. inaguensis is a rare flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands. It belongs to the legume family and is known for its distinctive yellow blooms. This variant is particularly found on the island of Inagua in the Bahamas. The plant thrives in coastal sandy soils and is adapted to salty air. Its unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
