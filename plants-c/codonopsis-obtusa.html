Codonopsis obtusa

Codonopsis obtusa, commonly known as the blunt codonopsis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Campanulaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it grows in moist, woodland environments. The plant is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.
