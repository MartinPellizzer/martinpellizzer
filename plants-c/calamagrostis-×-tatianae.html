Calamagrostis × tatianae

Calamagrostis × tatianae, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a hybrid grass species native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage that adds a soft, ornamental texture to gardens. The plant produces tall, slender culms with feathery plumes that bloom in late summer, creating a dramatic visual effect. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often used in wetland restoration and landscape design.

Calamagrostis × tatianae is a hardy perennial that prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively low maintenance once established.
