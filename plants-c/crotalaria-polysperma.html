Crotalaria polysperma

Crotalaria polysperma, commonly known as the many-seeded cowpea or the devil's bean, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers and has palmately lobed leaves. Its seed pods are hairy and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Crotalaria polysperma is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain areas.
