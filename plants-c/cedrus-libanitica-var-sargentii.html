Cedrus libanitica var. sargentii

Cedrus libanitica var. sargentii, commonly known as the Sargent's cedar, is a variety of the Lebanon cedar.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the eastern Mediterranean region. The plant is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and bluish-green foliage. Its cones are small and woody, and the tree produces aromatic wood that is highly valued.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental appeal and resilience in various climates.
