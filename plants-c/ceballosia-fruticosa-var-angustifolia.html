Ceballosia fruticosa var. angustifolia

Ceballosia fruticosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ceballosia, is a shrub native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, angular leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.

The species is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
