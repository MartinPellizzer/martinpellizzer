Carex damiaoshanensis

Carex damiaoshanensis, commonly known as the Damiaoshan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the mountainous regions of Hunan and Guizhou provinces. This perennial herbaceous plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences.

The plant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
