Cineraria ngwenyensis

Cineraria ngwenyensis, commonly known as the Ngwenyeni cineraria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically features silvery-gray foliage and produces showy blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria ngwenyensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
