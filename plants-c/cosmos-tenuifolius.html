Cosmos tenuifolius

Cosmos tenuifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf cosmos, is an annual flowering plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are narrow and finely divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, which bloom throughout the summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking low-maintenance ornamental plants.

Cosmos tenuifolius is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as butterflies and bees.
