Cranichis tubulosa

Cranichis tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
