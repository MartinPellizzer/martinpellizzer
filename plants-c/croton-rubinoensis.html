Croton rubinoensis

Croton rubinoensis, commonly known as the Red Croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Its leaves are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant appearance.

Croton rubinoensis thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
