Croton tsiampiensis var. ankaranensis

Croton tsiampiensis var. ankaranensis, commonly known as the Ankaranensis croton, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and sometimes lobed, with a rough texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

It thrives in tropical climates and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.
