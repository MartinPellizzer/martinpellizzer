Convolvulus dillenii

Convolvulus dillenii, commonly known as the Dillen's bindweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other flowering plants with similar climbing or trailing habits.

Convolvulus dillenii is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
