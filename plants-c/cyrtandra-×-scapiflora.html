Cyrtandra × scapiflora

Cyrtandra × scapiflora, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant in the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra pedata and Cyrtandra scapiflora, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant typically features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a distinctive corona. It is native to tropical regions of the Pacific, including Hawaii and parts of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
