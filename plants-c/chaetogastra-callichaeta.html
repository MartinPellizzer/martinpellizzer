Chaetogastra callichaeta

Chaetogastra callichaeta, commonly known as the hairy-leaved chaetogastra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its hairy foliage and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are densely covered in fine hairs.
