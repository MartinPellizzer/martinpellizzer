Cissus ochracea

Cissus ochracea, commonly known as the pale leaf fig, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically pale green with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade.

Cissus ochracea is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
