Carduus pyrochros

Carduus pyrochros, commonly known as the flame thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa, typically growing in dry, rocky soils. The plant features spiny leaves and produces bright orange to red flowers, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its name "pyrochros" refers to its fiery red coloration.

The species is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
