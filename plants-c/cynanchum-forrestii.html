Cynanchum forrestii

Cynanchum forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruits are follicles that release seeds with silky hairs.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
