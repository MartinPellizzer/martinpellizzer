Cratoxylum polyanthum

Cratoxylum polyanthum, commonly known as the many-flowered cratoxylum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in lowland tropical forests. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers, which are often arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
