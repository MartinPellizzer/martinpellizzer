Conophytum noisabisense

Conophytum noisabisense, commonly known as the "Succulent Stone" or "Cushion Stone," is a member of the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves.

Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing a unique adaptation to extreme drought conditions. Its leaves are typically thick and rounded, forming a compact, cushion-like structure that minimizes water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.

Conophytum noisabisense is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and striking appearance.
