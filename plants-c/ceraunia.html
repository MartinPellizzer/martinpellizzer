Ceraunia

Ceraunia, commonly known as the rain tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the legume family, Fabaceae.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences. Ceraunia species are native to various parts of Africa, Asia, and Australia, and they thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
