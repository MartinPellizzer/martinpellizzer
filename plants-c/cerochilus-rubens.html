Cerochilus rubens

Cerochilus rubens, commonly known as the red-horned orchid, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single or few flowers at the top. Its flowers are characterized by their deep red color and distinctive, spurred shape.

Cerochilus rubens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with appropriate humidity and light conditions.
