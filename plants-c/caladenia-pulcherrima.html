Caladenia pulcherrima

Caladenia pulcherrima, commonly known as the Splendid Spider-Orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, spider-like appearance. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers with intricate, folded petals and sepals. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats.

Caladenia pulcherrima is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and requires well-drained conditions.
