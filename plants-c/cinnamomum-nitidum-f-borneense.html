Cinnamomum nitidum f. borneense

Cinnamomum nitidum f. borneense, commonly known as Bornean cinnamon or Bornean camphor laurel, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the islands of Borneo.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue, waxy berries. Its aromatic bark and leaves contain essential oils, making it valuable in traditional medicine and perfumery.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and hill forest areas.
