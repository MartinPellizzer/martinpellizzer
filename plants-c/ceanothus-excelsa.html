Ceanothus excelsa

Ceanothus excelsa, commonly known as the tall ceanothus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. Ceanothus excelsa is well adapted to rocky soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
