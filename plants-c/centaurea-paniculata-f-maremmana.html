Centaurea paniculata f. maremmana

Centaurea paniculata f. maremmana, commonly known as the Maremma thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces showy, purple to pink composite flower heads that bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This subspecies is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
