Croton regnellianus

Croton regnellianus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored leaves that display a variety of shades and patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Croton regnellianus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
