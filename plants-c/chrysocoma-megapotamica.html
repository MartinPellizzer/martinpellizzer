Chrysocoma megapotamica

Chrysocoma megapotamica, commonly known as the yellow-flowered chrysocoma, is a shrub native to the highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and aromatic leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is typically found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil. Its botanical features include small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow blooms.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
