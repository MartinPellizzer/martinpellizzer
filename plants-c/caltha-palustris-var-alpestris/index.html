Caltha Palustris Var. Alpestris

Caltha palustris var. alpestris is a rare and delicate plant native to high altitude regions. It is a variant of the marsh marigold known for its striking yellow flowers. This plant thrives in moist, rocky soils and is often found in alpine meadows. Its beauty makes it a sought-after species among botanists and gardeners. However, it is highly sensitive to environmental changes and requires careful conservation.
