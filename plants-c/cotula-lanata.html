Cotula lanata

Cotula lanata, commonly known as woolly cotula, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the warmer months. Cotula lanata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
