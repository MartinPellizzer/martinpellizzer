Cerastium boissieri var. gibraltaricum

Cerastium boissieri var. gibraltaricum, commonly known as Gibraltar mouse-ear, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring.
