Callista acerosa

Callista acerosa, commonly known as the hard-leaved callista, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruits are small, round, and turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.
