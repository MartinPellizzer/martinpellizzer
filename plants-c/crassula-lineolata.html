Crassula lineolata

Crassula lineolata, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Money Plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula lineolata is a popular indoor plant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
