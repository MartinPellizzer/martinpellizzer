Commelina elata

Commelina elata, commonly known as the slender spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the family Commelinaceae, which includes many species of grass-like plants. The plant features slender, erect stems with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, blue or purple flowers are borne in clusters and are surrounded by three bracts.
