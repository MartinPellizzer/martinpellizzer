Coleus Bishopianus

Coleus bishopianus is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, colorful foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments. This species belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its leaves come in a wide range of hues, including red, purple, and green, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to light and moisture to flourish.
