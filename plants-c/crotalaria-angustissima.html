Crotalaria angustissima

Crotalaria angustissima, commonly known as the narrowleaf cowpea or slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. It has a taproot system and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria angustissima is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant in agricultural settings.
