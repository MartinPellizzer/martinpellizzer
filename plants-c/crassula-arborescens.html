Crassula arborescens

Crassula arborescens, commonly known as the tree crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a branching, tree-like structure with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green or reddish in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Crassula arborescens is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
