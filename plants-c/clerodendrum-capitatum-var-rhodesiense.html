Clerodendrum capitatum var. rhodesiense

Clerodendrum capitatum var. rhodesiense, commonly known as the Rhodesian hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
