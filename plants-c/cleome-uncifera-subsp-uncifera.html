Cleome uncifera subsp. uncifera

Cleome uncifera subsp.

uncifera, commonly known as the narrowleaf cleome, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States. It is a member of the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow flowers.

The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable.
