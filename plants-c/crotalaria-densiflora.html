Crotalaria densiflora

Crotalaria densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its erect, branching stems and dense clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces elongated pods containing several seeds.

Crotalaria densiflora is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.
