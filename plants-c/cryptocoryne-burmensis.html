Cryptocoryne burmensis

Cryptocoryne burmensis, commonly known as the Burmese cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and bright green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. It is popular among aquarists for its hardiness and ability to oxygenate water.

Cryptocoryne burmensis is also valued for its role in natural water filtration and habitat creation for aquatic life.
