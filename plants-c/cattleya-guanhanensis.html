Cattleya guanhanensis

Cattleya guanhanensis, commonly known as the Guanhan orchid, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to orange with intricate patterns. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cattleya guanhanensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for floral arrangements.
