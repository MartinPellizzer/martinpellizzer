Crambe macrocarpa

Crambe macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant crambe, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are used for oil extraction. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and economically valuable.
