Centaurea vulgaris var. jacea

Centaurea vulgaris var. jacea, commonly known as common centaury, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial species that typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats. The plant features bright yellow to orange-red flowers arranged in dense, composite-like inflorescences. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered with fine hairs.

It is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
