Crateva linearis

Crateva linearis, commonly known as the arrowhead plant or Indian arrowhead, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Crateva linearis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
