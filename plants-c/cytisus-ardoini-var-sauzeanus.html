Cytisus ardoini var. sauzeanus

Cytisus ardoini var. sauzeanus, commonly known as the Sauzean broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, often branched stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are small, trifoliate, and have a fern-like appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature in temperate climates.
