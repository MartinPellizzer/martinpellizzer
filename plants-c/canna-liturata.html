Canna liturata

Canna liturata, commonly known as the painted canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, colorful leaves that often feature intricate patterns of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces showy, spathaceous flowers in shades of orange, yellow, and red, which bloom in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Canna liturata is often used in gardens for its vibrant foliage and long-lasting floral displays.
