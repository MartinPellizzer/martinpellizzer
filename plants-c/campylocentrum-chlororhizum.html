Campylocentrum chlororhizum

Campylocentrum chlororhizum, commonly known as the yellow root orchid, is a tropical orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique yellow rhizomes and striking orange-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, often with a waxy coating.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
