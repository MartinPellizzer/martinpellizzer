Camellia kissi var. confusa

Camellia kissi var. confusa, commonly known as the African camellia, is a species native to tropical Africa.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 meters. The plant produces showy, double pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its ornamental flowers.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a suitable choice for shaded garden spaces.
