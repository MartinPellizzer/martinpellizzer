Cynanchum sibiricum f. latifolium

Cynanchum sibiricum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved Siberian milkvetch, is a variant of the Siberian milkvetch species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to regions of Asia, including Siberia and parts of China. The plant features broad, lanceolate leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties and its role in local ecosystems.
