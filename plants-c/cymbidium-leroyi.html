Cymbidium leroyi

Cymbidium leroyi, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a species of orchid native to China, particularly found in the provinces of Yunnan, Sichuan, and Guizhou.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in mountainous regions with cool, moist climates. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red. It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.

Cymbidium leroyi is also appreciated in traditional Chinese culture for its symbolic meaning of elegance and prosperity.
