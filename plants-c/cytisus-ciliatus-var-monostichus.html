Cytisus ciliatus var. monostichus

Cytisus ciliatus var. monostichus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, woody stems and is characterized by its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are narrow and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting surrounding plants.
