Campanula bellidifolia

Campanula bellidifolia, commonly known as the bellflower of the bellidifolia type, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula bellidifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in temperate climates.
