Cucubalus rotundifolius

Cucubalus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leafed soursop, is a tropical plant belonging to the family Annonaceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces a large, spiny fruit with a green, thick rind and a white, fibrous pulp. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, tropical climates.
