Carex × cryptochlaena

Carex × cryptochlaena, commonly known as the hybrid sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically brown or purple in color. It thrives in moist to wet environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Carex × cryptochlaena is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland restoration projects.
