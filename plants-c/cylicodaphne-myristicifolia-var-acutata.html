Cylicodaphne myristicifolia var. acutata

Cylicodaphne myristicifolia var. acutata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pandanus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and elongated, narrow leaves that are often stiff and pointed. The leaves are typically arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in spike-like inflorescences.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
