Chassalia divaricata

Chassalia divaricata, commonly known as the mountain chassalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features slender, branched stems and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of yellow to orange. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
