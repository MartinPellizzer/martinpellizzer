Columnea inaequilatera

Columnea inaequilatera, commonly known as the unequal-sided flame flower, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. Columnea inaequilatera is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
