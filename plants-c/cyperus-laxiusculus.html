Cyperus laxiusculus

Cyperus laxiusculus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded habitats, such as marshes and wetlands. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often wind-pollinated. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia.

Cyperus laxiusculus is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in both freshwater and brackish environments.
