Cupressus chengiana var. jiangensis

Cupressus chengiana var. jiangensis, commonly known as the Jiangxi cypress, is a rare and endemic coniferous species native to China.

It belongs to the cypress family (Cupressaceae) and is characterized by its dense, pyramidal shape and scale-like leaves. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions with well-drained soil and a temperate climate. Its cones are small and woody, containing seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical study and conservation efforts.
