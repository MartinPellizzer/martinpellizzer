Coleus kassneri

Coleus kassneri, commonly known as the Kassner's coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is closely related to other coleus species. Its foliage is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking visual appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
