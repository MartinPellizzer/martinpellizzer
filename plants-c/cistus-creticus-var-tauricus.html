Cistus creticus var. tauricus

Cistus creticus var. tauricus, commonly known as the Cretan rock rose, is a shrub native to the rocky soils of Crete.

It features small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has aromatic, evergreen leaves with a resinous scent. It is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Its hardy nature makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping in Mediterranean climates.
