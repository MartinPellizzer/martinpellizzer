Centaurea ruthenica var. lyrata

Centaurea ruthenica var. lyrata, commonly known as the lyrata centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny bracts, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are usually narrow and linear, with a rough texture.

It is often found in coastal areas and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.
