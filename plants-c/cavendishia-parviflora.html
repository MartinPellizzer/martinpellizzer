Cavendishia parviflora

Cavendishia parviflora, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are usually broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Cavendishia parviflora is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic value.
