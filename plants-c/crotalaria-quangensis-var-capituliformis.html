Crotalaria quangensis var. capituliformis

Crotalaria quangensis var. capituliformis, commonly known as the clustered crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed soils or along roadsides. Its seeds are encased in a hard pod, and it has a shallow root system that allows it to thrive in various soil conditions.

The species is also noted for its potential use in phytoremediation due to its ability to accumulate heavy metals.
