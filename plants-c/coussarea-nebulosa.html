Coussarea nebulosa

Coussarea nebulosa, commonly known as the misty coussarea, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland forests. Its leaves are usually elliptical with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

Coussarea nebulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
