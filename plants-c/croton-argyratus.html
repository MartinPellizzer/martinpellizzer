Croton argyratus

Croton argyratus, commonly known as silver croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with white and green patterns on its leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
