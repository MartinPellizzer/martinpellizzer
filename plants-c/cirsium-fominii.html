Cirsium fominii

Cirsium fominii, commonly known as the Fomin's thistle, is a perennial plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor conditions.

Cirsium fominii is often found in steppe and grassland habitats.
