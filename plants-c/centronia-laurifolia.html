Centronia laurifolia

Centronia laurifolia, commonly known as the glossy laurel.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant is evergreen and typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic plants like cinnamon and laurel.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
