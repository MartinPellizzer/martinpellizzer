Capsella bursa-pastoris grossotriangularis

Capsella bursa-pastoris grossotriangularis, commonly known as the triangular shepherd's purse, is a variant of the common shepherd's purse.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its distinctive triangular seed pods. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is native to Europe and parts of Asia. It features small white or yellow flowers and has a low, spreading growth habit.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological studies and horticultural practices.
