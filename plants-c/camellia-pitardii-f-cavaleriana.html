Camellia pitardii f. cavaleriana

Camellia pitardii f. cavaleriana, commonly known as the Cavaleriana camellia, is a rare and ornamental variant of the Camellia genus.

It is native to China and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant is a evergreen shrub with leathery, elliptical leaves that provide a striking contrast to its blooms. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation in temperate climates.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued by botanists and horticulturists alike.
