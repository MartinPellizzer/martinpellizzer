Cheirostylis jamesleungii

Cheirostylis jamesleungii, commonly known as the James Leung's orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid, shaded environments where it thrives.

This orchid is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists due to its ornamental value and limited natural distribution.
