Cyrtanthus obliquus

Cyrtanthus obliquus, commonly known as the red-hot cat's tail, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, tubular red flowers that are clustered in a dense, tail-like inflorescence, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Cyrtanthus obliquus is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
