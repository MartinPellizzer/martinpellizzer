Cerastium arvensiforme

Cerastium arvensiforme, commonly known as field chickweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming from spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, alternate leaves that are usually oval or lance-shaped. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, gardens, and along roadsides.

Its fibrous root system allows it to spread easily and colonize new areas.
