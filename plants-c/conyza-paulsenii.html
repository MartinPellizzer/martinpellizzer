Conyza paulsenii

Conyza paulsenii, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is an annual herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in a radiate head, giving it a star-like appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Conyza paulsenii is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through wind-dispersed seeds.
