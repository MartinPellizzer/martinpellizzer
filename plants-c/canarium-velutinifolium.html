Canarium velutinifolium

Canarium velutinifolium, commonly known as the velvet-leaf canarium, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, elliptical leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in panicle inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny fruits containing oily seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its aromatic wood and has traditional uses in local medicine and crafts.
