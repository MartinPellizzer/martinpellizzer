Cynanchum marnierianum

Cynanchum marnierianum, commonly known as Marnier's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweed species. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellowish-green with a distinctive corona, and it produces follicles that contain seeds with silky hairs.

Cynanchum marnierianum is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
