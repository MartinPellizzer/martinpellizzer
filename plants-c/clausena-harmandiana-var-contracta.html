Clausena harmandiana var. contracta

Clausena harmandiana var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved curry leaf tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often aromatic and used in traditional cooking. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value and culinary uses, it is cultivated in gardens and for local food applications.
