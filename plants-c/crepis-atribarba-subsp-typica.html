Crepis atribarba subsp. typica

Crepis atribarba subsp.

typica, commonly known as the typical bentgrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Asteraceae family. It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in late summer.
