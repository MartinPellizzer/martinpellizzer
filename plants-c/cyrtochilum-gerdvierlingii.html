Cyrtochilum gerdvierlingii

Cyrtochilum gerdvierlingii, commonly known as the Gerdvierling orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricately patterned flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate markings.

Cyrtochilum gerdvierlingii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
