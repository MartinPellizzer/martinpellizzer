Combretum majungense

Combretum majungense, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for dry and semi-arid environments.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
