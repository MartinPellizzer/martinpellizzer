Carduus × septentrionalis

Carduus × septentrionalis, commonly known as the northern thistle.

It is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny, lance-shaped leaves and purple to pinkish composite flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed soils and is found in regions of North America. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in harsh environments.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
