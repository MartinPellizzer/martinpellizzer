Chamaeranthemum venosum

Chamaeranthemum venosum, commonly known as the veiny chamaeranthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves with prominent venation, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers, which range in color from pink to red, attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
