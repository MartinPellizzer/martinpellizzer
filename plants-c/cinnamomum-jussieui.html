Cinnamomum jussieui

Cinnamomum jussieui, commonly known as the Jussieui cinnamon tree, is a species of evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown bark that resembles cinnamon. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface. Its essential oils, extracted from the bark and leaves, are used in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and its contribution to local biodiversity.
