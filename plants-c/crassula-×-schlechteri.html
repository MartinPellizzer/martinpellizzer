Crassula × schlechteri

Crassula × schlechteri, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It is a hybrid between Crassula ovata and Crassula multicava, which contributes to its adaptability and ornamental appeal.

Due to its low maintenance and ability to thrive in various conditions, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
