Coprosma reptans

Coprosma reptans, commonly known as creeping coprosma, is a low-growing shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy green on top and lighter in color underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. These flowers are followed by bright red or orange berries that are attractive to birds.

Coprosma reptans is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to form dense ground cover.
