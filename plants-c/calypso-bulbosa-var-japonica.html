Calypso bulbosa var. japonica

Calypso bulbosa var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese star orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. It has a short rhizome and produces a single flower per stem, which is usually white or pale pink.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
