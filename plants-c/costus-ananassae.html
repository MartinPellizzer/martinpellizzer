Costus ananassae

Costus ananassae, commonly known as pineapple costus, is a tropical plant belonging to the Costaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and long, narrow leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces striking inflorescences with bright yellow or orange flowers, which are clustered in a panicle shape. It is native to Central and South America and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
