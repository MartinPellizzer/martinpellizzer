Cardamine xinfenii

Cardamine xinfenii, commonly known as the Xinfen buttercup, is a rare and endangered species native to China.

It is a small herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or stream sides. It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family, which includes other well-known genera like Ranunculus and Anemone.

Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
