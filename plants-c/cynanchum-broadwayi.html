Cynanchum broadwayi

Cynanchum broadwayi, commonly known as Broadway's milkweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the United States.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their unique floral structures. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. Its milky sap, characteristic of the genus, contains toxic compounds that deter herbivores.

Cynanchum broadwayi typically grows in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky slopes.
