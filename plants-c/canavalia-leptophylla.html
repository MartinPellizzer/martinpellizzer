Canavalia leptophylla

Canavalia leptophylla, commonly known as the slender leaf cowpea, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features compound leaves with small, narrow leaflets and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural use. Its seeds are edible and can be used in various culinary applications.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.
