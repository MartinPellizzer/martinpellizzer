Croton pottsii

Croton pottsii, commonly known as the Potts' croton, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds to vibrant yellows and greens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
