Cyathea antillana

Cyathea antillana, commonly known as the Antillean tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its rhizome is thick and woody, supporting the upright structure of the plant.

Cyathea antillana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
