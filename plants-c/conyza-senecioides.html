Conyza senecioides

Conyza senecioides, commonly known as common ironweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in open habitats such as roadsides, fields, and disturbed soils, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lanceolate, hairy, and have a serrated margin, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in a dense cluster.

Conyza senecioides is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
