Campanula linifolia var. langsdorffiana

Campanula linifolia var. langsdorffiana, commonly known as the langsdorffiana bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its delicate appearance and long flowering period.
