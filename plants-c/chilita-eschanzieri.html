Chilita eschanzieri

Chilita eschanzieri, commonly known as the Schizachne grass, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
