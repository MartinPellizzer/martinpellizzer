Corybas stenotribonos

Corybas stenotribonos, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed helmet orchid, is a unique member of the orchid family native to New Zealand.

This terrestrial orchid is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flower that is typically greenish-yellow in color. It grows in wet, shaded environments, often in forest floors or along stream banks. The plant has a short, tuberous rootstock and produces a single, showy flower per stem.

Its specialized structure and habitat make it a rare and ecologically significant species.
