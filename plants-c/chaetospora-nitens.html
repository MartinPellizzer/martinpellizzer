Chaetospora nitens

Chaetospora nitens, commonly known as the glittering moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth form and bright green, glossy leaves that reflect light, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate regions on rocks or soil. Its structure consists of leafy shoots with small, clustered stems and is often found in humid, forested areas.

The reproductive structures, known as sorocarps, are borne on the upper surface of the leaves and contain the spores necessary for its propagation.
