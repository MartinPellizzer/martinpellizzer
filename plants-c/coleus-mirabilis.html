Coleus mirabilis

Coleus mirabilis, commonly known as the marvel plant, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, yellow, and purple. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically lavender or white in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage.

Coleus mirabilis is relatively easy to grow and can be propagated through stem cuttings.
