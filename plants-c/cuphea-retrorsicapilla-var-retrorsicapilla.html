Cuphea retrorsicapilla var. retrorsicapilla

Cuphea retrorsicapilla var. retrorsicapilla, commonly known as the Mexican heather or cuphea with red flowers, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Mexico.

It features dense clusters of tubular, bright red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has small, narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
