Clematis orientalis var. serrata

Clematis orientalis var. serrata, commonly known as the serrated oriental clematis, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and lavender, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
