Ceanothus × flexilis

Ceanothus × flexilis, commonly known as the coastal ceanothus, is a shrub native to coastal regions of California and southern Oregon.

It is characterized by its aromatic, blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has leathery, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for coastal climates.

Ceanothus × flexilis is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize coastal soils.
