Campanula barbata var. strictopedunculata

Campanula barbata var. strictopedunculata, commonly known as the narrow-stem bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue to violet. The leaves are lanceolate and often hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
