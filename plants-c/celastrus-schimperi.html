Celastrus schimperi

Celastrus schimperi, commonly known as the Ethiopian yellow wood, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
