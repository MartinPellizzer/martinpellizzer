Cryptocoryne balansae

Cryptocoryne balansae, commonly known as the Balansae cryptocroryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hardy, fast-growing plant that thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light levels. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically dark green with a slight sheen. It is often used in planted aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.

Cryptocoryne balansae is also valued for its resilience and ease of care, making it a popular choice among aquarium enthusiasts.
