Caladium rougieri

Caladium rougieri, commonly known as the Red Knight caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for garden displays and indoor decoration.

Caladium rougieri is typically propagated through tuberous roots, which can be planted in spring for optimal growth.
