Crepidium tenue

Crepidium tenue, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and are borne in a loose cluster at the top of the stem.

Crepidium tenue is a rare and endangered species, often found in specific, localized habitats.
