Camonea bambusetorum

Camonea bambusetorum, commonly known as the bamboo palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Arecaceae family, which includes palms and cycads. This plant is characterized by its slender, clustered stems and long, arching leaves that give it a graceful appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.
