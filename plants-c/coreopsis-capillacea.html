Coreopsis capillacea

Coreopsis capillacea, commonly known as slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers with a central disk. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads.
