Croton stenophyllus var. longifolius

Croton stenophyllus var. longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
