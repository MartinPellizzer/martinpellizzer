Convallaria majalis var. majuscula

Convallaria majalis var. majuscula, commonly known as the giant lily of the valley, is a variant of the lily of the valley plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of bell-shaped, white flowers. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and has a rhizomatous root system. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is native to parts of Europe.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in herbal medicine.
