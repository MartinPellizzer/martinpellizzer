Cyclosorus × Insularis

Cyclosorus × insularis is a rare and intriguing fern species that thrives in specific island environments. This hybrid plant combines traits from its parent species, creating a unique blend of characteristics. It is often found in moist, shaded areas where it forms dense, low-growing clusters. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a conservation concern. Its delicate fronds and unusual morphology make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
