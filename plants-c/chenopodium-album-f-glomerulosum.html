Chenopodium album f. glomerulosum

Chenopodium album f. glomerulosum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant features alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges and small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a dry, three-angled capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds.

This variety is characterized by its dense inflorescences and is often found in temperate regions across Europe, Asia, and North America.
