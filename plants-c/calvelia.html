Calvelia

Calvelia, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that resemble small stars. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous coating. Calvelia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth habit makes it suitable for use as a climbing plant or ground cover in tropical gardens.
