Cousinia korolkowi

Cousinia korolkowi, commonly known as the Korolkov's fleabane, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically silver-gray, covered in fine hairs that help reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Cousinia korolkowi is adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient species found in rocky and sandy soils.
