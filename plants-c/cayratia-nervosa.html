Cayratia nervosa

Cayratia nervosa, commonly known as the silver vine, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are small berries that turn red when mature.

Cayratia nervosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
