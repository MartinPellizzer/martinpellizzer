Clematis orientalis var. globosa

Clematis orientalis var. globosa, commonly known as the globe clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, globe-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that cluster in spherical inflorescences, giving it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
