Coelogyne louisiana

Coelogyne louisiana, commonly known as the Louisiana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a rhizomatous growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often with a red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated for its beauty and resilience in tropical gardens.
