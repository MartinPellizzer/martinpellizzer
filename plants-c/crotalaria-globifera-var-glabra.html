Crotalaria globifera var. glabra

Crotalaria globifera var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth globeflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its spherical, yellowish seed pods that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in sandy or coastal soils. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
