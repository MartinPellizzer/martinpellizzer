Codonopsis elliptica

Codonopsis elliptica, commonly known as the oval ginseng, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its medicinal properties. Its roots are used in traditional Chinese medicine to treat fatigue and stimulate energy.
