Cnicus firmus

Cnicus firmus, commonly known as the common sandmat, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial plant that typically forms dense mats on the ground, often found in sandy or disturbed soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs and have a slightly rough texture.

Cnicus firmus is adapted to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species in challenging environments.
