Corallodendron holosericeum

Corallodendron holosericeum, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive coral-like inflorescences, which consist of numerous small, clustered flowers. The plant is evergreen, with narrow, leathery leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems. It produces yellow or orange flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to different soil types.
