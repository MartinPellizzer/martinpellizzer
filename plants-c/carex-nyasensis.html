Carex nyasensis

Carex nyasensis, commonly known as the Nyasae sedge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to East Africa, particularly Tanzania and Malawi, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of streams.

Due to its ecological importance, it plays a role in maintaining soil stability and supporting local biodiversity.
