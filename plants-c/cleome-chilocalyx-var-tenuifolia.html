Cleome chilocalyx var. tenuifolia

Cleome chilocalyx var. tenuifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and showy, yellow-petaled flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This variety is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
