Coccothrinax Clarensis Subsp. Perrigida

Coccothrinax clarensis subsp. perrigida is a rare palm species native to the Caribbean. It is characterized by its slender trunk and distinctive, clustered leaves. This subspecies thrives in coastal areas and is often found in limestone soils. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its unique features make it an important subject for botanical study and ecological preservation.
