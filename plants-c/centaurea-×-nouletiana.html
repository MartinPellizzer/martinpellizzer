Centaurea × nouletiana

Centaurea × nouletiana, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
