Carex hainanensis

Carex hainanensis, commonly known as Hainan sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China, specifically the island of Hainan, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has grass-like foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are characteristic of the sedge family.

Carex hainanensis is often found in wetlands, along stream banks, and in forest understories.
