Clerodendrum serratum var. velutinum

Clerodendrum serratum var. velutinum, commonly known as the velvet leaf clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous tiny seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
