Chlorophytum rubromarginatum

Chlorophytum rubromarginatum, commonly known as the red-edged spider plant, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its upright, strap-like leaves that are typically dark green with a striking red or pink margin. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers on slender stems during the summer months. It is a hardy and drought-tolerant species, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and ease of care.
