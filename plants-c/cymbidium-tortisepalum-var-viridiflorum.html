Cymbidium tortisepalum var. viridiflorum

Cymbidium tortisepalum var. viridiflorum, commonly known as the green-flowered orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous inflorescences with bright green flowers that are highly prized in horticulture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is particularly valued for its unique coloration and elegant appearance in both garden and indoor settings.
