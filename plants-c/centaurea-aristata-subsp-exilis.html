Centaurea aristata subsp. exilis

Centaurea aristata subsp.

exilis, commonly known as the slender sicklethistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.

The plant features spiny leaves and bright yellow to orange flowers that bloom in summer. It has a compact growth habit with a basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that rises above them.

The subspecies exilis is distinguished by its smaller size and more slender stems compared to the typical form of Centaurea aristata.
