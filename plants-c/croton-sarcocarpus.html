Croton sarcocarpus

Croton sarcocarpus, commonly known as the scarlet croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored leaves that range from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red berries. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its vibrant appearance.

However, it is important to note that the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
