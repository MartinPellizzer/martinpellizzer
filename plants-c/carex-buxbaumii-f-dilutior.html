Carex buxbaumii f. dilutior

Carex buxbaumii f. dilutior, commonly known as the Buxbaum's sedge.

This plant is a variant of Carex buxbaumii, characterized by its distinctive foliage and growth habit. It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Central America. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetland or riparian habitats.
