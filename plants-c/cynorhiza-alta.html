Cynorhiza alta

Cynorhiza alta, commonly known as the high root plant, is a perennial herbaceous species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a robust, fibrous root system that plays a crucial role in nutrient absorption and soil stabilization. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often displaying shades of white or pale yellow.

Cynorhiza alta is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies for its anti-inflammatory and antiseptic qualities.
