Campanula hoffmeisteri

Campanula hoffmeisteri, commonly known as the Hoffmeister's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Campanula hoffmeisteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
