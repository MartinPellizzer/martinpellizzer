Centrolobium yavizanum

Centrolobium yavizanum, commonly known as the Yavizanum tree, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and often have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody pods containing several seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
