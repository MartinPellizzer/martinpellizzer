Ceropegia pillansii

Ceropegia pillansii, commonly known as the Pillans' ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are often small and opposite, contributing to its compact, shrub-like appearance.

Ceropegia pillansii is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and ability to tolerate bright, indirect light.
