Coleus grandifolius

Coleus grandifolius, commonly known as the Java shield, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep reds and purples to bright yellows and oranges. The leaves are typically broad and shield-shaped, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its striking appearance and is popular in both gardens and as a houseplant.
