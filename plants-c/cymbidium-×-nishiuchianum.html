Cymbidium × nishiuchianum

Cymbidium × nishiuchianum, commonly known as the Nishiuchi orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, upright growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pendulous, clustered inflorescences with lance-shaped petals and a distinctive, recurved lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its elegance and adaptability to cultivation.
