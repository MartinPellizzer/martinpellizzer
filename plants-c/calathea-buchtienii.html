Calathea buchtienii

Calathea buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtienii Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaf patterns that change with the time of day, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with intricate veining and a waxy surface that enhances its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil.

Calathea buchtienii is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its aesthetic beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
