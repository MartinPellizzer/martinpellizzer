Croton williamsii

Croton williamsii, commonly known as the Williams' croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that often display a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in panicles. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and may have serrated edges.

Croton williamsii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
