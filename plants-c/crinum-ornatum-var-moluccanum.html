Crinum ornatum var. moluccanum

Crinum ornatum var. moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan Crinum, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant produces strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
