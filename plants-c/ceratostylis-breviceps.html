Ceratostylis breviceps

Ceratostylis breviceps, commonly known as the short-headed horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and often have a distinctive horn-like spur.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
