Carex atrata var. pseudocilicica

Carex atrata var. pseudocilicica, commonly known as the dark sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikes of small, usually brownish flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in wetland ecosystems.
