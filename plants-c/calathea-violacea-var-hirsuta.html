Calathea violacea var. hirsuta

Calathea violacea var. hirsuta, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with deep green veins and a rich purple underside. The leaves are broad and oval, often featuring intricate patterns that change with light exposure. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in indoor gardening for its unique and dramatic appearance.
