Calcearia holttumii

Calcearia holttumii, commonly known as the silverleaf sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, silver-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, gray-green foliage that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Calcearia holttumii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
