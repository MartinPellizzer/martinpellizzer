Catalpa heterophylla

Catalpa heterophylla, commonly known as the Chinese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain sticky, gelatinous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens.

Its botanical features make it easily distinguishable from other catalpa species.
