Cyrtochilum sharoniae

Cyrtochilum sharoniae, commonly known as Sharon's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the humid forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with fleshy, dark green leaves that are adapted to thrive in shaded environments. Cyrtochilum sharoniae is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is known for its unique floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
