Chlorophytum cyperaceum

Chlorophytum cyperaceum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a popular ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is a monocot with grass-like leaves that are typically long, narrow, and slightly curly. The plant produces small, grass-like inflorescences that bear white or greenish flowers, followed by small, white berries. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of propagation through its offsets.

This hardy plant is well-suited for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
