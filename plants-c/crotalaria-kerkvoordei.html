Crotalaria kerkvoordei

Crotalaria kerkvoordei, commonly known as the Kerkvoorde's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its legume pods and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven leaflets and produces showy yellow or orange flowers. It is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Crotalaria kerkvoordei is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting pollinators.
