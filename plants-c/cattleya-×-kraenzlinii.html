Cattleya × kraenzlinii

Cattleya × kraenzlinii, commonly known as the Kraenzlinii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species prized for its vibrant, fragrant blooms.

It originates from the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features long, arching stems with large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It is a sympodial orchid, meaning it grows in a horizontal manner with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Due to its striking appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
