Callerya tenasserimensis

Callerya tenasserimensis, commonly known as the Assam flame of the mountain, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to the forests of northeastern India and is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract various pollinators such as bees and birds.
