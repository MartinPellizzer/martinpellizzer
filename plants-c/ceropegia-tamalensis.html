Ceropegia tamalensis

Ceropegia tamalensis, commonly known as the Tamalite or the Ghost Plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems. Due to its unique appearance and growth form, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and rockeries.
