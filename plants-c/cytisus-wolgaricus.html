Cytisus wolgaricus

Cytisus wolgaricus, commonly known as the Wolgaricus broom, is a flowering shrub native to the British Isles.

It features slender, woody stems that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are small, alternate, and trifoliate, giving it a delicate appearance.

Cytisus wolgaricus thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and heathlands.
