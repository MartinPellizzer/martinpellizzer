Chrysophyllum krugii

Chrysophyllum krugii, commonly known as the African star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to western and central Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and edible fruits, which are used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
