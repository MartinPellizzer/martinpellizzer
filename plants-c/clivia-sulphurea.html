Clivia sulphurea

Clivia sulphurea, commonly known as the yellow clivia, is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family and is native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are broad and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-draining soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Clivia sulphurea is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
