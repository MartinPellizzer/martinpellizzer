Cynoglossum creticum var. pictum

Cynoglossum creticum var. pictum, commonly known as the painted bellflower, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, often with intricate patterns that resemble painted designs. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to both wild and cultivated landscapes.
