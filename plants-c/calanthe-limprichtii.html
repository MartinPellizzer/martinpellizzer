Calanthe limprichtii

Calanthe limprichtii, commonly known as the Limpricht's calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen rhizome and its striking, showy inflorescence. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers during the spring and early summer. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and arranged in a rosette pattern around the rhizome.

Calanthe limprichtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
