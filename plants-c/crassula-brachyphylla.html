Crassula brachyphylla

Crassula brachyphylla, commonly known as the compact crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula brachyphylla is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
