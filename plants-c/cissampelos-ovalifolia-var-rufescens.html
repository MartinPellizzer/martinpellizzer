Cissampelos ovalifolia var. rufescens

Cissampelos ovalifolia var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. Its stems are characterized by a reddish-brown coloration, which gives it its common name.

It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
