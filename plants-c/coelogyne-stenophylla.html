Coelogyne stenophylla

Coelogyne stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically has linear, narrow leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Coelogyne stenophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
