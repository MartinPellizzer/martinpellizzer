Cavendishia guascensis

Cavendishia guascensis, commonly known as the Andean orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in high-altitude regions with cool, humid climates. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces clusters of fragrant, colorful blooms.

Cavendishia guascensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
