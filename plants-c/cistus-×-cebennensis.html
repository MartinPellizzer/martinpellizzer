Cistus × cebennensis

Cistus × cebennensis, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Iberian Peninsula and southern France.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes many species of evergreen shrubs found in Mediterranean climates. The plant is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom in spring. It is well adapted to rocky and well-drained soils, making it a hardy plant suitable for xeriscaping.

Its essential oils, derived from its leaves and flowers, are used in perfumery and traditional medicine.
