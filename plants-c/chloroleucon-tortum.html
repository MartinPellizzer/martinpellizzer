Chloroleucon tortum

Chloroleucon tortum, commonly known as the twisted chloroleucon, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to the Mediterranean climate.
