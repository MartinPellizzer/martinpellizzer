Cynara ferox

Cynara ferox, commonly known as the Spanish thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in scrublands and coastal areas.

Cynara ferox is known for its hardiness and ability to withstand drought conditions.
