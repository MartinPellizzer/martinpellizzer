Crassula globularioides subsp. illichiana

Crassula globularioides subsp.

illichiana, commonly known as the Illich's crassula, is a compact, succulent shrub native to South Africa. It features dense clusters of fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically green with a bluish or reddish tinge under stress.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

This subspecies is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in succulent gardens.
