Crotalaria occulta

Crotalaria occulta, commonly known as the hidden crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows as an annual or perennial herb, often found in disturbed soils and open habitats. Its seed pods are pod-like, containing several seeds, and it has a shallow root system.

Crotalaria occulta is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
