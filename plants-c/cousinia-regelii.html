Cousinia regelii

Cousinia regelii, commonly known as the Regel's cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Cousinia regelii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
