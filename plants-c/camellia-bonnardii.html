Camellia bonnardii

Camellia bonnardii, commonly known as the Bonnard camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Camellia bonnardii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its winter blooming period.
