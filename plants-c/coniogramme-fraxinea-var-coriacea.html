Coniogramme fraxinea var. coriacea

Coniogramme fraxinea var. coriacea, commonly known as the rough-leaf coniogramme, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. Its stems are woody and can become quite long as it climbs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
