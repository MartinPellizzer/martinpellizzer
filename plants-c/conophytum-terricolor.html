Conophytum terricolor

Conophytum terricolor, commonly known as the rainbow stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
