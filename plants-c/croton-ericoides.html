Croton ericoides

Croton ericoides, commonly known as the dwarf croton, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, leathery leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of green, yellow, and red. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its growth habit is bushy, with a tendency to form dense foliage.

Croton ericoides thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
