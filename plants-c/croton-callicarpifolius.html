Croton callicarpifolius

Croton callicarpifolius, commonly known as the red-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that ranges from deep green to bright red and yellow. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its fruit is a capsule that turns red when mature, containing several seeds.

Croton callicarpifolius is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaves and adaptability to various growing conditions.
