Conioselinum tenuisectum

Conioselinum tenuisectum, commonly known as narrow-leaved wormwood, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, aromatic stems that are often branched and covered with fine hairs. The leaves are narrow, linear, and finely divided, giving the plant a delicate, feathery appearance. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clustered inflorescences, which bloom in the summer months.

This plant is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
