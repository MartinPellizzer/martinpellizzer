Croton santolinus

Croton santolinus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton santolinus typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its vibrant, decorative leaves.
