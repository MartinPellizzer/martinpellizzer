Centaurea × nanianii

Centaurea × nanianii, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea stoebe (sow thistle). The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, with a distinctive central disk surrounded by ray florets.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
