Ceratostylis parvifolia

Ceratostylis parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved horned-stylis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
