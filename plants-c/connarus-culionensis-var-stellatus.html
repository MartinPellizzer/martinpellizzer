Connarus culionensis var. stellatus

Connarus culionensis var. stellatus, commonly known as the star-leaf connarus, is a rare shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves, which are arranged in a unique radial pattern. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within montane forests. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white in color.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a critically endangered species.
