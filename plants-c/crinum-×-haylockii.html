Crinum × haylockii

Crinum × haylockii, commonly known as the hybrid crinum lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with strap-shaped, grass-like leaves. Native to South Africa, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its striking floral display.
