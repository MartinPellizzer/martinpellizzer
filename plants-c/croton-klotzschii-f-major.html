Croton klotzschii f. major

Croton klotzschii f. major, commonly known as the giant croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter.

The leaves are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture and are often adorned with subtle yellow or orange veins.
