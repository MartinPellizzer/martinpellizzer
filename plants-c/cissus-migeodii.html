Cissus migeodii

Cissus migeodii, commonly known as the rock fig or African rock fig, is a climbing plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Vitaceae and is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, yellowish berry that contains several seeds.
