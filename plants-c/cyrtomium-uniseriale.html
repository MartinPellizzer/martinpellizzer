Cyrtomium uniseriale

Cyrtomium uniseriale, commonly known as the single-veined holly fern, is a species of evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, leathery fronds with a single central vein. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The fronds are usually evergreen, providing a lush green appearance throughout the year.
