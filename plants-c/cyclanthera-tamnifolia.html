Cyclanthera tamnifolia

Cyclanthera tamnifolia, commonly known as the African bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that develop into elongated, bitter-tasting fruits. Its botanical characteristics include a hairy stem and a distinctive, ribbed fruit surface.

Cyclanthera tamnifolia is often cultivated for its edible fruits, though they are known for their strong flavor.
