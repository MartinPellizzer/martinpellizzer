Clusia bittrichii

Clusia bittrichii, commonly known as the brightleaf glorybower, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Clusiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, orange-yellow flowers. The plant is evergreen, with a dense, bushy growth habit that makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Clusia bittrichii is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its resilience to urban environments.
