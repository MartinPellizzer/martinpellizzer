Clethra alnifolia var. denudata

Clethra alnifolia var. denudata, commonly known as the smooth clethra, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that turn yellow in the fall. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers in cylindrical clusters during late summer. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
