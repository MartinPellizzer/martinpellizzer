Chrysoglossum assamicum

Chrysoglossum assamicum, commonly known as the Assam silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs that give it a distinctive appearance.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
