Carissa ovata var. stolonifera

Carissa ovata var. stolonifera, commonly known as the creeping Natal plum, is a flowering shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing or prostrate growth habit, with numerous stolons that root at the nodes. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that give way to orange-red berries. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly waxy texture.

It is often cultivated as a ground cover or ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
