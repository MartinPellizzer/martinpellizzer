Crassula roggeveldii

Crassula roggeveldii, commonly known as the Roggeveld crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, upright growth with dense clusters of fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pink during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
