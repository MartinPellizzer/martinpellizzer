Cavendishia miconioides

Cavendishia miconioides, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its leaves are broad and leathery, providing structural support and aiding in water retention.

Cavendishia miconioides is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
