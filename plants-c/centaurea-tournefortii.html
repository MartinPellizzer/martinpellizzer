Centaurea tournefortii

Centaurea tournefortii, commonly known as the French thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, prickly leaves and vibrant purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its flowers bloom from late spring to early summer, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Centaurea tournefortii is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and hardy nature.
