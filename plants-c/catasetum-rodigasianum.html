Catasetum rodigasianum

Catasetum rodigasianum, commonly known as the Rodigasian Catasetum, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that can range from yellow to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of a single, showy flower that is often asymmetrical and adorned with intricate patterns.

The botanical features of Catasetum rodigasianum make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
