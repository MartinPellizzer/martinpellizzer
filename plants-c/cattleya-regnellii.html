Cattleya regnellii

Cattleya regnellii, commonly known as the Regnell's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow and pink. The plant has long, slender pseudobulbs and produces numerous, fragrant blooms during the growing season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cattleya regnellii is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
