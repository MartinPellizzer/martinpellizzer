Conyza glandulitecta

Conyza glandulitecta, commonly known as the glandular aster, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance and aid in water retention. It produces small yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by fluffy seeds dispersed by the wind.

Conyza glandulitecta is often found in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
