Consolida Hellespontica Subsp. Macedonica

Macedonian consolida, scientifically known as Consolida hellespontica subsp. macedonica, is a rare and endemic flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Macedonia. This delicate species belongs to the Papaveraceae family, which includes poppies and related plants, and is characterized by its vibrant, spurred petals and slender stems. It thrives in rocky and limestone soils, often found in alpine meadows and open slopes at higher elevations. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, the Macedonian consolida is considered vulnerable to environmental changes and human encroachment. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure for future generations.
