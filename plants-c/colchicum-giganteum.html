Colchicum giganteum

Colchicum giganteum, commonly known as the giant autumn crocus, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy purple or pink flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant produces a tall, leafless stem that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are basal, broad, and often have a waxy texture.

Colchicum giganteum is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and cooler climates.
