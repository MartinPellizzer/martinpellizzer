Cinnamosma fragrans

Cinnamosma fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant cinnamosma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark and leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their therapeutic properties.
