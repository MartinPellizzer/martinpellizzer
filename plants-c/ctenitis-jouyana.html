Ctenitis jouyana

Ctenitis jouyana, commonly known as the giant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a thick, sturdy trunk that supports its expansive crown of leaves. Its rhizome system allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Ctenitis jouyana plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and contributing to the biodiversity of its native ecosystems.
