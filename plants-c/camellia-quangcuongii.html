Camellia quangcuongii

Camellia quangcuongii, commonly known as the Quang Cuong camellia, is a species native to Vietnam.

It is a deciduous shrub that produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has leathery, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in subtropical gardens.

Camellia quangcuongii is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its winter blooms.
