Calonemorchis pulchra

Calonemorchis pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is relatively rare in the wild.
