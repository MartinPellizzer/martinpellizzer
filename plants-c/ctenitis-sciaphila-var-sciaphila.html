Ctenitis sciaphila var. sciaphila

Ctenitis sciaphila var. sciaphila, commonly known as the shade fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that thrive in low-light conditions. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its rhizome is creeping and produces clusters of small, spore-bearing leaves.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
