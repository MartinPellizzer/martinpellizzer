Cryptocentrum gracilipes

Cryptocentrum gracilipes, commonly known as the slender cryptocentrum, is a rare and delicate aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features slender, ribbon-like leaves that float on the water's surface, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant is typically found in slow-moving freshwater habitats, where it thrives in nutrient-rich conditions. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually submerged, contributing to its low visibility.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized aquatic environments.
