Crotalaria foliosa var. angustifolia

Crotalaria foliosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaved crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves. The plant produces striking yellow flowers with distinctive spurred petals, typical of the genus Crotalaria. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in sunny, well-drained environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
