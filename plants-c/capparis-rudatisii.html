Capparis rudatisii

Capparis rudatisii, commonly known as the rough caper, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its woody shrub structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically followed by round, green fruits. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Capparis rudatisii is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
