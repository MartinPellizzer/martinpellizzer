Croton leucophyllus var. trisepalis

Croton leucophyllus var. trisepalis, commonly known as the Three-lobed Croton, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vibrant, variegated foliage.

It features heart-shaped leaves with three distinct lobes, often displaying a mix of green, yellow, and red hues. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
