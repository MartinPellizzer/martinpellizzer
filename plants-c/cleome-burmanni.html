Cleome burmanni

Cleome burmanni, commonly known as the dwarf spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow or white flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, often with serrated edges.

Cleome burmanni is typically found in moist, open habitats such as fields and along stream banks.
