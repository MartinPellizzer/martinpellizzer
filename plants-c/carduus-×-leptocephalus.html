Carduus × leptocephalus

Carduus × leptocephalus, commonly known as the slender cardoon, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stalks and large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow composite flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting various pollinators. Native to the Mediterranean region, it has been cultivated for its edible leaves and ornamental value.

However, it can become invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
