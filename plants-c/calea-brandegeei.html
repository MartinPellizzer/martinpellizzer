Calea brandegeei

Calea brandegeei, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Calea brandegeei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique flowering behavior.
