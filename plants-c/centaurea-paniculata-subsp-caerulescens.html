Centaurea paniculata subsp. caerulescens

Centaurea paniculata subsp.

caerulescens, commonly known as blue centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a tall, branched stem.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
