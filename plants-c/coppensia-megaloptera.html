Coppensia megaloptera

Coppensia megaloptera, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 cm in diameter, featuring vibrant pink and white petals. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its unique morphology includes a robust rhizome and a single, erect stem that supports its ornamental blooms.

Coppensia megaloptera is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
