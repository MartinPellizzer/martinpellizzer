Ceropegia huernioides

Ceropegia huernioides, commonly known as the silver moon vine, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and waxy, silver-green leaves. Ceropegia huernioides is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
