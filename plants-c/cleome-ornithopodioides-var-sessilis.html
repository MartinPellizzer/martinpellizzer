Cleome ornithopodioides var. sessilis

Cleome ornithopodioides var. sessilis, commonly known as the sessile cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cleomaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and showy, yellow flowers. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds, facilitating its propagation.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of growth.
