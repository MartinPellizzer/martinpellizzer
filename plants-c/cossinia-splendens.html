Cossinia splendens

Cossinia splendens, commonly known as the splendour grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, preferring well-drained soils. Its striking appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Cossinia splendens is also valued for its ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
