Clematis Recta Var. Uncinata

Clematis recta var. uncinata is a unique and captivating vine known for its striking floral displays. This variety is prized for its delicate, fragrant blooms that appear in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile choice for gardeners. Its climbing habit requires support, often leading to its use in trellises or arbors. Despite its beauty, it requires regular pruning to maintain its shape and encourage new growth.
