Cassia dombeyana

Cassia dombeyana, commonly known as golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cassia dombeyana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
