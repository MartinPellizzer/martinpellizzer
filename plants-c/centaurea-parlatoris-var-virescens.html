Centaurea parlatoris var. virescens

Centaurea parlatoris var. virescens, commonly known as the greenleaf centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are lance-shaped with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, spike-like inflorescences, which bloom from summer to autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
