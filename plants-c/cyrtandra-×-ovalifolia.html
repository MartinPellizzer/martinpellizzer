Cyrtandra × ovalifolia

Cyrtandra × ovalifolia, commonly known as the hybrid oval-leafed heliconia, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra hastata and Cyrtandra ovalifolia, showcasing a blend of characteristics from both parent species. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. It features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, inflorescence-like structures that resemble heliconias, though it is not closely related to the true heliconias.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in tropical gardens.
