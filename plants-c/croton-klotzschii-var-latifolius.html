Croton klotzschii var. latifolius

Croton klotzschii var. latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features thick, waxy leaves that are typically broad and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. Its bark is often rough and fissured, with a distinctive reddish hue.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
