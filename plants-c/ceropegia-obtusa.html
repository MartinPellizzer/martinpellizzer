Ceropegia obtusa

Ceropegia obtusa, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, attracting specific pollinators like moths. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia obtusa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
