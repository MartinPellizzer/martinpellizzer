Cleisostoma callosum

Cleisostoma callosum, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with yellow petals and a distinctive golden center. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Cleisostoma callosum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or terrariums.
