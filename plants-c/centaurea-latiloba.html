Centaurea latiloba

Centaurea latiloba, commonly known as the broadleaf star-thistle, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features erect, spiny stems and produces vibrant blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with rough, hairy surfaces, and its flowers are arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Centaurea latiloba is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
