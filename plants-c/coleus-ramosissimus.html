Coleus ramosissimus

Coleus ramosissimus, commonly known as the spreading coleus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a metallic sheen. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coleus ramosissimus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking foliage and colorful blooms.
