Coleus Niveus

Coleus niveus is a striking plant known for its vibrant, velvety foliage. It belongs to the mint family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and ease of care. Its leaves come in shades of white, pink, and purple, creating a visually stunning display. Whether grown indoors or outdoors, Coleus niveus adds a unique touch to any space.
