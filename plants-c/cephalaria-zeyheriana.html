Cephalaria zeyheriana

Cephalaria zeyheriana, commonly known as the Zeyher's cephalaria, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and hairy stems. The plant produces showy, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
