Cymbidium erythrostylum

Cymbidium erythrostylum, commonly known as the red-lip orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, evergreen pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking red and yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is often tinged with red. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
