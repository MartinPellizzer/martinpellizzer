Calceolaria × herbeohybrida

Calceolaria × herbeohybrida, commonly known as the hybrid slipper orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Calceolaria microphylla and Calceolaria violacea.

It is characterized by its unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant is native to South America and thrives in cool, moist environments. It has hairy, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence.

This ornamental plant is popular in gardens for its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.
