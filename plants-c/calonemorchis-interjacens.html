Calonemorchis interjacens

Calonemorchis interjacens, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its yellow flowers are arranged in dense clusters, attracting various pollinators.
