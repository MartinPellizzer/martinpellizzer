Choripetalum australianum

Choripetalum australianum, commonly known as the Australian star-petalled orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant features a showy, star-shaped flower with reflexed petals that give it its common name. It typically grows in sandy soils in arid or semi-arid regions.

Choripetalum australianum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
