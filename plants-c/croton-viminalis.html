Croton viminalis

Croton viminalis, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, three-lobed seed capsules. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton viminalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaves and adaptability to various growing conditions.
