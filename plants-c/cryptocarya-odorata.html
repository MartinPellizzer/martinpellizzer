Cryptocarya odorata

Cryptocarya odorata, commonly known as the fragrant cryptocarya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland forests. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green, and have a pleasant, spicy scent when crushed.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that ripens to a dark purple color, attracting various wildlife species.
