Caladium devosianum

Caladium devosianum, commonly known as the pink hurricane caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of pink, red, and white. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other ornamental plants like dieffenbachia and philodendron. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

Caladium devosianum thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
