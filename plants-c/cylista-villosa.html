Cylista villosa

Cylista villosa, commonly known as the hairy cylista, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring. Cylista villosa is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
