Codonocarpus cotinifolius

Codonocarpus cotinifolius, commonly known as the Australian smoke bush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and semi-arid regions. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, leathery leaflets that give it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage and seasonal color changes.
