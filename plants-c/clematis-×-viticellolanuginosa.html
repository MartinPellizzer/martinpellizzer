Clematis × viticellolanuginosa

Clematis × viticellolanuginosa, commonly known as the Japanese Clematis, is a hybrid vine native to East Asia.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, twining stems that climb by means of tendrils, making it suitable for trellises or arbors. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, requiring regular watering during the growing season.
