Clerodendrum fragrans f. corymbosum

Clerodendrum fragrans f. corymbosum, commonly known as the fragrant corymbosa clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that produce fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
