Chaenanthe micrantha

Chaenanthe micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered chaenanthe, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
