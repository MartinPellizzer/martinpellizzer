Cosmos ramirezianus

Cosmos ramirezianus, commonly known as the Ramirez's cosmos, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features slender, hairy stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Cosmos ramirezianus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in full sun with well-drained soil.
