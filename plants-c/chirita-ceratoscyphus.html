Chirita ceratoscyphus

Chirita ceratoscyphus, commonly known as the horned caladium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant foliage.
