Centaurea × sub-sadleriana

Centaurea × sub-sadleriana, commonly known as the hybrid blueweed, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flower heads, which consist of both ray and disc florets. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often grayish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring through early autumn.
