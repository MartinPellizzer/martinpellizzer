Calathea longiflora

Calathea longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns and shades of green, silver, and pink. It produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters.

This plant prefers bright, indirect light and high humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
