Canna reevesii

Canna reevesii, commonly known as the Chinese canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern China.

It features large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, often with a deep green to reddish-purple hue. The plant produces showy, fiery red or orange flowers in late summer, adding vibrant color to gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full to partial sun, making it a popular choice for tropical and subtropical landscapes.

Canna reevesii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
