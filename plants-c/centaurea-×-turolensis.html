Centaurea × turolensis

Centaurea × turolensis, commonly known as the Turolensis star-thistle, is a hybrid plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, purple flower heads and bushy growth habit. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with composite flower heads.

Due to its invasive tendencies, it is often considered a weed in agricultural and natural areas.
