Chrysanthemum hendersonii

Chrysanthemum hendersonii, commonly known as the Henderson chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically produces yellow to orange flowers with a central disk, blooming in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular choice among horticulturists.
