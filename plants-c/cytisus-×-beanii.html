Cytisus × beanii

Cytisus × beanii, commonly known as the hybrid Scotch broom, is a deciduous shrub native to the British Isles.

It features bright yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has slender, arching stems with small, dark green leaves that are trifoliate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and disturbed habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
