Calibrachoa atropurpurea

Calibrachoa atropurpurea, commonly known as purple-flowered weeping calibrachoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its trailing growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from deep purple to violet. Its foliage is typically dark green with a slight velvety texture.

Calibrachoa atropurpurea is often cultivated as a ornamental plant in containers and hanging baskets due to its compact size and vibrant blooms.
