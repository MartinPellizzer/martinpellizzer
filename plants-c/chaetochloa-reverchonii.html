Chaetochloa reverchonii

Chaetochloa reverchonii, commonly known as the velvet grass, is a species of grass native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Chile.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
