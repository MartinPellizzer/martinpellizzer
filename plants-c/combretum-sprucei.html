Combretum sprucei

Combretum sprucei, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the dry season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive elliptical shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
