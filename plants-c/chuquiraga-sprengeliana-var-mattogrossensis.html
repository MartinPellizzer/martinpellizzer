Chuquiraga sprengeliana var. mattogrossensis

Chuquiraga sprengeliana var. mattogrossensis, commonly known as the Brazilian burro's tail, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features gray-green, fleshy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, yellowish flowers in the summer. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
