Chamaerhodiola gelida

Chamaerhodiola gelida, commonly known as the Arctic sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and alpine regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, clustered flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in harsh, cold environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in tundra and rocky habitats.
