Capparis thozetiana

Capparis thozetiana, commonly known as the Madagascar sour fig, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Capparaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and spiny leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fleshy, red berries. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
