Cymbidium tetrapetalum

Cymbidium tetrapetalum, commonly known as the four-petaled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, erect stems that bear clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically have four petals, which is a distinguishing characteristic of the species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
