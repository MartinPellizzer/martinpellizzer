Chrysosplenium yezoense

Chrysosplenium yezoense, commonly known as Yezo Enchanter's Nightshade, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Native to the mountainous regions of Japan, it thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.
