Cytisus + adami

Cytisus + adami, commonly known as the golden broom.

It is a shrub native to the Canary Islands, characterized by its bright yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
