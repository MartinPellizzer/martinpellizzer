Conophytum schwantesii

Conophytum schwantesii, commonly known as the Schwantes' conophytum, is a species of succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rounded leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
