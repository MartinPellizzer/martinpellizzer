Coleosanthus pendulus var. squarrosus

Coleosanthus pendulus var. squarrosus, commonly known as the squarrosus pendent lily, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, drooping stems that bear clusters of showy, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

This ornamental species is often cultivated for its elegant, cascading appearance in gardens and greenhouses.
