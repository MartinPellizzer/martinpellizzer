Cayratia mollissima var. lanceolata

Cayratia mollissima var. lanceolata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or Chinese mayapple, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are soft to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
