Clitoria occidentalis

Clitoria occidentalis, commonly known as the winged bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It features compound leaves with distinctive wing-like structures on the leaflets, giving it its common name. The plant produces vibrant purple or pink pea-like flowers, which are followed by pods containing edible seeds. Its roots are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Clitoria occidentalis is valued both for its nutritional content and its ornamental appeal in gardens.
