Cyperus virescens

Cyperus virescens, commonly known as green flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant typically grows in wet or moist soils, often in marshy areas or along waterways. It has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green or brown.

Cyperus virescens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in wet conditions.
