Caladenia exilis

Caladenia exilis, commonly known as the slender spider-orchid, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance. The plant produces a single, narrow flower with a distinctive labellum that is often pale pink or white. It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils.

Caladenia exilis is a terrestrial orchid that flowers during the autumn season.
