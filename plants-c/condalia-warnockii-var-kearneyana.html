Condalia warnockii var. kearneyana

Condalia warnockii var. kearneyana, commonly known as the Kearney's condalia, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round fruits. Its ornamental value and resilience make it a popular choice for xeriscaping in dry climates.
