Clutia Pulchella F. Genuina

Genuine clutia, scientifically known as Clutia pulchella f. genuina, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of Africa. This unique variant of the clutia species is distinguished by its vibrant, colorful foliage that changes with the seasons. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest understories. Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a conservation concern. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
