Ciconium hilare

Ciconium hilare, commonly known as the Happy Almond Tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces edible, almond-like seeds that are often used in traditional cuisine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
