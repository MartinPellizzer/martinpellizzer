Cirsium libanoticum subsp. arachnoideum

Cirsium libanoticum subsp.

arachnoideum, commonly known as the silky thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has a robust root system and thrives in well-drained soils. Its hairy stems and leaves give it a distinctive, silvery appearance.

It is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
