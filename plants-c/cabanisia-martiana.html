Cabanisia martiana

Cabanisia martiana, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family, which includes many bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are characterized by their bell-like shape and come in shades of blue and purple.
