Corryocactus brevistylus

Corryocactus brevistylus, commonly known as the short-styled cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and desert gardeners.
