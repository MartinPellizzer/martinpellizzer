Conicosia roodiae

Conicosia roodiae, commonly known as the Red-veined Zebra Plant, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of Namibia.

It features striking, waxy leaves with distinctive red veins that contrast sharply with its pale green or blue-gray foliage. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the rainy season. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to survive.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and sought-after species among collectors.
