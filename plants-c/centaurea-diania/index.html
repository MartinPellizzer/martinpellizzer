Centaurea Diania

Centaurea diania is a striking member of the sunflower family known for its vibrant blue and purple blooms. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies. With its delicate yet bold look, Centaurea diania stands out as both a beauty and a utility in the plant world.
