Cotoneaster hupehensis

Cotoneaster hupehensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of white or pink flowers in the spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. It is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its hardiness and ability to thrive in urban environments.
