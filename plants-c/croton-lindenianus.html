Croton lindenianus

Croton lindenianus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from yellow to red and orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton lindenianus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
