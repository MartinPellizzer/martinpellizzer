Cotyledon elymaitica

Cotyledon elymaitica, commonly known as the silver cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by distinctive seed pods. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a favored ornamental plant.
