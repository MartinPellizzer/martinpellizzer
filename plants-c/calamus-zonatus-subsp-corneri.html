Calamus zonatus subsp. corneri

Calamus zonatus subsp.

corneri, commonly known as the zigzag calamus, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae). It is characterized by its distinctive, zigzagging rhizomes and narrow, linear leaves.

The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix and a spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
