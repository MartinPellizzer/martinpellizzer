Crassula Perfoliata Var. Heterotricha

Crassula perfoliata var. heterotricha is a unique succulent native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, fleshy leaves that form a rosette pattern. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water to flourish. Its ability to store moisture makes it highly resilient in arid conditions. This variety is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts for its beauty and ease of care.
