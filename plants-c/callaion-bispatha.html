Callaion bispatha

Callaion bispatha, commonly known as the two-leaf water plant, is a species of aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It features narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stem. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are often submerged or floating on the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Its botanical structure makes it well adapted to aquatic life, with specialized tissues for gas exchange and buoyancy.
