Centaurothamnus Maximus

Centaurothamnus maximus is a striking plant native to the arid regions of Australia. It is known for its tall, erect growth and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy choice for dry landscapes. Its unique appearance has made it a popular subject in botanical studies and garden design. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more common native plants.
