Callitriche fassettii

Callitriche fassettii, commonly known as Fassett's water starwort, is a small aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, delicate leaves that are usually arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or floating.

Callitriche fassettii plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for microorganisms and invertebrates.
