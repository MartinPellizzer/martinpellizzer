Coelogyne schultesii

Coelogyne schultesii, commonly known as the Schultes' orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, lance-shaped leaf and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant blooms once every few years, typically in the summer, and its flowers have a delicate, orchid-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne schultesii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
