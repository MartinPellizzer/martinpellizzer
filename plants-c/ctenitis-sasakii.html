Ctenitis sasakii

Ctenitis sasakii, commonly known as the Sasakii rock fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Tectariaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Indonesia and the Philippines. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, finely divided fronds that give it a delicate appearance. Ctenitis sasakii typically grows on rocky substrates in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to indoor conditions.
