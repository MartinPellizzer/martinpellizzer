Cheiridopsis purpurascens

Cheiridopsis purpurascens, commonly known as the purple ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in arid climates.
