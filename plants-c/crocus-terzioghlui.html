Crocus terzioghlui

Crocus terzioghlui, commonly known as the Terzioglu crocus, is a species of flowering plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Turkey and is characterized by its delicate, early spring blooms. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, which appear before the leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
