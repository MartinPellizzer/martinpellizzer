Capnoides vesicaria

Capnoides vesicaria, commonly known as the blistering broom.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, particularly China and India. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Capnoides vesicaria is known for its ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
