Calanthe variegata

Calanthe variegata, commonly known as the variegated calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its strikingly patterned leaves, which often display a mix of green, white, and yellow hues. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil.

Calanthe variegata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
