Crocanthemum majus

Crocanthemum majus, commonly known as the tall tansy.

It is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family, characterized by its tall, branched stems and yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and along roadsides. Its leaves are aromatic and slightly hairy, while the flowers attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is typically perennial and can grow up to two meters in height.
