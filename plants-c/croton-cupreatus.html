Croton cupreatus

Croton cupreatus, commonly known as the copperleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking foliage, with leaves that range in color from coppery red to deep green, depending on the light conditions. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant leaves.

Croton cupreatus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
