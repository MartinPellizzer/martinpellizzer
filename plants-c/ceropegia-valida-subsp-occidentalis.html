Ceropegia valida subsp. occidentalis

Ceropegia valida subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the Western arrowroot or the string of hearts, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its attractive foliage and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
