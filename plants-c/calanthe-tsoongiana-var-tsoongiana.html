Calanthe tsoongiana var. tsoongiana

Calanthe tsoongiana var. tsoongiana, commonly known as the Tsoong's calanthe, is a rare and elegant orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and horticultural interest.

Due to its limited natural habitat and conservation status, it is often cultivated in specialized gardens.
