Camellia kwangtungensis

Camellia kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a red or yellow base. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Camellia kwangtungensis is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its winter blooming period.
