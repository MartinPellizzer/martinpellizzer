Citrus grandis var. sabon

Citrus grandis var. sabon, commonly known as the Sabon lime, is a variant of the citrus genus native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and fragrant, yellow-green fruit. The fruit has a thin, smooth rind and a high juice content, making it popular in culinary and aromatic applications. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and resistance to certain pests.
