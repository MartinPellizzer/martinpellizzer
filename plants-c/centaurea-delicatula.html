Centaurea delicatula

Centaurea delicatula, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branched stems and small, blue or purple flowers. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Centaurea delicatula is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
