Calceolaria mexicana subsp. perijensis

Calceolaria mexicana subsp.

perijensis, commonly known as the Perijan slipper orchid, is a member of the Campanulaceae family. It is native to the high-altitude regions of Mexico and Guatemala, where it thrives in cool, moist environments.

The plant is characterized by its distinctive, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral morphology.
