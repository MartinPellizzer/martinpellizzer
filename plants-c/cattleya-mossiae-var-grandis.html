Cattleya mossiae var. grandis

Cattleya mossiae var. grandis, commonly known as the large-flowered Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, featuring a vibrant mix of pink and white hues. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid with fleshy, segmented pseudobulbs and long, arching leaves. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
