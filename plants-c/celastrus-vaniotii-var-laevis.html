Celastrus vaniotii var. laevis

Celastrus vaniotii var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth staff-vine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its smooth, slender stems and opposite, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by orange to red berries containing several seeds.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
