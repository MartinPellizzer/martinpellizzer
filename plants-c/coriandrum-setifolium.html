Coriandrum setifolium

Coriandrum setifolium, commonly known as love-in-a-mist, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-covered stems and delicate, white or pale blue flowers that bloom in spikes. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Coriandrum setifolium is often grown as an ornamental plant for its elegant appearance and fragrance.
