Croton oleoides

Croton oleoides, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that ranges from green to red, orange, and yellow, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Croton oleoides is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
