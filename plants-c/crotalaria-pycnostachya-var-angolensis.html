Crotalaria pycnostachya var. angolensis

Crotalaria pycnostachya var. angolensis, commonly known as the Angola cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its seeds are enclosed in pods that mature into a hard, woody structure.

This species is native to southern Africa and is often found in dry, open habitats.
