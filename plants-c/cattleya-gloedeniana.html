Cattleya gloedeniana

Cattleya gloedeniana, commonly known as the Gloeden's cattleya, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has long, strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Cattleya gloedeniana is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
