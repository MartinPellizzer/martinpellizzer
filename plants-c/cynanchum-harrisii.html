Cynanchum harrisii

Cynanchum harrisii, commonly known as Harris's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the United States, particularly found in the southeastern region.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant typically grows in open woods and along stream banks, preferring moist, well-drained soils. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface, contributing to its distinctive botanical appearance.
