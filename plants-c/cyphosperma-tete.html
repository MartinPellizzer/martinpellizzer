Cyphosperma tete

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of feather-like leaves. The plant produces cones, with male and female cones typically occurring on separate plants, a feature common in cycads. Cyphosperma tete is adapted to arid and rocky environments, showcasing its resilience to harsh conditions.
