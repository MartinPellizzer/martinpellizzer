Cyperus capillaris

Cyperus capillaris, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or marshy environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale green or brown. Its root system helps in stabilizing soil and preventing erosion in wetland areas.

Cyperus capillaris is also used in traditional medicine in some cultures for its purported health benefits.
