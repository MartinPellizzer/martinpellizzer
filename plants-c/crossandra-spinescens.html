Crossandra spinescens

Crossandra spinescens, commonly known as the spiny crossandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Crossandra spinescens thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
