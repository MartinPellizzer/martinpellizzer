Correa × delicata

Correa × delicata, commonly known as the pink correa, is a hybrid plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically grows in shrub form, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a dense, attractive foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
