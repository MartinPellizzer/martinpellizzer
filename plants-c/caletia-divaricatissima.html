Caletia divaricatissima

Caletia divaricatissima, commonly known as the variable spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is known for its ability to bloom throughout the spring and summer seasons.

Caletia divaricatissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant in rockeries.
