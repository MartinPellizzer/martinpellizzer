Carex thailandica var. thailandica

Carex thailandica var. thailandica, commonly known as Thai sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces spikelets that are typically clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for water garden landscapes.
