Cryptostylis zeylanica

Cryptostylis zeylanica, commonly known as the Zeylanic spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that resemble spiders, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. It produces a single, showy inflorescence with numerous tiny flowers.

Cryptostylis zeylanica is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitat.
