Colona evrardii

Colona evrardii, commonly known as the Evrard's colona, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.
