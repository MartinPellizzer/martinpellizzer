Centaurea helenioides

Centaurea helenioides, commonly known as the Greek helene, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and spiny, hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea helenioides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.
