Crinum yorkensis

Crinum yorkensis, commonly known as the York lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy white or pink flowers. The plant produces clusters of strap-shaped leaves that are often evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to its floral display. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Crinum yorkensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral show.
