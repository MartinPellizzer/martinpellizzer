Correa × longiflora

Correa × longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered correa, is a hybrid shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to white, typically blooming in spring. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value.

Correa × longiflora is well-suited to coastal and Mediterranean climates due to its drought tolerance.
