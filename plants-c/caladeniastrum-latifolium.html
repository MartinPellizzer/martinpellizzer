Caladeniastrum latifolium

Caladeniastrum latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider orchid, is a species of orchid native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spider-like flower structure with a slender, curved labellum. The plant typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats. Its leaves are broad and strap-shaped, often with a waxy coating.

The flowering period occurs during the spring, producing a striking display of pink and white blooms.
