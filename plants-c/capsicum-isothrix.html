Capsicum isothrix

Capsicum isothrix, commonly known as the velvet pepper, is a unique species within the Capsicum genus.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence that covers its entire surface, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and compound leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow or white, and the fruit is a small, round pepper that can range in color from green to red.

This species is native to South America and is valued for its ornamental qualities as well as its potential culinary uses.
