Clinopodium longicaule

Clinopodium longicaule, commonly known as slender wood betony, is a perennial herb native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and has opposite, lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are small, tubular, and clustered in whorls, often blooming in late spring to early summer.

Clinopodium longicaule is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
