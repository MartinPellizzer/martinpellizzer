Cyrtopodium confusum

Cyrtopodium confusum, commonly known as the confused corydalis, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Cyrtopodium confusum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
