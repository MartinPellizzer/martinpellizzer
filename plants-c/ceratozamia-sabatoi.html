Ceratozamia sabatoi

The plant is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of large, feather-like leaves that can reach up to two meters in length. Its botanical features include a thick, fibrous root system and a unique reproductive structure known as a strobilus.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat destruction, Ceratozamia sabatoi is considered endangered and is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists.
