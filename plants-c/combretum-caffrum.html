Combretum caffrum

Combretum caffrum, commonly known as the Natal flame bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
