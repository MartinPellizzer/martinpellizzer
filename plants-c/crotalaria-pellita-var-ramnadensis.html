Crotalaria pellita var. ramnadensis

Crotalaria pellita var. ramnadensis, commonly known as the Ramnad yellow cowpea, is a flowering plant native to southern India.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
