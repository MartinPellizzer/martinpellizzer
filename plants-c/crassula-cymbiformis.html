Crassula cymbiformis

Crassula cymbiformis, commonly known as the "Cushion Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features dense, compact growth with fleshy, bluish-green leaves that form a low, mat-like structure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Crassula cymbiformis is popular as a houseplant due to its ease of care and attractive appearance.
