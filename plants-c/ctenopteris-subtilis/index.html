Ctenopteris Subtilis

Ctenopteris fern, scientifically known as Ctenopteris subtilis, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia. With its intricate fronds and delicate, feather-like structure, it is a popular choice among indoor gardeners and botanists alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it an ideal candidate for greenhouses and terrariums. Its unique appearance and ease of care have made it a favored ornamental plant in recent years. Despite its beauty, Ctenopteris subtilis remains relatively understudied, offering ample opportunities for further botanical research.
