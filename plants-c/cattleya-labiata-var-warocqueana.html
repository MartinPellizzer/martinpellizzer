Cattleya labiata var. warocqueana

Cattleya labiata var. warocqueana, commonly known as the Warocqueana Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces large, showy blooms with a distinctive color pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
