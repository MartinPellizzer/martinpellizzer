Cissus Leucophlea

Cissus leucophlea, known as the silver-leafed ivy, is a striking member of the grape family native to southern Africa. Its unique appearance features pale green leaves with a silvery sheen that catches the light beautifully. This climbing plant thrives in warm, humid environments and is often used as a decorative indoor plant. Its ability to climb and trail makes it a popular choice for hanging baskets and shelves. Despite its delicate look, it is surprisingly hardy and adaptable to various growing conditions.
