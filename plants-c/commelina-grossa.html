Commelina grossa

Commelina grossa, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features striking blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, typical of the Commelina genus. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Commelina grossa is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support biodiversity in aquatic ecosystems.
