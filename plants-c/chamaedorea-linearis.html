Chamaedorea linearis

Chamaedorea linearis, commonly known as the slender bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features slender, arching leaves that are elongated and narrow, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance. The plant typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 feet and has a compact, upright growth habit. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, making it a popular choice for indoor landscaping.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health and appearance.
