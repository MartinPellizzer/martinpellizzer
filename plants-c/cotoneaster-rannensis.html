Cotoneaster rannensis

Cotoneaster rannensis, commonly known as the Rann Desert cotoneaster, is a flowering plant native to the Thar Desert in India.

It is a small shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to harsh climatic conditions. The plant produces small, red berries that are an important food source for local wildlife. Its leaves are typically small, leathery, and arranged alternately along the stems.

Cotoneaster rannensis plays a significant role in stabilizing desert soils and supporting biodiversity in its native habitat.
