Chrysanthemum artemisioides

Chrysanthemum artemisioides, commonly known as the silverleaf chrysanthemum, is a perennial herb native to southern China.

It features finely divided, silver-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the autumn. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
