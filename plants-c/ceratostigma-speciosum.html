Ceratostigma speciosum

Ceratostigma speciosum, commonly known as the healing plant or fairy bells, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to China.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has dark green, waxy leaves that turn red in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ability to produce vibrant foliage and delicate blooms makes it a popular choice in horticulture.
