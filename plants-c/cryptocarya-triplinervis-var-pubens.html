Cryptocarya triplinervis var. pubens

Cryptocarya triplinervis var. pubens, commonly known as the hairy three-veined cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its hairy leaves, which give it its varietal name "pubens," meaning hairy in Latin. Its leaves are typically elliptical with three prominent veins, contributing to its scientific name.

This species is often found in lowland tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments.
