Cryptocoryne fusca

Cryptocoryne fusca, commonly known as the black water cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves that often appear almost black in color, especially in low-light conditions. This plant thrives in soft, acidic, and low-nutrient waters, making it a popular choice for blackwater aquariums. Its rhizomes form dense, bushy growths that help stabilize substrates and provide shelter for aquatic life.

Cryptocoryne fusca is known for its resilience and ability to adapt to varying water conditions, though it requires careful maintenance to prevent algae overgrowth.
