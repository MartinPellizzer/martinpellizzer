Casparia confusa

Casparia confusa, commonly known as the confused casparia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its palm-like appearance, with a central stem and large, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Casparia confusa is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks in its native range.
