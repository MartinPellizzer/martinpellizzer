Christisonia legocia

Christisonia legocia, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are often overlooked due to the prominent foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.

Christisonia legocia is sometimes considered a weed due to its rapid growth and adaptability.
