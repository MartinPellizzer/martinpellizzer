Chrysocoma herbacea

Chrysocoma herbacea, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pink.

The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
