Cyathea gleichenioides

Cyathea gleichenioides, commonly known as the Gleichenoid fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in persistent, fibrous roots. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Cyathea gleichenioides plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
