Caladium simsii

Caladium simsii, commonly known as the pink caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of pink, white, and green. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.

Caladium simsii is often grown as a seasonal plant, blooming in summer with small, pale yellow flowers.
