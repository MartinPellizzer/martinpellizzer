Cyrtorchis latibracteata

Cyrtorchis latibracteata, commonly known as the broad-bract orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring showy, often brightly colored petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters, and the leaves are usually broad and strap-like.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or tropical gardens.
