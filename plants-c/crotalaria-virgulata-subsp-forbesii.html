Crotalaria virgulata subsp. forbesii

Crotalaria virgulata subsp.

forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves.

The plant typically grows as an annual herb with upright stems and is often found in disturbed soils. Its seeds are encased in a hard, spiny pod, which aids in seed dispersal.

The species is noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in soil enrichment.
