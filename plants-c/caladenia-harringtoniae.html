Caladenia harringtoniae

Caladenia harringtoniae, commonly known as the Harrington's spider orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Western Australia.

It features a single, slender stem that bears a single, intricate flower with a vivid pinkish-red coloration. The plant has a narrow, grass-like leaf that is often overlooked due to its delicate appearance. It blooms during the late winter to early spring season, typically between June and August.

This species is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.
