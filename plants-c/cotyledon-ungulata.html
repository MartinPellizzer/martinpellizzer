Cotyledon ungulata

Cotyledon ungulata, commonly known as the shark's fin plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically green with red or purple margins. The plant produces striking orange or red flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil.

Cotyledon ungulata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
