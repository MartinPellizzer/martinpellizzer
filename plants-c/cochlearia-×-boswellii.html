Cochlearia × boswellii

Cochlearia × boswellii, commonly known as the hybrid sea kale, is a flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cochlearia bulbifera and Cochlearia humilis. This plant is typically found in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. It produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant is characterized by its rosette growth habit and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
