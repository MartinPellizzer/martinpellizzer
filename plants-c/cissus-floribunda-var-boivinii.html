Cissus floribunda var. boivinii

Cissus floribunda var. boivinii, commonly known as the Boivin's devil's ivy, is a climbing plant native to Madagascar.

It features heart-shaped, glossy dark green leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the wet season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Its vigorous growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
