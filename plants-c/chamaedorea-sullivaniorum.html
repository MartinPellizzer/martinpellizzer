Chamaedorea sullivaniorum

Chamaedorea sullivaniorum, commonly known as the Sullivan's bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central America.

It features slender, arching leaves that give it a graceful, bamboo-like appearance. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate watering to maintain its health.

Its air-purifying qualities and low maintenance make it a popular choice for both residential and commercial spaces.
