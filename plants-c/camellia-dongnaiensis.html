Camellia dongnaiensis

Camellia dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai camellia, is a species native to Vietnam.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with a waxy texture. Its leaves are elliptical, glossy, and have a leathery texture.

Camellia dongnaiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
