Campanula rotundifolia var. jurjurensis

Campanula rotundifolia var. jurjurensis, commonly known as the round-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from June to August. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and natural landscapes.
