Cardiospermum canescens

Cardiospermum canescens, commonly known as the white morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy white or pale pink flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Cardiospermum canescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
