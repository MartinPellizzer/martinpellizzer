Cassia perrieri

Cassia perrieri, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, drooping clusters of golden-yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces oblong, dark green leaves with a leathery texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Cassia perrieri thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
