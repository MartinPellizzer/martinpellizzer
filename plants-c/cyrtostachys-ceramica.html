Cyrtostachys ceramica

Cyrtostachys ceramica, commonly known as the Ceramica bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional landscaping.
