Crotalaria fruticosa

Crotalaria fruticosa, commonly known as the velvet bean plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub that can grow up to 2 meters tall, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain seeds used in various agricultural and industrial applications. It is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in crop rotation.

However, it contains toxic alkaloids, which can be harmful to livestock and humans if ingested.
