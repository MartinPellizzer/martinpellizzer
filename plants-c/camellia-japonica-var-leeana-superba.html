Camellia japonica var. leeana-superba

Camellia japonica var. leeana-superba, commonly known as the Leeana-Superba camellia, is a hybrid plant originating from the cross between Camellia japonica and Camellia reticulata.

It is prized for its large, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its floral displays. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soil and prefers partial shade to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

This variety is also noted for its cold hardiness and ability to bloom reliably in temperate climates.
