Cymbidium giganteum var. lowianum

Cymbidium giganteum var. lowianum, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 12 inches in length, often in shades of yellow and green. The plant features long, arching inflorescences and is known for its robust, evergreen foliage. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and outdoor gardens.
