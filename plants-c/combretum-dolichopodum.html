Combretum dolichopodum

Combretum dolichopodum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved combretum, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
