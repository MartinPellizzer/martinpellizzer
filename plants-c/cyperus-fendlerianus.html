Cyperus fendlerianus

Cyperus fendlerianus, commonly known as the desert flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas.

It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils and is well adapted to drought conditions. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of flowers. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in dry grasslands.

Cyperus fendlerianus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small organisms in its native environment.
