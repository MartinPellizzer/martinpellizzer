Calanthe stolzii

Calanthe stolzii, commonly known as the Stolz's calanthe, is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Calanthe stolzii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
