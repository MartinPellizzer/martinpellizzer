Costus pulchriflorus

Costus pulchriflorus, commonly known as the beautiful flower costus, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height, adorned with large, showy inflorescences composed of numerous small, white to pinkish flowers. The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant floral display and air-purifying properties.
