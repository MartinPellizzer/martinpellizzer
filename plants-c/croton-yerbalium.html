Croton yerbalium

Croton yerbalium, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often features shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia.

Croton yerbalium is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
