Ceratostylis cryptantha

Ceratostylis cryptantha, commonly known as the hidden horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, creeping rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine pubescence.

The orchid is notable for its unique floral structure and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
