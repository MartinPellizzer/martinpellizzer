Catasetum tricolor

Catasetum tricolor, commonly known as the three-colored orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tri-colored flowers that typically feature red, yellow, and green hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are often fragrant and attract specific pollinators, such as bees and wasps.

The species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively manageable care requirements.
