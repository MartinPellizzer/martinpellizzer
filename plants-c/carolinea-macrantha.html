Carolinea macrantha

Carolinea macrantha, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its roots are fibrous, and it has narrow, grass-like leaves.

Carolinea macrantha is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens.
