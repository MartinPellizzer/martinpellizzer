Conospermum taxifolium var. linifolium

Conospermum taxifolium var. linifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved conospermum, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for native landscaping.
