Calanthe tyoh-harae

Calanthe tyoh-harae, commonly known as the Tyoh-hara calanthe, is a species of orchid native to Japan.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, lanceolate leaves. The plant typically blooms with showy, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It prefers shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
