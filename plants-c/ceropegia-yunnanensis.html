Ceropegia yunnanensis

Ceropegia yunnanensis, commonly known as the sand mat or sand spider plant, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its succulent, trailing stems and small, fleshy leaves that help it store water. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a unique, spiky appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia yunnanensis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
