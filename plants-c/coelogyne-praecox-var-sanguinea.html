Coelogyne praecox var. sanguinea

Coelogyne praecox var. sanguinea, commonly known as the Red-flowered Early Coelogyne, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red and pink floral displays, which are particularly vivid during the flowering season. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its showy flowers.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
