Culcasia dinklagei

Culcasia dinklagei, commonly known as the Dinklage's giant taro, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, which includes other familiar plants like calla lilies and philodendrons. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
