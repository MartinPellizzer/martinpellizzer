Camaridium hagsaterianum

Camaridium hagsaterianum, commonly known as the silverleaf starbloom, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well adapted to its harsh environment.
