Cyathea fauriei

Cyathea fauriei, commonly known as the Faurie's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds. The plant is evergreen and can grow up to several meters in height, with a slow growth rate. Its fronds are typically bright green and can reach lengths of over two meters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cyathea fauriei thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
