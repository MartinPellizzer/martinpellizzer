Cormonema spinosum var. peruvianum

Cormonema spinosum var. peruvianum, commonly known as the Peruvian spineless corm, is a unique plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and compact growth habit, which helps it thrive in rocky and arid environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the spring season, adding ornamental value to its appearance. Its corm-like stems store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

This variety is prized for its resilience and distinctive appearance in horticultural settings.
