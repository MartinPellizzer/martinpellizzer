Chaetoseris neglecta

Chaetoseris neglecta, commonly known as the slender leaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads composed of ray and disk florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. Its leaves are narrow and hairy, while the flowers are yellow and produce small, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is a perennial herb that is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
