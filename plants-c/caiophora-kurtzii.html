Caiophora kurtzii

Caiophora kurtzii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Caiophora kurtzii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
