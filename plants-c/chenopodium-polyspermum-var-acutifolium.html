Chenopodium polyspermum var. acutifolium

Chenopodium polyspermum var. acutifolium, commonly known as sharpleaf goosefoot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and alternate, lanceolate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish flowers in dense clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

This species is also noted for its potential use in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
