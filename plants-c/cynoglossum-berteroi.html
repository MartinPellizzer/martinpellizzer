Cynoglossum berteroi

Cynoglossum berteroi, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and grows from a basal rosette of foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Cynoglossum berteroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
