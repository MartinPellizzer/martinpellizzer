Ceropegia cicatricosa

Ceropegia cicatricosa, commonly known as the scarred ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.
