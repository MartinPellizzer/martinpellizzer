Cryptocoryne bertelihansenii

Cryptocoryne bertelihansenii, commonly known as the Java crypts, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length, often with a slight wavy edge. The plant is well-suited for planted aquariums due to its ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that emerge from the water surface.

Cryptocoryne bertelihansenii is valued for its ease of care and aesthetic appeal in both freshwater and brackish environments.
