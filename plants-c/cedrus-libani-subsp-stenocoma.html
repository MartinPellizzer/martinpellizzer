Cedrus libani subsp. stenocoma

Cedrus libani subsp.

stenocoma, commonly known as the narrowcone cedar, is a subspecies of the Lebanon cedar. It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

The plant is characterized by its slender, conical shape and short, dense cones. Its foliage consists of scale-like leaves that are typically dark green.

This subspecies is adapted to arid and rocky environments, making it a resilient and hardy plant.
