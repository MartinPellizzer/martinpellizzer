Cynanchum erythranthum

Cynanchum erythranthum, commonly known as red milkweed or Indian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by distinctive, orange-red seed pods. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as fields and woodland edges.

Cynanchum erythranthum is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
