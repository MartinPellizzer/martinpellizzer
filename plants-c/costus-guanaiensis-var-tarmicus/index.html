Costus Guanaiensis Var. Tarmicus

Costus guanaiensis var. tarmicus is a lesser-known species of the Costaceae family native to the tropical regions of South America. This unique plant is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves and vibrant, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it has gained attention among botanists and horticulturists. However, it remains a rare and under-researched plant, highlighting the need for further study and conservation efforts.
