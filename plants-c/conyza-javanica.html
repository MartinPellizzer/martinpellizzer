Conyza javanica

Conyza javanica, commonly known as goatweed, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a branched stem covered with fine hairs and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a composite head. The plant has alternate, narrow, and hairy leaves that are often lobed or serrated. It is commonly found in disturbed soils, along roadsides, and in agricultural fields.

Conyza javanica is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various environmental conditions.
