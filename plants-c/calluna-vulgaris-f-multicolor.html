Calluna vulgaris f. multicolor

Calluna vulgaris f. multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored heather, is a variant of the common heather.

It is native to the northern hemisphere and thrives in acidic, well-drained soils. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and the presence of multiple flower colors, including pink, red, and white. It blooms in late summer to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens and natural landscapes.

Due to its hardiness and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and as a ground cover.
