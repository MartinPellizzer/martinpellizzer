Chionanthus microbotrys

Chionanthus microbotrys, commonly known as the Chinese snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
