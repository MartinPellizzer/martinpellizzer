Crassula confusa

Crassula confusa, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail" or "Coral Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of fleshy, blue-green leaves that form a compact, bushy structure. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Crassula confusa is popular in rock gardens and as a container plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
