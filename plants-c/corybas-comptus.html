Corybas comptus

Corybas comptus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hooded orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, hooded flower that resembles a helmet, typically ranging in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces a single, erect stem with a few narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers bloom during late winter to early spring, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Corybas comptus is a rare and endangered species, found primarily in the North Island of New Zealand.
