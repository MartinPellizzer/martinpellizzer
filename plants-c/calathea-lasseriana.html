Calathea lasseriana

Calathea lasseriana, commonly known as the Lasser's Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that enhances their visual appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
