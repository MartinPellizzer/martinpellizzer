Calonemorchis abbreviata

Calonemorchis abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often in a loose inflorescence, with pale yellow to greenish-white petals.

Calonemorchis abbreviata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
