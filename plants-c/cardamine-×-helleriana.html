Cardamine × helleriana

Cardamine × helleriana, commonly known as the Heller's watercress, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland floors. Its leaves are often submerged or partially submerged, adapted to aquatic or semi-aquatic conditions.

Cardamine × helleriana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
