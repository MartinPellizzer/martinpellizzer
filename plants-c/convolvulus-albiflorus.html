Convolvulus albiflorus

Convolvulus albiflorus, commonly known as whiteflower morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, funnel-shaped flowers that bloom in the morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Convolvulus albiflorus is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
