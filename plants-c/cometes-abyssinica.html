Cometes abyssinica

Cometes abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian comet, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flower spikes that can reach several feet in height. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically a vibrant red or orange in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
