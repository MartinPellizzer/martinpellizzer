Citriobatus multiflorus var. linearis

Citriobatus multiflorus var. linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved citriobatus, is a unique species within the Citriobatus genus.

It is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves that give it a distinctive appearance compared to other varieties. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a vibrant yellow or orange hue.

Botanically, it is noted for its adaptability to various soil types, though it prefers well-drained, nutrient-rich environments.
