Connarus grandis

Connarus grandis, commonly known as the giant connarus, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes other aromatic trees and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and distinctive, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark is thick and corky, often used in traditional medicine by local communities.
