Croton cneorifolius

Croton cneorifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaf croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It features simple, alternate leaves that are typically narrow and elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow, followed by capsules containing numerous seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streambanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
