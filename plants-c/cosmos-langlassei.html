Cosmos langlassei

Cosmos langlassei, commonly known as the Langlasse's cosmos, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Cosmos langlassei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
