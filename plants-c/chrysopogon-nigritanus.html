Chrysopogon nigritanus

Chrysopogon nigritanus, commonly known as blackstem grass, is a perennial grass species native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences with pale to brownish flowers.

Chrysopogon nigritanus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
