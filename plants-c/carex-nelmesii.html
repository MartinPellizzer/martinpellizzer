Carex nelmesii

Carex nelmesii, commonly known as the Nelmes' sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the northern regions of North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. This plant typically grows in moist to wet environments such as marshes, bogs, and along stream banks. Its foliage is grass-like, with narrow, linear leaves that are usually dark green in color.

Carex nelmesii produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.
