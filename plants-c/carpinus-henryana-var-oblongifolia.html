Carpinus henryana var. oblongifolia

Carpinus henryana var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the Chinese hornbeam, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers in early spring, followed by small, round fruits that mature in the fall. Its bark is smooth and gray, with a subtle flaky texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
