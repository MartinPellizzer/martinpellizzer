Coelogyne diphylla

Coelogyne diphylla, commonly known as the two-leaved orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a pseudobulb structure with a single leaf, and it produces two leaves from the same node, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne diphylla is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
