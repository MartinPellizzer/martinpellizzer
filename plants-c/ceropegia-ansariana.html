Ceropegia ansariana

Ceropegia ansariana, commonly known as the starry ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often with a distinctive hooded structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Ceropegia ansariana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
