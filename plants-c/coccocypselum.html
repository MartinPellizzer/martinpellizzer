Coccocypselum

Coccocypselum, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cactaceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom during the holiday season. Its leaves are often modified into short, rounded stems, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Coccocypselum is popular as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
