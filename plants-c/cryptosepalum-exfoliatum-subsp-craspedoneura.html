Cryptosepalum exfoliatum subsp. craspedoneura

Cryptosepalum exfoliatum subsp.

craspedoneura, commonly known as the smooth-flowered cryptosepalum, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure, with petals that are often fused at the base.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
