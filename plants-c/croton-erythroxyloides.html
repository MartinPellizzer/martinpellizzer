Croton erythroxyloides

Croton erythroxyloides, commonly known as the redwood croton, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Croton.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture with a variety of colors, including green, red, and orange.

Croton erythroxyloides is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
