Clematis bidens

Clematis bidens, commonly known as the common clematis, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a perennial vine that typically grows up to 6 feet tall, featuring heart-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Clematis bidens is known for its vigorous growth and can be cultivated as a climbing plant or in containers.
