Cynanchica lilaciflora subsp. coa

Cynanchica lilaciflora subsp.

coa, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia. It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves.

The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a legume that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
