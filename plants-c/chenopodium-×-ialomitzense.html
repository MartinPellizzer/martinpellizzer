Chenopodium × ialomitzense

Chenopodium × ialomitzense, commonly known as the Ialomitz plant, is a hybrid species within the Chenopodiaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and stems, which are adapted to arid environments. The plant typically grows in sandy or saline soils, showcasing its halophytic traits. Its flowers are small and greenish, often clustered in dense inflorescences.

This species is of interest in botanical studies due to its unique morphological features and ecological adaptations.
