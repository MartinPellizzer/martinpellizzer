Chenopodina Prostrata

Chenopodina prostrata is a low-growing, succulent plant commonly found in arid and semi-arid regions. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions. This species is known for its dense, mat-like growth habit and small, often bluish-gray leaves. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife. Due to its resilience, it is often used in ecological restoration projects.
