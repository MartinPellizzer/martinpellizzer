Cissus homblei

Cissus homblei, commonly known as the African dew plant, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often covered in tiny, translucent glands. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers followed by edible, sour-tasting berries. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cissus homblei is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
