Combretum trichanthum var. petitianum

Combretum trichanthum var. petitianum, commonly known as the small-flowered flame bush, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its small, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and sandy environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its vibrant floral display.
