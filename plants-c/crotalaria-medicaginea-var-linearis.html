Crotalaria medicaginea var. linearis

Crotalaria medicaginea var. linearis, commonly known as the linear spurred cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features yellow flowers with distinctive spurred petals, and its linear leaves are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. It is often grown as a forage crop due to its high nutritional value for livestock.

The plant also has medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
