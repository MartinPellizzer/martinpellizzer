Cyclamen rohlfsianum

Cyclamen rohlfsianum, commonly known as the Rohlfs' cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and vibrant, spurred flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens. Its unique floral structure includes a long, tubular corolla that facilitates pollination by insects.

Cyclamen rohlfsianum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
