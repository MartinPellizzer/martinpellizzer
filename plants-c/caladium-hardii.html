Caladium hardii

Caladium hardii, commonly known as the elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The leaves are typically brightly colored, with shades of white, pink, red, and green. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its striking foliage.
