Congea forbesii

Congea forbesii, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.
