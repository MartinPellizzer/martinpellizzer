Croton rhombifolius

Croton rhombifolius, commonly known as the diamond-leaf croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rhombus-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red and yellow. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Croton rhombifolius is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
