Cistus libanotis var. fastigiatus

Cistus libanotis var. fastigiatus, commonly known as the upright rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dense, upright growth with narrow, leathery leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to red, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its aromatic foliage and drought tolerance contribute to its popularity among gardeners in arid climates.
