Corysanthes longituba

Corysanthes longituba, commonly known as the long-tubed corysanthes, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, elongated floral tubes. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, hairy texture.

Corysanthes longituba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils in full sun.
