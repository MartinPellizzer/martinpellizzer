Cleome humboldtii

Cleome humboldtii, commonly known as the swamp cleome, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found near water bodies.

Cleome humboldtii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
