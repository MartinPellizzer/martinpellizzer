Clematis × guascoi var. monophylla

Clematis × guascoi var. monophylla, commonly known as the single leaf clematis, is a hybrid plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring large, single leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
