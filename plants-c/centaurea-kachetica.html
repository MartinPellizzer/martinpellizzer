Centaurea kachetica

Centaurea kachetica, commonly known as Kachetian cornflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces numerous small, composite flower heads.

Centaurea kachetica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
