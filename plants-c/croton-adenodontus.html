Croton adenodontus

Croton adenodontus, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with deep lobes and bright green, glossy surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton adenodontus is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
