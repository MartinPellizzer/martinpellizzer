Calochortus venustus var. caroli

Calochortus venustus var. caroli, commonly known as the Carol's fairy lantern, is a striking member of the Liliaceae family native to California.

It is characterized by its showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, thriving in full sun. Its bulbs are covered in a fibrous sheath and are adapted for storing water and nutrients.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
