Cypripedium × pendulum

Cypripedium × pendulum, commonly known as the hanging lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from several Cypripedium species.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically pink or red in color. The plant is native to eastern Asia and thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it produces a single flower per stem.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific care to mimic its natural habitat.
