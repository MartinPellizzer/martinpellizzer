Coris monspeliensis f. denticulata

Coris monspeliensis f. denticulata, commonly known as the denticulate coris, is a unique horticultural form of the Coris monspeliensis species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely toothed leaves that give it a striking appearance. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.

The foliage remains evergreen, providing year-round visual interest.
