Citrus × panuban

Citrus × panuban, commonly known as the Panuban lime, is a hybrid citrus plant originating from the Philippines.

It is characterized by its small, round, and highly aromatic fruits with a thick, yellowish-green rind. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, featuring glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit is prized for its high acidity and is widely used in culinary and medicinal applications.

The botanical features of Citrus × panuban make it a valuable species in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
