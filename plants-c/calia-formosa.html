Calia formosa

Calia formosa, commonly known as the beauty leaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative foliage plant. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Calia formosa is relatively low maintenance, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
