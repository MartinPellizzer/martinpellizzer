Coelogyne peltastes

Coelogyne peltastes, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and yellow. The plant has fleshy, strap-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne peltastes is a popular orchid among horticulturists due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
