Circaea Racemosa Var. Alpina

Circaea racemosa var. alpina is a delicate flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia. It thrives in rocky soils and high elevation environments where few plants can survive. Known for its clusters of small, white flowers, it adds a subtle beauty to mountainous landscapes. This variant of Circaea racemosa is particularly adapted to cold climates and harsh weather conditions. Its unique ecological role makes it an important species in alpine ecosystems.
