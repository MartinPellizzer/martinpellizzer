Caryochloa brasiliensis

Caryochloa brasiliensis, commonly known as the Brazilian feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a soft, bluish-green hue.

Caryochloa brasiliensis is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
