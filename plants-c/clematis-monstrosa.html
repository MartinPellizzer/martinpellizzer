Clematis monstrosa

Clematis monstrosa, commonly known as the giant clematis, is a vigorous climbing vine native to China.

It features large, showy flowers that range from white to deep purple, often with a distinctive, almost fuzzy appearance. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine, rusty-colored hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis monstrosa is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and can reach impressive heights when given proper support.
