Coreopsis Major F. Oemleri

Coreopsis major f. oemleri is a striking variant of the common coreopsis known for its vibrant yellow to orange flowers. This perennial plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners. Its compact growth habit and long blooming season add beauty to gardens throughout the summer. The plant is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. With its easy care requirements, it's a versatile addition to both wildflower and cultivated landscapes.
