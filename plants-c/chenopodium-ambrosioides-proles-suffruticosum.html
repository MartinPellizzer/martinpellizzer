Chenopodium ambrosioides proles suffruticosum

Chenopodium ambrosioides proles suffruticosum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub with succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically oval or triangular in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are pollinated by insects. Its leaves and stems are covered in fine, prickly hairs, giving it a distinctive texture.

It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.
