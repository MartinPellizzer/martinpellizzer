Calanthe breviscapa

Calanthe breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed calanthe, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically arranged in a raceme. Native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in cool, humid environments.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in specialized horticultural settings.
