Caldenbachia

Caldenbachia, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a member of the Davalliaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its rhizomes are fibrous and help anchor it to rocky or soil surfaces.

Caldenbachia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and air-purifying qualities.
