Cleistanthus lanuginosus

Cleistanthus lanuginosus, commonly known as the woolly cleistanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
