Chamaeangis dewevrei

Chamaeangis dewevrei, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a short, upright stem with a few narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
