Chukrasia velutina var. dongnaiensis

Chukrasia velutina var. dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks. Its bark is smooth and grayish, while its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
