Cardamine lyallii

Cardamine lyallii, commonly known as Lyall's watercress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family and is native to New Zealand.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. The plant features trifoliate leaves with a peppery aroma, similar to other members of its family. Its flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are arranged in racemes.

Cardamine lyallii is often found in alpine and subalpine regions, where it thrives in cool, damp conditions.
