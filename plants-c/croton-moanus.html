Croton moanus

Croton moanus, commonly known as the golden croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with leaves that display shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Croton moanus typically grows as an upright shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
