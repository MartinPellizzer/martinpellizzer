Calceolaria epilobioides

Calceolaria epilobioides, commonly known as the silverleaf calceolaria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Calceolariaceae and is characterized by its striking, hooded flowers that range in color from white to pink. Calceolaria epilobioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
