Chamaedorea repens

Chamaedorea repens, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen plant native to Central America and parts of Mexico. The plant features slender, arching leaves that are dark green with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

Chamaedorea repens is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
