Cryptostylis acutata var. acutata

Cryptostylis acutata var. acutata, commonly known as the acute-styled cryptostylis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with pale yellow petals and a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it requires specific conditions to flourish.
