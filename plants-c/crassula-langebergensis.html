Crassula langebergensis

Crassula langebergensis, commonly known as the Langeberg Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth pattern. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish or reddish tinge, especially when exposed to sunlight. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the warmer months.

Crassula langebergensis is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and indoor gardening.
