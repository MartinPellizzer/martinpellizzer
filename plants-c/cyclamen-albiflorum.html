Cyclamen albiflorum

Cyclamen albiflorum, commonly known as the whiteflower cyclamen, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces clusters of small, white, bell-shaped flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade. Its foliage and flowers are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Cyclamen albiflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
