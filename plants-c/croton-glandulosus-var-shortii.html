Croton glandulosus var. shortii

Croton glandulosus var. shortii, commonly known as the short's croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with a distinctive, often lobed shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
