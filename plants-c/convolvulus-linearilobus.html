Convolvulus linearilobus

Convolvulus linearilobus, commonly known as the narrow-lobed morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, winding stems with heart-shaped leaves that have distinct lobes. Its flowers are trumpet-shaped, usually pale blue to purple, and bloom in the morning.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
