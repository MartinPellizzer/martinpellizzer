Caladenia picta

Caladenia picta, commonly known as the Painted Spider-orchid, is a striking orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, spider-like appearance and vibrant, often colorful flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers feature intricate patterns and hues of red, yellow, and purple, which attract specific pollinators.

Caladenia picta is a member of the orchid family and is known for its unique and delicate floral structure.
