Calamintha chinensis var. grandiflora

Calamintha chinensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as Chinese germander, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and small, fragrant white or pale purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant scent and delicate foliage.
