Cypripedium × corningianum

Cypripedium × corningianum, commonly known as the Corning's lady's slipper, is a hybrid orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch, which is typically pink or white in color. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and thrives in moist, shaded forest environments. It produces showy, fragrant blooms in late spring to early summer.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
