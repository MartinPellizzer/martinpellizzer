Cyananthus microphyllus subsp. microphyllus

Cyananthus microphyllus subsp.

microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaved bluebell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, bell-shaped blue to purple flowers.
