Caladium bauersii

Caladium bauersii, commonly known as the "Rainbow Caladium," is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is prized for its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of white, pink, red, and green. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have intricate, mottled patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is often used in gardens and as a houseplant due to its striking foliage.
