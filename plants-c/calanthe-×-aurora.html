Calanthe × aurora

Calanthe × aurora, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating blend of species within the Calanthe genus.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often display vibrant colors such as pink, white, and purple. The plant typically features broad, elliptical leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This hybrid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a potted plant.
