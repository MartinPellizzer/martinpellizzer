Cyrtanthus sanguineus var. wakefieldii

Cyrtanthus sanguineus var. wakefieldii, commonly known as the Wakefield's rain lily, is a perennial bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features striking pink to red flowers that bloom in spring, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant produces narrow, grass-like leaves that are evergreen and drought-tolerant. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and vibrant color.
