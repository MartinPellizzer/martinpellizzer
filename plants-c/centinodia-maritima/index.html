Centinodia Maritima

Centinodia maritima is a rare and hardy plant native to coastal regions. It thrives in salty, sandy soils and is often found along the shores of the Mediterranean. This plant is known for its ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions. Its small, delicate flowers bloom in the summer months. Despite its resilience, it remains a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
