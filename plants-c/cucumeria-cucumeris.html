Cucumeria cucumeris

Cucumeria cucumeris, commonly known as the common cucumber, is a vine plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It produces elongated, green fruits that are typically harvested before they fully mature. The plant has palmate leaves with lobed edges and tendrils that help it climb and spread. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female blooms on the same plant.

The fruit is used in various culinary applications and is also valued for its medicinal properties.
