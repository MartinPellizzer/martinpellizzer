Chrysophyllum fulvum

Chrysophyllum fulvum, commonly known as the yellow star apple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The tree produces edible fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, with a sweet, nutty flavor. The plant is also valued for its hard, durable wood.
