Closia elata var. nana

Closia elata var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf clove bush, is a compact shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a dense canopy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Its compact size makes it suitable for use in ornamental gardens and as a hedge plant.
