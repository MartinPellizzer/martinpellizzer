Croton linifolius

Croton linifolius, commonly known as the silky croton, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are soft to the touch and have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Croton linifolius is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
