Chassalia assimilis var. angustifolia

Chassalia assimilis var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf chassalia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, angular stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of yellow disc flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.
