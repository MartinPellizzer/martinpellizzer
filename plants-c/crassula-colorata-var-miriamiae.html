Crassula colorata var. miriamiae

Crassula colorata var. miriamiae, commonly known as the Miriam's Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish or pinkish hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
