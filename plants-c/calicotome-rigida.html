Calicotome rigida

Calicotome rigida, commonly known as the rigid calicotome, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny stems and small, pale green leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-resistant.

Its root system helps prevent soil erosion, contributing to its ecological importance in its native habitat.
