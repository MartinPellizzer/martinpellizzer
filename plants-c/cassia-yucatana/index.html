Cassia Yucatana

Cassia yucatana is a striking tropical plant native to Central America and parts of the Caribbean. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and feathery foliage that add a dramatic touch to any landscape. This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to flourish. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
