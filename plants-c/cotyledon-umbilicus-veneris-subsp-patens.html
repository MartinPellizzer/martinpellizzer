Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris subsp. patens

Cotyledon umbilicus-veneris subsp.

patens, commonly known as the "Moonstone Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa. It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, especially when exposed to sunlight.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is a low-growing species, typically reaching heights of only a few inches.

Its unique leaf patterns and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
