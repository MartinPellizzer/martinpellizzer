Crotalaria kanaii

Crotalaria kanaii, commonly known as the Kanai's crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive spurred flowers.
