Chelone alba

Chelone alba, commonly known as white hellebore, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It features showy, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and velvety in texture. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Chelone alba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
