Crotalaria rubiginosa var. wightiana

Crotalaria rubiginosa var. wightiana, commonly known as the red root cowpea, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit, trifoliate leaves, and showy yellow flowers with a distinctive crown-like structure. It produces small, oblong pods containing several seeds, and its root system is notably deep and robust.

This species is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop due to its nutritional value and adaptability to various soil conditions.
