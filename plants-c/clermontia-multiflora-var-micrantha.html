Clermontia multiflora var. micrantha

Clermontia multiflora var. micrantha, commonly known as the Haleakalā silversword, is a rare endemic plant native to the island of Maui in Hawaii.

It is characterized by its rosette of silver-green, hairy leaves that form a dense, compact structure. The plant produces a single, branched stem that bears small, white, five-petaled flowers. It thrives in the harsh, high-elevation environments of the Haleakalā volcano.

Due to its limited habitat and conservation status, it is considered a critically endangered species.
