Calochortus maweanus var. roseus

Calochortus maweanus var. roseus, commonly known as the roseate mariposa lily, is a striking bulbous perennial native to California.

It features showy, cup-shaped flowers that range from pink to rose-purple, often with a contrasting yellow center. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in coastal and inland areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces a single flower per stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
