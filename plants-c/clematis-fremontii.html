Clematis fremontii

Clematis fremontii, commonly known as the Fremont's clematis, is a flowering vine native to the western United States.

It produces clusters of fragrant, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and is typically found in rocky or arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Clematis fremontii is valued for its ornamental beauty and hardy nature in challenging climates.
