Celosia missionis

Celosia missionis, commonly known as the flame flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, plume-like inflorescences that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant thrives in warm, sunny conditions and prefers well-draining soil. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a compact growth habit.

Celosia missionis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
