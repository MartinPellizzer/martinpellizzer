Cryophytum grandifolium

Cryophytum grandifolium, commonly known as the giant leaf cryptophyte, is a rare alpine plant characterized by its large, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in cold, arid environments.

The plant's unique morphology includes a short, sturdy stem and clustered foliage that minimizes heat loss. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in late summer.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, Cryophytum grandifolium is considered a vulnerable species, facing threats from climate change and habitat degradation.
