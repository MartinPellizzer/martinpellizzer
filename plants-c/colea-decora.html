Colea decora

Colea decora, commonly known as the decorative colea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 feet. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Colea decora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
