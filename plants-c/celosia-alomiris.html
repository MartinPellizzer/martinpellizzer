Celosia alomiris

Celosia alomiris, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a striking annual plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, spiky bracts that resemble flames, which give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is known for its showy, colorful inflorescences that can range from red to orange and yellow. Celosia alomiris thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and short blooming period make it a favorite in seasonal flower arrangements.
