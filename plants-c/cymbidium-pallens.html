Cymbidium pallens

Cymbidium pallens, commonly known as the white cymbidium orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in winter. The plant features broad, strap-like leaves and produces inflorescences with multiple white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Cymbidium pallens is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
