Cyperus Polystachyos Var. Hahnianus

Cyperus polystachyos var. hahnianus is a striking aquatic plant known for its unique, spiky foliage. It thrives in shallow water and is often found in wetlands and marshes around the world. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to purify water. Its feathery appearance makes it a popular choice for pond and aquarium setups. Despite its beauty, it can spread aggressively if not managed properly.
