Crinum exaltatum

Crinum exaltatum, commonly known as the tall crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South America.

It features tall, narrow leaves and produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant is characterized by its strong, fragrant scent, which is particularly noticeable in the evening. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Crinum exaltatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively hardy in warm climates.
