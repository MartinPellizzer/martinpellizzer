Centaurea antiochia var. antiochia

Centaurea antiochia var. antiochia, commonly known as the Antioch blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer, typically from July to September. The plant has slender, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as rocky slopes and fields.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
