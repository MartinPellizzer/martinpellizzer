Coelogyne laciniloba

Coelogyne laciniloba, commonly known as the lace-leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves that give it its common name, and its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Coelogyne laciniloba is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
