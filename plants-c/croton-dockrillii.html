Croton dockrillii

Croton dockrillii, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with lobed margins and bright green, glossy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by orange or red berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Due to its attractive appearance and adaptability, it is often cultivated in warm climates.
