Cybistax coriacea var. barbatula

Cybistax coriacea var. barbatula, commonly known as the barbatula betony, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its aromatic foliage and long blooming period.
