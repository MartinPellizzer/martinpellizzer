Carex paleacea f. erectiuscula

Carex paleacea f. erectiuscula, commonly known as the erect spike sedge, is a variant of the pale sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family, characterized by its upright growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that bloom in late spring to early summer. It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as marshes, pond edges, and stream banks.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland ecosystems.
