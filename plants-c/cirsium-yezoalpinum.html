Cirsium yezoalpinum

Cirsium yezoalpinum, commonly known as Yezo alpine thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Japan.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish-purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine habitats and is adapted to cold climates. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native environment.
