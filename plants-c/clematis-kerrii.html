Clematis kerrii

Clematis kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's Clematis, is a climbing vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, typically in spring. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its stems are covered in fine hairs. It prefers partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden trellises or arbors.

Clematis kerrii is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively low maintenance requirements.
