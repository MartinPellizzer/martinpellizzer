Chaetoseris qiliangshanensis

Chaetoseris qiliangshanensis, commonly known as the Qiliangshan dandelion, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in alpine meadows and rocky areas, thriving in cool, temperate climates. Its leaves are basal, lanceolate, and covered with fine pubescence, while its flowers are yellow with ray florets.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
