Clinopodium umbrosum var. shibetchensis

Clinopodium umbrosum var. shibetchensis, commonly known as Japanese wood betony, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate, with a soft, hairy texture, and it produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in whorled clusters.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses in Japanese herbal medicine.
