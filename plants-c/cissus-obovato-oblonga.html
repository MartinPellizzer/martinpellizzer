Cissus obovato-oblonga

Cissus obovato-oblonga, commonly known as the broad-ovate cissus, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped to oval leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are succulent and can develop aerial roots for support as it climbs.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various growing conditions.
