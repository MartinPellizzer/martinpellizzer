Crepidium laxum

Crepidium laxum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant has a slender, erect stem that can reach up to 30 cm in height, often bearing a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a distinctive three-lobed lip.

Crepidium laxum is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
