Clerodendrum pyrifolium

Clerodendrum pyrifolium, commonly known as the flame-of-the-forest, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and can be reddish or grayish in color.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
