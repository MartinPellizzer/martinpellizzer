Clathrotropis brachypetala

Clathrotropis brachypetala, commonly known as the short-petalled clathrotropis, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
