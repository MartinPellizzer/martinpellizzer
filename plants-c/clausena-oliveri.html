Clausena oliveri

Clausena oliveri, commonly known as the African star apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, star-shaped fruits with a sweet, acidic flavor. Clausena oliveri is often grown for its ornamental value and as a source of fruit in home gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
