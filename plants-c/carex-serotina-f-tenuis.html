Carex serotina f. tenuis

Carex serotina f. tenuis, commonly known as the slender late carex, is a variant of the late carex species native to North America.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae, characterized by its grass-like appearance. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikes of pale brownish or yellowish flowers. It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.
