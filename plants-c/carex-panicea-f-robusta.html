Carex panicea f. robusta

Carex panicea f. robusta, commonly known as the robust tussock sedge, is a perennial plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces fluffy, panicle-shaped inflorescences that are typically brownish or reddish in color. It thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
