Cycas distans

Cycas distans, commonly known as the Far Eastern cycad, is a primitive gymnosperm belonging to the family Cycadaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of China, Japan, and the Philippines. The plant features a stout, unbranched trunk with a crown of large, pinnately compound leaves. Its reproductive structures include a terminal cone, with male and female cones occurring on separate plants.

Cycas distans is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
