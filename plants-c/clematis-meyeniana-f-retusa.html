Clematis meyeniana f. retusa

Clematis meyeniana f. retusa, commonly known as the smooth Clematis, is a compact, deciduous vine native to New Zealand.

It features slender, wiry stems that climb by means of twining. The plant produces showy, single or double flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in spring. Its foliage is heart-shaped and has a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.
