Centaurea scabiosa subsp. adpressa

Centaurea scabiosa subsp.

adpressa, commonly known as the pressed centaury, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, spiky blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Its compact growth habit and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
