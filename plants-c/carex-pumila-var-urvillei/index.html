Carex Pumila Var. Urvillei

Carex pumila var. urvillei is a unique sedge species known for its compact growth and distinctive foliage. Native to certain regions of Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. This variety is often favored for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes. Its leaves are typically dark green with a subtle silver sheen. Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it has become a popular choice among horticulturists.
