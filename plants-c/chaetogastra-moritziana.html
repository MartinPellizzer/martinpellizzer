Chaetogastra moritziana

Chaetogastra moritziana, commonly known as the Moritz's spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to South Africa. The plant features slender, arching stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of white and green. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.
