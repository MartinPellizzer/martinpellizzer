Commersonia loxophylla

Commersonia loxophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mallee, is a small shrub native to southern Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It typically grows in sandy or clay soils in arid and semi-arid regions.
