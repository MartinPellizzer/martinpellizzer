Callista adunca

Callista adunca, commonly known as the broad-leafed lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-shaped leaves and produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Callista adunca is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty.
