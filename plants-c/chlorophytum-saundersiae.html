Chlorophytum saundersiae

Chlorophytum saundersiae, commonly known as the Whale's Tail Plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that can grow up to 2 meters in length, giving it a unique and striking appearance. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens with suitable conditions.
