Coptosperma graveolens subsp. graveolens

Coptosperma graveolens subsp.

graveolens, commonly known as the aromatic sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants.
