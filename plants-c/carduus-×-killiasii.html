Carduus × killiasii

Carduus × killiasii, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a hybrid between Carduus marianus and Carduus nutans, resulting in a plant with distinctive spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The species is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in disturbed soils. It is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex inflorescence and its role in ecological succession.
