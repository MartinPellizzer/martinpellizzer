Crataegus dodgei

Crataegus dodgei, commonly known as the Dodge hawthorn, is a species of shrub or small tree native to the United States.

It belongs to the genus Crataegus in the family Rosaceae, which includes other hawthorns and roses. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that appear in spring. Its fruit is a red or yellow berry that ripens in the fall, providing food for various wildlife species.
