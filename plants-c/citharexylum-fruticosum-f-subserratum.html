Citharexylum fruticosum f. subserratum

Citharexylum fruticosum f. subserratum, commonly known as the serrated citharexylum, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, ovate leaves with serrated margins, giving it its distinguishing name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its foliage is dense and often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a versatile addition to tropical and subtropical landscapes.
