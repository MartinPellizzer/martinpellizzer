Chimonobambusa neopurpurea

Chimonobambusa neopurpurea, commonly known as the purple bamboo, is a species of grass bamboo native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, upright culms that often exhibit a striking purplish hue, especially in younger growth. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
