Cremastosperma confusum

Cremastosperma confusum, commonly known as the confused grassvetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer, followed by pods containing several seeds. It is typically found in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important species for soil stabilization and ecological diversity in its native range.
