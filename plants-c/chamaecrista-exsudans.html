Chamaecrista exsudans

Chamaecrista exsudans, commonly known as the redroot cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a legume, characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
