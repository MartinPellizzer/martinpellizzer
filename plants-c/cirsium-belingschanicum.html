Cirsium belingschanicum

Cirsium belingschanicum, commonly known as the Chinese thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has a robust root system that allows it to survive in harsh conditions.

Its common name reflects its origin and the prickly nature of its foliage.
