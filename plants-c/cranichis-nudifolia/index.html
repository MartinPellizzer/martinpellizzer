Cranichis Nudifolia

Cranichis nudifolia is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of Central America. This plant is known for its striking white flowers and unique growth habits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-drained soil. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of both scientific interest and admiration.
