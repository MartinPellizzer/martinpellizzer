Cnicus pitcheri

Cnicus pitcheri, commonly known as the prairie sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of less than 10 inches. The plant produces yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs.

Cnicus pitcheri thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in open prairie habitats.
