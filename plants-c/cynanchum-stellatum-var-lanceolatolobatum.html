Cynanchum stellatum var. lanceolatolobatum

Cynanchum stellatum var. lanceolatolobatum, commonly known as the lanceolate lobed milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their milk-like sap. The plant features opposite, lanceolate leaves with lobed margins, and it produces distinctive, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to white. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical structure supports its role as a host plant for certain butterfly species.
