Cryptocoryne spiralis

Cryptocoryne spiralis, commonly known as spiral cryptocoryne, is a species of aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and attractive appearance. The plant thrives in freshwater environments with moderate to high light conditions. Its rhizomes form dense mats, making it an effective choice for aquariums and pond landscaping.

Cryptocoryne spiralis is relatively easy to care for and adapts well to a variety of water parameters.
