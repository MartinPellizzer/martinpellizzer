Callista ochreata

Callista ochreata, commonly known as the ochre lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllis family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy, often arranged in a rosette pattern. Callista ochreata thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
