Crassula sandrae

Crassula sandrae, commonly known as the Sandra's Crassula, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
