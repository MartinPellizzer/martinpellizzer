Convolvulus siculus var. eusiculus

Convolvulus siculus var. eusiculus, commonly known as the Sicilian bindweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, tubular flowers typically in shades of pink or purple. The plant has sprawling stems that can climb or trail along the ground, often forming dense mats. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
