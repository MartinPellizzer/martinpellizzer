Cyrtandra × umbelliflora

Cyrtandra × umbelliflora, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra umbellifera and another Cyrtandra species, likely Cyrtandra corymbosa. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a contrasting throat. It is native to Hawaii and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Cyrtandra × umbelliflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological diversity of Hawaiian flora.
