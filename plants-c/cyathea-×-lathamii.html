Cyathea × lathamii

Cyathea × lathamii, commonly known as the Latham's tree fern, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyathea australis and Cyathea medullifera.

It is a member of the family Cyatheaceae and is native to New Zealand. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cyathea × lathamii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
