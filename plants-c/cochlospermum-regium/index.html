Cochlospermum Regium

Cochlospermum regium is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant red flowers and unique, spiky fruit. Native to regions of Africa and parts of Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its toxic properties.
