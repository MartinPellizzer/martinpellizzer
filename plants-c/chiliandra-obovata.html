Chiliandra obovata

Chiliandra obovata, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are broad and oval, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Chiliandra obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
