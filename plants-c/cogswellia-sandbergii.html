Cogswellia sandbergii

Cogswellia sandbergii, commonly known as the sandberg's cogswellia, is a rare and unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit and thick, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
