Cucumis melo var. utilissimus

Cucumis melo var. utilissimus, commonly known as the honeydew melon, is a cultivar of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale green rind and sweet, pale yellow to white flesh. The plant is a vigorous vine that produces large, oblong fruits with a distinct aroma. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is typically harvested when fully mature, indicated by a change in color and a sweet aroma.
