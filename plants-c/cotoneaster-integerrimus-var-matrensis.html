Cotoneaster integerrimus var. matrensis

Cotoneaster integerrimus var. matrensis, commonly known as the Matrenskaya cotoneaster, is a shrub native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, oval leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in the spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

This variety is often cultivated in gardens for its hardiness and visual appeal.
