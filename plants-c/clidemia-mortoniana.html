Clidemia mortoniana

Clidemia mortoniana, commonly known as the Morton's stiltweed, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
