Cymbidium sinense var. pallidiflorum

Cymbidium sinense var. pallidiflorum, commonly known as the pale-flowered orchid, is a species native to China and is part of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a herbaceous plant with pseudobulbs that support its growth and store nutrients. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing white to pale yellow flowers with a faintly colored lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is appreciated for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
