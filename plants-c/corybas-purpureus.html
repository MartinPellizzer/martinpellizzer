Corybas purpureus

Corybas purpureus, commonly known as the purple hooded orchid, is a species of orchid native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, hood-like flower that is typically purple in color. The plant has a short, erect stem with a single, showy flower at the top. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often growing from the base of the stem.

Corybas purpureus thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.
