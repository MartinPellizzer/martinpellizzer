Centaurea × czetzii

Centaurea × czetzii, commonly known as the hybrid cornflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Centaurea cyanus (blue cornflower) and Centaurea solstitialis (yellow starthistle). The plant typically grows to a height of 30–100 cm, featuring bright blue or purple flower heads with prominent central disks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while the stems are branching and somewhat woody.

Centaurea × czetzii is native to the western United States and is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats.
