Clerodendrum moldenkeanum

Clerodendrum moldenkeanum, commonly known as the African balm, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in summer, followed by dark blue to black berries.
