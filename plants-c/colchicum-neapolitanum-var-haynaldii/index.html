Colchicum Neapolitanum Var. Haynaldii

Colchicum neapolitanum var. haynaldii is a rare and striking plant native to southern Italy. It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is known for its distinctive purple flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is often found in rocky soils and mountainous regions, thriving in cooler climates. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after species among plant enthusiasts and botanists. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered vulnerable and requires careful conservation efforts.
