Cousinia schischkinii

Cousinia schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's cudweed, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in rocky or sandy soils. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.
