Chrysanthemum lanuginosum

Chrysanthemum lanuginosum, commonly known as the downy chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
