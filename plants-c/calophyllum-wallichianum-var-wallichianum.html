Calophyllum wallichianum var. wallichianum

Calophyllum wallichianum var. wallichianum, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and simple, alternate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

The plant also has medicinal uses in traditional medicine.
