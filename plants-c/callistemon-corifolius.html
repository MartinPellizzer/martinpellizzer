Callistemon corifolius

Callistemon corifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The flowers are not only ornamental but also attract birds and bees, contributing to local biodiversity.
