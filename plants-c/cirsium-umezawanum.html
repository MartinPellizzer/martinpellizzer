Cirsium umezawanum

Cirsium umezawanum, commonly known as the Japanese thistle, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats and is known for its ornamental value. It has been cultivated for its unique appearance and is sometimes used in horticulture.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
