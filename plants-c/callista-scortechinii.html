Callista scortechinii

Callista scortechinii, commonly known as the mountain daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, often blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
