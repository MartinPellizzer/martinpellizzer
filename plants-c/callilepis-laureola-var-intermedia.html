Callilepis laureola var. intermedia

Callilepis laureola var. intermedia, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal and mountainous regions, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are evergreen, narrow, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand coastal conditions.
