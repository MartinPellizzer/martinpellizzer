Cytisus pseudonigricans

Cytisus pseudonigricans, commonly known as the dark flowering broom, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow, pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas. It produces small, woody pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
