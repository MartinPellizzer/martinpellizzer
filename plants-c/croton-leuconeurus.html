Croton leuconeurus

Croton leuconeurus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, with leaves that can range from bright red to deep orange and yellow. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, spiny capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant leaf colors.

Croton leuconeurus is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
