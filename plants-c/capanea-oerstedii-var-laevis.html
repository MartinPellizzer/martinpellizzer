Capanea oerstedii var. laevis

Capanea oerstedii var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth capanea, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense, spherical clusters, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks.
