Cotula mariae

Cotula mariae, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, often succulent leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cotula mariae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
