Citrus sabon f. jiyu

Citrus sabon f. jiyu, commonly known as the fragrant citrus or Chinese lemon, is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its small, oval-shaped fruits.

The plant produces fragrant, yellow-orange fruits with a thin, smooth skin and a high concentration of essential oils. It is native to China and is often cultivated for its aromatic properties and medicinal uses. The leaves are dark green, leathery, and have a strong citrus scent.

This variety is valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils for perfumery and food flavoring.
