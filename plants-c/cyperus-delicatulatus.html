Cyperus delicatulatus

Cyperus delicatulatus, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of water bodies. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its root system is fibrous and helps in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.

Cyperus delicatulatus is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and ability to filter water.
