Codiaeum alternifolium

Codiaeum alternifolium, commonly known as the flame leaf or croton plant, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has alternate, palmately lobed leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing seeds.

Codiaeum alternifolium thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
