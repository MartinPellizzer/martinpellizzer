Conyzigeron × huelsenii

Conyzigeron × huelsenii, commonly known as the Chinese dandelion, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and hairy stems, which are typical features of the genus Conyzigeron. The plant is native to East Asia and is often found in disturbed soils and open areas. It produces seeds that are dispersed by wind, similar to other members of the dandelion family.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
