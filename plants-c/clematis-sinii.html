Clematis sinii

Clematis sinii, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically white or pale blue with a distinctive, recurved shape. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that mature to a dark blue color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Clematis sinii is valued for its elegant blooms and adaptability to various garden conditions.
