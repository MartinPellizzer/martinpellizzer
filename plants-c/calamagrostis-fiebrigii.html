Calamagrostis fiebrigii

Calamagrostis fiebrigii, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-tipped panicles that create a striking visual effect in gardens. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers moist, well-drained soils.

Calamagrostis fiebrigii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract wildlife.
