Clausena anisata var. mollis

Clausena anisata var. mollis, commonly known as the smooth Indian bitter apple, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub with smooth, glabrous bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and clustered in axillary inflorescences, while the fruit is a yellow, fleshy drupe with a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant is valued for its edible fruit and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.

It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests and gardens.
