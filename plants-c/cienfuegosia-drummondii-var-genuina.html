Cienfuegosia drummondii var. genuina

Cienfuegosia drummondii var. genuina, commonly known as the Mexican flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in arid and semi-arid regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in warm climates.
