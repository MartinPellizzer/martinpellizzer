Calathea mishuyacu

Calathea mishuyacu, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of deep green, silver, and purple. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a waxy texture that gives them a premium appearance. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardens.

Its unique leaf patterns and elegant appearance make it a sought-after ornamental plant.
