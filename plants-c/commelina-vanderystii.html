Commelina vanderystii

Commelina vanderystii, commonly known as Vanderyst's spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many species of dayflowers and spiderworts. The plant features striking blue-violet flowers with three petals and three sepals, often accompanied by a distinctive white or yellow spatulate bract. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value has made it a subject of interest in horticulture and botanical study.
