Commiphora rosifolia

Commiphora rosifolia, commonly known as the rose-scented myrrh tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Madagascar.

The plant is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine and perfumery.
