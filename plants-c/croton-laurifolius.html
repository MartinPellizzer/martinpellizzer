Croton laurifolius

Croton laurifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, broad, elliptical leaves that are often a deep green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.

Croton laurifolius thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
