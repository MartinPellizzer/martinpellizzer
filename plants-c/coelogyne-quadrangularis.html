Coelogyne quadrangularis

Coelogyne quadrangularis, commonly known as the four-angled orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive four-angled stems and long, drooping inflorescences. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Coelogyne quadrangularis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
