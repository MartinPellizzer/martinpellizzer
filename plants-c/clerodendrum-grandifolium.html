Clerodendrum grandifolium

Clerodendrum grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically followed by ornamental, spiny fruits. Its leaves are large, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
