Chirita cicatricosa

Chirita cicatricosa, commonly known as the scarred chirita, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often variegated leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
