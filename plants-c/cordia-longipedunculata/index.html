Cordia Longipedunculata

Cordia tree, scientifically known as Cordia longipedunculata, is a striking member of the Boraginaceae family native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. This tree is recognized for its vibrant, showy flowers and its ability to thrive in diverse environmental conditions. Its unique foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for both landscaping and horticultural purposes. The Cordia tree also holds cultural and medicinal significance in many communities where it is traditionally used. With its ecological benefits and aesthetic appeal, it remains an important plant in both natural and cultivated ecosystems.
