Clintonia podanisia var. phyllostema

Clintonia podanisia var. phyllostema, commonly known as the white-flowered Clintonia, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a desirable ornamental plant for woodland gardens.
