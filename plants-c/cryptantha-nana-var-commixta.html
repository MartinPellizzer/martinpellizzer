Cryptantha nana var. commixta

Cryptantha nana var. commixta, commonly known as the dwarf cryptantha, is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, rocky habitats.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ecological and aesthetic value.
