Crotalaria sphaerocarpa var. grandiflora

Crotalaria sphaerocarpa var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered crotalaria, is a species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its erect growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its flowers are typically clustered in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The plant is known for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial in certain agricultural contexts.
