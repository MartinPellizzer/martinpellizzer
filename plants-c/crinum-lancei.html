Crinum lancei

Crinum lancei, commonly known as the lanceleaf crinum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features strap-shaped, lanceolate leaves that are typically green and arranged in a rosette. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, with a strong, spicy fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum lancei is known for its hardiness and ability to bloom reliably in warm climates.
