Croton arlineae

Croton arlineae, commonly known as the Arline's croton, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of hues including red, yellow, and green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by unique, spiny capsules.

Croton arlineae is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
