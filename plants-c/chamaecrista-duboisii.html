Chamaecrista duboisii

Chamaecrista duboisii, commonly known as the African burr legume, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.

Chamaecrista duboisii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native landscapes.
