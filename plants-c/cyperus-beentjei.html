Cyperus beentjei

Cyperus beentjei, commonly known as the Beentje flatsedge, is a species of flowering plant in the sedge family Cyperaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly found in wetlands and marshy areas. The plant has grass-like leaves and produces small, three-sided spikelets that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist soils and is often found in swampy or seasonally flooded habitats.

Cyperus beentjei is noted for its adaptability to wet environments and its role in stabilizing aquatic ecosystems.
