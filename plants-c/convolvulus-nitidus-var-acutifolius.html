Convolvulus nitidus var. acutifolius

Convolvulus nitidus var. acutifolius, commonly known as the sharp-leaved morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pale blue or violet in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry environments.

This variety is notable for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and runners.
