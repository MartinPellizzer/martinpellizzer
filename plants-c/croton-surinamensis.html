Croton surinamensis

Croton surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from bright yellow to orange and red, often with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
