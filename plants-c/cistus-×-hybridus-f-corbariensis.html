Cistus × hybridus f. corbariensis

Cistus × hybridus f. corbariensis, commonly known as the Corbariense rock rose, is a hybrid shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and vibrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its aromatic leaves and showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

This subspecies is notable for its adaptability and resilience in arid environments.
