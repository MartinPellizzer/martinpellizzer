Calathea fragilis

Calathea fragilis, commonly known as the fragile calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage. The leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with intricate patterns of green, silver, and pink. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful handling and consistent care to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
