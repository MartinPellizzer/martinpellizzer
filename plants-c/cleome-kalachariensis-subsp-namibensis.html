Cleome kalachariensis subsp. namibensis

Cleome kalachariensis subsp.

namibensis, commonly known as the Namibian spider flower, is a succulent shrub native to the arid regions of Namibia. It is characterized by its distinctive, spindly stems and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it store water, adapting it to its harsh, dry environment. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a hardy choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular ornamental plant in arid landscapes.
