Coryphopteris brevipilosa

Coryphopteris brevipilosa, commonly known as the short-pilose shield fern, is a species of fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage. The fronds are typically leathery and have a dense, overlapping structure that provides a shield-like appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
