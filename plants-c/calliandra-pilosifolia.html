Calliandra pilosifolia

Calliandra pilosifolia, commonly known as the silky calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences that are often pink or red in color. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental gardening.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and drought tolerance.
