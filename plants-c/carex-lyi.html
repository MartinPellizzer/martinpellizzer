Carex lyi

Carex lyi, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada sedge, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. Its grass-like appearance features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads.

Carex lyi is often found in meadows and along stream banks, contributing to the ecological diversity of its habitat.
