Croton diadenus

Croton diadenus, commonly known as the African croton, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful leaves. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that can be ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

Croton diadenus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
