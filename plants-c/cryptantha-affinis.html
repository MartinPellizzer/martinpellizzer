Cryptantha affinis

Cryptantha affinis, commonly known as the narrowleaf cryptantha, is a member of the Leguminosae (Fabaceae) family.

It is a flowering plant characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Native to the western United States, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats.

Its botanical features make it an important component of arid ecosystems.
