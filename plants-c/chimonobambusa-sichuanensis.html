Chimonobambusa sichuanensis

Chimonobambusa sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to China, particularly in the Sichuan province, where it thrives in temperate and subtropical climates. This bamboo is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing habitat for various wildlife species and contributing to soil stabilization.
