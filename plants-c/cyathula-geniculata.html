Cyathula geniculata

Cyathula geniculata, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and grows in moist, shady environments, often in mountainous regions. It belongs to the family Violaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Cyathula geniculata is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
