Cyathea windischiana

Cyathea windischiana, commonly known as the Windisch's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of fronds that can reach several meters in height, giving it a lush, tropical appearance. Its rhizome is thick and woody, providing structural support and enabling it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Cyathea windischiana is often found in humid forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
