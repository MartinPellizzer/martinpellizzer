Chlorophytum peralbum

Chlorophytum peralbum, commonly known as the silver sword, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features a rosette of narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
