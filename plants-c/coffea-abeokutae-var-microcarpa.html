Coffea abeokutae var. microcarpa

Coffea abeokutae var. microcarpa, commonly known as the Kookoobee coffee.

This variety is native to the highlands of Ethiopia, where it thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soils. It is characterized by its small, red berries and a unique flavor profile that includes notes of citrus and spice. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species within its native range.
