Cleome augustinensis var. goudotii

Cleome augustinensis var. goudotii, commonly known as the African Marvel, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, pink to red, showy flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it typically grows as an upright herbaceous annual. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, drought-tolerant plants.
