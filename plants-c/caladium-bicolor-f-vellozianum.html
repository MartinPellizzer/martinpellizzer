Caladium bicolor f. vellozianum

Caladium bicolor f. vellozianum, commonly known as the Vellozianum caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant patterns of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers partial to full shade. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, with showy flowers that are typically small and white.

It is often cultivated for its decorative value in gardens and as a houseplant.
