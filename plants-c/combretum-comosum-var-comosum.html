Combretum comosum var. comosum

Combretum comosum var. comosum, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble flames, which appear in late summer to early autumn. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape and often have a slightly pubescent surface. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental in tropical and subtropical gardens.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
