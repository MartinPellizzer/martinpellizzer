Cyathea maguirei

Cyathea maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, trunk-like stem covered in a layer of dead fronds. The plant has large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length, giving it a distinctive, elegant appearance. Cyathea maguirei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
