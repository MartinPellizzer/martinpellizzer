Croton venturii

Croton venturii, commonly known as the yellow croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that range from yellow to red and green. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, although it contains toxic compounds that require caution in handling.
