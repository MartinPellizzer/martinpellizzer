Cyrtandra × conferta

Cyrtandra × conferta, commonly known as the hybrid Cyrtandra, is a flowering plant belonging to the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Cyrtandra corymbosa and Cyrtandra pedata, both native to tropical regions of the Pacific. The plant is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests and rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
