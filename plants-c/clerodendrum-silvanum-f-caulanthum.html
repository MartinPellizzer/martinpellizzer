Clerodendrum silvanum f. caulanthum

Clerodendrum silvanum f. caulanthum, commonly known as the creeping clerodendrum, is a compact, trailing shrub native to tropical regions.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its unique growth habit makes it suitable for hanging baskets or as a ground cover.

The botanical characteristics of this species include its aromatic foliage and its ability to thrive in humid, shady conditions.
