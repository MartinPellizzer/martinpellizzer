Cirsium × submedium

Cirsium × submedium, commonly known as the intermediate thistle, is a hybrid plant species within the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers, which typically bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to other regions, including North America. It thrives in a variety of habitats, including meadows, fields, and disturbed soils.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often considered an invasive species in some areas.
