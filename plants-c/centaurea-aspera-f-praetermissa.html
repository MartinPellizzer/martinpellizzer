Centaurea aspera f. praetermissa

Centaurea aspera f. praetermissa, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, sparsely branched stems and narrow, linear leaves. The flowers are typically pale blue to purple, with ray florets and a central disk, forming composite flower heads. This subspecies is native to southern Europe and western Asia, often growing in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
