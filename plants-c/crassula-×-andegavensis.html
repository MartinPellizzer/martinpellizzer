Crassula × andegavensis

Crassula × andegavensis, commonly known as the Andegavensis Crassula, is a hybrid succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a bluish tint, arranged in a rosette formation. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
