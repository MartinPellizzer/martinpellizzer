Chamaecytisus zingeri

Chamaecytisus zingeri, commonly known as the Zinger's broom, is a low-growing shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has leathery, evergreen leaves that are typically gray-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
