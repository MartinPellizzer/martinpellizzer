Cassia chartacea var. tenuicaulis

Cassia chartacea var. tenuicaulis, commonly known as slender-stemmed cassia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are often covered with a fine layer of white or grayish pubescence. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by elongated, narrow pods containing flat, winged seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
