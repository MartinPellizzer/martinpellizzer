Crotalaria tenuis

Crotalaria tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with slender stems and small, yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate and trifoliate, with narrow, elongated leaflets.
