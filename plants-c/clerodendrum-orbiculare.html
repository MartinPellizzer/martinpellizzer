Clerodendrum orbiculare

Clerodendrum orbiculare, commonly known as the globe clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spherical fruit capsules that split open to release seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in warm, humid climates.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.
