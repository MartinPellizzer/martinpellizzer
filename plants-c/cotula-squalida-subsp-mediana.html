Cotula squalida subsp. mediana

Cotula squalida subsp.

mediana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing perennial with small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats. Cotula squalida subsp.

mediana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
