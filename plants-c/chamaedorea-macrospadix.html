Chamaedorea macrospadix

Chamaedorea macrospadix, commonly known as the giant bamboo palm, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Central and South America.

It features tall, slender stems that can grow up to 10 meters in height, with a dense canopy of elongated, leathery leaves. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive, upright growth habit.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and moderate humidity.
