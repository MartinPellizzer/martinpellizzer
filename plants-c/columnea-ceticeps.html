Columnea ceticeps

Columnea ceticeps, commonly known as the crimson flame plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. Columnea ceticeps is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
