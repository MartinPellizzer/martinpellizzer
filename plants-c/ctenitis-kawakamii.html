Ctenitis kawakamii

Ctenitis kawakamii, commonly known as Kawakami's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in lowland forests and along riverbanks. Its rhizome system supports the growth of its massive fronds, which are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants.

The species is valued for its unique appearance and ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
