Corydalis albipetala

It is native to the mountainous regions of Asia, particularly in China and the Himalayas. The plant features delicate, slender stems and produces small, white or pale pink flowers with two lobes that resemble a spurred structure. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Corydalis albipetala is valued in traditional medicine for its purported medicinal properties and is also cultivated for its ornamental value.
