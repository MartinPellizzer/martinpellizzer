Centronia dichromantha

Centronia dichromantha, commonly known as the two-colored centronia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and grayish in appearance. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in clusters that give the plant a delicate, daisy-like appearance.

Centronia dichromantha is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
