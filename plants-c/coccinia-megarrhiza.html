Coccinia megarrhiza

Coccinia megarrhiza, commonly known as the giant leaf begonia, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange or red berries that are edible. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental plant in horticulture.
