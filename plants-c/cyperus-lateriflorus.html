Cyperus lateriflorus

Cyperus lateriflorus, commonly known as the swamp spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and shallow water areas. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flower heads that are often reddish in color. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense stands in suitable habitats.

Cyperus lateriflorus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
