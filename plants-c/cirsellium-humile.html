Cirsellium humile

Cirsellium humile, commonly known as small blueweed, is a low-growing flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically reaches heights of less than 10 inches and features narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Cirsellium humile is often found in association with other wetland plants, contributing to the biodiversity of its ecosystem.
