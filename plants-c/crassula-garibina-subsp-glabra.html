Crassula garibina subsp. glabra

Crassula garibina subsp.

glabra, commonly known as the smooth crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves that are smooth to the touch.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer seasons. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of care in both indoor and outdoor settings.
