Clematis viticella

Clematis viticella, commonly known as the Virgin's Bower, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Europe.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets and produces small, fragrant, white flowers in early summer. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb trellises or other supports. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Clematis viticella is relatively easy to care for and is popular in gardens for its ornamental value.
