Chaetium

Chaetium, commonly known as goat's beard, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Iridaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, grass-like leaves and spikes of showy, often violet or blue flowers. The plant is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils. Chaetium species are often cultivated for their ornamental value in gardens.
