Caladium bicolor var. houbyanym

Caladium bicolor var. houbyanym, commonly known as the "Houbyanym" caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the main ornamental feature, making it a popular choice for gardens and container planting.

It is typically grown as an annual in regions with cold winters.
