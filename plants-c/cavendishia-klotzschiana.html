Cavendishia klotzschiana

Cavendishia klotzschiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect sunlight.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
