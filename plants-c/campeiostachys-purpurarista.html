Campeiostachys purpurarista

Campeiostachys purpurarista, commonly known as the purple-tinged flame grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique floral display and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
