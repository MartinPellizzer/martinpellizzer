Crossandra massaica

Crossandra massaica, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Crossandra massaica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
