Cynoglossum hupehense

Cynoglossum hupehense, commonly known as Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers that resemble bells, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has also been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
