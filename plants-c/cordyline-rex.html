Cordyline rex

Cordyline rex, commonly known as the red dragon tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, upright stems with broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to purple and green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters at the base of the leaves. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.

Cordyline rex thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, requiring moderate watering to prevent root rot.
