Conophytum minutum f. sellatum

Conophytum minutum f. sellatum, commonly known as the dwarf conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its small, rounded leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
