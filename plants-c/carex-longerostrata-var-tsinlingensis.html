Carex longerostrata var. tsinlingensis

Carex longerostrata var. tsinlingensis, commonly known as the Tsingling sedge, is a perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically green to brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in wetland habitats.
