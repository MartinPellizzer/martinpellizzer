Crassula flanaganii

Crassula flanaganii, commonly known as the Flanagan's crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the summer months.

Crassula flanaganii is well adapted to arid environments, making it a hardy and low-maintenance plant for gardeners.
