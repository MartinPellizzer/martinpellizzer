Crassula avasimontana

Crassula avasimontana, commonly known as the White Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a white or pale underside. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
