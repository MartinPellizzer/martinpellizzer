Ceropegia brevilobulata

Ceropegia brevilobulata, commonly known as the narrow-lobed milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Ceropegia brevilobulata is also noted for its ability to propagate through stem cuttings, which is a common method for cultivating it.
