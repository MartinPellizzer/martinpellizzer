Chrysanthemum pinnatifidum

Chrysanthemum pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf chrysanthemum, is a species native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its finely divided, pinnately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in a range of colors, including yellow, white, and pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Chrysanthemum pinnatifidum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and landscapes.
