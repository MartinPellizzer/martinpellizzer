Croton morobensis var. multinervis

Croton morobensis var. multinervis, commonly known as the Multinervous Croton, is a striking tropical plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that display a vibrant green color with prominent veins. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage.

Its unique leaf patterns and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
