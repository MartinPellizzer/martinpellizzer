Cornukaempferia argentifolia

Cornukaempferia argentifolia, commonly known as the silver-leafed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-white variegated leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant belongs to the Araceae family, sharing similarities with other arrowhead plants. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its rhizomes are also used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
