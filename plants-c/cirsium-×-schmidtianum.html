Cirsium × schmidtianum

Cirsium × schmidtianum, commonly known as the Schmidt's thistle, is a hybrid plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pink flower heads that bloom in summer. Native to the eastern United States, this species thrives in moist, disturbed soils such as along roadsides and in wetlands. Its robust growth and showy flowers make it a notable plant in ecological and ornamental contexts.

However, it can be invasive in certain areas, outcompeting native vegetation.
