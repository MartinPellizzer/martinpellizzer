Caulanthus glaucus

Caulanthus glaucus, commonly known as the silverleaf silversword, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of California and Baja California.

It is characterized by its dense, silvery-gray foliage that reflects sunlight, a feature that helps it survive in arid and saline environments.
