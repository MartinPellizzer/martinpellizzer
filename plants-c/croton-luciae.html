Croton luciae

Croton luciae, commonly known as the African croton, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, lobed leaves that often display a vibrant green or red coloration. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact form and dense foliage.

Croton luciae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
