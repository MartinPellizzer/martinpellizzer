Campanula nisyria

Campanula nisyria, commonly known as the Nisyrian bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped flowers, typically blue to violet in color. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy, and it blooms from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Campanula nisyria is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
