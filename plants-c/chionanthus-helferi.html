Chionanthus helferi

Chionanthus helferi, commonly known as the Chinese fringeflower, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Chionanthus helferi is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in landscape design.
