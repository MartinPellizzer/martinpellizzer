Calanthe yunnanensis

Calanthe yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan calanthe, is a perennial orchid native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It features a rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are densely packed at the base. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a purple or red lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Calanthe yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
