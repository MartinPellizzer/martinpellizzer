Crotalaria burkeana var. sparsipila

Crotalaria burkeana var. sparsipila, commonly known as the sparse-flowered crotalaria, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers and compound leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and savannas. It produces seed pods that contain several small, rounded seeds.

This species is also noted for its potential medicinal uses and its role in nitrogen fixation in soil.
