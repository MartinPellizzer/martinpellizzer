Ceratostylis ritaia

Ceratostylis ritaia, commonly known as the Rita's spike orchid, is a small terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single or few flowering spikes that bear small, greenish-white flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in shaded, moist environments. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It is valued for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
