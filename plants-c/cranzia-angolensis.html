Cranzia angolensis

Cranzia angolensis, commonly known as Angola grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Cranzia angolensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
