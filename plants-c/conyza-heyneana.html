Conyza heyneana

Conyza heyneana, commonly known as the common weed or goatweed, is a flowering plant native to Africa, Asia, and Australia.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 1 meter tall, with hairy stems and alternate leaves that are lance-shaped and have serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers in composite heads, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in agricultural fields and along roadsides.

Conyza heyneana is known for its ability to spread rapidly and is considered a weed in many regions.
