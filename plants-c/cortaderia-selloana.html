Cortaderia selloana

Cortaderia selloana, commonly known as pampas grass, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is a perennial grass that can grow up to 6 meters tall, featuring feathery, silver-white plumes that give it a dramatic appearance. The plant has long, narrow leaves that are typically blue-green in color, and it thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. It is known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions, making it a hardy choice for landscapes.

Its unique appearance and structural form make it a popular choice in gardens and as a landscape accent.
