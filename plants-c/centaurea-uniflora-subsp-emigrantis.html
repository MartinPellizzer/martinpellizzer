Centaurea uniflora subsp. emigrantis

Centaurea uniflora subsp.

emigrantis, commonly known as the Emigrant's Centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, violet-blue flowers in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to dry, sunny environments.
