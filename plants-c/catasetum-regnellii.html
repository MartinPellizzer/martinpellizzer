Catasetum regnellii

Catasetum regnellii, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are known for their unique structure, featuring a specialized column that aids in pollination by specific wasps.

Catasetum regnellii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and ease of cultivation.
