Carolinea fastuosa

Carolinea fastuosa, commonly known as the showy bluebell, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in hue.

Carolinea fastuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
