Croton kwebensis

Croton kwebensis, commonly known as the Natal croton, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and white. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its bark is thick and deeply fissured, providing a rugged appearance.

Croton kwebensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
