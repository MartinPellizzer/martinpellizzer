Crotalaria Impressa Subsp. Onobrychis

Onobrychis crotalaria, scientifically known as Crotalaria impressa subsp. onobrychis, is a lesser-known member of the legume family. Native to regions of southern Europe and parts of the Middle East, this plant thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its distinctive appearance features small, yellow flowers and bipinnate leaves that give it a unique visual appeal. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators and improving soil quality. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and ecological significance of this fascinating plant.
