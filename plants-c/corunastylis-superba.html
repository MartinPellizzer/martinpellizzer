Corunastylis superba

Corunastylis superba, commonly known as the shining star lily, is a striking member of the Iridaceae family.

It is native to South Africa and is characterized by its vibrant blue to violet flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, star-shaped blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting flowers and compact growth habit.
