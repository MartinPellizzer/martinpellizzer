Cytisus × watereri

Cytisus × watereri, commonly known as the hybrid firethorn, is a deciduous shrub native to the British Isles.

It features bright orange to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, thorned stems and small, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a hedge plant.

The hybrid is a cross between Cytisus scoparius and Cytisus rubens, resulting in a unique combination of characteristics from both parent species.
