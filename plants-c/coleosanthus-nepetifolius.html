Coleosanthus nepetifolius

Coleosanthus nepetifolius, commonly known as the false arum, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its unique inflorescence.

It features a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, which is typical of the Araceae family. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is native to parts of Central and South America.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor cultivation.
