Cleome deflersii

Cleome deflersii, commonly known as the Mexican spider flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its tall, branching stems and clusters of small, bright yellow or white flowers that resemble spiders, giving it its common name. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Cleome deflersii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
