Clusia marizii

Clusia marizii, commonly known as the Marizii glorybower, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom throughout the year. Clusia marizii is well adapted to humid, warm climates and thrives in partial to full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate various soil conditions.
