Calanthe Masuca Var. Purpurea

Calanthe masuca var. purpurea is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family known for its vibrant purple flowers. Native to the mountainous regions of Japan, this orchid thrives in cool, shaded environments. Its elegant appearance makes it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and intricate floral structures that bloom in spring. Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated with care in controlled settings.
