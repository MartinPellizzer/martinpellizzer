Chamaecrista capensis var. capensis

Chamaecrista capensis var. capensis, commonly known as the Cape burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces distinctive, spiny pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes used in erosion control and as a forage plant.
