Cyathea thysanolepis

Cyathea thysanolepis, commonly known as the silver tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive silver-green coloration on its fronds, which gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Cyathea thysanolepis plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various forest organisms.
