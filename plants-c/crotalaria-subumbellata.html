Crotalaria subumbellata

Crotalaria subumbellata, commonly known as the small-flowered cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by elongated seed pods. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and potential use in agricultural systems.
