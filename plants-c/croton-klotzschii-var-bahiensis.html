Croton klotzschii var. bahiensis

Croton klotzschii var. bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia croton, is a shrub native to Brazil.

It features striking, palmate leaves with a broad, lobed shape and a vibrant green color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and landscape design.
