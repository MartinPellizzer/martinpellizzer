Chuquiraga ulicina subsp. acicularis

Chuquiraga ulicina subsp.

acicularis, commonly known as the needleleaf chuquiraga, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in high-altitude environments and is adapted to cool, temperate climates.

Its botanical structure reflects its evolutionary adaptation to the harsh conditions of its native habitat.
