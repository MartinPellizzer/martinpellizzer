Cabucala multiflora

Cabucala multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Cabucala multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
