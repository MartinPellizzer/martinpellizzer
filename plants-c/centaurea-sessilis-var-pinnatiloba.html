Centaurea sessilis var. pinnatiloba

Centaurea sessilis var. pinnatiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leafed centaury, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright, branching stems covered in fine, grayish hairs. The leaves are narrow and pinnately lobed, giving the plant its varietal name. Its flowers are typically blue to purple, arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.
