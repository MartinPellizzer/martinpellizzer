Colymbada elegantissima

Colymbada elegantissima, commonly known as the elegant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, supported by long, flexible petioles. Its flowers are showy, with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in late summer. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater habitats.

It plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing shelter for fish and invertebrates.
