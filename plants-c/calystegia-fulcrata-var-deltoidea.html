Calystegia fulcrata var. deltoidea

Calystegia fulcrata var. deltoidea, commonly known as the deltoid morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms that typically open in the morning, hence its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and along stream banks.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to climb using its tendrils.
