Clerodendrum oreadum

Clerodendrum oreadum, commonly known as the mountain clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and violet. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are clustered in terminal panicles and are known to bloom during the summer months.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
