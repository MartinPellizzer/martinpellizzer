Cephalocereus arrabidae var. tilophorus

Cephalocereus arrabidae var. tilophorus, commonly known as the Arrabida cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive crown of spines and a slow growth rate. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming at night. It is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments with minimal rainfall.

Its unique morphology and nocturnal flowering make it a striking and resilient plant in its native habitat.
