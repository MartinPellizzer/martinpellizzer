Cornus × dunbarii

Cornus × dunbarii, commonly known as the Dunbar cornel.

It is a hybrid shrub that belongs to the Cornaceae family, characterized by its ornamental value and hardy nature. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically white or pink, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.

The fruit is a small, red drupe that ripens in late summer, adding visual interest to the plant in the fall.
