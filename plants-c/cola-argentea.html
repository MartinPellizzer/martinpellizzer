Cola argentea

Cola argentea, commonly known as silver cola, is a flowering plant native to western Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage due to a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its fruits are capsule-like structures containing seeds used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has some economic uses in local communities.
