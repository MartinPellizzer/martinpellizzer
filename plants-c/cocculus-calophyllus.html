Cocculus calophyllus

Cocculus calophyllus, commonly known as the beautiful cocculus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its woody vines and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, fleshy berries. Its botanical features include a complex root system and the presence of alkaloids in its tissues.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
