Cereus mojavensis var. zuniensis

Cereus mojavensis var. zuniensis, commonly known as the Zuni cactus, is a species native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is a columnar cactus with ribbed stems that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces large, white, night-blooming flowers that are typically borne at the base of the stem. It has a shallow root system adapted to arid conditions and thrives in full sun.

Its spines are relatively short and sparse, providing some protection while allowing for air circulation.
