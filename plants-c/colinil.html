Colinil

Colinil, commonly known as the golden spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its bright yellow sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation. The plant features alternate, simple leaves that are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are clustered in complex inflorescences called cyathia.

Colinil is native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and is often found growing in rocky soils.
