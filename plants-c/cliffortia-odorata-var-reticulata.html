Cliffortia odorata var. reticulata

Cliffortia odorata var. reticulata, commonly known as the sweet-scented rock rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are small, oval-shaped, and covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
