Clitoria stipularis f. latifolia

Clitoria stipularis f. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive butterfly-shaped flowers.

It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant features trifoliate leaves with broad, ovate leaflets and produces pods that contain edible seeds. Its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen make it a valuable crop for agricultural systems.

Additionally, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant purple and pink floral displays.
