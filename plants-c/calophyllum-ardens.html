Calophyllum ardens

Calophyllum ardens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The plant belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens and parks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is valued for both its aesthetic appeal and its ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
