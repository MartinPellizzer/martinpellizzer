Cynosorchis

Cynosorchis, commonly known as the African orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to various regions across Africa, particularly in tropical and subtropical areas. Cynosorchis species typically have showy, fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and purple. They are epiphytic or lithophytic, meaning they grow on other plants or rocks, and they require high humidity and indirect light to thrive.

The genus is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and striking floral display.
