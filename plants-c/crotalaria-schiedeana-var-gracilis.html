Crotalaria schiedeana var. gracilis

Crotalaria schiedeana var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical bean-like pods and compound leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are often clustered in racemes, and its foliage is usually trifoliate. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its nitrogen-fixing ability, it can be used in agricultural systems to improve soil fertility.
