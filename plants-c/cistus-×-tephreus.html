Cistus × tephreus

Cistus × tephreus, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is well adapted to rocky and dry environments, thriving in full sun and well-drained soils. Its essential oils, derived from the leaves and flowers, are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

Cistus × tephreus is often used in traditional herbal remedies and as a source of natural perfumes.
