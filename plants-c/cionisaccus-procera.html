Cionisaccus procera

Cionisaccus procera, commonly known as the giant sponge plant, is a unique species belonging to the family Cionidae.

It is characterized by its large, spongy, and porous structure, which allows it to absorb and retain water efficiently. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its growth is slow, and it can reach considerable sizes over time, making it a striking feature in its native habitat.

The plant's structure also provides a habitat for various microorganisms and small aquatic life.
