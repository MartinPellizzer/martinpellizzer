Cistus × panormitanus

Cistus × panormitanus, commonly known as the hybrid rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited to arid and semi-arid environments. Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to local biodiversity.

The plant's resilience and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
