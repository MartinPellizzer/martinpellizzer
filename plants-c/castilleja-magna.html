Castilleja magna

Castilleja magna, commonly known as the largeleaf Indian paintbrush, is a hemiparasitic flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Orobanchaceae family and is characterized by its showy, colorful bracts that resemble paintbrushes.
