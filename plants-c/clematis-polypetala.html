Clematis polypetala

Clematis polypetala, commonly known as the Chinese clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white to lilac. The plant has a climbing habit and can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

Clematis polypetala is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
