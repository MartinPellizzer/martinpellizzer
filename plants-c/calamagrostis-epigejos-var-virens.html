Calamagrostis epigejos var. virens

Calamagrostis epigejos var. virens, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features narrow, silver-blue leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Calamagrostis epigejos var. virens is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
