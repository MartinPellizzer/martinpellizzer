Ceropegia velutina

Ceropegia velutina, commonly known as the velvet leaf or string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favored ornamental plant.
