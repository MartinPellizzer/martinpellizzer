Cassia rogeonii

Cassia rogeonii, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Cassia rogeonii is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical and subtropical climates.
