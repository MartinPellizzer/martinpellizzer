Clausena dentata var. pubescens

Clausena dentata var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy sour orange, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, hairy leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, orange fruits with a tart flavor, often used in traditional medicine and cuisine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
