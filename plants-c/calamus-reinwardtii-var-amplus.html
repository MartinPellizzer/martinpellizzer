Calamus reinwardtii var. amplus

Calamus reinwardtii var. amplus, commonly known as the broad-leaved calamus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It thrives in wet, swampy habitats and is often found in mangrove forests.
