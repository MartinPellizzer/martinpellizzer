Cyclamen persicum var. giganteum

Cyclamen persicum var. giganteum, commonly known as the giant cyclamen, is a striking hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped foliage and vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant produces tuberous roots that store nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal climates. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Cyclamen persicum var. giganteum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and seasonal blooming cycle.
