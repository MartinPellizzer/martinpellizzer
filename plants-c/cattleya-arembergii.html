Cattleya arembergii

Cattleya arembergii, commonly known as the Areberg's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous blossoms, making it a striking addition to any garden or collection. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cattleya arembergii is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
