Conophytum germanum

Conophytum germanum, commonly known as the German dwarf cholla, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water. Conophytum germanum is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
