Capsicum scolnikianum

Capsicum scolnikianum, commonly known as the yellow bell pepper.

This plant is native to South America and belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae. It is characterized by its elongated, yellow fruit that matures to orange or red. The plant typically grows as an annual herb with hairy stems and lobed leaves.

It is cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary applications.
