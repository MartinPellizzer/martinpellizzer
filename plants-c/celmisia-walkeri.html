Celmisia walkeri

Celmisia walkeri, commonly known as the Stewart Island celmisia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in the understorey of forests.
