Croton excisus

Croton excisus, commonly known as the African croton, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or yellow. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Croton excisus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant foliage.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
