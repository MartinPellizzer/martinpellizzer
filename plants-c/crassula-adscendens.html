Crassula adscendens

Crassula adscendens, commonly known as the Christmas Jade, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture, adapted for storing water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for indoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for decorative purposes.
