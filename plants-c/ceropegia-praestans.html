Ceropegia praestans

Ceropegia praestans, commonly known as the starfish fig, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and fleshy, succulent stems. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pale green to white in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with a fine pubescence.

Ceropegia praestans is adapted to arid environments, making it a popular choice for indoor and rock garden cultivation.
