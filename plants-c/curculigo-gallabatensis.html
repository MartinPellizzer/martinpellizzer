Curculigo gallabatensis

Curculigo gallabatensis, commonly known as the Borneo arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, which includes orchids, and is characterized by its unique arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in tropical rainforests.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
