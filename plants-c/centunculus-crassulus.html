Centunculus crassulus

Centunculus crassulus, commonly known as the broad-leaved mouse-ear.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia, characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, rounded leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white or grayish down. Its flowers are tiny, clustered in dense heads, and usually appear in shades of pink or white.

Centunculus crassulus thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and rocky slopes.
