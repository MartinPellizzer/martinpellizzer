Cynodon barberi

Cynodon barberi, commonly known as the Cape buffalo grass, is a perennial grass native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear minute, wind-pollinated flowers. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy species with low water requirements.

Cynodon barberi is often used in landscaping and erosion control due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
