Crotalaria reflexa

Crotalaria reflexa, commonly known as the curlleaf cowpea, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its yellow flowers and palmate leaves, which are divided into three to five leaflets. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family. It produces small, cylindrical pods that contain several seeds.

Crotalaria reflexa is often used in traditional medicine and can also serve as a forage crop for livestock.
