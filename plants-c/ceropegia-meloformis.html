Ceropegia meloformis

Ceropegia meloformis, commonly known as the Melon-shaped Ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and succulent stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
