Croton moustiquensis

Croton moustiquensis, commonly known as the Moustique croton, is a flowering plant native to Central America and the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that can range from bright yellow to deep red, depending on the variety. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
