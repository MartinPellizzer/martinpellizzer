Cicer straussii

Cicer straussii, commonly known as the Strauss vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are often used as forage for livestock. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.

Cicer straussii is also valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
