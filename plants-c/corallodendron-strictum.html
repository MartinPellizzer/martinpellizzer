Corallodendron strictum

Corallodendron strictum, commonly known as the rigid coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The plant is adapted to tolerate salt spray and is often found in dune environments.
