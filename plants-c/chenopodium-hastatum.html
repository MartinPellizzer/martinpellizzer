Chenopodium hastatum

Chenopodium hastatum, commonly known as rough goosefoot, is a flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to North America and is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in disturbed soils and can be found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas.

Chenopodium hastatum is often used in traditional medicine and is also a common weed in agricultural settings.
