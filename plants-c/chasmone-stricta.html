Chasmone stricta

Chasmone stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved chasmone, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Chasmone stricta is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
