Conyza necessaria

Conyza necessaria, commonly known as the yellow starburst, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk, which is typical of the family. It thrives in open, sunny habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Conyza necessaria is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and vegetative propagation.
