Coronidium elatum subsp. minus

Coronidium elatum subsp.

minus, commonly known as the lesser crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
