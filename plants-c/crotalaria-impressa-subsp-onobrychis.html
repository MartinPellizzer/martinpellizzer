Crotalaria impressa subsp. onobrychis

Crotalaria impressa subsp.

onobrychis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed crotalaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in racemes.

The plant is native to parts of Africa and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has a taproot system and produces seeds in pods, which are typically hairy and contain several small seeds.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and floral display.
