Cyclantheropsis madagascariensis

Cyclantheropsis madagascariensis, commonly known as the Madagascar horned cucumber, is a unique member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, and somewhat horn-like fruits. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine with palmate leaves and produces yellow flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in humid, shaded environments.
