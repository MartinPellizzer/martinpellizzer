Chrysanthemum gayanum var. depressum

Chrysanthemum gayanum var. depressum, commonly known as the dwarf chrysanthemum, is a compact, herbaceous perennial native to Sri Lanka.

It features daisy-like flowers with white to pale yellow petals and a yellow disc, blooming in late summer to early autumn. The plant has a low-growing habit, with sturdy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value lies in its delicate flowers and ability to attract pollinators.
