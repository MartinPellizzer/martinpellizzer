Croton verbascifolius

Croton verbascifolius, commonly known as the woolly croton, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in the summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
