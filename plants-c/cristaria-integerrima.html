Cristaria integerrima

Cristaria integerrima, commonly known as the smooth water lily, is a aquatic plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves that are typically smooth and waxy. The plant produces white flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. Its roots anchor it in shallow water, while its rhizomes spread horizontally beneath the surface.

Cristaria integerrima plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
