Calochilus psednus

Calochilus psednus, commonly known as the Narrow-leafed Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and produces small, pale green or yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in dry habitats. It belongs to the genus Calochilus, which includes several other terrestrial orchids.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in coastal or inland regions of southern Australia.
