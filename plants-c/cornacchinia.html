Cornacchinia

Cornacchinia, commonly known as the New Zealand broom.

It is a shrub belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is native to New Zealand. The plant features small, yellow flowers and compound leaves, which are typical of many legumes. It thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in areas with mild climates.

Cornacchinia is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
