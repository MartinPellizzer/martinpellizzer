Columnea guatemalensis

Columnea guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan flame flower, is a climbing plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Columnea guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
