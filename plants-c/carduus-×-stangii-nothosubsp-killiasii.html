Carduus × stangii nothosubsp. killiasii

Carduus × stangii nothosubsp.

killiasii, commonly known as the Killias thistle, is a hybrid plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and a distinctive purple to pink flower head.

The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, showing adaptability to arid environments. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes many other thistle and daisy species.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural habitats.
