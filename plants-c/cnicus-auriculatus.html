Cnicus auriculatus

Cnicus auriculatus, commonly known as the yellow cinquefoil, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to August. The plant has basal leaves that are lobed and often covered in fine hairs, while the stems are usually hairy and upright. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas.

Cnicus auriculatus is a perennial herb that can grow up to 60 cm tall, with a distinctive appearance that makes it easily identifiable.
