Chrysanthemum carnosulum

Chrysanthemum carnosulum, commonly known as the woolly chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it is more often found in its natural habitat.
