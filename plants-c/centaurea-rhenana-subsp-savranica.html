Centaurea rhenana subsp. savranica

Centaurea rhenana subsp.

savranica, commonly known as the Savran centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and serrated leaves.

The plant typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
