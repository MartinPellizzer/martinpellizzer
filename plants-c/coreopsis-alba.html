Coreopsis alba

Coreopsis alba, commonly known as white coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and often branching.
