Centaurea dufourii var. humilis

Centaurea dufourii var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers with purple-tinged bracts, typically blooming from spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
