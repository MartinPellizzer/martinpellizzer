Cymboglossum maculiflorum

Cymboglossum maculiflorum, commonly known as the spotted-flowered cymboglossum, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. The plant is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are usually ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
