Ceropegia cordiloba

Ceropegia cordiloba, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, often with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia cordiloba is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
