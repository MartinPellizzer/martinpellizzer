Coelogyne mirabilis

Coelogyne mirabilis, commonly known as the marvel orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, long-lasting flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, yellow, and red. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-like and arranged in a fan shape, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Coelogyne mirabilis is often cultivated for its beauty and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
