Cattleya × sapho

Cattleya × sapho, commonly known as the Sapho Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid resulting from the cross between Cattleya labiata and Cattleya bicolor.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features large, showy flowers with a vibrant color palette, often displaying shades of pink, red, and white. Its pseudobulbs are robust, and it produces multiple inflorescences with numerous blooms during the growing season.

Cattleya × sapho is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and ease of cultivation.
