Calamovilfa longifolia var. magna

Calamovilfa longifolia var. magna, commonly known as the giant feather grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to certain regions of South America.

It features tall, slender inflorescences that resemble plumes, giving it a graceful and airy appearance. The plant typically grows to heights of up to 2 meters, making it a dramatic focal point in gardens. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silvery-blue hue that adds visual interest.

It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
