Cyathea macrosora var. reginae

Cyathea macrosora var. reginae, commonly known as the Queen's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its fronds can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, contributing to its ornamental value.

The species is noted for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a prized specimen in horticulture.
