Caladium bicolor var. surinamense

Caladium bicolor var. surinamense, commonly known as the Surinam arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that display vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in gardens and as a houseplant.

It is typically propagated through tuberous roots or by dividing the rhizomes.
