Ciconium bentinckianum

Ciconium bentinckianum, commonly known as the African elephant's foot, is a striking plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fleshy, basal leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in diameter, forming a dense rosette. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences that bear small, yellowish-green flowers. It is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
