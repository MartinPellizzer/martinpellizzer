Clematis × willisonii

Clematis × willisonii, commonly known as the Willisonii Clematis, is a hybrid shrub native to the United States.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and long, slender stems that can climb or trail. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, serrated leaves that are dark green and provide good contrast to the flowers.

Clematis × willisonii is relatively low maintenance but benefits from regular pruning to encourage flowering and maintain its shape.
