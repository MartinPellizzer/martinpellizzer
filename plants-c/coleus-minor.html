Coleus minor

Coleus minor, commonly known as the dwarf coleus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other herbs and shrubs known for their aromatic properties. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, with leaves that come in shades of red, purple, and green. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters.

Coleus minor is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of cultivation.
