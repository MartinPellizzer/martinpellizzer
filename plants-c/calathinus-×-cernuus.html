Calathinus × cernuus

Calathinus × cernuus, commonly known as the hybrid ivy or creeping ivy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Araliaceae family.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are typically followed by orange-red berries. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a ground cover or in hanging baskets.
