Cattleya esalqueana

Cattleya esalqueana, commonly known as the Esalqueana orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces large, showy blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Cattleya esalqueana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
