Crinum corradii

Crinum corradii, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often fragrant. The plant has strap-like leaves that are typically green and grow in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Crinum corradii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown in gardens or as a potted plant.
