Crepidium integrilabium

Crepidium integrilabium, commonly known as the smooth-lip orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and have a unique, intricate structure with a spurred lip.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
