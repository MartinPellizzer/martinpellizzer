Camissonia eastwoodiae

Camissonia eastwoodiae, commonly known as Eastwood's camissonia, is a rare flowering plant native to the central coast of California.

It is a member of the Camissoniaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal scrub habitats.
