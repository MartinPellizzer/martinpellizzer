Codiaeum weismannii

Codiaeum weismannii, commonly known as the flameleaf or croton plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from red, orange, and yellow to green and purple. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a velvety texture, often with a waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy.

This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.
