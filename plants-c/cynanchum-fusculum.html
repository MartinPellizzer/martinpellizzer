Cynanchum fusculum

Cynanchum fusculum, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds, and is characterized by its milky sap. The plant typically grows in open woods and along stream banks, preferring moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive hooded structure, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Cynanchum fusculum is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
