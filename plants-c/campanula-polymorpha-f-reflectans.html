Campanula polymorpha f. reflectans

Campanula polymorpha f. reflectans, commonly known as the reflected bellflower, is a hybrid perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of basal leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly lobed appearance.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
