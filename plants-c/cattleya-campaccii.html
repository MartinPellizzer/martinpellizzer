Cattleya campaccii

Cattleya campaccii, commonly known as the Campaccii orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet-blue blooms with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil.

Cattleya campaccii is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its striking appearance.
