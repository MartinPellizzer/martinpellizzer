Cnemidostachys oleoides

Cnemidostachys oleoides, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, shrub-like species that produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its leathery, aromatic leaves that emit a mild, buttery scent. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
