Cochlidium paucinervatum

Cochlidium paucinervatum, commonly known as the slender zigzag snail, is a species of air-breathing land snail.

It belongs to the family Cochlididae and is characterized by its small, elongated shell with a distinctive zigzag pattern. The shell is typically light brown to yellowish in color and has a smooth surface with fine spiral ridges. This species is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of Thailand and Vietnam.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.
