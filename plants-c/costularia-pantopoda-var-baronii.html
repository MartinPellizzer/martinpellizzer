Costularia pantopoda var. baronii

Costularia pantopoda var. baronii, commonly known as the Baron's costularia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense clusters, often with narrow, linear leaves. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its compact growth and seasonal blooms.
