Calamagrostis epigejos var. extremiorientalis

Calamagrostis epigejos var. extremiorientalis, commonly known as the Chinese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, silver-gray leaves that are often tinged with white, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces feathery, light-green to golden panicles that bloom in late summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscape design.
