Cattleya gloriosa

Cattleya gloriosa, commonly known as the noble orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has long, slender pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves that are arranged in a fan shape. Its flowers are highly fragrant and bloom in late summer or early autumn.

Cattleya gloriosa is prized for its beauty and is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
