Cryptostylis taiwaniana

Cryptostylis taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan cryptostylis, is a rare orchid native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native range.
