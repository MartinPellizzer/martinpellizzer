Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. tetrandrum

Chrysosplenium alternifolium var. tetrandrum, commonly known as the four-veined chrysosplenium, is a herbaceous perennial native to parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in small, ovate leaves with a distinctive four-veined pattern. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
