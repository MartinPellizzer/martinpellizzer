Cirsium × kerneri

Cirsium × kerneri, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a rosette of basal leaves and produces a tall flowering stem with numerous flower heads.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
