Coelogyne viscosa

Coelogyne viscosa, commonly known as the sticky coelogyne, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect pseudobulbs and long, linear leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne viscosa is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
