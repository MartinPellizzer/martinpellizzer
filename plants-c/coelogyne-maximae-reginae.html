Coelogyne maximae-reginae

Coelogyne maximae-reginae, commonly known as the Queen of the Orchids, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and yellow. The flowers are highly ornamental and often have a distinctive, intricate pattern on their petals and sepals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne maximae-reginae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
