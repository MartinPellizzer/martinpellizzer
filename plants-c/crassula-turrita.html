Crassula turrita

Crassula turrita, commonly known as the Turrited Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves arranged in a rosette pattern, which help it store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
