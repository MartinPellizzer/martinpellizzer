Cyanus dentato-folius

Cyanus dentato-folius, commonly known as the Guernsey carnation, is a flowering plant native to the island of Guernsey.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its bright blue or purple flowers with a distinctive central disk. The plant has serrated, toothed leaves that give it its scientific name, and it typically grows in rocky or coastal environments.
