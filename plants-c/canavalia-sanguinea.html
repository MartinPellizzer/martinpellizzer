Canavalia sanguinea

Canavalia sanguinea, commonly known as red bean plant or blood bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive red or purple flowers. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds that are often used in culinary applications. Canavalia sanguinea is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a versatile plant for both ornamental and practical uses.
