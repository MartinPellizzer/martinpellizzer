Calanthe discolor f. kanashiroi

Calanthe discolor f. kanashiroi, commonly known as the Kanashiro's Calanthe, is a rare and ornamental orchid species native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, pale pink to white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as mountain forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture that helps retain moisture.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical research and conservation efforts.
