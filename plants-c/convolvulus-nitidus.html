Convolvulus nitidus

Convolvulus nitidus, commonly known as the smooth morning glory, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, twining stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
