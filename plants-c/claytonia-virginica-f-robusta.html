Claytonia virginica f. robusta

Claytonia virginica f. robusta, commonly known as the robust Claytonia, is a variant of the spring beauty plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, woodland habitats. The plant features broad, ovate leaves with a waxy coating and produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Its foliage is often more robust and less hairy compared to the typical form.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
