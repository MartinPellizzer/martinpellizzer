Coreopsis ternata

Coreopsis ternata, commonly known as tickseed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet. The plant features showy, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers, which bloom from late spring to early fall. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its rustic appearance.

Coreopsis ternata is known for its hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
