Cynanchum jenmanii

Cynanchum jenmanii, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed or Jenman's milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers with a distinctive corolla, and its latex sap contains toxic compounds. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used in traditional Chinese medicine.
