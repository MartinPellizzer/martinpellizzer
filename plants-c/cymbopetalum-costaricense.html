Cymbopetalum costaricense

Cymbopetalum costaricense, commonly known as the flame violet, is a striking tropical plant native to Costa Rica and Panama.

It is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color.
