Chavica canaliculata

Chavica canaliculata, commonly known as the Persian melon or sweet melon, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, green fruits resembling small melons. The fruit has a sweet, aromatic flavor and is often used in culinary applications.
