Connarus igneus

Connarus igneus, commonly known as the flame tree, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a fiery display during the wet season. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, pointed leaflets that give it a unique appearance. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark and leaves of Connarus igneus have been used in traditional medicine for their potential therapeutic properties.
