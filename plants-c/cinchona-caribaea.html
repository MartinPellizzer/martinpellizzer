Cinchona caribaea

Cinchona caribaea, commonly known as the Carib or Carib bean, is a species of evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that contain seeds, and it thrives in humid, tropical climates.
