Ceropegia aristolochioides subsp. deflersiana

Ceropegia aristolochioides subsp.

deflersiana, commonly known as the Ghost Plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, tubular stems that often appear as if they are floating or suspended in air.

The plant produces small, pale green or white flowers that are typically borne on short, erect shoots. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
