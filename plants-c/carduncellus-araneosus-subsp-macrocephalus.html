Carduncellus araneosus subsp. macrocephalus

Carduncellus araneosus subsp.

macrocephalus, commonly known as the giant spurge. This plant is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa, characterized by its thick, fleshy stems and leaves.

It produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically followed by bright red berries. The species is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its stems and leaves.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils, thriving in full sun.
