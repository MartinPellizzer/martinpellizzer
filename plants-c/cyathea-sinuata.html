Cyathea sinuata

Cyathea sinuata, commonly known as the sinuous tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feather-like fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests. Its fronds are pinnately compound, with a distinctive sinuous or wavy margin that gives it its common name.

Cyathea sinuata is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
