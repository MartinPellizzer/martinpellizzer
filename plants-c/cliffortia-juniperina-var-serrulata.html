Cliffortia juniperina var. serrulata

Cliffortia juniperina var. serrulata, commonly known as the serrated cliffortia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils, often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and serrated, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with good drainage.
