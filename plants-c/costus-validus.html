Costus validus

Costus validus, commonly known as the red ginger or flame ginger, is a tropical plant belonging to the Zingiberaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America. The plant features tall, narrow leaves and produces striking, red-orange inflorescences that resemble ginger flowers. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Costus validus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
