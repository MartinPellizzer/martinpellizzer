Cattleya elongata var. tenebrosa

Cattleya elongata var. tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark-stemmed cattleya, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its dark green, often almost black, stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It prefers warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
