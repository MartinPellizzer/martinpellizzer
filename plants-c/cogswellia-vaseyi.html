Cogswellia vaseyi

Cogswellia vaseyi, commonly known as the Hottentot Fig, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Crassulaceae and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces distinctive, fig-like fruits that are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.
