Chenopodium ambrosioides subsp. retusum

Chenopodium ambrosioides subsp.

retusum, commonly known as rough goosefoot, is a herbaceous plant native to North America. It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves with serrated edges.

The plant produces small, greenish flowers in clusters, and its seeds are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions.
