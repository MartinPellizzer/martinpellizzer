Cynoglossum mexicanum

Cynoglossum mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican dead nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to Mexico and parts of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, lance-shaped leaves and blue or purple flowers. Cynoglossum mexicanum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
