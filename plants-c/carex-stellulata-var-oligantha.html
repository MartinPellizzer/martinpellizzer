Carex stellulata var. oligantha

Carex stellulata var. oligantha, commonly known as star sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late summer. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological restoration and wetland habitats.
