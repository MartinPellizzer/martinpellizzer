Cyclamen persicum f. puniceum

Cyclamen persicum f. puniceum, commonly known as the pink cyclamen, is a cultivar of the Persian cyclamen, characterized by its vibrant pink to red foliage and flowers.

This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a velvety texture, and the flowers bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or outdoor use during the winter months.

Cyclamen persicum f. puniceum requires well-draining soil and partial shade to flourish.
