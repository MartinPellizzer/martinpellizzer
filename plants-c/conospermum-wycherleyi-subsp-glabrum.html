Conospermum wycherleyi subsp. glabrum

Conospermum wycherleyi subsp.

glabrum, commonly known as the Smooth Conospermum, is a shrub native to Western Australia. It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically narrow and linear.

The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which appear in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in sandy or gravelly habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
