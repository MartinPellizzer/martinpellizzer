Chenopodium centrorubrum

Chenopodium centrorubrum, commonly known as redroot goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive reddish roots and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and can be found in wetlands and along stream banks. It produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Chenopodium centrorubrum is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
