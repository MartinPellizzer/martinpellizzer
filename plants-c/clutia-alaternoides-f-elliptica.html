Clutia alaternoides f. elliptica

Clutia alaternoides f. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic clutia, is a variant of the clutia plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by red, fleshy berries containing seeds.
