Coelogyne hookeriana var. brachyglossa

Coelogyne hookeriana var. brachyglossa, commonly known as the Hooker's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that produce multiple leaves and flower spikes. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers with white to pale yellow petals and a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
