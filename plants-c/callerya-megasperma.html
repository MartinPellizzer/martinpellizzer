Callerya megasperma

Callerya megasperma, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, pendulous clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its showy blooms and vigorous growth habit.

However, it can be invasive in some regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
