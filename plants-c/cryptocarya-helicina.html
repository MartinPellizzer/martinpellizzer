Cryptocarya helicina

Cryptocarya helicina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sassafras, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves that are often aromatic. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits.

Cryptocarya helicina is typically found in coastal and lowland forest habitats, where it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.
