Crassula ramuliflora var. transvaalensis

Crassula ramuliflora var. transvaalensis, commonly known as the Transvaal crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, bluish-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the edges. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or red during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its slow growth rate and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
