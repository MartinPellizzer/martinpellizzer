Campanula cochleariifolia var. foliosa

Campanula cochleariifolia var. foliosa, commonly known as the leafy bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Mediterranean region.

It features striking, bell-shaped blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and slightly lobed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
