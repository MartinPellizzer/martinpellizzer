Cotyledon breviflora f. unicolor

Cotyledon breviflora f. unicolor, commonly known as the single-colored cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically green with a striking white or pale variegation. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
