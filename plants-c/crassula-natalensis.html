Crassula natalensis

Crassula natalensis, commonly known as the Natal Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring or summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.

Crassula natalensis prefers well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
