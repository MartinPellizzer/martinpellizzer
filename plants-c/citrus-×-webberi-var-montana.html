Citrus × webberi var. montana

Citrus × webberi var. montana, commonly known as the Montane Kaffir lime.

This variety is a hybrid citrus plant characterized by its distinct, aromatic leaves and fruit. The leaves are typically broad, with a rough, textured surface and a strong citrus scent. The fruit is generally small, with a thick rind and a segmented interior.

It is cultivated for its aromatic foliage and is often used in culinary and ornamental applications.
