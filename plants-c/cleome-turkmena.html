Cleome turkmena

Cleome turkmena, commonly known as Turkmen cleome, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and distinctive yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for dry, arid environments.

Cleome turkmena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
