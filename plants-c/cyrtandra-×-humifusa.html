Cyrtandra × humifusa

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically produces small, violet or white flowers that are often borne in clusters. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and covered with a fine layer of pubescence.
