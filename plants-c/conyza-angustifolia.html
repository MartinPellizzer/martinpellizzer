Conyza angustifolia

Conyza angustifolia, commonly known as slenderleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in disturbed habitats such as roadsides, fields, and waste areas. The plant features slender, branching stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in composite heads, typical of the Asteraceae family.

It produces seeds that are dispersed by wind, contributing to its widespread distribution.
