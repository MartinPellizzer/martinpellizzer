Caladenia dorrienii

Caladenia dorrienii, commonly known as the Dorrien's spider orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and its distinctive, spider-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant produces its flowers on a single stem, with the petals and sepals forming a narrow, elongated shape. Caladenia dorrienii is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in sandy soils and is often found in open habitats.
