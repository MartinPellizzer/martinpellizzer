Chrysosplenium pilosum var. aureobracteatum

Chrysosplenium pilosum var. aureobracteatum, commonly known as the golden bract chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It features dense, mat-forming foliage with hairy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers clustered in umbels, which are often surrounded by golden bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant foliage and delicate floral display.
