Cymbidium adenoglossum

Cymbidium adenoglossum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive white or pale pink lip.

Cymbidium adenoglossum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
