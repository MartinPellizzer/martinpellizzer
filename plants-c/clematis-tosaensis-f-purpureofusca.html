Clematis tosaensis f. purpureofusca

Clematis tosaensis f. purpureofusca, commonly known as the purple-barked clematis, is a compact shrub native to Japan.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has distinctive, dark purple bark that adds visual interest throughout the year. Its leaves are typically ovate with a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental foliage and blooms.
