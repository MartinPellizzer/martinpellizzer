Campanula stricta var. jasionifolia

Campanula stricta var. jasionifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are narrow and lanceolate, often with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
