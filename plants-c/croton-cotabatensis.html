Croton cotabatensis

Croton cotabatensis, commonly known as the Cotabatensis Croton, is a shrub or small tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often colorful foliage. The plant features alternate, simple leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a characteristic feature of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Croton cotabatensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
