Crassula Setulosa Var. Curta

Crassula setulosa var. curta is a compact, succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features small, fleshy leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. This variety is prized for its drought tolerance and ease of care, making it ideal for indoor or outdoor gardens. Its unique shape and vibrant color make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts. With proper light and minimal watering, it thrives and adds a touch of elegance to any space.
