Centaurea Ovina Subsp. Besseriana

Centaurea ovina subsp. besseriana is a lesser-known but fascinating member of the sunflower family. This subspecies is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers and fine, silver-green foliage. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and ecosystems. Its resilience in harsh environments makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
