Chamaedorea hageniorum

Chamaedorea hageniorum, commonly known as the Mexican evergreen or Hagen's bamboo, is a member of the Arecaceae family.

It is a compact, slow-growing plant that typically reaches a height of about 3 to 5 feet. Native to the tropical regions of Mexico and Central America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with indirect light. The plant features slender, upright stems and feather-like leaves that give it a graceful, elegant appearance.

It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying qualities.
