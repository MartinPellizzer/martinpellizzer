Cattleya eldorado var. virginalis

Cattleya eldorado var. virginalis, commonly known as the Eldorado White Cattleya, is a rare and striking orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers with a subtle pinkish hue and a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water in the dry seasons.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
