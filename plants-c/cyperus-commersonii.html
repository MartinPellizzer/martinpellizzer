Cyperus commersonii

Cyperus commersonii, commonly known as the spiny flatsedge, is a perennial grass-like plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like clusters of flowers. It thrives in wet or marshy environments, often found in coastal areas and freshwater habitats.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily and form dense colonies.
