Cyclobalanopsis dongfangensis

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, chestnuts, and beeches. The plant is characterized by its dense, hard wood and broad, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by edible nuts enclosed in a cupule.
