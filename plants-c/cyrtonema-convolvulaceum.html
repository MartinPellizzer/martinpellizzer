Cyrtonema convolvulaceum

Cyrtonema convolvulaceum, commonly known as the Chinese morning glory, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cyrtonema convolvulaceum is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
