Centaurea angulosa

Centaurea angulosa, commonly known as angular centaury, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, spiny leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with red centers, which bloom from late spring to early summer. It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Centaurea angulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
