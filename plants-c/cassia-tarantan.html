Cassia tarantan

Cassia tarantan, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences that bloom in summer. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. Cassia tarantan thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
