Cissampelos hernandiifolia

Cissampelos hernandiifolia, commonly known as the African moonvine, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated, fleshy fruits. Its roots and leaves contain compounds with potential medicinal properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
