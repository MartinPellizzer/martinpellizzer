Cephalomanes zollingeri

It is characterized by its translucent, gelatinous body and vibrant, colorful patterns that serve as camouflage and warning signals. The animal lacks a shell and is often found in marine environments, particularly in the Indo-Pacific region. Its diet primarily consists of sponges, which it consumes using its radula.
