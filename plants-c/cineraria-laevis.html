Cineraria laevis

Cineraria laevis, commonly known as the smooth cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, woolly leaves and showy blue or violet flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Cineraria laevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
