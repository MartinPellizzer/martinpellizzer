Calathea lateralis

Calathea lateralis, commonly known as the silver sword calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green patterns on its broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. Its leaves are glossy and often have a waxy texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Calathea lateralis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
