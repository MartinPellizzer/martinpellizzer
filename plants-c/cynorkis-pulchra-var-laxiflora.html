Cynorkis pulchra var. laxiflora

Cynorkis pulchra var. laxiflora, commonly known as the elegant orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
