Cryptocarya hornei

Cryptocarya hornei, commonly known as the New Zealand native tree, is a member of the Lauraceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue, fleshy berries.

Cryptocarya hornei is native to New Zealand and is often found in coastal and lowland forest areas.
