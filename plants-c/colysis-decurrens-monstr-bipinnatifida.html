Colysis decurrens monstr. bipinnatifida

Colysis decurrens monstr.

bipinnatifida, commonly known as the twin-leafed fern, is a rare and delicate fern species characterized by its finely divided, bipinnate fronds. The fronds are elongated and gracefully arching, with a soft, feathery appearance that gives it a distinctive, ethereal look.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has a short rhizome system and produces sporangia on the undersides of its leaves.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often considered a conservation concern.
