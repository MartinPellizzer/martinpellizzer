Callirhoe pedata var. compacta

Callirhoe pedata var. compacta, commonly known as the compact rosemary.

It is a compact, low-growing shrub native to the southwestern United States. The plant features aromatic, needle-like leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

This variety is prized for its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
