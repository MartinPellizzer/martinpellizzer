Chenopodium ambrosioides f. rotundatum

Chenopodium ambrosioides f. rotundatum, commonly known as goosefoot, is a variant of the species characterized by its rounded leaves.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is native to the Americas. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as wetlands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating, which can cause skin irritation in some individuals.

This form is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
