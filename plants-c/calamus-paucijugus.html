Calamus paucijugus

Calamus paucijugus, commonly known as the few-winged calamus, is a member of the Aroid family (Araceae).

It is a rhizomatous herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are typically green with a slightly rough texture. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Aroid family.

Calamus paucijugus is often found in wetlands and along the edges of freshwater habitats.
