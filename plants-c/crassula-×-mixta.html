Crassula × mixta

Crassula × mixta, commonly known as the Jade Plant or Friendship Plant, is a hybrid succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint, arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Crassula × mixta is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and attractive appearance.
