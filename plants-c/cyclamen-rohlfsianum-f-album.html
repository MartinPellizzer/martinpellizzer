Cyclamen rohlfsianum f. album

Cyclamen rohlfsianum f. album, commonly known as the white cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its striking white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, often darker throat. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both gardeners and botanical enthusiasts.
