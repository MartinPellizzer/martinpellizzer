Crepinella Montana

Crepinella montana is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe. It is known for its unique, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky soils. Its delicate appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and gardeners alike. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
