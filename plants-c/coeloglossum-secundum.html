Coeloglossum secundum

Coeloglossum secundum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging inflorescence and striking, violet-blue flowers. Native to the Andes Mountains of South America, this plant thrives in cool, humid environments. The leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It is a perennial herb that prefers well-drained, acidic soils and partial shade.
