Comarum salesovianum

Comarum salesovianum, commonly known as the marsh woundwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and stream banks. Comarum salesovianum is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies in suitable habitats.
