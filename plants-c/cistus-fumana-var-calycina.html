Cistus fumana var. calycina

Cistus fumana var. calycina, commonly known as the pink rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, glandular leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces showy, pink to reddish flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
