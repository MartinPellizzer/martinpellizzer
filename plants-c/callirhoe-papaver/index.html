Callirhoe Papaver

Callirhoe papaver is a striking flowering plant known for its vibrant, poppy-like blooms. Native to the western United States, it thrives in rocky and sandy soils. This perennial plant is often found in coastal areas and open woodlands. Its showy flowers range from red to orange, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.
