Cryptantha decipiens var. corollata

Cryptantha decipiens var. corollata, commonly known as the pink evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are alternate and lance-shaped, while the stems are erect and often branched.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed habitats.
