Corydalis meifolia var. meifolia

Corydalis meifolia var. meifolia, commonly known as the spring corydalis, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, nodding flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white, appearing in early spring. The plant has slender, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in woodland or shaded areas.

Corydalis meifolia var. meifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
