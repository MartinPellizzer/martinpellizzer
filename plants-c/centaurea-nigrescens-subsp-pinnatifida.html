Centaurea nigrescens subsp. pinnatifida

Centaurea nigrescens subsp.

pinnatifida, commonly known as the slender stemcentaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves.

The species produces small, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in summer. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.

This subspecies is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience.
