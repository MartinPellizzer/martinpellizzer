Calathea Ornata

Prayer plant, scientifically known as Calathea ornata, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America. It is named for its unique leaves that fold upwards at night, resembling a praying posture. This plant is popular among indoor gardeners due to its striking foliage and air-purifying qualities. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity. With its vibrant colors and easy care, the prayer plant is a favorite for both novice and experienced plant lovers.
