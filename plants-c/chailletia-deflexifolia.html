Chailletia deflexifolia

Chailletia deflexifolia, commonly known as the drooping leaf fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that give it a graceful appearance. The plant features compound leaves with a feathery structure, and its rhizomes are often covered in a dense mat of roots. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
