Cattleya × tresederiana

Cattleya × tresederiana, commonly known as the Tresederian Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It originates from a cross between Cattleya labiata and Cattleya mossiae, resulting in a plant with striking color variations. The plant typically features tall, erect stems with multiple blooms that can range from yellow to orange and red hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its beauty and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
