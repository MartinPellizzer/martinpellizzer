Cynometra michelsonii

Cynometra michelsonii, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its hard, dense wood that is highly valued for its durability. The tree has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers followed by elongated pods.

It is often found in dry savanna and woodland habitats, where it plays an important ecological role.
