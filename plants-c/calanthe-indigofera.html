Calanthe indigofera

Calanthe indigofera, commonly known as the indigo orchid, is a tropical plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are striking, with vivid blue petals and a prominent, contrasting lip.

Calanthe indigofera thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
