Calamagrostis Silvatica Var. Pyramidalis

Calamagrostis silvatica var. pyramidalis is a striking grass species native to northern regions of the northern hemisphere. It is known for its tall, narrow foliage that forms a pyramid-like shape, giving it a distinctive appearance. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains a lesser-known species within the grass family.
