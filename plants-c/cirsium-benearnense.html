Cirsium benearnense

Cirsium benearnense, commonly known as the Benearn thistle, is a flowering plant native to the British Isles.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple flowers. The plant typically grows in grassy areas and along roadsides, preferring well-drained soils. It is an herbaceous perennial that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and protected species in certain regions.
