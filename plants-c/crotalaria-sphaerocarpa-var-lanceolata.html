Crotalaria sphaerocarpa var. lanceolata

Crotalaria sphaerocarpa var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume structure and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features palmate leaves with lanceolate leaflets and produces yellow or orange flowers in compact clusters. Its seed pods are spherical and contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to various parts of the world as a garden plant.
