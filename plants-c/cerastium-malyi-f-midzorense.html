Cerastium malyi f. midzorense

Cerastium malyi f. midzorense, commonly known as the Midzoren cushion plant, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Caucasus, specifically in Georgia.

This plant is well adapted to cold, rocky environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
