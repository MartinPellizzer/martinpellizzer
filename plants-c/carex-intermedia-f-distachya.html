Carex intermedia f. distachya

Carex intermedia f. distachya, commonly known as the slender sedge, is a variant of the common sedge species.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and wetland habitats.
