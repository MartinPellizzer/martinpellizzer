Colchicum neapolitanum var. longifolium

Colchicum neapolitanum var. longifolium, commonly known as the Naples colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its striking, nodding flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features basal leaves that are long and narrow, often reaching up to 30 cm in length. It thrives in woodland and shaded areas, preferring moist, well-drained soils.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
