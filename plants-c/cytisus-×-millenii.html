Cytisus × millenii

Cytisus × millenii, commonly known as the hybrid yellow flowering bush, is a deciduous shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers and slender, woody stems.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous properties, and it typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often found in regions with mild, temperate climates.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
