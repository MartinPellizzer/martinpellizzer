Ceropegia congestiflora

Ceropegia congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing stems and tubular flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are often clustered together, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.
