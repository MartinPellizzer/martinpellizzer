Calanthe seranica

Calanthe seranica, commonly known as the Chinese calanthe, is a species of orchid native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Calanthe seranica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
