Coccocypselum herbaceum

Coccocypselum herbaceum, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, surrounded by narrow, silver-gray leaves. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Its foliage and flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
