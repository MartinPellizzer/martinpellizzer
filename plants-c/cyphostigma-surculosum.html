Cyphostigma surculosum

Cyphostigma surculosum, commonly known as the African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its growth habit is typically rosette-forming, with leaves arranged in a compact, symmetrical pattern.

Cyphostigma surculosum is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal.
