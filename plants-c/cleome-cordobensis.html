Cleome cordobensis

Cleome cordobensis, commonly known as the Cordoba cleome, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branched stems and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, round seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for gardens and landscapes.

Cleome cordobensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
