Cyperus cellulosoreticulatus

Cyperus cellulosoreticulatus, commonly known as the reticulate flatsedge, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family. It thrives in wetland environments, often found in marshes and along the edges of water bodies.

Its fibrous roots contribute to soil stabilization in aquatic habitats.
