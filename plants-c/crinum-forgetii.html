Crinum forgetii

Crinum forgetii, commonly known as the African rain lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Crinum forgetii is also known for its strong, pleasant fragrance, which attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
