Cordyline maculata

Cordyline maculata, commonly known as the spotted cordyline, is a striking evergreen plant native to New Zealand.

It features tall, narrow leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
