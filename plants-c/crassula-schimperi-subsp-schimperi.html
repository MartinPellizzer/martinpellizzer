Crassula schimperi subsp. schimperi

Crassula schimperi subsp.

schimperi, commonly known as the Schimper's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green or bluish-green in color.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to form dense, low-growing clumps over time.
