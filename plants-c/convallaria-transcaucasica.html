Convallaria transcaucasica

Convallaria transcaucasica, commonly known as the Transcaucasian lily, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the family Convallariaceae and is characterized by its evergreen, rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, white or pinkish flowers during the spring season. Its leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and arranged in a basal rosette.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.
