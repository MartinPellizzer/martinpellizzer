Caladium variegatum

Caladium variegatum, commonly known as the flameleaf caladium, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with vibrant shades of red, pink, and white. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have intricate patterns that vary in color and design. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is widely cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
