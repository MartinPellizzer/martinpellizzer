Cucurbita maxima

Cucurbita maxima, commonly known as the giant pumpkin, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a sprawling annual vine that produces large, round fruits with thick, smooth rind and vibrant orange flesh. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that are both male and female. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

This species is widely cultivated for its edible fruits, which are used in various culinary and decorative applications.
