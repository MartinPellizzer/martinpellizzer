Cinnamomum cavaleriei

Cinnamomum cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum kwangsiense, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces dark brown, aromatic bark that is sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its unique fragrance and medicinal properties, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
