Cymbidium globosum

Cymbidium globosum, commonly known as the globular orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. The plant features long, strap-like leaves and is known for its compact, globular flower spikes. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
