Cardamine arenicola f. gracillima

Cardamine arenicola f. gracillima, commonly known as the slender rock buttercup, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ranunculaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or subalpine regions.
