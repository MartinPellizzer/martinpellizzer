Cordia utermarkiana

Cordia utermarkiana, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, which bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly waxy texture.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.
