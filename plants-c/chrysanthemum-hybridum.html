Chrysanthemum hybridum

Chrysanthemum hybridum, commonly known as the garden chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that come in a wide range of colors, including yellow, red, orange, and white. The plant typically grows to a height of 30 to 150 centimeters, with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its flowering period extends from late summer into autumn, adding vibrant color to landscapes during the cooler months.
