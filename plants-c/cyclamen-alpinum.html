Cyclamen alpinum

Cyclamen alpinum, commonly known as the Alpine cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and vibrant, bowl-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, adapting to cold climates with its short growing season. Cyclamen alpinum prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
