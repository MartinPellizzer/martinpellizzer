Chaetocarpus Cordifolius

Chaetocarpus cordifolius is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its ornamental value has made it popular among gardeners seeking exotic foliage. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation outside its natural habitat.
