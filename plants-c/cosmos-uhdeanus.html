Cosmos uhdeanus

Cosmos uhdeanus, commonly known as the Mexican star flower, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and features slender, hairy stems. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers bloom in clusters at the top of the stems.

Cosmos uhdeanus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and full sun.
