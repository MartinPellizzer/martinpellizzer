Cuviera talbotii

Cuviera talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's cuviera, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
