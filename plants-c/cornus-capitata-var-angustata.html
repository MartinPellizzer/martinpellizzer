Cornus capitata var. angustata

Cornus capitata var. angustata, commonly known as narrow-leaved cornel.

It is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of the Eastern Mediterranean. The plant features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red or purple berries in the fall. Its leaves are narrow and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a slightly serrated edge.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
