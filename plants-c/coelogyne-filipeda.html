Coelogyne filipeda

Coelogyne filipeda, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Philippines.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in forest understories, thriving in humid and shaded environments. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a red or purple lip. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in seasonal conditions.

Coelogyne filipeda is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
