Cincinalis maranthae

Cincinalis maranthae, commonly known as the red-veined maranta, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that often display red, purple, and green hues. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Cincinalis maranthae is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
