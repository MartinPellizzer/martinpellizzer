Chamaefistula larensis

Chamaefistula larensis, commonly known as the African boxthorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems with sharp spines and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
