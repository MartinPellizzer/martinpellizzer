Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. meyeri

Cotoneaster racemiflorus var. meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters during late spring. The plant produces bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It has simple, alternate leaves that are oval to lance-shaped.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
