Coursetia harmsii

Coursetia harmsii, commonly known as the Harms' coursetia, is a shrub native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woody structure and small, often hairy leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, with deep roots that help it access groundwater.

Coursetia harmsii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in local horticulture.
