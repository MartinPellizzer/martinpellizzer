Calycadenia multiglandulosa subsp. robusta

Calycadenia multiglandulosa subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust calycadenia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its hairy stems and leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of pink to purple.

The subspecies robusta is distinguished by its robust growth and dense glandular hairs on its foliage.
