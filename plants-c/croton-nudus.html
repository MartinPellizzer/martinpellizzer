Croton nudus

Croton nudus, commonly known as the smooth croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmate leaves that are typically bright green with a smooth surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Due to its attractive foliage, it is popular in tropical gardens and indoor plant collections.
