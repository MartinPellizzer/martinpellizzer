Coelogyne mishmensis

Coelogyne mishmensis, commonly known as the Mishmensis orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in a spiral arrangement along the stem. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are long and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

Coelogyne mishmensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, colorful blooms.
