Cestrum lundianum

Cestrum lundianum, commonly known as the purple cestrum, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, which bloom in the evening. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
