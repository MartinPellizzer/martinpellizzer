Chrysophyllum pauciflorum var. nervosum

Chrysophyllum pauciflorum var. nervosum, commonly known as the African star apple, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, round berry with a sweet, edible pulp surrounded by a tough outer layer.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
