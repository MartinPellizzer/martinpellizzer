Cassia mimosoides var. pratensis

Cassia mimosoides var. pratensis, commonly known as the slender cassia, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often multi-branched stems and bipinnate leaves that are finely divided. The plant produces bright yellow inflorescences composed of numerous small, clustered flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a valuable ornamental and potential medicinal plant.
