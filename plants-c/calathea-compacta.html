Calathea compacta

Calathea compacta, commonly known as the compact calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate foliage with variegated patterns in shades of green, silver, and white. The leaves are typically broad and oval, often featuring a waxy texture that enhances their visual appeal. This plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its unique and elegant appearance.
