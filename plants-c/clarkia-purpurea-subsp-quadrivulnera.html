Clarkia purpurea subsp. quadrivulnera

Clarkia purpurea subsp.

quadrivulnera, commonly known as the four-veined clarkia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It belongs to the evening primrose family (Onagraceae) and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers.
