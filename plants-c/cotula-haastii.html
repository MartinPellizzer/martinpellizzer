Cotula haastii

Cotula haastii, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically forms dense mats of silver-white foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Cotula haastii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
