Cocos botryophora var. ensifolia

Cocos botryophora var. ensifolia, commonly known as the sword leaf coconut.

It is a variant of the coconut palm characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, fibrous fruits that contain a single, edible kernel. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its economic use in coconut production.
