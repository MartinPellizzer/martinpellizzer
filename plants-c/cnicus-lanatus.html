Cnicus lanatus

Cnicus lanatus, commonly known as the hairy sowthistle, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, lobed leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa, it is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its growth habit and appearance make it easily identifiable in its natural habitat.
