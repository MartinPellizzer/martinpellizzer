Codonocalyx longifolius

Codonocalyx longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaved ciliate.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release its seeds.
