Cnestis yangambiensis

Cnestis yangambiensis, commonly known as the Yangambien starfruit, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Cnestideae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit, which is edible, is used in local cuisines and has potential medicinal applications.
