Callicarpa longifolia var. brevipes

Callicarpa longifolia var. brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf beautyberry, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered, violet-blue berries that appear in late summer. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its showy fruit and compact growth habit.
