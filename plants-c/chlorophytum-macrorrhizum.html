Chlorophytum macrorrhizum

Chlorophytum macrorrhizum, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail or Tiger's Head plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are often striped with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil and bright, indirect light.

Chlorophytum macrorrhizum is a popular houseplant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.
