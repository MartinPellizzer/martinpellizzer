Cristaria geraniifolia

Cristaria geraniifolia, commonly known as the silverleaf geranium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.

Cristaria geraniifolia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
