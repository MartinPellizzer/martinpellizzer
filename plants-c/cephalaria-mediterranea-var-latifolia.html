Cephalaria mediterranea var. latifolia

Cephalaria mediterranea var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved Mediterranean cephalaria, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are broad, ovate, and often lobed. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and subtle floral display.
