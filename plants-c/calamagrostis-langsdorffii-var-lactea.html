Calamagrostis langsdorffii var. lactea

Calamagrostis langsdorffii var. lactea, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, silver-tipped culms that create a striking visual effect in gardens and natural landscapes. The plant produces small, pale green to white inflorescences that add a soft, feathery texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in water feature gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its elegant appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
