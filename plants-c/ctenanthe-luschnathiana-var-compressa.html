Ctenanthe luschnathiana var. compressa

Ctenanthe luschnathiana var. compressa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf zebra plant, is a compact, evergreen foliage plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features striking, dark green leaves with bold, contrasting white or silver veins that create a zebra-like pattern. The plant typically grows to a height of about 12 to 18 inches and has a broad, upright growth habit. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This ornamental plant is prized for its attractive foliage and is often used in indoor gardening and as a houseplant.
