Conophytum concordans

Conophytum concordans, commonly known as the "Stemless Stone Plant," is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features a compact, rosette-shaped body with thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. Its leaves are typically green with a waxy coating, and it thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Conophytum concordans is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
