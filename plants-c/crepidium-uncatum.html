Crepidium uncatum

Crepidium uncatum, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, slender stem and a single, narrow leaf. The plant produces a single, pale green to yellowish flower with a short, narrow spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a unique and relatively rare orchid in the region.
