Cucumis melo proles cassaba

Cucumis melo proles cassaba, commonly known as the casaba melon, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its pale yellow, spherical fruit with a smooth, slightly ribbed rind. The fruit contains a sweet, juicy, and aromatic flesh that is typically pale green to white in color. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in warm climates with well-drained soil.

This melon is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which is used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications.
