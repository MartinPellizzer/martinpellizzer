Ceropegia bracteolata

Ceropegia bracteolata, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are typically covered in a fine, silvery or white pubescence. The plant produces delicate, tubular flowers that are often pink or lilac in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Ceropegia bracteolata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
