Clematis × jackmanii

Clematis × jackmanii, commonly known as the Jackmanii Clematis, is a hybrid vine native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and profuse, showy flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces large, typically blue or purple flowers with a white center, and its foliage is often dark green and densely arranged. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis × jackmanii is a popular choice for gardeners due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
