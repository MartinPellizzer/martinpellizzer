Calliandra macrocalyx var. macrocalyx

Calliandra macrocalyx var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the showy calliandra, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound, feathery leaves and showy, pink to red flower clusters that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is adapted to warm, humid climates and thrives in full sun with well-drained soil. Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It is often used in landscaping for its vibrant appearance and ability to thrive in coastal or sandy soils.
