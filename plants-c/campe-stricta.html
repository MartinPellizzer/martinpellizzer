Campe stricta

Campe stricta, commonly known as the rigid camphorweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Campe stricta is often found in sandy or rocky soils in open habitats.
