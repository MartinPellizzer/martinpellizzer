Cayaponia diffusa

Cayaponia diffusa, commonly known as the Cayaponia vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, greenish-white flowers and yellow-orange fruits. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in rainforests. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruits are edible and have a mild, sweet flavor, making it a valuable plant in some local cuisines.
