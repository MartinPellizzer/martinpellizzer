Centaurium centaurioides

Centaurium centaurioides, commonly known as the slender centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slightly hairy texture.

Centaurium centaurioides is often found in dry, sunny grasslands and rocky soils, and it is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
