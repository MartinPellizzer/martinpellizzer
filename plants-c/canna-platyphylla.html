Canna platyphylla

Canna platyphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf canna, is a striking ornamental plant native to Central and South America.

It features large, broad leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, with a glossy, dark green surface. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of orange, red, and yellow, typically blooming from summer to fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Canna platyphylla is often used in gardens for its dramatic foliage and long-lasting floral display.
