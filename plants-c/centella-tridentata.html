Centella tridentata

Centella tridentata, commonly known as Asiatic pennywort, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches heights of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features small, heart-shaped leaves with three distinct lobes, which give it its scientific name. It produces tiny white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters.

Centella tridentata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or near streams.
