Cryptantha tumulosa

Cryptantha tumulosa, commonly known as the tumulus cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. Cryptantha tumulosa is often found in sandy or gravelly areas, such as along coastal dunes or in desert scrub environments.
