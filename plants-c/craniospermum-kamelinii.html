Craniospermum kamelinii

Craniospermum kamelinii, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal regions. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit and adaptability make it a notable species in arid ecosystems.
