Calamagrostis Purpurea Subsp. Barbata

Calamagrostis purpurea subsp. barbata is a striking ornamental grass known for its feathery plumes and vibrant purple hues. Native to eastern Asia, this plant thrives in moist to wet soils and partial to full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. The plant's feathery inflorescences add texture and movement to any landscape. With its adaptability and aesthetic appeal, it's a valuable addition to water gardens and mixed borders.
