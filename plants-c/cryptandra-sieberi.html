Cryptandra sieberi

Cryptandra sieberi, commonly known as the Sieber's cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it thrives in well-drained soils.
