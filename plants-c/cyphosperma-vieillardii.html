Cyphosperma vieillardii

Cyphosperma vieillardii, commonly known as the African star fruit or golden star tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Cameroon. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems and traditional uses.
