Calophyllum molle

Calophyllum molle, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.
