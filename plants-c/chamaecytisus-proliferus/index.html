Chamaecytisus Proliferus

Chamaecytisus proliferus is a hardy evergreen shrub known for its vibrant orange-red flowers and dense, thorny growth. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is commonly used as a hedging plant. Its ability to tolerate poor soil conditions makes it a popular choice for coastal and urban landscapes. The plant's rapid growth and adaptability have made it a valuable addition to gardens and naturalized areas. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive in certain regions, requiring careful management.
