Chilita pottsii

Chilita pottsii, commonly known as the Potts' chilita, is a rare and striking succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems that are often covered in a distinctive blue-gray or pale green hue. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or purple during the warmer months. It is well adapted to drought conditions, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly sought after by collectors and conservationists alike.
