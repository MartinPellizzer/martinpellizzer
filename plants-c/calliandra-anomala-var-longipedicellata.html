Calliandra anomala var. longipedicellata

Calliandra anomala var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the long pedicel calliandra, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feathery inflorescences composed of numerous small, delicate flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange blooms that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm, arid climates.

The leaves are bipinnately compound, contributing to its ornamental appeal and ecological value for pollinators.
