Cyathea purpurascens

Cyathea purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk covered in a thick, fibrous layer. The plant produces large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Cyathea purpurascens is valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
