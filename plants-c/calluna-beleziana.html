Calluna beleziana

Calluna beleziana, commonly known as the beauty heath, is a flowering shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or heathland environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and seasonal blooms.

Calluna beleziana plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
