Crotalaria pohliana var. regnellii

Crotalaria pohliana var. regnellii, commonly known as the yellow rattle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual that grows up to 30 cm tall, featuring hairy stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow, spurred flowers that bloom from late summer to autumn. It is often found in disturbed soils and is a common weed in agricultural areas.

Its seeds are small and hardy, allowing them to persist in the soil for several years.
