Citta

Citta, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a revered aquatic plant native to Asia.

It thrives in shallow freshwater environments, often found in ponds and slow-moving rivers. The plant's most distinctive feature is its showy, water-lily-like flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.

The lotus is also known for its symbolic significance in many cultures, representing purity and spiritual growth.
