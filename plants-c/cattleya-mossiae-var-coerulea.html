Cattleya mossiae var. coerulea

Cattleya mossiae var. coerulea, commonly known as the Blue Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking blue-violet flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces pendulous blooms with a distinctive coloration. It requires bright, indirect light and a humid environment to thrive.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
