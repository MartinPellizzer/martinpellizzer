Cotyledon linearis

Cotyledon linearis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf African violet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often tinged with purple or red, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
