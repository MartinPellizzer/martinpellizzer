Cnicus turkestanicus

Cnicus turkestanicus, commonly known as the Turkestan thistle, is a perennial flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments. It has a robust root system that helps it survive in poor soil conditions.

Cnicus turkestanicus is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
