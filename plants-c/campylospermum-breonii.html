Campylospermum breonii

Campylospermum breonii, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy, providing a distinctive texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and arid environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
