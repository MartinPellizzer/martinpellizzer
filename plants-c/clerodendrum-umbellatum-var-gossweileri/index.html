Clerodendrum Umbellatum Var. Gossweileri

Clerodendrum gossweileri, scientifically known as Clerodendrum umbellatum var. gossweileri, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Africa. It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is prized for its vibrant, showy blooms that often display a range of colors from red to orange. This plant is commonly found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners and horticulturists worldwide. Despite its beauty, it requires careful cultivation to ensure its health and longevity.
