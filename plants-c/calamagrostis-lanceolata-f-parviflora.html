Calamagrostis lanceolata f. parviflora

Calamagrostis lanceolata f. parviflora, commonly known as the narrowleaf silvergrass, is a compact, ornamental grass with slender, silver-blue foliage.

It produces small, pale green to white panicles of flowers in late summer, adding subtle texture to gardens. This cultivar is prized for its fine texture and ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils. It is often used in water gardens or as a border plant due to its graceful appearance.

The plant is cold-hardy and offers seasonal interest with its foliage color and flowering display.
