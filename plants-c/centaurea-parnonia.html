Centaurea parnonia

Centaurea parnonia, commonly known as the Parnon thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny leaves and vibrant purple to red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in mountainous areas. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Centaurea parnonia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
