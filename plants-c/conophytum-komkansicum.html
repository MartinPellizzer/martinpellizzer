Conophytum komkansicum

Conophytum komkansicum, commonly known as the Komkansicum stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-shaped growth form. The plant features thick, fleshy leaves that store water, enabling it to survive in arid conditions. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.

Conophytum komkansicum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
