Crotalaria digitata

Crotalaria digitata, commonly known as the slender crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, digitate (finger-like) leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in racemes. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Crotalaria digitata is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which makes it beneficial in soil enrichment.
