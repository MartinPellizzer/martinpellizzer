Codiaeum superbiens

Codiaeum superbiens, commonly known as the croton plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated foliage, which can display shades of red, yellow, orange, and green. The leaves are typically large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light to maintain its vibrant appearance.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
