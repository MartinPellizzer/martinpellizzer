Camellia crapnelliana

Camellia crapnelliana, commonly known as the Crapnell's camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. The plant produces elliptical, glossy leaves that are dark green and leathery in texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Camellia crapnelliana is also valued for its winter blooming habit, adding color to gardens during the colder months.
