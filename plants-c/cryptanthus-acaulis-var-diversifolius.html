Cryptanthus acaulis var. diversifolius

Cryptanthus acaulis var. diversifolius, commonly known as the rainbow crane's-bill, is a strikingly colorful plant native to Brazil.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, low-growing rosettes of fleshy, succulent leaves. The leaves are typically bright red, orange, or yellow, and they often have a waxy texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of green and pink, which are borne on slender stalks.

This plant is well-suited for rock gardens or containers due to its compact growth habit and vibrant foliage.
