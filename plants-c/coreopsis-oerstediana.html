Coreopsis oerstediana

Coreopsis oerstediana, commonly known as the prairie coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the central United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open prairies and dry woodlands. The plant features hairy, upright stems and produces bright yellow, daisy-like flowers in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
