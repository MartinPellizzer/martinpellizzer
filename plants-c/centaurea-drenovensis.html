Centaurea drenovensis

Centaurea drenovensis, commonly known as the Drenovenskaya centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments. Its flowers are usually blue to purple in color, with ray florets surrounding a central disk.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native habitat.
