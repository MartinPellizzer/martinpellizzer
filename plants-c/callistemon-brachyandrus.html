Callistemon brachyandrus

Callistemon brachyandrus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
