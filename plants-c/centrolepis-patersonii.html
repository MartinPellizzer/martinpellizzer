Centrolepis patersonii

Centrolepis patersonii, commonly known as the Paterson's grass.

It is a small, evergreen shrub native to South Africa. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and slightly hairy. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or reddish in color.

Centrolepis patersonii is well adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.
