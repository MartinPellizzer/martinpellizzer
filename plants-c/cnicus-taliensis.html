Cnicus taliensis

Cnicus taliensis, commonly known as the Taliensian sowthistle, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions. Its leaves are lobed and hairy, while its flowers are yellow and arranged in a cluster.

It is sometimes considered a weed but has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
