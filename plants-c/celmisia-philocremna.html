Celmisia philocremna

Celmisia philocremna, commonly known as the silver fern, is a striking plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery coating. It produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring.

Celmisia philocremna thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.
