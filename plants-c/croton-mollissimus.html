Croton mollissimus

Croton mollissimus, commonly known as velvet croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its growth habit is shrub-like, with multiple stems and a dense foliage structure.

Croton mollissimus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique leaf texture and adaptability to various growing conditions.
