Cardaminopsis croatica

Cardaminopsis croatica, commonly known as the Croatian bittersweet, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
