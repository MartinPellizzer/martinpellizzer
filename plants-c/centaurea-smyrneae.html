Centaurea smyrneae

Centaurea smyrneae, commonly known as the Smyrna centaurea, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its upright stems and vibrant blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are arranged in dense, spike-like clusters.

Centaurea smyrneae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
