Cucumis melo f. viridis

Cucumis melo f. viridis, commonly known as the Green Melon, is a variant of the melon plant characterized by its distinct green, warty rind.

This form exhibits a unique morphology, with a rough, textured exterior that sets it apart from typical melons. The plant typically produces oval-shaped fruits that are firm and have a mild, sweet flavor. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The Green Melon is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit.
