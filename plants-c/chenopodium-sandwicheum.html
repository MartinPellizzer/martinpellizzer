Chenopodium sandwicheum

Chenopodium sandwicheum, commonly known as sandhill goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the family Amaranthaceae.

It is native to North America and typically grows in sandy or disturbed soils. The plant has alternate, ovate leaves with a rough texture and is often found in open habitats such as prairies and roadsides. It produces small, greenish flowers in clusters and is adapted to arid conditions.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine and is also a food source for certain wildlife species.
