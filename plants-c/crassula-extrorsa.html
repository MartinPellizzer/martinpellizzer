Crassula extrorsa

Crassula extrorsa, commonly known as the "Lemon Lime Crassula," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green to yellow-green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Crassula extrorsa is popular as a houseplant due to its compact growth habit and low maintenance requirements.
