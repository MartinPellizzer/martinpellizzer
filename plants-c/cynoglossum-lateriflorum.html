Cynoglossum lateriflorum

Cynoglossum lateriflorum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It features distinctive, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and wildflower landscapes.
