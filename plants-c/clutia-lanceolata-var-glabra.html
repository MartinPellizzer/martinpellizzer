Clutia lanceolata var. glabra

Clutia lanceolata var. glabra, commonly known as smooth clutia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
