Cotyledon repens

Cotyledon repens, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are often tinged with red or purple hues. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. Its unique leaf structure and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Cotyledon repens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water to flourish.
