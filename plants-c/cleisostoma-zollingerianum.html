Cleisostoma zollingerianum

Cleisostoma zollingerianum, commonly known as the Zollinger's cleisostoma, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and yellow. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
