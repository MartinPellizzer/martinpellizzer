Cryptandra monticola

Cryptandra monticola, commonly known as the mountain cryptandra, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Cryptandra monticola is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
