Cattleya × dallemagnei

Cattleya × dallemagnei, commonly known as the Dallemagne Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid celebrated for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features tall, upright stems with large, pendulous blooms that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
