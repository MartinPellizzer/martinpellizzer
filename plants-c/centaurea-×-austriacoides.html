Centaurea × austriacoides

Centaurea × austriacoides, commonly known as the Austrian centaury, is a hybrid perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flower heads, which are composed of numerous small florets arranged in a composite structure. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact, bushy habit. Native to Central Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in meadows and rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
