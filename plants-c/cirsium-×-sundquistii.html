Cirsium × sundquistii

Cirsium × sundquistii, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woodlands. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped with serrated edges, and the flowers are arranged in a composite head.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological role, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and natural areas.
