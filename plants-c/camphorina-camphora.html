Camphorina camphora

Camphorina camphora, commonly known as the camphor tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, black seeds. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and distinctive camphor scent.

Camphorina camphora plays an important role in both ecological systems and traditional medicine.
