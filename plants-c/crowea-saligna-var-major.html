Crowea saligna var. major

Crowea saligna var. major, commonly known as the large-leaved crowea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are often larger than those of the typical variety. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings for its vibrant blooms and attractive foliage.
