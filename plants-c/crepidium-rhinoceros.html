Crepidium rhinoceros

Crepidium rhinoceros, commonly known as the rhinoceros orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pale green or yellowish flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to the orchid family, with specific adaptations for its ecological niche.
