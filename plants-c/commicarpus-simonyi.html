Commicarpus simonyi

Commicarpus simonyi, commonly known as the Ethiopian velvet bean, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, hairy pods. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid conditions. Its small, yellow flowers are followed by inflated, velvety fruits that contain several seeds.
