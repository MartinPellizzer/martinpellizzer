Centaurea × maritima var. erectispina

Centaurea × maritima var. erectispina, commonly known as the erect-spined centaury, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and spiny, bristly stems. The plant produces vibrant blue to purple flowers, which are typically arranged in dense, conical heads. It is native to coastal regions of Europe and thrives in sandy or rocky soils.

This species is often found in maritime environments, where it demonstrates tolerance to salt spray and strong winds.
