Clematis yuntaishanica

Clematis yuntaishanica, commonly known as Yuntai Mountain Clematis, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Ranunculaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, typically white or pale blue flowers. The plant features compound leaves with three to five leaflets and has a fibrous root system. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
