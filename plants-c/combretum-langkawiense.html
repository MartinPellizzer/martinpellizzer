Combretum langkawiense

Combretum langkawiense, commonly known as the Langkawi flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its striking, fiery red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and disturbed habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
