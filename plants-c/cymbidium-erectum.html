Cymbidium erectum

Cymbidium erectum, commonly known as the upright orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of China, Japan, and Korea.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant with tall, erect stems that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, spike-like inflorescences with numerous fragrant, lance-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow and orange. Its leaves are long, narrow, and grass-like, often arranged in two rows along the stem.

Cymbidium erectum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a cut flower.
