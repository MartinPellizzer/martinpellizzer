Cliffortia tuberculata var. muricata

Cliffortia tuberculata var. muricata, commonly known as the roughleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It has a compact growth habit and is well adapted to arid environments.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
