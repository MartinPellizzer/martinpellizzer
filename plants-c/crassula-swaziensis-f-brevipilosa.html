Crassula swaziensis f. brevipilosa

Crassula swaziensis f. brevipilosa, commonly known as the Swazi Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, rounded rosettes of fleshy, dark green leaves with a bluish tint. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or red during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
