Crassula nudicaulis var. nudicaulis

Crassula nudicaulis var. nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-stemmed crassula, is a compact, evergreen succulent native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically pale green with a slight reddish tinge under direct sunlight. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
