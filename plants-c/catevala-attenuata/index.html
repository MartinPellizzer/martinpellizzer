Catevala Attenuata

Catevala attenuata is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its unique, elongated petals. Found primarily in specific regions with moist, shaded environments, it thrives in humid climates. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive shape and slender form. This plant is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance but holds significant ecological value. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect its dwindling habitat and ensure its survival.
