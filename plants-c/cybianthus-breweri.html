Cybianthus breweri

Cybianthus breweri, commonly known as the desert lily, is a perennial flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico.

It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and desert plains. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are narrow and grayish-green, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry climates.

Cybianthus breweri is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in desert ecosystems.
