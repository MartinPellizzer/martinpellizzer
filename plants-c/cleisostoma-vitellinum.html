Cleisostoma vitellinum

Cleisostoma vitellinum, commonly known as the golden cleistostoma, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms that are typically borne on pendulous inflorescences. Its leaves are usually ovate and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.
