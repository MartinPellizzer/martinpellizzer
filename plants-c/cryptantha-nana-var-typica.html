Cryptantha nana var. typica

Cryptantha nana var. typica, commonly known as the dwarf cryptantha, is a low-growing annual plant native to the western United States.

It typically reaches a height of less than 10 centimeters and produces small, blue to purple flowers in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its ability to colonize disturbed areas.
