Ceratostema glans

Ceratostema glans, commonly known as the silver bell vine, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of white or pale pink. The plant has slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Ceratostema glans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in gardens for its elegant appearance.
