Cracca Elegans

Cracca elegans is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. This perennial plant is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its showy blooms make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, Cracca elegans requires careful attention to soil and light conditions to flourish.
