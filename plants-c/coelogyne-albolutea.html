Coelogyne albolutea

Coelogyne albolutea, commonly known as the white-lilac orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white petals and sepals, which are often adorned with a faint pink or purple hue. The plant produces long, pendant-like inflorescences that bear numerous fragrant flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne albolutea is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
