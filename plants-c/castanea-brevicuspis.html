Castanea brevicuspis

Castanea brevicuspis, commonly known as the Chinese chestnut, is a deciduous tree native to China.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers in catkins, followed by spiny, oval nuts enclosed in a prickly husk. Its nuts are edible when mature and have been used historically for food and timber.
