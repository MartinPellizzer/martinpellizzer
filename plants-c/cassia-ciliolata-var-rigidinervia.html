Cassia ciliolata var. rigidinervia

Cassia ciliolata var. rigidinervia, commonly known as the rigid nerve cassia, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins and prominent, parallel veins. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences, which bloom during the warmer months. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, fibrous pods that develop after flowering.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
