Callitriche glomerata

Callitriche glomerata, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic or semi-aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and slow-moving streams. The plant has small, opposite leaves that are often floating or submergent, and it produces tiny, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.
