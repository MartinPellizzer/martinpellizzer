Cyrtostylis reflexa

Cyrtostylis reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed spider-orchid, is an epiphytic orchid native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, wiry stems and small, pale green to white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, typically growing on trees or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its flowers have a distinctive reflexed lip, which is a key identifying feature.

Cyrtostylis reflexa is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
