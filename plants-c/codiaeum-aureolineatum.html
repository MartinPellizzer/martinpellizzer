Codiaeum aureolineatum

Codiaeum aureolineatum, commonly known as the flame tree or croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful, and often variegated leaves that display shades of yellow, orange, and red. The leaves are typically lobed and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens for its vibrant foliage.
