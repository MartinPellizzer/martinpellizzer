Crossopetalum aquifolium

Crossopetalum aquifolium, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
