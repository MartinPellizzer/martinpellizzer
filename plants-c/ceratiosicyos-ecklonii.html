Ceratiosicyos ecklonii

Ceratiosicyos ecklonii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It is adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water absorption.

Its latex sap is milky and can be toxic if ingested.
