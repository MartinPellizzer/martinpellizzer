Calea lucidivenia var. orientalis

Calea lucidivenia var. orientalis, commonly known as the Oriental Clearleaf plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
