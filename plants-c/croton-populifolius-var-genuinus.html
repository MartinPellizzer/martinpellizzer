Croton populifolius var. genuinus

Croton populifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the broadleaf croton, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features large, palmately lobed leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to yellow and red. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
