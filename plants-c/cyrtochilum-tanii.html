Cyrtochilum tanii

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish-white in color. Cyrtochilum tanii thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in montane forests.
