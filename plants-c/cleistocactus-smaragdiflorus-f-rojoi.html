Cleistocactus smaragdiflorus f. rojoi

Cleistocactus smaragdiflorus f. rojoi, commonly known as the Rojo's emerald-flowered cactus, is a rare and striking plant native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape and vibrant green stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
