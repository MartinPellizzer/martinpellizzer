Cryptomeria compacta

Cryptomeria compacta, commonly known as the Japanese red cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its compact, dense growth habit and reddish-brown bark. The plant has needle-like leaves that are short and scale-like, contributing to its dense foliage. It produces small, inconspicuous cones that contain seeds.

Cryptomeria compacta is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and resilience in various climates.
