Ceropegia dalzielii

Ceropegia dalzielii, commonly known as the silver leaf ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, trailing stems and small, waxy leaves. Ceropegia dalzielii is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
