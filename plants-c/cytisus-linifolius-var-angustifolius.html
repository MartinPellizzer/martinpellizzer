Cytisus linifolius var. angustifolius

Cytisus linifolius var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved broom, is a shrub native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, wiry stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often growing in rocky or coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
