Carduus × grenieri

Carduus × grenieri, commonly known as the hybrid thistle, is a perennial plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Carduus nutans and Carduus benedictus, exhibiting characteristics of both parents. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.

Botanically, it is notable for its adaptability and role in ecological succession.
