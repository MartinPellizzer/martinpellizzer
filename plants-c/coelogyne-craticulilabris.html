Coelogyne craticulilabris

Coelogyne craticulilabris, commonly known as the broad-labellum coelogyne, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers with distinctive broad, lip-like petals. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a contrasting red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
