Cirsium senile

Cirsium senile, commonly known as old man's beard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its long, hanging clusters of small, pale yellow to white flowers that resemble a beard. The plant features spiny, lobed leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is native to Europe and parts of western Asia.

Cirsium senile is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and disturbed areas, where it thrives in full sun to partial shade.
