Crotalaria anagyroides var. angustifolia

Crotalaria anagyroides var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf crotalaria, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features erect stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces yellow, pea-like flowers clustered in spikes, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its seeds are encased in a distinctive, spiny pod that splits open when mature.

This species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
