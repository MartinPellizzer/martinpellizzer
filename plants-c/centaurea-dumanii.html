Centaurea dumanii

Centaurea dumanii, commonly known as the Turkish knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Centaurea dumanii is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in arid environments.
