Chenopodium mucronatum

Chenopodium mucronatum, commonly known as prickly goosefoot, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has alternate, rough, and often spiny leaves that are usually ovate or triangular in shape. Its small, greenish flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it produces small, round seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.
