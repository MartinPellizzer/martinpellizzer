Cotoneaster Cotoneaster

Cotoneaster, scientifically known as Cotoneaster cotoneaster, is a member of the Rosaceae family that thrives in temperate regions around the world. This shrub is prized for its vibrant autumn foliage and clusters of small, ornamental flowers that bloom in the spring. It is commonly used in landscaping due to its adaptability and ability to tolerate various soil conditions. The plant produces bright red berries that attract birds, making it an important component of local ecosystems. Its hardy nature and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
