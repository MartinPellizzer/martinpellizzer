Cyclamen repandum proles balearicum

Cyclamen repandum proles balearicum, commonly known as the Balearic cyclamen, is a flowering plant native to the Balearic Islands.

It is a perennial herb with distinctive heart-shaped, velvety leaves and clusters of delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or shaded areas. Its blooms typically appear in late winter to early spring, making it a notable early-flowering species.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
