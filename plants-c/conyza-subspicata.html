Conyza subspicata

Conyza subspicata, commonly known as slenderleaf fleabane, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as fields and roadsides. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the summer, producing small seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Conyza subspicata is often found in temperate regions and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
