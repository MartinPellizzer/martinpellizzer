Coelogyne camelostalix var. camelostalix

Coelogyne camelostalix var. camelostalix, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its striking, elongated pseudobulbs and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of yellow-orange blooms with red and brown markings, which appear during the dry season. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store water in its native habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
