Carduus edelbergii

Carduus edelbergii, commonly known as the Edelberg thistle, is a flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and wastelands.

Due to its hardy nature, it is considered a resilient weed in some regions.
