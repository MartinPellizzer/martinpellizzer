Corispermum welshii

Corispermum welshii, commonly known as Welsh's corisperm, is a member of the Chenopodiaceae family.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in saline or arid environments. The plant has small, green, ovate leaves and produces tiny, clustered flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in small, capsule-like structures.
