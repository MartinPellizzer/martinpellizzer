Chrysanthemum fontanesii

Chrysanthemum fontanesii, commonly known as the Fontanesii chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are sturdy and often branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
