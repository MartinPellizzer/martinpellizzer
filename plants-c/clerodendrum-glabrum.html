Clerodendrum glabrum

Clerodendrum glabrum, commonly known as the smooth clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including India and China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a globose capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
