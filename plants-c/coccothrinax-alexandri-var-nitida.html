Coccothrinax alexandri var. nitida

Coccothrinax alexandri var. nitida, commonly known as the slender palm, is a compact, slow-growing palm native to the Caribbean.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk with smooth, grayish-brown bark. The plant has pinnate leaves that are dark green and glossy, with long, narrow leaflets. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by black, spherical berries.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
