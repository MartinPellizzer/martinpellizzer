Canavalia gladiata f. erythrocarpa

Canavalia gladiata f. erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited sword bean, is a variant of the sword bean plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with a distinctive blade-like shape. The plant produces elongated pods that mature to a bright red color, containing edible seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.

The red-fruited form is notable for its ornamental appeal and potential use in traditional medicine.
