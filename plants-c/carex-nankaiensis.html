Carex nankaiensis

Carex nankaiensis, commonly known as the Nankai sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower spikes that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Carex nankaiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in stabilizing soil in wet habitats.
