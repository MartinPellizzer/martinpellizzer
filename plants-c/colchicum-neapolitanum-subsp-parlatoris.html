Colchicum neapolitanum subsp. parlatoris

Colchicum neapolitanum subsp.

parlatoris, commonly known as the Neapolitan colchicum, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It produces striking, showy flowers with bright red petals and a distinctive yellow or orange center.

The plant typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its foliage consists of broad, strap-like leaves that emerge in early spring.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
