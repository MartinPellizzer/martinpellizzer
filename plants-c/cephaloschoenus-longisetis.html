Cephaloschoenus longisetis

Cephaloschoenus longisetis, commonly known as the long-hair sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and swampy areas of eastern Australia.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the sedge family.
