Conostegia chiriquensis

Conostegia chiriquensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan silverleaf.

It is a shrub native to Central America, particularly found in countries like Guatemala and El Salvador. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the dry season.

The species is adapted to grow in dry, rocky soils and is often found in coastal areas.
