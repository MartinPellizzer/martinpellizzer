Calochortus × indecorus

Calochortus × indecorus, commonly known as the hybrid glory-of-the-snow, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Calochortus, which includes a variety of flowering plants known for their showy blooms. This particular species is a hybrid, resulting from crosses between Calochortus luteus and Calochortus elongatus. It typically produces striking yellow or orange flowers with a distinctive white or pink center.

The plant is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
