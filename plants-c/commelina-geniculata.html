Commelina geniculata

Commelina geniculata, commonly known as the zigzag commelina, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its flowering period occurs throughout the growing season, and it is often found in association with other wetland flora.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
