Chlorophytum africanum var. nordalianum

Chlorophytum africanum var. nordalianum, commonly known as the Nordalian Spider Plant, is a hybrid variety of the spider plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green markings. This plant is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions. It produces small, white flowers followed by delicate, dangling white berries.

The plant is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.
