Carpobrotus pillansii

Carpobrotus pillansii, commonly known as the Atlantic coast ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves and produces vibrant orange-red flowers. The plant thrives in coastal environments, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils. It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and preventing erosion.

Its common name reflects its ability to withstand harsh coastal conditions.
