Calandrinia sericea

Calandrinia sericea, commonly known as the silky purslane, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing annual herb with succulent, fleshy leaves and stems that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink to reddish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

Calandrinia sericea is known for its ability to tolerate drought conditions due to its succulent structure.
