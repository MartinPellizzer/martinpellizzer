Cassiope redowskii

Cassiope redowskii, commonly known as the Redowskii's mountain heather, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, needle-like leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain. It plays an important role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.

Cassiope redowskii is also valued for its ornamental appeal in alpine gardens.
