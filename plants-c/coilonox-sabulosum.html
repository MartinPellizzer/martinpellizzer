Coilonox sabulosum

Coilonox sabulosum, commonly known as the sandy soil grass.

It is a low-growing perennial plant native to arid and semi-arid regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to conserve water. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or brown in color.

Coilonox sabulosum is often found in sandy or gravelly soils and is well-suited to drought conditions.
