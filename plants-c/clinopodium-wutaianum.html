Clinopodium wutaianum

Clinopodium wutaianum, commonly known as the Wutaian mint, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong, minty fragrance.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
