Cryptanthus solidadeanus

Cryptanthus solidadeanus, commonly known as the Solidadean Waxplant, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

Cryptanthus solidadeanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive flowers and unique appearance.
