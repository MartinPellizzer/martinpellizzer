Centaurea limbata f. elata

Centaurea limbata f. elata, commonly known as the tall or slender knapweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its erect, slender stems and small, daisy-like yellow-orange flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. Its leaves are linear to lanceolate, with a hairy texture, and it produces numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is often found in the Mediterranean region and parts of Europe.
