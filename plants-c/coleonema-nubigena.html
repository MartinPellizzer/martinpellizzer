Coleonema nubigena

Coleonema nubigena, commonly known as the cloud-like coleonema, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, cloud-like appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is widely cultivated in gardens around the world.
