Columbaria minor

Columbaria minor, commonly known as the small urn plant, is a low-growing herbaceous species native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with five petals and a distinctive urn-shaped structure. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Columbaria minor is known for its delicate beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens.
