Cardamine scutata subsp. flexuosa

Cardamine scutata subsp.

flexuosa, commonly known as the flexuous watercress, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its small, kidney-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a basal rosette.

The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers with four petals, typical of the mustard family. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

Its roots are fibrous, and it is often found in temperate regions of Europe and Asia.
