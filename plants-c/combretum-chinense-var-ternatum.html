Combretum chinense var. ternatum

Combretum chinense var. ternatum, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree or red flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that appear in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
