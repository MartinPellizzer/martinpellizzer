Canna macrophylla

Canna macrophylla, commonly known as the giant cannas, is a striking tropical plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, showy leaves that can reach up to three feet in length. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, yellow, and orange, often with a distinctive purple or red spathe. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Canna macrophylla is a perennial herb that can grow up to six feet tall, making it a dramatic addition to gardens and landscapes.
