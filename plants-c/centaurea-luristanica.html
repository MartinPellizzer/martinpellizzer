Centaurea luristanica

Centaurea luristanica, commonly known as the Luristan thistle, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Iran.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky and arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are bright yellow with a purple center.

Centaurea luristanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
