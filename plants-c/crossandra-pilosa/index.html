Crossandra Pilosa

Crossandra pilosa is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant orange to red flowers and velvety foliage. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded forest understories. This species is popular among gardeners for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements. Its unique appearance makes it a sought-after addition to both indoor and outdoor gardens. With proper care, Crossandra pilosa can bloom continuously throughout the growing season.
