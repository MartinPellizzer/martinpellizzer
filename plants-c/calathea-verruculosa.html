Calathea verruculosa

Calathea verruculosa, commonly known as the Velvetleaf Calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves with intricate patterns and a deep green to purple hue. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture in its soil. Its foliage is highly ornamental, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Calathea verruculosa is relatively low maintenance but benefits from regular fertilization during the growing season.
