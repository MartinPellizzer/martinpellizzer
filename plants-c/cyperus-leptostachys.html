Cyperus leptostachys

Cyperus leptostachys, commonly known as slender flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family (Cyperaceae).

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, such as marshes, swamps, and along waterways. It has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters.

Cyperus leptostachys is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its ability to thrive in moist conditions.
