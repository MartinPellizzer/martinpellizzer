Campanula valdensis var. schleicheri

Campanula valdensis var. schleicheri, commonly known as the Schleicher's bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It features upright stems that bear clusters of bell-shaped, blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically lanceolate and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

This variety is valued in gardens for its attractive blooms and relatively low maintenance requirements.
