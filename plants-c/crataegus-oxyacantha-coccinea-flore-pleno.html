Crataegus oxyacantha coccinea-flore-pleno

Crataegus oxyacantha coccinea-flore-pleno, commonly known as the double-flowered red hawthorn, is a cultivar of the hawthorn plant native to Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit and its showy, double-flowered blooms that are typically a deep red or pink in color. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but often considered astringent. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
