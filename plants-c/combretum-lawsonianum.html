Combretum lawsonianum

Combretum lawsonianum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its striking red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. The plant has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
