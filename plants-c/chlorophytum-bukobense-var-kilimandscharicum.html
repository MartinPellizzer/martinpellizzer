Chlorophytum bukobense var. kilimandscharicum

Chlorophytum bukobense var. kilimandscharicum, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro spider plant, is a succulent plant native to East Africa.

It features long, arching leaves that are typically green with a white or silver stripe running down the center. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its adaptability to various light conditions makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The varietal name 'kilimandscharicum' refers to its native region, the Kilimanjaro area in Tanzania.
