Chrysophyllum roxburghii var. papuanum

Chrysophyllum roxburghii var. papuanum, commonly known as the Papuan cherimoya, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of New Guinea.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant is also valued for its potential in agroforestry systems due to its ability to grow in shaded conditions.
