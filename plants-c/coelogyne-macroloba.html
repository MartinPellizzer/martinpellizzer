Coelogyne macroloba

Coelogyne macroloba, commonly known as the large-leafed orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendant inflorescence bearing showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant has strap-shaped, evergreen leaves that are often clustered at the base. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media.

Coelogyne macroloba is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its striking floral display.
