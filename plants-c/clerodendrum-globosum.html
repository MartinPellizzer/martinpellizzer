Clerodendrum globosum

Clerodendrum globosum, commonly known as the globe clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule containing numerous small, dark seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
