Callista moniliformis

Callista moniliformis, commonly known as the chainflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, chain-like arrangement of yellow flowers along the stems. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Callista moniliformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
