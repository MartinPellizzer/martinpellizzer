Camellia longii

Camellia longii, commonly known as the Chinese camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soil and requires partial shade to full sun.

Camellia longii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
