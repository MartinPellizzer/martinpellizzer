Columnea karstenianum

Columnea karstenianum, commonly known as the flame flower or red flame plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting hummingbirds and other pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Columnea karstenianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
