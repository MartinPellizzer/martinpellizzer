Callista suavissima

Callista suavissima, commonly known as the sweet-flowered bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its blooming period typically occurs in late spring to early summer, adding a vibrant touch to gardens during that time.
