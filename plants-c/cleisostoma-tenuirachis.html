Cleisostoma tenuirachis

Cleisostoma tenuirachis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed cleisostoma, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative orchid.

Cleisostoma tenuirachis is characterized by its slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, making it a unique addition to tropical gardens.
