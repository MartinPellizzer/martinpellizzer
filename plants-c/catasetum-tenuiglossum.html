Catasetum tenuiglossum

Catasetum tenuiglossum, commonly known as the narrow-lipped catasetum, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendant, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip that is narrow and elongated. The plant produces a single, showy flower per stem, which is often adorned with intricate markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Catasetum tenuiglossum is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
