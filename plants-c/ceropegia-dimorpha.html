Ceropegia dimorpha

Ceropegia dimorpha, commonly known as the string of hearts or heartleaf ceropegia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale green, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
