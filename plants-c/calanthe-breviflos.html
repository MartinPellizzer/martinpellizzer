Calanthe breviflos

Calanthe breviflos, commonly known as the short-flowered calanthe, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as China, India, and the Philippines. This herbaceous plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous or forested areas. Its flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive hood-like structure covering the reproductive parts.

The plant's rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
