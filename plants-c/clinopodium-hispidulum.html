Clinopodium hispidulum

Clinopodium hispidulum, commonly known as rough hedge nettle, is a flowering plant in the family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a rough texture. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It is typically found in moist, disturbed soils in woodland and meadow habitats.

Clinopodium hispidulum is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported calming and digestive properties.
