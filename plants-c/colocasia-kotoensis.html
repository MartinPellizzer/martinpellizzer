Colocasia kotoensis

Colocasia kotoensis, commonly known as the Koto arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves and spadix inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its leaves can reach up to 50 cm in length, with a deep green color and a waxy texture.

Colocasia kotoensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
