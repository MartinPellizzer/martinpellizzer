Chryseis hypecoides

Chryseis hypecoides, commonly known as the golden shield fern, is a species of fern native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are often covered in a fine, golden-colored bloom. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices.
