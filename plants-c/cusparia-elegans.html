Cusparia elegans

Cusparia elegans, commonly known as the elegant spurge, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and small, star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, demonstrating drought tolerance through its thick, waxy leaves. Its flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Cusparia elegans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
