Cleisostoma nieuwenhuisii

Cleisostoma nieuwenhuisii, commonly known as the Nieuwenhuis's cleistostoma, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and strikingly colored flowers that typically range from yellow to orange. The plant produces showy, resupinate flowers with a unique structure that includes a spurred lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Cleisostoma nieuwenhuisii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
