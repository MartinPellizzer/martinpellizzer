Cistus × fontii

Cistus × fontii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the genus Cistus, which includes various species of flowering plants known for their resilience in arid environments. The plant features aromatic evergreen leaves and produces showy, pink or white flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens across southern Europe and parts of the United States.
