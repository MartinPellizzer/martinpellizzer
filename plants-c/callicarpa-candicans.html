Callicarpa candicans

Callicarpa candicans, commonly known as the red-berried beauty or Chinese lantern plant, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orange-red berries that appear in clusters, attracting birds and other wildlife. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that turn red in the fall, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Callicarpa candicans is often cultivated for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
