Centaurium tenuiflorum var. hermannii

Centaurium tenuiflorum var. hermannii, commonly known as Hermann's narrowflower centaury, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe.

It features slender, erect stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters at the tips of the stems. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open woodlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Gentianaceae family.
