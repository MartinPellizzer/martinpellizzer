Cyperus bonianus

Cyperus bonianus, commonly known as the Chinese sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or near water bodies.

Cyperus bonianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
