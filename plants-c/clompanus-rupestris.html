Clompanus rupestris

Clompanus rupestris, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to arid and rocky environments.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant typically grows in crevices and on rock surfaces, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its small, white or yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom in early spring.

Clompanus rupestris is known for its resilience and is often used in rock gardens due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
