Cucumella reticulata

Cucumella reticulata, commonly known as the netted cucumber, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive netted pattern on its fruit, which gives it its common name. The plant produces yellow flowers and has a trailing growth habit, often found in warm climates. Its fruits are typically harvested when green and used in various culinary applications.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
