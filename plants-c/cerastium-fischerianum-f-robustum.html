Cerastium fischerianum f. robustum

Cerastium fischerianum f. robustum, commonly known as the robust chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It features small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, succulent stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It is native to Europe and thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a common sight in gardens and natural habitats where it spreads through stolons.
