Calamagrostis × wirtgeniana

Calamagrostis × wirtgeniana, commonly known as 'Karl Foerster' or 'Silver Fountain Grass', is a perennial grass native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and panicles of silver-green flower heads that appear in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adding visual interest to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Calamagrostis × wirtgeniana is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to attract wildlife.
