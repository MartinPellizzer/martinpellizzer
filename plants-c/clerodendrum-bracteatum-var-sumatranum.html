Clerodendrum bracteatum var. sumatranum

Clerodendrum bracteatum var. sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran Clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy bracts that resemble petals, often in shades of red, orange, or yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture with serrated edges.

The flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, adding to the plant's ornamental value.
