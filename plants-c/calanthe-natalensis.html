Calanthe natalensis

Calanthe natalensis, commonly known as Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its elegant, strap-like leaves and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest floors or rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Calanthe natalensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
