Chaetanthera Chilensis Var. Tenuifolia

Chaetanthera chilensis var. tenuifolia is a unique and lesser-known plant native to southern Chile. It belongs to the Caprifoliaceae family, known for its ornamental and sometimes medicinal value. This variant is distinguished by its slender leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles. Its cultivation is challenging due to its specific environmental needs and limited availability.
