Cardamine polyodontes

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds, streams, and wet meadows. Its flowers are small and yellow, consisting of two sepals and two petals, and are usually clustered in groups of three to five.

The plant is characterized by its delicate appearance and its ability to thrive in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
