Cissus gilletii

Cissus gilletii, commonly known as the climbing cissus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Vitaceae.

It is native to southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. Cissus gilletii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
