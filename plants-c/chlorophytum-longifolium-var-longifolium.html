Chlorophytum longifolium var. longifolium

Chlorophytum longifolium var. longifolium, commonly known as the snake plant, is a hardy perennial plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright leaves that are typically dark green with white or yellowish stripes, which are characteristic of its varietal form. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions and requires minimal watering, making it an ideal choice for indoor gardening. Its foliage is not only aesthetically pleasing but also contributes to air purification by removing toxins.

This variety is particularly noted for its resilience and ease of care, which have contributed to its popularity among plant enthusiasts.
