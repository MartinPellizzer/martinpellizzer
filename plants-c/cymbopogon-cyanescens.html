Cymbopogon cyanescens

Cymbopogon cyanescens, commonly known as blue grass or blue oat grass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, blue-green blades and tufted inflorescences that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and open areas. Its foliage is aromatic and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Cymbopogon cyanescens is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
