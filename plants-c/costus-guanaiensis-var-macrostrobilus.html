Costus guanaiensis var. macrostrobilus

Costus guanaiensis var. macrostrobilus, commonly known as the broad-strobilus costus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Zingiberaceae family, which includes gingers, and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
