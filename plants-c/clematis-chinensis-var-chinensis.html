Clematis chinensis var. chinensis

Clematis chinensis var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Clematis, is a deciduous vine native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its climbing habit and showy blooms make it a valuable addition to gardens and landscapes.
