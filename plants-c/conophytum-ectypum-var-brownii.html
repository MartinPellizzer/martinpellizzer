Conophytum ectypum var. brownii

Conophytum ectypum var. brownii, commonly known as the Brown's conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, basal leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.
