Callista tortilis

Callista tortilis, commonly known as the spiral bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its unique spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a favored plant among horticulturists.
