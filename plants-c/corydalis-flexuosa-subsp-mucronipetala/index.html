Corydalis Flexuosa Subsp. Mucronipetala

Corydalis flexuosa subsp. mucronipetala is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western China. It is known for its small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and subtle fragrance. Due to its limited habitat and threatened status, it is considered a conservation priority. Its beauty and ecological importance make it a subject of both scientific study and botanical fascination.
