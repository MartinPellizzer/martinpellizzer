Cattleya × isabellae

Cattleya × isabellae, commonly known as the Isabellae Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves and produces large, showy blooms in shades of white, pink, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relative ease of cultivation.
