Convallaria majalis var. flore-pleno

Convallaria majalis var. flore-pleno, commonly known as the double-flowered lily-of-the-valley, is a cultivar of the lily-of-the-valley plant characterized by its dense, clustered flowers.

It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is native to Europe. The plant features fragrant, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. It produces a cluster of small, green berries that are toxic to humans and animals.
