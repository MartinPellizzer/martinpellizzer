Cattleya forbesii f. eburnea

Cattleya forbesii f. eburnea, commonly known as the eburnea cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant appearance and fragrant blooms.

It features large, velvety petals that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a subtle lilac or lavender hue. This plant is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows on trees or rocks in shaded environments. It requires bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

The flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a striking visual display to any collection.
