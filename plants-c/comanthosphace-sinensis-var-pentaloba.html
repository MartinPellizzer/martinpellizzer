Comanthosphace sinensis var. pentaloba

Comanthosphace sinensis var. pentaloba, commonly known as the Chinese horned orchid, is a unique and rare orchid species native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, horn-like floral structures that resemble the shape of a horn, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in mountainous regions. Its flowers are usually a mix of white, yellow, and green, with a distinctive central column.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unusual appearance.
