Ceropegia vahrmeijeri

Ceropegia vahrmeijeri, commonly known as the string of turtles, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, trailing stems that bear small, tubular flowers in shades of white and green. The plant's leaves are oval-shaped and often have a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
