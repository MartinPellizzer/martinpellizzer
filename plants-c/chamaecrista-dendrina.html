Chamaecrista dendrina

Chamaecrista dendrina, commonly known as the yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to tropical and southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring compound leaves with small, opposite leaflets. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in dense, branched clusters, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

Chamaecrista dendrina is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.
