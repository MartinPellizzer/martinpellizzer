Crassula abyssinica var. ovata

Crassula abyssinica var. ovata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
