Circaea × skvortsovii

Circaea × skvortsovii, commonly known as the hybrid white bead vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to the Caucasus region.

It features small, white, bead-like flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate flowers and cascading growth habit.
