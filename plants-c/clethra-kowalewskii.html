Clethra kowalewskii

Clethra kowalewskii, commonly known as the Korean sweet pepper, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

Clethra kowalewskii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant landscapes.
