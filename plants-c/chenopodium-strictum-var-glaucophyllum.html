Chenopodium strictum var. glaucophyllum

Chenopodium strictum var. glaucophyllum, commonly known as silverleaf goosefoot, is a species of flowering plant in the amaranth family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, bluish-gray leaves covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in dense clusters, typically in late summer. It is native to North America and thrives in disturbed soils and arid environments.

Silverleaf goosefoot is often found in fields, roadsides, and other open areas where it can spread rapidly.
