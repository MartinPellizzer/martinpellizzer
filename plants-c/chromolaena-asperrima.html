Chromolaena asperrima

Chromolaena asperrima, commonly known as rough chromolaena, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, rough-textured leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings due to its adaptability. It has a sprawling growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

Chromolaena asperrima is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resistance to drought conditions.
