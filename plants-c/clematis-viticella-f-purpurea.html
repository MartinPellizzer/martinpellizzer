Clematis viticella f. purpurea

Clematis viticella f. purpurea, commonly known as the purple clematis, is a compact, vigorous vine with distinctive purple foliage and showy, fragrant flowers.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, bell-shaped blooms in shades of purple and blue. This variety is prized for its hardiness and ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.

The plant is a popular choice for climbing structures and as a border plant due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
