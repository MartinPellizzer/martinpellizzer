Cheilanthes Perlanata

Cheilanthes perlanata is a rare and striking fern native to the arid regions of the southwestern united states and northern mexico. it belongs to the family polypodiaceae and is known for its unique, compact growth habit and vibrant, silvery-green foliage. this plant thrives in rocky soils and is often found in crevices and on cliffs, adapting to extreme environmental conditions. due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered threatened by habitat loss and climate change. its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
