Camelina bornmuelleriana

Camelina bornmuelleriana, commonly known as the Bornmueller's camelina, is a species of flowering plant in the family Brassicaceae.

It is native to Central Asia and is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces seeds that are often used in traditional medicine.
