Cadetia clausa

Cadetia clausa, commonly known as the closed cadetia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions. The plant features simple, alternate leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with four petals. Its fruit is a pod that dehisces to release seeds.

Cadetia clausa is often found in meadows, along roadsides, and in other open habitats.
