Croton Verreauxii Var. Genuinus

Croton verreauxii var. genuinus is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, variegated foliage. Native to the rainforests of Central Africa, this species thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves display a unique blend of green, yellow, and red hues, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. The plant's ornamental value has led to its cultivation in many parts of the world. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to light and moisture to flourish.
