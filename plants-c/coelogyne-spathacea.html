Coelogyne spathacea

Coelogyne spathacea, commonly known as the white-lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elegant, white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded forest environments. Its pseudobulbs are clustered and covered in a thick, fibrous sheath.

Coelogyne spathacea thrives in warm, moist conditions with good air circulation.
