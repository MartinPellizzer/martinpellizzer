Cerasus Avium F. Aspleniifolia

Cerasus avium f. aspleniifolia is a unique and striking variant of the cherry tree known for its distinctive foliage. This ornamental plant features leaves that resemble those of the maidenhair fern, giving it a delicate and exotic appearance. While it shares the general structure of the cherry tree, its foliage sets it apart from common varieties. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and landscapes. This plant offers a rare blend of beauty and botanical interest.
