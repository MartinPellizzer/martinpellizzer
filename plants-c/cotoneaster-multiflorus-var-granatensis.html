Cotoneaster multiflorus var. granatensis

Cotoneaster multiflorus var. granatensis, commonly known as the Chinese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, glossy green leaves that turn red or orange in the fall. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries in the autumn. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.

Cotoneaster multiflorus var. granatensis is also used in landscaping for its compact growth habit and seasonal color changes.
