Cybianthus maguirei

Cybianthus maguirei, commonly known as the Maguire's cybianthus, is a rare flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in wetlands and swampy areas, thriving in moist, acidic soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spiky fruits that disperse its seeds.
