Cotoneaster sargentii

Cotoneaster sargentii, commonly known as Sargent's cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces bright red berries in the fall, which are a food source for birds and other wildlife. Cotoneaster sargentii typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters and has dark green, oval-shaped leaves.

It is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
