Chlorophytum chevalieri

Chlorophytum chevalieri, commonly known as the Tiger's Tail, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It features long, strap-like leaves that are often variegated with white and green stripes, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance for low light and minimal water requirements.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both ornamental gardens and as a houseplant.
