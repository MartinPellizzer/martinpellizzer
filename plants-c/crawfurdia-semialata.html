Crawfurdia semialata

Crawfurdia semialata, commonly known as the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, orchid-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the summer months.

Crawfurdia semialata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
