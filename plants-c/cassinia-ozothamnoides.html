Cassinia ozothamnoides

Cassinia ozothamnoides, commonly known as the golden everlasting or everlastings, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow disk flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and heathlands.

Cassinia ozothamnoides is known for its long-lasting, fluffy flowers that retain their color and form for extended periods.
