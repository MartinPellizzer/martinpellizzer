Calamagrostis Silvatica Var. Villosa

Calamagrostis silvatica var. villosa is a striking grass species native to parts of eastern North America. It is known for its dense, tufted growth and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. The plant features soft, silky foliage that adds a unique texture to natural landscapes. Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for gardeners seeking native plants. This variety is also valued for its ecological benefits, supporting local wildlife and improving soil stability.
