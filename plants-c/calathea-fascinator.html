Calathea fascinator

Calathea fascinator, commonly known as the flameleaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns of green, pink, and white. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic value and relatively easy care requirements.
