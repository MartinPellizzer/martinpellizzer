Cryptocarya aristata

Cryptocarya aristata, commonly known as the mountain pepper, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a dark blue drupe, containing a single seed.
