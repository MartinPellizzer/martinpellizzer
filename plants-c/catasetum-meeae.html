Catasetum meeae

Catasetum meeae, commonly known as the Meeae orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant produces striking, spurred flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its pseudobulbs are thick and segmented, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments.

Catasetum meeae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
