Callistemon laevifolius

Callistemon laevifolius, commonly known as the smooth-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cylindrical, bright red flower spikes that appear in spring. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a smooth, glossy surface. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

This plant is also valued for its ability to attract native birds and insects.
