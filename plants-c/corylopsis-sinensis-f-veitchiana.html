Corylopsis sinensis f. veitchiana

Corylopsis sinensis f. veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's flame twisted vine, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to China.

It features striking, bright orange to red pendulous flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a standout in early-season gardens. The plant has narrow, twisted, dark green leaves that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil, preferring a slightly acidic to neutral pH.

This ornamental plant is often used in rock gardens or as a border plant due to its unique appearance and seasonal color display.
