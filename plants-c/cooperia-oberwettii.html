Cooperia oberwettii

Cooperia oberwettii, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a species of grass native to South America, particularly found in the Andean regions. This plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Cooperia oberwettii plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native range and is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
