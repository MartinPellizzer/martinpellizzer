Cheirostylis glandulifera

Cheirostylis glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular-styled orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, upright stem and a single, showy flower. The plant features a distinctive, elongated lip that is often marked with intricate patterns. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for absorbing nutrients from the forest floor.

Cheirostylis glandulifera thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland and montane forests.
