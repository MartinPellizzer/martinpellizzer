Condalia ferrea

Condalia ferrea, commonly known as ironwood or iron tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood, which gives it its common name. The plant has small, pale green leaves and produces yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

Condalia ferrea is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
