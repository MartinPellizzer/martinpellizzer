Cucurbita maxima var. virginica

Cucurbita maxima var. virginica, commonly known as the giant pumpkin, is a cultivar of the species Cucurbita maxima.

It is characterized by its large, round fruits that can weigh several hundred pounds. The plant is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other squashes and gourds. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it produces yellow flowers that are both male and female.

This variety is prized for its use in competitions and culinary applications due to its size and flavor.
