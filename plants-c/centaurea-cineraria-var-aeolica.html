Centaurea cineraria var. aeolica

Centaurea cineraria var. aeolica, commonly known as the Aeolian cineraria, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and rocky habitats. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
