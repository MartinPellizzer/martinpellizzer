Crinum makoyanum var. roseum

Crinum makoyanum var. roseum, commonly known as the pink crinum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking pink to lilac-colored flowers. The plant features narrow, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, bell-shaped blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
