Celmisia semicordata

Celmisia semicordata, commonly known as the mountain flax, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers range in color from white to pale yellow.

Celmisia semicordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
