Chamaedorea wallisii

Chamaedorea wallisii, commonly known as the Wallisii bamboo palm, is a species of flowering plant in the family Arecaceae.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and feathery, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into dark blue, fleshy fruits. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Its compact size and elegant appearance make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor landscaping.
