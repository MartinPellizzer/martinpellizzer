Ceratostylis tenuis

Ceratostylis tenuis, commonly known as the slender horned orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, wiry stem that often grows close to the ground. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a loose spike, which are characteristic of the Orchidaceae family. It prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The orchid has a delicate appearance and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
