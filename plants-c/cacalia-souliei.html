Cacalia souliei

Cacalia souliei, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves. It is often found in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
