Chrysanthemum glabratum

Chrysanthemum glabratum, commonly known as the smooth chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, white, and orange, typically blooming in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile garden plant.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and used in floral arrangements.
