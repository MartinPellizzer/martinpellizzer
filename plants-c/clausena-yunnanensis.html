Clausena yunnanensis

Clausena yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Clausena, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by elliptical, yellowish fruits.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
