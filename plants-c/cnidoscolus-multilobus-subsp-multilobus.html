Cnidoscolus multilobus subsp. multilobus

Cnidoscolus multilobus subsp.

multilobus, commonly known as the giant arrowhead or arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with deep lobes, which give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a spiny, capsule-like structure that contains several seeds.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
