Cymbidium iansonii

Cymbidium iansonii, commonly known as the Janson's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Thailand and Malaysia.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of white, pink, and red.

Cymbidium iansonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty and long-lasting blooms.
