Camellia campanulata

Camellia campanulata, commonly known as the bell-shaped camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant produces elliptical, leathery leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Camellia campanulata is also valued for its winter blooming habit, adding color to gardens during the colder months.
