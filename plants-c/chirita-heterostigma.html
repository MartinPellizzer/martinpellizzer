Chirita heterostigma

Chirita heterostigma, commonly known as the heterostigma begonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Begoniaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves and showy, spurred flowers that come in a variety of colors. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated as a houseplant and in botanical gardens.
