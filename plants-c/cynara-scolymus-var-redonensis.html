Cynara scolymus var. redonensis

Cynara scolymus var. redonensis, commonly known as the Redonensis artichoke, is a cultivated variety of the globe artichoke.

It is characterized by its robust, thistle-like appearance with dark green, spiny leaves. The plant produces large, edible flower heads that are similar to the common artichoke but often have a more intense flavor. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for both its culinary use and ornamental appeal in gardens.
