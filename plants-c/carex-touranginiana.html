Carex touranginiana

Carex touranginiana, commonly known as the Tourangin sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are often hidden among the foliage.
