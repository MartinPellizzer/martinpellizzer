Chondropetalum ebracteatum

Chondropetalum ebracteatum, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feathery fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a distinctive crown of overlapping, spirally arranged fronds that give it a unique appearance. Its rhizome is thick and fibrous, supporting the growth of the trunk and fronds.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
