Cineraria laciniata

Cineraria laciniata, commonly known as the shredded cineraria, is a striking flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its large, velvety, dark green leaves that are deeply lobed and often covered in a fine white or grayish coating. The plant produces showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring, adding a dramatic visual element to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and vibrant blooms make it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
