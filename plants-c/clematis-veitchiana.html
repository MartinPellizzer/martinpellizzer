Clematis veitchiana

Clematis veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's clematis, is a deciduous vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and can reach lengths of up to 15 meters, making it a striking addition to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis veitchiana is also valued for its ability to climb trellises and fences, adding vertical interest to landscapes.
