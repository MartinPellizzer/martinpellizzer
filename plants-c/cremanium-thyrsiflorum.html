Cremanium thyrsiflorum

Cremanium thyrsiflorum, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a striking evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and its distinctive, star-shaped leaves that are usually a vibrant green. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers in clusters during the winter months, which give it its festive common name. Its leaves are leathery and have a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in its native arid environment.

Cremanium thyrsiflorum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and hardy nature.
