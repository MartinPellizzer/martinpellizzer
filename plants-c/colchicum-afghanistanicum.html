Colchicum afghanistanicum

Colchicum afghanistanicum, commonly known as Afghan colchicum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Afghanistan.

It belongs to the Colchicaceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features basal leaves that are typically lance-shaped and covered in a dense layer of hairs. It produces a capsule fruit that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

Due to its habitat in high-altitude environments, it is adapted to cold and dry conditions.
