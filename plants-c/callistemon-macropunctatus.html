Callistemon macropunctatus

Callistemon macropunctatus, commonly known as the large-spotted bottlebrush, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink bottlebrush flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine, silky hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

The species is known for its ornamental value and ability to attract native birds and insects.
