Combretum turbinatum

Combretum turbinatum, commonly known as the turban bush, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, turban-shaped flower clusters that appear in late summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
