Carex hongnoensis

Carex hongnoensis, commonly known as Hongno's sedge, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is native to Korea and is characterized by its grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flower heads that are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
