Cubelium

Cubelium, commonly known as the "cubelium plant," is a unique species belonging to the family Cubeliales.

It is characterized by its distinctive cube-shaped leaves and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental appeal. Native to arid regions, it is adapted to thrive in low-water environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
