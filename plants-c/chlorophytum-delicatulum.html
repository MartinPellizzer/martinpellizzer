Chlorophytum delicatulum

Chlorophytum delicatulum, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its long, arching leaves that are typically variegated with white and green stripes. The plant produces small, grass-like flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well-known for its ability to thrive in a variety of light conditions and its ease of care, making it a popular houseplant.

Additionally, it is often used for air purification and can propagate easily through its offsets.
