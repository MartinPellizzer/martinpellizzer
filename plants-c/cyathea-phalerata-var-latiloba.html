Cyathea phalerata var. latiloba

Cyathea phalerata var. latiloba, commonly known as the broad-leaf tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of large, feathery fronds that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its fronds are compound, with a distinctive pale green color and a smooth, waxy texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
