Calycothrix achaeta

Calycothrix achaeta, commonly known as the slender thornweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy texture.

Calycothrix achaeta is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it a hardy species in its native environment.
