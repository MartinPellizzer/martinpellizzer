Cnidoscolus pringlei

Cnidoscolus pringlei, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by edible, spiny fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
