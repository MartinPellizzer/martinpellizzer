Clausena anisata subsp. abyssinica

Clausena anisata subsp.

abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian wild lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to Ethiopia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers.

The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or orange when mature. Its leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
