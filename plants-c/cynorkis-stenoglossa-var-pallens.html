Cynorkis stenoglossa var. pallens

Cynorkis stenoglossa var. pallens, commonly known as the pale-lip orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, slender stems and showy, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often arranged in a loose rosette.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
