Convolvulus pellitus f. anestius

Convolvulus pellitus f. anestius, commonly known as the pale convolvulus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Bindweed family (Convolvulaceae).

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces small, funnel-shaped flowers typically in shades of pink or white.
