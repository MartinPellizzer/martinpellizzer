Chamorchis viridis

Chamorchis viridis, commonly known as green chamorchis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its botanical name reflects its vibrant green foliage and the genus name "Chamorchis," which is derived from the Greek words for "green" and "flower.".

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
