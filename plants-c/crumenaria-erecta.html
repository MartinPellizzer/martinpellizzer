Crumenaria erecta

Crumenaria erecta, commonly known as the upright crumenaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Arecaceae, which includes palms.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and pinnately compound leaves that give it a distinctive, elegant appearance. Crumenaria erecta thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
