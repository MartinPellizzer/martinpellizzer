Cattleya victoriacarolinae

Cattleya victoriacarolinae, commonly known as the Victoria Carolina orchid, is a rare and exquisite species native to the rainforests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and green. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, adapted to thrive in the humid, shaded environments of its natural habitat.

This orchid is prized by collectors for its beauty and rarity, making it a sought-after specimen in horticultural circles.
