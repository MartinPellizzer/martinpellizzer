Cyperorchis sigmoidea

Cyperorchis sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a member of the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like foliage and compact inflorescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish or reddish in color. It is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Cyperorchis sigmoidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in specialty gardens.
