Coprosma linariifolia

Coprosma linariifolia, commonly known as the silver coprosma, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically silver-white underneath, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by red or orange berries. It thrives in coastal and forested areas, preferring well-drained soils and moderate to high light conditions.

Coprosma linariifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
