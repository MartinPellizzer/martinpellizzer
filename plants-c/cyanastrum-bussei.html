Cyanastrum bussei

Cyanastrum bussei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. Cyanastrum bussei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
