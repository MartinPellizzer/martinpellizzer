Cinnamomum taimoshanicum

Cinnamomum taimoshanicum, commonly known as the Taimoschan cinnamon, is a species native to the Russian Far East.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant produces aromatic, reddish-brown bark that is highly valued for its medicinal and culinary properties. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinct, spicy aroma.

The tree is also notable for its ability to thrive in cool, humid climates.
