Camellia rubroanthera

Camellia rubroanthera, commonly known as the red-anther camellia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and orange stamens, which give it a distinctive and vibrant appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers bloom in winter and early spring, adding color to the landscape during colder months.

Camellia rubroanthera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
