Crotalaria onobrychis

Crotalaria onobrychis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed crotalaria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant has compound leaves with slender, linear leaflets and produces distinctive, spiny seed pods. It is often found in dry, disturbed soils and can thrive in arid environments.

Crotalaria onobrychis is also known for its potential use in soil remediation due to its nitrogen-fixing abilities.
