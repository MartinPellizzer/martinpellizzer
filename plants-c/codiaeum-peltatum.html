Codiaeum peltatum

Codiaeum peltatum, commonly known as the flame maple or croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful leaves that display a range of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant has a short, woody stem and is often grown as a houseplant or in warm climates. Its foliage is the primary ornamental feature, making it popular in horticulture.

Codiaeum peltatum prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
