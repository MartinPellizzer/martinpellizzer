Cassia tuhovalyana

Cassia tuhovalyana, commonly known as the Tuhovalyana cassia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Cassia tuhovalyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
