Conioselinum latifolium

Conioselinum latifolium, commonly known as broadleaf conioselinum, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features basal leaves that are broad and lance-shaped, with a rough, hairy texture. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Conioselinum latifolium is often found in disturbed or marginal environments, showing adaptability to various soil conditions.
