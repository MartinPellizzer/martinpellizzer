Clematis fusca var. fusca

Clematis fusca var. fusca, commonly known as the purple clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves with three to five elliptical leaflets, often with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Clematis fusca var. fusca is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb trellises or walls.
