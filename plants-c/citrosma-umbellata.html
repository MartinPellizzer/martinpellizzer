Citrosma umbellata

Citrosma umbellata, commonly known as the white sand tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white flowers.
