Cordia obscura var. magnifolia

Cordia obscura var. magnifolia, commonly known as the magnolia cordia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a fragrant scent. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
