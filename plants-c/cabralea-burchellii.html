Cabralea burchellii

Cabralea burchellii, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are often pink or purple in color. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Cabralea burchellii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
