Croton avenius

Croton avenius, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Croton avenius is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
