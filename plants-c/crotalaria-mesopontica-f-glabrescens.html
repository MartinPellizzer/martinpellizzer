Crotalaria mesopontica f. glabrescens

Crotalaria mesopontica f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth crotalaria, is a variant of the crotalaria genus characterized by its reduced pubescence.

This plant is a flowering legume native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Greece. It features erect, branched stems and produces yellow flowers in racemose inflorescences. The leaves are pinnately compound, with ovate to lanceolate leaflets.

It is adapted to dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open, sunny environments.
