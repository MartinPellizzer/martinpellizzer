Capraria elliptica

Capraria elliptica, commonly known as the narrow-leaved capraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or coastal habitats. The plant features elliptical, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in loose inflorescences.

Capraria elliptica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
