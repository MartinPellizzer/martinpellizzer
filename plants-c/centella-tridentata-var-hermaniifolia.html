Centella tridentata var. hermaniifolia

Centella tridentata var. hermaniifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf centella, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, heart-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its stems are short and often hairy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
