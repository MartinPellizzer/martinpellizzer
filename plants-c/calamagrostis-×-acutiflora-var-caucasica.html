Calamagrostis × acutiflora var. caucasica

Calamagrostis × acutiflora var. caucasica, commonly known as the Caucasian feather reed, is a ornamental grass native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and prefers full sun to partial shade. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often used in landscape design for its elegant appearance.

This variety is noted for its cold hardiness and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
