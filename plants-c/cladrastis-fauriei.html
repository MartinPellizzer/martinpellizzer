Cladrastis fauriei

Cladrastis fauriei, commonly known as the Chinese trumpet tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its striking, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has compound, pinnate leaves that are alternate and have a delicate, feathery appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a suitable ornamental plant in gardens.

The bark is smooth and greyish-brown, becoming fissured with age, and the plant is noted for its rapid growth and adaptability.
