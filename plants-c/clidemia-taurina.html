Clidemia taurina

Clidemia taurina, commonly known as the tropical milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.

Clidemia taurina is also known for its role in supporting monarch butterfly populations by providing a host plant for their larvae.
