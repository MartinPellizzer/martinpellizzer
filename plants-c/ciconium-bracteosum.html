Ciconium bracteosum

Ciconium bracteosum, commonly known as the bracted ciconium, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ciconiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive bract-like structures that encase its inflorescence, giving it a unique appearance. The plant typically grows in wetland habitats, thriving in moist and swampy environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often with a showy display that attracts pollinators.

Ciconium bracteosum plays an important ecological role in its native wetlands by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
