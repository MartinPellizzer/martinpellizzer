Crambe cordifolia var. kotschyana

Crambe cordifolia var. kotschyana, commonly known as the Kotschy's heartleaf crambe, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its flowers are typically borne on slender pedicels, and the plant has a compact, bushy growth habit.

Crambe cordifolia var. kotschyana is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in alpine environments.
