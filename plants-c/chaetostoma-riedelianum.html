Chaetostoma riedelianum

Chaetostoma riedelianum, commonly known as the Riedel's spiny lobster orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its unique, spiny leaf structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is a sought-after plant in horticultural circles.
