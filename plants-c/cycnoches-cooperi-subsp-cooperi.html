Cycnoches cooperi subsp. cooperi

Cycnoches cooperi subsp.

cooperi, commonly known as the Cooper's cycnoches, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its erect, leafy stems and showy, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or wetlands. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.
