Cyperus globosus subsp. cymosus

Cyperus globosus subsp.

cymosus, commonly known as the clustered flatsedge, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae. It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or seasonally flooded areas, such as marshes and wetlands.

The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often hidden among the foliage. Its flowers are usually greenish or brownish and are adapted for wind pollination.

This subspecies is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
