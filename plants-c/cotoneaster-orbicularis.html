Cotoneaster orbicularis

Cotoneaster orbicularis, commonly known as the round-leaf cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, orbicular (rounded) leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in spring, followed by bright red berries in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Cotoneaster orbicularis is also valued for its role in wildlife habitats, providing food for birds and other small animals.
