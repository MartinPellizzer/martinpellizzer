Coelogyne barbata

Coelogyne barbata, commonly known as the hairy orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous inflorescence and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Coelogyne barbata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
