Conophytum subacutum

Conophytum subacutum, commonly known as the sharp-angled conophytum, is a small, compact succulent native to South Africa.

It features a basal rosette of fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a reddish tinge under stress. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves.

Due to its slow growth and striking appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
