Chrysastrum sagittatum

Chrysastrum sagittatum, commonly known as the arrowhead daisy, is a herbaceous perennial native to the United States.

It features bright yellow flowers with a central disk and ray florets that resemble arrowheads, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is well-suited for rock gardens, borders, or containers due to its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
