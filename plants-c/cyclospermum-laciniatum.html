Cyclospermum laciniatum

Cyclospermum laciniatum, commonly known as the laceleaf cyclospermum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided, almost fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Cyclospermum laciniatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
