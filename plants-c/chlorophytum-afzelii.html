Chlorophytum afzelii

Chlorophytum afzelii, commonly known as the African spear lily, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are usually bright green with a waxy texture. It produces small, white or pale green flowers in clusters during the summer months.

Native to southern Africa, it is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
