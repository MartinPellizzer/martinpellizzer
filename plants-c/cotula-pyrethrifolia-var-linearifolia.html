Cotula pyrethrifolia var. linearifolia

Cotula pyrethrifolia var. linearifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small yellow flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
