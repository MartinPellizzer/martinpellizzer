Cyclopogon trifasciatus

Cyclopogon trifasciatus, commonly known as the three-banded cyclopogon, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit.
