Cyperus pumilus var. leucolepis

Cyperus pumilus var. leucolepis, commonly known as the slender flatsedge, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are usually pale green or brown, and it is often found in disturbed soils.

It plays a role in stabilizing wetland ecosystems and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
