Cotula lidbeckia

Cotula lidbeckia, commonly known as the silver cotula, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cotula lidbeckia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
