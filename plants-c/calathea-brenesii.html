Calathea brenesii

Calathea brenesii, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped, featuring a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity to flourish.
