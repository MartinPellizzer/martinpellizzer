Chlorophytum leucolepis

Chlorophytum leucolepis, commonly known as the silver edge snake plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, narrow leaves that are dark green with distinct white or silver margins. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It is a member of the Asparagaceae family and is known for its air-purifying qualities.

Chlorophytum leucolepis is popular as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
