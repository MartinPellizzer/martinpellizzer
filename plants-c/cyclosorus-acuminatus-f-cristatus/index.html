Cyclosorus Acuminatus F. Cristatus

Cyclosorus acuminatus f. cristatus is a unique variant of the common water clover known for its striking, crest-like growth pattern. This plant is often found in shallow, still waters where it thrives in nutrient-rich environments. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice among aquatic hobbyists and botanists alike. The cristatus form is a natural mutation that results in an unusual, almost decorative foliage structure. Despite its ornamental appeal, it plays an important ecological role in aquatic ecosystems.
