Coreopsis auriculata var. glabrata

Coreopsis auriculata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States.

It is characterized by its bright yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive bicolor pattern. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

This variety is known for its hardiness and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
