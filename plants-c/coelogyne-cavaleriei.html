Coelogyne cavaleriei

Coelogyne cavaleriei, commonly known as the Cavalerie's orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded forest environments. The plant features a rhizomatous structure with fleshy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinct, fragrant scent.

Coelogyne cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
