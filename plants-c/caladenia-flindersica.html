Caladenia flindersica

Caladenia flindersica, commonly known as the Flinders Island Spider Orchid, is an orchid species native to Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils in coastal areas, particularly in South Australia and the Flinders Island region. Its flowers are usually pale green or yellow with intricate patterns, and it produces a single, slender stem with a few small, lance-shaped leaves.

The species is noted for its low-growing habit and is often found in heathlands and coastal scrub.
