Clematis simensis var. ruwenzoriensis

Clematis simensis var. ruwenzoriensis, commonly known as the Ruwenzori clematis, is a climbing vine native to the high-altitude regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its robust stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in montane forests. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a smooth texture.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
