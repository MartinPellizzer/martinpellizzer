Crassula parvipetala

Crassula parvipetala, commonly known as the "Baby Crassula" or "Miniature Crassula," is a compact, low-growing succulent native to South Africa.

It features dense clusters of small, fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically green with a bluish or purplish tinge. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens, containers, or as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and minimal water requirements.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
