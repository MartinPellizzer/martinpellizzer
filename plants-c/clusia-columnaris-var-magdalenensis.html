Clusia columnaris var. magdalenensis

Clusia columnaris var. magdalenensis, commonly known as the Magdalenensis Clusia, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually yellow or green, followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive foliage.

This variety is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and poor soil conditions.
