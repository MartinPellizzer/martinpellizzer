Clerodendrum strictum

Clerodendrum strictum, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters tall. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral displays.
