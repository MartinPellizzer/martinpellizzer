Cercidiphyllum japonicum var. pendulum

Cercidiphyllum japonicum var. pendulum, commonly known as the weeping Japanese maple, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its cascading branches that create a weeping effect, giving it a distinctive and ornamental appearance. The tree produces small, delicate leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in the fall. Its foliage provides seasonal interest, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

The plant thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, requiring moderate watering for optimal growth.
