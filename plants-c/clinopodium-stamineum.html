Clinopodium stamineum

Clinopodium stamineum, commonly known as slender woodruff, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its small, white to pale blue flowers are arranged in dense spikes and bloom in early summer.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a mild, aromatic scent.
