Centaurea segredoi subsp. tenuiloba

Centaurea segredoi subsp.

tenuiloba, commonly known as the narrow-leaved centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Iberian Peninsula. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

The plant typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, thriving in sunny, well-drained environments. Its leaves are linear and slightly hairy, while the flowers are bright yellow with purple markings.

It is often found in open habitats such as scrublands and woodland edges.
