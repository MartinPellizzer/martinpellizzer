Cenia sericea

Cenia sericea, commonly known as silky cenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is distinguished by its finely haired, silver-green foliage that gives it a silvery appearance. It produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming in spring.

Cenia sericea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and drought tolerance.
