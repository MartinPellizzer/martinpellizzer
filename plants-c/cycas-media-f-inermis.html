Cycas media f. inermis

Cycas media f. inermis, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a variant of the cycad species Cycas media.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in a dense, feathery pattern. The plant produces cycad-like cones, with male and female cones appearing on separate plants. It is native to southern China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Cycas media f. inermis is a slow-growing plant that prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
