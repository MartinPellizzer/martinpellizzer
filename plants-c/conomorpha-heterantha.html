Conomorpha heterantha

Conomorpha heterantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its daisy-like inflorescence. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small white to pale yellow flowers. It is typically found in arid or semi-arid regions, often growing in rocky soils.
