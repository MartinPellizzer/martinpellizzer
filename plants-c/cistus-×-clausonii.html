Cistus × clausonii

Cistus × clausonii, commonly known as the hybrid rock rose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, glandular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its flowering stems are often covered in fine hairs, and the plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

Cistus × clausonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to support local pollinators.
