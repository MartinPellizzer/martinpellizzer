Cynanchum crassiusculum

Cynanchum crassiusculum, commonly known as the thick-leaved milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family, which includes other milkweeds known for their milky sap. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are thick and waxy, often with a bluish-green hue. Its flowers are typically clustered in umbels, producing a showy display of yellow and green hues.

The plant is adapted to thrive in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in prairies and open woodlands.
