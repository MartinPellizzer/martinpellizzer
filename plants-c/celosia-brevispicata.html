Celosia brevispicata

Celosia brevispicata, commonly known as the dwarf crown flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces vibrant, spiky bracts that range in color from red to orange, resembling small crowns, which give it its common name. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Celosia brevispicata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ease of care.
