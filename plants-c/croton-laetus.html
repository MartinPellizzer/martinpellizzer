Croton laetus

Croton laetus, commonly known as the glossy abutilon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. The plant produces showy, lantern-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Croton laetus is often cultivated as a houseplant or in gardens for its vibrant foliage and unique floral display.
