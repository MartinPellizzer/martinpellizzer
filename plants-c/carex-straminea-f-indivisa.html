Carex straminea f. indivisa

Carex straminea f. indivisa, commonly known as the slender flatleaf sedge, is a variant of the species Carex straminea.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Asia, including China and Korea. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically pale green to yellowish in color. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

This sedge is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
