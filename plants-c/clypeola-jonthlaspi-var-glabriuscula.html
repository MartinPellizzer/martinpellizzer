Clypeola jonthlaspi var. glabriuscula

Clypeola jonthlaspi var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smoothleaf clypeola, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are usually smooth and oval in shape. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
