Calyptranthes oblanceolata

Calyptranthes oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf calyptranthes, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

Calyptranthes oblanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
