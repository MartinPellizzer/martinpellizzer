Cyperus obesus

Cyperus obesus, commonly known as the umbrella plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features a rosette of broad, waxy leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces a tall, spiky inflorescence that resembles an umbrella, giving it its common name. It thrives in wet or marshy environments and is often found in shallow water or along the edges of ponds.

Cyperus obesus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in aquatic gardens and as a water purifier.
