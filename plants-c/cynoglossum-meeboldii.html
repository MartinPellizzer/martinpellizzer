Cynoglossum meeboldii

Cynoglossum meeboldii, commonly known as the meebold bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to southern Europe.

It features distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long flowering period and ability to attract pollinators.
