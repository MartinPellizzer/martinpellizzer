Croton refractus

Croton refractus, commonly known as the red croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that range from green to red and orange hues. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with unique floral structures. Its leaves are often lobed or deeply divided, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Croton refractus thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
