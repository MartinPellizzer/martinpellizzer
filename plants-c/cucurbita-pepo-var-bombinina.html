Cucurbita pepo var. bombinina

Cucurbita pepo var. bombinina, commonly known as the Turk's Turban gourd, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, inflated, turban-shaped fruits with a thick, warty rind. The plant produces yellow flowers and is native to the Mediterranean region. Its fruit can vary in color, ranging from green to orange or red, depending on the variety and maturity.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes and can also be used in traditional cooking.
