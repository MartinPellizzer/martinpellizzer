Cordia tortuensis

Cordia tortuensis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
