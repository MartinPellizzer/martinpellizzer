Cosmianthemum latifolium

Cosmianthemum latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf cosmos, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers bloom throughout the growing season, adding vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
