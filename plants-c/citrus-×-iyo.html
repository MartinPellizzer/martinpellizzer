Citrus × iyo

Citrus × iyo, commonly known as the Natal orange.

It is a hybrid citrus species native to South Africa, characterized by its small, round, and slightly flattened fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with thick, waxy leaves and fragrant white flowers. Its fruit has a thin, yellow-orange peel and a sweet, juicy pulp with a mild citrus flavor.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.
