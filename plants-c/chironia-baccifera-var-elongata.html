Chironia baccifera var. elongata

Chironia baccifera var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated baccifera, is a variant of the baccifera plant species.

It is characterized by its elongated leaves and clusters of small, berry-like fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a woody stem and alternate leaves with serrated edges.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
