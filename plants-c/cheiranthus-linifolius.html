Cheiranthus linifolius

Cheiranthus linifolius, commonly known as the silky mustard, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, followed by fluffy, seed-bearing pods. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance.

It is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature and ornamental value.
