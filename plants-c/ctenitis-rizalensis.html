Ctenitis rizalensis

Ctenitis rizalensis, commonly known as the Rizal's tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in lowland forests. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth habit.

The fronds are usually bright green and can reach lengths of up to several meters, contributing to its ornamental value.
