Cymbidium pumilum

Cymbidium pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, compact plant that typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Cymbidium pumilum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
