Castanopsis cryptoneuron

Its leaves are leathery and alternate, often with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss.

Castanopsis cryptoneuron is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also important in its native ecosystems as a habitat and food source for various wildlife species.
