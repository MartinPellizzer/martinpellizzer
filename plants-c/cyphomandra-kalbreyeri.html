Cyphomandra kalbreyeri

Cyphomandra kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's bitter apple, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is native to the Andes mountains of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant produces small, yellow-orange fruits that are typically bitter and not suitable for human consumption. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in cool, humid climates.

It is often found growing in rocky or mountainous terrain.
