Connarus lambertii

Connarus lambertii, commonly known as the Lambert's connarus, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the rainforests of Madagascar.

It is a small shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense, leathery foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Due to its restricted habitat and fragmented distribution, Connarus lambertii is considered endangered and faces significant threats from habitat loss and climate change.
