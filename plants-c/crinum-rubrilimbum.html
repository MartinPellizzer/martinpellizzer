Crinum rubrilimbum

Crinum rubrilimbum, commonly known as the red-lipped crinum, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, with a distinctive red or pink lip. The plant has strap-like, lance-shaped leaves that are typically broad and grass-like in appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Crinum rubrilimbum is known for its strong fragrance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
