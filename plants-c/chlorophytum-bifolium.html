Chlorophytum bifolium

Chlorophytum bifolium, commonly known as the spider plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette of long, narrow leaves that are often striped with white or silver markings. The plant produces small, grass-like inflorescences that bear white or pale green flowers. It is well-known for its ability to produce offsets, which can be easily propagated to grow new plants.

Chlorophytum bifolium is a popular houseplant due to its low maintenance and air-purifying properties.
