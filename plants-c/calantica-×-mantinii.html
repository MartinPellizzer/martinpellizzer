Calantica × mantinii

Calantica × mantinii, commonly known as the hybrid snowbell, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Caprifoliaceae and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. However, it is relatively rare in cultivation and requires well-drained soil and full sun to thrive.
