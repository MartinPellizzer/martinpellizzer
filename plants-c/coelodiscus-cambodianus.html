Coelodiscus cambodianus

Coelodiscus cambodianus, commonly known as the Cambodian flame flower, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is known for its showy inflorescences that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically ovate with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.
