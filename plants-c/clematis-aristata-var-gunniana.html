Clematis aristata var. gunniana

Clematis aristata var. gunniana, commonly known as the Gunnian Clematis, is a deciduous climbing vine native to New Zealand.

It features finely divided, feathery leaves that are typically green with a slight reddish tinge. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of blue and purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires support for its vigorous growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
