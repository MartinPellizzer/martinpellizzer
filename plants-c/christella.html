Christella

Christella, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple ray flowers and silver-green foliage. The plants are typically found in coastal areas, where they thrive in saline and sandy soils. Christella species are often used in ornamental gardening due to their striking appearance and hardiness.

They play an important role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and supporting local wildlife.
