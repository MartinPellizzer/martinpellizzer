Centaurea sabulosa

Centaurea sabulosa, commonly known as sand centaury, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with erect, branching stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces striking blue to purple flowers in summer, which are clustered in dense, spherical heads. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats.

Centaurea sabulosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize sandy soils.
