Ceropegia moorei

Ceropegia moorei, commonly known as the string of hearts or angel's wings, is a climbing plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green markings. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
