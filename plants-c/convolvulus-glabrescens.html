Convolvulus glabrescens

Convolvulus glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual with trailing stems that produce heart-shaped leaves and showy blue to violet flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats. Its flowers bloom in the early morning, hence its common name, and it is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread vigorously.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for gardening purposes.
