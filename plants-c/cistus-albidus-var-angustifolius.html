Cistus albidus var. angustifolius

Cistus albidus var. angustifolius, commonly known as narrow-leaved rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features aromatic, silver-green foliage with narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces showy, pale yellow or white flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is drought-tolerant.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
