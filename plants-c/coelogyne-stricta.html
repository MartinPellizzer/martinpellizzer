Coelogyne stricta

Coelogyne stricta, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Coelogyne stricta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
