Ceropegia plicatiloba

Ceropegia plicatiloba, commonly known as the zigzag ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, trailing stems and distinctive, wavy leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky surfaces or in sandy soils.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for ornamental gardening in dry climates.
