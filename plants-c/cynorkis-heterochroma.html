Cynorkis heterochroma

Cynorkis heterochroma, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that often exhibit a mix of colors, including white, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid environments. Its leaves are usually elongated and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

The flowers are highly fragrant, attracting various pollinators such as bees and moths.
