Calanthe austrokiusiuensis

Calanthe austrokiusiuensis, commonly known as the Sichuan calanthe, is a rare and endemic orchid species native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist forest environments, preferring well-drained, acidic soils. Its floral structure includes a single, resupinate flower with a distinctive lip and spathulate sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
