Cypripedium × memoria-fournieri

Cypripedium × memoria-fournieri, commonly known as the hybrid lady's slipper orchid, is a rare and highly sought-after orchid species.

It is a hybrid between Cypripedium calceolus and Cypripedium micranthum, resulting in a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in cool, moist environments with partial shade, often found in temperate forests.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is considered a valuable specimen in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
