Cosmianthemum longibracteatum

Cosmianthemum longibracteatum, commonly known as the long-bract cosmos, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and has a sprawling growth habit. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique bracts, which are modified leaves, give it its common name and contribute to its visual appeal.
