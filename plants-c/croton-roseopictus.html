Croton roseopictus

Croton roseopictus, commonly known as the Variegated Croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, multicolored leaves that display shades of green, yellow, and red, creating a visually appealing foliage. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in bright, indirect light. Its leaves are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting colors, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Croton roseopictus requires well-draining soil and moderate watering to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
