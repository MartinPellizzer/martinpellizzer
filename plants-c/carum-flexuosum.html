Carum flexuosum

Carum flexuosum, commonly known as flexible caraway, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like parsley and dill. The plant is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in umbels. Its leaves are finely divided and emit a mild, aromatic scent.

Carum flexuosum is often grown for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils.
