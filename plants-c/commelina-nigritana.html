Commelina nigritana

Commelina nigritana, commonly known as black nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes various ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features striking dark purple to black flowers that bloom in clusters, often with white or yellow markings. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Commelina nigritana is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands.
