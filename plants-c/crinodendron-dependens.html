Crinodendron dependens

Crinodendron dependens, commonly known as the hanging crinodendron, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its unique growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
