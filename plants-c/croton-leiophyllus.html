Croton leiophyllus

Croton leiophyllus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved croton, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the species. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Croton leiophyllus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
