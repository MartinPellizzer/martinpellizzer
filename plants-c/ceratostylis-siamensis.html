Ceratostylis siamensis

Ceratostylis siamensis, commonly known as the Siam orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests and wetlands. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip.

Ceratostylis siamensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
