Croton azuensis

Croton azuensis, commonly known as the Azuense Croton, is a shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically alternate and can vary in shape, often displaying vibrant hues of red, orange, and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
