Cotyledon barbeyi var. barbeyi

Cotyledon barbeyi var. barbeyi, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, providing structural support and reducing water loss.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
