Clematis × lawsoniana var. gablenzii

Clematis × lawsoniana var. gablenzii, commonly known as the Gabelnzii Clematis, is a hybrid vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to climb using its tendrils. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its foliage is heart-shaped and provides a lush green backdrop to the flowers.

This variety is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
