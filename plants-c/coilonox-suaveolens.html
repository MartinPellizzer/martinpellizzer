Coilonox suaveolens

Coilonox suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented coilonox, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, small white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Coilonox suaveolens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and pleasant fragrance.
