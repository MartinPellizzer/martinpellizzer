Clerodendrum leucosceptrum

Clerodendrum leucosceptrum, commonly known as white-flowered clerodendrum, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and its flowers emit a mild fragrance. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

The species is also noted for its ability to thrive in partial shade to full sun conditions.
