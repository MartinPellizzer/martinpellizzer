Cieca triquetra

Cieca triquetra, commonly known as the three-lobed cieca, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves.

The leaves are deeply divided into three equal lobes, giving the plant a symmetrical and delicate appearance. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white in color.

Its root system is fibrous and adapted to absorb nutrients from poor soil conditions.
