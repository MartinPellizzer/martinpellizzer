Croton mutisianus

Croton mutisianus, commonly known as the Mutilis Croton, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often ornamental species. It typically grows as an understory plant in humid forests, thriving in partial shade and high humidity.
