Commelina ludens

Commelina ludens, commonly known as the slender commelina, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Commelinaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white vein pattern and produces small, blue-violet flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and texture to shaded garden spaces.
