Caladium veitchii

Caladium veitchii, commonly known as the elephant ear, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in diameter. The foliage is typically a vibrant mix of red, pink, and white, creating a striking visual display. This plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its dramatic appearance and is popular in tropical gardens.
