Cyclamen cyprium

Cyclamen cyprium, commonly known as the Cyprus cyclamen, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the island of Cyprus.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces showy, bowl-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically blooms in late winter to early spring, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers well-drained soil.

Cyclamen cyprium is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its delicate and long-lasting blooms.
