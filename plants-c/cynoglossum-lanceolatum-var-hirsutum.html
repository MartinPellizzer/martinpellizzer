Cynoglossum lanceolatum var. hirsutum

Cynoglossum lanceolatum var. hirsutum, commonly known as hairy heartworts, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped, hairy leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its hairy foliage and striking blooms make it a notable addition to gardens and natural habitats.
