Celosia toenjesii

Celosia toenjesii, commonly known as the flame flower or toenjesii celosia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, spiky inflorescences that resemble flames. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the flowers come in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

Celosia toenjesii is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking appearance and ability to thrive in warm, sunny conditions.
