Chromolaena Multiflosculosa

Chromolaena multiflosculosa is a tropical plant known for its rapid growth and invasive tendencies. It is native to Central and South America but has spread to many other regions worldwide. The plant features broad, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in disturbed soils and can quickly take over native vegetation. Due to its aggressive nature, it is often considered a problematic weed in agricultural and natural areas.
