Craterispermum brachynematum

Craterispermum brachynematum, commonly known as the short-stemmed crater pod, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, branching stems and small, elliptical leaves. Due to its resilience to drought, it is often found in rocky or sandy soils where water is scarce.
