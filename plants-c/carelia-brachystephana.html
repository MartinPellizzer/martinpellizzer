Carelia brachystephana

Carelia brachystephana, commonly known as the dwarf carelia, is a small evergreen shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features narrow, leathery leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of white or pale pink during the spring.

Carelia brachystephana is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its low maintenance requirements.
