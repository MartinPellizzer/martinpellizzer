Clarkia Biloba

Clarkia biloba is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the western United States. It is known for its vibrant pink to red petals and its unique, bell-shaped blooms. This species thrives in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
