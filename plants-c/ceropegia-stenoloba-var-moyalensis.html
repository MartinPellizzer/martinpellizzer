Ceropegia stenoloba var. moyalensis

Ceropegia stenoloba var. moyalensis, commonly known as the Moyal's ceropegia, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, trailing stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
