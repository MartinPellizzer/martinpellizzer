Calamagrostis × subulata

Calamagrostis × subulata, commonly known as silver feather reed grass, is a hybrid grass species native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and feathery plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its ornamental value comes from its distinctive color and upright growth habit.

It is often used in water gardens, rain gardens, and as a landscape accent plant.
