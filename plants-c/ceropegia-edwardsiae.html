Ceropegia edwardsiae

Ceropegia edwardsiae, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia edwardsiae is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
