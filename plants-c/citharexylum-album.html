Citharexylum album

Citharexylum album, commonly known as silverleaf citharexylum, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often clustered in leaf axils. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are usually covered in fine, silvery hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or disturbed areas.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.
