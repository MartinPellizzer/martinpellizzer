Calea pachensis var. trianae

Calea pachensis var. trianae, commonly known as the triana's calea, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires moderate to high light exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
