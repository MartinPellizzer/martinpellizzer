Cyclopia heterophylla

Cyclopia heterophylla, commonly known as the Cape gold or yellow star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a compact, shrub-like growth habit with hairy, glandular leaves. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Cyclopia heterophylla thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
