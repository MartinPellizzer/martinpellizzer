Cardamine hirsuta var. regeliana

Cardamine hirsuta var. regeliana, commonly known as the Regel's hairy watercress, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many edible and medicinal plants. The plant features small, alternate leaves with a hairy texture, and it produces clusters of tiny white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
