Clinopodium chinense subsp. chinense

Clinopodium chinense subsp.

chinense, commonly known as Chinese oregano, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia. It features aromatic, narrow leaves with a mint-like scent, and its small, tubular flowers are typically blue to purple in color.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a suitable candidate for woodland gardens. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional use in herbal medicine.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
