Chlorophytum calateifolium

Chlorophytum calateifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed spider plant, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features long, arching stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically green with white stripes. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
