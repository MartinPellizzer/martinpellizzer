Cyanocephalus tripartitus

Cyanocephalus tripartitus, commonly known as the three-part bluehead, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue flowers and distinctive three-parted structure in its inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as cloud forests. Its leaves are broad and have a slightly waxy texture, which helps in retaining moisture.
