Chimonobambusa marmorea f. variegata

Chimonobambusa marmorea f. variegata, commonly known as the variegated marble bamboo, is a unique ornamental bamboo species characterized by its striking variegated foliage.

Its leaves exhibit a mix of green, white, and pink hues, creating a visually appealing contrast against the dark green stems. This bamboo is native to China and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and partial shade. It grows as a clump-forming plant, reaching moderate heights, and is valued for its aesthetic appeal in garden settings.

Due to its slow growth and compact size, it is ideal for small spaces and container gardening.
