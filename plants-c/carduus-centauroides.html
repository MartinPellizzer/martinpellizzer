Carduus centauroides

Carduus centauroides, commonly known as the centaury thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky, or sandy soils. The plant features spiny, lobed leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Carduus centauroides is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and digestive properties.
