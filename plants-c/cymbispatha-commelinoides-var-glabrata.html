Cymbispatha commelinoides var. glabrata

Cymbispatha commelinoides var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed cymbispatha, is a member of the Commelinaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically smooth and glabrous, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
