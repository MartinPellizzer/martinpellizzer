Campylocentrum kuntzei

Campylocentrum kuntzei, commonly known as the Kuntze's starfruit, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.
