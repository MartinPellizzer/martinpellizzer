Cirsium pitouchaoense

Cirsium pitouchaoense, commonly known as the Pitouchao thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of China, specifically in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its spiny, upright stems and large, dark green, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically purple or violet, forming dense, spherical clusters.

Cirsium pitouchaoense is adapted to harsh, mountainous environments and plays a role in its local ecosystem.
