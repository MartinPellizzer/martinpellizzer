Cattleya × veitchii

Cattleya × veitchii, commonly known as the Veitchii Cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, and showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in low-light conditions. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in well-draining potting media.

This orchid is popular among growers for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
