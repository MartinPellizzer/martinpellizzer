Carduus × hastatus

Carduus × hastatus, commonly known as the common cocklebur.

It is a hybrid species within the Asteraceae family, characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and large, spherical prickly fruits. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas. Its flowers are yellow and composite, consisting of many tiny florets arranged in a disc.

The species is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
