Cyperus aucheri

Cyperus aucheri, commonly known as the silver spur grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically borne on slender stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along water margins.

Cyperus aucheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in water gardens and bog landscapes.
