Cyperus intermedius

Cyperus intermedius, commonly known as spikerush, is a flowering plant in the sedge family, Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in wetland environments such as marshes, swamps, and along the edges of ponds. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered spikelets that are often reddish in color. Cyperus intermedius is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in regions with high moisture levels.

It plays an important ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
