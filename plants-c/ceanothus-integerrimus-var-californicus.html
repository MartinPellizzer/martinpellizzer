Ceanothus integerrimus var. californicus

Ceanothus integerrimus var. californicus, commonly known as California ceanothus, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils in sunny to partially shaded areas. It is often found in chaparral and coastal scrub habitats.

Its aromatic leaves and showy flowers make it a valuable ornamental and ecological plant.
