Ceratolobus laevigatus var. borneensis

Ceratolobus laevigatus var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean horned bean, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its distinctive, horn-like seed pods. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers, followed by the unique, spiky fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
