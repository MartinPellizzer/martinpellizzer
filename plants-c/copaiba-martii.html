Copaiba martii

Copaiba martii, commonly known as yellow copaiba, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Burseraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark that exudes a fragrant, amber-colored resin. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions. Its resin has been traditionally used for medicinal and aromatic purposes.

The tree plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
