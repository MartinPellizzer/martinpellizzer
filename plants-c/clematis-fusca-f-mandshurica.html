Clematis fusca f. mandshurica

Clematis fusca f. mandshurica, commonly known as the Siberian Clematis, is a variant of the species Clematis fusca.

It is a deciduous vine characterized by its slender stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas of eastern Asia.

This form is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
