Cotoneaster nummularioides

Cotoneaster nummularioides, commonly known as the Japanese cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a glossy green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white flowers in early spring, followed by bright red berries that persist into winter. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.

Cotoneaster nummularioides prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
