Cymbidium maguanense

Cymbidium maguanense, commonly known as the Maguanense orchid, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and its inflorescence consists of several clustered flowers with a distinctive, fragrant scent.

Cymbidium maguanense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its beauty and resilience.
