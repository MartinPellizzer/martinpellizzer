Chrysopogon densipaniculatus

Chrysopogon densipaniculatus, commonly known as the dense panicle grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compact, dense inflorescence that forms a panicle, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green coloration.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
