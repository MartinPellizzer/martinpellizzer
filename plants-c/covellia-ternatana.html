Covellia ternatana

Covellia ternatana, commonly known as the Ternate orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the island of Ternate in Indonesia, where it thrives in tropical rainforest environments. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. It typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting epiphytic tendencies.

Covellia ternatana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by horticulturists for its unique floral display.
