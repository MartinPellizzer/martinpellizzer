Cerasus yunnanensis var. polybotrys

Cerasus yunnanensis var. polybotrys, commonly known as the Yunnan cherry or the Chinese cherry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
