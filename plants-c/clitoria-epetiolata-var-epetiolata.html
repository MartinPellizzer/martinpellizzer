Clitoria epetiolata var. epetiolata

Clitoria epetiolata var. epetiolata, commonly known as the white-winged bean, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, butterfly-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has slender, often hairless stems and trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets.

It produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of forage.
