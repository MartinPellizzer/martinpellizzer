Critoniopsis unguiculata

Critoniopsis unguiculata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved thornplant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers that are often followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
