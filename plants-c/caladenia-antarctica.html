Caladenia antarctica

Caladenia antarctica, commonly known as the Antarctic spider-orchid, is an endemic orchid species native to southern Australia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a slender, leafless stem that bears a single, showy flower. The flower has a distinctive spider-like appearance, with a narrow, hooded labellum and elongated sepals. It typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils in heathland or open forests.

The plant is adapted to cool, dry conditions and is often found in coastal regions.
