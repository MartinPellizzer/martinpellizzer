Cussonia bojeri

Cussonia bojeri, commonly known as the African bristle fern, is a member of the Araliaceae family.

It is a tall, herbaceous plant characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and distinctive, spadix-like inflorescence. Native to tropical Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and greenhouses.
