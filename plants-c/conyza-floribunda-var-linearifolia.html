Conyza floribunda var. linearifolia

Conyza floribunda var. linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaved cudweed, is a flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, yellowish flowers that form composite heads. Native to parts of Africa and the Middle East, this plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in disturbed soils and can be invasive in certain regions.

The plant has been studied for its potential medicinal properties and ecological roles in its native habitats.
