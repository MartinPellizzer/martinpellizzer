Caragana mongolioa

Caragana mongolioa, commonly known as the Siberian pea shrub, is a deciduous shrub native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in poor soils, making it a hardy species for reclamation projects.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
