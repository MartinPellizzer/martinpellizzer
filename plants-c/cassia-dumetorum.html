Cassia dumetorum

Cassia dumetorum, commonly known as flame acacia, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain seeds covered in a sticky, resinous substance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas.

Cassia dumetorum has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
