Cymbidium dianlan

Cymbidium dianlan, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a species native to China, particularly Yunnan province.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its long, arching inflorescences bearing clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in its natural habitat. Its leaves are strap-shaped and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value.

Cymbidium dianlan is appreciated for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
