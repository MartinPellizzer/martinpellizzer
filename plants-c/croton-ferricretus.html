Croton ferricretus

Croton ferricretus, commonly known as the ironwood croton, is a striking tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often a deep green or reddish hue, and its showy, colorful bracts that range from yellow to red. The plant is known for its slow growth and ability to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Croton ferricretus is also valued for its hardiness and resistance to pests and diseases.
