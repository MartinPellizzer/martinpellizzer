Cyathea aneitensis

Cyathea aneitensis, commonly known as the Aneitei Cyathea, is a species of tree fern native to the island of Kosrae in the Federated States of Micronesia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments, often growing in shaded, moist forest understories. Its rhizomes are thick and fibrous, supporting its upright growth form.

Cyathea aneitensis plays a significant ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for various organisms.
