Crassula ernestii

Crassula ernestii, commonly known as the Ernest's Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and fleshy, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Crassula ernestii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
