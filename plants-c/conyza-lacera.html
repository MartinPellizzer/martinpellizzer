Conyza lacera

Conyza lacera, commonly known as the slender weed or smallflower ironweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is an annual herb that typically grows in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. The plant has slender, branching stems and small, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly rough texture.

Conyza lacera is often found in temperate regions and is known for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
