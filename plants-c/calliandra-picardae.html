Calliandra picardae

Calliandra picardae, commonly known as the pink calliandra, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red inflorescences, which are composed of numerous small, fluffy flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Calliandra picardae is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
