Cattleya guttata var. elatior

Cattleya guttata var. elatior, commonly known as the spotted cattleya, is a hybrid orchid prized for its striking appearance.

It features large, showy flowers with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals and sepals. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

Its blooming period typically occurs during the spring and summer months, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
