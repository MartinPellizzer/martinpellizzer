Canavalia gladiolata

Canavalia gladiolata, commonly known as the sword bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features compound leaves with three leaflets, and its flowers are typically bright yellow with a distinctive spurred shape. The plant produces elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural systems.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a common weed in disturbed soils.
