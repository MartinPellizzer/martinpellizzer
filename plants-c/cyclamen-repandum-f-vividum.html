Cyclamen repandum f. vividum

Cyclamen repandum f. vividum, commonly known as the vivid cyclamen, is a hybrid form of the Persian cyclamen.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored foliage that often features vivid shades of green, red, and yellow. The plant produces showy, spurred flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, typically blooming in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, moist environments and prefers partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms make it a prized ornamental plant in horticulture.
