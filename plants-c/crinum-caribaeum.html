Crinum caribaeum

Crinum caribaeum, commonly known as the Caribbean crinum, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is a bulbous perennial that produces large, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often used in floral arrangements due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas and wetlands.

Crinum caribaeum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral displays.
