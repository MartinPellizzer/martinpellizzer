Conophytum obconellum

Conophytum obconellum, commonly known as the "Bulbous Stonecrop," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, rounded leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it ideal for rock gardens or container planting.

Conophytum obconellum is well-suited to full sun and requires minimal watering, making it a low-maintenance succulent.
