Chirita sericea var. scortechinii

Chirita sericea var. scortechinii, commonly known as the velvet leaf begonia, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive, soft texture. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.

Due to its delicate appearance, it requires careful handling and specific care to maintain its health and aesthetic appeal.
