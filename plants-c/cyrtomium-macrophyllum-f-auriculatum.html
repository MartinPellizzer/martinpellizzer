Cyrtomium macrophyllum f. auriculatum

Cyrtomium macrophyllum f. auriculatum, commonly known as the auriculate water fern, is a fern species characterized by its distinctive, auriculate (ear-shaped) lobes on the fronds.

The plant features bipinnate fronds with a delicate, feathery appearance, and its leaves are typically glossy and dark green. The fronds are evergreen, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
