Cyrtopodium pflanzii

Cyrtopodium pflanzii, commonly known as the Pflanz's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a unique lip structure. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Cyrtopodium pflanzii is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
