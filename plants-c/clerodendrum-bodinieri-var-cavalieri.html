Clerodendrum bodinieri var. cavalieri

Clerodendrum bodinieri var. cavalieri, commonly known as the "Cavalieri's Clerodendrum," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or yellow throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
