Chrysophyllum ferrugineotomentosum

Chrysophyllum ferrugineotomentosum, commonly known as the red-ironwood tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and rough, fissured bark. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, sweet-tart berries. It is valued for its hard, heavy wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
