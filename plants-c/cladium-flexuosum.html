Cladium flexuosum

Cladium flexuosum, commonly known as the slender sawgrass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to wetland and marshy environments in the Americas.

It is characterized by its long, flexible, grass-like leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant thrives in shallow water and is often found in areas with high humidity and nutrient-rich soils. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats, which help stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

Cladium flexuosum plays a vital role in wetland ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
