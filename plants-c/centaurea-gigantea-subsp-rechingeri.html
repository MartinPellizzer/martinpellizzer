Centaurea gigantea subsp. rechingeri

Centaurea gigantea subsp.

rechingeri, commonly known as the giant centaury, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its tall, spindly stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters.

The plant features showy, bright yellow flowers with red or orange centers, arranged in dense, conical inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
