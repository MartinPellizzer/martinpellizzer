Cryptocarya verrucosa

Cryptocarya verrucosa, commonly known as the rough-leaved cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its rough, often hairy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. It typically grows in lowland rainforests and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
