It is a non-vascular plant, meaning it lacks true roots, stems, and leaves in the traditional sense, and instead relies on rhizoids for anchorage.

Cheilanthes geraniifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, low-light conditions.
