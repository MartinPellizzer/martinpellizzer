Caladenia filamentosa var. denticulata

Caladenia filamentosa var. denticulata, commonly known as the fine-leaf spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in heathlands and open forests, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features slender, hairy stems and linear, often hairy leaves. Its flowers are pale green to yellowish with intricate patterns, and it blooms from late autumn to early spring.

It is a protected species due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity.
