Croton barorum var. mangokyensis

Croton barorum var. mangokyensis, commonly known as the Mangoky croton, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage, which often displays shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough, textured surface.

The Mangoky croton is valued in horticulture for its vibrant appearance and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
