Cineraria spathulata

Cineraria spathulata, commonly known as the spoonleaf cineraria, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is characterized by its broad, spoon-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal environments, thriving in well-drained soil. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and blooms.

Cineraria spathulata prefers full sun to partial shade and is relatively drought-tolerant once established.
