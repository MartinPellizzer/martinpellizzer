Campanula rhomboidalis var. reflexa

Campanula rhomboidalis var. reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It features upright stems with lance-shaped, hairy leaves that are often slightly lobed. The flowers are bell-shaped, typically blue to purple in color, and are borne in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
