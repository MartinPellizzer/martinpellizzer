Caladenia behrii

Caladenia behrii, commonly known as the Behri's spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is found in heathlands and open forests. The plant produces a single, slender stem that bears a single, strikingly colored flower with a distinctive spider-like appearance. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color and bloom during the spring.

Caladenia behrii is a member of the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its intricate floral structure and specialized pollination mechanisms.
