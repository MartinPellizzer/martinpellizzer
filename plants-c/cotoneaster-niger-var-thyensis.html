Cotoneaster niger var. thyensis

Cotoneaster niger var. thyensis, commonly known as the black cotoneaster, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black berries that persist through winter, providing ornamental value. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and winter interest in gardens.
