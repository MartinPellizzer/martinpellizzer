Cleisostoma comberi

Cleisostoma comberi, commonly known as the comb-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, comb-like leaf structure. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive lip petal. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Cleisostoma comberi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
