Croton pallens

Croton pallens, commonly known as the silver croton, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display shades of green, white, and silver. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, which includes many species with milky sap. Its leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

Croton pallens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid environments.
