Culcitium

Culcitium, commonly known as the African violet, is a genus of flowering plants native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plants produce showy, velvety flowers in a variety of colors, often with a distinctive white or purple center. Culcitium species are popular as houseplants due to their ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

They thrive in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.
