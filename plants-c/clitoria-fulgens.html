Clitoria fulgens

Clitoria fulgens, commonly known as the winged bean or flame flower, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its striking violet to red flowers, which are often adorned with distinctive wing-like petals. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds, which are used in various cuisines. Its leaves are trifoliate, with heart-shaped leaflets, and the plant thrives in warm, humid climates.

Clitoria fulgens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
