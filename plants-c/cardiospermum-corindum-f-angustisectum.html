Cardiospermum corindum f. angustisectum

Cardiospermum corindum f. angustisectum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf heartseed.

It is a variant of the heartseed plant, characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces distinctive red, inflated fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy, heart-shaped sac. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments.

Its ornamental value is notable due to its unique fruit structure and foliage.
