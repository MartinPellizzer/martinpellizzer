Cyrtopodium macrobulbon f. salvadorense

Cyrtopodium macrobulbon f. salvadorense, commonly known as the Salvadorense Cypripedium, is a rare orchid species native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive purple and yellow coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and covered in a thick, leathery epidermis.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated by enthusiasts.
