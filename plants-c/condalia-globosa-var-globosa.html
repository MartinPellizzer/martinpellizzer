Condalia globosa var. globosa

Condalia globosa var. globosa, commonly known as the globular condalia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical growth habit and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
