Camellia membranacea

Camellia membranacea, commonly known as the paper camellia, is a species of evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery petals that are often white or pale pink in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It prefers acidic, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Camellia membranacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and floral display.
