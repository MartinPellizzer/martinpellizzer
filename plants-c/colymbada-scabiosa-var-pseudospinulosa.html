Colymbada scabiosa var. pseudospinulosa, commonly known as the spiked honesty, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
