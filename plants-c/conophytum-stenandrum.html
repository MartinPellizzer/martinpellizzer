Conophytum stenandrum

Conophytum stenandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to survive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
