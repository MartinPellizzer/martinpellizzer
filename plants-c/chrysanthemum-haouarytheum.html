Chrysanthemum haouarytheum

Chrysanthemum haouarytheum, commonly known as the Yemen chrysanthemum, is a flowering plant native to the Arabian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to hot, dry climates.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species within its native range.
