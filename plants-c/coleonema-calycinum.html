Coleonema calycinum

Coleonema calycinum, commonly known as the Cape honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, compact growth. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom throughout the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, narrow, and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

The plant is adapted to coastal and sandy environments, showing some degree of salt tolerance.
