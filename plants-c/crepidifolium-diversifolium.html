Crepidifolium diversifolium

Crepidifolium diversifolium, commonly known as the broad-leafed crepidifolium, is a species of flowering plant in the family Araceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. Its flowers are clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and unique floral structure.
