Catonia sagittata

Catonia sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead catonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
