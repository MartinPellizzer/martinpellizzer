Cyperus × babae

Cyperus × babae, commonly known as the swamp cyperus, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grassy leaves and spikes of small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in wetland environments, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in moist soils.

Cyperus × babae plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various aquatic organisms.
