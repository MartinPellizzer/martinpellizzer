Cirsium monspessulanum var. compactum

Cirsium monspessulanum var. compactum, commonly known as the compact thistle, is a perennial plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and wastelands. Its leaves are covered in stiff, bristly hairs, and its stems are thick and ribbed.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
