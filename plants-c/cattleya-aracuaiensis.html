Cattleya aracuaiensis

Cattleya aracuaiensis, commonly known as the Aracuai orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its blooms, which can be pink, white, or purple, are highly prized for their beauty and fragrance.

Cattleya aracuaiensis is also valued for its relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
