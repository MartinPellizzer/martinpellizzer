Carex oligostachya

Carex oligostachya, commonly known as the narrowleaf sedges, is a species of flowering plant in the family Cyperaceae.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes, swamps, and along stream banks. Carex oligostachya plays an important role in wetland ecosystems by stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various organisms.
