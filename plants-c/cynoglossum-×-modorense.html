Cynoglossum × modorense

Cynoglossum × modorense, commonly known as the hybrid bellflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that resemble small bells, which bloom in summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Cynoglossum × modorense is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
