Calathea lindeniana

Calathea lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg's calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage with intricate patterns and vibrant hues. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and indirect light.

This plant is popular among collectors for its unique and eye-catching appearance.
