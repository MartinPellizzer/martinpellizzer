Caroxylon iljinii

Caroxylon iljinii, commonly known as the Iljin's carob tree, is a rare and ancient plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its woody, branching structure and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, though it is less commonly cultivated than other carob species.
