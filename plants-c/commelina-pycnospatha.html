Commelina pycnospatha

Commelina pycnospatha, commonly known as the dense-sheath commelina, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Commelinaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features grass-like leaves with a dense, fibrous sheath at the base, and it produces small, blue or purple flowers. Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
