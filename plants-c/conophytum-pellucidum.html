Conophytum pellucidum

Conophytum pellucidum, commonly known as the translucent conophytum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves that are often translucent or have a waxy coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Conophytum pellucidum is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
