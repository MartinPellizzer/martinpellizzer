Commelina Schweinfurthii Subsp. Ceciliae

Commelina schweinfurthii subsp. ceciliae is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America. It belongs to the spiderwort family and is known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. This subspecies is named after Cecilia, likely in recognition of its unique botanical significance. Its delicate appearance makes it a sought-after specimen among plant enthusiasts. Due to its limited distribution, it is also a subject of conservation interest.
