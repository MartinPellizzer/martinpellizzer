Coelogyne cruciformis var. longicuspa

Coelogyne cruciformis var. longicuspa, commonly known as the long-leafed crucifix orchid, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive cruciform (cross-shaped) floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. It is typically epiphytic, growing on trees in tropical rainforests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
