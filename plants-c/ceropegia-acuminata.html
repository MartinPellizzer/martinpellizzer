Ceropegia acuminata

Ceropegia acuminata, commonly known as the string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
