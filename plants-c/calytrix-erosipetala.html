Calytrix erosipetala

Calytrix erosipetala, commonly known as the pink-flowered calytrix, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Calytrix erosipetala is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive floral display.
