Chomelia ribesioides

Chomelia ribesioides, commonly known as the red-flowered chomelia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the warm seasons. The plant belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal species. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.

Due to its attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking to add color and texture to tropical landscapes.
