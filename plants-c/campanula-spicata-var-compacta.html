Campanula spicata var. compacta

Campanula spicata var. compacta, commonly known as the compact bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It features dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple, typically blooming in summer. The plant has upright, compact growth habit with slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
