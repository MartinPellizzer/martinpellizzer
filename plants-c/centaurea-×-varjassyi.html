Centaurea × varjassyi

Centaurea × varjassyi, commonly known as the Varjassyi knapweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers and is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves, and it produces small, bristly seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
