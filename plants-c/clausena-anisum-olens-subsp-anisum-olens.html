Clausena anisum-olens subsp. anisum-olens

Clausena anisum-olens subsp.

anisum-olens, commonly known as the anise-flavored Clausena, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers.

The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically harvested when mature. Its leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine and as a spice due to their distinctive anise-like scent.
