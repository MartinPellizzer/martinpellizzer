Centropogon polytrichus

Centropogon polytrichus, commonly known as the purpletop grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and vibrant purple to pink inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in prairie and wetland habitats. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib.

Centropogon polytrichus plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
