Croton curvipes

Croton curvipes, commonly known as the zigzag croton, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, zigzagging stems that add an ornamental appeal to its appearance. The plant produces vibrant, palmate leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, yellow, and red. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically clustered in spikes, while its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Croton curvipes thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
