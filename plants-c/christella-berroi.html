Christella berroi

Christella berroi, commonly known as the dwarf rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing year-round interest in gardens.

Christella berroi is valued for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in xerothermic conditions.
