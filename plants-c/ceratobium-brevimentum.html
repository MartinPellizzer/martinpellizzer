Ceratobium brevimentum

Ceratobium brevimentum, commonly known as the short-stemmed wireweed, is a small, mat-forming plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit. The plant has small, scale-like leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. It produces tiny, greenish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Ceratobium brevimentum is well adapted to drought conditions and is often found in disturbed soils and rocky areas.
