Cryptantha maritima var. maritima

Cryptantha maritima var. maritima, commonly known as the coastal cryptantha, is a flowering plant native to the western coast of North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils along coastal dunes and cliffs. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to withstand harsh coastal conditions.

It plays a role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and provides habitat for various insects and small animals.
