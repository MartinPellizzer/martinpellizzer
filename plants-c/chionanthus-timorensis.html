Chionanthus timorensis

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The Timor snowbell is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens for its delicate floral display.
