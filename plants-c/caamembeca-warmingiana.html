Caamembeca warmingiana

Caamembeca warmingiana, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vivid display during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a notable species in tropical and subtropical landscapes.
