Ceropegia turricula

Ceropegia turricula, commonly known as the spiral ceropegia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive spiral arrangement of leaves, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Ceropegia turricula is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.
