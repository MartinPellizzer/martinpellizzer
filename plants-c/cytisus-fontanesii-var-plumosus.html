Cytisus fontanesii var. plumosus

Cytisus fontanesii var. plumosus, commonly known as the plumose broom, is a shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features delicate, feathery leaves that give it a soft, airy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers in clusters, typical of the Fabaceae family. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and rocky environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and long-lasting blooms.
