Clematis rendatleri

Clematis rendatleri, commonly known as the Chinese vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five oval leaflets, and its flowers are typically blue or purple, appearing in late spring to early summer. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental vines.
