Clerodendrum bungei var. bungei

Clerodendrum bungei var. bungei, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a flowering shrub native to East Asia.

It features striking red, papery bracts that resemble small lanterns, which are not true flowers but modified leaves. The plant produces small, tubular yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
