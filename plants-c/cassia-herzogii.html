Cassia herzogii

Cassia herzogii, commonly known as the Herzog's cassia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Cassia herzogii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
