Chlorophytum tillariense

Chlorophytum tillariense, commonly known as the African lily, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its upright, strap-like leaves that form a rosette. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences with small, white to pale blue flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate watering, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its elegant foliage and delicate blooms.
