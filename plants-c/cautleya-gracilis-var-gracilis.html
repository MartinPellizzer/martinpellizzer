Cautleya gracilis var. gracilis

Cautleya gracilis var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish-white flowers. Its botanical features include a rhizomatous root system and a preference for well-drained, acidic soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable growing conditions.
