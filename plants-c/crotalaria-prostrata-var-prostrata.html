Crotalaria prostrata var. prostrata

Crotalaria prostrata var. prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate crotalaria, is a flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 cm, with a sprawling, prostrate growth habit. The plant features palmately trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.
