Caladenia nobilis

Caladenia nobilis, commonly known as the Noble Spider-orchid, is an exquisite orchid native to southeastern Australia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single, strikingly colored flower that resembles a spider, hence its common name. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing a single, intricate flower with a dark, velvety labellum. Its habitat includes dry grasslands and open forests, where it thrives in well-drained soils.

Caladenia nobilis is a protected species due to its rarity and specific environmental requirements.
