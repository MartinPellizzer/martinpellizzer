Chenopodium ugandae

Chenopodium ugandae, commonly known as Uganda goosefoot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is native to East Africa, particularly found in Uganda and surrounding regions. The plant is characterized by its alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Chenopodium ugandae is adapted to grow in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
