Chamaecyparis Obtusa F. Aurea

Chamaecyparis obtusa f. aurea is a striking variant of the Japanese cedar known for its vibrant golden foliage. This unique form showcases narrow, scale-like leaves that shimmer with a rich yellow hue throughout the growing season. Often cultivated as a ornamental tree, it adds a touch of elegance to gardens and landscapes. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for small spaces and container gardening. With proper care, this plant can thrive and provide year-round visual interest.
