Chrysosplenium gracilescens

Chrysosplenium gracilescens, commonly known as slender chrysosplenium, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and habitat conservation.
