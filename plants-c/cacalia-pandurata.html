Cacalia pandurata

Cacalia pandurata, commonly known as the narrowleaf bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Cacalia pandurata thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
