Callista glossotis

Callista glossotis, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte or lithophyte, often found on trees or rocks in humid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or greenish-yellow with prominent, hairy lips, and it blooms throughout the year in suitable conditions.

Callista glossotis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
