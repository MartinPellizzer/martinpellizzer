Ceropegia splendida

Ceropegia splendida, commonly known as the silver leaf or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Ceropegia splendida is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to thrive in indoor environments.
