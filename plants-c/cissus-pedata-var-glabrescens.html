Cissus pedata var. glabrescens

Cissus pedata var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth leaf cissus, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by edible, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor ornamental use.
