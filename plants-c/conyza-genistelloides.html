Conyza genistelloides

Conyza genistelloides, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is an annual herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a star-like appearance. The plant has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.

Conyza genistelloides is adapted to arid conditions and is known for its rapid growth and short life cycle.
