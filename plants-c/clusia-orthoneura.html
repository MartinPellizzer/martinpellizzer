Clusia orthoneura

Clusia orthoneura, commonly known as the waxleaf clusia, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that develop into orange-red berries. It is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought and pollution, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Clusia orthoneura is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of natural rubber.
