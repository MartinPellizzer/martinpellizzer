Cryptocarya obovata

Cryptocarya obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed cryptocarya, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are often aromatic. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in panicles.

The fruit is a small, round drupe that matures to a dark purple color.
