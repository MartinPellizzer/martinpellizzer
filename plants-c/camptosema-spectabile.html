Camptosema spectabile

Camptosema spectabile, commonly known as the showy camptosema, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that consist of clusters of small, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its leaves are alternate and often have a rough or hairy texture.

Camptosema spectabile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
