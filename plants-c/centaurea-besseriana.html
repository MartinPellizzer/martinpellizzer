Centaurea besseriana

Centaurea besseriana, commonly known as the Balkan knapweed, is a flowering plant native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. Its bright yellow flowers bloom in summer, attracting various pollinators.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
