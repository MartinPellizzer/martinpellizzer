Centaurea hekimhanensis

Centaurea hekimhanensis, commonly known as the Hekimhan thistle, is a flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its spiny leaves and purple to pink flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its botanical features include a basal rosette of leaves and a flowering stem that rises above the foliage.

It is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
