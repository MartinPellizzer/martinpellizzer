Crocus yalovensis

Crocus yalovensis, commonly known as the Yalovensis Crocus, is a species of flowering plant native to Turkey.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its early blooming habit, often appearing in late autumn or early winter. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, which are typically borne on short, sturdy stems. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
