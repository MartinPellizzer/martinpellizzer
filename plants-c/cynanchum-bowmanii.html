Cynanchum bowmanii

Cynanchum bowmanii, commonly known as Bowman's milkweed, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the United States.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae, which includes many species of milkweeds. The plant is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. It produces follicles that contain toxic latex, a feature common among milkweed species.

Bowman's milkweed is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
