Cyathea quadrata var. ivohibensis

Cyathea quadrata var. ivohibensis, commonly known as the Ivohibena tree fern, is a species native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate fronds. The plant exhibits a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizomes are fibrous and form a dense network that helps stabilize the soil.

This fern is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
