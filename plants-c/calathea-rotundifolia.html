Calathea rotundifolia

Calathea rotundifolia, commonly known as the velvet leaf calathea, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that display intricate patterns in shades of green, white, and pink. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. Its foliage is often used in interior design due to its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying properties.

Calathea rotundifolia requires consistent moisture and high humidity to maintain its vibrant appearance.
