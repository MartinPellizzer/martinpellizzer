Citrullus vulgaris

Citrullus vulgaris, commonly known as watermelon, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its sprawling vines and large, fleshy, lobed leaves. The plant produces bright green, spherical fruits with a thick rind and juicy, red or yellow flesh. Watermelon flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant.

The fruit is rich in water, sugars, and nutrients, making it a popular summer fruit worldwide.
