Cestrum kellermanii

Cestrum kellermanii, commonly known as the purple cestrum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy green appearance.

Cestrum kellermanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant night-time fragrance.
