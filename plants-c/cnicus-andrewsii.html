Cnicus andrewsii

Cnicus andrewsii, commonly known as the Andrew's prickly lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and daisy-like appearance. The plant features hairy, lobed leaves and produces small yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Cnicus andrewsii is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often considered a weed in agricultural areas.
