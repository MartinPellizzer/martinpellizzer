Cephalanthera longifolia f. longibracteata

Cephalanthera longifolia f. longibracteata, commonly known as the long-bract helmet orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to parts of Europe.

It is characterized by its elongated, hood-like flower that is typically pale green to yellowish in color. The plant features a slender, upright stem with a few broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne on long, slender pedicels and are adorned with distinctive, elongated bracts.

This variant is distinguished by its longer bracts compared to the typical form of Cephalanthera longifolia.
