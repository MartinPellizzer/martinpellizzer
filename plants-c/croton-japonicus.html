Croton japonicus

Croton japonicus, commonly known as Japanese croton, is a shrub native to Japan and Korea.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from yellow to red and orange. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Croton japonicus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
