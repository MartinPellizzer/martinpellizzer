Capsella bursa-pastoris gotlandica

Capsella bursa-pastoris gotlandica, commonly known as the Gotland chickweed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, white or pink flowers and hairy, lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open areas, such as roadsides and fields. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This subspecies is native to northern Europe and is often found in coastal regions.
