Coleus stenostachys

Coleus stenostachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed coleus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and ornamental plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often brightly colored, making it a popular choice for gardens. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically clustered in whorls along the stem.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant foliage.
