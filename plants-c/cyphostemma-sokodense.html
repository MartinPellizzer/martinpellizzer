Cyphostemma sokodense

Cyphostemma sokodense, commonly known as the Sokodense moonvine, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, heart-shaped leaves. Due to its unique appearance and growth habit, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
