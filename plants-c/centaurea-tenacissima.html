Centaurea tenacissima

Centaurea tenacissima, commonly known as Spanish redtop, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its bright red flowers and bristly, upright growth habit. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a common sight in disturbed habitats.

Centaurea tenacissima is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine.
