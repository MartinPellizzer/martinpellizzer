Crassula sieberiana var. acuminata

Crassula sieberiana var. acuminata, commonly known as the Cape Hottentot, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features compact, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight red or purple tinge under stress. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
