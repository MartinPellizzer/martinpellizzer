Cotyledon sprucei

Cotyledon sprucei, commonly known as the Silver Leaf Cotyledon, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax, giving it a silvery appearance. The leaves are typically oval or spoon-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

This plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.
