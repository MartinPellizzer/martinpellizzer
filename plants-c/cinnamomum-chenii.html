Cinnamomum chenii

Cinnamomum chenii, commonly known as Chinese cinnamon or Cinnamomum cassia, is a species of flowering plant in the樟科 ( Lauraceae ) family.

It is native to southern China, particularly in the provinces of Guangdong, Guangxi, and Yunnan. The plant is characterized by its aromatic bark, which is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its leaves are elliptical, dark green, and have a glossy appearance.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval-shaped berries.
