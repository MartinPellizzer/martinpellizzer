Crambe Orientalis Var. Juncea

Crambe orientalis var. juncea is a lesser-known member of the Brassicaceae family that thrives in temperate climates. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated in the gardening community. Its unique features make it a valuable addition to any garden or botanical collection.
