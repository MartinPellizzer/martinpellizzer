Chamaefistula rekoi

Chamaefistula rekoi, commonly known as the Rekoi's thornapple, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of East Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Chamaefistula rekoi is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
