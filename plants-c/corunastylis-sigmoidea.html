Corunastylis sigmoidea

Corunastylis sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Corunastylis sigmoidea is often found in coastal heathlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
