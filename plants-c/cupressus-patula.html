Cupressus patula

Cupressus patula, commonly known as the Mexican cypress, is a coniferous evergreen tree native to Mexico.

It has a narrow, conical shape with dense, scale-like leaves that are bluish-green in color. The tree produces small, spherical cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.

Cupressus patula prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings.
