Chrysosplenium macrostemon var. macrostemon

Chrysosplenium macrostemon var. macrostemon, commonly known as the broadstem golden-spleenwort, is a member of the Saxifragaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
