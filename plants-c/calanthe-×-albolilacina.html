Calanthe × albolilacina

Calanthe × albolilacina, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a fascinating botanical hybrid resulting from the cross between Calanthe inebriata and Calanthe marmorata.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and lilac. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Calanthe × albolilacina is valued in horticulture for its beauty and adaptability to cultivated conditions.
