Cayaponia sessiliflora

Cayaponia sessiliflora, commonly known as the Cayaponia or the Guariroba, is a shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by woody, oblong fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in undergrowth layers of tropical forests.

The plant has been used in traditional medicine for its potential healing properties.
