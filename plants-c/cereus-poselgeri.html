Cereus poselgeri

Cereus poselgeri, commonly known as the Poselger's Christmas cactus, is a flowering cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, spiny stems and is typically evergreen. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red, blooming during the holiday season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Cereus poselgeri is relatively low-maintenance and thrives in arid environments with minimal water.
