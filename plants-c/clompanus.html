Clompanus

Clompanus, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a unique species characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage.

The plant features small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a decorative touch to its appearance. Its leaves are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in dry environments.

Clompanus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
