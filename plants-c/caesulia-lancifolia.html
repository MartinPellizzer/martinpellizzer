Caesulia lancifolia

Caesulia lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pink or white flowers.
