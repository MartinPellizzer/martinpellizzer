Cyrtogonellum × rupicola

Cyrtogonellum × rupicola, commonly known as the hybrid rock daisy, is a perennial shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Its foliage consists of narrow, gray-green leaves that are adapted to conserve moisture.

Cyrtogonellum × rupicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging environmental conditions.
