Cynoglossum longepetiolatum

Cynoglossum longepetiolatum, commonly known as the long-petioled bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features showy, violet-blue flowers that resemble small bells, typically blooming in late summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with long petioles, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Cynoglossum longepetiolatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and naturalized areas.
