Coreopsis prestinaria

Coreopsis prestinaria, commonly known as the slender coreopsis, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and wetlands. The plant features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a slightly fuzzy texture.

Coreopsis prestinaria is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
