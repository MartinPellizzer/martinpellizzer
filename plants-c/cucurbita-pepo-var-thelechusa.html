Cucurbita pepo var. thelechusa

Cucurbita pepo var. thelechusa, commonly known as the Turk's Turban gourd, is a cultivated variety of the common gourd species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, elongated, and often twisted fruit shape, which can range in color from green to yellow or orange. The plant is a vine that produces large, lobed leaves and yellow flowers typical of the Cucurbitaceae family. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and can also be used in culinary applications when mature.
