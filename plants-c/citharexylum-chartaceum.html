Citharexylum chartaceum

Citharexylum chartaceum, commonly known as paperleaf cithara, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery leaves that are often white or pale green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and landscape design.
