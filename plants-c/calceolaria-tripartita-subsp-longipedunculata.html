Calceolaria tripartita subsp. longipedunculata

Calceolaria tripartita subsp.

longipedunculata, commonly known as the long pedunculate slipper orchid, is a member of the Campanulaceae family. It is native to the Andean regions of South America, particularly in countries like Peru and Ecuador.

This species is characterized by its striking, slipper-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces elongated peduncles that bear multiple flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands and rocky slopes.
