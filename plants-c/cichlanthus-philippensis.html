Cichlanthus philippensis

Cichlanthus philippensis, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its roots are fibrous, and it has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green.

The flowers are usually pale yellow or white, with a distinctive lip that aids in pollination by insects.
