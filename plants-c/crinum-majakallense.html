Crinum majakallense

Crinum majakallense, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its tall, strap-like leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces large, white or pinkish-white blooms that are often adorned with a yellow or greenish central stripe. Crinum majakallense thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
