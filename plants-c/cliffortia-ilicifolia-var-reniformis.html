Cliffortia ilicifolia var. reniformis

Cliffortia ilicifolia var. reniformis, commonly known as the reniform cliffortia, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with oval to kidney-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white, five-petaled flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for landscaping in Mediterranean climates.
