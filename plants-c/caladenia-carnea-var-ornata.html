Caladenia carnea var. ornata

Caladenia carnea var. ornata, commonly known as the Ornate Spider-orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and its distinctive, spider-like appearance, with long, narrow petals and a prominent labellum. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental appeal. This variety thrives in sandy soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a sought-after plant in horticulture and conservation efforts.
