Carex × microstachya f. superdioeca

Carex × microstachya f. superdioeca, commonly known as the dwarf sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a muted green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are usually wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in aquatic environments.
