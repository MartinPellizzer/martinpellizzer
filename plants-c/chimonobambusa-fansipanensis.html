Chimonobambusa fansipanensis

Chimonobambusa fansipanensis, commonly known as the Fansipan bamboo, is a species of grass family plant native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its slender, erect culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions.

This bamboo is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional crafts in its native habitat.
