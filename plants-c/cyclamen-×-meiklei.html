Cyclamen × meiklei

Cyclamen × meiklei, commonly known as the Meikle cyclamen, is a hybrid plant resulting from crosses between Cyclamen persicum and Cyclamen coum.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety foliage and vibrant, bowl-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors such as pink, red, and white. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe and thrives in cool, moist environments. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant for indoor or outdoor gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Cyclamen × meiklei prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for spring blooms.
