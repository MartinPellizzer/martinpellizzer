Crepidomanes bipunctatum var. venulosum

Crepidomanes bipunctatum var. venulosum, commonly known as the veined bladderwort, is a carnivorous plant belonging to the Lentibulariaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, bladder-like structures that trap tiny aquatic organisms for nutrient absorption. The varietal name 'venulosum' refers to the distinct vein patterns on its leaves, which are a key identifying feature.
