Cynodon distichloides

Cynodon distichloides, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping and erosion control. Its fine, needle-like leaves and dense growth habit contribute to its ornamental value in gardens.

Cynodon distichloides is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide forage for livestock.
