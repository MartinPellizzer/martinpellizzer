Crataegus × subsphaerica nothosubsp. negreanii

Crataegus × subsphaerica nothosubsp.

negreanii, commonly known as the Negreanu hawthorn, is a hybrid shrub native to southern Europe. It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit.

The plant produces small, white to pink flowers in early spring, followed by red, juicy berries in the autumn. Its leaves are typically oval with serrated edges, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in supporting local wildlife.
