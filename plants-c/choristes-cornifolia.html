Choristes cornifolia

Choristes cornifolia, commonly known as the horned fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like fronds that often have a serrated or toothed edge. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of yellow or green.

Choristes cornifolia is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its role in traditional medicine.
