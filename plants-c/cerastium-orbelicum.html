Cerastium orbelicum

Cerastium orbelicum, commonly known as the mountain chickweed, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit. The plant features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. Cerastium orbelicum is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
