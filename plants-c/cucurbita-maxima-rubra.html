Cucurbita maxima rubra

Cucurbita maxima rubra, commonly known as the red acorn squash, is a cultivar of the species Cucurbita maxima.

It is characterized by its deep orange to reddish skin and a sweet, nutty flavor. The plant is a trailing vine that produces large, elongated fruits with a smooth, thick rind. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun and well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in both culinary and ornamental applications due to its vibrant color and versatility.
