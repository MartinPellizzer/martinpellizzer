Carex × cayouettei

Carex × cayouettei, commonly known as Cayouette's sedge, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and cylindrical flower spikes. The plant typically grows in wet or moist environments, such as marshes and wetlands, where it thrives in full to partial sunlight. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense colonies, contributing to soil stabilization.

Carex × cayouettei is valued for its ecological role in wetland ecosystems and its ornamental appeal in water gardens.
