Conophytum supremum

Conophytum supremum, commonly known as the "Rainbow Pincushion," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in summer. Its compact growth habit and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.

Conophytum supremum is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.
