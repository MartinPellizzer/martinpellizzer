Crassula drakensbergensis

Crassula drakensbergensis, commonly known as the Drakensberg Crassula, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit. The plant features fleshy, triangular leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
