Croton dracena

Croton dracena, commonly known as the flame tree or dragon tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage, which can range from deep red to orange and yellow, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Croton dracena is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
