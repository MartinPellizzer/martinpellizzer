Carvalhoa campanulata

Carvalhoa campanulata, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it is relatively rare in the wild.
