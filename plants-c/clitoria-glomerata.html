Clitoria glomerata

Clitoria glomerata, commonly known as the winged bean or cluster bean, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and showy, pea-like flowers that range in color from purple to pink. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Clitoria glomerata is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it a useful plant in sustainable agriculture.

Its ornamental value and nutritional benefits contribute to its importance in both horticulture and food systems.
