Cyrtandra clarkei var. longipetiolata

Cyrtandra clarkei var. longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled cyrtandra, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family.

It is characterized by its elongated petioles, which distinguish it from other varieties within the species. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.
