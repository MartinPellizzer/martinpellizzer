Conophytum longifissum

Conophytum longifissum, commonly known as the Long-stemmed Stone Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically green or tinged with red, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Conophytum longifissum is valued for its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
