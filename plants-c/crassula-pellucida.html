Crassula pellucida

Crassula pellucida, commonly known as the Glass Plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, translucent leaves that resemble glass, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, or purple during the warmer months.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
