Clerodendrum arenarium var. macrocalyx

Clerodendrum arenarium var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered sand clerodendrum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that are typically pink or lilac in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
