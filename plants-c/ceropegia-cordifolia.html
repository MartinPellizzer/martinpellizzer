Ceropegia cordifolia

Ceropegia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf string of hearts, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are often fleshy and covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale green or yellowish-white, adapted for specific pollinators. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
