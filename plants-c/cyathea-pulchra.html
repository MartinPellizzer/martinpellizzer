Cyathea pulchra

Cyathea pulchra, commonly known as the elegant tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a distinctive crown of pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. Cyathea pulchra thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its slow growth rate and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for cultivation in tropical gardens.
