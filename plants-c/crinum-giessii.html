Crinum giessii

Crinum giessii, commonly known as the Cape lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a greenish tinge. The plant has strap-like, linear leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.

Crinum giessii is also known for its strong, sometimes unpleasant odor, which is more pronounced in its flowers.
