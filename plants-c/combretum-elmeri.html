Combretum elmeri

Combretum elmeri, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Combretaceae and is characterized by its showy, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, woody fruits that split open to release seeds.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
