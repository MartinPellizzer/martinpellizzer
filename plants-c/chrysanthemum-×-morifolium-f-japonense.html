Chrysanthemum × morifolium f. japonense

Chrysanthemum × morifolium f. japonense, commonly known as the Japanese chrysanthemum, is a hybrid variety originating from China and Japan.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and a wide range of flower colors, including white, yellow, red, and purple. The plant typically has finely divided, feathery leaves and produces numerous small, daisy-like flowers. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is widely cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and floral arrangements.
