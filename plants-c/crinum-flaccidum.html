Crinum flaccidum

Crinum flaccidum, commonly known as the soft crinum, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Crinum flaccidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its striking floral display.
