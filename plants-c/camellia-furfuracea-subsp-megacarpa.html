Camellia furfuracea subsp. megacarpa

Camellia furfuracea subsp.

megacarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited camellia, is a species native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red.

The plant produces woody, evergreen shrubs with elliptical, dark green leaves. Its fruits are large, fleshy capsules that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and as a decorative plant.
