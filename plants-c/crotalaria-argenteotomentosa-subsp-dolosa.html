Crotalaria argenteotomentosa subsp. dolosa

Crotalaria argenteotomentosa subsp.

dolosa, commonly known as silver-haired cowpea, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by pod-like fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
