Ctenitis modesta

Ctenitis modesta, commonly known as the slender tree fern, is a member of the Cyatheaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a crown of feathery, compound fronds. The plant is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. Its fronds are typically dark green and have a delicate, lace-like appearance.

Ctenitis modesta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in tropical gardens and as a houseplant.
