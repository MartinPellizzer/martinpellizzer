Cinnamomum austroyunnanense

Cinnamomum austroyunnanense, commonly known as Yunnan cinnamon, is a species of tree native to southwestern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its leaves are elliptical with a glossy surface and emit a distinct cinnamon-like fragrance.

The tree is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine and as a spice in some regions.
