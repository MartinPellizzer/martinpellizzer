Cephalaria sillingeri

Cephalaria sillingeri, commonly known as the Sierra Nevada cephalaria, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of California.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leafy structure. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high elevations. It produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
