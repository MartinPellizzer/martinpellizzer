Chaetogastra sherardioides

Chaetogastra sherardioides, commonly known as the desert hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its robust, woody stems and large, showy yellow or orange flowers that bloom during the dry season.
