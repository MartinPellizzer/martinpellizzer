Combretum lomuense

Combretum lomuense, commonly known as the Lomuense Combretum, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
