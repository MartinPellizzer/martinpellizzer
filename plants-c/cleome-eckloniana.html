Cleome eckloniana

Cleome eckloniana, commonly known as spider flower, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and branching.

Cleome eckloniana thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking vibrant, low-maintenance ornamental plants.
