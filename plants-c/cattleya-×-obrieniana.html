Cattleya × obrieniana

Cattleya × obrieniana, commonly known as the O'Brien cattleya, is a hybrid orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This species is highly valued in horticulture for its beauty and fragrance.
