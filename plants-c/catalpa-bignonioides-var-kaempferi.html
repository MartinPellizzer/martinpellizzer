Catalpa bignonioides var. kaempferi

Catalpa bignonioides var. kaempferi, commonly known as the Japanese catalpa, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in the spring. The tree produces long, bean-like pods that contain seeds covered in a silky, white coating. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate urban environments make it a versatile choice for landscaping.
