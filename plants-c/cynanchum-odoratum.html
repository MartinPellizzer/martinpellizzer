Cynanchum odoratum

Cynanchum odoratum, commonly known as sweet wheeze vine, is a climbing plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the family Asclepiadaceae and is characterized by its aromatic, milky sap. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces yellowish-green flowers with a distinctive scent. Its fruit is a follicle containing numerous seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded habitats.

Cynanchum odoratum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
