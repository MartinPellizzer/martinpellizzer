Cambogia solitaria

Cambogia solitaria, commonly known as the solitary wax apple, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, yellow-to-red fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant's leaves are simple and alternate, with a rough texture and a strong, aromatic scent. Its flowers are small and yellow, typically appearing in clusters.

The fruit contains a single large seed surrounded by a fleshy, acidic pulp.
