Cymbopogon confertiflorus

Cymbopogon confertiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flowered grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides. The plant features narrow, sword-shaped leaves and produces dense, spike-like inflorescences with small, pale green flowers. It is well adapted to warm climates and can tolerate a range of soil types.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in ornamental landscaping and for erosion control.
