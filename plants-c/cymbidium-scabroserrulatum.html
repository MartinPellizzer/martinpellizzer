Cymbidium scabroserrulatum

Cymbidium scabroserrulatum, commonly known as the rough-toothed orchid, is a species native to China and Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with tall, slender stems that bear clusters of pendulous flowers. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in two rows along the stem. The flowers are typically white or pale yellow with a distinctive, rough-textured lip.

This plant thrives in cool, humid environments and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture.
