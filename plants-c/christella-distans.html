Christella distans

Christella distans, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of violet, pink, and white, often with a distinctive purple throat. Its leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Christella distans thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
