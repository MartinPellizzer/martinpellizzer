Clitoria kaieteurensis

Clitoria kaieteurensis, commonly known as the Kaieteure legume, is a flowering plant native to the Kaieteure National Park in Guyana.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its distinctive butterfly-like flowers that range in color from violet to blue. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and hairy, while its pods are elongated and contain several seeds.

Clitoria kaieteurensis plays a role in local ecosystems and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
