Cortaderia speciosa

Cortaderia speciosa, commonly known as the Australian feather grass, is a tall, perennial grass native to Australia.

It features long, feathery plumes that give it a striking, ornamental appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Cortaderia speciosa is often used in landscaping for its elegant, wind-swaying foliage and its ability to attract birds and butterflies.
