Chalcas paniculata var. zollingeri

Chalcas paniculata var. zollingeri, commonly known as the Zollinger's chalcas, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, yellow to orange composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
