Campanula nanella

Campanula nanella, commonly known as the fairy bellflower, is a small, flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to violet. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it a compact and ornamental species. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its unique appearance.

Campanula nanella thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade, making it a versatile choice for rock gardens and borders.
