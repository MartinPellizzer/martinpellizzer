Clerodendrum variifolium var. scandens

Clerodendrum variifolium var. scandens, commonly known as the climbing clerodendrum, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange. The plant has opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often used in ornamental gardening for its striking foliage and floral display.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
