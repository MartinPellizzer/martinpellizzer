Queltia capax

Queltia capax, commonly known as the broad-leafed nightshade, is a perennial herb native to the humid tropical regions of South America.

It features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and dark green coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds, often used in traditional medicine.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
