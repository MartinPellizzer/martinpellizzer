Quintinia media

Quintinia media, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hakea, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in heathland environments.
