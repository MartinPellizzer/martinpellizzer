Queltia × biternaceus

Queltia × biternaceus, commonly known as the three-leafed queltia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three leaflets arranged symmetrically around a central stem. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with a compact and bushy form.

Queltia × biternaceus is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated for decorative purposes in warm climates.
