Qiongzhuea macrophylla

Qiongzhuea macrophylla, commonly known as the giant fan palm, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to three meters in diameter, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or green, followed by edible fruits resembling small grapes. Qiongzhuea macrophylla thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique morphology makes it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
