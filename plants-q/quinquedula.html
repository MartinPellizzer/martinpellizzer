Quinquedula

Quinquedula, commonly known as the five-lobed bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that typically range in color from blue to purple. The plant has five lobes on its corolla, giving it its scientific name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Quinquedula is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
