Quercus bidoupensis

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in cool, moist, and shaded environments. The plant features broad, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath, with a distinctive serrated edge. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, appearing in clusters, and it produces acorns that are an important food source for local wildlife.
