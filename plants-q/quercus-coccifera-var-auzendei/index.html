Quercus Coccifera Var. Auzendei

Quercus coccifera var. auzendei is a rare and hardy evergreen shrub native to the arid regions of the Mediterranean. It is known for its small, leathery leaves and distinctive, spiny fruits that provide food for local wildlife. This plant thrives in poor, rocky soils and is highly drought-resistant. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening. Despite its resilience, it remains a delicate species facing threats from climate change and habitat loss.
