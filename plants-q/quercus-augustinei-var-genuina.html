Quercus augustinei var. genuina

Quercus augustinei var. genuina, commonly known as the Mexican white oak, is a deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves with serrated margins.
