Quesnelia morreniana

Quesnelia morreniana, commonly known as the Morren's spider plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad that forms dense rosettes of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences that bear small, white flowers, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
