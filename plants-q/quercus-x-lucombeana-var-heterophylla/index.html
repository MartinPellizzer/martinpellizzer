Quercus × Lucombeana Var. Heterophylla

Quercus × lucombeana var. heterophylla is a unique hybrid oak known for its variable leaf shapes. It combines traits from both parent species, creating a striking visual diversity in its foliage. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas. Its adaptability makes it a valuable addition to ecological studies. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively rare and understudied species.
