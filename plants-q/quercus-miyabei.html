Quercus miyabei

Quercus miyabei, commonly known as the Japanese evergreen oak, is a species of flowering plant in the beech family Fagaceae.

It is native to southern Japan and is characterized by its leathery, elliptical leaves that remain green throughout the year. Quercus miyabei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
