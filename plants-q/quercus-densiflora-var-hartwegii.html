Quercus densiflora var. hartwegii

Quercus densiflora var. hartwegii, commonly known as Hartweg's denseflower oak, is a species of evergreen oak native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, urn-shaped acorns that are enclosed in a cupule. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within cloud forests.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
