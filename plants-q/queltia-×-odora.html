Queltia × odora

Queltia × odora, commonly known as the fragrant queltia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Queltia glabra and Queltia hirsuta.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in warm, humid climates. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.

Queltia × odora requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and vigor.
