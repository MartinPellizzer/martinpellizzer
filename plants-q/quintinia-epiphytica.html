Quintinia epiphytica

Quintinia epiphytica, commonly known as the epiphytic quintinia, is a rare and specialized plant species that thrives in tropical and subtropical environments.

It is an epiphyte, meaning it grows on the surface of other plants, typically trees, without deriving nutrients from them. The plant has adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain, which is crucial for its survival in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually small and thick, helping to retain water in arid conditions.

Quintinia epiphytica is often found in dense forest canopies, where it benefits from high humidity and indirect sunlight.
