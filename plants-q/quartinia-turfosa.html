Quartinia turfosa

Quartinia turfosa, commonly known as the slender quartinia, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its dense, cushion-like growth habit. The plant features small, evergreen leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and is often found in rocky or alpine environments.
