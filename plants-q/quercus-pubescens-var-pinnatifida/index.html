Quercus Pubescens Var. Pinnatifida

Quercus pubescens var. pinnatifida is a unique variant of the sessile oak known for its distinctive foliage. This plant thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested regions across Europe. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, giving it a graceful appearance. The tree is valued for its hardiness and ability to adapt to various soil conditions. It plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems and biodiversity.
