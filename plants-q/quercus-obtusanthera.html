Quercus obtusanthera

Quercus obtusanthera, commonly known as the broad-leaved oak, is a species of flowering plant in the beech family, Fagaceae.

Quercus obtusanthera plays an important role in forest ecosystems, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
