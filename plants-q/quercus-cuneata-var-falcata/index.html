Quercus Cuneata Var. Falcata

Quercus cuneata var. falcata is a unique variant of the Chinese chestnut known for its distinctive leaf shape. This plant is native to specific regions in China and has been studied for its ecological and horticultural value. Its leaves are narrow and often curved, giving it a striking appearance in the landscape. While not widely cultivated, it holds significance in botanical research and conservation efforts. Understanding this variant offers insights into plant adaptation and genetic diversity.
