Quercus percoriacea

Quercus percoriacea, commonly known as the roughleaf oak, is a species of flowering plant in the beech family, Fagaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. Quercus percoriacea is adapted to dry, rocky environments and is often found in high-altitude forests.
