Quassia tricarpa

Quassia tricarpa, commonly known as the African quassia, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Simaroubaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and compound leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green flowers and small, round fruits that contain seeds.
