Quercus Cerris Subvar. Fulhamensis

Quercus cerris subvar. fulhamensis is a rare and lesser-known variant of the common hornbeam. It is native to parts of southern England and is often found in urban environments. This subspecies is distinguished by its unique leaf shape and smaller size compared to its parent species. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this rare botanical treasure.
