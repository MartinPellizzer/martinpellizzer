Queltia × nivea

Queltia × nivea, commonly known as the white queltia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Queltia species.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil. Its leaves are typically broad and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

Queltia × nivea is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
