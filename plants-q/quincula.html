Quincula

Quincula, commonly known as the common quincula, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Violaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, often found in grasslands and woodland edges. The plant features erect stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are small, violet-colored, and clustered in spikes, blooming in late spring to early summer.

Quincula is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for various purposes.
