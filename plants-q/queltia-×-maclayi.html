Queltia × maclayi

Queltia × maclayi, commonly known as the Maclay's queltia, is a hybrid plant species within the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It is relatively easy to grow in gardens, making it a popular choice for those interested in native and hybrid plants.
