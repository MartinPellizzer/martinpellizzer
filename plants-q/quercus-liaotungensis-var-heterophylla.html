Quercus liaotungensis var. heterophylla

Quercus liaotungensis var. heterophylla, commonly known as the Liaotung oak variant, is a deciduous tree native to northeastern China.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, with some leaves being broader and others more elongated, giving it its varietal name. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and is adapted to temperate climates with well-drained soils. Its acorns are an important food source for various wildlife species.

The tree is valued for its hard wood and has been used in traditional construction and craftsmanship.
