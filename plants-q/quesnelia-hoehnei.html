Quesnelia hoehnei

It is a member of the Podocarpaceae family, characterized by its coniferous structure and evergreen foliage. The plant exhibits a unique growth habit, with a straight trunk and a dense, rounded crown. Its leaves are needle-like and arranged in spirals, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
