Quercus lowilliamsii

Quercus lowilliamsii, commonly known as the Low's oak, is a rare and endemic species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree characterized by its elliptical, dark green leaves that turn reddish-brown in autumn.
