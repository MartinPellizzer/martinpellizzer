Quercus langbianensis subsp. cambodiensis

Quercus langbianensis subsp.

The tree typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in lowland and hill forest ecosystems. It has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with a rough, pubescent upper surface.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller acorns and shorter petioles compared to other members of the Quercus langbianensis species complex.
