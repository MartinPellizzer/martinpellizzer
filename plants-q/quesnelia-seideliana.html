Quesnelia seideliana

Quesnelia seideliana, commonly known as the Seidel's grass.

It is a species of grass native to South America, particularly Brazil. This plant belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. Quesnelia seideliana typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the ecosystems it inhabits.
