Quintinia major

Quintinia major, commonly known as the broadleaf quintinia, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae, which is known for its diverse and often showy flowers. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on top. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in dense, spherical inflorescences.
