Queltia × gouanii, commonly known as the hybrid hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Queltia grandiflora and Queltia hirta, characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically features large, velvety leaves and produces vibrant pink to red blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Queltia × gouanii is valued in horticulture for its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
