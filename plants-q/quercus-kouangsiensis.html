Quercus kouangsiensis

Quercus kouangsiensis, commonly known as the Khammuan oak, is a species of flowering plant in the beech family Fagaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Laos, Vietnam, and Cambodia. This deciduous tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is characterized by its lobed leaves and acorns. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in montane forests.

It plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
