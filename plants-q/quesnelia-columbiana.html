Quesnelia columbiana

Quesnelia columbiana, commonly known as the Columbian pine.

It is a coniferous evergreen tree native to the Andes mountains of South America. The tree has needle-like leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and is often found in high-altitude regions.
