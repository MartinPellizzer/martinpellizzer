Quercus acutissima subsp. chenii

Quercus acutissima subsp.

chenii, commonly known as the Chinese sharpleaf oak, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China. It belongs to the Fagaceae family and is characterized by its acutely pointed leaves and small, cupule-enclosed acorns.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It exhibits a moderate growth rate and can reach heights of up to 15 meters.
