Quamoclit Longiflora

Quamoclit longiflora is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America. Known for its vibrant purple and pink blooms, it captivates botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this beautiful yet endangered plant.
