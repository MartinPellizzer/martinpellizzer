Queltia × aurantia

Queltia × aurantia, commonly known as the hybrid mandarin orange, is a citrus hybrid resulting from the cross between a mandarin and a pomelo.

It is characterized by its small, round, and fragrant fruits with a thin, smooth peel. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces flowers that are typically white with a slight pinkish tint. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This hybrid is valued for its sweet, juicy fruit and is commonly grown in subtropical regions.
