Quercus frainetto f. spectabilis

Quercus frainetto f. spectabilis, commonly known as the red oak or red oak variant, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, deeply lobed leaves that turn vibrant shades of red and orange in autumn.
