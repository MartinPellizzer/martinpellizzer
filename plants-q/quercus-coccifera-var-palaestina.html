Quercus coccifera var. palaestina

Quercus coccifera var. palaestina, commonly known as the Palestine strawberry tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, leathery, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible and have a tart, acidic flavor. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, rocky soils.

This species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in the region.
