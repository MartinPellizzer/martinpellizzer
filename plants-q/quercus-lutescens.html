Quercus lutescens

Quercus lutescens, commonly known as the yellow oak, is a species of flowering plant in the beech family, Fagaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, particularly in countries like Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
