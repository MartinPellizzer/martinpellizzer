Queltia × semipartita

Queltia × semipartita, commonly known as the variegated arrowhead, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species, Queltia multiflora and Queltia tincta.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with shades of green, white, and yellow, which adds visual interest to any setting. The plant thrives in moist, well-draining soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens or indoor environments. Its heart-shaped leaves are typically arranged in a spiral pattern along the stems, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Queltia × semipartita is also valued for its ability to purify indoor air, enhancing its desirability as a decorative and functional plant.
