Quinquefolium

Quinquefolium, commonly known as red clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are often five-lobed, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and pastures.

Red clover is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
