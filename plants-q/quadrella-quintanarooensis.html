Quadrella quintanarooensis

Quadrella quintanarooensis, commonly known as the Quintana rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of northern Mexico and southern Texas. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
