Quercus rivasmartinezii

Quercus rivasmartinezii, commonly known as the Rivasc Martinez oak, is a rare and endemic species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, acorn-like fruits and thick, leathery leaves that are often grayish-green in color.
