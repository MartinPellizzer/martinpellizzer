Quadrella torulosa

Quadrella torulosa, commonly known as the yellow star moss, is a member of the family Pterobryaceae.

It is a small, cushion-like moss that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant has a dense, compact growth habit with bright yellow-green leaves that are often curled or twisted. Quadrella torulosa is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological role in soil stabilization.
