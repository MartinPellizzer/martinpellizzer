Quamoclit pallescens

Quamoclit pallescens, commonly known as the pale quamoclit, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads.

Quamoclit pallescens is often noted for its ability to thrive in wet environments and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
