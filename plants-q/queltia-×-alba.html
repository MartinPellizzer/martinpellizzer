Queltia × alba

Queltia × alba, commonly known as the white queltia, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Queltia multiflora and Queltia tincta.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in summer. It is native to South America and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
