Quesnelia edmundoi var. rubrobracteata

Quesnelia edmundoi var. rubrobracteata, commonly known as the red-bract quesnelia, is a bromeliad native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, rosette-forming growth habit and vibrant, red-bracted inflorescence. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
