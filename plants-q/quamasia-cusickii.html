Quamasia cusickii

Quamasia cusickii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with a robust, branching stem that can grow up to 2 meters in height. Quamasia cusickii is well-suited to rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant in desert ecosystems.
