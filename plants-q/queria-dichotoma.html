Queria dichotoma

Queria dichotoma, commonly known as the dichotomous quillwort, is a small aquatic plant belonging to the family Podostomata.

It is characterized by its branching, thread-like stems that emerge from the substrate in freshwater environments. Due to its specialized habitat, it is considered a rare and sensitive indicator of water quality.
