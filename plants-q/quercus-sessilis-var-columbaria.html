Quercus sessilis var. columbaria

Quercus sessilis var. columbaria, commonly known as the columnar oak, is a variant of the sessile oak characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit.

This tree exhibits a dense, columnar form, making it suitable for urban landscapes and small gardens. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a pointed tip and a smooth margin. The acorns are small and often have a cap that covers most of the nut.

It is valued for its ornamental appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
