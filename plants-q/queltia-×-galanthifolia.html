Queltia × galanthifolia

Queltia × galanthifolia, commonly known as the hybrid snowdrop, is a perennial herbaceous plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species.

It typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces delicate, white or pale yellow flowers that resemble those of true snowdrops. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central America and is often found in moist, shaded habitats. Queltia × galanthifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.

Its botanical interest lies in its hybrid nature and the unique combination of traits it displays.
