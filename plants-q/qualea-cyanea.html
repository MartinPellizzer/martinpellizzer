Qualea cyanea

Qualea cyanea, commonly known as the blue-eyed river mint, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments along riverbanks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a bluish-green hue and produces small, fragrant white flowers. It is often found in swampy areas and is adapted to periodic flooding.

Qualea cyanea is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
