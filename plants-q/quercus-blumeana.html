Quercus blumeana

Quercus blumeana, commonly known as the Blume's oak, is a species of flowering plant in the beech family, Fagaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in countries like Vietnam and Thailand. The tree is characterized by its broad, lobed leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. Quercus blumeana is valued for its hard wood and is sometimes used in local crafts and construction.
