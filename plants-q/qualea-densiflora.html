Qualea densiflora

Qualea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered water lily, is a tropical aquatic plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its broad, floating leaves and showy, white flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in swampy areas. Its leaves are thick and waxy, helping to support its weight on the water's surface.

Qualea densiflora plays an important role in providing habitat for aquatic organisms and contributing to the ecological balance of its environment.
