Quercus pseudosetulosa

Quercus pseudosetulosa, commonly known as the Chinese black oak, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and small, acorn-like fruits. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a pointed tip and a rough upper surface. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
