Quesnelia violacea

Quesnelia violacea, commonly known as the violet orchid.

It is a species of orchid native to Brazil, characterized by its striking violet and white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments within the Atlantic Forest biome. Its leaves are elongated and covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs.

Quesnelia violacea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
