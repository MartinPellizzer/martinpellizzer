Quaqua dependens

Quaqua dependens, commonly known as the hanging quill plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features long, thin, hair-like leaves that droop downward, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy leaves. It produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.
