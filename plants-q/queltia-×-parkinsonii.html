Queltia × parkinsonii

Queltia × parkinsonii, commonly known as the Parkinson's queltia, is a hybrid plant species resulting from the cross between Queltia glabra and Queltia paniculata.

It is a member of the Malvaceae family, characterized by its showy, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on its genetic background.
