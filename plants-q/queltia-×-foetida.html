Queltia × foetida

Queltia × foetida, commonly known as the stinking queltia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, often fragrant or foul-smelling flowers, which can vary in color from white to yellow. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with rough, fissured bark and opposite leaves. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits a range of morphological variations depending on its parent species.
