Quercus pseudoaegilopsis

Quercus pseudoaegilopsis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved white oak, is a deciduous tree native to the southeastern United States.

Quercus pseudoaegilopsis is valued for its hardy nature and ecological importance as a food source for wildlife.
