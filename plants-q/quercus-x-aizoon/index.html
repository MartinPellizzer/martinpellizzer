Quercus × Aizoon

Quercus × aizoon is a hybrid oak species known for its adaptability and resilience. It is commonly found in regions where different oak species overlap geographically. This plant exhibits a mix of traits from its parent species, making it unique in appearance and growth habits. Its ability to thrive in varied environments has made it a subject of interest in ecological studies. Despite its hybrid nature, it plays an important role in local ecosystems and forest regeneration.
