Quercus shangxiensis

Quercus shangxiensis, commonly known as the Shangxi oak, is a deciduous tree native to China, specifically found in the Shaanxi Province.

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts. The plant is characterized by its serrated leaves and produces acorns that are an important food source for various wildlife species. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
