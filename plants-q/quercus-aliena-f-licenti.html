Quercus aliena f. licenti

Quercus aliena f. licenti, commonly known as the Licenti oak, is a rare variant of the alien oak species.

It is characterized by its small, acorn-like fruits and dense, dark green foliage. The leaves are typically oval-shaped with a pointed tip and a serrated edge. This plant is native to specific regions in Europe and is often found in rocky or mountainous areas.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and limited distribution.
