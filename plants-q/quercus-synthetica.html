Quercus synthetica

Quercus synthetica, commonly known as the synthetic oak, is a fictional plant often used in speculative biology and science fiction.

It is characterized by its unique combination of traits from multiple oak species, including a dense, layered leaf structure and a rapid growth rate. The plant exhibits a high degree of adaptability, thriving in a variety of soil types and climates. Its bark is thick and fissured, providing natural protection against environmental stressors.

While not a real species, it serves as a creative tool for exploring ecological and evolutionary concepts.
