Quercus liaotungensis f. capillata

Quercus liaotungensis f. capillata, commonly known as the Liaotung oak variant with tufted leaves, is a rare botanical form of the Liaotung oak.

It is characterized by its distinctive, slender leaves that often grow in tufts, giving it a unique appearance. This variant is native to specific regions in China, where it thrives in temperate forests. The plant is valued for its ecological role and is often found in mixed hardwood forests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
