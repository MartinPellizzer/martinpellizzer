Quercus persica var. belangeri

It belongs to the Fagaceae family, which includes oaks, beeches, and chestnuts. The tree is characterized by its lobed leaves, which are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. Its acorns are small and often have a cupule that partially encloses the nut.
