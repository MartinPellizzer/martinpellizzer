Quisqualis pierrei

Quisqualis pierrei, commonly known as the Chinese flame flower, is a climbing vine native to China.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires support for its vine-like growth.

Quisqualis pierrei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
