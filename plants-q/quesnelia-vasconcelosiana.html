Quesnelia vasconcelosiana

Quesnelia vasconcelosiana, commonly known as the Brazilian pine or the Brazilian pine tree, is a species of pine native to Brazil.

It is a tall evergreen conifer that can reach heights of up to 40 meters. The plant has needle-like leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in clusters. It produces cones that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its timber and is also important in the ecosystem of its native habitat.
