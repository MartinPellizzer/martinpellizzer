Mesembryanthemum spinuliferum

Mesembryanthemum spinuliferum, commonly known as the spiny ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are often covered in fine, spiny hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It is adapted to hot, dry climates and thrives in well-drained soils.

Mesembryanthemum spinuliferum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its drought tolerance and attractive foliage.
