Medicago italica var. tornata

Medicago italica var. tornata, commonly known as the Turkish clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. This variety is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in temperate climates. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

The plant is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soils.
