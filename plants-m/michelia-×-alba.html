Michelia × alba

Michelia × alba, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has glossy, dark green, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Michelia × alba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
