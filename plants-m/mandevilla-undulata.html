Mandevilla undulata

Mandevilla undulata, commonly known as the wavy mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with thick, fleshy stems and large, wavy-edged leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming from summer to fall. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla undulata is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and lush foliage.
