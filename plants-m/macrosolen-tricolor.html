Macrosolen tricolor

Macrosolen tricolor, commonly known as the three-color spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its strikingly colored flowers, which typically display three distinct colors—often red, yellow, and white—on the same bloom. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and wetland restoration projects.
