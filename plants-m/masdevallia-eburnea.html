Masdevallia eburnea

Masdevallia eburnea, commonly known as the ivory masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often pendulous stems and small, elegant flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia eburnea is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
