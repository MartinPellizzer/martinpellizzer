Monelasmum andongense

Monelasmum andongense, commonly known as the Andong milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is typically found in grassland habitats and is adapted to temperate climates. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its ecological significance lies in its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
