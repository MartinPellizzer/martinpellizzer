Merremia hastata

Merremia hastata, commonly known as the yellow morning glory, is a flowering vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange, opening in the morning and closing at dusk. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and can grow up to several meters in length, often climbing or trailing. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Merremia hastata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
