Maxillaria yzabalana

Maxillaria yzabalana, commonly known as the Yzabalana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to orange flowers with dark red or purple markings, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and often covered in a dense layer of brownish hairs.

Maxillaria yzabalana prefers shaded, humid environments and is often found growing in forest understories.
