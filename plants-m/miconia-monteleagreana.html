Miconia monteleagreana

Miconia monteleagreana, commonly known as the Monteleagreana miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
