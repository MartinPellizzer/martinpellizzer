Matricaria maroccana

Matricaria maroccana, commonly known as Moroccan chamomile, is a flowering plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals. The plant typically grows in dry, sunny environments and has a compact, bushy appearance. Its essential oil, extracted from the flowers, is valued for its aromatic and medicinal properties.

Matricaria maroccana is often used in traditional medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
