Masdevallia lilliputiana

Masdevallia lilliputiana, commonly known as the miniature masdevallia, is a rare and diminutive orchid native to the Andean cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically measure less than a centimeter in diameter, often displaying vivid shades of pink and white. The plant has a compact growth habit, with short, fleshy leaves and a slender, wiry stem. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
