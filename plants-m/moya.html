Moya

Moya, commonly known as the Moya tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its broad, palm-like leaves and smooth, glossy bark. The tree produces small, greenish-white flowers that eventually develop into oval, woody fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in tropical forests.
