Mammillaria pyrrhocephala var. confusa

Mammillaria pyrrhocephala var. confusa, commonly known as the fuzzy mammillaria, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, white to pale yellow spines and a short, cylindrical stem. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
