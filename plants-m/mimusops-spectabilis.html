Mimusops spectabilis

Mimusops spectabilis, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, spherical clusters of white flowers that appear at the ends of its branches, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy green surface. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
