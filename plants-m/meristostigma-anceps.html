Meristostigma anceps

Meristostigma anceps, commonly known as the African orchid or the Cape orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit. The plant features a rhizomatous stem with a cluster of narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are typically small, greenish-yellow, and arranged in a spike, often with a distinctive scent.

Meristostigma anceps is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.
