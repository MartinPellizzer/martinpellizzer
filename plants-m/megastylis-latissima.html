Megastylis latissima

Megastylis latissima, commonly known as the broad-styled orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, well-drained soils. Its unique floral structure includes a broad, spurred lip and prominent stamens, which are key features in its pollination strategy.

Megastylis latissima is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
