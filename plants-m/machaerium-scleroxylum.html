Machaerium scleroxylum

Machaerium scleroxylum, commonly known as the hardwood machaerium, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, characterized by its typical pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features bipinnate leaves with small, ovate leaflets and produces yellow to orange flowers clustered in spikes.

Its woody stems and hard, dense wood make it valuable for construction and fuel in its native regions.
