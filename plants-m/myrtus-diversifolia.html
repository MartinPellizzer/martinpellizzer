Myrtus diversifolia

Myrtus diversifolia, commonly known as the variable myrtle, is a flowering shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves that are often used in traditional herbal remedies. Its aromatic properties make it popular in perfumery and as a culinary spice.

Myrtus diversifolia thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
