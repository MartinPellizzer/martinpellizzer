Moenchia erecta

Moenchia erecta, commonly known as the upright moenchia, is a flowering plant species belonging to the Amaranthaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with greenish-white flowers that are typically inconspicuous. Moenchia erecta is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the Americas, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils.

It is often used in restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and withstand drought conditions.
