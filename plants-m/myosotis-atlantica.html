Myosotis atlantica

Myosotis atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the North Atlantic.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along shorelines and tidal zones. It is a perennial species that thrives in temperate climates with cool, humid conditions.

Myosotis atlantica plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
