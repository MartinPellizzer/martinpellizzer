Mimosa argentea

Mimosa argentea, commonly known as silver mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-white, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Mimosa argentea is also known for its sensitive leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait that adds to its unique botanical appeal.
