Maxillaria pseudonubigena

Maxillaria pseudonubigena, commonly known as the cloud-like maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant features linear, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria pseudonubigena is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
