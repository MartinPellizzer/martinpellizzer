Medinilla multialata

Medinilla multialata, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round, orange fruits that resemble tangerines, which are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Medinilla multialata thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal.

It is also valued for its ability to bloom year-round under proper care.
