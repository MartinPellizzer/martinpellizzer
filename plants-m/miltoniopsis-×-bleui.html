Miltoniopsis × bleui

Miltoniopsis × bleui, commonly known as the Blue Miltoniopsis, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking blue and violet-colored flowers, which are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. The plant features fleshy, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a decorative plant in greenhouses and gardens.
