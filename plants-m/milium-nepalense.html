Milium nepalense

Milium nepalense, commonly known as the Nepal millet, is a small annual grass native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of tiny, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, white to pale green flowers that are wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Milium nepalense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
