Mimosa ursina

Mimosa ursina, commonly known as the bear's breech, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Mimosa ursina is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal remedies.
