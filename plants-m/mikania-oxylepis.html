Mikania oxylepis

Mikania oxylepis, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a vigorous vine that can grow rapidly, often climbing or trailing along the ground. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Mikania oxylepis is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and light conditions.

It is also noted for its medicinal properties in some traditional herbal practices.
