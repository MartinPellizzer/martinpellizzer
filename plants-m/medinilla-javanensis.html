Medinilla javanensis

Medinilla javanensis, commonly known as the Javan velvet plant, is a tropical shrub native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, often with a distinctive red or purple calyx. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
