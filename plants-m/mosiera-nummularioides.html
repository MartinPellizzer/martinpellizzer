Mosiera nummularioides

Mosiera nummularioides, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from spring to summer. The plant has a compact, rounded growth habit with silvery-gray foliage that gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Mosiera nummularioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
