Minuartia Juniperina Var. Glandulifera

Minuartia juniperina var. glandulifera is a rare and delicate plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is commonly found in rocky and arid environments, thriving in harsh conditions where few other species can survive. This variant is distinguished by its glandular leaves and small, pale flowers that bloom in early spring. Its unique adaptations make it an important subject for ecological and botanical study. Due to habitat loss and climate change, this plant is increasingly threatened, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
