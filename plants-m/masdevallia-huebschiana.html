Masdevallia huebschiana

Masdevallia huebschiana, commonly known as the Huesch's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful flowers that display a range of hues including purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, humid environments with dappled sunlight. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat.

Masdevallia huebschiana is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and ornamental value.
