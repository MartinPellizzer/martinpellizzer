Myoporum refractum

Myoporum refractum, commonly known as the New Zealand myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers in summer. It is typically found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Myoporum refractum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.
