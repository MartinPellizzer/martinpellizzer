Maxillaria erubescens

Maxillaria erubescens, commonly known as the red-tipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking, showy flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically blooms in the summer, with flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. Maxillaria erubescens prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a favorite among orchid enthusiasts.
