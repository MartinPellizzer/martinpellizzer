Melandrium platyphyllum

Melandrium platyphyllum, commonly known as the broad-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
