Marsdenia philippinensis

Marsdenia philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
