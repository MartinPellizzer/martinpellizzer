Marathrum plumosum

Marathrum plumosum, commonly known as the plumose marathrum, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft, velvety texture.

Marathrum plumosum is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
