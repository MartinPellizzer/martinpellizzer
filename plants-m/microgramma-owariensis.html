Microgramma owariensis

Microgramma owariensis, commonly known as the Japanese holly fern, is a compact evergreen fern native to Japan.

It features intricate, finely divided fronds that are typically dark green with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves. It thrives in shady, moist environments and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
