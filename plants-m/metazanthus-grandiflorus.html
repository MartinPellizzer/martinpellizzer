Metazanthus grandiflorus

Metazanthus grandiflorus, commonly known as the grandiflorous beauty, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a woody stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and aromatic. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
