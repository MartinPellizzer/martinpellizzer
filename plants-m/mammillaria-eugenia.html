Mammillaria eugenia

Mammillaria eugenia, commonly known as the Eugenia mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
