Mattia lanata var. euryptera

Mattia lanata var. euryptera, commonly known as the woolly mattia, is a variant of the Mattia lanata species.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually grayish-green, and the plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
