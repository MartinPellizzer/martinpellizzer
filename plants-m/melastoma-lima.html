Melastoma lima

Melastoma lima, commonly known as the Java melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma lima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
