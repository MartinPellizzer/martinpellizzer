Marsdenia otoniensis

Marsdenia otoniensis, commonly known as the Otoni Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Marsdenia otoniensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
