Melastoma trachycaulon

Melastoma trachycaulon, commonly known as the rough-stemmed melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features rough, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are typically clustered in inflorescences and are often showy, with vibrant colors attracting pollinators.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
