Mammea yunnanensis

Mammea yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan mammea, is a small to medium-sized tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, bead-like fruits containing smooth, black seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats in mountainous regions and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
