Mayna grandiflora

Mayna grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora mayna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or orange. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. Mayna grandiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and exotic plant collections.
