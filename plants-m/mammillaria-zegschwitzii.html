Mammillaria zegschwitzii

Mammillaria zegschwitzii, commonly known as the Zegschwitz's mammillary cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact growth habit and typically features a pale green to blue-gray stem. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has a dense covering of fine, white spines and is well adapted to arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in succulent gardens and collections.
