Mentha × arvensiaquatica

Mentha × arvensiaquatica, commonly known as water mint, is a hybrid perennial herb that belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and dense clusters of small, pale purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as stream banks, wet meadows, and marshes. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinctive minty fragrance.

Mentha × arvensiaquatica is often used in herbal medicine and as a culinary herb due to its refreshing flavor and medicinal properties.
