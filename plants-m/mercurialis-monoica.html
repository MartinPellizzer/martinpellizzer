Mercurialis monoica

Mercurialis monoica, commonly known as the one-seed mercurial, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is an annual herb that grows up to 30 cm tall, featuring simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. Its fruit is a single, elongated, ribbed seed pod that splits open to release a single seed.

The plant is adapted to dry, disturbed soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
