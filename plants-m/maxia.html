Maxia

Maxia, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white petals, often blooming in spring and summer. Maxia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation.
