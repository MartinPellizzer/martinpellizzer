Mutisia lutzii

Mutisia lutzii, commonly known as the Lutz's mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy yellow ray flowers surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, typical of the family. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.

Mutisia lutzii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
