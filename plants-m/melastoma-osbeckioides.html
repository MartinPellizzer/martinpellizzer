Melastoma osbeckioides

Melastoma osbeckioides, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and striking, showy flowers that range from pink to violet in color. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red or purple when mature. Its growth habit includes a compact form with a bushy appearance, making it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
