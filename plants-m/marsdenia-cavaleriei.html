Marsdenia cavaleriei

Marsdenia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Yunnan moonseed, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and fleshy, ribbed stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.
