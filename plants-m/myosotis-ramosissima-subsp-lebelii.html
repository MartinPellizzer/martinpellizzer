Myosotis ramosissima subsp. lebelii

Myosotis ramosissima subsp.

lebelii, commonly known as the Lebel's Forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in the Andes region. It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers and slender, branching stems.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain meadows and forest understories. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
