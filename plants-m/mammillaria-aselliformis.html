Mammillaria aselliformis

Mammillaria aselliformis, commonly known as the mouse-eared cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
