Mammillaria coronaria

Mammillaria coronaria, commonly known as the Crowned Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive crown of white radial spines. The plant features short, cylindrical stems covered in areoles, from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria coronaria is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.
