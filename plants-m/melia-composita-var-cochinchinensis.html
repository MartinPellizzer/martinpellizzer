Melia composita var. cochinchinensis

Melia composita var. cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese ash or Chinese ash, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapindaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with three to five leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.
