Mammillaria acicularis

Mammillaria acicularis, commonly known as the needle mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in small, spine-like tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
