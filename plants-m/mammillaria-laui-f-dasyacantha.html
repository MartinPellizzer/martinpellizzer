Mammillaria laui f. dasyacantha

Mammillaria laui f. dasyacantha, commonly known as the dense spines mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique spines and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
