Milium Spica-Venti

Milium spica-venti is a unique flowering plant known for its distinctive wind-pollinated structure. It thrives in open, disturbed habitats where it can catch the breeze that carries its lightweight pollen. The plant's name translates to "spike of the wind," reflecting its adaptation to aerial pollination. With its slender stems and clustered flowers, it adds a subtle beauty to the landscape. This species is often overlooked but plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems.
