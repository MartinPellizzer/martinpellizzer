Miltonia candida

Miltonia candida, commonly known as the white lady orchid, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its elegant, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant features long, slender leaves and produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Miltonia candida is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
