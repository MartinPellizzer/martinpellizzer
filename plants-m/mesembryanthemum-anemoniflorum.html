Mesembryanthemum anemoniflorum

Mesembryanthemum anemoniflorum, commonly known as the starry mesembryanthemum, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
