Mattiastrum cynoglossoides

Mattiastrum cynoglossoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mat grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and linear, with a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, clustered flower spikes that bloom in shades of pink to purple, often during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
