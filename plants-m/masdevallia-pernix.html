Masdevallia pernix

Masdevallia pernix, commonly known as the silver-veined masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and intricate, silver-veined foliage. The flowers are typically small, with a vibrant mix of purple, white, and green hues, and are arranged in a dense, spike-like inflorescence. This species thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful management of moisture and temperature.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
