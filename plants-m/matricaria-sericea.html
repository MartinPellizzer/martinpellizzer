Matricaria sericea

Matricaria sericea, commonly known as silverleaf yarrow, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its silvery, soft foliage and flat, white to pale yellow flower heads. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Matricaria sericea is often used in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.
