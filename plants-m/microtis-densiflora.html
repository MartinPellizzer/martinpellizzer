Microtis densiflora

Microtis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered microtis, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to Australia.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils in woodland or heathland environments. Its unique floral structure includes a spurred lip, a feature common in many orchids.

Microtis densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegance and fragrance.
