Mammillaria standleyi

Mammillaria standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's bunny ear cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact stem that often remains close to the ground. The plant features white to pale green spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
