Melastoma heterostegium

Melastoma heterostegium, commonly known as the red-veined melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, and its stems are often covered in a distinctive reddish or purplish hue. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, winged seeds when mature.
