Mammillaria heyderi subsp. gaumeri

Mammillaria heyderi subsp.

gaumeri, commonly known as the Gaumer's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular to cylindrical stem with prominent ribs and areoles.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
