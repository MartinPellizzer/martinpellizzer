Myristica melinonii

Myristica melinonii, commonly known as the Madagascar nutmeg tree, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant, reddish-brown fruits that contain a single seed, which is encased in a thick, aromatic aril. Its essential oils, derived from the seeds and leaves, are valued for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

The tree plays an important role in the local ecosystem and is also of interest for its potential economic value.
