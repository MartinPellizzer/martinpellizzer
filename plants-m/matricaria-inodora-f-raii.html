Matricaria inodora f. raii

Matricaria inodora f. raii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces yellow-orange flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties, with some traditional uses in herbal remedies.
