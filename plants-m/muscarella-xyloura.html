Muscarella xyloura

Muscarella xyloura, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its segmented, fleshy stems that bear bright, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular indoor plant due to its long blooming period and ability to thrive in moderate temperatures.

Muscarella xyloura is also known for its ability to bloom around the holiday season, hence its common name.
