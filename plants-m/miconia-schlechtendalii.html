Miconia schlechtendalii

Miconia schlechtendalii, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Miconia schlechtendalii is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
