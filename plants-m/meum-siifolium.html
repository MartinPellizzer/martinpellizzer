Meum siifolium

Meum siifolium, commonly known as yellow toadflax, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Violaceae and is characterized by its yellow flowers with purple veins. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found along roadsides and in meadows.
