Melicope trichopetala

Melicope trichopetala, commonly known as the hairy-leaf melicope, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a hairy texture on the underside, giving it its common name. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitats.
