Myrcia sintenisiana

Myrcia sintenisiana, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink and are clustered in small inflorescences.

Myrcia sintenisiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
