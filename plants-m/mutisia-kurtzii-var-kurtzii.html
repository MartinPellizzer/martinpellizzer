Mutisia kurtzii var. kurtzii

Mutisia kurtzii var. kurtzii, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disk of smaller florets. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a rough texture.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
