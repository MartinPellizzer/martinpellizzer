Mikania cyanosma

Mikania cyanosma, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, blue-violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas and is known for its rapid growth habit. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Mikania cyanosma is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
