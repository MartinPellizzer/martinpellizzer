Medicago falcata var. cretacea

Medicago falcata var. cretacea, commonly known as the Cretan goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance. Its root system fixes nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.

It is often found in dry grasslands and along coastal areas.
