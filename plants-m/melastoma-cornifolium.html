Melastoma cornifolium

Melastoma cornifolium, commonly known as the horned melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
