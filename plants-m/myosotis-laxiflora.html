Myosotis laxiflora

Myosotis laxiflora, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It features slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, sky-blue flowers with five petals, blooming from late summer to early autumn. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Myosotis laxiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
