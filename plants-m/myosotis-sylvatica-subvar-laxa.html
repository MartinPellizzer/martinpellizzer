Myosotis sylvatica subvar. laxa

Myosotis sylvatica subvar. laxa, commonly known as the looseleaf forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The subvariety laxa differs from the typical M. sylvatica by having more loosely arranged leaves and slightly smaller flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a wildflower in natural landscapes.
