Maxillaria ferdinandiana var. luteola

Maxillaria ferdinandiana var. luteola, commonly known as the Yellow Ferdinand's Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinct, fragrant scent. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its vibrant color and ornamental value in horticulture.
