Malaxis cylindrostachya

Malaxis cylindrostachya, commonly known as the slender-stemmed marsh orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant has a slender, erect stem that bears a single or few flowering spikes. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a faintly colored lip.

It is characterized by its intricate root system and is often found in association with specific mycorrhizal fungi.
