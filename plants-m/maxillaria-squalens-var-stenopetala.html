Maxillaria squalens var. stenopetala

Maxillaria squalens var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact rhizome with a basal rosette of dark green, hairy leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
