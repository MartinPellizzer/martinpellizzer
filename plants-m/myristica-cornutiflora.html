Myristica cornutiflora

Myristica cornutiflora, commonly known as the horned nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the islands of Indonesia and Malaysia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark and distinctive, horn-shaped inflorescences. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fleshy, red berries containing a single seed. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin.

The tree is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
