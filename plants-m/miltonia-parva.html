Miltonia parva

Miltonia parva, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the mini Miltonia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its compact size and ornamental value.

Miltonia parva is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
