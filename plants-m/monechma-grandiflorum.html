Monechma grandiflorum

Monechma grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered monechma, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features upright stems with opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers in terminal clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a desirable addition to native plant gardens.
