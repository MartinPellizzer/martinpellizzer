Meliosma lanceolata f. nervosa

Meliosma lanceolata f. nervosa, commonly known as the veined meliosma, is a herbaceous plant characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology.

The leaves are lanceolate in shape, with a prominent venation pattern that gives the plant its specific epithet "nervosa.". It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often found in temperate forests. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color.

Its foliage is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value and adaptability to partial shade.
