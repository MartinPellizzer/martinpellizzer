Moraea iriopetala

Moraea iriopetala, commonly known as the mountain moraea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that come in shades of purple and blue. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating.

Moraea iriopetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
