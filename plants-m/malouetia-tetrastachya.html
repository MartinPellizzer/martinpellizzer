Malouetia tetrastachya

Malouetia tetrastachya, commonly known as the African star gooseberry, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often opposite and have a serrated margin. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature and are edible.

Malouetia tetrastachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
