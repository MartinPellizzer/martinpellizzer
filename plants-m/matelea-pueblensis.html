Matelea pueblensis

Matelea pueblensis, commonly known as the Puebla mescal bean, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
