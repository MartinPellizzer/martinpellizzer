Masdevallia leptoura

Masdevallia leptoura, commonly known as the slender-lobed masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as a small epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale to dark purple, with a distinctive lip that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its specific environmental requirements, it is a challenging plant to cultivate outside its native range.
