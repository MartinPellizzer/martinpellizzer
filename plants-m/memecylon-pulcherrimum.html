Memecylon pulcherrimum

Memecylon pulcherrimum, commonly known as the golden memecylon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree with simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.
