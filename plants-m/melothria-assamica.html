Melothria assamica

Melothria assamica, commonly known as the Assam miracle berry, is a climbing vine native to the Indian subcontinent.

It is characterized by its small, bright yellow fruits that are covered in a rough, spiny skin. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is known for its unique taste-modifying properties, which can alter the perception of other flavors when consumed.
