Moringa nux-eben

Moringa nux-eben, commonly known as the African horseradish, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun for optimal growth.
