Melastoma milleflorum

Melastoma milleflorum, commonly known as the common melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly rough texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
