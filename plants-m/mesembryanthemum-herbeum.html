Mesembryanthemum herbeum

Mesembryanthemum herbeum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.

Mesembryanthemum herbeum is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
