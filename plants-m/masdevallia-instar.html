Masdevallia instar

Masdevallia instar, commonly known as the star masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, star-shaped flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a compact, bushy growth habit. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Masdevallia instar prefers cool, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in specialty orchid collections.
