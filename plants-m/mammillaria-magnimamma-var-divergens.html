Mammillaria magnimamma var. divergens

Mammillaria magnimamma var. divergens, commonly known as the divergent mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white or pale blue spines. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
