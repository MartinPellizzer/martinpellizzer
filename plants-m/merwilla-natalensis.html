Merwilla natalensis

Merwilla natalensis, commonly known as Natal starling or Natal star-grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Poaceae, commonly known as the grass family. The plant is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces small, white to pale blue flowers arranged in dense clusters. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks.

Merwilla natalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering display.
