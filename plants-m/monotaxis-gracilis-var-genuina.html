Monotaxis gracilis var. genuina

Monotaxis gracilis var. genuina, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features thin, cylindrical stems that are often green or yellowish in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

This variant is distinguished by its slender growth habit and specific geographic distribution.
