Moringa sylvestris

Moringa sylvestris, commonly known as the wild moringa or African horseradish, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a member of the Moringaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, sunny climate for optimal growth. Its rapid growth rate and ability to withstand drought conditions make it a hardy species.

Moringa sylvestris is also noted for its potential medicinal and nutritional properties.
