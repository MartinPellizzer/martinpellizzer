Medicago kopetdaghi

Medicago kopetdaghi, commonly known as Kopetdag vetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates. It has small, hairy pods that contain seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Medicago kopetdaghi plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
