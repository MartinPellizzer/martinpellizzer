Mammillaria eichlamii

Mammillaria eichlamii, commonly known as the Eichlamii mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant features small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow or orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
