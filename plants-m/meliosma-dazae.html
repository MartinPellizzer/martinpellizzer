Meliosma dazae

Meliosma dazae, commonly known as the Dazae meliosma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliosmaceae and is characterized by its unique, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
