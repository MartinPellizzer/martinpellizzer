Masdevallia exaltata

Masdevallia exaltata, commonly known as the exalted masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that stores water and nutrients, typical of many orchid species. The plant produces striking, often brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique and elegant appearance.
