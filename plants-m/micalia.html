Micalia

Micalia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that often have a silvery or white underside. Micalia species typically produce showy, pale yellow or white flowers that bloom in clusters.
