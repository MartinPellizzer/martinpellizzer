Mosiera nummularioides subsp. ophiticola

Mosiera nummularioides subsp.

ophiticola, commonly known as the Ophiticola crassifolia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features dense, fleshy, dark green leaves that are rounded and often covered in a whitish powdery coating.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
