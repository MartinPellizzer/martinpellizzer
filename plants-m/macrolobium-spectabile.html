Macrolobium spectabile

Macrolobium spectabile, commonly known as the showy macrolobium, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its large, showy flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
