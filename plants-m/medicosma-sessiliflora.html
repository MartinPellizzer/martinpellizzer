Medicosma sessiliflora

Medicosma sessiliflora, commonly known as the sessile-flowered medic, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in grassland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite and sessile, with a rough, hairy texture.

Medicosma sessiliflora is known for its medicinal properties, traditionally used in herbal remedies.
