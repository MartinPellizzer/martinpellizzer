Myoporum tenuifolium

Myoporum tenuifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically gray-green in color, and it produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to coastal and sandy environments, often growing in well-drained soils. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a hardy plant for coastal gardens.

Myoporum tenuifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
