Microtropis rostrata

Microtropis rostrata, commonly known as the rostrated microtropis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and are clustered in inflorescences.

Microtropis rostrata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
