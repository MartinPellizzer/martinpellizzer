Maytenus orbiculata

Maytenus orbiculata, commonly known as the round-leaf mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray-brown bark and elliptical or orbiculate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green on top. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing a single seed. It is often found in humid lowland forests and is valued for its hardy nature and ornamental qualities.

Maytenus orbiculata is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
