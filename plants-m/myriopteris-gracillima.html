Myriopteris gracillima

Myriopteris gracillima, commonly known as the slender cliff brake, is a small, evergreen fern native to the western United States.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant has delicate, linear leaves that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Its fronds are pinnately divided, with a distinctive, narrow shape that helps it withstand windy and arid conditions.

Myriopteris gracillima is well adapted to harsh environments, making it a resilient and hardy species.
