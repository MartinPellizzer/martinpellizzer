Mauria cuatrecasasii

Mauria cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cauca orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the Andean region of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its morphology includes a pseudobulb and linear leaves, typical of many orchid species.
