Miconia longiracemosa

Miconia longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its large, showy, violet-blue bracts that resemble petals. The flowers are small and clustered in long, drooping racemes, which give the plant its common name.
