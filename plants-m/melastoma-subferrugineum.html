Melastoma subferrugineum

Melastoma subferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its small, elliptical leaves are often covered in fine, rusty-colored hairs.

Melastoma subferrugineum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
