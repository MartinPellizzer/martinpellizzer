Mimulus bigelovii

Mimulus bigelovii, commonly known as the Bigelow's monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and in open woodlands. It has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in disturbed habitats.
