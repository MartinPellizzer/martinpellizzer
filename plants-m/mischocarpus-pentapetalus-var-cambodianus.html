Mischocarpus pentapetalus var. cambodianus

Mischocarpus pentapetalus var. cambodianus, commonly known as the Cambodian wild apple, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers.
