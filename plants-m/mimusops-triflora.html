Mimusops triflora

Mimusops triflora, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Mimusops triflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
