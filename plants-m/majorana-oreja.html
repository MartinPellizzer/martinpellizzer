Majorana oreja

Majorana oreja, commonly known as pennyroyal, is an aromatic herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant leaves and clusters of tiny white flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soil. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.

However, it contains essential oils that can be toxic to pets and should be handled with care.
