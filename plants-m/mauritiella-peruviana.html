Mauritiella peruviana

Mauritiella peruviana, commonly known as the Andean palm, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of South America.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in cloud forests at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
