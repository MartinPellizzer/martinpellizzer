Maireana planifolia

Maireana planifolia, commonly known as the Narrow-leafed Maireana, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Maireana planifolia is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
