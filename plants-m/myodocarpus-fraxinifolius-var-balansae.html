Myodocarpus fraxinifolius var. balansae

Myodocarpus fraxinifolius var. balansae, commonly known as the Balansae myodocarpus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often lobed and have a distinctive fraxinifolius-like appearance. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
