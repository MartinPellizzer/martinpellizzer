Masdevallia acrochordonia

Masdevallia acrochordonia, commonly known as the acrochordon masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive, curled lip. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
