Mammillaria macrantha

Mammillaria macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
