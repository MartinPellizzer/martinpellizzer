Massowia lanceolata

Massowia lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf massowia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Massowia lanceolata is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats, making it a characteristic species of dry, sunny environments.
