Mussaenda fruticosa

Mussaenda fruticosa, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, branching stems. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, often resembling flames. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and ovate with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Mussaenda fruticosa thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.
