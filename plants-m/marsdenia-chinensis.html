Marsdenia chinensis

Marsdenia chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Marsdenia, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruits are follicles containing seeds with a tuft of hair.

Marsdenia chinensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
