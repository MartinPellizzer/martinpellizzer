Myristica smythiesii

Myristica smythiesii, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the valuable spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, white flowers and has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets. Its seeds are encased in a red, fleshy aril, which is often used in traditional medicine.

The tree is valued for its essential oils and has economic significance in regions where it is cultivated.
