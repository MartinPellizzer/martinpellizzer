Microcorys selaginoides

The plant exhibits a unique ability to survive extreme drought conditions through its desiccation-tolerant spores and specialized root systems. Microcorys selaginoides plays a minor ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates in its harsh environment.
