Maihueniopsis ovata f. calva

Maihueniopsis ovata f. calva, commonly known as the hairy flat sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the Cyperaceae family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous or alpine environments.
