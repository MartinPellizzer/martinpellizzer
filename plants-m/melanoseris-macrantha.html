Melanoseris macrantha

Melanoseris macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered melanthus, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and pinnately compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fibrous roots make it suitable for erosion control in arid and semi-arid regions.

Melanoseris macrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
