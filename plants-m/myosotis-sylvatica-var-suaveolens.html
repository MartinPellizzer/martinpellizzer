Myosotis sylvatica var. suaveolens

Myosotis sylvatica var. suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or violet petals and a fragrant, sweet scent that distinguishes it from other varieties of forget-me-nots. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and meadows. Its flowers bloom in early summer and are often used in floral arrangements due to their aesthetic appeal.

The leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its overall botanical appearance.
