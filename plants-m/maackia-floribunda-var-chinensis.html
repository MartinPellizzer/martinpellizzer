Maackia floribunda var. chinensis

Maackia floribunda var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese maackia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in clusters along the stems. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its spring flowering display.
