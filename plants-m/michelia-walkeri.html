Michelia walkeri

Michelia walkeri, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or fragrant Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white to pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin strips.

Michelia walkeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its elegant appearance and pleasant scent.
