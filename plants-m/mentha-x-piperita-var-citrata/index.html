Mentha × Piperita Var. Citrata

Peppermint lemon, scientifically known as Mentha × piperita var. citrata, is a hybrid plant that combines the refreshing qualities of peppermint and lemon. This aromatic herb is prized for its invigorating scent and versatile uses in both culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its leaves are often used to make teas, aromatherapy oils, and topical balms. Due to its unique flavor profile, it has become a popular choice for gardeners and herbalists alike.
