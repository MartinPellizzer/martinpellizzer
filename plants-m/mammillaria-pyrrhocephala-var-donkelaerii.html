Mammillaria pyrrhocephala var. donkelaerii

Mammillaria pyrrhocephala var. donkelaerii, commonly known as the Donkelaer's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical stem with numerous tubercles that bear white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has small, spiny areoles and produces pale pink to white blooms. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
