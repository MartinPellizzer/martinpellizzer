Meliosma henryi subsp. thorelii

Meliosma henryi subsp.

thorelii, commonly known as the Chinese honeysuckle vine, is a deciduous climbing plant native to China. It features heart-shaped, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a decorative vine.
