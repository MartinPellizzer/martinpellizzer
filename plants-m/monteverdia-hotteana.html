Monteverdia hotteana

Monteverdia hotteana, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Mexico.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Monteverdia hotteana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
