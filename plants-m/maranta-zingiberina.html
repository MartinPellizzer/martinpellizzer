Maranta zingiberina

Maranta zingiberina, commonly known as the gingerleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.

Maranta zingiberina thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
