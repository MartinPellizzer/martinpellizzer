Mitchella

Mitchella, commonly known as littleleaf mitchella, is a small evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically less than an inch in length. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. Mitchella thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing food for wildlife.
