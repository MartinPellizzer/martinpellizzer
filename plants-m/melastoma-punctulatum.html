Melastoma punctulatum

Melastoma punctulatum, commonly known as the spotted melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually pink or white in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
