Monteverdia splendens

Monteverdia splendens, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling stars, hence its common name. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Monteverdia splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
