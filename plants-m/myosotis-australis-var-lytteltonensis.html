Myosotis australis var. lytteltonensis

Myosotis australis var. lytteltonensis, commonly known as the Lyttelton forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring, typically from September to November. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in streamside habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
