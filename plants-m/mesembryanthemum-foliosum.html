Mesembryanthemum foliosum

Mesembryanthemum foliosum, commonly known as the leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the cooler months. It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptations to dry climates.

Mesembryanthemum foliosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
