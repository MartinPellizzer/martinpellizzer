Matthiola odoratissima var. tatarica

Matthiola odoratissima var. tatarica, commonly known as the Tatarian stock, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in racemes. It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and stems, which emit a spicy scent.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
