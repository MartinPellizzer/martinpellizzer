Microbrochis

Microbrochis, commonly known as the star orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Microbrochis species are characterized by their small, delicate flowers that often have a star-like shape, hence their common name. They typically grow in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories.

The genus is noted for its ornamental value and is appreciated by horticulturists for its unique floral structure and color variations.
