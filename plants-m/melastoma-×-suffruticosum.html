Melastoma × suffruticosum

Melastoma × suffruticosum, commonly known as the shrub mallow, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves that are typically opposite and elliptical in shape. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are often pink or red, which bloom in the summer months. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Melastoma × suffruticosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering display.
