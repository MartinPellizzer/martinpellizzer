Microkentia

Microkentia, commonly known as the umbrella tree, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features a short, thick trunk and broad, glossy, palmate leaves that give it its common name. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Microkentia is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor spaces where it can add a touch of greenery and elegance.
