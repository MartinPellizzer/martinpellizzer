Mesophlebion

Mesophlebion, commonly known as the water fern, is a type of aquatic plant that thrives in freshwater environments.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that float on the water's surface. The plant has a complex root system that helps anchor it in the substrate and absorb nutrients. Mesophlebion is often found in slow-moving streams, ponds, and marshes.
