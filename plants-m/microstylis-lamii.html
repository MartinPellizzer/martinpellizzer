Microstylis lamii

Microstylis lamii, commonly known as the slender-panicle grass, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems with whorled leaves that are narrow and grass-like. Its inflorescence is a dense, panicle-like cluster of small, spikelet-bearing branches.

Microstylis lamii is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
