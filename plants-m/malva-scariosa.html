Malva scariosa

Malva scariosa, commonly known as the scarlet mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, lobed leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

It is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.
