Mammillaria backebergiana var. ernestii

Mammillaria backebergiana var. ernestii, commonly known as the Ernest's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to arid conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This variety is relatively small, often reaching only a few centimeters in height, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
