Mikania psylobothrya

Mikania psylobothrya, commonly known as the slenderleaf Mikania, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, oval-shaped fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
