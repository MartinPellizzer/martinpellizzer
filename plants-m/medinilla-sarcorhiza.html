Medinilla sarcorhiza

Medinilla sarcorhiza, commonly known as the red-flowered medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Medinilla sarcorhiza is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
