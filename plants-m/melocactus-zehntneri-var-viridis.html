Melocactus zehntneri var. viridis

Melocactus zehntneri var. viridis, commonly known as the green melocactus, is a succulent plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem covered in dense, sharp spines and a distinctive green coloration. The plant produces yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
