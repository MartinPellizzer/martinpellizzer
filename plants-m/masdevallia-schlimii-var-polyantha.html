Masdevallia schlimii var. polyantha

Masdevallia schlimii var. polyantha, commonly known as the polyantha masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its delicate, spurred petals. It prefers cool, humid environments with high light intensity but requires careful watering to avoid root rot.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
