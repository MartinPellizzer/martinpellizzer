Masdevallia aurantiaca

Masdevallia aurantiaca, commonly known as the Orange Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and red flowers, which bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
