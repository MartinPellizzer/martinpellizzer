Maurandya barclayana f. coccinea

Maurandya barclayana f. coccinea, commonly known as the Scarlet Maurandya, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red to orange-red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are somewhat hairy to the touch.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
