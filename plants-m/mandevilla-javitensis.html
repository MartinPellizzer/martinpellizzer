Mandevilla javitensis

Mandevilla javitensis, commonly known as the Java mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow as a shrub or vine, often reaching several meters in length. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and ample sunlight.

Mandevilla javitensis is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
