Myosotis tenuiflora

Myosotis tenuiflora, commonly known as slender-flowered forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers with five petals, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and along streams.

Myosotis tenuiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also significant in New Zealand's native flora.
