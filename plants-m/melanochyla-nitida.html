Melanochyla nitida

Melanochyla nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and its striking, pale green to silver-colored leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in a loose spike. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light.

Melanochyla nitida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
