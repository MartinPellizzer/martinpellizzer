Malacocarpus pauciareolatus

Malacocarpus pauciareolatus, commonly known as the sparse-areola milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves.
