Mascarenhasia lisianthiflora var. hybrida

Mascarenhasia lisianthiflora var. hybrida, commonly known as the Mascarene lily, is a striking tropical plant native to the Mascarene Islands.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a contrasting throat. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated in warm climates for its dramatic floral display.
