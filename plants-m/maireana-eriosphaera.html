Maireana eriosphaera

Maireana eriosphaera, commonly known as the silky maireana, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought tolerance and resistance to harsh conditions.

Its unique botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
