Millettia orissae

Millettia orissae, commonly known as the Indian silk tree, is a deciduous leguminous plant native to India and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Millettia orissae is well adapted to tropical and subtropical climates.
