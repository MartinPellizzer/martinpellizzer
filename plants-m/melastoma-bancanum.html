Melastoma bancanum

Melastoma bancanum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to pink, often with a distinctive yellow or orange center. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Melastoma bancanum is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
