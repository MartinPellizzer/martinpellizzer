Mediolobivia aureiflora var. boedekeriana

Mediolobivia aureiflora var. boedekeriana, commonly known as the golden-flowered boedekeriana, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in dense, showy clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The foliage is typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, adding to its ornamental value. Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid environments with partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
