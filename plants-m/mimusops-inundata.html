Mimusops inundata

Mimusops inundata, commonly known as the water mimosa, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near water bodies. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
