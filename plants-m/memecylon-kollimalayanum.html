Memecylon kollimalayanum

Memecylon kollimalayanum, commonly known as the Himalayan memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. The plant has simple, opposite, and elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its small, greenish-white flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by small, round berries.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
