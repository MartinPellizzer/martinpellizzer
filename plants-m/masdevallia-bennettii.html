Masdevallia bennettii

Masdevallia bennettii, commonly known as Bennett's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, intricate floral structure, featuring showy, often ruffled petals and sepals. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, producing clusters of delicate, colorful flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in high humidity and requires well-draining, acidic soil to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts in controlled environments.
