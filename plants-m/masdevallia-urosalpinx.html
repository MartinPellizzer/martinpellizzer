Masdevallia urosalpinx

Masdevallia urosalpinx, commonly known as the "Cape of Good Hope orchid," is a terrestrial orchid native to South Africa.

It features slender, hairy pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with sepals and petals that are typically white or pale pink, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
