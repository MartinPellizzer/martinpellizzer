Monachosorum nipponicum

Monachosorum nipponicum, commonly known as the Japanese monk's orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, and it produces intricate, spurred orchid flowers with a unique coloration.

Due to its limited habitat and conservation status, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
