Masdevallia rubiginosa

Masdevallia rubiginosa, commonly known as the rusty masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking, velvety purple to brown inflorescence. The plant typically produces small, intricate flowers with a unique coloration pattern that resembles rust or tarnish. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unusual appearance and resilience.
