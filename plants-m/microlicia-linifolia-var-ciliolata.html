Microlicia linifolia var. ciliolata

Microlicia linifolia var. ciliolata, commonly known as the silky leaf microlicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in understory layers of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
