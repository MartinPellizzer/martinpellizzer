Mohria marginalis

Mohria marginalis, commonly known as the marginal mohria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry soils. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Mohria marginalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
