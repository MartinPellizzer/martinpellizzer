Melocactus intermedius var. tenuispinus

Melocactus intermedius var. tenuispinus, commonly known as the narrow-spined intermediate melocactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It has spines that are relatively short and sparse compared to other melocactus varieties.

This species is well adapted to dry conditions and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
