Melastoma hansenii

Melastoma hansenii, commonly known as the Hansen's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds, facilitating its propagation in suitable habitats.

Melastoma hansenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
