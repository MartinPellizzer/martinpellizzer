Meliadelpha

Meliadelpha, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the appearance of a flame. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or lance-shaped. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Meliadelpha is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
