Microstegium aristulatum

Microstegium aristulatum, commonly known as the slender spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats such as swamps, marshes, and along stream banks. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in wetland ecosystems.

Microstegium aristulatum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
