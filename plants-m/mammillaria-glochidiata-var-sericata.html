Mammillaria glochidiata var. sericata

Mammillaria glochidiata var. sericata, commonly known as the silver mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique look and low maintenance requirements.
