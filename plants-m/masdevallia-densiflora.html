Masdevallia densiflora

Masdevallia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered masdevallia, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its striking, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically produces clusters of small, densely packed blooms along the stems, which are often fragrant. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and indirect light.

Masdevallia densiflora is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
