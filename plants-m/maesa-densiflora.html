Maesa densiflora

Maesa densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered maesa, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, giving it its common name. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Maesa densiflora is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
