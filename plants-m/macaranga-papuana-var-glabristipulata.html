Macaranga papuana var. glabristipulata

Macaranga papuana var. glabristipulata, commonly known as the smooth-stippled macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to the Pacific islands, including Papua New Guinea and parts of Indonesia. This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
