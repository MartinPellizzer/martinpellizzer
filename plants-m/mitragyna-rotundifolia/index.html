Mitragyna Rotundifolia

Mitragyna rotundifolia is a lesser-known species within the Mitragyna genus, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This plant is characterized by its rounded leaves and small, clustered flowers, making it distinct from its more famous relative, kratom. While it shares some chemical similarities with other Mitragyna species, it has received less scientific attention and research. Its potential medicinal properties and ecological role in its native habitat make it an intriguing subject for further study.
