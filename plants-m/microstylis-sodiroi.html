Microstylis sodiroi

Microstylis sodiroi, commonly known as the slender-styled orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in the late spring or early summer. The plant has a terrestrial growth habit, with slender stems and narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive, elongated spur.

Microstylis sodiroi is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests or along stream banks.
