Myristica schlechteri

Myristica schlechteri, commonly known as the Malabar nut or Malabar pepper.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India. The plant belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic fruits. The fruit contains a single seed surrounded by a fleshy, yellowish pulp.

It is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
