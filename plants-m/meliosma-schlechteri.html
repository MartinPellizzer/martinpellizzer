Meliosma schlechteri

Meliosma schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's meliosma, is a member of the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and related trees.

Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.
