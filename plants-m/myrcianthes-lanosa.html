Myrcianthes lanosa

Myrcianthes lanosa, commonly known as the woolly myrcia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage, which gives it a woolly appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a rough texture.

Myrcianthes lanosa is often found in humid, shaded environments within forests.
