Miconia conosetifera

Miconia conosetifera, commonly known as the broadleaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Due to its rapid growth and invasive tendencies, it is considered a problematic species in some regions.
