Monguia

Monguia, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the evening, hence its common name. Its leaves are oval-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Monguia is often grown for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
