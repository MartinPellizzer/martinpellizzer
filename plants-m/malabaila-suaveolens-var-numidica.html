Malabaila suaveolens var. numidica

Malabaila suaveolens var. numidica, commonly known as the fragrant yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its yellow, bell-shaped flowers that emit a sweet, aromatic scent. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly hairy underside.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
