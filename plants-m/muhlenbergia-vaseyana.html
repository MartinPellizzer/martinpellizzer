Muhlenbergia vaseyana

Muhlenbergia vaseyana, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces small, fluffy seed heads that are characteristic of the grass family.

Muhlenbergia vaseyana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in arid environments.
