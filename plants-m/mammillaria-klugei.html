Mammillaria klugei

Mammillaria klugei, commonly known as the Kluge's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded appearance. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
