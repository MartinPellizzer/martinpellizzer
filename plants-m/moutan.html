Moutan

Moutan, commonly known as tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its thick, woody stems and large, showy flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces fruit that resembles a small, hairy capsule. Moutan is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been cultivated for centuries in traditional Chinese gardens.

Its roots are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
