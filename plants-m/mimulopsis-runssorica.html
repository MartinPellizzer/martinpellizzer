Mimulopsis runssorica

Mimulopsis runssorica, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of North America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability. Its leaves are often reduced to small, scale-like structures to minimize water loss.

Mimulopsis runssorica plays an important ecological role in its habitat by providing nectar for pollinators and stabilizing sandy soils.
