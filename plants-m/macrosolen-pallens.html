Macrosolen pallens

Macrosolen pallens, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a strong, sometimes unpleasant odor. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

The species is often found in wetlands, swamps, and along the edges of ponds.
