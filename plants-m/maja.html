Maja

Maja, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white or pink petals. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in grasslands and shrublands.

Maja is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
