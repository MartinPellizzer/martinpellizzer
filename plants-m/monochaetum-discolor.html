Monochaetum discolor

Monochaetum discolor, commonly known as the variable spikegrass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to Australia, particularly found in the southern regions of New South Wales and Victoria. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.
