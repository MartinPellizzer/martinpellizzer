Myristica subsessilis

Myristica subsessilis, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces aromatic seeds that are harvested for their essential oils and spices, including nutmeg and mace. Its dark green, leathery leaves are elliptical and typically measure up to 20 cm in length.

The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and has a dense canopy that supports a diverse ecosystem.
