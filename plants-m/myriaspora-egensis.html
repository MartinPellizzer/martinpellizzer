Myriaspora egensis

Myriaspora egensis, commonly known as the Egerton myriaspora, is a unique fungal species characterized by its intricate spore structures.

It belongs to the class Agaricomycetes and is notable for its complex morphology, including a distinctive cap and stem. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its spores are dispersed through wind, contributing to its wide distribution.
