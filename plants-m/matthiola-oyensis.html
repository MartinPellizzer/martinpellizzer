Matthiola oyensis

Matthiola oyensis, commonly known as the Oyeni stock, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the top of slender stems. Its leaves are narrow and alternate, with a slightly hairy texture.

Matthiola oyensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
