Microkentia billardierei

Microkentia billardierei, commonly known as the silver ponytail palm, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It is a member of the Arecaceae family and is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and a thick, trunk-like stem. The plant has a slow growth rate and is often cultivated as a decorative indoor plant due to its attractive appearance.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor environments with moderate humidity.
