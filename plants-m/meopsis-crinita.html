Meopsis crinita

Meopsis crinita, commonly known as the silky meops, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.

Meopsis crinita is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
