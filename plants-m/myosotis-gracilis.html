Myosotis gracilis

Myosotis gracilis, commonly known as slender forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers with five petals, often surrounded by yellowish sepals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Myosotis gracilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting pollinators.
