Macropanax rosthornii

The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, attracting various pollinators such as beetles and flies. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Macropanax rosthornii is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
