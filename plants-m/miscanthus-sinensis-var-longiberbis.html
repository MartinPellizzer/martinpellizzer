Miscanthus sinensis var. longiberbis

Miscanthus sinensis var. longiberbis, commonly known as the silvergrass or Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, arching stems and silvery-blue foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers in late summer, which contribute to its visual appeal. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic qualities and low maintenance requirements.
