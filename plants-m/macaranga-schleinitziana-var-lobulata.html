Macaranga schleinitziana var. lobulata

Macaranga schleinitziana var. lobulata, commonly known as the lobed macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with lobed margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in disturbed soils and secondary forests.

The species is notable for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
