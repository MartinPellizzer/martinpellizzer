Mimosa crinita

Mimosa crinita, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Mimosa crinita is also known for its sensitive leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait that adds to its unique appeal.
