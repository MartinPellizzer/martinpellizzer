Microsteris gracilis var. humilior

Microsteris gracilis var. humilior, commonly known as the slender microsteris, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features delicate, grass-like leaves that are narrow and slightly curled at the edges. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as an indicator species for specific wetland ecosystems.
