Macropiper puberulum f. glabrum

Macropiper puberulum f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leaved pepperbush, is a variant of the species Macropiper puberulum, characterized by its smooth, glabrous foliage.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various species of pepper plants. This plant is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it typically grows in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are usually lanceolate and emit a mild peppery aroma when crushed.

The plant is often found in lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
