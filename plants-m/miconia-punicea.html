Miconia punicea

Miconia punicea, commonly known as the red miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually a deep red or purple color. It produces showy, clustered flowers with bright yellow petals that attract pollinators such as bees and birds.

Miconia punicea is often found in moist, shaded understory habitats within tropical rainforests.
