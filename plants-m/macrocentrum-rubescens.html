Macrocentrum rubescens

Macrocentrum rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered spiderwort, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features striking red, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for wetland restoration and ornamental gardening.
