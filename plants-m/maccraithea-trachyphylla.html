Maccraithea trachyphylla

Maccraithea trachyphylla, commonly known as the rough-leaved maccraithea, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of southern Africa.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, adding a subtle aesthetic to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
