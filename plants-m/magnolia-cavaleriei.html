Magnolia cavaleriei

Magnolia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The Yunnan magnolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
