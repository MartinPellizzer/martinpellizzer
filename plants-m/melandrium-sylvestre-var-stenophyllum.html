Melandrium sylvestre var. stenophyllum

Melandrium sylvestre var. stenophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and typically grows in grassy areas and woodland edges. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, pinkish-white, and arranged in clusters, blooming in early summer.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.
