Mutisia obovata

Mutisia obovata, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features large, showy yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually obovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
