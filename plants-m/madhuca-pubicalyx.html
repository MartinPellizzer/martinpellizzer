Madhuca pubicalyx

Madhuca pubicalyx, commonly known as the Indian shea tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and smooth, dark bark. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, starchy fruits. The tree is also appreciated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
