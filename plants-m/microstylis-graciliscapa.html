Microstylis graciliscapa

Microstylis graciliscapa, commonly known as the slender-styled grass, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow, elongated leaves. The plant typically grows in wet or marshy environments, thriving in moist soils. Its inflorescence consists of small, spike-like clusters of flowers, which are often inconspicuous.
