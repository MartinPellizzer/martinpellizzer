Medinilla lasioclados

Medinilla lasioclados, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round, orange-red fruits that are edible and have a citrus-like flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both gardens and indoor cultivation.
