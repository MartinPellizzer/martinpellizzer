Medicago vassilczenkoi

Medicago vassilczenkoi, commonly known as the Vassil'czenko milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppe and desert regions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and can be used as forage for livestock in certain areas.
