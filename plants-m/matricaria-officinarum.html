Matricaria officinarum

Matricaria officinarum, commonly known as German chamomile, is a flowering plant in the sunflower family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers, which are harvested for their aromatic oils. Its leaves are also used in herbal remedies due to their calming properties.

Matricaria officinarum is widely cultivated for both medicinal and aromatic purposes.
