Miconia aguaclarensis

Miconia aguaclarensis, commonly known as the water clear stream plant, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments along stream banks and in humid forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
