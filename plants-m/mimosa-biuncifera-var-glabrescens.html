Mimosa biuncifera var. glabrescens

Mimosa biuncifera var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth mimosa, is a variant of the mimosa plant characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves.

It is a deciduous shrub native to parts of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are typically small and delicate, and its flowers are yellow and pea-like, appearing in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaped gardens.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate dry conditions.
