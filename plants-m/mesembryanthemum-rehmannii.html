Mesembryanthemum rehmannii

Mesembryanthemum rehmannii, commonly known as the rock purslane, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mesembryanthemum rehmannii is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its resilience and aesthetic appeal.
