Musa × sapientum var. galatayan

Musa × sapientum var. galatayan, commonly known as the Galatayan banana, is a variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of elongated, yellow fruit that ripen to a sweet, soft texture. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and warm temperatures. It is cultivated for its edible fruit, which is rich in potassium and other nutrients.

This variety is also valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience to certain pests and diseases.
