Microloma incanum

Microloma incanum, commonly known as the silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. Its flowers are yellow and resemble those of the dandelion, blooming in early spring.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.
