Melastoma brachyodon

Melastoma brachyodon, commonly known as the small-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish to reddish flowers. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with ciliate margins and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma brachyodon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
