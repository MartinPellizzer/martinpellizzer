Mischobulbum emeiense

Mischobulbum emeiense, commonly known as the Emei mountain spurge, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often spiny foliage. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and thrives in temperate climates. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
