Mesembryanthemum alatum

Mesembryanthemum alatum, commonly known as the fleshy ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in a white, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
