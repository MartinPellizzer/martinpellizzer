Maranta gigantea

Maranta gigantea, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Maranta gigantea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of starchy roots.
