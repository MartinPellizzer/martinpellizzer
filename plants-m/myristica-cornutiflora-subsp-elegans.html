Myristica cornutiflora subsp. elegans

Myristica cornutiflora subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant nutmeg. It is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its fragrant, woody fruits that contain aromatic seeds. The subspecies elegans is distinguished by its more slender and elegant appearance compared to other subspecies.

It is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
