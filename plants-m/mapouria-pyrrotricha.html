Mapouria pyrrotricha

Mapouria pyrrotricha, commonly known as the red-leaf mapouria, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features elliptical leaves that are typically red or purple on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds.

Mapouria pyrrotricha is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant foliage.
