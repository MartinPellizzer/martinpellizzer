Masdevallia filaria

Masdevallia filaria, commonly known as the slender masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without parasitizing them. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light.

Masdevallia filaria is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate beauty and unique floral structure.
