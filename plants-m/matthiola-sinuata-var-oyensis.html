Matthiola sinuata var. oyensis

Matthiola sinuata var. oyensis, commonly known as the Oyen's wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, showy flowers and aromatic foliage. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink, are often clustered in racemes.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
