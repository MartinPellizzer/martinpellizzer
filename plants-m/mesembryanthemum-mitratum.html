Mesembryanthemum mitratum

Mesembryanthemum mitratum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves and small, star-shaped flowers.
