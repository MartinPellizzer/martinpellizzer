Moraea margaretae

Moraea margaretae, commonly known as the Margaretae Moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, tubular flowers. Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
