Meliocarpus anisopterus

Meliocarpus anisopterus, commonly known as the narrow-leafed ironwood, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in coastal or near-coastal regions. The plant has dark green, narrow leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable timber and is often used in landscaping and restoration projects.
