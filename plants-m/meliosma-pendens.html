Meliosma pendens

Meliosma pendens, commonly known as the hanging meliosma, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, drooping inflorescences and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
