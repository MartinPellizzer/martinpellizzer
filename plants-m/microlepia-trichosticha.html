Microlepia trichosticha

Microlepia trichosticha, commonly known as the feather fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that are typically divided into numerous small, linear segments. The plant is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Microlepia trichosticha has rhizomes that are covered with a layer of fine, hair-like structures, contributing to its common name.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
