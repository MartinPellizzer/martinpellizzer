Myoporum serratum

Myoporum serratum, commonly known as the serrated myoporum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features lance-shaped leaves with serrated edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Myoporum serratum is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a ground cover in arid environments.
