Mentha × dumetorum

Mentha × dumetorum, commonly known as "pennyroyal," is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its square, erect stems and opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale purple or white, arranged in whorls along the stem. It is native to Europe and western Asia but has been introduced to other regions, where it can become invasive.

Mentha × dumetorum is known for its strong, distinctive scent and has been historically used in traditional medicine and as a pest repellent.
