Malcolmia patula var. tricolor

Malcolmia patula var. tricolor, commonly known as the three-color malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy, tri-colored flowers that typically display shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders. Its blooming period extends from late spring to early autumn, adding vibrant color to gardens.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
