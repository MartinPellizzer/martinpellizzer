Microchirita mollissima var. glabra

Microchirita mollissima var. glabra, commonly known as the silky microchirita, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
