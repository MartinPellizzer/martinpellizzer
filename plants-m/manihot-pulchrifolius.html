Manihot pulchrifolius

Manihot pulchrifolius, commonly known as the beautiful-leaf arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid lowland areas.
