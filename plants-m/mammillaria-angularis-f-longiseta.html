Mammillaria angularis f. longiseta

Mammillaria angularis f. longiseta, commonly known as the long-spined angular mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in prominent, angular tubercles. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its fleshy, spiny stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
