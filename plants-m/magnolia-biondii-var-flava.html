Magnolia biondii var. flava

Magnolia biondii var. flava, commonly known as the yellow magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The tree has smooth, light-gray bark and oval-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its unique floral coloration makes it a standout in gardens and landscapes.
