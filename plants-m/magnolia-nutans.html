Magnolia nutans

Magnolia nutans, commonly known as the nodding magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several winged seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
