Miconia farinasii

Miconia farinasii, commonly known as the pink miconia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a round, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Miconia farinasii is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
