Mammillaria micromeris var. unguispina

Mammillaria micromeris var. unguispina, commonly known as the spined mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant features a short, cylindrical stem with areoles that bear both radial and central spines. It produces yellow flowers in late spring or early summer.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
