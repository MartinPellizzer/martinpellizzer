Mandevilla pristina

Mandevilla pristina, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Mandevilla pristina is often cultivated as a ornamental plant in gardens and containers due to its beauty and adaptability.
