Melhania balakrishnanii

Melhania balakrishnanii, commonly known as the Balakrishnan's melhania, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melhaniaceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
