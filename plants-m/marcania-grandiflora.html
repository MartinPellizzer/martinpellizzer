Marcania grandiflora

Marcania grandiflora, commonly known as the grandiflora marcania, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along its stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Marcania grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
