Muhlenbergia brasiliensis

Muhlenbergia brasiliensis, commonly known as Brazilian muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, bluish-green leaves. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish to purple seed heads that are both ornamental and attractively structured. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Muhlenbergia brasiliensis is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
