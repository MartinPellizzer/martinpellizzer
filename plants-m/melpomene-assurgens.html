Melpomene assurgens

Melpomene assurgens, commonly known as the red velvet orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red petals and a distinctive yellow throat, which make it highly ornamental. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

Melpomene assurgens requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
