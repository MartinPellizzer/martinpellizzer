Meliosma pinnata var. oldhamii

Meliosma pinnata var. oldhamii, commonly known as the Oldham's meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with pinnate arrangement, which are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in racemes, typically blooming in the spring. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Its ornamental value is appreciated in horticulture due to its attractive foliage and unique growth habit.
