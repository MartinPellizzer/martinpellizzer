Malesherbia lanceolata

Malesherbia lanceolata, commonly known as the slender malesherbia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus plants. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
