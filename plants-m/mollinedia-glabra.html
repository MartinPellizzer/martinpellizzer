Mollinedia glabra

Mollinedia glabra, commonly known as the smooth mollinedia, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are smooth to the touch, as indicated by its species name "glabra.". It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring.

Mollinedia glabra is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
