Mertensia media

Mertensia media, commonly known as bluebells, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Mertensia media is often found in woodlands and along stream banks, making it a popular choice for naturalized gardens.
