Mesembryanthemum swartbergense

Mesembryanthemum swartbergense, commonly known as the Swartberg iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often blue-green leaves that store water. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
