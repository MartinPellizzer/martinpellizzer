Marginaria friedrichsthaliana

Marginaria friedrichsthaliana, commonly known as the Friedrichsthaliana thyme.

It is a low-growing perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features aromatic, narrow leaves and produces small, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its botanical characteristics include glandular hairs on the leaves, which contribute to its aromatic properties.
