Maranta porphyrocaulis

Maranta porphyrocaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, waxy leaves that display a variety of colors, including red, purple, and green, depending on light exposure. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

Maranta porphyrocaulis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
