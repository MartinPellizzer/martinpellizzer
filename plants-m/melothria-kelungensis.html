Melothria kelungensis

Melothria kelungensis, commonly known as the Kelungensis sourberry, is a tropical climbing vine native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It produces small, green, edible fruits that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates and requires support for its vining growth habit. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a serrated edge.

The fruit is known for its tart flavor and is sometimes used in local cuisines.
