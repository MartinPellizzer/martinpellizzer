Maxillaria vaginalis

Maxillaria vaginalis, commonly known as the violet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces fragrant, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria vaginalis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm climates.
