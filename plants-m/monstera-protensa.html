Monstera protensa

Monstera protensa, commonly known as the giant split-leaf philodendron, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive splits as the plant matures. The leaves can grow up to 3 feet in length and are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
