Myosotis collina var. stricta

Myosotis collina var. stricta, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, blue to violet flowers with five petals. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
