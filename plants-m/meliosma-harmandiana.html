Meliosma harmandiana

Meliosma harmandiana, commonly known as the Harmand's meliosma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliosmaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
