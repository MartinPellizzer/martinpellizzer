Maxillaria tenuifolia

Maxillaria tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, often speckled lip. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in shaded, humid environments. It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.

Maxillaria tenuifolia is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its relatively easy care requirements and aesthetic appeal.
