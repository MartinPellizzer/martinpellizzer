Mammillaria prolifera var. arachnoidea

Mammillaria prolifera var. arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider milkplant, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of succulents.
