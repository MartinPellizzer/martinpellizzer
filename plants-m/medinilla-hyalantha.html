Medinilla hyalantha

Medinilla hyalantha, commonly known as the glassy medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white flowers that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla hyalantha is popular as a ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy flowers.
