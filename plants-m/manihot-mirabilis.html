Manihot mirabilis

Manihot mirabilis, commonly known as the arrowleaf balsam, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.
