Melicope Pachypoda

Melicope pachypoda is a unique and rare plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This species is often found in mesic to wet forest habitats, where it thrives in shaded, moist environments. Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it faces threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this culturally and ecologically important plant.
