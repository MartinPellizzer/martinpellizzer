Mammillaria isotensis

Mammillaria isotensis, commonly known as the Isotens cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a pale green stem. It produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, red fruits.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
