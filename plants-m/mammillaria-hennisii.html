Mammillaria hennisii

Mammillaria hennisii, commonly known as the Hennisii Hedgehog Cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a dense covering of white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
