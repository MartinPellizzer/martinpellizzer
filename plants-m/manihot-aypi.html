Manihot aypi

Manihot aypi, commonly known as the African bitter millet, is a herbaceous annual plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers and is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
