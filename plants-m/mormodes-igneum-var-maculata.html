Mormodes igneum var. maculata

Mormodes igneum var. maculata, commonly known as the spotted flame orchid, is a striking terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers with distinctive black spots, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often near streams or in dense undergrowth. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

The orchid's unique appearance makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and enthusiasts of exotic flora.
