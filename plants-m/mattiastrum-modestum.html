Mattiastrum modestum

Mattiastrum modestum, commonly known as the small-leaved mat plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Mattiastrum modestum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
