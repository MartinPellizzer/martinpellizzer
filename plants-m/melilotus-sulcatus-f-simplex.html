Melilotus sulcatus f. simplex

Melilotus sulcatus f. simplex, commonly known as the smooth melilot, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It is native to Europe and is often found in disturbed soils, such as along roadsides or in dry meadows.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless leaves and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
