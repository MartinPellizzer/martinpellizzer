Maxillaria estradae

Maxillaria estradae, commonly known as the Estrada's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique floral structure. The plant typically produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria estradae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
