Marrubium africanum

Marrubium africanum, commonly known as African horehound, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its gray-green leaves and spikes of small blue or purple flowers. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in regions with Mediterranean climates.

Marrubium africanum is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
