Mattiastrum karakoricum

Mattiastrum karakoricum, commonly known as the Karakoram stonecrop, is a succulent plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Karakoram Mountains.

It belongs to the Crassulaceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form. Mattiastrum karakoricum is well-suited to rocky and gravelly soils, thriving in full sun and cold climates.
