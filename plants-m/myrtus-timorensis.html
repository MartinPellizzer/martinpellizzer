Myrtus timorensis

Myrtus timorensis, commonly known as Timor myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Timor.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes well-known plants like eucalyptus and tea tree. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves that are often aromatic, and it produces small, fragrant white flowers. Myrtus timorensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique and attractive species for horticultural cultivation.
