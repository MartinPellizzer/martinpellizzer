Maxillaria triangularis

Maxillaria triangularis, commonly known as the triangular orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive triangular-shaped pseudobulbs and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers with a unique, three-lobed lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria triangularis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
