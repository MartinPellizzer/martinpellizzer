Michelia balansae var. brevipes

Michelia balansae var. brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed Michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in horticulture.
