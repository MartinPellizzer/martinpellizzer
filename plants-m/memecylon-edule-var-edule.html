Memecylon edule var. edule

Memecylon edule var. edule, commonly known as the edible memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The berries are used in traditional cuisines and are valued for their nutritional content.
