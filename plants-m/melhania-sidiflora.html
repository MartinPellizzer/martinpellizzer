Melhania sidiflora

Melhania sidiflora, commonly known as the swordleaf melhania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Melhania sidiflora typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
