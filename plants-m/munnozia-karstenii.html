Munnozia karstenii

Munnozia karstenii, commonly known as the Karsten's mule's ear, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its gray-green, woolly leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and open habitats, such as slopes and meadows. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing drought tolerance.

Munnozia karstenii is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers.
