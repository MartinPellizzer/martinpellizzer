Mallotus yifengensis

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yifeng region of Hubei Province. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in inflorescences, which are typically borne on short peduncles.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its attractive foliage.
