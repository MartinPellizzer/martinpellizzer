Miliusa chunii

Miliusa chunii, commonly known as the Chinese starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapindaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor.

Miliusa chunii is valued for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
