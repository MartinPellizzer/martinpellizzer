Myosotis decumbens subsp. kerneri

Myosotis decumbens subsp.

kerneri, commonly known as the Kern's forget-me-not, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from April to May.

The plant has creeping stems that root at the nodes, allowing it to spread along the ground. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes.
