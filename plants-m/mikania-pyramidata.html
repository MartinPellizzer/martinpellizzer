Mikania pyramidata

Mikania pyramidata, commonly known as the climbing Mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in a cylindrical shape. It is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
