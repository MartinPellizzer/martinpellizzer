Mariosousa compacta

Mariosousa compacta, commonly known as the dwarf mariosousa, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in dense, compact clusters.

Mariosousa compacta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
