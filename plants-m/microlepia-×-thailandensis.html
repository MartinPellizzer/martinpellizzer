Microlepia × thailandensis

Microlepia × thailandensis, commonly known as the Thailand microlepia, is a hybrid fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its spores are released from the sori, which are clustered on the underside of the fronds.

Microlepia × thailandensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
