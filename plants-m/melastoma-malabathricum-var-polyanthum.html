Melastoma malabathricum var. polyanthum

Melastoma malabathricum var. polyanthum, commonly known as the polyanthum melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. Its flowers are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters, and are known for their fragrant scent.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, flat seeds.
