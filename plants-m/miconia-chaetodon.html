Miconia chaetodon

Miconia chaetodon, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Miconia chaetodon is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value has led to its introduction in various regions, though it can become invasive in some areas.
