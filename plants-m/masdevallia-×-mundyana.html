Masdevallia × mundyana

Masdevallia × mundyana, commonly known as the Mundy's masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower spikes that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant typically produces numerous small, lance-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Masdevallia × mundyana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
