Maxillaria dichroma

Maxillaria dichroma, commonly known as the dark orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black petals and sepals, which contrast with its white or light green lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Maxillaria dichroma is prized for its striking appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
