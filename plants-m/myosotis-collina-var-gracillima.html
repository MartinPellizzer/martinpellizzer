Myosotis collina var. gracillima

Myosotis collina var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of southern South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming, petite blossoms.
