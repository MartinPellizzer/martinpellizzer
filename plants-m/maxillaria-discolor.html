Maxillaria discolor

Maxillaria discolor, commonly known as the Spotted Maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, hairy pseudobulbs and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple with contrasting white or yellow markings. Maxillaria discolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
