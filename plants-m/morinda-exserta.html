Morinda exserta

Morinda exserta, commonly known as the red morinda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are bright red, fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Morinda exserta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
