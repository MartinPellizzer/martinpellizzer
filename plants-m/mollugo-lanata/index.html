Mollugo Lanata

Mollugo lanata is a small, herbaceous plant known for its unique appearance and adaptive traits. It thrives in sandy and disturbed soils, often found in coastal areas and along roadsides. The plant's hairy leaves and small, yellow flowers make it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. Despite its delicate look, it is highly resilient and can survive in harsh environmental conditions. This species plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local ecosystems.
