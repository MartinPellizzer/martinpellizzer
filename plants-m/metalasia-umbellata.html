Metalasia umbellata

Metalasia umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella lily, is a herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, umbellate inflorescence composed of numerous white to pale blue flowers. The plant features strap-like, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Metalasia umbellata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its elegant, delicate blooms.
