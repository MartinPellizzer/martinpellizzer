Matthiola tenuiflora

Matthiola tenuiflora, commonly known as slender-flowered stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It has a compact growth habit with linear, grass-like leaves and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Matthiola tenuiflora is also valued for its use in traditional herbal medicine.
