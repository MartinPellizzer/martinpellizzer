Mussaenda candidissima

Mussaenda candidissima, commonly known as the white mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm climates.
