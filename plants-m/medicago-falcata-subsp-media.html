Medicago falcata subsp. media

Medicago falcata subsp.

media, commonly known as the intermediate goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
