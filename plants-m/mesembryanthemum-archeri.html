Mesembryanthemum archeri

Mesembryanthemum archeri, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or ozark star, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum archeri thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
