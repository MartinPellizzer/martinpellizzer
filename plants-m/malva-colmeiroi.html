Malva colmeiroi

Malva colmeiroi, commonly known as the Portuguese mallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture and produces showy, pink or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

Malva colmeiroi is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
