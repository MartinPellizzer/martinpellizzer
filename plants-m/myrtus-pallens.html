Myrtus pallens

Myrtus pallens, commonly known as the silver myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. Myrtus pallens thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

It is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in herbal remedies.
