Mammillaria nunezii var. solisii

Mammillaria nunezii var. solisii, commonly known as the Solisii nipple cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered with white, woolly spines and prominent areoles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
