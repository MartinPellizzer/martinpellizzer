Myrcia vernicosa

Myrcia vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a dense, compact growth habit. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, while its flowers are small and white or pink in color.

Myrcia vernicosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
