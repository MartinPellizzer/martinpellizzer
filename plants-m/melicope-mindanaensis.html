Melicope mindanaensis

Melicope mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao melicope, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces yellowish-orange fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland to montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
