Marsdenia medogensis

Marsdenia medogensis, commonly known as the Yunnan moonseed, is a flowering plant native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, and its fruit is a follicle containing numerous seeds.

It is often grown for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
