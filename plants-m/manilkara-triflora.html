Manilkara triflora

Manilkara triflora, commonly known as the three-flowered manilkara, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
