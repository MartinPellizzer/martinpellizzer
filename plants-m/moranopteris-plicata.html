Moranopteris plicata

Moranopteris plicata, commonly known as the pleated fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its intricate, pleated fronds that give it a distinctive appearance. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, this plant thrives in moist, shaded environments. The leaves are typically small and closely arranged, forming a dense, carpet-like structure.

Moranopteris plicata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
