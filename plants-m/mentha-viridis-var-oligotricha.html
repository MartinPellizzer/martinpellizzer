Mentha viridis var. oligotricha

Mentha viridis var. oligotricha, commonly known as narrow-leaf mint, is a variant of the common mint species.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. This plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments. It has a creeping growth habit and produces small, pale blue or purple flowers.

Mentha viridis var. oligotricha is often used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
