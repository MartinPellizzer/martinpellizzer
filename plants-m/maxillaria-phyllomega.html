Maxillaria phyllomega

Maxillaria phyllomega, commonly known as the golden orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features striking, showy flowers with golden-yellow petals and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant has a compact growth habit with dark green, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white to silvery pubescence. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

Maxillaria phyllomega prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining potting media to thrive.
