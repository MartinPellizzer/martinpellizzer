Matthiola puntensis

Matthiola puntensis, commonly known as the Punt Peninsula stock, is a flowering plant native to the Punt Peninsula in southern Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other cruciferous plants. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils.
