Macfadyena phellosperma

Macfadyena phellosperma, commonly known as the flame bean or yellow shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces striking yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

The fruit is a pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
