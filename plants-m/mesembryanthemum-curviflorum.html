Mesembryanthemum curviflorum

Mesembryanthemum curviflorum, commonly known as the curled-flower ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to coastal and Mediterranean climates.
