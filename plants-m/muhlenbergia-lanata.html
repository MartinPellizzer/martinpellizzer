Muhlenbergia lanata

Muhlenbergia lanata, commonly known as the hairy muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and tufted growth habit, making it visually distinct. The plant typically grows in dry, open areas such as prairies and rocky slopes. Its seed heads are often plume-like, adding to its ornamental value.

Muhlenbergia lanata is valued in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
