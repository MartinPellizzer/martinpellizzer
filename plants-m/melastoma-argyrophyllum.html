Melastoma argyrophyllum

Melastoma argyrophyllum, commonly known as silverleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically ovate with a silvery-white underside, giving it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
