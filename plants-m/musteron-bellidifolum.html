Musteron bellidifolum

Musteron bellidifolum, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has upright, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
