Myosotis alpestris subsp. pyrenaica

Myosotis alpestris subsp.

pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Iberian Peninsula. It features delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, preferring well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded conditions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its characteristic appearance.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in high-altitude ecosystems.
