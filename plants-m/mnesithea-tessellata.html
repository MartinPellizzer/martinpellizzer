Mnesithea tessellata

Mnesithea tessellata, commonly known as the mosaic orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, mosaic-like patterns on its leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
