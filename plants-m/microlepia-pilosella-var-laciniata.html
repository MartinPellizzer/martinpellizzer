Microlepia pilosella var. laciniata

Microlepia pilosella var. laciniata, commonly known as the fringed sword fern, is a variant of the microlepia pilosella species.

It is a fern that belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. This fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
