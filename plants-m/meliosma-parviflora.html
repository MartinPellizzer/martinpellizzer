Meliosma parviflora

Meliosma parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are typically pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a dry capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Meliosma parviflora is often found in moist, shaded woodlands and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
