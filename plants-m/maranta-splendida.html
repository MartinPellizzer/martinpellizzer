Maranta splendida

Maranta splendida, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often change color in response to light conditions. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its ability to fold its leaves at night, a behavior that gives it its common name. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect sunlight to flourish.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
