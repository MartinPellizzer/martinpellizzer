Malva Alcea Var. Fastigiata

Malva alcea var. fastigiata is a striking variant of the common mallow known for its unique, upright growth habit. This plant features velvety leaves and showy pink flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. Its ornamental value has led to its cultivation in various regions across Europe and beyond. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in the wild, often found in disturbed or cultivated areas.
