Melissa candidissima

Melissa candidissima, commonly known as lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to Europe, North Africa, and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in sunny to partially shaded locations and prefers well-drained soil. It is often used in traditional medicine for its calming properties and is also a popular ingredient in herbal teas.

Melissa candidissima is a hardy plant that can be easily cultivated in gardens or containers.
