Meriania Denticulata

Meriania denticulata is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America. Known for its vibrant, colorful flowers, it thrives in warm, humid climates. This species belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is often found in forest understories. Its unique leaf structure and showy blooms make it a favorite among horticulturists. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in cultivation outside its native habitat.
