Myrcia triantha

Myrcia triantha, commonly known as the Brazilian beauty or myrtle myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, clustered white or pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are typically elliptical and glossy, with a pleasant aromatic scent.

Myrcia triantha is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
