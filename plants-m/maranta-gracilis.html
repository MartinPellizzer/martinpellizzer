Maranta gracilis

Maranta gracilis, commonly known as the slender prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often feature white and green patterns. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes other prayer plants known for their ornamental value. Maranta gracilis typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and collection.
