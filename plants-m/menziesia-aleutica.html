Menziesia aleutica

Menziesia aleutica, commonly known as the Aleutian menziesia, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to the western coast of North America.

It typically thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and forest understories. The plant features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually densely packed along its stems. Its flowers are inconspicuous, appearing in clusters and blooming in early spring.

Menziesia aleutica plays a vital role in its ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.
