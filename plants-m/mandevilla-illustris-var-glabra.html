Mandevilla illustris var. glabra

Mandevilla illustris var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth mandevilla, is a flowering vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and heart-shaped, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Mandevilla illustris var. glabra is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor gardening.
