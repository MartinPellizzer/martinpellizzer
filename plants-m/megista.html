Megista

Megista, commonly known as the giant dandelion, is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and bright yellow flowers that resemble dandelions. The plant can grow up to several meters tall, making it a striking presence in its natural habitat. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs, and it produces a milky sap that is often used in traditional medicine.

Megista is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
