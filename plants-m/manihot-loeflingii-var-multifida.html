Manihot loeflingii var. multifida

Manihot loeflingii var. multifida, commonly known as the broad-leaf arrowleaf, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often deeply divided, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated as an ornamental plant.
