Myrtus afzelii

Myrtus afzelii, commonly known as the Armenian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey. The plant is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are often used in traditional medicine.

Myrtus afzelii is valued for its ornamental beauty and aromatic properties.
