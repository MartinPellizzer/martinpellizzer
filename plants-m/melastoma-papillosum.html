Melastoma papillosum

Melastoma papillosum, commonly known as the papillate melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine pubescence.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
