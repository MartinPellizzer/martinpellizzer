Masdevallia leonii

Masdevallia leonii, commonly known as the Leon's Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, colorful floral displays. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and horticulturists.
