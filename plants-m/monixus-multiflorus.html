Monixus multiflorus

Monixus multiflorus, commonly known as the multi-flowered monix, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It features a basal rosette of lance-shaped, dark green leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces clusters of small, showy white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. Its flowering stems are upright and can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Monixus multiflorus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shaded garden environments.
