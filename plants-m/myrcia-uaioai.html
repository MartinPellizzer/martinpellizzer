Myrcia uaioai

Myrcia uaioai, commonly known as the "red myrtle," is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, red flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains seeds, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded areas of the forest understory.

Myrcia uaioai is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
