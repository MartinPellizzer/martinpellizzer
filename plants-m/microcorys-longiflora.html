Microcorys longiflora

Microcorys longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered microcorys, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, slender stems and clustered, elongated flowers. Native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces showy, greenish-white flowers with prominent lips, typical of many orchid species.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
