Monosteria

Monosteria, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to parts of Africa and is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces small, toxic berries that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, preferring well-drained soils.

Due to its toxic properties, it is important to handle the plant with care.
