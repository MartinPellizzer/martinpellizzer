Memecylon varians var. parvifolium

Memecylon varians var. parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved memecylon, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree with simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in axillary or terminal clusters. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are usually red or yellow when mature.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
