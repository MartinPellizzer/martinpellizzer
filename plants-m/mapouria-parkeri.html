Mapouria parkeri

Mapouria parkeri, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its foliage. The plant has pinnate leaves composed of several elliptical leaflets, which are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mapouria parkeri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
