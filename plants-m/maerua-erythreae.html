Maerua erythreae

Maerua erythreae, commonly known as the red thornbush, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It has a woody stem with thorny branches and small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, woody fruits containing seeds. It is well adapted to drought conditions and can thrive in poor soils.

Maerua erythreae plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
