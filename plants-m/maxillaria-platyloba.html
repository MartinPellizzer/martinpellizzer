Maxillaria platyloba

Maxillaria platyloba, commonly known as the flat-lip maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive flat lip. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria platyloba is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
