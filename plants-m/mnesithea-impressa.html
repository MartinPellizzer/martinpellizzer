Mnesithea impressa

Mnesithea impressa, commonly known as the lace fern, is a member of the Dryopteridaceae family.

It is a delicate, evergreen fern characterized by its finely divided, lace-like fronds. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its leaves.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and indoor plant collections.
