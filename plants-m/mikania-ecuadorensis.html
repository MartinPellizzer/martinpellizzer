Mikania ecuadorensis

Mikania ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian Mikania, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain areas.
