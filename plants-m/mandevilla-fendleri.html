Mandevilla fendleri

Mandevilla fendleri, commonly known as the yellow mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with thick, waxy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of funnel-shaped, yellow-orange flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla fendleri is often used in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and trailing growth habit.
