Microseris aphantocarpha var. elegans

Microseris aphantocarpha var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant salsify, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the genus Microseris, which is part of the family Brassicaceae, also known as the mustard family. The plant features slender, hairy stems and basal leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in a loose raceme, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

The plant is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
