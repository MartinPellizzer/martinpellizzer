Munroidendron racemosum var. forbesii

Munroidendron racemosum var. forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in racemes, giving it a dramatic and showy appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark is smooth and may exude a reddish sap when wounded.
