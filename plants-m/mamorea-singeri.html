Mamorea singeri

Mamorea singeri, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant is evergreen and thrives in well-drained soil with indirect sunlight. Its botanical features include thick, waxy leaves that help retain moisture.

Mamorea singeri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
