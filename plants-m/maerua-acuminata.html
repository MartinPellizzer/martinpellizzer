Maerua acuminata

Maerua acuminata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved maerua, is a shrub or small tree native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

Maerua acuminata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
