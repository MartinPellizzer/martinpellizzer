Maxillaria semiscabra

Maxillaria semiscabra, commonly known as the rough-leaved orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features dark green, rough-textured leaves and produces fragrant, pink to purple flowers with a distinctive lip. Its flowers are often clustered in racemes and bloom during the summer months.

Maxillaria semiscabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, moist conditions.
