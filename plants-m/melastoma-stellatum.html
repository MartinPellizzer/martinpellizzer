Melastoma stellatum

Melastoma stellatum, commonly known as the starleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its small, star-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.

Melastoma stellatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
