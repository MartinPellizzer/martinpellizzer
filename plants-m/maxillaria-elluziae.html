Maxillaria elluziae

Maxillaria elluziae, commonly known as the Elluzia orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudo-bulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale pink to white in color. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria elluziae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
