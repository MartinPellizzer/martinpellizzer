Metula

Metula, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and yellow. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.
