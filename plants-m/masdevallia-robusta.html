Masdevallia robusta

Masdevallia robusta, commonly known as the robust masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and vibrant, often bi-colored flowers that range from purple to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in cool to moderate temperatures. Its intricate inflorescence features a tall, slender stem with clusters of showy blooms.

Masdevallia robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
