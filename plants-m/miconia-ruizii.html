Miconia ruizii

Miconia ruizii, commonly known as the golden miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, yellow-orange flowers. It typically grows as an understory plant in humid, shaded environments.
