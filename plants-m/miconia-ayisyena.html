Miconia ayisyena

Miconia ayisyena, commonly known as the West African miconia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Congo Basin. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Miconia ayisyena is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential ecological and medicinal properties.
