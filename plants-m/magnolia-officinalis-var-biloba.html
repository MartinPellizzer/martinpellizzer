Magnolia officinalis var. biloba

Magnolia officinalis var. biloba, commonly known as the Yulania biloba, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has smooth, greyish-brown bark and simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in Chinese medicine.

The variety is distinguished by its smaller, more delicate flowers compared to the typical Magnolia officinalis.
