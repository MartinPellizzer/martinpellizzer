Monstera gaudichaudii

Monstera gaudichaudii, commonly known as the giant monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and they often have a distinctive pattern of light green veins.
