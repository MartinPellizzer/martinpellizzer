Melastoma iliasii

Melastoma iliasii, commonly known as the Iliasii Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
