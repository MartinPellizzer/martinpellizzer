Myrcia neohernandezii

Myrcia neohernandezii, commonly known as the Neohernandezii Myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the understory of tropical forests.

Myrcia neohernandezii is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
