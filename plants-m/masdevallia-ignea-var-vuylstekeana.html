Masdevallia ignea var. vuylstekeana

Masdevallia ignea var. vuylstekeana, commonly known as the Flame Orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive, elongated lip. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts for its intense colors and unique floral structure.
