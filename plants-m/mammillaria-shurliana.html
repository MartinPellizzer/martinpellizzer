Mammillaria shurliana

Mammillaria shurliana, commonly known as the Shurliana nipple cactus, is a small, solitary cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, tuberculate ribs and white or pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
