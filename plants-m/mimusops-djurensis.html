Mimusops djurensis

Mimusops djurensis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that encase a single seed, which is typically dispersed by animals. Due to its ornamental value and hard, durable wood, it is sometimes cultivated for landscaping and timber purposes.
