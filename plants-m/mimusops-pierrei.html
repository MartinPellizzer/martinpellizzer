Mimusops pierrei

Mimusops pierrei, commonly known as the African star fruit tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe, with a star-shaped pattern on the surface. Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.

The species is also valued for its timber and medicinal properties in traditional practices.
