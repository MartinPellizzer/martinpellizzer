Manihot pittieri

Manihot pittieri, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowleaf, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its latex contains compounds that have been used traditionally for medicinal purposes.

However, it is important to note that the plant can be toxic if ingested in large quantities.
