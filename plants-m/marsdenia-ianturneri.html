Marsdenia ianturneri

Marsdenia ianturneri, commonly known as the Ianturneri Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
