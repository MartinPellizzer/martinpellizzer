Myrtus nivea

Myrtus nivea, commonly known as snow myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are narrow, leathery, and have a distinctive aromatic scent. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
