Mammillaria colonensis

Mammillaria colonensis, commonly known as the Mexican hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, globular tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
