Maranta nitens

Maranta nitens, commonly known as the silver leaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with silver and green patterns. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in indoor settings.

Maranta nitens thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.
