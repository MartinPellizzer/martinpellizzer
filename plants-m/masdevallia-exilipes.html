Masdevallia exilipes

Masdevallia exilipes, commonly known as the slender masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has a short, creeping rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to the humid, shaded environments it inhabits.

Masdevallia exilipes is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
