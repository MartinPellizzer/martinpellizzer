Mentha viridis var. macrostemma

Mentha viridis var. macrostemma, commonly known as narrow-leaf mint, is a variant of the mint genus Mentha.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and aromatic, fragrant foliage. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas or along stream banks. Its flowers are small, greenish-white, and clustered in spikes, which are often hidden among the leaves.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
