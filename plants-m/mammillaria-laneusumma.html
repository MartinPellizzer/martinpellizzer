Mammillaria laneusumma

Mammillaria laneusumma, commonly known as the Lane's milk barrel cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
