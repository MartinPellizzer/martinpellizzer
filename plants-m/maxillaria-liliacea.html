Maxillaria liliacea

Maxillaria liliacea, commonly known as the Lily Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that resemble lilies, with white petals and a yellow lip. The plant typically grows as a pseudobulbous orchid, with upright stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria liliacea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
