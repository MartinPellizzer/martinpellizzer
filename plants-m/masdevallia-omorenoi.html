Masdevallia omorenoi

Masdevallia omorenoi, commonly known as the Omoreno's masdevallia, is a rare epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces intricate, spurred orchid blossoms that attract specific pollinators. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, often growing on trees or rocks in its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
