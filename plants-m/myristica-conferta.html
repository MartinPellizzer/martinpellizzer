Myristica conferta

Myristica conferta, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is closely related to the nutmeg and mace plants. The tree produces an aromatic fruit that contains the seeds used to make nutmeg and mace. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth surface with a distinctive dark green color.

The plant is valued not only for its spices but also for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
