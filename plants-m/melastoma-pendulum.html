Melastoma pendulum

Melastoma pendulum, commonly known as the hanging melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Melastoma pendulum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
