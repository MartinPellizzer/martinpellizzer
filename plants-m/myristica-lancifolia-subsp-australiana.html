Myristica lancifolia subsp. australiana

Myristica lancifolia subsp.

australiana, commonly known as the Australian nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to northern Australia and parts of New Guinea. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, aromatic fruits.

The plant produces a unique seed with a rich, nutmeg-like aroma, which is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different morphology compared to other Myristica species.
