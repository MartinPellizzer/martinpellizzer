Monstera momoi

Monstera momoi, commonly known as the pink monstera, is a rare and striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of Central America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that have a unique pinkish hue on the underside, which becomes more pronounced as the plant matures. Its foliage is often variegated with white and yellow markings, making it a highly sought-after ornamental plant.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is less commonly cultivated than other monstera species.
