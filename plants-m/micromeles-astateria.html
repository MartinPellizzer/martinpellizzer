Micromeles astateria

Micromeles astateria, commonly known as the narrowleaf astera, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that resemble daisies, with white to pale yellow petals and dark centers. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a silvery or woolly texture.

Micromeles astateria is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.
