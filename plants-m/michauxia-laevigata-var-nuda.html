Michauxia laevigata var. nuda

Michauxia laevigata var. nuda, commonly known as the naked Michauxia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
