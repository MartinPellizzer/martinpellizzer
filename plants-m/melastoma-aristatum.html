Melastoma aristatum

Melastoma aristatum, commonly known as the mountain melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountainous forests. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually pink or white, with five petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
