Modecca oblonga

Modecca oblonga, commonly known as the oblong leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale green in color. Modecca oblonga thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
