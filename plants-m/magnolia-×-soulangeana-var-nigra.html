Magnolia × soulangeana var. nigra

Magnolia × soulangeana var. nigra, commonly known as the black magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dark, almost black bark and large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad and oval, with a glossy green surface. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
