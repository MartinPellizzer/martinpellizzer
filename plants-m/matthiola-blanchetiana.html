Matthiola blanchetiana

Matthiola blanchetiana, commonly known as the Mediterranean stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

Matthiola blanchetiana prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
