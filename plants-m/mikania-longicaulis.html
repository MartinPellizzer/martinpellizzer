Mikania longicaulis

Mikania longicaulis, commonly known as the giant sensitiveplant, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs, and it can spread quickly through both seeds and vegetative propagation.

Mikania longicaulis is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as fields and along stream banks.
