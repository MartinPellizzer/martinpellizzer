Mondo cernuum

Mondo cernuum, commonly known as the nodding bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, often with a greenish hue. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves that are opposite on the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mondo cernuum is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
