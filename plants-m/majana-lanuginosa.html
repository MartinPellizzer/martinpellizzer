Majana lanuginosa

Majana lanuginosa, commonly known as the woolly majana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

Majana lanuginosa is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
