Marina minor

Marina minor, commonly known as lesser periwinkle, is a low-growing evergreen shrub native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in pairs along the stems. The plant produces delicate blue or violet flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to salty air and sandy soils, making it a hardy plant in maritime environments.

Marina minor is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in challenging conditions.
