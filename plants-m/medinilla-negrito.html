Medinilla negrito

Medinilla negrito, commonly known as black medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are often clustered in inflorescences. The plant has glossy, ovate leaves that are typically dark green in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla negrito is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display.
