Marsdenia dictyophylla

Marsdenia dictyophylla, commonly known as the lace-leaf Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, finely divided, feather-like leaves that give it a delicate, lace-like appearance. It produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne on racemes.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage and attractive blooms.
