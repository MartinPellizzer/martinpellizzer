Moeroris tukuyuana

Moeroris tukuyuana, commonly known as the Tukuyuana orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and requires cool, humid conditions to thrive. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in the steep, mountainous terrain where it is found.

Due to habitat loss and climate change, Moeroris tukuyuana is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
