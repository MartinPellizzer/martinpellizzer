Myristica verruculosa

Myristica verruculosa, commonly known as the rough nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has dark green, glossy leaves. Its seeds, known as nutmeg, are enclosed in a reddish, fleshy aril.

The tree is valued for its essential oils and has been used traditionally in various medicinal and culinary applications.
