Mikania funkiae

Mikania funkiae, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central and South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. Mikania funkiae is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as stream banks and forest edges.
