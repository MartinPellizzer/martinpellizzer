Macrorungia pubinervia

Macrorungia pubinervia, commonly known as the hairy nerve plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves with prominent venation patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology makes it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
