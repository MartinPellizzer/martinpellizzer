Maxillaria friedrichsthalii

Maxillaria friedrichsthalii, commonly known as the Friedrichsthal's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Mexico.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendant, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil. It is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.

Its blooming period typically occurs during the spring and early summer.
