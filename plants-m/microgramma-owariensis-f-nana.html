Microgramma owariensis f. nana

Microgramma owariensis f. nana, commonly known as the dwarf fern, is a compact variety of the microgramma genus.

It is a member of the Davalliaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Japan. The plant features small, leathery, evergreen fronds that are typically less than 10 cm in length. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and rock gardens.
