Mammillaria hermosana

Mammillaria hermosana, commonly known as the beautiful mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed structure. The plant typically features yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its foliage is covered in small, white or pale blue tubercles, which give it a distinctive appearance.

Mammillaria hermosana is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
