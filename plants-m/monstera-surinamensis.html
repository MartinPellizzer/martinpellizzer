Monstera surinamensis

Monstera surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam monstera, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are often a bright green color with a smooth texture.

Monstera surinamensis thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.
