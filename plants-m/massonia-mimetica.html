Massonia mimetica

Massonia mimetica, commonly known as the mimic massonia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, clustered leaves that help it store water. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage.

Massonia mimetica is well adapted to arid environments, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
