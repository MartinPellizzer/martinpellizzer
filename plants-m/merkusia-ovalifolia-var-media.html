Merkusia ovalifolia var. media

Merkusia ovalifolia var. media, commonly known as the dwarf oval-leaf merkusia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Due to its ornamental value and hardiness, it is a popular choice for gardeners seeking a low-maintenance, decorative shrub.
