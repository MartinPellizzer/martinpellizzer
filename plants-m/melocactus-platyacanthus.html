Melocactus platyacanthus

Melocactus platyacanthus, commonly known as the flat-spined melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular, ribbed stem and prominent, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

The cactus has a slow growth rate and is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance.
