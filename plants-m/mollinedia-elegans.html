Mollinedia elegans

Mollinedia elegans, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to South America.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and low maintenance requirements contribute to its appeal among plant enthusiasts.
