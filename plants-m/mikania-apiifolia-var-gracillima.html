Mikania apiifolia var. gracillima

Mikania apiifolia var. gracillima, commonly known as the slender leaf milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veining pattern. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its fibrous roots and rapid growth make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
