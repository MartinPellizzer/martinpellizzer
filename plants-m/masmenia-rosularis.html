Masmenia rosularis

Masmenia rosularis, commonly known as the Rosular Rose.

It is a succulent plant native to South Africa, characterized by its compact growth habit and fleshy, rounded leaves. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments.

Masmenia rosularis is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
