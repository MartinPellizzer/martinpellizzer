Maxillaria Lipinskae

Maxillaria lipinskae is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia. This unique species is named after the Polish botanist Jan Lipinski, who contributed significantly to the study of orchids. It is known for its vibrant purple and yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant thrives in humid environments with high altitudes and partial shade. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered a conservation priority.
