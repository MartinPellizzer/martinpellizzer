Mussaenda breviloba

Mussaenda breviloba, commonly known as the small-leaved Mussaenda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Mussaenda breviloba is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
