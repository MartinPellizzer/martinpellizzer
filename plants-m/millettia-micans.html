Millettia micans

Millettia micans, commonly known as the Indian velvet tree, is a deciduous leguminous shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Millettia micans is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
