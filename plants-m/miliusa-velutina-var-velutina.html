Miliusa velutina var. velutina

Miliusa velutina var. velutina, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by edible, kidney-shaped fruits with a sweet and sour flavor. The fruit's pericarp is covered with a fine, velvety texture, giving the plant its common name.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of food in tropical regions.
