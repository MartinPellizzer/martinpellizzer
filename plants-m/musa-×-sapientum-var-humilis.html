Musa × sapientum var. humilis

Musa × sapientum var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf banana.

This variety is a cultivated form of the banana plant, characterized by its shorter stature compared to typical banana plants. It features elongated, fleshy berries that are typically smaller and more clustered. The plant produces edible fruit with a sweet, soft texture and a mild flavor.

It is often grown in home gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive appearance.
