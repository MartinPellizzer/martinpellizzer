Memecylon anomalum

Memecylon anomalum, commonly known as the red-flowered memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Memecylon anomalum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and understorey habitats.
