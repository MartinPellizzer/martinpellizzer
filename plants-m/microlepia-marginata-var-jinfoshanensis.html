Microlepia marginata var. jinfoshanensis

Microlepia marginata var. jinfoshanensis, commonly known as the Jinfoshan fern, is a variant of the microlepia genus native to China.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, fern-like fronds that are typically bipinnate. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its leaves are usually dark green with a distinctive margin that is often curled or rolled.

This variant is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.
