Medicago tuberculata f. dextrorsa

Medicago tuberculata f. dextrorsa, commonly known as the curled medic, is a variant of the common medick.

It is a low-growing perennial plant characterized by its distinctive curled leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as roadsides and fields. Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves with a unique upward curl and small, pea-like flowers.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to fix nitrogen.
