Mecodium whitei

Mecodium whitei, commonly known as the white mecodium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.

Mecodium whitei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
