Melodorum olivaceum

Melodorum olivaceum, commonly known as the olive-leaved melodia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melodaceae.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight olive hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia.

Melodorum olivaceum prefers moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
