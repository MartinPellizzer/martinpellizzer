Melicope benguetensis

Melicope benguetensis, commonly known as the Benguet melicope, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically the northern regions of Luzon. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, round fruits.

The plant is often found in lowland to montane forests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
