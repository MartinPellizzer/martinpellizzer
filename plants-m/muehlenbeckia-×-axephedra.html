Muehlenbeckia × axephedra

Muehlenbeckia × axephedra, commonly known as silver mat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with silver-gray foliage due to a layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value comes from its striking foliage rather than its flowers.
