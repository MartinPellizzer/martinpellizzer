Macropsychanthus mindanaensis

Macropsychanthus mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao psychanthus, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its spiny stems and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing features such as succulent leaves and reduced foliage. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the morning and closing by midday.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
