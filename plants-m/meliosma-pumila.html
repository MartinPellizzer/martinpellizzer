Meliosma pumila

Meliosma pumila, commonly known as the smooth meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by winged fruits that aid in seed dispersal.

Meliosma pumila thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded, woodland environments.
