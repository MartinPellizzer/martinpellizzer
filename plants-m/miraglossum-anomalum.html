Miraglossum anomalum

Miraglossum anomalum, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, almost translucent leaves and striking, orchid-like flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. This plant thrives in shaded, humid environments, often growing on tree bark or in dense undergrowth. Its delicate structure makes it highly sensitive to environmental changes, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
