Macrostigma

Macrostigma, commonly known as the "garden spider orchid," is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a prominent, spurred lip. The plant is native to various regions in Asia, including China, Japan, and the Himalayas. Its leaves are usually grass-like and arranged in a basal rosette.

Macrostigma thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.
