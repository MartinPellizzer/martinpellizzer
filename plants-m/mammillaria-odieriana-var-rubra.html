Mammillaria odieriana var. rubra

Mammillaria odieriana var. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, rounded stems covered in white to pale green areoles, which bear clusters of short, pale blue or white radial spines. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
