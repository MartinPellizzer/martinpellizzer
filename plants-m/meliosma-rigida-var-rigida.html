Meliosma rigida var. rigida

Meliosma rigida var. rigida, commonly known as the rigid meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves with a leathery texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
