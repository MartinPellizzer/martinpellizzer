Maranta niveiflora

Maranta niveiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their vibrant foliage. The plant is characterized by its striking white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a contrasting green or red bract. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped and can be a deep green or variegated with silver markings.

Maranta niveiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
