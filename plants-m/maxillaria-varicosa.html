Maxillaria varicosa

Maxillaria varicosa, commonly known as the Variable Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with each inflorescence bearing several flowers arranged in a raceme. Its leaves are usually dark green, elliptical, and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Maxillaria varicosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
