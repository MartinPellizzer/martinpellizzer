Magnolia figo var. figo

Magnolia figo var. figo, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the evergreen magnolia, is a species native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has glossy, dark green, leathery leaves that remain on the tree throughout the year. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often developing shallow fissures with age.

It thrives in partial to full shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
