Miconia tocohirta

Miconia tocohirta, commonly known as the Tocantins miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
