Mikania allartii

Mikania allartii, commonly known as the allartii vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and slender, twining stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by small, oval-shaped fruits. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped with serrated edges, and the plant is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Mikania allartii is known for its ability to spread quickly, making it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential weed in certain regions.
