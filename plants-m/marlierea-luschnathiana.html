Marlierea luschnathiana

Marlierea luschnathiana, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshade plants. The plant features showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, yellowish seeds.
