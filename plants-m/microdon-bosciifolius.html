Microdon bosciifolius

Microdon bosciifolius, commonly known as the Bosciifolius fern, is a rare and delicate species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a lacy appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in dense tropical forests. Its reproductive structures are small and inconspicuous, typical of many ferns.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
