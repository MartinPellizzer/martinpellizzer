Magnolia axilliflora var. alba

Magnolia axilliflora var. alba, commonly known as the white magnolia, is a flowering plant native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its showy, white, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
