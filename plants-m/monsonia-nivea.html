Monsonia nivea

Monsonia nivea, commonly known as the snow monsonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact, low-growing habit with hairy leaves that help reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to xeriscapes.
