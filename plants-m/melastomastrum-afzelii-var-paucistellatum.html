Melastomastrum afzelii var. paucistellatum

Melastomastrum afzelii var. paucistellatum, commonly known as the small-fruited melastoma, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and rocky slopes.
