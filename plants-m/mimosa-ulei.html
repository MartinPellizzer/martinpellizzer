Mimosa ulei

Mimosa ulei, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for dry, rocky gardens.

Mimosa ulei is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which can improve soil fertility.
