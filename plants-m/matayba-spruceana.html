Matayba spruceana

Matayba spruceana, commonly known as the Mexican spruce, is a rare and unique conifer native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its conical shape and dense, dark green foliage that provides a striking visual contrast against its rugged habitat. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Matayba spruceana is adapted to high-altitude environments, thriving in cool, moist climates with well-drained soils.
