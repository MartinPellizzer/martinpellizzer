Mappa stricta

Mappa stricta, commonly known as the rigid map plant, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features woody stems that are typically straight and upright, giving it its common name. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences and are often pink or purple in color.

Mappa stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
