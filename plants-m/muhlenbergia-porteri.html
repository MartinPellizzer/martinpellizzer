Muhlenbergia porteri

Muhlenbergia porteri, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silvery-blue foliage and compact growth habit, making it an attractive ornamental plant. The plant produces delicate, feather-like inflorescences that add visual interest in the late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.

Muhlenbergia porteri is drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping, making it a popular choice in arid landscapes.
