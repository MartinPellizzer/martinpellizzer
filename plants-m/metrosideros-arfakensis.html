Metrosideros arfakensis

Metrosideros arfakensis, commonly known as the Arfakensis flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often covering the entire tree during the flowering season. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. Its leaves are typically small, opposite, and have a smooth texture.

The Arfakensis flame tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and public spaces.
