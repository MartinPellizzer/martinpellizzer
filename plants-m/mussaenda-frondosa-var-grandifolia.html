Mussaenda frondosa var. grandifolia

Mussaenda frondosa var. grandifolia, commonly known as the large-flowered Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright yellow, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The flowers are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant color and unique shape.
