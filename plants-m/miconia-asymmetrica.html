Miconia asymmetrica

Miconia asymmetrica, commonly known as the asymmetric miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy capsule containing numerous seeds.

Miconia asymmetrica is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy blooms.
