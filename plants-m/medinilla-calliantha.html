Medinilla calliantha

Medinilla calliantha, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet tangerine shrub, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, orange-colored fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla calliantha is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and birds.
