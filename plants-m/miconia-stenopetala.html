Miconia stenopetala

Miconia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy leaves and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
