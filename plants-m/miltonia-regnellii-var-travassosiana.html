Miltonia regnellii var. travassosiana

Miltonia regnellii var. travassosiana, commonly known as the Travassos's Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous flowers, and its leaves are broad and elliptical. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
