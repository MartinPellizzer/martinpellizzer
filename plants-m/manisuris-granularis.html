Manisuris granularis

Manisuris granularis, commonly known as the granular manisuris, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow or green. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy leaves that are often covered in fine, granular texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
