Mnesithea laevis var. laevis

Mnesithea laevis var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth mnesithea, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The leaves are typically smooth and lance-shaped, with a glossy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
