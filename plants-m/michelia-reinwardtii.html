Michelia reinwardtii

Michelia reinwardtii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. Michelia reinwardtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
