Mammillaria confinis

Mammillaria confinis, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and has 12 to 20 ribs. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in the spring.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
