Mirabilis californica

Mirabilis californica, commonly known as the California morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that open in the early morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with full sun exposure. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to dry, coastal climates.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a popular choice for gardens and restoration projects.
