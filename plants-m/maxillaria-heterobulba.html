Maxillaria heterobulba

Maxillaria heterobulba, commonly known as the "Pineapple Orchid," is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often resembling a pineapple. It thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria heterobulba is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
