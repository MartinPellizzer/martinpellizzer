Medicago timeroyi

Medicago timeroyi, commonly known as the Turkish clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive trifoliate shape, and small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes. The plant is characterized by its deep taproot system, which allows it to thrive in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along roadsides.

Medicago timeroyi is valued for its forage qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
