Marsdenia linearis var. latifolia

Marsdenia linearis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
