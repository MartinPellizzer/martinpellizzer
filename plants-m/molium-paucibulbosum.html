Molium paucibulbosum

Molium paucibulbosum, commonly known as the slender molium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small, annual herb that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It is characterized by its low-growing habit and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in some areas.
