Masdevallia molossoides

Masdevallia molossoides, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions with good air circulation. Its leaves are usually dark green and succulent, adapted to store water in its native arid environment.

Masdevallia molossoides is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
