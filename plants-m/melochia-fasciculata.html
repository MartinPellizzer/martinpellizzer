Melochia fasciculata

Melochia fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered melochia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
