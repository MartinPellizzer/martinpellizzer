Mammillaria dioica subsp. estebanensis

Mammillaria dioica subsp.

estebanensis, commonly known as the Estebanensis prickly pear, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico. It features a compact, globular stem with prominent tubercles and spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil to thrive.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
