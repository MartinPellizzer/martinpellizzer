Monarda chinensis

Monarda chinensis, commonly known as Chinese horsemint, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically arranged in spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Monarda chinensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
