Manisuris speciosa

Manisuris speciosa, commonly known as the showy manisuris, is a striking perennial plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red, often with intricate patterns on the petals. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, with lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
