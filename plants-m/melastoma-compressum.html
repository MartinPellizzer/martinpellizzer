Melastoma compressum

Melastoma compressum, commonly known as the broadleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a pink or red coloration.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its propagation in natural habitats.
