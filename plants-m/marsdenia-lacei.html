Marsdenia lacei

Marsdenia lacei, commonly known as the lacey Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and typically have a yellowish or greenish hue.

Marsdenia lacei is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
