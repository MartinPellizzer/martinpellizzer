Mammillaria radians var. scolymoides

Mammillaria radians var. scolymoides, commonly known as the Scolymoides mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular or slightly elongated stem with prominent, white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to reddish flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its attractive appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
