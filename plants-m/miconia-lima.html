Miconia lima

Miconia lima, commonly known as the Lima miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are typically red or yellow when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
