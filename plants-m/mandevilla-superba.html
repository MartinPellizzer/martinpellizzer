Mandevilla superba

Mandevilla superba, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to autumn. Its heart-shaped leaves are glossy and provide a striking contrast to the colorful flowers.

Mandevilla superba thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
