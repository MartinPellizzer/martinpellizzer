Musteron glabrum

Musteron glabrum, commonly known as the smooth musteron, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. The plant features slender, upright stems that are covered in smooth, hairless leaves. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Musteron glabrum is notable for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies for various ailments.
