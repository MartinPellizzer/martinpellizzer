Micranthes stellaris subsp. stellaris

Micranthes stellaris subsp.

stellaris, commonly known as the starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in early spring.

The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are often hairy to the touch. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade. Micranthes stellaris subsp.

stellaris is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
