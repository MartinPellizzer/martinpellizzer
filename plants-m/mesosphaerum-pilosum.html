Mesosphaerum pilosum

Mesosphaerum pilosum, commonly known as the silky mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
