Melastoma uniflorum

Melastoma uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flower melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.

Melastoma uniflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream sides.
