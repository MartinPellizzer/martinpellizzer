Mammillaria spinosissima f. isabellina

Mammillaria spinosissima f. isabellina, commonly known as the Isabellina mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a rounded, globular shape.

It features pale yellow to beige tubercles that are covered in white to pale pink spines. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is native to Mexico and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and compact growth habit, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
