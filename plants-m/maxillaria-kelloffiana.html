Maxillaria kelloffiana

Maxillaria kelloffiana, commonly known as the Kelloff's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange with darker markings, and they bloom during the summer months.

Maxillaria kelloffiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
