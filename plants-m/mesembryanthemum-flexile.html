Mesembryanthemum flexile

Mesembryanthemum flexile, commonly known as the fleshy mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its adaptive features for arid environments. The plant has fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping and succulent collections.
