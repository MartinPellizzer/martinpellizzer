Mitrephora excelsa

Mitrephora excelsa, commonly known as the giant mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, clustered fruits that are edible and used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
