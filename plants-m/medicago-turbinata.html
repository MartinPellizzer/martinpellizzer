Medicago turbinata

Medicago turbinata, commonly known as Turkey pea, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to southern Europe and western Asia but has been introduced to various parts of the world. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, turban-shaped seed pods. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, contributing to its ornamental value.

Medicago turbinata is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
