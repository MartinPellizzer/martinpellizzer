Medicago falcata var. major

Medicago falcata var. major, commonly known as the large sickle vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a robust root system that helps prevent soil erosion. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and disturbed areas.

This species is valued for its forage quality and ecological benefits in restoration projects.
