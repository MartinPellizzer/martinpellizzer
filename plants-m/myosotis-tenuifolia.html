Myosotis tenuifolia

Myosotis tenuifolia, commonly known as slender forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, often in clusters at the ends of its slender stems. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or meadows.

Myosotis tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
