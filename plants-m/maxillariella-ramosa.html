Maxillariella ramosa

Maxillariella ramosa, commonly known as the branched orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its inflorescence consists of a raceme of short, clustered flowers. Maxillariella ramosa prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a suitable choice for indoor cultivation.

It is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate watering and good air circulation to thrive.
