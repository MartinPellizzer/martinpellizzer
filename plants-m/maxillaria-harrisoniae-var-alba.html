Maxillaria harrisoniae var. alba

Maxillaria harrisoniae var. alba, commonly known as the White Harrison's Maxillaria, is a captivating orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

This variant is distinguished by its striking white flowers, which contrast beautifully with its green foliage. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in horticulture. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent moisture to thrive, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
