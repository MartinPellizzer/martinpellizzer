Murraya japonensis

Murraya japonensis, commonly known as the Japanese murraya, is a small evergreen shrub native to East Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Murraya japonensis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
