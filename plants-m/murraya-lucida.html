Murraya lucida

Murraya lucida, commonly known as the flame tree or Indian flame tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round, orange-red fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Murraya lucida is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
