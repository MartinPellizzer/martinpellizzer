Minuartia recurva var. thevenaei

Minuartia recurva var. thevenaei, commonly known as the Alpine sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to alpine and arctic regions.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color and are adapted to withstand cold and harsh environmental conditions. The flowers are white or pale pink, with five petals, and bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

This variety is notable for its resilience and is a common species in high-altitude ecosystems.
