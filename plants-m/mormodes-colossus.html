Mormodes colossus

Mormodes colossus, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the forests of Borneo and Sumatra. This plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The inflorescence consists of a single, elongated spike bearing numerous blooms, often in shades of pink and white.

Its unique morphology and vibrant colors make it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticultural displays.
