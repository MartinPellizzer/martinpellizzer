Marsdenia breviramosa

Marsdenia breviramosa, commonly known as the short-stemmed Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, China, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive crown of lobes at the base.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical forests, growing as a climbing or trailing plant.
