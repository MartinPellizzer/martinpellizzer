Melorima

Melorima, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stems rising from the water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate. Melorima is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, often representing purity and enlightenment.

Botanically, it belongs to the Nelumbonaceae family and is closely related to water lilies.
