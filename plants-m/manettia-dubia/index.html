Manettia Dubia

Manettia dubia is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, known for its diverse and often ornamental species. This plant is characterized by its small, clustered white flowers and glossy, heart-shaped leaves. Due to its limited habitat and threats from deforestation, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical species.
