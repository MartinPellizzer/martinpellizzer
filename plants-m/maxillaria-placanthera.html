Maxillaria placanthera

Maxillaria placanthera, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and yellow. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often clustering around the base of the pseudobulbs.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
