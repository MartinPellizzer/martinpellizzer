Myosotis uncata

Myosotis uncata, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Myosotis uncata has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a perennial species that is valued for its ornamental beauty and its ability to thrive in challenging ecological conditions.
