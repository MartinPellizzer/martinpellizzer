Mirbelia viminalis

Mirbelia viminalis, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Mirbelia viminalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
