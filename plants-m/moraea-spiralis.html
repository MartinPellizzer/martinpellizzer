Moraea spiralis

Moraea spiralis, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spiral-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base.

Moraea spiralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
