Malouetia grandiflora

Malouetia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered malouetia, is a tropical shrub native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
