Medicago hispida var. tricycla

Medicago hispida var. tricycla, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, each consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in racemes, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Goat's rue is known for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
