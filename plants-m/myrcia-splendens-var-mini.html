Myrcia splendens var. mini

Myrcia splendens var. mini, commonly known as the miniature flame tree, is a compact shrub or small tree native to the humid regions of South America.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape, with a slightly serrated margin. The plant produces clusters of small, bright orange-red flowers that bloom in the summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant foliage and showy floral display, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sun for optimal growth.
