Manilkara sulcata var. sacleuxii

Manilkara sulcata var. sacleuxii, commonly known as the Sacleux's manilkara, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, green fruits that turn yellow when ripe. Its latex is milky and viscous, containing compounds used in traditional medicine and industry.
