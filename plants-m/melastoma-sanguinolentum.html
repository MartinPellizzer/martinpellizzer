Melastoma sanguinolentum

Melastoma sanguinolentum, commonly known as the blood-red melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
