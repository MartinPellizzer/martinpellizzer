Mammillaria tlalocii

Mammillaria tlalocii, commonly known as the Mexican fairy duster, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
