Micropholis Madeirensis

Micropholis madeirensis is a rare and unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Madagascar. It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes well-known trees like cinnamon and avocado. This plant is characterized by its small size and distinctive leaf structure, making it an interesting subject for botanical study. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it faces significant conservation challenges.
