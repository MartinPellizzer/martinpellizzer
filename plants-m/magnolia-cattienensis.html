Magnolia cattienensis

Magnolia cattienensis, commonly known as the Cattien magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant floral display.
