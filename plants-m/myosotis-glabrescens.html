Myosotis glabrescens

Myosotis glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, surrounded by green, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers have five petals and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

The species is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and is part of the Boraginaceae family.
