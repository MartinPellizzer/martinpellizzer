Mikania filgueirasii

Mikania filgueirasii, commonly known as the Brazilian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces fluffy, white seeds that are dispersed by the wind, aiding in its spread across various habitats. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
