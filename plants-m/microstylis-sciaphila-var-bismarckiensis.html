Microstylis sciaphila var. bismarckiensis

Microstylis sciaphila var. bismarckiensis, commonly known as the Bismarck mistflower, is a rare and delicate herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its unique, spatulate leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its varietal name, *bismarckiensis*, honors its native region, reflecting its geographic origin.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of great interest to botanists and conservationists.
