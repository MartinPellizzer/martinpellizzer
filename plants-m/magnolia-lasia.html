Magnolia lasia

Magnolia lasia, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Magnolia lasia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
