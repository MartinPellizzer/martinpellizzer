Myosotis perpusilla

Myosotis perpusilla, commonly known as the dwarf forget-me-not, is a small flowering plant native to southern South America.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few centimeters, making it a compact and ornamental species. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Myosotis perpusilla thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.
