Mesembryanthemum aureum

Mesembryanthemum aureum, commonly known as the golden mesemb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, typical of xerophytic adaptations. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
