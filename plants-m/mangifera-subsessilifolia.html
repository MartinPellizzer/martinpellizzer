Mangifera subsessilifolia

Mangifera subsessilifolia, commonly known as the small-leaf mango, is a species of flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and yellow-orange fruit. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its flowers are small and clustered, and the fruit is generally less than 10 centimeters in diameter.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
