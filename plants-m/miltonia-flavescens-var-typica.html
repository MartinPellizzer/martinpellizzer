Miltonia flavescens var. typica

Miltonia flavescens var. typica, commonly known as the yellow Miltonia, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features vibrant, showy flowers with a yellowish-orange coloration and red markings. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, warm environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a fan shape.

This orchid thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting mix for optimal growth.
