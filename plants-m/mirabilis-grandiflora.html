Mirabilis grandiflora

Mirabilis grandiflora, commonly known as the angel's trumpets, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its foliage is broad and lance-shaped, with a smooth, glossy texture.

Mirabilis grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative plant.
