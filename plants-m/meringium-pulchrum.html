Meringium pulchrum

Meringium pulchrum, commonly known as the beautiful meringia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth, waxy texture.

Meringium pulchrum is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and botanical collections.
