Mikania niederleinii

Mikania niederleinii, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in moist, tropical environments.

Mikania niederleinii is often found in shaded areas near streams or in forest understories.
