Melastoma banksii

Melastoma banksii, commonly known as the small-leaved melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

Melastoma banksii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
