Myristica mollissima

Myristica mollissima, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg (Myristica fragrans). The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and large, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries containing the aromatic seeds used as spices.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its essential oils and medicinal properties.
