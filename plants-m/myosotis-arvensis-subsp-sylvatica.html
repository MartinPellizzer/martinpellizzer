Myosotis arvensis subsp. sylvatica

Myosotis arvensis subsp.

sylvatica, commonly known as forest forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender stems and heart-shaped leaves, with clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. Its flowers are typically 5-lobed and have a distinctive blue or purple coloration.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
