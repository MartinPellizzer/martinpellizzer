Microtatorchis schlechteri var. productilis

Microtatorchis schlechteri var. productilis, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is a flowering species native to tropical regions of Africa. It features dense, velvety foliage and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
