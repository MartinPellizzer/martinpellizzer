Menispermum diversifolium var. molle

Menispermum diversifolium var. molle, commonly known as the soft-leaved arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces a tuberous root that is sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both horticulture and ethnobotany.
