Melianthus minor

Melianthus minor, commonly known as the dwarf melianthus, is a small shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, pale green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Melianthus minor is relatively drought-tolerant and prefers full sun to partial shade.
