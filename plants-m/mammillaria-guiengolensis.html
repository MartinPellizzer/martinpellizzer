Mammillaria guiengolensis

Mammillaria guiengolensis, commonly known as the Guaiengolensis Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its compact, globular shape with dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers, which are usually yellow or orange, bloom in late spring or early summer.

This cactus is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.
