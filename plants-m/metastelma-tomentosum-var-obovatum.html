Metastelma tomentosum var. obovatum

Metastelma tomentosum var. obovatum, commonly known as the woolly metastelma, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly covering on its stems and leaves, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and ornamental flowers make it a valuable addition to both horticultural collections and natural habitats.
