Moraea grandis

Moraea grandis, commonly known as the giant moraea, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The flowers are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive central boss and reflexed petals. This species thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or grassland habitats.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable climates.
