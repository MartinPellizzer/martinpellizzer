Mapouria decumbens

Mapouria decumbens, commonly known as the creeping mapouria, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along riverbanks.
