Melastoma tibouchina

Melastoma tibouchina, commonly known as the tropical beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple to red berries that appear in clusters, which are a key feature of its botanical appearance. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with smooth edges and a slightly glossy surface. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season.

Melastoma tibouchina is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking fruit and adaptability to various soil types.
