Microsisymbrium axillare

Microsisymbrium axillare, commonly known as the axillary microsymbrium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. Its root system is well adapted to nutrient-poor soils, and it plays a role in nitrogen fixation through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia.
