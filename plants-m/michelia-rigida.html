Michelia rigida

Michelia rigida, commonly known as the rigid michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Michelia rigida is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in horticulture for its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.
