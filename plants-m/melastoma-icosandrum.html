Melastoma icosandrum

Melastoma icosandrum, commonly known as the red-stemmed melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture on the underside. Its flowers are typically bright red or pink, with five petals and prominent stamens.

Melastoma icosandrum is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
