Monardella macrantha subsp. eumacrantha

Monardella macrantha subsp.

eumacrantha, commonly known as the showy monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs.

The plant is characterized by its tall, erect stems and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

This subspecies is often found in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky slopes.
