Masdevallia gracilenta

Masdevallia gracilenta, commonly known as the slender masdevallia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, often pendulous flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its striking appearance and unique growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
