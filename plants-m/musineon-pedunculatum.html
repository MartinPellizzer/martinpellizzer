Musineon pedunculatum

Musineon pedunculatum, commonly known as the slender musineon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Musineonaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, particularly in the Andean foothills. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species in its native habitat.
