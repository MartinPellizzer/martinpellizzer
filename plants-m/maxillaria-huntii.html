Maxillaria huntii

Maxillaria huntii, commonly known as the Hunt's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers bloom during the summer months and are known for their intricate, spurred petals.

Maxillaria huntii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
