Marah

Marah, commonly known as the bitter melon, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other vegetables like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant produces long, pointed fruits that are typically green with a bitter taste. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Marah is valued not only for its edible fruits but also for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
