Melastoma cryptodon

Melastoma cryptodon, commonly known as the narrow-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its opposite, lanceolate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Melastoma cryptodon is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
