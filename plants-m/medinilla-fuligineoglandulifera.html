Medinilla fuligineoglandulifera

Medinilla fuligineoglandulifera, commonly known as the sooty glandular medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark, sooty leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has a woody stem with glandular structures that give it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its unique botanical features make it a subject of interest in botanical studies and horticulture.
