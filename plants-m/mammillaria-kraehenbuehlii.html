Mammillaria kraehenbuehlii

Mammillaria kraehenbuehlii, commonly known as the Kraehenbuehli's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and white to yellow spines. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
