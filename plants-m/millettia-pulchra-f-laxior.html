Millettia pulchra f. laxior

Millettia pulchra f. laxior, commonly known as the slender millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its climbing habit and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which are often followed by elongated, bean-like pods.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
