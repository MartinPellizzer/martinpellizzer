Melastoma capitatum

Melastoma capitatum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, red or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
