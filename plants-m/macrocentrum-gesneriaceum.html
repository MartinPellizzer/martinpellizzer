Macrocentrum gesneriaceum

Macrocentrum gesneriaceum, commonly known as the silverleaf gloxinia, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants with showy flowers. The plant is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of tubular, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its attractive appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
