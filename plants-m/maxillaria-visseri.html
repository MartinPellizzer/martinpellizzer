Maxillaria visseri

Maxillaria visseri, commonly known as the Visser's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green leaves and pseudobulbs, which are typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are often pale yellow with red or brown markings, adding to its ornamental value.

Maxillaria visseri thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
