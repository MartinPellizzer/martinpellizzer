Mammillaria tropica

Mammillaria tropica, commonly known as the tropical hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of 5 to 10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded form. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the ribs.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
