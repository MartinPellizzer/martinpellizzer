Mammillaria roseana

Mammillaria roseana, commonly known as the roseate mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, globular shape and pale pink to rose-colored spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, blooming in spring or early summer.

Mammillaria roseana is a popular choice for indoor cactus collections due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
