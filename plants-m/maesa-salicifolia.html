Maesa salicifolia

Maesa salicifolia, commonly known as the willow-leaf maesa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, China, and Southeast Asia. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
