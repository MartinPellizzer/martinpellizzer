Monardella odoratissima var. discolor

Monardella odoratissima var. discolor, commonly known as the pink monkeyflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to California.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range from pink to white, often with a contrasting color on the lower lip. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It plays an important role in supporting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
