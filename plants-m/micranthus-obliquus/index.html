Micranthus Obliquus

Micranthus obliquus is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Mexico. It is known for its unique, small flowers that bloom in shades of blue and purple. This species thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires minimal water. Its delicate appearance contrasts with its hardy nature, making it a favorite among botanists. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
