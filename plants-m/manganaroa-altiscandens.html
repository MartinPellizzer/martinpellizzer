Manganaroa altiscandens

Manganaroa altiscandens, commonly known as the silverleaf fern, is a tropical plant characterized by its delicate, arching fronds.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. The plant features a rhizomatous root system that allows it to spread and colonize shaded environments. Its leaves are typically bipinnate, with a silvery sheen due to a dense covering of fine hairs.

Manganaroa altiscandens thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
