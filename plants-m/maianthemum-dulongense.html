Maianthemum dulongense

Maianthemum dulongense, commonly known as the dwarf false lily of the valley, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Convallariaceae and is characterized by its small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early summer.
