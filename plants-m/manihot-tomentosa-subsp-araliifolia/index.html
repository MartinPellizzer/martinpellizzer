Manihot Tomentosa Subsp. Araliifolia

Manihot tomentosa subsp. araliifolia is a unique variant of the manioc plant known for its distinctive foliage. This subspecies features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It is native to tropical regions of South America and thrives in humid, shaded environments. While not commonly cultivated for food, it is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens. Its adaptability and aesthetic qualities make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
