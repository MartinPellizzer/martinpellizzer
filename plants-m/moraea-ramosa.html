Moraea ramosa

Moraea ramosa, commonly known as the branched moraea, is a species of flowering plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. Moraea ramosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
