Melastomastrum porteresii

Melastomastrum porteresii, commonly known as the Porteresia melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

Melastomastrum porteresii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
