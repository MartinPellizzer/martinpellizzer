Meioperis Multiflora

Meioperis multiflora is a rare and ancient plant species native to the mountainous regions of southern China. It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is known for its unique floral structure and medicinal properties. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in shaded forest understories. Its leaves and flowers have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries. Due to habitat loss and overharvesting, Meioperis multiflora is now critically endangered and requires urgent conservation efforts.
