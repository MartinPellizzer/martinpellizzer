Maesa purpureohirsuta

Maesa purpureohirsuta, commonly known as the purple-hairy maesa, is a flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it prefers well-drained, acidic soils.
