Malephora pienaarii

Malephora pienaarii, commonly known as the Pienaar's malephora, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescences that bear small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often near water sources. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
