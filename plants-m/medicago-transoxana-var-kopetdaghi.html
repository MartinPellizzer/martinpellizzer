Medicago transoxana var. kopetdaghi

Medicago transoxana var. kopetdaghi, commonly known as Kopetdagh alfalfa, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, typical of the Fabaceae family. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in nutrient-poor soils.

This variety is often used in forage and soil stabilization due to its hardy nature.
