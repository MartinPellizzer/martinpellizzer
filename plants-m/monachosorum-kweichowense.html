Monachosorum kweichowense

Monachosorum kweichowense, commonly known as the Kweichow orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant blooms with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in mountainous regions with rich, well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually a mix of white and purple, with a unique, lip-like structure that aids in pollination.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to survive.
