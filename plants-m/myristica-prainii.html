Myristica prainii

Myristica prainii, commonly known as the Prain's nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and dark red, fleshy berries that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The tree is valued for its medicinal properties and is often cultivated for its aromatic seeds and oils.
