Maxillaria lindleyana

Maxillaria lindleyana, commonly known as the Lindley's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces inflorescences with several blooms, each featuring a velvety, dark purple or maroon coloration. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria lindleyana is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
