Mammillaria bicolor var. nivea

Mammillaria bicolor var. nivea, commonly known as the white mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a compact, globular shape with a gray-green stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor rock gardens.
