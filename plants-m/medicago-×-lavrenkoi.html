Medicago × lavrenkoi

Medicago × lavrenkoi, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant produces cylindrical, dehiscent pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry grasslands and along roadsides.

Medicago × lavrenkoi is valued for its forage quality and nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
