Masdevallia ignea var. coccinea

Masdevallia ignea var. coccinea, commonly known as the Crimson Flame Orchid, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features vibrant red petals and a contrasting yellow lip, making it a visually captivating species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, requiring well-draining soil and consistent moisture.

Its unique coloration and elegant form make it a prized addition to botanical collections and cultivated gardens.
