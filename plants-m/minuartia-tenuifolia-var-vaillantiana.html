Minuartia tenuifolia var. vaillantiana

Minuartia tenuifolia var. vaillantiana, commonly known as the slender rock-cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and arid environments.

It features small, delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with hairy, succulent leaves that help it retain moisture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and alpine settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a hardy and resilient addition to dry, sunny landscapes.
