Majorana maru

Majorana maru, commonly known as sweet fennel, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features feathery, finely divided leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in umbels. The plant is characterized by its aromatic seeds, which are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun.

Majorana maru is known for its subtle anise-like fragrance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible parts.
