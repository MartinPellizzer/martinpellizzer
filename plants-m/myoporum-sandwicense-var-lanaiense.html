Myoporum sandwicense var. lanaiense

Myoporum sandwicense var. lanaiense, commonly known as Lanai myoporum, is a rare endemic plant native to the Hawaiian island of Lanai.

It is a shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant thrives in coastal and lowland habitats, often found in sandy or rocky soils. It plays a vital role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for native wildlife.

Due to habitat loss and invasive species, it is listed as endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
