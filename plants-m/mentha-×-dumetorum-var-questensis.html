Mentha × dumetorum var. questensis

Mentha × dumetorum var. questensis, commonly known as "quest" mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly minty scent and serrated, lance-shaped leaves. This variety is native to the mountainous regions of the European Alps and is often found in moist, shady environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color.

It is valued for its unique fragrance and is occasionally used in herbal teas and aromatherapy.
