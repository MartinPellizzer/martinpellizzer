Mammillaria polythele var. columnaris

Mammillaria polythele var. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar growth habit, which distinguishes it from other varieties of Mammillaria. The plant features a smooth, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and white to yellowish spines. It produces small, showy flowers that range from yellow to red, typically in the spring.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
