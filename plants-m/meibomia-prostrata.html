Meibomia prostrata

Meibomia prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate meibomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Meibomia prostrata thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
