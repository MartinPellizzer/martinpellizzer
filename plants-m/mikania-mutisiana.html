Mikania mutisiana

Mikania mutisiana, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and distinctive heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing in the early morning. Its stems are covered in fine hairs and can climb or trail along the ground.

Mikania mutisiana is often found in humid, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth.
