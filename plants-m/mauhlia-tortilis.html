Mauhlia tortilis

Mauhlia tortilis, commonly known as the twisted mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive spirally twisted stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Mauhlia tortilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
