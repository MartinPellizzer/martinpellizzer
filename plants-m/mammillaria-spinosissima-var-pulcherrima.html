Mammillaria spinosissima var. pulcherrima

Mammillaria spinosissima var. pulcherrima, commonly known as the showy mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a spherical or slightly elongated stem.

It features numerous white to pale yellow tubercles, each adorned with radial spines that are often more pronounced in mature plants. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer, adding to its ornamental appeal. Native to Mexico, it thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
