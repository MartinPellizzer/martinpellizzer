Monostachya oreoboloides

Monostachya oreoboloides, commonly known as the mountain grass or oreoboloides grass, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, fibrous stems. Monostachya oreoboloides is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
