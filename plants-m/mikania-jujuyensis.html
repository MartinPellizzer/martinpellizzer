Mikania jujuyensis

Mikania jujuyensis, commonly known as the "jungle vine" or "juyuyensis vine," is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly found in Argentina, Bolivia, and Peru.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Mikania jujuyensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.

Its botanical structure makes it an important component of its native ecosystems.
