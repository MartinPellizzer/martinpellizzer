Mesembryanthemum notabile

Mesembryanthemum notabile, commonly known as the noble ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and coastal environments. Its ability to store water in its thick leaves allows it to survive in drought conditions.

Mesembryanthemum notabile is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and resilience.
