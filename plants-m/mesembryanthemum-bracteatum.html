Mesembryanthemum bracteatum

Mesembryanthemum bracteatum, commonly known as the snowball mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and flowers adapted to arid environments. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil with minimal water.
