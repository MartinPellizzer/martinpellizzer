Minuartia athoa subsp. neoiraklitsa

Minuartia athoa subsp.

neoiraklitsa, commonly known as the Balkan rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family. It is characterized by its mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink.

The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in rocky or gravelly soils, often found in alpine or subalpine regions of the Balkans.
