Maxillaria galeata

Maxillaria galeata, commonly known as the Galeata orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria galeata is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
