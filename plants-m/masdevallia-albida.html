Masdevallia albida

Masdevallia albida, commonly known as the White Masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and sepals that resemble a ghostly bloom, giving it a unique and ethereal appearance. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation, making it a challenging species for cultivation. Its flowers are typically short-lived, opening in the early morning and closing by midday.

Masdevallia albida is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity in the botanical world.
