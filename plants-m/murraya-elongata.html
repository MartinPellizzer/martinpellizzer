Murraya elongata

Murraya elongata, commonly known as the longleaf murraya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its fragrant flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces small, fleshy berries that are edible.

Murraya elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
