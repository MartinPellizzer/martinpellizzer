Micrechites ovalifolius

Micrechites ovalifolius, commonly known as the oval-leaved rock cress, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a small annual herb with simple, alternate leaves that are oval in shape and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in early spring. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens as an ornamental plant.
