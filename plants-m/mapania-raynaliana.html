Mapania raynaliana

Mapania raynaliana, commonly known as the Raynalian flameleaf, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, flame-like red leaves that change color with the seasons, making it a visually captivating species. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its woody stems are covered in a thick, waxy cuticle that helps retain moisture in its humid, shaded habitat.
