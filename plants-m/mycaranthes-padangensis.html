Mycaranthes padangensis

Mycaranthes padangensis, commonly known as the Padang starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in humid, lowland areas with rich, well-drained soil. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. Its leaves are ovate and have a slightly hairy texture, while its flowers are clustered in small inflorescences.

The plant is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal uses.
