Miconia tacanensis

Miconia tacanensis, commonly known as the Tican Island miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia tacanensis is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.
