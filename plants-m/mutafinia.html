Mutafinia

Mutafinia, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, silver-green leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.

Mutafinia is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
