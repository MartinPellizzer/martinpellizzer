Medinilla tapete-magicum

Medinilla tapete-magicum, commonly known as the magic velvet medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to prevent leaf scorch. Its unique appearance and continuous blooming make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Medinilla tapete-magicum is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of care in suitable conditions.
