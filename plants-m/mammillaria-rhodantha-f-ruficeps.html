Mammillaria rhodantha f. ruficeps

Mammillaria rhodantha f. ruficeps, commonly known as the red-capped mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces vibrant pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its unique red cap-like structure on the stem is a distinguishing characteristic.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
