Melianthus major

Melianthus major, commonly known as the giant melianthus, is a striking shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter, giving it a dramatic and tropical appearance. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for garden cultivation in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and ornamental value make it a popular choice for landscaping in suitable regions.
