Mimosa gracilis subsp. capillipes

Mimosa gracilis subsp.

capillipes, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a herbaceous plant native to South America. It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are pinnately divided, giving it a lacy appearance.

The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flower heads that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Its rapid growth and attractive foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
