Macradenia brassavolae var. albiflora

Macradenia brassavolae var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered macradenia, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated edge. This variant is distinguished by its notably white flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
