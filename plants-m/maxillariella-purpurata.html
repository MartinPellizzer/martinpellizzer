Maxillariella purpurata

Maxillariella purpurata, commonly known as the purple-stemmed orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant features dark green, strap-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, pinkish-purple flowers with a distinctive red-tinged lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillariella purpurata is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
