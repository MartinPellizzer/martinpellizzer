Mikania hotteana

Mikania hotteana, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs.

Mikania hotteana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
