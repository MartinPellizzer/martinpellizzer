Miconia spennerostachya

Miconia spennerostachya, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that are borne on short pedicels. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature, attracting various birds and animals.

Miconia spennerostachya is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy flowers.
