Magnolia liliifera var. championii

Magnolia liliifera var. championii, commonly known as the Champion's magnolia, is a rare and ornamental variety native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in spring, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant features broad, glossy leaves and is known for its slow growth rate. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
