Mesosphaerum subviolaceum

Mesosphaerum subviolaceum, commonly known as the violet-leaved mesosphaerum, is a member of the Mesosphaerum genus within the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet-colored foliage, which sets it apart from other species in the genus. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species in its native habitat.
