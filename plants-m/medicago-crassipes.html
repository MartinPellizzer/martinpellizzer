Medicago crassipes

Medicago crassipes, commonly known as the large medick, is a perennial legume native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in dry and disturbed soils. It is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along riverbanks.

Medicago crassipes is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment.
