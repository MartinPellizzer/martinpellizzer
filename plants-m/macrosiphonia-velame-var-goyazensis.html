Macrosiphonia velame var. goyazensis

Macrosiphonia velame var. goyazensis, commonly known as the Goyazensis velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the study of tropical flora and its ecological interactions.
