Maranta morrenii

Maranta morrenii, commonly known as the Morren's prayer plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage rather than its flowers. Its leaves are typically oval or lance-shaped and can grow up to 15 cm in length.

Maranta morrenii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
