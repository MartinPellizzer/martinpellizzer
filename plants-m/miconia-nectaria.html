Miconia nectaria

Miconia nectaria, commonly known as the sweetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Miconia nectaria is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
