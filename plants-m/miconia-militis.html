Miconia militis

Miconia militis, commonly known as the military miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. Its flowers are typically showy, with five petals and a distinctive yellow or orange coloration.

Miconia militis is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
