Michelia microcarpa

Michelia microcarpa, commonly known as the Chinese michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Michelia microcarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
