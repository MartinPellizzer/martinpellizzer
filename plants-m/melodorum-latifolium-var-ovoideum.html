Melodorum latifolium var. ovoideum

Melodorum latifolium var. ovoideum, commonly known as the broad-leafed melody plant, is a variant of the Melodorum genus native to Southeast Asia.

It features broad, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often with a subtle melodic scent. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to tropical and subtropical gardens.
