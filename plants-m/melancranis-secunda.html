Melancranis secunda

Melancranis secunda, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers.
