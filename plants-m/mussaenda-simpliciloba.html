Mussaenda simpliciloba

Mussaenda simpliciloba, commonly known as the yellow mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow to orange inflorescences that resemble umbels, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mussaenda simpliciloba is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
