Mesembryanthemum chrysoleucum

Mesembryanthemum chrysoleucum, commonly known as the yellow snowplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, upright stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to xeriscaped landscapes.
