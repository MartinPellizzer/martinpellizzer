Menodora laevis

Menodora laevis, commonly known as the smooth menodora, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Menodoridae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Menodora laevis is known for its medicinal properties, with various parts of the plant used in traditional herbal remedies.
