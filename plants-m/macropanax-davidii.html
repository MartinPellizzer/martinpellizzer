Macropanax davidii

Macropanax davidii, commonly known as the David's giant aralia, is a striking evergreen shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage and historical significance in botany.
