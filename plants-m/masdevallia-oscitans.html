Masdevallia oscitans

Masdevallia oscitans, commonly known as the whispering masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that often have a unique, almost translucent appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and dark green, contributing to its overall elegant appearance.

Masdevallia oscitans is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively compact growth habit.
