Murraya ovatifoliolata

Murraya ovatifoliolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf murraya, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features compound leaves with ovate to lanceolate leaflets, giving it a distinctive foliage appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Murraya ovatifoliolata thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
