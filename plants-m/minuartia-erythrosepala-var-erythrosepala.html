Minuartia erythrosepala var. erythrosepala

Minuartia erythrosepala var. erythrosepala, commonly known as the red-flowered rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage on rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine, white or grayish coating, known as a powdery bloom.
