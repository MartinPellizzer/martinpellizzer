Millotia tenuifolia var. tenuifolia

Millotia tenuifolia var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender millotia, is a perennial herb native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features slender, upright stems with small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by small, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for gardens with warm climates.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and unique floral structure.
