Melodinus intermedius

Melodinus intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate melodinus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy surface. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Melodinus intermedius is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
