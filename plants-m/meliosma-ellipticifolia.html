Meliosma ellipticifolia

Meliosma ellipticifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes trees and shrubs known for their aromatic wood and medicinal properties. The plant features simple, alternate, elliptical leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, winged fruits.

Meliosma ellipticifolia is valued for its ornamental foliage and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
