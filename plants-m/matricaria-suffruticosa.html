Matricaria suffruticosa

Matricaria suffruticosa, commonly known as the hedge mustard, is a perennial shrub native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features erect, branched stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Matricaria suffruticosa is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
