Mammillaria valdeziana

Mammillaria valdeziana, commonly known as the Valdezian hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent areoles that bear white to yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly coating and is often found in rocky soils at higher elevations. It is a popular species among cactus enthusiasts due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.

Mammillaria valdeziana is generally cold-hardy and requires well-draining soil to thrive.
