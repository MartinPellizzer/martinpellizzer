Metalasia tristis

Metalasia tristis, commonly known as the silverleaf acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, often twisted stems and bipinnate leaves that are finely divided. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense, cylindrical spikes. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

The seeds are encased in hard pods and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its spread in suitable habitats.
