Miconia hexamera

Miconia hexamera, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its flowers are typically purple or pink, with five petals and numerous stamens.
