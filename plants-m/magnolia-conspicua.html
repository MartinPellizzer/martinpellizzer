Magnolia conspicua

Magnolia conspicua, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Magnolia conspicua is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
