Macroclinium coffeicola

Macroclinium coffeicola, commonly known as the coffee-colored macroclinium, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant features striking, dark green leaves with a glossy texture and produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
