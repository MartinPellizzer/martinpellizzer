Mitella trifida var. integripetala

Mitella trifida var. integripetala, commonly known as the smoothleaf mitella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin that distinguishes it from other varieties.
