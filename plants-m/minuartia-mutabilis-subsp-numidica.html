Minuartia mutabilis subsp. numidica

Minuartia mutabilis subsp.

numidica, commonly known as the Mediterranean sand-verbena, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. It features small, typically pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.

The plant has a mat-forming growth habit with thin, fleshy leaves adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ability to tolerate drought makes it a resilient species in Mediterranean climates.
