Mapania cuspidata var. merayap

Mapania cuspidata var. merayap, commonly known as the Merayap mapania, is a rare and unique variant of the mapania genus native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, featuring elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a pointed tip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in dense tropical forests. The flowers are small and clustered, typically displaying a pale yellow or greenish hue.
