Masdevallia reichenbachiana var. funebris

Masdevallia reichenbachiana var. funebris, commonly known as the funeral masdevallia, is a striking orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals that range from deep purple to black, giving it its somber common name. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with slender stems and clustered blooms that appear in late winter or early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
