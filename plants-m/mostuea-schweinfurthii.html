Mostuea schweinfurthii

Mostuea schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vibrant orange or red flowers, which resemble lilies. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Mostuea schweinfurthii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
