Myrtus media

Myrtus media, commonly known as sweet myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features fragrant, narrow leaves and small, pale pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces small, dark blue berries that are edible but often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and hedges.

Myrtus media is also valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
