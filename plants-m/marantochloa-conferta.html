Marantochloa conferta

Marantochloa conferta, commonly known as the dwarf arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its upright, slender stems and lanceolate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or reddish in color. It is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.

Marantochloa conferta prefers shady, moist environments and is relatively easy to care for in a home setting.
