Millettia erythrocalyx var. erythrocalyx

Millettia erythrocalyx var. erythrocalyx, commonly known as the red calyx millettia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive red calyx that persists on the fruit, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
