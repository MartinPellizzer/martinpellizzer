Mikania stuebelii var. larensis

Mikania stuebelii var. larensis, commonly known as the Larensis Mikania, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is a climbing vine with opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid conditions make it both ecologically significant and potentially invasive in certain regions.
