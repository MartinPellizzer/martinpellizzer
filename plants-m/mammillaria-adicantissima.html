Mammillaria adicantissima

Mammillaria adicantissima, commonly known as the clustered mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny clusters of cylindrical stems that often grow close together. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
