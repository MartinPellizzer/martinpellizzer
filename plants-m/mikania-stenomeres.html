Mikania stenomeres

Mikania stenomeres, commonly known as the narrow-leaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded areas and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
