Masdevallia longicaudata

Masdevallia longicaudata, commonly known as the long-tailed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendulous inflorescence and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
