Muscari letourneuxii

Muscari letourneuxii, commonly known as the Letourneux musk mallow, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers. It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance and fragrance.
