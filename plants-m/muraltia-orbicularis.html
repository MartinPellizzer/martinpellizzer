Muraltia orbicularis

Muraltia orbicularis, commonly known as the globe flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical, red or orange berries that remain on the plant throughout the winter. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique fruit structure and vibrant colors make it a striking addition to the landscape.
