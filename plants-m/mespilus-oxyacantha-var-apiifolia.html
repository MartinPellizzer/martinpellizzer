Mespilus oxyacantha var. apiifolia

Mespilus oxyacantha var. apiifolia, commonly known as the sweet cherry, is a variant of the blackthorn species.

It is characterized by its small, dark purple berries that are rich in sugars and acids. The plant has dark green, oval-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soil.

The flowers are white and clustered, appearing before the leaves in early spring.
