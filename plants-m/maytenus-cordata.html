Maytenus cordata

Maytenus cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Cornaceae, which includes willows and snowberries. The plant is characterized by its elliptical to ovate leaves that are often heart-shaped, giving it its common name. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by small, red berries that are edible for some bird species.
