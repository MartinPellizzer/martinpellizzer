Macaranga angolensis var. mollis

Macaranga angolensis var. mollis, commonly known as the soft-leaved macaranga, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The species is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed habitats.
