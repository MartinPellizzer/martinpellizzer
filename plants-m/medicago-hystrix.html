Medicago hystrix

Medicago hystrix, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense, spike-like inflorescences. Its root system is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.

Medicago hystrix is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage plant for livestock.
