pseudobarbatum, commonly known as the false goatweed, is a flowering plant in the family Orobanchaceae. It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on other plants for water and nutrients through its root system.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by supporting insect pollinators and serving as a food source for certain herbivores.
