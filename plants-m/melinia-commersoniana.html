Melinia commersoniana

Melinia commersoniana, commonly known as the Commerson's melinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and riverbanks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive violet or purple coloration. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in terminal inflorescences.

Melinia commersoniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
