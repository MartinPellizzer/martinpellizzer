Magnolia rustica rubra

Magnolia rustica rubra, commonly known as the red magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep red, depending on the variety. The leaves are broad and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
