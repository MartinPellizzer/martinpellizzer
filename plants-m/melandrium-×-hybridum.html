Melandrium × hybridum

Melandrium × hybridum, commonly known as the hybrid catchfly, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its upright stems and clusters of small, pink or white flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It typically grows in disturbed soils and can be found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas.
