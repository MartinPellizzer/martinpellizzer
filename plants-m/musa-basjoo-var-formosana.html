Musa basjoo var. formosana

Musa basjoo var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan bamboo banana, is a unique variant of the banana plant native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its slender, bamboo-like stems and large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of white flowers followed by elongated, curved fruits that are typically harvested when they are still green. It is a hardy species that can tolerate cold temperatures, making it suitable for cultivation in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value and unique appearance make it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike.
