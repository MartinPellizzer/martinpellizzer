Millettia warneckei var. prophyrocalyx

Millettia warneckei var. prophyrocalyx, commonly known as the red-flowered millettia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, deep red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a visually appealing ornamental species. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral display and vigorous growth habit make it a popular choice for gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
