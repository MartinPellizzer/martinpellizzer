Melodinus perakensis

Melodinus perakensis, commonly known as the Perak melody vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is characterized by its long, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
