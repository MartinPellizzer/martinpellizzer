Mammillaria brandegeei var. gabbii

Mammillaria brandegeei var. gabbii, commonly known as the Gabb's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a spiny, ribbed structure.

It features dense, white to pale yellow spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is native to the arid regions of Mexico and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
