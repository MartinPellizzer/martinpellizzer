Mammillaria elongata var. subechinata

Mammillaria elongata var. subechinata, commonly known as the long-spined hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in numerous small, tuberculate ribs. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spines are typically straight and can vary in length, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections due to its compact size and ornamental value.
