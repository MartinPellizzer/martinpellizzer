Melastoma vimineum

Melastoma vimineum, commonly known as the climbing melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its twining stems that climb along other vegetation. The plant produces small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often with five petals. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Melastoma vimineum is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
