Melinis Grandiflora

Melinis grandiflora is a striking ornamental grass known for its tall, arching stems and vibrant, feather-like leaves. Native to the southern United States, it thrives in full sun and well-drained soils. This plant is popular in landscaping for its ability to add texture and movement to garden designs. It also serves as a habitat for beneficial insects and birds. With its graceful appearance and low maintenance requirements, Melinis grandiflora is a versatile choice for both residential and commercial landscapes.
