Mammillaria lasiacantha f. diablensis

Mammillaria lasiacantha f. diablensis, commonly known as the Devil's Thorn, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its distinctive appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
