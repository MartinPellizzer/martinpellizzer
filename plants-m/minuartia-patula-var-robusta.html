Minuartia patula var. robusta

Minuartia patula var. robusta, commonly known as the robust rockcress, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are linear and often covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is well adapted to harsh environments and is often found in alpine or coastal regions.
