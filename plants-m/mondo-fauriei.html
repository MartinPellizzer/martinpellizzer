Mondo fauriei

Mondo fauriei, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, velvety leaves and showy flowers. Mondo fauriei is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
