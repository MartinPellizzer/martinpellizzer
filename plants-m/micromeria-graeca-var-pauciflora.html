Micromeria graeca var. pauciflora

Micromeria graeca var. pauciflora, commonly known as the Greek micromeria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing shrub with small, narrow leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its botanical characteristics include aromatic foliage and a compact growth habit.

It is often found in dry, rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
