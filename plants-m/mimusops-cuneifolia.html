Mimusops cuneifolia

Mimusops cuneifolia, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain hard, angular seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued for its durable wood.

Mimusops cuneifolia thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate.
