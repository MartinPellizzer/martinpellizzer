Mammillaria haudeana

Mammillaria haudeana, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the holiday season. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant.
