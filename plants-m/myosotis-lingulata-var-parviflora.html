Myosotis lingulata var. parviflora

Myosotis lingulata var. parviflora, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a low-growing perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features small, delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native gardens and ecological restoration projects.
