Monorchis Limprichtii

Monorchis limprichtii is a rare and enigmatic plant species that belongs to the orchid family. Found primarily in the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of scientific interest. The plant is named after the botanist who first described it, highlighting its historical significance. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, Monorchis limprichtii is now considered endangered.
