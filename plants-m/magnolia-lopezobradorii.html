Magnolia lopezobradorii

Magnolia lopezobradorii, commonly known as the López Obregón magnolia, is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a humid, subtropical climate.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
