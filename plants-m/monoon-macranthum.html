Monoon macranthum

Monoon macranthum, commonly known as the large-flowered monoon, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Monoon macranthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
