Myrcia chytraculia var. zuzygium

Myrcia chytraculia var. zuzygium, commonly known as the pink myrtle, is a shrub native to the coastal regions of Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It produces small, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and adaptability.
