Masdevallia Estradae Var. Xanthina

Masdevallia estradae var. xanthina is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador. This variant is known for its vibrant yellow petals and intricate, delicate structure that sets it apart from other members of its genus. Discovered relatively recently, it has captured the attention of botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike. Its unique coloration and habitat make it a subject of both scientific study and conservation concern. The plant's beauty and rarity underscore the importance of preserving biodiversity in fragile ecosystems.
