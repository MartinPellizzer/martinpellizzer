Myristica dardainei

Myristica dardainei, commonly known as the Dardaine's nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, oily seeds that are used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red berries containing the valuable seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
