Manilkara eickii

Manilkara eickii, commonly known as the Eickii sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and produces smooth, pale yellow fruits that are edible. The tree has a thick, buttressed trunk and can grow up to 30 meters tall. Its bark is rough and fissured, often exuding a milky sap when damaged.

The plant is valued for its timber and is also used in traditional medicine.
