Mitella japonica var. formosana

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a hairy texture.
