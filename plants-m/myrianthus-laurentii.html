Myrianthus laurentii

Myrianthus laurentii, commonly known as the African flame bean, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its habitat. The plant has compound leaves with lance-shaped leaflets and produces pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Myrianthus laurentii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
