Melastoma decipiens

Melastoma decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often violet or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and understoreys. Its leaves are opposite and usually have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is noted for its attractive appearance and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
