Mesosphaerum canescens

Mesosphaerum canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf mesosphaerum, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Mesosphaerum canescens is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
