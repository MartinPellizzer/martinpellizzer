Meliosma angulata

Meliosma angulata, commonly known as the angular meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in axillary inflorescences. Meliosma angulata is often found in shaded, moist forest environments, thriving in humid tropical climates.
