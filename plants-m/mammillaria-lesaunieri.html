Mammillaria lesaunieri

Mammillaria lesaunieri, commonly known as the Lesaunier's bunny ear cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a smooth, pale green body and a distinctive crown of short, white spines. The plant features a short stem with radial ribs that are often covered in a fine, white coating. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
