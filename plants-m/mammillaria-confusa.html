Mammillaria confusa

Mammillaria confusa, commonly known as the fuzzy mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of fine, white bristles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
