Matucana multicolor

Matucana multicolor, commonly known as the multicolored matucana, is a species of cycad native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive, feather-like leaves that display a range of colors, including green, red, and orange. The plant produces small, cone-like structures that contain its seeds, which are dispersed by wind. Matucana multicolor thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
