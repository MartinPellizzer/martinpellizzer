Medicago obscura proles tornata

Medicago obscura proles tornata, commonly known as the tornate clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to certain regions of Europe and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides. It has a deep root system that helps it thrive in poor soil conditions.

The species is notable for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation, benefiting the surrounding soil.
