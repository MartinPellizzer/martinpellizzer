Maxillaria klugii

Maxillaria klugii, commonly known as the Klug's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria klugii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
