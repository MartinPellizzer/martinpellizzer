Macronema glomeratum

Macronema glomeratum, commonly known as the clustered leaf orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and clustered inflorescences. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, yellow, or green, often with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Macronema glomeratum thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
