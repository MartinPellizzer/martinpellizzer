Miltonioides stenoglossa

Miltonioides stenoglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers with a unique structure. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, each featuring a distinctive narrow lip and intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its long-lasting blooms and vibrant colors.
