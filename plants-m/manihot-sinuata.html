Manihot sinuata

Manihot sinuata, commonly known as the arrowleaf manioc, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a milky sap that contains toxic compounds, which must be processed before the roots can be consumed.
