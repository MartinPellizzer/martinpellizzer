Macaglia verruculosa

Macaglia verruculosa, commonly known as the warty macaglia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its warty, rough-textured leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes. Its unique foliage makes it a distinctive addition to horticultural collections.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
