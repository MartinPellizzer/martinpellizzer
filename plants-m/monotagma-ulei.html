Monotagma ulei

Monotagma ulei, commonly known as the yellow wood or yellow ironwood, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are leathery and aromatic, often used in traditional medicine.

The wood is dense and durable, making it valuable for construction and crafting.
