Millettia hockii

Millettia hockii, commonly known as the Hockii Millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pods that contain flat, edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.

Millettia hockii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
