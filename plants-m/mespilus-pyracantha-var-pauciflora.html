Mespilus pyracantha var. pauciflora

Mespilus pyracantha var. pauciflora, commonly known as the dwarf firethorn, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, bright red berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. The plant has dense, spiny foliage that provides habitat for birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden borders or container planting.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant berries and winter foliage.
