Monteverdia urbaniana

Monteverdia urbaniana, commonly known as the urban ivy, is a climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. Its vines can grow up to 10 meters in length, making it suitable for trellises or walls.

Monteverdia urbaniana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
