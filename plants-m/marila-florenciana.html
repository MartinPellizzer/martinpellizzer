Marila florenciana

Marila florenciana, commonly known as the flowering marila, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

Marila florenciana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
