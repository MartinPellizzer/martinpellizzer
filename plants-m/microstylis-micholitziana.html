Microstylis micholitziana

Microstylis micholitziana, commonly known as the narrow-leaved microstylis, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the Cyperaceae family.
