Mnesithea laevis var. chenii

Mnesithea laevis var. chenii, commonly known as Chen's smooth mnesithea, is a variant of the species native to specific regions in China.

It is characterized by its smooth, velvety leaves that are typically pale green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually borne in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized botanical specimen.
