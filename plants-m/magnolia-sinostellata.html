Magnolia sinostellata

Magnolia sinostellata, commonly known as the Chinese star magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it typically grows in moist, well-drained soils.

Magnolia sinostellata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant floral display.
