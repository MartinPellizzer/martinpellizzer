Monoon chloroxanthum

Monoon chloroxanthum, commonly known as the yellow monoon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Monotomaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in wet, tropical environments and is often found in swampy or marshy areas. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
