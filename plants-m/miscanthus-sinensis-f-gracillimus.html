Miscanthus sinensis f. gracillimus

Miscanthus sinensis f. gracillimus, commonly known as the graceful silver grass, is a ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching blades that are typically silver-white in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, feathery seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in garden settings.
