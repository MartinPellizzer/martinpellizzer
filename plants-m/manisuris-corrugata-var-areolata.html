Manisuris corrugata var. areolata

Manisuris corrugata var. areolata, commonly known as the spotted manisuris, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, corrugated leaves that are often covered in small, raised spots or tubercles. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
