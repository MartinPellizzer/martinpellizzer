Myrospermum pereirae

Myrospermum pereirae, commonly known as the Brazilian sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring well-drained soils. It produces small, oval-shaped seeds that are often used in local traditional practices.
