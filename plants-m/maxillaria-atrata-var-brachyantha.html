Maxillaria atrata var. brachyantha

Maxillaria atrata var. brachyantha, commonly known as the Black Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers, which are often covered in a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, spurred orchid flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments with well-drained soil.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
