Maxillaria nardoides

Maxillaria nardoides, commonly known as the Nardoid Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically feature a yellow lip with a distinctive red or purple spot, and the petals are often pink or white. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
