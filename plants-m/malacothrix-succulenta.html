Malacothrix succulenta

Malacothrix succulenta, commonly known as the stonecrop saxifrage, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
