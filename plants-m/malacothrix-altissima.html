Malacothrix altissima

Malacothrix altissima, commonly known as the tall hairroot, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
