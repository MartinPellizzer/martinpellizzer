Medicago bonarotiana

Medicago bonarotiana, commonly known as the Bonarot alfalfa, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a robust root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation. It is often used in forage and ecological restoration projects.

Its adaptability to various soil types makes it a valuable plant in agricultural and environmental contexts.
