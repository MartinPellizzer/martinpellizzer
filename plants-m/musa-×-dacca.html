Musa × dacca

Musa × dacca, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid species within the genus Musa.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of yellow, edible berries. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its fruit, which is rich in nutrients. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The fruit has a sweet, mild flavor and is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
