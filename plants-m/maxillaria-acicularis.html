Maxillaria acicularis

Maxillaria acicularis, commonly known as the needle orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns that attract specific pollinators. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
