Mesomelaena deusta

Mesomelaena deusta, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. Mesomelaena deusta is typically found in wetland areas and along riverbanks in tropical and subtropical regions.
