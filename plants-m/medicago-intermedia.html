Medicago intermedia

Medicago intermedia, commonly known as Turkey rhombus, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various environments. It produces small, lens-shaped seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread.

Medicago intermedia is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
