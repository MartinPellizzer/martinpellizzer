Mammillaria backebergiana subsp. backebergiana

Mammillaria backebergiana subsp.

backebergiana, commonly known as the Backeberg mammillaria, is a small cactus native to South Africa. It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
