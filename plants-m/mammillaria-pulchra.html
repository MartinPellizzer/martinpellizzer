Mammillaria pulchra

Mammillaria pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and is covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor succulent gardens.
