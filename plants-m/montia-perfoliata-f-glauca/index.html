Montia Perfoliata F. Glauca

Montia perfoliata f. glauca is a striking variant of the common mouse-ear plant known for its unique bluish-gray foliage. This frost-tolerant plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its distinctive appearance makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking ornamental value. The glaucous hue of its leaves is due to a waxy coating that helps retain moisture. This plant not only adds visual interest but also supports local pollinators and wildlife.
