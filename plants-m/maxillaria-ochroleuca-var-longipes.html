Maxillaria ochroleuca var. longipes

Maxillaria ochroleuca var. longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with bright yellow petals and a contrasting deep orange lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
