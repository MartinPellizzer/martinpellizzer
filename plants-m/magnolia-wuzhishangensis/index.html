Magnolia Wuzhishangensis

Magnolia wuzhishangensis is a rare and ancient flowering plant native to China. First discovered in the 1990s, it is named after the Wuzhishang region where it was found. This species is notable for its large, showy flowers and its ecological significance in subtropical forests. Due to habitat loss, it is now critically endangered and protected by conservation efforts. Its discovery has provided valuable insights into the evolution of magnolia species.
