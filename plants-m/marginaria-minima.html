Marginaria minima

Marginaria minima, commonly known as the small marginaria, is a diminutive flowering plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny white or pale yellow flowers. Its flowers are typically short-lived, relying on wind pollination for reproductive success.
