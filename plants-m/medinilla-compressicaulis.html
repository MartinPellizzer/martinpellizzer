Medinilla compressicaulis

Medinilla compressicaulis, commonly known as the broadleaf medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers. The leaves are typically broad and leathery, providing a lush appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

The plant's ornamental value comes from its striking floral displays and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
