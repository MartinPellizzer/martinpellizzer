Mandevilla tenuicarpa

Mandevilla tenuicarpa, commonly known as the slender mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces small, green, egg-shaped fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Mandevilla tenuicarpa is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and hanging baskets.
