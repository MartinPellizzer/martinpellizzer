Mibora minima

Mibora minima, commonly known as the miniature mibora, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Mibora minima thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
