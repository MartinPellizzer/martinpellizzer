Muscari weissii

Muscari weissii, commonly known as the Weiss's grape hyacinth, is a bulbous plant native to southern Europe.

It produces dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically blooms in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to gardens and natural landscapes. Muscari weissii thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a border plant due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
