Myristica corticosa var. tomentella

Myristica corticosa var. tomentella, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its thick, hairy bark and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces unique, fleshy fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its botanical features include a compact growth habit and a preference for humid, shaded environments.

The species is also noted for its ornamental value and ecological importance in tropical ecosystems.
