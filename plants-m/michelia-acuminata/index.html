Michelia Acuminata

Michelia acuminata is a rare and beautiful evergreen shrub native to southern China. It is known for its fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions. Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after addition to gardens and botanical collections. Despite its charm, it remains relatively obscure in the wider horticultural world.
