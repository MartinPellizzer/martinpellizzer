Maxillaria angelae

Maxillaria angelae, commonly known as the Angel's Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically features broad, dark green leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and collections.

Maxillaria angelae is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
