Melastoma soleniferum

Melastoma soleniferum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
