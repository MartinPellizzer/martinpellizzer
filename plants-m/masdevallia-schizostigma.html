Masdevallia schizostigma

Masdevallia schizostigma, commonly known as the split-leaf masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It features elongated, strap-like leaves that are often divided, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with high air circulation.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
