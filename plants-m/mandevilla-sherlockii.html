Mandevilla sherlockii

Mandevilla sherlockii, commonly known as the Sherlock mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mandevilla sherlockii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
