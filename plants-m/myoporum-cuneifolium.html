Myoporum cuneifolium

Myoporum cuneifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in spikes, which bloom in spring. It is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or gravelly soils.

Myoporum cuneifolium is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
