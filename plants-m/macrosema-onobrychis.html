Macrosema onobrychis

Macrosema onobrychis, commonly known as the yellow clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in meadows and grasslands. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Its flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, contributing to ecological balance.
