Masdevallia obscurans

Masdevallia obscurans, commonly known as the dark masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, often hairy pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and pink, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, requiring well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
