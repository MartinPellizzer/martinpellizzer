Michelia platypetala

Michelia platypetala, commonly known as the flat-petaled michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Michelia platypetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
