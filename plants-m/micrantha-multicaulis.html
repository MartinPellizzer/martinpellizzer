Micrantha multicaulis

Micrantha multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered micrantha, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features a short, caulescent stem with numerous small, clustered leaves. Its flowers are tiny, white or pale green, and are clustered in dense inflorescences.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and is often found in association with other epiphytic or lithophytic plants.
