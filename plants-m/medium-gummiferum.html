Medium gummiferum

Medium gummiferum, commonly known as the rubber tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves and a milky sap that contains latex. The plant has a robust, upright stem with a smooth, greenish-brown bark. Its leaves are large, broad, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

This species is well adapted to warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to thrive.
