Mammillaria incerta

Mammillaria incerta, commonly known as the uncertain mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and covered in white to yellowish tubercles. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers during the spring and summer months. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
