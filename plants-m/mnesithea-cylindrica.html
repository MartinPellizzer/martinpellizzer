Mnesithea cylindrica

Mnesithea cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical mnesithea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, cylindrical stems that are often hairy and can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Mnesithea cylindrica is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
