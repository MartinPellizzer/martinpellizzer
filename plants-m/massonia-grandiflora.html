Massonia grandiflora

Massonia grandiflora, commonly known as the Natal carpet or Natal star, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a low-growing, evergreen shrub that features dense, mat-forming foliage. The plant produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and hardy nature make it a popular choice among gardeners in temperate climates.
