Microclisia australis

Microclisia australis, commonly known as the Australian microclisia, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a small, shrub-like species that typically grows in dry, sandy soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

Microclisia australis is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal and inland regions with low rainfall.
