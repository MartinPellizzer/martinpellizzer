Melandrium album var. latifolium

Melandrium album var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf honesty, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant produces white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, followed by small, papery pods that contain seeds. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
