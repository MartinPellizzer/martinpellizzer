Metalasia luteola

Metalasia luteola, commonly known as the yellow metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has hairy stems and leaves. Its small, daisy-like flowers are composed of ray florets and tubular disc florets.

Metalasia luteola is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
