Monostachya

Monostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaf grass, is a genus of flowering plants in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, linear leaves. The flowers are typically arranged in a single, terminal spikelet, giving the plant its name. Monostachya species are native to various regions of Africa and are often found in grasslands and open habitats.
