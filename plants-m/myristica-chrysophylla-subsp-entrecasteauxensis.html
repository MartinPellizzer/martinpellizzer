Myristica chrysophylla subsp. entrecasteauxensis

Myristica chrysophylla subsp.

entrecasteauxensis, commonly known as the golden leaf myristica, is a member of the Myristicaceae family. It is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines.

The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green in color. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, while the fruit is a capsule that contains aromatic seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
