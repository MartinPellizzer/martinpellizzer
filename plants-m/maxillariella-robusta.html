Maxillariella robusta

Maxillariella robusta, commonly known as the robust orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomes and pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting lip. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for cultivation in warm climates.

Maxillariella robusta is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
