Muhlenbergia peruviana f. versicolor

Muhlenbergia peruviana f. versicolor, commonly known as the rainbow muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking variegated foliage, which displays shades of green, silver, and pink along the leaf blades. The plant produces delicate, feathery panicles of pale blue to purple flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its unique coloration and graceful appearance make it a standout in landscape design.
