Mammillaria gigantea subsp. flavovirens

Mammillaria gigantea subsp.

flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green mammillaria, is a cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem with a greenish-yellow hue and is covered in prominent, tuberculate ribs.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is relatively cold-hardy and requires well-draining soil to thrive.

Due to its attractive appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
