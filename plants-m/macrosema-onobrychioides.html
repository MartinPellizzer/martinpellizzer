Macrosema onobrychioides

Macrosema onobrychioides, commonly known as the yellow burr medic, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small annual herb with yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces pods that contain hard, spiny seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by attachment to animals. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in grassland and rocky areas.

Its botanical features make it an important plant for both ecological and agricultural purposes.
