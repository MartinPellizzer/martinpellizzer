Miltonioides leucomelas subsp. acutum

Miltonioides leucomelas subsp.

acutum, commonly known as the Acute-leafed Miltoniopsis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family. It is characterized by its broad, acuminate leaves that are typically a vibrant green with a waxy texture.

The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

This subspecies is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
