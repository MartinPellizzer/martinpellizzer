Maguireothamnus jauaensis

Maguireothamnus jauaensis, commonly known as the Jauaensis goldenbush, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and leaf structures. The plant typically features narrow, gray-green leaves with a fuzzy texture, adapted to arid environments. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value.

Maguireothamnus jauaensis is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.
