Miltonia × cogniauxiae var. bicolor

Miltonia × cogniauxiae var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor orchid, is a hybrid epiphytic orchid native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bi-colored petals that often feature a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces large, showy flowers with a velvety texture and a distinct, fragrant scent. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated as a houseplant and for commercial floral use.
