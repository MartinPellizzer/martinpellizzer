Mammillaria rufocrocea

Mammillaria rufocrocea, commonly known as the red-spined hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green tubercles. The plant features red or reddish-brown spines that give it its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique spines make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
