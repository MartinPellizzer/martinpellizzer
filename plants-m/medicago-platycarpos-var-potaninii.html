Medicago platycarpos var. potaninii

Medicago platycarpos var. potaninii, commonly known as the Potanin's goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces cylindrical, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky or sandy soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
