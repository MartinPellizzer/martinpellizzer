Miconia norlindii

Miconia norlindii, commonly known as the red miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are typically pink or red, arranged in clusters, and attract various pollinators.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
