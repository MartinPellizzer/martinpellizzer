Melanthium wurmbeum

Melanthium wurmbeum, commonly known as the yellow false nettle, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Polygonaceae family, characterized by its distinctive, triangular stems and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. Melanthium wurmbeum plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is often found in forest understories.
