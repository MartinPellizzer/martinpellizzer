Mesua ferrea var. angustifolia

Mesua ferrea var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The leaves are typically narrow and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical climates.
