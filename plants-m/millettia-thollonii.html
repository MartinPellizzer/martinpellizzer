Millettia thollonii

Millettia thollonii, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The tree produces long, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its bark and leaves are also valued for their medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

Millettia thollonii is often found in savanna and woodland habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
