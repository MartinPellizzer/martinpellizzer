Molongum macrophyllum

Molongum macrophyllum, commonly known as the large-leaf molong, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in arid regions.

Its ornamental value has led to its use in garden landscapes across southern Australia.
