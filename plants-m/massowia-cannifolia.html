Massowia cannifolia

Massowia cannifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and distinctive spatulate inflorescence. The plant typically grows in wet, marshy environments and thrives in humid climates. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
