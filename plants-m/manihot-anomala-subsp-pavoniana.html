Manihot anomala subsp. pavoniana

Manihot anomala subsp.

pavoniana, commonly known as the purple arrowleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purple hue on the underside.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
