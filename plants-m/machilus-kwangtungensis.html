Machilus kwangtungensis

Machilus kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong machilus, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its oval-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green on top, with a lighter underside. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, dark purple berries.

This species is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
