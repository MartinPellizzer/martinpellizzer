Matelea quirosii

Matelea quirosii, commonly known as the Quito's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many species with showy, often fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a soft, velvety texture.
