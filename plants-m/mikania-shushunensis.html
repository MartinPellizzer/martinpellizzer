Mikania shushunensis

Mikania shushunensis, commonly known as the Chinese Mikania, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to China and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread vegetatively, it can become invasive in certain regions.
