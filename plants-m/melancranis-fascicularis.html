Melancranis fascicularis

Melancranis fascicularis, commonly known as the clustered melancranis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Melancranidaceae.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Due to its unique floral structure, it is of interest to botanists studying pollination mechanisms in specialized plant families.
