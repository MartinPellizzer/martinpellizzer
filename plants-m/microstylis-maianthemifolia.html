Microstylis maianthemifolia

Microstylis maianthemifolia, commonly known as the small-styled starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features small, star-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a rhizomatous root system and spreads by stolons, forming dense mats. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval, toothed leaflets.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
