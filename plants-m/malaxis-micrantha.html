Malaxis micrantha

Malaxis micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered malaxis, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments such as forests and rocky areas. The plant features a slender, creeping rhizome with small, green leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive, narrow labellum.

Malaxis micrantha is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in low-light conditions.
