Mammillaria glabrata var. leucacantha

Mammillaria glabrata var. leucacantha, commonly known as the white-spined hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to bluish-green body with white or pale yellow tubercles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant has short, cylindrical spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique look and is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
