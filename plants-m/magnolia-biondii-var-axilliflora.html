Magnolia biondii var. axilliflora

Magnolia biondii var. axilliflora, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the yellow magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that appear in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has smooth, gray bark and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The variety is noted for its fragrant blossoms and adaptability to various climates.
