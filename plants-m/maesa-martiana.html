Maesa martiana

Maesa martiana, commonly known as the red maesa, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually white or pink in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
