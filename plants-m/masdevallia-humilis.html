Masdevallia humilis

Masdevallia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate nature, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
