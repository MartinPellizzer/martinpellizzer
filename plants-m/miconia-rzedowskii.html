Miconia rzedowskii

Miconia rzedowskii, commonly known as the Mexican miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central America and northern South America. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.
