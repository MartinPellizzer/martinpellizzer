Memorialis pentandra var. hypericifolia

Memorialis pentandra var. hypericifolia, commonly known as the purple-flowered coral tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, clusters of purple or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces distinctive, woody, coral-like seed pods. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in gardens and public spaces.
