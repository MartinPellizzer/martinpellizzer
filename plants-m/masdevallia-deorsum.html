Masdevallia deorsum

Masdevallia deorsum, commonly known as the Upside-down Orchid, is a rare and exotic orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its unique growth habit, with its flowers facing downward, which is an unusual trait among orchids. The plant has dark green, leathery leaves and produces intricate, colorful flowers with a distinct, often fragrant scent. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to bloom successfully.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanical enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
