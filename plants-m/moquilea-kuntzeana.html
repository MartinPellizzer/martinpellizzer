Moquilea kuntzeana

Moquilea kuntzeana, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry environments, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers come in shades of white, yellow, and orange.

Moquilea kuntzeana is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological significance in its native habitat.
