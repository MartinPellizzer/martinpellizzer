Masdevallia × geleniana

Masdevallia × geleniana, commonly known as the Gelenian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are known for their diverse shapes and vibrant hues, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Masdevallia × geleniana requires specific care, including high humidity and well-draining potting media, to thrive.
