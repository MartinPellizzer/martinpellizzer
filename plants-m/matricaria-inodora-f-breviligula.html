Matricaria inodora f. breviligula

Matricaria inodora f. breviligula, commonly known as the short-stemmed chamomile, is a botanical variant of the chamomile plant.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and shorter stems compared to the typical form. The plant features small, daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center, typical of the Asteraceae family. It has aromatic leaves that emit a mild, pleasant scent, often used in herbal preparations.

This form is valued for its medicinal properties and is cultivated for both ornamental and therapeutic purposes.
