Myrtus Vitiensis

Myrtus vitiensis is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical regions of Vanuatu. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes well-known species like eucalyptus and clove. This plant is characterized by its fragrant leaves and small, showy white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a valuable species for both ecological and botanical research.
