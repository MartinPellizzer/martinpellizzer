Melaleuca Chrysantherea

Melaleuca chrysantherea is a striking plant native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and aromatic foliage, it thrives in sandy soils and saline environments. This species belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like eucalyptus and tea trees. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively rare in the wild, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.
