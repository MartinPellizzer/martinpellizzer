Mammillaria livida

Mammillaria livida, commonly known as the pale mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its low-growing, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

Mammillaria livida is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
