Metalasia juniperoides

Metalasia juniperoides, commonly known as the African juniper, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage, which is typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, berry-like cones that are often red or purple when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

The botanical features of Metalasia juniperoides make it an important component of local ecosystems and a popular ornamental plant.
