Matelea porphyrantha

Matelea porphyrantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered matelea, is a species of flowering plant in the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves and produces capsule fruits containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
