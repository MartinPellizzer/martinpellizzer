Mundia

Mundia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a distinctive blue or purple throat. Its leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, and it thrives in bright, indirect light.

Mundia is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.
