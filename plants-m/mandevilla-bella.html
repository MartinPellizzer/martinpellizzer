Mandevilla bella

Mandevilla bella, commonly known as the sweetheart vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, often from spring to fall. Its leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a glossy green surface.

Mandevilla bella is well-suited for container gardening or as a climbing plant in gardens with ample sunlight.
