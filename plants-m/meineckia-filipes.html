Meineckia filipes

Meineckia filipes, commonly known as the slender feather fern, is a delicate fern native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features intricate, finely divided fronds that give it a lacy appearance, with the leaves arranged in a feathery pattern. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as along forest floors or in shaded gardens. Its rhizomes are often covered in a thin, fibrous sheath, and it produces small, spore-bearing structures called sori on the underside of its fronds.

Meineckia filipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in humid, shaded garden settings.
