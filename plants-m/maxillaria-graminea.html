Maxillaria graminea

Maxillaria graminea, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its grass-like foliage and compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
