Myrtus nivalis

Myrtus nivalis, commonly known as snow myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery white wax. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Myrtus nivalis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in hedges or as a decorative plant in gardens.
