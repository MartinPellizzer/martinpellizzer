Melinis wightii

Melinis wightii, commonly known as silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-green leaves and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a fibrous root system and reproduces primarily through seed dispersal.

Melinis wightii is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
