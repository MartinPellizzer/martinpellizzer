Micromeles coronata

Micromeles coronata, commonly known as the Crowned Micromeles, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of yellow flowers with a distinctive crown-like structure. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in Mediterranean flora.
