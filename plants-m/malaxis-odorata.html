Malaxis odorata

Malaxis odorata, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a pseudobulb and produces delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink with a distinctive, sweet scent.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and aromatic qualities.
