Maturna suaveolens

Maturna suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-smelling maturna, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a fragrant aroma when crushed.

The plant is characterized by its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
