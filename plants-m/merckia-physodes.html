Merckia physodes

Merckia physodes, commonly known as the white-flowered merckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its strap-shaped, evergreen leaves. The plant produces clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to thrive in well-drained, sandy soils.

Merckia physodes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in garden settings.
