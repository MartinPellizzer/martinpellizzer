Mammillaria rhodantha f. stenocephala

Mammillaria rhodantha f. stenocephala, commonly known as the narrow-headed pink mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in short, cylindrical spines and small, rounded areoles. The plant produces pale pink to white flowers during the spring or early summer. Its foliage is typically green with a slight bluish tinge, and it is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.

This cultivar is prized for its compact size and elegant floral display.
