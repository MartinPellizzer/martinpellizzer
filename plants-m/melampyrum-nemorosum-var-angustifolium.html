Melampyrum nemorosum var. angustifolium

Melampyrum nemorosum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved cow-wheat, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its typical legume morphology. The plant features small, yellowish-orange flowers arranged in clusters, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and hedgerows.

This variety is distinguished by its narrower foliage compared to other varieties of melampyrum.
