Memecylon occultum

Memecylon occultum, commonly known as the hidden memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and less-studied species.
