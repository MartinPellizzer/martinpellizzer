Maxillaria phoenicanthera

Maxillaria phoenicanthera, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fiery orange to red flowers that resemble flames, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually arranged in racemes and are known for their strong, sweet fragrance.

Botanically, it belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its complex and showy floral structures.
