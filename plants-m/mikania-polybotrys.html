Mikania polybotrys

Mikania polybotrys, commonly known as the Chinese milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Mikania polybotrys is also notable for its milky sap, which can be toxic if ingested.
