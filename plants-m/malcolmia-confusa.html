Malcolmia confusa

Malcolmia confusa, commonly known as the Egyptian morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value and easy cultivation make it a favored plant among gardeners.
