Micromeria Thymbra

Micromeria thymbra is a small, aromatic plant native to the Mediterranean region. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, known for its fragrant herbs and medicinal properties. This species is characterized by its tiny, narrow leaves and clusters of delicate blue or purple flowers. It thrives in rocky and dry environments, often found in coastal areas and mountainous regions. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in local ecosystems and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
