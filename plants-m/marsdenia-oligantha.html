Marsdenia oligantha

Marsdenia oligantha, commonly known as the slender Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and hairy stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Marsdenia oligantha is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
