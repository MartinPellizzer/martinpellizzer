Microstegium ciliatum var. integrum

Microstegium ciliatum var. integrum, commonly known as the smooth wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to eastern North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with smooth margins and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its stems are often hairy, and the leaves are covered in fine, white hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in partially shaded conditions.
