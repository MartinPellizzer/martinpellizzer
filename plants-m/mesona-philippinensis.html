Mesona philippinensis

Mesona philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine mesona, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
