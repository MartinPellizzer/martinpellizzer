Maxillaria stenobulbon

Maxillaria stenobulbon, commonly known as the narrow-rooted maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces strikingly colorful flowers with a distinctive spurred lip, often in shades of pink, purple, or white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

Maxillaria stenobulbon is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
