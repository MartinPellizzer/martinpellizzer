Medicago sphaerocarpos

Medicago sphaerocarpos, commonly known as the globular vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, inflated pods. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

Medicago sphaerocarpos is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as fields and along roadsides.
