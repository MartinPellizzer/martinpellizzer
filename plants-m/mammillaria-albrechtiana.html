Mammillaria albrechtiana

Mammillaria albrechtiana, commonly known as the Albrechtian nipple cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the ribs.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
