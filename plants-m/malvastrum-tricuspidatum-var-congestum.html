Malvastrum tricuspidatum var. congestum

Malvastrum tricuspidatum var. congestum, commonly known as the Chinese sunset flower, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features showy, semi-double to fully double, pink to red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant has hairy, oval-shaped leaves with three distinct lobes, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
