Modiolastrum australe

Modiolastrum australe, commonly known as the Australian sandbind, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in sandy or gravelly soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are usually linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, helping it conserve water in dry conditions.

Modiolastrum australe is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environments.
