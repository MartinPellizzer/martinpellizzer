Mimosa xanthocentra var. miseranda

Mimosa xanthocentra var. miseranda, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features small, fern-like, bipinnate leaves that turn a striking silver-white color during the dry season. The plant produces fluffy, pale yellow to cream-colored spherical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
