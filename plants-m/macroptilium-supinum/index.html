Macroptilium Supinum

Climbing morning glory, scientifically known as Macroptilium supinum, is a vibrant and fast-growing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions. This plant is celebrated for its striking blue and violet flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. It thrives in sunny environments and is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal. Its ability to climb and spread rapidly makes it both a beautiful and sometimes invasive species. Despite its beauty, care must be taken to manage its growth to prevent it from overtaking other plants.
