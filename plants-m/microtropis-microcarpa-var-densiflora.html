Microtropis microcarpa var. densiflora

Microtropis microcarpa var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered microtropis, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of tiny, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
