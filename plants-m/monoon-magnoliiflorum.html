Monoon magnoliiflorum

Monoon magnoliiflorum, commonly known as the white hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves and a woody stem, making it a striking ornamental species. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
