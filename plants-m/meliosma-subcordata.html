Meliosma subcordata

Meliosma subcordata, commonly known as the small-leafed meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptic to ovate in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

The fruit is a capsule that dehisces to release small, winged seeds.
