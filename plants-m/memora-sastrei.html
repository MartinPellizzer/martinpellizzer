Memora sastrei

Memora sastrei, commonly known as the Sastre's ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent petals and a unique, almost ethereal appearance that resembles a ghostly silhouette. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the cooler months, typically between September and November. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires specific humidity and temperature conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, Memora sastrei is considered a conservation concern and is often protected in its natural range.
