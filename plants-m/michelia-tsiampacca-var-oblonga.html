Michelia tsiampacca var. oblonga

Michelia tsiampacca var. oblonga, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or oblong-leaf michelia, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
