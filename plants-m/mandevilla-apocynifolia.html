Mandevilla apocynifolia

Mandevilla apocynifolia, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla apocynifolia is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
