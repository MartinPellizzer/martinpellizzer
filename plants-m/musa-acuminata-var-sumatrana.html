Musa acuminata var. sumatrana

Musa acuminata var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran banana, is a cultivar native to Indonesia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a short, sturdy stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces large, clustered inflorescences that bear yellow, edible berries. It is valued for its high yield and sweet, creamy fruit.

This variety is also notable for its resistance to certain diseases and its adaptability to tropical climates.
