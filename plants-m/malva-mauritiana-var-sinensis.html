Malva mauritiana var. sinensis

Malva mauritiana var. sinensis, commonly known as Chinese mallow, is a herbaceous plant native to China.

It features large, velvety leaves that are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pale pink to white flowers with five petals, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
