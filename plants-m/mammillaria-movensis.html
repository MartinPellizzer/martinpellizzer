Mammillaria movensis

Mammillaria movensis, commonly known as the Movense Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and xeriscaping.
