Meridiana othonnites

Meridiana othonnites, commonly known as the narrowleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like yellow flowers surrounded by white ray florets. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It has linear, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Meridiana othonnites is often used in rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
