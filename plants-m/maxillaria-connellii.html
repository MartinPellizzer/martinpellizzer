Maxillaria connellii

Maxillaria connellii, commonly known as the Connell's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in cool, shaded environments with high humidity. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

Maxillaria connellii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
