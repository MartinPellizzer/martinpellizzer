Microstylis setipes

Microstylis setipes, commonly known as the slender-styled grass, is a member of the Poaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is a defining botanical feature.

Microstylis setipes is notable for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists studying grass morphology.
