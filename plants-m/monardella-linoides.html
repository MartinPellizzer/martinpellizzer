Monardella linoides

Monardella linoides, commonly known as narrowleaf monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pink to purple and are clustered in whorls along the stems.

It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
