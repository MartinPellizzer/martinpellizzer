Malus × atrosanguinea

Malus × atrosanguinea, commonly known as the dark red crabapple, is a hybrid species of the genus Malus.

It is characterized by its small, ornamental fruits that range in color from deep red to black. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree, reaching heights of up to 8 meters. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a serrated margin and turn a striking red or orange in the fall.

The flowers are white or pale pink, appearing in early spring before the leaves emerge.
