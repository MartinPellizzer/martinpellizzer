Mertensia asiatica

Mertensia asiatica, commonly known as the Asian bluebells, is a herbaceous perennial native to East Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, toothed leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Mertensia asiatica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
