Mammillaria hertrichiana var. robustior

Mammillaria hertrichiana var. robustior, commonly known as the robust mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
