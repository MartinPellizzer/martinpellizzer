Minuartia mutabilis var. faurei

Minuartia mutabilis var. faurei, commonly known as the variable rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that can change color from white to pink or red depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or arctic regions. Its leaves are linear and covered with a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps protect it from harsh weather.

This variety is notable for its adaptability and ability to survive in extreme climates.
