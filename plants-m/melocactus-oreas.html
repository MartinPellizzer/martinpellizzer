Melocactus oreas

Melocactus oreas, commonly known as the Mexican apple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces vibrant red or yellow flowers during the summer months. Its fruit, resembling a small apple, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Melocactus oreas is adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
