Monstera pertusa f. modesta

Monstera pertusa f. modesta, commonly known as the modest monstera, is a variegated foliage plant with distinctive split leaves.

It is a hybrid form of Monstera pertusa, characterized by its unique, lighter green variegation patterns. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with irregular fenestrations, giving it a tropical appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements.
