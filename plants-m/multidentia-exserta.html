Multidentia exserta

Multidentia exserta, commonly known as the slender toothed plant, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features narrow, linear leaves with small, tooth-like projections along the edges. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

Multidentia exserta thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
