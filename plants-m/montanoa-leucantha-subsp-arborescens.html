Montanoa leucantha subsp. arborescens

Montanoa leucantha subsp.

arborescens, commonly known as the tree montanoa, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect growth habit and showy yellow flowers.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and is often found in rocky or limestone soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.

The subspecies is distinguished by its more tree-like form compared to other members of the species.
