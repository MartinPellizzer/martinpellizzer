Musa arnoldiana

Musa arnoldiana, commonly known as the African banana, is a species of banana plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellowish-green bananas that are typically harvested when they are still immature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This plant is often cultivated for its edible fruit and is also valued for its ornamental appeal.
