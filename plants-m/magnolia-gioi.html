Magnolia gioi

Magnolia gioi, commonly known as the dwarf magnolia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to China.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white to pale pink. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that remain throughout the year, providing ornamental value in both winter and summer. It thrives in partial to full shade and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

Due to its size and beauty, it is a popular choice for garden landscapes and container planting.
