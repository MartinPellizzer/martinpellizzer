Malaxis monophyllos var. brachypoda

Malaxis monophyllos var. brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed lady's slipper orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its unique, pouch-like flower that resembles a slipper, typically ranging in color from white to pink. The plant produces a single, erect stem with a few leaves and a single, showy flower. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
