Morinda billardierei

Morinda billardierei, commonly known as the pink morinda, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Morinda billardierei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
