Mammillaria vagaspina

Mammillaria vagaspina, commonly known as the Spiny Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny stems that are typically cylindrical in shape. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
