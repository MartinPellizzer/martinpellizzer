Maclura sieberi

Maclura sieberi, commonly known as the Chinese yellow wood, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and alternate, simple leaves. The species is also valued for its hard, durable wood.
