Melastoma zeylanicum

Melastoma zeylanicum, commonly known as the Java melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a rough texture.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
