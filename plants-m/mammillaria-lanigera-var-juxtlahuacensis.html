Mammillaria lanigera var. juxtlahuacensis

Mammillaria lanigera var. juxtlahuacensis, commonly known as the Juxtlahuacan mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often sought after by cactus enthusiasts.
