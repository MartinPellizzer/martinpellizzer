Melhania velutina

Melhania velutina, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The fruit is valued for its high oil content and is used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts.
