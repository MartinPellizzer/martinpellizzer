Mammillaria erythra

Mammillaria erythra, commonly known as the red-hair mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical or slightly elongated stem that is covered in white to grayish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
