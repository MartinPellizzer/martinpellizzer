Memecylon caloneuron

Memecylon caloneuron, commonly known as the golden memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
