Myrtus violacea

Myrtus violacea, commonly known as violet myrtle, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in spring. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and a compact growth habit. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Myrtus violacea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
