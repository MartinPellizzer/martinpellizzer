Mostacillastrum retrofractum

Mostacillastrum retrofractum, commonly known as the fractured grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant features narrow, blade-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water.

Its unique growth pattern and resilience make it an important part of its native ecosystem.
