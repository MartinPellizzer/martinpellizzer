Monstera dubia

Monstera dubia, commonly known as the "shoestring plant," is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fenestrated leaves that become more pronounced as the plant matures. The leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and they can grow quite large, reaching up to 1 meter in length. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
