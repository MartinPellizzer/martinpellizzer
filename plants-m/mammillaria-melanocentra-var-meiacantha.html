Mammillaria melanocentra var. meiacantha

Mammillaria melanocentra var. meiacantha, commonly known as the Meiacantha mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
