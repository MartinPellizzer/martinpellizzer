Mammillaria Rekoi

The Mammillaria rekoi is a striking member of the cactus family native to Mexico. Known for its unique appearance, it features a compact stem covered in white spines and yellow flowers. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens. Its beauty and resilience have made it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. Despite its hardiness, it needs care to avoid overwatering and extreme temperatures.
