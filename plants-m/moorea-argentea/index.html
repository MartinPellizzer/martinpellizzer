Moorea Argentea

Moorea argentea is a rare and striking plant native to the island of Moorea in French Polynesia. It is known for its large, silvery-white leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests. Due to its unique appearance and limited habitat, it is considered a vulnerable species. Its beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
