Mostuea amabilis

Mostuea amabilis, commonly known as the pink dwarf lily, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Alstroemeriaceae and is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinct, pointed shape. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often forming a basal rosette.
