Mammillaria rhodantha var. odieriana

Mammillaria rhodantha var. odieriana, commonly known as the Odiernan Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem covered in white to pale pink tubercles, which are adorned with short, white bristles. The plant produces small, vibrant pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
