Miconia nigripes

Miconia nigripes, commonly known as black-leaved miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its dark, glossy foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces elliptical, woody fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Miconia nigripes is notable for its ornamental value and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
