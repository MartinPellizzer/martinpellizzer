Myrcia goyazensis var. latifolia

Myrcia goyazensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests and is often found in shaded, moist environments. It produces small, dark blue to black berries that are edible but have a strong, spicy flavor.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
