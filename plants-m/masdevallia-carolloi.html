Masdevallia carolloi

Masdevallia carolloi, commonly known as the Carollo's masdevallia, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and intricate, often fragrant floral structures. The plant typically produces small, brightly colored flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in high humidity and requires specific light conditions to flourish.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts who provide precise environmental controls.
