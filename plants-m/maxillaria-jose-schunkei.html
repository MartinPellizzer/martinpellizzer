Maxillaria jose-schunkei

Maxillaria jose-schunkei, commonly known as the Schunke's maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a sympodial orchid with a pseudobulbous structure, featuring a short, thick stem that supports the plant's growth. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

This species is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
