Magnolia xianianhei

Magnolia xianianhei, commonly known as the Xianianhei magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
