Minuartia minutiflora

Minuartia minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf rock cress, is a small, mat-forming perennial plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

The plant typically grows in crevices and on exposed rock surfaces, where it thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils.
