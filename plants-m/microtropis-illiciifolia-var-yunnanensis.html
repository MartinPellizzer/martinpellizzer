Microtropis illiciifolia var. yunnanensis

Microtropis illiciifolia var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan star anise, is a shrub native to China, particularly in Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its small, aromatic berries that resemble those of the star anise. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within subtropical forests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated margin.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
