Metrosideros queenslandica

Metrosideros queenslandica, commonly known as the Queensland bottle tree, is a striking evergreen tree native to northern Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive swollen trunk, which stores water and helps it survive in arid conditions. The tree produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a glossy green appearance.

The species is also notable for its fast growth rate and adaptability to various soil types.
