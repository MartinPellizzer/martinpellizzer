Machaeranthera canescens var. ambigua

Machaeranthera canescens var. ambigua, commonly known as the gray bluestem, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its grayish, hairy stems and blue to purple flower heads.
