Miconia lenticellata

Miconia lenticellata, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered with small, raised pores called lenticels. It produces showy, clustered inflorescences with pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the year.

Miconia lenticellata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and vibrant floral display.
