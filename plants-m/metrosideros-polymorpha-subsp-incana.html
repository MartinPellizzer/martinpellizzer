Metrosideros polymorpha subsp. incana

Metrosideros polymorpha subsp.

incana, commonly known as the silver-leafed ʻōhiʻa, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is characterized by its silvery-gray, fuzzy leaves that distinguish it from other ʻōhiʻa subspecies.
