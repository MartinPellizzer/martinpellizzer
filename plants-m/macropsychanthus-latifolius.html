Macropsychanthus latifolius

Macropsychanthus latifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf macropsychanthus, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to gardens and ecological restoration projects.

The species is also noted for its adaptability to various environmental conditions.
