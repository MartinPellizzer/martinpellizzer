Melodinus chinensis

Melodinus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
