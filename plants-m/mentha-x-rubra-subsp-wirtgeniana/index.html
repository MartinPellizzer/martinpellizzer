Mentha × Rubra Subsp. Wirtgeniana

Mentha × rubra subsp. wirtgeniana is a unique hybrid mint known for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties. This subspecies is a cross between Mentha × rubra and Mentha × piperita, combining traits from both parent plants. It is commonly used in herbal remedies and aromatherapy due to its refreshing scent and therapeutic benefits. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its versatility makes it a popular choice for both gardeners and herbalists alike.
