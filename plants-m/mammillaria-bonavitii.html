Mammillaria bonavitii

Mammillaria bonavitii, commonly known as the Bonaventura's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant typically features white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
