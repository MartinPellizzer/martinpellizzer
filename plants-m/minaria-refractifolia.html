Minaria refractifolia

Minaria refractifolia, commonly known as the glassy leaf minaria, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that give it its common name. Native to tropical and subtropical regions, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color.

Its unique leaf structure and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.
