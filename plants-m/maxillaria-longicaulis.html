Maxillaria longicaulis

Maxillaria longicaulis, commonly known as the long-stemmed maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright, often branched stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria longicaulis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
