Montbretia tenuiflora

Montbretia tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered montbretia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, grass-like stems that can grow up to 1.5 meters in height, adorned with bright orange or yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens in Mediterranean climates.

Due to its showy blooms and ease of cultivation, it is often used in ornamental horticulture.
