Myosotis sparsiflora subsp. propinqua

Myosotis sparsiflora subsp.

propinqua, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. It features slender, upright stems that bear small, delicate blue or violet flowers arranged in loose clusters.

The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its charming, sky-blue blooms.
