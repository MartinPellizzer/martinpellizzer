Myosotis discolor subsp. rosmatina

Myosotis discolor subsp.

rosmatina, commonly known as the Rosmatina forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. It is characterized by its delicate blue to violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves.
