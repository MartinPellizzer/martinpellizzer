Macroditassa lagoensis var. cucullata

Macroditassa lagoensis var. cucullata, commonly known as the Lake Lagoon bellflower, is a rare and specialized plant native to specific wetland habitats in South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant thrives in nutrient-rich, moist soils and is often found in marshy areas near water bodies. Its leaves are broad and slightly hairy, adapted to withstand wet conditions.
