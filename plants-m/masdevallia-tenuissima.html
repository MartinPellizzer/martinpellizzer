Masdevallia tenuissima

Masdevallia tenuissima, commonly known as the slender leafling orchid, is a rare and delicate epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and intricate, often fragrant flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, typically growing on tree branches or rock surfaces. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to habitat destruction, it is also considered endangered in some regions.
