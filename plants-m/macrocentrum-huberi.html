Macrocentrum huberi

Macrocentrum huberi, commonly known as the African violet, is a member of the Gesneriaceae family and is native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from purple to white. The plant typically grows as a compact, bushy herb with a short, thick stem. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular houseplant worldwide.
