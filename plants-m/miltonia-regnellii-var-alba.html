Miltonia regnellii var. alba

Miltonia regnellii var. alba, commonly known as the white Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its elegant, fragrant flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are predominantly white with subtle pink or purple markings, creating a striking contrast.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
