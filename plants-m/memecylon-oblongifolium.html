Memecylon oblongifolium

Memecylon oblongifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf memecylon, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate, and elliptical-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
