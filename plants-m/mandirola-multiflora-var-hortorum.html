Mandirola multiflora var. hortorum

Mandirola multiflora var. hortorum, commonly known as the garden mandirola, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
