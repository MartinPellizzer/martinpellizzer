Melastoma minahassae

Melastoma minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds, aiding in its dispersal in the wild.
