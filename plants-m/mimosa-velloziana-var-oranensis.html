Mimosa velloziana var. oranensis

Mimosa velloziana var. oranensis, commonly known as the Oranensis mimosa, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to dry conditions. It has small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
