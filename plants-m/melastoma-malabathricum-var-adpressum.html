Melastoma malabathricum var. adpressum

Melastoma malabathricum var. adpressum, commonly known as the flat-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, often pink or red flowers. The leaves are typically oval-shaped and arranged in opposite pairs, while the stems are often woody and branched. This variant is distinguished by its flattened, compact growth habit compared to other varieties of the species.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
