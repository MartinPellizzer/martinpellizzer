Marsdenia glomerata

Marsdenia glomerata, commonly known as the clustered Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, Sri Lanka, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, flat seeds.

Marsdenia glomerata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.
