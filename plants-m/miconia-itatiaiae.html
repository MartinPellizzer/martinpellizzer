Miconia itatiaiae

Miconia itatiaiae, commonly known as the Itatiaia miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Atlantic rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by distinctive, woolly, orange-red fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forest growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
