Maerua schinzii

Maerua schinzii, commonly known as the African saltbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, saline soils and can tolerate drought conditions. It plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Its ecological importance makes it a valuable species in desert ecosystems.
