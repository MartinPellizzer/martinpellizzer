Mauranthemum Gaetulum

Mauranthemum gaetulum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Europe. Known for its striking violet-blue petals and unique growth pattern, it thrives in cool, humid environments. This plant is often found in shaded forest understories where it competes for limited light and nutrients. Due to habitat loss and climate change, its population has drastically declined over the past decades. Conservation efforts are now underway to protect and restore this endangered species.
