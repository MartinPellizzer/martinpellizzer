Melocactus melocactoides var. natalensis

Melocactus melocactoides var. natalensis, commonly known as Natal melocactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a ribbed, cylindrical stem with a spiny crown, adapted to arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers during the rainy season, which are followed by small, red fruits. Its slow growth rate and distinctive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
