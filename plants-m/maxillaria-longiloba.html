Maxillaria longiloba

Maxillaria longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its erect, branching stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with pale yellow to white petals and sepals, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria longiloba is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
