Misanteca cymbarum

Misanteca cymbarum, commonly known as the Mexican firecracker, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

The plant produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, often growing in dry, rocky habitats.

Misanteca cymbarum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
