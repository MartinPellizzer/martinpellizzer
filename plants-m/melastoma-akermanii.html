Melastoma akermanii

Melastoma akermanii, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma akermanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
