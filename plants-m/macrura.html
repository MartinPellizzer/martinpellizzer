Macrura

Macrura, commonly known as the giant spiderwort, is a striking plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a robust, upright stem and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
