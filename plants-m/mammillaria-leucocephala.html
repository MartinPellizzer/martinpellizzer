Mammillaria leucocephala

Mammillaria leucocephala, commonly known as the white-headed hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white, appearing in the spring.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
