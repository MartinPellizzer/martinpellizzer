Memecylon pseudogaleatum

Memecylon pseudogaleatum, commonly known as the slender memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
