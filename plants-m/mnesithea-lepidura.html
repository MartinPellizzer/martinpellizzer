Mnesithea lepidura

Mnesithea lepidura, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, pale green to yellowish flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically velvety and have a leathery texture, which contributes to its common name.

Mnesithea lepidura is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ease of care.
