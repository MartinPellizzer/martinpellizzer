Magnolia cyathiformis

Magnolia cyathiformis, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 10 meters in height.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
