Myristica ensifolia

Myristica ensifolia, commonly known as the swordleaf myristica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers that are followed by distinctive, woody fruits. Its seeds are encased in a thick, aromatic aril, which is often used in traditional medicine.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in local ecosystems.
