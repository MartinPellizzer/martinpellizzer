Mammillaria centraliplumosa var. gracilis

Mammillaria centraliplumosa var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a smooth, pale green to blue-gray surface. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has short, spiny areoles and is well adapted to arid environments.

This variety is prized for its compact size and elegant appearance in succulent collections.
