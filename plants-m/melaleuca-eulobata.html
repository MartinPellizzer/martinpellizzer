Melaleuca eulobata

Melaleuca eulobata, commonly known as the slender bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, cylindrical flowers that resemble bottle brushes, typically in shades of red or pink. The plant has aromatic, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

Melaleuca eulobata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture.
