Magnolia burchelliana

Magnolia burchelliana, commonly known as the Burchell's magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate to elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
