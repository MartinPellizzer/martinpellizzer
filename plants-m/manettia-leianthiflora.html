Manettia leianthiflora

Manettia leianthiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf manettia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a dark green upper surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in racemes, and have five white petals with yellow stamens.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
