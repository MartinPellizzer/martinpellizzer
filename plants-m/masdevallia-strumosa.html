Masdevallia strumosa

Masdevallia strumosa, commonly known as the Strobilanthes orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, often with intricate patterns.

Masdevallia strumosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
