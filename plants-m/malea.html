Malea

Malea, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and other similar plants. The plant features showy yellow flowers with a central disc, and it often grows in grasslands and open woodlands. Malea is characterized by its upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
