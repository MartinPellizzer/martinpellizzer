Mimosa rigescens var. foliolosa

Mimosa rigescens var. foliolosa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and delicate pink to white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has a creeping or ascending growth habit and produces small, bean-like pods that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
