Miconia sneidernii

Miconia sneidernii, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia sneidernii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
