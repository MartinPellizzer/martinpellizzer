Miscanthus luzonensis

Miscanthus luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon silver grass, is a perennial grass native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, silver-gray plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as forest edges and grasslands. It has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Miscanthus luzonensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
