Maxillaria gigantea

Maxillaria gigantea, commonly known as the giant maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive dry periods.

The flowers are usually fragrant and come in shades of white, pink, and purple, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
