Muhlenbergia caxamarcensis

Muhlenbergia caxamarcensis, commonly known as the Mexican muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically brown to reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Muhlenbergia caxamarcensis is often found in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.
