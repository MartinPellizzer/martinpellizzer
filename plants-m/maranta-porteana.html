Maranta porteana

Maranta porteana, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The leaves are typically arrow-shaped and display vibrant patterns of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
