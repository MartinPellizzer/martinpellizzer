Myosurus minimus var. interior

Myosurus minimus var. interior, commonly known as the interior lesser mouse-ear.

This small perennial herb is native to the western United States, particularly in areas with moist, rocky soils. It features tiny, hairy leaves and produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to its specific ecological niche.
