Monotes hypoleucus var. hypoleucus

Monotes hypoleucus var. hypoleucus, commonly known as the blue mistflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, blue to purple flower clusters that bloom in late summer and early fall. The plant has slender, branching stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and natural landscapes.
