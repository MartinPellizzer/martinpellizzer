Miconia morii

Miconia morii, commonly known as the Hawaiian miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, violet-pink flowers. It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered an invasive species in some regions.
