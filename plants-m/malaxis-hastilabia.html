Malaxis hastilabia

Malaxis hastilabia, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted for nutrient absorption in poor soils.

Malaxis hastilabia is part of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
