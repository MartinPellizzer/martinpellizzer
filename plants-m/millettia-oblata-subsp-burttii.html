Millettia oblata subsp. burttii

Millettia oblata subsp.

burttii, commonly known as the Burtt's millettia, is a climbing shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers.

The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of many legumes. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
