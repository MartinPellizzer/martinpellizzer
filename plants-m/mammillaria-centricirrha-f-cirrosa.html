Mammillaria centricirrha f. cirrosa

Mammillaria centricirrha f. cirrosa, commonly known as the cirrosa mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem covered in white to pale green tubercles, which are often adorned with delicate, white or pale pink spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its compact size and attractive appearance.

This variety is noted for its slow growth and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.
