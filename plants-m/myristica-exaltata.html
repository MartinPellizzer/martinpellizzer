Myristica exaltata

Myristica exaltata, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody structure and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by reddish berries containing the aromatic seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Its botanical features include a thick, fibrous bark and a dense canopy of dark green foliage.

The tree is also valued for its essential oils and has been historically significant in global trade.
