Maxillaria atrata

Maxillaria atrata, commonly known as the dark-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark violet to almost black petals and sepals, which contrast with its white or pale yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size and bloom during the dry season.

Maxillaria atrata is valued for its striking appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
