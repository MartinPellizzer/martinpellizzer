Momordica tubulosa

Momordica tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant is characterized by its slender, tendriled stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its fruit is a long, slender, green to yellowish gourd with a warty surface.

The plant is valued for its edible fruit, which is used in various traditional cuisines and herbal remedies.
