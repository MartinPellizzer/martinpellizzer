Melicope lasioneura

Melicope lasioneura, commonly known as the narrow-leaf melicope, is a member of the Rutaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red upon maturity. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Melicope lasioneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous cultures.
