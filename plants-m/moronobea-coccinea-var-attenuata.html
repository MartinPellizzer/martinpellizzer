Moronobea coccinea var. attenuata

Moronobea coccinea var. attenuata, commonly known as the red moronobea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
