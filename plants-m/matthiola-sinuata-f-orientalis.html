Matthiola sinuata f. orientalis

Matthiola sinuata f. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features finely divided, silvery-green leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This cultivar is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
