Muhlenbergia solisii

Muhlenbergia solisii, commonly known as the Solisii muhlenbergia, is a species of grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seed heads that resemble miniature clouds. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in native plant gardens.

Muhlenbergia solisii is relatively low maintenance and thrives in well-drained soils.
