Musa yunnanensis

Musa yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan banana, is a species native to southern China.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically smaller and less sweet compared to common bananas. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for cultivation, it is sometimes grown in botanical gardens and research settings.
