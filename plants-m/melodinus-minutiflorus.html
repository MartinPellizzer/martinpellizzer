Melodinus minutiflorus

Melodinus minutiflorus, commonly known as the tiny flowered melodic vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a subtle sweet scent. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, with a moderate size, adapting well to humid environments. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Due to its ornamental value and unique floral characteristics, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
