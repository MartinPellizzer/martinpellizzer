Miconia ceramicarpa var. navioensis

Miconia ceramicarpa var. navioensis, commonly known as Navio's miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
