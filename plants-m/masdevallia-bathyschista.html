Masdevallia bathyschista

Masdevallia bathyschista, commonly known as the deep-sea masdevallia, is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure with long, slender petals and a strikingly colored lip. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific conditions to bloom, including high air humidity and indirect light. Its natural habitat is threatened by deforestation, making conservation efforts crucial for its survival.

Botanically, it is notable for its specialized adaptations to its high-altitude, misty environment.
