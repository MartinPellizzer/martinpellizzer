Mammillaria geminispina

Mammillaria geminispina, commonly known as the twin-spined mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to grayish spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
