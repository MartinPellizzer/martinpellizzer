Mandevilla novocapitalis

Mandevilla novocapitalis, commonly known as the Brazilian mandevilla, is a striking climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow, blooming throughout the growing season. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mandevilla novocapitalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
