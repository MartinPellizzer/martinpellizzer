Myristica nitida

Myristica nitida, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, glossy leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces two types of aromatic seeds: the outer shell, known as mace, and the inner kernel, which is the spice nutmeg. Myristica nitida thrives in humid, shaded environments and is cultivated for its valuable aromatic products.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and commercial contexts.
