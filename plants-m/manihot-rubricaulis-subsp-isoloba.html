Manihot rubricaulis subsp. isoloba

Manihot rubricaulis subsp.

isoloba, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowleaf, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
