Millettia pulchra var. pulchra

Millettia pulchra var. pulchra, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, woody pods that contain flat, winged seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
