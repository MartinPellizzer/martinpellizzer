Magnolia sulawesiana

Magnolia sulawesiana, commonly known as the Sulawesi magnolia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
