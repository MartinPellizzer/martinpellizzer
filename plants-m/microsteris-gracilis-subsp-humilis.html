Microsteris gracilis subsp. humilis

Microsteris gracilis subsp.

humilis, commonly known as the slender microsteris, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and narrow leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks. Its subspecies status highlights subtle differences in morphology compared to the broader species.

The botanical interest in this plant lies in its unique ecological adaptations and potential medicinal properties.
