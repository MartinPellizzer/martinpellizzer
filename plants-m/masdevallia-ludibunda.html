Masdevallia ludibunda

Masdevallia ludibunda, commonly known as the laughing masdevallia, is a tropical orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often brightly colored flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its intricate floral structure includes showy spathes and spurred petals, which are adapted for pollination by specific insects.

Masdevallia ludibunda is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively compact size.
