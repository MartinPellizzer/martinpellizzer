Microtropis platyphylla

Microtropis platyphylla, commonly known as the broad-leaf microtropis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, typically in shades of yellow or white. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
