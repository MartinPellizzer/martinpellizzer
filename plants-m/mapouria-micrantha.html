Mapouria micrantha

Mapouria micrantha, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in clusters. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are usually arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Mapouria micrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
