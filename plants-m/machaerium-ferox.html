Machaerium ferox

Machaerium ferox, commonly known as the feisty machaerium, is a tropical shrub native to regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, thorny growth habit. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three small, ovate leaflets.

Machaerium ferox is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in dry, sunny environments.
