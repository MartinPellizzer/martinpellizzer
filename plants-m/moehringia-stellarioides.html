Moehringia stellarioides

Moehringia stellarioides, commonly known as starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the northern hemisphere.

It typically features small, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring, often in shades of white or pale purple. The plant has slender, branched stems and small, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
