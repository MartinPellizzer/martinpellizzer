Micropuntia gracilicylindrica

Micropuntia gracilicylindrica, commonly known as the slender column cactus, is a species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing, cylindrical cactus with a ribbed structure that helps it store water efficiently. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking visual element to its desert habitat. Its stems are covered in small, white spines that provide protection from herbivores.

This cactus is well adapted to arid environments, showcasing typical xerophytic features.
