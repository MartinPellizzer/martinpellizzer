Murraya lobata

Murraya lobata, commonly known as the flame tree or Indian flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pinnately compound leaves and clusters of fragrant, orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible but not widely consumed by humans. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Murraya lobata is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
