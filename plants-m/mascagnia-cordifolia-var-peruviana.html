Mascagnia cordifolia var. peruviana

Mascagnia cordifolia var. peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian heartleaf, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Peru.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun.
