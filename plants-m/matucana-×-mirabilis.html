Matucana × mirabilis

Matucana × mirabilis, commonly known as the golden torch cactus, is a hybrid succulent native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, yellow to orange spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water once established.

Due to its striking appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and as a decorative plant in arid landscapes.
