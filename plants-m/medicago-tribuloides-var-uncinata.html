Medicago tribuloides var. uncinata

Medicago tribuloides var. uncinata, commonly known as goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its spiny pods that contain elliptical seeds with hooked tips, aiding in seed dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This species is valued for its medicinal properties and historical use in traditional herbal remedies.
