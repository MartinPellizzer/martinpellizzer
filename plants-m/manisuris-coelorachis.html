Manisuris coelorachis

Manisuris coelorachis, commonly known as the golden star lily, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Manisuris coelorachis is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is a popular choice in shaded garden settings.
