Microloma spinosum

Microloma spinosum, commonly known as the spinous bluemink, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, spiny stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
