Mentha × wuerlii

Mentha × wuerlii, commonly known as pineapple mint, is a hybrid mint plant that combines traits from spearmint and watermint.

It is characterized by its aromatic, serrated leaves that emit a fresh, minty scent. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Mentha × wuerlii is often used in culinary applications and as an ornamental plant in gardens.
