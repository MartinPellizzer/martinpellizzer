Mikania salicifolia

Mikania salicifolia, commonly known as the silky leaf morning glory, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves and showy blue-violet flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in some areas.
