Mimulus assamicus

Mimulus assamicus, commonly known as the Assam monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow to orange flowers, which are typically tubular in shape and attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
