Maireana astrotricha

Maireana astrotricha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed spinifex, is a perennial shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, wiry foliage and small, needle-like leaves that help reduce water loss in harsh environments. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are typically visited by insects and birds. It is well adapted to drought and can thrive in poor soil conditions.

Maireana astrotricha plays an important role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
