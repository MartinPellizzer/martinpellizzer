Marsdenia roylei

Marsdenia roylei, commonly known as the Himalayan Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Indian subcontinent, including Nepal, Bhutan, and northern India. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, typically blooming during the summer months.

The plant is often found growing in rocky or forested areas at high altitudes.
