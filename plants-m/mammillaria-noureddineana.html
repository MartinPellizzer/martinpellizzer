Mammillaria noureddineana

Mammillaria noureddineana, commonly known as the Noureddine's bunny ear cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
