Mentha × niliaca var. rosanii

Mentha × niliaca var. rosanii, commonly known as rosemary mint, is a hybrid plant that combines traits of rosemary and mint.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly minty scent and is often used in herbal teas and culinary applications. The plant typically grows as a low, sprawling shrub with small, oval-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
