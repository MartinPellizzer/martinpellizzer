Miconia camposnovaesii

Miconia camposnovaesii, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 50 cm in diameter.
