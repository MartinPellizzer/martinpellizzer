Malapoenna tomentosa

Malapoenna tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly malapoenna, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Malapoenna tomentosa is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique texture and aesthetic appeal.
