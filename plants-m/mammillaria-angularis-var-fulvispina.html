Mammillaria angularis var. fulvispina

Mammillaria angularis var. fulvispina, commonly known as the angular hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features prominent, angular ribs that are covered in white to grayish spines, giving it a rugged, textured look. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is native to the arid regions of Mexico and thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This variety is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique morphology and relatively easy care requirements.
