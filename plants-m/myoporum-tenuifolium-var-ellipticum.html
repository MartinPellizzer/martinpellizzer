Myoporum tenuifolium var. ellipticum

Myoporum tenuifolium var. ellipticum, commonly known as narrow-leaved myoporum, is a shrub native to coastal regions of southern Australia.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to sandy soils and salt-laden environments, making it a hardy choice for coastal landscaping.

Its ornamental value and resilience make it a popular plant in both natural and cultivated settings.
