Metrosideros elegans

Metrosideros elegans, commonly known as the Tahitian beauty or the flame tree, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that produces striking red or orange flowers during the warmer months. The plant has smooth, reddish bark and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy in appearance. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are a key feature in its ornamental value.

The species is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is found in tropical and subtropical regions.
