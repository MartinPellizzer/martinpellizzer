Masdevallia lineolata

Masdevallia lineolata, commonly known as the lineolated masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful flower spikes that bear multiple small, elongated blooms. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and indirect light.

Masdevallia lineolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
