Miconia media subsp. borealis

Miconia media subsp.

borealis, commonly known as the northern miconia, is a shrub or small tree native to the temperate regions of North America. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streamside areas.

The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered, and usually white or pale pink, appearing in late spring to early summer.

The species is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
