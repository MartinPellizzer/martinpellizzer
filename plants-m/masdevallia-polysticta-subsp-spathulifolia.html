Masdevallia polysticta subsp. spathulifolia

Masdevallia polysticta subsp.

spathulifolia, commonly known as the Spatulate-leaf Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America. It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its distinctive, spatulate-shaped leaves.

The plant produces intricate, showy inflorescences with flowers that often display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique appearance and fragrance.
