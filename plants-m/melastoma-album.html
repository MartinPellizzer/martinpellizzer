Melastoma album

Melastoma album, commonly known as the white melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas.
