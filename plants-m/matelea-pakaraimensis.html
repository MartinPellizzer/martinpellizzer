Matelea pakaraimensis

Matelea pakaraimensis, commonly known as the Pakaraima moonflower, is a flowering plant native to the Guyana Highlands.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other flowering plants with toxic properties. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.
