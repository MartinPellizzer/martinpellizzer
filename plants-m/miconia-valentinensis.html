Miconia valentinensis

Miconia valentinensis, commonly known as the Valentine's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its rapid growth rate and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments make it a common ornamental plant in tropical gardens.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions due to its aggressive spreading habit.
