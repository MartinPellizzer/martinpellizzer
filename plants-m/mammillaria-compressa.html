Mammillaria compressa

Mammillaria compressa, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in prominent, white spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in early summer.

Mammillaria compressa is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
