Mussaenda kanehirae

Mussaenda kanehirae, commonly known as the Kauai Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has compound leaves with elliptical or ovate leaflets, and it typically grows as a shrub or small tree. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
