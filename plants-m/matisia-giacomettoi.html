Matisia giacomettoi

Matisia giacomettoi, commonly known as the Giacometto's matisia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in humid lowland forests and can grow up to 20 meters in height.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
