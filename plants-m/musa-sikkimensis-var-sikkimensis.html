Musa sikkimensis var. sikkimensis

Musa sikkimensis var. sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim banana, is a tropical plant native to the eastern Himalayas.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudostem formed by the tightly packed leaves. The plant produces large, elongated berries that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow when mature. It is characterized by its high leaf surface area, which aids in photosynthesis and water retention.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
