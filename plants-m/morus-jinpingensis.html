Morus jinpingensis

Morus jinpingensis, commonly known as the Jinping mulberry, is a rare species native to China.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive leaves and fruiting habits. The plant typically grows in mountainous regions and prefers temperate climates. Its leaves are heart-shaped with a smooth texture, and the fruit is small and aggregate.

Due to its limited distribution and unique features, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
