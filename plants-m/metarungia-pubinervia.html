Metarungia pubinervia

Metarungia pubinervia, commonly known as the African nettle, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture and prominent veins. Its inflorescence consists of small, greenish-white flowers arranged in spikes.

The plant is known for its stinging hairs, which can cause irritation upon contact with the skin.
