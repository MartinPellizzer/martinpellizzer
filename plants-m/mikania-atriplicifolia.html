Mikania atriplicifolia

Mikania atriplicifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf Mikania, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a common sight in disturbed areas. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture, while its small yellow flowers appear in clusters.

Mikania atriplicifolia is also noted for its role in ecological systems, providing food for various insects and birds.
