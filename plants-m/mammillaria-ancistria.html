Mammillaria ancistria

Mammillaria ancistria, commonly known as the Mexican hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to grayish tubercles. The plant typically produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
