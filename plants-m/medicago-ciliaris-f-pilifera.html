Medicago ciliaris f. pilifera

Medicago ciliaris f. pilifera, commonly known as the silky medic, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in racemes, which are typically borne on the upper parts of the stems. Its hairy leaves and stems give it a distinctive, silvery appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats such as fields and roadsides.
