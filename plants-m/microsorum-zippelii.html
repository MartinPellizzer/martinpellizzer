Microsorum zippelii

Microsorum zippelii, commonly known as the Zippel's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically divided into small, lance-shaped segments. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded, muddy substrates. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, forming dense mats that help stabilize the sediment.

Microsorum zippelii is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.
