Montia verna

Montia verna, commonly known as spring beauty, is a small flowering plant native to North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators like bees and flies.

Montia verna is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
