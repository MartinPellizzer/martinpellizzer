Mammillaria candida var. estanzuelensis

Mammillaria candida var. estanzuelensis, commonly known as the Estanzuelensis Cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem that is typically pale green to white in color. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
