Melhania phillipsiae

Melhania phillipsiae, commonly known as the Philippine orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphytic orchid, attaching itself to trees in tropical rainforests. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with red or purple markings, and it blooms during the wet season.

Melhania phillipsiae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
