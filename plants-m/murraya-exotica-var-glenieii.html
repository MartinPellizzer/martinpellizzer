Murraya exotica var. glenieii

Murraya exotica var. glenieii, commonly known as the Glengarry hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, red berries that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
