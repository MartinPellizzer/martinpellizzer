Melastoma montanum

Melastoma montanum, commonly known as the mountain melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant produces striking, showy flowers that are usually red or pink, with five petals and prominent stamens. Its leaves are ovate and have a glossy appearance, and it is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates.

Melastoma montanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
