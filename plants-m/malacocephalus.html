Malacocephalus

Malacocephalus, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is native to regions in Africa, Asia, and the Americas.
