Mammillaria straminea

Mammillaria straminea, commonly known as the pale mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and white or pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is a low-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
