Marrubium pseudoalysson

Marrubium pseudoalysson, commonly known as false horehound, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the plantain family, Plantaginaceae, and is characterized by its hairy, basal leaves and small, pale blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
