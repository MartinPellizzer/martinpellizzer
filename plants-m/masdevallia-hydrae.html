Masdevallia hydrae

Masdevallia hydrae, commonly known as the Hydrae Masdevallia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with intricate, often colorful flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to thrive. Its flowers are characterized by their elongated, spurred petals and a distinctive, often fragrant scent.

Masdevallia hydrae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
