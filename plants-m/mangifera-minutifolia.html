Mangifera minutifolia

Mangifera minutifolia, commonly known as the dwarf mango, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branched trunk and dense, dark green foliage. The leaves are elliptical, with a leathery texture and a pointed tip. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a small, round mango with a thin skin and sweet, aromatic pulp.
