Mammillaria patonii var. sinalensis

Mammillaria patonii var. sinalensis, commonly known as the Sinalense mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, tuberculate areoles, with short, pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
