Minuartia nuttallii var. fragilis

Minuartia nuttallii var. fragilis, commonly known as the fragile sandflower, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arid and rocky environments.

The plant is adapted to survive in harsh conditions with minimal water, featuring a deep root system and waxy leaf surfaces to reduce water loss.
