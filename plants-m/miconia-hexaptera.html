Miconia hexaptera

Miconia hexaptera, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. Miconia hexaptera is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
