Meliosma petiolaris

Meliosma petiolaris, commonly known as the narrow-leaf meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are usually less than 2 inches long. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by winged fruits, typical of the genus Meliosma.
