Mayepea macrocarpa

Mayepea macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant mayepea, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, typically displaying vibrant colors such as red, orange, or yellow. The plant has a woody stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along the branches. Its fruit is a large, woody capsule that splits open to release numerous seeds.

Mayepea macrocarpa plays an important ecological role by providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
