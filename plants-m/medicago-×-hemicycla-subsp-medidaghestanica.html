Medicago × hemicycla subsp. medidaghestanica

Medicago × hemicycla subsp.

medidaghestanica, commonly known as the Caucasian vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Caucasus region. It features trifoliate leaves with oval leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters.

The plant is characterized by its robust growth habit and ability to thrive in dry, rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

This subspecies is valued for its forage quality and ecological role in nitrogen fixation.
