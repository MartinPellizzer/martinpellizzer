Mangifera taipa

Mangifera taipa, commonly known as the taipa mango, is a species of flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces clusters of yellow-orange fruits with a sweet, fibrous pulp. The fruit is often used in local cuisines and traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ecological and agricultural studies.
