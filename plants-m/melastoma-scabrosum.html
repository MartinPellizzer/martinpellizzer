Melastoma scabrosum

Melastoma scabrosum, commonly known as the rough melastoma, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and dense foliage. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptic, with a rough, hairy texture on the upper surface.

Melastoma scabrosum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
