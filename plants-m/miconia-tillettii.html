Miconia tillettii

Miconia tillettii, commonly known as the Tilt's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It often grows in moist, shaded environments within lowland forests.
