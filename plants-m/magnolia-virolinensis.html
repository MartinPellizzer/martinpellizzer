Magnolia virolinensis

Magnolia virolinensis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the yellow magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive, recurved petal structure.

Magnolia virolinensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
