Maranta sanderiana

Maranta sanderiana, commonly known as the silver sword or sandera's arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery wax, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color.

Maranta sanderiana is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique leaf patterns and ease of care.
