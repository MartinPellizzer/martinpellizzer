Mahonia jingxiensis

Mahonia jingxiensis, commonly known as the Jingxi mahonia, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring, followed by red berries in the autumn. It thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.

Mahonia jingxiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
