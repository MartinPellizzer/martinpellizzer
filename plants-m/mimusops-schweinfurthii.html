Mimusops schweinfurthii

Mimusops schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by yellow, fleshy fruits. The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
