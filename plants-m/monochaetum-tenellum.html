Monochaetum tenellum

Monochaetum tenellum, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a species of flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is native to Australia and is typically found in wetland or swampy habitats. The plant is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, inconspicuous flowers. It plays a role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
