Microlicia acerosa

Microlicia acerosa, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by winged fruits that disperse its seeds.

Microlicia acerosa is often found in dry savannas and woodlands, where it plays an important ecological role.
