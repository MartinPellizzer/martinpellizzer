Medicago × blancheana subsp. bonarotiana

Medicago × blancheana subsp.

bonarotiana, commonly known as the Bonarotian goat's rue, is a hybrid legume native to the Mediterranean region. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, blue to purple pea-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial species with a deep taproot system, allowing it to survive in arid conditions.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
