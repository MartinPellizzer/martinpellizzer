Microstachys crotonoides

Microstachys crotonoides, commonly known as the red-flowered croton, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround small yellow flowers, creating a striking visual display. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Microstachys crotonoides is relatively drought-tolerant once established, making it suitable for gardens in tropical and subtropical regions.
