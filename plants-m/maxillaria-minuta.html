Maxillaria minuta

Maxillaria minuta, commonly known as the miniature maxillaria, is a small orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Maxillaria minuta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
