Melastoma warrineri

Melastoma warrineri, commonly known as the Warriner's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often violet or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, preferring well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, with a glossy surface.

Melastoma warrineri is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
