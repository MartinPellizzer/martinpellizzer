Monanthotaxis bokoli

Monanthotaxis bokoli, commonly known as the Bokoli tree, is a species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows to a height of 10-15 meters. The plant features large, simple, and elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in panicles, often with a white or pale yellow color.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
