Melastoma patens

Melastoma patens, commonly known as the tropical wild strawberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are red when mature, often containing several seeds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

The flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals and numerous stamens, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
