Matricaria karjaginii

Matricaria karjaginii, commonly known as Karjagin's chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small yellow flowers that develop into white, fluffy seeds, typical of the genus Matricaria. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species in its native habitat.
