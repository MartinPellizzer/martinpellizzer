Mentha × rotundifolia var. meduanensis

Mentha × rotundifolia var. meduanensis, commonly known as the Meduanese round-leaf mint, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its rounded, slightly toothed leaves and aromatic, often menthol-like scent. This variety is native to parts of Europe and Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks.

The plant is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other mint species.
