Microstigma

Microstigma, commonly known as the small-stigma plant, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, clustered stigmas that are often difficult to see without magnification. The plant typically grows in temperate regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or pale green, and they bloom in the spring.

Microstigma is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
