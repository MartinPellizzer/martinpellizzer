Medinilla whitfordii

Medinilla whitfordii, commonly known as the white medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. Medinilla whitfordii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and greenhouses.
