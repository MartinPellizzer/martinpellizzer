Marattia caudata

Marattia caudata, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach heights of up to 6 meters. The plant has a distinctive, branched rhizome system that supports its massive foliage. Its leaves are pinnately divided and often have a leathery texture.

Marattia caudata thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
