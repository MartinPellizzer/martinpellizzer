Machilus macranthus var. brevifolius

Machilus macranthus var. brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese woodbine, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically less than 5 cm in length. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that matures to a dark purple color.
