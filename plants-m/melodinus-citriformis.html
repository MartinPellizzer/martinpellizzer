Melodinus citriformis

Melodinus citriformis, commonly known as the citron melodine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, citrus-scented leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces oval, citrus-like fruits that are typically harvested when ripe. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and fragrance, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
