Myoporum euphrasioides

Myoporum euphrasioides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy green. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale blue flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or gravelly soils.

Myoporum euphrasioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and attractive foliage.
