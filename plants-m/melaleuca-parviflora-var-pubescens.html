Melaleuca parviflora var. pubescens

Melaleuca parviflora var. pubescens, commonly known as the silky tea tree, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers in clusters, which bloom from late summer to early autumn. Its aromatic foliage and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adaptable to a range of environmental conditions.
