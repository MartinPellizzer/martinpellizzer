Minthostachys mollis var. mollis

Minthostachys mollis var. mollis, commonly known as the slender mint, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, hairy leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their cooling and digestive properties.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and fragrance.
