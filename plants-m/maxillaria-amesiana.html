Maxillaria amesiana

Maxillaria amesiana, commonly known as the Amesian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its inflorescence consists of a single raceme with several flowers. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded, moist habitats.

Maxillaria amesiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
