Mentha × dumetorum subsp. ayassei

Mentha × dumetorum subsp.

ayassei, commonly known as "chamomile mint," is a hybrid species within the mint family (Lamiaceae). It is characterized by its aromatic, serrated leaves and small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant typically grows in moist, temperate regions and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments. It is valued for its medicinal properties, including anti-inflammatory and calming effects.

Its essential oils are used in aromatherapy and traditional medicine.
