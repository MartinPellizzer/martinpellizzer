Machilus obovatifolius var. obovatifolius

Machilus obovatifolius var. obovatifolius, commonly known as the broad-leaved machilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the regions of Yunnan and Sichuan. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible berries.

This plant is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
