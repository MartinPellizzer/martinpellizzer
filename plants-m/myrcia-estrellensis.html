Myrcia estrellensis

Myrcia estrellensis, commonly known as the Estrellensis Myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of eastern Brazil, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and leathery, elliptical leaves. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature.

Myrcia estrellensis is often found in shaded, moist environments within its native habitat.
