Margarocarpus laevis

Margarocarpus laevis, commonly known as the smooth margarocarpus, is a member of the family Arecaceae, which includes palms.

It is characterized by its slender, unbranched stem and pinnately compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a small, round drupe that contains a single seed.

This species is native to tropical regions and thrives in humid, well-drained soils.
