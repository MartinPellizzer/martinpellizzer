Millettia goetzeana

Millettia goetzeana, commonly known as the purple-veined senna, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with prominent purple veins and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated pods containing small, dark seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
