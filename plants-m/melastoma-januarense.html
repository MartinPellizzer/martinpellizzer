Melastoma januarense

Melastoma januarense, commonly known as the Christmas melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically blooms during the winter months, producing bright red or pink bracts that attract pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
