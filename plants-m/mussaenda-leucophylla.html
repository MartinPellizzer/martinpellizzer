Mussaenda leucophylla

Mussaenda leucophylla, commonly known as white-leaf mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
