Myosotis refracta subsp. refracta

Myosotis refracta subsp.

refracta, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to parts of Asia and Europe. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender, upright stems covered in small, blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
