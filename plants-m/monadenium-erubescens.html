Monadenium erubescens

Monadenium erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered monadenium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces vibrant pink to red flowers. The plant has small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is largely due to its striking floral display.
