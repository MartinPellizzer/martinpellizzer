Mammillaria fittkaui subsp. limonensis

Mammillaria fittkaui subsp.

limonensis, commonly known as the yellow mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and cylindrical, spiny stems.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun exposure. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

This subspecies is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its striking appearance and compact size.
