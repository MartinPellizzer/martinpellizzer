Machilus gongshanensis

Machilus gongshanensis, commonly known as the Gongshan machilus, is a rare evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant is typically found in subtropical forests at elevations above 1,500 meters. Its fruits are small drupes that contain a single seed, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife.
