Melastoma nabotan

Melastoma nabotan, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, often with a distinctive white or yellow center. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Melastoma nabotan is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
