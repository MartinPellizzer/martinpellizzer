Matelea calchaquina

Matelea calchaquina, commonly known as the yellow nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom at night. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. Its leaves are usually heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

Matelea calchaquina is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
