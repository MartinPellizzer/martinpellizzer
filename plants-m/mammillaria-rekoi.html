Mammillaria rekoi

Mammillaria rekoi, commonly known as the Reko mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and is covered in white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
