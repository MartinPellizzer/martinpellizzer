Miconia hutchisonii

Miconia hutchisonii, commonly known as the red miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, bright red, fleshy fruits.

Miconia hutchisonii is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments, making it a common sight in tropical rainforests.
