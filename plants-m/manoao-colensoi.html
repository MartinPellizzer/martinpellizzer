Manoao colensoi

Manoao colensoi, commonly known as Colenso's manuka, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant is typically found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils. It has a compact growth habit and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value.

The leaves are evergreen and have a distinctive, fragrant aroma.
