Miconia macrodon

Miconia macrodon, commonly known as the giant miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers with prominent, fleshy petals. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is considered a problematic species in some regions.
