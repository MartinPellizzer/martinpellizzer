Mammillaria elongata var. viperina

Mammillaria elongata var. viperina, commonly known as the viper's tail cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 30 cm in height, adorned with white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
