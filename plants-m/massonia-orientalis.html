Massonia orientalis

Massonia orientalis, commonly known as the oriental massonia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Massonia orientalis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
