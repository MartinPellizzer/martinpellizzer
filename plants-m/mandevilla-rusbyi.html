Mandevilla rusbyi

Mandevilla rusbyi, commonly known as the Rusby's mandevilla, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla rusbyi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
