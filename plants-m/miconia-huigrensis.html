Miconia huigrensis

Miconia huigrensis, commonly known as the Huigren's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, broad leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences that produce small, white or pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
