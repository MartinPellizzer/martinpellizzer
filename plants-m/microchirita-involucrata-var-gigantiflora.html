Microchirita involucrata var. gigantiflora

Microchirita involucrata var. gigantiflora, commonly known as the giant-flowered microchirita, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, such as forest understories, where it thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture.
