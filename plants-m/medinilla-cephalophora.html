Medinilla cephalophora

Medinilla cephalophora, commonly known as the helmet medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its distinctive, helmet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has broad, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Medinilla cephalophora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display.
