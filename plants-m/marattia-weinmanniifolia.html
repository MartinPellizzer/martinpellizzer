Marattia weinmanniifolia

Marattia weinmanniifolia, commonly known as the Weinmannia fern, is a tropical evergreen fern belonging to the family Marattiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, compound leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as tropical rainforests. Its fronds are often divided into numerous pinnae, giving it a lush, feathery appearance.

Marattia weinmanniifolia is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
