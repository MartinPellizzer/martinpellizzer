Muhlenbergia romaschenkoi

Muhlenbergia romaschenkoi, commonly known as the Mexican feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to Mexico.

It features slender, arching stems that produce feathery plumes of light green to silver-blue flowers. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Muhlenbergia romaschenkoi is valued for its ornamental beauty and low maintenance requirements in landscaping.
