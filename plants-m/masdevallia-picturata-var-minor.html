Masdevallia picturata var. minor

Masdevallia picturata var. minor, commonly known as the miniature picturata masdevallia, is a rare orchid species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, intricate floral patterns that resemble painted designs. The plant typically produces small, colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, often with contrasting markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific cultivation needs, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
