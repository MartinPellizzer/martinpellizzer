Mastixia kaniensis subsp. kaniensis

Mastixia kaniensis subsp.

kaniensis, commonly known as the Kaniensis mastixia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its slender, woody stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green in color.

The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, fleshy, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
