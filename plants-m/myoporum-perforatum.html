Myoporum perforatum

Myoporum perforatum, commonly known as the New Zealand myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, ovate leaves with a distinctive perforated or fenestrated texture. The plant produces clusters of pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland areas.

Myoporum perforatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
