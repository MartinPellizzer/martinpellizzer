Mallotus atrovirens

Mallotus atrovirens, commonly known as the black-leaf mallot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody, spiny fruits containing seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
