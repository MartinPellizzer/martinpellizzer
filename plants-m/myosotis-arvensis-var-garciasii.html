Myosotis arvensis var. garciasii

Myosotis arvensis var. garciasii, commonly known as the Garciasii forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Andean region of South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant features slender stems and produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
