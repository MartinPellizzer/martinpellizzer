Mosannona discolor

Mosannona discolor, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a striking appearance. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture making.
