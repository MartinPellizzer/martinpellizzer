Mesembryanthemum tigrinum

Mesembryanthemum tigrinum, commonly known as the Tiger's Eye or Tiger's Eye Flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves, making it drought-tolerant. It typically blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red, with a striking, eye-like appearance in the center of its flowers.

Mesembryanthemum tigrinum is popular as a decorative plant due to its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
