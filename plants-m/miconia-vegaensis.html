Miconia vegaensis

Miconia vegaensis, commonly known as the Vega's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a smooth surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia vegaensis is often found in humid, lowland areas and is adapted to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
