Mikania campos-portoana

Mikania campos-portoana, commonly known as the Brazilian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Mikania campos-portoana is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as riverbanks and forest edges.
