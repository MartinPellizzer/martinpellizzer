Memecylon ambiguum

Memecylon ambiguum, commonly known as the ambiguo memecylon, is a tropical plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

The flowers are small, clustered, and usually pink or white, with showy bracts that attract pollinators.
