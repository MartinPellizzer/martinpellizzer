Medicago prostrata subsp. vukovicii

Medicago prostrata subsp.

vukovicii, commonly known as the prostrate sainfoin, is a low-growing perennial legume native to the Balkans. It features small, yellow pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves, typical of the Fabaceae family.

This subspecies is characterized by its prostrate growth habit, often spreading along the ground. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential as a forage crop.
