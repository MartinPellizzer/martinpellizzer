Matthiola longipetala f. oasicola

Matthiola longipetala f. oasicola, commonly known as the alpine stock, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with characteristic cruciform flowers.
