Muhlenbergia thurberi

Muhlenbergia thurberi, commonly known as Thurber's muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-blue foliage that adds visual interest to landscapes. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Muhlenbergia thurberi is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
