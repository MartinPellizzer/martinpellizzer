Mikania brachypoda

Mikania brachypoda, commonly known as creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a perennial vine characterized by its rapid growth and extensive root system. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Mikania brachypoda is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its adaptability to different environmental conditions.
