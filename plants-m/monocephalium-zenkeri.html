Monocephalium zenkeri

Monocephalium zenkeri, commonly known as the African buttercup, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy yellow flowers with a central disc, and its leaves are typically alternate and lobed. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
