Musa proboscidea

Musa proboscidea, commonly known as the giant banana plant, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of flowers that develop into elongated, fleshy fruits. It is closely related to other banana species but is distinguished by its unique morphology and growth habits.

Due to its size and appearance, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
