Moraea luteoalba

Moraea luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-flowered moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

Moraea luteoalba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
