Markhamia puberula

Markhamia puberula, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showing adaptability to dry conditions. It has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Markhamia puberula is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
