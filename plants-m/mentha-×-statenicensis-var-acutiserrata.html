Mentha × statenicensis var. acutiserrata

Mentha × statenicensis var. acutiserrata, commonly known as the acute-toothed mint, is a hybrid species of mint native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its narrow, serrated leaves that are often sharply toothed, giving it its botanical name. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in terminal spikes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and is used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
