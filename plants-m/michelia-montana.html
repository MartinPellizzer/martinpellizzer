Michelia montana

Michelia montana, commonly known as the mountain michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurel and avocado plants. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Michelia montana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
