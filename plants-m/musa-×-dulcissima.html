Musa × dulcissima

Musa × dulcissima, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and produces edible, elongated fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its fruit, which has a sweet and creamy texture.

The plant's rapid growth and high yield make it a popular choice for commercial cultivation.
