Martensianthus breviflorus

Martensianthus breviflorus, commonly known as the narrow-flowered martensia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its small, tubular flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
