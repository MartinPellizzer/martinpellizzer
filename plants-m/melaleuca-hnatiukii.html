Melaleuca hnatiukii

Melaleuca hnatiukii, commonly known as the Hnatiuk's melaleuca, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to eastern Australia, where it typically grows in coastal or sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in heathlands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
