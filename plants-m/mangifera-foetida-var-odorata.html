Mangifera foetida var. odorata

Mangifera foetida var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant mango.

This variant is characterized by its distinctive aromatic fragrance, which is more pronounced than that of typical mangoes. The plant produces small, round fruits with a thick, fibrous pulp and a sweet, aromatic flavor. It thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Due to its unique scent and flavor, it is often cultivated for both culinary and ornamental purposes.
