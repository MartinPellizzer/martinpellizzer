Micranthemum procerorum

Micranthemum procerorum, commonly known as the slender water moss, is a small, aquatic plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. The plant has thin, ribbon-like leaves that are often arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are usually submerged or floating.

Micranthemum procerorum is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to provide habitat for aquatic life.
