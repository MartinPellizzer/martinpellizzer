Microchaeta lavarum

Microchaeta lavarum, commonly known as the Lava Plant, is a unique species native to volcanic regions.

It thrives in high-temperature environments, often found near geothermal areas. The plant has a distinctive appearance with dark, glossy leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its roots are adapted to absorb minerals from the volcanic soil, contributing to its resilience.

Microchaeta lavarum plays a role in stabilizing soil in extreme habitats.
