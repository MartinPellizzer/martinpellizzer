Macrosepalum

Macrosepalum, commonly known as the giant sepal orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its large, showy sepals that often resemble petals, giving it a striking appearance. These plants are native to tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The leaves are typically broad and sword-shaped, while the flowers are borne on long, slender stems.

Macrosepalum species are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant colors and unique floral structures.
