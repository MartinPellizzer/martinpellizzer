Myristica mediterranea

Myristica mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean myristica, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces oval, woody fruits that contain a single seed, and its roots are known for their medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, humid climate to flourish.

Myristica mediterranea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional uses in herbal medicine.
