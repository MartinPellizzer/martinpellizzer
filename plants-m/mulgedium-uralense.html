Mulgedium uralense

Mulgedium uralense, commonly known as the Ural mulgedium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the cold climates of Central Asia.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate to subarctic regions. The plant features a basal rosette of broad, lance-shaped leaves with a hairy texture. Its flowering stems bear small, clustered inflorescences with pale blue to purple flowers.

Mulgedium uralense is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
