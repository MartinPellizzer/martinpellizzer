Machilus tingzhourensis

Machilus tingzhourensis, commonly known as the Tingzhou privet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Guizhou and Sichuan. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. It produces small white flowers in clusters, which are followed by dark blue berries.

This species is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
