Minuartia armena

Minuartia armena, commonly known as Armenian rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

Minuartia armena is adapted to rocky and arid environments, making it a hardy species found in mountainous regions.
