Myrtus glabrata

Myrtus glabrata, commonly known as smooth myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are typically covered in a fine white bloom, giving them a slightly fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by dark purple berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Myrtus glabrata is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in perfumery and herbal medicine.
