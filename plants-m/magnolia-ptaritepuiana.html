Magnolia ptaritepuiana

Magnolia ptaritepuiana, commonly known as the Taiti magnolia, is a rare and endemic species native to the tropical rainforests of New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinct aromatic scent.

The species is of particular interest to botanists due to its unique genetic makeup and limited distribution.
