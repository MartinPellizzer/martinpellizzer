Mosla cavaleriei

Mosla cavaleriei, commonly known as Chinese bugloss, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, blue to purple flowers are typically arranged in dense clusters at the ends of the branches.

Mosla cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
