Manilkara densiflora

Manilkara densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered manilkara, is a tropical tree native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are typically followed by round, fleshy fruits containing a single seed. Its bark is thick and often fissured, providing a rugged texture.

The species is valued for its ecological role and has been studied for potential medicinal properties.
