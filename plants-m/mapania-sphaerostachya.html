Mapania sphaerostachya

Mapania sphaerostachya, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, blue or purple flower heads that resemble a globe, which are composed of tightly packed bracts. The plant has a rosette of silver-gray, fuzzy leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Mapania sphaerostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract beneficial insects.
