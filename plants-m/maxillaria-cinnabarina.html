Maxillaria cinnabarina

Maxillaria cinnabarina, commonly known as the Scarlet Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in seasonal environments. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria cinnabarina thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light.
