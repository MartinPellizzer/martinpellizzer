Masdevallia linearifolia

Masdevallia linearifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, narrow leaves and pendant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high air circulation for optimal growth. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat with minimal rainfall.

Masdevallia linearifolia is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
