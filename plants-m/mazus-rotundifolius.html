Mazus rotundifolius

Mazus rotundifolius, commonly known as the round-leaf mazus, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fleshy, rounded leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mazus rotundifolius is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its compact growth habit.
