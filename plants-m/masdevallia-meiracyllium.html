Masdevallia meiracyllium

Masdevallia meiracyllium, commonly known as the Meiracyllium masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

Its foliage consists of broad, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.
