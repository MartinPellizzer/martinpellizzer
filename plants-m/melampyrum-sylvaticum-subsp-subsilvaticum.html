Melampyrum sylvaticum subsp. subsilvaticum

Melampyrum sylvaticum subsp.

subsilvaticum, commonly known as the woodland touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow-orange flowers.
