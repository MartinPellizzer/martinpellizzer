Melinis goetzenii

Melinis goetzenii, commonly known as the Goetzen's bluestem, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
