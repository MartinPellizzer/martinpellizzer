Mentha × dumetorum var. kupcokiana

Mentha × dumetorum var. kupcokiana, commonly known as the Kupcokian hemp mint, is a hybrid plant within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. It is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild medicinal properties.
