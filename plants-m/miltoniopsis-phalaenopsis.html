Miltoniopsis phalaenopsis

Miltoniopsis phalaenopsis, commonly known as the pink-tinged phalaenopsis, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from Miltoniopsis and Phalaenopsis species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with a unique blend of colors, often featuring a pinkish tinge. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

The flowers typically bloom in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a favored plant among orchid enthusiasts.
