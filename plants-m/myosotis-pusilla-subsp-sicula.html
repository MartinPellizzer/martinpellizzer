Myosotis pusilla subsp. sicula

Myosotis pusilla subsp.

sicula, commonly known as Sicilian forget-me-not, is a small herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.

The plant features delicate blue to purple flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its calming properties.
