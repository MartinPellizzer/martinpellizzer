Moraea multiflora

Moraea multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves and produces multiple racemes of vibrant blue or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Moraea multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its prolonged flowering period.
