Medinilla viguieri

Medinilla viguieri, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet flower, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round, orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla viguieri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
