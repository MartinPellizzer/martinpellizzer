Mussaenda odorifera

Mussaenda odorifera, commonly known as the sweet trumpet or fragrant Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and it produces small, clustered fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.
