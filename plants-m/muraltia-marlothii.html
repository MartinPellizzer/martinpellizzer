Muraltia marlothii

Muraltia marlothii, commonly known as the black thorn, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its thorny stems and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
