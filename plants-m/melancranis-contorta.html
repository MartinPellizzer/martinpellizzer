Melancranis contorta

Melancranis contorta, commonly known as the twisted melancranis, is a rare and lesser-known plant species belonging to the Melancranis genus.

It is characterized by its unique, spiral-shaped leaves that curl tightly around its stem, creating a distinctive appearance. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually green or brown, and they bloom in early spring.
