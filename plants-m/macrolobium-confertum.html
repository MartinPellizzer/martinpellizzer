Macrolobium confertum

Macrolobium confertum, commonly known as the broad-lobed senna, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often deeply divided. The plant produces bright yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which are typical of the Fabaceae family. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

Macrolobium confertum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage.
