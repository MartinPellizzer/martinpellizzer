Mammillaria gracilis var. pulchella

Mammillaria gracilis var. pulchella, commonly known as the elegant mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow tubercles, which are often adorned with clusters of white bristles. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of succulent plants.
