Malaxis andamanica

Malaxis andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andaman Islands.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

Malaxis andamanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in tropical rainforests.
