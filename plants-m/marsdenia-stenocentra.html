Marsdenia stenocentra

Marsdenia stenocentra, commonly known as the narrow-centre Marsdenia, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by follicles containing numerous seeds.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
