Millettia reticulata

Millettia reticulata, commonly known as the reticulate millettia, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
