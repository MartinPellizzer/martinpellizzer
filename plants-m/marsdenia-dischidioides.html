Marsdenia dischidioides

Marsdenia dischidioides, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The leaves are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name.
