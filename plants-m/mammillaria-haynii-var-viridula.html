Mammillaria haynii var. viridula

Mammillaria haynii var. viridula, commonly known as the Green Mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a compact, globular stem covered in short, cylindrical ribs and small, green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
