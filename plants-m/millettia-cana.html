Millettia cana

Millettia cana, commonly known as the yellow millettia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in humid forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for use in vertical gardens or as a foliage plant in shaded areas.
