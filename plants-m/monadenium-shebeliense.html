Monadenium shebeliense

Monadenium shebeliense, commonly known as the Ethiopian star grass, is a flowering plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a star-like pattern, which are clustered in dense heads. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing on rocky soils.
