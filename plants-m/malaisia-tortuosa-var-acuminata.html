Malaisia tortuosa var. acuminata

Malaisia tortuosa var. acuminata, commonly known as the slender swamp hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and showy, pink to red flowers.
