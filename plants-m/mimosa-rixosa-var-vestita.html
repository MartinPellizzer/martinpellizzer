Mimosa rixosa var. vestita

Mimosa rixosa var. vestita, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a variant of the mimosa plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
