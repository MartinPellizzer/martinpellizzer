Mila caespitosa subsp. caespitosa

Mila caespitosa subsp.

caespitosa, commonly known as the tufted mila, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It typically grows in open, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in grassland or shrubland habitats.

The plant forms dense, low-growing tufts with narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish or pale yellow.

The subspecies is characterized by its compact growth habit and is valued for its ornamental appeal in native plant gardens.
