Mentha × subarvensis

Mentha × subarvensis, commonly known as black mint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its dark purple to black stems and leaves, which distinguish it from other mint species. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale purple or blue, and it is often found in the northern hemisphere.

Mentha × subarvensis is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb.
