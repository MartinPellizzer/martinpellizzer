Mesembryanthemum proximum

Its flowers, which range in color from white to pink and yellow, bloom in the early morning and close by midday, a trait known as nyctinasty.

Mesembryanthemum proximum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
