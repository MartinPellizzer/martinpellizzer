Murbeckiella pinnatifida

Murbeckiella pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leafed murbeckiella, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white or pinkish flowers, which are typically followed by small, capsule-like fruits. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
