Macrothelypteris keraudreniana

Macrothelypteris keraudreniana, commonly known as the Keraudren's huperzia, is a species of fern belonging to the family Huperziaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen, leathery fronds that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant has a distinct, robust rhizome that supports its growth in shaded, moist environments. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical regions with high humidity.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptability make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
