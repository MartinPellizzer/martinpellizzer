Maranta warscewiczii

Maranta warscewiczii, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage. The plant features dark green, velvety leaves with intricate patterns and a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its rhizomatous growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant.
