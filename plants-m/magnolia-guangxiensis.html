Magnolia guangxiensis

Magnolia guangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
