Malabaila stolzii

Malabaila stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Malabaila stolzii is known for its ability to attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.
