Meliosma pinnata subsp. ferruginea

Meliosma pinnata subsp.

ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty-leaf meliosma, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. It is characterized by its alternate, pinnately compound leaves with a distinctive rusty-brown underside.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in native habitats.
