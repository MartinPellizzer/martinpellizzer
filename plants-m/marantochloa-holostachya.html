Marantochloa holostachya

Marantochloa holostachya, commonly known as the golden flame plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often overlooked compared to its vibrant leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Marantochloa holostachya is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic value.
