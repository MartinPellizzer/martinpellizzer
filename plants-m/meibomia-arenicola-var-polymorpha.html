Meibomia arenicola var. polymorpha

Meibomia arenicola var. polymorpha, commonly known as the coastal meibomia, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of eastern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and hairy foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is adapted to coastal and arid environments.

Its botanical features make it an important component of coastal vegetation in its native habitat.
