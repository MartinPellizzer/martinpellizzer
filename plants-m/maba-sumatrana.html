Maba sumatrana

Maba sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran maba, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Sumatra.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, with smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elongated, woody fruits containing several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
