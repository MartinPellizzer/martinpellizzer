Mammillaria atrata

Mammillaria atrata, commonly known as the dark spined hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly hair and prominent, dark spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
