Magnolia × soulangeana

Magnolia × soulangeana, commonly known as the street magnolia or Chinese magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and urban landscapes.
