Medicago agropyretorum

Medicago agropyretorum, commonly known as the yellow medic, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its seeds are encased in pods, which are often used in traditional herbal medicine.

Medicago agropyretorum plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
