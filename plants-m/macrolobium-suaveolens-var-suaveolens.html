Macrolobium suaveolens var. suaveolens

Macrolobium suaveolens var. suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented macrolobium, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant aroma and unique foliage.
