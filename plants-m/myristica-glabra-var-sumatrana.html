Myristica glabra var. sumatrana

Myristica glabra var. sumatrana, commonly known as nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its aromatic seeds, which are the source of the highly valued spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and develops dark, rough-skinned fruits containing the aromatic seeds. The botanical features include a smooth, grayish-brown bark and lanceolate leaves with a leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is cultivated for its medicinal and culinary uses.
