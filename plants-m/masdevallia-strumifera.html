Masdevallia strumifera

Masdevallia strumifera, commonly known as the fluted masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescence and intricate, colorful flowers that range from red to purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that help it conserve moisture in its humid environment.

Masdevallia strumifera is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
