Medicago decandollei

Medicago decandollei, commonly known as the Decandolle's clover, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Medicago decandollei is also cultivated as a forage crop for livestock due to its nutritional value.
