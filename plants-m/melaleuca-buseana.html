Melaleuca buseana

Melaleuca buseana, commonly known as the Busean tea tree, is a small to medium-sized shrub native to the coastal regions of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal scrub.

Melaleuca buseana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to withstand coastal conditions.
