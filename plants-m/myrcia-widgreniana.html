Myrcia widgreniana

Myrcia widgreniana, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form with glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Myrcia widgreniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
