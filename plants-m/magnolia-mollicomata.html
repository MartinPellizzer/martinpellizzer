Magnolia mollicomata

Magnolia mollicomata, commonly known as the silky magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, waxy texture. The tree produces small, red berries that contain the seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
