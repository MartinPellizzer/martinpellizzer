Melastoma acinodendron

Melastoma acinodendron, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, red or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Melastoma acinodendron is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
