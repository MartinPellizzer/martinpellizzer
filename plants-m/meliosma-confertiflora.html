Meliosma confertiflora

Meliosma confertiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered meliosma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
