Muhlenbergia flavida var. strictior

Muhlenbergia flavida var. strictior, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, pale to golden flowers in late summer, which are clustered in dense panicles. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and low maintenance requirements in arid landscapes.
