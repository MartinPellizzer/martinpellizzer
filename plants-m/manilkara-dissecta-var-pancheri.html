Manilkara dissecta var. pancheri

Manilkara dissecta var. pancheri, commonly known as the Pancheri sapodilla, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces edible fruits with a sweet, creamy pulp similar to that of the common sapodilla. Its bark is thick and corky, often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for both its ornamental appeal and its economic utility in fruit production.
