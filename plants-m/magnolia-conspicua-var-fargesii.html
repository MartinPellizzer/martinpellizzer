Magnolia conspicua var. fargesii

Magnolia conspicua var. fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. The plant thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of elegance in traditional Chinese horticulture.
