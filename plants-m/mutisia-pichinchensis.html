Mutisia pichinchensis

Mutisia pichinchensis, commonly known as the Pichincha mutisia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

Mutisia pichinchensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
