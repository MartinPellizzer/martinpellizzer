Murraya odorata

Murraya odorata, commonly known as the Chinese tallowtree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and often emit a fragrant scent. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that contain toxic seeds. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

However, it is considered an invasive species in many regions due to its ecological impact.
