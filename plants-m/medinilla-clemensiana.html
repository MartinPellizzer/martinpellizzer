Medinilla clemensiana

Medinilla clemensiana, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This evergreen shrub is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The flowers are typically pink or red and have a velvety texture, which gives the plant its common name. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires partial shade to flourish.

Medinilla clemensiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
