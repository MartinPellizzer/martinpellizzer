Mammillaria heteracantha

Mammillaria heteracantha, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and white during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and flowering habits, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
