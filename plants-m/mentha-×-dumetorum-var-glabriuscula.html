Mentha × dumetorum var. glabriuscula

Mentha × dumetorum var. glabriuscula, commonly known as "pennyroyal," is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of tiny, pale blue or white flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in wet meadows, stream banks, and woodland areas.

Due to its aromatic properties, it has been used historically in herbal remedies and as a natural insect repellent.
