Maranta friedrichsthaliana

Maranta friedrichsthaliana, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that often display a unique silver or white vein pattern. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant.
