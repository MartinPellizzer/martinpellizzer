Myoporum sandwicense var. degeneri

Myoporum sandwicense var. degeneri, commonly known as the Hawaiian myoporum, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is adapted to thrive in coastal and dryland environments, often found on rocky soils. Its growth habit is typically low and spreading, with a woody structure.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for native wildlife.
