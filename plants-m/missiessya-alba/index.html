Missiessya Alba

Missiessya alba is a rare and delicate flowering plant known for its striking white blooms. Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Russia, it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers resemble small, cup-shaped lanterns, adding a unique visual appeal to its habitat. Despite its beauty, the plant is highly sensitive to environmental changes and is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect this elusive species from extinction.
