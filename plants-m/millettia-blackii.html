Millettia blackii

Millettia blackii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often covering the entire canopy in the spring. The plant has pinnate leaves and produces woody pods that contain flat, edible seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental species.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
