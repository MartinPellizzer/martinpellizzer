Mammillaria polygona

Mammillaria polygona, commonly known as the many-armed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green tubercles. The plant typically grows to a height of 5-10 centimeters and produces yellow flowers during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
