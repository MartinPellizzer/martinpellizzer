Mesua elmeri

Mesua elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's flame of the woods, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a vibrant splash of color to its natural habitat. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Mesua elmeri is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its traditional uses in medicine and as a source of timber.
