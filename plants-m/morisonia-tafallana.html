Morisonia tafallana

Morisonia tafallana, commonly known as the Tafallana morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and thrives in sandy soils with ample sunlight. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its vines can climb or trail along the ground.

Morisonia tafallana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a symbol of resilience in its native habitat.
