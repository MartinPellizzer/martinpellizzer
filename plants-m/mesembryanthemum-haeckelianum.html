Mesembryanthemum haeckelianum

Mesembryanthemum haeckelianum, commonly known as the "living snow" or "ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that resemble small ice crystals, hence its common name. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
