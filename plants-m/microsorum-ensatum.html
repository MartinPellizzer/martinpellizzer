Microsorum ensatum

Microsorum ensatum, commonly known as the Chinese water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating fronds that are divided into small, serrated leaflets. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in ponds and streams. Its rhizomes spread along the water's surface, anchoring the plant and facilitating nutrient absorption.

Microsorum ensatum is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
