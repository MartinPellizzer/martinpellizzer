Mesua planigemma

Mesua planigemma, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a striking appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil types make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
