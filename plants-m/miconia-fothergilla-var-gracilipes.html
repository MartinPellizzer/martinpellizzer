Miconia fothergilla var. gracilipes

Miconia fothergilla var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender fothergilla, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers moist, well-drained soil.

The slender fothergilla is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
