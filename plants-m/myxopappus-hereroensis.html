Myxopappus hereroensis

Myxopappus hereroensis, commonly known as the Herero dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and alternate leaves. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions, adapting to harsh environmental conditions. It produces small, yellow flowers that develop into fluffy seed heads, facilitating wind dispersal.
