Myrcia moaensis

Myrcia moaensis, commonly known as the Moa River myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil, particularly found in the states of Pará and Maranhão. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and clusters of white or pinkish flowers. It produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red when ripe.

Myrcia moaensis is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical rainforests.
