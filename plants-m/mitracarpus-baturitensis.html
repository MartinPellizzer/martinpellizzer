Mitracarpus baturitensis

Mitracarpus baturitensis, commonly known as the Baturite flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, flame-like inflorescences. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for cultivation in warm regions.
