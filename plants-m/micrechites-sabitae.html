Micrechites sabitae

Micrechites sabitae, commonly known as the Sabita's rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows in rocky or arid environments. The plant has small, simple leaves and produces clusters of tiny white or pale yellow flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in small, capsule-like structures, typical of the Brassicaceae family.

Micrechites sabitae is often found in regions with limited soil and high sunlight exposure.
