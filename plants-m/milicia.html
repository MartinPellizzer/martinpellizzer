Milicia

Milicia, commonly known as the African shea tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Ghana, Nigeria, and Cameroon. The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture-making. Its fruit produces a nutritious seed that is used in traditional medicine and food.

The plant also plays an important role in local ecosystems and supports biodiversity.
