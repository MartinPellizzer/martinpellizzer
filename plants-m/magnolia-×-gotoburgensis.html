Magnolia × gotoburgensis

Magnolia × gotoburgensis, commonly known as the Gotoburg magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant typically grows to a height of 8–12 meters and has a broad, rounded crown. Its leaves are ovate to elliptical, with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
