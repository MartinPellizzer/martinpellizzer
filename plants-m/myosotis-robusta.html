Myosotis robusta

Myosotis robusta, commonly known as the robust forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features upright, branched stems that bear clusters of small, blue to purple flowers with five petals. The leaves are lance-shaped, slightly hairy, and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland and streamside habitats.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
