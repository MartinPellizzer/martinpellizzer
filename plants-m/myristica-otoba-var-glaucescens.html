Myristica otoba var. glaucescens

Myristica otoba var. glaucescens, commonly known as the glossy myristica, is a species of evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, oily seeds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by reddish-brown, fleshy fruits. Its botanical significance lies in its unique chemical composition, which includes essential oils and aromatic compounds.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and traditional uses in local medicine and perfumery.
