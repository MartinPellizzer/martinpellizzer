Melastoma argyratum

Melastoma argyratum, commonly known as silverleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a silvery or whitish pubescence, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in dense inflorescences, and are typically pink or white in color.

The plant produces berries that are red when mature, containing several seeds.
