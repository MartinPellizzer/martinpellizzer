Muhlenbergia filiformis var. fortis

Muhlenbergia filiformis var. fortis, commonly known as the stiff muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
