Mormodes cartonii var. aurantiaca

Mormodes cartonii var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a striking tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features vibrant orange and yellow flowers that resemble small stars, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, often covered in a waxy coating to retain moisture.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated in greenhouses or shaded gardens.
