Medicago murex subvar. rodriguezii

Medicago murex subvar. rodriguezii, commonly known as the Rodriguez's goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to specific regions of the Mediterranean.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments. It has a deep root system that allows it to tolerate drought conditions.

This subvariety is valued for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation and as a forage plant for livestock.
