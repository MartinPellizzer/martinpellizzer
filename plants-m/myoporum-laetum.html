Myoporum laetum

Myoporum laetum, commonly known as the silver bush, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features dense, silver-gray foliage that provides a striking visual contrast against its surroundings. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in spring. It is well adapted to coastal environments and can tolerate salt spray and sandy soils.

Myoporum laetum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
