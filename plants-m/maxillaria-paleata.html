Maxillaria paleata

Maxillaria paleata, commonly known as the pale pink orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale pink to white blooms with a distinctive yellowish lip, which is a key feature in its pollination strategy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria paleata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
