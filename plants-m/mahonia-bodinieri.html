Mahonia bodinieri

Mahonia bodinieri, commonly known as the golden mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves that are typically dark green with a leathery texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in late winter to early spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its ornamental value comes from its foliage, flowers, and berries, which provide seasonal interest.
