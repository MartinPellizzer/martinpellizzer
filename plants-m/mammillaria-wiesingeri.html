Mammillaria wiesingeri

Mammillaria wiesingeri, commonly known as the Wiesinger's nipple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in early spring.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
