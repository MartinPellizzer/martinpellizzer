Metrosideros polymorpha f. perglabra

Metrosideros polymorpha f. perglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved 'Ohia, is a variant of the Hawaiian 'Ohia tree.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves, which distinguish it from other forms of the species. This plant is native to the Hawaiian Islands and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is known for its vibrant red or yellow flowers.

The smooth-leaved form is often found in areas with well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
