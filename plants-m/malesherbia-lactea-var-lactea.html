Malesherbia lactea var. lactea

Malesherbia lactea var. lactea, commonly known as the milkweed tree, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its smooth, light green bark and opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
