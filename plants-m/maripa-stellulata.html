Maripa stellulata

Maripa stellulata, commonly known as the star-leaf maripa, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, star-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas or near water sources.

Maripa stellulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
