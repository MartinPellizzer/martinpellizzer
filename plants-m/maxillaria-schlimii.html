Maxillaria schlimii

Maxillaria schlimii, commonly known as the Schlim's orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its blooms typically appear in late winter to early spring.

Maxillaria schlimii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
