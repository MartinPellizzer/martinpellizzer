Maharanga bracteata

Maharanga bracteata, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk covered in a mass of overlapping, leathery fronds.
