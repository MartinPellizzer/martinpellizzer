Maesa lanceolata var. rufescens

Maesa lanceolata var. rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed maesa, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
