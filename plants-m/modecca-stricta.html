Modecca stricta

Modecca stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf modecca, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
