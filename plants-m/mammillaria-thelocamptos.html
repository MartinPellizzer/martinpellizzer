Mammillaria thelocamptos

Mammillaria thelocamptos, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly coating that covers its stems, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in a compact, globular shape with numerous ribs and spines. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
