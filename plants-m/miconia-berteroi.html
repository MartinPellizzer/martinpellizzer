Miconia berteroi

Miconia berteroi, commonly known as the Brazilian glorybower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches.

Miconia berteroi is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
