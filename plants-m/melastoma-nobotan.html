Melastoma nobotan

Melastoma nobotan, commonly known as the red-root melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma nobotan is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
