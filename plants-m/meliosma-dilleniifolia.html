Meliosma dilleniifolia

Meliosma dilleniifolia, commonly known as the Dilleniifolia Meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliosmaceae.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
