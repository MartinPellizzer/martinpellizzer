Monosis volkameriifolia

Monosis volkameriifolia, commonly known as the Volkameria leafy tree, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are leathery in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
