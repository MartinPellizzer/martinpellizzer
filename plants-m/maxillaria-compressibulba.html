Maxillaria compressibulba

Maxillaria compressibulba, commonly known as the compressed bulb Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulb-like structure and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are usually broad and fleshy, adapted for water storage.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and relatively easy care requirements.
