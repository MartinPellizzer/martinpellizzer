Morinda confusa

Morinda confusa, commonly known as the red morinda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking red flowers and woody stems. The plant produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but often have a bitter taste. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
