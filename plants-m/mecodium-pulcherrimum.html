Mecodium pulcherrimum

Mecodium pulcherrimum, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that features yellow flowers with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has fuzzy, gray-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact growth habit and vibrant floral display.
