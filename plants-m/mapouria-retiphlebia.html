Mapouria retiphlebia

Mapouria retiphlebia, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to eastern and southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The tree is also valued for its ability to provide shade and support for various wildlife species.
