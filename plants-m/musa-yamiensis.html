Musa yamiensis

Musa yamiensis, commonly known as the Yami banana, is a species of flowering plant in the Musaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly the Philippines. The plant features large, broad leaves and produces clusters of edible bananas. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Musa yamiensis is valued both for its fruit and its ornamental qualities.
