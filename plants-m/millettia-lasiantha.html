Millettia lasiantha

Millettia lasiantha, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The tree thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.
