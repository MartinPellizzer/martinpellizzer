Mimosa modesta var. ursinoides

Mimosa modesta var. ursinoides, commonly known as the red mimosa, is a variant of the mimosa plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or pink flowers, which bloom in spherical clusters. The plant has feathery, bipinnate leaves that are sensitive to touch, causing them to fold when disturbed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking addition to gardens and landscapes.
