Mentha mollis var. retinervis

Mentha mollis var. retinervis, commonly known as soft mint or woolly mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, which bloom in late summer. Mentha mollis var. retinervis is often found in rocky soils and dry environments, thriving in full sun.

It is valued for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in herbal teas and culinary applications.
