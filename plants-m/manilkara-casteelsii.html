Manilkara casteelsii

Manilkara casteelsii, commonly known as the Casteels' manilkara, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces yellowish-white flowers followed by spherical, spiny fruits that contain a single, smooth, dark brown seed. Its latex is milky and viscous, often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential applications in the rubber and pharmaceutical industries.
