Mussaenda monticola

Mussaenda monticola, commonly known as the mountain Mussaenda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange in color. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Mussaenda monticola is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
