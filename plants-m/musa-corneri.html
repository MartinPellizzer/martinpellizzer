Musa corneri

Musa corneri, commonly known as the Corner banana, is a species of flowering plant in the Musaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and tall, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible berries that are typically consumed fresh or used in local cuisines. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Musa corneri is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
