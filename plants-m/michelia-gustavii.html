Michelia gustavii

Michelia gustavii, commonly known as the Chinese Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
