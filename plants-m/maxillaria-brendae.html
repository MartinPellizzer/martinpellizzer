Maxillaria brendae

Maxillaria brendae, commonly known as the Brendae's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.

Maxillaria brendae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
