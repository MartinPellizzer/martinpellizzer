Mesembryanthemum canescens

Mesembryanthemum canescens, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a versatile ornamental plant.
