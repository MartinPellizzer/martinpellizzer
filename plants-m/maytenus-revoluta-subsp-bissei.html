Maytenus revoluta subsp. bissei

Maytenus revoluta subsp.

bissei, commonly known as the Mexican mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Mexico. It belongs to the family Cornaceae and is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are often hairy on the underside.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
