Meistera koenigii

Meistera koenigii, commonly known as the Indian pennywort, is a perennial herb native to the Indian subcontinent.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and often have a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Meistera koenigii is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
