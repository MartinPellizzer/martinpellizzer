Mimetes argenteus

Mimetes argenteus, commonly known as the silver mimetes, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and yellow-orange flowers that bloom in winter. The plant is native to the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mimetes argenteus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
