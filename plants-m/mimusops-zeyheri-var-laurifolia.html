Mimusops zeyheri var. laurifolia

Mimusops zeyheri var. laurifolia, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded crown and its showy, white, clustered flowers that bloom in winter. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the white blossoms. The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to coastal and warm climates.

It produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible for some animals but not typically consumed by humans.
