Macrotorus genuflexus

Macrotorus genuflexus, commonly known as the bent-knee orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant's leaves are elongated and covered in fine, silky hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.
