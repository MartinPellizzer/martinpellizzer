Meehania Fargesii

Meehania fargesii is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It is known for its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is now considered endangered. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this unique botanical treasure.
