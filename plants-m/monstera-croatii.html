Monstera croatii

Monstera croatii, commonly known as the Croatian monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop fenestrations, or holes, as they mature. The plant has a robust stem with aerial roots that allow it to climb toward light. Its foliage is typically a deep green, with a slightly glossy surface.

Monstera croatii thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.
