Myosotis pyrenaea

Myosotis pyrenaea, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine habitats, often in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are basal, spoon-shaped, and covered in a fine pubescence.

Myosotis pyrenaea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual charm.
