Mentha meduanensis

Mentha meduanensis, commonly known as the Medean mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forests or rocky areas. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a serrated edge and emit a strong, menthol-like fragrance.

Mentha meduanensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
