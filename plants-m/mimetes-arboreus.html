Mimetes arboreus

Mimetes arboreus, commonly known as the tree lily, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, showy white or pale pink flowers that resemble lilies. The plant is native to the Western Cape and grows in coastal fynbos vegetation. It produces a single, elongated flower spike with numerous bell-shaped blooms that open sequentially.

Mimetes arboreus is a perennial herb that thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.
