Medicago helix var. inermis

Medicago helix var. inermis, commonly known as the creeping medic, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a low-growing, spreading habit, making it suitable for ground cover. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment.
