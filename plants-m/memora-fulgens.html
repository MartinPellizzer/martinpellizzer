Memora fulgens

Memora fulgens, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from orange to red, often with a yellow or white center. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its dramatic blooms.

Memora fulgens thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.
