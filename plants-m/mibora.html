Mibora

Mibora, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Mibora thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.
