Melastoma portoricense

Melastoma portoricense, commonly known as the Puerto Rican melastoma, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean island of Puerto Rico.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

Melastoma portoricense plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
