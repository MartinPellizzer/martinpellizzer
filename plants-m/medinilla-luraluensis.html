Medinilla luraluensis

Medinilla luraluensis, commonly known as the Luraluensis Medinilla, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant in tropical regions.
