Micromeria forbesii var. forbesii

Micromeria forbesii var. forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' micromeria, is a small, aromatic shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and desert scrub habitats. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It is characterized by its fragrant foliage, which contains essential oils used in traditional herbal remedies.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
