Manglietia utilis

Manglietia utilis, commonly known as the white mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and striking white flowers with a strong, spicy fragrance. The plant produces round, segmented fruits that are typically pink or red when mature. Its ornamental value is high due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

Manglietia utilis is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant in warm climates.
