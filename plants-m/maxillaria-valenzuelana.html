Maxillaria valenzuelana

Maxillaria valenzuelana, commonly known as the Valenzuelan orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Maxillaria valenzuelana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
