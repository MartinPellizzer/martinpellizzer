Mesembryanthemum introrsum

Mesembryanthemum introrsum, commonly known as the upright ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white, powdery wax. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments with minimal water.
