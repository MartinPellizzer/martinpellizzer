Mesosphaerum strictum

Mesosphaerum strictum, commonly known as the narrowleaf mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its upright growth habit and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the spring. It is adapted to survive in dry environments with minimal water availability.

Mesosphaerum strictum is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in drought-tolerant gardens.
