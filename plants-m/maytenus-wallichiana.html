Maytenus wallichiana

Maytenus wallichiana, commonly known as the Himalayan mayten, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Celtaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves, which are opposite and often glandular. Maytenus wallichiana is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
