Maxillaria linearifolia

Maxillaria linearifolia, commonly known as the linear leaf orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, linear leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive pattern of darker markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria linearifolia is a popular choice for both indoor and greenhouse cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
