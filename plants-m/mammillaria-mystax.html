Mammillaria mystax

Mammillaria mystax, commonly known as the hairless hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a short stem and clusters of spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and has a woolly texture on its surface. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria mystax is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
