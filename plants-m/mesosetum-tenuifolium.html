Mesosetum tenuifolium

Mesosetum tenuifolium, commonly known as slenderleaf grass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands and along stream banks. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce tiny grains.

Mesosetum tenuifolium plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various insects and wildlife.
