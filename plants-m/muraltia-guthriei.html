Muraltia guthriei

Muraltia guthriei, commonly known as the Guthrie's muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that are edible for birds and some mammals. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Muraltia guthriei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
