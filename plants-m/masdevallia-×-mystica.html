Masdevallia × mystica

Masdevallia × mystica, commonly known as the mystical orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that resemble miniature orchids, with elongated petals and a prominent lip. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle.

Masdevallia × mystica is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements in a humid, shaded environment.
