Modecca cissampeloides

Modecca cissampeloides, commonly known as the silverleaf lantana, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant has opposite, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it its common name. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

The silverleaf lantana is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and floral display.
