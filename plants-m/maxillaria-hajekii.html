Maxillaria hajekii

Maxillaria hajekii, commonly known as the Hajek's maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with dark red or purple markings.

Maxillaria hajekii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor gardens.
