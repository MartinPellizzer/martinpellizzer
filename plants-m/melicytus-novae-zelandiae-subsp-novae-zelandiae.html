Melicytus novae-zelandiae subsp. novae-zelandiae

Melicytus novae-zelandiae subsp.

novae-zelandiae, commonly known as the New Zealand melicytus, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are an important food source for native birds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance in New Zealand's native ecosystems.
