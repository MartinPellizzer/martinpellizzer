Magnolia bidoupensis

Magnolia bidoupensis, commonly known as the Bidoup magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. Its bark is smooth and grey, and it can grow as a small tree or shrub.

Magnolia bidoupensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
