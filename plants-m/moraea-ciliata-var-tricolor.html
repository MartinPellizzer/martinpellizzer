Moraea ciliata var. tricolor

Moraea ciliata var. tricolor, commonly known as the Three-color Moraea, is a striking perennial bulbous plant native to southern Africa.

It features showy, three-toned flowers that range from white to pink and red, creating a vibrant display. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 50 centimeters, with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique coloration and ornamental value make it a sought-after addition to botanical collections.
