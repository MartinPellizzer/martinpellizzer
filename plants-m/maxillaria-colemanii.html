Maxillaria colemanii

Maxillaria colemanii, commonly known as the Coleman's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of white or pale pink blossoms. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria colemanii is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
