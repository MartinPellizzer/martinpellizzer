Muhlenbergia alopecuroides

Muhlenbergia alopecuroides, commonly known as hairless muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads in late summer, which are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it a popular choice for native gardens.

Muhlenbergia alopecuroides is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
