Mammillaria antesbergeriana

Mammillaria antesbergeriana, commonly known as the Beforeberger's nipple cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a diameter of up to 10 centimeters, characterized by its white to pale yellow spines and ribbed structure. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red or orange fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
