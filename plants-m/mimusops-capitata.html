Mimusops capitata

Mimusops capitata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
