Macaranga Tomentosa

Macaranga tomentosa is a fast-growing tropical tree native to Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique characteristics, including its hairy leaves and rapid growth rate. This plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as forest edges and cleared areas. Its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions makes it an important pioneer species. Despite its ecological significance, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions.
