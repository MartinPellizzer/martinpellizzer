Maytenus obtusifolia var. latifolia

Maytenus obtusifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved mayten, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are broad and ovate in shape. The leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.

This plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
