Macrolobium unijugum var. fanshawei

Macrolobium unijugum var. fanshawei, commonly known as the Fan Shawei orchid, is a unique variant of the genus Macrolobium, native to specific regions in China.

It is characterized by its striking, large, and often colorful flowers that display a distinctive shape and pattern. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its botanical features include elongated, fleshy pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle.
