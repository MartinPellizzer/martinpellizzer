Maxillaria nanegalensis

Maxillaria nanegalensis, commonly known as the Brazilian velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a velvety texture. The plant typically features dark green, elliptical leaves and blooms in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria nanegalensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
