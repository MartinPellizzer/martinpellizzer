Moschoxylum propinquum

Moschoxylum propinquum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved daisy or narrow-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are showy, with white petals surrounding a yellow center, and it blooms from late spring to early autumn.

Moschoxylum propinquum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
