Melaleuca thymoides

Melaleuca thymoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Melaleuca thymoides is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
