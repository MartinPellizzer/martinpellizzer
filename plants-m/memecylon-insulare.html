Memecylon insulare

Memecylon insulare, commonly known as the island memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape.
