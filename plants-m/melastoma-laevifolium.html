Melastoma laevifolium

Melastoma laevifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are usually pink or red, clustered in racemes, and bloom during the wet season.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.
