Madhuca butyracea

Madhuca butyracea, commonly known as the buttery wax tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adaptable to various soil types.
