Mesembryanthemum carinatum

Mesembryanthemum carinatum, commonly known as the Zebra Plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, fleshy leaves that resemble a zebra's stripes. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by afternoon. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mesembryanthemum carinatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
