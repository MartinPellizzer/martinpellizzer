Mangostana cambogia

Mangostana cambogia, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant white flowers. The fruit is distinctive for its bright red outer rind and segmented, white, juicy flesh. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

It is valued not only for its unique flavor but also for its potential medicinal properties.
