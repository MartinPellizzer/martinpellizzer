Monstera boliviana

Monstera boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Bolivia.

It is characterized by its large, fenestrated leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a robust stem with aerial roots that allow it to climb and anchor itself to trees or other structures. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

Monstera boliviana thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
