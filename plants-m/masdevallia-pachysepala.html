Masdevallia pachysepala

Masdevallia pachysepala, commonly known as the thick-sepaled masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and produces showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves and has a unique inflorescence with clustered, spurred flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
