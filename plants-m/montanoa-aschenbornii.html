Montanoa aschenbornii

Montanoa aschenbornii, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red tubular flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners in temperate climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from summer into fall.
