Mammillaria spinosissima var. mirabilis

Mammillaria spinosissima var. mirabilis, commonly known as the Mirabilis Mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in prominent, white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
