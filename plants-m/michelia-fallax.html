Michelia fallax

Michelia fallax, commonly known as the white-flowered michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Michelia fallax is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
