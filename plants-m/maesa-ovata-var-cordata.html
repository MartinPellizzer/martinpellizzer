Maesa ovata var. cordata

Maesa ovata var. cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf maesa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
