Mammillaria phymatothele var. trohartii

Mammillaria phymatothele var. trohartii, commonly known as the Trohart's mammillaria, is a rare and compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
