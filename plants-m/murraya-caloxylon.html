Murraya caloxylon

Murraya caloxylon, commonly known as the Indian murraya, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are edible but often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth texture and a slightly aromatic scent.
