Miconia folsomii

Miconia folsomii, commonly known as the Folsom's miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. It produces showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens, although it can be invasive in certain environments.
