Millettia limbutuensis

Millettia limbutuensis, commonly known as the Limbutoensis Millettia, is a climbing plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Cameroon and the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant features compound leaves with trifoliate or pinnate arrangements, and its flowers are typically small and clustered in racemes. It produces leguminous pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

The species is valued for its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
