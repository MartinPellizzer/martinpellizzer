Mangifera acutigemma

Mangifera acutigemma, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mango, is a species of flowering plant in the Anacardiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruit, though not as widely cultivated as other mango varieties, is edible and has a distinct flavor.

The tree is often grown for its ornamental value as well as for its potential medicinal properties.
