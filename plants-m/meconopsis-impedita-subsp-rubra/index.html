Meconopsis Impedita Subsp. Rubra

Red himalayan poppy, scientifically known as Meconopsis impedita subsp. rubra, is a strikingly beautiful flower native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas. With its vibrant crimson petals and dark velvety centers, it stands out among the rugged mountainous terrain where it thrives. This rare and delicate plant is often found in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it faces extreme weather conditions. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of fascination for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Despite its beauty, the red himalayan poppy remains a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural habitat.
