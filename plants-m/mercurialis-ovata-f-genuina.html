Mercurialis ovata f. genuina

Mercurialis ovata f. genuina, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mercury.

This variant is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa. It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant thrives in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
