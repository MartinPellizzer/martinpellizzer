Myristica pilosella

Myristica pilosella, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family, which includes other nutmeg and mace-producing species. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed, which is encased in a hard, aromatic shell.

The tree is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
