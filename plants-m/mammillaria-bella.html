Mammillaria bella

Mammillaria bella, commonly known as the "Beautiful Mammillaria," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in short, white to pale pink spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria bella is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
