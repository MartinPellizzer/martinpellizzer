Masdevallia schoonenii

Masdevallia schoonenii, commonly known as the "Blue Orchid," is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vivid blue-violet petals and sepals, which are adorned with intricate white and purple markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a challenging but rewarding plant for experienced growers.

Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
