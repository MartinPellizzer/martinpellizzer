Muscarella lipothrix

Muscarella lipothrix, commonly known as the hairy leaf muscaria, is a unique plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It features broad, leathery leaves that are often covered in a fine, hairy texture, giving it a rugged appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Due to its unusual appearance and growth habits, it is a relatively rare and specialized species in the botanical world.
