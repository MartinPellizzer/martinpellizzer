Maytenus laevigata

Maytenus laevigata, commonly known as the smooth mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are typically leathery and dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry to moist forests.

Maytenus laevigata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
