Meliosma oppositifolia

Meliosma oppositifolia, commonly known as the opposite-leaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite arrangement of simple, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elongated, woody fruits.

The plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
