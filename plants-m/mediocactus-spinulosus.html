Mediocactus spinulosus

Mediocactus spinulosus, commonly known as the spiny cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and produces yellow flowers during the warmer months. The plant has spines that are both protective and adapted to reduce water loss. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a shallow root system suited for dry environments.

Mediocactus spinulosus is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
