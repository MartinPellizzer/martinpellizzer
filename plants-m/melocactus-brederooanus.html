Melocactus brederooanus

Melocactus brederooanus, commonly known as the Brederoo melon cactus, is a rare and striking species native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical, ribbed stem that can grow up to 1 meter in height, often adorned with spines and a distinctive yellowish or greenish hue. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
