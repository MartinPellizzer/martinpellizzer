Melastoma latifolium

Melastoma latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant features opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
