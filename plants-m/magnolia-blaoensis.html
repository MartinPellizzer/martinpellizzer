Magnolia blaoensis

Magnolia blaoensis, commonly known as the Blao magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with a faintly flaky texture.

The Blao magnolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
