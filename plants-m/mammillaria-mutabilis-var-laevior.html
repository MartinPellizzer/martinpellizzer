Mammillaria mutabilis var. laevior

Mammillaria mutabilis var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in short, rounded tubercles that are less pronounced than in other varieties. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
