Muhlenbergia lucida

Muhlenbergia lucida, commonly known as shining muhlenbergia, is a species of grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and distinctive silver-gray foliage that reflects light, giving it a shimmering appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear fluffy, white to pale blue seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Muhlenbergia lucida is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
