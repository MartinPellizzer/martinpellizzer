Mentha × dumortieri

Mentha × dumortieri, commonly known as hybrid mint, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Mentha spicata (spearmint) and Mentha viridis (ovalleaf mint).

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves that emit a strong, refreshing scent. The plant typically grows as an upright herb with square stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mentha × dumortieri is widely cultivated for its use in culinary, medicinal, and aromatic applications.
