Microtea debilis var. ovata

Microtea debilis var. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf microtea, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features delicate, ovate-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and slightly succulent. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale green. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forests.

Due to its unique leaf shape and compact size, it is a popular choice for small garden spaces and indoor cultivation.
