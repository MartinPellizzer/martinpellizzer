Melargyra rosea

Melargyra rosea, commonly known as the pink starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, star-shaped pink petals that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down.

Melargyra rosea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, eye-catching flowers.
