Mandevilla lucida

Mandevilla lucida, commonly known as the shining mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged opposite each other on the stems. Mandevilla lucida is a vigorous climber, often used in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and lush foliage.

It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
