Mediolobivia rubriflora

Mediolobivia rubriflora, commonly known as the crimson flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its vivid red and orange flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the humid rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant features tall, slender stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers bloom in clusters, emitting a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
