Miconia skutchii

Miconia skutchii, commonly known as the Skutch's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia skutchii is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical rainforests.
