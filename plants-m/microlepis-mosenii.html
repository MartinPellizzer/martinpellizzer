Microlepis mosenii

Microlepis mosenii, commonly known as the African feather grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery appearance with narrow, blade-like leaves that are often silver-green in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in loose clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland and rocky habitats.

Microlepis mosenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and as a groundcover in suitable climates.
