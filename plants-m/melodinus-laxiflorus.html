Melodinus laxiflorus

Melodinus laxiflorus, commonly known as the soft-flowered melody vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically large and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
