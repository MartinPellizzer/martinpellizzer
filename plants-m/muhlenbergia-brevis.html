Muhlenbergia brevis

Muhlenbergia brevis, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, grass-like foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and woodland edges. Its flowers are small and spike-like, appearing in late summer to early autumn.

Muhlenbergia brevis is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
