Macrolenes ciliatiloba

Macrolenes ciliatiloba, commonly known as the silky leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves with fine, silky hairs along the edges. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
