Microthelys rubrocalosa

Microthelys rubrocalosa, commonly known as the red-calyx microthelys, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in clusters. The plant has slender stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are notable for their vibrant red calyces, which contrast with the pale petals.

Microthelys rubrocalosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.
