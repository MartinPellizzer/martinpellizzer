Meliosma simplicifolia

Meliosma simplicifolia, commonly known as the simple-leaved meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by elongated, woody fruits.

Meliosma simplicifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
