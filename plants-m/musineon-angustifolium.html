Musineon angustifolium

Musineon angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaf musineon, is a rare flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically less than 2 centimeters wide. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale green in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
