Mimosa chiliantha

Mimosa chiliantha, commonly known as the Chilean mimosa, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Chile and Argentina.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, curling when disturbed. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a bright touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Its ornamental value and adaptability make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
