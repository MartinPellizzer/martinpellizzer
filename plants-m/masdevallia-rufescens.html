Masdevallia rufescens

Masdevallia rufescens, commonly known as the red-brown masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its striking, often vibrant flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically produces a single spike with several flowers, each featuring intricate patterns and a velvety texture. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light exposure but should be protected from direct sunlight.

Masdevallia rufescens is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
