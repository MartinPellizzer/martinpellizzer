Miltonia spectabilis var. atrorubens

Miltonia spectabilis var. atrorubens, commonly known as the Red-tinged Violet, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals that range from deep purple to red, with a contrasting white or cream-colored lip. This plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It produces fragrant, showy flowers that bloom during the summer months.

The variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
