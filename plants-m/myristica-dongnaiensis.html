Myristica dongnaiensis

Myristica dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic seeds and oily exocarp. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and develops woody, kidney-shaped fruits that contain the aromatic seeds. Its essential oils and nutmeg-like seeds are valued for their culinary and medicinal uses.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
