Memecylon glomeratum

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often covered with fine hairs.
