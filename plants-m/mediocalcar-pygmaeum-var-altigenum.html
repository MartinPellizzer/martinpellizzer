Mediocalcar pygmaeum var. altigenum

Mediocalcar pygmaeum var. altigenum, commonly known as the dwarf calcar.

This small, succulent plant is native to arid regions and features compact, fleshy leaves that store water. It produces small, pale flowers during the warmer months, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has a shallow root system adapted to drought conditions.

It is often used in rock gardens and as a houseplant due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
