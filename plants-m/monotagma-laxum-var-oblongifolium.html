Monotagma laxum var. oblongifolium

Monotagma laxum var. oblongifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf pincushion cactus, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, spiny areoles. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to small, scale-like structures, typical of many cacti.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for arid garden environments.
