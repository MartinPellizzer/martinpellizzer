Manilkara darienensis

Manilkara darienensis, commonly known as the Darien manilkara, is a tropical tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The tree produces smooth, yellowish-brown fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. Its bark is thick and fissured, often exuding a latex-like substance.
