Mimusops ugandensis

Mimusops ugandensis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and dense, dark green foliage. Due to its ornamental value and hard wood, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
