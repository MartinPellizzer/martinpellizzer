Miconia brunescens

Miconia brunescens, commonly known as the dark-leaved miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berry-like, typically green when immature and turning red when mature.

Miconia brunescens is often found in humid, lowland forests and is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.
