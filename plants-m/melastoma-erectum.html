Melastoma erectum

Melastoma erectum, commonly known as the red-rooted strobilanthes, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, winged seeds when mature.
