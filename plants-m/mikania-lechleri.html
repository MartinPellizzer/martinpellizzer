Mikania lechleri

Mikania lechleri, commonly known as the "dragon's blood tree," is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its woody stems and heart-shaped leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by small, oval-shaped fruits. Its sap is highly viscous and has a deep red color, which has been traditionally used in various medicinal and cultural practices.

The plant's unique appearance and valuable sap make it a notable species in both ecological and ethnobotanical contexts.
