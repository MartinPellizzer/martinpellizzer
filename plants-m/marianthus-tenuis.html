Marianthus tenuis

Marianthus tenuis, commonly known as the slender marshmallow, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Marianthus tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
