Maurandya semperflorens f. alba

Maurandya semperflorens f. alba, commonly known as the white morning glory, is a hybrid cultivar of the morning glory family (Convolvulaceae).

It is characterized by its cascading clusters of large, white, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped, hairy leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and as a container plant.
