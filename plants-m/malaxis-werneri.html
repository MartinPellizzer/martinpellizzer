Malaxis werneri

Malaxis werneri, commonly known as the Werneri's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant has a slender rhizome and produces a single, erect stem with a few leaves. Its flowers are usually greenish-yellow with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Malaxis werneri is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
