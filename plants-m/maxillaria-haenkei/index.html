Maxillaria Haenkei

Maxillaria haenkei is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. Known for its vibrant flowers and unique structure, this plant captivates both novice and experienced growers alike. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to flourish. The orchid's intricate blooms often feature a mix of colors, adding a splash of beauty to any collection. With proper care, Maxillaria haenkei can bloom repeatedly, making it a rewarding addition to any greenhouse or indoor garden.
