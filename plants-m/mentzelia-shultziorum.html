Mentzelia shultziorum

Mentzelia shultziorum, commonly known as Shultz's blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, branched stems and showy, yellow to orange-red tubular flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.
