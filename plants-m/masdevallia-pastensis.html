Masdevallia pastensis

Masdevallia pastensis, commonly known as the Pastensis orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces striking, intricate flowers with a wide range of colors. The plant typically blooms during the warmer months, showcasing its ornamental value. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant hues make it a subject of interest for both botanists and horticulturists.
