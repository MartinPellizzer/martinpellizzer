Monsonia salmoniflora

Monsonia salmoniflora, commonly known as the salmon-flowered monsonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking salmon-pink to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.
