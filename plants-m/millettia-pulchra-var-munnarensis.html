Millettia pulchra var. munnarensis

Millettia pulchra var. munnarensis, commonly known as the Munnar beauty, is a climbing plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its attractive, palmately lobed leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and conservation efforts.

The varietal form munnarensis is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and slightly different foliage structure compared to the typical variety.
