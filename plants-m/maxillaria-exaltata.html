Maxillaria exaltata

Maxillaria exaltata, commonly known as the Tall Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The petals and sepals are typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive violet or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
