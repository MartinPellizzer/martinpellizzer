Miconia artibonitensis

Miconia artibonitensis, commonly known as the Artibonite miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean, particularly Haiti.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia artibonitensis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed habitats, making it an important species in ecological studies.
