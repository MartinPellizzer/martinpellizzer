Mimetes myrtifolius

Mimetes myrtifolius, commonly known as the silverleaf mountain catchfly, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
