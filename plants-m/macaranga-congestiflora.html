Macaranga congestiflora

Macaranga congestiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered yellowish-green flowers that are often surrounded by bracts. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Macaranga congestiflora is also known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
