Medinilla multinervia

Medinilla multinervia, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves with prominent veins, which give it its scientific name *multinervia*. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, often arranged in clusters. Its ornamental value comes from its lush foliage and long-lasting blooms, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.

Medinilla multinervia thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and indirect sunlight.
