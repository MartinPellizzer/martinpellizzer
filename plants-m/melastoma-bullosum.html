Melastoma bullosum

Melastoma bullosum, commonly known as the bubble bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
