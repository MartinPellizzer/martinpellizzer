Mandevilla andina

Mandevilla andina, commonly known as the Andean mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its colorful flowers.

Mandevilla andina thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
