Mormodes densiflora

Mormodes densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces showy, often yellow or greenish-yellow blooms. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
