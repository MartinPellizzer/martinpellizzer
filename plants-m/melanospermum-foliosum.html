Melanospermum foliosum

Melanospermum foliosum, commonly known as the blackseed leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dark green, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by black, spherical seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for arid climates.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscaped gardens.
