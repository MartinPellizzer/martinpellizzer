Mitella × inamii

Mitella × inamii, commonly known as the hybrid snowbell, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a soft, hairy texture.
