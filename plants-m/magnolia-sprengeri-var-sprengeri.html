Magnolia sprengeri var. sprengeri

Magnolia sprengeri var. sprengeri, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the yellow magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant, white to yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and the plant is relatively disease-resistant, though it may be susceptible to scale insects.
