Menispermum dauricum var. mexicanum

Menispermum dauricum var. mexicanum, commonly known as Mexican birthwort, is a member of the Menispermaceae family and is native to Mexico.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces bright red berries that are edible when ripe but contain toxic compounds in their raw state. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural and ecological studies.
