Meibomia elata

Meibomia elata, commonly known as the silky meibomia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving them a silky texture. Its flowers are small and usually clustered in terminal inflorescences, with white or pale pink petals.

Meibomia elata is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
