Mesembryanthemum minutiflorum

Mesembryanthemum minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to South Africa.

It features small, fleshy leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces tiny, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, usually blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
