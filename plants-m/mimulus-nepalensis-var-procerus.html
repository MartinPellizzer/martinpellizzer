Mimulus nepalensis var. procerus

Mimulus nepalensis var. procerus, commonly known as the long-stamen yellow monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with prominent stamens that extend beyond the petals. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats at high altitudes. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often hairy as well.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
