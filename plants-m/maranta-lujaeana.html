Maranta lujaeana

Maranta lujaeana, commonly known as the Lujan's arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green hues. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Maranta lujaeana is valued both for its aesthetic appeal and its historical use in traditional medicine.
