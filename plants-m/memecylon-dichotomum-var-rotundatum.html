Memecylon dichotomum var. rotundatum

Memecylon dichotomum var. rotundatum, commonly known as the round-leaved memecylon, is a species native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves that are often rounded at the base. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its foliage is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
