Monstera gigas

Monstera gigas, commonly known as the giant monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to three feet in length. The plant is a climbing vine that can reach heights of over 100 feet in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and they undergo a dramatic change in color as they mature.

Monstera gigas is prized for its striking foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
