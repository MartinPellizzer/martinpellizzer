Masdevallia nivea

Masdevallia nivea, commonly known as the Snowy Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, white or pale pink flowers that resemble snowflakes, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as a small epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light.

Masdevallia nivea is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
