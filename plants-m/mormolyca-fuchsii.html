Mormolyca fuchsii

Mormolyca fuchsii, commonly known as the fuchsii orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricately patterned flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and purple. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its growth is slow, and it is often found in association with other epiphytic plants in tropical rainforests.
