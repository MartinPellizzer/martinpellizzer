Malaxis latisegmenta

Malaxis latisegmenta, commonly known as the broad-segmented orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with pale yellow to greenish-white petals and sepals. Its inflorescence typically consists of several flowers arranged along a spike.

Malaxis latisegmenta is often found in shaded, moist habitats such as forests and rocky areas.
