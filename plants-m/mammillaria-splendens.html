Mammillaria splendens

Mammillaria splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Globular Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact, rounded shape and a gray-green color. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its appearance. It has cylindrical, spineless stems that are covered in white, woolly areoles.

Mammillaria splendens thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
