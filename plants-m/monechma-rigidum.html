Monechma rigidum

Monechma rigidum, commonly known as the rigid monechma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, calcareous soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides.
