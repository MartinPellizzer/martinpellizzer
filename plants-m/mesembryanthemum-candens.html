Mesembryanthemum candens

Mesembryanthemum candens, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to both gardens and landscapes.
