Maytenus erythrocarpa

Maytenus erythrocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

Maytenus erythrocarpa is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used historically in construction and tool-making.
