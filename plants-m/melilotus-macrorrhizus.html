Melilotus macrorrhizus

Melilotus macrorrhizus, commonly known as large-rooted melilot, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which bloom in early summer. Its roots are thick and fleshy, often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.
