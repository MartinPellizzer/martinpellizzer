Maxillariella caespitifica

Maxillariella caespitifica, commonly known as the clustered Maxillariella, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a compact, rosette-like growth habit with closely packed leaves. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, appearing in clusters during the growing season.

The orchid's botanical structure reflects its adaptation to moist, forested habitats.
