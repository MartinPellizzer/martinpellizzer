Mimosa arenosa var. arenosa

Mimosa arenosa var. arenosa, commonly known as the desert mimosa, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in spherical heads. It is well adapted to drought conditions, with deep roots that allow it to survive in sandy or rocky soils.

Mimosa arenosa var. arenosa is also known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
