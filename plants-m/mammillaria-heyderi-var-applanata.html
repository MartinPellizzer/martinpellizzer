Mammillaria heyderi var. applanata

Mammillaria heyderi var. applanata, commonly known as the flat-topped hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a flat, rounded top that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
