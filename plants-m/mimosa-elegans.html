Mimosa elegans

Mimosa elegans, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa elegans is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique leaf movement and attractive foliage.
