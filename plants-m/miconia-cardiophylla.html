Miconia cardiophylla

Miconia cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Miconia cardiophylla is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and coastal areas.
