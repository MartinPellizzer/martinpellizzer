Musa peekelii

Musa peekelii, commonly known as the Peckel's banana, is a rare and ornamental plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to three meters in length. The plant produces striking, elongated flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. While it does not produce edible fruit, it is valued for its aesthetic appeal in botanical gardens.

Its growth habit makes it a popular choice for tropical landscapes and indoor cultivation.
