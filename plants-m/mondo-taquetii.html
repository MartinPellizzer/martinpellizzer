Mondo taquetii

Mondo taquetii, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Mondo taquetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
