Mandevilla spigeliiflora

Mandevilla spigeliiflora, commonly known as the zigzag mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the edges, giving it its common name. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a climbing or trailing plant.

Mandevilla spigeliiflora is prized for its ornamental value and ability to bloom profusely throughout the growing season.
