Melampyrum laxum var. aristatum

Melampyrum laxum var. aristatum, commonly known as the erect yellow lousewort, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family.

It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on host plants for water and nutrients while performing photosynthesis. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it plays a role in supporting pollinators in its native range.
