Mammillaria nejapensis var. longispina

Mammillaria nejapensis var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, radial spines that are notably long and slender. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
