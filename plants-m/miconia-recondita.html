Miconia recondita

Miconia recondita, commonly known as the hidden miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white to pinkish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it has been introduced to other tropical regions, though it can become invasive in some areas.
