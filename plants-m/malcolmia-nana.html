Malcolmia nana

Malcolmia nana, commonly known as dwarf moonflower, is a low-growing herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths. The plant has hairy, alternate leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its short stature and unique blooming habit make it a distinctive addition to gardens and natural habitats.
