Metrosideros polymorpha var. micrantha

Metrosideros polymorpha var. micrantha, commonly known as the ʻākala, is a variant of the ʻohiʻa tree native to Hawaii.

It is a small, shrub-like species with distinctive red or orange-red flowers that bloom throughout the year. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a glossy appearance, while the bark is smooth and often reddish in color. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in coastal and lowland forest habitats.
