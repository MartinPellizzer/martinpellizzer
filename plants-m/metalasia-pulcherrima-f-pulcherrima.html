Metalasia pulcherrima f. pulcherrima

Metalasia pulcherrima f. pulcherrima, commonly known as the flame tree or royal poinciana, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescences that display vibrant red and orange hues, resembling flames. The plant has compound leaves with pinnate arrangement, and its flowers are borne on long, pendulous racemes. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark is smooth and light-colored, with a fibrous texture that distinguishes it from other species.
