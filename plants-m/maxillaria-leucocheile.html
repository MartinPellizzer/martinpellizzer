Maxillaria leucocheile

Maxillaria leucocheile, commonly known as the white-lipped maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria leucocheile thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
