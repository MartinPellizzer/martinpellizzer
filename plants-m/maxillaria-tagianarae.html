Maxillaria tagianarae

Maxillaria tagianarae, commonly known as the Tagianara orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria tagianarae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
