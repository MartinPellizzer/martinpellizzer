Melastoma malabathricum var. mariannum

Melastoma malabathricum var. mariannum, commonly known as the Mariannum Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
