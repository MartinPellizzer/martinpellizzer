Merciera vaginata

Merciera vaginata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in loose inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, flat seeds.

Merciera vaginata is known for its rapid growth and is often considered a weed in agricultural settings.
