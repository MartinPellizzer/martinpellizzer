Mercurialis annua var. angustifolia

Mercurialis annua var. angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf mercury.

It is an annual herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, branching stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences. It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
