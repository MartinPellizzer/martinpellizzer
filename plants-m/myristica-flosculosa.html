Myristica flosculosa

Myristica flosculosa, commonly known as the fragrant nutmeg tree, is a small evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark grey bark and elliptical, leathery leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that are clustered in spikes, which are followed by large, round, yellowish fruits. The fruit contains a single, hard, aromatic seed that is highly valued for its essential oils and medicinal properties.

Myristica flosculosa is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance and fragrance.
