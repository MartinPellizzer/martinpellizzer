Microlicia minima var. minima

Microlicia minima var. minima, commonly known as the small microlicia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows to a height of less than 10 centimeters, with slender, branching stems covered in fine, hairy hairs. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a velvety texture, often with a bluish-green hue. It produces small, greenish-white flowers in clusters, which are followed by small, capsule-like fruits.

This plant is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth or along stream banks.
