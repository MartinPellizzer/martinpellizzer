Metrosideros pumila var. makaionensis

Metrosideros pumila var. makaionensis, commonly known as the Makaion small flame tree, is a compact shrub or small tree native to the island of Makaian in the Cook Islands.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant thrives in coastal and sunny environments, often growing on rocky or sandy soils. Its ornamental value is notable, making it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
