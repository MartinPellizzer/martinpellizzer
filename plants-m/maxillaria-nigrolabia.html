Maxillaria nigrolabia

Maxillaria nigrolabia, commonly known as the black-lipped maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with dark purple and white petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are notable for their intricate patterns and strong scent.

Maxillaria nigrolabia is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
