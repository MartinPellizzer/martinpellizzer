Mesechites linearifolius

Mesechites linearifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf mesechites, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences, usually in shades of red or purple. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
