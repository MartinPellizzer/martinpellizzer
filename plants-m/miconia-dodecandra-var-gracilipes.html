Miconia dodecandra var. gracilipes

Miconia dodecandra var. gracilipes, commonly known as the slender miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are followed by round, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions as a decorative plant.
