Medinilla lordbergiana

Medinilla lordbergiana, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This tropical plant is native to the Philippines and belongs to the Myrtaceae family. It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and indoor cultivation.
