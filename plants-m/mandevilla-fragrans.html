Mandevilla fragrans

Mandevilla fragrans, commonly known as the sweet scent mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to summer. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Mandevilla fragrans is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant aroma.
