Malva Alcea Proles Fastigiata

Malva alcea proles fastigiata is a striking member of the Malvaceae family known for its upright growth and vibrant pink to purple flowers. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience. Its unique fastigiata form gives it a columnar shape, distinguishing it from other mallows. Often cultivated for ornamental purposes, it also has historical uses in herbal medicine. With its elegant appearance and hardy nature, it remains a cherished addition to many botanical gardens and landscapes.
