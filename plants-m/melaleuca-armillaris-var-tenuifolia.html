Melaleuca armillaris var. tenuifolia

Melaleuca armillaris var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaved tea tree, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces aromatic, honey-scented nectar, making it attractive to nectar-feeding birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
