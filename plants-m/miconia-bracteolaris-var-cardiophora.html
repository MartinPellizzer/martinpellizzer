Miconia bracteolaris var. cardiophora

Miconia bracteolaris var. cardiophora, commonly known as the heart-shaped miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that are often followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive texture.

This species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
