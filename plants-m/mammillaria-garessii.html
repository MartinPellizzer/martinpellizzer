Mammillaria garessii

Mammillaria garessii, commonly known as the Garessi's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, compact cactus with a globular shape and a gray-green to blue-gray coloration. The plant features prominent white spines and is characterized by its dense, clustered growth pattern. It produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria garessii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
