Moraea umbellata

Moraea umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble an umbrella, hence its common name. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. Moraea umbellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
