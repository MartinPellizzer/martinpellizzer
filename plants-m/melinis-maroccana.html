Melinis maroccana

Melinis maroccana, commonly known as the African grass or red grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish hue, which becomes more pronounced as the plant matures. The plant has slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to various climates.

Melinis maroccana is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
