Maxillaria lepidota var. albida

Maxillaria lepidota var. albida, commonly known as the White-veined Orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with contrasting veining. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
