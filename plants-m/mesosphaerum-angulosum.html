Mesosphaerum angulosum

Mesosphaerum angulosum, commonly known as the angular mesosphaerum, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, angular leaf morphology and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in dense tropical forests. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with alternate, serrated leaves.
