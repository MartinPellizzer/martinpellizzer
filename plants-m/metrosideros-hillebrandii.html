Metrosideros hillebrandii

Metrosideros hillebrandii, commonly known as the Hillebrand's Christmasberry, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, often in sandy or rocky soils. The plant produces showy, red or orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough, textured surface.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
