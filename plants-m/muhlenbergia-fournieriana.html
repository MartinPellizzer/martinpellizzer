Muhlenbergia fournieriana

Muhlenbergia fournieriana, commonly known as the Mexican muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often mistaken for cotton, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for native plant gardens.
