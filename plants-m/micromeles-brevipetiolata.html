Micromeles brevipetiolata

Micromeles brevipetiolata, commonly known as the short-stemmed nightshade, is a small shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant features simple, oval-shaped leaves with a hairy surface. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple, and it produces small, round berries that are toxic to humans.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal potential, it is sometimes cultivated in subtropical regions.
