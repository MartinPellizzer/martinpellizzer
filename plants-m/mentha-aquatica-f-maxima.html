Mentha aquatica f. maxima

Mentha aquatica f. maxima, commonly known as the large water mint, is a hybrid variety of the water mint plant.

It is characterized by its robust growth and larger leaves compared to the typical water mint. This plant thrives in moist or aquatic environments, often found near streams, ponds, and wet meadows. Its aromatic leaves and flowering spikes make it a distinctive feature in wetland habitats.

It is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its potential use in herbal remedies.
