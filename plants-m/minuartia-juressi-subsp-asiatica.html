Minuartia juressi subsp. asiatica

Minuartia juressi subsp.

asiatica, commonly known as the Asian sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Asia. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in rocky habitats.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, blooming in early spring. Its leaves are linear and succulent, adapted to conserve water in dry environments.

It is a hardy species often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
