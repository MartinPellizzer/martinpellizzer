Mandevilla dardanoi

Mandevilla dardanoi, commonly known as the Dardanoi mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and large, showy flowers that range from white to pink and red. The plant produces trumpet-shaped blooms that are typically 3 to 5 inches long, adding a dramatic visual impact to gardens. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Mandevilla dardanoi thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
