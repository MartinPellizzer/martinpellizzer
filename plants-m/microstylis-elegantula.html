Microstylis elegantula

Microstylis elegantula, commonly known as the elegant feathergrass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, arching stems that bear delicate, thread-like leaves, giving it a graceful appearance. The plant produces small, pale green spikelets that are arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Microstylis elegantula is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in wet environments.
