Mentha ocymiodora

Mentha ocymiodora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mint, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and pointed, with a strong, cool menthol scent.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
