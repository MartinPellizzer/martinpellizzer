Macrolobium explicans

Macrolobium explicans, commonly known as the broad-lobed cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, fan-shaped leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces cones rather than flowers, with male and female cones typically growing on separate plants. Its leaves are compound, consisting of numerous leaflets arranged in a feather-like pattern.

Native to the tropical regions of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
