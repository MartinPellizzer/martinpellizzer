Maxillaria cruentata

Maxillaria cruentata, commonly known as the Crimson Maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces striking red or pink flowers with a distinctive, often fragrant scent. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are borne on tall, slender stems. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Maxillaria cruentata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
