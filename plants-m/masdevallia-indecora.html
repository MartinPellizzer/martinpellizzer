Masdevallia indecora

Masdevallia indecora, commonly known as the decorative masdevallia, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that are typically borne on slender, wiry stems. The plant has strap-like leaves that are usually dark green and arranged in a compact rosette. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Masdevallia indecora is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
