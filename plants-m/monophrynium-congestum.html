Monophrynium congestum

Monophrynium congestum, commonly known as the clustered monophrynium, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet, marshy environments. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-white in color, and are arranged in dense inflorescences.

Monophrynium congestum is known for its ability to thrive in humid conditions and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
