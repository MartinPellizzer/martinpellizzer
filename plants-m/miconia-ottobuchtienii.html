Miconia ottobuchtienii

Miconia ottobuchtienii, commonly known as the Otto's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
