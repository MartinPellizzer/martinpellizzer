Metrosideros corifolia

Metrosideros corifolia, commonly known as the New Zealand Christmas tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, bright red or orange-red flowers that bloom profusely during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight waxy coating. It thrives in coastal and subcoastal regions, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure.
