Mammillaria rhodantha var. fuscata

Mammillaria rhodantha var. fuscata, commonly known as the fuscate mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with a greyish-green color and prominent white or pale areoles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its slow growth rate makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
