Mammillaria tesopacensis var. rubraflora

Mammillaria tesopacensis var. rubraflora, commonly known as the Red-flowered Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
