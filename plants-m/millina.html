Millina

Millina, commonly known as the fairy slipper, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plants are characterized by their unique, slipper-shaped flowers that are often brightly colored. They typically grow as terrestrial or epiphytic orchids, thriving in humid, tropical environments.

Millina species are valued for their ornamental beauty and are often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
