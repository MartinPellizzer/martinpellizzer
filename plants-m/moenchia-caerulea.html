Moenchia caerulea

Moenchia caerulea, commonly known as blue moenchia, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Moenchia caerulea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and delicate appearance.
