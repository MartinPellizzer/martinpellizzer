Magnolia kwangtungensis

Magnolia kwangtungensis, commonly known as the Guangdong magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a faintly flaky texture.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
