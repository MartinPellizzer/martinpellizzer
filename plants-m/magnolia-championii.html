Magnolia championii

Magnolia championii, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin layers.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual appeal.
