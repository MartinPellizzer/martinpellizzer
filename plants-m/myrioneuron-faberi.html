Myrioneuron faberi

Myrioneuron faberi, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Botanically, it belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
