Mammea micrantha

Mammea micrantha, commonly known as the small-fruited mammea, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that belongs to the family Clusiaceae. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, clustered flowers. Its fruits are fleshy, red or yellow, and contain several seeds.

Mammea micrantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
