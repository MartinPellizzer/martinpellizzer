Megaliabum

Megaliabum, commonly known as the large-flowered milkweed, is a member of the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, often pink or purple, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as butterflies and hummingbirds. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its stems are usually woody at the base and can grow to a height of several feet.

Megaliabum thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.
