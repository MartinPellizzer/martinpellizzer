Marathrum rubrum

Marathrum rubrum, commonly known as the red flame lily, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that form a rosette at the base. Its botanical classification places it in the family Amaryllidaceae, which includes many ornamental and economically important plants.

Marathrum rubrum thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
