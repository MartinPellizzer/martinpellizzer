Macroplectrum implicatum

Macroplectrum implicatum, commonly known as the woolly spider-plant, is a unique member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.
