Magnolia quangninhensis

Magnolia quangninhensis, commonly known as the Quang Ninh magnolia, is a rare and endemic species native to Vietnam.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often along riverbanks or in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and dark green color.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution and unique floral structure.
