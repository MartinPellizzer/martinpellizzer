Microtis graniticola

Microtis graniticola, commonly known as the granite microtis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, spurred flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant is often found in quartzitic or granitic habitats, which contribute to its common name.
