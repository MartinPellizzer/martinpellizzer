Mammillaria pfeifferiana var. altissima

Mammillaria pfeifferiana var. altissima, commonly known as the tall mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a dense covering of white glochids.

It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
