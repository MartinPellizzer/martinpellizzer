Medicago pungens

Medicago pungens, commonly known as the prickly medick, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia and is characterized by its small, spiny leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is a perennial herb that thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides. Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment.

Medicago pungens is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
