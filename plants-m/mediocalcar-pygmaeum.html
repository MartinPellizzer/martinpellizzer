Mediocalcar pygmaeum

Mediocalcar pygmaeum, commonly known as the dwarf calcar.

It is a small, herbaceous plant native to arid regions of southern Africa. The plant features succulent leaves that store water, adapted to its dry environment. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of yellow or white.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
