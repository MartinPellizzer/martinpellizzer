Mandevilla venulosa

Mandevilla venulosa, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun.

Mandevilla venulosa is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant flowers and vigorous growth habit.
