Myristica wrayi

Myristica wrayi, commonly known as the African nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa, particularly found in countries like Tanzania and Uganda.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain a valuable nutmeg-like seed. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets. Its seeds are used in traditional medicine and are valued for their aromatic properties, similar to true nutmeg.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
