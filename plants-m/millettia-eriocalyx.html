Millettia eriocalyx

Millettia eriocalyx, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves with small, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by pod fruits containing edible seeds. It is often grown for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.

The species is also valued in agroforestry systems due to its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
