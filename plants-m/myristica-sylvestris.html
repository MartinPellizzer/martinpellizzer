Myristica sylvestris

Myristica sylvestris, commonly known as wild nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, oily seeds that resemble the more well-known nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. Its bark is thick and rough, often covered with a waxy coating.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
