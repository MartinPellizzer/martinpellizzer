Meum mutellina var. adonidifolium

Meum mutellina var. adonidifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf meum, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils. The plant features slender, upright stems with opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, yellowish-green flowers appear in clusters during the summer months.

The species is characterized by its distinct foliage and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature.
