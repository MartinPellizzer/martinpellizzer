Micromeria fruticosa var. italica

Micromeria fruticosa var. italica, commonly known as the Italian thyme, is a low-growing, aromatic shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, needle-like leaves that are covered in fine, glandular hairs, giving it a distinctive texture and strong, pungent fragrance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink to purple in color, blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and Mediterranean-style landscapes.

Its essential oils are valued for their culinary and medicinal uses.
