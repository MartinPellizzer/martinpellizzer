Miconia aymardii

Miconia aymardii, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or golden star.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of white, waxy hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, and it produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible.

Miconia aymardii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit structure.
