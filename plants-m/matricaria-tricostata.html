Matricaria tricostata

Matricaria tricostata, commonly known as the slender chamomile, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, yellow flowers with white centers, resembling those of true chamomile. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it has a strong, aromatic scent.

Matricaria tricostata is often used in herbal medicine for its calming and anti-inflammatory properties.
