Melastoma barbeyanum

Melastoma barbeyanum, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces bright red, fleshy fruits that are typically eaten fresh or used in traditional medicine. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

Melastoma barbeyanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
