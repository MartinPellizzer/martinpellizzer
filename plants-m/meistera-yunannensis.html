Meistera yunannensis

Meistera yunannensis, commonly known as the Yunnan meistera, is a striking ornamental plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Araceae family, characterized by its unique inflorescence structure consisting of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. The plant is noted for its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length, providing a lush, tropical appearance. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.

Meistera yunannensis is also valued for its potential in horticultural research due to its adaptability and aesthetic appeal.
