Millettia leptobotrys

Millettia leptobotrys, commonly known as the slender-leaved millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.
