Michelia aurantiaca

Michelia aurantiaca, commonly known as the orange-flowered michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. Its bark is smooth and greyish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Michelia aurantiaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
