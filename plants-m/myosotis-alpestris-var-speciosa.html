Myosotis alpestris var. speciosa

Myosotis alpestris var. speciosa, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet petals, which are typically smaller than those of the common forget-me-not. The plant thrives in alpine meadows and rocky slopes, where it tolerates cold temperatures and poor soil conditions. Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens that mimic its natural habitat.
