Miliusa dioeca

Miliusa dioeca, commonly known as the yellow sterculia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain one or two seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

The tree's botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
