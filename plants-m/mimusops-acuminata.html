Mimusops acuminata

Mimusops acuminata, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain hard, smooth seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Mimusops acuminata is valued for its durable wood and is often used in traditional medicine and ornamental horticulture.
