Massonia nervosa

Massonia nervosa, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Massonia nervosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
