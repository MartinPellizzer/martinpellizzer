Menkea sphaerocarpa

Menkea sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe menkea, is a species of flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit and produces small, spherical fruits that are typically red or orange in color. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and displays showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink or white. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or open areas.

Menkea sphaerocarpa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to local wildlife.
