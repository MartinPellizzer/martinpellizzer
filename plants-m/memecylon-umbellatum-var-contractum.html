Memecylon umbellatum var. contractum

Memecylon umbellatum var. contractum, commonly known as the compact memecylon, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are usually clustered in umbels, producing a showy display of white or pink petals.

The fruit is a small capsule that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its ecological role in forest understories.
