Mammillaria odieriana var. rigidior

Mammillaria odieriana var. rigidior, commonly known as the rigid mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale green tubercles. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
