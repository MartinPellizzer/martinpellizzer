Medium grandiflorum

Medium grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered form of the species, is a plant characterized by its showy, medium-sized flowers.

It typically features vibrant petals that can range in color from white to pink or red, depending on the cultivar. The plant usually has a compact growth habit with sturdy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its botanical significance lies in its ornamental value and adaptability to various climates.
