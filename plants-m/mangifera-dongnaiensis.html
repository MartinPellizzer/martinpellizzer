Mangifera dongnaiensis

Mangifera dongnaiensis, commonly known as the Dong Nai mango, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand.

It is a tropical evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring broad, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The fruit is typically small to medium in size, with a sweet and aromatic pulp that is rich in vitamins and minerals.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its edible fruit and ornamental value.
