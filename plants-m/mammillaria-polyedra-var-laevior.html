Mammillaria polyedra var. laevior

Mammillaria polyedra var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with smooth, rounded tubercles that are less pronounced than in typical Mammillaria species. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique morphology and ease of care make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
