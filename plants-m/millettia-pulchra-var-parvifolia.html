Millettia pulchra var. parvifolia

Millettia pulchra var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved beauty tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its ornamental value due to its striking pink or red flowers. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a shade tree.
