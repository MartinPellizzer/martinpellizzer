Molongum

Molongum, commonly known as the mountain pepper, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and leathery, elliptical leaves. It thrives in dry, rocky environments and is often found in the southern regions of Australia.

The fruit is a small, round capsule that contains several seeds.
