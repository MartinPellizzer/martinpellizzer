Monechma pseudopatulum var. latifolium

Monechma pseudopatulum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf monechma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a distinctive white to pale green coloration. Its small, clustered flowers bloom in late spring, producing a subtle fragrance.

The species is characterized by its robust root system and adaptability to wetland conditions.
