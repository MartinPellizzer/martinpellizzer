Malacothamnus fasciculatus var. nuttallii

Malacothamnus fasciculatus var. nuttallii, commonly known as Nuttall's tufted broom, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically narrow and gray-green, with a fuzzy texture.
