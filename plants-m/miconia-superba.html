Miconia superba

Miconia superba, commonly known as the royal miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers that are often fragrant. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia superba is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
