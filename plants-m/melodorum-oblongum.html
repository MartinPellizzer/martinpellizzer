Melodorum oblongum

Melodorum oblongum, commonly known as the oblong starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wet meadows. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. Its flowering period usually occurs in late spring to early summer.

Melodorum oblongum is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
