Mucuna cylindrosperma

Mucuna cylindrosperma, commonly known as the cylindrical cowpea, is a leguminous plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets. The plant produces elongated, cylindrical pods that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Mucuna cylindrosperma is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
