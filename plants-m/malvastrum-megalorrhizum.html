Malvastrum megalorrhizum

Malvastrum megalorrhizum, commonly known as the giant root mallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer.
