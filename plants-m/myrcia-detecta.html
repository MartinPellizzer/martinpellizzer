Myrcia detecta

Myrcia detecta, commonly known as the red myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. Its fruits are small, red berries that contain numerous seeds.

Myrcia detecta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth.
