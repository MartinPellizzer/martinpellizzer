Mimosa densa var. densa

Mimosa densa var. densa, commonly known as the dense mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various conditions make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
