Miconia chrysocoma

Miconia chrysocoma, commonly known as the golden miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
