Maxillaria matthewsii

Maxillaria matthewsii, commonly known as the Matthews' orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Maxillaria matthewsii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
