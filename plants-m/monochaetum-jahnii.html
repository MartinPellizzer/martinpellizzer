Monochaetum jahnii

Monochaetum jahnii, commonly known as the Jahn's spikeless grass, is a perennial grass species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or swampy habitats, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils.
