Megotigea crinita

Megotigea crinita, commonly known as the featherleaf orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its long, feathery leaves that give it its common name. It produces vibrant, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and purple, which bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
