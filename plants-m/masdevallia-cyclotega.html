Masdevallia cyclotega

Masdevallia cyclotega, commonly known as the cyclocarp orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in greenhouses or conservatories.
