Monotes rubriglans subsp. rubriglans

Monotes rubriglans subsp.

rubriglans, commonly known as the red-stemmed monotes, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its erect, herbaceous growth habit and distinctive red-colored stems that become more pronounced with age.

The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.
