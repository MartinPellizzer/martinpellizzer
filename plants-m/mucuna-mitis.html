Mucuna mitis

Mucuna mitis, commonly known as the velvet bean or mucuna, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, hairy, pod-like fruits. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and is known for its rapid growth habit. Mucuna mitis has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

It is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
