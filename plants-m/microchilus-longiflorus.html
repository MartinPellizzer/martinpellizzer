Microchilus longiflorus

Microchilus longiflorus, commonly known as the long-flowered microchilus, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a specialty plant.
