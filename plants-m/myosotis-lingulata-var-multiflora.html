Myosotis lingulata var. multiflora

Myosotis lingulata var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
