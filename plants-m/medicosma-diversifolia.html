Medicosma diversifolia

Medicosma diversifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed medicinal plant, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2-5 meters. The plant is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves and small, clustered white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Medicosma diversifolia is valued for its traditional medicinal uses, particularly in treating ailments related to the respiratory and digestive systems.
