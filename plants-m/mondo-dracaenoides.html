Mondo dracaenoides

Mondo dracaenoides, commonly known as the narrow leaf dracaena, is a tropical evergreen plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features narrow, dark green, strap-like leaves that grow in a rosette pattern, giving it a compact and elegant appearance. The plant is characterized by its fibrous, woody stem and its ability to thrive in low-light conditions. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its low maintenance requirements and air-purifying qualities.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is closely related to other dracaena species.
