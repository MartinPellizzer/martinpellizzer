Malanea tafelbergensis

Malanea tafelbergensis, commonly known as the Tafelberg malanea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
