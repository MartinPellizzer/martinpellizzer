Michelia parviflora

Michelia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered Michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Michelia parviflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
