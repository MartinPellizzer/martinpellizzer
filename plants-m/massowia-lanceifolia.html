Massowia lanceifolia

Massowia lanceifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf massowia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
