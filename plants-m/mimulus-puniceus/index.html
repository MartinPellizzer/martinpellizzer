Mimulus Puniceus

Mimulus puniceus, also known as the scarlet monkeyflower, is a vibrant and resilient plant native to the western United States. This annual herbaceous species is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers and fuzzy, hairy leaves. It thrives in rocky and arid environments, often found in coastal bluffs and dry slopes. The plant's bold color and unique appearance make it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. Its ecological importance includes supporting pollinators and stabilizing soil in its native habitats.
