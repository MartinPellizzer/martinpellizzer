Macrochordion strictum

Macrochordion strictum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved arrowhead, is a perennial aquatic plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

The plant features narrow, arrow-shaped leaves that are often submerged, with a distinctive spongy texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters at the water's surface.
