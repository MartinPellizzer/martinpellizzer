Millettia litoralis

Millettia litoralis, commonly known as the coastal millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows near coastal areas, tolerating saline and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a leathery texture.

Millettia litoralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging coastal environments.
