Mammillaria sonorensis var. gentryi

Mammillaria sonorensis var. gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
