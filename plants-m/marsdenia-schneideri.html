Marsdenia schneideri

Marsdenia schneideri, commonly known as the Schneider's Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is a climbing vine characterized by its slender stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by follicles containing seeds. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in tropical and subtropical environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
