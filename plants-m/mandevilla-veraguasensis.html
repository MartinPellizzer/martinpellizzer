Mandevilla veraguasensis

Mandevilla veraguasensis, commonly known as the Veraguas mandevilla, is a vigorous evergreen vine native to Central America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white throat. The plant has thick, fleshy stems and heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla veraguasensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
