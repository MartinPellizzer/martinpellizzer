Mesanthemum pubescens f. nigra

Mesanthemum pubescens f. nigra, commonly known as the black-flowered mesanthemum, is a striking plant characterized by its dark, almost black flowers.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its succulent traits and adaptations to arid environments. The plant features fleshy, pubescent leaves that help conserve moisture. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, adding a dramatic visual contrast with their deep color.

This cultivar is often appreciated in rock gardens and dry landscapes for its ornamental value and resilience.
