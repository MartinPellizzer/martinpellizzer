Machaeranthera Townshendii

Townshend's blazingstar, scientifically known as Machaeranthera townshendii, is a striking native wildflower found primarily in the central and southern United States. This herbaceous plant is prized for its vibrant, fiery orange-red blooms that appear in late summer and early fall. It thrives in open prairies, woodland edges, and disturbed soils, showcasing its adaptability to various environments. With its unique appearance and ecological importance, it plays a vital role in supporting pollinators and maintaining biodiversity. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this rare and beautiful species from habitat loss and over-collection.
