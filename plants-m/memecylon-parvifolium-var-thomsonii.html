Memecylon parvifolium var. thomsonii

Memecylon parvifolium var. thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's small-leaf memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
