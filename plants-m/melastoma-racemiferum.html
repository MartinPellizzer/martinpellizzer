Melastoma racemiferum

Melastoma racemiferum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly waxy texture.

Melastoma racemiferum is often found in moist, shaded forest areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
