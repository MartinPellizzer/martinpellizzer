Mentzelia speciosa

Mentzelia speciosa, commonly known as the showy mentzelia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. The plant produces striking, showy yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a bluish-green hue with a fuzzy texture.

Mentzelia speciosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in supporting local pollinators.
