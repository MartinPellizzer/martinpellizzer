Mesembryanthemum politum

Mesembryanthemum politum, commonly known as the silverleaf iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
