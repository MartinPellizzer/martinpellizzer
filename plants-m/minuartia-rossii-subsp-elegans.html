Minuartia rossii subsp. elegans

Minuartia rossii subsp.

elegans, commonly known as the elegant sand-flower, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is characterized by its delicate, hairy leaves and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer.

Native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia, this species thrives in rocky and sandy soils with full sun exposure. The plant is often found in alpine and arctic environments, where it demonstrates remarkable cold tolerance.

Its unique morphology and ecological adaptations make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
