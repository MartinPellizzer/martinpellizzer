Mohlana latifolia

Mohlana latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf mohlana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tubular, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sun. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Mohlana latifolia is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
