Mastichodendron floribundum

Mastichodendron floribundum, commonly known as the flowering mastichodendron, is a unique plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, clustered inflorescences that produce small, fragrant flowers. The plant has a woody stem with a thick, bark-like exterior, which gives it a distinctive appearance. Its leaves are simple and alternate, often with a glossy surface.

While it is primarily grown for its ornamental value, it also holds cultural significance in some traditional practices.
