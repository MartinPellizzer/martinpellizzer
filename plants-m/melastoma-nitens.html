Melastoma nitens

Melastoma nitens, commonly known as the white-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red or purple calyx. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
