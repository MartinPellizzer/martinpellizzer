Mesembryanthemum spiniforme

Mesembryanthemum spiniforme, commonly known as the spiny ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, often spiny leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, yellow, and white, typically during the warmer months. It is well adapted to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a distinctive addition to Mediterranean and desert landscapes.
