Medicago sylvestris var. erectiuscular

Medicago sylvestris var. erectiuscular, commonly known as the upright medick, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit, with slender stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three oval to lanceolate leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by cylindrical, hairy pods containing several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands, meadows, and along roadsides.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
