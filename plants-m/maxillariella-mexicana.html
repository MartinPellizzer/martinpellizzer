Maxillariella mexicana

Maxillariella mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It is a small, epiphytic plant that typically grows on trees or rocks in humid environments. The plant features compact, dark green leaves and produces fragrant, pinkish-white flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened in its native range.
