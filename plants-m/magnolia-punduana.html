Magnolia punduana

Magnolia punduana, commonly known as the KwaZulu-Natal magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 15 meters tall.

Magnolia punduana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
