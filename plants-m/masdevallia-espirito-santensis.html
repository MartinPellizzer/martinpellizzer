Masdevallia espirito-santensis

Masdevallia espirito-santensis, commonly known as the Espirito Santo masdevallia, is a rare orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, elongated flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with high elevation, often on tree trunks or rocky outcrops. Its leaves are usually leathery and dark green, adapted to conserve moisture in its native habitat.
