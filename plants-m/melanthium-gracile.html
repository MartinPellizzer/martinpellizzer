Melanthium gracile

Melanthium gracile, commonly known as slender false nettle, is a herbaceous perennial native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants commonly referred to as buckwheats and sorrels. Melanthium gracile is often found in moist woodland habitats and is known for its ecological value to pollinators.
