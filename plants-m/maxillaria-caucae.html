Maxillaria caucae

Maxillaria caucae, commonly known as the Cauca Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth in high-altitude environments. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with white markings, which are typically fragrant. Its leaves are strap-shaped and grass-like, adapted to the cool, moist conditions of its habitat.

Maxillaria caucae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
