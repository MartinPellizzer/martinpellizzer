Maxillaria geckophora

Maxillaria geckophora, commonly known as the gecko orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and hairy, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers with a complex, often colorful pattern on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria geckophora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
