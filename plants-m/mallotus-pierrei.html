Mallotus pierrei

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and yellowish flowers. The tree produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and contain a single seed. It is often found in coastal and lowland forests, where it plays an important ecological role.
