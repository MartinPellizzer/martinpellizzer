Melastoma octandrum

Melastoma octandrum, commonly known as the eight-stamen melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Melastoma octandrum is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a resilient species in tropical and subtropical regions.
