Mandevilla longiflora

Mandevilla longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered mandevilla, is a vigorous climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often with a subtle fragrance. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture, and it produces elongated, cylindrical fruit pods. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla longiflora is popular in ornamental horticulture for its ornate blooms and vigorous growth habit.
