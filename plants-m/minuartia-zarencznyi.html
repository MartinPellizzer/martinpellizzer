Minuartia zarencznyi

Minuartia zarencznyi, commonly known as the Zarencznyi sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant has small, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, adapting it to harsh environmental conditions. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils, often found in alpine or arctic regions.

This species is known for its resilience and is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil in challenging environments.
