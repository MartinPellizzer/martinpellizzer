Macroscepis stevensii

Macroscepis stevensii, commonly known as the yellow-flowered macrocephalum, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. The plant is characterized by its bright yellow ray florets and a central disk of smaller, tubular flowers. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

It is native to southern Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
