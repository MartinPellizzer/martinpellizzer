Melocactus poliacanthus

Melocactus poliacanthus, commonly known as the red-hair melocactus, is a columnar cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive red or orange spines that give it its common name. The plant has a dense, woolly covering of white to grayish hairs on its stem. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Melocactus poliacanthus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
