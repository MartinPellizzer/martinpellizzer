Medicago polycarpa var. denticulata

Medicago polycarpa var. denticulata, commonly known as the dented goat's rue, is a perennial leguminous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with serrated margins and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several small, oblong seeds.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and waste areas.
