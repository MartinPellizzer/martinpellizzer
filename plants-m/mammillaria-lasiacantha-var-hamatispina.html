Mammillaria lasiacantha var. hamatispina

Mammillaria lasiacantha var. hamatispina, commonly known as the needle spine mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with numerous radial spines that are typically white to yellowish in color. The spines are often described as needle-like and are arranged in dense clusters around the stem. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
