Marlea Villosa

Marlea villosa is a unique and resilient plant native to southern Africa. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Known for its soft, woolly foliage, this plant has a distinctive appearance that sets it apart from other species. Despite its delicate look, it is highly drought-tolerant and requires minimal care. Marlea villosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature.
