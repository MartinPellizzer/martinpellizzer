Masdevallia × macvitiae

Masdevallia × macvitiae, commonly known as the MacVitiae orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces pendant or upright inflorescences with showy, asymmetrical flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Masdevallia × macvitiae thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
