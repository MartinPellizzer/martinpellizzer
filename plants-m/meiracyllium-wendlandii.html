Meiracyllium wendlandii

Meiracyllium wendlandii, commonly known as Wendland's meiracyllium, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, requiring minimal water once established.
