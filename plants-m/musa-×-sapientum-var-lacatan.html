Musa × sapientum var. lacatan

Musa × sapientum var. lacatan, commonly known as the red banana, is a cultivar of the banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red-purple peel, which turns yellow as it ripens. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with large, broad leaves that form a pseudo-stem. It produces clusters of berries, each containing a single seed, and thrives in warm, humid climates.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
