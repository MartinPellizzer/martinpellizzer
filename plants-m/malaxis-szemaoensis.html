Malaxis szemaoensis

Malaxis szemaoensis, commonly known as the Szechuan Malaxis, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant grows in shaded, moist environments, typically in mountainous regions. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its delicate appearance and ecological significance.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
