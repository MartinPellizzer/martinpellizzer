Myosotis sicula var. virgata

Myosotis sicula var. virgata, commonly known as the Sicilian forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

It is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, contributing to the floral diversity of its habitat.
