Maxillaria fimbriatiloba

Maxillaria fimbriatiloba, commonly known as the fringed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and fringed petals, which give it its common name.

Maxillaria fimbriatiloba prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
