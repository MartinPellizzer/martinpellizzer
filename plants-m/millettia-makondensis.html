Millettia makondensis

Millettia makondensis, commonly known as the Makondensis Millettia, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
