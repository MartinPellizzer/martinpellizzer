Medinilla angustoacuminata

Medinilla angustoacuminata, commonly known as the narrow-pointed medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly pubescent. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often with a pink or white coloration. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla angustoacuminata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
