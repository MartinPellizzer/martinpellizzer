Milium strictum

Milium strictum, commonly known as rigid millet, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white to pale green flowers. It is native to temperate regions of Asia and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.

Milium strictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
