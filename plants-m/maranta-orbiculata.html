Maranta orbiculata

Maranta orbiculata, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that fold upwards at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf patterns and colors. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
