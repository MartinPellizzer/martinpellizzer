Mammillaria mollihamata

Mammillaria mollihamata, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, white, woolly covering that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in a globular shape with short, cylindrical stems. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
