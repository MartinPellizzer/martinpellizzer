Monanthochilus iboensis

Monanthochilus iboensis, commonly known as the Ibo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Nigeria.

It is a small, terrestrial herb with a single, hairy stem and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered threatened and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
