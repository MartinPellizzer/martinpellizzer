Morinda speciosa

Morinda speciosa, commonly known as the Chinese starfruit or Indian mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped yellow flowers. The plant produces red, fleshy berries that are edible and often used in traditional medicine. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant flowers and unique fruit structure.
