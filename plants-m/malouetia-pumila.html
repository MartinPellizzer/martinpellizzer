Malouetia pumila

Malouetia pumila, commonly known as the dwarf pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are oval-shaped and often have a slight waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Malouetia pumila is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
