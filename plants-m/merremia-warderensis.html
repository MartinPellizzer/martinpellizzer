Merremia warderensis

Merremia warderensis, commonly known as the Warder's morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange.
