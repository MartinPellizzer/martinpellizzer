Mandevilla permixta

Mandevilla permixta, commonly known as the hybrid mandevilla, is a vigorous trailing or climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom profusely throughout the growing season. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla permixta is often used in containers, hanging baskets, or as a ground cover due to its rapid growth and ornamental value.
