Masdevallia maloi

Masdevallia maloi, commonly known as the Maloi orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, lance-shaped leaves and a compact growth habit. The flowers are typically pale green to brownish in color, with intricate patterns that mimic the appearance of moss or lichen. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful watering to avoid root rot.

This orchid is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
