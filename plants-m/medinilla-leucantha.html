Medinilla leucantha

Medinilla leucantha, commonly known as the white medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla leucantha is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
