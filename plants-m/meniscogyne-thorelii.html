Meniscogyne thorelii

Meniscogyne thorelii, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant belonging to the Araceae family.

It is native to Central and West Africa, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features a rhizomatous root system that stores starch, making it valuable for its edible tubers. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a waxy texture.

The plant also produces striking spadix inflorescences, typical of the Araceae family.
