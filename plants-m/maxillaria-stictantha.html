Maxillaria stictantha

Maxillaria stictantha, commonly known as the spotted maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. The plant typically has dark green, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Maxillaria stictantha is also notable for its ability to bloom multiple times throughout the year under suitable conditions.
