Microsteira decaryi

Microsteira decaryi, commonly known as the African dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, strap-like leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

Microsteira decaryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
