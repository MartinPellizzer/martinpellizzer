Montjolya mollis

Montjolya mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf montjolya, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Montjolya mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
