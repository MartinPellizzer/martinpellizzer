Machilus yunnanensis

Machilus yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan Machilus, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in catkins, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
