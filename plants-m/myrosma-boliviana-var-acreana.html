Myrosma boliviana var. acreana

Myrosma boliviana var. acreana, commonly known as the Acre myrosma, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a distinctive aromatic scent when crushed.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to the rich, nutrient-dense soils of the rainforest.
