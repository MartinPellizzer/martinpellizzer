Melastoma annuum

Melastoma annuum, commonly known as the annual Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual or biennial herb with opposite, ovate leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Melastoma annuum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or gardens.
