Magnolia yajlachhi

Magnolia yajlachhi, commonly known as the Kashmiri magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the Himalayan region.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 10 meters tall.

The flowers are primarily pollinated by insects, and the tree produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds.
