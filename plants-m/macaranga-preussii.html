Macaranga preussii

Macaranga preussii, commonly known as the velvetleaf or African elephant tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety layer of hairs.
