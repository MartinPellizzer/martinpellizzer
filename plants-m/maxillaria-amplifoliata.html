Maxillaria amplifoliata

Maxillaria amplifoliata, commonly known as the broad-leafed maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are often broader than they are long. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the summer months. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
