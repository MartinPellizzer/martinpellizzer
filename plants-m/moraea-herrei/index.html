Moraea Herrei

Moraea herrei is a striking member of the Iridaceae family known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to South Africa, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun. Its blooms, often in shades of red, orange, and yellow, make it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators. Due to its limited distribution, Moraea herrei is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
