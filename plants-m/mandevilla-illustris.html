Mandevilla illustris

Mandevilla illustris, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its foliage consists of large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that provide a lush backdrop for the flowers.

Mandevilla illustris is often cultivated as a climbing plant or in containers due to its vigorous growth and ornamental value.
