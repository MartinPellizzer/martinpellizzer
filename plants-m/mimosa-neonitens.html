Mimosa neonitens

Mimosa neonitens, commonly known as the neon mimosas, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Its rapid growth and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental planting.
