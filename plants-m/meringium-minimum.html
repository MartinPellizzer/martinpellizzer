Meringium minimum

Meringium minimum, commonly known as the dwarf meringium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features delicate, pale green leaves that are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs. The plant produces tiny, white flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Meringium minimum thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to gardens and natural habitats.
