Machilus macrophyllus var. arisanensis

Machilus macrophyllus var. arisanensis, commonly known as the Arisan mahonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Taiwan and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in mountainous regions. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by dark blue berries.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
