Mussaenda longifolia

Mussaenda longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered Mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink and orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Mussaenda longifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
