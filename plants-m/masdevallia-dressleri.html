Masdevallia dressleri

Masdevallia dressleri, commonly known as the Christmas Masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features a compact, rhizomatous growth habit with a single, erect stem that bears a few lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and red, typically blooming during the holiday season. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a prized ornamental plant.

It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.
