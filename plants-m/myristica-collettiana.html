Myristica collettiana

Myristica collettiana, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and dense, glossy green foliage. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that give way to red, fleshy fruits containing the aromatic seeds used as nutmeg and mace. Its roots and leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is highly valued for its economic and cultural significance in the spice trade.
