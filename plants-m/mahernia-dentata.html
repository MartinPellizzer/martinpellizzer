Mahernia dentata

Mahernia dentata, commonly known as the toothed mahernia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. Its leaves are serrated and have a distinctive toothed margin, contributing to its common name.

Mahernia dentata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
