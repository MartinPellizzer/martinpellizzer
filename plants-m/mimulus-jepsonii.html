Mimulus jepsonii

Mimulus jepsonii, commonly known as the Jepson's monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and meadows.
