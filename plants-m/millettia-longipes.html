Millettia longipes

Millettia longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed millettia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its long, slender stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by pod fruits containing several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
