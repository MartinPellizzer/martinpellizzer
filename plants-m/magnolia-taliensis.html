Magnolia taliensis

Magnolia taliensis, commonly known as the Taliensis magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
