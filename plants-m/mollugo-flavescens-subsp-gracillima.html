Mollugo flavescens subsp. gracillima

Mollugo flavescens subsp.

gracillima, commonly known as the slender bladderwort, is a small, herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its delicate, hairless stems and small, yellowish-green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in small inflorescences. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
