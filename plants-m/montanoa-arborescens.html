Montanoa arborescens

Montanoa arborescens, commonly known as the tree mallow, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters tall, featuring a woody stem and broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Montanoa arborescens is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.
