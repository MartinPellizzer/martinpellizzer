Monstera imrayana

Monstera imrayana, commonly known as the Imrayana monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive fenestrations as they mature. The plant is an evergreen climbing vine that can reach significant heights in its natural habitat. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by black berries.

Monstera imrayana is popular as a houseplant due to its striking foliage and air-purifying qualities.
