Muhlenbergia subtilis

Muhlenbergia subtilis, commonly known as the silky muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, fluffy seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping. Its ornamental value comes from its soft texture and graceful appearance, which enhance garden aesthetics.

It is also valued for its low maintenance requirements and ability to withstand drought conditions.
