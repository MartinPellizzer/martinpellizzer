Morysia velutina

Morysia velutina, commonly known as the velvet morysia, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a sought-after species in botanical gardens.

Morysia velutina is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
