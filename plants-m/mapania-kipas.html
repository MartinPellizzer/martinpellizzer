Mapania kipas

Mapania kipas, commonly known as the Kipas tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

The Kipas tree also plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
