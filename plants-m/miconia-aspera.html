Miconia aspera

Miconia aspera, commonly known as the rough miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, lowland forests. Miconia aspera is also known for its ability to form dense thickets, which can impact local ecosystems.
