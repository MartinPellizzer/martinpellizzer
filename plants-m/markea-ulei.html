Markea ulei

Markea ulei, commonly known as the African butter tree, is a small evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by edible, oval-shaped fruits that are rich in oils. Its leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
