Morelosia radula

Morelosia radula, commonly known as the naked foot liverwort, is a small, leafy liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its simple, undivided leaves that are often covered in a layer of fine, translucent cells. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. It lacks true roots and instead absorbs nutrients through its leaves.
