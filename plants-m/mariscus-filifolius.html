Mariscus filifolius

Mariscus filifolius, commonly known as the slenderleaf mariscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported diuretic properties.

Mariscus filifolius is also valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid conditions.
