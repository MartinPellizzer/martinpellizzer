Malaxis dolichostachys

Malaxis dolichostachys, commonly known as the long-stalked malaxis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. It features a slender, wiry stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf.

The flower spike produces several small, greenish-white flowers with a distinct lip, characteristic of many orchid species.
