Melastoma glabrum

Melastoma glabrum, commonly known as the smooth melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a smooth texture, contributing to its common name.

Melastoma glabrum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
