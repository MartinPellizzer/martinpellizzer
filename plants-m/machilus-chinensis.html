Machilus chinensis

Machilus chinensis, commonly known as Chinese machilus, is a flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub native to eastern Asia, particularly China. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, dark blue to black drupe, which contains a single seed.
