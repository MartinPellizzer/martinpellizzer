Melastoma raussinia

Melastoma raussinia, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces clusters of showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma raussinia is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
