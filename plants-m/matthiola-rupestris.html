Matthiola rupestris

Matthiola rupestris, commonly known as the rock stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, blooming in spring and early summer. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Matthiola rupestris thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and alpine settings.
