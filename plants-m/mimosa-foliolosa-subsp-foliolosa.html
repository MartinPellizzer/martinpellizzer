Mimosa foliolosa subsp. foliolosa

Mimosa foliolosa subsp.

foliolosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sensitive plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America. It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which fold shut when touched, a response triggered by rapid changes in turgor pressure within the leaflets.

The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique foliage and sensitivity to touch make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
