Myosotis austrosibirica

Myosotis austrosibirica, commonly known as the Siberian forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of Central Asia and Siberia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, alpine or subarctic environments. The plant produces small, blue or purple flowers with five petals, which bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to its harsh climate.
