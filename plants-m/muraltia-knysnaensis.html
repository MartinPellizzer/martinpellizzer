Muraltia knysnaensis

Muraltia knysnaensis, commonly known as Knysna mallow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive reddish-brown underside. Its showy, five-petaled flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in spring and summer.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, brown seeds.
