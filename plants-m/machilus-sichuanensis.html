Machilus sichuanensis

Machilus sichuanensis, commonly known as the Sichuan Machilus, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, followed by dark blue-black berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in subtropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
