Melia azedarach var. sambucina

Melia azedarach var. sambucina, commonly known as the Chinese white ash or the pretty ash, is a variant of the chinaberry tree.

It is a deciduous tree native to Asia, characterized by its showy, fragrant white flowers that appear in clusters. The leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a soft texture. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This variant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional uses in herbal medicine.
