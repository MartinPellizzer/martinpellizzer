Myristica dasycarpa

Myristica dasycarpa, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and aromatic fruits. The plant produces oval, woody fruits that enclose a single seed, which is surrounded by a thick, aromatic aril. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The tree is valued for its potential medicinal properties and is also studied for its role in traditional herbal practices.
