Magnolia balansae

Magnolia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, often in shades of white and pink. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It is a deciduous tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinct appearance that adds to its ornamental value.
