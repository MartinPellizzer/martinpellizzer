Mikania nana

Mikania nana, commonly known as dwarf mikania, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows close to the ground, with slender stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually yellow and occur in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Mikania nana is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
