Miconia ovalifolia

Miconia ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia ovalifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.
