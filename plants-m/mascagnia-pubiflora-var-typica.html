Mascagnia pubiflora var. typica

Mascagnia pubiflora var. typica, commonly known as the hairy mascagnia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

Mascagnia pubiflora var. typica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
