Myosotis × degener

Myosotis × degener, commonly known as the hybrid forget-me-not, is a perennial flowering plant that belongs to the Boraginaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Myosotis sylvatica and Myosotis rubicunda, exhibiting characteristics of both parent species. The plant typically grows to a height of 15–30 cm, with slender stems and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually blue or violet, with five petals, and bloom from late spring to early autumn.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or meadows.
