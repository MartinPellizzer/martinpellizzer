Masdevallia ivanii

Masdevallia ivanii, commonly known as the Ivan's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, anchoring itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small but striking, with intricate patterns and vibrant hues.

Due to its limited distribution and specific environmental requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its native region.
