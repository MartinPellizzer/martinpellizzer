Mammillaria scrippsiana var. pseudoscrippsiana

Mammillaria scrippsiana var. pseudoscrippsiana, commonly known as the Scrippsian mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features a rounded stem with cylindrical, ribbed segments that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is known for its slow growth and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in arid environments.
