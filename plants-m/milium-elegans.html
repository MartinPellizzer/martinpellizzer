Milium elegans

Milium elegans, commonly known as silvergrass, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses and cereal crops. This perennial plant features slender, silver-gray blades that are often used in ornamental landscaping due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Milium elegans produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer, adding delicate beauty to its appearance.
