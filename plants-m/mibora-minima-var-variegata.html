Mibora minima var. variegata

Mibora minima var. variegata, commonly known as the variegated mibora, is a small, compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves with striking variegation of white and yellow, creating a visually appealing contrast against its deep green base. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for indoor cultivation.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for collectors and plant enthusiasts.
