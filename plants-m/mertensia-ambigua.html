Mertensia ambigua

Mertensia ambigua, commonly known as the Canada anemone, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring, often before the leaves emerge. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, while its stems are hairy and upright.

Mertensia ambigua is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance, providing nectar for early pollinators.
