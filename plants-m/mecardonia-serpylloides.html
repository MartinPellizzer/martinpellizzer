Mecardonia serpylloides

Mecardonia serpylloides, commonly known as the narrowleaf meadow grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open, grassy areas and meadows. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowers are wind-pollinated, which is common among grasses.
