Marsdenia stefaninii

Marsdenia stefaninii, commonly known as the Stefaninii Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to Madagascar and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, typical of the genus. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
