Microchirita mollissima

Microchirita mollissima, commonly known as the velvetleaf microchirita, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
