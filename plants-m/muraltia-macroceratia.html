Muraltia macroceratia

Muraltia macroceratia, commonly known as the red muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, the Americas, and Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. These berries are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural dye.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
