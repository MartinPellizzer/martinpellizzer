Masdevallia unguentum

Masdevallia unguentum, commonly known as the golden lip orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, dark green leaves and its most distinctive feature: a vibrant orange or red flower with a unique, lip-shaped petal that resembles a lip. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a splash of color to its natural habitat.

Masdevallia unguentum is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and rarity.
