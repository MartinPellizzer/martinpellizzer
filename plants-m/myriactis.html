Myriactis

Myriactis, commonly known as the golden star, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Myriactis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
