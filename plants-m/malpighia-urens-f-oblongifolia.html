Malpighia urens f. oblongifolia

Malpighia urens f. oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaf beautyberry, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malpighiaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, showy, violet to red berries that appear in clusters, attracting birds and other wildlife. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, with elliptical to oblong-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. Native to tropical regions of the Americas, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
