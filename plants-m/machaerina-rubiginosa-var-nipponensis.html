Machaerina rubiginosa var. nipponensis

Machaerina rubiginosa var. nipponensis, commonly known as the Japanese red lily, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers with a distinctive red or brownish coloration. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a waxy texture, while its stems are slender and erect.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
