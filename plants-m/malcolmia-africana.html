Malcolmia africana

Malcolmia africana, commonly known as African malva, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often found in grasslands and along riverbanks, where it contributes to local biodiversity.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
