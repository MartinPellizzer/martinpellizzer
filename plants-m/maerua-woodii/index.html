Maerua Woodii

Maerua woodii, scientifically known as Maerua woodii, is a rare and critically endangered shrub native to the arid regions of southern Africa. This unique plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, waxy leaves that help it conserve water in harsh environments. Despite its resilience, Maerua woodii faces severe threats from habitat loss and overgrazing, leading to its near extinction in the wild. Conservation efforts are urgently needed to protect this species and preserve its ecological role. Its survival is a vital indicator of the health of its fragile desert ecosystem.
