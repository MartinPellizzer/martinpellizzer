Medicago archiducis-nicolai

Medicago archiducis-nicolai, commonly known as the Archduke Nicholas clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and dry meadows.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it ecologically significant in soil enrichment.
