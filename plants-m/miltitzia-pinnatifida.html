Miltitzia pinnatifida

Miltitzia pinnatifida, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It features compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance. The plant produces striking clusters of orange-red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall.

Miltitzia pinnatifida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
