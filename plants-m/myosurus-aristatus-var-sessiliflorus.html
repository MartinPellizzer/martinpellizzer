Myosurus aristatus var. sessiliflorus

Myosurus aristatus var. sessiliflorus, commonly known as the sessile-flower rush, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, commonly known as the sedge family. The plant is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, linear leaves and small, clustered flower heads. Its flowers are typically brown or reddish and are sessile, meaning they lack stalks.
