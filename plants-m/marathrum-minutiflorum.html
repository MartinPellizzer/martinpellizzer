Marathrum minutiflorum

Marathrum minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf flame flower, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a small shrub with narrow, linear leaves and produces clusters of bright orange-red tubular flowers. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
