Macrothelypteris oligophlebia

Macrothelypteris oligophlebia, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, tree-like fronds that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has a robust rhizome system that supports its extensive growth. Its fronds are bipinnate, with a distinctive, segmented appearance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
