Malaxis acuminata var. biloba

Malaxis acuminata var. biloba, commonly known as the bilobed malaxis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically arranged in a loose inflorescence and have a distinctive bilobed lip.

This orchid thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.
