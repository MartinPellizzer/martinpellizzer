Myristica flavovirens

Myristica flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green nutmeg.

It is a species of evergreen tree belonging to the Myristicaceae family, native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and elongated, elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, while the fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the aromatic seeds.

The plant is valued for its unique fragrance and is often cultivated for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
