Musa schweinfurthii

Musa schweinfurthii, commonly known as the African banana, is a species of banana plant native to Central and West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange berries that are edible and have a tangy, slightly acidic flavor. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated for both its fruit and aesthetic appeal.
