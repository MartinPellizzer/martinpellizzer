Mesanthemum erici-rosenii

Mesanthemum erici-rosenii, commonly known as the Cape rose or ericaceous rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and ability to attract pollinators.
