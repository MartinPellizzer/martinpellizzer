Mandevilla loeseneriana

Mandevilla loeseneriana, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and is often cultivated as a decorative shrub or climber. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla loeseneriana is popular for its ornamental value and ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
