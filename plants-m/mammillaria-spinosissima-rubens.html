Mammillaria spinosissima rubens

Mammillaria spinosissima rubens, commonly known as the red spiny mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in small, rounded tubercles that bear white to pale pink flowers. The plant has prominent spines, which are typically reddish in color, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ornamental value lies in its vibrant flowers and unique spiny appearance.
