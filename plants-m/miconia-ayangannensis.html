Miconia ayangannensis

Miconia ayangannensis, commonly known as the Ayangán or Ayanganné tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful leaves and showy inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside habitats. Its leaves are usually opposite, with a glossy surface and serrated margins, while its flowers are clustered in dense, showy bracts.

Miconia ayangannensis is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
