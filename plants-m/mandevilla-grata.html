Mandevilla grata

Mandevilla grata, commonly known as the sweet mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow up to 10 feet in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla grata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
