Maxillaria concavilabia

Maxillaria concavilabia, commonly known as the concave-labied maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique, concave lip. The plant typically blooms in the summer, with flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often grown as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
