Metastelma burchellii

Metastelma burchellii, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the winter months. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and a compact growth habit.

Metastelma burchellii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.
