Montanoa echinacea

Montanoa echinacea, commonly known as the Mexican coneflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and thrives in full sun. Its hairy stems and lobed leaves are adapted to arid environments.

Montanoa echinacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
