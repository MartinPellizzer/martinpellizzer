Mostuea ulugurensis

Mostuea ulugurensis, commonly known as the Uluguru banana, is a species of banana plant native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clustered, elongated fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. It is adapted to grow in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This species is valued for its edible fruit and is also studied for its potential in agricultural and ecological research.
