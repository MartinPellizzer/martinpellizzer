Mimosa misera var. subinermis

Mimosa misera var. subinermis, commonly known as the soft mimosa, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variety is less prickly than other mimosa species, making it more suitable for ornamental gardening.
