Mikania rufa

Mikania rufa, commonly known as the redstem mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and streambanks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have hairy surfaces.

Mikania rufa is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
