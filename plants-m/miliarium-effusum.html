Miliarium effusum

Miliarium effusum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical annual plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal spikes. Its seeds are small and oval, often dispersed by wind or through animal activity.

Miliarium effusum is often found in disturbed soils and can become invasive in certain regions.
