Maughania Strobilifera

Maughania strobilifera is a rare and intriguing plant native to the cloud forests of Central America. It is known for its unique appearance and specialized ecological role in its habitat. This species thrives in humid, shaded environments where it forms dense, low-growing mats. Its distinctive reproductive structures set it apart from other plants in the region. Due to habitat loss, Maughania strobilifera is now considered endangered and requires urgent conservation efforts.
