Mimosa micracantha var. plurijuga

Mimosa micracantha var. plurijuga, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mimosoid, is a shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences.

The species is adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
