Myosotis pontica

Myosotis pontica, commonly known as the Pontic forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus and parts of Turkey.

It features delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Myosotis pontica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and naturalized areas.
