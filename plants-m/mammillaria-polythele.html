Mammillaria polythele

Mammillaria polythele, commonly known as the Mexican pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and prominent tubercles. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its stems are covered in fine, white spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with bright sunlight.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
