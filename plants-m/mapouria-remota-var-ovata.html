Mapouria remota var. ovata

Mapouria remota var. ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf mapouria, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
