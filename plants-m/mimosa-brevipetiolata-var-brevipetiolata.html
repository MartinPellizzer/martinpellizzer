Mimosa brevipetiolata var. brevipetiolata

Mimosa brevipetiolata var. brevipetiolata, commonly known as the dwarf mimosa, is a small shrub native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically clustered at the base of the plant. The plant produces small, yellow, ball-shaped flowers that are borne in clusters. It is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and erosion control.
