Moehringia trinervia var. pentandra

Moehringia trinervia var. pentandra, commonly known as the five-veined moehringia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pink or white flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer, adding delicate beauty to its habitat.
