Mutisia retrorsa var. normalis

Mutisia retrorsa var. normalis, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow ray florets and greenish-yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has a woody stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, spherical capitula that are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
