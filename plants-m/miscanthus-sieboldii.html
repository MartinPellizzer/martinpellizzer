Miscanthus sieboldii

Miscanthus sieboldii, commonly known as the silver grass or Japanese silver grass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features tall, slender stems that are covered in a silvery-white bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces fluffy, feathery plumes that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its foliage and floral display provide seasonal interest, from summer to autumn.
