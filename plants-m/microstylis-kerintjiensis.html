Microstylis kerintjiensis

Microstylis kerintjiensis, commonly known as the Kerintji grass, is a perennial herb native to the savannas of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It blooms with pale blue to purple flowers, which are arranged in dense clusters.

Microstylis kerintjiensis is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in open, disturbed habitats.
