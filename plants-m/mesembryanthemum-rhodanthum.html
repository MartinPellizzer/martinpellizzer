Mesembryanthemum rhodanthum

Mesembryanthemum rhodanthum, commonly known as the red-hot poker, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and showy, orange-red flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
