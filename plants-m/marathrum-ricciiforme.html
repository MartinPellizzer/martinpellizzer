Marathrum ricciiforme

Marathrum ricciiforme, commonly known as the velvet tassel plant, is a unique flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, and often colorful inflorescences that resemble tassels or plumes. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually small, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are borne in dense clusters, giving the plant its distinctive appearance and making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
