Millettia wrightiana

Millettia wrightiana, commonly known as Wright's millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals. Millettia wrightiana is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important component of diverse tropical ecosystems.
