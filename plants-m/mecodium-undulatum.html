Mecodium undulatum

Mecodium undulatum, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to purple. Mecodium undulatum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
