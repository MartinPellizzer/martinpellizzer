Mammillaria longimamma var. gigantothele

Mammillaria longimamma var. gigantothele, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a rare and striking cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height, making it one of the tallest species in the Mammillaria genus. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments.
