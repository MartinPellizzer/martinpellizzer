Monadenium stapelioides var. congestum

Monadenium stapelioides var. congestum, commonly known as the clustered milk bush, is a unique succulent native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, branched stem structure with thick, fleshy leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tinge. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, adding a subtle ornamental touch. Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it well adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
