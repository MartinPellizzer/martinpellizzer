Mikania sprucei

Mikania sprucei, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a vigorous climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant thrives in moist, tropical environments and can quickly spread through its extensive root system. Its rapid growth makes it both a valuable ornamental plant and a potential invasive species in certain regions.

Mikania sprucei is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
