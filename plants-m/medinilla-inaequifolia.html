Medinilla inaequifolia

Medinilla inaequifolia, commonly known as the unequal-leaved medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped leaves that vary in size along the stem. Medinilla inaequifolia is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
