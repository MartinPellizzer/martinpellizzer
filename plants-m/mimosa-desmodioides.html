Mimosa desmodioides

Mimosa desmodioides, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features delicate, feathery leaves that fold shut when touched, a response known as thigmonasty. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along riverbanks.

Its showy yellow flowers bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
