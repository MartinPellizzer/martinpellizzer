Mammillaria vallensis var. brevispina

Mammillaria vallensis var. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to bluish-green body covered in short, white to grayish spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters, with a diameter of up to 12 centimeters. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and arid landscapes due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appearance.
