Miconia puracensis

Miconia puracensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Central America, particularly found in the highlands of Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
