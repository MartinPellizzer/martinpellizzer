Maxillaria rhodosticta

Maxillaria rhodosticta, commonly known as the pink-streaked orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple, often with intricate streaking patterns. Its pseudobulbs are covered in a dense layer of brownish hairs, and it thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria rhodosticta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
