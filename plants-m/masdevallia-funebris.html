Masdevallia funebris

Masdevallia funebris, commonly known as the funeral orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive appearance, featuring dark, velvety flowers that range from deep purple to black. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in cloud forests at high altitudes. Its leaves are long and narrow, and it produces small, clustered blooms that are highly prized by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and challenging species to cultivate.
