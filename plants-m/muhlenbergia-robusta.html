Muhlenbergia robusta

Muhlenbergia robusta, commonly known as the robust muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Muhlenbergia robusta is often used in landscaping for its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
