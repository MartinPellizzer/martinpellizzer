Maranta florestina

Maranta florestina, commonly known as the forest maranta, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant colors, such as red and purple veins. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Maranta florestina is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
