Mammillaria compressa var. rubrispina

Mammillaria compressa var. rubrispina, commonly known as the red-spined pincushion cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, rounded tubercles covered with white to pale blue spines, which give it a pincushion-like appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique spines and compact growth make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
