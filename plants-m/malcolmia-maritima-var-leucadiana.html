Malcolmia maritima var. leucadiana

Malcolmia maritima var. leucadiana, commonly known as the white-flowered sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a low-growing annual or biennial herb characterized by its silver-gray foliage and showy white or pale yellow flowers. The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dunes or along shorelines. Its flowers typically bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal soils.
