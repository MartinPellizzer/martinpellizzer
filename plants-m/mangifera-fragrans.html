Mangifera fragrans

Mangifera fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant mango, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and produces sweet, aromatic fruits. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are small and clustered, often fragrant, which attracts pollinators like bees.

The tree can grow up to 30 meters tall and is widely cultivated for its edible fruit.
