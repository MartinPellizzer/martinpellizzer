Melinis monachne var. minor

Melinis monachne var. minor, commonly known as little quillwort, is a small aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow, freshwater environments such as ponds and marshes. The plant has narrow, ribbon-like leaves that are arranged in a whorled pattern around the stem. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often submerged or floating.

This variety is valued for its role in aquatic ecosystems, providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
