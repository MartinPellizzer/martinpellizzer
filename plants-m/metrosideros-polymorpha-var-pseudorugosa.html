Metrosideros polymorpha var. pseudorugosa

Metrosideros polymorpha var. pseudorugosa, commonly known as the rough-leaved 'Ohia, is a variant of the native Hawaiian 'Ohia tree.

It is characterized by its rough, textured leaves and is typically found in moist, forested areas of Hawaii. The plant is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is known for its vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. It plays an important ecological role in Hawaiian ecosystems, providing habitat and nectar for native birds and insects.
