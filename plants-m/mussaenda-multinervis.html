Mussaenda multinervis

Mussaenda multinervis, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Mussaenda multinervis is frequently cultivated for its vibrant flowers and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
