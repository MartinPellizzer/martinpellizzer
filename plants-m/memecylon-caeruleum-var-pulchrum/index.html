Memecylon Caeruleum Var. Pulchrum

Memecylon caeruleum var. pulchrum is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia. Known for its vibrant blue flowers and lush green foliage, it stands out in any garden or natural setting. This variant of Memecylon caeruleum is particularly prized for its ornamental value and unique coloration. It thrives in humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its delicate appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and garden enthusiasts alike.
