Morus alba var. laciniata

Morus alba var. laciniata, commonly known as the Chinese mulberry or shredded mulberry, is a variant of the white mulberry tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely serrated leaves that give it a feathery appearance. This plant is native to China and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. The leaves are used in traditional Chinese medicine and as a food source for silkworms.

It thrives in temperate climates and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
