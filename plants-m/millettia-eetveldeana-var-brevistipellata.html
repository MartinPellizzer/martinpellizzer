Millettia eetveldeana var. brevistipellata

Millettia eetveldeana var. brevistipellata, commonly known as the short-tipped millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, with a distinctive yellow or greenish coloration.

The varietal name "brevistipellata" refers to its short, blunt stipules, a key identifying feature.
