Mesanthemum albidum

Mesanthemum albidum, commonly known as the white mesanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Mesanthemum albidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
