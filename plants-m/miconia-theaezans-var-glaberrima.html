Miconia theaezans var. glaberrima

Miconia theaezans var. glaberrima, commonly known as the glabrous miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, where it thrives in humid, lowland forests. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
