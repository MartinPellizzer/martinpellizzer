Microsteira firingalavae

Microsteira firingalavae, commonly known as the Fireleaf Microsteira, is a unique flowering plant native to the volcanic regions of Eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves that exhibit a striking red or orange hue, especially under sunlight. The plant produces tiny, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
