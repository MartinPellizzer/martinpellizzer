Maguireothamnus speciosus subsp. jauaensis

Maguireothamnus speciosus subsp.

jauaensis, commonly known as the Jauaensis goldenbush, is a shrub native to the arid regions of northern Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in heads, which are surrounded by leaf-like bracts. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with low rainfall and high temperatures.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
