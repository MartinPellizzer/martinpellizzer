Melastoma antennatum

Melastoma antennatum, commonly known as the slender melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, violet-blue flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine pubescence.

Melastoma antennatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
