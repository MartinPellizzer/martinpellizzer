Melasma asperrimum

Melasma asperrimum, commonly known as the rough melasma, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rugged, textured leaves and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its botanical name reflects the roughness of its foliage, which is a distinguishing feature.

It is often found in tropical and subtropical climates where humidity is high.
