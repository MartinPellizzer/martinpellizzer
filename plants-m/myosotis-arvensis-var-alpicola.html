Myosotis arvensis var. alpicola

Myosotis arvensis var. alpicola, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows. The plant features delicate blue to violet flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, white pubescence.

It is adapted to cold climates and thrives in well-drained, nutrient-poor soils.
