Mammillaria megacantha

Mammillaria megacantha, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are covered in prominent, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun to thrive.
