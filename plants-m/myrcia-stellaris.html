Myrcia stellaris

Myrcia stellaris, commonly known as the star myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, with a strong aromatic scent when crushed.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a dark red color and is edible.
