Macroclinium roseum

Macroclinium roseum, commonly known as the pink torch orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers that resemble torches, with a velvety texture. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It requires bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.
