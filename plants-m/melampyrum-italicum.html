Melampyrum italicum

Melampyrum italicum, commonly known as Italian touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant produces striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a reddish or purple tinge.

The species is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
