Majana scutellariodes

Majana scutellariodes, commonly known as the African bluebell, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Majana scutellariodes is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
