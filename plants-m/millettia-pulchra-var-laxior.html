Millettia pulchra var. laxior

Millettia pulchra var. laxior, commonly known as the pink shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking pink or red flowers that bloom in pendulous clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its rapid growth and ornamental value. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three leaflets that are often hairy and serrated.

The tree is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is used in ornamental horticulture.
