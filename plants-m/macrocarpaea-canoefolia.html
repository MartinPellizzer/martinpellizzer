Macrocarpaea canoefolia

Macrocarpaea canoefolia, commonly known as the Canary Island heath, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and rhododendrons. The plant is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form with small, leathery leaves. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, appearing in clusters during the spring and summer months.
