Microula ciliaris

Microula ciliaris, commonly known as the Kashmiri sandmat, is a flowering plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its erect, branching stems and small, greenish flowers. Microula ciliaris is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
