Malva punctata

Malva punctata, commonly known as the spotted mallows, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive purplish or reddish margin. The plant produces showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Malva punctata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
