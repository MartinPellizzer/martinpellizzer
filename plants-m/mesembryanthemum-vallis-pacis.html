Mesembryanthemum vallis-pacis

Mesembryanthemum vallis-pacis, commonly known as the valley of peace iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators. Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a versatile and hardy addition to arid landscapes.
