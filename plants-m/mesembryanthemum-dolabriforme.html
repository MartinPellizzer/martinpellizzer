Mesembryanthemum dolabriforme

Mesembryanthemum dolabriforme, commonly known as the broad-leafed ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, helping it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring and summer.
