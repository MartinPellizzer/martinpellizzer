Maba major

Maba major, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a striking flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night, emitting a strong fragrance to attract nocturnal pollinators. The plant has a woody stem and broad, palmate leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its flowers typically bloom during the evening and close by dawn, making it a unique addition to any botanical garden.

The species is also valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
