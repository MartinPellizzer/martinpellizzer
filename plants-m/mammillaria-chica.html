Mammillaria chica

Mammillaria chica, commonly known as the "Chica Cactus," is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a greenish-gray stem covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
