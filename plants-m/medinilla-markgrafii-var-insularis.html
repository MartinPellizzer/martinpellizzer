Medinilla markgrafii var. insularis

Medinilla markgrafii var. insularis, commonly known as the Island Medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It features attractive, glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
