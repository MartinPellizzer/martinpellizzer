Malanea glabra

Malanea glabra, commonly known as the smooth malanea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its simple, smooth leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in forest understories.
