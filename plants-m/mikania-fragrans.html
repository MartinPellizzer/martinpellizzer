Mikania fragrans

Mikania fragrans, commonly known as sweetleaf, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic foliage and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, often found in moist, shaded areas. Its leaves are heart-shaped and emit a sweet scent when crushed.

Mikania fragrans is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
