Maxillaria undatiflora

Maxillaria undatiflora, commonly known as the undulate-flowered orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are typically pale green to white, with intricate patterns and a unique undulating shape.

Maxillaria undatiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
