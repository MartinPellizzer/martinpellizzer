Magnolia clementinana

Magnolia clementinana, commonly known as the Cuban magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Cuba.

It features large, showy, white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy green surface. The tree has a fast growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
