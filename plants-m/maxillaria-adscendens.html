Maxillaria adscendens

Maxillaria adscendens, commonly known as the ascending maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers are borne on erect, branching inflorescences and often have a velvety texture.

Maxillaria adscendens is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
