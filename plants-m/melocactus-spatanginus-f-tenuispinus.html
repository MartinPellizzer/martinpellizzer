Melocactus spatanginus f. tenuispinus

Melocactus spatanginus f. tenuispinus, commonly known as the slender-spined melocactus, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem that often remains relatively small in size. The plant produces spines that are typically slender and widely spaced, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the ribs.

This variety is well adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and thriving in full sun.
