Musa × sapientum var. liukiuensis

Musa × sapientum var. liukiuensis, commonly known as the Liukiu banana, is a variety of banana native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is a diploid plant, characterized by its relatively small, elongated fruits that are typically harvested when they reach a mature yellow color. The plant has broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often used in traditional Japanese cuisine and crafts. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique genetic makeup, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists for its potential in breeding programs.
