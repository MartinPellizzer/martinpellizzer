Masdevallia palmensis

Masdevallia palmensis, commonly known as the Palmensis Masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces intricate, showy inflorescences with flowers that range from violet to deep purple, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light intensity for optimal growth.

Due to its limited natural habitat and specific care requirements, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
