Murraya stenocarpa

Murraya stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited murraya, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellowish fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
