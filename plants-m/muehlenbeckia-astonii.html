Muehlenbeckia astonii

Muehlenbeckia astonii, commonly known as silverweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing perennial that forms dense mats of silver-green foliage. The plant features small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving them a distinctive silvery appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and montane environments.
