Mammillaria odieriana var. aurea

Mammillaria odieriana var. aurea, commonly known as the golden mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in small, rounded tubercles that are often adorned with white or yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
