Miconia mantuensis

Miconia mantuensis, commonly known as the mantuensis miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in the Amazon basin. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
