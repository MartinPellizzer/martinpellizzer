Mimosa baptistae

Mimosa baptistae, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is known for its sensitive response to touch, causing the leaves to fold when disturbed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Mimosa baptistae is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility.
