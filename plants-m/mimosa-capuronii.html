Mimosa capuronii

Mimosa capuronii, commonly known as the Capuron's sensitive plant, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that are clustered in racemes. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to arid environments.

Mimosa capuronii is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
