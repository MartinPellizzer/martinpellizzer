Monstera alcirana

Monstera alcirana, commonly known as the African giant leaf plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to West Africa.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its stems are long and vine-like, often requiring support as it climbs.

The leaves are glossy and have a unique, almost sculptural appearance that makes it a popular ornamental plant.
