Millettia leucantha var. latifolia

Millettia leucantha var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved millettia, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural use in shaded gardens.
