Maxillaria tigrina

Maxillaria tigrina, commonly known as the Tiger Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green to blackish leaves that are often adorned with white or yellow markings. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow, with a distinctive tiger-like pattern on the petals. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments.

Maxillaria tigrina is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
