Magnolia ventii

Magnolia ventii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Magnolia ventii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
