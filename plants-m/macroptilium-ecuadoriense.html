Macroptilium ecuadoriense

Macroptilium ecuadoriense, commonly known as the Ecuadorian macroptilium, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

It is often found in forest understories and is adapted to the humid, shaded environments of its native habitat.
