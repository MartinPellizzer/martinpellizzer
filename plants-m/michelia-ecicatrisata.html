Michelia ecicatrisata

Michelia ecicatrisata, commonly known as the fragrant michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape and have a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it often has a pleasant, spicy aroma.

Michelia ecicatrisata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in traditional medicine.
