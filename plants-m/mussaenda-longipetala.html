Mussaenda longipetala

Mussaenda longipetala, commonly known as the long-petaled mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that are typically yellow or orange in color. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
