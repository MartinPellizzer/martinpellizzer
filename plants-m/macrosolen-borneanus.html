Macrosolen borneanus

Macrosolen borneanus, commonly known as the Bornean giant fern, is a large, tropical fern native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its massive, compound leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture. Its fronds are typically bipinnate, with numerous small, lance-shaped pinnae.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical botanical gardens.
