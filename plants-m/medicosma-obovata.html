Medicosma obovata

Medicosma obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate medicosma, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features lanceolate to obovate leaves that are often hairy, and it produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. Medicosma obovata is typically found in grasslands and open woodlands, thriving in well-drained soils.

It is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
