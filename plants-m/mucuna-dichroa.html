Mucuna dichroa

Mucuna dichroa, commonly known as the black urd, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, two-toned pods that range from green to brown. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its seeds are enclosed in tough, fibrous pods and are often used in traditional medicine.

Mucuna dichroa is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
