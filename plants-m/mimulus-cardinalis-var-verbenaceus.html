Mimulus cardinalis var. verbenaceus

Mimulus cardinalis var. verbenaceus, commonly known as the scarlet monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant red-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny habitats such as streambanks and meadows. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.
