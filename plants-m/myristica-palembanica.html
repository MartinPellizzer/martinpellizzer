Myristica palembanica

Myristica palembanica, commonly known as the Palembang nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and spherical fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and cuisine. Its bark and leaves are also valued for their medicinal properties.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
