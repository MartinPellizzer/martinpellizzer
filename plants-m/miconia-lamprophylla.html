Miconia lamprophylla

Miconia lamprophylla, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains multiple seeds.

Miconia lamprophylla is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
