Mastixia trichotoma var. trichotoma

Mastixia trichotoma var. trichotoma, commonly known as the slender mastixia, is a shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, elongated, woody fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
