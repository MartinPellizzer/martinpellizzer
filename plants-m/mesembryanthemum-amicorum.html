Mesembryanthemum amicorum

Mesembryanthemum amicorum, commonly known as the friendship flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
