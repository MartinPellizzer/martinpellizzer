Maba vieillardii

Maba vieillardii, commonly known as the African maba tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and long, pinnate leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by green, fleshy fruits that turn red when mature. The plant has traditional uses in local medicine and is also important for wildlife habitat.
