Mephitidia moonii

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom only under moonlight, attracting nocturnal pollinators such as moths and certain species of bats. Its roots are known to secrete a mild sedative compound, which has led to its use in traditional Andean medicine.

Due to its habitat specificity and sensitivity to environmental changes, Mephitidia moonii is considered a vulnerable species.
