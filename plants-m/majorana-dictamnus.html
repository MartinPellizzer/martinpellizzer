Majorana dictamnus

Majorana dictamnus, commonly known as wild thyme or caraway, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has aromatic leaves that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.

Majorana dictamnus is also valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
