Mandevilla bahiensis

Mandevilla bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Mandevilla bahiensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
