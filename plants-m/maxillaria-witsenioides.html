Maxillaria witsenioides

Maxillaria witsenioides, commonly known as the Witsen's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of yellow and green. Its roots are adapted for nutrient absorption in the nutrient-poor soils of its natural habitat.

Maxillaria witsenioides is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
