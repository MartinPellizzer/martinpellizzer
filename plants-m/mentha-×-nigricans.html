Mentha × nigricans

Mentha × nigricans, commonly known as black mint, is a hybrid perennial herb in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its dark purple to blackish leaves and strong, aromatic scent. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, with square stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments.

Black mint is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
