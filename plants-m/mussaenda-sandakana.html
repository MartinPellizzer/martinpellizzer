Mussaenda sandakana

Mussaenda sandakana, commonly known as the yellow mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which are clustered in umbels and attract various pollinators. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mussaenda sandakana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
