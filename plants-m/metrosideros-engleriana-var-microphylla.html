Metrosideros engleriana var. microphylla

Metrosideros engleriana var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often in spring and summer. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it has a shallow root system.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.
