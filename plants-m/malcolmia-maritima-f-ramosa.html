Malcolmia maritima f. ramosa

Malcolmia maritima f. ramosa, commonly known as the coastal malcolmia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, branched stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer.
