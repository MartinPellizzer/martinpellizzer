Michelia calyptrata

Michelia calyptrata, commonly known as the Chinese Michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, brown seeds.
