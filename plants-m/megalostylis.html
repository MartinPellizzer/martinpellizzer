Megalostylis

Megalostylis, commonly known as the giant stylus orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, Madagascar, and the Arabian Peninsula. The plants are characterized by their large, showy flowers with prominent, often colorful sepals and petals. They typically grow as terrestrial herbs or small shrubs, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The genus is notable for its unique floral structure, which includes a specialized column and a long, slender stamen.
