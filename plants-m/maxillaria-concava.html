Maxillaria concava

Maxillaria concava, commonly known as the concave maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves that form a dense rosette. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a unique, concave lip that is often marked with intricate patterns. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria concava is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
