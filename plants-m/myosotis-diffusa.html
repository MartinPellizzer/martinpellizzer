Myosotis diffusa

Myosotis diffusa, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This plant is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
