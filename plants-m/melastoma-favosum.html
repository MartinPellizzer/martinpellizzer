Melastoma favosum

Melastoma favosum, commonly known as the red-root melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite and often have a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
