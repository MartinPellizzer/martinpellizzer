Myristica hellwigii

Myristica hellwigii, commonly known as the yellow nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark and edible seeds. The plant produces large, leathery leaves and yellowish flowers that are followed by fruits containing the aromatic seeds. The seeds, known as mace, are used in culinary and medicinal applications.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both traditional medicine and the spice trade.
