Mandevilla anceps

Mandevilla anceps, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Mandevilla anceps thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and containers.
