Myriandra nitida

Myriandra nitida, commonly known as the silky myriandra, is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Myriandra nitida is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.
