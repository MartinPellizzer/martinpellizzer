Mammillaria lindsayi var. robustior

Mammillaria lindsayi var. robustior, commonly known as the robust mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow tubercles, which are typically adorned with small, white radial spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
