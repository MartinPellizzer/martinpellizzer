Magnolia sargentiana var. robusta

Magnolia sargentiana var. robusta, commonly known as the Sargent magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. This variety is known for its robust growth and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to a variety of garden settings.
