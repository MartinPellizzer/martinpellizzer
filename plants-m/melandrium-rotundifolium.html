Melandrium rotundifolium

Melandrium rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its showy, pink or white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has rounded, succulent leaves that are often hairy, and it typically grows in moist, sandy soils. It is an annual or biennial species, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
