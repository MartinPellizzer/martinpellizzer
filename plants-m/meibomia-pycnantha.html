Meibomia pycnantha

Meibomia pycnantha, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stem. It produces woody capsules as fruit, which contain numerous seeds.

Meibomia pycnantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
