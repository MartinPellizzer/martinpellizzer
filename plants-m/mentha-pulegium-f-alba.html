Mentha pulegium f. alba

Mentha pulegium f. alba, commonly known as white pennyroyal, is a variant of the pennyroyal plant characterized by its pale, almost white flowers.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, shaded areas. Its leaves are typically round and emit a strong, mint-like aroma.

This variety is less commonly cultivated than its green-flowered counterpart but is still valued for its medicinal and aromatic properties.
