Mammillaria herrmannii var. flavicans

Mammillaria herrmannii var. flavicans, commonly known as the yellow mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with a yellowish-green color and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
