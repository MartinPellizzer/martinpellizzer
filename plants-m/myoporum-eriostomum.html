Myoporum eriostomum

Myoporum eriostomum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal and rocky environments.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in challenging conditions.
