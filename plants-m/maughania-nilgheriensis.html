Maughania nilgheriensis

Maughania nilgheriensis, commonly known as the Nilgiri mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in forests and rocky hillsides. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.

The species is also of ecological importance, providing habitat and nectar for various pollinators.
