Mikania eucosma

Mikania eucosma, commonly known as the velvetleaf mikania, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and often forms dense stands in disturbed habitats. Its stems are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

Mikania eucosma is also known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its resilience to drought conditions.
