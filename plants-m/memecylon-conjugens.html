Memecylon conjugens

Memecylon conjugens, commonly known as the slender memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, elliptic leaves with smooth or slightly hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, typically in shades of white or pink, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
