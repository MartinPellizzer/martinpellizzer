Melastoma celebicum

Melastoma celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes melastoma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Melastoma celebicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
