Millettia reticulata var. stenophylla

Millettia reticulata var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant features pinnately compound leaves with slender, elongated leaflets, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in racemose inflorescences, and are usually white or pale yellow.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in subtropical gardens.
