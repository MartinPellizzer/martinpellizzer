Monotaxis paxii

Monotaxis paxii, commonly known as the Christmas star, is a unique flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with small, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
