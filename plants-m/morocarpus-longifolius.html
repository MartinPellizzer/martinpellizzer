Morocarpus longifolius

Morocarpus longifolius, commonly known as the long-leaf morocarpus, is a unique member of the Moraceae family, characterized by its distinctive morphology and ecological adaptations.

Morocarpus longifolius plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitat and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
