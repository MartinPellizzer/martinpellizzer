It is native to the island of Rodriguez, which is part of the Mascarene Islands in the Indian Ocean. The plant is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.
