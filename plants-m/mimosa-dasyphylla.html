Mimosa dasyphylla

Mimosa dasyphylla, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces fluffy, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread make it both a desirable and sometimes invasive species in certain regions.
