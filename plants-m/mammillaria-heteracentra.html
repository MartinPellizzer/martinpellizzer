Mammillaria heteracentra

Mammillaria heteracentra, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the axils of the areoles.

Mammillaria heteracentra is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its low maintenance and unique appearance.
