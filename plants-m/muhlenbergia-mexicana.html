Muhlenbergia mexicana

Muhlenbergia mexicana, commonly known as Mexican muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, silver-green leaves. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-white seed heads that are visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscapes.

Muhlenbergia mexicana is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
