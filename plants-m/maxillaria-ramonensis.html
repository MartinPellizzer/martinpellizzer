Maxillaria ramonensis

Maxillaria ramonensis, commonly known as the Ramon orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
