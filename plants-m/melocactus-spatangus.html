Melocactus spatangus

Melocactus spatangus, commonly known as the spiny melon, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and spines that are often reddish in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens.
