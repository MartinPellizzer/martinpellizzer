Melinia harleyi

Melinia harleyi, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white. Its leaves are usually velvety and heart-shaped, contributing to its ornamental appeal.
