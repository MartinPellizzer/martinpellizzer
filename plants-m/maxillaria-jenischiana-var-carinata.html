Maxillaria jenischiana var. carinata

Maxillaria jenischiana var. carinata, commonly known as the ridged maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom during the warmer months.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively ease of cultivation.
