Melicytus alpinus

Melicytus alpinus, commonly known as the alpine melicytus, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red or black when mature. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Melicytus alpinus plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various insect and bird species.
