Myosotis sylvatica var. lithospermifolia

Myosotis sylvatica var. lithospermifolia, commonly known as the Lithospermum Forget-me-not, is a variant of the common forget-me-not with distinctive botanical features.

It typically has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy, and its flowers are blue to purple in color, with five petals. The plant is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

This variant is notable for its unique leaf structure and floral morphology, which distinguish it from the standard Myosotis sylvatica.
