Monotagma rudanii

Monotagma rudanii, commonly known as the African star grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. Monotagma rudanii is often found in open grasslands and along riverbanks, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
