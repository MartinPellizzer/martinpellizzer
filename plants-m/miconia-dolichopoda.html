Miconia dolichopoda

Miconia dolichopoda, commonly known as the long-stemmed miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and showy, clustered flowers.
