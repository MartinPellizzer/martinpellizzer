Memorialis dalzellii

Memorialis dalzellii, commonly known as the Dalzell's memorial orchid, is a rare and unique orchid species native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically feature a combination of violet and white hues. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowering structure. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
