Melastoma urceolatum

Melastoma urceolatum, commonly known as the urn-shaped melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
