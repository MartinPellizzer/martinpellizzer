Mussaenda oblonga

Mussaenda oblonga, commonly known as the golden trumpet or red trumpet, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the variety. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate to oblong leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mussaenda oblonga is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
