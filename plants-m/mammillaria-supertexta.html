Mammillaria supertexta

Mammillaria supertexta, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical shape and white to pale blue spines. The plant features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
