Mammillaria tuberculosa

Mammillaria tuberculosa, commonly known as the Tubercled Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and prominent tubercles, which are the small, rounded bumps that give it its name. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
