Medemia nobilis

Medemia nobilis, commonly known as the noble medemia, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, velvety leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Medemia nobilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
