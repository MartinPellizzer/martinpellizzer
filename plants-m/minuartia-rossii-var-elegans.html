Minuartia rossii var. elegans

Minuartia rossii var. elegans, commonly known as the elegant sandflower, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in well-drained, sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in alpine or rocky habitats. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens or as a ground cover in dry landscapes.
