Manihot stellata

Manihot stellata, commonly known as the starleaf arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.
