Macrotyloma prostratum

Macrotyloma prostratum, commonly known as the prostrate cowpea, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that produces small, yellow flowers and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and is often used for forage in livestock farming.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil fertility and sustainable agriculture.
