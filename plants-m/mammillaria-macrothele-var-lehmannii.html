Mammillaria macrothele var. lehmannii

Mammillaria macrothele var. lehmannii, commonly known as the Lehmann's mammillaria, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a compact, spiny stem with prominent areoles and short, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in succulent collections.
