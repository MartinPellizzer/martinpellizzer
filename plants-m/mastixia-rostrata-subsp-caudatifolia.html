Mastixia rostrata subsp. caudatifolia

Mastixia rostrata subsp.

caudatifolia, commonly known as the slender mastixia, is a shrub native to southern Australia. It features small, narrow leaves that are often hairy, and it produces yellowish-green flowers in clusters.

The plant is adapted to arid environments, with a deep root system that allows it to survive in dry conditions. It is part of the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is valued for its ornamental potential.
