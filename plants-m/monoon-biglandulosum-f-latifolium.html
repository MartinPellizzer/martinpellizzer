Monoon biglandulosum f. latifolium

Monoon biglandulosum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaved monoon, is a variant of the monoon genus native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ornamental species within the Monoon family.
