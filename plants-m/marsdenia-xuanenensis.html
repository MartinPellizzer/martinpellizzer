Marsdenia xuanenensis

Marsdenia xuanenensis, commonly known as the Xuan'en Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan Province, where it grows in humid, mountainous regions. Marsdenia xuanenensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated for horticultural purposes.
