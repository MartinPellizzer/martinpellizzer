Magnolia siamensis

Magnolia siamensis, commonly known as the Siam magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Thailand and southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and landscaped areas.
