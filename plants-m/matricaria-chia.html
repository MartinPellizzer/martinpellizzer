Matricaria chia

Matricaria chia, commonly known as Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its bright orange to yellow ray flowers and dark green, heart-shaped leaves. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Matricaria chia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in full sun with well-drained soil.

It is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
