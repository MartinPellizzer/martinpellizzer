Mammillaria karwinskiana var. flavescens

Mammillaria karwinskiana var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to yellow spines and clusters of bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-15 centimeters and thrives in well-draining soil. It prefers full sun and minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
