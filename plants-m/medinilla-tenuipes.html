Medinilla tenuipes

Medinilla tenuipes, commonly known as the slender medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in whorls around the stems. The plant produces small, fragrant, cream-colored flowers that are clustered in loose inflorescences, often appearing in late winter to early spring. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Medinilla tenuipes is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
