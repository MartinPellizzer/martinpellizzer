Melinia campanulata

Melinia campanulata, commonly known as the bellflower melinia, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to parts of Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, typically blooming in the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable addition to gardens and natural habitats.

Melinia campanulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
