Malache arachnoidea

Malache arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-like malache, is a unique species belonging to the family Malachoidae.

It is characterized by its intricate, web-like patterns on its leaves, which give it its common name. The plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage exhibits a striking contrast between deep green and pale yellow veins, enhancing its ornamental appeal.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its aesthetic and scientific value.
