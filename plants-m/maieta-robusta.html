Maieta robusta

Maieta robusta, commonly known as the robust mayweed, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and serrated leaves. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed soils and can reach heights of up to 1 meter. Its yellow flowers bloom from June to September, and it is known for its strong, aromatic scent.

Maieta robusta is often found in meadows, roadsides, and waste areas, where it thrives in sunny conditions.
