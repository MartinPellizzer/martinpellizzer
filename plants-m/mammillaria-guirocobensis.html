Mammillaria guirocobensis

Mammillaria guirocobensis, commonly known as the Guirocobense mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white to light green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
