Metalasia compacta

Metalasia compacta, commonly known as the compact metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a compact, rosette formation.

Metalasia compacta is adapted to grow in dry, sandy soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
