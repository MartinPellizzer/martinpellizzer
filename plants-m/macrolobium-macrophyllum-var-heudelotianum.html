Macrolobium macrophyllum var. heudelotianum

Macrolobium macrophyllum var. heudelotianum, commonly known as the Heudelot's giant water lily, is a striking aquatic plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and ponds. Its flowers are showy, with white petals and a yellow center, blooming in the early morning.

The leaves are thick and waxy, providing a habitat for various aquatic organisms.
