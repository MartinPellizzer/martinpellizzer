Myrosmodes ustulata

Myrosmodes ustulata, commonly known as the blackwood tree, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree with a straight trunk and a broad, rounded crown. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a dark, glossy appearance. The tree produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in traditional construction and furniture-making.
