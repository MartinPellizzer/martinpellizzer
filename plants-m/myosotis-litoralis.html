Myosotis litoralis

Myosotis litoralis, commonly known as sea forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in moist, sandy or rocky habitats near the shoreline. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, adapted to withstand coastal conditions.

Myosotis litoralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing coastal soils.
