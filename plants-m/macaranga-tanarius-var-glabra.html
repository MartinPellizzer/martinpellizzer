Macaranga tanarius var. glabra

Macaranga tanarius var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its smooth, often glabrous leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are usually alternate, simple, and have a distinctive shape with a short petiole.

The flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
