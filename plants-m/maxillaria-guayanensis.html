Maxillaria guayanensis

Maxillaria guayanensis, commonly known as the Guayana maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with a single or few leaves, and its flowers are borne on terminal spikes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria guayanensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
