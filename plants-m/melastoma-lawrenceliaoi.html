Melastoma lawrenceliaoi

Melastoma lawrenceliaoi, commonly known as the Lawrence's melastoma, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
