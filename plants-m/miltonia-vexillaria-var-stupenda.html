Miltonia vexillaria var. stupenda

Miltonia vexillaria var. stupenda, commonly known as the Royal Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, velvety petals in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a contrasting lip. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, producing new growths from the base of the previous ones. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its botanical beauty makes it a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and botanical gardens.
