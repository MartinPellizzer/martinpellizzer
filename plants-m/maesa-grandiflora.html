Maesa grandiflora

Maesa grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered maesa, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves that are glossy and oval-shaped. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are berry-like and contain several seeds.

Maesa grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
