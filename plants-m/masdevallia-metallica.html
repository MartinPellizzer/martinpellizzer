Masdevallia metallica

Masdevallia metallica, commonly known as the silver-veined masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety dark green leaves with distinctive silver veins, which give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale purple, often arranged in a loose cluster. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light intensity to maintain its vibrant foliage.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate beauty, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
