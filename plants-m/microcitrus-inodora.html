Microcitrus inodora

Microcitrus inodora, commonly known as the sweet lime or kaffir lime, is a small evergreen citrus tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, glossy leaves and small, round, yellow-orange fruits. The plant produces small, oval-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is highly aromatic and is often used in cooking for its distinct citrus flavor.

Microcitrus inodora thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
