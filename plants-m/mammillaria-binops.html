Mammillaria binops

Mammillaria binops, commonly known as the two-lobed mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
