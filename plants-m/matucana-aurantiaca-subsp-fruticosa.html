Matucana aurantiaca subsp. fruticosa

Matucana aurantiaca subsp.

fruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered matucana, is a species of flowering plant native to South America. It belongs to the Arecaceae family, commonly known as the palm family.

This subspecies is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and vibrant orange to red flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The plant produces small, edible fruits that are sometimes used in local cuisines.
