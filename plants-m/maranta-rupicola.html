Maranta rupicola

Maranta rupicola, commonly known as the rock maranta, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage that displays a range of colors including red, pink, and green. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, showcasing its adaptability to challenging conditions. Its leaves are often hairy and have a distinctive, almost metallic sheen.

Maranta rupicola is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in indoor settings with proper care.
