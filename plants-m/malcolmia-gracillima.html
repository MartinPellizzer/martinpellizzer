Malcolmia gracillima

Malcolmia gracillima, commonly known as the slender malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces delicate, yellow flowers with a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Malcolmia gracillima is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
