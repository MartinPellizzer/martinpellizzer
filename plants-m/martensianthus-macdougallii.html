Martensianthus macdougallii

Martensianthus macdougallii, commonly known as the MacDougall's martensia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
