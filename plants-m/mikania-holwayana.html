Mikania holwayana

Mikania holwayana, commonly known as the creeping Mikania, is a vigorous climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has fibrous roots and stems that can spread rapidly, often forming dense mats. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in disturbed areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain ecosystems.
