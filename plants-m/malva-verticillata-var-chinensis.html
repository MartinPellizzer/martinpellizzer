Malva verticillata var. chinensis

Malva verticillata var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese mallow, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features palmately lobed leaves with a hairy texture and vibrant purple to pink flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces capsule fruits containing numerous small, smooth seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Its fibrous stems and leaves have been used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural fibers.
