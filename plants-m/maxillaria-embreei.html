Maxillaria embreei

Maxillaria embreei, commonly known as the Embree's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green hues. Its leaves are typically broad and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, moist environments.

Maxillaria embreei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
