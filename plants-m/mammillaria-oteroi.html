Mammillaria oteroi

Mammillaria oteroi, commonly known as the Oteroi hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a spiny, segmented stem that resembles a hedgehog. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
