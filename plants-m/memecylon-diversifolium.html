Memecylon diversifolium

Memecylon diversifolium, commonly known as the variable-leaf memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, simple, and often variable-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with showy pink or purple petals, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in botanical studies.
