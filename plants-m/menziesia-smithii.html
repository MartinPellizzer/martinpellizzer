Menziesia smithii

Menziesia smithii, commonly known as Smith's menziesia, is a deciduous shrub native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils within coniferous forest understories. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its flowers are white or pale pink, appearing in clusters, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The fruit is a small, dark blue to black berry that ripens in the fall.
