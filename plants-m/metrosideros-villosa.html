Metrosideros villosa

Metrosideros villosa, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

The silver flame tree is often used in landscaping due to its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
