Melochia subcordata

Melochia subcordata, commonly known as the heartleaf melochia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. Melochia subcordata is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
