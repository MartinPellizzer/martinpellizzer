Mandevilla guerrerensis

Mandevilla guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerensis mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, typically blooming in summer. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and provide a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Mandevilla guerrerensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in gardens and as a houseplant.
