Melastoma schizocarpum

Melastoma schizocarpum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered purple or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture.

The fruit is a schizocarp, splitting into two parts when mature, each containing a single seed.
