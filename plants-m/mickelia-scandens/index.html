Mickelia Scandens

Mickelia scandens is a unique climbing plant native to parts of Africa and Madagascar. It is known for its striking, waxy leaves that shimmer in the sunlight. This plant thrives in humid environments and is often found in tropical rainforests. Its ability to climb and spread makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to thrive in non-native settings.
