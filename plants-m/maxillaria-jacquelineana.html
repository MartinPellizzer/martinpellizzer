Maxillaria jacquelineana

Maxillaria jacquelineana, commonly known as the Jacqueline orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers. The plant features long, narrow leaves and blooms in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria jacquelineana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
