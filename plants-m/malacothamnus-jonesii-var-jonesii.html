Malacothamnus jonesii var. jonesii

Malacothamnus jonesii var. jonesii, commonly known as Jones' malacothamnus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, woolly foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
