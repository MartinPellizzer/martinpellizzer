Minuartia Oreina

Minuartia oreina is a small, flowering plant native to the rocky and sandy soils of southern Europe and northern Africa. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its vibrant and delicate blooms. This species is often found in coastal areas and dry habitats, thriving in harsh environmental conditions. Its flowers, which range from white to pink, are typically small and clustered in tight inflorescences. Despite its modest appearance, Minuartia oreina plays an important role in its ecosystem, supporting pollinators and contributing to biodiversity.
