Meliosma cuneifolia var. glabriuscula

Meliosma cuneifolia var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the smooth-leafed meliosma, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its ovate to cuneate leaves that are usually smooth and glabrous on both surfaces. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne in the axils of the leaves. Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its attractive, glossy appearance and ability to thrive in shaded conditions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to moist, acidic soils.
