Meistera chinensis

Meistera chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and has lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture. Meistera chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
