Maireana microphylla

Maireana microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved maireana, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white or pinkish flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, exhibiting drought tolerance and succulent-like features. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
