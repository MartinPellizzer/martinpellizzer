Mussaenda kajewskii

Mussaenda kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewski's Mussaenda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Mussaenda kajewskii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
