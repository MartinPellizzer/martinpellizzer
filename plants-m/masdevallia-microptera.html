Masdevallia microptera

Masdevallia microptera, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, strap-like leaves and a short, pseudobulb-like stem. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
