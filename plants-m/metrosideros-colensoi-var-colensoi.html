Metrosideros colensoi var. colensoi

Metrosideros colensoi var. colensoi, commonly known as the Colenso's Christmas Bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant thrives in coastal and subcoastal areas, often growing in sandy or rocky soils. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens, though it requires well-drained soil and full sun.
