Michelia wardii

Michelia wardii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds.

Michelia wardii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
