Milium paniceum

Milium paniceum, commonly known as slender millet, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, panicled inflorescence. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and thrives in temperate climates. Its small, spikelet-like flowers are often wind-pollinated, and it produces tiny, dry seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Milium paniceum is often found in meadows, along stream banks, and in woodland edges.
