Melastoma coronatum

Melastoma coronatum, commonly known as the white melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a distinctive crown-like appearance. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
