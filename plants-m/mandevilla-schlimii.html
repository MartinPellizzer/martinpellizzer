Mandevilla schlimii

Mandevilla schlimii, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces long, trailing stems that can be trained to climb or grown as a bush. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement.

Mandevilla schlimii thrives in warm, humid conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
