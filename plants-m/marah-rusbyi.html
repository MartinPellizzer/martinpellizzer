Marah rusbyi

Marah rusbyi, commonly known as the African starfish, is a unique succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a distinctive, star-like shape with fleshy, ribbed stems that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its rugged appearance. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a waxy cuticle, reducing water loss and enhancing its drought tolerance.

Marah rusbyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking visual appeal and low maintenance requirements.
