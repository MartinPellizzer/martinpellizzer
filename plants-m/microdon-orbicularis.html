Microdon orbicularis

Microdon orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, orb-like shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species.
