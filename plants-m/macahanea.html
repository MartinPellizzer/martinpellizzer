Macahanea

Macahanea, commonly known as the Mexican bellflower, is a genus of flowering plants native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the nightshade family, Solanaceae, and is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that come in various colors such as white, purple, and blue. Macahanea species are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes cultivated in gardens for their vibrant floral displays.
