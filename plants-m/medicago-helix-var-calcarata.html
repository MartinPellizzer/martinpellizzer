Medicago helix var. calcarata

Medicago helix var. calcarata, commonly known as the spiral medic.

This variant is a perennial legume characterized by its distinctive spiral-shaped stems and trifoliate leaves. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen and prevent soil erosion.
