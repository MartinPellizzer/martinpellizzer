Myosotis debilis

Myosotis debilis, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, delicate blue or violet flowers. The plant typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers bloom in clusters at the tips of the stems.

It is often found in grassy areas and along stream banks, where it contributes to the local biodiversity.
