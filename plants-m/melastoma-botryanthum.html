Melastoma botryanthum

Melastoma botryanthum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Its leaves are opposite and often have a velvety texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
