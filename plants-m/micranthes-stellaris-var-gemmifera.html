Micranthes stellaris var. gemmifera

Micranthes stellaris var. gemmifera, commonly known as the starflower or gemmiferous starflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring, typically in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces tiny, clustered fruits called cymes. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
