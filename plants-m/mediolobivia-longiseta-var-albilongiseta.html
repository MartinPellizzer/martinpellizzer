Mediolobivia longiseta var. albilongiseta

Mediolobivia longiseta var. albilongiseta, commonly known as the silver-haired lobivia, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a compact, rounded stem with thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery hair, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to dry climates and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance needs, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
