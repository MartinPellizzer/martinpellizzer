Myosotis acutata

Myosotis acutata, commonly known as the sharpleaf forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Myosotis acutata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
