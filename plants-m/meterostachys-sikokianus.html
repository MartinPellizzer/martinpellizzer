Meterostachys sikokianus

Meterostachys sikokianus, commonly known as the Sikokian grass, is a perennial herb native to the grassland regions of southern Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soils. Its flowering period usually occurs during the rainy season, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
