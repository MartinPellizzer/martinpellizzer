Magnolia kobushii

Magnolia kobushii, commonly known as the Japanese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its flowers are typically pink or white, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Magnolia kobushii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking spring display.
