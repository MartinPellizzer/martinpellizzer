Margaritaria nobilis

Margaritaria nobilis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a perennial aquatic plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that float on the surface of freshwater bodies. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically submerged or floating. Its roots are fibrous and help stabilize the surrounding water ecosystem.

Margaritaria nobilis is also valued for its cultural and medicinal significance in various traditional practices.
