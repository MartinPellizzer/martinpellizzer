Marsdenia cordifolia

Marsdenia cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf Marsdenia, is a climbing plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It is native to regions in Southeast Asia, including parts of India and Indonesia.

Marsdenia cordifolia is often found in tropical and subtropical forests, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
