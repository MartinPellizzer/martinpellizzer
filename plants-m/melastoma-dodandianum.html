Melastoma dodandianum

Melastoma dodandianum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically red or pink in color. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
