Mutisia conantha

Mutisia conantha, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. The plant features showy yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical structure includes a woody stem and compound leaves with a rough texture.
