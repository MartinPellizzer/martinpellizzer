Myrtus splendens

Myrtus splendens, commonly known as the shining myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring. The plant is closely related to other members of the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. Myrtus splendens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a long history of use in traditional medicine.

Its aromatic properties make it a popular choice for gardens and aromatic purposes.
