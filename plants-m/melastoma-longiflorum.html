Melastoma longiflorum

Melastoma longiflorum, commonly known as the long-flowered melastoma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that are typically pink or red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its inflorescence consists of clusters of small, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters.

Melastoma longiflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
