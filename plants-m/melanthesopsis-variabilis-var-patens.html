Melanthesopsis variabilis var. patens

Melanthesopsis variabilis var. patens, commonly known as the variable melanthus, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and wetlands. It features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces yellow to orange composite flowers.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptability and role in local ecosystems.
