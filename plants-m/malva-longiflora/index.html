Malva Longiflora

Malva longiflora is a flowering plant known for its delicate pink blossoms and soft, velvety leaves. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. This species is often found in meadows, woodlands, and along stream banks. Its unique floral structure and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for gardeners and botanists alike. Malva longiflora plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance.
