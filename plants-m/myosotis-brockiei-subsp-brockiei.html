Myosotis brockiei subsp. brockiei

Myosotis brockiei subsp.

brockiei, commonly known as the Stewart's forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

This subspecies is part of the Myosotidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
