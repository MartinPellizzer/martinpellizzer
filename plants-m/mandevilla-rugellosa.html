Mandevilla rugellosa

Mandevilla rugellosa, commonly known as the rugellosa mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla rugellosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
