Maxillaria deniseae

Maxillaria deniseae, commonly known as Denise's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to orange flowers with darker markings, often resembling a miniature orchid. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Maxillaria deniseae is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
