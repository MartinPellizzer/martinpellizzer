Masdevallia eucharis

Masdevallia eucharis, commonly known as the White Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a compact, rhizomatous structure. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it prefers cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Masdevallia eucharis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
