Mandevilla eximia

Mandevilla eximia, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous climbing growth habit and large, showy flowers that range from red to orange in color. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow up to 20 feet long if supported properly. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal blooming.

Mandevilla eximia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to climb trellises or walls.
