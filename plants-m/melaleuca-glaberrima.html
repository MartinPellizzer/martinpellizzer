Melaleuca glaberrima

Melaleuca glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth tea tree, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae.

It is native to the eastern coast of Australia, particularly in Queensland and New South Wales. The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in winter.

Melaleuca glaberrima is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
