Mimosa calliandroides

Mimosa calliandroides, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its flowers are known to close at night and in response to touch, a trait called thigmonasty.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
