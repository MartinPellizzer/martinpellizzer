Merciera tenuifolia var. thunbergiana

Merciera tenuifolia var. thunbergiana, commonly known as the Thunberg's narrow-leaved mercier, is a shrub native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It features small, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers during the spring season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to coastal environments.
