Mussaenda gossweileri

Mussaenda gossweileri, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its clusters of bright orange or yellow flowers, which appear in the axils of the leaves. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
