Maackia hupehensis

Maackia hupehensis, commonly known as Chinese maackia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. It is characterized by its woody stems and is often used in ornamental gardening.

The seeds are encased in a pod and can be used for propagation.
