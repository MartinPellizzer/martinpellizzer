Microchloa indica var. kunthii

Microchloa indica var. kunthii, commonly known as the Kunth's microchloa, is a small, perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its compact size and dense foliage make it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.

Microchloa indica var. kunthii is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in marginal environments.
