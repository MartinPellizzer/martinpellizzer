Memorialis matsudae

Memorialis matsudae, commonly known as the Japanese flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a vibrant splash of color to the landscape. The plant has compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The botanical features of Memorialis matsudae make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.
