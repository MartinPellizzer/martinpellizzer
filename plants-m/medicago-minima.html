Medicago minima

Medicago minima, commonly known as lesser medick, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow or white flowers in early spring. The plant is often found in grasslands, disturbed soils, and along roadsides. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Medicago minima is also used in forage and as a cover crop in agricultural settings.
