Melastoma aquaticum

Melastoma aquaticum, commonly known as the water melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in wet or marshy environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually pink or white and bloom in clusters.

Melastoma aquaticum is notable for its ability to thrive in waterlogged soils and its role in wetland ecosystems.
