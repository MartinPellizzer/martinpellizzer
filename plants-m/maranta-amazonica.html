Maranta amazonica

Maranta amazonica, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a species native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful foliage. The plant features upright stems with broad, lance-shaped leaves that can exhibit a variety of hues, including red, purple, and green. Maranta amazonica is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
