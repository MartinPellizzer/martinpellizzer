Melanoloma pullata var. leucoloma

Melanoloma pullata var. leucoloma, commonly known as the white-flowered melanoloma, is a rare and striking variant of the melanoloma genus.

It is characterized by its pale to white flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. The plant is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. It has a compact growth habit and produces small, clustered blooms throughout the growing season.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
