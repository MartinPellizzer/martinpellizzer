Malacocera gracilis

Malacocera gracilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and delicate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
