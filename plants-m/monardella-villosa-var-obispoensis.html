Monardella villosa var. obispoensis

Monardella villosa var. obispoensis, commonly known as Obispo's mint, is a perennial herb native to California.

It features hairy, gray-green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in chaparral and coastal sage scrub ecosystems. It is valued for its aromatic foliage and is used in traditional herbal medicine.

This variety is distinguished by its specific geographic distribution and unique floral characteristics.
