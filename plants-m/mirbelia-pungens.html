Mirbelia pungens

Mirbelia pungens, commonly known as the prickly mirbelia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and clusters of yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the family Fabaceae, which includes legumes, and it has a woody stem with a rough, bark-like texture. Its flowers are typically small and appear in late winter to early spring.
