Meliosma lepidota subsp. lepidota

Meliosma lepidota subsp.

lepidota, commonly known as the smoothleaf meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the eastern United States. It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically oval to elliptic in shape.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or pale green flowers in the spring, followed by clusters of small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.

Its foliage is often used in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in partial shade.
