Maesa picta

Maesa picta, commonly known as the painted maesa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a slight pinkish tinge. Its leaves are ovate and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
