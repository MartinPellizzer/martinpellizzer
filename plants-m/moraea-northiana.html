Moraea northiana

Moraea northiana, commonly known as the Natal lily, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple, often with a contrasting veined pattern. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy or rocky soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, forming a dense rosette near the ground.

Moraea northiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
