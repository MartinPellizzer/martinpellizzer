Machilus austroguizhouensis

Machilus austroguizhouensis, commonly known as the southern Guizhou machilus, is a rare evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, white flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its fruits are small drupes that contain a single seed.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
