Myristica ridleyana

Myristica ridleyana, commonly known as the Ridley nutmeg, is a small evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant produces distinctive red, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed, which is used for its aromatic properties. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The Ridley nutmeg is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
