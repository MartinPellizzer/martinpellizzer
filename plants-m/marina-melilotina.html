Marina melilotina

Marina melilotina, commonly known as sweet clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Marina melilotina is also used in agriculture and as a forage crop for livestock.
