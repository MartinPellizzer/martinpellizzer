Myristica maingayi

Myristica maingayi, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain a highly valued seed kernel. The plant produces small, white flowers and has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets. Its essential oils and aromatic compounds are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
