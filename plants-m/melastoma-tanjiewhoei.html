Melastoma tanjiewhoei

Melastoma tanjiewhoei, commonly known as the Tanjiewhoei melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
