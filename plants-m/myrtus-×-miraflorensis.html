Myrtus × miraflorensis

Myrtus × miraflorensis, commonly known as the hybrid myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers. The plant is evergreen, with glossy, dark green foliage that provides ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Myrtus × miraflorensis is also valued for its use in perfumery and as a source of essential oils.
