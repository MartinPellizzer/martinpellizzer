Monochaetum candolleanum

Monochaetum candolleanum, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, fragrant flowers.
