Maireana glomerifolia

Maireana glomerifolia, commonly known as the clustered maireana, is a perennial shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. It prefers well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Its ecological significance includes providing habitat and nectar for native pollinators.
