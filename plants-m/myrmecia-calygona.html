Myrmecia calygona

Myrmecia calygona, commonly known as the black antwings, is a species of ant-like plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its erect growth habit and small, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It is adapted to thrive in dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Myrmecia calygona is known for its ability to attract a variety of pollinators, including bees and birds.
