Miconia purulensis

Miconia purulensis, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually green with a reddish or purple tinge on the underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature, containing numerous seeds.

Miconia purulensis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is known for its rapid growth rate.
