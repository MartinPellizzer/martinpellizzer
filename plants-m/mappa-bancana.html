Mappa bancana

Mappa bancana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect sunlight. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Mappa bancana is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
