Muraltia selago

Muraltia selago, commonly known as the silky acacia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, fluffy seed pods that develop after flowering. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Muraltia selago is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a garden setting.
