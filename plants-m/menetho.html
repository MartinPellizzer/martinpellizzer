Menetho

Menetho, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight to prevent leaf scorch. Menetho is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
