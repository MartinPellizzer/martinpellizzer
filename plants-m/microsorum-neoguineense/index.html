Microsorum Neoguineense

Microsorum neoguineense is a lesser-known fern species native to the tropical regions of New Guinea. It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds. This plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in dense forests or along stream banks. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it an intriguing subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
