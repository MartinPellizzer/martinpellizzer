Muhlenbergia peruviana f. viridis

Muhlenbergia peruviana f. viridis, commonly known as the green muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like foliage that displays a vibrant green color, especially during the growing season. The plant typically forms dense clumps and has slender, upright stems that can reach heights of up to 3 feet. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal in gardens.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
