Maxillaria convencionis

Maxillaria convencionis, commonly known as the Convencion orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive color pattern. The plant typically has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and blooms during the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria convencionis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
