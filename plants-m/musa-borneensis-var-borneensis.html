Musa borneensis var. borneensis

Musa borneensis var. borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean banana, is a species native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and elongated, clustered fruit. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its fruit, which is usually harvested when green, is used both for culinary purposes and as a source of income for local communities.

The plant thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.
