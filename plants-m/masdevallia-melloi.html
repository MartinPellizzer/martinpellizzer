Masdevallia melloi

Masdevallia melloi, commonly known as the Mello's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that typically feature a combination of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and produces racemes of showy blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Masdevallia melloi is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
