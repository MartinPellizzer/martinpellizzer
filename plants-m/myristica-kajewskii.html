Myristica kajewskii

Myristica kajewskii, commonly known as the Kajewskii nutmeg, is a species of flowering plant in the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and produces fragrant, fleshy fruits that contain aromatic seeds. These seeds are used in traditional medicine and for their essential oils.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
