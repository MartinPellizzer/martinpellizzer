Magnolia maingayi

Magnolia maingayi, commonly known as the Borneo magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It typically grows as an evergreen tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The bark is smooth and grayish, while the flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
