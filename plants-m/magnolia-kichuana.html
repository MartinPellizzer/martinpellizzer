Magnolia kichuana

Magnolia kichuana, commonly known as the Kichuana magnolia, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are typically glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Magnolia kichuana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and tropical landscapes.
