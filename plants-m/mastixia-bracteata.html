Mastixia bracteata

Mastixia bracteata, commonly known as the bracteate mastixia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Mastixia bracteata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth.
