Mesua Nagassarium

The mesua nagassarium is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the rainforests of india. it is known for its large, showy pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of dense forests. its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. due to habitat loss, the mesua nagassarium is considered endangered and requires protection to ensure its survival.
