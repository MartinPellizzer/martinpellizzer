Melastoma moluccanum

Melastoma moluccanum, commonly known as the Moluccan melastoma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with prominent red stamens, which bloom from spring to summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, dark seeds.

Melastoma moluccanum is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
