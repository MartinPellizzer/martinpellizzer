Maytenus hainanensis

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy berries that are often red when mature.
