Melastoma verrucosum

Melastoma verrucosum, commonly known as the warty melastoma, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly warty texture on the underside. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red, with five petals and prominent stamens. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Melastoma verrucosum is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
