Miscanthus purpurascens

Miscanthus purpurascens, commonly known as purple miscanthus, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, arching stalks and striking purple-pink foliage that adds vibrant color to gardens. The plant produces feathery plumes in the fall, enhancing its ornamental value. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for perennial borders and wildlife habitats.

Its drought tolerance and ability to attract pollinators make it a popular choice in ecological landscaping.
