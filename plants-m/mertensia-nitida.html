Mertensia nitida

Mertensia nitida, commonly known as the slender bluebells, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has slender, upright stems with opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Mertensia nitida is known for its attractive foliage and early-season blooms, contributing to its ornamental value.
