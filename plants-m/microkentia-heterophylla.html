Microkentia heterophylla

Microkentia heterophylla, commonly known as the variegated microkentia, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves with striking white or yellow variegation, which adds visual interest to indoor spaces. The plant has a bushy growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its tolerance of low light and ability to thrive in humid environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for both residential and commercial interiors.
