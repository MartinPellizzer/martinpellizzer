Musa × sapientum var. flabellata

Musa × sapientum var. flabellata, commonly known as the banana plant with a fan-shaped leaf.

This variety is characterized by its broad, flabellate (fan-like) leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It belongs to the Musaceae family and is a cultivar of the common banana, Musa × sapientum. The plant typically produces large, elongated fruits that are often used for consumption.

It thrives in tropical climates and requires warm, humid conditions for optimal growth.
