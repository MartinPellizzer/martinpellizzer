Matthiola sinuata var. integrifolia

Matthiola sinuata var. integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The leaves are smooth and entire, giving it its varietal name "integrifolia," which means "whole-leaved.". Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a cut flower.
