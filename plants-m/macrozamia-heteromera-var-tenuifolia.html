Macrozamia heteromera var. tenuifolia

Macrozamia heteromera var. tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved cycad, is a rare and ancient plant species native to the rainforests of eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and its distinctive, feather-like leaves that are narrow and strap-shaped. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs, typical of cycads. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
