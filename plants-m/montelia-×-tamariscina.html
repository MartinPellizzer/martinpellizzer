Montelia × tamariscina

Montelia × tamariscina, commonly known as the hybrid tamarisk, is a flowering plant belonging to the Tamaricaceae family.

It is a naturally occurring hybrid between Montelia tamariscina and Montelia microphylla, characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant typically features narrow, needle-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or red in color. It is native to arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
