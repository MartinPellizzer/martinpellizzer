Meliosma oldhamii var. sinensis

Meliosma oldhamii var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its lanceolate, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
