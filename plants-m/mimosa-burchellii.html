Mimosa burchellii

Mimosa burchellii, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its bipinnately compound leaves that fold shut when touched, a response triggered by changes in turgor pressure. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique sensitivity to touch makes it an intriguing subject for both scientific study and botanical observation.
