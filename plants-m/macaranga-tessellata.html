Macaranga tessellata

Macaranga tessellata, commonly known as the tessellated paper plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive tessellated pattern on the underside. The plant is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is often found in lowland rainforests. Its leaves are used in traditional crafts due to their fibrous texture and durability.
