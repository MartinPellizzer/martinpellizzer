Mesembryanthemum leptarthron

Mesembryanthemum leptarthron, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the warmer months. Its ability to survive in harsh conditions makes it both ecologically and horticulturally significant.
