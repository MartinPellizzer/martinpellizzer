Melastoma decemfidum

Melastoma decemfidum, commonly known as the ten-lobed melastoma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Melastoma decemfidum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
