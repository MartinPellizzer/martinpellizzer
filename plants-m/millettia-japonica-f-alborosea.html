Millettia japonica f. alborosea

Millettia japonica f. alborosea, commonly known as the white-flowered Japanese millettia, is a unique variant of the millettia genus native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its striking white or pale pink flowers, which contrast with its dark green foliage. The plant is a climbing shrub that produces small, winged seeds, typical of the legume family. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This ornamental plant is valued for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a specimen plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
