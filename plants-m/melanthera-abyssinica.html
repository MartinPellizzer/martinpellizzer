Melanthera abyssinica

Melanthera abyssinica, commonly known as the Abyssinian melanthemium, is a flowering plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats. It has linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds.
