Myosotis decumbens

Myosotis decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate forget-me-not, is a low-growing flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features slender, creeping stems that often lie close to the ground, forming dense mats. The plant produces small, sky-blue or white flowers with five petals, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in a fine, soft pubescence.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.
