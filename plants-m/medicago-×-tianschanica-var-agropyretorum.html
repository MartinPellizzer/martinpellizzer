Medicago × tianschanica var. agropyretorum

Medicago × tianschanica var. agropyretorum, commonly known as the Tianshan yellowflower medick, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils and steppe habitats. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is used in some traditional herbal practices.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
