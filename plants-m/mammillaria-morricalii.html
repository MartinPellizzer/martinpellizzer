Mammillaria morricalii

Mammillaria morricalii, commonly known as the Morrical's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and dense, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
