Mammillaria senilis var. haseloffii

Mammillaria senilis var. haseloffii, commonly known as the White Cactus, is a compact, slow-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or slightly columnar stem with white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
