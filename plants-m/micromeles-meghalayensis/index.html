Micromeles Meghalayensis

Meghalayan orchid, scientifically known as Micromeles meghalayensis, is a rare and exquisite species native to the northeastern Indian state of Meghalaya. This delicate orchid thrives in the moist, shaded environments of the region's dense forests and mountainous terrains. Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a standout among the diverse orchid family. Discovered relatively recently, it has captured the attention of botanists and nature enthusiasts alike. Conservation efforts are now underway to protect this fragile species from habitat loss and environmental degradation.
