Moeroris Nozeraniana

Common rue, scientifically known as Moeroris nozeraniana, is a hardy herbaceous plant native to temperate regions. It is widely appreciated for its strong, aromatic leaves and its historical use in herbal medicine. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and medicinal herb collections. Its distinctive appearance and pleasant fragrance have made it a symbol of protection and healing in various cultures. Despite its benefits, it contains toxic compounds and should be handled with care.
