Megastigma conzattii

Megastigma conzattii, commonly known as the Zanzibar star.

It is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of East Africa, particularly Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Megastigma conzattii is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant and unique floral display.
