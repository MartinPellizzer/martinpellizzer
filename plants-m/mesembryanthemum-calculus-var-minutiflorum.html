Mesembryanthemum calculus var. minutiflorum

Mesembryanthemum calculus var. minutiflorum, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for arid and semi-arid environments.
