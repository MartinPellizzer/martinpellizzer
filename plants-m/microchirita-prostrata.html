Microchirita prostrata

Microchirita prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate microchirita, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically less than 1 inch in length, arranged in a dense rosette pattern. The plant produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in short inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Due to its compact size and delicate appearance, it is a sought-after species among collectors of rare and exotic plants.
