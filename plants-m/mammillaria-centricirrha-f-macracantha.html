Mammillaria centricirrha f. macracantha

Mammillaria centricirrha f. macracantha, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its stems are covered in small, white spines and have a distinctive rib structure.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in arid gardens and indoor settings.
