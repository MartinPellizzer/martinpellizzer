Masdevallia rex

Masdevallia rex, commonly known as the royal masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant has a compact growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Masdevallia rex thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
