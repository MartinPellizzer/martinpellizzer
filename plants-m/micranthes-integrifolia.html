Micranthes integrifolia

Micranthes integrifolia, commonly known as the smoothleaf pimpernel, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant typically grows in moist, wooded areas and has small, white to pale yellow flowers. Its leaves are entire, meaning they have no teeth or lobes, and are often covered in a fine layer of hair.

It is an herbaceous perennial that blooms from spring to early summer.
