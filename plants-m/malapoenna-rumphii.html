Malapoenna rumphii

Malapoenna rumphii, commonly known as the Rumphii's orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

Malapoenna rumphii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant colors and unique floral structure.
