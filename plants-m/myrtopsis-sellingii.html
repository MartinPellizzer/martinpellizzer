Myrtopsis sellingii

Myrtopsis sellingii, commonly known as the African myrtle, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and glossy, narrow leaves. It typically grows as a shrub or small tree, often found in coastal and sandy soils.

Myrtopsis sellingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
