Muhlenbergia polystachya

Muhlenbergia polystachya, commonly known as the tall muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-blue seed heads that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, preferring well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in full sun to partial shade.

Muhlenbergia polystachya is also known for its ecological benefits, providing food and habitat for various wildlife species.
