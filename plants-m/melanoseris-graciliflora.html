Melanoseris graciliflora

Melanoseris graciliflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered melanoseris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Melanoseris.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. Native to specific regions in southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its growth habit is usually low and spreading, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often of interest to botanists and horticulturists.
