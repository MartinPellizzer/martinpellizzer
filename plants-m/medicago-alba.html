Medicago alba

Medicago alba, commonly known as white medic, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small white or pink flowers. The plant is a perennial herb that thrives in disturbed soils and can often be found in fields and along roadsides. It has a taproot system and produces pods containing several seeds.

Medicago alba is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant in ecological restoration efforts.
