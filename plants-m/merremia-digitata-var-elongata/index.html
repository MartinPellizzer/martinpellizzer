Merremia Digitata Var. Elongata

Merremia digitata var. elongata is a striking climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America. Known for its heart-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers, it adds a dramatic touch to gardens and natural landscapes. This variant of Merremia digitata is distinguished by its elongated leaves and strong, flexible stems that allow it to climb and spread efficiently. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. While it is a beautiful ornamental plant, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
