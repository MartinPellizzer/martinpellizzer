Marathrum trichophorum

Marathrum trichophorum, commonly known as the woolly thyme, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves covered in fine, white hairs that give it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
