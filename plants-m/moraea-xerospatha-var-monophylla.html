Moraea xerospatha var. monophylla

Moraea xerospatha var. monophylla, commonly known as the Cape bell, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant is adapted to dry conditions, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for rock gardens or arid landscapes. It blooms in late winter to early spring, adding vibrant color to gardens during cooler months.

The variety monophylla is distinguished by its single leaf per stem, setting it apart from other varieties within the species.
