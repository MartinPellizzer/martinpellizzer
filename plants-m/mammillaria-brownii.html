Mammillaria brownii

Mammillaria brownii, commonly known as the Brown's hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in small, white spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and features a green stem with areoles that bear the spines. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Mammillaria brownii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
