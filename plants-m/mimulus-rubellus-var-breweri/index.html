Mimulus Rubellus Var. Breweri

Mimulus rubellus var. breweri is a strikingly colorful plant known for its vibrant red and orange flowers. Native to the western United States, it thrives in moist, shady environments along streambanks and woodlands. This variant of the common monkeyflower is prized for its ornamental value and ecological importance. Its unique flower structure attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and invasive species.
