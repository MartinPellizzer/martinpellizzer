Meringurus

Meringurus, commonly known as the sacred lotus of ancient Egypt, is a flowering plant belonging to the Nelumbonaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom on tall, slender stalks emerging from water. Meringurus was revered in Egyptian culture for its symbolic and spiritual significance.
