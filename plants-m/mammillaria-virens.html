Mammillaria virens

Mammillaria virens, commonly known as the green mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, greenish-gray areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
