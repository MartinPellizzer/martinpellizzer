Meladenia densiflora

Meladenia densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and typically have a pale green or yellowish hue.

Meladenia densiflora thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
