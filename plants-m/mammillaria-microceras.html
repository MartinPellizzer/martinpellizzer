Mammillaria microceras

Mammillaria microceras, commonly known as the miniature ball cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a diameter typically less than 5 centimeters. Its surface is covered in white to pale yellow areoles, which bear short, white bristles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, and it is well adapted to arid environments.

It is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its compact size and unique appearance.
