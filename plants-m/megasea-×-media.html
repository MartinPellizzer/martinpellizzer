Megasea × media

Megasea × media, commonly known as the hybrid sea holly, is a perennial plant that exhibits characteristics of both its parent species, Megasea and Media.

It typically features striking, spiky flowers that range in color from blue to purple, which bloom in late summer. The plant has silvery, fuzzy foliage that helps it reflect sunlight and conserve moisture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its botanical uniqueness lies in its hybrid nature, which results in a variety of morphological variations.
