Modecca tamnifolia

Modecca tamnifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its compact size and ability to bloom throughout the year with proper care.

Modecca tamnifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in indoor settings.
