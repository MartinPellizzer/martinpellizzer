Macrocalyx

Macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-calyx plant, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its prominent, showy calyx that often persists through flowering and fruiting. Their botanical features make them popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and unique floral structure.
