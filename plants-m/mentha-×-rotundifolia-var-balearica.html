Mentha × rotundifolia var. balearica

Mentha × rotundifolia var. balearica, commonly known as the Balearic round-leaf mint, is a hybrid mint species native to the Balearic Islands.

It is characterized by its rounded, slightly hairy leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland areas or along stream banks. It produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This variety is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other members of the mint family.
