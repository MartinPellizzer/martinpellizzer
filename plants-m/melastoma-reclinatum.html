Melastoma reclinatum

Melastoma reclinatum, commonly known as the prostrate melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub characterized by its dark green, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma reclinatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
