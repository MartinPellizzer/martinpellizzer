Mesanthemum pubescens

Mesanthemum pubescens, commonly known as the silky mesembs, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in fine, silky hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and purple during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
