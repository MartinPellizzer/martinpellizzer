Mischocarpus hainanensis

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in China's Hainan Island. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are alternate and elliptical in shape. The tree is valued for its durable wood and has been used in traditional medicine and as a source of tallow.
