Marrubium velutinum subsp. cylleneum

Marrubium velutinum subsp.

cylleneum, commonly known as the hairy hartsangel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is found in Mediterranean climates.

The plant features greyish-green, hairy leaves that are lance-shaped and often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from pale yellow to brownish hues.

It is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and antispasmodic properties.
