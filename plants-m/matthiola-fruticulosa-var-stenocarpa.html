Matthiola fruticulosa var. stenocarpa

Matthiola fruticulosa var. stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruited stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features pinnately lobed leaves and develops small, round, green fruits that mature to a dark purple or black color. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils with full sun exposure.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a source of nectar for pollinators.
