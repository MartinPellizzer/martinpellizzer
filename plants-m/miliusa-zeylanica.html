Miliusa zeylanica

Miliusa zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon gooseberry, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and dense foliage. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or red when ripe, containing a single seed. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
