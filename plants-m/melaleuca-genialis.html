Melaleuca genialis

Melaleuca genialis, commonly known as the silver bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and cylindrical, bottlebrush-like flowers that bloom in winter. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Melaleuca genialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its hardiness and visual interest.
