Melica commersonii

Melica commersonii, commonly known as Commerson's brome, is a grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue.

Melica commersonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
