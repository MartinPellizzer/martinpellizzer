Mendoncia velloziana

Mendoncia velloziana, commonly known as the Velloziana lily, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous terrain. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces bulbous structures that store nutrients for growth.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
