Miltonia spectabilis var. aspersa

Miltonia spectabilis var. aspersa, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green foliage and striking, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink and lavender. The plant produces pendulous, showy blooms that are often adorned with a contrasting lip. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
