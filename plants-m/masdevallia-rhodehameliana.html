Masdevallia rhodehameliana

Masdevallia rhodehameliana, commonly known as the Rhodehameliana orchid, is a species native to the Andean region of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with a unique combination of colors and patterns that make it highly ornamental.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.
