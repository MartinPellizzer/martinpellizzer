Megastachya owariensis

Megastachya owariensis, commonly known as the Japanese orchid or Owari orchid, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, showy flowers that typically bloom in late summer. The plant features tall, slender stems with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-green to white flowers. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
