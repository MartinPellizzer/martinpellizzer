Monsonia dregeana

Monsonia dregeana, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink or purple. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Monsonia dregeana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
