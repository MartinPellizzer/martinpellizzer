Maxillaria elatior

Maxillaria elatior, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, providing a lush green backdrop to its striking blooms.

Maxillaria elatior is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
