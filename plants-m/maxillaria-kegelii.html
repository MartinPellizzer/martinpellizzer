Maxillaria kegelii

Maxillaria kegelii, commonly known as the Kegel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria kegelii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
