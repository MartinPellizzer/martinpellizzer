Morgania glabra var. floribunda

Morgania glabra var. floribunda, commonly known as the smooth morgania, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a suitable choice for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated for its delicate floral display.
