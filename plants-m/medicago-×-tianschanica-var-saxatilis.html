Medicago × tianschanica var. saxatilis

Medicago × tianschanica var. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock medic, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit, trifoliate leaves, and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing a deep root system for water retention. It produces small, cylindrical pods containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh conditions.
