Mammillaria centricirrha f. zuccariniana

Mammillaria centricirrha f. zuccariniana, commonly known as the Zuccariniana Mammillaria, is a unique cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant in succulent collections.
