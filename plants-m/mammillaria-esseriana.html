Mammillaria esseriana

Mammillaria esseriana, commonly known as the Esser's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.
