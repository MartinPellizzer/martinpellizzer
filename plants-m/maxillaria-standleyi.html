Maxillaria standleyi

Maxillaria standleyi, commonly known as the Standley's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both greenhouse and indoor cultivation.

Maxillaria standleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
