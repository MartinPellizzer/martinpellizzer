Miconia hirtellicaulis

Miconia hirtellicaulis, commonly known as the hairy-stemmed miconia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers. Miconia hirtellicaulis is also notable for its ability to produce copious amounts of fruit, which serves as a food source for various wildlife species.
