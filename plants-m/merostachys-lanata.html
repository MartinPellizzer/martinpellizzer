Merostachys lanata

Merostachys lanata, commonly known as the woolly grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit and hairy, often silver-gray foliage. Its inflorescence consists of spikelets that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

Merostachys lanata is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
