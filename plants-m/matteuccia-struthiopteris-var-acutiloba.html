Matteuccia struthiopteris var. acutiloba

Matteuccia struthiopteris var. acutiloba, commonly known as the sharp-lobed shield fern, is a variant of the barren sword fern.

It is a perennial plant belonging to the family Onocophoraceae, characterized by its distinctive, shield-shaped fronds. The fronds are typically bipinnate, with sharp, acuminate lobes that give the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.
