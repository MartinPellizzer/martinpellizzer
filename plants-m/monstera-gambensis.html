Monstera gambensis

Monstera gambensis, commonly known as the African giant leaf plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to western Africa.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The leaves are typically perforated with distinctive holes and notches, giving the plant its dramatic appearance. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This plant is popular as a houseplant due to its striking foliage and air-purifying properties.
