Mammillaria valida

Mammillaria valida, commonly known as the Valid's hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and is covered in white to grayish spines. The plant typically grows to a height of 5 to 10 centimeters and features small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
