Mikania pyrifolia

Mikania pyrifolia, commonly known as flame vine, is a climbing plant native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures. Its fruits are dry, three-lobed capsules that release seeds when mature.

Mikania pyrifolia is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
