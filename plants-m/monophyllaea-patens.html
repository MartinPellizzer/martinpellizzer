Monophyllaea patens

Monophyllaea patens, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and bell-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
