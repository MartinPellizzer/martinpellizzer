Millettia impressa

Millettia impressa, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some regions for medicinal purposes.
