Muhlenbergia meziana

Muhlenbergia meziana, commonly known as the desert muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and delicate, pinkish-purple inflorescences that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.

Muhlenbergia meziana plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and nectar for local pollinators.
