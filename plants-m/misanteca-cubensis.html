Misanteca cubensis

Misanteca cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant native to the Caribbean, particularly Cuba.

It belongs to the family Viscaraceae and grows on the branches of various host trees, such as mahogany and ceiba. Due to its parasitic nature, it can significantly affect the health of its host tree if left unchecked.
