Mammillaria verticealba var. zacatecasensis

Mammillaria verticealba var. zacatecasensis, commonly known as the Zacatecas milk barrel cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to white body with prominent radial spines and a distinctive white or pale pink coloration. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its otherwise muted appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a potted plant.
