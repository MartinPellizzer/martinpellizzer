Misipus

Misipus, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically has a short, thick stem with rosettes of waxy, dark green leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on long, slender stalks and bloom in clusters.

Misipus is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
