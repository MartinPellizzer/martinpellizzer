Moraea gladiata

Moraea gladiata, commonly known as the sword lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and tall, showy inflorescences. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting black or purple markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Moraea gladiata is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
