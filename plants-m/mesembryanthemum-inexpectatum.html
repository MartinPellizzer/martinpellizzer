Mesembryanthemum inexpectatum

Mesembryanthemum inexpectatum, commonly known as the unexpected iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its unique adaptations to arid environments. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white. Its ornamental value and drought tolerance make it a desirable addition to xeriscapes.
