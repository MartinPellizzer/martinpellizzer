Maxillaria lawrencei

Maxillaria lawrencei, commonly known as the Lawrence's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structures. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple, and are often fragrant.

Maxillaria lawrencei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
