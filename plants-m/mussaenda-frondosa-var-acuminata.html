Mussaenda frondosa var. acuminata

Mussaenda frondosa var. acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green foliage and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers. The plant is a shrub that can grow up to 3 meters tall, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to ornamental gardens.
