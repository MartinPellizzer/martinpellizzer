Mammea punctata var. punctata

Mammea punctata var. punctata, commonly known as the spotted mammea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces orange-red, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
