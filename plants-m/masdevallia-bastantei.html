Masdevallia bastantei

Masdevallia bastantei, commonly known as the Bolivian masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Bolivia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces showy, long-lasting blooms. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to the cool, humid conditions of its native habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and unique appearance, it is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
