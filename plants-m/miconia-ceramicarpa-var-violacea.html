Miconia ceramicarpa var. violacea

Miconia ceramicarpa var. violacea, commonly known as the purple-veined miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, striking leaves with distinctive purple veins. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
