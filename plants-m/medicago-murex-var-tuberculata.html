Medicago murex var. tuberculata

Medicago murex var. tuberculata, commonly known as the tuberculate goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, followed by elongated, bean-like pods containing lens-shaped seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and restoration projects.
