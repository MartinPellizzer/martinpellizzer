Mentha × kerneri var. skofitziana

Mentha × kerneri var. skofitziana, commonly known as the Skofitziana mint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly fuzzy leaves that are often a mix of green and silver in color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and mild mint scent, making it a popular choice for garden use.
