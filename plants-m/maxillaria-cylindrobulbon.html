Maxillaria cylindrobulbon

Maxillaria cylindrobulbon, commonly known as the cylindrical bulbous orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its robust, pseudobulb-like stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces multiple racemes of fragrant blooms, which can last for several weeks during the flowering season. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the base of the plant.

This species is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
