Machaerina myriantha

Machaerina myriantha, commonly known as the New Zealand tree fern, is a distinctive plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Cyatheaceae family and is characterized by its tall, unbranched trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in optimal conditions. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to thrive in moist, shaded environments.

The fronds are typically dark green and can be used for ornamental purposes in gardens.
