Malvaviscus arboreus var. palmanus

Malvaviscus arboreus var. palmanus, commonly known as the velvetleaf or tropical hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are velvety to the touch, and its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas or near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
