Mimusops globosa

Mimusops globosa, commonly known as the globe thornapple, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, glossy, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. Its fruit is a spherical, fleshy capsule that splits open to reveal several seeds.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
