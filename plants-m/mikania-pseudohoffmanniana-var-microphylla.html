Mikania pseudohoffmanniana var. microphylla

Mikania pseudohoffmanniana var. microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved mikania, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings.

This variant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
