Myriactis candelabrum

Myriactis candelabrum, commonly known as the candlestick plant, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a branched, tree-like structure with multiple stems that resemble candlesticks, hence its common name. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months.

Its compact size and distinctive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
