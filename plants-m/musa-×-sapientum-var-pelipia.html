Musa × sapientum var. pelipia

Musa × sapientum var. pelipia, commonly known as the Pelipia banana, is a cultivated variety of the genus Musa.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. The plant exhibits a robust growth habit with broad, lance-shaped leaves that can reach several meters in length. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its fruit quality and is often grown for both commercial and ornamental purposes.
