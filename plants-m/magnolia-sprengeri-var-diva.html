Magnolia sprengeri var. diva

Magnolia sprengeri var. diva, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the white magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, elongated fruits that contain winged seeds.

It thrives in partial to full shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
