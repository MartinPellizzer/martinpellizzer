Morina bulleyana

Morina bulleyana, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with spiny margins and yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Morina bulleyana is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional Chinese medicine.
