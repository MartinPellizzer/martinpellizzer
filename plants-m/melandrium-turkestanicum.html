Melandrium turkestanicum

Melandrium turkestanicum, commonly known as the Turkestan catchfly, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are linear and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often branched.
