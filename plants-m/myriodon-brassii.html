Myriodon brassii

Myriodon brassii, commonly known as the yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant produces small, yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers. Myriodon brassii thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the summer months.
