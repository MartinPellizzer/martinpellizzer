Monstera holtoniana

Monstera holtoniana, commonly known as the Holton's monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive splits as the plant matures. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.

It requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain optimal growth.
