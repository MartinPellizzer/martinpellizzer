Maxillaria binotii

Maxillaria binotii, commonly known as the spotted maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces flowers that are typically pale pink to white with a yellowish or greenish tint. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and its pseudobulbs are somewhat elongated. Maxillaria binotii thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
