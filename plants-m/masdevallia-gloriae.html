Masdevallia gloriae

Masdevallia gloriae, commonly known as the Gloria's Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful inflorescences and delicate, strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are notable for their symmetry and vibrant hues, which range from white to deep red.

Masdevallia gloriae thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for specialized orchid collections.
