Malva vidalii

Malva vidalii, commonly known as the Vidal's mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly found in regions of Argentina and Chile. The plant features showy, pinkish-purple flowers that bloom during the spring and summer seasons. Its leaves are broad and palmately lobed, often covered in fine hairs.

Malva vidalii is typically found in moist, sunny habitats such as grasslands and riverbanks.
