Myristica stenophylla

Myristica stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, white flowers and has compound leaves with a leathery texture. Its seeds, known as nutmeg, are enclosed within a reddish outer layer called the mace.

This species is valued for its economic importance in the spice trade and its ecological role in tropical forests.
