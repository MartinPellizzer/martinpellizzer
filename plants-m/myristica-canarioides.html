Myristica canarioides

Myristica canarioides, commonly known as the Canarioides nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic bark and seeds used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and develops dark brown, hard-shelled fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its botanical features include compound leaves with elliptical leaflets and a distinctive, fragrant resinous odor.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is also used in the production of essential oils.
