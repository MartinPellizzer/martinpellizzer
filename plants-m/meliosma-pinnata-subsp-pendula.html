Meliosma pinnata subsp. pendula

Meliosma pinnata subsp.

pendula, commonly known as the weeping meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its pendulous, lanceolate leaves that hang gracefully from its branches, giving it a weeping appearance.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
