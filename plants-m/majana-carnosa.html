Majana carnosa

Majana carnosa, commonly known as the woolly majana, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
