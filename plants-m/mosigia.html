Mosigia

Mosigia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that are covered in fine, reflective hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible, round fruits resembling small apples. Mosigia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
