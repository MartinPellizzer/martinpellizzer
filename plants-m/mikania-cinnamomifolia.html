Mikania cinnamomifolia

Mikania cinnamomifolia, commonly known as the cinnamon leaf mikania, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, oval-shaped fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain areas.
