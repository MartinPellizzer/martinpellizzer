Mystacorchis mystax

Mystacorchis mystax, commonly known as the beard orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its unique appearance, featuring a hairy, fleshy stem and a single, showy flower that is often brightly colored. The plant is native to southern Africa, particularly South Africa, where it thrives in arid and rocky environments. Its flowers are typically pollinated by insects, and it produces small, round seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

The orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens due to its striking appearance.
