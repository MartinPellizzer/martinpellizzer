Macrosiphonia brachysiphon var. magnifica

Macrosiphonia brachysiphon var. magnifica, commonly known as the magnificent short-siphon fern, is a striking variant of the short-siphon fern.

It is characterized by its lush, dark green fronds that are broader and more robust than those of the typical species. The plant features a tall, upright rhizome with dense clusters of spore-bearing sorus on the undersides of its leaves. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
