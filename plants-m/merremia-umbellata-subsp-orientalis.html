Merremia umbellata subsp. orientalis

Merremia umbellata subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia. It is a climbing vine characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The leaves are typically heart-shaped and can be hairy, while the stems are woody and often have a climbing habit. This species is known for its rapid growth and is often used in ornamental gardening.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
