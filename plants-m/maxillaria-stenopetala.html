Maxillaria stenopetala

Maxillaria stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine, soft pubescence. Its flowers are typically borne on terminal racemes and have a unique, intricate structure with prominent sepals and petals.

Maxillaria stenopetala is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
