Mesembryanthemum turbiniforme

Mesembryanthemum turbiniforme, commonly known as the turban-shaped ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
