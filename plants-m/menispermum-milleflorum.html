Menispermum milleflorum

Menispermum milleflorum, commonly known as the white moonseed, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Menispermaceae.

It is native to the eastern United States and thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically palmately lobed and have a distinctive, somewhat heart-shaped appearance.

The fruit is a red, fleshy drupe that matures in the fall, containing several seeds.
