Miconia littlei

Miconia littlei, commonly known as the Hawaiian miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in the wet forests of Hawaii. The plant has dark green, elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a three-lobed capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
