Mammillaria Lasiacantha Subsp. Hyalina

Mammillaria lasiacantha subsp. hyalina is a striking cactus native to the deserts of Mexico. It is known for its unique, glassy white spines that give it a frosty appearance. This subspecies thrives in arid environments with minimal rainfall and intense sunlight. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections. Despite its beauty, it requires careful handling due to its sharp spines and sensitivity to overwatering.
