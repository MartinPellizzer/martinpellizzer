Mertensia davurica

Mertensia davurica, commonly known as Siberian bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It features striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale blue to lavender, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in well-drained, moist soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mertensia davurica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and ability to naturalize in suitable climates.
