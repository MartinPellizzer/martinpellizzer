Myrstiphyllum loniceroides

Myrstiphyllum loniceroides, commonly known as the New Zealand honeysuckle, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, and it can grow up to 3 meters in height.

Myrstiphyllum loniceroides is often found in coastal and forested areas, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.
