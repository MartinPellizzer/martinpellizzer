Mammillaria radians var. echinus

Mammillaria radians var. echinus, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
