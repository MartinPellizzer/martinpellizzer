Matricaria lesbiaca

Matricaria lesbiaca, commonly known as the Lesbia's chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, white to yellow flowers that are often surrounded by pale green ray florets. Matricaria lesbiaca typically grows in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and coastal areas.

It is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
