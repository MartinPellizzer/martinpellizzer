Monstera gentryi

Monstera gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with distinctive notches and holes, which are more pronounced as the plant matures. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil. Its rapid growth rate makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Monstera gentryi is also valued for its ability to purify the air and add a lush, tropical aesthetic to any space.
