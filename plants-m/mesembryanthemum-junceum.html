Mesembryanthemum junceum

Mesembryanthemum junceum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesembryanthemum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and unique appearance make it an attractive addition to low-maintenance gardens.
