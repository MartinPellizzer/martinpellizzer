Micromelum monophyllum

Micromelum monophyllum, commonly known as the Australian sour orange, is a small evergreen tree native to Australia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round, orange-yellow fruits that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy regions.

Due to its ornamental value and unique characteristics, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
