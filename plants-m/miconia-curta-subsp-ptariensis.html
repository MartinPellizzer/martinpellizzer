Miconia curta subsp. ptariensis

Miconia curta subsp.

ptariensis, commonly known as the short miconia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a glossy surface.

The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, which are often followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
