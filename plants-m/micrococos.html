Micrococos

Micrococos, commonly known as the sea holly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue or purple flowers and silver-white leaves. The plant is native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean and the western coast of Africa. Micrococos species are often found growing in sandy or rocky soils along the shoreline.
