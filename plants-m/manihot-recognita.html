Manihot recognita

Manihot recognita, commonly known as the African arrowleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forest understories. Its fruits are capsules that contain several seeds.

Botanically, it is part of the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its latex-producing species.
