Microstegium somae

Microstegium somae, commonly known as the African silver grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, wiry stems that are often covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly rough texture.

Microstegium somae is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
