Mammillaria rhodantha var. droegeana

Mammillaria rhodantha var. droegeana, commonly known as the Droegge's nipple cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
