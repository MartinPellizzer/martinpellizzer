Mammillaria magnimamma var. bockii

Mammillaria magnimamma var. bockii, commonly known as the Bock's giant mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, rounded, ribbed stems that are often covered in white to yellowish spines. The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
