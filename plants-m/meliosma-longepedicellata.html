Meliosma longepedicellata

Meliosma longepedicellata, commonly known as the long pedicellate meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It has compound leaves with opposite arrangement and produces small, woody fruits.
