Masdevallia wageneriana var. colombiana

Masdevallia wageneriana var. colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often iridescent flowers that come in a range of colors, including purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of narrow, leathery leaves that are adapted to the humid, shaded environments of its natural habitat.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and exotic appearance.
