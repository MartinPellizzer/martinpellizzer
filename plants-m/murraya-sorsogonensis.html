Murraya sorsogonensis

Murraya sorsogonensis, commonly known as the Sorsogon Province murraya, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white, followed by orange or red berries. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas and lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
