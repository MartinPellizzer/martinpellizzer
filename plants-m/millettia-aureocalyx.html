Millettia aureocalyx

Millettia aureocalyx, commonly known as the golden-flowered millettia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and attract various pollinators. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. Its bark is often smooth and may exude a reddish sap.

Millettia aureocalyx is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
