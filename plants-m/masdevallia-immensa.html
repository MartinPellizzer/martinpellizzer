Masdevallia immensa

Masdevallia immensa, commonly known as the giant masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in length, showcasing vibrant colors such as pink, red, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, anchoring itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and leathery, adapted to withstand the humid and shaded conditions of its environment.

Masdevallia immensa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
