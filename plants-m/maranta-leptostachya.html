Maranta leptostachya

Maranta leptostachya, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins, creating a striking visual effect. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically yellow or green in color.

Maranta leptostachya is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
