Mandevilla luetzelburgii

Mandevilla luetzelburgii, commonly known as the Luetzelburgii mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped, dark green leaves and produces clusters of tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant is known for its vibrant, showy blooms that range from red to pink and orange. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun.

Mandevilla luetzelburgii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its beauty and ability to climb trellises or walls.
