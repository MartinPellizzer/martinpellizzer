Monstera fendleri

Monstera fendleri, commonly known as the Mexican giant leaf monstera, is a tropical plant native to southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to three feet in length. The plant has a climbing habit and produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by orange fruits. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
