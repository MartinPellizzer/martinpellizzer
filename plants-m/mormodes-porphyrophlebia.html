Mormodes porphyrophlebia

Mormodes porphyrophlebia, commonly known as the purple-leaved orchid, is a rare and striking member of the orchid family.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple foliage and delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique coloration makes it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often challenging to cultivate outside its native range.
