Micromeles alnifolia f. oblongocarpa

Micromeles alnifolia f. oblongocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit alder, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and produces small, clustered flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats. It has a compact growth habit and is relatively drought-tolerant.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
