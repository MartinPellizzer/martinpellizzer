Morinda costata

Morinda costata, commonly known as the red morinda or Chinese red root, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its erect stems and alternate, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Morinda costata is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
