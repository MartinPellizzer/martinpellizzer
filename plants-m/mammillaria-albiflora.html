Mammillaria albiflora

Mammillaria albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a spiny, segmented stem that often develops a white or pale coloration. The plant produces delicate, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
