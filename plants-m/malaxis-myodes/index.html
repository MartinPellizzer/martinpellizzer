Malaxis Myodes

Malaxis myodes, also known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it is known for its pale greenish-white flowers that resemble ghostly apparitions, hence its common name. this epiphytic orchid thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. due to its limited habitat and threats from habitat loss, it is considered endangered. its unique beauty and ecological significance make it a subject of fascination for botanists and conservationists alike.
