Maba zenkeri

Maba zenkeri, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its strength and durability. The tree has a straight trunk with smooth, gray bark and produces small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Maba zenkeri is also notable for its ability to thrive in poor soils and its role in traditional medicine and local construction.
