Matucana winteri

Matucana winteri, commonly known as the Winter's matucana, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family and is native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is a terrestrial bromeliad that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in height. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often hidden within the foliage.

Matucana winteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in the right conditions.
