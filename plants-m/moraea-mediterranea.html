Moraea mediterranea

Moraea mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and North Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like foliage and striking, showy flowers. The plant typically produces vibrant purple or pink blooms that appear in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Moraea mediterranea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its colorful display.
