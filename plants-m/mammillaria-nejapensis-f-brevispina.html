Mammillaria nejapensis f. brevispina

Mammillaria nejapensis f. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with a grayish-green color and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
