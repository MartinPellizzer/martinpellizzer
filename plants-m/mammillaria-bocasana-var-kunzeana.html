Mammillaria bocasana var. kunzeana

Mammillaria bocasana var. kunzeana, commonly known as the Bocasana mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with numerous small, white to pale green tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a vibrant touch to its otherwise muted color palette. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its slow growth and compact size.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
