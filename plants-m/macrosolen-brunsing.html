Macrosolen brunsing

Macrosolen brunsing, commonly known as the Brun's giant fern, is a large, tropical fern native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments with high levels of atmospheric moisture. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to spread and establish dense colonies in its natural habitat.

The fronds are typically bioluminescent at night, a unique feature that aids in attracting pollinators and deterring herbivores.
