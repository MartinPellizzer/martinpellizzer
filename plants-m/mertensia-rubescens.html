Mertensia rubescens

Mertensia rubescens, commonly known as pink heartleaf, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a reddish tinge on the underside. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, pink to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mertensia rubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in garden settings.
