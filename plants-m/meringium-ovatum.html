Meringium ovatum

Meringium ovatum, commonly known as the silverleaf meringia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.
