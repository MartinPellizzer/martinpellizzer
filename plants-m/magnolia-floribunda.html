Magnolia floribunda

Magnolia floribunda, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

The flowers have a fragrant, sweet scent and are often used in ornamental gardening.
