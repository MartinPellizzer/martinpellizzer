Mallotus pycnostachys

Mallotus pycnostachys, commonly known as the dense-stamen mallot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with numerous small leaflets. The plant produces clusters of small yellow flowers surrounded by red bracts, creating a striking visual display. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests.
