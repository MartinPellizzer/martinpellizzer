Myrianthus holstii

Myrianthus holstii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has palmately lobed leaves and produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a valuable addition to tropical and subtropical gardens.
