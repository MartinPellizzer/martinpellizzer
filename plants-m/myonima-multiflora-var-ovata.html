Myonima multiflora var. ovata

Myonima multiflora var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leaved myonima, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various garden settings.
