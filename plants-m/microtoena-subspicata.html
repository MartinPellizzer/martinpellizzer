Microtoena subspicata

Microtoena subspicata, commonly known as the slender microtoena, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

The species is noted for its unique floral structure, which includes a prominent spur and specialized pollination mechanisms.
