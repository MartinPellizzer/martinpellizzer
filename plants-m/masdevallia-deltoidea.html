Masdevallia deltoidea

Masdevallia deltoidea, commonly known as the delta-shaped masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped or delta-like petals that give it its common name. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often brightly colored, ranging from red to orange, and are adorned with intricate patterns.

Masdevallia deltoidea requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
