Microtis frutetorum

Microtis frutetorum, commonly known as the Orange-flowered Microtis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant produces a cluster of narrow, linear leaves that are typically evergreen. It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
