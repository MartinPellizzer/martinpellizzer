Mitozus tenuicaulis

Mitozus tenuicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed mitozus, is a unique plant species characterized by its delicate, elongated stems and small, clustered leaves.

It is a perennial herb native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant's foliage is often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, which helps to reflect excess light and reduce water loss. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically appearing in clusters at the base of the plant.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, Mitozus tenuicaulis is considered a rare and endangered species.
