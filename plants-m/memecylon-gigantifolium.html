Memecylon gigantifolium

Memecylon gigantifolium, commonly known as the giant-leaf memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by elliptical, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
