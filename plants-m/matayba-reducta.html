Matayba reducta

Matayba reducta, commonly known as the red-barked matayba, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes plants like eucalyptus and clove. The tree is characterized by its smooth, reddish-barked trunk and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, round berries.
