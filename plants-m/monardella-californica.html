Monardella californica

Monardella californica, commonly known as California monardella, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats such as chaparral and grasslands. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

Monardella californica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a food source for native pollinators.
