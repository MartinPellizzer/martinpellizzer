Metaporana

Metaporana, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its woody stem and evergreen nature make it suitable for ornamental gardening.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
