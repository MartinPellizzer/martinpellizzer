Micranthocereus hofackerianus

Micranthocereus hofackerianus, commonly known as the Hofacker's night bloomer, is a cactus native to Bolivia.

It is a columnar cactus with a tall, slender stem that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, white, nocturnal flowers that open at night, attracting nocturnal pollinators. Its foliage consists of spines and is adapted to arid environments.

It is valued in horticulture for its unique flowering habit and ornamental appeal.
