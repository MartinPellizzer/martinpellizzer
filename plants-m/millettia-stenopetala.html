Millettia stenopetala

Millettia stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled millettia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pod fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

Millettia stenopetala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
