Mimosa skinneri var. skinneri

Mimosa skinneri var. skinneri, commonly known as the desert mimosa, is a shrub native to arid regions of North America.

It features slender, branching stems that are often covered in a fine, white, silky coating. The plant produces small, spherical seed pods that turn from green to brown as they mature. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of small, feather-like leaflets.

It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements.
