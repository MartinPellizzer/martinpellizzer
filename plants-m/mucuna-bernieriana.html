Mucuna bernieriana

Mucuna bernieriana, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and produces pods containing edible seeds. The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many nitrogen-fixing species.

Mucuna bernieriana is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
