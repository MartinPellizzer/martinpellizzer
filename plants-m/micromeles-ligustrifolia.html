Micromeles ligustrifolia

Micromeles ligustrifolia, commonly known as the small-leafed ligustrum, is a species of flowering plant in the family Oleaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features small, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface and produces clusters of yellowish-white flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a small, spherical drupe that ripens to a dark purple color.

Micromeles ligustrifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
