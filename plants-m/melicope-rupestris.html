Melicope rupestris

Melicope rupestris, commonly known as the rock melicope, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows on rocky or coastal slopes. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by round, fleshy fruits.

Melicope rupestris is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
