Maxillaria ferruginea

Maxillaria ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a velvety red or orange hue. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their intricate, intricate structure.

Maxillaria ferruginea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
