Mikania Scandens Var. Congesta

Mikania scandens var. congesta is a fast-growing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is known for its dense foliage and rapid spread, making it both a valuable ornamental plant and a potential invasive species. This variant of Mikania scandens is characterized by its clustered inflorescences and small, white flowers. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed habitats. Due to its aggressive growth, it requires careful management to prevent ecological disruption.
