Montia meridensis

Montia meridensis, commonly known as the southern miner's lettuce, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant features small, spoon-shaped leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.

Montia meridensis is often found in regions with cool, humid climates and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.
