Mammillaria crassispina var. gracilior

Mammillaria crassispina var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow spines. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing at the tips of the stems during the spring and summer. It prefers well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
