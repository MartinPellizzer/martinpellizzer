Millettia lecomtei

Millettia lecomtei, commonly known as the Lecomte's millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and potential ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
