Marsdenia leichhardtiana

Marsdenia leichhardtiana, commonly known as the Leichhardt's Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its erect, sparsely branched stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Marsdenia leichhardtiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering habits.
