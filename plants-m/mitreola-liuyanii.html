Mitreola liuyanii

Mitreola liuyanii, commonly known as the Chinese nightshade, is a small, herbaceous plant native to China.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10-30 cm and features hairy stems and small, yellow flowers. Its berries are toxic and should not be consumed, despite their ornamental appeal.

The species is often found in moist, shaded environments in its native habitat.
