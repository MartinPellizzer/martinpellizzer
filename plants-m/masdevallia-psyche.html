Masdevallia psyche

Masdevallia psyche, commonly known as the "Psyche Masdevallia," is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers with a unique, almost ethereal appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

The orchid's blooming period occurs during the cooler months, producing delicate, fragrant flowers that attract specific pollinators.
