Mikania scandens subvar. mollis

Mikania scandens subvar. mollis, commonly known as softleaf mile-a-minute, is a fast-growing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are soft and hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by elliptical fruits. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
