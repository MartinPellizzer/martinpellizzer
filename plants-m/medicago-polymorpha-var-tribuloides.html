Medicago polymorpha var. tribuloides

Medicago polymorpha var. tribuloides, commonly known as goat's rue, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, spiny fruits called pods, which contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in ecological and agricultural contexts.
