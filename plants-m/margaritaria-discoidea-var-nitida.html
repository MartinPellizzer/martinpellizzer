Margaritaria discoidea var. nitida

Margaritaria discoidea var. nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a spherical berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

The species is known for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in semi-shaded environments.
