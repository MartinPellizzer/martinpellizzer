Mammillaria Heyderi F. Applanata

Applanata mammillaria, scientifically known as Mammillaria heyderi f. applanata, is a unique cactus species native to the arid regions of Mexico. This plant is distinguished by its flat, disc-like shape and lack of typical spines, giving it a strikingly elegant appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for succulent enthusiasts. Its compact size and ornamental value have made it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens. Due to its rarity and specific care requirements, it is often sought after by collectors and botanists alike.
