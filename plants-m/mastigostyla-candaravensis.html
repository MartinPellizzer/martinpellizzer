Mastigostyla candaravensis

Mastigostyla candaravensis, commonly known as the Malagasy cactus, is a unique plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its succulent stems and spines. The plant exhibits a low-growing, mat-forming growth habit, often covering rocky or arid landscapes. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces small, fleshy fruits.
