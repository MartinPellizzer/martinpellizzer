Menispermum dauricum var. pauciflorum

Menispermum dauricum var. pauciflorum, commonly known as the Daurian arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of East Asia.

It is a member of the Menispermaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, palmate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in umbels. Its fruit is a follicle containing several elliptical seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
