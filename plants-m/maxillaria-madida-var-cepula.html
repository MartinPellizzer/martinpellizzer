Maxillaria madida var. cepula

Maxillaria madida var. cepula, commonly known as the Madida Cepula orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the warm season.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
