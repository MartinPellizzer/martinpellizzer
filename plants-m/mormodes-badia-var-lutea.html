Mormodes badia var. lutea

Mormodes badia var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow mormodes, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the warm season. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
