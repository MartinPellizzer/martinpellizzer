Medinilla heteromorphophylla

Medinilla heteromorphophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, waxy, and often fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant features alternate, leathery, and elliptical leaves that can vary in shape, contributing to its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Medinilla heteromorphophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
