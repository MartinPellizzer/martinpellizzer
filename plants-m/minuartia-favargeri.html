Minuartia favargeri

Minuartia favargeri, commonly known as the favargeri rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, bright green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. The plant produces clusters of tiny, four-petaled white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer.
