Mayna longifolia var. heliocarpa

Mayna longifolia var. heliocarpa, commonly known as the sun-bearing mayna, is a variant of the mayna plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its elongated leaves and distinctive fruit structure. The plant produces fleshy, orange-hued fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. Its botanical features include a woody stem and small, clustered flowers.

This variety is valued for both its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
