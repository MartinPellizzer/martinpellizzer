Melodorum minuticalyx

Melodorum minuticalyx, commonly known as the small-fruited melody vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pale yellow or white, arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Melodorum minuticalyx is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and delicate floral display.
