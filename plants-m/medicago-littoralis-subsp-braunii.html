Medicago littoralis subsp. braunii

Medicago littoralis subsp.

braunii, commonly known as the coastal medic, is a perennial legume native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia. It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant thrives in saline and alkaline soils, often found in dunes and coastal grasslands. It has a deep root system that helps stabilize sandy soils and prevent erosion.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important species for soil enrichment in coastal ecosystems.
