Muhlenbergia sylvatica var. californica

Muhlenbergia sylvatica var. californica, commonly known as California wood oatgrass, is a perennial grass native to the western United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland and chaparral habitats. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, pinkish-white seed heads in late summer. It is characterized by its clumping growth habit and ability to thrive in partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
