Medicago × annularis

Medicago × annularis, commonly known as the yellow sweetclover, is a hybrid perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a robust root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its seeds are small and can remain viable in the soil for several years, contributing to its persistence in various habitats.
