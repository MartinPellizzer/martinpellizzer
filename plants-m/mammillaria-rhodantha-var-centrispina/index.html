Mammillaria Rhodantha Var. Centrispina

Red-tipped hedgehog cactus, scientifically known as Mammillaria rhodantha var. centrispina, is a striking member of the cacti family native to arid regions of Mexico. This unique plant is named for its distinctive red-tipped spines and its resemblance to the spiky body of a hedgehog. It thrives in dry, well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens or indoor cultivation. Its compact size and vibrant color make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts. With proper care, this cactus can flourish and add a touch of exotic beauty to any collection.
