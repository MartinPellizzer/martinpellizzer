Maxillaria muscicola

Maxillaria muscicola, commonly known as the mossy maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, dark violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and velvety, while its roots are often covered in a dense layer of moss.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
