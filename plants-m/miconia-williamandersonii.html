Miconia williamandersonii

Miconia williamandersonii, commonly known as the Anderson's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, white to pinkish flowers arranged in clusters, followed by bright red berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
