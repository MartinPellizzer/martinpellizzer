Muscari maweanum

Muscari maweanum, commonly known as the Mawean musk grape, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the eastern Mediterranean region and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It is often used in ornamental gardening for its unique appearance and aromatic qualities.
