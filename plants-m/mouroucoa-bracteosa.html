Mouroucoa bracteosa

Mouroucoa bracteosa, commonly known as the bracteosa palm, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that eventually develop into elongated, cylindrical fruits. Its growth habit makes it suitable for ornamental use in warm climates.

The species is noted for its ability to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.
