Millettia nivea

Millettia nivea, commonly known as the white millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with alternate arrangement and small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round pods containing several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes many nitrogen-fixing plants.
