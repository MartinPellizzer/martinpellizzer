Macaranga densiflora

Macaranga densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered mallow, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Macaranga densiflora is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
