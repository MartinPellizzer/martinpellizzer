Matourea platychila

Matourea platychila, commonly known as the narrow-leaved matourea, is a member of the Malvaceae family.

It is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with five petals.
