Machilus miaoshanensis

Machilus miaoshanensis, commonly known as the Miaoshan laurel, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by oval, dark blue berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
