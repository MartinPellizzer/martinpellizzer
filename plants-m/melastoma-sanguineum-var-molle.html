Melastoma sanguineum var. molle

Melastoma sanguineum var. molle, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-red flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruits are small, round berries that turn red when mature.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
