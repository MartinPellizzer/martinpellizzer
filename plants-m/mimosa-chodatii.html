Mimosa chodatii

Mimosa chodatii, commonly known as the desert mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant has compound leaves that are sensitive to touch, causing them to fold when disturbed. Its small, yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the spring.

Mimosa chodatii is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.
