Mondo bodinieri

Mondo bodinieri, commonly known as the "Chinese evergreen," is a member of the Agavaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its rosette of thick, fleshy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces tall, branched inflorescences bearing small, white or pale green flowers. It is native to Southeast Asia and is well adapted to humid, shady environments.

Mondo bodinieri is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
