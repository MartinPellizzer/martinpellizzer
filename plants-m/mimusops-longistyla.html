Mimusops longistyla

Mimusops longistyla, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain hard, woody seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal forests and secondary vegetation.

Due to its ornamental value and durable wood, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
