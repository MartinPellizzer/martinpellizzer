Myristica integra

Myristica integra, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the cinnamon tree. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into dark red, fleshy fruits containing the aromatic seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a smooth texture with a glossy surface.

The tree is valued for its commercial spices and has cultural significance in traditional medicine and rituals.
