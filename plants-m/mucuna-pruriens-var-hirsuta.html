Mucuna pruriens var. hirsuta

Mucuna pruriens var. hirsuta, commonly known as hairy urd, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, trifoliate leaves and distinctive, twisted, hairy pods that contain elliptical, brown seeds. The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in diverse soil conditions. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
