Medicago gerardi

Medicago gerardi, commonly known as Turkey Red Clover, is a perennial legume native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and purple or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has a robust root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and as a cover crop.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
