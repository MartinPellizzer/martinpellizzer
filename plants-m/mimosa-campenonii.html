Mimosa campenonii

Mimosa campenonii, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of pink to pale purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 6 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.

Its flowers are not only ornamental but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
