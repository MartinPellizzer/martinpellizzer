Mimosa castanoclada

Mimosa castanoclada, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its leaves are often covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a soft texture.

Mimosa castanoclada is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping.
