Mycelis deltoidea var. talyschensis

Mycelis deltoidea var. talyschensis, commonly known as the Talyschen Delta Mycelium, is a rare and unique variant of the Mycelis deltoidea species.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fan-shaped mycelial structure that forms dense, interconnected networks beneath the soil surface. This variant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forest soils rich in organic matter. Its growth is closely associated with specific fungal symbionts that contribute to its ecological niche.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its role in soil health and nutrient cycling within its native habitat.
