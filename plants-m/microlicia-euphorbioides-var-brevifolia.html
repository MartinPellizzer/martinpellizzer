Microlicia euphorbioides var. brevifolia

Microlicia euphorbioides var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved microlicia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, elliptical leaves. Its flowers are clustered in cyathia, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family, and are often yellowish-green in color.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
