Mesembryanthemum heterophyllum

Mesembryanthemum heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species with fleshy, often blue or purple-tinged leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the warm season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
