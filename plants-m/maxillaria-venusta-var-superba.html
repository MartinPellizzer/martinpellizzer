Maxillaria venusta var. superba

Maxillaria venusta var. superba, commonly known as the Superb Lady's Slipper, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and bloom in late winter to early spring.

The variety 'superba' is prized for its vibrant colors and ornamental value in horticulture.
