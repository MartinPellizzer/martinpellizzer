Malva morenii var. reichenbachiana

Malva morenii var. reichenbachiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving them a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and ecological gardening.
