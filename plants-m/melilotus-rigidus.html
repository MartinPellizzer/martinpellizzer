Melilotus rigidus

Melilotus rigidus, commonly known as rigid milkvetch, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with numerous small leaflets, and its root system is fibrous and well-developed. It thrives in dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.

Melilotus rigidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.
