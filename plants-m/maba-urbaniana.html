Maba urbaniana

Maba urbaniana, commonly known as the African maba tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sterculiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, palmately lobed leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by round, woody fruits containing a single seed. It is often found in dry, open woodlands and can grow up to 20 meters in height.

The plant is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in various local communities.
