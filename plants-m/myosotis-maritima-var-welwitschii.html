Myosotis maritima var. welwitschii

Myosotis maritima var. welwitschii, commonly known as the Welwitsch's coastal forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in coastal areas, thriving in sandy or rocky soils. It is often found in maritime climates with moderate temperatures and salty air.

Its small stature and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
