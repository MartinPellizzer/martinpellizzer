Mesembryanthemum juritzii

Mesembryanthemum juritzii, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Mesembryanthemum juritzii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
