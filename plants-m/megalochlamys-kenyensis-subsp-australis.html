Megalochlamys kenyensis subsp. australis

Megalochlamys kenyensis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the East African orchid, is a flowering plant native to Kenya and Tanzania. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, and it produces showy, often yellow or greenish-yellow blooms.
