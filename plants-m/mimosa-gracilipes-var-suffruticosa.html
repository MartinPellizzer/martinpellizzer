Mimosa gracilipes var. suffruticosa

Mimosa gracilipes var. suffruticosa, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a shrub-like plant native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding shut when disturbed. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry gardens.

Its unique foliage and ability to respond to stimuli make it an interesting addition to botanical collections.
