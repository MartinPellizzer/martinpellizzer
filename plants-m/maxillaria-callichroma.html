Maxillaria callichroma

Maxillaria callichroma, commonly known as the golden maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking golden-yellow flowers with red veins, which bloom during the summer months. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are elongated and dark green, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Maxillaria callichroma is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
