Miconia cladonia

Miconia cladonia, commonly known as the tropical tree fern, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a dense crown of feathery fronds. The plant is native to tropical regions of the Americas and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its rhizome system allows it to spread and form dense colonies.

The leaves are typically elongated and have a waxy surface, contributing to its ability to retain moisture in its natural habitat.
