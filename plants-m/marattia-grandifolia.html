Marattia grandifolia

Marattia grandifolia, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, upright fronds that can reach several meters in height. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit and produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds. Its leaves are typically pinnately compound, with a feathery appearance.

Marattia grandifolia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
