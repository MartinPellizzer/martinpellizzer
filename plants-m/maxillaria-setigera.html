Maxillaria setigera

Maxillaria setigera, commonly known as the feathered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, thin sepals and petals that resemble feathers, giving it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria setigera is a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
