Monardella kruckebergii

Monardella kruckebergii, commonly known as Kruckeberg's monardella, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. This species is characterized by its slender stems, opposite leaves, and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streamside areas.
