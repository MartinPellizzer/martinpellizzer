Macrolobium demonstrans var. talbotii

Macrolobium demonstrans var. talbotii, commonly known as the Talbot's giant lobelia, is a striking member of the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white or grayish pubescence. Native to the high-altitude regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts who appreciate its dramatic floral display.
