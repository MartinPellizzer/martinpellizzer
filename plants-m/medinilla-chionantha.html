Medinilla chionantha

Medinilla chionantha, commonly known as the snowmedinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble snowflakes, hence its common name. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Medinilla chionantha is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under proper care.
