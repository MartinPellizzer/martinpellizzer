Mimosa debilis var. parapitiensis

Mimosa debilis var. parapitiensis, commonly known as the parapitiensis mimosa, is a delicate shrub native to parts of South America.

It features small, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, curling when disturbed. The plant produces fluffy, yellowish-pink flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique leaf response and soft, dainty flowers.
