Monopyle macrocarpa var. costaricana

Monopyle macrocarpa var. costaricana, commonly known as the Costa Rican starwood, is a tropical tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within lowland tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
