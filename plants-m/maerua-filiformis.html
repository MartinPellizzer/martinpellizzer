Maerua filiformis

Maerua filiformis, commonly known as the slender maerua, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with deep roots that help it access water from underground sources. It is often found in sandy soils and can thrive in areas with low rainfall.

Maerua filiformis is also valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
