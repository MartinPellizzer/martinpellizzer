Minuartia decipiens

Minuartia decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats on rocky or sandy substrates. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, linear, and often covered in fine hairs.

It is well adapted to harsh environments and is found in regions such as Europe, Asia, and North America.
