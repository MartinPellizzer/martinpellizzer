Mesanthemum tuberosum

Mesanthemum tuberosum, commonly known as the tuberous mesanthemum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves, which store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically during the warm seasons. Its tuberous roots are an important feature, enabling it to survive droughts by storing nutrients and moisture.

Mesanthemum tuberosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
