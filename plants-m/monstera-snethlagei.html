Monstera snethlagei

Monstera snethlagei, commonly known as the Swiss cheese plant, is a tropical vine native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive holes and splits as they mature. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture in its soil. It is a popular houseplant due to its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.

Its growth habit involves climbing, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises.
