Mastosuke rubiginosa

Mastosuke rubiginosa, commonly known as the red-stained mastosuke, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a slight waxy coating. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and shaded landscapes.
