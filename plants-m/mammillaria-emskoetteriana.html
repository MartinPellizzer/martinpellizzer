Mammillaria emskoetteriana

Mammillaria emskoetteriana, commonly known as the Emskoetter's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant typically grows to a height of 5 to 10 centimeters and features prominent areoles with clusters of white bristles. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
