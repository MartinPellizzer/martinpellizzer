Microlepia smithii

Microlepia smithii, commonly known as the common cliff fern, is a fern species native to various regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines.

Microlepia smithii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
