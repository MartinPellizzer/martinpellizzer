Magnolia lotungensis

Magnolia lotungensis, commonly known as the Hainan magnolia, is a species of flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers. The plant typically grows in subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture, and it produces fragrant blossoms in early spring.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
