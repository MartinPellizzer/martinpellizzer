Macadamia Youngiana

Macadamia youngiana is a rare and unique species of evergreen tree native to the tropical rainforests of Queensland, Australia. It is known for its distinctive appearance, featuring glossy dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. This plant is highly valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections. Due to its limited natural range and vulnerability to habitat loss, it is considered a conservation concern. Its cultivation and study are important for both ecological and horticultural purposes.
