Mesembryanthemum strumosum

Mesembryanthemum strumosum, commonly known as the "Twinleaf Ice Plant," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It blooms during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer.

Mesembryanthemum strumosum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal.
