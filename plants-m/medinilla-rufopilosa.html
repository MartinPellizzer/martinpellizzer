Medinilla rufopilosa

Medinilla rufopilosa, commonly known as the red-haired medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of small, pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Medinilla rufopilosa is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
