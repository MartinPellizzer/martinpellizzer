Masdevallia hornii

Masdevallia hornii, commonly known as the Horn's masdevallia, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. Its leaves are typically narrow and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance. The flowers, which bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple, are known for their delicate and intricate petal arrangements.

Masdevallia hornii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively manageable care requirements.
