Mimosa papposa var. segrex

Mimosa papposa var. segrex, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant is characterized by its soft, feathery leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.

The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
