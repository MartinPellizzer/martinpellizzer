Merope argentea

Merope argentea, commonly known as silverleaf merope, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid or semi-arid environments.

Merope argentea is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
