Monenteles spicatus

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its narrow, spiky leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a tendency to form dense, low-growing mats.
