Myristica sprucei

Myristica sprucei, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has dark green, leathery leaves. Its seeds are enclosed in a hard, aromatic aril, which is used to produce nutmeg and mace.

Myristica sprucei is valued both for its medicinal properties and its role in the spice trade.
