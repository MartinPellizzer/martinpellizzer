Maxillariella estradae

Maxillariella estradae, commonly known as the Estrada's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, compact growth habit. The plant typically produces fragrant, pinkish-brown flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillariella estradae is noted for its ornamental value and is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure.
