Maranta maacelli

Maranta maacelli, commonly known as the silver stripe maranta, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, commonly referred to as the arrowroot family. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta maacelli is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
