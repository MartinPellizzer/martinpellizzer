Myristica riedelii

Myristica riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into the distinctive nutmeg-like fruits. Its seeds, encased in an aromatic aril, are valued for their essential oils and culinary uses.

Myristica riedelii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting a variety of wildlife and contributing to forest biodiversity.
