Maesa andamanica var. longipedicellata

Maesa andamanica var. longipedicellata, commonly known as the long-fruited maesa, is a shrub native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. The species is noted for its ornamental value and potential ecological importance in its native habitat.
