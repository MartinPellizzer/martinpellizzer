Miltoniopsis

Miltoniopsis, commonly known as the mini-orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. They are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. Miltoniopsis species typically have strap-like leaves and produce flowers in shades of white, pink, and red.

They are popular in horticulture for their ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
