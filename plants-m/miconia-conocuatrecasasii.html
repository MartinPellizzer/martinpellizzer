Miconia conocuatrecasasii

Miconia conocuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Mexican beautyberry, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers, which are followed by bright purple berries. These berries are not only ornamental but also serve as a food source for various wildlife.

Miconia conocuatrecasasii is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and green spaces.
