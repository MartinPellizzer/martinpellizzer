Machaeranthera wrightii

Machaeranthera wrightii, commonly known as Wright's blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind.

Wright's blazingstar is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
