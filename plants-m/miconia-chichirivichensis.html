Miconia chichirivichensis

Miconia chichirivichensis, commonly known as the Chichiriviche miconia, is a striking tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter, often with a waxy surface and a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds, aiding in its dispersal by birds and mammals.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
