Magnolia canandeana

Magnolia canandeana, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrant blossoms.
