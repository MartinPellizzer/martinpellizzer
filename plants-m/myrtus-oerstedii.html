Myrtus oerstedii

Myrtus oerstedii, commonly known as the rock myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often aromatic and have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in late spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Myrtus oerstedii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
