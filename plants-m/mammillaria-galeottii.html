Mammillaria galeottii

Mammillaria galeottii, commonly known as the Galeotti's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and dense clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. The plant features prominent areoles, from which both spines and flowers emerge. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
