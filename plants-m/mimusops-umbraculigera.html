Mimusops umbraculigera

Mimusops umbraculigera, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in winter. The tree has smooth, grey bark and large, ovate leaves that are typically arranged opposite each other. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
