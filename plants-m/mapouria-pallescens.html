Mapouria pallescens

Mapouria pallescens, commonly known as the white mapouria, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape and have a smooth, waxy texture. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a pale or white coloration.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
