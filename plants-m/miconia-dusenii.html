Miconia dusenii

Miconia dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Miconia dusenii is part of the Melastomataceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.

Its ecological role includes providing food for various bird species and contributing to the biodiversity of its habitat.
