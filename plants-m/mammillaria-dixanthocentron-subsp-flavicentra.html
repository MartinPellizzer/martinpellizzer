Mammillaria dixanthocentron subsp. flavicentra

Mammillaria dixanthocentron subsp.

flavicentra, commonly known as the golden centred mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem with prominent, whitish tubercles that bear short, spiny bristles.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from areoles on the stem. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This subspecies is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
