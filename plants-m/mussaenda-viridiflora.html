Mussaenda viridiflora

Mussaenda viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered Mussaenda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that are usually dark green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that are often overlooked due to their subtle appearance. It thrives in tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Mussaenda viridiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
