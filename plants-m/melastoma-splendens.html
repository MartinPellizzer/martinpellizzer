Melastoma splendens

Melastoma splendens, commonly known as the splendid melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Melastoma splendens is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
