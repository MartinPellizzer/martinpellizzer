Medicago sativa grex euasiatica

Medicago sativa grex euasiatica, commonly known as the Eurasian medick, is a perennial legume native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow pea-like flowers. This plant is well adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It plays a role in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Its seeds are used in traditional herbal medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
