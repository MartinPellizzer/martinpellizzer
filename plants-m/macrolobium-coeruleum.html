Macrolobium coeruleum

Macrolobium coeruleum, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from deep blue to purple, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is noted for its complex floral structure, which includes specialized petals and sepals. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, Macrolobium coeruleum is highly sought after in horticulture and conservation efforts.
