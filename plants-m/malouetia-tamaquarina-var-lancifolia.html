Malouetia tamaquarina var. lancifolia

Malouetia tamaquarina var. lancifolia, commonly known as the lanceleaf malouetia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves, which are typically dark green and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and along stream banks.
