Milla uniflora var. conspicua

Milla uniflora var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy milla, is a striking member of the Apiaceae family, characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The species is noted for its ornamental value, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.
