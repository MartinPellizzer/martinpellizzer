Micromelum minutum

Micromelum minutum, commonly known as the small-flowered starfruit, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible, star-shaped fruits that are similar in appearance to the common starfruit, though smaller and less commonly cultivated.

It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers warm, humid climates, making it a popular ornamental and edible plant in horticulture.
