Miconia prancei

Miconia prancei, commonly known as the Prance's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
