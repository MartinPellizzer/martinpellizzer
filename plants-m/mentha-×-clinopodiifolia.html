Mentha × clinopodiifolia

Mentha × clinopodiifolia, commonly known as hedge mint, is a hybrid perennial plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and dense clusters of small, pale purple flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of western Asia, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in herbal teas and culinary applications.

Mentha × clinopodiifolia is also valued for its role in traditional medicine and as a decorative plant in gardens.
