Maxillaria grandiflora var. alba

Maxillaria grandiflora var. alba, commonly known as the white-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
