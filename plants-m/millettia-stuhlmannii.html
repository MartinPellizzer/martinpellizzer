Millettia stuhlmannii

Millettia stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The tree produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, and its bark is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
