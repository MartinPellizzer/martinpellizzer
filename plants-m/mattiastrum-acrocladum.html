Mattiastrum acrocladum

Mattiastrum acrocladum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-green leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or violet in color. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.
