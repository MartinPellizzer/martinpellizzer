Mesodetra alata

Mesodetra alata, commonly known as the winged bean, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive winged pods that enclose the edible seeds. The plant has compound leaves with a trifoliate structure, and its flowers are typically pale yellow or greenish-white. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often grown for its nutritious seeds and edible leaves.

The plant also has a deep root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
