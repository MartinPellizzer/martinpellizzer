Maba gossweileri

Maba gossweileri, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized deciduous tree native to tropical Africa. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and compound leaves that are alternate and pinnate. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The sap of Maba gossweileri is milky and has been traditionally used in various local remedies.
