Melastoma tetramerum var. pentapetalum

Melastoma tetramerum var. pentapetalum, commonly known as the five-petaled melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually bisexual, with five petals and numerous stamens, contributing to its ornamental value.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
