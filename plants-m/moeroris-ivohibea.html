Moeroris ivohibea

Moeroris ivohibea, commonly known as the Madagascar starfish plant, is a unique succulent native to Madagascar.

It features a rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the surface. The plant produces delicate, star-like flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its striking appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
