Mitrephora korthalsiana

Mitrephora korthalsiana, commonly known as the Java magnolia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement and often have a waxy texture. It produces small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature.
