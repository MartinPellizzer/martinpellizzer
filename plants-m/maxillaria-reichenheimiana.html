Maxillaria reichenheimiana

Maxillaria reichenheimiana, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, yellow, and orange. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming compact pseudobulbs with fleshy leaves. Its flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are known for their intricate, showy appearance.

Maxillaria reichenheimiana is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
