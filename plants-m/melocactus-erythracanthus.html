Melocactus erythracanthus

Melocactus erythracanthus, commonly known as the red spined melon cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and the presence of prominent, reddish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture efficiently in its harsh environment.

Melocactus erythracanthus is often cultivated as a decorative cactus due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
