Medinilla myrtiformis var. papuana

Medinilla myrtiformis var. papuana, commonly known as the Papuan Medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to New Guinea.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy texture. The plant produces striking, showy inflorescences with clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
