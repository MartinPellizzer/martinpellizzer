Malvastrum limense

Malvastrum limense, commonly known as the yellow mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy yellow flowers with five petals and a distinctive central disk. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a soft, hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands and disturbed areas.

Malvastrum limense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
