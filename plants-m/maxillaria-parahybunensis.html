Maxillaria parahybunensis

Maxillaria parahybunensis, commonly known as the Parahyba orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow throat. It prefers warm, humid environments and is typically found in shaded forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both horticultural collections and botanical research.
