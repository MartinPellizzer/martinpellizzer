Manisuris altissima

Manisuris altissima, commonly known as the tall manisuris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in the summer months. Manisuris altissima is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
