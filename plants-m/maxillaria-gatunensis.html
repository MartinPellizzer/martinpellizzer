Maxillaria gatunensis

Maxillaria gatunensis, commonly known as the Gatun orchid, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its natural habitat.

Maxillaria gatunensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining substrates for optimal growth.
