Microcoelia melinantha

Microcoelia melinantha, commonly known as the starry microcoelia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate yellow flowers arranged in a compact cluster. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seeds in a small, rounded head. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
