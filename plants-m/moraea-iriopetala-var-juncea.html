Moraea iriopetala var. juncea

Moraea iriopetala var. juncea, commonly known as the narrow-leaved moraea, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, giving the plant a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
