Melastoma godeffroyi

Melastoma godeffroyi, commonly known as the yellow melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from yellow to red in color. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a rough texture, while its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Melastoma godeffroyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
