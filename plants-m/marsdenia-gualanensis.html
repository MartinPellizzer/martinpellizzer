Marsdenia gualanensis

Marsdenia gualanensis, commonly known as the Gualan milkvine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. Marsdenia gualanensis is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.
