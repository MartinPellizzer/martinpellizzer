Myriactis sakirana

Myriactis sakirana, commonly known as the sacred flame orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which resemble flames, and its unique, spiral-shaped leaves. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil. Myriactis sakirana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and cultural significance in traditional rituals.

Its botanical features make it a rare and sought-after species among horticulturists and botanists.
