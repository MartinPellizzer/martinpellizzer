Mammillaria fasciculata

Mammillaria fasciculata, commonly known as the clustered hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense arrangement of spines and a compact growth habit. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
