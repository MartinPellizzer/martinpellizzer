Mattiastrum polyanthum

Mattiastrum polyanthum, commonly known as the golden shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a climbing shrub that produces clusters of bright yellow, pendulous flowers throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and as a houseplant.
