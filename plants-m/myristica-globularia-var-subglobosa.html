Myristica globularia var. subglobosa

Myristica globularia var. subglobosa, commonly known as the globular nutmeg.

It is a tropical plant belonging to the Myristicaceae family, characterized by its compact, rounded growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers and develops unique, globular fruits that contain aromatic seeds. Its leaves are typically glossy and leathery, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is closely related to the spice-producing species Myristica fragrans, sharing similar botanical features.
