Muhlenbergia spica-venti

Muhlenbergia spica-venti, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silver-blue foliage and fluffy, feathery seed heads that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping. Its ornamental value comes from its elegant, arching growth habit and the way it adds texture and movement to landscapes.

Muhlenbergia spica-venti is also valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
