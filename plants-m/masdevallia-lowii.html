Masdevallia lowii

Masdevallia lowii, commonly known as the lowii masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapting to the humid, shaded environments it inhabits.

Masdevallia lowii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
