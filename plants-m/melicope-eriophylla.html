Melicope eriophylla

Melicope eriophylla, commonly known as the white-leaf melicope, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its smooth, light green to white leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers followed by dark purple to black berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Melicope eriophylla plays an important role in the ecological diversity of Hawaii's native plant communities.
