Marsdenia robusta

Marsdenia robusta, commonly known as the robust Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
