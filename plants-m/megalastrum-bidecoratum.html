Megalastrum bidecoratum

Megalastrum bidecoratum, commonly known as the ornate beautyflower, is a strikingly decorative plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features large, showy flowers that are typically a mix of white, pink, and purple, arranged in clusters that resemble intricate patterns. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant in gardens and greenhouses.
