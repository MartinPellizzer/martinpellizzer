Mesembryanthemum oligandrum

Mesembryanthemum oligandrum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
