Maxillaria funerea

Maxillaria funerea, commonly known as the funeral orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its pseudobulbs and fleshy roots. The plant produces dark green, strap-like leaves and bears small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple and brown. Its flowers typically bloom in the summer, and the plant is noted for its relatively easy care requirements.

Maxillaria funerea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique appearance.
