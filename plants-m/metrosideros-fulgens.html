Metrosideros fulgens

Metrosideros fulgens, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, providing a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are often dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, with a tendency to flake off in strips.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its dramatic floral display.
