Mutisia spinosa var. pulchella

Mutisia spinosa var. pulchella, commonly known as the purple mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking purple to pinkish flowers. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in dry, open habitats. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered with fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
