Malouetia pubescens var. glabra

Malouetia pubescens var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth malouetia, is a tropical shrub native to regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves, which are typically ovate to lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in terminal clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

This species is often valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in warm climates for landscaping purposes.
