Magnolia beccarii

Magnolia beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari magnolia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the islands of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, often with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a broad, rounded crown.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape specimen.
