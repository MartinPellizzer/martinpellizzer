Microstylis minutiflora

Microstylis minutiflora, commonly known as the minute-flowered microstylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tiny, clustered flowers that are typically less than 1 millimeter in diameter. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, inconspicuous fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
