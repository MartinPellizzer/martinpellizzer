Mimulus ringens var. ringens

Mimulus ringens var. ringens, commonly known as the water lentil, is a small aquatic plant native to North America.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds, ditches, and wetlands. The plant has slender, floating stems with small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in nutrient-rich waters.
