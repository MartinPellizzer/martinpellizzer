Melastoma cyanoides

Melastoma cyanoides, commonly known as blue mistflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, blue-violet flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Melastoma cyanoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
