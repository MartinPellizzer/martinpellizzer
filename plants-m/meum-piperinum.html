Meum piperinum

Meum piperinum, commonly known as black horehound, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs. The plant is characterized by its dark purple to black flowers and hairy, narrow leaves. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Meum piperinum is known for its strong, bitter scent and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
