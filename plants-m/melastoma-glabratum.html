Melastoma glabratum

Melastoma glabratum, commonly known as the smooth melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom during the rainy season. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Melastoma glabratum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands.
