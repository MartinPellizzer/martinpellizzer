Maurandella petrophila

Maurandella petrophila, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, and it is often found in rocky outcrops and mountainous regions.
