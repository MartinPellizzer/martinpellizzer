Medicago orbicularis subsp. castellana

Medicago orbicularis subsp.

castellana, commonly known as the Castellana globeflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe. It is characterized by its distinctive spherical flower heads, which are typically pink to purple in color.

The plant produces small, oval leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and limestone soils.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
