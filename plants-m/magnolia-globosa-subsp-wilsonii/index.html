Magnolia Globosa Subsp. Wilsonii

Magnolia globosa subsp. wilsonii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of southeastern China. This subspecies is known for its large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. It is closely related to other magnolia species but possesses unique characteristics that set it apart. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
