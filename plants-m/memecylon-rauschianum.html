Memecylon rauschianum

Memecylon rauschianum, commonly known as the Rausch's memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by elliptical berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
