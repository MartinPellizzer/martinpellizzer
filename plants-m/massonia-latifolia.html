Massonia latifolia

Massonia latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved massonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are typically oval or rhombic in shape and have a waxy texture.

Massonia latifolia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
