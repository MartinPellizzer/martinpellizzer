Microsteira axillaris

Microsteira axillaris, commonly known as the axillary microsteira, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats. The plant features slender, upright stems with small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and clustered in axillary inflorescences, usually in shades of white or pale yellow.

It is a hardy species that thrives in well-drained conditions and is relatively drought-tolerant.
