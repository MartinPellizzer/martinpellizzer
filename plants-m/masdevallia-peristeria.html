Masdevallia peristeria

Masdevallia peristeria, commonly known as the silver star orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and features lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces elegant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pale blue, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in shaded, humid environments with well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
