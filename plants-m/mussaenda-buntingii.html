Mussaenda buntingii

Mussaenda buntingii, commonly known as the African flame bush, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange-red bracts that surround clusters of small, white, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Mussaenda buntingii thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
