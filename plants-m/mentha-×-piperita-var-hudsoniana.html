Mentha × piperita var. hudsoniana

Mentha × piperita var. hudsoniana, commonly known as Hudson's mint, is a hybrid mint species native to North America.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly serrated leaves and strong menthol scent. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, pale blue or purple flowers in late summer.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
