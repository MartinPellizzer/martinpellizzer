Mesembryanthemum gladiatum

Mesembryanthemum gladiatum, commonly known as the "Cape tulip," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

The plant produces showy, daisy-like blooms in shades of pink, red, and yellow, often appearing during the summer months. Mesembryanthemum gladiatum is well adapted to arid environments, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
