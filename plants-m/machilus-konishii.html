Machilus konishii

Machilus konishii, commonly known as the Japanese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and fragrant, small flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or red in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Machilus konishii is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various climates.
