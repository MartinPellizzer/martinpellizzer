Microtis media

Microtis media, commonly known as the common microtis, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers. Microtis media is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in suitable climates.
