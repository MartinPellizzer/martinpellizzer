Melastoma exiguum

Melastoma exiguum, commonly known as the small melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma exiguum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
