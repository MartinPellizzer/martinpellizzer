Mesoglossum londesboroughianum

Mesoglossum londesboroughianum, commonly known as the Londesboroughianum orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the British Isles.

It is characterized by its unique floral structure, featuring a distinctive lip that plays a crucial role in pollination. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as woodland floors or rocky crevices. Its flowers bloom in late spring, producing a delicate, fragrant display that attracts specific pollinators.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
