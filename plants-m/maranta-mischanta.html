Maranta mischanta

Maranta mischanta, commonly known as the silver tinge arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage. The plant features strikingly colored leaves with a silver or pinkish tinge, which intensify in sunlight. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers indirect light.

Maranta mischanta is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant and unique leaf patterns.
