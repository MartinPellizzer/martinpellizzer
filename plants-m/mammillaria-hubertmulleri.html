Mammillaria hubertmulleri

Mammillaria hubertmulleri, commonly known as the Hubert Muller's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines and prominent areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
