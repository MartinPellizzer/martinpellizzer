Machilus nanchuanensis

Machilus nanchuanensis, commonly known as the Nanchuan mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within mountainous regions. Its fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.

Machilus nanchuanensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
