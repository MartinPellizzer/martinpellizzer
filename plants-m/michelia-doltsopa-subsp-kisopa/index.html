Michelia Doltsopa Subsp. Kisopa

Michelia doltsopa subsp. kisopa is a rare and lesser-known member of the Magnoliaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of southern China, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This subtropical evergreen shrub is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique plant species.
