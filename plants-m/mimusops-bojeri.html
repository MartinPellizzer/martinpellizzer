Mimusops bojeri

Mimusops bojeri, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain smooth, whitish seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in furniture making.
