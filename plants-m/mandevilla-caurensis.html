Mandevilla caurensis

Mandevilla caurensis, commonly known as the Cauca mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has thick, woody stems and heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Mandevilla caurensis is also valued for its ability to climb or trail, making it suitable for trellises and hanging baskets.
