Microsorum griseorhizoma

Microsorum griseorhizoma, commonly known as the gray-rooted sword fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green fronds that are often used in water gardens and terrariums. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, with thick, wiry rhizomes that help anchor it in moist environments. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Microsorum griseorhizoma thrives in shallow, still water and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ease of care.
