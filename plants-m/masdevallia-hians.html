Masdevallia hians

Masdevallia hians, commonly known as the "Twin-lobed Masdevallia," is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that often display a combination of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often forming a rosette shape.

Due to its limited distribution and specific environmental requirements, Masdevallia hians is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
