Mitostigma tomentosum

Mitostigma tomentosum, commonly known as the woolly mitostigma, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
