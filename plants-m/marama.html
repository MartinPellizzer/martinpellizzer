Marama

Marama, commonly known as *Kali tragus*, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its spiny, branched stems and small, greenish flowers. The plant produces small, round, edible seeds that are often used in local cuisines. Marama is adapted to arid environments, making it a resilient species in harsh climates.

Its growth pattern and physical features make it easily distinguishable from other similar plants in its native habitat.
