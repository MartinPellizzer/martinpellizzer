Monstera lenneana

Monstera lenneana, commonly known as the Lennie's monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, featuring distinctive fenestrations. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and support itself as it grows.

It is a popular houseplant due to its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.
