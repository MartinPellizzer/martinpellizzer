Malaisia scandens var. rolfei

Malaisia scandens var. rolfei, commonly known as the climbing orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems that climb using aerial roots. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a velvety texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
