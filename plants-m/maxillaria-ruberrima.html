Maxillaria ruberrima

Maxillaria ruberrima, commonly known as the fiery maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with contrasting dark markings. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive dry periods.

Maxillaria ruberrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
