Myosotis compressa

Myosotis compressa, commonly known as the narrowleaf forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers with five petals, typically arranged in clusters at the tips of its stems. The plant has lance-shaped, serrated leaves that are often hairy beneath, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and stream banks.

Myosotis compressa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming, dainty blossoms.
