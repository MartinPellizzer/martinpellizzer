Malache urens

Malache urens, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that are typically oval or heart-shaped. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate sunlight to flourish.

Malache urens is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
