Mimusops kerstingii

Mimusops kerstingii, commonly known as the kersting's glorybower, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy appearance.

Mimusops kerstingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to warm, sunny climates.
