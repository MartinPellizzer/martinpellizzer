Maranta longiscapa

Maranta longiscapa, commonly known as the long-stemmed arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage and unique growth habits. The plant features striking, often variegated leaves that can be broad and lance-shaped, with a waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta longiscapa is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
