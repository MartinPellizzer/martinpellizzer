Mandirola × naegelia var. picturata

Mandirola × naegelia var. picturata, commonly known as the painted mandirola, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking variegation in its foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with intricate patterns of green, white, and yellow. The plant is native to regions with temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. It produces small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white, adding to its ornamental value.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
