Mammillaria escobaria

Mammillaria escobaria, commonly known as the Escobaria mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative cactus in gardens and collections.
