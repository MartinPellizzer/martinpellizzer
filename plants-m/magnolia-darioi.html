Magnolia darioi

Magnolia darioi, commonly known as the Dario's magnolia, is a rare and striking species native to the mountainous regions of China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens due to its rarity and aesthetic beauty.
