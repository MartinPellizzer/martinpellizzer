Margaritaria discoidea f. glabra

Margaritaria discoidea f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth margarita plant, is a variant of the margarita plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves that are typically oval to elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This cultivar is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in indoor and outdoor settings.
