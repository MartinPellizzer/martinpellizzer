Mimetes splendidus

Mimetes splendidus, commonly known as the splendid mimetes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Mimetes splendidus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
