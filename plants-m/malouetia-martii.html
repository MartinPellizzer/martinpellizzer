Malouetia martii

Malouetia martii, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant produces clusters of white, fluffy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.

Malouetia martii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.
