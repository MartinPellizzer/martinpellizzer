Meeboldia variabilis

Meeboldia variabilis, commonly known as the variable meeboldia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and ovate, often with a slightly hairy surface.

Meeboldia variabilis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
