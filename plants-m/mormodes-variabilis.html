Mormodes variabilis

Mormodes variabilis, commonly known as the variable orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and waxy, adapted to retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Mormodes variabilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
