Medicago tornata f. inermis

Medicago tornata f. inermis, commonly known as the slender medic.

This plant is a variant of the common medick, characterized by its reduced leaf hairs and shorter stature. It is a low-growing perennial forage plant native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being oval to lanceolate in shape.

It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by elongated pods containing several seeds.
