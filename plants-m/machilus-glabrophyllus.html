Machilus glabrophyllus

Machilus glabrophyllus, commonly known as the smooth-leafed machilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, where it grows in temperate forest regions. The plant is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are typically smooth and glossy. Machilus glabrophyllus is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
