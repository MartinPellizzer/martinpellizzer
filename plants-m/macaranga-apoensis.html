Macaranga apoensis

Macaranga apoensis, commonly known as the white mangrove, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. This species is often found growing in coastal areas and is adapted to saline conditions, making it an important component of mangrove ecosystems.
