Myosotis × suzae

Myosotis × suzae, commonly known as the hybrid forget-me-not, is a flowering plant that results from the crossbreeding of Myosotis species.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and prefers moist, well-drained soil in partial to full shade. Its foliage consists of lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and slightly hairy.

Myosotis × suzae is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is native to Europe and parts of Asia.
