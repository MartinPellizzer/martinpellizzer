Mammillaria melanocentra subsp. linaresensis

Mammillaria melanocentra subsp.

linaresensis, commonly known as the Linares milk barrel cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem with numerous radial spines and a pale green to yellowish color.

The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a houseplant.
