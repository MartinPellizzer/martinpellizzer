Mosenthinia

Mosenthinia, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in the evening. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its leaves are covered in fine hairs that reflect sunlight, helping to reduce water loss.

Mosenthinia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and unique blooming habit.
