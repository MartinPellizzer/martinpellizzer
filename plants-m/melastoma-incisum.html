Melastoma incisum

Melastoma incisum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf melastoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate to lanceolate leaves with serrated margins. It produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink or white, which bloom during the rainy season.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
