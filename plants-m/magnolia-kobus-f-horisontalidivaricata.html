Magnolia kobus f. horisontalidivaricata

Magnolia kobus f. horisontalidivaricata, commonly known as the horizontalis variegata magnolia, is a unique cultivar characterized by its striking variegated foliage.

This plant is a hybrid of Magnolia kobus, featuring broad, lance-shaped leaves with a distinct yellow and white variegation pattern. The flowers are large, showy, and typically white or pale pink, appearing in early spring before the leaves fully emerge. It is a deciduous shrub or small tree, often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

The variegation makes it a popular choice for adding visual interest to landscapes.
