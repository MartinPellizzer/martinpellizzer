Macrolobium microcalyx var. allenii

Macrolobium microcalyx var. allenii, commonly known as the Allen's dwarf lily, is a rare and striking member of the Liliaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces elongated, cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in cool, moist environments, often found in shaded forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and unique morphology, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
