Medicago Suffruticosa Subsp. Maroccana

Medicago suffruticosa subsp. maroccana is a lesser-known member of the legume family native to North Africa. This shrub-like plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing remarkable resilience to drought. Its small, yellow flowers bloom in spring, adding a splash of color to rocky landscapes. Though not widely cultivated, it plays an important role in local ecosystems by preventing soil erosion. Researchers are increasingly studying its potential for sustainable agriculture in harsh climates.
