Mundulea ankazobeensis

Mundulea ankazobeensis, commonly known as the Madagascar acacia, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing resilience to drought conditions. It plays a significant role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various wildlife species.

Its unique botanical features make it an important subject of study in plant ecology and conservation.
