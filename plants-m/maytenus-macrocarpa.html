Maytenus macrocarpa

Maytenus macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited mayten, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Maytenus macrocarpa is valued for its hardy nature and potential medicinal properties.
