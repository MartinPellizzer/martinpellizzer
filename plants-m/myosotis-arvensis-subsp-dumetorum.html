Myosotis arvensis subsp. dumetorum

Myosotis arvensis subsp.

dumetorum, commonly known as the field forget-me-not, is a flowering plant species native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as meadows, woodlands, and stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are usually prostrate or ascending.
