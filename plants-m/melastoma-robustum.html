Melastoma robustum

Melastoma robustum, commonly known as the rough melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous small, black seeds.

Melastoma robustum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along stream banks.
