Mammillaria odieriana var. subcurvata

Mammillaria odieriana var. subcurvata, commonly known as the Curved Mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
