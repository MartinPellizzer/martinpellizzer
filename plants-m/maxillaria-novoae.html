Maxillaria novoae

Maxillaria novoae, commonly known as the New Guinea Maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of New Guinea.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its evergreen foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Maxillaria novoae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under suitable conditions.
