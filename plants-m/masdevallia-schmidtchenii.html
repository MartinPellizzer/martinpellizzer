Masdevallia schmidtchenii

Masdevallia schmidtchenii, commonly known as the Schmidtchen's masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and challenging species to cultivate.
