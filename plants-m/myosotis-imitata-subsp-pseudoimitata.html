Myosotis imitata subsp. pseudoimitata

Myosotis imitata subsp.

pseudoimitata, commonly known as the mimic forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly different leaf shape and flower structure compared to the typical *Myosotis imitata*. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and rocky areas.
