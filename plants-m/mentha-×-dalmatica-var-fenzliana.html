Mentha × dalmatica var. fenzliana

Mentha × dalmatica var. fenzliana, commonly known as Fenzli's mint, is a hybrid perennial herb native to the Balkan region.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly fuzzy leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is known for its strong, minty scent.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils.
