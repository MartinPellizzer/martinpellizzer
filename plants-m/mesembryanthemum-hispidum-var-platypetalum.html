Mesembryanthemum hispidum var. platypetalum

Mesembryanthemum hispidum var. platypetalum, commonly known as the woolly mesembryanthemum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive flat, rounded shape, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it an attractive addition to dryland landscapes.
