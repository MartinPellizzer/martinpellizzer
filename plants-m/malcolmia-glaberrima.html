Malcolmia glaberrima

Malcolmia glaberrima, commonly known as the pale morning glory, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its delicate, pale blue to white flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped, lobed leaves that are typically hairy on the underside. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Malcolmia glaberrima is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
