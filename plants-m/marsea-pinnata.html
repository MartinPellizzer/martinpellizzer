Marsea pinnata

Marsea pinnata, commonly known as the broadleaf marsea, is a perennial flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its large, pinnately compound leaves that can reach several feet in length. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Marsea pinnata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
