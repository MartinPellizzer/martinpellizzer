Medinilla amoena

Medinilla amoena, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms.

Medinilla amoena is also valued for its aromatic foliage and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
