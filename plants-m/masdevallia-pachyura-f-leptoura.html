Masdevallia pachyura f. leptoura

Masdevallia pachyura f. leptoura, commonly known as the slender-leaved masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves and compact, often clustered inflorescences. The flowers are typically small, with intricate patterns of purple and white, and they bloom during the cooler months. This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique foliage and delicate floral display.

It requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
