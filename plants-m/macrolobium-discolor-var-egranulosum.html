Macrolobium discolor var. egranulosum

Macrolobium discolor var. egranulosum, commonly known as the spotted macrolobium, is a tropical plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that are typically green with distinctive white or yellow spots. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular specimen for botanical gardens and collectors.
