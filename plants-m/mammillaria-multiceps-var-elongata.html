Mammillaria multiceps var. elongata

Mammillaria multiceps var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem that is often slightly ribbed, with a gray-green to bluish-green coloration. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is prized for its compact size and unique, elongated shape.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
