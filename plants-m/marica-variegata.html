Marica variegata

Marica variegata, commonly known as the variegated marica, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It features vibrant, multicolored leaves with shades of green, yellow, and white, creating a visually appealing display. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically in shades of pink or white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique foliage, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in horticultural settings.
