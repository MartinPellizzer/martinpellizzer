Magnolia denudata var. emarginata

Magnolia denudata var. emarginata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the saucer magnolia, is a deciduous flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both horticultural and ecological purposes.
