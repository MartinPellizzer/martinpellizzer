Murex burmanni

Murex burmanni, commonly known as the Burmann's sea snail, is a species of predatory sea snail.

It belongs to the family Muricidae, which includes many colorful and often spiny sea snails. The shell of Murex burmanni is typically dark purple or black with intricate, spiral patterns. It is found in tropical and subtropical marine environments, often in shallow coastal waters.

This species plays a role in marine ecosystems as both a predator and prey.
