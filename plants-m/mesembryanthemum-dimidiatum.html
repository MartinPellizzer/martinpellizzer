Mesembryanthemum dimidiatum

Mesembryanthemum dimidiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
