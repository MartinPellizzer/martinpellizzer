Muehlenbeckia complexa var. grandifolia

Muehlenbeckia complexa var. grandifolia, commonly known as the silver fern or coastal silver fern, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-green foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, pale green to yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in coastal environments and is often found growing on rocky substrates.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate salt and wind.
