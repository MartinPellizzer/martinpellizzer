Musa zaifui

Musa zaifui, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a species of banana native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically eaten raw or cooked. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued both for its culinary use and its ornamental appeal.
