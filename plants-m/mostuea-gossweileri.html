Mostuea gossweileri

Mostuea gossweileri, commonly known as the African iron plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers, which bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in arid regions.
