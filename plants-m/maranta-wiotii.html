Maranta wiotii

Maranta wiotii, commonly known as the Wioti arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver or white markings. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal.

Maranta wiotii is also valued for its edible tubers, which are used in traditional cuisines.
