Medinilla dolichophylla

Medinilla dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves that are elongated and lance-shaped. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Medinilla dolichophylla is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
