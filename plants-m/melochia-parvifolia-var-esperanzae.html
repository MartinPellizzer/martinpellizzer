Melochia parvifolia var. esperanzae

Melochia parvifolia var. esperanzae, commonly known as Esperanza's small-leaf melochia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for warm climates. Its rapid growth and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for landscaping.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some areas due to its aggressive spreading habit.
