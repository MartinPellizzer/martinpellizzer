Magnolia hookeri

Magnolia hookeri, commonly known as the Hooker's magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, glossy leaves and can grow up to 10 meters tall in its natural habitat. It is a member of the Magnoliaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
