Malaxis javesiae subsp. javesiae

Malaxis javesiae subsp.

javesiae, commonly known as the Javanese Malaxis, is a species of orchid native to Indonesia. It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its rhizomes and pseudobulbs.

The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, typically blooming during the rainy season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
