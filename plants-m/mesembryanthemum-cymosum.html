Mesembryanthemum cymosum

Mesembryanthemum cymosum, commonly known as the ice plant or coastal ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often found along coastal areas and rocky outcrops.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and coastal gardens.
