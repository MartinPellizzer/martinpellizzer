Mammillaria barbata var. morricalii

Mammillaria barbata var. morricalii, commonly known as the Morrical's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, which give it a distinctive, almost fuzzy appearance. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its slow growth and compact size.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
