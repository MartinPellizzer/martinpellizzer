Musa × sapientum var. cubensis

Musa × sapientum var. cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the genus Musa.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and typically yellow-orange colored fruit, which is rich in nutrients such as potassium and vitamin C. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, growing as a clump of pseudostems formed from the tightly packed leaves. It thrives in warm, tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The fruit is widely consumed globally and is also used in various culinary and industrial applications.
