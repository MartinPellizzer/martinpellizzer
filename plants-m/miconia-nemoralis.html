Miconia nemoralis

Miconia nemoralis, commonly known as the tropical beauty leaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Americas, particularly Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red or purple. It produces showy, clustered inflorescences with small, pink or white flowers.

Miconia nemoralis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking foliage and floral display.
