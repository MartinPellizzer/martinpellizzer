Micrechites tenuifolius

Micrechites tenuifolius, commonly known as the slender-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, daisy-like yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

The plant's botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
