Miscanthus sinensis var. longiaxis

Miscanthus sinensis var. longiaxis, commonly known as the narrowleaf Japanese feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, arching culms with long, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces feathery, panicle-like inflorescences that are often pink to reddish in hue, adding ornamental value. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its elegant appearance and is commonly used in landscaping and as a ornamental grass in gardens.
