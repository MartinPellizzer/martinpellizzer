Marathrum minutiflorum f. diversifolium

Marathrum minutiflorum f. diversifolium, commonly known as the variable-leaf marathrum, is a unique cultivar of the marathrum plant species.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and its distinctive variation in leaf morphology. The leaves exhibit a range of shapes and sizes, giving it a striking visual appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its botanical interest lies in its morphological diversity, which makes it a valuable addition to horticultural collections.
