Maxillaria crispiloba

Maxillaria crispiloba, commonly known as the crinkled maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically produces multiple inflorescences with flowers that have a distinctive crinkled appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria crispiloba is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
