Maxillaria acutipetala

Maxillaria acutipetala, commonly known as the sharp-petaled orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its blooms typically appear in spring and summer, and it thrives in humid, shady environments.

Maxillaria acutipetala is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
