Masdevallia elegans

Masdevallia elegans, commonly known as the elegant masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful watering to avoid root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and resilience.
