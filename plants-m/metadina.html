Metadina

Metadina, commonly known as the ghost flower, is a rare and enigmatic plant found in specific tropical regions.

It is characterized by its unique, translucent petals that appear to shimmer with an otherworldly glow. The plant's botanical structure includes a short stem with broad, waxy leaves that help it retain moisture in its arid habitat.
