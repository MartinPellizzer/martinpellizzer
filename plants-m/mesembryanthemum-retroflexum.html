Mesembryanthemum retroflexum

Mesembryanthemum retroflexum, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or ozymandias, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum retroflexum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
