Myristica lenta

Myristica lenta, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and aromatic bark. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fruiting bodies containing the valuable seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Its roots and leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is highly valued for its economic and cultural significance in global spice trade.
