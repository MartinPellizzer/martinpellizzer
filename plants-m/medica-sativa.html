Medica sativa

Medica sativa, commonly known as the hemp plant, is a member of the Cannabaceae family.

It is a tall annual plant that can grow up to 5 meters in height, with serrated leaves and greenish-white flowers. The plant contains various chemical compounds, including cannabinoids such as CBD and THC, which have been studied for their medicinal properties. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its versatility has made it an important crop in agriculture, medicine, and industry.
