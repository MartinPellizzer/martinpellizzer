Megacarpha pyriformis

Megacarpha pyriformis, commonly known as the oval spikerush, is a perennial wetland plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and other grass-like plants. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, typically growing in dense clusters at the base.
