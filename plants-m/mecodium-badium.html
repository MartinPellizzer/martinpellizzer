Mecodium badium

Mecodium badium, commonly known as the rock daisy, is a small, flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. The plant produces yellow flowers with a distinctive, flattened shape, and its leaves are usually covered in fine hairs. It is often found in coastal areas and is well adapted to arid conditions.
