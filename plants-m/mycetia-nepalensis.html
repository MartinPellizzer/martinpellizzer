Mycetia nepalensis

Mycetia nepalensis, commonly known as the Nepal mycetia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Fabaceae.

It is native to the Himalayan regions of Nepal, India, and Bhutan, typically growing in moist, forested areas. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release seeds, contributing to its role in ecological systems.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
