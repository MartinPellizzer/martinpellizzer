Maxillaria gymnochila

Maxillaria gymnochila, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, leathery leaf and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers with velvety petals and sepals. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom during the dry season. The plant thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
