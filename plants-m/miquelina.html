Miquelina

Miquelina, commonly known as the golden star, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form a basal rosette, and its growth habit is compact and upright. Miquelina thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and drought tolerance.
