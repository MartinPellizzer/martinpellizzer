Machaerium leiophyllum var. latifolium

Machaerium leiophyllum var. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf machaerium, is a tropical shrub native to regions of South America.

It features compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets that are often hairy on the underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
