Melpomene pennellii

Melpomene pennellii, commonly known as the red hibiscus, is a species of flowering plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes other hibiscus species and cotton. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are woody and often branched.
