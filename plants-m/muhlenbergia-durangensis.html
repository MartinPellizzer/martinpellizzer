Muhlenbergia durangensis

Muhlenbergia durangensis, commonly known as the Durango muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces inflorescences of fluffy, pinkish to reddish plumes that are visually striking. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to arid environments.
