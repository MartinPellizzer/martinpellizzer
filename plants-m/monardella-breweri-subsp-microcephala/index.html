Monardella Breweri Subsp. Microcephala

Monardella breweri subsp. microcephala is a rare and delicate wildflower native to the mountainous regions of California. This subspecies is known for its small, clustered blue flowers and its preference for moist, shaded habitats. It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and supporting biodiversity. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique plant for future generations.
