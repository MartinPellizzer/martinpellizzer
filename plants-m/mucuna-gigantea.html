Mucuna gigantea

Mucuna gigantea, commonly known as the giant urd, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, butterfly-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces elongated pods containing numerous elliptical seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Mucuna gigantea is known for its rapid growth and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural systems.

Its extensive root system also helps prevent soil erosion in degraded landscapes.
