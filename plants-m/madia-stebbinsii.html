Madia stebbinsii

Madia stebbinsii, commonly known as Stebbins' madia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows in open, dry habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.

Madia stebbinsii is known for its delicate appearance and is often found in areas with well-drained soils.
