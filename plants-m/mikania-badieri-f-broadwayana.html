Mikania badieri f. broadwayana

Mikania badieri f. broadwayana, commonly known as the broadwayana variant of Mikania badieri, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in humid, tropical environments and thrives in partial to full sunlight. It has a rapid growth rate and is known for its ability to spread quickly through its aerial roots.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive if not managed carefully.
