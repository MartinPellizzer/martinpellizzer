Melaleuca stenostachya

Melaleuca stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stamened tea tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and showy white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It produces small, woody fruits called capsules, which release seeds when mature.

Melaleuca stenostachya is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.
