Maranta veitchii

Maranta veitchii, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white. Maranta veitchii is a rhizomatous plant, meaning it spreads through underground stems, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
