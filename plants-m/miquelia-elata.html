Miquelia elata

Miquelia elata, commonly known as the Chinese mallow, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Miquelia elata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
