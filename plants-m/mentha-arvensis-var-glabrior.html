Mentha arvensis var. glabrior

Mentha arvensis var. glabrior, commonly known as narrow-leaf field mint, is a variant of the broad-leaf field mint.

It is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features slender, aromatic leaves that are typically narrower than those of the standard variety. It produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in late summer.

This variety is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine for its refreshing scent and potential health benefits.
