Medinilla cogniauxii var. amgustifolia

Medinilla cogniauxii var. amgustifolia, commonly known as the velvet tangerine medinilla, is a tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
