Microtatorchis carinata

Microtatorchis carinata, commonly known as the zigzag fern, is a unique fern species characterized by its intricate, zigzag-shaped fronds.

Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil. The plant exhibits a distinctive rhizomatous growth habit, with its stems forming a dense, low-growing mat. Its leaves are bi-lobed and have a serrated margin, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Microtatorchis carinata is valued in horticulture for its ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
