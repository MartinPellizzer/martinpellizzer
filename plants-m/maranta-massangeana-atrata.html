Maranta massangeana atrata

Maranta massangeana atrata, commonly known as the Black Maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that often feature intricate, silver-veined patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among collectors and indoor gardeners.
