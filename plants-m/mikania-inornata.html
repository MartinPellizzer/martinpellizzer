Mikania inornata

Mikania inornata, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small yellow flowers. Native to the Americas, this plant is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture.

The plant is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
