Mosiera flavicans subsp. pastelillensis

Mosiera flavicans subsp.

pastelillensis, commonly known as the Pastelilla flamebush, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in clusters along the branches.

The leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green appearance. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
