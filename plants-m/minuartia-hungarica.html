Minuartia hungarica

Minuartia hungarica, commonly known as the Hungarian rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant features basal leaves that are pinnately lobed and small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe, including Hungary, Romania, and parts of the Balkans.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is often found in alpine or subalpine regions, where it thrives in well-drained, sunny environments.
