Medinilla sumatrana

Medinilla sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran velvet tangerine, is a tropical flowering plant native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
