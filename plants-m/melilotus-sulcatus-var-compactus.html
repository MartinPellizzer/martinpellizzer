Melilotus sulcatus var. compactus

Melilotus sulcatus var. compactus, commonly known as the compact melilot, is a perennial legume native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, yellowish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces trifoliate leaves and has a fibrous root system that helps in soil stabilization. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential use in ecological restoration projects.
