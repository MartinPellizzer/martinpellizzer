Maximiliana

Maximiliana, commonly known as the Mexican beautyberry, is a shrub native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, bright purple berries that appear in the fall. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval or elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Maximiliana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique fruit display.
