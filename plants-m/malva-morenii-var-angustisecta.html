Malva morenii var. angustisecta

Malva morenii var. angustisecta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mallows, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are deeply lobed and narrow, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pinkish-purple flowers with five petals, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to gardens and ecological landscapes.
