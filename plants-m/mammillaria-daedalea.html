Mammillaria daedalea

Mammillaria daedalea, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a dense covering of white, woolly bristles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
