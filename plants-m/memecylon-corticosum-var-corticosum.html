Memecylon corticosum var. corticosum

Memecylon corticosum var. corticosum, commonly known as the rough-leaved memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its rough, often hairy leaves. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland forests. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or white, and it produces small, fleshy berries.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
