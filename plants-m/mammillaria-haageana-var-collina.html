Mammillaria haageana var. collina

Mammillaria haageana var. collina, commonly known as the Collina mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with dense, white to pale blue spines and small, pale green or yellowish flowers. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant. It requires full sun and well-draining soil to thrive.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
