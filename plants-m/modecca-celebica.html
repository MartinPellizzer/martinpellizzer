Modecca celebica

Modecca celebica, commonly known as the Celebes starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to the Indonesian island of Celebes.

It is characterized by its small, star-shaped fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. The plant has compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small white flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is often used in local cuisines and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes other tropical trees and shrubs.
