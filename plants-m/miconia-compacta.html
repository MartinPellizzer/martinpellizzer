Miconia compacta

Miconia compacta, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and striking, showy flowers that range from white to pink. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that contain numerous seeds. Miconia compacta is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

It is also known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets, making it an invasive species in some regions.
