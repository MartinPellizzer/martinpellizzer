Mussaenda procera

Mussaenda procera, commonly known as the African bridal wreath, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves with a smooth texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Mussaenda procera is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive flowers and compact growth habit.
