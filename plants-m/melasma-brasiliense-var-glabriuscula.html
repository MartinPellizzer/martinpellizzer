Melasma brasiliense var. glabriuscula

Melasma brasiliense var. glabriuscula, commonly known as the Brazilian melasma, is a rare and unique plant species native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant has a woody stem with a smooth, glabrous surface, contributing to its varietal name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered an endangered species.
