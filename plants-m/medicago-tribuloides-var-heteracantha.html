Medicago tribuloides var. heteracantha

Medicago tribuloides var. heteracantha, commonly known as goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant has a taproot system and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in dry grasslands.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable in ecological restoration projects.
