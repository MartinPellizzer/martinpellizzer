Mattiastrum dieterlei

Mattiastrum dieterlei, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a unique aquatic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that resemble ghostly apparitions, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshes and wetlands. Its rhizomes spread horizontally along the substrate, allowing it to colonize new areas efficiently.

Mattiastrum dieterlei is valued in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for small aquatic life.
