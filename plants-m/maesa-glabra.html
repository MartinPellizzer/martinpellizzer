Maesa glabra

Maesa glabra, commonly known as the smooth maesa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the rainy season.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
