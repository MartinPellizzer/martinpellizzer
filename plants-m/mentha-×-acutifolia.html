Mentha × acutifolia

Mentha × acutifolia, commonly known as narrow-leaf mint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, pointed leaves and strong aromatic scent. This plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, shaded environments. Mentha × acutifolia is valued for its use in herbal medicine and as a culinary herb.

Its flowering stems produce small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
