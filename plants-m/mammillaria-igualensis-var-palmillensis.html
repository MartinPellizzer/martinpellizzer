Mammillaria igualensis var. palmillensis

Mammillaria igualensis var. palmillensis, commonly known as the Iguales Palmillensis Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, white to pale blue spines and is characterized by its small, rounded areoles. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
