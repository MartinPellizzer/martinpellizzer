Mikania verschaffeltii

Mikania verschaffeltii, commonly known as the Brazilian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is known for its milky sap, which contains latex and is used in traditional medicine.

Mikania verschaffeltii thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
