Macrachaenium gracile

Macrachaenium gracile, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered in spikes, typically appearing in shades of green or brown.
