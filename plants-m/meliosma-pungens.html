Meliosma pungens

Meliosma pungens, commonly known as the spiny meliosma, is a perennial shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often serrated leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, round fruits.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
