Mercklinia rosea

Mercklinia rosea, commonly known as the pink orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

This plant is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep rose, often with intricate patterns on the petals. The leaves are broad and glossy, typically arranged in a rosette formation, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its beauty and rarity, Mercklinia rosea is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
