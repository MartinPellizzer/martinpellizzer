Masdevallia platycrater

Masdevallia platycrater, commonly known as the broad-ventured masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically features long, slender petals and a spurred lip, which are typical of many orchid species. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
