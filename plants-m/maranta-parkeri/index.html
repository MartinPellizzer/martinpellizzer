Maranta Parkeri

Prayer plant, scientifically known as Maranta parkeri, is a tropical herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of South America. It is named for its unique ability to fold its leaves shut at night, resembling prayerful hands. This striking plant features vibrant, colorful foliage that changes with the time of day. Maranta parkeri thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist soil to flourish. Its beauty and intriguing behavior make it a popular choice for indoor gardening enthusiasts.
