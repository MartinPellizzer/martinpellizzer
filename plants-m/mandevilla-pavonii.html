Mandevilla pavonii

Mandevilla pavonii, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens or as a houseplant.

Mandevilla pavonii is a member of the Apocynaceae family and is known for its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
