Melilotus heterophyllus

Melilotus heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable melilot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its variable leaf morphology, which can range from trifoliate to pinnate, depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Melilotus heterophyllus is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
