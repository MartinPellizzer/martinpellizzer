Mimusops elata

Mimusops elata, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and oval-shaped leaves. The plant is also appreciated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in various soil types.
