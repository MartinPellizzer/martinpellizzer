Maxillaria pentura

Maxillaria pentura, commonly known as the purple-tipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, greenish-yellow pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces striking violet to purple flowers with a contrasting yellow lip, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. Maxillaria pentura thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
