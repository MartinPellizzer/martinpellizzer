Marathrum elatum

Marathrum elatum, commonly known as the tall marathrum, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Native to southern Africa, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its foliage consists of long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

Marathrum elatum is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant appearance.
