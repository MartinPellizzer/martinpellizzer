Mycetia balansae

Mycetia balansae, commonly known as the African senna, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to arid conditions.

Mycetia balansae is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
