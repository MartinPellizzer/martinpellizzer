Mammillaria goodridgei var. bullardiana

Mammillaria goodridgei var. bullardiana, commonly known as the Bullard's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with prominent white or pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
