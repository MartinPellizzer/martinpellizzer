Magnolia compressa var. lanyuensis

Magnolia compressa var. lanyuensis, commonly known as the Lanyu magnolia, is a rare and endemic species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and glossy, and the bark is smooth with a reddish hue.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in its native habitat.
