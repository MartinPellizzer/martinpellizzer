Malanea ribesioides var. tomentosa

Malanea ribesioides var. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly malanea, is a shrub characterized by its dense, hairy foliage that gives it a soft, velvety appearance.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants, and is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, and its leaves are ovate with a serrated margin.
