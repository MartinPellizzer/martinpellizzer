Mariscus brachystachys

Mariscus brachystachys, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed mariscus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or purple in color, blooming in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slight waxy coating that helps it retain moisture.

Mariscus brachystachys is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and is well adapted to dry, sunny environments.
