Mallotus tenuifolius var. tenuifolius

Mallotus tenuifolius var. tenuifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mallot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, spiny capsules. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
