Masdevallia scandens

Masdevallia scandens, commonly known as the climbing masdevallia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. This epiphytic plant is characterized by its slender, vine-like stems that climb along tree trunks or branches. Its flowers are typically small, with intricate patterns and a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

The plant requires high humidity and indirect light to flourish, making it a popular choice for terrariums and greenhouses.
