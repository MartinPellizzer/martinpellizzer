Maranta fascinator

Maranta fascinator, commonly known as the silver sword plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves with a silvery underside. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Maranta fascinator is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
