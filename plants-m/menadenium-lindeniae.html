Menadenium lindeniae

Menadenium lindeniae, commonly known as the Lindenberg's spider plant, is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a compact, evergreen perennial that features rosettes of narrow, dark green leaves. The plant produces delicate, spider-like inflorescences with small, white flowers that bloom in late spring. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
