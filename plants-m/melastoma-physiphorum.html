Melastoma physiphorum

Melastoma physiphorum, commonly known as the broad-leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, berry-like fruits.

Melastoma physiphorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
