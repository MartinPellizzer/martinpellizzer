Masdevallia diversifolia

Masdevallia diversifolia, commonly known as the diverse-leaf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers and a compact growth habit. The plant typically produces multiple inflorescences with showy, spurred petals that vary in hue. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia diversifolia thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light and temperature conditions for optimal growth.
