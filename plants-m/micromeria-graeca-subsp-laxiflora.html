Micromeria graeca subsp. laxiflora

Micromeria graeca subsp.

laxiflora, commonly known as the Greek micromeria, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region. It is a small shrub characterized by its narrow, aromatic leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
