Michelia pulneyensis

Michelia pulneyensis, commonly known as the Chinese michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin layers.

Michelia pulneyensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
