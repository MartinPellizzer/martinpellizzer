Monsonia asiatica

Monsonia asiatica, commonly known as the Asian monsonia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and clustered, showy flowers. The plant features palmate leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, pink to red flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Monsonia asiatica is often appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
