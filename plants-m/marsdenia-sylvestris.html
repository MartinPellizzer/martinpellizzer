Marsdenia sylvestris

Marsdenia sylvestris, commonly known as the forest Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Asclepiadaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly found in countries like India, China, and Indonesia. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
