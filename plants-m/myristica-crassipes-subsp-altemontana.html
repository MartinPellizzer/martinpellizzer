Myristica crassipes subsp. altemontana

Myristica crassipes subsp.

altemontana, commonly known as the swamp nutmeg, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and fruits.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsules containing aromatic seeds. Its botanical features include smooth, dark green leaves and a woody stem.

The subspecies is notable for its potential medicinal properties and is often found in wetland habitats.
