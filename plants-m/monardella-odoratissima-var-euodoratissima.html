Monardella odoratissima var. euodoratissima

Monardella odoratissima var. euodoratissima, commonly known as the sweet peppergrass, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its small, tubular flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom in late summer.

The variety euodoratissima is distinguished by its stronger fragrance and more robust growth compared to the typical form.
