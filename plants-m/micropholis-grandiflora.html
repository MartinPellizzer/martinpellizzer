Micropholis grandiflora

Micropholis grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered micropholis, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually simple and alternate, with a glossy surface.

Micropholis grandiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.
