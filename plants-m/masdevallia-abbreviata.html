Masdevallia abbreviata

Masdevallia abbreviata, commonly known as the short-stemmed masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and slender, often hairy stems. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
