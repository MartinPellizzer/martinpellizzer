Magnolia macrophylla var. dealbata

Magnolia macrophylla var. dealbata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the evergreen magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pale pink, often with a yellow center. The leaves are broad, glossy, and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the blooms. This variety is noted for its cold hardiness and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
