Masdevallia minuta

Masdevallia minuta, commonly known as the miniature masdevallia, is a small orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact size, typically reaching only a few centimeters in height, and its delicate, intricate floral structure. The plant produces small, usually white or pale pink flowers that are clustered along the stem, often resembling miniature versions of larger orchid blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
