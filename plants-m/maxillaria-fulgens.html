Maxillaria fulgens

Maxillaria fulgens, commonly known as the fiery orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange and yellow flowers with a distinctive dark red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria fulgens is prized for its vibrant blooms and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
