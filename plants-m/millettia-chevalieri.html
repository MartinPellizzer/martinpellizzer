Millettia chevalieri

Millettia chevalieri, commonly known as the chevalier's millettia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind or animals.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its ecological role and potential medicinal properties.
