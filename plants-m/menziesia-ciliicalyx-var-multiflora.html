Menziesia ciliicalyx var. multiflora

Menziesia ciliicalyx var. multiflora, commonly known as the multiflora menziesia, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and dark green, elliptical leaves with finely toothed margins. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers in late spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
