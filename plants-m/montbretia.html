Montbretia

Montbretia, commonly known as the "fire flower," is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the genus Gladiolus and is characterized by its vibrant, sword-shaped leaves and showy, funnel-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 feet and produces clusters of brightly colored blooms in shades of red, orange, yellow, and pink. Montbretia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.

Its botanical features make it both ornamental and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
