Mammillaria macromeris

Mammillaria macromeris, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and prominent, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
