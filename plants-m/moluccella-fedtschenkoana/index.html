Moluccella Fedtschenkoana

Moluccella bellflower, scientifically known as Moluccella fedtschenkoana, is a rare and striking plant native to the high altitudes of the Russian Far East. With its delicate, bell-shaped flowers and vibrant coloration, it stands out among the alpine flora of its habitat. This species is particularly notable for its unique morphology and ecological significance in its native environment. Due to its limited distribution and fragile ecosystem, it faces increasing threats from climate change and habitat loss. Conservation efforts are essential to preserve this botanical gem for future generations.
