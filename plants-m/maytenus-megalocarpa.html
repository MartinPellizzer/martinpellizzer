Maytenus megalocarpa

Maytenus megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant mayten, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

Maytenus megalocarpa is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in construction and crafting.
