Mammillaria hemisphaerica

Mammillaria hemisphaerica, commonly known as the globe cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spherical cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 centimeters and features a pale green or blue-gray stem. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in early spring.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
