Macroselinum

Macroselinum, commonly known as the golden rain tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant belongs to the family Fabaceae and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated pods and can be dispersed by wind.

The tree is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
