Medicago liaosiensis

Medicago liaosiensis, commonly known as the Liaosi alfalfa, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, typical of the Fabaceae family. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a valuable forage species. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Its seeds are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
