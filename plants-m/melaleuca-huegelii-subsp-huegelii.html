Melaleuca huegelii subsp. huegelii

Melaleuca huegelii subsp.

huegelii, commonly known as the Narrow-leaf Paperbark, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a whitish bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces small, bottle-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
