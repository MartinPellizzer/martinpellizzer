Mikania attenuata

Mikania attenuata, commonly known as creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Mikania attenuata is also known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats.
