Muhlenbergia geminiflora

Muhlenbergia geminiflora, commonly known as the slender muhlenbergia, is a species of grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and compact growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, preferring drier conditions.

Muhlenbergia geminiflora is often used in native plant landscapes for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
