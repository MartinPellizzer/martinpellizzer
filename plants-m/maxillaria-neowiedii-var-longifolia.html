Maxillaria neowiedii var. longifolia

Maxillaria neowiedii var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features slender, upright pseudobulbs that bear broad, leathery leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellowish throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
