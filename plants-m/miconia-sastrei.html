Miconia sastrei

Miconia sastrei, commonly known as the Sastre's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible but often have a bland taste. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
