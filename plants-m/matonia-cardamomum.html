Matonia cardamomum

Matonia cardamomum, commonly known as the fragrant evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, clustered white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's unique scent has made it a valuable resource in both culinary and aromatic applications.
