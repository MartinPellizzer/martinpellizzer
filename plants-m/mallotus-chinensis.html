Mallotus chinensis

Mallotus chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hollies, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant features alternate, leathery, oval-shaped leaves with a glossy green surface. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by spherical, woody fruits.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
