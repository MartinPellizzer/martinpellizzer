Maxillaria stenostele

Maxillaria stenostele, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of fragrant, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces light green to yellowish-green leaves that are somewhat fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern. Its flowers, which bloom in spring or early summer, come in a variety of colors including white, pink, and purple.

Maxillaria stenostele is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
