Microstylis parvula

Microstylis parvula, commonly known as the small-styled grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, with a short, narrow inflorescence.
