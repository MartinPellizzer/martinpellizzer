Maharanga intricata

Maharanga intricata, commonly known as the intricate maharanga, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its complex, branched structure and vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds enclosed in a fleshy pulp. Its leaves are typically broad and slightly serrated, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Maharanga intricata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
