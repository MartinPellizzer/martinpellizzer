Mentzelia pinnatifida

Mentzelia pinnatifida, commonly known as the desert bluebells, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant has opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.
