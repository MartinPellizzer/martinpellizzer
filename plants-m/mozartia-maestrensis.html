Mozartia maestrensis

Mozartia maestrensis, commonly known as the Maestrense grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Due to its ornamental appeal and resilience, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
