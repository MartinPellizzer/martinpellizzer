Miconia quadrangularis f. minor

Miconia quadrangularis f. minor, commonly known as the dwarf miconia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique fruit.

However, it can be invasive in certain environments if not managed properly.
