Malus elzholzii

Malus elzholzii, commonly known as the Elzholzii apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of central Asia, particularly in the Tian Shan and Pamir Mountains. The plant is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white flowers in early spring. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a smooth margin and a dark green color.

The fruit, though edible, is small and not widely cultivated for commercial purposes.
