Mutisia weberbaueri

Mutisia weberbaueri, commonly known as the Weberbauer's mutisia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads and distinctive foliage. The plant typically grows as a shrub, featuring woody stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small and clustered, often displaying a pale yellow to white coloration.
