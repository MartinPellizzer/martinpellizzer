Microseris intermedia

Microseris intermedia, commonly known as the intermediate marsh cinquefoil, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes, meadows, and stream banks. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow and arranged in clusters, blooming in early spring.

Microseris intermedia is characterized by its fibrous root system and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
