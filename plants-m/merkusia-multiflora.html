Merkusia multiflora

Merkusia multiflora, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, elongated fruits called achenes, which are dispersed by wind.

Merkusia multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
