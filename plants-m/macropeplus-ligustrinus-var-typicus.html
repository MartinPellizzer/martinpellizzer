Macropeplus ligustrinus var. typicus

Macropeplus ligustrinus var. typicus, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically broad and elongated. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in low-light conditions, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It produces small, inconspicuous white flowers, though it is primarily cultivated for its foliage.
