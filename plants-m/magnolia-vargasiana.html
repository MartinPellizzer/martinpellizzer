Magnolia vargasiana

Magnolia vargasiana, commonly known as the Vargas magnolia, is a species native to the Philippines.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a smooth margin. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and landscapes.
