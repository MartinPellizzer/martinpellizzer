Mandevilla convolvulacea

Mandevilla convolvulacea, commonly known as the flame vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant thrives in warm climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth. It produces long, trailing stems that can be trained to grow along trellises or walls.

Mandevilla convolvulacea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
