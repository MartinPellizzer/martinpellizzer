Mallotus glomerulatus

Mallotus glomerulatus, commonly known as the clustered mallot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, elliptical leaflets arranged in a pinnate pattern. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in shades of yellow or green.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that splits open to release seeds.
