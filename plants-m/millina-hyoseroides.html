Millina hyoseroides

Millina hyoseroides, commonly known as the Indian nettle, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes many stinging plants. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, greenish flowers in clusters. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
