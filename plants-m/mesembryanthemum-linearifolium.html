Mesembryanthemum linearifolium

Mesembryanthemum linearifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
