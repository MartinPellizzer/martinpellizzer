Mesembryanthemum brevistaminum

Mesembryanthemum brevistaminum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the morning. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves. It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its unique adaptations to drought and high sunlight exposure.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
