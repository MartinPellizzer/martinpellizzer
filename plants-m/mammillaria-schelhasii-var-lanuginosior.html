Mammillaria schelhasii var. lanuginosior

Mammillaria schelhasii var. lanuginosior, commonly known as the woolly mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem covered in dense, white to pale blue woolly hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
