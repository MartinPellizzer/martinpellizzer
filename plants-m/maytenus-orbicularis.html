Maytenus orbicularis

Maytenus orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf mayten, is a small evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries that are edible for some bird species. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Maytenus orbicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
