Melastoma astrolasion

Melastoma astrolasion, commonly known as the star-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Melastoma astrolasion is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
