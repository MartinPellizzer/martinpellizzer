Melastoma oliganthum

Melastoma oliganthum, commonly known as the small-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with slightly hairy surfaces. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, winged seeds.

Melastoma oliganthum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and stream sides.
