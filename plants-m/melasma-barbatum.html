Melasma barbatum

Melasma barbatum, commonly known as the beard lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive appearance with a leathery, greenish-black upper surface and a lighter, powdery lower surface. The plant forms symbiotic relationships with algae and cyanobacteria, which contribute to its nutrient acquisition. It is often found on bark, stones, and soil in temperate regions.

Its slow growth and ability to survive in harsh conditions make it an indicator species for environmental health.
