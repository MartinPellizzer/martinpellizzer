Mycetia acuminata

Mycetia acuminata, commonly known as the slender mycetia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Leguminosae.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes, which are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. Mycetia acuminata is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
