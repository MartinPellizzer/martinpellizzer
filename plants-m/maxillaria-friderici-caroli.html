Maxillaria friderici-caroli

Maxillaria friderici-caroli, commonly known as the royal orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. It blooms during the summer months and prefers warm, humid conditions.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
