Macroclinium saraca-taquerense

Macroclinium saraca-taquerense, commonly known as the Taquerense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, moist environments. It features long, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, colorful blooms.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
