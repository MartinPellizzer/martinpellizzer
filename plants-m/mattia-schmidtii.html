Mattia schmidtii

Mattia schmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidts' mat, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and hairy foliage. Mattia schmidtii is well adapted to harsh alpine environments, thriving in poor, rocky soils and cold climates.
