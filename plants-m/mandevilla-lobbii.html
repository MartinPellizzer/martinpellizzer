Mandevilla lobbii

Mandevilla lobbii, commonly known as the Madagascar mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a vigorous climber with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla lobbii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
