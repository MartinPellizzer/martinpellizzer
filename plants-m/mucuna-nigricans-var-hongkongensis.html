Mucuna nigricans var. hongkongensis

Mucuna nigricans var. hongkongensis, commonly known as Hong Kong velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces dense, velvety pods containing lens-shaped seeds. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often used in traditional medicine and as a forage crop.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
