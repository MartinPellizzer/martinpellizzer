Maerua insignis

Maerua insignis, commonly known as the red sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season. Its leaves are usually small and fleshy, helping it conserve water in dry conditions.

Maerua insignis is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
