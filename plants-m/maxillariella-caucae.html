Maxillariella caucae

Maxillariella caucae, commonly known as the Cauca Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, brightly colored blooms in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillariella caucae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
