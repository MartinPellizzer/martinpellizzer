Meistera ghatica

Meistera ghatica, commonly known as the golden meistera, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Meistera ghatica is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
