Muhlenbergia metcalfei

Muhlenbergia metcalfei, commonly known as the silverhair grass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. This plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established. It produces fluffy, pinkish-white seed heads in the fall, adding ornamental value to gardens.

Muhlenbergia metcalfei is often used in native plant gardens for its low maintenance and ecological benefits.
