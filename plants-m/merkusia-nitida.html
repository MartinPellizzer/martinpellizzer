Merkusia nitida

Merkusia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf merkusia, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its finely textured, silvery-gray leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers clustered in loose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Merkusia nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
