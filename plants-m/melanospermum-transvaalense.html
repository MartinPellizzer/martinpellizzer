Melanospermum transvaalense

Melanospermum transvaalense, commonly known as the Transvaal melon or yellow starbursts, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant produces small, yellow, star-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its fruit is a small, round, yellowish capsule that contains several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant flowers.
