Myristica subcordata var. subcordata

Myristica subcordata var. subcordata, commonly known as the lesser nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown seeds that resemble nutmeg in appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits containing the aromatic seeds. The leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical with a smooth margin.

It is valued for its seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
