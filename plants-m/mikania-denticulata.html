Mikania denticulata

Mikania denticulata, commonly known as the toothed morning glory, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in moist, sunny habitats such as forests and stream banks.

Mikania denticulata is also noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage.
