Miltonia spectabilis var. bicolor

Miltonia spectabilis var. bicolor, commonly known as the bicolor orchid, is a hybrid orchid that exhibits a striking color contrast between its petals and sepals.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are typically large and showy, with a unique bicolor pattern that enhances its ornamental value.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a potted plant.
