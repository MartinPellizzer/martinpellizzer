Malaxis lata

Malaxis lata, commonly known as the broad-leafed malaxis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stems and broad, lanceolate leaves. Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a raceme, which are often overlooked due to their subtle appearance.

Its botanical features make it a unique and intriguing species within the orchid family.
