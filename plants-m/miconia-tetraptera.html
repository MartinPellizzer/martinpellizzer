Miconia tetraptera

Miconia tetraptera, commonly known as the four-winged miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is considered a weed in some areas.
