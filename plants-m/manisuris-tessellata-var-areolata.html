Manisuris tessellata var. areolata

Manisuris tessellata var. areolata, commonly known as the spotted leaf fern, is a unique variant of the tessellata species within the genus Manisuris.

It is characterized by its distinctive, finely divided leaves that form a dense, carpet-like foliage. The plant exhibits a compact growth habit, making it suitable for small spaces such as terrariums or hanging baskets. Its leaves are often adorned with small, raised dots or spots, giving it a textured appearance.

This variety thrives in humid environments with indirect light, requiring consistent moisture and well-draining soil.
