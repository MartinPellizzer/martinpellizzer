Medicago nigra var. pentacycla

Medicago nigra var. pentacycla, commonly known as black medic, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, dark purple to black flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets and a robust root system that helps it thrive in various soil conditions. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in fields.

Black medic is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it a valuable plant for soil enrichment.
