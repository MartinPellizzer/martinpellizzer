Myristica chrysophylla var. entrecasteauxensis

Myristica chrysophylla var. entrecasteauxensis, commonly known as the golden leaf myristica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant bark and leaves. The plant is notable for its distinctive golden-yellow leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
