Mecranium puberulum

Mecranium puberulum, commonly known as the velvetleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, purple flowers. The plant belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is known for its unique floral structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value has made it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
