Mammillaria gigantothele

Mammillaria gigantothele, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. Its roots are adapted to absorb water efficiently in arid environments.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts of cacti.
