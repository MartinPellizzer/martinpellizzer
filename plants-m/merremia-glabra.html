Merremia glabra

Merremia glabra, commonly known as the smooth merremia, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
