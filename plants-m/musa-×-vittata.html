Musa × vittata

Musa × vittata, commonly known as the banana hybrid or ornamental banana, is a hybrid species within the Musa genus.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant typically produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that develop into edible bananas. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

Musa × vittata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of fruit in tropical regions.
