Melhania damarana

Melhania damarana, commonly known as the Damaran melon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, pumpkins, and gourds. The plant produces large, orange-colored fruits that are edible and have a sweet, nutty flavor. Its vines are vigorous climbers, often requiring support for growth.
