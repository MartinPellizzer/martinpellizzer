Mostuea rubrinervis

Mostuea rubrinervis, commonly known as the red-veined banana.

It is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa. The plant features large, broad leaves with striking red veins contrasting against its green foliage. It produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer months.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant leaf coloration.
