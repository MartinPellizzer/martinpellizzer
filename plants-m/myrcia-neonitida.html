Myrcia neonitida

Myrcia neonitida, commonly known as the pink myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy pink to red flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, shaded areas.

Myrcia neonitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
