Melastoma micranthum

Melastoma micranthum, commonly known as the small-flowered melastoma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that are often fragrant. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma micranthum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
