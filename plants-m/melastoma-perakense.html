Melastoma perakense

Melastoma perakense, commonly known as the Perak Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and along riverbanks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

Melastoma perakense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
