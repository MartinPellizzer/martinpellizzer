Microlicia blanchetiana

Microlicia blanchetiana, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a herbaceous plant with a short, creeping stem and produces small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is adapted to the humid climate of its native habitat. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months.

Microlicia blanchetiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
