Minuartia hybrida subsp. conferta

Minuartia hybrida subsp.

conferta, commonly known as the dense sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arctic and alpine regions. It typically thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, and its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and showy blooms.

This subspecies is known for its resilience and ability to colonize disturbed or barren landscapes.
