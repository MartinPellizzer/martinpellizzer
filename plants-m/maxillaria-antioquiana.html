Maxillaria antioquiana

Maxillaria antioquiana, commonly known as the Antioquian orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with violet to purple petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria antioquiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
