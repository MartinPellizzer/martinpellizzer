Moeroris tanzaniana

Moeroris tanzaniana, commonly known as the Tanzanian orchid, is a flowering plant native to Tanzania.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, while its flowers come in a range of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Moeroris tanzaniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and plays a role in the local biodiversity of its habitat.
