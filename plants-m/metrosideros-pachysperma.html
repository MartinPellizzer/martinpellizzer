Metrosideros pachysperma

Metrosideros pachysperma, commonly known as the Tahitian Christmas tree, is a flowering plant native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds.
