Macrolobium limbatum var. fanshawei

Macrolobium limbatum var. fanshawei, commonly known as the Fan Shawei fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, deeply lobed fronds.

The leaves are typically bipinnate, with a feathery appearance that gives the plant a lush, ornamental look. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in the undergrowth of tropical rainforests. The plant's botanical structure includes a rhizomatous root system that helps anchor it in moist soil.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical plant collections.
