Malus baccata f. columnaris

Malus baccata f. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar crabapple, is a compact, upright shrub or small tree native to China.

It features narrow, columnar growth habit with dense, dark green foliage that provides year-round visual interest. The plant produces small, showy white or pink flowers in spring, followed by edible, red berries in autumn. It is well-suited for small gardens or as a hedge due to its compact size and ornamental value.

The variety is valued for its adaptability to various soil types and resistance to many common pests and diseases.
