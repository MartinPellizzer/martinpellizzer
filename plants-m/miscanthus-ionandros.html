Miscanthus ionandros

Miscanthus ionandros, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of climates. Its ornamental value is enhanced by its graceful appearance and ability to attract wildlife.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
