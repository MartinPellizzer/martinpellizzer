Mallotus tenuifolius var. floccosus

Mallotus tenuifolius var. floccosus, commonly known as the silky mallot, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers followed by woody, spiky fruits. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
