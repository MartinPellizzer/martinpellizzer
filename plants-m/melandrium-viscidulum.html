Melandrium viscidulum

Melandrium viscidulum, commonly known as sticky catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

Its leaves are covered in fine, sticky hairs, which give it its common name and help deter herbivores.

Melandrium viscidulum is also known for its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a versatile species in various habitats.
