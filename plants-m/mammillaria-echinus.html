Mammillaria echinus

Mammillaria echinus, commonly known as the spiny globular cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny, globular shape and is typically found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has a short, thick stem with areoles that bear both spines and flowers. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria echinus is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
