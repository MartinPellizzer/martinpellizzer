Menispermum chinense

Menispermum chinense, commonly known as Chinese arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its climbing habit and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy yellow flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. Its roots are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its medicinal properties.
