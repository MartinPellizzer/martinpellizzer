Melia azedarach var. umbraculifera

Melia azedarach var. umbraculifera, commonly known as the Chinese sumac, is a deciduous tree native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often arranged in a feather-like pattern, and its distinctive, showy clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces red, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds, and it is known for its rapid growth and adaptability to various soil types.
