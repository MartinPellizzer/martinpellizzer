Megastachya spectabilis

Megastachya spectabilis, commonly known as the showy goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy yellow flower clusters that bloom in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Megastachya spectabilis thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
