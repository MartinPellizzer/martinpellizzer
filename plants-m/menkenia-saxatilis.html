Menkenia saxatilis

Menkenia saxatilis, commonly known as the rock menkenia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its small, evergreen leaves and showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its unique morphology and ability to thrive in harsh environments make it an interesting subject for botanical study.

Menkenia saxatilis is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
