Mandevilla barretoi

Mandevilla barretoi, commonly known as the golden mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous, evergreen shrub that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features thick, waxy leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant in gardens and as a houseplant.

Mandevilla barretoi is well-suited for trellises or hanging baskets due to its climbing growth habit.
