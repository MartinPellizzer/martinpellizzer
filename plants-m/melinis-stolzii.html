Melinis stolzii

Melinis stolzii, commonly known as the stolzii grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems that often form dense clumps. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green coloration.

Melinis stolzii is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a hardy plant in regions with low rainfall.
