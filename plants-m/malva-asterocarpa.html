Malva asterocarpa

Malva asterocarpa, commonly known as the star-fruit malva, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruit is a distinctive star-shaped capsule, which opens to release seeds.

The leaves are palmately lobed and often covered in fine hairs.
