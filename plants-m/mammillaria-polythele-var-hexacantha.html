Mammillaria polythele var. hexacantha

Mammillaria polythele var. hexacantha, commonly known as the six-armed mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with radial spines that are typically white to yellow in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
