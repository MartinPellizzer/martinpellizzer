Melocactus martialis

Melocactus martialis, commonly known as the golden melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is often covered with a dense layer of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
