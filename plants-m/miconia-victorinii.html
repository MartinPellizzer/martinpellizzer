Miconia victorinii

Miconia victorinii, commonly known as the Victorinii Miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
