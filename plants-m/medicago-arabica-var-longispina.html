Medicago arabica var. longispina

Medicago arabica var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, elliptical pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used traditionally for its purported health benefits.
