Muehlenbeckia chilensis

Muehlenbeckia chilensis, commonly known as Chilean muhlenbeckia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Chile.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and clusters of small, showy flowers.
