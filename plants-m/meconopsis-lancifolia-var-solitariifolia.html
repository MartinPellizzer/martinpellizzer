Meconopsis lancifolia var. solitariifolia

Meconopsis lancifolia var. solitariifolia, commonly known as the solitary leaf meconopsis, is a rare and striking alpine plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in rocky, high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are usually solitary, giving the plant its common name, and it is often found in small colonies.
