Mimusops occidentalis

Mimusops occidentalis, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring, creating a striking visual display. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and oval-shaped with a smooth texture.

The fruit is a spherical capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
