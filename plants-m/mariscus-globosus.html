Mariscus globosus

Mariscus globosus, commonly known as the globe thistle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiky blue or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy perennial suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
