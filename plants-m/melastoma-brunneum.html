Melastoma brunneum

Melastoma brunneum, commonly known as the brown melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Melastoma brunneum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
