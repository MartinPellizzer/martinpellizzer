Medicago tornata subsp. obscura

Medicago tornata subsp.

obscura, commonly known as the dark medic, is a perennial legume native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is characterized by its dark green, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer.

The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in disturbed soils and arid conditions. It is often found in grasslands, wastelands, and along roadsides.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and ecological restoration.
