Milnea edulis

Milnea edulis, commonly known as the sweet milnea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple and white petals, which bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant produces edible fruits that are small, round, and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often used in traditional herbal remedies.

Milnea edulis thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight to flourish.
