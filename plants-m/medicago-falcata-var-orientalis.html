Medicago falcata var. orientalis

Medicago falcata var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with a distinctive heart-shaped leaflet and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps it thrive in arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
