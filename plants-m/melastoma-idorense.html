Melastoma idorense

Melastoma idorense, commonly known as the Idorenses Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that have a rough texture. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.

Melastoma idorense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
