Michelia uniflora

Michelia uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The fruit is a small, smooth, yellowish drupe that ripens in the fall.
