Mimusops longiciliata

Mimusops longiciliata, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to regions across Africa.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often used in traditional medicine.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is commonly found in coastal and lowland areas.
