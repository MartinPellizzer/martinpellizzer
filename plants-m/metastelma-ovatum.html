Metastelma ovatum

Metastelma ovatum, commonly known as the golden bell vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, slender stems and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Metastelma ovatum is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
