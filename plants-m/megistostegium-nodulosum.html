Megistostegium nodulosum

Megistostegium nodulosum, commonly known as the nodular stemplant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its erect, hairy stems and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine pubescence.

It plays a role in stabilizing dunes and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
