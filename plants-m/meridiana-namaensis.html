Meridiana namaensis

Meridiana namaensis, commonly known as the Namib Desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the Namib Desert in Namibia.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water in its arid environment. The plant produces striking white or pale pink flowers during the brief rainy season. It has a shallow root system adapted to quickly absorb moisture from infrequent rains.

Due to its unique adaptations, Meridiana namaensis is a symbol of resilience in extreme desert conditions.
