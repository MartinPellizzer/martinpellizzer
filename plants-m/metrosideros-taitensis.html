Metrosideros taitensis

Metrosideros taitensis, commonly known as the Taiti flame tree, is a small evergreen tree native to the island of Taiti in the Cook Islands.

It is characterized by its distinctive bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often giving the tree a fiery appearance. The leaves are lance-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens and parks.
