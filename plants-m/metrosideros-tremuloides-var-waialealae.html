Metrosideros tremuloides var. waialealae

Metrosideros tremuloides var. waialealae, commonly known as the Haleakalā silversword, is a unique plant endemic to the island of Maui in Hawaii.

It is a member of the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its silvery, woolly leaves and striking red flowers. The plant thrives in the high-altitude, volcanic soils of the Haleakalā volcano. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this distinctive botanical species from threats such as habitat loss and invasive species.
