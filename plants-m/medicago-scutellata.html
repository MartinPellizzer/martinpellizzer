Medicago scutellata

Medicago scutellata, commonly known as goat's head, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is an annual or biennial herb that grows up to 60 cm tall, featuring trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, spherical pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Medicago scutellata is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
