Microlonchus cichoraceus

Microlonchus cichoraceus, commonly known as the slenderleaf lettuce, is a member of the sunflower family (Asteraceae).

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its upright stems and alternate, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in a composite head, typical of the Asteraceae family. It is native to regions of North America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for ecological studies and potential horticultural use.
