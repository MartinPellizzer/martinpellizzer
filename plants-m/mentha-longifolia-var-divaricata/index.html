Mentha Longifolia Var. Divaricata

Mentha longifolia var. divaricata is a unique variant of the mint family known for its distinctive appearance and aromatic qualities. This plant features slender, often twisted stems and lance-shaped leaves that give it a striking visual appeal. While it shares some traits with common mint, it has a more delicate and refined scent. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often used in herbal teas and traditional remedies. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and fragrance.
