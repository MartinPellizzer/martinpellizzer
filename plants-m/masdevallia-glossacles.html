Masdevallia glossacles

Masdevallia glossacles, commonly known as the velvet tresses orchid, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves and intricate, often fragrant flower spikes. The plant typically produces small, colorful flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom sequentially along the inflorescence. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
