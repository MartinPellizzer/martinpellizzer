Myricaria elegans

Myricaria elegans, commonly known as the elegant myricaria, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Europe and Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its silver-gray foliage and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. Its leaves are narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Myricaria elegans is also known for its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions, making it a hardy and resilient species.
