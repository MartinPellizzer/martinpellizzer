Michelia pealiana

Michelia pealiana, commonly known as the pealiana star magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Michelia pealiana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant floral display.
