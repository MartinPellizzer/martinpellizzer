Monelasmum dupuisii

Monelasmum dupuisii, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
