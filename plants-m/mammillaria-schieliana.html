Mammillaria schieliana

Mammillaria schieliana, commonly known as the Schiel's pincushion cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
