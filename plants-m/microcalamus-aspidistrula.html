Microcalamus aspidistrula

Microcalamus aspidistrula, commonly known as the narrow-leaf bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Vietnam and China. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions.
