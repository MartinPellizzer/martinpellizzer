Maba grisebachii

Maba grisebachii, commonly known as the gray maba tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, lowland forests and is often found in secondary growth areas.
