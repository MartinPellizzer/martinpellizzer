Mandevilla duidae

Mandevilla duidae, commonly known as the Brazilian mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a white or yellow throat. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla duidae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
