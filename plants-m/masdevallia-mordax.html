Masdevallia mordax

Masdevallia mordax, commonly known as the blood orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns resembling bloodstains. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in high humidity and cool temperatures. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

Masdevallia mordax is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
