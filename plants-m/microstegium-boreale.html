Microstegium boreale

Microstegium boreale, commonly known as the northern wood sorrel, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white or pale green underside, and it produces small, yellow flowers in the spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
