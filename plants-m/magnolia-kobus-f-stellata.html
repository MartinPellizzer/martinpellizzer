Magnolia kobus f. stellata

Magnolia kobus f. stellata, commonly known as the star magnolia, is a cultivar of the magnolia tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth margin.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
