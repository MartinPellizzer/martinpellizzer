Mandevilla pittieri

Mandevilla pittieri, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a vigorous evergreen shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit and is often cultivated for its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces clusters of bright pink to red flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are glossy, heart-shaped, and provide a lush backdrop to the vibrant blooms.

Mandevilla pittieri thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is well-suited for containers or as a landscape accent in tropical and subtropical climates.
