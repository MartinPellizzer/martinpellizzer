Mimosa domingensis

Mimosa domingensis, commonly known as the Dominican mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry forests and along coastal areas.

Mimosa domingensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
