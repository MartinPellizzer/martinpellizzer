Monstera acacoyaguensis

Monstera acacoyaguensis, commonly known as the Mexican giant monstera, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its leaves have distinctive notches and holes, which are more pronounced as the plant matures.

It is a popular houseplant due to its striking appearance and air-purifying qualities.
