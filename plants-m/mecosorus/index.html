Mecosorus

Cushion moss, scientifically known as Mecosorus, is a unique type of moss that forms dense, compact growths resembling small cushions or mats. Found primarily in moist, shaded environments, it thrives in cool and humid climates across various regions of the world. This genus is notable for its ability to colonize rocky substrates and contribute to soil stabilization in ecological systems. Its intricate structure and ecological importance make it a fascinating subject for both scientific study and natural appreciation. Despite its small size, cushion moss plays a significant role in maintaining biodiversity and ecosystem health.
