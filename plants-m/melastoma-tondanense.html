Melastoma tondanense

Melastoma tondanense, commonly known as the Java melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces showy, pink to red flowers in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Melastoma tondanense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
