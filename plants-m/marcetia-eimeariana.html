Marcetia eimeariana

Marcetia eimeariana, commonly known as the Eimearian marsh orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific wetland habitats in southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-poor soils and requires a humid environment to thrive. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation areas.
