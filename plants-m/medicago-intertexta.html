Medicago intertexta

Medicago intertexta, commonly known as the common vetch, is a flowering plant native to Europe, Asia, and Africa.

It is a legume characterized by its trailing growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish flowers that are clustered in racemes. Its seeds are small and often used as a food source for livestock.

Medicago intertexta is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings.
