Millettia elskensii var. yangambiensis

Millettia elskensii var. yangambiensis, commonly known as the Yangambi rosewood, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central Africa, particularly in the Democratic Republic of the Congo. The plant is characterized by its woody stems, compound leaves, and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several flat, hard seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and durable wood, it is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
