Myristica colinridsdalei

Myristica colinridsdalei, commonly known as the Colinridsdale's myristica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dark, aromatic seeds and thick, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically pollinated by insects. Its fruits are covered in a yellow, fleshy aril that becomes reddish as it matures.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and potential ecological significance in its native habitat.
