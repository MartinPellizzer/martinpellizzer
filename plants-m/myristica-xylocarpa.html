Myristica xylocarpa

Myristica xylocarpa, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody trunk and broad, leathery leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into fruiting bodies containing the valuable spices nutmeg and mace. Its seeds, enclosed in a hard, woody capsule, are harvested for their aromatic oils and medicinal properties.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in tropical ecosystems.
