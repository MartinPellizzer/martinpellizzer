Magnolia biondii

Magnolia biondii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a straight trunk and a rounded crown.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and parks for its aesthetic appeal.
