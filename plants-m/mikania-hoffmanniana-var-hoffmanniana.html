Mikania hoffmanniana var. hoffmanniana

Mikania hoffmanniana var. hoffmanniana, commonly known as Hoffmann's Mikania, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a rough, hairy texture and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
