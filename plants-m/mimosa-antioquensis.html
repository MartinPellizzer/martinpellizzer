Mimosa antioquensis

Mimosa antioquensis, commonly known as the Antioquia mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Andes region of Colombia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as cloud forests. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
