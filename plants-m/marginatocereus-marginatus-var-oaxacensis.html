Marginatocereus marginatus var. oaxacensis

Marginatocereus marginatus var. oaxacensis, commonly known as the Oaxacan cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Cactaceae family and is characterized by its spiny, columnar stems. The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters at the ends of the stems.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in harsh conditions.
