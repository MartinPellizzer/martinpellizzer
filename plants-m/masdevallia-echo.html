Masdevallia echo

Masdevallia echo, commonly known as the echo orchid, is a unique member of the Orchidaceae family native to the cloud forests of South America.

This epiphytic plant is characterized by its intricate, often pendulous inflorescence and a wide array of color variations in its flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth. Its flowers, which can range from white to deep purple, are often adorned with intricate patterns that mimic the surrounding environment.

Masdevallia echo is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and the challenge of cultivating it under precise conditions.
