Masdevallia wamachukorum

Masdevallia wamachukorum, commonly known as the Wamachuko orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Kenya.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, thriving in the understory of montane forests. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
