Mirkooa rotundifolia

Mirkooa rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leafed mirkooa, is a unique flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to reddish-orange, attracting various pollinators.
