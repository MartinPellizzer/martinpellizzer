Masdevallia nikoleana

Masdevallia nikoleana, commonly known as the Nikoleana Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces inflorescences with a mix of upright and drooping flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light levels but requires careful watering to avoid root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
