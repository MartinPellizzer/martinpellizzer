Miconia media subsp. ecuadorensis

Miconia media subsp.

ecuadorensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family. It is native to the humid lowland forests of Ecuador and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
