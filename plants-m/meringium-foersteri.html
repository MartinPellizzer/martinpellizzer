Meringium foersteri

Meringium foersteri, commonly known as the Foerster's meringue, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun exposure.
