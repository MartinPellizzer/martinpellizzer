Melpomene allosuroides

Melpomene allosuroides, commonly known as the flame violet, is a tropical flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange, often with a contrasting throat. The plant belongs to the Violaceae family and is known for its delicate, velvety leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
