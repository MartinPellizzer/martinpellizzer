Mammillaria lindsayi var. rubriflora

Mammillaria lindsayi var. rubriflora, commonly known as the red-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
