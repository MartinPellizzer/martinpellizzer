Massonia candida

Massonia candida, commonly known as the white massonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. It thrives in well-drained sandy soils and is often found in coastal areas.
