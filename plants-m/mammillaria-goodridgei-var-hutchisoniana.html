Mammillaria goodridgei var. hutchisoniana

Mammillaria goodridgei var. hutchisoniana, commonly known as the Goodridge's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with a greenish-gray color and prominent tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
