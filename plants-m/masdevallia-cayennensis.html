Masdevallia cayennensis

Masdevallia cayennensis, commonly known as the Cayenne masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces striking, brightly colored flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant typically features elongated, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Masdevallia cayennensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
