Macairea adenostemon var. rotundata

Macairea adenostemon var. rotundata, commonly known as the round-leaved macairea, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has rounded, velvety leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
