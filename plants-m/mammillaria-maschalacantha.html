Mammillaria maschalacantha

Mammillaria maschalacantha, commonly known as the Maschalacantha Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of spines and white to pale green areoles. The plant typically grows up to 10 centimeters in height and features a compact, rounded shape. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
