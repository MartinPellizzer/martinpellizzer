Madhuca glaberrima

Madhuca glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth madhuca, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by edible, fleshy fruits containing a single seed. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
