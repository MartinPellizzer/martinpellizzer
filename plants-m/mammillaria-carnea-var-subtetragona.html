Mammillaria carnea var. subtetragona

Mammillaria carnea var. subtetragona, commonly known as the Four-angled Crimson Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale pink spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its unique, four-angled stem shape gives it a distinctive appearance.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
