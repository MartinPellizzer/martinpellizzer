Mammillaria grandicornis

Mammillaria grandicornis, commonly known as the giant horned cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
