Monardella lanceolata

Monardella lanceolata, commonly known as narrowleaf monardella, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in open woodlands and meadows.

Monardella lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and serves as a nectar source for pollinators.
