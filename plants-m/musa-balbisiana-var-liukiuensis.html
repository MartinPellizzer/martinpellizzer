Musa balbisiana var. liukiuensis

Musa balbisiana var. liukiuensis, commonly known as the Liukiu banana, is a variant of the wild banana species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clustered, elongated fruits that are typically smaller than cultivated bananas. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is often used in botanical research due to its genetic diversity and adaptability.
