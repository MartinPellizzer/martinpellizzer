Mammillaria angularis var. longiseta

Mammillaria angularis var. longiseta, commonly known as the long-hair mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, radial spines and shorter, hair-like bristles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
