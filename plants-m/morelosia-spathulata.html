Morelosia spathulata

Morelosia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are highly ornamental and are sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.

Morelosia spathulata is a member of the Orchidaceae family and exhibits typical orchid features such as specialized roots and a complex pollination mechanism.
