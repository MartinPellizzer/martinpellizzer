Mammillaria strobiliformis f. pubescens

Mammillaria strobiliformis f. pubescens, commonly known as the fuzzy ball cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features a dense, rounded stem covered in short, white, woolly hairs, giving it a fuzzy texture. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is native to Mexico and thrives in well-drained soil with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its unique look and relatively easy care requirements.
