Myristica sumbavana

Myristica sumbavana, commonly known as Sumbawan nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces a large, fleshy fruit that contains a single seed, which is encased in a reddish, aromatic aril. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential economic value.
