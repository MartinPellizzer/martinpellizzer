Miconia galactantha

Miconia galactantha, commonly known as the white miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that often have a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia galactantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and continuous flowering.
