Musa × chiliocarpa

Musa × chiliocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of elongated, yellow-orange fruits. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a sweet and slightly tangy flavor. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit production, it is widely grown in gardens and agricultural settings.
