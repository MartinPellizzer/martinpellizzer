Mimosa dolens var. foliolosa

Mimosa dolens var. foliolosa, commonly known as the leafy mimosa, is a variant of the mimosa plant characterized by its distinctive foliage.

It is native to South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features compound leaves with multiple leaflets, giving it a feathery appearance. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in spherical heads, which open in response to touch.

This variation is notable for its adaptability and ornamental value in gardens.
