Medicago asiatica

Medicago asiatica, commonly known as the small medick, is a low-growing annual legume native to Asia.

It features trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in soil enrichment.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.
