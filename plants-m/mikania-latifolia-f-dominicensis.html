Mikania latifolia f. dominicensis

Mikania latifolia f. dominicensis, commonly known as the Dominican milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean, particularly the Dominican Republic.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, often resembling a globular shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and its role in supporting local pollinator populations.
