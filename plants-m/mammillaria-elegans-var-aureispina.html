Mammillaria elegans var. aureispina

Mammillaria elegans var. aureispina, commonly known as the golden spined mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white to pale green tubercles, with short, pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires bright light and minimal water.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
