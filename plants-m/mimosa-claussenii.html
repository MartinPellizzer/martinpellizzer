Mimosa claussenii

Mimosa claussenii, commonly known as the Cape York mimosa, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellow flowers. The plant has a distinctive feature of its leaves folding shut when touched, a response known as thigmonasty. It thrives in sandy or rocky soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Mimosa claussenii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
