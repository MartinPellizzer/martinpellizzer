Medinilla dallciana

Medinilla dallciana, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This evergreen shrub is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It produces small, orange-red berries that are edible but not widely consumed. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.

It is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
