Manihot variifolia

Manihot variifolia, commonly known as the variable arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that vary in size and shape, giving it its common name. The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. Its stems are typically green or reddish, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits.
