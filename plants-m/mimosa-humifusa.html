Mimosa humifusa

Mimosa humifusa, commonly known as the low mimosa, is a small, deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are compound and often appear in pairs. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flower heads that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mimosa humifusa is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
