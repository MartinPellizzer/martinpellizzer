Mesosphaerum dubium

Mesosphaerum dubium, commonly known as the uncertain sphere plant, is a rare and enigmatic species belonging to the family Mesosphaeraceae.

It is characterized by its unique, spherical or globular growth form, with a dense covering of small, scale-like structures. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or yellowish in color. It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.
