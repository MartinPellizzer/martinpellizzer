Melicope maliliensis

Melicope maliliensis, commonly known as the Moluccan melicope, is a small to medium-sized tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

Melicope maliliensis is valued for its ornamental potential and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
