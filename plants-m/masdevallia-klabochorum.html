Masdevallia klabochorum

Masdevallia klabochorum, commonly known as the Klaboch's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces small, intricately patterned flowers in shades of pink and purple. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high altitudes and is typically found in cloud forests. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to the harsh mountain climate.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized orchid species.
