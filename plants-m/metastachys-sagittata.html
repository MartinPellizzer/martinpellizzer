Metastachys sagittata

Metastachys sagittata, commonly known as the arrowhead plant, is a member of the Araceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a velvety texture. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in a spadix, surrounded by a modified leaf called a spathe.

Metastachys sagittata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and air-purifying qualities.
