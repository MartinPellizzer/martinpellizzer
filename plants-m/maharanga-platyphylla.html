Maharanga platyphylla

Maharanga platyphylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved maharanga, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.
