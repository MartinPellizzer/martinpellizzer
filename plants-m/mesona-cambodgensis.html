Mesona cambodgensis

Mesona cambodgensis, commonly known as the Cambodian mesona, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.

Mesona cambodgensis is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
