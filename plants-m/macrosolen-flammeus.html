Macrosolen flammeus

Macrosolen flammeus, commonly known as the flame spurge, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers, which bloom in clusters along the stems. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, aiding in water retention. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its toxic sap, it should be handled with care.
