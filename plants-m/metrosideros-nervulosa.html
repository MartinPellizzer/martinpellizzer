Metrosideros nervulosa

Metrosideros nervulosa, commonly known as the Tahitian Christmas tree, is a flowering plant native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves with prominent veins and its clusters of bright red flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in coastal and limestone areas, often forming dense thickets.
