Mammillaria baumii var. radiaissima

Mammillaria baumii var. radiaissima, commonly known as the Radiant Mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow tubercles, which are adorned with white radial spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
