Melastoma luteum

Melastoma luteum, commonly known as the yellow melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dry seeds.
