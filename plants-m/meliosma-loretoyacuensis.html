Meliosma loretoyacuensis

Meliosma loretoyacuensis, commonly known as the Loretoyacuensis meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliosmaceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often pubescent and have a distinctive venation pattern. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
