Mesembryanthemum dressianum

Mesembryanthemum dressianum, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in winter, producing bright yellow or orange flowers that attract pollinators. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
