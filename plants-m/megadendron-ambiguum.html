Megadendron ambiguum

Megadendron ambiguum, commonly known as the ambiguous giant tree, is a rare and enigmatic plant species characterized by its massive, sprawling growth habit.

Despite its impressive size, Megadendron ambiguum is not commonly cultivated due to its specific ecological requirements and the challenges of its propagation.
