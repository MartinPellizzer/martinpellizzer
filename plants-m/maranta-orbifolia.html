Maranta orbifolia

Maranta orbifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant in the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage, which features broad, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically arranged in spikes, and it thrives in moist, shady environments.

Maranta orbifolia is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
