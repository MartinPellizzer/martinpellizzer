Morinda kanalensis

Morinda kanalensis, commonly known as the red morinda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, hard seeds.
