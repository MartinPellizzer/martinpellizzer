Manilkara grisebachii

Manilkara grisebachii, commonly known as the Guiana apple or Guiana cherry, is a tropical evergreen tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish-brown bark and broad, ovate leaves.
