Mentzelia gracilenta var. veatchiana

Mentzelia gracilenta var. veatchiana, commonly known as Veatch's slender blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and produces small, yellowish to reddish flowers. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, and it is adapted to arid environments.

This variety is noted for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators in its native range.
