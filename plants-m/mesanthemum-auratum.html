Mesanthemum auratum

Mesanthemum auratum, commonly known as the golden mesembs, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
