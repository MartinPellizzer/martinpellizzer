Mitrasacme volubilis, commonly known as the climbing mitrasacme, is a vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and ability to climb using its tendrils. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
