Martagon

Martagon, commonly known as the Turk's cap lily, is a species of flowering plant in the lily family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Balkans and Turkey. The plant produces showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive recurved shape. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that grow from a rhizomatous root system.

Martagon is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
