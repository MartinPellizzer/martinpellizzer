Meliosma fordii var. sinii

Meliosma fordii var. sinii, commonly known as the Chinese meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The leaves are typically dark green on the upper surface and paler underneath, and they often have a distinctive waxy coating. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color, and it is valued for its ornamental foliage rather than its blooms.

This plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.
