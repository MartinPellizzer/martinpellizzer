Miconia juruensis

Miconia juruensis, commonly known as the Juruensis miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America, particularly found in the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often visited by various pollinators. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia juruensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, contributing to biodiversity and providing food for wildlife.
