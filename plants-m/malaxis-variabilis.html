Malaxis variabilis

Malaxis variabilis, commonly known as the variable Malaxis, is an orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant has a slender, erect stem with several linear leaves. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, often clustered in a loose spike.

It is characterized by its variable morphology, which contributes to its common name.
