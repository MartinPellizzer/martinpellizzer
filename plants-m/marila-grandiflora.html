Marila grandiflora

Marila grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered marila, is a striking perennial plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The flowers are typically pink or purple, with five petals and a prominent central stamen. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
