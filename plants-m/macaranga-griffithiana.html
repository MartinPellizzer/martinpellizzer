Macaranga griffithiana

Macaranga griffithiana, commonly known as the Griffith's makara, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape.
