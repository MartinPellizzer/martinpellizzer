Merostachys anomala

Merostachys anomala, commonly known as the yellow panicgrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are often used in ornamental gardening due to their delicate appearance.
