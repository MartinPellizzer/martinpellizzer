Macrocnemum tubulosum

Macrocnemum tubulosum, commonly known as the slender false nettle, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the family Urticaceae, which includes stinging nettles, and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and can spread through rhizomes, making it a common weed in certain regions.
