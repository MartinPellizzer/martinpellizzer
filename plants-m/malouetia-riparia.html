Malouetia riparia

Malouetia riparia, commonly known as the river hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, large, showy flowers that typically range in color from yellow to red. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near rivers and streams. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Malouetia riparia is valued in horticulture for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various tropical climates.
