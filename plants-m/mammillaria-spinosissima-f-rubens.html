Mammillaria spinosissima f. rubens

Mammillaria spinosissima f. rubens, commonly known as the red-tipped mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems that are typically green with red or pinkish tips, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique coloration, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in arid gardens and collections.
