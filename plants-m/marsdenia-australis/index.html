Marsdenia Australis

Marsdenia australis is a unique and resilient plant native to the coastal regions of southern Australia. It thrives in harsh, saline environments where few other plants can survive. This evergreen shrub is known for its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring. Its ability to withstand strong winds and salt spray makes it a valuable species for coastal restoration projects. Despite its hardy nature, it remains a rare and often overlooked part of Australia's diverse flora.
