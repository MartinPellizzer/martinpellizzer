Mapouria langsdorffiana

Mapouria langsdorffiana, commonly known as the red-flowered mapouria, is a small evergreen shrub native to Madagascar.

It features dark green, leathery leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
