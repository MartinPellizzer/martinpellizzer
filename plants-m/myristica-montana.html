Myristica montana

Myristica montana, commonly known as the mountain nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The tree produces unique fruits that contain a richly scented seed, which is used in traditional medicine and as a spice. Its botanical features include smooth, dark brown bark and leathery, elliptical leaves.

Myristica montana is also valued for its essential oils, which have various industrial and medicinal applications.
