Madagasikaria

Madagasikaria, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped blooms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink, and are often fragrant.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
