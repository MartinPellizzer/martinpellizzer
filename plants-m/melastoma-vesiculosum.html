Melastoma vesiculosum

Melastoma vesiculosum, commonly known as the bubble fruit or bubble tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant produces small, round, fleshy fruits that resemble bubbles, which are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in herbal medicine.
