Maxillaria ruberrima f. aurea

Maxillaria ruberrima f. aurea, commonly known as the golden maxillaria, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and its showy, bright yellow flowers with a red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It blooms during the summer months, producing fragrant flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This cultivar is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and ornamental value.
