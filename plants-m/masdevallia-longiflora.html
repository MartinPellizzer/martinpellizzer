Masdevallia longiflora

Masdevallia longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant produces a single, tall scape with numerous small, clustered flowers, making it a striking addition to botanical collections. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia longiflora thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.
