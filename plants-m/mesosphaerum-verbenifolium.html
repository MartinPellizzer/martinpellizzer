Mesosphaerum verbenifolium

Mesosphaerum verbenifolium, commonly known as the Mexican verbena.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico and belongs to the Verbenaceae family. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to purple, often blooming in summer. Its leaves are typically opposite, ovate, and have a rough texture.

Mesosphaerum verbenifolium is often found in rocky or dry habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
