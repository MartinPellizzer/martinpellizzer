Myristica firmipes

Myristica firmipes, commonly known as the slender nutmeg tree, is a tropical plant native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic seeds that are used in traditional medicine. The plant produces yellowish flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves. Its fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains the valuable seed.

The tree is valued for its aromatic oils and has been historically significant in the spice trade.
