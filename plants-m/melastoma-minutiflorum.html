Melastoma minutiflorum

Melastoma minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flower melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a smooth surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
