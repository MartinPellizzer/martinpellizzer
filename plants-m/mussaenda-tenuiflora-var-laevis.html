Mussaenda tenuiflora var. laevis

Mussaenda tenuiflora var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and clusters of small, bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The flowers are often used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants due to their vibrant color and unique appearance.
