Myoporum obscurum

Myoporum obscurum, commonly known as New Zealand myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple, which bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal and inland areas, growing in a variety of soil types.

Myoporum obscurum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in landscaping.
