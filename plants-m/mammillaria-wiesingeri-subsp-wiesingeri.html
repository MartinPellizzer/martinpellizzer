Mammillaria wiesingeri subsp. wiesingeri

Mammillaria wiesingeri subsp.

wiesingeri, commonly known as the Wiesinger's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem with radial spines and a dense covering of white to light green areoles.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for succulent collectors.
