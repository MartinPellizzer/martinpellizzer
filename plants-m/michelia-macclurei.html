Michelia macclurei

Michelia macclurei, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

The flowers are not only aesthetically pleasing but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
