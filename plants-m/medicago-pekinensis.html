Medicago pekinensis

Medicago pekinensis, commonly known as Peking clover, is a perennial legume native to China.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and disturbed areas.

Its seeds are edible and have been used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
