Mandevilla angustata

Mandevilla angustata, commonly known as the narrowleaf mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its climbing habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Mandevilla angustata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its showy blooms and ability to add vertical interest to gardens.
