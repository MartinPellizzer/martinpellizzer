Miltoniopsis vexillaria

Miltoniopsis vexillaria, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety petals that range in color from deep purples to vibrant reds. The plant typically grows as a compact, epiphytic orchid with a short, stocky stem. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are known for their intricate, spurred shape.

Miltoniopsis vexillaria thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
