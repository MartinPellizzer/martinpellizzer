Malus Baccata Var. Daochengensis

Malus baccata var. daochengensis is a rare and unique apple tree species native to the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau. This variant is known for its distinctive appearance and hardiness in high-altitude environments. It features small, yellowish fruits that are adapted to the extreme climate of its region. The plant is of significant interest to botanists and conservationists due to its limited distribution. Its study offers insights into plant adaptation and biodiversity in harsh ecosystems.
