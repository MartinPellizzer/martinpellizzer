Maerua socotrana

Maerua socotrana, commonly known as Socotra cactus, is a unique plant native to the Socotra archipelago.

It is a flowering shrub that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing adaptations to extreme drought conditions. The plant features spiny stems and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and deterring herbivores.

Maerua socotrana is also notable for its role in the local ecosystem and its potential medicinal uses.
