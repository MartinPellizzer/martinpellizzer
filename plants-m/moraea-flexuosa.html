Moraea flexuosa

Moraea flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features strap-shaped leaves and produces tall, zigzagging stems that support its showy blooms. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Moraea flexuosa is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral structure.
