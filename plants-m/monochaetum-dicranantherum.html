Monochaetum dicranantherum

Monochaetum dicranantherum, commonly known as the slender-leafed grass.

It is a member of the Poaceae family and is native to Australia, specifically found in the southeastern region. This grass is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers. It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

The plant plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for small invertebrates.
