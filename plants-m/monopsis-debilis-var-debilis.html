Monopsis debilis var. debilis

Monopsis debilis var. debilis, commonly known as the slender monopsis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and along stream banks. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in loose inflorescences, adding subtle beauty to its habitat.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its native range.
