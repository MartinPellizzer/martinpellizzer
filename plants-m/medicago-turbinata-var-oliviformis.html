Medicago turbinata var. oliviformis

Medicago turbinata var. oliviformis, commonly known as the oliveleaf medic, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically olive-green in color, and its small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil enrichment and forage.

Its adaptability to harsh environments makes it a resilient plant in ecological restoration projects.
