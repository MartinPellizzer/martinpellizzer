Medium

Medium, commonly known as the medium plant, refers to a general category of plants that are neither small nor large in size.

These plants typically have moderate growth habits and are often used in landscaping for their versatility. Botanically, they may belong to various families, such as Asteraceae, Poaceae, or Fabaceae, depending on the species. They usually have well-developed root systems and can thrive in a range of soil types.

Their foliage and flowering patterns vary, but they generally contribute to ecological balance and aesthetic value in gardens.
