Melicope albiflora

Melicope albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered melicope, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features simple, opposite leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance. Its flowers are white and clustered in inflorescences, attracting various pollinators.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that contains a single seed.
