Magnolia globosa var. sinensis

Magnolia globosa var. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the large-flowered magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin layers.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
