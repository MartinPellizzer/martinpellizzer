Mesembryanthemum pageanum

Mesembryanthemum pageanum, commonly known as the Cape violet, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum pageanum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and drought tolerance.
