Marica humilis var. lutea

Marica humilis var. lutea, commonly known as the yellow marica, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its foliage. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
