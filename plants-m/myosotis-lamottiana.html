Myosotis lamottiana

Myosotis lamottiana, commonly known as the Lamott's forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to southern South America.

It features delicate blue to purple flowers with five petals, typically arranged in clusters at the ends of its stems. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and grow along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Myosotis lamottiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant, delicate blooms.
