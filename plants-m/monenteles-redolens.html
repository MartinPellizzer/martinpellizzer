Monenteles redolens

Monenteles redolens, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
