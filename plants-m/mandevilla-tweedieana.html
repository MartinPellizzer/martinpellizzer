Mandevilla tweedieana

Mandevilla tweedieana, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of tubular, pinkish-red flowers. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Mandevilla tweedieana is popular in gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
