Millettia dinklagei

Millettia dinklagei, commonly known as the Dinklage's legume, is a flowering plant species belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow or green flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its growth habit is typically shrub-like, with woody stems and small, trifoliate leaves.

Millettia dinklagei is valued for its ecological role in nitrogen fixation and its potential use in traditional medicine.
