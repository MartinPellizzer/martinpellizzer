Macowania hamata

Macowania hamata, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Macowania hamata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a member of the Amaryllidaceae family.
