Mesembryanthemum vermeuleniae

Mesembryanthemum vermeuleniae, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum vermeuleniae is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
