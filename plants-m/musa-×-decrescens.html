Musa × decrescens

Musa × decrescens, commonly known as the Chinese banyan, is a tropical plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and can grow into a massive tree with a broad canopy. The plant is notable for its ability to produce both male and female flowers on the same individual, making it monoecious. Its roots can spread extensively, forming a network that supports the tree's growth and stability.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
