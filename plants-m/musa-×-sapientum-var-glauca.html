Musa × sapientum var. glauca

Musa × sapientum var. glauca, commonly known as the glaucous banana, is a variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its striking bluish-green leaves and elongated, yellow fruit. The plant is a hybrid, originating from crosses between different banana species. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit, which has a sweet, mild flavor.
