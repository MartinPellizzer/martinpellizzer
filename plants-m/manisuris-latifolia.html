Manisuris latifolia

Manisuris latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf manisuris, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the warm season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
