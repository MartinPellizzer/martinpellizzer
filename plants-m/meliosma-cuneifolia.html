Meliosma cuneifolia

Meliosma cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical trees. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters, followed by small, winged fruits.

Meliosma cuneifolia thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and stream banks.
