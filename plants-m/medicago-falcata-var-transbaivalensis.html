Medicago falcata var. transbaivalensis

Medicago falcata var. transbaivalensis, commonly known as the Transbaikal goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in dry, sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It has small, elliptical seeds that are dispersed by wind.

This variety is valued for its hardiness and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
