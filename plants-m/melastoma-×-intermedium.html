Melastoma × intermedium

Melastoma × intermedium, commonly known as the hybrid Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a glossy appearance.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
