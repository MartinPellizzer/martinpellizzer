Musa barioensis

Musa barioensis, commonly known as the Bario banana, is a species of banana plant native to the Malaysian state of Kedah.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellow fruits that are typically harvested when they are fully mature. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its unique genetic makeup, it is of interest to botanists and agricultural researchers.
