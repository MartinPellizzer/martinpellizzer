Malcolmia intermedia

Malcolmia intermedia, commonly known as the blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters, often in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its characteristic appearance.

The plant is known for its ability to thrive in poor soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.
