Maxillaria maderoi

Maxillaria maderoi, commonly known as the Mader's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Maxillaria maderoi typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique appearance.
