Montiastrum lineare

Montiastrum lineare, commonly known as the slender montia, is a herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves that are typically arranged in a basal rosette. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Montiastrum lineare is a member of the Montiaceae family and is known for its ornamental value in native plant gardens.
