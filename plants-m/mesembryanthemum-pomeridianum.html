Mesembryanthemum pomeridianum

Mesembryanthemum pomeridianum, commonly known as the afternoon glory, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a succulent species belonging to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and low maintenance requirements.
