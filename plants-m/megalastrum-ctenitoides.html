Megalastrum ctenitoides

Megalastrum ctenitoides, commonly known as the hairy leaf tree, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it a unique addition to botanical gardens and a subject of interest for researchers studying tropical flora.
