Montia rivularis subsp. lamprosperma

Montia rivularis subsp.

lamprosperma, commonly known as the shining leaf watercress, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to North America. It typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as stream banks and wet meadows.

The plant features small, alternate, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are white or pale pink, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This subspecies is distinguished by its shiny foliage and is often found in temperate regions with high humidity.
