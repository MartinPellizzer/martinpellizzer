Malaxis spectabilis

Malaxis spectabilis, commonly known as the showy malaxis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to the wet tropical regions of South America. The plant features a slender, branching stem with numerous small, greenish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the humid environment.

It is often found in shaded, moist forest understories where it thrives in the shade of larger trees.
