Myosotis antarctica subsp. antarctica

Myosotis antarctica subsp.

antarctica, commonly known as the Antarctic forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of the Southern Hemisphere. It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

This species is adapted to survive in harsh, alpine environments with low temperatures and limited nutrients. Its growth is typically limited to rocky or gravelly soils in high-altitude areas.
