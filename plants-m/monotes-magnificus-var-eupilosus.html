Monotes magnificus var. eupilosus

Monotes magnificus var. eupilosus, commonly known as the splendid magnolia, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Central America. The plant features broad, glossy leaves and produces fragrant, white to pinkish blooms during the spring and early summer. Its unique floral structure includes numerous stamens that create a dramatic visual effect.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
