Morinda palmetorum

Morinda palmetorum, commonly known as the dwarf morinda, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged in a compact, bushy form. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by bright red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
