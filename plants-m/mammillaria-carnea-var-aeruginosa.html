Mammillaria carnea var. aeruginosa

Mammillaria carnea var. aeruginosa, commonly known as the Azure Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to bluish-gray body covered in short, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with bright, indirect light.

Its unique coloration and compact form make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
