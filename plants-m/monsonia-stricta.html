Monsonia stricta

Monsonia stricta, commonly known as the rigid monsonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features upright, slender stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are narrow and linear, with a slightly hairy surface.

Monsonia stricta is well adapted to arid environments and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
