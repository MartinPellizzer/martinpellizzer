Mikania natalensis

Mikania natalensis, commonly known as Natal Mikania, is a climbing vine native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by oval, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain habitats.
