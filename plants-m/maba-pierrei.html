Maba pierrei

Maba pierrei, commonly known as the African moonflower, is a climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that open at night. The plant has compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. It thrives in humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

The flowers emit a strong fragrance to attract nocturnal pollinators such as moths.
