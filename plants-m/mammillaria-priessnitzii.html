Mammillaria priessnitzii

Mammillaria priessnitzii, commonly known as the Priessnitz's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are often followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice among succulent collectors.
