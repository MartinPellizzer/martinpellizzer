Maxillaria lutea

Maxillaria lutea, commonly known as the yellow maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces striking yellow flowers with a velvety texture. The plant typically features strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. Maxillaria lutea thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
