Myrcia heineriana

Myrcia heineriana, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil, where it thrives in humid, tropical climates. Myrcia heineriana is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its aromatic properties.
