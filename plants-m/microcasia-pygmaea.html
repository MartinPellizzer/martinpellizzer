Microcasia pygmaea

Microcasia pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf microcasia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, often colorful flowers. Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
