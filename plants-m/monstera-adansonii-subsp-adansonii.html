Monstera adansonii subsp. adansonii

Monstera adansonii subsp.

adansonii, commonly known as the Adansonii Monstera, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America. It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves that develop fenestrations as the plant matures.

The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its aerial roots allow it to climb and support itself on structures such as trellises or trees.

The leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy surface, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
