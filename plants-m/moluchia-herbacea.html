Moluchia herbacea

Moluchia herbacea, commonly known as the slender moluchia, is a tropical shrub belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are typically divided into five to seven leaflets. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is native to regions of Africa and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
