Maxillaria piresiana

Maxillaria piresiana, commonly known as the Pires's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria piresiana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
