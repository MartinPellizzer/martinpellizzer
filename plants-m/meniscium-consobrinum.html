Meniscium consobrinum

Meniscium consobrinum, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. The plant has lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Meniscium consobrinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
