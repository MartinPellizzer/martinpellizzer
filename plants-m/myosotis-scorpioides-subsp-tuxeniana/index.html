Myosotis Scorpioides Subsp. Tuxeniana

Myosotis scorpioides subsp. tuxeniana is a striking member of the Myosotis genus known for its vibrant blue and purple flowers. This subspecies is native to parts of Europe and Asia and thrives in moist, shady environments. It is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. The plant is prized for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens. Its delicate appearance and unique coloration make it a standout among water-loving plants.
