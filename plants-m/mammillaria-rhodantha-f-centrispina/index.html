Mammillaria Rhodantha F. Centrispina

Red-tipped hedgehog cactus, scientifically known as Mammillaria rhodantha f. centrispina, is a striking member of the cacti family native to Mexico. This unique plant is characterized by its vibrant red tips on its spiny, cylindrical stems. It thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for desert gardens. The plant's appearance is both dramatic and delicate, with clusters of small, white flowers blooming in spring. Its ornamental value and resilience make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts and collectors.
