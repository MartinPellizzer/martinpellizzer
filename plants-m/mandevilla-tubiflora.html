Mandevilla tubiflora

Mandevilla tubiflora, commonly known as the orange mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically orange or red in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and deeply veined. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Mandevilla tubiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
