Maxillariella houtteana

Maxillariella houtteana, commonly known as the Houttean orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, brightly colored blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with a contrasting lip. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it prefers shaded, humid environments.

Maxillariella houtteana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
