Maireana brevifolia

Maireana brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved maireana, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, narrow leaves and clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resilience to drought and heat. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
