Maxillaria elianae

Maxillaria elianae, commonly known as the Erianella orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, enabling it to thrive in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

Maxillaria elianae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
