Miltonia clowesii var. lamarcheana

Miltonia clowesii var. lamarcheana, commonly known as the Lamarche's Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features a compact, bushy growth habit with broad, pleated leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty in both home and greenhouse settings.
