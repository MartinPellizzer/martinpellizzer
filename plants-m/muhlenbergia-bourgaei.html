Muhlenbergia bourgaei

Muhlenbergia bourgaei, commonly known as the Mexican feathergrass, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that appear in summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Muhlenbergia bourgaei is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and low maintenance requirements.
