Menziesia × kamatae

Menziesia × kamatae, commonly known as the Kamatae wintergreen, is a hybrid shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries, and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, bell-shaped white flowers in early spring, followed by dark blue, edible berries. It thrives in acidic, well-drained soils and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Menziesia × kamatae is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
