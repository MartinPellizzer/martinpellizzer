Masdevallia welischii

Masdevallia welischii, commonly known as the Welisch's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that feature a combination of sepals, petals, and a prominent lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of narrow, elongated leaves that are well adapted to the humid, shaded conditions of its environment.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
