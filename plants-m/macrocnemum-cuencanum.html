Macrocnemum cuencanum

Macrocnemum cuencanum, commonly known as the Cuencan wildbean, is a flowering plant native to parts of Central America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments.
