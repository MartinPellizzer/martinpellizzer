Myoporum cyanantherum

Myoporum cyanantherum, commonly known as the blue anther myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and showy blue-violet flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and has a compact, shrub-like form. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences.

Myoporum cyanantherum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
