Myosotis arvensis subsp. umbrata

Myosotis arvensis subsp.

umbrata, commonly known as the shadow forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of Europe. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

The plant features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

It is characterized by its preference for partial to full shade and its ability to thrive in nutrient-poor soils.
