Muehlenbeckia hastulata var. rotundata

Muehlenbeckia hastulata var. rotundata, commonly known as the roundleaf muhlenbeckia, is a low-growing perennial shrub native to New Zealand.

It features slender, trailing stems that produce small, oval-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of tiny, yellowish-green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its ornamental value comes from its compact growth habit and attractive foliage.
