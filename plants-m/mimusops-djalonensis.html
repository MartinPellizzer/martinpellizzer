Mimusops djalonensis

Mimusops djalonensis, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to West Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by round, yellowish fruits. Its wood is dense and hard, making it valuable for furniture and construction.
