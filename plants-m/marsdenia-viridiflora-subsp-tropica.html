Marsdenia viridiflora subsp. tropica

Marsdenia viridiflora subsp.

tropica, commonly known as the tropical green-flowered Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, often with a subtle fragrance.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage and potential medicinal properties.
