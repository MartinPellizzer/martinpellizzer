Mariscus manimae var. apiculatus

Mariscus manimae var. apiculatus, commonly known as the Apiculate Mariscus, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions in South America.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests or along riverbanks.
