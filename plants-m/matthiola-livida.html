Matthiola livida

Matthiola livida, commonly known as the pale stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbages and mustards. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Matthiola livida is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation.
