Mattiastrum gracile

Mattiastrum gracile, commonly known as the slender mat grass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to South America.

It features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically less than 2 inches in width. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation, it is sometimes used in landscaping and restoration projects.
