Mimosa rekoana

Mimosa rekoana, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several pairs of oval-shaped leaflets.

Mimosa rekoana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
