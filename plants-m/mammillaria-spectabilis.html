Mammillaria spectabilis

Mammillaria spectabilis, commonly known as the showy mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, white or yellowish spines and spherical, often ribbed stems. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
