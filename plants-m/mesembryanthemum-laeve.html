Mesembryanthemum laeve

Mesembryanthemum laeve, commonly known as the smooth leaf ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments. Its unique adaptations, such as thick leaves and a shallow root system, help it conserve water efficiently.

Mesembryanthemum laeve is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
