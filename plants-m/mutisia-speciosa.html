Mutisia speciosa

Mutisia speciosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow-orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a robust, woody stem and broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Mutisia speciosa is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
