Microlaena giulianettii

Microlaena giulianettii, commonly known as the Giant Tussock Grass, is a perennial tussock-forming grass native to southern Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces distinctive spikelets that are often tinged with purple or brown, and it has fibrous roots that help stabilize soil. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Microlaena giulianettii is often found in grasslands and open woodlands, contributing to the ecological diversity of these habitats.
