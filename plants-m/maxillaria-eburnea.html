Maxillaria eburnea

Maxillaria eburnea, commonly known as the Ivory Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, white to pale pink flowers that resemble ivory, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as a pseudobulbous orchid with strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms. Maxillaria eburnea thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
