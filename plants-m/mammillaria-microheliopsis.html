Mammillaria microheliopsis

Mammillaria microheliopsis, commonly known as the miniature sunball cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a diameter of about 5 to 10 centimeters. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its stems are covered in small, white tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental value.
