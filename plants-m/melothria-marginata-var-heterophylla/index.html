Melothria Marginata Var. Heterophylla

Melothria marginata var. heterophylla is a lesser-known but fascinating species of sea grape found in coastal regions. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf morphology, which differs significantly from the standard variety. It thrives in sandy soils near the shoreline, where it plays a vital role in stabilizing dune ecosystems. Despite its ecological importance, it remains underappreciated in horticultural circles. Understanding this plant offers insights into coastal biodiversity and adaptation strategies.
