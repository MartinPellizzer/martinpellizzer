Miconia purpurea

Miconia purpurea, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia purpurea is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
