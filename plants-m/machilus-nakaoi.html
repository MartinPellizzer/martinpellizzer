Machilus nakaoi

Machilus nakaoi, commonly known as the Japanese machilus, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Japan.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often dark green and glossy. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Machilus nakaoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
