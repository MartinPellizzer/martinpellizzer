Malpighia nayaritensis

Malpighia nayaritensis, commonly known as the Nayarit beautyberry, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malpighiaceae.

It is native to Mexico, particularly the state of Nayarit, and grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or yellow when ripe. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
