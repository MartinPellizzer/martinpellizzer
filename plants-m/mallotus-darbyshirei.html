Mallotus darbyshirei

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
