Mammillaria criniformis var. rosea

Mammillaria criniformis var. rosea, commonly known as the Roseate Bunny Ears, is a compact cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pinkish spines and dense, woolly areoles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique coloration and compact growth make it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
