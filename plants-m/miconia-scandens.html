Miconia scandens

Miconia scandens, commonly known as the "butterfly tree," is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia scandens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
