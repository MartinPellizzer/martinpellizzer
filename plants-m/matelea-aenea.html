Matelea aenea

Matelea aenea, commonly known as the copperleaf or silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America. The plant is characterized by its striking copper-colored foliage and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other ornamental plants.

Matelea aenea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in well-drained soil with partial to full sun.
