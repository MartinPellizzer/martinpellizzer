Miconia leariedeliana

Miconia leariedeliana, commonly known as the "beautiful leaf miconia," is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions where it can outcompete native vegetation.
