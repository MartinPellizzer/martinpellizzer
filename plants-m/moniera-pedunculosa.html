Moniera pedunculosa

Moniera pedunculosa, commonly known as the clustered bellflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Campanulaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Moniera pedunculosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
