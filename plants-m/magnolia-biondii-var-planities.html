Magnolia biondii var. planities

Magnolia biondii var. planities, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broad-leafed magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a spreading canopy and a straight trunk.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.
