Malva florida

Malva florida, commonly known as the common mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in many regions around the world. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in gardens, fields, and along waterways.

Malva florida is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its soothing properties.
