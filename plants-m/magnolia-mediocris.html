Magnolia mediocris

Magnolia mediocris, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
