Melastoma ferrugineum

Melastoma ferrugineum, commonly known as the rusty melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to red. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an important component of tropical and subtropical ecosystems.
