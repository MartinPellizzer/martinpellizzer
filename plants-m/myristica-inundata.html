Myristica inundata

Myristica inundata, commonly known as the water nutmeg, is a tropical plant belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is characterized by its woody, shrub-like structure with dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

Myristica inundata thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in lowland rainforests.
