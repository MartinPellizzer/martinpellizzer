Microstegium japonicum

Microstegium japonicum, commonly known as Japanese wood sorrel, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to East Asia.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive reddish or purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Microstegium japonicum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions as a garden plant.
