Miconia prietoi

Miconia prietoi, commonly known as the Puerto Rican miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is native to the island of Puerto Rico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. Its flowers are showy, with five pink or purple petals and are borne in clusters.

Miconia prietoi plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
