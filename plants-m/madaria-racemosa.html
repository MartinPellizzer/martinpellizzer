Madaria racemosa

Madaria racemosa, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces clusters of bright red, spider-like flowers in the spring. The plant features strap-like, dark green leaves that are narrow and pointed. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for woodland gardens and naturalized areas.
