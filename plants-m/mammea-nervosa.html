Mammea nervosa

Mammea nervosa, commonly known as the silky mammea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by red, fleshy berries. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The tree is also valued for its ornamental appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
