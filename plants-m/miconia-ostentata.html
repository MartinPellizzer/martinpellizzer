Miconia ostentata

Miconia ostentata, commonly known as the showy miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia ostentata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy inflorescences.
