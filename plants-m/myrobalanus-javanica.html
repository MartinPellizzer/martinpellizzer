Myrobalanus javanica

Myrobalanus javanica, commonly known as the Java persimmon, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in Indonesia, Malaysia, and Thailand. The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine for its various health benefits.
