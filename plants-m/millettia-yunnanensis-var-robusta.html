Millettia yunnanensis var. robusta

Millettia yunnanensis var. robusta, commonly known as the Yunnan robust millettia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and racemose inflorescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered together. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
