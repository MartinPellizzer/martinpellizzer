Melastoma obvolutum

Melastoma obvolutum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered red or pink flowers. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Melastoma obvolutum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
