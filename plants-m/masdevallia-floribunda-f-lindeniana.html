Masdevallia floribunda f. lindeniana

Masdevallia floribunda f. lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindenberg's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its vibrant, showy inflorescences that bear numerous small, colorful flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of delicate, spurred flowers in shades of pink, white, and red, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures, high humidity, and bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and horticultural appeal, it is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
