Magnolia wilsonii var. petrosa

Magnolia wilsonii var. petrosa, commonly known as the Petrosa magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad and elliptical, with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 15 meters.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
