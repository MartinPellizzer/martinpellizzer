Miltonia clowesii

Miltonia clowesii, commonly known as the Christmas orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom during the holiday season, hence its common name. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually bright pink or red with white or yellow lips, and it produces a pleasant fragrance.

Miltonia clowesii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both greenhouse and outdoor settings.
