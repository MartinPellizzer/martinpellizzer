Meriania cordifolia

Meriania cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf meriania, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Meriania cordifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
