Mimosa barbigera var. erecta

Mimosa barbigera var. erecta, commonly known as the erect mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas. It has a short, upright stem and is known for its rapid growth habit.

The species is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowers.
