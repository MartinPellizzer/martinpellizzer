Mentha × elegans

Mentha × elegans, commonly known as the slender mint, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and slender, aromatic stems. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a strong mint scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This plant is often used in ornamental gardening and for its essential oils.
