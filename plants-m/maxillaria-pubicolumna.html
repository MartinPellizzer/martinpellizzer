Maxillaria pubicolumna

Maxillaria pubicolumna, commonly known as the pubicolumned orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, featuring pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting purple or brown lip. Its leaves are typically linear and arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

Maxillaria pubicolumna thrives in humid, shaded environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in cultivation.
