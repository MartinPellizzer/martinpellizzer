Metalasia lanceolata

Metalasia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Metalasia lanceolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
