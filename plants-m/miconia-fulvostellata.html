Miconia fulvostellata

Miconia fulvostellata, commonly known as the golden star miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are typically green with a yellowish underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white to yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are fleshy, orange berries that contain numerous seeds.

Miconia fulvostellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy flowers.
