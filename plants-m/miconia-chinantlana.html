Miconia chinantlana

Miconia chinantlana, commonly known as the Mexican miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to Central America.

It is a large shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually white or pale pink in color.

Miconia chinantlana is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of tropical and subtropical conditions.
