Manihot moluccana

Manihot moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant's underground stems, or tubers, are the primary part used for culinary purposes. These tubers are starchy and have a mild, slightly sweet flavor.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its edible roots in tropical regions.
