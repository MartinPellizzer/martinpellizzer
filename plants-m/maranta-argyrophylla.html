Maranta argyrophylla

Maranta argyrophylla, commonly known as the silver-leafed maranta, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants valued for their foliage. The plant is characterized by its striking, silver-white leaves with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Maranta argyrophylla is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
