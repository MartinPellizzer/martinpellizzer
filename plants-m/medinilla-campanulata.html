Medinilla campanulata

Medinilla campanulata, commonly known as the bell medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Medinilla campanulata is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its beauty and adaptability to various growing conditions.
