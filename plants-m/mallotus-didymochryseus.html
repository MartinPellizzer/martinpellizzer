Mallotus didymochryseus

Mallotus didymochryseus, commonly known as the golden-leaf mallot, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and striking golden-yellow leaves that turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has medicinal uses in traditional medicine.

The tree thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
