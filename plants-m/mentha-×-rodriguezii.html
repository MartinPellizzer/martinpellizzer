Mentha × rodriguezii

Mentha × rodriguezii, commonly known as Rodriguez mint, is a hybrid mint species that combines traits from various Mentha species.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and vigorous growth habit, making it a popular choice for ornamental and culinary purposes. The plant typically features square stems and opposite leaves with a serrated margin. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its essential oils, which contribute to its distinctive fragrance, are often used in aromatherapy and herbal remedies.
