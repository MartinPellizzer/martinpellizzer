Marsdenia reichenbachii

Marsdenia reichenbachii, commonly known as the Chinese star vine, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems that can twine around supports. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers, which are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. Native to China, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
