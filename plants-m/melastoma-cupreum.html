Melastoma cupreum

Melastoma cupreum, commonly known as the copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, copper-colored leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to white, often with a reddish hue. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

Melastoma cupreum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
