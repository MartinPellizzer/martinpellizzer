Mionandra

Mionandra, commonly known as the mountain gum, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Australia and consists of several species, most of which are shrubs or small trees. The plants are characterized by their aromatic leaves and showy, often pink or red flowers. They typically grow in moist, well-drained soils in forested or rocky areas.
