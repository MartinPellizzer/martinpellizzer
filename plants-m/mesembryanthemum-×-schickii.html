Mesembryanthemum × schickii

Mesembryanthemum × schickii, commonly known as the hybrid ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring and summer, producing vibrant pink or red flowers that attract pollinators. Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value, it is widely cultivated in arid and semi-arid regions.
