Maxillaria fractiflexa

Maxillaria fractiflexa, commonly known as the flexible maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to greenish flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, with a smooth texture.

Maxillaria fractiflexa is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
