Melastoma subgrande

Melastoma subgrande, commonly known as the large-flowered melastoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions such as Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Melastoma subgrande is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.
