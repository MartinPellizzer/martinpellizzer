Margaritaria anomala var. cheloniphorbe

Margaritaria anomala var. cheloniphorbe, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a shrub native to parts of Africa.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically ovate to elliptical in shape. The plant produces yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, which are often borne on short peduncles. Its milky sap contains latex and has been used traditionally for various purposes.

The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.
