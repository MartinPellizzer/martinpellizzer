Manilkara jaimiqui subsp. jaimiqui

Manilkara jaimiqui subsp.

jaimiqui, commonly known as the Jaimiqui sapodilla, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, oblong leaves and produces a yellowish fruit with a sweet, aromatic pulp.

The plant belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is valued for its edible fruit and ornamental qualities. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
