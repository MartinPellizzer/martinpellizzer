Maranta louisae

Maranta louisae, commonly known as the velvet leaf maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental species known for their vibrant foliage. Maranta louisae typically grows as an upright herb, reaching heights of up to 3 feet.

It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
