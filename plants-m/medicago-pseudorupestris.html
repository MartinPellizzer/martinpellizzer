Medicago pseudorupestris

Medicago pseudorupestris, commonly known as the rock medic, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer, followed by pods containing several seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils.

Medicago pseudorupestris is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as slopes and gravelly areas.
