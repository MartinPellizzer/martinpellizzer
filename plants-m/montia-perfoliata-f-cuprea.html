Montia perfoliata f. cuprea

Montia perfoliata f. cuprea, commonly known as the red-stemmed leafy weed, is a variant of the common montia species.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its distinctive red stems and heart-shaped leaves. The leaves are often covered with a fine layer of silky hairs, giving them a soft texture. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it easily identifiable and ecologically significant in its native habitat.
