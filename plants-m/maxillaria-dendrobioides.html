Maxillaria dendrobioides

Maxillaria dendrobioides, commonly known as the tree orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters, resembling a small tree. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
