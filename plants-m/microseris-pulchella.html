Microseris pulchella

Microseris pulchella, commonly known as the showy cress, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Microseris pulchella typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks.
