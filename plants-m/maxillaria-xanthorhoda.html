Maxillaria xanthorhoda

Maxillaria xanthorhoda, commonly known as the golden maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, with fleshy, dark green leaves. It prefers warm, humid environments and is often found in shaded forest understories.

Its flowers bloom in late summer to early autumn, making it a notable addition to orchid collections.
