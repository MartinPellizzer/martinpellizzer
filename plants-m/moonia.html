Moonia

Moonia, commonly known as the moonflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, white, night-blooming flowers that emit a strong, sweet fragrance. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 feet. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, and it thrives in warm, humid climates.

Moonia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
