Malva Rosea

Malva rosea is a delicate flowering plant known for its soft pink blooms and velvety leaves. It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus. Native to southern Europe and parts of Asia, this plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun. Its flowers attract pollinators like bees and butterflies, making it a valuable addition to gardens. With its gentle beauty and easy care requirements, Malva rosea is a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike.
