Maniltoa brassii

Maniltoa brassii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic appeal.

Maniltoa brassii is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of indoor environments.
