Mentha sphaerostachya

Mentha sphaerostachya, commonly known as spherical-stalked mint, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is native to regions of Central Asia and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Mentha sphaerostachya is often found in moist, rocky habitats and is valued for its aromatic properties.

It is a perennial herb that can grow up to 1 meter in height.
