Malpighia nummulariifolia var. calcarea

Malpighia nummulariifolia var. calcarea, commonly known as the limestone snowberry, is a shrub native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces edible berries that are typically red or orange when mature. It thrives in limestone soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its foliage is usually grayish-green, with leaves that are rounded and somewhat succulent in texture.
