Masdevallia ignea var. pulchra

Masdevallia ignea var. pulchra, commonly known as the fiery flame orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It features vibrant red and orange petals with a contrasting dark brown lip, making it highly ornamental. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
