Masdevallia odnalorii

Masdevallia odnalorii, commonly known as the Odnalorii masdevallia, is a rare orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features narrow, linear leaves and produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in high-altitude cloud forests, where it relies on specific microclimatic conditions for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
