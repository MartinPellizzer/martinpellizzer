Muraltia tenuifolia var. minor

Muraltia tenuifolia var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf muraltia, is a compact shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by red, fleshy berries. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in warm climates.
