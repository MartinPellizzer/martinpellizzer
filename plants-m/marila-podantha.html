Marila podantha

Marila podantha, commonly known as the narrow-leafed marila, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Acanthaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is more commonly seen in its natural habitat.
