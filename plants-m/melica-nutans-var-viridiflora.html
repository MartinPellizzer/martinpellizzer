Melica nutans var. viridiflora

Melica nutans var. viridiflora, commonly known as green-flowered nodding grass, is a perennial grass species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist habitats such as meadows, wetlands, and stream banks. The plant features slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its inflorescence consists of a spike-like cluster of small, greenish flowers that nod towards the ground.

The species is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in temperate climates.
