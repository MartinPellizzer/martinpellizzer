Myosotis nemorosa subsp. brevisetacea

Myosotis nemorosa subsp.

brevisetacea, commonly known as the short-stalked wood forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in woodland habitats, preferring moist, shaded environments.

The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
