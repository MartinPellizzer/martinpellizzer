Mammillaria microcarpa f. swinglei

Mammillaria microcarpa f. swinglei, commonly known as the Swingle's miniature mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in small, white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a potted plant.
