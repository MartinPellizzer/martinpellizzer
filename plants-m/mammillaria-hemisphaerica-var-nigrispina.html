Mammillaria hemisphaerica var. nigrispina

Mammillaria hemisphaerica var. nigrispina, commonly known as the Black Spine Mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a distinctive dark purple to black coloration on its spines, which sets it apart from other varieties. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for cultivation in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
