Mesembryanthemum laurifolium

Mesembryanthemum laurifolium, commonly known as the evergreen ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring and summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
