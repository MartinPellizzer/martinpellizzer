Mentha balearica

Mentha balearica, commonly known as the Balearic mint, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a serrated margin. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stem. Mentha balearica is known for its aromatic, cool scent and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.

It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.
