Manga acida

Manga acida, commonly known as sour apple, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree that produces small, sour-tasting fruits. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval or elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications due to its acidic properties.
