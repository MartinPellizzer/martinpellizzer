Miltonia endresii

Miltonia endresii, commonly known as the Endres's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in controlled environments.
