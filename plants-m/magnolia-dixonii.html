Magnolia dixonii

Magnolia dixonii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a rare and ornamental tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in late spring, often in shades of white and pink. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth, dark green surface. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued in horticulture and conservation efforts.
