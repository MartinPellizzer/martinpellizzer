Megabotrya meliifolia

Megabotrya meliifolia, commonly known as the fragrant honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a dark purple color.

Megabotrya meliifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
