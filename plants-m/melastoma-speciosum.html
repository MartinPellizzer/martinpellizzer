Melastoma speciosum

Melastoma speciosum, commonly known as the beautiful melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
