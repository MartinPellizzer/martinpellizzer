Mandevilla clandestina

Mandevilla clandestina, commonly known as the hidden mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces waxy, elliptical leaves that are typically a deep green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla clandestina is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
