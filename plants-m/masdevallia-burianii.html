Masdevallia burianii

Masdevallia burianii, commonly known as the Burian's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth.

Masdevallia burianii is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively compact size.
