Manihot esculenta var. mutabilis

Manihot esculenta var. mutabilis, commonly known as the African yam bean, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are rich in protein and starch. It is cultivated for both its seeds and the tubers that develop from its roots.
