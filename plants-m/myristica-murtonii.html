Myristica murtonii

Myristica murtonii, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody bark and compound leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits. Its seeds, known as nutmeg, are enclosed in a hard, aromatic shell and are highly valued for their spice and medicinal properties.

The plant is also noted for its essential oils, which contribute to its distinctive fragrance and therapeutic uses.
