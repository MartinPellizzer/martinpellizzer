Marshallia williamsonii

Marshallia williamsonii, commonly known as Williamson's coneflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces fluffy, seed-filled cones that are distinctive in appearance.

Marshallia williamsonii is a perennial species that blooms from late spring to early summer.
