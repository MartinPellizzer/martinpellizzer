Microlicia euphorbioides var. parvifolia

Microlicia euphorbioides var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved microlicia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features small, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy green appearance. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers, which are followed by small, round fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the Euphorbiaceae family.
