Menodora intricata

Menodora intricata, commonly known as the intricate menodora, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Menodoridae and is characterized by its complex, often branched stems. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough, hairy texture.

Menodora intricata is noted for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
