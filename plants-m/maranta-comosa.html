Maranta comosa

Maranta comosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage with intricate patterns of green and silver. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a starch source.
