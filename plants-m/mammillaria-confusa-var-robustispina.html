Mammillaria confusa var. robustispina

Mammillaria confusa var. robustispina, commonly known as the robust spined mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in prominent, spiny tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental spines make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
