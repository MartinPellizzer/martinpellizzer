Mesembryanthemum sordidum

Mesembryanthemum sordidum, commonly known as the dirty ice plant, is a succulent flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Mesembryanthemum sordidum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and drought tolerance.
