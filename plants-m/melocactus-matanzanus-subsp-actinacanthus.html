Melocactus matanzanus subsp. actinacanthus

Melocactus matanzanus subsp.

actinacanthus, commonly known as the Matanzan melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and prominent, spiny areoles.

The plant produces large, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in the evening. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its slow growth and unique appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
