Megalastrum

Megalastrum, commonly known as the giant starflower, is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its showy, composite flower heads.

It is native to South America and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features a woody stem with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in dense, star-like clusters.

Megalastrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
