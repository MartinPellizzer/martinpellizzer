Medicago sativa var. versicolor

Medicago sativa var. versicolor, commonly known as the purple medic, is a variant of the common medick, characterized by its striking purple flowers.

It is a low-growing perennial legume that thrives in temperate climates, often found in meadows and disturbed soils. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pea-like pods containing seeds. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful forage crop.

Its vibrant color makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening as well.
