Murraya burmanni

Murraya burmanni, commonly known as the Burmann's murraya, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its fragrant, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its leaves and fruits have been used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
