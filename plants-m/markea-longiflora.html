Markea longiflora

Markea longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered markea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated inflorescences that bear numerous small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silky pubescence, while its roots are known for their medicinal properties.

Markea longiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic benefits.
