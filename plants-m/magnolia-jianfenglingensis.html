Magnolia jianfenglingensis

Magnolia jianfenglingensis, commonly known as the Hainan magnolia, is a rare and endemic species native to the island of Hainan in China.

It is a deciduous tree characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with distinct vertical lenticels.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also studied for its ecological significance in its native habitat.
