Menodora scabra var. laevis

Menodora scabra var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth menodora, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Menodoreaceae and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and alternate, palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are typically clustered in branched inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The species is noted for its medicinal properties, with various parts used in traditional herbal remedies.
