Mibora verna var. elongata

Mibora verna var. elongata, commonly known as the elongated mibora, is a flowering plant native to specific regions in South America.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a woody stem and opposite leaf arrangement.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
