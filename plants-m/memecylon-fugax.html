Memecylon fugax

Memecylon fugax, commonly known as the quick-growing memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves that are often glandular-dotted. Due to its rapid growth rate, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
