Mesembryanthemum luederitzii

Mesembryanthemum luederitzii, commonly known as the Luederitz's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in winter. It is a member of the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptations to dry climates.
