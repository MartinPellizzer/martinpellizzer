Miconia krugii

Miconia krugii, commonly known as the Krug's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some tropical gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
