Microstachys polymorpha

Microstachys polymorpha, commonly known as the variable spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its variable growth form, which can range from herbaceous to shrub-like, depending on environmental conditions. The plant typically has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. Its flowering period varies with the season, and it is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Microstachys polymorpha is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to different climates.
