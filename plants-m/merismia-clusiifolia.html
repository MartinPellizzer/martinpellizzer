Merismia clusiifolia

Merismia clusiifolia, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptical in shape. It belongs to the family Clusiaceae, also known as the guttiferae family.

Merismia clusiifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
