Maxillaria stachyobiorum

Maxillaria stachyobiorum, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically feature a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
