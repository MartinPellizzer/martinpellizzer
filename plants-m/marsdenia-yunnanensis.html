Marsdenia yunnanensis

Its leaves are alternate, simple, and usually have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in its native arid or semi-arid environment.

Marsdenia yunnanensis is valued not only for its ornamental appeal but also for its traditional uses in Chinese medicine.
