Morinda sandwicensis var. hosakae

Morinda sandwicensis var. hosakae, commonly known as the Hawaiian morinda, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland areas. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a reddish or pinkish hue.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
