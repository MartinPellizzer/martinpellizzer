Maurocenia frangularia

Maurocenia frangularia, commonly known as the yellow bell, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in coastal areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
