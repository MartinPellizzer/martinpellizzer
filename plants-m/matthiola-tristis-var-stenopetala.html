Matthiola tristis var. stenopetala

Matthiola tristis var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-petaled stock, is a herbaceous annual plant native to southern Europe.

It features slender, branching stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in disturbed habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and historical use in perfumery.
