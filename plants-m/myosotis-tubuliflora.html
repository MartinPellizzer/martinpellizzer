Myosotis tubuliflora

Myosotis tubuliflora, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to New Zealand.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, blue to purple flowers with five petals. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins and stream banks.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
