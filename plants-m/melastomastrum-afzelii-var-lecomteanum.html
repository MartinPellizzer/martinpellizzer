Melastomastrum afzelii var. lecomteanum

Melastomastrum afzelii var. lecomteanum, commonly known as the Lecomte's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family, characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are usually clustered in cymes and feature five petals, with a distinctive calyx that persists after flowering.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
