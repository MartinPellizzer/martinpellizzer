Melastoma pulverulentum

Melastoma pulverulentum, commonly known as the dusty melastoma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough, sometimes hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its fruit is a capsule that releases small, wind-dispersed seeds.
