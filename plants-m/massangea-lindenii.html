Massangea lindenii

Massangea lindenii, commonly known as the Lindberg's massanga, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to the trunks of trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, Massangea lindenii is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
