Magnolia figo

Magnolia figo, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, and it prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

The botanical features of Magnolia figo make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.
