Millettia elliptica

Millettia elliptica, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It features pinnate leaves with elliptical leaflets and produces clusters of fragrant, pea-like flowers in shades of purple and white. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Millettia elliptica is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
