Melastoma hombronianum

Melastoma hombronianum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered red or pink flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It has small, elliptical leaves with a smooth texture and is typically shrub-like in growth habit.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
