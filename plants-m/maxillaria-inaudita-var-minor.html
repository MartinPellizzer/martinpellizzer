Maxillaria inaudita var. minor

Maxillaria inaudita var. minor, commonly known as the miniature maxillaria, is a rare orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and challenging species for cultivation.
