Magnolia fordiana var. calcarea

Magnolia fordiana var. calcarea, commonly known as the limestone magnolia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in calcareous soils, which is reflected in its scientific name. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a slightly waxy appearance.

This variety is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with specific soil and climate conditions.
