Montanoa gigas

Montanoa gigas, commonly known as the giant montanoa, is a striking member of the Asteraceae family native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its arid habitat.

Montanoa gigas is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
