Madhuca amicorum

Madhuca amicorum, commonly known as the friendly tree, is a tropical evergreen species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, which is used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
