Maxillaria Adendrobium

Maxillaria adendrobium is a rare and captivating orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. This epiphytic plant is known for its striking floral display, featuring vibrant pink and white petals that resemble delicate butterflies. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture levels. Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are essential to protect this species from habitat loss and over-collection.
