Medicago daghestanica

Medicago daghestanica, commonly known as the Daghestan clover, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is native to the Caucasus region, particularly in the Republic of Dagestan in Russia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassy areas and along roadsides.

Medicago daghestanica is notable for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
