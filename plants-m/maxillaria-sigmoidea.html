Maxillaria sigmoidea

Maxillaria sigmoidea, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped flower stems. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinct three-lobed lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria sigmoidea is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
