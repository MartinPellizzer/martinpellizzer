Macrothelypteris sumatrana

Macrothelypteris sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran shield fern, is a large, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, shield-shaped fronds that can grow up to two meters in length. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

This fern is often found in tropical rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appearance and ecological significance.
