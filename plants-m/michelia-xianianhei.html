Michelia xianianhei

Michelia xianianhei, commonly known as the Xianianhei Michelia, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, blackish berries that are edible but not widely consumed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its aromatic properties.
