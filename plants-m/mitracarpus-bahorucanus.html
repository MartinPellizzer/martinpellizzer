Mitracarpus bahorucanus

Mitracarpus bahorucanus, commonly known as the Madagascar star bean, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in dry, rocky habitats.
