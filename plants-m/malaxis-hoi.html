Malaxis hoi

Malaxis hoi, commonly known as the Hoi's lady's tresses, is an epiphytic orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and intricate, spiraled inflorescence of small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted for absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air and substrate.

Malaxis hoi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
