Mucuna melanocarpa

Mucuna melanocarpa, commonly known as the black urd or black velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark purple to black pods that contain small, yellow seeds. The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in agricultural systems. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate and hairy.

The plant also produces a distinctive, sweet-smelling odor, which can attract certain pollinators.
