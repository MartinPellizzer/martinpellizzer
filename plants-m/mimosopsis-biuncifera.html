Mimosopsis biuncifera

Mimosopsis biuncifera, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mimosopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its seeds are enclosed in pods that split open when mature, dispersing the seeds through the wind.

Mimosopsis biuncifera is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
