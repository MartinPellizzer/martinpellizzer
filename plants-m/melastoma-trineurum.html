Melastoma trineurum

Melastoma trineurum, commonly known as the purple flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. These flowers are followed by bright red or orange berries that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to grow in a variety of soil types.
