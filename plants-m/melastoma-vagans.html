Melastoma vagans

Melastoma vagans, commonly known as the wandering melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red, floral bracts. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as tropical forests and is often found in undergrowth. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Melastoma vagans produces small, clustered flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
