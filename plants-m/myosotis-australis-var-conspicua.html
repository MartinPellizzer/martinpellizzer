Myosotis australis var. conspicua

Myosotis australis var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking blue to purple flowers, which are typically larger and more showy than those of the typical variety. The plant produces small, heart-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Myosotis australis var. conspicua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
