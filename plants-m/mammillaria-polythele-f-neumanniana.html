Mammillaria polythele f. neumanniana

Mammillaria polythele f. neumanniana, commonly known as the Neumann's mammillaria, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly spines and short, cylindrical ribs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a potted plant.
