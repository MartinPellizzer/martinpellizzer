Microtrichia

Microtrichia, commonly known as the "spider plant," is a member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is a perennial evergreen plant characterized by its rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Microtrichia is native to South Africa and thrives in well-drained soil with moderate sunlight.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its hardiness and ability to propagate easily through offsets.
