Mammillaria martiana

Mammillaria martiana, commonly known as the Martian Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
