Melandrium oldhamianum f. album

Melandrium oldhamianum f. album, commonly known as the white-flowered oldham's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has hairy, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.
