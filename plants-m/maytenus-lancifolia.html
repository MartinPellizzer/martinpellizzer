Maytenus lancifolia

Maytenus lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features lanceolate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its branches. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody, elongated fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in lowland forests.

Maytenus lancifolia is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
