Mammillaria macracantha var. retrocurva

Mammillaria macracantha var. retrocurva, commonly known as the hooked mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, white radial spines and smaller, pale blue to white radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and container growing due to its slow growth and ornamental appearance.

Its unique spine structure and compact form make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
