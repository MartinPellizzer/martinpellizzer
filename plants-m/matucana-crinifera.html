Matucana crinifera

Matucana crinifera, commonly known as the lace fern, is a striking epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a lacy appearance, with each frond composed of numerous small, overlapping pinnae. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth. Its unique morphology makes it a popular choice for ornamental cultivation in terrariums and greenhouses.

Matucana crinifera is also valued for its ability to purify the air and enhance the aesthetic appeal of indoor spaces.
