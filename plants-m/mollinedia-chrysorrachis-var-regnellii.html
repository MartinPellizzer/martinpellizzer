Mollinedia chrysorrachis var. regnellii

Mollinedia chrysorrachis var. regnellii, commonly known as the golden feather fern, is a unique variant of the golden feather plant.

It is a perennial fern native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its finely divided, feathery fronds that give it a delicate and ornamental appearance. Its foliage is typically bright green with a slight golden hue, especially when exposed to bright light.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
