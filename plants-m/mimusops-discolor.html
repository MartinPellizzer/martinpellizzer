Mimusops discolor

Mimusops discolor, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs, hence its common name. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

Mimusops discolor is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty in horticulture.
