Muhlenbergia × breviaristata

Muhlenbergia × breviaristata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is a hybrid between Muhlenbergia capillaris and Muhlenbergia rigida, characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often pink or reddish in color, adding ornamental value to gardens.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it a suitable choice for native plant gardens and erosion control.
