Muraltia mutabilis

Muraltia mutabilis, commonly known as the variable muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, and it is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Muraltia mutabilis thrives in well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid climate for optimal growth.
