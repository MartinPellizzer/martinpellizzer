Magnolia conspicua var. speciosa

Magnolia conspicua var. speciosa, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall, with a rounded crown and smooth, gray bark.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and parks.
