Mitrephora vandiflora var. chartacea

Mitrephora vandiflora var. chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped, papery leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. Its foliage is highly valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.

The species is also noted for its potential in ecological restoration due to its ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
