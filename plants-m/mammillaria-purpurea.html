Mammillaria purpurea

Mammillaria purpurea, commonly known as the purple globular cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact growth habit and a pale green to reddish body. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in early summer.

Mammillaria purpurea is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its slow growth and low maintenance requirements.
