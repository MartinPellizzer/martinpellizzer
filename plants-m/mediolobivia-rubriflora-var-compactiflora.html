Mediolobivia rubriflora var. compactiflora

Mediolobivia rubriflora var. compactiflora, commonly known as the Crimson Compact Lobivia, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It features dense, rounded stems covered in short, pale green spines. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the summer months, which bloom in clusters at the stem tips. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
