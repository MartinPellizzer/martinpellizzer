Mediolobivia orurensis

Mediolobivia orurensis, commonly known as the golden spire cactus, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a tall, cylindrical stem covered in dense, golden-yellow spines that provide protection from herbivores and excessive sunlight. The plant produces small, white flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. Its root system is adapted to absorb moisture efficiently from the dry soil, allowing it to thrive in harsh environments.

Mediolobivia orurensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
