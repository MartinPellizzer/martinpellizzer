Microstylis stolzii

Microstylis stolzii, commonly known as the slender-styled grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, narrow leaves that are typically green in color. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the grass family. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for ornamental purposes.
