Magnolia chiguila

Magnolia chiguila, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. Its flowers emit a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
