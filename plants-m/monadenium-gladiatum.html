Monadenium gladiatum

Monadenium gladiatum, commonly known as the swordleaf candelabra, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, sword-shaped leaves and clustered, yellow-orange flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Monadenium gladiatum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine in some regions.
