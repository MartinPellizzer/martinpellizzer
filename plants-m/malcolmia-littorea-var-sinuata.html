Malcolmia littorea var. sinuata

Malcolmia littorea var. sinuata, commonly known as the Sinuous Malva, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with soft, hairy stems and lobed, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to cream-colored flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and rockeries.
