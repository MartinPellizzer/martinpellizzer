Maerua stuhlmannii

Maerua stuhlmannii, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid regions.

Maerua stuhlmannii is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in erosion-prone areas.
