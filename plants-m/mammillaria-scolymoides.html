Mammillaria scolymoides

Mammillaria scolymoides, commonly known as the Mexican pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and appear in the axils of the areoles.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
