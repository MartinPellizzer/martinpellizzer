Millettia klainei var. floribunda

Millettia klainei var. floribunda, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking clusters of yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it suitable for warm climates.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant blossoms and rapid growth habit.
