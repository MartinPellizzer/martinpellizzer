Mikania panamensis

Mikania panamensis, commonly known as Panama morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy and twining.

Mikania panamensis is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
