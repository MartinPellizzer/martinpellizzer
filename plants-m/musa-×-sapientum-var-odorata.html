Musa × sapientum var. odorata

Musa × sapientum var. odorata, commonly known as the fragrant banana, is a cultivar of the banana plant known for its aromatic leaves and fruit.

This variant is characterized by its distinctive scent, which is more pronounced than in typical banana varieties. The plant produces large, elongated leaves that are broad and often have a waxy texture. Its fruit is typically smaller and less sweet compared to common bananas, but it is valued for its fragrance.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight for optimal growth.
