Monnina lorenziana

Monnina lorenziana, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.
