Mammillaria pfeifferiana var. fulvispina

Mammillaria pfeifferiana var. fulvispina, commonly known as the golden spine mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems and dense, white to golden spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and compact growth habit.
