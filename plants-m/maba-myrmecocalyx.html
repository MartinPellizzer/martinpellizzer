Maba myrmecocalyx

Maba myrmecocalyx, commonly known as the black mamba tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite and elliptic, with a glossy dark green surface.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood, which has been used traditionally in construction and crafts.
