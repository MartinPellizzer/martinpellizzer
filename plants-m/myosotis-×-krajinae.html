Myosotis × krajinae

Myosotis × krajinae, commonly known as the Krajina forget-me-not, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Myosotis sylvatica and Myosotis laxa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically reaches a height of 10–30 cm and has hairy stems and leaves with a rough texture. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their beauty and short blooming period.
