Melastoma venosum

Melastoma venosum, commonly known as the veiny melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma venosum is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
