Mimosa puberula

Mimosa puberula, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and small, delicate yellow flowers. The plant produces oval, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland areas.

Mimosa puberula is known for its sensitivity to touch, as its leaves fold shut when disturbed.
