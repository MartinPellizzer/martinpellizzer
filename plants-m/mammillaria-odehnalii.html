Mammillaria odehnalii

Mammillaria odehnalii, commonly known as the Odehnalii mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts due to its ornamental appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.

Mammillaria odehnalii thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.
