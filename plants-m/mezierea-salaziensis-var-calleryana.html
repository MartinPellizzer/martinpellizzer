Mezierea salaziensis var. calleryana

Mezierea salaziensis var. calleryana, commonly known as the Chinese honeylocust, is a deciduous legume native to eastern Asia.

It features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets and produces small, yellowish flowers in clusters. The plant is characterized by its long, slender pods that contain edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental landscaping.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions due to its rapid growth and prolific seed production.
