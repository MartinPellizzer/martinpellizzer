Mimosa foliolosa var. vernicosa

Mimosa foliolosa var. vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil conditions contribute to its adaptability in different climates.
