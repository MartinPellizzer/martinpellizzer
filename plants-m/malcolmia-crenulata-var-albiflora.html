Malcolmia crenulata var. albiflora

Malcolmia crenulata var. albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with succulent, fleshy leaves that are often lobed or toothed. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late spring to early summer. Its flowering period makes it a notable species in gardens and natural habitats.

It is adapted to dry conditions and thrives in well-drained soils.
