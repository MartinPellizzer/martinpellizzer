Mimulus brandegeei

Mimulus brandegeei, commonly known as the Brandegge's monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, orange-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many areas.
