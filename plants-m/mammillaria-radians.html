Mammillaria radians

Mammillaria radians, commonly known as the radiate mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, white woolly covering that covers the stems and flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, and they bloom in the spring.

Mammillaria radians is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
