Mimosa kuhnisteroides

Mimosa kuhnisteroides, commonly known as the dwarf mimosa, is a small, deciduous shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are often clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and sensitivity to touch, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
