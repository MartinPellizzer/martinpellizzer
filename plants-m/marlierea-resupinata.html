Marlierea resupinata

Marlierea resupinata, commonly known as the creeping marlierea, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
