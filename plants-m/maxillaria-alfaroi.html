Maxillaria alfaroi

Maxillaria alfaroi, commonly known as the Alfaroi orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria alfaroi is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
