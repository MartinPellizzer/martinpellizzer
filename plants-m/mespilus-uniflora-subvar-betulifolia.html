Mespilus uniflora subvar. betulifolia

Mespilus uniflora subvar. betulifolia, commonly known as the birchleaf medlar, is a rare and ancient plant species native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers in early spring, followed by edible, apple-like fruits that ripen in the fall.
