Moya boliviana

Moya boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian moya, is a striking plant native to the Andean regions of Bolivia.

It is a flowering shrub that belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades. The plant is characterized by its vibrant purple and white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically ovate and covered in a fine layer of white pubescence.

Moya boliviana is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
