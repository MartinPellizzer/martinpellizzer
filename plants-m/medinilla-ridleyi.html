Medinilla ridleyi

Medinilla ridleyi, commonly known as the Ridley's Medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with fleshy, tubular bracts that resemble petals. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a glossy appearance.

Medinilla ridleyi is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant floral display and compact growth habit.
