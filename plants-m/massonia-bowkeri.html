Massonia bowkeri

Massonia bowkeri, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a succulent shrub, with fleshy, narrow leaves that help it retain water in arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Massonia bowkeri is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
