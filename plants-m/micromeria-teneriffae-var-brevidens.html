Micromeria teneriffae var. brevidens

Micromeria teneriffae var. brevidens, commonly known as the Canary Islands micromeria, is a rare and endemic plant native to Tenerife.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky, arid environments. The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of pale yellow flowers. It is adapted to the harsh conditions of its native habitat, showing resilience to drought and high temperatures.
