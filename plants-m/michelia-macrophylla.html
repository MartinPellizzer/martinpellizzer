Michelia macrophylla

Michelia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf michelia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its large, ovate leaves that can grow up to 20 centimeters in length. Michelia macrophylla prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden environments.
