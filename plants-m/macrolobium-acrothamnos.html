Macrolobium acrothamnos

Macrolobium acrothamnos, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
