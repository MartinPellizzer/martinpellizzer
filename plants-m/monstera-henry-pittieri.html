Monstera henry-pittieri

Monstera henry-pittieri, commonly known as the Henry Pittieri Monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive fenestrations as they mature. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its stems are thick and vine-like, allowing it to climb and spread across supports.

It is a popular houseplant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
