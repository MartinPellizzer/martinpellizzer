Mauritiella nannostachys

Mauritiella nannostachys, commonly known as the dwarf palm, is a small, ornamental palm native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It features a slender, unbranched trunk that is often covered in a fibrous sheath, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish-white flowers, followed by dark blue, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.

Due to its compact size and attractive foliage, it is a popular choice for container gardening and indoor spaces.
