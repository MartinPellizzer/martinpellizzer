Mimulus orizabae

It belongs to the family Phrymaceae and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces seed capsules that release small, winged seeds.
