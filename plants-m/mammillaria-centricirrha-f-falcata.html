Mammillaria centricirrha f. falcata

Mammillaria centricirrha f. falcata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, tuberculate ribs and a pale green to bluish-gray coloration. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-draining soil with minimal water.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
