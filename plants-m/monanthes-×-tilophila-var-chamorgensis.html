Monanthes × tilophila var. chamorgensis

Monanthes × tilophila var. chamorgensis, commonly known as the Chamorro orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the Pacific islands, particularly Guam.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has strap-like leaves and produces clusters of small, delicate blossoms. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and cultural significance in the region.
