Mentha trachiloma

Mentha trachiloma, commonly known as the African mint, is a perennial herb native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its square stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorls along the stems. Mentha trachiloma is known for its aromatic, menthol-like scent and is often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.
