Morinda undulata

Morinda undulata, commonly known as the wavy morinda, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves with wavy margins, which give it its common name. Morinda undulata is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
