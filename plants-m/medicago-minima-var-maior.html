Medicago minima var. maior

Medicago minima var. maior, commonly known as the greater burr medic.

This plant is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. It thrives in dry, disturbed soils and is often found in grasslands and along roadsides. The plant produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It is a hardy perennial that plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
