Meliosma panamensis

Meliosma panamensis, commonly known as the Panamanian meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to Central America, particularly Panama, and is typically found in humid lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with a distinctive, often fragrant aroma. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or yellowish in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
