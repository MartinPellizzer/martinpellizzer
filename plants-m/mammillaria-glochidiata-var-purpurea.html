Mammillaria glochidiata var. purpurea

Mammillaria glochidiata var. purpurea, commonly known as the Purple Pincushion Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant features a compact growth habit and produces yellow flowers in the spring. Its stems are typically covered in small, rounded tubercles, giving it a pincushion-like appearance.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
