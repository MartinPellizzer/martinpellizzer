Mammillaria bucareliensis

Mammillaria bucareliensis, commonly known as the Bucareli milk barrel cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
