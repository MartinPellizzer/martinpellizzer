Memecylon memoratum

Memecylon memoratum, commonly known as the yellow memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
