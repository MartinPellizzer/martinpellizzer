Mahonia monyulensis

Mahonia monyulensis, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a serrated edge. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in late spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil.

Mahonia monyulensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
