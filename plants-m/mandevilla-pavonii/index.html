Mandevilla Pavonii

Mandevilla pavonii is a striking tropical vine known for its vibrant, showy flowers. Native to South America, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires plenty of sunlight. Its trumpet-shaped blooms come in shades of pink, red, and white, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Easy to care for, it's ideal for both novice and experienced gardeners. With proper pruning and support, Mandevilla pavonii can grow into a stunning, flowering spectacle.
