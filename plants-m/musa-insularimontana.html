Musa insularimontana

Musa insularimontana, commonly known as the mountain banana, is a species of banana plant native to the Philippines.

It is a herbaceous plant that grows in tropical climates, characterized by its large, broad leaves and clustered flowers. The plant produces edible fruit that is typically smaller and less sweet compared to common bananas. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for cultivation, it is sometimes grown in gardens and botanical collections.
