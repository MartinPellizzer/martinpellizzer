Miconia iodopila

Miconia iodopila, commonly known as the purple-leaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often a striking purple or reddish color. Its showy, pinkish-white flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom during the wet season.

Miconia iodopila is often found in humid, lowland forests and can grow up to several meters in height.
