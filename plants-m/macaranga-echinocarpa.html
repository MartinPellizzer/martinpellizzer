Macaranga echinocarpa

Macaranga echinocarpa, commonly known as the spiny macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. The species is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open spaces.
