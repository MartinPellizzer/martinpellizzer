Megalastrum eugenii

Megalastrum eugenii, commonly known as the giant star vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its long, flexible stems and large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical gardens.
