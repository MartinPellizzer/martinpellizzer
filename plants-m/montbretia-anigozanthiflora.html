Montbretia anigozanthiflora

Montbretia anigozanthiflora, commonly known as the African daffodil, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features bright orange to yellow flowers that resemble daffodils, with narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces a tall stem with clustered blooms, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is complemented by its ability to naturalize and spread easily in suitable climates.
