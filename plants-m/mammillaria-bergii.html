Mammillaria bergii

Mammillaria bergii, commonly known as the Berg's hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and gardeners.
