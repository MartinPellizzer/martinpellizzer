Maxillaria valerioi

Maxillaria valerioi, commonly known as the Valerio's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has dark green, elliptical leaves and can thrive in shaded to semi-shaded environments. It prefers well-draining soil and moderate humidity, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Maxillaria valerioi is often appreciated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
