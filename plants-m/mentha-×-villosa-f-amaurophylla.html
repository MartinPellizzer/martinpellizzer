Mentha × villosa f. amaurophylla

Mentha × villosa f. amaurophylla, commonly known as the purple mint, is a rare and ornamental variant of the mint family.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple foliage, which sets it apart from typical green mints. This plant is a hybrid, originating from crosses between Mentha × villosa and other Mentha species. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by plant enthusiasts.
