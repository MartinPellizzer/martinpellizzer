Masdevallia constricta f. glabra

Masdevallia constricta f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and its striking, velvety flowers that range in color from deep purples to rich reds. The plant's leaves are smooth and glabrous, which is a distinguishing feature of this particular form. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity and specific cultivation requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled greenhouse conditions.
