Melilotus × schoenheitianus

Melilotus × schoenheitianus, commonly known as yellow sweet clover, is a hybrid perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant has trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Yellow sweet clover is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability and historical use in agriculture and medicine.
