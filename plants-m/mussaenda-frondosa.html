Mussaenda frondosa

Mussaenda frondosa, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble clusters of small yellow flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface and a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Mussaenda frondosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
