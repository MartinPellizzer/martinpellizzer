Maranta lindeniana

Maranta lindeniana, commonly known as the Lindens' maranta, is a species of flowering plant in the family Marantaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage with intricate patterns and vibrant colors. It typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 30 cm.

Maranta lindeniana is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
