Mesembryanthemum acutipetalum

Mesembryanthemum acutipetalum, commonly known as the sharp-lobed ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

This hardy plant is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in rock gardens or as a ground cover in dry climates.
