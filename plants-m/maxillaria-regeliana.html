Maxillaria regeliana

Maxillaria regeliana, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a pseudobulb and a single, hairy leaf. The plant produces pendulous flowers with velvety petals and sepals, typically in shades of purple and white. It blooms during the dry season, often in response to specific environmental cues.

Maxillaria regeliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in greenhouses and tropical gardens.
