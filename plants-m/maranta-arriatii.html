Maranta arriatii

Maranta arriatii, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful foliage that changes with the seasons, often displaying shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes other tropical ornamentals known for their decorative leaves. Maranta arriatii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique aesthetic makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and shaded outdoor spaces.
