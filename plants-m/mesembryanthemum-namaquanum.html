Mesembryanthemum namaquanum

Mesembryanthemum namaquanum, commonly known as the Namib Desert ice plant, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Namibia and South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum namaquanum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in rock gardens and arid landscapes.
