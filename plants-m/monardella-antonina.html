Monardella antonina

Monardella antonina, commonly known as Antonina's monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features upright, branching stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, clustered in whorls along the stems, and bloom in late spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry meadows and open woodlands.
