Microcos brassii

Microcos brassii, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers with a distinctive brown center, which bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a bushy growth habit and produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
