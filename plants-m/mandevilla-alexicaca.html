Mandevilla alexicaca

Mandevilla alexicaca, commonly known as the scarlet mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has woody stems and is often cultivated as a trailing or climbing plant due to its flexible growth habit. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture, and it thrives in warm, sunny environments.

Mandevilla alexicaca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
