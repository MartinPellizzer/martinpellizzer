Millettia warneckei var. warneckei

Millettia warneckei var. warneckei, commonly known as the Warneckei millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a potential source of medicinal compounds and its ecological importance in tropical ecosystems.
