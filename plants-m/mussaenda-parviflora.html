Mussaenda parviflora

Mussaenda parviflora, commonly known as the small-flower mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often a deep green. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are highly fragrant. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Mussaenda parviflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
