Mitrasacme squamigera

Mitrasacme squamigera, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by triangular, winged fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands and along riverbanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
