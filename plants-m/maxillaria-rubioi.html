Maxillaria rubioi

Maxillaria rubioi, commonly known as the Red-veined Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive red-veined pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange with contrasting red veins, making it a striking ornamental plant.

Maxillaria rubioi is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
