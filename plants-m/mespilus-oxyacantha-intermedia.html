Mespilus oxyacantha intermedia

Mespilus oxyacantha intermedia, commonly known as the middle hybrid of bullace and blackthorn, is a botanical hybrid that exhibits traits of both parent species.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically found in temperate regions with well-drained soils. The plant produces small, dark purple berries that are often used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are ovate with serrated edges, and it flowers in early spring with white or pale pink blossoms.

This hybrid is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various environmental conditions.
