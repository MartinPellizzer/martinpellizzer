Mammillaria leucacantha

Mammillaria leucacantha, commonly known as the white-tipped hedgehog cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
