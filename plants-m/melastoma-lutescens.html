Melastoma lutescens

Melastoma lutescens, commonly known as the yellow melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its attractive yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and mountainous regions. Its leaves are ovate with a glossy surface, and the stems are often hairy.

Melastoma lutescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
