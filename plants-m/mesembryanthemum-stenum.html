Mesembryanthemum stenum

Mesembryanthemum stenum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to hot, dry climates. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in conditions where other plants would struggle.

Mesembryanthemum stenum is also known for its rapid growth and is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements.
