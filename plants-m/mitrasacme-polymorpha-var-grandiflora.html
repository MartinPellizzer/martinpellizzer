Mitrasacme polymorpha var. grandiflora

Mitrasacme polymorpha var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered mitrasacme, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species in botanical gardens and tropical plant collections.
