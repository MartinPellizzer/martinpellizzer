Manilkara sohihy

Manilkara sohihy, commonly known as the Sohihy sapodilla, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, elliptical leaves. The tree produces yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, sweet-sour fruits with a thick, starchy pulp. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
