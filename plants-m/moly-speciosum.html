Moly speciosum

Moly speciosum, commonly known as the showy moly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae, which includes plants known for their distinctive leaf structures and often edible parts. The plant features striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red or purple tint. Its leaves are typically broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Moly speciosum is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in cool, moist environments.
