Masdevallia heathii

Masdevallia heathii, commonly known as the Heath's Masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically arranged in a raceme. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires protection from direct sunlight.

Masdevallia heathii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
