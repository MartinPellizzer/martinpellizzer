Mollia nitida

Mollia nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, hairy leaves and showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Mollia nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and delicate blooms.
