Microlepia pilosissima

Microlepia pilosissima, commonly known as the hairy sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine in some regions.

The hairy sword fern is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological role in forest ecosystems.
