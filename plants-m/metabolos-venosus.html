Metabolos venosus

Metabolos venosus, commonly known as the Veinleaf Metabolos, is a unique plant species characterized by its intricate venation patterns that run throughout its foliage.

Its leaves are typically broad and lobed, with a deep green coloration that enhances its visual appeal. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions. It produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, which are typically white or pale yellow in color.

The botanical features of Metabolos venosus make it a subject of interest for both horticulturists and botanists.
