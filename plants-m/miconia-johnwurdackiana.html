Miconia johnwurdackiana

Miconia johnwurdackiana, commonly known as the Wurdack's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It typically grows as an understory plant in humid, shaded forest environments.
