Melastoma rubiginosum

Melastoma rubiginosum, commonly known as the red-stained melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a glossy surface, while its fruit is a capsule that disperses seeds through wind.

Melastoma rubiginosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
