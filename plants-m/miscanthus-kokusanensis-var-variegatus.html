Miscanthus kokusanensis var. variegatus

Miscanthus kokusanensis var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated Japanese silver grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to Japan.

It features narrow, silver-blue leaves with distinctive white and green variegation, creating a visually appealing contrast. The plant produces tall, feathery plumes in late summer, adding movement and texture to garden landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for perennial borders and container gardens.
