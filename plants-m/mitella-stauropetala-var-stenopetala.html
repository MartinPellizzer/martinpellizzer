Mitella stauropetala var. stenopetala

Mitella stauropetala var. stenopetala, commonly known as the narrow-leaved star-flower, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It features delicate, star-shaped white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often found in woodland gardens.
