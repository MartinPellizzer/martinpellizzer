Montia chamissoi

Montia chamissoi, commonly known as Chamisso's monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Mexico.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, often blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats, such as stream banks and meadows. Its leaves are usually alternate and have a slightly hairy texture.
