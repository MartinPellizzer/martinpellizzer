Merremia quinata

Merremia quinata, commonly known as the yellow wood vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Merremia quinata is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
