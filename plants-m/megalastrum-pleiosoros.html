Megalastrum pleiosoros

Megalastrum pleiosoros, commonly known as the giant orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. Native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the forest floor, and it often grows in association with other epiphytic plants.

The plant's botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and its role in the ecological diversity of its native habitat.
