Masdevallia polyantha

Masdevallia polyantha, commonly known as the pink masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous inflorescence and vibrant pink to purple flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces small, greenish-brown pseudobulbs.

Masdevallia polyantha is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
