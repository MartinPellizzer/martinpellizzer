Myristica korthalsii

Myristica korthalsii, commonly known as the Malabar nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the Western Ghats of India and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy fruits containing aromatic seeds. Myristica korthalsii is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional Ayurvedic medicine.

Its unique botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
