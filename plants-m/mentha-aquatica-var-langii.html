Mentha aquatica var. langii

Mentha aquatica var. langii, commonly known as water mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to moist habitats in Europe and parts of Asia.

It features opposite, broadly ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a strong mint scent.
