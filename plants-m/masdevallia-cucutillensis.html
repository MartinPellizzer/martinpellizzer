Masdevallia cucutillensis

Masdevallia cucutillensis, commonly known as the Cucutilla masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features linear, grass-like leaves and produces blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and is often found in cloud forests at high altitudes.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
