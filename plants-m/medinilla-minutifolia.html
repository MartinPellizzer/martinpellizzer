Medinilla minutifolia

Medinilla minutifolia, commonly known as the miniature medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptic, glossy leaves that are dark green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant.

Medinilla minutifolia is also valued for its ability to produce fruit, which is edible and has a unique flavor.
