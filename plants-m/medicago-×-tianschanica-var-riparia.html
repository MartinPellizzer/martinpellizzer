Medicago × tianschanica var. riparia

Medicago × tianschanica var. riparia, commonly known as the riparian medic, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It thrives in moist, sandy soils along riverbanks and other riparian habitats. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. It is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and prevent erosion in wetland areas.

Its seeds are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
