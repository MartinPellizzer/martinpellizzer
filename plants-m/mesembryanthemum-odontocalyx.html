Mesembryanthemum odontocalyx

Mesembryanthemum odontocalyx, commonly known as the toothed-lobed ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. Its leaves are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss.

Mesembryanthemum odontocalyx is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental value.
