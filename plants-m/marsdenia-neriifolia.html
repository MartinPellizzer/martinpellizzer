Marsdenia neriifolia

Marsdenia neriifolia, commonly known as the black-leafed Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, often glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and unique floral structure.

Marsdenia neriifolia thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.
