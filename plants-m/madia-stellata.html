Madia stellata

Madia stellata, commonly known as starry madia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical fruit capsules.

Madia stellata is often used in native plant gardens due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
