Musa exotica

Musa exotica, commonly known as the banana plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of berries, commonly referred to as bananas, which are rich in potassium and other nutrients. Musa exotica thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is widely cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.
