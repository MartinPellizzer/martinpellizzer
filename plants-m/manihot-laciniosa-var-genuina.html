Manihot laciniosa var. genuina

Manihot laciniosa var. genuina, commonly known as the pink arrowleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive pinkish or reddish coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
