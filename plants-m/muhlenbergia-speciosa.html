Muhlenbergia speciosa

Muhlenbergia speciosa, commonly known as the pink muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its graceful, arching foliage that turns a striking pinkish-red in the fall. The plant produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Muhlenbergia speciosa is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
