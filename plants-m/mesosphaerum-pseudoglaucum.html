Mesosphaerum pseudoglaucum

Mesosphaerum pseudoglaucum, commonly known as the blue-green mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a bluish-gray hue, while its flowers are small and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.
