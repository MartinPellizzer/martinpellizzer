Monimiopsis rivularis

Monimiopsis rivularis, commonly known as the river monimiopsis, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Monimiaceae family, characterized by its aromatic foliage and unique floral structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as riverbanks and forest understories. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, with a distinctive scent that attracts specific pollinators.
