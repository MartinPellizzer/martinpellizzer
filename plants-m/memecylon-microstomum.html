Memecylon microstomum

Memecylon microstomum, commonly known as the small-mouth memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by elliptical, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
