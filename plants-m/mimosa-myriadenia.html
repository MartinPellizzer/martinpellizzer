Mimosa myriadenia

Mimosa myriadenia, commonly known as the many-leafed mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically arranged in a pinnate pattern. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
