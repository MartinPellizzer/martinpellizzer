Mentha × Timbalii

Mentha × timbalii is a hybrid mint plant known for its unique combination of traits from its parent species. It is commonly used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its aromatic leaves and essential oils. This plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. Its versatility makes it a popular choice for gardens and herbalists alike. Mentha × timbalii continues to be a valued plant in both traditional and modern contexts.
