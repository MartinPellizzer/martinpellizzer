Momordica kirkii

Momordica kirkii, commonly known as African bitter melon, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins. The plant produces elongated, spiny fruits that are typically green when mature, though they can turn yellow or orange. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are yellow with five petals.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional African medicine for various health purposes.
