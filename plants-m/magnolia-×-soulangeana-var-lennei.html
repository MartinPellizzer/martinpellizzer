Magnolia × soulangeana var. lennei

Magnolia × soulangeana var. lennei, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or lennei magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant typically grows to a height of 8–15 meters, featuring smooth, gray bark and dark green, ovate leaves. Its flowers are usually pink to red, with a distinctive coloration that fades as they age.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in gardens and parks due to its striking floral display.
