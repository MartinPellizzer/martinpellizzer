Magnolia fargesii

Magnolia fargesii, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of western China.

It is characterized by its large, white to pale pink, showy flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
