Magnolia xinganensis

Magnolia xinganensis, commonly known as the Xingan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to northeastern China.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree has simple, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn, and its bark is smooth and grayish-brown. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local ecosystems.
