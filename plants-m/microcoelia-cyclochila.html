Microcoelia cyclochila

Microcoelia cyclochila, commonly known as the cyclochila orchid, is a species of flowering plant in the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Its growth habit is epiphytic, meaning it grows on the surface of trees or other plants without being parasitic.
