Medinilla clathrata

Medinilla clathrata, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf.

It is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its thick, velvety, waxy leaves that give it its common name. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink in color.

Medinilla clathrata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
