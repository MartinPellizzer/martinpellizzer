Melandrium angustiflorum subsp. tenellum

Melandrium angustiflorum subsp.

tenellum, commonly known as the narrow-flowered catchfly, is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family. It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright stems and small, clustered flowers.

The flowers are typically pink to reddish in color and bloom during the summer months. The plant has hairy leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and open habitats across Europe and parts of Asia.
