Muhlenbergia matudae

Muhlenbergia matudae, commonly known as Matudae muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as rocky slopes and grasslands. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils.

Muhlenbergia matudae is often used in xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal.
