Macaranga celebica

Macaranga celebica, commonly known as the Celebes macaranga, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a fast-growing, deciduous tree that can reach heights of up to 20 meters. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia.

The bark and leaves of Macaranga celebica have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
