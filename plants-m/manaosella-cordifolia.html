Manaosella cordifolia

Manaosella cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf arrowroot, is a flowering plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and striking inflorescence. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in a spadix, typical of the arum family. Its rhizomes are used in traditional medicine and as a starchy food source.
