Maesa aurea

Maesa aurea, commonly known as golden maesa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle ornamental value. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a leathery texture.

Maesa aurea is valued in traditional medicine for its potential medicinal properties.
