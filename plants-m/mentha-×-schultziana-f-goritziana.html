Mentha × schultziana f. goritziana

Mentha × schultziana f. goritziana, commonly known as the Goritziana mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly fuzzy leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue to purple in color, which appear in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its use in herbal teas and as a decorative plant in gardens.
