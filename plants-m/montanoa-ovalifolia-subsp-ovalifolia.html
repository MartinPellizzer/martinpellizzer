Montanoa ovalifolia subsp. ovalifolia

Montanoa ovalifolia subsp.

ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces showy yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which attract various pollinators. It typically thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This subspecies is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
