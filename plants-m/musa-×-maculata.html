Musa × maculata

Musa × maculata, commonly known as the spotted banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its striking variegated leaves that display a mix of green, yellow, and white patterns. The plant produces edible fruits, although they are typically smaller and less sweet compared to common bananas. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Musa × maculata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage.
