Metrosideros perforata

Metrosideros perforata, commonly known as the Tahitian Christmas tree, is a flowering plant native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the winter months, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits.
