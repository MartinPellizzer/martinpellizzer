Machaerium compressicaule var. manaoense

Machaerium compressicaule var. manaoense, commonly known as the Manaoense Machaerium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically arranged in dense inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as understorey forests and along riverbanks.
