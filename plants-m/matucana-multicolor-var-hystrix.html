Matucana multicolor var. hystrix

Matucana multicolor var. hystrix, commonly known as the zigzag peace lily, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves with prominent, zigzagging veins that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that appear on spadices, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Matucana multicolor var. hystrix is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance requirements.
