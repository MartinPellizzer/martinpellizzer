Malus laosensis

Malus laosensis, commonly known as the Laos apple, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Laos and Vietnam, and grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a smooth texture, while its flowers are white or pink and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, edible apple-like berry that is used in local cuisine and traditional medicine.
