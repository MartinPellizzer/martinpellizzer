Mallotus samarensis

Mallotus samarensis, commonly known as the Samarensis holly, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stems. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
