Mammillaria polythele var. quadrispina

Mammillaria polythele var. quadrispina, commonly known as the four-spined mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with radial spines and a dense covering of white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
