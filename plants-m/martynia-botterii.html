Martynia botterii

Martynia botterii, commonly known as the red-flowered morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
