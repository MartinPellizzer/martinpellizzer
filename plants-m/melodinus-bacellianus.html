Melodinus bacellianus

Melodinus bacellianus, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often found in open woodlands and grasslands. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
