Mikania microptera

Mikania microptera, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, opposite leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to violet, often appearing in clusters. Mikania microptera thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and invasive tendencies make it a significant weed in agricultural and natural habitats.
