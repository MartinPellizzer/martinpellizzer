Monilaria primosii

Monilaria primosii, commonly known as the slender leaf orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to the mountainous regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, pale green or white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories where it relies on specific mycorrhizal fungi for nutrient uptake. Its conservation status is vulnerable due to habitat loss and over-collection.

This orchid is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists due to its unique ecological requirements and limited distribution.
