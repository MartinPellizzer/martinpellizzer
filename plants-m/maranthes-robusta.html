Maranthes robusta

Maranthes robusta, commonly known as the robust maranthes, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences. The plant features strap-like, narrow leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. Its flowers, which bloom in summer, come in shades of white, pink, and red, often with a distinctive red or orange stripe down the center.

Maranthes robusta is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and hardy nature.
