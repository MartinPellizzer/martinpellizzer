Myristica clarkeana

Myristica clarkeana, commonly known as the Clarke's nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain a valuable seed. The plant produces a reddish-brown seed with a fragrant, oily kernel, similar to the spice nutmeg. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture.

The tree is typically found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
