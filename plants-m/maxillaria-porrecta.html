Maxillaria porrecta

Maxillaria porrecta, commonly known as the straight-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a unique, straight lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, warm environments. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with a prominent, elongated lip that distinguishes it from other Maxillaria species.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
