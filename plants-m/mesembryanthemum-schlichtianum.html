Mesembryanthemum schlichtianum

Mesembryanthemum schlichtianum, commonly known as the Schlicht's iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in drought conditions.
