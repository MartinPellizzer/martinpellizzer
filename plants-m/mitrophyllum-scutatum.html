Mitrophyllum scutatum

Mitrophyllum scutatum, commonly known as the shieldleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, showy yellow flowers surrounded by pale green to white ray florets. Its leaves are typically opposite, narrow, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
