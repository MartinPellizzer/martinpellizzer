Mosigia pinnatifida

Mosigia pinnatifida, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mosigia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its finely divided, pinnately arranged leaves that give it a delicate, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
