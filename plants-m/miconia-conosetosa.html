Miconia conosetosa

Miconia conosetosa, commonly known as the Hawaiian miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia conosetosa is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
