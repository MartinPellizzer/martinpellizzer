Monelasmum spinulososerratum

Monelasmum spinulososerratum, commonly known as the spiny serrated milkweed, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its hairy, elliptical leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant contains latex, which is often milky and can be toxic to some animals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland habitats.

Its unique appearance and ecological role make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
