Melanoseris stewartii

Melanoseris stewartii, commonly known as Stewart's melastome, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red or purple calyx. Its leaves are typically opposite and have a distinctive, velvety texture.

Melanoseris stewartii thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and thickets.
