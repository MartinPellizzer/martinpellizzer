Mallotus paxii

Mallotus paxii, commonly known as the white-flowered hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with prominent yellow stamens. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in coastal areas or near freshwater habitats.
