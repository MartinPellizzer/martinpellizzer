Melastoma acutisepalum

Melastoma acutisepalum, commonly known as the acute-sepal melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five sepals that are acute, hence the species name.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
