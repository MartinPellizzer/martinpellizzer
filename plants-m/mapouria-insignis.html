Mapouria insignis

Mapouria insignis, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant has compound leaves that are opposite and pinnate, with small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Mapouria insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can grow up to 15 meters tall.
