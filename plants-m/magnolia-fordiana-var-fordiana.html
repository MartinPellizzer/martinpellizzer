Magnolia fordiana var. fordiana

Magnolia fordiana var. fordiana, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture, turning yellow in autumn. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental qualities in gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
