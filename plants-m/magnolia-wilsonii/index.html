Magnolia Wilsonii

Magnolia wilsonii is a rare and striking flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China. Known for its large, showy white petals and fragrant blooms, it is a member of the Magnoliaceae family. This species is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and ancient plant species.
