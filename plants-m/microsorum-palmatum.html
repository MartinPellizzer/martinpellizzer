Microsorum palmatum

Microsorum palmatum, commonly known as the Japanese water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically green and often display a bluish tint. This plant thrives in slow-moving or still freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its rhizomes form dense mats on the substrate, helping to stabilize the sediment and provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.

Microsorum palmatum is popular in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in freshwater aquariums.
