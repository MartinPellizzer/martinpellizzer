Mariscus viridis

Mariscus viridis, commonly known as the green mariscus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a mild, aromatic scent.

Mariscus viridis is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
