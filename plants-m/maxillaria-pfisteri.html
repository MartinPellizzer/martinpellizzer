Maxillaria pfisteri

Maxillaria pfisteri, commonly known as the Pfister's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth during dry periods. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a yellow throat. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Maxillaria pfisteri is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
