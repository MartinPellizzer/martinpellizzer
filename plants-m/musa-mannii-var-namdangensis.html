Musa mannii var. namdangensis

Musa mannii var. namdangensis, commonly known as the Namdang banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered fruit bunches. The plant produces edible bananas that are typically smaller and sweeter than common commercial varieties. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil and requires ample sunlight.

Due to its unique flavor and limited cultivation, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
