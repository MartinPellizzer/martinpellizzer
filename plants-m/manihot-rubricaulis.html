Manihot rubricaulis

Manihot rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowleaf, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish tint on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
