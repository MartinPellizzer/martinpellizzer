Macropetalum burchellii var. grandiflorum

Macropetalum burchellii var. grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered moonviolet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, violet-blue flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
