Mimetes capitulatus

Mimetes capitulatus, commonly known as the Cape snowbush, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing structure with fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting to its arid environment. The plant produces showy, cone-shaped flower heads in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically blooming in winter. Its flowers are clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences, which give it the common name "snowbush.".

Mimetes capitulatus is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
