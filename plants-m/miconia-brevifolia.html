Miconia brevifolia

Miconia brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green leaves that are typically short and broad. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Miconia brevifolia is often found in humid, lowland forest environments.
