Moraea obtusa

Moraea obtusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and delicate, funnel-shaped flowers. Moraea obtusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is relatively low maintenance once established.
