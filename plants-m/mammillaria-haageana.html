Mammillaria haageana

Mammillaria haageana, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
