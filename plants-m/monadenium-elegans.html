Monadenium elegans

Monadenium elegans, commonly known as the elegant monadenium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a spike-like inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid environments.

The species is notable for its rapid growth cycle and ability to adapt to harsh climatic conditions.
