Mniopsis crulsiana

Mniopsis crulsiana, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant has a compact, low-growing structure with silvery-gray foliage that helps reduce water loss. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and limited rainfall.

Mniopsis crulsiana is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and resilience.
