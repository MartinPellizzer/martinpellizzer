Masdevallia morenoi

Masdevallia morenoi, commonly known as the Moreno's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its ornamental value in both greenhouse and cultivated settings. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, contributing to its overall aesthetic appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
