Maytenus pancheriana

Maytenus pancheriana, commonly known as the Pancheria mayten.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in clusters, and it produces small, red fruits that are edible.

Maytenus pancheriana is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
