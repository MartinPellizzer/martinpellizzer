Myrceugenia itatiaiensis

Myrceugenia itatiaiensis, commonly known as the Itatiaia myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of southeastern Brazil, where it thrives in humid, subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Itatiaia myrtle produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature, containing numerous seeds.
