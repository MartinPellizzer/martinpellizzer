Maxillaria Macrantha

Large-flowered maxillaria, scientifically known as Maxillaria macrantha, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America. This species is celebrated for its large, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, from white to pink and purple. It thrives in humid environments and is often found growing in shaded forest understories. The plant is admired by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for both collectors and gardeners.
