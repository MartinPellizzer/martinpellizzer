Miltonia regnellii f. travassosiana

Miltonia regnellii f. travassosiana, commonly known as the Travassosiana Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically produces blooms in shades of pink and white, with a contrasting yellow lip. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
