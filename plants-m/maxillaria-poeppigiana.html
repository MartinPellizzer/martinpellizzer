Maxillaria poeppigiana

Maxillaria poeppigiana, commonly known as the Poeppig's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria poeppigiana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
