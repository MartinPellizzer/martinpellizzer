Malacothrix arachnoidea

Malacothrix arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a rare and striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique, spider-like appearance, with long, slender inflorescences that bear small, intricate flowers. Native to the arid regions of southern Africa, this plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and full sun exposure. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow with dark markings, resembling the appearance of a spider.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, Malacothrix arachnoidea is considered a conservation concern.
