Myristica polyantha

Myristica polyantha, commonly known as the fragrant nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces unique fruits that contain a reddish-brown seed, which is valued for its essential oils and fragrance. Myristica polyantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both horticulture and perfumery.
