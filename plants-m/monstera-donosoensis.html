Monstera donosoensis

Monstera donosoensis, commonly known as the Donoso's monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, featuring a distinctive split or fenestrated pattern. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil. Its stems are long and vine-like, often requiring support as it climbs toward the canopy.

This species is prized for its unique foliage and is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
