Monstera karwinskyi

Monstera karwinskyi, commonly known as the Mexican giant shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant features a climbing habit and produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are typically deeply lobed, with a distinctive pattern of holes and notches.

It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
