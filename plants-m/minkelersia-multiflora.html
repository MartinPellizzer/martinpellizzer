Minkelersia multiflora

Minkelersia multiflora, commonly known as the mountain lily, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

It features slender, erect stems that bear clusters of showy, white or pale pink flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or mountainous terrain.

Due to its unique habitat and ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
