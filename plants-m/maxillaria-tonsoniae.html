Maxillaria tonsoniae

Maxillaria tonsoniae, commonly known as the Tonsone's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support broad, ovate leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. Its flowers typically exhibit a combination of white, yellow, and green hues, with a prominent lip.

Maxillaria tonsoniae is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique floral display.
