Monstera klotzschiana

Monstera klotzschiana, commonly known as the giant monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and requires a support structure to climb. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly glossy texture.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments.
