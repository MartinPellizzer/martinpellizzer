Millotia tenuifolia var. nudescens

Millotia tenuifolia var. nudescens, commonly known as the slender-leaved millotia, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, narrow leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its low-growing habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
