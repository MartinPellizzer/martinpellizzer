Manettia cordifolia var. glabra

Manettia cordifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth manettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its smooth, glabrous leaves are a distinguishing feature, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
