Metastelma ephedroides

Metastelma ephedroides, commonly known as the Chinese ephedra, is a small shrub native to arid regions of China.

It belongs to the family Ephedraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and scale-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers and is known for containing alkaloids, particularly ephedrine, which has medicinal uses. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to withstand extreme temperatures and limited water availability.

Due to its chemical composition, it has been historically used in traditional Chinese medicine.
