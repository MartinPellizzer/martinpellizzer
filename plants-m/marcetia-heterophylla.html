Marcetia heterophylla

Marcetia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf marsh orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, often variable leaf shapes. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually greenish-yellow with a faintly colored lip.

Marcetia heterophylla is noted for its adaptability to wetland environments and its role in local ecosystems.
