Malanea macrophylla

Malanea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaved malanea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
