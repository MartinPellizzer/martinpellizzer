Mesembryanthemum dekenahi

Mesembryanthemum dekenahi, commonly known as the Deken's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are adapted to store water, typical of plants in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, often blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
