Mikania carnosa

Mikania carnosa, commonly known as the hairy Mikania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features hairy stems and leaves, which contribute to its distinctive appearance. It produces small, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Mikania carnosa is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as along stream banks and in open woodlands.
