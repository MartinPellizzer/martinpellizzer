Maxillaria goeringii

Maxillaria goeringii, commonly known as the Goering's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria goeringii is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
