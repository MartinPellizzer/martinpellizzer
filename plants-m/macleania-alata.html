Macleania alata

Macleania alata, commonly known as the silverleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its unique foliage and delicate blooms make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
