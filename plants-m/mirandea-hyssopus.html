Mirandea hyssopus

Mirandea hyssopus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved hyssopus, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, tubular blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly serrated, often with a fragrant, mint-like aroma.

Mirandea hyssopus thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.
