Myosotis lithuanica

Myosotis lithuanica, commonly known as the Lithuanian forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to the forests of Lithuania.

It produces small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Myosotis lithuanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and its role in supporting local pollinators.
