Myosotis sylvatica subsp. subarvensis

Myosotis sylvatica subsp.

subarvensis, commonly known as woodland forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features slender stems with heart-shaped leaves and produces clusters of blue or violet flowers. Its flowers are bilaterally symmetrical, with five petals and a prominent central stamen.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
