Maranta mariae

Maranta mariae, commonly known as the Marie's prayer plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, variegated leaves that often display shades of red, pink, and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta mariae is popular among plant enthusiasts for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
