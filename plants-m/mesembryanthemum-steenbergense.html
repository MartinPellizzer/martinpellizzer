Mesembryanthemum steenbergense

Mesembryanthemum steenbergense, commonly known as the Steenberg's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It typically blooms in spring and summer, producing white or pale pink flowers.
