Mallotus oppositifolius var. genuinus

Mallotus oppositifolius var. genuinus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved holly, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
