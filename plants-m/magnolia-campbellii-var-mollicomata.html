Magnolia campbellii var. mollicomata

Magnolia campbellii var. mollicomata, commonly known as the Campbell's magnolia, is a rare and ornamental species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty collections.
