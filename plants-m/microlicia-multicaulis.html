Microlicia multicaulis

Microlicia multicaulis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant features velvety, waxy leaves that are often heart-shaped and dark green in color. Its showy, tubular flowers come in a variety of colors, including purple, pink, and white.

Microlicia multicaulis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
