Meliosma lepidota

Meliosma lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its finely textured, velvety leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported soothing properties.

Meliosma lepidota thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands and along stream banks.
