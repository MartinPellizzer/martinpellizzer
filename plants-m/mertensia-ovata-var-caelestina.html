Mertensia ovata var. caelestina

Mertensia ovata var. caelestina, commonly known as sky blue mistflower, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It features striking, sky-blue flower clusters that bloom in early spring, adding a vibrant touch to woodland gardens. The plant has lance-shaped, dark green leaves that are aromatic when crushed. It thrives in partial to full shade and moist, well-drained soil.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and role in supporting pollinators.
