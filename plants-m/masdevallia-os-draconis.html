Masdevallia os-draconis

Masdevallia os-draconis, commonly known as the Dragon's Breath Masdevallia, is a unique orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that resemble dragon-like features, hence its common name. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually a mix of red, orange, and yellow, creating a vivid contrast.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful attention to maintain its health and blooming cycle.
