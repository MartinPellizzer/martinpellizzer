Micromelum octandrum

Micromelum octandrum, commonly known as the small-flowered melicite, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, typically blooming during the wet season. Its fruit is a spherical drupe that contains a single seed, and it is often found in lowland rainforests.

Micromelum octandrum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
