Mammillaria laui subsp. dasyacantha

Mammillaria laui subsp.

dasyacantha, commonly known as the dense-spined mammillaria, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico. It features a compact, rounded stem covered in numerous short, white spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique spination and compact growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
