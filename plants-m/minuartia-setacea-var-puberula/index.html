Minuartia Setacea Var. Puberula

Minuartia setacea var. puberula is a small, flowering plant native to arid and rocky environments. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is known for its delicate, clustered blooms. This variant is distinguished by its soft, hairy texture and pale pink or white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to rock gardens. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and biodiversity.
