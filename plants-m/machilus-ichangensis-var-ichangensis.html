Machilus ichangensis var. ichangensis

Machilus ichangensis var. ichangensis, commonly known as the Ichang privet, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers in clusters, followed by oval, woody berries that are typically dark purple when mature.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
