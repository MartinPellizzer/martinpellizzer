Musa itinerans var. hainanensis

Musa itinerans var. hainanensis, commonly known as the Hainan banana, is a variant of the Musa genus native to southern China.

It is a herbaceous plant with large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellowish-white flowers followed by banana-like fruits. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring warm temperatures and high humidity.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential agricultural use.
