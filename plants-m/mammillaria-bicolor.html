Mammillaria bicolor

Mammillaria bicolor, commonly known as the white globular cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
