Myristica pilosigemma

Myristica pilosigemma, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and small, fragrant yellowish-white flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and its historical significance in the spice trade.
