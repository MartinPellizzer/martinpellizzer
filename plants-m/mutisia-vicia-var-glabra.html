Mutisia vicia var. glabra

Mutisia vicia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth mutisia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves, which are typically alternate and lanceolate in shape. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are often surrounded by pale green bracts. It is native to South America and thrives in moist, temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
