Mammillaria lassonneriei

Mammillaria lassonneriei, commonly known as the Lassonnerie's bunny ear cactus, is a small, compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with white to pale green spines and prominent areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
