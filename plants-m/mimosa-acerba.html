Mimosa acerba

Mimosa acerba, commonly known as the staghorn mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fern-like leaves and pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically growing up to 6 meters in height. Its bark is thick and fissured, giving it a rugged appearance.

Mimosa acerba is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
