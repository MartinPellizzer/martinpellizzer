Melastoma magnificum

Melastoma magnificum, commonly known as the magnificent melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma magnificum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
