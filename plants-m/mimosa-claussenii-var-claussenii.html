Mimosa claussenii var. claussenii

Mimosa claussenii var. claussenii, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, soft, gray-green foliage and its distinctive, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to the landscape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a desirable plant for coastal and sandy soils.
