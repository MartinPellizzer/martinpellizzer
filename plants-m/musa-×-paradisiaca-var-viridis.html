Musa × paradisiaca var. viridis

Musa × paradisiaca var. viridis, commonly known as the Green Banana Plant, is a variant of the banana plant characterized by its unique foliage.

The leaves are broad, lance-shaped, and typically have a vibrant green color, sometimes with a bluish tint. This variety is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value rather than for fruit production. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring high humidity and well-drained soil.

It is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add a lush, tropical feel to gardens.
