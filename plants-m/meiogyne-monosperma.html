Meiogyne monosperma

Meiogyne monosperma, commonly known as the African water lily, is a herbaceous aquatic plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It typically grows in shallow freshwater habitats such as ponds and marshes. The plant has broad, floating leaves that provide shade and support for aquatic life. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive scent that attracts pollinators.
