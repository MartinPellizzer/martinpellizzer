Manihot irwinii

Manihot irwinii, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are the primary part used, and they are known for their starchy, edible qualities.
