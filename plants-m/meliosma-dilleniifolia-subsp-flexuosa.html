Meliosma dilleniifolia subsp. flexuosa

Meliosma dilleniifolia subsp.

flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible meliosma, is a member of the Meliaceae family, characterized by its unique foliage and growth habits. It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

The plant features lanceolate, opposite leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, often displaying a striking green to reddish hue. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically arranged in clusters, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
