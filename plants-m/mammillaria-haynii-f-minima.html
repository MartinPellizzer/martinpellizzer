Mammillaria haynii f. minima

Mammillaria haynii f. minima, commonly known as the miniature mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, globular stem with a grey-green color and small, white to yellowish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its small size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
