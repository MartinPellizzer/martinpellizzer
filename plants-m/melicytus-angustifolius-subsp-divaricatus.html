Melicytus angustifolius subsp. divaricatus

Melicytus angustifolius subsp.

divaricatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tara, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its slender, often twisted trunk and dark green, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. It thrives in moist, temperate climates and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in native forests.
