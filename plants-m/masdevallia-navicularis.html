Masdevallia navicularis

Masdevallia navicularis, commonly known as the boat orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, boat-shaped pseudobulbs and slender, lanceolate leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its delicate appearance.
