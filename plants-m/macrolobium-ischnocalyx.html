Macrolobium ischnocalyx

Macrolobium ischnocalyx, commonly known as the narrow-leaved macrolobium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often arranged in clusters. Its woody stems and thick, leathery leaves help it survive in humid, tropical environments.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.
