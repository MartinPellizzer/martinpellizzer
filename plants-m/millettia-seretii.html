Millettia seretii

Millettia seretii, commonly known as the African senna, is a woody climbing plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods that contain small, flat seeds. It is often found in forest understories and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Millettia seretii is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
