Magnolia conifera var. chingii

Magnolia conifera var. chingii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or Ching's magnolia, is a rare and ornamental evergreen tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a glossy, dark green upper surface. The tree has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 15 meters in optimal conditions.

It is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
