Mentha × brachystachya var. stenodonta

Mentha × brachystachya var. stenodonta, commonly known as narrow-leaved water mint, is a hybrid plant in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers arranged in whorled clusters along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as stream banks and wet meadows.

Its aromatic foliage and medicinal properties make it a notable species in both horticulture and traditional herbal use.
