Millettia chrysophylla

Millettia chrysophylla, commonly known as the golden-leaf millettia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking golden-yellow leaves. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
