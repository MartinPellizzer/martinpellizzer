Marlierea langsdorffii

Marlierea langsdorffii, commonly known as the Brazilian nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Brazil.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale purple. Its fruits are fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

The plant is part of the Solanaceae family, which includes other nightshades like tomatoes and peppers.
