Miltonia moreliana

Miltonia moreliana, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Brazil.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple with a contrasting white or yellow lip. The plant has aerial roots and grows on trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Bloom time occurs during the summer months, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
