Masdevallia rosea

Masdevallia rosea, commonly known as the rose orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in late summer, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia rosea requires specific care, including high humidity and well-draining soil, to thrive.
