Monnina pumila

Monnina pumila, commonly known as the smooth monnina, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small, pink to purple flowers bloom in clusters at the tips of the stems.

Monnina pumila is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
