Mallotus dunnii

Mallotus dunnii, commonly known as the Australian holly, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-yellow flowers, followed by orange-red berries that are toxic to humans and animals.
