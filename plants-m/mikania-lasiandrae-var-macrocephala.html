Mikania lasiandrae var. macrocephala

Mikania lasiandrae var. macrocephala, commonly known as the large-headed Mikania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant has slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges. It produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

This variant is distinguished by its larger flower heads compared to other varieties within the species.
