Melastoma laurinum

Melastoma laurinum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually red or pink in color, which bloom during the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a slightly glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
