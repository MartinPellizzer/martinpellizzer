Myosotis afropalustris

Myosotis afropalustris, commonly known as the African forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as marshes and wetlands. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help retain moisture.

Myosotis afropalustris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
