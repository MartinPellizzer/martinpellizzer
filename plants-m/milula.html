Milula

Milula, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Milula is popular as a houseplant due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
