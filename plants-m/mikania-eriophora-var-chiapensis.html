Mikania eriophora var. chiapensis

Mikania eriophora var. chiapensis, commonly known as the Chiapan Mikania, is a flowering plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its rapid growth and adaptability, it can become invasive in certain habitats.
