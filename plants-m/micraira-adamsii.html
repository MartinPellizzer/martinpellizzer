Micraira adamsii

Micraira adamsii, commonly known as the Adams' micraira, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to lance-shaped. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in green or reddish hues.

Micraira adamsii is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in association with other woodland flora.
