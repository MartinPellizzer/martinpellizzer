Mammillaria melanocentra subsp. rubrograndis

Mammillaria melanocentra subsp.

rubrograndis, commonly known as the red giant mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico. It features a globular stem with prominent, rounded tubercles that are often covered in white or pale blue spines.

The plant produces showy, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
