Murraya siamensis

Murraya siamensis, commonly known as the Siam murraya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Murraya siamensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
