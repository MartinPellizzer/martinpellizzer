Melastoma stenostachyum

Melastoma stenostachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as tropical forests. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a smooth texture, while its flowers are often pink or red, attracting pollinators like bees and birds.

The fruit is a capsule that disperses seeds through wind or animals, contributing to its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
