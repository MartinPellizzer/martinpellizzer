Melastoma caesium

Melastoma caesium, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are usually opposite and elliptical, with a glossy surface.
