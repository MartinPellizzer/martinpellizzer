Micranthes pallida

Micranthes pallida, commonly known as the pale mountain saxifrage, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of North America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are basal, rosette-like, and covered in fine hairs.

Micranthes pallida is adapted to cold climates and has a low-growing habit to withstand harsh weather conditions.
