Marilaunidium stenocarpum

Marilaunidium stenocarpum, commonly known as the narrow-fruited marilaunidium, is a unique species within the Proteaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and distinctive, narrow, woody fruits. Native to the south-western region of Western Australia, it thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to dry, Mediterranean climates. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring.
