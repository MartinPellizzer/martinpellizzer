Medicago obscura var. spinosa

Medicago obscura var. spinosa, commonly known as the spiny medic.

It is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southwestern Asia. The plant features small, yellow pea-like flowers and trifoliate leaves with serrated edges. Its stems are typically spiny, which serves as a defense mechanism against herbivores.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.
