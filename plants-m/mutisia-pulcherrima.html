Mutisia pulcherrima

Mutisia pulcherrima, commonly known as the beautiful mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, yellow-orange ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, making it visually striking. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
