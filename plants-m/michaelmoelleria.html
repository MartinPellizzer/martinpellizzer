Michaelmoelleria

Michaelmoelleria, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

The plants typically have slender, upright stems and are native to tropical and subtropical regions of Southeast Asia. Michaelmoelleria species are epiphytic, meaning they grow on the surfaces of other plants without being parasitic.
