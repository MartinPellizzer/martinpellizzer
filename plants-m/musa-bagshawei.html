Musa bagshawei

Musa bagshawei, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a species of banana native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of pale yellow flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically consumed fresh or used in culinary applications. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is cultivated in various tropical regions around the world.
