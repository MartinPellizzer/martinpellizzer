Mentha Sylvestris Var. Viridis

Mentha sylvestris var. viridis is a lesser-known variant of the common mint plant known for its unique characteristics. This plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodland areas. Its leaves are a striking shade of green, which sets it apart from other mint species. It is valued for its aromatic properties and potential medicinal uses. Despite its beauty and benefits, it remains relatively obscure in popular horticulture.
