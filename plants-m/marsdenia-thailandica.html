Marsdenia thailandica

Marsdenia thailandica, commonly known as Thai milk vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and woody stems. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
