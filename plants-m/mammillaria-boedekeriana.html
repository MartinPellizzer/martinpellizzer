Mammillaria boedekeriana

Mammillaria boedekeriana, commonly known as the Boedeker's pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to yellow spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or white, appearing in the axils of the radial spines.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
