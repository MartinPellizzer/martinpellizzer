Mammillaria candida f. humboldtii

Mammillaria candida f. humboldtii, commonly known as the Humboldt's white globular cactus, is a compact, spherical cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, thick stem covered in white, woolly hairs that give it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
