Matricaria maritima f. eligulata

Matricaria maritima f. eligulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved yarrow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, yellowish-white composite flowers. This plant is typically found in coastal areas and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained environments. It is often used in herbal medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory properties.

The subspecies is distinguished by its narrower leaves compared to the typical form of Matricaria maritima.
