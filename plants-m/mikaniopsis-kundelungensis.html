Mikaniopsis kundelungensis

Mikaniopsis kundelungensis, commonly known as the Kundelungue grass, is a perennial herb native to the Democratic Republic of the Congo.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth surface and a bluish-green hue.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and forage for various wildlife species.
