Micromeria marifolia

Micromeria marifolia, commonly known as desert sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a fine layer of white hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Micromeria marifolia plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for native insects in its native range.
