Mammillaria ingens

Mammillaria ingens, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to 2 meters in height. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a dense covering of white, woolly hairs.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
