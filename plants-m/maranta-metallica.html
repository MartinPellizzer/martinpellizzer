Maranta metallica

Maranta metallica, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a striking tropical species native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a metallic sheen, giving it a unique and eye-catching appearance. The plant is known for its ability to produce strikingly colored flowers, often in shades of red or purple. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal.

Maranta metallica thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect light for optimal growth.
