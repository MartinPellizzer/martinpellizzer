Mimosa levigata

Mimosa levigata, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and delicate, fern-like foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, often in response to touch or light. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Mimosa levigata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and unique growth habits.
