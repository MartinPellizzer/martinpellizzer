Maxillaria cuneiformis

Maxillaria cuneiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria cuneiformis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in a controlled environment.
