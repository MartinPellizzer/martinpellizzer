Meliosma simiarum

Meliosma simiarum, commonly known as the tropical tree or false mahogany, is a member of the Meliaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often clustered in panicles, and it produces woody capsules as fruits.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
