Mesanthemum pilosum

Mesanthemum pilosum, commonly known as the silky mesembs, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, cylindrical stems covered in fine, silky hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive addition to succulent collections.
