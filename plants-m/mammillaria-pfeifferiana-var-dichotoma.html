Mammillaria pfeifferiana var. dichotoma

Mammillaria pfeifferiana var. dichotoma, commonly known as the dichotomous mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with prominent, white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique branching pattern and delicate appearance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
