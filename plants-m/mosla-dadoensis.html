Mosla dadoensis

Mosla dadoensis, commonly known as Chinese wallflower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant features square stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stems.

Mosla dadoensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
