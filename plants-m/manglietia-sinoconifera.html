Manglietia sinoconifera

Manglietia sinoconifera, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Chinese magnolia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has large, glossy, ovate leaves and produces woody, cone-like fruits that contain seeds. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.

The species is also noted for its ecological importance in its native habitat.
