Morelotia involuta

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is native to tropical regions of Africa and Madagascar. The plant has elongated, narrow leaves that curl in a helical pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in moist forest understories.
