Mallostoma setosum

Mallostoma setosum, commonly known as the hairy thistle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its spiny, lobed leaves and purple to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.

Mallostoma setosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional medicinal uses.
