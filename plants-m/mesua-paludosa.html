Mesua paludosa

Mesua paludosa, commonly known as the swamp mesua, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in wet, swampy habitats and is often found in mangrove forests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

Mesua paludosa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
