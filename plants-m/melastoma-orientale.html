Melastoma orientale

Melastoma orientale, commonly known as the oriental melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
