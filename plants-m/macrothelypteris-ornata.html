Macrothelypteris ornata

Macrothelypteris ornata, commonly known as the ornate shield fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, leathery fronds that can grow up to several feet in length. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its shield-shaped pinnae and a rugged, textured surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Thelypteridaceae and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
