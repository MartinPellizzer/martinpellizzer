Microlaena avenacea

Microlaena avenacea, commonly known as slender ryegrass, is a perennial grass native to Australia.

It is characterized by its fine, tufted leaves and panicle-like inflorescence. The plant typically grows in a variety of soil types, including sandy and clay soils, and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas. It is valued for its use in pasture and erosion control due to its hardiness and rapid growth.

Microlaena avenacea is also an important component of the flora in many Australian ecosystems.
