Mimosa elongata

Mimosa elongata, commonly known as the longleaf mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant features compound leaves that are sensitive to touch, causing them to fold when disturbed. Its small, yellowish flowers are borne in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

Mimosa elongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
