Mandevilla martiana var. typica

Mandevilla martiana var. typica, commonly known as the orange mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla martiana var. typica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
