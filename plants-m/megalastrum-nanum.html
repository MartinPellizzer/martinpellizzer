Megalastrum nanum

Megalastrum nanum, commonly known as the dwarf starflower, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in undergrowth of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
