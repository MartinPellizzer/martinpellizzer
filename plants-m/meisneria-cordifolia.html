Meisneria cordifolia

Meisneria cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf meisneria, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically a deep green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Meisneria cordifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and pleasant scent.
