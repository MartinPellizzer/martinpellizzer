Maytenus ovata var. arguta

Maytenus ovata var. arguta, commonly known as the argute mayten, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences. Its fruits are small, drupes that turn red when mature.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
