Malaxis microtatantha

Malaxis microtatantha, commonly known as the slender lady's tresses, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Asia and Australia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiraled inflorescence composed of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a dense rosette at the base.

Malaxis microtatantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its unique floral structure.
