Malcolmia africana var. trichocarpa

Malcolmia africana var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the African malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features opposite, hairy, ovate leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with five petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
