Massonia zeyheri

Massonia zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's massonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family. Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and rockeries.
