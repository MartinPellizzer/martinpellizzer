Maligia fastigiata

Maligia fastigiata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved maligia, is a unique plant species characterized by its upright growth habit and elongated, narrow leaves.

It belongs to the family Maligiacaceae and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant exhibits a distinctive foliage pattern, with leaves arranged in a dense, vertical manner along its stem. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and potentially endangered species.
