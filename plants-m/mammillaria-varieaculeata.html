Mammillaria varieaculeata

Mammillaria varieaculeata, commonly known as the variegated mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
