Mikania nodosa

Mikania nodosa, commonly known as the nodose morning glory, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a hairy surface and produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and forest edges.

Mikania nodosa is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
