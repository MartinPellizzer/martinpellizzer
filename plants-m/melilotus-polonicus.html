Melilotus polonicus

Melilotus polonicus, commonly known as Polish melilot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Europe and is characterized by its yellow flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is often found in meadows, pastures, and along roadsides. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Melilotus polonicus is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
