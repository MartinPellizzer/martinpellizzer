Malouetia retroflexa

Malouetia retroflexa, commonly known as the red-flowered malouetia, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
