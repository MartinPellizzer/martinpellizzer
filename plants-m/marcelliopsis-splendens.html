Marcelliopsis splendens

Marcelliopsis splendens, commonly known as the golden star fern, is a striking evergreen fern native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, arching fronds that are adorned with bright yellow to orange spore cases, giving it a distinctive and eye-catching appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories where it thrives in high humidity. Its foliage is dense and lush, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical and subtropical climates.

Marcelliopsis splendens is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ability to add a vibrant touch to shaded garden spaces.
