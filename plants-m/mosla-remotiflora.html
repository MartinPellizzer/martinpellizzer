Mosla remotiflora

Mosla remotiflora, commonly known as Chinese bugloss, is a perennial herb native to East Asia.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It is often found in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun.

Mosla remotiflora is valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
