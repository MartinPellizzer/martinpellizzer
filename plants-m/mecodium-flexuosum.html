Mecodium flexuosum

Mecodium flexuosum, commonly known as the climbing mecodium, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing stems and small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. Its unique growth habit allows it to climb or trail, adding visual interest to landscapes.

Mecodium flexuosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
