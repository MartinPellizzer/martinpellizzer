Malva sylvestris var. mauritiana

Malva sylvestris var. mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius mallow, is a perennial flowering plant native to the island of Mauritius.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy pink or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, black seeds.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions for horticultural purposes.
