Musa × discolor

Musa × discolor, commonly known as the spotted banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that are often marked with distinctive silver or white spots. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellow fruits that are typically harvested when they are still firm. Native to Southeast Asia, it is cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular choice for tropical gardens and commercial cultivation.
