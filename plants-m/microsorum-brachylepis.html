Microsorum brachylepis

Microsorum brachylepis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that typically grow in a floating or submerged manner. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize new areas effectively.

Microsorum brachylepis is valued in aquatic gardening for its ornamental appearance and ability to provide habitat for small aquatic life.
