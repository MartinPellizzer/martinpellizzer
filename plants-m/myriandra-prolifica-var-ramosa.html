Myriandra prolifica var. ramosa

Myriandra prolifica var. ramosa, commonly known as the prolific myriandra, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
