Microcoelia obovata

Microcoelia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate microcoelia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its ovate to broadly ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a smooth or slightly serrated margin. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its attractive foliage and blooms.
