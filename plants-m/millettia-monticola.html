Millettia monticola

Millettia monticola, commonly known as the mountain millettia, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain lens-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Millettia monticola thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often found in forested areas.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological and medicinal research.
