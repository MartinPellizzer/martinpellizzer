Mogorium gimea

Mogorium gimea, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which resemble flames and bloom during the warmer months. The plant has strap-like, dark green leaves that provide a dramatic backdrop to its showy blossoms. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mogorium gimea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and private collections.
