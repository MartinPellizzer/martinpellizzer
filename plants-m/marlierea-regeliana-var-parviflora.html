Marlierea regeliana var. parviflora

Marlierea regeliana var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered marlierea, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale pink flowers. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
