Margaretta rosea subsp. orbicularis

Margaretta rosea subsp.

orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular margaret rose, is a species of flowering plant native to South America. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color.

The leaves are broad and orbicular, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and floral arrangements.
