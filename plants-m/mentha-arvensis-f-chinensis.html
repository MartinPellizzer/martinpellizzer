Mentha arvensis f. chinensis

Mentha arvensis f. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese mint or horse mint, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. It is often used in traditional medicine for its aromatic and cooling properties.

Its essential oils contain menthol and other compounds that contribute to its medicinal and culinary uses.
