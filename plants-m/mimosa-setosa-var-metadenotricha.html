Mimosa setosa var. metadenotricha

Mimosa setosa var. metadenotricha, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in various conditions contribute to its widespread cultivation.
