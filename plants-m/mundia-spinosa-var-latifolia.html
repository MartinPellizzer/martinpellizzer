Mundia spinosa var. latifolia

Mundia spinosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf mundia, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and compound leaves with broad, ovate leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles. Its fruit is a dry, three-lobed capsule containing several seeds.

It is often found in open woodland and savanna habitats, thriving in well-drained soils.
