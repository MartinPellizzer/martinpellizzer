Mascagnia nobilis

Mascagnia nobilis, commonly known as the noble mascagnia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, and they bloom in the summer months.
