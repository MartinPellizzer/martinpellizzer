Matelea campechiana

Matelea campechiana, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, white, night-blooming flowers. The plant's unique blooming habit and striking appearance make it a notable species in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
