Manotes sanguineoarillata

Manotes sanguineoarillata, commonly known as the blood-red aril plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking red arils that encase its seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant has a woody stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy in appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a distinct, aromatic scent.

The botanical interest of Manotes sanguineoarillata lies in its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties.
