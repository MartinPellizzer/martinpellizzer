Mestoklema tuberosum var. macrorhizum

Mestoklema tuberosum var. macrorhizum, commonly known as the giant arrowroot, is a tuberous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its underground stems, or tubers, are the primary edible part, known for their starchy texture and mild flavor.

This plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
