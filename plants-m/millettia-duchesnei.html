Millettia duchesnei

Millettia duchesnei, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing legume native to China.

It features compound leaves with three to five leaflets, and its flowers are typically purple or violet, arranged in long, hanging clusters. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the legume family, Fabaceae.
