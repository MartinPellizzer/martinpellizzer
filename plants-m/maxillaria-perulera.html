Maxillaria perulera

Maxillaria perulera, commonly known as the Peruvian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Peru and Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms in the summer, with flowers that have a distinctive hooded lip. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Maxillaria perulera is valued for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
