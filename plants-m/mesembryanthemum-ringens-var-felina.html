Mesembryanthemum ringens var. felina

Mesembryanthemum ringens var. felina, commonly known as the cat's paw plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it both ornamental and easy to care for.
