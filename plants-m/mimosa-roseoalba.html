Mimosa roseoalba

Mimosa roseoalba, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. The plant is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and shrublands.

Mimosa roseoalba is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in disturbed soils.
