Mendoncia glabra

Mendoncia glabra, commonly known as the smooth mendoncia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, pale yellow to white flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
