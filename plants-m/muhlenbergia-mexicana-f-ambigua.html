Muhlenbergia mexicana f. ambigua

Muhlenbergia mexicana f. ambigua, commonly known as the Mexican muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, wiry stems. The leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue. It produces small, fluffy panicles of seeds during the flowering season, which contribute to its ornamental value.

This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
