Muhlenbergia mucronata

Muhlenbergia mucronata, commonly known as the slender muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a dull green. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, fluffy seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks.

Muhlenbergia mucronata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
