Mimusops costata

Mimusops costata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to well-drained soils in full sun.
