Mukdenia rossii var. multiloba

Mukdenia rossii var. multiloba, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern Asia.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are hairy and covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
