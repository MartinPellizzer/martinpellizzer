Malache mollis

Malache mollis, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its soft, silvery-gray foliage. The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
