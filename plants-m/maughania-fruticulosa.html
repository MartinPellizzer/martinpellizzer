Maughania fruticulosa

Maughania fruticulosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety hair. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along roadsides.
