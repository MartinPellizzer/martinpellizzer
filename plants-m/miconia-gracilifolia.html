Miconia gracilifolia

Miconia gracilifolia, commonly known as the slender-leaf miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia gracilifolia is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests and is known for its rapid growth rate.
