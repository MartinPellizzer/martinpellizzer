Microlepia mollis

Microlepia mollis, commonly known as the soft shield fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, soft fronds that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and stream sides. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, oval-shaped leaves.

Microlepia mollis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its graceful appearance.
