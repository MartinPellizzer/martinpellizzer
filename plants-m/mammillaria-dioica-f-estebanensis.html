Mammillaria dioica f. estebanensis

Mammillaria dioica f. estebanensis, commonly known as the Estebanensis mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, white radial spines and a dense covering of small, white tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.
