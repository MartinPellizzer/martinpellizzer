Microrhynchus nudicaulis

Microrhynchus nudicaulis, commonly known as the slender-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the eastern United States.

It is a small plant with a single, slender stem that typically grows from 10 to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive greenish-yellow color and a spurred lip. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, shaded woodlands.

It is an important species for pollinators and is often found in association with other orchid species in its habitat.
