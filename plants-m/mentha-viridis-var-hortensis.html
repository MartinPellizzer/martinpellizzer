Mentha viridis var. hortensis

Mentha viridis var. hortensis, commonly known as garden mint, is a cultivated variety of the watermint species.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow, pointed leaves that are often a vibrant green. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in whorls along the stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is widely used in herbal teas and culinary applications due to its aromatic, refreshing scent.
