Masdevallia ignea var. stobartiana

Masdevallia ignea var. stobartiana, commonly known as the Stobart's fiery orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns of contrasting colors. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
