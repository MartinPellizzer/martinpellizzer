Meliosma longepaniculata

Meliosma longepaniculata, commonly known as the longpanicle meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery panicles that can reach several feet in length, making it a striking ornamental plant. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate forests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in the panicles, followed by small, woody fruits.
