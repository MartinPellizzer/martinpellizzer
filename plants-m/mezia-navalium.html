Mezia navalium

Mezia navalium, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and silver-green foliage. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to salty, well-drained soils. Mezia navalium is often found in rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.

It blooms in late summer and early autumn, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
