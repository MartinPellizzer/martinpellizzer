Mussaenda laxiflora

Mussaenda laxiflora, commonly known as the soft-flowered Mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Mussaenda laxiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
