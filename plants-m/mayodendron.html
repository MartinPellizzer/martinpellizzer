Mayodendron

Mayodendron, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Mayodendron thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
