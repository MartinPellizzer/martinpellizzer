Murraya exotica

Murraya exotica, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom profusely during the dry season. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It produces small, orange berries that contain a single seed.

Murraya exotica is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
