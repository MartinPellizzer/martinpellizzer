Myrtus Orbicularis

Myrtus orbicularis is a unique evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands. It is known for its round, glossy leaves and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. This plant thrives in warm, sunny climates and well-drained soil. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes. It also has historical uses in traditional medicine and as a source of essential oils.
