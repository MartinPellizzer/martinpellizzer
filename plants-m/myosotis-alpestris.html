Myosotis alpestris

Myosotis alpestris, commonly known as alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or violet petals and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces small, clustered flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Myosotis alpestris is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
