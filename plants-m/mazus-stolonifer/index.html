Mazus Stolonifer

Mazus stolonifer is a low-growing perennial plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific islands. It is known for its creeping habit and small, delicate flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or coastal areas. Its stolons allow it to spread quickly, forming dense mats that help prevent soil erosion. Despite its modest appearance, Mazus stolonifer plays an important role in its ecosystem and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
