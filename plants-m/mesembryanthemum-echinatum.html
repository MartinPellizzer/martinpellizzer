Mesembryanthemum echinatum

Mesembryanthemum echinatum, commonly known as the needle-leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is adapted to arid environments and stores water in its thick leaves.
