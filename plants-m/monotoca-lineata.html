Monotoca lineata

Monotoca lineata, commonly known as the narrowleaf tansy, is a perennial herb native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and yellow composite flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are linear and often have a distinct, serrated edge.

Monotoca lineata is known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
