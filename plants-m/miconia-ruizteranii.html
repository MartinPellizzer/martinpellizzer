Miconia ruizteranii

Miconia ruizteranii, commonly known as the Ruizteranii miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in humid lowland forests. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves and showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in clusters. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible for some bird species.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
