Mezilaurus navalium

Mezilaurus navalium, commonly known as the coastal lantana, is a flowering plant native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy or rocky soils near the shoreline. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, adapting to the harsh coastal environment.

Mezilaurus navalium is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging coastal conditions.
