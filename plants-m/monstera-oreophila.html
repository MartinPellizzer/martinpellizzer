Monstera oreophila

Monstera oreophila, commonly known as the mountain monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length, featuring distinctive notches and holes. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil with high organic content. Its aerial roots allow it to climb trees and other structures, making it an excellent choice for indoor hanging baskets or vertical gardens.

Monstera oreophila is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for with proper light and watering conditions.
