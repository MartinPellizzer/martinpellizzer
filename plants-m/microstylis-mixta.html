Microstylis mixta

Microstylis mixta, commonly known as the hybrid microstylis, is a perennial herb native to the wetlands of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Cyperaceae, which includes sedges, and is characterized by its grass-like appearance. Due to its adaptability, it is often found in disturbed wetland habitats and is of interest to botanists studying wetland ecosystems.
