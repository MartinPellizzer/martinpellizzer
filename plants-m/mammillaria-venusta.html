Mammillaria venusta

Mammillaria venusta, commonly known as the Venus's hair or the hairless hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white, woolly spines. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria venusta is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
