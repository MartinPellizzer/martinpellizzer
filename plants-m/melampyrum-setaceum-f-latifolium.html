Melampyrum setaceum f. latifolium

Melampyrum setaceum f. latifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf yellow lantana, is a variant of the yellow lantana species native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers. The leaves are broader than those of the typical variety, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats, often found in meadows and woodland edges.

This plant is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
