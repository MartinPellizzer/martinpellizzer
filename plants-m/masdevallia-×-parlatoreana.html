Masdevallia × parlatoreana

Masdevallia × parlatoreana, commonly known as the Parlatoreana orchid, is a hybrid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, purple, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and delicate appearance.
