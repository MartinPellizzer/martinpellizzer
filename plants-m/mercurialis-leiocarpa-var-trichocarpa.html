Mercurialis leiocarpa var. trichocarpa

Mercurialis leiocarpa var. trichocarpa, commonly known as the hairy mercury plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, giving the plant its "hairy" appearance.
