Miltonia karwinskii

Miltonia karwinskii, commonly known as the Karwinski's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of bright pink or violet blooms with white petals and a yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Miltonia karwinskii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
