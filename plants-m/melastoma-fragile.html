Melastoma fragile

Melastoma fragile, commonly known as the fragile melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Melastoma fragile is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
