Misanteca urceolata

Misanteca urceolata, commonly known as the urn-shaped mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on various host trees.

It has small, oval-shaped leaves and produces white or pale yellow flowers. The plant's berries are typically red or orange and contain several seeds. It is native to parts of Central and South America.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and medicinal research.
