Matricaria inodora var. eligulata

Matricaria inodora var. eligulata, commonly known as the European chamomile, is a herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features finely divided, silver-green leaves and small, yellow flower heads that resemble chamomile. The plant is cultivated for its aromatic properties and is often used in herbal teas. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its medicinal uses include calming effects and digestive support.
