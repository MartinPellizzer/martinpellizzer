Metrosideros abietina

Metrosideros abietina, commonly known as the silver flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to New Caledonia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and striking clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has a distinctive, fragrant scent and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant floral display. Its wood is lightweight and has been historically used for various crafts and construction.

The species is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
