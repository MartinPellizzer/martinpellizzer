Microstachys marginata var. minor

Microstachys marginata var. minor, commonly known as the narrow-leaf microstachys, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves that are typically narrow and have a distinct margin. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with delicate, spikelet-like flowers. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical features make it a notable species for ecological studies and botanical gardens.
