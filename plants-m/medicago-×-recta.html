Medicago × recta

Medicago × recta, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in agricultural settings. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used for forage and soil improvement.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and horticultural contexts.
