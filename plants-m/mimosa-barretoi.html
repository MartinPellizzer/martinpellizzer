Mimosa barretoi

Mimosa barretoi, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its small, fluffy seed pods release seeds through a mechanism called autochory, aiding in its dispersal.

Mimosa barretoi is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
