Mimosa brevicalyx

Mimosa brevicalyx, commonly known as the Chinese mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched, a response triggered by light or touch stimuli. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various soil conditions contribute to its adaptability in different climates.
