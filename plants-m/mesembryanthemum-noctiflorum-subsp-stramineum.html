Mesembryanthemum noctiflorum subsp. stramineum

Mesembryanthemum noctiflorum subsp.

The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptations to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
