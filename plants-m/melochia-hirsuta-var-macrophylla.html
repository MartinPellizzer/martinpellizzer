Melochia hirsuta var. macrophylla

Melochia hirsuta var. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant velvetleaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically heart-shaped or ovate.

The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family and is native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas. Its leaves are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

This species is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain areas.
