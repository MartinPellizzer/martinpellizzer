Miconia subcompressa subsp. plumieri

Miconia subcompressa subsp.

plumieri, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green on the upper surface.

The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by orange to red berries. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is adapted to humid, warm climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
