Maytenus rupestris

Maytenus rupestris, commonly known as the rock mayten, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain several seeds.

Maytenus rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
