Macrosolen tetragonus

Macrosolen tetragonus, commonly known as the four-angled macrolen, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its square or four-angled stems, which are a distinguishing feature of the genus Macrosolen. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
