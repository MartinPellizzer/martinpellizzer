Melanochyla scalarinervis

Melanochyla scalarinervis, commonly known as the scalycarp, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melanthiaceae and is characterized by its striking, clustered inflorescences of small, white to pale pink flowers. The flowers emit a subtle fragrance, attracting various pollinators such as bees and moths.
