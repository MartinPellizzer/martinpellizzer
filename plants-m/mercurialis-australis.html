Mercurialis australis

Mercurialis australis, commonly known as the Australian mercury plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Australia.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid and semi-arid regions. The plant features slender stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

It is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
