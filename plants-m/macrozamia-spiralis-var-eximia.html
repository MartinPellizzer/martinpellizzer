Macrozamia spiralis var. eximia

Macrozamia spiralis var. eximia, commonly known as the spiral cycad, is a rare and ancient plant native to the subtropical regions of eastern Australia.

It is a slow-growing, long-lived species that thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water.
