Meliosma boliviensis var. sumacensis

Meliosma boliviensis var. sumacensis, commonly known as the Bolivian sumac, is a shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Lythraceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges.
