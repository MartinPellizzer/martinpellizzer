Microtatorchis

Microtatorchis, commonly known as the small torch cactus, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Cactaceae.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of spines. The plant typically produces bright yellow or orange flowers during the summer months. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Microtatorchis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and resilience.
