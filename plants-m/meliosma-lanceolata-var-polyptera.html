Meliosma lanceolata var. polyptera

Meliosma lanceolata var. polyptera, commonly known as the narrow-leaf meliosma, is a perennial shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale green flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are aromatic and contain essential oils, which contribute to its use in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
