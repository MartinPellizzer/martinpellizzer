Masdevallia carderi

Masdevallia carderi, commonly known as the Carderi Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces pendulous flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
