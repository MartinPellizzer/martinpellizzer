Miconia erosa

Miconia erosa, commonly known as the pink miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, berry-like structure that turns red when mature.

Miconia erosa is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
