Medinilla linearifolia

Medinilla linearifolia, commonly known as the linear-leaf medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are often arranged in a spiral pattern along its stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental plant. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to thrive in well-drained, moist soil.

Medinilla linearifolia is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in warm, humid environments.
