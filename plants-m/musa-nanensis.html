Musa nanensis

Musa nanensis, commonly known as the Nan Province banana, is a species of banana native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces clusters of fruit that are typically smaller than those of other banana varieties. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a sweet and slightly tangy flavor.

Due to its ornamental value and fruit production, Musa nanensis is also appreciated in horticultural settings.
