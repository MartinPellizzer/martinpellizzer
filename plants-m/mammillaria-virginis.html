Mammillaria virginis

Mammillaria virginis, commonly known as the Virgin's Milk Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white or pale spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
