Maranta olivaris

Maranta olivaris, commonly known as the Olive Maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often display intricate patterns of silver or white veins. The plant produces delicate, orchid-like flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom during the warmer months. Maranta olivaris thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and ornamental purposes.
