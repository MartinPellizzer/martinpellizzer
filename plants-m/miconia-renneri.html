Miconia renneri

Miconia renneri, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often reddish in color when young. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a distinctive, three-lobed capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia renneri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
