Melastoma acutum

Melastoma acutum, commonly known as the acute melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves with a glossy green surface and serrated edges. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
