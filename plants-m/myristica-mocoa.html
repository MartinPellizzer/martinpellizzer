Myristica mocoa

Myristica mocoa, commonly known as the Mocoa nut tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Amazon Basin.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces fruits that contain a single seed, which is encased in a hard, aromatic shell. The seeds are valued for their essential oils and are used in traditional medicine and as a flavoring agent.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
