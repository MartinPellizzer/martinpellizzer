Menisciopsis rubida

Menisciopsis rubida, commonly known as the red meniscus plant, is a rare and striking member of the Menispermaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive red, fleshy, and often swollen stems that resemble a meniscus, hence its scientific name. Its leaves are usually compound, with a glossy, dark green color and a slightly serrated edge.

While it is not widely cultivated, Menisciopsis rubida is valued for its unique appearance and potential medicinal properties.
