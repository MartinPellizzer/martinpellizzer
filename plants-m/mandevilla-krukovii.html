Mandevilla krukovii

Mandevilla krukovii, commonly known as the Krukovii mandevilla, is a vigorous climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant produces opposite, simple leaves that are ovate to elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mandevilla krukovii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to climb trellises or walls.
