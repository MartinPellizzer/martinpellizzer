Matthiola odoratissima var. ovatifolia

Matthiola odoratissima var. ovatifolia, commonly known as the sweet rocket, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while the stems are upright and branching.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual and aromatic qualities.
