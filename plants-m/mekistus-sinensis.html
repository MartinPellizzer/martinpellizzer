Mekistus sinensis

Mekistus sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese ironwood, is a rare and ancient tree species native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, hard wood and thick, dark green foliage. The plant has a slow growth rate and can live for several centuries, making it a symbol of longevity in Chinese culture. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is protected in its native habitat.
