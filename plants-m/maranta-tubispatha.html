Maranta tubispatha

Maranta tubispatha, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Maranta tubispatha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant.
