Miconia stellata

Miconia stellata, commonly known as the star miconia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Miconia stellata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its attractive appearance.
