Meineckia ovata

Meineckia ovata, commonly known as the ovalleaf myrtle, is a flowering plant native to southeastern Australia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Meineckia ovata is well adapted to dry, sunny conditions and is often found in coastal and sandy soils.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for horticultural and ecological studies.
