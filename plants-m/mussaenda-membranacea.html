Mussaenda membranacea

Mussaenda membranacea, commonly known as the membranous Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy underside, and it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

Mussaenda membranacea is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
