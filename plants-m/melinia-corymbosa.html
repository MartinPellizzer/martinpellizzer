Melinia corymbosa

Melinia corymbosa, commonly known as the clustered melinia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Melinia corymbosa is often found in rocky areas and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
