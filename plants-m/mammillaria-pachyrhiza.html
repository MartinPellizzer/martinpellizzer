Mammillaria pachyrhiza

Mammillaria pachyrhiza, commonly known as the thick-stemmed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its stems are thick and fleshy, adapted for water storage in arid environments.

It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.
