Melicope densiflora

Melicope densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered melicope, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Melicope densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
