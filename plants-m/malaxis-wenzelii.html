Malaxis wenzelii

Malaxis wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous, and it has a short rhizome that supports its growth.

The species is noted for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
