Myginda latifolia var. styliflora

Myginda latifolia var. styliflora, commonly known as the broadleaf myginda, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and pink, which are typically fragrant. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
