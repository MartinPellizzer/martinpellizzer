Melastoma rubrum

Melastoma rubrum, commonly known as the red-stemmed melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and pink. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

Melastoma rubrum is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
