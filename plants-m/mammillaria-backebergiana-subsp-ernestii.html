Mammillaria backebergiana subsp. ernestii

Mammillaria backebergiana subsp.

ernestii, commonly known as the Ernest's mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to South Africa. It features a compact, globular shape with short, ribbed stems that are typically green to bluish-gray in color.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It has prominent tubercles and spines, which are characteristic of the Mammillaria genus.

This subspecies is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
