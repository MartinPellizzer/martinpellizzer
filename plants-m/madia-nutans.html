Madia nutans

Madia nutans, commonly known as the nodding madia, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Madia nutans is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
