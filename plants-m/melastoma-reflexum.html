Melastoma reflexum

Melastoma reflexum, commonly known as the red-rooted melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, opposite leaves and clusters of pink or white flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Melastoma reflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
