Mimosa thermarum

Mimosa thermarum, commonly known as the heat-sensitive mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is known for its unique response to touch, causing its leaves to fold shut when disturbed. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-drained soil.

Mimosa thermarum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and intriguing botanical behavior.
