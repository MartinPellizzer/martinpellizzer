Memecylon elegans var. dichotomum

Memecylon elegans var. dichotomum, commonly known as the slender memecylon, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often producing white or pale yellow blooms.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds, facilitating wind dispersal.
