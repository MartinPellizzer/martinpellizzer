Mimosa annularis var. xinguensis

Mimosa annularis var. xinguensis, commonly known as the Xingu mimosa, is a shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is adapted to humid, tropical conditions and thrives in well-drained, nutrient-rich soils. It is often found in secondary growth areas and along riverbanks.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological importance, it is a notable species in the study of Amazonian flora.
