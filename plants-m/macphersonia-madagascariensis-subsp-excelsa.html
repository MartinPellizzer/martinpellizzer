Macphersonia madagascariensis subsp. excelsa

Macphersonia madagascariensis subsp.

excelsa, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid native to Madagascar. It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive star-shaped appearance, often in shades of white and purple.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in the rainforest canopy. Its botanical features include a pseudobulb structure and long, strap-like leaves.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
