Mussaenda longisepala

Mussaenda longisepala, commonly known as the long-sepaled Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Mussaenda longisepala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
