Mocquerysia multiflora

Mocquerysia multiflora, commonly known as the Andean wax tree, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically blooming in the spring. It is a member of the Ericaceae family, which includes other shrubs and trees adapted to acidic soils.
