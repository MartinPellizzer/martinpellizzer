Myristica nana

Myristica nana, commonly known as the dwarf nutmeg tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by red, fleshy berries that contain the aromatic seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Myristica nana typically grows in shaded, humid environments and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.

Due to its unique appearance and fragrance, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
