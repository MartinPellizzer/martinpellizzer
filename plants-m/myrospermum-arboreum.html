Myrospermum arboreum

Myrospermum arboreum, commonly known as the African silver bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
