Matucana klopfensteinii

Matucana klopfensteinii, commonly known as the yellow poinsettia, is a striking member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

Native to Mexico, this plant is characterized by its vibrant yellow bracts that surround the small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value, especially during the holiday season.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
