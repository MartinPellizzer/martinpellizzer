Mandevilla hesperia

Mandevilla hesperia, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy, clustered blooms throughout the growing season, often attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. Its heart-shaped leaves are glossy and provide a lush backdrop to the bright flowers.

Mandevilla hesperia thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
