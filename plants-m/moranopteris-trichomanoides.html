Moranopteris trichomanoides

Moranopteris trichomanoides, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Hymenophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, finely divided fronds that are typically less than 10 centimeters in length. Moranopteris trichomanoides is noted for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in shaded gardens or as a houseplant.
