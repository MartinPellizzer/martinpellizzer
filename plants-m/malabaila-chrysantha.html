Malabaila chrysantha

Malabaila chrysantha, commonly known as the golden flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a vibrant and eye-catching appearance. The plant has compound leaves composed of several oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish.

Malabaila chrysantha is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
