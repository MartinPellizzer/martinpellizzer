Magnolia pyramidata

Magnolia pyramidata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the pyramid magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its distinctive pyramidal shape and showy, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. The plant thrives in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 8, preferring partial to full sun and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
