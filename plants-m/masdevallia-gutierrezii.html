Masdevallia gutierrezii

Masdevallia gutierrezii, commonly known as the Gutiérrez’s masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns and a unique structure that includes a prominent, spurred lip. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in cool, humid environments with high altitude. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette, aiding in water retention in its native habitat.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
