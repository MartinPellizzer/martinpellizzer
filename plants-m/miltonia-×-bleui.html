Miltonia × bleui

Miltonia × bleui, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a hybrid orchid species that combines the characteristics of Miltonia and Miltoniopsis.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid environments. The plant features showy, butterfly-shaped flowers that range in color from deep blue to purple, with intricate patterns and a velvety texture. Its pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves are typical of the Epidendroideae subfamily, and it requires indirect light and consistent moisture for optimal growth.

This ornamental plant is popular in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
