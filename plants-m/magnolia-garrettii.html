Magnolia garrettii

Magnolia garrettii, commonly known as the Garrett magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often peeling in thin layers.

Magnolia garrettii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and adaptability to various soil types.
