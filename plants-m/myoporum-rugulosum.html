Myoporum rugulosum

Myoporum rugulosum, commonly known as the rough myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, ovate leaves that are covered in a distinctive rough, scaly texture. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Myoporum rugulosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in horticulture for landscaping purposes.
