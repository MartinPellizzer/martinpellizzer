Maba ubanghensis

Maba ubanghensis, commonly known as the Philippine fern, is a species of fern native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky crevices. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds.

Maba ubanghensis plays an important role in its ecosystem by contributing to soil stability and providing habitat for small organisms.
