Monsonia nivea var. villosa

Monsonia nivea var. villosa, commonly known as the velvet monsonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage, which gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique texture.
