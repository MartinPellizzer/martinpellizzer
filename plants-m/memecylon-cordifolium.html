Memecylon cordifolium

Memecylon cordifolium, commonly known as the heart-leaved memecylon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a heart-shaped base, and are often glossy in appearance. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by red or orange berries.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
