Monstera maderaverde

Monstera maderaverde, commonly known as the Mexican giant monstera, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive holes as they mature. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its foliage is often used in interior design for its dramatic, eye-catching appearance.

Monstera maderaverde is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its resilience and air-purifying qualities.
