Maxillaria squalens

Maxillaria squalens, commonly known as the smoky maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals that give it a smoky appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a distinctive, wrinkled texture.

Maxillaria squalens is valued for its unique appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
