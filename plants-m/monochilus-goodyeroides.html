Monochilus goodyeroides

Monochilus goodyeroides, commonly known as the Goodyera orchid, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like India, Thailand, and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its slender, evergreen leaves and produces delicate, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive lip. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
