Miconia jamesluteynii

Miconia jamesluteynii, commonly known as the Luteyn's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the Lesser Antilles.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves that are often bright green with a waxy texture. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
