Mimosa guilandinae var. guilandinae

Mimosa guilandinae var. guilandinae, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are soft and feathery, giving it a delicate appearance. Its small, yellowish-white flowers appear in clusters and bloom in the spring.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
