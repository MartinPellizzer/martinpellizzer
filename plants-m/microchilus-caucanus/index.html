Microchilus Caucanus

Microchilus caucanus is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus. It is known for its small, intricate blooms that bloom in early spring. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forested areas. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among botanists and plant enthusiasts. Due to habitat loss, it is now considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to survive.
