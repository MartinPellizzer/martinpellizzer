Mikania lima

Mikania lima, commonly known as giant beggar's ticks, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and hairy stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in heads, and its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs. Mikania lima is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed habitats, making it both a common weed and an invasive species in some areas.
