Magnolia wilsonii f. nicholsoniana

Magnolia wilsonii f. nicholsoniana, commonly known as the Nicholson's magnolia, is a rare and ornamental plant native to China.

It is a hybrid form of Magnolia wilsonii, characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves and is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its limited natural range and ornamental appeal, it is a sought-after specimen in horticulture.
