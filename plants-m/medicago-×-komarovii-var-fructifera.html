Medicago × komarovii var. fructifera

Medicago × komarovii var. fructifera, commonly known as the fruit-bearing goat's rue, is a hybrid legume species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Medicago × komarovii var. fructifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
