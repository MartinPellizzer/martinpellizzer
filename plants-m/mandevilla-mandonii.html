Mandevilla mandonii

Mandevilla mandonii, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant.

Mandevilla mandonii requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
