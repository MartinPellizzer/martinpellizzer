Miconia reitzii

Miconia reitzii, commonly known as the white miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
