Myosotis petiolata var. pansa

Myosotis petiolata var. pansa, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from September to November. The plant has slender, upright stems with lance-shaped leaves that are slightly hairy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest margins or streamside habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and conservation efforts.
