Malva althaeoides

Malva althaeoides, commonly known as the silky mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy pink or purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a soft, fuzzy texture, and it produces fluffy, seed-filled pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or along riverbanks.

Malva althaeoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
