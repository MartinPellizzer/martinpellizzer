Montia asarifolia

Montia asarifolia, commonly known as the fernleaf monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and parts of Canada.

It belongs to the family Montiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. Its leaves are usually alternate and deeply lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Montia asarifolia is often found in mountainous regions and is adapted to thrive in cool, humid conditions.
