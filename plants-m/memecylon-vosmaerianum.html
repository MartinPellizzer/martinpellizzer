Memecylon vosmaerianum

Memecylon vosmaerianum, commonly known as the African violetleaf, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a leathery texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
