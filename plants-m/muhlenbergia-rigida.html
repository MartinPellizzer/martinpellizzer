Muhlenbergia rigida

Muhlenbergia rigida, commonly known as rigid muhlenbergia, is a species of grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like blades that are often silver-white in color. The plant produces panicles of small, spikelet-based flowers that are typically pink to purple in hue. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as prairies and woodland edges.

Muhlenbergia rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
