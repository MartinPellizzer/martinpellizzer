Masdevallia cryptocopis

Masdevallia cryptocopis, commonly known as the cryptic masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces clusters of delicate, elongated flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing a range of colors from pale pink to deep red. Its unique morphology and restricted habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
