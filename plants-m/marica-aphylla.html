Marica aphylla

Marica aphylla, commonly known as the leafless marica, is a unique plant native to the arid regions of South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, commonly referred to as the ice plant family, and is characterized by its lack of true leaves. Marica aphylla is adapted to extreme conditions, storing water in its fleshy stems and relying on minimal rainfall for survival.
