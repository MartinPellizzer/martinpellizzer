Millotia muelleri

Millotia muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's millotia, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils.

Millotia muelleri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
