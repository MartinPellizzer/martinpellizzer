Mammillaria seegeri var. mirabilis

Mammillaria seegeri var. mirabilis, commonly known as the Mirabilis Mammillaria, is a small, compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem with a grayish-green color and prominent white radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
