Mouroucoa hancorniifolia

Mouroucoa hancorniifolia, commonly known as the horned leaf mouroucoa, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, waxy leaves that have a distinctive horn-like appearance, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its foliage is dense and often displays a vibrant green color with a slight sheen.

Mouroucoa hancorniifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
