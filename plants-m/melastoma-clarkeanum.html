Melastoma clarkeanum

Melastoma clarkeanum, commonly known as the Clarke's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a glossy appearance.

It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
