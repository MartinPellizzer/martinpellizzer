Machilus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

Machilus yunnanensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Machilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by ellipsoid berries that turn from green to dark purple as they mature.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its aesthetic appeal.
