Mandevilla sandemanii

Mandevilla sandemanii, commonly known as the sandemanii mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for hanging baskets and garden displays. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, glossy leaves that add visual interest.

Mandevilla sandemanii thrives in warm, sunny conditions and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
