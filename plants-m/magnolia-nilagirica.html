Magnolia nilagirica

Magnolia nilagirica, commonly known as the Nilagiri magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic to ovate in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in hillside forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
