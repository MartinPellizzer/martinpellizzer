Maxillaria sterrocaulos

Maxillaria sterrocaulos, commonly known as the slender-stemmed maxillaria, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and inflorescence. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, displaying a range of colors from white to yellow and pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
