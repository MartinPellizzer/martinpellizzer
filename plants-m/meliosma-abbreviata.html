Meliosma abbreviata

Meliosma abbreviata, commonly known as the narrowleaf meliosma, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive, waxy coating on the underside. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
