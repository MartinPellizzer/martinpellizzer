Machilus duthiei

Machilus duthiei, commonly known as the Duthie's laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the Lauraceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage, with lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by dark blue berries.

Machilus duthiei is often found in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance.
