Malaxis marthaleidae

Malaxis marthaleidae, commonly known as the Marthaleidae orchid, is a species of orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant features a slender, wiry rhizome and produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base of the plant.

Botanically, it is characterized by its unique floral structure and specific ecological preferences.
