Mentha aquatica f. dubia

Mentha aquatica f. dubia, commonly known as water mint, is a variant of the water mint plant.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant typically grows in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. It produces small, pale blue or white flowers in whorls along the stem.

This subspecies is often used in herbal remedies and has a distinct, aromatic scent.
