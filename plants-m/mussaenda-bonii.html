Mussaenda bonii

Mussaenda bonii, commonly known as the golden trumpet bush, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that resemble trumpets, which are composed of clustered flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and a smooth, dark green upper surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Mussaenda bonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
