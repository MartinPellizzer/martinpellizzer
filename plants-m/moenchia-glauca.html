Moenchia glauca

Moenchia glauca, commonly known as blue moenchia, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Amaranthaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, glaucous leaves that range from blue to gray-green in color. Moenchia glauca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
