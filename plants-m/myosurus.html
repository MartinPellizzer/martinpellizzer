Myosurus

Myosurus, commonly known as mouse tail, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Apiaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems and small, clustered flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Myosurus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
