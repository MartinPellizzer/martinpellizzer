Muhlenbergia peruviana var. elatior

Muhlenbergia peruviana var. elatior, commonly known as the tall muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, airy plumes of flowers that are often pink or purple in color. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 2 meters. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and low-maintenance addition to landscapes.
