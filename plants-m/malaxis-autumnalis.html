Malaxis autumnalis

Malaxis autumnalis, commonly known as the autumn malaxis, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and meadows. The plant features a slender, hairy stem with a single, lance-shaped leaf. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and often have a faint fragrance.

It blooms in late summer to early autumn, making it a distinctive addition to the orchid family during the cooler months.
