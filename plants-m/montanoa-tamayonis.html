Montanoa tamayonis

Montanoa tamayonis, commonly known as Mexican silverleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to Mexico, characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family and is often found in rocky or open habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Montanoa tamayonis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
