Malcolmia aculeolata

Malcolmia aculeolata, commonly known as the prickly malcolmia, is an annual herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features hairy, branching stems and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has distinctive spiny or prickly leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Malcolmia aculeolata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
