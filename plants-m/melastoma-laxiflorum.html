Melastoma laxiflorum

Melastoma laxiflorum, commonly known as the slender-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, often showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough texture, while the flowers are usually pink or purple.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
