Melandrium glaberrimum

Melandrium glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth lady's tresses, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, grass-like foliage and its striking, spicate inflorescence that resembles a loose cluster of small, pale pink or white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and wet meadows. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in rocky or calcareous soils.

Melandrium glaberrimum is noted for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value.
