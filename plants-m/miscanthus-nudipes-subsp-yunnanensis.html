Miscanthus nudipes subsp. yunnanensis

Miscanthus nudipes subsp.

yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan feather reed, is a perennial grass native to southern China. It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery panicles that bloom in late summer.

The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its foliage is narrow and grass-like, with a silver-green hue that adds ornamental value.

It is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid climates.
