Michelia glabra

Michelia glabra, commonly known as the smooth Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Michelia glabra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
