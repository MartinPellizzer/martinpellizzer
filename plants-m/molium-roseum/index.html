Molium Roseum

Molium roseum is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant known for its vibrant pink blooms and delicate foliage. Native to the mountainous regions of South America, it thrives in cool, moist environments with ample sunlight. This species is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ease of cultivation. Its flowers, which bloom in spring, attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Despite its beauty, Molium roseum remains relatively uncommon in gardens outside its native habitat.
