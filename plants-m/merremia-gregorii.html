Merremia gregorii

Merremia gregorii, commonly known as the Gregor's morning glory, is a flowering vine native to Central America.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
