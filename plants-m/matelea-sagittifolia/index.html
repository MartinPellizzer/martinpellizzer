Matelea Sagittifolia

Matelea sagittifolia is a striking member of the Apocynaceae family known for its vibrant, arrow-shaped flowers. Native to the cloud forests of Central America, this plant thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its unique foliage and showy blooms make it a popular choice among horticulturists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in mainstream gardening circles. This article explores the characteristics, habitat, and care requirements of this remarkable species.
