Macrolomia

Macrolomia, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are native to the tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in Central and South America. Macrolomia species are characterized by their intricate, often colorful flowers that resemble spiders, hence their common name. They typically grow as terrestrial or epiphytic orchids, often found in shaded, humid environments.
