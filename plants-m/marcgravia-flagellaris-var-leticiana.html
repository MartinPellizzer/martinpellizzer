Marcgravia flagellaris var. leticiana

Marcgravia flagellaris var. leticiana, commonly known as the Letician's Glory, is a climbing epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features elongated, thin leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are typically followed by distinctive, showy fruits. Its unique morphology and growth habit make it a striking addition to greenhouse collections.

It thrives in high humidity and bright, indirect light, requiring careful attention to maintain its health.
