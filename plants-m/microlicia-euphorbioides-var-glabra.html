Microlicia euphorbioides var. glabra

Microlicia euphorbioides var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth microlicia, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in umbels, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies of tropical flora.
