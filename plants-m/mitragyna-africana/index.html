Mitragyna Africana

African kratom, scientifically known as Mitragyna africana, is a lesser-known but potent species of the Mitragyna genus native to sub-Saharan Africa. This plant has been traditionally used by local communities for its stimulating and analgesic properties, often harvested for its leaves which contain alkaloids like mitragynine and 7-hydroxymitragynine. Despite its growing global interest, African kratom remains relatively understudied compared to its more famous relative, kratom from Southeast Asia. Its unique chemical profile and cultural significance make it a subject of both scientific curiosity and traditional reverence.
