Mammillaria fragilis

Mammillaria fragilis, commonly known as the fragile nipple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and pale green to yellowish stems. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and has a slow growth rate. It features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a delicate touch to its appearance.

Due to its delicate nature, it requires careful handling and specific care to thrive.
