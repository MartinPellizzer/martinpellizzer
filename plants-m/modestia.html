Modestia

Modestia, commonly known as the modestia plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Modestia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
