Maxillaria suareziorum

Maxillaria suareziorum, commonly known as the Suarez's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features long, slender petals and a distinctive lip that is often marked with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Maxillaria suareziorum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively ease of cultivation.
