Melocarpum robecchii

Melocarpum robecchii, commonly known as the Mediterranean milkvetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in clusters, which are followed by legume-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Melocarpum robecchii is valued for its role in ecological restoration and as a source of forage for livestock.
