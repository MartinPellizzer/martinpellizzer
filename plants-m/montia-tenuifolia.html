Montia tenuifolia

Montia tenuifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf springbeauty, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It typically features delicate, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers in the spring. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are usually narrow and lance-shaped, while its flowers are clustered in umbels.

Montia tenuifolia is a member of the Montiaceae family and is often found in temperate regions.
