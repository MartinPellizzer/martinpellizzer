Miconia chimantensis

Miconia chimantensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the humid lowland forests of Ecuador. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. Miconia chimantensis plays an important ecological role in its native habitat by providing food and shelter for various wildlife.
