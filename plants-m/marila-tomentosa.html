Marila tomentosa

Marila tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly marila, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features hairy, grayish stems and leaves, which give it its common name. Its flowers are small and white, often clustered in loose inflorescences.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil conditions.
