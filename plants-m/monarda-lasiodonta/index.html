Monarda Lasiodonta

Purple coneflower, scientifically known as Monarda lasiodonta, is a striking native plant that thrives in the moist woodlands of the eastern United States. Its vibrant purple petals and distinctive yellow center make it a favorite among pollinators like bees and butterflies. This species is also valued for its medicinal properties, with traditional uses in herbal remedies. Despite its beauty, it faces threats from habitat loss and invasive species. Protecting this plant is essential for maintaining biodiversity and ecological balance.
