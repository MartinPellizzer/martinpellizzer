Melandrium apetalum subsp. attenuatum

Melandrium apetalum subsp.

attenuatum, commonly known as the slender catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, inconspicuous flowers.

The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, while its flowers are usually pink or white.
