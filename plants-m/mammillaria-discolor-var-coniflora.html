Mammillaria discolor var. coniflora

Mammillaria discolor var. coniflora, commonly known as the white-pearled mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical stem covered in white, woolly hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
