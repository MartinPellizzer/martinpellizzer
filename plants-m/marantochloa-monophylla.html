Marantochloa monophylla

Marantochloa monophylla, commonly known as the single-leaf marantaceae, is a unique species within the Marantaceae family.

It is characterized by its single, broad, and often variegated leaf that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its striking foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening and botanical collections.
