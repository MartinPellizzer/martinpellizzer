Mellera

Mellera, commonly known as the white mulberry, is a deciduous tree native to Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and heart-shaped leaves. The tree produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically white or pink in color. It is often cultivated for its leaves, which are used to feed silkworms in sericulture.

Additionally, mellera has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
