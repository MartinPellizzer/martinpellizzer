Molina pentaptera

Molina pentaptera, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silver-gray, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark is smooth and often fissured, and it can grow up to 10 meters in height.

Molina pentaptera is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
