Myrtekmania filipes

Myrtekmania filipes, commonly known as the slender myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes other aromatic plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fragrant foliage contains essential oils, making it valuable in traditional medicine and aromatherapy.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
