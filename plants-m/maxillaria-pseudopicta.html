Maxillaria pseudopicta

Maxillaria pseudopicta, commonly known as the picta orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure, which stores water and nutrients. The plant produces striking, colorful flowers with intricate patterns, often in shades of pink, red, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Maxillaria pseudopicta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
