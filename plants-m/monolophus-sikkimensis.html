Monolophus sikkimensis

Monolophus sikkimensis, commonly known as the Sikkim lily, is a striking alpine plant native to the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky and high-altitude environments, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces elongated, spiky seed capsules.

Monolophus sikkimensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens in cooler climates.
