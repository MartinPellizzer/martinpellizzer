Microlicia doryphylla

Microlicia doryphylla, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered inflorescences of yellow flowers that resemble a shower, hence its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, round fruits that enclose a single seed.

Microlicia doryphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
