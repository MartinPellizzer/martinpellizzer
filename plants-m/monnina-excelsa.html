Monnina excelsa

Monnina excelsa, commonly known as tall ironweed, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils in woodland or wetland habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are often reddish in color.
