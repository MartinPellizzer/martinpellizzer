Machaonia martinicensis var. dodgei

Machaonia martinicensis var. dodgei, commonly known as the Martinique mahonia, is a shrub native to the Caribbean island of Martinique.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are leathery and dark green, with a distinctive serrated margin.
