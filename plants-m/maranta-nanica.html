Maranta nanica

Maranta nanica, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants valued for their foliage. The plant is characterized by its striking, silver-green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Maranta nanica is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
