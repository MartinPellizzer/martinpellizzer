Metrosideros pullei var. parvifolia

Metrosideros pullei var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved Christmas bush, is a variant of the Christmas bush native to New Zealand.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright red flowers that bloom during the summer months. The leaves are small, ovate, and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display. This species thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens for its seasonal color.
