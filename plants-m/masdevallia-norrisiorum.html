Masdevallia norrisiorum

Masdevallia norrisiorum, commonly known as the Norris's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It features intricate, showy flowers with vibrant red and white petals, often adorned with contrasting markings. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its delicate structure and unique coloration make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat fragility, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
