Mikania hexagona

Mikania hexagona, commonly known as the creeping oxeye, is a vigorous vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers with a distinctive dark center. The plant has a sprawling growth habit and can form dense mats, often invading disturbed areas. Its stems are covered with fine hairs and can twine around other vegetation.

Mikania hexagona is known for its rapid growth and is considered an invasive species in many regions outside its native range.
