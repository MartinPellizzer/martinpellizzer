Mikania longipes

Mikania longipes, commonly known as the giant Mikania, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rapid growth and long, slender stems that can reach lengths of up to 10 meters. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruits are small, round, and covered in soft, white hairs.

Mikania longipes is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments.
