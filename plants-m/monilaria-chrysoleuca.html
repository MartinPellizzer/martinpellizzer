Monilaria chrysoleuca

Monilaria chrysoleuca, commonly known as the golden leaf lichen, is a foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It has a distinctive yellowish-green thallus with a smooth, leathery texture. The plant forms a symbiotic relationship with algae, which provides it with nutrients through photosynthesis. It is often found on bark, rocks, and soil in temperate regions.

Due to its slow growth and sensitivity to environmental changes, it serves as an indicator species for ecological health.
