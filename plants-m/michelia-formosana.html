Michelia formosana

Michelia formosana, commonly known as the Taiwan Michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Michelia formosana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance.
