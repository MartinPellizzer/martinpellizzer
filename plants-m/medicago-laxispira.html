Medicago laxispira

Medicago laxispira, commonly known as the spiral medic.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. The plant is characterized by its distinctive spiral-shaped seed pods, which give it its common name. It typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is native to parts of Europe and Asia.
