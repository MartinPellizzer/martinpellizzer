Microsorum fortuni

Microsorum fortuni, commonly known as the golden thread fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, narrow leaves that are typically bright green with a slightly serrated edge. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded, moist habitats. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize substrates efficiently.

Microsorum fortuni is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
