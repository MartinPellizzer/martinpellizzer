Melaleuca micrantha var. micrantha

Melaleuca micrantha var. micrantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, clustered white or pinkish flowers that appear in spring. The plant produces capsules as fruit, containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some historical use in traditional medicine.
