Mananthes austroguangxiensis

Mananthes austroguangxiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi mananthes, is a rare flowering plant native to the Guangxi region of China.

It belongs to the family Amaranthaceae and is characterized by its lanceolate leaves and vibrant red-pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. It is notable for its ornamental value and is of interest to botanists due to its limited distribution.

Conservation efforts are ongoing to protect this species from habitat loss and over-collection.
