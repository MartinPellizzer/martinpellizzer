Melastoma laniflorum

Melastoma laniflorum, commonly known as the silky-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glandular-hairy leaves and showy, violet to pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Melastoma laniflorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
