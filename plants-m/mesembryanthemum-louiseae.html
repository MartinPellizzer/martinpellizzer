Mesembryanthemum louiseae

Mesembryanthemum louiseae, commonly known as the Louise's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, adapting it to arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its low maintenance and drought tolerance make it an ideal plant for dry climates.
