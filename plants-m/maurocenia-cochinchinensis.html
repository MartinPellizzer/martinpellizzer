Maurocenia cochinchinensis

Maurocenia cochinchinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue or violet. Its flowers are usually clustered in racemes, and it is known for its ornamental value in gardens.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
