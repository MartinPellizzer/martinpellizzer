Myoporum debile

Myoporum debile, commonly known as the slender myoporum, is a shrub native to the eastern coast of Australia.

It is characterized by its small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale blue flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dunes and sandy soils.

Myoporum debile is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize soil in coastal environments.
