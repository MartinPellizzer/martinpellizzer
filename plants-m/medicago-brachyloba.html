Medicago brachyloba

Medicago brachyloba, commonly known as the short-lobed medick, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in dry conditions.

Medicago brachyloba is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
