Muhlenbergia gracilis var. breviaristata

Muhlenbergia gracilis var. breviaristata, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass species native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, bluish-green foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often adorned with silky hairs, enhancing its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and native gardens.

This variety is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
