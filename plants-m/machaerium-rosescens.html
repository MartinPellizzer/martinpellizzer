Machaerium rosescens

Machaerium rosescens, commonly known as the pink machaerium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
