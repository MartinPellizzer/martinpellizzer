Miltonia × bleuana f. aurea

Miltonia × bleuana f. aurea, commonly known as the golden Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, golden-yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green and velvety to the touch. It blooms during the summer months, producing clusters of showy, fragrant flowers.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
