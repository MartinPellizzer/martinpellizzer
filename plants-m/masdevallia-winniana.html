Masdevallia winniana

Masdevallia winniana, commonly known as the Wynn's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit and its striking, colorful flowers that typically feature intricate patterns and a wide range of hues. The plant produces small, wiry pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its humid, shaded habitat. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-shaped, contributing to its overall aesthetic appeal.

Masdevallia winniana is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
