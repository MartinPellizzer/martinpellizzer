Mesembryanthemum mallesoniae

Mesembryanthemum mallesoniae, commonly known as the Malleson's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptations to arid environments. The plant features fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, and it produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
