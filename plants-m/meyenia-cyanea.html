Meyenia cyanea

Meyenia cyanea, commonly known as the blue meyenia, is a strikingly colorful flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep blue to violet, often with contrasting white or yellow markings. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for coastal environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating.

Meyenia cyanea is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
