Myristica pubicarpa

Myristica pubicarpa, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into woody, spiny capsules. The fruit contains a single large seed, which is encased in a reddish, aromatic aril.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local cultures.
