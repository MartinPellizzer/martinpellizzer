Melilotus boumettii

Melilotus boumettii, commonly known as the African melilot, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.

Melilotus boumettii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a forage crop for livestock.
