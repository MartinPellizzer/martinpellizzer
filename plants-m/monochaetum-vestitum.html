Monochaetum vestitum

Monochaetum vestitum, commonly known as the velvet spikegrass, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its dense, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky layer of hairs, giving it a velvety appearance. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers.

This plant is typically found in rocky or sandy soils in arid and semi-arid regions.
