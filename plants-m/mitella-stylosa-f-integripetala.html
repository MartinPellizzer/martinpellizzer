Mitella stylosa f. integripetala

Mitella stylosa f. integripetala, commonly known as the smooth-leaved mitella, is a variant of the mitella plant characterized by its smooth, undivided leaves.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to the Pacific Northwest of North America. The plant features delicate, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its foliage is typically glossy and evergreen, providing visual interest throughout the growing season.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to moist, shaded garden environments.
