Mammillaria winterae subsp. aramberri

Mammillaria winterae subsp.

aramberri, commonly known as the Aramberri mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
