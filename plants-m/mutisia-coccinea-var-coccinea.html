Mutisia coccinea var. coccinea

Mutisia coccinea var. coccinea, commonly known as the scarlet mutisia, is a striking flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red bracts that surround its small, yellow flowers, creating a visually dramatic display. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and covered in fine, silky hairs.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun exposure.
