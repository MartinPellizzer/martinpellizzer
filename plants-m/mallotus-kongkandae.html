Mallotus kongkandae

Mallotus kongkandae, commonly known as the Kona mallot, is a tropical tree native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with elongated leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
