Medinilla rotundiflora

Medinilla rotundiflora, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or pink hibiscus.

It is a flowering plant native to the Philippines, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a tangy, citrus-like flavor. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla rotundiflora is valued for its aesthetic appeal and unique fragrance.
