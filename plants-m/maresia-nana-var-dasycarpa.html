Maresia nana var. dasycarpa

Maresia nana var. dasycarpa, commonly known as the dwarf maresia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense, dark green foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its fruit is a spherical, fleshy berry that ripens to a deep red color.

This species is well adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal dune environments.
