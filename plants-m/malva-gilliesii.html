Malva gilliesii

Malva gilliesii, commonly known as the New Zealand marshmallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a soft, velvety texture and produces showy pink or purple flowers. It thrives in wet, marshy environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Malva gilliesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
