Molinia ujhelyii

Molinia ujhelyii, commonly known as the Hungarian moor grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central and Eastern Europe.

Molinia ujhelyii plays an important ecological role in maintaining the structure and biodiversity of its habitat.
