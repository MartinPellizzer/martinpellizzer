Metastelma bahamense

Metastelma bahamense, commonly known as the Bahamian mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Metastelma bahamense is often found in disturbed habitats and is considered a rare species within its native range.
