Microchilus globosicalcar

Microchilus globosicalcar, commonly known as the globular thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Asteraceae.

It is characterized by its spherical, spiny head of flowers, which is typically composed of numerous small, tightly packed florets. The plant produces both ray and disk florets, giving it a distinctive appearance. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and rocky environments.

Its botanical structure includes a short, stout stem and basal leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
