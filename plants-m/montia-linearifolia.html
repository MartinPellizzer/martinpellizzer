Montia linearifolia

Montia linearifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf montia, is a small annual herb native to western North America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and forest floors. The plant has slender, linear leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. Its small, white flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom in late spring to early summer.

Montia linearifolia is often found in calcareous soils and is a member of the Montiaceae family.
