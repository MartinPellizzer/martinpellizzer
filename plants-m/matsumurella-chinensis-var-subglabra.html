Matsumurella chinensis var. subglabra

Matsumurella chinensis var. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth-leaved Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or purple flowers. The plant features smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
