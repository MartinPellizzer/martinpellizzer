Machaerium gardneri

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring or early summer. Machaerium gardneri is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils, and it plays a role in nitrogen fixation due to its association with rhizobia bacteria.
