Masdevallia notosibirica

Masdevallia notosibirica, commonly known as the Siberian masdevallia, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant for cultivation.
