Microglossa

Microglossa, commonly known as the narrow-leaved twayblade, is a small perennial herb belonging to the orchid family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces tiny, greenish flowers that are typically clustered in a spike. Microglossa is often found in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and meadows.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the orchid family with limited but specific habitat requirements.
