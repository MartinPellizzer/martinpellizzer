Machilus wenshanensis

Machilus wenshanensis, commonly known as the Wenshan machilus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically the Yunnan Province, and is found in subtropical forests. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy dark green upper surface.

The flowers are small and clustered, and the fruit is a drupe, which is an important part of its ecological role in its native habitat.
