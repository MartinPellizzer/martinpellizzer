Mammillaria conspicua

Mammillaria conspicua, commonly known as the showy mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.

Mammillaria conspicua is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
