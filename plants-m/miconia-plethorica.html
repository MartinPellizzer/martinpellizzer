Miconia plethorica

Miconia plethorica, commonly known as the giant miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Miconia plethorica is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some areas due to its aggressive spread and impact on native ecosystems.
