Miconia neomicrantha

Miconia neomicrantha, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Miconia neomicrantha is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticulture.
