Malpigiantha fruticosa

Malpigiantha fruticosa, commonly known as the Brazilian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The bark is smooth and often exhibits a reddish or brownish color, which becomes more pronounced with age.
