Mammillaria xaltianguensis var. aguilensis

Mammillaria xaltianguensis var. aguilensis, commonly known as the Aguilensii Mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with white to pale yellow spines and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
