Melicope oblanceolata

Melicope oblanceolata, commonly known as the broadleaf melicope, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptic to oblanceolate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal and lowland forests.

Melicope oblanceolata is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in some Pacific cultures.
