Maxillaria minus

Maxillaria minus, commonly known as the small maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, with a distinctive lip that is usually darker in color. Its pseudobulbs are short and cylindrical, and it has linear, grass-like leaves.

Maxillaria minus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
