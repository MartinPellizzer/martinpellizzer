Marumia muricata

Marumia muricata, commonly known as the roughleaf marumia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its rough, serrated leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.

Marumia muricata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local cultures.
