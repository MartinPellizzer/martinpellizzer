Microsorum spectrum var. spectrum

Microsorum spectrum var. spectrum, commonly known as the golden phantom fern, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, lace-like fronds that shimmer with a golden hue, giving it its common name. The plant thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is often cultivated in freshwater aquariums. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, forming dense, carpet-like growths.

It is valued for its aesthetic appeal and ease of care in aquatic environments.
