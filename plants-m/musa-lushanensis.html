Musa lushanensis

Musa lushanensis, commonly known as the Lushan banana, is a species native to China, particularly the Lushan region.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered fruit bunches. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its fruit, while edible, is less commonly cultivated compared to other banana varieties.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in tropical botany and horticulture.
