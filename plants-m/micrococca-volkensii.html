Micrococca volkensii

Micrococca volkensii, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has small, narrow leaves and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments. It is often found in rocky soils and is known for its ornamental value.

Micrococca volkensii is also valued for its use in traditional medicine.
