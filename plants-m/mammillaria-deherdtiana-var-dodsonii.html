Mammillaria deherdtiana var. dodsonii

Mammillaria deherdtiana var. dodsonii, commonly known as the Dodson's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
