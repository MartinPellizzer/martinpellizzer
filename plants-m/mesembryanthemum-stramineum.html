Mesembryanthemum stramineum

Mesembryanthemum stramineum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, powdery coating that helps reduce water loss.

Mesembryanthemum stramineum is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
