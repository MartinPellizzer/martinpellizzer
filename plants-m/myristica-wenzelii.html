Myristica wenzelii

Myristica wenzelii, commonly known as the Wenzel's nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and oval-shaped fruits that contain aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a glossy surface, while its bark is thick and rugged.

The plant is valued for its essential oils and has been used in various medicinal applications in indigenous communities.
