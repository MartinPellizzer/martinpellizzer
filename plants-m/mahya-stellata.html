Mahya stellata

Mahya stellata, commonly known as the star mahya, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, star-shaped white or pink flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Mahya stellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
