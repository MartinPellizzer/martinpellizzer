Monanthotaxis glomerulata

Monanthotaxis glomerulata, commonly known as the clustered leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, clustered fronds that form a compact, bushy appearance. The plant has slender, wiry stems that support its delicate, lance-shaped leaves. Its reproductive structures are unique, with spore-bearing clusters forming on the undersides of the fronds.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
