Mesembryanthemum macrorrhizum

Mesembryanthemum macrorrhizum, commonly known as the giant root ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. Its root system is notably deep and robust, enabling it to access moisture from lower soil layers.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
