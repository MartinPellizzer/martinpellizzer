Melastoma bukitrayense

Melastoma bukitrayense, commonly known as the Bukitrayen melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories and rocky slopes. Its leaves are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a glossy surface, while the flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals.

Melastoma bukitrayense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
