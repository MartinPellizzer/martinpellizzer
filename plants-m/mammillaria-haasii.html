Mammillaria haasii

Mammillaria haasii, commonly known as the Haas's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent areoles and short, white bristles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by pale green fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
