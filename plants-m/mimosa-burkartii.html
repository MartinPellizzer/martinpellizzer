Mimosa burkartii

Mimosa burkartii, commonly known as the Burkart's sensitive plant, is a species native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters, with pinnate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in dry, rocky soils.

Mimosa burkartii is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to adapt to various environmental conditions.
