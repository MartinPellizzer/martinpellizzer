Mesadenus Affinis

Mesadenus affinis is a rare and unique flowering plant native to the southern United States. It is known for its striking violet-blue petals and its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. This plant is often found in wetlands and along the edges of streams. Its delicate appearance belies its resilience in challenging ecological conditions. Due to habitat loss, Mesadenus affinis is considered a species of conservation concern.
