Mapania petiolata var. angustifolia

Mapania petiolata var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mapania, is a variant of the mapania plant characterized by its slender, elongated leaves.

It is a member of the Araceae family, known for its unique floral structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or wetlands. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a waxy surface, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in an inflorescence.
