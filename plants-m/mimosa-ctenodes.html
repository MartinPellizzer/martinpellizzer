Mimosa ctenodes

Mimosa ctenodes, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched, a characteristic known as thigmonastic response. Its small, yellow flowers are clustered in spherical inflorescences, adding a striking visual element.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
