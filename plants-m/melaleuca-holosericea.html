Melaleuca holosericea

Melaleuca holosericea, commonly known as the silky tea tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often found in coastal regions and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Melaleuca holosericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to tolerate salty air, making it a popular choice for coastal gardens.
