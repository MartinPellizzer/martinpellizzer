Maxillaria kalbreyeri

Maxillaria kalbreyeri, commonly known as the Kalbreyer's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features a sympodial growth pattern, with each pseudobulb supporting a pair of leaves. Its flowers are borne on terminal racemes and are known for their intricate, intricate petal structure.

Maxillaria kalbreyeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled environment.
