Michelia amoena

Michelia amoena, commonly known as the fragrant Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its ornamental, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top and lighter underneath. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it suitable for shaded garden areas.

Michelia amoena is valued for its pleasant fragrance and is often cultivated in horticulture for ornamental purposes.
