Myosotis traversii subsp. traversii

Myosotis traversii subsp.

traversii, commonly known as the silverweed Forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors and streambanks. Its flowers bloom in spring and are pollinated by insects.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
