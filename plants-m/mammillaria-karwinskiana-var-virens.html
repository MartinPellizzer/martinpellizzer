Mammillaria karwinskiana var. virens

Mammillaria karwinskiana var. virens, commonly known as the Green Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, pale green flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
