Melastoma culionense

Melastoma culionense, commonly known as the Culion bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mountain forests. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a smooth surface.

Melastoma culionense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
