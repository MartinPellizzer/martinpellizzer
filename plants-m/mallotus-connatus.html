Mallotus connatus

Mallotus connatus, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are small, black drupes that are typically produced in late summer.

The species is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
