Michelia kisopa

Michelia kisopa, commonly known as the Kiso pineapple flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits with a sweet, aromatic pulp. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Michelia kisopa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
