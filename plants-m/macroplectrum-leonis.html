Macroplectrum leonis

Macroplectrum leonis, commonly known as the lion's tail, is a striking member of the Araliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its long, drooping inflorescences that resemble a lion's tail, hence its common name. Its leaves are typically large, compound, and have a glossy appearance.
