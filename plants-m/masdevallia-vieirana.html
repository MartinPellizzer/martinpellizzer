Masdevallia vieirana

Masdevallia vieirana, commonly known as the Vieira's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with several leaves and produces inflorescences that bear a cluster of showy, bilaterally symmetrical flowers. Its foliage is usually dark green and strap-shaped, contributing to its ornamental value.

Masdevallia vieirana thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light, making it a prized addition to specialized orchid collections.
