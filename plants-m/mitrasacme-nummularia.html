Mitrasacme nummularia

Mitrasacme nummularia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent native to South Africa.

It features rounded, coin-shaped leaves that are typically silver or gray-green in color, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the summer months. It is drought-tolerant and prefers well-draining soil and full sun.

Its compact growth habit makes it an attractive addition to rock gardens or as a container plant.
