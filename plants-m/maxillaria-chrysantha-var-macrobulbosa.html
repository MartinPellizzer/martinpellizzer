Maxillaria chrysantha var. macrobulbosa

Maxillaria chrysantha var. macrobulbosa, commonly known as the large bulb Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically yellow or orange with red or purple markings. The plant produces thick, fleshy pseudobulbs that store water, allowing it to thrive in drier conditions. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its striking appearance and relative ease of care contribute to its popularity among orchid enthusiasts.
