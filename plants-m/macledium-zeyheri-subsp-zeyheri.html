Macledium zeyheri subsp. zeyheri

Macledium zeyheri subsp.

zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's macledium, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, clustered white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance. It prefers well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
