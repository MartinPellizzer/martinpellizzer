Maxillaria brachybulbon

Maxillaria brachybulbon, commonly known as the short-stemmed maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its leaves are dark green and somewhat fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native arid environments.

The flowers bloom during the dry season, making it a notable species for orchid enthusiasts.
