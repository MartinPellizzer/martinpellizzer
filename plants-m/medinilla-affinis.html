Medinilla affinis

Medinilla affinis, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This evergreen shrub is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. It produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
