Mammillaria fulvispina var. pyrrhocentra

Mammillaria fulvispina var. pyrrhocentra, commonly known as the red-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with numerous white to pale yellow tubercles, each adorned with prominent, reddish-brown spines. The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for cultivation in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Its unique spination and compact growth make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
