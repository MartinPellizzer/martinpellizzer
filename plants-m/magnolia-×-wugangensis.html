Magnolia × wugangensis

Magnolia × wugangensis, commonly known as the Wugang magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a distinct, layered structure. The tree has a broad, rounded crown and produces dark, smooth bark that becomes fissured with age.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks for its aesthetic appeal.
