Maxillaria bolivarensis

Maxillaria bolivarensis, commonly known as the Bolivian maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Bolivia and Peru.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are characterized by their intricate patterns and spurred petals.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
