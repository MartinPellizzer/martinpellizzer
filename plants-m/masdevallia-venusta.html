Masdevallia venusta

Masdevallia venusta, commonly known as the elegant masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that range from deep purples to vibrant reds, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are long and strap-like, contributing to its graceful appearance.

Masdevallia venusta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
