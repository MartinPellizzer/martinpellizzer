Milium effusum var. subacaule

Milium effusum var. subacaule, commonly known as the slender milkweed, is a low-growing perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It features wiry, ascending stems that are often reddish in color, and it produces small, white to pale pink flowers in clusters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered with fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

The plant is notable for its ability to tolerate wet conditions and its role in supporting local insect populations.
