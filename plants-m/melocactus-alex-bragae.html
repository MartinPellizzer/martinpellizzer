Melocactus alex-bragae

Melocactus alex-bragae, commonly known as the Alex Braga melocactus, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its short, columnar stem and distinctive, spiny areoles. The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are typically followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
