Microjambosa reticulata

Microjambosa reticulata, commonly known as the net-veined jambosa, is a tropical shrub native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a distinctive reticulate venation pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by edible, berry-like fruits. Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for horticultural study.

However, it is not widely cultivated due to its limited commercial value and specific growing requirements.
