Marcetia nervulosa

Marcetia nervulosa, commonly known as the narrow-veined fern, is a delicate herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Marcetia nervulosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its elegant, lace-like foliage.
