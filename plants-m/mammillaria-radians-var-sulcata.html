Mammillaria radians var. sulcata

Mammillaria radians var. sulcata, commonly known as the Sulcate Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
