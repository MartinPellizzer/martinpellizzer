Michelia polyneura

Michelia polyneura, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or fragrant magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it produces small, capsule-like fruits.

Michelia polyneura is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant fragrance and showy blooms.
