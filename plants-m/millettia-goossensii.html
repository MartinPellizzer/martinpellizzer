Millettia goossensii

Millettia goossensii, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pod fruits that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Millettia goossensii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial in agricultural settings.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
