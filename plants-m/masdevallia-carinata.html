Masdevallia carinata

Masdevallia carinata, commonly known as the spurred masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit and its intricate, often colorful flowers with spurred petals. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Masdevallia carinata is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
