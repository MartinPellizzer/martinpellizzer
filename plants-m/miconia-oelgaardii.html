Miconia oelgaardii

Miconia oelgaardii, commonly known as the Oelgaard miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests. It produces small, fleshy fruits that are an important food source for various bird species.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
