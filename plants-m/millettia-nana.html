Millettia nana

Millettia nana, commonly known as the dwarf millettia, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Millettia nana typically grows in shaded, moist environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
