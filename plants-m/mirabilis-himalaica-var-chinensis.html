Mirabilis himalaica var. chinensis

Mirabilis himalaica var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese Marvelous Flower, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Nyctaginaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that open in the evening and close by dawn. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It has lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of red, pink, or yellow flowers.
