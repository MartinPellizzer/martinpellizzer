Mahonia fortunei var. szechuanica

Mahonia fortunei var. szechuanica, commonly known as the Sichuan mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, with spiny margins. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by blue-black berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, acidic soil.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and hardy nature.
