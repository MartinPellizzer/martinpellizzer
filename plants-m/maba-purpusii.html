Maba purpusii

Maba purpusii, commonly known as the Chinese mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink or purple flowers. Maba purpusii is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
