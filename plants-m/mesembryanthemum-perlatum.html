Mesembryanthemum perlatum

Mesembryanthemum perlatum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
