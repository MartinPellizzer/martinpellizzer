Mariposa splendens

Mariposa splendens, commonly known as the Splendid Mariposa, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the high-altitude regions of Central America, particularly in Mexico and Guatemala. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display vibrant purple and white hues, often with intricate patterns. Its roots are adapted to thrive in rocky, well-drained soils, and it often grows in open, sunny habitats.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists alike.
