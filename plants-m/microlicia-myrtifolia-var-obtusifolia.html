Microlicia myrtifolia var. obtusifolia

Microlicia myrtifolia var. obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved microlicia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and a bluish-green hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters. Its growth habit is compact, with dense foliage that makes it suitable for ornamental use in gardens.

The varietal form, obtusifolia, distinguishes itself by its broader leaf shape compared to the typical variety.
