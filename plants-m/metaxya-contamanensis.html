Metaxya contamanensis

Metaxya contamanensis, commonly known as the Indian balsa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, flat seeds.
