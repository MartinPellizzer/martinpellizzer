Macaranga mallotiformis

Macaranga mallotiformis, commonly known as the broad-leafed macaranga, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. Its rapid growth and ability to colonize open spaces make it an important pioneer species in ecological succession.
