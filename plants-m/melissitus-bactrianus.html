Melissitus bactrianus

Melissitus bactrianus, commonly known as the Bactrian honeybee, is a species of bee native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dark coloration and robust body structure, adapted for foraging in arid environments. The plant associated with this bee, often referred to as its floral resource, is typically a variety of flowering plants that provide nectar and pollen. Melissitus bactrianus plays a vital role in pollination within its native habitat.
