Meliosma arnottiana subsp. oldhamii

Meliosma arnottiana subsp.

oldhamii, commonly known as the Oldham's meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia. It features opposite, elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, often exhibiting a silvery or pale green underside.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in clusters during the spring. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental foliage in horticultural settings.
