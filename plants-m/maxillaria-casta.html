Maxillaria casta

Maxillaria casta, commonly known as the Casta orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink, white, and purple. Its flowers are borne on lateral branches and have a distinctive, intricate structure.

Maxillaria casta is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
