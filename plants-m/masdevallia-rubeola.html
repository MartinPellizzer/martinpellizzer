Masdevallia rubeola

Masdevallia rubeola, commonly known as the red-stemmed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically produces a single inflorescence with several small, showy blooms. Its foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Masdevallia rubeola prefers cool, humid conditions and is often grown as a shade-loving orchid in cultivation.
