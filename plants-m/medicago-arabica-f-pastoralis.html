Medicago arabica f. pastoralis

Medicago arabica f. pastoralis, commonly known as the pastoral medic.

This plant is a subspecies of the common medick, characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. The plant typically grows in grasslands and disturbed soils, thriving in temperate climates.

It is often used in agricultural settings for soil enrichment and as a forage crop.
