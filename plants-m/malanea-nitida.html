Malanea nitida

Malanea nitida, commonly known as the silverleaf malanea, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Malanea nitida is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
