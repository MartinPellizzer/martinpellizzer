Millettia purpurea

Millettia purpurea, commonly known as the purple millettia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Millettia purpurea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
