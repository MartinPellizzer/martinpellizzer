Matricaria maritima

Matricaria maritima, commonly known as sea chamomile, is a flowering plant native to coastal areas of Europe and North Africa.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has hairy, linear leaves and thrives in sandy or saline soils. It is often used in herbal medicine for its calming properties.

Its essential oil is also valued for its fragrance and therapeutic uses.
