Moussonia fruticosa

Moussonia fruticosa, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spreading growth habit and its striking, fiery orange to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, spherical fruits that split open to release its seeds. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant in warm climates.

The leaves are typically small, elliptical, and arranged alternately along the stems.
