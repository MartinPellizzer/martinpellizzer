Malcolmia lacera var. patula

Malcolmia lacera var. patula, commonly known as the narrowleaf malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features slender, branching stems covered in fine hairs, and its leaves are linear to lanceolate in shape. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
