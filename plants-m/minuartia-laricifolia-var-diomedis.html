Minuartia laricifolia var. diomedis

Minuartia laricifolia var. diomedis, commonly known as the white sand saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink.
