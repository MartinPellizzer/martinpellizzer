Mediocalcar sigmoideum

Mediocalcar sigmoideum, commonly known as the zigzag cactus, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a distinctive, serpentine stem that grows in a zigzag pattern, allowing it to climb or trail along the ground. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are modified into spines, reducing water loss and providing protection from herbivores.

Due to its specialized adaptations, it thrives in environments with limited water and high sunlight exposure.
