Monoptilon

Monoptilon, commonly known as the silverleaf plant, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in heads. Monoptilon is native to regions of southern Africa and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in arid conditions.
