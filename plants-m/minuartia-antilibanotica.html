Minuartia antilibanotica

Minuartia antilibanotica, commonly known as the Libanotica rockcress, is a small flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is native to rocky and arid regions of the Middle East and parts of southern Europe. The plant typically grows in crevices and on rock surfaces, showcasing its adaptation to harsh, dry environments. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern, helping to conserve water and reduce exposure to strong sunlight.
