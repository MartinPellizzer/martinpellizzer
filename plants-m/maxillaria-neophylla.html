Maxillaria neophylla

Maxillaria neophylla, commonly known as the Neophyll's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant features pseudobulbs that support its foliage, and its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette. Maxillaria neophylla thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

It is a popular choice for both indoor cultivation and botanical gardens due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
