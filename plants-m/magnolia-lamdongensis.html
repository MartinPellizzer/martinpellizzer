Magnolia lamdongensis

Magnolia lamdongensis, commonly known as the Lamdong magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the highlands of southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and partial to full sun exposure.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
