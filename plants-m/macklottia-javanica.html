Macklottia javanica

Macklottia javanica, commonly known as the Javanese daisy, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Macklottia javanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
