Mimosa biceps

Mimosa biceps, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Mimosa biceps thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth and unique leaf movement make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
