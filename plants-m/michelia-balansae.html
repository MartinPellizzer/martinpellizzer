Michelia balansae

Michelia balansae, commonly known as the Balansae Michelia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, particularly found in the regions of Yunnan and Sichuan. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy surface.

Michelia balansae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its aromatic properties.
