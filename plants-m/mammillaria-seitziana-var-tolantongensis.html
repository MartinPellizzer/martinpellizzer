Mammillaria seitziana var. tolantongensis

Mammillaria seitziana var. tolantongensis, commonly known as the Tolantongo mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with numerous cylindrical or conical tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
