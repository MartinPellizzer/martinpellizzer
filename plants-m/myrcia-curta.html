Myrcia curta

Myrcia curta, commonly known as the small myrtle or dwarf myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its compact shrub form. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that contain seeds.

Myrcia curta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
