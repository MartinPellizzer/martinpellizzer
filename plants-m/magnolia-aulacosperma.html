Magnolia aulacosperma

Magnolia aulacosperma, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broad-leafed magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. The tree produces woody, cone-like fruit that contains seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
