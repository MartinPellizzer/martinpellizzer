Maxillaria chimalapana

Maxillaria chimalapana, commonly known as the Chimalapa orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in the summer, with flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria chimalapana is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
