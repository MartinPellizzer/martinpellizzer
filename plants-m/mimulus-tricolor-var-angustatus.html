Mimulus tricolor var. angustatus

Mimulus tricolor var. angustatus, commonly known as the narrow-leaf yellow monkeyflower, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces vibrant yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive spurred petal. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. It is often found in stream banks, meadows, and disturbed areas.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological restoration and pollinator support.
