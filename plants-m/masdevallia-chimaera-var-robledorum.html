Masdevallia chimaera var. robledorum

Masdevallia chimaera var. robledorum, commonly known as the Robledor's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features intricate, often bi-colored flowers with a distinctive, elongated lip that is typically adorned with intricate patterns. The plant has a compact growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its humid, high-altitude environment. Its foliage consists of narrow, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts due to its rarity and unique floral display.
