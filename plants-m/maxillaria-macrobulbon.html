Maxillaria macrobulbon

Maxillaria macrobulbon, commonly known as the giant maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, forming pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and fleshy, adapted to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers often have a distinctive coloration, with a white or pale pink base and a darker, velvety center.
