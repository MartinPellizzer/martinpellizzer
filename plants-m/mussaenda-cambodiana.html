Mussaenda cambodiana

Mussaenda cambodiana, commonly known as the Cambodian flame flower, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to red, depending on the stage of blooming. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Mussaenda cambodiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
