Mimulus tricolor

Mimulus tricolor, commonly known as the tri-color monkey flower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant, three-colored petals that typically display shades of red, yellow, and orange. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Mimulus tricolor is a perennial that can be grown in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
