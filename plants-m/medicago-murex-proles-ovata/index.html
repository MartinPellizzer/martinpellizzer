Medicago Murex Proles Ovata

Turk's cap, scientifically known as Medicago murex proles ovata, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is recognized for its distinctive appearance, featuring bright red or orange flowers that resemble tiny caps. This plant thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid landscapes. Its unique shape and vibrant color make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its historical and medicinal uses.
