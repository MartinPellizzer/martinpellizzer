Masdevallia idae

Masdevallia idae, commonly known as the Ida's Masdevallia, is a rare and delicate orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that typically display a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high levels of indirect light. Its pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves are adaptations to its high-altitude habitat.

Due to its rarity and specific ecological requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
