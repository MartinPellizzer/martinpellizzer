Micranthes Brachypetala

Micranthes brachypetala is a small, flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of western North America. It is known for its delicate, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. This species thrives in rocky soils and high-altitude environments, making it a rare and resilient plant. Its scientific name reflects its short petals and compact growth habit. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
