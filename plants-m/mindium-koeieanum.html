Mindium koeieanum

Mindium koeieanum, commonly known as the silverleaf mindium, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for arid climates. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
