Miconia australis

Miconia australis, commonly known as the Australian miconia, is a tropical shrub native to southeastern Australia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia australis is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and coastal scrublands.
