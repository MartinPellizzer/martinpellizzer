Moraea helmei

Moraea helmei, commonly known as the Cape bellflower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Moraea helmei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
