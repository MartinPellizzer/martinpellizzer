Millettia vasta

Millettia vasta, commonly known as the giant sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and can grow up to 30 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, and its fruit is a legume containing several seeds. Millettia vasta is often found in lowland and hill forest areas, thriving in humid, warm climates.

Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
