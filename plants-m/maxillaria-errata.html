Maxillaria errata

Maxillaria errata, commonly known as the Orchid of the Andes, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces racemes of fragrant, yellowish-green flowers with darker markings. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil. It blooms during the cooler months, typically from late winter to early spring.

Its unique floral structure and adaptability make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
