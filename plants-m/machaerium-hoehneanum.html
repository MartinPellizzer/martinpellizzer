Machaerium hoehneanum

Machaerium hoehneanum, commonly known as the purple machaerium, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its striking purple to violet inflorescences. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters, with pinnate leaves and showy, clustered flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
