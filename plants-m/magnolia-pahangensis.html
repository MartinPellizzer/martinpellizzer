Magnolia pahangensis

Magnolia pahangensis, commonly known as the Pahang magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Malaysia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptic in shape and have a smooth texture. It thrives in humid, subtropical climates and is often found in secondary forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
