Millettia rigens

Millettia rigens, commonly known as the yellow millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its pinnate leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in secondary forests.

Due to its ornamental value and nitrogen-fixing ability, it is sometimes cultivated in agroforestry systems.
