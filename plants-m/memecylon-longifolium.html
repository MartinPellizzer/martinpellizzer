Memecylon longifolium

Memecylon longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
