Matelea bicolor

Matelea bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southern United States and parts of Mexico.

The plant belongs to the Convolvulaceae family, which includes other morning glories, and is known for its climbing habit. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.
