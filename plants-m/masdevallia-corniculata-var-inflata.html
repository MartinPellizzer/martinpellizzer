Masdevallia corniculata var. inflata

Masdevallia corniculata var. inflata, commonly known as the inflated masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, pendulous inflorescence and vibrant, often red or orange flowers with a distinctive, inflated lip. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation. Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a standout species among orchid enthusiasts.

It requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture to flourish.
