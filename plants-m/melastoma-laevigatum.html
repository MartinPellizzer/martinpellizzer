Melastoma laevigatum

Melastoma laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of pink or red, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.

Melastoma laevigatum is often found in moist, shaded forest environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
