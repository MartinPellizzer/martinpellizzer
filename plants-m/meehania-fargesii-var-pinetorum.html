Meehania fargesii var. pinetorum

Meehania fargesii var. pinetorum, commonly known as the pineleaf meehania, is a variant of the meehania plant native to China.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves that resemble pine needles, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, clustered in terminal inflorescences.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique foliage.
