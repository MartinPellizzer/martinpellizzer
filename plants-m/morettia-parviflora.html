Morettia parviflora

Morettia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered morettia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in the spring. The plant has slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in high-altitude grasslands.

Morettia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
