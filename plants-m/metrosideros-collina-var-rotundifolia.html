Metrosideros collina var. rotundifolia

Metrosideros collina var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the coastal kauri, is a variant of the New Zealand native pohutukawa.

It is characterized by its rounded, leathery leaves that are typically a deep green. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often found growing along coastal areas and in sheltered bays.

This variety is well adapted to the harsh conditions of the coastal environment, including salt spray and strong winds.
