Myrtus lucida

Myrtus lucida, commonly known as glossy myrtle, is a flowering shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine and as a spice, while the plant is also valued for its ornamental beauty.

Myrtus lucida thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
