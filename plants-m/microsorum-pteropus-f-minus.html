Microsorum pteropus f. minus

Microsorum pteropus f. minus, commonly known as the lesser water fern, is a variant of the popular water fern species.

It is characterized by its smaller, more compact leaves compared to the standard form, with a darker green coloration. The plant thrives in freshwater environments, often found in slow-moving streams and ponds. Its rhizome system allows it to spread easily, making it a versatile aquatic plant.

It is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ease of maintenance.
