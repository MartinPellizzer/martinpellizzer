Mirabilis coahuilensis

Mirabilis coahuilensis, commonly known as the Coahuila marvel, is a flowering plant native to the Chihuahuan Desert in northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often appearing at night. The plant produces a distinctive, fragrant scent that attracts nocturnal pollinators such as moths. It thrives in arid conditions and has adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments.
