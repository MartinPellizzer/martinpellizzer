Medinilla palawanensis

Medinilla palawanensis, commonly known as the Palawan medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth surface and a glossy appearance.
