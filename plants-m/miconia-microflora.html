Miconia microflora

Miconia microflora, commonly known as the small-flowered miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves and clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
