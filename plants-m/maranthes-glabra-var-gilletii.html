Maranthes glabra var. gilletii

Maranthes glabra var. gilletii, commonly known as the smooth maranthes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and covered in a fine, silky texture.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and delicate floral display.
