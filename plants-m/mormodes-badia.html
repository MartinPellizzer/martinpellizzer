Mormodes badia

Mormodes badia, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a striking orchid species native to Madagascar.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
