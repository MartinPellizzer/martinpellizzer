Montanoa mollissima

Montanoa mollissima, commonly known as velvetleaf montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and Central America.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

Montanoa mollissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage and floral display.
