Metastelma tomentosum var. warmingii

Metastelma tomentosum var. warmingii, commonly known as the woolly metastelma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
