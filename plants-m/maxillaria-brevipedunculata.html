Maxillaria brevipedunculata

Maxillaria brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow. The plant typically has elliptical, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. Its inflorescence consists of a short peduncle bearing a few flowers, which bloom during the warm season.

This orchid prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to thrive.
