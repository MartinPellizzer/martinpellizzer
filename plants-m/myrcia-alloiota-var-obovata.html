Myrcia alloiota var. obovata

Myrcia alloiota var. obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically broad and ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale pink, followed by small, fleshy berries.

This variety is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
