Macroscepis pleistantha

Macroscepis pleistantha, commonly known as the African violet.

This plant is native to southern Africa and belongs to the Gesneriaceae family. It is characterized by its rosette of velvety, dark green leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple and white.
