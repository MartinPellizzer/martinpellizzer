Mesembryanthemum villosum

Mesembryanthemum villosum, commonly known as the woolly ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in fine, white hairs that give it a woolly appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
