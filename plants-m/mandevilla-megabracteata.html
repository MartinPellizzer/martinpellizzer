Mandevilla megabracteata

Mandevilla megabracteata, commonly known as the giant bract mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces distinctive bracts, which are modified leaves that serve as colorful, attractive foliage. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Mandevilla megabracteata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
