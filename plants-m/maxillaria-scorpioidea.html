Maxillaria scorpioidea

Maxillaria scorpioidea, commonly known as the scorpion orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features long, slender pseudobulbs covered in dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with a distinctive, claw-like appearance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

Maxillaria scorpioidea is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate with proper care.
