Metaplexis stauntonii

Metaplexis stauntonii, commonly known as the desert morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a herbaceous annual with striking blue to violet flowers that open in the morning and close by midday. The plant has slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in arid environments and is adapted to poor, well-drained soils.

Its rapid growth cycle and showy blooms make it a notable species in desert ecosystems.
