Musa × sapientum f. hookeri

Musa × sapientum f. hookeri, commonly known as the dwarf banana.

This plant is a cultivar of the banana genus, characterized by its compact size and smaller fruit compared to typical bananas. It features broad, sword-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and slightly hairy. The flowers are clustered in a panicle, and the fruit develops in clusters of small, elongated berries.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance requirements.
