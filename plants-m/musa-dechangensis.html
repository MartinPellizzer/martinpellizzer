Musa dechangensis

Musa dechangensis, commonly known as the Dechang banana, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan Province.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible berries that are typically consumed fresh or used in local cuisine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is occasionally cultivated for both aesthetic and culinary purposes.
