Masdevallia wageneriana var. rodolfoi

Masdevallia wageneriana var. rodolfoi, commonly known as the Wagener's Masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of vibrant, pinkish-red flowers with intricate, velvety petals. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and partial shade. Its unique morphology and coloration make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts.
