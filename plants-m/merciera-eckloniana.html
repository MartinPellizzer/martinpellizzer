Merciera eckloniana

Merciera eckloniana, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, round fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
