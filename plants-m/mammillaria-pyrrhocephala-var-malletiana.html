Mammillaria pyrrhocephala var. malletiana

Mammillaria pyrrhocephala var. malletiana, commonly known as the Mallet's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spiny cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with numerous small, rounded tubercles that bear white or pale yellow spines. The flowers are typically pink to reddish in color and bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that prefers well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is often cultivated by cactus enthusiasts.
