Masdevallia polysticta var. crassicaudata

Masdevallia polysticta var. crassicaudata, commonly known as the broad-tailed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green to blackish leaves that are broad and strap-like, giving it its common name. The plant produces intricate, colorful inflorescences with small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and resilience.
