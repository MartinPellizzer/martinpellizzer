Mattia pilosa

Mattia pilosa, commonly known as the hairy mat plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to tropical regions.

It features a dense mat of soft, hairy foliage that helps it retain moisture in arid environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered in fine, silky hairs.

Mattia pilosa is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soil in rocky or sandy areas.
