Muhlenbergia sylvatica var. setiglumis

Muhlenbergia sylvatica var. setiglumis, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, arching stems with fine, silvery-white to gray-green foliage that gives it a distinctive, feathery appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in floral arrangements. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and low maintenance requirements in landscaping.
