Maba merguensis

Maba merguensis, commonly known as the African maba tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and compound leaves. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers followed by fleshy, red berries that contain seeds. It is valued for its timber, which is used in construction and furniture making.

The plant also has traditional medicinal uses in various African cultures.
