Mammillaria notesteinii

Mammillaria notesteinii, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
