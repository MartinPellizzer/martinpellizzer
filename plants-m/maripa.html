Maripa

Maripa, commonly known as *Piper auritum*, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other species like black pepper. The plant is characterized by its trailing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often hairy. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into round, blue-black berries.

Maripa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ability to thrive in humid environments.
