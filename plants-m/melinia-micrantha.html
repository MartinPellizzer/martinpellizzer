Melinia micrantha

Melinia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered melinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually hairy and have a rough texture. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in late summer. Its root system is fibrous and often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Melinia micrantha thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and forest margins.
