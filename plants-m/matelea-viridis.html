Matelea viridis

Matelea viridis, commonly known as green matelea, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and related species. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers. Its leaves are typically opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
