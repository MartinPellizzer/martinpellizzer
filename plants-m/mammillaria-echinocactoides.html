Mammillaria echinocactoides

Mammillaria echinocactoides, commonly known as the Mexican Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique features, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in succulent collections.
