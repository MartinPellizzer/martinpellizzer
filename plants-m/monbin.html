Monbin

Monbin, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, often with a rosette of leaves at the base. Monbin thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
