Masdevallia cloesii

Masdevallia cloesii, commonly known as the Cloesii Masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, strap-like leaves and clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in rocky or forested areas. Its flowers are short-lived but highly ornamental, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Masdevallia cloesii is known for its slow growth and specific care requirements, which make it a challenging but rewarding plant to cultivate.
