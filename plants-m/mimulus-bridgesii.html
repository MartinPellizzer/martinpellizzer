Mimulus bridgesii

Mimulus bridgesii, commonly known as the Sierra primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks. It has heart-shaped leaves and is part of the evening primrose family.

Mimulus bridgesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
