Magnolia henaoi

Magnolia henaoi, commonly known as the Cali magnolia, is a flowering plant native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall.

The flowers emit a sweet fragrance, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
