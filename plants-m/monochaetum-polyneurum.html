Monochaetum polyneurum

Monochaetum polyneurum, commonly known as the slender spike grass, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.
