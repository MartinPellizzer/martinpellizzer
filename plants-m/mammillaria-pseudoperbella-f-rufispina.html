Mammillaria pseudoperbella f. rufispina

Mammillaria pseudoperbella f. rufispina, commonly known as the red-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its spines are typically reddish, giving it its distinctive appearance.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
