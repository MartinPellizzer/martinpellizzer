Melocactus oreas subsp. alex-bragae

Melocactus oreas subsp.

alex-bragae, commonly known as the Alex Braga melocactus, is a species native to Brazil. It is a small, cylindrical cactus with a dense, spiny exterior that helps protect it from herbivores and excessive moisture loss.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, preferring well-drained environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This subspecies is appreciated for its compact size and ornamental value in cactus collections.
