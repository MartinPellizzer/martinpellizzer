Millettia aboensis var. glabrescens

Millettia aboensis var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with alternating leaflets and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant bears pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves distinguish it from other varieties within the Millettia genus.
