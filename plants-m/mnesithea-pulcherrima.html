Mnesithea pulcherrima

Mnesithea pulcherrima, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release flat, winged seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
