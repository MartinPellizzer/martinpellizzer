Microtropis platyphylla var. elliptica

Microtropis platyphylla var. elliptica, commonly known as the broad-leafed microtropis, is a tropical climbing vine characterized by its elliptical, broad leaves.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its stems are hairy and can grow quite long, often reaching several meters in length.
