Magnolia Officinalis F. Rubicunda

Red-flowered magnolia, scientifically known as Magnolia officinalis f. rubicunda, is a striking ornamental tree native to eastern Asia. Its vibrant crimson blossoms stand out against its lush green foliage, making it a favorite among gardeners and botanists alike. This unique variety is a fascinatin variant of the magnolia species, prized for both its beauty and its historical significance. The plant thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. Its blooming season typically spans from late spring to early summer, adding a dramatic flair to gardens and landscapes.
