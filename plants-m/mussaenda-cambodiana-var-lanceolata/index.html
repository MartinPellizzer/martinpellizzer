Mussaenda Cambodiana Var. Lanceolata

Mussaenda cambodiana var. lanceolata is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, spire-like inflorescences. Native to Southeast Asia, this variant boasts elongated, lance-shaped leaves that add to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its unique floral display. The plant is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
