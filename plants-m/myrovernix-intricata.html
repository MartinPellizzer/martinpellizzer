Myrovernix intricata

Myrovernix intricata, commonly known as the spiralleaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its intricate, spiraling leaf patterns and small, pale violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with well-drained soil, often growing in rocky crevices or on tree trunks.
