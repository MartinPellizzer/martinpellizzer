Minuartia × antalyensis

Minuartia × antalyensis, commonly known as the Antalya rockcress, is a hybrid species within the genus Minuartia, which belongs to the family Brassicaceae.

It is a small, mat-forming plant characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The leaves are typically linear to oblanceolate, often with a bluish or grayish hue due to a dense covering of trichomes. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters, which are borne on short pedicels.

This plant is adapted to grow in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments.
