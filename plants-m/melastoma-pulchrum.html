Melastoma pulchrum

Melastoma pulchrum, commonly known as the pretty melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are showy, with five pink or red petals and prominent yellow stamens.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
