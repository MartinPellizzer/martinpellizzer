Macaranga rowlandii

Macaranga rowlandii, commonly known as the silver macaranga, is a fast-growing shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

The plant belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in disturbed soils and sunny environments. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a soft, velvety texture.
