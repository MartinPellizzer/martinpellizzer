Maxillariella curtipes

Maxillariella curtipes, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a slender, wiry stem with a few leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are spurred at the base, a characteristic feature of many orchid species.

Maxillariella curtipes is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
