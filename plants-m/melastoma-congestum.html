Melastoma congestum

Melastoma congestum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated margins. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, with five petals and prominent stamens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, smooth seeds when mature.
