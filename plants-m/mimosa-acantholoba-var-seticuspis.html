Mimosa acantholoba var. seticuspis

Mimosa acantholoba var. seticuspis, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and bipinnately compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in spherical heads. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in arid or semi-arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
