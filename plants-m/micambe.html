Micambe

Micambe, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically grows in compact rosettes with thick, fleshy leaves that store moisture. Micambe thrives in humid, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Its popularity as a houseplant is due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
