Misopates Orontium Var. Grandiflorum

Misopates orontium var. grandiflorum is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It is known for its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. This variant of Misopates orontium stands out due to its larger floral display compared to other varieties. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Its unique appearance makes it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts alike.
