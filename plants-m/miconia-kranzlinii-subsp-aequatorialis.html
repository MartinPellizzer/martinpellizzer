Miconia kranzlinii subsp. aequatorialis

Miconia kranzlinii subsp.

aequatorialis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green.

The plant produces showy, clustered inflorescences with bright yellow flowers that attract various pollinators. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
