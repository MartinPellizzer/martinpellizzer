Memecylon simulans

Memecylon simulans, commonly known as the dwarf memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
