Masdevallia sandhageniana

Masdevallia sandhageniana, commonly known as the Sandhagen's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful inflorescences and delicate, strap-like foliage. This orchid thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, typically growing on tree bark in shaded environments. Its flowers are highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for their unique shape and vibrant hues.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging plant to cultivate outside its natural range.
