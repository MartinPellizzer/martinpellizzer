Mammillaria xanthotricha var. laevior

Mammillaria xanthotricha var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth yellow mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with a smooth, pale green surface, distinguishing it from the typical spiny form of its species. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
