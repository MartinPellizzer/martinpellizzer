Mussaenda polyneura

Mussaenda polyneura, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, resembling flames. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mussaenda polyneura is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
