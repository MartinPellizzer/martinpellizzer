Melastoma elongatum

Melastoma elongatum, commonly known as the slender melastoma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with opposite, ovate leaves that are often hairy. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma elongatum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories.
