Melastoma spondylanthum

Melastoma spondylanthum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.
