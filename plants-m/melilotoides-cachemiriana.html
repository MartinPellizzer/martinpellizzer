Melilotoides cachemiriana

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing abilities. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows.
