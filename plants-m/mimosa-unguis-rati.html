Mimosa unguis-rati

Mimosa unguis-rati, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its delicate, fern-like foliage and yellowish-pink flowers that bloom in clusters. Its seeds are encased in a hard, woody pod, and the plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mimosa unguis-rati is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
