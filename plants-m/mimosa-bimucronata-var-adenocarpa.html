Mimosa bimucronata var. adenocarpa

Mimosa bimucronata var. adenocarpa, commonly known as the腺果 Mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, delicate leaflets. The plant produces yellow, spherical flower heads that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, and hairy capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
