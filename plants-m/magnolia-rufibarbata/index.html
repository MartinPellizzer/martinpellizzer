Magnolia Rufibarbata

Magnolia rufibarbata is a striking species of magnolia native to eastern Asia, known for its vibrant red and pink flowers. This deciduous tree features broad, heart-shaped leaves that provide a lush canopy during the growing season. Its unique bark, which peels in thin, papery layers, adds to its visual appeal. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. With its showy blooms and elegant form, Magnolia rufibarbata is a prized addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
