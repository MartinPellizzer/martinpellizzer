Maxillaria polyantha

Maxillaria polyantha, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, and it produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, fragrant blossoms. Maxillaria polyantha thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
