Millettia splendens

Millettia splendens, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and features compound leaves with multiple leaflets. Its bark is smooth and often light-colored, while its roots are fibrous and well-developed.

Millettia splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
