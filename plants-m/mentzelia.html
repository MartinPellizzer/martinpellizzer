Mentzelia

Mentzelia, commonly known as the blazing star, is a genus of flowering plants in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

These plants are native to North America and are often found in arid and semi-arid regions. They are characterized by their showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to red, and they typically bloom in the summer. Mentzelia species are often drought-tolerant and can thrive in rocky or sandy soils.

Many species are also used in traditional herbal medicine.
