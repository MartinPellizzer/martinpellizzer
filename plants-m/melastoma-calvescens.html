Melastoma calvescens

Melastoma calvescens, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are usually oval-shaped with a smooth surface and a slightly glossy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
