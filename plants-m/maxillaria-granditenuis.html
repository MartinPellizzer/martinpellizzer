Maxillaria granditenuis

Maxillaria granditenuis, commonly known as the large-leafed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, slender pseudobulbs and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow and purple coloration. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria granditenuis is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
