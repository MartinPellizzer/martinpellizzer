Maranta regalis

Maranta regalis, commonly known as the royal maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, ornate leaves that display intricate patterns in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually appearing in clusters during the warmer months.

Maranta regalis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
