Myrosma lutea

Myrosma lutea, commonly known as the yellow myrosma, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding vibrant color to its desert habitat. The plant has narrow, succulent leaves that help it conserve water in its dry environment. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy species adapted to harsh conditions.

Myrosma lutea is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ecological benefits in native gardens.
