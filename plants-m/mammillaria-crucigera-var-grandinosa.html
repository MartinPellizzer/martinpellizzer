Mammillaria crucigera var. grandinosa

Mammillaria crucigera var. grandinosa, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems covered in white to yellow spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
