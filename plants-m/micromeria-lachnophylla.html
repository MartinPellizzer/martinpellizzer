Micromeria lachnophylla

Micromeria lachnophylla, commonly known as the silverleaf micromeria, is a small evergreen shrub native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its silvery, woolly foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal scrub and chaparral habitats.

Micromeria lachnophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native plant gardens.
