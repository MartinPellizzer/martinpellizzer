Monogramma graminoides

Monogramma graminoides, commonly known as the common rock fern, is a perennial fern native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its compact, low-growing habit and dense foliage. Monogramma graminoides thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories or on rocky slopes.
