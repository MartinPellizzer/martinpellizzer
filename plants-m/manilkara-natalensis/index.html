Manilkara Natalensis

Manilkara natalensis is a rare and unique plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is known for its distinctive appearance and ecological significance. This tree can grow up to 20 meters tall with thick, rough bark and large, elliptical leaves. Its fruit produces a milky sap that has been used by local communities for various purposes. Despite its value, it remains under threat due to habitat loss and overharvesting.
