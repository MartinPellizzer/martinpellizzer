Maytenus boarioides

Maytenus boarioides, commonly known as the African mayten, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, elongated fruits containing several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it resilient to drought conditions.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
