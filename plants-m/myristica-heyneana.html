Myristica heyneana

Myristica heyneana, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Its seeds, known as nutmeg and mace, are widely used as spices and medicinal ingredients.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its economic value.
