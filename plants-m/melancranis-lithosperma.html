Melancranis lithosperma

Melancranis lithosperma, commonly known as the Chinese sandthorn, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of China.

It is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and pale green, spiny leaves. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that contain a single seed. It is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with poor soil conditions.

Botanically, it belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is valued for its hardy nature and potential medicinal uses.
