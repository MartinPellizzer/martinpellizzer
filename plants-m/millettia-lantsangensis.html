Millettia lantsangensis

Millettia lantsangensis, commonly known as the Yunnan wistaria, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and pinnate leaves. The plant produces fragrant, pendulous flowers that are typically purple or pink in color. It thrives in humid, temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.
