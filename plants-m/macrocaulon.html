Macrocaulon

Macrocaulon, commonly known as the purple false indigo, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, violet-blue flowers. The plant has pinnately compound leaves and produces pods that contain several seeds.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
