Maguireothamnus jauaensis var. breweri

Maguireothamnus jauaensis var. breweri, commonly known as the Breweri's goldenbush, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and yellowish-green foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy plant for arid landscape designs.
