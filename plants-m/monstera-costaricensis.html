Monstera costaricensis

Monstera costaricensis, commonly known as the Costarica monstera, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop fenestrations as they mature. The plant thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its striking appearance.

Monstera costaricensis is also valued for its ability to purify air and enhance indoor environments.
