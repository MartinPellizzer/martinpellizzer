Masdevallia patriciana

Masdevallia patriciana, commonly known as the Patrician Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces intricate, showy flowers in shades of pink, purple, and white. The plant typically grows at high elevations, often in rocky or forested habitats, and thrives in cool, humid conditions. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its foliage and floral structures.

Masdevallia patriciana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and unique floral morphology.
