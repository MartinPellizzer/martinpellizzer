Mammillaria macrothele var. biglandulosa

Mammillaria macrothele var. biglandulosa, commonly known as the Biglandular Mammillaria, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines, which are often arranged in clusters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are typically ribbed and covered in areoles, which are characteristic of the Cactaceae family.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
