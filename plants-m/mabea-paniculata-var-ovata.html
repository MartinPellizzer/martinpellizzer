Mabea paniculata var. ovata

Mabea paniculata var. ovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed mabea, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in paniculate inflorescences, which are often fragrant. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
