Macrolobium suaveolens var. rondonianum

Macrolobium suaveolens var. rondonianum, commonly known as the Rondonian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland tropical regions.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the rainforest ecosystem.
