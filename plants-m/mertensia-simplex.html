Mertensia simplex

Mertensia simplex, commonly known as bluebells, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, aromatic leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Mertensia simplex is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits to pollinators.
