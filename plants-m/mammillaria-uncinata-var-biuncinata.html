Mammillaria uncinata var. biuncinata

Mammillaria uncinata var. biuncinata, commonly known as the "Bishop's hat" or "Mammillaria with two hooks," is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative succulent.
