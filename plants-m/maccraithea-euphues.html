Maccraithea euphues

Maccraithea euphues, commonly known as the silverleaf maccraithea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
