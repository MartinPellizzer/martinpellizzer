Mallotus clellandii

Mallotus clellandii, commonly known as the white-flowered holly.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of white flowers. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain smooth, black seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subtropical regions.

Mallotus clellandii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
