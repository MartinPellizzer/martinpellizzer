Mengea tenuifolia

Mengea tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf mengea, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Mengea tenuifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
