Mussaenda elegans var. minor

Mussaenda elegans var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf flame bush, is a compact, evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a red or pink throat. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in garden landscapes for its vibrant floral display.
