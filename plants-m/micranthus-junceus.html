Micranthus junceus

Micranthus junceus, commonly known as narrowleaf rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers. Native to North America, it typically grows in wet or moist environments such as marshes and stream banks. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it spread vegetatively.

Its botanical features make it an important component of wetland ecosystems.
