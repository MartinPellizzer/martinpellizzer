Miltonia regnellii f. veitchiana

Miltonia regnellii f. veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant features pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, each having a velvety texture and intricate patterns. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among horticulturists for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
