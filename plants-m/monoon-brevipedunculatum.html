Monoon brevipedunculatum

Monoon brevipedunculatum, commonly known as the short-stalked monoon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or water.
