Marsdenia caurensis

Marsdenia caurensis, commonly known as the slender Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant features slender, climbing stems and produces small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

It is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
