Madhuca leerii

Madhuca leerii, commonly known as the small wax tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine.
