Medinilla malabrigoi

Medinilla malabrigoi, commonly known as the Malabrigoi Medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its attractive, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

It prefers warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
