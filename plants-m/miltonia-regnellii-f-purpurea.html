Miltonia regnellii f. purpurea

Miltonia regnellii f. purpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety purple petals and a striking white lip, which contribute to its ornamental appeal. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. It produces showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months.

Due to its beauty and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice for both indoor and greenhouse gardening.
