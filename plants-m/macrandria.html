Macrandria

Macrandria, commonly known as the golden star orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow and green foliage. The plant produces delicate, star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle fragrance. Macrandria thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
