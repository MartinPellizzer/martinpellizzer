Miltonia candida var. grandiflora

Miltonia candida var. grandiflora, commonly known as the white orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers with a subtle pinkish hue at the base. The plant produces fragrant blooms that can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its elegant appearance and ease of cultivation in warm climates.
