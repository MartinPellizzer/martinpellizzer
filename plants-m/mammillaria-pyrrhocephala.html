Mammillaria pyrrhocephala

Mammillaria pyrrhocephala, commonly known as the red-headed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its unique red coloration on the stem and flowers makes it a striking addition to any collection.

It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
