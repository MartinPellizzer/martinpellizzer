Mandevilla cercophylla

Mandevilla cercophylla, commonly known as the scarlet mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red or orange tubular flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and is typically grown as a climber or shrub. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Mandevilla cercophylla is popular in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
