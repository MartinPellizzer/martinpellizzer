Machaerium saraense

Machaerium saraense, commonly known as Sara's machaerium, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. Machaerium saraense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
