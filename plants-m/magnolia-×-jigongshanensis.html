Magnolia × jigongshanensis

Magnolia × jigongshanensis, commonly known as the Jigongshan magnolia, is a hybrid plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, glossy leaves and can grow as a deciduous tree or shrub. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens.

Its botanical features make it a unique example of hybridization in the Magnoliaceae family.
