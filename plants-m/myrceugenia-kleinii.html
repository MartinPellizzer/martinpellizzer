Myrceugenia kleinii

Myrceugenia kleinii, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a species of flowering plant native to the temperate rainforests of southern Brazil.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other well-known species like eucalyptus and clove. Myrceugenia kleinii thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
