Mystroxylon oligocarpum

Mystroxylon oligocarpum, commonly known as the golden burl, is a member of the Burseraceae family and is native to parts of Central and South America.

Its botanical features make it an important species in tropical ecosystems, though it is relatively rare and faces conservation challenges.
