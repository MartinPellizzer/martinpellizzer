Malaxis subtilis

Malaxis subtilis, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from the soil.

Malaxis subtilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
