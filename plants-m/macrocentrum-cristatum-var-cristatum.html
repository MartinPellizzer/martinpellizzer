Macrocentrum cristatum var. cristatum

Macrocentrum cristatum var. cristatum, commonly known as the crested or curled fern, is a unique variety of the genus Macrocentrum.

It is characterized by its distinctive, curled fronds that give it an ornamental appearance. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its foliage is typically dark green with a velvety texture, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

This variety is often cultivated for its visual appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
