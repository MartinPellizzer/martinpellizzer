Medicago × media

Medicago × media, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it a valuable forage crop. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in pasture and erosion control.

Its botanical features include a robust root system and a bushy growth habit.
