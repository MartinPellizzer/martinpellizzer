Myosotis lingulata var. stolonifera

Myosotis lingulata var. stolonifera, commonly known as the stoloniferous blue-eyed Mary, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping stems that produce numerous small, sky-blue flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is a perennial species that spreads through stolons, forming dense mats of foliage and blooms.
