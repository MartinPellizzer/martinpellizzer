Medinilla urophylla

Medinilla urophylla, commonly known as the velvet medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are thick and waxy, providing a lush appearance, while its flowers bloom in spring or early summer.

Medinilla urophylla is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and air-purifying qualities.
