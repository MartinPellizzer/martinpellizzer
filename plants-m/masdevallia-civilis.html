Masdevallia civilis

Masdevallia civilis, commonly known as the "Civil's Masdevallia," is a member of the Orchidaceae family native to the Andean regions of South America.

This terrestrial orchid typically grows in cool, humid environments at high altitudes. It features delicate, often pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored, spurred flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a short, fleshy rhizome and linear, grass-like leaves.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
