Macroclinium confertum

Macroclinium confertum, commonly known as the broad-leaved orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom during the warmer months.

The orchid has a complex root system and is often propagated through seed or division.
