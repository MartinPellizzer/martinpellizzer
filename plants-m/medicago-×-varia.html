Medicago × varia

Medicago × varia, commonly known as the variable medick, is a hybrid legume species that exhibits a range of morphological variations.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are usually alternate and have a distinctive shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and waste areas.

Medicago × varia has a deep root system that helps it tolerate drought conditions.
