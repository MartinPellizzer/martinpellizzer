Massonia coronata

Massonia coronata, commonly known as the Crowned Massonia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
