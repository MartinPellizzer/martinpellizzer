Myristica warburgii subsp. siphonantha

Myristica warburgii subsp.

siphonantha, commonly known as the fragrant nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg.

The plant typically grows in humid, lowland areas with rich, well-drained soil. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical, while its flowers are small and yellowish.

The subspecies is distinguished by its elongated fruiting structures and unique fragrance, making it valuable in both culinary and medicinal applications.
