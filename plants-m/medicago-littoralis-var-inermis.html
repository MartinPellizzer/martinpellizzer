Medicago littoralis var. inermis

Medicago littoralis var. inermis, commonly known as coastal medick, is a perennial legume native to coastal regions of Europe and Asia.

It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found along shorelines and dunes. The plant features trifoliate leaves with small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its root system helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

It is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
