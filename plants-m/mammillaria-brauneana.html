Mammillaria brauneana

Mammillaria brauneana, commonly known as the Braune's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of spines and tubercles. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a pale green to bluish-gray color. Its flowers are usually yellow or pale orange, appearing in the axils of the areoles.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
