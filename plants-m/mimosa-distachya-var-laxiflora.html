Mimosa distachya var. laxiflora

Mimosa distachya var. laxiflora, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Americas.

It features slender, branched stems with pinnately compound leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, globular flower heads that are clustered in dense, branched inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in prairies and disturbed areas.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and ability to colonize open spaces, making it a common sight in native plant communities.
