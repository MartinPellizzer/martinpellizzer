Macrozamia peroffskyana

Macrozamia peroffskyana, commonly known as the slender cycad, is a slow-growing, evergreen plant native to eastern Australia.

It belongs to the Cycadaceae family and is characterized by its stout, unbranched trunk and a crown of stiff, feather-like leaves. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a warm, sunny climate to flourish.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is also protected in many regions.
