Maxillaria paredesiorum

Maxillaria paredesiorum, commonly known as the Paredesiorum Orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth during dry periods. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It prefers shady, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and ecological role in its native habitats.
