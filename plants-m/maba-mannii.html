Maba mannii

Maba mannii, commonly known as the African milkwood tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and alternate leaves. The tree is often found in dry, rocky habitats and plays a role in local ecosystems.
