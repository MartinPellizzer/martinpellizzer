Macaranga megalophylla

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
