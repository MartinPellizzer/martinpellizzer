Miconia impressinervis

Miconia impressinervis, commonly known as the broadleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves that are often veined with a distinctive pattern. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia impressinervis is known for its rapid growth and has been introduced to various regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
