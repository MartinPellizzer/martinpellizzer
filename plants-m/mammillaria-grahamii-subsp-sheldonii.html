Mammillaria grahamii subsp. sheldonii

Mammillaria grahamii subsp.

sheldonii, commonly known as Sheldon's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem with prominent tubercles and white to pale blue spines.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This subspecies is valued for its compact size and ornamental appeal in succulent collections.
