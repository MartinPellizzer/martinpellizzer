Madhuca pacifica

Madhuca pacifica, commonly known as the Pacific madhuca, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and fragrant white flowers.
