Maytenus peduncularis

Maytenus peduncularis, commonly known as the Brazilian mayten, is a deciduous tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Cornaceae and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by woody, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds. Maytenus peduncularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
