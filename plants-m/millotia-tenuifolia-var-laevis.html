Millotia tenuifolia var. laevis

Millotia tenuifolia var. laevis, commonly known as the smooth millotia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are smooth and glabrous on both surfaces. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticultural and ecological studies.
