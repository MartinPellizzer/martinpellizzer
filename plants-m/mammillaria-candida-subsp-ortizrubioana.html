Mammillaria candida subsp. ortizrubioana

Mammillaria candida subsp.

ortizrubioana, commonly known as the Ortiz Rubio's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles, which are adorned with white or pale yellow spines.

The plant produces small, showy yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

This subspecies is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
