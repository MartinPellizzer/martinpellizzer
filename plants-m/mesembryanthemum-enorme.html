Mesembryanthemum enorme

Mesembryanthemum enorme, commonly known as the giant ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring and early summer. Its unique structure and ability to survive in harsh conditions make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

However, it requires well-draining soil and full sunlight to grow optimally.
