Mertensia viridis var. caelestina

Mertensia viridis var. caelestina, commonly known as sky blue cohosh, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its botanical characteristics include a rhizomatous root system and showy, star-shaped blooms.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.
