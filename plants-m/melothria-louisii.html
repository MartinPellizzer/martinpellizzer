Melothria louisii

Melothria louisii, commonly known as the dwarf sourkraut, is a small, trailing vine native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and gourds, and is characterized by its hairy, heart-shaped leaves. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges and disturbed areas.
