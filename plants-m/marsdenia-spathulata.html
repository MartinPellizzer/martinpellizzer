Marsdenia spathulata

Marsdenia spathulata, commonly known as the broad-leafed Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant contains milky sap and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
