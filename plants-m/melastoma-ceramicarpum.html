Melastoma ceramicarpum

Melastoma ceramicarpum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its showy, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
