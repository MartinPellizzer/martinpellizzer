Mulgedium roborovskii

Mulgedium roborovskii, commonly known as the Siberian sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the cold regions of Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of less than 10 centimeters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are fleshy and succulent, adapted to retain moisture in arid environments.

Mulgedium roborovskii thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
