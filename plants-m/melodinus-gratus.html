Melodinus gratus

Melodinus gratus, commonly known as the sweet-scented bellflower, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible and contain seeds. Melodinus gratus thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and botanical collections.
