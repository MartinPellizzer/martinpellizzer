Masdevallia × urbanae

Masdevallia × urbanae, commonly known as the hybrid masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Masdevallia leuca and Masdevallia rhombifolia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers with a unique combination of traits from both parent species. The plant typically produces small, delicate blooms that are arranged in a spike, showcasing a variety of colors including white, pink, and red. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Masdevallia × urbanae is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and the genetic diversity it represents.
