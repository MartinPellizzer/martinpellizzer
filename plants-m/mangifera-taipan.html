Mangifera taipan

Mangifera taipan, commonly known as the Taipan mango, is a tropical fruit tree native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, elongated mangoes with a distinctive yellow-orange skin and a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, sharing botanical characteristics with other mango species.

The tree can reach heights of up to 10 meters and is valued for both its fruit and ornamental qualities.
