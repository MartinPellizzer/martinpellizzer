Mimosa axillaris

Mimosa axillaris, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often in response to touch or light. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and sensitivity to environmental stimuli make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
