Muhlenbergia mexicana var. filiformis

Muhlenbergia mexicana var. filiformis, commonly known as the Mexican muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, hair-like leaves that give it a delicate appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands or along stream banks. Its inflorescence consists of a panicle of small, spikelet-like flowers, which are usually pale green to brown in color.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
