Miconia dorsaliporosa

Miconia dorsaliporosa, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Miconia dorsaliporosa is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
