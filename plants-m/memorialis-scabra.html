Memorialis scabra

Memorialis scabra, commonly known as the rough-stemmed memora, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that are covered in fine, rough hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Memorialis scabra is often grown in gardens for its delicate appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
