Mauria obtusifolia

Mauria obtusifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved mauria, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and broad, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in a compact cluster, which bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Mauria obtusifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
