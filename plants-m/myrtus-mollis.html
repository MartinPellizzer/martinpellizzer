Myrtus mollis

Myrtus mollis, commonly known as the small myrtle, is a compact evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy underneath. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. Myrtus mollis thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its pleasant fragrance and hardy nature.
