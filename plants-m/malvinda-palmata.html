Malvinda palmata

Malvinda palmata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or malvinda palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palm-like leaves that are typically green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are often pink or white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Malvinda palmata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands.
