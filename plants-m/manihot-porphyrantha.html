Manihot porphyrantha

Manihot porphyrantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered arrowleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves with a purple hue on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments. The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological adaptations.
