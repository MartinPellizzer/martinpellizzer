Maxillaria harrisoniae var. angustior

Maxillaria harrisoniae var. angustior, commonly known as the narrow-leaved harrisonia, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern, while the pseudobulbs are cylindrical and somewhat fleshy. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
