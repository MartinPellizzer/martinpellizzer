Messerschmidia salzmannii

Messerschmidia salzmannii, commonly known as the Salzmann's rockrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.
