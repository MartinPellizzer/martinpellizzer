Melocactus baarsianus

Melocactus baarsianus, commonly known as the Baars' melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of spines. The plant produces a bright yellow to orange fruit, which is edible and resembles a small melon. It thrives in arid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
