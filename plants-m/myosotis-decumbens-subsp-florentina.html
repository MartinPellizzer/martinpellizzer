Myosotis decumbens subsp. florentina

Myosotis decumbens subsp.

florentina, commonly known as the florentine forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe. It features delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July.

The plant has a low-growing habit with creeping stems that root at the nodes, forming dense mats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and woodland edges.
