Mentha haplocalyx var. piperascens

Mentha haplocalyx var. piperascens, commonly known as the giant Chinese mint, is a variant of the Mentha haplocalyx species native to China.

It is characterized by its large, serrated leaves and a strong, minty aroma. This plant is a perennial herb that thrives in moist, shady environments. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic herbs like basil and thyme.

The plant is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported cooling and digestive properties.
