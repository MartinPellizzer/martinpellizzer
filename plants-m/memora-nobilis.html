Memora nobilis

Memora nobilis, commonly known as the noble memorial plant, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its elegant, upright growth habit and its clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces dark purple berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans. Memora nobilis thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from spring to early summer.
