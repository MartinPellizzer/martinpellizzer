Mammillaria parrasensis

Mammillaria parrasensis, commonly known as the Parrasensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens and indoor collections.
