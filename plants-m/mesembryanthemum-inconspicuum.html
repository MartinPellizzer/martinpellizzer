Mesembryanthemum inconspicuum

Mesembryanthemum inconspicuum, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

Its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions makes it a resilient choice for low-maintenance landscaping.
