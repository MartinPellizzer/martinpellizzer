Meibomia capitata

Meibomia capitata, commonly known as the Cape York hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal or sandy soils. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a hairy texture.
