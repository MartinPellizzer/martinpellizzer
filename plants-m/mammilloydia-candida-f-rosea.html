Mammilloydia candida f. rosea

Mammilloydia candida f. rosea, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pink spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces delicate, pinkish-white flowers during the summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low water requirements.

Its unique coloration and gentle appearance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
