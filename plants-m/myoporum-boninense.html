Myoporum boninense

Myoporum boninense, commonly known as the Bonin myoporum, is a shrub native to the Bonin Islands.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or rocky habitats.

Myoporum boninense is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native environment.
