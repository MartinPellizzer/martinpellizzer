Maxillaria × dunstervillei

Maxillaria × dunstervillei, commonly known as the hybrid orchid, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Maxillaria tenuiflora and Maxillaria sanguinea.

It is a sympodial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces pseudo-bulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red with white or yellow markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright but indirect light and requires well-draining potting media.

This hybrid is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
