Masdevallia scabrilinguis

Masdevallia scabrilinguis, commonly known as the rough-leaved masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, rough-textured layer of pubescence. The plant produces intricate inflorescences with small, colorful flowers in shades of pink and white. Its growth habit is sympodial, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This species is valued for its unique foliage and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in specialized collections.
