Melhania randii

Melhania randii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Melhania randii is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its potential use in traditional medicine.
