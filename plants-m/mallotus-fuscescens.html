Mallotus fuscescens

Mallotus fuscescens, commonly known as the black mangrove, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to the coastal regions of the Americas, particularly in tropical and subtropical areas. The plant plays an important ecological role in stabilizing coastal ecosystems and providing habitat for various species.
