Myosotis sericea

Myosotis sericea, commonly known as silky forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to purple flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has narrow, silver-tomentose leaves that give it a distinctive silvery appearance. It typically grows in moist, shady habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.

Myosotis sericea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming, dainty blossoms.
