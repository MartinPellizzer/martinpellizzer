Mussaenda caudatiloba

Mussaenda caudatiloba, commonly known as the long-tailed Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Mussaenda caudatiloba thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
