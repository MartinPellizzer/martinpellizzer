Madorius

Madorius, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom above the water surface, while its leaves are broad and floating. The plant has a complex root system that helps stabilize the sediment in its aquatic habitat. Madorius is often cultivated for its ornamental value and holds cultural significance in various Asian traditions.

Its flowers typically range in color from white to pink and yellow, depending on the species.
