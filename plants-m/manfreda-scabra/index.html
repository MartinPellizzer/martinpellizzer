Manfreda Scabra

Manfreda scabra is a striking desert plant known for its unique, spiny leaves and vibrant flowers. Native to the arid regions of North America, it thrives in harsh, dry environments. This species belongs to the Agavaceae family and is often found in rocky soils and crevices. Its ability to store water makes it highly adapted to drought conditions. Despite its tough exterior, Manfreda scabra is a symbol of resilience and beauty in the desert landscape.
