Mesembryanthemum coralliflorum

Mesembryanthemum coralliflorum, commonly known as the coral flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and clustered, small, bright yellow flowers. Mesembryanthemum coralliflorum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
