Mesembryanthemum pugioniforme

Mesembryanthemum pugioniforme, commonly known as the swordleaf ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, sword-shaped leaves that are typically blue-gray in color, adapted for water storage and reducing transpiration. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often blooming in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its resilience to drought and heat makes it an ideal plant for arid climates.
