Medicago falcata var. minor

Medicago falcata var. minor, commonly known as the lesser goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces cylindrical, erect pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
