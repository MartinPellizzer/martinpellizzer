Melastoma smilacifolium

Melastoma smilacifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a silvery underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and have five petals that are often pink or white.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
