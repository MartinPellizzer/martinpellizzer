Microlicia bivalvis

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and hairy foliage. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
