Muscarella catoxys

Muscarella catoxys, commonly known as the narrow-leaf muscari, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its grass-like leaves and clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers.
