Medusea tuberculata

Medusea tuberculata, commonly known as the warty medusa, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive, warty appearance.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia. The plant features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that are often covered in small, raised bumps or tubercles. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, often in shades of green and yellow.

Medusea tuberculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
