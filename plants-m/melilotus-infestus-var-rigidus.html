Melilotus infestus var. rigidus

Melilotus infestus var. rigidus, commonly known as rigid melilot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its erect, rigid stems and compound leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces yellow pea-like flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Due to its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
