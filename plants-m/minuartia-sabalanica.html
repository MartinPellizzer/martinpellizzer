Minuartia sabalanica

Minuartia sabalanica, commonly known as the Sabalan rock-cress, is a small, flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its cruciform flowers and capsule fruits. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. It has a low-growing habit with fleshy, blue-green leaves and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.
