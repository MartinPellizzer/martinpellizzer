Melastoma longisepalum

Melastoma longisepalum, commonly known as the long-sepal melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
