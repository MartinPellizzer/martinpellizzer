Matthiola pseudoxyceras var. perennis

Matthiola pseudoxyceras var. perennis, commonly known as the everlasting wallflower, is a perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that remain open for an extended period. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
