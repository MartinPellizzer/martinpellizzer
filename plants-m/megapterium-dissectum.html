Megapterium dissectum

Megapterium dissectum, commonly known as the dissected winged bean, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately compound leaves that are deeply lobed and resemble the wings of a bird. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisines.
