Mikania decora

Mikania decora, commonly known as the showy Mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and are often covered in fine hairs.

Mikania decora is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
