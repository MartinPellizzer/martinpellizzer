Micranthes stellaris var. obovata

Micranthes stellaris var. obovata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It features small, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming in early spring. The leaves are lanceolate to obovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface, and are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
