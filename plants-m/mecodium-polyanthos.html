Mecodium polyanthos

Mecodium polyanthos, commonly known as the yellow mecodium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features basal rosettes of fleshy, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, clustered yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its compact size and vibrant floral display.
