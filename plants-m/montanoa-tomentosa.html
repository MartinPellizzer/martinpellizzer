Montanoa tomentosa

Montanoa tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are ovate to lanceolate in shape and covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Montanoa tomentosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
