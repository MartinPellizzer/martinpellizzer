Mangostana morella

Mangostana morella, commonly known as the mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Garcinieae and is characterized by its thick, ridged, dark purple bark. The tree produces a strikingly segmented fruit with a bright red outer rind and a white, juicy, segmented flesh. The fruit is rich in xanthones, which are bioactive compounds with potential health benefits.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species in both horticulture and traditional medicine.
