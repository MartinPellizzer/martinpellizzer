Myrtus brasiliana var. diversifolia

Myrtus brasiliana var. diversifolia, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle or variegated myrtle, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings, adding visual interest. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that are followed by small, dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
