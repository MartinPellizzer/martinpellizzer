Medicago falcata var. dzumagalensis

Medicago falcata var. dzumagalensis, commonly known as the Dzumagalen falcate medick, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, typical of the Fabaceae family. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in grasslands and steppe regions. It has small, elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is noted for its adaptability to harsh conditions and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
