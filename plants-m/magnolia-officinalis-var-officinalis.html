Magnolia officinalis var. officinalis

Magnolia officinalis var. officinalis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the officinal magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its flowers are typically pink to white in color and are arranged in clusters.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional Chinese medicine for its therapeutic properties.
