Maxillaria porphyrostele var. minutipunctulata

Maxillaria porphyrostele var. minutipunctulata, commonly known as the miniature speckled maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features a pseudobulbous structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are often adorned with intricate speckles and patterns.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
