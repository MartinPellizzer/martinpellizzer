Melodorum griffithii

Melodorum griffithii, commonly known as the Himalayan bellflower, is a perennial herb native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its bell-shaped, blue or violet flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in cool, moist conditions.
