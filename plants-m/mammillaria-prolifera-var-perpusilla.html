Mammillaria prolifera var. perpusilla

Mammillaria prolifera var. perpusilla, commonly known as the miniature hedgehog cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense cluster of small, spherical stems covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces delicate, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
