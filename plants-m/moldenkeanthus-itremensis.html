Moldenkeanthus itremensis

Moldenkeanthus itremensis, commonly known as the Itremense velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central Africa.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental appearance and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
