Mammillaria rhodeocentra var. gracillima

Mammillaria rhodeocentra var. gracillima, commonly known as the graceful mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated as a popular ornamental plant.
