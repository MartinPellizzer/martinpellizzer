Microtropis chartacea

Microtropis chartacea, commonly known as the paperleaf microtropis, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thin, papery leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
