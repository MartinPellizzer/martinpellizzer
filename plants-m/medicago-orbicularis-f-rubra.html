Medicago orbicularis f. rubra

Medicago orbicularis f. rubra, commonly known as the red-flowered burclover, is a variant of the annual legume Medicago orbicularis.

It is characterized by its distinctive red flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
