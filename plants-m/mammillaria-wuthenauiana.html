Mammillaria wuthenauiana

Mammillaria wuthenauiana, commonly known as the Wuthenau's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
