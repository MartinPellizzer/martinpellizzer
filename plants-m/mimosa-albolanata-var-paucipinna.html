Mimosa albolanata var. paucipinna

Mimosa albolanata var. paucipinna, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features slender, branching stems with small, bipinnate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces yellow, spherical flower heads that open in response to touch, a characteristic known as thigmonastic movement. Its flowers are followed by flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This species is adapted to dry, open habitats and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.
