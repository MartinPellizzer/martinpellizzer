Medinilla sandakanensis

Medinilla sandakanensis, commonly known as the Sandakan Medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its dense, glossy foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of pink or white flowers that bloom throughout the year, making it a popular ornamental species. Medinilla sandakanensis is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
