Muehlenbeckia chilensis var. brachybotrys

Muehlenbeckia chilensis var. brachybotrys, commonly known as the Chilean muhlenbeckia, is a low-growing, herbaceous plant native to southern South America.

It features dense, mat-forming growth with small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a ground cover.

Its compact size and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
