Musa × bidigitalis

Musa × bidigitalis, commonly known as the elephant ear plant, is a hybrid species within the Musa genus.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in diameter. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

The plant requires well-draining soil and regular watering to maintain its health and vigor.
