Malpighia pallida

Malpighia pallida, commonly known as the pale malpighia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malpighiaceae.

It is native to Central and South America, particularly found in regions of Mexico and the Caribbean. The plant is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its fruits are small, fleshy berries that contain several seeds.

Malpighia pallida is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various growing conditions.
