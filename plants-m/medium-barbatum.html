Medium barbatum

Medium barbatum, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, violet-blue flowers that bloom in summer. Its berries, which are toxic, are often mistaken for edible fruits.

Medium barbatum is typically found in dry, rocky soils and is part of the Solanaceae family.
