Myristica mindanaensis

Myristica mindanaensis, commonly known as the Mindanao nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, reddish-brown seeds that resemble nutmeg. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers and has pinnate leaves with a leathery texture. Its fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed, which is the valuable part used in traditional medicine and as a spice.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
