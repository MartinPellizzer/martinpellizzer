Muhlenbergia umbrosa f. attenuata

Muhlenbergia umbrosa f. attenuata, commonly known as the slender muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, arching blades that are typically blue-green in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value, especially in late summer and autumn. It thrives in partial to full shade and is well-suited for woodland gardens.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a valuable addition to native plant landscapes.
