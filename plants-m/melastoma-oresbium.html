Melastoma oresbium

Melastoma oresbium, commonly known as the red-rooted beauty, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough texture with a waxy coating.

Melastoma oresbium is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
