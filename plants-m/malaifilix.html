Malaifilix

Malaifilix, commonly known as the flameleaf orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored leaves that range from orange to red. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Malaifilix thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect sunlight.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
