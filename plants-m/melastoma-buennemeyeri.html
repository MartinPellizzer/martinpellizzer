Melastoma buennemeyeri

Melastoma buennemeyeri, commonly known as the pink melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that disperses seeds through wind, contributing to its natural distribution.
