Manihot rigidifolia

Manihot rigidifolia, commonly known as the rigid thornapple, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, spiny stems. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically ovate with a rough texture and may have a slightly hairy surface.

Manihot rigidifolia is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
