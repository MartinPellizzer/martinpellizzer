Myrcia deflexa var. dussii

Myrcia deflexa var. dussii, commonly known as the Dussi myrtle, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
