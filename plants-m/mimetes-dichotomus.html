Mimetes dichotomus

Mimetes dichotomus, commonly known as the mountain pride, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its erect, branched stems and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to white, and it produces small, oblong fruits that contain seeds.

Mimetes dichotomus is often used in rock gardens and is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
