Malcolmia graeca

Malcolmia graeca, commonly known as the Greek malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling those of the genus Senecio. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Malcolmia graeca is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
