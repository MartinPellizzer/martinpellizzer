Matucana haynii subsp. hystrix

Matucana haynii subsp.

hystrix, commonly known as the hystrix matucana, is a species of cactus native to Brazil. It is characterized by its cylindrical stems that are typically green to blue-green in color.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by spiny, ribbed fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
