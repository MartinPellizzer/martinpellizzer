Mimosa xanthocentra var. mansii

Mimosa xanthocentra var. mansii, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait known as thigmonastic response. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value comes from its delicate foliage and long-lasting blooms.
