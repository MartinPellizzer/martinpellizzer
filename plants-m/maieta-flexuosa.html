Maieta flexuosa

Maieta flexuosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It features slender, twisting stems that allow it to climb and spread across surfaces. The leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and typically white or pale blue, appearing in clusters.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to cover trellises or fences.
