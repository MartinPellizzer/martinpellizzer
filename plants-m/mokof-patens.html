Mokof patens

Mokof patens, commonly known as the broadleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green on the upper surface and lighter in color on the underside. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow, followed by the development of small, round fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
