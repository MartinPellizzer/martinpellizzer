Massonia obermeyerae

Massonia obermeyerae, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with succulent, fleshy leaves that help it store water. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value and adaptability to arid conditions make it a favored plant among succulent enthusiasts.
