Maxillaria humilis

Maxillaria humilis, commonly known as the dwarf maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a compact, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are usually borne on short, lateral spikes and bloom during the late spring to early summer.

Maxillaria humilis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
