Moniera humifusa

Moniera humifusa, commonly known as the ground orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with rhizomatous stems that often lie close to the ground. The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Moniera humifusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
