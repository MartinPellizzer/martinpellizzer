Megastachya uninervia

Megastachya uninervia, commonly known as the mountain orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It features a slender stem with a single, upright flower spike that bears showy, violet-blue blossoms. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
