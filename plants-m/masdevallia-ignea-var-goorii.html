Masdevallia ignea var. goorii

Masdevallia ignea var. goorii, commonly known as the fiery orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It features striking red and orange flowers with intricate patterns, making it a visually appealing species. The plant has a pseudobulb structure and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
