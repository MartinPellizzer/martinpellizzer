Meniscium delicatum

Meniscium delicatum, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features delicate, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, elongated fruits called capsules. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland edges or meadows.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological benefits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
