Melastoma baumeanum

Melastoma baumeanum, commonly known as the Baumean beauty or the purple beauty, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom during the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Melastoma baumeanum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
