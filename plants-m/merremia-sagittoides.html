Merremia sagittoides

Merremia sagittoides, commonly known as the arrowhead vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces small, green, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
