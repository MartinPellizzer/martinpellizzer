Mesembryanthemum Macrophyllum

Mesembryanthemum macrophyllum is a striking succulent native to southern Africa. Known for its vibrant, showy flowers and thick, fleshy leaves, it thrives in arid environments. This plant is commonly referred to as the "living stone" due to its unique appearance. It is popular among gardeners for its low maintenance and ability to bloom throughout the year. Its resilience and beauty make it a favorite in both indoor and outdoor settings.
