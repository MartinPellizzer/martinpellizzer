Mora megistosperma

Mora megistosperma, commonly known as the giant mora, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, leathery leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Botanically, it is notable for its robust root system and ability to adapt to varying soil conditions.
