Mammillaria erythrosperma var. similis

Mammillaria erythrosperma var. similis, commonly known as the red-seeded mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a green to blue-green stem covered in white to yellowish tubercles. The plant produces vibrant red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its small, rounded spines are often tinged with red or purple, adding to its ornamental appeal.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its hardiness and aesthetic value.
