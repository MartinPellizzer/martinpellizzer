Marsdenia parva

Marsdenia parva, commonly known as the small Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are typically lance-shaped and have a leathery texture. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually yellow or orange in color, arranged in clusters.

Marsdenia parva is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
