Mussaenda frondosa var. pilosissima

Mussaenda frondosa var. pilosissima, commonly known as the silky Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow or orange flowers, which are often surrounded by red bracts that serve as attractive displays. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and prefers partial to full sun exposure.

This ornamental plant is often cultivated for its striking foliage and showy inflorescences.
