Mesembryanthemum subincanum

Mesembryanthemum subincanum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a bright contrast to its foliage. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to xeriscaping and succulent collections.
