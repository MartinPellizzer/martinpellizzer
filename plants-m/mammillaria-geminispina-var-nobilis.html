Mammillaria geminispina var. nobilis

Mammillaria geminispina var. nobilis, commonly known as the noble mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, white to pale yellow spines and spherical, ribbed stems. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pink or red, blooming in late spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for collectors due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
