Medicago cretacea

Medicago cretacea, commonly known as the Cretaceous medic.

It is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region and parts of southern Europe. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and trifoliate leaves, which are typically gray-green in color. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by pods containing several seeds.

Medicago cretacea is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
