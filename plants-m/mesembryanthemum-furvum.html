Mesembryanthemum furvum

Mesembryanthemum furvum, commonly known as the yellow carpet flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and flowers that open in response to light. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with five petals, which bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
