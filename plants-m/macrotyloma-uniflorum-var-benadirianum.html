Macrotyloma uniflorum var. benadirianum

Macrotyloma uniflorum var. benadirianum, commonly known as the Benadir cowpea, is a variant of the cowpea species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a leguminous plant characterized by its erect growth habit and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically pale yellow or white, and the plant produces pods containing edible seeds. It is well adapted to dry conditions and is often grown as a forage crop.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both agricultural and ecological purposes.
