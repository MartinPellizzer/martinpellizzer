Myosotis australis

Myosotis australis, commonly known as the Australian forget-me-not, is a herbaceous plant native to Australia.

It features delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, seed-like fruits that are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
