Magnolia lacei

Magnolia lacei, commonly known as the lace magnolia, is a species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its delicate, white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has large, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in cool, humid climates and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
