Microphyes robustus var. robustus

Microphyes robustus var. robustus, commonly known as the robust microphyte, is a species of aquatic plant characterized by its dense, floating mats of small, oval leaves.

It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in ponds and lakes. The plant's robust growth habit allows it to form thick vegetation that can dominate water surfaces. Its leaves are typically green and have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.

This species plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
