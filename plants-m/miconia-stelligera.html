Miconia stelligera

Miconia stelligera, commonly known as the sky mist miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Miconia stelligera is often found in moist, shaded areas such as rainforests and is known for its rapid growth rate.

Its ecological significance lies in its role as a food source for certain insect species and its potential use in traditional medicine.
