Mammillaria cornimamma

Mammillaria cornimamma, commonly known as the Cornimamma Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, thick stem and a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and has a rounded, segmented appearance. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short pedicels.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
