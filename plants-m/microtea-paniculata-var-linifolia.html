Microtea paniculata var. linifolia

Microtea paniculata var. linifolia, commonly known as the slender microtea, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like leaves and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories or wet meadows. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal practices for its mild aromatic properties.

The species is noted for its delicate structure and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
