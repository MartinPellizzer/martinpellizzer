Mimusops micrantha

Mimusops micrantha, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, rounded clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and elliptical, with a glossy green appearance.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, and the tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
