Millettia scabricaulis

Millettia scabricaulis, commonly known as the rough-stemmed millettia, is a climbing plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its woody stems that are covered in a rough, scaly texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into multiple pairs of leaflets.

Millettia scabricaulis is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in forested areas.
