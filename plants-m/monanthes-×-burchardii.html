Monanthes × burchardii

Monanthes × burchardii, commonly known as the Burchard's lily, is a hybrid plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. Monanthes × burchardii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant blooms.
