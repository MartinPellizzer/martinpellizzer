Marina stilligera

Marina stilligera, commonly known as the coastal tansy, is a flowering plant native to the western coast of the United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along coastal areas. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late summer. It is often found in maritime climates with high salinity and wind exposure.

Marina stilligera is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
