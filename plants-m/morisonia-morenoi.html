Morisonia morenoi

Morisonia morenoi, commonly known as the Moreno's morisonia, is a rare flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern California.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces showy, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. The plant thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to the harsh, coastal environment with its succulent leaves. It is listed as endangered due to habitat loss and limited distribution.
