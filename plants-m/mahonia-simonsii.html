Mahonia simonsii

Mahonia simonsii, commonly known as the evergreen holly, is a shrub native to the western United States.

It features compound leaves with spiny edges and yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant is evergreen, retaining its foliage year-round, and is well adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.

Mahonia simonsii is also valued for its ability to thrive in partial shade and its low maintenance requirements.
