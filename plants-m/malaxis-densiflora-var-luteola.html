Malaxis densiflora var. luteola

Malaxis densiflora var. luteola, commonly known as the yellow malaxis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers with a distinctive lip that is typically darker in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

This variety is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of India and Indonesia.
