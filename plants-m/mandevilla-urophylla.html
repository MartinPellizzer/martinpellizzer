Mandevilla urophylla

Mandevilla urophylla, commonly known as the red mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading vines, making it popular in ornamental gardening. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, typically blooming throughout the growing season. Its leaves are dark green and glossy, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Mandevilla urophylla thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.
