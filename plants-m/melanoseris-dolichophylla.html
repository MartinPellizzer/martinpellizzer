Melanoseris dolichophylla

Melanoseris dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf starwort, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky soils and sunny environments, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Its flowers are small and star-shaped, usually in shades of white or pale pink, and bloom during the spring and early summer.

Melanoseris dolichophylla is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
