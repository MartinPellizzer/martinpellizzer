Mammillaria rhodantha f. fulvispina

Mammillaria rhodantha f. fulvispina, commonly known as the red-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with prominent, reddish spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant.
