Metabolos rugosus f. glabrescens

Metabolos rugosus f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved rough metabolos, is a variant of the rough metabolos characterized by its reduced surface hair.

This plant is native to specific regions of southern Africa, where it thrives in rocky and arid environments. Its leaves are typically narrow and elongated, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other forms. The flowers are small and clustered, often displaying shades of white and pale yellow.

It is valued in horticulture for its resilience and ornamental appeal in dry garden settings.
