Meineckia gracilipes

Meineckia gracilipes, commonly known as the slender fringed gentian, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight hairy texture.

Meineckia gracilipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
