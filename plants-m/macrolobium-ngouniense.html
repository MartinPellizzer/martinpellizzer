Macrolobium ngouniense

Macrolobium ngouniense, commonly known as the Cameroon flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Cameroon.

It belongs to the Leguminosae family and is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces pods that contain edible seeds.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
