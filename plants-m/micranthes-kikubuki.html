Micranthes kikubuki

Micranthes kikubuki, commonly known as the Japanese mountain aster, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in the fall. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous regions, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are sturdy and upright.

Micranthes kikubuki is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
