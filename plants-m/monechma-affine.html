Monechma affine

Monechma affine, commonly known as the slender false nettle, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces. Monechma affine thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
