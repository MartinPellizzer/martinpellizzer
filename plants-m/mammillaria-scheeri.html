Mammillaria scheeri

Mammillaria scheeri, commonly known as the Scheeri's bunny ear cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
