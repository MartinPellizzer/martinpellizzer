Mammillaria schiedeana

Mammillaria schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiedea mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
