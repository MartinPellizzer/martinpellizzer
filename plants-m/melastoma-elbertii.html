Melastoma elbertii

Melastoma elbertii, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma elbertii produces small, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
