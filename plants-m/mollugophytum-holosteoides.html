Mollugophytum holosteoides

Mollugophytum holosteoides, commonly known as the golden bellflower, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often with a distinctive golden hue. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to bloom throughout the growing season.
