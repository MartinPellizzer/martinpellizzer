Masdevallia shuttleworthii

Masdevallia shuttleworthii, commonly known as the Shuttleworth's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces small, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique and delicate appearance.
