Metrosideros pullei

Metrosideros pullei, commonly known as the Pulu Tree, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, greyish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces bright red flowers that are clustered in dense, showy inflorescences, which bloom throughout the summer months. It is typically found in coastal and lowland forest habitats, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

The Pulu Tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
