Mussaenda sericea

Mussaenda sericea, commonly known as silky mussaenda, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink or red, depending on the variety. The plant has slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mussaenda sericea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
