Millettia thonningii

Millettia thonningii, commonly known as African senna, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Millettia thonningii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.
