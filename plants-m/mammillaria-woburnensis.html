Mammillaria woburnensis

Mammillaria woburnensis, commonly known as the Woburn prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
