Mutisia ilicifolia var. decandolleana

Mutisia ilicifolia var. decandolleana, commonly known as the Decandolleana mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces yellowish to orange composite flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or understoreys.

Its botanical structure supports its role in local ecosystems, providing habitat and nectar for pollinators.
