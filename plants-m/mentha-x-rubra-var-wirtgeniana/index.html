Mentha × Rubra Var. Wirtgeniana

Mentha × rubra var. wirtgeniana is a unique hybrid mint known for its aromatic leaves and vibrant purple flowers. This variety is prized in both culinary and medicinal applications due to its strong menthol scent and therapeutic properties. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight. The plant is often used in herbal teas and as a natural remedy for digestive issues. Its distinct appearance and versatile uses make it a valuable addition to gardens and herbal collections.
