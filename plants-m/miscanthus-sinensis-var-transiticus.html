Miscanthus sinensis var. transiticus

Miscanthus sinensis var. transiticus, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery, silver-green plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist to wet soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its elegant foliage and ornamental seed heads.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to wetland restoration projects and native plant gardens.
