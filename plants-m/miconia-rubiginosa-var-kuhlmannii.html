Miconia rubiginosa var. kuhlmannii

Miconia rubiginosa var. kuhlmannii, commonly known as the Kuhlmann's miconia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height, characterized by its dense, dark green foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth surface and a waxy texture. It produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries.

This plant is noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
