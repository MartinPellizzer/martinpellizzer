Melastoma coeleste

Melastoma coeleste, commonly known as the blue melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, oval-shaped, and have a glossy surface.

Melastoma coeleste is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
