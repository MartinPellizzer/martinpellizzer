Machilus dominii

Machilus dominii, commonly known as the Chinese laurustin, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Machilus dominii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to a variety of soil types.
