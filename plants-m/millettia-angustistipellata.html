Millettia angustistipellata

Millettia angustistipellata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved millettia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including countries like India, China, and Thailand. The plant is characterized by its slender, often climbing habit and trifoliate leaves with narrow, elliptical leaflets. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with a distinctive yellow or orange hue.

The seeds are enclosed in elongated pods, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
