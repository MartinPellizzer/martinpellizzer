Meeboldina cana

Meeboldina cana, commonly known as the white meebold, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, white, tubular flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, and it is often found in coastal regions.

Meeboldina cana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
