Marcgravia polyantha subsp. occidentalis

Marcgravia polyantha subsp.

occidentalis, commonly known as the Western arrowroot. It is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and striking, spadix-like inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
