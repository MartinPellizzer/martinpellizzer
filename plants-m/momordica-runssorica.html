Momordica runssorica

Momordica runssorica, commonly known as the horned melon, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiny, green fruit that resembles a hornet's nest. The plant produces large, yellow flowers that are often followed by the unique, warty fruit. The fruit's interior contains a sweet, yellowish pulp with black seeds.

It is cultivated for its edible fruit and has traditional uses in various Asian cuisines.
