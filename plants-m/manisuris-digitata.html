Manisuris digitata

Manisuris digitata, commonly known as the hairy nightshade, is a herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features distinctive hairy leaves with a lobed or digitate shape, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Manisuris digitata is often found in rocky or disturbed soils and is known for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
