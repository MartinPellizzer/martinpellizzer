Melicope neurococca

Melicope neurococca, commonly known as the Hawaiian melicope, is a member of the Rutaceae family.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and yellow, often clustered in inflorescences, and it produces fruit that is a woody capsule.
