Maxillaria melina

Maxillaria melina, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its sympodial growth habit and pseudo-bulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in fine hairs.

Maxillaria melina is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
