Moehringia dielsiana

Moehringia dielsiana, commonly known as the Diels' moehringia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in alpine and subalpine regions. The plant features delicate, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of tiny, pink to purple flowers. It is native to the mountainous areas of Central Asia and the Himalayas.

Moehringia dielsiana is often found in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations.
