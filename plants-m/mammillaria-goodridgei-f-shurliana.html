Mammillaria goodridgei f. shurliana

Mammillaria goodridgei f. shurliana, commonly known as the Shurliana mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the deserts of northern Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent tubercles and white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stem.

This variety is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
