Medinilla javensis

Medinilla javensis, commonly known as the Java Medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces clusters of pink or red flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla javensis is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
