Mediocactus wercklei

Mediocactus wercklei, commonly known as the Werckle's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is a small, columnar cactus that typically grows to a height of about 15 to 30 centimeters. The plant features a ribbed stem with spines and produces clusters of yellow flowers during the warmer months. It has a shallow root system adapted to absorb moisture from the soil after rare rains.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for cultivation in dry, well-drained environments.
