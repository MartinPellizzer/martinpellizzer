Mimosa egregia

Mimosa egregia, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, and it is often found in open, sunny habitats. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to well-drained soils.

Mimosa egregia is also known for its sensitive leaves that fold when touched, a trait shared with other mimosa species.
