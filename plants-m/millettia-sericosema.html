Millettia sericosema

Millettia sericosema, commonly known as the silky millettia, is a tropical climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, twining stems and compound leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by elongated pods containing flattened, winged seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
