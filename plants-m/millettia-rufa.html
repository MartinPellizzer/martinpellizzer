Millettia rufa

Millettia rufa, commonly known as the red millettia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, yellow flowers that are followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional uses in some regions.
