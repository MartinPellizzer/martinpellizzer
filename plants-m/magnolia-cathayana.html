Magnolia cathayana

Magnolia cathayana, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on the upper surface. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often developing fissures with age.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
