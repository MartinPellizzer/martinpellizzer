Macradenia triandra

Macradenia triandra, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features tall, slender stems that bear large, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant produces striking, trumpet-shaped blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Macradenia triandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
