Magnolia duclouxii

Magnolia duclouxii, commonly known as the Ducloux magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to cream-colored flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with shallow fissures.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
