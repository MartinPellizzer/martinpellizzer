Medicago prostrata f. glandulosa

Medicago prostrata f. glandulosa, commonly known as the prostrate medic.

This low-growing perennial plant is characterized by its glandular-stemmed structure, which contributes to its distinctive appearance. It features trifoliate leaves with a hairy texture and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils.

It is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support pollinator populations.
