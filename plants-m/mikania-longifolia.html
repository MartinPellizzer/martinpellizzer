Mikania longifolia

Mikania longifolia, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, funnel-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Mikania longifolia is also known for its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.
