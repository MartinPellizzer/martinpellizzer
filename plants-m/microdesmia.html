Microdesmia

Microdesmia, commonly known as the starfruit or carambola, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, five-lobed fruits that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its fruit is edible, with a tangy flavor and a distinctive star-shaped cross-section when sliced.
