Masdevallia coccinea var. militaris

Masdevallia coccinea var. militaris, commonly known as the Crimson Warbler Orchid, is a striking member of the orchid family native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

This terrestrial orchid features vibrant red flowers with contrasting yellow and orange markings, which are highly attractive to pollinators. It typically grows in cool, shaded environments with high humidity and well-drained, acidic soil. The plant has a pseudobulb structure that stores water and nutrients, aiding its survival in its native habitat.

Its unique coloration and delicate appearance make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
