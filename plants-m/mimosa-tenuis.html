Mimosa tenuis

Mimosa tenuis, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

Mimosa tenuis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique leaf movements and delicate appearance.
