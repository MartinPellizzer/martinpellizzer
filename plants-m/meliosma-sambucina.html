Meliosma sambucina

Meliosma sambucina, commonly known as the purple-flowered meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical trees. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically purple or reddish in color, which attract various pollinators.
