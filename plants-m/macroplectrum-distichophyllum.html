Macroplectrum distichophyllum

Macroplectrum distichophyllum, commonly known as the two-ranked grass.

It is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, linear leaves arranged in two rows, giving it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

The plant is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils.
