Mimosa nellyrenza

Mimosa nellyrenza, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is part of the Mimosa genus, which is known for its sensitive leaves that fold when touched. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its soft, pastel-colored blooms and graceful appearance.
