Masdevallia titan

Masdevallia titan, commonly known as the giant masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 inches in diameter, making it one of the most visually impressive orchids in the genus. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its vibrant, colorful blooms often feature intricate patterns and are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its size and beauty, it is a popular subject in botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
