Miscanthus yunnanensis

Miscanthus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan silver grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and fluffy, silver-tan plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens and landscapes.

Miscanthus yunnanensis is also noted for its adaptability and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
