Monardella oblonga

Monardella oblonga, commonly known as narrowleaf monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It produces showy, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color.

Its foliage is characterized by narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.
