Megaclinium nummularia

Megaclinium nummularia, commonly known as silver dollar plant, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its round, silvery-green leaves that resemble silver coins. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Due to its ornamental value and hardy nature, it is often cultivated in coastal and Mediterranean climates.
