Maba rufa

Maba rufa, commonly known as the red maba tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The tree is characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and compound leaves with three to five leaflets. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and has traditional uses in local communities.
