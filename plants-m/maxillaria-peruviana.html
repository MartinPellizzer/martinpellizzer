Maxillaria peruviana

Maxillaria peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

Maxillaria peruviana thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
