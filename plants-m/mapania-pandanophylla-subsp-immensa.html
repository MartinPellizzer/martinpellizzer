Mapania pandanophylla subsp. immensa

Mapania pandanophylla subsp.

immensa, commonly known as the giant pandan plant, is a striking tropical species native to parts of Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to several meters in length.

The plant belongs to the Pandanaceae family and is closely related to other pandan species. Its leaves are often used in traditional cuisine and for their aromatic properties.

The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
