Mytilicoccus costulatus

Mytilicoccus costulatus, commonly known as the sea hare algae.

This marine plant is a type of red algae that thrives in shallow coastal waters. It has a distinctive branching structure with a soft, gelatinous texture. Mytilicoccus costulatus plays an important role in marine ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various organisms.

It is often found attached to rocks or other substrates in tidal zones.
