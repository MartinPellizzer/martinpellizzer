Mimosa niomarlei

Mimosa niomarlei, commonly known as the white mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which is known for its leguminous plants. The plant features small, delicate, white flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it a feathery appearance. Its leaves are bipinnate, meaning they are divided into many small leaflets, which contribute to its ornamental value.

Mimosa niomarlei is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and is adapted to grow in well-drained soils.
