Mallotus walkerae var. laxiflorus

Mallotus walkerae var. laxiflorus, commonly known as the broad-flowered mallotus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often palmately arranged with several leaflets. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role.
