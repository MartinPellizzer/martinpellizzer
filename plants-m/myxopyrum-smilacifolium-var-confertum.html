Myxopyrum smilacifolium var. confertum

Myxopyrum smilacifolium var. confertum, commonly known as the denseleaf smilacifolium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems that often twine around other vegetation. The leaves are alternate, simple, and typically have a heart-shaped base with a waxy, glossy surface. Its small, greenish-white flowers are clustered in loose panicles and bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland understories.
