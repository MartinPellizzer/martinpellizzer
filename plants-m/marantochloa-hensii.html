Marantochloa hensii

Marantochloa hensii, commonly known as the Hensii Marantchloa, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with reddish veins, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.
