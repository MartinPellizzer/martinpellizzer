Mammillaria microcarpa var. milleri

Mammillaria microcarpa var. milleri, commonly known as the Honeycomb Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly bristles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant has small, pale green stems with prominent areoles, from which it produces pale yellow flowers. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems.

This variety is prized in horticulture for its unique look and relatively easy care requirements.
