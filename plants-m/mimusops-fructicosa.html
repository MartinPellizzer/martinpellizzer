Mimusops fructicosa

Mimusops fructicosa, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The tree's bark is thick and rough, often with a reddish or brownish hue. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to reveal glossy, black seeds.

Mimusops fructicosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in various African cultures.
