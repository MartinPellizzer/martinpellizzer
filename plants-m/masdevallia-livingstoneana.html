Masdevallia livingstoneana

Masdevallia livingstoneana, commonly known as the Livingstone's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and elongated, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal inflorescences and exhibit a variety of hues, including red, orange, and yellow.

This orchid thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
