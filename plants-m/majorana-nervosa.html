Majorana nervosa

Majorana nervosa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf marjoram, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which is characterized by square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in spikes, which bloom in summer. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a strong, spicy scent.

Majorana nervosa is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, particularly in traditional herbal remedies.
