Mandevilla holstii

Mandevilla holstii, commonly known as the velvet tangerine mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and striking, velvety orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a slightly waxy texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mandevilla holstii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and lush foliage.
