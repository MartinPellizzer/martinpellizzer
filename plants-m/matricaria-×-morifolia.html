Matricaria × morifolia

Matricaria × morifolia, commonly known as German chamomile, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which consist of white petals surrounding a yellow central disk. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and prefers well-drained soil in full sun. Its leaves are finely divided and aromatic, often used in herbal teas.

Matricaria × morifolia is native to Europe and is cultivated for its calming properties and medicinal uses.
