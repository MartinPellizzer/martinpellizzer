Malacothrix Sonchoides Var. Torreyi

Malacothrix sonchoides var. torreyi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of the southwestern United States. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, known for its composite flower heads and ecological importance. This variant is distinguished by its unique leaf structure and pale yellow blooms that appear in late spring. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts.
