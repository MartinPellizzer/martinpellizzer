Maxillaria schweinfurthiana

Maxillaria schweinfurthiana, commonly known as the Schweinfurth's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant has linear to lanceolate leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its inflorescence is typically a raceme with numerous flowers, and it blooms during the warmer months.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
