Mezoneuron mindorense var. inermis

Mezoneuron mindorense var. inermis, commonly known as the Mindoro velvet tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, edible pods that contain seeds surrounded by a fibrous pulp. It is adapted to tropical climates and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in secondary forests and along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and potential for sustainable use, it is of interest for conservation and horticultural purposes.
