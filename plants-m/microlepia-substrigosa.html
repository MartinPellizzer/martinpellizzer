Microlepia substrigosa

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its delicate, lance-shaped fronds that are often covered in fine, hair-like structures. Microlepia substrigosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
