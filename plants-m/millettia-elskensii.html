Millettia elskensii

Millettia elskensii, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces woody pods that contain edible seeds, which are often used in traditional cuisine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to seasonal droughts, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
