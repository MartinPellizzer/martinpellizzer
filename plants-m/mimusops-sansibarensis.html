Mimusops sansibarensis

Mimusops sansibarensis, commonly known as the East African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of East Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and broad, ovate leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the dry season. The plant is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also important in local ecosystems.
