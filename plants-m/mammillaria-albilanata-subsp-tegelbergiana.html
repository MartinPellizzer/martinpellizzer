Mammillaria albilanata subsp. tegelbergiana

Mammillaria albilanata subsp.

tegelbergiana, commonly known as the White-Flowered Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico. It features a spiny, ribbed stem with a pale green to white coloration, and it produces small, white flowers during the warmer months. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, with a shallow root system and a thick, water-storing stem.

It is prized in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
