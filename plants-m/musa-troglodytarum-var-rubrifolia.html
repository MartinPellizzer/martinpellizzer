Musa troglodytarum var. rubrifolia

Musa troglodytarum var. rubrifolia, commonly known as the red-leaf banana, is a rare and ornamental variant of the banana plant.

It is characterized by its striking red-tinged leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance compared to typical banana plants. The plant is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. It produces small, edible bananas, though its primary value lies in its aesthetic appeal.

Due to its rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
