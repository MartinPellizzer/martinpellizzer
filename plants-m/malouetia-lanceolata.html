Malouetia lanceolata

Malouetia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed malouetia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are lanceolate, hence the species name, and are usually arranged oppositely on the stem.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
