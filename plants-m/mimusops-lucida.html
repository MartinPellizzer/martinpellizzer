Mimusops lucida

Mimusops lucida, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The tree produces small, white or cream-colored flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by orange to red berries. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and fine grain, making it useful in furniture and construction.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
