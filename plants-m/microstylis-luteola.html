Microstylis luteola

Microstylis luteola, commonly known as the yellow microstylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, yellowish flowers in dense clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.
