Mesechites bicorniculatus

Mesechites bicorniculatus, commonly known as the two-horned fern, is a unique tropical plant belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive fronds that feature two prominent, horn-like projections, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as tropical forests, where it thrives in humid conditions. Its rhizome is thick and creeping, supporting the growth of its delicate, bi-lobed leaves.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
