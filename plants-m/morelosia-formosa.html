Morelosia formosa

Morelosia formosa, commonly known as the Mexican beauty flower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees.
