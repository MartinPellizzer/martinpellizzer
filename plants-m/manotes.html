Manotes

Manotes, commonly known as the "false heather," is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in late spring. Its leaves are typically dark green and leathery, and it thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.

Manotes is often used in ornamental gardening for its compact growth and attractive floral display.
