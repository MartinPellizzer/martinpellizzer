Mecranium microdictyum

Mecranium microdictyum, commonly known as the small-spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and arranged in a basal rosette, while its stems are short and often hidden beneath the foliage.

The flowers are small but intricate, with a unique structure that aids in pollination by specific insect species.
