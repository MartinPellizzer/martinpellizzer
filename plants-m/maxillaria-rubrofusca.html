Maxillaria rubrofusca

Maxillaria rubrofusca, commonly known as the red-fusca orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces dark green, lance-shaped leaves and bears fragrant, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive red throat. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria rubrofusca is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
