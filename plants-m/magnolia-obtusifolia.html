Magnolia obtusifolia

Magnolia obtusifolia, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broadleaf magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture, often with a waxy appearance. The tree produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
