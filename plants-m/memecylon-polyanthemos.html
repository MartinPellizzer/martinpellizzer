Memecylon polyanthemos

Memecylon polyanthemos, commonly known as the fragrant memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a faint fragrance. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Memecylon polyanthemos is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
