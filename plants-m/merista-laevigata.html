Merista laevigata

Merista laevigata, commonly known as the smooth merista, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth texture, which is reflected in its scientific name.

Merista laevigata is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments.
