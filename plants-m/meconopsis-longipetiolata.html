Meconopsis longipetiolata

Meconopsis longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled meconopsis, is a herbaceous perennial native to the alpine regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with long petioles, which help it thrive in high-altitude environments. It prefers well-drained, acidic soils and partial to full shade.

Meconopsis longipetiolata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
