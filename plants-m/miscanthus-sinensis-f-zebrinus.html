Miscanthus sinensis f. zebrinus

Miscanthus sinensis f. zebrinus, commonly known as the zebra grass, is a striking ornamental grass characterized by its distinctive striped foliage.

It features narrow, arching leaves that are green with white or silver stripes running vertically along their length. This plant typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet, forming dense clumps with a fountain-like shape. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The foliage remains attractive throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for garden borders and landscape design.
