Myosotis rivularis

Myosotis rivularis, commonly known as water forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It thrives in moist environments such as stream banks, wet meadows, and marshy areas. The plant features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette.

Myosotis rivularis is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in wetland habitats.
