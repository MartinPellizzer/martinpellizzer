Myoporum beckeri

Myoporum beckeri, commonly known as the New Zealand myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.

Myoporum beckeri is also valued for its use in landscaping and as a hedging plant.
