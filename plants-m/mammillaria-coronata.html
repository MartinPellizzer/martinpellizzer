Mammillaria coronata

Mammillaria coronata, commonly known as the Crowns of Thorns, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent gardens.
