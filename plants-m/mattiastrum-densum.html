Mattiastrum densum

Mattiastrum densum, commonly known as the dense mat plant, is a flowering plant native to the wet tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in spikes. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in a fine layer of hair.

Mattiastrum densum is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
