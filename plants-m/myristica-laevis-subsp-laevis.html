Myristica laevis subsp. laevis

Myristica laevis subsp.

laevis, commonly known as the smooth nutmeg. It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

The plant belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glossy leaves and fragrant, aromatic seeds. The fruit is a drupe that contains a single seed, which is the source of the spice nutmeg.

This subspecies is distinguished by its relatively smooth bark and leaves compared to other members of the species.
