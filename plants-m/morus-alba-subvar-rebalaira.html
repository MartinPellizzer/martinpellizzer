Morus alba subvar. rebalaira

Morus alba subvar. rebalaira, commonly known as the white mulberry, is a variant of the mulberry tree native to Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, aggregate fruits that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It plays an important role in the silkworm breeding process due to its nutritious leaves.

Its bark and leaves have also been used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
