Mammillaria pachytele

Mammillaria pachytele, commonly known as the thick-stemmed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure to thrive.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
