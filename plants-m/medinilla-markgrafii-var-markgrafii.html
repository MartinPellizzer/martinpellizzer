Medinilla markgrafii var. markgrafii

Medinilla markgrafii var. markgrafii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, often appearing during the holiday season. The plant produces showy, fleshy bracts that enclose the actual flowers, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla markgrafii var. markgrafii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking floral display.
