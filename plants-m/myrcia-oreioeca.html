Myrcia oreioeca

Myrcia oreioeca, commonly known as the mountain myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, while its fruit is a small, woody capsule containing several seeds.

Myrcia oreioeca is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
