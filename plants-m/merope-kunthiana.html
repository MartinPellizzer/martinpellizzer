Merope kunthiana

Merope kunthiana, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in a waxy cuticle to reduce water loss.

Merope kunthiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscapes.
