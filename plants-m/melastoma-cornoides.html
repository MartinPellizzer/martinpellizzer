Melastoma cornoides

Melastoma cornoides, commonly known as the red-flowered Melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and often bears small, glandular dots. It produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Melastoma cornoides is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
