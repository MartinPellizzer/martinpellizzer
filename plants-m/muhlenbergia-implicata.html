Muhlenbergia implicata

Muhlenbergia implicata, commonly known as the silky muhlenbergia, is a species of grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and chaparral.
