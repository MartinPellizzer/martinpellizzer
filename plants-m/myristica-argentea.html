Myristica argentea

Myristica argentea, commonly known as silver nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indo-Pacific region.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, silver-green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by unique, three-lobed fruits that contain the aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine. Its essential oils and seeds are valued for their medicinal and aromatic properties.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
