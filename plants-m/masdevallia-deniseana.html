Masdevallia deniseana

Masdevallia deniseana, commonly known as the Denise's orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb that supports its growth in nutrient-poor environments. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Masdevallia deniseana is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and unique growth habits.
