Myriolepis ferulacea

Myriolepis ferulacea, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, often hairy stems and small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and pointed, with a rough texture. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in dry, sandy soils.

This plant is often found in open grasslands and is adapted to arid environments.
