Manulea multispicata

Manulea multispicata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-forming leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light. It is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
