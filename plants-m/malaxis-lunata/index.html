Malaxis Lunata

Malaxis lunata, also known as the moon orchid, is a rare and striking epiphytic plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. With its unique, moon-like flower shape, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on trees or rocks. Its delicate white petals and green sepals create a striking contrast that mimics the phases of the moon. Due to its rarity and beauty, Malaxis lunata is highly sought after in both horticultural and conservation circles.
