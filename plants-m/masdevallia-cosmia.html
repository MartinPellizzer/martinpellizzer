Masdevallia cosmia

Masdevallia cosmia, commonly known as the Cosmian Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces small, intricate blooms with a distinctive pattern of sepals and petals. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette formation.

Masdevallia cosmia is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
