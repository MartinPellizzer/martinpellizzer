Medinilla honbaensis

Medinilla honbaensis, commonly known as the Honba's torch, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble torches, with bright red petals and a yellow center. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Medinilla honbaensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
