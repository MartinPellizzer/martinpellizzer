Millettia penduliformis

Millettia penduliformis, commonly known as the hanging millettia, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendant-like flowers that hang in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are pinnate, with numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets.

Millettia penduliformis is often found in tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
