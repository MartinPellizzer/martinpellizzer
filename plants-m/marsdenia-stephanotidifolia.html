Marsdenia stephanotidifolia

Marsdenia stephanotidifolia, commonly known as the silver vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves.
