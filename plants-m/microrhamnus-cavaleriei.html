Microrhamnus cavaleriei

Microrhamnus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese buckthorn, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the family Rhamnaceae and is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or black when mature. It is often found in rocky or mountainous areas and is adapted to dry, acidic soils.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
