Moraea macra

Moraea macra, commonly known as the Giant Moraea, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, often with a distinctive white throat. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette at the base.

Moraea macra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
