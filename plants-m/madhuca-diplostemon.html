Madhuca diplostemon

Madhuca diplostemon, commonly known as the white-leaf mahua, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and large, elliptical leaves.
