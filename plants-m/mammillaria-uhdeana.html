Mammillaria uhdeana

Mammillaria uhdeana, commonly known as the Uhde's bunny ear cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive rib structure and a pale green to bluish-gray color. The plant features prominent white to yellow spines and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Mammillaria uhdeana is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
