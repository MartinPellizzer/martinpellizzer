Mercurialis annua f. ovata

Mercurialis annua f. ovata, commonly known as the ovate mercury plant, is a variant of the common mercury plant.

It is a low-growing annual herb characterized by its ovate-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in disturbed soils and along roadsides. Its botanical features include opposite leaves and a branching stem.

The species is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
