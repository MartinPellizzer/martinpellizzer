Mimosa lucida

Mimosa lucida, commonly known as the shining mimosa, is a species of flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often found in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in full sun. It is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and has the ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

The plant's rapid growth and unique appearance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
