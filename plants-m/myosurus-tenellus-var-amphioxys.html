Myosurus tenellus var. amphioxys

Myosurus tenellus var. amphioxys, commonly known as the slender mossy orchid, is a rare and delicate orchid species native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, pale green to yellowish leaves and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a unique tubular shape. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, often growing on tree trunks or rocks in tropical rainforests. Its botanical significance lies in its specialized adaptations to epiphytic growth and its role in the ecological balance of its habitat.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and requires conservation efforts to ensure its survival.
