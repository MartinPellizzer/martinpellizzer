Melastoma sumatranum

Melastoma sumatranum, commonly known as the Sumatran melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are usually opposite and oval-shaped, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, round seeds.
