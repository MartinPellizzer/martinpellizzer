Mimusops schinzii

Mimusops schinzii, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Africa.

It features smooth, greyish-brown bark and opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy upper surface. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in summer. Its fruit is a globose capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental beauty.
