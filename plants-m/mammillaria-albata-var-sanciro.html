Mammillaria albata var. sanciro

Mammillaria albata var. sanciro, commonly known as the White Globe Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a soft, fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
