Matucana hystrix

Matucana hystrix, commonly known as the hairy matucana, is a species of cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white, woolly hairs. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
