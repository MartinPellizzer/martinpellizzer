Microlicia euphorbioides var. macrocarpa

Microlicia euphorbioides var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited microlicia, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and produces small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments.

This species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological importance in its native habitat.
