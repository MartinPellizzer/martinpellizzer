Maxillaria aristeguietae

Maxillaria aristeguietae, commonly known as the Aristeguieta's maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Ecuador.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with a distinctive, narrow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. It blooms during the dry season, showcasing its ornamental value in botanical gardens and collections.

Its unique morphology and restricted distribution make it a subject of interest for orchid enthusiasts and researchers.
