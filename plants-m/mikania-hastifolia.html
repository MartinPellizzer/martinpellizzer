Mikania hastifolia

Mikania hastifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-like leaves that are usually bright green and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Mikania hastifolia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its rapid growth and attractive foliage.
