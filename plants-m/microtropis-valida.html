Microtropis valida

Microtropis valida, commonly known as the golden shower vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hanging clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has simple, opposite leaves and produces small, elliptical fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
