Melastoma kudoi

Melastoma kudoi, commonly known as the Kudoi Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic value.

The species is also known for its ability to thrive in tropical and subtropical climates.
