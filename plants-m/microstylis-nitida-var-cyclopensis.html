Microstylis nitida var. cyclopensis

Microstylis nitida var. cyclopensis, commonly known as the cyclopensis variety of the nitida species, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with pale yellow to brownish flowers. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along stream banks.

This variety is noted for its compact growth habit and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
