Mimulus suksdorfii

Mimulus suksdorfii, commonly known as the Saksdorf's monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, sandy soils along streambanks and in woodland areas. The plant produces striking yellow-orange flowers with five petals, which bloom in late spring to early summer.
