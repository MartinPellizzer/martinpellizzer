Muhlenbergia crispiseta

Muhlenbergia crispiseta, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features fine, silver-gray leaves that are often rolled at the edges, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest throughout the growing season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Muhlenbergia crispiseta is drought-tolerant and contributes to erosion control in natural landscapes.
