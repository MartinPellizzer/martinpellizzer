Meriania grandidens

Meriania grandidens, commonly known as the giant meriania, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, with a distinctive red calyx. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
