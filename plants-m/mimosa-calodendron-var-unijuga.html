Mimosa calodendron var. unijuga

Mimosa calodendron var. unijuga, commonly known as the single-lobed mimosa, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are bipinnate, giving it a lacy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
