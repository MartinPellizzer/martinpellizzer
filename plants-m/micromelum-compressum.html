Micromelum compressum

Micromelum compressum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved pittosporum, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and slightly leathery in texture. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by edible, orange-colored fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and woodland areas.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in gardens and used in native landscaping.
