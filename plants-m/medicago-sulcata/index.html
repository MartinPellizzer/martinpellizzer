Medicago Sulcata

Medicago sulcata is a hardy legume known for its deep root system and ability to thrive in arid conditions. This plant is often used for soil stabilization and erosion control due to its resilience. It has small, oval leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Medicago sulcata is also valued for its role in sustainable agriculture and livestock forage. Its adaptability makes it a key species in ecological restoration projects.
