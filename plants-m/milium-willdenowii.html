Milium willdenowii

Milium willdenowii, commonly known as slender millet, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant produces tiny, white to pale green flowers that are clustered in dense panicles. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along streambanks.

Milium willdenowii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
