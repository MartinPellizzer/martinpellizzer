Muhlenbergia sobolifera

Muhlenbergia sobolifera, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically gray-green in color. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy, white to pale blue flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.

Muhlenbergia sobolifera is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
