Medinilla trinervia

Medinilla trinervia, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive velvety appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Medinilla trinervia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and tropical landscapes.
