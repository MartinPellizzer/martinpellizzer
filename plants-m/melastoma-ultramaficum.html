Melastoma ultramaficum

Melastoma ultramaficum, commonly known as the ultramafic melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions with ultramafic soils.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically has opposite, simple leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in nutrient-poor, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
