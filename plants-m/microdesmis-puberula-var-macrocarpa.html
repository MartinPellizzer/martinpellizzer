Microdesmis puberula var. macrocarpa

Microdesmis puberula var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited microdesmis, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and ovate, hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several small, smooth seeds.

This species is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
