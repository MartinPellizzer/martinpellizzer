Mesembryanthemum splendens subsp. pentagonum

Mesembryanthemum splendens subsp.

pentagonum, commonly known as the "Purple Iceplant," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues.

The plant produces vibrant, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and purple, typically during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy leaves.

This species is popular in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
