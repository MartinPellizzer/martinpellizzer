Mammillaria goodridgei var. blossfeldiana

Mammillaria goodridgei var. blossfeldiana, commonly known as the Blossfeld's bunny ear cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, white or pale blue spines and small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its low water requirements and compact size.

Its slow growth rate makes it an ideal choice for collectors and those with limited space.
