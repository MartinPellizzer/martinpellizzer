Mucuna eurylamellata

Mucuna eurylamellata, commonly known as the broad-leaf velvet bean, is a tropical legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, palmately compound leaves and distinctive, velvety pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces a strong, distinctive odor.

Mucuna eurylamellata is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
