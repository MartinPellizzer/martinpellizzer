Masdevallia xanthina subsp. klabochorum

Masdevallia xanthina subsp.

klabochorum, commonly known as the Klaboch's masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Central and South America. It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting markings.

This species thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, humid conditions and partial shade. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and segmented, supporting its unique growth habit.

The plant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
