Metroxylon sagu

Metroxylon sagu, commonly known as the sago palm, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like leaves. The plant produces a starchy sap, which is harvested for the production of sago and palm sugar. Its trunk is composed of a fibrous material that is used in various industrial applications.

The sago palm plays an important role in both traditional and modern economies in its native regions.
