Malapoenna tersa

Malapoenna tersa, commonly known as the Malapoenna tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in humid, lowland areas and thrives in well-drained soil. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
