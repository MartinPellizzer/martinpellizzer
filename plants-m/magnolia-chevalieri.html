Magnolia chevalieri

Magnolia chevalieri, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface. The bark is smooth and grayish, becoming fissured with age.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
