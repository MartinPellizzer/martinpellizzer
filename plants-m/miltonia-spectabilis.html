Miltonia spectabilis

Miltonia spectabilis, commonly known as the orchid bell, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically elliptical and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant flowers.

Miltonia spectabilis is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
