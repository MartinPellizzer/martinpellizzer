Mulgedium aureum

Mulgedium aureum, commonly known as the golden mulgedium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems covered in fine, silvery hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture, often with a subtle aromatic scent.

Mulgedium aureum thrives in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
