Magnolia decandollei

Magnolia decandollei, commonly known as the Decandolle's magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The bark is smooth and grayish, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
