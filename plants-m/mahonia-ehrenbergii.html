Mahonia ehrenbergii

Mahonia ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Mexican barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico.

It features compound leaves with spiny tips and yellow flowers that bloom in late winter. The plant produces red berries that are edible but have a tart flavor. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its hardiness and adaptability make it suitable for various climates, including USDA zones 7b to 10.
