Myrtus gayana

Myrtus gayana, commonly known as the Australian myrtle, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant white flowers. The flowers are followed by small, woody capsules that contain aromatic, oil-rich seeds.

Myrtus gayana is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its essential oils.
