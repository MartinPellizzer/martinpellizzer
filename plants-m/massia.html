Massia

Massia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact rosette of velvety, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically blooming from spring to autumn. Massia thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

It is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
