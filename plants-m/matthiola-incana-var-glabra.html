Matthiola incana var. glabra

Matthiola incana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and clusters of bright yellow or white flowers. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly serrated edge.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
