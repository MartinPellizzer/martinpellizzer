Melilotus argutus

Melilotus argutus, commonly known as the goat's rue or goat's beard, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands.

Melilotus argutus is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
