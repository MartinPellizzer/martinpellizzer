Magnolia wolfii

Magnolia wolfii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Magnolia wolfii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
