Maxillaria brenesii

Maxillaria brenesii, commonly known as the Brenes's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive pattern on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Maxillaria brenesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate for orchid enthusiasts.
