Mertensia dschagastanica

Mertensia dschagastanica, commonly known as the Caucasian bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It is characterized by its attractive, bell-shaped blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down.

Mertensia dschagastanica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
