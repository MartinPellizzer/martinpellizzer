Microseris macrochaeta

Microseris macrochaeta, commonly known as the tall hairy tansy, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant characterized by its upright stems and hairy foliage. The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically arranged in composite flower heads. It is native to North America and thrives in dry, disturbed soils.

Microseris macrochaeta is often found in prairies, roadsides, and open woodlands.
