Marsdenia hilariana var. macieliana

Marsdenia hilariana var. macieliana, commonly known as the Maciel's Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and showy, tubular flowers. The plant produces small, white to yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate to elliptic, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
