Marantochloa microphylla

Marantochloa microphylla, commonly known as the small-leafed arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes various ornamental and economically important plants. The plant features upright, slender stems that are often covered in a distinctive white, powdery coating. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a bright green color with a slightly waxy texture.

Marantochloa microphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
