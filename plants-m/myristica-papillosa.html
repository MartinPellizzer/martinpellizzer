Myristica papillosa

Myristica papillosa, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by hairy, orange-colored berries. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a rough texture.

The tree is valued for its aromatic seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications.
