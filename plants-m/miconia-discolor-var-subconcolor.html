Miconia discolor var. subconcolor

Miconia discolor var. subconcolor, commonly known as the smoothleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves that are usually dark green on both sides. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn from green to yellow or red as they mature.

The species is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive plant in certain regions.
