Mammillaria longiseta

Mammillaria longiseta, commonly known as the long-spined mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
