Monotes glandulosissimus

Monotes glandulosissimus, commonly known as the sticky monotes, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
