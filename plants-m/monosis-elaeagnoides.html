Monosis elaeagnoides

Monosis elaeagnoides, commonly known as silverleaf buckthorn, is a shrub native to parts of Asia and the Americas.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and small, dark berries. The plant has compound leaves with serrated margins and produces yellowish flowers in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Due to its invasive potential in some regions, it is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
