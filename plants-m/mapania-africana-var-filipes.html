Mapania africana var. filipes

Mapania africana var. filipes, commonly known as the African feather fern, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds that give it a distinctive, ornamental appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies or along stream banks. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant's botanical structure includes bipinnate leaves with a finely divided, almost hair-like appearance.
