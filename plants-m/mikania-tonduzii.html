Mikania tonduzii

Mikania tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduzii mile-a-minute weed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and sprawling habit. The plant features small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs.

Due to its aggressive growth, it is considered an invasive species in many regions where it has been introduced.
