Mussaenda punctulata

Mussaenda punctulata, commonly known as the spotted Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Mussaenda punctulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
