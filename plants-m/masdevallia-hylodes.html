Masdevallia hylodes

Masdevallia hylodes, commonly known as the Hylodes masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often at high elevations. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia hylodes is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
