Mimosa myuros

Mimosa myuros, commonly known as the pink mistflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, pink or white flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling mist or clouds. It is known for its ability to thrive in sandy soils and withstand coastal conditions.

Mimosa myuros is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden landscapes.
