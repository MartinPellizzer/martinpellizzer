Meliosma myriantha var. discolor

Meliosma myriantha var. discolor, commonly known as the variegated meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive variegation pattern, often displaying shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically followed by dark, leathery fruits. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its striking foliage rather than its flowers or fruits.
