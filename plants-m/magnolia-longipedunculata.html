Magnolia longipedunculata

Magnolia longipedunculata, commonly known as the long peduncle magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its flowers are borne on long, slender peduncles, which are distinctive features of the species.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its elegant floral display.
