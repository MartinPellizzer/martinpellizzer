Maxillaria langlassei

Maxillaria langlassei, commonly known as the Langlasse's maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs that produce leaves and flower spikes sequentially. The plant features long, slender leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. Its flowers are usually white or pale pink, with a distinctive lip that is often contrasting in color.

Maxillaria langlassei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
