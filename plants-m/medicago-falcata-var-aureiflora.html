Medicago falcata var. aureiflora

Medicago falcata var. aureiflora, commonly known as the golden crown vetch, is a variant of the common vetch that is characterized by its striking yellow flowers.

It is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. It is often used in erosion control due to its rapid growth and extensive root system.

Its ornamental value comes from its vibrant yellow blooms, which appear in late spring to early summer.
