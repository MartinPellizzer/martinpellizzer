Microsorum bamlerianum

Microsorum bamlerianum, commonly known as the bamboo sword fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its sword-shaped, linear fronds that are typically dark green and slightly glossy. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in shaded, moist habitats. Its rhizomes are thick and creeping, allowing it to spread and form dense mats.

Microsorum bamlerianum is popular in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
