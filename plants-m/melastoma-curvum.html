Melastoma curvum

Melastoma curvum, commonly known as the curved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often violet or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind or water.
