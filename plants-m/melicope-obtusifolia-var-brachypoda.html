Melicope obtusifolia var. brachypoda

Melicope obtusifolia var. brachypoda, commonly known as the broadleaf melicope, is a shrub or small tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its simple, ovate to elliptic leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by small, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal or lowland areas.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
