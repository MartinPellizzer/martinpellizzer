Minyranthes

Minyranthes, commonly known as the common milkweed, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its milky sap, which is a distinctive botanical feature. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its flowers are clustered in umbels and are usually pink or purple in color.
