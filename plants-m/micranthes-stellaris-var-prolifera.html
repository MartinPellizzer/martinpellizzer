Micranthes stellaris var. prolifera

Micranthes stellaris var. prolifera, commonly known as the starflower or starry false Solomon's seal, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features small, star-shaped white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has alternate, lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly serrated margin. It typically grows in moist, shady woodland habitats and spreads via rhizomes or stolons.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
