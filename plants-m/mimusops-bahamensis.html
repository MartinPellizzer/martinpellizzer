Mimusops bahamensis

Mimusops bahamensis, commonly known as the Bahamian satinash, is a small evergreen tree native to the Caribbean, particularly the Bahamas.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and leathery in texture. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. These flowers are followed by spherical, woody fruits that contain several seeds.

Mimusops bahamensis is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to salty air and sandy soils.
