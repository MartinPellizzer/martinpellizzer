Masdevalliantha masdevalliopsis

Masdevalliantha masdevalliopsis, commonly known as the Masdevallia orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, epiphytic plant native to the cloud forests of South America. The plant features dense, dark green foliage and produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. Its inflorescence is typically a raceme of resupinate flowers, and it blooms during the dry season.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a houseplant.
