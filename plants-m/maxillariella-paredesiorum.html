Maxillariella paredesiorum

Maxillariella paredesiorum, commonly known as the Paredesiorum orchid, is a miniature orchid native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
