Masdevallia blanda

Masdevallia blanda, commonly known as the slender masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant prefers cool, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded, forested areas. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, and its leaves are narrow and grass-like.

Masdevallia blanda is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
