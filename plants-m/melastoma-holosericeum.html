Melastoma holosericeum

Melastoma holosericeum, commonly known as the velvetleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually glossy and have a velvety texture, which contributes to its common name.

The species is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
