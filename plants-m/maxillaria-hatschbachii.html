Maxillaria hatschbachii

Maxillaria hatschbachii, commonly known as the Hatschbach's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the humid forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, yellow-green to white flowers with a distinctive, spurred lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Maxillaria hatschbachii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
