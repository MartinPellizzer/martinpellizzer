Memorialis ternata var. concinna

Memorialis ternata var. concinna, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
