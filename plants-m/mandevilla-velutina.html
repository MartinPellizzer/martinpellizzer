Mandevilla velutina

Mandevilla velutina, commonly known as the velvet mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that give it its common name. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Mandevilla velutina is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and lush foliage.
