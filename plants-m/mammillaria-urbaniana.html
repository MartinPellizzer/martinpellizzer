Mammillaria urbaniana

Mammillaria urbaniana, commonly known as the urbaniana cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
