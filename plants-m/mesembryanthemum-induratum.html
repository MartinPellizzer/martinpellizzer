Mesembryanthemum induratum

Mesembryanthemum induratum, commonly known as the hard-stemmed ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

Mesembryanthemum induratum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
