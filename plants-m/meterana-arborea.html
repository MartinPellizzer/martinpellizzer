Meterana arborea

Meterana arborea, commonly known as the tree fern, is a perennial plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Cyatheaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant has a rhizomatous root system that helps it anchor in moist, shaded environments. Its growth is slow, and it can live for several decades, making it a long-lived species.

The fronds are used in traditional medicine and as ornamental plants in gardens.
