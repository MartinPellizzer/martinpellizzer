Muscari hermonense

Muscari hermonense, commonly known as the Hermon bluebells, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of the Eastern Mediterranean, including parts of Turkey and Lebanon. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
