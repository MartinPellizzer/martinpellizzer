Myosotis hervei

Myosotis hervei, commonly known as the Herve's forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft texture.

Myosotis hervei is often found in damp, shaded areas such as forest floors and stream sides.
