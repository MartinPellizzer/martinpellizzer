Marsdenia isaura

Marsdenia isaura, commonly known as the yellow star vine, is a climbing plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It features slender, twining stems and small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, simple leaves that are usually ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a tropical look.
