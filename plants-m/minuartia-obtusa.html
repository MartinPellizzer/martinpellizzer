Minuartia obtusa

Minuartia obtusa, commonly known as the bluntleaf rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically has succulent, lobed leaves that are adapted to cold and dry environments. It produces small, yellow or white flowers that bloom in early spring.

Minuartia obtusa is often found growing on rocky slopes and in open, sunny habitats.
