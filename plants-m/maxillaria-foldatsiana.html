Maxillaria foldatsiana

Maxillaria foldatsiana, commonly known as the Foldats' orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with erect, branched stems and linear, leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive hood-like structure. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Maxillaria foldatsiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
