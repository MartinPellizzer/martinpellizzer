Mentha insularis f. compacta

Mentha insularis f. compacta, commonly known as the compact mint, is a compact form of the island mint.

It is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States, particularly found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors. The plant features small, aromatic leaves and produces tiny, pale blue to purple flowers. It has a strong, minty fragrance and is often used in herbal teas and as a culinary herb.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for container gardening and small garden spaces.
