Mammillaria hahniana

Mammillaria hahniana, commonly known as the Hahn's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white or yellow spines. The plant typically grows to a height of 5 to 10 centimeters and features small, tubular flowers that are usually yellow or orange. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
