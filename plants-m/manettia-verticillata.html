Manettia verticillata

Manettia verticillata, commonly known as the clustered manettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, whorled arrangement of leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as an erect shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of branches, often in spring or early summer.

The leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface.
