Magnolia × brozzonii

Magnolia × brozzonii, commonly known as the brozzonii magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It typically grows to a height of 10–15 meters and prefers well-drained, acidic soils.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
