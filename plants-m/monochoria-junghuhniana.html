Monochoria junghuhniana

Monochoria junghuhniana, commonly known as the narrowleaf water fern, is a aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically narrow and elongated. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Monochoria junghuhniana is often found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of rivers.
