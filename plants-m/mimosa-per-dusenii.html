Mimosa per-dusenii

Mimosa per-dusenii, commonly known as the velvetleaf mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are composed of multiple pairs of small, feathery leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in dense, spherical clusters. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Mimosa per-dusenii is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
