Marsdenia yuei

Marsdenia yuei, commonly known as the Yue's Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan province, where it grows in humid, forested areas. The plant is characterized by its trailing stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often have a waxy texture.

Marsdenia yuei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
