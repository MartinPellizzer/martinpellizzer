Melilotus arboreus

Melilotus arboreus, commonly known as the tree melilot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is a perennial shrub that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its branching structure and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in early summer. Its seeds are encased in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind.

Melilotus arboreus is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in well-drained soils in temperate climates.
