Memecylon excelsum

Memecylon excelsum, commonly known as the shining memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers with five petals, typically in shades of white or pink, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
