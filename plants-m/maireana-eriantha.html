Maireana eriantha

Maireana eriantha, commonly known as the white-flowered spinifex, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and covered in fine hairs, adapted to reduce water loss in arid environments.

Maireana eriantha is often found in sandy soils and is well-suited to coastal and inland regions with low rainfall.
