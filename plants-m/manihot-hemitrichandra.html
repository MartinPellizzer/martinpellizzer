Manihot hemitrichandra

Manihot hemitrichandra, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are used as a starch source and have a texture similar to cassava.
