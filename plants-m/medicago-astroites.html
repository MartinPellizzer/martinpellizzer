Medicago astroites

Medicago astroites, commonly known as the star vetch, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Medicago astroites is typically found in grasslands, open woodlands, and disturbed soils, thriving in well-drained environments.

Its growth habit and ecological role make it an important species in certain habitats.
