Machaonia cymosa var. glabrescens

Machaonia cymosa var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-flowered machaonia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically glossy and dark green, with a smooth texture that distinguishes it from other varieties.
