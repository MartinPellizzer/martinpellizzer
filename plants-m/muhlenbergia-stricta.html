Muhlenbergia stricta

Muhlenbergia stricta, commonly known as pink muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to North America.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a vibrant pink to red hue, especially in the fall. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes that add ornamental value to gardens and natural landscapes. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Muhlenbergia stricta is often used in native plant gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
