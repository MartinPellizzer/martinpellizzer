Myristica longifolia var. erratica

Myristica longifolia var. erratica, commonly known as the errant myristica, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy fruits containing hard, dark seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a glossy surface.

This variant is notable for its unique growth habits and is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value.
