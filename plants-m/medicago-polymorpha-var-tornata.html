Medicago polymorpha var. tornata

Medicago polymorpha var. tornata, commonly known as the variable medick, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to Europe and Asia but has been introduced to various regions around the world. It is often found in disturbed soils, such as roadsides and fields, due to its tolerance of poor soil conditions.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in ecological restoration and soil improvement.
