Mammillaria multiceps

Mammillaria multiceps, commonly known as the clustered mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
