Moniera stricta

Moniera stricta, commonly known as the rigid fern, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its upright, stiff stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Moniera stricta thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
