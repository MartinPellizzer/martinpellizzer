Medicago arabica f. elegans

Medicago arabica f. elegans, commonly known as the elegant clover, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility.

Its compact growth habit and attractive foliage make it a valuable addition to ecological and landscape designs.
