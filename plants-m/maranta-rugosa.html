Maranta rugosa

Maranta rugosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and decorative plants. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often marked with intricate patterns and veins. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color.

Maranta rugosa is often grown as a houseplant due to its attractive foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
