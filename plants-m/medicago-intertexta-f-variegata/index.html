Medicago Intertexta F. Variegata

Medicago intertexta f. variegata is a striking variant of the common medick plant known for its unique variegated foliage. This plant features leaves with a mix of green and white or yellow patterns, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, adapting well to various climates. Its compact growth habit and attractive appearance make it suitable for rock gardens and borders. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to other legumes in the Medicago genus.
