Maranta spectabilis

Maranta spectabilis, commonly known as the nightshade maranta, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, ornamental leaves that display vibrant, often contrasting colors. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes other ornamental foliage plants. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and can change color in response to light conditions.

Maranta spectabilis is often grown as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
