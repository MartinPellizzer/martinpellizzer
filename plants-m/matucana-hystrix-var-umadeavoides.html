Matucana hystrix var. umadeavoides

Matucana hystrix var. umadeavoides, commonly known as the Umadeavoides Pothos, is a variant of the pothos plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its thick, waxy leaves that are typically a deep green with a slight variegation. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or green in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

This variant is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
