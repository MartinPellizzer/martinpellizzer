Muhlenbergia sieberiana

Muhlenbergia sieberiana, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that bloom in late summer. This plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it adaptable to various arid and semi-arid environments. Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.

Muhlenbergia sieberiana also plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing habitat for local wildlife.
