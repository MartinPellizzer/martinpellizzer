Millettia fruticosa

Millettia fruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered milk tree, is a deciduous shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, alternate leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in summer. The plant produces small, woody pods containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or secondary forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
