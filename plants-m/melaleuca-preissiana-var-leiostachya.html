Melaleuca preissiana var. leiostachya

Melaleuca preissiana var. leiostachya, commonly known as the slender bottlebrush, is a flowering plant native to Western Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical inflorescences that resemble bottle brushes, typically composed of red or pink flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, whitish bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and coastal areas.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract native pollinators.
