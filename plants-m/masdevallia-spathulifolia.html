Masdevallia spathulifolia

Masdevallia spathulifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed masdevallia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features lanceolate to spatulate leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic potting mix.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
