Mikania schenckii

Mikania schenckii, commonly known as the Schenck's Mikania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant produces yellow flowers with prominent white ray florets, which are typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are usually opposite, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Mikania schenckii is often found in disturbed areas and can be invasive in certain regions.
