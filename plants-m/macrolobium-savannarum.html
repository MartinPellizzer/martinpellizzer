Macrolobium savannarum

Macrolobium savannarum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces showy, orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savannas and woodland areas.

Its rapid growth and striking foliage make it a popular ornamental plant in horticulture.
