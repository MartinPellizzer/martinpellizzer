Muraltia brachyceras

Muraltia brachyceras, commonly known as the dwarf muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, dark green leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a leathery texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by small, red berries in the summer. It is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
