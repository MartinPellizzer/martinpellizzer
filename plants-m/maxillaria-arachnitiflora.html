Maxillaria arachnitiflora

Maxillaria arachnitiflora, commonly known as the spider-flowered orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, spider-like floral structure, which gives it its common name. The plant produces fragrant, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters along the stems. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria arachnitiflora is a popular choice for both ornamental and horticultural cultivation due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
