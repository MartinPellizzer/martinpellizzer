Masdevallia ludibundella

Masdevallia ludibundella, commonly known as the laughing masdevallia, is a tropical orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, pendulous flowers that often have a vibrant mix of colors, including red, yellow, and green. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it absorb moisture and nutrients from the air.

Masdevallia ludibundella is valued in horticulture for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
