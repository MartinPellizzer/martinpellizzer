Mammillaria eschauzieri

Mammillaria eschauzieri, commonly known as the Eschauzier's pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact, rounded body covered in white to pale green spines. The plant features short, cylindrical stems and produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
