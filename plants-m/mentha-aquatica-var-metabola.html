Mentha aquatica var. metabola

Mentha aquatica var. metabola, commonly known as the water mint variant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to moist environments in Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the mint family, Lamiaceae, and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in whorls along the upper stems. Its aromatic leaves contain essential oils that contribute to its distinct minty fragrance.

It thrives in wet soils near streams, ponds, and marshes, making it a common sight in aquatic habitats.
