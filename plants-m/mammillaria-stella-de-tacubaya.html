Mammillaria stella-de-tacubaya

Mammillaria stella-de-tacubaya, commonly known as the Tacubaya star cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a compact growth habit and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant features a central stem that is often slightly ribbed and has a pale green to bluish-gray color. It produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
