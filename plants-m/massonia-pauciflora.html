Massonia pauciflora

Massonia pauciflora, commonly known as the silver leaf or desert star, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, adapted to store water and reduce transpiration.

The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
