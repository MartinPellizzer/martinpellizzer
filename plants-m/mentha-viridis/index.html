Mentha Viridis

Mentha viridis, also known as green mint, is a versatile and aromatic plant native to various regions across Europe and Asia. This species is characterized by its slender stems and fragrant, serrated leaves that emit a refreshing minty scent. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. The plant is widely used in herbal teas, culinary dishes, and traditional medicine for its soothing properties. Its ability to grow in diverse conditions makes it a valuable addition to both gardens and natural ecosystems.
