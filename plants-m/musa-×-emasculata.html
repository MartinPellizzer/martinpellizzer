Musa × emasculata

Musa × emasculata, commonly known as the banana hybrid, is a cultivated plant resulting from the crossbreeding of different banana species.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces clusters of fruit known as hands. This plant is primarily grown for its edible fruits, which are rich in potassium and other nutrients. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities, making it a popular choice for landscaping.
