Momordica foetida var. villosa

Momordica foetida var. villosa, commonly known as the hairy bitter melon, is a variant of the bitter melon species native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, spiny stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive appearance. The fruit is typically small, green, and has a bitter taste, though it is sometimes used in traditional medicine. It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and pumpkins.
