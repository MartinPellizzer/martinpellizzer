Magnolia rustica

Magnolia rustica, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has smooth, gray bark and produces woody fruits that contain seeds. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and turn yellow in autumn before dropping.

Magnolia rustica is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various soil types.
