Malcolmia patula var. bicolor

Malcolmia patula var. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that produces small, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and border plantings.
