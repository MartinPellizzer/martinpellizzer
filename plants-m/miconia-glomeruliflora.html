Miconia glomeruliflora

Miconia glomeruliflora, commonly known as the clustered-flowered miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a round, fleshy berry that turns red when mature.

Miconia glomeruliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy inflorescences.
