Maurocenia insignis

Maurocenia insignis, commonly known as the golden torch orchid, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is a perennial herb with a unique, torch-like inflorescence that features vibrant yellow flowers. The plant has strap-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Maurocenia insignis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
