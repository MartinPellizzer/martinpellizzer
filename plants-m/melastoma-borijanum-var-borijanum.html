Melastoma borijanum var. borijanum

Melastoma borijanum var. borijanum, commonly known as the Borijanum melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a red calyx. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
