Microrrhinum praetermissum

Microrrhinum praetermissum, commonly known as the lesser caraway, is a small annual herbaceous plant belonging to the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in umbels. The plant produces tiny, oval-shaped seeds that are often used in traditional herbal medicine. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, it thrives in disturbed soils and open habitats.
