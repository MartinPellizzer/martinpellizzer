Malope malacoides var. intermedia

Malope malacoides var. intermedia, commonly known as the hybrid yellow mallow, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features showy, yellow flowers with five petals that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has hairy, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
