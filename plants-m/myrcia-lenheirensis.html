Myrcia lenheirensis

Myrcia lenheirensis, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in well-drained soil. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Myrcia lenheirensis also has traditional uses in folk medicine.
