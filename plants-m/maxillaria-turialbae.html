Maxillaria turialbae

Maxillaria turialbae, commonly known as the Turalba Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has dark green, velvety leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is a terrestrial orchid that prefers well-drained soil and partial shade.

Its flowers bloom in late spring or early summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and greenhouses.
