Mammillaria elegans var. globosa

Mammillaria elegans var. globosa, commonly known as the globular mammillaria, is a compact, globular cactus with a dense arrangement of small, white to pale green tubercles.

It originates from the arid regions of Mexico, where it thrives in full sun and well-draining soil. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its textured foliage. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its small size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
