Marica northiana var. splendens

Marica northiana var. splendens, commonly known as the splendens marica, is a strikingly ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant, tubular flowers that range in color from bright yellow to orange, often with a red throat. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters, with dense foliage that provides a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, sandy soils.

This variety is prized for its long blooming period, which can extend from late winter through early spring.
