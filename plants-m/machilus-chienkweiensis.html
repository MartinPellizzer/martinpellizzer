Machilus chienkweiensis

Machilus chienkweiensis, commonly known as the Chienkwei mohave, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by oval, woody fruits.

Due to its limited distribution and rarity, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies.
