Mammillaria ehrenbergii

Mammillaria ehrenbergii, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent gardens and rockeries.
