Masdevallia roezlii var. rubra

Masdevallia roezlii var. rubra, commonly known as the Red-veined Masdevallia, is a striking orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, gracefully curved pseudobulbs and vibrant, velvety flowers that display a mix of red, pink, and white hues. The plant prefers cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and high air circulation. Its leaves are typically dark green and lance-shaped, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

This variant is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and elegant floral structure.
