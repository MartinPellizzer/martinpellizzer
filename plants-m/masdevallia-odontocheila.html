Masdevallia odontocheila

Masdevallia odontocheila, commonly known as the toothed-leaf masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves with a velvety texture. Its flowers are usually pale pink or white, with distinctive lip structures that aid in pollination.

Masdevallia odontocheila thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil for optimal growth.
