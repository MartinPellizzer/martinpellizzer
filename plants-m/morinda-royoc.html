Morinda royoc

Morinda royoc, commonly known as red moringa, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically reddish in color. The plant produces yellow flowers followed by orange or red fruits that contain flat, kidney-shaped seeds. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Morinda royoc is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
