Moricandia crenulata

Moricandia crenulata, commonly known as the leafy orache, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its succulent, lobed leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in arid or semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many other cruciferous plants.
