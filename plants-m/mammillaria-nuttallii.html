Mammillaria nuttallii

Mammillaria nuttallii, commonly known as the Nuttall's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mammillaria nuttallii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
