Mesembryanthemum juttae

Mesembryanthemum juttae, commonly known as the Jutta's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often blue-green leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white, typically blooming in spring and summer. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to rock gardens and dry landscapes.
