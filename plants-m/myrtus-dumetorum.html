Myrtus dumetorum

Myrtus dumetorum, commonly known as the rock myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces tiny, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale pink. Its fruit is a small, leathery berry that contains several seeds.

Myrtus dumetorum is valued for its ornamental appeal and has historical uses in traditional medicine.
