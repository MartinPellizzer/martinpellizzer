Myoporum serratum var. glandulosum

Myoporum serratum var. glandulosum, commonly known as the glandular myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a glandular surface, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal and lowland areas, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
