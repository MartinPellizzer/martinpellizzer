Micranthemum reflexum

Micranthemum reflexum, commonly known as water pennywort, is a small aquatic plant native to the Americas.

It typically grows in slow-moving freshwater habitats such as ponds and streams. The plant has tiny, opposite leaves that are usually less than 1 centimeter in length. It produces small, green flowers that are often submerged or floating.

Micranthemum reflexum is often used in aquariums due to its ornamental value and ability to provide shelter for aquatic life.
