Masdevallia melanoglossa

Masdevallia melanoglossa, commonly known as the black-lipped masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its striking appearance. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with compact growth and a delicate, clustered inflorescence. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high air circulation for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and coloration make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
