Manihot dulcis var. multifida

Manihot dulcis var. multifida, commonly known as the arrowleaf batata, is a variant of the sweet potato plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and tuberous roots that are rich in carbohydrates and nutrients. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its tubers are often used in culinary applications, particularly in Latin American and Caribbean cuisines.

The multifida variety is valued for its unique leaf morphology and adaptability to various growing conditions.
