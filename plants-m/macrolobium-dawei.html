Macrolobium dawei

Macrolobium dawei, commonly known as the giant lobed fern, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, broad, and often lobed fronds.

Native to Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests. The plant's fronds can grow up to several meters in length, featuring a delicate, feathery appearance. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a waxy texture, which helps in retaining moisture.

Macrolobium dawei is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its role in ecological systems.
