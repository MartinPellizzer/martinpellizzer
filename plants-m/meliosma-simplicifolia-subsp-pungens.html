Meliosma simplicifolia subsp. pungens

Meliosma simplicifolia subsp.

pungens, commonly known as the hairy meliosma, is a shrub native to parts of East Asia. It is characterized by its simple, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and taxonomic studies.
