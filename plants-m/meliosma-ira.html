Meliosma ira

Meliosma ira, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical shrub belonging to the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Meliosma ira is often found in moist, shaded environments in tropical regions.
