It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptic leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its flowers have a subtle fragrance and are often used in traditional Japanese gardens.
