Millettia luzonensis

Millettia luzonensis, commonly known as the Luzon mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive pink to purple flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet being ovate to elliptic in shape.

Millettia luzonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
