Muhlenbergia monandra

Muhlenbergia monandra, commonly known as the silver muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit, making it an attractive ornamental plant. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest during the late summer and fall. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, preferring arid or semi-arid conditions.

Muhlenbergia monandra is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
