Microlicea holosericea

Microlicea holosericea, commonly known as the silky microlicea, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of tiny, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in tropical rainforests. Its flowers have a unique structure with long, slender petals that give it a silky appearance.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
