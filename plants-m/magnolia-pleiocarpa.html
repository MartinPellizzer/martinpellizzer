Magnolia pleiocarpa

Magnolia pleiocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release numerous small, flat seeds.

Magnolia pleiocarpa is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
