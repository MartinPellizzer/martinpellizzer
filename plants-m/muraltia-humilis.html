Muraltia humilis

Muraltia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, with dense, dark green foliage. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale pink, and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a flattened pod that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
