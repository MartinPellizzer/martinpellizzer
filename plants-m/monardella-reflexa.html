Monardella reflexa

Monardella reflexa, commonly known as narrowleaf monardella, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and bloom from late spring to early summer.

It thrives in sunny, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and meadows.
