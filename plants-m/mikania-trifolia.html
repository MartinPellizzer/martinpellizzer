Mikania trifolia

Mikania trifolia, commonly known as creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in disturbed areas, such as roadsides and waste grounds.

Mikania trifolia is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
