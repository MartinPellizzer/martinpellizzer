Mentha Arvensis F. Simplex

Mentha arvensis f. simplex is a lesser-known variant of the common mint family known for its unique characteristics. This plant is often found growing in moist, shaded areas and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. Unlike its more widely recognized relatives, it has a simpler leaf structure and a milder scent. It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in local ecosystems.
