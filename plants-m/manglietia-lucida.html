Manglietia lucida

Manglietia lucida, commonly known as the golden manglietia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy white flowers with a strong fragrance, typically blooming in the late afternoon. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
