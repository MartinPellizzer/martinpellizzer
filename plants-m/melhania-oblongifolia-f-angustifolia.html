Melhania oblongifolia f. angustifolia

Melhania oblongifolia f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf melhania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
