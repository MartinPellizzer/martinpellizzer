Maranta regeliana

Maranta regeliana, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, often colorful leaves that fold upward at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in both indoor and outdoor settings. Its leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped, with intricate patterns and a waxy texture.

Maranta regeliana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil to flourish.
