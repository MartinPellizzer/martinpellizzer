Musa acuminata var. bantamensis

Musa acuminata var. bantamensis, commonly known as the Bantam banana, is a small, ornamental banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, pointed leaves that are typically dark green with a silvery underside. The plant produces small, elongated fruit that is not typically consumed but is valued for its ornamental appeal. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and indoor spaces.
