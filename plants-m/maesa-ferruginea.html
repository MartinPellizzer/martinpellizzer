Maesa ferruginea

Maesa ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty maesa, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, leathery leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, acidic soils and is often found in forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.
