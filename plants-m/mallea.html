Mallea

Mallea, commonly known as the red sandalwood, is a genus of flowering plants native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Proteaceae and is characterized by its woody, often twisted stems and small, leathery leaves. Due to overharvesting, many species of Mallea are now classified as threatened or endangered.
