Maxillaria jostii

Maxillaria jostii, commonly known as the Jost's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments within forests. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its flowering stems. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with a distinctive coloration that ranges from white to pink and purple.

Maxillaria jostii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
