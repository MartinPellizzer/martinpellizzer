Magnolia salicifolia var. fasciata

Magnolia salicifolia var. fasciata, commonly known as the variegated willowleaf magnolia, is a striking ornamental tree native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with white or cream-colored margins, creating a distinctive variegated appearance. The tree produces large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. It thrives in partial to full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its unique foliage and elegant blossoms make it a valuable addition to both residential and public spaces.
