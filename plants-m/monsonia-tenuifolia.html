Monsonia tenuifolia

Monsonia tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved monsonia, is a herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Geraniaceae and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value and relatively low maintenance, it is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
