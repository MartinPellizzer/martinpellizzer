Microchirita mollissima var. mollissima

Microchirita mollissima var. mollissima, commonly known as the velvet leaf or velvet plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
