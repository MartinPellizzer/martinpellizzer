Michelia maudiae

Michelia maudiae, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the white-flowered michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Michelia maudiae prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its pleasant scent and attractive foliage.
