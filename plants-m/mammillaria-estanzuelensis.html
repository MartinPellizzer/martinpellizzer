Mammillaria estanzuelensis

Mammillaria estanzuelensis, commonly known as the Estanzuelensis Pincushion Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish, emerging from the areoles.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
