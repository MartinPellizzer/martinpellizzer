Mesua lepidota var. parviflora

Mesua lepidota var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It features striking, fiery red to orange-pink flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its surroundings. The plant has glossy, dark green, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The flowers are typically followed by small, woody pods containing flat, winged seeds.
