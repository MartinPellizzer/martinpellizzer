Merwilla

Merwilla, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy red or orange flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has compound leaves with three to five leaflets and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. Merwilla thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Its rapid growth and vibrant floral display make it a notable feature in the botanical world.
