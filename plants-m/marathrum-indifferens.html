Marathrum indifferens

Marathrum indifferens, commonly known as the false orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or pale yellow in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its delicate appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
