Mondo kansuense

Mondo kansuense, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a glossy texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Mondo kansuense is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to indoor growing conditions.
