Miltonia stellata

Miltonia stellata, commonly known as the star orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink with a contrasting purple or violet lip. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, providing a lush green backdrop to its showy blooms.

Miltonia stellata thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and consistent moisture for optimal growth.
