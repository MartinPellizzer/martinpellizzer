Medicago coronata var. uncata

Medicago coronata var. uncata, commonly known as the slender goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, ascending stems with small, trifoliate leaves that have a distinctive heart-shaped base. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. Its seed pods are narrow and elongated, containing several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This species is adapted to rocky and dry habitats, showing resilience to drought and poor soil conditions.
