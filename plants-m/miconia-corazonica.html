Miconia corazonica

Miconia corazonica, commonly known as the Heartleaf Miconia, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is considered a problematic species in some regions.
