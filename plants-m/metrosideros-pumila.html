Metrosideros pumila

Metrosideros pumila, commonly known as the small flame tree, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are oval to lance-shaped with a pointed tip. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.
