Markea pendula

Markea pendula, commonly known as the weeping markea, is a unique species within the Markea genus.

It is characterized by its drooping, cascading foliage that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are elongated and often have a soft, velvety texture.

Markea pendula is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to create a dramatic visual effect in gardens.
