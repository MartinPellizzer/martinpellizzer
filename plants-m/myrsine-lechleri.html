Myrsine lechleri

Myrsine lechleri, commonly known as sangre de drago, is a shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black bark, which contains high concentrations of tannins. The plant has elliptical leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, white flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds.

Due to its medicinal properties, it has been used by indigenous communities for centuries.
