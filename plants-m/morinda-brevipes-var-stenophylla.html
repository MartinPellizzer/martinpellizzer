Morinda brevipes var. stenophylla

Morinda brevipes var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf morinda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptic leaves with a smooth margin. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers that are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.
