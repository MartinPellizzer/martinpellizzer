Minuartia verna var. coreana

Minuartia verna var. coreana, commonly known as the Korean spring sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its prostrate or ascending growth habit. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers in clusters during the spring season. Its leaves are typically linear and covered in a fine layer of white powdery coating, known as farina.
