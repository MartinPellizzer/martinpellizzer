Maranta strigosa

Maranta strigosa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its rhombic, dark green leaves with a distinctive zigzag pattern along the midrib. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its attractive, variegated leaves.

Maranta strigosa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
