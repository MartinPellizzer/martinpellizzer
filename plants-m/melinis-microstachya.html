Melinis microstachya

Melinis microstachya, commonly known as little bluestem, is a native North American grass species that thrives in a variety of soil types.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green blades and its ability to form dense, drought-resistant turf. This plant is often found in prairies, meadows, and disturbed sites, contributing to soil stabilization and erosion control. Its flowering stems produce small, spike-like inflorescences that are typically tan or brown in color.

Melinis microstachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting wildlife and maintaining grassland ecosystems.
