Melastoma discolor

Melastoma discolor, commonly known as the purple-flowered Melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma discolor is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
