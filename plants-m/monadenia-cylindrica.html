Monadenia cylindrica

Monadenia cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical monadenia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils. The plant features simple, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and along riverbanks.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
