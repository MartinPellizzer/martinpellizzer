Matucana haynii

Matucana haynii, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowroot, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces striking inflorescences with a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Matucana haynii is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in local medicine.
