Monocera macrocera

Monocera macrocera, commonly known as the large-winged orchid, is a species of orchid native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that feature prominent, broad wings on the petals. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories. Its flowering period occurs during the wet season, and it relies on specific pollinators for reproduction.

The botanical features of Monocera macrocera make it a notable species within the Orchidaceae family.
