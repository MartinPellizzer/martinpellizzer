Maxillaria rollissonii

Maxillaria rollissonii, commonly known as the Rock Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and often features dark green to grayish leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Maxillaria rollissonii thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for botanical gardens and orchid enthusiasts.
