Meyera

Meyera, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and chrysanthemums. The plant features showy yellow or white flowers surrounded by greenish bracts, and it produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Meyera thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its widespread cultivation in various regions.
