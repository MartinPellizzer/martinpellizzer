Macrosolen papillosus

Macrosolen papillosus, commonly known as the hairy spider plant, is a striking member of the Asparagaceae family.

It is characterized by its long, arching stems that bear clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. The plant's most distinctive feature is its hairy, fleshy leaves, which are often covered in fine, soft hairs. Its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
