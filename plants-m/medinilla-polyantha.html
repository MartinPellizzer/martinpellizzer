Medinilla polyantha

Medinilla polyantha, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy green foliage and its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn from green to red as they mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial shade.

Medinilla polyantha is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive appearance and seasonal flowering.
