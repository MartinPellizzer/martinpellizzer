Melastoma pusilliflorum

Melastoma pusilliflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink or white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, winged seeds when mature.
