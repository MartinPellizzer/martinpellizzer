Maerua gillettii

Maerua gillettii, commonly known as the desert thornbush, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments with minimal water requirements. Its woody stems are often covered in sharp spines, which provide protection from herbivores.

Maerua gillettii plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for desert wildlife.
