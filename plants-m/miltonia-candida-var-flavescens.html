Miltonia candida var. flavescens

Miltonia candida var. flavescens, commonly known as the yellow-flowered orchid, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, with a distinctive spurred lip. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, requiring well-draining potting media.

This variety is prized for its vibrant blooms and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
