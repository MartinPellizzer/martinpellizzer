Matucana herzogiana

Matucana herzogiana, commonly known as the Herzog's matucana, is a species of flowering plant in the Arecaceae family.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.

Matucana herzogiana prefers warm, humid environments and is typically grown in containers or as part of a tropical landscape.
