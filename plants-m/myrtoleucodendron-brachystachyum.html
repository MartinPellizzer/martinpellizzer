Myrtoleucodendron brachystachyum

Myrtoleucodendron brachystachyum, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed myrtle-leaved tree, is a species native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.
