Magnolia hunanensis

Magnolia hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
