Mandevilla subsessilis

Mandevilla subsessilis, commonly known as the scarlet mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and long, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant blooms throughout the growing season. It thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated as a decorative vine or shrub.

Mandevilla subsessilis is valued for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to various garden environments.
