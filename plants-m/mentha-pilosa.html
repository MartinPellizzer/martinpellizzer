Mentha pilosa

Mentha pilosa, commonly known as Chinese mint or shi zhu yao, is a perennial herb native to Asia.

It is characterized by its square stems, opposite leaves, and small, fragrant flowers. The plant has a distinctive minty aroma and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are commonly harvested for culinary and medicinal purposes.

Mentha pilosa thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.
