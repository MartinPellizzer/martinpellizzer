Mammillaria zephyranthiflora

Mammillaria zephyranthiflora, commonly known as the Zephyranthus mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a covering of fine, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spiky appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
