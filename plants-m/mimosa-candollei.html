Mimosa candollei

Mimosa candollei, commonly known as the golden mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its leaves are sensitive to touch, curling up when disturbed, a trait that adds to its unique appearance.

Mimosa candollei is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
