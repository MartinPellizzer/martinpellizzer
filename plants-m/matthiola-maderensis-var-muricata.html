Matthiola maderensis var. muricata

Matthiola maderensis var. muricata, commonly known as the rough stocky mustard, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves and stems, which contribute to its rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for rock gardens or borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
