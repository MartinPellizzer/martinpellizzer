Modecca mannii

Modecca mannii, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its compact rosette of fleshy, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, often with a contrasting central stripe. Modecca mannii is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
