Mandevilla speciosa

Mandevilla speciosa, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant features waxy, dark green leaves and can grow as a trailing or climbing plant. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla speciosa is popular in ornamental gardening for its showy blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
