Melochia argentina var. lanceolata

Melochia argentina var. lanceolata, commonly known as the lanceleaf melochia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It features heart-shaped to lanceolate leaves that are often hairy and have a distinctive greenish-white underside. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or near water sources.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for tropical gardens and landscapes.
