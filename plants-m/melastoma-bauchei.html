Melastoma bauchei

Melastoma bauchei, commonly known as the purple false heather, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and emits a fragrant scent.

Melastoma bauchei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
