Mimosa lanuginosa var. lanuginosa

Mimosa lanuginosa var. lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flower clusters that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
