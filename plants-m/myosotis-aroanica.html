Myosotis aroanica

Myosotis aroanica, commonly known as the New Zealand forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream sides. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Myosotis aroanica is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also an important species in New Zealand's native flora.
