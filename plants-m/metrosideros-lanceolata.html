Metrosideros lanceolata

Metrosideros lanceolata, commonly known as the New Zealand flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, bright red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, often giving it a fiery appearance.
