Megalastrum villosulum

Megalastrum villosulum, commonly known as the hairy leaf vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
