Macrolobium colombianum var. ocumarense

Macrolobium colombianum var. ocumarense, commonly known as the Ocumare mountain bean, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological studies and potential agricultural applications.
