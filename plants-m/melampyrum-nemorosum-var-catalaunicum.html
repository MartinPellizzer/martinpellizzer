Melampyrum nemorosum var. catalaunicum

Melampyrum nemorosum var. catalaunicum, commonly known as the Catalan willow herb, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in woodland and shaded areas. The plant produces clusters of small, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.
