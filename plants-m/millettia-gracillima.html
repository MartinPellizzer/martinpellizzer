Millettia gracillima

Millettia gracillima, commonly known as the slender millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It features pinnate leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant bears small, spherical pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.

Millettia gracillima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
