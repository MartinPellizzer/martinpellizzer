Microlicia barretoana

Microlicia barretoana, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit or yellow starfruit, is a member of the Myristicaceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest of Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of yellowish-white flowers. Its fruit, which resembles a starfruit, is edible and has a sweet, tangy flavor.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in tropical regions.
