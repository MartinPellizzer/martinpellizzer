Magnolia inodora

Magnolia inodora, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the white magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The tree has a broad, rounded crown and smooth, grayish-brown bark. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a serrated margin.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
