Microstylis aurata

Microstylis aurata, commonly known as the golden thread plant, is a small, herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features delicate, grass-like leaves that are typically green with a slight golden hue along the edges. The plant produces tiny, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and shaded borders.

Microstylis aurata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.
