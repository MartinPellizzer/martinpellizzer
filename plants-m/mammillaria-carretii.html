Mammillaria carretii

Mammillaria carretii, commonly known as the Carreti's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and dense, spiny areoles. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a compact, rounded shape.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
