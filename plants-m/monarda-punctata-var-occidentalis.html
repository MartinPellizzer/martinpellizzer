Monarda punctata var. occidentalis

Monarda punctata var. occidentalis, commonly known as the western bee balm, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and a strong, spicy scent. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a popular choice for native gardens.
