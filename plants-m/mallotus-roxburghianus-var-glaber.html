Mallotus roxburghianus var. glaber

Mallotus roxburghianus var. glaber, commonly known as the smooth mallotus, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and alternate, simple leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, followed by woody, spiny fruits containing several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
