Memecylon edule var. laeta

Memecylon edule var. laeta, commonly known as the sweetleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by edible, fleshy fruits resembling small apples. The fruit contains a single seed and is often consumed fresh or used in local cuisines.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
