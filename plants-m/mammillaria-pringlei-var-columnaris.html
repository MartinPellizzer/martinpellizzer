Mammillaria pringlei var. columnaris

Mammillaria pringlei var. columnaris, commonly known as the columnar mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features a sparsely branched structure with a smooth, greenish-gray surface. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This variety is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
