Mammillaria centricirrha f. guilleminiana

Mammillaria centricirrha f. guilleminiana, commonly known as the Guilleminiana mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent white spines and a dense covering of small, white radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

This variety is prized for its ornamental appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
