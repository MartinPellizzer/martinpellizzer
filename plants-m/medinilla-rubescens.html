Medinilla rubescens

Medinilla rubescens, commonly known as the red velvet orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from deep red to pink. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla rubescens is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
