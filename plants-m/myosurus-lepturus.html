Myosurus lepturus

It is characterized by its delicate, hairless stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. Myosurus lepturus is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed soils, making it an important pioneer species in wetland ecosystems.
