Masdevallia britoi

Masdevallia britoi, commonly known as the British masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful flower structures. The plant typically features a pseudobulb with fleshy leaves and produces delicate, pendulous flowers in shades of pink, white, and violet. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled conditions by enthusiasts and botanists.
