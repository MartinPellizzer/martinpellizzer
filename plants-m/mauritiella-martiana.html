Mauritiella martiana

Mauritiella martiana, commonly known as the Mauritius palm, is a species of palm native to the island of Mauritius.

It is a tall, slender tree with a smooth, greyish trunk and compound, feather-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into clustered, spiny fruits. Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and ecosystem.

It is also valued for its ornamental qualities and historical significance.
