Myosotis lanata

Myosotis lanata, commonly known as the downy forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, woolly stems and leaves, which give it a distinctive, soft appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Myosotis lanata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
