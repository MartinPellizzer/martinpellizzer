Maxillaria angustisegmenta

Maxillaria angustisegmenta, commonly known as the narrow-segmented maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria angustisegmenta is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
