Malcolmia maritima alba

Malcolmia maritima alba, commonly known as the white coastal mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, showing tolerance to salt and wind. It is often found in dunes and along shorelines, contributing to stabilizing sand dunes.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
