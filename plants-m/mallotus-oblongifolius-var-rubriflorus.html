Mallotus oblongifolius var. rubriflorus

Mallotus oblongifolius var. rubriflorus, commonly known as the red-flowered mallotus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces striking red flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to its appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its botanical features make it a valuable species for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
