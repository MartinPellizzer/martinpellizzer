Mikania officinalis

Mikania officinalis, commonly known as the Chinese milkvetch, is a climbing vine belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to East Asia and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant contains various bioactive compounds, including flavonoids and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. It is often used in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions, posing a challenge to local ecosystems.
