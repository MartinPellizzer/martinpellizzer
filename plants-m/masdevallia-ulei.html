Masdevallia ulei

Masdevallia ulei, commonly known as the Ulei Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light conditions to bloom.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural range, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
