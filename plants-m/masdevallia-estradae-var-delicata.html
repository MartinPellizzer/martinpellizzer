Masdevallia estradae var. delicata

Masdevallia estradae var. delicata, commonly known as the delicate masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It features intricate, lance-shaped leaves and produces strikingly colorful, fragrant flowers with a unique, spurred lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a splash of vibrancy to its natural habitat.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
