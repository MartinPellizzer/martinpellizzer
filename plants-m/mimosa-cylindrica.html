Mimosa cylindrica

Mimosa cylindrica, commonly known as the cylindrical mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces a distinctive, spherical seed pod that turns brown as it matures. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Mimosa cylindrica is also known for its sensitive leaves, which fold shut when touched, a trait that adds to its unique appearance.
