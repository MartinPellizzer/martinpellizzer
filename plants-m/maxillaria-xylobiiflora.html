Maxillaria xylobiiflora

Maxillaria xylobiiflora, commonly known as the xylobiiflora orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish, with a distinctive lip that is often darker in color.

Maxillaria xylobiiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
