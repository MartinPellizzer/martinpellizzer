Mahonia bijuga

Mahonia bijuga, commonly known as the evergreen mahonia, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of southeastern China.

It features compound leaves that are dark green and glossy, with a leathery texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Mahonia bijuga is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate a range of environmental conditions.
