Mallotus stipularis

Mallotus stipularis, commonly known as the red-flowered mallotus, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of bright red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
