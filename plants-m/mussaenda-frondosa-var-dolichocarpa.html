Mussaenda frondosa var. dolichocarpa

Mussaenda frondosa var. dolichocarpa, commonly known as the long-leaf Mussaenda, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, glossy green leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. The plant produces clusters of small, white, waxy flowers that resemble a crown, giving it a striking appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden landscapes in tropical and subtropical regions.
