Mimosa palmetorum

Mimosa palmetorum, commonly known as the palmate mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its palm-like leaves, which are divided into feather-like segments. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Mimosa palmetorum is also known for its sensitivity to touch, as its leaves fold shut when disturbed.
