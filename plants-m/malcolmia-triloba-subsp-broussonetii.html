Malcolmia triloba subsp. broussonetii

Malcolmia triloba subsp.

broussonetii, commonly known as the Broussonet's malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pale yellow flowers.

The plant features alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have a slightly succulent texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate blooms.
