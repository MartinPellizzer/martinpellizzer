Maranta ruiziana

Maranta ruiziana, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage with a zigzag pattern along the leaf veins. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants with colorful, often brightly colored leaves. It typically grows as an understory plant in humid, shady environments, preferring well-drained, fertile soil.

Maranta ruiziana is valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in indoor conditions with proper care.
