Mentha × piperita var. langii

Mentha × piperita var. langii, commonly known as peppermint langii, is a cultivated variety of peppermint.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Mentha piperita and Mentha × didyma. This variety is characterized by its aromatic, menthol-rich leaves and strong, cooling scent. It is often used in herbal teas and aromatherapy due to its therapeutic properties.

The plant typically grows as an upright perennial with square stems and opposite leaves.
