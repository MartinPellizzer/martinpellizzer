Michelia × longifolia

Michelia × longifolia, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or fragrant Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and smooth, bark-like stems. Its flowers are typically white or pale yellow, with a strong, sweet scent that attracts pollinators.

Michelia × longifolia is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
