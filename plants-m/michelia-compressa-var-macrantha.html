Michelia compressa var. macrantha

Michelia compressa var. macrantha, commonly known as the large-flowered michelia, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight pubescence on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are not only aesthetically pleasing but also attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
