Mimosa adenophylla var. mitis

Mimosa adenophylla var. mitis, commonly known as the soft mimosa, is a low-growing shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are often covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that appear in clusters, typically in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Its unique appearance and easy care make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
