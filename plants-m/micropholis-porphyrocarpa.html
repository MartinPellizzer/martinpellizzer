Micropholis porphyrocarpa

Micropholis porphyrocarpa, commonly known as the purple-fruited micropholis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Anacardiaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
