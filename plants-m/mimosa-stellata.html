Mimosa stellata

Mimosa stellata, commonly known as the star mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often grown as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa stellata is also known for its sensitive leaves, which fold shut when touched, a trait that adds to its intriguing botanical appeal.
