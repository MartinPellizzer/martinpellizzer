Mammillaria bellisiana

Mammillaria bellisiana, commonly known as the bellisiana mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its small size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
