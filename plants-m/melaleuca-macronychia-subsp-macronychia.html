Melaleuca macronychia subsp. macronychia

Melaleuca macronychia subsp.

macronychia, commonly known as the broad-leaved bottlebrush, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family, Myrtaceae. It is native to northern Australia, particularly found in the northern territories and parts of Queensland.

The plant is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and its striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticulture for its drought tolerance and unique floral display.
