Marcetella

Marcetella, commonly known as the common rockrose, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Rosaceae.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant features small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, adapted to conserve water.

Marcetella is often used in rock gardens due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
