Microlicia cardiophora

Microlicia cardiophora, commonly known as the heart-leaved microlicia, is a small, evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that often have a heart-shaped base. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that are typically borne on short pedicels. Its fruits are small, capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Microlicia cardiophora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
