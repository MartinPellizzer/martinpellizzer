Mathiolaria annua

Mathiolaria annua, commonly known as the annual mathiolaria, is an annual herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has simple, opposite leaves that are usually lance-shaped. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often green or yellowish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Due to its rapid life cycle, it is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.
