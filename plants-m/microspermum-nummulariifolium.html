Microspermum nummulariifolium

Microspermum nummulariifolium, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to parts of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, round, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically purple or blue in color. It thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in arid regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
