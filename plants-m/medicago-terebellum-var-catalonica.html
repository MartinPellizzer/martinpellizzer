Medicago terebellum var. catalonica

Medicago terebellum var. catalonica, commonly known as the Catalan clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It has been historically used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a forage plant and its adaptation to Mediterranean climates.
