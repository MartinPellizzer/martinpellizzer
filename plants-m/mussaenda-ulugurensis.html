Mussaenda ulugurensis

Mussaenda ulugurensis, commonly known as the Uluguru Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to the Uluguru Mountains in Tanzania.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its striking, bright yellow inflorescences that resemble umbrellas. The leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is adapted to the tropical and subtropical climates of its native region.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
