Magnolia changhungtana

Magnolia changhungtana, commonly known as the Hubei magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with a faintly flaky texture.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
