Mutisia herzogiana

Mutisia herzogiana, commonly known as the Herbst's mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Mutisia herzogiana is often found in dry, open habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
