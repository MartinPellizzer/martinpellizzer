Mimulopsis solmsii var. orophila

Mimulopsis solmsii var. orophila, commonly known as the alpine mimulopsis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs, which helps reduce water loss.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations to high-altitude conditions.
