Medinilla dumosa

Medinilla dumosa, commonly known as the woolly medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pink or white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in humid, warm environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla dumosa is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
