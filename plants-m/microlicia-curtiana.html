Microlicia curtiana

Microlicia curtiana, commonly known as the African orchid or velvet orchid, is a tropical plant native to regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are elongated and often covered in a fine, fuzzy texture.

Microlicia curtiana thrives in warm, moist climates and requires careful cultivation to maintain its ornamental appeal.
