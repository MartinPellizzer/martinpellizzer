Myoporum acuminatum f. ellipticum

Myoporum acuminatum f. ellipticum, commonly known as the narrowleaf myoporum, is a compact shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, elliptical leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slight pubescence on the underside. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or gravelly soils.

This variety is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
