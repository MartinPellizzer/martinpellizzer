Mentzelia bartonioides

Mentzelia bartonioides, commonly known as the desert blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a perennial herb with a slender, branching stem that often grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant produces showy, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in hot, dry conditions.
