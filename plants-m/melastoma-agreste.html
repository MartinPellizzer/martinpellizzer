Melastoma agreste

Melastoma agreste, commonly known as the small-leaved melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, followed by fleshy, berry-like fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value and ecological importance make it a notable species in its native habitat.
