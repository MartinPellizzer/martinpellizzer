Melastoma laxum

Melastoma laxum, commonly known as the slender melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it often grows in moist, shaded environments. It produces berries that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Melastoma laxum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
