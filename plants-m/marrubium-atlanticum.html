Marrubium atlanticum

Marrubium atlanticum, commonly known as Atlantic horehound, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of the Iberian Peninsula.

It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. The plant has aromatic, hairy leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers. It is part of the Boraginaceae family, known for its medicinal properties.

Marrubium atlanticum is valued for its potential health benefits and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
