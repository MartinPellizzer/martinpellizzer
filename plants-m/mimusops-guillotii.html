Mimusops guillotii

Mimusops guillotii, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, dark-brown bark and glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant is also sometimes cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and seasonal flowering.
