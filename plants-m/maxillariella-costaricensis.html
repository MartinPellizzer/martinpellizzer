Maxillariella costaricensis

Maxillariella costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, often greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on rocks or in forest understories. Its leaves are usually grass-like and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillariella costaricensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
