Melastoma triste

Melastoma triste, commonly known as the sad melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red, with five petals and prominent stamens. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical forests.

Melastoma triste is also known for its medicinal properties, used in traditional medicine for various health purposes.
