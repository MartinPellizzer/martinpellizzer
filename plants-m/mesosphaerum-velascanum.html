Mesosphaerum velascanum

Mesosphaerum velascanum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvetleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Mesosphaerum velascanum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and riverbanks.
