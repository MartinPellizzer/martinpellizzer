Maxillaria soconuscana

Maxillaria soconuscana, commonly known as the Soconusco orchid, is a species native to Mexico, particularly in the southern regions of the country.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its upright growth habit and the presence of pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria soconuscana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
