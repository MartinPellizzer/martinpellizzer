Malache schrankii

Malache schrankii, commonly known as the Schrank's malache, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically red or orange in color. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.
