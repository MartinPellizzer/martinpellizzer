Mammillaria aulacothele var. sulcimamma

Mammillaria aulacothele var. sulcimamma, commonly known as the Sulci-mamma mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. Its leaves are modified into spines, and it has a distinctive rib structure.

It is well-suited for arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
