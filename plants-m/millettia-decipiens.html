Millettia decipiens

Millettia decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive millettia, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and Malaysia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in yellow or greenish hues. Its seeds are enclosed in elongated, pod-like fruits that split open when mature.

Millettia decipiens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
