Masdevallia dodsonii

Masdevallia dodsonii, commonly known as the Dodson's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features intricate, often colorful flowers with a unique, elongated shape that sets it apart from other orchid species. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with leaves that are narrow and grass-like, adapted to its humid, shaded habitat. It requires specific conditions, including high humidity and indirect light, to thrive.

Due to its rarity and beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
