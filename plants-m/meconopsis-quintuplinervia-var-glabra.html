Meconopsis quintuplinervia var. glabra

Meconopsis quintuplinervia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth meconopsis, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. It prefers cool, moist environments and thrives in well-drained, acidic soils.

This variety is distinguished by its smooth, glabrous stem and foliage, making it a unique and ornamental species within the Meconopsis genus.
