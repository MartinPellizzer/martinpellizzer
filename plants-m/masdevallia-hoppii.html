Masdevallia hoppii

Masdevallia hoppii, commonly known as the Hoppii Masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking, intricate flower structure. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, colorful blooms in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
