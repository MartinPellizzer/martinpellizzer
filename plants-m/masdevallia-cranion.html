Masdevallia cranion

Masdevallia cranion, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped flower that resembles a cap or a helmet, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, moist environments. Its flowers are usually a combination of white, green, and yellow, with intricate patterns that add to its visual appeal.

Masdevallia cranion is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and rarity in cultivation.
