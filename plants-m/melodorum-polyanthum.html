Melodorum polyanthum

Melodorum polyanthum, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Melodorum polyanthum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
