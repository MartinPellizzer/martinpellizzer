Melastoma conglomeratum

Melastoma conglomeratum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a waxy texture.

Melastoma conglomeratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
