Musa basjoo var. lushanensis

Musa basjoo var. lushanensis, commonly known as the Lushan banana, is a cold-hardy variant of the Japanese banana plant.

It is native to Japan and is characterized by its ability to survive in colder climates compared to other banana species. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. It produces edible, sweet fruit that is typically harvested when fully mature.

This variety is often cultivated for both its ornamental value and its unique culinary qualities.
