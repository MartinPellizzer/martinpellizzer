Mikania micrantha f. hirsuta

Mikania micrantha f. hirsuta, commonly known as the hairy Mikania, is a variant of the invasive Mikania micrantha species.

It is a fast-growing vine characterized by its hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in tropical and subtropical regions, often spreading aggressively in disturbed habitats.

Its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats make it a significant ecological concern in many areas.
