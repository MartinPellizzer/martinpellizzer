Mammillaria bocasana subsp. eschauzieri

Mammillaria bocasana subsp.

eschauzieri, commonly known as the Eschauzier's mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a globular shape and a gray-green to blue-gray color. It features small, tuberculate stems and clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant is native to Mexico and thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
