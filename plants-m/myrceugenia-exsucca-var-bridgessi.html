Myrceugenia exsucca var. bridgessi

Myrceugenia exsucca var. bridgessi, commonly known as the Bridgess's myrtle, is a species of flowering plant native to the temperate rainforests of southern South America.

The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when mature, which are an important food source for local wildlife.
