Miconia dolichostachya

Miconia dolichostachya, commonly known as the long-stemmed miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, bract-covered inflorescences with small, white to pink flowers. Its fruits are bright red, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Miconia dolichostachya is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
