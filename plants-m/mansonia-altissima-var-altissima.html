Mansonia altissima var. altissima

Mansonia altissima var. altissima, commonly known as the tall mangrove, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is a deciduous tree that typically grows in coastal areas, particularly in brackish or saline waters. The plant has a distinctive appearance with thick, waxy leaves and a sturdy, buttressed trunk. Its flowers are small and clustered, and it produces woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by water.
