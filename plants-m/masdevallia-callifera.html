Masdevallia callifera

Masdevallia callifera, commonly known as the Callifera Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, terrestrial growth habit and its unique, intricately patterned flowers. The plant typically produces showy, spurred orchid blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, with a distinctive callus on the lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, often growing in rocky or shaded forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
