Mimosa regnellii f. rectispina

Mimosa regnellii f. rectispina, commonly known as the straight-spined mimosa, is a shrub native to the arid regions of South America.

It features compound leaves that are typically bipinnate, with small, fern-like leaflets. The plant produces yellow, spherical flower heads that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is adapted to dry environments, with deep roots and a low-growing habit.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
