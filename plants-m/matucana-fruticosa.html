Matucana fruticosa

Matucana fruticosa, commonly known as the Andean chuchuhuasi, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruit is a small, hard capsule that contains several seeds.

Matucana fruticosa is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
