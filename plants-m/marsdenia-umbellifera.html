Marsdenia umbellifera

Marsdenia umbellifera, commonly known as the umbrella milkweed, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea.

Marsdenia umbellifera is typically found in forested areas and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
