Malvastrum plumosum

Malvastrum plumosum, commonly known as the silky mallow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fluffy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has palmately divided leaves with a fuzzy texture, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Malvastrum plumosum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
