Myrmechila truncata

Myrmechila truncata, commonly known as the dwarf cycad, is a unique plant belonging to the Cycadaceae family.

It is characterized by its short, stocky trunk and a crown of pinnate, feather-like leaves that give it a distinct appearance. Native to the tropical regions of Africa, this plant thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

The plant's botanical features reflect its adaptation to arid and semi-arid environments, with a thick, water-storing trunk and a compact structure.
