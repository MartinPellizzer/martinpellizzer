Myosotis superalpina

Myosotis superalpina, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and slender, hairy stems. The plant thrives in rocky, alpine environments where it forms low-growing mats or clusters. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, adapted to withstand cold and harsh conditions.

Myosotis superalpina is often found in meadows and on mountain slopes, where it blooms in late spring to early summer.
