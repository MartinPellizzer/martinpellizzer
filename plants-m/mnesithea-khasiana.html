Mnesithea khasiana

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its hairy stems and broad, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
