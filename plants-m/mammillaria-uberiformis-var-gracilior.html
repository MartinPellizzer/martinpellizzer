Mammillaria uberiformis var. gracilior

Mammillaria uberiformis var. gracilior, commonly known as the slender mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and is covered in small, white spines. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
