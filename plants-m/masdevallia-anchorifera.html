Masdevallia anchorifera

Masdevallia anchorifera, commonly known as the anchored masdevallia, is a rare orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful moisture management. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen foliage and flowering structure.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is a challenging species to cultivate outside its natural range.
