Macrolobium colombianum var. bicuspidum

Macrolobium colombianum var. bicuspidum, commonly known as the Colombian macrolobium, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod fruits and nitrogen-fixing ability. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white and purple. Its seeds are enclosed in hairy, elongated pods, which contribute to its distinctive botanical appearance.

This plant is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
