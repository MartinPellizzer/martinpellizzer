Masdevallia picturata subsp. minor

Masdevallia picturata subsp.

minor, commonly known as the miniature painted masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America. It features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces intricate, colorful flowers with a unique pattern of purple and pink markings.

The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or among mossy substrates. Its compact size and vibrant blooms make it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species.
