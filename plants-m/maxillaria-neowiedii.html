Maxillaria neowiedii

Maxillaria neowiedii, commonly known as the Neowed's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments with well-draining soil.

Maxillaria neowiedii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
