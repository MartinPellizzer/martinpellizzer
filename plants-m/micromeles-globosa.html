Micromeles globosa

Micromeles globosa, commonly known as the globular thistle, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to the Mediterranean region and is characterized by its spherical, spiny fruiting structures. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are typically covered in fine hairs and have a slightly succulent texture.

Micromeles globosa is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its ornamental appearance.
