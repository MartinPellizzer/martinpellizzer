Myosotis oblongata

Myosotis oblongata, commonly known as the heart's-ease or water forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as woodlands and stream sides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Myosotis oblongata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a wildflower in natural habitats.
