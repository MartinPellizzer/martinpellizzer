Mimosa heringeri

Mimosa heringeri, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Mimosa heringeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
