Melia bombolo

Melia bombolo, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, round fruits that turn from green to yellow as they mature. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a waxy texture.
