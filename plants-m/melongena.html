Melongena

Melongena, commonly known as the eggplant, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy fruit that can range in color from purple to white or yellow. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves and produces small, white flowers that develop into the edible fruit. The fruit is typically harvested when it is still immature, before the skin hardens.

Botanically, melongena is valued for its nutritional content and versatility in culinary applications.
