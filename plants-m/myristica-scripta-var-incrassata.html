Myristica scripta var. incrassata

Myristica scripta var. incrassata, commonly known as the thick-leafed nutmeg.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indonesian archipelago, characterized by its dense, aromatic leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces hard, woody fruits that contain the aromatic seeds used in spice production. Its botanical features include smooth, glossy bark and elliptical leaves with a leathery texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and economic significance.
