Muhlenbergia trifida

Muhlenbergia trifida, commonly known as the pink muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish plumes that appear in late summer, giving it a striking visual appearance. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as prairies and disturbed soils. Its slender stems and small, grass-like leaves are adapted to thrive in dry conditions.

Muhlenbergia trifida is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
