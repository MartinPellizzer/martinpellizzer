Magnolia obovata var. soulangeana

Magnolia obovata var. soulangeana, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or saucer magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pink to white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a glossy, dark green upper surface. The plant produces small, round, green fruits that contain seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
