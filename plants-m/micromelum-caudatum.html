Micromelum caudatum

Micromelum caudatum, commonly known as the silky tamarind, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and trifoliate leaves with hairy undersides. Its small, fragrant flowers are followed by distinctive, fleshy, orange-red fruits that contain several seeds.
