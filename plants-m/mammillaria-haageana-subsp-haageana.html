Mammillaria haageana subsp. haageana

Mammillaria haageana subsp.

haageana, commonly known as the Mexican night bloomer, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem covered in small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which open at night. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and blooming habit, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and xeriscaping.
