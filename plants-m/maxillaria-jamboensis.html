Maxillaria jamboensis

Maxillaria jamboensis, commonly known as the Jambo orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces compact, pseudobulbous stems and has linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are showy, with colorful petals and sepals that often display a striking contrast.

Maxillaria jamboensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
