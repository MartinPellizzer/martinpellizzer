Myosotis collina subsp. gracillima

Myosotis collina subsp.

gracillima, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to the mountainous regions of South America. It features slender, upright stems with small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and wildflower displays.
