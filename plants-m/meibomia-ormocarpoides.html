Meibomia ormocarpoides

Meibomia ormocarpoides, commonly known as the African starleaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in terminal panicles, while the fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

The plant is often found in moist, wooded areas and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
