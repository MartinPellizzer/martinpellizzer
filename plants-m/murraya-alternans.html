Murraya alternans

Murraya alternans, commonly known as the variegated murraya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that often display a striking variegation of green and cream or white. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.

Murraya alternans is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
