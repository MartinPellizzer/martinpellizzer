Maxillaria bradeorum

Maxillaria bradeorum, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, typically blooming in the summer. Maxillaria bradeorum is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

Its growth habit and floral display make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
