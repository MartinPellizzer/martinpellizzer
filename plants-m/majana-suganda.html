Majana suganda

Majana suganda, commonly known as the sweet-smelling orchid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy blossoms. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are broad and glossy, while its flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Majana suganda is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its cultural significance in traditional practices.
