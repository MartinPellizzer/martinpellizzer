Masdevallia delhierroi

Masdevallia delhierroi, commonly known as the Delhierroi orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with delicate, lance-shaped leaves and intricate, spurred flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant prefers cool, humid environments and is often found growing in shaded forest understories. Its floral structure is characteristic of the Masdevallia genus, featuring a unique, elongated column and a showy, hooded lip.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
