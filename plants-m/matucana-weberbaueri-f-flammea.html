Matucana weberbaueri f. flammea

Matucana weberbaueri f. flammea, commonly known as the flame torch cactus, is a striking epiphytic cactus native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, branching stems that are often covered in a reddish or orange hue, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange, often blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice for indoor and outdoor cultivation.
