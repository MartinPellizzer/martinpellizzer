Myrica chimanimaniana

Myrica chimanimaniana, commonly known as the African myrica, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and rocky areas. The plant has small, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female flowers occurring on separate plants.

The fruit is a small, woody drupe that ripens to a reddish color, providing food for various wildlife species.
