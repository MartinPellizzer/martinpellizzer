Magnolia compressa var. formosana

Magnolia compressa var. formosana, commonly known as the Formosan magnolia, is a species native to Taiwan.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are broad, elliptical, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The Formosan magnolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blossoms.
