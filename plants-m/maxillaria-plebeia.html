Maxillaria plebeia

Maxillaria plebeia, commonly known as the common maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, characterized by its pseudobulbs and fleshy roots. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green, often with a distinctive red or orange lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria plebeia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
