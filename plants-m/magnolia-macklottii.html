Magnolia macklottii

Magnolia macklottii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broad-leaf magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has broad, heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall in its natural habitat.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
