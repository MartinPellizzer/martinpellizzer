Marsdenia rotundifolia

Marsdenia rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, thick stems and opposite, rounded leaves. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and unique floral display.
