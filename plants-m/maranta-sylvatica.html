Maranta sylvatica

Maranta sylvatica, commonly known as the forest maranta, is a species of flowering plant in the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, ornamental leaves that often display vibrant patterns and colors. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Maranta sylvatica is appreciated for its aesthetic value and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
