Medicago plagiospira

Medicago plagiospira, commonly known as the spiral medic.

It is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae, characterized by its distinctive spiral-arranged leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, followed by pod-like fruits containing several seeds. It is native to parts of Europe and Asia and is often found in grasslands and disturbed soils.

Medicago plagiospira is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
