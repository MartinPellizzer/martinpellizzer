Maximowiczia insularis

Maximowiczia insularis, commonly known as the island grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of the Pacific, including parts of Southeast Asia and the Philippines. This plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to saline and sandy soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
