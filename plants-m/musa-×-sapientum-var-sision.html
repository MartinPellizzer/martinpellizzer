Musa × sapientum var. sision

Musa × sapientum var. sision, commonly known as the red banana, is a cultivar of the banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elongated, red-purple fruit that turns yellow when ripe. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of fruit known as hands. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The fruit is often used in both culinary and ornamental applications due to its vibrant color and unique flavor.
