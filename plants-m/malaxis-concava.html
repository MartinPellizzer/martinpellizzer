Malaxis concava

Malaxis concava, commonly known as the narrow-leaf helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-like flower structure and has a slender, often branching stem. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas.

Malaxis concava is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
