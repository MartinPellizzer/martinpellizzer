Muhlenbergia parviglumis

Muhlenbergia parviglumis, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often white or pale purple, adding ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Muhlenbergia parviglumis is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.
