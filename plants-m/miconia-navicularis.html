Miconia navicularis

Miconia navicularis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often overlooked due to their modest appearance. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia navicularis is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
