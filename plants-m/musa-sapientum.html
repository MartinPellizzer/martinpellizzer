Musa sapientum

Musa sapientum, commonly known as the banana plant, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, sword-shaped leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible berries called bananas, which are rich in potassium and other nutrients. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Musa sapientum is cultivated worldwide for both its fruit and ornamental value.
