Microchilus chicalensis

Microchilus chicalensis, commonly known as the Chicalense orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Mexico.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, intricate flowers with vibrant colors. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in montane forests. Its botanical features include narrow leaves and a compact growth habit, which help it thrive in its specialized habitat.
