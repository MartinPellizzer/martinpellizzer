Microlicia glossophylla

Microlicia glossophylla, commonly known as the velvetleaf or velvet tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage that gives it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by woody, capsule-like fruits. Microlicia glossophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for hedges and ornamental landscaping in warm climates.
