Masdevallia ignea var. rubescens

Masdevallia ignea var. rubescens, commonly known as the fiery masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

This variant is characterized by its vivid red and orange flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green, velvety leaves. It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is noted for its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant thrives in cool, high-humidity environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for specialized orchid growers.

Its unique coloration and elegant form make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
