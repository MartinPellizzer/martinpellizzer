Medicago lupulina f. stipularis

Medicago lupulina f. stipularis, commonly known as the stipular milkvetch, is a variant of the common milkvetch.

It is a perennial legume native to parts of Asia and the Middle East. The plant is characterized by its slender stems and small, yellow flowers that appear in racemes. Its leaves are pinnately compound with stipules, giving it its specific epithet.

It is often found in dry, rocky habitats and is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability.
