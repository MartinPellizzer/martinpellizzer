Mussaenda glabra

Mussaenda glabra, commonly known as the smooth mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of showy, tubular yellow flowers that are highly attractive to pollinators. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Mussaenda glabra is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to grow in partial shade.
