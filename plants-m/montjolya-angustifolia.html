Montjolya angustifolia

Montjolya angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf montjolya, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. Native to tropical regions of Africa, it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant in horticulture and botanical gardens.
