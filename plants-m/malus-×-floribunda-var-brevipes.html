Malus × floribunda var. brevipes

Malus × floribunda var. brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf flowering crabapple, is a compact, ornamental tree or shrub native to China.

It features showy clusters of white or pink flowers in early spring, which are followed by small, edible fruits. The plant has a rounded form with dense, dark green foliage that provides year-round visual interest. It is well-suited for small gardens and can thrive in a variety of soil types with moderate watering.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and resistance to many common diseases.
