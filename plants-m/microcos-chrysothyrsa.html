Microcos chrysothyrsa

Microcos chrysothyrsa, commonly known as the golden thorn, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its woody stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Microcos chrysothyrsa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
