Micromelum minutum var. glabrescens

Micromelum minutum var. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-leaved mallow, is a small shrub native to tropical regions of Australia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, though the variety glabrescens has a smoother texture. The plant produces clusters of white or pale pink flowers, which bloom during the warmer months. It is often found in sandy or coastal soils and is adapted to withstand saline conditions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in suitable climates.
