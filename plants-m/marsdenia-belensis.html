Marsdenia belensis

Marsdenia belensis, commonly known as the Bornean Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, with slender stems and opposite, ovate leaves. Its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow, with a distinctive corona structure.

Marsdenia belensis is often found in lowland rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments.
