Magnolia elliptilimba

Magnolia elliptilimba, commonly known as the elliptic magnolia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are elliptical in shape, glossy, and have a smooth texture. The plant prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and landscapes.
