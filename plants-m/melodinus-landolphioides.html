Melodinus landolphioides

Melodinus landolphioides, commonly known as the yellow-eyed gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, pumpkins, and gourds. The plant produces large, yellow flowers that are followed by elongated, green fruits. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while the stems are hairy and woody.

The fruit is edible when mature and is used in local cuisines for its mild flavor and nutritional value.
