Maranta insignis

Maranta insignis, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that fold upwards at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves often display intricate patterns in shades of red, pink, and green.

Maranta insignis thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.
