Mesembryanthemum wettsteinii

Mesembryanthemum wettsteinii, commonly known as the Wettstein's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white powder, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink and white, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its adaptations to arid environments make it a resilient and attractive addition to xeriscapes.
