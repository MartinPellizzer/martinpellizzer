Mentzelia cordata

Mentzelia cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The showy yellow flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
