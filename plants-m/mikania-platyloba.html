Mikania platyloba

Mikania platyloba, commonly known as the broadleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color, which bloom in the morning. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, often with a hairy texture.

Mikania platyloba is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges, where it can spread aggressively.
