Mammillaria sphacelata subsp. sphacelata

Mammillaria sphacelata subsp.

sphacelata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and numerous radial spines.

The plant typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in well-drained soils. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
