Mikania ternifolia

Mikania ternifolia, commonly known as creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Mikania ternifolia is considered an invasive species in many regions due to its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively.
