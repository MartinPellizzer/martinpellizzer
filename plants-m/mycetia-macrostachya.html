Mycetia macrostachya

Mycetia macrostachya, commonly known as the giant mycetia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
