Manihot stricta

Manihot stricta, commonly known as the arrowleaf tree or arrowleaf manioc, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant produces small greenish-yellow flowers and is known for its latex, which can be toxic.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.
