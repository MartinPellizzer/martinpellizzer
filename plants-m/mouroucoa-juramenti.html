Mouroucoa juramenti

Mouroucoa juramenti, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and orange flowers that resemble flames, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are a deep green and often covered in a waxy cuticle to retain moisture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Mouroucoa juramenti is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice for indoor and greenhouse displays.
