Macaranga villosula, commonly known as the hairy macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves that are often covered with fine, white trichomes. Macaranga villosula is also known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
