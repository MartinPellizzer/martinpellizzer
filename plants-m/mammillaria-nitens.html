Mammillaria nitens

Mammillaria nitens, commonly known as the nitens hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.

Mammillaria nitens requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
