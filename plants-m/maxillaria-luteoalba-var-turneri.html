Maxillaria luteoalba var. turneri

Maxillaria luteoalba var. turneri, commonly known as the Yellow White Orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, often found in humid tropical environments. Its leaves are usually broad and grass-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its delicate floral display.
