Mandevilla guanabarica

Mandevilla guanabarica, commonly known as the Guanabara mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to the Caribbean and South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant has opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla guanabarica is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and vigorous growth habit.
