Melinis muenzneri

Melinis muenzneri, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive silver-gray foliage, which results from dense pubescence on the leaf blades. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its inflorescence consists of a loose panicle with small, spikelet-like flowers.

Melinis muenzneri is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
