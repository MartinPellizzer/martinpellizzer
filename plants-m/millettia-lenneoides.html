Millettia lenneoides

Millettia lenneoides, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The tree is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in summer. Its fruit is a legume pod that splits open to release its seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
