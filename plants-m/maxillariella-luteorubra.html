Maxillariella luteorubra

Maxillariella luteorubra, commonly known as the orange-stemmed orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually yellow to orange in color, with a distinctive red or orange lip.

It is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouse settings.
