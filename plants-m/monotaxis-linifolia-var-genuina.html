Monotaxis linifolia var. genuina

Monotaxis linifolia var. genuina, commonly known as the narrow-leaved monotaxis, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
