Marattia stokesii

Marattia stokesii, commonly known as Stokes' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Marattiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, pinnately compound fronds. The plant has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 10 meters in suitable conditions. Its fronds are typically dark green and can grow to lengths of over two meters.

Marattia stokesii thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
