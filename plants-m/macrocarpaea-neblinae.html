Macrocarpaea neblinae

Macrocarpaea neblinae, commonly known as the cloud forest geranium, is a flowering plant native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and has elliptical leaves with a slightly hairy surface.

Its botanical features make it well adapted to the humid, nutrient-poor conditions of its natural habitat.
