Medicago obscura f. sinistrorsa

Medicago obscura f. sinistrorsa, commonly known as the left-handed sickle vetch, is a rare and specialized plant species characterized by its unique morphological features.

The plant is distinguished by its twisted, helical stems and small, clustered leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss.
