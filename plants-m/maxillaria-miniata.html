Maxillaria miniata

Maxillaria miniata, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the dwarf orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. Its flowers are borne on terminal inflorescences and bloom during the spring and summer months.

Maxillaria miniata is appreciated for its compact size and ornamental value in horticulture.
