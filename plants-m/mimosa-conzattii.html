Mimosa conzattii

Mimosa conzattii, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Mimosa conzattii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.
