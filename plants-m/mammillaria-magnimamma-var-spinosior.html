Mammillaria magnimamma var. spinosior

Mammillaria magnimamma var. spinosior, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with numerous cylindrical ribs that are adorned with prominent, spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
