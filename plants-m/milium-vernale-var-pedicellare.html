Milium vernale var. pedicellare

Milium vernale var. pedicellare, commonly known as the early millet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in open, disturbed areas such as fields and roadsides. The plant produces small, greenish-white spikelets that are clustered in dense, panicle-like inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a prominent midrib.

This variety is notable for its early flowering habit, often blooming in late spring to early summer.
