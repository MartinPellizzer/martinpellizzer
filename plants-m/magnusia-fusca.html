Magnusia fusca

Magnusia fusca, commonly known as the dark magnolia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, showy, dark purple to almost black flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, and the plant can grow as a small tree or shrub.

Magnusia fusca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
