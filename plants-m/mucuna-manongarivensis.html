Mucuna manongarivensis

Mucuna manongarivensis, commonly known as the Madagascar velvet bean, is a climbing leguminous plant native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence and trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by elongated, cylindrical pods containing flat, kidney-shaped seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.

Mucuna manongarivensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in local medicine.
