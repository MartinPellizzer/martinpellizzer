Mammillaria flavovirens f. cristata

Mammillaria flavovirens f. cristata, commonly known as the cristate yellow-green mammillaria, is a unique cactus species characterized by its distinctive crest-like growth form.

It originates from the arid regions of Mexico, where it thrives in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant features bright yellow to greenish-white tubercles covered in fine, white bristles. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the central crest.

This ornamental cactus is prized for its compact size and unusual appearance, making it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
