Melhania acuminata var. acuminata

Melhania acuminata var. acuminata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a perennial herb with a short, thick, hairy stem and broad, velvety leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white, typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Melhania acuminata var. acuminata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
