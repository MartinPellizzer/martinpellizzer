Miconia Stephanotricha

Miconia stephanotricha is a striking tropical plant known for its large, velvety leaves and showy flowers. Native to the rainforests of South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates with abundant sunlight. This species belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is often found in lowland and montane regions. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. However, it also poses ecological challenges due to its invasive potential in certain environments.
