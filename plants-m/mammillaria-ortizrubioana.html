Mammillaria ortizrubioana

Mammillaria ortizrubioana, commonly known as the Ortizrubioana hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and features a pale green stem. It produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are often surrounded by a ring of bristles.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
