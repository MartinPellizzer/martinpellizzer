Masdevallia gigas

Masdevallia gigas, commonly known as the giant masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in length, featuring vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are thick and segmented, storing water and nutrients, which helps it survive in its natural habitat.

Masdevallia gigas is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
