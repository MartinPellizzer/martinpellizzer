Mimosa sirissa

Mimosa sirissa, commonly known as the Indian silk tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has compound leaves that are bipinnate, giving it a feathery appearance. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in spherical inflorescences.

The bark and leaves have been traditionally used in herbal medicine for their purported health benefits.
