Masdevallia bellissima

Masdevallia bellissima, commonly known as the beautiful masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in a spiral pattern along the stem. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually strap-like and arranged in a rosette, providing structural support to the flower spike.

Masdevallia bellissima is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
