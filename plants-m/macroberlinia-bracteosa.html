Macroberlinia bracteosa

Macroberlinia bracteosa, commonly known as the African bractean.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its woody structure and compound leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds.

Macroberlinia bracteosa is often found in open woodlands and savanna habitats.
