Monteverdia peruana

Monteverdia peruana, commonly known as the Peruvian monteverdia, is a species native to the cloud forests of Peru.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae, which includes laurels and avocados. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface.

Monteverdia peruana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded gardens.
