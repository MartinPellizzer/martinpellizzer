Masdevallia pachyura

Masdevallia pachyura, commonly known as the thick-leafed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its ability to thrive in cool, humid environments. The plant produces striking, lance-shaped leaves that are thick and leathery, adapted to conserve moisture. Its flowers are typically small, with intricate patterns and a range of colors from white to pink and purple.

Masdevallia pachyura is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
