Maxillaria steelei

Maxillaria steelei, commonly known as the Steel's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of showy, violet to purple flowers with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. Maxillaria steelei thrives in shaded, moist environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Its unique floral structure and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
