Monosepalum dischorense

Monosepalum dischorense, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom from the center of the plant. The plant has a single sepal, which is a distinctive botanical feature, and its leaves are typically velvety and dark green. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Monosepalum dischorense is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
