Maurandya erubescens

Maurandya erubescens, commonly known as the scarlet trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for hanging baskets or trellises. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period and ability to attract pollinators.
