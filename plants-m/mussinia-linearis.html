Mussinia linearis

Mussinia linearis, commonly known as the slender moss campion, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of about 10 to 20 centimeters. The plant features slender, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and it produces small, pink or purple flowers clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine or subalpine habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in rock gardens.
