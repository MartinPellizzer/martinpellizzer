Microstylis hieronymi

Microstylis hieronymi, commonly known as the small-styled hieronymi sedge, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its grass-like foliage and compact growth habit. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its inflorescence consists of small, clustered spikelets that are often inconspicuous.

Microstylis hieronymi is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and is valued for its ornamental appeal in wetland gardens.
