Manihot heterophylla

Manihot heterophylla, commonly known as the variable arrowleaf, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from arrow-shaped to ovate. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, often reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its small, greenish-yellow flowers are clustered in cyathia, a feature typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its unique foliage.
