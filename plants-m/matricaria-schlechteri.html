Matricaria schlechteri

Matricaria schlechteri, commonly known as the Schlechter's chamomile, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant produces small, yellow flowers surrounded by white, petal-like structures called phyllaries. It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Matricaria schlechteri is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine for its calming effects.
