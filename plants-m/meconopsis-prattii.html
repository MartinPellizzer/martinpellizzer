Meconopsis prattii

Meconopsis prattii, commonly known as the Himalayan blue poppy, is a striking flowering plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vivid blue to violet petals, which are often speckled with contrasting patterns. The plant produces showy, cup-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer, adding a splash of color to its alpine habitat. Meconopsis prattii thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is a sought-after species among botanists and gardeners.
