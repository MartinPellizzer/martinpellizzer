Melastoma microlepidotum

Melastoma microlepidotum, commonly known as the small-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
