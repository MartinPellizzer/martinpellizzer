Myosotis barrelieri

Myosotis barrelieri, commonly known as the Barrelier's forget-me-not, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of up to 20 centimeters. The plant features small, delicate blue or purple flowers with five petals, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in a fine, hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest floors or along stream banks.
