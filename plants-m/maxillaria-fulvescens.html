Maxillaria fulvescens

Maxillaria fulvescens, commonly known as the golden maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with purple or brown markings, and they bloom in late summer or early autumn.

Maxillaria fulvescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
