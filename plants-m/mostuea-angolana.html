Mostuea angolana

Mostuea angolana, commonly known as the Angola sword fern, is a tropical plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related plants. The plant features tall, slender stems with long, narrow leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense mats in moist, shaded environments.

The botanical characteristics of Mostuea angolana make it an important species in both ecological and medicinal contexts.
