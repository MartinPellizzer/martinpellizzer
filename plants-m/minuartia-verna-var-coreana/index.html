Minuartia Verna Var. Coreana

Minuartia verna var. coreana is a delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. It thrives in rocky and gravelly soils, often found in alpine and subalpine environments. This variety is known for its small, star-shaped white or pale blue flowers that bloom in early spring. Its compact growth habit and resilience make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays. Despite its beauty, it remains a relatively obscure species within the broader Minuartia genus.
