Mammillaria plaschnickii var. straminea

Mammillaria plaschnickii var. straminea, commonly known as the Straminea mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with prominent, white, woolly tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
