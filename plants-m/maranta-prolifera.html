Maranta prolifera

Maranta prolifera, commonly known as the creeping arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit and produces striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often used in indoor gardening.

Its leaves are also valued for their aesthetic appeal in landscaping and as ornamental plants.
