Manettia capitata

Manettia capitata, commonly known as the white-flowered manettia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical inflorescence composed of numerous small, white flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Manettia capitata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
