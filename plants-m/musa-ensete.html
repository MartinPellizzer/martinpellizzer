Musa ensete

Musa ensete, commonly known as the Ethiopian banana, is a large herbaceous plant native to Ethiopia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stem and large, paddle-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fruit that are typically consumed when green and unripe. Its cultivation is closely tied to traditional agricultural practices in the region.

Musa ensete plays a vital role in the local ecosystem and serves as a staple food source for many communities.
