Millettia koordersii

Millettia koordersii, commonly known as the Koorders' legume, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and clustered flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are often followed by elongated, bean-like pods. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
