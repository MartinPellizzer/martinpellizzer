Microcos involucrata

Microcos involucrata, commonly known as the clustered microcos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
