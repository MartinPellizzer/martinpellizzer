Micropholis urbanii

Micropholis urbanii, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a small shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, yellow flowers and small, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that are typically consumed by birds and small mammals. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in sandy or rocky soils.

Micropholis urbanii is known for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
