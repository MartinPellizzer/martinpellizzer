Myoporum rapense subsp. rapense

Myoporum rapense subsp.

rapense, commonly known as New Zealand myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its woody stems and dense, evergreen foliage.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly rough texture.

It is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and erosion control capabilities.
