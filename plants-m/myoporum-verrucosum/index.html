Myoporum Verrucosum

Myoporum verrucosum is a unique shrub native to Australia's arid regions. It is known for its striking silver-green foliage and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions. This plant is often used in landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal. Its small, fragrant flowers add a touch of beauty to the landscape. Despite its resilience, it requires careful management to prevent it from becoming invasive in non-native environments.
