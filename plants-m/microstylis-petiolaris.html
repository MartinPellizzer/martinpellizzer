Microstylis petiolaris

Microstylis petiolaris, commonly known as the slender-stemmed microstylis, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically wind-pollinated. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
