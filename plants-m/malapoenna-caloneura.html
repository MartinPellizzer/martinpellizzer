Malapoenna caloneura

Malapoenna caloneura, commonly known as the narrow-winged malapoenna, is a unique plant species native to specific regions of South Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive, narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and requires a sunny to partially shaded environment. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists studying plant adaptation and diversity.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
