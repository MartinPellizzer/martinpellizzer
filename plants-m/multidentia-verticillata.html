Multidentia verticillata

Multidentia verticillata, commonly known as the spiral-leaved plant, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its whorled arrangement of leaves, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are often deeply lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or streamside habitats.
