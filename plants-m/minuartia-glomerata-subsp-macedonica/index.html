Minuartia Glomerata Subsp. Macedonica

Minuartia glomerata subsp. macedonica is a rare and delicate plant native to the Balkans, particularly found in the mountainous regions of Macedonia. This subspecies belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its colorful and often hardy flowering plants. It thrives in rocky and limestone soils, often growing in open, sunny habitats. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pink or white, adding a subtle beauty to its natural environment. Due to habitat loss and limited distribution, it is considered a vulnerable species requiring conservation efforts.
