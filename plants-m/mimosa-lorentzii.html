Mimosa lorentzii

Mimosa lorentzii, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or pea family. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its soft, silvery foliage.

Mimosa lorentzii is also noted for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to drought conditions.
