Mammillaria pfeifferi var. altissima

Mammillaria pfeifferi var. altissima, commonly known as the tall mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can reach heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of white to pale pink flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a dense covering of fine, white hairs.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
