Masdevallia calura

Masdevallia calura, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that often display shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often on rock surfaces or in forest understories. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms during the warmer months.

Masdevallia calura is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
