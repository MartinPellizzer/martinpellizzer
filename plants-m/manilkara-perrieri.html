Manilkara perrieri

Manilkara perrieri, commonly known as the Perrier's sycamore, is a tropical tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, compound leaves and smooth, gray bark. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its latex sap is used in traditional medicine and has economic significance in local communities.
