Meadia Hendersonii Var. Cruciata

Meadia hendersonii var. cruciata is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. Known for its striking violet-blue flowers and unique foliage, it captivates botanists and plant enthusiasts alike. This variant of Meadia hendersonii is particularly notable for its distinct cruciform leaf shape, which sets it apart from other members of the genus. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a threatened species. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this beautiful and ecologically important plant.
