Microsorum alatum

Microsorum alatum, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Marbled fern, is a tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features elegant, dark green, lance-shaped leaves with distinctive marbled patterns of lighter green and silver. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, moist soil. It is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its attractive foliage and low maintenance requirements.

Microsorum alatum is also valued for its air-purifying properties and ability to thrive in indoor conditions.
