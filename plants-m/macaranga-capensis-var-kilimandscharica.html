Macaranga capensis var. kilimandscharica

Macaranga capensis var. kilimandscharica, commonly known as the Kilimanjaro macaranga, is a species native to eastern Africa.

It is a fast-growing shrub or small tree that thrives in moist, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves with a smooth, waxy surface. It produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by elliptical, woody fruits.

This species is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
