Myrtus stahlii

Myrtus stahlii, commonly known as the Stahli's myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its narrow, leathery leaves and small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruits are small, dark berries that contain aromatic seeds.

Myrtus stahlii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its fragrant foliage.
