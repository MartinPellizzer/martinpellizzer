Maxillariella stenophylla

Maxillariella stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered growth habit. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a fragrant scent, typically blooming during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in shaded, humid environments.

Maxillariella stenophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
