Magnolia × highdownensis

Magnolia × highdownensis, commonly known as the Highdown magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous shrub or small tree native to the United Kingdom.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stems. Its flowers are typically pink to white, with a distinctive mottled pattern.

Magnolia × highdownensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blooms and attractive foliage.
