Meteoromyrtus

Meteoromyrtus, commonly known as the honeycomb plant, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive, honeycomb-like patterns on its leaves and bark. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in coastal or humid environments. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, berry-like fruits.

Meteoromyrtus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
