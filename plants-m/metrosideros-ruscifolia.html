Metrosideros ruscifolia

Metrosideros ruscifolia, commonly known as the New Zealand Christmas tree, is a small evergreen shrub or tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and bright red or orange flowers that bloom in late summer to early autumn.
