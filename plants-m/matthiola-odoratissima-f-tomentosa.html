Matthiola odoratissima f. tomentosa

Matthiola odoratissima f. tomentosa, commonly known as the woolly stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and stems, which give it a distinctive, fluffy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its visual appeal and mild, spicy scent.
