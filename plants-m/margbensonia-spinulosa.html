Margbensonia spinulosa

Margbensonia spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny margbensia, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a compact, rounded growth habit with thick, fleshy leaves that are covered in fine, spiny projections. The plant produces vibrant yellow to orange flowers during the summer months, adding a striking visual element to its appearance. Its leaves are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in low-rainfall environments.

Margbensonia spinulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
