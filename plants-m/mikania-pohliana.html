Mikania pohliana

Mikania pohliana, commonly known as the creeping Mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Mikania pohliana is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
