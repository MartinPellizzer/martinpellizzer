Mimusops sylvestris

Mimusops sylvestris, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The tree produces small, round fruits that are encased in a thick, waxy coating. It is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in furniture and musical instruments.
