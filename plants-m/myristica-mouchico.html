Myristica mouchico

Myristica mouchico, commonly known as the fragrant nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Moluccas (Spice Islands) in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces a unique fruit that contains a large, hard-shelled seed, which is commercially valuable for its aromatic oil and spice. The tree's leaves are elliptical and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Myristica mouchico is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its economic importance in the spice trade.
