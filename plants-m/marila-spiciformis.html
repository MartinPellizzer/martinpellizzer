Marila spiciformis

Marila spiciformis, commonly known as the spiciform marila, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, lance-shaped leaves arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. Its flowering stems are often adorned with spines, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Marila spiciformis is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or gravelly habitats.
