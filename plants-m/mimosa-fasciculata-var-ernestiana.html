Mimosa fasciculata var. ernestiana

Mimosa fasciculata var. ernestiana, commonly known as the Ernestian mimosa, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding shut when disturbed. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in rounded clusters, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and adaptability contribute to its ornamental value in horticulture.
