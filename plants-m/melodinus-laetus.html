Melodinus laetus

Melodinus laetus, commonly known as the happy melody vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a slightly pubescent surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. These flowers are followed by bright orange or red berries that contain the seeds.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
