Martinella

Martinella, commonly known as the white mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the genus *Garcinia* in the family Clusiaceae. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. Its fruit, which is typically small and round, has a thick, purple rind and a white, segmented pulp.
