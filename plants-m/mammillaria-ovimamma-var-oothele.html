Mammillaria ovimamma var. oothele

Mammillaria ovimamma var. oothele, commonly known as the Woolly Mammillaria, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, white to grayish-white woolly spines that give it a soft, fluffy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, red fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
