Medicosma articulata

Medicosma articulata, commonly known as the jointed medics, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that grows up to 1 meter tall, characterized by its branching stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in spring. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive jointed stems and medicinal uses.

Medicosoma articulata is often found in grasslands and along roadsides, thriving in well-drained soils.
