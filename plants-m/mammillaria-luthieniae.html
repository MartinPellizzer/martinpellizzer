Mammillaria luthieniae

Mammillaria luthieniae, commonly known as the Luthien's hedgehog cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly bristles that help protect it from intense sunlight and dehydration. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
