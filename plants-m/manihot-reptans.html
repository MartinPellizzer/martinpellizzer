Manihot reptans

Manihot reptans, commonly known as creeping arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that features tubers underground, which are used as a starchy food source. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a waxy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value comes from its attractive foliage, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.
