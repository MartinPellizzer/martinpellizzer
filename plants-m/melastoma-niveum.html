Melastoma niveum

Melastoma niveum, commonly known as the white melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture.

The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive appearance.
