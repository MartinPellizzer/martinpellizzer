Medicago monantha subsp. noeana

Medicago monantha subsp.

noeana, commonly known as the Narrowleaf Medick, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia. It typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

The plant features slender, upright stems with trifoliate leaves composed of three oval to lance-shaped leaflets. Its small, yellow flowers are arranged in dense, globular clusters, and it produces small, hairy pods that contain several seeds.

It is valued in traditional herbal medicine for its potential health benefits.
