Myostemma pratensis

Myostemma pratensis, commonly known as the meadow myostemma, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Myostemmataceae and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in meadows and grasslands. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green or white, with a subtle fragrance.

Myostemma pratensis is of interest to botanists due to its unique ecological niche and limited distribution.
