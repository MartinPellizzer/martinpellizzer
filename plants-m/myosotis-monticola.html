Myosotis monticola

Myosotis monticola, commonly known as the mountain forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue-violet flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high elevations, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Myosotis monticola has lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered inflorescences.

It is often found in mountainous habitats and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological significance in alpine ecosystems.
