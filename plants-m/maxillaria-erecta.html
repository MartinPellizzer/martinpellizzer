Maxillaria erecta

Maxillaria erecta, commonly known as the upright maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and yellow. The plant typically has dark green, strap-shaped leaves and thrives in humid, well-drained environments. It blooms during the summer months and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm climates.

Maxillaria erecta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown by orchid enthusiasts.
