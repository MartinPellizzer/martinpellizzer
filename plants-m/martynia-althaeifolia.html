Martynia althaeifolia

Martynia althaeifolia, commonly known as the purple morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant purple or violet flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in warm, sunny environments and is often grown as an ornamental plant.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
