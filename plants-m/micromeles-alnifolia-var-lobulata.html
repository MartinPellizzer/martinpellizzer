Micromeles alnifolia var. lobulata

Micromeles alnifolia var. lobulata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed alder.

It is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region, characterized by its small, elliptic leaves with a pointed tip. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters, followed by winged fruits. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological studies and horticultural use.
