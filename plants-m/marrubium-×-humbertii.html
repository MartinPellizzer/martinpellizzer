Marrubium × humbertii

Marrubium × humbertii, commonly known as the hybrid horehound, is a cross between Marrubium vulgare and Marrubium officinale.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, calcareous soils. The plant features gray-green, hairy leaves and produces small, blue or purple flowers in clusters. It is often found in meadows, woodland edges, and rocky areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
