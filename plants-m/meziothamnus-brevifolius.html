Meziothamnus brevifolius

Meziothamnus brevifolius, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mezee, is a small shrub native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters at the tips of the branches. It thrives in well-drained, sandy soils and is adapted to the Mediterranean climate of its native region.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to succulent gardens.
