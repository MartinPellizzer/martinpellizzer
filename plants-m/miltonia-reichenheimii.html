Miltonia reichenheimii

Miltonia reichenheimii, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. The plant has strap-like leaves that are dark green and velvety to the touch. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Miltonia reichenheimii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
