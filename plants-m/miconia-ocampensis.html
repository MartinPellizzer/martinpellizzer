Miconia ocampensis

Miconia ocampensis, commonly known as Ocampo's miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, showy leaves and clusters of small, white to pink flowers. Miconia ocampensis is noted for its ecological importance in providing habitat and food for various tropical fauna.
