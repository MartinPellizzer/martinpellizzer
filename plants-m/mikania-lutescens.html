Mikania lutescens

Mikania lutescens, commonly known as yellow crownbeard, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in disturbed or open habitats. Its leaves are opposite, lance-shaped, and covered with fine hairs, while its flowers form dense clusters at the ends of the stems.

Mikania lutescens is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.
