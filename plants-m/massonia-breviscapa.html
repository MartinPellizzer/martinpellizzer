Massonia breviscapa

Massonia breviscapa, commonly known as the short-stemmed massonia, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features dense, fleshy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it store water. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or white, often during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
