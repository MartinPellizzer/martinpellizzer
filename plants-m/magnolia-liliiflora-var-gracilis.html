Magnolia liliiflora var. gracilis

Magnolia liliiflora var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender magnolia, is a compact, deciduous shrub native to eastern China.

It features lance-shaped, dark green leaves that turn yellow in autumn. The plant produces showy, white, fragrant flowers with numerous stamens, typical of the Magnoliaceae family. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Its ornamental value lies in its elegant blooms and graceful, upright growth habit.
