Machilus bracteatus

Machilus bracteatus, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, including China, Japan, and Korea. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically narrow and glossy. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries.
