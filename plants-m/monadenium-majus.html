Monadenium majus

Monadenium majus, commonly known as the giant African violet, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features thick, fleshy stems and leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its compact size makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
