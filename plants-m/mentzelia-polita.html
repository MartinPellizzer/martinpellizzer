Mentzelia polita

Mentzelia polita, commonly known as the smooth mentzelia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish to greenish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Mentzelia polita is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments.
