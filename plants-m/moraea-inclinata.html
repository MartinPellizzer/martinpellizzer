Moraea inclinata

Moraea inclinata, commonly known as the leaning moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
