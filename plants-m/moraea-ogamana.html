Moraea ogamana

Moraea ogamana, commonly known as the Orange River Moraea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is often found in the Western and Eastern Cape provinces of South Africa.
