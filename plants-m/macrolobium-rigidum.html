Macrolobium rigidum

Macrolobium rigidum, commonly known as the rigid macrolobium, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its woody, shrub-like structure with thick, leathery leaves that are often arranged in pairs. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by wind or animals.

Macrolobium rigidum is native to tropical regions of South America and is often found in dry, open habitats.
