Meringium hosei

Meringium hosei, commonly known as the Japanese meringium, is a flowering plant native to Japan.

It belongs to the family Lamiaceae, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slight pubescence.

Meringium hosei is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil and full sun.
