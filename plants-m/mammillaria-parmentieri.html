Mammillaria parmentieri

Mammillaria parmentieri, commonly known as the Parmentier's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and typically produces yellow flowers. The plant has a dense covering of fine spines and is well adapted to arid environments. It thrives in full sun and requires minimal water, making it suitable for rock gardens or desert landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
