Memecylon angustifolium

Memecylon angustifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
