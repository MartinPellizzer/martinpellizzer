Mitreola petiolatoides

Mitreola petiolatoides, commonly known as the small-leaved mitreola, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often oval to elliptic in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family. Its fruits are capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Mitreola petiolatoides is typically found in moist, shaded environments in tropical and subtropical regions.
