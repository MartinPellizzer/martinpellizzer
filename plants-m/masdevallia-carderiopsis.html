Masdevallia carderiopsis

Masdevallia carderiopsis, commonly known as the cloud forest orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, spurred orchid flowers in shades of pink and purple, typically blooming during the cooler months. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and elegant appearance make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
