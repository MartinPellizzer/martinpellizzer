Maeviella

Maeviella, commonly known as the golden groundsel, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. Maeviella is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
