Matucana haynii var. pectinifera

Matucana haynii var. pectinifera, commonly known as the golden matucana, is a compact, slow-growing bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features broad, strap-like leaves that are often tinged with yellow or gold, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped inflorescences that range in color from red to orange, adding visual interest. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
