Myoporum cordifolium

Myoporum cordifolium, commonly known as the heartleaf myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant is evergreen, with a compact shrub form that makes it suitable for landscaping. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade.

Myoporum cordifolium is also valued for its ability to attract native pollinators.
