Mandevilla semirii

Mandevilla semirii, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Mandevilla semirii thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated as a decorative vine in gardens and containers.
