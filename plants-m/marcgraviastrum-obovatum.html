Marcgraviastrum obovatum

Marcgraviastrum obovatum, commonly known as the silver leaf tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or whitish underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by ornamental, red or orange berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in warm climates.
