Musa × emasculata var. kimbende

Musa × emasculata var. kimbende, commonly known as the Kimbende banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces clusters of fruit that are typically smaller and more compact compared to other banana varieties. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its edible fruit. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The fruit has a sweet, creamy texture and is often used in both culinary and commercial applications.
