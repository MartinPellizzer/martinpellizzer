Misanteca vernicosa

Misanteca vernicosa, commonly known as the glossy leaf vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, waxy leaves that give it a shiny appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Misanteca vernicosa thrives in humid, warm environments and is often found in rainforests.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
