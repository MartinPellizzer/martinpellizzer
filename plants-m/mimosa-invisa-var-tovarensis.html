Mimosa invisa var. tovarensis

Mimosa invisa var. tovarensis, commonly known as the Tovarensis Mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features bipinnate leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait known as thigmonastic response. It produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
