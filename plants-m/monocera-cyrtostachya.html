Monocera cyrtostachya

Monocera cyrtostachya, commonly known as the red-flowered sturtshrub, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant produces small, red or pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which are often covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and have a leathery texture, adapted to reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Monocera cyrtostachya is also known for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
