Mikania gracillima

Mikania gracillima, commonly known as slender Mikania, is a species of flowering plant in the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas, particularly in areas with humid climates. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, opposite leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically yellow and are arranged in clusters, producing small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Mikania gracillima is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges, where it can spread rapidly due to its aggressive growth habit.
