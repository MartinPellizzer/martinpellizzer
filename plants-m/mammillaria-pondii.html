Mammillaria pondii

Mammillaria pondii, commonly known as the Mexican fairy duster, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces clusters of small, bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
