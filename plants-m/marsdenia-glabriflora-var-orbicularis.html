Marsdenia glabriflora var. orbicularis

Marsdenia glabriflora var. orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive orbicular (rounded) leaves, which are typically thick and leathery. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, often in shades of white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
