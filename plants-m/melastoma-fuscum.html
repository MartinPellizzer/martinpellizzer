Melastoma fuscum

Melastoma fuscum, commonly known as the dark melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Melastoma fuscum is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and mountainous regions.
