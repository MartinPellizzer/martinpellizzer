Melastoma lineare

Melastoma lineare, commonly known as the linear melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that are typically pink or red. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma lineare is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
