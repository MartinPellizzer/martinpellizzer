Malouetia pubescens

Malouetia pubescens, commonly known as the hairy malouetia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its pubescent (hairy) stems and leaves. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.
