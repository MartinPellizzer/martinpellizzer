Miconia willdenowii

Miconia willdenowii, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white flowers. The plant produces bright yellow-orange fruits that resemble star-shaped berries. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its rapid growth and invasive potential, it is considered a problematic species in some regions.
