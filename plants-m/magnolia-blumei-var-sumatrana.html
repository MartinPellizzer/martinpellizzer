Magnolia blumei var. sumatrana

Magnolia blumei var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broadly ovate and have a smooth, glossy surface. The plant thrives in humid, warm climates with well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
