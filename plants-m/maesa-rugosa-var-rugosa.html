Maesa rugosa var. rugosa

Maesa rugosa var. rugosa, commonly known as the rough maesa, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a rough, textured surface, giving it its scientific name. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually in green to reddish bracts, and bloom in the spring.

The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
