Maranta anderssoniana

Maranta anderssoniana, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Marantaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, zigzagging stems and dark green, velvety leaves. It produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in the evening.

Maranta anderssoniana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
