Melittis sarmatica

Melittis sarmatica, commonly known as the Caucasian melittis, is a perennial herb native to the Caucasus region.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces distinctive, globose fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous areas.

Melittis sarmatica is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
