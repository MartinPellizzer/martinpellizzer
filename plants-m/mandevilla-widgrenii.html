Mandevilla widgrenii

Mandevilla widgrenii, commonly known as the yellow mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with woody stems and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla widgrenii is often cultivated as a ornamental plant for its showy blooms and tropical appearance.
