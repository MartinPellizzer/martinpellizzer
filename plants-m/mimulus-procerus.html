Mimulus procerus

Mimulus procerus, commonly known as the slender monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in moist, rocky habitats such as streambanks and meadows. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Mimulus procerus is a perennial herb that thrives in temperate climates and is often found in coastal regions.
