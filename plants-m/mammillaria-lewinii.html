Mammillaria lewinii

Mammillaria lewinii, commonly known as the Lewin's hedgehog cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and arid environments, showcasing its adaptation to harsh conditions. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and rock gardens.
