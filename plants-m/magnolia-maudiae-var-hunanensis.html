Magnolia maudiae var. hunanensis

Magnolia maudiae var. hunanensis, commonly known as the Hunan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, dark green upper surface. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
