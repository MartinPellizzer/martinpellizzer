Magnolia kachirachirai

Magnolia kachirachirai, commonly known as the Kachirachirai magnolia, is a rare and ornamental tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the stem. Its flowers are typically borne on long, slender pedicels, adding to its visual appeal.

The tree is valued for its aesthetic qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental beauty.
