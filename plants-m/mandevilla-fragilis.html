Mandevilla fragilis

Mandevilla fragilis, commonly known as the delicate mandevilla, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its trailing or climbing growth habit. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a velvety texture.

Mandevilla fragilis is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
