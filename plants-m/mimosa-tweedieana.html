Mimosa tweedieana

Mimosa tweedieana, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Mimosa tweedieana is also known for its sensitive leaves that fold when touched, a trait shared with other mimosa species.
