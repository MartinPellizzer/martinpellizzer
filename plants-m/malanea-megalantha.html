Malanea megalantha

Malanea megalantha, commonly known as the giant malanea, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. Its leaves are broad and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas with well-drained soil.
