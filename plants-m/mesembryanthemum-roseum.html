Mesembryanthemum roseum

Mesembryanthemum roseum, commonly known as the pink ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, pink to purple flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
