Melastoma sanguineum var. laevifolium

Melastoma sanguineum var. laevifolium, commonly known as the smooth-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The leaves are usually ovate to elliptical in shape and have a smooth, glossy surface. This species is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and undergrowth.

It plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and shelter for small organisms.
