Microdesmis haumaniana

Microdesmis haumaniana, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes nightshades, and is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and forest edges, thriving in well-drained soils. It has simple, ovate leaves and produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is not widely distributed outside its native range.
