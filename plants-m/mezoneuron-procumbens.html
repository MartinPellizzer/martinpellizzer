Mezoneuron procumbens

Mezoneuron procumbens, commonly known as the creeping mezoneuron, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. The plant features a low-growing, spreading habit with slender stems and small, opposite leaves. Its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, producing tiny, pale yellow blooms.
