Maxillaria darienensis

Maxillaria darienensis, commonly known as the Darien orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet scent.

Maxillaria darienensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
