Melothria filipes

Melothria filipes, commonly known as the slender leaf melon, is a tropical climbing vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and small, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that develop into edible, green fruits resembling miniature melons. Its fruit is typically harvested when fully mature, though it is less commonly cultivated than other cucurbits.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in tropical regions of South America.
