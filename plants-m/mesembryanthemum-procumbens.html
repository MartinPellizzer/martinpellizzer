Mesembryanthemum procumbens

Mesembryanthemum procumbens, commonly known as the carpet flower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, succulent species with fleshy, often hairy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid environments. Mesembryanthemum procumbens is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
