Microtis magnadenia

Microtis magnadenia, commonly known as the large-flowered microtis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae.

It is native to Australia and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces compact, spike-like inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or heathlands.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
