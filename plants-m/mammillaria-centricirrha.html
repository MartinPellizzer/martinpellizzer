Mammillaria centricirrha

Mammillaria centricirrha, commonly known as the starry mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny appearance. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
