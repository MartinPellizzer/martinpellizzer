Macowania revoluta

Macowania revoluta, commonly known as the revolute macaw or the red-crowned macaw, is a striking bird native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant plumage, with a bright red head and a blue body. The bird's wings are long and pointed, aiding in its agile flight through dense forests. Its beak is strong and curved, adapted for cracking nuts and seeds.

This species plays an important role in seed dispersal, contributing to the regeneration of its native ecosystems.
