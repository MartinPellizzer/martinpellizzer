Miltonia vexillaria var. rubella

Miltonia vexillaria var. rubella, commonly known as the red-flowered orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, velvety red petals and a contrasting white or pink lip, which gives it a striking appearance. This orchid typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers bloom in clusters and are known for their strong, spicy fragrance.

The plant prefers bright, indirect light and consistent moisture for optimal growth.
