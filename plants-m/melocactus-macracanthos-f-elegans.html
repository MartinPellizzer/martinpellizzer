Melocactus macracanthos f. elegans

Melocactus macracanthos f. elegans, commonly known as the Elegant Melocactus, is a rare and striking cactus species native to the high-altitude regions of southern Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, globular stem and dense, spiny areoles that bear clusters of white or pale yellow flowers. The plant's unique appearance is enhanced by its vibrant, showy blooms that appear during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure to flourish.

Due to its limited natural range and specific growing conditions, it is a sought-after specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
