Miconia pauciglandulosa

Miconia pauciglandulosa, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, and often glossy dark green leaves that are typically arranged in opposite pairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its fruit is a fleshy, red or orange berry that contains multiple seeds.

Miconia pauciglandulosa is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments.
