Miconia aguirrei

Miconia aguirrei, commonly known as the Mexican miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia aguirrei is often found in moist, shaded understory habitats within tropical forests.
