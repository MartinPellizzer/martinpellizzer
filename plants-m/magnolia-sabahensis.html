Magnolia sabahensis

Magnolia sabahensis, commonly known as the Sabah magnolia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Borneo.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to cream-colored flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter on the underside. It typically grows in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

The bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinctive fragrance when crushed.
