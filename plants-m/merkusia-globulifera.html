Merkusia globulifera

Merkusia globulifera, commonly known as the globe-leaf tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure containing several seeds.

Merkusia globulifera is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to thrive in humid, tropical climates.
