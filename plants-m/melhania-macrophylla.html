Melhania macrophylla

Melhania macrophylla, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces striking, fiery red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the dry season. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

The tree is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
