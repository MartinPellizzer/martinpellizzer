Mollia nivea

Mollia nivea, commonly known as the white moss or snow moss, is a small, perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as woodlands and rocky crevices. The plant features delicate, thread-like leaves that are usually covered in a fine, white coating, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, often white or pale green, and appear in clusters.

Mollia nivea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings for its unique appearance.
