Microlaena thomsonii

Microlaena thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's microlaena, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, erect stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green to brown flowers. It is often found in sandy or gravelly soils in coastal and inland regions.
