Millettia congestiflora

Millettia congestiflora, commonly known as the clustered millettia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink to purple. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in forested areas. Its seeds are encased in pods and are dispersed by wind or animals.

Millettia congestiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
