Masdevallia harryana var. atrosanguinea

Masdevallia harryana var. atrosanguinea, commonly known as the Black Blood Masdevallia, is a striking orchid species native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its dark, velvety petals and sepals, which give it its dramatic appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often a deep red or maroon, with intricate patterns that enhance its visual appeal.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and elegance.
