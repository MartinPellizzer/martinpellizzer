Malus melliana

Malus melliana, commonly known as the Chinese white apple, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its ornamental white flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional Chinese cuisine. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a serrated margin.

Malus melliana is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
