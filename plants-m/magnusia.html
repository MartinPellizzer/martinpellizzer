Magnusia

Magnusia, commonly known as the magnolia, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Magnoliaceae.

These plants are characterized by their large, showy blossoms that often appear before the leaves in spring. The flowers are typically fragrant and come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. Magnusia trees are deciduous or evergreen, depending on the species, and can grow as shrubs or small trees.

They are native to eastern Asia, the southeastern United States, and the Caribbean.
