Mammillaria lewisiana

Mammillaria lewisiana, commonly known as the Lewis's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and spines that are often white or yellowish. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
