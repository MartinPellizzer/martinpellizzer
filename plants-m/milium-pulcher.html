Milium pulcher

Milium pulcher, commonly known as the beautiful millet, is a flowering plant in the grass family, Poaceae.

It is native to regions of Asia, including China and Japan, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, white or pale green flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
