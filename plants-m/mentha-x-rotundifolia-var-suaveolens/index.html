Mentha × Rotundifolia Var. Suaveolens

Mentha × rotundifolia var. suaveolens is a unique hybrid mint known for its rounded leaves and sweet, aromatic scent. This plant thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in temperate regions around the world. Its fragrant oils make it a popular choice for culinary and medicinal uses. Unlike common mints, it has a more compact growth habit and is prized for its distinct flavor profile. Despite its modest size, it holds significant value in both traditional and modern herbal practices.
