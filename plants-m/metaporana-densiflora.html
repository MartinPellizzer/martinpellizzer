Metaporana densiflora

Metaporana densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered metaporana, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy green surface with serrated edges.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens for its visual appeal.
