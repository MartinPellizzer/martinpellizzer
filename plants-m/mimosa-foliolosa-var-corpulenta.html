Mimosa foliolosa var. corpulenta

Mimosa foliolosa var. corpulenta, commonly known as the giant mimosa, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fern-like leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces delicate, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a soft aesthetic to its appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Its rapid growth and unique foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
