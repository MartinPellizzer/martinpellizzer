Massonia depressa

Massonia depressa, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
