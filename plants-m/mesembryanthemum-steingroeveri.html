Mesembryanthemum steingroeveri

Mesembryanthemum steingroeveri, commonly known as the Steingrover's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
