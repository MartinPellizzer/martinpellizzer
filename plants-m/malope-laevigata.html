Malope laevigata

Malope laevigata, commonly known as the smooth malope, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The plant is characterized by its branched stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
