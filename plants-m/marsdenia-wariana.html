Marsdenia wariana

Marsdenia wariana, commonly known as the Philippine star vine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in forest understories.
