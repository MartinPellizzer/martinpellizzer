Matthiola oxyceras var. angulosa

Matthiola oxyceras var. angulosa, commonly known as the angular wallflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and clusters of small, brightly colored flowers that typically range from yellow to orange. The leaves are linear and often have a slightly serrated edge, contributing to its distinctive appearance. Native to southern Europe, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
