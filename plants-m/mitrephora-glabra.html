Mitrephora glabra

Mitrephora glabra, commonly known as the smooth mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew and poison ivy. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by edible, spiny fruits.
