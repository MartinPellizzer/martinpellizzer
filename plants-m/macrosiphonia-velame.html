Macrosiphonia velame

Macrosiphonia velame, commonly known as the velvet sponge or sea sponge, is a marine organism belonging to the family Spongiidae.

It is characterized by its dense, fibrous structure and a soft, velvety texture that gives it its common name. This sponge is typically found in shallow coastal waters and is often attached to rocky substrates. It plays a vital role in marine ecosystems by filtering water and providing habitat for various small organisms.

Its unique morphology and ecological importance make it an important subject in marine biology studies.
