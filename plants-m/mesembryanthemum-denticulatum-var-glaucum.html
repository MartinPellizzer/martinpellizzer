Mesembryanthemum denticulatum var. glaucum

Mesembryanthemum denticulatum var. glaucum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves covered in a dense layer of fine white hairs, giving it a silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a valuable addition to arid landscapes.
