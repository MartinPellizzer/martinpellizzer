Myrtus klossii

Myrtus klossii, commonly known as Kloss's myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Kauai in Hawaii.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, narrow leaves and small white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Myrtus klossii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional Hawaiian medicine.
