Myosotis nemorosa subsp. lamottiana

Myosotis nemorosa subsp.

lamottiana, commonly known as the lamottian water violet, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

The plant features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, waxy surface.

The subspecies is distinguished by its slightly larger flowers and more robust growth compared to other Myosotis species.
