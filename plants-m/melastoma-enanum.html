Melastoma enanum

Melastoma enanum, commonly known as the dwarf melastoma, is a small shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red, which bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, black seeds when mature.
