Myosotis multiflora

Myosotis multiflora, commonly known as the woodland forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, while its stems are slender and often branched.

Myosotis multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
