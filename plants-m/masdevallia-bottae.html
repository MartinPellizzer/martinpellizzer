Masdevallia bottae

Masdevallia bottae, commonly known as the Bottae Masdevallia, is a rare and elegant orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with slender stems and small, showy flowers that come in shades of pink, white, and purple. Its unique morphology and habitat requirements make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited natural range and specific environmental needs, it is often cultivated in controlled settings to ensure its survival.
