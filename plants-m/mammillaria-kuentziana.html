Mammillaria kuentziana

Mammillaria kuentziana, commonly known as the Kuentziana pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a diameter typically ranging from 5 to 10 centimeters. The plant features dense, white to pale yellow spines and produces yellow flowers in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
