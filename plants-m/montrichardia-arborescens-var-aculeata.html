Montrichardia arborescens var. aculeata

Montrichardia arborescens var. aculeata, commonly known as the spiny arrowhead, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are often spiny along the edges. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, preferring warm and humid conditions. It produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding ornamental value to its appearance.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in aquatic and semi-aquatic habitats.
