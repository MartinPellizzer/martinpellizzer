Maxillaria rubrilabia

Maxillaria rubrilabia, commonly known as the red-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks or branches in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale green to white with a deep red or orange lip, making it visually appealing.

Maxillaria rubrilabia thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
