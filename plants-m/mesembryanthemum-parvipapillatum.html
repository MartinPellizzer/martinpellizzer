Mesembryanthemum parvipapillatum

Mesembryanthemum parvipapillatum, commonly known as the small-papillate ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and specialized adaptations for arid environments. It is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
