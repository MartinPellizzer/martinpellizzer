Mesembryanthemum abyssinicum

Mesembryanthemum abyssinicum, commonly known as the Abyssinian ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to harsh conditions with its water-storing tissues.
