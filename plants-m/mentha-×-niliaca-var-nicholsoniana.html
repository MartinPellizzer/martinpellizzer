Mentha × niliaca var. nicholsoniana

Mentha × niliaca var. nicholsoniana, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mint, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. This variety is native to regions of southern Europe and western Asia. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

The plant is often used in herbal teas and for its medicinal properties.
