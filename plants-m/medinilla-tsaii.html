Medinilla tsaii

Medinilla tsaii, commonly known as the Taiwan medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically pink or white, with a distinctive, waxy appearance that resembles a cluster of grapes. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This plant is often cultivated as an ornamental species due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
