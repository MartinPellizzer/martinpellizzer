Mammillaria montensis

Mammillaria montensis, commonly known as the Montesinos hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and covered in white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments and is well adapted to drought conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the spring.

Mammillaria montensis is popular among cactus enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
