Manettia luteorubra var. paraguariensis

Manettia luteorubra var. paraguariensis, commonly known as Paraguayan yellow-rose.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil and Paraguay. The plant features showy, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red calyx. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in shaded, moist environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular species among horticulturists and botanists.
