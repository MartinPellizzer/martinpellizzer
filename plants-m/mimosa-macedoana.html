Mimosa macedoana

Mimosa macedoana, commonly known as the Macedonian mimosa, is a species of flowering plant native to the Balkan region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that close when touched. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa macedoana is often found in woodland areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits.
