Millettia geerinckiana

Millettia geerinckiana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous racemes of yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets that contribute to its lush appearance.
