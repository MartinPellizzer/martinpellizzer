Melodorum mollissimum

Melodorum mollissimum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant characterized by its soft, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae and is native to regions in Africa, Asia, and the Pacific. The plant features large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a smooth texture. It produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Melodorum mollissimum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various growing conditions.
