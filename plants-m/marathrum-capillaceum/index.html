Marathrum Capillaceum

Marathrum capillaceum is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high altitudes of South America. It is known for its slender stems and tiny, thread-like leaves that give it its scientific name. This species thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous regions with rich soil. Due to its limited habitat, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its unique appearance and ecological significance make it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
