Mesua larnachiana

Mesua larnachiana, commonly known as the Larnachiana torchwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The tree is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
