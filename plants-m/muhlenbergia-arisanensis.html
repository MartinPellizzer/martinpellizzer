Muhlenbergia arisanensis

Muhlenbergia arisanensis, commonly known as the Japanese muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-gray foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often seen in late summer and early autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Muhlenbergia arisanensis is valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
