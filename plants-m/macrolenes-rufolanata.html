Macrolenes rufolanata

Macrolenes rufolanata, commonly known as the red-leaved macrolene, is a unique plant species characterized by its striking red foliage.

It is native to tropical regions and thrives in warm, humid climates with ample sunlight. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are thick and waxy, which helps it retain moisture in dry conditions.

Macrolenes rufolanata is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant coloration and distinctive appearance.
