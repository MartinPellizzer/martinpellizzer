Mansoa truncata var. parviflora

Mansoa truncata var. parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered mansoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in the understory of tropical forests. The plant features small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil conditions.
