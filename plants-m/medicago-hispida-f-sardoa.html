Medicago hispida f. sardoa

Medicago hispida f. sardoa, commonly known as the Sardonic goat's rue, is a botanical variant of the goat's rue species.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its spiny stems and small, yellow flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in rocky and arid environments. The plant has hairy leaves and produces pods that contain seeds with a distinctive, rough texture.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to dry, sunny conditions.
