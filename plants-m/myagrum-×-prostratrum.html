Myagrum × prostratrum

Myagrum × prostratrum, commonly known as the prostrate myrtle, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Myrtaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, spreading habit and glossy, dark green foliage. The leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a slightly aromatic scent when crushed. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences.

This plant is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil conditions.
