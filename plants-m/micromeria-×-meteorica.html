Micromeria × meteorica

Micromeria × meteorica, commonly known as the desert star.

This hybrid plant is a member of the Asteraceae family and is native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico. It is characterized by its small, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has gray-green foliage and is adapted to thrive in hot, dry environments.

It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
