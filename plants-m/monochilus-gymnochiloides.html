Monochilus gymnochiloides

Monochilus gymnochiloides, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers and is often found in tropical rainforests. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a fan-like pattern.

The orchid thrives in humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
