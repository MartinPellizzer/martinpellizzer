Melampyrum Nemorosum Subsp. Catalaunicum

Melampyrum nemorosum subsp. catalaunicum is a lesser-known member of the buckwheat family. It thrives in shaded, moist environments like woodlands and hedgerows. This subspecies is native to parts of southern Europe and the Mediterranean region. Its distinctive red-orange flowers bloom in late spring, attracting pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains underappreciated and often overlooked in botanical studies.
