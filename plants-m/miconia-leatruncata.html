Miconia leatruncata

Miconia leatruncata, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its fruit is a berry that turns red when mature and contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia leatruncata is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along streambanks.
