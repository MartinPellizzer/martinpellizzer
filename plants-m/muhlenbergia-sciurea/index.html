Muhlenbergia Sciurea

Muhlenbergia sciurea is a striking ornamental grass known for its feathery plumes and vibrant autumn color. Native to the United States, it thrives in a variety of soil types and is drought tolerant once established. This plant is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements. Its unique foliage adds texture and movement to garden designs. Muhlenbergia sciurea is a versatile choice for both residential and commercial landscapes.
