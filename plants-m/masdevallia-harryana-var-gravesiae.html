Masdevallia harryana var. gravesiae

Masdevallia harryana var. gravesiae, commonly known as the Harryana Gravesia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures and its ability to thrive in high-altitude, humid environments. The plant typically features slender, dark green leaves and produces delicate, fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and unique morphology.
