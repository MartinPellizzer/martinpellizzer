Magnolia obovata

Magnolia obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The leaves are broad and ovate, with a smooth texture and a deep green color. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological benefits.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
