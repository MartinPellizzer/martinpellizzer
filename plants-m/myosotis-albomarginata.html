Myosotis albomarginata

Myosotis albomarginata, commonly known as the white-margin forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to New Zealand.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive white margin. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

Myosotis albomarginata is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local pollinators.
