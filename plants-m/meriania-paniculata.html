Meriania paniculata

Meriania paniculata, commonly known as the red-flowered meriania, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in panicles, making it a visually appealing plant. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

The plant is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
