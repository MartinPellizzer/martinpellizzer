Musa martini

Musa martini, commonly known as the Martini banana, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that grows in tropical climates, characterized by its large, broad leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellow, edible berries that are often used in culinary applications. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires a warm, humid environment.

Musa martini is valued for its ornamental beauty and its fruit's unique flavor.
