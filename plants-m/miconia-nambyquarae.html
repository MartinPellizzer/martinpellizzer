Miconia nambyquarae

Miconia nambyquarae, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit tree, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by fruits that resemble small starfruits in appearance. Miconia nambyquarae is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets, which can have ecological impacts in its native habitat.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
