Magnolia javieri

Magnolia javieri, commonly known as the Java magnolia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, dark green leaves that are oval in shape and have a smooth texture. It typically grows as a small to medium-sized tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with a pleasant fragrance when crushed.
