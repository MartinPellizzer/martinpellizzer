Microstylis spiralipetala

Microstylis spiralipetala, commonly known as the spiral-petaled microstylis, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, intricate flowers that spiral around a central stem, creating a unique and visually striking appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces tiny, pale yellow blooms that are often overlooked due to their size. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and nutrient-rich soil.

Due to habitat loss, Microstylis spiralipetala is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
