Monvillea minensis

Monvillea minensis, commonly known as the Minensis orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, greenish-white blooms with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in horticulture.
