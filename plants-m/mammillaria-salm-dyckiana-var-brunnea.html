Mammillaria salm-dyckiana var. brunnea

Mammillaria salm-dyckiana var. brunnea, commonly known as the brown mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with a grayish-green color and prominent, brownish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
