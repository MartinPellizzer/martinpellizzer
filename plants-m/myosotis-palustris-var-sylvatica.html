Myosotis palustris var. sylvatica

Myosotis palustris var. sylvatica, commonly known as the woodland forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to moist woodland areas in Europe.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It prefers well-drained, acidic soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for shaded garden landscapes.
