Machaerium lilacinum

Machaerium lilacinum, commonly known as the purple machaerium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, arranged in dense, globular clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it has a fibrous root system.
