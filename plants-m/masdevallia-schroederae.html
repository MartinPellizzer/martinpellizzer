Masdevallia schroederae

Masdevallia schroederae, commonly known as the Schroeder's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, spathaceous inflorescence and vibrant, often iridescent flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for their unique shape and coloration.

Masdevallia schroederae is known for its slow growth and specific care requirements, making it a challenging but rewarding species for experienced growers.
