Monocera tuberculata

Monocera tuberculata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its rosette-shaped leaves and vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in a variety of colors. The plant belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor cultivation and greenhouse environments.
