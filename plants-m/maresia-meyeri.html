Maresia meyeri

Maresia meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's maresia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils and salty air. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, cylindrical seed heads.

Maresia meyeri is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and ornamental value.
