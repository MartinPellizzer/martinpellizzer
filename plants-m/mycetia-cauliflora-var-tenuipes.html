Mycetia cauliflora var. tenuipes

Mycetia cauliflora var. tenuipes, commonly known as the slender-stemmed mycetia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its unique cauliflorous growth habit, where flowers and fruits develop directly from the trunk or branches. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that are often accompanied by a strong, unpleasant odor. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a valuable species in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
