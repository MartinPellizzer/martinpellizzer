Mammillaria schelhasii var. sericata

Mammillaria schelhasii var. sericata, commonly known as the silver mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique aesthetic make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
