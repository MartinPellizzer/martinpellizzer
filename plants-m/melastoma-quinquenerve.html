Melastoma quinquenerve

Melastoma quinquenerve, commonly known as the five-veined melastoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of India, Indonesia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with five distinct veins, giving it its common name. Its flowers are typically showy, with five petals and a prominent yellow or red stamen.

The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are often consumed by birds and other wildlife.
