Maranta bellula

Maranta bellula, commonly known as the dwarf arrowroot or nightshade maranta, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their vibrant foliage. The plant features striking, heart-shaped leaves that often display intricate patterns in shades of silver, pink, and red. It is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid conditions.

Maranta bellula is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in low-light environments.
