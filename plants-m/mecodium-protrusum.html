Mecodium protrusum

Mecodium protrusum, commonly known as the creeping mecodium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is characterized by its prostrate growth habit. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small and usually white or pale green, clustered in dense inflorescences.

Mecodium protrusum is adapted to arid environments and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions.
