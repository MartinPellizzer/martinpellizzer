Medinilla andrarangensis

Medinilla andrarangensis, commonly known as the Mindanao Medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and is often found in moist, shaded habitats.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant or in botanical gardens.
