Myrcia roraimae

Myrcia roraimae, commonly known as Roraima myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the Amazon rainforest, specifically found in the region of Roraima in Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests.

Myrcia roraimae plays a role in the local ecosystem by providing food and shelter for various insect and bird species.
