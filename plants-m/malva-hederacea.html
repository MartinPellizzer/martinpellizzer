Malva hederacea

Malva hederacea, commonly known as broadleaf mallows, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are typically green with a soft texture. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Malva hederacea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional herbal medicine.
