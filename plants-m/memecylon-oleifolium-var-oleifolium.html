Memecylon oleifolium var. oleifolium

Memecylon oleifolium var. oleifolium, commonly known as the oilleaf memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, giving them a glossy appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule that contains several seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
