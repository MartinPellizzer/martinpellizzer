Musilia

Musilia, commonly known as the bluebell, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has narrow, grass-like leaves. Musilia prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

It is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
