Melampodium Divaricatum

Melampodium divaricatum is a unique flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the sunflower family and is known for its vibrant yellow blooms. this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure. it is often used in native gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits. its distinctive foliage and long-lasting flowers make it a popular choice among gardeners.
