Mussaenda philippica f. aurorae

Mussaenda philippica f. aurorae, commonly known as the golden mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are typically ovate and have a slightly hairy texture. The plant prefers full sun to partial shade and well-drained soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
