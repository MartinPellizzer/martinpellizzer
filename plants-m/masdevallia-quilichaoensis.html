Masdevallia quilichaoensis

Masdevallia quilichaoensis, commonly known as the Quilichao masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower spikes and velvety foliage. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments with high elevation. Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered vulnerable, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
