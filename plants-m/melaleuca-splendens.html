Melaleuca splendens

Melaleuca splendens, commonly known as the splendour bottlebrush, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its striking, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces small, aromatic leaves and has a rough, fibrous bark that becomes flaky with age. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy areas.

Melaleuca splendens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral displays.
