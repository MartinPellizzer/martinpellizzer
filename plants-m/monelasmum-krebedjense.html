Monelasmum krebedjense

Monelasmum krebedjense, commonly known as the Kredens mountain vine, is a climbing plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Africa.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae, characterized by its leguminous properties and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features compound leaves with serrated edges and produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically white or pale purple. Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
