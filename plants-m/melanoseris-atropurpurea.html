Melanoseris atropurpurea

Melanoseris atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-flowered sedge, is a perennial plant native to parts of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and related grass-like plants. The plant is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces dense clusters of small, purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors.
