Mentha × speckmoseriana

Mentha × speckmoseriana, commonly known as Speckmoser's mint, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is a cross between Mentha longifolia and Mentha rotundifolia, resulting in a plant with aromatic, slightly fuzzy leaves. The leaves are typically oval to round in shape and have a strong, minty scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This plant is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its flavorful oils and essential compounds.
