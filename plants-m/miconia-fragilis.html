Miconia fragilis

Miconia fragilis, commonly known as the delicate miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Neotropical region.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Miconia fragilis is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
