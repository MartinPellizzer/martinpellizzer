Micromeria × benthamineolens

Micromeria × benthamineolens, commonly known as the hybrid shrub, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring compact, bushy growth with aromatic, glandular leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs, while its stems are woody and branched.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrant foliage and delicate floral display.
