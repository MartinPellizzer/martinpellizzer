Morus Alba F. Macrophylla

Morus alba f. macrophylla is a unique variation of the common white mulberry tree known for its large, heart-shaped leaves. This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping due to its striking foliage. While it shares the same basic structure as its parent species, it exhibits distinctive leaf characteristics that set it apart. The plant is hardy and adaptable, thriving in a variety of climates and soil conditions. Its beauty and resilience make it a popular choice for both gardeners and horticultural enthusiasts.
