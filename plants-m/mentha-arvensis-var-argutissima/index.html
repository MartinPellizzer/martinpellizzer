Mentha Arvensis Var. Argutissima

Mentha arvensis var. argutissima is a lesser-known member of the mint family known for its distinct appearance and aromatic qualities. This variant of field mint is often found growing in moist, shaded areas across temperate regions. Its leaves are elongated and serrated, giving it a unique visual identity compared to other mint species. While not as commonly cultivated as peppermint or spearmint, it holds value in traditional medicine and herbal practices. Its subtle yet potent scent makes it a versatile plant for both culinary and therapeutic uses.
