Melanoseris bonatii

Melanoseris bonatii, commonly known as the Sicilian bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Italy.

It belongs to the Campanulaceae family and is characterized by its showy blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves, often growing in rocky or dry habitats. It is relatively small in size, typically reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters.
