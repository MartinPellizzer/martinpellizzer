Mesosphaerum savannarum

Mesosphaerum savannarum, commonly known as the savanna mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to the grasslands of southern Africa.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in its arid environment.

The plant's root system is extensive, helping it to anchor in loose, sandy soils and access moisture deep underground.
