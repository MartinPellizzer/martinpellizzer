Masdevallia obrieniana

Masdevallia obrieniana, commonly known as the O'Brien's masdevallia, is a rare and exotic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful blooms that feature a unique combination of petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are highly prized by orchid enthusiasts for their striking appearance and rarity.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is challenging to cultivate outside of its native environment.
