Millettia auriculata f. extensa

Millettia auriculata f. extensa, commonly known as the broad-leafed millettia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often broader than they are long. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that are typically arranged in racemes. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several elliptical seeds.

This variety is native to parts of Southeast Asia and is valued for its ornamental foliage and potential ecological benefits.
