Mandevilla inexperata

Mandevilla inexperata, commonly known as the red mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or vine, reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and elliptical with a glossy surface.

Mandevilla inexperata thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
