Mahonia ganpinensis var. confusa

Mahonia ganpinensis var. confusa, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features compound leaves with spiny margins and a deep green color, often with a slight variegation. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by blue-black berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a hardy ornamental plant.

Its adaptability and aesthetic appeal make it a popular choice for garden landscapes.
