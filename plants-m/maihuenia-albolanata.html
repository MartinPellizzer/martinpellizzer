Maihuenia albolanata

Maihuenia albolanata, commonly known as the Andean snowgrass, is a perennial flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, commonly known as grasses, and is adapted to cold, harsh environments. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white to pale pink florets in dense clusters. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in alpine and subalpine habitats.
