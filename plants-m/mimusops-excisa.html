Mimusops excisa

Mimusops excisa, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, showy white flowers that bloom in clusters, resembling snowballs. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth margin. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mimusops excisa is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various climates.
