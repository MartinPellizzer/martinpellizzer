Mussaenda inflata

Mussaenda inflata, commonly known as the flame flower or red trumpet bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that resemble red or orange trumpets, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.
