Medica inermis

Medica inermis, commonly known as the smooth medick, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically reaches a height of about 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval, smooth-edged leaflets. Its small, pea-like flowers are usually pink or purple, and it produces elongated pods that contain several small, rounded seeds.

Medica inermis is often found in disturbed soils and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to fix nitrogen.
