Mallotus nitidus

Mallotus nitidus, commonly known as the glossy flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its hardy nature and ability to tolerate various soil conditions.
