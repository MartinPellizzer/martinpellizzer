Monticalia guadalupe subsp. caucana

Monticalia guadalupe subsp.

caucana, commonly known as the Guadalupe mountain heather, is a shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central America. It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many flowering plants adapted to acidic soils.
