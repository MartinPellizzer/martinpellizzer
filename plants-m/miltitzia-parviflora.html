Miltitzia parviflora

Miltitzia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered miltitzia, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and have a smooth, glossy surface.

Miltitzia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
