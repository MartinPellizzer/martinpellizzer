Minuartia macrocarpa var. jooi

Minuartia macrocarpa var. jooi, commonly known as the rock saxifrage, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

It features small, clustered leaves that are typically blue-green in color, adapted to withstand harsh, arid conditions. The plant produces delicate, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it a hardy choice for rock gardens and alpine landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and resilience make it a popular ornamental plant in horticultural settings.
