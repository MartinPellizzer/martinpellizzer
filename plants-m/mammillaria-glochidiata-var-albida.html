Mammillaria glochidiata var. albida

Mammillaria glochidiata var. albida, commonly known as the white-furred hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly bristles that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
