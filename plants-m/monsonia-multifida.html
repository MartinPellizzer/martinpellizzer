Monsonia multifida

Monsonia multifida, commonly known as the African snowflake, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in winter. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Monsonia multifida is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
