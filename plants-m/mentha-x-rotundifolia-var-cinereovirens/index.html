Mentha × Rotundifolia Var. Cinereovirens

Cinereovirens mint, scientifically known as Mentha × rotundifolia var. cinereovirens, is a unique hybrid mint prized for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties. This variant of the round-leaf mint is characterized by its grayish-green foliage and subtle, refreshing scent. Native to parts of Europe and Asia, it thrives in moist, shady environments and is often cultivated for both culinary and therapeutic uses. Its versatility makes it a popular choice among herbalists and gardeners alike. With its calming effects and adaptability, Cinereovirens mint remains a valuable plant in traditional and modern wellness practices.
