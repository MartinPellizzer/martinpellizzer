Monardella breweri

Monardella breweri, commonly known as Brewer's monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features showy, tubular pink or purple flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy texture.

Monardella breweri thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in rocky soils.
