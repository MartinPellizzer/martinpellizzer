Mimosa hassleriana

Mimosa hassleriana, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid leaf movement in response to touch, a phenomenon called thigmonasty. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its botanical features make it both visually appealing and ecologically interesting.
