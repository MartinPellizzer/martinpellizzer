Microlepia pingpienensis

Microlepia pingpienensis, commonly known as the Pingpien fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are often used in traditional medicine, though its primary botanical interest lies in its morphology and ecological role.
