Manilkara vitiensis

Manilkara vitiensis, commonly known as the Vitiensis sycamore, is a tropical tree native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and smooth, gray bark. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by woody, spiny fruits containing a single seed. Its latex is used in traditional medicine and as a natural adhesive.
