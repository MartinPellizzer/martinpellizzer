Malva grossheimii

Malva grossheimii, commonly known as the Cape mallow, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Malva grossheimii is typically found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.
