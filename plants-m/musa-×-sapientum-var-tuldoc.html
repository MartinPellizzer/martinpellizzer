Musa × sapientum var. tuldoc

Musa × sapientum var. tuldoc, commonly known as the Thai banana.

This variety is characterized by its large, elongated, and slightly curved leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of berries that are generally yellow or orange when ripe, with a sweet and tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications due to its nutritional value and aromatic properties.
