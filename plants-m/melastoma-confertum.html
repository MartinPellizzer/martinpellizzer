Melastoma confertum

Melastoma confertum, commonly known as the red-rooted melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. Its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
