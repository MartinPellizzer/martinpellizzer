Masdevallia sijmiana

Masdevallia sijmiana, commonly known as the Sijmiana orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers. The plant features strap-like leaves and is known for its vibrant, colorful blooms that range from purple to red and orange. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
