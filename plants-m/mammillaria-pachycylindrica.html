Mammillaria pachycylindrica

Mammillaria pachycylindrica, commonly known as the narrow-cylindrical mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is often ribbed and covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
