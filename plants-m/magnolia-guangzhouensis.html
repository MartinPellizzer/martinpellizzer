Magnolia guangzhouensis

Magnolia guangzhouensis, commonly known as Guangzhou magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
