Maxillaria echinochila

Maxillaria echinochila, commonly known as the spiny-lipped orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with a distinctive spiny appearance on the lip.

The botanical characteristics of Maxillaria echinochila make it a unique and ornamental addition to botanical collections.
