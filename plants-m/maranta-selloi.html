Maranta selloi

Maranta selloi, commonly known as the velvet leaf maranta, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display intricate patterns of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental species known for their vibrant foliage. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta selloi is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
