Manihot conulifera

Manihot conulifera, commonly known as the arrowleaf manioc, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a milky latex sap, which is toxic and can cause skin irritation. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.

However, due to its toxic properties, it requires careful handling and processing before consumption.
