Myoporum dulce

Myoporum dulce, commonly known as sweet myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and subalpine regions.

Myoporum dulce is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
