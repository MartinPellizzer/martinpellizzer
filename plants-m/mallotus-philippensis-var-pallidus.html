Mallotus philippensis var. pallidus

Mallotus philippensis var. pallidus, commonly known as the pale mallot, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its elliptical, leathery leaves that are usually pale green in color. It produces small, clustered yellowish-white flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has limited use in traditional medicine.
