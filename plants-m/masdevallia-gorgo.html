Masdevallia gorgo

Masdevallia gorgo, commonly known as the Gorgo Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that feature a unique combination of petals and sepals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its leaves are usually dark green and leathery, adapted to the humid, shaded environments it inhabits.

Masdevallia gorgo is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and delicate appearance.
