Minuartia hybrida var. parviflora

Minuartia hybrida var. parviflora, commonly known as the rock jasmine, is a low-growing perennial plant native to rocky and alpine environments.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often blooming in early spring. The plant has succulent, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in arid conditions. It is typically found in mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.
