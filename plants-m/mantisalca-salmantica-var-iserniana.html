Mantisalca salmantica var. iserniana

Mantisalca salmantica var. iserniana, commonly known as the Isernian rockrose, is a perennial shrub native to southern Spain.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage of small, leathery leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, yellowish-white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features make it a valuable addition to Mediterranean-style gardens and rockeries.
