Mendoncia gilgiana subsp. gilgiana

Mendoncia gilgiana subsp.

gilgiana, commonly known as the Gilgian rockrose, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky and arid environments, exhibiting a compact growth habit with small, leathery leaves.

The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping.

Its botanical features include simple, alternate leaves and a strong, woody stem structure.
