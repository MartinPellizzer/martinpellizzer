Matonia foxworthyi

Matonia foxworthyi, commonly known as the Foxworth's matonia, is a rare flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in arid or semi-arid environments. It has a low, sprawling growth habit with fleshy, succulent leaves.
