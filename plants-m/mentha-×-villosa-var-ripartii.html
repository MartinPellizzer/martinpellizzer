Mentha × villosa var. ripartii

Mentha × villosa var. ripartii, commonly known as the woolly mint or ripartii mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy, grey-green leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland edges. It produces small, tubular flowers that are usually purple or blue in color.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
