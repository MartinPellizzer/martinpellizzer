Melloa

Melloa, commonly known as the silverleaf melloa, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery, fuzzy foliage that helps reduce water loss in hot, dry climates. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. Melloa is a hardy shrub that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
