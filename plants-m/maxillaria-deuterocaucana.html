Maxillaria deuterocaucana

Maxillaria deuterocaucana, commonly known as the Cauca Orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that typically display a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant has pseudobulbs and linear leaves, typical of many orchid genera. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria deuterocaucana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
