Mangifera caesia

Mangifera caesia, commonly known as the white mango, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The fruit is typically small, with a thin, fibrous skin and a sweet, aromatic pulp. The plant thrives in humid, tropical climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental qualities.
