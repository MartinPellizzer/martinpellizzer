Mammillaria schiedeana subsp. schiedeana

Mammillaria schiedeana subsp.

schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiedea mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
