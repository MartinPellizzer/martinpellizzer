Mallotus xylacanthus

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces woody capsules containing numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
