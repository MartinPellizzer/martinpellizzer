Masdevallia caudivolvula

Masdevallia caudivolvula, commonly known as the Caudivolvula orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, spurred petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light intensity for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
