Montanoa ovalifolia

Montanoa ovalifolia, commonly known as the oval-leaf montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy yellow flowers. The plant features opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often hairy, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Montanoa ovalifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its ability to attract pollinators.
