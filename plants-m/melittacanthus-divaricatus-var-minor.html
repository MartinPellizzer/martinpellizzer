Melittacanthus divaricatus var. minor

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, featuring a combination of green and variegated patterns. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing nectar that attracts specific pollinators.
