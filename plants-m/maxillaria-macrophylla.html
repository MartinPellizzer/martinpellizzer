Maxillaria macrophylla

Maxillaria macrophylla, commonly known as the giant maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 10 centimeters in diameter. The plant features broad, dark green leaves with a waxy texture and a thick, fleshy stem. It produces fragrant, often pink or white blooms that are clustered in inflorescences.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
