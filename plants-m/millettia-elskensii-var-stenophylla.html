Millettia elskensii var. stenophylla

Millettia elskensii var. stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets, and it produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and is often found in humid, shaded environments. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally used in traditional medicine.

The species is also notable for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
