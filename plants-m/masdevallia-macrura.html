Masdevallia macrura

Masdevallia macrura, commonly known as the long-stemmed masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, pendulous flowers with intricate patterns and a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and vibrant blooms.
