Macrosamanea macrocalyx

Macrosamanea macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant cupuaçu, is a climbing plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are similar in appearance to the cupuaçu but larger and more robust. Its botanical structure includes a woody stem with adventitious roots and palmate leaves.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its potential in both ecological and agricultural contexts.
