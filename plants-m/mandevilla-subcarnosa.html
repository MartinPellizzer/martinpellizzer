Mandevilla subcarnosa

Mandevilla subcarnosa, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant has waxy, dark green leaves that are opposite and ovate in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla subcarnosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
