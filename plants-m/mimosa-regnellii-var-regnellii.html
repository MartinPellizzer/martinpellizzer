Mimosa regnellii var. regnellii

Mimosa regnellii var. regnellii, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is typically found in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy, well-drained soils. It produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its soft, feathery appearance.
