Mapania meditensis

Mapania meditensis, commonly known as the Mediterranean mapania, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a shrub characterized by its woody stems and small, aromatic leaves. The plant produces clusters of white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Mapania meditensis is adapted to dry climates and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
