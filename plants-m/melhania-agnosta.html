Melhania agnosta

Melhania agnosta, commonly known as the African milk bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

The sap of Melhania agnosta is milky and can be toxic if ingested, so caution is advised when handling the plant.
