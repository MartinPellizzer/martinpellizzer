Mammillaria karwinskii

Mammillaria karwinskii, commonly known as the Karwinskii pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a rounded body covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and xeriscaping.
