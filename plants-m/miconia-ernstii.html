Miconia ernstii

Miconia ernstii, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that disperses seeds through wind and animal activity.

Miconia ernstii is known for its rapid growth and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
