Maranta herderiana

Maranta herderiana, commonly known as the night blooming arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of silver and green. Maranta herderiana is known for its unique ability to open its leaves at night, a trait that gives it its common name.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
