Mussaenda platyphylla

Mussaenda platyphylla, commonly known as the flatleaf Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its bright yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, making it a striking ornamental plant. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The plant is often cultivated for its attractive foliage and showy blooms in gardens and landscapes.
