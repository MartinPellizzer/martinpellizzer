Mammillaria acanthophlegma var. monacantha

Mammillaria acanthophlegma var. monacantha, commonly known as the Monacantha Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in dense, white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
