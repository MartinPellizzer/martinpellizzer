Melastoma koordersii

Melastoma koordersii, commonly known as the red-flowered Melastoma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, black seeds.
