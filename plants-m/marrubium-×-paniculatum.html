Marrubium × paniculatum, commonly known as the hybrid horehound, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green foliage and produces clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, thriving in full sun conditions. It is native to southern Europe and has been introduced to other regions for ornamental purposes.
