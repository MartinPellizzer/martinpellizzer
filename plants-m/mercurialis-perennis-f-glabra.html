Mercurialis perennis f. glabra

Mercurialis perennis f. glabra, commonly known as the smooth mercury plant, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, hairless stems and leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that are surrounded by modified leaves called cyathia. It thrives in moist, calcareous soils and is often found in wet meadows and riverbanks.
