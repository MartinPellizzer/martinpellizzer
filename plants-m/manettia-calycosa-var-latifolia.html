Manettia calycosa var. latifolia

Manettia calycosa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf manettia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, waxy leaves that are typically broad and have a velvety texture. The flowers are small, white, and clustered in umbels, often with a slight pinkish tinge. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
