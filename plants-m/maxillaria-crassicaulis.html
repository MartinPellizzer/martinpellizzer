Maxillaria crassicaulis

Maxillaria crassicaulis, commonly known as the thick-stemmed orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its robust, fleshy stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellow throat and purple petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Maxillaria crassicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and resilience in greenhouse settings.
