Maxillaria oreocharis

Maxillaria oreocharis, commonly known as the chocolate orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark brown to black, velvety pseudobulbs and leathery leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of orange, red, and purple, often with contrasting patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria oreocharis is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
