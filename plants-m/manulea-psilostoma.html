Manulea psilostoma

Manulea psilostoma, commonly known as the narrow-leaved manulea, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid conditions. Its leaves are narrow and often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.
