Melosmon orientale

Melosmon orientale, commonly known as the oriental melosmon, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Melanthiaceae.

It is characterized by its grass-like appearance, with narrow, grass-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that bloom in spring. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
