Marattia purpurascens

Marattia purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-stemmed water fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Marattiaceae.

It is characterized by its large, leathery, bipinnate fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, typically found in tropical and subtropical regions. Its stems are often tinged with a distinctive purple hue, which is more pronounced in younger plants.

Marattia purpurascens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can be found in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
