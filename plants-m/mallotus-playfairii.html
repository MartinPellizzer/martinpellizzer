Mallotus playfairii

Mallotus playfairii, commonly known as the white soursop, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit, which resembles a greenish-yellow melon, is edible and has a slightly acidic taste.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as for its fruit.
