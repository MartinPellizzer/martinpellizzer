Minuartia subtilis subsp. filicaulis

Minuartia subtilis subsp.

filicaulis, commonly known as the slender hairbedstraw, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arctic and alpine regions. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, pale green to white flowers.
