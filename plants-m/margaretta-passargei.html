Margaretta passargei

Margaretta passargei, commonly known as the Cape fynbos orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Western Cape region of South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, showy flowers that are often a blend of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in coastal fynbos vegetation, where it thrives in well-drained, sandy soils. Margaretta passargei is noted for its delicate appearance and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
