Michelia kingii

Michelia kingii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Michelia kingii thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional Chinese medicine.
