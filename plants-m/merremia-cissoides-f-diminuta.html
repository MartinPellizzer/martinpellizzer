Merremia cissoides f. diminuta

Merremia cissoides f. diminuta, commonly known as the dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Convolvulaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of blue or purple. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and a trailing growth habit, making it suitable for ground cover or hanging baskets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and its ability to climb or spread along surfaces.
