Mutisia involucrata

Mutisia involucrata, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its pinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in humid forests and along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and horticultural research.
