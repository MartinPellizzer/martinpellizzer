Mandevilla moricandiana var. bahiensis

Mandevilla moricandiana var. bahiensis, commonly known as the Bahia mandevilla, is a striking tropical vine native to Brazil.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting white or yellow throat. The plant produces heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and ample sunlight.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
