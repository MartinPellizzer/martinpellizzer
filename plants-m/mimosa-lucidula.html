Mimosa lucidula

Mimosa lucidula, commonly known as the glossy mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors or along stream banks. It produces small, round pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Mimosa lucidula is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
