Microstylis macrophylla

Microstylis macrophylla, commonly known as the broad-leafed microstylis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique leaf morphology and its role in local ecosystems.
