Macrothyrsus discolor

Macrothyrsus discolor, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant produces pods that contain edible seeds, which are used in traditional cuisines. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

Due to its nitrogen-fixing ability, it plays a role in soil enrichment in its native environment.
