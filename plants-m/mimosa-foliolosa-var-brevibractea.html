Mimosa foliolosa var. brevibractea

Mimosa foliolosa var. brevibractea, commonly known as the narrow-bract mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features compound leaves with small, oval leaflets that are typically arranged in pairs. The plant produces small, yellow, globular flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It is well adapted to dry and arid environments, making it a resilient species.

The plant is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
