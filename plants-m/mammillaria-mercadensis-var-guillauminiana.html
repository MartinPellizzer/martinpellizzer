Mammillaria mercadensis var. guillauminiana

Mammillaria mercadensis var. guillauminiana, commonly known as the Guillauminiana Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense clusters of small, white to pale yellow radial spines and a short, cylindrical stem. The plant has a distinctive appearance with rounded tubercles and a relatively smooth surface. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
