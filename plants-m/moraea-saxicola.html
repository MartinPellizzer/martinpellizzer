Moraea saxicola

Moraea saxicola, commonly known as the rock moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its compact, grass-like foliage. The plant produces striking, tubular flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting dark veins. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
