Meerburgia

Meerburgia, commonly known as the red-flowered carpetweed, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is also known as the ice plant family. Its most striking feature is its clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in summer.

Meerburgia is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
