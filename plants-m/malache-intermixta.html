Malache intermixta

Malache intermixta, commonly known as the mixed leaf begonia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage that features a mix of green, white, and pink hues. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its vibrant appearance.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in homes and greenhouses.
