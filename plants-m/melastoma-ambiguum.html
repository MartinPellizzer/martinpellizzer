Melastoma ambiguum

Melastoma ambiguum, commonly known as the ambiguous melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and often have a distinctive pink or red color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
