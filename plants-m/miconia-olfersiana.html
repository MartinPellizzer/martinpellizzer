Miconia olfersiana

Miconia olfersiana, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a reddish underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are bright red berries that contain numerous seeds.

Miconia olfersiana is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as rainforests and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
