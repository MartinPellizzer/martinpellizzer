Melodorum villosum

Melodorum villosum, commonly known as the silky bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers sunny to partially shaded environments.

Melodorum villosum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
