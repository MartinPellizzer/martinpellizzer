Mesua nagassarium var. salicina

Mesua nagassarium var. salicina, commonly known as the Salicaceae or the Indian rose.

This variant is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the Indian subcontinent. It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a pleasant aromatic scent. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, arranged in clusters, and have a fragrant, sweet odor.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its therapeutic properties.
