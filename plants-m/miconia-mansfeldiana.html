Miconia mansfeldiana

Miconia mansfeldiana, commonly known as the yellow miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
