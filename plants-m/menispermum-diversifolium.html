Menispermum diversifolium

Menispermum diversifolium, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
