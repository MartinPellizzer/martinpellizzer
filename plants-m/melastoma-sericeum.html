Melastoma sericeum

Melastoma sericeum, commonly known as the silverleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that are borne in clusters. Its leaves are usually covered in a fine, silvery-white pubescence, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Melastoma sericeum is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forest understories.
