Myrcia leptoclada var. glazioviana

Myrcia leptoclada var. glazioviana, commonly known as Glaziov's myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil, where it typically grows in humid, tropical forests. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It produces edible berries that are used in traditional medicine and local cuisine.
