Malabaila aucheri

Malabaila aucheri, commonly known as the silverleaf germander, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with silvery-gray, fuzzy foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers in clusters during the summer months. It is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its hardy nature and ornamental appeal.

Malabaila aucheri prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
