Multidentia fanshawei

Multidentia fanshawei, commonly known as the Chinese fanflower, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fan-shaped inflorescences. The plant features lance-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple and pink. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements make it a favored plant in both gardens and botanical collections.
