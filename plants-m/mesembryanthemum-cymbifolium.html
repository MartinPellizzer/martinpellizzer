Mesembryanthemum cymbifolium

Mesembryanthemum cymbifolium, commonly known as the carpet flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, making it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping. Mesembryanthemum cymbifolium is also valued for its ornamental appeal and easy care requirements.
