Megacodon venosus

Megacodon venosus, commonly known as the veined giant orchid, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with intricate veining patterns that give it its common name. The plant belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is known for its complex floral structure and vibrant colors. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

Megacodon venosus thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires careful cultivation due to its specific environmental needs.
