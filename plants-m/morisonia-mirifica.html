Morisonia mirifica

Morisonia mirifica, commonly known as the fairy lantern, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking, lantern-like flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain seeds covered in a fine, silky coating. Morisonia mirifica thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in coastal areas.

Its unique appearance and growth habits make it a notable species in botanical studies.
