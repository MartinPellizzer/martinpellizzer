Mimulus decorus

Mimulus decorus, commonly known as the elegant monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers, which are tubular in shape and attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and in woodland areas. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Mimulus decorus is valued in gardens for its beauty and ecological benefits.
