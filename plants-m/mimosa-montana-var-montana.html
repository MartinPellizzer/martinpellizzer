Mimosa montana var. montana

Mimosa montana var. montana, commonly known as the mountain mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to the southwestern United States.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are bipinnate, giving it a lacy appearance. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flower heads that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and native plant gardens.
