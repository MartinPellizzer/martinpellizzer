Melastoma mandioccanum

Melastoma mandioccanum, commonly known as the red-rooted melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and its stems are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Melastoma mandioccanum is often found in moist, shaded habitats and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
