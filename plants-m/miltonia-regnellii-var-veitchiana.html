Miltonia regnellii var. veitchiana

Miltonia regnellii var. veitchiana, commonly known as the Veitch's Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant produces pendulous blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, often with contrasting markings. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively ease of cultivation.
