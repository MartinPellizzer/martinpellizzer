Malcolmia heterophylla

Malcolmia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive blue to violet flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and vary in size. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
