Mammillaria acultzingensis

Mammillaria acultzingensis, commonly known as the Acultzingensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly bristles. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
