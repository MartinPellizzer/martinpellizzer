Musa homblei

Musa homblei, commonly known as the Hombli banana, is a species of banana plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of yellow, edible fruits that are typically harvested when they are fully mature. Musa homblei is known for its high nutritional value, containing significant amounts of potassium and dietary fiber.

It is often cultivated for both its fruit and its ornamental value in gardens.
