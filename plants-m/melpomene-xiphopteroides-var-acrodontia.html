Melpomene xiphopteroides var. acrodontia

Melpomene xiphopteroides var. acrodontia, commonly known as the Acrodontia lily, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and orange. The plant features strap-like, evergreen leaves that are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
