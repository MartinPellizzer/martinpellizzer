Masdevallia striatella

Masdevallia striatella, commonly known as the streaked masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant features long, narrow leaves and produces delicate, pendulous flowers with intricate patterns of stripes and spots. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Masdevallia striatella is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
