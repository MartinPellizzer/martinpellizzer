Magnolia sprengeri

Magnolia sprengeri, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the fragrant magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It features showy, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that develop into woody, oval capsules containing numerous seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
