Musa campestris var. limbangensis

Musa campestris var. limbangensis, commonly known as the Limbang banana, is a variant of the banana plant native to the highland regions of Borneo.

It is a herbaceous plant that grows in clusters, with tall, sturdy stems that bear large, broad leaves. The plant produces elongated, curved fruits that are typically harvested when they reach a mature yellow color. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its robust growth and potential use in both culinary and ornamental applications.
