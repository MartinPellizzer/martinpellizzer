Masdevallia kautskyi

Masdevallia kautskyi, commonly known as the Kautsky's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean region of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often brightly colored flowers with a unique, spurred lip. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions with dappled sunlight. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, fragrant blooms during the cooler months.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and challenging orchid to cultivate outside its native environment.
