Maranta pleiostachys

Maranta pleiostachys, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, glossy, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Maranta pleiostachys is also valued for its starchy roots, which can be used in cooking.
