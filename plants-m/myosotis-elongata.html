Myosotis elongata

Myosotis elongata, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Myosotis elongata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
