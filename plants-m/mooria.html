Mooria

Mooria, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a deciduous tree that produces clusters of yellow flowers, which bloom in the summer months. The plant has compound leaves with several small leaflets, and its flowers are often used in traditional medicine. Mooria is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for its fragrant blossoms.

Its botanical name is *Chionanthus retusus*.
