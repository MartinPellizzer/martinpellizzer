Miltonia regnellii purpurea

Miltonia regnellii purpurea, commonly known as the purple-veined orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It features showy, butterfly-shaped flowers with a distinctive purple-veined pattern on its white petals. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, adding a vibrant touch to greenhouses and conservatories.

The species is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its beauty and fragrance.
