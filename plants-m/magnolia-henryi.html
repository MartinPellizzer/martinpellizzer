Magnolia henryi

Magnolia henryi, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has a broad, rounded canopy and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and elliptical in shape, with a smooth margin.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
