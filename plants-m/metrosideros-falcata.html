Metrosideros falcata

Metrosideros falcata, commonly known as the New Zealand flame tree, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, reddish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves.
