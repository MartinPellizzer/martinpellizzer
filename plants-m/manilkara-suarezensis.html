Manilkara suarezensis

Manilkara suarezensis, commonly known as the Suarez's manilkara, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits containing a single seed. Its bark is thick and deeply fissured, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

The plant is also valued for its durable wood, which is used in construction and furniture making.
