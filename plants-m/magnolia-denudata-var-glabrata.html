Magnolia denudata var. glabrata

Magnolia denudata var. glabrata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the saucer magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth texture and dark green upper surface. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain winged seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
