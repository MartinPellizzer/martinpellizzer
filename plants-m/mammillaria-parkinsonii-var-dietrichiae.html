Mammillaria parkinsonii var. dietrichiae

Mammillaria parkinsonii var. dietrichiae, commonly known as the Desert Rose, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered with prominent, white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental spines make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
