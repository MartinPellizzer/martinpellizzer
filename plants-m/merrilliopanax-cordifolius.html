Merrilliopanax cordifolius

Merrilliopanax cordifolius, commonly known as the heart-leaved merrilliopanax, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which are typically glossy and have a deep green color. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by round, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
