Mascarenhasia macrocalyx

Mascarenhasia macrocalyx, commonly known as the Mascarene star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Mascarene Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has a slender, terrestrial growth habit with a single, erect stem bearing several inflorescences. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, and the flowers are known for their fragrant scent.

This species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and limited distribution.
