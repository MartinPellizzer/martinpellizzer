Miconia lorenzouribei

Miconia lorenzouribei, commonly known as the Lorena's miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. Its flowers are typically showy, with five to seven petals, and are often pink or purple in color.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, winged seeds, aiding in its dispersal by wind.
