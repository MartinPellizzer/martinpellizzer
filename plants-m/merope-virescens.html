Merope virescens

Merope virescens, commonly known as the green merope, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow, often blooming during the rainy season. Merope virescens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for both horticultural study and ecological research.
