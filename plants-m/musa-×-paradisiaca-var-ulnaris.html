Musa × paradisiaca var. ulnaris

Musa × paradisiaca var. ulnaris, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a variant of the banana plant characterized by its unique leaf morphology.

It features elongated, narrow leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, distinguishing it from other banana varieties. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that are not typically consumed. Its fruit is usually smaller and less sweet compared to common bananas, making it more ornamental than edible.

This variety is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a decorative plant.
