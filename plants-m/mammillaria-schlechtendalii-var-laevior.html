Mammillaria schlechtendalii var. laevior

Mammillaria schlechtendalii var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth mammillaria, is a cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems that are covered in small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green foliage. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
