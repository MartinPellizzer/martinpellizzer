Mikania fockeana var. pubescens

Mikania fockeana var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy Mikania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine, hairy pubescence, giving it its varietal name. Its small, yellowish flowers are typically arranged in clusters and bloom during the warmer months.

This species is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges due to its adaptability and aggressive growth.
