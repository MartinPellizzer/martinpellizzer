Metalasia humilis

Metalasia humilis, commonly known as the dwarf metalasia, is a small, herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and thrives in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are usually pale blue or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems.

Metalasia humilis is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
