Mammillaria densispina

Mammillaria densispina, commonly known as the dense-spined mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in dense, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and rock gardens.
