Mertensia virginica f. rosea

Mertensia virginica f. rosea, commonly known as the pink heartleaf bluebell, is a cultivar of the common bluebell.

It is characterized by its striking pink to lavender flowers, which are more intensely colored than those of the typical species. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green, providing a striking contrast to its floral display. It is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value in woodland gardens and native plant landscapes.
