Matayba discolor var. atropurpurea

Matayba discolor var. atropurpurea, commonly known as the purple-leaf matayba, is a variant of the matayba plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking purple foliage, which contrasts with its green stems and leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical in shape, and have a leathery texture.

The flowers are small, white or pale yellow, and are clustered in inflorescences, adding to its ornamental value.
