Micromeria cristata subsp. cristata

Micromeria cristata subsp.

cristata, commonly known as the mountain dittany, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a small shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, narrow leaves.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone habitats.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
