Menispermum radiatum

Menispermum radiatum, commonly known as the broadleaf arrowhead, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive yellowish flowers. The plant produces a yellow or orange fruit that splits open to reveal bright red seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found along stream banks and in woodlands.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
