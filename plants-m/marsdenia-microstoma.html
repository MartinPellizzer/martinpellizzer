Marsdenia microstoma

Marsdenia microstoma, commonly known as the small-stoma marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, typically blooming in the summer months. Its fruits are follicles that release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.

Marsdenia microstoma is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
