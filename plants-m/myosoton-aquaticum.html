Myosoton aquaticum

Myosoton aquaticum, commonly known as water starwort, is a small aquatic plant found in freshwater environments.

It has slender, branching stems that often float on the water surface or grow submerged. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and whorled, adapting to its aquatic habitat.

Myosoton aquaticum plays a role in stabilizing water bodies and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
