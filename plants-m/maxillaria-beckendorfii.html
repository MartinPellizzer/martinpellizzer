Maxillaria beckendorfii

Maxillaria beckendorfii, commonly known as the Beckendorf's orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces showy, dark purple to violet blooms with yellow and green markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria beckendorfii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
