Myosotis lithospermifolia

Myosotis lithospermifolia, commonly known as the stone root forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial native to China.

It features slender stems and produces small, blue to violet flowers with five petals. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in mountainous regions.

Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
