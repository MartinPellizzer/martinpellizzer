Masdevallia telloi

Masdevallia telloi, commonly known as the Tello's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and strap-like leaves that are dark green and glossy. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

Masdevallia telloi thrives in cool, humid environments and requires careful attention to light and moisture levels for optimal growth.
