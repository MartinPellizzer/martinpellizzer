Myoporum chinensis

Myoporum chinensis, commonly known as Chinese myoporum, is a shrub native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, grayish bark and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade.

Myoporum chinensis is also valued for its ability to tolerate coastal conditions and is sometimes used in erosion control.
