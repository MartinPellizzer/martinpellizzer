Melothria pringlei

Melothria pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's little cucumber, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers, squash, and gourds. The plant produces small, green, edible fruits that are often used in local cuisines. Its vines are trailing and can grow up to several meters in length.

The flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female blooms, a characteristic common in many members of the Cucurbitaceae family.
