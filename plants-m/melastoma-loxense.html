Melastoma loxense

Melastoma loxense, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, preferring moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough texture, while its fruits are capsule-like and contain several seeds.

Melastoma loxense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
