Myrcia sintenisii

Myrcia sintenisii, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to Brazil and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine, and it is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Myrcia sintenisii plays an important ecological role in its native habitat, supporting various insect and bird species.
