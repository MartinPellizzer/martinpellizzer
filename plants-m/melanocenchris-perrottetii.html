Melanocenchris perrottetii

Melanocenchris perrottetii, commonly known as the Perrottet's melastome, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its showy, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a distinctive, sometimes serrated edge.

The fruit is a capsule that disperses seeds through wind or animal activity.
