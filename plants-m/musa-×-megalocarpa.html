Musa × megalocarpa

Musa × megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant plantain, is a hybrid banana species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces clusters of large, elongated berries that are typically yellow when ripe. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
