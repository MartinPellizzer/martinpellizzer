Mayepea implicata

Mayepea implicata, commonly known as the tropical mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that grows on the branches of host trees in tropical and subtropical regions.

Mayepea implicata has a complex relationship with its host, drawing water and nutrients while also providing some ecological benefits to the ecosystem.
