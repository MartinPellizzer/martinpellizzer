Masdevallia calagrasalis

Masdevallia calagrasalis, commonly known as the Cloud Forest Orchid, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers with elongated petals and a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a slender stem and lanceolate leaves, which are adapted to its moist, cool habitat.

Due to its specific ecological requirements, it is considered a delicate and challenging species to cultivate.
