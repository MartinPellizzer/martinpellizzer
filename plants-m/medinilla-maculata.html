Medinilla maculata

Medinilla maculata, commonly known as the spotted medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces showy, waxy flowers that are often adorned with small, red or purple spots. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Medinilla maculata is popular as a ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
