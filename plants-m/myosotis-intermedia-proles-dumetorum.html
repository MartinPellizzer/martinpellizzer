Myosotis intermedia proles dumetorum

Myosotis intermedia proles dumetorum, commonly known as the woodland forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early to mid-summer. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
