Mimosa vernicosa

Mimosa vernicosa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant produces a sticky, resinous substance that gives it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa vernicosa is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and unique appearance.
