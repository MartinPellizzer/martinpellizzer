Maranta parkeri

Maranta parkeri, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage, with dark green leaves that have a velvety texture and intricate patterns. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and requires high humidity and indirect sunlight.

Maranta parkeri is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
