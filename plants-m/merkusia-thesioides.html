Merkusia thesioides

Merkusia thesioides, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.
