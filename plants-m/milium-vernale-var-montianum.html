Milium vernale var. montianum

Milium vernale var. montianum, commonly known as the alpine milkweed, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe.

It is a member of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or mountainous habitats.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
