Medicago justischmidtii

Medicago justischmidtii, commonly known as the Schmidt's vetch, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

Medicago justischmidtii is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
