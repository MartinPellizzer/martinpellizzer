Mesembryanthemum teretifolium

Mesembryanthemum teretifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, narrow leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically pink or purple, blooming in response to sunlight. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.

Mesembryanthemum teretifolium has a compact growth habit and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant.
