Maharanga emodi var. stelligera

Maharanga emodi var. stelligera, commonly known as the slender maharanga, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, preferring well-drained soils and sunny to partially shaded conditions.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
