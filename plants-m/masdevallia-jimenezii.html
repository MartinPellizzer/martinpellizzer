Masdevallia jimenezii

Masdevallia jimenezii, commonly known as the Jimenez's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are usually small, with a unique morphology that includes specialized petals and sepals.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
