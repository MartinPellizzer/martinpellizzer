Malcolmia × hybrida

Malcolmia × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid malva, is a flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves and showy, yellow-centered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, where it is often found in meadows and along roadsides.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms and easy maintenance.
