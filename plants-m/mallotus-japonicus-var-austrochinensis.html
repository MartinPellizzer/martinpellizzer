Mallotus japonicus var. austrochinensis

Mallotus japonicus var. austrochinensis, commonly known as the Chinese hibiscus or Chinese mallotus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often pubescent on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing numerous seeds. It is native to southeastern Asia, including regions of China, Japan, and the Philippines.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
