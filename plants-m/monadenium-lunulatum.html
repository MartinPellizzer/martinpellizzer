Monadenium lunulatum

Monadenium lunulatum, commonly known as the moonflower cactus, is a unique succulent native to South America.

It features a compact, globular stem with a distinctive pattern of white and green markings. The plant produces small, night-blooming flowers that are typically white or pale purple. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
