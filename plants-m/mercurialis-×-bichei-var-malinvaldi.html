Mercurialis × bichei var. malinvaldi

Mercurialis × bichei var. malinvaldi, commonly known as the Malinvald's mercury plant, is a hybrid species within the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy stems and leaves that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are clustered in cyathia, typical of the genus Mercurialis. It is native to southern Europe and thrives in dry, sunny environments.

Due to its hybrid nature, it exhibits variable morphology and is often found in rocky or arid habitats.
