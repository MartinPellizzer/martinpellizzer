Megalastrum longipilosum var. longipilosum

Megalastrum longipilosum var. longipilosum, commonly known as the long-hairy megalastrum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its hairy stems and leaves, which are covered in fine, long hairs. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its attractive foliage and blooms.
