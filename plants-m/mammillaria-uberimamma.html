Mammillaria uberimamma

Mammillaria uberimamma, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can reach up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant features white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. Its leaves are typically spineless and are covered in small, white tubercles.

It is a slow-growing plant that requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
