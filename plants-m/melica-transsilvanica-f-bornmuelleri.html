Melica transsilvanica f. bornmuelleri

Melica transsilvanica f. bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Transylvanian meadowgrass, is a variant of the species Melica transsilvanica, which belongs to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance and is typically found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges. The plant exhibits a compact growth form with narrow, linear leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. It is native to Central and Eastern Europe and is often associated with calcareous soils.
