Mimosa pubifera

Mimosa pubifera, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Mimosa pubifera is known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed sites.
