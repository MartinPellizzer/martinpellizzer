Millettia breviflora

Millettia breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered millettia, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a small, deciduous tree or shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale yellow. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several elliptical seeds.

Millettia breviflora is often found in disturbed sites and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
