Myoporum acuminatum var. ellipticum

Myoporum acuminatum var. ellipticum, commonly known as the narrowleaf myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. It is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to thrive in coastal conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for erosion control and as a drought-tolerant ornamental plant.
