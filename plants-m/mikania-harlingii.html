Mikania harlingii

Mikania harlingii, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and trailing habit. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.
