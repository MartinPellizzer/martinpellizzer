Medinilla minahassae

Medinilla minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa velvet tangerine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, showy flowers. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Medinilla minahassae is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and pleasant fragrance.
