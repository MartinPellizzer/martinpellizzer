Masdevallia tonii

Masdevallia tonii, commonly known as the Tonn's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful inflorescences. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces flowers in shades of pink, red, and white. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
