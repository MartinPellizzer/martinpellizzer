Masdevallia simula var. guatemalensis

Masdevallia simula var. guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan masdevallia, is a member of the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces striking, asymmetrical flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance.
