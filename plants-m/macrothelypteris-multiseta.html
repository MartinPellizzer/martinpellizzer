Macrothelypteris multiseta

Macrothelypteris multiseta, commonly known as the giant chain fern, is a large, tropical fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, arching fronds that can reach lengths of up to 6 feet. The plant has a robust rhizome system that allows it to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with numerous small, serrated leaflets.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to create a lush, green canopy in humid gardens.
