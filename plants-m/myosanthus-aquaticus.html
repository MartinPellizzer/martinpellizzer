Myosanthus aquaticus

Myosanthus aquaticus, commonly known as water hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in moist, sunny environments. It is often found near water bodies such as ponds and streams.

Myosanthus aquaticus is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to purify water through natural filtration.
