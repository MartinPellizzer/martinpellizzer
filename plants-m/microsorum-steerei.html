Microsorum steerei

Microsorum steerei, commonly known as the Steere's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in slow-moving freshwater environments. The plant has delicate, floating leaves that are typically divided into small, serrated segments. Its rhizomes are submerged and help anchor it in the substrate while absorbing nutrients.

Microsorum steerei is valued in aquascaping for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
