Myristica nivea

Myristica nivea, commonly known as the white nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by a unique fruit that contains a white, aromatic seed known as white nutmeg. The tree is closely related to the black nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans) and shares similar ecological and botanical features.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and aromatic properties.
