Myristica oligocarpa

Myristica oligocarpa, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruit that contains the valuable spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has large, glossy, ovate leaves. Its fruit, which ripens to a dark red color, contains a single seed surrounded by a yellowish aril.

The tree is also cultivated for its essential oils and has cultural and economic significance in regions where it is grown.
