Mesua rosea

Mesua rosea, commonly known as the rose apple or the red rose tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling roses. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, yellowish fruits that are edible but not widely consumed. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.

The tree is also valued in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
