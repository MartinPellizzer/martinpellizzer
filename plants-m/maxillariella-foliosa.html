Maxillariella foliosa

Maxillariella foliosa, commonly known as the leafy maxillariella, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage, with leaves that are typically broad and fleshy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and yellow, often clustered in a raceme. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
