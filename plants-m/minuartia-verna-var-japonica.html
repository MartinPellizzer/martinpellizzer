Minuartia verna var. japonica

Minuartia verna var. japonica, commonly known as the Japanese winter creeper, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to April. This variety is native to East Asia, including Japan, and is valued for its ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
