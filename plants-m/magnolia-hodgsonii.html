Magnolia hodgsonii

Magnolia hodgsonii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Magnolia hodgsonii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
