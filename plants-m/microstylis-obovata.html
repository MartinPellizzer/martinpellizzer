Microstylis obovata

Microstylis obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate microstylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and wetlands. The plant features oval-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and taper toward the tip. Its flowers are small and greenish-white, arranged in spikes, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Microstylis obovata is often found in sandy or loamy soils and is adapted to thrive in humid, temperate climates.
