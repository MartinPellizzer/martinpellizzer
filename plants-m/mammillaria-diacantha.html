Mammillaria diacantha

Mammillaria diacantha, commonly known as the zigzag cactus, is a species native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem with distinct, wavy ridges that give it its common name. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It has small, spiny areoles and is adapted to arid environments with minimal water requirements.

Mammillaria diacantha is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts due to its compact size and unique appearance.
