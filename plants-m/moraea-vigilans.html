Moraea vigilans

Moraea vigilans, commonly known as the night-blooming starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that open at night. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, violet or purple flowers with a distinctive central column. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Moraea vigilans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique blooming habit.
