Mammillaria crispiseta

Mammillaria crispiseta, commonly known as the crinkled mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for succulent collections.
