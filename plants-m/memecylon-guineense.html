Memecylon guineense

Memecylon guineense, commonly known as African wild tea, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some indigenous communities.
