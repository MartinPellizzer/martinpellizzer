Macrocnemum stipulaceum

Macrocnemum stipulaceum, commonly known as the stipulate macrocnemum, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves with distinct stipules. The plant produces clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. It is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
