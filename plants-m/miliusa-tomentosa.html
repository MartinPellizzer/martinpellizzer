Miliusa tomentosa

Miliusa tomentosa, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.
