Magnolia michauxii

Magnolia michauxii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or Michaux's magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
