Monolopia glabrata

Monolopia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth leaved daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and smooth, with a grayish-green hue, and it produces small, white to pale yellow flowers.

Monolopia glabrata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
