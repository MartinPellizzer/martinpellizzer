Mesembryanthemum horizontale

Mesembryanthemum horizontale, commonly known as the horizontal mesembryanthemum, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are typically bluish-green in color, often with a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in the early morning and close by midday. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soil with plenty of sunlight.

This species is popular in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
