Miconia rusbyana

Miconia rusbyana, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia rusbyana is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy flowers.
