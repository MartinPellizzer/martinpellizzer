Mastixia pentandra subsp. chinensis

Mastixia pentandra subsp.

chinensis, commonly known as Chinese mastix, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions.

The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, fragrant flowers that are often white or pale yellow. It produces fleshy, berry-like fruits that are used in traditional medicine and as a source of natural resins.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been used in various herbal remedies for centuries.
