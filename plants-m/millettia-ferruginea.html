Millettia ferruginea

Millettia ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty millettia, is a tropical climbing plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that are often clustered in racemose inflorescences. The plant produces legumes as its fruit, which contain several elliptical seeds. Native to regions of Southeast Asia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
