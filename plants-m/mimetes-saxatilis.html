Mimetes saxatilis

Mimetes saxatilis, commonly known as the rock mimetes, is a flowering plant native to the Western Cape of South Africa.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, cushion-like growth form. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to Mediterranean-type climates.

Mimetes saxatilis is often found in fynbos vegetation and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological significance.
