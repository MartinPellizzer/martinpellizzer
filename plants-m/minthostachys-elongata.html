Minthostachys elongata

Minthostachys elongata, commonly known as the slender mint.

It is a perennial herb native to South America, particularly found in regions of Brazil and Peru. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are aromatic and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, tubular, and typically violet or purple in color, arranged in dense spikes.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of essential oils.
