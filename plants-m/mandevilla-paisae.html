Mandevilla paisae

Mandevilla paisae, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and its ability to climb or trail along supports. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pale pink to deep red. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green, heart-shaped leaves that add visual contrast to the blooms.

Mandevilla paisae thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
