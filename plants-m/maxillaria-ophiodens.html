Maxillaria ophiodens

Maxillaria ophiodens, commonly known as the snake orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, snake-like appearance, with a slender, dark green stem and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinct, spicy scent. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil and bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria ophiodens is prized for its ornamental value and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
