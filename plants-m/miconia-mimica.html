Miconia mimica

Miconia mimica, commonly known as the mimic miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia mimica is notable for its ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments and is often found in dense forest understories.
