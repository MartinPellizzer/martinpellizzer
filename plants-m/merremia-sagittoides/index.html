Merremia Sagittoides

Merremia sagittoides is a vigorous climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. It is known for its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. This plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to cover trellises or fences. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth. Despite its beauty, it can become invasive if not properly managed.
