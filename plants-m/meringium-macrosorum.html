Meringium macrosorum

Meringium macrosorum, commonly known as the giant meringium, is a distinctive plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the family Meringiaceae and is native to specific regions in South America. The plant features elongated, strap-like leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a unique, waxy texture.

Meringium macrosorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens and specialty collections.
