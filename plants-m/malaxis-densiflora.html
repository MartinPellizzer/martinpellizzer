Malaxis densiflora

Malaxis densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered malaxis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, often epiphytic growth habit and possesses long, narrow leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive, hairy appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Due to its unique morphology and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
