Mediolobivia rubelliflora

Mediolobivia rubelliflora, commonly known as the crimson flame orchid, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which bloom in dense clusters during the wet season. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps it anchor in the moist, nutrient-rich soil of its natural habitat. Its leaves are elongated and waxy, providing a protective layer against excessive moisture.

Mediolobivia rubelliflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in botanical gardens.
