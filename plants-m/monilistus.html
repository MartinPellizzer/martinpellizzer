Monilistus

Monilistus, commonly known as the velvet tamarisk, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has a woody stem covered in a layer of fine, silky hairs, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Monilistus is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to withstand harsh environmental conditions.
