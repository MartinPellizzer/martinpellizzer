Monstera velloziana

Monstera velloziana, commonly known as the Velloziana monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its striking foliage. Its stems are typically supported by tendrils that allow it to climb toward light.

The leaves are usually a deep green with a slightly variegated pattern, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
