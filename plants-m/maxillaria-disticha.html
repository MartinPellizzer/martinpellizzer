Maxillaria disticha

Maxillaria disticha, commonly known as the twin-lobed orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria disticha is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
