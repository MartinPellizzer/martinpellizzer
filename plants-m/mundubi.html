Mundubi

Mundubi, commonly known as the African violet, is a popular ornamental plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its compact rosette growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant has thick, fleshy leaves that help it retain moisture in its natural habitat. It thrives in bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a favored choice for indoor gardening.

The Mundubi's ability to bloom repeatedly throughout the year adds to its appeal as a decorative plant.
