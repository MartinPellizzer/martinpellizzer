Metanarthecium luteoviride var. nutans

Metanarthecium luteoviride var. nutans, commonly known as the yellow-green nodding moss, is a species of moss characterized by its delicate, branching structure and vibrant yellow-green coloration.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or along rocky outcrops. The plant produces small, capsule-like spore cases that are typically positioned on the tips of its leaf-like structures. Its leaves are usually narrow and have a distinct midrib, contributing to its overall appearance.

This variety is notable for its unique growth habit and color, making it a distinctive member of the moss family.
