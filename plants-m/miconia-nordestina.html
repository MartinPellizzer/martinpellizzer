Miconia nordestina

Miconia nordestina, commonly known as the northeastern miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of eastern Brazil, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
