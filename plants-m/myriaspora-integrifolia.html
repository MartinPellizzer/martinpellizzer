Myriaspora integrifolia

Myriaspora integrifolia, commonly known as the smooth-leafed myriaspora, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, smooth leaves and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant belongs to the family Myriasporaceae and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, and it plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat for insects.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique morphology and ecological significance.
