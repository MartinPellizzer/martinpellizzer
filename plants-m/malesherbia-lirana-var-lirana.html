Malesherbia lirana var. lirana

Malesherbia lirana var. lirana, commonly known as the silverleaf mallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to South America.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
