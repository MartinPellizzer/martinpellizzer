Mammillaria olorina

Mammillaria olorina, commonly known as the golden mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and is covered in dense, white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
