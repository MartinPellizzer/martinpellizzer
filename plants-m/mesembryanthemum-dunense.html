Mesembryanthemum dunense

Mesembryanthemum dunense, commonly known as the Dun Mountain ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems and small, star-shaped flowers. Mesembryanthemum dunense is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
