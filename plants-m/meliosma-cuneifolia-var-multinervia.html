Meliosma cuneifolia var. multinervia

Meliosma cuneifolia var. multinervia, commonly known as the broad-leafed meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, dark green leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and woodlands. Its flowers are small, inconspicuous, and appear in clusters, while its fruit is a small, capsule-like structure.

It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in native plant gardens.
