Maranta bracteosa

Maranta bracteosa, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage and unique growth habits. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with red or purple veins. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow.

Maranta bracteosa is often grown as a houseplant due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
