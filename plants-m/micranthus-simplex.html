Micranthus simplex

Micranthus simplex, commonly known as the small-flowered grass.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere. The plant features slender, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Micranthus simplex is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
