Mertensia rupestris

Mertensia rupestris, commonly known as the rock mertensia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically from June to July. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine white or gray pubescence. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mertensia rupestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in rock gardens or alpine settings.
