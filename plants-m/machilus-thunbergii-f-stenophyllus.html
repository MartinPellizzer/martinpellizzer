Machilus thunbergii f. stenophyllus

Machilus thunbergii f. stenophyllus, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Chinese boxwood, is a variant of the Chinese boxwood species.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It is native to East Asia, particularly China, and thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil.

Due to its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage, it is often used in horticulture for landscaping and as a topiary plant.
