Modiolastrum geranioides

Modiolastrum geranioides, commonly known as the rock geranium, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces vibrant pink or red flowers that bloom in spring and summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
