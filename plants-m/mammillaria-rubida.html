Mammillaria rubida

Mammillaria rubida, commonly known as the red mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to grayish spines. The plant produces clusters of bright red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
