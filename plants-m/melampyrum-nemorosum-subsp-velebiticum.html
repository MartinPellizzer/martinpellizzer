Melampyrum nemorosum subsp. velebiticum

Melampyrum nemorosum subsp.

velebiticum, commonly known as the Velebit yellowtop, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orobanchaceae family. It is a hemiparasitic species, meaning it partially relies on its host plants for water and nutrients.
