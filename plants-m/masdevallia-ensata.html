Masdevallia ensata

Masdevallia ensata, commonly known as the elegant masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in clusters along the stem. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, with fleshy, strap-like leaves that help it survive in its high-altitude habitat. Its flowers are usually spurred and have a complex structure typical of the orchid family.

Masdevallia ensata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and adaptability to cooler, shaded environments.
