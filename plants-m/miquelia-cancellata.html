Miquelia cancellata

Miquelia cancellata, commonly known as the Chinese mallow, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and prominent veins. The plant produces showy, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Miquelia cancellata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some cultures.
