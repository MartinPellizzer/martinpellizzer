Masdevallia × pourbaixii

Masdevallia × pourbaixii, commonly known as the Pourbaix masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the cloud forests of South America, particularly in regions of Ecuador and Colombia. This plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often colorful flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The leaves are typically dark green and strap-like, while the pseudobulbs are short and stout.

It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires careful attention to watering and humidity levels for optimal growth.
