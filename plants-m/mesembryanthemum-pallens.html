Mesembryanthemum pallens

Mesembryanthemum pallens, commonly known as the pale ice plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Mesembryanthemum pallens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate drought conditions.
