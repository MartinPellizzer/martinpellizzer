Mammillaria theresae

Mammillaria theresae, commonly known as the Theresae Pincushion Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
