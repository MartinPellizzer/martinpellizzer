Melocactus rufispinus f. curvispinus

Melocactus rufispinus f. curvispinus, commonly known as the curly spine melocactus, is a rare and distinctive variant of the melocactus genus.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is typically green to reddish in color, with a smooth, spiny surface. The spines are notably curved, giving the plant its unique appearance. It is native to a limited region in Mexico, where it thrives in arid and rocky environments.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and requires careful care to maintain its health.
