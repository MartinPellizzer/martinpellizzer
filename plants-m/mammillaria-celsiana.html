Mammillaria celsiana

Mammillaria celsiana, commonly known as the Celsian Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
