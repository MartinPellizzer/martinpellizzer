Musa charlioi

Musa charlioi, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clustered, elongated fruit that is typically green when mature and turns yellow as it ripens. Musa charlioi is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.

It prefers warm, humid climates and well-drained soil for optimal growth.
