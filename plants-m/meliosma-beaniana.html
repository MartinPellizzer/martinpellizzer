Meliosma beaniana

Meliosma beaniana, commonly known as the Chinese meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other tropical trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody capsules containing several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and horticultural collections.
