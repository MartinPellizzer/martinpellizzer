Matricaria transcaucasica

Matricaria transcaucasica, commonly known as Caucasian chamomile, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with white centers. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as meadows and rocky areas. Its leaves are finely divided and aromatic, while its flowers bloom from late spring to early autumn.

Matricaria transcaucasica is valued for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
