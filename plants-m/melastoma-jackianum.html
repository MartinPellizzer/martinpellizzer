Melastoma jackianum

Melastoma jackianum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including regions of Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to red. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, dark seeds.
