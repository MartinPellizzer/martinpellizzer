Myosotis obovata

Myosotis obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its blue to violet petals, which are often marked with a yellow center, and its oval-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, delicate flowers that bloom in spring and early summer.

Myosotis obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
