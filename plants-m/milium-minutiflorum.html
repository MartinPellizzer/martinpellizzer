Milium minutiflorum

Milium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered milletgrass, is a low-growing perennial herb native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features tufted clusters of tiny, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, three-angled seed capsules. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as meadows and rocky areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable component of ecological and botanical studies.
