Malaxis umbelliflora

Malaxis umbelliflora, commonly known as the umbrella orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rhizomatous growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forests and rocky areas. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

The orchid is known for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
