Maireana rohrlachii

Maireana rohrlachii, commonly known as the golden maireana, is a shrub native to the arid regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, silver-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments, with a deep root system to access water. It is often found in sandy soils and is part of the Myrtaceae family.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
