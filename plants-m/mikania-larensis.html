Mikania larensis

Mikania larensis, commonly known as the creeping mikania, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America and is characterized by its rapid growth and trailing habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically visited by various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and often covered in fine hairs.

Mikania larensis is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges, where it can form dense mats.
