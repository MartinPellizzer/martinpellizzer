Microcitrus australis

Microcitrus australis, commonly known as the Australian lime, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It produces small, fragrant citrus fruits that are typically green when mature, though they can turn yellow or orange. The plant has small, dark green leaves and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and edible fruit. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Microcitrus australis is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators and its resilience to coastal conditions.
