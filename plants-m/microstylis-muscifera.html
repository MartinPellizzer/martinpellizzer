Microstylis muscifera

Microstylis muscifera, commonly known as the musk moss spike-rush, is a member of the Cyperaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or wetlands. The plant has slender, grass-like stems with small, scale-like leaves. Its flowers are unisexual, with male and female spikes occurring on separate plants.

Microstylis muscifera plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small invertebrates.
