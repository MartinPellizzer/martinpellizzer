Miconia tuckeri

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, showy bracts that often resemble petals. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, thriving in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.
