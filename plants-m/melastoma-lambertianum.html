Melastoma lambertianum

Melastoma lambertianum, commonly known as the Lambert's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often pink or red flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
