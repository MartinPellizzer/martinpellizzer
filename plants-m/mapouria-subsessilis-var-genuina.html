Mapouria subsessilis var. genuina

Mapouria subsessilis var. genuina, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Mapouria subsessilis var. genuina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
