Maxillaria haberi

Maxillaria haberi, commonly known as the Haber's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and pseudobulbs that store water, aiding in its survival in seasonal environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with yellow markings, and they bloom during the dry season.

Maxillaria haberi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
