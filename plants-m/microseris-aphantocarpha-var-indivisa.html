Microseris aphantocarpha var. indivisa

Microseris aphantocarpha var. indivisa, commonly known as the slender sea rocket, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, white or pale yellow flowers and linear, grass-like leaves. This variety is typically found in coastal areas, where it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits containing numerous seeds.

It plays a role in stabilizing dune ecosystems and is often used in ecological restoration projects.
