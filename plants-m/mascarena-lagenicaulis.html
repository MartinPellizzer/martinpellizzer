Mascarena lagenicaulis

Mascarena lagenicaulis, commonly known as the Cape fennel, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, feathery leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in coastal areas and is adapted to sandy soils. It is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.

Its unique morphology and ecological niche make it an interesting subject for botanical study.
