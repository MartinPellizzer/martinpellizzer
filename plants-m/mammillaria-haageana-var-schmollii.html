Mammillaria haageana var. schmollii

Mammillaria haageana var. schmollii, commonly known as the Schmollii Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with numerous small, rounded tubercles that bear white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has a shallow root system and thrives in well-draining soil with ample sunlight. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal watering, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
