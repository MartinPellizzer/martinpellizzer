Miconia polystachya

Miconia polystachya, commonly known as the showy miconia, is a tropical plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
