Majorana × majorica

Majorana × majorica, commonly known as the hybrid marjoram, is a cross between Majorana hortensis and Majorana syriaca.

It is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features aromatic, lance-shaped leaves with a strong, spicy fragrance. Its flowers are small, clustered in loose spikes, and typically bloom in shades of pink or white.

It is often cultivated for its culinary and medicinal uses due to its essential oils and therapeutic properties.
