Malya tenuis

Malya tenuis, commonly known as the slender malya, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically pink or white in color. Malya tenuis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
