Malvastrum roseum

Malvastrum roseum, commonly known as the roseum mallow, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a soft, fuzzy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Malvastrum roseum is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
