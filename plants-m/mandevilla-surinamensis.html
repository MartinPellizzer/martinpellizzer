Mandevilla surinamensis

Mandevilla surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam mandevilla, is a vigorous evergreen shrub or climber native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped, fragrant flowers that range from pink to red. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to autumn.

Mandevilla surinamensis is a popular choice for gardeners due to its adaptability and ability to climb or trail in various settings.
