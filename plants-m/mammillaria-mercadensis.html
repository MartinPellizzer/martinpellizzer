Mammillaria mercadensis

Mammillaria mercadensis, commonly known as the Mercado's bunny ear cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant typically produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively low maintenance, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
