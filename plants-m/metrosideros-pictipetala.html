Metrosideros pictipetala

Metrosideros pictipetala, commonly known as the Kauai Hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Hawaii.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to pink and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy appearance.
