Masdevallia syringodes

Masdevallia syringodes, commonly known as the Christmas Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often brightly colored floral structures. The plant typically produces a single, showy inflorescence with flowers that are adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting hues. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and dappled sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
