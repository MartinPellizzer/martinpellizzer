Masdevallia trechsliniana

Masdevallia trechsliniana, commonly known as the Trechslin's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces striking, pendulous flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging but rewarding plant for orchid enthusiasts.
