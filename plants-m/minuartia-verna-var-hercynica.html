Minuartia verna var. hercynica

Minuartia verna var. hercynica, commonly known as the spring rockcress, is a flowering plant in the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 2 to 5 centimeters. The plant features dense, mat-forming growth with small, bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

This variety is often found in rocky or gravelly soils in alpine and subalpine regions.
