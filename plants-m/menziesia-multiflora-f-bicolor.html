Menziesia multiflora f. bicolor

Menziesia multiflora f. bicolor, commonly known as the white-flowered twinflower, is a rare and unique variant of the Menziesia multiflora species.

It is characterized by its striking white flowers, which contrast sharply with its dark green foliage. This plant is native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its botanical features include small, elliptical leaves and clustered inflorescences.

The f. bicolor form is distinguished by its pale floral coloration, making it a notable ornamental and ecological species in its native habitat.
