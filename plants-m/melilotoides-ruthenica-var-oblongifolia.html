Melilotoides ruthenica var. oblongifolia

Melilotoides ruthenica var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed melilot, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-like fruits. The plant typically grows in meadows and grasslands, preferring well-drained soils. Its flowers are small and yellowish, arranged in racemes, and bloom in early summer.
