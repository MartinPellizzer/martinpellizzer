Maxillaria vallecaucana

Maxillaria vallecaucana, commonly known as the Vallecaucana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and linear leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration, typically in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria vallecaucana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
