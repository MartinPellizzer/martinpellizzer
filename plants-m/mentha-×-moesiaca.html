Mentha × moesiaca

Mentha × moesiaca, commonly known as the Hungarian mint, is a hybrid perennial herb in the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its aromatic, serrated leaves and clusters of small, pale purple to white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It is valued for its strong mint aroma and is often used in herbal teas and culinary applications.

Mentha × moesiaca is also noted for its vigorous growth and ability to spread through rhizomes.
