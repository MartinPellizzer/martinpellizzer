Masdevallia sanctae-fidei

Masdevallia sanctae-fidei, commonly known as the Saint Faith masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures and strikingly patterned leaves. This plant thrives in cool, humid environments with dappled sunlight, making it a challenging species for cultivation. Its unique morphology and limited distribution contribute to its status as a botanical curiosity.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
