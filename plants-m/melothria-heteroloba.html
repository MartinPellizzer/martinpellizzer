Melothria heteroloba

Melothria heteroloba, commonly known as the African sour fig, is a climbing shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and gourds, and is characterized by its tendrils and lobed leaves. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
