Mikania oniaensis

Mikania oniaensis, commonly known as the Brazilian Mikania, is a climbing herbaceous plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by fluffy, white seed heads.

Mikania oniaensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties.
