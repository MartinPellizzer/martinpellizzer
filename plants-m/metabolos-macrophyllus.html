Metabolos macrophyllus

Metabolos macrophyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf metabolos, is a flowering plant species characterized by its large, broad leaves.

It belongs to the family Metabolaceae and is native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant exhibits a robust root system that helps it thrive in moist, well-drained soils. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, with vibrant colors that attract pollinators.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
