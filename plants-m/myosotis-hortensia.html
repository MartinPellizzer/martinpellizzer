Myosotis hortensia

Myosotis hortensia, commonly known as the garden forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 10 to 30 centimeters and has heart-shaped, hairy leaves. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it suitable for woodland gardens.

Myosotis hortensia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
