Merremia gemella var. splendens

Merremia gemella var. splendens, commonly known as the golden shower vine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in long, pendulous clusters. The leaves are heart-shaped and often have a velvety texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a decorative element in landscapes.
