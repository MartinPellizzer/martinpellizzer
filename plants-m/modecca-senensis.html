Modecca senensis

Modecca senensis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. It is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care in indoor environments.
