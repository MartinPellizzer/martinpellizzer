Mammillaria magallanii

Mammillaria magallanii, commonly known as the Magallanes hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, cylindrical stem and dense, white radial spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
