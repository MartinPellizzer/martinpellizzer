Mesosphaerum lantanifolium

Mesosphaerum lantanifolium, commonly known as the fragrant leaf mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments within tropical and subtropical forests. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and a woody stem structure.

It is valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
