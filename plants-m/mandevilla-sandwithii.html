Mandevilla sandwithii

Mandevilla sandwithii, commonly known as the sandwithii mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are broad and ovate, with a glossy texture that adds to its ornamental appeal.

Mandevilla sandwithii is often cultivated as a climbing plant or in containers due to its rapid growth and adaptability to various climates.
