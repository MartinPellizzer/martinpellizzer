Miconia nutans

Miconia nutans, commonly known as the drooping miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that often have a waxy texture. Its ecological impact is significant, as it can become invasive in certain regions.
