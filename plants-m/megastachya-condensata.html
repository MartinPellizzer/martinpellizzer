Megastachya condensata

Megastachya condensata, commonly known as the condensed spikegrass, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescence composed of numerous small, pale green to brownish flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and swampy areas. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and the plant produces small, wind-pollinated flowers.

Megastachya condensata is part of the Poaceae family, which includes grasses and related plants.
