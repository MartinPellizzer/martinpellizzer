Miconia yunquensis

Miconia yunquensis, commonly known as the Yunquensis Miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, specifically Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically in cloud forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
