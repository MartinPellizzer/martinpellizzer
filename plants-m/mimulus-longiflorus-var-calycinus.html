Mimulus longiflorus var. calycinus

Mimulus longiflorus var. calycinus, commonly known as the California monkeyflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features showy, bright orange to yellow flowers that bloom from spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along streambanks and in open woodlands. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance as a nectar source for pollinators.
