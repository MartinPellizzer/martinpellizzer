Masdevallia laucheana

Masdevallia laucheana, commonly known as the Lauche's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces a single, showy flower with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
