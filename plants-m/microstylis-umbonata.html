Microstylis umbonata

Microstylis umbonata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or umbrella plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features broad, ovate leaves with a velvety texture and a distinctive umbellate inflorescence. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbrella-like structures. Its roots are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.
