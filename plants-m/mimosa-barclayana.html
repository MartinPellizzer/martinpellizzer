Mimosa barclayana

Mimosa barclayana, commonly known as the golden mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its sensitive leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait called thigmonastic response. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa barclayana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
