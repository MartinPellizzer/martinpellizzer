Meriania glabra

Meriania glabra, commonly known as the smooth meriania, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has smooth, glabrous leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
