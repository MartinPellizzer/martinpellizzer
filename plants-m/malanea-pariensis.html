Malanea pariensis

Malanea pariensis, commonly known as the Malayan parietal vine, is a climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Malvaceae, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces small, pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.
