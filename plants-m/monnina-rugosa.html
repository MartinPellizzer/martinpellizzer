Monnina rugosa

Monnina rugosa, commonly known as rough monnina, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Polygalaceae and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy, rugose (wrinkled) leaves. Monnina rugosa is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used by indigenous peoples for various purposes.
