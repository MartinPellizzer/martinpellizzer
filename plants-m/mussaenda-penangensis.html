Mussaenda penangensis

Mussaenda penangensis, commonly known as the Penang Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its bright yellow inflorescences. The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
