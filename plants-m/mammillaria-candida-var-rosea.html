Mammillaria candida var. rosea

Mammillaria candida var. rosea, commonly known as the roseate mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces small, vibrant pink or rose-colored flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
