Mentha × calaminthoides

Mentha × calaminthoides, commonly known as narrow-leaf peppermint, is a hybrid mint species that combines traits from several Mentha species.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and a strong, refreshing menthol scent. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often found in temperate regions and is valued for its aromatic properties and use in herbal teas.

Mentha × calaminthoides is also used in traditional medicine for its purported cooling and digestive benefits.
