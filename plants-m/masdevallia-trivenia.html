Masdevallia trivenia

Masdevallia trivenia, commonly known as the Three-veined Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces intricate, spurred orchid flowers with a wide range of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia trivenia thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light intensity for optimal growth.
