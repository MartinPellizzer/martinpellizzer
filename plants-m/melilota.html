Melilota

Melilota, commonly known as sweet clover, is a perennial legume belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it valuable in soil enrichment. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Melilota is often used in forage and as a cover crop due to its hardy nature and ecological benefits.
