Malva tenuifolia

Malva tenuifolia, commonly known as slender mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Malva tenuifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and traditional medicinal uses.
