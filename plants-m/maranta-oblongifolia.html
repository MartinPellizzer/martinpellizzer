Maranta oblongifolia

Maranta oblongifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns of red and silver. The plant produces small, delicate white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

Its ornamental value is further enhanced by its ability to retain its vibrant foliage year-round.
