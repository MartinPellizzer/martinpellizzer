Matthiola damascena f. ecbatanensis

Matthiola damascena f. ecbatanensis, commonly known as the Ecbatana stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, four-petaled flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces small, cylindrical seed pods that contain numerous tiny seeds. Native to the region of Ecbatana, which is in present-day Iran, it thrives in temperate climates.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical significance in horticulture.
