Microcerasus Microcarpa Var. Diffusa

Microcerasus microcarpa var. diffusa is a rare and lesser-known species of flowering plant belonging to the Ericaceae family. Native to the mountainous regions of China, this variant exhibits unique morphological characteristics that distinguish it from its parent species. Its small, clustered flowers and evergreen foliage make it an intriguing subject for botanical study. Despite its beauty, it remains under documented and largely overlooked in horticultural circles. This article aims to shed light on its ecological significance and potential for cultivation.
