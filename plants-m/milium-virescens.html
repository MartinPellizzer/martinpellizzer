Milium virescens

Milium virescens, commonly known as green millet, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It belongs to the grass family, Poaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in spikelets, typical of the Poaceae family. It is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and wetlands.

Milium virescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
