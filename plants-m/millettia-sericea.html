Millettia sericea

Millettia sericea, commonly known as the silky millettia, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, silky-haired leaves and small, clustered yellow flowers. The plant produces elongated pods containing flattened, brown seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Millettia sericea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
