Musa acuminata var. zebrina

Musa acuminata var. zebrina, commonly known as the zebra banana, is a variant of the banana plant characterized by its striking striped leaves.

This tropical plant belongs to the Musaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. Its leaves display a unique pattern of light and dark green stripes, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellowish-green bananas that ripen to a golden hue.

While primarily cultivated for ornamental purposes, it can also yield edible fruit under suitable conditions.
