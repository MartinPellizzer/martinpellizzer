Mammillaria wildii

Mammillaria wildii, commonly known as the White Lady Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.

Mammillaria wildii requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.
