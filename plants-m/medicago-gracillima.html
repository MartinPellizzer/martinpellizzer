Medicago gracillima

Medicago gracillima, commonly known as slender medic.

It is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia, characterized by its delicate, slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Medicago gracillima is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix nitrogen through symbiotic relationships with rhizobia bacteria.
