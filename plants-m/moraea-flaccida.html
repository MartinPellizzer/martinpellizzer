Moraea flaccida

Moraea flaccida, commonly known as the soft moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom in late summer. Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ease of care.
