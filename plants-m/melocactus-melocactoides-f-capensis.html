Melocactus melocactoides f. capensis

Melocactus melocactoides f. capensis, commonly known as the Cape melocactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to South Africa.

It features a spiny, ribbed stem that is typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are borne on short, erect pedicels. It is well adapted to arid environments, with a shallow root system and the ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stem.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
