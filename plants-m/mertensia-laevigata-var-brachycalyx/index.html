Mertensia Laevigata Var. Brachycalyx

Mertensia laevigata var. brachycalyx is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the eastern united states. it belongs to the borage family and is known for its striking blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring. this variant is distinguished by its shorter calyx compared to other varieties of mertensia. it thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
