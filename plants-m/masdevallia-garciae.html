Masdevallia garciae

Masdevallia garciae, commonly known as the Garcia's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and partial shade.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and specialized orchid species.
