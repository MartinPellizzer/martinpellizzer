Mammillaria deficum

Mammillaria deficum, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to bluish-gray body covered in white or pale spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
