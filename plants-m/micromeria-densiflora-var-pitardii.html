Micromeria densiflora var. pitardii

Micromeria densiflora var. pitardii, commonly known as the Pitard's denseflowered micromeria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a desirable species for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

It is also valued for its potential in traditional herbal medicine.
