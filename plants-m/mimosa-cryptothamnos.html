Mimosa cryptothamnos

Mimosa cryptothamnos, commonly known as the sensitive plant or mimic plant, is a species native to South America.

It is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Its unique sensitivity to touch makes it a popular subject in botanical studies and horticulture.
