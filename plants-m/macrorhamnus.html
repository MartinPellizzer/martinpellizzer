Macrorhamnus

Macrorhamnus, commonly known as the goldenbush, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Rhamnaceae.

It consists of several species, primarily found in the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plants are shrubs with small, often yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the spring. They have simple, alternate leaves that are usually leathery and glossy.

The fruits are small, dry capsules that contain several seeds, typical of the Rhamnaceae family.
