Masdevallia velutina

Masdevallia velutina, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety-textured flowers that range in color from deep purples to soft pinks. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded forest environments, often on tree trunks or rocks. Its leaves are fleshy and dark green, adapted to conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

Masdevallia velutina is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
