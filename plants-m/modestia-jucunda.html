Modestia jucunda

Modestia jucunda, commonly known as the joyful modesty, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, oval-shaped leaves that are typically a vibrant green, often with a slight sheen. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for woodland gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and ease of care, it is often cultivated in both garden and indoor settings.
