Mandevilla hatschbachii

Mandevilla hatschbachii, commonly known as the yellow mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous, evergreen vine that produces clusters of vibrant yellow flowers throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow quite long, making it suitable for trellises or as a ground cover. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun for optimal growth.

Mandevilla hatschbachii is prized for its ornamental value and ability to add color to gardens and landscapes.
