Matricaria globifera

Matricaria globifera, commonly known as the globe daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and ragworts. The plant is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and small, white or yellow flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are gray-green and covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance.

Matricaria globifera is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soil.
