Manglietia crassifolia

Manglietia crassifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf manglietia, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Magnoliaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. It is often found in humid, shaded forest environments.
