Madhuca stylosa

Madhuca stylosa, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves. The tree produces fragrant, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
