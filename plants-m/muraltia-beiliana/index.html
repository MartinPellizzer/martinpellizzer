Muraltia Beiliana

Coralbean, scientifically known as Muraltia beiliana, is a striking flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is prized for its vibrant, coral-pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice among horticulturists and garden enthusiasts. With proper care, Coralbean can add a splash of color and visual interest to any landscape.
