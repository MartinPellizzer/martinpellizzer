Mezobromelia fulgens

Mezobromelia fulgens, commonly known as the fiery bromeliad, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Bromeliaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy inflorescences that can range from red to orange. The plant features a rosette of narrow, waxy leaves that form a central cup, which collects water and supports small aquatic organisms. Mezobromelia fulgens thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
