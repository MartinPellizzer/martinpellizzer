Masdevallia ricii

Masdevallia ricii, commonly known as the Ricii Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically produces intricate, spurred blooms that are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Masdevallia ricii thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light and temperature conditions for optimal growth.
