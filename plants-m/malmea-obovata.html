Malmea obovata

Malmea obovata, commonly known as the broad-leafed malmea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in coastal or mountainous regions. The plant features oval, leathery leaves that are usually gray-green in color. Its flowers are small, clustered, and typically white or pale pink, appearing in spring.

Malmea obovata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature.
