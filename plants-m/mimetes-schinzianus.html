Mimetes schinzianus

Mimetes schinzianus, commonly known as the Schinz's mimetes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow to orange flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in fynbos vegetation. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Mimetes schinzianus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
