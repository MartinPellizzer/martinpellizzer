Maranta micans

Maranta micans, commonly known as the silver sword or creeping arrowroot, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that often display a metallic sheen. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Maranta micans is a herbaceous perennial that thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its ornamental value lies primarily in its attractive foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and shaded garden settings.
