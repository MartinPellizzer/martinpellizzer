Mallotus arboreus var. platyphyllus

Mallotus arboreus var. platyphyllus, commonly known as the broadleaf mallotus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white flowers followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Its foliage makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and ecological restoration projects.
