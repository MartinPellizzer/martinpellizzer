Masdevallia gomeziana

Masdevallia gomeziana, commonly known as the Gomezian masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically produces small, elongated pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually adorned with intricate patterns and vivid hues, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled environments to ensure its survival.
