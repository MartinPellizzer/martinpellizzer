Mikania confertissima

Mikania confertissima, commonly known as the giant hempvine, is a vigorous climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and extensive root system. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are heart-shaped with serrated edges and can grow quite large.

Mikania confertissima is often considered an invasive species in regions where it is introduced.
