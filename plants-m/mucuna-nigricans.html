Mucuna nigricans

Mucuna nigricans, commonly known as black urd, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive, bean-like pods that contain several seeds. The plant is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It produces a significant amount of biomass, making it useful for soil enrichment and as a forage crop.

Additionally, it has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
