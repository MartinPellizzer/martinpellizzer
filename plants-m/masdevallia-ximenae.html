Masdevallia ximenae

Masdevallia ximenae, commonly known as the Ximenae masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant produces pendulous, showy flowers that are typically violet or purple in color, with intricate patterns and a strong fragrance. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its beauty and rarity, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
