Millettia erythrocalyx

Millettia erythrocalyx, commonly known as the red calyx millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive red calyx that persists on the fruit. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are pinnately compound, and it produces small, clustered flowers that are often white or pale yellow.

The fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
