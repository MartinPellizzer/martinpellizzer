Macowania abyssinica

Macowania abyssinica, commonly known as Abyssinian macaw, is a tropical plant native to the highlands of Ethiopia.

It is characterized by its striking blue and yellow plumage, which serves as a warning to potential predators. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that are edible for both humans and animals. Its seeds are often dispersed by birds, contributing to its spread across the region.

The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to flourish.
