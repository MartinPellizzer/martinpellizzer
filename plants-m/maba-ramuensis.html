Maba ramuensis

Maba ramuensis, commonly known as the Ramu maba, is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of New Guinea and parts of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and small, clustered flowers that are often yellow or green. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, aiding in its dispersal.
