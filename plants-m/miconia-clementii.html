Miconia clementii

Miconia clementii, commonly known as the Brazilian cherry or purple miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces showy, clustered white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year in warm climates. Its fruit is a small, round, red berry that contains several seeds.

Miconia clementii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and floral display.
