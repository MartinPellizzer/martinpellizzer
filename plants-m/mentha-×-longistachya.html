Mentha × longistachya

Mentha × longistachya, commonly known as the hybrid mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It is a cross between Mentha haplocalyx and Mentha arvensis, resulting in a plant with strong aromatic properties. The leaves are lance-shaped, serrated, and have a distinctive menthol scent. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

This plant is valued for its use in herbal medicine and as a flavoring agent.
