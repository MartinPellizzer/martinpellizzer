Microchirita oculata

Microchirita oculata, commonly known as the eye-leaf plant, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, succulent leaves that often feature a distinctive eye-like pattern or marking. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments and prefers well-draining soil. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Microchirita oculata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
