Mammillaria longimamma f. melaleuca

Mammillaria longimamma f. melaleuca, commonly known as the Melaleuca mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with prominent, white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces small, vibrant orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
