Memecylon pusilliflorum

Memecylon pusilliflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, with five petals and numerous stamens. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
