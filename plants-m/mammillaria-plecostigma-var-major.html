Mammillaria plecostigma var. major

Mammillaria plecostigma var. major, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 30 inches in height. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the summer months. Its stems are covered in white, woolly spines and are often ribbed.

It is a popular choice for collectors due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
