Malcolmia littorea var. goffartii

Malcolmia littorea var. goffartii, commonly known as the coastal malva, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features upright stems covered in soft, velvety hairs, and its leaves are heart-shaped with wavy margins. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom from late spring to early autumn. It thrives in coastal areas, tolerating salt spray and sandy soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and erosion control.
