Maesa rufescens

Maesa rufescens, commonly known as the red-stemmed maesa, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers and evergreen leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as mountain forests. Its red-brown stems are a distinguishing feature, and it produces small, edible berries.
