Masdevallia coccinea var. lindenii

Masdevallia coccinea var. lindenii, commonly known as the red-tipped masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It features vibrant crimson petals with contrasting white and pink markings, making it a visually appealing species. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often during the cooler months, and it requires high humidity and indirect light to thrive.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and elegance.
