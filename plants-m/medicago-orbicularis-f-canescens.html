Medicago orbicularis f. canescens

Medicago orbicularis f. canescens, commonly known as the woolly globe pea, is a variant of the globe pea species characterized by its distinctive, rounded, and hairy seed pods.

This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, sunny environments. Its leaves are trifoliate, with small, rounded leaflets, and it produces small, yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is often found in disturbed soils and is adapted to arid conditions, making it a resilient species in Mediterranean climates.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.
