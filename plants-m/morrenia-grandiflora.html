Morrenia grandiflora

Morrenia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered morrenia, is a striking plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 feet tall, featuring thick, woody stems and large, showy yellow flowers. The leaves are typically alternate, simple, and have a rough, hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in dry, rocky habitats.

The plant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
