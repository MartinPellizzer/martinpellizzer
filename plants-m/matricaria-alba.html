Matricaria alba

Matricaria alba, commonly known as German chamomile, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its daisy-like white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom in the summer months. The plant has feathery, aromatic leaves that are often used in herbal teas. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular garden plant.

Matricaria alba is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its calming and anti-inflammatory effects.
