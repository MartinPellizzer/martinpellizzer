Massonia visserae

Massonia visserae, commonly known as the Visser's massonia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are thick and fleshy, storing water to help it survive in dry conditions.

It produces small, star-shaped flowers that are often yellow or orange in color.
