Mesua megalocarpa

Mesua megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Mesua megalocarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
