Mangifera indica var. mekongensis

Mangifera indica var. mekongensis, commonly known as the Mekong mango, is a tropical fruit tree native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, elongated mangoes with a smooth, thin skin that ranges in color from yellow to orange. The fruit has a sweet and aromatic flavor with a fibrous texture, making it highly valued in local markets. This variety thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated for both commercial production and traditional use in regional cuisines.
