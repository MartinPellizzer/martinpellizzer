Mazinna

Mazinna, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the genus Saintpaulia and is characterized by its vibrant, velvety petals and compact rosette of dark green leaves. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers warm, humid environments. Mazinna is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

It produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of purple, blue, and white, often in clusters.
