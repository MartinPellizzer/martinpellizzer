Microstylis polyodon

Microstylis polyodon, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as wetlands and forest floors. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female structures, a common feature in many Cyperaceae species.

Microstylis polyodon plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
