Masdevallia chimaera var. severa

Masdevallia chimaera var. severa, commonly known as the Severa masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and intricate, spurred flowers that range in color from deep purples to vibrant reds. The plant thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, making it a challenging but rewarding species for experienced growers. Its unique morphology and seasonal blooming habits make it a prized addition to botanical collections.

Due to its limited natural habitat, conservation efforts are crucial for its survival.
