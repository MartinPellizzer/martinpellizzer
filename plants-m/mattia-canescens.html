Mattia canescens

Mattia canescens, commonly known as the silverleaf daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-white leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies, sunflowers, and thistles. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Mattia canescens is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
