Microstylis maclaudii

Microstylis maclaudii, commonly known as the Maclaud's microstylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae, or sedge family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typical of the Cyperaceae family. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands or along stream banks.
