Mauritia nannostachys

Mauritia nannostachys, commonly known as the dwarf palm, is a small, compact palm native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a slender, unbranched stem that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences with yellowish-white flowers, followed by dark red, fleshy fruits. Its leaves are pinnately compound, with long, narrow leaflets that give it a feathery appearance.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates.
