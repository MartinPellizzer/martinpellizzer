Mentha × ivesii

Mentha × ivesii, commonly known as the hybrid mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It is a cross between spearmint (Mentha spicata) and watermint (Mentha aquatica), resulting in a plant with distinctive aromatic leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm, featuring square stems and opposite, serrated leaves. Its flowers are small, tubular, and clustered in whorls, producing a mild mint scent.

Mentha × ivesii is often found in moist, shaded environments such as stream banks and woodland edges.
