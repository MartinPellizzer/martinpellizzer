Matelea laevis

Matelea laevis, commonly known as the smooth matelea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is native to Mexico and parts of Central America, where it typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
