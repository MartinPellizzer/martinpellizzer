Mahonia oiwakensis var. tenuifoliola

Mahonia oiwakensis var. tenuifoliola, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to specific regions of China.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive dark green color and serrated edges, which contribute to its ornamental value. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers in late spring, followed by blue-black berries that are attractive to birds. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or woodland settings.

Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to both garden and conservation landscapes.
