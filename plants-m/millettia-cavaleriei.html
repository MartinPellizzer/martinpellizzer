Millettia cavaleriei

Millettia cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, trailing vines and clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.

Millettia cavaleriei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
