Meterana

Meterana, commonly known as the white mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Meterana is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its delicate and ethereal look.
