Mallotus oblongifolius var. helferi

Mallotus oblongifolius var. helferi, commonly known as the Helfer's broadleaf mallot, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its elongated, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in lowland forests and along riverbanks.
