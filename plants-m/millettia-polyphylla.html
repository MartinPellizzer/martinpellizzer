Millettia polyphylla

Millettia polyphylla, commonly known as the Chinese silk tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are often pinnately arranged and can be quite large. The tree produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pink, arranged in clustered inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several seeds, and it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.

The plant is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its potential health benefits.
