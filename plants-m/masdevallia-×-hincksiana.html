Masdevallia × hincksiana

Masdevallia × hincksiana, commonly known as the Hincks' masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower structures with specialized sepals and petals. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, thriving in cool, humid environments. Its leaves are usually dark green and strap-like, adapting to its high-altitude habitat.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique floral displays.
