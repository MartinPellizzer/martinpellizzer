Mammillaria orestera

Mammillaria orestera, commonly known as the Orestera Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant features prominent areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria orestera is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
