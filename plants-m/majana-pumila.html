Majana pumila

Majana pumila, commonly known as the dwarf majana, is a small shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus plants, and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small yellow flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 30 centimeters and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Majana pumila is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
