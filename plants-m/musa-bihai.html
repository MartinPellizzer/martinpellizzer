Musa bihai

Musa bihai, commonly known as the pink banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The fruit, which is typically not eaten due to its high acidity, is often used for ornamental purposes. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

It is a member of the Musaceae family and is closely related to the common banana.
