Maripa spectabilis

Maripa spectabilis, commonly known as the showy maripa, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in open areas with well-drained soil. Its striking yellow and orange flowers are arranged in a cluster, making it a visually appealing ornamental plant.

Maripa spectabilis is also valued for its ecological role, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and birds.
