Melastoma ornatum

Melastoma ornatum, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth, dark green upper surface and a velvety underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
