Mammillaria schaeferi

Mammillaria schaeferi, commonly known as the Schaefer's nipple cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a ribbed stem and spines that are often white or yellow. The flowers are typically yellow or orange and bloom in the spring. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

This plant is relatively easy to care for, making it a favorite among cactus enthusiasts.
