Melochia clinopodium

Melochia clinopodium, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a climbing shrub native to Central and South America.

It features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in the morning. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.

Melochia clinopodium is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
