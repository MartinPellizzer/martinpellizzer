Monelasmum cupreum

Monelasmum cupreum, commonly known as copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its attractive, copper-colored leaves that change hue with the seasons. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping in arid climates.

Due to its ornamental foliage, it is often cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
