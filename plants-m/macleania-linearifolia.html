Macleania linearifolia

Macleania linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves. The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, often found in humid, shaded environments.
