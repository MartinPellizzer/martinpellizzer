Magnolia nicholsoniana

Magnolia nicholsoniana, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the white magnolia, is a species native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It is a deciduous tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall.

Its botanical features make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
