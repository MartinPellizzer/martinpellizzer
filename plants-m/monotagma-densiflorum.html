Monotagma densiflorum

Monotagma densiflorum, commonly known as the denseflower tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy appearance.

The tree is often found in moist, well-drained soils and is valued for its ornamental qualities and ecological benefits.
