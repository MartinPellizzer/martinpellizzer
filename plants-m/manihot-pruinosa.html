Manihot pruinosa

Manihot pruinosa, commonly known as the Pruinosa arrowleaf, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy, powdery coating. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, dry seeds.

Manihot pruinosa is adapted to grow in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
