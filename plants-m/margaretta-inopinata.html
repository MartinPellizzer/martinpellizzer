Margaretta inopinata

Margaretta inopinata, commonly known as the purple margaret, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its foliage consists of heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Margaretta inopinata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
