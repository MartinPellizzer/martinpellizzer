Manihot multiflora

Manihot multiflora, commonly known as the arrowleaf mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
