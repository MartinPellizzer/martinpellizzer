Myosotis semidecandra

Myosotis semidecandra, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It features slender, erect stems that bear small, blue to violet flowers with five petals. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically hairy and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky areas.

Myosotis semidecandra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
