Mucuna tjerolang

Mucuna tjerolang, commonly known as the Java velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, hairy pods that contain several seeds. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in tropical climates. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters.

Botanically, it belongs to the Fabaceae family and is valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties.
