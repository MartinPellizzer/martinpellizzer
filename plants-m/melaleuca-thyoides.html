Melaleuca thyoides

Melaleuca thyoides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub that typically grows in coastal and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in winter and spring. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a greyish-green hue.

Melaleuca thyoides is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in Indigenous Australian medicine.
