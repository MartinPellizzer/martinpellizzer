Murraya kwangsiensis

Murraya kwangsiensis, commonly known as the Guangxi murraya, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia, particularly China.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and fragrant white or yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a glossy appearance.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that ripens to a red or orange color, attracting various wildlife species.
