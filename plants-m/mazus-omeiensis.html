Mazus omeiensis

Mazus omeiensis, commonly known as the Chinese mazus, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It belongs to the family Primulaceae and is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit. The plant features small, hairy leaves and produces clusters of pale blue or violet flowers. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Mazus omeiensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
