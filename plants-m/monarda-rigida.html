Monarda rigida

Monarda rigida, commonly known as stiff beardtongue, is a perennial herb native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and gray-green, hairy leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink, attracting pollinators like bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Monarda rigida is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native gardens.
