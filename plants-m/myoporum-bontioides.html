Myoporum bontioides

Myoporum bontioides, commonly known as the coastal myoporum, is a shrub native to coastal regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and small, pale blue or white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has adapted to sandy, well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for coastal landscapes. Its roots help stabilize sand dunes, contributing to coastal erosion control.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and resilience.
