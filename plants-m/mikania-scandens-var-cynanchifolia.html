Mikania scandens var. cynanchifolia

Mikania scandens var. cynanchifolia, commonly known as giant mile-a-minute weed, is a vigorous climbing vine native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It possesses heart-shaped, opposite leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinct veining pattern. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that develop into fluffy, white to pale brown seed heads. It is characterized by its rapid growth rate and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.

Botanically, it belongs to the Asteraceae family and is known for its invasive potential in various ecosystems.
