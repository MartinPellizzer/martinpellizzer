Muhlenbergia aurea

Muhlenbergia aurea, commonly known as golden muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that turns a striking golden hue in the autumn. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bear small, fluffy seeds. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Muhlenbergia aurea is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
