Mammillaria nunezii subsp. bella

Mammillaria nunezii subsp.

bella, commonly known as the Núñez's mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale yellow spines.

The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in arid environments. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.
