Medicago × kultiassovii

Medicago × kultiassovii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is adapted to dry, well-drained soils and thrives in temperate climates. It is often used in forage systems due to its high nutritional value and drought tolerance.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil fertility in agricultural settings.
