Mesosetum ansatum

Mesosetum ansatum, commonly known as the spikegrass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, cylindrical stems. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, spikelet-based flowers. Its leaves are narrow and have a rough texture, often with a bluish-gray hue.

Mesosetum ansatum is typically found in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds.
