Melpomene vulcanica

Melpomene vulcanica, commonly known as the Flame Violet, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from deep red to orange and yellow, often with intricate patterns. The plant belongs to the family Violaceae and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, warm climates. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Melpomene vulcanica is often cultivated in gardens for its visual impact and is relatively easy to care for with proper soil and watering conditions.
