Maranta klotzschiana

Maranta klotzschiana, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The plant features rhizomatous growth and produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a velvety texture that gives it its common name.

Maranta klotzschiana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
