Marginaria cardiophylla

Marginaria cardiophylla, commonly known as the heart-leaf margarita, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family, known for its showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant is native to specific regions in South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
