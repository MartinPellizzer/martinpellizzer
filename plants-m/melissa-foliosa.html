Melissa foliosa

Melissa foliosa, commonly known as lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It is characterized by its fragrant, serrated leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in partial shade to full sun and prefers well-drained soil. Its leaves are often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to their calming properties.

Melissa foliosa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential soothing effects on the nervous system.
