Machaerium ferrugineum var. lindenianum

Machaerium ferrugineum var. lindenianum, commonly known as the red-stemmed machaerium, is a tropical shrub native to regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive reddish-brown stems and feathery, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in dense, panicle-like clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in warm climates.
