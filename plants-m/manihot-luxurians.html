Manihot luxurians

Manihot luxurians, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces a starchy, edible root that is used as a food source in various regions. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its rapid growth and high yield make it a valuable crop for both subsistence and commercial farming.
