Mussaenda capsulifera

Mussaenda capsulifera, commonly known as the tropical flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, tubular inflorescences that are typically bright yellow or orange in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

Mussaenda capsulifera thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
