Morinda villosa

Morinda villosa, commonly known as the hairy morinda, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its woody shrub structure with hairy stems and leaves. Morinda villosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional medicinal uses in some cultures.
