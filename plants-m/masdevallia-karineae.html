Masdevallia karineae

Masdevallia karineae, commonly known as the Karine's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers with a distinctive lip that is typically more elaborate than the other petals. The plant typically grows as a small epiphyte, anchoring itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to the humid, shaded conditions of its environment.

Masdevallia karineae is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral structure and rarity in cultivation.
