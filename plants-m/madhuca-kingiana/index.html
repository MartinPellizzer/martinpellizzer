Madhuca Kingiana

Madhuca kingiana is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. Known for its striking silver-green foliage and fragrant flowers, it is a popular ornamental plant in horticulture. This species thrives in humid climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among gardeners and landscape designers. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in mainstream botanical discussions.
