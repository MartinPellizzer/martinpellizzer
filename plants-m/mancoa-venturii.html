Mancoa venturii

Mancoa venturii, commonly known as the yellow star orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking yellow petals and a distinctive star-shaped appearance. The plant typically grows in high-altitude grasslands and rocky areas, thriving in cool and humid conditions. Its flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, adding vibrant color to its natural habitat.

Mancoa venturii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
