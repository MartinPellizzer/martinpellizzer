Minuartia rostrata var. maroccana

Minuartia rostrata var. maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the rocky regions of North Africa.

It features small, delicate white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a mat-forming habit with hairy, fleshy leaves adapted to arid conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in crevices and on rock faces.

Its botanical characteristics make it well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
