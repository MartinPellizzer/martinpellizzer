Masdevallia loui

Masdevallia loui, commonly known as the Loui's Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures and unique leaf morphology. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and delicate, with a wide range of hues that can vary among individual plants.

Masdevallia loui is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its aesthetic and botanical interest.
