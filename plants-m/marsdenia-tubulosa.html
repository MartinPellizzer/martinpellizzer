Marsdenia tubulosa

Marsdenia tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for horticultural cultivation.
