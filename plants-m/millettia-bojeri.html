Millettia bojeri

Millettia bojeri, commonly known as the African senna, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical Africa and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces small, flat pods that contain several seeds. It is often found in open woodlands and along riverbanks, thriving in well-drained soils.
