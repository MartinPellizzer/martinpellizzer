Melastoma repens

Melastoma repens, commonly known as the wandering melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom throughout the year. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
