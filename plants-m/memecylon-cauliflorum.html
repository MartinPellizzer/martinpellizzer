Memecylon cauliflorum

Memecylon cauliflorum, commonly known as the tropical evergreen tree, is a species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its cauliflorous flowering pattern, where flowers develop directly on the trunk and branches. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. It produces small, white or greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, contributing to its role in forest ecosystems.
