Microloma armatum var. armatum

Microloma armatum var. armatum, commonly known as the red-stemmed everlasting pea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clusters of small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant produces small, tough, evergreen leaves that are often used in floral arrangements due to their durability. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral design.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators.
