Miscanthus × longiberbis var. changii

Miscanthus × longiberbis var. changii, commonly known as the silvergrass or Chinese silvergrass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its striking silver-blue foliage and tall, arching inflorescences that add visual interest in gardens. This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

The plant is also noted for its ability to attract pollinators and its potential use in bioenergy production.
