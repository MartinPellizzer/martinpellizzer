Manihot laciniosa

Manihot laciniosa, commonly known as the laceleaf arrowleaf, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, palmately lobed leaves that resemble a lace pattern, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by yellow, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in humid tropical environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
