Melissitus ruthenicus var. liaosiensis

Melissitus ruthenicus var. liaosiensis, commonly known as the Liaosian goldenrod, is a perennial herb native to eastern China.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas or along stream banks. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers in late summer. Its inflorescence is a panicle, which is characteristic of the Asteraceae family.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
