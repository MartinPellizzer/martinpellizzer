Mikania anisodora

Mikania anisodora, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are followed by spherical, fuzzy fruits. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and agricultural fields.

Mikania anisodora is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
