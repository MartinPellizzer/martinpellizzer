Malaxis fastigiata

Malaxis fastigiata, commonly known as the upright malaxis, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It features a slender, erect stem with a single or few leaves arranged along its length. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in a dense inflorescence. Its roots are specialized for absorbing moisture from the forest floor.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
