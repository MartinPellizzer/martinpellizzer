Masdevallia carmenensis

Masdevallia carmenensis, commonly known as Carmen's Masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, showy flowers that display a striking combination of colors, often featuring deep reds and purples. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are narrow and strap-like, adapted to capture moisture from the humid air.

Due to its limited distribution and delicate nature, it is considered a rare and highly sought-after species in botanical collections.
