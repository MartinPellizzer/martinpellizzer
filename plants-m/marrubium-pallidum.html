Marrubium pallidum

Marrubium pallidum, commonly known as pale horehound, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the family Boraginaceae and is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and pale blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.
