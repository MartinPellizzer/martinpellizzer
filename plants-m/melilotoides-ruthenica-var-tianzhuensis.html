Melilotoides ruthenica var. tianzhuensis

Melilotoides ruthenica var. tianzhuensis, commonly known as the Tianzhu melilot, is a flowering plant native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is known for its yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its medicinal properties have been recognized in traditional Chinese medicine, where it is used for its purported anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.

The species is also valued for its role in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
