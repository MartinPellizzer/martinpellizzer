Millettia cavitensis

Millettia cavitensis, commonly known as the hollow-stemmed legume, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its unique hollow stems, which provide structural support while reducing weight. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Millettia cavitensis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
