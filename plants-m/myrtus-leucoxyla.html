Myrtus leucoxyla

Myrtus leucoxyla, commonly known as the snow myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features narrow, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine white bloom, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. Myrtus leucoxyla is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in ornamental gardening.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
