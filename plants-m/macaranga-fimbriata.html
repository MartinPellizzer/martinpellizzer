Macaranga fimbriata

Macaranga fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed paper tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive fringed margin. The leaves are used in traditional medicine and are also valued for their fibrous qualities.
