Mangifera odorata

Mangifera odorata, commonly known as the fragrant mango, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces edible fruits that are rich in vitamins and minerals. Its flowers are typically yellowish-green with a strong, pleasant aroma.

The plant is valued not only for its fruit but also for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses in local cultures.
