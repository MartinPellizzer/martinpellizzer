Maccraithea Sophronites

Maccraithea sophronites is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of central Asia. Despite its beauty, this species is highly endangered due to habitat loss and over-collection. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
