Myosotis goyenii subsp. goyenii

Myosotis goyenii subsp.

goyenii, commonly known as the Goyen's forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its delicate blue to violet flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens.
