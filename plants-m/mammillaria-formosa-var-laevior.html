Mammillaria formosa var. laevior

Mammillaria formosa var. laevior, commonly known as the smooth mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, rounded stem covered with small, rounded tubercles that are less pronounced than in other varieties. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-draining soil with plenty of sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
