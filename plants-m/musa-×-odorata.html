Musa × odorata

Musa × odorata, commonly known as the sweet banana or Musa basjoo, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of large, yellow, edible fruits that are rich in potassium and other nutrients. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its fruit.

Musa × odorata is relatively cold-hardy and can thrive in a variety of soil types, making it a versatile plant for both tropical and temperate climates.
