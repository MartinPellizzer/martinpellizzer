Murraya tetramera

Murraya tetramera, commonly known as the four-parted murraya, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rutaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers and elliptical, dark green leaves. The plant produces small, orange-red fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Murraya tetramera is also used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
