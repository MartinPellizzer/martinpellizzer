Mikania almagroi

Mikania almagroi, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its rapid growth and trailing habit. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Mikania almagroi is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open fields.
