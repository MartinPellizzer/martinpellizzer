Muhlenbergia aristata

Muhlenbergia aristata, commonly known as the pink muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, pinkish-purple plumes during the summer months. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for xeriscaping and rock gardens. Its fine, grass-like leaves are typically bluish-green and contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Muhlenbergia aristata is valued for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
