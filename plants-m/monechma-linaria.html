Monechma linaria

Monechma linaria, commonly known as the slender everlasting pea, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, clustered flowers that range from white to pale pink, often with a slight purple hue. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in coastal areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscaping.
