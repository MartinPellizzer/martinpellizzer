Melancranis comparbergensis

It is characterized by its unique morphological features, including its dark green, velvety leaves and striking, bi-colored flowers. The plant thrives in nutrient-poor, well-drained soils and requires a specific microclimate to flourish. Its reproductive cycle is closely tied to seasonal rainfall patterns, making it highly sensitive to environmental changes.
