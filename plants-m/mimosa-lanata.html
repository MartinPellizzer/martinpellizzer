Mimosa lanata

Mimosa lanata, commonly known as the woolly mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Mimosa lanata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
