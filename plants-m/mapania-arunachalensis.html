Mapania arunachalensis

Mapania arunachalensis, commonly known as the Arunachal rose.

It is a flowering plant native to the eastern Himalayas, specifically found in the state of Arunachal Pradesh in India. The plant belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy pink or white flowers. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
