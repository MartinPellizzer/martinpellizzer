Melandrium keiskei f. albescens

Melandrium keiskei f. albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered honesty, is a variant of the common honesty plant.

It is a low-growing perennial herb with delicate, white flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features linear, grass-like leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its simple, elegant appearance.
