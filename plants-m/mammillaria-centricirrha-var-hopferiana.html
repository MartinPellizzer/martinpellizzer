Mammillaria centricirrha var. hopferiana

Mammillaria centricirrha var. hopferiana, commonly known as the Hopfer's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and slow growth rate make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
