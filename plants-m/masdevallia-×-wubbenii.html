Masdevallia × wubbenii

Masdevallia × wubbenii, commonly known as the Wubben’s Masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that feature intricate patterns and a unique structure typical of the Masdevallia genus. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of long, strap-like leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
