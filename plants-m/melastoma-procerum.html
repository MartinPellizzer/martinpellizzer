Melastoma procerum

Melastoma procerum, commonly known as the slender melastoma, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and mountainous regions. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a hairy texture.

Melastoma procerum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
