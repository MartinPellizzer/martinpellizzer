Mesembryanthemum gibbosum

Mesembryanthemum gibbosum, commonly known as the Cape diamond or rockrose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, adapted to its arid environment. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with a distinctive diamond-shaped pattern, blooming in the early morning. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to xeriscapes.
