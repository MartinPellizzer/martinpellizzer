Mimosa montana var. sandemanii

Mimosa montana var. sandemanii, commonly known as the Sandeman's mimosa, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, causing them to fold when disturbed. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or coastal landscapes.

Its unique foliage and adaptability make it a popular choice for drought-tolerant gardens.
