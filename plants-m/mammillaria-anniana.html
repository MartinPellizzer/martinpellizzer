Mammillaria anniana

Mammillaria anniana, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
