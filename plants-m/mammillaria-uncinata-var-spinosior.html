Mammillaria uncinata var. spinosior

Mammillaria uncinata var. spinosior, commonly known as the spinous mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in prominent, spiny tubercles. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spines are typically straight and can be quite stiff, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
