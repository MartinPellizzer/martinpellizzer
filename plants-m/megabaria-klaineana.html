Megabaria klaineana

Megabaria klaineana, commonly known as the Klamath lily, is a striking plant native to the western United States.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and wetlands. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a yellow or orange throat. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Megabaria klaineana plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators and habitat for small organisms.
