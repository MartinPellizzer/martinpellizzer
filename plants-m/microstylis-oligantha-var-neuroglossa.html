Microstylis oligantha var. neuroglossa

Microstylis oligantha var. neuroglossa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped microstylis, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its small, delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers have a distinctive, elongated lip.

This variety is noted for its unique floral structure and is of interest to botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
