Mezia sericea

Mezia sericea, commonly known as the silky mezia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Mezia sericea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
