Medicago sativa var. caerulea

Medicago sativa var. caerulea, commonly known as blue medic, is a variant of the common medick plant.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its blue flowers and trifoliate leaves. The plant is native to Europe and has been introduced to various regions around the world. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in meadows and disturbed soils.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and as a forage crop.
