Maxillaria multiflora

Maxillaria multiflora, commonly known as the fragrant orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces multiple flowering spikes throughout the year. The flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with intricate patterns and a strong, sweet fragrance. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
