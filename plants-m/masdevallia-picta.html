Masdevallia picta

Masdevallia picta, commonly known as the pictured masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate patterns on its flowers, which often feature vibrant red, yellow, and green hues. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in cool, shaded environments with high humidity. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its foliage and flowers.

Masdevallia picta is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
