Mikania castroi

Mikania castroi, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a rough texture.

Mikania castroi is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and clearings due to its adaptability and aggressive growth.
