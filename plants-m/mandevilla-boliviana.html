Mandevilla boliviana

Mandevilla boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

Mandevilla boliviana requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
