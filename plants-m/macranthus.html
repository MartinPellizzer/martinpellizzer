Macranthus

Macranthus, commonly known as the giant spiderwort, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Commelinaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often violet or blue flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged in a spiral pattern around the stem. Macranthus species are native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.
