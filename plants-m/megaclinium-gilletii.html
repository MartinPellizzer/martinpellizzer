Megaclinium gilletii

Megaclinium gilletii, commonly known as the desert flax, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
