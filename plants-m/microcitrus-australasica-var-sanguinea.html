Microcitrus australasica var. sanguinea

Microcitrus australasica var. sanguinea, commonly known as the blood orange citron.

It is a small evergreen shrub native to Australia, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, round citrus fruits that are typically red or orange in color, with a sweet and tangy flavor. It thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

The variety is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its edible fruit.
