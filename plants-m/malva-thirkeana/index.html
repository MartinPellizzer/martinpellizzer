Malva Thirkeana

Thirke's mallows, scientifically known as Malva thirkeana, are a rare and striking species of flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America. This unique plant is characterized by its velvety foliage and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. Despite its beauty, Malva thirkeana is critically endangered due to habitat destruction and limited distribution. It is often found in rocky soils at high altitudes, thriving in cool and moist environments. Conservation efforts are underway to protect and propagate this rare botanical gem.
