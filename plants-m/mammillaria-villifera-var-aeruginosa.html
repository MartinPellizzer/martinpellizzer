Mammillaria villifera var. aeruginosa

Mammillaria villifera var. aeruginosa, commonly known as the Azure Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, spiny areoles covered with white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The stems are typically pale green to blue-gray, and the plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments, with shallow roots and a slow growth rate.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
