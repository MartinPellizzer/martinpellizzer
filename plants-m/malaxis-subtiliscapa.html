Malaxis subtiliscapa

Malaxis subtiliscapa, commonly known as the slender-veined Malaxis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its rhizome is short and it produces a single, erect stem with a few flowers at the apex.

The species is noted for its subtle appearance and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
