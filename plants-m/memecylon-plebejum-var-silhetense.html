Memecylon plebejum var. silhetense

Memecylon plebejum var. silhetense, commonly known as Silhet memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the eastern Himalayas.

It typically grows in moist, shaded forest understories and thrives in humid tropical climates. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a glossy green color. Its small, clustered flowers are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, berry-like fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
