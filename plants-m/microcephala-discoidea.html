Microcephala discoidea

Microcephala discoidea, commonly known as the star-shaped dandelion, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, yellow, dandelion-like flowers that are typically arranged in a star pattern. The plant has a basal rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in meadows and along stream banks.

Microcephala discoidea is known for its delicate appearance and is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
