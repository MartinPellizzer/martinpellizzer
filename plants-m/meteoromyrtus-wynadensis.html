Meteoromyrtus wynadensis

Meteoromyrtus wynadensis, commonly known as the Wynad Myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, small white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, round berries that are edible. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often found in shaded forest areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional medicine practices.
