Memecylon phanerophlebium

Memecylon phanerophlebium, commonly known as the broad-leaf memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Memecylon phanerophlebium is often found in moist, shaded habitats in lowland tropical forests.
