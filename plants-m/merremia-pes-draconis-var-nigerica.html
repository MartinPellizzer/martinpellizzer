Merremia pes-draconis var. nigerica

Merremia pes-draconis var. nigerica, commonly known as the Black Dragon's Foot.

It is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and produces large, showy yellow flowers. Its fruit is a distinctive, spiny capsule that contains seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in some cultures.
