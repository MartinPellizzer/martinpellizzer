Macropeplus ligustrinus var. grandiflorus

Macropeplus ligustrinus var. grandiflorus, commonly known as the large-flowered evergreen hibiscus, is a species native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its evergreen, broad-ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming from spring to autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to add vibrant color to gardens and landscapes.
