Myristica hackenbergii

Myristica hackenbergii, commonly known as the African nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, woody fruits that contain aromatic seeds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves with a waxy texture. Its seeds, known as nutmegs, are used in traditional medicine and cuisine.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.
