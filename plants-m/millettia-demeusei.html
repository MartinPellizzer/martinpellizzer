Millettia demeusei

Millettia demeusei, commonly known as the Demeuse's millettia, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a decorative plant.
