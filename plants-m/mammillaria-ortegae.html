Mammillaria ortegae

Mammillaria ortegae, commonly known as the Ortega's bunny ear cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems that often bear white or yellow spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, pink or white flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and indoor gardening.
