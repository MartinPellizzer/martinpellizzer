Magnolia sirindhorniae

Magnolia sirindhorniae, commonly known as the Sirindhorn magnolia, is a rare and flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves and produces fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that are highly ornamental. It is named in honor of Queen Sirindhorn of Thailand, reflecting its cultural significance.
