Medinilla sessilifolia

Medinilla sessilifolia, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, velvety leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. Medinilla sessilifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical gardens.
