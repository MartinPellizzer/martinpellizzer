Micromelum glabrescens

Micromelum glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth micromelum, is a small evergreen tree native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and simple, alternate leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible berries. It thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
