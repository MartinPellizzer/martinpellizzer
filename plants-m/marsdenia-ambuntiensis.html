Marsdenia ambuntiensis

Marsdenia ambuntiensis, commonly known as the Ambuntien Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

This species is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
