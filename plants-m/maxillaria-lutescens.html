Maxillaria lutescens

Maxillaria lutescens, commonly known as the yellow maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with well-drained soil. It blooms during the summer months and is appreciated for its ornamental value in botanical gardens.

Its unique morphology and vibrant colors make it a popular subject in horticultural studies.
