Magnolia × kewensis

Magnolia × kewensis, commonly known as the Kew magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous shrub or small tree native to the United Kingdom.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that range from white to pink, often with a yellowish-green throat. The plant has large, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy on top. It produces woody, cone-like structures called capsules that contain numerous seeds.

The botanical characteristics of Magnolia × kewensis make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and botanical collections.
