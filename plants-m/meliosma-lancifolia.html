Meliosma lancifolia

Meliosma lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged opposite on the stems. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by woody fruits containing kidney-shaped seeds.

Meliosma lancifolia is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
