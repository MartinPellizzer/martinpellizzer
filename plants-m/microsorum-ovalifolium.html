Microsorum ovalifolium

Microsorum ovalifolium, commonly known as the broad-leaf sword fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features lance-shaped, dark green fronds that are often broad at the base, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces sporangia on the underside of its fronds, which are clustered in sori. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest undergrowth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
