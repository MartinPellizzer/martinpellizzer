Manettia cordifolia f. boliviana

Manettia cordifolia f. boliviana, commonly known as the Bolivian heartleaf manettia, is a member of the Rubiaceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Native to the Andean regions of Bolivia, it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after species for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
