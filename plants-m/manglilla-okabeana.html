Manglilla okabeana

Manglilla okabeana, commonly known as the Okabe's manglilla, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa.

Manglilla okabeana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in coastal gardens for its unique appearance.
