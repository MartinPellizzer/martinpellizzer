Melastoma brachyotum

Melastoma brachyotum, commonly known as the small-leaved melastoma, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, which bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.

Melastoma brachyotum is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical and subtropical regions.
