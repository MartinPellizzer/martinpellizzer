Maxillariella anceps

Maxillariella anceps, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink and white flowers, which resemble a slipper in shape. The plant has a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb that supports its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are typically dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulb.

Maxillariella anceps is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
