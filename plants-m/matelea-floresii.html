Matelea floresii

Matelea floresii, commonly known as the Floresii's milkweed, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many milkweed species. The plant features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
