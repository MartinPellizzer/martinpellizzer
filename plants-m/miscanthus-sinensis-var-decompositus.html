Miscanthus sinensis var. decompositus

Miscanthus sinensis var. decompositus, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a perennial grass native to China.

It is characterized by its elegant, arching inflorescences that resemble plumes, often tinged with silver or white. The plant produces slender, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

This variety is popular in ornamental landscaping for its graceful appearance and low maintenance requirements.
