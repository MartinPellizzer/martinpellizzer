Mentha × subtomentosa

Mentha × subtomentosa, commonly known as woolly mint, is a hybrid mint species native to parts of Asia and the Middle East.

It is characterized by its fuzzy, gray-green leaves and strong, aromatic scent. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Mentha × subtomentosa is often used in traditional medicine for its purported cooling and digestive properties.

Its flowers are small and pale blue to purple, appearing in clusters along the stems.
