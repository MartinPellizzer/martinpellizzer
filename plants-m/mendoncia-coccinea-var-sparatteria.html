Mendoncia coccinea var. sparatteria

Mendoncia coccinea var. sparatteria, commonly known as the red flame lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It features vivid red, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden landscapes.

Its unique color and form make it a standout in both cultivated gardens and natural habitats.
