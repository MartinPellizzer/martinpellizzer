Matthiola leprosa

Matthiola leprosa, commonly known as the pale-flowered stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants such as cabbages and mustard. Matthiola leprosa is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
