Miscanthus sinensis var. variegatus

Miscanthus sinensis var. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated silver grass, is a striking ornamental grass native to East Asia.

It features elegant, arching blades that are predominantly silver-white with green or pinkish variegation, creating a visually appealing contrast. This variety thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil, making it suitable for a variety of garden settings. It is known for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements, making it a popular choice for landscaping.

The plant produces feathery plumes in late summer, adding a dynamic element to the garden throughout the growing season.
