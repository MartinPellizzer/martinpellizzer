Marilaunidium undulatum

Marilaunidium undulatum, commonly known as the wavy-leaved marilaunidium, is a rare and unique plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its undulating, wavy leaf margins, which give it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical rainforests. Its foliage is dark green and glossy, with a distinct aromatic scent when touched.

Due to its limited habitat and slow growth, it is considered a conservation concern.
