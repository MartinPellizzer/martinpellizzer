Magnolia morii

Magnolia morii, commonly known as the Japanese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Japan.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 10 meters tall.

Magnolia morii is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
