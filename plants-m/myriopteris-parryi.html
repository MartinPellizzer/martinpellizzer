Myriopteris parryi

Myriopteris parryi, commonly known as Parry's lip fern, is a delicate fern species native to the western United States.

Its spore-producing structures, called sori, are arranged in a unique pattern along the margins of the fronds.

Myriopteris parryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shady garden settings.
