Masdevallia ejiriana

Masdevallia ejiriana, commonly known as the Ecuadorian Masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to thrive. Its flowers are usually small to medium in size, with intricate patterns and a distinctive lip.

Masdevallia ejiriana is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in specialized orchid collections.
