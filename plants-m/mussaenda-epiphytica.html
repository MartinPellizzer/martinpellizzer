Mussaenda epiphytica

Mussaenda epiphytica, commonly known as the white flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that resemble flames, typically in shades of white or yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found growing on other plants or trees as an epiphyte.

Mussaenda epiphytica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
