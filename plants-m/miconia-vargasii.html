Miconia vargasii

Miconia vargasii, commonly known as the Vargas miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pink flowers that are often followed by showy, red, fleshy fruits. Miconia vargasii is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets, making it an invasive species in some regions.

Its botanical features make it an important subject in ecological and horticultural studies.
