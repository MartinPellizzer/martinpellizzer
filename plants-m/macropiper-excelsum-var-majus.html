Macropiper excelsum var. majus

Macropiper excelsum var. majus, commonly known as the giant pepperbush, is a tropical shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. It produces small, peppery fruits that are often used in traditional cuisines.

Due to its ornamental value and unique flavor, it is cultivated in some regions for both culinary and horticultural purposes.
