Melastoma glaberrimum

Melastoma glaberrimum, commonly known as the smooth melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color, which attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy appearance.

Melastoma glaberrimum is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates, thriving in well-drained soils and partial to full sunlight.
