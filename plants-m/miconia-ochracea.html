Miconia ochracea

Miconia ochracea, commonly known as the ochraceous miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
