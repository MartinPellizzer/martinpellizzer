Mandevilla illustris var. typica

Mandevilla illustris var. typica, commonly known as the showy mandevilla, is a flowering vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from pink to red. The plant produces fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to autumn. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Mandevilla illustris var. typica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its vibrant flowers and vigorous growth habit.
