Micromeles yuana

Micromeles yuana, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its large, white, fragrant flowers that open at night. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. Its leaves are ovate and hairy, while its fruits are small, round berries that contain seeds.
