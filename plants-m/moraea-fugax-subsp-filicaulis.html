Moraea fugax subsp. filicaulis

Moraea fugax subsp.

filicaulis, commonly known as the slender-leafed moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers.

The flowers typically bloom in spring and are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open grasslands or rocky areas.
