Myriophyllum intermedium

Myriophyllum intermedium, commonly known as water milfoil, is a submerged aquatic plant native to North America.

It has finely divided, feathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stem. The plant produces small, reddish flowers that float on the water surface. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Myriophyllum intermedium plays an important role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various aquatic organisms.
