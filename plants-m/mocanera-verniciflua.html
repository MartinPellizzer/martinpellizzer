Mocanera verniciflua

Mocanera verniciflua, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and its striking clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The tree is relatively drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun.
