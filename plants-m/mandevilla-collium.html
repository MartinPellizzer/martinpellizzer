Mandevilla collium

Mandevilla collium, commonly known as the golden mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mandevilla collium is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
