Mammea timorensis

Mammea timorensis, commonly known as the Timor mammea, is a tropical tree native to the island of Timor.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves and showy, red-orange flowers. The plant produces woody, spiny capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
