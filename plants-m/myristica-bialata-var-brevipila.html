Myristica bialata var. brevipila

Myristica bialata var. brevipila, commonly known as the short-stemmed nutmeg.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia. The plant belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic seeds, which are used in various culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a leathery texture.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits containing the valuable spice.
