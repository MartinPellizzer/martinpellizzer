Maranta blumei

Maranta blumei, commonly known as the silver sword or velvetleaf, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, heart-shaped leaves that often display a vibrant silver or white midrib, contrasting with deep green foliage. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental value and unique leaf patterns. Maranta blumei thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its foliage remains attractive throughout the year, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
