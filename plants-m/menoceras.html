Menoceras

Menoceras, commonly known as the golden bellvista, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Amaranthaceae.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant is native to South Africa and thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery bloom.

Menoceras is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
