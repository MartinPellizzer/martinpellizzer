Malus domestica var. renettea

Malus domestica var. renettea, commonly known as the Red Delicious apple.

This variety is characterized by its distinctive deep red to crimson skin, which often has a slight blush of yellow or green. The fruit is typically large, with a sweet and juicy flesh that has a low acidity. It is primarily cultivated for its appearance and flavor, making it a popular choice for fresh consumption and baking.

The tree is a medium-sized apple tree that produces fruit in late summer to early autumn.
