Mesembryanthemum knysnanum

Mesembryanthemum knysnanum, commonly known as the Knysna mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and flowers that open in response to sunlight. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday.

Its unique ability to store water in its leaves makes it well-suited for xerophytic conditions.
