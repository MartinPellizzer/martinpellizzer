Moraea mechowii

Moraea mechowii, commonly known as the Mechow's glory, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

Moraea mechowii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
