Maxillaria schistostele

Maxillaria schistostele, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and dark green, strap-like leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern, typically featuring dark purple or brown markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria schistostele is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
