Matricaria capensis

Matricaria capensis, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its daisy-like flowers, which are typically white with a yellow center. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often used in gardens for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for dry climates.

Matricaria capensis is also valued for its medicinal properties, particularly in traditional African medicine.
