Mantisalca duriaei var. iserniana

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, terrestrial growth habit. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers with a distinctive three-lobed lip, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas with rich, humus-filled soil.
