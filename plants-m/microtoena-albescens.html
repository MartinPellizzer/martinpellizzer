Microtoena albescens

Microtoena albescens, commonly known as the white-flowered microtoena, is a small, herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its delicate, opposite leaves and showy, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually tubular with two lips, a feature common in many gesneriad species.

Microtoena albescens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in shaded garden settings.
