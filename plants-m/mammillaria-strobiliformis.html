Mammillaria strobiliformis

Mammillaria strobiliformis, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are often covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow during the winter months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for indoor gardening.
