Meliosma flexuosa

Meliosma flexuosa, commonly known as the flexible meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, particularly India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
