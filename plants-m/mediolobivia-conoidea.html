Mediolobivia conoidea

Mediolobivia conoidea, commonly known as the spiral spire plant, is a unique species characterized by its helical growth pattern.

It features a single, elongated stem that twists upward in a spiral formation, creating a striking visual appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, arranged spirally along the stem.

Mediolobivia conoidea thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright light for optimal growth.
