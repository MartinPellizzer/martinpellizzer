Medicago × blancheana

Medicago × blancheana, commonly known as the Blancheana clover, is a hybrid perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for various ecological and agricultural settings. It plays a role in nitrogen fixation due to its symbiotic relationship with rhizobia bacteria.

Its ornamental value and adaptability contribute to its use in landscaping and restoration projects.
