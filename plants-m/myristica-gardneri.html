Myristica gardneri

Myristica gardneri, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and distinctive yellow flowers. The plant produces small, red, fleshy fruits that enclose the valuable nutmeg and mace spices. Its bark and leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
