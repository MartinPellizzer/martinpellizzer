Maranta pulchra

Maranta pulchra, commonly known as the Crimson Flame Plant, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its vibrant, red-bronze leaves that develop intricate patterns as they mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta pulchra is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is popular in indoor gardening.
