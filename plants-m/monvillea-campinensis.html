Monvillea campinensis

Monvillea campinensis, commonly known as the Campinense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
