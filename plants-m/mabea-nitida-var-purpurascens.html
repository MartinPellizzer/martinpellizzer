Mabea nitida var. purpurascens

Mabea nitida var. purpurascens, commonly known as the purple-leaf mabea, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that often display a striking purple or reddish hue, especially under full sun exposure. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and adaptability to various soil types.
