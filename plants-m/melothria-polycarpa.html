Melothria polycarpa

Melothria polycarpa, commonly known as the tropical cucumber or wild cucumber, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is a climbing vine that produces small, edible fruits with a mild, tangy flavor. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is native to tropical regions of the Americas.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications and has medicinal uses in traditional practices.
