Magnolia yunnanensis

Magnolia yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan, China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The tree has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also important in traditional Chinese medicine.
