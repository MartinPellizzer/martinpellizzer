Microsorum monstrosum

Microsorum monstrosum, commonly known as the giant chain sword, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow, sword-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, making it a popular choice for large aquariums. The plant thrives in moderate to high light conditions and requires regular nutrient supplementation to maintain its vibrant green color. It is a hardy and fast-growing species that can be propagated easily through rhizome division.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a favorite among aquatic plant enthusiasts.
