Maxillaria grandiflora var. amesiana

Maxillaria grandiflora var. amesiana, commonly known as the Amesian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces large, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically has dark green, velvety leaves and blooms in shades of yellow, orange, and red. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
