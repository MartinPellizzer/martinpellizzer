Monardella caroliniana

Monardella caroliniana, commonly known as Carolina monardella, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features upright stems with opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink to purple and bloom in late summer.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.
