Mimosa isthmensis

Mimosa isthmensis, commonly known as the Isthmus mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Isthmus of Panama.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has bipinnate leaves, which are divided into small, feather-like segments. Its flowers are usually yellow or pale green, clustered in spherical or cylindrical inflorescences.

Mimosa isthmensis is known for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
