Medicago Halophila

Medicago halophila is a hardy plant known for its ability to thrive in salty soils. It belongs to the legume family and is commonly found in coastal and arid regions. This species plays an important role in stabilizing sandy environments and preventing soil erosion. Its resilience makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects. Despite its tough environment, it produces small but nutritious seeds that support local wildlife.
