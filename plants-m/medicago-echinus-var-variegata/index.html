Medicago Echinus Var. Variegata

Medicago echinus var. variegata is a unique variant of the goat's head plant known for its striking variegated foliage. This plant is native to regions with dry climates and thrives in well-drained soils. Its leaves are often marked with contrasting colors, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is valued not only for its aesthetic appeal but also for its resilience in challenging environments.
