Mediocalcar uniflorum

Mediocalcar uniflorum, commonly known as the single-flowered rock orchid, is a rare and specialized orchid species native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and unique floral structure, featuring a single, showy flower that typically blooms in late summer. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and requires a cool, moist environment to flourish.
