Melochia melissifolia var. brachyphylla

Melochia melissifolia var. brachyphylla, commonly known as the short-stemmed melochia, is a tropical shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are typically heart-shaped, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin, and exhibit a vibrant green coloration. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from pale yellow to orange, often followed by spherical, fleshy fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along riverbanks.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
