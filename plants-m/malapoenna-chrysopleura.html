Malapoenna chrysopleura

Malapoenna chrysopleura, commonly known as the golden-leafed orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow or golden-green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, often arranged in a loose inflorescence. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
