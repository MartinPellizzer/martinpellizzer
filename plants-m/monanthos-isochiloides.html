Monanthos isochiloides

Monanthos isochiloides, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sunflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its upright growth habit and yellow-orange flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces composite flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy addition to gardens in suitable climates.
