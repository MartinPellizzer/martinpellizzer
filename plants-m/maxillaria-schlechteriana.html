Maxillaria schlechteriana

Maxillaria schlechteriana, commonly known as the Schlechter's maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its flowers are borne on erect, branching inflorescences. The leaves are elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern, with a waxy texture that helps it retain moisture.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
