Mesoneuron trichopodum

Mesoneuron trichopodum, commonly known as the featherleaf mesoneuron, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Mesonotidae and is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It is adapted to survive in dry environments, with deep root systems and reduced leaf surface area to minimize water loss.

Mesoneuron trichopodum is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in xeriscaping.
