Macrostylis tenuis

Macrostylis tenuis, commonly known as the slender-styled grass.

It is a species of grass native to parts of Africa, particularly South Africa. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, typical of the grass family.

Macrostylis tenuis is often found in grasslands and open habitats, where it thrives in well-drained soils.
