Medinilla peninsula

Medinilla peninsula, commonly known as the "pink lady," is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features striking, velvety pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blossoms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Medinilla peninsula is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a houseplant.
