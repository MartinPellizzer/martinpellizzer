Medicago sativa var. tumida

Medicago sativa var. tumida, commonly known as the inflated medic.

This variant is a perennial legume characterized by its swollen, inflated pods that give it a distinctive appearance. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in dry, rocky soils. The plant is valued for its forage quality and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Its botanical features include trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer.
