Mammillaria webbiana var. longispina

Mammillaria webbiana var. longispina, commonly known as the long-spined mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique spines make it a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
