Myristica kjellbergii

Myristica kjellbergii, commonly known as the Kjellberg's nutmeg, is a species of flowering plant in the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly found in the islands of Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that lead to the development of its distinctive fruit. The fruit contains a single seed, which is encased in a hard, aromatic shell.

This species is valued for its unique aromatic compounds and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
