Melochia morongii var. denudata

Melochia morongii var. denudata, commonly known as the naked melochia, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates.

The leaves and flowers are sometimes used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
