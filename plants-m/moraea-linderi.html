Moraea linderi

Moraea linderi, commonly known as the Linder's glory, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes many showy flowering plants. The plant features tall, slender stems that bear clusters of vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that provide a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

Moraea linderi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and floral arrangements.
