Myrsine Rockii

Myrsine rockii is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of China. It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is known for its vibrant, often pink or red flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in cool, humid climates and is often found in rocky or forested areas. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate and ecologically important plant.
