Mikania scandens var. sagittifolia

Mikania scandens var. sagittifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf Mikania, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead base, and its stems are hairy and twining. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by fluffy, white seed heads. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
