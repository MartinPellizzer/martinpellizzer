Mikania clematidiflora

Mikania clematidiflora, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread over other vegetation. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed areas.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
