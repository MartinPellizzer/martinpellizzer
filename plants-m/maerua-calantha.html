Maerua calantha

Maerua calantha, commonly known as the white thorn or African snowball, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It features small, white, clustered flowers that bloom in summer, giving it a snowball-like appearance. The plant has a woody stem with thorny branches, and its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it suitable for arid environments.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and use in traditional medicine.
