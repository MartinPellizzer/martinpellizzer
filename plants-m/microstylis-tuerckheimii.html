Microstylis tuerckheimii

Microstylis tuerckheimii, commonly known as the small-styled grass, is a small perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. The plant has slender, erect stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often hairy. Its tiny, inconspicuous flowers are arranged in spikelets, characteristic of the grass family.

Microstylis tuerckheimii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
