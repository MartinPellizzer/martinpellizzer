Macropiper guahamense

Macropiper guahamense, commonly known as Guahamense pepper.

It is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes various pepper species. Its leaves are typically alternate and have a rough texture, while its flowers are small and clustered.

The fruit is a drupe, containing a single seed, and it is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
