Memecylon subcordatum

Memecylon subcordatum, commonly known as the small-leaf memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters at the leaf axils. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a small capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
