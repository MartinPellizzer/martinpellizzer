Mussaenda ramosissima

Mussaenda ramosissima, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, branched structure and clusters of showy, orange-red bract-like flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in full sun.

Mussaenda ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly used in landscaping and as a hedge plant.
