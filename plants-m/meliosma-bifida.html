Meliosma bifida

Meliosma bifida, commonly known as the split-leaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves that are deeply divided into two lobes, giving it its common name. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

The tree can grow up to 10 meters tall and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
