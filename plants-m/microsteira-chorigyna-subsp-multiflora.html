Microsteira chorigyna subsp. multiflora

Microsteira chorigyna subsp.

multiflora, commonly known as the coastal microsteira, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of southern Africa. It is a perennial herb with small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of tiny, white or pale blue flowers.

The plant thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in dune environments. It is adapted to salt spray and can tolerate moderate drought conditions.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental flowers make it a distinctive species in its habitat.
