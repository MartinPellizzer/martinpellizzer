Microloma massonii

Microloma massonii, commonly known as the Masson's microloma, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1 meter, with narrow, linear leaves. Its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters, and bloom during the summer months.

It is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
