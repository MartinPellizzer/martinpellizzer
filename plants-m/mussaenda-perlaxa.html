Mussaenda perlaxa

Mussaenda perlaxa, commonly known as the golden trumpet bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Mussaenda perlaxa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
