Microsorum lucidum

Microsorum lucidum, commonly known as the golden thread fern, is a species of fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, creeping rhizomes and delicate, pinnately compound fronds. The fronds are typically bright green and have a smooth, glossy texture, which contributes to its common name. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

This plant is popular in horticulture for its ornamental value and ease of care.
