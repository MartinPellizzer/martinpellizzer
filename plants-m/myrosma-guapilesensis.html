Myrosma guapilesensis

Myrosma guapilesensis, commonly known as the Guapiles myrosma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes other aromatic plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
