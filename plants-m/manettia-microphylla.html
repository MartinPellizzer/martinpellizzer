Manettia microphylla

Manettia microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved manettia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are fragrant and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies. Its fruit is a small, round capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Manettia microphylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
