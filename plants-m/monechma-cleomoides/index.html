Monechma Cleomoides

Cleome, scientifically known as Monechma cleomoides, is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southeastern United States. With its distinctive, umbrella-like clusters of bright yellow blooms, it stands out in any garden or natural setting. This annual plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced gardeners. Its unique appearance and easy care requirements have made it a favorite among those seeking to add visual interest to their landscapes. Despite its modest size, Cleome plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and enhancing biodiversity.
