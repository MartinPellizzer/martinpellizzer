Medicago sphaerocarpa

Medicago sphaerocarpa, commonly known as the globe vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its spherical, inflated seed pods that give it its common name. The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish-white flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

Medicago sphaerocarpa is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration projects.
