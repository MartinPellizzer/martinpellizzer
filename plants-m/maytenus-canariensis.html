Maytenus canariensis

Maytenus canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Island holly, is a small evergreen tree native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the family Phyllanthaceae and is characterized by its dense, glossy dark green leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces red berries that are edible for birds but toxic to humans. It thrives in coastal areas and is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value.
