Mimosa albida var. strigosa

Mimosa albida var. strigosa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-white foliage, which gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
