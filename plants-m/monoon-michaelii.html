Monoon michaelii

Monoon michaelii, commonly known as the mountain hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in mountainous regions. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a velvety texture.
