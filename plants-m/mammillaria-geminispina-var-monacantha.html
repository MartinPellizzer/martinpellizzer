Mammillaria geminispina var. monacantha

Mammillaria geminispina var. monacantha, commonly known as the Monacan Thorned Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to pale blue spines, which are often arranged in clusters. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
