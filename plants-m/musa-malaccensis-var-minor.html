Musa malaccensis var. minor

Musa malaccensis var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf banana plant, is a tropical species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a member of the Musaceae family and is characterized by its compact stature compared to other banana varieties. The plant produces small, clustered bunches of bananas that are typically harvested when they are still green. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a smooth, waxy texture.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a food source in its native habitat.
