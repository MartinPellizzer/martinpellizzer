Margaris

Margaris, commonly known as the margarita plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and is native to the Mediterranean region. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Margaris is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
