Melastoma arboreum

Melastoma arboreum, commonly known as the tropical tree or shrub, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are opposite and ovate in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white, arranged in clusters. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain several seeds.

Melastoma arboreum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
