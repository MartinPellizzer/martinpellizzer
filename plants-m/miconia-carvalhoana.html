Miconia carvalhoana

Miconia carvalhoana, commonly known as the Brazilian angel's trumpets, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a flowering shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant produces large, showy, violet-colored flowers that are typically clustered in panicles. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often have a velvety texture.

Miconia carvalhoana is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
