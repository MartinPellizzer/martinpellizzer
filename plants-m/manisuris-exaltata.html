Manisuris exaltata

Manisuris exaltata, commonly known as the tall manisuris, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its leaves are elongated and lance-shaped, often with a slightly rough texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
