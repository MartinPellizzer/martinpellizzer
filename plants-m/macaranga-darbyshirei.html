Macaranga darbyshirei

Macaranga darbyshirei, commonly known as the Darbyshire's makara, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its alternate, simple, and often hairy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.
