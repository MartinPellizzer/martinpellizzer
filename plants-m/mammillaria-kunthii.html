Mammillaria kunthii

Mammillaria kunthii, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a distinct, pale green body and a crown of white to pink flowers. The plant features arecentric spines and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
