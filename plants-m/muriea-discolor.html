Muriea discolor

Muriea discolor, commonly known as the variable muriea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and the Americas.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are typically green with a distinctive white or pale underside. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded or semi-shaded habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
