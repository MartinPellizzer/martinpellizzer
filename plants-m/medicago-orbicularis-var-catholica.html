Medicago orbicularis var. catholica

Medicago orbicularis var. catholica, commonly known as the European goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as roadsides and wastelands. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor soils.

Its seeds are encased in a distinctive, spiny pod, which aids in seed dispersal.
