Melocactus acunae var. flavispinus

Melocactus acunae var. flavispinus, commonly known as the yellow-spined melocactus, is a striking cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in dense, yellow spines that give it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique morphology make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
