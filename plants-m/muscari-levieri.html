Muscari levieri

Muscari levieri, commonly known as the Levieri's grape hyacinth, is a bulbous perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asparagaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. Muscari levieri is valued for its early spring bloom and its ability to naturalize over time.
