Maesa bonii

Maesa bonii, commonly known as the golden star hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers with a central red star, which bloom throughout the year. The plant has palmately lobed leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Maesa bonii is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
