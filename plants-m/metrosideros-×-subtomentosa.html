Metrosideros × subtomentosa

Metrosideros × subtomentosa, commonly known as the Christmas Bush, is a hybrid shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, bright red flowers that bloom profusely during the summer months. The plant has ovate to lanceolate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Its rapid growth and attractive appearance make it a valuable addition to coastal and urban landscapes.
