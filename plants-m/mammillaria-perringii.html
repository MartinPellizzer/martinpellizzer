Mammillaria perringii

Mammillaria perringii, commonly known as the Perring's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly bristles. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and features prominent areoles with spines. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the top of the plant.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
