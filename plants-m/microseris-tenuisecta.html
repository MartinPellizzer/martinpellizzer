Microseris tenuisecta

Microseris tenuisecta, commonly known as the slenderleaf tansy, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of western North America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, linear leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and thrives in open, sunny environments. Its leaves are finely divided and often appear silvery due to a dense covering of fine hairs.
