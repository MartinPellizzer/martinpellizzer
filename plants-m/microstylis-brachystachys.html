Microstylis brachystachys

Microstylis brachystachys, commonly known as the short-stalked spike-rush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that are typically less than 10 centimeters in height. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.

Microstylis brachystachys is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing wetland habitats.
