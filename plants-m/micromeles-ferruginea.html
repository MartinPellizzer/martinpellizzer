Micromeles ferruginea

Micromeles ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty leaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange with reddish streaks, and it produces small, round seeds.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
