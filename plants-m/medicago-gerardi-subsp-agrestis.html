Medicago gerardi subsp. agrestis

Medicago gerardi subsp.

agrestis, commonly known as the red brome, is a flowering plant native to the western United States. It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with small, yellow flowers.

The plant has trifoliate leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in dry, disturbed areas such as roadsides and open fields.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it an important plant in ecological restoration projects.
