Moniera procumbens f. bicolor

Moniera procumbens f. bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored prostrate moniera, is a variant of the moniera plant characterized by its prostrate growth habit.

It features a distinctive bicoloration in its foliage, typically displaying a combination of green and white or yellow hues. The plant is native to specific regions in South America and thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are narrow and elongated, often forming a dense mat on the ground.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
