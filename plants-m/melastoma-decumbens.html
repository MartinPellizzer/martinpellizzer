Melastoma decumbens

Melastoma decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside.

Melastoma decumbens is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
