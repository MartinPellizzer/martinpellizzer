Melicytus novae-zelandiae subsp. centurionis

Melicytus novae-zelandiae subsp.

centurionis, commonly known as the Centurion tree, is a species native to New Zealand. It is a medium-sized evergreen tree with smooth, grey-brown bark and elliptical, dark green leaves.

The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bright red berries. It is adapted to a range of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

The species is noted for its ecological importance, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
