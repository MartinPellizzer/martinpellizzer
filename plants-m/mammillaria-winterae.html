Mammillaria winterae

Mammillaria winterae, commonly known as the Winter's Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
