Mutafinia capensis

Mutafinia capensis, commonly known as the Cape mutafinia, is a South African plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It is adapted to arid conditions and is drought-tolerant.

Mutafinia capensis is often used in local horticulture for its ornamental value and resilience.
