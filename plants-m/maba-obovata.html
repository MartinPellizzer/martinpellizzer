Maba obovata

Maba obovata, commonly known as the broad-leaf maba, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its large, obovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. Its bark is thick and corky, and it is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

The tree is valued for its durable wood and is often found in lowland rainforests.
