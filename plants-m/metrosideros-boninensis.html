Metrosideros boninensis

Metrosideros boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin firetree, is a flowering plant native to the Bonin Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces vibrant red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy green surface.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions.
