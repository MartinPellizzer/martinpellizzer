Masdevallia calosiphon

Masdevallia calosiphon, commonly known as the elegant masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It features intricate, delicate flowers with a unique coloration pattern, often displaying a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in its natural habitat.

Masdevallia calosiphon is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
