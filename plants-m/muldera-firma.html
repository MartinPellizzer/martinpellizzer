Muldera firma

Muldera firma, commonly known as the firm mulled herb, is a perennial plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It is characterized by its dense, woody stems and broad, leathery leaves that are dark green in color. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a hardy addition to gardens.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for both ornamental and medicinal purposes.
