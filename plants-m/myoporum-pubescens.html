Myoporum pubescens

Myoporum pubescens, commonly known as the silver myoporum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant has small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale blue, blooming in spring. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Myoporum pubescens is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to stabilize soils.
