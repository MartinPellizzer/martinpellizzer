Maniltoa floribunda

Maniltoa floribunda, commonly known as the velvetleaf or false hibiscus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red, typically blooming in clusters. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Maniltoa floribunda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a garden plant.
