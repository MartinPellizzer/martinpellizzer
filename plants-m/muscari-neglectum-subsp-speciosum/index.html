Muscari Neglectum Subsp. Speciosum

Muscari neglectum subsp. speciosum is a striking bulbous plant known for its vibrant blue to purple flower clusters. It belongs to the family Asparagaceae and is native to the Mediterranean region. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries. Its unique appearance makes it a favorite among horticulturists and botanists alike. The plant is also valued for its ability to thrive in well-drained soils and full sun conditions.
