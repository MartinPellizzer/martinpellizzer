Mostacillastrum orbignyanum

Mostacillastrum orbignyanum, commonly known as the Brazilian grass or orbignyanum grass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant is characterized by its tall, slender stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture.

It plays a role in stabilizing soils in its native habitat and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
