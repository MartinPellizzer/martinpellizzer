Monophyllaea insignis var. insignis

Monophyllaea insignis var. insignis, commonly known as the showy bellflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from purple to blue. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are upright and branching.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
