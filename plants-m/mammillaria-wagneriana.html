Mammillaria wagneriana

Mammillaria wagneriana, commonly known as the Wagner's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly bristles. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5-10 centimeters and features prominent areoles with clusters of white spines. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles in late spring.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
