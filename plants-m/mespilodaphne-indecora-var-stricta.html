Mespilodaphne indecora var. stricta

Mespilodaphne indecora var. stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mespilodaphne, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by edible, round fruits with a sweet, tangy flavor. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for both ornamental and culinary purposes.
