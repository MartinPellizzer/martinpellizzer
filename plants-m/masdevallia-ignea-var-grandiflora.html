Masdevallia ignea var. grandiflora

Masdevallia ignea var. grandiflora, commonly known as the fiery torch orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a distinctive yellow throat. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences that bear numerous small, showy blooms, typically appearing in late winter to early spring. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

This variety is prized for its intense coloration and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its dramatic visual impact.
