Myristica lepidota subsp. lepidota

Myristica lepidota subsp.

lepidota, commonly known as the hairy nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Indian Ocean islands. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg.

The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture. Its unique botanical features include the presence of a hard, aromatic seed kernel and a fibrous outer husk.

This subspecies is notable for its ornamental value and historical use in traditional medicine.
