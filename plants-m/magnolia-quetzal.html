Magnolia quetzal

Magnolia quetzal, commonly known as the Quetzal magnolia, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant typically grows as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant floral displays.

The magnolia quetzal is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
