Miltonia vexillaria var. leopoldii

Miltonia vexillaria var. leopoldii, commonly known as the Leopold's Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are broad and elliptical, providing a lush, green backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
