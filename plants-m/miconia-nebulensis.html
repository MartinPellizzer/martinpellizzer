Miconia nebulensis

Miconia nebulensis, commonly known as the misty miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the humid forests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating, giving them a misty appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the wet season. Its fruit is a globose, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia nebulensis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.
