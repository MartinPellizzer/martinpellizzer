Monochilus longilabris

Monochilus longilabris, commonly known as the long-lipped orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive elongated labellum, which gives it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in tropical rainforests, where it thrives in humid and shaded environments. Its flowers are usually small and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Monochilus longilabris is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
