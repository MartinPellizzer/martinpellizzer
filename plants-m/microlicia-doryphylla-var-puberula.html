Microlicia doryphylla var. puberula

Microlicia doryphylla var. puberula, commonly known as the velvetleaf microlicia, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, pubescent foliage that gives it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are opposite and ovate, with a waxy surface that helps reduce water loss.

It is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.
