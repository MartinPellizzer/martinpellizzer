Myrciaria phitrantha

Myrciaria phitrantha, commonly known as the Thai starfruit or Thai yellow starfruit, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white flowers and small, yellow-orange fruits that resemble starfruits. The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a glossy texture, while the fruits are edible and have a slightly tart flavor.

The plant is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its culinary uses in local cuisines.
