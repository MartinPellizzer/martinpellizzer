Mammillaria seegeri var. pruinosa

Mammillaria seegeri var. pruinosa, commonly known as the Pruinose Hedgehog Cactus, is a small, spiny cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with dense, white to pale blue spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
