Mesua singaporiana

Mesua singaporiana, commonly known as the Singapore flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has smooth, pale grey bark and elliptical, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The tree produces small, woody fruits that contain several seeds.
