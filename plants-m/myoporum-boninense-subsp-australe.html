Myoporum boninense subsp. australe

Myoporum boninense subsp.

australe, commonly known as the Bonin myoporum, is a shrub native to the Bonin Islands. It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and slightly leathery.

The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, attracting various pollinators. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in horticulture.
