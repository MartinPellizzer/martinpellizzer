Macrotyloma axillare var. glabrum

Macrotyloma axillare var. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth axillary burr legume, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes, followed by distinctive burr-like pods that enclose its seeds. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it useful in agricultural settings.
