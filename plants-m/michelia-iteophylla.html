Michelia iteophylla

Michelia iteophylla, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth margin. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The flowers are often used in traditional Chinese medicine and are also valued for their aesthetic appeal in gardens.
