Melastoma glabrescens

Melastoma glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia, Malaysia, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its showy, pink to red bracts that surround its small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and rocky areas.

Melastoma glabrescens is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
