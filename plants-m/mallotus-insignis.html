Mallotus insignis

Mallotus insignis, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed.

Mallotus insignis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
