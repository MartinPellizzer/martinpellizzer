Malapoenna utilis

Malapoenna utilis, commonly known as the Malapoenna tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood used in construction and crafts.
