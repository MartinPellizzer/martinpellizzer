Macrocentrum gracile

Macrocentrum gracile, commonly known as the slender tamarind, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces elongated, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. Native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia, it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
