Mentha lilibaea

Mentha lilibaea, commonly known as the lemon mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, fragrant leaves that emit a strong lemon scent. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Mentha lilibaea thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

It is often used in culinary and medicinal applications due to its distinctive flavor and potential health benefits.
