Minuartia schischkinii

Minuartia schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that typically grows in rocky or alpine environments. The plant features tiny, clustered white flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, adapted to reduce water loss in harsh conditions.

It is native to Central Asia and is often found in mountainous regions with well-drained soils.
