Metalasia ericoides

Metalasia ericoides, commonly known as the heath-like plant, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes heathers and blueberries. The plant is characterized by its small, dense foliage and evergreen leaves. It typically grows in acidic, well-drained soils and thrives in sunny to partially shaded environments.

Metalasia ericoides is often used in ornamental horticulture for its compact form and attractive appearance.
