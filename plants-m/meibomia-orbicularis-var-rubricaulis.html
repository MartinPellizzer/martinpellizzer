Meibomia orbicularis var. rubricaulis

Meibomia orbicularis var. rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed meibomia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family, which includes coffee and gardenias. The plant is characterized by its orbicular (rounded) leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its stems are distinctly reddish, giving it its common name.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
