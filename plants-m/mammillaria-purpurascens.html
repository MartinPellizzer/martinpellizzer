Mammillaria purpurascens

Mammillaria purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-spined Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant features prominent, reddish-purple spines that distinguish it from other Mammillaria species. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and striking coloration make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
