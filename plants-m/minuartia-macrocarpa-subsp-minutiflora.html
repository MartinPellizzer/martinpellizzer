Minuartia macrocarpa subsp. minutiflora

Minuartia macrocarpa subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions. It typically forms dense mats of foliage and produces small, white or pale yellow flowers.

The plant has thin, wiry stems and narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is adapted to harsh environments, tolerating cold temperatures and poor soil conditions.

Its flowers bloom in early spring, contributing to the floral diversity of its habitat.
