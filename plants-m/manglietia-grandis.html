Manglietia grandis

Manglietia grandis, commonly known as the giant manglietia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers with a strong fragrance, which bloom in clusters. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.

Manglietia grandis is also notable for its slow growth and long lifespan, making it a rare and sought-after species.
