Maxillaria mosenii var. echinochila

Maxillaria mosenii var. echinochila, commonly known as the echinochila maxillaria, is a hybrid orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant features long, arching inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice for collectors. Its foliage consists of dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
