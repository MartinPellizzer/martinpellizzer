Mammillaria centricirrha var. macrothele

Mammillaria centricirrha var. macrothele, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stems that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features prominent, spiny areoles with clusters of white to pale yellow flowers. Its fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and desert landscapes due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
