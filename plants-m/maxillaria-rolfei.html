Maxillaria rolfei

Maxillaria rolfei, commonly known as the Rolfe's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-shaped leaves and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The flowers are typically borne on tall, slender stems and bloom during the warmer months. This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.

It requires well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
