Masdevallia richardsoniana

Masdevallia richardsoniana, commonly known as the Richardson's masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, often fragrant flowers that come in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-draining soil. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its natural habitat.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
