Millettia pulchra

Millettia pulchra, commonly known as the pink orchid or pink lady's slipper, is a striking member of the Fabaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its vibrant pink or purple flowers, which resemble orchids in appearance. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Millettia pulchra is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its role in traditional medicine.
