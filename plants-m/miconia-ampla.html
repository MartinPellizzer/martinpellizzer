Miconia ampla

Miconia ampla, commonly known as the giant miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that are often covered in a fuzzy coating. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia ampla is known for its rapid growth and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming an invasive species.
