Michelia fulgens

Michelia fulgens, commonly known as the fragrant beauty or Chinese star magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its flowers emit a sweet, spicy fragrance, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.

Michelia fulgens thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, preferring a humid climate.
