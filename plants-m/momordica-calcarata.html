Momordica calcarata

Momordica calcarata, commonly known as the horned melon, is a climbing vine native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive warty, spiky fruit that resembles a horned helmet. The plant belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes cucumbers and pumpkins. Its leaves are broad and heart-shaped, with a rough texture.
