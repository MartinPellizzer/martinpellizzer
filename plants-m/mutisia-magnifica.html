Mutisia magnifica

Mutisia magnifica, commonly known as the Andean beauty or golden mutisia, is a striking flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has palmate leaves with a waxy texture and a strong, somewhat unpleasant odor. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool, moist conditions.

Mutisia magnifica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
