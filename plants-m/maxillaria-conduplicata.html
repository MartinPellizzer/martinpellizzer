Maxillaria conduplicata

Maxillaria conduplicata, commonly known as the double-lobed orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its inflorescence typically bears multiple flowers arranged in a raceme.

The species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
