Myrcianthes karsteniana

Myrcianthes karsteniana, commonly known as the Karsten's myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
