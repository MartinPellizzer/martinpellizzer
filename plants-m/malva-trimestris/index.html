Malva Trimestris

Malva trimestris is a lesser-known species of flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family. It is native to parts of southern Africa, where it thrives in dry, rocky habitats. This plant is characterized by its hairy stems and distinctive, palmately lobed leaves. Its small, pinkish-white flowers bloom in late spring, adding a subtle beauty to its natural environment. Despite its modest appearance, Malva trimestris plays an important ecological role in its native ecosystem.
