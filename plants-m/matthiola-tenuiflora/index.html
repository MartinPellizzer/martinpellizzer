Matthiola Tenuiflora

Matthiola tenuiflora is a delicate flowering plant known for its vibrant, fragrant blooms. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in sunny, well-drained soils. This annual or biennial species is often found in rocky or coastal areas due to its drought-resistant nature. Its small, clustered flowers come in shades of white, pink, and purple, making it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements. Despite its modest size, Matthiola tenuiflora holds cultural and ecological significance in its native habitats.
