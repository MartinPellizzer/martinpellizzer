Monttea aphylla var. glandulifera

Monttea aphylla var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular-leafed monttea, is a rare and unique variant of the monttea genus.

It is characterized by its distinctive, glandular-dotted leaves that give it a textured appearance. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, usually greenish-white in color.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
