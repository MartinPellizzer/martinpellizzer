Masdevallia splendida var. parlatoreana

Masdevallia splendida var. parlatoreana, commonly known as the Parlatoreana Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It features vibrant, lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its showy flowers. The blooms are typically bright red or orange with intricate patterns, often resembling a spider's web, and are highly prized in horticulture. This variety thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a sought-after specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
