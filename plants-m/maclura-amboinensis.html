Maclura amboinensis

Maclura amboinensis, commonly known as the Amboina gooseberry, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, orange to red berries that are edible but have a tart, acidic flavor. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
