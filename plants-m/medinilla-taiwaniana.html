Medinilla taiwaniana

Medinilla taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan Medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant features glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, edible fruits that are sometimes used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Medinilla taiwaniana is also valued for its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
