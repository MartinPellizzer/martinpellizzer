Musa × sapientum var. baca

Musa × sapientum var. baca, commonly known as the Baca banana, is a cultivated variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and tender fruit with a sweet, creamy texture. The plant produces clusters of fruit, known as hands, which are typically yellow when ripe. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring warm temperatures and high humidity.

The leaves are broad and lance-shaped, contributing to the plant's ornamental appeal.
