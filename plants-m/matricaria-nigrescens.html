Matricaria nigrescens

Matricaria nigrescens, commonly known as the blackhead daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that consist of both ray and disk flowers. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are yellow with brown centers, and they bloom from late spring to early summer.

Matricaria nigrescens is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as rocky soils and open fields.
