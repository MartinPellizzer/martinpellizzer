Mussaenda pubescens f. clematidiflora

Mussaenda pubescens f. clematidiflora, commonly known as the clematidiflora Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its hairy stems and large, showy yellow-orange inflorescences. The leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine pubescence. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
