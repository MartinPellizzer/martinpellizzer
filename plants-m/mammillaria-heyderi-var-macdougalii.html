Mammillaria heyderi var. macdougalii

Mammillaria heyderi var. macdougalii, commonly known as the MacDougal's hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white, woolly spines and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for rock gardens or indoor cultivation. It requires well-draining soil and minimal water to thrive.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
