Melastoma saigonense

Melastoma saigonense, commonly known as the Vietnamese beautyberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its attractive, clustered, violet-blue berries that appear in late summer. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Melastoma saigonense is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth.
