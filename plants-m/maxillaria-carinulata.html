Maxillaria carinulata

Maxillaria carinulata, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive zigzag pattern on the petals. The flowers are typically white or pale pink, with a yellow lip that is often marked with purple or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
