Machaerium densicomum

Machaerium densicomum, commonly known as the densefeathered grass, is a tropical plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, feathery foliage and slender, branching stems. The plant typically grows in wetlands and marshy areas, where it thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its small, clustered flowers are usually pink or purple, adding ornamental value to its natural habitat.

Machaerium densicomum plays an important role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity in wetland ecosystems.
