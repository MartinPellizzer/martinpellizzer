Mammillaria rhodantha f. neglecta

Mammillaria rhodantha f. neglecta, commonly known as the Neglected Pink Globular Cactus, is a compact, spherical cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense cluster of short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale pink spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range from pink to red, often appearing in late spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
