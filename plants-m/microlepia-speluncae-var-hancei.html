Microlepia speluncae var. hancei

Microlepia speluncae var. hancei, commonly known as the cave microlepia, is a rare fern species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its delicate, slender fronds that often grow in shaded, humid environments. The plant typically thrives in rocky crevices or underground caves, where it forms dense, low-growing colonies. Its spores are dispersed through the air, allowing it to colonize new areas in its habitat.

Due to its unique ecological niche, it is considered a valuable indicator of undisturbed, subterranean ecosystems.
