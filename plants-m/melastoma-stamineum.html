Melastoma stamineum

Melastoma stamineum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical leaves that are opposite and covered in fine hairs. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Melastoma stamineum is often found in moist, shaded areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
