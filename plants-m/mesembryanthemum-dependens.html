Mesembryanthemum dependens

Mesembryanthemum dependens, commonly known as the hanging iceplant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that often hang down in a trailing habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
