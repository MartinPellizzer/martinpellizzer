Mandevilla oaxacana

Mandevilla oaxacana, commonly known as the Oaxaca mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant produces tubular, velvety leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a climber or shrub.

Mandevilla oaxacana is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various garden settings.
