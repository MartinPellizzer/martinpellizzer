Malva Wigandii

Malva wigandii is a striking flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa. Known for its vibrant pink to purple petals and fuzzy, heart-shaped leaves, it is a member of the Malvaceae family. This species thrives in harsh environments, making it a resilient choice for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Its unique appearance and ecological importance have made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike. Despite its beauty, Malva wigandii remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
