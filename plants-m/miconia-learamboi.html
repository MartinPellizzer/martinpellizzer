Miconia learamboi

Miconia learamboi, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that are often fragrant. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia learamboi is known for its rapid growth and has been introduced to other regions, sometimes becoming invasive.
