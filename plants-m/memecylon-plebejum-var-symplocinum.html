Memecylon plebejum var. symplocinum

Memecylon plebejum var. symplocinum, commonly known as the small memecylon, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree with opposite, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, fleshy berries. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it a notable species in the study of tropical flora and ecological diversity.
