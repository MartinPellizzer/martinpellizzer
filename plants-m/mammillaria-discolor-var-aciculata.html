Mammillaria discolor var. aciculata

Mammillaria discolor var. aciculata, commonly known as the needle-tipped mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with dense, spiny tubercles that give it a distinctive, needle-like appearance. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique morphology, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
