Marumia affinis

Marumia affinis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a compact shrub, reaching heights of up to 30 centimeters. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Marumia affinis is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
