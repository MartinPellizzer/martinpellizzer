Mitrephora keithii

Mitrephora keithii, commonly known as the Philippine satinwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellowish-white flowers. Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and cultural contexts.
