Mammillaria matudae

Mammillaria matudae, commonly known as the Matudae Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and typically has white to pale pink flowers. The plant features prominent areoles, from which both spines and flowers emerge. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
