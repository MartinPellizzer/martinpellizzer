Maxillaria venusta superba

Maxillaria venusta superba, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It features vibrant, showy flowers with a mix of purple, pink, and white hues, often with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are dark green and strap-like, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
