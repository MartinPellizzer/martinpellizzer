Mimosa falcipinna

Mimosa falcipinna, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Mimosa falcipinna is known for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate salt spray, which makes it a popular choice for erosion control.
