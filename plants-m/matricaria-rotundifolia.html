Matricaria rotundifolia

Matricaria rotundifolia, commonly known as German chamomile, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its daisy-like white flowers with a yellow center, which bloom throughout the summer. The plant has finely divided, aromatic leaves that are often used in herbal teas. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal cultivation.

Matricaria rotundifolia is valued for its calming properties and is often used in traditional medicine.
