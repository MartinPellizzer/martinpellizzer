Mammillaria melanacantha

Mammillaria melanacantha, commonly known as the black spine cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
