Masdevallia scopaea

Masdevallia scopaea, commonly known as the hooded masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and distinctive, hooded flower structure. The plant typically produces small, fragrant blooms in shades of white and pink, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure but requires careful moisture management.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
