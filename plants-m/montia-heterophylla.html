Montia heterophylla

Montia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable leaf speedwell, is a flowering plant native to western North America.

It is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which can range from ovate to linear, depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in the spring. It typically grows in moist, shaded areas such as forest floors and streambanks.
