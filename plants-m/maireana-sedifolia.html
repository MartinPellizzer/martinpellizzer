Maireana sedifolia

Maireana sedifolia, commonly known as the silky maireana, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage that gives it a distinctive, almost silvery appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and ornamental gardening.
