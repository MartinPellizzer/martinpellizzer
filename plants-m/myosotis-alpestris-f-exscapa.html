Myosotis alpestris f. exscapa

Myosotis alpestris f. exscapa, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that bear small, sky-blue to violet flowers with five petals. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving them a soft appearance. This plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in meadows and rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to high-altitude conditions with limited soil nutrients.
