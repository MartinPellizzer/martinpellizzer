Maxillaria variabilis subvar. lutea

Maxillaria variabilis subvar. lutea, commonly known as the Yellow Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a strong fragrance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are cylindrical, and it produces strap-like leaves that are green with a waxy texture.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
