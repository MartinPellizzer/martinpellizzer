Maxillaria monteverdensis

Maxillaria monteverdensis, commonly known as the Monteverde Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and pseudobulbs that support its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a yellowish lip, and bloom during the dry season.

Maxillaria monteverdensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in tropical gardens and greenhouses.
