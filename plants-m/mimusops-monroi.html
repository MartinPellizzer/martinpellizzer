Mimusops monroi

Mimusops monroi, commonly known as the African snowball, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in winter, giving it its common name. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mimusops monroi is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
