Mantisalca yserniana var. spinulosa

Mantisalca yserniana var. spinulosa, commonly known as the spiny mantisalca, is a succulent plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny stems and leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a striking addition to rock gardens and xeriscapes.
