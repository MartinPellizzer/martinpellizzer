Mimetes lyriger

Mimetes lyriger, commonly known as the lyre bell orchid, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, lyre-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its unique floral structure includes a tall, slender scape with clustered blossoms that resemble a lyre.

Mimetes lyriger is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
