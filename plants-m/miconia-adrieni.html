Miconia adrieni

Miconia adrieni, commonly known as the Adriani miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
