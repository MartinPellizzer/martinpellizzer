Muhlenbergia curviaristata f. nipponica

Muhlenbergia curviaristata f. nipponica, commonly known as the Japanese muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silvery-green foliage. The plant produces delicate, fluffy panicles of pale purple to pink flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a border plant.

This ornamental grass is valued for its graceful appearance and low maintenance requirements.
