Meibomia nudiflora f. foliolata

Meibomia nudiflora f. foliolata, commonly known as the leafy meibomia, is a variant of the meibomia plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive leaf morphology, featuring elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged in a whorled pattern. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, blooming during the warmer months. Its foliage is dense and provides good ground cover, making it suitable for ornamental use in shaded gardens.

The foliolata form is distinguished by its more pronounced leaf structure compared to the typical variety.
