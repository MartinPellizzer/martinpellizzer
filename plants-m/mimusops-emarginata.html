Mimusops emarginata

Mimusops emarginata, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It features smooth, grayish-brown bark that peels in thin, papery layers. The plant has opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers are clustered in umbels and bloom in the summer.

The tree produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed.
