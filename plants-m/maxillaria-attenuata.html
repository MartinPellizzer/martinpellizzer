Maxillaria attenuata

Maxillaria attenuata, commonly known as the slender maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with white petals and a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria attenuata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
