Masdevallia aurorae

Masdevallia aurorae, commonly known as the Aurora Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, brightly colored flowers that range from pink to purple, with intricate patterns resembling auroras. The plant typically grows as a small, compact orchid with pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. It thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Its unique floral display and rare occurrence in the wild make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
