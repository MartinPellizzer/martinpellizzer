Moraea moggii subsp. moggii

Moraea moggii subsp.

moggii, commonly known as the Natal starling lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces striking, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

The plant typically grows in open grasslands and is adapted to well-drained soils. It blooms during the cooler months, often in spring or early summer.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
