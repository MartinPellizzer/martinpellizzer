Mentha × niliaca var. promecophylla

Mentha × niliaca var. promecophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mint, is a hybrid species within the Mentha genus.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves that are typically narrow and pointed. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale blue or purple in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and is often used in herbal teas and culinary applications.
