Mercurialis perennis f. ovatifolia

Mercurialis perennis f. ovatifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf mercury plant, is a variant of the common mercury plant.

It is a herbaceous perennial native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It produces clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

This form is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and can thrive in well-drained soils.
