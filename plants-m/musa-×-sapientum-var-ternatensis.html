Musa × sapientum var. ternatensis

Musa × sapientum var. ternatensis, commonly known as the Ternate banana, is a variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its elongated, curved fruit that typically measures around 15 to 20 centimeters in length. The plant has large, broad leaves that are usually a deep green color with a waxy surface. It produces clusters of fruit known as hands, each containing multiple individual bananas.

This variety is native to Southeast Asia and is valued for its unique flavor and texture.
