Matricaria heterocarpa

Matricaria heterocarpa, commonly known as German chamomile, is a flowering plant in the daisy family (Asteraceae).

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it is characterized by its daisy-like yellow flowers with white ray florets. The plant is often cultivated for its aromatic properties and is used in herbal medicine. Its flowers contain essential oils that are used in teas and topical applications.

Matricaria heterocarpa thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
