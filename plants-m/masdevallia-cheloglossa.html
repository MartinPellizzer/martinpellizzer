Masdevallia cheloglossa

Masdevallia cheloglossa, commonly known as the "Lipstick Orchid," is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble small, colorful lips, which give it its common name. The plant typically grows as a miniature to small terrestrial orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence bears a few to several flowers.

Masdevallia cheloglossa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
