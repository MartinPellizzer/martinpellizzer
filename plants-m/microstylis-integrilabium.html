Microstylis integrilabium

Microstylis integrilabium, commonly known as the smooth-styled spikerush, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of pale green to brown flowers, which are typically wind-pollinated. Microstylis integrilabium thrives in wetland habitats, such as marshes and along the edges of ponds.

It is often found in temperate regions and plays a role in stabilizing soil in aquatic environments.
