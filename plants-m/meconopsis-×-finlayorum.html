Meconopsis × finlayorum

Meconopsis × finlayorum, commonly known as the Himalayan blue poppy, is a hybrid plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its striking blue or violet petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky, alpine environments where it thrives in cool and moist conditions. Its flowers bloom in early summer, adding vibrant color to the mountainous landscape.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
