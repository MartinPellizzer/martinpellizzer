Myoporum turbinatum

Myoporum turbinatum, commonly known as the Turban Myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slightly hairy texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a delicate floral display. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Myoporum turbinatum prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
