Muxiria

Muxiria, commonly known as the water lily, is a aquatic plant that thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

It features broad, floating leaves that provide shade and support for various aquatic life. The plant produces showy, often fragrant flowers that bloom above the water surface. Its roots anchor it in the sediment, while its rhizomes help in spreading vegetatively.

Muxiria plays a vital role in maintaining water quality and providing habitat for aquatic organisms.
