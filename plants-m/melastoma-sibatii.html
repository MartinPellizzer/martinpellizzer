Melastoma sibatii

Melastoma sibatii, commonly known as the Sibatii Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen shrub form. The plant produces showy, often pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its leaves are typically oval-shaped with a glossy surface, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Melastoma sibatii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
