Masdevallia mooreana

Masdevallia mooreana, commonly known as the Moore's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that display a variety of hues, including red, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it thrive in its natural habitat.

Masdevallia mooreana is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its unique and striking floral display.
