Micromeria teneriffae f. subramosa

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, adapting to the harsh environmental conditions of the Canary Islands. This plant plays a significant role in the local ecosystem and is of interest to botanists studying Mediterranean flora.
