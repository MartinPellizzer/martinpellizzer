Myristica intermedia var. minor

Myristica intermedia var. minor, commonly known as the lesser nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and oval-shaped fruits that contain aromatic seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a smooth texture with a slightly glossy surface.

The plant is valued for its essential oils and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
