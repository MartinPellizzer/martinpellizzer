Magnolia auriculata

Magnolia auriculata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture. The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall and is known for its ornamental value.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
