Mansoa schwackei

Mansoa schwackei, commonly known as the yellow mansoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces pods that contain small, hard seeds.

Mansoa schwackei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
