Myristica negrosensis

Myristica negrosensis, commonly known as the Negros nutmeg, is a species of flowering plant in the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the Philippines, specifically the island of Negros, where it grows in lowland rainforests. The plant produces a unique fruit that contains a dark, aromatic seed, which is used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its botanical features include a smooth, reddish-brown bark and lance-shaped leaves.

The species is of interest for its potential economic and medicinal value, though it faces threats from habitat loss and overharvesting.
