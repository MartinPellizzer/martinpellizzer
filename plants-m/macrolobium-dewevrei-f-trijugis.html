Macrolobium dewevrei f. trijugis

Macrolobium dewevrei f. trijugis, commonly known as the trijugate macrolobium, is a unique variant of the Macrolobium dewevrei species.

It is characterized by its distinctive trijugate leaves, which have three distinct lobes at the base. This plant is native to specific regions in Central Africa, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
