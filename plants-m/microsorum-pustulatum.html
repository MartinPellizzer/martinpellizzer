Microsorum pustulatum

Microsorum pustulatum, commonly known as the bubble moss, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, rounded, bubble-like spore cases that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments, such as ponds and streams. Its leaves are usually dark green and have a velvety texture.

Microsorum pustulatum is popular in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
