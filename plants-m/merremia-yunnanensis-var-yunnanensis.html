Merremia yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

Merremia yunnanensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan morning glory, is a flowering vine native to China.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and rocky slopes. It produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
