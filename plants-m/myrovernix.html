Myrovernix

Myrovernix, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a striking aquatic plant native to tropical regions.

It features large, showy flowers that bloom on long, slender stalks rising from water. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, aiding in photosynthesis. Its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate, while its rhizomes spread horizontally beneath the water.

Myrovernix is revered in many cultures for its symbolic and medicinal properties.
