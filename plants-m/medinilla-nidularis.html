Medinilla nidularis

Medinilla nidularis, commonly known as the hanging medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its foliage is evergreen, with broad, glossy leaves that add to its aesthetic appeal.
