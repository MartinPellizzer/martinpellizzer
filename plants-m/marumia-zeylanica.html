Marumia zeylanica

Marumia zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon nightshade, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and potatoes. The plant features erect stems with opposite, ovate leaves that have a slightly hairy texture. Its flowers are typically white or pale blue, with five petals and a yellow center, blooming in the evening.

The fruit is a small, round berry that turns from green to red as it matures.
