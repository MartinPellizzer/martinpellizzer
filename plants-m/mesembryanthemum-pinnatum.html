Mesembryanthemum pinnatum

Mesembryanthemum pinnatum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, paddle-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white powder. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for arid and semi-arid environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
