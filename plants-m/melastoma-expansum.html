Melastoma expansum

Melastoma expansum, commonly known as the broadleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, violet to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are usually broad and opposite, with a velvety texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
