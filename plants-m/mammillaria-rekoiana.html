Mammillaria rekoiana

Mammillaria rekoiana, commonly known as the Rekoiana Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale blue hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
