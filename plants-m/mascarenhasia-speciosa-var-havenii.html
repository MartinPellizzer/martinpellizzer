Mascarenhasia speciosa var. havenii

Mascarenhasia speciosa var. havenii, commonly known as the Mascarene star orchid, is a rare and striking orchid species native to the Mascarene Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with a distinctive star-shaped pattern on the petals. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in coastal forests or on rocky outcrops. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, with a waxy surface that helps retain moisture in its native habitat.
