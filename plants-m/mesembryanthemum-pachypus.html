Mesembryanthemum pachypus

Mesembryanthemum pachypus, commonly known as the thickstem ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant thrives in arid environments and is adapted to store water in its thick leaves and stems. It produces vibrant pink or purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer.
