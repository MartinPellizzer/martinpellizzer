Microdon Dubius

Microdon dubius is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the temperate regions of Europe. It is known for its intricate, star-shaped blooms that appear in late spring. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are often overlooked due to their modest size but are rich in nectar, attracting a variety of pollinators. Conservation efforts are crucial for preserving this rare and beautiful plant.
