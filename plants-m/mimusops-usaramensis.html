Mimusops usaramensis

Mimusops usaramensis, commonly known as the Usaramensis satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and elliptical, glossy leaves.
