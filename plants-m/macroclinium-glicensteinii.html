Macroclinium glicensteinii

Macroclinium glicensteinii, commonly known as the golden macroclinium, is a striking orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow and green foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its intricate floral structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically arranged in clustered inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
