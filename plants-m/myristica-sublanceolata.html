Myristica sublanceolata

Myristica sublanceolata, commonly known as the narrow-lanceleaf nutmeg, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fleshy, orange-red berries containing aromatic seeds. Its essential oils and spices are valued for their culinary and medicinal uses.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
