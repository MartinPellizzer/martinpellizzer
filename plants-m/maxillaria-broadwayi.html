Maxillaria broadwayi

Maxillaria broadwayi, commonly known as the Broadway orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers that have a distinctive, narrow lip. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.

Maxillaria broadwayi is known for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
