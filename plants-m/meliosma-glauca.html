Meliosma glauca

It is a deciduous shrub characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical with a smooth margin and a glossy surface. It produces small, oval berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.
