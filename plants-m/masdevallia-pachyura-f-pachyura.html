Masdevallia pachyura f. pachyura

Masdevallia pachyura f. pachyura, commonly known as the Pachyura Masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, intricate flower structure with prominent, showy sepals and petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its botanical features include a short, erect stem and a dense, velvety leaf cover.

This orchid is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and delicate beauty.
