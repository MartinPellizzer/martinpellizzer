Malus canicularis

Malus canicularis, commonly known as the Chinese white pear, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and alternate, simple leaves. The tree produces small, white flowers in the spring, which are followed by edible, sweet, and aromatic fruits.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and fruit production in temperate climates.
