Megaclinium sandersonii

Megaclinium sandersonii, commonly known as the sanderson's sedge, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Cyperaceae family, which includes sedges and grass-like plants. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in wetlands, marshes, and along stream banks. Its slender, triangular stems are often clustered, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers in spikelets.
