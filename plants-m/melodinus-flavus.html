Melodinus flavus

Melodinus flavus, commonly known as the golden melodinus, is a tropical flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow flowers, which bloom in clusters and emit a sweet fragrance. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Melodinus flavus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
