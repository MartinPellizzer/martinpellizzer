Maukschia laevis

Maukschia laevis, commonly known as the smooth maukschia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of South America.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in a smooth, glabrous surface. The plant produces small, white to pale pink flowers arranged in dense, spike-like clusters. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a waxy texture.

Maukschia laevis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
