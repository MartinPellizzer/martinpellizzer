Mandevilla duartei

Mandevilla duartei, commonly known as the Duarte's mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves that are often velvety in texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla duartei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
