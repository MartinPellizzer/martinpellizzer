Maranta sobolifera

Maranta sobolifera, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant produces small, white flowers that are borne on erect spikes, typically appearing in late summer. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread easily in moist, shaded environments.

Maranta sobolifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
