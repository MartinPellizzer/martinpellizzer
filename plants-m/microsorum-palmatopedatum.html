Microsorum palmatopedatum

Microsorum palmatopedatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed sword fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad, lance-shaped fronds that are often divided into smaller, serrated segments. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving freshwater environments such as ponds and streams. Its rhizomes form dense mats, helping to stabilize sediment and improve water quality.

Microsorum palmatopedatum is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of water conditions.
