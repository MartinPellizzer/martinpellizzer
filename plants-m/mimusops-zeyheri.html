Mimusops zeyheri

Mimusops zeyheri, commonly known as the Natal mahogany, is a medium-sized evergreen tree native to southern Africa.

It features smooth, greyish bark and elliptical leaves that are glossy on the upper surface. The tree produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in summer, followed by orange-red, fleshy fruits. Its wood is highly valued for its durability and fine grain, making it important in local timber industries.

The plant is also used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
