Microcharis microcharoides

Microcharis microcharoides, commonly known as the dwarf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 10-30 centimeters. The plant produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are usually blue or violet in color. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its attractive blooms and compact size.
