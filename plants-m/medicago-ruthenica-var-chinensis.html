Medicago ruthenica var. chinensis

Medicago ruthenica var. chinensis, commonly known as Chinese clover, is a perennial legume native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils and tolerate drought conditions. It is often used in erosion control and as a forage crop due to its nutritional value.

Additionally, it plays a role in nitrogen fixation, enriching the soil with essential nutrients.
