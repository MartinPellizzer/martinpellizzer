Maxillaria mungoschraderi

Maxillaria mungoschraderi, commonly known as the Schrader's maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually pink to reddish in color and bloom during the late spring or early summer.

Maxillaria mungoschraderi is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
