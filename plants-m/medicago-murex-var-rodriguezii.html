Medicago murex var. rodriguezii

Medicago murex var. rodriguezii, commonly known as the Rodriguez's goatweed, is a variant of the goat's rue plant native to specific regions of the Mediterranean.

It is a herbaceous annual plant characterized by its slender stems and trifoliate leaves. The flowers are typically small, pale blue to purple, and arranged in racemose inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed or arid environments.

This variant is of interest to botanists due to its unique genetic traits and ecological adaptations.
