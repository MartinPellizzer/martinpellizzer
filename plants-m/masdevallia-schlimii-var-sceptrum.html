Masdevallia schlimii var. sceptrum

Masdevallia schlimii var. sceptrum, commonly known as the Crowned Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, long-lasting blooms that often feature a unique crown-like pattern on the petals. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, violet to purple flowers with intricate markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively manageable care requirements.
