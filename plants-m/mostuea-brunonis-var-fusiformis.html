Mostuea brunonis var. fusiformis

Mostuea brunonis var. fusiformis, commonly known as the narrow-leafed dwarf banana, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features a short, stocky rhizome with narrow, elongated leaves that are typically dark green and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear yellowish-white flowers, followed by small, round berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Due to its compact size, it is suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant in humid climates.
