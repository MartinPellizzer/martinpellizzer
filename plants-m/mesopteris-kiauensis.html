Mesopteris kiauensis

Mesopteris kiauensis, commonly known as the giant water fern, is a unique fern species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, floating fronds that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant thrives in aquatic or semi-aquatic environments, often found in slow-moving freshwater habitats. Its fronds are divided into numerous pinnae, which are covered in small, delicate pinnae.

The plant plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and oxygen in its native wetland ecosystems.
