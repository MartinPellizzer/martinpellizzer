Mesosphaerum velutinum

Mesosphaerum velutinum, commonly known as the velvetleaf mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Mesosphaerum velutinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
