Medicago lessingii

Medicago lessingii, commonly known as the lesser medic, is a flowering plant native to the western United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is often found in desert and semi-arid environments. It has a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh, arid conditions.

Medicago lessingii is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility in its native habitats.
