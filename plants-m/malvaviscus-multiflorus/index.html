Malvaviscus Multiflorus

Malvaviscus multiflorus is a unique flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas. It is known for its vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in clusters throughout the growing season. The plant has a trailing habit and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil to flourish. This species is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.
