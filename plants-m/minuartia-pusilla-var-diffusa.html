Minuartia pusilla var. diffusa

Minuartia pusilla var. diffusa, commonly known as the dwarf rockcress, is a low-growing flowering plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold climates. It is often found in mountainous areas and tundra environments.
