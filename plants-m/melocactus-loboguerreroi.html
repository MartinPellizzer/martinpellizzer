Melocactus loboguerreroi

Melocactus loboguerreroi, commonly known as the Loboguerrero cactus, is a species native to the tropical highlands of Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, columnar stem and dense covering of spines, which provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the rainy season, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
