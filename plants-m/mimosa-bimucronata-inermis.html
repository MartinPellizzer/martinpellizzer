Mimosa bimucronata inermis

Mimosa bimucronata inermis, commonly known as the soft mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. It has a shallow root system and thrives in well-drained soils.

Its rapid growth and ability to spread through seeds make it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential weed in certain environments.
