Meliosma pinnata subsp. macrophylla

Meliosma pinnata subsp.

macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae. It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often pubescent on the underside.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers with white petals and yellow stamens, typically blooming in the spring. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
