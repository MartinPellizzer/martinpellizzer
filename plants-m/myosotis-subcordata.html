Myosotis subcordata

Myosotis subcordata, commonly known as the New Zealand forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. It belongs to the genus Myosotis, which includes several species of flowering plants found in the Northern Hemisphere.

Myosotis subcordata is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in New Zealand's native flora.
