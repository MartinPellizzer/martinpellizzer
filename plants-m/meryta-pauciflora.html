Meryta pauciflora

Meryta pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered meryta, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest habitats. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often pubescent and have a leathery texture. Its small, white or pale yellow flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences.

Meryta pauciflora is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage and flowering display.
