Marrubium × virescens

Marrubium × virescens, commonly known as the green horehound, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Boraginaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, often glandular stems and leaves, which are typically gray-green in color. Marrubium × virescens is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported medicinal properties.
