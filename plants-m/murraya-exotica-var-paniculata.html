Murraya exotica var. paniculata

Murraya exotica var. paniculata, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright red to orange flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to the landscape. The plant has glossy, dark green, trifoliate leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its showy blooms. It produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by birds and other animals.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces due to its seasonal display of colorful flowers.
