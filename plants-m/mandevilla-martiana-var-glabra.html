Mandevilla martiana var. glabra

Mandevilla martiana var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth mandevilla, is a vigorous climbing plant native to South America.

It features lush, evergreen foliage with large, heart-shaped leaves that are smooth and glossy. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its rapid growth and ornamental value, it is often used in gardens and as a houseplant.
