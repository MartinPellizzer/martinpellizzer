Mortolopuntia schickendantzii

Mortolopuntia schickendantzii, commonly known as the Schickendantzii prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its flat, paddle-shaped stems that are covered in clusters of sharp spines. The plant produces yellow or orange flowers during the summer months, which are followed by edible, fleshy fruits. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sun exposure.

Its structure and adaptations make it well-suited for desert landscapes and xeriscaping.
