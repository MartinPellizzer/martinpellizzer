Memorialis bracteata

Memorialis bracteata, commonly known as the golden bract fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feather-like fronds that are typically golden-yellow in color. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by spore-bearing cones, typical of ferns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardens.
