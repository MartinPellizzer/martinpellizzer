Myosotis decumbens subsp. decumbens

Myosotis decumbens subsp.

decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate forget-me-not, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern South America. It features slender, prostrate stems that root at the nodes, forming a mat-like growth habit.

The plant produces small, delicate blue or violet flowers with five petals, typical of the Myosotis genus. Its leaves are alternate, ovate to lanceolate, and covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.
