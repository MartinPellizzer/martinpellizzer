Myosotis heteropoda

Myosotis heteropoda, commonly known as the variable forget-me-not, is a flowering plant species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The plant typically grows in damp, shaded areas such as forest floors and streambanks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture.
