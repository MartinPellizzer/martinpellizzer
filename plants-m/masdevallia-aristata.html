Masdevallia aristata

Masdevallia aristata, commonly known as the aristate masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, lance-shaped leaves and striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, thriving in cool, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers feature a unique structure with a spurred lip and showy petals, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.

Masdevallia aristata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its beauty and resilience in controlled environments.
