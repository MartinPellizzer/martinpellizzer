Masdevallia × senghasiana

Masdevallia × senghasiana, commonly known as the Senghasian orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique and delicate beauty.
