Medinilla hollrungiana

Medinilla hollrungiana, commonly known as the Christmas Medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, dark green leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in warm climates. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Medinilla hollrungiana is also noted for its ability to bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
