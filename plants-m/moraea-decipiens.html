Moraea decipiens

Moraea decipiens, commonly known as the deceptive moraea, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that resemble those of other ornamental plants. The plant produces clusters of tubular, often brightly colored flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Moraea decipiens is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
