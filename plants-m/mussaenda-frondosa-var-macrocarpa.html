Mussaenda frondosa var. macrocarpa

Mussaenda frondosa var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the giant flame bush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, bright orange to red bracts that surround small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant has large, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and prominent veins. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
