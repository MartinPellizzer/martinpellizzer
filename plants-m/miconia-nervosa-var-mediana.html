Miconia nervosa var. mediana

Miconia nervosa var. mediana, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and have a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

This species is often found in moist, shaded environments and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
