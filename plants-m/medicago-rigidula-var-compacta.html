Medicago rigidula var. compacta

Medicago rigidula var. compacta, commonly known as dwarf medic, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. The plant is adapted to rocky and dry soils, making it a hardy species suitable for xeriscaping. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a useful plant in ecological restoration projects.

It is also valued for its role in nitrogen fixation, benefiting surrounding vegetation.
