Moraea striata

Moraea striata, commonly known as the striped moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant features strap-shaped leaves that are typically grass-like in appearance. Moraea striata thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and floral arrangements.
