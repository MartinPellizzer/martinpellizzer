Melinis trichotoma

Melinis trichotoma, commonly known as the hairy grass or slender grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves with a hairy texture. The plant produces spikelets that are typically arranged in clusters, and its flowers are small and inconspicuous. Melinis trichotoma is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
