Maytenus commutata

Maytenus commutata, commonly known as the Brazilian mayten, is a deciduous tree native to South America.

It belongs to the family Rutaceae and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist forested areas.

Maytenus commutata is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
