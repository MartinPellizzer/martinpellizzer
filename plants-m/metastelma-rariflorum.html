Metastelma rariflorum

Metastelma rariflorum, commonly known as the rare-flowered metastelma, is a unique plant species native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that feature small, clustered flowers with intricate petal arrangements. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil to flourish. Its botanical significance lies in its rarity and the complexity of its floral structure, which has made it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered an endangered species in its natural range.
