Macrocentrum cristatum var. microphylla

Macrocentrum cristatum var. microphylla, commonly known as the miniature crowned fern, is a unique variant of the crowned fern species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate fronds that are densely packed. The plant features a short, upright rhizome with a crown of tightly clustered leaves. Its fronds are typically bi- or tri-pinnate, with a narrow, lanceolate shape.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings and as a potted plant.
