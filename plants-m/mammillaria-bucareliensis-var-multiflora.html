Mammillaria bucareliensis var. multiflora

Mammillaria bucareliensis var. multiflora, commonly known as the Bucareli's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or slightly cylindrical stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and collections.
