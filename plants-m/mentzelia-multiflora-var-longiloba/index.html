Mentzelia Multiflora Var. Longiloba

Mentzelia multiflora var. longiloba is a rare and striking desert plant native to the southwestern United States. Known for its vibrant yellow flowers and unique leaf structure, it thrives in harsh, arid environments. This variant of Mentzelia is particularly adapted to extreme heat and limited water availability. Its ecological significance lies in its role as a food source for native pollinators and its contribution to desert biodiversity. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this delicate species from habitat loss and climate change.
