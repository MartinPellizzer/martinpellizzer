Mikaniopsis camarae

Mikaniopsis camarae, commonly known as the Camarae Mikaniopsis, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange. It is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests and riverbanks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
