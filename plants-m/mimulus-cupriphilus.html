Mimulus cupriphilus

Mimulus cupriphilus, commonly known as copperleaf monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking copper-colored leaves and bright orange to red flowers. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in streamside habitats. It belongs to the family Plantaginaceae and is known for its ability to tolerate heavy metals in the soil.
