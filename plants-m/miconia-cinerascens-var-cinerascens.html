Miconia cinerascens var. cinerascens

Miconia cinerascens var. cinerascens, commonly known as the cinerascens miconia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a large shrub or small tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, characterized by its broad, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are typically followed by distinctive, elongated fruits. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.

The species is also noted for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
