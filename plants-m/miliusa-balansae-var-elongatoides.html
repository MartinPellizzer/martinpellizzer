Miliusa balansae var. elongatoides

Miliusa balansae var. elongatoides, commonly known as the elongated miliusa, is a species native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the Philippines.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 20 meters in height, characterized by its glossy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, followed by edible, fleshy berries.

It thrives in tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
