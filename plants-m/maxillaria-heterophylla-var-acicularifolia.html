Maxillaria heterophylla var. acicularifolia

Maxillaria heterophylla var. acicularifolia, commonly known as the needle-leaf maxillaria, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, needle-like leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant orchid flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
