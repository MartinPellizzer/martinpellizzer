Mammillaria lasiacantha f. magallanii

Mammillaria lasiacantha f. magallanii, commonly known as the Magallanii mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its gray-green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
