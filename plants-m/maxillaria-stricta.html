Maxillaria stricta

Maxillaria stricta, commonly known as the rigid maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, pleated leaves. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers with white to pale pink petals and a distinctive, often yellow or orange-colored lip. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Maxillaria stricta is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
