Melastoma picklesii

Melastoma picklesii, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a rough texture, while its stems are often woody.
