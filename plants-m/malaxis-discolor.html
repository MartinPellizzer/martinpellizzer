Malaxis discolor

Malaxis discolor, commonly known as the pale orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Malaxis discolor is valued for its unique floral structure and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
