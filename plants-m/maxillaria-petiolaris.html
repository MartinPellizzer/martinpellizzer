Maxillaria petiolaris

Maxillaria petiolaris, commonly known as the petioled maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features long, slender stems with clustered, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale green to yellow, with a distinct, hooded lip.

Maxillaria petiolaris is prized for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
