Monotropa morisonii

It is a member of the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its lack of chlorophyll, making it a mycoheterotroph that obtains nutrients through a symbiotic relationship with fungi. Due to its unique ecological role and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and specialized species.
