Medicago murex proles ovata

Medicago murex proles ovata, commonly known as the oval-leaf sickle vetch, is a leguminous plant characterized by its distinctive ovate leaves and yellow flowers.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is native to parts of Europe and Asia. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in sunny, well-drained environments.

Its botanical features make it a valuable plant for ecological studies and agricultural use.
