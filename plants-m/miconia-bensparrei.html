Miconia bensparrei

Miconia bensparrei, commonly known as the Bensparri miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, palmate leaves with five to seven lobes. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in clusters at the ends of branches. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Miconia bensparrei is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
