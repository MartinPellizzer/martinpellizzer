Maxillaria viridis var. pluriflora

Maxillaria viridis var. pluriflora, commonly known as the green maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, greenish-white flowers with yellow markings, often blooming in late winter to early spring. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it suitable for both indoor and greenhouse cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
