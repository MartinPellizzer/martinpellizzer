Microlaena stipoides var. micrantha

Microlaena stipoides var. micrantha, commonly known as slender weeping grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to southeastern Australia.

It features fine, needle-like leaves that are typically a pale blue-green color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that develop into fluffy, seed-bearing spikes, which are adapted for wind dispersal. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Its ecological importance includes providing habitat and food for various insect species.
