Mesechites

Mesechites, commonly known as the lesser nettle, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Urticaceae family.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Mesechites is often found in woodland areas and along stream banks.

Its leaves contain stinging hairs that can cause irritation upon contact with the skin.
