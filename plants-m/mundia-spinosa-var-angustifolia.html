Mundia spinosa var. angustifolia

Mundia spinosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mundia, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, often spiny stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal uses, it is occasionally cultivated in horticultural settings.
