Mimusops longipes

Mimusops longipes, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a tropical tree native to Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, light-colored bark and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Mimusops longipes is valued for its durable wood and is often used in traditional medicine and ornamental horticulture.
