Mallotus rhamnifolius var. ovatifolius

Mallotus rhamnifolius var. ovatifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf mallotus, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub with simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate or elliptic in shape. The leaves are usually glossy and dark green, with a leathery texture. It produces small, greenish-white flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds.

This plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional uses.
