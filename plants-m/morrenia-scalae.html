Morrenia scalae

Morrenia scalae, commonly known as the mountain beauty, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, bright yellow or orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or open areas, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are woody and often branched.

Morrenia scalae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
