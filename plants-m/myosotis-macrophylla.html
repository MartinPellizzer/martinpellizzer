Myosotis macrophylla

Myosotis macrophylla, commonly known as the giant forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue to purple petals and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in wetland or bog environments, thriving in moist, nutrient-rich soils. Its flowers bloom in spring and are often clustered in loose racemes.

Myosotis macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate water conditions.
