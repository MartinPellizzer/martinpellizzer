Melastoma heterophyllum

Melastoma heterophyllum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its flowers are clustered in cymes and are usually pink or white, with five petals and prominent stamens.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
