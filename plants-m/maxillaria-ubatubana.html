Maxillaria ubatubana

Maxillaria ubatubana, commonly known as the Ubatuba orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and attractive, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs and has linear, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with a distinctive yellow lip, and it blooms during the summer months.

Maxillaria ubatubana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
