Millettia austroyunnanensis

Millettia austroyunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan millettia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in forested areas.

Its wood is valued for its strength and is used in traditional craftsmanship.
