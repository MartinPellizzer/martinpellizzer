Munnozia campii

Munnozia campii, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its striking, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid environments. It has narrow, linear leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small seeds.

Munnozia campii is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it a resilient species in its native habitat.
