Micrechites andamanicus

Micrechites andamanicus, commonly known as the Andaman dwarf orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to the Andaman Islands.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact stature and delicate flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually small and exhibit a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
