Mikania martiana

Mikania martiana, commonly known as climbing hemp vine, is a vigorous, herbaceous vine native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its opposite, lance-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant has fibrous roots and can grow rapidly, often climbing or trailing along the ground. Its stems are covered with fine hairs, and it is known for its ability to spread aggressively in disturbed areas.

Mikania martiana is often found in moist, sunny environments such as forests and along stream banks.
