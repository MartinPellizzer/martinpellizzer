Mediolobivia haagei var. orurensis

Mediolobivia haagei var. orurensis, commonly known as the golden spire plant, is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a tall, branching stem covered in dense, cylindrical leaves that are typically a vibrant yellow-green color. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its striking appearance and adaptability, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
