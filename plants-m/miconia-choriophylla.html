Miconia choriophylla

Miconia choriophylla, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep purple or dark green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Miconia choriophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.
