Michelia caloptila

Michelia caloptila, commonly known as the fragrant michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its fruit is a small, spherical drupe that turns from green to red as it matures.

Michelia caloptila is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and pleasant fragrance.
