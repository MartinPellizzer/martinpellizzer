Magnolia pilosissima

Magnolia pilosissima, commonly known as the silky magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The tree has a slow growth rate and can reach heights of up to 20 meters. Its leaves are broad, ovate, and covered in a fine, silvery down, giving it a distinctive appearance.

The bark is smooth and gray, becoming fissured with age, and the tree is valued for its ornamental beauty in gardens.
