Medicago todaroana

Medicago todaroana, commonly known as the Turkish clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in arid and rocky soils. It is often found in disturbed areas and along roadsides.

Medicago todaroana is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in soil restoration efforts.
