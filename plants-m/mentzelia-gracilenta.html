Mentzelia gracilenta

Mentzelia gracilenta, commonly known as slender mentzelia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a herbaceous annual that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-yellow and are arranged in clusters.

Mentzelia gracilenta is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in disturbed soils or open habitats.
