Mirabilis ovata f. pantothrix

Mirabilis ovata f. pantothrix, commonly known as the starry nightshade, is a striking ornamental plant characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Nyctaginaceae family and is native to tropical regions of the Americas. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a velvety texture, and its flowers are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive star-shaped appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.
