Muhlenbergia jonesii

Muhlenbergia jonesii, commonly known as Jones' muhlenbergia, is a species of grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, gray-green blades. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear feathery, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

Muhlenbergia jonesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
