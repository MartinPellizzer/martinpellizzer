Mammillaria gaumeri

Mammillaria gaumeri, commonly known as the Gaumer's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and spines that are often yellow or orange in color. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria gaumeri is prized for its compact size and unique appearance in succulent collections.
