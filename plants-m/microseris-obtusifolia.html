Microseris obtusifolia

Microseris obtusifolia, commonly known as the bluntleaf rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate, bluish-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellow, and arranged in clusters, typical of the family.

It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.
