Mammillaria mazatlanensis subsp. patonii

Mammillaria mazatlanensis subsp.

patonii, commonly known as the Paton's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular to cylindrical stem with white to pale green spines and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
