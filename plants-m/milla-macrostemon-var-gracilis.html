Milla macrostemon var. gracilis

Milla macrostemon var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender milla, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often grown in ornamental gardens and as a decorative plant.
