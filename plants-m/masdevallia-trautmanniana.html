Masdevallia trautmanniana

Masdevallia trautmanniana, commonly known as the Trautmann's masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, intricate flowers that often display a mix of red, yellow, and green hues. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and elongated leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments with partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its unique floral structure and colorful blooms make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
