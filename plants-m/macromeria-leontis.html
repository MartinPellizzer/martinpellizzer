Macromeria leontis

Macromeria leontis, commonly known as the lion's tail, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Macromeria leontis is also valued for its resilience and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
