Malanea obovata

Malanea obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate malanea, is a flowering plant native to the southern regions of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in forest understories.
