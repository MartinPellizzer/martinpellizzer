Mertensia paniculata var. laevigata

Mertensia paniculata var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth bluebells, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to violet in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for early pollinators.
