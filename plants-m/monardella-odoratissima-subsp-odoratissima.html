Monardella odoratissima subsp. odoratissima

Monardella odoratissima subsp.

odoratissima, commonly known as the fragrant monardella, is a perennial herb native to California. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants.

The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of pink to purple. It is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.

Its strong, sweet fragrance makes it a notable plant in its native environment.
