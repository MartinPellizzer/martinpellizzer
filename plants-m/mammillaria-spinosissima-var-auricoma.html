Mammillaria spinosissima var. auricoma

Mammillaria spinosissima var. auricoma, commonly known as the auricoma mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem covered in small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
