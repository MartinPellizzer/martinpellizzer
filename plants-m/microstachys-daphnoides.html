Microstachys daphnoides

Microstachys daphnoides, commonly known as the silverleaf daphniphyllum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that often have a silvery or grayish underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening and as a decorative plant in tropical landscapes.
