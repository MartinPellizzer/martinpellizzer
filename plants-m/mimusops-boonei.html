Mimusops boonei

Mimusops boonei, commonly known as the Boone's African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by spherical, woody fruits containing several seeds.
