Mesosphaerum cordatum

Mesosphaerum cordatum, commonly known as the heart-shaped leaf shrub, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, tropical environments and thrives in well-drained soil. Its botanical features make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
