Mammillaria sphaerotricha

Mammillaria sphaerotricha, commonly known as the globular mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, spherical shape and is covered in white to yellow spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a dense, woolly appearance. It produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by small, red fruits.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
