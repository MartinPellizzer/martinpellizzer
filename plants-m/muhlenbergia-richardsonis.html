Muhlenbergia richardsonis

Muhlenbergia richardsonis, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a grass species native to the central and southern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and tufted inflorescences that bloom in late summer. This perennial plant thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for native landscaping. Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense clumps make it an effective erosion control plant.

Muhlenbergia richardsonis also provides habitat and food for various wildlife species.
