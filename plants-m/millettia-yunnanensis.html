Millettia yunnanensis

Millettia yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan Millettia, is a deciduous tree native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
