Mediocactus vagans

Mediocactus vagans, commonly known as the wandering cactus, is a species of cactus native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems that can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, which help minimize water loss in its harsh environment.

Mediocactus vagans is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils and full sunlight.
