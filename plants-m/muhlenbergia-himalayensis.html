Muhlenbergia himalayensis

Muhlenbergia himalayensis, commonly known as the Himalayan muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the mountainous regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and fine, silver-green foliage. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often visible during the late summer and autumn seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaped landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
