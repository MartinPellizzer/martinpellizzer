Mussaenda nivea

Mussaenda nivea, commonly known as the white mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, white bract-like flowers that resemble petals, which are actually modified leaves. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with smooth, glossy green leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mussaenda nivea is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
