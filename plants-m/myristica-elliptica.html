Myristica elliptica

Myristica elliptica, commonly known as the elliptical nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the Indo-Pacific region, including parts of Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its fruit is a woody capsule that contains aromatic seeds used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.

The tree is valued for its essential oils and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
