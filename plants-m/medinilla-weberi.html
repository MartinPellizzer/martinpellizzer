Medinilla weberi

Medinilla weberi, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces striking, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant blooms.

Medinilla weberi is prized in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in tropical and subtropical regions.
