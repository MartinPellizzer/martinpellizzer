Millettia oblata

Millettia oblata, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing shrub native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its cascading blooms.

Millettia oblata is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
