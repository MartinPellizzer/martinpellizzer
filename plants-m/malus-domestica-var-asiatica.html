Malus domestica var. asiatica

Malus domestica var. asiatica, commonly known as the Asian apple, is a cultivar of the domestic apple tree.

It is characterized by its medium-sized, round fruits that typically have a smooth, glossy skin. The fruit color ranges from yellow to red, depending on the specific variety and ripening conditions. It is known for its aromatic fragrance and crisp, juicy flesh.

This variety is often grown for both its ornamental value and edible fruit in temperate climates.
