Mesembryanthemum rheolens

Mesembryanthemum rheolens, commonly known as the yellow bush or yellow mesem, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to drought conditions. Its unique morphology includes a prostrate or upright growth habit, depending on environmental conditions.

Mesembryanthemum rheolens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.
