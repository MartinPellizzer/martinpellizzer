Mussaenda erosa

Mussaenda erosa, commonly known as the velvet flower or velvet plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features soft, velvety leaves that are opposite and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Mussaenda erosa is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
