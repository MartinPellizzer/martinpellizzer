Myristica stenocarpa

Myristica stenocarpa, commonly known as the narrow-fruit nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule-like fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its seeds are used to produce nutmeg and mace, which are valued for their spice and medicinal properties.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
