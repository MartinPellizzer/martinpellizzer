Muhlenbergia magna

Muhlenbergia magna, commonly known as the giant muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, bluish-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, fluffy seed heads that are highly visible during the late summer and early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Muhlenbergia magna is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
