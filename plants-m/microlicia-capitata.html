Microlicia capitata

Microlicia capitata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant has fleshy, velvety leaves that are often used as a traditional remedy in some cultures. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light to flourish.

Microlicia capitata is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of cultivation.
