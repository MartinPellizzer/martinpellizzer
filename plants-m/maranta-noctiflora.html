Maranta noctiflora

Maranta noctiflora, commonly known as the night-flowering maranta, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that open at night and close during the day. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its unique nocturnal blooming habit makes it a fascinating subject for botanical study.
