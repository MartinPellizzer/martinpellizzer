Mammillaria pusilla f. major

Mammillaria pusilla f. major, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale blue spines.

It features small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. The plant is native to Mexico and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a favored plant among cactus enthusiasts.
