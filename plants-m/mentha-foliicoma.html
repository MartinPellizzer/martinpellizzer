Mentha foliicoma

Mentha foliicoma, commonly known as the leafy mint, is a species of flowering plant in the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its square, hairy stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue or purple, arranged in whorled clusters. Mentha foliicoma is native to parts of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
