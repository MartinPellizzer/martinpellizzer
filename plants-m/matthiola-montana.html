Matthiola montana

Matthiola montana, commonly known as the mountain stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other mustard family plants. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in sunny environments. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and often white or pink in color, appearing in clusters.

Matthiola montana is known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
