Malus schischkinii

Malus schischkinii, commonly known as the Shishkin apple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white flowers that appear before the leaves in spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically red or yellow in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact growth habit and seasonal display make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
