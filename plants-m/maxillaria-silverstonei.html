Maxillaria silverstonei

Maxillaria silverstonei, commonly known as the Silverstone orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces inflorescences bearing small, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often in shades of white and green. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria silverstonei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
