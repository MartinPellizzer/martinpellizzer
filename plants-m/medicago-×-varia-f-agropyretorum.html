Medicago × varia f. agropyretorum

Medicago × varia f. agropyretorum, commonly known as the yellow medick, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves with a distinctive yellowish-green color and small, pea-like flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

Its ornamental value and ecological benefits make it a useful plant in restoration and landscaping projects.
