Marsdenia pauciflora

Marsdenia pauciflora, commonly known as the few-flowered Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including China, India, and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically opposite and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Marsdenia pauciflora is often found in forested areas and is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
