Monstera kessleri

Monstera kessleri, commonly known as the Split-leaf Monstera, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive splits as the plant matures. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is a popular houseplant due to its striking foliage and relatively easy care requirements.

Monstera kessleri is also known for its ability to purify the air, making it a beneficial addition to indoor spaces.
