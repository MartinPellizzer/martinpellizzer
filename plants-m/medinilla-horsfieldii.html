Medinilla horsfieldii

Medinilla horsfieldii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking, bell-shaped, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters, often during the holiday season. The plant has glossy, dark green, leathery leaves that are arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-draining soil and partial shade.

Medinilla horsfieldii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its long-lasting, showy blooms.
