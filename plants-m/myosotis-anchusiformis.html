Myosotis anchusiformis

Myosotis anchusiformis, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of South America.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in its harsh environment.

Myosotis anchusiformis is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in high-altitude conditions.
