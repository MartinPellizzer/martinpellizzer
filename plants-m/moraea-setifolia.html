Moraea setifolia

Moraea setifolia, commonly known as the slender spurred orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pink. Moraea setifolia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained conditions.
