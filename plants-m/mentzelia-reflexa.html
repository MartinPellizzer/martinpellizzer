Mentzelia reflexa

Mentzelia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed blazing star, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble small daisies. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in desert or semi-desert environments. Mentzelia reflexa is known for its ecological importance, providing nectar for pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
