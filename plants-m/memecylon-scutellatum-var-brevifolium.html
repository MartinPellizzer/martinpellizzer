Memecylon scutellatum var. brevifolium

Memecylon scutellatum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the short-leaved memecylon, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The leaves are ovate to elliptic, with a short petiole, and are often covered in fine, appressed hairs. The flowers are small, white, and clustered in terminal panicles, while the fruit is a small, three-lobed drupe.

This plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
