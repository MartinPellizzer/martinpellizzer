Monelasmum afzelii

Monelasmum afzelii, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. Its milky latex is a characteristic feature, often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
