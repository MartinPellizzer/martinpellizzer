Myristica rufula

Myristica rufula, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody structure and compound leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into red, fleshy berries. The seeds inside the berries are the source of the highly valued spices nutmeg and mace.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and commercial contexts.
