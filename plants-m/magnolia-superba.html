Magnolia superba

Magnolia superba, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has smooth, grayish-brown bark and opposite, simple, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. Its flowers emit a sweet, spicy fragrance, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.

Magnolia superba is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
