Malacurus lanatus

Malacurus lanatus, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Malacurus lanatus is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
