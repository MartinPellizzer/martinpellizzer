Mussaenda divaricata var. mollis

Mussaenda divaricata var. mollis, commonly known as the velvet flower or silky Mussaenda, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink and yellow. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are soft and hairy, giving it a velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
