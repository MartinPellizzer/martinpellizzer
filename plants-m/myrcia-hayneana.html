Myrcia hayneana

Myrcia hayneana, commonly known as the red myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, reddish seeds.

It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and pleasant aroma.
