Medicago sativa grex praecocissima

Medicago sativa grex praecocissima, commonly known as early alfalfa, is a fast-growing legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is known for its deep root system, which helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation. It is widely cultivated for forage and as a cover crop.

Its botanical characteristics make it valuable in agricultural and ecological contexts.
