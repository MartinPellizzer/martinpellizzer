Monsonia densiflora

Monsonia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered monsonia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in grassland or woodland habitats. The plant features a basal rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves that are covered in fine hairs. Its showy, tubular flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in summer.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
