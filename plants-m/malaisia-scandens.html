Malaisia scandens

Malaisia scandens, commonly known as the climbing malay raspberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a vine that produces small, red, edible berries similar to those of the raspberry. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is often found in tropical rainforests. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is used in traditional medicine.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners in warm climates.
