Manisuris goaensis

Manisuris goaensis, commonly known as the Goa orchid, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate and often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest undergrowth, where it can thrive. Its roots are often specialized for absorbing nutrients from the surrounding soil, a common adaptation in orchids.

Manisuris goaensis is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
