Malcolmia serbica

Malcolmia serbica, commonly known as the Serbian malva, is a perennial herb native to the Balkan Peninsula.

It features upright stems that are covered in soft, hairy foliage. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are palmately lobed, with a slightly woolly texture.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas.
