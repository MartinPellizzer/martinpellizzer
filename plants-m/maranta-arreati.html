Maranta arreati

Maranta arreati, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, patterned leaves that change color and orientation in response to light, a phenomenon known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its foliage often displays vibrant red, pink, and green hues, making it a popular choice for decorative purposes.

Maranta arreati thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
