Mapouria retusa

Mapouria retusa, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

Mapouria retusa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
