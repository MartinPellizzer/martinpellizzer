Maxillaria wojii

Maxillaria wojii, commonly known as the Wojii orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive hooded lip. Maxillaria wojii thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
