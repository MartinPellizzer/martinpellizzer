Mimosa coronillifolia

Mimosa coronillifolia, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its leaves are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety appearance.

Mimosa coronillifolia is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive foliage and bloom.
