Mentha longifolia f. lanyiana

Mentha longifolia f. lanyiana, commonly known as Lanyian mint, is a horticultural form of the spearmint species.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, fragrant leaves that emit a strong mint scent. The plant typically produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and use in culinary and medicinal applications.
