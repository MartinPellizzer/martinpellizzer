Malaxis tenggerensis

Malaxis tenggerensis, commonly known as the Tengger orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Tengger Desert in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in arid environments. Its rhizomes are fibrous, and it produces a single flower spike with several blooms.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
