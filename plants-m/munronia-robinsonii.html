Munronia robinsonii

Munronia robinsonii, commonly known as the Florida munroonia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in wetland habitats, such as swamps and marshes. The plant features showy, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Munronia robinsonii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
