Maxillaria inflexa

Maxillaria inflexa, commonly known as the wrinkled maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that bear elliptical leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers with a unique, wrinkled appearance on the lip. Its flowers are typically pink to purple in color and bloom during the warmer months.

Maxillaria inflexa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
