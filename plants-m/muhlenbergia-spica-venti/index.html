Muhlenbergia Spica-Venti

Muhlenbergia spica-venti is a distinctive grass species native to the central and eastern United States. It is known for its unique, bottlebrush-like inflorescence that gives it a striking appearance in the landscape. This plant thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in open woodlands and prairies. Its adaptability makes it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and restoration projects. With its elegant form and ecological benefits, Muhlenbergia spica-venti is a noteworthy member of the grass family.
