Mesembryanthemum umbelliflorum

Mesembryanthemum umbelliflorum, commonly known as the starflower or honeycomb flower, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and showy, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. Mesembryanthemum umbelliflorum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
