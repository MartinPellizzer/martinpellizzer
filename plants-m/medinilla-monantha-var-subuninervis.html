Medinilla monantha var. subuninervis

Medinilla monantha var. subuninervis, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet tangerine vine, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms. It prefers partial shade and well-draining soil, making it suitable for tropical and subtropical gardens.

The variety subuninervis is distinguished by its finely veined leaves and compact growth habit.
