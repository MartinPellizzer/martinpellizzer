Malva durieui

Malva durieui, commonly known as the Durie's mallows, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. The plant features showy, pentagonal flowers with five petals, typically in shades of pink or purple. Its leaves are velvety and heart-shaped, often with a distinctive waxy coating.

Malva durieui is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens due to its striking appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
