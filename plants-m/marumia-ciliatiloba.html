Marumia ciliatiloba

Marumia ciliatiloba, commonly known as the hairy leaf marumia, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, hairy foliage and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale pink. The plant thrives in humid, tropical environments and is often found in lowland forest understories. Its leaves are elongated with a serrated edge, and the stems are often covered in fine, soft hairs.

Marumia ciliatiloba is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
