Marsdenia viridiflora

Marsdenia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, lance-shaped leaves.
