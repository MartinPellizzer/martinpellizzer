Maxillaria villonacensis

Maxillaria villonacensis, commonly known as the Ecuadorian orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of Ecuador.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces strikingly colored flowers, typically in shades of pink and white, with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
