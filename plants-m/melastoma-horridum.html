Melastoma horridum

Melastoma horridum, commonly known as the rough melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, showy flowers that are usually pink or red in color, which bloom in clusters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough or hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
