Maxillaria aggregata

Maxillaria aggregata, commonly known as the clustered maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in warm, humid environments. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a controlled setting.

Maxillaria aggregata is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its attractive appearance and adaptability.
