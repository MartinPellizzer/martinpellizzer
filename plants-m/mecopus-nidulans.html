Mecopus nidulans

Mecopus nidulans, commonly known as the common meadow cranesbill, is a flowering plant belonging to the Geraniaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright, herbaceous stems and distinctive, doubly serrated leaves. Mecopus nidulans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also an important nectar source for pollinators.
