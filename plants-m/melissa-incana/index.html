Melissa Incana

Melissa incana is a hardy perennial shrub known for its fragrant leaves and bright yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun. This plant is commonly found in wetlands, stream banks, and other damp environments. Its medicinal properties have been recognized for centuries in traditional herbal remedies. Melissa incana is both an ecological asset and a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
