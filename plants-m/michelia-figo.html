Michelia figo

Michelia figo, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or fragrant michelia, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 6 meters. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its most distinctive feature is the fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is appreciated for its pleasant aroma.
