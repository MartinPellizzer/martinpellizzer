Morinda longiloba

Morinda longiloba, commonly known as the red morinda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters in height, characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and showy, pink to red flowers. The plant produces small, yellowish fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Morinda longiloba is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
