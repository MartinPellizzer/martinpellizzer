Miltonia spectabilis var. lineata

Miltonia spectabilis var. lineata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety green leaves and large, showy flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant produces pendant, fragrant blooms that are often adorned with intricate patterns and contrasting colorations. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in greenhouse environments.
