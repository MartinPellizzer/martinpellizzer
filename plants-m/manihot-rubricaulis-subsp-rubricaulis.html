Manihot rubricaulis subsp. rubricaulis

Manihot rubricaulis subsp.

rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed arrowleaf, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It is characterized by its distinctive arrowhead-shaped leaves with a reddish stem, which gives it its common name.

The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and research settings.
