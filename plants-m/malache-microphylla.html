Malache microphylla

Malache microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved malache, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or white, clustered in small inflorescences.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.
