Meliosma depressiva

Meliosma depressiva, commonly known as the flatleaf meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are often oval to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-yellow, and it produces small, woody fruits.
