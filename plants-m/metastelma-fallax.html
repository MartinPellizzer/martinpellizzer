Metastelma fallax

Metastelma fallax, commonly known as the golden star vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, adding vibrant color to its foliage. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture and a deep green hue. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Metastelma fallax is also valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
