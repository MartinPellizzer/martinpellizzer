Mammillaria scheinvariana

Mammillaria scheinvariana, commonly known as the Scheinvarian's bunny ear cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem covered in small, white, woolly spines and has a distinctive appearance with its rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for indoor cultivation due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Mammillaria scheinvariana is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts for its unique look and ease of care.
