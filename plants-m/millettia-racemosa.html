Millettia racemosa

Millettia racemosa, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in racemose inflorescences. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Millettia racemosa also has traditional uses in medicine and as a source of timber.
