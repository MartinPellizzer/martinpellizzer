Monadenium stellatum

Monadenium stellatum, commonly known as the star cactus, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of South America.

It features a short, thick stem with clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant has fleshy, ribbed leaves that store water, adapting it to its dry habitat. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.
