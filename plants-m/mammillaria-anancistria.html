Mammillaria anancistria

Mammillaria anancistria, commonly known as the Mexican fairy duster, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its gray-green foliage. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
