Melastoma royenii

Melastoma royenii, commonly known as the Royal Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma royenii is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
