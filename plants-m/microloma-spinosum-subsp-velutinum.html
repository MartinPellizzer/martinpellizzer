Microloma spinosum subsp. velutinum

Microloma spinosum subsp.

velutinum, commonly known as the velvet spiny microloma, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its spiny stems and velvety, dark green foliage.

The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for drought-tolerant landscaping.
