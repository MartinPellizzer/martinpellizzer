Masdevallia severa

Masdevallia severa, commonly known as the Severa Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its ability to thrive in high-altitude environments. The plant typically produces striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are well adapted to its natural habitat.

Masdevallia severa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
