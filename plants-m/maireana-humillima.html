Maireana humillima

Maireana humillima, commonly known as the small-leafed maireana, is a low-growing, evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Maireana humillima is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
