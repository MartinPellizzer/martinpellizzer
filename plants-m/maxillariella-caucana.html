Maxillariella caucana

Maxillariella caucana, commonly known as the Caucana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It typically grows in rocky or open habitats, often in high-altitude environments. The plant features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, usually white or pale pink, with a distinct lip that is often darker in color.

Maxillariella caucana is known for its resilience and ability to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.
