Magnolia tarahumara

Magnolia tarahumara, commonly known as the Sierra Madre magnolia, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, silky hair. Its bark is smooth and gray, and it can grow up to 10 meters tall.

The magnolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often found in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
