Malcolmia parviflora

Malcolmia parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, pale yellow flowers. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Malcolmia parviflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
