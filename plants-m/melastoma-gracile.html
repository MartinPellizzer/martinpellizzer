Melastoma gracile

Melastoma gracile, commonly known as the small-flowered melastoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma gracile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and blooms.
