Mammillaria megacantha var. rigidior

Mammillaria megacantha var. rigidior, commonly known as the rigid spinescent mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in dense, white to yellowish spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, pink or red flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
