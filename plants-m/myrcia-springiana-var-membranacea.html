Myrcia springiana var. membranacea

Myrcia springiana var. membranacea, commonly known as the membranous myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The fruit is a small, woody capsule that contains several seeds.
