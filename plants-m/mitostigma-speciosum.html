Mitostigma speciosum

Mitostigma speciosum, commonly known as the showy mitostigma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has a woody stem and produces small, elliptical leaves with a smooth, glossy surface. Its flowers are borne in clusters and are often visited by pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Mitostigma speciosum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
