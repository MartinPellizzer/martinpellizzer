Marianthus bignoniaceus

Marianthus bignoniaceus, commonly known as the yellow shower tree, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Bignoniaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces long, pendulous inflorescences that resemble cascading showers, hence its common name. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate with a smooth surface.

Marianthus bignoniaceus is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and public spaces.
