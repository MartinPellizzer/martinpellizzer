Melilota messanensis

Melilota messanensis, commonly known as the Messanese melilot, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to arid environments. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
