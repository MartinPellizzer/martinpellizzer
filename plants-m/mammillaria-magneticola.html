Mammillaria magneticola

Mammillaria magneticola, commonly known as the magnetic ball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, globular shape and bright yellow to orange spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded form. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and bloom in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria magneticola is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
