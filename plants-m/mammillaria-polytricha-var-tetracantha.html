Mammillaria polytricha var. tetracantha

Mammillaria polytricha var. tetracantha, commonly known as the four-armed mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and compact size make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
