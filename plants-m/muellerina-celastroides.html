Muellerina celastroides

Muellerina celastroides, commonly known as the slender false mallow, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small, herbaceous annual that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are usually hairy and have a distinctive heart-shaped base. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences, often appearing in late spring to early summer.

Muellerina celastroides is adapted to arid environments and is often found in sandy or rocky soils.
