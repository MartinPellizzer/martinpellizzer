Melicope polybotrya

Melicope polybotrya, commonly known as the Hawaiian sandalwood, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in rainforests.
