Medinilla parviflora

Medinilla parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are often heart-shaped or ovate in form. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white or pale pink flowers, which are typically borne on short, peduncles. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla parviflora is well-suited for indoor growing and requires well-draining soil and consistent moisture.
