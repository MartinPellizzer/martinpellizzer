Matthiola lonchophora

Matthiola lonchophora, commonly known as the narrow-leaved stock, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbages and mustards. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of hairs.
