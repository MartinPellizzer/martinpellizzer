Magnolia zhengyiana

Magnolia zhengyiana, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens.
