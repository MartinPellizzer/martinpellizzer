Maxillaria kreysigii

Maxillaria kreysigii, commonly known as the crested lady's slipper, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single, fleshy leaf and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The flowers are characterized by their unique, slipper-shaped petals and a distinctive crest on the lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
