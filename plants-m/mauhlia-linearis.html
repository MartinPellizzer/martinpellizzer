Mauhlia linearis

Mauhlia linearis, commonly known as the slender maidenhair fern, is a delicate evergreen fern native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It features finely divided, linear leaves that give it a graceful, airy appearance. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, acidic soil. Its fronds are typically bright green and can grow up to 30 inches in length.

Mauhlia linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
