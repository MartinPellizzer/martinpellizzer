Metalasia fastigiata

Metalasia fastigiata, commonly known as the upright metalasia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features upright, cylindrical stems that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale blue in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, growing in a dense, compact arrangement along the stem.

Metalasia fastigiata is often grown as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
