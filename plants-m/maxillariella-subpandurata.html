Maxillariella subpandurata

Maxillariella subpandurata, commonly known as the pandurata orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with a short rhizome and compact, clustered pseudobulbs. Its flowers are usually small and fragrant, with a distinctive pandurata-like shape, giving it its common name.

The botanical characteristics of this orchid make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
