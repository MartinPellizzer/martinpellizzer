Mammillaria spinosissima var. vulpina

Mammillaria spinosissima var. vulpina, commonly known as the fox tail cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, pink to red flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it has a slow growth rate.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
