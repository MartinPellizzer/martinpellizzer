Mucuna sloanei

Mucuna sloanei, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a tropical legume native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and distinctive purple-pink flowers. The plant produces long, slender pods that contain several dark, kidney-shaped seeds. Mucuna sloanei is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

It is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
