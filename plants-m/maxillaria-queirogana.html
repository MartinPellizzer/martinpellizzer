Maxillaria queirogana

Maxillaria queirogana, commonly known as the Queiroga's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short rhizome and pseudobulbs that support its foliage. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a dark throat. Its leaves are ovate and arranged in two rows, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Maxillaria queirogana is valued in horticulture for its beauty and relatively easy cultivation requirements.
