Megyathus

Megyathus, commonly known as the Hungarian thorn.

It is a shrub native to Central and Eastern Europe, characterized by its thorny stems and small, greenish-yellow flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in open woodlands or along roadsides.

The botanical features of Megyathus make it an important species in both ecological and traditional medicinal contexts.
