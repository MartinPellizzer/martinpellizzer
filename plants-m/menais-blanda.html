Menais blanda

Menais blanda, commonly known as the slender menais, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically green and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, white or pale purple flowers in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Its botanical structure makes it a valuable addition to native gardens and wetland restoration projects.
