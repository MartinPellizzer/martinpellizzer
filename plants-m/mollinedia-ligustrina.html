Mollinedia ligustrina

Mollinedia ligustrina, commonly known as the Mexican olive, is a shrub or small tree native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like olives and lilacs. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.
