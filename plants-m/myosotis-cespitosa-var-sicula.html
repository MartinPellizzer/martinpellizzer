Myosotis cespitosa var. sicula

Myosotis cespitosa var. sicula, commonly known as Sicilian forget-me-not, is a perennial flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a low-growing, mat-forming habit with clusters of blue to purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
