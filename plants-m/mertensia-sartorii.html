Mertensia sartorii

Mertensia sartorii, commonly known as the purple mertensia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its attractive, showy blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture, adding to its ornamental value.

Mertensia sartorii is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and ability to thrive in woodland settings.
