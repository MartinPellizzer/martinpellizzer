Melastoma martianum

Melastoma martianum, commonly known as the tropical wild strawberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense foliage and small, elliptical leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, and it is often used in traditional medicine.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
