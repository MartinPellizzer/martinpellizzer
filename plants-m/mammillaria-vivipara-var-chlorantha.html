Mammillaria vivipara var. chlorantha

Mammillaria vivipara var. chlorantha, commonly known as the green mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white to yellowish tubercles, which are small, rounded bumps. The plant produces pale green flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
