Myristica laxiflora

Myristica laxiflora, commonly known as the slender nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and compound leaves with elongated, serrated leaflets. Its flowers are small and yellowish, and it produces an aromatic seed kernel similar to true nutmeg.

The botanical features of Myristica laxiflora make it a valuable species in both ecological and potential commercial contexts.
