Mapania multiflora

Mapania multiflora, commonly known as the Chinese bellflower, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to blue or purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and mountainous regions. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are hairy.

Mapania multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
