Myristica ampliata

Myristica ampliata, commonly known as the large nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves and produces fragrant, reddish-brown fruits that contain a single seed. The bark and seeds of Myristica ampliata are used in traditional medicine for their purported medicinal properties.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and economic contexts within its native habitat.
