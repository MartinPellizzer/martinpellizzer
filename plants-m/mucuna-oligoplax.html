Mucuna oligoplax

Mucuna oligoplax, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety pubescence that covers its stems and leaves, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

Mucuna oligoplax is also valued for its use in traditional medicine and as a source of protein-rich seeds.
