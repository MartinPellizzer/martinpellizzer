Marrubium acetabulosum

Marrubium acetabulosum, commonly known as the acetabular marrubium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in sunny, well-drained environments. The plant features narrow, gray-green leaves with a rough texture and produces small, pale blue or violet flowers in clusters. Its flowering period occurs from late spring to early summer, and it is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Marrubium acetabulosum is valued for its medicinal properties and has been used traditionally in herbal remedies.
