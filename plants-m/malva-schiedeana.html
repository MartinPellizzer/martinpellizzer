Malva schiedeana

Malva schiedeana, commonly known as the Schiedea mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are palmately lobed, and it produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Malva schiedeana is also notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
