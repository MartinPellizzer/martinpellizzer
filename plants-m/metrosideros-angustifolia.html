Metrosideros angustifolia

Metrosideros angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf kowhai, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and lowland forest areas. The plant produces striking yellow or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting a variety of pollinators. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a reddish tinge when young.
