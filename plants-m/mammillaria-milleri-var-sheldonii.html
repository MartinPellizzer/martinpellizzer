Mammillaria milleri var. sheldonii

Mammillaria milleri var. sheldonii, commonly known as Sheldon's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the deserts of northern Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in small, white to pale yellow tubercles that are adorned with radial spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, requiring minimal water and full sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
