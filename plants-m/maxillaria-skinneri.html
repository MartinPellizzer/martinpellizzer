Maxillaria skinneri

Maxillaria skinneri, commonly known as the Skinner's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive yellowish-green to orange coloration. Its leaves are strap-like, narrow, and arranged in two rows along the pseudobulbs.

It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
