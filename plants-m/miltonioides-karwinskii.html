Miltonioides karwinskii

Miltonioides karwinskii, commonly known as the Karwinskii's orchid, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of showy, violet-colored blooms with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
