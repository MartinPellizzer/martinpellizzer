Melastoma fontanesii

Melastoma fontanesii, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and understoreys. Its leaves are usually opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
