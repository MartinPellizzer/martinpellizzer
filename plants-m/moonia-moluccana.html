Moonia moluccana

Moonia moluccana, commonly known as the Moluccan moonia, is a flowering plant native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows up to 1 meter in height, featuring slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often blooming in summer. Its botanical characteristics make it a unique addition to subtropical and tropical gardens.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight for optimal growth.
