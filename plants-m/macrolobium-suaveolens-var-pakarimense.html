Macrolobium suaveolens var. pakarimense

Macrolobium suaveolens var. pakarimense, commonly known as the Pakarimense hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pink to red flowers that bloom during the wet season. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a fragrant scent. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found near rivers and in dense undergrowth.

Its botanical features make it an important species for both ecological studies and horticultural interest.
