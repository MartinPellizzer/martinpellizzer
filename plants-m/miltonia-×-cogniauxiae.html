Miltonia × cogniauxiae

Miltonia × cogniauxiae, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant pink and white flowers, which resemble a slipper or a lady's shoe, giving it its common name. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are dark green and strap-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

This orchid is prized for its beauty and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
