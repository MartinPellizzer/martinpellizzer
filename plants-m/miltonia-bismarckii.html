Miltonia bismarckii

Miltonia bismarckii, commonly known as the Bismarck orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or branches, exhibiting an epiphytic growth habit. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments.

The flowers bloom in spring and summer, adding ornamental value to gardens and greenhouses.
