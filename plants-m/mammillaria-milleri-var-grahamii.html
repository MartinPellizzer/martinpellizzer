Mammillaria milleri var. grahamii

Mammillaria milleri var. grahamii, commonly known as the Graham's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with radial spines and a dense covering of white, woolly hair. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
