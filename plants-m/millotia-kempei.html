Millotia kempei

Millotia kempei, commonly known as the Malayan starfruit, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, yellowish-green leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female structures, typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The fruit, which is edible, contains a single seed enclosed in a fleshy, aromatic pulp.
