Mesembryanthemum subalatum

Mesembryanthemum subalatum, commonly known as the silverleaf iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, bluish-gray leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white, pink, and yellow, typically blooming in late spring to early summer.
