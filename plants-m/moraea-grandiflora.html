Moraea grandiflora

Moraea grandiflora, commonly known as the Giant Moraea, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and large, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms in spring, producing clusters of funnel-shaped blossoms in shades of pink, red, and white. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

Moraea grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to gardens with well-drained soil.
