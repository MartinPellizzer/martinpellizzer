Mariscus psilostachys

Mariscus psilostachys, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mariscus, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and traditional herbal practices.
