Macrosolen kunstleri

Macrosolen kunstleri, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid environments and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
