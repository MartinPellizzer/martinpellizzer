Melothria natalensis

Melothria natalensis, commonly known as Natal sour sop, is a small, climbing shrub native to southern Africa.

It produces small, green, egg-shaped fruits that turn yellow when ripe. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and hairy stems, typical of the Cucurbitaceae family. The fruit has a tangy, acidic flavor and is often consumed fresh or used in local culinary traditions.

It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soils and full sun.
