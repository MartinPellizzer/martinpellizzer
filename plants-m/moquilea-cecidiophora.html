Moquilea cecidiophora

Moquilea cecidiophora, commonly known as the Mexican sunflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It is characterized by its bright orange to red tubular flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant is part of the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers. Moquilea cecidiophora thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, often growing in arid or semi-arid environments.
