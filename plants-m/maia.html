Maia

Maia, commonly known as the "maia plant," is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often pink or purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically has heart-shaped leaves and a woody stem, making it a striking addition to gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, preferring temperate climates.

Maia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
