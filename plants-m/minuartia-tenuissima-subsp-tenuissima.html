Minuartia tenuissima subsp. tenuissima

Minuartia tenuissima subsp.

tenuissima, commonly known as the slender sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arctic and alpine regions. It typically features small, clustered leaves that are narrow and often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are usually white or pale pink, with four petals and numerous stamens, typical of the Caryophyllaceae family. It thrives in well-drained, rocky soils and is adapted to cold climates.

This subspecies is often found in tundra and mountainous habitats across the Northern Hemisphere.
