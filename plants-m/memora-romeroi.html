Memora romeroi

Memora romeroi, commonly known as the silverleaf memora, is a rare flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native range.
