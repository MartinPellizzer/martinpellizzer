Miconia plumifera var. bangii

Miconia plumifera var. bangii, commonly known as the Bangi miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom throughout the year, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in tropical gardens.
