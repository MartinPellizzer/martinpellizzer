Maxillaria pendens

Maxillaria pendens, commonly known as the hanging maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces pendulous, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The flowers are typically borne on slender, arching stems, giving the plant its characteristic appearance. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
