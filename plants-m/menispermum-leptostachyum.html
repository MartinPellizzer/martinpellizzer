Menispermum leptostachyum

Menispermum leptostachyum, commonly known as the slender-stalked arrowroot, is a member of the Menispermaceae family.

It is a climbing shrub that produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive veinal pattern. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are small and greenish-yellow, typically appearing in clusters.

The plant is notable for its edible tuberous roots, which have been used in traditional medicine and as a food source.
