Malus domestica var. rinkii

Malus domestica var. rinkii, commonly known as the Rinkii apple, is a cultivar of the domestic apple tree.

It is characterized by its distinctive red and yellow striped skin, which gives it a striking appearance. The fruit is typically medium to large in size and has a firm, crisp texture with a sweet-tart flavor. It is primarily grown for its ornamental value due to its vibrant coloration and unique appearance.

This variety is also valued for its potential in breeding programs aimed at developing new apple cultivars.
