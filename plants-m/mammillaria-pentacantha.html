Mammillaria pentacantha

Mammillaria pentacantha, commonly known as the five-armed hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered with white, woolly spines and prominent radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for rock gardens and cactus collections.
