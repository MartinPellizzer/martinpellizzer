Masdevallia rafaeliana

Masdevallia rafaeliana, commonly known as the Rafael's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, intricately patterned flowers that range in color from deep red to purple. The plant typically produces a single, showy bloom per stem, which is often adorned with contrasting markings. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts and conservationists.
