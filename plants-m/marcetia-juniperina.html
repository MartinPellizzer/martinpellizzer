Marcetia juniperina

Marcetia juniperina, commonly known as the Juniper-like Heath, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Ericaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in coastal areas. It has a compact, shrub-like form with needle-like leaves that resemble those of juniper.

Its flowers are usually pink or white and bloom in late spring or early summer.
