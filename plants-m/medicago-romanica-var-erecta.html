Medicago romanica var. erecta

Medicago romanica var. erecta, commonly known as the upright medick, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils and grasslands. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

This species is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, benefiting soil fertility.
