Microstegium rupestre

Microstegium rupestre, commonly known as rockstar grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to eastern North America.

It typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, often found in open woodlands and along stream banks. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems that are often reddish in color, and its leaves are narrow with a rough texture. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.

Microstegium rupestre is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in challenging environmental conditions.
