Meriania leucantha var. nana

Meriania leucantha var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf white meriania, is a compact shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
