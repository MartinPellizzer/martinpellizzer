Myrcia schiedeana

Myrcia schiedeana, commonly known as the Brazilian myrtle, is a species of flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are small, woody capsules that contain aromatic seeds.

Myrcia schiedeana is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in shaded, moist environments.
