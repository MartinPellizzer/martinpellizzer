Merostachys kunthii

Merostachys kunthii, commonly known as the silver hairgrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-hued leaves and produces small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and have a distinctive silvery sheen due to dense pubescence.

Merostachys kunthii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appearance and ability to thrive in humid conditions.
