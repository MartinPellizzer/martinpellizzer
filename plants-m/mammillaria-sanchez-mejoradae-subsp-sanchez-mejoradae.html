Mammillaria sanchez-mejoradae subsp. sanchez-mejoradae

Mammillaria sanchez-mejoradae subsp.

sanchez-mejoradae, commonly known as the Sanchezmeyorada Mammillaria, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico. It features a compact, rounded stem with a dense covering of white to pale green spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its slow growth rate and unique appearance make it a popular choice for collectors and cactus enthusiasts.
