Myristica canarica

Myristica canarica, commonly known as the Canarica nutmeg, is a member of the Myristicaceae family.

It is a small evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India. The plant produces distinctive red, fleshy fruits that contain a single seed. The seeds are encased in a hard, aromatic shell, which is often used in traditional medicine.

It is valued for its essential oils and has historical significance in local herbal practices.
