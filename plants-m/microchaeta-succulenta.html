Microchaeta succulenta

Microchaeta succulenta, commonly known as the succulent microchaeta, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, fleshy leaves and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, succulent leaves. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
