Medicago rigidula var. minor

Medicago rigidula var. minor, commonly known as lesser medick, is a low-growing perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in disturbed soils, grasslands, and along roadsides. It has a deep root system that helps in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation.

This species is also valued for its use in forage and as a cover crop in agricultural settings.
