Montbretiopsis florentiae

Montbretiopsis florentiae, commonly known as the Florence montbretia, is a striking perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red, often with a dark stripe down the center. The plant has strap-like leaves that are narrow and grass-like, typically growing in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Montbretiopsis florentiae is noted for its long blooming period, usually from late spring to early summer.
