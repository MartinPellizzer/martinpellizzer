Magnolia × lennei

Magnolia × lennei, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the lennei magnolia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub or small tree native to China.

It features large, showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with showy, petal-like bracts. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves that are elliptical in shape and provide a striking contrast to its floral display. It thrives in USDA hardiness zones 6 to 9, preferring partial shade and well-drained soil.

The flowers bloom in early spring, making it a popular ornamental plant in gardens and landscapes.
