Melicope cornuta var. decurrens

Melicope cornuta var. decurrens, commonly known as the narrow-leaved melicope, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when immature and turn red when mature. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The leaves and bark of this plant have been used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
