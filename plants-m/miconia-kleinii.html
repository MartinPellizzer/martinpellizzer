Miconia kleinii

Miconia kleinii, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean and northern South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically bright green with a waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia kleinii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique floral display.
