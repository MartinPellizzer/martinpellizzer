Micranthes nelsoniana var. aestivalis

Micranthes nelsoniana var. aestivalis, commonly known as the summer bellflower, is a herbaceous perennial native to the southeastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in moist, well-drained soils. It is often found in wetlands, stream banks, and other moist habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens and wetland restoration projects.
