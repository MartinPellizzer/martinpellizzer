Muraltia recurva

Muraltia recurva, commonly known as the red muraltia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its climbing habit, trifoliate leaves, and showy pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a legume pod containing several elliptical seeds.
