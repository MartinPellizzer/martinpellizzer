Mentha aquatica f. maculosa

Mentha aquatica f. maculosa, commonly known as the spotted water mint, is a variant of the water mint plant.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves and small, pale blue to purple flowers. It thrives in moist environments such as wet meadows, stream banks, and marshy areas.

Its foliage is often speckled with dark spots, giving it its common name.
