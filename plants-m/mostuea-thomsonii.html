Mostuea thomsonii

Mostuea thomsonii, commonly known as Thomson's spider lily, is a striking tropical plant native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Aroid family, which includes plants like the peace lily and philodendron. The plant is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that resemble spider-like structures, hence its common name. Its leaves are broad and arrow-shaped, often with a waxy texture.

It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
