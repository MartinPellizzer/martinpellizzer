Madhuca beccarii

Madhuca beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari's tallow tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and dense, glossy foliage.
