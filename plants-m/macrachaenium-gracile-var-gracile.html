Macrachaenium gracile var. gracile

Macrachaenium gracile var. gracile, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils.

Its flowering spikes are usually small and inconspicuous, with tiny, wind-pollinated flowers.
