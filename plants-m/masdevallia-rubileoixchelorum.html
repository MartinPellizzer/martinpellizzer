Masdevallia rubileoixchelorum

Masdevallia rubileoixchelorum, commonly known as the Mexican red-veined masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Mexico.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and green speckled leaves and its delicate, showy flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocky surfaces, showcasing its epiphytic nature. Its unique coloration and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat threats, it is considered endangered.
