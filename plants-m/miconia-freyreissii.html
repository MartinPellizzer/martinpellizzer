Miconia freyreissii

Miconia freyreissii, commonly known as the Brazilian star tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens, though it can be invasive in certain areas.
