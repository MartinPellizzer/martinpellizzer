Magnolia simii

Magnolia simii, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a striking evergreen tree native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a pleasant, spicy fragrance.

Magnolia simii is highly valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
