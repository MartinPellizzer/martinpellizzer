Mayepea densiflora

Mayepea densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered mayepea, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid tropical forests.

Mayepea densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
