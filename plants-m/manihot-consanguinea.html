Manihot consanguinea

Manihot consanguinea, commonly known as the arrowleaf or arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and can grow up to 1.5 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are followed by capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Botanically, it is part of the Euphorbiaceae family, known for its latex-producing properties.
