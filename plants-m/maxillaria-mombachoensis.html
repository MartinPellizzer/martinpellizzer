Maxillaria mombachoensis

Maxillaria mombachoensis, commonly known as the Mombacho orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of Central America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers that are often pink or purple in color, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
