Matthiola sericea

Matthiola sericea, commonly known as silky stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant produces small, elongated fruits called siliques, which contain numerous seeds. Matthiola sericea is typically found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and historical use in perfumery.
