Maxillaria lasallei

Maxillaria lasallei, commonly known as the Lasalle's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria lasallei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
