Marara

Marara, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a small herbaceous perennial, often with a compact rosette form. Marara thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires indirect sunlight for optimal growth.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
