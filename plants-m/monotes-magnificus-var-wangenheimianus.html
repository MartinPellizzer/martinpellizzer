Monotes magnificus var. wangenheimianus

Monotes magnificus var. wangenheimianus, commonly known as the Wangenheim's magnificus, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, waxy leaves that display a striking gradient of colors, ranging from deep purples to vibrant reds. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires a cool, humid environment to flourish.
