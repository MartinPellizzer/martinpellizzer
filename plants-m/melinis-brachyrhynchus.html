Melinis brachyrhynchus

Melinis brachyrhynchus, commonly known as the narrowawn grass, is a perennial grass species native to North America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny flowers. It thrives in a variety of soil types and is often found in open areas such as fields and roadsides.

Melinis brachyrhynchus is also known for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
