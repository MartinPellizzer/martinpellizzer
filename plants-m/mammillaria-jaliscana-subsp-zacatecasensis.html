Mammillaria jaliscana subsp. zacatecasensis

Mammillaria jaliscana subsp.

zacatecasensis, commonly known as the Zacatecas mammillaria, is a small cactus native to the arid regions of Zacatecas, Mexico. It features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white to grayish spines.

The flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from areoles near the top of the stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xerophytic gardens.

This subspecies is notable for its compact growth and unique spination pattern.
