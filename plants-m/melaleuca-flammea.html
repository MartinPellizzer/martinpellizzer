Melaleuca flammea

Melaleuca flammea, commonly known as the flame myrtle, is a flowering plant native to eastern Australia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. It produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, dark brown seeds.

Melaleuca flammea is often found in coastal and heathland environments, thriving in well-drained soils.
