Musa beccarii var. beccarii

Musa beccarii var. beccarii, commonly known as the Beccari banana, is a species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant with a pseudostem composed of tightly packed leaves. The plant produces large, elongated, yellowish-green berries that are typically harvested when mature. It is cultivated for its edible fruit, which has a sweet and aromatic flavor.

The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
