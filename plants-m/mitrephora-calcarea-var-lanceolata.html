Mitrephora calcarea var. lanceolata

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
