Maillardia borbonica

Maillardia borbonica, commonly known as the Madagascar lily, is a striking herbaceous perennial native to Madagascar.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink, often with a distinctive red or orange throat. The plant has strap-like, grassy leaves that are often variegated with contrasting colors. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Maillardia borbonica is valued in gardens for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for.
