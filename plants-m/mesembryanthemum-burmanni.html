Mesembryanthemum burmanni

Mesembryanthemum burmanni, commonly known as the Burmann's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. Mesembryanthemum burmanni is a popular choice for xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
