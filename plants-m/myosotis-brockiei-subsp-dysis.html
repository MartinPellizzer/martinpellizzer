Myosotis brockiei subsp. dysis

Myosotis brockiei subsp.

dysis, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of New Zealand. It is characterized by its small, sky-blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine meadows, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine, silky hair.

This subspecies is notable for its resilience to harsh environmental conditions and its role in the ecological diversity of alpine ecosystems.
