Mesembryanthemum translucens

Mesembryanthemum translucens, commonly known as the transparent ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

Mesembryanthemum translucens is often used in rock gardens and as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
