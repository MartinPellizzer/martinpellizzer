Medinilla miniata

Medinilla miniata, commonly known as the miniature medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form. The plant produces small, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a subtle scent. Medinilla miniata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
