Mitrosicyos

Mitrosicyos, commonly known as the African snake plant or snake root, is a member of the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to southern Africa and is characterized by its robust, sprawling vines with heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces yellow-orange flowers that are often followed by orange or red fruits.
