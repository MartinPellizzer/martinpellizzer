Medicago polymorpha var. orbicularis

Medicago polymorpha var. orbicularis, commonly known as the roundleaf medick, is a perennial legume native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, orbicular leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and thrives in well-drained soils, often found in meadows and disturbed areas. It is valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in ecological restoration and agricultural settings.

Its compact growth habit and adaptability contribute to its widespread distribution and ecological significance.
