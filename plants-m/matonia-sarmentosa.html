Matonia sarmentosa

Matonia sarmentosa, commonly known as the creeping matonia, is a perennial shrub native to South America.

It features woody stems that grow close to the ground, often forming dense mats. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and typically have a slightly serrated edge.

Matonia sarmentosa is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks.
