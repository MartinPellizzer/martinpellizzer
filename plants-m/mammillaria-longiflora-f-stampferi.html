Mammillaria longiflora f. stampferi

Mammillaria longiflora f. stampferi, commonly known as the Stampfer's long-flowered mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent tubercles and spines that are typically white to yellowish in color. The plant produces pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by succulent enthusiasts.
