Mimulus nanus var. bicolor

Mimulus nanus var. bicolor, commonly known as the dwarf yellow monkeyflower, is a compact, herbaceous annual native to California.

It features small, brightly colored flowers that are typically yellow with a red or orange throat, creating a striking contrast. The plant has hairy stems and leaves that are ovate to lanceolate in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal scrub and grassland habitats.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a popular choice for rock gardens and native plant collections.
