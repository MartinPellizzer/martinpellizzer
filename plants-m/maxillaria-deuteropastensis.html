Maxillaria deuteropastensis

Maxillaria deuteropastensis, commonly known as the Pastorelli's maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and has pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers, which are usually pink or purple, bloom in summer and are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Maxillaria deuteropastensis is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions, making it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
