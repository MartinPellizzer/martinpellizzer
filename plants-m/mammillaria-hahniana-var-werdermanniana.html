Mammillaria hahniana var. werdermanniana

Mammillaria hahniana var. werdermanniana, commonly known as the Werdermann's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a gray-green color and prominent white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to prevent root rot.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
