Morisonia Scabrida

Morisonia scabrida is a unique flowering plant native to the southeastern united states. it belongs to the onagraceae family and is known for its striking pink and white blooms. the plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade. its hairy stems and rough leaves give it a distinctive appearance. despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more common garden plants.
