Musa yunnanensis var. jingdongensis

Musa yunnanensis var. jingdongensis, commonly known as the Jingdong banana, is a variant native to China, specifically Yunnan Province.

It is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Musaceae family, characterized by its large, broad leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically smaller and more elongated compared to other banana varieties. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, requiring warm temperatures and high humidity for optimal growth.

Due to its unique genetic makeup, it is of interest for botanical research and potential agricultural use.
