Mammillaria nuttallii var. robustior

Mammillaria nuttallii var. robustior, commonly known as the robust mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
