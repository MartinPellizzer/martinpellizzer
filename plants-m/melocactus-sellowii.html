Melocactus sellowii

Melocactus sellowii, commonly known as the Sellow's melon cactus, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a columnar cactus with a distinctive ribbed structure and a short, spiny areola. The plant produces large, pale yellow flowers that bloom in the evening. Its growth is slow, and it can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters.

It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil and minimal watering.
