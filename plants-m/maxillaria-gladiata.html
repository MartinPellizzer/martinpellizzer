Maxillaria gladiata

Maxillaria gladiata, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves and colorful, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees or rocks in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often arranged in clusters and can last for several weeks, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Maxillaria gladiata is noted for its resilience and ability to thrive in a variety of growing conditions.
