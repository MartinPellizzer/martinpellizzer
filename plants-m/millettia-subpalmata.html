Millettia subpalmata

Millettia subpalmata, commonly known as the Chinese ironwood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and distinctive palmately lobed leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by woody pods containing flat, seed-like structures. It is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.

Millettia subpalmata thrives in humid, well-drained soils and is commonly found in lowland rainforests.
