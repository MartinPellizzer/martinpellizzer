Malacothrix foliosa var. indecora

Malacothrix foliosa var. indecora, commonly known as the ornate leaf malacothrix, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features a compact, bushy growth habit with fleshy, green stems that often bear a powdery coating. The leaves are ovate, slightly pointed, and arranged in a rosette pattern, with a velvety texture that gives it a distinctive appearance. It produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the warmer months.

This variety is valued for its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to dry, well-drained soils.
