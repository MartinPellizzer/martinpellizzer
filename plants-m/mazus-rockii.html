Mazus rockii

Mazus rockii, commonly known as the rock mazus, is a low-growing perennial plant native to China.

It features small, dark green leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs, giving it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early summer. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Mazus rockii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
