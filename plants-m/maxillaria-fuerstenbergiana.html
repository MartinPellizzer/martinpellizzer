Maxillaria fuerstenbergiana

Maxillaria fuerstenbergiana, commonly known as the Fuerstenberg's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features a sympodial structure, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral development. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
