Mikania linearifolia

Mikania linearifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf morning glory, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. The plant is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. Its flowering period occurs primarily during the warmer months, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Mikania linearifolia is valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
