Meliosma megalobotrys

Meliosma megalobotrys, commonly known as the giant leaf meliosma, is a tropical plant belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length, making it a striking feature in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in panicles, followed by small, round fruits. Its leaves are usually leathery and have a distinct, rough texture.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.
