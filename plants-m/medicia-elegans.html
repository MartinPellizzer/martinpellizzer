Medicia elegans

Medicia elegans, commonly known as the elegant medic plant, is a flowering species native to certain regions of South America.

It features delicate, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a subtle bluish hue. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture, while its roots are known for their medicinal properties.

Medicina elegans is valued in traditional medicine for its potential anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.
