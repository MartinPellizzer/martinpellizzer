Monomeria barbata

Monomeria barbata, commonly known as the hairy monomeria, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the family Monomeraceae and is characterized by its small, herbaceous growth habit. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are typically hairy, giving it the common name, and it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Monomeria barbata is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, where it plays a role in local ecosystems.
