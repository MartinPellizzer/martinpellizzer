Mandevilla thevetioides

Mandevilla thevetioides, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from pink to red, often with a white throat. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow up to 20 feet long if supported. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla thevetioides is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and vigorous growth.
