Maytenus buxifolia var. latifolia

Maytenus buxifolia var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf mayten, is a shrub or small tree native to South America.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with thick, leathery leaves that are broader than those of the typical varietal form. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in axillary inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
