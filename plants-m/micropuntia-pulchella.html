Micropuntia pulchella

Micropuntia pulchella, commonly known as the elegant cactus or the pink fairy cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems covered in white to pale green spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
