Muhlenbergia wolfii

Muhlenbergia wolfii, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental landscaping due to their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Muhlenbergia wolfii is also valued for its drought tolerance and ability to stabilize soil in arid environments.
