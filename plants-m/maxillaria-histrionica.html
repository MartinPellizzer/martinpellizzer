Maxillaria histrionica

Maxillaria histrionica, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a compact, upright orchid with pseudobulbs and linear leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, butterfly-shaped blooms.

Maxillaria histrionica prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for both indoor and greenhouse cultivation.
