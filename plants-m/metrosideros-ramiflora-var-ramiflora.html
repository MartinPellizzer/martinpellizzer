Metrosideros ramiflora var. ramiflora

Metrosideros ramiflora var. ramiflora, commonly known as the coastal kohuhu, is a shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces showy, red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in coastal environments and is adapted to salt-laden air and well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in native ecosystems.
