Manihot palmata var. multifida

Manihot palmata var. multifida, commonly known as the featherleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are deeply divided into narrow, feather-like segments. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its rhizomes are used to extract starch, making it economically important in some regions.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local communities.
