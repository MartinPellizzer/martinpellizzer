Moraea edulis var. odora

Moraea edulis var. odora, commonly known as the sweet moraea, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It produces striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, fragrant scent. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The flowers bloom in spring and are pollinated primarily by insects.
