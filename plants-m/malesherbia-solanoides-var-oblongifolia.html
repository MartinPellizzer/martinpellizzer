Malesherbia solanoides var. oblongifolia

Malesherbia solanoides var. oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed malesherbia, is a species native to South America.

It is a perennial shrub characterized by its woody stems and dense foliage. The leaves are typically broad and ovate, with a velvety texture and a dark green color. It produces small, clustered flowers in shades of pink or purple, which bloom in the spring.

This plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
