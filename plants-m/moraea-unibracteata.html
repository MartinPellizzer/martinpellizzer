Moraea unibracteata

Moraea unibracteata, commonly known as the single-bract moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Moraea unibracteata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
