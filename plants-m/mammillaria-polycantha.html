Mammillaria polycantha

Mammillaria polycantha, commonly known as the many-armed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant features numerous areoles, from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in spring or early summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
