Myracrodruon macrocalyx

Myracrodruon macrocalyx, commonly known as the Natal plum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in coastal areas and sandy soils. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible fruits that are yellow or red when mature. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough or hairy texture.

The Natal plum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
