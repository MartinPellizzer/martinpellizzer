Maranta brachystachys

Maranta brachystachys, commonly known as the silver stripe arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that display a combination of green, silver, and white markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or red in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta brachystachys is often grown as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
