Maxillaria schultzei

Maxillaria schultzei, commonly known as the Schultze's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and green. The plant typically has two to three erect, lance-shaped leaves and blooms during the dry season. Its flowers are often borne on lateral spikes and are noted for their intricate petal structures.

Maxillaria schultzei is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
