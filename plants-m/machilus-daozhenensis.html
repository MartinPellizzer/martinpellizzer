Machilus daozhenensis

Machilus daozhenensis, commonly known as the Daozhen privet, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the Yunnan and Sichuan provinces. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are typically elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow, followed by small, fleshy berries.

Machilus daozhenensis is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
