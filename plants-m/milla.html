Milla

Milla, commonly known as the golden crown or African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers in shades of white, yellow, and purple, often with contrasting markings. Its leaves are typically velvety and heart-shaped, providing a lush backdrop for the blooms.

Milla thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.
