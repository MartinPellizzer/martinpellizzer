Malus × denboerii

Malus × denboerii, commonly known as the White Pear, is a hybrid deciduous tree belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its ornamental white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree produces small, edible fruits that are typically yellow or green, though they are not as prized for culinary use as other pears. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability, it is often cultivated for ornamental purposes.
