Mundulea anceps var. mangokyensis

Mundulea anceps var. mangokyensis, commonly known as the Mangoky acacia, is a shrub or small tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid and nutrient-poor soils.

It plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and forage for wildlife.
