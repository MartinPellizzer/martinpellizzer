Mesoneuron motleyanum

Mesoneuron motleyanum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a sprawling growth habit with hairy, silver-green leaves that help reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from pale purple to deep violet, typically blooming at night. Its fruit is a fleshy, berry-like structure that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Due to its toxic properties, it is often avoided by livestock and requires caution during handling.
