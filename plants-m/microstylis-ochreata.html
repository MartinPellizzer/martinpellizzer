Microstylis ochreata

Microstylis ochreata, commonly known as the ochre spikegrass, is a small, flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. Microstylis ochreata typically grows in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds, where it thrives in moist, acidic soils.
