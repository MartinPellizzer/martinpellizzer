Maxillaria dodsonii

Maxillaria dodsonii, commonly known as the Dodson's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, and it thrives in cool, humid environments.

Botanically, it is notable for its intricate floral structure and adaptability to high-altitude conditions.
