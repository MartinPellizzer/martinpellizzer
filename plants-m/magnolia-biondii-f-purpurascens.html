Magnolia biondii f. purpurascens

Magnolia biondii f. purpurascens, commonly known as the Purple-leaf Magnolia, is a hybrid cultivar that exhibits distinctive botanical features.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that display a striking purplish hue, especially during the growing season. The plant produces large, showy flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens.

This cultivar is valued for its unique foliage and floral display, contributing to its appeal in horticultural settings.
