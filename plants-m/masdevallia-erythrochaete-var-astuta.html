Masdevallia erythrochaete var. astuta

Masdevallia erythrochaete var. astuta, commonly known as the red-tipped masdevallia, is a tropical orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and vibrant, velvety flowers that range from deep red to orange in color. The plant produces showy, lance-shaped petals and a distinctive, elongated lip that enhances its ornamental appeal. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure and requires well-draining, acidic potting mix.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking color and relatively compact size.
