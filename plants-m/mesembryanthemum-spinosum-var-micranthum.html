Mesembryanthemum spinosum var. micranthum

Mesembryanthemum spinosum var. micranthum, commonly known as the spiny ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, spiny leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant thrives in arid environments and is adapted to store water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It is often found in rocky soils and is popular as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

The variety micranthum is distinguished by its smaller flowers compared to other varieties of Mesembryanthemum spinosum.
