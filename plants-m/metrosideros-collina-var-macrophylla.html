Metrosideros collina var. macrophylla

Metrosideros collina var. macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf pohutukawa, is a variant of the pohutukawa species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its large, lance-shaped leaves that are typically broader than those of the typical variety. The plant produces clusters of bright red or orange flowers, which bloom during the spring and summer months. It thrives in coastal areas and is often found growing on cliffs or sandy soils.

This variant is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in New Zealand's coastal ecosystems.
