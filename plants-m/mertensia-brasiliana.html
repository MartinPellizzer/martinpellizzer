Mertensia brasiliana

Mertensia brasiliana, commonly known as Brazilian bluebells, is a herbaceous perennial native to Brazil.

It features striking deep blue, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has heart-shaped, palmately lobed leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery down. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mertensia brasiliana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
