Mammillaria guillauminiana

Mammillaria guillauminiana, commonly known as the Guillaumin's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 to 15 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles in the early summer.

The plant is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
