Micranthes japonica

Micranthes japonica, commonly known as Japanese micranthus, is a herbaceous perennial native to Japan.

It features small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, blooming in early summer. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Micranthes japonica is valued in horticulture for its delicate appearance and ability to naturalize in suitable conditions.
