Monochilus regius

Monochilus regius, commonly known as the royal monochilus, is a rare and striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
