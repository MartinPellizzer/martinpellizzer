Miconia miles-morgani

Miconia miles-morgani, commonly known as the "mystery miconia," is a tropical shrub native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Due to its ornamental value, it has been introduced to other regions, though it is considered invasive in some areas.
