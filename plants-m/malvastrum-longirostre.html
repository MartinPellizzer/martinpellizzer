Malvastrum longirostre

Malvastrum longirostre, commonly known as the narrow-beaked mallow, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, elongated stems and large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, wind-dispersed seeds. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it is well adapted to dry conditions with deep roots and waxy leaves.

It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of habitats.
