Memecylon pyrifolium

Memecylon pyrifolium, commonly known as the fire-leaf memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle. The plant produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers followed by small, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural studies.
