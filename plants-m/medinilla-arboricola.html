Medinilla arboricola

Medinilla arboricola, commonly known as the tree medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, glossy, dark green foliage and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round, red fruits that contain seeds. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla arboricola is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
