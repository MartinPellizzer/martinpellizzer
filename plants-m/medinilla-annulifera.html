Medinilla annulifera

Medinilla annulifera, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces fruit that resembles a small, round orange. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla annulifera is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and seasonal blooming.
