Myoporum sandwicense subsp. lanaiense

Myoporum sandwicense subsp.

lanaiense, commonly known as the Lanai myoporum, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas. It has small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of hair.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in its native habitat.
