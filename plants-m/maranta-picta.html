Maranta picta

Maranta picta, commonly known as the Painted Wandering君子兰, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful leaf patterns that resemble painted designs, hence its common name. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green, red, and yellow. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers in clusters, typically in white or pale pink.

Maranta picta thrives in humid, shaded environments and is popular as a decorative houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal.
