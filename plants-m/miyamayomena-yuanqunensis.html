Miyamayomena yuanqunensis

Miyamayomena yuanqunensis, commonly known as the Yunnan mountain orchid, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in forest understories where it receives dappled sunlight. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to anchor in rocky or loamy soils, while its leaves are narrow and strap-like.
