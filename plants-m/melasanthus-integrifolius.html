Melasanthus integrifolius

Melasanthus integrifolius, commonly known as the smooth melasanthus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or white. The plant has simple, entire leaves that are often arranged in opposite pairs along the stem. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that releases small, winged seeds when mature.
