Maxillaria brocklehurstiana

Maxillaria brocklehurstiana, commonly known as the Brocklehurst's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a sympodial epiphyte, meaning it grows on other plants without being parasitic, and it thrives in humid, warm environments. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinctive yellow or green throat. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive periods of drought.

It is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
