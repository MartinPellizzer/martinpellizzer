Melastoma borneense

Melastoma borneense, commonly known as the Bornean melastoma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with smooth or slightly hairy surfaces, and its stems are often woody and branched. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Melastoma borneense is valued for its ornamental qualities and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
