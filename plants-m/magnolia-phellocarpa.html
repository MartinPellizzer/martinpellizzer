Magnolia phellocarpa

Magnolia phellocarpa, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broad-leaf magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has a broad, spreading crown and can grow up to 20 meters tall. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The bark is typically grayish-brown and becomes fissured with age.
