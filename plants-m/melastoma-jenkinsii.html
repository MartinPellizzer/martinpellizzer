Melastoma jenkinsii

Melastoma jenkinsii, commonly known as the Jenkins' melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to pink. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma jenkinsii produces small, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
