Melica Aquatica

Melica aquatica is a unique aquatic grass species that thrives in wetland environments. It is commonly found in marshes, ponds, and along the edges of slow-moving streams. This plant plays a vital role in stabilizing soil and filtering water, contributing to ecosystem health. Its slender stems and delicate leaves make it a distinctive feature of wetland landscapes. Despite its modest appearance, Melica aquatica is essential for maintaining the balance of aquatic habitats.
