Melastoma kuchingense

Melastoma kuchingense, commonly known as the Kuching melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Borneo.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas of tropical rainforests. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticultural settings.
