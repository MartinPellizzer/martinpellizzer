Melinia angustifolia

Melinia angustifolia, commonly known as narrowleaf melinia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows in grasslands and open woodlands, preferring well-drained soils. The plant has slender, upright stems with narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are usually greenish-white and appear in clusters.

Melinia angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
