Maxillariella × yucatanensis

Maxillariella × yucatanensis, commonly known as the Yucatan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the Yucatán Peninsula.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in tropical and subtropical regions for horticultural purposes.
