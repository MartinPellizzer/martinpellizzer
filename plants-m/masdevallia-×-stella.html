Masdevallia × stella

Masdevallia × stella, commonly known as the star masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid that combines traits from its parent species, Masdevallia tenuifolia and Masdevallia rhombifolia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting blooms that often display a mix of red, orange, and yellow hues. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic potting mix.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively manageable care requirements.
