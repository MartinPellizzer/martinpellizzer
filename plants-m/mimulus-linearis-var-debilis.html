Mimulus linearis var. debilis

Mimulus linearis var. debilis, commonly known as the slender yellow monkeyflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features slender, linear leaves that are typically less than 2 inches in length, arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers with a distinctive bilabiate shape, typical of the Physostegia genus. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in streamside habitats.
