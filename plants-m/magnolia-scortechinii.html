Magnolia scortechinii

Magnolia scortechinii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the pink magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
