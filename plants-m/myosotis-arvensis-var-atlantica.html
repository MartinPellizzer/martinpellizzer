Myosotis arvensis var. atlantica

Myosotis arvensis var. atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy or gravelly soils along shorelines and dunes. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slightly hairy texture.

This variety is notable for its ability to thrive in coastal environments despite exposure to salt spray and strong winds.
