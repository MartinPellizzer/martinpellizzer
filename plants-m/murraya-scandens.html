Murraya scandens

Murraya scandens, commonly known as the climbing murraya, is a tropical evergreen shrub or vine native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, followed by orange-red berries. The plant has glossy, oval-shaped leaves and is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Murraya scandens is also valued as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering habit.
