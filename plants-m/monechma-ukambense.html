Monechma ukambense

Monechma ukambense, commonly known as the African milkweed, is a herbaceous plant native to eastern Africa.

It typically grows in open grasslands and forest margins, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. The plant features opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pale pink.

Monechma ukambense produces capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
