Millettia obtusifoliolata

Millettia obtusifoliolata, commonly known as the broad-leaved millettia, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves with rounded leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing lens-shaped seeds. It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its hard, durable wood.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
