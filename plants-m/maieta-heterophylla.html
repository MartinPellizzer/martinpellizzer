Maieta heterophylla

Maieta heterophylla, commonly known as the variable maieta, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy foliage and small, daisy-like flowers. Due to its unique appearance and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
