Mentzelia texana

Mentzelia texana, commonly known as Texas blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant produces striking yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs.

Mentzelia texana is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
