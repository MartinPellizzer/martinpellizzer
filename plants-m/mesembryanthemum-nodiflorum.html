Mesembryanthemum nodiflorum

Mesembryanthemum nodiflorum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is well adapted to arid environments due to its ability to store water in its thick leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and resilience make it a valuable addition to both ornamental gardens and ecological restoration projects.
