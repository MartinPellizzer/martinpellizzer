Maxillaria brachypoda

Maxillaria brachypoda, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. The plant features a short, thick rhizome with several fleshy, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually pink or white, appearing in clusters along the stem.

Maxillaria brachypoda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in warm, moist conditions.
