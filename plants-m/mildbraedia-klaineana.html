Mildbraedia klaineana

Mildbraedia klaineana, commonly known as the ghost orchid, is a rare and enigmatic flowering plant native to the rainforests of Queensland, Australia.

It is an epiphytic orchid, meaning it grows on the surface of trees without deriving nutrients from them. The plant produces small, pale greenish-white flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.

Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it is considered critically endangered.
