Melastoma quinquenervium

Melastoma quinquenervium, commonly known as the five-veined melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with five distinct veins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
