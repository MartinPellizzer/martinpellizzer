Mammillaria kewensis

Mammillaria kewensis, commonly known as the Kew's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a gray-green stem. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and minimal water.
