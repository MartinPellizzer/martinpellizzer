Melastoma bipartitum

Melastoma bipartitum, commonly known as the two-part melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters, and are known for their attractive appearance.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
