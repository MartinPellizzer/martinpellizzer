Melastoma kostermansii

Melastoma kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically range in color from pink to red. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
