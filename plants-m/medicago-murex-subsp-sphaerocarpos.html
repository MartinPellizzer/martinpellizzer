Medicago murex subsp. sphaerocarpos

Medicago murex subsp.

sphaerocarpos, commonly known as the spherical carpos medick, is a perennial herbaceous plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae. It is characterized by its small, stipitate leaves and distinctive, spherical pods that mature to a reddish color.

The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences. It is typically found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky soils.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and traditional herbal uses.
