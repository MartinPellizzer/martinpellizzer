Machilus sheareri

Machilus sheareri, commonly known as the Chinese laurustin, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Machilus sheareri is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and seasonal interest.
