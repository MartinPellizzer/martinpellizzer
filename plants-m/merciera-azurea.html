Merciera azurea

Merciera azurea, commonly known as the Azure Balm, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun exposure.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
