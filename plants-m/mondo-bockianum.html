Mondo bockianum

Mondo bockianum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a wide range of colors. The plant has fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of white wax. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mondo bockianum is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
