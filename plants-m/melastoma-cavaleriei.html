Melastoma cavaleriei

Melastoma cavaleriei, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
