Mimosa riedelii

Mimosa riedelii, commonly known as the velvetleaf mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features compound leaves that are covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it a soft texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in spherical clusters, which bloom in spring. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, well-drained soils.

Mimosa riedelii is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful plant in certain ecological contexts.
