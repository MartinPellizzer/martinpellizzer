Muhlenbergia rara

Muhlenbergia rara, commonly known as the rare muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and produces delicate, feathery seed heads. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or prairies. Its botanical features include narrow leaves and a clumping growth habit.

Due to its rarity, it is of particular interest to botanists and conservationists.
