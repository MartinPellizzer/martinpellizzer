Monanthotaxis velutina

Monanthotaxis velutina, commonly known as the velvetleaf monanthotaxis, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Araceae and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves covered in a fine, velvety pubescence. Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though care must be taken to prevent its spread.
