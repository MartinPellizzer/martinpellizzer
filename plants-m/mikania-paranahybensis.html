Mikania paranahybensis

Mikania paranahybensis, commonly known as the Brazilian Mikania, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to spread aggressively. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its small yellow flowers are arranged in clusters and bloom throughout the growing season.

Mikania paranahybensis is often found in moist, shaded areas and can thrive in a variety of soil types.
