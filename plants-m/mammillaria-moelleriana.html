Mammillaria moelleriana

Mammillaria moelleriana, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, segmented stems that are often covered in white or pale pink spines. The plant typically produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. Its succulent nature allows it to store water efficiently, making it well adapted to arid environments.

Mammillaria moelleriana is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
