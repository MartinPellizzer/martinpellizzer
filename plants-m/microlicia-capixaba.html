Microlicia capixaba

Microlicia capixaba, commonly known as the Capixaba orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces a single, often showy flower per stem. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
