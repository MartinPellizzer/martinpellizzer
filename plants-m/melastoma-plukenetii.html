Melastoma plukenetii

Melastoma plukenetii, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink to purple flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that are typically green when unripe and turn red when mature. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma plukenetii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine.
