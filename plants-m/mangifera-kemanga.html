Mangifera kemanga

Mangifera kemanga, commonly known as the red mango, is a tropical fruit tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, reddish-orange fruits with a sweet and tangy flavor. The plant produces elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. Its flowers are small and yellow, typically appearing in clusters.

The tree thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
