Microcos stylocarpa

Microcos stylocarpa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and produces clusters of small, daisy-like yellow flowers. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
