Malesherbia arequipensis

Malesherbia arequipensis, commonly known as the Arequipa malesherbia, is a shrub native to the Andean region of South America.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in high-altitude environments with well-drained soils.
