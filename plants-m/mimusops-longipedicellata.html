Mimusops longipedicellata

Mimusops longipedicellata, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

The tree is valued for its hard, heavy wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
