Myoporum laxiflorum

Myoporum laxiflorum, commonly known as the slenderflower myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring woody stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often blooming in spring. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine, short hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Myoporum laxiflorum is adapted to a range of soil types and is often found in coastal and forested areas.
