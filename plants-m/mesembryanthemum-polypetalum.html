Mesembryanthemum polypetalum

Mesembryanthemum polypetalum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces bright yellow flowers with numerous petals, typically blooming in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental value make it a desirable addition to Mediterranean and coastal climates.
