Muhlenbergia mollicoma

Muhlenbergia mollicoma, commonly known as silky muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features slender, upright stems that produce fluffy, silver-gray seed heads, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are soft to the touch, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sunlight, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Muhlenbergia mollicoma is valued for its drought tolerance and ability to provide habitat for wildlife.
