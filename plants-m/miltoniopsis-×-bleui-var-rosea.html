Miltoniopsis × bleui var. rosea

Miltoniopsis × bleui var. rosea, commonly known as the rose-flowered hybrid of Miltoniopsis, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of South America. The plant typically produces clusters of pink to rose-colored blooms with contrasting white or cream petals. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
