Macrolobium flexuosum var. flexuosum

Macrolobium flexuosum var. flexuosum, commonly known as the flexible broadleaf.

It is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America, characterized by its broad, leathery leaves that are often curled or rolled. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are adapted to withstand strong winds, which is a key feature of its natural habitat.

It is often used in ornamental gardening due to its unique foliage and drought tolerance.
