Magnolia archilana

Magnolia archilana, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and are borne in clusters at the base of the branches.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
