Michelia excelsa

Michelia excelsa, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or fragrant magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white or pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Michelia excelsa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
