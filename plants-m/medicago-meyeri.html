Medicago meyeri

Medicago meyeri, commonly known as Meyer's clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and fix atmospheric nitrogen, benefiting surrounding vegetation. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its adaptability makes it a valuable plant for ecological restoration projects.
