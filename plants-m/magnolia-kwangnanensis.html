Magnolia kwangnanensis

Magnolia kwangnanensis, commonly known as the Guangnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, with distinct ridges.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is valued for its ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
