Melastoma mutisii

Melastoma mutisii, commonly known as the tropical wild strawberry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows up to 1.5 meters tall, featuring opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy green surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds, often consumed by birds and mammals.

Melastoma mutisii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
