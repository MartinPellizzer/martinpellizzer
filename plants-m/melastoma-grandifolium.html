Melastoma grandifolium

Melastoma grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny seeds.

Melastoma grandifolium is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
