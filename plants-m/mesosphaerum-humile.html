Mesosphaerum humile

Mesosphaerum humile, commonly known as the small sphere plant, is a small, herbaceous species native to southern Africa.

It features a compact, low-growing habit with numerous small, rounded stems that form a dense mat. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in umbels, typical of the Asteraceae family. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Mesosphaerum humile thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
