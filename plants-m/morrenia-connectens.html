Morrenia connectens

Morrenia connectens, commonly known as the yellow morrenia, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are usually narrow and slightly hairy, and it has a woody stem with a rough texture.

Morrenia connectens is adapted to withstand drought conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
