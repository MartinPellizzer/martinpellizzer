Mimusops subsericea var. acmanthera

Mimusops subsericea var. acmanthera, commonly known as the white mangosteen, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its fruit is a round, segmented berry that turns from green to yellow when mature.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has been used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
