Masdevallia leucantha

Masdevallia leucantha, commonly known as the white masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Masdevallia leucantha is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
