Melochia decumbens

Melochia decumbens, commonly known as the prostrate melochia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a low-growing, sprawling shrub that typically reaches heights of less than 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in dense inflorescences.

Melochia decumbens is often found in disturbed soils and is used in traditional medicine in some parts of Africa and Southeast Asia.
