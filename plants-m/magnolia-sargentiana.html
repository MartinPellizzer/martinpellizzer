Magnolia sargentiana

Magnolia sargentiana, commonly known as the Sargent magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.

The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it adaptable to various climates.
