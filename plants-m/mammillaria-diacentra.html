Mammillaria diacentra

Mammillaria diacentra, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and typically has 10 to 15 prominent, narrow ribs. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
