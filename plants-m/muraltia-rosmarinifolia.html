Muraltia rosmarinifolia

Muraltia rosmarinifolia, commonly known as the rosemary bush or rosemary tree, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, fragrant white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has leathery, dark green leaves with a strong, aromatic scent reminiscent of rosemary. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for Mediterranean climates.

Muraltia rosmarinifolia is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and its ability to attract pollinators.
