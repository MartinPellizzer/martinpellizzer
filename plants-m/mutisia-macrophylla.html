Mutisia macrophylla

Mutisia macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Mutisia macrophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
