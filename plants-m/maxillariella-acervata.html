Maxillariella acervata

Maxillariella acervata, commonly known as the clustered Maxillariella, is a small orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes with small, white to pale yellow blooms. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillariella acervata is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
