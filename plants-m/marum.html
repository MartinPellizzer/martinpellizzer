Marum

Marum, commonly known as *Acmella oleracea*, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant contains compounds like mucilage and alkaloids, which contribute to its medicinal properties. It is often used in traditional medicine for its anti-inflammatory and analgesic effects.

Marum thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is commonly found in wetlands and marshy areas.
