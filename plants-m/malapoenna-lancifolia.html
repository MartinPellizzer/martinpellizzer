Malapoenna lancifolia

Malapoenna lancifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved malapoenna, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are often pale yellow or greenish in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Malapoenna lancifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique foliage.
