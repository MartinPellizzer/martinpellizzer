Musa brieyi

Musa brieyi, commonly known as the Philippine banana, is a species of banana native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are typically consumed raw or cooked. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and for local consumption.
