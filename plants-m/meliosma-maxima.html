Meliosma maxima

Meliosma maxima, commonly known as the giant meliosma, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
