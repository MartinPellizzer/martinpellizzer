Microkentia schlechteri

Microkentia schlechteri, commonly known as the lace fern, is a compact, evergreen fern native to Southeast Asia.

It features delicate, arching fronds with a lacy, feathery appearance, giving it a refined and ornamental look. The plant typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 feet, making it suitable for indoor cultivation. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for both indoor and shaded outdoor gardens.
