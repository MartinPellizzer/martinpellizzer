Matthiola sinuata var. glabrata

Matthiola sinuata var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth wallflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically range in color from white to yellow. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It produces elongated pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
