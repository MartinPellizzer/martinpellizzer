Mammillaria pottsii var. multicaulis

Mammillaria pottsii var. multicaulis, commonly known as the clustered mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with multiple short, cylindrical branches that give it a clustered appearance. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its small, spiny areoles are characteristic of the Mammillaria genus.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
