Mammillaria rhodantha f. pfeifferi

Mammillaria rhodantha f. pfeifferi, commonly known as the Pfeiffer's pink mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pink spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
