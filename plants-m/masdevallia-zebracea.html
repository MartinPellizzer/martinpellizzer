Masdevallia zebracea

Masdevallia zebracea, commonly known as the zebracea masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its striking, often colorful flowers that display intricate patterns and vibrant hues. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to thrive in high-altitude, humid environments.

Masdevallia zebracea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
