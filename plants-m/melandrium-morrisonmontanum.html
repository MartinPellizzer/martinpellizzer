Melandrium morrisonmontanum

Melandrium morrisonmontanum, commonly known as the Morrison Mountains catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the high elevations of the Morrison Mountains in Colorado.

It is a member of the Caryophyllaceae family, characterized by its succulent leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers.
