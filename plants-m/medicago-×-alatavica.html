Medicago × alatavica

Medicago × alatavica, commonly known as the Alatavica clover, is a hybrid plant species within the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It is known for its hardy nature and ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.

Medicago × alatavica is often used in forage and soil stabilization due to its nutritional value and ecological benefits.
