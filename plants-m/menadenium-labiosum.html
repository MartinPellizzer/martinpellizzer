Menadenium labiosum

Menadenium labiosum, commonly known as the Lipstems or Lipstems plant, is a member of the Acanthaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Menadenium labiosum is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its vibrant floral display and compact growth habit.
