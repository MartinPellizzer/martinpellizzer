Machaerium steinbachianum

Machaerium steinbachianum, commonly known as the pink machaerium, is a tropical perennial legume native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking pink to red flowers and feathery, trifoliate leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its inflorescence consists of dense, spike-like clusters of small, showy blooms.

Machaerium steinbachianum is often used in ornamental horticulture for its vibrant floral display.
