Matelea oblongata

Matelea oblongata, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a flowering plant native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes milkweeds and other flowering plants with showy blooms. The flowers typically open at dusk and close by dawn, making it a unique addition to gardens with its ephemeral beauty.
