Mammillaria crassior

Mammillaria crassior, commonly known as the thick-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and dense spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and features a green to bluish-green color. It blooms with yellow flowers during the spring or early summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
