Monanthes × filifolia

Monanthes × filifolia, commonly known as the filifoliate monanthes, is a hybrid plant that exhibits characteristics of its parent species.

It is native to regions with temperate climates and thrives in well-drained soil. The plant features narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its flowers are typically small and clustered, producing a delicate, fragrant bloom.

Botanically, it is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
