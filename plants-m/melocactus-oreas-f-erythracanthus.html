Melocactus oreas f. erythracanthus

Melocactus oreas f. erythracanthus, commonly known as the red-spined melocactus, is a distinctive cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem covered in dense, dark green spines that often have a reddish tint. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
