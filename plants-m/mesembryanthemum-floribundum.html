Mesembryanthemum floribundum

Mesembryanthemum floribundum, commonly known as the flowering carpet, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it ideal for rock gardens or xeriscaping. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.

Mesembryanthemum floribundum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and easy care requirements.
