Magnolia fordiana var. forrestii

Magnolia fordiana var. forrestii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or forrest's magnolia, is a striking deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant features broad, heart-shaped leaves that turn yellow in autumn, adding to its ornamental value. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for temperate climates.

The species is valued in horticulture for its elegant appearance and fragrance.
