Melastoma berteroanum

Melastoma berteroanum, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to violet. Its leaves are typically opposite and oval-shaped, with a glossy surface.

The fruit is a small, segmented berry that ripens to a deep purple or red, containing several seeds.
