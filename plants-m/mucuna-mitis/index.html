Mucuna Mitis

Mucuna mitis is a tropical legume native to parts of Asia and Africa. It is known for its distinctive velvety pods and its ability to thrive in humid environments. This plant has been traditionally used in herbal medicine for its potential health benefits. Its seeds contain high levels of protein and are often used as a food source. Mucuna mitis continues to be a subject of scientific interest due to its unique properties and ecological significance.
