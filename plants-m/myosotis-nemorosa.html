Myosotis nemorosa

Myosotis nemorosa, commonly known as the woods forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of small, blue or purple flowers with five petals. Its leaves are heart-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem.

Myosotis nemorosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
