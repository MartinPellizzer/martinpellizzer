Myrcia taubatensis

Myrcia taubatensis, commonly known as the Taubaté myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to Brazil, particularly in the states of São Paulo and Minas Gerais. The plant is characterized by its evergreen foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds.

Myrcia taubatensis is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
