Macrolobium defloccatum

Macrolobium defloccatum, commonly known as the Floccate Macrolobium, is a unique flowering plant native to the tropical rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that display a striking combination of vibrant colors and intricate patterns. The plant's leaves are broad and lobed, contributing to its distinctive appearance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a vulnerable species in its natural range.
