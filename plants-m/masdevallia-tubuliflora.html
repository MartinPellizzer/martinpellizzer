Masdevallia tubuliflora

Masdevallia tubuliflora, commonly known as the tubular orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, tubular-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces showy, violet or purple blooms that are often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique and elegant appearance.
