Masdevallia ignea var. aurantiaca

Masdevallia ignea var. aurantiaca, commonly known as the fiery flame orchid, is a striking member of the Orchidaceae family native to the cloud forests of South America.

This variety is characterized by its vibrant orange and red floral displays, which are highly sought after in horticulture for their intense coloration. The plant features wiry, leafless stems and produces small, pendulous flowers in clusters, typical of many orchid species. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light, requiring careful attention to humidity and airflow.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a prized exotic plant in specialized collections.
