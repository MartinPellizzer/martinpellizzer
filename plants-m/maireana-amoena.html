Maireana amoena

Maireana amoena, commonly known as the slender spinifex, is a small shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dense, wiry foliage and small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showing resistance to drought and salt. Its leaves are typically narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss.

Maireana amoena is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ability to stabilize soil in sandy or rocky areas.
