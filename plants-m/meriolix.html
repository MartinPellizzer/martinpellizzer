Meriolix

Meriolix, commonly known as the purple milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its striking purple to pink flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators such as butterflies and bees. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and natural habitats.

Meriolix plays an important role in supporting local ecosystems by providing nectar and shelter for wildlife.
