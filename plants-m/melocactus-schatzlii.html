Melocactus schatzlii

Melocactus schatzlii, commonly known as the Schatzlii melon cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, round, ribbed stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces a single, large, yellow or orange flower that blooms at the top of the stem during the summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
