Millettia wellensii

Millettia wellensii, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils and can grow up to 20 meters tall.

Due to its ornamental value and fast growth rate, it is sometimes cultivated in urban landscapes.
