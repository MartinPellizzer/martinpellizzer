Mimosa distans subsp. elongata

Mimosa distans subsp.

elongata, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a perennial shrub native to South America. It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are compound and pinnate.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
