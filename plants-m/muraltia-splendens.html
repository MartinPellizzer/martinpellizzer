Muraltia splendens

Muraltia splendens, commonly known as the splendour muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by red, fleshy fruits. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in partial shade.

Muraltia splendens is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
