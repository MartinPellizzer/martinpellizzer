Miconia micrantha

Miconia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically small, clustered in inflorescences, and feature showy, often pink or white petals.

Miconia micrantha is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
