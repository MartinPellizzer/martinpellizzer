Macrosolen rotundatus

Macrosolen rotundatus, commonly known as the roundleaf macrosolen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves that are typically rounded or heart-shaped. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in tropical gardens.
