Marsdenia wightii

Marsdenia wightii, commonly known as the Wight's Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asclepiadaceae family.

It is native to the Indian subcontinent and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels, often with a distinctive corona. Marsdenia wightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
