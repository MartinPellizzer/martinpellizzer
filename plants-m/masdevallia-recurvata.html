Masdevallia recurvata

Masdevallia recurvata, commonly known as the recurved masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features long, slender pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette. Its flowers typically have a unique recurved lip, which is a distinguishing feature of the genus.

Masdevallia recurvata is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
