Mezoneuron cucullatum var. robustum

Mezoneuron cucullatum var. robustum, commonly known as the broad-leaved mezoneuron, is a perennial herb native to the eastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in rich, well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental foliage and is often used in native plant gardens.
