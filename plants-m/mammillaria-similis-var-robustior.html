Mammillaria similis var. robustior

Mammillaria similis var. robustior, commonly known as the robust mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular to cylindrical stem with white to pale green spines and a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
