Miconia heteromera

Miconia heteromera, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of soft, velvety hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, three-lobed capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia heteromera is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of moist, shaded environments.
