Maltea vacillans

Maltea vacillans, commonly known as the wavering maltea, is a unique flowering plant native to the temperate regions of southern Europe.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants, and is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves. Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in its native range.
