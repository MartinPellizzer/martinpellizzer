Melastoma guianense

Melastoma guianense, commonly known as the Guianese melastoma, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Melastoma guianense produces small, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various wildlife species.
