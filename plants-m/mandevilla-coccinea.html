Mandevilla coccinea

Mandevilla coccinea, commonly known as the red mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red tubular flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves and can grow up to 20 feet in length if supported. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla coccinea is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and ability to climb or trail.
