Mammillaria plinthimorpha

Mammillaria plinthimorpha, commonly known as the Mexican pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a pale green body covered in white, woolly spines. The plant features numerous areoles from which both spines and flowers emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
