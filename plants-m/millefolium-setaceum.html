Millefolium setaceum

Millefolium setaceum, commonly known as the stiff-leaved yarrow, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features finely divided, linear leaves that are often described as resembling a feather, giving it a delicate appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Millefolium setaceum is also valued for its aromatic properties and historical use in traditional medicine.
