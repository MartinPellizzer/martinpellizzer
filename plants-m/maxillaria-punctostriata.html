Maxillaria punctostriata

Maxillaria punctostriata, commonly known as the spotted-striated orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a unique coloration pattern. The flowers typically feature a dark purple or brown lip with white or yellow markings, and the petals and sepals are often a contrasting color. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
