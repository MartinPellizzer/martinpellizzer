Myosotis pyrenaica

Myosotis pyrenaica, commonly known as the Pyrenean forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Pyrenees mountain range.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant produces small, blue or violet flowers with five petals, which bloom in early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Myosotis pyrenaica is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting pollinators.
