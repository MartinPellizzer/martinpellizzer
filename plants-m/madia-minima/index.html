Madia Minima

Small madia, scientifically known as Madia minima, is a delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of western North America. It thrives in harsh environments, showcasing remarkable resilience to drought and extreme temperatures. With its small, daisy-like blooms, it adds a subtle beauty to rocky slopes and sandy soils. Despite its modest size, it plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance. This plant is a testament to nature's ability to adapt and flourish in even the most challenging conditions.
