Mediocactus murrillii

Mediocactus murrillii, commonly known as the Murrill's cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of northern Mexico.

It is a small, columnar cactus with a distinctive green body and white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red fruits. It is adapted to survive in harsh desert conditions with minimal water.

Mediocactus murrillii is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and hardy nature.
