Musa bakeri

Musa bakeri, commonly known as the Baker's banana, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant that typically grows to a height of about 2-3 meters. The plant features large, broad leaves that are usually a deep green color. Its flowers are clustered in a spike and are typically yellow or white in color.

The fruit is a long, slender banana-like fruit that is edible and has a mild flavor.
