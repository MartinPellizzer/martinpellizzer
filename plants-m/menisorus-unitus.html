Menisorus unitus

Menisorus unitus, commonly known as the silverleaf menisorus, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its leaves are simple and opposite, with a rough texture.

Menisorus unitus is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to grow in well-drained soil.
