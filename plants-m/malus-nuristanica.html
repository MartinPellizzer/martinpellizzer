Malus nuristanica

Malus nuristanica, commonly known as the Nuristan apple, is a species of flowering plant in the rose family, Rosaceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of eastern Afghanistan and northern Pakistan. The plant is characterized by its ornamental white flowers and small, edible fruits. It thrives in temperate climates with well-drained soil and requires full sunlight.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
