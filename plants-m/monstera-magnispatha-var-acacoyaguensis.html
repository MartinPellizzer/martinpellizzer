Monstera magnispatha var. acacoyaguensis

Monstera magnispatha var. acacoyaguensis, commonly known as the Acacoyaguensis Monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that have distinctive notches and holes, a feature that sets it apart from other monstera species. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Its rapid growth rate and unique foliage make it a striking addition to any plant collection.

The varietal name "acacoyaguensis" refers to its origin in the region of Acacoyagua in Panama.
