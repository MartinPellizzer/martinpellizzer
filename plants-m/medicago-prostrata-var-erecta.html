Medicago prostrata var. erecta

Medicago prostrata var. erecta, commonly known as the upright goat's rue, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit, in contrast to the prostrate form of the typical M. prostrata. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are trifoliate, consisting of three oval-shaped leaflets.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny environments.
