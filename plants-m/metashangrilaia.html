Metashangrilaia

Metashangrilaia, commonly known as the celestial orchid, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy flowers that resemble the mythical Shangrila landscape. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces fragrant blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is highly valued in botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
