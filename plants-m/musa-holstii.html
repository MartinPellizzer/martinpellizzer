Musa holstii

Musa holstii, commonly known as the Giant Banana Plant, is a striking member of the Musaceae family native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters, giving it a dramatic appearance. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, banana-like fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-draining soil and requires abundant sunlight.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a landscape plant in tropical gardens.
