Mayepea

Mayepea, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It belongs to the Solanaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like tomatoes and peppers. The plant produces small, purple berries that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves and flowers are also utilized in herbal remedies for their purported health benefits.

Mayepea thrives in warm, tropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.
