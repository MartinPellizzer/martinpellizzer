Maxillaria chrysantha var. acutifolia

Maxillaria chrysantha var. acutifolia, commonly known as the Acute-leaved Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, yellow-orange flowers with a distinctive red lip. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves that are acuminate, giving it its common name. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

This orchid is popular among enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
