Maranta pumila

Maranta pumila, commonly known as the dwarf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a member of the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant features heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are often variegated with silver and green patterns. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

Maranta pumila is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often grown as a houseplant due to its compact size and attractive foliage.
