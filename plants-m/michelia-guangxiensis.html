Michelia guangxiensis

Michelia guangxiensis, commonly known as Guangxi michelia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Magnoliaceae.

It is native to southern China, particularly the Guangxi region, and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
