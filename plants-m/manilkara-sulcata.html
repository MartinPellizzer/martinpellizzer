Manilkara sulcata

Manilkara sulcata, commonly known as the Burmese sapodilla, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its large, simple leaves and showy yellow flowers. The tree produces a fruit with a thick, sweet pulp that is similar to the sapodilla but with a distinct flavor. Its bark is smooth and grayish, often marked with shallow grooves.
