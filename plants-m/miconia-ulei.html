Miconia ulei

Miconia ulei, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia ulei is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
