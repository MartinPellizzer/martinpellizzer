Minuartia thymifolia

Minuartia thymifolia, commonly known as the thyme-leaved sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its tiny, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in late spring to early summer.
