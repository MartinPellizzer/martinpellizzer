Miconia buntingii

Miconia buntingii, commonly known as the golden miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains several seeds.

Miconia buntingii is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and persistent flowers.
