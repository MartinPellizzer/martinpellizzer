Mentha × dumetorum var. griseoviridis

Mentha × dumetorum var. griseoviridis, commonly known as greenish gray mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its gray-green foliage, which gives it a distinct appearance compared to other mint varieties. The plant typically grows in moist, temperate climates and is often found in shaded or partially shaded environments. Its leaves are aromatic and contain essential oils, contributing to its medicinal and culinary uses.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil conditions.
