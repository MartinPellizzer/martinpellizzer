Malache subpandurata

Malache subpandurata, commonly known as the pandurata malache, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Nigeria and Ghana. The plant is characterized by its striking, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
