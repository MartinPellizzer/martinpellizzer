Macaranga calcifuga

Macaranga calcifuga, commonly known as the limestone macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its palmate leaves, which are often covered in fine hairs, and its small, inconspicuous flowers.
