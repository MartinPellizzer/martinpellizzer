Matucana krahnii

Matucana krahnii, commonly known as the Krahnii Ctenanthe, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with deep green and white patterns. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of the Araceae family. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Matucana krahnii is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
