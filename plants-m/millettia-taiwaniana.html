Millettia taiwaniana

Millettia taiwaniana, commonly known as the Taiwan millettia, is a flowering plant native to Taiwan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its pods are woody and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

Millettia taiwaniana is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic features.
