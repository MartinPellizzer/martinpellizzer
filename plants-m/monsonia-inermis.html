Monsonia inermis

Monsonia inermis, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Monsonia inermis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
