Maxillaria polybulbon

Maxillaria polybulbon, commonly known as the clustered maxillaria, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plant features dense clusters of small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting the foliage and flower spikes.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a compact rosette pattern.
