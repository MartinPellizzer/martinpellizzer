Mandevilla muelleri

Mandevilla muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with thick, fleshy stems and large, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, typically blooming from spring to fall. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil and plenty of sunlight.

Mandevilla muelleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its vibrant blooms and lush foliage.
