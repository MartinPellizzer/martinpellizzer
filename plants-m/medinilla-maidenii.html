Medinilla maidenii

Medinilla maidenii, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet tangerine shrub, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen shrub form and glossy, dark green leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Medinilla maidenii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
