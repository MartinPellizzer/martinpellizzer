Masdevallia stumpflei

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its leaves are usually narrow and leathery, adapted to thrive in the humid, shaded conditions of its environment.

Masdevallia stumpflei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique beauty and challenging cultivation requirements.
