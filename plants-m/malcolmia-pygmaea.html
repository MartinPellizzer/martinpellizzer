Malcolmia pygmaea

Malcolmia pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf malcolmia, is a small annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features delicate, hairy stems and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of only a few inches, making it a low-growing species. Its leaves are narrow and slightly lobed, often covered in fine hairs.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
