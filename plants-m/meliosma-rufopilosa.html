Meliosma rufopilosa

Meliosma rufopilosa, commonly known as the red-haired meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its small, simple leaves that are often covered in fine, reddish hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it grows in moist, shaded environments.

The bark and leaves of Meliosma rufopilosa have been used in traditional medicine for their purported therapeutic properties.
