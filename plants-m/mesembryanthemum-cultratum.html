Mesembryanthemum cultratum

Mesembryanthemum cultratum, commonly known as the cut-leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves that are adapted to store water. It is a hardy, drought-tolerant species that thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.
