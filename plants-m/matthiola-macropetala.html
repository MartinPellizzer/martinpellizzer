Matthiola macropetala

Matthiola macropetala, commonly known as the large-petaled stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. It typically grows to a height of 30 to 60 centimeters and produces small, capsule-like fruits.
