Meliosma acuminatissima

Meliosma acuminatissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the southeastern United States.

It features elliptical, glossy, dark green leaves that are typically arranged oppositely on the stems. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually white or pale green, followed by clusters of red, fleshy berries. Its leaves have a distinctive, waxy texture and emit a mild, aromatic scent.

It thrives in moist, acidic soils and is often found in shaded woodland environments.
