Masdevallia radiosa

Masdevallia radiosa, commonly known as the Radiant Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that come in a range of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are often adorned with intricate patterns and a unique, spurred lip that aids in pollination.

Masdevallia radiosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its striking appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
