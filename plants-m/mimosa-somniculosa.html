Mimosa somniculosa

Mimosa somniculosa, commonly known as the sleepy mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant is known for its sensitive response to touch, causing its leaves to fold shut when disturbed. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Mimosa somniculosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique botanical behavior.
