Maytenus diospyroides

Maytenus diospyroides, commonly known as the Mexican persimmon, is a small evergreen tree native to Mexico and Central America.

It belongs to the family Celtidaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The species is noted for its ornamental value and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
