Myristica forbesii

Myristica forbesii, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 15 meters tall, characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits containing aromatic seeds. Its seeds are used to produce nutmeg and mace, which are valued for their spice and medicinal properties.

The tree thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
