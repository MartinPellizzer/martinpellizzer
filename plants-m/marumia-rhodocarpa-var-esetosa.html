Marumia rhodocarpa var. esetosa

Marumia rhodocarpa var. esetosa, commonly known as the hairy redwood, is a rare and unique plant species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in mountainous areas with high rainfall. Its flowers are small and clustered, producing a subtle fragrance that attracts pollinators such as bees and moths.
