Montrichardia aculeatum

Montrichardia aculeatum, commonly known as the spiny arrowhead, is a tropical aquatic plant native to South America.

It features heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and prominent spines along the veins, which give it its common name. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshes and ponds. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color, and it produces berries that contain toxic seeds.

Montrichardia aculeatum is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to oxygenate water.
