Mammillaria mammillaris

Mammillaria mammillaris, commonly known as the woolly hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of 5-10 centimeters and has a distinctive rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and appear in the spring.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
