Mondo regnieri

Mondo regnieri, commonly known as the Regnieri's mondo, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing perennial that produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that form dense mats, making it suitable for ground cover in gardens. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight.

Its botanical features make it an attractive and hardy choice for ornamental landscaping.
