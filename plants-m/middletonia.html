Middletonia

Middletonia, commonly known as the middletonia orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. These plants are characterized by their compact growth habit and showy, often fragrant flowers. Middletonia species typically have pseudobulbs and produce inflorescences with clustered blooms.

They are appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for their ornamental value and unique floral structures.
