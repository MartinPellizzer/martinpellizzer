Miconia densiflora

Miconia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

Miconia densiflora is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
