Machilus fukienensis

Machilus fukienensis, commonly known as the Fukien Chinese laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its bark is smooth and light brown, and it produces dark blue, fleshy berries that are edible.

Machilus fukienensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
