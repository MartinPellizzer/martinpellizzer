Magnolia × brooklynensis

Magnolia × brooklynensis, commonly known as Brooklyn magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to the United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a glossy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained, fertile soil.

This ornamental tree is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is often planted in urban landscapes for its beauty and resilience.
