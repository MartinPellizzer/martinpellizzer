Mixandra butyracea

Mixandra butyracea, commonly known as the buttery tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses.
