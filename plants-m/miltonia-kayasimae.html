Miltonia kayasimae

Miltonia kayasimae, commonly known as the Kayasimae orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are relatively short-lived, lasting only a few weeks, and it produces small, greenish-white fruits.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
