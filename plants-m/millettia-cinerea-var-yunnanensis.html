Millettia cinerea var. yunnanensis

Millettia cinerea var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan milk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southwest China.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant is adapted to mountainous regions and thrives in well-drained soils. Its seeds are encased in a hairy pod and are dispersed by wind, contributing to its distribution in forested areas.

It is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional uses in local medicine.
