Maxillaria furfuracea

Maxillaria furfuracea, commonly known as the velvety maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, pale yellow to white flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shady environments. Its flowers bloom in late summer and early autumn, producing a sweet, spicy fragrance.

Maxillaria furfuracea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or warm climates.
