Maranta kegeljanii

Maranta kegeljanii, commonly known as the Kegeljan's arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, with a velvety texture and intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture and well-draining soil.

Maranta kegeljanii is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
