Macrolenes reticulata

Macrolenes reticulata, commonly known as the Chinese lantern plant, is a species of flowering plant in the nightshade family.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red, papery bracts that encase small, inconspicuous flowers. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking, lantern-like appearance.
