Myrtus racemulosa var. conferta

Myrtus racemulosa var. conferta, commonly known as the dense myrtle, is a compact shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, aromatic leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of tiny white flowers in spring, which are followed by small, dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its dense growth habit and fragrant foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.
