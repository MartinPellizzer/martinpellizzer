Momordica multiflora var. multiflora

Momordica multiflora var. multiflora, commonly known as the horned cucumber, is a vigorous vining plant native to Asia.

It produces distinctive, spiky, fruit-like structures that are edible when young and tender. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in culinary applications, particularly in Asian cuisines.
