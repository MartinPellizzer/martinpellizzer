Millettia duclouxii

Millettia duclouxii, commonly known as the African yellowwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant has a shallow root system and is often found in disturbed soils, making it adaptable to various environments.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and dispersed by wind, contributing to its ecological role in forest regeneration.
