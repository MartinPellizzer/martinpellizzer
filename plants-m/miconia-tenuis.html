Miconia tenuis

Miconia tenuis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant produces large, heart-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a waxy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale pink.

Miconia tenuis is notable for its ability to thrive in shaded environments and its potential use in horticulture.
