Minuartia mediterranea

Minuartia mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It is characterized by its small, clustered leaves and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions. It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, which includes other cruciferous plants.

Minuartia mediterranea is well adapted to harsh environments and is often used in rock gardens due to its hardy nature.
