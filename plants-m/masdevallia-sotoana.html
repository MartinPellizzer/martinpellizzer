Masdevallia sotoana

Masdevallia sotoana, commonly known as the Sotoana orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with delicate, elongated leaves and intricate, colorful flowers. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, where it thrives in cool, moist, and shaded environments. Its flowers are often fragrant and display a variety of hues, including pink, purple, and white.

Masdevallia sotoana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
