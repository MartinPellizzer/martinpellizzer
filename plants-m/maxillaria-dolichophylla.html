Maxillaria dolichophylla

Maxillaria dolichophylla, commonly known as the long-leaf maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant features long, narrow leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of pubescence. Its flowers typically bloom in clusters and are known for their vibrant hues, ranging from yellow to orange.

Maxillaria dolichophylla is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
