Maxillaria janiceae

Maxillaria janiceae, commonly known as the Janice's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its flowers are usually pink to purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often marked with contrasting patterns.

Maxillaria janiceae is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
