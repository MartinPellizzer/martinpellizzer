Masdevallia cerastes

Masdevallia cerastes, commonly known as the horned masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and striking, often brightly colored flowers that resemble horns. The plant typically produces a single, showy bloom per stem, which can be pink, red, or orange in color. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and partial shade.

Masdevallia cerastes is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its distinctive appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
