Magnolia thamnodes

Magnolia thamnodes, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall in its natural habitat.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
