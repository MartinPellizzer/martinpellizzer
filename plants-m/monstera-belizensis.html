Monstera belizensis

Monstera belizensis, commonly known as the Belize monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop fenestrations as they mature. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect sunlight. Its stems are aerial and can climb supports, making it an ideal choice for hanging baskets or trellises.

The foliage is often a deep green, with some varieties exhibiting variegation patterns.
