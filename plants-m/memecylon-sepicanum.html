Memecylon sepicanum

Memecylon sepicanum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by capsule fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its foliage.
