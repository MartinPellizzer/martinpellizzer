Mammillaria carnea

Mammillaria carnea, commonly known as the pink hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered with white, woolly spines and pink to red flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a popular choice for succulent gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appeal.

Mammillaria carnea is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and well-draining soil.
