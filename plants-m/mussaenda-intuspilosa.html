Mussaenda intuspilosa

Mussaenda intuspilosa, commonly known as the flame bush, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange, resembling flames. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are often hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-drained soil.

Mussaenda intuspilosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
