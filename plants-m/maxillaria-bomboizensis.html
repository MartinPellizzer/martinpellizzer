Maxillaria bomboizensis

Maxillaria bomboizensis, commonly known as the Bornean maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Borneo.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically blooms during the late dry season, with flowers that have a distinctive, intricate pattern on their petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria bomboizensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
