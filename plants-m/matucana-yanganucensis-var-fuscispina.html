Matucana yanganucensis var. fuscispina

Matucana yanganucensis var. fuscispina, commonly known as the dark-spined matucana, is a rare bromeliad native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its striking, dark green leaves with prominent, spiny marginal teeth. The plant produces a tall, branched inflorescence that bears small, white flowers. It thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a challenging but rewarding species for experienced growers.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a valuable subject for botanical study.
