Maranta sanguinea

Maranta sanguinea, commonly known as the red-rooted arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking red roots and ornamental foliage. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta sanguinea is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
