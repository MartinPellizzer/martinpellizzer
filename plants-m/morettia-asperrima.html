Morettia asperrima

Morettia asperrima, commonly known as the rough morettia, is a flowering plant native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant typically grows in high-altitude environments, often in rocky or mountainous regions. Its leaves are thick and leathery, adapted to withstand the harsh climate of its natural habitat.

Morettia asperrima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts of rare orchids.
