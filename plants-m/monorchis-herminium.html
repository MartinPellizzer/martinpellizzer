Monorchis herminium

Monorchis herminium, commonly known as the Herminium orchid, is a species of terrestrial orchid native to the temperate regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and its showy, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant produces a single flower per stem, which is usually pale yellow to greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodlands or along stream banks.

Monorchis herminium is known for its delicate appearance and is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities.
