Maranta assymetrica

Maranta assymetrica, commonly known as the asymmetrical arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its unique, asymmetrical leaf patterns and vibrant, often variegated colors. Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense clumps.

While it is primarily cultivated for its ornamental value, it can also be grown as a decorative indoor plant in suitable conditions.
