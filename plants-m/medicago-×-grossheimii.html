Medicago × grossheimii

Medicago × grossheimii, commonly known as the hybrid sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Middle East and parts of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage systems.

Its botanical features make it an important species in ecological and agricultural contexts.
