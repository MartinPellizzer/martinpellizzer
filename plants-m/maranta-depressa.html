Maranta depressa

Maranta depressa, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, zigzagging stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with silver and green. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Maranta depressa is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental foliage and relatively low maintenance requirements.
