Muhlenbergia plumiseta

Muhlenbergia plumiseta, commonly known as the silver feather grass, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Muhlenbergia plumiseta is also valued for its role in stabilizing soil and supporting local wildlife.
