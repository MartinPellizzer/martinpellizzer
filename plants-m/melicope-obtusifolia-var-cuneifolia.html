Melicope obtusifolia var. cuneifolia

Melicope obtusifolia var. cuneifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved melicope, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its elliptical to ovate leaves that are usually leathery and have a smooth upper surface. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, followed by spherical, woody fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and traditional herbal practices.
