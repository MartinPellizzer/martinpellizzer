Maranta divaricata f. major

Maranta divaricata f. major, commonly known as the Giant Flame Plant, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, colorful leaves that display vibrant red, orange, and yellow patterns, often resembling flames. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its showy foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity to maintain its lush appearance.

Maranta divaricata f. major is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
