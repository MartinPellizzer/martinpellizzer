Maieta rubra

Maieta rubra, commonly known as the red maieta, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its striking red petals and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and prefers well-drained soil. It blooms in the spring, producing clusters of small, showy flowers.

Maieta rubra is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for.
