Melastoma superbum

Melastoma superbum, commonly known as the velvetleaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and typically have five petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
