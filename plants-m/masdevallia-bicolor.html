Masdevallia bicolor

Masdevallia bicolor, commonly known as the two-colored masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking dichromatic petals, with one side typically displaying a deep violet hue and the other a pale yellow or white. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Masdevallia bicolor is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
