Melodorum fulgens

Melodorum fulgens, commonly known as the golden bellvista, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a visually appealing display. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy, contributing to its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Melodorum fulgens is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
