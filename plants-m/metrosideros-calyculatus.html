Metrosideros calyculatus

Metrosideros calyculatus, commonly known as the Christmas Bush, is a small evergreen shrub native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom during the summer months. The plant produces small, oval-shaped fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions and full sun.

The Christmas Bush is often used in ornamental gardening due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
