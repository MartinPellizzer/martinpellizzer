Mettenia lepidota

Mettenia lepidota, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a tropical plant native to regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Mettenia lepidota is often found in shaded, moist environments such as forests or along stream banks.
