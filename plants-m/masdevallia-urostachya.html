Masdevallia urostachya

Masdevallia urostachya, commonly known as the silver-tipped masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and clustered, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces striking, violet-blue flowers with long, slender sepals and petals. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light.

Its unique appearance and delicate beauty make it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
