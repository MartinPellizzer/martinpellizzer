Masdevallia milagroi

Masdevallia milagroi, commonly known as the "Miraculous Masdevallia," is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant, colorful blooms that range from red to orange, often with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences that enhance its ornamental appeal. It thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, making it a challenging but rewarding species for orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited natural habitat and habitat destruction, it is considered endangered and is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses.
