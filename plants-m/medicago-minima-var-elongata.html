Medicago minima var. elongata

Medicago minima var. elongata, commonly known as the slender medick, is a low-growing perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers in early summer. The plant is adapted to a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions. It is often found in grasslands, meadows, and along roadsides.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it valuable for soil enrichment and as a forage plant.
