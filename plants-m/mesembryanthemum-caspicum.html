Mesembryanthemum caspicum

Mesembryanthemum caspicum, commonly known as the Caspian ice plant, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers. Mesembryanthemum caspicum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental value.
