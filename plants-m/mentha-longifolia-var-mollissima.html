Mentha longifolia var. mollissima

Mentha longifolia var. mollissima, commonly known as woolly mint, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine for its aromatic and medicinal properties.
