Mammillaria haageana var. validior

Mammillaria haageana var. validior, commonly known as the "Giant Hedgehog Cactus," is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly hairs that help protect it from intense sunlight and water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its stems are ribbed and covered with small, tubercles that give it a distinctive, spiky appearance.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in a well-draining soil mix.
