Micrococca holstii

Micrococca holstii, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The plant has a rounded, dense canopy with dark green, leathery leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and fragrant blooms.

Micrococca holstii prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for warm, arid climates.
