Miconia cernuiflora

Miconia cernuiflora, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy capsule that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia cernuiflora is known for its rapid growth and is considered an invasive species in some regions.
