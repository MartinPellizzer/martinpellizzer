Molpadia

Molpadia, commonly known as the glassy fan palm, is a unique plant native to the southern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and a dense crown of long, narrow leaves that resemble a fan. The plant has a distinctive appearance with its smooth, glassy texture and ability to grow in a variety of soil types. Molpadia is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can thrive in both full sun and partial shade.

Its slow growth rate makes it a desirable choice for smaller garden spaces.
