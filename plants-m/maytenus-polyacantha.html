Maytenus polyacantha

Maytenus polyacantha, commonly known as the many-stemmed mayten, is a shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the family Celastraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite, simple leaves. The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitats.
