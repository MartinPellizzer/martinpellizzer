Maxillaria prunina

Maxillaria prunina, commonly known as the Purple-veined Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, violet or purple flowers with yellow markings. The plant typically blooms during the summer months and prefers bright, indirect light and moderate humidity. Its leaves are elliptical and often have a waxy texture, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Maxillaria prunina is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
