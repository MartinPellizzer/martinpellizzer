Medicago × varia f. rivularis

Medicago × varia f. rivularis, commonly known as the river medick, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in moist, calcareous soils along riverbanks and wet meadows. It has a creeping growth habit with stolons that help it spread and stabilize soil.

As a member of the Fabaceae family, it plays a role in nitrogen fixation, benefiting the surrounding ecosystem.
