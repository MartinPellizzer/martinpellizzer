Macropharynx spectabilis

Macropharynx spectabilis, commonly known as the showy pharynx plant, is a unique species native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated flowers that resemble a pharynx, giving it its scientific name. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in tropical or subtropical forests. Its leaves are broad and glossy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Botanically, it is notable for its complex inflorescence and specialized pollination mechanisms.
