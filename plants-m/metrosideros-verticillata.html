Metrosideros verticillata

Metrosideros verticillata, commonly known as the Christmas Bush, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal and forested areas. The plant features bright red or orange flowers that bloom in dense clusters, often around Christmas time, hence its common name. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, and it produces small, round fruits that contain seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is also an important component of New Zealand's native flora.
