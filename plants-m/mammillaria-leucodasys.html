Mammillaria leucodasys

Mammillaria leucodasys, commonly known as the white-flowered hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly hairs that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
