Maranta linearis

Maranta linearis, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight.

Maranta linearis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of care.
