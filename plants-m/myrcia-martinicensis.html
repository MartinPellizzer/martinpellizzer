Myrcia martinicensis

Myrcia martinicensis, commonly known as the Martinique myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Caribbean, particularly found in the Lesser Antilles, including Martinique and Guadeloupe. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, round berries that are typically red when ripe.

Myrcia martinicensis is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
