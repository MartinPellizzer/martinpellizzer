Melolobium collinum var. brevifolium

Melolobium collinum var. brevifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaved melolobium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the western United States.

It features small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring, typically from March to May. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
