Maxillaria pergracilis

Maxillaria pergracilis, commonly known as the slender maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its slender, upright pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale pink, often with a subtle fragrance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria pergracilis is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
