Mesembryanthemum cupreum

Mesembryanthemum cupreum, commonly known as the copper ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It blooms during the cooler months, typically in spring or early summer.

Mesembryanthemum cupreum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
