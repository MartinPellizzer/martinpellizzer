Microstylis megalantha

Microstylis megalantha, commonly known as the giant stylis, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that bear tiny, inconspicuous flowers. Its botanical features make it suitable for ornamental use in gardens and landscapes.

It thrives in well-drained soils and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
