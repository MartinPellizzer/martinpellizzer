Mammillaria elegans var. schmollii

Mammillaria elegans var. schmollii, commonly known as the Schmollii Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features dense, white to pale green tubercles covered in fine, white radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that emerge from the axils of the areoles. Native to Mexico, it thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
