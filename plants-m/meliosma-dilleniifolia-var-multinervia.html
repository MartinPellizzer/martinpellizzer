Meliosma dilleniifolia var. multinervia

Meliosma dilleniifolia var. multinervia, commonly known as the multivenation meliosma, is a tropical plant characterized by its finely veined, ovate leaves.

The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, with a prominent midrib and numerous secondary veins that give it its distinguishing name. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually green or white, followed by small, round fruits.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive foliage.
