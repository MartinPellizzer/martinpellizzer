Melpomene anfractuosa

Melpomene anfractuosa, commonly known as the zigzag passionflower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, zigzag-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers that resemble those of the passionfruit. The plant produces bright red or orange flowers with five petals and a distinctive crown of filaments. Its fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a deep red color.

Melpomene anfractuosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
