Mammillaria rekoi var. pseudorekoi

Mammillaria rekoi var. pseudorekoi, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to bluish stem with prominent white tubercles and spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
