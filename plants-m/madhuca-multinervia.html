Madhuca multinervia

Madhuca multinervia, commonly known as the small-leaf tallow tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are globose, woody capsules that release seeds when mature.
