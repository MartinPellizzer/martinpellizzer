Maxillaria nagelii

Maxillaria nagelii, commonly known as the Nagel's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid genera. Its flowers are usually yellow with purple and green markings, and they bloom in late winter to early spring.

Maxillaria nagelii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
