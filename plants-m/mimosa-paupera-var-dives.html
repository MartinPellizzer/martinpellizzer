Mimosa paupera var. dives

Mimosa paupera var. dives, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces fluffy, white seed pods that split open when mature, dispersing seeds by wind. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its rapid growth and ornamental appeal make it a versatile plant for both ornamental and ecological purposes.
