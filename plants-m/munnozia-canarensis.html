Munnozia canarensis

Munnozia canarensis, commonly known as the Canary Island mallow, is a flowering plant native to the Canary Islands.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky, coastal environments. The plant features showy pink to purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions.

Munnozia canarensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
