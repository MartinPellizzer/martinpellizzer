Macrura argentea

Macrura argentea, commonly known as silverleaf macrura, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for xeriscaping.
