Mammillaria senilis

Mammillaria senilis, commonly known as the old man cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white, woolly hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
