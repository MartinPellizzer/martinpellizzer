Mimosa lanuginosa

Mimosa lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, woolly foliage and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in spring. It is often found in dry, sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

Mimosa lanuginosa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique texture and appearance.
