Malcolmia flexuosa subsp. naxensis

Malcolmia flexuosa subsp.

naxensis, commonly known as the Cyprus carnation, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region. It is a low-growing herb with hairy, branched stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves.

The plant produces showy, pink to purple flowers in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

Its ornamental value and adaptability have made it a popular choice in horticulture.
