Matucana haynii subsp. haynii

Matucana haynii subsp.

haynii, commonly known as the yellow torch cactus, is a species of cactus native to southern Brazil. It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can reach up to 3 meters in height.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It has a dense covering of spines, which provide protection from herbivores.

This plant prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for arid gardens.
