Moronobea grandiflora

Moronobea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered moronobea, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces showy, pale yellow to orange flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough texture.

Moronobea grandiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking floral display.
