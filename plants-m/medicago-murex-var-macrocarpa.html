Medicago murex var. macrocarpa

Medicago murex var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-seeded goat's rue, is a variant of the goat's rue plant native to parts of Europe and western Asia.

It is a herbaceous annual or biennial plant characterized by its erect stems and pinnately compound leaves. The flowers are typically blue or violet, arranged in racemes, and the pods are inflated, containing several seeds. It thrives in disturbed soils and is often found in fields, roadsides, and wastelands.

The plant is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in traditional herbal remedies.
