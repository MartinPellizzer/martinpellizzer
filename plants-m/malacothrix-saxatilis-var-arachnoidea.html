Malacothrix saxatilis var. arachnoidea

Malacothrix saxatilis var. arachnoidea, commonly known as the spider-hair rock-moss, is a unique plant species characterized by its distinctive morphology.

It is a small, mat-forming plant that typically grows on rocky substrates in arid or semi-arid environments. The leaves are narrow and often covered with fine, hair-like structures that give it its common name. This variety is particularly adapted to withstand harsh conditions, including high temperatures and low moisture.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for studies on plant adaptation and survival in extreme habitats.
