Masdevallia olmosii

Masdevallia olmosii, commonly known as the Olmos's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, elongated flowers that often display vibrant hues of red, purple, and yellow. The plant typically produces a single inflorescence with several flowers, each featuring intricate, spurred petals and a distinctive lip. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable addition to orchid collections.
