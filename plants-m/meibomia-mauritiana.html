Meibomia mauritiana

Meibomia mauritiana, commonly known as the Mauritius starling or the starling tree, is a flowering plant native to the Mascarene Islands.

It is a small evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 5 meters. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often with a distinctive star-like appearance. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and usually elliptical in shape.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
