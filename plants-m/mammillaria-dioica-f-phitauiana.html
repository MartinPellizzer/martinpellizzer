Mammillaria dioica f. phitauiana

Mammillaria dioica f. phitauiana, commonly known as the Phitau's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to South Africa.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue tubercles, which are modified leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a delicate contrast to its rugged appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its unique coloration and ease of care, making it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
