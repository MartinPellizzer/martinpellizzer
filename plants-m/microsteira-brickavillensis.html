Microsteira brickavillensis

Microsteira brickavillensis, commonly known as the Brickaville microsteira, is a rare flowering plant native to the semi-arid regions of southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand periods of drought. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to conserve moisture.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation areas.
