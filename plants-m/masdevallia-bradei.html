Masdevallia bradei

Masdevallia bradei, commonly known as the Brade's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces delicate, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows at high altitudes, thriving in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Masdevallia bradei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique floral display and relatively easy care requirements.
