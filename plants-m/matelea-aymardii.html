Matelea aymardii

Matelea aymardii, commonly known as the Yuma nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that contain seeds, which are dispersed by animals. Matelea aymardii thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often growing in rocky soils and along riverbanks.
