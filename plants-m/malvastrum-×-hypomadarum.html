Malvastrum × hypomadarum

Malvastrum × hypomadarum, commonly known as the hybrid mallows, is a flowering plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Malvastrum coromandelianum and Malvastrum affine. The plant is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to purple, often with a distinctive white or yellow center. It typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters, and thrives in well-drained soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and landscapes for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various climates.
