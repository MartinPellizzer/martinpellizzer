Mimusops spiculosa

Mimusops spiculosa, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal forests and dry woodlands.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is also used in traditional medicine.
