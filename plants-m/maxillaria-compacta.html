Maxillaria compacta

Maxillaria compacta, commonly known as the compact orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit, featuring a short, stocky rhizome. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow lip. Its leaves are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Maxillaria compacta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
