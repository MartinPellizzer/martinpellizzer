Mitrephora reflexa

Mitrephora reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew and mango. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. It produces small, fragrant flowers followed by fleshy, red or orange berries.
