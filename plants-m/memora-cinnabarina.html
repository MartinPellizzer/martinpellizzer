Memora cinnabarina

Memora cinnabarina, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the mountainous regions of East Asia.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep red to orange, often with a distinctive red stripe down the middle. The plant produces a cluster of small, white, star-shaped flowers at the base of its leaves, which bloom in early spring. Its leaves are long, narrow, and slightly twisted, giving it a unique appearance.

Memora cinnabarina is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also valued in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
