Maprounea guianensis f. obtusata

Maprounea guianensis f. obtusata, commonly known as the Guianan maprouna, is a flowering plant species belonging to the family Euphorbiaceae.

It is characterized by its woody structure and is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly in Brazil, Colombia, and Venezuela. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered in cyathia, a unique structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
