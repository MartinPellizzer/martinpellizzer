Massonia bakeriana

Massonia bakeriana, commonly known as the Baker's massonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent perennial that thrives in arid and semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, cylindrical stems and produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange. It is well adapted to withstand drought conditions due to its thick, water-storing leaves.

The species is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and resilience.
