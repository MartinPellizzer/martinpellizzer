Myristica intermedia

Myristica intermedia, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. Its fruit is a capsule that contains aromatic seeds used in various culinary and medicinal applications.

The tree is also valued for its essential oils and has historical significance in trade and traditional medicine.
