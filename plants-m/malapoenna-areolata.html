Malapoenna areolata

Malapoenna areolata, commonly known as the spotted orchid, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features a slender, upright stem that can grow up to 60 centimeters in height. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and purple, often with a distinctive spotted pattern on the petals. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Malapoenna areolata thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
