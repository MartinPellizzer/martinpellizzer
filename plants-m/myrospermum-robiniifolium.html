Myrospermum robiniifolium

Myrospermum robiniifolium, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody, spherical fruits. It is well adapted to arid environments and has a deep root system for water storage.

The wood of this plant is extremely dense and durable, making it valuable for traditional uses.
