Melandrium keiskei var. minus

Melandrium keiskei var. minus, commonly known as the lesser yellow catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, yellowish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in disturbed soils and open habitats, such as fields and roadsides. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine pubescence, while its flowers bloom in clusters at the ends of the stems.
