Melastoma rotundifolium

Melastoma rotundifolium, commonly known as the round-leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine pubescence. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that are typically pink or red, and they bloom in clusters.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
