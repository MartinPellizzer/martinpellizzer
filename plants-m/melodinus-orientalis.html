Melodinus orientalis

Melodinus orientalis, commonly known as the oriental melodinus, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of pink and white. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate, with a smooth texture.

Melodinus orientalis is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its ecological importance in supporting local pollinators.
