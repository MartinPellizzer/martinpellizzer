Myristica macrothyrsa

Myristica macrothyrsa, commonly known as the large-thyrsed nutmeg, is a member of the Myristicaceae family, native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its smooth, dark brown bark and pinnate leaves. The plant produces clusters of yellowish-green flowers followed by woody capsules containing aromatic seeds.

Its fruits are typically harvested when they turn a deep red, and the seeds are used for their essential oils and medicinal properties.
