Mormodes paraensis

Mormodes paraensis, commonly known as the Paraense orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to the humid tropical environment.

The orchid thrives in shaded, moist conditions and is often found growing on trees or rocks in the understory of the rainforest.
