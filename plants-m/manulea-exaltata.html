Manulea exaltata

Manulea exaltata, commonly known as the tall manulea, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are adapted to reduce water loss in its dry environment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Manulea exaltata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
