Mohlana nemoralis

Mohlana nemoralis, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a species of orchid native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, often growing in rocky or wooded areas. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding environment.

Mohlana nemoralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its unique floral display.
