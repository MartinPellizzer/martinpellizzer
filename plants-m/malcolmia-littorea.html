Malcolmia littorea

Malcolmia littorea, commonly known as coastal malva or sea malva, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing herb with hairy, glandular leaves that are typically lobed or heart-shaped. The plant produces showy, five-petaled flowers in shades of pink to white, which bloom in summer. It thrives in coastal areas and sandy soils, often growing in disturbed or saline environments.

Malcolmia littorea is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to stabilize coastal dunes.
