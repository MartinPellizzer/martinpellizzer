Mimosa blanchetii

Mimosa blanchetii, commonly known as the white mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is often found in moist, open habitats such as grasslands and forest edges. It has a shallow root system and is adapted to thrive in well-drained soils.

Mimosa blanchetii is also known for its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas.
