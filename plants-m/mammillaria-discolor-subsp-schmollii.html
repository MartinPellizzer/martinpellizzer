Mammillaria discolor subsp. schmollii

Mammillaria discolor subsp.

schmollii, commonly known as the Schmollii mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles.

The plant produces small, pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant in succulent collections.
