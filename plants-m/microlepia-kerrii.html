Microlepia kerrii

Microlepia kerrii, commonly known as the Kerri's sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Davalliaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The plant features a creeping rhizome with lance-shaped, serrated fronds that are typically green and glossy. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.

Microlepia kerrii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
