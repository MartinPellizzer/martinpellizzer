Mimosa micheliana

Mimosa micheliana, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves that are covered in fine, soft hairs, giving it a velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Mimosa micheliana is also known for its rapid growth and ability to tolerate various environmental conditions.
