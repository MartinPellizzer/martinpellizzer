Monadenium echinulatum f. glabrescens

Monadenium echinulatum f. glabrescens, commonly known as the smooth-flowered monadenium, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves and colorful flowers. This succulent is adapted to arid environments and requires well-drained soil and minimal water to thrive.
