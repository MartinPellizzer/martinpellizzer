Mundulea ambatoana

Mundulea ambatoana, commonly known as the Ambatoana acacia, is a shrub native to the dry forests of Madagascar.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions. It plays an important role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat and food for various animals.

Its wood is used for fuel and construction in the region.
