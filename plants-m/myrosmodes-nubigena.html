Myrosmodes nubigena

Myrosmodes nubigena, commonly known as the cloud mist orchid, is a rare and elusive flowering plant native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with high humidity and partial shade, often growing on rocky outcrops or tree trunks.
