Melastoma succosum

Melastoma succosum, commonly known as the succulent melastoma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including regions of Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
