Matthiola daghestanica

Matthiola daghestanica, commonly known as the Daghestan stock, is a flowering plant native to the Caucasus region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale purple, arranged in clusters. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has a long blooming period.
