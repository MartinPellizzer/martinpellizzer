Masdevallia hubeinii

Masdevallia hubeinii, commonly known as the Hubei masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to China.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often colorful flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically has dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous small, showy flowers. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high light exposure but is sensitive to direct sunlight.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique floral display.
