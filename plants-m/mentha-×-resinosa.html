Mentha × resinosa

Mentha × resinosa, commonly known as black mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black leaves and strong, menthol-like fragrance. The plant typically grows as a perennial herb, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland areas or along stream banks.

Its aromatic properties make it useful in both culinary and medicinal applications.
