Magnolia exoniensis

Magnolia exoniensis, commonly known as Exeter magnolia, is a rare and ancient species native to the UK.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. It typically grows in woodland areas and is known for its ornamental value.

Due to its rarity, it is protected and often found in conservation sites.
