Musa tikap

Musa tikap, commonly known as the banana plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces a clustered inflorescence with both male and female flowers, followed by the development of fruiting bunches. Musa tikap is cultivated for its edible fruit, which is rich in potassium and other nutrients.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
