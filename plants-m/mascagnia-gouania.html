Mascagnia gouania

Mascagnia gouania, commonly known as the African nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually yellow or greenish-yellow in color. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a rough texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
