Medicusia

Medicusia, commonly known as the healing herb, is a perennial plant native to temperate regions.

It features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and produces small, clustered white flowers in the summer. The plant's roots are the primary source of its medicinal compounds, which have been used for centuries in traditional medicine. Medicusia thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a versatile garden plant.

Its botanical name reflects its historical significance in healing practices.
