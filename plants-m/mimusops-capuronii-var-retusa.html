Mimusops capuronii var. retusa

Mimusops capuronii var. retusa, commonly known as the Madagascar ebony, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dark, dense wood, which is highly valued for its strength and durability. This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has traditional uses in local communities.
