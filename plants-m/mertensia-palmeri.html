Mertensia palmeri

Mertensia palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's bluebelle, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils. The plant produces clusters of striking blue-violet flowers during the spring season. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often covered in a fine, white pubescence.

Mertensia palmeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
