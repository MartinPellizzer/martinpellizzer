Melinospermum pumilum

Melinospermum pumilum, commonly known as the dwarf melilot, is a low-growing annual plant native to the western United States.

It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, branching stems and is often found in dry, sandy soils. Its leaves are alternate and pinnately divided, giving it a delicate appearance.

Melinospermum pumilum is adapted to arid environments and is often used in restoration projects due to its hardy nature.
