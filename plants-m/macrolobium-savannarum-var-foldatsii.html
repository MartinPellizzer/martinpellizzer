Macrolobium savannarum var. foldatsii

Macrolobium savannarum var. foldatsii, commonly known as the Foldats' macrolobium, is a shrub native to the savannas of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woody structure and produces clusters of small, fragrant yellow flowers. The leaves are typically elliptical and have a leathery texture, adapted to withstand the dry conditions of its habitat. This plant is often found in open grasslands with scattered trees, thriving in well-drained soils.

Its botanical features make it an important component of the local flora and a subject of interest in ecological studies.
