Mattiastrum shepardii

Mattiastrum shepardii, commonly known as Shepard's matweed, is a low-growing annual plant native to the southwestern United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dense, mat-like growth habit. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Mattiastrum shepardii is typically found in disturbed soils and arid environments.
