Mimosa somnambulans

Mimosa somnambulans, commonly known as the sleepy bush, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers. The plant is known for its sensitive response to touch, with its leaves folding shut when disturbed. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens.

Its unique behavior and aesthetic appeal make it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
