Mirbelia grandiflora

Mirbelia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered mirbelia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and heathlands.

Mirbelia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
