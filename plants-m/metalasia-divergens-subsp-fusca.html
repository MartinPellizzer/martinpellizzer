Metalasia divergens subsp. fusca

Metalasia divergens subsp.

fusca, commonly known as the purple-flowered metalasia, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa. It belongs to the family Amaryllidaceae and is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers.
