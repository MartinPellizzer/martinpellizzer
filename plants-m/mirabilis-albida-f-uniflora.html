Mirabilis albida f. uniflora

Mirabilis albida f. uniflora, commonly known as the single-flower four o'clock, is a strikingly beautiful plant native to South America.

It is a variant of the common four o'clock plant, characterized by its single, large, showy flower that opens in the late afternoon. The plant produces vibrant pink to red blossoms, which are often accompanied by waxy, dark green leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and unique flowering habit.
