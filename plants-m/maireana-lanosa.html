Maireana lanosa

Maireana lanosa, commonly known as the silky maireana, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its woolly, silver-gray foliage. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in arid and semi-arid regions, thriving in well-drained soils.

Maireana lanosa is adapted to drought conditions and is a hardy, low-maintenance plant suitable for landscaping in dry climates.
