Mimosa ostenii

Mimosa ostenii, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is a member of the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and it has the ability to fix nitrogen in the soil through symbiotic bacteria in its roots. Mimosa ostenii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and ease of care.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it adaptable to various garden environments.
