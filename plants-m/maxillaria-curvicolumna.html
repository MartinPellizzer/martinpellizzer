Maxillaria curvicolumna

Maxillaria curvicolumna, commonly known as the slender column orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a short, horizontal rhizome and erect, hairy pseudobulbs. The plant produces racemes of fragrant, violet-colored flowers with a distinctive, narrow column. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in two rows along the stem.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, organic soil for optimal growth.
