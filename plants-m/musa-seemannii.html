Musa seemannii

Musa seemannii, commonly known as the dwarf banana plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Musaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its compact size compared to other banana species. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are typically used in local cuisines. Its leaves are large, broad, and have a smooth texture, contributing to its ornamental value.

Musa seemannii is also cultivated for its ornamental appeal and as a parent species in banana hybrid breeding programs.
