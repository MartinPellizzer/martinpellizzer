Meliosma thomsonii

Meliosma thomsonii, commonly known as the Thomson's meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-white in color. Its growth habit is compact, making it suitable for rock gardens or as a container plant.

Meliosma thomsonii is valued for its ornamental foliage and ability to thrive in shady, moist environments.
