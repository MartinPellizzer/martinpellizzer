Muhlenbergia pulcherrima

Muhlenbergia pulcherrima, commonly known as the pink muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that range from green to reddish hues. The plant produces showy, plume-like inflorescences that are often pink or purple, adding ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Muhlenbergia pulcherrima is drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping due to its low maintenance requirements.
