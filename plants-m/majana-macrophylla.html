Majana macrophylla

Majana macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf majana, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a unique and exotic plant.
