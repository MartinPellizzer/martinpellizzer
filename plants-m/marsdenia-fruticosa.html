Marsdenia fruticosa

Marsdenia fruticosa, commonly known as the red-flowered Marsdenia, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Asclepiadaceae family and is characterized by its woody stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are follicles that release small, winged seeds.

Marsdenia fruticosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
