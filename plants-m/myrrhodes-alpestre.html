Myrrhodes alpestre

Myrrhodes alpestre, commonly known as the alpine myrrh.

It is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color.

Myrrhodes alpestre is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats at high elevations.
