Memecylon ulopterum var. ulopterum

Memecylon ulopterum var. ulopterum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved memecylon, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical to ovate in shape, with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its flowers are small, white or pale green, and clustered in dense inflorescences, while the fruit is a small, three-lobed capsule.

This plant is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.
