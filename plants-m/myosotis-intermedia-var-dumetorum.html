Myosotis intermedia var. dumetorum

Myosotis intermedia var. dumetorum, commonly known as the Alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to alpine regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or mountainous habitats, preferring well-drained soils. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and is often found in high-altitude meadows.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
