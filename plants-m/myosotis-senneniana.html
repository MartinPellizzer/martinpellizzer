Myosotis senneniana

Myosotis senneniana, commonly known as the New Zealand forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks. It has a fibrous root system and produces small, capsule-like fruits that disperse its seeds.

Myosotis senneniana is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in its native habitat.
