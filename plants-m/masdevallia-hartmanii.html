Masdevallia hartmanii

Masdevallia hartmanii, commonly known as the Hartman's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, intricate flower structure with vibrant, often contrasting colors. The plant typically produces a single, showy bloom that can range from yellow to orange, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity, making it a challenging but rewarding species for orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
