Mammillaria aylostera

Mammillaria aylostera, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a thick, ribbed stem that can develop a crown of flowers. The plant features prominent areoles, which are specialized structures from which spines, flowers, and branches emerge. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, and it produces small, round fruits.

Mammillaria aylostera is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.
