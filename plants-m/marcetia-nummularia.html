Marcetia nummularia

Marcetia nummularia, commonly known as the pennywort, is a small, evergreen perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features rosettes of fleshy, round, dark green leaves that are often marked with silver or white veins. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that appear in spring. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in rocky or streamside habitats.

Marcetia nummularia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in horticultural settings for its compact growth and attractive foliage.
