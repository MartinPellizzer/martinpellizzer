Mikania pohlii

Mikania pohlii, commonly known as the Mexican morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central America and parts of South America.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its rapid growth and climbing habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a hairy texture.

Mikania pohlii is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and flowering display.
