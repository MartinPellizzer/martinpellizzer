Mammillaria echinata

Mammillaria echinata, commonly known as the spiny mammilla, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
