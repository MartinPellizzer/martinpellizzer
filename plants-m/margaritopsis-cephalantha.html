Margaritopsis cephalantha

Margaritopsis cephalantha, commonly known as the pearl flower, is a striking member of the Acanthaceae family.

Margaritopsis cephalantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
