Martynia jussieui

Martynia jussieui, commonly known as the purple false nightshade, is a flowering plant belonging to the Solanaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking violet to purple flowers and hairy, ovate leaves. It typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 60 centimeters.
