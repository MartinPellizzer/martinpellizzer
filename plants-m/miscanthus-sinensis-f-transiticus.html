Miscanthus sinensis f. transiticus

Miscanthus sinensis f. transiticus, commonly known as the silver feather reed grass, is a decorative grass species prized for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.

It features slender, arching blades that are a striking silver-green color, giving it a soft, feathery appearance. The plant is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and its tolerance to both drought and partial shade. It produces delicate, plume-like flower spikes in the late summer, adding visual interest to the garden.

This ornamental grass is often used in borders, as a backdrop, or in naturalized settings due to its graceful form and low maintenance requirements.
