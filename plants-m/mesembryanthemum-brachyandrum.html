Mesembryanthemum brachyandrum

Mesembryanthemum brachyandrum, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a valuable addition to both ornamental and ecological landscapes.
