Melochia ovata

Melochia ovata, commonly known as the roundleaf melochia, is a tropical shrub native to Africa and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are yellow with five petals and are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Melochia ovata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported anti-inflammatory and healing properties.
