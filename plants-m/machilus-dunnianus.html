Machilus dunnianus

Machilus dunnianus, commonly known as the Chinese laurel, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces berries that are typically orange-red in color and contain several seeds. It is often found in subtropical regions and is valued for its ornamental qualities and aromatic properties.

Machilus dunnianus is also used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
