Malcolmia broussonetii var. tricolor

Malcolmia broussonetii var. tricolor, commonly known as the Three-color Malcomia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with distinctive tri-colored flowers that range from white to pink and purple. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, clustered blooms that attract pollinators. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant, variable flower colors.
