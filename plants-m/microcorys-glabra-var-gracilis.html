Microcorys glabra var. gracilis

Microcorys glabra var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender smooth microcorys, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters. It is typically found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

Due to its delicate structure, it is often overlooked but plays a role in local ecosystems.
