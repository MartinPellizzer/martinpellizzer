Manihot brevipedicellata

Manihot brevipedicellata, commonly known as the short-stemmed arrowleaf manioc, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Paraguay. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers arranged in clusters. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.

The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
