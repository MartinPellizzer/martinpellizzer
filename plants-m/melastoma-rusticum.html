Melastoma rusticum

Melastoma rusticum, commonly known as the rough melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with rough, hairy surfaces, and its flowers are usually pink or red, arranged in clusters. Melastoma rusticum produces small, elliptical fruits that contain several seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
