Mammillaria polythele var. affinis

Mammillaria polythele var. affinis, commonly known as the Affinis Mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
