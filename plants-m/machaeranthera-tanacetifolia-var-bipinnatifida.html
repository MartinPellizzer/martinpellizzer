Machaeranthera tanacetifolia var. bipinnatifida

Machaeranthera tanacetifolia var. bipinnatifida, commonly known as the bipinnatifid goldenweed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its finely divided, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish to brownish composite flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes.
