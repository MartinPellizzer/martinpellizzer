Mentha × aquaticoarvensis f. subglabra

Mentha × aquaticoarvensis f. subglabra, commonly known as the smooth water mint, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, aromatic leaves that are often hairless or nearly so, giving it the subspecies name *subglabra*. This variety typically grows in moist or aquatic environments, such as ponds and stream edges. Its flowers are small and clustered in whorls, producing a distinctive minty fragrance.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal remedies.
