Melanophylla dianeae

Melanophylla dianeae, commonly known as the Diane's darkleaf plant, is a rare and unique species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, almost black foliage, which gives it its scientific name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Melanophylla dianeae thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires high levels of atmospheric moisture to survive.
