Monstera cenepensis

Monstera cenepensis, commonly known as the giant monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant has a strong, adventitious root system that allows it to climb and anchor itself to trees or other structures. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a smooth texture and a slightly glossy appearance.

Monstera cenepensis thrives in humid, warm environments with indirect sunlight and well-draining soil.
