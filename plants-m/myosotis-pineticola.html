Myosotis pineticola

Myosotis pineticola, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine habitats, thriving in well-drained soil and full sun. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often covered in a fine layer of white hairs.

Myosotis pineticola is a low-growing perennial that produces numerous flowers, making it a popular choice for alpine gardens.
