Macrolobium trunciflorum

Macrolobium trunciflorum, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually borne on short peduncles and are composed of five petals.

The plant is well adapted to humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental species.
