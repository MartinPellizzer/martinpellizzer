Melodorum polyanthoides

Melodorum polyanthoides, commonly known as the fragrant star jasmine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white, star-shaped flowers that emit a sweet fragrance, especially at night. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, with opposite, ovate leaves that have a glossy surface. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.

Melodorum polyanthoides is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic uses.
