Myosotis micrantha subsp. minutiflora

Myosotis micrantha subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf forget-me-not, is a small flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its delicate blue or violet flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The subspecies is distinguished by its smaller flowers and shorter stature compared to other members of the Myosotis genus. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and streamside areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
