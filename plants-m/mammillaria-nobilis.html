Mammillaria nobilis

Mammillaria nobilis, commonly known as the noble globular cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and dense covering of small, white spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 10 centimeters and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and attractive appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
