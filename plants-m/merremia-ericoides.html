Merremia ericoides

Merremia ericoides, commonly known as the silky nightshade, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically white or purple. The plant has a woody stem and is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed areas or along fences.

The fruits are small berries that turn red when mature, and the plant is known for its ornamental value in gardens.
