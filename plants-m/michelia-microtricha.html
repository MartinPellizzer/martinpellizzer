Michelia microtricha

Michelia microtricha, commonly known as the Chinese michelia, is a flowering plant native to southern China.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows to a height of 2 to 4 meters. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical in shape, with a glossy green upper surface.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
