Medicago truncatula var. breviaculeata

Medicago truncatula var. breviaculeata, commonly known as the short-leaf medick, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its small, trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in well-drained soils and dry conditions. It is often found in grasslands, along roadsides, and in disturbed areas.

This species is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it useful in ecological restoration and sustainable agriculture.
