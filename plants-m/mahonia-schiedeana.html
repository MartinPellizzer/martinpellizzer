Mahonia schiedeana

Mahonia schiedeana, commonly known as the Mexican barberry, is a deciduous shrub native to Mexico.

It features compound leaves that are typically dark green and have a leathery texture. The plant produces bright yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Mahonia schiedeana is also valued for its ability to tolerate drought and poor soil conditions.
