Masdevallia dalstroemii

Masdevallia dalstroemii, commonly known as the Dalstroem's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers that feature intricate patterns and a unique structure typical of the Masdevallia genus. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that help it conserve moisture in its natural habitat.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its striking floral display.
