Mimusops marginata

Mimusops marginata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant is characterized by its dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth surface.

The fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
