Mikania lepidophora

Mikania lepidophora, commonly known as the velvetleaf mikania, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types. It is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its fibrous roots and sprawling growth habit make it a common weed in agricultural settings.
