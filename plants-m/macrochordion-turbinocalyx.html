Macrochordion turbinocalyx

Macrochordion turbinocalyx, commonly known as the turbinated horned orchid, is a species of orchid native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, turbinated (spiral-shaped) flowers that are often brightly colored. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as tropical forests. Its leaves are broad and fleshy, adapted to conserve water in its native habitat.

The orchid's unique floral structure and growth habits make it a subject of interest in botanical studies.
