Magnolia microtricha

Magnolia microtricha, commonly known as the dwarf magnolia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and lighter in color underneath. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a subtle scent when rubbed.

Magnolia microtricha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty and fragrance.
