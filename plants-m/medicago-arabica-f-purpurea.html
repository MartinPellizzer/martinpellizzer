Medicago arabica f. purpurea

Medicago arabica f. purpurea, commonly known as purple medic, is a variant of the common medick plant.

It is characterized by its distinctive purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often found in disturbed areas, along roadsides, and in dry grasslands.

The purple coloration of its flowers is due to the presence of anthocyanin pigments.
