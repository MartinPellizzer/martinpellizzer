Mimetes spectabilis

Mimetes spectabilis, commonly known as the showy mimetes, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking, daisy-like inflorescence. The plant produces yellow flowers with a central disk, which are arranged in a flat, showy head. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Mimetes spectabilis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms.
