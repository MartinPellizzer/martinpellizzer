Mandevilla torosa var. coulteri

Mandevilla torosa var. coulteri, commonly known as Coulter's mandevilla, is a vigorous, fast-growing vine native to Mexico.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, which bloom throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are dark green and provide a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

This variety is popular in gardens and as a container plant due to its ornamental value and adaptability.
