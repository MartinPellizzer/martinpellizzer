Maranta binotii

Maranta binotii, commonly known as the two-spotted arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often adorned with distinctive red and green markings. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically followed by bright red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often grown as a decorative houseplant.

Maranta binotii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify the air.
