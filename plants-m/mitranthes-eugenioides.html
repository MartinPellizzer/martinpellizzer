Mitranthes eugenioides

Mitranthes eugenioides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in open grasslands and rocky areas. The plant features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are usually pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are upright and often branched.

Mitranthes eugenioides is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
