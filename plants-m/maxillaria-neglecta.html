Maxillaria neglecta

Maxillaria neglecta, commonly known as the Neglected Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest undergrowth. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that produce a single leaf per node. Its flowers are usually pale green to white, with intricate patterns and a pleasant fragrance.

Maxillaria neglecta is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
