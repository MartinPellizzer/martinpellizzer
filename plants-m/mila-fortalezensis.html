Mila fortalezensis

Mila fortalezensis, commonly known as the Brazilian milkweed, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. Mila fortalezensis is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
