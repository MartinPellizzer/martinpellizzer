Monteverdia urbanii

Monteverdia urbanii, commonly known as the urban ivy, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually white or pale yellow. It thrives in humid environments and is often found growing on walls or trees.

Due to its adaptability, it is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain areas.
