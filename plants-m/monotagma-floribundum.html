Monotagma floribundum

Monotagma floribundum, commonly known as the Cape rose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of showy, pink or red flowers. The plant features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.

The botanical name reflects its flowering nature and its origin in the flora of the Cape region.
