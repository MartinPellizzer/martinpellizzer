Malapoenna macrantha

Malapoenna macrantha, commonly known as the Natal orchid, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces clusters of pale yellow to orange flowers.

Malapoenna macrantha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
