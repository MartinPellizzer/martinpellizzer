Musa acuminata var. breviformis

Musa acuminata var. breviformis, commonly known as the dwarf banana plant, is a variety of the banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact size and shorter, broader leaves compared to other banana species. This variety produces small, edible bananas that are often used in local cuisines. The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to thrive in tropical climates.

It is also used in horticultural research due to its genetic significance in banana breeding programs.
