Mucuna cyclocarpa

Mucuna cyclocarpa, commonly known as the cyclocarpa velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It produces distinctive, spherical pods that are covered in a dense, velvety layer of hairs. The plant is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fallow fields.

The seeds of Mucuna cyclocarpa contain compounds that have been studied for their potential medicinal properties.
