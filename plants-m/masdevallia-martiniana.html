Masdevallia martiniana

Masdevallia martiniana, commonly known as the Martin's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, intricate floral structure with vibrant colors and intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with slender stems and delicate, fleshy leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and rarity make it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.
