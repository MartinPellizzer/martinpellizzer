Mammillaria seegeri var. gracilispina

Mammillaria seegeri var. gracilispina, commonly known as the slender-spined mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a compact, globular stem with a greenish-gray color and short, cylindrical spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors and rock gardens.
