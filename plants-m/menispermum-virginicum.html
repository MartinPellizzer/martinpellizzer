Menispermum virginicum

Menispermum virginicum, commonly known as Indian Cucumber Root, is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Menispermaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces a fleshy, cucumber-like fruit that contains several seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.

Its botanical features make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
