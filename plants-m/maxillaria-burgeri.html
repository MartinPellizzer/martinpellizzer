Maxillaria burgeri

Maxillaria burgeri, commonly known as the Burger's Maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically yellow or orange with dark markings, blooming during the dry season. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Maxillaria burgeri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
