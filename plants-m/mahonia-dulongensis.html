Mahonia dulongensis

Mahonia dulongensis, commonly known as the Dulong's Mahonia, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and spiny stems. The plant produces yellow flowers in early spring, followed by blue-black berries that are attractive to birds. Mahonia dulongensis typically grows in woodland areas and is well-suited to moist, well-drained soils.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and hardy nature.
