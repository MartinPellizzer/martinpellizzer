Mahonia muelleri

Mahonia muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's mahonia, is a shrub native to Australia.

It features compound leaves with a distinctive blue-green hue and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and is often used in ornamental gardening. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

Mahonia muelleri is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
