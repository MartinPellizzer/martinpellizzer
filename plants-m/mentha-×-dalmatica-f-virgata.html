Mentha × dalmatica f. virgata

Mentha × dalmatica f. virgata, commonly known as the slender mint, is a hybrid plant in the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright stems and aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pale blue to violet in color. It is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a source of aromatic compounds.
