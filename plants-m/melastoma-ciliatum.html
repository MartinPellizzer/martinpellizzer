Melastoma ciliatum

Melastoma ciliatum, commonly known as the silky melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with ciliate margins, giving it its common name. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with a pink or white color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, smooth seeds.
