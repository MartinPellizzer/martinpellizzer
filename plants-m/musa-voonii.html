Musa voonii

Musa voonii, commonly known as the African plantain, is a species of banana plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and produces clusters of fruit that are typically used for culinary purposes. The plant thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth. Its flowers are usually hidden within the fruit clusters, and the plant is cultivated for both its edible fruit and ornamental value.

Musa voonii is an important crop in many African countries, contributing to local food security and economies.
