Maxillaria cachacoensis

Maxillaria cachacoensis, commonly known as the Cachacoense orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
