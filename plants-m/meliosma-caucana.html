Meliosma caucana

Meliosma caucana, commonly known as the Caucasian meliosma, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of the Caucasus.

It belongs to the family Meliosmaceae and is characterized by its slender, woody stems and opposite, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its limited distribution and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
