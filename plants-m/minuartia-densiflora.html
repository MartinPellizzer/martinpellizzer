Minuartia densiflora

Minuartia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered rock cress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It is characterized by its mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to cold climates with short growing seasons. Its leaves are usually scale-like or very narrow, reducing water loss in harsh environments.

Minuartia densiflora plays a role in stabilizing soil in its native habitats and is often found in tundra and mountainous areas.
