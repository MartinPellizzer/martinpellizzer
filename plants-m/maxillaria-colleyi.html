Maxillaria colleyi

Maxillaria colleyi, commonly known as the Colley's Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers. The flowers typically feature a yellow lip with intricate patterns and a range of colors including white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This species is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
