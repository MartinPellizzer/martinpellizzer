Miconia planifilamentosa

Miconia planifilamentosa, commonly known as the flat filament miconia, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, showy bracts that resemble petals. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth surface and prominent veins. It produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color.
