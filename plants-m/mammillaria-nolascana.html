Mammillaria nolascana

Mammillaria nolascana, commonly known as the Nolascan pincushion cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5-10 centimeters and features a ribbed stem that may develop a flattened shape with age. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
