Misanteca parvifolia

Misanteca parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved mistflower, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. Misanteca parvifolia produces delicate white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators like bees and butterflies.
