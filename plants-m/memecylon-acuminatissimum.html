Memecylon acuminatissimum

Memecylon acuminatissimum, commonly known as the slender memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters, featuring dense, lance-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by spherical, berry-like fruits. It prefers moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species in ecological studies and traditional medicine.
