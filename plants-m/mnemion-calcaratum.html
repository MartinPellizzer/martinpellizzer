Mnemion calcaratum

Mnemion calcaratum, commonly known as the rock cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its basal rosette of pinnately lobed leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is typically found in rocky or gravelly soils, often in alpine or arctic regions. It has a compact growth habit and is adapted to cold climates.

Mnemion calcaratum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in harsh environments.
