Marsdenia tikalana

Marsdenia tikalana, commonly known as the Tikalana milkvine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with milky sap. The plant features opposite, simple leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are typically small, clustered in inflorescences, and have five petals.
