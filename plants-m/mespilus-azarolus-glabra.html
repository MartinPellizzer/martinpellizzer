Mespilus azarolus glabra

Mespilus azarolus glabra, commonly known as the smooth white mume, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to parts of China.

It is characterized by its smooth, grey bark and fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, sweet-tart berries that are edible and often used in traditional Chinese medicine. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and have a serrated margin.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and medicinal properties.
