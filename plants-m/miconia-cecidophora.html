Miconia cecidophora

Miconia cecidophora, commonly known as the tropical beauty leaf, is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters tall, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by birds and other animals.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, and it is also valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
