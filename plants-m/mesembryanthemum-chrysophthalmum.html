Mesembryanthemum chrysophthalmum

Mesembryanthemum chrysophthalmum, commonly known as the "sun eye" or "yellow eye," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, ribbed stems and small, clustered flowers. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. Its flowers, which range in color from yellow to orange, open in response to sunlight, enhancing its phototropic behavior.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
