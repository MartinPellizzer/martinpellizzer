Mussaenda luteola

Mussaenda luteola, commonly known as the yellow mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow, lantern-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth texture with a slightly hairy underside.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is well-suited to tropical and subtropical climates.
