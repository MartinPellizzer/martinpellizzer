Millettia obovata

Millettia obovata, commonly known as the broad-ovate millettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces pods that contain kidney-shaped seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Millettia obovata thrives in humid environments and is often found in forest understories or along riverbanks.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and potential medicinal research.
