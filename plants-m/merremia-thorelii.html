Merremia thorelii

Merremia thorelii, commonly known as the African morning glory, is a flowering vine native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found growing along riverbanks or in disturbed soils.

Merremia thorelii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
