Mandevilla sanderi

Mandevilla sanderi, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical climbing plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla sanderi is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
