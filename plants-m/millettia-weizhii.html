Millettia weizhii

Millettia weizhii, commonly known as the Chinese tamarind tree, is a deciduous leguminous tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and yellow flowers. The tree produces pods containing edible seeds that are used in traditional Chinese cuisine. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is valued for its ornamental and medicinal properties.

Millettia weizhii is also appreciated for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for agricultural ecosystems.
