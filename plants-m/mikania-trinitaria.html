Mikania trinitaria

Mikania trinitaria, commonly known as the climbing hemp vine, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is a fast-growing, herbaceous vine characterized by its opposite, heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces fluffy, white to pale pink seeds that are dispersed by wind, aiding in its spread. Native to the Americas, it is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and invasive potential make it a common weed in disturbed habitats.
