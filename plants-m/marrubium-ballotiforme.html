Marrubium ballotiforme

Marrubium ballotiforme, commonly known as the ball-shaped hartsenna, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or dry habitats. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in fine hairs.

The species is characterized by its distinctive ball-like seed pods that split open when mature.
