Mammillaria spinosissima f. eximia

Mammillaria spinosissima f. eximia, commonly known as the elegant mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a globular shape and a dense covering of white, woolly bristles.

It originates from the arid regions of Mexico, where it thrives in full sun and well-draining soil. The plant features short, cylindrical stems that are ribbed and adorned with prominent spines. Its flowers are typically pale yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles in late spring.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
