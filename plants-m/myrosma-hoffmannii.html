Myrosma hoffmannii

Myrosma hoffmannii, commonly known as the Hoffmann's myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes eucalyptus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in winter. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal climates.

Myrosma hoffmannii is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
