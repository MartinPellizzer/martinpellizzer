Memecylon bakerianum

Memecylon bakerianum, commonly known as the red-flowered memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, opposite leaves with a rough texture. The plant produces clusters of showy, red or pink flowers that attract various pollinators. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Memecylon bakerianum is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
