Myrobalanus flavicans

Myrobalanus flavicans, commonly known as the yellow mangosteen, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible fruits that are typically yellow when ripe, though they can vary in color. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in coastal areas.
