Maxillaria huancabambae var. cuzcoensis

Maxillaria huancabambae var. cuzcoensis, commonly known as the Cuzco Maxillaria, is a rare orchid species native to the Peruvian Andes.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with violet and white petals. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils at high altitudes. It is a terrestrial orchid that requires specific environmental conditions to thrive.
