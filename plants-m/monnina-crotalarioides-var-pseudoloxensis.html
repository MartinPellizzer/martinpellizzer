Monnina crotalarioides var. pseudoloxensis

Monnina crotalarioides var. pseudoloxensis, commonly known as the Narrowleaf Monnina, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Polygonaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. This variety is notable for its ornamental value and adaptability to harsh environmental conditions.
