Malesherbia humilis var. humilis

Malesherbia humilis var. humilis, commonly known as the dwarf malesherbia, is a low-growing shrub native to South America.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically covered in a dense layer of fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from yellow to orange, often blooming in the spring. It is adapted to dry environments and is often found in rocky or arid soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging growing conditions.
