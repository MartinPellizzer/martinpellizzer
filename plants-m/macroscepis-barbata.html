Macroscepis barbata

Macroscepis barbata, commonly known as the barbed macrocephalus, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its distinctive, barbed bracts that give it its common name. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
