Medinilla basaltarum

Medinilla basaltarum, commonly known as the basalt medinilla, is a striking tropical plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Medinilla basaltarum is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
