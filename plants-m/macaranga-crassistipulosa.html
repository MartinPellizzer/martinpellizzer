Macaranga crassistipulosa

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in catkin-like inflorescences. It is often found in moist, shaded areas of tropical forests.
