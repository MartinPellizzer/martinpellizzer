Manilkara celebica

Manilkara celebica, commonly known as the Celebes sapodilla, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Indonesia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, greyish-brown bark and large, glossy leaves. The tree produces oval, yellowish fruits that are encased in a thick, spiny husk. Its latex, which is milky and sticky, has been traditionally used for various purposes, including as a natural adhesive.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appearance and its potential economic importance in local communities.
