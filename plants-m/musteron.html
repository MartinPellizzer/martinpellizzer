Musteron

Musteron, commonly known as the common mustard, is a herbaceous plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its upright stems and bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, triangular pods that contain several seeds. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Musteron is widely cultivated for its seeds, which are used in cooking and as a spice.
