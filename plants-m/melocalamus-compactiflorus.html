Melocalamus compactiflorus

Melocalamus compactiflorus, commonly known as the dense-flower bamboo, is a species native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and is typically found in moist, shaded environments. The plant features slender, hollow stems that are often clustered closely together, forming dense stands. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.

The flowering occurs in small, clustered inflorescences, which are often concealed within the foliage.
