Malaxis termensis var. elata

Malaxis termensis var. elata, commonly known as the tall Malaxis orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, erect stem and elongated leaves. The plant produces a single, showy inflorescence bearing several small, greenish-white flowers with prominent yellow calli. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and less commonly cultivated orchid.
