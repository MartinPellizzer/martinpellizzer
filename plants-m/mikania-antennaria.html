Mikania antennaria

Mikania antennaria, commonly known as the slender antelope plant, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and hairy stems. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forests. It has opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Mikania antennaria is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
