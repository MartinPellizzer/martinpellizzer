Manihot mossamedensis

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are rich in starch. The plant typically grows in moist, tropical environments and has heart-shaped leaves with a smooth surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often greenish-yellow in color.
