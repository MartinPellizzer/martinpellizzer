Mertensia gigantea

Mertensia gigantea, commonly known as the giant bluebells, is a striking perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its large, showy blue or purple bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant features heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are smooth and glossy. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mertensia gigantea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
