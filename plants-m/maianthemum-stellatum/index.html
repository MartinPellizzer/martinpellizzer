Maianthemum Stellatum

Maianthemum stellatum, also known as star-flowered false lily of the valley, is a delicate perennial plant native to parts of eastern North America. It is characterized by its clusters of small, white, star-shaped flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in woodlands and forest understories. Its foliage consists of broad, lance-shaped leaves that form a dense carpet on the forest floor. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance and quiet growth habits.
