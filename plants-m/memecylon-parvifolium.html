Memecylon parvifolium

Memecylon parvifolium, commonly known as the small-leaved memecylon, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with smooth margins. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
