Macrolobium campestre var. arboreum

Macrolobium campestre var. arboreum, commonly known as the tree hibiscus, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It features large, showy flowers with five broad, waxy petals that range in color from yellow to red. The plant has dark green, lobed leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes in tropical regions.
