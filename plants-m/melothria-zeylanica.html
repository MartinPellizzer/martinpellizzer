Melothria zeylanica

Melothria zeylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon gooseberry, is a tropical vine native to Sri Lanka and other parts of Southeast Asia.

It produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits with a tart flavor, often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. The plant has climbing stems with heart-shaped leaves and is characterized by its rapid growth habit. Its flowers are typically greenish-yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit's high vitamin C content makes it a valuable plant in both horticulture and herbal remedies.
