Myoporum brevipes

Myoporum brevipes, commonly known as the narrow-leaved myoporum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is adapted to dry, sandy soils and is often found in coastal and inland regions.

Myoporum brevipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
