Mapouria himantophylla

Mapouria himantophylla, commonly known as the African sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the family Moraceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often lobed. The tree produces small, aggregate fruits that are encased in a smooth, green husk. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in riverine forests.
