Mikania mosenii

Mikania mosenii, commonly known as the creeping Mikania, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats, often found in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are opposite, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

The small yellow flowers appear in clusters and are followed by fluffy, wind-dispersed seeds.
