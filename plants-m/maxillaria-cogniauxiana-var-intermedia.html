Maxillaria cogniauxiana var. intermedia

Maxillaria cogniauxiana var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
