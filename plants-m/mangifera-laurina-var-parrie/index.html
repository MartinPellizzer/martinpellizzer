Mangifera Laurina Var. Parrie

Mangifera laurina var. parrie is a lesser-known member of the mango family known for its unique foliage and ornamental value. This plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It features broad, glossy leaves that give it a lush, tropical appearance. While not commonly cultivated for fruit, it is prized for its aesthetic appeal in landscaping. Its adaptability makes it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor settings.
