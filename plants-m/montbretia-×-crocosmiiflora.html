Montbretia × crocosmiiflora

Montbretia × crocosmiiflora, commonly known as the hybrid crocosmia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South Africa.

It features tall, upright stems that bear striking orange-red to yellow-orange inflorescences composed of numerous narrow, tubular flowers. The plant's foliage consists of broad, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.

Due to its ornamental value, it is widely cultivated for its vibrant blooms and ease of care.
