Maxillaria gracillima

Maxillaria gracillima, commonly known as the slender maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering stems. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, contributing to its compact and elegant appearance.

Maxillaria gracillima thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
