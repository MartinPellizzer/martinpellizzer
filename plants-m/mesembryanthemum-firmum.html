Mesembryanthemum firmum

Mesembryanthemum firmum, commonly known as the hardy ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers. The plant is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for xeriscaping. It produces a profusion of white or pink flowers in late spring to early summer.

Mesembryanthemum firmum is often used in rock gardens and as a ground cover due to its low maintenance and ornamental appeal.
