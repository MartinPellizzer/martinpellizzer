Mecardonia acuminata var. brevifolia

Mecardonia acuminata var. brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved mecardonia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for low-maintenance gardening.
