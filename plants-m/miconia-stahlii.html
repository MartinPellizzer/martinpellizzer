Miconia stahlii

Miconia stahlii, commonly known as the Stahli miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The plant produces showy, pink to purple bracteate inflorescences that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. Its fruits are small, berry-like structures that contain numerous seeds.

Miconia stahlii is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
