Mesembryanthemum × hybridum

Mesembryanthemum × hybridum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Mesembryanthemum × hybridum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to prevent soil erosion in dry landscapes.
