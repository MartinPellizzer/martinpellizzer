Mammillaria rhodocentra var. gracilispina

Mammillaria rhodocentra var. gracilispina, commonly known as the slender spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem covered in white to pale blue spines that are slender and often recurved. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant.
