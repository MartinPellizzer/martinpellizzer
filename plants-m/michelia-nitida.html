Michelia nitida

Michelia nitida, commonly known as the white-flowered michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale bark and fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green on top and pale underneath. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Michelia nitida is also valued for its use in traditional Chinese medicine.
