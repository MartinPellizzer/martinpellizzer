Merostachys magnispicula

Merostachys magnispicula, commonly known as the giant spikerush, is a tall, grass-like plant native to the wetlands of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, cylindrical clusters of spikelets that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in shallow water or moist soil, thriving in environments with high humidity. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its inflorescence consists of multiple spikelets arranged in a spike.
