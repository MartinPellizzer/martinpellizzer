Miscanthidium gracilius

Miscanthidium gracilius, commonly known as the slender feathergrass, is a perennial grass species native to eastern Asia.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, feathery inflorescences that resemble plumes. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and marshes. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in stabilizing soil in wetland environments.

Its botanical characteristics make it a useful plant for wetland restoration projects.
