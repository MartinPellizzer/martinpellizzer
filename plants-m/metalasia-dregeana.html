Metalasia dregeana

Metalasia dregeana, commonly known as the silverleaf metalasia, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It features silver-gray, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rocky or arid gardens.

Its unique foliage and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.
