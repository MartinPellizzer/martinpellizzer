Mammillaria centricirrha f. gebweileriana

Mammillaria centricirrha f. gebweileriana, commonly known as the Gebweilerian mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, radial spines and a dense covering of white to pale yellow areoles. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for cultivation in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
