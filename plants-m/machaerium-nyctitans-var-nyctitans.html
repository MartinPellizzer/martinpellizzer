Machaerium nyctitans var. nyctitans

Machaerium nyctitans var. nyctitans, commonly known as the night-blooming machaerium, is a tropical flowering plant native to regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that open at night. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its nocturnal blooming habit makes it a unique addition to botanical gardens and shade gardens.
