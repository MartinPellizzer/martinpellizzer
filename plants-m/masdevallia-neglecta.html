Masdevallia neglecta

Masdevallia neglecta, commonly known as the neglected masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, dark green leaves and delicate, pendulous flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light, making it a popular choice for shade gardens. Its intricate floral structure and unique growth habit contribute to its appeal among orchid enthusiasts.

Masdevallia neglecta is relatively easy to cultivate, provided it receives proper drainage and consistent moisture.
