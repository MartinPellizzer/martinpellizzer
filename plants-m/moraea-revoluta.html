Moraea revoluta

Moraea revoluta, commonly known as the spiral lily, is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It features striking, spiral-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy blooms in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its unique floral structure and long-lasting blooms.
