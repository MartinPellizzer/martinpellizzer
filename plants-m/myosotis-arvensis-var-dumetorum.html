Myosotis arvensis var. dumetorum

Myosotis arvensis var. dumetorum, commonly known as the woodland forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. The plant features slender stems that bear clusters of small, blue or violet flowers with five petals. Its leaves are heart-shaped and often have a slightly hairy surface.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
