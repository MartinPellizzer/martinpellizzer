Melaleuca asterocarpa subsp. histoclada

Melaleuca asterocarpa subsp.

histoclada, commonly known as the narrow-leaf melaleuca, is a shrub or small tree native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, urn-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, hard seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy habitats.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
