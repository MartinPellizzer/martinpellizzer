Mycaranthes stenophylla

Mycaranthes stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mycaranthes, is a succulent plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, cylindrical stems that store water, allowing it to survive in dry environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its leaves are narrow and often reduced to small scales, a feature common in many succulents.

Mycaranthes stenophylla is valued for its unique appearance and adaptability to harsh, semi-desert conditions.
