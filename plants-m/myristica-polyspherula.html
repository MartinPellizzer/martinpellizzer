Myristica polyspherula

Myristica polyspherula, commonly known as the fragrant nutmeg tree, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to the Moluccas in Indonesia.

It is a small evergreen tree that produces small, yellowish flowers followed by spherical, red or orange fruits. The plant is notable for its aromatic seeds, which are used in culinary and medicinal applications. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The tree thrives in tropical climates and requires high humidity and well-drained soil for optimal growth.
