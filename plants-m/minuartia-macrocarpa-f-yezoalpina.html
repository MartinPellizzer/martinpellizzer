Minuartia macrocarpa f. yezoalpina

Minuartia macrocarpa f. yezoalpina, commonly known as the Yezo alpine rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing perennial herb that typically thrives in alpine and arctic environments. The plant features small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are generally fleshy and succulent, adapted to conserve moisture in harsh climates.

It is often found growing on rocky slopes and in exposed, well-drained soils.
