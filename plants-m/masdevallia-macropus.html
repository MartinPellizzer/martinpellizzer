Masdevallia macropus

Masdevallia macropus, commonly known as the large-leaf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves and striking, showy flowers that often feature vibrant colors such as red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in high-altitude cloud forests, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions with dappled sunlight. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, slender stems and can last for several weeks.

Masdevallia macropus is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
