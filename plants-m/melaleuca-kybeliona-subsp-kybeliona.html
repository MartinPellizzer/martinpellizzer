Melaleuca kybeliona subsp. kybeliona

Melaleuca kybeliona subsp.

kybeliona, commonly known as the Northern Territory Melaleuca, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often grey-green in color and covered in a fine, powdery bloom.

The plant produces small, bottle-shaped flowers that are typically pink to red in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and semi-arid regions.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its drought tolerance and unique foliage.
