Moraea mira

Moraea mira, commonly known as the Cape bell or blue bell, is a striking bulbous plant native to South Africa.

It features slender, grass-like leaves and produces tall, slender stems topped with clusters of bell-shaped flowers in shades of blue, purple, and white. The plant belongs to the Iridaceae family, which includes irises and gladiolus. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Moraea mira is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated in gardens for its elegant, fleeting blooms.
