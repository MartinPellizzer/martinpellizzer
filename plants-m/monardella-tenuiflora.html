Monardella tenuiflora

Monardella tenuiflora, commonly known as narrowleaf monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, tubular flowers are typically pink or purple and bloom in summer.

It is often used in native gardens due to its ecological value and ornamental appeal.
