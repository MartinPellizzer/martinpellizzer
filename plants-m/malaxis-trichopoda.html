Malaxis trichopoda

Malaxis trichopoda, commonly known as the hairy root malaxis, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is a terrestrial herb with a short rhizome and a single, fleshy, hairy stem. The plant produces a single, showy, greenish-yellow flower with a distinctive, hairy, spurred lip. Its leaves are ovate and often covered in fine pubescence.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.
