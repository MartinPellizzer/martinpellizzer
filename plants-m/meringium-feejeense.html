Meringium feejeense

Meringium feejeense, commonly known as the Feejeense meringium, is a rare and unique plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense rainforests. Its leaves are thin and translucent, allowing light to pass through, which contributes to its striking visual appearance.

Due to habitat loss, Meringium feejeense is considered endangered and is the subject of conservation efforts.
