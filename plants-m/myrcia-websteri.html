Myrcia websteri

Myrcia websteri, commonly known as the Brazilian pepper tree, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, white or pinkish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature.
