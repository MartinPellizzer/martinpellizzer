Myosotis arvensis var. grandiflora

Myosotis arvensis var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered bluebell, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Primulaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, sky-blue flowers that bloom in early spring.
