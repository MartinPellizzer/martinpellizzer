Myristica platyphylla

Myristica platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaf nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and small, yellowish flowers. The plant produces unique fruits that contain the aromatic seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Its leaves are thick and leathery, with a distinctive, pungent scent.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for both its ornamental and commercial uses.
