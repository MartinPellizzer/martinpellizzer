Metalasia trivialis

Metalasia trivialis, commonly known as the common metalasia, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and Asia.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along riverbanks.

Metalasia trivialis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
