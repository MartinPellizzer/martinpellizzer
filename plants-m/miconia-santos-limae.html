Miconia santos-limae

Miconia santos-limae, commonly known as the Santos-Lima miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the Atlantic Forest biome of eastern Brazil, where it thrives in humid, tropical environments. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from white to pink. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
