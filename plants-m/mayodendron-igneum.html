Mayodendron igneum

Mayodendron igneum, commonly known as the Flameleaf Tree, is a striking plant characterized by its vibrant, flame-like foliage.

Native to tropical regions, it features broad, heart-shaped leaves that turn brilliant red or orange during the growing season. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by ornamental, berry-like fruits. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its unique appearance and seasonal color changes make it a standout in botanical collections.
