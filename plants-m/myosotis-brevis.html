Myosotis brevis

Myosotis brevis, commonly known as the dwarf forget-me-not, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has slender, hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

Myosotis brevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
