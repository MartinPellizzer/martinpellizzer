Mazus Miquelii Var. Stolonifer

Mazus miquelii var. stolonifer is a unique and often overlooked member of the primulaceae family. This plant is known for its creeping growth habit and vibrant, clustered flowers that bloom in early spring. Native to specific regions in China, it thrives in moist, shaded environments. Its stoloniferous nature allows it to spread easily, making it both a resilient and adaptable species. Despite its beauty and ecological importance, it remains underappreciated in many botanical circles.
