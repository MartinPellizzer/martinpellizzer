Mougeotia inflata

Mougeotia inflata, commonly known as the inflated stonewort, is a freshwater green alga belonging to the order Coleochaetales.

It is characterized by its branching, filamentous structure with elongated, inflated cells that give it a distinctive appearance. The species plays a role in aquatic ecosystems by providing habitat and food for microorganisms and small invertebrates.
