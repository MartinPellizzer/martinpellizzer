Myosotis australis subsp. australis

Myosotis australis subsp.

australis, commonly known as the New Zealand forget-me-not, is a herbaceous plant native to New Zealand. It features slender stems and produces small, blue to purple flowers with five petals.

The leaves are typically alternate, ovate, and have a hairy texture. It thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
