Manihot humilis

Manihot humilis, commonly known as the dwarf arrowleaf or small manioc, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, yellowish-green flowers. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded, humid environments.
