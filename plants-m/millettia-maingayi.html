Millettia maingayi

Millettia maingayi, commonly known as the African milk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant is notable for its milky sap, which is exuded when the stems or leaves are damaged.

Millettia maingayi is often found in savannas and woodland areas, where it plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various animals.
