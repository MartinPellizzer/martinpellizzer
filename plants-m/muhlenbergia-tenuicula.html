Muhlenbergia tenuicula

Muhlenbergia tenuicula, commonly known as slender muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and compact, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often used in ornamental gardening for their aesthetic appeal. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.
