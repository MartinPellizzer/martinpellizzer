Mimosa ferrisiae

Mimosa ferrisiae, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are typically a soft green. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a vibrant touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Mimosa ferrisiae is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
