Microgonium benlii

Microgonium benlii, commonly known as the Benlii microgonium, is a rare aquatic plant native to specific regions in East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and unique reproductive structures. The plant thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments, often found in marshy areas. Its botanical significance lies in its role in maintaining ecological balance within its habitat.
