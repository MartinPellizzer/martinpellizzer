Mimosa calothamnos

Mimosa calothamnos, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa calothamnos is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
