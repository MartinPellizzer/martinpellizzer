Maesa dependens var. pubescens

Maesa dependens var. pubescens, commonly known as the hairy maesa, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Ericaceae.

It is characterized by its evergreen shrub form and features small, leathery leaves covered in fine, hairy pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
