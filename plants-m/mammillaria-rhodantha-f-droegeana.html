Mammillaria rhodantha f. droegeana

Mammillaria rhodantha f. droegeana, commonly known as the Droegena's pink mammillaria, is a fascinating cactus species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in white to pinkish spines. The plant produces pale pink to white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
