Mandevilla dodsonii

Mandevilla dodsonii, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, making it a popular choice for gardens and containers. Its leaves are typically ovate and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful flowers.

Mandevilla dodsonii thrives in warm, sunny conditions and is often used in ornamental horticulture.
