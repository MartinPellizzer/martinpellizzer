Magnolia officinalis var. pubescens

Magnolia officinalis var. pubescens, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the pubescent magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a slightly pubescent underside. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and has traditional uses in Chinese medicine.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and culturally significant species in the Magnoliaceae family.
