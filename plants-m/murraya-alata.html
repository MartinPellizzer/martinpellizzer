Murraya alata

Murraya alata, commonly known as the flame tree or Indian flame tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green with a slightly glossy surface. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries containing several seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Murraya alata is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
