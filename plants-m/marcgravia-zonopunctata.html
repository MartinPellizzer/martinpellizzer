Marcgravia zonopunctata

Marcgravia zonopunctata, commonly known as the zebra-veined tree.

It is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, elongated leaves with a striking zebra-like pattern of green and white. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences.

Marcgravia zonopunctata is often found in humid, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
