Mammillaria melanocentra

Mammillaria melanocentra, commonly known as the black spine mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, and its spines are typically black or dark brown. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
