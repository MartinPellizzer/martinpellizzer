Memecylon erubescens

Memecylon erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red, often with a contrasting white or yellow center. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Memecylon erubescens is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
