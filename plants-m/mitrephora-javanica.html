Mitrephora javanica

Mitrephora javanica, commonly known as the Java mitrephora, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant, white flowers. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.
