Melastoma cyanocarpum

Melastoma cyanocarpum, commonly known as the blue-fruited melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, blue-black berries that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its foliage is evergreen, with dark green leaves that have a glossy appearance.
