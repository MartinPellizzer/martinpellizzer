Masdevallia guatemalensis

Masdevallia guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the highlands of Central America.

It features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and produces intricate, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light. Its leaves are typically dark green and strap-like, and it blooms during the spring and early summer.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
