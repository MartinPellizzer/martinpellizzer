Myrcia Neograndis

Myrcia neograndis is a striking evergreen shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. It is known for its vibrant red flowers and dark, glossy leaves that thrive in humid, tropical climates. This plant is often used in ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and hardy nature. Its unique fragrance and medicinal properties have also made it a subject of scientific interest. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in the broader horticultural community.
