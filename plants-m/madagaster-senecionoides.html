Madagaster senecionoides

Madagaster senecionoides, commonly known as the Madagascar ragwort, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single flower. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas. It features upright stems with alternate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

The flowers are yellow and produce small, wind-dispersed seeds, contributing to its ecological role in its native environment.
