Medinilla mansfeldiana

Medinilla mansfeldiana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant produces fleshy, berry-like fruits that are edible and often used in local cuisines. Medinilla mansfeldiana thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening and floral arrangements.
