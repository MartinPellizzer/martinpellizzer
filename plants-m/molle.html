Molle

Molle, commonly known as the velvet tamarind, is a species of flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to the arid and semi-arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and produces small, pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. The fruit is often used in traditional medicine and as a source of food.

Its ability to thrive in harsh environments makes it an important species in ecological restoration efforts.
