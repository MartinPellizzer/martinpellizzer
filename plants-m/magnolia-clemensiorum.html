Magnolia clemensiorum

Magnolia clemensiorum, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its bark is grayish-brown and becomes fissured with age.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and parks.
