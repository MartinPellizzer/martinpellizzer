Maxillaria saragurensis

Maxillaria saragurensis, commonly known as the Saragura orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant produces intricate, fragrant blooms with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green tones. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria saragurensis is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
