Muehlenbeckia tamnifolia f. tenuifolia

Muehlenbeckia tamnifolia f. tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf muhlenbeckia, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant native to New Zealand.

It features slender, creeping stems that produce small, pale green to reddish leaves with a distinctive, narrow shape. The plant produces tiny, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color, arranged in clusters along the stems. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understoreys or on rocky slopes.

Its foliage is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in native landscaping.
