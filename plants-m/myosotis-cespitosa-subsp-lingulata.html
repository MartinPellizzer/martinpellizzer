Myosotis cespitosa subsp. lingulata

Myosotis cespitosa subsp.

lingulata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks.

The plant features basal rosettes of lanceolate leaves and produces clusters of small, sky-blue to violet flowers. Its flowers are showy and attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
