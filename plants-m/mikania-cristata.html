Mikania cristata

Mikania cristata, commonly known as the crowned mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. Its small yellow flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences, and it is often found in moist, disturbed habitats.
