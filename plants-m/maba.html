Maba

Maba, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the family Rubiaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by red, fleshy fruits that contain seeds. Maba is valued for its durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.

It also has medicinal uses in traditional African medicine.
