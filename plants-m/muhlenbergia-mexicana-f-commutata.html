Muhlenbergia mexicana f. commutata

Muhlenbergia mexicana f. commutata, commonly known as the Mexican muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit and fine, blue-green foliage. The plant produces delicate, feather-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

This ornamental grass is valued for its drought tolerance and aesthetic appeal in native plant gardens.
