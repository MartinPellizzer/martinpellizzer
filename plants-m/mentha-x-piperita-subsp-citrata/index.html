Mentha × Piperita Subsp. Citrata

Peppermint lemon, scientifically known as Mentha × piperita subsp. citrata, is a hybrid plant that combines the invigorating qualities of peppermint with the citrusy zest of lemon. This unique hybrid is prized for its aromatic leaves and refreshing flavor, making it a popular choice in culinary and herbal applications. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often grown in gardens or as a houseplant. The plant’s essential oils are widely used in aromatherapy, skincare, and natural remedies due to their stimulating and soothing properties. Its distinct scent and versatility have made it a favorite among herbalists and home gardeners alike.
