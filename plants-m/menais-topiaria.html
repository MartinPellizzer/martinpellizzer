Menais topiaria

Menais topiaria, commonly known as the ornamental boxwood, is a slow-growing evergreen shrub prized for its dense, compact foliage.

It belongs to the family Buxaceae and is native to parts of Asia, particularly China and Japan. The plant's leaves are small, oval, and dark green, providing a lush, textured appearance. It is often used in topiary art due to its ability to be shaped into intricate forms.

Its resilience to pruning makes it a popular choice for garden design and landscaping.
