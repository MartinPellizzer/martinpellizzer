Melica berteroana

Melica berteroana, commonly known as the Bertero's brome, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Argentina and Chile.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences. It typically grows in moist, temperate environments such as meadows and grasslands.
