Metalasia

Metalasia, commonly known as the goldenrod, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae.

These plants are native to temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere and are characterized by their bright yellow flowers. Metalasia species typically have alternate leaves and produce small, composite flower heads. They are often found in meadows, grasslands, and along roadsides.

Many species are valued for their ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
