Microtidium

Microtidium, commonly known as the common microtidium, is a small, herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of about 10-20 centimeters and features narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the warm season. It prefers moist, well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Microtidium is often used in ornamental gardening due to its delicate appearance and easy care requirements.
