Mandevilla karwinskii

Mandevilla karwinskii, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical perennial shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces showy, fragrant blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant flowers.

Mandevilla karwinskii is often cultivated as a climbing plant or trained against walls and trellises.
