Macrolobium savannarum var. savannarum

Macrolobium savannarum var. savannarum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, palmately lobed leaves that are often red or orange when young. The tree produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange, which bloom in the dry season. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it well-suited for arid and semi-arid environments.

The plant is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and ability to withstand drought conditions.
