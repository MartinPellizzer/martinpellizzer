Moraea decussata

Moraea decussata, commonly known as the Zulu lily, is a perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It features striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in a raceme. The plant has strap-shaped leaves that are often blue-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Moraea decussata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
