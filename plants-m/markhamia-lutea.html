Markhamia lutea

Markhamia lutea, commonly known as the yellow markhamia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow to orange-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is a perennial shrub that can reach heights of up to 1 meter.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
