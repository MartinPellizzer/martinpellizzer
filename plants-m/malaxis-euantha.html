Malaxis euantha

Malaxis euantha, commonly known as the slender helmet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant has a short, erect stem with a few broad, ovate leaves. It grows in shaded, humid environments, typically in forest understories.

Its unique floral structure and limited distribution make it a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts.
