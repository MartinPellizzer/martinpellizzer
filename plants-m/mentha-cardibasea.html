Mentha cardibasea

Mentha cardibasea, commonly known as broadleaf mint, is a perennial herb native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and strong, aromatic mint scent. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually purple or blue, arranged in dense clusters.

Mentha cardibasea is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
