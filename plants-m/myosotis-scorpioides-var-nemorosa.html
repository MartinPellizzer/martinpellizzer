Myosotis scorpioides var. nemorosa

Myosotis scorpioides var. nemorosa, commonly known as woodland forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and along stream banks. The plant produces clusters of small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting pollinators in natural habitats.
