Machilus balansae

Machilus balansae, commonly known as the Balansae laurustinus, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China, specifically found in the provinces of Yunnan and Guizhou. The plant is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub, characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, oval berries.

The plant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
