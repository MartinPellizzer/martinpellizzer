Mikania sericea

Mikania sericea, commonly known as silky mikania, is a vigorous climbing vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its soft, silvery-hairy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges. It has a rapid growth rate and can become invasive in certain regions.

Its fibrous stems and leaves are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
