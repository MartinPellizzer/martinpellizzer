Microlicia pinheiroi

Microlicia pinheiroi, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a single, hairy stem and a few lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces a single, showy flower with a dark violet or purple coloration, often with yellow or white markings. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and is adapted to the tropical climate of its native habitat.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
