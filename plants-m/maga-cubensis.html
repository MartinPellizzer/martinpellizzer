Maga cubensis

Maga cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban maga, is a rare and unique plant species native to Cuba.

It is characterized by its distinctive, palm-like appearance with a thick, fibrous trunk and broad, waxy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in clusters at the top of the plant. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for botanical research and conservation efforts.
