Mesembryanthemum optatum

Mesembryanthemum optatum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesemb, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is known for its diverse and often strikingly beautiful flora. The plant features fleshy, silver-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. It produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the morning and close by midday.

Mesembryanthemum optatum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
