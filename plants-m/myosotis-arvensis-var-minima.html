Myosotis arvensis var. minima

Myosotis arvensis var. minima, commonly known as the dwarf forget-me-not, is a small flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or violet flowers and lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It has a compact growth habit, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland gardens.

Its flowers bloom in early summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies.
