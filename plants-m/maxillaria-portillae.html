Maxillaria portillae

Maxillaria portillae, commonly known as the Portilla's orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and pseudobulbs that support its growth. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with intricate patterns and a strong scent.

Maxillaria portillae is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and collections.
