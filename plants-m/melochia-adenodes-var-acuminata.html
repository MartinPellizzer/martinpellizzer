Melochia adenodes var. acuminata

Melochia adenodes var. acuminata, commonly known as the sharp-pointed melochia, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with serrated edges and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. The plant produces distinctive, spiny capsules that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils or along forest edges.

Its unique morphology and adaptability make it a notable species in ecological studies.
