Meliosma multiflora

Meliosma multiflora, commonly known as the silverleaf meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage that often has a silvery or white underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the spring. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a leathery texture.

Meliosma multiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
