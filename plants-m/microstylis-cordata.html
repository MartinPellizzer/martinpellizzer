Microstylis cordata

Microstylis cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf microstylis, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and along stream banks. The plant features heart-shaped leaves that are usually less than 2 inches in diameter, with a smooth, waxy surface.
