Micropera draco

Micropera draco, commonly known as the dragon's tear plant, is a unique species belonging to the Acanthaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that resemble tiny tears, typically in shades of blue or violet. The plant has a compact growth habit with lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Native to tropical regions, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
