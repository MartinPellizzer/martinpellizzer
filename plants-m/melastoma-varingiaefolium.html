Melastoma varingiaefolium

Melastoma varingiaefolium, commonly known as the variable-leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptical in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
