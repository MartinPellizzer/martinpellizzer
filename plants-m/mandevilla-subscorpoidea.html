Mandevilla subscorpoidea

Mandevilla subscorpoidea, commonly known as the scarlet mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped, glossy leaves and can grow up to 10 meters in length if supported. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for tropical gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, typically from spring to fall.
