Malvastrum tridactylites var. stellatum

Malvastrum tridactylites var. stellatum, commonly known as the starflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its showy, star-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.
