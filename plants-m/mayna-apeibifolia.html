Mayna apeibifolia

Mayna apeibifolia, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, tubular flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments. It produces small, round berries that contain the seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

Mayna apeibifolia is valued for its ornamental beauty and ease of care in indoor settings.
