Metrosideros ochrantha

Metrosideros ochrantha, commonly known as the kauri pohutukawa, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is known for its striking red flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a glossy green surface.
