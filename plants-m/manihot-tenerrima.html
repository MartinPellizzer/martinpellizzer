Manihot tenerrima

Manihot tenerrima, commonly known as the arrowleaf or arrowhead plant, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and can grow up to 3 meters in height. The plant produces small, inconspicuous green flowers followed by fleshy, spiny fruits containing seeds. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to various soil types.
