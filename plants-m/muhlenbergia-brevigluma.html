Muhlenbergia brevigluma

Muhlenbergia brevigluma, commonly known as the shortgrass muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in open woodlands and disturbed sites, reaching heights of 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features slender, cylindrical stems and narrow, grass-like leaves with a bluish-green hue. Its inflorescence consists of compact, spike-like clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
