Moraea edulis var. umbellata

Moraea edulis var. umbellata, commonly known as the umbrella lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, umbrella-shaped inflorescence composed of numerous pale to deep blue flowers. The plant typically grows in grassland habitats and thrives in well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
