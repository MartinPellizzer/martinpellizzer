Magnolia shizhenii

Magnolia shizhenii, commonly known as the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it produces woody, cone-like fruit containing seeds.

This species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
