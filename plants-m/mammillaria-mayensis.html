Mammillaria mayensis

Mammillaria mayensis, commonly known as the Mayan mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in collections of succulents.
