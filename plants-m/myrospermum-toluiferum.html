Myrospermum toluiferum

Myrospermum toluiferum, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its bright yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves and thrives in well-drained soils. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators.

Myrospermum toluiferum is also known for its aromatic properties and has been used in traditional medicine.
