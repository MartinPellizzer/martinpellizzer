Michelia constricta

Michelia constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved michelia, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has simple, elliptic leaves that are often arranged in whorls along the stems. It thrives in partial shade and well-drained soil, making it suitable for ornamental gardens.

Michelia constricta is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
