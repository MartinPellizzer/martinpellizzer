Microrrhinum pluttulum

Microrrhinum pluttulum, commonly known as the small horned poppy, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, papery petals that range in color from pale yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or dry soils and is often found in disturbed habitats. Its seed pods are distinctive, featuring small, horn-like projections that aid in seed dispersal.

Microrrhinum pluttulum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in well-drained soil.
