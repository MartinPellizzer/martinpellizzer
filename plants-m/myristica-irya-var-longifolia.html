Myristica irya var. longifolia

Myristica irya var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-leaved nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree belonging to the Myristicaceae family.

It is native to the islands of Indonesia, particularly Sumatra and Java, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its elongated, leathery leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its fruit, which resembles a drupe, contains a single seed enclosed in a hard, aromatic shell.

This species is valued for its ornamental qualities and its potential use in traditional medicine and perfumery.
