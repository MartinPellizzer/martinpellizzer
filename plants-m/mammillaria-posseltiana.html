Mammillaria posseltiana

Mammillaria posseltiana, commonly known as the Posselt's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white to grayish spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a short, thick stem. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
