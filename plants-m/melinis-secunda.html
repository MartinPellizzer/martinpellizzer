Melinis secunda

Melinis secunda, commonly known as the silver grass, is a perennial grass species native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-gray foliage and delicate, feathery inflorescences. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in poor soils.

Melinis secunda is also known for its adaptability to various climatic conditions.
