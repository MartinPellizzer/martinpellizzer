Meliosma Simplicifolia Subsp. Laui

Meliosma simplicifolia subsp. laui is a rare and delicate plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia. It is known for its unique leaf structure and subtle floral displays. This subspecies thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories. Its limited distribution makes it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its natural habitat.
