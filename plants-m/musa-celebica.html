Musa celebica

Musa celebica, commonly known as the Celebes banana, is a species of banana native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It is a herbaceous plant with large, broad, lance-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clustered, elongated fruits that are typically yellow when ripe, with a sweet and mildly aromatic flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil for optimal growth.

Musa celebica is primarily cultivated for its edible fruits and is an important crop in local agriculture.
