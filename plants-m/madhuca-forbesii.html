Madhuca forbesii

Madhuca forbesii, commonly known as the Forbes' tallow tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. Its ornamental value and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions make it a popular choice for landscaping in tropical regions.
