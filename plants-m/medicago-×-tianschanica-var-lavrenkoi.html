Medicago × tianschanica var. lavrenkoi

Medicago × tianschanica var. lavrenkoi, commonly known as the Tianshan milkvetch, is a perennial legume native to Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in steppes and mountainous regions. It has a deep root system that allows it to access water from deeper soil layers.

This species is valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
