Mauria sericea

Mauria sericea, commonly known as silverleaf dandelion, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, silky hairs. The plant produces small, yellow composite flowers that bloom in early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Mauria sericea is adapted to arid conditions and is known for its resilience in dry environments.
