Myrsine polyantha

Myrsine polyantha, commonly known as the Christmasberry, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

The leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a glossy surface, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
