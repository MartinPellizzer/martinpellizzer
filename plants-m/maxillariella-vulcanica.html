Maxillariella vulcanica

Maxillariella vulcanica, commonly known as the Vulcan's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the volcanic regions of Central America.

It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth. Its unique habitat and striking floral structure make it a notable species in botanical studies.

Maxillariella vulcanica is adapted to thrive in nutrient-poor, volcanic soils, which contribute to its specialized ecological niche.
