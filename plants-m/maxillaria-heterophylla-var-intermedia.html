Maxillaria heterophylla var. intermedia

Maxillaria heterophylla var. intermedia, commonly known as the Intermediate Maxillaria, is a hybrid orchid species native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
