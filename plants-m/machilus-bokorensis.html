Machilus bokorensis

Machilus bokorensis, commonly known as the Bokorensis-laurel, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in the tropical rainforests of Cambodia and Vietnam. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a glossy surface. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by small, dark berries.

Machilus bokorensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
