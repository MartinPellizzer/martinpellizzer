Magnolia sprengeri var. elongata

Magnolia sprengeri var. elongata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the long-leaf magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The leaves are elongated and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
