Melonura striatula

Melonura striatula, commonly known as the striped melonura, is a species of flowering plant native to specific regions in Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, striped leaves that vary in color from green to reddish-brown. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the warm season. Melonura striatula thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical forests or along riverbanks.

Its unique appearance and habitat make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts.
