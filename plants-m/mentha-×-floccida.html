Mentha × floccida

Mentha × floccida, commonly known as woolly mint, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Mentha aquatica and Mentha rotundifolia.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, pale blue to purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

Mentha × floccida is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies and as a ornamental plant.
