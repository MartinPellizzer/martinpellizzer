Maxillaria paranaensis

Maxillaria paranaensis, commonly known as the Parana orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral structures. Its flowers are usually fragrant and come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple.

Maxillaria paranaensis thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
