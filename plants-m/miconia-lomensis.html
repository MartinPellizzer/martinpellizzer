Miconia lomensis

Miconia lomensis, commonly known as the Lomentos Miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America, particularly in countries like Brazil and Colombia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
