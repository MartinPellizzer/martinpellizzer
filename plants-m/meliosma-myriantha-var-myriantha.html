Meliosma myriantha var. myriantha

Meliosma myriantha var. myriantha, commonly known as the broad-leaved meliosma, is a deciduous shrub native to the eastern United States.

It features opposite, ovate to elliptic leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers in terminal clusters during early spring. Its leaves turn yellow in autumn, adding seasonal interest to the landscape.

It prefers moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade, making it a valuable addition to woodland gardens.
