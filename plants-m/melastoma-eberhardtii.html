Melastoma eberhardtii

Melastoma eberhardtii, commonly known as the Eberhardt's Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually opposite and have a velvety texture.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, contributing to its propagation in the wild.
