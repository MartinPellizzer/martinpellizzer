Mammillaria gladiata

Mammillaria gladiata, commonly known as the sword cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
