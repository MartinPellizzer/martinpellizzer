Masdevallia × courtauldiana

Masdevallia × courtauldiana, commonly known as the Courtauld's masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers that feature intricate patterns and spurred petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to the humid, shaded conditions of its environment.

This species is highly valued by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental beauty and unique floral structure.
