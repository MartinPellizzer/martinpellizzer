Maba polygantha

Maba polygantha, commonly known as the African wild mango, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and compound leaves. Maba polygantha plays an important ecological role in its native habitats, providing food and shelter for various wildlife species.
