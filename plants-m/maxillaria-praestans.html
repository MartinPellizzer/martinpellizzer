Maxillaria praestans

Maxillaria praestans, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its pendulous, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Maxillaria praestans thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
