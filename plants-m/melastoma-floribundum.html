Melastoma floribundum

Melastoma floribundum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as a shrub, often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth surface with a slightly waxy texture.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
