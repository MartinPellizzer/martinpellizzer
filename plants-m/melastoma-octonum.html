Melastoma octonum

Melastoma octonum, commonly known as the eight-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

The flowers are usually pink or red, with five to eight petals, and are accompanied by prominent bracts.
