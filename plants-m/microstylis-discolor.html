Microstylis discolor

Microstylis discolor, commonly known as the pale-styled grass, is a small, herbaceous plant native to the southeastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale green to white inflorescences. Its flowering period typically occurs in late spring to early summer.

Microstylis discolor is often found in moist, sandy soils in open woodlands and along streambanks.
