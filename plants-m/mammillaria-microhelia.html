Mammillaria microhelia

Mammillaria microhelia, commonly known as the miniature hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly bristles. The plant typically grows to a height of less than 5 centimeters, making it one of the smallest species in the Mammillaria genus. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, emerging from the areoles on the stem.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
