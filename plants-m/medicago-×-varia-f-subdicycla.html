Medicago × varia f. subdicycla

Medicago × varia f. subdicycla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed clover, is a perennial forage plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers.

It is a hybrid species within the genus Medicago, native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia. The plant has a deep root system that enhances soil structure and nutrient cycling. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in pasture and erosion control.

Its botanical features make it an important species in agricultural and ecological contexts.
