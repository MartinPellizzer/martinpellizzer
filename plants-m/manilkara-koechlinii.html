Manilkara koechlinii

Manilkara koechlinii, commonly known as the Koechlin's chicle tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of yellowish-green flowers. The tree produces a milky latex sap, which is the source of natural chewing gum. Its fruit, resembling a small, spiny capsule, contains a single large, starchy seed.

This plant is valued for its ecological role and historical use in traditional medicine and gum production.
