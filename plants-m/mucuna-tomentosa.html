Mucuna tomentosa

Mucuna tomentosa, commonly known as the hairy urd or velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and distinctive pod-like fruits that contain edible seeds. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. Its leaves are trifoliate, with each leaflet having a velvety texture.

Mucuna tomentosa is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing properties, making it useful in agricultural settings.
