Moricandia sonchifolia var. homaeophylla

Moricandia sonchifolia var. homaeophylla, commonly known as the Chinese silverleaf, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments and has a sprawling growth habit. Its leaves are silver-white on the underside, giving it a distinctive appearance.

It is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its unique foliage and delicate flowers.
