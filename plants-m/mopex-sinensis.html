Mopex sinensis

Mopex sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese orchid, is a flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, fragrant blooms. The plant typically features tall, slender stems with clustered flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. Its leaves are usually long and narrow, arranged in a rosette pattern.

Mopex sinensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
