Miltonia spectabilis var. moreliana

Miltonia spectabilis var. moreliana, commonly known as the Morelian orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with numerous blooms, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

The leaves are broad and velvety, contributing to its lush, exotic appearance.
