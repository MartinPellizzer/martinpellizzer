Mentha aquatica f. pusilla

Mentha aquatica f. pusilla, commonly known as water mint dwarf form, is a compact variant of the water mint plant.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its small, narrow leaves and delicate, aromatic flowers. This subspecies thrives in moist environments such as stream banks and wet meadows. Its foliage is typically dark green and emits a strong mint scent.

The plant is often used in herbal medicine and as a culinary herb due to its aromatic properties.
