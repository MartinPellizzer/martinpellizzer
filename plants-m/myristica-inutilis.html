Myristica inutilis

Myristica inutilis, commonly known as the useless myristica, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical rainforests. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by oval-shaped fruits that contain a single seed. Its botanical features include smooth, dark green leaves and a distinct, aromatic scent.

Despite its name, it has some traditional uses in local medicine and as a source of essential oils.
