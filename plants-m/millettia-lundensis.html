Millettia lundensis

Millettia lundensis, commonly known as the Lund's Millettia, is a climbing legume native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, showy flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological significance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
