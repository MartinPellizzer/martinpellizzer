Maxillaria cogniauxiana var. pygmaea

Maxillaria cogniauxiana var. pygmaea, commonly known as the dwarf maxillaria, is a compact orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It features small, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pale pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant has a short, sturdy stem and produces pseudobulbs that store water, aiding its survival in dry conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, arranged in a rosette pattern around the base.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
