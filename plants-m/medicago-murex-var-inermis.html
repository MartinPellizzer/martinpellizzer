Medicago murex var. inermis

Medicago murex var. inermis, commonly known as the soft medick, is a low-growing perennial legume native to temperate regions of Europe and western Asia.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers arranged in dense clusters. The plant is characterized by its soft, hairy stems and its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types, including poor and rocky soils. It is often found in grasslands, meadows, and along roadsides.

Medicago murex var. inermis is valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
