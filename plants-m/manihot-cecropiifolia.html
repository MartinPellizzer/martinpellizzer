Manihot cecropiifolia

Manihot cecropiifolia, commonly known as the arrowleaf manioc, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive arrowhead shape. The plant produces a milky latex sap, which is a key feature in its identification and has various traditional uses. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical structure supports its role in both ecological systems and local cultural practices.
