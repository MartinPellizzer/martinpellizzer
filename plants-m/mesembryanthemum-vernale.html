Mesembryanthemum vernale

Mesembryanthemum vernale, commonly known as the common iceplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, waxy leaves. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Mesembryanthemum vernale is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its hardy nature and attractive appearance.
