Monardella candicans var. exilis

Monardella candicans var. exilis, commonly known as slender white beeblossom, is a flowering plant native to California.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its slender stems and small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and woodlands. It produces small, oval-shaped leaves that are often hairy and have a minty aroma.

This variety is less common than the typical form of Monardella candicans and is often found in specific microhabitats.
