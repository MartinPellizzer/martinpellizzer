Mammillaria scheidweileriana

Mammillaria scheidweileriana, commonly known as the Schiedweiler's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring dense, white spines and a rough, textured surface. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5-10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil to thrive.
