Miconia ruschiana

Miconia ruschiana, commonly known as the red miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy, pinkish-white flowers. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
