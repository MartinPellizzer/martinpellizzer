Mentha × villosa var. soluta

Mentha × villosa var. soluta, commonly known as the woolly mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves that give it a distinctive, fuzzy appearance. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer.

Mentha × villosa var. soluta is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine for its aromatic and potentially therapeutic properties.
