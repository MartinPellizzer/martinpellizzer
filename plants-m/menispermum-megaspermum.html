Menispermum megaspermum

Menispermum megaspermum, commonly known as the giant Dutchman's pipe, is a deciduous climbing vine native to eastern North America.

It belongs to the Aristolochiaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and distinctive, pipe-like flowers that are often purple or greenish in color. The plant produces large, showy fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by animals.

It thrives in moist, shaded woodlands and is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
