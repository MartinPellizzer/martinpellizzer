Mimosa capillipes

Mimosa capillipes, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant has a slender, upright stem and is often found in moist, open habitats. Its flowers are typically arranged in racemes and bloom in the spring.

Mimosa capillipes is also known for its sensitive leaves that fold when touched, a trait shared with other mimosa species.
