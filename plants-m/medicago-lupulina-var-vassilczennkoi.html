Medicago lupulina var. vassilczennkoi

Medicago lupulina var. vassilczennkoi, commonly known as the Vassilczennkoi goatweed, is a variant of the goat's rue plant native to Central Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers in umbellate clusters. Its seed pods are slender and dehiscent, releasing small, kidney-shaped seeds.

This variety is notable for its adaptability to arid environments and its use in traditional medicine.
