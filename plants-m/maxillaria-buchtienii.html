Maxillaria buchtienii

Maxillaria buchtienii, commonly known as the Buchtien's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green leaves and blooms with violet to purple flowers that have a distinctive, intricate pattern. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria buchtienii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
