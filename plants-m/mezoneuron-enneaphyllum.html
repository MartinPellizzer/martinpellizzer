Mezoneuron enneaphyllum

Mezoneuron enneaphyllum, commonly known as the nine-leaf mezoneuron, is a flowering plant species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small shrub characterized by its compact growth habit and dense foliage. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in whorls of nine, giving it its scientific name. Its flowers are small and clustered, often appearing in shades of white or pale yellow.

Mezoneuron enneaphyllum is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in rocky or gravelly soils.
