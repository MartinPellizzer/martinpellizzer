Macropanax baviensis

Macropanax baviensis, commonly known as Bavi giant fern, is a unique plant native to the mountainous regions of northern Vietnam.

It belongs to the Araliaceae family and is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can reach lengths of up to 2 meters. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in dense forest understories. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
