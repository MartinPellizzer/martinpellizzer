Mesembryanthemum turrigerum

Mesembryanthemum turrigerum, commonly known as the rock rose, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, water-storing leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers. Mesembryanthemum turrigerum is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in rock gardens and as a houseplant.
