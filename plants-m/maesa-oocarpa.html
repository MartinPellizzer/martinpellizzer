Maesa oocarpa

Maesa oocarpa, commonly known as the red-fruited maesa, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Ericaceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, red berries. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical with a smooth texture and a glossy appearance.
