Minuartia glomerata subsp. burnatii

Minuartia glomerata subsp.

burnatii, commonly known as the Burnat's sandwort, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae. It is characterized by its dense, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink in color.
