Mimosa venustula

Mimosa venustula, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is part of the Fabaceae family, also known as the legume or bean family, which is known for nitrogen-fixing abilities. Mimosa venustula typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full sunlight.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscaping.
