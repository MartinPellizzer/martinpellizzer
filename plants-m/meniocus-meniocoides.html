Meniocus meniocoides

Meniocus meniocoides, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to tree trunks and branches in its natural habitat. Its roots are adapted to absorb moisture and nutrients from the air and rain.

Meniocus meniocoides is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses and botanical gardens.
