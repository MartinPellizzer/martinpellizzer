Mammillaria lasiacantha f. cuchillensis

Mammillaria lasiacantha f. cuchillensis, commonly known as the Cuchillensis mammillaria, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a dense covering of white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a striking contrast to its bluish-green body. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This cultivar is prized for its ornamental value and ease of care in succulent gardens.
