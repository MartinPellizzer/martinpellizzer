Mezia includens

Mezia includens, commonly known as the rock orchid, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe and Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, spurred flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils, preferring sunny to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, while its flowers are usually pale green to yellowish in color.

Mezia includens is known for its resilience in harsh alpine conditions and is often found in subalpine and alpine zones.
