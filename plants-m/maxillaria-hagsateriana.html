Maxillaria hagsateriana

Maxillaria hagsateriana, commonly known as the Hagsater's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color, with a distinctive lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria hagsateriana is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
