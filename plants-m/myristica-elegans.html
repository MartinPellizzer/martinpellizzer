Myristica elegans

Myristica elegans, commonly known as the elegant nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, which is encased in a reddish outer layer. Its essential oils and aromatic compounds are highly valued in both culinary and medicinal applications.

The tree thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its economic value.
