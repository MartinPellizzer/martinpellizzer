Machilus dinganensis

Machilus dinganensis, commonly known as the Ding'an Machilus, is a small evergreen tree native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels and avocado trees. The plant features elliptical, dark green leaves with a glossy surface and a distinct aromatic scent. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by oval, woody fruits.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
