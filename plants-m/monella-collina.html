Monella collina

Monella collina, commonly known as the red spiderling, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small annual herb that typically grows up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features narrow, hairy leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in the summer. Its fruits are red, dry capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds.

Monella collina is often found in dry, rocky soils and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural settings.
