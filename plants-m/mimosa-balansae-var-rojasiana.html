Mimosa balansae var. rojasiana

Mimosa balansae var. rojasiana, commonly known as the Rojasian sensitive plant, is a flowering shrub native to South America, particularly found in Argentina and Uruguay.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is notable for its rapid leaf movement in response to touch, a trait known as thigmonastic response. Its small, yellowish-white flowers bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to its habitat.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable candidate for xeriscaping in arid regions.
