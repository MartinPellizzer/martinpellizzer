Macodes angustilabris

Macodes angustilabris, commonly known as the narrow-leafed tree.

It is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Its bark is smooth and grayish, often with a distinctive pattern of lenticels.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
