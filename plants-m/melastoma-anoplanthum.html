Melastoma anoplanthum

Melastoma anoplanthum, commonly known as the broad-leaved melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate to elliptical leaves. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or red in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma anoplanthum is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting local wildlife.
