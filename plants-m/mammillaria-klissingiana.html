Mammillaria klissingiana

Mammillaria klissingiana, commonly known as the Klissing's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a greenish-gray stem covered in white, woolly hairs. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
