Melanoselinum annuum

Melanoselinum annuum, commonly known as the black garlic mustard, is a biennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other mustard plants. The plant features small, green, edible leaves that are often used in traditional cuisines. Its flowers are white or pale yellow and arranged in clusters, while its fruit is a small, elongated silique.

The plant is also known for its strong, pungent odor, similar to that of garlic or mustard.
