Myosotis arvensis var. maritima

Myosotis arvensis var. maritima, commonly known as sea forget-me-not, is a coastal plant characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

It thrives in saline and sandy soils, often found along shorelines and dunes. The plant is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds, making it a resilient species in maritime environments. Its flowers bloom in early spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

This variety is considered a valuable component of coastal ecosystems due to its ecological role and ornamental appeal.
