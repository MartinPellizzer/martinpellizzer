Microdesmis caseariifolia f. sinensis

Microdesmis caseariifolia f. sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese flame tree, is a flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets arranged in pairs. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is part of the Leguminosae family, which includes many plants with nitrogen-fixing capabilities.
