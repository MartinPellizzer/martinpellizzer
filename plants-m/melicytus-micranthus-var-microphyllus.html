Melicytus micranthus var. microphyllus

Melicytus micranthus var. microphyllus, commonly known as the small-leaf melicytus, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, ovate leaves that are typically less than 2 cm in length, giving it its varietal name. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by red, fleshy berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it an important species for ecological studies in its native habitat.
