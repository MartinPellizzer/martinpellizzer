Mionandra camareoides var. paraguariensis

Mionandra camareoides var. paraguariensis, commonly known as Paraguayan arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces a starchy tuberous root that is used as a food source. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its nutritional value.
