Metrosideros collina var. villosa

Metrosideros collina var. villosa, commonly known as the velvet flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, bright red flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before the leaves in spring. The leaves are typically covered in a fine, velvety pubescence, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and public spaces.
