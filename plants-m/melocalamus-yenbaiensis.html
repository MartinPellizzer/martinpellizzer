Melocalamus yenbaiensis

Melocalamus yenbaiensis, commonly known as the Yenbai bamboo, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its tall, slender culms that can reach heights of up to 10 meters. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically green in color and arranged in a dense, clumping growth habit. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and conservation areas.
