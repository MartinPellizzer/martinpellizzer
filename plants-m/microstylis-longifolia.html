Microstylis longifolia

Microstylis longifolia, commonly known as the slender-styled longleaf, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions.

It features slender, elongated leaves that are typically arranged alternately along its stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. Its root system is fibrous, allowing it to thrive in moist, well-drained soils.

Microstylis longifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate foliage.
