Mikania lucida f. hirticaulis

Mikania lucida f. hirticaulis, commonly known as the hairy Mikania, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant features slender, climbing stems that often have a hairy texture, especially in this particular form. Its leaves are usually ovate to lanceolate, with serrated edges and a glossy surface.

The small yellow flowers appear in clusters, contributing to its ornamental value in gardens.
