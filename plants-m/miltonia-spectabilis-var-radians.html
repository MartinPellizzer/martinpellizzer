Miltonia spectabilis var. radians

Miltonia spectabilis var. radians, commonly known as the Radiant Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid prized for its vibrant, showy flowers.

It features large, velvety petals that often display a striking combination of pink, white, and red hues. The plant typically produces multiple flower spikes, each bearing several blooms that open sequentially. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

This variety is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
