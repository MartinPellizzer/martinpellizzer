Moraea kamiesensis

Moraea kamiesensis, commonly known as the Kamiesens mountain orchid, is a striking bulbous perennial plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically blooms in late summer, producing clusters of tubular or spurred flowers in shades of yellow, orange, or red. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is also of interest to botanists and conservationists.
