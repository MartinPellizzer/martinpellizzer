Melissa perennis

Melissa perennis, commonly known as lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to Europe, North Africa, and the Middle East.

It features aromatic, feather-like leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is valued for its calming properties and is often used in herbal teas and aromatherapy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for gardens and medicinal cultivation.

Melissa perennis has a long history of use in traditional medicine for its soothing effects on the nervous system.
