Maximiliana crassispatha

Maximiliana crassispatha, commonly known as the broad-leafed magnolia, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in the spring. The leaves are broad and elliptical, often with a waxy surface that reflects light. The plant is typically found in humid, shaded environments within tropical forests.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
