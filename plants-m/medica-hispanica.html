Medica hispanica

Medica hispanica, commonly known as Spanish medicine, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It is a perennial herb with slender, upright stems and small, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white to pale blue flowers that bloom in late summer. Its fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dark seeds.

Medica hispanica is often found in dry, rocky soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates.
