Martynia capensis

Martynia capensis, commonly known as the red devil flower, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is a herbaceous annual with bright red, velvety flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, and it thrives in full sun and well-drained soil. Its vibrant color makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

It is also known for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
