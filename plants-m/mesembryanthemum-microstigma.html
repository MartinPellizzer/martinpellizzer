Mesembryanthemum microstigma

Mesembryanthemum microstigma, commonly known as the narrow-stigma ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, typically blooming in spring and summer. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, thriving in full sun and low water conditions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
