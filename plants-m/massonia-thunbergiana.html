Massonia thunbergiana

Massonia thunbergiana, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, gray-green leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white wax, giving it a distinctive silvery appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink or white during the warmer months. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
