Manihot appanii

Manihot appanii, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its erect, herbaceous stems and alternate leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its roots are edible and used as a starch source in some regions.
