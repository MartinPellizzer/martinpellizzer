Maxillaria admonens

Maxillaria admonens, commonly known as the admonition orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and floral structures, with leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Its flowers are often pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that aid in pollination by specific insect species.

Maxillaria admonens thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
