Manihot catingae

Manihot catingae, commonly known as the Amazonian arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit. The plant produces edible tubers that are rich in starch and have a mild flavor. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and often used in traditional medicine.
