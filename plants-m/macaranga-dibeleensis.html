Macaranga dibeleensis

Macaranga dibeleensis, commonly known as the African paper tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and West Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white waxy coating.
