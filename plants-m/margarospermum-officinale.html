Margarospermum officinale

Margarospermum officinale, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the Gesneriaceae family and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, tubular flowers. Its ornamental value makes it a popular houseplant worldwide.
