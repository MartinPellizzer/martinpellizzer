Microtoena brevipedunculata

Microtoena brevipedunculata, commonly known as the short-stemmed orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid characterized by its delicate, grass-like leaves and inconspicuous, greenish-white flowers. Native to Southeast Asia, it typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. The plant has a short rhizome and produces a cluster of short peduncles bearing its small flowers.

Its botanical features make it a unique and specialized species within the orchid family.
