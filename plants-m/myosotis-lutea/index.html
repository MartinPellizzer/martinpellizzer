Myosotis Lutea

Yellow forget-me-not, scientifically known as Myosotis lutea, is a delicate flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia. With its vibrant yellow petals and subtle blue undertones, it stands out among the more commonly known blue forget-me-nots. This species thrives in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks, making it a popular choice for gardeners seeking a touch of wild beauty. Its small, star-shaped flowers bloom in early spring, adding a cheerful splash of color to the landscape. Despite its modest appearance, the Yellow forget-me-not holds cultural and symbolic significance, often associated with remembrance and enduring love.
