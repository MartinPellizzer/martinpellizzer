Machilus Thunbergii

Machilus thunbergii is a small evergreen tree native to eastern asia. it is known for its fragrant leaves and small white flowers. the plant is commonly found in humid forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities. it has been used in traditional medicine for centuries. its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for landscaping and conservation efforts.
