Mesembryanthemum stellatum

Mesembryanthemum stellatum, commonly known as the star flower or evening star, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

Mesembryanthemum stellatum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique appearance and resilience to drought.
