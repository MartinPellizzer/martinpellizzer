Metrosideros francii

Metrosideros francii, commonly known as the Tahitian Christmas tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Society Islands in French Polynesia and is characterized by its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom during the winter months. The plant has small, lance-shaped leaves and produces fruit that is a capsule containing numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to coastal and tropical environments.
