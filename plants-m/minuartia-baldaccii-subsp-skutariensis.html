Minuartia baldaccii subsp. skutariensis

Minuartia baldaccii subsp.

skutariensis, commonly known as the Skutari stonecress, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the Balkan Peninsula. It typically thrives in rocky and calcareous soils, often found in alpine and subalpine regions.

The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, with four petals. Its leaves are linear and closely arranged, forming a dense carpet on the ground.

This subspecies is adapted to harsh environmental conditions, making it a resilient member of its ecological community.
