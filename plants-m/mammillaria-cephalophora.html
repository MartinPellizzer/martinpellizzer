Mammillaria cephalophora

Mammillaria cephalophora, commonly known as the "Crown Mammillaria," is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its delicate nature, it is often cultivated as a prized ornamental plant in arid gardens.
