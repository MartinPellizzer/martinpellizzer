Myrtillocactus geometrizans f. hintonii

Myrtillocactus geometrizans f. hintonii, commonly known as the Hinton's blue cactus, is a unique hybrid of the blue cactus and the Hinton's cactus.

It is characterized by its striking blue to violet spines and a distinctive, geometric shape. The plant is native to the arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water due to its succulent nature.

This rare and ornamental cactus is highly valued in horticulture for its aesthetic appeal and hardiness.
