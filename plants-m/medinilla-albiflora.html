Medinilla albiflora

Medinilla albiflora, commonly known as the white-flowered medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features dense, leathery foliage with elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla albiflora is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and long-lasting blooms.
