Medusandra richardsiana

Medusandra richardsiana, commonly known as the Richards' starfish plant, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Africa.

The plant belongs to the Araliaceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments. Its foliage is often variegated, featuring a mix of green, white, and yellow tones.
