Mentha × villosa f. leptodentata

Mentha × villosa f. leptodentata, commonly known as the slender-toothed mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and flowering stems that often grow in dense clusters. The leaves are typically narrow and have a serrated edge, with a distinctive minty fragrance. This subspecies is native to regions of Europe and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

It is valued for its culinary and medicinal uses, similar to other mint species.
