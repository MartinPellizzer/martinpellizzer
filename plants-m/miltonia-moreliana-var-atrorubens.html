Miltonia moreliana var. atrorubens

Miltonia moreliana var. atrorubens, commonly known as the Red-veined Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers with deep red petals and a contrasting white or pink lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its foliage consists of broad, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern.

This variety is prized for its vibrant colors and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
