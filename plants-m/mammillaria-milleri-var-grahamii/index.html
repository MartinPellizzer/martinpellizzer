Mammillaria Milleri Var. Grahamii

Mammillaria milleri var. grahamii is a striking cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. Known for its unique appearance, it features dense clusters of white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This variety is prized by collectors for its compact size and elegant, ribbed structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for desert gardens. Its beauty and hardiness make it a popular choice for both novice and experienced growers.
