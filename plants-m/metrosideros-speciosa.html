Metrosideros speciosa

Metrosideros speciosa, commonly known as the Tahitian cherry or 'Ahupua'a, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and is characterized by its showy, red or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. It thrives in coastal and wet forest environments, and its rapid growth makes it a popular choice for landscaping.
