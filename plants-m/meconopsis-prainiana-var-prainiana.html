Meconopsis prainiana var. prainiana

Meconopsis prainiana var. prainiana, commonly known as the Himalayan blue poppy, is a striking alpine plant native to the high altitudes of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue to purple flowers, which bloom in early summer and are often surrounded by striking white bracts. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in cool, moist environments. Its leaves are palmately lobed and often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

Meconopsis prainiana var. prainiana is a popular ornamental plant in gardens, though it requires specific conditions to flourish.
