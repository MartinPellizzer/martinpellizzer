Mnemosilla forskalii

Mnemosilla forskalii, commonly known as the memory orchid, is a rare and enigmatic plant native to the high altitudes of the Andes.

Mnemosilla forskalii thrives in cool, humid environments with well-draining soil, making it a challenging species to cultivate.
