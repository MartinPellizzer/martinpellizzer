Melinis ugandensis

Melinis ugandensis, commonly known as Uganda grass, is a perennial grass species native to East Africa.

It typically grows in moist to wet habitats, such as marshes and swampy areas. The plant has slender, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green color.

The inflorescence is a panicle, consisting of small spikelets that produce wind-pollinated flowers.
