Myriogyne latifolia

Myriogyne latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved myriogyne, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the wet season. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Myriogyne latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated as a houseplant.
