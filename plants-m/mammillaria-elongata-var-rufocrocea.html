Mammillaria elongata var. rufocrocea

Mammillaria elongata var. rufocrocea, commonly known as the red-crown hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, spiny areoles covered with white, woolly hair, giving it a distinctive appearance. The stems are typically pale green, with a reddish tinge at the base, and the flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright light, making it suitable for arid environments.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
