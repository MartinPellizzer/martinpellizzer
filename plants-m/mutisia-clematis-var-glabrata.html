Mutisia clematis var. glabrata

Mutisia clematis var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shrubland and dry forest habitats, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are usually smooth and narrow, and it produces small, inconspicuous fruits.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
