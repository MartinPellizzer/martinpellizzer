Millettia elskensii var. elskensii

Millettia elskensii var. elskensii, commonly known as the purple-leafed sycamore, is a deciduous tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves that turn a vibrant purple in the autumn. The tree produces small, pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters, followed by winged seeds dispersed by the wind. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in warm climates.
