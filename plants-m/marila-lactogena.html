Marila lactogena

Marila lactogena, commonly known as the milkweed plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its milky sap, which is a distinctive botanical feature. The plant typically has opposite, oval-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It is native to various regions in Africa and is often found in grasslands and open woodlands.

The plant is also known for its role in supporting certain butterfly species, particularly the milkweed butterfly.
