Mitella latiflora

Mitella latiflora, commonly known as broadleaf mitella, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the eastern United States.

It features delicate, clustered white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has smooth, lance-shaped leaves that are often tinged with red or purple at the base. It typically grows in moist, shaded woodlands and thrives in partial to full shade.

Mitella latiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing nectar for pollinators.
