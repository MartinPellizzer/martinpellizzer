Maranta grandifolia

Maranta grandifolia, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green heart-shaped leaves that often have a velvety texture. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by orange-red berries containing numerous seeds. Maranta grandifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.

Its rhizomatous growth habit allows it to spread and form dense foliage, making it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor decoration.
