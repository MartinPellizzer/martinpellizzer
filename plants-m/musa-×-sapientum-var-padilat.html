Musa × sapientum var. padilat

Musa × sapientum var. padilat, commonly known as the red banana, is a cultivar of the banana plant characterized by its striking red-purple peel.

This variety is botanically classified within the genus Musa, which includes a wide range of tropical plants. The fruit typically has a sweet, mild flavor and is smaller than the common Cavendish banana. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

The plant's leaves are large, broad, and deeply veined, typical of the Musa genus.
