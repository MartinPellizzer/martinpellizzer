Medicago orbicularioides

Medicago orbicularioides, commonly known as the orbicular vetch, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial legume characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers. The plant has a robust root system that helps it thrive in poor soils. It is often found in rocky or dry habitats, demonstrating its adaptability to harsh environments.

Medicago orbicularioides plays a role in nitrogen fixation, making it beneficial for soil fertility.
