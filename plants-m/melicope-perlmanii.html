Melicope perlmanii

Melicope perlmanii, commonly known as the Hawaiian melicope, is a small evergreen tree native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family and is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain a single seed, and it is often found in moist, shaded areas of rainforests.

Melicope perlmanii is also notable for its aromatic essential oils, which have been used in traditional Hawaiian medicine.
