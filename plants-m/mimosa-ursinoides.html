Mimosa ursinoides

Mimosa ursinoides, commonly known as the velvetleaf mimosa, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions of the Americas.

Mimosa ursinoides is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height, and it is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.
