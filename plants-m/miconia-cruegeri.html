Miconia cruegeri

Miconia cruegeri, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia cruegeri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and attractive blooms.
