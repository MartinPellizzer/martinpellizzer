Melastoma porphyreum

Melastoma porphyreum, commonly known as the purple flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 3 meters. The plant features showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds. Its leaves are ovate and glossy, with a dark green color that contrasts well with the vibrant flowers.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
