Miconia caiuia

Miconia caiuia, commonly known as the "Brazilian beauty," is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pink flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia caiuia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
