Mandevilla montana var. peruviana

Mandevilla montana var. peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian mandevilla, is a flowering vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth habit and large, showy flowers that come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant produces tubular flowers that attract hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, adding to its ornamental value.

It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and full sun for optimal growth.
