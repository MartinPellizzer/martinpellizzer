Maranthes chrysophylla

Maranthes chrysophylla, commonly known as the golden leaf maranthes, is a striking ornamental plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow foliage, which contrasts beautifully with its slender, upright stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and botanical collections.
