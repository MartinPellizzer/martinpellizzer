Maranta bella

Maranta bella, commonly known as the night beauty, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, ornamental leaves that change color throughout the day, often displaying vibrant red, pink, and purple hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its showy foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

Maranta bella is popular as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
