Miconia pulchra

Miconia pulchra, commonly known as the beautiful miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia pulchra is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
