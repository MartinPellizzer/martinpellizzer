Miliusa umpangensis

Miliusa umpangensis, commonly known as the Umpang mango, is a tropical tree native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and compound leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that give way to yellow-orange fruits with a fibrous pulp. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

Due to its limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
