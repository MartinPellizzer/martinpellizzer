Malaxis tjiwideiensis

Malaxis tjiwideiensis, commonly known as the Tjiwidei orchid, is a rare and endemic species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on tree trunks or roots.
