Meliosma fordii

Meliosma fordii, commonly known as the Chinese ironwood, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Terniaceae and is characterized by its smooth, dark bark and elliptical leaves with a glossy upper surface. It is valued for its hard, dense wood and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.

Meliosma fordii is also noted for its adaptability to various soil types and its ability to thrive in partial shade.
