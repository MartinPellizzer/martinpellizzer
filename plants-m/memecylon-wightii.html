Memecylon wightii

Memecylon wightii, commonly known as the Indian memecylon, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Memecylon wightii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
