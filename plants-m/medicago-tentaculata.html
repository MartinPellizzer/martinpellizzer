Medicago tentaculata

Medicago tentaculata, commonly known as the prickly medic.

It is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region and parts of Central Asia. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several small, kidney-shaped seeds.

It is often found in disturbed soils and is used in some areas for forage and erosion control.
