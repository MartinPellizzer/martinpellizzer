Monostemma tetrapterum

Monostemma tetrapterum, commonly known as the four-winged monostema, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its distinctive four-winged seeds, which are adapted for dispersal by wind. The plant typically grows as a small shrub with woody stems and narrow, linear leaves. It produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom during the rainy season.

Monostemma tetrapterum is valued for its resilience in harsh environments and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
