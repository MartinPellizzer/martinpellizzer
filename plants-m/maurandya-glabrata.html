Maurandya glabrata

Maurandya glabrata, commonly known as the smooth maurandya, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features clusters of small, tubular flowers that are usually pink or red in color. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often hairy, with a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for garden borders and containers.
