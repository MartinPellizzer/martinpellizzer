Melanoseris leptantha

Melanoseris leptantha, commonly known as the slender-leaved melastome, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its small, showy flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a hairy texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Melanoseris leptantha thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
