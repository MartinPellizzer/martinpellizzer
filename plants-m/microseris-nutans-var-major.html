Microseris nutans var. major

Microseris nutans var. major, commonly known as the nodding microseris, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Brassicaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. Its leaves are basal, linear, and covered in fine hairs.

It is a perennial species that produces capsule fruits containing numerous small seeds.
