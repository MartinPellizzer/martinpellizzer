Moutan officinalis

Moutan officinalis, commonly known as the tree peony, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red and white. The plant has palmate leaves and produces woody stems that can grow up to several meters in height. Its roots are valued in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The plant is also cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its vibrant blooms and elegant appearance.
