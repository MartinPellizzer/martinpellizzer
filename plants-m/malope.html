Malope

Malope, commonly known as yellow catchfly, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Malvaceae.

It consists of several species, most of which are native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa. The plants typically have upright, branched stems and produce showy, often yellow or red flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are usually alternate, simple, and covered in fine hairs.
