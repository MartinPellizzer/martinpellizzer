Madhuca elmeri

Madhuca elmeri, commonly known as the Elmer's candlewood, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
