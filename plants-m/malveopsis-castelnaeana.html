Malveopsis castelnaeana

Malveopsis castelnaeana, commonly known as the Castelnau's malveopsis, is a rare and endemic flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants. The plant is characterized by its showy, pentapetalous flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in humid, shaded environments within the rainforests of Madagascar.
