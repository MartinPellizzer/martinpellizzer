Mammillaria Lasiacantha F. Wohlschlageri

Mammillaria lasiacantha f. wohlschlageri is a rare and striking cactus variety native to specific regions of Mexico. It is known for its distinctive white to pale yellow spines and clustered, spherical growth pattern. This fascinating plant is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts due to its unique appearance and ease of care. Its name honors the botanist who first described it, adding to its scientific significance. Cultivating this variety requires attention to light, temperature, and watering to ensure its health and longevity.
