Metalasia bolusii

Metalasia bolusii, commonly known as the Bolus's metalasia, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers with yellow centers and white to pale pink petals. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Metalasia bolusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
