Medicago × ladak

Medicago × ladak, commonly known as the Ladak clover, is a hybrid perennial legume native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that typically bloom in early summer. The plant is well adapted to rocky and arid environments, showcasing a deep root system for water absorption. It plays a role in soil stabilization and nitrogen fixation due to its symbiotic relationship with rhizobia bacteria.

However, it is less commonly cultivated compared to other clover species due to its specific ecological requirements.
