Morinda vieillardii

Morinda vieillardii, commonly known as the African angelica, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptic to ovate leaves that are often hairy on the underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in umbels, and range in color from white to pink.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
