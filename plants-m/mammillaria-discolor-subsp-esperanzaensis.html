Mammillaria discolor subsp. esperanzaensis

Mammillaria discolor subsp.

esperanzaensis, commonly known as the Esperanza Mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the arid regions of Mexico. It features a globular stem with a pale green to bluish-gray coloration, often adorned with white or pale spines.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to dry climates, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
