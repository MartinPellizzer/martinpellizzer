Mcneillia Rosanoi

Mcneillia rosanoi is a rare and fascinating plant native to the mountainous regions of Mexico. It is known for its striking pink and white flowers that bloom in early spring. This species belongs to the Ericaceae family, which includes many shrubs and evergreens. Due to its limited habitat and declining population, it is considered endangered. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical treasure.
