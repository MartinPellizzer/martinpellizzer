Masdevallia helenae

Masdevallia helenae, commonly known as Helen's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures and velvety foliage. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial orchid, often in shaded, moist environments. Its flowers, which can be pink, purple, or white, are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a conservation concern.
