Microsorum vieillardii

Microsorum vieillardii, commonly known as the Blackwater Sword, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, elongated leaves that often appear almost black in low light conditions. The plant thrives in soft, acidic water and is often found in slow-moving streams and rivers. Its rhizomes spread horizontally, allowing it to colonize large areas in its natural habitat.

Microsorum vieillardii is popular in aquariums for its aesthetic appeal and ability to improve water quality.
