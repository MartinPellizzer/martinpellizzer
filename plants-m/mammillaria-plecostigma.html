Mammillaria plecostigma

Mammillaria plecostigma, commonly known as the plecostigma mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and numerous white or pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
