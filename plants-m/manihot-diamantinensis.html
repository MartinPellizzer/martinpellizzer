Manihot diamantinensis

Manihot diamantinensis, commonly known as the diamondleaf arrowleaf, is a perennial shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its distinctive diamond-shaped leaves and is part of the Euphorbiaceae family. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.
