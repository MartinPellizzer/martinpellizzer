Mitrephora ochracea

Mitrephora ochracea, commonly known as the yellow mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew and mango. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape.
