Maranta tonckat

Maranta tonckat, commonly known as the "prayer plant" or "nightshade plant," is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, colorful leaves that often change color in response to light and temperature. Its leaves are typically oval or heart-shaped and can display patterns of red, pink, and green.
