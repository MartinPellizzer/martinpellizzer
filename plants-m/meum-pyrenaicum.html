Meum pyrenaicum

Meum pyrenaicum, commonly known as the Pyrenean meadowfoam, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes many flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine pubescence. Its flowers are white or pale yellow, and it blooms from late spring to early summer.
