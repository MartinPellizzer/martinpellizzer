Musa × sapientum var. fieleto

Musa × sapientum var. fieleto, commonly known as the fieleto banana, is a variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of fruit that typically mature in a yellow hue. The plant is a member of the Musaceae family and is cultivated for its edible, fleshy berries. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and nutritional content.
