Myoporum acuminatum var. parviflorum

Myoporum acuminatum var. parviflorum, commonly known as the smallflower myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color. The plant produces small, white to pale blue flowers clustered in dense, rounded inflorescences. It is well adapted to coastal and sandy soils, making it a hardy plant in various environmental conditions.

Its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh climates make it a popular choice for landscaping.
