Maranta lietzei

Maranta lietzei, commonly known as the Lietze's arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important plants. The plant is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from deep red to purple. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white.

Maranta lietzei is valued both for its ornamental beauty and for its potential medicinal properties.
