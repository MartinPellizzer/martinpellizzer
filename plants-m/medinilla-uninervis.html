Medinilla uninervis

Medinilla uninervis, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

It is a flowering plant native to the Philippines, characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, round, orange fruits that are edible and have a tangy flavor. It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its unique appearance and pleasant scent make it a popular choice for gardens and indoor spaces.
