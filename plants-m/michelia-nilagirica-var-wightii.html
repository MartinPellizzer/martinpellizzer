Michelia nilagirica var. wightii

Michelia nilagirica var. wightii, commonly known as the Nilgiri Michelia, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small evergreen shrub characterized by its aromatic, ovate leaves and showy, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers. The flowers are typically clustered in terminal panicles and bloom during the winter season. The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its pleasant scent.

It plays a significant role in the local flora and is adapted to the humid, subtropical climate of its native habitat.
