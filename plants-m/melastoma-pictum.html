Melastoma pictum

Melastoma pictum, commonly known as the painted melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its leaves are usually ovate or elliptical with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Melastoma pictum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
