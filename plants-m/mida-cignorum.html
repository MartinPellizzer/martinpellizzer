Mida cignorum

Mida cignorum, commonly known as the white lotus, is a sacred aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in freshwater environments, particularly in slow-moving rivers and ponds. The plant features large, showy white flowers that bloom in the early morning and close by midday. Its leaves are broad and floating, providing habitat for various aquatic life.

The white lotus is also valued for its medicinal properties and cultural significance in many Asian traditions.
