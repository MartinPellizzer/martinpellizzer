Mandevilla scorpioidea

Mandevilla scorpioidea, commonly known as the scarlet mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from red to orange. The plant produces showy blooms throughout the growing season, typically from spring to fall. Its leaves are broad and glossy, often with a slightly waxy texture.

Mandevilla scorpioidea is often cultivated as a trailing or climbing plant in gardens and landscapes.
