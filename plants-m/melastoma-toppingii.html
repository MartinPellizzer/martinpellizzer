Melastoma toppingii

Melastoma toppingii, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in moist, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually opposite and ovate, with a glossy surface.

Melastoma toppingii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
