Malcolmia littorea var. spathulata

Malcolmia littorea var. spathulata, commonly known as the broadleaf malcolmia, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its semi-succulent, fleshy leaves that are typically oval or spatulate in shape. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal areas.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
