Masdevallia ignea var. splendens

Masdevallia ignea var. splendens, commonly known as the Splendour Flame Orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red and orange flowers, which are adorned with intricate patterns resembling flames. The plant typically grows on tree branches or rocks, relying on atmospheric moisture rather than soil for nutrients. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique coloration and dramatic floral display.
