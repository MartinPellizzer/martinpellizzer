Maccraithea agathodaemonis

Maccraithea agathodaemonis, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a flowering plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on tall, erect stems. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and religious ceremonies.

The sacred lotus is symbolically significant in many cultures, representing purity and spiritual awakening.
