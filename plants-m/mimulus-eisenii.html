Mimulus eisenii

Mimulus eisenii, commonly known as the Sierra primrose, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its delicate, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils in mountainous regions. It has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Mimulus eisenii is part of the evening primrose family and is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance.
