Marcetia disticha

Marcetia disticha, commonly known as the two-branched marsh plant, is a flowering plant native to wetland and marsh environments in Europe and parts of Asia.

Marcetia disticha plays an important ecological role in stabilizing wetland soils and providing habitat for various invertebrates.
