Musa × emasculata var. zengani

Musa × emasculata var. zengani, commonly known as the Zengani banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and often curved banana-like fruits. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with tall, robust stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences, and the fruit develops from the female flowers.

This variety is valued for its unique fruit morphology and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes or for its edible fruit.
