Myriocephalus gascoynensis

Myriocephalus gascoynensis, commonly known as the Gascoyne's myriocephalus, is a unique succulent native to South Africa.

It features a cylindrical, branching stem with a distinctive, spiny appearance. The plant produces small, pale green to blue flowers during the cooler months. It is adapted to arid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Its botanical characteristics make it a rare and interesting specimen for succulent enthusiasts.
