Michelia tignifera

Michelia tignifera, commonly known as the fragrant wood orchid, is a deciduous shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its bark is thick and flaky, often with a reddish-brown hue.

Michelia tignifera is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional Chinese medicine.
