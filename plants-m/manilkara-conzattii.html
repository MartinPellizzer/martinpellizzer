Manilkara conzattii

Manilkara conzattii, commonly known as the Malabar chestnut, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Western Ghats of India.

It belongs to the family Sapotaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The tree produces edible, chestnut-like fruits that are encased in a thick, spiny husk. Its bark is smooth and reddish-brown, with a distinctive aromatic scent.
