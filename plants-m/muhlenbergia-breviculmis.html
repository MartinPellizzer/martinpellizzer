Muhlenbergia breviculmis

Muhlenbergia breviculmis, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a native grass species found primarily in the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact growth habit, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often visible during the late summer and early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires minimal water once established.

Muhlenbergia breviculmis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, providing habitat for native wildlife.
