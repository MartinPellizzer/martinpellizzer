Marattia brooksii

Marattia brooksii, commonly known as the Brooks' tree fern, is a species of tree fern native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Marattiaceae and is characterized by its tall, slender trunk and large, feather-like fronds. The plant exhibits a unique growth pattern, with the trunk remaining relatively bare while the fronds grow in a dense, cascading manner. Its rhizome system is robust, allowing it to anchor firmly in moist, shaded environments.

Marattia brooksii plays an important ecological role in its habitat, providing shelter and food for various wildlife species.
