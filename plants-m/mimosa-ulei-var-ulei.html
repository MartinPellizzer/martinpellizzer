Mimosa ulei var. ulei

Mimosa ulei var. ulei, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a small, deciduous shrub native to parts of Africa and the Middle East.

It features delicate, feathery leaves that are soft to the touch and turn reddish in autumn. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, followed by fluffy, grayish seed pods. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Due to its rapid growth and ability to spread, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
