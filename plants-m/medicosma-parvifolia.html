Medicosma parvifolia

Medicosma parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved medicinal plant, is a herbaceous species native to specific regions in Africa.

It features small, narrow leaves that are typically arranged oppositely along its stems. The plant produces small, white or greenish flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its roots and leaves are used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.
