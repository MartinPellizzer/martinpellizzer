Mnesithea nigrescens

Mnesithea nigrescens, commonly known as the darkening mnesithea, is a perennial herb native to tropical regions of Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that often bear clusters of small, dark green leaves. The plant produces delicate, white to pale purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly rough texture.

Mnesithea nigrescens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
