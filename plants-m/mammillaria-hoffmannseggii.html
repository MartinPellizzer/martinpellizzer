Mammillaria hoffmannseggii

Mammillaria hoffmannseggii, commonly known as the Hoffmannsegg's pincushion cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines and has a compact, rounded shape. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, adding a striking contrast to its spiny exterior. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
