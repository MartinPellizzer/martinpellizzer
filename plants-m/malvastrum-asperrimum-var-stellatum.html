Malvastrum asperrimum var. stellatum

Malvastrum asperrimum var. stellatum, commonly known as the star mullein, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features large, velvety, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving them a soft, fuzzy texture. The plant produces showy, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink to purple, which bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its unique appearance and ornamental value make it a popular choice for gardeners seeking drought-tolerant plants.
