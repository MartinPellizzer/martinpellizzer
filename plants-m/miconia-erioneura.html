Miconia erioneura

Miconia erioneura, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a three-lobed capsule that contains numerous small, round seeds.

Miconia erioneura is often found in disturbed habitats and can become invasive in certain regions.
