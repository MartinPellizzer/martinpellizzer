Monnina macrostachya var. stenophylla

Monnina macrostachya var. stenophylla, commonly known as narrow-leaf bluestem, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the central United States.

It belongs to the family Onagraceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, blue to purple flowers. The leaves are typically narrow and lance-shaped, contributing to its common name. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, often found in prairie and grassland habitats.
