Melastoma aggregatum

Melastoma aggregatum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and usually have five petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release the seeds.
