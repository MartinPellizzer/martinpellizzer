Maxillaria truncatilabia

Maxillaria truncatilabia, commonly known as the Brazilian orchid or the pink lady orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a sympodial growth habit, featuring pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white, often with a distinctive lip that is truncate or notched. Its leaves are typically elliptical and covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
