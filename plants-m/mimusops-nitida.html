Mimusops nitida

Mimusops nitida, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, pale grey bark and dense clusters of white, fragrant flowers that bloom in winter. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptic in shape, with a glossy green upper surface and a pale underside. The tree produces small, round, yellowish fruits that contain a single seed.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its various therapeutic properties.
