Mundulea telfairii

Mundulea telfairii, commonly known as the Telfair's acacia, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, attracting pollinators such as bees and birds.

The species plays a role in stabilizing soils and providing forage for wildlife in its native habitat.
