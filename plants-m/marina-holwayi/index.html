Marina Holwayi

Marina holwayi is a unique succulent native to the arid regions of Namibia. It is known for its striking blue-gray foliage and ability to store water in its thick leaves. This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water to survive. Its compact size makes it ideal for rock gardens and container planting. Marina holwayi is a great example of nature's adaptation to harsh environments.
