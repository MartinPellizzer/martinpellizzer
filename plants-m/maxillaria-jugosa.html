Maxillaria jugosa

Maxillaria jugosa, commonly known as the showy maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, fragrant flowers that often display a mix of yellow, orange, and red hues. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, allowing it to thrive in seasonal environments. Its leaves are typically dark green and broad, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms.

Maxillaria jugosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
