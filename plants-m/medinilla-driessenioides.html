Medinilla driessenioides

Medinilla driessenioides, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This evergreen shrub is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its dense, leathery foliage and showy, fragrant flowers. The leaves are typically dark green and glossy, while the flowers are pink or white, often with a waxy texture. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
