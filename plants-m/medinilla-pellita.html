Medinilla pellita

Medinilla pellita, commonly known as the velvet medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers. The plant belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is often cultivated for its ornamental value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla pellita is also valued for its potential use in traditional medicine.
