Melastoma linusii

Melastoma linusii, commonly known as the red-rooted melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range from pink to purple in color. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and stream banks. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a velvety texture.

Melastoma linusii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and flowers.
