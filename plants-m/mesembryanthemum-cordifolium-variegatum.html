Mesembryanthemum cordifolium variegatum

Mesembryanthemum cordifolium variegatum, commonly known as the variegated heartleaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, or yellow during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
