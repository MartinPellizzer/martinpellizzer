Melastoma adscendens

Melastoma adscendens, commonly known as the ascending melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and undergrowth. It has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
