Maranta compressa

Maranta compressa, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its broad, velvety leaves that are often a deep green with red or purple veins. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink, which are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

Maranta compressa is often cultivated for its attractive foliage rather than its flowers.
