Melastoma rugosum

Melastoma rugosum, commonly known as the rough-leaved melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its rough, textured leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in the summer months.

Melastoma rugosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
