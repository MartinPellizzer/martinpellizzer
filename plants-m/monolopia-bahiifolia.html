Monolopia bahiifolia

Monolopia bahiifolia, commonly known as the Bahia leaf or creeping leaf, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae and is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and is often found in coastal areas. It has a low-growing habit with a mat-forming structure, making it suitable for ground cover.
