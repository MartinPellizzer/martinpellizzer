Microgonium beccarianum

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, clustered leaves and tiny, inconspicuous flowers. The plant exhibits a unique reproductive strategy, producing specialized structures called microgonia, which are minute reproductive organs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in tropical rainforests.
