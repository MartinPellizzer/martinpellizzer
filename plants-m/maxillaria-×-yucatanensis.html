Maxillaria × yucatanensis

Maxillaria × yucatanensis, commonly known as the Yucatan orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Mexico and parts of Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit, producing pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. The plant typically features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Maxillaria × yucatanensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
