Medicago minima var. longiseta

Medicago minima var. longiseta, commonly known as the slender goat's rue, is a low-growing perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces and produces yellow flowers in early spring. The plant is characterized by its long, spreading stems and is often found in rocky or sandy soils. It has a fibrous root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in natural habitats.
