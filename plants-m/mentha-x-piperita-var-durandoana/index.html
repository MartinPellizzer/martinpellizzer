Mentha × Piperita Var. Durandoana

Mentha × piperita var. durandoana is a unique hybrid mint plant known for its distinct aroma and medicinal properties. It is a cross between spearmint and peppermint, combining the best traits of both parent plants. This variety is prized for its strong menthol content, which makes it popular in aromatherapy and natural remedies. Its leaves are often used to make teas, essential oils, and topical treatments. Despite its popularity, it remains a relatively lesser-known variant within the mint family.
