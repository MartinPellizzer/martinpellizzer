Manihot glabrata

Manihot glabrata, commonly known as the smooth manioc, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and is known for its latex-rich sap, which can be toxic. It is often cultivated for its starchy roots, which are used as a food source.

However, the plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
