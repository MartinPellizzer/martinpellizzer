Mascarenhasia lisianthiflora

Mascarenhasia lisianthiflora, commonly known as the Mascarene starflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mascarene Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is characterized by its striking star-shaped flowers, which are typically white or pale blue in color. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves and grows as a herbaceous perennial. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, often found in coastal areas.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
