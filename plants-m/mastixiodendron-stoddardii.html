Mastixiodendron stoddardii

Mastixiodendron stoddardii, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a flowering plant native to sub-Saharan Africa.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments. The plant produces yellow flowers and small, fleshy fruits that are often used in traditional medicine. Its leaves are compound and have a distinctive, waxy appearance.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique adaptations and potential medicinal properties.
