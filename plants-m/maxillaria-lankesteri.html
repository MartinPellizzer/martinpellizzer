Maxillaria lankesteri

Maxillaria lankesteri, commonly known as the Lankenster's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, strap-like leaves and has a single, erect stem that bears several flowers in a spiral arrangement. Its flowers are usually violet or purple with white or yellow markings, and they bloom during the late spring to early summer.

Maxillaria lankesteri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a container plant.
