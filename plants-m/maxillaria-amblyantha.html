Maxillaria amblyantha

Maxillaria amblyantha, commonly known as the woolly maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and hairy, dark green leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria amblyantha is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
