Millettia nitida var. minor

Millettia nitida var. minor, commonly known as the slender millettia, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, compound leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its small, elongated pods contain seeds that are dispersed by wind.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
