Mouroucoa rubens

Mouroucoa rubens, commonly known as the red mouroucoa, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and emit a mild fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mouroucoa rubens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
