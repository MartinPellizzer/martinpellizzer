Mimetes nitens

Mimetes nitens, commonly known as the white star protea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Proteaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped inflorescence. The plant produces a dense cluster of small, white flowers that resemble a star, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained, acidic soils and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.

Mimetes nitens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also used in floral arrangements.
