Masdevallia reflexa

Masdevallia reflexa, commonly known as the reflexed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers with elongated petals and sepals. The plant typically thrives in high-altitude cloud forests, where it benefits from cool, humid conditions and dappled sunlight. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette, providing structural support for the delicate inflorescence.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, Masdevallia reflexa is a subject of interest for botanists and orchid enthusiasts alike.
