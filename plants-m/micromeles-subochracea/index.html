Micromeles Subochracea

Micromeles subochracea is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of Mexico. It is known for its small, pale yellow flowers that bloom during the brief rainy season. This species thrives in rocky soils and requires minimal water to survive. Due to habitat loss and climate change, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique and endangered plant.
