Mammillaria schwarzii

Mammillaria schwarzii, commonly known as the Schwarz's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant features areolate ribs that are often ribbed and covered with clusters of small, rounded tubercles. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
