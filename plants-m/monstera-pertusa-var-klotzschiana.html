Monstera pertusa var. klotzschiana

Monstera pertusa var. klotzschiana, commonly known as the Variegated Monstera, is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that feature a unique variegation pattern of green, white, and yellow. The plant is a member of the Araceae family, which includes other popular houseplants like peace lilies and philodendrons. Its leaves can grow up to 1 meter in length, making it a dramatic addition to any indoor space.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
