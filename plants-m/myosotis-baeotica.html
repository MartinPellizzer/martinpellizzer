Myosotis baeotica

Myosotis baeotica, commonly known as the alpine forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the alpine regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers and hairy, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils at high altitudes. It has a compact growth habit and is often found in mountainous environments.

Myosotis baeotica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with suitable conditions.
