Malmea dimera

Malmea dimera, commonly known as the silverleaf malmea, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features silvery-gray leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. Its small, white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Malmea dimera is often found in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to dry, coastal environments.
