Mammillaria fuscata var. russea

Mammillaria fuscata var. russea, commonly known as the Russean Hedgehog Cactus, is a compact, low-growing cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with small, spiny tubercles that give it a distinctive hedgehog-like appearance. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique morphology and hardy nature make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
