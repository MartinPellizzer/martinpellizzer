Memora caracasana

Memora caracasana, commonly known as the Caracasana spider flower, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, spider-like inflorescences that consist of numerous small, white to pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an annual herb, reaching heights of up to 1 meter. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs.

Memora caracasana thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
