Melochia aristata

Melochia aristata, commonly known as the velvetleaf or arrowleaf melochia, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in umbels, followed by distinctive, three-lobed seed pods. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Melochia aristata is also known for its medicinal uses in traditional herbal practices.
