Myrteola phylicoides var. glabrata

Myrteola phylicoides var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth myrtle, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has smooth, glossy foliage and is often found in moist, shaded environments. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional remedies.
