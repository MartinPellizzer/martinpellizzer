Myristica philippinensis

Myristica philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and has glossy, elliptical leaves. Its seeds, known as nutmeg, are enclosed in a reddish outer layer called mace.

This species is valued for its economic and medicinal properties in its native region.
