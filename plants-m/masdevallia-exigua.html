Masdevallia exigua

Masdevallia exigua, commonly known as the miniature orchid or the dwarf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact size, with slender stems and small, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often epiphytic on trees or rocks. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it produces intricate, fragrant blooms in shades of pink, red, and white.

Due to its delicate appearance and unique habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
