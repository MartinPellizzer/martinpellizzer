Myosotis koelzii

Myosotis koelzii, commonly known as the Koelz's forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate blue to violet flowers and heart-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. It belongs to the Boraginaceae family and is known for its ornamental value.
