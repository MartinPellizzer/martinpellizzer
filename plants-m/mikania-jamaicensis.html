Mikania jamaicensis

Mikania jamaicensis, commonly known as the Jamaican Mikania, is a climbing vine native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and feathery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical heads, typical of the daisy family. Its stems are often hairy and can twine around other plants or structures.

Mikania jamaicensis is often found in moist, shaded areas and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
