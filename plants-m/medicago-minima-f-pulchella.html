Medicago minima f. pulchella

Medicago minima f. pulchella, commonly known as the pink burr medic.

It is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, pink to purple flowers and trifoliate leaves. This variety is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions. The plant produces small, burr-like seed pods that aid in seed dispersal.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
