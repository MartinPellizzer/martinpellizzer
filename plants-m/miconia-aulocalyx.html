Miconia aulocalyx

Miconia aulocalyx, commonly known as the scarlet miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hair. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous small seeds.

Miconia aulocalyx is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm, humid climates for its striking foliage and floral display.
