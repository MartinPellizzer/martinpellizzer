Maxillaria loretoensis

Maxillaria loretoensis, commonly known as the Loreto orchid, is a species of orchid native to South America, particularly found in Ecuador.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and bears fragrant, pink to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. Maxillaria loretoensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a sought-after plant among orchid enthusiasts.
