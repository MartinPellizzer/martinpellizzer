Mentha × villosa var. nicholsoniana

Mentha × villosa var. nicholsoniana, commonly known as Nicholson's woolly mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the mountainous regions of the Himalayas.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and aromatic, often fuzzy stems. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or pale blue, arranged in whorled clusters. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in alpine meadows and rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
