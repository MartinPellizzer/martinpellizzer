Myrsine verrucosa subsp. microphylla

Myrsine verrucosa subsp.

microphylla, commonly known as the small-leaved myrsine, is a member of the Rubiaceae family. It is a shrub characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green.

The leaves and fruits are used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
