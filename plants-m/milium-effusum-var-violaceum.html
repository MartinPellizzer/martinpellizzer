Milium effusum var. violaceum

Milium effusum var. violaceum, commonly known as violet moss, is a flowering plant in the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, violet-colored inflorescences. The plant typically thrives in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in a fine, silky texture.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in horticultural settings for its aesthetic appeal.
