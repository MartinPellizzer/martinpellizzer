Musa × sapientum var. suaveolens

Musa × sapientum var. suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented banana, is a variety of banana plant characterized by its fragrant foliage and edible fruit.

This plant belongs to the genus Musa and is part of the Musaceae family, which includes other banana and plantain species. The leaves are large, broad, and emit a mild, sweet aroma, distinguishing it from common banana varieties. The fruit typically has a softer texture and a sweeter flavor compared to typical bananas.

It is cultivated for both ornamental purposes and as a food source in tropical regions.
