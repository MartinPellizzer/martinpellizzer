Mimosa rottleri

Mimosa rottleri, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa rottleri is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.
