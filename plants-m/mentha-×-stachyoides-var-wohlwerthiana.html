Mentha × stachyoides var. wohlwerthiana

Mentha × stachyoides var. wohlwerthiana, commonly known as the woolly mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family (Lamiaceae).

It is characterized by its hairy, silver-gray foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, pale purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and aromatic properties.
