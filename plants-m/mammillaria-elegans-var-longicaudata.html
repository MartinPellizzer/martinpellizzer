Mammillaria elegans var. longicaudata

Mammillaria elegans var. longicaudata, commonly known as the long-tailed elegant mammillaria, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a distinctive long, trailing caudex that gives it its common name. The plant produces white to pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
