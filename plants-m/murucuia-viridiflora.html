Murucuia viridiflora

Murucuia viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered murucuia, is a flowering plant native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its vibrant green flowers and broad, lance-shaped leaves. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are often clustered in terminal inflorescences and are pollinated by insects.

Murucuia viridiflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine.
