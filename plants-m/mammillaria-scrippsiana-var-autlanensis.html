Mammillaria scrippsiana var. autlanensis

Mammillaria scrippsiana var. autlanensis, commonly known as the Autlan mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and dense covering of white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its compact growth and ornamental appeal in arid gardens.
