Mentha × genevensis

Mentha × genevensis, commonly known as Geneva mint, is a hybrid mint species originating from Switzerland.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and aromatic, slightly menthol-like leaves. The plant typically features square stems and opposite leaves that are often gray-green in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Mentha × genevensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and mild mint scent.
