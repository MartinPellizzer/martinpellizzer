Microtis aemula

Microtis aemula, commonly known as the narrowleaf microtis, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale blue to purple flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
