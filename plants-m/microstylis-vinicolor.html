Microstylis vinicolor

Microstylis vinicolor, commonly known as the purple-stemmed spikerush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems that often display a distinctive purple hue. The plant produces small, clustered spikelets that are typically green or brown in color. Microstylis vinicolor thrives in moist environments such as wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds.

It plays an important ecological role by providing habitat and food for various aquatic and terrestrial organisms.
