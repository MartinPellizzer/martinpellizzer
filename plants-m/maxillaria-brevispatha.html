Maxillaria brevispatha

Maxillaria brevispatha, commonly known as the short-pathed maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in humid, tropical environments. Its leaves are oval and arranged in a rosette pattern, and it blooms during the summer months.

Maxillaria brevispatha is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
