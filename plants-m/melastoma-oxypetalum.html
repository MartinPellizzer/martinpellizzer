Melastoma oxypetalum

Melastoma oxypetalum, commonly known as the red-stemmed melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pink to red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma oxypetalum is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in tropical climates.
