Mussaenda collenettei

Mussaenda collenettei, commonly known as the red-flowered mussaenda, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular red flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture and a mild fragrance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mussaenda collenettei is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
