Miconia filiformis

Miconia filiformis, commonly known as the slender miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Malvaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its broad, palmate leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It grows as a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 5 meters.
