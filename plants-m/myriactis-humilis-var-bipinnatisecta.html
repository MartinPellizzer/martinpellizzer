Myriactis humilis var. bipinnatisecta

Myriactis humilis var. bipinnatisecta, commonly known as the narrowleaf yarrow, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in flat-topped clusters, typical of the Asteraceae family.
