Mesembryanthemum pictum

Mesembryanthemum pictum, commonly known as the "Painted Evening Primrose," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers. Mesembryanthemum pictum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
