Myosurus Minimus Var. Shortii

Myosurus minimus var. shortii is a rare and delicate plant known for its tiny, moss-like appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or forested areas. This variant of Myosurus minimus is distinguished by its shorter stature compared to the typical form. Its small size and unique growth pattern make it a subject of interest for botanists and plant enthusiasts. Despite its modest appearance, it plays an important role in its ecosystem.
