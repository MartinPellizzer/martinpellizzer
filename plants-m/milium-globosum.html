Milium globosum

Milium globosum, commonly known as globular millet, is a herbaceous plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its compact, rounded inflorescence that resembles a small globe, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, spikelet-like flowers that are typically green or brown in color. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Milium globosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its unique appearance.
