Millettia micrantha

Millettia micrantha, commonly known as the small-flowered Millettia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pod-like fruits that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.

Millettia micrantha thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.
