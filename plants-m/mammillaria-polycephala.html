Mammillaria polycephala

Mammillaria polycephala, commonly known as the clustered hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly bristles. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange and appear in the spring.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
