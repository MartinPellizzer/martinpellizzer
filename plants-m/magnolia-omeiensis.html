Magnolia omeiensis

Magnolia omeiensis, commonly known as the Sichuan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
