Maurandya scandens var. erubescens

Maurandya scandens var. erubescens, commonly known as the scarlet balloon vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous, trailing vine that produces clusters of showy, tubular flowers in shades of red and orange. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a climbing plant.

The flowers attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds, making it a valuable addition to ecological landscapes.
