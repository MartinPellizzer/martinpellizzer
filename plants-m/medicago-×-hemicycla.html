Medicago × hemicycla

Medicago × hemicycla, commonly known as the yellow sweet clover, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically bright green with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that are clustered in dense, cylindrical inflorescences. It is native to Europe and has been introduced to various parts of the world, often growing in disturbed soils and along roadsides.

Medicago × hemicycla is known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a valuable plant in agricultural and ecological contexts.
