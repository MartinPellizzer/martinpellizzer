Minuartia verna var. juressi

Minuartia verna var. juressi, commonly known as the spring rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, mat-forming growth habit and small, clustered flowers that typically bloom in early spring. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers with four petals, and its leaves are narrow and linear, adapted to thrive in rocky or gravelly soils. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in alpine or arctic environments.

This variety is notable for its resilience to harsh conditions and its role in stabilizing soil in its natural habitat.
