Mammillaria egregia

Mammillaria egregia, commonly known as the Egregious Bunny Ears, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and dense covering of white to light green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Mammillaria egregia is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
