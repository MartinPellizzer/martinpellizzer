Mnemion × contemptum

Mnemion × contemptum, commonly known as the hybrid lily, is a cross between two species of the genus Mnemion, typically Mnemion corymbosum and Mnemion tenuifolium.

It is characterized by its elegant, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a subtle lavender hue. The plant is native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia and thrives in well-drained, loamy soils. It prefers full sun to partial shade and is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens.

Its botanical significance lies in its role as a natural hybrid that showcases the genetic diversity within the genus.
