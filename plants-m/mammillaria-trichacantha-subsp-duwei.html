Mammillaria trichacantha subsp. duwei

Mammillaria trichacantha subsp.

duwei, commonly known as the Duwei mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular or cylindrical stem with prominent tubercles covered in white to grayish hairs.

The plant produces pale yellow to orange flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
