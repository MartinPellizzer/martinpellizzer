Mandevilla harleyi

Mandevilla harleyi, commonly known as the red mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with large, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces trumpet-shaped blooms that are highly attractive to hummingbirds and other pollinators. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite in arrangement.

Mandevilla harleyi thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
