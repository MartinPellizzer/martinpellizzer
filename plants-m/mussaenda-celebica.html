Mussaenda celebica

Mussaenda celebica, commonly known as the Java flame bush, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its showy, bright yellow or orange inflorescences, which are clustered in terminal panicles. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a velvety texture.

Mussaenda celebica thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sun.
