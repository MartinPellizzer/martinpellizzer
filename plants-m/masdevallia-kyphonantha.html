Masdevallia kyphonantha

Masdevallia kyphonantha, commonly known as the helmet orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, helmet-shaped inflorescence that gives it its common name. The plant has narrow, strap-like leaves and produces small, colorful flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Its intricate floral structure and vibrant colors make it a prized addition to botanical collections.
