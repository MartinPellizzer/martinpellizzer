Mammillaria diaphanacantha

Mammillaria diaphanacantha, commonly known as the translucent mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense, white to pale blue spines that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
