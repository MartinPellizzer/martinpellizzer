Micromeria longiflora

Micromeria longiflora, commonly known as the long-flowered micromeria, is a perennial shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

Its leaves are usually narrow and covered in fine hairs, which help reduce water loss in dry conditions.

Micromeria longiflora is also noted for its aromatic foliage, which contains essential oils used in traditional medicine.
