Myrciaria longipes var. pellucida

Myrciaria longipes var. pellucida, commonly known as the white myrtle, is a shrub native to southern Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, fragrant white flowers. The plant has dark green, glossy leaves and produces small, round fruits that are edible. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates with well-drained soil.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and aromatic properties.
