Musa azizii

Musa azizii, commonly known as the Azizii banana, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous plant characterized by its large, broad leaves and clustered inflorescence. The plant produces elongated, curved fruits that are typically yellow when ripe. It belongs to the Musaceae family and is closely related to other banana species.

Musa azizii is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in tropical gardens.
