Mikania congesta

Mikania congesta, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a fast-growing vine native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped leaves and produces small, showy flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant is known for its vigorous growth habit and ability to spread quickly, often forming dense mats. Its stems are covered in fine hairs and can climb or trail along the ground.

Mikania congesta is often found in disturbed areas, such as along roadsides and in open woodlands.
