Melothria indica

Melothria indica, commonly known as the Indian sour gooseberry, is a tropical vine native to Southeast Asia.

It produces small, acidic berries that are often used in traditional cuisines. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in humid, warm climates. Its flowers are yellow and typically appear in clusters.

The botanical features of Melothria indica make it a unique addition to both ornamental and edible plant collections.
