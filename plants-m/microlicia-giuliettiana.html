Microlicia giuliettiana

Microlicia giuliettiana, commonly known as the Juliettiana orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces small, clustered blooms with intricate patterns and vibrant colors, often in shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts.
