Mimosa coorgensis

Mimosa coorgensis, commonly known as the Coorg mimosa, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features bipinnate leaves that are sensitive to touch, causing them to fold when disturbed. It produces small, yellow, spherical flower heads that bloom in the spring.

The plant is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested areas.
