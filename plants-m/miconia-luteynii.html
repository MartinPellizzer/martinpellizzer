Miconia luteynii

Miconia luteynii, commonly known as the yellow miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of showy yellow flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
