Melastoma barbatum

Melastoma barbatum, commonly known as the velvetleaf or redroot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that range in color from pink to red. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that have a velvety texture. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Melastoma barbatum is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
