Miscanthus ryukyuensis

Miscanthus ryukyuensis, commonly known as Ryukyu muhly, is a perennial grass native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping and ecological restoration projects.

Its adaptability makes it a resilient choice for coastal and wetland environments.
