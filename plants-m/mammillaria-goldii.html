Mammillaria goldii

Mammillaria goldii, commonly known as the golden mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of small, white to yellowish spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
