Mucuna iriomotensis

Mucuna iriomotensis, commonly known as the Iriomote velvetbean, is a flowering plant native to the Ryukyu Islands of Japan.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod-bearing fruits. The plant is notable for its hairy, velvety appearance, which gives it its common name. It produces small, yellow flowers and is often found growing in moist, shaded environments.
