Miconia phanerostila

Miconia phanerostila, commonly known as the showy miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that are often accompanied by a strong, unpleasant odor. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy drupe that contains several seeds.

Miconia phanerostila is often cultivated for its ornamental value despite its invasive potential in some regions.
