Mariscus paniceus var. queenslandicus

Mariscus paniceus var. queenslandicus, commonly known as the Queensland panicum, is a perennial grass species native to Australia.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and produces fluffy, panicle-like inflorescences that are typically pale green to golden in color. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands. It is adapted to full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.

Its ornamental value is appreciated in gardens for its feathery appearance and seasonal flowering.
