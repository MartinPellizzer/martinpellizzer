Mollinedia viridiflora var. glabra

Mollinedia viridiflora var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth green-flowered mollinedia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are smooth and often glossy. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on slender pedicels. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
