Merremia sibirica var. jiuhuaensis

Merremia sibirica var. jiuhuaensis, commonly known as the Jiuhua morning glory, is a climbing vine native to China.

It belongs to the Convolvulaceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces violet to blue flowers that bloom in the morning, hence its common name. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and horticultural displays.
