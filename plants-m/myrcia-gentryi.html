Myrcia gentryi

Myrcia gentryi, commonly known as Gentry's myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, fragrant white or pink flowers and glossy, dark green leaves. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Myrcia gentryi is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
