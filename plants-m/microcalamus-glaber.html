Microcalamus glaber

Microcalamus glaber, commonly known as the smooth bamboo, is a species of grass belonging to the Poaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems that often form dense clumps. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are smooth and glabrous, giving it its scientific name. It is native to East Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments.

Microcalamus glaber is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant appearance and hardy nature.
