Meliosma dilleniifolia subsp. cuneifolia

Meliosma dilleniifolia subsp.

cuneifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae. It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and have a smooth texture.

The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in temperate regions.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Meliaceae family, with unique leaf morphology and growth habits.
