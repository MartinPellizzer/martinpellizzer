Mesembryanthemum rectum

Mesembryanthemum rectum, commonly known as the "arrowleaf ice plant," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum rectum is well adapted to arid environments and is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance.
