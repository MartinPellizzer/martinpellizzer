Micromeria × hybrida

Micromeria × hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid silverleaf sage, is a shrub native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its aromatic, gray-green foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. It typically grows in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Micromeria × hybrida is often used in xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
