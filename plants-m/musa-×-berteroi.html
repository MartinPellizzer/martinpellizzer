Musa × berteroi

Musa × berteroi, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid plant species within the Musa genus.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of yellow, banana-like fruits. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is often cultivated for its edible fruit. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Musa × berteroi is also valued for its ornamental appeal due to its striking foliage.
