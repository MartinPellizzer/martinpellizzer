Moacroton

Moacroton, commonly known as the golden leaf fern, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It features feathery, delicate fronds that are typically a vibrant green with a subtle golden hue on the underside. The plant thrives in high humidity and indirect light, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Moacroton is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify the air.
