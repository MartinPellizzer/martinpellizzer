Meibomia limensis var. pilosiuscula

Meibomia limensis var. pilosiuscula, commonly known as the silky meibomia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories. Its botanical features include opposite leaf arrangement and capsule fruits that release tiny seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and adaptability to humid conditions.
