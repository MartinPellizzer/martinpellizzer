Matthiola maderensis

Matthiola maderensis, commonly known as the Mader's stock, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes other well-known plants like cabbage and mustard. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale purple, blooming in spring. Its foliage is aromatic and features lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture.

Matthiola maderensis is often grown in gardens for its ornamental value and fragrance.
