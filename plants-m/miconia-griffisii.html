Miconia griffisii

Miconia griffisii, commonly known as the Caribbean miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean region.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia griffisii is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forest understories and rocky slopes.
