Melastoma vile

Melastoma vile, commonly known as the small melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish-red flowers. The plant typically features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dry seeds.
