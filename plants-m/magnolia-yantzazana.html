Magnolia yantzazana

Magnolia yantzazana, commonly known as the Yantzaza magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval in shape and have a smooth texture. Its bark is smooth and grayish in color, and it can grow up to 20 meters tall in its natural habitat.

The Yantzaza magnolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its beautiful floral display.
