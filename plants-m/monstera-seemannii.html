Monstera seemannii

Monstera seemannii, commonly known as the Shingle Leaf Monstera, is a tropical climbing plant native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its distinctive, fenestrated leaves that resemble a shingle pattern, giving it its common name. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach lengths of up to 10 meters when supported. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a slightly waxy texture.

It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
