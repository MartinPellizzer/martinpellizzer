Muhlenbergia mexicana var. commutata

Muhlenbergia mexicana var. commutata, commonly known as Mexican muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It features slender, wiry stems and produces feathery, panicle-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer. The leaves are narrow, grass-like, and have a bluish-green hue with a slight pubescence. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or arid environments.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance in landscaping.
