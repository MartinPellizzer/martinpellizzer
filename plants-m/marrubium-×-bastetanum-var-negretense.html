Marrubium × bastetanum var. negretense

Marrubium × bastetanum var. negretense, commonly known as the Negretense horehound, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Plantaginaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, gray-green leaves and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun. It is often found in rocky or arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions.

The species is valued for its medicinal properties and ornamental appeal in gardens.
