Medinilla formosana var. hayatana

Medinilla formosana var. hayatana, commonly known as the Philippine velvet plant, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, round berries that turn red when mature, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Its unique appearance and continuous flowering make it a popular choice for indoor and outdoor gardening.
