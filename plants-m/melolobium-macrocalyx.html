Melolobium macrocalyx

Melolobium macrocalyx, commonly known as the large-flowered melolobium, is a striking South African plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant features compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces seed pods that split open when mature. Native to the Cape Province, it thrives in well-drained soils and full sun.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens in regions with a Mediterranean climate.
