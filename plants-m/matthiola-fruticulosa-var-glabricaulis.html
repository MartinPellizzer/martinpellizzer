Matthiola fruticulosa var. glabricaulis

Matthiola fruticulosa var. glabricaulis, commonly known as the smooth-stemmed stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and clusters of fragrant, four-petaled flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically has smooth, hairless stems and leaves that are lance-shaped with a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
