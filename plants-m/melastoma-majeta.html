Melastoma majeta

Melastoma majeta, commonly known as the "Java nightshade," is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, clustered flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The plant has a hairy stem and is known for its ornamental value in horticulture.
