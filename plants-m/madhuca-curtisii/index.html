Madhuca Curtisii

Madhuca curtisii is a unique species of tree native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia. It is known for its striking appearance and ecological significance within its habitat. This plant plays a vital role in supporting local wildlife and maintaining biodiversity. Its flowers are particularly notable for their fragrance and nectar-rich blooms. Despite its beauty, Madhuca curtisii remains relatively understudied and faces threats from deforestation and habitat loss.
