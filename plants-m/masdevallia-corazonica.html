Masdevallia corazonica

Masdevallia corazonica, commonly known as the Heart's Desire orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, heart-shaped petals. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small, with vibrant colors ranging from red to purple, and it blooms throughout the year in suitable conditions.

Due to its striking appearance and rarity, it is highly valued in horticulture.
