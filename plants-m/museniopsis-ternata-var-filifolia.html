Museniopsis ternata var. filifolia

Museniopsis ternata var. filifolia, commonly known as the threadleaf museniopsis, is a rare and delicate plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, thread-like leaves that give it its common name, and it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered near the base of the stems. Its botanical features make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists due to its limited distribution and unique morphology.
