Mimosa claussenii var. dolichopoda

Mimosa claussenii var. dolichopoda, commonly known as the longpod mimosa, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features compound leaves that are typically trifoliate, with small, oval leaflets. The plant produces fluffy, yellow to brown pods that remain on the plant for an extended period. It is adapted to arid environments and thrives in well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
