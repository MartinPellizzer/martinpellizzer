Micrococca berberidea

Micrococca berberidea, commonly known as the yellow bells, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its bright yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

Micrococca berberidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
