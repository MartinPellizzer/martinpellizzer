Memora albiflora

Memora albiflora, commonly known as the whiteflower memora, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, white petals and small, clustered flowers that bloom during the rainy season. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly serrated edge.

Memora albiflora is valued in traditional medicine for its purported therapeutic properties.
