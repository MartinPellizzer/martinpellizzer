Microsorum mindanense

Microsorum mindanense, commonly known as the Mindanao water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically divided into small, serrated segments. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in marshy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, allowing it to colonize new areas efficiently.

Microsorum mindanense plays a role in maintaining water quality by absorbing nutrients and providing habitat for small aquatic organisms.
