Mimosa purpurascens

Mimosa purpurascens, commonly known as the pink mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves and showy pink to red flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant is often found in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and disturbed soils. It has a short lifespan, typically living for only one growing season.

The species is also known for its sensitive leaves that fold when touched, a trait shared with other members of the Mimosa genus.
