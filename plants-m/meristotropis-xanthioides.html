Meristotropis xanthioides

Meristotropis xanthioides, commonly known as the yellow-flowered meristotropis, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow daisy-like flowers. The plant has a compact growth habit with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, tubular florets arranged in a composite head. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and drought tolerance.
