Myosotis arvensis var. multicaulis

Myosotis arvensis var. multicaulis, commonly known as the common forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It features slender, upright stems that often branch near the base, supporting clusters of small, blue or purple flowers. The leaves are typically lance-shaped and arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors or stream banks.

The plant is characterized by its delicate appearance and is often found in disturbed soils or gardens.
