Myosurus aristatus var. gracilis

Myosurus aristatus var. gracilis, commonly known as the slender spike-rush, is a flowering plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spike-like clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, nutrient-poor soils, often in wetlands or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and cylindrical, with a smooth surface and a slightly fibrous texture.

This variety is distinguished from the typical form by its more delicate and slender growth habit.
