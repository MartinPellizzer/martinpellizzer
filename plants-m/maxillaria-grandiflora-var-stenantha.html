Maxillaria grandiflora var. stenantha

Maxillaria grandiflora var. stenantha, commonly known as the narrow-leaved maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, narrow leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a distinctive yellow throat. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate in a well-draining potting mix.
