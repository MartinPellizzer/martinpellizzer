Mesembryanthemum pinnatifidum

Mesembryanthemum pinnatifidum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a succulent perennial native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow leaves that are often covered in a powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
