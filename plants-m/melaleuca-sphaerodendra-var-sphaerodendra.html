Melaleuca sphaerodendra var. sphaerodendra

Melaleuca sphaerodendra var. sphaerodendra, commonly known as the narrow-leaved bottlebrush, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, needle-like leaves and distinctive bottlebrush-like flowers that are typically red or pink. The plant produces small, woody fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or sandy environments.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate harsh environmental conditions.
