Miconia pauciflora

Miconia pauciflora, commonly known as the little-flowered miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers arranged in clusters, which are typically borne on short pedicels. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Miconia pauciflora is often found in shaded, humid environments within tropical rainforests.
