Moraea bellendenii

Moraea bellendenii, commonly known as the Bellenden's moraea, is a striking perennial herb native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. Moraea bellendenii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
