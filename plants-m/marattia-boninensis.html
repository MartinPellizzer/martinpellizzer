Marattia boninensis

Marattia boninensis, commonly known as the Bonin fern, is a species of tree fern native to the Bonin Islands.

It belongs to the family Marattiaceae and is characterized by its large, feather-like fronds that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant has a robust trunk that is covered in a fibrous sheath, and its roots form a dense network that helps stabilize the soil.

Marattia boninensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in coastal forests.
