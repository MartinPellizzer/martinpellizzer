Mesembryanthemum peersii

Mesembryanthemum peersii, commonly known as the Cape gooseberry or ozark beauty, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

The plant belongs to the Aizoaceae family, known for its adaptive features to arid environments. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for xeriscaping.
