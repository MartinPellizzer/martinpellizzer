Mecranium ovatum

Mecranium ovatum, commonly known as the oval shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its unique, shield-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are broad and ovate, with a leathery texture and a distinctive pattern of veins.
