Mussaenda pubescens

Mussaenda pubescens, commonly known as the velvet flower or golden trumpet, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive, velvety appearance. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sun.

Mussaenda pubescens is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
