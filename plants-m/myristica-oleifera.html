Myristica oleifera

Myristica oleifera, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, aromatic foliage and woody structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits containing the aromatic seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Its essential oils are also extracted for use in perfumes and traditional medicine.

The tree thrives in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil and requires a warm, sheltered environment for optimal growth.
