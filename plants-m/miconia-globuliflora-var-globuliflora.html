Miconia globuliflora var. globuliflora

Miconia globuliflora var. globuliflora, commonly known as the globular-flowered miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are often borne in dense, globular inflorescences. Its fruit is a fleshy, orange-red berry that contains several seeds.

Miconia globuliflora var. globuliflora is known for its rapid growth and is often considered an invasive species in regions outside its native range.
