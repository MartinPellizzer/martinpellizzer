Meliosma longipetiolata

Meliosma longipetiolata, commonly known as the long-petioled meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated petioles that support its simple, alternate leaves. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia and is often found in humid, shaded environments.
