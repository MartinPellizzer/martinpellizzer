Mammillaria echinaria

Mammillaria echinaria, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
