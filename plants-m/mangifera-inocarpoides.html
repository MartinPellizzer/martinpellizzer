Mangifera inocarpoides

Mangifera inocarpoides, commonly known as the wild mango or Indian mango, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically appearing in axillary inflorescences.

The fruit is a drupe, with a fibrous mesocarp and a single, large seed.
