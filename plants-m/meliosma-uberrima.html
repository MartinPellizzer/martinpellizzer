Meliosma uberrima

Meliosma uberrima, commonly known as the velvetleaf meliosma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia and the Pacific.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique leaf structure and its role in local ecosystems.
