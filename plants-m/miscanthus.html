Miscanthus

Miscanthus, commonly known as silver grass, is a genus of ornamental grasses native to Asia.

It features tall, slender stalks with feathery plumes that add visual interest to gardens. The plants are perennial, thriving in full sun and well-drained soil. They are often used in landscaping for their elegant appearance and low maintenance requirements.

Miscanthus species vary in height and color, making them versatile choices for both garden design and ecological restoration projects.
