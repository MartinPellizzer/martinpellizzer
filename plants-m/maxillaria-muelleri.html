Maxillaria muelleri

Maxillaria muelleri, commonly known as the Mueller's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and has a sympodial growth pattern. Its flowers are typically borne on terminal inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months.

Maxillaria muelleri is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
