Malanea sarmentosa f. tomentosa

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments.
