Mollinedia grosseserrata

Mollinedia grosseserrata, commonly known as the serrated mollinedia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features a basal rosette of lanceolate, serrated leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers arranged in a terminal raceme during the spring and summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in shaded to partially shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value and unique foliage, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
