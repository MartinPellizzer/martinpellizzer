Mandevilla ligustriflora

Mandevilla ligustriflora, commonly known as the sweet mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, adding to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil and ample sunlight.

Mandevilla ligustriflora is often cultivated for its vibrant blooms and is popular in gardens and as a houseplant.
