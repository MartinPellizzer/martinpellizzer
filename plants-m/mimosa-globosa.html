Mimosa globosa

Mimosa globosa, commonly known as the globular mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its spherical, clustered inflorescences that resemble small globes, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are bipinnate, consisting of multiple pairs of delicate, feathery leaflets.

Mimosa globosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adaptable to various soil types.
