Melastoma fulvum

Melastoma fulvum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a species of flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma fulvum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
