Maxillaria rugosa

Maxillaria rugosa, commonly known as the rough maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, and its leaves are usually dark green with a velvety texture. Maxillaria rugosa thrives in bright, indirect light and requires moderate humidity and well-draining soil.

It is a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
