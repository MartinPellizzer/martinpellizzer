Mammillaria spinosissima var. herrmannii

Mammillaria spinosissima var. herrmannii, commonly known as the Herrmann's mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent ribs and tubercles, covered in white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its striking appearance and is a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
