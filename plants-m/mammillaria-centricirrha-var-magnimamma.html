Mammillaria centricirrha var. magnimamma

Mammillaria centricirrha var. magnimamma, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, ribbed stems that can grow up to 30 cm in height. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its growth is slow, making it a popular choice for collectors.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
