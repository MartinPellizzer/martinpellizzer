Madhuca vulcanica

Madhuca vulcanica, commonly known as the volcanic wax tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the island of Guam.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is an important species in the biodiversity of its native habitat.
