Musa × sapientum var. angao

Musa × sapientum var. angao, commonly known as the Angao banana, is a cultivated variety of the banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and often curved fruit, which can weigh up to 20 kilograms. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

The Angao banana is primarily grown for its edible fruit, which is rich in nutrients and has a sweet, creamy texture.
