Marrubium noeanum

Marrubium noeanum, commonly known as the European harts tongue plant, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered in fine, grayish-white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, pale blue to violet flowers during the summer months. Its leaves are narrow, tongue-shaped, and often have a distinctive waxy coating.

Marrubium noeanum is adapted to dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges.
