Meriania megaphylla

Meriania megaphylla, commonly known as the large-leaf meriania, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by bright orange or red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
