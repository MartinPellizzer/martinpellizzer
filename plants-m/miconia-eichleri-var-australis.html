Miconia eichleri var. australis

Miconia eichleri var. australis, commonly known as the Australian miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to parts of Southeast Asia and northern Australia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the warmer months. These flowers are followed by distinctive, egg-shaped fruits that contain numerous seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and showy blooms.
