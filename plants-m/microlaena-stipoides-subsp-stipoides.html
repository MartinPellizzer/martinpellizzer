Microlaena stipoides subsp. stipoides

Microlaena stipoides subsp.

stipoides, commonly known as slender microlaena, is a perennial grass native to Australia. It typically grows in coastal and inland regions, often found in sandy or gravelly soils.

The plant has slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences that produce numerous tiny flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive blue-green coloration.

It plays an important role in stabilizing soils and is sometimes used in rehabilitation projects.
