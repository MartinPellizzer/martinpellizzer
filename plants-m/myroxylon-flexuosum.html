Myroxylon flexuosum

Myroxylon flexuosum, commonly known as the balata tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its tall, straight trunk and broad, glossy leaves. The tree produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are followed by woody pods containing latex-rich seeds. The latex, known as balata, is used in the production of rubber and other industrial products.

It thrives in humid, tropical climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.
