Marzaria

Marzaria, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a tropical plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, silvery-green leaves that reflect light, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. Its fruit is a small, round berry that turns red when mature.

Marzaria is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
