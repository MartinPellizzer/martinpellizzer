Marica tenuifolia

Marica tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf marica, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, delicate white or pale blue flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Marica tenuifolia is also valued for its role in supporting local pollinators.
