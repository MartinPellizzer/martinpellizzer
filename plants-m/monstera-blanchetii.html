Monstera blanchetii

Monstera blanchetii, commonly known as the African shield fern, is a tropical plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Araceae family and is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that resemble a shield. The plant produces striking white flowers followed by bright red berries, which are edible but not commonly consumed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Monstera blanchetii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and unique foliage.
