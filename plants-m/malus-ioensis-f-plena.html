Malus ioensis f. plena

Malus ioensis f. plena, commonly known as the Flowering Crabapple, is a cultivar of the species Malus ioensis.

It is characterized by its abundant, double flowers that bloom in spring, adding ornamental value to gardens. The plant is a small to medium-sized tree, typically reaching heights of 6 to 12 feet. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside.

It is relatively hardy and suitable for USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8.
