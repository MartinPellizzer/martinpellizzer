Muhlenbergia dumosa var. minor

Muhlenbergia dumosa var. minor, commonly known as the slender muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-gray foliage and delicate, fluffy seed heads that appear in late summer. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. It is drought-tolerant and low-maintenance, requiring minimal water once established.

The slender muhlenbergia is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in dry, rocky environments.
