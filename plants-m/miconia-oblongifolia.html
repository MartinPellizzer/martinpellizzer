Miconia oblongifolia

Miconia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, white hairs. It produces showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Miconia oblongifolia is known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain environments.
