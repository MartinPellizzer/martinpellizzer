Mammillaria magnifica var. minor

Mammillaria magnifica var. minor, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Gods," is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly bristles that give it a fluffy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
