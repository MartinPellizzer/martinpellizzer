Microstylis purpurea

Microstylis purpurea, commonly known as the purple-stamen grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Poaceae family, which includes grasses, and is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear pale green to purple-colored flowers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue.

Microstylis purpurea is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories or along stream banks.
