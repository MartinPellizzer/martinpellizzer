Musa lawitiensis

Musa lawitiensis, commonly known as the Tongan banana, is a species of banana plant native to the islands of Tonga.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of edible, yellow fruit. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.

Musa lawitiensis plays an important role in the local agriculture and culture of Tonga.
