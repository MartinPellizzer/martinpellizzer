Maxillaria lilliputana

Maxillaria lilliputana, commonly known as the miniature maxillaria, is a miniature orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, well-drained environments.

Due to its compact size and ornamental flowers, it is a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
