Minuartia Schimperi

Minuartia schimperi is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the arid regions of Ethiopia and Eritrea. It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family, known for its showy, often brightly colored blooms. This species thrives in rocky and sandy soils, often growing in crevices and on exposed slopes. Its flowers, typically pink or white, bloom in late spring and early summer, adding a touch of beauty to its harsh environment. Despite its resilience, Minuartia schimperi remains a rare and little-known plant, highlighting the diversity of life in extreme climates.
