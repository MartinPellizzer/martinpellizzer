Myosotis laxa var. baltica

Myosotis laxa var. baltica, commonly known as the Baltic forget-me-not, is a perennial herb native to the coastal regions of the Baltic Sea.

It features delicate blue-violet flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, sandy soils along dunes and shorelines. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological role in stabilizing coastal habitats.
