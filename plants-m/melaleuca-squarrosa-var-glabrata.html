Melaleuca squarrosa var. glabrata

Melaleuca squarrosa var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth bottlebrush, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and bright red or pink bottlebrush-like flowers that appear in summer. The leaves are narrow, linear, and often covered in a fine, powdery coating. This plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal heathlands and open forests.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
