Monocera

Monocera, commonly known as the slender spurge, is a flowering plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually smooth and elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the spurge family. Monocera is often found in moist, disturbed soils in temperate regions.

It is considered an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and ease of cultivation.
