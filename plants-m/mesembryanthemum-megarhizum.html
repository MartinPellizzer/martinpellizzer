Mesembryanthemum megarhizum

Mesembryanthemum megarhizum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It produces a large number of small, wind-dispersed seeds, aiding in its propagation.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
