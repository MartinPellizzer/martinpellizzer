Mammillaria elongata var. erecta

Mammillaria elongata var. erecta, commonly known as the tall hedgehog cactus, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with prominent, elongated tubercles that give it a distinctive spiny appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its slow growth rate, it is ideal for rock gardens or as a container plant.
