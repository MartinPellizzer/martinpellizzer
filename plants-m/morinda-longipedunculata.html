Morinda longipedunculata

Morinda longipedunculata, commonly known as the long-stemmed morinda, is a flowering plant belonging to the Rubiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly East Africa, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers. Morinda longipedunculata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
