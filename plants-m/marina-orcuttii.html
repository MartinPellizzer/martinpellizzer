Marina orcuttii

Marina orcuttii, commonly known as the Orcutt's marigold, is a species of flowering plant in the sunflower family, Asteraceae.

It is native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico, where it thrives in rocky, arid environments. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange composite flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh habitat.

Marina orcuttii is often found in crevices and on cliffs, showcasing its resilience to extreme conditions.
