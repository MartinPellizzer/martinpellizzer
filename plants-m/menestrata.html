Menestrata

Menestrata, commonly known as the "Scented Cabbage" or "Sweet Cabbage," is a herbaceous plant native to southern Europe.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes other well-known plants like mustard and cabbages. The plant features rosettes of aromatic, lobed leaves and produces small, yellow flowers in clusters. Its leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their digestive and anti-inflammatory properties.

Menestrata is valued for its distinctive fragrance and culinary uses in Mediterranean cuisine.
