Mussaenda tenuiflora

Mussaenda tenuiflora, commonly known as the narrow-flowered Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that are typically pink or red in color. The plant has opposite, simple, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mussaenda tenuiflora is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
