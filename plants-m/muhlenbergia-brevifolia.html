Muhlenbergia brevifolia

Muhlenbergia brevifolia, commonly known as the narrowleaf muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in open woodlands and sandy soils, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions. The plant features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a fine layer of hair. Its small, fluffy seed heads appear in late summer, adding visual interest to the landscape.

Muhlenbergia brevifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local wildlife.
