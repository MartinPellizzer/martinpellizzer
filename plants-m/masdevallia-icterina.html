Masdevallia icterina

Masdevallia icterina, commonly known as the Yellow Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers, which are typically clustered in a raceme along a slender stem. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in moisture retention. It thrives in high humidity and partial shade, making it a popular choice for tropical greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its striking appearance and specific growing requirements, it is often sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
