Mesembryanthemum filamentosum

Mesembryanthemum filamentosum, commonly known as the filamentous ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves covered in fine, hair-like structures that help reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring and summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
