Metrosideros ramiflora var. villosa

Metrosideros ramiflora var. villosa, commonly known as the silky flame tree, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive, showy red flowers that appear in clusters, often during the spring and summer months. The leaves are typically narrow and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving the plant its varietal name. It thrives in coastal and semi-coastal environments, often growing in sandy or well-drained soils.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
