Melastoma heteromallum

Melastoma heteromallum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, often violet or pink flowers. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
