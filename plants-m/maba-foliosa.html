Maba foliosa

Maba foliosa, commonly known as the leaf maba tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its large, palmate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its bark and leaves have been used in traditional medicine for their purported healing properties.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and thrives in humid, tropical climates.
