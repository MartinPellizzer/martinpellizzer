Mollugo nudicaulis

Mollugo nudicaulis, commonly known as the naked-stem sandwort, is a small, annual herbaceous plant native to arid and sandy environments.

The plant produces small, bright yellow flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom from late spring to early summer.
