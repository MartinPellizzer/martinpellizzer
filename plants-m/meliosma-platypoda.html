Meliosma platypoda

Meliosma platypoda, commonly known as the flat-leaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliosmaceae.

It is native to parts of Southeast Asia, including Indonesia and the Philippines. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments, often found in lowland forests.

Meliosma platypoda is valued for its ornamental foliage and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
