Meibomia × humifusa

Meibomia × humifusa, commonly known as the groundsel or meibomia hybrid, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Meibomia mollis and Meibomia trichotoma. The plant typically grows as a low, sprawling shrub with hairy stems and small, yellowish-green leaves. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are characterized by their tubular shape and yellowish color.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
