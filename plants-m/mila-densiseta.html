Mila densiseta

Mila densiseta, commonly known as the dense-hair mila, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Mila densiseta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens with appropriate conditions.
