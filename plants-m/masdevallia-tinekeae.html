Masdevallia tinekeae

Masdevallia tinekeae, commonly known as the Tineke's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

This species belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. It typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
