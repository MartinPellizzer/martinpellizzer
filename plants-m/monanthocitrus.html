Monanthocitrus

Monanthocitrus, commonly known as the African starfruit or Natal starfruit, is a species of flowering plant in the family Rutaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its distinctive star-shaped fruit, which is typically yellow or orange when ripe. The fruit is often used in local cuisines and has a tart, juicy flavor similar to that of the common starfruit.
