Melastoma finlaysonii

Melastoma finlaysonii, commonly known as the Philippine blueberry, is a shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, which bloom in clusters. Its fruit is a fleshy, red berry that contains several seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some traditional uses in local medicine.
