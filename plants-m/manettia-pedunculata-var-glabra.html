Manettia pedunculata var. glabra

Manettia pedunculata var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth manettia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

The smooth, hairless texture of its stems and leaves is a distinguishing feature of this variety.
