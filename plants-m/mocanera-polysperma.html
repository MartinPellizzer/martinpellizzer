Mocanera polysperma

Mocanera polysperma, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in dense inflorescences. Its seeds are enclosed in small, three-lobed capsules that split open when mature.

Mocanera polysperma is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along riverbanks.
