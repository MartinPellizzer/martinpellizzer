Montravelia

Montravelia, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a popular ornamental plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the family Cactaceae and is characterized by its segmented stems and vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically blooms during the holiday season, producing flowers in shades of red, pink, orange, and white. Its leaves are arranged in a zigzag pattern, and it thrives in bright, indirect light with moderate watering.

Montravelia is well-suited for indoor cultivation and is prized for its long-lasting blooms and ease of care.
