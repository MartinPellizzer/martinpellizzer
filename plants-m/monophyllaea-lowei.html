Monophyllaea lowei

Monophyllaea lowei, commonly known as the lowei's ghost flower, is a rare and unique plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, translucent petals that appear to glow in low light conditions. The plant has a short lifespan, with flowers blooming only briefly before withering. Its botanical significance lies in its unusual morphology and limited distribution.

Due to habitat destruction, it is considered critically endangered in the wild.
