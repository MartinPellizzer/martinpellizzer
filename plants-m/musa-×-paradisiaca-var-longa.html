Musa × paradisiaca var. longa

Musa × paradisiaca var. longa, commonly known as the banana plant, is a tropical herbaceous plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to several meters in length. The plant produces a clustered inflorescence with both male and female flowers, leading to the development of the edible fruit. The fruit, known as the banana, is typically yellow when ripe and contains a high amount of potassium and other nutrients.

This variety is widely cultivated for its fruit, which is a staple food in many tropical regions.
