Maxillaria angustibulbosa

Maxillaria angustibulbosa, commonly known as the narrow-lipped maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive lip. The plant typically blooms in the summer, with flowers ranging in color from white to pale pink. Its leaves are linear and arranged in two rows, contributing to its compact appearance.

Maxillaria angustibulbosa is relatively easy to cultivate, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
