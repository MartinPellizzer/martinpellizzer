Medicago italica var. laevis

Medicago italica var. laevis, commonly known as slender Medicago, is a herbaceous annual plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, globular clusters, and its leaves are typically trifoliate with a smooth, waxy surface. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional herbal medicine.

Its ecological role includes providing nectar for pollinators and serving as a forage plant for livestock.
