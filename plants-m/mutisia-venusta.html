Mutisia venusta

Mutisia venusta, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences that bloom in the summer months. The plant typically grows in moist, sunny environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a velvety texture.

Mutisia venusta is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
