Mammillaria stella-aurata var. gracilispina

Mammillaria stella-aurata var. gracilispina, commonly known as the starlight hedgehog cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, spiny stems covered with white, woolly hairs that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its slow growth rate and compact size.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
