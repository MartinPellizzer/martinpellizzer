Miconia jashaferi

Miconia jashaferi, commonly known as the Jashaferi miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
