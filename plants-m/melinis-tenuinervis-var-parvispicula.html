Melinis tenuinervis var. parvispicula

Melinis tenuinervis var. parvispicula, commonly known as the slender-veined grass, is a variant of the grass species Melinis tenuinervis.

It is characterized by its fine, narrow leaves with delicate, lightly veined structures. The plant typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. It has small, inconspicuous flowers that are usually arranged in spikelets.

This variety is often found in temperate regions and is adapted to a range of soil conditions.
