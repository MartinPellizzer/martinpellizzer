Macropiper oxycarpum

Macropiper oxycarpum, commonly known as the oxycarpum pepperbush, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Piperaceae family, which includes other pepper plants. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that appear in clusters. Its fruits are berries that mature to a red color, often used in traditional medicine.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and pharmacological contexts.
