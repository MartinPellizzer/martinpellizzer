Mauria multiflora

Mauria multiflora, commonly known as the many-flowered mauria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact, shrub-like growth habit and produces clusters of small, fragrant white or pink flowers. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.

Mauria multiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
