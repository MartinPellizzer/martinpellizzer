Monoon membranifolium

Monoon membranifolium, commonly known as the paper-leaf monoon, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, thin, and papery leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, often attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical features make it a unique and ornamental addition to tropical gardens.
