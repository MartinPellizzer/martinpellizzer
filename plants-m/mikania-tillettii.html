Mikania tillettii

Mikania tillettii, commonly known as the tropical blueweed, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features slender, branched stems and small, blue to purple flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs.

Mikania tillettii is often found in disturbed areas and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
