Mesembryanthemum labyrintheum

Mesembryanthemum labyrintheum, commonly known as the labyrinthal ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum labyrintheum is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and drought tolerance.
