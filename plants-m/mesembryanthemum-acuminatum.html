Mesembryanthemum acuminatum

Mesembryanthemum acuminatum, commonly known as the sharp-leaved ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often hairy leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink, red, and yellow. Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
