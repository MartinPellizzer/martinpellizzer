Margaretta orbicularis

Margaretta orbicularis, commonly known as the orbicular margaret, is a perennial herb native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features heart-shaped leaves that are typically smooth and dark green, with a slightly waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Margaretta orbicularis is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ease of care.
