Mandevilla pulchra

Mandevilla pulchra, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical perennial vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from orange to red. The plant produces opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mandevilla pulchra is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
