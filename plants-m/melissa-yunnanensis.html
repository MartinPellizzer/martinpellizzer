Melissa yunnanensis

Melissa yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to China.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, small leaves and clusters of white or pale blue flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial shade.

Melissa yunnanensis is often used in traditional medicine for its calming properties.
