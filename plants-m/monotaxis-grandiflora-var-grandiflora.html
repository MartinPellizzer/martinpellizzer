Monotaxis grandiflora var. grandiflora

Monotaxis grandiflora var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered monotaxis, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves that are arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem. It thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
