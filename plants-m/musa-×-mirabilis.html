Musa × mirabilis

Musa × mirabilis, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and often curved leaves, which can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that eventually develop into edible, elongated fruits. The fruit has a mild, sweet flavor and is typically consumed when fully ripe.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.
