Maxillaria vallisnerioides

Maxillaria vallisnerioides, commonly known as the orchid of the valley, is a tropical epiphytic orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a compact, pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its humid, shaded forest habitat. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive lip. It thrives in well-draining potting media and requires moderate light and consistent moisture.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
