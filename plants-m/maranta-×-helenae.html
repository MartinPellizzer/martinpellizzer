Maranta × helenae

Maranta × helenae, commonly known as the velvet leaf.

It is a hybrid plant native to South America, characterized by its striking, velvety, dark green leaves with intricate patterns. The leaves are typically heart-shaped and often display a marbled or mottled appearance. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires indirect light to flourish.

This plant is popular as a decorative foliage plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
