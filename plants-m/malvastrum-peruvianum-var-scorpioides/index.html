Malvastrum Peruvianum Var. Scorpioides

Peruvian mallow, scientifically known as Malvastrum peruvianum var. scorpioides, is a striking flowering plant native to the arid regions of South America. This species is characterized by its vibrant purple to pink flowers and distinctive velvety leaves that catch the sunlight. It thrives in harsh environments, making it a resilient addition to xeriscaping and native plant gardens. Often mistaken for other mallows, it stands out due to its unique growth habit and colorful blooms. Its ecological importance and ornamental value have made it a popular choice among botanists and gardeners alike.
