Magnolia neomagnifolia

Magnolia neomagnifolia, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture. The tree can grow up to 15 meters tall with a dense, rounded canopy.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.
