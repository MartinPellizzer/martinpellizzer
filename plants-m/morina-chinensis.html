Morina chinensis

Morina chinensis, commonly known as Chinese moringa, is a shrub native to China and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, dark green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has some traditional medicinal uses.

Morina chinensis thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded environment.
