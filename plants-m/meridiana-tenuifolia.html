Meridiana tenuifolia

Meridiana tenuifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved meridiana, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pale purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Meridiana tenuifolia is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
