Merremia yunnanensis var. pallescens

Merremia yunnanensis var. pallescens, commonly known as the Yunnan morning glory, is a climbing vine native to China.

It features heart-shaped leaves with a waxy texture and produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range from white to pale purple. The plant is characterized by its long, hairy stems and distinctive, spiral-shaped tendrils that aid in climbing. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, shaded environments.

The flowers bloom in the early morning, giving the plant its common name.
