Melodinus philliraeoides

Melodinus philliraeoides, commonly known as the African bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb with erect, branched stems and lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of bell-shaped, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Melodinus philliraeoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine in some regions.
