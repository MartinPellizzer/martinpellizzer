Melaleuca stricta

Melaleuca stricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, dark seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and heathland environments.

Melaleuca stricta is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
