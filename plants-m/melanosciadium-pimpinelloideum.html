Melanosciadium pimpinelloideum

Melanosciadium pimpinelloideum, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a member of the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, disturbed soils and is often found in wetlands or along stream banks. Its seeds are small and can remain viable in the soil for several years.
