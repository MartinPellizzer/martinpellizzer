Mammillaria bicolor var. longispina

Mammillaria bicolor var. longispina, commonly known as the Long-spined White Globe Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to white body covered in short, white spines and prominent, long, yellowish spines that give it its common name. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its attractive appearance and relatively easy care, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
