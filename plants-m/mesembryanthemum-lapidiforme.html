Mesembryanthemum lapidiforme

Mesembryanthemum lapidiforme, commonly known as the stonecress or rock cress, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

The plant thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss.
