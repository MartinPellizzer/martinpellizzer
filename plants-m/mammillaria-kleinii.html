Mammillaria kleinii

Mammillaria kleinii, commonly known as the Klein's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and a dense covering of small, white spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters, with a diameter of up to 8 centimeters. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, blooming in late spring or early summer.

Mammillaria kleinii is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
