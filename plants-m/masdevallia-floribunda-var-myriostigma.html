Masdevallia floribunda var. myriostigma

Masdevallia floribunda var. myriostigma, commonly known as the "Myriostigma Masdevallia," is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous inflorescence that bears numerous small, brightly colored flowers, often in shades of pink and white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in specialized greenhouses or under controlled conditions.
