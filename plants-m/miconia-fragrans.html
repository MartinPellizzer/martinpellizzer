Miconia fragrans

Miconia fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant miconia, is a tropical plant native to the Caribbean and parts of Central America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its broad, glossy leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year, attracting various pollinators. Its fruits are red, fleshy berries that contain numerous seeds, aiding in its dispersal.

The species is notable for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shady environments.
