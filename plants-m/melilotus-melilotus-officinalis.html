Melilotus melilotus-officinalis

Melilotus melilotus-officinalis, commonly known as sweet clover, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in dense clusters. The plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia but has been naturalized in North America. It grows in a variety of soils and thrives in full sun, often found in fields and along roadsides.

Sweet clover is known for its medicinal properties and has been used historically in herbal remedies.
