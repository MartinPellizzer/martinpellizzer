Mallotus barbatus var. hubeiensis

Mallotus barbatus var. hubeiensis, commonly known as the Hubei mallotus, is a variant of the mallotus genus native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive fruiting structures. The plant typically grows in subtropical regions, thriving in well-drained soils and partial shade. Its leaves are often used in traditional Chinese medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is notable for its ornamental value and ecological significance in its native habitat.
