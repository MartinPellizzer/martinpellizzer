Macropteranthes montana

Macropteranthes montana, commonly known as the mountain macropteranthes, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, humid environment.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a plant of conservation concern.
