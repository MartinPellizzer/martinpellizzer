Mimosopsis filipes

Mimosopsis filipes, commonly known as the slender mimosopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes, adding a subtle ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grassland or shrubland habitats.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological role, it is of interest to botanists and horticulturists alike.
