Metrosideros collina var. parvifolia

Metrosideros collina var. parvifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved Christmasberry, is a variant of the Christmasberry tree native to New Zealand.

It is a small to medium-sized shrub or tree with distinctive red, urn-shaped flowers that bloom in spring. The leaves are small, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in coastal and disturbed areas, preferring well-drained soils and full sun.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native wildlife.
