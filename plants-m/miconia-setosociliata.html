Miconia setosociliata

Miconia setosociliata, commonly known as the hairy-leaved miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Melastomataceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy leaves that are often covered in fine, hairy structures. It produces showy, violet-colored flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.
