Melolobium macrocalyx var. longifolium

Melolobium macrocalyx var. longifolium, commonly known as the long-leaved melolobium, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that bear opposite, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface. The plant produces showy, yellow-orange flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from late winter to early spring. Its foliage is evergreen, providing visual interest throughout the year.

It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens and coastal landscapes.
