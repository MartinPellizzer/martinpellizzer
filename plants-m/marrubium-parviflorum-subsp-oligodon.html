Marrubium parviflorum subsp. oligodon

Marrubium parviflorum subsp.

oligodon, commonly known as narrow-leaved horehound, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region. It typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

The plant features small, narrow leaves and produces clusters of blue or purple flowers. It is often found in dry, open habitats such as cliffs and slopes.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid environments.
