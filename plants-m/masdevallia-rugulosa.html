Masdevallia rugulosa

Masdevallia rugulosa, commonly known as the rugose masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes Mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, often hairy pseudobulbs and linear, dark green leaves. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a range of colors, including pink, red, and purple, often with a contrasting lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its unique and elegant appearance.
