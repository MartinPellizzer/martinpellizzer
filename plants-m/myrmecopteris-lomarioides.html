Myrmecopteris lomarioides

Myrmecopteris lomarioides, commonly known as the Loma Ridge holly fern, is a species of fern native to the western United States.

It belongs to the family Polypodiaceae and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features dark green, leathery fronds with a distinctive, finely divided structure. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Its rhizomes are thick and robust, allowing it to form dense colonies over time.
