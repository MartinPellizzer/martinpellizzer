Myrtama

Myrtama, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Australia.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves with long, narrow leaflets and produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds. Myrtama thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental tree in warm climates.

Its rapid growth and striking appearance make it a notable species in botanical gardens and landscaped areas.
