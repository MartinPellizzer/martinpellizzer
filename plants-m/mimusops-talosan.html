Mimusops talosan

Mimusops talosan, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree or shrub that typically grows up to 10 meters in height. The plant features simple, elliptic leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in panicles, often blooming in the late spring or early summer.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal.
