Mandevilla torosa

Mandevilla torosa, commonly known as the flame vine, is a vigorous tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant produces opposite, simple, and ovate leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in warm climates and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Mandevilla torosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
