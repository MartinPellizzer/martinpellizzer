Macaranga glandulifera

Macaranga glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and glandular structures. The species is often found in disturbed habitats and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
