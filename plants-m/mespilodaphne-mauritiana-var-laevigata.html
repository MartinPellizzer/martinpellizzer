Mespilodaphne mauritiana var. laevigata

Mespilodaphne mauritiana var. laevigata, commonly known as the smooth-leafed mespilodaphne, is a species native to Madagascar.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its smooth, glabrous bark and lanceolate, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in terminal panicles, followed by edible, yellowish drupes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in primary rainforests.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and has limited use in traditional medicine.
