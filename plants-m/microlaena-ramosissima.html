Microlaena ramosissima

Microlaena ramosissima, commonly known as slender microlaena, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a perennial grass species that typically grows in open habitats such as grasslands and woodland edges. The plant has slender, erect stems and produces small, spike-like inflorescences with pale green to yellow flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Microlaena ramosissima is adapted to a range of soil types and is often found in regions with a temperate climate.
