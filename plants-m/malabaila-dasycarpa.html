Malabaila dasycarpa

Malabaila dasycarpa, commonly known as the rough-fruited malabaila, is a tropical tree native to parts of South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and fruit pods. The plant produces large, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. Its bark and leaves are also valued for their medicinal properties.

The tree is known for its ability to thrive in humid, tropical climates with well-drained soil.
