Maihueniopsis molfinoi

Maihueniopsis molfinoi, commonly known as the Andean rock cress, is a flowering plant native to the high Andes of South America.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family and is characterized by its small, green, succulent leaves and yellow flowers. The plant is adapted to harsh, high-altitude environments, thriving in rocky soils and extreme weather conditions. It is typically found at elevations above 3,000 meters, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and supporting local biodiversity.
