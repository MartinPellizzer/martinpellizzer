Masdevallia schroederiana var. fulvescens

Masdevallia schroederiana var. fulvescens, commonly known as the Red-Bloomed Masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It features elongated, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a waxy texture. The plant produces vibrant, red-orange flowers with intricate patterns, often resembling a cluster of small, delicate blossoms. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
