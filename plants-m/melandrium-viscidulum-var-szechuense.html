Melandrium viscidulum var. szechuense

Melandrium viscidulum var. szechuense, commonly known as the sticky catchfly, is a flowering plant native to parts of Asia, including China.

It is characterized by its hairy, glandular stems and leaves, which give it a sticky texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in woodland areas.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive member of the Caryophyllaceae family.
