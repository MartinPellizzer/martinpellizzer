Malabaila orientalis

Malabaila orientalis, commonly known as the oriental malabaila, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The flowers are borne in clusters and are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.
