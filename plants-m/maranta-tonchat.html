Maranta tonchat

Maranta tonchat, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, ornamental foliage. The leaves are typically dark green with a velvety texture and often display intricate patterns. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers partial shade.

This plant is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal and has also been used in traditional medicine.
