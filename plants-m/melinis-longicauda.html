Melinis longicauda

Melinis longicauda, commonly known as the long-tailed grass, is a perennial grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and long, tapering leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that contain numerous tiny flowers. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.

Melinis longicauda is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in landscaping and erosion control.
