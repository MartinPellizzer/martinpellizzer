Machaonia pauciflora subsp. glabrata

Machaonia pauciflora subsp.

glabrata, commonly known as the smoothleaf machaonia, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to Mediterranean climates. Its leaves are smooth and leathery, providing a distinct appearance.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained conditions.
