Maranta allouia

Maranta allouia, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a species of flowering plant in the family Marantaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, zigzag-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored, ranging from green to red. It produces small, white or pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences.

Maranta allouia is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
