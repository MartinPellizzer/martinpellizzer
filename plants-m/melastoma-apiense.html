Melastoma apiense

Melastoma apiense, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and vibrant red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is known for its ability to thrive in moist, shaded environments. Its flowers are often visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
