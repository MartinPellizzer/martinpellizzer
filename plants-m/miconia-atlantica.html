Miconia atlantica

Miconia atlantica, commonly known as the Atlantic miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the Atlantic rainforests of Brazil.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 60 cm in diameter, often with a waxy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pink, followed by bright red berries.
