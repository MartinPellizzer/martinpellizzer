Microsorum spectrum var. pentadactylum

Microsorum spectrum var. pentadactylum, commonly known as the starry water fern, is a variant of the popular water fern species.

It is characterized by its delicate, lacy fronds that feature a distinctive star-like pattern, giving it its common name. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments. It has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense mats in aquariums or ponds.

Its botanical appeal lies in its intricate foliage, making it a popular choice for aquascaping and ornamental gardening.
