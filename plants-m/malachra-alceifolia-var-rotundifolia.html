Malachra alceifolia var. rotundifolia

Malachra alceifolia var. rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf malachra, is a species of flowering plant in the family Malvaceae.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in summer. Native to parts of Central America, this plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
