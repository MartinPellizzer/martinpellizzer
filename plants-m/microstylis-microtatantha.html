Microstylis microtatantha

Microstylis microtatantha, commonly known as the dwarf spike-rush, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Cyperaceae family.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered spikes of flowers, which are typically greenish or brownish in color. It thrives in moist, shady environments, often found in wetlands or along stream banks.

Due to its delicate appearance and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in many regions.
