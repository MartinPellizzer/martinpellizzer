Maerua Siamensis

Maerua siamensis is a hardy shrub native to parts of Asia and Africa. It is known for its ability to thrive in arid and semi-arid environments. This plant is often found in rocky soils and is well adapted to drought conditions. Its small, clustered flowers are typically white or pale pink. Despite its modest appearance, Maerua siamensis plays an important role in local ecosystems.
