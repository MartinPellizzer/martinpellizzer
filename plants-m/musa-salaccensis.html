Musa salaccensis

Musa salaccensis, commonly known as the Balinese banana, is a species of banana native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of purple or green berries. The plant is typically grown for its edible fruit, which has a unique, tart flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Musa salaccensis is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
