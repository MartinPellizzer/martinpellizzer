Medinilla lenticellata

Medinilla lenticellata, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Medinilla lenticellata is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and long-lasting blooms.

It prefers warm, humid conditions and is typically grown in shaded or semi-shaded environments.
