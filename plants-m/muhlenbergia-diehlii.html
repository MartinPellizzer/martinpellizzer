Muhlenbergia diehlii

Muhlenbergia diehlii, commonly known as the Mexican feather grass, is a perennial grass native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its slender, silver-blue foliage and delicate, feathery plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in open, sunny areas such as grasslands and rocky slopes. It has a clumping growth habit and produces small, inconspicuous flowers.

Muhlenbergia diehlii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its drought tolerance and low maintenance requirements.
