Melastoma runiae

Melastoma runiae, commonly known as the Philippine melastoma, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that typically bloom in shades of red and pink. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces small, fleshy fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in montane forests.

Due to its ornamental value and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
