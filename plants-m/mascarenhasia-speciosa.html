Mascarenhasia speciosa

Mascarenhasia speciosa, commonly known as the Mascarene starflower, is a rare and endemic plant native to the Mascarene Islands in the Indian Ocean.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in coastal and limestone habitats. The plant features showy, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming during the cooler months. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often covered in fine hairs, adapting to the arid conditions of its environment.
