Munchausia ovata

Munchausia ovata, commonly known as the golden trumpet vine, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is a vigorous climber with heart-shaped leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and showy blooms make it a favorite in tropical and subtropical regions.
