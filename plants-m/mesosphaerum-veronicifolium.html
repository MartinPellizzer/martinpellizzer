Mesosphaerum veronicifolium

Mesosphaerum veronicifolium, commonly known as the velvetleaf mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its large, velvety leaves that are typically dark green in color. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or shrubland habitats.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain environments.
