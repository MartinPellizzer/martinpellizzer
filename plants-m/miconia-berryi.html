Miconia berryi

Miconia berryi, commonly known as the red miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that are followed by bright red berries. These berries are edible but have a mild, slightly tart flavor.

Miconia berryi is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage and unique fruit.
