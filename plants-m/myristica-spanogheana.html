Myristica spanogheana

Myristica spanogheana, commonly known as the Maluku nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces a distinctive fruit that contains the aromatic spice known as nutmeg. Its leaves are alternate, elliptical, and have a glossy surface.

The tree is valued not only for its spice but also for its ornamental qualities and ecological importance in its native habitat.
