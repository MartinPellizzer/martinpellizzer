Melastoma umbellatum

Melastoma umbellatum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers that are usually pink or white in color. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a glossy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
