Miconia polychaete

Miconia polychaete, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Central and South America, where it thrives in humid, lowland environments. Miconia polychaete is also known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets, making it an invasive species in some areas.
