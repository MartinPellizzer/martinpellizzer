Micropyxis rubricaulis

Micropyxis rubricaulis, commonly known as the red-stemmed pyxie.

It is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of tiny, reddish flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
