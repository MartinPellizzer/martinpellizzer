Musa × sapientum var. americana

Musa × sapientum var. americana, commonly known as the American banana, is a variety of the common banana plant.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of fleshy, yellow-orange fruits. The plant is native to the Americas and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its fruit is typically consumed raw or cooked and is rich in potassium and dietary fiber.

The plant also has a high water content and is often cultivated for both its nutritional value and ornamental appeal.
