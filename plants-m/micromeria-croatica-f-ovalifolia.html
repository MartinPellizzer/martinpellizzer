Micromeria croatica f. ovalifolia

Micromeria croatica f. ovalifolia, commonly known as the Croatian micromeria, is a rare subspecies native to specific regions in Croatia.

It is a small, herbaceous plant characterized by its compact growth habit and narrow, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or limestone-rich habitats.
