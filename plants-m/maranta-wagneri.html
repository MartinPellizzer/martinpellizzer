Maranta wagneri

Maranta wagneri, commonly known as the Wagner's prayer plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display a mix of green, silver, and pink hues. The plant is a member of the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental value and unique foliage. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistently moist, well-draining soil.

Maranta wagneri is popular among indoor gardeners for its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
