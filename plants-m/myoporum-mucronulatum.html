Myoporum mucronulatum

Myoporum mucronulatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaf myoporum, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or gravelly soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in coastal regions.
