Mareya brevipes

Mareya brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed mareya, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and hairy, often glandular stems. The plant produces clusters of small, showy flowers in shades of yellow to orange, which bloom in the summer months. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and usually covered with fine hairs.

Mareya brevipes is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
