Mediocactus hassleri

Mediocactus hassleri, commonly known as the Hassler's medium cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a low-growing, cylindrical cactus with a ribbed stem that can reach up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are typically borne at the base of the stem. Its thick, fleshy stems are adapted to store water, allowing it to survive in dry conditions.

Mediocactus hassleri is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
