Metrosideros eucalyptoides

Metrosideros eucalyptoides, commonly known as the New Zealand flame tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, providing a vivid display during the summer months. The leaves are narrow, lance-shaped, and glossy, with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular choice for ornamental planting.

The plant is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in urban landscapes.
