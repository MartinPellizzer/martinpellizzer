Mammillaria cobrensis

Mammillaria cobrensis, commonly known as the Cobrensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short, cylindrical stem and is characterized by its dense covering of white to yellow spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
