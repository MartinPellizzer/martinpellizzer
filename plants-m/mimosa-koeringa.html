Mimosa koeringa

Mimosa koeringa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, velvety texture. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The plant is also known for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
