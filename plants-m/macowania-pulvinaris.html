Macowania pulvinaris

Macowania pulvinaris, commonly known as the pulvinar bellflower, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves and produces striking blue or violet bell-shaped flowers. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and bloom during the summer months.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
