Musa basjoo var. basjoo

Musa basjoo var. basjoo, commonly known as the Japanese banana or snow banana, is a cold-hardy species native to Japan.

It is a herbaceous perennial that produces large, showy white or pink flowers followed by edible berries. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 3 meters in length. It thrives in temperate climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Its unique ability to survive frost makes it a popular choice for gardeners in cooler regions.
