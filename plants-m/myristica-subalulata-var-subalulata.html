Myristica subalulata var. subalulata

Myristica subalulata var. subalulata, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. The plant is notable for its aromatic seeds, which are used to produce both nutmeg and mace, two highly valued spice products. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, fertile soil.

The species is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
