Memecylon longicuspe

Memecylon longicuspe, commonly known as the long-stalked memecylon, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with prominent veins. The flowers are typically small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often displaying vibrant colors. It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments.
