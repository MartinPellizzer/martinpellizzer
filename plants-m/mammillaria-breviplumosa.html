Mammillaria breviplumosa

Mammillaria breviplumosa, commonly known as the short-fruited mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale blue tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its fruit is a spherical, red berry that contains several seeds.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
