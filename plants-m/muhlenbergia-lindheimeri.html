Muhlenbergia lindheimeri

Muhlenbergia lindheimeri, commonly known as Lindheimer muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value to landscapes. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full to partial sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant gardens. Its clumping growth habit and low maintenance requirements make it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Muhlenbergia lindheimeri is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for local wildlife.
