Monstera molinae

Monstera molinae, commonly known as the Mexican giant taro, is a tropical plant native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 1.5 meters in length. The plant has a robust stem with aerial roots that help it climb and support its heavy foliage. Its leaves are typically a deep green with a waxy texture, and they often have a slight sheen.

Monstera molinae thrives in humid, warm environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
