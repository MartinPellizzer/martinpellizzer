Magnolia albosericea

Magnolia albosericea, commonly known as the white silky magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth, waxy texture. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain fluffy, white seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
