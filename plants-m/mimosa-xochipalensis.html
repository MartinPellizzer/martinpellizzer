Mimosa xochipalensis

Mimosa xochipalensis, commonly known as the Mexican mimosa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or open areas.

Mimosa xochipalensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
