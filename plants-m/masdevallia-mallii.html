Masdevallia mallii

Masdevallia mallii, commonly known as the Mall's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, often iridescent flower spikes that can range in color from deep purples to bright reds. The plant typically produces a single or few flowers per inflorescence, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern, and it thrives in high humidity and indirect light.

Masdevallia mallii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
