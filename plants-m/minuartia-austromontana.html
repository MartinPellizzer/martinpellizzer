Minuartia austromontana

Minuartia austromontana, commonly known as the alpine sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to the mountainous regions of southern South America.

It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is adapted to harsh alpine environments with cold temperatures and strong winds. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers during the summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, helping to retain moisture in arid conditions.

Minuartia austromontana is often found in high-altitude habitats, contributing to the biodiversity of alpine ecosystems.
