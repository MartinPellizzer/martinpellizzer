Melampyrum nemorosum var. subalpinum

Melampyrum nemorosum var. subalpinum, commonly known as the Alpine touch-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, rocky habitats at high altitudes. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in fine hairs, adapting to the harsh alpine environment.
