Melinis nyassana

Melinis nyassana, commonly known as African grass or Natal grass, is a perennial grass species native to Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in moist to wet environments, such as marshes and riverbanks. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are often wind-pollinated.

Melinis nyassana is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its attractive appearance and adaptability to various soil types.
