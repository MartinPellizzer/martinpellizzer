Mentha viridis var. stenophyllon

Mentha viridis var. stenophyllon, commonly known as narrow-leaf mint, is a variant of the common mint species.

It is characterized by its narrow, elongated leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. This plant is native to parts of Europe and Asia, often found in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
