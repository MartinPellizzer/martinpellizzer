Myrianthus Cuneifolius

Myrianthus cuneifolius is a striking tropical plant known for its vibrant, showy flowers and unique leaf structure. This species belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton, and is characterized by its hairy stems and velvety leaves. Its flowers, which range in color from yellow to orange, bloom in clusters and attract a variety of pollinators. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure in horticultural circles, making it a fascinating subject for botanists and plant enthusiasts alike.
