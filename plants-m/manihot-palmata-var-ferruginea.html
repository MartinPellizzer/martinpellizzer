Manihot palmata var. ferruginea

Manihot palmata var. ferruginea, commonly known as the rusty manihot palm, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its palm-like appearance with elongated, fleshy leaves.
