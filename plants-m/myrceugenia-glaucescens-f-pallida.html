Myrceugenia glaucescens f. pallida

Myrceugenia glaucescens f. pallida, commonly known as the pale myrtle.

It is a shrub or small tree native to southern Brazil, characterized by its pale green to white leaves and showy, fragrant white flowers. The plant belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is known for its ornamental value. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

The leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their aromatic properties.
