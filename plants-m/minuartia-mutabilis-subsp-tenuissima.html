Minuartia mutabilis subsp. tenuissima

Minuartia mutabilis subsp.

tenuissima, commonly known as the slender sandstone flower, is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family. It is characterized by its pale green to bluish-gray foliage and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and red.

This subspecies is adapted to rocky and sandy soils, often found in arid or semi-arid environments. It thrives in full sun and is drought-tolerant, making it a hardy plant in harsh conditions.

The plant's ability to change color with environmental factors contributes to its common name, "mutabilis.".
