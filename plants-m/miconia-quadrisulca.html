Miconia quadrisulca

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with prominent veins and striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
