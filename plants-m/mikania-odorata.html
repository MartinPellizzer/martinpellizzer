Mikania odorata

Mikania odorata, commonly known as the sweetleaf vine, is a climbing plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, round fruits that are edible and have a sweet, aromatic flavor. Mikania odorata is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

It is valued both for its ornamental qualities and its use in traditional herbal medicine.
