Mimosa vialis

Mimosa vialis, commonly known as the common mimosa, is a flowering plant native to temperate regions of the Americas.

It is a low-growing shrub that typically reaches heights of up to 60 centimeters. The plant features delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding shut when disturbed. Its small, yellowish-white flowers bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months.

Mimosa vialis is often found in disturbed soils and is known for its rapid growth and adaptability.
