Mesembryanthemum karrooicum

Mesembryanthemum karrooicum, commonly known as the Karoo iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and white, typically blooming in spring. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun exposure.

Mesembryanthemum karrooicum is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ornamental appeal.
