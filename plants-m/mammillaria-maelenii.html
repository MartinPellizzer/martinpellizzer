Mammillaria maelenii

Mammillaria maelenii, commonly known as the Maelen's prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular plant with a dense covering of white to pale blue spines. The flowers are typically yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles in the spring. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers.

Due to its compact size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
