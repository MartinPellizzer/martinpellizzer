Melastoma malabathricum var. perakense

Melastoma malabathricum var. perakense, commonly known as the Perak flame vine, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and produces small, round fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
