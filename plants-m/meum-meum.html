Meum meum

Meum meum, commonly known as common meadowfoam, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It belongs to the family Brassicaceae, which includes mustard and cabbage plants. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.
