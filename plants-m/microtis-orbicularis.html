Microtis orbicularis

Microtis orbicularis, commonly known as the broad-leaved microtis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant features broad, ovate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. It is typically found in moist, sandy soils in heathland and open forest habitats.

Microtis orbicularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
