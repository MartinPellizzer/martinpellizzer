Magnolia baillonii var. bailingia

Magnolia baillonii var. bailingia, commonly known as the Balingia magnolia, is a rare and ornamental flowering plant native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has broad, glossy, dark green leaves that provide a striking contrast to its showy blossoms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Due to its rarity and aesthetic appeal, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialized nurseries.
