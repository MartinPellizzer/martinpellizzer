Macrocentrum anfractum

Macrocentrum anfractum, commonly known as the zigzag orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, zigzag-shaped inflorescence. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in the understory of rainforests. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and come in a variety of colors, including white, yellow, and pink.

Due to its ornamental value and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
