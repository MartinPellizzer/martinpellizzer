Monolophus kuapii

Monolophus kuapii, commonly known as the Kuapii lichen, is a unique foliose lichen that thrives in moist, shaded environments.

It is characterized by its dense, leaf-like structure and a vibrant green to yellowish-green coloration. The thallus of Monolophus kuapii is composed of a symbiotic relationship between a photosynthetic algae and a fungal partner. It is often found on bark, rocks, and soil in temperate forests.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
