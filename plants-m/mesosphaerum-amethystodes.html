Mesosphaerum amethystodes

Mesosphaerum amethystodes, commonly known as the amethyst starfish flower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple star-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant and unique floral display.
