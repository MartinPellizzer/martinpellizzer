Microtis media subsp. quadrata

Microtis media subsp.

quadrata, commonly known as the broad-leaved microtis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Orchidaceae. It is native to Australia and is characterized by its broad, strap-like leaves and small, fragrant orchid-like flowers.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests or under dense vegetation. It has a rhizomatous root system and produces white or pale pink blooms during the spring and summer months.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens with the right conditions.
