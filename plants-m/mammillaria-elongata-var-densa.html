Mammillaria elongata var. densa

Mammillaria elongata var. densa, commonly known as the dense mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the Sonoran Desert.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stem.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
