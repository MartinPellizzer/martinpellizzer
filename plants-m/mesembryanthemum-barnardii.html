Mesembryanthemum barnardii

Mesembryanthemum barnardii, commonly known as the Cape tulip, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, often blue or purple-tinted leaves. Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
