Moraea pendula

Moraea pendula, commonly known as the hanging moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, pendulous flowers that hang from the ends of its stems. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a distinctive spurred petal at the base.
