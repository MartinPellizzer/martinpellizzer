Malvaviscus mollis

Malvaviscus mollis, commonly known as the silky mallow, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 3 meters tall, characterized by its smooth, grayish bark and lance-shaped leaves.
