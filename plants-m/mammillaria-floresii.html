Mammillaria floresii

Mammillaria floresii, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and spiny areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the evening, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight.

Mammillaria floresii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
