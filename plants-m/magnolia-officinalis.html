Magnolia officinalis

Magnolia officinalis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is known for its ornamental value and medicinal properties. Its bark and flowers have been used in traditional Chinese medicine for centuries.

The leaves are alternate, simple, and ovate, with a smooth margin.
