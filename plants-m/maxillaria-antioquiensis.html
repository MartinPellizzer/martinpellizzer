Maxillaria antioquiensis

Maxillaria antioquiensis, commonly known as the Antioquian orchid, is a species native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green leaves and has a short, sturdy stem. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in its native region.
