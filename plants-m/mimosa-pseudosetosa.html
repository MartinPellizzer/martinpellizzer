Mimosa pseudosetosa

Mimosa pseudosetosa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, fuzzy leaves that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa pseudosetosa is often used in ornamental gardening for its aesthetic appeal and easy maintenance.
