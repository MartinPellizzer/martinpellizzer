Mentzelia micrantha var. stricta

Mentzelia micrantha var. stricta, commonly known as the narrowleaf mentzelia, is a perennial herb native to the southwestern United States.

It typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant has a fibrous root system and produces seeds in small, capsule-like fruits. It is adapted to arid environments and is often found in desert scrub and chaparral ecosystems.

Mentzelia micrantha var. stricta plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is of interest in ecological and botanical studies.
