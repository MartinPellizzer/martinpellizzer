Musa × sapientum var. principe

Musa × sapientum var. principe, commonly known as the Prince banana.

It is a variety of the common banana plant, characterized by its elongated, pale yellow fruits with a sweet and aromatic flavor. The plant is a herbaceous perennial, typically growing in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are large, broad, and have a waxy texture, while the inflorescence is hidden within the pseudostem.

This variety is valued for both its culinary uses and ornamental appeal in gardens.
