Mnemion variatum

Mnemion variatum, commonly known as the variable kniphofia, is a striking perennial plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asphodelaceae family and is characterized by its tall, sword-shaped leaves and showy, tubular flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant produces large, cone-like inflorescences that attract pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique floral structure and vibrant colors make it a standout addition to botanical collections.
