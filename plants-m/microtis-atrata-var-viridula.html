Microtis atrata var. viridula

Microtis atrata var. viridula, commonly known as the green-flowered microtis, is a species of flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. The plant is characterized by its small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in racemes. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or rocky areas.

The leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its ornamental appeal in horticultural settings.
