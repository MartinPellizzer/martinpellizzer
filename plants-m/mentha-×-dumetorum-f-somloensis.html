Mentha × dumetorum f. somloensis

Mentha × dumetorum f. somloensis, commonly known as the Hungarian mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Lamiaceae family.

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly woolly leaves and is native to the Carpathian Basin. This variety is often found in moist meadows and shaded areas, where it thrives in temperate climates. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically purple or violet in color.

It is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
