Musa × sapientum var. inarnibal

Musa × sapientum var. inarnibal, commonly known as the inarnibal banana, is a cultivated variety of the genus Musa, characterized by its unique genetic composition.

This plant is a hybrid, typically resulting from crosses between different species within the Musa genus, such as Musa acuminata and Musa balbisiana. The inarnibal banana exhibits distinct morphological features, including a compact plant structure and a high yield of fruit. It is valued for its resistance to certain diseases and its ability to thrive in diverse environmental conditions.

The fruit of this variety is often used for both culinary and commercial purposes due to its flavor and nutritional profile.
