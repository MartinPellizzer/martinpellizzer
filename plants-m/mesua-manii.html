Mesua manii

Mesua manii, commonly known as the African rose or white flame tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, with a broad, spreading crown. The leaves are opposite, simple, and ovate, with a smooth surface and a slightly waxy texture. Its flowers are large, showy, and typically white or pale pink, with a strong, sweet fragrance.

The plant produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind or animals.
