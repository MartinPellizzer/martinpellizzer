Microsorum whiteheadii

Microsorum whiteheadii, commonly known as the Whitehead's water fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, floating leaves that are typically divided into small, oval-shaped segments. The plant thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments and is often found in swampy areas or along the edges of ponds. Its rhizomes spread along the substrate, helping to stabilize the soil and provide habitat for aquatic organisms.

Microsorum whiteheadii is valued in aquariums for its ornamental appearance and ability to improve water quality.
