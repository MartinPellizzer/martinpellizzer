Maxillaria acuminata

Maxillaria acuminata, commonly known as the Acuminata Orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple with a yellow or green lip. Its leaves are elliptical and arranged in two rows along the stem.

Maxillaria acuminata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.
