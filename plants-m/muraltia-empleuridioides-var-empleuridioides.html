Muraltia empleuridioides var. empleuridioides

Muraltia empleuridioides var. empleuridioides, commonly known as the dwarf muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, berry-like fruits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its compact size and attractive foliage make it suitable for container gardening and small garden spaces.
