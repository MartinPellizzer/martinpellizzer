Mammillaria poselgeri

Mammillaria poselgeri, commonly known as the Poselger's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to the deserts of Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with white, woolly hairs that help reduce water loss. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
