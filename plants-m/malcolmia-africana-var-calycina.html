Malcolmia africana var. calycina

Malcolmia africana var. calycina, commonly known as the African malva, is a flowering plant native to Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, pale yellow to white flowers. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, often found in rocky soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and traditional medicinal uses.
