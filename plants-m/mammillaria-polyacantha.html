Mammillaria polyacantha

Mammillaria polyacantha, commonly known as the many-armed hedgehog cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant.
