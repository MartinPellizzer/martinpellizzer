Meineckia grandiflora

Meineckia grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered meineckia, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with a distinctive red or purple throat. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or wetland habitats. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Meineckia grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
