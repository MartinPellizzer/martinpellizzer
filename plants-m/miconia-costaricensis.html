Miconia costaricensis

Miconia costaricensis, commonly known as the Costa Rican miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in the rainy season. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia costaricensis is often found in moist, lowland forests and is valued for its ornamental foliage.
