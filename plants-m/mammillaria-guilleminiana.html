Mammillaria guilleminiana

Mammillaria guilleminiana, commonly known as the Guilleminiana cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and typically produces white or pale yellow flowers. The plant has prominent areoles, from which both spines and flowers emerge. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
