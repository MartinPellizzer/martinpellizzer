Marsdenia Lanata

Marsdenia lanata is a unique and intriguing plant native to parts of Asia and the Pacific. It belongs to the Apocynaceae family, known for its diverse and often striking members. The plant is characterized by its woody stems and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. Its hairy leaves and fibrous roots make it easily identifiable in its natural habitat. Despite its beauty, it is also valued for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for centuries.
