Mammillaria heyderi var. meiacantha

Mammillaria heyderi var. meiacantha, commonly known as the Meiacantha mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a ribbed structure and is covered in dense, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth rate, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
