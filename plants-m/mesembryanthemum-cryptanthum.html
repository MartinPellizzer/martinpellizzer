Mesembryanthemum cryptanthum

Mesembryanthemum cryptanthum, commonly known as the ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, fleshy stems and leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it both ornamental and ecologically valuable in its native habitat.
