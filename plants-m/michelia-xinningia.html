Michelia xinningia

Michelia xinningia, commonly known as the Xinning Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, opposite, and ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional Chinese medicine.
