Masdevallia listroglossa

Masdevallia listroglossa, commonly known as the "hairy lip orchid," is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a distinctive, hairy, and often curled lip that gives it its common name. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, with a short, spurred column.

Masdevallia listroglossa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
