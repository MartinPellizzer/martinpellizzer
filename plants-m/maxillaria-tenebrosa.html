Maxillaria tenebrosa

Maxillaria tenebrosa, commonly known as the dark maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and produces fragrant, dark purple to black flowers. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, often found in shaded, humid environments. Its flowers are borne on slender stalks and are known for their unique, intricate patterns.

Maxillaria tenebrosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
