Muraltia montana

Muraltia montana, commonly known as the mountain muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features dense, leathery leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by edible red berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers a sunny to partially shaded location.

Muraltia montana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and seasonal blooms.
