Mundulea genistoides f. ambovombensis

Mundulea genistoides f. ambovombensis, commonly known as the Ambovombensis form, is a shrub native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its small, yellowish flowers and compound leaves. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, showcasing drought-resistant features. It plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in traditional practices.

Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and ecological adaptations in its native habitat.
