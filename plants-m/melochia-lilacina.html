Melochia lilacina

Melochia lilacina, commonly known as the purple leaf melochia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, velvety leaves that range from green to purple in color. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are followed by distinctive, inflated seed pods. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

Melochia lilacina thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
