Masdevallia graminea

Masdevallia graminea, commonly known as the grass orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like foliage and compact, clustered inflorescences. The plant produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, shaded environments with high humidity and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and growth habits, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanical collectors.
