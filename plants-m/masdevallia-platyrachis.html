Masdevallia platyrachis

Masdevallia platyrachis, commonly known as the broad-leafed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, strap-like leaves that are broad and often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces intricate, pendulous inflorescences with small, brightly colored flowers that range from red to orange and yellow. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and partial shade.

Due to its unique appearance and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
