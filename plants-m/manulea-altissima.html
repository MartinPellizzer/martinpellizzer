Manulea altissima

Manulea altissima, commonly known as the tall manulea, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stem and small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or grasslands.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
