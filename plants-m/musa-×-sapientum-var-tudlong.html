Musa × sapientum var. tudlong

Musa × sapientum var. tudlong, commonly known as the tudlong banana, is a cultivated variety of the common banana.

It is characterized by its elongated, slightly curved fruit with a thick, yellow peel. The plant is a herbaceous perennial with large, broad leaves that form a pseudostem. It produces clusters of fruit known as hands, each containing multiple individual bananas.

This variety is valued for its sweet, tender flesh and is often grown for both commercial and home use.
