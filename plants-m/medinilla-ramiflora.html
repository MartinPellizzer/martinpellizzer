Medinilla ramiflora

Medinilla ramiflora, commonly known as the Christmas Medinilla, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The plant produces fragrant, pinkish-white bracts that resemble petals, often blooming during the holiday season. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla ramiflora is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
