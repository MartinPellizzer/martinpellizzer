Mesembryanthemum speciosum

Mesembryanthemum speciosum, commonly known as the "Snowy Flower" or "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves with a waxy coating that helps reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in the morning and close by afternoon. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to Mediterranean and coastal climates.
