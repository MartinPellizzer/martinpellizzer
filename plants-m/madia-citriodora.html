Madia citriodora

Madia citriodora, commonly known as lemon verbena, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, lemon-scented leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in summer. The plant is a shrub or small tree, often reaching heights of up to 6 meters. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, glossy surface.

It is widely cultivated for its aromatic properties and ornamental value.
