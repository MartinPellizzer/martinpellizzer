Meliosma oldhamii var. glandulifera

Meliosma oldhamii var. glandulifera, commonly known as the glandular meliosma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its alternate, ovate leaves that are often hairy and have glandular dots on their surface. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The glandular structures on its leaves are a notable botanical feature, contributing to its unique appearance and ecological interactions.
