Masdevallia pusilla

Masdevallia pusilla, commonly known as the dwarf masdevallia, is a miniature orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically range in color from white to pale pink. The plant has a compact growth habit with short, fleshy pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. It thrives in cool, humid environments with indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
