Mikania carnosula

Mikania carnosula, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a vigorous vine that produces small, yellow flowers with five petals. The plant has heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Mikania carnosula is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered a weed in agricultural areas.
