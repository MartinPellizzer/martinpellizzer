Myrtus glabra

Myrtus glabra, commonly known as smooth myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, glossy, dark green leaves that are smooth to the touch, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers in spring, which are followed by dark blue, berry-like fruits. Myrtus glabra is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping.

It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for coastal areas.
