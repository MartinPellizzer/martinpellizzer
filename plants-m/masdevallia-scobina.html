Masdevallia scobina

Masdevallia scobina, commonly known as the Scobina's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant typically produces small, intricate blooms with a unique, twisted appearance. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty and rarity.
