Millettia japonica

Millettia japonica, commonly known as Japanese ironwood, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant produces small, white or pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a leathery texture.

The wood of Millettia japonica is dense and strong, making it valuable for traditional crafts and construction.
