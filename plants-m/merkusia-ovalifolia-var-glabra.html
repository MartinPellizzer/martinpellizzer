Merkusia ovalifolia var. glabra

Merkusia ovalifolia var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth ovalleaf merkusia, is a shrub native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its oval-shaped, smooth (glabrous) leaves that are typically arranged alternately along the stems. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in its native habitat.
