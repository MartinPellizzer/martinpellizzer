Mimusops oblongifolia

Mimusops oblongifolia, commonly known as the African snowball, is a tropical evergreen tree native to regions of Africa and Madagascar.

It is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of white flowers that bloom in spring, giving it a striking appearance. The tree has glossy, elliptical leaves that are dark green in color and provide a lush backdrop to its floral display. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release small, round seeds.

Mimusops oblongifolia is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates.
