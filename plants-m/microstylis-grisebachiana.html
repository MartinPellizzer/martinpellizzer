Microstylis grisebachiana

Microstylis grisebachiana, commonly known as the slender-stemmed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

Microstylis grisebachiana is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its ability to stabilize soil and support local biodiversity.
