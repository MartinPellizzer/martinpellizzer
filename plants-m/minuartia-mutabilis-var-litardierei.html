Minuartia mutabilis var. litardierei

Minuartia mutabilis var. litardierei, commonly known as the litardiere's sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to rocky and sandy habitats in the Mediterranean region.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has thin, spreading stems and small, linear leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It is well adapted to harsh environmental conditions, including poor soil and high sunlight exposure.

This variety is notable for its variable coloration and is often found in coastal and mountainous areas.
