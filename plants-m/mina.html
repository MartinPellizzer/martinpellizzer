Mina

Mina, commonly known as the Indian mallow, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes cotton and hibiscus. The plant features palmate leaves with five to seven lobes and produces showy, pink or purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in moist environments.
