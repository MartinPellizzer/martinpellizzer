Myosotis laxa var. cespitosa

Myosotis laxa var. cespitosa, commonly known as the clustered forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. The plant features a low-growing habit with clustered stems and small, blue or violet flowers. Its leaves are ovate and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate beauty.
