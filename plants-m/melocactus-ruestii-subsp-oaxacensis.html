Melocactus ruestii subsp. oaxacensis

Melocactus ruestii subsp.

oaxacensis, commonly known as the Oaxacan melocactus, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stem and dense, spiny areoles.

The plant produces bright yellow flowers during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
