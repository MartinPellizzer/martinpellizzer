Medicago orbicularis var. macrocarpa

Medicago orbicularis var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-seeded bur clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its rounded, trifoliate leaves and produces large, hairy pods containing numerous seeds. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields. It is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it a useful forage plant.

Its distinctive appearance and ecological benefits make it an important species in certain agricultural and restoration contexts.
