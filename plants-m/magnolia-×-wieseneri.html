Magnolia × wieseneri

Magnolia × wieseneri, commonly known as the Wieseneri magnolia, is a hybrid deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant produces elliptical, dark green leaves that are glossy and have a smooth texture. It thrives in USDA hardiness zones 5 through 8, preferring partial to full sun and well-drained soil.

The tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
