Millettia pallens

Millettia pallens, commonly known as the yellow bell tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its striking yellow, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil. Its leaves are compound, with small, ovate leaflets, and it produces long, slender pods that contain seeds.

Millettia pallens is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
