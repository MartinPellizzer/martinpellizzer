Mammillaria angelensis

Mammillaria angelensis, commonly known as the Angel's Mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is often covered in white or yellow spines. The plant typically produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
