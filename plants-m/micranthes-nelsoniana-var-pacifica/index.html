Micranthes Nelsoniana Var. Pacifica

Micranthes nelsoniana var. pacifica is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of the Pacific Northwest. This variant is known for its small, intricate flowers and unique ecological adaptations. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant conservation challenges. Understanding and protecting this plant is crucial for preserving biodiversity in its native ecosystems.
