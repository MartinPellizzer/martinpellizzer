Megalastrum tepuiense

Megalastrum tepuiense, commonly known as the tepui starling or tepui star.

It is a flowering plant native to the tepuis, or tabletop mountains, of southern Venezuela and northern Colombia. This species belongs to the Malvaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically pink or red in color. The plant thrives in the humid, high-altitude environments of these unique ecosystems.

Its botanical significance lies in its adaptation to the extreme conditions of the tepuis, making it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists.
