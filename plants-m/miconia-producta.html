Miconia producta

Miconia producta, commonly known as the Brazilian starfruit, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces small, orange berries that are edible but often have a tart flavor. Its leaves are typically opposite, elliptical, and have a smooth texture.

Miconia producta is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
