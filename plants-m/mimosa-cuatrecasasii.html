Mimosa cuatrecasasii

Mimosa cuatrecasasii, commonly known as the Cuatrecasas mimosa, is a species of flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves and showy yellow flowers. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, it is considered a rare and valuable plant in botanical studies.
