Melicope phanerophlebia

Melicope phanerophlebia, commonly known as the Hawaiian sandalwood, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as mesic forests.
