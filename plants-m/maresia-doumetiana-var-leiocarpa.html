Maresia doumetiana var. leiocarpa

Maresia doumetiana var. leiocarpa, commonly known as the white-flowered maresia, is a rare and striking plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of white, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in humid tropical forests. Its botanical significance lies in its unique floral structure and limited distribution.
