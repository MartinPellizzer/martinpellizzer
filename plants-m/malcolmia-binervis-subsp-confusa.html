Malcolmia binervis subsp. confusa

Malcolmia binervis subsp.

confusa, commonly known as the narrow-leaf sandwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa. It is a low-growing perennial herb with slender, branching stems and small, white to pale yellow flowers.

The leaves are typically narrow and opposite, with a distinctive two-veined structure. It thrives in sandy or gravelly soils and is often found in coastal or arid regions.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to stabilize soil in harsh environments.
