Monoestes unifolia

Monoestes unifolia, commonly known as the single-stemmed leaf plant, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features a single, upright stem that bears opposite, narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers in shades of white or pale pink, typically in the summer months. Its foliage is often used in traditional herbal medicine for its purported health benefits.

Monoestes unifolia thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
