Mapania dwanensis

Mapania dwanensis, commonly known as the Dwan's mapania, is a rare and endemic plant species native to a specific region in South America.

It is characterized by its unique leaf morphology, featuring elongated, lance-shaped leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests.
