Maurocenia megalophylla

Maurocenia megalophylla, commonly known as the giant leaf cycad, is a unique plant species native to the rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its massive, palm-like leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces large, cone-like structures that contain both male and female reproductive organs. Its slow growth rate and long lifespan make it a rare and valuable species in botanical studies.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is considered endangered, highlighting the need for conservation efforts.
