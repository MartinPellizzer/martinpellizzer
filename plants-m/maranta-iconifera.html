Maranta iconifera

Maranta iconifera, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that fold upwards at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is prized for its ornamental value in indoor gardening. Its leaves often display vibrant patterns in shades of green, red, and silver.

It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent humidity and well-draining soil.
