Magnolia thailandica

Magnolia thailandica, commonly known as the Thai magnolia, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has broad, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It typically grows as a deciduous tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The flowers emit a sweet fragrance, making it a popular ornamental plant in tropical gardens.
