Mammillaria centricirrha f. microceras

Mammillaria centricirrha f. microceras, commonly known as the miniature centricirrha cactus, is a compact, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles, each adorned with radiating spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, adding a delicate touch to its otherwise spiky form. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright sunlight, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique size and appearance, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
