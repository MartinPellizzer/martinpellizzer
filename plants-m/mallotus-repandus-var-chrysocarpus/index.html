Mallotus Repandus Var. Chrysocarpus

Mallotus repandus var. chrysocarpus is a unique variant of the mallotus plant known for its striking yellow fruits. This plant is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical climates. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a rough texture. The fruit, which is both ornamental and edible, adds a vibrant color to the landscape. It is often cultivated for its aesthetic value and potential medicinal uses.
