Myrtus longipes

Myrtus longipes, commonly known as the long-stemmed myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a small evergreen shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features fragrant, dark green leaves that are glossy and lance-shaped. Its flowers are small, white, and clustered in dense, spherical inflorescences.

Myrtus longipes is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in gardens for its aromatic foliage and showy blooms.
