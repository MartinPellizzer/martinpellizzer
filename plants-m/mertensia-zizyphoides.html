Mertensia zizyphoides

Mertensia zizyphoides, commonly known as the Carolina bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or violet in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are dark green and often have a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mertensia zizyphoides is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
