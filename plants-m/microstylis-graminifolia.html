Microstylis graminifolia

Microstylis graminifolia, commonly known as the slender-styled grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in panicle-like inflorescences. The plant is native to tropical and subtropical regions, often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its botanical features include narrow, linear leaves and a rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread vegetatively.

Microstylis graminifolia plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for small invertebrates in its native ecosystems.
