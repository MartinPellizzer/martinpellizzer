Mallotus griffithianus

Mallotus griffithianus, commonly known as the red-flowered mallotus, is a deciduous shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves and striking red flowers. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
