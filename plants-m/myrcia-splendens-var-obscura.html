Myrcia splendens var. obscura

Myrcia splendens var. obscura, commonly known as the dark myrtle, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces edible berries that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental beauty.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
