Monanthocitrus cornuta

Monanthocitrus cornuta, commonly known as the horned citrus or horned mandarin, is a species of citrus native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small evergreen tree characterized by its fragrant, orange-yellow fruits. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a slightly rough texture. Its flowers are solitary and fragrant, typically appearing before the leaves.

The fruit is generally edible, though it is less commonly cultivated for commercial use compared to other citrus varieties.
