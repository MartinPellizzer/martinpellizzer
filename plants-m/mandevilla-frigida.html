Mandevilla frigida

Mandevilla frigida, commonly known as the winter mandevilla, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It is characterized by its fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces opposite, simple, and ovate leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mandevilla frigida is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
