Micromeria cypria var. villosissima

Micromeria cypria var. villosissima, commonly known as the hairy micromeria, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, aromatic leaves covered in fine, white hairs, which give it a fuzzy appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a delicate touch to its habitat. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or coastal areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
