Muhlenbergia columbi

Muhlenbergia columbi, commonly known as the Columbia muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the United States.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and fine, silver-blue foliage that adds ornamental value to gardens. The plant typically produces delicate, feathery seed heads that appear in late summer or early autumn. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and drought-tolerant landscapes.

Muhlenbergia columbi is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
