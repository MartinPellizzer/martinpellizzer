Melilotus ruthenicus

Melilotus ruthenicus, commonly known as yellow sweet clover, is a flowering plant in the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Europe and parts of Asia, and it thrives in temperate climates. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and fragrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It has small, round, green pods that contain several seeds.

Melilotus ruthenicus is often used in agricultural settings for soil enrichment and as a forage crop.
