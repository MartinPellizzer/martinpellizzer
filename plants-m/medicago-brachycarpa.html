Medicago brachycarpa

Medicago brachycarpa, commonly known as the short-carpel medic.

It is a low-growing perennial forage legume native to the Mediterranean region. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. Its seeds are enclosed in short, inflated pods, giving it its specific epithet "brachycarpa.".

It is valued for its nutritional content and ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.
