Maranta divaricata

Maranta divaricata, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a tropical species native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, variegated leaves that often display a mix of green, white, and pink hues. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage rather than showy flowers. It thrives in humid, shady environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique leaf patterns make it a popular choice for indoor gardening and decorative purposes.
