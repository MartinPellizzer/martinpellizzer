Microsorum pteropus var. zosteriforme

Microsorum pteropus var. zosteriforme, commonly known as the flame sword fern, is a striking aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It features long, narrow leaves with a distinctive red or orange coloration along the midrib, giving it its vibrant appearance. The plant has a rhizomatic growth habit, allowing it to spread across the substrate in aquariums. Its leaves are often used in planted tanks for both aesthetic appeal and biological filtration.

It prefers moderate to high light and nutrient-rich water conditions for optimal growth.
