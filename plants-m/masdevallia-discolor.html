Masdevallia discolor

Masdevallia discolor, commonly known as the White Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, elongated pseudobulbs and delicate, white to pale green flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires high air humidity and good drainage for optimal growth. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating.

The flowers bloom in a spiral arrangement, making it a striking addition to specialized orchid collections.
