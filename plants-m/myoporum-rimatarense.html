Myoporum rimatarense

Myoporum rimatarense, commonly known as the Cook Strait myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically covered in a fine layer of white hair, giving them a silvery appearance. The plant produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and rocky habitats.

Myoporum rimatarense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping in its native region.
