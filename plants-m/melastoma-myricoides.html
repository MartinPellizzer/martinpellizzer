Melastoma myricoides

Melastoma myricoides, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking red or pink flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters, with opposite, oval-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne in dense clusters and are often visited by bees and other pollinators.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
