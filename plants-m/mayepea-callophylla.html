Mayepea callophylla

Mayepea callophylla, commonly known as the golden thorn, is a flowering plant native to parts of Australia.

It is characterized by its woody stems and distinctive yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in shrublands and open woodlands.

Mayepea callophylla is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
