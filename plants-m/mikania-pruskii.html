Mikania pruskii

Mikania pruskii, commonly known as the Pruskii morning glory, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It is a vigorous vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of purple and blue. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth and climbing habit. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Mikania pruskii is also valued for its ornamental appeal and has been introduced to other regions as a garden plant.
