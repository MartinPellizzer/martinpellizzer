Muhlenbergia huachucana

Muhlenbergia huachucana, commonly known as the Huachucana muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and feathery plumes that give it a decorative appearance. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky habitats and is well adapted to arid conditions. It produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer or early autumn.

Muhlenbergia huachucana is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in native plant gardens.
