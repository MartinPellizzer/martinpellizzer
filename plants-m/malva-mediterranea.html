Malva mediterranea

Malva mediterranea, commonly known as the Mediterranean mallow, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of North Africa.

It features showy, pink or purple flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has heart-shaped, velvety leaves and a robust root system that allows it to thrive in dry, rocky soils. It is often found in coastal areas and is adapted to withstand salty and windy conditions.

Malva mediterranea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
