Microlespedeza makinoi

Microlespedeza makinoi, commonly known as the Japanese dwarf clover, is a small perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest understories. It has a shallow root system and is often used in ecological restoration projects.

Microlespedeza makinoi is valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, improving soil fertility in degraded habitats.
