Mallotus capensis

Mallotus capensis, commonly known as the Cape holly, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant produces small, spherical berries that are typically red when mature. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Mallotus capensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
