Magnolia lozanoi

Magnolia lozanoi, commonly known as the Lozano magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the mountainous regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape with a smooth margin. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its spring blossoms.
