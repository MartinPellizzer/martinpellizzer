Maxillaria tonduzii

Maxillaria tonduzii, commonly known as the Tonduz orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers that have a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals and sepals. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria tonduzii is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
