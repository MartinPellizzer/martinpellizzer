Maranta tessellata var. kegeljanii

Maranta tessellata var. kegeljanii, commonly known as the Kegeljanii prayer plant, is a striking tropical plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, patterned leaves that often display shades of green, red, and silver, creating a visually appealing mosaic. The plant is noted for its unique ability to fold its leaves at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty, which is a response to changes in light. Its foliage is often used in ornamental horticulture due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.

The species thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture to maintain its lush appearance.
