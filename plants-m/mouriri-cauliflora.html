Mouriri cauliflora

Mouriri cauliflora, commonly known as the red mouriri, is a tropical tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its cauliflorous flowering habit, where flowers and fruits develop directly on the trunk and branches. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often consumed by various wildlife species. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a glossy appearance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for its unique floral display.
