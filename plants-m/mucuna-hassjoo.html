Mucuna hassjoo

Mucuna hassjoo, commonly known as the velvet bean, is a tropical leguminous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and distinctive bean-like pods that contain edible seeds. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine for their purported health benefits.

Mucuna hassjoo also plays a role in soil enrichment due to its nitrogen-fixing properties.
