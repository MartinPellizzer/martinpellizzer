Myristica beddomei

Myristica beddomei, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height, characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by large, fleshy berries that enclose the aromatic seeds used for nutmeg and mace.

Its unique botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and commercial contexts.
