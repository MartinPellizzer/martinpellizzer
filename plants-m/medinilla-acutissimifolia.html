Medinilla acutissimifolia

Medinilla acutissimifolia, commonly known as the sharp-leaved Medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features glossy, elliptical leaves with a pointed tip, giving it its scientific name. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-white flowers that bloom during the warmer months. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Medinilla acutissimifolia is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage.
