Miltonia flava

Miltonia flava, commonly known as the yellow Miltonia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow petals and a distinctive, often contrasting lip that enhances its visual appeal. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in clusters, often displaying a striking combination of color and form.

Miltonia flava is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
