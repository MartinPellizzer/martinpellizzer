Monanthes minima subsp. adenoscepes

Monanthes minima subsp.

adenoscepes, commonly known as the dwarf monanthes, is a small, herbaceous plant native to specific regions of southern Africa. It belongs to the Acanthaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, lance-shaped leaves.
