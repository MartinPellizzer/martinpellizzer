Meriania haemantha subsp. haemantha

Meriania haemantha subsp.

haemantha, commonly known as the red-flowered meriania, is a striking tropical shrub native to Central and South America. It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that are typically red or orange, with five petals arranged in a bell shape.

The plant features simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical and often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
