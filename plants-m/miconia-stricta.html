Miconia stricta

Miconia stricta, commonly known as the broadleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are berry-like, turning from green to red as they mature.

Miconia stricta is known for its rapid growth and is often found in humid, lowland tropical environments.
