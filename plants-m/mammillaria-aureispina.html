Mammillaria aureispina

Mammillaria aureispina, commonly known as the golden spine cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and yellowish spines. The plant features pale green to bluish-gray stems that are covered in areoles, which bear the characteristic spines. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
