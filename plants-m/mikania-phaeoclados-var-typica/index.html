Mikania Phaeoclados Var. Typica

Mikania vine, scientifically known as Mikania phaeoclados var. typica, is a fast-growing, climbing plant native to tropical and subtropical regions. It is characterized by its slender stems, heart-shaped leaves, and clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. This versatile vine is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments, thriving in moist, well-drained soil. Its rapid growth rate makes it both a valuable ornamental plant and a potential weed in agricultural areas. Despite its beauty, it can be invasive if not managed properly.
