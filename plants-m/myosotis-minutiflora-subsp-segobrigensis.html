Myosotis minutiflora subsp. segobrigensis

Myosotis minutiflora subsp.

segobrigensis, commonly known as the Segobriga forget-me-not, is a small, delicate flowering plant native to southern Africa. It produces tiny, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in rocky or gravelly soils and is often found in mountainous regions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it is adapted to cool, moist environments.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
