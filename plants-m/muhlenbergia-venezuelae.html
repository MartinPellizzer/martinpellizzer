Muhlenbergia venezuelae

Muhlenbergia venezuelae, commonly known as the Venezuelan muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to South America, specifically Venezuela.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its tufted growth habit and slender, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky slopes. Its flowering stems bear small, spike-like inflorescences that produce delicate, feathery flowers.

Muhlenbergia venezuelae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
