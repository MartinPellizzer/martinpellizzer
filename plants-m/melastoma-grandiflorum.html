Melastoma grandiflorum

Melastoma grandiflorum, commonly known as the large-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, pink or red flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
