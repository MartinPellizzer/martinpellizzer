Mormodes cogniauxii

Mormodes cogniauxii, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a flowering plant native to the island of Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, intricate floral structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers are often brightly colored, featuring a complex arrangement of petals and sepals.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a threatened species in its native range.
