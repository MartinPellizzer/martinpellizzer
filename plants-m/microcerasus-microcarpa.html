Microcerasus microcarpa

Microcerasus microcarpa, commonly known as the dwarf Chinese hawthorn, is a small evergreen shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit. The plant features small, dark green leaves and produces white or pinkish flowers in early spring. Its fruit is a small, red drupe that matures in late summer.

Microcerasus microcarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable climates.
