Mammillaria glochidiata var. rosea

Mammillaria glochidiata var. rosea, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and dense covering of white to pink spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are typically reduced to spines, and it thrives in well-drained soil with minimal water.

It is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its ornamental appearance and hardy nature.
