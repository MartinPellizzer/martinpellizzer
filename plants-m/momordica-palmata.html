Momordica palmata

Momordica palmata, commonly known as horned melon, is a tropical vine native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, spiky, green fruit that resembles a horned helmet. The plant produces yellow flowers and has heart-shaped leaves with a rough texture. The fruit is edible when ripe, with a sweet and sometimes musky flavor.

It is often cultivated for its unique appearance and culinary uses.
