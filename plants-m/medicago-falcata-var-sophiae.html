Medicago falcata var. sophiae

Medicago falcata var. sophiae, commonly known as the Sophie's goat's rue, is a variant of the goat's rue plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellow flowers arranged in racemes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
