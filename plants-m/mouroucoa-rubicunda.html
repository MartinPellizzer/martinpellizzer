Mouroucoa rubicunda

Mouroucoa rubicunda, commonly known as the red-flowered mouroucoa, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and unique, elongated leaves that are often covered in a fine, waxy coating. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot. Its flowers bloom in clusters, adding a dramatic visual impact to its surroundings.

Mouroucoa rubicunda is primarily valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
