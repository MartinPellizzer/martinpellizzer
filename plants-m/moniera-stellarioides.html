Moniera stellarioides

Moniera stellarioides, commonly known as the star-like moss, is a species of liverwort belonging to the family Porellaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate, and star-shaped thalli that form dense, carpet-like growths on moist, shaded substrates. Its leaves are closely arranged and lack true stems, typical of the thallose growth form of liverworts.

Moniera stellarioides plays a role in soil stabilization and provides microhabitats for various invertebrates.
