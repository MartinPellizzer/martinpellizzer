Microcarpaea alterniflora

Microcarpaea alterniflora, commonly known as the alternate-flower microcarp, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil and Peru. The plant is characterized by its alternate arrangement of leaves and small, clustered flowers. It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories.
