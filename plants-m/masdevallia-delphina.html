Masdevallia delphina

Masdevallia delphina, commonly known as Delphina's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flowers with intricate patterns and a unique structure. The plant typically grows as a small, terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and elongated leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on a single stem and can vary in color from white to pink and purple.

Masdevallia delphina is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
