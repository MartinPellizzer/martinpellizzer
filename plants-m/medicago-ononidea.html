Medicago ononidea

Medicago ononidea, commonly known as wild licorice, is a perennial legume native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to purple. The plant produces cylindrical, hairy pods containing several seeds. It thrives in a variety of soil types but prefers well-drained conditions.

Medicago ononidea is often found in open woodlands, fields, and along roadsides.
