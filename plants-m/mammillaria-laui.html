Mammillaria laui

Mammillaria laui, commonly known as the Laui's nipple cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria laui is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation due to its ornamental appearance and low maintenance requirements.
