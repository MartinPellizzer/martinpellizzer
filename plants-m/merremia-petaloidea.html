Merremia petaloidea

Merremia petaloidea, commonly known as the yellow shower vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a climbing vine with heart-shaped leaves and produces showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is characterized by its long, trailing stems and is often found in moist, sunny habitats. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Merremia petaloidea is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
