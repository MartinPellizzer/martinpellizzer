Montanoa fragrans

Montanoa fragrans, commonly known as the fragrant montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, fragrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and covered in fine hairs.

Montanoa fragrans is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate fragrance and visual beauty.
