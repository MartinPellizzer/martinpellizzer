Medicago sativa var. decolorans

Medicago sativa var. decolorans, commonly known as the yellow medic.

This variant is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia. It is characterized by its yellow flowers and the gradual fading of its foliage, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it valuable in agricultural and ecological contexts.

It is often used in forage production and soil improvement due to its hardy nature and adaptability.
