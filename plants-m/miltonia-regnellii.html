Miltonia regnellii

Miltonia regnellii, commonly known as the regnellii orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of pink, red, and white. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, often arranged in a compact rosette.

Miltonia regnellii thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and consistent moisture for optimal growth.
