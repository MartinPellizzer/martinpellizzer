Miconia formosa var. angustifolia

Miconia formosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The species is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded, humid environments.
