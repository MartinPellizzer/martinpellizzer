Mussaenda cambodiana var. annamensis

Mussaenda cambodiana var. annamensis, commonly known as Annam Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that resemble umbels, typically in shades of pink or white. The plant has lance-shaped leaves with a smooth surface and a slightly hairy underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
