Minuartia stojanovii subsp. pirinica

Minuartia stojanovii subsp.

pirinica, commonly known as the Pirin rockcress, is a member of the Brassicaceae family. It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial plant characterized by its small, clustered flowers.

The plant typically features bright yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. It is native to the high-altitude regions of the Pirin Mountains in Bulgaria.

Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are adapted to withstand harsh mountain conditions.
