Marianthemum medium

Marianthemum medium, commonly known as the common marianthemum, is a low-growing perennial plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its compact appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full to partial sunlight, making it a suitable choice for rock gardens or borders.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable addition to both wildflower meadows and cultivated landscapes.
