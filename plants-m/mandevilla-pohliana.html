Mandevilla pohliana

Mandevilla pohliana, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with large, showy flowers that range from pink to red. The plant produces tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and bees. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its vibrant blooms.

Mandevilla pohliana thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and plenty of sunlight.
