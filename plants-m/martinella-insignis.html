Martinella insignis

Martinella insignis, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety foliage and showy, clustered flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant thrives in bright, indirect light and prefers well-draining soil to prevent root rot. Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for indoor gardening.

The flowers typically bloom throughout the year under optimal conditions.
