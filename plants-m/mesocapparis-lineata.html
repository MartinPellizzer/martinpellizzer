Mesocapparis lineata

Mesocapparis lineata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved sandthorn, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Capparaceae.

It is a shrub characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, oval fruits that are typically covered in fine hairs. Native to arid and semi-arid regions of southern Africa, it thrives in sandy or gravelly soils.

Its leaves are narrow and linear, giving it a distinctive appearance that helps it conserve water in its harsh environment.
