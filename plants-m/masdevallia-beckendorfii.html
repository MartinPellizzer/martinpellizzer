Masdevallia beckendorfii

Masdevallia beckendorfii, commonly known as the Beckendorf's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower spikes that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with intricate patterns and a distinctive, elongated shape.

Due to its rarity and specific environmental requirements, it is a challenging species to cultivate outside its native range.
