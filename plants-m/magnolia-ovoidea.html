Magnolia ovoidea

Magnolia ovoidea, commonly known as the egg-shaped magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blooms.
