Memecylon grandifolium

Memecylon grandifolium, commonly known as the large-leaved memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, round berries. It thrives in humid, shady environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Memecylon grandifolium is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional medicinal uses in local communities.
