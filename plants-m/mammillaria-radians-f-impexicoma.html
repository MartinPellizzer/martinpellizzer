Mammillaria radians f. impexicoma

Mammillaria radians f. impexicoma, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent radial ribs and small, white to pale blue spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and ease of cultivation.
