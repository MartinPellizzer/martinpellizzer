Maxillaria caloglossa

Maxillaria caloglossa, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, velvety purple to brown flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern. The plant typically blooms in summer and is known for its strong, sweet fragrance. It prefers well-drained soil and partial shade, making it a popular choice for both greenhouse and outdoor cultivation.

Maxillaria caloglossa is valued for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
