Myristica spadicea

Myristica spadicea, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg (Myristica fragrans). The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and produces a fruit that contains the spice nutmeg inside a hard, woody shell. Its flowers are small, greenish-yellow, and fragrant, typically growing in clusters.

The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall and thrives in humid, shaded environments.
