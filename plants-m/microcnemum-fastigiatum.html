Microcnemum fastigiatum

Microcnemum fastigiatum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved moss cushion, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant belonging to the family Pottiaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and small, narrow leaves that are often strongly curved. Microcnemum fastigiatum plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for microorganisms in its native ecosystems.
