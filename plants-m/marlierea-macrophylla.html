Marlierea macrophylla

Marlierea macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf marlierea, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that can reach up to 20 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its flowering stems are often covered in fine, silky hairs.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
