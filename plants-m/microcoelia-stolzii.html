Microcoelia stolzii

Microcoelia stolzii, commonly known as the Stolzii microcoelia, is a small, flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It produces small, daisy-like blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Due to its ornamental value and unique appearance, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
