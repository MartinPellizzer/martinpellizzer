Memecylon prasinum

Memecylon prasinum, commonly known as the green memecylon, is a tropical evergreen shrub belonging to the Meliaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its small, lance-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round fruits that contain a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
