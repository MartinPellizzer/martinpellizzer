Microstylis wallichii var. brachycheila

Microstylis wallichii var. brachycheila, commonly known as the short-styled microstylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cyperaceae, or sedge family.

It is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and small, clustered inflorescences. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often with a distinct midrib.

The flowers are unisexual, with separate male and female structures, a common feature in many sedges.
