Mutisia dealbata

Mutisia dealbata, commonly known as the white mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant has a woody stem and is often found in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Mutisia dealbata is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its striking appearance.
