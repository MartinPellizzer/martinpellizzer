Medinilla megacarpa

Medinilla megacarpa, commonly known as the giant cup plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, cup-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla megacarpa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
