Mesanthemum cupricola

Mesanthemum cupricola, commonly known as the copperleaf, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its striking copper-colored foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically arranged in a compact inflorescence. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens.

Its unique coloration and compact growth habit make it a visually appealing ornamental plant.
