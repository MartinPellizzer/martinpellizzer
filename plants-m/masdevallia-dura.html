Masdevallia dura

Masdevallia dura, commonly known as the hardy masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes, often on rock faces or in forest understories. Its pseudobulbs are elongated and somewhat cylindrical, and its leaves are usually broad and fleshy.

Masdevallia dura is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its resilience and striking floral display.
