Memecylon elegans var. minor

Memecylon elegans var. minor, commonly known as the dwarf memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, dark green foliage with elliptical leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its compact size and ornamental value make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical climates.
