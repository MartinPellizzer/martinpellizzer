Masdevallia estradae var. ludibunda

Masdevallia estradae var. ludibunda, commonly known as the Ludibunda masdevallia, is a rare orchid species native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its humid, shaded habitat. Its leaves are usually elongated and fleshy, adapted to store moisture in its native environment.
