Muhlenbergia alta

Muhlenbergia alta, commonly known as tall muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery plumes that give it a graceful appearance. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, preferring well-drained soils. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue.

Muhlenbergia alta is often used in ornamental landscaping due to its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
