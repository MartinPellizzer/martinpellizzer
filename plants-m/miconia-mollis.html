Miconia mollis

Miconia mollis, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically velvety and dark green on the upper surface. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Miconia mollis is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant but can become invasive in certain regions.
