Melaleuca caja-putti

Melaleuca caja-putti, commonly known as the Caja-putti melaleuca, is a species of flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and tea trees. The plant is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows in coastal or sandy soils and is often found in heathlands or open forests.

Melaleuca caja-putti is valued for its ornamental qualities and its ability to thrive in harsh environmental conditions.
