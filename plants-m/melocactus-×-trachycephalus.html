Melocactus × trachycephalus

Melocactus × trachycephalus, commonly known as the zigzag melon cactus, is a hybrid species native to the arid regions of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, ribbed stems that exhibit a zigzag pattern, which helps reduce water loss in its harsh environment. The plant produces yellow to orange flowers during the warmer months, adding a striking visual contrast to its rugged appearance. Its spines are typically short and dense, providing protection from herbivores.

Melocactus × trachycephalus is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts due to its hardiness and unique aesthetic.
