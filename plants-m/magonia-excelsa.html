Magonia excelsa

Magonia excelsa, commonly known as the giant magnolia, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white flowers that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant has broad, glossy leaves and can grow to impressive heights, often exceeding 30 meters. Its bark is smooth and grayish, with a pleasant aroma when cut.

The flowers bloom in the spring and are highly valued for their ornamental beauty and use in traditional medicine.
