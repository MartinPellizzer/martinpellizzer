Maranta gibba

Maranta gibba, commonly known as the zigzag plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that often display vibrant red or purple markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that are typically borne on short stalks. Maranta gibba is a rhizomatous herb that thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its unique leaf patterns and compact growth make it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
