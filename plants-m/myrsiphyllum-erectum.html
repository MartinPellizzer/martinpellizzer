Myrsiphyllum erectum

Myrsiphyllum erectum, commonly known as the erect myrsiphyllum, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its erect growth habit and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are usually narrow and leathery, with a distinctive aromatic scent.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal regions.
