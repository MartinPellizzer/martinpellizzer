Muantum roseum

Muantum roseum, commonly known as the Fairy Rose, is a rare and exquisite flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its delicate, velvety petals that range in color from pale pink to a soft lavender hue. The plant produces small, fragrant blooms during the spring and early summer months. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists alike.
