Massonia lanceolata

Massonia lanceolata, commonly known as the narrowleaf massonia, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a powdery white coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the warmer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
