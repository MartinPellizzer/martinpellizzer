Matricaria chamomilla subsp.

courrantiana, commonly known as the Narrow-leaved Chamomile, is a perennial herb native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. It is characterized by its finely divided, linear leaves and small, yellow flower heads that resemble chamomile.
