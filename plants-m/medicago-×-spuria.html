Medicago × spuria

Medicago × spuria, commonly known as the goat's rue, is a hybrid legume species characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers.

It is native to temperate regions of Europe and Asia and thrives in disturbed soils. The plant is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil fertility. It produces small, pod-like fruits containing several seeds.

Medicago × spuria is often found in meadows, roadsides, and along the edges of forests.
