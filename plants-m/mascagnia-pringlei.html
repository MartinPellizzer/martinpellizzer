Mascagnia pringlei

Mascagnia pringlei, commonly known as the Pringle's lily, is a striking member of the Amaryllidaceae family.

It is native to the mountainous regions of South America, particularly in Argentina and Chile. Mascagnia pringlei is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its dramatic floral display.
