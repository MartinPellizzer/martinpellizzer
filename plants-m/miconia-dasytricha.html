Miconia dasytricha

Miconia dasytricha, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in dense, velvety hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are followed by distinctive, spiny fruits. Miconia dasytricha is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil types.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions, outcompeting native vegetation.
