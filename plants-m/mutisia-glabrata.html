Mutisia glabrata

Mutisia glabrata, commonly known as the smooth mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has smooth, glabrous stems and leaves. Its flowers are yellow and bloom in the spring, attracting various pollinators.

Mutisia glabrata is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
