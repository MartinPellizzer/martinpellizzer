Maxillaria luteoalba

Maxillaria luteoalba, commonly known as the yellow-white maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with yellow and white petals. The plant typically has a short, thick rhizome and pseudobulbs, which help it store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually oval and arranged in a fan-like pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

This orchid thrives in warm, humid environments and is often cultivated for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
