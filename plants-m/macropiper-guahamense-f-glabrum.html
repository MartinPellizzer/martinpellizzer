Macropiper guahamense f. glabrum

Macropiper guahamense f. glabrum, commonly known as the smooth-leafed guahamense pepper.

It is a variant of the Macropiper genus, which belongs to the Piperaceae family, known for its aromatic and often spicy fruits. This plant is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves and small, clustered fruits that are typically used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. It thrives in humid, tropical environments, often found in the understory of rainforests.

The plant's botanical features make it an important species for both ecological studies and ethnobotanical research.
