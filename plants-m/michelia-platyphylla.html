Michelia platyphylla

Michelia platyphylla, commonly known as the flat-leaved michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, ovate to elliptical leaves that are usually arranged oppositely on the stems. Its fruit is a follicle that contains several seeds, and it is often found in moist, shaded environments.

Michelia platyphylla is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and fragrance.
