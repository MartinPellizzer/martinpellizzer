Mammillaria melanocentra var. runyonii

Mammillaria melanocentra var. runyonii, commonly known as the Runyon's nipple cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with numerous small, rounded tubercles that give it a distinctive, spiny appearance. The plant produces white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
