Mouriri gonavensis var. gonavensis

Mouriri gonavensis var. gonavensis, commonly known as the Gonávës mourning tree, is a species native to the Caribbean islands.

It is a small to medium-sized tree characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by small, fleshy fruits. It thrives in moist, tropical climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree has a slow growth rate and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
