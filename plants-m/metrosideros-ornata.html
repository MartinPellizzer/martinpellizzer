Metrosideros ornata

Metrosideros ornata, commonly known as the Tahitian Glorybower, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands, including Tahiti and other parts of French Polynesia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of about 3 to 6 meters. The plant is characterized by its bright red or orange flowers, which are clustered in dense inflorescences and bloom during the warmer months. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.
