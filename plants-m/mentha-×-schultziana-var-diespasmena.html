Mentha × schultziana var. diespasmena

Mentha × schultziana var. diespasmena, commonly known as the Mentha × schultziana var. diespasmena, is a hybrid mint species native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its aromatic leaves and flowering spikes, which are typically purple or pink in color. The plant is perennial, growing upright with a bushy habit and can reach heights of up to 60 cm. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often used in herbal teas and aromatherapy due to its distinctive menthol-like fragrance.
