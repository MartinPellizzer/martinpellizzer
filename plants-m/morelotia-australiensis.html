Morelotia australiensis

Morelotia australiensis, commonly known as the Australian flame tree, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that appear in late winter to early spring. The plant has compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets and produces pods that contain seeds.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens and urban landscapes.
