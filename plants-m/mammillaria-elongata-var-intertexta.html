Mammillaria elongata var. intertexta

Mammillaria elongata var. intertexta, commonly known as the Mexican moon cactus, is a striking cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a tall, cylindrical stem covered in white to yellow spines and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun, requiring well-draining conditions to prevent root rot. Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
