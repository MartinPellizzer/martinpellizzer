Millettia sinensis

Millettia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese silk tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to East Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Millettia sinensis is also valued in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
