Meisteria longiloba

Meisteria longiloba, commonly known as the long-stemmed beauty, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that can grow up to 3 feet in length, often with a waxy texture and deep green coloration. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Meisteria longiloba is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
