Micranthes × geum

Micranthes × geum, commonly known as the garden cinquefoil, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Rosaceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, five-petaled flowers that range in color from yellow to red. The plant typically grows to a height of 30–60 cm and has narrow, lance-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy texture. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long blooming period, which extends from late spring to early autumn.
