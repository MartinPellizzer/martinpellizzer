Melastoma solaniflorum

Melastoma solaniflorum, commonly known as the purple-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of showy, violet to purple flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are typically red or orange when mature. Its leaves are usually elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Melastoma solaniflorum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
