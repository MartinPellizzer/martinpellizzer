Myristica warburgii subsp. hybrida

Myristica warburgii subsp.

hybrida, commonly known as the hybrid nutmeg, is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia. It is a small evergreen tree characterized by its fragrant, aromatic fruits that contain the valuable nutmeg spice.

The plant produces a unique combination of compounds, including myristicin and elemicin, which contribute to its distinct flavor and medicinal properties. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Due to its commercial importance, it is cultivated in various regions for its nutmeg and mace products.
