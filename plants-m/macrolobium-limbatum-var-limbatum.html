Macrolobium limbatum var. limbatum

Macrolobium limbatum var. limbatum, commonly known as the broad-leafed macrolobium, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink, attracting various pollinators. It is typically found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, well-drained soils.

This variant is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
