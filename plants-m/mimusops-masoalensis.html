Mimusops masoalensis

Mimusops masoalensis, commonly known as the Madagascar oil tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves and showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The tree produces small, round fruits that contain a single large seed surrounded by a sticky, aromatic pulp.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and has traditional uses in local medicine and as a source of natural oils.
