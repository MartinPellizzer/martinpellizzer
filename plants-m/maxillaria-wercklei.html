Maxillaria wercklei

Maxillaria wercklei, commonly known as the Wercklei orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers. The plant features a pseudobulb that supports its growth and stores water, allowing it to survive in seasonal environments. Its flowers are typically pale pink to white with yellow and green markings, and they bloom during the warmer months.

Maxillaria wercklei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
