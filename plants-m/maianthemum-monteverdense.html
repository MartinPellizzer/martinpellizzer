Maianthemum monteverdense

Maianthemum monteverdense, commonly known as the Monteverde false lily, is a rare and endangered plant native to the cloud forests of Monteverde, Costa Rica.

It is a member of the Liliaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments with high humidity and partial shade, typical of its montane habitat. Its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements make it highly vulnerable to habitat loss and climate change.

Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this unique botanical species.
