Mariscus Luteus

Mariscus luteus is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa. Known for its vibrant yellow blooms and succulent foliage, it stands out in its natural habitat. This species thrives in well-drained soils and requires minimal water, making it ideal for xeriscaping. Its unique appearance has made it a popular choice among gardeners and botanists alike. Despite its beauty, Mariscus luteus remains relatively obscure in the global horticultural community.
