Medinilla engleri

Medinilla engleri, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This flowering plant is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its showy, waxy flowers that come in shades of pink and white. It has glossy, dark green leaves and produces small, fragrant blossoms that bloom in clusters. The plant is often cultivated as an ornamental due to its attractive appearance and ability to thrive in tropical climates.

It prefers partial shade and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor gardening.
