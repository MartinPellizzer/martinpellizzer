Marsdenia purpurella

Marsdenia purpurella, commonly known as the purple-flowered Marsdenia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and opposite, ovate leaves that are often glabrous. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, depending on the variety. These flowers are typically borne on inflorescences at the ends of the stems.

Marsdenia purpurella is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.
