Maurocenia pinnata

Maurocenia pinnata, commonly known as the pink-flowered lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in clusters at the top of the stems. Its leaves are large, lance-shaped, and arranged in a pinnate pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Maurocenia pinnata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses due to its vibrant blooms and unique foliage.
