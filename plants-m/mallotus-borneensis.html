Mallotus borneensis

Mallotus borneensis, commonly known as the Bornean mallot, is a flowering plant species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Borneo.

It belongs to the family Euphorbiaceae and is characterized by its compound leaves with opposite arrangement. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color.

It is often found in lowland rainforests and is valued for its ornamental and potential medicinal properties.
