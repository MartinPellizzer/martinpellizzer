Mimosa subinermis

Mimosa subinermis, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for dry, arid environments. It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.

Mimosa subinermis is also known for its sensitivity to touch, as its leaves fold when disturbed.
