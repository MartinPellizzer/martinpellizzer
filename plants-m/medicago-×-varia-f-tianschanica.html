Medicago × varia f. tianschanica

Medicago × varia f. tianschanica, commonly known as the Tianshan clover, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Central Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers. The plant is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, often found in mountainous regions. It has a deep root system that allows it to thrive in poor soils and withstand drought conditions.

Additionally, it plays a role in soil stabilization and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
