Musa × alphurica

Musa × alphurica, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of edible, sweet fruit. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. It requires well-drained soil and consistent moisture for optimal growth.

Its fruit is often used in both culinary and medicinal applications.
