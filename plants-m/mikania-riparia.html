Mikania riparia

Mikania riparia, commonly known as river morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a herbaceous vine characterized by its trailing growth habit and heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically blue or violet in color. It thrives in moist environments, often found along riverbanks and in wetlands.

Mikania riparia is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered an invasive species in certain regions.
