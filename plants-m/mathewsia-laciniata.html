Mathewsia laciniata

Mathewsia laciniata, commonly known as the narrow-leafed daisy.

It is a flowering plant native to southern Africa, characterized by its delicate, daisy-like flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant typically grows in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky outcrops. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, with a central disc of smaller florets.

Mathewsia laciniata is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
