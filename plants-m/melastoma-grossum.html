Melastoma grossum

Melastoma grossum, commonly known as the broad-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth upper surface and a hairy underside. Its flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds.
