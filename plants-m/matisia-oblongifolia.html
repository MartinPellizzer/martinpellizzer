Matisia oblongifolia

Matisia oblongifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf matisia, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are often pubescent. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, followed by dark, round berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.
