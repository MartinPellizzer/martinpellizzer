Maxillariella cobanensis

Maxillariella cobanensis, commonly known as the Coban orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and blooms in shades of white, pink, and purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillariella cobanensis is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
