Manotes zenkeri

Manotes zenkeri, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy red flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The tree has smooth, grayish bark and broad, palmate leaves that are lobed and hairy when young. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular ornamental plant.

Manotes zenkeri is also valued for its rapid growth and ability to provide shade in tropical landscapes.
