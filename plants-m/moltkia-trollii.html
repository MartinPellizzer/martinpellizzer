Moltkia trollii

Moltkia trollii, commonly known as the mountain holly, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is a deciduous shrub that typically grows in rocky and well-drained soils. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

Moltkia trollii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and compact growth habit.
