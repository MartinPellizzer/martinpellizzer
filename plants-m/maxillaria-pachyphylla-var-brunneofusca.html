Maxillaria pachyphylla var. brunneofusca

Maxillaria pachyphylla var. brunneofusca, commonly known as the brown-fusca maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features a pseudobulbous structure with fleshy, dark green leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating. The plant produces fragrant, yellowish-brown flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip. It thrives in cool, humid environments with well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited natural habitat, it is a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
