Microstylis roohutuensis

Microstylis roohutuensis, commonly known as the Tuatara plant, is a rare endemic species found exclusively in New Zealand.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale green flowers. Its leaves are narrow and often have a bluish-green hue, adapted to the humid, shaded environments of its native habitat.
