Marmaroxylon basijugum

Marmaroxylon basijugum, commonly known as the lesser sand verbena, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy leaves and is often found in sandy or rocky soils. It is adapted to arid environments and is drought-tolerant.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
