Miscanthus sinensis f. viridis

Miscanthus sinensis f. viridis, commonly known as the Green Panic Grass, is a compact, ornamental grass that features narrow, grass-like leaves with a striking green coloration.

It is a cold-hardy perennial that thrives in full sun to partial shade and is well-suited for moist to average soil conditions. The plant produces delicate, feathery plumes in the fall, adding visual interest to the landscape. It is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to form dense clumps.

Botanically, it belongs to the Poaceae family and is valued for its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
