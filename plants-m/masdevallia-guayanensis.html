Masdevallia guayanensis

Masdevallia guayanensis, commonly known as the Guayana masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, intricate flowers. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
