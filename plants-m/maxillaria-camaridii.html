Maxillaria camaridii

Maxillaria camaridii, commonly known as the Camaridii orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. The plant features slender, upright stems and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. Its leaves are lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Maxillaria camaridii is valued in horticulture for its delicate blooms and relatively easy care requirements.
