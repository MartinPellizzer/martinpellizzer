Mesua calophylloides

Mesua calophylloides, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its vivid common name. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green in color. It thrives in humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
