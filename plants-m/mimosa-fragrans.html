Mimosa fragrans

Mimosa fragrans, commonly known as the sweet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and pleasant scent. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial shade, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Mimosa fragrans is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
