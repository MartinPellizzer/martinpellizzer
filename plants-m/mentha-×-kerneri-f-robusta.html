Mentha × kerneri f. robusta

Mentha × kerneri f. robusta, commonly known as robust mint, is a hybrid perennial herb native to China.

It is characterized by its aromatic, square stems and opposite, serrated leaves that are covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, pale blue to purple flowers in whorled clusters, typically blooming in late summer. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This variety is valued for its strong menthol scent and is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine.
