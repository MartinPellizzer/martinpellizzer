Montanoa tomentosa var. xanthiifolia

Montanoa tomentosa var. xanthiifolia, commonly known as the yellowleaf montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils in shaded to partially shaded environments.
