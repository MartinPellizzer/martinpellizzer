Morelosia

Morelosia, commonly known as the Mexican nightshade, is a genus of flowering plants in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is native to Mexico and Central America, where it thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. The plants typically have showy, tubular flowers that range in color from white to purple, attracting pollinators like hummingbirds and bees. Their leaves are usually broad and dark green, with some species exhibiting a slight waxy coating.
