Microloma namaquense

Microloma namaquense, commonly known as the Namaqua starling or Namaqua starling grass, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky or sandy soils, often in arid or semi-arid regions. The plant features small, star-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color, which bloom in clusters. It is well adapted to drought conditions, with a deep root system that allows it to survive in harsh environments.

Microloma namaquense is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
