Melastoma bubalinum

Melastoma bubalinum, commonly known as the small-leaved melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, often with a distinctive tubular shape. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Melastoma bubalinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
