Macaranga ducis var. glabra

Macaranga ducis var. glabra, commonly known as smooth macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its smooth, often glossy leaves that are typically ovate to elliptic in shape. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and is commonly found in tropical rainforests. It produces small, clustered flowers that are usually greenish-yellow in color.
