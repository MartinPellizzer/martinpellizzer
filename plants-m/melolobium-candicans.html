Melolobium candicans

Melolobium candicans, commonly known as the flame flower, is a striking plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange to red flowers that bloom in late summer and early autumn. The plant produces clusters of tubular flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and birds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its long-lasting blooms and unique foliage.
