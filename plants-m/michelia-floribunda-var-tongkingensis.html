Michelia floribunda var. tongkingensis

Michelia floribunda var. tongkingensis, commonly known as the Tongking magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth margin and a glossy green upper surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
