Matucana calliantha var. prolifera

Matucana calliantha var. prolifera, commonly known as the golden torch cactus, is a hybrid species within the Cactaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, columnar stems that often branch into multiple stems, forming a dense, tree-like structure. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer, adding a striking visual contrast to its green foliage. It is native to Brazil and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses for its unique appearance and resilience.
