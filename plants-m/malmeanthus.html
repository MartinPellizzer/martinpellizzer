Malmeanthus

Malmeanthus, commonly known as the yellow daisy, is a genus of flowering plants native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble daisies. The plant features bright yellow ray florets surrounding a central disk of tubular flowers, which attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.
