Monoon biglandulosum

Monoon biglandulosum, commonly known as the broad-leafed monoon, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine, glandular hairs. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
