Masdevallia margaretae

Masdevallia margaretae, commonly known as the Margaretae Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, intricate flowers that typically feature a combination of white, pink, and purple hues. The plant produces small, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a dense layer of fine, white trichomes. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and specific cultivation needs, it is a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.
