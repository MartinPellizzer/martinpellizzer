Miscanthus × changii

Miscanthus × changii, commonly known as silver grass, is a hybrid grass species native to East Asia.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in the fall. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often used in landscaping for its aesthetic appeal. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it adaptable to a variety of garden environments.

Miscanthus × changii is also valued for its potential in bioenergy production due to its high biomass yield.
