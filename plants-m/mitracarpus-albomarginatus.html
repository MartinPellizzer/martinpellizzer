Mitracarpus albomarginatus

Mitracarpus albomarginatus, commonly known as the white-margin daisies, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers with white petals and yellow centers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage is usually narrow and grass-like, with a distinctive white margin on the leaf edges.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
