Magnolia manguillo

Magnolia manguillo, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, ovate leaves that are arranged alternately along the branches. Its bark is smooth and gray, with a slightly flaky texture.

The Magnolia manguillo is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to a variety of soil types.
