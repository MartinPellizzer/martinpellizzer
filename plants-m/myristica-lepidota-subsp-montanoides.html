Myristica lepidota subsp. montanoides

Myristica lepidota subsp.

montanoides, commonly known as the mountain myristica, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia. It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, oily seeds and thick, waxy leaves.

The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments within lowland and montane forests. Its fruit is typically orange to reddish in color, and the seeds are encased in a hard, aromatic aril.

This subspecies is valued for its potential in traditional medicine and as a source of natural oils.
