Margaritaria discoidea

Margaritaria discoidea, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a species native to the Americas.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant is characterized by its dense, silver-gray foliage, which gives it its common name. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a waxy texture.

The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark, spherical fruits that contain seeds.
