Musa nagensium var. hongii

Musa nagensium var. hongii, commonly known as Hong's banana, is a variant of the banana plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a herbaceous perennial with large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces clusters of elongated, yellowish-orange fruit that are edible and have a sweet, tropical flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued both for its ornamental appeal and its culinary uses.
