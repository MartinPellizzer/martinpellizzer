Monechma cleomoides

Monechma cleomoides, commonly known as the slender cleome, is a herbaceous plant native to parts of Africa and the Caribbean.

It belongs to the family Cleomaceae and is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and often have a hairy texture, while its flowers are clustered in racemes.

Monechma cleomoides is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its delicate appearance and ability to attract pollinators.
