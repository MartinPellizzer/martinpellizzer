Manihot isoloba

Manihot isoloba, commonly known as the arrowleaf batata, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and edible tuberous roots.
