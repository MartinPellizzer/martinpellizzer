Maxillaria deflexa

Maxillaria deflexa, commonly known as the drooping maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green leaves and has a sympodial growth pattern. Its flowers are often pink or purple, with a unique lip that is recurved or deflexed.

Maxillaria deflexa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
