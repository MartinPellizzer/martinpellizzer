Medinilla hasseltii var. griffithii

Medinilla hasseltii var. griffithii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its pendulous, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, resembling Christmas ornaments. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and oval in shape. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining, fertile soil.

Medinilla hasseltii var. griffithii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its striking floral display.
