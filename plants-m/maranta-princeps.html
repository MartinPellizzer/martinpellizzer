Maranta princeps

Maranta princeps, commonly known as the silver stripe arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, arrow-shaped leaves that feature distinctive silver veins. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental species known for their vibrant foliage. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light for optimal growth.

Maranta princeps is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively low maintenance requirements.
