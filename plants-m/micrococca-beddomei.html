Micrococca beddomei

Micrococca beddomei, commonly known as the Beddome's micrococca, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is a small shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in umbels. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and usually oval in shape.

Micrococca beddomei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
