Myristica valida

Myristica valida, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg and mace plants. The tree produces a large, oval-shaped fruit that contains the aromatic seeds used as spice. Its leaves are dark green, glossy, and have a waxy texture.

The plant is valued not only for its economic importance as a spice source but also for its ecological role in tropical ecosystems.
