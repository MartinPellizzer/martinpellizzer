Meristostylus brachycalyx

Meristostylus brachycalyx, commonly known as the narrow-calyx mistletoe, is a hemiparasitic plant that attaches to the branches of its host trees.

Due to its parasitic nature, it can have significant impacts on the health and growth of its host trees.
