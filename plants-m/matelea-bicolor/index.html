Matelea Bicolor

Matelea bicolor is a strikingly beautiful flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico. It is known for its vibrant, showy flowers that bloom in spring and summer. This species belongs to the Apocynaceae family, which includes many plants with toxic properties. The plant's unique appearance and ecological significance make it a subject of interest for botanists and nature enthusiasts. Its ability to thrive in arid environments highlights its adaptability and resilience.
