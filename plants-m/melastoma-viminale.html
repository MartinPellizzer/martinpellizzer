Melastoma viminale

Melastoma viminale, commonly known as the slender melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink or white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Melastoma viminale is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
