Moniera rotundifolia

Moniera rotundifolia, commonly known as the round-leaf moniera, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions.

It belongs to the family Monieraceae and is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
