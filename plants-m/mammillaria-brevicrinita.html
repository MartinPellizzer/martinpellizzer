Mammillaria brevicrinita

Mammillaria brevicrinita, commonly known as the short-hair mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and short, white bristles that cover its stem. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing species that prefers well-draining soil and full sun.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appearance, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in arid gardens.
