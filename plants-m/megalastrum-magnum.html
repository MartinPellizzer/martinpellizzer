Megalastrum magnum

Megalastrum magnum, commonly known as the giant starflower, is a striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy, star-shaped flowers that bloom in vibrant shades of yellow and orange. The plant has a woody stem with broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine layer of white hair. It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountainous forests.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
