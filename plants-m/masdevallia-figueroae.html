Masdevallia figueroae

Masdevallia figueroae, commonly known as the Figueroa's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often colorful floral displays. The plant typically produces intricate, spurred orchid flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. Its leaves are usually elongated and arranged in a rosette pattern, adapted to the humid, shaded conditions of its natural habitat.

Masdevallia figueroae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
