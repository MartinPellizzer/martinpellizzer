Maxillaria hennisiana

Maxillaria hennisiana, commonly known as the Hennis's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate floral structure and vibrant coloration. The plant typically features slender, upright pseudobulbs that support narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually pale pink to white, with distinctive markings that enhance its ornamental appeal.

Maxillaria hennisiana thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires specific care to flourish in cultivation.
