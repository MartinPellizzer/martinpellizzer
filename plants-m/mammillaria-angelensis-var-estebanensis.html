Mammillaria angelensis var. estebanensis

Mammillaria angelensis var. estebanensis, commonly known as the Esteban's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with prominent white to yellowish spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its delicate structure, it is often grown as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
