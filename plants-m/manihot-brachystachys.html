Manihot brachystachys

Manihot brachystachys, commonly known as the dwarf arrowleaf manioc, is a perennial shrub native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its broad, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often cultivated for its starchy roots, which are used in various culinary applications.
