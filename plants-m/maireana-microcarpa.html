Maireana microcarpa

Maireana microcarpa, commonly known as the small-fruited maireana, is a shrub native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its small, oval leaves and compact growth habit. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are often followed by tiny, woody fruits. It is typically found in arid and semi-arid regions, adapted to thrive in harsh, dry conditions.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies in native Australian flora.
