Minuartia Imbricata Var. Koreana

Minuartia imbricata var. koreana is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Korea. It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is known for its small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in the summer months. This variety is distinguished by its compact growth habit and distinctive leaf arrangement. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species.
