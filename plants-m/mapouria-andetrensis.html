Mapouria andetrensis

Mapouria andetrensis, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a species of orchid native to Madagascar.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern.

Mapouria andetrensis thrives in humid, shaded environments and is highly valued for its ornamental beauty.
