Maxillaria bradei

Maxillaria bradei, commonly known as the Brade's orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces attractive, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria bradei is appreciated for its ornamental value and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral structure.
