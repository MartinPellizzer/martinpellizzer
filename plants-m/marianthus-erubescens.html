Marianthus erubescens

Marianthus erubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered marianthus, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a smooth, glossy surface.

The flowers are borne in clusters and are often used in ornamental gardening due to their vibrant hues and fragrance.
