Muhlenbergia ciurea

Muhlenbergia ciurea, commonly known as the purple muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems with narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically a deep blue-green color. The plant produces fluffy, silver-gray seed heads that are highly ornamental and attract wildlife. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Muhlenbergia ciurea is often used in native gardens for its aesthetic appeal and ecological benefits.
