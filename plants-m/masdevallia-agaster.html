Masdevallia agaster

Masdevallia agaster, commonly known as the starry masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. The plant produces a compact, upright inflorescence with numerous small, clustered blooms. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia agaster thrives in cool, high-altitude environments and requires high humidity and indirect light for optimal growth.
