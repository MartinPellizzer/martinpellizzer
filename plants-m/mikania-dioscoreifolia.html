Mikania dioscoreifolia

Mikania dioscoreifolia, commonly known as creeping morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a vine that produces small, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed soils.

Mikania dioscoreifolia is also recognized for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
