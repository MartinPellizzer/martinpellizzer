Melissa debilis

Melissa debilis, commonly known as lemon balm, is a low-growing perennial herb native to southern Europe and western Asia.

It features fragrant, small, bright green leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal use.

Melissa debilis is valued for its calming properties and is often used in teas and aromatherapy.
