Morrenia stormiana

Morrenia stormiana, commonly known as the storm's morrenia, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean islands.

It is characterized by its striking, showy yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with woody stems and thick, leathery leaves. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in coastal areas and disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
