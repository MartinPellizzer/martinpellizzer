Mammillaria ancistrina

Mammillaria ancistrina, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed stem and a dense covering of white spines. The plant typically grows to a height of around 10 centimeters and features a pale green to bluish-green color. It produces yellow flowers in the spring, which are followed by red fruits.

This cactus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
