Masdevallia lindenii var. harryana

Masdevallia lindenii var. harryana, commonly known as the Harryana masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety petals that often display a combination of deep purples, reds, and whites. The plant typically blooms during the cooler months, producing intricate inflorescences with fragrant, showy flowers. It thrives in high-altitude environments with cool temperatures and high humidity, making it a challenging but rewarding species for orchid enthusiasts.

Its unique coloration and delicate appearance have made it a prized addition to specialized botanical collections.
