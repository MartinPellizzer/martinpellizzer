Magnolia Kisopa

Magnolia kisopa is a rare and striking plant native to the rainforests of western Africa. It is known for its large, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring and summer. The tree can grow up to 20 meters tall with a broad, spreading canopy. Its leaves are glossy and dark green, providing a lush backdrop for its showy blossoms. This species is highly valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and specialty nurseries.
