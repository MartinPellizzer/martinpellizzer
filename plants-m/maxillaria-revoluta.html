Maxillaria revoluta

Maxillaria revoluta, commonly known as the curled lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the summer months, with flowers emerging from the axils of the leaves. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette pattern and are covered in a fine, velvety texture.

Maxillaria revoluta is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
