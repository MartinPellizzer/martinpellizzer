Myrtus excelsa

Myrtus excelsa, commonly known as the Christmas tree or the myrtle tree, is a species of evergreen shrub or small tree native to the Canary Islands.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, dark green leaves and small white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It thrives in coastal areas with well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value and medicinal properties.
