Madaria elegans var. corymbosa

Madaria elegans var. corymbosa, commonly known as the coral tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, feathery inflorescences that resemble coral in color and structure. The plant produces clusters of small, bright red flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its leaves are alternate, simple, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
