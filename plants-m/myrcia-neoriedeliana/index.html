Myrcia Neoriedeliana

Myrcia neoriedeliana is a rare shrub native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil. It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, known for its aromatic leaves and ornamental value. This plant is characterized by its dense foliage and small, fragrant flowers. Due to habitat loss, it faces significant threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserve this unique species for future generations.
