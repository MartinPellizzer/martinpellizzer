Miconia rubens

Miconia rubens, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green with a reddish underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are often surrounded by bright red bracts, making it visually striking. Its fruit is a woody capsule that splits open to release seeds.

Miconia rubens is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
