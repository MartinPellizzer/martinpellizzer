Magnolia duperreana

Magnolia duperreana, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, fragrant, white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular ornamental tree in gardens.
