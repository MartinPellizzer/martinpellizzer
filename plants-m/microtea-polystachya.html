Microtea polystachya

Microtea polystachya, commonly known as the slender bamboo orchid, is a flowering plant belonging to the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its tall, slender stems and clusters of small, greenish-white flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest undergrowth. Its leaves are linear and arranged in a spiral pattern along the stem.

Microtea polystachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its unique appearance.
