Muraltia leptorhiza

Muraltia leptorhiza, commonly known as the slender muraltia, is a climbing shrub native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its pod-bearing fruits and nitrogen-fixing capabilities. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink. Its fruit is a legume, containing several seeds enclosed in a fibrous pod.

Muraltia leptorhiza is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
