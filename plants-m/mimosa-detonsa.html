Mimosa detonsa

Mimosa detonsa, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Mimosa detonsa is often used in ecological restoration projects due to its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.
