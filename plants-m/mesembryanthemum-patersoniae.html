Mesembryanthemum patersoniae

Mesembryanthemum patersoniae, commonly known as the Paterson's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, bluish-green leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements make it a distinctive addition to both natural and cultivated landscapes.
