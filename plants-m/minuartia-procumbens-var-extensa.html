Minuartia procumbens var. extensa

Minuartia procumbens var. extensa, commonly known as the extended sand-flower, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that bloom in shades of white or pink. This variety is notable for its adaptability to extreme climates and its role in stabilizing soil in barren landscapes.
