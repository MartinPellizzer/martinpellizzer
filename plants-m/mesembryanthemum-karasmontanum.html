Mesembryanthemum karasmontanum

Mesembryanthemum karasmontanum, commonly known as the Karasmontan sand rose, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, thick leaves that store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically during the warmer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it an attractive and low-maintenance ornamental plant.
