Mitrasacme diffusa

Mitrasacme diffusa, commonly known as the ghost plant, is a unique species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, translucent leaves that appear almost ethereal in appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires consistent moisture to flourish.

Due to its rare and elusive nature, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
