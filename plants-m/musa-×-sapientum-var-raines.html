Musa × sapientum var. raines

Musa × sapientum var. raines, commonly known as the 'Raines' banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is a cultivated variety of the common banana, characterized by its large, elongated, yellow-orange fruit with a sweet and creamy texture. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, with a pseudostem formed by the tightly packed leaves. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained, fertile soil for optimal growth.

The fruit is often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications due to its flavor and versatility.
