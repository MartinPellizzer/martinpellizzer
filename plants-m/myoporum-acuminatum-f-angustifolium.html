Myoporum acuminatum f. angustifolium

Myoporum acuminatum f. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence. The plant produces small, pale green flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to coastal environments and can tolerate salt spray and sandy soils.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for coastal landscaping.
