Mammillaria elongata var. anguinea

Mammillaria elongata var. anguinea, commonly known as the long-spined hedgehog cactus, is a succulent native to Mexico.

It features a cylindrical stem with numerous small, spiny tubercles that give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its slow growth rate and unique morphology make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
