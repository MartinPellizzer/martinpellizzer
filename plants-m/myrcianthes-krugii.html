Myrcianthes krugii

Myrcianthes krugii, commonly known as Krug's myrcia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Caribbean islands.

It belongs to the family Myrtaceae, which includes plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a whitish, powdery bloom. Its fragrant flowers, which are typically pink or white, are clustered in dense inflorescences.

The fruit is a small, three-lobed berry that turns red when mature, and it is an important food source for various bird species.
