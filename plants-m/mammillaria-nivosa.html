Mammillaria nivosa

Mammillaria nivosa, commonly known as the snow mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and white to pale blue spines that give it a frosty appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice for succulent collectors.
