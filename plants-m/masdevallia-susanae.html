Masdevallia susanae

Masdevallia susanae, commonly known as the Susanae Masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It belongs to the Epidendroideae subfamily and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful flower structure. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are usually small but striking, with a unique arrangement of petals and sepals that contribute to its ornamental value.

Due to its limited habitat and susceptibility to environmental changes, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
