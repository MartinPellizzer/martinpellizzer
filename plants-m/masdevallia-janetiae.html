Masdevallia janetiae

Masdevallia janetiae, commonly known as Janet's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid with a distinctive, intricate flower structure. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink, with intricate patterns and a fragrant scent.

It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires specific light and temperature conditions for optimal growth.
