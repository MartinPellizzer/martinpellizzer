Millettia elongatistyla

Millettia elongatistyla, commonly known as the long-styled millettia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and distinctive floral structure. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemose inflorescences. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind.

Millettia elongatistyla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
