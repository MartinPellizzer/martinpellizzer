Mimosa linearis

Mimosa linearis, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It features delicate, fern-like leaves that are linear and often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or wetland habitats.

Mimosa linearis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits in native plant gardens.
