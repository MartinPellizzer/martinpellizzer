Meryta latifolia

Meryta latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf meryta, is a flowering plant native to the Pacific Islands.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal areas and rocky habitats. The plant has broad, leathery leaves that are often glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small and clustered, usually white or pale yellow, and it produces small, round fruits.

Meryta latifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in tropical gardens.
