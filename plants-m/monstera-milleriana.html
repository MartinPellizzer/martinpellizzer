Monstera milleriana

Monstera milleriana, commonly known as the Swiss cheese plant, is a tropical climbing plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that develop distinctive holes and splits as they mature. The plant has a vigorous growth habit and can reach heights of several meters when supported by a trellis or other structure. Its foliage is evergreen, providing year-round visual interest in both indoor and outdoor settings.

Monstera milleriana thrives in bright, indirect light and requires regular watering to maintain its lush appearance.
