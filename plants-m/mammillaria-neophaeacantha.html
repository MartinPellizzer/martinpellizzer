Mammillaria neophaeacantha

Mammillaria neophaeacantha, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems that are covered in white to pale pink spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
