Mnesithea annua

Mnesithea annua, commonly known as the annual mnesithea, is an herbaceous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and features opposite, lance-shaped leaves that are often hairy. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom from summer to autumn. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Mnesithea annua is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
