Maxillariella sanguinea

Maxillariella sanguinea, commonly known as the red-stemmed orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments. The plant features dark green, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of fragrant, pinkish-red flowers. Its blooming season usually occurs during the summer months.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
