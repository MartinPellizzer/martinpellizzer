Mutisia inflexa

Mutisia inflexa, commonly known as the yellow mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a shrub-like growth habit with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

Mutisia inflexa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
