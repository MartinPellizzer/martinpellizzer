Maranta unilateralis

Maranta unilateralis, commonly known as the upright arrowroot, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive, arrow-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Maranta unilateralis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its ease of care and aesthetic qualities.
