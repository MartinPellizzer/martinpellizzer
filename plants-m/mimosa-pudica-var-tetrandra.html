Mimosa pudica var. tetrandra

Mimosa pudica var. tetrandra, commonly known as the sensitive plant or害羞草, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched, a response triggered by mechanical stimulation. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the summer months. It thrives in warm, humid environments and prefers well-drained soil.

This variety is often cultivated as a decorative plant due to its unique sensitivity and aesthetic appeal.
