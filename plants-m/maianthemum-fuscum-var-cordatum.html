Maianthemum fuscum var. cordatum

Maianthemum fuscum var. cordatum, commonly known as the heartleaf false shamrock, is a species of flowering plant in the family Convallariaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves that are heart-shaped, giving it its common name. This variety is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage.
