Myrcianthes cisplatensis var. latifolia

Myrcianthes cisplatensis var. latifolia, commonly known as the broad-leaved myrcianthes, is a species native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree characterized by its lanceolate, leathery leaves that are broad at the base. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by small, round fruits containing a single seed. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in understory layers of tropical forests.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has some traditional uses in local communities.
