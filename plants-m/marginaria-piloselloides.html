Marginaria piloselloides

Marginaria piloselloides, commonly known as the hairy groundsel, is a flowering plant native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the family Asteraceae, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is characterized by its composite flower heads. It is an herbaceous perennial that produces small, wind-dispersed seeds.
