Magnolia steyermarkii

Magnolia steyermarkii, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to central Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
