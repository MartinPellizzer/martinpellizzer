Moraea filicaulis

Moraea filicaulis, commonly known as the broad-leaved moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often violet or purple, tubular flowers. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.
