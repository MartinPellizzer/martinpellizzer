Microcerasus Prostrata F. Griffithii

Griffith's japanese maple, scientifically known as Microcerasus prostrata f. griffithii, is a unique and striking ornamental plant prized for its delicate foliage and vibrant seasonal color changes. Native to the mountainous regions of eastern Asia, this variety is a popular choice for gardeners seeking both beauty and resilience in their landscapes. Its compact growth habit and intricate leaf patterns make it a standout addition to shaded gardens and rockeries. Despite its small size, the plant offers a dramatic visual impact, especially during the autumn when its leaves turn brilliant shades of red and orange. With proper care, Griffith's japanese maple can thrive for decades, making it a rewarding long-term addition to any collection.
