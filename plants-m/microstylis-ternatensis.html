Microstylis ternatensis

Microstylis ternatensis, commonly known as the Ternate microstylis, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial grass that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, narrow leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a single spikelet, which is usually terminal and sometimes accompanied by a few lateral branches.

Microstylis ternatensis is often found in moist, well-drained soils in forested or disturbed areas.
