Masdevallia crassicaudis

Masdevallia crassicaudis, commonly known as the thick-tailed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and its striking, often colorful flowers. The plant typically features long, fleshy pseudobulbs and narrow, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on terminal spikes and can vary in color from white to pink and purple.

Masdevallia crassicaudis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
