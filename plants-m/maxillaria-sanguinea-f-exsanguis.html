Maxillaria sanguinea f. exsanguis

Maxillaria sanguinea f. exsanguis, commonly known as the blood-red orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, deep red flowers with a contrasting white or pink lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its inflorescence consists of several showy blooms that appear during the dry season.

The botanical features of this orchid make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its vibrant color and unique floral structure.
