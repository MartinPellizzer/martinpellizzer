Mesembryanthemum pillansii

Mesembryanthemum pillansii, commonly known as the Karoo ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid conditions. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well-suited for dry, rocky environments.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping and rock gardens.
