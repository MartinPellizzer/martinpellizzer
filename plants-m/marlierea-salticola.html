Marlierea salticola

Marlierea salticola, commonly known as the salticola or the jumping plant.

It is a flowering plant native to South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
