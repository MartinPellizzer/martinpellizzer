Mostuea sennii

Mostuea sennii, commonly known as the African lily, is a striking ornamental plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, erect stems and large, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant produces narrow, sword-shaped leaves that are often variegated with yellow or white markings. Mostuea sennii thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.

It is often cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in gardens and as a cut flower.
