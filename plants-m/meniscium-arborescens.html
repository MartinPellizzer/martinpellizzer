Meniscium arborescens

Meniscium arborescens, commonly known as the tree meniscium, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is often found in rocky soils and is adapted to dry, sunny environments.
