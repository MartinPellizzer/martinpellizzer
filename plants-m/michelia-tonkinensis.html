Michelia tonkinensis

Michelia tonkinensis, commonly known as the Vietnamese Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are usually pubescent on the underside. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
