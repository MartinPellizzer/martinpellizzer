Mimosa rubicaulis subsp. rubicaulis

Mimosa rubicaulis subsp.

rubicaulis, commonly known as the red mimosa, is a shrub native to southern Africa. It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers.

The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
