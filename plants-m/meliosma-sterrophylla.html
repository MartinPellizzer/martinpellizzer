Meliosma sterrophylla

Meliosma sterrophylla, commonly known as the broadleaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters.
