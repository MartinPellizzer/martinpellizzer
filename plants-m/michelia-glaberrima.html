Michelia glaberrima

Michelia glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to eastern Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually oval to elliptical in shape. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in traditional Chinese medicine.
