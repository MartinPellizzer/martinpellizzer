Mussaenda scabridior

Mussaenda scabridior, commonly known as the rough mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically has hairy, rough-textured leaves that are opposite in arrangement. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forest understories or along stream banks.

Mussaenda scabridior is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
