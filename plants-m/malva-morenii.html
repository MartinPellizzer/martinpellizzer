Malva morenii

Malva morenii, commonly known as the Moreton's mallows, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its broad, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Malva morenii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
