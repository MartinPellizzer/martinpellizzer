Maxillaria wageneri

Maxillaria wageneri, commonly known as the Wagener's Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically has a single pseudobulb with a few leaves and blooms during the warmer months. Its flowers are often borne on lateral spikes and have a distinctive, intricate pattern on the petals and sepals.

Maxillaria wageneri is appreciated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
