Maxillaria augustae-victoriae

Maxillaria augustae-victoriae, commonly known as the Victoria's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, dark violet flowers with white markings. The plant features strap-like leaves that are dark green and slightly corrugated. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria augustae-victoriae is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral display.
