Malope grandiflora

Malope grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered malope, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and showy, yellow-orange flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Malope grandiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its vibrant floral display.
