Mitella makinoi

Mitella makinoi, commonly known as the Japanese mitella, is a perennial herb native to Japan.

It belongs to the Saxifragaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, clustered inflorescences. The plant features small, white to pale pink flowers with five petals, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are usually ovate to elliptic, with a smooth or slightly toothed margin.

Mitella makinoi thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas.
