Maxillaria trinitatis

Maxillaria trinitatis, commonly known as the Trinidad orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Caribbean islands, particularly Trinidad and Tobago.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, strap-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, well-drained soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria trinitatis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
