Machaerium caratinganum

Machaerium caratinganum, commonly known as the pink machaerium, is a tropical perennial plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, pink or white flowers arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are trifoliate, with three oval-shaped leaflets, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Machaerium caratinganum is often used in ornamental gardening for its attractive foliage and flowering habits.
