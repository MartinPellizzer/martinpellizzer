Mimosa malitiosa

Mimosa malitiosa, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding shut when disturbed. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate ornamental quality. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Its rapid growth and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for erosion control and landscaping.
