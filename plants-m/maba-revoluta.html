Maba revoluta

Maba revoluta, commonly known as the African sandalwood, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the family Burseraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, dark-colored heartwood. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The tree is valued for its fragrant timber and is often found in dry, open woodlands and savannas.
