Monopsis stellarioides var. schimperiana

Monopsis stellarioides var. schimperiana, commonly known as Schimper's starwort, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with slender stems and small, star-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of tiny, white or pale pink flowers, typically arranged in racemes. It is native to southern Africa and thrives in rocky or sandy soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it prefers cool and moist conditions.
