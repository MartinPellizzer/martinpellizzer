Mammillaria columnaroides

Mammillaria columnaroides, commonly known as the columnar mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem that can grow up to 30 centimeters in height. The plant features dense clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its spines are typically yellow to brown and are relatively short compared to other Mammillaria species.

This plant thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.
