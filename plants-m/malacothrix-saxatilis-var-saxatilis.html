Malacothrix saxatilis var. saxatilis

Malacothrix saxatilis var. saxatilis, commonly known as the rock malacothrix, is a perennial flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and clusters of small, tubular flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or mountainous regions. It is adapted to arid conditions, making it drought-tolerant and suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in harsh environments.
