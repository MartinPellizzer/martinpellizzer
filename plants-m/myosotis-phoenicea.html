Myosotis phoenicea

Myosotis phoenicea, commonly known as the scarlet forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, showy flowers that range in color from pink to deep red. The plant typically grows in rocky or coastal areas, thriving in well-drained soils. Its leaves are lance-shaped and slightly hairy, while its stems are upright and often branched.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
