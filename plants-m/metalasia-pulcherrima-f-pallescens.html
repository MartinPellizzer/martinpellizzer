Metalasia pulcherrima f. pallescens

Metalasia pulcherrima f. pallescens, commonly known as the pale form of the flame tree, is a unique variant of the flame tree species native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its pale yellow to white flowers, which contrast with the typical red or orange blooms of the standard variety. The plant belongs to the Fabaceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in warm, humid climates. Its leaves are compound, with small, oval-shaped leaflets, and it produces long, pendulous racemes of flowers.

This form is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its distinctive coloration and visual appeal.
