Mimosa claussenii var. corneiseta

Mimosa claussenii var. corneiseta, commonly known as the horned mimosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens, though it can be invasive in certain regions.
