Moraea tricuspidata

Moraea tricuspidata, commonly known as the Three-lobed Moraea, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

Moraea tricuspidata is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its vibrant, long-lasting blooms.
