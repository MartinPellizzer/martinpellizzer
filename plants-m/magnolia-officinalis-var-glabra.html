Magnolia officinalis var. glabra

Magnolia officinalis var. glabra, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the bare-branch magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, fragrant white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant is known for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal. Its leaves are alternate, ovate, and have a serrated margin.

The tree is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its medicinal properties.
