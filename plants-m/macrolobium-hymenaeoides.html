Macrolobium hymenaeoides

Macrolobium hymenaeoides, commonly known as the African ironwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, dark bark and dense, hard wood that is highly valued for its durability. The tree has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth surface. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by pods containing flat, winged seeds.

This species is often found in dry woodlands and is known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
