Maba tenuinervia

Maba tenuinervia, commonly known as the narrow-veined maba, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and other hardwoods. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves with prominent venation. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically in pendulous inflorescences.
