Micranthes yukonensis

Micranthes yukonensis, commonly known as Yukon mountain sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the Yukon region of Canada.

It is a member of the Brassicaceae family, characterized by its small, cruciform flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to cold, alpine environments. It produces small, green leaves with a hairy texture, and its flowers are usually white or pale purple.
