Myoporum stokesii

Myoporum stokesii, commonly known as Stokes' myoporum, is a flowering plant native to the coastal regions of New Zealand.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters, featuring dense, evergreen foliage with narrow, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pale yellow, which bloom in late winter to early spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or coastal habitats.

Myoporum stokesii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its hardy nature and visual interest.
