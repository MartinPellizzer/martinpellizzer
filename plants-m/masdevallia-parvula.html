Masdevallia parvula

Masdevallia parvula, commonly known as the miniature masdevallia, is a small orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, compact growth habit and vibrant, often colorful flowers. The plant typically produces small, intricate blooms in shades of pink, red, and white, which are highly prized by orchid enthusiasts. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its rarity and specific growing conditions, it is a sought-after species in horticulture.
