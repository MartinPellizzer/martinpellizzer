Masdevallia edwallii

Masdevallia edwallii, commonly known as the Edwall's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, often brightly colored flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically grows as a miniature epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its native habitat. Its leaves are usually fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Due to its rarity and unique beauty, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
