Mesembryanthemum senarium

Mesembryanthemum senarium, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, silver-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal make it a versatile addition to Mediterranean and warm climate gardens.
