Miconia brevitheca

Miconia brevitheca, commonly known as the short-leaf miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that are often borne on short peduncles. Miconia brevitheca is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.
