Myoporum tubiflorum

Myoporum tubiflorum, commonly known as the coastal myoporum, is a shrub native to coastal regions of New Zealand.

It has aromatic, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pale purple, which bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to coastal conditions with salt spray.

Myoporum tubiflorum is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value.
