Miltonia × festiva

Miltonia × festiva, commonly known as the pink-veined orchid, is a hybrid orchid species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety petals with distinctive pink veins. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are usually pink or purple and bloom in clusters during the late winter to early spring.

Miltonia × festiva is prized for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its beauty in both home gardens and greenhouses.
