Mandevilla exilicaulis

Mandevilla exilicaulis, commonly known as the slender mandevilla, is a climbing vine native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from pink to red, often with a contrasting throat. The plant has smooth, slightly hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Mandevilla exilicaulis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
