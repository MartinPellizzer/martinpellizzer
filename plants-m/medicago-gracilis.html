Medicago gracilis

Medicago gracilis, commonly known as slender medick, is a low-growing perennial legume native to Europe, Asia, and North Africa.

It features small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom from spring to summer. The plant is characterized by its delicate, creeping stems and is often found in grasslands, meadows, and disturbed soils. It has a shallow root system and is well adapted to a variety of soil types.

Medicago gracilis is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, which benefits soil fertility.
