Melia koetjape

Melia koetjape, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Sapotaceae family and is characterized by its smooth, gray bark and large, palmate leaves. The tree produces clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by woody, spiny fruits. Its ornamental value is notable, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

The plant is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
