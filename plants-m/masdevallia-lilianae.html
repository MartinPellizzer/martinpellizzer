Masdevallia lilianae

Masdevallia lilianae, commonly known as the Lillian's masdevallia, is a rare orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, lance-shaped leaves and delicate, spurred flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. The plant thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation, making it a challenging species to cultivate. It belongs to the Masdevallia genus, which is known for its diverse and often striking floral forms.

Due to its limited habitat and specific care requirements, Masdevallia lilianae is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.
