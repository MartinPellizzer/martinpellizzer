Malaxis miyakei

Malaxis miyakei, commonly known as the Miyake's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Philippines.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a short, erect stem and few leaves. The plant produces delicate, greenish-yellow flowers with a short spur, typical of the Malaxis genus. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in mountainous regions.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
