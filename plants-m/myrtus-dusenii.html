Myrtus dusenii

Myrtus dusenii, commonly known as the Dusen's myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the island of Socotra.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, leathery leaves and small, white to pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, fleshy berries that are typically red or purple when mature. It thrives in arid and rocky environments, showcasing adaptations to its unique ecological niche.

Myrtus dusenii is notable for its ornamental value and is also of interest for its potential medicinal properties.
