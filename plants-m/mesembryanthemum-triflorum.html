Mesembryanthemum triflorum

Mesembryanthemum triflorum, commonly known as the three-flowered iceplant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum triflorum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
