Melastoma agrestis

Melastoma agrestis, commonly known as the rough melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and often have a rough texture.

Melastoma agrestis is known for its ability to thrive in a variety of soil types and is sometimes used in horticulture for its ornamental value.
