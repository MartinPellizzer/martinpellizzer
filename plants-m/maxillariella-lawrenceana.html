Maxillariella lawrenceana

Maxillariella lawrenceana, commonly known as the Lawrence's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green, often with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette, adapted to store water in arid environments.

It thrives in shaded, moist habitats and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
