Maughania prostrata

Maughania prostrata, commonly known as the prostrate maughania, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous perennial that typically forms a dense mat of foliage. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are usually pale yellow to orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Due to its compact growth habit, it is sometimes used in ornamental gardening for ground cover.
