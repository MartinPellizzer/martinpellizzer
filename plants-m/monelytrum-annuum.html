Monelytrum annuum

Monelytrum annuum, commonly known as annual bluegrass, is a cool-season grass native to Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its fine, slender blades and bluish-green color, which distinguish it from other grass species. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in lawns, gardens, and along roadsides. It is an aggressive colonizer and can quickly form dense mats, making it both a desirable and problematic species in different settings.

Monelytrum annuum is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
