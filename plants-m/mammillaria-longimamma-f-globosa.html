Mammillaria longimamma f. globosa

Mammillaria longimamma f. globosa, commonly known as the globular mammillaria, is a compact, spherical cactus native to Mexico.

It features short, cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles, which are often adorned with fine, white hairs. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its unique globular shape and delicate appearance make it a popular choice among succulent enthusiasts.
