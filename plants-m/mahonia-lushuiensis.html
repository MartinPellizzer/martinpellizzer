Mahonia lushuiensis

Mahonia lushuiensis, commonly known as the Lushui Mahonia, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves that are compound and deeply lobed. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are clustered in branched inflorescences. Its berries are dark blue and edible, though they are often used in traditional medicine.

Mahonia lushuiensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various soil types.
