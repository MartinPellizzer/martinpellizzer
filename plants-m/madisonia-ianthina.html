Madisonia ianthina

Madisonia ianthina, commonly known as the blue-eyed grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the family Xanthorrhoeaceae and is characterized by its slender, grass-like stems and clusters of small, blue-violet flowers.
