Mimosa invisa var. macrostachya

Mimosa invisa var. macrostachya, commonly known as the giant mimosa or velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that fold shut when touched, a response triggered by mechanical stimulation. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in dense, spherical clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it a popular ornamental plant in gardens.
