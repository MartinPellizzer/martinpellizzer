Millettia thonneri

Millettia thonneri, commonly known as the African velvet bean, is a climbing legume native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that develop into pod fruits containing edible seeds. The plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is adaptable to various soil types.

Millettia thonneri is also valued as a forage crop for livestock due to its nutritional content.
