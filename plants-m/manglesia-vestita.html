Manglesia vestita

Manglesia vestita, commonly known as the velvet mangle, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Manglesia vestita is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine in some regions.
