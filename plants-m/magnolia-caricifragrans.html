Magnolia caricifragrans

Magnolia caricifragrans, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the fragrant magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual appeal.
