Myristica clemensii

Myristica clemensii, commonly known as the Clements' nutmeg, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myristicaceae.

It is native to the island of Madagascar and is characterized by its aromatic seeds, which are used in traditional medicine. The plant produces a fruit that contains a single seed, which is encased in a thick, fleshy aril. Myristica clemensii is a small tree or shrub, typically growing to a height of about 3 meters.
