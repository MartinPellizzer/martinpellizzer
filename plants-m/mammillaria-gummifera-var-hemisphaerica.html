Mammillaria gummifera var. hemisphaerica

Mammillaria gummifera var. hemisphaerica, commonly known as the globular mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, globular stem covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring or summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
