Malcolmia orsiniana

Malcolmia orsiniana, commonly known as the desert pincushion, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of the Middle East and North Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it well adapted to harsh desert environments. Its compact growth habit and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits to pollinators.
