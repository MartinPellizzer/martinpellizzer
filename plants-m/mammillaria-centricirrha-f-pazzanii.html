Mammillaria centricirrha f. pazzanii

Mammillaria centricirrha f. pazzanii, commonly known as the Pazzanii mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with dense, white to pale yellow spines and a distinctive, woolly appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its spiny structure. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
