Malacothamnus Densiflorus

Malacothamnus densiflorus is a striking member of the sunflower family native to the western United States. This perennial shrub is known for its dense clusters of vibrant purple flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in rocky soils and is often found in dry, sunny habitats like hillsides and meadows. Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a popular choice for native gardens and ecological restoration projects. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively obscure compared to more widely cultivated plants.
