Melanosticta

Melanosticta, commonly known as the black-stained orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

These plants are characterized by their striking dark pigmentation, particularly in their petals and sepals, which often appear black or deep purple. They are native to tropical and subtropical regions, primarily in Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands. Melanosticta species typically grow as epiphytes, attaching themselves to trees in humid environments.

Their unique coloration and habitat make them popular among orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
