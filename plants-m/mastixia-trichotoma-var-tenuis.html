Mastixia trichotoma var. tenuis

Mastixia trichotoma var. tenuis, commonly known as the slender mastixia, is a shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, pale greenish-yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in heathlands and sandy soils, preferring well-drained conditions. It produces small, woody fruits that contain seeds dispersed by wind.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local wildlife.
