Mammillaria rekoi var. leptacantha

Mammillaria rekoi var. leptacantha, commonly known as the narrow-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with cylindrical, spiny tubercles that are typically green to bluish-green in color. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cactus collections.
