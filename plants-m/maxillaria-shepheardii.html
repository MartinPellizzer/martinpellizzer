Maxillaria shepheardii

Maxillaria shepheardii, commonly known as the Shepherd's Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, butterfly-shaped flowers. The plant typically produces showy, violet to purple blooms with yellow markings, which attract pollinators such as bees and hummingbirds. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

Maxillaria shepheardii is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
