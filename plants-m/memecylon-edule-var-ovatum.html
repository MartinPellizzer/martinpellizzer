Memecylon edule var. ovatum

Memecylon edule var. ovatum, commonly known as the small-leaf memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves and showy, five-petaled flowers. The plant produces small, edible berries that are often used in local cuisines. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value and edible fruits, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and agricultural settings.
