Melicope alpestris

Melicope alpestris, commonly known as the mountain melicope, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands.

It typically grows in coastal and lowland areas, often in sandy or rocky soils. The plant has simple, opposite leaves that are usually leathery and dark green in color. Its small, fragrant flowers are followed by spherical, fleshy fruits that contain several seeds.

Melicope alpestris is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
