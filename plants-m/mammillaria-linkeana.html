Mammillaria linkeana

Mammillaria linkeana, commonly known as the Linke's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a smooth, greenish-gray body and white spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 centimeters and features a dense covering of white bristles. It produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red or orange fruits.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
