Machilus minkweiensis

Machilus minkweiensis, commonly known as the Minkwei's laurel, is a small evergreen tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurels, avocados, and cinnamon trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It produces small, yellowish flowers followed by dark blue berries.

Machilus minkweiensis is often found in subtropical forests and is valued for its ornamental qualities and potential medicinal uses.
