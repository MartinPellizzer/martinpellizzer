Manihot paviifolia

Manihot paviifolia, commonly known as the pink arrowleaf, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that often display a striking pink or red coloration on the underside. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are fibrous and can be used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.
