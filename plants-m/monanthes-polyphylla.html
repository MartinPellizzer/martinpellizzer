Monanthes polyphylla

Monanthes polyphylla, commonly known as the many-leaved bellflower, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and prefers full sun to partial shade. Its leaves are often lobed and can vary in shape depending on the environment.

Monanthes polyphylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
