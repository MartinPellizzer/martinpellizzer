Maerua currorii

Maerua currorii, commonly known as the red karka tree, is a shrub or small tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, waxy leaves that help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in harsh, semi-arid conditions.

Maerua currorii plays an important role in local ecosystems by providing habitat and food for various animals.
