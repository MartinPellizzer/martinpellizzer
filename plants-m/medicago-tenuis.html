Medicago tenuis

Medicago tenuis, commonly known as narrowleaf Medick, is a flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically reaches heights of 10 to 30 centimeters. The plant has slender, upright stems with small, trifoliate leaves composed of three oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are small, yellowish-white, and clustered in dense, spherical heads.

Medicago tenuis is often found in dry, disturbed soils and is adapted to thrive in temperate climates.
