Microstemon

Microstemon, commonly known as the fairy lantern or dwarf lantern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Myrtaceae.

It consists of several species native to Australia, characterized by their small, showy flowers that resemble tiny lanterns. The plants typically grow as shrubs or small trees, with aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pink blossoms. Microstemon species are often found in coastal or sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Their unique floral structure and ornamental appeal make them popular in horticulture and botanical gardens.
