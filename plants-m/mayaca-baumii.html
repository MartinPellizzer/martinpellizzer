Mayaca baumii

Mayaca baumii, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, pendulous clusters of yellow flowers that resemble showers of golden droplets. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil.

Mayaca baumii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and can be grown as a shade-tolerant shrub or small tree.
