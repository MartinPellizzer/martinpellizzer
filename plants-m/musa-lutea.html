Musa lutea

Musa lutea, commonly known as the yellow banana, is a species of flowering plant in the genus Musa.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its large, yellowish-orange banana-like fruits. The plant produces long, narrow leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. It thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil.

Musa lutea is primarily cultivated for its edible fruits, which have a mild, sweet flavor.
