Mettenia globosa

Mettenia globosa, commonly known as the globular mettenia, is a unique plant native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a succulent shrub characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and fleshy, spiny leaves. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. Its thick, water-storing leaves help it survive in arid environments, making it well-adapted to dry conditions.

Mettenia globosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its distinctive appearance and low maintenance requirements.
