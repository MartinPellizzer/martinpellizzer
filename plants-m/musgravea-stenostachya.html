Musgravea stenostachya

Musgravea stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stemmed musgravea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered greenish-white flowers that bloom during the cooler months. The plant has fibrous roots that help it retain moisture in dry environments, and its leaves are narrow and often covered in a fine layer of wax. It is typically found in rocky soils and is adapted to withstand high temperatures and low rainfall.

Musgravea stenostachya is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in xeriscaped gardens.
