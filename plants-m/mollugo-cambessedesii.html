Mollugo cambessedesii

Mollugo cambessedesii, commonly known as the desert sandpurslane, is a low-growing annual herb native to arid regions of the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces yellow flowers that are typically borne on short pedicels, and it is adapted to thrive in sandy, well-drained soils. Its rapid growth and ability to colonize disturbed areas make it a common weed in desert environments.
