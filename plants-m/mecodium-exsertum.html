Mecodium exsertum

Mecodium exsertum, commonly known as the narrowleaf mecodium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, with a slightly hairy surface.

Mecodium exsertum is typically found in sandy or gravelly soils in open habitats.
