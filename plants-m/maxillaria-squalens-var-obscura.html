Maxillaria squalens var. obscura

Maxillaria squalens var. obscura, commonly known as the dark squalens orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a compact, upright growth habit and produces clusters of dark purple to black flowers. The plant features long, slender leaves and has a unique appearance due to its dark-colored blooms. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

This variety is prized for its striking coloration and is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
