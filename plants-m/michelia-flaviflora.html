Michelia flaviflora

Michelia flaviflora, commonly known as the golden michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy and dark green. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Michelia flaviflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
