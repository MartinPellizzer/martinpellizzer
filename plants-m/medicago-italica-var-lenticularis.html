Medicago italica var. lenticularis

Medicago italica var. lenticularis, commonly known as the lentil vetch, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from pale pink to white. The plant produces lens-shaped seed pods, which give it its specific variety name, lenticularis. It is a hardy annual that thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

This plant is also valued for its nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
