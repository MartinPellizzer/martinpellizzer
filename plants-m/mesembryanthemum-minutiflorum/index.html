Mesembryanthemum Minutiflorum

Mesembryanthemum minutiflorum is a small, flowering plant known for its unique appearance and resilience. Native to southern Africa, it thrives in arid and rocky environments. Its tiny, clustered flowers bloom in shades of pink and white, creating a striking visual effect. The plant is also called the "ice plant" due to its waxy leaves that reflect sunlight. It is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its low maintenance and drought tolerance.
