Michelia macclurei var. sublanea

Michelia macclurei var. sublanea, commonly known as the fragrant magnolia, is a variant of the Michelia macclurei species native to China.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth margin.

The botanical features of this plant make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
