Mimosa callidryas

Mimosa callidryas, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and delicate pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, bean-like pods that contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.

The plant is also noted for its ability to fix nitrogen, contributing to soil fertility.
