Myrcia huanocensis

Myrcia huanocensis, commonly known as the Huanocense Myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid forest understories.

Myrcia huanocensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and has potential uses in traditional medicine.
