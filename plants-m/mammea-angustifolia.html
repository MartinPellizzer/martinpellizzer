Mammea angustifolia

Mammea angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mammea, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Clusiaceae and is characterized by its elongated, narrow leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its fruit is a woody capsule containing several seeds.

Mammea angustifolia is also known for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
