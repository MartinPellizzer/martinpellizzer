Macaranga marikoensis

Macaranga marikoensis, commonly known as the Mariko's makara, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including parts of Indonesia and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptical in shape. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers clustered in cyathia, which are typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.
