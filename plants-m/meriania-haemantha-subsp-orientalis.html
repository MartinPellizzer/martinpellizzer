Meriania haemantha subsp. orientalis

Meriania haemantha subsp.

orientalis, commonly known as the oriental meriania, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia. It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that are typically red or pink in color.

The plant has palmate leaves that are often deeply lobed, contributing to its ornamental appeal. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial shade.

The species is valued in horticulture for its beauty and is sometimes cultivated as a decorative plant.
