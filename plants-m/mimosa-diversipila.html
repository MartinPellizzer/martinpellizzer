Mimosa diversipila

Mimosa diversipila, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its bipinnate leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in clusters. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Mimosa diversipila is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique foliage and rapid growth habit.
