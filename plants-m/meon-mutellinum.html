Meon mutellinum

Meon mutellinum, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of small, white to pale purple flowers. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically yellow or orange when ripe. It is often found in dry, rocky soils and thrives in full sun.

Meon mutellinum is known for its toxic properties, making it potentially harmful to both humans and animals.
