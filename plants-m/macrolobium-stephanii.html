Macrolobium stephanii

Macrolobium stephanii, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a tropical shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with broad, velvety foliage, and its flowers are typically yellow or orange in color. It produces elongated pods containing edible seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a food source.

The plant is adapted to arid conditions and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.
