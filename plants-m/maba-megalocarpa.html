Maba megalocarpa

Maba megalocarpa, commonly known as the giant maba tree, is a species native to the tropical rainforests of Southeast Asia.

It is a large deciduous tree that can grow up to 30 meters in height, with a broad, spreading canopy. The tree produces small, yellowish flowers followed by large, woody fruits containing several seeds. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a rough texture, while its bark is thick and fissured.

The plant is valued for its timber and has traditional uses in local medicine.
