Mertensia platyphylla

Mertensia platyphylla, commonly known as the broadleaf bluebell, is a herbaceous perennial native to the eastern United States.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue to purple in color, blooming in early spring. The plant has lance-shaped leaves that are broad at the base and taper towards the tip. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Mertensia platyphylla is valued in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
