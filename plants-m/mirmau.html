Mirmau

Mirmau, commonly known as the African violet, is a tropical plant native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the family Gesneriaceae and is characterized by its velvety, waxy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. Mirmau is a popular ornamental plant due to its beauty and ease of care.
