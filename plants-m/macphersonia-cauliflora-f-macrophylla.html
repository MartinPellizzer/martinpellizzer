Macphersonia cauliflora f. macrophylla

Macphersonia cauliflora f. macrophylla, commonly known as the giant caladium, is a striking ornamental plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 inches in length, showcasing vibrant colors such as red, purple, and white. The plant is a variegated form of Macphersonia cauliflora, known for its dramatic foliage rather than its flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil and partial to full shade.

This cultivar is popular in tropical gardens for its ornamental value and ability to add a bold statement to any landscape.
