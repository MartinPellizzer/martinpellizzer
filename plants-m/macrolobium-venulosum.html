Macrolobium venulosum

Macrolobium venulosum, commonly known as the veined broadleaf tree, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often veined with a distinct, pale color. The plant belongs to the Leguminosae family, which includes many nitrogen-fixing species. Its flowers are typically clustered in racemes and can range in color from white to pink.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood and is often found in lowland tropical forests.
