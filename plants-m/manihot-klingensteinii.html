Manihot klingensteinii

Manihot klingensteinii, commonly known as the arrowleaf manioc, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its arrow-shaped leaves and milky sap. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a source of starch.

However, the plant contains toxic compounds that require careful processing before consumption.
