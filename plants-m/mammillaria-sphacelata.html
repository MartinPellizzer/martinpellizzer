Mammillaria sphacelata

Mammillaria sphacelata, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a diameter typically ranging from 5 to 10 centimeters. The plant features prominent, spiny ribs that are often covered in white or pale blue glochids. Its flowers are usually pale yellow or greenish-white, blooming in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
