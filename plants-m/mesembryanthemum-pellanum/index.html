Mesembryanthemum Pellanum

Mesembryanthemum pellanum is a unique succulent plant native to southern Africa. It is known for its striking, fuzzy leaves that change color with the seasons. This plant thrives in arid environments and requires minimal water to survive. Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it highly adaptable to harsh conditions. Despite its tough exterior, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
