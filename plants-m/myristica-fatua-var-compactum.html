Myristica fatua var. compactum

Myristica fatua var. compactum, commonly known as the compact nutmeg plant, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, glossy leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by orange-red berries containing the aromatic seeds used as spice. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a woody stem with thick, leathery bark.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential medicinal properties.
