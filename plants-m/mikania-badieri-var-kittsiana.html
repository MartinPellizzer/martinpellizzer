Mikania badieri var. kittsiana

Mikania badieri var. kittsiana, commonly known as the Kittsiana variant of the Mikania badieri, is a flowering plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows as a climbing vine, with hairy stems and heart-shaped leaves. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in inflorescences, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in the understory of forests.
