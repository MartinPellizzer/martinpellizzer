Michelia sericea

Michelia sericea, commonly known as the silver magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The leaves are broadly ovate, glossy, and have a silvery underside, giving the plant its common name. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

The plant is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
