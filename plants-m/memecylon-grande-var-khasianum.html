Memecylon grande var. khasianum

Memecylon grande var. khasianum, commonly known as the Khasi memecylon, is a species native to the northeastern regions of India.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub characterized by its smooth, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers. The plant belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is often found in moist, shaded forest understories. Its fruits are capsule-like and contain several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
