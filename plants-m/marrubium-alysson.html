Marrubium alysson

Marrubium alysson, commonly known as alpine horehound, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of Europe.

It typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, thriving in well-drained soils and full sunlight. The plant has silver-gray foliage with hairy leaves, and its flowering stems bear small, blue or purple flowers. It is valued for its medicinal properties, particularly for its expectorant and antispasmodic effects.

Marrubium alysson is also used in traditional herbal remedies for respiratory ailments.
