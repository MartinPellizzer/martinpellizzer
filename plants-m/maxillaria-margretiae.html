Maxillaria margretiae

Maxillaria margretiae, commonly known as the Margaret's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant features linear, dark green leaves and has a pseudobulbous structure typical of many orchid species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria margretiae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
