Micrachne obtusiflora

Micrachne obtusiflora, commonly known as the broad-leaved sandmat, is a flowering plant native to arid and semi-arid regions of Australia.

It is a low-growing perennial shrub that thrives in sandy soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are typically broad and fleshy, which helps store water for survival in harsh environments.

Micrachne obtusiflora plays a role in stabilizing sandy soils and providing habitat for various desert organisms.
