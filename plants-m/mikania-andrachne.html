Mikania andrachne

Mikania andrachne, commonly known as false jute, is a flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its hairy, slender stems and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and open woods. Its leaves are alternate, heart-shaped, and covered in fine hairs.

Mikania andrachne is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
