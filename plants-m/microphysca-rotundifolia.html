Microphysca rotundifolia

Microphysca rotundifolia, commonly known as the rounded-leaved microphysca, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features compact, rounded leaves that are typically a deep green color, providing a dense foliage appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, which bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun to partial shade conditions.

Due to its ornamental value and drought tolerance, it is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
