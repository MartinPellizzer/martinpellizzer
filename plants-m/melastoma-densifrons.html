Melastoma densifrons

Melastoma densifrons, commonly known as the dense-fruited melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its shrub-like growth habit. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and produces showy, clustered flowers in shades of red and pink. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.

Melastoma densifrons is often found in moist, shaded forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities.
