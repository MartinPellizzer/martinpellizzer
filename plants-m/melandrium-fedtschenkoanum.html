Melandrium fedtschenkoanum

Melandrium fedtschenkoanum, commonly known as the Fedtschenko's catchfly, is a flowering plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the Caryophyllaceae family and is characterized by its low-growing, herbaceous structure. The plant produces small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late summer. Its leaves are typically opposite and covered in fine hairs, adapting to cold and windy environments.

It thrives in well-drained, sandy or rocky soils and is often found in tundra and alpine habitats.
