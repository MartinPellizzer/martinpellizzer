Mesua nagassarium var. sclerophylla

Mesua nagassarium var. sclerophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leaved flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often appearing before or during the rainy season. The leaves are narrow and leathery, which is a typical feature of sclerophyllous plants adapted to dry conditions. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas or disturbed sites.

This species is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
