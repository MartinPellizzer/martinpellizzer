Mesembryanthemum tenuifolium

Mesembryanthemum tenuifolium, commonly known as the narrow-leaf ice plant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a succulent species characterized by its fleshy, narrow leaves and small, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, making it well adapted to dry climates. It is often used in xeriscaping due to its low water requirements and ability to tolerate poor soil conditions.

The flowers typically bloom in summer, adding a decorative touch to gardens and natural landscapes.
