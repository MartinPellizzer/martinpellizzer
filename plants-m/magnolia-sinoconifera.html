Magnolia sinoconifera

Magnolia sinoconifera, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has smooth, greyish-brown bark and elliptical leaves that turn yellow in autumn. Its flowers are typically white or pale pink and have a strong, sweet fragrance.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
