Mespilus uniflora subvar. inermis

Mespilus uniflora subvar. inermis, commonly known as the smooth white mume, is a flowering plant native to parts of East Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant produces small, edible fruits that are often used in traditional medicine and culinary applications. Its leaves are simple and alternate, with a glossy surface and serrated edges.

This subvariety is distinguished by its smooth, hairless stems and fruits, setting it apart from other members of the genus.
