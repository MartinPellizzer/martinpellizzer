Minuartia filifolia var. erlangeriana

Minuartia filifolia var. erlangeriana, commonly known as the Erlanger's sandwort, is a low-growing, mat-forming plant native to arctic and alpine regions.

It features small, white or pinkish flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in rocky or sandy soils. The plant has slender, thread-like leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in water retention. It is well adapted to cold climates and can thrive in full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often found in mountainous areas and is valued for its resilience and ornamental appeal in rock gardens.
