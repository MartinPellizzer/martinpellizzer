Myristica cucullata

Myristica cucullata, commonly known as the helmet nut or nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The plant produces a unique fruit that resembles a helmet, giving it its common name. The seeds within the fruit are used to make nutmeg, while the dried seed coat yields mace.

This species is valued for its aromatic properties and has been historically significant in global trade.
