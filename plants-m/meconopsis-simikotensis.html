Meconopsis simikotensis

Meconopsis simikotensis, commonly known as the Simikot Mountains blue poppy, is a striking alpine plant native to the Himalayas, particularly in Nepal and India.

It is characterized by its vibrant blue or violet petals, which are often adorned with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows in rocky, high-altitude environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, adding a splash of color to the mountainous landscape.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
