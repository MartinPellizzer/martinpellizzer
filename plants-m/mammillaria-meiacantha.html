Mammillaria meiacantha

Mammillaria meiacantha, commonly known as the narrow-ribbed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in numerous small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It is a slow-growing cactus that thrives in well-drained soil and full sun.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
