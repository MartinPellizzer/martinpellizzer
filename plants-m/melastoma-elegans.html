Melastoma elegans

Melastoma elegans, commonly known as the elegant melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded areas and has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth texture. Its flowers are usually pink or red, with five petals and a distinctive spurred structure.

Melastoma elegans is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping in tropical and subtropical regions.
