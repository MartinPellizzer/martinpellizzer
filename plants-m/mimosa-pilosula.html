Mimosa pilosula

Mimosa pilosula, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its soft, feathery appearance. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens in temperate climates.

Mimosa pilosula is also known for its sensitive leaves, which fold shut when touched, a trait that adds to its unique botanical appeal.
