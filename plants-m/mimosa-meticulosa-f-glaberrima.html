Mimosa meticulosa f. glaberrima

Mimosa meticulosa f. glaberrima, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers that appear in clusters. The plant is known for its sensitive leaves that fold when touched, a trait called thigmonastic response. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its foliage is covered in fine, silky hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
