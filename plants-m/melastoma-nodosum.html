Melastoma nodosum

Melastoma nodosum, commonly known as the nodding melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in racemes and are characterized by their pink to reddish color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, elliptical seeds.
