Macairea calvescens

Macairea calvescens, commonly known as the velvetleaf.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. The plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish flowers. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Macairea calvescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
