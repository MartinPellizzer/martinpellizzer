Medea

Medea, commonly known as the Medea plant, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the genus Medea in the family Iridaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers that resemble those of the iris. The plant typically grows in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil. Medea species are often cultivated for their ornamental value due to their vibrant blooms.
