Minuartia lineata f. kashmirica

Minuartia lineata f. kashmirica, commonly known as the Kashmir stonecress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming habit with small, clustered leaves and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically thrives in rocky or alpine environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. Its leaves are often lined with fine, white hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

This variety is native to the Himalayan regions, including parts of Kashmir, where it grows in high-altitude meadows.
