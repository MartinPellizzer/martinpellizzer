Melodinus pulchrinervius

Melodinus pulchrinervius, commonly known as the elegant veined melodine, is a climbing plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, veined leaves that display a complex pattern of light and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant white flowers with a sweet scent, which attract pollinators such as bees and moths. Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and greenhouses.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that ripens to a reddish color and is edible in some regions.
