Mattiastrum cristatum var. paropamisi

Mattiastrum cristatum var. paropamisi, commonly known as the Paropamisi spikeplant, is a strikingly ornamental plant characterized by its dense, spike-like inflorescences.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, and is native to the mountainous regions of Turkey. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and alpine displays.

Its unique appearance and hardy nature make it a valuable addition to botanical collections.
