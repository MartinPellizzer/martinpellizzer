Millettia taolanaroensis

Millettia taolanaroensis, commonly known as the Madagascar silk tree, is a flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its unique, elongated pods. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are bipinnate, and it produces showy, pendulous inflorescences with small, white or pale pink flowers.
