Myrtus lineata

Myrtus lineata, commonly known as the narrowleaf myrtle, is a shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, dark green, narrow leaves that are often fragrant. The plant produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in spring, followed by dark blue berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Myrtus lineata is also valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
