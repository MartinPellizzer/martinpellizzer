Millettia nigrescens

Millettia nigrescens, commonly known as the black-stemmed millettia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including India, China, and parts of Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its dark, almost black stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. It produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, followed by flat, bean-like pods containing several seeds.

Millettia nigrescens is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.
