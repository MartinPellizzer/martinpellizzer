Metalasia tenuifolia

Metalasia tenuifolia, commonly known as the slender leaf metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters. It is adapted to arid conditions and has a deep root system for water retention.

Metalasia tenuifolia is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
