Minuartia maroccana

Minuartia maroccana, commonly known as the Moroccan rockcress, is a low-growing perennial plant native to North Africa.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many flowering plants with small, four-petaled flowers. The plant typically has hairy, succulent stems and leaves that are often clustered at the base. It thrives in rocky or sandy soils and is adapted to arid environments.
