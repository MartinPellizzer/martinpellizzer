Massonia setulosa

Massonia setulosa, commonly known as the woolly massonia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its dense, woolly foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically white or pink in color. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Massonia setulosa is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique texture and attractive blooms.
