Mesechites plumeriiflorus

Mesechites plumeriiflorus, commonly known as the flame tree, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a dramatic visual display. The plant has compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets that are often arranged in a feathery pattern. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The bark of Mesechites plumeriiflorus is smooth and can be light to dark brown in color.
