Magnolia wendtii

Magnolia wendtii, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Magnolia wendtii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
