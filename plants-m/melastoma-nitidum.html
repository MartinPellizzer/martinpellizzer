Melastoma nitidum

Melastoma nitidum, commonly known as the shining melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptic leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Its flowers are small, clustered in inflorescences, and have five petals with a distinctive white to pink coloration.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, black seeds.
