Marginaria raishaensis

Marginaria raishaensis, commonly known as the Raisha sandwort, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of the Middle East.

It belongs to the family Caryophyllaceae and is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure. The plant features small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring. Its leaves are narrow and succulent, adapted to conserve water in its harsh environment.
