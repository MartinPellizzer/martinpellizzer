Mammillaria ritteriana var. quadricentralis

Mammillaria ritteriana var. quadricentralis, commonly known as the four-armed mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, pink to reddish flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
