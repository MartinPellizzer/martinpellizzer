Mesembryanthemum breve

Mesembryanthemum breve, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its small, fleshy leaves and clusters of star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick, succulent leaves. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its short stature and ornamental flowers make it a unique addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
