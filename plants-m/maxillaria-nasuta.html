Maxillaria nasuta

Maxillaria nasuta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to tropical regions of South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces a single, upright stem. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow to orange with red or purple markings. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, and it thrives in moist, well-drained soil.

Maxillaria nasuta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
