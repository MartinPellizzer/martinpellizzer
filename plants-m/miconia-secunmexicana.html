Miconia secunmexicana

Miconia secunmexicana, commonly known as the Mexican miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white, attracting various pollinators. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in humid lowland forests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
