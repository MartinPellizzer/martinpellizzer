Mariscus sieberianus var. nossibeensis

Mariscus sieberianus var. nossibeensis, commonly known as the Nossibe marsh orchid, is a rare and endemic plant species found in specific wetland habitats.

It is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and small, showy flowers that typically bloom in the spring. The plant thrives in moist, nutrient-rich soils and is often associated with marshy or swampy environments. Its reproductive cycle is closely tied to the seasonal flooding patterns of its native habitat.
