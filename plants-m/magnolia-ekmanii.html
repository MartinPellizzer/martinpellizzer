Magnolia ekmanii

Magnolia ekmanii, commonly known as the dwarf magnolia, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to the mountainous regions of southern China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It prefers well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it suitable for garden cultivation.

Due to its ornamental value and compact size, it is often used in landscaping and as a specimen plant.
