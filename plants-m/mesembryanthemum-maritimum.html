Mesembryanthemum maritimum

Mesembryanthemum maritimum, commonly known as the sea daisies or coastal evening primrose, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and showy yellow or orange flowers that open in the evening. The plant thrives in sandy, well-drained soils and is often found along coastal areas and dry, sunny habitats. It is drought-tolerant and has adapted to harsh environmental conditions.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for rock gardens and coastal landscaping.
