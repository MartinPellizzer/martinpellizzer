Maxillaria cobanensis

Maxillaria cobanensis, commonly known as the Coban orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive lip. Its leaves are broad and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria cobanensis thrives in shaded, humid environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
