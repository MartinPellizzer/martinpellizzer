Miconia diaphanea

Miconia diaphanea, commonly known as the glassy miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that bloom during the wet season. It is a member of the Melastomataceae family, which includes many ornamental and ecologically significant plants.
