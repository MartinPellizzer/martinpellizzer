Medicago tribuloides var. breviaculeata

Medicago tribuloides var. breviaculeata, commonly known as the short-bract sainfoin, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with small, rounded leaflets and produces yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in summer. The plant has a fibrous root system and is adapted to dry, rocky soils. It is often found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical characteristics make it a valuable forage plant for livestock.
