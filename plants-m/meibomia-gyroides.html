Meibomia gyroides

Meibomia gyroides, commonly known as the spiral vine, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its helical twisting growth pattern, which gives it a unique and ornamental appearance. The plant produces small, fragrant white or yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its leaves are typically opposite and ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Meibomia gyroides is often cultivated for its aesthetic value in gardens and as a houseplant.
