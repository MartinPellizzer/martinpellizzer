Mozambe levigata

Mozambe levigata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved mozambique plant, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom in clusters, often in shades of white and purple. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to bright indirect light.

Mozambe levigata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to adapt to indoor environments.
