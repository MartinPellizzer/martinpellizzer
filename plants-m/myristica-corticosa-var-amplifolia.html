Myristica corticosa var. amplifolia

Myristica corticosa var. amplifolia, commonly known as the broad-leafed nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its broad, elliptical leaves and fragrant, reddish-brown seeds. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits containing the aromatic seeds used in spice production.

Its botanical features make it valuable for both ornamental and commercial purposes in tropical horticulture.
