Microsorum diversifolium

Microsorum diversifolium, commonly known as the variable sword fern, is a species of aquatic fern native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its long, slender fronds that can reach up to 1 meter in length. The plant typically grows in slow-moving freshwater environments such as streams and ponds. Its leaves are pinnately divided with varying leaflet shapes, contributing to its common name.

Microsorum diversifolium is often used in aquascaping due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability to different water conditions.
