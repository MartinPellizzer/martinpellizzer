Myrosma canniformis

Myrosma canniformis, commonly known as the cannonleaf myrosma, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its distinctive, cannon-shaped leaves that are thick and leathery, providing a unique appearance. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

Myrosma canniformis is also valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
