Microparacaryum intermedium var. stellatum

Microparacaryum intermedium var. stellatum, commonly known as the star-shaped star moss, is a species of liverwort characterized by its distinctive star-like leaf arrangement.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in terrariums or as a decorative element in humid environments.
