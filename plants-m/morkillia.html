Morkillia

Morkillia, commonly known as the snowball plant, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Arctic and subarctic regions.

It belongs to the family Polygonaceae and is characterized by its rosette of fleshy, dark green leaves. Morkillia thrives in cold, moist environments and is often found in tundra and alpine habitats.

Its unique morphology and adaptability to harsh climates make it an interesting subject in botanical studies.
