Mascagnia vacciniifolia var. glabrata

Mascagnia vacciniifolia var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth-leaved mascagnia, is a species native to certain regions of the Americas.

It is a shrub characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and small, fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. The leaves are typically smooth and glossy, giving the plant a distinct appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and adaptability to various garden settings.
