Mesanthus

Mesanthus, commonly known as the Chinese lantern tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Nyssaceae.

It is native to eastern Asia, particularly China, and features showy, lantern-shaped flowers that bloom in late summer. The leaves are typically simple and alternate, with a smooth or slightly serrated margin. The plant is valued for its ornamental value, especially for its vibrant red or orange flowers.

It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
