Masdevallia collantesii

Masdevallia collantesii, commonly known as the Collantes' masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful inflorescences and delicate, strap-like leaves. The plant typically grows on tree trunks or rocks, thriving in the humid and cool conditions of its natural habitat. Its flowers, which can range from white to pink and violet, are highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in conservation efforts.
