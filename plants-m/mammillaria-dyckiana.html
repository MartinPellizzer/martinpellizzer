Mammillaria dyckiana

Mammillaria dyckiana, commonly known as the Dyck's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a short stem and numerous radial spines. The plant features white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. Its growth habit is typically solitary or in small clusters.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
