Mikania complanata

Mikania complanata, commonly known as the flatleaf morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy blue or purple flowers. The plant is often found in moist, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and riverbanks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous seeds.

Mikania complanata is also known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
