Maxillaria machinazensis

Maxillaria machinazensis, commonly known as the Machinazensis orchid, is a species native to Central America, particularly found in Costa Rica and Panama.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb structure that supports its growth in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration, often featuring a mix of white, yellow, and green tones. Its flowering period typically occurs during the late spring to early summer months.

Maxillaria machinazensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
