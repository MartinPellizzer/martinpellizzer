Maxillaria minutiflora

Maxillaria minutiflora, commonly known as the miniature orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit. The plant produces pale yellow or greenish-white blooms that are often clustered in small inflorescences. Its leaves are typically narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Maxillaria minutiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in botanical gardens and by orchid enthusiasts.
