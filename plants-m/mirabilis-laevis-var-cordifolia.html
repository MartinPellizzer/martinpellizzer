Mirabilis laevis var. cordifolia

Mirabilis laevis var. cordifolia, commonly known as the heartleaf marvel.

This variant of the marvel plant is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves, which give it its common name. It is a flowering plant native to South America, specifically Brazil. The flowers are typically tubular and come in shades of red, orange, and yellow.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
