Myosotis turcica

Myosotis turcica, commonly known as Turkish forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Turkey and the surrounding regions of the Eastern Mediterranean.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors and stream banks. The plant features slender stems and small, blue or violet flowers that bloom from late spring to early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Myosotis turcica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
