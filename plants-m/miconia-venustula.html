Miconia venustula

Miconia venustula, commonly known as the velvetleaf miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine, velvety hairs, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellowish in color. Its fruit is a smooth, fleshy capsule that splits open to release numerous tiny seeds.

Miconia venustula is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of moist, tropical environments.
