Mentha × Niliaca Subsp. Timbalii

Mentha × niliaca subsp. timbalii is a rare hybrid mint plant known for its unique aromatic properties. It is native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia, particularly in parts of Thailand and Vietnam. This subspecies is prized for its intense menthol scent and its use in traditional medicine and perfumery. Due to its limited distribution, it is considered endangered and requires careful conservation efforts. Its distinctive characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and horticulturists alike.
