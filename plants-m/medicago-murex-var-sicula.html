Medicago murex var. sicula

Medicago murex var. sicula, commonly known as Sicilian goat's rue, is a variant of the goat's rue plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herbaceous plant characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in Mediterranean shrublands. It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and has the ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen through symbiotic bacteria in its root nodules.

This botanical feature makes it valuable for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
