Merkusia attenuata

Merkusia attenuata, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to East Africa.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves and clusters of showy, violet-colored flowers. The plant thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light. It is a popular houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.

Merkusia attenuata typically grows in compact rosettes, with its flowers emerging from the center of the foliage.
