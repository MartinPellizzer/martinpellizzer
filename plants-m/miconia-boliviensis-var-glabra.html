Miconia boliviensis var. glabra

Miconia boliviensis var. glabra, commonly known as the smooth miconia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, white to pinkish flowers that are typically clustered in inflorescences. It is often found in humid, lowland forests and can grow up to several meters in height.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in some regions, though it can be invasive in certain environments.
