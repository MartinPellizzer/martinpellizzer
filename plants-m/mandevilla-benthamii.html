Mandevilla benthamii

Mandevilla benthamii, commonly known as the Benthams' mandevilla, is a climbing shrub native to South America.

It features showy, tubular flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a yellow throat. The plant has opposite, simple, and often hairy leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is typically grown as a tropical ornamental.

Mandevilla benthamii produces fragrant flowers and is popular for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
