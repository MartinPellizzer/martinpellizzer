Meridiana lichtensteinii

Meridiana lichtensteinii, commonly known as the Lichtenstein's daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white petals and a yellow center. Meridiana lichtensteinii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
