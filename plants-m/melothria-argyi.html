Melothria argyi

Melothria argyi, commonly known as the Chinese sour cucumber, is a climbing vine native to East Asia.

It produces small, green, edible fruits that are typically sour and tangy in flavor. The plant has heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface and is often grown for its ornamental value as well as its edible parts. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires a support structure for its vine growth.

The fruit is rich in vitamin C and is used in traditional Chinese cuisine.
