Micromystria

Micromystria, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a genus of flowering plants in the orchid family, Orchidaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands. The plants typically have small, delicate leaves and produce striking, fragrant flowers in shades of white, yellow, and pink. Micromystria species are often epiphytic, growing on trees or rocks in humid environments.
