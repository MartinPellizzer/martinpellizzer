Millettia densiflora

Millettia densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered millettia, is a member of the legume family Fabaceae.

It is a climbing shrub that produces small, clustered yellow flowers, which are often followed by elongated, bean-like pods. The plant is native to tropical regions of Asia and is characterized by its pinnate leaves and strong, woody stems. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
