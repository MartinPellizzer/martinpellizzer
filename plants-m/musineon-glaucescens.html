Musineon glaucescens

Musineon glaucescens, commonly known as the blue musineon, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate leaves and clusters of blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its botanical features include a fibrous root system and a woody stem that becomes more branched with age.

Musineon glaucescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
