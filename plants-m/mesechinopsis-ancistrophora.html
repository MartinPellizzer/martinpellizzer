Mesechinopsis ancistrophora

Mesechinopsis ancistrophora, commonly known as the spiny leaf cactus, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems and spiny, fleshy leaves that help it conserve water in arid environments. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it suitable for rock gardens or xeriscaping.

Due to its hardy nature, it is relatively low maintenance once established.
