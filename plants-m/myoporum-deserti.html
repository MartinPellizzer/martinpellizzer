Myoporum deserti

Myoporum deserti, commonly known as the desert myoporum, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a shrub characterized by its small, aromatic leaves and clusters of white or pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant is adapted to withstand drought and high temperatures, making it well-suited for xerophytic environments. Its root system is deep and extensive, allowing it to access water from deeper soil layers.

Myoporum deserti is often used in landscaping for its hardiness and ornamental value in dry climates.
