Michelia opipara

Michelia opipara, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or opipara michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that split open to release seeds.

Michelia opipara is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
