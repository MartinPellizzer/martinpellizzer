Maytenus obtusifolia var. elliptica

Maytenus obtusifolia var. elliptica, commonly known as the elliptic mayten, is a woody shrub or small tree native to South America.

It typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, thriving in humid, shaded environments. The plant features simple, elliptical leaves that are usually dark green and glossy on top. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, followed by fleshy, orange-red berries that contain the seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
