Mammillaria erythrosperma

Mammillaria erythrosperma, commonly known as the red-seeded hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, spineless cactus with a globular shape and bright green to blue-green stems. The plant produces vibrant orange to red flowers during the summer months. Its fruit is a small, fleshy capsule that turns red when mature, giving it its common name.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
