Mairia hirsuta subsp. robusta

Mairia hirsuta subsp.

robusta, commonly known as the robust mairia, is a shrub native to tropical regions of South America. It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its dense, hairy foliage and clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers.
