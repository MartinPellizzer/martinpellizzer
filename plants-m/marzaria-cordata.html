Marzaria cordata

Marzaria cordata, commonly known as the heartleaf marzaria, is a unique plant species characterized by its heart-shaped leaves.

It is native to specific regions in South America, where it thrives in moist, shaded environments. The plant features delicate, often fragrant flowers that bloom in the spring. Its leaves are typically a vibrant green, with a slightly waxy texture.

Marzaria cordata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to purify the air.
