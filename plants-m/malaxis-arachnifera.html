Malaxis arachnifera

Malaxis arachnifera, commonly known as the spider orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, spider-like inflorescence. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers with a distinctive, elongated spur. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Malaxis arachnifera is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
