Melandrium densifolium

Melandrium densifolium, commonly known as the dense-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is characterized by its dense, ovate leaves and showy pink or white flowers that bloom in summer. Native to Europe and parts of Asia, this species thrives in moist, well-drained soils in woodland or grassland habitats. The plant produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

It is often found in shaded or semi-shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
