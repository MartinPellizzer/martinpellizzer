Miconia eurychaenioides

Miconia eurychaenioides, commonly known as the red miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to 5 meters in height, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a velvety texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits.

Its leaves are often used in traditional medicine, and the plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
