Malcolmia broussonetii

Malcolmia broussonetii, commonly known as the blueweed, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb that typically reaches heights of up to 30 centimeters. The plant features hairy stems and leaves that are alternate and ovate in shape. Its flowers are small, blue to purple in color, and bloom in clusters during the summer months.

It is often found in dry, sandy soils and is adapted to thrive in arid environments.
