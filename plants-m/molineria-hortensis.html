Molineria hortensis

Molineria hortensis, commonly known as the garden false orchid, is a species of flowering plant native to the eastern United States.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its slender, grass-like appearance with narrow, blade-like leaves. The plant produces small, clustered inflorescences that resemble orchids, hence its common name. It typically blooms in late spring to early summer, showcasing delicate, pale yellow to greenish flowers.

Molineria hortensis is often found in moist, open habitats such as meadows and woodland edges.
