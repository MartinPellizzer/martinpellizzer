Melanocenchris royleana

Melanocenchris royleana, commonly known as the Kashmir rose or Himalayan rose, is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Himalayas.

It belongs to the Rosaceae family and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves and showy, pink to white flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments. It is often found in alpine meadows and is valued for its ornamental qualities.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
