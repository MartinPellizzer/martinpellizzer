Myrtus molinae

Myrtus molinae, commonly known as Molina's myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant features narrow, dark green leaves and produces small, white, fragrant flowers in the spring. Its fruit is a dark purple berry that contains several small seeds.

Myrtus molinae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its aromatic properties.
