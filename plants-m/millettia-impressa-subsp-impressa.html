Millettia impressa subsp. impressa

Millettia impressa subsp.

impressa, commonly known as the African velvet tamarind, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa. It is characterized by its compound leaves, which are pinnate and often covered in a fine, velvety texture.

The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by woody pods containing flat, edible seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in savanna and woodland habitats.

The species is valued for its ornamental appearance and has traditional uses in local communities.
