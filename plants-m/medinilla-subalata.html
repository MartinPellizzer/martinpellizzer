Medinilla subalata

Medinilla subalata, commonly known as the silver medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters and thrives in warm, humid environments. Its flowers are often used in floral arrangements due to their elegant appearance and long-lasting bloom.

Medinilla subalata prefers partial shade and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
