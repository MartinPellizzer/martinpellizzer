Morisonia osmantha

Morisonia osmantha, commonly known as the pink morning glory, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its striking pink to purple flowers that bloom in the early morning. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces small, capsule-like fruits. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight.

Morisonia osmantha is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of water bodies.
