Malaxis petiolaris

Malaxis petiolaris, commonly known as the slender malaxis, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as under forest canopies. Its flowers are small and pale green, often with a slight pinkish hue, and are arranged in a loose raceme.

The orchid has a rhizomatous root system and produces a single, narrow leaf.
