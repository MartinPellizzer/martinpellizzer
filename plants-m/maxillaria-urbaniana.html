Maxillaria urbaniana

Maxillaria urbaniana, commonly known as the Urban's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically blooms during the warmer months, and its flowers are often clustered in racemes. The leaves are usually broad and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the colorful blooms.

It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.
