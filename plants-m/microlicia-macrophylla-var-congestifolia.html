Microlicia macrophylla var. congestifolia

Microlicia macrophylla var. congestifolia, commonly known as the clustered leaf microlicia, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that are often arranged in a dense, clustered pattern. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a unique and visually striking specimen in botanical gardens and conservation areas.
