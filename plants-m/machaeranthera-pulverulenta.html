Machaeranthera pulverulenta

Machaeranthera pulverulenta, commonly known as the dustflower, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

The plant produces small, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in late summer, typically from July to September. It thrives in dry, sandy soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and gravelly slopes.
