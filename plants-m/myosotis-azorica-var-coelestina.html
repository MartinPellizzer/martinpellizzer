Myosotis azorica var. coelestina

Myosotis azorica var. coelestina, commonly known as the sky-blue forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Azores.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a velvety texture.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its beauty.
