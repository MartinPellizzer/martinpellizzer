Mesosphaerum eriocalyx

Mesosphaerum eriocalyx, commonly known as the silky mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale yellow in color. The plant has a compact growth habit with hairy leaves that give it a soft, velvety appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

The species is of interest to botanists due to its unique floral structure and limited distribution.
