Muhlenbergia anemagrostoides

Muhlenbergia anemagrostoides, commonly known as the purple muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems and produces delicate, feathery plumes that are often tinged with purple or pink hues. The plant typically grows in open woodlands and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to form dense, drought-tolerant clumps.

Its botanical characteristics make it a popular choice for native plant gardens and ecological restoration projects.
