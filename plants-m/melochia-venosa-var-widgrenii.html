Melochia venosa var. widgrenii

Melochia venosa var. widgrenii, commonly known as the velvetleaf melochia, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its velvety, dark green leaves that are often covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by distinctive, round, fleshy fruits. It is native to regions of Africa and thrives in warm, humid climates.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
