Mikania firmula

Mikania firmula, commonly known as the slender wisteria, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, opposite leaves that are typically 5-10 cm in length. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers arranged in racemes, which bloom in the summer months. Mikania firmula is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb various structures.

It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.
