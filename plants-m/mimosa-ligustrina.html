Mimosa ligustrina

Mimosa ligustrina, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features compound leaves that are soft and fuzzy to the touch, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, typically in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its rapid growth and adaptability make it both useful and sometimes invasive in certain regions.
