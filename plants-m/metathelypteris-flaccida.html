Metathelypteris flaccida

It is characterized by its delicate, frond-like structures that are typically pale green and somewhat translucent. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in temperate regions with high humidity. Its reproductive structures, including sori, are located on the underside of the fronds, typical of many fern species.

The plant's botanical features make it an interesting subject for study in both ecological and taxonomic contexts.
