Macrolobium conchyliophorum

Macrolobium conchyliophorum, commonly known as the horned orchid, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped floral structures. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. Its roots are adapted to absorb nutrients from the rich, moist soil of its natural habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and conservation programs.
