Mammillaria columbiana subsp. hennisii

Mammillaria columbiana subsp.

hennisii, commonly known as the Hennings' mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular stem with prominent, white spines and small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring.

The plant has a dense, woolly covering that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat. It is typically grown as a houseplant due to its slow growth and ornamental appearance.

This subspecies is prized by cactus enthusiasts for its unique morphology and ease of care.
