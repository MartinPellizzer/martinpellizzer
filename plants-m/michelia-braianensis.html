Michelia braianensis

Michelia braianensis, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or white-flowered michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and partial to full shade.

Michelia braianensis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often used in gardens and landscapes.
