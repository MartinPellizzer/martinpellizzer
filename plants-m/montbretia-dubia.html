Montbretia dubia

Montbretia dubia, commonly known as the yellow montbretia, is a striking perennial plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, sturdy stems and clusters of bright orange to yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces seeds in elongated pods. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardeners.

However, it can be invasive in some regions if not managed properly.
