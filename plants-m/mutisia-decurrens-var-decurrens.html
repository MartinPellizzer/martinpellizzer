Mutisia decurrens var. decurrens

Mutisia decurrens var. decurrens, commonly known as the slender mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features elongated, narrow leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers. It is typically found in open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed areas.

Its botanical structure supports its role in ecological systems, providing nectar for pollinators.
