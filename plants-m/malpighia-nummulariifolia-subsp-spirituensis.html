Malpighia nummulariifolia subsp. spirituensis

Malpighia nummulariifolia subsp.

spirituensis, commonly known as the Spirituense Strawberry. It is a shrub native to the Caribbean, characterized by its small, bright red berries that resemble strawberries.

The plant features pinnately lobed leaves and produces flowers that are typically white or pale pink. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in coastal areas.

The berries are edible and have a sweet, slightly acidic flavor, making them popular in local cuisines.
