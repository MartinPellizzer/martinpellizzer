Macleania tovarensis

Macleania tovarensis, commonly known as the Tovar's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences and dark green, glossy leaves. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.
