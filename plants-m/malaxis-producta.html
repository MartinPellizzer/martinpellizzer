Malaxis producta

Malaxis producta, commonly known as the orchid with the delicate, pendulous flowers, is a species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, greenish-white blossoms. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often in forest understories. Its roots are fibrous and adapted to absorb moisture from the surrounding soil.

Malaxis producta is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in horticulture for its unique floral display.
