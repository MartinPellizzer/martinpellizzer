Millettia darassana

Millettia darassana, commonly known as the African satinwood, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces flat, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.

The plant also plays a role in soil stabilization and provides habitat for various wildlife species.
