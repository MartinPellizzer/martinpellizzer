Meleagrinex pernambucana

Meleagrinex pernambucana, commonly known as the Pernambuco orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often fragrant blooms. The plant typically features elongated, strap-like leaves and produces clusters of showy flowers in shades of white, yellow, and orange. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Meleagrinex pernambucana is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its cultural significance in Brazilian horticulture.
