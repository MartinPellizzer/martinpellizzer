Mammillaria ebenacantha

Mammillaria ebenacantha, commonly known as the Warty Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in prominent, white warts or tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, requiring minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and desert gardens.
