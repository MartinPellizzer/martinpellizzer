Monardella palmeri

Monardella palmeri, commonly known as Palmer's monardella, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs and shrubs. The plant features showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in whorls along the stem. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a rough texture, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Monardella palmeri thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
