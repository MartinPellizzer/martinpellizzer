Masdevallia torta

Masdevallia torta, commonly known as the twisted masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often twisted flower structure. The plant typically produces small, delicate flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, which bloom during the cooler months. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Masdevallia torta is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
