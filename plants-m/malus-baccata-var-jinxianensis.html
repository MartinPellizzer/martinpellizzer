Malus baccata var. jinxianensis

Malus baccata var. jinxianensis, commonly known as the Jinxian crabapple, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its ornamental value, featuring showy white to pink flowers in early spring. The plant produces small, edible berries that turn red in autumn, attracting wildlife. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for gardens and landscapes.

Its compact size and seasonal interest make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
