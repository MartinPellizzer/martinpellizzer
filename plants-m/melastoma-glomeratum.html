Melastoma glomeratum

Melastoma glomeratum, commonly known as the clustered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are usually pink or white, arranged in dense clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and oval in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
