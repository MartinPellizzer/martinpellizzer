Manihot attenuata

Manihot attenuata, commonly known as the arrowleaf or arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Americas.

It features heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often glossy and have a waxy texture. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into round, yellowish fruits containing edible seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

The plant is adapted to moist, well-drained soils and thrives in partial to full shade.
