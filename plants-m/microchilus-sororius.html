Microchilus sororius

Microchilus sororius, commonly known as the slender violet, is a flowering plant belonging to the Violaceae family.

It is a small, herbaceous perennial that typically grows in moist, woodland environments. The plant features delicate, lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of small, violet-blue flowers. It is native to parts of Europe and is often found in shaded, temperate regions.

Due to its modest size and ornamental appeal, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
