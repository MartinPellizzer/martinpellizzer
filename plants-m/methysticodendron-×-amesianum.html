Methysticodendron × amesianum

Methysticodendron × amesianum, commonly known as the Moonlight Orchid, is a hybrid plant that combines traits from its parent species, Methysticodendron and Amesia.

It is characterized by its striking, bioluminescent flowers that emit a soft blue glow at night, a unique feature among flowering plants. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments, typically found in temperate forest understories. Its leaves are broad and velvety, with a slight iridescence that changes color depending on light conditions.

Due to its rarity and unusual characteristics, it is highly valued in botanical research and conservation efforts.
