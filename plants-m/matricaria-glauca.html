Matricaria glauca

Matricaria glauca, commonly known as blue五月花, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has narrow, silver-gray leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Matricaria glauca is also known for its mild aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal remedies.
