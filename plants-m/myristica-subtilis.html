Myristica subtilis

Myristica subtilis, commonly known as the delicate nutmeg.

It is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by oval, woody fruits. Its seeds, known as nuts, contain aromatic oils and are used in traditional medicine.

The botanical characteristics of Myristica subtilis make it a unique species within its genus.
