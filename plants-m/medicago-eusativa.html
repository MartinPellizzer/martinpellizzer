Medicago eusativa

Medicago eusativa, commonly known as Turkey Strap, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its distinctive, strap-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in late summer, followed by narrow, elongated pods. It is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in dry, sunny conditions.

Medicago eusativa is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial for soil enrichment.
