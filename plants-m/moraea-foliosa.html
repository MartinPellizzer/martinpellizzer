Moraea foliosa

Moraea foliosa, commonly known as the leafy moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its narrow, grass-like leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Moraea foliosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and rockeries.
