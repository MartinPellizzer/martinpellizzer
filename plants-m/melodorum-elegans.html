Melodorum elegans

Melodorum elegans, commonly known as the elegant melodic orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its gracefully curved, pale green leaves and clusters of fragrant, white to pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in its natural habitat. Its flowers bloom in late spring to early summer, producing a sweet, melodic scent that attracts pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in greenhouse environments for its aesthetic and aromatic qualities.
