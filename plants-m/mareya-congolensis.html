Mareya congolensis

Mareya congolensis, commonly known as the Congo mareya, is a flowering plant native to Central Africa.

It belongs to the nightshade family (Solanaceae) and is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that typically bloom in shades of yellow and orange. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a slightly hairy surface, and it produces small, round berries that contain the seeds. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
