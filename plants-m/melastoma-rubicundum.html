Melastoma rubicundum

Melastoma rubicundum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and showy, clustered flowers. The flowers are typically bright red or pink, with five petals and prominent stamens, making it a visually striking plant. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The plant is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
