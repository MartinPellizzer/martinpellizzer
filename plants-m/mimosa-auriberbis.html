Mimosa auriberbis

Mimosa auriberbis, commonly known as the golden mimosoid, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its feathery, bipinnate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in spherical inflorescences. It is often found in dry, open habitats and is adapted to thrive in arid conditions.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ecological restoration projects.
