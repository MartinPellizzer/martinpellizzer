Masdevallia carnosa

Masdevallia carnosa, commonly known as the showy masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andes mountains of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, often hairy stems and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows at high elevations, where it thrives in cool, humid conditions with dappled sunlight. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, and it is known for its delicate, intricate flower structure.

Masdevallia carnosa is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and unique appearance.
