Maxillaria strictifolia

Maxillaria strictifolia, commonly known as the rigid-leaved maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its stiff, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant produces striking, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, often with intricate patterns on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria strictifolia is a popular choice for indoor cultivation due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
