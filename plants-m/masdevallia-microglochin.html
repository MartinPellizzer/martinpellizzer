Masdevallia microglochin

Masdevallia microglochin, commonly known as the dwarf glochin masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically features a short, slender stem with a few small, strap-like leaves. Its flowers are usually borne on the stem and are noted for their intricate, symmetrical structure.

Masdevallia microglochin is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
