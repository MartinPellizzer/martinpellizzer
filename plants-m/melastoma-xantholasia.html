Melastoma xantholasia

Melastoma xantholasia, commonly known as the yellow-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is characterized by its showy, yellow or orange flowers that bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators. The plant typically has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and a slightly serrated margin. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is native to parts of Southeast Asia.

Melastoma xantholasia is often found in forest understories and is valued for its ornamental qualities in horticulture.
