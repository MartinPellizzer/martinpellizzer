Menziesia bryantha

Menziesia bryantha, commonly known as Bryan's menziesia, is a deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of the United States.

It typically grows in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and streamside areas. The plant features small, ovate leaves with a glossy green upper surface and a lighter underside. Its flowers are white and clustered in elongated inflorescences, blooming in early spring.

The fruit is a small, reddish-brown capsule that contains several seeds.
