Mariscus coloratus var. macrocephalus

Mariscus coloratus var. macrocephalus, commonly known as the showy mariscus, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow throat. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its leaves are broad and glossy, providing a lush backdrop to its striking blooms.

The species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in woodland gardens.
