Maxillaria moutinhoi

Maxillaria moutinhoi, commonly known as the Moutinho's orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate floral structure. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, violet-colored flowers. It is a terrestrial orchid that thrives in shaded, humid environments.

Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
