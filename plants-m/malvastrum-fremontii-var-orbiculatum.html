Malvastrum fremontii var. orbiculatum

Malvastrum fremontii var. orbiculatum, commonly known as the Fremont's mallow, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its broad, orbicular leaves and showy, pink to purple flowers that bloom in summer. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, often found in dry, rocky habitats.
