Mammillaria viridiflora

Mammillaria viridiflora, commonly known as the green-flowered mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to yellowish tubercles. The plant produces small, green flowers that contrast with its gray-green foliage. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
