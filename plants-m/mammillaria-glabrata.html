Mammillaria glabrata

Mammillaria glabrata, commonly known as the smooth mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered with white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer seasons. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and succulent collections.
