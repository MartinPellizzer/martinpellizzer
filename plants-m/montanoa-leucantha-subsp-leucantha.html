Montanoa leucantha subsp. leucantha

Montanoa leucantha subsp.

leucantha, commonly known as white montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico. It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its slender, upright stems and small, clustered leaves.

The plant produces showy, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open woodlands or rocky areas.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in gardens, though it is primarily of interest to botanists and horticulturalists.
