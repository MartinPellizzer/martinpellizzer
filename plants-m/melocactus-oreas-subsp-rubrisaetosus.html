Melocactus oreas subsp. rubrisaetosus

Melocactus oreas subsp.

rubrisaetosus, commonly known as the red-spined melocactus, is a species native to Mexico. It is characterized by its cylindrical stem covered in prominent, reddish spines.

The plant typically grows in rocky, arid environments, adapting to harsh desert conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing during the warmer months.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in dry climates.
