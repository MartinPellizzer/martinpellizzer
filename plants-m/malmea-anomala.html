Malmea anomala

Malmea anomala, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy yellow or orange daisy-like flowers. The plant has a compact, bushy growth habit with hairy, lobed leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

Malmea anomala is valued for its ornamental appeal and is cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
