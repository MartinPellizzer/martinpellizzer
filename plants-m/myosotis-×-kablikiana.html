Myosotis × kablikiana

Myosotis × kablikiana, commonly known as the hybrid forget-me-not, is a flowering plant in the Boraginaceae family.

It is a natural hybrid between Myosotis sylvatica and Myosotis rhamnoides, resulting in a plant with distinctive blue to violet flowers. The species is native to Europe and has been cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant color and delicate appearance. It typically grows in moist, shady environments such as woodland edges and garden beds.

The plant's foliage is heart-shaped with a hairy texture, adding to its visual appeal.
