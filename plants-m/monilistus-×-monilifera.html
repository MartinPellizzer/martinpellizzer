Monilistus × monilifera

Monilistus × monilifera, commonly known as the hybrid fig tree, is a naturally occurring hybrid between *Ficus monilis* and *Ficus monilifera*.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often variegated with white or yellow markings. The plant produces small, clustered figs that are typically green when unripe and turn yellow or red when mature. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and greenhouses.
