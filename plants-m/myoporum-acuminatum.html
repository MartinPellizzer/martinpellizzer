Myoporum acuminatum

Myoporum acuminatum, commonly known as the New Zealand flax or kōwhai, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2–6 meters. The plant features lance-shaped leaves that are usually blue-green in color, and it produces small, fragrant yellow flowers in late summer. Myoporum acuminatum is well adapted to coastal environments and can thrive in sandy or rocky soils.

It is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardy nature.
