Muhlenbergia lateriflora

Muhlenbergia lateriflora, commonly known as silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the central and eastern United States.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage and upright growth habit, making it a popular ornamental plant. The plant produces panicles of small, blue-green flowers in late summer, which attract pollinators. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established.

Muhlenbergia lateriflora is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for wildlife.
