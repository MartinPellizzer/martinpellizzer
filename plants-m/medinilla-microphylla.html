Medinilla microphylla

Medinilla microphylla, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or fairy apple, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its small, glossy, dark green leaves and fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from pink to white. The plant produces small, round, edible fruits that have a sweet, citrus-like flavor. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance.

Medinilla microphylla is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal remedies.
