Megacarpaea delavayi var. grandiflora

Megacarpaea delavayi var. grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered megacarpaea, is a flowering plant native to the Yunnan province in China.

It is characterized by its showy, pink to reddish-purple flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows as a shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a woody capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
