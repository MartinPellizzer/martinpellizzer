Mammea lateriflora

Mammea lateriflora, commonly known as the coastal mammea, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its woody structure and is often found growing in coastal and wetland areas. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or greenish in color. Its leaves are alternate and ovate, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Mammea lateriflora is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
