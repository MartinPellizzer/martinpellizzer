Melochia melissifolia var. mollis

Melochia melissifolia var. mollis, commonly known as velvetleaf melochia, is a tropical plant characterized by its soft, velvety foliage.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family and is native to regions of Africa and the Americas. The plant features heart-shaped leaves with a distinctive waxy texture and a slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, clustered in terminal panicles.
