Marrubium bornmuelleri

Marrubium bornmuelleri, commonly known as the Bornmueller's horehound, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in dry, rocky soils and sunny environments. The plant features erect stems covered in fine hairs and produces small, blue or violet flowers arranged in clusters. Its leaves are narrow and gray-green, with a slightly hairy texture.

Marrubium bornmuelleri is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in traditional herbal remedies.
