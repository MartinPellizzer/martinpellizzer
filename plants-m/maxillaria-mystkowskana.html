Maxillaria mystkowskana

Maxillaria mystkowskana, commonly known as the mystkowskana orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its pseudobulbs and long, arching stems. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Maxillaria mystkowskana is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and unique floral structure.
