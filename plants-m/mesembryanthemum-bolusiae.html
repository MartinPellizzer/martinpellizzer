Mesembryanthemum bolusiae

Mesembryanthemum bolusiae, commonly known as the Bolus's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically blooming during the cooler months. It is well adapted to rocky soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant for coastal and Mediterranean climates.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
