Miconia stevensiana

Miconia stevensiana, commonly known as the yellow miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. Its fruits are round, fleshy berries that turn from green to red as they mature.

Miconia stevensiana is often found in wet forests and is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.
