Mammillaria ovimamma var. brevispina

Mammillaria ovimamma var. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined ovimamma cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a pale green to blue-gray stem covered in short, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
