Maxillaria obscura

Maxillaria obscura, commonly known as the dark maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and its compact, low-growing habit. The plant produces fragrant, pale green to brownish flowers with a distinctive, intricate pattern on the lip. It thrives in shaded, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria obscura is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
