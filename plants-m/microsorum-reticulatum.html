Microsorum reticulatum

Microsorum reticulatum, commonly known as the Christmas moss, is a species of aquatic fern that is widely cultivated as a decorative plant in water gardens and aquariums.

It is characterized by its delicate, lace-like fronds that form dense, carpet-like growths on the substrate. The plant is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in slow-moving freshwater environments. It is valued for its ability to oxygenate water and provide habitat for small aquatic life.

Microsorum reticulatum is relatively easy to care for, requiring moderate lighting and regular water changes to maintain its vibrant appearance.
