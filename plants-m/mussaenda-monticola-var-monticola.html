Mussaenda monticola var. monticola

Mussaenda monticola var. monticola, commonly known as the mountain mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of bright yellow, lantern-shaped flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 1.5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
