Meliosma tenuiflora

Meliosma tenuiflora, commonly known as the slender-flowered meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Asia, including parts of India and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. It produces tiny, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, often appearing in the axils of the leaves.

The fruit is a small, smooth, and smooth-seeded capsule that splits open when mature.
