Myrceugenia ovata var. regnelliana

Myrceugenia ovata var. regnelliana, commonly known as the Regnell's myrtle, is a species native to the temperate rainforests of southern South America.

It is a small evergreen shrub or tree that typically grows to a height of 3 to 8 meters. The plant has dark green, lance-shaped leaves with a waxy coating, and its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale pink. The fruit is a small, dark purple berry that contains several seeds.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental and ecological significance.
