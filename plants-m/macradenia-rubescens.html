Macradenia rubescens

Macradenia rubescens, commonly known as the red-flowered macradenia, is a striking member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa, particularly found in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. The plant is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which are typically arranged in clusters and bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often have a glossy texture.

Macradenia rubescens is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual impact.
