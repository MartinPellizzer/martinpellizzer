Musineon trachyspermum

Musineon trachyspermum, commonly known as wild cumin, is an annual herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It features slender, branched stems and narrow, linear leaves that are aromatic and slightly hairy. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in spikes, which are followed by oblong, aromatic seeds. Its seeds are widely used in traditional medicine and culinary applications due to their pungent scent and medicinal properties.

Musineon trachyspermum thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a hardy plant suitable for Mediterranean climates.
