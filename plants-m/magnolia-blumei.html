Magnolia blumei

Magnolia blumei, commonly known as the Mexican magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to central Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pinkish flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually hairy on the underside. Its bark is smooth and grayish, and it can grow up to 15 meters tall.

Magnolia blumei is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its fragrance and visual appeal.
