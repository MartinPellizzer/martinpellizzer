Melastoma croceum

Melastoma croceum, commonly known as the yellow melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges, and it typically grows in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, berry-like fruits that are red when mature.

Melastoma croceum is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
