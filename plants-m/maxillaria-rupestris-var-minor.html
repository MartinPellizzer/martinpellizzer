Maxillaria rupestris var. minor

Maxillaria rupestris var. minor, commonly known as the miniature rock orchid, is a miniature orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces small, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink. The plant typically grows on rock faces or in rocky soil, showcasing its epiphytic or lithophytic nature. It prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining substrates for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for indoor cultivation.
