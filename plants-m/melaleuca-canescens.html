Melaleuca canescens

Melaleuca canescens, commonly known as the grey melaleuca, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its silver-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, bottle-shaped flowers that are typically white or cream in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Melaleuca canescens is known for its aromatic leaves and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
