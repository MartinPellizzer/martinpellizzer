Murraya koenigii

Murraya koenigii, commonly known as curry leaf tree, is a small evergreen shrub native to South and Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, narrow elliptical leaves that are widely used in Indian cuisine for their aromatic properties. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Its botanical features make it both a valuable ornamental plant and an important ingredient in traditional cooking.
