Miconia purpureovillosa

Miconia purpureovillosa, commonly known as the purple-hair miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered with fine, purple-tinged hairs. Its flowers are showy, with five petals that range in color from pink to deep purple.

Miconia purpureovillosa is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.
