Meliosma dolichomischa

Meliosma dolichomischa, commonly known as the long-stemmed meliosma, is a member of the Meliaceae family, which includes mahogany and related trees.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like Thailand and Vietnam. The plant features simple, opposite leaves that are often elliptical in shape with a smooth margin. Its flowers are small and clustered, typically greenish-yellow in color, and it produces capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.

The species is valued for its ornamental potential and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
