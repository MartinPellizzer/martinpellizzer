Myoporum acuminatum var. angustifolium

Myoporum acuminatum var. angustifolium, commonly known as narrow-leaved myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically blue-green in color, with a slightly rough texture. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. It is adapted to grow in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and forested areas.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for landscaping in temperate climates.
