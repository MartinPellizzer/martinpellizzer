Melastoma palungense

Melastoma palungense, commonly known as the Malayan Melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a glossy surface and is often found in moist, shaded forest understories. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain several seeds.

Melastoma palungense is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
