Mammillaria pallescens

Mammillaria pallescens, commonly known as the pale mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces small, white flowers during the summer months. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
