Melilotus sulcatus proles leiospermus

Melilotus sulcatus proles leiospermus, commonly known as the smooth-leaved melilot, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, yellowish-green flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces pods containing small, smooth seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas such as roadsides and fields.

Its botanical features make it an important species for ecological studies and herbal applications.
