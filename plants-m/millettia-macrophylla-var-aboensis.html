Millettia macrophylla var. aboensis

Millettia macrophylla var. aboensis, commonly known as the Aboensis Millettia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pea-like flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its seeds are enclosed in pods and are adapted for dispersal by animals, contributing to its ecological role in forest ecosystems.
