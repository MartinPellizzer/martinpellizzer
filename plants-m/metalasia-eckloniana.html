Metalasia eckloniana

Metalasia eckloniana, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a deciduous tree native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, silver-green leaves that provide a striking visual contrast. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by woody pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and mountainous regions.

The silverleaf tree is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
