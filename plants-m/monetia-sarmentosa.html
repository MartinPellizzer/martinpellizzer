Monetia sarmentosa

Monetia sarmentosa, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and hairy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its fruits are berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

Monetia sarmentosa is often found in rocky or sandy soils and can thrive in dry conditions.
