Maranta legrelleana

Maranta legrelleana, commonly known as the golden arrowroot, is a species of flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their striking foliage. The plant is characterized by its rhombic-shaped leaves that are often a vibrant green with a reddish tinge. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Maranta legrelleana is often cultivated for its attractive leaves rather than its flowers.
