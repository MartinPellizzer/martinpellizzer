Medinilla rubrifructus

Medinilla rubrifructus, commonly known as the red-fruited medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, red berries that are edible but not commonly consumed by humans.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and floral display.
