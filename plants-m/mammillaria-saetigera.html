Mammillaria saetigera

Mammillaria saetigera, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems and the presence of spines that give it its common name. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in the spring or early summer.

Mammillaria saetigera is a popular choice for collectors due to its compact size and striking appearance.
