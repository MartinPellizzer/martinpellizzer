Maranta albertii

Maranta albertii, commonly known as the prayer plant, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that fold upwards at night, a behavior known as nyctinasty. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers that bloom in the evening. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and high humidity.

Its unique foliage makes it a popular ornamental plant among indoor gardeners.
