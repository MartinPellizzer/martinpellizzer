Melastoma setosum

Melastoma setosum, commonly known as the hairy melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance.

Melastoma setosum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive blooms.
