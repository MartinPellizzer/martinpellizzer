Mimetes dregei

Mimetes dregei, commonly known as the Cape daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble a single bloom. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy yellow to orange flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Mimetes dregei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in garden landscaping.
