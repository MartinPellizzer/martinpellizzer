Miconia gentryi

Miconia gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's miconia, is a species of flowering plant in the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white bracts that resemble flowers. It typically grows as an understory shrub in humid, shaded environments.

Miconia gentryi is noted for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets, which can impact local ecosystems.
