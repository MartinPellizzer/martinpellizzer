Miconia loretensis

Miconia loretensis, commonly known as the Loretto miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are an important food source for various bird species. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
