Mentha × rotundifolia var. melillensis

Mentha × rotundifolia var. melillensis, commonly known as the Melillan mint, is a hybrid plant belonging to the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, slightly hairy leaves that are typically a deep green color. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in the summer months. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and is often found in rocky or limestone soils.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
