Melochia hirsutissima

Melochia hirsutissima, commonly known as the hairy melochia, is a tropical shrub belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, ovate leaves and showy, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous small, smooth seeds. It is native to regions of Africa, Asia, and the Pacific Islands.

Melochia hirsutissima is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
