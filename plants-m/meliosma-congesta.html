Meliosma congesta

Meliosma congesta, commonly known as the clustered meliosma, is a small evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Meliaceae, which includes mahogany and related trees. The plant is characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and glossy, dark green leaves. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are followed by round, fleshy fruits.

Meliosma congesta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
