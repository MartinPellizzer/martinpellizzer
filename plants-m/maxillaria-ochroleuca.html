Maxillaria ochroleuca

Maxillaria ochroleuca, commonly known as the Yellow-lipped Cattleya, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically feature a pale yellow lip and a contrasting dark green or brownish upper lip. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with multiple blooms, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts. Its flowers are often fragrant, adding to its ornamental appeal.

Maxillaria ochroleuca thrives in warm, humid environments with good air circulation and indirect light.
