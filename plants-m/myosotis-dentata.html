Myosotis dentata

Myosotis dentata, commonly known as the toothed forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to parts of Europe and Asia.

It is characterized by its small, blue or purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves with distinct teeth along the edges. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowers bloom in early spring, adding a splash of color to the landscape.

Myosotis dentata is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional herbal medicine.
