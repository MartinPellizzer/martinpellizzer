Machilus khasyanus

Machilus khasyanus, commonly known as the Kashmiri laurel, is a small evergreen tree native to the Himalayan region.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its dark green, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows at elevations between 1,500 and 3,000 meters, thriving in temperate climates. Its fruit is a small, dark berry that contains several seeds.

The tree is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been studied for its potential medicinal properties.
