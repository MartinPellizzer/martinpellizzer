Maytenus andina

Maytenus andina, commonly known as the Andean mayten, is a species of flowering plant native to the Andes Mountains in South America.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, temperate forests. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are often elliptical or ovate in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with both male and female flowers present on the same plant.

Maytenus andina is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
