Mezierea salaziensis

Mezierea salaziensis, commonly known as the Salazian meadow grass, is a herbaceous plant native to Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as marshes and floodplains. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves and produces small, greenish flowers arranged in spikelets. It is characterized by its rhizomatous growth habit, which allows it to spread and form dense vegetation.

Mezierea salaziensis plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various microorganisms and invertebrates.
