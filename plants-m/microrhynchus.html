Microrhynchus

Microrhynchus, commonly known as the slender pearly everlasting, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its small, daisy-like flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, fluffy seeds that are dispersed by the wind. Native to arid and semi-arid regions, it is well adapted to dry environments.

Its botanical features make it a hardy plant often used in xeriscaping and ecological restoration projects.
