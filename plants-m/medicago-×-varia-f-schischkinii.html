Medicago × varia f. schischkinii

Medicago × varia f. schischkinii, commonly known as the Schischkin's variant of the variable medicago, is a botanical hybrid within the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its distinctive trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, which bloom in early summer. The plant is typically found in disturbed soils and open habitats, showcasing its adaptability to various environmental conditions. Its morphology includes a slender stem with hairy surfaces, and it produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds.

This subspecies is of interest to botanists due to its hybrid origins and unique morphological traits.
