Mercurialis × pubescens

Mercurialis × pubescens, commonly known as the pubescent mercury plant, is a hybrid species resulting from the cross between Mercurialis annua and Mercurialis tetonica.

Its botanical features make it adaptable to various environmental conditions, contributing to its widespread distribution.
