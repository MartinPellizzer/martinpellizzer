Monococcus echinophorus

Monococcus echinophorus, commonly known as the spiny wheatgrass, is a flowering plant native to the arid regions of southwestern Australia.

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, wind-pollinated flowers. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, adapted to reduce water loss in its harsh environment.
