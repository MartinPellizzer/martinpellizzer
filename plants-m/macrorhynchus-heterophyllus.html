Macrorhynchus heterophyllus

Macrorhynchus heterophyllus, commonly known as the variable-leaf orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its diverse leaf morphology, which can vary significantly between different populations. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, tropical environments. Its flowers are usually small and clustered, often displaying a range of colors from white to pink.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
