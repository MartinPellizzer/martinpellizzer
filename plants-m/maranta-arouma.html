Maranta arouma

Maranta arouma, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety foliage that ranges from deep green to reddish hues. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers followed by capsules containing numerous tiny seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maranta arouma is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its unique and attractive leaves.
