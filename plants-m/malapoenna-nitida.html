Malapoenna nitida

Malapoenna nitida, commonly known as the slender lily, is a striking flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stem and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are arranged spirally along the stem, contributing to its elegant appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.

Malapoenna nitida is also valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated for its delicate, showy blooms.
