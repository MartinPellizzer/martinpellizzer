Monocera serrata

Monocera serrata, commonly known as the serrated monocera, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Cucurbitaceae.

It is characterized by its tendrils, which help it climb and support its growth. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically unisexual, with separate male and female flowers on the same plant. The fruit is a small, oval-shaped berry that matures to a yellowish color.
