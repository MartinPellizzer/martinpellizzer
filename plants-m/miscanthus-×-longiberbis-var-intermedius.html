Miscanthus × longiberbis var. intermedius

Miscanthus × longiberbis var. intermedius, commonly known as the intermediate silvergrass, is a hybrid grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and feathery, silver-green plumes that give it a distinctive appearance. This plant is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its elegant foliage and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions. It prefers full sun and is drought-tolerant once established.

The variety is also valued for its potential in bioenergy production and ecological landscaping.
