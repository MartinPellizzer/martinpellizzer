Medicosma heterophylla

Medicosma heterophylla, commonly known as the variable-leaf lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its variable leaf shapes, which range from lanceolate to ovate, depending on environmental conditions. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a violet or purple tinge. It typically grows in grassland or rocky habitats and is adapted to dry conditions.

Medicosma heterophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential therapeutic properties.
