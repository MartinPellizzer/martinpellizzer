Moeroris ndikinimekiana

Moeroris ndikinimekiana, commonly known as the Malawi water lily, is a aquatic plant native to the Lake Malawi region.

It is characterized by its broad, rounded leaves that float on the water surface, supported by long, slender stalks. The plant produces white or pink flowers that bloom during the warmer months. Its roots anchor it in the muddy substrate at the lake's edge.

Moeroris ndikinimekiana plays a vital role in the local ecosystem by providing habitat for aquatic life and contributing to water oxygenation.
