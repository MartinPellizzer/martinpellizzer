Mammillaria centricirrha f. uberimamma

Mammillaria centricirrha f. uberimamma, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a distinctive cactus species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is characterized by its large, spherical stems that are covered in white, woolly spines, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding to its ornamental value. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for desert gardens.

Due to its slow growth and specific care requirements, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
