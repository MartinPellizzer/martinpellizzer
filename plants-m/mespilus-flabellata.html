Mespilus flabellata

Mespilus flabellata, commonly known as the flabellate mespilus, is a species of flowering plant in the Rosaceae family.

It is native to parts of Asia, particularly China, where it thrives in temperate climates. The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been cultivated for both its foliage and fruit in horticultural settings.
