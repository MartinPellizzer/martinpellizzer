Myristica multinervia

Myristica multinervia, commonly known as the Malayan nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the seeds used for nutmeg and mace. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets, and its flowers are typically yellow or greenish-yellow. Myristica multinervia thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it an important species in both ecological and commercial contexts.
