Miconia silverstonei

Miconia silverstonei, commonly known as the silverstone miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to several meters. Its leaves are often covered in a fine, silvery pubescence, giving it its common name.

Miconia silverstonei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
