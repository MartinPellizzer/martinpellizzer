Melastomastrum cornifolium

Melastomastrum cornifolium, commonly known as the horned leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive, horn-shaped leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a notable species in the Melastomataceae family.
