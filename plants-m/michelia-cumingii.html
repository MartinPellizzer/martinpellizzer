Michelia cumingii

Michelia cumingii, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or Cuming's michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate to elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

Michelia cumingii is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
