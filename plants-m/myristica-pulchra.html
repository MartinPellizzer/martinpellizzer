Myristica pulchra

Myristica pulchra, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It is a member of the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dark green, leathery leaves and fragrant, yellowish flowers. The tree produces an oval-shaped fruit that contains a single seed, which is used to make both nutmeg and mace.

The bark and leaves also contain essential oils that have been used in traditional medicine for their aromatic and medicinal properties.
