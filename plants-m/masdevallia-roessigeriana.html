Masdevallia roessigeriana

Masdevallia roessigeriana, commonly known as the Rosigeriana Masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically produces showy blooms in shades of pink, white, and violet, with intricate patterns on the petals and sepals. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for greenhouse cultivation.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and delicate species in the wild.
