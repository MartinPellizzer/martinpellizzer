Miconia croatii

Miconia croatii, commonly known as the Croatian miconia, is a species of flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, heart-shaped leaves. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that are often fragrant. It is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
