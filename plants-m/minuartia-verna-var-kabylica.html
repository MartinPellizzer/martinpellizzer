Minuartia verna var. kabylica

Minuartia verna var. kabylica, commonly known as the early spring rock-cress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a small, mat-forming perennial that thrives in rocky or gravelly soils, often found in alpine or arctic regions. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers with four petals, typically blooming in early spring. Its leaves are linear and closely spaced, adapted to withstand harsh environmental conditions.

This variety is notable for its resilience and is often used in horticultural settings for its ornamental value.
