Masdevallia antonii

Masdevallia antonii, commonly known as the Antonii masdevallia, is a striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its vibrant, long-lasting flowers that come in shades of red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows as a miniature epiphyte, attaching itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its compact growth form.

Masdevallia antonii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
