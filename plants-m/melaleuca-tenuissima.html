Melaleuca tenuissima

Melaleuca tenuissima, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves and small, white or pale yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces aromatic, woody capsules that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is often found in coastal heathlands and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Melaleuca tenuissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological communities.
