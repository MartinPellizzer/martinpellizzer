Myosotis densiflora

Myosotis densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of small, blue to purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shady environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, velvety hair.

Myosotis densiflora is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
