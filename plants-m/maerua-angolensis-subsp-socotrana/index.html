Maerua Angolensis Subsp. Socotrana

Maerua angolensis subsp. socotrana is a unique plant native to the Socotra archipelago. It thrives in the harsh, arid conditions of its coastal and rocky habitats. This subspecies is known for its distinctive leaf structure and striking yellow flowers. Its ecological importance makes it a key species in the region's biodiversity. Conservation efforts are crucial to protect this rare and fascinating plant.
