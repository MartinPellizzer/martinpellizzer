Miconia multiglandulosa

Miconia multiglandulosa, commonly known as the glossy miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers, which are followed by distinctive, velvety, orange-red fruits. Miconia multiglandulosa has a complex vascular system and exhibits a high degree of branching, contributing to its dense growth habit.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
