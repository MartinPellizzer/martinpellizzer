Microcos malayana

Microcos malayana, commonly known as the Malay dwarf mallow, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 meter. The plant features palmately lobed leaves and produces clusters of small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive member of the Malvaceae family.
