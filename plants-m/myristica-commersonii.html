Myristica commersonii

Myristica commersonii, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Banda Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, glossy leaves and fragrant flowers. The tree produces dark red, fleshy berries that contain the valuable seeds used for nutmeg and mace. Its bark and leaves are also used in traditional medicine for their medicinal properties.

The plant is economically significant due to the high demand for its spice products.
