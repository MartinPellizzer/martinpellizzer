Maxillaria cyanea

Maxillaria cyanea, commonly known as the blue orchid, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking violet to blue petals and sepals, which give it its distinctive appearance. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. Its flowers are fragrant and bloom in clusters, often during the rainy season.

Maxillaria cyanea is valued for its ornamental beauty and is cultivated by orchid enthusiasts worldwide.
