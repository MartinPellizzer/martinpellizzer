Maranta vieirae

Maranta vieirae, commonly known as the "Brazilian nightshade," is a striking tropical plant native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental plants known for their vibrant foliage. The plant features arrow-shaped leaves that are often adorned with intricate patterns and a striking red or purple underside. Maranta vieirae is typically grown as a houseplant due to its preference for warm, humid environments.

Its unique appearance and ease of care make it a popular choice among plant enthusiasts.
