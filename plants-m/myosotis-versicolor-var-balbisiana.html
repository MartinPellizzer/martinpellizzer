Myosotis versicolor var. balbisiana

Myosotis versicolor var. balbisiana, commonly known as the Balbisian forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade. Its leaves are lance-shaped and often have a slight woolly texture.

This variant is closely related to other species in the Myosotis genus, sharing similar ecological and botanical traits.
