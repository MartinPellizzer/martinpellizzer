Microchloa patentiflora

Microchloa patentiflora, commonly known as the slender grass.

It is a small, tufted grass species native to tropical and subtropical regions. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves that are often described as delicate and feathery. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikes, typical of the grass family.

Microchloa patentiflora is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks.
