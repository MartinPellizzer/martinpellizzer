Mammillaria gigantea

Mammillaria gigantea, commonly known as the giant mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its tall, cylindrical stem and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its slow growth and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
