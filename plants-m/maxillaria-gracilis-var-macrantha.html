Maxillaria gracilis var. macrantha

Maxillaria gracilis var. macrantha, commonly known as the Macrantha Maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features slender, upright pseudobulbs that produce linear leaves, and its flowers are typically pale yellow to greenish-white with a distinctive red or orange lip. The plant blooms in late spring to early summer, producing clusters of small, fragrant flowers. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil with consistent moisture.

This species is appreciated for its elegant appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
