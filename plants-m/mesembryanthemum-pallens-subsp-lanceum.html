Mesembryanthemum pallens subsp. lanceum

Mesembryanthemum pallens subsp.

lanceum, commonly known as the lanceolate ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa. It features fleshy, linear leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid environments.

The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the cooler months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its adaptability to harsh conditions makes it a resilient and ornamental addition to drought-tolerant landscapes.
