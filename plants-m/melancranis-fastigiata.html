Melancranis fastigiata

Melancranis fastigiata, commonly known as the upright fern, is a species of fern characterized by its tall, slender fronds.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant has a rhizomatous growth habit, allowing it to spread and form dense colonies. Its leaves are typically dark green and have a leathery texture, which helps it retain moisture.

Melancranis fastigiata is often used in ornamental horticulture for its attractive appearance and ease of cultivation.
