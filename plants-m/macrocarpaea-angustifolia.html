Macrocarpaea angustifolia

Macrocarpaea angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved macrocarpaea, is a species of flowering plant in the family Acanthaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Africa and is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves. Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
