Mammillaria cadereytensis var. quadrispina

Mammillaria cadereytensis var. quadrispina, commonly known as the four-spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem with prominent, white radial spines and smaller, pale blue to white central spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique spination and compact growth make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
