Morus yunnanensis

Morus yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan mulberry, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It belongs to the Moraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and grayish bark. The plant produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically consumed by wildlife. It thrives in temperate climates and is often found in mountainous regions.

This species is valued for its ecological role and historical use in traditional practices.
