Machaeranthera confertifolia

Machaeranthera confertifolia, commonly known as the clustered bracted blazingstar, is a flowering plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads that resemble single blooms. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces showy, yellow to orange flower heads that bloom in late summer. It thrives in dry, open habitats such as prairies and rocky soils.
