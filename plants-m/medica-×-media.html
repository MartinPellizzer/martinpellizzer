Medica × media

Medica × media, commonly known as the hybrid ivy, is a vigorous climbing plant that produces heart-shaped leaves with a glossy surface.

It is characterized by its ability to thrive in shaded environments, making it a popular choice for vertical gardening and indoor spaces. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are not particularly showy but are followed by dark blue berries. It is often used for its ornamental value and its capacity to cover walls and trellises.

However, it can be invasive in certain regions if not properly managed.
