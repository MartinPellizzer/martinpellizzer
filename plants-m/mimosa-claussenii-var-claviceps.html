Mimosa claussenii var. claviceps

Mimosa claussenii var. claviceps, commonly known as the yellow mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and clusters of small, yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or containers. It is also known for its ability to thrive in arid conditions, showcasing its adaptability to dry environments.

The species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ease of care.
