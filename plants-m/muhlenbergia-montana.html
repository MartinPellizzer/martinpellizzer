Muhlenbergia montana

Muhlenbergia montana, commonly known as the mountain muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-green foliage and produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add visual interest in the fall. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for xeriscaping and rock gardens. It is drought-tolerant and requires minimal maintenance once established.

Muhlenbergia montana is also valued for its ecological benefits, providing habitat and food for native wildlife.
