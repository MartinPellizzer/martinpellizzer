Mukia Maderaspatana

Mukia maderaspatana is a rare and ancient plant native to the Western Ghats of India. It is known for its unique appearance and ecological significance in its habitat. This plant thrives in specific microclimates and is often found in rocky and arid regions. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, it faces increasing threats to its survival. Conservation efforts are crucial to preserving this endemic species for future generations.
