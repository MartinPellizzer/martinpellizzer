Myristica velutina

Myristica velutina, commonly known as the velvet nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers followed by oval, reddish-brown fruits containing a single seed. Its seeds, known as nutmegs, are highly valued for their aromatic and medicinal properties.

The tree is also noted for its ornamental value due to its attractive appearance and unique foliage.
