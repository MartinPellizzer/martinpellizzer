Myosotis minutiflora subsp. minutiflora

Myosotis minutiflora subsp.

minutiflora, commonly known as the dwarf forget-me-not, is a small flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers and narrow, grass-like leaves.

The species thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest floors or along stream banks. It produces small, capsule-like fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds.

This subspecies is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local insect populations.
