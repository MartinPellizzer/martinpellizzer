Momordica laotica

Momordica laotica, commonly known as the Laotian bitter melon, is a vine species native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other cucurbits like cucumbers and squash. The plant produces elongated, bitter fruits that are typically harvested when mature. Its leaves are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while the stems are slender and trailing.

Momordica laotica is valued for its medicinal properties and is used in traditional herbal remedies.
