Masdevallia segurae

Masdevallia segurae, commonly known as the Segura's masdevallia, is a rare and striking orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically features a compact growth habit with a pseudobulb and elongated, narrow leaves. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with a unique arrangement of petals and sepals that contribute to its ornamental appeal.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a species of conservation concern.
