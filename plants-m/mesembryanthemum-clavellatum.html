Mesembryanthemum clavellatum

Mesembryanthemum clavellatum, commonly known as the "Lion's Tail," is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are covered in fine, white hairs, which help reduce water loss in arid environments. The plant produces striking, pink to purple flowers with a distinctive, tufted appearance that resembles a lion's tail. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Mesembryanthemum clavellatum is also valued for its ability to bloom throughout the year under suitable conditions.
