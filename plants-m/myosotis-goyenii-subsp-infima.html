Myosotis goyenii subsp. infima

Myosotis goyenii subsp.

infima, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand. It is characterized by its small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in early spring.

The plant typically grows in damp, shaded environments such as forest floors and streambanks. It has slender stems and lance-shaped leaves, and its flowers are often clustered in loose inflorescences.

This subspecies is part of the Myosotis genus, which includes several species of the forget-me-not family.
