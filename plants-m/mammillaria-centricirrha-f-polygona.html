Mammillaria centricirrha f. polygona

Mammillaria centricirrha f. polygona, commonly known as the clustered mammillaria, is a compact cactus species native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and prominent white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its unique appearance and is often cultivated in rock gardens or as a container plant.
