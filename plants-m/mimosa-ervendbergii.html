Mimosa ervendbergii

Mimosa ervendbergii, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray leaves, which are covered in fine, soft hairs. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal areas.

Mimosa ervendbergii is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to tolerate coastal conditions.
