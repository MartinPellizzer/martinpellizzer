Moraea trita var. foliata

Moraea trita var. foliata, commonly known as the leafy moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often colorful inflorescences. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically arranged in a fan shape. It produces showy flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, which bloom in spring or early summer.
