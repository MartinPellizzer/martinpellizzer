Mesembryanthemum deliciosum

Mesembryanthemum deliciosum, commonly known as the delicious mesem, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, characterized by its fleshy leaves and showy flowers. The plant produces vibrant yellow or orange flowers that bloom in summer, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its leaves are thick and often have a waxy coating, which helps it retain moisture in arid conditions.

Mesembryanthemum deliciosum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental value.
