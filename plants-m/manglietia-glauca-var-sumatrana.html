Manglietia glauca var. sumatrana

Manglietia glauca var. sumatrana, commonly known as the Sumatran manglietia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the forests of Sumatra.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant white flowers with prominent stamens, which bloom in the early morning. Its bark is thick and corky, often featuring a distinctive grayish-blue hue.
