Masdevallia serendipita

Masdevallia serendipita, commonly known as the serendipity masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and delicate, often brightly colored flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically has a short rhizome and produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowers. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a rosette, while its flowers bloom in clusters from the base of the pseudobulbs.

This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires specific care to mimic its natural habitat.
