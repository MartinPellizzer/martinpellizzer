Montanoa tomentosa var. cordifolia

Montanoa tomentosa var. cordifolia, commonly known as the woolly montanoa, is a flowering plant native to Mexico.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its soft, hairy foliage and showy yellow flowers. The leaves are typically heart-shaped, which is reflected in its scientific name, *cordifolia*. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in cloud forests.

This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
