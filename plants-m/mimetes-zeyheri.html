Mimetes zeyheri

Mimetes zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's mimic dandelion, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its dandelion-like appearance with yellow flowers that turn into fluffy white seed heads. The plant produces small, oval leaves that are covered in fine hairs, and it thrives in well-drained soils.

Mimetes zeyheri is often found in grasslands and rocky areas, and it plays a role in supporting local insect populations.
