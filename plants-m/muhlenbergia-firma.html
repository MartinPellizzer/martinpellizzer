Muhlenbergia firma

Muhlenbergia firma, commonly known as the silver bluestem, is a perennial grass native to the eastern United States.

It is characterized by its upright growth habit and distinctive silver-gray foliage that gives it a striking appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, blue-green flowers in late summer, adding ornamental value to gardens. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in full sun, making it a popular choice for native plant gardens.

Its fine texture and ability to form dense clumps make it an excellent ground cover and erosion control plant.
