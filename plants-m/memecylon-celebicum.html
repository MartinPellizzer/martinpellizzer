Memecylon celebicum

Memecylon celebicum, commonly known as the Celebes memecylon, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to the Moluccas and parts of Indonesia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
