Mapouria compagniata

Mapouria compagniata, commonly known as the African violet.

It is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa, characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, velvety flowers. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and produces clusters of small, tubular flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mapouria compagniata is popular as a houseplant due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
