Maxillaria notylioglossa

Maxillaria notylioglossa, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, often pendulous growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically features dark green, velvety leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchid species. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with a yellow lip, and it blooms during the warmer months.

Maxillaria notylioglossa prefers bright, indirect light and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
