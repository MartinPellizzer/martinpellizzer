Mesosetum canastrense

Mesosetum canastrense, commonly known as the Canastrense grass, is a perennial grass species native to the Caribbean region.

It is characterized by its slender, erect growth habit and narrow, linear leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Mesosetum canastrense is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in stabilizing soil in tropical environments.
