Medicosma emarginata

Medicosma emarginata, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and along riverbanks, thriving in full sun. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its stems are erect and branching.

Medicosma emarginata is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
