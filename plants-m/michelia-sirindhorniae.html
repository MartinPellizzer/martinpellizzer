Michelia sirindhorniae

Michelia sirindhorniae, commonly known as the Royal Thai Michelia, is a flowering plant native to Thailand.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows as a small evergreen tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are glossy and elliptical, while its flowers are clustered in panicles.
