Messerschmidia minor

Messerschmidia minor, commonly known as the lesser messerschmidia, is a small, delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family (Orchidaceae) and is characterized by its slender stems and tiny, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces small, clustered inflorescences.
