Medicago sativa var. falcata

Medicago sativa var. falcata, commonly known as goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and distinctive, spurred flowers that range in color from pink to purple. This plant is known for its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil, making it beneficial for soil enrichment. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, open habitats.

However, it can be invasive in some regions due to its aggressive growth habit.
