Megastachya rigida

Megastachya rigida, commonly known as rigid spikegrass, is a perennial herb native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its tall, rigid stems that can reach heights of up to 2 meters. The plant produces dense clusters of spike-like inflorescences, which are adorned with small, tubular flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a rough, fibrous texture.

Megastachya rigida is often found in moist, open habitats such as swamps and wetlands.
