Memorialis lythroides

Memorialis lythroides, commonly known as the silverleaf nightshade, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features dark green, broad leaves with a silvery underside, giving it its common name. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically violet or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Due to its toxic properties, it should be handled with care.
