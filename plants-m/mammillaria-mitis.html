Mammillaria mitis

Mammillaria mitis, commonly known as the small white mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly tubercles. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its compact size and ornamental appeal, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in rock gardens.
