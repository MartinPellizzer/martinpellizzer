Muhlenbergia viridissima

Muhlenbergia viridissima, commonly known as green muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, grass-like leaves that are typically bright green and have a smooth texture. The plant produces small, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil.

Muhlenbergia viridissima is often used in native plant gardens for its drought tolerance and ecological benefits.
