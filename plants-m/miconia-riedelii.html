Miconia riedelii

Miconia riedelii, commonly known as the Riedel's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in diameter. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers, which are followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Miconia riedelii is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

However, it is also considered an invasive species in some regions due to its aggressive spread.
