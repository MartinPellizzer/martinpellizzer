Monadenium invenustum var. angustum

Monadenium invenustum var. angustum, commonly known as the narrow-leaved monadenium, is a succulent plant native to arid regions of southern Africa.

It features a short, thick stem with fleshy, narrow leaves that are often blue-green in color. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is adapted to survive in dry conditions, storing water in its leaves and stems.

Due to its unique appearance and resilience, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
