Maytenus rhodesica

Maytenus rhodesica, commonly known as Rhodes' mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to South Africa.

It belongs to the family Cornaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape.
