Medinilla rubicunda var. hasseltii

Medinilla rubicunda var. hasseltii, commonly known as the red medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, ovate leaves that are dark green with a slight waxy texture. The plant produces clusters of small, fragrant, pinkish-red flowers that bloom throughout the year, especially during the warmer months. Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for both indoor and outdoor cultivation in humid climates.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in horticultural displays.
