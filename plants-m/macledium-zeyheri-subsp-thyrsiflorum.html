Macledium zeyheri subsp. thyrsiflorum

Macledium zeyheri subsp.

thyrsiflorum, commonly known as the Cape thorn, is a shrub native to South Africa. It is characterized by its dense, thorny branches and small, oval-shaped leaves.

The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers, which are followed by small, red berries. It thrives in coastal and sandy soils, often found in fynbos vegetation.

Due to its hardy nature, it is sometimes cultivated as an ornamental plant.
