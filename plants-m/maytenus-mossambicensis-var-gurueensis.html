Maytenus mossambicensis var. gurueensis

Maytenus mossambicensis var. gurueensis, commonly known as the Gurueena mayten, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in humid, lowland forests. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate or elliptical in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered, with yellowish-green petals, and it produces small, fleshy berries as fruit.

The plant is valued for its hardy nature and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
