Medicago mollissima

Medicago mollissima, commonly known as velvet brome, is a perennial grass native to Eurasia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and dense, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear yellowish to reddish flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed areas or along roadsides.

Velvet brome is valued for its forage quality and its ability to stabilize soil in agricultural settings.
