Malvella

Malvella, commonly known as the velvetleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces showy, yellow flowers with prominent red stamens, which attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It is native to the Americas but has been introduced to various regions around the world.

Malvella is also known for its fibrous stems, which have been used in traditional crafts and fiber production.
