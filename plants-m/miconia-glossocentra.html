Miconia glossocentra

Miconia glossocentra, commonly known as the glossy-centred miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its large, dark green, glossy leaves that are often heart-shaped. It produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with prominent stamens, which are typical of the genus Miconia.

The species is known for its rapid growth and is sometimes considered invasive in certain areas.
