Mammillaria schelhasii var. triuncinata

Mammillaria schelhasii var. triuncinata, commonly known as the Triuncinate Pincushion Cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to pale yellow spines, which provide protection from herbivores and intense sunlight. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cactus collections.
