Mikania lasiandrae

Mikania lasiandrae, commonly known as the velvetleaf mikania, is a climbing vine native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and small, yellowish flowers. The plant has hairy stems and leaves, which give it a velvety texture. It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and along stream banks.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
