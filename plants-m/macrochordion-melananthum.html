Macrochordion melananthum

Macrochordion melananthum, commonly known as the black anther grass, is a flowering plant belonging to the grass family Poaceae.

It is characterized by its tall, slender stems and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, pale flowers. Its dark purple anthers are a distinctive feature, giving it its common name.

It is typically found in moist, open habitats such as wet meadows and stream banks.
