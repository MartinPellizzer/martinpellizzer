Mimosa patula

Mimosa patula, commonly known as the sensitive plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a subtle visual appeal. Mimosa patula thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Its rapid growth and unique leaf movement make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.
