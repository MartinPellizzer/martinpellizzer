Mammillaria meissneri

Mammillaria meissneri, commonly known as the Meissner's bunny ear cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded stem covered in short, white spines and is known for its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as a decorative houseplant due to its unique appearance.

It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight to flourish.
