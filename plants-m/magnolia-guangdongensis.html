Magnolia guangdongensis

Magnolia guangdongensis, commonly known as Guangdong magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along the stems. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
