Maxillariella microdendron

Maxillariella microdendron, commonly known as the miniature dendron orchid, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, moist environments. The plant features a compact, branching structure with narrow, grass-like leaves. Its flowers are small, fragrant, and usually white or pale pink, appearing in clusters.

It is appreciated for its delicate appearance and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
