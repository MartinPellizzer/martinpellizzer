Maieta guianensis var. peruviana

Maieta guianensis var. peruviana, commonly known as the Peruvian velvetleaf, is a tropical plant native to the Amazon Basin.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are covered in a dense, velvety pubescence. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive and ecologically significant species in its native habitat.
