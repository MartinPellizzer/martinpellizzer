Missiessya javanica

Missiessya javanica, commonly known as the Java orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, fragrant flowers that typically bloom in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has linear, grass-like leaves and produces elongated, pendulous flower spikes. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Missiessya javanica is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and greenhouses.
