Mimusops obtusifolia

Mimusops obtusifolia, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of white, waxy flowers that resemble snowballs, blooming in summer. The plant has simple, opposite, elliptical leaves with a leathery texture and dark green color. It produces small, round, yellow or orange fruits that contain a single seed.

Mimusops obtusifolia is often used in traditional medicine and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
