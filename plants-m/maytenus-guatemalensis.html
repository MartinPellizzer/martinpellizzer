Maytenus guatemalensis

Maytenus guatemalensis, commonly known as the Guatemalan mayten, is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree native to Central America.

It is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually elliptical or ovate in shape. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Maytenus guatemalensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
