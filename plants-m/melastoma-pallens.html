Melastoma pallens

Melastoma pallens, commonly known as the white-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has opposite, oval-shaped leaves with slightly hairy surfaces. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that releases tiny seeds when mature.
