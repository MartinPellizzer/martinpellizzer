Malleostemon roseus

Malleostemon roseus, commonly known as the pink malleostemon, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery bloom. It thrives in well-drained soils and is typically found in coastal and sandy habitats.

Malleostemon roseus is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive floral display.
