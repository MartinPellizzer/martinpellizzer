Monanthos obovatus

Monanthos obovatus, commonly known as the broad-leafed tickseed, is a flowering plant native to North America.

It is characterized by its showy yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. The plant has opposite, ovate to obovate leaves that are often hairy. It typically grows in open woods and prairies, thriving in well-drained soils.
