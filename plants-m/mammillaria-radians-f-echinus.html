Mammillaria radians f. echinus

Mammillaria radians f. echinus, commonly known as the spiny mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect sunlight.

Due to its compact size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
