Monstera chiapensis

Monstera chiapensis, commonly known as the Chiapas monstera, is a tropical vine native to Central America.

It is characterized by its large, deeply lobed leaves that can grow up to 1 meter in length. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments with bright, indirect light. Its stems are typically climbing and can reach lengths of several meters.

Monstera chiapensis is valued for its striking foliage and is a popular houseplant among foliage enthusiasts.
