Miltonia morelii

Miltonia morelii, commonly known as the Morelii orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the rainforests of Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant features a pseudobulb structure with fleshy leaves that store water, adapting to its humid, shaded habitat. Its flowers are borne on slender peduncles and often have a sweet fragrance, attracting pollinators like bees and hummingbirds.

Miltonia morelii is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements in cultivation.
