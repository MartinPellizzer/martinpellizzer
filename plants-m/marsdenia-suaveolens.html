Marsdenia suaveolens

Marsdenia suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented Marsdenia, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Apocynaceae family and is characterized by its climbing habit and opposite, elliptical leaves.
