Miscanthus sinensis f. variegatus

Miscanthus sinensis f. variegatus, commonly known as the variegated silver grass, is a striking ornamental grass with distinctive foliage.

Its leaves are narrow and feather-like, displaying a mix of silver-white and green hues along their length. This variety thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soil. It is known for its graceful, arching form and can reach heights of up to 3 feet.

The plant is often used in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and low maintenance requirements.
