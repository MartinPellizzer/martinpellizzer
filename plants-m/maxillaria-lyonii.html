Maxillaria lyonii

Maxillaria lyonii, commonly known as the Lyon's Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous growth habit. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with contrasting markings. Its leaves are typically elliptical and arranged in a rosette pattern around the pseudobulbs.

Maxillaria lyonii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining potting media for optimal growth.
