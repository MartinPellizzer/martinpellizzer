Monotes dasyanthus var. mutetetwa

Monotes dasyanthus var. mutetetwa, commonly known as the Mutetetwa daisy, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow and white. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

The variety is noted for its compact growth habit and resilience to drought conditions.
