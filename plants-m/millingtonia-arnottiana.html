Millingtonia arnottiana

Millingtonia arnottiana, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a flowering plant native to northern Australia.

It is a small, evergreen shrub that typically grows in coastal heathlands and sandy soils. The plant is characterized by its showy, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink, appearing in late summer to early autumn. Its leaves are narrow and evergreen, providing a lush backdrop to the vibrant blooms.

Millingtonia arnottiana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to tolerate salt spray and well-drained conditions.
