Mesua rivulorum

Mesua rivulorum, commonly known as the river mesua, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters, often emitting a strong scent. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy surface. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found near rivers and streams.

Mesua rivulorum is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
