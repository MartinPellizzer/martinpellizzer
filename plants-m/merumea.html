Merumea

Merumea, commonly known as the sea holly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its striking blue or purple flowers and spiny, fleshy leaves. Native to coastal regions of the Mediterranean, it thrives in salty, well-drained soils. The plant has a compact growth habit and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for coastal gardens and maritime landscapes.
