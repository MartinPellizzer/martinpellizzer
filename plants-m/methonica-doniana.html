Methonica doniana

Methonica doniana, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy leaves and showy, clustered flowers in shades of purple, pink, and white. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers indirect sunlight. Its rhizomatous root system allows it to spread and form dense mats.

Methonica doniana is widely cultivated as a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant blooms and compact growth habit.
