Miltonia × binotii var. flavescens

Miltonia × binotii var. flavescens, commonly known as the golden Miltonia, is a hybrid orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its vibrant, orange-pink flowers that bloom in late winter to early spring. The plant typically grows as a sympodial orchid with pseudobulbs and produces showy, fragrant blossoms. It prefers bright, indirect light and consistent humidity to thrive.

Its foliage consists of dark green, strap-like leaves that contribute to its ornamental appeal.
