Miconia leathyrsiflora

Miconia leathyrsiflora, commonly known as the thick-leaved miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves that are often leathery in texture. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers that are typically followed by bright red, fleshy berries. Miconia leathyrsiflora is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in humid, shaded environments.

However, it is considered an invasive species in some regions due to its aggressive spread and ecological impact.
