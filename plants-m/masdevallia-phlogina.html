Masdevallia phlogina

Masdevallia phlogina, commonly known as the flame orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its unique, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in cool, humid environments and thrives in shaded, well-drained soils. Its flowers are usually small to medium-sized, with intricate patterns and a variety of hues, including red, orange, and yellow.

Masdevallia phlogina is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
