Medinilla copelandii

Medinilla copelandii, commonly known as the Christmas bell, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It features showy, bell-shaped flowers that are typically pink or red, with a distinctive waxy texture. The plant has smooth, dark green leaves that are glossy and leathery in texture. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla copelandii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its striking appearance and seasonal blooming.
