Microlepia Smithii

Microlepia smithii is a rare and delicate fern native to the wet forests of New Zealand. Known for its intricate fronds and unique appearance, it thrives in cool, humid environments. This plant is often found in shaded, mossy areas where it forms dense, low-growing colonies. Its beauty has made it a prized species among plant enthusiasts and conservationists. Due to habitat loss, Microlepia smithii is now considered endangered, highlighting the need for continued protection.
