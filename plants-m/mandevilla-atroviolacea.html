Mandevilla atroviolacea

Mandevilla atroviolacea, commonly known as the dark viola mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from deep purple to nearly black. The plant features opposite, heart-shaped leaves that are often velvety in texture. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

Mandevilla atroviolacea is prized for its ornamental value and ability to climb trellises or walls.
