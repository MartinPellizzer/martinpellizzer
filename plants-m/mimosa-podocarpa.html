Mimosa podocarpa

Mimosa podocarpa, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its soft, feathery foliage and clusters of small, yellowish-white flowers. The plant produces distinctive, elongated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often growing in moist, well-drained soils.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
