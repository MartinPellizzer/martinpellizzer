Memecylon lawsonii var. lawsonii

Memecylon lawsonii var. lawsonii, commonly known as the slender memecylon, is a species native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree, typically reaching heights of up to 10 meters. The plant features opposite, elliptical leaves that are usually glossy and dark green in color. Its flowers are small and clustered in dense inflorescences, often with a white or pale yellow hue.

The fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that contains a single seed, contributing to its role in local ecosystems.
