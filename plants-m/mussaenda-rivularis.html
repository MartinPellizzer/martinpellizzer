Mussaenda rivularis

Mussaenda rivularis, commonly known as the river Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows along streambanks and moist habitats. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. Its showy, clustered inflorescences consist of small, white to yellowish flowers surrounded by bright orange bracts.

Mussaenda rivularis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant floral display.
