Melicope megastigma

Melicope megastigma, commonly known as the Hawaiian melicope, is a member of the Rutaceae family and is native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 10 meters in height. The plant has simple, alternate, elliptical leaves with a rough texture and a fragrant aroma. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in inflorescences, while the fruit is a woody, spherical drupe.

The plant is adapted to the tropical climate of Hawaii and is often found in moist, shaded forest environments.
