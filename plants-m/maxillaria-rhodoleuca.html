Maxillaria rhodoleuca

Maxillaria rhodoleuca, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and long, narrow leaves. The plant produces striking pink to lavender flowers with a distinctive slipper-shaped pouch. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria rhodoleuca is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
