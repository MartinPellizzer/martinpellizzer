Micromeria canariensis subsp. canariensis

Micromeria canariensis subsp.

canariensis, commonly known as the Canary Islands micromeria, is a small shrub native to the Canary Islands. It typically grows in rocky and coastal habitats, showing adaptations to the arid and windy conditions of its environment.

The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, white to pale yellow flowers. It is characterized by its compact growth habit and is often found in coastal scrublands.

Due to its limited distribution and specific ecological requirements, it is considered a rare and endemic species.
