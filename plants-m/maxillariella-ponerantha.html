Maxillariella ponerantha

Maxillariella ponerantha, commonly known as the Ponerantha orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, often with a yellowish throat. Its leaves are fleshy and arranged in a rosette pattern, which helps it retain moisture in its natural habitat.

Maxillariella ponerantha is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
