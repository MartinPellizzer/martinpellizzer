Mitrephora kostermansii

Mitrephora kostermansii, commonly known as the Koster's mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew and mango. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves with opposite arrangement and small, clustered flowers. Its fruit is a drupe, typically containing a single seed.
