Masdevallia wuellneri

Masdevallia wuellneri, commonly known as the Wuellner's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and striking, often colorful inflorescences that bloom during the cooler months. The plant typically produces intricate, showy flowers with intricate patterns and a variety of hues, ranging from deep purples to bright yellows. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light intensity and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its limited natural range and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a challenging species for cultivation.
