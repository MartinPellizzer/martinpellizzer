Marantochloa leucantha var. lasiocolea

Marantochloa leucantha var. lasiocolea, commonly known as the silky-leaved marantchloa, is a rare and striking plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental and sometimes edible plants. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves that are covered in a fine, silky hair, giving it a unique texture. It produces clusters of small, pale green flowers that are often hidden beneath the foliage.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by plant enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
