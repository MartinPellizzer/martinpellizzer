Mauria heterophylla var. contracta

Mauria heterophylla var. contracta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mauria, is a perennial herb native to the arid regions of southern Africa.

It is characterized by its reduced, narrow leaves that are often densely arranged along the stem. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to withstand drought conditions.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
