Myrtus claraensis

Myrtus claraensis, commonly known as the Clara's myrtle, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants such as eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in spring. Its fruit is a small, dark blue berry that contains several seeds.

Myrtus claraensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to thrive in well-drained soils.
