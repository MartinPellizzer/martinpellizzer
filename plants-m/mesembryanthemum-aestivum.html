Mesembryanthemum aestivum

Mesembryanthemum aestivum, commonly known as the summer iceplant, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its succulent, fleshy leaves and vibrant, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of yellow, orange, and red.
