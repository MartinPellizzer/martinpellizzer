Macrosolen evenius

Macrosolen evenius, commonly known as the Madagascar orchid, is a unique epiphytic plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of pink and white. The plant has long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that help it absorb moisture from the air. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in its native region.
