Macrocarpaea Arborea

Macrocarpaea tree, scientifically known as Macrocarpaea arborea, is a unique species native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia. This tree is characterized by its dense canopy and strikingly ornamental foliage, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Its leaves are typically broad and glossy, providing a lush green appearance throughout the year. The tree also produces small, fragrant flowers that attract pollinators and add to its ecological value. Due to its aesthetic appeal and adaptability, Macrocarpaea arborea is increasingly being cultivated in both residential and commercial landscapes.
