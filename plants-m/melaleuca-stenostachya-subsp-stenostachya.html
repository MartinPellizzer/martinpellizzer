Melaleuca stenostachya subsp. stenostachya

Melaleuca stenostachya subsp.

stenostachya, commonly known as the narrow-stamened melaleuca, is a shrub or small tree native to northern Australia. It is characterized by its aromatic, narrow leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pink.

The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, black seeds. It thrives in sandy or coastal soils and is often found in heathlands and along the coastline.

This subspecies is notable for its ecological importance in supporting local wildlife and its resilience to harsh environmental conditions.
