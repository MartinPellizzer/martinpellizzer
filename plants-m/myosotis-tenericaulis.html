Myosotis tenericaulis

Myosotis tenericaulis, commonly known as the slender forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its delicate blue or purple flowers and slender, upright stems. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Myosotis tenericaulis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
