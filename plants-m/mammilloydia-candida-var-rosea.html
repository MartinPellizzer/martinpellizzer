Mammilloydia candida var. rosea

Mammilloydia candida var. rosea, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a succulent cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in soft, white to pink spines. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact size and ornamental spines make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
