Mesua elegans

Mesua elegans, commonly known as the flame of the forest, is a tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a vibrant display during the flowering season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and greyish, with a distinct aromatic scent.

Mesua elegans is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
