Melastoma strigillosum

Melastoma strigillosum, commonly known as the hairy melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a hairy texture, giving it its common name. Its flowers are clustered in dense inflorescences and are usually pink or red in color.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release its seeds, contributing to its reproductive strategy.
