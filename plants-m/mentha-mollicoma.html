Mentha mollicoma

Mentha mollicoma, commonly known as velvet mint, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to Europe and western Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, velvety leaves that are covered in fine hairs, giving it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Mentha mollicoma is often used in herbal teas and traditional medicine for its aromatic and cooling properties.
