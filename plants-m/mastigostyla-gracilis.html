Mastigostyla gracilis

Mastigostyla gracilis, commonly known as the slender hornwort, is a type of freshwater algae belonging to the order Charales.

It is characterized by its filamentous structure, with long, branching filaments that bear small, spirally arranged cells. The plant typically grows in shallow, slow-moving waters and is often found attached to submerged surfaces such as rocks or aquatic plants.
