Myosotis vestita

Myosotis vestita, commonly known as the silverweed forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the temperate regions of the Northern Hemisphere.

It is characterized by its small, blue to purple flowers and hairy, silver-tinted leaves. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. It belongs to the genus Myosotis, which includes several species of forget-me-nots.

Myosotis vestita is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in supporting local pollinators.
