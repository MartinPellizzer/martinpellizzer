Myrcia rufula var. martiana

Myrcia rufula var. martiana, commonly known as the red myrtle, is a shrub or small tree native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Myrtaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like eucalyptus and clove. The plant is characterized by its dark, glossy leaves and small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink.

Its fruits are small, woody berries that contain several seeds, and the plant is often used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
