Magnolia baillonii

Magnolia baillonii, commonly known as the Bailloni magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a smooth margin. The tree has a rounded crown and can grow up to 15 meters tall.

It is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
