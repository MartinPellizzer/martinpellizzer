Metrosideros collina var. pumila

Metrosideros collina var. pumila, commonly known as the small flame tree, is a compact shrub or small tree native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its dense, rounded growth habit and its distinctive red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. The leaves are typically small, elliptical, and have a glossy, dark green appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular ornamental plant in coastal and temperate gardens.
