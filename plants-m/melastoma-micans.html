Melastoma micans

Melastoma micans, commonly known as the little bladdernut, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its small, fragrant, pink to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical, glossy leaves and produces small, round berries that turn from green to red as they mature. It is often found in moist, shaded areas such as forests and along stream banks.

Melastoma micans is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
