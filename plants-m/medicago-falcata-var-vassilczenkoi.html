Medicago falcata var. vassilczenkoi

Medicago falcata var. vassilczenkoi, commonly known as the Vassilczenkoi goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the steppes of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers, typical of the Fabaceae family. The plant has a deep root system, allowing it to thrive in dry and semi-arid conditions. It is often found in open grasslands and is adapted to nutrient-poor soils.

This variant is notable for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and supporting pollinators.
