Mimosa monancistra

Mimosa monancistra, commonly known as the white mimosoid, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are composed of numerous small, thread-like leaflets. The plant produces yellowish-white flowers arranged in spherical inflorescences, which are often mistaken for small balls. It thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it adaptable to various environmental conditions.

The plant is also known for its ability to fix nitrogen, which can improve soil fertility.
