Masdevallia neukermansii

Masdevallia neukermansii, commonly known as the Neukermans' masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and its intricate, often colorful flowers that resemble miniature orchids. The plant typically produces a single or few flowers per spike, which are adorned with intricate patterns and a wide range of hues. Its leaves are usually arranged in a rosette and are covered in a dense layer of fine, velvety hairs.

This species is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively modest care requirements.
