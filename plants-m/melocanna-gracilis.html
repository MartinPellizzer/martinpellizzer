Melocanna gracilis

Melocanna gracilis, commonly known as the slender bamboo, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the grass family (Poaceae) and is characterized by its tall, slender stalks and dense foliage. It has a robust root system that helps stabilize soil and prevent erosion.
