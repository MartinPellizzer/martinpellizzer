Mammillaria fulvispina

Mammillaria fulvispina, commonly known as the yellow spine cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white to yellow spines, which give it its common name. The plant has a compact growth habit with a short, thick stem and produces yellow flowers in the spring. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
