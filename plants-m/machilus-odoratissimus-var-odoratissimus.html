Machilus odoratissimus var. odoratissimus

Machilus odoratissimus var. odoratissimus, commonly known as the fragrant machilus, is a small evergreen tree or shrub native to southern China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family, which includes laurel and avocado trees. The plant is characterized by its narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green. It produces small, fragrant white flowers followed by dark blue berries.

The species is valued for its aromatic properties and is often used in traditional medicine and as an ornamental plant.
