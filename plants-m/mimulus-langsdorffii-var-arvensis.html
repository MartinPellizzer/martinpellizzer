Mimulus langsdorffii var. arvensis

Mimulus langsdorffii var. arvensis, commonly known as the field monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange or yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and streambanks. It has heart-shaped leaves and produces elongated seed pods that contain numerous small, black seeds.

This variety is often found in disturbed soils and is relatively easy to cultivate in gardens.
