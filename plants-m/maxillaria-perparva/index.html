Maxillaria Perparva

Small maxillaria, scientifically known as Maxillaria perparva, is a delicate orchid native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. With its compact size and intricate floral structure, this species stands out among its relatives in the orchid family. It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink, often with a subtle purple or red tinge. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation. Due to its rarity and unique beauty, Maxillaria perparva is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
