Metrosideros collina var. newellii

Metrosideros collina var. newellii, commonly known as the Newell's flame tree, is a variant of the flame tree species native to New Zealand.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen shrub or tree characterized by its distinctive red or orange-red flowers that bloom in clusters. The leaves are typically elliptical, glossy, and dark green, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant floral display. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and lowland areas.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and as a street tree.
