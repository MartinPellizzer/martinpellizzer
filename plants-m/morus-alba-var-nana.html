Morus alba var. nana

Morus alba var. nana, commonly known as the dwarf white mulberry, is a compact variety of the white mulberry tree.

It is characterized by its small, rounded leaves and vigorous growth habit. This plant is native to Asia but has been cultivated in various regions for its ecological and ornamental value. It produces small, fuzzy white berries that are edible for birds and some animals.

The dwarf white mulberry is often used in urban landscaping due to its adaptability and low maintenance requirements.
