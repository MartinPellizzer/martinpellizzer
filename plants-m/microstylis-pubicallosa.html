Microstylis pubicallosa

Microstylis pubicallosa, commonly known as the silky microstylis, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically bloom in shades of white or pale yellow. The plant has slender, grass-like leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a distinctive appearance. It thrives in moist, shaded environments, often found in forest understories.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists.
