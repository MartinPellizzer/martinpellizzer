Mentha × dumetorum f. poliotricha

Mentha × dumetorum f. poliotricha, commonly known as the purple mint, is a botanical variant of the hybrid mint species.

It is characterized by its distinctive violet or purple flower spikes, which distinguish it from other mint varieties. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often found in woodland areas or along stream banks. Its leaves are usually a deep green with a slightly serrated edge, and it emits a strong, aromatic scent.

The plant is valued in horticulture for its ornamental qualities and is also used in some traditional herbal remedies.
