Melocactus rubrispinus

Melocactus rubrispinus, commonly known as the red-spined melon cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stem that is typically green to reddish in color. The plant produces clusters of bright yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its spines are reddish and can be quite dense, providing protection from herbivores.

Melocactus rubrispinus is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water once established.
