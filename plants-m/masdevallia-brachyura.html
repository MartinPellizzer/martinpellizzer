Masdevallia brachyura

Masdevallia brachyura, commonly known as the short-leaf masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, epiphytic growth habit and its preference for cool, humid environments. The plant produces intricate, often fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically during the cooler months. Its leaves are usually short and clustered, adapted to its natural habitat on tree branches.

Masdevallia brachyura is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
