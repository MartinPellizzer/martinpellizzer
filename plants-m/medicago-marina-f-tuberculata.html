Medicago marina f. tuberculata

Medicago marina f. tuberculata, commonly known as the sea medic, is a halophytic plant adapted to coastal environments.

It thrives in saline soils and is often found along shores and dunes. The plant has small, trifoliate leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in summer. It is characterized by its succulent stems and small, tuberculate leaves, which help reduce water loss.

This species plays a role in stabilizing coastal soils and is of interest in ecological restoration projects.
