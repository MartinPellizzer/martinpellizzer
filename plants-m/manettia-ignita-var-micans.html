Manettia ignita var. micans

Manettia ignita var. micans, commonly known as the glossy manettia, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its small, elliptical leaves that are glossy and dark green, often with a slight pubescence. The plant produces clusters of small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruits are spherical berries that turn red when mature, containing several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value in shaded garden settings.
