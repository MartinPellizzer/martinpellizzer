Microstylis cylindrostachya

Microstylis cylindrostachya, commonly known as the slender-styled grass, is a member of the Poaceae family, characterized by its grass-like appearance.

It is a small, herbaceous plant that typically grows in moist, temperate environments. The plant features narrow, cylindrical leaves and produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets. Its reproductive structures are adapted for wind pollination, a common trait among grasses.

Microstylis cylindrostachya is often found in wet meadows and along stream banks, where it contributes to the ecological stability of these habitats.
