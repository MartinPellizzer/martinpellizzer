Mussaenda aegyptiaca

Mussaenda aegyptiaca, commonly known as the Egyptian Mussaenda, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It features showy, clustered yellow bracts that resemble flowers, though they are actually modified leaves. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in umbels.

Mussaenda aegyptiaca is often cultivated for its ornamental value and thrives in warm, humid climates.
