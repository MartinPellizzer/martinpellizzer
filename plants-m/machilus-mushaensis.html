Machilus mushaensis

Machilus mushaensis, commonly known as the Mushinga tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to the mountainous regions of southern China, particularly in Yunnan Province. The tree is characterized by its evergreen leaves, which are elliptical and have a smooth upper surface. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically white or pale yellow and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a small, dark-colored drupe that contains a single seed.
