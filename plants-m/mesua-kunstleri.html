Mesua kunstleri

Mesua kunstleri, commonly known as the red flame tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red flowers that bloom in clusters during the rainy season. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and glossy in appearance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and full sunlight.

Mesua kunstleri is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.
