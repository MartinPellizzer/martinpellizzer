Muraltia karroica

Muraltia karroica, commonly known as the red mombasa bean, is a small evergreen shrub native to eastern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces small, round, red or orange fruits that contain edible seeds. It is often grown for its ornamental value and as a source of food in local communities.

Muraltia karroica is adapted to tropical and subtropical climates and prefers well-drained soils.
