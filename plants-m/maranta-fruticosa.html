Maranta fruticosa

Maranta fruticosa, commonly known as the red-rooted arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes various ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant is characterized by its upright, branching stems and heart-shaped leaves that are often brightly colored. Its roots are fibrous and have been traditionally used for their starchy properties.

Maranta fruticosa thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
