Mammillaria bocensis var. movasana

Mammillaria bocensis var. movasana, commonly known as the Movasana mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in white to pale yellow tubercles. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
