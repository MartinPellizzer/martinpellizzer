Maxillaria perparva

Maxillaria perparva, commonly known as the miniature orchid or small maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is a small, terrestrial orchid with a compact growth habit and is often found in shaded, humid environments. The plant produces delicate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, typically in spring or early summer. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it has a short, thick rhizome.

Maxillaria perparva is valued for its ornamental appeal and is relatively easy to cultivate in suitable conditions.
