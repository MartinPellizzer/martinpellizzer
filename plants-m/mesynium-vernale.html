Mesynium vernale

Mesynium vernale, commonly known as the spring mesynium, is a herbaceous plant native to certain regions of Europe.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream sides. The plant features small, greenish-yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are alternate, lance-shaped, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

Mesynium vernale is known for its delicate appearance and its role in supporting early pollinators in its habitat.
