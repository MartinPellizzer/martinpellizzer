Masdevallia hystrix

Masdevallia hystrix, commonly known as the spiked masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its distinctive, wiry, spiky leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with intricate patterns. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires high air circulation.

Due to its unique appearance and delicate nature, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
