Manihot caerulescens

Manihot caerulescens, commonly known as blue arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by capsules containing numerous seeds. Its roots are edible and have a starchy texture similar to other arrowroot species.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value as well as its culinary uses.
