Mammillaria lindsayi var. cobrensis

Mammillaria lindsayi var. cobrensis, commonly known as the Cobrensis mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white to pale yellow tubercles, with short, white spines. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
