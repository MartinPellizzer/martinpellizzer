Manihot aipi var. lutescens

Manihot aipi var. lutescens, commonly known as the yellow arrowleaf.

It is a perennial shrub native to South America, characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves with a yellowish-green hue. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that develop into woody capsules containing numerous seeds. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in tropical and subtropical regions.

Due to its ornamental value and potential medicinal uses, it is cultivated in some horticultural settings.
