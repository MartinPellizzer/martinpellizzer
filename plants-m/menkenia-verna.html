Menkenia verna

Menkenia verna, commonly known as the spring bellflower, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 centimeters. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that are usually blue or purple in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Menkenia verna is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
