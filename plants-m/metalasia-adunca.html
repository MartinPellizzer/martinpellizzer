Metalasia adunca

Metalasia adunca, commonly known as the narrow-leaf metalasia, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in grassland habitats, often found in regions with well-drained soils. The plant features slender, upright stems and narrow, linear leaves that are usually covered in fine hairs. Its small, clustered flowers are typically pale blue or purple, blooming during the warmer months.

Metalasia adunca is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
