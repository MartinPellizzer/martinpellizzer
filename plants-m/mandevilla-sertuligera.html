Mandevilla sertuligera

Mandevilla sertuligera, commonly known as the flame vine, is a tropical flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of bright orange-red tubular flowers. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped blooms that attract pollinators such as hummingbirds and butterflies. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped and glossy, providing a striking contrast to the vibrant flowers.

Mandevilla sertuligera thrives in warm, sunny climates and is often cultivated as a climbing plant or in containers.
