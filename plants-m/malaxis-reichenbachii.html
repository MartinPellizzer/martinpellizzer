Malaxis reichenbachii

Malaxis reichenbachii, commonly known as the velvet orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, hairy leaves that give it a velvety appearance. The plant produces small, greenish-yellow flowers that are often clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its unique morphology and habitat preferences make it a notable species in botanical studies.
