Mesembryanthemum lectum

Mesembryanthemum lectum, commonly known as the carpet flower, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family, which is characterized by its fleshy leaves and adaptations to arid environments. Mesembryanthemum lectum is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
