Manilkara multifida

Manilkara multifida, commonly known as the star-leaf soursop, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia and the Pacific Islands.

It is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are deeply lobed, giving it a distinctive, star-like appearance. The tree produces smooth, green, spiny fruits that are typically harvested when fully mature. Its botanical features include rough, fissured bark and small, white flowers that bloom in clusters.

The plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some regions for its fruit and timber.
