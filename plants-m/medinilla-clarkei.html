Medinilla clarkei

Medinilla clarkei, commonly known as the Clarke's medinilla, is a tropical shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant typically grows to a height of 1 to 2 meters and thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are oval-shaped with a glossy surface, and the flowers are often borne on short peduncles.

Medinilla clarkei is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
