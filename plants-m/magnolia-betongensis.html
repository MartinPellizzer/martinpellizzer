Magnolia betongensis

Magnolia betongensis, commonly known as the Betong magnolia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom in spring, typically in shades of white to pale pink. The plant has broad, lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and arranged alternately along the branches. It thrives in humid climates with well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape tree.
