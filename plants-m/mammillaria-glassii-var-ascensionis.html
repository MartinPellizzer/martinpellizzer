Mammillaria glassii var. ascensionis

Mammillaria glassii var. ascensionis, commonly known as the Ascension Island mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to the subtropical region of Ascension Island.

It features a globular stem covered with small, white to pale blue tubercles that give it a distinctive, spiky appearance. The plant produces pale yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by small, red berries. It is well adapted to arid conditions and requires minimal water, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.

Its unique morphology and limited distribution make it a rare and sought-after specimen in botanical collections.
