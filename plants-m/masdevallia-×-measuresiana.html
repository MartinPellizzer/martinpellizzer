Masdevallia × measuresiana

Masdevallia × measuresiana, commonly known as the hybrid masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from crosses between different masdevallia species.

It is native to the Andean regions of South America, where it thrives in cool, humid environments. The plant typically features intricate, showy flowers in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and white. It has a pseudobulbous structure with fleshy leaves that help store water, adapting it to its high-altitude habitat.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique floral characteristics.
