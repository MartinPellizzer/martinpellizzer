Maireana campanulata

Maireana campanulata, commonly known as the slender bellflower, is an evergreen shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, upright growth habit and produces small, bell-shaped flowers that are typically blue or purple in color. The plant features small, leathery leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Maireana campanulata is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
