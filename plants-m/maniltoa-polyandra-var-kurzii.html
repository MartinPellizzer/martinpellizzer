Maniltoa polyandra var. kurzii

Maniltoa polyandra var. kurzii, commonly known as the Kurz's maniltoa, is a rare and striking plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, adding to its ornamental appeal.
