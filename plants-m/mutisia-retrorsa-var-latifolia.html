Mutisia retrorsa var. latifolia

Mutisia retrorsa var. latifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features broad, lance-shaped leaves with a rough texture and a distinctive, aromatic scent. Its flowers are typically yellow or orange, arranged in clusters, and bloom during the warmer months.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
