Microstylis longidens

Microstylis longidens, commonly known as the long-spurred microstylis, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Orchidaceae.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers that typically feature a unique spurred structure. The plant is native to certain regions of Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, while the inflorescence consists of a single, erect spike bearing several blooms.

Due to its ornamental value and rarity, it is often cultivated in specialized botanical gardens.
