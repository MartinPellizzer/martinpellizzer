Memecylon arenicola

Memecylon arenicola, commonly known as the coastal memecylon, is a shrub native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It typically grows in sandy or coastal soils, showcasing its adaptability to harsh environments. The plant features small, elliptical leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, aiding in moisture retention. Its flowers are usually white or pale yellow, clustered in small inflorescences.

Memecylon arenicola plays a role in stabilizing coastal dunes and providing habitat for local wildlife.
