Mimusops cuneata

Mimusops cuneata, commonly known as the African snowball, is a deciduous tree native to tropical Africa.

It is characterized by its smooth, greyish bark and clusters of white, waxy flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant produces small, round, yellow or orange fruits that contain seeds covered in a sticky pulp. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking floral display.

The tree is also valued for its hard, durable wood in some regions.
