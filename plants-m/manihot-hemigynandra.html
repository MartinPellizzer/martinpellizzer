Manihot hemigynandra

Manihot hemigynandra, commonly known as the African arrowleaf, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that are clustered in umbels, and it is often found in moist, shaded habitats. Its latex is milky and can be toxic, making it a plant to handle with care.
