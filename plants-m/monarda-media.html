Monarda media

Monarda media, commonly known as scarlet bee balm, is a herbaceous perennial native to North America.

It features showy, tubular red flowers that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. The plant has lance-shaped, aromatic leaves with a mint-like scent. It typically grows to a height of 2 to 3 feet and thrives in full sun to partial shade.

Monarda media is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.
