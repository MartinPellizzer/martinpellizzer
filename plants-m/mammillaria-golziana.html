Mammillaria golziana

Mammillaria golziana, commonly known as the Golzian pincushion cactus, is a small, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features dense, white to pale green spines and is characterized by its rounded, segmented body. The plant typically grows up to 10 centimeters in height and is known for its compact, spiny appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
