Mesembryanthemum taylorii

Mesembryanthemum taylorii, commonly known as Taylor's ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum taylorii is often used in rock gardens and as a decorative plant due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements.
