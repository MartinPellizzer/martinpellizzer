Menodora integrifolia f. subprostrata

Menodora integrifolia f. subprostrata, commonly known as the prostrate moonseed, is a variant of the moonseed plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is a herbaceous perennial characterized by its prostrate growth habit, with stems that lie close to the ground. The leaves are typically ovate to elliptic, with entire margins and a smooth texture. Its flowers are small, yellowish-green, and clustered in umbels, typical of the Menodora genus.

The plant is notable for its medicinal properties and is sometimes used in traditional herbal remedies.
