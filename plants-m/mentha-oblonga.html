Mentha oblonga

Mentha oblonga, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mint, is a perennial herb native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its slender, lance-shaped leaves and a strong, aromatic scent. The plant produces small, pale blue or violet flowers that bloom in summer. Mentha oblonga is often found in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun.

It is widely used in traditional medicine and as a culinary herb due to its refreshing flavor.
