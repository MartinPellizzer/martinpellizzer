Myrsine sandwicensis var. denticulata

Myrsine sandwicensis var. denticulata, commonly known as the Kona myrsine, is a shrub native to the Hawaiian Islands.

It belongs to the Rubiaceae family and is characterized by its small, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Due to habitat loss, it is considered a vulnerable species and is protected in conservation efforts.
