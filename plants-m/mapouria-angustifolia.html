Mapouria angustifolia

Mapouria angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf mapouria, is a flowering plant native to parts of Africa and the Arabian Peninsula.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features narrow, lance-shaped leaves and produces small, fragrant yellow flowers. It is often found in rocky soils and can thrive in regions with low rainfall.

Mapouria angustifolia is valued for its ornamental appeal and has some traditional uses in local communities.
