Melastomastrum capitatum

Melastomastrum capitatum, commonly known as the Cape flower, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from red to orange, often with a distinctive yellow center. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are typically hairy and have a rough texture. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Melastomastrum capitatum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens.
