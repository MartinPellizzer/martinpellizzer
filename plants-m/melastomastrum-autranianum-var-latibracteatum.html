Melastomastrum autranianum var. latibracteatum

Melastomastrum autranianum var. latibracteatum, commonly known as the broad-bract melastoma, is a flowering plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its showy, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and prominent veins. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The broad bracts that give it its name are a distinctive feature, adding to its ornamental value.
