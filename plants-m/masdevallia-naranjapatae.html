Masdevallia naranjapatae

Masdevallia naranjapatae, commonly known as the orange-flowered masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange petals and a compact, bushy growth habit. The plant typically produces small, clustered flowers with a distinctively colored lip. It thrives in cool, high-altitude environments with high humidity and indirect light.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and valuable species in botanical collections.
