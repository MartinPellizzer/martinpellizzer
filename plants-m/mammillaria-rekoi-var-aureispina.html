Mammillaria rekoi var. aureispina

Mammillaria rekoi var. aureispina, commonly known as the golden spined mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in short, white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the summer months. Its thick, fleshy stems store water, allowing it to thrive in arid environments.

It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
