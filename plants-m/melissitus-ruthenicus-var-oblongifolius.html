Melissitus ruthenicus var. oblongifolius

Melissitus ruthenicus var. oblongifolius, commonly known as the broadleaf melissitus, is a rare variant of the melissitus species native to specific regions of Eastern Europe.

It is characterized by its elongated, broad leaves that are typically dark green and slightly hairy on the underside.
