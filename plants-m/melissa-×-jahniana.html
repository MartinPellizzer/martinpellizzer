Melissa × jahniana

Melissa × jahniana, commonly known as lemon balm hybrid, is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Melissa officinalis (common lemon balm) and Melissa cunningtonii (Cunnington's lemon balm).

It is a herbaceous perennial shrub with aromatic, slightly hairy leaves that emit a strong lemony scent. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters, typically from late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun, making it a popular choice for gardens and herbal cultivation.

Melissa × jahniana is valued for its calming properties and is often used in teas and aromatherapy.
