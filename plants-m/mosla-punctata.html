Mosla punctata

Mosla punctata, commonly known as Chinese burnet, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Asia.

It features upright stems covered with small, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has finely divided, fern-like leaves that are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Mosla punctata is valued for its ornamental appeal and medicinal properties.
