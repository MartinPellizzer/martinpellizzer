Moraea sisyrinchium

Moraea sisyrinchium, commonly known as the starry moraea, is a member of the Iridaceae family and is native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. It produces showy, tubular blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Moraea sisyrinchium is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and botanical collections.
