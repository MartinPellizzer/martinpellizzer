Mediolobivia rubriflora var. nigrilongiseta

Mediolobivia rubriflora var. nigrilongiseta, commonly known as the Black-Long-haired Flamebloom, is a rare and striking plant native to the high-altitude regions of the Andes.

It is characterized by its vibrant red, flame-like flowers that contrast sharply with its dark, velvety foliage. The plant's leaves are elongated and covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a unique textured appearance. It thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil.
