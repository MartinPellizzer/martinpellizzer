Michelia shiluensis

Michelia shiluensis, commonly known as the Shilu magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are ovate to elliptic in shape. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it often grows in moist, shaded forest environments.

Michelia shiluensis is valued for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
