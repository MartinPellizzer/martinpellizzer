Maxillaria heterophylla var. magnifolia

Maxillaria heterophylla var. magnifolia, commonly known as the Magnificent Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that come in a range of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are broad and strap-like, and it produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients.

This variety is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
