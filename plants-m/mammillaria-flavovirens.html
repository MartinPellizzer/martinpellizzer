Mammillaria flavovirens

Mammillaria flavovirens, commonly known as the yellow-green mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stems covered in white to yellow spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its compact size and attractive appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and container cultivation.
