Malpighiodes spruceana

Malpighiodes spruceana, commonly known as the Spruce's malpighioid, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Malpighiaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its small, clustered flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. Its leaves are opposite and often have a velvety texture.

It is often found in moist, shaded environments such as rainforests and is valued for its ornamental appeal.
