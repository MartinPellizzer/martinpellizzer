Medinilla merrittii

Medinilla merrittii, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round, yellow fruits that are edible and have a sweet, tangy flavor. Medinilla merrittii thrives in humid, warm climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and blooms.

It prefers partial shade and well-draining soil for optimal growth.
