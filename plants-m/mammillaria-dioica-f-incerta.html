Mammillaria dioica f. incerta

Mammillaria dioica f. incerta, commonly known as the uncertain mammillaria, is a rare and distinctive cactus species native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is characterized by its small, globular shape and a dense covering of white to pale green spines. The plant typically grows in rocky soils and thrives in full sun, requiring minimal water due to its adapted succulent structure. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from the areoles in the spring.

This variety is noted for its unique morphology and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its ornamental value.
