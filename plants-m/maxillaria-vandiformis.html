Maxillaria vandiformis

Maxillaria vandiformis, commonly known as the Vandifom Orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, often with intricate patterns. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its pseudobulbs are short and stout, supporting its evergreen foliage.

Maxillaria vandiformis is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
