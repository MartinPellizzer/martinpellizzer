Microstylis schlechteri

Microstylis schlechteri, commonly known as the slender-styled orchid, is a small, terrestrial orchid native to parts of Africa.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, grass-like foliage and tiny, inconspicuous flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories or rocky crevices. Its flowers are usually greenish-white with a slight purple tinge, and they bloom during the rainy season.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and less-studied species in botanical research.
