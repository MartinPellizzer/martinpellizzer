Millettia tenuipes

Millettia tenuipes, commonly known as the slender millettia, is a flowering plant belonging to the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including parts of China, India, and Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and trifoliate leaves composed of three small, ovate leaflets. Its small, yellowish-green flowers are borne in clusters and are followed by elongated, bean-like pods that contain several seeds.

Millettia tenuipes is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and shrublands.
