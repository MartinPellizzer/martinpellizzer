Monocera ceylanica

Monocera ceylanica, commonly known as the Ceylon star anise, is a flowering plant native to Sri Lanka.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its aromatic, star-shaped fruits. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that develop into the distinctive, wrinkled, star-shaped berries. Its leaves are glossy and lance-shaped, with a strong, spicy scent.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its use in traditional medicine.
