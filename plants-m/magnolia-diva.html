Magnolia diva

Magnolia diva, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white or pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
