Moraea gigantea

Moraea gigantea, commonly known as the giant moraea, is a striking perennial herb native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that can reach up to 15 centimeters in diameter, typically in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features strap-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color and can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Moraea gigantea is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated for its medicinal properties.
