Maranta conspicua

Maranta conspicua, commonly known as the nightshade maranta, is a striking tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, showy leaves that display a range of colors, including red, pink, and purple, often changing with the time of day. The plant produces small, white flowers that are typically borne on slender stems. Its foliage is known for its unique ability to change color in response to light, a phenomenon called nyctinasty.

Maranta conspicua is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ease of care.
