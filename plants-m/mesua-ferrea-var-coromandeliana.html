Mesua ferrea var. coromandeliana

Mesua ferrea var. coromandeliana, commonly known as the Coromandel flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking red or orange flowers that appear in clusters, giving it a fiery appearance. The leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a glossy green surface. The tree produces small, woody pods that contain several seeds.

It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is adapted to warm, humid climates.
