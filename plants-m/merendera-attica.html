Merendera attica

Merendera attica, commonly known as the Greek mullein, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe and parts of Asia.

It belongs to the Scrophulariaceae family and is characterized by its tall, erect stems and fuzzy, woolly leaves. Merendera attica is also valued for its medicinal properties, with historical use in traditional remedies.
