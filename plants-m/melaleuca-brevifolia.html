Melaleuca brevifolia

Melaleuca brevifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaved tea tree, is a shrub or small tree native to Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and small, often white or pinkish flowers that appear in clusters. The plant produces woody capsules that contain numerous small, flat seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in coastal and sandy environments.

Melaleuca brevifolia is valued for its aromatic foliage and has been used in traditional medicine.
