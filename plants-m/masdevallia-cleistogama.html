Masdevallia cleistogama

Masdevallia cleistogama, commonly known as the closed-flower masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, small, and often hidden flowers that are typically enclosed within leaf-like bracts. The plant produces intricate, tubular flowers with specialized structures that facilitate pollination by specific insects. Its leaves are usually clustered at the base and are often covered in a dense layer of fine hairs.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its delicate and unusual floral display.
