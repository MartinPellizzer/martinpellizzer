Mariposa clavata var. pallida

Mariposa clavata var. pallida, commonly known as the pale winged dandelion, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its yellow composite flowers and pinnately lobed leaves. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and disturbed soils. Its flowers bloom in early spring, and the plant is known for its delicate, wind-dispersed seeds.

It is often found in California and parts of Oregon.
