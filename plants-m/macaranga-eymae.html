Macaranga eymae

Macaranga eymae, commonly known as the silver macaranga, is a member of the Euphorbiaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Macaranga eymae is also notable for its role in supporting local biodiversity and its use in traditional medicine.
