Mammillaria spinosissima f. auricoma

Mammillaria spinosissima f. auricoma, commonly known as the auricoma mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a globular shape and a gray-green stem.

It features prominent, spiny areoles that bear white to pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. The plant has a slow growth rate and is well-suited for rock gardens or as a container plant. It thrives in full sun and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid climates.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
