Monsonia filia

Monsonia filia, commonly known as the Cape primrose, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Geraniaceae family and is characterized by its showy, clustered flowers that range in color from pink to purple. The plant typically grows as a low-growing shrub with hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Monsonia filia is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
