Mentha Longifolia Var. Stenotricha

Mentha longifolia var. stenotricha is a lesser-known species of mint with a unique appearance and subtle fragrance. This plant is characterized by its narrow, hairy leaves and compact growth habit. It is native to certain regions of Asia and is often found in moist, shaded environments. Despite its modest size, it holds value in traditional medicine and horticulture. Its distinct features make it a fascinating subject for botanical study and cultivation.
