Mammillaria milleri f. gueldemanniana

Mammillaria milleri f. gueldemanniana, commonly known as the Gueldemann's mammillaria, is a rare and striking cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in dense, white to pale yellow spines. The plant produces small, showy flowers that range from yellow to orange in color. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is a prized specimen among cactus enthusiasts.
