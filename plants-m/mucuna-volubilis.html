Mucuna volubilis

Mucuna volubilis, commonly known as the cow pea or velvet bean, is a leguminous plant native to tropical regions.

It is characterized by its trailing growth habit and trifoliate leaves with hairy surfaces. The plant produces small, pod-like fruits that contain several seeds. It is known for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility.

Mucuna volubilis is also valued for its medicinal properties and use in traditional herbal remedies.
