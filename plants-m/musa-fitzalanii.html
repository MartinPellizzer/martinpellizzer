Musa fitzalanii

Musa fitzalanii, commonly known as the Red River banana, is a species of banana native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clusters of yellow, edible fruits. The plant thrives in tropical climates with high humidity and well-drained soil. It is often cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.

Musa fitzalanii is a member of the Musaceae family and is closely related to other banana species.
