Millettia urophylla

Millettia urophylla, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a deciduous leguminous plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves and clusters of yellow, pendulous flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant produces elongated, woody pods that contain flat, bean-like seeds. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sunlight, making it suitable for ornamental and agroforestry purposes.

Its ability to fix nitrogen makes it beneficial for soil enrichment in agricultural settings.
