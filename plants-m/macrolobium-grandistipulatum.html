Macrolobium grandistipulatum

Macrolobium grandistipulatum, commonly known as the large-leafed legume, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves and distinctive, elongated stipules. The plant produces yellowish-green flowers arranged in dense clusters, which are followed by elongated, pod-like fruits. Native to tropical regions of South America, it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both ecological and horticultural studies.
