Melastoma saxatile

Melastoma saxatile, commonly known as the rock melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in rocky or limestone soils, often in tropical and subtropical regions. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or white, with showy petals and prominent stamens. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and often have a rough texture.

Melastoma saxatile is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual interest.
