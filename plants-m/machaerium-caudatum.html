Machaerium caudatum

Machaerium caudatum, commonly known as the long-tailed machaerium, is a tropical plant native to regions of South America.

It is a member of the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its flowers are usually pink or purple, arranged in dense, racemose inflorescences.
