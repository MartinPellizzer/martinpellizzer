Magnolia excelsa

Magnolia excelsa, commonly known as the giant magnolia, is a striking tropical tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are oval in shape and have a smooth texture. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, often marked with lenticels.

The tree can grow up to 40 meters tall, making it one of the tallest magnolia species.
