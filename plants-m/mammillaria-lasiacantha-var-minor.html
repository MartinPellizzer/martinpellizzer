Mammillaria lasiacantha var. minor

Mammillaria lasiacantha var. minor, commonly known as the small mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical or slightly elongated stem with small, white to pale yellow radial spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is well-suited for rock gardens and containers due to its compact size and low maintenance requirements.

Its slow growth rate makes it ideal for collectors seeking a unique and ornamental cactus.
