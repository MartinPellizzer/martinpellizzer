Microtis parviflora

Microtis parviflora, commonly known as the small-flowered microtis, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae, commonly known as the orchid family. This species is characterized by its compact growth habit and small, delicate flowers that are typically white or pale pink. The plant prefers well-drained soils and is often found in heathlands and open forests.
