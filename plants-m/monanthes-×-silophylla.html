Monanthes × silophylla

Monanthes × silophylla, commonly known as the hybrid silophyll.

It is a hybrid plant resulting from the cross between Monanthes and Silphium, belonging to the Asteraceae family. The plant typically features broad, waxy leaves and produces yellow or orange composite flowers. It is native to regions with temperate climates and is often found in disturbed soils.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens for its striking foliage and floral display.
