Mespilodaphne pretiosa var. angustifolia

Mespilodaphne pretiosa var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mespilodaphne, is a species native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree with lanceolate, glossy dark green leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by edible, sweet-tart berries. Its botanical significance lies in its unique morphology and potential medicinal properties.

It is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical and subtropical forests.
