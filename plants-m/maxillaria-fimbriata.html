Maxillaria fimbriata

Maxillaria fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant typically blooms during the dry season, and its flowers have a distinctive fringed appearance on the petals. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria fimbriata is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
