Mentha arvensis var. praeclara

Mentha arvensis var. praeclara, commonly known as the noble mint, is a variant of the field mint species.

It is characterized by its aromatic, serrated leaves and strong menthol scent. This plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its flowering stems bear small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in late summer.

The plant is valued for its medicinal properties and is often used in herbal remedies.
