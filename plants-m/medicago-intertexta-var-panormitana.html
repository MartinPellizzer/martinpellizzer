Medicago intertexta var. panormitana

Medicago intertexta var. panormitana, commonly known as the Panormitana medick, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows in dry, rocky soils and is often found in open habitats such as steppes and grasslands. It has a deep root system that helps it survive in arid conditions.

This variety is valued for its role in soil stabilization and as a forage plant for livestock.
