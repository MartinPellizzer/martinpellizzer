Mosdenia waterbergensis

Mosdenia waterbergensis, commonly known as the Waterberg mosdenia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky and sandy soils, often in mountainous regions. It produces small, yellow to orange flowers that bloom in late summer.

Mosdenia waterbergensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
