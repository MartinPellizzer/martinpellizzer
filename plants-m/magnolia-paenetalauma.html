Magnolia paenetalauma

Magnolia paenetalauma, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the Yunnan magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to southwestern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves appear. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its bark is grayish-brown and becomes fissured with age.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
