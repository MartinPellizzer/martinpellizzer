Maxillaria briggittheae

Maxillaria briggittheae, commonly known as the Briggitthea orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs and linear, grass-like leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive yellow lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria briggittheae is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
