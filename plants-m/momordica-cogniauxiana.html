Momordica cogniauxiana

Momordica cogniauxiana, commonly known as the African bitter gourd, is a climbing vine native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Cucurbitaceae family, which includes other gourds and cucurbits. The plant produces distinctive, warty, elongated fruits that are typically green when mature. Its leaves are broad and lobed, and it has tendrils that aid in climbing.

The fruit is used in traditional medicine and as a vegetable in some African cuisines.
