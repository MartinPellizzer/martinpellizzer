Mentha capensis

Mentha capensis, commonly known as Cape mint, is a perennial herb native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its aromatic, serrated leaves and clusters of small, pale blue to purple flowers. The plant contains essential oils that contribute to its strong minty scent and medicinal properties. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mentha capensis is often used in traditional medicine for its purported calming and digestive benefits.
