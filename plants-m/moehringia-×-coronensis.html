Moehringia × coronensis

Moehringia × coronensis, commonly known as the silverweed, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Caryophyllaceae.

It is characterized by its low-growing habit, with dense clusters of small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. This plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate appearance.
