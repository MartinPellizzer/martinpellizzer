Mammillaria discolor var. nitens

Mammillaria discolor var. nitens, commonly known as the nitent white mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a spherical stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles, which are often adorned with short, white spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental appearance, it is popular among cactus enthusiasts and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
