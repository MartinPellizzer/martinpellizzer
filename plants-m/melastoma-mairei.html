Melastoma mairei

Melastoma mairei, commonly known as the purple mangosteen, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking violet or purple flowers that bloom in clusters, adding visual interest to gardens. The plant has dark green, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in tropical rainforests.

Melastoma mairei is valued not only for its ornamental beauty but also for its medicinal properties in traditional medicine.
