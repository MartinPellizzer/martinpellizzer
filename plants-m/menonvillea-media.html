Menonvillea media

Menonvillea media, commonly known as the silver spider plant, is a member of the Bromeliad family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its striking, silver-green foliage with a spider-like appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers that bloom in clusters, adding a delicate touch to its ornamental value. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Menonvillea media is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its low maintenance and aesthetic appeal.
