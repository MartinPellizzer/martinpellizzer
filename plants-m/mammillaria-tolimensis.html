Mammillaria tolimensis

Mammillaria tolimensis, commonly known as the Tolima Hedgehog Cactus, is a species of cactus native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded tubercles. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun conditions. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

Due to its unique appearance and habitat, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
