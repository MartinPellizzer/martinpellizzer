Maranta macilenta

Maranta macilenta, commonly known as the slender arrowroot, is a herbaceous plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and medicinal plants. The plant features arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often variegated with shades of green and silver. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically white or pale pink.

Maranta macilenta is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been used in traditional medicine for its potential health benefits.
