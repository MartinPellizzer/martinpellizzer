Medicago tuberculata var. chiotica

Medicago tuberculata var. chiotica, commonly known as the Chiot Trifoliate, is a perennial legume native to the eastern Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which are typically trifoliate with three oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellow flowers arranged in dense, clustered inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and its role in ecological restoration projects.
