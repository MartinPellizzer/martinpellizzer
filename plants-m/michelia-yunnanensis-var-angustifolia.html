Michelia yunnanensis var. angustifolia

Michelia yunnanensis var. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf Michelia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the Lauraceae family and is characterized by its elliptical, narrow leaves that are often aromatic. The plant produces fragrant, white flowers with yellow stamens, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This variant is valued for its ornamental qualities and is occasionally cultivated in gardens.
