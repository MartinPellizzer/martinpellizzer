Magnolia pseudokobus

Magnolia pseudokobus, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the broad-leaf magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.

The tree is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
