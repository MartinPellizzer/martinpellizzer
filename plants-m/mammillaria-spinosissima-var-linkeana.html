Mammillaria spinosissima var. linkeana

Mammillaria spinosissima var. linkeana, commonly known as the Linke's mammillaria, is a compact cactus with a globular shape and a grey-green to blueish hue.

It features prominent, sharp spines that are typically white to yellowish in color, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is native to Mexico and thrives in well-drained soil with bright sunlight.

Due to its unique morphology and ornamental value, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
