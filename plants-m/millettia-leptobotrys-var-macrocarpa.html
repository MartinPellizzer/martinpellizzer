Millettia leptobotrys var. macrocarpa

Millettia leptobotrys var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-fruited millettia, is a climbing legume native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features compound leaves with elongated, narrow leaflets and produces distinctive, showy flowers in clusters. The plant bears large, woody pods that contain several elliptical seeds. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in secondary forests.

Its botanical characteristics make it valuable for ecological studies and potential medicinal applications.
