Macroclinium chasei

Macroclinium chasei, commonly known as the Chase's macroclinium, is a flowering plant belonging to the Orchidaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that typically come in shades of yellow and orange. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts interested in rare orchids.
