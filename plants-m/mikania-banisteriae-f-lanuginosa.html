Mikania banisteriae f. lanuginosa

Mikania banisteriae f. lanuginosa, commonly known as the silky mikania, is a flowering plant belonging to the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is native to South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in tropical and subtropical regions. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Its rapid growth and spreading habit make it both a useful ornamental plant and a potential invasive species in certain areas.
