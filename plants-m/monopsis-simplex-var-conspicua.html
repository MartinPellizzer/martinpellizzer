Monopsis simplex var. conspicua

Monopsis simplex var. conspicua, commonly known as the showy monopsis, is a striking member of the Apiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, open habitats such as meadows and stream banks. Its leaves are pinnately divided, with a feathery appearance that adds to its ornamental value.
