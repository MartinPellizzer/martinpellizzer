Mandevilla longipes

Mandevilla longipes, commonly known as the longiped mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant features waxy, dark green leaves that are often glossy and elongated. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a climber or shrub.

Mandevilla longipes is popular in ornamental gardening for its vibrant blooms and adaptability to various growing conditions.
