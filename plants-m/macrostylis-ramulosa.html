Macrostylis ramulosa

Macrostylis ramulosa, commonly known as the slender-stemmed macrostylis, is a flowering plant native to certain regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Araceae, which includes many aquatic and semi-aquatic species. The plant is characterized by its slender, branching stems and small, clustered leaves. Its inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a spathe, typical of the Araceae family.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in wetlands or along the edges of ponds.
