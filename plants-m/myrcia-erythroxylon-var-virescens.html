Myrcia erythroxylon var. virescens

Myrcia erythroxylon var. virescens, commonly known as the green myrcia, is a species of flowering plant in the family Myrtaceae.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and small, clustered white or pale pink flowers. It typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, often found in humid, lowland forests.

The fruit is a small, fleshy berry that contains several seeds, and the plant is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
