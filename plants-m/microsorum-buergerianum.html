Microsorum buergerianum

Microsorum buergerianum, commonly known as the Buerger's sword fern, is a species of fern belonging to the family Polypodiaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, including China, Japan, and the Philippines. The plant features elongated, lance-shaped fronds that are often dark green and glossy. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-draining, humus-rich soil.

Microsorum buergerianum is valued in horticulture for its ornamental foliage and ease of care.
