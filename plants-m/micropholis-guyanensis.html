Micropholis guyanensis

Micropholis guyanensis, commonly known as the Guyana micropholis, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the family Myristicaceae and is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are usually elliptical in shape. Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is of interest to botanists and conservationists alike.
