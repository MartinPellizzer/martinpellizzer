Michelia yulan

Michelia yulan, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its fragrant, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Michelia yulan is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy blossoms.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
