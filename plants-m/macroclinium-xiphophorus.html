Macroclinium xiphophorus

Macroclinium xiphophorus, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, sword-shaped leaves. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and orange, often with a distinctive red lip. It thrives in humid, well-drained environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is popular in horticulture but is also threatened by habitat loss.
