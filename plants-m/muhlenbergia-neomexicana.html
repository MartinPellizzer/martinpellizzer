Muhlenbergia neomexicana

Muhlenbergia neomexicana, commonly known as the New Mexico muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southwestern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces delicate, feathery plumes during the flowering season. The plant thrives in well-drained soils and is drought-tolerant, making it well-suited for xeriscaping. Its foliage is typically blue-green in color, adding a striking visual contrast to the landscape.

This species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological benefits, supporting local wildlife and improving soil stability.
