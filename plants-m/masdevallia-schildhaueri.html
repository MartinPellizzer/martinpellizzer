Masdevallia schildhaueri

Masdevallia schildhaueri, commonly known as the Schildhauer's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration pattern. The plant typically features dark green, leathery leaves and produces blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high light exposure, making it a popular choice for intermediate orchid growers.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is often cultivated in controlled greenhouse conditions.
