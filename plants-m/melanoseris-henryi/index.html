Melanoseris Henryi

Melanoseris henryi is a rare and intriguing plant native to the mountainous regions of western China. It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which includes daisies and sunflowers, yet it possesses unique characteristics that set it apart. Its name, derived from Greek, reflects its dark-colored leaves and its association with the botanist Henry. This plant thrives in high-altitude environments, where it adapts to harsh conditions with specialized structures. Due to its limited distribution and ecological significance, Melanoseris henryi is a subject of growing scientific interest and conservation efforts.
