Mirabellia

Mirabellia, commonly known as the sweet cherry tree, is a deciduous tree native to Europe and parts of Asia.

It produces small, round fruits that are typically pink to red in color when ripe. The plant has alternate, simple, and ovate leaves with serrated edges. It blooms in the spring with clusters of white to pale pink flowers.

The fruit is rich in vitamins and is widely cultivated for its culinary and ornamental value.
