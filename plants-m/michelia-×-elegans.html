Michelia × elegans

Michelia × elegans, commonly known as the fragrant Michelia, is a hybrid plant belonging to the family Magnoliaceae.

It is characterized by its showy, fragrant white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and elliptical, with a glossy dark green upper surface.

Native to China, it is valued in horticulture for its ornamental beauty and pleasant aroma.
