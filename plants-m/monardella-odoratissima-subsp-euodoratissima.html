Monardella odoratissima subsp. euodoratissima

Monardella odoratissima subsp.

euodoratissima, commonly known as the sweet peppergrass, is a flowering plant native to California. It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes many aromatic herbs.

The plant features slender, upright stems and lance-shaped leaves with a slight pubescence. Its flowers are typically pink to purple, arranged in whorls along the stem, and emit a strong, sweet scent.

It thrives in dry, sunny habitats and is often found in open woodlands and grasslands.
