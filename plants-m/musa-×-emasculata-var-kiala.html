Musa × emasculata var. kiala

Musa × emasculata var. kiala, commonly known as the Kiala banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, elongated, and often curved banana-like fruits, which are typically harvested when they are still green. The plant is known for its vigorous growth and ability to produce multiple harvests throughout the year. Its leaves are broad, heart-shaped, and have a glossy green appearance.

The fruit is valued for its unique flavor and is often used in both culinary and ornamental contexts.
