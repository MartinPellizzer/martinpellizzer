Mabea taquari

Mabea taquari, commonly known as the Brazilian star apple, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a small tree or shrub that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a round, yellowish or reddish berry that contains a single large seed.

The leaves are simple, alternate, and have a slightly serrated margin.
