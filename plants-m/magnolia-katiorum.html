Magnolia katiorum

Magnolia katiorum, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.

The bark is smooth and grayish-brown, becoming fissured with age.
