Mesembryanthemum bellum

Mesembryanthemum bellum, commonly known as the "Ice Plant," is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, succulent leaves and showy, daisy-like flowers that bloom in shades of pink, yellow, and white. The plant is adapted to arid environments, with a shallow root system that allows it to absorb moisture quickly. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves helps it survive in dry conditions.
