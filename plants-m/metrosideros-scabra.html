Metrosideros scabra

Metrosideros scabra, commonly known as the rough bark tree or kōwhai kākāriki, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to New Zealand and is characterized by its rough, fissured bark and small, urn-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows as a small tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and have a rough texture, while its flowers are usually pale yellow or red.

The rough bark tree is often found in coastal and lowland areas, and it plays an important ecological role in its native habitat.
