Mesembryanthemum pellanum

Mesembryanthemum pellanum, commonly known as the African ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, blue-green leaves that are covered in a fine, white powdery coating, which helps reduce water loss. The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in summer, adding a bright touch to its appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves makes it drought-tolerant and suitable for arid environments.
