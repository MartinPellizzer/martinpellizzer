Mangifera indica var. teloor

Mangifera indica var. teloor, commonly known as the Teloor mango, is a variety of the mango tree native to India.

It is characterized by its large, elongated fruits with a sweet and aromatic flavor. The tree produces dark green, leathery leaves and is typically found in tropical and subtropical climates. The Teloor mango is valued for its high juice content and is often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications.

Botanically, it belongs to the Anacardiaceae family and is closely related to other mango varieties.
