Mallotus cavaleriei

Mallotus cavaleriei, commonly known as the Chinese holly or Chinese holly tree, is a deciduous shrub or small tree native to China.

It is characterized by its dense, spiny foliage and small, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on top and lighter underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by red or orange berries that are an important food source for birds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.

Mallotus cavaleriei is also valued in traditional Chinese medicine for its purported health benefits.
