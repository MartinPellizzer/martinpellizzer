Maxillaria auyantepuiensis

Maxillaria auyantepuiensis, commonly known as the Angel's Wings orchid, is a species native to the tepuis of Venezuela and Guyana.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or shaded environments, showcasing its adaptability to challenging habitats. Its flowers are usually borne on tall, slender stems and can exhibit a range of hues, including pink, purple, and white.

This species is noted for its ornamental value and is appreciated by horticulturists for its unique appearance.
