Muhlenbergia wallisii

Muhlenbergia wallisii, commonly known as Wallis's muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and produces small, fluffy seed heads that are often pinkish or reddish in color. The plant typically thrives in full sun to partial shade and is drought-tolerant once established. It is often used in native plant gardens for its ornamental value and ecological benefits.

Muhlenbergia wallisii is also valued for its ability to stabilize soil and support local wildlife.
