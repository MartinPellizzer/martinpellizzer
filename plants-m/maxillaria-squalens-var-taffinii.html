Maxillaria squalens var. taffinii

Maxillaria squalens var. taffinii, commonly known as the Taffinii Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and dark green, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces fragrant, white to pale pink flowers with a distinctive, velvety appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
