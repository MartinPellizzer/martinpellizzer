Mesua philippinensis

Mesua philippinensis, commonly known as the Philippine flame tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its striking red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, often resembling flames. The plant has elliptic to ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy on top, with a lighter underside. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in lowland forests.

The tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is also used in traditional medicine.
