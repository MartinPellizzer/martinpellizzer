Mimosa brevispica

Mimosa brevispica, commonly known as the short-stemmed sensitive plant, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family.

It is native to South America and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves that fold shut when touched. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers in clusters, which bloom in the spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Mimosa brevispica is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its unique leaf movement and attractive appearance.
