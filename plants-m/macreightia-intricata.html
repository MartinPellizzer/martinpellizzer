Macreightia intricata

Macreightia intricata, commonly known as the intricate macreightia, is a rare flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, spiral-shaped leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained, nutrient-rich soil. Its unique growth pattern and ornamental value make it a sought-after species among botanists and horticulturists.
