Melicope vitiensis var. minor

Melicope vitiensis var. minor, commonly known as the Vitiensis Melicope, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Fiji.

It belongs to the Rutaceae family, which includes citrus and other aromatic plants. The plant is characterized by its simple, opposite leaves that are usually oval to elliptic in shape. It produces small, white flowers followed by small, round fruits that are typically green when mature.
