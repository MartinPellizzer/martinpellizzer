Miconia igniaria

Miconia igniaria, commonly known as the fiery miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, dark green leaves and striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to purple. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

Due to its invasive potential, it is sometimes considered a weed in certain regions.
