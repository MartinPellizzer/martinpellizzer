Merremia nervosa

Merremia nervosa, commonly known as the slender antelope horn.

It is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of the Americas, characterized by its slender, serpentine stems and distinctive horn-like flowers. The plant produces yellowish-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. Its leaves are typically heart-shaped with a rough texture and prominent veins.

Merremia nervosa is often found in moist, shaded environments and is valued for its ornamental appeal in horticulture.
