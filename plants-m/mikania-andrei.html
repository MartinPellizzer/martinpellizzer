Mikania andrei

Mikania andrei, commonly known as the creeping mikania, is a flowering plant native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, daisy-like flowers. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and produces yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. It is often found in moist, disturbed areas such as roadsides and forest edges.

Mikania andrei is known for its rapid growth and ability to spread through both seeds and rhizomes.
