Millettia leonensis

Millettia leonensis, commonly known as the Leon's Millettia, is a species of flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers. The plant produces pods that contain several seeds, which are often dispersed by wind. Millettia leonensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its foliage.

It thrives in well-drained soil and requires moderate to high levels of sunlight.
