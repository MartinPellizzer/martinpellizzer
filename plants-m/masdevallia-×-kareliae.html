Masdevallia × kareliae

Masdevallia × kareliae, commonly known as the Karelia orchid, is a hybrid orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, upright growth habit and its striking, often colorful flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring. The plant typically features lance-shaped leaves and produces intricate, spurred flowers with a variety of hues, including red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires high humidity and well-draining potting media.

Due to its ornamental value, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively easy care requirements.
