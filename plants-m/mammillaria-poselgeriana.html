Mammillaria poselgeriana

Mammillaria poselgeriana, commonly known as the Poselger's nipple cactus, is a species native to the arid regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are pale yellow and bloom in late spring or early summer.

It is well adapted to dry environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
