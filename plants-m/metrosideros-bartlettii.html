Metrosideros bartlettii

Metrosideros bartlettii, commonly known as the Bartlett's Christmas Bush, is a species of flowering plant in the myrtle family.

It is native to the Hawaiian Islands and is characterized by its bright red flowers that bloom during the holiday season.
