Melochia hirsuta var. glabrata

Melochia hirsuta var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth melochia, is a variant of the melochia species native to tropical regions.

It is a shrub or small tree characterized by its smooth, often glabrous bark and alternate, ovate leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in inflorescences, followed by round, fleshy berries. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats.

Its botanical features make it a subject of interest in both horticulture and ecological studies.
