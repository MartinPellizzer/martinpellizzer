Molium roseum

Molium roseum, commonly known as the pink molium, is a flowering plant native to southern Europe.

It is characterized by its delicate, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters and has slender, hairy stems. Its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped, often with a slight curl at the edges.

Molium roseum prefers well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.
