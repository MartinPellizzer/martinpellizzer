Mildella straminea

Mildella straminea, commonly known as the pale milkweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its slender, branched stems and small, pale green to yellowish flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny, wind-dispersed seeds. Mildella straminea is often found in open, disturbed habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.

It plays a role in supporting local pollinators and is of interest in ecological studies due to its adaptability.
