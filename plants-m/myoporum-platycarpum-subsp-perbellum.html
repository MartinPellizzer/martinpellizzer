Myoporum platycarpum subsp. perbellum

Myoporum platycarpum subsp.

perbellum, commonly known as the silver myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand. It features silver-gray foliage with a dense, compact growth habit.

The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that are clustered in terminal spikes. Its leaves are opposite and have a waxy coating, which helps reduce water loss in arid conditions.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and hardy nature.
