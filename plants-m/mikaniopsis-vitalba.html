Mikaniopsis vitalba

Mikaniopsis vitalba, commonly known as the common cat's tail, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features a creeping rhizomatous stem that allows it to spread rapidly across the ground. The plant produces small, inconspicuous greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters at the ends of the stems. Its leaves are typically lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Mikaniopsis vitalba is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of environmental conditions.
