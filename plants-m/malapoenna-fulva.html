Malapoenna fulva

Malapoenna fulva, commonly known as the yellow flame tree, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking yellow-orange flowers that bloom in clusters during the warmer months. The plant has compound leaves that are pinnately arranged, and its bark is smooth with a reddish-brown color. Malapoenna fulva is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its vibrant floral display.

It thrives in full sun and well-drained soil, making it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes.
