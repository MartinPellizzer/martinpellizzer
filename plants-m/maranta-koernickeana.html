Maranta koernickeana

Maranta koernickeana, commonly known as the velvetleaf arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family and is characterized by its striking, often variegated foliage. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically green or yellow in color. Its rhizomes are edible and have been used in traditional cuisines.

The species is valued for both its ornamental beauty and its potential agricultural uses.
