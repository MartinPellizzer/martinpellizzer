Melothria fluminensis var. ovata

Melothria fluminensis var. ovata, commonly known as the Brazilian sour cucumber, is a tropical vine native to South America.

It produces small, edible fruits that are typically green and have a tangy, acidic flavor. The plant has heart-shaped leaves and thrives in warm, humid climates. It is often cultivated for its unique taste and ornamental value.

Its botanical features make it a distinctive addition to both gardens and culinary traditions.
