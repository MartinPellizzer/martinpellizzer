Mimosa lithoreas

Mimosa lithoreas, commonly known as the coastal mimosa, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy, yellow flower heads that open in response to touch. The plant thrives in sandy, coastal environments and is adapted to withstand salt spray and strong winds. Its flowers bloom in spring and attract various pollinators, including bees and butterflies.

Mimosa lithoreas is often used in coastal landscaping due to its hardiness and aesthetic appeal.
