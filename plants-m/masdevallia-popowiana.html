Masdevallia popowiana

Masdevallia popowiana, commonly known as the Popowian orchid, is a species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in shaded, humid environments at high altitudes. The plant features a sympodial growth habit, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its flowers are usually small and showy, often displaying a range of colors including pink, red, and white.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
