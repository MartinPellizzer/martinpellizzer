Microcephalum ehrenbergianum

Microcephalum ehrenbergianum, commonly known as the dwarf snowbell, is a small shrub native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It belongs to the Oleaceae family and is characterized by its delicate, bell-shaped white flowers that bloom in early spring. Microcephalum ehrenbergianum is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance once established.
