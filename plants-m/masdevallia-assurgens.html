Masdevallia assurgens

Masdevallia assurgens, commonly known as the pink lady's slipper orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its unique, intricate flower structure with a prominent, pouch-like lip that resembles a slipper. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires high air humidity and indirect light to thrive. Its flowers, which range in color from pink to white, are highly sought after for their beauty and rarity.

Masdevallia assurgens is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts due to its striking appearance and challenging growing requirements.
