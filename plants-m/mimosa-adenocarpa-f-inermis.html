Mimosa adenocarpa f. inermis

Mimosa adenocarpa f. inermis, commonly known as the soft mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, causing them to fold when disturbed. The plant produces fluffy, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

Its delicate appearance and unique foliage make it a distinctive addition to gardens and landscapes.
