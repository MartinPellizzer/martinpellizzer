Matthiola chenopodiifolia var. porphyrantha

Matthiola chenopodiifolia var. porphyrantha, commonly known as the purple-flowered stock, is a perennial plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and clusters of fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to deep purple. The leaves are typically narrow and slightly hairy, with a bluish-green hue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
