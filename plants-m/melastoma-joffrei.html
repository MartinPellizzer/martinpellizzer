Melastoma joffrei

Melastoma joffrei, commonly known as the Joffre's melastoma, is a shrub native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest undergrowth. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals and a distinctive calyx.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
