Mosiera prismatica

Mosiera prismatica, commonly known as the prismatic mosiera, is a flowering plant native to the island of Socotra.

It is characterized by its distinctive, prism-like stems and small, clustered leaves. The plant produces bright yellow flowers that bloom in the spring, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies. It thrives in arid and semi-arid environments, adapting to the harsh climate of its native habitat.

Due to its unique morphology and limited distribution, it is considered a rare and ecologically significant species.
