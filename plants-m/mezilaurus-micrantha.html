Mezilaurus micrantha

Mezilaurus micrantha, commonly known as the African violet, is a flowering plant native to eastern Africa.

It is characterized by its compact, bushy growth habit and vibrant, showy flowers that come in a variety of colors. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often velvety in texture. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Mezilaurus micrantha is a popular ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
