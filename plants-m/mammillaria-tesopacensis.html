Mammillaria tesopacensis

Mammillaria tesopacensis, commonly known as the Tesopacensis Hedgehog Cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, tuberculate spines. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a pale green to bluish-green stem. It produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are followed by red or orange fruits.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
