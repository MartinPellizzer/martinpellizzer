Melastoma malabathricum var. nanum

Melastoma malabathricum var. nanum, commonly known as the dwarf melastoma, is a compact shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It features small, oval-shaped leaves that are typically glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in spring and summer. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, black seeds.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
