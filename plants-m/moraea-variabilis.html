Moraea variabilis

Moraea variabilis, commonly known as the variable moraea, is a species of flowering plant in the Iridaceae family.

It is native to South Africa, where it typically grows in grassy areas and rocky habitats. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow, often with contrasting dark veins. It is a perennial herb that thrives in full sun and well-drained soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
