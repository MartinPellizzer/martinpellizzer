Masdevallia brevis

Masdevallia brevis, commonly known as the short-stemmed masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and relatively small, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant features a pseudobulb structure, which helps it store water and nutrients in its native high-altitude, cool climate. Its leaves are usually narrow and grass-like, adapted to the humid, shaded environments where it thrives.

Masdevallia brevis is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
