Mikania banisteriae var. gabrielii

Mikania banisteriae var. gabrielii, commonly known as Gabriel's Mikania, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and distinctive leaves that are usually heart-shaped or ovate. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, spherical fruits.

It is often found in moist, shaded areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
