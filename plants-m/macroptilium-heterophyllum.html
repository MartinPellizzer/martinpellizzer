Macroptilium heterophyllum

Macroptilium heterophyllum, commonly known as the small cowpea, is a leguminous plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant produces pods containing edible seeds, which are used in various culinary applications. It has a fibrous root system that helps in nitrogen fixation, improving soil fertility.

Its adaptability to diverse climates makes it a valuable crop in agricultural systems.
