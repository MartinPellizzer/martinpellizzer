Mentha pulegium var. gibraltarica

Mentha pulegium var. gibraltarica, commonly known as Gibraltar pennyroyal, is a variant of the pennyroyal plant native to the Iberian Peninsula.

It is a low-growing perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, slightly menthol-like leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in rocky or woodland areas.

Due to its strong scent and medicinal properties, it has been historically used in traditional herbal remedies.
