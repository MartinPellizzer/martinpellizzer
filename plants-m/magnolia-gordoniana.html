Magnolia gordoniana

Magnolia gordoniana, commonly known as the Gordon magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, white to pale pink flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are simple, alternate, and ovate with a serrated margin. The tree produces small, round, green fruits that contain winged seeds.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and parks.
