Melocalamus cucphuongensis

Melocalamus cucphuongensis, commonly known as the Cuc Phuong bamboo, is a species of bamboo native to Vietnam.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect culms that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant has narrow, linear leaves that are typically a bright green color, and it produces small, inconspicuous flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.

This bamboo is valued for its ecological role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.
