Melissa asiatica

Melissa asiatica, commonly known as Asian lemon balm, is a perennial herb native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Lamiaceae family, which includes other aromatic plants like mint and lavender. The plant is characterized by its fragrant, glossy leaves and small, pale yellow flowers. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full sun.

Melissa asiatica is often used in traditional medicine for its calming and digestive properties.
