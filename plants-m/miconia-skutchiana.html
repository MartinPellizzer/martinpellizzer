Miconia skutchiana

Miconia skutchiana, commonly known as the Skutch's miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central America.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, glossy, ovate leaves. The plant produces showy, pinkish-white flowers with five petals, which are typically clustered in inflorescences. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in the understory of tropical rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and greenhouses.
