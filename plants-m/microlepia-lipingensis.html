Microlepia lipingensis

Microlepia lipingensis, commonly known as the Liping sword fern, is a species of fern native to China.

It belongs to the family Dryopteridaceae and is characterized by its distinctive, lance-shaped fronds. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forest understories. Its rhizomes are creeping and produce clusters of small, oval-shaped sporangia.
