Manihot gracilis var. pronifolia

Manihot gracilis var. pronifolia, commonly known as the slender arrowleaf manioc, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It features slender, lance-shaped leaves that are often arranged alternately along the stem. The plant produces small, greenish flowers that develop into capsule fruits containing numerous seeds. It is adapted to moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its botanical characteristics make it a subject of interest in both ecological and agricultural studies.
