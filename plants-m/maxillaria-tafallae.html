Maxillaria tafallae

Maxillaria tafallae, commonly known as the Tafallae orchid, is a species of orchid native to Ecuador.

It is a terrestrial orchid that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features a pseudobulb with a single leaf and produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of white and pink. Its flowers are characterized by a unique lip that is often adorned with intricate patterns.

This orchid is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated by enthusiasts for its beauty.
