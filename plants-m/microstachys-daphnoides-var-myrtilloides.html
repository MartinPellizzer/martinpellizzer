Microstachys daphnoides var. myrtilloides

Microstachys daphnoides var. myrtilloides, commonly known as the purple stemmed honeysuckle, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, woody stems that often display a distinctive purplish hue. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, arranged in clusters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a slightly hairy texture.

It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.
