Mesembryanthemum pallens subsp. luteum

Mesembryanthemum pallens subsp.

luteum, commonly known as the yellow carpetweed, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa. It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are typically green with a bluish tinge.

The plant produces small, yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the cooler months. It is well adapted to arid environments and can thrive in full sun with minimal water.

Its compact growth habit makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
