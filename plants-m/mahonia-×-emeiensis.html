Mahonia × emeiensis

Mahonia × emeiensis, commonly known as the Emei mahonia, is a hybrid evergreen shrub native to China.

It is characterized by its compound, spiny leaves that are dark green and glossy on top with a lighter underside. The plant produces clusters of yellow flowers in early spring, which are followed by blue-black berries in the fall. It thrives in partial to full shade and is often used in woodland gardens for its ornamental value.

Mahonia × emeiensis is relatively low maintenance and drought-tolerant once established.
