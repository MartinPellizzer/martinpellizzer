Maxillaria sotoana

Maxillaria sotoana, commonly known as the Soto's orchid, is a species of orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and blooms with white or pale pink petals that often have a contrasting purple or red lip. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Maxillaria sotoana is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
