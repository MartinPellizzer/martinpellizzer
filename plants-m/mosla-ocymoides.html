Mosla ocymoides

Mosla ocymoides, commonly known as Chinese burnet, is a perennial herb native to eastern Asia.

It features upright, branched stems covered with fine, hairy foliage. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom in summer. Its leaves are aromatic and often used in traditional medicine.

It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in woodland or rocky habitats.
