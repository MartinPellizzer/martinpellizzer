Mozartia emarginata

Mozartia emarginata, commonly known as the zigzag grass, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Poaceae family and is characterized by its distinctive zigzag arrangement of leaves. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny white or pale blue florets. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
