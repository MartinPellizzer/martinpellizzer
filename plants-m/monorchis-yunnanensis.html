Monorchis yunnanensis

Monorchis yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan orchid, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Yunnan Province in China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its elegant, lance-shaped leaves and delicate, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments at elevations ranging from 1,500 to 3,000 meters. Its flowers, which can be white, yellow, or green, often feature intricate patterns and are pollinated by specific insect species.

Monorchis yunnanensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and private collections.
