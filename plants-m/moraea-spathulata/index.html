Moraea Spathulata

Spathulate moraea, scientifically known as Moraea spathulata, is a striking perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of southern Africa. This plant is celebrated for its elegant, spatulate-shaped leaves and its clusters of vibrant, tubular flowers that bloom in late summer. Often found in rocky or sandy soils, it thrives in well-drained environments and requires minimal water once established. Its unique morphology and ornamental value make it a popular choice for rock gardens and botanical collections. Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, conservation efforts are essential to ensure its survival in the wild.
