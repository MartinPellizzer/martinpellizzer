Moltkia petraea

Moltkia petraea, commonly known as the rock senna, is a perennial shrub native to the Mediterranean region.

It features small, yellow, pea-like flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has compound leaves with oval-shaped leaflets and is adapted to grow in rocky, well-drained soils. It is often found in dry, sunny habitats such as cliffs and slopes.

Moltkia petraea is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in harsh environments.
