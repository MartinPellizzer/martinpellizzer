Mandevilla steyermarkii

Mandevilla steyermarkii, commonly known as the yellow mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow flowers and trailing growth habit, making it popular in ornamental gardening. The plant produces tubular, trumpet-shaped flowers that bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are typically ovate and have a glossy, dark green appearance.

Mandevilla steyermarkii thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a houseplant or in outdoor gardens.
