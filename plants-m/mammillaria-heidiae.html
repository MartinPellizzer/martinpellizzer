Mammillaria heidiae

Mammillaria heidiae, commonly known as the Heidii's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and bright yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. It is relatively easy to care for, requiring minimal water and moderate temperatures.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
