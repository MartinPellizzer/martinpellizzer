Maxillaria yanganensis

Maxillaria yanganensis, commonly known as the Yangan orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to China.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its sympodial growth habit. The plant produces pseudobulbs that bear alternate, lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are typically pale green to yellowish with a contrasting purple or red lip.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas.
