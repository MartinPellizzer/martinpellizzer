Mentha × rotundifolia var. maderensis

Mentha × rotundifolia var. maderensis, commonly known as the Mader's round-leaf mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its rounded, somewhat succulent leaves that are often a deep green color. The plant produces small, pale blue or purple flowers arranged in whorled clusters, typical of the mint genus. It is native to the Iberian Peninsula and thrives in moist, shaded environments.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in herbal teas and traditional medicine.
