Mammillaria freudenbergeri

Mammillaria freudenbergeri, commonly known as the Freudenberger's mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its rounded, ribbed stems that are typically green with a slight bluish tint. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
