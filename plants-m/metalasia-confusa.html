Metalasia confusa

Metalasia confusa, commonly known as the African daisy, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its daisy-like flowers with white ray florets and yellow disk florets. The plant typically grows in open grasslands and has a bushy, compact growth habit. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often covered in fine hairs.

Metalasia confusa is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in garden settings for its vibrant blooms.
