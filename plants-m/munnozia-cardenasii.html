Munnozia cardenasii

Munnozia cardenasii, commonly known as the Cienega daisy, is a flowering plant native to the Sonoran Desert.

It is characterized by its low-growing, mat-forming structure with narrow, blue-green leaves. The plant produces small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.

Munnozia cardenasii is often found in washes and rocky areas, where it plays a role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for local wildlife.
