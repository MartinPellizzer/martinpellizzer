Melastoma nemorosum

Melastoma nemorosum, commonly known as the tropical wild cherry, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves with a rough texture and emits a sweet, fragrant odor. Its flowers are usually pink or white, with five petals, and appear in clusters.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
