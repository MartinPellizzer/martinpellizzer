Markea coccinea

Markea coccinea, commonly known as the scarlet markea, is a striking flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red petals and broad, waxy leaves that give it a tropical appearance. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub, reaching heights of up to 2 meters. Its flowers bloom in clusters, attracting pollinators such as bees and butterflies.

Markea coccinea thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
