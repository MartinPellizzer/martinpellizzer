Metrosideros collina f. sericea

Metrosideros collina f. sericea, commonly known as the silver-leafed flame tree, is a variant of the flame tree species native to New Zealand.

It is characterized by its distinctive silvery-white undersides on the leaves, which give it a striking appearance. The plant produces vibrant orange-red flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators such as birds and insects. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight, making it suitable for coastal and garden environments.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental qualities and is often used in landscaping due to its hardy nature and visual appeal.
