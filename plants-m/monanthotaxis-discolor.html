Monanthotaxis discolor

Monanthotaxis discolor, commonly known as the variable monanthotaxis, is a rare and distinctive plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique, spirally arranged leaves that are often variegated with white and green patterns. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale yellow, which bloom in the early morning. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release tiny, wind-dispersed seeds.
