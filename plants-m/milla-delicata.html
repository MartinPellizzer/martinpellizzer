Milla delicata

Milla delicata, commonly known as the delicate milla, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of South America.

It is characterized by its small, ornamental flowers that bloom in shades of white and pink. The plant has narrow, grass-like leaves and thrives in well-drained soil with ample sunlight. It is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal and fragrance.

Due to its specific habitat requirements, it is considered a rare and endangered species in the wild.
