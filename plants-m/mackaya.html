Mackaya

Mackaya, commonly known as the golden trumpet tree, is a flowering plant belonging to the Bignoniaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of the Americas and is characterized by its striking yellow-orange tubular flowers. The plant typically grows as a tree or shrub, reaching heights of up to 10 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and often have a rough texture.
