Microstylis crispifolia

Microstylis crispifolia, commonly known as the crinkled leaf microstylis, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant produces tiny, white or pale yellow flowers clustered in dense inflorescences, which bloom during the late spring to early summer. It thrives in shaded, moist environments, often found in forest understories where it competes with other shade-tolerant flora.
