Mikania discolor

Mikania discolor, commonly known as the creeping morning glory, is a vigorous vine native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant is known for its rapid growth and ability to climb or trail along the ground. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed areas.

Mikania discolor is also noted for its medicinal properties in traditional herbal medicine.
