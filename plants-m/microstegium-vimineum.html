Microstegium vimineum

Microstegium vimineum, commonly known as little bluestem, is a perennial grass native to eastern North America.

It is characterized by its fine, blue-green foliage and produces small, spike-like inflorescences that appear in late summer. The plant thrives in moist to wet soils and is often found in wetlands, swamps, and along stream banks. It plays an important role in stabilizing soil and providing habitat for various wildlife species.

Microstegium vimineum is also valued for its ornamental qualities and is used in ecological restoration projects.
