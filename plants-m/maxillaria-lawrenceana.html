Maxillaria lawrenceana

Maxillaria lawrenceana, commonly known as the Lawrence's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, fragrant flowers. The plant typically grows as a pseudobulbous orchid with lance-shaped leaves and produces clusters of dark violet or purple flowers. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Maxillaria lawrenceana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
