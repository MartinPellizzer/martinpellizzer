Mammillaria odieriana

Mammillaria odieriana, commonly known as the Odier's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem covered in white, woolly hairs that help protect it from intense sunlight and moisture loss. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts.
