Maxillaria ponerantha

Maxillaria ponerantha, commonly known as the Orchid of the Amazon, is a terrestrial orchid native to the rainforests of South America.

It is characterized by its sympodial growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features long, narrow leaves and has a complex inflorescence with multiple floral spikes. Its flowers are often yellow or orange with purple or red markings, making it a striking ornamental species.

Maxillaria ponerantha thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
