Margaretta cornetii var. pallida

Margaretta cornetii var. pallida, commonly known as the pale margaret.

It is a flowering plant native to the eastern United States, particularly found in moist woodland habitats. The plant features delicate, pale pink to white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are simple, ovate, and often have a slightly serrated edge.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
