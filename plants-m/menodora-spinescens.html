Menodora spinescens

Menodora spinescens, commonly known as the spiny menodora, is a flowering plant native to parts of South America.

It is characterized by its spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves, which help it thrive in its natural habitat. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters, typically during the warmer months. Its fruits are capsule-like structures that release seeds when mature.

Menodora spinescens is often found in dry, rocky environments and is adapted to withstand arid conditions.
