Matelea jenmanii

Matelea jenmanii, commonly known as the Mexican moonflower, is a flowering plant belonging to the Apocynaceae family.

It is native to Mexico and is characterized by its large, showy, white flowers that open at night. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Matelea jenmanii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and nocturnal blooming habit.
