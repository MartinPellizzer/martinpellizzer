Markea grandiflora

Markea grandiflora, commonly known as the large-flowered markea, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep pink. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, simple, and have a glossy, dark green surface.

Markea grandiflora is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates.
