Mesosphaerum crenatum var. albiflorum

Mesosphaerum crenatum var. albiflorum, commonly known as the white-flowered mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its glossy, elliptical leaves and clusters of small, white flowers that bloom in spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. It has a compact growth habit and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and delicate blooms.

The species is valued for its aesthetic appeal and is relatively easy to care for in suitable conditions.
