Mansoa minensis

Mansoa minensis, commonly known as the small mansoa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that are typically pale yellow or white. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
