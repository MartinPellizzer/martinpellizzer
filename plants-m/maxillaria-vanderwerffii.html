Maxillaria vanderwerffii

Maxillaria vanderwerffii, commonly known as the Vanderwerff's maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Costa Rica.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves that are covered in a dense layer of fine, white pubescence. Its flowers are typically pink or purple, with a distinctive, spurred lip that is often a different color.

Maxillaria vanderwerffii blooms in the spring and is appreciated for its ornamental value in horticulture.
