Maxillaria mariaisabeliae

Maxillaria mariaisabeliae, commonly known as the Mariaisabel Orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that support its foliage and flowering structure. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with a distinct yellow lip, and it blooms during the summer months.

This species is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in greenhouses or as a houseplant.
