Mediocactus hahnianus

Mediocactus hahnianus, commonly known as the Hahn's cactus, is a species native to the deserts of South America.

It is a columnar cactus with a smooth, ribbed stem that can grow up to several meters in height. The plant produces vibrant yellow flowers during the warmer months, which are clustered at the top of the stem. It has a shallow root system adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water.

The plant is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
