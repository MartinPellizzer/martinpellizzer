Myrtus friedrichsthalii var. brevipes

Myrtus friedrichsthalii var. brevipes, commonly known as the dwarf myrtle, is a compact shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features small, dark green leaves that are aromatic and covered in a fine, white powdery bloom. The plant produces tiny, clustered white flowers that give way to small, red berries in the fall. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or container planting.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its fragrant foliage and evergreen nature.
