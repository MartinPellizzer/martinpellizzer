Mila nealeana f. senilis

Mila nealeana f. senilis, commonly known as the silver-leafed fern, is a unique variant of the Mila nealeana species.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which gives it a striking appearance in both natural and cultivated settings. The plant is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. Its fronds are typically long and narrow, with a delicate, feathery texture.

This fern is often valued for its ornamental qualities and is cultivated for use in landscaping and indoor gardening.
