Maxillaria dalstromii

Maxillaria dalstromii, commonly known as the Dalstrom's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple. The plant has a sympodial growth pattern, with pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its leaves are usually elliptical and arranged in a basal rosette.

Maxillaria dalstromii thrives in warm, humid environments with bright, indirect light.
