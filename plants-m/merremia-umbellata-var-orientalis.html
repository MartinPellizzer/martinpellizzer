Merremia umbellata var. orientalis

Merremia umbellata var. orientalis, commonly known as the oriental spiderflower, is a climbing vine native to tropical regions of Asia.

It features heart-shaped, velvety leaves that are often tinged with purple or reddish hues. The plant produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that are typically yellow or orange with a distinctive purple throat. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variant is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens and as a houseplant.
