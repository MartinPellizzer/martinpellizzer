Mycetia longiflora f. howii

Mycetia longiflora f. howii, commonly known as the Howii's mycetia, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, climbing stems and compound leaves with small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically pale yellow to greenish in color. It is native to parts of Southeast Asia and thrives in tropical and subtropical climates.

Due to its ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
