Muhlenbergia gracilis

Muhlenbergia gracilis, commonly known as slender muhlenbergia, is a grass species native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, grass-like leaves and slender, arching stems that often form dense clumps. This plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. Its delicate appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

Muhlenbergia gracilis is also valued for its ecological role, providing habitat and food for various insects and birds.
