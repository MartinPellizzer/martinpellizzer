Maxillaria cogniauxiana var. longifolia

Maxillaria cogniauxiana var. longifolia, commonly known as the long-flowered cinnamon orchid, is a species native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It is a terrestrial orchid with pseudobulbs that support its growth and storage of nutrients. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, cinnamon-scented flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a velvety texture. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it thrives in humid, shaded environments.

This variety is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
