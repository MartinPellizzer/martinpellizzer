Megapterium fremontii

Megapterium fremontii, commonly known as the Fremont's giant ragweed, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant can grow up to 6 feet tall and is often found in disturbed soils along roadsides and in agricultural fields. It has a robust root system that allows it to thrive in dry conditions.

Its seeds are dispersed by wind, contributing to its invasive potential in certain regions.
