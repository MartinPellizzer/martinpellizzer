Megalastrum insigne

Megalastrum insigne, commonly known as the noble flamebush, is a shrub native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that range in color from red to orange, often resembling flames. The plant produces small, elliptical leaves that are arranged alternately along its stems. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.

Megalastrum insigne is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and landscapes.
