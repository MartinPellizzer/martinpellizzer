Medinilla gracilis

Medinilla gracilis, commonly known as the slender medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its fragrant, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and are often clustered in inflorescences. The plant has glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically arranged in whorls around the stems. It thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Medinilla gracilis is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and pleasant fragrance.
