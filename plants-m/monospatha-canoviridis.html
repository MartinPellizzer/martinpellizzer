Monospatha canoviridis

Monospatha canoviridis, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage and delicate, fragrant blooms. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments and prefers well-drained soil. Its flowers, which can range in color from white to pale yellow, are often clustered in dense inflorescences.

Monospatha canoviridis is valued both for its ornamental beauty and its ecological role in supporting pollinators.
