Microseris insignis

Microseris insignis, commonly known as the slender globemallow, is a herbaceous perennial native to the western United States.

It features a basal rosette of narrow, gray-green leaves and produces showy yellow flowers with a distinctive globular shape. The plant thrives in dry, rocky habitats and is adapted to full sun exposure. Its small, capsule-like fruits contain numerous seeds that are dispersed by wind.

Microseris insignis is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
