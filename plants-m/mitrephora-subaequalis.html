Mitrephora subaequalis

Mitrephora subaequalis, commonly known as the slender mitrephora, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew and mango. The plant is characterized by its simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. It produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by edible, fleshy fruits.
