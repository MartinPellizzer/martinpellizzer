Mentha × nemorosa var. morrenii

Mentha × nemorosa var. morrenii, commonly known as the Morren's wood mint, is a hybrid perennial herb native to Europe.

It belongs to the mint family (Lamiaceae) and is characterized by its square stems and opposite leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically pink or purple, arranged in dense spikes. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in woodland areas.

This variety is valued for its aromatic foliage and is sometimes cultivated for ornamental purposes.
