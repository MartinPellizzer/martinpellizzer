Musa chevalieri

Musa chevalieri, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several meters in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-white flowers that eventually develop into elongated, curved fruits. The fruit is typically eaten raw or cooked and has a mild, sweet flavor.

It is valued not only for its edible fruit but also for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
