Miconia chartacea var. brevifolia

Miconia chartacea var. brevifolia, commonly known as the short-leaved miconia, is a species native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows up to 5 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually smooth and glossy. It produces small, white flowers that are clustered in panicles, followed by red, fleshy fruits.

This species is known for its rapid growth and is often found in moist, shaded environments within tropical forests.
