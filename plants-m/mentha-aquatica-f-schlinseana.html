Mentha aquatica f. schlinseana

Mentha aquatica f. schlinseana, commonly known as the Schlinsean water mint, is a variant of the water mint plant.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its aromatic, slightly fuzzy leaves and square stems. This plant typically grows in moist or aquatic environments, such as marshes and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a strong minty scent, which is used in herbal remedies.

The flowers are small, tubular, and typically blue or purple, appearing in whorls along the stems.
