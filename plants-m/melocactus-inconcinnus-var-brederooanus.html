Melocactus inconcinnus var. brederooanus

Melocactus inconcinnus var. brederooanus, commonly known as the Brederoo melon cactus, is a compact, globular cactus native to southern Africa.

It features a ribbed, spiny stem with a distinctive yellowish-green to reddish coloration. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months, typically in spring or early summer. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance and slow growth make it a popular choice for succulent collections.
