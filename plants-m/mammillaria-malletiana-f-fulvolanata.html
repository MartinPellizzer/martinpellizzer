Mammillaria malletiana f. fulvolanata

Mammillaria malletiana f. fulvolanata, commonly known as the yellow-flowered mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent white tubercles and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant has a slow growth rate and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot. It is prized for its unique coloration and is often cultivated in succulent gardens.

Due to its specific care requirements, it is best suited for experienced growers.
