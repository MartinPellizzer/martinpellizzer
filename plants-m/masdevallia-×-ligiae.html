Masdevallia × ligiae

Masdevallia × ligiae, commonly known as the hybrid masdevallia, is a hybrid orchid species resulting from the cross between Masdevallia leucophaea and Masdevallia tenuifolia.

It is native to the cloud forests of South America, particularly in regions of Ecuador and Peru. The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit and intricate, often fragrant flowers with a wide range of colors. It typically blooms during the cooler months, showcasing its ornamental value in both indoor and greenhouse settings.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of cultivation, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
