Mahonia brevipes

Mahonia brevipes, commonly known as the short-stemmed mahonia, is a deciduous shrub native to the Pacific Northwest of North America.

It features compound leaves with spiny margins and bright yellow flowers that bloom in late spring. The plant is characterized by its upright growth habit and can reach heights of up to 3 meters. It thrives in partial shade to full sun and is adapted to moist, well-drained soils.

Mahonia brevipes is often used in landscaping for its ornamental value and hardiness.
