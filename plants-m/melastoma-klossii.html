Melastoma klossii

Melastoma klossii, commonly known as the Kloss's melastoma, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its attractive, showy flowers that typically bloom in shades of red, pink, or white. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves that are often covered in fine hairs, and its stems are usually shrub-like in growth habit. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Melastoma klossii is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in local ecosystems by providing nectar for pollinators.
