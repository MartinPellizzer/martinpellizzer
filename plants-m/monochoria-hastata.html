Monochoria hastata

Monochoria hastata, commonly known as the water lettuce, is a floating aquatic plant native to tropical and subtropical regions.

It has broad, waxy leaves that are arranged in a rosette pattern, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments.

Monochoria hastata is often used in water gardens but can become invasive in some ecosystems.
