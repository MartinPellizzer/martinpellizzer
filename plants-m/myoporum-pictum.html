Myoporum pictum

Myoporum pictum, commonly known as the painted myoporum, is a shrub native to New Zealand.

It features striking blue-green leaves with prominent veins, giving it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding visual interest. It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in coastal environments.

Myoporum pictum is also valued for its drought tolerance and hardy nature.
