Maxillaria rodriguesii

Maxillaria rodriguesii, commonly known as Rodrigues's orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the island of Rodrigues in the Indian Ocean.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces pale yellow to greenish-white blooms with distinctive red or orange markings. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
