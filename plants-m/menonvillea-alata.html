Menonvillea alata

Menonvillea alata, commonly known as the silver velvet plant, is a member of the Bromeliaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking silver-green foliage with a velvety texture. Its leaves are often arranged in a rosette pattern, forming a compact, decorative shape.

It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically hidden within the leaf axils.
