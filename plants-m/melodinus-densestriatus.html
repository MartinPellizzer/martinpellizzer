Melodinus densestriatus

Melodinus densestriatus, commonly known as the dense-striated melodine, is a flowering plant belonging to the family Apocynaceae.

It is characterized by its dense, striated leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces clusters of small, tubular flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It is native to tropical regions of Africa and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
