Melastoma prasinum

Melastoma prasinum, commonly known as the green melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in tropical and subtropical regions, often in moist, shaded environments. The plant features opposite, ovate leaves that are usually dark green and glossy. Its small, fragrant flowers are typically pink or white, and it produces berries that contain several seeds.

Melastoma prasinum is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes used in traditional medicine.
