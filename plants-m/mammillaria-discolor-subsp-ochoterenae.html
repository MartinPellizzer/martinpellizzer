Mammillaria discolor subsp. ochoterenae

Mammillaria discolor subsp.

ochoterenae, commonly known as the Ochoterenae mammillaria, is a small cactus native to northern Mexico. It is characterized by its pale green to blue-gray stems, which are covered in white, woolly spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, thriving in full sun and well-draining soil.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is a prized species among cactus enthusiasts.
