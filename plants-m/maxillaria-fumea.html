Maxillaria fumea

Maxillaria fumea, commonly known as the smoky maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, lance-shaped leaves and produces striking, fragrant flowers with a pale yellow to orange coloration. The flowers are typically borne on upright, branched inflorescences and are noted for their subtle, spicy scent. This orchid thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.

It is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts due to its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
