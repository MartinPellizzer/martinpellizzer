Miscanthus nudipes var. yunnanensis

Miscanthus nudipes var. yunnanensis, commonly known as the Yunnan feather reed, is a grass species native to China.

It is characterized by its slender, arching stems and fluffy, silver-gray plumes that appear in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often used in landscaping for its elegant appearance.

This variety is also noted for its adaptability and resilience in various environmental conditions.
