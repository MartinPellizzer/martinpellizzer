Mimosa gilliesii

Mimosa gilliesii, commonly known as the silver mimosa, is a perennial shrub native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery, velvety foliage and delicate, fern-like leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding when disturbed. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring, adding a subtle floral display. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and dry landscapes.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is often cultivated in arid regions for ornamental purposes.
