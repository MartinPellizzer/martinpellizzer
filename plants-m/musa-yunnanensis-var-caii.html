Musa yunnanensis var. caii

Musa yunnanensis var. caii, commonly known as the Yunnan banana, is a tropical plant native to China.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clustered inflorescences. The plant produces edible fruit that is typically smaller and more elongated compared to common bananas. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

This variety is valued for its unique flavor and potential agricultural significance in certain regions.
