Mattiastrum subscaposum

Mattiastrum subscaposum, commonly known as the slender-stemmed mat grass, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It features slender, often ascending stems that are typically less than 30 centimeters in height. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually white or pale pink, arranged in dense inflorescences. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a bluish-green hue when fresh.

Mattiastrum subscaposum is often found in rocky or sandy soils in open habitats such as grasslands and coastal areas.
