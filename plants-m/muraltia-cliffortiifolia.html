Muraltia cliffortiifolia

Muraltia cliffortiifolia, commonly known as the red muraltia, is a small evergreen shrub native to the Canary Islands.

It features glossy, dark green leaves that are typically elliptical in shape. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters, followed by bright red, edible berries. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for coastal gardens.

Due to its ornamental value and unique fruit, it is often cultivated in subtropical regions.
