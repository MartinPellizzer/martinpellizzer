Macroclinium escobarianum

Macroclinium escobarianum, commonly known as the Escobaria macroclinium, is a flowering plant native to the Caribbean.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Its leaves are usually broad and lance-shaped, and it produces small, fragrant blooms in various colors.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
