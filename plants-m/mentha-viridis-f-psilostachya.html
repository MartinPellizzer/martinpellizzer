Mentha viridis f. psilostachya

Mentha viridis f. psilostachya, commonly known as green mint or narrow-leaved mint, is a variant of the common mint species.

It is characterized by its slender, elongated leaves and a more refined appearance compared to typical mint varieties. This plant is native to Europe and parts of Asia, often found in moist, shaded environments. It produces small, tubular flowers that are typically greenish-white in color.

Mentha viridis f. psilostachya is valued for its aromatic leaves and is used in culinary and medicinal applications.
