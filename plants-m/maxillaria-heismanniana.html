Maxillaria heismanniana

Maxillaria heismanniana, commonly known as the Heismann's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white, pink, and purple, often with a distinctive red or purple lip. It thrives in warm, humid environments with bright indirect light and well-draining soil.

Maxillaria heismanniana is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for orchid enthusiasts.
