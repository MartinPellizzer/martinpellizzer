Masdevallia coccinea var. harryana

Masdevallia coccinea var. harryana, commonly known as the Harryan Red Masdevallia, is a striking orchid species native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers with intricate, velvety petals and a prominent, often contrasting lip. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with compact growth habit and pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients. Its foliage consists of narrow, grass-like leaves that are often covered in a waxy cuticle.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated for its unique and showy floral display.
