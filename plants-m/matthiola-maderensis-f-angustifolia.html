Matthiola maderensis f. angustifolia

Matthiola maderensis f. angustifolia, commonly known as the narrow-leaf stock, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, linear leaves and small, clustered flowers that typically range in color from white to pale purple. Native to the Mediterranean region, this plant thrives in well-drained soils and full sun conditions. It is often found in rocky or sandy habitats, showcasing its adaptability to arid environments.

The plant's ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation in suitable climates.
