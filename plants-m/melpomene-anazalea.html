Melpomene anazalea

Melpomene anazalea, commonly known as the Anazalea orchid, is a tropical flowering plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers that typically come in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant features long, slender stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves that are often glossy in appearance. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Melpomene anazalea is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
