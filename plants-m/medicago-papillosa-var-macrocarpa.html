Medicago papillosa var. macrocarpa

Medicago papillosa var. macrocarpa, commonly known as the large-seeded goat's rue, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with rounded lobes and produces small, yellow, pea-like flowers. The plant is characterized by its distinctive, inflated pods that contain several seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in dry, rocky habitats.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and historical use in traditional medicine.
