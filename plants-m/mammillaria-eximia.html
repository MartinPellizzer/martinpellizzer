Mammillaria eximia

Mammillaria eximia, commonly known as the Mexican snowball cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its dense, globular shape and white to pale green spines. The plant features small, tubular flowers that bloom in shades of yellow to orange. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria eximia is prized for its compact size and ornamental appeal in succulent collections.
