Mammillaria pennispinosa

Mammillaria pennispinosa, commonly known as the needle spine cactus, is a species of flowering cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines and small, rounded areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria pennispinosa is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections due to its compact size and unique appearance.
