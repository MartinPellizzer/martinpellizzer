Monstera viridispatha

Monstera viridispatha, commonly known as the Emerald Shield Monstera, is a tropical plant native to the rainforests of Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically a deep green with a waxy texture. The plant features a climbing habit, often using its aerial roots to anchor itself to trees or other structures. Its leaves have a unique pattern of light green veins that contrast with the darker foliage.

Monstera viridispatha thrives in bright, indirect light and requires consistent moisture and humidity to flourish.
