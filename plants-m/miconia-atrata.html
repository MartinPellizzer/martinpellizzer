Miconia atrata

Miconia atrata, commonly known as the dark-leaved miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green on the upper surface and lighter on the underside. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white or yellow flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruits are fleshy berries that turn red when mature and contain numerous seeds.

Miconia atrata is often found in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
