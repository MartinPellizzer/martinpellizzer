Manglietia celebica

Manglietia celebica, commonly known as the Celebes manglietia, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Indonesian island of Sulawesi.

It belongs to the family Magnoliaceae and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant is notable for its long, slender seed pods that contain numerous winged seeds, aiding in wind dispersal. It thrives in humid, shaded environments within lowland and hill forests.

Due to its rarity and ecological significance, it is considered a valuable species for conservation efforts.
