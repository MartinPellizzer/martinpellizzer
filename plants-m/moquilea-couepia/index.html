Moquilea Couepia

Coral tree, scientifically known as Moquilea couepia, is a striking ornamental plant native to the tropical regions of South America. Its vibrant, flame-like flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow make it a popular choice for gardens and public spaces. The tree thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil to flourish. Its unique appearance and seasonal blooms add a dramatic visual impact to any landscape. Despite its beauty, it is important to note that the plant is toxic to both humans and animals.
