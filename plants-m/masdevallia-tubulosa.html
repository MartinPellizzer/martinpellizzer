Masdevallia tubulosa

Masdevallia tubulosa, commonly known as the tubular masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, tubular-shaped flowers that range in color from white to deep violet. The plant typically grows as a terrestrial or epiphytic orchid, often found in humid, shaded environments. Its leaves are usually lance-shaped and arranged in a rosette pattern, contributing to its unique appearance.

Masdevallia tubulosa is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
