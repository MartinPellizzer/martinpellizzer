Melandrium platyphyllum f. involucrata

Melandrium platyphyllum f. involucrata, commonly known as the narrow-leaved catchfly, is a flowering plant belonging to the Caryophyllaceae family.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in open, disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields. The plant features slender, branching stems and small, clustered flowers with five petals. Its leaves are linear and often covered in fine hairs, contributing to its distinctive appearance.
