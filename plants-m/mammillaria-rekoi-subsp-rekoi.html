Mammillaria rekoi subsp. rekoi

Mammillaria rekoi subsp.

rekoi, commonly known as the Rekoi mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico. It features a globular shape with cylindrical stems covered in white to pale green spines.

The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It prefers well-draining soil and full sun exposure.

Due to its small size and ornamental appeal, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor collections.
