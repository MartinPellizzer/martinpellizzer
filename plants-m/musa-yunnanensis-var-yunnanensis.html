Musa yunnanensis var. yunnanensis

Musa yunnanensis var. yunnanensis, commonly known as Yunnan banana, is a species native to China, particularly in the Yunnan province.

It is a member of the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, broad leaves and clusters of fruit. The plant typically grows as a herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of several meters. Its flowers are usually concealed within the leaf sheaths, and the fruit is a long, curved banana-like structure.

This variety is valued for its ornamental qualities and has some local use in traditional medicine.
