Moraea robusta

Moraea robusta, commonly known as the robust moraea, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its upright, grass-like foliage and striking, showy flowers. The flowers are typically pink to purple in color, with a distinctive spurred petal at the base. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is cultivated in various regions with a suitable climate.
