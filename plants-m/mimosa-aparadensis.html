Mimosa aparadensis

Mimosa aparadensis, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and showy yellow flowers that appear in clusters. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full sunlight. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, particularly in Brazil.

Mimosa aparadensis is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
