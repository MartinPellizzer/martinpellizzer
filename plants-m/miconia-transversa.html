Miconia transversa

Miconia transversa, commonly known as the broadleaf miconia, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in length. The plant produces small, clustered white or pink flowers that bloom during the wet season. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia transversa is often found in lowland rainforests and is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets.
