Myrceugenia gertii

Myrceugenia gertii, commonly known as the Gertii myrtle, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the temperate rainforests of southern Brazil, particularly in the state of Rio de Janeiro. The plant is characterized by its glossy, dark green leaves and clusters of small, fragrant white flowers. It produces small, edible berries that are typically red when ripe.

Myrceugenia gertii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic qualities.
