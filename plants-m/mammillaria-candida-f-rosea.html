Mammillaria candida f. rosea

Mammillaria candida f. rosea, commonly known as the pink mammillaria, is a rare and delicate cactus native to Mexico.

It features a compact, globular stem covered in soft, white to pale pink tubercles. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers that range from white to pink, often with a subtle blush. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires bright, indirect light.

Due to its rarity and unique appearance, it is highly valued by succulent enthusiasts.
