Musa juwiniana

Musa juwiniana, commonly known as the Java banana, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Musaceae family and is characterized by its large, elongated leaves and clustered fruit bunches. The plant produces edible bananas that are typically smaller and more elongated compared to common commercial varieties. It thrives in warm, humid climates with well-drained soil.

Musa juwiniana is often cultivated for both its fruit and ornamental value.
