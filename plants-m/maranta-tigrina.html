Maranta tigrina

Maranta tigrina, commonly known as the tiger's tail or tiger plant, is a member of the Marantaceae family.

It is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil. The plant is characterized by its striking, variegated foliage with dark green veins and bright red or orange margins. Its leaves are often described as having a tiger-like pattern, hence its common name.

Maranta tigrina is a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and ability to thrive in low-light conditions.
