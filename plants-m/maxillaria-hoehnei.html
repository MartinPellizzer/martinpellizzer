Maxillaria hoehnei

Maxillaria hoehnei, commonly known as the Hohne's Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces fragrant, showy flowers with a distinctive coloration. The plant typically features dark green, lance-shaped leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. Its flowers, which bloom in late summer, are often pale yellow with red or brown markings.

Maxillaria hoehnei is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
