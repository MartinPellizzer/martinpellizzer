Moltkia × kemal-paschii

Moltkia × kemal-paschii, commonly known as the hybrid fumitory, is a cross between Moltkia eminii and Moltkia paschii.

It is a low-growing, herbaceous plant characterized by its delicate, sprawling stems and clusters of small, pinkish-white flowers. The plant produces slender, seed-bearing capsules that release tiny seeds when mature. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, often found in rocky or dry habitats.

Its botanical features make it a desirable ornamental plant in gardens and natural landscapes.
