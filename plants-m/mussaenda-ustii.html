Mussaenda ustii

Mussaenda ustii, commonly known as the narrow-leaved Mussaenda, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its clusters of small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has opposite, narrow, and often hairy leaves that are typically green with a slightly glossy surface. Mussaenda ustii thrives in humid, warm climates and is often found in lowland forest areas.

It is valued in horticulture for its ornamental flowers and adaptability to various soil types.
