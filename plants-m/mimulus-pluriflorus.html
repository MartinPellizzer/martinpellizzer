Mimulus pluriflorus

Mimulus pluriflorus, commonly known as the yellow monkeyflower, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its vibrant yellow-orange flowers, which bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils along stream banks and in meadows. It has opposite, oval-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

Mimulus pluriflorus is a perennial species that thrives in full sun to partial shade.
