Micromeles karchungii

Micromeles karchungii, commonly known as the Karchung mountain vine, is a climbing plant native to the Himalayas.

It belongs to the family Passifloraceae and is characterized by its woody stems and tendrils that aid in climbing. The plant produces striking flowers with five sepals, five petals, and a distinctive crown of filaments. Its fruits are fleshy berries that mature to a reddish color, containing numerous seeds.
