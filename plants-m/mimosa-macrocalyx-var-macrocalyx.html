Mimosa macrocalyx var. macrocalyx

Mimosa macrocalyx var. macrocalyx, commonly known as the giant mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to South America.

It features compound leaves that are sensitive to touch, folding shut when disturbed, a characteristic known as thigmonasty. The plant produces small, yellow, spherical flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardens.

Its rapid growth and distinctive foliage make it both visually striking and ecologically interesting.
