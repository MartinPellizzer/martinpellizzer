Mikania hookeriana var. kittsiana

Mikania hookeriana var. kittsiana, commonly known as the Kitts's mile-a-minute vine, is a fast-growing, herbaceous perennial native to the Caribbean.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems that can spread rapidly, often covering large areas in a short time. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically arranged in clusters, and its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often hairy. It thrives in moist, sunny environments and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and forest edges.

Due to its aggressive growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
