Mezilaurus manausensis

Mezilaurus manausensis, commonly known as the Manaus mahogany, is a tropical tree native to the Amazon Basin.

It belongs to the family Lauraceae and is characterized by its dark, aromatic wood. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth upper surface. It produces small, greenish flowers and spherical, dark brown fruits.

Mezilaurus manausensis is valued for its timber but is also threatened by deforestation and habitat loss.
