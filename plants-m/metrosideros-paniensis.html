Metrosideros paniensis

Metrosideros paniensis, commonly known as the Panié or Panié tree, is a species of flowering plant in the Myrtaceae family.

It is native to the island of Pohnpei in the Caroline Islands of Micronesia. The plant is characterized by its small, glossy leaves and clusters of bright red or orange flowers that bloom throughout the year. It grows as a small to medium-sized tree, often reaching heights of up to 10 meters.

The species is notable for its ecological importance and cultural significance to the local communities of Pohnpei.
