Myrtillocactus geometrizans

Myrtillocactus geometrizans, commonly known as the Christmas cactus, is a flowering cactus native to Brazil.

It is characterized by its segmented stems that form a branched, shrub-like structure. The plant produces vibrant flowers in shades of red, pink, and purple during the holiday season. It thrives in bright, indirect light and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Its unique appearance and festive blooming make it a popular ornamental plant.
