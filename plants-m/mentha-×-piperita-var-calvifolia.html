Mentha × piperita var. calvifolia

Mentha × piperita var. calvifolia, commonly known as peppermint with smooth leaves, is a hybrid mint species characterized by its smooth, hairless foliage.

It produces small, pale blue or purple flowers that bloom in whorls along the stems. The plant is known for its strong menthol scent and is widely used in aromatherapy and herbal remedies. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers full sun to partial shade.

This variety is often cultivated for its aromatic leaves and medicinal properties.
