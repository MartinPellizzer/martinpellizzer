Millettia pulchra var. chinensis

Millettia pulchra var. chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese evergreen or the silver thread plant, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, elongated leaves that are often variegated with silver and green markings. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers followed by long, cylindrical seed pods. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
