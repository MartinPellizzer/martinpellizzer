Muehlenbeckia horrida

Muehlenbeckia horrida, commonly known as the rough muehlenbeckia, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a perennial shrub that typically grows in coastal and subalpine regions, thriving in well-drained soils. The plant features narrow, slightly hairy leaves and produces small, inconspicuous yellow flowers. It is often used in horticulture for its hardy nature and ability to stabilize soil.

Due to its ecological importance, it is also valued in restoration projects.
