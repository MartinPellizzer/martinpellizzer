Monadenium reflexum

Monadenium reflexum, commonly known as the desert star, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Africa.

It is a succulent shrub that features small, fleshy leaves and produces yellow or orange flowers. The plant is adapted to survive in harsh, dry environments by storing water in its stems and leaves. Its flowering period typically occurs during the rainy season, and it plays a role in supporting local wildlife.

Monadenium reflexum is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging climates.
