Mammillaria boolii

Mammillaria boolii, commonly known as the Boole's hedgehog cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with a smooth, bluish-green surface. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
