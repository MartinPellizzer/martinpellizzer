Masdevallia carruthersiana

Masdevallia carruthersiana, commonly known as the Carruthers' masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, evergreen growth habit and produces striking, long-lasting flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features a single pseudobulb with a few leaves and blooms during the cooler months. It thrives in high humidity and requires bright, indirect light for optimal growth.

Due to its unique appearance and specific care requirements, it is a prized addition to specialized orchid collections.
