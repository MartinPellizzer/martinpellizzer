Mutisia castellanosii var. comechingoniana

Mutisia castellanosii var. comechingoniana, commonly known as the Comechingona mutisia, is a flowering plant native to the Andean regions of South America.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and opposite leaf arrangement. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often found in cloud forests. Its foliage is usually dark green and leathery, while its flowers are small and yellow, arranged in dense clusters.

It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ecological role in supporting local biodiversity.
