Mohria vestita

Mohria vestita, commonly known as the white mohria, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, which is characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that are typically arranged in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Mohria vestita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
