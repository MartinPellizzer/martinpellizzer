Monopsis stellarioides subsp. stellarioides

Monopsis stellarioides subsp.

stellarioides, commonly known as starwort, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the western United States. It features a rosette of narrow, grass-like leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs.

The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers arranged in a cluster at the tip of each leaf stem. It thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in open, sunny habitats.

Its botanical characteristics make it well adapted to arid and semi-arid environments.
