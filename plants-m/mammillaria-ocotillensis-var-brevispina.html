Mammillaria ocotillensis var. brevispina

Mammillaria ocotillensis var. brevispina, commonly known as the short-spined mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and is covered in small, white to pale green tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring or early summer. It has short, spiny bristles and is well adapted to arid environments.

This variety is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
