Machaerium minutiflorum

Machaerium minutiflorum, commonly known as the small-flowered machaerium, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Leguminosae family, characterized by its compound leaves and pod fruits. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its small, white to pale pink flowers bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
