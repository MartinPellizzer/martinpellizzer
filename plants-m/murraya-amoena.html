Murraya amoena

Murraya amoena, commonly known as the white coral tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its fragrant, white, coral-like flowers that bloom in clusters, giving it its common name. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its fruit is a small, fleshy drupe that turns red when mature.

Murraya amoena is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
