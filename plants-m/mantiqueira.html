Mantiqueira

Mantiqueira, commonly known as the "Tree of Life," is a species of flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest region of Brazil.

It is a medium-sized evergreen tree that can grow up to 20 meters tall, featuring a straight trunk and a broad, rounded canopy. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a glossy green upper surface. Its flowers are small, clustered in panicles, and are typically white or pale yellow, producing a sweet, fragrant aroma.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several seeds, which are dispersed by wind and animals.
