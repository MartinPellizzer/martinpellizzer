Mimosa sericantha

Mimosa sericantha, commonly known as the velvet mimosa, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy yellow flowers. The plant is known for its sensitive leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait called thigmonastic response. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and rocky areas.

Mimosa sericantha is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
