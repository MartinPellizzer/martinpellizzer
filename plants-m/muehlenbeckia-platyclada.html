Muehlenbeckia platyclada

Muehlenbeckia platyclada, commonly known as the silverweed, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a low-growing perennial that typically forms dense mats of foliage. The plant features small, white to pale yellow flowers that bloom in clusters during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are usually hairy and have a silvery-blue appearance, which gives it its common name.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in coastal or wetland environments.
