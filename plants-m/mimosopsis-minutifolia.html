Mimosopsis minutifolia

Mimosopsis minutifolia, commonly known as the small-leaved mimosopsis, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a member of the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are clustered in racemes, typical of many legumes. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
