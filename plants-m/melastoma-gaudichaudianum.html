Melastoma gaudichaudianum

Melastoma gaudichaudianum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its showy, often brightly colored flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub with opposite, ovate leaves that are glossy in appearance. Its flowers are usually clustered in inflorescences and feature five petals, often with a distinctive red or pink hue.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its visual appeal.
