Mammillaria duwei

Mammillaria duwei, commonly known as the Duwei's hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a ribbed structure and spines that are typically white or pale yellow. The plant produces yellow flowers during the summer months, which are followed by red, spherical fruits. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
