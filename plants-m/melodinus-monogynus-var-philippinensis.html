Melodinus monogynus var. philippinensis

It is native to the Philippines and is characterized by its woody vines and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in lowland to montane forest areas, often climbing through the canopy. Its flowers are usually pink to red in color and are pollinated by insects.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind.
