Millettia dunnii

Millettia dunnii, commonly known as the Chinese wisteria, is a climbing legume native to China.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family and is characterized by its long, flexible stems and compound leaves. The plant produces pendulous clusters of fragrant, violet-blue flowers, which bloom in the spring. Its seeds are encased in hard, woody pods that split open to release the seeds.

Millettia dunnii is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its showy floral display.
