Muretia Lutea

Muretia lutea is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant yellow flowers and distinctive woody structure. This plant is often found growing in humid, shaded environments. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening. Despite its beauty, it remains relatively unknown to the general public.
