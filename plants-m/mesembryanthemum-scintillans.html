Mesembryanthemum scintillans

Mesembryanthemum scintillans, commonly known as the star flower or ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, often blue-green leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers that bloom in shades of pink, white, and purple. The plant is adapted to arid environments, storing water in its thick leaves and stems. It thrives in full sun and well-draining soil, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its unique appearance and drought tolerance make it a striking addition to gardens in Mediterranean and desert climates.
