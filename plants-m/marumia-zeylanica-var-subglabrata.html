Marumia zeylanica var. subglabrata

Marumia zeylanica var. subglabrata, commonly known as the glossy leaf marumia, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its smooth, waxy leaves that give it a distinctive glossy appearance. The plant typically grows as an evergreen shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters. Its leaves are elliptical in shape and emit a mild aromatic scent when crushed.

The flowers are small and clustered, often white or pale yellow, and attract various pollinators.
