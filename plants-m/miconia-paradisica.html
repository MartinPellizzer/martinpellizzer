Miconia paradisica

Miconia paradisica, commonly known as the paradise miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a tropical shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height. The plant is characterized by its large, palmate leaves that are often brightly colored. Its flowers are showy, with five petals and numerous stamens, typically in shades of white, pink, or red.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release numerous small seeds.
