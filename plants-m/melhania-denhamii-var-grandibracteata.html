Melhania denhamii var. grandibracteata

Melhania denhamii var. grandibracteata, commonly known as the grandibracteate melhania, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that typically range in color from red to pink, with prominent bracts that enhance its visual appeal. The plant is native to tropical regions of South America, particularly Brazil, and thrives in warm, humid climates. Its foliage consists of broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs.

This species is valued in horticulture for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
