Mammillaria haehneliana

Mammillaria haehneliana, commonly known as the Hahnel's bunny ear cactus, is a small, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular or cylindrical stem with prominent, rounded tubercles that resemble bunny ears, giving it its common name. The plant produces delicate, white or pale pink flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it well-suited for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ease of care, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
