Mammillaria cubensis

Mammillaria cubensis, commonly known as the Cuban hedgehog cactus, is a species native to Cuba.

It is a small, globular cactus with a dense covering of white, woolly hairs. The plant typically grows to a height of about 5 to 10 centimeters and has a compact, rounded shape. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, emerging from areoles on the stem.

This species is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
