Maughania faginea

Maughania faginea, commonly known as the yellow bellflower, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It features showy, bell-shaped yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. The plant has slender, upright stems and narrow, lance-shaped leaves. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its vibrant color and ability to attract pollinators.
