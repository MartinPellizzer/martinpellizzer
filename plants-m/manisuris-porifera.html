Manisuris porifera

Manisuris porifera, commonly known as the sponge-like orchid, is a unique flowering plant characterized by its unusual, porous, and sponge-like structure in its floral parts.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is native to specific regions in Southeast Asia. The plant's botanical features include showy, fragrant flowers that are often yellow or green in color. It typically grows in shaded, moist environments, such as forests or rocky areas.
