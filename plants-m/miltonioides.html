Miltonioides

Miltonioides, commonly known as the flame violet, is a genus of orchids native to the tropical regions of South America.

These plants are characterized by their showy, colorful flowers that resemble violets, often with intricate patterns and vibrant hues. They typically grow as epiphytes, attaching themselves to trees and absorbing moisture and nutrients from the air. The flowers bloom in clusters, and the genus includes several species such as Miltoniopsis and Miltoniopsis.

Their botanical appeal lies in their striking appearance and unique growth habits.
