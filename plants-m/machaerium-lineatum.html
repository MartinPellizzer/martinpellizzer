Machaerium lineatum

Machaerium lineatum, commonly known as the slender machaerium, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its slender, often climbing stems. The plant produces small, pea-like flowers that are typically green or white in color. Its leaves are pinnately compound, consisting of several oval-shaped leaflets.
