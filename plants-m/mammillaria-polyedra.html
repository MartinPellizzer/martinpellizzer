Mammillaria polyedra

Mammillaria polyedra, commonly known as the star cactus, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its spherical shape and short, cylindrical stems covered in white, woolly spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Mammillaria polyedra is a popular choice for indoor gardening due to its compact size and ornamental appearance.
