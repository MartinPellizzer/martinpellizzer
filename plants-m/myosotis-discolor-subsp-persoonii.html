Myosotis discolor subsp. persoonii

Myosotis discolor subsp.

persoonii, commonly known as the Persian forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to Central Asia and parts of the Middle East. It is characterized by its delicate blue to purple flowers and heart-shaped leaves.

The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or stream banks. Its flowers bloom in spring and are known for their ornamental value in gardens.

The subspecies persoonii is distinguished by its slightly larger flowers and more robust growth compared to the typical form of *Myosotis discolor*.
