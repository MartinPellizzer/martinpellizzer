Mimosa regnellii var. supersetosa

Mimosa regnellii var. supersetosa, commonly known as the broad-leaf mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its bipinnate leaves, which are divided into numerous small leaflets. The plant produces fluffy, yellow to pinkish-white spherical flower heads that appear in clusters. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in open areas such as grasslands and disturbed sites.

The species is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in certain ecological contexts.
