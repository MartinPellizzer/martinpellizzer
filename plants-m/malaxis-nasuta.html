Malaxis nasuta

Malaxis nasuta, commonly known as the slender orchid, is a species of orchid native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and small, greenish-white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as forest floors or rocky crevices. It has a complex root system that helps it absorb nutrients from poor soil conditions.

Malaxis nasuta is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
