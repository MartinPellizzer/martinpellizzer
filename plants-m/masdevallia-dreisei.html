Masdevallia dreisei

Masdevallia dreisei, commonly known as the Ecuadorian masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and elongated, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces inflorescences with a cluster of small, showy flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. Its flowers are typically fragrant and bloom during the cooler months.

Masdevallia dreisei is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
