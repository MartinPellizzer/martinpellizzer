Millettia pseudoracemosa

Millettia pseudoracemosa, commonly known as the pink millettia, is a flowering plant species native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its compound leaves and showy, pinkish-white flowers. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 5 meters.

Its seeds are enclosed in pod-like fruits, and the plant is valued for its ornamental appeal and potential ecological benefits.
