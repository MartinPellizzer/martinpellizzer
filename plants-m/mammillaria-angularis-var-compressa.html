Mammillaria angularis var. compressa

Mammillaria angularis var. compressa, commonly known as the compressed angular mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular stem with prominent, angular ribs that are often covered in white to yellowish spines. The plant produces small, yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.

Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cactus collections.
