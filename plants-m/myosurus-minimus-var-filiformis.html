Myosurus minimus var. filiformis

Myosurus minimus var. filiformis, commonly known as the slender moss gentian, is a small, herbaceous plant belonging to the Gentianaceae family.

It is characterized by its delicate, blue or violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, and it produces tiny, clustered inflorescences.

Due to its habitat specificity, it is often found in regions with high humidity and well-drained soil.
