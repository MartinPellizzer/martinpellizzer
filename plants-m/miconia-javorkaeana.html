Miconia javorkaeana

Miconia javorkaeana, commonly known as the Java miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves and showy, clustered flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments within lowland forests. Its flowers are usually pink or white, and they bloom in clusters, attracting various pollinators.

Miconia javorkaeana is also known for its rapid growth and can become invasive in certain regions.
