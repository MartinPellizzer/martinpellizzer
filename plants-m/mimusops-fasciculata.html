Mimusops fasciculata

Mimusops fasciculata, commonly known as the African snowball tree, is a flowering plant native to tropical Africa.

It is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 10 meters tall, featuring a broad, rounded crown. The tree produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom in spring, giving it its common name. Its leaves are simple, alternate, and have a waxy surface, while its bark is smooth and grayish.

The plant is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.
