Micropera obtusa

Micropera obtusa, commonly known as the blunt micropera, is a small herbaceous plant native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. The plant features slender, upright stems covered in fine hairs. Its leaves are elongated and have a slightly waxy texture, often with a bluish-green hue.

The flowers are small, clustered in dense inflorescences, and are usually white or pale pink in color.
