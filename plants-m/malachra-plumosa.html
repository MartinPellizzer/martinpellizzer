Malachra plumosa

Malachra plumosa, commonly known as the featherleaf malachra, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and showy yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its unique appearance makes it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.

Malachra plumosa is relatively low maintenance, requiring minimal water once established.
