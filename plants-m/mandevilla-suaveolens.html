Mandevilla suaveolens

Mandevilla suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet scent mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in a variety of colors, including red, pink, and yellow. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. It thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a decorative vine or shrub.

Mandevilla suaveolens is known for its strong, sweet fragrance, which attracts pollinators like bees and butterflies.
