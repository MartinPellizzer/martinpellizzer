Melastoma obovatum

Melastoma obovatum, commonly known as the broad-leaf melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features opposite, ovate to obovate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are usually red or pink, with five petals and numerous stamens, making it visually striking.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, dark seeds.
