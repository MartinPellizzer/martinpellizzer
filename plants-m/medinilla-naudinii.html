Medinilla naudinii

Medinilla naudinii, commonly known as the velvet tangerine, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage that gives it its common name. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that range in color from white to pink, often appearing in clusters. It thrives in humid, warm climates and requires well-drained soil.

Medinilla naudinii is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive appearance and air-purifying qualities.
