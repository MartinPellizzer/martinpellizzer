Myosotis sylvatica proles suaveolens

Myosotis sylvatica proles suaveolens, commonly known as the sweet-scented woodland forget-me-not, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to Europe.

It features delicate blue to violet flowers with a distinctive, sweet fragrance, which is particularly noticeable in the evening. The plant has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically covered in fine hairs. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

This species is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is a popular choice in gardens and wildflower meadows.
