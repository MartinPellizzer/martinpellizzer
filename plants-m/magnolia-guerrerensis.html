Magnolia guerrerensis

Magnolia guerrerensis, commonly known as the Guerrerense magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to the Mexican state of Guerrero.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is characterized by its large, showy white to pink flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant has simple, alternate, ovate leaves with a smooth texture and a dark green upper surface. Its bark is smooth and grayish-brown, and it thrives in well-drained soils in temperate climates.

The species is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
