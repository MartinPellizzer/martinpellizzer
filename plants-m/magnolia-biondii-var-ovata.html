Magnolia biondii var. ovata

Magnolia biondii var. ovata, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the white magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to China.

It is characterized by its large, showy white flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The tree has smooth, gray bark and elliptical leaves that turn yellow in the fall. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
