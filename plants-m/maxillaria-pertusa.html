Maxillaria pertusa

Maxillaria pertusa, commonly known as the Rosy Maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant typically features long, slender pseudobulbs and lance-shaped leaves. Its flowers are borne on terminal spikes and are known for their intricate, velvety petals.

Maxillaria pertusa thrives in warm, humid environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
