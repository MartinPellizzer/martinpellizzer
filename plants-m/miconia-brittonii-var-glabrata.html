Miconia brittonii var. glabrata

Miconia brittonii var. glabrata, commonly known as the smooth miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family.

It is a shrub or small tree native to the tropical regions of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, glossy, elliptical leaves that are typically dark green in color. It produces clusters of small, white to pinkish flowers that are often followed by bright red, berry-like fruits.

The species is noted for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens and as a landscape plant.
