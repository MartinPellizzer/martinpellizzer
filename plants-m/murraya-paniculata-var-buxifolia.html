Murraya paniculata var. buxifolia

Murraya paniculata var. buxifolia, commonly known as the glossy buckthorn, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage with glossy, oval-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, fragrant yellow flowers that are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Its compact growth habit makes it suitable for hedges and topiary.
