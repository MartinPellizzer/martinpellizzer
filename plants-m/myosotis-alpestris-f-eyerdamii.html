Myosotis alpestris f. eyerdamii

Myosotis alpestris f. eyerdamii, commonly known as the Eyerdam's alpine forget-me-not, is a low-growing perennial herb native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer, typically in clusters along the stems. The plant has hairy, lance-shaped leaves that are arranged alternately along the stem. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in meadows and rocky areas at high elevations.

This cultivar is valued for its ornamental appeal and its ability to add color to alpine gardens.
