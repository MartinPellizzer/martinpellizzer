Miconia globuliflora var. dominicae

Miconia globuliflora var. dominicae, commonly known as the Dominica miconia, is a shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean island of Dominica.

It is characterized by its dense, dark green foliage and produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers. The plant's leaves are typically ovate to elliptic in shape, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.

The fruit is a globose, fleshy berry that contains numerous small, hard seeds.
