Miconia perijensis

Miconia perijensis, commonly known as the Perijan miconia, is a tropical shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, inconspicuous white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Miconia perijensis is known for its rapid growth and ability to form dense thickets, which can impact local ecosystems.

It is also notable for its potential ecological role in supporting insect and bird populations.
