Medicago × casellasii

Medicago × casellasii, commonly known as the Casellasii clover, is a hybrid plant belonging to the legume family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pea-like flowers that range in color from white to pink. This plant is native to the Mediterranean region and thrives in well-drained soils. It has been cultivated for its ornamental value and its ability to fix nitrogen in the soil.

Medicago × casellasii is also used in ecological restoration projects due to its adaptability and resilience.
