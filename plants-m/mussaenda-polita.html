Mussaenda polita

Mussaenda polita, commonly known as the golden glorybower, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of 1 to 3 meters, featuring lance-shaped leaves with a smooth texture. The plant is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that are usually yellow or orange in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Mussaenda polita is valued for its ornamental appeal and is commonly cultivated in gardens and landscapes.
