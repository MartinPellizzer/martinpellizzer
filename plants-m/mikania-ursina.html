Mikania ursina

Mikania ursina, commonly known as the ant plant, is a climbing vine native to the Americas.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its rapid growth and ability to form dense mats. The plant has opposite, heart-shaped leaves and produces small yellow flowers arranged in clusters. Its stems are covered with stiff, hair-like structures that help in climbing and deterring herbivores.

Mikania ursina is often used in traditional medicine for its purported healing properties.
