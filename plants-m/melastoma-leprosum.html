Melastoma leprosum

Melastoma leprosum, commonly known as the red-rooted melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its striking red or purple flowers. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forests and understoreys. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and have a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small seeds, which are dispersed by wind or water.
