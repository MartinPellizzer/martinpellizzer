Myonima borboniae

Myonima borboniae, commonly known as the Madagascar star orchid, is a unique flowering plant native to Madagascar.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, often in forest understories. Its leaves are elliptical and dark green, while its flowers come in shades of white and pink.

Myonima borboniae is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated for horticultural purposes.
