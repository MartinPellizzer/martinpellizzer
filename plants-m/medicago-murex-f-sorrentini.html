Medicago murex f. sorrentini

Medicago murex f. sorrentini, commonly known as the Sorrentine goat's rue, is a subspecies of the goat's rue plant native to southern Europe.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows in rocky or calcareous soils. The plant features trifoliate leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers arranged in racemes. It is adapted to dry, sunny environments and is often found in Mediterranean climates.

Its botanical characteristics make it an interesting subject for ecological and horticultural study.
