Mutisia retusa var. glaberrima

Mutisia retusa var. glaberrima, commonly known as the smooth mutisia, is a perennial shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features gray-green, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. Its flowers are typically yellow with a central disk, and it produces small, dry fruits called achenes.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in forest understories.
