Myristica minahassae

Myristica minahassae, commonly known as the Minahassa nutmeg, is a small evergreen tree native to the tropical regions of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree (Myristica fragrans). The plant produces small, yellowish flowers and oval-shaped fruits that contain aromatic seeds. Its botanical characteristics include dark green, glossy leaves and a distinctive, woody aroma.

The plant is valued for its seeds, which are used in traditional medicine and as a spice.
