Mikania szyszylowiczii

Mikania szyszylowiczii, commonly known as the Chinese cowpea vine, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Due to its rapid growth, it can become invasive in certain regions.
