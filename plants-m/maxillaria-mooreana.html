Maxillaria mooreana

Maxillaria mooreana, commonly known as the Moore's orchid, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clusters of small, colorful blooms in shades of pink, white, and purple. Its leaves are usually dark green and arranged in a rosette pattern, aiding in water retention.

Maxillaria mooreana is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to care for in suitable growing conditions.
