Microlicia euphorbioides var. mattagrossensis

Microlicia euphorbioides var. mattagrossensis, commonly known as the Mattagrossen microlicia, is a shrub native to the Amazon rainforest.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family, characterized by its latex-producing sap and diverse morphological features. The plant typically features simple, alternate leaves with a smooth or slightly hairy surface. Its flowers are small and clustered, often arranged in cup-like structures called cyathia, a common trait in the Euphorbiaceae family.

The species is adapted to the humid, nutrient-rich environments of the tropical rainforest.
