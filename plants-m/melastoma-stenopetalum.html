Melastoma stenopetalum

Melastoma stenopetalum, commonly known as the narrow-petaled melastoma, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are usually pink or red in color, with five petals that are narrow and pointed. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The fruit is a capsule that contains several small, black seeds.
