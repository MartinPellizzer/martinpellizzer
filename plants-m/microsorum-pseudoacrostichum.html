Microsorum pseudoacrostichum

Microsorum pseudoacrostichum, commonly known as the Java fern, is a hardy aquatic plant native to Southeast Asia.

It has narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically bright green and arranged in a rosette pattern. The plant does not produce flowers and is propagated through rhizome division or by attaching spores to a substrate. It thrives in low to moderate light conditions and is popular in planted aquariums.

Its ability to tolerate a wide range of water parameters makes it a favored choice for both beginner and advanced aquarists.
