Manilkara argentea

Manilkara argentea, commonly known as the silver star or silver leaf mahogany, is a tropical tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its smooth, silver-gray bark and large, elliptical leaves that are glossy on top and pale underneath. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers followed by bright red, fleshy berries that contain several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and has also been used in traditional medicine.

The tree is valued for its hard, durable wood, which is used in furniture and construction.
