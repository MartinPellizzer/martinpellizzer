Monochaetum diffusum

Monochaetum diffusum, commonly known as the scattered-hair grass, is a flowering plant native to the wet tropical regions of Australia.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, tufted growth habit with hairy leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in spikelets, which are typically wind-pollinated.
