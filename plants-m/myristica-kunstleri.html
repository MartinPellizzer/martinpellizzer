Myristica kunstleri

Myristica kunstleri, commonly known as the Borneo nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the island of Borneo.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is closely related to the nutmeg tree, Myristica fragrans. The plant is characterized by its aromatic leaves and small, yellowish flowers that develop into bright red, fleshy fruits. The seeds within the fruit are used in traditional medicine and have a distinct, spicy aroma similar to nutmeg.

It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in lowland rainforests.
