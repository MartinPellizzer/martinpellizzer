Moraea villosa subsp. elandsmontana

Moraea villosa subsp.

elandsmontana, commonly known as the Elandsmontana glory flower, is a perennial herb native to South Africa. It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy inflorescences that typically bloom in summer.

The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of vivid blue to purple flowers. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
