Mimosa laevigata

Mimosa laevigata, commonly known as the smooth mimosa, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features bipinnate leaves composed of numerous small, oval-shaped leaflets. Its flowers are small, yellow, and clustered in spherical inflorescences.

Mimosa laevigata is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in a variety of soil conditions.
