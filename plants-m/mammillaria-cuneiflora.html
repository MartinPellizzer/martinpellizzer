Mammillaria cuneiflora

Mammillaria cuneiflora, commonly known as the narrow-leafed mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its compact, globular shape and spiny, ribbed stems. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance requirements, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
