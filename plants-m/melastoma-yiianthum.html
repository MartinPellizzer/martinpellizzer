Melastoma yiianthum

Melastoma yiianthum, commonly known as the red-flowered melastoma, is a shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves that are dark green and glossy. The plant produces showy, clustered flowers that range from pink to deep red, often with a yellow or orange center. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
