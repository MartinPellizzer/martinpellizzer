Mulgedium orbelicum

Mulgedium orbelicum, commonly known as the silverleaf orchid, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking silver-gray foliage and elegant, fragrant blossoms that bloom in late spring. The plant thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil to flourish. Its unique botanical features make it a sought-after species among horticulturists and botanical enthusiasts.

Due to habitat loss and over-collection, it is also classified as an endangered species.
