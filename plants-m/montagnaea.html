Montagnaea

Montagnaea, commonly known as the mountain fern, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Montagnaeaceae.

It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and small, clustered flowers that often bloom in spring. These plants are typically found in moist, shaded environments, such as forest floors or rocky crevices. Montagnaea species are known for their ability to thrive in cool, humid climates.
