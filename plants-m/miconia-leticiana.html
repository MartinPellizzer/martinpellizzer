Miconia leticiana

Miconia leticiana, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a tropical shrub native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green with a distinctive purple or reddish underside. The plant produces small, clustered white flowers that are followed by bright red, fleshy fruits. Miconia leticiana is known for its rapid growth and ability to thrive in shaded, moist environments.

Due to its ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens, though it can become invasive in certain regions.
