Meliosma crassifolia

Meliosma crassifolia, commonly known as the broadleaf meliosma, is a species of flowering plant in the family Meliaceae.

It is native to tropical regions of Southeast Asia, including countries like Thailand and Malaysia. The plant is characterized by its broad, lance-shaped leaves and small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-yellow in color. The tree can reach heights of up to 10 meters and is valued for its ornamental foliage and potential medicinal properties.
