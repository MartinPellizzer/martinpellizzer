Maxillaria colombiana

Maxillaria colombiana, commonly known as the Colombian maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Colombia.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces fragrant, pink to purple flowers with a distinctive yellow lip. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid tropical environments. It thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright, indirect light.

Maxillaria colombiana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
