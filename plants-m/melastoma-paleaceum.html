Melastoma paleaceum

Melastoma paleaceum, commonly known as the paleaceum melastoma, is a flowering plant belonging to the Melastomataceae family.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Asia, particularly Southeast Asia. The plant is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves and showy, clustered flowers that are typically pink or white in color. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

The fruit is a capsule that contains numerous small, dark seeds.
