Masdevallia flaveola

Masdevallia flaveola, commonly known as the yellow masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and its striking yellow flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically produces multiple inflorescences bearing numerous small, fragrant blooms. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light.

Its unique floral structure and coloration make it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
