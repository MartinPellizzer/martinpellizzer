Maranta mediopicta

Maranta mediopicta, commonly known as the spotted arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It is a member of the Marantaceae family, known for its ornamental foliage. The plant features striking, arrow-shaped leaves with distinctive red and green markings. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light.

Maranta mediopicta is often cultivated as a houseplant due to its aesthetic appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
