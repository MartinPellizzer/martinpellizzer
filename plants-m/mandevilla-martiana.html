Mandevilla martiana

Mandevilla martiana, commonly known as the red mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is a vigorous climber with long, trailing stems that can reach several meters in length. The plant produces large, showy flowers in shades of red, pink, and white, which bloom throughout the growing season. Its leaves are heart-shaped and glossy, providing a lush green backdrop to the vibrant blossoms.

Mandevilla martiana thrives in warm climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant in gardens and as a houseplant.
