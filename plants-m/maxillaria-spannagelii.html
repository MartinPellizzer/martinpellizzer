Maxillaria spannagelii

Maxillaria spannagelii, commonly known as the Spannagel's orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces pendulous inflorescences with showy, fragrant flowers that are typically pink or purple in color. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria spannagelii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
