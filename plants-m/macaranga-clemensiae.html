Macaranga clemensiae

Macaranga clemensiae, commonly known as the silverleaf macaranga, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, including countries such as Malaysia and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a silvery or whitish pubescence. It thrives in tropical rainforests and is often found in moist, shaded environments.

The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers followed by capsule fruits containing numerous seeds.
