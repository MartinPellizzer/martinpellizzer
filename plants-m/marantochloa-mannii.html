Marantochloa mannii

Marantochloa mannii, commonly known as the red-leaved marantaceae, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant, glossy red foliage that contrasts beautifully with its green stems. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes a variety of ornamental plants known for their colorful leaves. Marantochloa mannii typically grows as an upright herb with clustered leaves that can reach several inches in length.
