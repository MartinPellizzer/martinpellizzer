Miltonia flavescens var. grandiflora

Miltonia flavescens var. grandiflora, commonly known as the yellow orchid, is a tropical orchid native to the Amazon rainforest.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellow flowers with a distinctive greenish throat. The plant produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, supporting its growth in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers typically bloom in summer and can last for several weeks.

It is a popular ornamental plant due to its vibrant color and elegant appearance.
