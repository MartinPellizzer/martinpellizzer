Micromelum integerrimum var. mollissimum

Micromelum integerrimum var. mollissimum, commonly known as the silky hibiscus, is a tropical shrub native to parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its soft, hairy leaves and showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. The plant produces small, round fruits that contain numerous seeds. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

The species is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
