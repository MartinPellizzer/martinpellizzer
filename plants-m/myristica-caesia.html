Myristica caesia

Myristica caesia, commonly known as the purple nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Maluku Islands of Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic leaves and distinctive purple seed pods. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that are followed by fleshy, orange-colored fruits containing hard, aromatic seeds. Myristica caesia is valued for its ornamental value and its use in traditional medicine.

Its unique appearance and fragrance make it a notable species in botanical gardens and research.
