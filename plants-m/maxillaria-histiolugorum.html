Maxillaria histiolugorum

Maxillaria histiolugorum, commonly known as the spotted maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, velvety flowers that often display a mix of purple, pink, and white hues. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs with broad, dark green leaves that are covered in a fine, hairy texture. Its flowers are borne on lateral spikes and are known for their intricate patterns and strong fragrance.

Maxillaria histiolugorum is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
