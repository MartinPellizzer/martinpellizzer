Mimosa biuncifera var. flexuosa

Mimosa biuncifera var. flexuosa, commonly known as the weeping mimosa, is a deciduous shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery foliage and its ability to produce showy yellow flowers in spring. The plant has compound leaves that are often drooping, giving it its common name. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

The species is also notable for its sensitivity to touch, as its leaves fold when disturbed.
