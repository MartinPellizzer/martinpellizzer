Mertensia nevadensis

Mertensia nevadensis, commonly known as the Sierra buckthorn, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue-violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as forest floors and stream banks. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Mertensia nevadensis plays an important role in supporting local pollinators and maintaining ecological balance in its habitat.
