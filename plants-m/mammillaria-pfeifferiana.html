Mammillaria pfeifferiana

Mammillaria pfeifferiana, commonly known as the Pfeiffer's bunny ear cactus, is a species native to the dry regions of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive shape and a grayish-green color. The plant features short, cylindrical spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
