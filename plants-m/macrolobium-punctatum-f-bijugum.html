Macrolobium punctatum f. bijugum

Macrolobium punctatum f. bijugum, commonly known as the two-winged macrolobium, is a rare and distinctive plant species belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is characterized by its unique leaf structure, featuring two pairs of opposite lobes on each leaflet, giving it a striking appearance. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a delicate fragrance. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, often found in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is highly sought after by botanists and horticulturists.
