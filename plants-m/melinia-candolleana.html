Melinia candolleana

Melinia candolleana, commonly known as the golden shower tree, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its drooping clusters of bright yellow, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. The plant has compound leaves composed of several narrow, lance-shaped leaflets. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Melinia candolleana is also valued for its ability to provide shade and its role in supporting local biodiversity.
