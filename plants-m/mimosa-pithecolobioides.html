Mimosa pithecolobioides

Mimosa pithecolobioides, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a shrub or small tree native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that are covered in fine, silky hairs, giving it a soft, fuzzy appearance. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically greenish-white in color. Its fruits are spherical, three-lobed pods that contain several seeds.

Mimosa pithecolobioides is often found in disturbed soils and can thrive in a variety of light conditions.
