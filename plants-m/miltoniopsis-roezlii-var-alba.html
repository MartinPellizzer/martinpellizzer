Miltoniopsis roezlii var. alba

Miltoniopsis roezlii var. alba, commonly known as the white Miltoniopsis, is a hybrid orchid species native to the cloud forests of Central America.

It is characterized by its delicate, white flowers that bloom in clusters, often with a slight pinkish hue at the base. The plant typically grows as a miniature orchid, with short, erect stems and broad, lance-shaped leaves. It prefers cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and well-draining soil.

This variety is prized for its elegance and is often cultivated for ornamental purposes in greenhouses and home collections.
