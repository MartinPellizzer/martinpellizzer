Mammillaria Thomasii

Mammillaria thomasii is a small, spiny cactus native to Mexico. It is known for its distinctive white or pale pink flowers that bloom in late spring. This species thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water to survive. Its compact size and unique appearance make it a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts. Despite its beauty, it is often overlooked in favor of more showy cactus varieties.
