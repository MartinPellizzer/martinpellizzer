Melloca tuberosa

Melloca tuberosa, commonly known as the mountain yam, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are often used in traditional medicine. The plant typically grows in humid, shaded environments, such as forest understories. Its leaves are large, heart-shaped, and have a rough texture.

Melloca tuberosa is valued for its medicinal properties, including potential anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects.
