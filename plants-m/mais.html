Mais

Mais, commonly known as maize, is a tall annual grass native to the Americas.

It produces large, clustered ears of kernels that are typically yellow or white in color. The plant has long, narrow leaves and a robust stem that supports its heavy ear heads. It thrives in temperate climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Maize is a staple crop worldwide, valued for its nutritional content and versatility in culinary uses.
