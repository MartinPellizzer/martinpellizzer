Muscari acutilobum

Muscari acutilobum, commonly known as the sharp-lobed muscari, is a species of flowering plant in the Amaryllis family.

It is native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia, particularly in regions with temperate climates. Muscari acutilobum thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and woodland settings.
