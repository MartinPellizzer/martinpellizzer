Mesembryanthemum subrigidum

Mesembryanthemum subrigidum, commonly known as the rigid ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

Mesembryanthemum subrigidum is often used in rock gardens and xeriscaping due to its drought tolerance and ornamental appeal.
