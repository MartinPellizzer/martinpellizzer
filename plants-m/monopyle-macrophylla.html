Monopyle macrophylla

Monopyle macrophylla, commonly known as the large-leaf monopyle, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its broad, glossy leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces small, fragrant white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in lowland forests.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for ornamental gardening in tropical regions.
