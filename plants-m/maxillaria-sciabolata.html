Maxillaria sciabolata

Maxillaria sciabolata, commonly known as the sword orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its elongated, sword-shaped leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid, shaded environments. Its flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, and the species is known for its delicate, intricate floral structure.

Maxillaria sciabolata is appreciated by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
