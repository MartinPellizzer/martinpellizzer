Manihot tweedieana

Manihot tweedieana, commonly known as the African arrowroot, is a herbaceous perennial plant native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its tuberous roots, which are the primary part used for culinary purposes.
