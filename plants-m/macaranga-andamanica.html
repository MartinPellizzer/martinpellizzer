Macaranga andamanica

Macaranga andamanica, commonly known as the Andaman nettle, is a flowering plant native to the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its opposite, ovate leaves with serrated edges. Its latex contains compounds that can cause skin irritation.
