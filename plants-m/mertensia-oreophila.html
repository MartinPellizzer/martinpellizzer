Mertensia oreophila

Mertensia oreophila, commonly known as the mountain bluebell, is a perennial herb native to the mountainous regions of the western United States.

It is characterized by its striking blue, bell-shaped flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in shaded to partially shaded environments. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a smooth, waxy texture.

Mertensia oreophila is valued for its ornamental beauty and ecological importance in supporting pollinators.
