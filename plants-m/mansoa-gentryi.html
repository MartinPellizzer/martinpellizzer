Mansoa gentryi

Mansoa gentryi, commonly known as the Gentry's mansoa, is a flowering plant species native to the cloud forests of Central America.

The plant typically grows as an understory shrub, adapting to the humid, shaded conditions of its natural habitat. Mansoa gentryi is valued for its ornamental appeal and plays a role in the ecological dynamics of its native ecosystems.
