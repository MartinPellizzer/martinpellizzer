Miscanthus oligostachyus

Miscanthus oligostachyus, commonly known as silver reed or narrowleaf bluestem, is a tall, perennial grass native to East Asia.

It features slender, silver-gray leaves that are often used in ornamental gardening for their striking appearance. The plant produces panicles of small, fluffy flowers that add a soft, feathery texture to the landscape. It thrives in full sun and well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping and erosion control.

Its drought tolerance and ability to form dense stands make it a valuable plant in both ecological and horticultural contexts.
