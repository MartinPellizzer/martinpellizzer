Maireana Brevifolia

Maireana brevifolia is a small, drought-tolerant shrub native to the arid regions of Australia. It is known for its compact growth habit and ability to thrive in harsh, sandy soils. The plant features narrow, linear leaves and produces small, pale yellow flowers during the warmer months. It is often used in landscaping for its low maintenance and ornamental value. Maireana brevifolia is a hardy species that plays an important role in stabilizing desert ecosystems.
