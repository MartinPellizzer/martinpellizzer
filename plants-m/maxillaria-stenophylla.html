Maxillaria stenophylla

Maxillaria stenophylla, commonly known as the narrow-leafed maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and striking, often fragrant flowers that come in a variety of colors, including white, pink, and purple. The plant typically produces pseudobulbs that store water and nutrients, aiding in its survival in dry conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Maxillaria stenophylla is popular among orchid enthusiasts for its ornamental value and relatively easy care requirements.
