Maxillaria xantholeuca

Maxillaria xantholeuca, commonly known as the yellow-leaved maxillaria, is a species of orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact, pseudobulbous structure and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of yellow and green. The plant typically blooms during the spring and early summer, with flowers that have a distinctive, spurred lip. Its leaves are usually ovate and arranged in a basal rosette, contributing to its overall ornamental appeal.

This orchid is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts.
