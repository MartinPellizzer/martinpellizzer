Mammillaria centricirrha var. divergens

Mammillaria centricirrha var. divergens, commonly known as the diverging mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in white to pale yellow spines. The plant typically grows in rocky or sandy soils and thrives in full sun. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in late spring or early summer.

Due to its unique appearance and slow growth, it is a popular choice among cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
