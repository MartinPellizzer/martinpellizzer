Masdevallia lenae

Masdevallia lenae, commonly known as the Lenae's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the cloud forests of Ecuador.

It is characterized by its compact size and intricate, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, velvety texture. The plant produces delicate, pendulous inflorescences with flowers that are typically a mix of white, pink, and purple hues. Masdevallia lenae thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, making it a challenging but rewarding species for orchid enthusiasts.

Its unique appearance and limited distribution make it a prized specimen in botanical collections.
