Minuartia aksoyi

Minuartia aksoyi, commonly known as the Turkish rockcress, is a flowering plant belonging to the Brassicaceae family.

It is a low-growing, mat-forming perennial that typically thrives in rocky or gravelly soils. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in early spring. Its leaves are narrow and linear, often covered in a fine layer of white hair.
