Meistera nilgirica

Meistera nilgirica, commonly known as the Nilgiri meistera, is a flowering plant native to the Western Ghats of India.

It is characterized by its elegant, pendulous flowers that are typically white or pale pink in color. The plant thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forested areas. Its leaves are elliptical and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its delicate blooms.

Meistera nilgirica is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic beauty.
