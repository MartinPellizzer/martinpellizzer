Medinilla patens

Medinilla patens, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet tangerine tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its dark green, velvety leaves and clusters of fragrant, white or pale pink flowers. The plant produces small, round, yellow-orange fruits that are edible and have a sweet, citrus-like flavor. Medinilla patens thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and showy blooms.
