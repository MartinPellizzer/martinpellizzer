Mutisia brachyantha

Mutisia brachyantha, commonly known as the silverleaf mutisia, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage and showy yellow flowers that bloom in the spring. The plant typically grows in dry, open habitats such as grasslands and shrublands. It has a woody stem with a fibrous root system, and its leaves are opposite and ovate in shape.

Mutisia brachyantha is often used in ornamental gardening due to its striking appearance and drought tolerance.
