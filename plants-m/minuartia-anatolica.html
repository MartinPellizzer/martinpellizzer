Minuartia anatolica

Minuartia anatolica, commonly known as the Anatolian rockcress, is a small, mat-forming flowering plant native to the rocky and arid regions of Turkey.

It belongs to the Brassicaceae family, which includes many other flowering plants with characteristic four-petaled flowers. The flowers, which are usually white or pale yellow, bloom in early spring and attract various pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
