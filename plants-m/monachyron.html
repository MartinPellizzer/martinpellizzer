Monachyron

Monachyron, commonly known as the sacred lotus, is a tropical aquatic plant native to Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that bloom on long, upright stalks above the water surface. The plant has broad, heart-shaped leaves that float on the water's surface, while its roots anchor it to the muddy bottom. Monachyron is known for its symbolic significance in various cultures, often representing purity and spiritual enlightenment.

Botanically, it belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family and is closely related to other water lilies.
