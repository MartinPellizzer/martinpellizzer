Mammillaria viridula var. minima

Mammillaria viridula var. minima, commonly known as the miniature green mammillaria, is a small cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem with a greenish-gray color and is covered in small, white spines. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring or early summer. It is a compact species, typically growing to only a few centimeters in height.

Due to its size and unique appearance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
