Melpomene moniliformis

Melpomene moniliformis, commonly known as the red spider lily, is a striking plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its vibrant red flowers, which bloom in clusters and are often adorned with a white or yellowish throat. The plant features strap-like leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, providing a striking contrast to its colorful blooms. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and prefers partial to full shade, making it a popular choice for shaded garden areas.

Melpomene moniliformis is also known for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated for its aesthetic appeal in horticulture.
