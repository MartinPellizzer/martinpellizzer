Mammillaria lasiacantha var. denudata

Mammillaria lasiacantha var. denudata, commonly known as the naked mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a short, cylindrical stem covered in small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. Its leaves are reduced to spines, and it has a dense covering of white, woolly hairs.

It is well-suited for rock gardens and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
