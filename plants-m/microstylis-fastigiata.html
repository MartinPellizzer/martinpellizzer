Microstylis fastigiata

Microstylis fastigiata, commonly known as the upright microstylis, is a species of flowering plant belonging to the family Poaceae, or grass family.

It is characterized by its slender, upright growth habit and narrow, grass-like leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers arranged in dense clusters, typically near the base of the plant. It is native to certain regions of South America and thrives in moist, well-drained soils.

Due to its delicate appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens for its ornamental value.
