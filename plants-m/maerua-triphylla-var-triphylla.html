Maerua triphylla var. triphylla

Maerua triphylla var. triphylla, commonly known as the three-leaf maerua, is a shrub native to arid and semi-arid regions of Africa.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves, which consist of three small, oval-shaped leaflets. The plant produces small, yellowish-white flowers that are clustered in dense inflorescences. It is drought-tolerant and thrives in well-drained soils, making it suitable for xeriscaping.

The species is also valued for its medicinal properties in traditional African medicine.
