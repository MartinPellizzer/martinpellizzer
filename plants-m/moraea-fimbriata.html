Moraea fimbriata

Moraea fimbriata, commonly known as the fringed moraea, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Iridaceae family and is characterized by its striking, showy flowers that bloom in spring. Moraea fimbriata is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and is also studied for its potential medicinal properties.
