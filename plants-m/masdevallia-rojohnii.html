Masdevallia rojohnii

Masdevallia rojohnii, commonly known as the Red-tipped Masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It features intricate, often pendulous inflorescences with brightly colored, spurred flowers that range from red to orange. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments with well-drained soil. Its leaves are usually long and strap-like, contributing to its elegant appearance.

Due to its unique floral structure and habitat requirements, it is a popular choice among orchid enthusiasts and botanists.
