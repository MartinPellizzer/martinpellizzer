Myosotis chinensis

Myosotis chinensis, commonly known as the Chinese forget-me-not, is a perennial flowering plant native to eastern Asia.

It typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and streambanks. The plant produces small, delicate blue or purple flowers that bloom in early summer. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a slightly hairy texture.

Myosotis chinensis is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its charming appearance.
