Mesadenia pulverulenta

Mesadenia pulverulenta, commonly known as the sandstone lily, is a flowering plant native to arid regions of Australia.

It is a perennial herb that typically grows in rocky soils and sandy environments. The plant produces small, white or pale pink flowers that bloom in spring. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, adapted to conserve water in dry conditions.

Mesadenia pulverulenta is often found in coastal and inland areas where it thrives in well-drained soils.
