Malaxis Cordiglottis

The malaxis cordiglottis is a rare and enigmatic orchid species native to the humid forests of Southeast Asia. Known for its delicate, spurred flowers and unique growth habits, it thrives in shaded, moist environments. This orchid is often overlooked due to its subtle appearance and limited distribution. Its scientific name reflects its distinctive floral structure and geographical origin. Despite its beauty, it remains poorly studied and threatened by habitat loss.
