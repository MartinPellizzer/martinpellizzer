Masdevallia naevia

Masdevallia naevia, commonly known as the speckled masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and its striking, often colorful flowers with intricate patterns. The plant typically features long, slender leaves and produces inflorescences with numerous small, showy blooms. Its flowers are usually pink or purple with contrasting streaks and spots, giving it its common name.

Masdevallia naevia thrives in high humidity and cool temperatures, making it a popular choice for indoor cultivation.
