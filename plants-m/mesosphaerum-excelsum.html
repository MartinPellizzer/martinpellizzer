Mesosphaerum excelsum

Mesosphaerum excelsum, commonly known as the tall mesosphaerum, is a striking member of the Malvaceae family.

It is characterized by its tall, erect growth habit and large, showy flowers that bloom in summer. The plant features palmate leaves with lobed margins, adding to its ornamental appeal. Native to South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates and well-drained soils.

Due to its unique appearance, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as an ornamental plant.
