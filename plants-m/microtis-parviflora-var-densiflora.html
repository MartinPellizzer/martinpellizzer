Microtis parviflora var. densiflora

Microtis parviflora var. densiflora, commonly known as the dense-flowered microtis, is a perennial herb native to Australia.

It belongs to the family Orchidaceae and is characterized by its compact growth habit and dense clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers. The plant typically grows in well-drained sandy soils in open, sunny habitats. Its leaves are linear and grass-like, often forming a rosette at the base.

This variety is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
