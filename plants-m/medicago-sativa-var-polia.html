Medicago sativa var. polia

Medicago sativa var. polia, commonly known as Turkey red clover, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and small, pinkish-white flowers that bloom in early spring. This variety is valued for its ability to fix atmospheric nitrogen, improving soil fertility. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often used in forage and cover cropping.

Its adaptability makes it a popular choice in ecological restoration projects.
