Monactis kingii

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive yellow-orange composite flowers. The plant typically grows in rocky, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun. Its foliage consists of narrow, linear leaves that are arranged in a basal rosette.
