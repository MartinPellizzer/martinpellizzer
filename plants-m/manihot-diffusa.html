Manihot diffusa

Manihot diffusa, commonly known as the arrowleaf or arrowhead plant, is a species of flowering plant in the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its distinctive arrow-shaped leaves. The plant is herbaceous, with smooth, waxy leaves that are typically green on top and lighter in color underneath. It produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are often clustered in a cyathium, a unique structure typical of the Euphorbiaceae family.

The plant is sometimes cultivated for its ornamental value due to its attractive foliage.
