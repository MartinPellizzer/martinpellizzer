Musa × corbieri

Musa × corbieri, commonly known as the Chinese banana, is a hybrid plant belonging to the Musaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. The plant produces clusters of berries that are typically yellow or orange when mature. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value due to its striking foliage.

However, it is not typically grown for fruit production compared to other banana species.
