Myxospermum

Myxospermum, commonly known as the sticky seed plant, is a genus of flowering plants belonging to the family Myrtaceae.

It is characterized by its small, often sticky seeds that adhere to animals and clothing, aiding in seed dispersal. The plants typically have aromatic leaves and small, clustered flowers with showy, pink or white petals. They are native to regions of South America, particularly in Brazil and Argentina.

Myxospermum species are often found in moist, tropical environments and are valued for their ornamental qualities.
