Melianthus dregeanus

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its striking yellow flowers that resemble stars. The plant has palmate leaves with five to seven lobes, and its stems are often covered in a sticky, resinous substance. The flowers are primarily visited by bees and other pollinators, contributing to its ecological significance.
