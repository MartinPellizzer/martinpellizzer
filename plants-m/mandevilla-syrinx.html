Mandevilla syrinx

Mandevilla syrinx, commonly known as the flame vine, is a climbing perennial shrub native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to orange and yellow. The plant has opposite, ovate leaves with a slightly hairy surface. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and requires well-draining soil.

Mandevilla syrinx is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.
