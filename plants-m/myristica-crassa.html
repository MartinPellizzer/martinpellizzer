Myristica crassa

Myristica crassa, commonly known as the rough nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its aromatic, woody fruits that contain the spice nutmeg. The plant produces small, yellow flowers and has compound leaves with elliptical leaflets. Its seeds, enclosed in a hard, woody aril, are the source of the valuable spice nutmeg.

The plant is also known for its medicinal properties and has been used in traditional medicine for various ailments.
