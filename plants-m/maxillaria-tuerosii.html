Maxillaria tuerosii

Maxillaria tuerosii, commonly known as the Tuerosi orchid, is a species native to Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces dark green, velvety leaves and has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to orange, with a distinctive dark lip, and bloom in the summer months.

Maxillaria tuerosii is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in warm, humid environments.
