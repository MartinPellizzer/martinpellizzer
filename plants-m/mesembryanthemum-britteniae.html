Mesembryanthemum britteniae

Mesembryanthemum britteniae, commonly known as the Britten's ice plant, is a succulent shrub native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that store water, enabling it to thrive in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange, typically during the cooler months. It is often grown as an ornamental plant due to its unique appearance and drought tolerance.

Mesembryanthemum britteniae is well-suited for rock gardens and xeriscaping, requiring minimal water and care.
