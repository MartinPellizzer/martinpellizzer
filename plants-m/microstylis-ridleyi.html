Microstylis ridleyi

Microstylis ridleyi, commonly known as the slender-styled orchid, is a tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the orchid family, Orchidaceae, and is characterized by its delicate, elongated flowers. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments such as rainforests. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, while its flowers are small and often pale in color.

Microstylis ridleyi is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
