Macaranga brachytricha

Macaranga brachytricha, commonly known as the short-stemmed paper tree, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces small, clustered yellow flowers, followed by capsule fruits that contain numerous tiny seeds. It is native to Southeast Asia and is often found in moist, lowland forests.

Macaranga brachytricha is valued for its fast growth and is sometimes used in reforestation efforts.
