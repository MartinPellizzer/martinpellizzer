Medicago littoralis f. braunii

Medicago littoralis f. braunii, commonly known as the coastal medic, is a perennial legume native to coastal regions of Europe and western Asia.

It thrives in saline and alkaline soils, often found in dunes, salt marshes, and along the shores. The plant has trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellow pea-like flowers. Its seed pods are elongated and contain several small, rounded seeds.

It plays an important role in stabilizing coastal soils and providing forage for livestock.
