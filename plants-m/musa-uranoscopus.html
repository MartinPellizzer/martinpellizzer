Musa uranoscopus

Musa uranoscopus, commonly known as the giant banana plant, is a striking member of the Musaceae family.

It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Malaysia and Indonesia. This plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can reach up to 3 meters in length. It produces clusters of large, yellow bananas that are typically harvested when fully mature.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
