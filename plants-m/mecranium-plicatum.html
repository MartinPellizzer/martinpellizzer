Mecranium plicatum

Mecranium plicatum, commonly known as the pleated orchid, is a flowering plant native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its unique, pleated leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces striking, showy flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a pleasant fragrance. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its ornamental value, it is cultivated in botanical gardens and by enthusiasts worldwide.
