Maxillaria kefersteinioides

Maxillaria kefersteinioides, commonly known as the Kefirstein orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to South America, particularly Brazil.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its compact growth habit and showy, fragrant flowers. The plant typically produces clustered inflorescences with pale yellow to greenish-white petals and sepals, often with a distinctive purple or reddish lip. It thrives in shaded, moist environments and is often found in forest understories.

Due to its ornamental value, it is occasionally cultivated by orchid enthusiasts.
