Manilkara hexandra

Manilkara hexandra, commonly known as the durian tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is renowned for producing the distinctive and highly fragrant fruit, the durian. The plant has a thick, rough, and often spiny bark that provides protection against pests and environmental stress. Its leaves are large, broad, and glossy, typically arranged alternately along the branches.

The tree thrives in humid climates and is valued both for its fruit and its potential medicinal properties.
