Metamagnolia macrophylla

Metamagnolia macrophylla, commonly known as the giant magnolia, is a striking tropical plant characterized by its large, showy flowers.

It belongs to the Magnoliaceae family and is native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand and Malaysia. The plant features broad, ovate leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. Its flowers, which can reach up to 30 cm in diameter, are usually white or pale yellow with a strong, sweet fragrance.

Metamagnolia macrophylla is often cultivated for its ornamental value in warm climates due to its dramatic appearance and long blooming period.
