Masdevallia pozoi

Masdevallia pozoi, commonly known as the Pozo's masdevallia, is a rare and exquisite orchid native to the high-altitude cloud forests of Peru.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its intricate, often colorful floral structures. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, anchoring itself to tree bark in its natural habitat. Its flowers are usually small, with intricate patterns and a wide range of hues, making it a prized specimen among orchid enthusiasts.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a vulnerable species in the wild.
