Melica hohenackeri

Melica hohenackeri, commonly known as Hohenacker's brome, is a species of grass native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its slender, erect stems and small, spike-like inflorescences. The plant typically grows in rocky or alpine environments, where it thrives in cool, moist conditions. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a slight sheen, and it produces wind-pollinated flowers.

Melica hohenackeri is of interest to botanists due to its ecological adaptations and limited distribution.
