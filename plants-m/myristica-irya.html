Myristica irya

Myristica irya, commonly known as the nutmeg tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Maluku Islands in Indonesia.

It belongs to the Myristicaceae family and is characterized by its fragrant, aromatic fruits and seeds. The tree produces large, fleshy berries that contain a single seed, which is encased in a hard, woody shell. The seeds are harvested for their aromatic oils and are used in both culinary and medicinal applications.

Myristica irya thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
