Melothria zehnerioides

Melothria zehnerioides, commonly known as the Zehner's bitter melon, is a tropical vine belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is characterized by its slender, trailing stems and heart-shaped, lobed leaves. The plant produces small, green, bitter fruits that are typically harvested when mature. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.

Due to its bitter taste, it is often used in traditional cuisines and herbal remedies.
