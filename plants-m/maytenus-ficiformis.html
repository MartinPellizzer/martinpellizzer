Maytenus ficiformis

Maytenus ficiformis, commonly known as the hanging mayten, is a tropical evergreen tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its long, pendulous branches that give it a distinctive appearance, often resembling a curtain or veil. The plant has compound leaves with elongated leaflets and produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by orange-red berries. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in tropical forests.

Maytenus ficiformis is valued for its ornamental qualities and has been used in traditional medicine for various purposes.
