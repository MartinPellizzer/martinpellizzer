Manihot olfersiana

Manihot olfersiana, commonly known as the arrowleaf manioc, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of South America.

It belongs to the Euphorbiaceae family and is characterized by its large, arrow-shaped leaves. The plant produces small, greenish flowers arranged in clusters, and its roots are used in traditional medicine and as a food source. The plant is notable for its ability to accumulate toxic compounds, which can be harmful if not properly processed.
