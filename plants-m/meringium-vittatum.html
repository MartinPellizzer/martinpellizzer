Meringium vittatum

Meringium vittatum, commonly known as the spotted meringium, is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is characterized by its striking, velvety leaves that display a unique mottled pattern of green and silver. The plant produces small, pale yellow flowers that bloom in late spring, adding a subtle yet elegant touch to its foliage. Meringium vittatum thrives in cool, moist environments and requires well-drained soil to prevent root rot.
