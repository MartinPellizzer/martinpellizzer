Mertensia klotzschii

Mertensia klotzschii, commonly known as the Pacific bluebells, is a perennial herb native to the western United States.

It features basal leaves that are broad and heart-shaped, often with a waxy coating. The plant produces clusters of blue to purple bell-shaped flowers in early spring. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and prefers partial to full shade.

Its botanical characteristics make it a distinctive and attractive addition to woodland gardens.
