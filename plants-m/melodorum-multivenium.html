Melodorum multivenium

Melodorum multivenium, commonly known as the multivenous melodorum, is a rare flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its striking, multicolored petals that range from deep purples to bright yellows, often with intricate venation patterns. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers that bloom during the late spring and early summer months. Its leaves are broad and lance-shaped, with a slightly waxy texture that helps it retain moisture in its arid habitat.

Due to its unique appearance and limited distribution, it is considered a botanical curiosity and is often sought after by horticulturists and botanists.
