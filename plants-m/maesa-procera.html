Maesa procera

Maesa procera, commonly known as the tall maesa, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Africa and Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that can grow up to several meters in height, characterized by its dense, evergreen foliage. The plant produces small, fragrant white or pale yellow flowers arranged in clusters, which bloom during the wet season. Its leaves are typically elliptical or ovate, with a smooth or slightly hairy surface.

Maesa procera is often found in moist, shaded habitats such as forests and along riverbanks.
