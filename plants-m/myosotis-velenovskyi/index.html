Myosotis Velenovskyi

Myosotis velenovskyi is a rare and delicate flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia. Known for its striking blue-violet petals and compact growth habit, it thrives in cool, moist environments. This species belongs to the Myosotis genus, which includes the well-known forget-me-nots. Its limited distribution and unique characteristics make it a subject of interest for botanists and conservationists. Due to habitat loss and environmental changes, Myosotis velenovskyi faces threats to its survival.
