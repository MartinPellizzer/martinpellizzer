Masdevallia echinata

Masdevallia echinata, commonly known as the spiny masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often hairy leaves and striking, spurred flowers that come in a range of colors including red, orange, and yellow. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil. Its unique floral structure and vibrant hues make it a prized species among orchid enthusiasts.

Masdevallia echinata is also noted for its relatively compact size and ease of cultivation under the right conditions.
