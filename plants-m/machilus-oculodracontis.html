Machilus oculodracontis

Machilus oculodracontis, commonly known as the dragon's eye tree, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to southern China and is characterized by its aromatic, evergreen leaves and small, fragrant flowers. The tree produces distinctive fruits that are yellow-green in color and contain a single seed. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often found in forested areas.

Due to its ornamental value and medicinal properties, it is cultivated in some regions for both horticultural and traditional uses.
