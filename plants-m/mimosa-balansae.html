Mimosa balansae

Mimosa balansae, commonly known as the Balansae sensitive plant, is a species native to South America.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, which includes legumes and beans. The plant is characterized by its bipinnate leaves that fold shut when touched, a trait known as thigmonastic response. Its flowers are typically small and yellow, arranged in clustered inflorescences.

Mimosa balansae is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in ecological restoration projects.
