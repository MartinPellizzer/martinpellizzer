Morinda polyneura

Morinda polyneura, commonly known as the red-flowered morinda, is a shrub or small tree native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its lance-shaped leaves and clusters of bright red flowers that bloom in the summer. The plant produces small, round berries that are typically orange to red in color. It thrives in tropical and subtropical climates and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

Morinda polyneura is valued for its ornamental appeal and has traditional uses in some cultures.
