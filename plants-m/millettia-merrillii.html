Millettia merrillii

Millettia merrillii, commonly known as the Merrill's legume, is a flowering plant belonging to the Fabaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia, particularly found in countries like China, Vietnam, and Indonesia. The plant is characterized by its compound leaves and small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink. Its fruit is a legume pod that contains several seeds, which are often used in traditional medicine.

Millettia merrillii is valued for its ornamental appeal and has been studied for its potential pharmacological properties.
