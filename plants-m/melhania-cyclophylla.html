Melhania cyclophylla

Melhania cyclophylla, commonly known as the cyclophylla melon, is a tropical plant belonging to the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Central and South America and is characterized by its trailing growth habit and large, lobed leaves. Melhania cyclophylla is valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated in warm climates for its vibrant foliage and edible fruits.
