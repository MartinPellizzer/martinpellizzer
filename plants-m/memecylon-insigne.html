Memecylon insigne

Memecylon insigne, commonly known as the golden memecylon, is a tropical shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Melastomataceae and is characterized by its opposite, elliptical leaves with a glossy surface. The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.
