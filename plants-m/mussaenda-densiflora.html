Mussaenda densiflora

Mussaenda densiflora, commonly known as the denseflower Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its showy, clustered inflorescences that range in color from yellow to orange. The plant typically grows as a shrub or small tree, reaching heights of up to 3 meters. Its leaves are opposite, elliptical, and often covered with fine hairs.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.
