Mondo Dracaenoides Var. Reptans

Mondo dracaenoides var. reptans is a popular houseplant known for its compact size and striking foliage. Its leaves are typically dark green with a subtle sheen and often feature a light green or yellowish variegation. This plant thrives in indirect light and requires minimal maintenance, making it ideal for beginners. It is also prized for its ability to purify indoor air and add a touch of elegance to any space. Despite its small stature, it can grow quite dense, creating a lush, vibrant display.
