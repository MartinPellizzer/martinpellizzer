Mammillaria supertexta var. leucostoma

Mammillaria supertexta var. leucostoma, commonly known as the White-flowered Mammillaria, is a compact cactus native to Mexico.

It features a rounded, ribbed stem covered in white to pale blue tubercles, which give it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring or early summer. It is a slow-growing species that thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for collectors and succulent enthusiasts.
