Mimosa gracilis subsp. filiformis

Mimosa gracilis subsp.

filiformis, commonly known as the slender mimosa, is a shrub native to South America, particularly Brazil. It is characterized by its delicate, fern-like leaves and produces small, yellow, spherical flowers.

The plant is typically found in moist, shaded environments such as forest understories. Its stems are slender and often twining, which helps it climb or trail along the ground.

The species is noted for its rapid growth and is sometimes used in ornamental gardening.
