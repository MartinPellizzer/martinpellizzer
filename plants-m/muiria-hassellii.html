Muiria hassellii

Muiria hassellii, commonly known as the Tasmanian sandplain lily, is a flowering plant native to Tasmania.

It is a perennial herb with narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, white or pale pink flowers. The plant typically grows in sandy or rocky soils and is adapted to coastal and inland environments. It is often found in open heathlands and is characteristic of the sandplain ecosystems.

Muiria hassellii is an important component of the local flora and is valued for its ecological role in supporting native pollinators.
