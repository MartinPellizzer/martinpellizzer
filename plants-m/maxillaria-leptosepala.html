Maxillaria leptosepala

Maxillaria leptosepala, commonly known as the slender-sepaled orchid, is a species native to Central and South America.

It is a terrestrial orchid with a pseudobulb and linear leaves. The plant produces clusters of fragrant, white to pale yellow flowers with elongated petals and sepals. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil.

Maxillaria leptosepala is appreciated for its ornamental value and is often cultivated in gardens and greenhouses.
