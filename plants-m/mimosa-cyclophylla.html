Mimosa cyclophylla

Mimosa cyclophylla, commonly known as the Australian mimosa, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub that typically grows in sandy soils and coastal areas. The plant features small, fern-like leaves that close when touched, a characteristic known as thigmonastic response. Its yellow flowers appear in clusters and bloom in spring.

It is often used in horticulture for its ornamental value and ability to thrive in harsh environments.
