Mimosa foliolosa var. macrocephala

Mimosa foliolosa var. macrocephala, commonly known as the giant mimosa, is a striking plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, fern-like leaves and showy, pale yellow to white flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests or along stream banks. Its rapid growth and ability to thrive in various soil types make it a popular choice for ornamental gardening.

However, it can become invasive in certain regions if not managed properly.
