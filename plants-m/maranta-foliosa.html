Maranta foliosa

Maranta foliosa, commonly known as the arrowroot plant, is a tropical species native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its striking, colorful foliage that often displays shades of red, purple, and green. The plant belongs to the Marantaceae family and is known for its rhizomatous growth habit. Its leaves are typically arrow-shaped, which gives it its common name.

Maranta foliosa is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and ability to thrive in shaded environments.
