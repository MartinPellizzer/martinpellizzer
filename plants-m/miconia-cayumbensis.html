Miconia cayumbensis

Miconia cayumbensis, commonly known as the purple miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to the Caribbean and Central America.

It is characterized by its large, broad, heart-shaped leaves that are often a deep green with a reddish underside. The plant produces clusters of small, white, fragrant flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a distinctive, fleshy, purple berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia cayumbensis is often cultivated for ornamental purposes due to its striking foliage and showy flowers.
