Mesosphaerum passerinum

Mesosphaerum passerinum, commonly known as the silverleaf mesosphaerum, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its silvery-gray foliage, which is covered in fine, dense hairs. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that are typically clustered in terminal inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Mesosphaerum passerinum is valued for its ornamental appeal and drought tolerance.
