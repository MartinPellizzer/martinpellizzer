Muscari sosnowskyi

It is native to the Caucasus region and is characterized by its dense, spherical clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens and woodland settings.

Muscari sosnowskyi is also valued for its ornamental appeal and is often cultivated for its unique appearance and scent.
