Maxillaria densa

Maxillaria densa, commonly known as the dense maxillaria, is a terrestrial orchid native to Central and South America.

It features a sympodial growth habit with a rhizome that produces multiple pseudobulbs. The plant produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of white and pink, often with a yellow or green lip. Its leaves are elongated and grass-like, typically arranged in a rosette pattern.

Maxillaria densa thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil for optimal growth.
