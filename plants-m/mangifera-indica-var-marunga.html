Mangifera indica var. marunga

Mangifera indica var. marunga, commonly known as the Marunga mango, is a tropical fruit tree native to Southeast Asia.

It produces large, elongated mangoes with a smooth, thin skin that ranges in color from yellow to orange. The flesh is typically sweet and fibrous, with a high sugar content and a mild aroma. This variety is valued for its flavor and is often used in both fresh consumption and culinary applications.

It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
