Madisonia carrisii

Madisonia carrisii, commonly known as the Carrisii's madisonia, is a rare and delicate aquatic plant native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its small, floating leaves that are typically oval or elliptical in shape. The plant produces tiny, white flowers that bloom in the summer months, adding a subtle aesthetic to its habitat. It thrives in still or slow-moving freshwater environments with soft, acidic water.
