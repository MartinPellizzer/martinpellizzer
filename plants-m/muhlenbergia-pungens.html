Muhlenbergia pungens

Muhlenbergia pungens, commonly known as the silver muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It is characterized by its fine, silver-blue foliage that gives it a distinctive appearance. The plant produces slender, spike-like inflorescences that bloom in late summer to early fall. It thrives in full sun to partial shade and prefers well-drained soils.

Muhlenbergia pungens is often used in ornamental landscaping for its ornamental value and drought tolerance.
