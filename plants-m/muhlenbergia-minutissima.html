Muhlenbergia minutissima

Muhlenbergia minutissima, commonly known as the dwarf muhlenbergia, is a small, herbaceous grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, open habitats such as wetlands and forest edges. The plant features fine, grass-like leaves and produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that are often seen in the fall. It is valued for its ornamental appeal and ability to thrive in wet conditions.

Muhlenbergia minutissima is also an important plant for wildlife, providing food and habitat for various insects and birds.
