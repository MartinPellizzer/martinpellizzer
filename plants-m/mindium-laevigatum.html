Mindium laevigatum

Mindium laevigatum, commonly known as the smooth mindium, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the temperate regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that are often covered in a fine, silky texture. The plant produces small, clustered flowers that range in color from white to pale pink, typically blooming in late spring to early summer. Its leaves are lance-shaped and have a smooth, waxy surface, contributing to its distinctive appearance.

Mindium laevigatum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as woodlands and stream banks.
