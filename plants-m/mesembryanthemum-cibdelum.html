Mesembryanthemum cibdelum

Mesembryanthemum cibdelum, commonly known as the silver leaf mesem, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It is characterized by its fleshy, silver-green leaves that are often covered in a fine, powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow and orange during the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun to partial shade.

Due to its drought tolerance, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.
