Magnolia fuscata var. hebeclada

Magnolia fuscata var. hebeclada, commonly known as the Chinese magnolia or the yellow magnolia, is a deciduous tree native to eastern China.

It is characterized by its large, showy, yellow flowers that bloom in early spring before the leaves emerge. The leaves are broad, ovate, and have a smooth texture, turning yellow in the fall. The tree has a compact, rounded form with a smooth, gray bark that becomes fissured with age.

It is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often cultivated in gardens for its striking floral display.
