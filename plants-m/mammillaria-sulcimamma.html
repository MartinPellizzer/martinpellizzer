Mammillaria sulcimamma

Mammillaria sulcimamma, commonly known as the zigzag mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its short, cylindrical stem and prominent, rib-like structures. The plant features dense, white to pale yellow spines and produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires bright sunlight, making it a popular choice for succulent gardens.

Its unique appearance and relatively easy care make it a favored plant among cactus enthusiasts.
