Memecylon cyanocarpum

Memecylon cyanocarpum, commonly known as the blue-fruited memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to the wet tropical forests of Southeast Asia.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is occasionally cultivated in botanical gardens.
