Macroptilium lathyroides var. bustarretianum

Macroptilium lathyroides var. bustarretianum, commonly known as the Bostartian burr legume, is a perennial shrub native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the legume family, Fabaceae, and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and erect, spiny stems. The plant produces small, yellow flowers followed by distinctive, inflated pods that contain several seeds. It is adapted to arid and semi-arid environments, making it a resilient species in challenging climates.

Its botanical features make it useful for both ecological restoration and as a forage plant in certain regions.
