Moonia heterophylla

Moonia heterophylla, commonly known as the variable moonia, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family and is characterized by its distinctive, often hairy, foliage. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers in shades of yellow to orange, typically blooming in spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is a popular choice among gardeners in Mediterranean climates.
