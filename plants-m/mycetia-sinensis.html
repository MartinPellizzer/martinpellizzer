Mycetia sinensis

Mycetia sinensis, commonly known as the Chinese mycetia, is a climbing shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It belongs to the family Fabaceae and is characterized by its trifoliate leaves and yellow-orange flowers. The plant produces legume pods that contain small, dark seeds. It is often found in tropical and subtropical regions, growing in moist, shaded environments.

Mycetia sinensis is valued for its ornamental qualities and traditional medicinal uses.
