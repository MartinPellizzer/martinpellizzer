Mandevilla oblongifolia

Mandevilla oblongifolia, commonly known as the oblong-leaf mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically dark green and glossy. The plant produces clusters of vibrant, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as a decorative plant.

Mandevilla oblongifolia is a vigorous climber, often used in gardens to cover trellises or walls.
