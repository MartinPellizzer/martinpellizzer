Malaxis spicata f. variagata

Malaxis spicata f. variagata, commonly known as the variegated spicata malaxis, is a rare and striking orchid species native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its unique foliage, featuring variegated leaf patterns with shades of green, white, and yellow. The plant produces intricate, pendulous inflorescences with small, delicate flowers in shades of pink and white. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Due to its rarity and ornamental value, it is often cultivated by enthusiasts and conservationists alike.
