Manihot floribunda

Manihot floribunda, commonly known as the arrowleaf or arrowhead plant, is a herbaceous perennial native to the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped, arrowhead-shaped leaves that are often glossy and dark green. The plant produces clusters of small, white or pale yellow flowers, which are typically borne on erect stems. It thrives in moist, well-drained soil and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Manihot floribunda is also known for its ability to tolerate partial shade and is commonly used in ornamental gardens.
