Moraea deserticola

Moraea deserticola, commonly known as the desert lily, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its striking, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink and purple. The plant typically grows in arid or semi-arid environments, showcasing its adaptability to harsh conditions. It has narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, clustered flowers during the cooler months.

Moraea deserticola is often found in rocky soils and is valued for its ornamental appeal in gardens.
