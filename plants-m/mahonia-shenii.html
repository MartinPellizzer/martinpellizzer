Mahonia shenii

Mahonia shenii, commonly known as the Chinese mahonia, is a shrub native to China.

It belongs to the Berberidaceae family and is characterized by its evergreen foliage and spiny stems. The plant produces yellow flowers in late spring, which are followed by blue-black berries. Mahonia shenii is often cultivated for its ornamental value and hardy nature.

It thrives in well-drained soil and partial to full sun.
