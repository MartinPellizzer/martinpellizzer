Mitrastemon yamamotoi var. yamamotoi

Mitrastemon yamamotoi var. yamamotoi, commonly known as Yamamoto's mitrastemon, is a flowering plant native to East Asia.

It is a member of the Lamiaceae family, characterized by its square stems and aromatic leaves. The plant produces small, tubular flowers that are typically violet or blue in color, arranged in dense clusters. It thrives in moist, shaded environments and is often found in forest understories.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for botanical gardens and conservation efforts.
