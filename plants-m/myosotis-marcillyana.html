Myosotis marcillyana

Myosotis marcillyana, commonly known as the Corsican forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region, particularly Corsica.

It is characterized by its small, delicate blue or violet flowers that bloom in early spring. The plant typically grows in moist, shaded environments such as woodland floors or along stream banks. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a soft, velvety texture.

Myosotis marcillyana is valued for its ornamental beauty and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its aesthetic appeal.
