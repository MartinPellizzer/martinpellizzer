Microcos reticulata

Microcos reticulata, commonly known as the Chinese daphne, is a deciduous shrub native to China.

It features small, fragrant flowers that bloom in late winter or early spring, typically in shades of white, pink, and red. The plant has finely divided, feathery leaves that give it a delicate appearance. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its ornamental value is enhanced by its early blooming habit and aromatic foliage.
