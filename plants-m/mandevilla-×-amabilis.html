Mandevilla × amabilis

Mandevilla × amabilis, commonly known as the tropical mandevilla, is a hybrid flowering plant native to the Caribbean and South America.

It is a vigorous evergreen shrub or climbing vine that produces showy, trumpet-shaped flowers in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has thick, waxy leaves that are typically dark green and glossy, and it thrives in warm, humid climates. Mandevilla × amabilis is often cultivated for its ornamental value in gardens and as a houseplant.

It requires well-draining soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth and blooming.
