Mesembryanthemum brachyphyllum

Mesembryanthemum brachyphyllum, commonly known as the dwarf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, blue-gray leaves that are often covered in a waxy coating to reduce water loss. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of pink, red, and yellow, typically during the winter months. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid environments.
