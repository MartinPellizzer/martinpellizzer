Milium racemosum

Milium racemosum, commonly known as the clustered millet, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its compact, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear numerous tiny, white to pale yellow florets. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Milium racemosum is often found in open, sunny habitats such as grasslands and rocky areas.
