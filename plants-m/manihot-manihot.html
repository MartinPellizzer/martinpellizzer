Manihot manihot

Manihot manihot, commonly known as cassava, is a tropical plant native to South America.

It is a dicotyledonous shrub that produces edible roots and tubers. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in clusters. Its roots are rich in starch and are a staple food in many tropical regions.

The plant is also known for its ability to thrive in poor soil conditions.
