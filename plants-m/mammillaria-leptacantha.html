Mammillaria leptacantha

Mammillaria leptacantha, commonly known as the slender mammillaria, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its cylindrical stems covered in small, white spines and rounded tubercles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and compact size, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and indoor cultivation.
