Memecylon membranifolium

Memecylon membranifolium, commonly known as the membranous-leaf memecylon, is a small evergreen shrub native to tropical regions of Asia.

It is characterized by its delicate, membranous leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in clusters, which are followed by small, berry-like fruits. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in rainforests.

Due to its ornamental value and ecological importance, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
