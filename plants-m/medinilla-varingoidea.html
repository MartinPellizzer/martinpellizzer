Medinilla varingoidea

Medinilla varingoidea, commonly known as the velvet tangerine.

This tropical plant is native to the Philippines and belongs to the Myristicaceae family. It is characterized by its dense, velvety foliage and produces showy, fragrant flowers in shades of pink and white. The plant thrives in humid, warm environments and requires well-draining soil.

It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its aesthetic appeal and unique foliage texture.
