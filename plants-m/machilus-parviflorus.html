Machilus parviflorus

Machilus parviflorus, commonly known as the small-flowered chinquapin, is a species of flowering plant in the family Lauraceae.

It is native to China and is characterized by its evergreen leaves and small, white flowers. The plant produces drupes that are typically dark purple to black when mature. It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous regions.
