Maxillaria porphyrostele

Maxillaria porphyrostele, commonly known as the purple-stem orchid, is a terrestrial orchid native to the tropical regions of South America.

It is characterized by its dark green, strap-like leaves and produces striking orchid-like flowers with a velvety purple or brownish hue. The plant typically grows in shaded, moist environments, often in forest understories. Its inflorescence consists of a raceme of fragrant, showy flowers that bloom in late summer.

Maxillaria porphyrostele is noted for its ornamental value and is cultivated for its unique and attractive floral display.
