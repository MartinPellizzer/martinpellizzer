Morus rubra f. laevis

Morus rubra f. laevis, commonly known as the smooth mulberry, is a cultivar of the red mulberry tree.

It is characterized by its smooth, glabrous leaves, which distinguish it from other mulberry varieties. The plant is native to eastern North America and thrives in temperate climates. It produces small, aggregate fruits that are typically dark purple when ripe.

This variety is often cultivated for its ornamental value and as a food source for wildlife.
