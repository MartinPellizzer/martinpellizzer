Manihot longiracemosa

Manihot longiracemosa, commonly known as the long raceme arrowleaf, is a tropical plant belonging to the Euphorbiaceae family.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can reach up to 30 cm in length. The plant produces clusters of small, greenish-yellow flowers arranged in elongated inflorescences. It is native to Central and South America and thrives in moist, tropical environments.

Due to its unique foliage and ornamental value, it is sometimes cultivated in botanical gardens.
