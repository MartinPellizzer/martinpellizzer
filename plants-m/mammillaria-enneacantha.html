Mammillaria enneacantha

Mammillaria enneacantha, commonly known as the nine-armed hedgehog cactus, is a species native to the deserts of Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive appearance, featuring numerous radial spines that give it its common name. The plant has a short, thick stem and produces yellow flowers during the summer months. It is well adapted to arid environments, storing water in its fleshy stems.

Due to its unique appearance and hardiness, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts and collectors.
