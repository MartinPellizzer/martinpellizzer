Maxillaria rubroglossa

Maxillaria rubroglossa, commonly known as the red-lip orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Orchidaceae family and is characterized by its vibrant, showy flowers. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees in humid forest environments. Its flowers feature a distinctive red lip with contrasting yellow and green markings, enhancing its visual appeal.

Maxillaria rubroglossa thrives in warm, humid conditions with bright but indirect light.
