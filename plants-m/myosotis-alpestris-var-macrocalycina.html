Myosotis alpestris var. macrocalycina

Myosotis alpestris var. macrocalycina, commonly known as the Alpine Forget-me-not, is a perennial herbaceous plant native to the alpine regions of Europe.

It features slender, upright stems that bear clusters of delicate blue or violet flowers. The leaves are lance-shaped and covered in a fine, silky hair, giving them a soft appearance. This variety is distinguished by its larger calyces compared to the typical species, which are often tinged with purple.

It thrives in cool, moist environments and is often found in mountain meadows and rocky slopes.
