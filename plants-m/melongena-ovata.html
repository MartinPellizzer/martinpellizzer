Melongena ovata

Melongena ovata, commonly known as the eggplant or brinjal, is a flowering plant in the nightshade family, Solanaceae.

It is characterized by its thick, fleshy, and usually purple-colored fruit, which is often used in various cuisines worldwide. The plant has large, heart-shaped leaves that are typically green and covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are typically white or pale purple and are borne in clusters.

Melongena ovata is native to India and has been cultivated for thousands of years for its edible fruit.
