Mammillaria Nunezii

Mammillaria nunezii is a small, cactus-like plant native to Mexico. It is known for its distinctive white spines and compact, globular shape. This species is often grown as a popular ornamental plant due to its unique appearance. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water. Its beauty and resilience make it a favorite among succulent enthusiasts.
