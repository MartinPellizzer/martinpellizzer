Medinilla confertiflora

Medinilla confertiflora, commonly known as the clustered medinilla, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of fragrant, waxy flowers that range in color from white to pink and red. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and often covered in a fine layer of hairs. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla confertiflora is also valued for its ability to produce abundant blooms throughout the year under proper care.
