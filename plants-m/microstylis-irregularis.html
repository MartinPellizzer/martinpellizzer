Microstylis irregularis

Microstylis irregularis, commonly known as the irregular stamen grass, is a small herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of Africa.

It belongs to the grass family Poaceae and is characterized by its slender, erect stems and narrow leaves. The plant produces small, inconspicuous flowers that are typically arranged in spikelets, which are clustered in panicles. Microstylis irregularis is notable for its unique floral morphology, particularly the irregular arrangement of its stamens.

It thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in open woodland or grassland habitats.
