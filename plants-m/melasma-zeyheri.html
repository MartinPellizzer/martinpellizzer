Melasma zeyheri

Melasma zeyheri, commonly known as the Zeyher's melasma, is a species of flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features showy, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or orange in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.

Melasma zeyheri is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its vibrant blooms.
