Mammillaria aureoviridis

Mammillaria aureoviridis, commonly known as the yellow-green hedgehog cactus, is a compact, spineless cactus native to Mexico.

It features a globular shape with a greenish-yellow stem covered in small, rounded tubercles. The plant produces small, white flowers during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and low maintenance, it is a popular choice for cactus enthusiasts.
