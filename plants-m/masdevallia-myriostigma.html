Masdevallia myriostigma

Masdevallia myriostigma, commonly known as the "Crimson Lady," is a terrestrial orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its delicate, linear leaves and striking, fragrant flowers with a deep red hue. The plant typically grows in cool, shaded environments with high humidity. Its pseudobulbs store water and nutrients, allowing it to survive in its native habitat.

The flowers bloom in late winter to early spring, making it a sought-after species among orchid enthusiasts.
