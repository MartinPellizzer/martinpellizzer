Muscari albicaule

Muscari albicaule, commonly known as the white musk grape.

It is a bulbous perennial plant native to southern Europe and parts of western Asia. The plant produces clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pale blue. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade.

Muscari albicaule is often used in gardens for its ornamental value and is relatively low maintenance.
