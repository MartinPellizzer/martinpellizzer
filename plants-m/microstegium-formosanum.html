Microstegium formosanum

Microstegium formosanum, commonly known as the Taiwan hairgrass, is a species of flowering plant in the grass family Poaceae.

It is native to East Asia, particularly Taiwan, and is characterized by its fine, tufted growth habit. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences that bear tiny, two-lobed flowers. Microstegium formosanum is often found in moist, shaded environments such as forests and stream banks.
