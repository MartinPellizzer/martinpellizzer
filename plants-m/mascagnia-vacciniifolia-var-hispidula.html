Mascagnia vacciniifolia var. hispidula

Mascagnia vacciniifolia var. hispidula, commonly known as the hairy mascagnia, is a flowering plant native to the southeastern United States.

Its unique appearance and adaptability make it a valuable addition to native plant gardens.
