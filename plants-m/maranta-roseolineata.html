Maranta roseolineata

Maranta roseolineata, commonly known as the pink-veined arrowroot, is a tropical plant native to Central and South America.

It belongs to the Marantaceae family, which includes many ornamental and economically important species. The plant is characterized by its striking, pink-veined leaves that are often used in horticulture for their aesthetic value. It thrives in warm, humid environments and requires indirect light and well-draining soil.

Maranta roseolineata is also valued for its potential medicinal properties and is sometimes cultivated for its rhizomes.
