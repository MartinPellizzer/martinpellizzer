Matthiola Incana Subsp. Annua

Matthiola incana subsp. annua is a flowering plant known for its delicate, fragrant blooms and compact growth habit. Native to the Mediterranean region, it thrives in well-drained soil and full sun exposure. This annual species is often cultivated in gardens for its ornamental value and ability to attract pollinators. Its flowers, which range from white to pink and purple, bloom in clusters and add a touch of elegance to summer landscapes. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to watering and soil conditions to ensure successful growth and flowering.
