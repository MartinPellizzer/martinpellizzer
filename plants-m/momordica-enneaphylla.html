Momordica enneaphylla

Momordica enneaphylla, commonly known as the nine-leaf bitter gourd, is a flowering plant in the Cucurbitaceae family.

It is native to Southeast Asia and is characterized by its distinctive, spiny stems and heart-shaped leaves.
