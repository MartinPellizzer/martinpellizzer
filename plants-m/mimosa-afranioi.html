Mimosa afranioi

Mimosa afranioi, commonly known as the African sensitive plant, is a shrub native to southern Africa.

It is characterized by its compound leaves that fold shut when touched, a response triggered by rapid changes in turgor pressure within its cells. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers that bloom in spring, followed by bean-like pods containing seeds. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for xeriscaping.

Due to its unique movement and ornamental value, it is often cultivated in gardens and botanical collections.
