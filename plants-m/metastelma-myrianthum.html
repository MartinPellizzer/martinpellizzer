Metastelma myrianthum

Metastelma myrianthum, commonly known as the mountain star.

It is a flowering plant native to the high-altitude regions of South America, particularly in the Andes. The plant is characterized by its striking, star-shaped flowers that range in color from white to pink. It typically grows in rocky soils and is adapted to cold, arid environments.

Due to its unique appearance and ecological significance, it is a subject of interest in botanical studies and conservation efforts.
