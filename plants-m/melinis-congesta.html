Melinis congesta

Melinis congesta, commonly known as purple lovegrass, is a perennial grass native to South America.

It is characterized by its tufted growth habit and dense, compact foliage. The plant produces small, spikelet-based inflorescences that are typically purple or reddish in color. Its leaves are narrow and have a bluish-green hue, often with a slightly hairy texture.

Melinis congesta is adapted to a variety of soil types and is often found in disturbed or open habitats.
