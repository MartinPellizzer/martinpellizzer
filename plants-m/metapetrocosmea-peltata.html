Metapetrocosmea peltata

Metapetrocosmea peltata, commonly known as the broad-leafed rock orchid, is a flowering plant native to specific regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant thrives in rocky, well-drained soils and is often found in mountainous or forested areas.
