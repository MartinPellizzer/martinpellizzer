Masdevallia calochrysos

Masdevallia calochrysos, commonly known as the golden flame orchid, is a striking epiphytic orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its vibrant orange and yellow flowers, which are often adorned with intricate patterns and a unique, flame-like appearance. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, thriving in high humidity and partial shade. Its leaves are linear and often clustered at the base, forming a rosette shape.

Masdevallia calochrysos is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and relatively compact size, making it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor cultivation.
