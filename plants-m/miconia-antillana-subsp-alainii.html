Miconia antillana subsp. alainii

Miconia antillana subsp.

alainii, commonly known as the Antillean miconia, is a member of the Melastomataceae family and is native to the Caribbean region. It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in moist, shaded habitats within tropical forests.
