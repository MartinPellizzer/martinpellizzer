Medicago dicarpa

Medicago dicarpa, commonly known as the narrow-leafed trefoil, is a perennial legume native to the Mediterranean region.

It features trifoliate leaves with narrow, lance-shaped leaflets and produces small, yellowish-white flowers arranged in clusters. The plant is characterized by its erect, branching stems and is often found in dry, rocky soils. It has a deep root system that helps prevent soil erosion.

Medicago dicarpa is also valued for its ability to fix nitrogen, making it beneficial in agricultural and ecological contexts.
