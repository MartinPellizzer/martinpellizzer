Macrolobium zenkeri

Macrolobium zenkeri, commonly known as the giant spider-plant, is a striking member of the Araceae family.

It is native to the tropical rainforests of Central and South America. The plant is characterized by its large, broad leaves that can grow up to several feet in length. Its unique inflorescence consists of a spadix surrounded by a colorful spathe, typical of many aroid plants.

Due to its size and ornamental value, it is often cultivated as a houseplant or in shaded garden settings.
