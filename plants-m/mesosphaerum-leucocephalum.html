Mesosphaerum leucocephalum

Mesosphaerum leucocephalum, commonly known as the white-headed mesosphaerum, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a small shrub characterized by its dense, compact growth habit and bright yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The plant features simple, lance-shaped leaves that are often covered in fine hairs. Its flowers are clustered in terminal inflorescences, and the species is noted for its ornamental value in gardens.

Mesosphaerum leucocephalum prefers well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for cultivation in arid or semi-arid regions.
