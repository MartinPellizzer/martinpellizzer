Medinilla ovalifolia

Medinilla ovalifolia, commonly known as the velvet tangerine or velvet leaf, is a flowering plant native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its glossy, oval-shaped leaves that give it its scientific name. The plant produces fragrant, showy flowers in shades of pink and white, often arranged in clusters. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant.

Medinilla ovalifolia is also valued for its ability to tolerate partial shade and its decorative foliage.
