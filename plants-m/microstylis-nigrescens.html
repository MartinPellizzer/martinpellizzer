Microstylis nigrescens

Microstylis nigrescens, commonly known as the black-stemmed grass, is a species of flowering plant in the family Poaceae.

It is a perennial herb characterized by its slender, erect stems that often turn dark purple or black with age. The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences containing numerous tiny, spikelet-like flowers. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, with a smooth texture and a bluish-green color.

Microstylis nigrescens is typically found in moist, shaded habitats such as woodlands and stream banks.
