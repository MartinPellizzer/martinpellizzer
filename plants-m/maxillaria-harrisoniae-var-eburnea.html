Maxillaria harrisoniae var. eburnea

Maxillaria harrisoniae var. eburnea, commonly known as the Snowy Maxillaria, is a tropical orchid native to South America.

It features showy, fragrant flowers that are typically white or pale pink, with a distinctive, velvety texture. The plant has a pseudobulb structure typical of many orchids, supporting its growth in shaded, humid environments. Its leaves are dark green and strap-like, often arranged in a compact rosette.

This variety is prized for its ornamental value and is relatively easy to cultivate under the right conditions.
