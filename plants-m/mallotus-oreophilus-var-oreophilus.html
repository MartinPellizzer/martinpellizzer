Mallotus oreophilus var. oreophilus

Mallotus oreophilus var. oreophilus, commonly known as the mountain mallot, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a small to medium-sized tree or shrub that typically grows in moist, shaded forest environments. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually ovate to elliptic in shape. Its flowers are small and clustered in inflorescences, often with yellowish or greenish petals.

The fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, hairy seeds.
