Mentha × yvesii

Mentha × yvesii, commonly known as Yves' mint, is a hybrid mint plant resulting from the cross between Mentha spicata (spearmint) and Mentha piperita (peppermint).

It is characterized by its aromatic, slightly peppermint-scented leaves and is often used in culinary and medicinal applications. The plant typically grows as an upright shrub with square stems and opposite leaves. It thrives in temperate climates and prefers well-drained soil.

Mentha × yvesii is widely cultivated for its essential oils and is also popular in herbal teas and aromatherapy.
