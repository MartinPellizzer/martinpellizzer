Malacothamnus fremontii var. fremontii

Malacothamnus fremontii var. fremontii, commonly known as Fremont's malacothamnus, is a flowering plant native to the western United States.

It is a member of the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads and narrow, silvery-green leaves. The plant produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or sandy habitats.
