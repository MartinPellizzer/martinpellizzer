Masdevallia strobelii

Masdevallia strobelii, commonly known as the Strobel's masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the cloud forests of South America.

It is characterized by its intricate, often colorful inflorescences and its ability to thrive in high-altitude, humid environments. The plant typically grows as an epiphyte, attaching itself to trees without deriving nutrients from them. Its flowers are usually small but striking, with a wide range of colors including red, orange, and yellow.

Masdevallia strobelii is valued by orchid enthusiasts for its beauty and unique growth habits.
