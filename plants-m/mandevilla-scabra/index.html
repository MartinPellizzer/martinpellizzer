Mandevilla Scabra

Mandevilla scabra is a striking tropical vine known for its velvety, waxy leaves and clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers. Native to South America, it thrives in warm, humid climates and is often cultivated as an ornamental plant. This plant is prized for its vibrant blooms that range from pink to red and can reach impressive sizes. It is relatively easy to care for, making it a popular choice for gardeners and indoor plant enthusiasts. With its lush foliage and showy flowers, Mandevilla scabra adds both beauty and interest to any landscape.
