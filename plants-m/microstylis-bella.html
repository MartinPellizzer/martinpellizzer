Microstylis bella

Microstylis bella, commonly known as the elegant feather grass, is a low-growing perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It features slender, upright stems that bear delicate, feathery inflorescences, which are typically pink to purplish in color. The plant thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, marshes, and along the edges of ponds. Its fine, grass-like leaves are narrow and slightly hairy, contributing to its ornamental appeal.

Microstylis bella is valued for its ability to stabilize soil and provide habitat for small aquatic organisms.
