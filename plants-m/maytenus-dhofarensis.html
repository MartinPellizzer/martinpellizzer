Maytenus dhofarensis

It is a small to medium-sized evergreen tree that typically grows in dry, rocky habitats. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are usually leathery and have a dark green upper surface. Its small, inconspicuous flowers are followed by woody, spherical fruits that contain a single seed.
