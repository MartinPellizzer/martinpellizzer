Mimosa pohlii

Mimosa pohlii, commonly known as the silky mimosa, is a small, deciduous shrub native to South Africa.

It features feathery, bipinnate leaves that are soft to the touch and turn a striking red-brown color in autumn. The plant produces fluffy, yellow spherical flower heads that bloom in late spring. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and borders.

Mimosa pohlii is also valued for its ability to attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies.
