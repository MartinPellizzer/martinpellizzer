Mammillaria schiedeana var. dumetorum

Mammillaria schiedeana var. dumetorum, commonly known as the "Cactus of the Desert," is a compact, globular cactus native to Mexico.

It features a dense covering of white, woolly hairs that help protect it from intense sunlight and dehydration. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, adding a striking contrast to its green body. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its unique appearance and low maintenance make it a popular choice for succulent enthusiasts.
