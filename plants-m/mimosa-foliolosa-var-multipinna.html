Mimosa foliolosa var. multipinna

Mimosa foliolosa var. multipinna, commonly known as the clustered mimosa, is a shrub native to South America.

It features small, delicate leaves that are often clustered at the ends of its branches, giving it a unique appearance. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in spring, attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or borders.

Its rapid growth and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental horticulture.
