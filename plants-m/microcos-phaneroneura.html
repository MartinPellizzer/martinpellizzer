Microcos phaneroneura

Microcos phaneroneura, commonly known as the common microcos, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a shrub that typically grows to a height of about 1 to 2 meters. The plant features small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters during the summer months. Its leaves are simple, opposite, and have a slightly hairy texture.

The plant is adapted to dry conditions and is often found in rocky or sandy soils.
