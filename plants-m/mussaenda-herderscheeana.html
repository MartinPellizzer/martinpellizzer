Mussaenda herderscheeana

Mussaenda herderscheeana, commonly known as the yellow Mussaenda, is a flowering plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows to a height of 2 to 5 meters. The plant is characterized by its showy, yellow-orange inflorescences, which are clustered in panicles and attract various pollinators. Its leaves are opposite, ovate, and often covered with fine hairs.

The plant thrives in tropical and subtropical climates, preferring well-drained soil and partial to full sunlight.
