Machilus kusanoi

Machilus kusanoi, commonly known as the Japanese waxleaf privet, is a small evergreen shrub native to Japan.

It features lance-shaped leaves that are glossy and dark green, with a waxy texture. The plant produces small, white flowers in clusters during the spring, which are followed by dark blue berries. It is often cultivated as an ornamental plant due to its attractive foliage and adaptability to various soil types.

Machilus kusanoi prefers partial shade to full sun and is relatively low maintenance.
