Myrceugenia regnelliana f. itatiaiensis

Myrceugenia regnelliana f. itatiaiensis, commonly known as the Itatiaia myrtle, is a flowering plant native to the Atlantic Forest of Brazil.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in humid, shaded environments. The plant features elliptical leaves with a smooth surface and a dark green color. Its small, fragrant flowers are usually white or pale pink, and it produces small, fleshy berries.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is also used in traditional medicine.
