Metastelma rupicola

Metastelma rupicola, commonly known as the rock metaphytes, is a succulent plant native to southern Africa.

It typically grows on rocky outcrops and is adapted to arid or semi-arid environments. The plant features fleshy, sometimes spiny stems and small, clustered leaves that help reduce water loss. Its flowers are usually yellow or orange, appearing in clusters during the warmer months.

Metastelma rupicola is valued for its ornamental appeal and resilience in challenging conditions.
