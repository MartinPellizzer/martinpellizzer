Myosotis ramosissima

Myosotis ramosissima, commonly known as the clustered forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to the mountainous regions of Central Asia.

It is characterized by its dense clusters of small, blue to violet flowers that bloom in late summer. The plant typically grows in alpine or subalpine environments, where it thrives in well-drained soils and full sunlight. Its leaves are narrow and grass-like, often arranged in a rosette at the base of the plant.

Myosotis ramosissima is valued for its ornamental appeal and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its delicate floral display.
