Mesembryanthemum cyathiforme

Mesembryanthemum cyathiforme, commonly known as the Cape groundsel, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Aizoaceae family and is characterized by its fleshy, gray-green leaves and yellow flowers that bloom in spring. The species is also known for its ability to store water in its leaves, aiding in its survival in arid environments.
