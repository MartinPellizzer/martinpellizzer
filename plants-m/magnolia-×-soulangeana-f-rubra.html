Magnolia × soulangeana f. rubra

Magnolia × soulangeana f. rubra, commonly known as the red magnolia, is a hybrid plant that exhibits striking ornamental features.

It is characterized by its large, showy flowers that range from pink to deep red, often with a white or pale pink base. The plant is a deciduous shrub or small tree, typically reaching heights of 6 to 12 feet. Its foliage consists of broad, ovate leaves that are dark green and glossy.

The species is valued in horticulture for its early spring blooms and ornamental appeal.
