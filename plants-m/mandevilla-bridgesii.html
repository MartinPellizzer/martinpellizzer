Mandevilla bridgesii

Mandevilla bridgesii, commonly known as the everblooming mandevilla, is a vigorous climbing plant native to South America.

It features showy, trumpet-shaped flowers that range in color from red to pink and orange, blooming continuously throughout the growing season. The plant has heart-shaped, dark green leaves that are glossy and provide a lush backdrop to its vibrant flowers. It thrives in warm climates and requires well-draining soil and full sun for optimal growth.

Mandevilla bridgesii is often used in gardens and as a decorative vine due to its ornamental value and ease of care.
