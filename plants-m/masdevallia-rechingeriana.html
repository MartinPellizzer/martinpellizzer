Masdevallia rechingeriana

Masdevallia rechingeriana, commonly known as the Rechinger's masdevallia, is a member of the Orchidaceae family and is native to the high-altitude regions of South America.

It is a small to medium-sized orchid, typically growing up to 30 centimeters in height, with striking, lance-shaped leaves. The plant produces intricate, colorful flowers that are often adorned with intricate patterns and vibrant hues, including shades of red, orange, and yellow. It thrives in cool, humid environments with bright, indirect light and requires well-draining, acidic soil.

Due to its unique appearance and rarity, it is highly sought after by orchid enthusiasts and botanists alike.
