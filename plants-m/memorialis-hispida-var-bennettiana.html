Memorialis hispida var. bennettiana

Memorialis hispida var. bennettiana, commonly known as the Bennett's rough memorialis, is a member of the Asteraceae family.

It is characterized by its hairy, rough stems and leaves, which give it its scientific name. The plant produces small, daisy-like flowers that are typically yellow or white in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in rocky or arid environments.

This variant is noted for its hardy nature and ability to withstand drought conditions.
