Marica foliosa

Marica foliosa, commonly known as the featherleaf tree, is a species of flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is characterized by its delicate, feathery leaves that give it its common name. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers in clusters during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-drained soils and prefers full sun to partial shade.

Marica foliosa is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
