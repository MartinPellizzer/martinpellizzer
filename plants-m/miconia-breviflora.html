Miconia breviflora

Miconia breviflora, commonly known as the short-flowered miconia, is a tropical shrub native to South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 centimeters in diameter. The plant produces small, clustered white or pale yellow flowers that bloom throughout the year. Its fruit is a bright red, fleshy berry that contains numerous seeds.

Miconia breviflora is known for its rapid growth and is often considered an invasive species in certain regions.
