Myrosmodes brevis

Myrosmodes brevis, commonly known as the short-leaf myrosmodes, is a small evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia.

The plant is characterized by its compact growth habit, glossy dark green leaves, and small, fragrant white flowers that bloom in clusters. Myrosmodes brevis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is valued for its ability to thrive in shaded environments.
