Myrcia Carangola

Myrcia carangola is a striking evergreen shrub native to the tropical regions of South America. It is known for its vibrant red flowers and aromatic leaves, making it a popular choice in ornamental horticulture. This plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of rainforests. Its unique appearance and ecological importance have made it a subject of interest for botanists and gardeners alike. Despite its beauty, Myrcia carangola remains relatively obscure in mainstream horticultural circles.
