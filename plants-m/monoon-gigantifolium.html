Monoon gigantifolium

Monoon gigantifolium, commonly known as the giant-leaf monoon, is a striking tropical plant native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its enormous, heart-shaped leaves that can reach lengths of up to 3 meters. The plant produces small, fragrant flowers in clusters, typically white or pale yellow, which bloom during the wet season. Its rapid growth and large foliage make it a popular choice for ornamental landscaping.

However, it requires high humidity and warm temperatures to thrive, making it well-suited for tropical and subtropical climates.
