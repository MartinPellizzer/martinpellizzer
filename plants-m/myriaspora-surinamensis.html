Myriaspora surinamensis

Myriaspora surinamensis, commonly known as the Surinam sponge coral, is a unique marine organism that belongs to the class Anthozoa.

It is characterized by its soft, branching structure and is typically found in shallow tropical waters. The plant exhibits a symbiotic relationship with zooxanthellae, which provide it with essential nutrients through photosynthesis. Its delicate, sponge-like appearance makes it a distinctive feature in coral reef ecosystems.
