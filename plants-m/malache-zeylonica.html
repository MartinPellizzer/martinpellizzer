Malache zeylonica

Malache zeylonica, commonly known as the Ceylon cinnamon tree, is a small evergreen tree native to Sri Lanka.

It is characterized by its aromatic, cinnamon-scented bark, which is harvested for its medicinal and culinary uses. The plant has simple, alternate leaves that are elliptical in shape and have a smooth texture. Its flowers are small and inconspicuous, typically greenish-white in color, and it produces small, red berries.

The plant thrives in tropical climates and requires well-drained soil for optimal growth.
