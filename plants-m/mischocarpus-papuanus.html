Mischocarpus papuanus

Mischocarpus papuanus, commonly known as the Papuan nut tree, is a tropical evergreen tree native to the Pacific Islands, particularly Papua New Guinea.

This species is valued not only for its edible fruits but also for its ecological and cultural significance in its native habitats.
