Medinilla entii

Medinilla entii, commonly known as the Philippine velvet flower, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It belongs to the Melastomataceae family and is characterized by its striking, velvety purple flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has elliptical, dark green leaves with a slightly pubescent surface. It thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often cultivated for its ornamental value.

Medinilla entii is also valued for its potential medicinal properties in traditional herbal practices.
