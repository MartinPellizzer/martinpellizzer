Meistera mentawaiensis

Meistera mentawaiensis, commonly known as the Mentawai arrowhead, is a striking plant native to the rainforests of Indonesia.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that can grow up to 30 cm in length. The plant thrives in humid, shaded environments and is often found in the undergrowth of tropical forests. Its unique leaf shape and vibrant green color make it a popular choice for collectors and enthusiasts.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and endangered species.
