Myrmecodia oninensis

Myrmecodia oninensis, commonly known as the ant-plant, is a unique tropical climbing shrub characterized by its specialized structures called domatia, which provide shelter for colonies of ants.

These ants, in turn, protect the plant from herbivores and contribute to its nutrient cycling through their waste. The plant's leaves are opposite and elliptical, with a smooth texture and a pale green color. It is native to Southeast Asia and thrives in humid, shaded environments.

Myrmecodia oninensis is often cultivated for its ornamental value and its intriguing symbiotic relationship with ants.
