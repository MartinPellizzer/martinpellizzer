Microthelys constricta

Microthelys constricta, commonly known as the narrow-leaf microthelys, is a member of the Orchidaceae family.

It is a terrestrial orchid characterized by its slender, upright stems and linear leaves. The plant produces small, greenish-white flowers arranged in a spike, which are typically short-lived. It is native to the southeastern United States, particularly Florida and Georgia.

Due to its limited distribution and habitat specificity, it is considered a rare and endangered species.
