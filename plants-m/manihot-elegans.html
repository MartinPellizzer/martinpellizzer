Manihot elegans

Manihot elegans, commonly known as arrowleaf balsam, is a herbaceous plant native to tropical regions of the Americas.

It is characterized by its heart-shaped leaves and clusters of small, yellowish-green flowers. The plant produces capsule fruits that contain numerous seeds, which are dispersed by wind. It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest understories.
