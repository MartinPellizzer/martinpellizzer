Macrosolen subsessilis

Macrosolen subsessilis, commonly known as the narrow-leaf spiderwort, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 to 60 centimeters. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces clusters of small, blue to violet flowers. It blooms during the summer months and is often found in moist, well-drained soils.

The species is valued for its ornamental qualities and is sometimes cultivated in gardens.
