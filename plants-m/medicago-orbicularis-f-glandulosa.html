Medicago orbicularis f. glandulosa

Medicago orbicularis f. glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular sickle vetch, is a variant of the sickle vetch species.

It is a perennial forage legume characterized by its distinctive round, sickle-shaped leaves. The plant features prominent glandular hairs on its stems and leaves, which contribute to its unique appearance. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in grasslands and disturbed areas.

This variety is valued for its forage quality and nitrogen-fixing ability, making it useful in agricultural settings.
