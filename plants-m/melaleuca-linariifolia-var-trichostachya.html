Melaleuca linariifolia var. trichostachya

Melaleuca linariifolia var. trichostachya, commonly known as the narrow-leaved paperbark, is a shrub or small tree native to eastern Australia.

It is characterized by its narrow, linear leaves and distinctive, clustered, pale yellow or cream-colored flowers. The plant produces woody, cylindrical capsules that contain numerous small seeds. It typically grows in coastal or wetland areas, preferring well-drained soils.

This variety is notable for its ornamental value and is sometimes cultivated in gardens for its attractive foliage and floral display.
