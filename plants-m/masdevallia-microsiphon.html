Masdevallia microsiphon

Masdevallia microsiphon, commonly known as the miniature siphon orchid, is a species of orchid native to the Andean highlands of South America.

It is characterized by its small, delicate flowers and compact growth habit, often resembling miniature versions of larger orchid species. The plant typically grows in shaded, humid environments, often on tree trunks or among mossy rocks. Its flowers are usually pale green or white, with intricate patterns that enhance its ornamental appeal.

Masdevallia microsiphon is valued in horticulture for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
