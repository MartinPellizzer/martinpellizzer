Mammillaria conica

Mammillaria conica, commonly known as the cone cactus, is a species native to Mexico.

It is a small, globular cactus with a distinctive conical shape, featuring a tall, narrow stem. Its surface is covered in white, woolly spines and yellow or green areoles. The plant produces yellow flowers during the spring and summer months.

It is well adapted to arid environments and requires minimal water and well-draining soil.
