Mimosa Sensibilis Var. Urucumensis

Mimosa sensibilis var. urucumensis is a striking plant known for its sensitive leaves that fold at the slightest touch. Native to the rainforests of Brazil, it thrives in humid and shaded environments. The plant's vivid red flowers make it a popular choice for ornamental gardens. Its unique behavior has made it a subject of fascination for botanists and plant lovers alike. Despite its beauty, it requires careful attention to grow successfully in non-native settings.
