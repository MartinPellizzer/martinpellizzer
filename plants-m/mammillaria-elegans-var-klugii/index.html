Mammillaria Elegans Var. Klugii

Mammillaria elegans var. klugii is a unique and captivating cactus species native to Mexico. Known for its striking appearance, it features short, cylindrical stems covered in dense, white spines. This variety is prized by collectors for its compact size and elegant form. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments. Its beauty and resilience make it a popular choice for both indoor and outdoor gardens.
