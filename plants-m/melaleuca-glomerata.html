Melaleuca glomerata

Melaleuca glomerata, commonly known as the narrow-leaf melaleuca, is a flowering plant native to Australia.

It is a shrub or small tree that typically grows in coastal and wetland areas. The plant has narrow, linear leaves and produces small, clustered white or pink flowers. Its bark is often flaky and reddish in color, and it is known for its aromatic properties.

Melaleuca glomerata is also valued for its ability to thrive in saline environments.
