Maxillaria ligulata

Maxillaria ligulata, commonly known as the narrow-lipped orchid, is a tropical orchid native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its compact growth habit and produces showy, fragrant flowers with a distinctive narrow lip. The plant typically grows on trees or rocks, exhibiting an epiphytic lifestyle. Its flowers are usually pale yellow to greenish-white, with intricate patterns that attract pollinators.

Maxillaria ligulata is valued in horticulture for its ornamental appeal and relatively easy care requirements.
