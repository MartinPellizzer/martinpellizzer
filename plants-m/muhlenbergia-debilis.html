Muhlenbergia debilis

Muhlenbergia debilis, commonly known as the slender muhlenbergia, is a perennial grass native to the southeastern United States.

It typically grows in moist, well-drained soils and thrives in full sun to partial shade. The plant features slender, arching stems with fine, grass-like leaves that are often bluish-green in color. It produces delicate, fluffy seed heads that add ornamental value to gardens.

Muhlenbergia debilis is valued for its low maintenance and ability to form dense, attractive clumps.
