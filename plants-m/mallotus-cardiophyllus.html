Mallotus cardiophyllus

Mallotus cardiophyllus, commonly known as the heart-leaf mallot, is a tropical evergreen shrub native to Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its distinctive heart-shaped leaves, which are usually dark green and glossy. The plant produces small, yellowish-green flowers followed by woody, spherical fruits containing several seeds. It is often cultivated for its ornamental value and is also used in traditional medicine.

Its growth habit makes it suitable for hedges and as a landscape plant in warm climates.
