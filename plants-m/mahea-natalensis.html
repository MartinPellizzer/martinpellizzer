Mahea natalensis

Mahea natalensis, commonly known as Natal hibiscus, is a flowering plant native to southern Africa.

It belongs to the Malvaceae family, which includes hibiscus and cotton plants. The plant features showy, five-petaled flowers that are typically pink or red in color. Its leaves are heart-shaped and have a rough texture, while its stems are woody and often branched.
