Massonia amoena

Massonia amoena, commonly known as the silver leaf plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features fleshy, dark green leaves with a silvery-blue hue on the upper surface, giving it its distinctive appearance. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of white and pink, typically during the spring and summer months. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it suitable for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and containers.
