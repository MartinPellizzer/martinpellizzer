Microstachys bidentata

The plant produces small, spike-like inflorescences bearing tiny, two-lobed flowers, which are typically greenish or brownish in color. Microstachys bidentata thrives in moist, well-drained soils and is often found in wetlands, ditches, and along the edges of streams.
