Macroceratides pseudostizolobium

Macroceratides pseudostizolobium, commonly known as the African sandthorn, is a shrub native to tropical Africa.

It belongs to the Fabaceae family, characterized by its compound leaves and legume fruits. The plant features trifoliate leaves with serrated edges and produces small, yellowish flowers. Its root system is deep and robust, allowing it to thrive in arid and sandy soils.

The plant is often used in traditional medicine and for its hard, fibrous wood.
