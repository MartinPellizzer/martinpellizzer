Meratia

Meratia, commonly known as the silverleaf tree, is a genus of flowering plants in the family Fabaceae.

It is characterized by its small, often clustered flowers that are typically pale or white in color. The leaves are usually pinnately compound, with a silvery or grayish underside that gives the plant its common name. These plants are valued for their ornamental appeal and are sometimes used in traditional medicine.
