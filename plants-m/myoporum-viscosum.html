Myoporum viscosum

Myoporum viscosum, commonly known as sticky myoporum, is a shrub native to Australia.

It is characterized by its sticky, glandular leaves that give it a distinctive texture. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers that bloom in spring. It is often found in coastal and sandy soils, thriving in well-drained conditions.

Myoporum viscosum is valued for its ornamental appeal and is used in landscaping and restoration projects.
