Myrianthus mildbraedii

Myrianthus mildbraedii, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a striking ornamental plant native to southern Africa.

It is a deciduous shrub or small tree that can grow up to 6 meters in height, characterized by its bright red or orange flowers that bloom in clusters. The plant has compound leaves with multiple leaflets and produces small, round fruits that split open to release seeds.

Its botanical features make it a popular choice for gardens and landscapes due to its vibrant floral display and adaptability to various soil types.
