Myosotis × bollandica

Myosotis × bollandica, commonly known as the Dutch forget-me-not, is a hybrid species within the Myosotidaceae family.

It is characterized by its small, blue or violet flowers that bloom in early summer. The plant typically grows to a height of 10–30 cm and has hairy stems and lance-shaped leaves. Native to Europe, it thrives in moist, shady environments such as woodland floors and meadows.

Its ornamental value makes it a popular choice for garden cultivation.
