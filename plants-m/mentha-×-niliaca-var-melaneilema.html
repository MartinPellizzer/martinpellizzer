Mentha × niliaca var. melaneilema

Mentha × niliaca var. melaneilema, commonly known as black mint, is a hybrid species within the mint family, Lamiaceae.

It is characterized by its dark purple to black leaves and stems, which distinguish it from other mint varieties. The plant typically grows as an herbaceous perennial, reaching heights of up to 60 cm. Its flowers are small, tubular, and usually violet or purple in color, arranged in whorled clusters.

This variety is valued for its aromatic properties and is sometimes used in traditional medicine and culinary applications.
