Miconia virescens

Miconia virescens, commonly known as the green miconia, is a tropical shrub or small tree native to Central and South America.

It is characterized by its large, heart-shaped leaves that are usually glossy and dark green. The plant produces showy, white or pale pink flowers that are clustered in inflorescences. Its fruit is a capsule that splits open to release small, winged seeds.

Miconia virescens is often found in humid, shaded environments within tropical rainforests.
