Microstachys nummulariifolia

Microstachys nummulariifolia, commonly known as the silver dollar plant, is a succulent species native to southern Africa.

It features small, round, silver-gray leaves that are thick and fleshy, adapted for storing water. The plant produces small, bell-shaped flowers in shades of yellow or orange during the growing season. It thrives in well-draining soil and requires minimal water, making it ideal for arid environments.

Its compact growth habit and ornamental foliage make it a popular choice for rock gardens and container planting.
