Muscari vandasii

Muscari vandasii, commonly known as the Vandasii grape hyacinth, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It belongs to the Amaryllidaceae family and is characterized by its dense clusters of small, bell-shaped flowers that range in color from blue to purple. It prefers well-drained soil and full sun to partial shade for optimal growth.
