Marica tigridia

Marica tigridia, commonly known as the tiger iris, is a striking perennial plant native to the southwestern United States and northern Mexico.

It features showy, vibrant orange or yellow flowers with dark purple or black markings that resemble a tiger's stripes. The plant has sword-shaped, grass-like leaves that are typically bluish-green in color. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Marica tigridia is known for its drought tolerance and ability to bloom reliably in suitable conditions.
