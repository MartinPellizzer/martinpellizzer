Mammillaria maycobensis

Mammillaria maycobensis, commonly known as the Maycobensis prickly pear, is a species of cactus native to Mexico.

It is characterized by its globular shape and dense covering of white, woolly spines. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in the summer months. It thrives in well-drained soil and requires full sun exposure.

Due to its unique appearance and hardy nature, it is a popular choice for rock gardens and arid landscapes.
