Muraltia brachypetala

Muraltia brachypetala, commonly known as the short-petalled muraltia, is a species of flowering plant in the legume family, Fabaceae.

It is native to tropical and subtropical regions of Africa, particularly in countries like Kenya and Tanzania. Muraltia brachypetala is often found in coastal areas and is sometimes used in traditional medicine for its purported health benefits.
