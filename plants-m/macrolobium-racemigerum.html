Macrolobium racemigerum

Macrolobium racemigerum, commonly known as the African flame tree, is a tropical tree native to Africa and parts of Southeast Asia.

It is characterized by its large, showy, red-orange flowers that bloom in clusters, creating a striking visual display. The plant has pinnately compound leaves with long, slender leaflets that provide a lush green canopy. Its bark is smooth and light brown, often exuding a milky sap when wounded.

The African flame tree is valued for its ornamental beauty and is often planted in gardens and as a street tree in urban areas.
