Mastersiella foliosa

Mastersiella foliosa, commonly known as the leafy mastersiella, is a flowering plant native to South Africa.

It belongs to the Asteraceae family, characterized by its composite flower heads. The plant features narrow, grass-like leaves and produces small, yellowish flowers that bloom in spring. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for rock gardens or dry landscapes.

Due to its ornamental value and adaptability, it is often cultivated in horticultural settings.
