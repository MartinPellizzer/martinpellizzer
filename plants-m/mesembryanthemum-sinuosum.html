Mesembryanthemum sinuosum

Mesembryanthemum sinuosum, commonly known as the zigzag ice plant, is a succulent species native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, ribbed stems and leaves that are often covered in a fine, white powdery coating. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers in shades of yellow, orange, and red, typically blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its ability to store water in its leaves allows it to survive in arid conditions.
