Metabolos rugosus f. javanicus

Metabolos rugosus f. javanicus, commonly known as the Javanese rough metabolos, is a unique variant of the metabolos genus characterized by its rugged, textured foliage.

This plant exhibits a compact growth habit with dense, dark green leaves that have a distinctive rough texture due to the presence of papillae. It is native to the tropical regions of Southeast Asia, particularly Indonesia, where it thrives in humid, shaded environments. The plant's leaves are often used in traditional herbal medicine for their purported anti-inflammatory properties.

Its ornamental value has also led to its cultivation in botanical gardens and as a houseplant.
