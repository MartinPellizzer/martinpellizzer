Masdevallia glandulosa

Masdevallia glandulosa, commonly known as the glandular masdevallia, is a species of orchid native to the Andean regions of South America.

It is characterized by its compact, sympodial growth habit and produces intricate, often fragrant flowers with a wide range of colors. The plant features dense, hairy structures on its stems and leaves, which contribute to its common name. It thrives in cool, humid environments and requires well-drained, acidic soil.

Masdevallia glandulosa is prized by orchid enthusiasts for its unique appearance and relatively easy care requirements.
