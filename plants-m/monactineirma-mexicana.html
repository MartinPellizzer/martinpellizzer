Monactineirma mexicana

Monactineirma mexicana, commonly known as the Mexican monactine.

It is a rare flowering plant native to the highlands of central Mexico. The plant features small, clustered white flowers with a distinct scent, and its leaves are narrow and lance-shaped. It thrives in well-drained soils and requires a cool, humid environment.

Due to habitat loss, it is considered endangered and is protected in several conservation reserves.
