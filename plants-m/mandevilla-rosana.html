Mandevilla rosana

Mandevilla rosana, commonly known as the pink mandevilla, is a flowering plant native to South America.

It is characterized by its vigorous growth and cascading clusters of trumpet-shaped flowers. The plant produces showy, pink to red blooms that attract pollinators such as bees and butterflies. Its foliage consists of glossy, dark green leaves that are often variegated with white or cream markings.

Mandevilla rosana is well-suited for container gardening and can thrive in both indoor and outdoor environments.
