Medinilla medinillana

Medinilla medinillana, commonly known as the "Bottlebrush Medinilla," is a tropical evergreen shrub native to the Philippines.

It is characterized by its dense, bottlebrush-like inflorescences that bloom in shades of red, pink, and white. The plant has glossy, dark green leaves that are opposite and elliptical in shape. It thrives in warm, humid climates and requires well-draining soil to prevent root rot.

Medinilla medinillana is often cultivated as an ornamental plant for its attractive flowers and foliage.
