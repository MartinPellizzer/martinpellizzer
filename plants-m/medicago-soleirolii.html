Medicago soleirolii

Medicago soleirolii, commonly known as the soleirolii medick, is a flowering plant native to the Mediterranean region.

It is a low-growing annual herb with slender stems and small, trifoliate leaves. The plant produces yellow flowers that bloom in late spring to early summer. It thrives in well-drained soils and is often found in disturbed habitats such as roadsides and fields.

Medicago soleirolii is valued for its ecological role in supporting pollinators and its potential use in sustainable agriculture.
