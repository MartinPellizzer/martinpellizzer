Masdevallia dunstervillei

Masdevallia dunstervillei, commonly known as the Dunsterville's masdevallia, is a terrestrial orchid native to the cloud forests of Colombia.

It features slender, wiry stems and small, dark green leaves that are often covered in a fine, white pubescence. The plant produces intricate, fragrant flowers with bright yellow sepals and petals, typically in late spring. It thrives in cool, humid environments with high air circulation.

Due to its rarity and specific habitat requirements, it is considered a delicate and challenging species to cultivate.
