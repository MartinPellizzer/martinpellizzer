Mesembryanthemum intermedium var. album

Mesembryanthemum intermedium var. album, commonly known as the white-leaf ice plant, is a succulent plant native to South Africa.

It features fleshy, thick leaves that are typically white or pale green, adapted to store water in arid environments. The plant produces small, star-shaped flowers that are usually pink or white, blooming in summer. It thrives in well-drained soil and full sun, making it a popular choice for rock gardens and xeriscaping.

Its low maintenance and drought tolerance contribute to its appeal in landscaping.
