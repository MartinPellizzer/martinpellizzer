Myosotis marginata

Myosotis marginata, commonly known as the New Zealand forget-me-not, is a flowering plant native to New Zealand.

It is a herbaceous perennial that typically grows to a height of about 30 cm. The plant features basal leaves that are heart-shaped and covered in fine hairs, while its stems are hairy and upright. Its flowers are small, blue to purple, and have five petals, often appearing in clusters.

It thrives in moist, shady environments and is often found in forest margins and along stream banks.
