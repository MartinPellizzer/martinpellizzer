Myoporum tasmanicum

Myoporum tasmanicum, commonly known as Tasmanian myoporum, is a shrub native to Tasmania, Australia.

It features narrow, lance-shaped leaves that are typically gray-green in color, providing a distinctive visual appeal. The plant produces small, white or pale yellow flowers during the spring and summer months, which are clustered in dense inflorescences. It thrives in well-drained soils and full sun, making it suitable for coastal and dry garden environments.

Myoporum tasmanicum is valued for its ornamental qualities and resilience in harsh conditions.
